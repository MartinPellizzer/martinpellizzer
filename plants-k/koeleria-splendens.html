Koeleria splendens

Koeleria splendens, commonly known as the splendour grass, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central Asia.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, pale blue to purple flowers. Koeleria splendens is often found in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in well-drained soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine displays.
