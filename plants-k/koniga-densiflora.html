Koniga densiflora

Koniga densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered koniga, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a glossy surface, while its bark is smooth and grayish in color.

Koniga densiflora is often found in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and along stream banks.
