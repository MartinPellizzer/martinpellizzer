Kalanchoe flammea

Kalanchoe flammea, commonly known as the flame kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with red or purple margins. The plant produces vibrant orange to red flowers in clusters during the winter months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in bright, indirect sunlight.

Kalanchoe flammea is popular as a decorative plant due to its striking appearance and ease of care.
