Kohlrauschia prolifera subsp. nanteuilii

Kohlrauschia prolifera subsp.

nanteuilii, commonly known as the Nanteuil's kohlrauschia, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of blue or purple.

The plant thrives in well-drained sandy soils and full sun to partial shade conditions. Its foliage is typically narrow and grass-like, with a silver-green hue.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
