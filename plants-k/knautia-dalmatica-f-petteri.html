Knautia dalmatica f. petteri

Knautia dalmatica f. petteri, commonly known as the Dalmatian knautia, is a compact, flowering perennial native to the Balkans.

It features upright, branched stems that bear clusters of pink to purple, fluffy flower heads resembling small clouds. The plant has lance-shaped, hairy leaves that are typically gray-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value lies in its showy blooms and drought tolerance.
