Kennedia ovata

Kennedia ovata, commonly known as the pink mistflower, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking pink to red flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces fluffy, seed-filled pods that mature in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal regions and dry forests.

Kennedia ovata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
