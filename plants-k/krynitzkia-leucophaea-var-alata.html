Krynitzkia leucophaea var. alata

Krynitzkia leucophaea var. alata, commonly known as the silver-leaf krynitzkia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

Due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
