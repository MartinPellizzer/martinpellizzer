Kaempferia glauca

Kaempferia glauca, commonly known as the blue ginger, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and related plants. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking, orchid-like flowers in shades of blue and purple. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-draining soil.
