Krynitzkia cycloptera

Krynitzkia cycloptera, commonly known as the cycloptera cycad, is a unique species belonging to the Cycadaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, fan-shaped leaves that grow in a tight, compact rosette. The plant produces cones, with the male cones being cylindrical and the female cones spherical, a feature typical of cycads. It is native to South Africa and thrives in arid and semi-arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species.
