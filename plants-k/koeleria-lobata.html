Koeleria lobata

Koeleria lobata, commonly known as the mountain flax, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It is a perennial grass that typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils at high elevations. The plant produces small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a rough texture.

Koeleria lobata is often found in alpine meadows and is adapted to cold, arid conditions.
