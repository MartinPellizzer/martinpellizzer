Kalanchoe crouchii

Kalanchoe crouchii, commonly known as the Christmas chandelier, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red, blooming in winter. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. It produces offsets and aerial plantlets, which can be used for propagation.

Kalanchoe crouchii is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its vibrant blooms and ease of care.
