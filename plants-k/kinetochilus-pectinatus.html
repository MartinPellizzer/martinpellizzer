Kinetochilus pectinatus

Kinetochilus pectinatus, commonly known as the velvetleaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, velvety-textured leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest for both horticultural and ecological studies.
