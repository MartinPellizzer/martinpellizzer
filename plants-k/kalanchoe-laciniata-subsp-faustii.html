Kalanchoe laciniata subsp. faustii

Kalanchoe laciniata subsp.

faustii, commonly known as the fleshy leaf kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often lobed or notched, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The plant produces showy, brightly colored flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red during the winter months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems. Kalanchoe laciniata subsp.

faustii is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance requirements.
