Knema percoriacea f. percoriacea

Knema percoriacea f. percoriacea, commonly known as the white knema, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves with a smooth, waxy surface. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, fleshy berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has limited use in traditional medicine.
