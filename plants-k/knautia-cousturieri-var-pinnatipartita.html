Knautia cousturieri var. pinnatipartita

Knautia cousturieri var. pinnatipartita, commonly known as the Balkan knautia, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It features showy, pink to purple flower heads that resemble daisies, composed of numerous small, ray-like florets. The plant has basal leaves that are pinnately divided, giving it a feathery appearance. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, loamy soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to attract pollinators.
