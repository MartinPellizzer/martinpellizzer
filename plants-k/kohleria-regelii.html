Kohleria regelii

Kohleria regelii, commonly known as the velvet plant, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, lance-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity to flourish.
