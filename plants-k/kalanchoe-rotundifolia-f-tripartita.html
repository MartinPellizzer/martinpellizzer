Kalanchoe rotundifolia f. tripartita

Kalanchoe rotundifolia f. tripartita, commonly known as the three-lobed Kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are rounded and often have a waxy texture. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of orange, yellow, and red during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

This variety is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of care.
