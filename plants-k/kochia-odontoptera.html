Kochia odontoptera

Kochia odontoptera, commonly known as the sharpleaf kochia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its upright, branching structure and small, greenish-yellow flowers that appear in late summer. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, aiding in water retention. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.
