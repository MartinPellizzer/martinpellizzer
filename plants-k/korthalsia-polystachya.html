Korthalsia polystachya

Korthalsia polystachya, commonly known as the African star grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, branching stems and clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. The plant produces small, three-lobed leaves that are typically hairy and arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Korthalsia polystachya is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
