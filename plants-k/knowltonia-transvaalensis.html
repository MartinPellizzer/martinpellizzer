Knowltonia transvaalensis

Knowltonia transvaalensis, commonly known as the Transvaal orchid, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, enabling it to survive in its arid environment. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to anchor it in rocky or sandy soils.

The orchid is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
