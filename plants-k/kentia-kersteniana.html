Kentia kersteniana

Kentia kersteniana, commonly known as the Kentia palm, is a striking tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and feathery, arching fronds that give it a graceful appearance. The plant has a slow growth rate, making it ideal for indoor cultivation. Its leaves are typically dark green and glossy, adding a lush, tropical feel to any space.

Kentia kersteniana is well-suited for low-light conditions and requires moderate watering to thrive.
