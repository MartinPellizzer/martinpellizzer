Kobresia vidua

Kobresia vidua, commonly known as the desert feathergrass, is a low-growing perennial grass native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia.

It thrives in harsh environments with minimal rainfall and high temperatures, often found in steppes and deserts. The plant has slender, wiry stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are adapted to withstand extreme conditions. Its deep root system helps it survive droughts and anchor it in loose, sandy soils.

Kobresia vidua plays a crucial role in stabilizing soil and preventing erosion in its native habitats.
