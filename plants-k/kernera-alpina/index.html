Kernera Alpina

Kernera alpina is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high altitudes of southern Africa. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions with cool, moist climates. This species is known for its striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in early spring. Due to habitat loss and climate change, Kernera alpina is facing increasing threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique and beautiful plant for future generations.
