Knema glauca var. andamanica

Knema glauca var. andamanica, commonly known as the Andaman knema, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Andaman Islands.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by edible, fleshy berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
