Kobresia prainii

Kobresia prainii, commonly known as the Prain's kobresia, is a low-growing perennial grass found in high-altitude regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant typically thrives in cold, arid environments, such as alpine meadows and rocky slopes. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Kobresia prainii plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and preventing erosion in its native habitats.
