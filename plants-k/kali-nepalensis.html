Kali nepalensis

Kali nepalensis, commonly known as the Nepal nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan regions of Nepal and northern India.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant is characterized by its attractive blue or violet flowers and oval-shaped leaves. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes.

Kali nepalensis is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
