Kurrimia robusta var. thorelii

Kurrimia robusta var. thorelii, commonly known as the Thorelii kurrimia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features palmate leaves with lobed margins, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and is used in both ornamental gardening and traditional medicine.
