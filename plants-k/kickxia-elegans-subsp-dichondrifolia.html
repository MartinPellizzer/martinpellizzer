Kickxia elegans subsp. dichondrifolia

Kickxia elegans subsp.

dichondrifolia, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features slender, upright stems and small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue or violet in color.

The leaves are narrow and grass-like, arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
