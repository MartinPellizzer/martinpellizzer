Kellettia odorata

Kellettia odorata, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Kellettia odorata is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its cultural significance in some local communities.
