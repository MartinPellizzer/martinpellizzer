Kochia odontoptera var. curta

Kochia odontoptera var. curta, commonly known as the shortleaf kochia, is a perennial flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its upright, shrub-like growth habit and distinctive, spine-tipped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.

Kochia odontoptera var. curta is adapted to withstand drought and is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements.
