Kuhnistera violacea

Kuhnistera violacea, commonly known as the violet kuhnistera, is a rare and striking member of the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant violet-blue flowers and heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. Native to the mountainous regions of Central America, this plant thrives in cool, moist environments. Its unique coloration and ornamental value make it a sought-after specimen in botanical gardens.

Due to habitat loss, it is classified as endangered, highlighting the need for conservation efforts.
