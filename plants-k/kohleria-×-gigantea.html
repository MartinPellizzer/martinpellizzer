Kohleria × gigantea

Kohleria × gigantea, commonly known as the giant kohleria, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to South America and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors such as red, orange, and yellow. The plant features thick, waxy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, providing a striking visual contrast to its colorful blooms. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Kohleria × gigantea is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
