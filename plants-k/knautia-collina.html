Knautia collina

Knautia collina, commonly known as the alpine knautia, is a perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features upright, branched stems that are covered in soft, hairy foliage. Its flowers are typically pink to purple, forming showy clusters that bloom in late summer.

Knautia collina thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for alpine gardens.
