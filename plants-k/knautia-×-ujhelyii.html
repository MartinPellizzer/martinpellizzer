Knautia × ujhelyii

Knautia × ujhelyii, commonly known as the hybrid knautia, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the tribe Cardueae within the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences that range in color from pink to purple, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Native to the Carpathian region, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and ecologically valuable in diverse habitats.
