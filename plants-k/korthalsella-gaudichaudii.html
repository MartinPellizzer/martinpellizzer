Korthalsella gaudichaudii

Korthalsella gaudichaudii, commonly known as the white star orchid, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to the wet tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Korthalsella gaudichaudii is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is noted for its ornamental value in horticulture.
