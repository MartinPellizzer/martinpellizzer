Kohleria vestita

Kohleria vestita, commonly known as the silver leaf kohleria, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety silver-green leaves. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, typically blooming during the warmer months. Kohleria vestita thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and unique floral display.
