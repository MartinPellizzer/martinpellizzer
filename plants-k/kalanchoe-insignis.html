Kalanchoe insignis

Kalanchoe insignis, commonly known as the "Woolly Kalanchoe," is a succulent plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive, fuzzy appearance. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically during the winter months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Kalanchoe insignis is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens and rockeries.
