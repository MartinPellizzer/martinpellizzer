Kaempferia vittata

Kaempferia vittata, commonly known as the Zebra Orchid, is a striking tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features vibrant, zebra-striped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, typically blooming in spring or early summer. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Kaempferia vittata is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
