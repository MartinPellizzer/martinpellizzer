Keetia angustifolia

Keetia angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf keetia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, shaded environments. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, clustered in small inflorescences, and bloom during the warmer months.

Keetia angustifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual interest.
