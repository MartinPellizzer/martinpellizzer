Knautia asperifolia

Knautia asperifolia, commonly known as the rough knautia, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a member of the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and profuse displays of pink to purple flowers. Knautia asperifolia is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
