Kunzmannia roraimae

Kunzmannia roraimae, commonly known as the Roraima grass, is a unique flowering plant native to the tepuis of South America.

The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with pale green to brownish flowers, which are adapted to the harsh environmental conditions of its high-altitude environment. Kunzmannia roraimae is notable for its ecological significance in maintaining the fragile ecosystems of the tepuis.
