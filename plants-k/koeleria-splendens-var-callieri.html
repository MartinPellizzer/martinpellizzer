Koeleria splendens var. callieri

Koeleria splendens var. callieri, commonly known as the purple grass or mountain grass, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is a member of the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, blue to purple florets. Its foliage is typically green with a bluish tinge, and it thrives in cool, moist environments.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens or as a ground cover in alpine settings.
