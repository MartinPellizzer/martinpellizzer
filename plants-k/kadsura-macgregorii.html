Kadsura macgregorii

Kadsura macgregorii, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or MacGregor's kadsura, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

Kadsura macgregorii is also noted for its ability to purify indoor air, making it a popular choice for interior landscaping.
