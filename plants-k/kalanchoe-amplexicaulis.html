Kalanchoe amplexicaulis

Kalanchoe amplexicaulis, commonly known as the hanging kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, and it produces vibrant clusters of tubular flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems. It blooms during the winter months, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and easy care requirements make it a favored ornamental plant.
