Kefersteinia pastorellii

Kefersteinia pastorellii, commonly known as the desert star, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that produces striking yellow flowers with a distinctive star-like shape. The plant has small, waxy leaves that help it conserve water in its harsh environment. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires minimal irrigation.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is often cultivated in xeriscapes and rock gardens.
