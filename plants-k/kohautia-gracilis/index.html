Kohautia Gracilis

Kohautia gracilis is a rare and delicate fern native to the mountainous regions of New Zealand. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest floors. This species is known for its slender, arching fronds that give it a graceful appearance. Due to its limited habitat and susceptibility to environmental changes, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique plant for future generations.
