Kionophyton riodelayensis

Kionophyton riodelayensis, commonly known as the Rio Dela Rey grass, is a flowering plant native to South America, specifically found in Argentina and Uruguay.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are typically green or pale yellow in color. It thrives in moist, sandy soils and is often found in grassland and wetland habitats.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
