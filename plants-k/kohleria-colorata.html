Kohleria colorata

Kohleria colorata, commonly known as the pink flamingo plant, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. This evergreen shrub is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range from pink to red, often resembling the color of a flamingo. The leaves are typically oval-shaped and glossy, providing a striking contrast to the colorful blooms.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.
