Kohleria serrulata

Kohleria serrulata, commonly known as the serrated kohleria, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges, giving it its common name. It produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, which bloom in clusters.
