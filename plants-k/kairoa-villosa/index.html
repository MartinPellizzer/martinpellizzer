Kairoa Villosa

Kairoa villosa is a unique and rare plant native to the mountainous regions of New Zealand. It is known for its striking appearance and ecological significance within its habitat. This plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas. Its fibrous roots and waxy leaves help it survive in harsh conditions. Due to its limited range and habitat loss, Kairoa villosa is considered a conservation concern.
