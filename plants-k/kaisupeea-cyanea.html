Kaisupeea cyanea

Kaisupeea cyanea, commonly known as the blue kaisupeea, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue-violet flowers, which bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a waxy texture.
