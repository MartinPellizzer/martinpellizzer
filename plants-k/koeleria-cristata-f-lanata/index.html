Koeleria Cristata F. Lanata

Koeleria cristata f. lanata is a rare and striking variant of the common koeleria plant. This frost-tolerant species is known for its dense, woolly appearance that resembles a fluffy crown. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils. The plant's unique texture and color make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements have made it a favorite among gardeners in dry climates.
