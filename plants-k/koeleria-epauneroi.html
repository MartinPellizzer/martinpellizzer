Koeleria epauneroi

Koeleria epauneroi, commonly known as the Epauneroi grass, is a flowering plant species native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences, which are typical of many grass species. Koeleria epauneroi thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
