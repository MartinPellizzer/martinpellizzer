Kindia

Kindia, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the genus Saintpaulia and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant has velvety, waxy leaves that are typically heart-shaped and dark green in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers indirect sunlight.
