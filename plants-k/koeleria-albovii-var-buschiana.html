Koeleria albovii var. buschiana

Koeleria albovii var. buschiana, commonly known as the Busch's iceplant, is a flowering plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit and features small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is adapted to cold, harsh environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in tundra and alpine habitats.

Koeleria albovii var. buschiana is a member of the Poaceae family, which includes grasses.
