Koeleria bungeana

Koeleria bungeana, commonly known as the Bunge's tufted grass, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, wiry stems that often form dense mats. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Koeleria bungeana is adapted to harsh, arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
