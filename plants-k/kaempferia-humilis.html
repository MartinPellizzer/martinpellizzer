Kaempferia humilis

Kaempferia humilis, commonly known as the dwarf flame orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, colorful flowers. The plant typically features strap-like leaves and produces showy, often red or orange, flower spikes. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Kaempferia humilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
