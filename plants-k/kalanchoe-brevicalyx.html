Kalanchoe brevicalyx

Kalanchoe brevicalyx, commonly known as the Christmas chrysanthemum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems covered in thick, waxy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces showy, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, typically blooming in late winter to early spring. It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and vibrant floral display.

Kalanchoe brevicalyx is also noted for its ability to propagate through stem cuttings, making it easy to grow and share.
