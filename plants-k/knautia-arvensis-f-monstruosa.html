Knautia arvensis f. monstruosa

Knautia arvensis f. monstruosa, commonly known as the giant knautia, is a striking variant of the common knautia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range from pink to violet, often with a distinctive yellow center. This perennial plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. Its foliage is typically gray-green and woolly, adding texture to the landscape.

The plant is noted for its robust growth and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
