Kleinia × peregrina

Kleinia × peregrina, commonly known as the Cape Flats daisy, is a hybrid plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. It produces small, cylindrical seed heads that are often used in traditional medicine.

Kleinia × peregrina is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in challenging climates.
