Kalanchoe vantieghemii

Kalanchoe vantieghemii, commonly known as the Van Tieghem's kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

Kalanchoe vantieghemii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and low maintenance requirements.
