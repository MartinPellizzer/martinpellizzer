Kotschya capitulifera var. robusta

Kotschya capitulifera var. robusta, commonly known as the giant kotschya, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in dense, terminal clusters. The plant has a woody stem with thick, fleshy leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ecologically important species in its native habitats.
