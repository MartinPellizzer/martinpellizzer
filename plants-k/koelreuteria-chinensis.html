Koelreuteria chinensis

Koelreuteria chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese lantern tree, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, lantern-shaped fruit that turn from green to orange-red in autumn. The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities, particularly the vibrant fall foliage and unique fruit clusters. It thrives in a variety of soil types and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.

Koelreuteria chinensis is often planted in urban landscapes for its aesthetic appeal and adaptability.
