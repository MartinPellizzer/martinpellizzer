Knautia rigidiuscula var. travnicensis

Knautia rigidiuscula var. travnicensis, commonly known as the Travnicense knautia, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Balkan region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its prolonged blooming period and ability to attract pollinators.
