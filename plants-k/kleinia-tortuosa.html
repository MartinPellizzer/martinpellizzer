Kleinia tortuosa

Kleinia tortuosa, commonly known as the zigzag kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a zigzag pattern along the stems. The plant produces clusters of small, pink or red flowers during the winter months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright sunlight to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
