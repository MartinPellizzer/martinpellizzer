Knema longepilosa

Knema longepilosa, commonly known as the long-haired knema, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia, including countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.
