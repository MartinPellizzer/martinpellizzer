Koniga maroccana

Koniga maroccana, commonly known as the Moroccan carnation, is a flowering plant native to North Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically produces clusters of small, pink or white petals with a subtle fragrance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both garden beds and floral arrangements.
