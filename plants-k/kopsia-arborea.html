Kopsia arborea

Kopsia arborea, commonly known as the tree kopsia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant features palmate leaves with five to seven lobes and produces clusters of small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Kopsia arborea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and flowering display.
