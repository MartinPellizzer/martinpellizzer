Kaempferia evae

Kaempferia evae, commonly known as the Eave's flame orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, colorful flower spikes. The plant typically grows as a rhizomatous herb, with fleshy, strap-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually vivid red or orange, with a distinctive pattern of darker markings.

Kaempferia evae thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
