Kleinia breviflora

Kleinia breviflora, commonly known as the Cape fuchsia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, often with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for gardeners in arid climates.
