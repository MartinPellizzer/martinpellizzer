Koeleria ciliata var. rigidiuscula

Koeleria ciliata var. rigidiuscula, commonly known as the stiff-haired koeleria, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It is a member of the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit.
