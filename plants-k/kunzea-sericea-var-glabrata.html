Kunzea sericea var. glabrata

Kunzea sericea var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth kunzea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its silver-green, hairless leaves and small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is adapted to coastal and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained environments. It is often used in coastal landscaping due to its salt tolerance and hardiness.

Its aromatic foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental purposes.
