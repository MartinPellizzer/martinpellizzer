Koelpinia turanica

Koelpinia turanica, commonly known as the Turanian orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central Asia.

It is a terrestrial herb that typically grows in rocky or semi-arid environments. The plant features a slender stem with a few leaves and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
