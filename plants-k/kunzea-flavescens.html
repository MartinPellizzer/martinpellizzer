Kunzea flavescens

Kunzea flavescens, commonly known as the yellow kunzea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, attracting various pollinators. Kunzea flavescens is typically found in heathlands and coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils.

It has aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
