Kohleria hirsuta

Kohleria hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy kohleria, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, white pubescence. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Kohleria hirsuta requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture to flourish.
