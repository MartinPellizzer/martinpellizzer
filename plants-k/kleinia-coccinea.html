Kleinia coccinea

Kleinia coccinea, commonly known as the red Kleinia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, which is characterized by its fleshy leaves and vibrant flowers. The plant features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powder, known as farina. Its flowers are typically bright red or orange, adding a striking visual element to its appearance.

Kleinia coccinea is well adapted to arid environments, making it a hardy and low-maintenance plant for gardens in dry climates.
