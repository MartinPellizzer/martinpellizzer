Kalanchoe fadeniorum

Kalanchoe fadeniorum, commonly known as the Fadeniorum Kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces clusters of vibrant, tubular flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red during the winter months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Kalanchoe fadeniorum is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its low maintenance and striking appearance.
