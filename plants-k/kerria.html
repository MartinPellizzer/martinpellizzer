Kerria

Kerria, commonly known as the yellow archangel, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It features attractive, glossy, dark green leaves and produces clusters of bright yellow flowers in spring. The plant is known for its vigorous growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. Kerria is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardiness.

It also attracts pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
