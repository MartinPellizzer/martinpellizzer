Kedrostis foetidissima

Kedrostis foetidissima, commonly known as the stinking cucumber, is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and Asia. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and yellow flowers. Its fruit, which resembles a cucumber, is typically green and has a strong, unpleasant odor.

The plant is often cultivated for its edible fruit, though it is less common than other cucurbit species.
