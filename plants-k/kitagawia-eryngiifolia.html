Kitagawia eryngiifolia

Kitagawia eryngiifolia, commonly known as the Japanese thistle or eryngium, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its spiky, blue-purple flower heads and narrow, gray-green leaves. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is often found in dry, rocky habitats. It has a compact growth habit and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
