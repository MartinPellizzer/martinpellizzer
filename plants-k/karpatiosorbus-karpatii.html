Karpatiosorbus karpatii

Karpatiosorbus karpatii, commonly known as the Carpathian rowan, is a deciduous shrub native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, thorny branches and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant produces bright red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous or rocky habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
