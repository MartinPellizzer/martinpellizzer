Kosteletzkya Semota

Kosteletzkya semota is a unique flowering plant native to the southeastern united states. it is known for its striking yellow flowers and is often found in wetland areas. this species is also referred to as the swamp sunflower due to its habitat preferences. it plays an important role in local ecosystems by providing food for pollinators. despite its beauty, it remains relatively overlooked in mainstream botanical discussions.
