Kengyilia pulcherrima

Kengyilia pulcherrima, commonly known as the Chinese aster, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, purple, and white. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and features hairy, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and ecologically valuable.
