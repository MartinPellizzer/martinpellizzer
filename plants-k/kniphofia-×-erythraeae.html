Kniphofia × erythraeae

Kniphofia × erythraeae, commonly known as the red hot poker, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asphodelaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, torch-like inflorescences that are typically bright red, orange, or yellow. The plant is native to southern Africa and is known for its upright, grass-like foliage. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Kniphofia × erythraeae is also valued for its long blooming period and drought tolerance.
