Kalanchoe dixoniana

Kalanchoe dixoniana, commonly known as the "Lion's Tail" or "Christmas Star," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, velvety blue-green leaves with white and purple markings, and it produces showy, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, and orange. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it ideal for rock gardens or containers. It blooms in winter, adding color to gardens during the colder months.

Kalanchoe dixoniana is also known for its ability to propagate through offsets and its resilience in various climates.
