Kyrstenia laeta

Kyrstenia laeta, commonly known as the happy kyrstenia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are usually narrow and lance-shaped, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Kyrstenia laeta is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
