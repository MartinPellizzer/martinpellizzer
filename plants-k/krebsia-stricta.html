Krebsia stricta

Krebsia stricta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved krebsia, is a perennial herb native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or stream banks. The plant has slender, upright stems that are often covered in fine hairs. Its leaves are elongated and narrow, with a dark green upper surface and a lighter underside.

Krebsia stricta produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or white, followed by small, capsule-like fruits containing several seeds.
