Kohleria hirsuta var. hirsuta

Kohleria hirsuta var. hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy kohleria, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Kohleria hirsuta var. hirsuta is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and indoor spaces.
