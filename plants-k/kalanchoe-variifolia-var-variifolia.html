Kalanchoe variifolia var. variifolia

Kalanchoe variifolia var. variifolia, commonly known as the variegated kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems and broad, waxy leaves that are often variegated with white, pink, or yellow markings. The plant produces clusters of vibrant orange or red flowers during the winter months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in bright, indirect sunlight.

Its unique foliage and seasonal blooms make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
