Kentia beccarii

Kentia beccarii, commonly known as the beccari palm, is a species of palm native to New Guinea.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in length. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by dark blue-black berries. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and requires well-draining soil.

Kentia beccarii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its elegant appearance and air-purifying properties.
