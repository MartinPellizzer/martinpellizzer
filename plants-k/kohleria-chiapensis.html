Kohleria chiapensis

Kohleria chiapensis, commonly known as the Chiapan mountain bellflower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

Kohleria chiapensis is valued in horticulture for its beauty and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
