Koeleria mollis var. rigidiuscula

Koeleria mollis var. rigidiuscula, commonly known as the stiffleaf false brome, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It typically grows in open, rocky habitats at high elevations, often in alpine or subalpine zones. The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its inflorescence consists of dense, spike-like clusters of small, greenish flowers that are wind-pollinated.

This variety is noted for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in harsh, cold environments.
