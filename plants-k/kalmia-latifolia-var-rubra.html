Kalmia latifolia var. rubra

Kalmia latifolia var. rubra, commonly known as the flame azalea, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features dense clusters of showy, red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has elliptical, dark green leaves that provide a striking contrast to its vibrant floral display. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and natural landscapes.
