Kniphofia natalensis var. angustifolia

Kniphofia natalensis var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved red hot poker, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, upright stems that bear dense clusters of bright red to orange-red tubular flowers, which are highly attractive to pollinators. The foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are evergreen, providing a lush backdrop to the vibrant blooms. This plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a focal point in perennial borders.

Its ornamental value and ability to attract wildlife make it a popular choice in both garden and horticultural settings.
