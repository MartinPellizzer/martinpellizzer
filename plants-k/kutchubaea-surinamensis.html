Kutchubaea surinamensis

Kutchubaea surinamensis, commonly known as the Surinam starfish flower, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange, often with a contrasting red or purple center. The plant has a woody stem and produces small, clustered leaves that are glossy and lance-shaped. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
