Kyllinga sojauxii

Kyllinga sojauxii, commonly known as the African arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features arrowhead-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, white or pink flowers. The plant is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along water bodies due to its preference for moist soil. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense mats.

Kyllinga sojauxii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in water gardens.
