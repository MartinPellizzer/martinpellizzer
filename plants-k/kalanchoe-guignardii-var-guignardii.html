Kalanchoe guignardii var. guignardii

Kalanchoe guignardii var. guignardii, commonly known as the Guignard's kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to Madagascar.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish or purplish tinge. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red during the winter months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Kalanchoe guignardii var. guignardii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and seasonal blooms.
