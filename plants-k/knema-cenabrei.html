Knema cenabrei

Knema cenabrei, commonly known as the white knema, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered in spikes, followed by spherical, woody fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The leaves and bark of Knema cenabrei have been used in traditional medicine for their purported therapeutic properties.
