Kibatalia

Kibatalia, commonly known as the African snowball, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded clusters of white flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Kibatalia is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and birds.
