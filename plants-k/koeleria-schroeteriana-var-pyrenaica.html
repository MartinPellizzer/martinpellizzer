Koeleria schroeteriana var. pyrenaica

Koeleria schroeteriana var. pyrenaica, commonly known as the Pyrenean koeleria, is a flowering plant native to the Pyrenees mountain range.

It is a low-growing perennial grass that typically reaches heights of up to 30 centimeters. The plant features slender, erect stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale blue to purple flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.
