Koanophyllon gibbosum

Koanophyllon gibbosum, commonly known as the woollyleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Koanophyllon gibbosum is adapted to arid environments and thrives in rocky or sandy soils.
