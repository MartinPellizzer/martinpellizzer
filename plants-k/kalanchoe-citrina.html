Kalanchoe citrina

Kalanchoe citrina, commonly known as the yellow kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often tinged with yellow or orange, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers in clusters during the winter months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

Kalanchoe citrina is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and ornamental value.
