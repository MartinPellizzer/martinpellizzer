Kleinia schweinfurthii

Kleinia schweinfurthii, commonly known as the Schweinfurth's ice plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that form a dense mat on the ground. The plant produces small, white flowers during the summer months, which are clustered in branched inflorescences. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

Kleinia schweinfurthii is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.
