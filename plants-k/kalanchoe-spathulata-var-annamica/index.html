Kalanchoe Spathulata Var. Annamica

Kalanchoe spathulata var. annamica is a unique and striking plant known for its vibrant, fleshy leaves and showy flowers. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in warm, sunny climates and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant. Its thick, waxy leaves store moisture, making it highly adaptable to arid environments. The plant produces clusters of colorful blooms in shades of pink, red, and orange, adding a dramatic touch to any garden or indoor space. With its compact growth habit and long-lasting flowers, Kalanchoe spathulata var. annamica is a popular choice for both novice and experienced gardeners.
