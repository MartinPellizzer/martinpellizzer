Kleinhovia hospita

Kleinhovia hospita, commonly known as the Cape snowbush, is a low-growing, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in winter, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant has a compact, mat-like growth habit with dense, dark green foliage. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Kleinhovia hospita is drought-tolerant and suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover in arid landscapes.
