Kalanchoe floribunda

Kalanchoe floribunda, commonly known as the Christmas chandelier, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems that bear clusters of bright orange or red flowers during the winter months. The plant has thick, waxy leaves that help it store water, making it drought-tolerant. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.

Kalanchoe floribunda thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.
