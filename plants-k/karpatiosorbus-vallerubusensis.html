Karpatiosorbus vallerubusensis

Karpatiosorbus vallerubusensis, commonly known as the Carpathian rowan, is a shrub native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its small, yellow-orange berries that ripen in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or forested areas, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are dark green and elliptical, with a slightly serrated edge.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
