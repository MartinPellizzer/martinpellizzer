Krynitzkia multicaulis var. abortiva

Krynitzkia multicaulis var. abortiva, commonly known as the dwarf krynitzkia, is a low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features a compact rosette of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white powder. The plant produces small, yellow flowers on short stalks during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
