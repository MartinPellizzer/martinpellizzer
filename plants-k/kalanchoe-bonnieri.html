Kalanchoe bonnieri

Kalanchoe bonnieri, commonly known as the Cape Town kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, typically during the summer months. It is a member of the Crassulaceae family and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Kalanchoe bonnieri is also noted for its ability to propagate through offsets and leaf cuttings.
