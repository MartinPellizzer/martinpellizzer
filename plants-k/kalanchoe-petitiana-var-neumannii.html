Kalanchoe petitiana var. neumannii

Kalanchoe petitiana var. neumannii, commonly known as the Neumann's Kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slight waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture in arid conditions. The plant produces vibrant, tubular flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often blooming in winter. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact growth habit.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and low-maintenance gardening.
