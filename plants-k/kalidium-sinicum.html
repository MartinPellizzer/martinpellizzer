Kalidium sinicum

Kalidium sinicum, commonly known as the Chinese grass or Chinese kudzu, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish or brownish in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in dense clumps.

Kalidium sinicum is adapted to a variety of soil types and is often found in moist, disturbed habitats.
