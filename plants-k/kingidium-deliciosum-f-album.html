Kingidium deliciosum f. album

Kingidium deliciosum f. album, commonly known as the white-flowered kingidium, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its showy, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture and is often found in moist, shaded areas of rainforests.
