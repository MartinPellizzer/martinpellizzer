Karpatiosorbus Croceocarpa

The karpatiosorbus croceocarpa is a rare and unique shrub native to the Carpathian Mountains. It belongs to the family Rosaceae and is known for its striking yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. This plant thrives in rocky and forested areas, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its scientific name reflects both its geographic origin and its distinctive yellow fruiting structures. Due to habitat loss and climate change, it is now considered a vulnerable species requiring conservation efforts.
