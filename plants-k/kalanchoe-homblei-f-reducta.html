Kalanchoe homblei f. reducta

Kalanchoe homblei f. reducta, commonly known as the compact kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tint. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red during the winter months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Due to its compact growth habit, it is popular as a decorative houseplant.
