Koeleria transsilvanica var. tenuipes

Koeleria transsilvanica var. tenuipes, commonly known as the slender leaf koeleria, is a flowering plant native to Central and Eastern Europe.

It is a member of the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky areas.

It is known for its ornamental value due to its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
