Kalanchoe stearnii

Kalanchoe stearnii, commonly known as the Stemless Kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and clusters of vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has thick, waxy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. It is a perennial species that prefers full sun and well-draining soil.

Kalanchoe stearnii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and ease of care.
