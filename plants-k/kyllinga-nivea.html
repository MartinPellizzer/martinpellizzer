Kyllinga nivea

Kyllinga nivea, commonly known as white kniphofia, is a striking ornamental plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that features tall, grass-like leaves and tall spikes of white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and blooms during the summer months. Its inflorescence consists of dense clusters of small, tubular flowers that are often arranged in a spike-like structure.

Kyllinga nivea is well-suited for gardens and landscapes due to its drought tolerance and attractive appearance.
