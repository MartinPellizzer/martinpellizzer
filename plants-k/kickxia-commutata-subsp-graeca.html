Kickxia commutata subsp. graeca

Kickxia commutata subsp.

graeca, commonly known as the Greek vetch, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments. Its leaves are trifoliate, and the fruit is a legume pod containing several seeds.
