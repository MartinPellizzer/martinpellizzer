Karpatiosorbus Eximia

Karpatiosorbus eximia is a rare and distinctive plant native to the Carpathian Mountains. It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is known for its unique floral structure and ornamental value. This species thrives in specific microhabitats, often found in moist, shaded environments. Its conservation status is of great concern due to habitat loss and climate change. Studying Karpatiosorbus eximia provides insight into the ecological diversity of the Carpathian region.
