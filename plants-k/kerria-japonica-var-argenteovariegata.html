Kerria japonica var. argenteovariegata

Kerria japonica var. argenteovariegata, commonly known as the silver-variegated kerria, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features showy, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by ornamental red fruits in the fall. The leaves are ovate with a distinctive silver-white variegation that adds visual interest throughout the growing season. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

This variety is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
