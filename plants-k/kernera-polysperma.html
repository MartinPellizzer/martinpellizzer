Kernera polysperma

Kernera polysperma, commonly known as the mountain daisy, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow daisy-like flowers and hairy, glandular leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils in open habitats. It produces numerous small seeds enclosed in a schizocarp, which splits into two parts upon maturity.

Kernera polysperma is often found in alpine and subalpine regions, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions.
