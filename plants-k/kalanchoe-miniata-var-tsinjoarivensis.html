Kalanchoe miniata var. tsinjoarivensis

Kalanchoe miniata var. tsinjoarivensis, commonly known as the Madagascar flame or red-flowered kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to Madagascar.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings. The plant produces vibrant orange to red flowers in clusters during the winter months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Due to its compact size and striking appearance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and rock gardens.
