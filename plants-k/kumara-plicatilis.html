Kumara plicatilis

Kumara plicatilis, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, pleated leaf structure. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
