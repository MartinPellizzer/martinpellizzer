Kermadecia

Kermadecia, commonly known as the New Zealand broom, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils and salty air. Kermadecia is known for its ability to thrive in harsh maritime environments.
