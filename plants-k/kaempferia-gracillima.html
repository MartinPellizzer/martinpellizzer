Kaempferia gracillima

Kaempferia gracillima, commonly known as the slender ginger orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and clusters of fragrant, pale yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows up to 30 cm in height and prefers warm, humid environments with well-draining soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.

Kaempferia gracillima is a herbaceous perennial that blooms during the warmer months.
