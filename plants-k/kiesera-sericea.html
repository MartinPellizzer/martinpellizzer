Kiesera sericea

Kiesera sericea, commonly known as silverleaf kiesera, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. It is often found growing in rocky or sandy soils in arid or semi-arid environments.

Kiesera sericea is a perennial shrub that is well adapted to drought conditions.
