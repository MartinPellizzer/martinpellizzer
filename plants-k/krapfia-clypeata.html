Krapfia clypeata

Krapfia clypeata, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its foliage is thick and velvety, often with a waxy texture that helps retain moisture.

Krapfia clypeata is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
