Kentia divaricata

Kentia divaricata, commonly known as the lace fern, is a tropical evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, arching fronds with a lacy, feathery appearance, making it a popular ornamental plant. The plant has a rhizomatous root system that helps it spread and form dense clumps. It prefers bright, indirect light and moderate humidity, thriving in warm, humid environments.

Its foliage remains lush and vibrant throughout the year, contributing to its aesthetic appeal in both indoor and outdoor settings.
