Kalanchoe humifica

Kalanchoe humifica, commonly known as the humidifier kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow during the spring and summer months. Its stems are often branched and can develop a woody texture over time.

Kalanchoe humifica is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and ability to bloom indoors.
