Kuhnistera adenopoda

Kuhnistera adenopoda, commonly known as the velvetleaf kuhnistera, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine, velvety hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
