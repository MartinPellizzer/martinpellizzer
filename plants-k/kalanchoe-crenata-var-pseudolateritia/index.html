Kalanchoe Crenata Var. Pseudolateritia

Paddle plant, scientifically known as Kalanchoe crenata var. pseudolateritia, is a striking succulent native to South Africa. Its unique, paddle-shaped leaves are thick and fleshy, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. This plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires minimal water, making it ideal for beginners. Its ability to store water in its leaves allows it to survive in arid conditions. With its vibrant foliage and low maintenance needs, the paddle plant is both decorative and resilient.
