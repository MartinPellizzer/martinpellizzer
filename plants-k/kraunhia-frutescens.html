Kraunhia frutescens

Kraunhia frutescens, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 cm. The plant features showy, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center, blooming from spring to autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
