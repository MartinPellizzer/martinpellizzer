Kniphofia conrathii

Kniphofia conrathii, commonly known as the Conrath's torch lily, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Hemerocallidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, upright stems that bear showy, red-orange inflorescences. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically evergreen, providing a lush backdrop to its dramatic flowers. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its vibrant appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
