Koeleria spicata

Koeleria spicata, commonly known as slender wheatgrass, is a flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, open habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. The plant has slender, upright stems with narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its inflorescence consists of dense, spike-like clusters of small, greenish flowers.

Koeleria spicata is valued for its hardiness and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
