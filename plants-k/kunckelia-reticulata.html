Kunckelia reticulata

Kunckelia reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined kunkelia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, finely veined leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in the spring. Kunckelia reticulata is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in arid environments.
