Kalanchoe rosei var. variifolia

Kalanchoe rosei var. variifolia, commonly known as the variegated kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are often variegated with yellow, white, and green markings. The plant produces vibrant, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow during the winter months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light.

This species is popular as a decorative plant due to its compact size and striking foliage.
