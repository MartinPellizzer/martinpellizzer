Koenigia tortuosa var. glabrifolia

Koenigia tortuosa var. glabrifolia, commonly known as the smooth-leaved stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to rocky habitats in southern Africa.

It features compact, low-growing rosettes of fleshy, gray-green leaves that are covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it an ideal choice for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular ornamental plant among succulent enthusiasts.
