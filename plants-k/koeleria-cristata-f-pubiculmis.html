Koeleria cristata f. pubiculmis

Koeleria cristata f. pubiculmis, commonly known as the hairy crested grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and distinctive hairy leaves that give it its specific epithet. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences with pale green to blue flowers, typical of the grass family. It is often found in dry, open habitats such as meadows and rocky areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
