Karpatiosorbus adamii

Karpatiosorbus adamii, commonly known as the Carpathian rowan, is a deciduous shrub native to the Carpathian Mountains in Central and Eastern Europe.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces small, white flowers in early spring, which are followed by bright red berries in the fall. Its leaves are elliptical with serrated edges and turn yellow in autumn.

Karpatiosorbus adamii is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
