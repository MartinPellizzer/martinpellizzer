Knautia × ramosissima

Knautia × ramosissima, commonly known as the hybrid knautia, is a perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and profuse display of pink to purple flower heads, which are composed of numerous small, tubular florets. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and features hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, loamy soils.

Knautia × ramosissima is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
