Knautia rigidiuscula

Knautia rigidiuscula, commonly known as the rigid knautia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features upright, hairy stems and basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine pubescence. Its flowers are typically pink to purple, arranged in dense, spherical clusters at the top of the stems.

Knautia rigidiuscula is adapted to rocky and arid environments, showcasing a drought-resistant growth habit.
