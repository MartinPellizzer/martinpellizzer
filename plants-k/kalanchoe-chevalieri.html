Kalanchoe chevalieri

Kalanchoe chevalieri, commonly known as the velvet leaf kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety coating, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically during the spring and summer months. It is a member of the Crassulaceae family and is well adapted to rocky, well-drained soils.

Kalanchoe chevalieri is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
