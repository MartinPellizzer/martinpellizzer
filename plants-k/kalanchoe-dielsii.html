Kalanchoe dielsii

Kalanchoe dielsii, commonly known as the Cape town kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, adapted for water storage. The plant produces vibrant, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in clusters during the spring and summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires ample sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Kalanchoe dielsii is also noted for its resilience to drought and ability to withstand mild frost.
