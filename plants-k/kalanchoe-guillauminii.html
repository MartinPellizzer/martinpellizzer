Kalanchoe guillauminii

Kalanchoe guillauminii, commonly known as the Guillauminii Kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to Madagascar.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, adapted for water storage. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often blooming in winter. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Kalanchoe guillauminii is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
