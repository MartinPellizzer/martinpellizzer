Kaempferia yunnanensis

Kaempferia yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan ginger orchid, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, often with a yellow throat. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation.
