Kniphofia Insignis Var. Albiflora

Kniphofia insignis var. albiflora is a striking member of the Asphodelaceae family known for its vibrant, showy flowers. This plant is native to southern Africa and thrives in well-drained soils with full sun exposure. Its white and pink blooms make it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements. The variety albiflora is particularly noted for its elegant appearance and hardy nature. It is a great addition to any landscape seeking both beauty and resilience.
