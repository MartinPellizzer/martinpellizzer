Kalanchoe × kewensis

Kalanchoe × kewensis, commonly known as the Kew's Kalanchoe, is a hybrid plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and showy, brightly colored flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of tubular or bell-shaped blooms in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It is a popular ornamental plant due to its vibrant flowers and ability to thrive in well-drained soil with minimal water.

Kalanchoe × kewensis is also known for its resilience, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
