Kalanchoe alternans

Kalanchoe alternans, commonly known as the silver arrow or arrowleaf kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to Madagascar.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green with a waxy coating, aiding in water retention. The plant produces vibrant, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow during the winter months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Kalanchoe alternans is popular as a decorative plant due to its resilience and long-lasting blooms.
