Koeleria splendens var. albanica

Koeleria splendens var. albanica, commonly known as the Albanian splendour grass, is a perennial grass species native to the Balkan region.

It features slender, wiry stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by wind, a common adaptation in grasses. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or alpine environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in marginal habitats.
