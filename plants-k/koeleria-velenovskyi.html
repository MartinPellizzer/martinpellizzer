Koeleria velenovskyi

Koeleria velenovskyi, commonly known as the Velenovsky's grass, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Koeleria velenovskyi is typically found in arid or semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils.
