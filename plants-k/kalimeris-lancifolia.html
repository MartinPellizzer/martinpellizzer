Kalimeris lancifolia

Kalimeris lancifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf fleabane, is a herbaceous perennial native to East Asia.

It features upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white to pale pink petals, blooming in late summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Kalimeris lancifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
