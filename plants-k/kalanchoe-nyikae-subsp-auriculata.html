Kalanchoe nyikae subsp. auriculata

Kalanchoe nyikae subsp.

auriculata, commonly known as the auriculate kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to East Africa. It features fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that are green with a distinctive waxy coating.

The plant produces clusters of bright orange or yellow flowers during the dry season. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
