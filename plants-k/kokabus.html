Kokabus

Kokabus, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the genus Saintpaulia and is characterized by its vibrant, velvety flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant has rosettes of thick, waxy leaves that help retain moisture in its native habitat. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Kokabus is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
