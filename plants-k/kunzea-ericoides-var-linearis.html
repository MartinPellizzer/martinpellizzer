Kunzea ericoides var. linearis

Kunzea ericoides var. linearis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved kunzea, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a dense, compact habit. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal heathlands.

Its foliage is aromatic and can be used in essential oil distillation.
