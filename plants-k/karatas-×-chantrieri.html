Karatas × chantrieri

Karatas × chantrieri, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between *K. kiefferi* and *K. edulis*.

It is a deciduous shrub native to China, characterized by its striking, papery bracts that resemble small lanterns. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by bright red, inflated seed pods. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Its unique foliage and seasonal color changes make it a visually appealing addition to landscapes.
