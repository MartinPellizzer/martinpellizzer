Kalanchoe chapototii

Kalanchoe chapototii, commonly known as the Mexican firecracker, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features fleshy, upright stems and vibrant orange to red flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has thick, waxy leaves that help it store water, making it well adapted to arid environments. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, requiring minimal water once established.

Kalanchoe chapototii is popular as a decorative plant due to its striking appearance and ease of care.
