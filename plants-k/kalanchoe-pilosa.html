Kalanchoe pilosa

Kalanchoe pilosa, commonly known as the woolly kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage that gives it a fuzzy appearance. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers during the winter months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate sunlight.

Kalanchoe pilosa is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
