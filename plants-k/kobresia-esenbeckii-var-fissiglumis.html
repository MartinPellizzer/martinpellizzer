Kobresia esenbeckii var. fissiglumis

Kobresia esenbeckii var. fissiglumis, commonly known as the fissile Kobresia, is a low-growing perennial grass found in arid and semi-arid regions.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and has narrow, grass-like leaves with a bluish-green color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It is well adapted to harsh environments, with deep root systems that help it survive in nutrient-poor soils.

This species is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion.
