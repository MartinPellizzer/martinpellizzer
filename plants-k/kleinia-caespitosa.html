Kleinia caespitosa

Kleinia caespitosa, commonly known as the coastal dwarf daisies, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and fleshy, gray-green leaves. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the summer months. It thrives in coastal areas with well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often used in xeriscaping and rock gardens.
