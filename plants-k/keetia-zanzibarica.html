Keetia zanzibarica

Keetia zanzibarica, commonly known as Zanzibar wax tree, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of East Africa.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows up to 5 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its waxy, grayish-green leaves and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in coastal forests and sandy soils.

Keetia zanzibarica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage and floral display.
