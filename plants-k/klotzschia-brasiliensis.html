Klotzschia brasiliensis

Klotzschia brasiliensis, commonly known as the Brazilian torch cactus, is a unique species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, spiny stems that can grow up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces striking yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Klotzschia brasiliensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in dry climates.
