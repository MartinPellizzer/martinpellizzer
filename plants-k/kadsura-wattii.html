Kadsura wattii

Kadsura wattii, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and striking, spadix-like flowers. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its foliage is often used in ornamental horticulture for its aesthetic appeal.

Kadsura wattii is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
