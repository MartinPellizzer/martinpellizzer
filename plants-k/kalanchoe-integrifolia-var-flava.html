Kalanchoe integrifolia var. flava

Kalanchoe integrifolia var. flava, commonly known as the yellow leaf kalanhoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically yellow to green in color. The plant produces vibrant orange or red flowers during the winter months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires ample sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in rock gardens.
