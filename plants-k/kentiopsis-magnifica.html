Kentiopsis magnifica

Kentiopsis magnifica, commonly known as the grandiose daisy, is a striking plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It features showy, daisy-like flowers with vibrant yellow centers surrounded by white petals, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with silvery-gray foliage that helps reflect sunlight in its harsh environment. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Kentiopsis magnifica is also valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
