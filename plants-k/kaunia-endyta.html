Kaunia endyta

Kaunia endyta, commonly known as the common rock orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and often clustered in loose spikes.

Kaunia endyta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
