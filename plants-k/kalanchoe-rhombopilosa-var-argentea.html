Kalanchoe rhombopilosa var. argentea

Kalanchoe rhombopilosa var. argentea, commonly known as the silver leaf kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are covered in a silvery, powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments. The leaves are typically oval or rhombus-shaped, giving the plant its specific species name. It produces vibrant orange or yellow flowers during the winter months, adding a striking contrast to its foliage.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens or containers due to its compact growth habit and low maintenance requirements.
