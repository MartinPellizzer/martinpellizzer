Koeleria albescens subsp. pontarlieri

Koeleria albescens subsp.

pontarlieri, commonly known as the Alpine Koeleria, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family. It is a small, tufted grass that typically grows in alpine and subalpine environments.

The plant is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in late summer. It is adapted to cold climates and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils.

This subspecies is of interest to botanists due to its unique ecological niche and limited distribution.
