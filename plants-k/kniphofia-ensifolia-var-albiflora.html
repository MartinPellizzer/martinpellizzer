Kniphofia ensifolia var. albiflora

Kniphofia ensifolia var. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered red hot poker, is a striking perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems that bear dense clusters of tubular, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant features broad, sword-shaped leaves that are evergreen and contribute to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its vibrant floral display makes it a popular choice for gardens seeking a dramatic, summer-blooming effect.
