Koeleria amurensis

Koeleria amurensis, commonly known as Siberian grass, is a perennial grass species native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes and riverbanks. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Koeleria amurensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in wetland restoration projects.
