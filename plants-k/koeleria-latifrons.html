Koeleria latifrons

Koeleria latifrons, commonly known as the broadleaf koeleria, is a flowering plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It is a member of the grass family (Poaceae) and typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous tiny flowers. Its leaves are broad and flat, adapted to withstand harsh cold climates.

Koeleria latifrons is often found in alpine and tundra habitats, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local ecosystems.
