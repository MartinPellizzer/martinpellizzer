Koeleria Cristata Var. Amurensis

Koeleria cristata var. amurensis is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia. It is known for its distinctive tufted appearance and delicate, grass-like foliage. This variant of Koeleria cristata is often found in rocky and well-drained soils, thriving in cooler climates. Its flowers, which bloom in early summer, add a soft pink or purple hue to its compact form. Due to its limited distribution and unique characteristics, it is a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
