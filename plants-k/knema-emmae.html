Knema emmae

Knema emmae, commonly known as the Emma's knema, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the rainforests of northern Australia, particularly in Queensland. The plant is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree, typically reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptical in shape, with a smooth surface and a glossy appearance.

The flowers are small, white or pale pink, and are clustered in dense inflorescences, contributing to its ornamental value.
