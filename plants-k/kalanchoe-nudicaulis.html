Kalanchoe nudicaulis

Kalanchoe nudicaulis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, narrow leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tinge. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red during the winter months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Kalanchoe nudicaulis is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and ornamental value.
