Kalanchoe hypseloleuce

Kalanchoe hypseloleuce, commonly known as the silver leaf kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are covered in a silvery-white coating, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and orange during the winter months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect sunlight.

Kalanchoe hypseloleuce is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and ease of care.
