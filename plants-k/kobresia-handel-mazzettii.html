Kobresia handel-mazzettii

Kobresia handel-mazzettii, commonly known as the Tibetan feather grass, is a low-growing perennial grass native to the high-altitude regions of the Tibetan Plateau.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and fine, silver-green foliage that reflects the harsh environmental conditions of its habitat. The plant thrives in cold, arid environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes. Its rhizomatous root system helps stabilize the soil, making it an important component of the local ecosystem.

Kobresia handel-mazzettii is adapted to withstand extreme temperatures and low nutrient availability, showcasing remarkable resilience in its native range.
