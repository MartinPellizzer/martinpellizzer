Kefersteinia minutiflora

Kefersteinia minutiflora, commonly known as the small-flowered kefersteinia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers during the summer months. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Kefersteinia minutiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens and coastal gardens.
