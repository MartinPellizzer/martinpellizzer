Knautia Purpurea F. Montenegrina

Knautia purpurea f. montenegrina is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Montenegro. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, known for its showy floral displays. This particular form of Knautia is distinguished by its deep purple blooms and compact growth habit. It thrives in rocky soils and sunny alpine environments. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical study and conservation efforts.
