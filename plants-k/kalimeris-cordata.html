Kalimeris cordata

Kalimeris cordata, commonly known as the heartleaf fleabane, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

Kalimeris cordata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant yellow flowers.
