Kyllinga cardosoi

Kyllinga cardosoi, commonly known as the pink kyllinga, is a herbaceous plant native to the tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It typically grows in wet or marshy environments, often in low-lying areas with abundant moisture. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery bloom. Its flowers are clustered in dense spikes and are usually pink or reddish in color.

Kyllinga cardosoi is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of wetland habitats.
