Klasea pallida

It is characterized by its pale, almost translucent petals that give it its common name. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that bloom during the cooler months, typically in spring. Klasea pallida is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments, with a deep root system to access scarce water resources.
