Kleinia japonica

Kleinia japonica, commonly known as the Japanese snowbell, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in a fine layer of white or grayish hairs.

Kleinia japonica is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
