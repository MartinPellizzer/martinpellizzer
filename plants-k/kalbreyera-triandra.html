Kalbreyera triandra

Kalbreyera triandra, commonly known as the African orchid, is a striking plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, branched stems and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically blooms in late summer, producing clusters of pale yellow to greenish-white petals. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern at the base.

Kalbreyera triandra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
