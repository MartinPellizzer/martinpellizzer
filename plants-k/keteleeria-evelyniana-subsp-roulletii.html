Keteleeria evelyniana subsp. roulletii

Keteleeria evelyniana subsp.

roulletii, commonly known as the Roullet's Chinese fir, is a member of the Cupressaceae family. It is a tall evergreen conifer with a straight trunk and a conical crown.

The leaves are scale-like, arranged in opposite pairs, and are typically dark green. The cones are woody and contain two to three seeds.

This subspecies is native to southern China and is valued for its ornamental and timber qualities.
