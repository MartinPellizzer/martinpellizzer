Kunstleria geesinkii

Kunstleria geesinkii, commonly known as the African feather grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It features slender, arching stems that bear delicate, feathery inflorescences, giving it a graceful appearance. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and prefers well-drained soils in full sun. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a silver-blue hue that adds visual interest.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
