Knema cinerea var. rubens

Knema cinerea var. rubens, commonly known as the red knema, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are typically reddish in color. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers, followed by capsules that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in humid, lowland rainforest environments and is often found in secondary growth areas.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation projects.
