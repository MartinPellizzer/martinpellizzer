Kleinia gonoclada

Kleinia gonoclada, commonly known as the coastal dandelion, is a succulent plant native to the coastal regions of South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, fleshy foliage and produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often found growing in sandy or rocky soils. It is adapted to withstand coastal conditions, including salt spray and strong winds.

Kleinia gonoclada is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
