Kengyilia kokonorica

Kengyilia kokonorica, commonly known as the Qinghai bellflower, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Qinghai-Tibet Plateau.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving at high altitudes. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in a fine layer of hair.

Kengyilia kokonorica is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological importance in its native habitat.
