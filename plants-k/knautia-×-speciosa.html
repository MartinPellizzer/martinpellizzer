Knautia × speciosa

Knautia × speciosa, commonly known as the hybrid meadow knautia, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences of pink to purple flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and features hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are often gray-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Knautia × speciosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as butterflies and bees.
