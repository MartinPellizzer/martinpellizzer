Kailarsenia suffruticosa

Kailarsenia suffruticosa, commonly known as the red-flowered kailarsenia, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, woody stems and small, elliptical leaves that are usually dark green in color. The plant produces clusters of bright red, tubular flowers that bloom during the wet season, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its botanical features make it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and landscapes.

Kailarsenia suffruticosa is also valued for its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.
