Kalanchoe Integra Var. Annamica

Kalanchoe integra var. annamica is a striking succulent native to Southeast Asia. It is known for its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. This plant thrives in bright light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens. Its unique foliage features fleshy, paddle-like leaves that add visual interest. With minimal care, it can flourish and bring a touch of color to any space.
