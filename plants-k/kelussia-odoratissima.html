Kelussia odoratissima

Kelussia odoratissima, commonly known as the African daisy or sweet milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a strong, sweet fragrance. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its visual appeal and unique scent.

Kelussia odoratissima prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.
