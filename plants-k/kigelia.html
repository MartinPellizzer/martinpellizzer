Kigelia

Kigelia, commonly known as the sausage tree, is a deciduous tree native to Africa.

It is characterized by its long, pendulous, sausage-shaped fruits that hang from the branches. The plant produces striking white or pink flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are large, palmately lobed, and have a velvety texture.

Kigelia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has cultural significance in many African communities.
