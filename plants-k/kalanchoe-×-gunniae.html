Kalanchoe × gunniae

Kalanchoe × gunniae, commonly known as the hybrid kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle to reduce water loss. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically during the winter months. It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Kalanchoe blossfeldiana and Kalanchoe thyrsiflora.

Kalanchoe × gunniae is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and long-lasting blooms.
