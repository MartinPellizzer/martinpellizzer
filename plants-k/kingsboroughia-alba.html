Kingsboroughia alba

Kingsboroughia alba, commonly known as the white kingsboroughia, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom during the late winter to early spring season. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in well-drained sandy soils and requires full sun exposure to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
