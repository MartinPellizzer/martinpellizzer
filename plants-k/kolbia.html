Kolbia

Kolbia, commonly known as the "white lily," is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Liliaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, white, cup-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and has narrow, grass-like leaves. Kolbia thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and ecologically significant in its native habitat.
