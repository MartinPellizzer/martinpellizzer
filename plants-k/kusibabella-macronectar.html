Kusibabella macronectar

Kusibabella macronectar, commonly known as the giant nectar vine, is a tropical plant characterized by its long, trailing stems and broad, heart-shaped leaves.

The plant is native to the humid regions of Southeast Asia and thrives in warm, moist environments. Its most distinctive feature is its large, showy fruit, which contains a sweet, sticky nectar.
