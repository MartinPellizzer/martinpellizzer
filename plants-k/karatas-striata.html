Karatas striata

Karatas striata, commonly known as the striped karatas, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking, pale yellow to white ray florets with dark purple markings. The plant produces small, tubular disc florets that are typically hidden within the showy outer petals. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, often with a bluish-gray hue.

Karatas striata thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
