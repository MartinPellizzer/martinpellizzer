Koeleria macrantha subsp. helvetica

Koeleria macrantha subsp.

helvetica, commonly known as Alpine feathergrass, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It is a perennial grass that typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, often on rocky soils.

The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, pale blue to purple flowers in panicles. It is well adapted to cold climates and can tolerate poor soil conditions. Koeleria macrantha subsp.

helvetica is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing mountainous terrain.
