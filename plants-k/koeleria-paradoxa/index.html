Koeleria Paradoxa

Koeleria paradoxa is a rare and intriguing grass species native to the high-altitude regions of the Tibetan Plateau. This plant thrives in extreme environmental conditions, showcasing remarkable adaptations to cold and arid climates. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike. Despite its small size, Koeleria paradoxa plays a vital role in its fragile ecosystem. Studying this plant offers valuable insights into the resilience of flora in harsh environments.
