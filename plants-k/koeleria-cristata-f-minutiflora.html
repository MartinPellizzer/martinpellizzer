Koeleria cristata f. minutiflora

Koeleria cristata f. minutiflora, commonly known as the dwarf flaxgrass, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family, Poaceae.

It is a low-growing perennial species characterized by its fine, tufted foliage and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant produces tiny, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in dry, sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as prairies and rocky slopes.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
