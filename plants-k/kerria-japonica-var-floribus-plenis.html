Kerria japonica var. floribus-plenis

Kerria japonica var. floribus-plenis, commonly known as the full-flowered Japanese kerria, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and its profuse display of yellow, single or double flowers in spring. The leaves are dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant blooms. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a versatile addition to gardens.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
