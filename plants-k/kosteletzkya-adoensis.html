Kosteletzkya adoensis

Kosteletzkya adoensis, commonly known as the African marshmallow, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

Kosteletzkya adoensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
