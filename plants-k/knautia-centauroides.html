Knautia centauroides

Knautia centauroides, commonly known as the mountain knautia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats. The plant produces clusters of pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are basal, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Knautia centauroides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
