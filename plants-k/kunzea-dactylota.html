Kunzea dactylota

Kunzea dactylota, commonly known as the narrow-leaved kunzea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes eucalyptus and tea trees. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Kunzea dactylota is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant landscaping.
