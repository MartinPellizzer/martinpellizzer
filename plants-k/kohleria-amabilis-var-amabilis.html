Kohleria amabilis var. amabilis

Kohleria amabilis var. amabilis, commonly known as the pink kohleria, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant features velvety, dark green leaves with a waxy texture and produces clusters of tubular, pink to red flowers. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Kohleria amabilis var. amabilis is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
