Kadsura omeiensis

It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is characterized by its unique, heart-shaped leaves and striking, hooded flowers. The plant is notable for its medicinal properties, traditionally used in Chinese herbal medicine. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.
