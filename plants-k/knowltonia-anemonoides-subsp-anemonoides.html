Knowltonia anemonoides subsp. anemonoides

Knowltonia anemonoides subsp.

anemonoides, commonly known as the Anemonoides Trumpet Creeper, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States. It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red.

The plant produces showy, clustered blooms that attract pollinators such as butterflies and hummingbirds. Its foliage consists of heart-shaped, palmate leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down.
