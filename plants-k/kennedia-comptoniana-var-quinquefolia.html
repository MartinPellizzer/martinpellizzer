Kennedia comptoniana var. quinquefolia

Kennedia comptoniana var. quinquefolia, commonly known as the Five-leafed Kennedia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive five-lobed leaves. The plant produces showy, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy ornamental plant.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological and horticultural purposes.
