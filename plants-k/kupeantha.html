Kupeantha

Kupeantha, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. The plant has large, palmate leaves that provide dense shade, making it a popular ornamental species. Kupeantha thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Its rapid growth rate and vibrant floral display make it a favored choice for gardens and landscapes.
