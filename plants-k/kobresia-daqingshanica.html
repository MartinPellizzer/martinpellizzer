Kobresia daqingshanica

Kobresia daqingshanica, commonly known as the Daqing Mountain kobresia, is a low-growing perennial grass found in the Qinghai-Tibet Plateau.

It belongs to the family Poaceae and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is adapted to high-altitude environments with cold temperatures and strong winds.

Kobresia daqingshanica plays a role in stabilizing soil and maintaining ecological balance in its native habitat.
