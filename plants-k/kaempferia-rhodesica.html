Kaempferia rhodesica

Kaempferia rhodesica, commonly known as the Rhodesean ginger or red ginger, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other gingers and related plants. The plant features rhizomatous growth and produces striking, colorful inflorescences with showy, spatheate flowers. Its leaves are broad, lance-shaped, and often have a waxy texture.
