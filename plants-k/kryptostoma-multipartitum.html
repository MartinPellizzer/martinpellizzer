Kryptostoma multipartitum

Kryptostoma multipartitum, commonly known as the multibract fern, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are often adorned with small, scale-like bracts. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories or rocky crevices. Its reproductive structures include small, inconspicuous flowers that develop into tiny, dry fruits.

Kryptostoma multipartitum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
