Kniphofia grantii

Kniphofia grantii, commonly known as the red hot poker, is a striking perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems that bear dense clusters of bright red, orange, or yellow tubular flowers. The plant's foliage consists of long, narrow, grass-like leaves that are evergreen in some varieties. Kniphofia grantii thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its vibrant floral display makes it a favorite among gardeners seeking bold, colorful plants.
