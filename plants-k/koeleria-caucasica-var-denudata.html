Koeleria caucasica var. denudata

Koeleria caucasica var. denudata, commonly known as the Caucasian hairgrass, is a perennial grass species native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its fine, grass-like foliage and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in temperate climates. It is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along riverbanks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden use in shady or partially sunny areas.
