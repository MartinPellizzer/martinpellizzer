Keysseria pinguiculiformis f. nana

Keysseria pinguiculiformis f. nana, commonly known as the dwarf keysseria, is a unique succulent plant characterized by its compact growth habit.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are typically rounded and arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant is native to arid or semi-arid regions, where it has adapted to store water in its thick, succulent leaves. Its flowers, when present, are usually small and may appear in shades of yellow or orange.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ease of care in suitable growing conditions.
