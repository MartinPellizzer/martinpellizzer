Kalmia latifolia f. rubra

Kalmia latifolia f. rubra, commonly known as the Flame Azalea, is a cultivar of the Kalmia latifolia species native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its striking red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant is an evergreen shrub with dense, dark green foliage that provides a lush backdrop for its vibrant blossoms. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its botanical features include showy inflorescences and a compact growth habit that adds ornamental value to landscaped areas.
