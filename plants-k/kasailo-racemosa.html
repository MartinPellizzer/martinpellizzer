Kasailo racemosa

Kasailo racemosa, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, showy flowers that bloom in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that are often velvety in texture, adapted to store water in arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Kasailo racemosa is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
