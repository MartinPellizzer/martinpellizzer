Kyllinga alba

Kyllinga alba, commonly known as white arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes and along the edges of ponds. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread easily in moist soils.

Kyllinga alba is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and adaptability.
