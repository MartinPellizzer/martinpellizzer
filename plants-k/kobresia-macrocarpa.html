Kobresia macrocarpa

Kobresia macrocarpa, commonly known as the large-fruited Kobresia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family, commonly found in alpine and arid regions.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. It thrives in harsh environments with poor soil conditions and low precipitation.

Kobresia macrocarpa plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various small organisms.
