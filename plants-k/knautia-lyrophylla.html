Knautia lyrophylla

Knautia lyrophylla, commonly known as the alpine knautia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, adapting to the harsh conditions of its environment.

Knautia lyrophylla is often used in rock gardens and alpine arrangements due to its ornamental value and hardiness.
