Kentia storckii

Kentia storckii, commonly known as the Kentia palm, is a tropical evergreen plant native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that give it a graceful appearance. The plant is well adapted to humid, warm climates and thrives in well-draining soil. It is often cultivated as an indoor plant due to its tolerance of low light and minimal care requirements.

Kentia storckii is also valued for its air-purifying properties and ability to enhance indoor environments.
