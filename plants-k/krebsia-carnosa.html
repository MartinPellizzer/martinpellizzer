Krebsia carnosa

Krebsia carnosa, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are velvety to the touch, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered white or yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Krebsia carnosa is often found in moist, shaded environments such as swamps and along riverbanks.

It is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage.
