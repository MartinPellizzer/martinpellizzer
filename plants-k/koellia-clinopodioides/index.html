Koellia Clinopodioides

Koellia mint, scientifically known as Koellia clinopodioides, is a rare and lesser-known member of the Lamiaceae family. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, this plant thrives in rocky and arid environments. Its aromatic leaves and compact growth make it a unique addition to botanical gardens and herb collections. Despite its medicinal potential, it remains underappreciated and poorly studied. This article explores the characteristics, habitat, and significance of Koellia mint in the natural world.
