Kaliphora madagascariensis

Kaliphora madagascariensis, commonly known as the Madagascar orchid, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are usually elongated and grass-like, while its flowers come in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and pink.

Kaliphora madagascariensis plays an important role in the local ecosystem and is a symbol of Madagascar's unique biodiversity.
