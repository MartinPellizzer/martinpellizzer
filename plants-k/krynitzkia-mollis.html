Krynitzkia mollis

Krynitzkia mollis, commonly known as the soft krynitzkia, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils. The plant features slender, upright stems covered in soft, hairy foliage. Its flowers are small, clustered in loose inflorescences, and are usually pale green to white in color.

Krynitzkia mollis is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
