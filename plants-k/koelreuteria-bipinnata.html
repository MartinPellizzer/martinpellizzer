Koelreuteria bipinnata

Koelreuteria bipinnata, commonly known as the golden rain tree, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its bipinnate leaves that turn bright yellow in the fall, giving it its common name. The tree produces small, fragrant yellow flowers in the spring, followed by woody, bead-like fruits. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Its rapid growth and showy foliage make it a valuable addition to urban landscapes.
