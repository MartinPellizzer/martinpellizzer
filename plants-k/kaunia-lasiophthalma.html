Kaunia lasiophthalma

Kaunia lasiophthalma, commonly known as the woolly sunflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, fuzzy foliage and bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Kaunia lasiophthalma is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
