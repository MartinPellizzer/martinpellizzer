Koeleria Macrantha F. Lanata

Koeleria macrantha f. lanata is a unique plant variety known for its distinctive hairy leaves and compact growth habit. It belongs to the grass family and is native to specific regions in North America. This form is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in diverse soil conditions. Its fluffy appearance makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking texture and visual interest. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to watering and sunlight to flourish.
