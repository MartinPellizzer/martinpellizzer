Kraussia speciosa

Kraussia speciosa, commonly known as the yellowwood tree, is a deciduous tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and striking yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It produces small, black, spherical fruits that contain one or two seeds.

Kraussia speciosa is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
