Kaempferia minuta

Kaempferia minuta, commonly known as the dwarf flame orchid, is a small, herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, brightly colored flowers. The plant typically produces narrow, grass-like leaves and blooms with spathaceous flowers that are often red or orange in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Kaempferia minuta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in horticultural settings.
