Kennedia coccinea var. mollyae

Kennedia coccinea var. mollyae, commonly known as the Molly's scarlet glory, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is a shrub characterized by its vibrant red flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant features narrow, dark green leaves and produces striking, tubular flowers that attract nectar-feeding birds and insects. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and adaptability to various climate conditions.
