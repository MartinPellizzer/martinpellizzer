Knautia dipsacifolia f. amoena

Knautia dipsacifolia f. amoena, commonly known as the showy knautia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caprifoliaceae family.

It is characterized by its clusters of pink to purple, showy flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant is native to southern Europe and is often found in meadows and grassy slopes. It has a bushy growth habit with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly used in garden landscapes.
