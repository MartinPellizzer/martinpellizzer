Knema kunstleri subsp. macrophylla

Knema kunstleri subsp.

macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf knema, is a tropical tree native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its broad, glossy leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters.

The plant produces woody, elongated fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in primary lowland rainforests.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and potential medicinal research.
