Kalanchoe × flaurantia

Kalanchoe × flaurantia, commonly known as the flaming Katy, is a hybrid succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating called farina. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically blooming in winter. It is a popular ornamental plant due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in a variety of light conditions.

Kalanchoe × flaurantia is also known for its ability to propagate through leaf cuttings, making it easy to grow from home.
