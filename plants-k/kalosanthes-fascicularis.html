Kalosanthes fascicularis

Kalosanthes fascicularis, commonly known as the clustered orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Kalosanthes fascicularis is valued in horticulture for its unique floral display and ability to thrive in controlled indoor conditions.
