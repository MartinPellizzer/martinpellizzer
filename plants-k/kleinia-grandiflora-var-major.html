Kleinia grandiflora var. major

Kleinia grandiflora var. major, commonly known as the Cape Flats dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and woolly, silver-green foliage. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to arid conditions.

Its medicinal properties have been recognized in traditional African medicine.
