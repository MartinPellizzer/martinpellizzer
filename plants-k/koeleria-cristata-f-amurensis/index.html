Koeleria Cristata F. Amurensis

Koeleria cristata f. amurensis is a rare and fascinating plant belonging to the grass family. It is a flowering plant known for its distinctive appearance and ecological significance. This form of Koeleria is native to specific regions in eastern Asia, particularly the Amur River basin. Its unique features make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Despite its beauty, it remains poorly studied and threatened by habitat loss.
