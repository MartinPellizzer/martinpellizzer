Kaempferia simaoensis

Kaempferia simaoensis, commonly known as the Simao ginger orchid, is a rare and endemic orchid species native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of small, colorful blooms in shades of pink and white, which appear during the spring and summer seasons. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Kaempferia simaoensis is considered a vulnerable species.
