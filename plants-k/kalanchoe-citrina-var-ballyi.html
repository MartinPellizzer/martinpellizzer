Kalanchoe citrina var. ballyi

Kalanchoe citrina var. ballyi, commonly known as the Bally's variegated kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves with a distinctive variegation of green, yellow, and white. The plant produces vibrant orange to red flowers during the winter months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Kalanchoe citrina var. ballyi is drought-tolerant and suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
