Knoxia veronicoides

Knoxia veronicoides, commonly known as the silverleaf knoxia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features basal rosettes of silver-gray, velvety leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, pale blue or lavender flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
