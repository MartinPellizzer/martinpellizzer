Klonion

Klonion, commonly known as the "false dandelion," is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, spoon-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Klonion is often found in disturbed soils and open areas, such as roadsides and fields.

It has a fibrous root system and is known for its ability to spread vegetatively through runners.
