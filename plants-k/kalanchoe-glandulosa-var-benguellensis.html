Kalanchoe glandulosa var. benguellensis

Kalanchoe glandulosa var. benguellensis, commonly known as the Benguella Kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are sometimes used in traditional medicine for their potential healing properties.

It is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low water requirements.
