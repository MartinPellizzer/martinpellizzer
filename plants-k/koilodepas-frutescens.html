Koilodepas frutescens

Koilodepas frutescens, commonly known as the African velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs, giving it a velvety texture. Its small, yellow flowers are clustered in terminal inflorescences, and it produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds.

Koilodepas frutescens is often found in disturbed habitats and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural areas.
