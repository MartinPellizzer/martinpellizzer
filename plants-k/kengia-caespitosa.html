Kengia caespitosa

Kengia caespitosa, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb that grows in dense clumps, with a compact, bushy appearance. The plant produces showy, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom throughout the warmer months. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

Kengia caespitosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
