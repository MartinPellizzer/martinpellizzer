Kuepferia

Kuepferia, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to Africa.

These plants are characterized by their rosette-shaped foliage and vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in a variety of colors. Kuepferia species typically have thick, fleshy leaves that help them retain moisture in arid environments. They are popular houseplants due to their ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
