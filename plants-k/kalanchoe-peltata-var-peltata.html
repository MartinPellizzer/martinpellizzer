Kalanchoe peltata var. peltata

Kalanchoe peltata var. peltata, commonly known as the fleshy leaf kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the edges. The plant produces clusters of bright orange or yellow flowers during the winter months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
