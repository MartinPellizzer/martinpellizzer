Kniphofia uvaria var. nobilis

Kniphofia uvaria var. nobilis, commonly known as the noble torch lily, is a striking perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, sword-shaped leaves and produces showy, red and orange inflorescences that resemble torches. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds.

The variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and hardy nature, suitable for both garden cultivation and horticultural collections.
