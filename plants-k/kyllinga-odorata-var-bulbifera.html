Kyllinga odorata var. bulbifera

Kyllinga odorata var. bulbifera, commonly known as the bulbiferous spurge or swamp spurge, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

The plant produces small, triangular leaves and has a rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread vegetatively. This adaptation aids in its propagation and survival in wetland environments.
