Kali tragus

Kali tragus, commonly known as goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, spiny bracts that enclose its yellow-orange flowers, giving it a distinctive appearance. Kali tragus is often found in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Its leaves are narrow and hairy, and it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
