Kipandiorchis jiewhoei

Kipandiorchis jiewhoei, commonly known as the Jiewhoe's orchid, is a rare and endemic orchid species native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on the trunks of trees or in rocky crevices. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, adapted to conserve moisture in its natural habitat.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
