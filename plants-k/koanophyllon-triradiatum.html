Koanophyllon triradiatum

Koanophyllon triradiatum, commonly known as the three-rayed blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Koanophyllon triradiatum is noted for its ecological importance as a food source for various pollinators and as a component of local plant communities.
