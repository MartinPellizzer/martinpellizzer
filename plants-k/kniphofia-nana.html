Kniphofia nana

Kniphofia nana, commonly known as the dwarf red hot poker, is a perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, fiery red to orange-red flower spikes that bloom in summer. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are dark green and often covered in a fine white powder. Kniphofia nana thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and ability to attract pollinators.
