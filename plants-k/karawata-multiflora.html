Karawata multiflora

Karawata multiflora, commonly known as the multi-flowered karawata, is a shrub or small tree native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and a glossy appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.

Karawata multiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional landscaping.
