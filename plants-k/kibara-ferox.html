Kibara ferox

Kibara ferox, commonly known as the African giant hyena plant, is a striking member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its large, dark green, velvety leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant produces clusters of bright red or orange flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and birds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Kibara ferox is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can grow quite large, reaching heights of up to 6 feet.
