Knautia × alleizettei

Knautia × alleizettei, commonly known as the hybrid knautia, is a perennial flowering plant that belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Knautia arvensis and Knautia montana, resulting in a plant with distinctive, fluffy, pink to purple flower heads. The species is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, composite-like inflorescences that bloom in late summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often found in meadows and rocky soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant flowers and ease of care.
