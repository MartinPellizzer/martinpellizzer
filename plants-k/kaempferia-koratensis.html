Kaempferia koratensis

Kaempferia koratensis, commonly known as the Korat orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial herb with fleshy roots and broad, lance-shaped leaves. Its inflorescence bears several showy, often pink or red flowers that bloom during the rainy season.

Kaempferia koratensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
