Kielmeyera rubriflora var. major

Kielmeyera rubriflora var. major, commonly known as the red-flowered kielmeyera, is a species native to Brazil.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree characterized by its dense, glossy green foliage. The plant produces striking red flowers that are clustered in panicles, typically blooming in the spring. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly serrated margin.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
