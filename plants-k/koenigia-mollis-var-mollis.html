Koenigia mollis var. mollis

Koenigia mollis var. mollis, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that appear in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental foliage make it both a hardy and aesthetically pleasing addition to gardens.
