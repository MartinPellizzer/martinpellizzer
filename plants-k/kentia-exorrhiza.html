Kentia exorrhiza

Kentia exorrhiza, commonly known as the slender kentia palm, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and feathery, compound leaves that give it a graceful appearance. The plant has a fibrous root system that helps it thrive in moist, well-drained soils. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.

Kentia exorrhiza is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
