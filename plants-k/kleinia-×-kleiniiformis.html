Kleinia × kleiniiformis

Kleinia × kleiniiformis, commonly known as the hybrid ice plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, characterized by its fleshy leaves and unique floral structures. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically thick and succulent, adapted to store water in arid environments.

This hybrid is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils with minimal water.
