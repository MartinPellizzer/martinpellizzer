Koenigia filicaulis var. caespitosa

Koenigia filicaulis var. caespitosa, commonly known as the tufted stonecrop, is a low-growing succulent native to the Mediterranean region.

It features fleshy, linear leaves that are often bluish-green in color, arranged in dense clusters. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
