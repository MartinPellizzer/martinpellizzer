Karimbolea verrucosa

Karimbolea verrucosa, commonly known as the warty leaf orchid, is a rare and striking plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, warty-textured leaves and clusters of fragrant, orchid-like flowers. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing in the undergrowth of tropical forests. Its botanical features include a short, rhizomatous stem and lance-shaped leaves with a velvety surface.
