Kniphofia modesta

Kniphofia modesta, commonly known as the red hot poker, is a striking perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, upright stems that bear dense, cylindrical clusters of bright red to orange tubular flowers. The foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are evergreen in mild climates. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and birds.
