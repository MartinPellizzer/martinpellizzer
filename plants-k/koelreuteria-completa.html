Koelreuteria completa

Koelreuteria completa, commonly known as the Chinese lantern tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its distinctive, papery bracts that turn red in autumn, giving the plant its common name. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in early summer, followed by spherical, woody fruits that split open to reveal seeds. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and ovate, with a smooth margin.

Koelreuteria completa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental display and adaptability to various soil types.
