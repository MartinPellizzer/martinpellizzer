Kalanchoe oblongifolia

Kalanchoe oblongifolia, commonly known as the African violet, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of showy, tubular flowers in shades of pink, purple, and white. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Kalanchoe oblongifolia is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal.
