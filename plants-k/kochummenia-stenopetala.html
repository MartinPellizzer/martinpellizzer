Kochummenia stenopetala

Kochummenia stenopetala, commonly known as the narrow-petaled orchid, is a tropical plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

Kochummenia stenopetala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique floral display.
