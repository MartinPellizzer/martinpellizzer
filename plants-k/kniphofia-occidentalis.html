Kniphofia occidentalis

Kniphofia occidentalis, commonly known as the red hot poker, is a striking perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, sword-shaped leaves and its showy, torch-like inflorescences that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. It produces tuberous roots that store nutrients, supporting its rapid growth and flowering.

Kniphofia occidentalis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
