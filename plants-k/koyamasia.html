Koyamasia

Koyamasia, commonly known as the mountain orchid, is a unique flowering plant native to the high altitudes of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant blooms. The plant typically grows in rocky or forested areas, preferring cool and moist environments. Its leaves are narrow and strap-like, while its flowers come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and purple.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, koyamasia is considered a protected species in many regions.
