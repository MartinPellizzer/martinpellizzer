Kalanchoe boisii

Kalanchoe boisii, commonly known as the silver arrow, is a succulent plant native to Madagascar.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are covered in a silvery-gray pubescence, which helps reduce water loss in arid conditions. The plant produces vibrant orange to red flowers in clusters during the winter months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Kalanchoe boisii is also noted for its ability to propagate through offsets and leaf cuttings.
