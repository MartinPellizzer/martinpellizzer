Kalanchoe schimperiana

Kalanchoe schimperiana, commonly known as the Schimper's kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often blooming in late winter or early spring. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ability to propagate through offsets and its low maintenance requirements further enhance its appeal as a decorative plant.
