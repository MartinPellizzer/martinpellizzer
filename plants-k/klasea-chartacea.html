Klasea chartacea

Klasea chartacea, commonly known as the paperleaf klasea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the temperate regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, chartaceously textured leaves that are typically heart-shaped and have a silvery-green hue. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters during the spring and early summer. Klasea chartacea thrives in well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

It is often used in ornamental gardening for its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements.
