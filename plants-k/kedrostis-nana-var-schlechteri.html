Kedrostis nana var. schlechteri

Kedrostis nana var. schlechteri, commonly known as the dwarf African spider plant, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features rosettes of narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bright green with a slight bluish tinge. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in summer, which are borne on slender, erect spikes. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its compact size and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
