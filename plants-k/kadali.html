Kadali

Kadali, commonly known as *Gynura aurata*, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its vibrant, dandelion-like flowers. The plant features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly serrated edge, and it produces yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. Kadali is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage and long-lasting blooms.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.
