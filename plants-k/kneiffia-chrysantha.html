Kneiffia chrysantha

Kneiffia chrysantha, commonly known as the golden sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces vibrant yellow flowers that bloom throughout the warmer months. The plant thrives in well-drained sandy soils and full sun exposure, making it well-suited for arid or semi-arid environments. Its foliage consists of small, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, aiding in water conservation.

Kneiffia chrysantha is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its bright, showy blooms.
