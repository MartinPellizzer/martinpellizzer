Koeleria Subalpestris

Koeleria subalpestris is a hardy grass species commonly found in alpine and subalpine regions. It thrives in rocky and sandy soils, often growing in open landscapes where competition from other plants is minimal. This plant is known for its resilience to cold temperatures and strong winds, making it a staple of high-altitude ecosystems. Its delicate blue-green foliage and small, spike-like flowers add a subtle beauty to its natural habitat. Koeleria subalpestris plays an important role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife in mountainous areas.
