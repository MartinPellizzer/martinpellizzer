Kalanchoe macrantha

Kalanchoe macrantha, commonly known as the Christmas chrysanthemum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture, adapted to store water. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which bloom during the winter months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Kalanchoe macrantha is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its vibrant blooms and low maintenance requirements.
