Koeleria fominii

Koeleria fominii, commonly known as the Fomin's needlegrass, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is a member of the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Koeleria fominii is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in harsh climates.
