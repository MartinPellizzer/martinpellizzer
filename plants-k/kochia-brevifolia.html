Kochia brevifolia

Kochia brevifolia, commonly known as the shortleaf kochia, is a flowering plant in the amaranth family.

It is native to North America and is characterized by its upright, branching structure and small, spiny leaves. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to red, depending on the variety. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in arid environments.

Kochia brevifolia is also known for its ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions.
