Kleinia selloi

Kleinia selloi, commonly known as the Sello's ice plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, characterized by its fleshy leaves and distinctive flower structures. The plant features a low-growing, mat-forming habit with small, blue-green leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, appearing in clusters during the summer months.

Kleinia selloi is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves to survive dry conditions.
