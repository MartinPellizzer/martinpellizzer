Kaoue germainii

Kaoue germainii, commonly known as the African wild coffee.

It is a small evergreen tree native to the tropical rainforests of Central and West Africa. The plant has simple, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. Its flowers are small and white, clustered in inflorescences, and it produces small, round berries that turn from green to red when mature.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local communities.
