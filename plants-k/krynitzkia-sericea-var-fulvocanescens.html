Krynitzkia sericea var. fulvocanescens

Krynitzkia sericea var. fulvocanescens, commonly known as the silverleaf krynitzkia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
