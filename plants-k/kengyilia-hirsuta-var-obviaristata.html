Kengyilia hirsuta var. obviaristata

Kengyilia hirsuta var. obviaristata, commonly known as the hairy kengyilia, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets. The plant is distinguished by its hairy, glandular leaves and bright yellow to orange flower heads. It typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in cool and moist conditions.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies in mountainous regions.
