Keraselma Virgatum

Keraselma virgatum is a rare and delicate flowering plant known for its striking violet petals and unique growth habits. Native to specific regions with high humidity and cool temperatures, it thrives in shaded, moist environments. Its intricate flower structure and short blooming season make it a sought-after specimen among botanists and plant enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, Keraselma virgatum remains under-researched and endangered due to habitat loss.
