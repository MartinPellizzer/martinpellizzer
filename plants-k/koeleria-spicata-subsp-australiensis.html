Koeleria spicata subsp. australiensis

Koeleria spicata subsp.

australiensis, commonly known as the Australian spikegrass, is a flowering plant native to Australia. It is a perennial grass species characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spike-like inflorescences.

The plant produces tiny, wind-pollinated flowers that typically bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open habitats.

Its botanical features make it an important component of native ecosystems in southern Australia.
