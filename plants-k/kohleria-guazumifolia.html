Kohleria guazumifolia

Kohleria guazumifolia, commonly known as the Guazumifolia kohleria, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

Kohleria guazumifolia is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.
