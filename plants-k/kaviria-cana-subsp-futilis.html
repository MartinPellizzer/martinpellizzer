Kaviria cana subsp. futilis

Kaviria cana subsp.

futilis, commonly known as the slender kaviria, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, daisy-like yellow flowers.

Native to southern Africa, this species thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant is often found in open grasslands and rocky outcrops, where it adapts well to poor soil conditions.

Its ecological role includes providing nectar for pollinators and serving as a food source for certain herbivores.
