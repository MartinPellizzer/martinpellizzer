Kalanchoe poincarei var. suarezensis

Kalanchoe poincarei var. suarezensis, commonly known as the Suarezensis Kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and fleshy, dark green leaves with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces vibrant orange to red flowers in clusters during the winter months, typically from December to March. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and rock gardens.
