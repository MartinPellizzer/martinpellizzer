Knema laurina var. malayana

Knema laurina var. malayana, commonly known as the Malayan knema, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, oval-shaped leaves and fragrant, white flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically orange-red when mature. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its ornamental value and ecological importance make it a notable species in botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
