Knema stylosa

Knema stylosa, commonly known as the red-flowered knema, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, glossy leaves and clusters of bright red flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its botanical features make it an important component of tropical ecosystems.
