Kungia schoenlandii var. stenostachya

Kungia schoenlandii var. stenostachya, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed kungia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in the spring. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil conditions.
