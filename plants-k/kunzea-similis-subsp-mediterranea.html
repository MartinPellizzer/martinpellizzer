Kunzea similis subsp. mediterranea

Kunzea similis subsp.

mediterranea, commonly known as the Mediterranean kunzea, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It typically grows in dry, sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

The plant has small, narrow leaves and produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. It is valued for its aromatic properties and is often used in traditional herbal medicine.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to coastal and semi-arid environments.
