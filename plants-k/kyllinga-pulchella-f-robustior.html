Kyllinga pulchella f. robustior

Kyllinga pulchella f. robustior, commonly known as the robust dwarf sedge, is a compact, herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikes of white or pale pink flowers. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along the edges of water bodies. Its foliage is typically dark green, and it has a short, rhizomatous growth habit.

Kyllinga pulchella f. robustior is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in aquatic environments.
