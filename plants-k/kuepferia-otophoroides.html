Kuepferia otophoroides

Kuepferia otophoroides, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is a perennial herb that thrives in full sun and well-drained conditions.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
