Koenigia × fennica

Koenigia × fennica, commonly known as the hybrid snowflake, is a perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its striking white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils and full sun. Its foliage is usually covered in a fine layer of white, fuzzy hairs, which help to reflect sunlight and reduce water loss.

Koenigia × fennica is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
