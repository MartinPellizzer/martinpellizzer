Keithia

These plants are characterized by their dense, overlapping leaves that form a rosette, often resembling a mantle, hence their common name. Keithia species are typically found in temperate regions, particularly in mountainous areas with acidic soils. The genus includes several species, such as *Keithia marmorata*, which is native to New Zealand and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
