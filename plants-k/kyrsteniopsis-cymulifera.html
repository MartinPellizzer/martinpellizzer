Kyrsteniopsis cymulifera

Kyrsteniopsis cymulifera, commonly known as the spiral-leaved fern, is a unique species native to the humid forests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its intricate, spiraling arrangement of fronds, which gives it a distinctive and ornamental appearance. The plant typically grows as a creeping perennial, with slender rhizomes that spread across the forest floor. Its leaves are pinnately divided, with a soft, velvety texture and a deep green color.

Kyrsteniopsis cymulifera thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in the understorey of tropical rainforests.
