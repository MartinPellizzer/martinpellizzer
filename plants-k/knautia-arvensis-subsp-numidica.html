Knautia arvensis subsp. numidica

Knautia arvensis subsp.

numidica, commonly known as the Numidian knautia, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and northern Africa. It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.

The plant features upright stems covered in soft, hairy foliage and produces clusters of pink to purple, fluffy flowers. Its flowers are typically arranged in dense, spherical inflorescences, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The species is valued in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
