Kalanchoe crenata var. coccinea

Kalanchoe crenata var. coccinea, commonly known as the red-edge Kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems and rosettes of fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are green with red or pink edges. The plant produces vibrant orange to red flowers in clusters during the winter months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens, containers, and as a houseplant.
