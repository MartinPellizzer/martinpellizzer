Kyllinga merxmuelleri

Kyllinga merxmuelleri, commonly known as the silverleaf kyllinga, is a herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery-white coating, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas or near freshwater sources.

Kyllinga merxmuelleri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
