Knema attenuata

Knema attenuata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved knema, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves with a smooth texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that release tiny seeds when mature.

Knema attenuata is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental and ecological significance.
