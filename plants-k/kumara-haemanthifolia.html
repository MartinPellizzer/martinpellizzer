Kumara haemanthifolia

Kumara haemanthifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf kumara, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Araceae and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces striking yellow or green spadices, which are the modified leaves that enclose the inflorescence. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded, humid environments.

Kumara haemanthifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
