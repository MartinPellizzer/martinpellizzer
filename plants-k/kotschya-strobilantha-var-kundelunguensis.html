Kotschya strobilantha var. kundelunguensis

Kotschya strobilantha var. kundelunguensis, commonly known as the Congo starleaf, is a unique tropical plant native to the Democratic Republic of the Congo.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored, with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by distinctive, cone-like fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
