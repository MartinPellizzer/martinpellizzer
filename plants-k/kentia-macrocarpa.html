Kentia macrocarpa

Kentia macrocarpa, commonly known as the Kentia palm, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant produces small, clustered flowers followed by oval-shaped fruits containing seeds. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance of low light and moderate watering.

Its slow growth rate makes it a popular choice for ornamental purposes.
