Karpatiosorbus hoppeana

Karpatiosorbus hoppeana, commonly known as the Carpathian rowan, is a species of shrub native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, thorny growth habit. The plant produces small, white flowers in spring, followed by red berries in the autumn. Its leaves are elliptical and have a serrated edge, contributing to its ornamental value.

Karpatiosorbus hoppeana is adapted to temperate climates and thrives in well-drained soils.
