Keysseria maviensis f. emarginata

Keysseria maviensis f. emarginata, commonly known as the Emarginate Keysseria, is a unique botanical variant characterized by its distinctive leaf morphology.

This plant exhibits a compact growth habit with lanceolate leaves that have a notable emarginate base, giving it a unique appearance. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is typically found in arid or semi-arid environments. The foliage is often covered in fine pubescence, which aids in reducing water loss.

Its limited distribution makes it a rare and ecologically significant species within its native range.
