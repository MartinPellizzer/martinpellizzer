Kallstroemia Perennans

Kallstroemia perennans is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the eastern United States. Known for its vibrant pink to red blooms, it adds a splash of color to gardens and natural landscapes. This perennial species thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice among horticulturists. Its unique appearance and ease of care have made it a favorite in modern landscaping. With its delicate petals and long blooming season, Kallstroemia perennans is both ornamental and ecologically valuable.
