Kalimeris flexuosa

Kalimeris flexuosa, commonly known as the flexible aster, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and streambanks. It produces showy yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Kalimeris flexuosa is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
