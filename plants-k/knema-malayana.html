Knema malayana

Knema malayana, commonly known as the Malayan knema, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, followed by yellowish fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland and hill forest ecosystems.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
