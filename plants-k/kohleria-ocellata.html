Kohleria ocellata

Kohleria ocellata, commonly known as the spotted kohleria, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Kohleria ocellata is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.
