Krynitzkia glomerata var. acuta

Krynitzkia glomerata var. acuta, commonly known as the sharpleaf krynitzkia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, acuminate leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in suitable climates.
