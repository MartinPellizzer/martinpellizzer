Kalanchoe homblei

Kalanchoe homblei, commonly known as the Hombli's Kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating to store water. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red during the winter months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light.

Kalanchoe homblei is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and vibrant floral display.
