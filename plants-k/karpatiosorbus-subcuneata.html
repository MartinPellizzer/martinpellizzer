Karpatiosorbus subcuneata

Karpatiosorbus subcuneata, commonly known as the Carpathian rowan, is a species of flowering plant native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces red berries that are edible for humans and wildlife. It typically grows in woodland areas and has a shrub-like habit with thorny stems.
