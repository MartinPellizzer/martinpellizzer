Knema pulchra

Knema pulchra, commonly known as the beautiful knema, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, white to pinkish flowers that are clustered in spikes, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Knema pulchra is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance.

Its wood is also used in traditional crafts and construction.
