Kalanchoe multiflora

Kalanchoe multiflora, commonly known as the mother of thousands, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers during the winter months. It is a popular ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and low maintenance requirements.

Kalanchoe multiflora also has the ability to propagate through its offsets, making it easy to propagate at home.
