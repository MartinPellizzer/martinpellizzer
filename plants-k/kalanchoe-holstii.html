Kalanchoe holstii

Kalanchoe holstii, commonly known as the "Snowy Kalanchoe," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the winter months. The leaves are thick and covered in a fine, powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and ornamental value.
