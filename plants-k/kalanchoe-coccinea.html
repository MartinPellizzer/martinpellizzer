Kalanchoe coccinea

Kalanchoe coccinea, commonly known as the red Kalanchoe, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a succulent shrub that features fleshy, thick leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that range from red to orange in color. The plant produces its flowers in clusters during the winter months, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight to flourish.

Kalanchoe coccinea is also known for its ability to survive in arid conditions, showcasing its adaptability to dry environments.
