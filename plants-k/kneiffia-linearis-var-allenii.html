Kneiffia linearis var. allenii

Kneiffia linearis var. allenii, commonly known as the Allen's narrowleaf kniphofia, is a striking perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, grass-like foliage and showy, red-orange inflorescences that bloom in late summer. The plant prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes. Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
