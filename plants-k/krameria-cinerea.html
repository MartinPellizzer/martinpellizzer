Krameria cinerea

Krameria cinerea, commonly known as silverleaf krameria, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils and open woodlands.

Krameria cinerea is a perennial shrub that is adapted to withstand drought and high temperatures.
