Kaempferia ceciliae

Kaempferia ceciliae, commonly known as the Cecilia's flame orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers. The plant typically grows as a rhizomatous herb, with fleshy, sword-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually red or orange with yellow and purple markings, and they bloom in clusters.

It prefers warm, humid conditions and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
