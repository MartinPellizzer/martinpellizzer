Kentia procera

Kentia procera, commonly known as the Kentia palm, is a striking evergreen plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and feathery, arching fronds that give it a graceful appearance. The plant has a slow growth rate, making it ideal for indoor cultivation. Its leaves are typically a deep green, and it produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by dark blue berries.

Kentia procera is well-suited to low-light conditions and requires moderate watering, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced plant enthusiasts.
