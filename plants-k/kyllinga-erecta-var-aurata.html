Kyllinga erecta var. aurata

Kyllinga erecta var. aurata, commonly known as the golden kyllinga, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features dense, upright clusters of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often tinged with red or purple hues. The plant produces showy, spike-like inflorescences with bright yellow bracts that give it its common name. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for garden borders and container planting.
