Kalanchoe longiflora

Kalanchoe longiflora, commonly known as the long-flowered Kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems and produces clusters of vibrant, tubular flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant is characterized by its ability to store water in its thick leaves, which helps it survive in arid conditions. It blooms in winter, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

The species is also noted for its ease of propagation through stem cuttings.
