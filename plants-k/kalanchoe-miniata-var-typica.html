Kalanchoe miniata var. typica

Kalanchoe miniata var. typica, commonly known as the miniature kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red during the winter months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in bright, indirect light with well-draining soil.

Kalanchoe miniata var. typica is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and ornamental value.
