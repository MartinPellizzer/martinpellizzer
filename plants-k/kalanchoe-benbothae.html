Kalanchoe benbothae

Kalanchoe benbothae, commonly known as the Christmas Star, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings. The plant produces striking, star-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow during the winter months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Kalanchoe benbothae is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
