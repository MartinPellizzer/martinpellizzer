Kalanchoe × forbesiae

Kalanchoe × forbesiae, commonly known as the "Flaming Katy," is a hybrid succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, especially at the edges. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of orange, yellow, and red during the winter months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright sunlight.

Kalanchoe × forbesiae is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
