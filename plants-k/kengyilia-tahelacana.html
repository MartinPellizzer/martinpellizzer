Kengyilia tahelacana

Kengyilia tahelacana, commonly known as the Tahelacana groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The flowers bloom in spring, producing a showy yellow color that attracts pollinators like bees and butterflies.
