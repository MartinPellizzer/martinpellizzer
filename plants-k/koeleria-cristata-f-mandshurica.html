Koeleria cristata f. mandshurica

Koeleria cristata f. mandshurica, commonly known as the Mandshurian tufted grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing tiny, blue to purple flowers. It is native to regions of eastern Asia, including parts of Russia and China.

This variety is often found in alpine or arctic environments, thriving in cool, moist conditions.
