Karaka colorata

Karaka colorata, commonly known as the rainbow karaka, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It features vibrant, colorful bracts that range from red to orange and yellow, giving it its common name. The plant belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. Its foliage consists of glossy, dark green leaves with a slightly waxy texture.

Karaka colorata thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.
