Krainzia densispina

Krainzia densispina, commonly known as the dense-spined krainzia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form. The plant features narrow, leathery leaves and produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Krainzia densispina is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
