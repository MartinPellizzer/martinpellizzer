Koeiea flexuosa

Koeiea flexuosa, commonly known as the silver dune grass, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in sandy or coastal environments. The plant has narrow, silver-gray leaves that help reduce water loss in arid conditions. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the spring.

Koeiea flexuosa is well adapted to coastal dunes and plays a role in stabilizing sandy soils.
