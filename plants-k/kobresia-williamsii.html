Kobresia williamsii

Kobresia williamsii, commonly known as the Williams' kobresia, is a low-growing perennial grass found in arid and semi-arid regions.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spikelet-based flowers. It is well adapted to harsh environments, often thriving in sandy or rocky soils.

Kobresia williamsii plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for desert wildlife.
