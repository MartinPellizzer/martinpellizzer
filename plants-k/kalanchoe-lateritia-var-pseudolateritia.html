Kalanchoe lateritia var. pseudolateritia

Kalanchoe lateritia var. pseudolateritia, commonly known as the rock chandelier, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, clustered rosettes of thick, fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces vibrant orange to red flowers during the winter months, which bloom in terminal clusters. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil with bright, indirect sunlight.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
