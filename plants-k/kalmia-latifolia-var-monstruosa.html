Kalmia latifolia var. monstruosa

Kalmia latifolia var. monstruosa, commonly known as the Florida flame azalea, is a variant of the broadleaf kalmia native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red to orange-red flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full shade. Its foliage consists of broad, dark green leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its showy blooms.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in garden landscapes for its vibrant floral display.
