Kohleria saxicola

Kohleria saxicola, commonly known as the rock kohleria, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone habitats, thriving in well-drained soil. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery bloom.

Kohleria saxicola is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
