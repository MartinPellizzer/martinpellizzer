Kentia rubricaulis

Kentia rubricaulis, commonly known as the red-stemmed Kentia palm, is a striking tropical plant native to the island of New Guinea.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk with a distinctive reddish hue, which becomes more pronounced with age. The plant features broad, feather-like leaves that grow in a rosette pattern, giving it a lush and elegant appearance. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Kentia rubricaulis is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and aesthetic appeal.
