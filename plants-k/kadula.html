Kadula

Kadula, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It belongs to the genus Saintpaulia and is characterized by its velvety, waxy leaves and clusters of showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Kadula is popular as a houseplant due to its compact size and long blooming period.

Its botanical features make it a favorite among indoor gardeners seeking both beauty and ease of care.
