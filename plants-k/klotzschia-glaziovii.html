Klotzschia glaziovii

Klotzschia glaziovii, commonly known as the Glaziov's bulbous lily, is a striking member of the Amaryllidaceae family native to the arid regions of Mexico.

Its flowers typically appear in late spring or early summer, adding a dramatic visual impact to its surroundings.

Klotzschia glaziovii is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its resilience and ornamental value.
