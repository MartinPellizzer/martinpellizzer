Kentia monostachya

Kentia monostachya, commonly known as the Kentia palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that give it a lush, feathery appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by clustered, round fruits that contain edible seeds. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance of low light and moderate watering.

Its elegant foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental purposes in both residential and commercial settings.
