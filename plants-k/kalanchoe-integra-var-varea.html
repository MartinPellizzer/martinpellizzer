Kalanchoe integra var. varea

Kalanchoe integra var. varea, commonly known as the Variegated Kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of orange, red, and yellow during the winter months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and rock gardens.
