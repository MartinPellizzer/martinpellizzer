Kalopanax ricinifolius var. chinensis

Kalopanax ricinifolius var. chinensis, commonly known as Chinese cow-itch, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with serrated margins and small, yellowish-green flowers that appear in clusters. The plant produces berries that mature to a dark blue color, containing seeds surrounded by a fleshy aril. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is noted for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.
