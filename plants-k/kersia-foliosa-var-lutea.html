Kersia foliosa var. lutea

Kersia foliosa var. lutea, commonly known as the yellow kersia, is a flowering plant native to parts of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers that bloom in spring, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat. The plant typically grows in shrubland and coastal areas, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are simple and elliptical, often with a slightly waxy texture.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
