Kleinia isabellae

Kleinia isabellae, commonly known as the Isabella's ice plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, which is characterized by its fleshy leaves and showy flowers. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in winter. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments.

It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
