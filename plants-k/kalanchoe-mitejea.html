Kalanchoe mitejea

Kalanchoe mitejea, commonly known as the Christmas chandelier, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings. The plant produces clusters of bright orange or red flowers during the winter months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Kalanchoe mitejea is drought-tolerant and makes a striking addition to rock gardens or container displays.
