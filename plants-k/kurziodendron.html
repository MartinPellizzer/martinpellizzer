Kurziodendron

Kurziodendron, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a tropical plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to its surroundings. The plant has a broad, spreading canopy with compound leaves that provide shade. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Kurziodendron is also valued for its ability to withstand drought conditions once established.
