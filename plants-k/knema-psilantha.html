Knema psilantha

Knema psilantha, commonly known as the smooth knema, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, glossy leaves and fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that are typically red or orange in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
