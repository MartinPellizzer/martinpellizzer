Kalanchoe macrantha var. marmorata

Kalanchoe macrantha var. marmorata, commonly known as the marble queen kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves with a distinctive marbled pattern of green and silver. The plant produces vibrant orange or red flowers in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Due to its compact size and ornamental leaves, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and rock gardens.
