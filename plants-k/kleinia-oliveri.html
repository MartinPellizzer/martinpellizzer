Kleinia oliveri

Kleinia oliveri, commonly known as the Oliver's ice plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, characterized by its fleshy leaves and vibrant flowers. The plant features a low-growing, mat-forming habit with thick, ribbed stems that store water. Its flowers are typically small, star-shaped, and come in shades of yellow, white, and red.

Kleinia oliveri thrives in arid environments and is well adapted to withstand drought and high temperatures.
