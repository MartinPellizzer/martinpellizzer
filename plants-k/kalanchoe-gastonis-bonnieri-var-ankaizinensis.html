Kalanchoe gastonis-bonnieri var. ankaizinensis

Kalanchoe gastonis-bonnieri var. ankaizinensis, commonly known as the Ankaizinensis Kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often blooming in winter. It is a compact shrub that can reach up to 30 cm in height, making it suitable for container gardening.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
