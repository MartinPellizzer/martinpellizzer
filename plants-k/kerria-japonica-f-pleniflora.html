Kerria japonica f. pleniflora

Kerria japonica f. pleniflora, commonly known as the Japanese kerria with double flowers, is a hybrid shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and its profusion of showy, double yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are dark green, glossy, and oval-shaped, providing a lush backdrop to the blooms. This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

It is also relatively disease-resistant and suitable for partial to full sun exposure.
