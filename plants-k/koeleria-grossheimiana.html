Koeleria grossheimiana

Koeleria grossheimiana, commonly known as the mountain flax, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the western United States and northern Mexico.

The plant produces small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer, typically in clusters at the ends of the stems.
