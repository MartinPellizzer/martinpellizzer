Knowltonia Afra

Knowltonia afra is a rare and striking plant native to the arid regions of South Africa. It is known for its unique appearance, featuring tall, spindly stems and vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in the winter months. This plant has adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments by storing water in its thick, fleshy stems. Its beauty and resilience make it a fascinating subject for botanists and plant enthusiasts alike. Despite its allure, Knowltonia afra remains relatively obscure and is often overlooked in the world of horticulture.
