Kallstroemia solandri

Kallstroemia solandri, commonly known as the goldenstar bush, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring dense, glossy green foliage. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers that are clustered in spherical inflorescences, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Kallstroemia solandri is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
