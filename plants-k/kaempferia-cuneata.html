Kaempferia cuneata

Kaempferia cuneata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed ginger or cuneate arrowroot, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other ginger species. The plant features sword-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
