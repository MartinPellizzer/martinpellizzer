Kalimeris incisa var. yomena

Kalimeris incisa var. yomena, commonly known as the Japanese yomena, is a herbaceous perennial native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant features finely cut, serrated leaves that are often covered in a soft, velvety texture. Its flowers are typically yellow to orange, forming small, daisy-like clusters that bloom in late summer.

Kalimeris incisa var. yomena is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soil.
