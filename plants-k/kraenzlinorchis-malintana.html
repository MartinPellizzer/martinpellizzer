Kraenzlinorchis malintana

Kraenzlinorchis malintana, commonly known as the Malintan orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and green, typically blooming in the summer months. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Kraenzlinorchis malintana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
