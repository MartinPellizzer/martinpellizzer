Karpatiosorbus fischeri

Karpatiosorbus fischeri, commonly known as the Carpathian rowan, is a species of shrub native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its small, white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces red berries that are edible for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas with well-drained soil.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
