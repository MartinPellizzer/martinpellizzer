Kalanchoe miniata var. miniata

Kalanchoe miniata var. miniata, commonly known as the miniature kalanhoe or dwarf kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings, and it produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red. The plant is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems. It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.

Kalanchoe miniata var. miniata is also known for its ability to bloom profusely during the winter months.
