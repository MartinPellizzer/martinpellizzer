Kyllinga Chrysantha Var. Comosipes

Kyllinga chrysantha var. comosipes is a strikingly beautiful plant known for its vibrant, golden-yellow foliage. It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, commonly referred to as the sedge family. This variety is particularly admired for its dense, grass-like appearance and its ability to thrive in moist, well-drained soils. It is often used in ornamental gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of maintenance. Its unique coloration and compact growth make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor landscapes.
