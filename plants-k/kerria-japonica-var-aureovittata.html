Kerria japonica var. aureovittata

Kerria japonica var. aureovittata, commonly known as the golden stripe kerria, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features vibrant yellow flowers with a distinctive golden stripe along the petals, which bloom in early spring. The plant has glossy, dark green foliage that turns orange-red in the fall. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden borders or hedges.

Its ornamental value comes from both its showy flowers and seasonal color changes.
