Keteleeria davidiana subsp. chien-peii

Keteleeria davidiana subsp.

chien-peii, commonly known as the Chinese fir, is a species of conifer native to eastern Asia. It is characterized by its needle-like leaves and cone-bearing structure, typical of the Cupressaceae family.

The plant thrives in temperate climates and is often found in mountainous regions with well-drained soils. Its bark is thick and fissured, providing protection against environmental stressors.
