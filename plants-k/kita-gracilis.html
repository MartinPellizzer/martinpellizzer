Kita gracilis

Kita gracilis, commonly known as the slender spurge, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, characterized by its milky sap and distinctive floral structures. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a waxy coating, while its flowers are small and clustered in cyathia.

Kita gracilis is valued in traditional medicine for its potential anti-inflammatory properties.
