Kaempferia pandurata

Kaempferia pandurata, commonly known as the flame orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, flame-like inflorescence. The plant typically grows as a rhizomatous herb, with fleshy leaves and showy, brightly colored flowers. Its blooms, which can be red, orange, or yellow, are often adorned with intricate patterns.

Kaempferia pandurata is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its cultural significance in traditional medicine.
