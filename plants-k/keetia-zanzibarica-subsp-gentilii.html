Keetia zanzibarica subsp. gentilii

Keetia zanzibarica subsp.

gentilii, commonly known as the Zanzibar keetia, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of East Africa. It is a shrub characterized by its small, fragrant yellow flowers and leathery, elliptical leaves.

The plant thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal dunes and rocky habitats. It is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique appearance.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable in some areas.
