Kickxia bombycina

Kickxia bombycina, commonly known as the silvery bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of western Asia.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family, characterized by its bell-shaped flowers. The plant features slender, hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically pale blue or white, with a distinctive silvery-blue hue, and bloom in late spring to early summer.
