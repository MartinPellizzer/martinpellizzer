Krascheninikovia heterantha var. linearifolia

Krascheninikovia heterantha var. linearifolia, commonly known as the linear-leaved krascheninikovia, is a perennial grass species native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in its harsh environment. The plant produces small, pale green flowers in dense spikes, which are arranged in a panicle inflorescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand extreme temperature fluctuations.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often used in ecological restoration projects in degraded landscapes.
