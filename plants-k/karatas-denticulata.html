Karatas denticulata

Karatas denticulata, commonly known as the toothed karatas, is a species of flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Karatas denticulata is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
