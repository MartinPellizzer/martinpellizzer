Kalanchoe crenata var. pseudolateritia

Kalanchoe crenata var. pseudolateritia, commonly known as the silver pincushion, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are pale green with a silvery-blue hue, covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the winter months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its compact growth habit, it is often used as a decorative indoor plant or in rock gardens.
