Klainedoxa ovalifolia

Klainedoxa ovalifolia, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a striking perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a distinctive yellow or orange throat. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is often cultivated in subtropical and temperate regions.
