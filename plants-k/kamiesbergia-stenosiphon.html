Kamiesbergia stenosiphon

Kamiesbergia stenosiphon, commonly known as the narrow-siphon grass, is a species of flowering plant in the family Poaceae.

It is native to South Africa, specifically found in the Karoo region, where it thrives in arid and semi-arid environments.
