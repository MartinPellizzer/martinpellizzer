Knowltonia chilensis

Knowltonia chilensis, commonly known as the Chilean orchid, is a flowering plant native to the temperate regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant blossoms. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests or mountainous regions. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil efficiently.

Knowltonia chilensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
