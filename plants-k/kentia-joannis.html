Kentia joannis

Kentia joannis, commonly known as the Kentia palm, is a tropical evergreen plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It features a slender, unbranched trunk covered in smooth, dark green leaves. The leaves are long, narrow, and have a waxy texture, giving the plant a sleek appearance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture without being overwatered.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its air-purifying qualities and low maintenance needs.
