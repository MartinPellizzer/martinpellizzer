Kochia villosissima

Kochia villosissima, commonly known as the velvetleaf kochia, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its hairy, succulent stems and leaves that are often covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Kochia villosissima is also known for its ability to tolerate drought and poor soil quality.
