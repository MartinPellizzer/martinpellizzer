Kosmosiphon azureus

Kosmosiphon azureus, commonly known as the blue ghost cactus, is a rare and striking plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

The plant produces striking blue or violet flowers during the rainy season, which are often visited by pollinators such as bees and moths.
