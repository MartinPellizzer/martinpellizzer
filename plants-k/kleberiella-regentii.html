Kleberiella regentii

Kleberiella regentii, commonly known as the Regent's bell, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with woody stems and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Kleberiella regentii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
