Kalanchoe petitiaesii

Kalanchoe petitiaesii, commonly known as the miniature kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slight blue or purple tint. The plant produces small, colorful flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red during the winter months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Kalanchoe petitiaesii is drought-tolerant and is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and ease of care.
