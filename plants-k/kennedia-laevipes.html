Kennedia laevipes

Kennedia laevipes, commonly known as the smooth Kennedia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and distinctive pod fruits. The plant features slender, ascending stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a smooth texture, contributing to its common name.

Kennedia laevipes is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
