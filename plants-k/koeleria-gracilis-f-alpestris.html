Koeleria gracilis f. alpestris

Koeleria gracilis f. alpestris, commonly known as the alpine slender grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically produces pale blue to purple flowers, which are clustered in dense spikes. It thrives in alpine and arctic environments, often growing in rocky or sandy soils.
