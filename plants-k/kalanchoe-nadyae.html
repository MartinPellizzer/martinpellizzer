Kalanchoe nadyae

Kalanchoe nadyae, commonly known as the Nadya's Kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white powdery wax, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically during the winter months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Kalanchoe nadyae is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
