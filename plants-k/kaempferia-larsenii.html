Kaempferia larsenii

Kaempferia larsenii, commonly known as the purple-flowered ginger, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking violet or purple flowers that bloom in clusters along the stem. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Kaempferia larsenii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
