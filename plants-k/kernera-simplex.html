Kernera simplex

Kernera simplex, commonly known as the mountain daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with hairy, narrow leaves and bright yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant typically blooms in spring and summer, producing numerous small, composite flowers that attract pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Kernera simplex is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its cheerful appearance.
