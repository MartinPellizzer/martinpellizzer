Kungia schoenlandii var. lepidotricha

Kungia schoenlandii var. lepidotricha, commonly known as the Kungia lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that bear clusters of large, showy white or pale pink flowers with a distinctive, intricate pattern of purple and green markings. The leaves are long, narrow, and arranged in a rosette at the base, giving the plant a dramatic, architectural appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its unique floral structure and striking coloration make it a standout in both cultivated and natural settings.
