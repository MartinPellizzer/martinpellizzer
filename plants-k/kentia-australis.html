Kentia australis

Kentia australis, commonly known as the Kentia palm, is a tropical evergreen plant native to the coastal regions of eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound leaves that give it a graceful appearance. The plant has a slow growth rate and is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its ability to thrive in low-light conditions. Its foliage remains lush and green throughout the year, making it a popular choice for ornamental purposes.

Kentia palms are also known for their air-purifying properties, contributing to improved indoor air quality.
