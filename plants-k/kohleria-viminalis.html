Kohleria viminalis

Kohleria viminalis, commonly known as the silver leaf kohleria, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Kohleria viminalis is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental leaves and vibrant blooms.
