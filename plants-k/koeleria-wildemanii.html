Koeleria wildemanii

Koeleria wildemanii, commonly known as the wildeman's grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial grass that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences with spikelets. It is native to regions in the western United States and is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

Koeleria wildemanii is characterized by its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
