Kohautia caespitosa var. dolichostyla

Kohautia caespitosa var. dolichostyla, commonly known as the long-styled kohautia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, grass-like foliage. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, white to pale blue flowers. Its leaves are typically narrow and have a bluish-green hue, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.
