Koeleria insubrica

Koeleria insubrica, commonly known as the slender needlegrass, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a perennial grass that typically grows in rocky and alpine environments. The plant features slender, erect stems with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its small, spike-like inflorescence bears tiny blue to purple flowers.

Koeleria insubrica is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in harsh environments.
