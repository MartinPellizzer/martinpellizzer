Koeleria gracilis f. congesta

Koeleria gracilis f. congesta, commonly known as the dense koeleria, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense clusters of narrow, grass-like leaves. The inflorescence consists of small, spike-like clusters of florets, which are typically pale green to blue-gray in color. This plant is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
