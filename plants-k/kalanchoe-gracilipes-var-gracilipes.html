Kalanchoe gracilipes var. gracilipes

Kalanchoe gracilipes var. gracilipes, commonly known as the slender kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tint. The plant produces clusters of bright orange or yellow flowers during the winter months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Kalanchoe gracilipes var. gracilipes is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact growth habit and attractive blooms.
