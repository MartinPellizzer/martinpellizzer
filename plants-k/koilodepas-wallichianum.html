Koilodepas wallichianum

Koilodepas wallichianum, commonly known as the Himalayan arrowhead, is a flowering plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is native to the eastern Himalayas, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant features arrow-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It is often found in mountainous regions at elevations ranging from 1,500 to 3,000 meters.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
