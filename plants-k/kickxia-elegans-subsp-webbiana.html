Kickxia elegans subsp. webbiana

Kickxia elegans subsp.

webbiana, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and western Asia. It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are usually hairy. Its flowers are small, bell-shaped, and typically blue or violet in color, blooming in late spring to early summer.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
