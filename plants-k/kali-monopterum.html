Kali monopterum

Kali monopterum, commonly known as the Indian nettle, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Urticaceae, which includes many species known for their stinging hairs. Kali monopterum is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
