Kalanchoe densiflora var. minor

Kalanchoe densiflora var. minor, commonly known as the compact kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces clusters of small, bright orange or yellow flowers during the winter months. It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its compact growth habit and vibrant blooms.

Its botanical characteristics make it well-suited for rock gardens and container gardening.
