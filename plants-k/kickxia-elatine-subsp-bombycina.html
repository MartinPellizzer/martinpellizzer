Kickxia elatine subsp. bombycina

Kickxia elatine subsp.

bombycina, commonly known as the silky knotweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers.

The leaves are typically narrow and slightly hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.
