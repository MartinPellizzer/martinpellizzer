Knema alvarezii

Knema alvarezii, commonly known as Alvarez's knema, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia, particularly found in Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is a small to medium-sized tree, typically reaching heights of up to 15 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and usually elliptical in shape, with a glossy surface.

The flowers are small and clustered, producing a distinctive aromatic scent that attracts various pollinators.
