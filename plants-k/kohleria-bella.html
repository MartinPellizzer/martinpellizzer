Kohleria bella

Kohleria bella, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety silver-green foliage. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in the spring and summer. Kohleria bella is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental leaves and vibrant blooms.
