Klopstockia utilis

Klopstockia utilis, commonly known as the useful klopstock, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to certain regions of South America.

It features upright, branching stems covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a serrated edge. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white to pale pink, which bloom in the spring. Klopstockia utilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and as a border plant in cultivated landscapes.
