Kydia glabrescens

Kydia glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth kydia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer seasons. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Kydia glabrescens is adapted to dry conditions and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
