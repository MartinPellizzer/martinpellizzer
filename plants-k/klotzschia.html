Klotzschia

Klotzschia, commonly known as the desert bell, is a genus of flowering plants native to arid regions of the Americas.

These plants are characterized by their unique, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. Klotzschia species typically have succulent, fleshy leaves that help them store water, adapting to their harsh environments. They are often found in rocky soils and can thrive in full sun.
