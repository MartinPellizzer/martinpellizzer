Kingstonia nervosa

Kingstonia nervosa, commonly known as the velvet tree, is a unique member of the Proteaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage that gives it its common name. The plant has a distinctive appearance with its thick, leathery leaves and striking, clustered white flowers. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Due to its specific environmental requirements, it is considered a challenging plant to grow outside its native habitat.
