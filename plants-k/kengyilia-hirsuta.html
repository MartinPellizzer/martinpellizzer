Kengyilia hirsuta

Kengyilia hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy fleur-de-lis, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy, lance-shaped leaves and showy, yellow flowers with a distinctive three-lobed structure. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in alpine environments. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
