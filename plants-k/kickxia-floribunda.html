Kickxia floribunda

Kickxia floribunda, commonly known as the blue flax or blue bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and prefers full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Kickxia floribunda is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
