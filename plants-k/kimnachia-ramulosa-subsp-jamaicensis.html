Kimnachia ramulosa subsp. jamaicensis

Kimnachia ramulosa subsp.

jamaicensis, commonly known as the Jamaican thornapple, is a member of the Solanaceae family. It is a shrub characterized by its spiny stems and opposite, ovate leaves.

The plant produces small, white to pale green flowers that are clustered in cymes. Its fruits are fleshy berries that turn red when mature, containing numerous seeds.

It is native to the Caribbean, particularly Jamaica, and is adapted to tropical and subtropical climates.
