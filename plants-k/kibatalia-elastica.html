Kibatalia elastica

Kibatalia elastica, commonly known as the rubber tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is known for its ability to exude a milky sap, which has been historically used for rubber production. Its growth habit includes a dense, bushy form with multiple stems.

Kibatalia elastica thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
