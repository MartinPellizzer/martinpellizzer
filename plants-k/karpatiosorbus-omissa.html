Karpatiosorbus omissa

Karpatiosorbus omissa, commonly known as the Carpathian rowan, is a deciduous shrub native to the Carpathian Mountains.

The plant produces red berries in the autumn, which are an important food source for birds and small mammals. Karpatiosorbus omissa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitat.
