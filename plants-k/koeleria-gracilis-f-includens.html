Koeleria gracilis f. includens

Koeleria gracilis f. includens, commonly known as the slender wild oat, is a flowering plant in the grass family, Poaceae.

It is characterized by its fine, grass-like foliage and small, spike-like inflorescences. This variety is native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America, where it thrives in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant typically grows to a height of 10 to 30 centimeters and is often found in disturbed or open habitats.

Its botanical features make it an important component of local ecosystems and a subject of study in plant ecology.
