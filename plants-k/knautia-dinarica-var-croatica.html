Knautia dinarica var. croatica

Knautia dinarica var. croatica, commonly known as the Dinaric knautia, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Dinaric Alps.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its fluffy, pink to purple flower heads that bloom in late summer. The plant has basal and upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and ability to attract pollinators.
