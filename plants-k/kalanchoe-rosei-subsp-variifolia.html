Kalanchoe rosei subsp. variifolia

Kalanchoe rosei subsp.

variifolia, commonly known as the Variegated Kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings.

The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, or orange during the winter months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Due to its compact growth habit, it is a popular choice for container gardening and rock gardens.
