Kyllinga melanosperma

Kyllinga melanosperma, commonly known as blackseed grass, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by dark, spherical seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Botanically, it belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges and related plants.
