Knoxia mollis

Knoxia mollis, commonly known as the velvetleaf knoxia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Knoxia mollis thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique leaf texture and subtle floral display.
