Knautia budensis

Knautia budensis, commonly known as the Balkan knautia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, pink to purple ray florets surrounding a central disk of yellow tubular flowers. It typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in full sun.

Knautia budensis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
