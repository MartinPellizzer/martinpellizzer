Koelpinia tenuissima

Koelpinia tenuissima, commonly known as the slender sandwort, is a low-growing, perennial herbaceous plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It features small, clustered leaves and produces tiny, greenish-white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant is adapted to harsh environments, with a shallow root system that allows it to thrive in sandy or rocky soils.
