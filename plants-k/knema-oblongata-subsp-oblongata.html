Knema oblongata subsp. oblongata

Knema oblongata subsp.

oblongata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved knema, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia. It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its elliptical, glossy leaves with a reddish underside.

The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that release tiny seeds when mature.

This species is valued for its timber and is often found in lowland rainforests.
