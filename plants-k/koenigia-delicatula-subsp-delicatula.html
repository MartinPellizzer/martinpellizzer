Koenigia delicatula subsp. delicatula

Koenigia delicatula subsp.

delicatula, commonly known as the slender snowdrop, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Africa. It features delicate, linear leaves and small, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring.

The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade. It is often found in rocky or sandy habitats, where it forms dense mats.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
