Koeleria tuzsonii var. albanica

Koeleria tuzsonii var. albanica, commonly known as the Albanian needlegrass, is a perennial grass species native to the Balkan region.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. The plant has slender, wiry stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a rough texture.

It is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soils and supporting local biodiversity.
