Kohleria karsteniana

Kohleria karsteniana, commonly known as the silver leaf kohleria, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that range from silver to dark green. Kohleria karsteniana is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and vibrant blooms.
