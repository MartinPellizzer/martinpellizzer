Knautia macedonica f. lyrophylla

Knautia macedonica f. lyrophylla, commonly known as the Lyrophylla knautia, is a striking flowering plant native to the Balkan region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces showy, pink to purple flower clusters resembling a pom-pom. The plant features narrow, hairy leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained, sunny soils and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
