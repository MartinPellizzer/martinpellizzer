Kaschgaria komarovii

Kaschgaria komarovii, commonly known as the Central Asian bellflower, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

Kaschgaria komarovii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is occasionally cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
