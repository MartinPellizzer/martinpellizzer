Kaempferia siphonantha

Kaempferia siphonantha, commonly known as the purple-flowered ginger, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking violet or purple inflorescences and lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments within tropical forests. It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both horticultural collections and botanical research.
