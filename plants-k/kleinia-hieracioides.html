Kleinia hieracioides

Kleinia hieracioides, commonly known as the silverleaf dandelion, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa.

It features compact, densely packed leaves that are typically silver or gray-green in color, covered in fine, hair-like structures. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by fluffy seed heads resembling dandelion puffs. It is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Kleinia hieracioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in drought conditions.
