Karatas Makoyana

The karatas makoyana is a unique and resilient plant native to specific regions of southeast asia. it thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in dense forest undergrowth. this plant is known for its striking, colorful foliage that changes with the seasons. its leaves are not only visually appealing but also have traditional uses in local cultures. despite its beauty, it remains relatively unknown to the wider world.
