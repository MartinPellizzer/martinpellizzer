Koeleria oreophila subsp. rosei

Koeleria oreophila subsp.

rosei, commonly known as the alpine silky grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family. It is native to high-altitude regions of the Himalayas and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit.

The plant produces small, pinkish to reddish florets that bloom in late summer, adding a delicate color to its rocky habitat. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, adapted to withstand cold and arid conditions. Koeleria oreophila subsp.

rosei is often found in alpine meadows and is valued for its ornamental appeal in rock gardens.
