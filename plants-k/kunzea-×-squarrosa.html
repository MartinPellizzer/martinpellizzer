Kunzea × squarrosa

Kunzea × squarrosa, commonly known as the squarrosa kunzea, is a hybrid shrub native to Australia.

It typically grows in coastal and sandy environments, showcasing adaptability to harsh conditions. The plant features small, needle-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. Its flowers are usually pink or red, arranged in clusters, and attract various pollinators.

Kunzea × squarrosa is valued for its ornamental qualities and resilience in arid landscapes.
