Knautia drymeja var. arvernensis

Knautia drymeja var. arvernensis, commonly known as the Arverne knautia, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It features upright stems that bear clusters of pink to purple flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with serrated edges, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is valued for its attractive flowers and is often used in ecological landscaping to support pollinators.
