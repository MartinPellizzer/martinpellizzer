Knema odorata

Knema odorata, commonly known as the fragrant knema, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, white flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy berries that are often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are often used for their pleasant scent and are sometimes employed in perfumery.

Knema odorata is valued for both its ornamental qualities and its ecological importance in its native habitats.
