Kerria japonica var. plena

Kerria japonica var. plena, commonly known as the full-flowered Japanese kerria, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its lush, dark green foliage and profuse display of double, yellow-orange flowers in early spring. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible but not particularly palatable. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, moist soil.

This ornamental plant is valued for its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various garden conditions.
