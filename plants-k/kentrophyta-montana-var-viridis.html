Kentrophyta montana var. viridis

Kentrophyta montana var. viridis, commonly known as the green mountain kentrophyte, is a rare and specialized plant species native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

It thrives in cool, moist environments with well-drained soil, often found in rocky crevices and alpine meadows. The plant is characterized by its vibrant green foliage and compact, bushy growth habit. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in a fine, waxy cuticle to reduce water loss in its harsh habitat.
