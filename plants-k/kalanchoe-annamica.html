Kalanchoe annamica

Kalanchoe annamica, commonly known as the Annamite Kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to Vietnam.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that store water, adapting to its arid environment. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, typically during the spring and summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Kalanchoe annamica is also noted for its ability to propagate through offsets and its resilience to drought conditions.
