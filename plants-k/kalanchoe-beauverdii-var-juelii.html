Kalanchoe beauverdii var. juelii

Kalanchoe beauverdii var. juelii, commonly known as the Juel's Kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating called farina. The plant produces clusters of bright orange to yellow flowers during the winter months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil and bright, indirect light.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and rock gardens.
