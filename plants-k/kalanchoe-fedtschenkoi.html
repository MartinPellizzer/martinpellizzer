Kalanchoe fedtschenkoi

Kalanchoe fedtschenkoi, commonly known as the Mother of Millions, is a succulent plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red during the winter months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

Kalanchoe fedtschenkoi is also notable for its ability to propagate through offsets and leaf cuttings.
