Kitamuraster

Kitamuraster, commonly known as the Japanese flowering maple, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its ornamental, palmately lobed leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers in early spring, which are followed by orange-red berries. Kitamuraster thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its seasonal foliage changes from green to vibrant red or yellow in autumn, adding visual interest throughout the year.
