Kalanchoe uniflora

Kalanchoe uniflora, commonly known as the single-flower kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces a single, showy flower at the tip of each stem, typically in shades of yellow or orange. It is a perennial species that blooms in spring and early summer.

Kalanchoe uniflora is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its resilience and vibrant floral display.
