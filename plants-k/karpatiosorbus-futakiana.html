Karpatiosorbus futakiana

Karpatiosorbus futakiana, commonly known as the Turkish rowan, is a species of shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Turkey and is characterized by its dense, thorny branches and small, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of white flowers in spring, which are followed by red berries in the autumn. It is well adapted to harsh alpine environments and is often found growing at elevations between 1,500 and 2,500 meters.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
