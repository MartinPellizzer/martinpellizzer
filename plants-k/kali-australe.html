Kali australe

Kali australe, commonly known as the Australian nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like tomatoes and tobacco. The plant features hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or purple. Its fruits are berries that turn from green to yellow or red as they mature.

Kali australe is often found in dry, sandy soils and can thrive in arid environments.
