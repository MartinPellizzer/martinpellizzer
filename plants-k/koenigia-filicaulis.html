Koenigia filicaulis

Koenigia filicaulis, commonly known as the featherleaf stonecrop, is a low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, linear leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its unique foliage and compact growth habit make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
