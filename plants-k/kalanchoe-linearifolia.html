Kalanchoe linearifolia

Kalanchoe linearifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf Kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to Madagascar.

It features fleshy, linear leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish tinge. The plant produces vibrant orange or yellow flowers during the winter months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Kalanchoe linearifolia is drought-tolerant and suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
