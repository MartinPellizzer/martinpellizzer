Knersia

Knersia, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray leaves that are covered in a fine, waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by small, round fruits. Knersia is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its adaptability to poor soils makes it a valuable plant in ecological restoration projects.
