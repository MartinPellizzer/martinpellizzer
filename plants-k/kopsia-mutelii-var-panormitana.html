Kopsia mutelii var. panormitana

Kopsia mutelii var. panormitana, commonly known as the Mediterranean kopsia, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It prefers well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value and adaptability contribute to its popularity in horticultural settings.
