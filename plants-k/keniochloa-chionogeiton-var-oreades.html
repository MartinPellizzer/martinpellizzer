Keniochloa chionogeiton var. oreades

Keniochloa chionogeiton var. oreades, commonly known as the silverhair grass, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-tinged foliage and compact growth habit. The plant typically thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine environments. Its inflorescence consists of dense, spike-like clusters of small, pale green to silver-colored flowers.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing rocky and barren slopes.
