Kaempferia kirkii var. elatior

Kaempferia kirkii var. elatior, commonly known as the Giant Flame Orchid, is a striking tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features vibrant orange and red petals with a distinctive yellow lip, making it a visually appealing ornamental plant. The plant typically grows as a pseudobulbous herb, with thick, fleshy stems that store water and nutrients. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its flowering period usually occurs during the summer months, producing clusters of showy, fragrant blooms.
