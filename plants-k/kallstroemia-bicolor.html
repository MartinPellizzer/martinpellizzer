Kallstroemia bicolor

Kallstroemia bicolor, commonly known as the pink and white phlox, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sun. Its foliage is lance-shaped and often covered in a fine, silvery down.

Kallstroemia bicolor is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and landscapes for its vibrant blooms.
