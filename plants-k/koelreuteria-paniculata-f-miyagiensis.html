Koelreuteria paniculata f. miyagiensis

Koelreuteria paniculata f. miyagiensis, commonly known as the Miyagi's Chinese lantern tree, is a compact, ornamental variant of the Chinese lantern tree.

It is characterized by its showy, orange-red inflorescences that appear in late summer, resembling small lanterns. The plant has ovate to elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside. It is native to Japan and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

This form is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and public spaces.
