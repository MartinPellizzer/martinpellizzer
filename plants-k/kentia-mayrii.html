Kentia mayrii

Kentia mayrii, commonly known as the Mayrii palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and pinnate leaves that are long and feathery. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Kentia mayrii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its elegant appearance and air-purifying qualities.
