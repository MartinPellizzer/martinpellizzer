Kyllinga brevifolia var. stellulata

Kyllinga brevifolia var. stellulata, commonly known as the starleaf kyllinga, is a herbaceous plant characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive foliage.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving them a soft texture. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that are typically pink or red, adding visual interest to gardens. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is cultivated in various regions for landscaping and horticultural purposes.
