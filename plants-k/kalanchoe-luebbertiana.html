Kalanchoe luebbertiana

Kalanchoe luebbertiana, commonly known as the Luebbertiana Kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle, aiding in water retention. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically during the winter months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Kalanchoe luebbertiana is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
