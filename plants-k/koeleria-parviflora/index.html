Koeleria Parviflora

Koeleria parviflora is a small, delicate flowering plant native to the arid regions of western North America. It is known for its tiny, pale blue or lavender flowers that bloom in early spring. This plant thrives in rocky soils and is often found in open plains and mountainous areas. Its compact size and hardy nature make it well adapted to harsh environmental conditions. Koeleria parviflora plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and maintaining ecological balance.
