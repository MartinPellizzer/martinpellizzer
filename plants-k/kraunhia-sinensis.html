Kraunhia sinensis

Kraunhia sinensis, commonly known as Chinese bellflower, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple, typically blooming in summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value is complemented by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
