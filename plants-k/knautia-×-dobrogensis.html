Knautia × dobrogensis

Knautia × dobrogensis, commonly known as the Dobrogian hybrid, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the family Polygonaceae.

It is a natural hybrid between Knautia arvensis and Knautia montana, exhibiting characteristics of both parent species. The plant produces showy, pink to purple flower clusters that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and prolonged flowering period.
