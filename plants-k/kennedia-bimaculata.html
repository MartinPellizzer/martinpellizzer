Kennedia bimaculata

Kennedia bimaculata, commonly known as the Two-spotted Kennedia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a perennial shrub that produces striking red or orange flowers with distinctive black spots. The plant belongs to the Fabaceae family, also known as the legume or pea family. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

Kennedia bimaculata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to well-drained soils in full sun.
