Kerria japonica f. albescens

Kerria japonica f. albescens, commonly known as the white-flowered Japanese kerria, is a deciduous shrub characterized by its ornamental, pale yellow to white flowers.

It is a compact variety of the species Kerria japonica, which is native to Japan and China. The plant features broad, serrated leaves that turn red in the fall, adding seasonal interest. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and is often used in gardens for its delicate floral display.

This cultivar is particularly valued for its extended blooming period and low maintenance requirements.
