Kutchubaea duckei

Kutchubaea duckei, commonly known as the Malabar nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale purple, often blooming at night. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

The fruit is a fleshy berry that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by animals.
