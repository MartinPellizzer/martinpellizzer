Kernera Ovalis

Kernera ovalis is a unique plant native to the arid regions of Australia. It is known for its distinctive oval-shaped leaves and its ability to thrive in harsh, dry environments. This species is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to withstand extreme temperatures. Its survival strategies include storing water in its thick foliage. Despite its tough appearance, Kernera ovalis plays an important role in its ecosystem.
