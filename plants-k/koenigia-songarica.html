Koenigia songarica

Koenigia songarica, commonly known as the Songarica ice plant, is a succulent species native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white, typically during the summer months. It prefers well-draining soil and full sun exposure, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
