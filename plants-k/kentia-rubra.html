Kentia rubra

Kentia rubra, commonly known as the red kentia palm, is a species of palm native to the tropical rainforests of northeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into red, fleshy fruits containing black seeds. Kentia rubra is well adapted to humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its elegant appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
