Kalanchoe beauverdii var. beauverdii

Kalanchoe beauverdii var. beauverdii, commonly known as the Cape tulip, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, typically during the winter months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Kalanchoe beauverdii var. beauverdii is popular as a decorative plant due to its striking appearance and ease of care.
