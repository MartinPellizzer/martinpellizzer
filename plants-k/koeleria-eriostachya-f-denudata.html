Koeleria eriostachya f. denudata

Koeleria eriostachya f. denudata, commonly known as the naked-stemmed wheatgrass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is a perennial grass characterized by its slender, leafy stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

This subspecies is noted for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in arid environments.
