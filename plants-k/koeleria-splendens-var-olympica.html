Koeleria splendens var. olympica

Koeleria splendens var. olympica, commonly known as the Olympic Splendour grass, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of the Pacific Northwest.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense, tufted foliage that turns a striking golden hue in the fall. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous slender, blue-green flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements further enhance its appeal in garden settings.
