Kyrstenia rufa

Kyrstenia rufa, commonly known as the red-flowered kyrstenia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its most distinctive characteristic is its clusters of bright red, tubular flowers that bloom in late summer.

Kyrstenia rufa is often found in rocky soils and sunny habitats, making it a hardy and resilient plant.
