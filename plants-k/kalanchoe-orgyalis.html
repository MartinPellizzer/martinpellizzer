Kalanchoe orgyalis

Kalanchoe orgyalis, commonly known as the silver leaf kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery-white coating, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically blooming in spring or early summer. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.

Kalanchoe orgyalis is also known for its ability to propagate easily through stem cuttings.
