Kalanchoe humilis

Kalanchoe humilis, commonly known as the dwarf kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tint. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red during the winter months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires plenty of sunlight.

Kalanchoe humilis is drought-tolerant and suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.
