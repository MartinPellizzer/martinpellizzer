Kalimeris yomena subsp. angustifolia

Kalimeris yomena subsp.

angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf yomena, is a perennial herb native to East Asia. It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically hairy and have a strong aromatic scent.

The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in flat-topped clusters, blooming in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in garden landscapes.
