Knowltonia sellowii

Knowltonia sellowii, commonly known as the Sellow's orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually elongated and grass-like, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

This orchid is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is appreciated for its unique floral structure.
