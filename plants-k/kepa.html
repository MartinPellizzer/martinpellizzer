Kepa

Kepa, commonly known as the African violet, is a popular flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, clustered flowers. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Kepa is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ability to bloom throughout the year under proper care.

Its compact size and vibrant flowers make it a favorite among indoor gardeners.
