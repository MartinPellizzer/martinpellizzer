Kablikia truncata

Kablikia truncata, commonly known as the narrowleaf hibiscus, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. The plant features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. Its leaves are usually narrow and lance-shaped, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Kablikia truncata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a landscape plant.
