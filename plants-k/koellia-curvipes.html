Koellia curvipes

Koellia curvipes, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a tropical orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Koellia curvipes is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
