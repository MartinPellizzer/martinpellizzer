Kaempferia xiengkhouangensis

Kaempferia xiengkhouangensis, commonly known as the Xiengkhouang ginger orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of pink to purple flowers with a distinct, spicy scent. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
