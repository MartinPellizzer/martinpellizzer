Knautia lancifolia var. transsilvanica

Knautia lancifolia var. transsilvanica, commonly known as the Transylvanian lance-leaved knautia, is a perennial plant native to the Carpathian region.

It features slender, upright stems covered in soft, hairy foliage. The plant produces clusters of pink to purple flowers with narrow, lance-shaped petals. It thrives in well-drained, sandy or gravelly soils and prefers full sun.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in supporting pollinators.
