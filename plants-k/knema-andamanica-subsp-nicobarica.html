Knema andamanica subsp. nicobarica

Knema andamanica subsp.

nicobarica, commonly known as the Nicobar knema, is a small evergreen tree native to the Andaman and Nicobar Islands. It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its simple, elliptical leaves with a glossy surface.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by dark purple or black berries. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
