Knema yunnanensis

Knema yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan knema, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly in China, Vietnam, and Myanmar. The plant is characterized by its small, ovate leaves with a smooth surface and a fragrant, aromatic scent. Its flowers are typically white or pale yellow, and it produces small, fleshy berries as fruit.

Knema yunnanensis is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental and potential medicinal properties.
