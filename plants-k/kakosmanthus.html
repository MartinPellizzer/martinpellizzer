Kakosmanthus

Kakosmanthus, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy blooms that range in color from white to pink and deep red. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that provide a striking contrast to its vibrant flowers. It thrives in wet, tropical environments and is often found near water bodies.

The botanical features of Kakosmanthus make it a symbol of purity and spiritual significance in many cultures.
