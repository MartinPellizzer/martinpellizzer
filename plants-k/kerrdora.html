Kerrdora

Kerrdora, commonly known as the moonflower orchid, is a unique plant with striking botanical features.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its large, fragrant white flowers that bloom at night. The plant has long, slender leaves and produces a single, showy flower per stem. Kerrdora thrives in warm, humid climates and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Its nocturnal blooming habit makes it a fascinating subject for botanical study and horticultural appreciation.
