Kantou

Kantou, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the genus Physalis and is characterized by its vibrant, papery calyces that enclose the fruit. The plant produces small, orange or red berries that are edible when ripe. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.
