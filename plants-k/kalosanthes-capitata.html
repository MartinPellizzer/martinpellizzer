Kalosanthes capitata

Kalosanthes capitata, commonly known as the clustered star orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, clustered growth habit. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Kalosanthes capitata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and tropical landscapes.
