Ktenospermum linifolium

Ktenospermum linifolium, commonly known as the silky senna, is a climbing shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery leaves and yellow-orange flowers. The plant produces long, slender pods that contain small, black seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
