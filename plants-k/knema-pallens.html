Knema pallens

Knema pallens, commonly known as the white knema, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, followed by bright red or orange berries. Knema pallens is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and horticultural contexts.
