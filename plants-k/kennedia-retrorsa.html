Kennedia retrorsa

Kennedia retrorsa, commonly known as the wattle tree, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces bright yellow, pea-like flowers. It is often found in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in full sun.

Kennedia retrorsa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
