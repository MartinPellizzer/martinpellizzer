Krainzia longiflora

Krainzia longiflora, commonly known as the long-flowered krainzia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features wiry, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. Its most distinctive feature is the elongated, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of yellow to orange.

Krainzia longiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
