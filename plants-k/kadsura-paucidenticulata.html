Kadsura paucidenticulata

Kadsura paucidenticulata, commonly known as the slender kadsura, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is characterized by its unique, twisting vines and heart-shaped leaves.
