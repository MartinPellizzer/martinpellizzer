Kalanchoe lanceolata var. glabra

Kalanchoe lanceolata var. glabra, commonly known as the smoothleaf kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and often tinged with red or purple at the edges. The plant produces vibrant, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red during the winter months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires plenty of sunlight.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
