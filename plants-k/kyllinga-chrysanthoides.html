Kyllinga chrysanthoides

Kyllinga chrysanthoides, commonly known as golden kyllinga, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features upright, grass-like leaves that are typically bright green with a smooth texture. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Kyllinga chrysanthoides is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual interest.
