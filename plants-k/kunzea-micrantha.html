Kunzea micrantha

Kunzea micrantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved kunzea, is a small evergreen shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes eucalyptus and tea trees, and is characterized by its aromatic foliage. The plant typically grows in coastal and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions. Its small, fragrant white flowers appear in summer, attracting nectar-feeding insects.

Kunzea micrantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local biodiversity.
