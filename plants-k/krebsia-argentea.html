Krebsia argentea

Krebsia argentea, commonly known as silverleaf krebsia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Krebsia argentea is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.

Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a distinctive waxy coating that helps reduce water loss.
