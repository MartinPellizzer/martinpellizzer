Koeleria barrelieri

Koeleria barrelieri, commonly known as the Barrelier's grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, adapted to conserve water in harsh environments.

Koeleria barrelieri is often found in sandy or rocky soils and is valued for its resilience and ornamental appeal.
