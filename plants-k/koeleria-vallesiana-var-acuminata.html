Koeleria vallesiana var. acuminata

Koeleria vallesiana var. acuminata, commonly known as the slender basket grass, is a perennial grass species native to the western United States.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. The plant features slender, tufted inflorescences with spikelets that are often tinged with purple or brown. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native plant landscaping.
