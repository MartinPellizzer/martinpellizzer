Koeleria gracilis var. californiensis

Koeleria gracilis var. californiensis, commonly known as California slender wheatgrass, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance. This variety typically grows in open, dry habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences with tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.
