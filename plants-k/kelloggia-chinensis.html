Kelloggia chinensis

Kelloggia chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple, typically blooming in late summer. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in woodland areas and along stream banks.

Kelloggia chinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
