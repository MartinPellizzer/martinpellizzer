Kalanchoe deflersii

Kalanchoe deflersii, commonly known as the mother of thousands, is a succulent plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. The plant produces clusters of bright orange or yellow flowers during the winter months. It is a popular ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and ease of care.

Kalanchoe deflersii is also notable for its ability to propagate through offsets and leaf cuttings.
