Kentia gibbsiana

Kentia gibbsiana, commonly known as the Kentia palm, is a species native to New Caledonia.

It is a tall, slender palm with a single, unbranched trunk that can reach heights of up to 20 meters. The plant features pinnately compound leaves that are long and feathery, giving it a graceful appearance. Its foliage is evergreen, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor landscaping.

The Kentia palm is known for its adaptability to various growing conditions, including moderate light and well-draining soil.
