Keckiella Cordifolia

Keckiella cordifolia is a unique flowering plant native to the arid regions of western North America. It is known for its heart-shaped leaves and striking clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. This resilient species thrives in sandy soils and can withstand extreme temperatures and drought conditions. Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for xeriscaping and desert gardens. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively unknown to the general public.
