Kerria japonica f. stellata

Kerria japonica f. stellata, commonly known as the star-form kerria, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its profuse display of bright yellow, star-shaped flowers that appear in early spring. The foliage consists of dark green, ovate leaves that provide a striking contrast to the vibrant blooms. This variety is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a versatile addition to many landscapes.
