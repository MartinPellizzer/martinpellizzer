Kennedia marryattiana

Kennedia marryattiana, commonly known as the Marryatt's Kennedia, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 meter, featuring narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking bright red, pea-like flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy habitats.

Kennedia marryattiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
