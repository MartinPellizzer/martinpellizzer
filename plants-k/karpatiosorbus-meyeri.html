Karpatiosorbus meyeri

Karpatiosorbus meyeri, commonly known as the Carpathian rowan, is a deciduous shrub native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, thorny growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers in early spring, followed by red berries in the fall. Its leaves are oval-shaped with serrated edges, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

Karpatiosorbus meyeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in its native habitat.
