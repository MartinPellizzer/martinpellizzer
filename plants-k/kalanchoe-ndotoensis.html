Kalanchoe ndotoensis

Kalanchoe ndotoensis, commonly known as the snowflake plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with a waxy coating that helps it retain moisture. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and red during the winter months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Kalanchoe ndotoensis is also noted for its ability to propagate through leaf cuttings, which is a common method for propagating succulents.
