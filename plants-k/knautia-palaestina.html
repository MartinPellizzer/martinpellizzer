Knautia palaestina

Knautia palaestina, commonly known as the Palestinian knautia, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its showy, pink to purple ray florets and yellow disk florets. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and covered in fine pubescence.

Knautia palaestina is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
