Kalanchoe macrantha var. somaliensis

Kalanchoe macrantha var. somaliensis, commonly known as the Somali Kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of Somalia.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in dry conditions. The plant produces showy, orange-red flowers during the winter months, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its compact growth habit and low maintenance requirements make it suitable for both novice and experienced gardeners.

It is also valued for its ability to bloom reliably under proper care.
