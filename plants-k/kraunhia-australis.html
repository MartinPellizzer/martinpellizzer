Kraunhia australis

Kraunhia australis, commonly known as the Southern kraunhia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant features upright stems with narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink and white. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its compact size and delicate appearance.

Kraunhia australis is well adapted to coastal conditions and can tolerate salt spray and sandy environments.
