Kuruna debilis

Kuruna debilis, commonly known as the slender kuruna, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as undergrowth in dense forests. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, usually greenish-white in color, and appear in clusters.

Kuruna debilis is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits, though its botanical study remains limited.
