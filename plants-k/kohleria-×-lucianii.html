Kohleria × lucianii

Kohleria × lucianii, commonly known as the hybrid kohleria, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant features velvety, dark green foliage with a waxy texture, which adds to its ornamental appeal. Kohleria × lucianii thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings due to its preference for humid conditions.
