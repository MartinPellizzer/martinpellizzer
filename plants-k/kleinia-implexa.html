Kleinia implexa

Kleinia implexa, commonly known as the Cape marigold, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers arranged in dense, spherical clusters. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine, silvery hairs.

Kleinia implexa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
