Kalanchoe rogersii

Kalanchoe rogersii, commonly known as the Rogers' kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems and clusters of bright orange or red flowers that bloom in the spring. The leaves are thick and often have a waxy texture, which helps the plant store water. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

This plant is popular as a decorative indoor plant due to its vibrant blooms and low maintenance requirements.
