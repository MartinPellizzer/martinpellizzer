Kalanchoe schweinfurthii

Kalanchoe schweinfurthii, commonly known as the Schwerfurt kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of orange, red, and yellow, often blooming in the spring or early summer. It is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and drought tolerance.

Kalanchoe schweinfurthii is also known for its ability to propagate through offsets, making it easy to grow and propagate at home.
