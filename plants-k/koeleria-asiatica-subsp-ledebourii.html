Koeleria asiatica subsp. ledebourii

Koeleria asiatica subsp.

ledebourii, commonly known as the Tibetan feathergrass, is a low-growing perennial grass native to the high altitudes of Asia. It features slender, wiry stems and small, spike-like inflorescences that produce numerous tiny flowers.

The plant thrives in cold, arid environments and is adapted to rocky or sandy soils. Its foliage is typically bluish-green, and it is often found in alpine meadows and mountainous regions.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in harsh climates.
