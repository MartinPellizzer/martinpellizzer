Koeleria koidzumiana

Koeleria koidzumiana, commonly known as the Koidzumi's grass, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous tiny, pale blue to purple flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, arid environments.

Koeleria koidzumiana is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes at high elevations.
