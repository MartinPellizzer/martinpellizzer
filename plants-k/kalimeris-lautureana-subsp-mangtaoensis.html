Kalimeris lautureana subsp. mangtaoensis

Kalimeris lautureana subsp.

mangtaoensis, commonly known as the Mangtao bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern China. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in the summer.

The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in forested areas or mountainous regions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and branching.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
