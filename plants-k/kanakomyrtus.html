Kanakomyrtus

Kanakomyrtus, commonly known as the New Caledonia myrtle, is a flowering plant native to the island of New Caledonia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes eucalyptus and clove. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white or pink flowers and leathery, glossy leaves. Kanakomyrtus typically grows in moist, shaded environments within the tropical rainforests of New Caledonia.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique adaptations and role in the local ecosystem.
