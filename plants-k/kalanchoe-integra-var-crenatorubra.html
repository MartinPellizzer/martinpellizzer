Kalanchoe integra var. crenatorubra

Kalanchoe integra var. crenatorubra, commonly known as the red-edge kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves with a distinctive red margin, which gives it its common name. The plant produces vibrant orange to red flowers in clusters during the winter season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Kalanchoe integra var. crenatorubra is drought-tolerant and often used in rock gardens or as a container plant.
