Knautia arvensis var. longifolia

Knautia arvensis var. longifolia, commonly known as the longleaf knautia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features upright, branching stems that can grow up to 60 cm in height. The plant produces clusters of pink to purple flowers with five petals, arranged in dense, rounded inflorescences. Its leaves are lance-shaped, serrated, and slightly hairy, contributing to its ornamental value.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.
