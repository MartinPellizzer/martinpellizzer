Kalanchoe vatrinii var. intermedia

Kalanchoe vatrinii var. intermedia, commonly known as the Christmas chandelier, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a white powdery coating called farina. The plant produces clusters of vibrant orange or red flowers during the winter months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique appearance and seasonal blooming make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
