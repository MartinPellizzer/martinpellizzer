Klasea radiata subsp. biebersteiniana

Klasea radiata subsp.

biebersteiniana, commonly known as the Bieberstein's klasea, is a member of the Acanthaceae family. It is a perennial herb characterized by its upright growth habit and lance-shaped leaves.

The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink to purple in color. It is native to regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in moist, shaded environments.
