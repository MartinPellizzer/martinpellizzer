Koeleria Californica

Koeleria californica, commonly known as California needlegrass, is a hardy perennial grass native to the western United States. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils, often found in coastal and inland regions. This plant is valued for its drought tolerance and ability to stabilize soil, making it an important species in ecological restoration. Its fine, needle-like leaves and delicate seed heads make it a distinctive feature in natural landscapes. Koeleria californica plays a vital role in supporting local wildlife and maintaining ecosystem balance.
