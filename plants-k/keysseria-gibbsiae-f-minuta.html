Keysseria gibbsiae f. minuta

Keysseria gibbsiae f. minuta, commonly known as the dwarf keysseria, is a compact, low-growing plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and clusters of yellow flowers. The species is noted for its ornamental value, with its dense foliage and delicate blooms making it a popular choice for rock gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.

This plant is also valued for its resilience and ability to withstand coastal conditions.
