Koanophyllon nervosum

Koanophyllon nervosum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed blueweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in dense clusters. Native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico, this plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. It has a mat-forming growth habit and is often found in sandy or gravelly soils.

Koanophyllon nervosum is adapted to withstand drought and has a deep root system to access water from deeper soil layers.
