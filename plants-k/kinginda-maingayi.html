Kinginda maingayi

Kinginda maingayi, commonly known as the red-flowered daisy, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking red or pink daisy-like flowers. The plant has narrow, hairy leaves that help reduce water loss in its harsh environment. It produces small, wind-pollinated flowers that bloom in spring and summer.

Kinginda maingayi is often found in sandy soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
