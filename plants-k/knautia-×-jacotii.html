Knautia × jacotii

Knautia × jacotii, commonly known as the hybrid knautia, is a perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is a cross between Knautia arvensis and Knautia montana, resulting in a plant with distinctive, fluffy, pink to purple flower heads. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, featuring hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often found in meadows and rocky soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and wildflower habitats.
