Koeleria balansae

Koeleria balansae, commonly known as the Balansae needlegrass, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, spikelet-based flowers. Koeleria balansae is typically found in alpine and arctic environments, where it thrives in well-drained, rocky soils.

Its botanical features make it an important component of high-altitude ecosystems.
