Kunzea praestans

Kunzea praestans, commonly known as the Grey Kunzea, is a shrub native to the coastal regions of southern Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, silver-green leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and coastal dunes.

Kunzea praestans is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local wildlife.
