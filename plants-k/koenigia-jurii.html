Koenigia jurii

Koenigia jurii, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or pale red. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Koenigia jurii is noted for its drought tolerance and ability to form compact, low-growing mats.
