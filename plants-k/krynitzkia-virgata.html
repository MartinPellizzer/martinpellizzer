Krynitzkia virgata

Krynitzkia virgata, commonly known as the silver leaf tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray, velvety foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by yellowish-orange fruit. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

Krynitzkia virgata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticulture for its unique foliage.
