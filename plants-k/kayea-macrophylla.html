Kayea macrophylla

Kayea macrophylla, commonly known as the giant kava or kava kava, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves and clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers. The plant thrives in moist, tropical climates and is often found in coastal areas or lowland forests. Its root system is known for containing compounds used in traditional medicine.

Kayea macrophylla plays a significant role in both ecological and cultural contexts in its native regions.
