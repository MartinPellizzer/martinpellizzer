Kefersteinia graminea

Kefersteinia graminea, commonly known as the slender grass.

It is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa, characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and fine, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear minute, pale green to brown flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Kefersteinia graminea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
