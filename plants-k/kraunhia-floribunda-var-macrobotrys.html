Kraunhia floribunda var. macrobotrys

Kraunhia floribunda var. macrobotrys, commonly known as the giant flame violet, is a striking perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features large, showy flowers that are typically bright red or orange with a yellow throat, arranged in dense, terminal clusters. The plant has lance-shaped, hairy leaves that are opposite and often emit a mild fragrance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its unique appearance and vibrant blooms make it a standout in both cultivated and natural settings.
