Kairoa cromeana

Kairoa cromeana, commonly known as the New Zealand kairoa, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils and salt-laden air. It plays an important role in the local ecosystem, providing habitat and nectar for native pollinators.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species.
