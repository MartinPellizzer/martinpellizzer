Kniphofia odorata

Kniphofia odorata, commonly known as the red hot poker, is a striking perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, spire-like inflorescences that bear vivid red, orange, or yellow tubular flowers. The plant's foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are evergreen in warmer climates. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Kniphofia odorata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
