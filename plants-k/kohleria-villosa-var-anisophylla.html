Kohleria villosa var. anisophylla

Kohleria villosa var. anisophylla, commonly known as the velvet leaf kohleria, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage that gives it its common name. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, clinging to trees in the understory of humid forests. Its leaves are often variegated with shades of green, silver, and white, adding visual interest.

It produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink or white, which bloom during the warmer months.
