Kosaria tropaeolifolia

Kosaria tropaeolifolia, commonly known as the golden groundsel, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that are typically borne in clusters at the ends of its stems. The plant has finely divided, feathery leaves that give it a delicate appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and unique foliage.
