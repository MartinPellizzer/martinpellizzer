Kaurorchis evasa

Kaurorchis evasa, commonly known as the New Guinea orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of New Guinea.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome and multiple pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often arranged in loose inflorescences. Its leaves are typically broad and grass-like, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Kaurorchis evasa thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
