Kaempferia pleiantha

Kaempferia pleiantha, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant features fleshy, sword-shaped leaves and produces clusters of bright orange or yellow flowers with a distinctive red or purple lip. It thrives in humid, shady environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Kaempferia pleiantha is a pseudobulbous plant that requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture.
