Kalanchoe subrosulata

Kalanchoe subrosulata, commonly known as the Rosy Glow Kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a rosy or reddish tinge along the edges. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Kalanchoe subrosulata is often used in rock gardens or as a container plant due to its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
