Koeleria alpicola

Koeleria alpicola, commonly known as the alpine koeleria, is a flowering plant native to mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. Koeleria alpicola is an important component of alpine ecosystems, contributing to soil stabilization and providing habitat for various small organisms.
