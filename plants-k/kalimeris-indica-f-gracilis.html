Kalimeris indica f. gracilis

Kalimeris indica f. gracilis, commonly known as the slender chrysanthemum, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to China.

It features finely divided, fern-like leaves that are often covered in a soft, silvery down. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers with white centers, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, fertile soil.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its delicate appearance.
