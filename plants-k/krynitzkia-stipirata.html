Krynitzkia stipirata

Krynitzkia stipirata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved krynitzkia, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in a cluster.
