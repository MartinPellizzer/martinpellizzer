Krebsia cytisoides

Krebsia cytisoides, commonly known as the yellow flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are opposite and pinnate, with small, ovate leaflets. The plant produces bright yellow, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. Its bark is smooth and greyish, with a distinct aromatic scent.

Krebsia cytisoides is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
