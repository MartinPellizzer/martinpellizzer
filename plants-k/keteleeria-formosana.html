Keteleeria formosana

Keteleeria formosana, commonly known as the Formosan fir, is a coniferous tree native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the family Pinaceae and is characterized by its tall, straight trunk and needle-like leaves. The tree produces cones that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in mountainous regions with well-drained soil.

The Formosan fir plays an important role in the ecological systems of its native habitat.
