Koeleria philippiana

Koeleria philippiana, commonly known as the Philippine grass or Philippine koeleria, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and along stream banks.

Koeleria philippiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
