Keraudrenia × katatona

Keraudrenia × katatona, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a naturally occurring hybrid between Keraudrenia corymbosa and Keraudrenia rufescens.

It is characterized by its elegant, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant is a terrestrial orchid with a sympodial growth habit, featuring pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

This hybrid is often cultivated for its beauty and is found in various regions of Southeast Asia.
