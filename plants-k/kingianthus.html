Kingianthus

Kingianthus, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a striking tropical plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual impact. The plant has compound leaves that are often feathery in appearance, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens in warm climates.

Kingianthus is also noted for its rapid growth and ability to provide shade, making it both aesthetically pleasing and functional in landscaping.
