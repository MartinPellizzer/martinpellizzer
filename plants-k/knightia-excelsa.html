Knightia excelsa

Knightia excelsa, commonly known as the tall lily, is a striking plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a herbaceous perennial that can grow up to 3 meters in height, featuring long, narrow leaves and showy, fragrant white flowers. The plant is characterized by its tall, sturdy stem and its ability to thrive in well-drained, sandy soils. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

The flowers bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
