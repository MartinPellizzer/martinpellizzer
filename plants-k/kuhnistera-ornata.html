Kuhnistera ornata

Kuhnistera ornata, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, often blooming during the late spring and early summer. Kuhnistera ornata thrives in well-drained, acidic soil and requires a humid, shaded environment to flourish.
