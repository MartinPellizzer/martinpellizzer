Karkinetron

Karkinetron, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features vibrant orange and yellow flowers that resemble flames, blooming during the warmer months. The plant has long, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Karkinetron is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is popular in botanical gardens and private collections.
