Knema glauca var. riparia

Knema glauca var. riparia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved knema, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, glossy, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by fleshy, red or orange berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and potential ornamental use.
