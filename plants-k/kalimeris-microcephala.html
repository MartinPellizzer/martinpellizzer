Kalimeris microcephala

Kalimeris microcephala, commonly known as the small-headed aster, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture.

Kalimeris microcephala is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
