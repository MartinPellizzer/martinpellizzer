Kurkas laniflorum

Kurkas laniflorum, commonly known as the velvetflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy leaves and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Kurkas laniflorum is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique foliage and delicate blooms.

It is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
