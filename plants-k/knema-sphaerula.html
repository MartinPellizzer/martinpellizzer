Knema sphaerula

Knema sphaerula, commonly known as the small-sphere knema, is a small evergreen tree native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its dense, glossy foliage and small, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically orange to red in color when mature. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland and mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
