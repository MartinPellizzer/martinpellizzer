Kunthia pinnata

Kunthia pinnata, commonly known as the featherleaf kusha, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feathery leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that are typically yellow or white in color. Kunthia pinnata is native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa and is adapted to thrive in dry conditions.

It is often used in traditional medicine and has ecological significance in its native habitats.
