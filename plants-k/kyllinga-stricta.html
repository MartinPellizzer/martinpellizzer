Kyllinga stricta

Kyllinga stricta, commonly known as narrowleaf spurge, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a herbaceous annual with slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

Kyllinga stricta is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
