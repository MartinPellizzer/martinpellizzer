Koeleria janoensis

Koeleria janoensis, commonly known as the Janos needlegrass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico, where it thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. Koeleria janoensis is often found in disturbed soils and is used in some regions for forage by wildlife.
