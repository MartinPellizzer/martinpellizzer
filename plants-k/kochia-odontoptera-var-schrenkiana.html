Kochia odontoptera var. schrenkiana

Kochia odontoptera var. schrenkiana, commonly known as the Schrenk's kochia, is a flowering plant native to central Asia.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows in dry, sandy soils. The plant features branched stems with spiny leaves and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in clusters. It is known for its ability to thrive in arid environments and is often found in steppe and desert regions.

The species is valued for its ornamental potential and has been introduced to other parts of the world for gardening purposes.
