Karpatiosorbus dracofolia

Karpatiosorbus dracofolia, commonly known as the Dragon's Leaf barberry, is a shrub native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny branches and vibrant, dark green foliage. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in early spring, which are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and natural habitats.
