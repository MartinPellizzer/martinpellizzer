Kailarsenia campanula

Kailarsenia campanula, commonly known as the bellflower, is a flowering plant characterized by its bell-shaped blooms.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is native to various regions in Asia. The plant typically features upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers range in color from white to blue or purple, depending on the species.

Kailarsenia campanula is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
