Koeleria Oreophila Subsp. Barbatipalea

Koeleria oreophila subsp. barbatipalea is a rare and specialized grass species found in high-altitude environments. It thrives in rocky and arid regions where few plants can survive. This subspecies is distinguished by its unique foliage and seed structure. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species. Studying Koeleria oreophila subsp. barbatipalea offers insights into plant adaptation to extreme climates.
