Knowltonia rotundifolia

Knowltonia rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leafed orchid, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant features strap-like leaves that are rounded at the tips, giving it its common name. It typically blooms in late spring or early summer, producing clusters of white or pale pink flowers.

Knowltonia rotundifolia prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland habitats.
