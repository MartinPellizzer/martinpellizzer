Kalanchoe bracteata var. aurantiaca

Kalanchoe bracteata var. aurantiaca, commonly known as the orange-flowered kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture, adapted to store water. The plant produces vibrant orange flowers in clusters, which bloom in the spring and summer months. It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

The variety is distinguished by its striking color and compact growth habit.
