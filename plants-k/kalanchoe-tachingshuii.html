Kalanchoe tachingshuii

Kalanchoe tachingshuii, commonly known as the Tachingshuii Kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to China.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves that store water, typical of desert plants. The plant produces vibrant, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often blooming in winter or early spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Kalanchoe tachingshuii is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
