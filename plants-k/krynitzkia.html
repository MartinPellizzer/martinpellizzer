Krynitzkia

Krynitzkia, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to southern Africa.

These plants are characterized by their compact rosettes of velvety, fleshy leaves and showy, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of purple, white, and pink. Krynitzkia species are often cultivated as ornamental plants due to their attractive appearance and ability to thrive in indoor environments. They prefer well-draining soil and indirect sunlight, making them popular houseplants.
