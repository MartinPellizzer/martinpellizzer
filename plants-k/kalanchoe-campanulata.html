Kalanchoe campanulata

Kalanchoe campanulata, commonly known as the bell-shaped kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, typically during the winter months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Kalanchoe campanulata is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and vibrant floral display.
