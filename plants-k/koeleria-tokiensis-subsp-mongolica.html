Koeleria tokiensis subsp. mongolica

Koeleria tokiensis subsp.

mongolica, commonly known as the Mongolian Koeleria, is a flowering plant native to the steppes of Central Asia. It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit.

The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It typically thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
