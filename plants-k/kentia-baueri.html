Kentia baueri

Kentia baueri, commonly known as the Kentia palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that give it a graceful appearance. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its air-purifying properties and low maintenance requirements.

Kentia baueri is also valued for its ability to adapt to indoor conditions, making it a popular choice for both residential and commercial spaces.
