Kyllinga intricata

Kyllinga intricata, commonly known as the delicate spikegrass, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences that resemble spikes. The plant is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of water bodies. Its botanical characteristics include a fibrous root system and rhizomes that help it spread vegetatively.

Kyllinga intricata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in moist environments.
