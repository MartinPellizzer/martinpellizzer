Koeleria phleoides var. nitens

Koeleria phleoides var. nitens, commonly known as the silver hairgrass, is a perennial grass species native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-blue foliage and delicate, fluffy seed heads that give it a distinctive appearance. This plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. It has a low-growing habit and forms dense mats, which help prevent soil erosion.

The botanical features of Koeleria phleoides var. nitens make it a valuable plant for ecological restoration and ornamental use.
