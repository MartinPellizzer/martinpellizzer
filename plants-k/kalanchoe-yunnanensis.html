Kalanchoe yunnanensis

Kalanchoe yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan Kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to China.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, typically during the winter months. It is a perennial species that can grow up to 30 centimeters tall, with a compact, rosette-like growth habit.

Kalanchoe yunnanensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its resilience and aesthetic appeal.
