Kalanchoe lindmanii

Kalanchoe lindmanii, commonly known as the Lindman's kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, typically during the spring and summer months. It is a member of the Crassulaceae family, which includes many other succulent species.

Kalanchoe lindmanii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements.
