Kleinia longiflora

Kleinia longiflora, commonly known as the long-flowered ice plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a desirable plant for both ornamental and ecological purposes.
