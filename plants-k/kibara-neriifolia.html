Kibara neriifolia

Kibara neriifolia, commonly known as the black-leafed neriifolia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along riverbanks. Its botanical features include opposite leaf arrangement and a woody stem.

It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
