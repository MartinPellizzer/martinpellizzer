Knema stenocarpa

Knema stenocarpa, commonly known as the narrow-leaved knema, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland and hill forest ecosystems.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local communities.
