Kalanchoe laciniata var. brachycalyx

Kalanchoe laciniata var. brachycalyx, commonly known as the compact kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the edges, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red during the winter months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

This variety is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its resilience and ornamental value.
