Koeleria novozelandica var. pubiculmis

Koeleria novozelandica var. pubiculmis, commonly known as the pubescent New Zealand grass, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves with a hairy texture, particularly on the lower stems. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, greenish flowers.

This variety is often found in moist, open habitats such as streambanks and wetlands.
