Kyrstenia espinosarum

Kyrstenia espinosarum, commonly known as the spiny kyrstenia, is a rare and endemic plant species native to specific regions of South America.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by capsule fruits containing minute seeds. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
