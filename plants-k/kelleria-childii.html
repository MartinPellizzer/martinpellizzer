Kelleria childii

Kelleria childii, commonly known as the African starfish, is a unique succulent native to South Africa.

It features a compact, rosette-shaped growth pattern with fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces striking, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Due to its distinctive appearance and low maintenance, Kelleria childii is a popular choice for succulent collections.
