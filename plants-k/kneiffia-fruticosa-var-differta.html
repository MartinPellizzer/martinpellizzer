Kneiffia fruticosa var. differta

Kneiffia fruticosa var. differta, commonly known as the yellow-flowered kniphofia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and its striking yellow-orange inflorescences. The plant produces tall, cylindrical flower spikes that are composed of numerous small, tubular flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming a basal rosette.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and drought tolerance.
