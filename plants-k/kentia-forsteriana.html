Kentia forsteriana

Kentia forsteriana, commonly known as the Kentia palm, is a tropical evergreen plant native to the coastal regions of eastern Australia.

It features a slender, unbranched trunk that is covered in smooth, dark green bark. The plant produces feather-like, compound leaves that grow in a rosette pattern at the top of the trunk. Kentia palms are known for their ability to thrive in low-light conditions and their relatively slow growth rate.

They are popular indoor plants due to their aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
