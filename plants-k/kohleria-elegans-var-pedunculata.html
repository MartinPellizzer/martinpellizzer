Kohleria elegans var. pedunculata

Kohleria elegans var. pedunculata, commonly known as the showy kohleria, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to violet, often with contrasting yellow or white markings. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs, giving it a velvety texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to maintain its vibrant appearance.

This variety is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
