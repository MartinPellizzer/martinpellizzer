Kirganelia floribunda

Kirganelia floribunda, commonly known as the velvetleaf or velvetleaf hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, or white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a velvety texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
