Kadsura coccinea var. annamensis

Kadsura coccinea var. annamensis, commonly known as the Annam red kadsura, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is characterized by its striking red and orange flowers. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often found in moist, shaded forest environments. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
