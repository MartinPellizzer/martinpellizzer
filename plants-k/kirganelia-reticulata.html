Kirganelia reticulata

Kirganelia reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The leaves are typically oval-shaped with a finely serrated margin and a distinctive net-like vein pattern. This species thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and forested areas.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
