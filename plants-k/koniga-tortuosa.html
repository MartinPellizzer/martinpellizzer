Koniga tortuosa

Koniga tortuosa, commonly known as the twisted vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions.

It features elongated, twisting stems that allow it to climb and entwine around supports. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a glossy, dark green surface. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to create dense, lush foliage in gardens.
