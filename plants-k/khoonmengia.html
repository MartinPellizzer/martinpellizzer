Khoonmengia

Khoonmengia, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking orange to red flowers that bloom in clusters during the rainy season. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, serrated leaflets. Khoonmengia thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
