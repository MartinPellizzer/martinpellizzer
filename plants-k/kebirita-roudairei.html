Kebirita roudairei

Kebirita roudairei, commonly known as the Madagascar starfish plant, is a unique succulent native to Madagascar.

It features a compact, rosette-shaped growth pattern with fleshy, blue-green leaves that have a star-like appearance. The plant produces delicate, star-shaped flowers in shades of purple and white, typically during the warm season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its striking appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice for collectors and succulent enthusiasts.
