Kohleria deppeana

Kohleria deppeana, commonly known as the velvetleaf kohleria, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It features showy, velvety flowers that range in color from pink to purple, often with contrasting white or yellow markings. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its foliage is broad and dark green, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Kohleria deppeana thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and unique appearance.
