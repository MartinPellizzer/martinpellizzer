Kiggelaria africana var. obtusa

Kiggelaria africana var. obtusa, commonly known as the African wild fig, is a small evergreen tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Moraceae family and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and elliptical leaves with a glossy upper surface. The plant produces small, clustered flowers followed by edible fig-like fruits that are an important food source for various wildlife.
