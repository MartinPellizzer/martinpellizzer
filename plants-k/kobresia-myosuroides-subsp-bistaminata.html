Kobresia myosuroides subsp. bistaminata

Kobresia myosuroides subsp.

bistaminata, commonly known as the Tibetan feather grass, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Tibetan Plateau. It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit.

The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in cold, arid environments and is adapted to withstand strong winds and low temperatures.

This subspecies is often found in alpine meadows and plays a role in stabilizing soil in its harsh habitat.
