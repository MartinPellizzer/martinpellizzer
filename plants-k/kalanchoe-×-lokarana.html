Kalanchoe × lokarana

Kalanchoe × lokarana, commonly known as the hybrid kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that store water, enabling it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically during the winter months. It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its resilience and ornamental value.

Kalanchoe × lokarana prefers well-draining soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
