Kerria japonica var. denticulata

Kerria japonica var. denticulata, commonly known as the Japanese kerria, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features attractive, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by orange-red berries. The leaves are ovate with finely serrated edges, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental value and ease of care in garden settings.
