Kunzea rostrata

Kunzea rostrata, commonly known as narrow-leaf kunzea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes eucalyptus and tea trees. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, pale greenish-white flowers. It thrives in coastal and sandy soils, often found in heathlands and coastal dunes.

Kunzea rostrata is valued for its aromatic properties and is used in traditional bush tinctures.
