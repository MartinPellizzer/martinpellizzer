Kalbreyeracanthus

Kalbreyeracanthus, commonly known as the flame flower, is a striking tropical plant native to regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, resembling flames. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a velvety texture.

Kalbreyeracanthus thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
