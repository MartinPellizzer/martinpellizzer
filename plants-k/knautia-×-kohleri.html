Knautia × kohleri

Knautia × kohleri, commonly known as the Kohler's knautia, is a hybrid perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and features lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, loamy soils.

Knautia × kohleri is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
