Kalanchoe floripendula

Kalanchoe floripendula, commonly known as the hanging kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces clusters of bright orange or red flowers that hang downward, giving it its common name. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires plenty of sunlight.

Kalanchoe floripendula is popular as a houseplant due to its vibrant blooms and low maintenance requirements.
