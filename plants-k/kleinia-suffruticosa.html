Kleinia suffruticosa

Kleinia suffruticosa, commonly known as the firecracker plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, compact growth with fleshy, ribbed stems and leaves that are typically blue-gray in color. The plant produces striking orange-red flowers in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Kleinia suffruticosa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
