Kalanchoe ambolensis

Kalanchoe ambolensis, commonly known as the Madagascar window leaf, is a succulent plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture in its arid environment. The plant produces vibrant orange or yellow flowers during the cooler months, usually in winter. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Kalanchoe ambolensis is a popular ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and ease of care.
