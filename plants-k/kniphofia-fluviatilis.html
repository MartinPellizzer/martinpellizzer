Kniphofia fluviatilis

Kniphofia fluviatilis, commonly known as the river red hot poker, is a striking perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, sturdy stems that bear bright red or orange-red flowers arranged in dense, torch-like inflorescences. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Kniphofia fluviatilis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and birds.
