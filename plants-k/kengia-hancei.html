Kengia hancei

Kengia hancei, commonly known as the African flame plant, is a striking evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features striking red, orange, and yellow flowers that bloom in late spring and early summer. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with dark green, glossy leaves. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil.

Kengia hancei is popular in ornamental gardening for its vibrant floral displays and unique appearance.
