Kalimeris alyssoides

Kalimeris alyssoides, commonly known as the narrowleaf fleabane, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white to pale pink petals. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It has finely divided, gray-green leaves that are often covered in a fuzzy layer.
