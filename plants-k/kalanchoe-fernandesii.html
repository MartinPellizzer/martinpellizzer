Kalanchoe fernandesii

Kalanchoe fernandesii, commonly known as the Cape coral or flame plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple hues. The plant produces clusters of bright orange or yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Kalanchoe fernandesii is drought-tolerant and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance.
