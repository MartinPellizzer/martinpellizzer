Kalanchoe crenata var. verea

Kalanchoe crenata var. verea, commonly known as the silver arrow or silver edge kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves with a distinctive silver or white margin, which helps reflect sunlight and reduce water loss. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow during the winter months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Kalanchoe crenata var. verea is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
