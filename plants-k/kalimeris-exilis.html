Kalimeris exilis

Kalimeris exilis, commonly known as the slender aster, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with pale yellow to white petals. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or grassland habitats. It produces small, elongated leaves and has a compact, herbaceous growth habit.

Kalimeris exilis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
