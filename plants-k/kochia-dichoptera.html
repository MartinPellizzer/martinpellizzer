Kochia dichoptera

Kochia dichoptera, commonly known as the two-lobed kochia, is an annual flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, triangular-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy bracts that resemble flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, disturbed soils and is often found in prairies and along roadsides.
