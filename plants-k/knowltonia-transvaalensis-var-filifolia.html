Knowltonia transvaalensis var. filifolia

Knowltonia transvaalensis var. filifolia, commonly known as the Filifoliate Natal Lily, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, showy white to pale pink flowers. The leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette at the base, while the inflorescence consists of a single spike of flowers. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
