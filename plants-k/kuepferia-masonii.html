Kuepferia masonii

Kuepferia masonii, commonly known as the Mason's kuepferia, is a rare and striking plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage and clusters of small, showy blue or purple flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it well-suited for rock gardens or xeriscaping. Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a sought-after species among succulent enthusiasts.

Kuepferia masonii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local pollinators.
