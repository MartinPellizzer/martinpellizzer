Kochia cannonii

Kochia cannonii, commonly known as the silverleaf kochia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage and vibrant red or orange flowers that appear in late summer. The plant is often found in dry, rocky soils and thrives in arid environments. Kochia cannonii is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and is known for its drought tolerance.
