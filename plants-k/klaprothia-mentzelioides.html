Klaprothia mentzelioides

Klaprothia mentzelioides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved klaprothia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun exposure. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are small and yellow.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
