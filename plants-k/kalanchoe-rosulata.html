Kalanchoe rosulata

Kalanchoe rosulata, commonly known as the Rosulate Kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped growth pattern, with thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, typically in the winter months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.

Its ability to store water in its leaves allows it to survive in arid conditions.
