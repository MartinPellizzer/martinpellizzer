Krigia Biflora Var. Viridis

Krigia biflora var. viridis is a rare and striking plant native to the arid regions of western North America. Known for its vibrant green foliage and delicate yellow flowers, it thrives in harsh, sun-drenched environments. This variant of Krigia biflora is particularly prized for its unique coloration and resilience. Its small stature and compact growth make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Due to habitat loss, it is now considered a threatened species requiring conservation efforts.
