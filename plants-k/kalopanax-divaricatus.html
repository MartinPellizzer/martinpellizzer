Kalopanax divaricatus

Kalopanax divaricatus, commonly known as the Chinese cowitch or Japanese cowitch, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Araliaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves with a distinctive, serrated margin. Kalopanax divaricatus is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
