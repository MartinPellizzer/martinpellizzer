Koenigia fertilis

Koenigia fertilis, commonly known as the fertilized koenigia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its compact, succulent growth habit. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Koenigia fertilis is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
