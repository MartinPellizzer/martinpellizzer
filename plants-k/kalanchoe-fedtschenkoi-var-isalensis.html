Kalanchoe fedtschenkoi var. isalensis

Kalanchoe fedtschenkoi var. isalensis, commonly known as the Isalensis Kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to Madagascar.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in the spring. It is a member of the Crassulaceae family and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant appearance and ease of care.

The species is also noted for its ability to propagate through stem cuttings, making it a popular choice for gardeners and plant enthusiasts.
