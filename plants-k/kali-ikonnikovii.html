Kali ikonnikovii

Kali ikonnikovii, commonly known as the Ikonnikov's kali, is a species of flowering plant native to specific regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing adaptations to drought conditions. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in a fine layer of hairs, which helps reduce water loss.

The flowers, which can be pink or red, are clustered in dense panicles and attract various pollinators.
