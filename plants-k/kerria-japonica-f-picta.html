Kerria japonica f. picta

Kerria japonica f. picta, commonly known as the variegated Japanese kerria, is a hybrid shrub native to Japan.

It features striking foliage with variegated leaves that display shades of white, yellow, and green, adding visual interest to gardens. The plant produces clusters of bright orange-yellow flowers in early spring, which are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, moist soil.

This ornamental plant is valued for its attractive foliage and is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal.
