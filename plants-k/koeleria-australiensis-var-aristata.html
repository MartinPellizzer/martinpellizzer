Koeleria australiensis var. aristata

Koeleria australiensis var. aristata, commonly known as the Australian koeleria, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the grass family, Poaceae, and is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It typically thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
