Kniphofia stricta

Kniphofia stricta, commonly known as the red hot poker, is a striking perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems that bear clusters of vivid red, orange, or yellow tubular flowers, which are arranged in spike-like inflorescences. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically evergreen, providing a lush backdrop to its showy blooms. Kniphofia stricta thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its unique appearance and long flowering period make it a standout addition to any garden.
