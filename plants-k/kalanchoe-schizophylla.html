Kalanchoe schizophylla

Kalanchoe schizophylla, commonly known as the leafy kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tinge. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

Kalanchoe schizophylla is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its resilience and attractive blooms.
