Kalanchoe citrina var. erythreae

Kalanchoe citrina var. erythreae, commonly known as the red-flowered kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often blooming in winter. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright sunlight to flourish.

This variety is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of care.
