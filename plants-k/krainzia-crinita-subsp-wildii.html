Krainzia crinita subsp. wildii

Krainzia crinita subsp.

wildii, commonly known as the Tufted Krainzia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive tufted foliage and showy yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun exposure. It is known for its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in arid environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
