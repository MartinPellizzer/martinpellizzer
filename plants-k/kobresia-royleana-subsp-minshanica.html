Kobresia royleana subsp. minshanica

Kobresia royleana subsp.

minshanica, commonly known as the Minshan Kobresia, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the high-altitude regions of China. It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit.

The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.

This subspecies is often found in alpine meadows and is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil in mountainous regions.
