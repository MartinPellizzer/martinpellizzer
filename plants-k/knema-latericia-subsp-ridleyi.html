Knema latericia subsp. ridleyi

Knema latericia subsp.

ridleyi, commonly known as the ridley's knema, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia. It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves with a smooth, glossy surface.

The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, black seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in tropical gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
