Knautia longifolia var. aurea

Knautia longifolia var. aurea, commonly known as the golden knautia, is a perennial flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It features showy, fluffy clusters of yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, upright stems covered in soft, hairy foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its nectar-rich flowers attract bees and other pollinators, contributing to its ecological value.
