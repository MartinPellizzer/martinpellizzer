Kurrimia paniculata

Kurrimia paniculata, commonly known as the white flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in panicles, giving it a fiery appearance. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical or ovate in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Kurrimia paniculata is also known for its rapid growth and ability to tolerate various soil types.
