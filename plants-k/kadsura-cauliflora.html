Kadsura cauliflora

Kadsura cauliflora, commonly known as the white kadsura, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, white flowers that bloom along the stems. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically yellow or red when mature. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
