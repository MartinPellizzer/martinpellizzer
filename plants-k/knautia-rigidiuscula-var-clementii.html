Knautia rigidiuscula var. clementii

Knautia rigidiuscula var. clementii, commonly known as the Alpine knautia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environmental conditions. The plant features upright stems covered in soft, hairy foliage, with clusters of pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its botanical characteristics include small, toothed leaves and a compact growth habit, making it a distinctive member of the Caprifoliaceae family.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and ecological importance in alpine ecosystems.
