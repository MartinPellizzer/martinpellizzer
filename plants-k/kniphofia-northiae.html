Kniphofia northiae

Kniphofia northiae, commonly known as the Orange Fire Lily, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Hemerocallidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant orange-red tubular flowers that bloom in summer. The plant features tall, grass-like leaves and produces showy inflorescences that attract pollinators such as bees and birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
