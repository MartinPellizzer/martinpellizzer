Kengyilia laxiuscula

Kengyilia laxiuscula, commonly known as the Chinese aster, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is known for its showy, purple to pink flowers. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Kengyilia laxiuscula is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
