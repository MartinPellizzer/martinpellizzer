Kunzea micrantha subsp. micrantha

Kunzea micrantha subsp.

micrantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaf kunzea, is a shrub native to the coastal regions of southern Australia. It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, white to pale pink flowers that appear in clusters.

The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to coastal conditions, including salt spray and strong winds. It is often found in heathlands and coastal dunes, contributing to the biodiversity of these ecosystems.

The species is also valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
