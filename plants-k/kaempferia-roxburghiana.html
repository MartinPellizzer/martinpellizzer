Kaempferia roxburghiana

Kaempferia roxburghiana, commonly known as the flame orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers that range from red to orange and yellow. The plant typically grows as a rhizomatous herb, with fleshy leaves and a compact, pseudobulb-like structure. Its flowers are borne on erect spikes and often have a distinctive, spurred shape.

Kaempferia roxburghiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
