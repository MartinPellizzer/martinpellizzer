Kalosanthes bicolor

Kalosanthes bicolor, commonly known as the two-colored orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its strikingly contrasting colors, with green and white petals that create a visually appealing display. It typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments.

Kalosanthes bicolor is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its role in traditional medicine in some regions.
