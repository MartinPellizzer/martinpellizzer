Koeleria atroviolacea var. tsinghaica

Koeleria atroviolacea var. tsinghaica, commonly known as the dark violet koeleria, is a perennial grass species native to eastern China.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, violet-colored flowers that bloom in late summer, adding a striking visual element to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological benefits in native plant gardens.
