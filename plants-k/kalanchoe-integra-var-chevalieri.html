Kalanchoe integra var. chevalieri

Kalanchoe integra var. chevalieri, commonly known as the Cape Town beauty, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a reddish or purple tint, especially under stress or in cooler conditions. The plant produces vibrant orange or yellow flowers in clusters during the winter months. It is drought-tolerant and prefers well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
