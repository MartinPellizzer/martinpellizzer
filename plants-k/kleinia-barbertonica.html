Kleinia barbertonica

Kleinia barbertonica, commonly known as the Cape tulip, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy foliage that is typically green with red or purple markings. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in winter. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a striking addition to arid landscapes.
