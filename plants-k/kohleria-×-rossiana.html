Kohleria × rossiana

Kohleria × rossiana, commonly known as the rossiana kohleria, is a hybrid plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features velvety, dark green leaves with a slightly waxy texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Kohleria × rossiana is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
