Kopsia griffithii var. griffithii

Kopsia griffithii var. griffithii, commonly known as the Griffith's kopsia, is a flowering plant native to the eastern Himalayas.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are usually pink or red in color. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas at elevations between 1,500 and 3,000 meters.
