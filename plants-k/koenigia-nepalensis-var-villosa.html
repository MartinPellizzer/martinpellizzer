Koenigia nepalensis var. villosa

Koenigia nepalensis var. villosa, commonly known as the hairy koenigia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Nepal and northern India.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it its varietal name "villosa.". The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red, adding a splash of color to its natural habitat. It thrives in rocky and arid environments, often growing at high altitudes.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in rock gardens and alpine settings.
