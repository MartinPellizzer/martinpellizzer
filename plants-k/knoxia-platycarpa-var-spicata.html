Knoxia platycarpa var. spicata

Knoxia platycarpa var. spicata, commonly known as the spicate knoxia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, spiky inflorescence. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish to orange flowers arranged in a dense, spike-like cluster. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its unique appearance, it is occasionally cultivated for ornamental purposes.
