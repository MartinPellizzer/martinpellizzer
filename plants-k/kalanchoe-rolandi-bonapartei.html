Kalanchoe rolandi-bonapartei

Kalanchoe rolandi-bonapartei, commonly known as the Cape Town Kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems and clusters of vibrant orange to red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has thick, waxy leaves that store water, enabling it to thrive in arid conditions. It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its striking appearance and ease of care.

Kalanchoe rolandi-bonapartei is well-suited for rock gardens and containers, making it a popular choice for gardeners in dry climates.
