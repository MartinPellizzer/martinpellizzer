Katapsuxis silaifolia var. orientalis

Katapsuxis silaifolia var. orientalis, commonly known as the Eastern Silaifol.

This variant is a flowering plant native to specific regions in the eastern Mediterranean. It features narrow, elongated leaves with a distinctive silvery underside. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink.

It is typically found in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in sunny, arid environments.
