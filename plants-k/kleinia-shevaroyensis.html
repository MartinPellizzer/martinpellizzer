Kleinia shevaroyensis

Kleinia shevaroyensis, commonly known as the Shevaroy hills dwarf daisies, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun conditions. It produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom during the summer months.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
