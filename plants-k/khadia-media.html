Khadia media

Khadia media, commonly known as the narrow-leafed knapweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb with a basal rosette of narrow, gray-green leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or disturbed areas.

Khadia media is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
