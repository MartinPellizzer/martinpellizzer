Kalanchoe guignardii var. schistosepala

Kalanchoe guignardii var. schistosepala, commonly known as the rock-kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems covered in a distinctive powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces clusters of bright orange to yellow flowers during the winter months, adding vibrant color to its habitat. Its leaves are thick and somewhat waxy, adapted for storing moisture.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and ease of care.
