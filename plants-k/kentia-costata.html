Kentia costata

Kentia costata, commonly known as the Costate Kentia, is a species of cycad native to New Caledonia.

It is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk and its distinctive, feather-like leaves that are arranged in a tight spiral. The leaves are long and narrow, with a waxy texture and a dark green color. This plant is known for its slow growth and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and air-purifying properties.
