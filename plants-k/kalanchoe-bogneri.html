Kalanchoe bogneri

Kalanchoe bogneri, commonly known as the Bogner's kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, typical of many succulents. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Kalanchoe bogneri is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
