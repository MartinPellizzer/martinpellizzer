Kerneria pilosa var. radiata

Kerneria pilosa var. radiata, commonly known as the hairy kerneria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its hairy, glandular stems and leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.
