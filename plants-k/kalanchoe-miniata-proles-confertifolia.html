Kalanchoe miniata proles confertifolia

Kalanchoe miniata proles confertifolia, commonly known as the compact kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings, and it produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant is known for its compact growth habit, making it suitable for container gardening. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate sunlight, with minimal water during its dormant period.

This species is also valued for its ability to propagate through offsets, enhancing its ornamental appeal.
