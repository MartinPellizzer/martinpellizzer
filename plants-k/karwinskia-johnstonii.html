Karwinskia johnstonii

Karwinskia johnstonii, commonly known as the Johnston's karwinskia, is a rare shrub native to the coastal regions of California and Baja California.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, clustered white flowers that bloom in the spring.
