Kleinia acaulis var. ecklonis

Kleinia acaulis var. ecklonis, commonly known as the Cape tulip, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, compact growth with fleshy, cylindrical stems that are often red or purple in color. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its striking appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
