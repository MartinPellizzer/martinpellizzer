Kurziodendron viridissimum

Kurziodendron viridissimum, commonly known as the greenleaf kurziodendron, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a waxy texture, which helps it retain moisture in its native habitat.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
