Kentia oleracea

Kentia oleracea, commonly known as the Kentia palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and feathery, pinnate leaves that give it a graceful appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by dark blue berries, which are edible but not commonly consumed by humans. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Kentia oleracea is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
