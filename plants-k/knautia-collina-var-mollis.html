Knautia collina var. mollis

Knautia collina var. mollis, commonly known as the soft knautia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant features soft, hairy leaves and produces clusters of pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers sunny to partially shaded locations.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and meadows.
