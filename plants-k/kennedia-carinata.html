Kennedia carinata

Kennedia carinata, commonly known as the mountain holly or yellow holly, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in coastal or mountainous regions. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly serrated edge.

Kennedia carinata is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
