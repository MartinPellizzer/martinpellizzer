Kalanchoe winteri

Kalanchoe winteri, commonly known as the Christmas chandelier, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are fleshy and covered in a thin, waxy coating. The plant produces clusters of bright orange or yellow flowers during the winter months, which is unusual for many succulents. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Kalanchoe winteri is popular as a decorative plant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
