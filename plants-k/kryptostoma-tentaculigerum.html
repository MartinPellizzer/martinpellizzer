Kryptostoma tentaculigerum

Kryptostoma tentaculigerum, commonly known as the tentacle-bearing kryptostoma, is a species of sea anemone found in shallow tropical waters.

It belongs to the order Actinioidea and is characterized by its elongated, slender body and numerous tentacles surrounding its oral disc. The organism exhibits a simple yet effective structure for capturing prey, with its tentacles equipped with nematocysts for defense and feeding. It typically attaches itself to substrates such as coral or rock using a basal disc.

Kryptostoma tentaculigerum plays a role in marine ecosystems as both a predator and a prey species.
