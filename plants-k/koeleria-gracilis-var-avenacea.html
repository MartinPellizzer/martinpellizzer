Koeleria gracilis var. avenacea

Koeleria gracilis var. avenacea, commonly known as slender wild oat, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its fine, hair-like leaves and small, spike-like inflorescences. This variety typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as fields and roadsides. It produces small, wind-dispersed seeds that contribute to its ecological role in soil stabilization.

The plant is often used in restoration projects due to its hardy nature and ability to thrive in various soil conditions.
