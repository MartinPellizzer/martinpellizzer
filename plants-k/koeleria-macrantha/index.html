Koeleria Macrantha

Koeleria macrantha is a hardy grass species native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America. It thrives in rocky soils and is often found in desert environments where water is scarce. This plant is known for its tall, slender stems and small, blue-green leaves that help it conserve moisture. Koeleria macrantha is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion in harsh landscapes. Its resilience makes it an important part of native plant ecosystems.
