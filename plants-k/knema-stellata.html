Knema stellata

Knema stellata, commonly known as the star-knema, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by spherical, woody fruits containing numerous seeds. Knema stellata is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.

Its growth habit and ecological role make it an important species in tropical ecosystems.
