Knowltonia transvaalensis var. transvaalensis

Knowltonia transvaalensis var. transvaalensis, commonly known as the Transvaal orchid, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, pendulous flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.
