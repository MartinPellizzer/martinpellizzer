Kosteletzkya Macrantha F. Lignosa

Kosteletzkya macrantha f. lignosa is a unique variant of the Kosteletzkya macrantha species, known for its distinctive woody growth habit. This plant thrives in coastal and sandy environments, often found along the Atlantic and Gulf coasts of the United States. Its robust structure and adaptability make it a resilient plant in harsh, saline conditions. The plant features showy yellow flowers that bloom in summer, adding visual interest to its natural habitat. Despite its hardy nature, it remains a relatively rare and underappreciated member of the Malvaceae family.
