Koelpinia linearis var. filiformis

Koelpinia linearis var. filiformis, commonly known as the slender koelpinia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, clustered inflorescences. The plant produces delicate, often pale yellow or greenish flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is occasionally cultivated by gardeners interested in native flora.
