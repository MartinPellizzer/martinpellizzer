Klugia notoniana var. scabra

Klugia notoniana var. scabra, commonly known as the rough klugia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its rough, hairy stems and leaves, which give it its varietal name "scabra.". The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or sandy environments.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in ornamental gardening.
