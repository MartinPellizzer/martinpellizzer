Kunzea juniperoides subsp. juniperoides

Kunzea juniperoides subsp.

juniperoides, commonly known as silver kunzea, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia. It is characterized by its aromatic, needle-like leaves that are typically silvery-gray in color.

The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and heathland environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential use in horticulture.
