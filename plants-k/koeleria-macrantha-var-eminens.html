Koeleria macrantha var. eminens

Koeleria macrantha var. eminens, commonly known as the alpine feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, feathery panicles of pale blue to purple flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in harsh environments.

Koeleria macrantha var. eminens is also noted for its low maintenance requirements and adaptability to cold climates.
