Kalanchoe spathulata var. dixoniana

Kalanchoe spathulata var. dixoniana, commonly known as the Cape Town beauty, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, dark green leaves that are slightly pointed and arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and orange, typically blooming in winter. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light.

This variety is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal.
