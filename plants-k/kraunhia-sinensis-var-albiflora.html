Kraunhia sinensis var. albiflora

Kraunhia sinensis var. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered Chinese bellflower, is a perennial herb native to China.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of small, white, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments and thrives in well-drained soil. Its foliage consists of lance-shaped leaves with a smooth texture.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
