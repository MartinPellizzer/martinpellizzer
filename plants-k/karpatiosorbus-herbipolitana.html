Karpatiosorbus herbipolitana

Karpatiosorbus herbipolitana, commonly known as the Carpathian hawthorn, is a shrub native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, thorny growth habit. The plant produces small, white flowers in spring, followed by red berries in autumn. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with a smooth margin and a slightly serrated edge.

Karpatiosorbus herbipolitana is adapted to rocky and forested habitats, thriving in temperate climates.
