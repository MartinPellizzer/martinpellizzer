Kniphofia schlechteri

Kniphofia schlechteri, commonly known as the red-hot poker, is a striking perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the genus Kniphofia, which is part of the grass family, Poaceae. The plant is characterized by its tall, erect stems and dense clusters of bright orange-red tubular flowers. Its foliage consists of long, narrow, grass-like leaves that are evergreen.
