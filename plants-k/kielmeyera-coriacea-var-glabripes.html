Kielmeyera coriacea var. glabripes

Kielmeyera coriacea var. glabripes, commonly known as the smooth-leaved kielmeyera, is a species native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant is characterized by its thick, leathery leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in yellow or greenish hues, and it produces woody capsules as fruit.

The plant is valued for its hardy nature and is sometimes used in ornamental horticulture.
