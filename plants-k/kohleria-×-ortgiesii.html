Kohleria × ortgiesii

Kohleria × ortgiesii, commonly known as the orchid bell, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its foliage consists of dark green, velvety leaves that are often used in floral arrangements.

Kohleria × ortgiesii is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home environment.
