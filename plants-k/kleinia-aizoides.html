Kleinia aizoides

Kleinia aizoides, commonly known as the Aizooides carpet plant, is a succulent species native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, commonly referred to as the ice plant family, and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features small, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white wax, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, appearing in shades of yellow, orange, or red, depending on the variety.

Kleinia aizoides is well adapted to rocky and sandy soils, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
