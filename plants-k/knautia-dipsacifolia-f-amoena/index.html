Knautia Dipsacifolia F. Amoena

Amoena knautia, scientifically known as Knautia dipsacifolia f. amoena, is a striking perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans. It is celebrated for its showy, pink to purple flower clusters that bloom in late spring and early summer. This cultivar is a popular choice among gardeners for its vibrant color and hardy nature. Its foliage is dark green and finely divided, adding texture to any garden setting. With its elegant appearance and adaptability, Amoena knautia is a valuable addition to both wild and cultivated landscapes.
