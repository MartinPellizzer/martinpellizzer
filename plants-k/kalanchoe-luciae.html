Kalanchoe luciae

Kalanchoe luciae, commonly known as the Christmas chandelier, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, branching structure and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in winter. The leaves are thick, fleshy, and often have a waxy coating, typical of succulents. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
