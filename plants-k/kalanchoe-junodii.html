Kalanchoe junodii

Kalanchoe junodii, commonly known as the Cape York Kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powder, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires plenty of sunlight.

Kalanchoe junodii is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
