Kali tragus subsp. ponticum

Kali tragus subsp.

ponticum, commonly known as the Pontic goatweed, is a flowering plant native to the eastern Mediterranean region. It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and bristly foliage.

The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in dry, sunny environments.
