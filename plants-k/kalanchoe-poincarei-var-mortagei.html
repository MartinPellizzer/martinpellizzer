Kalanchoe poincarei var. mortagei

Kalanchoe poincarei var. mortagei, commonly known as the Christmas chandelier, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that form a rosette structure. The plant produces vibrant orange to red flowers during the winter months, which bloom in pendulous clusters. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and rock gardens.
