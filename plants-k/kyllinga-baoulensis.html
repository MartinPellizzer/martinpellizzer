Kyllinga baoulensis

Kyllinga baoulensis, commonly known as African arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant features grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikelets that are often mistaken for grass. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.
