Keetia venosa

Keetia venosa, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical shrub belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its rough, velvety leaves that are typically green with a distinctive mottled appearance. It is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and forest edges due to its adaptability and rapid growth.
