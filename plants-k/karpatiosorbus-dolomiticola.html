Karpatiosorbus dolomiticola

Karpatiosorbus dolomiticola, commonly known as the Dolomite Sorbus, is a rare shrub native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant features small, white flowers that appear in early summer, followed by red or orange berries. It thrives in calcareous soils and is adapted to the harsh, mountainous environment.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species.
