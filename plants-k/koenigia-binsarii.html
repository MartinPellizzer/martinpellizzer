Koenigia binsarii

Koenigia binsarii, commonly known as the Armenian carpet grass, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Armenia and Turkey.

It features dense, mat-forming foliage with small, grass-like leaves that are typically silver-green in color. The plant produces delicate, pinkish-white flowers in early summer, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Koenigia binsarii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in harsh, arid environments.
