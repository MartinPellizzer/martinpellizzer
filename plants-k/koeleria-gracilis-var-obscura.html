Koeleria gracilis var. obscura

Koeleria gracilis var. obscura, commonly known as the dark koeleria, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It is a perennial grass that typically grows in open, rocky habitats at high elevations. The plant features slender, erect stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often tinged with a dark green color. Its flowers are small, spike-like inflorescences that produce pale blue or purple florets.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
