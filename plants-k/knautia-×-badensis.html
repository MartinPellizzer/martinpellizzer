Knautia × badensis

Knautia × badensis, commonly known as the hybrid knautia, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the family Caprifoliaceae.

It is a natural hybrid between Knautia arctica and Knautia macedonica, resulting in a plant with distinctive, showy flowers. The species is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of pink to purple, tubular flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, Knautia × badensis is appreciated by gardeners for its beauty and ease of cultivation.
