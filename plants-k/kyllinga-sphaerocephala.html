Kyllinga sphaerocephala

Kyllinga sphaerocephala, commonly known as the globe kyllinga, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features succulent, grass-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, spherical inflorescences that are typically pink or red, adding visual interest. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along water edges.

Kyllinga sphaerocephala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping or as a ground cover.
