Knautia arvensis var. cardonica

Knautia arvensis var. cardonica, commonly known as the Cardoon Knautia, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, sunny grasslands and rocky soils. The plant features upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of pink or purple flowers in late summer. Its flowers are composed of many small, petal-like florets arranged in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family.
