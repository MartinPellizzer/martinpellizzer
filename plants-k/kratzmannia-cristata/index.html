Kratzmannia Cristata

Kratzmannia cristata is a striking succulent native to southern Africa. It is known for its unique, spiky appearance and vibrant green foliage. This plant thrives in arid environments and requires minimal water to survive. Its name reflects its distinctive crown-like shape and rugged beauty. It is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts seeking both ornamental value and resilience.
