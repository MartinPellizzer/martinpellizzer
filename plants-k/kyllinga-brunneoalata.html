Kyllinga brunneoalata

Kyllinga brunneoalata, commonly known as the purple-flowered kyllinga, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features upright, grass-like foliage that is often tinged with purple or reddish hues. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with delicate, pink to purple flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along water edges.

Kyllinga brunneoalata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
