Keyserlingia hortensis var. hydaspidis

Keyserlingia hortensis var. hydaspidis, commonly known as the water hawthorn, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling hawthorn blossoms. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, making it a popular choice for aquatic and bog gardens. Its leaves are small, oval, and glossy, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

This variety is valued for its aesthetic qualities and ability to enhance wetland habitats.
