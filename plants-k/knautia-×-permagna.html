Knautia × permagna

Knautia × permagna, commonly known as the giant knautia, is a hybrid perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and large, showy flower heads that range in color from pink to purple. The plant produces numerous ray florets surrounding a central disc, typical of the Asteraceae family. Its foliage consists of serrated, gray-green leaves that are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

Knautia × permagna is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
