Kalopanax ricinifolius var. maximowiczii

Kalopanax ricinifolius var. maximowiczii, commonly known as the giant arrowhead plant, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in umbellate clusters, followed by spiny, berry-like fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its unique foliage and ecological significance make it a notable species in its native habitats.
