Kelleronia revoilii var. macropetala

Kelleronia revoilii var. macropetala, commonly known as the giant kelleronia, is a striking member of the Araceae family native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescence, which consists of a spadix surrounded by a broad, colorful spathe. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that can grow up to several feet in length, contributing to its dramatic appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high humidity and well-draining soil to flourish.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and exotic ornamental plant.
