Keithia nitida

Keithia nitida, commonly known as the silver fern, is a species of flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its distinctive silvery-white leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Keithia nitida thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it a notable species in New Zealand's botanical landscape.
