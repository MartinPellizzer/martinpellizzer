Kadua Elatior

Kadua elatior is a striking plant native to the high altitudes of Madagascar. It is known for its vibrant purple and white flowers that bloom during the dry season. This species belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes many ornamental and medicinal plants. Its unique appearance and habitat make it a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists.
