Kalanchoe millotii

Kalanchoe millotii, commonly known as the millot's kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, pink, and yellow, typically during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are covered in a dense layer of fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Kalanchoe millotii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements.
