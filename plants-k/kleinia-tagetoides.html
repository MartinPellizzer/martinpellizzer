Kleinia tagetoides

Kleinia tagetoides, commonly known as the Cape marigold, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that are often tinged with red or purple hues. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers in late summer, which are clustered in dense, showy inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow, slightly hairy, and adapted to store water, reflecting its drought-tolerant nature.

Kleinia tagetoides thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
