Kernera sagittata

Kernera sagittata, commonly known as the arrowleaf kernera, is a herbaceous perennial native to Australia.

It features slender, upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that have a distinctive arrowhead shape. The plant produces small, yellow flowers clustered in dense, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or grasslands.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to native gardens and ecological restoration projects.
