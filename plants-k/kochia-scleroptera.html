Kochia scleroptera

Kochia scleroptera, commonly known as burning bush, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red, orange, or purple foliage that becomes more pronounced in the fall. Kochia scleroptera is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking seasonal color changes.
