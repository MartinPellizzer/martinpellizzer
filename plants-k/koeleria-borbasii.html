Koeleria borbasii

Koeleria borbasii, commonly known as the Borbasii grass, is a perennial grass species native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as steppes and rocky areas. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a blue-green coloration.

Koeleria borbasii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soils in arid environments.
