Knautia Bidens

Knautia bidens is a charming flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It is known for its showy, daisy-like blooms that add vibrant color to gardens and wild landscapes. This species thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. Its delicate appearance belies its hardy nature, allowing it to flourish in a variety of climates. With its historical use in herbal medicine, Knautia bidens holds both aesthetic and practical value.
