Kleinia linifolia

Kleinia linifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf daisies, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is drought-tolerant, making it well-suited for arid environments. Kleinia linifolia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and hardy nature.
