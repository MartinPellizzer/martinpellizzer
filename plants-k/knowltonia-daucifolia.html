Knowltonia daucifolia

Knowltonia daucifolia, commonly known as the purple coneflower, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, violet-purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces fluffy, seed-filled cones that disperse its seeds by wind. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and ecologically valuable.
