Kashmiria

Kashmiria, commonly known as the Kashmir rose, is a species of flowering plant native to the Kashmir region of South Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pink or white flowers that bloom in the spring and summer seasons. The plant typically grows in mountainous areas with cool climates and well-drained soil. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

The Kashmir rose is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its historical and cultural significance in the region.
