Kohleria rugata

Kohleria rugata, commonly known as the velvetleaf kohleria, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with a contrasting throat. It thrives in humid, shady environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
