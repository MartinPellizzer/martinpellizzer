Kita

Kita, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a water plant native to Asia.

It has large, showy flowers that bloom on long, winding stems above the water surface. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that float on the water's surface. Its roots grow in the muddy bottom of ponds and lakes.

The lotus is known for its ability to purify water and is often associated with spiritual symbolism in many cultures.
