Kalopanax autumnalis

Kalopanax autumnalis, commonly known as the autumnalis or the Chinese sweetgum, is a deciduous tree native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its palmate leaves that turn vibrant yellow in the fall, adding ornamental value to its landscape. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in early spring, followed by spherical, spiny fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it adaptable to various garden settings.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture and ecological restoration projects.
