Kochia pyramidata var. lobostoma

Kochia pyramidata var. lobostoma, commonly known as the silverleaf kochia, is a flowering plant in the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking silvery-blue foliage, which becomes more pronounced as the plant matures. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that appear in dense, spike-like clusters during the summer months. Native to the western United States, it thrives in arid and semi-arid environments.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
