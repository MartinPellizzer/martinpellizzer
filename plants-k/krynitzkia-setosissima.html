Krynitzkia setosissima

Krynitzkia setosissima, commonly known as the woolly krynitzkia, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and small, fragrant yellow flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Krynitzkia setosissima is also valued for its resilience and low maintenance requirements.
