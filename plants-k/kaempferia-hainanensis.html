Kaempferia hainanensis

Kaempferia hainanensis, commonly known as the Hainan ginger orchid, is a tropical plant native to China's Hainan Island.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and showy, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of bright yellow or orange flowers with intricate patterns, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a decorative plant.
