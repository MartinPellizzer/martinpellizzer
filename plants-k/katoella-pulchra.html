Katoella pulchra

Katoella pulchra, commonly known as the red-flowered katoella, is a striking member of the Proteaceae family native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers, which are arranged in dense, spherical inflorescences. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powder, giving it a unique appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
