Kalabotis

Kalabotis, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a popular flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the genus Schlumbergera and is characterized by its segmented, leaf-like stems that resemble leaves. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, pink, and white during the holiday season, typically between November and January. Kalabotis thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is a tropical plant that prefers cooler temperatures and moderate watering.
