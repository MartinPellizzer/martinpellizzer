Kibara vrieseana

Kibara vrieseana, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with bold white and green stripes on its lance-shaped leaves. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a favored ornamental plant.
