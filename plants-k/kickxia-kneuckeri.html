Kickxia kneuckeri

Kickxia kneuckeri, commonly known as the narrowleaf lily, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Central Asia.

It features slender, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
