Kniphofia neumanii

Kniphofia neumanii, commonly known as the red hot poker, is a striking perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, sword-shaped leaves and produces showy, spike-like flower clusters in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant is part of the Hemerocallidaceae family and is known for its long-lasting blooms that last several weeks. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated in gardens around the world.
