Koeleria cristata f. grandiflora

Koeleria cristata f. grandiflora, commonly known as the tufted grass or mountain grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically features narrow, grass-like leaves and is adapted to grow in rocky or alpine environments. It is often found in mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

Koeleria cristata f. grandiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
