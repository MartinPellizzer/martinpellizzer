Kniphofia triangularis

Kniphofia triangularis, commonly known as the red hot poker, is a striking perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, spiky flower spikes that range in color from red to orange, giving it its common name. The plant features long, narrow, grass-like leaves that form a rosette at the base. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its striking appearance and long blooming period make it a standout in both garden and floral arrangements.
