Krigia chilensis

Krigia chilensis, commonly known as the Chilean krigia, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Chile.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant typically grows as an annual or biennial herb with slender stems and small, yellow flowers. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often covered in fine hairs.
