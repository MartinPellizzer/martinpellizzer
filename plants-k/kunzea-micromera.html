Kunzea micromera

Kunzea micromera, commonly known as the narrow-leaved kunzea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes eucalyptus and tea trees. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and heathland environments.

Kunzea micromera is valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
