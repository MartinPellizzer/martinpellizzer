Kefersteinia auriculata

Kefersteinia auriculata, commonly known as the ear-shaped daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, ear-shaped bracts that surround the central flower head. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to its foliage. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Kefersteinia auriculata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
