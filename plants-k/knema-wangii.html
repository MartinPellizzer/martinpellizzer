Knema wangii

Knema wangii, commonly known as the white knema, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the rainforests of Borneo and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and clusters of white flowers. The plant produces small, round, yellowish fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understorey of tropical forests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
