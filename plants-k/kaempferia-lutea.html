Kaempferia lutea

Kaempferia lutea, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its striking yellow and orange flowers. The plant typically grows as a low-growing herb with fleshy, sword-shaped leaves. Its flowers are borne on erect spikes and are often fragrant.

Kaempferia lutea is valued both for its ornamental beauty and traditional medicinal uses.
