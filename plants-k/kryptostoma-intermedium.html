Kryptostoma intermedium

Kryptostoma intermedium, commonly known as the narrow-leafed kryptostoma, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and linear, with a prominent midrib, and it produces small, yellow to orange flowers.

Kryptostoma intermedium is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
