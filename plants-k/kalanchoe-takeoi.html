Kalanchoe takeoi

Kalanchoe takeoi, commonly known as the mother of thousands, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, typically during the winter months. Its unique ability to propagate through plantlets that form along the edges of its leaves makes it easy to propagate.

Kalanchoe takeoi is well-suited for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance requirements and attractive appearance.
