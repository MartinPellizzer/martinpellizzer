Kadsura coccinea var. sichuanensis

Kadsura coccinea var. sichuanensis, commonly known as the Sichuan red kadsura, is a species of flowering plant in the Aristolochiaceae family.

It is native to China, particularly in the Sichuan province, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its striking red and purple flowers, which are often mistaken for berries due to their appearance. This variant is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits, though its use is limited due to its toxicity.
