Kigelia madagascariensis

Kigelia madagascariensis, commonly known as the sausage tree, is a deciduous tree native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Bignoniaceae and is characterized by its long, woody, sausage-shaped fruits. The plant produces striking yellow or orange flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are opposite and pinnately compound, with small, ovate leaflets.
