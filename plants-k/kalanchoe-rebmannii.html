Kalanchoe rebmannii

Kalanchoe rebmannii, commonly known as the Rebmann's kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems and rosettes of thick, waxy leaves that are typically green with red or purple veins. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, often in late winter or early spring. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a desirable ornamental plant.
