Konig

Konig, commonly known as the Konig plant, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its daisy-like flowers, which typically feature a yellow center surrounded by white or pale pink petals. The plant is native to various regions in Europe and Asia, where it thrives in open, sunny habitats. Konig plants are often found in meadows, along roadsides, and in disturbed soils.

They are valued for their ornamental appeal and have been used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
