Kudoa Paradoxa

Kudoa paradoxa, scientifically known as Kudoa paradoxa, is a fascinating marine protist found in various oceanic environments. Despite its small size, Kudoa paradoxa plays a significant role in marine ecosystems by influencing host behavior and nutrient cycling.
