Kopsia caudata var. glabra

Kopsia caudata var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth kopsia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant has smooth, hairless stems and leaves that are lance-shaped with a slightly waxy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
