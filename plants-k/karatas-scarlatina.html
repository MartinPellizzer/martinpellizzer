Karatas scarlatina

Karatas scarlatina, commonly known as the scarlet karatas, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its bright red, cone-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant produces small, oval-shaped seeds that are dispersed by wind. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, with a grayish-green color.

Karatas scarlatina is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
