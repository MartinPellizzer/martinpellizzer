Koilodepas cordisepalum

Koilodepas cordisepalum, commonly known as the heart-sepal orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped sepals and petals, which give it its common name. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments within tropical forests. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are often white or pale yellow in color.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
