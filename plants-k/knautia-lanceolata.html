Knautia lanceolata

Knautia lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceleaf knautia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces showy, pink to purple flower clusters that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in meadows and rocky habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and wildflower displays.
