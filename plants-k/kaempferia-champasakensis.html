Kaempferia champasakensis

Kaempferia champasakensis, commonly known as the Cambodian flame orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows in shady, humid environments, often near the forest floor. Its leaves are broad and sword-shaped, and it produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

Kaempferia champasakensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
