Karpatiosorbus croceocarpa

Karpatiosorbus croceocarpa, commonly known as the Carpathian rowan, is a deciduous shrub native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in early spring, which are followed by orange-red berries in the fall. Its leaves are oval-shaped with serrated edges, and the plant is adapted to cold climates.

Karpatiosorbus croceocarpa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
