Koeleria cristata var. violacea

Koeleria cristata var. violacea, commonly known as the violet crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, spike-like inflorescence that bears small, violet to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly habitats and is often found in alpine environments. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.

Koeleria cristata var. violacea is a hardy perennial that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil.
