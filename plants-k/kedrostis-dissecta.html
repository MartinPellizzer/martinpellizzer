Kedrostis dissecta

Kedrostis dissecta, commonly known as the African giant hyena lily, is a striking member of the Araceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, where it thrives in moist, well-drained soils. The plant features large, arrow-shaped leaves with a distinctive veining pattern, and its showy, spadix-like inflorescence is surrounded by a colorful spathe. It is a herbaceous perennial that can grow up to 2 meters tall, making it a dramatic addition to gardens.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is often cultivated in warm climates.
