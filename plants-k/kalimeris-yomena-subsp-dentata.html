Kalimeris yomena subsp. dentata

Kalimeris yomena subsp.

dentata, commonly known as the Japanese fleur-de-lis, is a flowering plant native to East Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals.

The subspecies dentata is distinguished by its toothed leaf margins, which set it apart from other varieties of Kalimeris. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

This plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
