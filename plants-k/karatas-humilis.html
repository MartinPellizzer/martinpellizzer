Karatas humilis

Karatas humilis, commonly known as the dwarf karatas, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, narrow leaves and produces yellow flowers in early spring. The plant is adapted to dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and as a border plant.

Karatas humilis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
