Knema conferta var. scortechinii

Knema conferta var. scortechinii, commonly known as the white knema, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is valued for its timber and has ecological significance in its native habitats.
