Kleinia saginata

Kleinia saginata, commonly known as the silver carpet plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features dense, low-growing foliage with silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
