Kalopanax innovans

Kalopanax innovans, commonly known as the Chinese cowparnassus, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves with serrated margins. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in umbels. Its fruit is a follicle containing several seeds, and it is often found in moist, shaded forest environments.
