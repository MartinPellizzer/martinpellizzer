Kalanchoe spathulata

Kalanchoe spathulata, commonly known as the broad-leaf kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically broad and oval-shaped, with a waxy coating to store water. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often blooming in winter. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Kalanchoe spathulata is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and vibrant floral display.
