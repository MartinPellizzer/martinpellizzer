Kallstroemia grandiflora var. detonsa

Kallstroemia grandiflora var. detonsa, commonly known as the showy mountain-mallow, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its large, showy pink or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a soft, velvety texture.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
