Kalanchoe manginii

Kalanchoe manginii, commonly known as the ghost plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a silvery-blue hue, often adorned with small, translucent spots. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the winter months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

Kalanchoe manginii is easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
