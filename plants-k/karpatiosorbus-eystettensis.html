Karpatiosorbus eystettensis

Karpatiosorbus eystettensis, commonly known as the Eystetten rowan, is a deciduous shrub native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its small, white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces bright red berries that are edible for humans and wildlife. It typically grows in moist, acidic soils and thrives in temperate climates.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
