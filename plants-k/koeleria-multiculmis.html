Koeleria multiculmis

Koeleria multiculmis, commonly known as the blue grama, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, blue-green leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a common component of native grasslands.

Koeleria multiculmis plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soils and providing forage for wildlife.
