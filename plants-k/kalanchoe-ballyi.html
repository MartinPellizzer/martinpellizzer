Kalanchoe ballyi

Kalanchoe ballyi, commonly known as the Bally's kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating to store water. The plant produces vibrant, tubular flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often blooming in winter. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Kalanchoe ballyi is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
