Kreodanthus ovatilabius

Kreodanthus ovatilabius, commonly known as the broad-leafed kreodanthus, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, ovate leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. Kreodanthus ovatilabius thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in dense forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a unique subject for both ecological studies and horticultural interest.
