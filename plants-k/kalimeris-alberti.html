Kalimeris alberti

Kalimeris alberti, commonly known as the Albert's goldenrod, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features serrated leaves and produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to gardens.

Kalimeris alberti is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
