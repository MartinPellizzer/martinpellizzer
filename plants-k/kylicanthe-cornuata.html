Kylicanthe cornuata

Kylicanthe cornuata, commonly known as the horned hibiscus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has palmate leaves with a glossy surface and a distinctive, horn-like structure at the base of its flowers. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil.

Kylicanthe cornuata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
