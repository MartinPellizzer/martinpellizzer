Knautia arvensis subsp. subscaposa

Knautia arvensis subsp.

subscaposa, commonly known as the slender knautia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in dry meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils.

The plant features upright stems that are often hairy, with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are clustered in dense, spherical heads, producing a striking pink to purple coloration.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators.
