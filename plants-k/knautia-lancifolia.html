Knautia lancifolia

Knautia lancifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaved knautia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy perennial suitable for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate appearance and ability to attract pollinators.
