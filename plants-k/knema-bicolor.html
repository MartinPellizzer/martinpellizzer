Knema bicolor

Knema bicolor, commonly known as the white-flowered knema, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and clusters of white or pale pink flowers. Its fruit is a small, woody capsule that contains several seeds.
