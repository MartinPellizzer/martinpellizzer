Karatas cardinalis

Karatas cardinalis, commonly known as the cardinal's spurge, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking red-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has a low-growing, sprawling habit with fleshy, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant color and drought tolerance.
