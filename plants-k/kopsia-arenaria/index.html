Kopsia Arenaria

Kopsia arenaria is a hardy, drought-tolerant plant native to arid and coastal regions of southern Africa. It features small, pale green leaves and clusters of delicate, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring. This species thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is well adapted to harsh environmental conditions. Its ability to survive with minimal water makes it a valuable plant for xeriscaping and ecological restoration. Kopsia arenaria remains a fascinating example of plant resilience in challenging climates.
