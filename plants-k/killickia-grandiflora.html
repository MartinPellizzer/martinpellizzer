Killickia grandiflora

Killickia grandiflora, commonly known as the grandiflora killickia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and requires partial to full sunlight.

Killickia grandiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
