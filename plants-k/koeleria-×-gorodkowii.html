Koeleria × gorodkowii

Koeleria × gorodkowii, commonly known as the hybrid goatgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as steppes and grasslands. Its foliage is usually narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green color.

Koeleria × gorodkowii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
