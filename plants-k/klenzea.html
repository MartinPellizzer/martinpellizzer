Klenzea

Klenzea, commonly known as the bluebell or klenzea bellflower, is a genus of flowering plants in the nightshade family.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in woodland areas and prefers moist, well-drained soil. Klenzea species are often found in temperate regions of Europe and parts of Asia.

Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.
