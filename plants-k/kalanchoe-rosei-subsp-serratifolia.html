Kalanchoe rosei subsp. serratifolia

Kalanchoe rosei subsp.

serratifolia, commonly known as the serrated leaf kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features thick, fleshy leaves that are serrated along the edges, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow during the winter months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established. Kalanchoe rosei subsp.

serratifolia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage and long-lasting blooms.
