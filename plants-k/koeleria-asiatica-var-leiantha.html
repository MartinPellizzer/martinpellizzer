Koeleria asiatica var. leiantha

Koeleria asiatica var. leiantha, commonly known as the Asian Koeleria, is a low-growing perennial grass species native to parts of Asia.

It typically features fine, hair-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bloom in early summer. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often thriving in rocky or sandy soils. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species in the study of grassland ecosystems.
