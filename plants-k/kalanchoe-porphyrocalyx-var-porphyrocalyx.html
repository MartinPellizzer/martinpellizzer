Kalanchoe porphyrocalyx var. porphyrocalyx

Kalanchoe porphyrocalyx var. porphyrocalyx, commonly known as the red-leaved kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically deep red to purple in color, often with a waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the winter months. It prefers well-draining soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its unique foliage and seasonal blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
