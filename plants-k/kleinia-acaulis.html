Kleinia acaulis

Kleinia acaulis, commonly known as the silverleaf chamaedorea, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, low-growing form with dense clusters of silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine, fuzzy hairs. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
