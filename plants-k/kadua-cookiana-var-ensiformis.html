Kadua cookiana var. ensiformis

Kadua cookiana var. ensiformis, commonly known as the narrowleaf kadua, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and small, clustered yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. It is a perennial shrub that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters.
