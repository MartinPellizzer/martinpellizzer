Kadua glomerata var. laevis

Kadua glomerata var. laevis, commonly known as the smooth kadua, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial with slender stems and small, clustered flowers. Its leaves are usually smooth and lance-shaped, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.
