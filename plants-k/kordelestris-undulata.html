Kordelestris undulata

Kordelestris undulata, commonly known as the wavy leaf fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, undulating fronds that give it a unique and elegant appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest understories. Its leaves are pinnately divided, with a soft, feathery texture.

Kordelestris undulata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid, low-light conditions.
