Kennedynella

Kennedynella, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to Africa.

These plants are characterized by their dense, velvety foliage and showy, often colorful flowers that bloom in clusters. Kennedynella species typically have upright stems and are popular as ornamental plants due to their compact growth habit. They thrive in well-draining soil and require indirect sunlight to prevent leaf scorch.
