Kalanchoe miniata

Kalanchoe miniata, commonly known as the "miniature kalanchoe," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are often green with red or purple edges, and it produces small, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red. The plant is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.

Kalanchoe miniata is also known for its ability to bloom throughout the year under proper care.
