Knema percoriacea f. longepilosa

Knema percoriacea f. longepilosa, commonly known as the long-haired knema, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its unique morphological features. The plant typically grows as a small tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are elliptical, with a smooth surface and a distinct pubescent texture.

The inflorescences are axillary, bearing small white or pale yellow flowers that attract various pollinators.
