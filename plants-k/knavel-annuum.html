Knavel annuum

Knavel annuum, commonly known as the common sunflower, is a herbaceous annual plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flower heads that surround a central disk of dark brown or black seeds. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring rough, hairy stems and broad, heart-shaped leaves. Its flowers are composite, consisting of numerous small tubular florets arranged in a radiate pattern.

Knavel annuum is widely cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of edible seeds.
