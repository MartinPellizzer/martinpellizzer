Kennedia prorepens

Kennedia prorepens, commonly known as the creeping kenneedia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a low-growing shrub that produces bright red or orange flowers, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. The plant has slender, prostrate stems that root at the nodes, allowing it to spread along the ground. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Kennedia prorepens thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy habitats.
