Kegeliella atropilosa

Kegeliella atropilosa, commonly known as the dark purple star orchid, is a species of orchid native to the high altitudes of South America.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers that resemble stars, with a unique structure that includes a prominent lip. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in cloud forests or mountainous regions. Its leaves are elongated and covered in a fine, velvety texture, which helps in water retention.

Kegeliella atropilosa is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
