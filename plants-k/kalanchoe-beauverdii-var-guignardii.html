Kalanchoe beauverdii var. guignardii

Kalanchoe beauverdii var. guignardii, commonly known as the Guignard's Kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of orange, yellow, and red, typically blooming in spring or early summer. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for container gardening and rock gardens.

It is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal water and well-draining soil to thrive.
