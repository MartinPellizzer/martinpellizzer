Kalanchoe salazarii

Kalanchoe salazarii, commonly known as the Salazar's kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often blooming in winter. It is a low-maintenance plant that thrives in bright, indirect sunlight and well-draining soil.

Kalanchoe salazarii is popular as a houseplant due to its resilience and ornamental appeal.
