Keiskea elsholtzioides

Keiskea elsholtzioides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved keiskea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically blooming in spring. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
