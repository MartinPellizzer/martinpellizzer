Kleinia chordifolia

Kleinia chordifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf ice plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid and coastal environments.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
