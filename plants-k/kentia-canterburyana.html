Kentia canterburyana

Kentia canterburyana, commonly known as the Kentia palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to the island of New Zealand and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and pinnate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into dark blue berries. It is valued for its ornamental appearance and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in gardens.

Kentia canterburyana is relatively low maintenance and thrives in humid, well-lit environments.
