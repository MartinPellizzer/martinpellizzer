Kionophyton

Kionophyton, commonly known as the desert star, is a unique succulent plant native to arid regions of Africa.

It features a distinctive star-shaped structure composed of fleshy, segmented stems that store water, allowing it to survive in harsh, dry environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the rainy season, adding a striking visual contrast to its rugged appearance. Its leaves are typically reduced to spines, minimizing water loss through transpiration.

Kionophyton is valued for its resilience and is often cultivated in xeriscapes and rock gardens.
