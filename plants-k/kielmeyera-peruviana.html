Kielmeyera peruviana

Kielmeyera peruviana, commonly known as the Peruvian angel's trumpets, is a tropical evergreen tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, oval-shaped leaflets. It produces small, round fruits that contain seeds surrounded by a fleshy, yellowish pulp.

Kielmeyera peruviana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in warm climates.
