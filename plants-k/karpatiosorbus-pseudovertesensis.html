Karpatiosorbus pseudovertesensis

Karpatiosorbus pseudovertesensis, commonly known as the Carpathian rowan, is a shrub native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It belongs to the genus Sorbus, which includes species known for their showy flowers and berries. The plant typically grows in forested areas and is adapted to temperate climates. It has small, elliptical leaves and produces clusters of yellow flowers in early spring.

The fruit is a small, orange-red berry that ripens in late summer.
