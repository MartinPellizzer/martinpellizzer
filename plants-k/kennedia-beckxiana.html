Kennedia beckxiana

Kennedia beckxiana, commonly known as the pink mistflower, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 meter, featuring slender, woody stems. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to red flowers that bloom in spring, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Kennedia beckxiana is well adapted to dry conditions and is often found in sandy soils.
