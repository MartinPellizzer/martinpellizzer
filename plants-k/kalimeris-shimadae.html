Kalimeris shimadae

Kalimeris shimadae, commonly known as the Japanese aster, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically blooms in late summer to early autumn, producing vibrant yellow to orange flowers with a central disc of smaller florets. Kalimeris shimadae is often found in moist, woodland areas and thrives in partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
