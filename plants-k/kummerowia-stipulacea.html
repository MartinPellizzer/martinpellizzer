Kummerowia stipulacea

Kummerowia stipulacea, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a perennial shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and is considered an invasive species in some areas.

Botanically, it belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and has a fibrous root system.
