Kralikella africana

Kralikella africana, commonly known as the African starflower, is a perennial herb native to the grasslands of southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of lance-shaped leaves covered in fine hairs, and it produces striking yellow flowers with a star-like appearance. The plant has a short, stout stem that rises from the center of the rosette, supporting the flower cluster. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to full sun exposure.

Kralikella africana is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its distinctive floral display.
