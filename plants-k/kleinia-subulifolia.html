Kleinia subulifolia

Kleinia subulifolia, commonly known as the needle-leaved Kleinia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features small, needle-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color, arranged in a dense rosette pattern. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
