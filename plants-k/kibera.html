Kibera

Kibera, commonly known as the African violet, is a popular ornamental plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Gesneriaceae and is characterized by its velvety foliage and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a compact shrub with upright stems and produces flowers in shades of purple, blue, and white. Kibera thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ability to bloom continuously makes it a favorite among indoor gardeners.
