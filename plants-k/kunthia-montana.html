Kunthia montana

Kunthia montana, commonly known as the mountain kunthia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in high-altitude regions, thriving in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Kunthia montana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
