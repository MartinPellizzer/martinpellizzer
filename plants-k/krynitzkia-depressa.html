Krynitzkia depressa

Krynitzkia depressa, commonly known as the dwarf krynitzkia, is a small, mat-forming succulent native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, ribbed leaves that are typically blue-green in color, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange, which bloom during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements, it is often used in succulent collections and as a ground cover in dry landscapes.
