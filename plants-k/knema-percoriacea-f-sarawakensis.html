Knema percoriacea f. sarawakensis

Knema percoriacea f. sarawakensis, commonly known as the Sarawak knema, is a tropical evergreen tree belonging to the Myrtaceae family.

It is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and elliptical, glossy dark green leaves. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

This species is native to the rainforests of Borneo and is valued for its aromatic wood and ecological significance.
