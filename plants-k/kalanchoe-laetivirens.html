Kalanchoe laetivirens

Kalanchoe laetivirens, commonly known as the "Lucky Plant" or "Good Luck Plant," is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture, and it produces vibrant orange to red flowers during the winter months. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in bright, indirect sunlight and well-draining soil.

It is also popular for its air-purifying qualities and ease of care, making it a favored choice for indoor gardening.
