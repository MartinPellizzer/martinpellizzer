Koeleria Spicata

Koeleria spicata is a hardy grass species native to arid and semi-arid regions across the globe. It thrives in poor soils and is often found in rocky or sandy environments. This plant is known for its delicate, spike-like inflorescence and its ability to withstand drought conditions. Koeleria spicata plays an important role in stabilizing soil and preventing erosion in its native habitats. Its resilience makes it a valuable species for ecological restoration projects.
