Kalimeris fruticosa

Kalimeris fruticosa, commonly known as the Chinese aster or Chinese thistle, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring upright stems and finely divided, fern-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn.
