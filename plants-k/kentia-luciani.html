Kentia luciani

Kentia luciani, commonly known as the Kentia palm, is a tropical plant native to the island of New Guinea.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that give it a graceful appearance. The plant prefers bright, indirect light and thrives in humid environments with well-draining soil. It is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Kentia luciani is known for its ability to purify the air and enhance the aesthetic of any space.
