Koenigia × Fennica

Koenigia × fennica is a rare and striking hybrid plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions. It thrives in harsh, cold climates and is known for its unique rosette structure and vibrant flower colors. This plant is often found in rocky and coastal areas where it withstands strong winds and low temperatures. Its ability to survive in such extreme conditions makes it a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts. Despite its resilience, Koenigia × fennica remains a delicate species that requires careful conservation efforts.
