Knesebeckia martiana

Knesebeckia martiana, commonly known as the flame violet, is a striking perennial plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range from pink to deep red. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and emits a mild, sweet fragrance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
