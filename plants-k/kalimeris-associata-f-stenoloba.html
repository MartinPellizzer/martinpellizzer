Kalimeris associata f. stenoloba

Kalimeris associata f. stenoloba, commonly known as the narrow-leaved kalimeris, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and daisy-like yellow flowers with a central disk. Native to East Asia, this plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight. It is often found in woodland edges and mountainous regions, where it contributes to local biodiversity.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in temperate climates.
