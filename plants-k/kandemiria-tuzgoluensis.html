Kandemiria tuzgoluensis

Kandemiria tuzgoluensis, commonly known as the Tuzgolu endemic plant, is a unique species native to the Tuzgolu region in Turkey.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showing adaptations to withstand drought conditions. Its botanical features include a fibrous root system and a short, sturdy stem.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
