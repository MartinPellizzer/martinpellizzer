Kalanchoe hybrida

Kalanchoe hybrida, commonly known as the African violet, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, often blooming in winter. It prefers bright, indirect sunlight and well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Kalanchoe hybrida is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and long-lasting blooms.
