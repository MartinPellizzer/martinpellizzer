Kennedia macrophylla

Kennedia macrophylla, commonly known as the large-flowered kennedia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its showy, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features compound leaves with multiple leaflets and a woody stem that can become somewhat shrub-like. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in coastal and sandy regions.

Kennedia macrophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
