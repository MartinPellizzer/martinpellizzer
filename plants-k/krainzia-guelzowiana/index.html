Krainzia Guelzowiana

Krainzia guelzowiana is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the Andes mountains of South America. It thrives in high-altitude environments where the climate is cool and the soil is well-drained. This species is known for its small, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. Due to its limited natural range and sensitivity to environmental changes, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique and beautiful plant for future generations.
