Kohleria × dubia

Kohleria × dubia, commonly known as the hybrid kohleria, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows as a compact shrub with glossy, dark green foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Kohleria × dubia is popular among succulent and tropical plant enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of care.
