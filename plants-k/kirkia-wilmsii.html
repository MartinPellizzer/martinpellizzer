Kirkia wilmsii

Kirkia wilmsii, commonly known as the yellow kirkia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has small, leathery leaves and produces orange-red berries that are an important food source for birds. Kirkia wilmsii thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in its native habitat.
