Koeniga

Koeniga, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other composite flowers. The plant features showy yellow flower heads with white ray florets, giving it a daisy-like appearance. Koeniga thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

It is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
