Kleinia nogalensis

Kleinia nogalensis, commonly known as the Natal daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form. The plant produces showy, daisy-like yellow flowers with a dark central disc, typically blooming in spring and summer. Its leaves are fleshy, succulent, and often covered in a fine layer of white wax.

Kleinia nogalensis is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient plant in harsh conditions.
