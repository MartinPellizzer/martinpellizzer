Karpatiosorbus barabitsii

Karpatiosorbus barabitsii, commonly known as the Carpathian rowan, is a species of shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the Carpathian Mountains in Central and Eastern Europe. The plant is characterized by its dense, spiny branches and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in early spring. Its berries, which are red and edible, ripen in late summer and are an important food source for birds and other wildlife.

This hardy plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.
