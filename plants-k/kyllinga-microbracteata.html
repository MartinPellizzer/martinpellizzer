Kyllinga microbracteata

Kyllinga microbracteata, commonly known as the small-bract kyllinga, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of water bodies. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and small, clustered inflorescences. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, and it produces small, three-lobed seeds.

Kyllinga microbracteata is often used in ornamental gardening due to its delicate appearance and adaptability to moist environments.
