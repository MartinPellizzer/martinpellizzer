Kalanchoe neglecta

Kalanchoe neglecta, commonly known as the silver leaf kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, silver-green leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces clusters of showy, yellow flowers during the winter months, adding a vibrant touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for low-maintenance gardening.
