Kniphofia uvaria var. glaucescens

Kniphofia uvaria var. glaucescens, commonly known as the blue torch lily, is a striking perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, erect stems topped with dense, conical clusters of vibrant blue to purple flowers. The leaves are narrow, grass-like, and often have a bluish-gray hue, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is prized for its showy blooms and drought tolerance, ideal for xeriscaping.
