Knema plumulosa

Knema plumulosa, commonly known as the white knema, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its fruit is a small, capsule-like structure that splits open to release seeds.

Knema plumulosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
