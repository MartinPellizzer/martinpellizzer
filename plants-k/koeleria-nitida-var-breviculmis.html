Koeleria nitida var. breviculmis

Koeleria nitida var. breviculmis, commonly known as the dwarf silky grass, is a low-growing perennial grass native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It features fine, silver-green foliage and produces delicate, fluffy seed heads that add visual interest to the landscape. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often used in rock gardens or as a ground cover. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its botanical features make it an attractive and hardy addition to native plant gardens.
