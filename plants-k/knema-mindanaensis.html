Knema mindanaensis

Knema mindanaensis, commonly known as Mindanao knema, is a small evergreen tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, dark bark and elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by edible, fleshy berries. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland and hillside forests.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and traditional herbal practices.
