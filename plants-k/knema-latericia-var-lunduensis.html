Knema latericia var. lunduensis

Knema latericia var. lunduensis, commonly known as the Lunduensis knema, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical rainforests of Borneo, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and clusters of fragrant white flowers. It produces small, round berries that are typically green when unripe and turn red when mature.

This variant is notable for its unique ecological role and is often found in lowland and hill forest ecosystems.
