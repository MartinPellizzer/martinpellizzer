Karpatiosorbus dominii

Karpatiosorbus dominii, commonly known as the Carpathian rowan, is a rare shrub native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its small, white flowers and red berries. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with cool, moist climates. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a serrated margin.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered endangered and is of significant botanical interest.
