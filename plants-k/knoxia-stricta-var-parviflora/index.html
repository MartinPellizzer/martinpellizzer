Knoxia Stricta Var. Parviflora

Knoxia parviflora, scientifically known as Knoxia stricta var. parviflora, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern China. This species belongs to the family Campanulaceae, which includes numerous bell-shaped flowers, and is characterized by its small, pale blue or white blooms. Despite its beauty, Knoxia parviflora is critically endangered due to habitat loss and over-collection for ornamental purposes. Conservation efforts are underway to protect and propagate this unique species in botanical gardens and protected reserves. Its survival highlights the importance of preserving biodiversity in threatened ecosystems.
