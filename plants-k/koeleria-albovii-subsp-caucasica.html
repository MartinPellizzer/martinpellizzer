Koeleria albovii subsp. caucasica

Koeleria albovii subsp.

caucasica, commonly known as Caucasian koeleria, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae. It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters.

The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale blue to white florets. It is native to the Caucasus region and thrives in rocky or sandy soils.

Botanically, it is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive floral structure.
