Kedarnatha oreomyrrhiformis

Kedarnatha oreomyrrhiformis, commonly known as the Himalayan sandalwood, is a rare and endemic species found in the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by small, woody capsules containing its seeds.
