Koeleria robinsoniana var. australis

Koeleria robinsoniana var. australis, commonly known as the Australian koeleria, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, pale blue to white flowers. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions.

Due to its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
