Knautia sylvatica var. dinarica

Knautia sylvatica var. dinarica, commonly known as the Dinaric knautia, is a perennial herb native to the Balkan region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils, often found in mountainous areas. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are hairy to the touch.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
