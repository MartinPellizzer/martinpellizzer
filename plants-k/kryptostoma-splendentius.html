Kryptostoma splendentius

Kryptostoma splendentius, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a rare and striking orchid species native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow petals and a distinctive star-shaped arrangement of flowers, which make it visually captivating. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees and deriving moisture and nutrients from the air and rain. Its flowers bloom during the warmer months, attracting a variety of pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, Kryptostoma splendentius is highly valued in botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
