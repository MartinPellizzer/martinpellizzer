Kohleria magnifica

Kohleria magnifica, commonly known as the flame flower, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with contrasting yellow or white markings. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in its natural habitat. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Kohleria magnifica is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its vibrant blooms and unique appearance.
