Kraenzlinella smaragdina

Kraenzlinella smaragdina, commonly known as the emerald leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green foliage and small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often found in the understory of rainforests. Its unique leaf structure and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for collectors and horticulturists.

Kraenzlinella smaragdina requires careful cultivation, including high humidity and indirect light, to thrive.
