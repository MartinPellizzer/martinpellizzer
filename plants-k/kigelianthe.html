Kigelianthe

Kigelianthe, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery red or orange flowers that bloom in dense clusters, creating a dramatic visual display. The plant has pinnate leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets, and its stems are often covered in a thick, waxy coating. Kigelianthe thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

It is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
