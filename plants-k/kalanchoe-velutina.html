Kalanchoe velutina

Kalanchoe velutina, commonly known as the velvet leaf kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves covered in a soft, velvety coating, which helps it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of orange, yellow, and red during the winter months. It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

Kalanchoe velutina is also known for its ability to propagate through leaf cuttings, making it easy to grow from home.
