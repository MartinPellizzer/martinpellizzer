Kallias

Kallias, commonly known as the bluebell, is a flowering plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in woodland areas and prefers well-drained, moist soil. Kallias has strap-like, evergreen leaves that remain throughout the year.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is native to parts of Europe and Asia.
