Kobresia filiformis

Kobresia filiformis, commonly known as the slender kobresia, is a low-growing perennial grass native to arid and semi-arid regions of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, thread-like leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of silky hairs, which help reduce water loss in harsh environments. The plant forms dense mats or cushions, which provide soil stabilization and habitat for small organisms. It is adapted to extreme conditions, including high solar radiation and low precipitation.

Kobresia filiformis plays a significant ecological role in preventing soil erosion in its native habitats.
