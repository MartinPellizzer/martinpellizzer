Koeleria spicata subsp. wrangelensis

Koeleria spicata subsp.

wrangelensis, commonly known as Wrangel's feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to the Arctic and subarctic regions of Eurasia. It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and produces dense spikes of small, pale blue to purple flowers.

The plant is adapted to cold climates and thrives in well-drained, sandy or rocky soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a bluish-green coloration.

Wrangel's feathergrass plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for arctic wildlife.
