Kniphofia andongensis

Kniphofia andongensis, commonly known as the Andong red hot poker, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the genus Kniphofia, which is part of the Asphodelaceae family, known for its showy, spike-like flower clusters. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces vibrant red and orange flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a valuable addition to both wild and cultivated landscapes.
