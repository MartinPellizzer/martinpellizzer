Kohleria spicata var. wageneri

Kohleria spicata var. wageneri, commonly known as the Wagener's kohleria, is a species of flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its foliage consists of dark green, glossy leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is cultivated in greenhouses and as a houseplant due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
