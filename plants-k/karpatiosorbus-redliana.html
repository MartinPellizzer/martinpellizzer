Karpatiosorbus redliana

Karpatiosorbus redliana, commonly known as the Carpathian rowan, is a shrub native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, thorny branches and small, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of white flowers in spring, followed by bright red berries in the autumn. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas with well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
