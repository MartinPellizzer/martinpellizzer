Koelreuteria edulis

Koelreuteria edulis, commonly known as the Chinese flowering ash, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive yellowish-orange flowers that appear in late summer. The tree produces small, woody fruits that split open to reveal three winged seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

Koelreuteria edulis is also valued for its ecological benefits, providing food for birds and insects.
