Koeleria lucana

Koeleria lucana, commonly known as the Sicilian grass, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Koeleria lucana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in sustainable landscaping.
