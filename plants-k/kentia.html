Kentia

Kentia, commonly known as the Kentia palm, is a tropical plant native to New Caledonia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that give it a graceful appearance. The plant has a slow growth rate, making it ideal for indoor cultivation. It prefers bright, indirect light and moderate humidity, thriving in warm, stable environments.

Its air-purifying qualities and elegant foliage make it a popular choice for both residential and commercial spaces.
