Kaempferia kamolwaniae

Kaempferia kamolwaniae, commonly known as the Kamolwana orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, often colorful flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial with broad, strap-like leaves. Its flowers are notable for their intricate patterns and can vary in color from red to orange and yellow.

Kaempferia kamolwaniae is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
