Kaempferia linearis

Kaempferia linearis, commonly known as the sword lily or narrow leaf ginger, is a herbaceous perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and turmeric. The plant features long, narrow leaves that are typically dark green and glossy, and it produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. Kaempferia linearis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for tropical gardens.
