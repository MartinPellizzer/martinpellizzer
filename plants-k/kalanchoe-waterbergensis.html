Kalanchoe waterbergensis

Kalanchoe waterbergensis, commonly known as the Waterberg Kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, yellow, and orange during the winter months. It is typically shrub-like in growth habit, with multiple stems branching out from the base.

Kalanchoe waterbergensis is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental value.
