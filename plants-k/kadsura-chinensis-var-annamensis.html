Kadsura chinensis var. annamensis

Kadsura chinensis var. annamensis, commonly known as the Annam kadsura, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, heart-shaped leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces intricate, pipe-shaped flowers that are often greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical forests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some regions.
