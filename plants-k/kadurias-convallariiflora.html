Kadurias convallariiflora

Kadurias convallariiflora, commonly known as the pink lady's tresses, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is a terrestrial herb with a slender, creeping stem that produces dense clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically blooms in late spring to early summer, and its flowers are arranged in a dense, trichotomy pattern. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The plant has a long flowering period and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
