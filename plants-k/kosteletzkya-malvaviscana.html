Kosteletzkya malvaviscana

Kosteletzkya malvaviscana, commonly known as the swamp mallow, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to wetland habitats in the southeastern United States.

The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, and its stems are often covered in fine, soft hairs.
