Karpatiosorbus schuwerkiorum

Karpatiosorbus schuwerkiorum, commonly known as the Carpathian rowan, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas with well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
