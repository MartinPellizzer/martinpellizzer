Kalosanthes coccinea

Kalosanthes coccinea, commonly known as the red-flowered orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red petals and yellow sepals. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers bloom in clusters and are known for their striking color and subtle fragrance.

Kalosanthes coccinea is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
