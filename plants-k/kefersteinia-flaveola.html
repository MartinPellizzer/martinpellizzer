Kefersteinia flaveola

Kefersteinia flaveola, commonly known as the yellow star orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features slender, branching stems and produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers with a distinctive star-like shape. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Kefersteinia flaveola is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or terrariums.
