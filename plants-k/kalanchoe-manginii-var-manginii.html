Kalanchoe manginii var. manginii

Kalanchoe manginii var. manginii, commonly known as the Manginii Kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, ribbed leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture in arid conditions. The plant produces clusters of bright orange to red flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant blooms make it both ornamental and easy to care for.
