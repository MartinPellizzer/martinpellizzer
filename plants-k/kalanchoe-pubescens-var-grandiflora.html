Kalanchoe pubescens var. grandiflora

Kalanchoe pubescens var. grandiflora, commonly known as the grandiflora kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hair, giving it a fuzzy appearance. The plant produces showy, bright orange or yellow flowers during the winter months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and rock gardens.
