Kobresia brunnescens

Kobresia brunnescens, commonly known as the brown kobresia, is a perennial grass species native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming structure with dark green, narrow leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets, typical of the Poaceae family. Kobresia brunnescens is adapted to harsh environmental conditions, including high salinity and low precipitation.

It plays a vital role in stabilizing soils and providing habitat for various desert organisms.
