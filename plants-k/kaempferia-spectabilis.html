Kaempferia spectabilis

Kaempferia spectabilis, commonly known as the showy ginger, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, colorful inflorescences with fleshy, often brightly colored bracts. The plant has a rhizomatous root system and produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and tropical gardens.
