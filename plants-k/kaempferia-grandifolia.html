Kaempferia grandifolia

Kaempferia grandifolia, commonly known as the giant flame orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a rhizomatous herb with broad, strap-shaped leaves that are often a deep green color. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in clusters along the stem. It thrives in warm, humid conditions with bright indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Kaempferia grandifolia is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
