Kelleria patula

Kelleria patula, commonly known as the African moonflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its large, white, night-blooming flowers. The plant has a woody stem and produces small, green, egg-shaped fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in savanna habitats.
