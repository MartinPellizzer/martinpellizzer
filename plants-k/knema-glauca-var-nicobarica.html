Knema glauca var. nicobarica

Knema glauca var. nicobarica, commonly known as the Nicobar knema, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Nicobar Islands.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces fleshy, yellowish-red berries that are an important food source for local wildlife. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
