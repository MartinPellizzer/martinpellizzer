Knappia agrostidea

Knappia agrostidea, commonly known as the tufted grass or knappia grass, is a species of flowering plant in the Poaceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, often hairy stems. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Knappia agrostidea is native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
