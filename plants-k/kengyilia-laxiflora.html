Kengyilia laxiflora

Kengyilia laxiflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered kengyilia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

Kengyilia laxiflora is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in gardens in cooler climates.
