Knema conferta var. borneensis

Knema conferta var. borneensis, commonly known as Bornean knema, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves with a smooth, waxy surface. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland and hill dipterocarp forests.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
