Koeleria Cristata Var. Albescens

Koeleria cristata var. albescens is a strikingly beautiful plant known for its unique white florets and delicate structure. Native to the mountainous regions of central Asia, this variety thrives in harsh, alpine environments. Its feathery appearance and soft, fluffy texture make it a popular choice among gardeners seeking a touch of elegance. The plant is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. With its graceful form and resilience, Koeleria cristata var. albescens stands out as a remarkable addition to both wild and cultivated landscapes.
