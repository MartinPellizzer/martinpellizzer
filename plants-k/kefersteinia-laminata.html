Kefersteinia laminata

Kefersteinia laminata, commonly known as the ghost flower, is a rare and striking plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

The plant is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its foliage is typically broad and strap-like, with a waxy surface that helps retain moisture.
