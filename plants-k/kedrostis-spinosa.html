Kedrostis spinosa

Kedrostis spinosa, commonly known as the spiny senna, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-bearing fruits. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Kedrostis spinosa is also valued for its hardy nature, making it adaptable to various environmental conditions.
