Kalanchoe olivacea

Kalanchoe olivacea, commonly known as the olive tree kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy coating, which helps it store water. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the spring. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal watering.

Kalanchoe olivacea is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and resilience.
