Kaernbachia

Kaernbachia, commonly known as the silverleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features basal leaves that are silver-white on the underside, giving it its common name, and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Kaernbachia is adapted to rocky and arid environments, with a deep root system that helps it survive in poor soil conditions.
