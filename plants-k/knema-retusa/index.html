Knema Retusa

Knema retusa is a lesser-known tropical plant native to the dense rainforests of Southeast Asia. It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is known for its unique aromatic properties. This small evergreen tree or shrub is often found in humid, shaded environments where it thrives. Its leaves are dark green and glossy, while its flowers are small and fragrant. Despite its modest appearance, Knema retusa holds cultural and medicinal significance in local communities.
