Kyllinga alba-purpurea

Kyllinga alba-purpurea, commonly known as white and purple spikerush, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and small, clustered spikes of white and purple flowers. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy environments, such as along stream banks or in swampy areas. Its leaves are narrow and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Kyllinga alba-purpurea is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive color contrast and ability to thrive in moist conditions.
