Knautia dipsacifolia subsp. dipsacifolia

Knautia dipsacifolia subsp.

dipsacifolia, commonly known as the Alpine knautia, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It belongs to the family Caprifoliaceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of pink to purple flowers.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms and ecological benefits.
