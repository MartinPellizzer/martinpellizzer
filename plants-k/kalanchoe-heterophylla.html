Kalanchoe heterophylla

Kalanchoe heterophylla, commonly known as the variable leaf kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves that vary in shape and size depending on environmental conditions. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically during the winter months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright sunlight to flourish.

Kalanchoe heterophylla is drought-tolerant and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance.
