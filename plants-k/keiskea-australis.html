Keiskea australis

Keiskea australis, commonly known as the Australian keiskea, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in a fine layer of hair.

Keiskea australis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
