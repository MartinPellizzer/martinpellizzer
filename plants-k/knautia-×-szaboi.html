Knautia × szaboi

Knautia × szaboi, commonly known as the hybrid knautia, is a perennial plant belonging to the Caprifoliaceae family.

It is a cross between Knautia arvensis and Knautia montana, resulting in a plant with distinctive, fluffy, pinkish-white flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows and rocky soils, thriving in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage is lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Knautia × szaboi is valued in gardens for its showy blooms and ability to attract pollinators.
