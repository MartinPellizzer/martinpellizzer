Kraussia pavettoides

Kraussia pavettoides, commonly known as the silver tree, is a small evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
