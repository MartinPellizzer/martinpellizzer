Kalimares amellus

Kalimares amellus, commonly known as the golden mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a glossy green surface and a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Kalimares amellus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
