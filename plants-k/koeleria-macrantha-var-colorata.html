Koeleria macrantha var. colorata

Koeleria macrantha var. colorata, commonly known as the purplegrass, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

Koeleria macrantha var. colorata is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in poor soils, making it a suitable choice for native plant gardens.
