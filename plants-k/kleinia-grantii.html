Kleinia grantii

Kleinia grantii, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical leaves that are often blue-green in color, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Kleinia grantii is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
