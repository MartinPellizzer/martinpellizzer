Kalimeris incisa var. robusta

Kalimeris incisa var. robusta, commonly known as the robusta kalimeris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and serrated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers with a distinctive disc-shaped center, typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for gardeners in temperate climates.
