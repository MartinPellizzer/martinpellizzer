Kibessia teysmanniana

Kibessia teysmanniana, commonly known as the Philippine orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers that often display a mix of red, orange, and yellow hues. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Kibessia teysmanniana is valued for its ornamental beauty and plays a role in the biodiversity of its native ecosystems.
