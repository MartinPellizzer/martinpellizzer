Kniphofia thodei

Kniphofia thodei, commonly known as the Orange Firecracker, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the genus Kniphofia, which includes numerous species of ornamental grass-like plants. The plant features tall, sword-shaped leaves and vibrant orange-red flower spikes that bloom in late summer. Its flowers are arranged in dense, cylindrical clusters, creating a dramatic visual impact.

Kniphofia thodei is well-suited for gardens with full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
