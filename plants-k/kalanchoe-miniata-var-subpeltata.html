Kalanchoe miniata var. subpeltata

Kalanchoe miniata var. subpeltata, commonly known as the silver leaf kalanchoe, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features succulent, fleshy leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery-white wax, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of bright orange to red tubular flowers during the winter months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

This variety is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of care.
