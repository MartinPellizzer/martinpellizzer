Kinginda thorelii

Kinginda thorelii, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain hard, black seeds. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
