Kalanchoe sexangularis var. sexangularis

Kalanchoe sexangularis var. sexangularis, commonly known as the angular kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that are typically green with a subtle blue or purple tint. The plant produces vibrant orange or red flowers in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Its compact growth habit makes it an ideal choice for rock gardens or container planting.
