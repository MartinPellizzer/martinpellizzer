Kentia microspadix

Kentia microspadix, commonly known as the narrow-leafed cycad, is a species of cycad native to New Caledonia.

It is characterized by its narrow, dark green leaves that grow in a dense, fan-like arrangement. The plant produces small, cone-like structures called strobili, which contain both male and female reproductive organs. Kentia microspadix is a slow-growing plant that thrives in warm, humid climates with well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
