Kalanchoe × houghtonii

Kalanchoe × houghtonii, commonly known as the Houghton's kalanchoe, is a hybrid succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which bloom in late winter to early spring. It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

Kalanchoe × houghtonii is also valued for its resilience and ability to tolerate drought conditions.
