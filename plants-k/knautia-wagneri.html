Knautia wagneri

Knautia wagneri, commonly known as Wagner's knautia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink or purple flower clusters that bloom in late summer. Wagner's knautia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
