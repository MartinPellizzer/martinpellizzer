Koeleria Nitida Var. Californica

Koeleria nitida var. californica is a delicate flowering plant native to the western United States. It thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions. Known for its fine, grass-like leaves and clusters of small, blue to purple flowers, this plant adds a subtle beauty to natural landscapes. It is valued for its low maintenance and ability to withstand harsh conditions. This variety is a great example of nature's resilience and adaptability.
