Kunzea ericifolia var. glabrior

Kunzea ericifolia var. glabrior, commonly known as the smooth kunzea, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage with small, elliptical leaves that are generally smooth and glabrous. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers in clusters, typically blooming in spring. It is well adapted to coastal and sandy soils, often found in heathlands and coastal scrub.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for landscaping in suitable climates.
