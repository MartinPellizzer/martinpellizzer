Koenigia panjutinii

Koenigia panjutinii, commonly known as the Panjutinii stonecrop, is a perennial succulent native to Central Asia.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color, adapted to store water. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange, usually in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or arid environments.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a desirable addition to xeriscapes.
