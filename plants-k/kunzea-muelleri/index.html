Kunzea Muelleri

Kunzea muelleri is a unique plant native to the coastal regions of southern Australia. It thrives in sandy soils and is often found in heathlands and scrub environments. This species is known for its aromatic leaves and small, clustered flowers that bloom in winter. It plays an important role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and food for native wildlife. Despite its hardy nature, it is considered a rare and endangered species requiring conservation efforts.
