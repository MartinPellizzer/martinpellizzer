Knowltonia helleborifolia

Knowltonia helleborifolia, commonly known as the Easter lily, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its showy, white, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has narrow, strap-like leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Knowltonia helleborifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a symbol of purity and renewal.
