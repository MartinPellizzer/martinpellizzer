Kaempferia ornata

Kaempferia ornata, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful inflorescences. The plant typically features broad, sword-shaped leaves and produces flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Kaempferia ornata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
