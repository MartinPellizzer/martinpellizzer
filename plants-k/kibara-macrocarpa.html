Kibara macrocarpa

Kibara macrocarpa, commonly known as the giant kibara, is a large evergreen tree native to the tropical rainforests of Africa.

It is characterized by its massive, broad leaves and can grow to impressive heights, often exceeding 30 meters. The tree produces large, fleshy fruits that are an important food source for various wildlife species. Its bark is thick and deeply fissured, providing protection against pests and environmental stress.

The plant plays a significant role in the ecological balance of its habitat due to its size and longevity.
