Koeleria gracilis f. laxa

Koeleria gracilis f. laxa, commonly known as the slender silky grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial grass that typically forms dense, mat-like growths. The plant features slender, wiry stems and small, spike-like inflorescences that produce tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a bluish-green hue.

This variety is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy plant found in various regions across North America.
