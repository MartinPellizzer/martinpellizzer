Kailarsenia lineata

Kailarsenia lineata, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, zigzagging stems that give it a distinctive appearance. The leaves are alternate, elliptical, and glossy, with a dark green color and prominent veins. It produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by red, fleshy fruits.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in warm, humid environments.
