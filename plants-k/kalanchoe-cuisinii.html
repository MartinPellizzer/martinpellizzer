Kalanchoe cuisinii

Kalanchoe cuisinii, commonly known as the Cape Town beauty, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a reddish or purple hue along the edges. The plant produces vibrant, tubular flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, typically blooming in winter. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Kalanchoe cuisinii is drought-tolerant and suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
