Kyllingiella microcephala

Kyllingiella microcephala, commonly known as the dwarf kyllingiella, is a small, succulent plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tinge, adapted to store water and reduce transpiration. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange, which bloom during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xerophytic gardens.

Its slow growth rate and unique appearance make it a desirable addition to rock gardens and succulent collections.
