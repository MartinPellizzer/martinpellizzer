Koeleria Youngii

Koeleria youngii is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the arid regions of western North America. It belongs to the grass family and is known for its small, intricate flowers that bloom in early spring. This species is often found in rocky soils and dry slopes, thriving in harsh environmental conditions. Due to its limited range and vulnerability to habitat loss, Koeleria youngii is considered endangered. Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it an important subject for conservation efforts.
