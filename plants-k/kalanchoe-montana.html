Kalanchoe montana

Kalanchoe montana, commonly known as the mountain kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting to arid environments. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, typically during the spring and summer months. It is a perennial shrub that can grow up to 3 feet tall, with a compact and bushy growth habit.

Kalanchoe montana is well-suited for rock gardens and containers due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
