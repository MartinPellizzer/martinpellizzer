Kleistrocalyx

Kleistrocalyx, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color. Kleistrocalyx thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in wetland or riparian habitats.

Its botanical features make it both ecologically significant and sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
