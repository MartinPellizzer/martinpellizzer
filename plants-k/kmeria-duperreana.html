Kmeria duperreana

Kmeria duperreana, commonly known as the mountain holly, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are leathery and have a rough texture. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by red berries that are edible for some bird species. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Kmeria duperreana is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
