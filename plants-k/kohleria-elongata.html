Kohleria elongata

Kohleria elongata, commonly known as the long-leaf kohleria, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.
