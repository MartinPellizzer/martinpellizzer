Kitigorchis foliosa

Kitigorchis foliosa, commonly known as the leafy orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white and pale pink, which bloom during the warmer months. Native to the tropical regions of South America, it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
