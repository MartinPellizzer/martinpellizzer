Kohleria reticulata

Kohleria reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined kohleria, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that come in a range of vibrant colors. Kohleria reticulata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
