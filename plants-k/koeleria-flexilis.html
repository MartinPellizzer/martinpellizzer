Koeleria flexilis

Koeleria flexilis, commonly known as the flexible Koeleria, is a flowering plant native to the Arctic and alpine regions of North America and Eurasia.

It is a low-growing perennial grass that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are usually pale blue or purple and bloom in late summer.

Koeleria flexilis is well adapted to cold climates and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
