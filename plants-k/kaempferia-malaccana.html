Kaempferia malaccana

Kaempferia malaccana, commonly known as the Malacca torch orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, torch-like inflorescence. The plant typically grows as a rhizomatous herb, with narrow, grass-like leaves and showy, colorful flowers that range from red to orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Kaempferia malaccana is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its traditional uses in herbal medicine.
