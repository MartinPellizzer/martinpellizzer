Kadsura acuminata

Kadsura acuminata, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or the arrowleaf kadsura, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves that are often elliptical or arrow-shaped. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by red or orange berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often used in traditional medicine.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and landscape design.
