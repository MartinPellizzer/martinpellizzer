Kieseria stricta

Kieseria stricta, commonly known as the narrow-leaf kieseria, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in clusters.

Kieseria stricta is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
