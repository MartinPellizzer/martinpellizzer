Kunzea recurva

Kunzea recurva, commonly known as the narrow-leaved kunzea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in coastal heathlands and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions. It has a compact growth habit and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Kunzea recurva is also valued for its aromatic foliage and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
