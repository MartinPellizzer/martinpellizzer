Kalanchoe wildii

Kalanchoe wildii, commonly known as the Cape Town flower, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that store water, typical of arid environments. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often blooming in winter. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and container planting.

Kalanchoe wildii is also noted for its ability to propagate through offsets and its resilience to drought conditions.
