Koeleria afromontana

Koeleria afromontana, commonly known as the Ethiopian feather grass, is a flowering plant native to the highlands of Ethiopia.

It is a member of the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its fine, wiry leaves and delicate panicles of small, spikelet-based flowers. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
