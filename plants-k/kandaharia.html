Kandaharia

Kandaharia, commonly known as the Kashmiri rose, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pink or white petals and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes. The plant produces small, edible fruits that are used in traditional cuisines. Its leaves are evergreen and have a serrated edge, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Kandaharia thrives in cool, moist climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
