Kalanchoe rosei

Kalanchoe rosei, commonly known as the pink kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a reddish or pinkish tint. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, often blooming in winter. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

Kalanchoe rosei is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and ornamental value.
