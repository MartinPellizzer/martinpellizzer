Kyllinga odorata var. latifolia

Kyllinga odorata var. latifolia, commonly known as broadleaf swamp morning glory, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features striking, showy flowers that are typically lavender to pink in color, with a distinctive spurred petal. The plant has broad, waxy leaves that are often arranged in a spiral pattern along its stem. It thrives in moist, sunny environments such as wetlands and marshes.

Its botanical characteristics make it both ornamental and ecologically significant in its native habitats.
