Kalanchoe × sogae

Kalanchoe × sogae, commonly known as the hybrid kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that store water, typical of succulents, and produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in arid conditions and requires minimal water, making it ideal for xeriscaping. Kalanchoe × sogae is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is popular in gardens and as a houseplant.

Its flowering period typically occurs in winter, adding color to the landscape during colder months.
