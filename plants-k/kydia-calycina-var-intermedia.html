Kydia calycina var. intermedia

Kydia calycina var. intermedia, commonly known as the New Zealand native honeysuckle, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It produces small, round berries that turn red when mature.

The species is adapted to a variety of soil types and is often found in coastal and forested areas.
