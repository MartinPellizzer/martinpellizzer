Kalanchoe jongmansii

Kalanchoe jongmansii, commonly known as the "Paddle Plant," is a succulent native to Madagascar.

It features thick, fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish tinge. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and prefers well-draining soil, making it an ideal choice for rock gardens or containers.

Kalanchoe jongmansii is also known for its ability to propagate easily through stem cuttings.
