Klotzschiphytum mauritianum

Klotzschiphytum mauritianum, commonly known as the Mauritius grass, is a flowering plant native to the island of Mauritius.

It is a perennial herb with tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Due to its invasive nature, it can outcompete native vegetation in certain regions.
