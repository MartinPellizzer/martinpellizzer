Knema oblongata

Knema oblongata, commonly known as the oblong leaf knema, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its elliptical, glossy leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. Knema oblongata is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental qualities and potential medicinal uses.

Its growth habit and ecological role make it an important component of tropical ecosystems.
