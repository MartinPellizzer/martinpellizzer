Kalanchoe rubinea

Kalanchoe rubinea, commonly known as the red-flowered kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, typical of desert plants. The plant produces vibrant red flowers in clusters, which bloom during the winter months. Its leaves are often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

Kalanchoe rubinea is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements.
