Knautia Collina Var. Mollis

Knautia collina var. mollis is a delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It is known for its soft, fluffy blooms that appear in late summer. This variety is a popular choice among gardeners for its beauty and ease of care. Its flowers range from pink to white, creating a striking visual display. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a versatile addition to any garden.
