Kielmeyera variabilis var. stenophylla

Kielmeyera variabilis var. stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leafed kielmeyera, is a species native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and dense, fragrant flowers. The plant exhibits a high degree of morphological variability, which contributes to its adaptability in different environmental conditions. Its leaves are typically glossy and dark green, while the flowers are clustered in terminal inflorescences.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
