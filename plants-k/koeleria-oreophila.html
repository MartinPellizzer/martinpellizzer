Koeleria oreophila

Koeleria oreophila, commonly known as the mountain koeleria, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It is a member of the grass family (Poaceae) and typically grows in rocky or alpine environments. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny blue to purple flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, adapted to withstand cold and arid conditions.

Koeleria oreophila is often found in meadows and along mountain trails at high elevations.
