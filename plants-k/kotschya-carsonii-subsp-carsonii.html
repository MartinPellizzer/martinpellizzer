Kotschya carsonii subsp. carsonii

Kotschya carsonii subsp.

carsonii, commonly known as Carson's kotschya, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. It is a perennial shrub characterized by its woody stems and dense clusters of small, tubular flowers.

The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, such as rocky soils and desert regions. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to orange in color, and it blooms during the spring and early summer.
