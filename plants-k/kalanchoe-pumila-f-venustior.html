Kalanchoe pumila f. venustior

Kalanchoe pumila f. venustior, commonly known as the dwarf kalanchoe or miniature kalanchoe, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating, giving them a velvety appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red during the spring and summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, requiring minimal water to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its compact size and ornamental foliage.
