Kniphofia media

Kniphofia media, commonly known as the red-hot poker, is a striking perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems that bear clusters of bright orange-red tubular flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are evergreen and form a dense rosette at the base. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its striking appearance and long flowering period make it a valuable addition to many landscapes.
