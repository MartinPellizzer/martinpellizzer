Keckiella breviflora

Keckiella breviflora, commonly known as the short-flowered keckiella, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a small, herbaceous annual that typically grows in dry, rocky soils. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs.

Keckiella breviflora is adapted to arid environments and is often found in desert scrub and chaparral habitats.
