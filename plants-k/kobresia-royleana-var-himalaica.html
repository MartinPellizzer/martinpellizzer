Kobresia royleana var. himalaica

Kobresia royleana var. himalaica, commonly known as the Himalayan kobresia, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, adapted to withstand the cold and harsh environmental conditions of its habitat.

This species plays a vital role in stabilizing soil in alpine and arctic ecosystems.
