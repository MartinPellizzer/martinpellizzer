Kniphofia × hybrida

Kniphofia × hybrida, commonly known as the red hot poker, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asphodelaceae family.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its striking, torch-like inflorescences that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Kniphofia × hybrida is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and birds.
