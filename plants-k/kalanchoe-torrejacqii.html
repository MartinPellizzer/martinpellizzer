Kalanchoe torrejacqii

Kalanchoe torrejacqii, commonly known as the "Coral Cactus," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often tinged with red or pink hues, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of orange, yellow, and red, typically during the winter months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

Kalanchoe torrejacqii is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and striking visual appeal.
