Kalimeris ciliata

Kalimeris ciliata, commonly known as the Chinese aster or silky aster, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has hairy, linear leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Kalimeris ciliata is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
