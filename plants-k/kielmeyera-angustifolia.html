Kielmeyera angustifolia

Kielmeyera angustifolia, commonly known as Brazilian pepper tree or *Caryocar* species, is a tropical evergreen tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptic to lanceolate in shape. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers followed by red, fleshy berries that are an important food source for various wildlife. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in both ecological and horticultural contexts.
