Knautia baldensis

Knautia baldensis, commonly known as the alpine knautia, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant purple or pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, thriving in high-altitude environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, adapted to withstand cold and arid conditions.

Knautia baldensis is a member of the Caprifoliaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal in alpine gardens.
