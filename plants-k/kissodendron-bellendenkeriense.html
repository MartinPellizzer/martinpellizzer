Kissodendron bellendenkeriense

Kissodendron bellendenkeriense, commonly known as the Bellendenkeriense ironwood, is a rare and unique plant native to the tropical rainforests of Borneo.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and highly aromatic, resinous leaves that emit a strong, woody scent. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color, followed by the development of hard, woody fruits. Its slow growth rate and limited distribution make it a valuable but endangered species in its natural habitat.

Due to its unique botanical features, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
