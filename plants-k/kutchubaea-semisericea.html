Kutchubaea semisericea

Kutchubaea semisericea, commonly known as the African white orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, white, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte or lithophyte, often found on trees or rocks in humid environments. Its leaves are elongated and often covered in a dense layer of fine, silky hairs.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in greenhouses and botanical gardens worldwide.
