Knowltonia hirsuta

Knowltonia hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy strelitzia, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Strelitziaceae family and is characterized by its large, colorful inflorescences that resemble tropical birds. The plant features broad, sword-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, hairy texture, giving it its common name. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Knowltonia hirsuta is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
