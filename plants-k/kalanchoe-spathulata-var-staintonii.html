Kalanchoe spathulata var. staintonii

Kalanchoe spathulata var. staintonii, commonly known as the Stainton's Kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish or purplish tinge. The plant produces showy, brightly colored flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which bloom in the spring. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
