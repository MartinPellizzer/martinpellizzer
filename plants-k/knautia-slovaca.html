Knautia slovaca

Knautia slovaca, commonly known as the Slovak knautia, is a flowering plant native to Central Europe.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils. It produces showy, pinkish-purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.
