Knautia plumosa

Knautia plumosa, commonly known as the feathery knautia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its soft, fluffy flower heads that resemble a cloud or a puff of cotton, which are composed of numerous small, pink to purple flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and has hairy, upright stems. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Knautia plumosa thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
