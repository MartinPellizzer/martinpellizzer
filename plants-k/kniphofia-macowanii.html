Kniphofia macowanii

Kniphofia macowanii, commonly known as the Macowan's torch lily, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the genus Kniphofia, which includes over 70 species of flowering plants. The plant is characterized by its tall, reddish-brown stems and clusters of bright orange to yellow tubular flowers. Its foliage consists of long, narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green.

Kniphofia macowanii is well-suited for rock gardens and dry landscapes due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
