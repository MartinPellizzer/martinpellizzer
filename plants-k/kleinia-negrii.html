Kleinia negrii

Kleinia negrii, commonly known as the blackfoot daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dark purple to black stems and bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It produces small, daisy-like blooms that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Kleinia negrii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and drought tolerance.
