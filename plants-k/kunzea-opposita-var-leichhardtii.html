Kunzea opposita var. leichhardtii

Kunzea opposita var. leichhardtii, commonly known as the Leichhardt kunzea, is a shrub native to the eastern coast of Australia.

It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to coastal and dry inland environments. The plant features narrow, opposite leaves and produces small, white or pale pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It is often found in heathlands and coastal dunes, where it contributes to the local biodiversity.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting native wildlife.
