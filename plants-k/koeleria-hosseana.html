Koeleria hosseana

Koeleria hosseana, commonly known as the slender goatgrass, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the grass family, Poaceae, and is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale blue to purple flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a bluish-green color.
