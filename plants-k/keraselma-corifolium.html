Keraselma corifolium

Keraselma corifolium, commonly known as the silverleaf carnation, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant has silvery-gray foliage that gives it its common name and helps it reflect sunlight. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Keraselma corifolium is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
