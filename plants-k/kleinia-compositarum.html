Kleinia compositarum

Kleinia compositarum, commonly known as the Cape marigold, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It has a compact, bushy growth habit with hairy leaves that help reduce water loss.

Kleinia compositarum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
