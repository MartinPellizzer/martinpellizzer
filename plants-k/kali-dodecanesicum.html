Kali dodecanesicum

Kali dodecanesicum, commonly known as the Christmas star, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped flowers that bloom in winter, typically from December to February. The plant produces small, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Kali dodecanesicum is a popular ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and seasonal blooming habit.
