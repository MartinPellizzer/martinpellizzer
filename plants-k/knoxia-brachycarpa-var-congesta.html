Knoxia brachycarpa var. congesta

Knoxia brachycarpa var. congesta, commonly known as the clustered knoxia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in shrubland and rocky habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, cylindrical fruits.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its compact growth and seasonal blooms.
