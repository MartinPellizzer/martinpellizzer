Knightia deplanchei

Knightia deplanchei, commonly known as the desert milkweed, is a perennial shrub native to arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its woody stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. Knightia deplanchei is adapted to thrive in harsh, drought-prone environments with minimal water requirements.
