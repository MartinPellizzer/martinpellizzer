Koeleria Glauca Var. Albescens

Koeleria glauca var. albescens is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central Asia. It is known for its small, blue-green leaves and clusters of pale blue or white flowers that bloom in early summer. This variety is a subspecies of Koeleria glauca, which is part of the grass family Poaceae. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this unique plant from environmental threats and habitat loss.
