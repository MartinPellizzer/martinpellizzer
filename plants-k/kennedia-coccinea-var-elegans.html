Kennedia coccinea var. elegans

Kennedia coccinea var. elegans, commonly known as the Elegant Kennedia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange-red tubular flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and is typically found in sandy or rocky soils. It is a member of the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
