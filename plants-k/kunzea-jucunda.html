Kunzea jucunda

Kunzea jucunda, commonly known as the slender kunzea, is a small evergreen shrub native to Western Australia.

It typically grows in coastal and sandy soils, often found in heathlands and coastal dunes. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are narrow and glossy, providing a dense canopy that supports local wildlife.

Kunzea jucunda is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitat.
