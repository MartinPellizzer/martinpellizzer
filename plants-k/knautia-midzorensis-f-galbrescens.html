Knautia midzorensis f. galbrescens

Knautia midzorensis f. galbrescens, commonly known as the Galbrescens form of the Midzorensis knautia, is a horticultural variant of a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the former Yugoslavia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and profuse display of pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features finely divided, fern-like leaves that are often covered in a soft, silvery down. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This form is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to temperate climates.
