Kania nettotensis

Kania nettotensis, commonly known as the West Indian nettle, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean region.

It belongs to the family Urticaceae, which includes various species of nettles. The plant is characterized by its finely hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges. It produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters.

Kania nettotensis is typically found in moist, shaded areas such as forests and along stream banks.
