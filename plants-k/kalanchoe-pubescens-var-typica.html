Kalanchoe pubescens var. typica

Kalanchoe pubescens var. typica, commonly known as the woolly kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves covered in soft, white hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow during the winter months. It is a low-maintenance plant that thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
