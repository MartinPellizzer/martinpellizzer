Korallia tereticaulos

Korallia tereticaulos, commonly known as the narrow-leaved coral plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features compact, branching stems that produce small, clustered flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are covered in a fine, powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and resilience, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
