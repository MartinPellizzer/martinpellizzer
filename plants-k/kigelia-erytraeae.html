Kigelia erytraeae

Kigelia erytraeae, commonly known as the sausage tree, is a deciduous tree native to Africa.

It is characterized by its long, sausage-shaped, woody fruits that hang from the branches. The plant has large, palmate leaves and produces fragrant, white flowers with a strong scent. The bark is thick and often cracked, providing a distinctive appearance.

It is valued for its medicinal properties and is also used in traditional crafts and as a source of timber.
