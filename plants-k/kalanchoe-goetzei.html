Kalanchoe goetzei

Kalanchoe goetzei, commonly known as the Goetze's kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow during the winter months. It is a member of the Crassulaceae family, known for its ability to propagate through offsets and leaf cuttings.

Kalanchoe goetzei is popular as a ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements.
