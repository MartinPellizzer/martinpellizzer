Koelpinia macrantha

Koelpinia macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered koelpinia, is a member of the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbellate inflorescences.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant features slender, hairy stems that produce clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. Its leaves are pinnately divided, with a distinctive, feathery appearance.

Koelpinia macrantha is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its delicate foliage and subtle floral display.
