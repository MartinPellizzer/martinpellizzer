Klainedoxa gabonensis var. oblongifolia

Klainedoxa gabonensis var. oblongifolia, commonly known as the Gabon wax tree, is a shrub native to Central Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, waxy foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The leaves are typically oblong in shape, hence the variety name, and are arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental qualities in horticulture.
