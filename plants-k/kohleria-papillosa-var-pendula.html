Kohleria papillosa var. pendula

Kohleria papillosa var. pendula, commonly known as the hanging kohleria, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and showy, tubular flowers.
