Koeleria gracilis var. pubiculmis

Koeleria gracilis var. pubiculmis, commonly known as the pubescent slender grass, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Poaceae family, characterized by its fine, grass-like foliage and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as fields and roadsides. Its leaves are slender and covered with short, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It produces small, pale blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.
