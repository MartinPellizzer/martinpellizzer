Knema missionis

Knema missionis, commonly known as the Mission Island knema, is a small evergreen tree native to the tropical rainforests of Indonesia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its simple, elliptical leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant produces dark, glossy berries that are an important food source for local wildlife. Knema missionis typically grows in low light conditions and thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

Its botanical features make it a unique and ecologically significant species in its native habitat.
