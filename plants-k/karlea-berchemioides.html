Karlea berchemioides

Karlea berchemioides, commonly known as the Cape jasmine or African jasmine, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has small, oval leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and birds.
