Knema globularia

Knema globularia, commonly known as the globularia or white wood.

It is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers and smooth, dark green leaves. Its fruits are small, woody capsules that contain aromatic seeds.

The tree is valued for its timber and is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
