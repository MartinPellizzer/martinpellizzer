Krainzia pottsii

Krainzia pottsii, commonly known as the Potts' krainzia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and white. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice among gardeners interested in native flora.
