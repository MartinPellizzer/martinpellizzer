Kentrothamnus foliosus

Kentrothamnus foliosus, commonly known as the narrow-leafed wattle, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. It has small, oval-shaped leaves that are often clustered at the base of the stems.

Kentrothamnus foliosus is often used in landscaping due to its hardy nature and attractive yellow blooms.
