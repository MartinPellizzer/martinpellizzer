Kobresia siamensis

Kobresia siamensis, commonly known as the Siam kobresia, is a low-growing perennial grass native to Southeast Asia.

It thrives in wet, marshy environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. Its flowering stems produce small, spike-like inflorescences that bloom during the rainy season.

Kobresia siamensis plays an important role in stabilizing wetland soils and supporting local biodiversity.
