Koenigia mollis var. frondosa

Koenigia mollis var. frondosa, commonly known as the silverleaf stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the coastal regions of South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically silver or gray in color, covered in a fine layer of white powder. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it an ideal plant for xeriscaping.
