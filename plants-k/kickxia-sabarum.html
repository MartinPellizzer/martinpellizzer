Kickxia sabarum

Kickxia sabarum, commonly known as the rock dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in dry, sunny habitats. Its leaves are lance-shaped and hairy, while its flowers are yellow and dandelion-like.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
