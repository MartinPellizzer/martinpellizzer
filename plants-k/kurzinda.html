Kurzinda

Kurzinda, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 10-30 centimeters and prefers bright, indirect light. Kurzinda is popular as a houseplant due to its ease of care and long blooming period.

Its flowers come in a variety of colors, including purple, pink, white, and red.
