Kickxia commutata var. filiformis

Kickxia commutata var. filiformis, commonly known as the slender hairgrass, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, thread-like leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers that typically bloom in early summer. The plant grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. It is a member of the Poaceae family, which includes grasses and sedges.
