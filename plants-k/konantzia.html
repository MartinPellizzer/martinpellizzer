Konantzia

Konantzia, commonly known as the desert thorn, is a hardy shrub native to arid regions of the Middle East and North Africa.

It is characterized by its small, spiny leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant is adapted to survive in extreme drought conditions, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems. Its roots can extend deep into the soil to access moisture, making it highly resilient.

Konantzia is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
