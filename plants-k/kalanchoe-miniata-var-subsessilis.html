Kalanchoe miniata var. subsessilis

Kalanchoe miniata var. subsessilis, commonly known as the compact kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish or reddish tinge. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of pink, red, and orange during the winter months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its compact growth habit and ornamental value in gardens and indoor settings.
