Kengia hancei var. jeholensis

Kengia hancei var. jeholensis, commonly known as the Jehol kengia, is a unique plant species native to China.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has a woody stem with a smooth, reddish-brown bark. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a velvety texture.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
