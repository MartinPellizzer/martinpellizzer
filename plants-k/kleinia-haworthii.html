Kleinia haworthii

Kleinia haworthii, commonly known as the silverleaf dandelion, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a basal rosette of fleshy, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by fluffy, wind-dispersed seeds. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Kleinia haworthii is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its hardiness and ornamental value.
