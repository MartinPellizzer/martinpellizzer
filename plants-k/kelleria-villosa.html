Kelleria villosa

Kelleria villosa, commonly known as the hairy kelleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grassland and rocky areas. The plant features a hairy stem and lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. Its small, white flowers are arranged in spikes and bloom in spring.

Kelleria villosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.
