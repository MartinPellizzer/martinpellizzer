Knoxia mollis var. hookeriana

Knoxia mollis var. hookeriana, commonly known as Hooker's knoxia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It typically grows to a height of about 30-60 centimeters and features slender, upright stems covered in soft, hairy foliage. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink to purple, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly serrated edge, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sun, making it a suitable choice for garden cultivation.
