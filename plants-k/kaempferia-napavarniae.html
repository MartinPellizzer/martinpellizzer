Kaempferia napavarniae

Kaempferia napavarniae, commonly known as the Thai orchid or Phuket orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, often colorful flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, with rhizomatous stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually borne on upright spikes and display a range of hues, including red, pink, and white.

Kaempferia napavarniae is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its potential medicinal properties.
