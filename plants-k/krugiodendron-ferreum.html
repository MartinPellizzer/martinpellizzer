Krugiodendron ferreum

Krugiodendron ferreum, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and clusters of fragrant, yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has a straight, slender trunk and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a leathery texture.

Krugiodendron ferreum is also valued for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its tolerance to urban environments.
