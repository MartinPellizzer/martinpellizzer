Kydia zizyphifolia

Kydia zizyphifolia, commonly known as the Chinese thornapple, is a deciduous shrub native to China and parts of Southeast Asia.

It features compound leaves with serrated edges and produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters during the spring. The plant is characterized by its spiny stems and is often found in dry, rocky habitats. Its fruits are fleshy berries that mature to a dark red color, attracting various wildlife.

Kydia zizyphifolia is valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
