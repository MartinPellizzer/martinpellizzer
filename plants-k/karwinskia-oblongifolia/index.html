Karwinskia Oblongifolia

Karwinskia oblongifolia is a rare and striking plant native to the high altitudes of southern Mexico. It is known for its unique, elongated leaves that resemble narrow, green cigars. This species thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in cloud forests. Due to its limited habitat and slow growth, it is considered endangered. Its distinctive appearance and ecological significance make it a fascinating subject for botanical study.
