Koeleria polyantha

Koeleria polyantha, commonly known as the pink oat grass, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, pinkish to purple flowers in dense spikes. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in alpine meadows and is adapted to cold, arid environments.

Koeleria polyantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
