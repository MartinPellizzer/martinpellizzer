Keraselma reniforme

Keraselma reniforme, commonly known as the kidneyleaf plant, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which are typically green and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Keraselma reniforme is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage.
