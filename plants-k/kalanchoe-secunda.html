Kalanchoe secunda

Kalanchoe secunda, commonly known as the silver leaf kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of white, powdery bloom, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow during the winter months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light.

Kalanchoe secunda is popular as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and ease of care.
