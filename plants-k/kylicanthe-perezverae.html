Kylicanthe perezverae

Kylicanthe perezverae, commonly known as the Zambian flame tree, is a striking tropical plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that resemble flames, which bloom in clusters during the rainy season. The plant has compound leaves with long, narrow leaflets that provide a lush green canopy. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique appearance and seasonal blooming make it a notable species in botanical gardens and landscapes.
