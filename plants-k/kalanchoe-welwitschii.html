Kalanchoe welwitschii

Kalanchoe welwitschii, commonly known as the Welwitschii kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that store water, enabling it to thrive in drought conditions. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, typically during the summer months. It grows as a shrub or small tree, often reaching heights of up to 3 meters.

Kalanchoe welwitschii is well adapted to hot, dry climates and requires minimal care once established.
