Kleinia polycotoma

Kleinia polycotoma, commonly known as the Cape rose, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, compact growth with fleshy, gray-green leaves that are covered in fine white hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Kleinia polycotoma is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and resilience.
