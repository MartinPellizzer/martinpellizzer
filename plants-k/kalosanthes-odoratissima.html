Kalosanthes odoratissima

Kalosanthes odoratissima, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pendulous flowers that emit a sweet, spicy fragrance. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are long and strap-like, and it produces small, clustered blooms throughout the year.

Due to its ornamental value and unique scent, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
