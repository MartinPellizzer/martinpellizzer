Kerria japonica f. plena

Kerria japonica f. plena, commonly known as the Japanese kerria double form, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its showy, double-layered flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant features broad, dark green leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
