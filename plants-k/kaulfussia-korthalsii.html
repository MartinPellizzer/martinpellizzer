Kaulfussia korthalsii

Kaulfussia korthalsii, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, often with a distinctive dark eye at the base. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Kaulfussia korthalsii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
