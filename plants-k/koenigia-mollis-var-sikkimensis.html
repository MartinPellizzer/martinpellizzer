Koenigia mollis var. sikkimensis

Koenigia mollis var. sikkimensis, commonly known as the Sikkim sandmat, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Himalayan region.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, succulent leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to high-altitude environments.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
