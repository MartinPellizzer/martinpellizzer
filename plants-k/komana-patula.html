Komana patula

Komana patula, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to yellow and orange. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces seeds in a fluffy, disk-shaped structure.

Komana patula is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
