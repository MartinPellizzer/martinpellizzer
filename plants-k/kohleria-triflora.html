Kohleria triflora

Kohleria triflora, commonly known as the three-flowered kohleria, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Kohleria triflora is popular among indoor gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
