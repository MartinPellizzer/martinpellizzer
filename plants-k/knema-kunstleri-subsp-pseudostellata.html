Knema kunstleri subsp. pseudostellata

Knema kunstleri subsp.

pseudostellata, commonly known as the white knema, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia. It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, aromatic leaves and showy white flowers.

The plant produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
