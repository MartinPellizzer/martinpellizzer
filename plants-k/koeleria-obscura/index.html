Koeleria Obscura

Koeleria obscura is a small, resilient grass species native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America. It thrives in harsh environments, often found in deserts, prairies, and rocky soils where few plants can survive. This plant is known for its compact growth habit and ability to withstand extreme temperatures and limited water availability. Its tiny, spike-like flowers bloom in early spring, adding a subtle beauty to the landscape. Despite its modest appearance, Koeleria obscura plays an important role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife.
