Kalanchoe varians

Kalanchoe varians, commonly known as the variable kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the edges. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, typically during the winter months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Kalanchoe varians is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
