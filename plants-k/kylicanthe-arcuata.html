Kylicanthe arcuata

Kylicanthe arcuata, commonly known as the curved-leaf hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a waxy surface and a strong, aromatic scent. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Kylicanthe arcuata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
