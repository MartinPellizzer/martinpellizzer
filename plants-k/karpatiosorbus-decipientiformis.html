Karpatiosorbus decipientiformis

Karpatiosorbus decipientiformis, commonly known as the Carpathian rowan, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the Carpathian Mountains in Central and Eastern Europe. The plant is characterized by its small, white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by bright red berries in the autumn. It typically grows in forested areas and is known for its ornamental value.

The species is also valued for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
