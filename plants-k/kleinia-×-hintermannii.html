Kleinia × hintermannii

Kleinia × hintermannii, commonly known as the hybrid ice plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is often cultivated in gardens and rockeries.
