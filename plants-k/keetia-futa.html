Keetia futa

Keetia futa, commonly known as the African buttercup, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its showy, yellow flowers with five petals. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are palmately lobed, and it produces small, green fruits that contain seeds.
