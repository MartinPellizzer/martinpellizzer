Karatas candida

Karatas candida, commonly known as the white karatas, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

The fruit is a dry capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
