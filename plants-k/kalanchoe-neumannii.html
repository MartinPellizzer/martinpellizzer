Kalanchoe neumannii

Kalanchoe neumannii, commonly known as the Neumann's kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating to store water. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers during the winter months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Kalanchoe neumannii is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.
