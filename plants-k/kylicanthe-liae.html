Kylicanthe liae

Kylicanthe liae, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has compound leaves with lance-shaped leaflets and produces small, round fruits that turn from green to red as they mature. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental species.

Its rapid growth and vibrant blooms make it a valuable addition to gardens and landscapes.
