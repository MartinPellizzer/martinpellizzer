Kalanchoe miniata var. confertifolia

Kalanchoe miniata var. confertifolia, commonly known as the compact kalanchoe, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings, and it produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems. It blooms in winter, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a desirable addition to rock gardens and containers.
