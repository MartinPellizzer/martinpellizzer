Kefersteinia wercklei

Kefersteinia wercklei, commonly known as the rock cress, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers in early spring. Its leaves are basal, lance-shaped, and covered in a fine layer of white wax.
