Kopsia lancibracteolata

Kopsia lancibracteolata, commonly known as the narrow bract kopsia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has lance-shaped leaves and produces small, bract-like structures that surround its flowers. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Kopsia lancibracteolata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a garden plant.
