Kigelia moosa

Kigelia moosa, commonly known as the sausage tree, is a deciduous tree native to eastern and southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, woody, sausage-shaped fruits that hang in clusters. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth texture. Its flowers are large, fragrant, and typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color.

The tree is valued for its unique fruit, which is used in traditional medicine and as a source of edible seeds.
