Kurrimia robusta var. roxburghii

Kurrimia robusta var. roxburghii, commonly known as the Roxburgh's kurrimia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a velvety texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
