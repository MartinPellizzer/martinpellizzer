Kleinia picticaulis

Kleinia picticaulis, commonly known as the painted ice plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine, white powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
