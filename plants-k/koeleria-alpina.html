Koeleria alpina

Koeleria alpina, commonly known as the alpine grass or alpine koeleria, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the northern hemisphere.

It is a perennial grass that typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in cold and arid conditions. The plant features slender, upright stems with small, narrow leaves and produces delicate, spike-like flower clusters. Koeleria alpina is known for its ability to withstand harsh climates, making it a hardy species often found in alpine meadows.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important component of high-altitude ecosystems.
