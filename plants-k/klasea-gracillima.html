Klasea gracillima

Klasea gracillima, commonly known as the slender klasea, is a flowering plant native to the southern regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, grass-like foliage and produces small, clustered inflorescences. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are usually pale green or white, and it is known for its drought tolerance.

Klasea gracillima is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
