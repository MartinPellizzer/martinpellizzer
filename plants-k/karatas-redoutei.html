Karatas redoutei

Karatas redoutei, commonly known as the Redoute's karatas, is a species of flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Kazakhstan and Uzbekistan. The plant is characterized by its vibrant red to purple flowers, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to gardens in suitable climates.

Its ornamental value has led to its cultivation in botanical gardens and as a decorative plant.
