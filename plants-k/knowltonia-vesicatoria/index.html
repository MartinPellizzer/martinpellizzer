Knowltonia Vesicatoria

Knowltonia vesicatoria is a rare and striking member of the orchid family found primarily in the southeastern United States. This unique plant is known for its vibrant, colorful flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires specific conditions to flourish. Due to its limited habitat and threats from development, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this beautiful and ecologically important species.
