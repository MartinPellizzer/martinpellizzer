Karos

Karos, commonly known as the karos plant, is a unique species native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, spiral-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot. Its flowers, which bloom in shades of purple and pink, attract various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The karos plant is also valued for its ornamental beauty and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
