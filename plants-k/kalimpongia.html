Kalimpongia

Kalimpongia, commonly known as the ghost orchid, is a rare and enigmatic flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale green, with a unique, almost translucent appearance. Kalimpongia is highly sensitive to environmental changes, making it vulnerable to habitat destruction and climate shifts.
