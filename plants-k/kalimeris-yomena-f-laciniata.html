Kalimeris yomena f. laciniata

Kalimeris yomena f. laciniata, commonly known as the Japanese aster or lace-leaf aster, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant features showy yellow to orange flower heads that bloom in late summer to early autumn. Its foliage is distinctive for its finely cut, lace-like leaves, which give it a delicate appearance.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens due to its vibrant flowers and attractive foliage.
