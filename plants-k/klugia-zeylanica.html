Klugia zeylanica

Klugia zeylanica, commonly known as the Ceylon arrowroot, is a perennial herb native to Sri Lanka.

It belongs to the family Araceae and is characterized by its tuberous roots, which are the primary edible part of the plant. The plant features broad, arrow-shaped leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers. The tubers are starchy and used in various culinary applications, similar to other arrowroot species.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
