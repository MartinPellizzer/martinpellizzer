Koelera serrata

Koelera serrata, commonly known as the serrated koelera, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Acanthaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. Koelera serrata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
