Koeleria Barbinodis Var. Sclerophylla

Koeleria barbinodis var. sclerophylla is a lesser-known grass species native to arid regions of central Asia. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and drought-resistant features. This variant is particularly adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water availability. Its unique morphology and ecological role make it an interesting subject for botanical study. Despite its resilience, it remains underappreciated in both scientific and horticultural circles.
