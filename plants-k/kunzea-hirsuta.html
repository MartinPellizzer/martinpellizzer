Kunzea hirsuta

Kunzea hirsuta, commonly known as the silver kunzea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which results from dense covering of fine hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Kunzea hirsuta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in horticulture for its drought tolerance and unique appearance.
