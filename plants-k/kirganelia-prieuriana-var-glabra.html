Kirganelia prieuriana var. glabra

Kirganelia prieuriana var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth kirganelia, is a shrub native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are typically glabrous, meaning they lack hairs. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are often fragrant. Its bark is smooth and greyish, and it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates.

It is often found in moist, shaded areas within its native habitat.
