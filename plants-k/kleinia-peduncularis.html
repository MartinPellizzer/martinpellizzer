Kleinia peduncularis

Kleinia peduncularis, commonly known as the rock candelabra, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, compact growth with short, fleshy stems that form a rounded, cushion-like structure. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of yellow to orange, typically blooming in spring. Its leaves are thick and waxy, adapted to store water and withstand arid conditions.

Kleinia peduncularis thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
