Kadsura peltigera

Kadsura peltigera, commonly known as the Chinese kadsura, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, pipe-shaped flowers. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are often clustered in umbels. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a rough texture.
