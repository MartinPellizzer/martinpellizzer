Kniphofia insignis

Kniphofia insignis, commonly known as the flame flower or red hot poker, is a striking perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, sword-shaped leaves and produces striking, torch-like inflorescences of bright red or orange tubular flowers. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. Its flowers attract pollinators such as bees and birds, contributing to ecological diversity.

Kniphofia insignis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature in temperate climates.
