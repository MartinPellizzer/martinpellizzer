Kalanchoe rhombopilosa

Kalanchoe rhombopilosa, commonly known as the stonecrop kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow during the winter months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright sunlight.

Kalanchoe rhombopilosa is popular as a decorative plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
