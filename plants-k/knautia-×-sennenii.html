Knautia × sennenii

Knautia × sennenii, commonly known as the hybrid knautia, is a perennial plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range from pink to purple. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and features hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its blooming period occurs in late spring to early summer, adding vibrant color to gardens.
