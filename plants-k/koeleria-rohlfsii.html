Koeleria rohlfsii

Koeleria rohlfsii, commonly known as the Rohlfs' needlegrass, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, two-lobed flowers. It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as plains, slopes, and disturbed areas.

Koeleria rohlfsii is adapted to arid conditions and is often found in regions with low precipitation and well-drained soils.
