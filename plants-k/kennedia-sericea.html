Kennedia sericea

Kennedia sericea, commonly known as the silky Kennedia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils. The plant features narrow, silvery-green leaves and produces clusters of bright red, pea-like flowers. It is well adapted to dry conditions and is often found in heathlands and coastal dunes.

Kennedia sericea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
