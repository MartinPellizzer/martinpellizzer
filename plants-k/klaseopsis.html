Klaseopsis

Klaseopsis, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Fabaceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and deciduous leaves. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence, giving it its common name.

Klaseopsis species are valued for their ornamental appeal and are sometimes used in traditional medicine.
