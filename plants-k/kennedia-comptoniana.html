Kennedia comptoniana

Kennedia comptoniana, commonly known as the Compton's Kennedia, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 meter, featuring dense, aromatic foliage. The plant produces bright red or orange flowers that are clustered in spherical inflorescences, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and sandy environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
