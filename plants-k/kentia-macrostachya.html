Kentia macrostachya

Kentia macrostachya, commonly known as the Kentia palm, is a species native to eastern Australia.

It is a tall, slender palm with a single trunk and long, feather-like leaves that give it a graceful appearance. The plant is characterized by its dark green, glossy fronds and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and prefers indirect sunlight.

Kentia macrostachya is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
