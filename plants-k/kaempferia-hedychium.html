Kaempferia hedychium

Kaempferia hedychium, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, and it produces clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
