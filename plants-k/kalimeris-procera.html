Kalimeris procera

Kalimeris procera, commonly known as the tall composite or Chinese aster, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers bloom in shades of yellow to orange in the fall.

Kalimeris procera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
