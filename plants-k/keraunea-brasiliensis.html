Keraunea brasiliensis

Keraunea brasiliensis, commonly known as the Brazilian orchid, is a species of orchid native to the rainforests of Brazil.

It is characterized by its elegant, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with each stem producing a new growth from the base of the previous one. Its roots are specialized for absorbing nutrients from the rich, moist soil of its native habitat.

Keraunea brasiliensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
