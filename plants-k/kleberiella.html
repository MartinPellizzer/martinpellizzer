Kleberiella

Kleberiella, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to tropical Africa.

These plants are characterized by their rosettes of velvety, waxy leaves and their showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. Kleberiella species typically thrive in well-draining soil and require bright, indirect light to flourish. They are popular houseplants due to their compact size and ability to bloom throughout the year.
