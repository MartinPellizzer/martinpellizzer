Kalanchoe ritchieana

Kalanchoe ritchieana, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a succulent plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in winter. The plant has thick, waxy leaves that help it store water, adapting it to arid environments. It produces clusters of flowers in shades of red, pink, and orange, often blooming around the holiday season.

Kalanchoe ritchieana is a popular houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
