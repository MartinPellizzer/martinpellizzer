Kalanchoe beauverdii var. typica

Kalanchoe beauverdii var. typica, commonly known as the Cape Town beauty, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, which helps it store water. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often blooming in winter. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright sunlight to flourish.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for container gardening and rockeries.
