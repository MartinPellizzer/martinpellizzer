Kentia mooreana

Kentia mooreana, commonly known as the Kentia palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to the tropical rainforests of eastern Australia, particularly in Queensland. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound leaves that can reach several meters in length. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often used as an ornamental plant in gardens and indoor spaces.

Kentia mooreana is also valued for its ability to purify the air and its relatively low maintenance requirements.
