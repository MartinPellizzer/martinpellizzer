Koelzella uloptera

Koelzella uloptera, commonly known as the slender leaf orchid, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pale pink. The plant prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, thriving in moist but not waterlogged environments.
