Kyllinga capensis

Kyllinga capensis, commonly known as Cape spurge, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features erect stems covered in fine, white hairs and produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are narrow and pointed, with a slightly waxy texture.

Kyllinga capensis is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
