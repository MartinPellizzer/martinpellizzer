Kefersteinia subquadrata

Kefersteinia subquadrata, commonly known as the quadrangular stonecrop, is a member of the Crassulaceae family.

It is a succulent plant characterized by its fleshy, quadrangular stems and small, clustered leaves. Native to southern Africa, it thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and full sun exposure. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
