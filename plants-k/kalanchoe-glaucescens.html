Kalanchoe glaucescens

Kalanchoe glaucescens, commonly known as the silver velvet kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are covered in a fine, whitish coating, giving it a silvery appearance. The plant produces vibrant, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow during the winter months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light.

Kalanchoe glaucescens is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and ornamental value.
