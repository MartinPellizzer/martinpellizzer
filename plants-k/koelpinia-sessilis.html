Koelpinia sessilis

Koelpinia sessilis, commonly known as the sessile koelpinia, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or white in color. Koelpinia sessilis is often found in open habitats such as coastal cliffs, gravelly slopes, and disturbed areas.

It is a hardy species that is well adapted to dry and sunny conditions.
