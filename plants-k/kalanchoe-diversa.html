Kalanchoe diversa

Kalanchoe diversa, commonly known as the silver leaf kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems and thick, silver-green leaves that are covered in a waxy cuticle, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces vibrant clusters of tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow during the winter months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Kalanchoe diversa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and resilience.
