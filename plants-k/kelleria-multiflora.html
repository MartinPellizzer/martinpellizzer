Kelleria multiflora

Kelleria multiflora, commonly known as the many-flowered kelleria, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slight hairy texture.

Kelleria multiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
