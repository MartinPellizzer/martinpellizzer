Karpatiosorbus vallusensis

Karpatiosorbus vallusensis, commonly known as the Carpathian rowan, is a species of shrub native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces small, white flowers in early spring, followed by red berries in the autumn. It thrives in rocky and well-drained soils, often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
