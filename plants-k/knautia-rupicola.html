Knautia rupicola

Knautia rupicola, commonly known as the rock knautia, is a perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central and Eastern Europe.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range from pink to purple. Knautia rupicola is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
