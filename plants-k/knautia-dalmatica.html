Knautia dalmatica

Knautia dalmatica, commonly known as the Dalmatian knautia, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its showy, pink to purple flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and often branched.

Knautia dalmatica is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
