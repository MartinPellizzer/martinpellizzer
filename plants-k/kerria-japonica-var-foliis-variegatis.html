Kerria japonica var. foliis-variegatis

Kerria japonica var. foliis-variegatis, commonly known as the variegated Japanese kerria, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features showy yellow flowers that appear in early spring, often before the leaves emerge. The leaves are ovate with a distinctive variegated pattern of white, yellow, and green. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

This plant is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in gardens for its vibrant foliage and seasonal blooms.
