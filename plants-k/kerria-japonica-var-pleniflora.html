Kerria japonica var. pleniflora

Kerria japonica var. pleniflora, commonly known as the Japanese kerria, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and clusters of bright yellow, double flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are ovate, with a glossy green upper surface and a lighter underside. This variety is noted for its abundant flowering, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, moist soil.
