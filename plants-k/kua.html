Kua

Kua, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a tropical plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are surrounded by a spathe, a modified leaf that protects the true flowers. Kua is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in warm, humid environments.

It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its adaptability and aesthetic appeal.
