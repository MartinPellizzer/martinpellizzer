Kochia cinerea

Kochia cinerea, commonly known as silverleaf kochia, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that appear in clusters during the summer months. Kochia cinerea is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Its hardy nature makes it a common weed in agricultural and urban landscapes.
