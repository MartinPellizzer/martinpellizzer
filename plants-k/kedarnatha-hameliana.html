Kedarnatha hameliana

Kedarnatha hameliana, commonly known as the Kedarnath bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan regions of India.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes. The plant features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue, often with a violet or purple hue. Its leaves are broad, ovate, and covered in a fine, velvety texture.

Kedarnatha hameliana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
