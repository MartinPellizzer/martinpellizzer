Kali Tamariscinum

Kali tamariscinum is a striking plant known for its vibrant orange and red flowers that bloom in late summer. It is native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. This species thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a hardy choice for desert gardens. Its unique appearance and resilience have made it a popular subject in botanical studies. Despite its beauty, it requires careful cultivation to flourish in non-native environments.
