Kleinia grandiflora

Kleinia grandiflora, commonly known as the Cape rose or mountain glory, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, pink to red flowers. The plant features dense, silver-gray foliage with woolly hairs, which help it retain moisture in its arid habitat. It produces small, tubular flowers that are pollinated by insects, and its root system is adapted to store water.

Kleinia grandiflora is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and resilience.
