Kohleria rubricaulis

Kohleria rubricaulis, commonly known as the red-stemmed kohleria, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant features velvety, dark green leaves with a reddish hue on the stems, giving it its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Kohleria rubricaulis requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture to flourish.
