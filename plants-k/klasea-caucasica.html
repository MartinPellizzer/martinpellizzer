Klasea caucasica

Klasea caucasica, commonly known as Caucasian Klasea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Caucasus region.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features basal leaves that are elongated and have a rough texture, often with a bluish-green hue. Its flowering stems bear small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink.

Klasea caucasica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
