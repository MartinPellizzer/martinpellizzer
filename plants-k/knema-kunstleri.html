Knema kunstleri

Knema kunstleri, commonly known as the Sunda knema, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, white flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

Knema kunstleri is valued for its timber, which is used in furniture and construction due to its durability and fine grain.
