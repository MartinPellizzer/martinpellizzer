Kalanchoe pubescens var. decolorata

Kalanchoe pubescens var. decolorata, commonly known as the variegated kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are usually green with white or yellow variegation. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow during the winter months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in bright, indirect sunlight.

Its unique leaf patterns and seasonal blooms make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
