Kopsia scortechinii

Kopsia scortechinii, commonly known as the Malayan kopsia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant features opposite, elliptic to ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences and are usually yellow or orange in color.

Kopsia scortechinii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its showy blooms.
