Kohleria hondensis

Kohleria hondensis, commonly known as the Honduran kohleria, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is native to Honduras.

It is a flowering plant characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from violet to red. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its leaves are usually oval-shaped with a smooth texture and dark green coloration.

Kohleria hondensis thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
