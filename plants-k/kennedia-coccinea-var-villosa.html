Kennedia coccinea var. villosa

Kennedia coccinea var. villosa, commonly known as the hairy red bush, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features slender, branching stems covered in soft, hairy foliage, which gives it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in coastal and sandy environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native pollinators.
