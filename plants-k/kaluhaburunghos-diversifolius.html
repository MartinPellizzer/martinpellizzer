Kaluhaburunghos diversifolius

Kaluhaburunghos diversifolius, commonly known as the Variegated Leaf Fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its strikingly variegated foliage, featuring a combination of green, white, and yellow hues on its broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread and form dense clumps. Its fronds are typically arching, contributing to its ornamental appeal in shaded garden settings.

Kaluhaburunghos diversifolius thrives in humid environments and requires consistent moisture and indirect sunlight for optimal growth.
