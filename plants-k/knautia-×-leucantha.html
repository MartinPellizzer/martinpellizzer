Knautia × leucantha

Knautia × leucantha, commonly known as the white knautia, is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the Caprifoliaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, fluffy clusters of white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet, with lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking both ornamental value and ecological benefits.
