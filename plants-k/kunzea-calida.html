Kunzea calida

Kunzea calida, commonly known as the coastal kunzea, is a shrub native to the coastal regions of southern Australia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in dune environments. It is well adapted to coastal conditions, including salt spray and strong winds.

Kunzea calida is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in coastal landscaping.
