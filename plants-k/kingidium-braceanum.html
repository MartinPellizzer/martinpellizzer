Kingidium braceanum

Kingidium braceanum, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are velvety to the touch, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in moist forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
