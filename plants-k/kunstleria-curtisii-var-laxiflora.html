Kunstleria curtisii var. laxiflora

Kunstleria curtisii var. laxiflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered kunstleria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its delicate, elongated inflorescences that bear small, pale green to white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a tendency to form dense clumps.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
