Kohleria wageneri

Kohleria wageneri, commonly known as the wax plant, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, waxy leaves that store water, adapting it to its natural habitat. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with contrasting green or white markings. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Kohleria wageneri is also valued for its compact growth habit and ability to bloom throughout the year under proper care.
