Kitamuria

Kitamuria, commonly known as the Japanese spurge, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and small yellow flowers. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its compact growth habit and vibrant foliage. However, it contains toxic sap, so care should be taken when handling it.
