Koenigia pilosa var. pilosa

Koenigia pilosa var. pilosa, commonly known as the woolly stonecrop, is a low-growing succulent native to southern Africa.

It features dense, hairy foliage that helps it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its compact size and ornamental appearance make it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
