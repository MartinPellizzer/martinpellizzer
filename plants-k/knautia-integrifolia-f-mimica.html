Knautia integrifolia f. mimica

Knautia integrifolia f. mimica, commonly known as the mimosa knautia, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces showy, pink to purple flower heads that are composed of numerous small florets, resembling a dandelion. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.
