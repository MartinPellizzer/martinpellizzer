Koeleria recurviflora

Koeleria recurviflora, commonly known as the recurved grass or slender wild oat, is a flowering plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It is a member of the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale blue or greenish flowers, typically blooming in late summer. Koeleria recurviflora is adapted to harsh environmental conditions, including low temperatures and strong winds.
