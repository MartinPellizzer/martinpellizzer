Kalosanthes media

Kalosanthes media, commonly known as the velvet tamarind, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub that typically grows up to 2 meters in height. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a velvety texture, and its flowers are clustered in panicles. The fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release several seeds.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some cultures.
