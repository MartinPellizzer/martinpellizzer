Kohleria fruticosa

Kohleria fruticosa, commonly known as the evergreen kohleria, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and often tinged with red or purple at the base. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and greenhouses.

Kohleria fruticosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the year under suitable conditions.
