Kalimeris pinnatifida var. angustifolia

Kalimeris pinnatifida var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaved golden marguerite, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces yellow-orange flower heads with white ray florets. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or grassland habitats.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
