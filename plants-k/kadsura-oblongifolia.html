Kadsura oblongifolia

Kadsura oblongifolia, commonly known as the Chinese kadsura, is a woody vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green and emit a mild fragrance. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers followed by red, fleshy berries that are edible but often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value and medicinal properties make it a notable species in botanical gardens and herbal practices.
