Kalanchoe robynsiana

Kalanchoe robynsiana, commonly known as the Robyn's kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that store water, typical of desert plants. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red during the spring and summer months. It is a member of the Crassulaceae family and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Kalanchoe robynsiana is drought-tolerant and thrives in bright, indirect sunlight.
