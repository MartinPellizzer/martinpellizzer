Kolbea Breyniana

Blue ice plant, scientifically known as Kolbea breyniana, is a striking succulent native to South Africa. This unique plant is named for its ability to display a bluish or icy appearance under certain light conditions. It thrives in arid environments and is well adapted to withstand drought and extreme temperatures. The plant features fleshy, ribbed leaves that store water and a compact, rounded growth habit. Its striking coloration and resilience make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
