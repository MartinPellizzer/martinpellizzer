Katherinea verrucifera

Katherinea verrucifera, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, waxy leaves and showy, clustered flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
