Kotschya thymodora subsp. thymodora

Kotschya thymodora subsp.

thymodora, commonly known as the thyme-leaved kotschya, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, herbaceous foliage.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense heads, typical of the daisy family. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.
