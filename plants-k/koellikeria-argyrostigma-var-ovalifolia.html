Koellikeria argyrostigma var. ovalifolia

Koellikeria argyrostigma var. ovalifolia, commonly known as the silver-stemmed ovalleaf koellikeria, is a shrub native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery, glandular stems and oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, rounded fruits. It thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and is often found in dune environments.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
