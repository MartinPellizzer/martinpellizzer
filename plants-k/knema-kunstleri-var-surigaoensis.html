Knema kunstleri var. surigaoensis

Knema kunstleri var. surigaoensis, commonly known as the Surigao knema, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the Philippines, specifically found in the coastal areas of Surigao del Norte. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. It produces small white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, followed by oval-shaped fruits.

This variety is often found in moist, lowland forest environments and is valued for its ornamental and potential medicinal properties.
