Kalosanthes versicolor

Kalosanthes versicolor, commonly known as the variable kalosanthes, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its strikingly colorful and often variegated foliage. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its flowers are small but numerous, often displaying a range of hues from white to pink and purple.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
