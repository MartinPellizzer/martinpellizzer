Koyamacalia palmatisecta

Koyamacalia palmatisecta, commonly known as the broadleaf koyama palm, is a species of flowering plant in the family Arecaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that are deeply divided, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its trunk is slender and often exhibits a smooth, grayish-brown bark.

This palm is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and as a houseplant.
