Kielmeyera rubriflora var. rubriflora

Kielmeyera rubriflora var. rubriflora, commonly known as the red-flowered kielmeyera, is a species native to the Brazilian Atlantic Forest.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree with lanceolate, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces striking red-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. Its fruits are small, woody capsules that release seeds when mature.

This plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
