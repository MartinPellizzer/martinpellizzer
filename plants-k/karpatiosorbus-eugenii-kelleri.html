Karpatiosorbus eugenii-kelleri

Karpatiosorbus eugenii-kelleri, commonly known as the Carpathian barberry, is a shrub native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It belongs to the family Rosaceae and is characterized by its thorny stems and clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant produces red, fleshy berries that are edible for birds and some mammals. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to temperate climates.
