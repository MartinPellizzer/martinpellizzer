Kerria japonica f. aureovariegata

Kerria japonica f. aureovariegata, commonly known as the golden variegated kerria, is a decorative shrub native to Japan.

It features showy, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, often with a subtle pink or red hue. The leaves are typically variegated, displaying shades of white, yellow, and green, adding visual interest throughout the growing season. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and is adaptable to a variety of soil types.

This plant is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
