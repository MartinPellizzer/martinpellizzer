Kadsura calophylla

Kadsura calophylla, commonly known as the Chinese kadsura, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Aristolochiaceae and is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and unique, intricate flowers. However, the plant contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
