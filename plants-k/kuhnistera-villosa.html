Kuhnistera villosa

Kuhnistera villosa, commonly known as the hairy kuhnistera, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and taxonomic study.
