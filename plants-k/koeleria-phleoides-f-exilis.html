Koeleria phleoides f. exilis

Koeleria phleoides f. exilis, commonly known as the slender leaf grass, is a flowering plant species belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, spikelet-based inflorescences. This variety is typically found in arid or semi-arid environments, where it thrives in well-drained soils. The plant has a low-growing habit and is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
