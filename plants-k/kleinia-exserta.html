Kleinia exserta

Kleinia exserta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rock fig, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, compact growth with fleshy, narrow leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

Kleinia exserta is often used in rock gardens due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
