Karatas lagopus

Karatas lagopus, commonly known as the Himalayan snow lotus, is a perennial flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, cushion-like growth habit. The plant produces small, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in cold, arid environments and is adapted to withstand harsh mountain conditions.

Due to its medicinal properties and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species.
