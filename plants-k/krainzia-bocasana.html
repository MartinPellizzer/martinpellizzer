Krainzia bocasana

Krainzia bocasana, commonly known as the Bocas Island krainzia, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean island of Bocas del Toro.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils and salt spray. It produces small, showy yellow flowers that bloom throughout the dry season.

Krainzia bocasana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens in tropical regions.
