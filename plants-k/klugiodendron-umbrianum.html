Klugiodendron umbrianum

Klugiodendron umbrianum, commonly known as the Umbrian flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery red or orange flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are typically ovate or elliptical in shape. Its bark is smooth and ranges from gray to reddish-brown in color.

Klugiodendron umbrianum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and parks.
