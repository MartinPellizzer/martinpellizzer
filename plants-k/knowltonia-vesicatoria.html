Knowltonia vesicatoria

Knowltonia vesicatoria, commonly known as the sweetshrub, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the family Caprifoliaceae and is characterized by its fragrant, showy flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The sweetshrub is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in partial shade.
