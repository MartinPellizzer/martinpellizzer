Kleinia walkeri

Kleinia walkeri, commonly known as the silver leaf chandelier, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive silvery appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a subtle ornamental touch. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
