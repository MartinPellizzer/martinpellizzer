Krigia × shinnersiana

Krigia × shinnersiana, commonly known as the Shinners' krigia, is a hybrid perennial plant native to the central United States.

It is characterized by its delicate, daisy-like flowers that bloom in early spring, typically in shades of white and yellow. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and native plant landscapes.

Krigia × shinnersiana is also valued for its role in supporting local pollinators and its low maintenance requirements.
