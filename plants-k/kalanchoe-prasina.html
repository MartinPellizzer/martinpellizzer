Kalanchoe prasina

Kalanchoe prasina, commonly known as the silver leaf kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to Madagascar.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are covered in a powdery white coating, giving it a distinctive silvery appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the spring and summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Kalanchoe prasina is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
