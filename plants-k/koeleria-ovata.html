Koeleria ovata

Koeleria ovata, commonly known as the ovate spikegrass, is a perennial grass native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It typically grows in open habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant has slender, erect stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often slightly hairy. Its inflorescence consists of a spike-like cluster of small, spikelet-bearing branches.

Koeleria ovata is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various insects and small animals.
