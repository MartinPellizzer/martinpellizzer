Kuhnistera

Kuhnistera, commonly known as the ghost orchid, is a rare and enigmatic plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a type of orchid that lacks traditional leaves and instead absorbs nutrients through its roots. The plant produces striking white or pale pink flowers with a delicate, almost translucent appearance. It thrives in wet, swampy environments and is highly sensitive to changes in its habitat.

Due to its rarity and specific ecological requirements, it is considered a conservation concern.
