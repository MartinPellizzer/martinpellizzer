Kaempferia macrochlamys

Kaempferia macrochlamys, commonly known as the giant flame orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, colorful inflorescences. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and produces showy flowers with a unique, often fiery red or orange coloration. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Kaempferia macrochlamys is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
