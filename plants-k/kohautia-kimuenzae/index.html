Kohautia Kimuenzae

Kohautia kimuenzae is a rare and fascinating plant native to the high altitudes of Kenya. It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is known for its striking appearance and unique adaptations to its environment. This species was first discovered in the 1980s and has since captivated botanists and plant enthusiasts alike. Its small size and vibrant foliage make it a standout among tropical plants. Despite its beauty, Kohautia kimuenzae remains elusive and is critically endangered due to habitat loss and climate change.
