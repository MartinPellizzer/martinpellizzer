Kornasia

Kornasia, commonly known as the golden bell tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and produces small, woody pods that contain several seeds. Kornasia thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
