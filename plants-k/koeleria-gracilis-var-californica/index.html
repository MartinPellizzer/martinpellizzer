Koeleria Gracilis Var. Californica

Koeleria gracilis var. californica is a delicate flowering plant native to the western United States. It is known for its slender stems and clusters of small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in early summer. This variety thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Its low-growing habit and drought tolerance make it ideal for arid landscapes. Despite its modest appearance, it adds a touch of elegance to any garden setting.
