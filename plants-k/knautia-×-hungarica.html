Knautia × hungarica

Knautia × hungarica, commonly known as the Hungarian knautia, is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the Caprifoliaceae family.

It is characterized by its clustered, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, with slender, hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Native to Central Europe, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
