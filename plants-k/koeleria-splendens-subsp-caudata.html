Koeleria splendens subsp. caudata

Koeleria splendens subsp.

caudata, commonly known as the tufted feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to North America. It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and fluffy, silver-gray inflorescences that give it a distinctive appearance.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in native plant gardens.
