Kalanchoe aliciae

Kalanchoe aliciae, commonly known as the "Alicia's Kalanchoe," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of orange, red, and yellow, often in late winter or early spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright sunlight to flourish.

Kalanchoe aliciae is drought-tolerant and suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
