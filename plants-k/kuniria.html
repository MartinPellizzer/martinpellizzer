Kuniria

Kuniria, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its striking, drooping clusters of yellow flowers. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. Kuniria is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can also be used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
