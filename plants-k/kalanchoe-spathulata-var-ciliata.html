Kalanchoe spathulata var. ciliata

Kalanchoe spathulata var. ciliata, commonly known as the ciliate arrowleaf Kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are arrowhead-shaped with serrated edges, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow during the winter months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its foliage and flowers add both ornamental value and a touch of exotic beauty to any garden setting.
