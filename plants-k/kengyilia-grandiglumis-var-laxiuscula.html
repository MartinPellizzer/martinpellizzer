Kengyilia grandiglumis var. laxiuscula

Kengyilia grandiglumis var. laxiuscula, commonly known as the broad-leaved kengyilia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of western China.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
