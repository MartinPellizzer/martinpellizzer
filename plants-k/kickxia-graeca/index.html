Kickxia Graeca

Kickxia graeca is a striking member of the Amaryllidaceae family known for its vibrant blue flowers and unique appearance. Native to the mountainous regions of Europe, this plant thrives in rocky and limestone soils. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a rosette pattern. The plant produces tall, slender stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped blooms. Despite its beauty, Kickxia graeca is relatively rare and requires specific environmental conditions to flourish.
