Koeleria barrosii

Koeleria barrosii, commonly known as the Barros' grass, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Argentina and Chile.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, typically with a bluish-green hue.

Koeleria barrosii is often found in arid or semi-arid environments, adapting well to poor soil conditions.
