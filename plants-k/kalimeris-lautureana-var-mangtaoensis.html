Kalimeris lautureana var. mangtaoensis

Kalimeris lautureana var. mangtaoensis, commonly known as the Mangtao bellflower, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white to pale pink petals. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in mountainous regions. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
