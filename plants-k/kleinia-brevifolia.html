Kleinia brevifolia

Kleinia brevifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ice plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It thrives in arid and coastal environments, adapting well to poor soil conditions.

Kleinia brevifolia is often used in rock gardens and as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.
