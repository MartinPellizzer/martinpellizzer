Kalanchoe alternans var. lanceolata

Kalanchoe alternans var. lanceolata, commonly known as the lance-leaf kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, lance-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red, often blooming in winter. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

This species is drought-tolerant and suitable for rock gardens or container planting.
