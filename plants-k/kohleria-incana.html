Kohleria incana

Kohleria incana, commonly known as the silverleaf kohleria, is a species native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Gesneriaceae family, known for its ornamental value. Kohleria incana thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
