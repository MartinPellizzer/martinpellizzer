Katherinea cymbidioides

Katherinea cymbidioides, commonly known as the Christmas orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom during the holiday season, hence its common name. The plant has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in tropical and subtropical climates. Its leaves are strap-like and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Katherinea cymbidioides is often cultivated for its beauty and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
