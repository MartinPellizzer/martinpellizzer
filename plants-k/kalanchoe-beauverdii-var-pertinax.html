Kalanchoe beauverdii var. pertinax

Kalanchoe beauverdii var. pertinax, commonly known as the Cape violet, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that store water, adapting to arid environments. The plant produces vibrant violet or purple flowers during the winter months. It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.
