Kaempferia chayanii

Kaempferia chayanii, commonly known as the Purple Orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking violet or purple flowers. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial herb with rhizomatous roots and sword-shaped leaves. Its inflorescence consists of a cluster of showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Kaempferia chayanii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
