Kaempferia fongyuensis

Kaempferia fongyuensis, commonly known as the Fong Yu orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in the tropical regions of Thailand and Malaysia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous growth habit. The plant produces striking, colorful flowers that are typically pink or red, with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
