Kalimeris angustifolia

Kalimeris angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf fleabane, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features upright stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellow-centered composite flowers in late summer, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or borders.

Kalimeris angustifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in gardens.
