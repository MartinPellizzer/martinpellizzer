Koeleria idahoensis var. pseudocristatoides

Koeleria idahoensis var. pseudocristatoides, commonly known as the Idaho hairgrass, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, two-lobed flowers, typical of the genus Koeleria.
