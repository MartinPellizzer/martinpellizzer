Kochia Macroptera

Kochia macroptera is a unique flowering plant known for its striking, feathery seed heads that catch the wind. Native to arid and semi-arid regions, it thrives in harsh environments with minimal water. This plant is often mistaken for a weed due to its resilience and ability to spread easily. Despite its common appearance, it plays an important role in supporting local wildlife and soil stability. Its beauty and adaptability make it a subject of both ecological and aesthetic interest.
