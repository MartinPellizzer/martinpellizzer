Kiggelaria serrata

Kiggelaria serrata, commonly known as the African snowberry, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its simple, opposite, and often serrated leaves. The plant produces small, white, clustered flowers that bloom in spring. Its berries, which are typically red or yellow, are edible and used in local cuisines.

Kiggelaria serrata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
