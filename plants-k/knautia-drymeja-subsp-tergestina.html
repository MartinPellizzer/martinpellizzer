Knautia drymeja subsp. tergestina

Knautia drymeja subsp.

tergestina, commonly known as the Sicilian knautia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region, particularly Sicily. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of pink to purple flowers.

The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as meadows and slopes. Its foliage consists of lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it blooms from late spring to early summer.
