Kalanchoe heckelii

Kalanchoe heckelii, commonly known as the Cape fuchsia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple, often blooming in spring. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.

Kalanchoe heckelii is popular as a ornamental plant due to its vibrant flowers and low maintenance requirements.
