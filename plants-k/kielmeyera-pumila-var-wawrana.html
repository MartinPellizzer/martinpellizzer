Kielmeyera pumila var. wawrana

Kielmeyera pumila var. wawrana, commonly known as the Wawrana kielmeyera, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It features glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its fruit is a capsule containing several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
