Klasea monardii

Klasea monardii, commonly known as the showy geranium, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces showy, pink to red flowers during the summer months. The plant has opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Klasea monardii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native plant gardens.
