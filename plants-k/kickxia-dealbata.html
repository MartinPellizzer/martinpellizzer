Kickxia dealbata

Kickxia dealbata, commonly known as the white-flowered bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, branching stems that often grow in open, sunny habitats. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue, blooming from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Kickxia dealbata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
