Kentia elegans

Kentia elegans, commonly known as the Kentia palm, is a tropical evergreen plant native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that grow in a rosette pattern. The plant has a slow growth rate, making it ideal for indoor cultivation. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Kentia palms are also known for their air-purifying qualities and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
