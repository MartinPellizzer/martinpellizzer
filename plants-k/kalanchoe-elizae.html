Kalanchoe elizae

Kalanchoe elizae, commonly known as the Christmas Star, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, which help it store water and survive in arid conditions. The plant produces vibrant, star-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically blooming in winter. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Kalanchoe elizae is also noted for its ability to propagate through offsets, which can be easily separated and grown into new plants.
