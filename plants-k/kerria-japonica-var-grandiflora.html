Kerria japonica var. grandiflora

Kerria japonica var. grandiflora, commonly known as the Japanese kerria, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, single flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has attractive, glossy green leaves that turn red in the fall. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a versatile ornamental plant.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it suitable for garden borders and foundation plantings.
