Kibera officinalis

Kibera officinalis, commonly known as the African moonflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, white, nocturnal blossoms that open at night and close by dawn. The plant belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is known for its aromatic leaves and flowers. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, well-drained soils.

Kibera officinalis has been used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
