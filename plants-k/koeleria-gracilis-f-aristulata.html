Koeleria gracilis f. aristulata

Koeleria gracilis f. aristulata, commonly known as the slender oatgrass form with aristulate inflorescence, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family, Poaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, spikelet-like flowers. The plant typically grows in open, dry habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. This subspecies is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
