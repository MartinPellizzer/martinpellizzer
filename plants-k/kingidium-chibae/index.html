Kingidium Chibae

Kingidium chibae is a rare and enigmatic flowering plant native to the Philippines. It belongs to the orchid family and is known for its unique and striking appearance. This plant is often found in humid, shaded environments within tropical rainforests. Due to its limited distribution and habitat loss, it is considered critically endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this remarkable species for future generations.
