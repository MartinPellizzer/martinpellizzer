Kryptostoma egregium

Kryptostoma egregium, commonly known as the golden spider plant, is a unique member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, spider-like inflorescences that resemble clusters of tiny yellow flowers. The plant produces small, bell-shaped blooms that are often surrounded by a fluffy, white bract, giving it its common name. Native to South Africa, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Kryptostoma egregium is prized for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
