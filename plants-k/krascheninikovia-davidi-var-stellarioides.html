Krascheninikovia davidi var. stellarioides

Krascheninikovia davidi var. stellarioides, commonly known as the silverleaf saxifrage, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Arctic and alpine regions of Europe and Asia.

The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in tundra and mountainous habitats.
