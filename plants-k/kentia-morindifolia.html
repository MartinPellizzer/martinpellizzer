Kentia morindifolia

Kentia morindifolia, commonly known as the Kentia palm, is a tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that give it a graceful appearance. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is often cultivated as an indoor plant due to its adaptability and aesthetic appeal.

Kentia morindifolia is also valued for its air-purifying properties and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
