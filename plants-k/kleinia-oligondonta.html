Kleinia oligondonta

Kleinia oligondonta, commonly known as the desert star or firecracker plant, is a succulent shrub native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It features compact, branching stems covered in dense clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting it to survive in dry environments. Its flowers bloom in winter, adding vibrant color to the landscape during the cooler months.

Kleinia oligondonta is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and drought tolerance.
