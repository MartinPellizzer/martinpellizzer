Kyllinga nervosa var. flava

Kyllinga nervosa var. flava, commonly known as yellow flame grass, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features upright, slender stems that are typically reddish or yellowish in color. The leaves are lance-shaped with a prominent central vein, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces small, clustered flowers that range from yellow to orange, often followed by spiky, red bracted seed heads.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
