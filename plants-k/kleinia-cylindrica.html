Kleinia cylindrica

Kleinia cylindrica, commonly known as the cylindrical Kleinia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, upright growth habit with dense clusters of narrow, cylindrical leaves that are often covered in a whitish powdery coating. The leaves are typically green to bluish-green and have a waxy texture, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments. It produces small, yellowish flowers during the summer months, adding a subtle ornamental value to the plant.

This species is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and attractive appearance.
