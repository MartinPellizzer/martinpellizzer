Koeleria splendens var. pseudorigidula

Koeleria splendens var. pseudorigidula, commonly known as the silver hairgrass, is a low-growing perennial grass native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It features fine, silver-tomentose leaves that give it a distinctive, frosty appearance. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing pale blue to purple flowers. It is adapted to rocky and sandy soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Koeleria splendens var. pseudorigidula is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance in landscaping.
