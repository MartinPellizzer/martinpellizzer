Kalanchoe spathulata var. schumacheri

Kalanchoe spathulata var. schumacheri, commonly known as the Schumacher's Kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish tint, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often blooming in winter. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

Kalanchoe spathulata var. schumacheri is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
