Kentia moluccana

Kentia moluccana, commonly known as the Kentia palm, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that give it a graceful appearance. The plant has a slow growth rate, making it ideal for indoor cultivation. Its leaves are typically dark green and can reach lengths of up to 3 meters.

Kentia palms are known for their ability to thrive in low-light conditions and require moderate watering.
