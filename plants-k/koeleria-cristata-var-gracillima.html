Koeleria cristata var. gracillima

Koeleria cristata var. gracillima, commonly known as the slender spikegrass, is a perennial grass species native to arctic and alpine regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and distinctive, spike-like inflorescence. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments. Its narrow leaves and compact stature make it a hardy and resilient species.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil in harsh environments and is of interest in ecological and botanical studies.
