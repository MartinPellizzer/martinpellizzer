Kalbreyera

Kalbreyera, commonly known as the fairy duster, is a striking member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. Kalbreyera thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-drained soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and botanical collections.
