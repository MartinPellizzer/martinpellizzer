Kippistia diffusiflora

Kippistia diffusiflora, commonly known as the spreading-flowered kippistia, is a perennial herb native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or along stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The species is notable for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its use in traditional herbal medicine.
