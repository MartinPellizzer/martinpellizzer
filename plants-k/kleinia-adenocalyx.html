Kleinia adenocalyx

Kleinia adenocalyx, commonly known as the Cape snowflake, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers during the winter months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
