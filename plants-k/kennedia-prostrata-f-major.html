Kennedia prostrata f. major

Kennedia prostrata f. major, commonly known as the large-flowered prostrate kennedia, is a low-growing shrub native to Australia.

It features prostrate or ascending stems that are covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces bright orange to red flowers that are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow, linear, and slightly hairy, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
