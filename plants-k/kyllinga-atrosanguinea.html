Kyllinga atrosanguinea

Kyllinga atrosanguinea, commonly known as black-swan sedum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its dark red or purple stems and leaves give it a striking appearance, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Kyllinga atrosanguinea is drought-tolerant and requires minimal maintenance once established.
