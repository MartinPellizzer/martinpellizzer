Kalanchoe rosei var. seyrigii

Kalanchoe rosei var. seyrigii, commonly known as the Desert rose or the Cape rose, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems and produces showy, rose-like flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. The leaves are thick and often have a waxy texture, which helps the plant retain moisture in arid environments. It blooms during the winter months, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

This plant is relatively easy to care for, requiring minimal water and well-draining soil.
