Koeleria grandiflora var. major

Koeleria grandiflora var. major, commonly known as the large-flowered koeleria, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves, with clusters of small, pinkish to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its inflorescence is a panicle, and the flowers are borne on short pedicels.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens and alpine settings.
