Koeleria alpigena

Koeleria alpigena, commonly known as alpine koeleria, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is a member of the grass family (Poaceae) and typically grows in alpine and subalpine environments. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny blue to purple flowers. It is adapted to cold climates and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils.

Koeleria alpigena is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in high-altitude ecosystems.
