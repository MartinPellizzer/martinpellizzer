Kunthia

Kunthia, commonly known as the Indian dandelion, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow composite flowers that bloom in spring. The genus includes several species, such as Kunthia indica, which is native to the Indian subcontinent. These plants are often found in arid and semi-arid regions, thriving in well-drained soils.

Kunthia species are known for their medicinal properties and are used in traditional herbal remedies.
