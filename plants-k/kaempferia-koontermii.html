Kaempferia koontermii

Kaempferia koontermii, commonly known as the Koontermii flame orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its striking, brightly colored flowers. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial herb with broad, strap-like leaves. Its inflorescence consists of several showy, red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters.

Kaempferia koontermii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
