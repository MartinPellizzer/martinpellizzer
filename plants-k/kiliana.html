Kiliana

Kiliana, commonly known as the golden trumpet vine, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, green berries that mature to a red color. Kiliana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can thrive in warm, humid climates.

It is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
