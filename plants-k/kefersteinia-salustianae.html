Kefersteinia salustianae

Kefersteinia salustianae, commonly known as the desert dandelion, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa.

The plant has a low-growing, succulent habit, with thick, fleshy leaves adapted to conserve water. It produces small yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by fluffy seeds dispersed by the wind.

Kefersteinia salustianae is well-suited to harsh environments and plays a role in stabilizing sandy soils.
