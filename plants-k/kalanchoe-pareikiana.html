Kalanchoe pareikiana

Kalanchoe pareikiana, commonly known as the silver star kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to Madagascar.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are covered in a silvery, powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss in arid conditions. The plant produces vibrant, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red during the winter months. It is well adapted to full sun and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.

Kalanchoe pareikiana is also noted for its ability to propagate through offsets and leaf cuttings.
