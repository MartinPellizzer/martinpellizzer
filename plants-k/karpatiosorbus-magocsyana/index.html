Karpatiosorbus Magocsyana

Karpatiosorbus magocsyana is a rare and unique shrub native to the Carpathian Mountains. This plant belongs to the genus Sorbus, which includes many species of rowans and whitebeams. It is known for its striking white flowers and distinctive fruiting structures. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a species of conservation concern. Studying Karpatiosorbus magocsyana offers insights into the biodiversity and ecological significance of the Carpathian region.
