Kniphofia modesta var. lutescens

Kniphofia modesta var. lutescens, commonly known as the Orange Firetorch, is a striking perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features vibrant orange-red flower spikes that rise above grass-like foliage, creating a dramatic visual impact in gardens. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are evergreen. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its showy blooms attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, contributing to both ornamental and ecological value.
