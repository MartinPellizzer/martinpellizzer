Kerria japonica f. semiplena

Kerria japonica f. semiplena, commonly known as the semi-double kerria, is a cultivar of the Japanese kerria plant.

It is characterized by its profuse flowering, producing semi-double flowers that are typically bright yellow. The plant has arching, deciduous foliage that turns red in the fall. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

This ornamental shrub is often used in gardens for its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.
