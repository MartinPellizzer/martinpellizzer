Koelreuteria elegans subsp. elegans

Koelreuteria elegans subsp.

elegans, commonly known as the Chinese lantern tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China. It is characterized by its showy, papery bracts that turn red or orange in the fall, giving it its common name.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers in clusters, followed by woody, spherical fruits that split open to reveal seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Its foliage is typically dark green and glossy, providing an attractive appearance throughout the growing season.
