Koeleria phleoides var. postiana

Koeleria phleoides var. postiana, commonly known as the plains grass or slender needlegrass, is a perennial grass native to the grasslands of North America.

It typically grows in open, disturbed areas and thrives in well-drained soils. The plant has slender, erect stems that are often covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are narrow and grass-like. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers.

This species is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various insects and small animals.
