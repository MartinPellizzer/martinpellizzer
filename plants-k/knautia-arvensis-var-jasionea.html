Knautia arvensis var. jasionea

Knautia arvensis var. jasionea, commonly known as the meadow knautia, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink to reddish-purple composite flowers. The plant typically grows in grasslands, meadows, and disturbed soils, thriving in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and often branched.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
