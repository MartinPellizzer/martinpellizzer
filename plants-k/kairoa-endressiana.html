Kairoa endressiana

Kairoa endressiana, commonly known as the slender kairoa, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils. It is a perennial shrub that can reach heights of up to 2 meters.
