Kalanchoe variifolia

Kalanchoe variifolia, commonly known as the variegated kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and yellow. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to maintain its vibrant leaf color.

Kalanchoe variifolia is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
