Kalanchoe pubescens var. alexiana

Kalanchoe pubescens var. alexiana, commonly known as the silver arrow, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. The plant produces vibrant orange or yellow flowers during the winter months, which are borne on tall, branching stems. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light.

This variety is popular as a decorative plant due to its unique foliage and long-lasting blooms.
