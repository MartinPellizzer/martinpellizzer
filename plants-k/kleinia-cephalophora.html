Kleinia cephalophora

Kleinia cephalophora, commonly known as the Cape snowbush, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, compact growth with small, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color. The plant produces showy clusters of white or pink flowers during the winter months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Kleinia cephalophora is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
