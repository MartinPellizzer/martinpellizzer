Kleinia rowleyana

Kleinia rowleyana, commonly known as the silver leaf chandelier, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of fleshy, silver-gray leaves that form a compact, bushy structure. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a subtle ornamental touch. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires ample sunlight to maintain its vibrant coloration.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
