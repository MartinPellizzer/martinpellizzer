Knema pectinata subsp. pectinata

Knema pectinata subsp.

pectinata, commonly known as the white knema, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia. It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and aromatic leaves.

The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are followed by round, fleshy fruits. Its leaves are typically elliptical with a glossy surface and are used in traditional medicine.
