Koenigia filicaulis var. villosa

Koenigia filicaulis var. villosa, commonly known as the woolly sword fern, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive, soft appearance. The plant features a creeping rhizome with upright, sword-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, silvery hairs. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or shaded areas.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for rock gardens and shaded borders in horticulture.
