Kingidium deliciosum var. bellum

Kingidium deliciosum var. bellum, commonly known as the sweet bellflower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, violet-blue flowers. The plant features hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly rough texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.
