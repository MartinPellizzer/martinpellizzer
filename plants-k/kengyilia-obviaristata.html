Kengyilia obviaristata

Kengyilia obviaristata, commonly known as the Chinese dandelion, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and hairy leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is known for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine. It produces small, wind-dispersed seeds that aid in its propagation.

Kengyilia obviaristata is valued both for its ecological role and its potential health benefits.
