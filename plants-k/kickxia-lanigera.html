Kickxia lanigera

Kickxia lanigera, commonly known as the woolly knotweed, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia and parts of Europe.

It is characterized by its hairy, cylindrical stems and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in alpine or steppe environments. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Kickxia lanigera is a perennial herb that thrives in temperate climates and is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
