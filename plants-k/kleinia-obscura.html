Kleinia obscura

Kleinia obscura, commonly known as the desert geranium, is a succulent shrub native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss in its harsh environment. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pink or red flowers during the rainy season, adding a splash of color to the landscape. Its root system is deep and extensive, allowing it to access water from deeper soil layers.

Kleinia obscura is well adapted to drought conditions, making it a resilient plant in its native habitat.
