Koeleria asiatica var. glabrifolia

Koeleria asiatica var. glabrifolia, commonly known as the smoothleaf silkygrass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Asia.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. The plant has slender, erect stems with narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its inflorescence consists of a spike-like arrangement of small, fluffy flowers that give it a delicate appearance.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in native plant landscaping.
