Kurrimia ceylanica var. montana

Kurrimia ceylanica var. montana, commonly known as the mountain kurrimia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Asia.

It is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and are typically borne on long, slender stalks. The plant features palmate leaves with lobed margins, and it thrives in well-drained, sandy or loamy soils. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes due to its striking appearance.

Kurrimia ceylanica var. montana prefers full sun to partial shade and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.
