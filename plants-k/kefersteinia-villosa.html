Kefersteinia villosa

Kefersteinia villosa, commonly known as the woolly kefersteinia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
