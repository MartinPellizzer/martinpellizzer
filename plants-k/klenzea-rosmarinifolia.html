Klenzea rosmarinifolia

Klenzea rosmarinifolia, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Klenzea rosmarinifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
