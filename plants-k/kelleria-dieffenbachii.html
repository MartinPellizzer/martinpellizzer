Kelleria dieffenbachii

Kelleria dieffenbachii, commonly known as the elephant's foot plant, is a unique succulent native to South Africa.

It features a thick, fleshy stem that resembles an elephant's foot, which stores water for survival in arid conditions. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are thick and waxy, adapted to reduce water loss.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unusual appearance and low maintenance requirements.
