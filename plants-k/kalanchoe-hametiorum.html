Kalanchoe hametiorum

Kalanchoe hametiorum, commonly known as the mother of millions, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often blooming in winter. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in bright, indirect sunlight.

Kalanchoe hametiorum is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and long-lasting blooms.
