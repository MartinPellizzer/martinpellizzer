Knautia magnifica var. lutescens

Knautia magnifica var. lutescens, commonly known as the golden knautia, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe.

It features showy, daisy-like flowers that range from pale yellow to golden orange, often with a dark central disc. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are aromatic and slightly hairy. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, loamy soil.

Its blooms appear in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
