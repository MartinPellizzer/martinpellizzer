Kalanchoe gloveri

Kalanchoe gloveri, commonly known as the glover's kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, adapted to store water. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, typically blooming in spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires plenty of sunlight.

Kalanchoe gloveri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements.
