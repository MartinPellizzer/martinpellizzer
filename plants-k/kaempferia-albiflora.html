Kaempferia albiflora

Kaempferia albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered ginger, is a species of flowering plant in the Zingiberaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its elegant, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters.
