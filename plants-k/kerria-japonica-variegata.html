Kerria japonica variegata

Kerria japonica variegata, commonly known as the variegated Japanese kerria, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features attractive, lobed leaves that display a striking variegation of white, yellow, and green. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow, single flowers in the spring, followed by ornamental red berries in the fall. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a versatile addition to gardens.

Its compact growth habit and seasonal color changes make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
