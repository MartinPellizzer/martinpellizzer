Koompassia grandiflora

Koompassia grandiflora, commonly known as the giant flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects. The plant has a straight, tall trunk with a smooth, greyish-brown bark. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several pairs of elliptical leaflets.

Koompassia grandiflora thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland rainforests.
