Knema rigidifolia subsp. rigidifolia

Knema rigidifolia subsp.

rigidifolia, commonly known as the rigid-leaved knema, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Indo-Australian archipelago. It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its dense, dark green leaves that are stiff and often arranged in opposite pairs.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, followed by yellowish or reddish berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
