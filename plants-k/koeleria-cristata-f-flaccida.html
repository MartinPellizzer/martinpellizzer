Koeleria cristata f. flaccida

Koeleria cristata f. flaccida, commonly known as the flaccid tufted grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial grass that typically forms dense, mat-like clumps. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences with delicate, blue to purple flowers. It is native to arctic and alpine regions and is adapted to harsh, cold environments.

This variety is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is valued for its ornamental appearance in garden settings.
