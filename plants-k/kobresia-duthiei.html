Kobresia duthiei

Kobresia duthiei, commonly known as the Duthie's sedge, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is native to the high-altitude regions of the Tibetan Plateau and adjacent areas in Asia. This perennial herb typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, often forming dense mats or cushions. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets.

Its adaptations to cold and arid conditions make it an important component of alpine ecosystems.
