Koeleria vallesiana var. glabriuscula

Koeleria vallesiana var. glabriuscula, commonly known as the Valley Koeleria, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a perennial grass that typically grows in dry, open habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. The plant has slender, upright stems with narrow leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, spike-like clusters that appear in late spring to early summer.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small insects and animals.
