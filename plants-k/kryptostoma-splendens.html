Kryptostoma splendens

Kryptostoma splendens, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking golden-yellow petals and a distinctive star-shaped bloom. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in the dry seasons.

Kryptostoma splendens is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
