Koechlea mitis

Koechlea mitis, commonly known as the slender gray snail, is a species of air-breathing land snail.

It belongs to the family Aegidriidae and is native to parts of Europe, including France, Italy, and Spain. The snail has a glossy, pale to grayish shell that is typically elongated and narrow. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forests and gardens.
