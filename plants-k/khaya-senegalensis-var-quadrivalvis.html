Khaya senegalensis var. quadrivalvis

Khaya senegalensis var. quadrivalvis, commonly known as African shea tree or butterwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. The tree produces large, woody pods containing oily seeds used for making shea butter.

It is valued for its durable wood and ecological importance in savanna ecosystems.
