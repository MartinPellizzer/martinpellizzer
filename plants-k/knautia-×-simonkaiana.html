Knautia × simonkaiana

Knautia × simonkaiana, commonly known as the hybrid knautia, is a perennial plant native to the Balkans.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink to purple flower heads that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and features finely divided, gray-green leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and ability to attract pollinators.
