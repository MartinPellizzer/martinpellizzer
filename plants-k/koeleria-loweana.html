Koeleria loweana

Koeleria loweana, commonly known as the low's sedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to the western United States.

It typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is often found in open areas such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

Koeleria loweana is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for native wildlife.
