Klasea centauroides subsp. centauroides

Klasea centauroides subsp.

centauroides, commonly known as the Cape rose, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant has a trailing growth habit and produces small, oval-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and ability to attract pollinators.
