Karatas laciniosa

Karatas laciniosa, commonly known as the laceleaf tree, is a deciduous tree native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery leaves that are often described as resembling lace, hence its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the rainy season. Its fruit is a small, round capsule that contains several seeds.

Karatas laciniosa is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
