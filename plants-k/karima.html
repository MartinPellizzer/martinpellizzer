Karima

Karima, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other similar plants. The plant features showy yellow flowers with white petals, often surrounded by a ring of narrow, grass-like leaves. Its ornamental value is complemented by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
