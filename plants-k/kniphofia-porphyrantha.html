Kniphofia porphyrantha

Kniphofia porphyrantha, commonly known as the purple torch lily, is a striking perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, sturdy stems that bear clusters of vibrant red and orange flowers, resembling a torch. The plant has long, strap-like leaves that are typically green with a slight red or purple tint. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Kniphofia porphyrantha is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and birds.
