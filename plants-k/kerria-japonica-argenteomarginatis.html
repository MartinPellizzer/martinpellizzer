Kerria japonica argenteomarginatis

Kerria japonica argenteomarginatis, commonly known as the silver-margin kerria, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features showy, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by ornamental red berries in the fall. The leaves are ovate with a waxy texture and have distinctive white margins that give it its common name. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

This plant is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators and its adaptability to various growing conditions.
