Kedrostis nana var. zeyheri

Kedrostis nana var. zeyheri, commonly known as the dwarf African spider plant, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features rosettes of narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically a vibrant green, with some varieties exhibiting variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that are followed by spiny, three-lobed seed pods. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its low maintenance and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a resilient, decorative plant.
