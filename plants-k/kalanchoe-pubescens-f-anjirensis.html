Kalanchoe pubescens f. anjirensis

Kalanchoe pubescens f. anjirensis, commonly known as the Anjirensis Kalanchoe, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a velvety texture and a slight pubescence. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red, often blooming in late winter or early spring. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

This variety is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of care.
