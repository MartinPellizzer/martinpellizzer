Klonion gracile

Klonion gracile, commonly known as the slender klonion, is a small, herbaceous plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth or along stream banks. The plant features delicate, elongated leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually in loose inflorescences, and are typically white or pale pink in color.

Klonion gracile is valued in traditional medicine for its purported therapeutic properties.
