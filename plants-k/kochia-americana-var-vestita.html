Kochia americana var. vestita

Kochia americana var. vestita, commonly known as Russian spinach, is a flowering plant in the amaranth family.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into fluffy, pinkish seed heads. It is native to Eurasia but has been naturalized in parts of North America.

Kochia americana var. vestita is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique texture and color.
