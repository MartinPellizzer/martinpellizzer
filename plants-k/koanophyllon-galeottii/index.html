Koanophyllon Galeottii

Koanophyllon galeottii is a rare and fascinating plant native to the mountainous regions of central Mexico. It belongs to the sunflower family and is known for its unique, small, and often spiny leaves. This species thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to harsh, arid environments. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered vulnerable to environmental changes. Studying Koanophyllon galeottii offers valuable insights into plant adaptation and conservation in fragile ecosystems.
