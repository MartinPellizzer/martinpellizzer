Kohleria × pulchra

Kohleria × pulchra, commonly known as the showy kohleria, is a hybrid plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is prized for its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 feet, with opposite, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Kohleria × pulchra is popular among indoor gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
