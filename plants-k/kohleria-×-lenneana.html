Kohleria × lenneana

Kohleria × lenneana, commonly known as the hybrid kohleria, is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors such as red, pink, and white. The plant features fleshy, semi-succulent leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Kohleria × lenneana is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
