Kryptostoma godefroyi

Kryptostoma godefroyi, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow and green foliage and its unique, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded forest environments. Its botanical features include a sympodial growth habit and specialized roots that aid in nutrient absorption.

Kryptostoma godefroyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
