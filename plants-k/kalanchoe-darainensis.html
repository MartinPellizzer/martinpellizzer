Kalanchoe darainensis

Kalanchoe darainensis, commonly known as the Darainensis Kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle, which helps it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically during the winter months. It is a perennial species that thrives in full sun and well-draining soil.

Kalanchoe darainensis is also noted for its ability to propagate through offsets, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
