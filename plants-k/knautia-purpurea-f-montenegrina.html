Knautia purpurea f. montenegrina

Knautia purpurea f. montenegrina, commonly known as the Montenegro knautia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Balkan region.

It features upright stems covered in soft, hairy foliage and produces clusters of showy, pink to purple flowers in late summer. The plant typically grows in dry meadows and rocky soils, thriving in full sun. Its flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, contributing to local biodiversity.

The variety is distinguished by its compact growth habit and vibrant floral display.
