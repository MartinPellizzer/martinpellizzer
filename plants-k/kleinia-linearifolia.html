Kleinia linearifolia

Kleinia linearifolia, commonly known as the linearleaf ice plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, linear leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange, typically during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.

Kleinia linearifolia is often used in rock gardens and as an ornamental plant due to its hardy nature and attractive appearance.
