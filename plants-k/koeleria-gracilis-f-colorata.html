Koeleria gracilis f. colorata

Koeleria gracilis f. colorata, commonly known as the colored slender grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and small, clustered inflorescences. The foliage is typically blue-green in color, with the inflorescences displaying a range of hues from pink to purple, giving it its "colorata" designation. This plant is often found in dry, open habitats such as meadows and rocky soils.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
