Koeleria caucasica

Koeleria caucasica, commonly known as the Caucasian wild oat, is a perennial grass native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its slender, hairless stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Koeleria caucasica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
