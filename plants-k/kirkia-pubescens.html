Kirkia pubescens

Kirkia pubescens, commonly known as the Natal orchid, is a striking plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces showy, orchid-like flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant features a tall, erect stem with lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs. Its flowers bloom in summer and are often clustered in loose inflorescences.

Kirkia pubescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
