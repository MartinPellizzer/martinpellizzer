Kalanchoe uniflora var. brachycalyx

Kalanchoe uniflora var. brachycalyx, commonly known as the narrow-leaved kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically narrow and covered in a waxy cuticle to reduce water loss. The plant produces vibrant orange to red flowers in clusters during the winter months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
