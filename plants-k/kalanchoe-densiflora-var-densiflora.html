Kalanchoe densiflora var. densiflora

Kalanchoe densiflora var. densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tint, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red during the winter months. It is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

Kalanchoe densiflora var. densiflora is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and low maintenance requirements.
