Kerria japonica var. aureovariegata

Kerria japonica var. aureovariegata, commonly known as the golden variegated kerria, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features showy, bright yellow flowers that appear in early spring, followed by red-orange berries in the fall. The leaves are typically variegated with white and yellow markings, adding visual interest throughout the growing season. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This ornamental plant is valued for its attractive foliage and seasonal blooms in gardens and landscapes.
