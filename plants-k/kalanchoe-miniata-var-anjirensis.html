Kalanchoe miniata var. anjirensis

Kalanchoe miniata var. anjirensis, commonly known as the Anjirensis Kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems and produces clusters of vibrant red flowers during the winter months. The leaves are thick and somewhat woolly, with a bluish-green hue. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

This variety is popular as a decorative plant due to its striking appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
