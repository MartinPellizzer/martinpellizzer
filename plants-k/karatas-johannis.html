Karatas johannis

Karatas johannis, commonly known as the Johann's karatas, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of the Mediterranean.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping.
