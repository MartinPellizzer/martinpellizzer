Knautia rigidiuscula var. veneta

Knautia rigidiuscula var. veneta, commonly known as the Venetian knautia, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, hairy stems that bear clusters of pink to purple flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its prolonged flowering period and ability to attract pollinators.
