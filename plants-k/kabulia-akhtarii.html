Kabulia akhtarii

Kabulia akhtarii, commonly known as the Akhtari mountain bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, violet-blue flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, thriving at high altitudes.
