Keetia koritschoneri

Keetia koritschoneri, commonly known as the Koritschoneri keetia, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a shrub characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has leathery, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green in color. It prefers well-drained soils and a sunny to partially shaded location.

Keetia koritschoneri is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
