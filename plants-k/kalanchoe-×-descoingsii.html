Kalanchoe × descoingsii

Kalanchoe × descoingsii, commonly known as the hybrid kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that are often covered in a powdery white coating, known as a farina. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow during the winter months. It is a hybrid between Kalanchoe blossfeldiana and Kalanchoe pumila, resulting in a variety that is highly popular in horticulture.

Kalanchoe × descoingsii is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it an ideal choice for indoor or outdoor gardens.
