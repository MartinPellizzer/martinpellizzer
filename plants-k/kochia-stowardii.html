Kochia stowardii

Kochia stowardii, commonly known as desert sandmat, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant produces bright yellow flowers in late summer, which are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences. Kochia stowardii is well adapted to harsh, dry environments, with deep root systems that allow it to survive in poor soils.
