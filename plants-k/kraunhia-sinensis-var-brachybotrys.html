Kraunhia sinensis var. brachybotrys

Kraunhia sinensis var. brachybotrys, commonly known as the compact bellflower, is a perennial herb native to China.

It features upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are densely packed along the stem. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue to purple, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a low-maintenance, decorative plant.
