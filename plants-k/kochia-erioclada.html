Kochia erioclada

Kochia erioclada, commonly known as the silverleaf kochia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, powdery hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in arid environments.
