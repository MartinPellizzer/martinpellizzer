Kleinia obesa

Kleinia obesa, commonly known as the fatplant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers during the cooler months. Its dense, compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.

Kleinia obesa is well adapted to full sun and requires minimal water, making it ideal for low-maintenance gardening.
