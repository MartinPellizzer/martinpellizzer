Kaempferia takensis

Kaempferia takensis, commonly known as the Tawean orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact, rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or red with contrasting yellow markings. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often arranged in a fan-like pattern.

Kaempferia takensis thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
