Krenakanthus roseolilacinus

Krenakanthus roseolilacinus, commonly known as the pink-flowered krenakanthus, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking pink and white flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has small, narrow leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens.

Its unique floral display makes it a popular choice among gardeners seeking ornamental plants.
