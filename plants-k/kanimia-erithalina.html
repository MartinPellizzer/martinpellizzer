Kanimia erithalina

Kanimia erithalina, commonly known as the red-flowered kanimia, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red petals and elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters during the wet season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
