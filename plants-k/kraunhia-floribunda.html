Kraunhia floribunda

Kraunhia floribunda, commonly known as the Cape rose, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, with narrow, silvery-green leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is complemented by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
