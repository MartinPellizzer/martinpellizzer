Kaempferia speciosa

Kaempferia speciosa, commonly known as the flame lily or red ginger, is a tropical perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, sword-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that range from red to orange in color. The plant belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers, and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil and indirect sunlight.

Kaempferia speciosa is also valued for its use in traditional medicine and as a decorative plant in gardens and indoor spaces.
