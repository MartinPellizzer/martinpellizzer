Kunzea × rosea

Kunzea × rosea, commonly known as the rosemary myrtle, is a hybrid shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, needle-like leaves that emit a strong, resinous scent. The plant typically grows in coastal and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions. Its flowers are small, white or pink, and appear in clusters during the spring and summer months.

Kunzea × rosea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and medicinal properties.
