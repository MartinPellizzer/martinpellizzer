Koeleria narbonnensis

Koeleria narbonnensis, commonly known as the narrowleaf koeleria, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the grass family (Poaceae) and typically grows in dry, rocky soils. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny blue to purple flowers. It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and coastal areas.

Koeleria narbonnensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local pollinators.
