Scleria arundinacea

Scleria arundinacea, commonly known as the reed sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges and grass-like plants. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy habitats, often near water sources such as streams and ponds. Its slender, grass-like stems are often used in traditional crafts and basketry due to their flexibility.
