Sutera canescens

Sutera canescens, commonly known as the silverleaf mallow, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, fuzzy hairs. The plant produces showy, pink or purple flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Sutera canescens is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
