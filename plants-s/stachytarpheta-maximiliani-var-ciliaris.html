Stachytarpheta maximiliani var. ciliaris

Stachytarpheta maximiliani var. ciliaris, commonly known as the purpletop verbena, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in terminal clusters. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal.
