Sabina gracilior

Sabina gracilior, commonly known as the Chinese thuja, is a small evergreen conifer native to China.

It features scale-like leaves that are densely packed on its branches, giving it a compact and ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, spherical cones that mature to a reddish-brown color. It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a foundation plant.

Its slow growth rate and hardy nature make it a popular choice for landscaping in temperate climates.
