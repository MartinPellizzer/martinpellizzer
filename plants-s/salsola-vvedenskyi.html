Salsola vvedenskyi

Salsola vvedenskyi, commonly known as the Siberian saltbush, is a flowering plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia, including parts of Russia and Mongolia. The plant is characterized by its succulent leaves and small, greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters. It thrives in saline and alkaline soils, making it well adapted to harsh environmental conditions.

Salsola vvedenskyi plays a role in stabilizing soil in desert ecosystems and is sometimes used in phytoremediation efforts.
