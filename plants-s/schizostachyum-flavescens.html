Schizostachyum flavescens

Schizostachyum flavescens, commonly known as the yellow palmetto, is a tropical plant belonging to the Arecaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, fan-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of yellowish-pink flowers, which are followed by small, round fruits. Schizostachyum flavescens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in landscaping due to its attractive appearance.
