Stigmatodon apparicianus

Stigmatodon apparicianus, commonly known as the rare Appalachian orchid, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pale greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest understories, preferring rich, organic soils. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, and it has a short, erect stem.
