Strobilanthes rex

Strobilanthes rex, commonly known as the king of the strobilanthes, is a striking flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, showy inflorescences that resemble a crown, giving it its common name. The plant produces vibrant, colorful flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in clusters along the stem. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and requires ample sunlight to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
