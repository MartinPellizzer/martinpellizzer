Saxifraga × rayei

Saxifraga × rayei, commonly known as the hybrid saxifrage, is a flowering plant belonging to the Saxifragaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage, often found in rocky or alpine environments. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pink, with five petals and numerous stamens. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, where it thrives in moist, shaded habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and rockeries.
