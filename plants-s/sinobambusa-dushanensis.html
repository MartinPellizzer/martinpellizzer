Sinobambusa dushanensis

Sinobambusa dushanensis, commonly known as the Dushan bamboo, is a species of flowering bamboo native to China.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect culms that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in panicles, and it has narrow, linear leaves with a smooth texture. It is often found in mountainous regions and is valued for its ornamental and ecological significance.

The species is also used in traditional Chinese medicine and as a source of sustainable materials.
