Schefflera bojeri

Schefflera bojeri, commonly known as the Bojer's umbrella plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features a distinctive, umbrella-like crown composed of multiple, palmate leaves that radiate from a central stem. The plant has thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Schefflera bojeri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor conditions.
