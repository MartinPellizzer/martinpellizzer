Satyrium setchuenicum

Satyrium setchuenicum, commonly known as the Sichuan orchid, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of Sichuan, China.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a few broad, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green to white with intricate patterns, and they are often clustered in racemes.

This orchid thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forests or rocky areas at higher elevations.
