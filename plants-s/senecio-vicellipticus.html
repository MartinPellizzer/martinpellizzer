Senecio vicellipticus

Senecio vicellipticus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf Senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid regions. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces yellow flowers arranged in dense, terminal clusters. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by composite flower heads.
