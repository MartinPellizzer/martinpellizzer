Seriphidium kemrudicum var. karabugasicum

Seriphidium kemrudicum var. karabugasicum, commonly known as Karabugasik grass, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small yellow flowers. This variety thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in steppes and rocky soils. It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing forage for livestock.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological and agricultural contexts.
