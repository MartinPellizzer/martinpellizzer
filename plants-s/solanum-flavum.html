Solanum flavum

Solanum flavum, commonly known as the yellow nightshade, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is native to South America and is characterized by its yellow flowers and oval, green leaves. The plant produces small, yellow berries that are toxic to humans and animals. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Due to its toxic properties, it is important to handle the plant with care.
