Stenotus interior

Stenotus interior, commonly known as the narrowleaf ironweed, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes, swamps, and along stream banks. The plant features upright stems that are often reddish-brown in color and are covered with fine hairs. Its leaves are lance-shaped, alternate, and have a distinctive blue-green hue.

The showy purple flowers appear in late summer, forming dense, spherical clusters at the top of the plant.
