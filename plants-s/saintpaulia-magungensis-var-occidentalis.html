Saintpaulia magungensis var. occidentalis

Saintpaulia magungensis var. occidentalis, commonly known as the Western African violet, is a compact, low-growing plant native to the rainforests of western Africa.

It features broad, velvety leaves with a dark green hue and produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is a popular ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and vibrant blooms.

Saintpaulia magungensis var. occidentalis is often cultivated as a houseplant, preferring indirect light and consistent moisture.
