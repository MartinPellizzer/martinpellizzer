Sarcophrynium prionogonium var. ivorense

Sarcophrynium prionogonium var. ivorense, commonly known as the Ivory Coast sarcophrynium, is a tropical shrub native to West Africa.

It features opposite, ovate leaves with a dark green upper surface and a lighter underside. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique subject for ecological and horticultural study.
