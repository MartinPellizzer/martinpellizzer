Syagrus pleioclada

Syagrus pleioclada, commonly known as the palm tree, is a species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers, which are followed by ornamental fruits. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

Syagrus pleioclada is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in landscaping and horticulture.
