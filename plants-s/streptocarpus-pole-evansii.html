Streptocarpus pole-evansii

Streptocarpus pole-evansii, commonly known as the Pole-Evans streptocarpus, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family native to South Africa.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its upright growth habit and attractive, tubular flowers that range in color from violet to white.
