Swertia carinthiaca

Swertia carinthiaca, commonly known as Alpine gentian, is a perennial herb native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It typically grows in rocky and mountainous environments, thriving in cool, moist conditions. The plant features upright stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are blue to violet in color, with five petals and prominent yellow veins, blooming in late summer.

Swertia carinthiaca is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
