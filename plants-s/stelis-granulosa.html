Stelis granulosa

Stelis granulosa, commonly known as the granular stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a short rhizome with several erect, linear leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered, often with a distinctive coloration.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
