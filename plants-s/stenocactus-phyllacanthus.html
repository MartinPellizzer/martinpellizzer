Stenocactus phyllacanthus

Stenocactus phyllacanthus, commonly known as the Mexican feather cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stem that can grow up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are modified into spines, and it has a shallow root system adapted to arid environments.

It thrives in full sun and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for xeriscaping.
