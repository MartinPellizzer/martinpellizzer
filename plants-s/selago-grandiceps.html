Selago grandiceps

Selago grandiceps, commonly known as the giant selago, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Aizoaceae family, known for its succulent characteristics and ability to thrive in arid environments. The plant features fleshy, ribbed stems and produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and white. It is often found growing in rocky soils and along coastal areas.

Due to its adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens.
