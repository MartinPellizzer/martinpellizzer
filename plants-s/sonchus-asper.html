Sonchus asper

Sonchus asper, commonly known as prickly lettuce, is a herbaceous plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It features a rosette of hairy, lance-shaped leaves with a rough, prickly texture. The plant produces yellow flowers that develop into spherical, spiky seed heads. It grows in a variety of habitats, including roadsides, fields, and disturbed soils.

Botanically, it is characterized by its alternate leaves and is part of the Asteraceae family.
