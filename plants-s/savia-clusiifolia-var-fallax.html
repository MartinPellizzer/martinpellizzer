Savia clusiifolia var. fallax

Savia clusiifolia var. fallax, commonly known as the variable clusiifolia, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of South America. The plant is characterized by its alternate, simple, and often glossy leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered in cyathia, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family.

The species is noted for its variability in leaf shape and color, which contributes to its common name.
