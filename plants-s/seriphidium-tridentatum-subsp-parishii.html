Seriphidium tridentatum subsp. parishii

Seriphidium tridentatum subsp.

parishii, commonly known as the Parish's tansy, is a member of the Asteraceae family and is native to arid regions of the southwestern United States. It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in desert or semi-desert environments.
