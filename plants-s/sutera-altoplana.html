Sutera altoplana

Sutera altoplana, commonly known as the mountain sutera, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a low-growing shrub that thrives in rocky, arid environments. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, often during the warmer months. Its leaves are narrow and fleshy, adapted to conserve water in its harsh habitat.

Sutera altoplana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native ecosystem.
