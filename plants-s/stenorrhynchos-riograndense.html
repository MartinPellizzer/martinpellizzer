Stenorrhynchos riograndense

Stenorrhynchos riograndense, commonly known as the Rio Grande orchid, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces spike-like inflorescences with small, brightly colored blooms that attract pollinators. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
