Swartzia panacoco var. kamarangensis

Swartzia panacoco var. kamarangensis, commonly known as the Kamarangensis Swartzia, is a tropical tree species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and distinctive, compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by elongated, woody pods containing flat, bean-like seeds.

It is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its hard, durable wood used in construction and furniture-making.
