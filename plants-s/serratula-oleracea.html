Serratula oleracea

Serratula oleracea, commonly known as common sowthistle, is an annual herbaceous plant native to Europe and Asia.

It features a rosette of spoon-shaped, hairy leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a single, erect head, which bloom from late spring to early autumn. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and waste areas.

Serratula oleracea is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and vegetative propagation.
