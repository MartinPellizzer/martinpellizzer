Sisymbrium arenosum

Sisymbrium arenosum, commonly known as sand mustard, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other mustard plants. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions. It produces small yellow flowers and has pinnately divided leaves.
