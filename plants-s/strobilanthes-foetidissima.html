Strobilanthes foetidissima

Strobilanthes foetidissima, commonly known as the stinkweed, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has a distinctive, strongly unpleasant odor, which is a key feature in its ecological interactions. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical forests.

Its unique scent attracts specific pollinators, making it an important component of its ecosystem.
