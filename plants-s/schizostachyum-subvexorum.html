Schizostachyum subvexorum

Schizostachyum subvexorum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved palma.

It is a species of palm native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its slender, arching leaves and robust trunk. The plant typically grows in wetland or swampy environments, thriving in humid, tropical climates. Its leaves are often used in traditional crafts and as thatching materials.

Schizostachyum subvexorum is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
