Sideritis latiuscula

Sideritis latiuscula, commonly known as narrow-leaved ironweed, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants known for their medicinal properties. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are typically pink to purple, arranged in dense spikes, and bloom during the summer months.

Sideritis latiuscula is valued for its aromatic foliage and has been traditionally used in herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
