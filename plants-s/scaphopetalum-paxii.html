Scaphopetalum paxii

Scaphopetalum paxii, commonly known as the Mexican sunflower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant orange to yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a rough texture.

Scaphopetalum paxii thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
