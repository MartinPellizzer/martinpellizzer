Stenoloma schlechtendalii

Stenoloma schlechtendalii, commonly known as the African feather grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, arching blades that form dense tufts, giving it a feathery appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. Its inflorescence consists of spikelets arranged in a panicle, with small, pale flowers.

Stenoloma schlechtendalii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its elegant, grass-like foliage.
