Senecio helenitis f. pratensis

Senecio helenitis f. pratensis, commonly known as the meadow senecio, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It has lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs.

This variety is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
