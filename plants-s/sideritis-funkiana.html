Sideritis funkiana

Sideritis funkiana, commonly known as the Balkan germander, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. The plant features slender, erect stems and small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or violet in color. Its leaves are aromatic and have been traditionally used in herbal medicine for their soothing properties.

Sideritis funkiana thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.
