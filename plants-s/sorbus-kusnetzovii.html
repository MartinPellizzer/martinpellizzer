Sorbus kusnetzovii

Sorbus kusnetzovii, commonly known as the Kusnetzov's whitebeam, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the Caucasus region and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and alternate, simple leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters, followed by bright red, berry-like fruits that are edible for birds and mammals.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental tree due to its attractive appearance and adaptability to various soil types.
