Sideritis perfoliata var. condensata

Sideritis perfoliata var. condensata, commonly known as the dense-leaved ironweed, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that bear opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture. The plant produces small, pale blue to violet flowers in whorled clusters, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are often described as being densely packed, giving the plant its varietal name.

Sideritis perfoliata var. condensata is valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
