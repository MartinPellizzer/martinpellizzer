Solori scandens

Solori scandens, commonly known as the climbing sunflower, is a vigorous vine native to the Americas.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture and produces bright yellow flowers that resemble sunflowers. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to climb various structures using its tendrils. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its botanical characteristics include a fibrous root system and a branching stem that supports its climbing habit.
