Sedum liebmannianum

Sedum liebmannianum, commonly known as the Liebmann's stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow to orange. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is drought-tolerant.

Sedum liebmannianum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its resilience and aesthetic appeal.
