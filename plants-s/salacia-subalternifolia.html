Salacia subalternifolia

Salacia subalternifolia, commonly known as the lesser leaf salacia, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and opposite, lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in axillary inflorescences. Its fruit is a small, fleshy berry that turns red when mature.

Salacia subalternifolia is valued in traditional medicine for its potential medicinal properties.
