Sideritis montserratiana

Sideritis montserratiana, commonly known as the Montserrat ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its narrow, hairy leaves and small, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of purple and pink. It typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species.
