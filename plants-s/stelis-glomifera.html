Stelis glomifera

Stelis glomifera, commonly known as the clustered spike orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its erect, branching stems and small, clustered flowers. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive spurred lip.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both horticultural and conservation studies.
