Scolopia gossweileri

Scolopia gossweileri, commonly known as the desert milkweed, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of North America.

It is characterized by its erect stems and clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant produces a milky sap that is toxic to many animals, including livestock. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to hot, dry climates.
