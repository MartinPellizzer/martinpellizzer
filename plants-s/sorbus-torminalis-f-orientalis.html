Sorbus torminalis f. orientalis

Sorbus torminalis f. orientalis, commonly known as the oriental whitebeam, is a variant of the common whitebeam.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to parts of Asia, including China and the Russian Far East. The plant features small, white to pale yellow flowers that appear in clusters, followed by small, orange-red berries. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a serrated edge.

The oriental whitebeam is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
