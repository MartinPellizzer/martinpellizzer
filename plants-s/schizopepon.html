Schizopepon

Schizopepon, commonly known as the Chinese gooseberry, is a climbing vine native to East Asia.

It produces small, yellow, edible fruits that are rich in vitamin C and antioxidants. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often cultivated for its nutritional value. It thrives in temperate climates and requires support for its vigorous growth.

Schizopepon is also valued for its ornamental foliage and is relatively easy to grow in home gardens.
