Scutellaria turgaica

Scutellaria turgaica, commonly known as Turkish skullcap, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. The plant features square, hairy stems and opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. Its small, tubular flowers are typically blue to purple in color, arranged in whorls along the stem.

Scutellaria turgaica is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional herbal medicine.
