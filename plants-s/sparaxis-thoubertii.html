Sparaxis thoubertii

Sparaxis thoubertii, commonly known as the yellow rain lily, is a bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features strap-like, grassy leaves and produces showy, cone-shaped inflorescences with bright yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to dry conditions and thrives in well-drained soil. It blooms in late summer to early autumn, adding vibrant color to gardens.

Sparaxis thoubertii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm climates.
