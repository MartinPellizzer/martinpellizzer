Senecio pinnatilobatus

Senecio pinnatilobatus, commonly known as the narrow-lobed groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow. The leaves are pinnately lobed, giving the plant its scientific name, and are typically gray-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

This species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in arid landscapes.
