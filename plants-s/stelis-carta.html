Stelis carta

Stelis carta, commonly known as the cart-like orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its upright, slender stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, and the flowers are usually white or pale pink with intricate patterns.

Stelis carta is native to Southeast Asia and is appreciated for its ornamental value in horticulture.
