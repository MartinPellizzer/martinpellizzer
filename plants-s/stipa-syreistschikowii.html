Stipa syreistschikowii, commonly known as the Siberian needlegrass, is a perennial grass species native to the cold climates of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers, typical of the Poaceae family.
