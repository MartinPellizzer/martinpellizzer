Salvia tatsienensis

Salvia tatsienensis, commonly known as the Yunnan sage, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or woodland settings.

Its ornamental value and adaptability have made it a popular choice among horticulturists.
