Sedum drymarioides var. stellariifolium

Sedum drymarioides var. stellariifolium, commonly known as the star-leaved stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the western United States.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, and its flowers are typically yellow or orange, blooming in summer. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
