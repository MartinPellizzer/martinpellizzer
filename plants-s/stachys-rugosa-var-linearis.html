Stachys rugosa var. linearis

Stachys rugosa var. linearis, commonly known as narrow-leaved hedge nettle, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, linear leaves that are hairy and often have a rough texture. The plant produces small, pale purple to white flowers arranged in whorls along the stems. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive species within the Lamiaceae family.
