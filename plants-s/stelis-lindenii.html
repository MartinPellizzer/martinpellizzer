Stelis lindenii

Stelis lindenii, commonly known as the Lindens' orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically blooms during the summer months, with flowers arranged in a raceme. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Stelis lindenii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
