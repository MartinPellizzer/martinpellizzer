Solanum jamesonii

Solanum jamesonii, commonly known as the Jameson's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the evening, and its berries are typically red when mature. Solanum jamesonii is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests or rocky areas.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves.
