Sedum jaliscanum subsp. angustifolium

Sedum jaliscanum subsp.

angustifolium, commonly known as narrowleaf stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Mexico. It features fleshy, narrow leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its hardy nature allows it to survive in a variety of environmental conditions.
