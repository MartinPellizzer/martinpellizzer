Solanum swartzianum subsp. argyrophyllum

Solanum swartzianum subsp.

argyrophyllum, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a shrub native to South America. It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers.

The plant is characterized by its silver-gray leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruits are typically berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

This species is often found in dry, open habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
