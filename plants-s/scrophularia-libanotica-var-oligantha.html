Scrophularia libanotica var. oligantha

Scrophularia libanotica var. oligantha, commonly known as the Lebanon figwort, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. The plant features slender stems and small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and blooms.
