Sulcorebutia longigibba

Sulcorebutia longigibba, commonly known as the long-stemmed crassula, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are often covered in a whitish bloom, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
