Stelis filiformis

Stelis filiformis, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its delicate, elongated stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive lip that is often adorned with intricate patterns.

Stelis filiformis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
