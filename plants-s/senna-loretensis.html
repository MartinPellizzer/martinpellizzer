Senna loretensis

Senna loretensis, commonly known as the Loretto senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and pod fruits. The plant is noted for its bright yellow flowers, which are often clustered in racemes, and its compound leaves with small leaflets. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in forest understories or along riverbanks.

Senna loretensis is also valued for its medicinal properties, with various parts of the plant used in traditional remedies.
