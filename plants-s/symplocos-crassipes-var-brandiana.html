Symplocos crassipes var. brandiana

Symplocos crassipes var. brandiana, commonly known as the Brand's Indian plum, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and elliptical leaves.
