Sarcocornia dunensis

Sarcocornia dunensis, commonly known as the coastal bladderwrack, is a halophytic plant adapted to saline environments.

The plant thrives in intertidal zones, where it tolerates periodic submersion and desiccation. Sarcocornia dunensis plays a vital role in stabilizing coastal soils and providing habitat for marine organisms.
