Satureja montana var. obovata

Satureja montana var. obovata, commonly known as mountain savory, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It features small, fragrant leaves that are oval in shape and have a slightly serrated edge. The plant produces tiny, pale blue or purple flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine environments.

Its aromatic properties make it a valuable plant in both culinary and medicinal applications.
