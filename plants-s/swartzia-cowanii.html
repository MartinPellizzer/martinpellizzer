Swartzia cowanii

Swartzia cowanii, commonly known as the African rosewood, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, aromatic wood, which is highly valued for furniture and musical instruments. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are typically elliptical or ovate in shape. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale pink, followed by woody, capsule-like fruits.

Swartzia cowanii thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal lowland forests.
