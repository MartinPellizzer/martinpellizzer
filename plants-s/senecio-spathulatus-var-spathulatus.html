Senecio spathulatus var. spathulatus

Senecio spathulatus var. spathulatus, commonly known as the broad-leaf senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy yellow flowers. The plant features lance-shaped to spatulate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in various regions with a suitable climate.
