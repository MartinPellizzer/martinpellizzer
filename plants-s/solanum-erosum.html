Solanum erosum

Solanum erosum, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, pubescent hairs. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple, typically blooming in the spring. Its fruit is a globose berry that turns red when mature, containing several seeds.

Solanum erosum is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is part of the nightshade family, Solanaceae.
