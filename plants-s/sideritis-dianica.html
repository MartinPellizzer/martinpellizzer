Sideritis dianica

Sideritis dianica, commonly known as the Greek ironwort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Greece.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It is characterized by its hairy stems and serrated leaves.

Sideritis dianica is often found in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in full sun.
