Scaevola lobelia var. sericea

Scaevola lobelia var. sericea, commonly known as the silverleaf scaevola, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its small, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The leaves are covered in a fine, silvery down, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

This variety is known for its compact growth habit and drought tolerance.
