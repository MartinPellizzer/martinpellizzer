Sideritis pallida

Sideritis pallida, commonly known as white heath, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its pale blue or white flowers and silver-gray foliage. It typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

Sideritis pallida is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
