Singularybas

Singularybas, commonly known as the moonflower vine, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its large, white, night-blooming flowers.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is native to Central and South America. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, often with a velvety texture.

The flowers emit a sweet fragrance at night, attracting nocturnal pollinators like moths.
