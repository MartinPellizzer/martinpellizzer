Statice barulensis

Statice barulensis, commonly known as the coastal statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and spikes of small, showy flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant thrives in saline and well-drained soils, often found in coastal dunes and rocky shores. It is drought-tolerant and has adapted to withstand strong winds and salt spray.

Statice barulensis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize sandy soils.
