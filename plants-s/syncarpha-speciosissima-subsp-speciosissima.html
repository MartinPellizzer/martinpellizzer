Syncarpha speciosissima subsp. speciosissima

Syncarpha speciosissima subsp.

speciosissima, commonly known as the showy synkarpa, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant produces showy, yellow to orange ray flowers surrounded by a dense array of disc flowers. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils at elevations ranging from 2,000 to 4,000 meters.

Its botanical features make it a notable species for ecological studies and horticultural interest.
