Sebifera balongy

Sebifera balongy, commonly known as the Balongy tree, is a species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage and can grow up to 30 meters in height. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a waxy texture.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
