Senecio Glandulosus

Senecio glandulosus is a unique and striking member of the Asteraceae family known for its vibrant yellow flowers and distinctive, glandular leaves. Native to southern Africa, this plant thrives in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its compact growth habit and ability to store water make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping. The plant's resilience and ornamental value have made it a sought-after addition to many botanical collections. With its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements, Senecio glandulosus is both a fascinating subject for study and a valuable plant for gardeners.
