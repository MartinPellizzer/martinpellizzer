Siolmatra

Siolmatra, commonly known as the moonflower, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, white, night-blooming flowers that emit a sweet fragrance. The plant has broad, lance-shaped leaves and thrives in warm, humid climates. Siolmatra is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine.

Its blooming cycle is closely tied to the lunar cycle, making it a symbol of mystery and beauty.
