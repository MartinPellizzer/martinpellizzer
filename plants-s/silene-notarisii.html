Silene notarisii

Silene notarisii, commonly known as the Notarisii catchfly, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pinkish-purple flowers that bloom in early summer.
