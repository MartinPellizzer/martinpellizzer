Sedum aizoon f. latifolium

Sedum aizoon f. latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in dry conditions. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It has a low-growing habit and is often used in rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its ability to tolerate poor soil and full sun makes it a hardy and versatile ornamental plant.
