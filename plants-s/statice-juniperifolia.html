Statice juniperifolia

Statice juniperifolia, commonly known as the Juniperleaf statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and spikes of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its drought tolerance and long-lasting blooms make it a valuable addition to xeriscapes.

Statice juniperifolia is also known for its aromatic foliage, which can be used in herbal teas and perfumery.
