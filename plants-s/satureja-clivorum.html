Satureja clivorum

Satureja clivorum, commonly known as alpine savory, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils. It is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.

Satureja clivorum is also an important plant in ecological studies due to its adaptability to harsh climates.
