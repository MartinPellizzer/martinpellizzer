Solanum tuberosum var. alaudinum

Solanum tuberosum var. alaudinum, commonly known as the Alaudinum potato, is a botanical variant of the common potato.

It is characterized by its unique tuber morphology, which includes smaller, more elongated tubers compared to standard potato varieties. The plant belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and shares the same genetic background as other Solanum tuberosum species. It exhibits a range of color variations in its foliage and tubers, often including shades of green, purple, and red.

This variety is of interest in botanical studies due to its potential for genetic diversity and adaptability to different environmental conditions.
