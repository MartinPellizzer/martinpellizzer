Sigmatogyne bia

Sigmatogyne bia, commonly known as the coastal morning glory, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trumpet-shaped, pale blue to purple flowers that bloom in the early morning. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found near the shoreline.

Sigmatogyne bia is adapted to salt spray and has a unique ability to tolerate coastal conditions.
