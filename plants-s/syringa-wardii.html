Syringa wardii

Syringa wardii, commonly known as the Chinese lilac, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, clustered flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. Syringa wardii thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to adapt to various garden conditions.
