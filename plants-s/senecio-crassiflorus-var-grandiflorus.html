Senecio crassiflorus var. grandiflorus

Senecio crassiflorus var. grandiflorus, commonly known as the grandiflorus senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves and showy yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
