Stellaster amblyopetalus

Stellaster amblyopetalus, commonly known as the star-petaled aster, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped petals, which are typically white or pale blue in color. The plant produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind, aiding in its propagation. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Its floral structure is adapted for attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
