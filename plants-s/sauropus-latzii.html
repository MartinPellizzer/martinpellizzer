Sauropus latzii

Sauropus latzii, commonly known as the silverleaf sauropus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with a silvery underside, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.

Sauropus latzii is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
