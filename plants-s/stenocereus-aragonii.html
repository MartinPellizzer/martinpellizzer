Stenocereus aragonii

Stenocereus aragonii, commonly known as the organ pipe cactus, is a columnar cactus native to the deserts of the southwestern United States and northwestern Mexico.

It features a tall, unbranched stem with ribbed structure and produces clusters of white, night-blooming flowers. The plant has spiny areoles and is adapted to arid environments with deep root systems. It is also known for its ability to store water in its thick, fleshy stems.

The organ pipe cactus is a popular ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and resilience.
