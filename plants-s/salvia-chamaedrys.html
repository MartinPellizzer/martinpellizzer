Salvia chamaedrys

Salvia chamaedrys, commonly known as carpet sage, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, fragrant blue or violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, gray-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its aromatic properties and ornamental value make it a valued addition to both gardens and herbal collections.
