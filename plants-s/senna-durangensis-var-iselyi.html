Senna durangensis var. iselyi

Senna durangensis var. iselyi, commonly known as the Durango senna, is a shrub native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its typical legume structure. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces yellow flowers in clusters. Its seeds are enclosed in elongated pods, typical of the legume family.

It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments.
