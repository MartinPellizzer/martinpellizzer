Saxifraga cotyledon subf. oederiana

Saxifraga cotyledon subf.

oederiana, commonly known as the fleshy saxifrage, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are often reddish or purple in color, especially when exposed to sunlight.

The plant produces small, white or pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in rocky or well-drained soil and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
