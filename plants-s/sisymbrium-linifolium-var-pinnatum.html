Sisymbrium linifolium var. pinnatum

Sisymbrium linifolium var. pinnatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved winter cress, is a flowering plant in the family Brassicaceae.

It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and small yellow flowers that appear in clusters. The plant is native to the western United States and thrives in disturbed soils and open areas. It has a taproot system and produces elongated pods that contain several seeds.
