Swertia dimorpha

Swertia dimorpha, commonly known as the Himalayan gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, thriving in cool and moist conditions. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

Swertia dimorpha is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
