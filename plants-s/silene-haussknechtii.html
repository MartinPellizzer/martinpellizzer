Silene haussknechtii

Silene haussknechtii, commonly known as the Hausknecht's campion, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or alpine environments. The plant produces showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in its arid habitat.

Silene haussknechtii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil.
