Sudamerlycaste dunstervillei

Sudamerlycaste dunstervillei, commonly known as the Dunsterville's cycad, is a rare and ancient plant species native to the rainforests of Brazil.

It belongs to the cycad family, characterized by its stout, unbranched trunk and large, feather-like leaves. The plant produces cones rather than flowers, a feature typical of gymnosperms, and it has a slow growth rate.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered endangered, making conservation efforts crucial for its survival.
