Stylochaeton natalense subsp. obliquinerve

Stylochaeton natalense subsp.

obliquinerve, commonly known as the Natal grassveld tree, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and legume fruits.

The plant features trifoliate leaves with oblique veins, giving it its subspecies name. It is typically found in grassland habitats and has a relatively low growth form.

The species is of interest in ecological studies due to its role in local ecosystems and potential for use in restoration projects.
