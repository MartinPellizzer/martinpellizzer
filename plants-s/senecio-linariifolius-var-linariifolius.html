Senecio linariifolius var. linariifolius

Senecio linariifolius var. linariifolius, commonly known as the linear-leaved groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and produces small yellow flowers arranged in a composite head. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
