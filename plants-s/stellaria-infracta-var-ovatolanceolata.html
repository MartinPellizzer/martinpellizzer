Stellaria infracta var. ovatolanceolata

Stellaria infracta var. ovatolanceolata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved starwort, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, ascending stems with lanceolate to ovate leaves that are often hairy. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is typically found in meadows and woodland edges.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive species within the Stellaria genus.
