Stylobasium lineare var. parviflora

Stylobasium lineare var. parviflora, commonly known as the narrow-leaved stylobasium, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth. Its roots are fibrous and can form dense mats, contributing to soil stabilization.

It is often found in regions with high humidity and well-drained soils.
