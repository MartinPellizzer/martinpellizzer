Senecio laetivirens

Senecio laetivirens, commonly known as the Happy Yellow Groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces bright yellow flowers in spring. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it store water, adapting it to arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its vibrant blooms attract pollinators like bees and butterflies, contributing to ecological diversity.
