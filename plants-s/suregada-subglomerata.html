Suregada subglomerata

Suregada subglomerata, commonly known as the clustered leaf orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and clustered leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
