Senecio desideratus f. elatiusculus

Senecio desideratus f. elatiusculus, commonly known as the slender senecio, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It is native to South Africa and prefers well-drained, sandy soils.

This succulent is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
