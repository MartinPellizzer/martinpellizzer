Stellaria media subsp. catalaunica

Stellaria media subsp.

catalaunica, commonly known as Catalaunian mouse-ear, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It features small, white flowers with five petals arranged in a star-like pattern, typical of the Caryophyllaceae family.

The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and ecologically valuable species in its native habitats.
