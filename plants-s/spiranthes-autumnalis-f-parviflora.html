Spiranthes autumnalis f. parviflora

Spiranthes autumnalis f. parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered autumn spider orchid, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, spiraling arrangement of flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and wetlands. Its flowers are small and white to pale green, often appearing in late summer to early autumn.

It is a perennial herb that produces a basal rosette of leaves and a flowering stem.
