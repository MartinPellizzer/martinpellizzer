Sabatia stellaris

Sabatia stellaris, commonly known as starflower, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its showy, star-shaped purple to pink blossoms that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, sandy soils along stream banks and wetlands. It has alternate, lance-shaped leaves with hairy surfaces.

Sabatia stellaris is a member of the Geraniaceae family and is often found in open habitats with partial shade.
