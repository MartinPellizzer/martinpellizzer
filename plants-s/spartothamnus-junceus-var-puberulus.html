Spartothamnus junceus var. puberulus

Spartothamnus junceus var. puberulus, commonly known as the silverleaf spartothamnus, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is a shrub characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Its botanical features make it a hardy and drought-tolerant species suitable for xeriscaping.
