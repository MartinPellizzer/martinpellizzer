Sulcorebutia steinbachii f. violaciflora

Sulcorebutia steinbachii f. violaciflora, commonly known as the violet-flowered crassula, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, spoon-shaped leaves that are typically green with a bluish tint, and it produces clusters of small, violet-pink flowers during the summer months. The plant is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact size and drought tolerance. It prefers bright sunlight and well-draining soil to thrive.

Its unique flower color makes it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
