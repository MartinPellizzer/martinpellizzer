Solanum malletii

Solanum malletii, commonly known as the Mallet's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant produces small, violet-colored flowers that bloom in the evening, attracting nocturnal pollinators. Its leaves are ovate and hairy, with a distinctive waxy coating.

Solanum malletii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
