Silene wherryi

Silene wherryi, commonly known as Wherry's campion, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its pink to red flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in mountainous regions. Silene wherryi has small, ovate leaves and produces capsules as its fruiting structure.
