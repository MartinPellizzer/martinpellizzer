Solanum preslei

Solanum preslei, commonly known as the Presle's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that bloom in the evening. The plant typically grows as an annual or biennial herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a rough texture.

The fruit is a globose berry that turns red when mature, containing several seeds.
