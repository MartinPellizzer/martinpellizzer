Senecio leptophyllus

Senecio leptophyllus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils. The plant features small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is often found in open, sunny habitats and is adapted to arid conditions.
