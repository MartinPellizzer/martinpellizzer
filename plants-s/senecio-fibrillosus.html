Senecio fibrillosus

Senecio fibrillosus, commonly known as the silky filaments sunflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and vibrant yellow ray flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or arid environments.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
