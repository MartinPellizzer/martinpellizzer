Stipa valdemonensis

Stipa valdemonensis, commonly known as the Valdemonica feathergrass, is a species of grass native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and fine, silver-green foliage. The plant produces narrow, spike-like inflorescences that are often adorned with delicate, feathery bristles. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to temperate to cold climates.

Stipa valdemonensis is often found in alpine meadows and rocky habitats, where it contributes to the ecological diversity of these environments.
