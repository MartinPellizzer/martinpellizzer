Senecio cannabifolius f. integrifolius

Senecio cannabifolius f. integrifolius, commonly known as the Cannabis-leaf Senecio, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that resemble those of Cannabis sativa, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid regions, preferring well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its flowering heads consist of small yellow disk flowers surrounded by pale green to white ray florets.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage.
