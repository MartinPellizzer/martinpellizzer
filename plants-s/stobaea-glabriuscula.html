Stobaea glabriuscula

Stobaea glabriuscula, commonly known as the white star orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that bear clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Stobaea glabriuscula is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
