Senecio bollei var. flaccidus

Senecio bollei var. flaccidus, commonly known as the Flaccid Senecio, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the warmer months, which attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
