Stephanomeria minor var. myrioclada

Stephanomeria minor var. myrioclada, commonly known as the silverleaf sunflower, is a flowering plant native to California.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and bright yellow ray flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It produces small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in the summer months.

This variety is known for its drought tolerance and is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements.
