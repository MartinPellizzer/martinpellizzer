Senecio umbrosus

Senecio umbrosus, commonly known as the shadowy groundsels, is a perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically covered in a fine layer of white hairs. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Senecio umbrosus is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to add texture to shaded gardens.
