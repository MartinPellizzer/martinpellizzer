Stelmotis

Stelmotis, commonly known as the white clover, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which consist of three oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers that are clustered in spherical heads. Stelmotis thrives in temperate climates and is often found in meadows, pastures, and lawns.

It is an important forage plant for livestock and also supports pollinators such as bees.
