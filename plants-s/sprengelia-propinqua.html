Sprengelia propinqua

Sprengelia propinqua, commonly known as the flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Sprengelia propinqua is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
