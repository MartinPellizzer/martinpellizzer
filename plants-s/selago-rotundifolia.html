Selago rotundifolia

Selago rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leaf selago, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky soils and sunny environments. The plant features small, round leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its flowers are usually yellow or greenish-yellow, and they appear in clusters during the spring and summer months.

Selago rotundifolia is known for its resilience and is often found in coastal and arid habitats.
