Scolochloa arundinacea

Scolochloa arundinacea, commonly known as bamboo grass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along water bodies.

Scolochloa arundinacea is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in aquatic environments.
