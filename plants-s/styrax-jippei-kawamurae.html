Styrax jippei-kawamurae

Styrax jippei-kawamurae, commonly known as the Japanese styrax, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Japan.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. Its bark is smooth and reddish-brown, with a distinctive aromatic scent.

Styrax jippei-kawamurae is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional Japanese gardens.
