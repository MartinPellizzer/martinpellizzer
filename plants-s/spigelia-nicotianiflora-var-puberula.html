Spigelia nicotianiflora var. puberula

Spigelia nicotianiflora var. puberula, commonly known as the hairy nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, and it produces clusters of showy, pink to purple flowers. Its fruit is a capsule that contains numerous small, toxic berries.
