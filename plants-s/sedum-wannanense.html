Sedum wannanense

Sedum wannanense, commonly known as the Wannan stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to China.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers. Sedum wannanense is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance in landscaping.
