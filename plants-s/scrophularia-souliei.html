Scrophularia souliei

Scrophularia souliei, commonly known as the Chinese figwort, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Scrophulariaceae family and is characterized by its upright stems and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces showy, two-lipped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forested areas.

Scrophularia souliei is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.
