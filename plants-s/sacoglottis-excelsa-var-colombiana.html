Sacoglottis excelsa var. colombiana

Sacoglottis excelsa var. colombiana, commonly known as the Colombian giant hogweed, is a striking plant native to the Andean regions of Colombia.

The flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer, are typically white or pale pink, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
