Setiacis diffusa

Setiacis diffusa, commonly known as the spreading setia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This plant is often used in ornamental gardening for its cascading appearance and fragrant blooms.
