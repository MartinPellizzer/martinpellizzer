Stylosanthes guianensis var. robusta

Stylosanthes guianensis var. robusta, commonly known as the robust stylosanthes, is a perennial legume native to South America.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and trifoliate leaves with hairy surfaces. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers arranged in racemes, followed by elongated, curved pods containing several seeds. It is well adapted to dry and semi-arid conditions, making it a valuable forage crop in tropical regions.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it beneficial for soil fertility and sustainable agriculture.
