Stachys talbotii

Stachys talbotii, commonly known as Talbot's hedge nettle, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and features square stems covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, pinkish to purple flowers in whorled clusters, which bloom from late spring to early autumn. Its leaves are lance-shaped, hairy, and have a slightly serrated edge.

Stachys talbotii is often found in dry, rocky soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
