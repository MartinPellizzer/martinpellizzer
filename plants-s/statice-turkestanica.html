Statice turkestanica

Statice turkestanica, commonly known as the Turkistan glassflower, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, spike-like inflorescences. The plant produces small, colorful flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in fine hairs.

Statice turkestanica is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its long-lasting flowers and drought tolerance.
