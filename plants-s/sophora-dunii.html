Sophora dunii

Sophora dunii, commonly known as the Chinese wisteria, is a deciduous climbing plant native to China.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its long, cascading clusters of fragrant, purple-blue flowers. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces seed pods that split open to release its seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

The plant is also valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its potential health benefits.
