Solanum insulae-pinorum

Solanum insulae-pinorum, commonly known as the island pineapple solanum, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit, with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically yellow or greenish-yellow, and it produces small, red berries that contain the seeds.

Due to its restricted habitat, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
