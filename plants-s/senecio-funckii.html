Senecio funckii

Senecio funckii, commonly known as the Funck's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features a basal rosette of leaves and produces yellow composite flowers in the summer months. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Senecio funckii is often found in fynbos vegetation and is adapted to Mediterranean climates.
