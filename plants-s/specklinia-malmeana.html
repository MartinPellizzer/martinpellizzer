Specklinia malmeana

Specklinia malmeana, commonly known as the Malme's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its upright, branching stems and small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

Specklinia malmeana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
