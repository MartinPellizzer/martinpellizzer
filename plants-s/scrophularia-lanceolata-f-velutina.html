Scrophularia lanceolata f. velutina

Scrophularia lanceolata f. velutina, commonly known as the velvet figwort, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe.

It features hairy, lance-shaped leaves that give it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodlands or along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in ecological and horticultural contexts.
