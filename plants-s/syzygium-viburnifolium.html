Syzygium viburnifolium

Syzygium viburnifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf apple or Australian apple, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to eastern Australia and is characterized by its glossy, lance-shaped leaves and fragrant white flowers.
