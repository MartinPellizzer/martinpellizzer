Sphaeralcea arequipensis

Sphaeralcea arequipensis, commonly known as the Arequipa globemallow, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow to orange flowers that bloom in summer, typically from June to September. The plant has a low-growing habit with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Sphaeralcea arequipensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
