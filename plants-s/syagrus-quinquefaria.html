Syagrus quinquefaria

Syagrus quinquefaria, commonly known as the royal palm, is a tropical plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, fan-shaped leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into clusters of black, edible fruits. Syagrus quinquefaria is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in landscaping in warm climates.

It plays an important role in the ecosystems of its native habitats, providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
