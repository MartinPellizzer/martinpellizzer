Saussurea sikkimensis

Saussurea sikkimensis, commonly known as the Sikkim arrowhead, is a perennial herb native to the Himalayan regions of India, Nepal, and Bhutan.

It is characterized by its distinctive arrowhead-shaped leaves and tall, sparsely branched stems. The plant produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the summer months, adding vibrant color to its alpine habitat. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and well-drained soil.

Due to its rarity and ecological significance, it is often protected in conservation efforts.
