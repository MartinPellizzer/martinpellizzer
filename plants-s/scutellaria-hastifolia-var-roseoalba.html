Scutellaria hastifolia var. roseoalba

Scutellaria hastifolia var. roseoalba, commonly known as the rose-lavender skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by square stems and opposite leaves. The plant features showy, two-lipped flowers that are typically lavender to pink in color, with a distinctive hooded structure. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.
