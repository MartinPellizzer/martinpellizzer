Sempervivum × lineatum

Sempervivum × lineatum, commonly known as the chicken's foot or hen and chick, is a hybrid succulent plant belonging to the Crassulaceae family.

It is characterized by its rosettes of fleshy, thick leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of Central and Southern Europe, where it thrives in rocky and well-drained soils. It produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, or white during the summer months.

Sempervivum × lineatum is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that is well-suited for rock gardens and containers.
