Senecio friesiorum

Senecio friesiorum, commonly known as the Friesian groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in coastal areas. The plant features narrow, gray-green leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Senecio friesiorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
