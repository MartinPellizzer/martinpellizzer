Schwerinia cordifolia

Schwerinia cordifolia, commonly known as the heartleaf gesneriad, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Schwerinia cordifolia is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
