Saxifraga stellaris var. cryptopetala

Saxifraga stellaris var. cryptopetala, commonly known as the star saxifrage, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It features compact, mat-forming growth with small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink. The leaves are usually succulent, with a rosette structure that helps conserve moisture in harsh environments. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous habitats.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and adaptability to cold climates.
