Silene caesia subsp. samothracica

Silene caesia subsp.

samothracica, commonly known as the Samothracean campion, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family. It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender stems and clusters of pink to purple flowers.
