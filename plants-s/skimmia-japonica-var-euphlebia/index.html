Skimmia Japonica Var. Euphlebia

Skimmia japonica var. euphlebia is a compact, evergreen shrub prized for its glossy foliage and fragrant, orange-red flowers. Native to southern China, this variety thrives in shady, moist environments and is often used in ornamental horticulture. Its slow growth and dense form make it an excellent choice for garden borders or as a specimen plant. The plant's unique appearance and winter blooming habit add year-round interest to the landscape. Despite its beauty, it requires careful cultivation to ensure optimal health and flowering.
