Statice delicatula

Statice delicatula, commonly known as the slender statice, is a flowering plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is native to regions of southern Europe and western Asia, often found in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and the stems are usually erect and branching.

Statice delicatula is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in coastal or dry garden environments.
