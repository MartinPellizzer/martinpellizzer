Scutellaria distans

Scutellaria distans, commonly known as the distant skullcap, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue to purple in color, arranged in whorls along the stem. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Scutellaria distans is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodland areas and stream banks.
