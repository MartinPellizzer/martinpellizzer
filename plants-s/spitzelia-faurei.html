Spitzelia faurei

Spitzelia faurei, commonly known as the golden grass or African golden grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to South Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that produce dense, golden-yellow plumes in late summer to early autumn. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green with a slight blue or grayish tint. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Spitzelia faurei is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and is relatively low maintenance once established.
