Selaginella cesatii

Selaginella cesatii, commonly known as the desert spikemoss, is a vascular plant belonging to the family Selaginellaceae.

It is a small, evergreen moss-like plant that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant has creeping stems that produce small, scale-like leaves, and it reproduces via spores rather than seeds. Selaginella cesatii is adapted to survive in harsh conditions, with features such as succulent tissues that store water.

It is often found in rocky soils and is an important component of desert ecosystems.
