Stelis Mediocarinata

Stelis mediocarinata is a rare and delicate orchid species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. This epiphytic plant thrives in humid, shaded environments where it attaches itself to trees and rocks. Its unique floral structure features slender petals and a strikingly colored lip that sets it apart from other orchids. Despite its beauty, Stelis mediocarinata is highly endangered due to habitat loss and illegal collection. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure the survival of this fascinating and vulnerable plant.
