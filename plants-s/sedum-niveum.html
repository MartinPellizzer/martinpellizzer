Sedum niveum

Sedum niveum, commonly known as snow sedum, is a low-growing succulent native to Arctic and alpine regions.

It features dense clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in harsh, cold environments. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ability to withstand extreme temperatures makes it a hardy plant for cold climates.
