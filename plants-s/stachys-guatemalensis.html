Stachys guatemalensis

Stachys guatemalensis, commonly known as Guatemalan hedge nettle, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves with hairy surfaces and produces small, tubular flowers in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Stachys guatemalensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some indigenous cultures.
