Sedum microstachyum

Sedum microstachyum, commonly known as the dwarf stonecrop, is a low-growing succulent native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features dense clusters of tiny, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has fleshy, upright leaves that are typically blue-green in color, adapted to store water. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy choice for rock gardens.

Its compact size and ornamental flowers make it a popular plant for alpine and xeriscaping landscapes.
