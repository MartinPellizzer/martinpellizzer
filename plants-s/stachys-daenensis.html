Stachys daenensis

Stachys daenensis, commonly known as Persian betony, is a flowering plant native to the mountains of Iran.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features square, hairy stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture. Its flowers are typically violet to blue in color, arranged in whorled clusters at the ends of the branches.

Stachys daenensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses in herbal medicine.
