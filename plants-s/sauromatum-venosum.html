Sauromatum venosum

Sauromatum venosum, commonly known as the spider lily, is a striking plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, showy flowers that resemble spiders, hence its common name. The plant produces vibrant orange or red flowers with long, slender petals and a distinctive, narrow tube. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

The botanical features of Sauromatum venosum make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and botanical collections.
