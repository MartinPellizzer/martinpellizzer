Stelis guatemalensis

Stelis guatemalensis, commonly known as the Guatemalan stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Central America.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit.

Stelis guatemalensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique floral display.
