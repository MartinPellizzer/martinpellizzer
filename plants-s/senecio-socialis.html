Senecio socialis

Senecio socialis, commonly known as the social groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, yellow flowers that are clustered in dense heads, surrounded by narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a hardy and resilient plant suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
