Sedum chingtungense

Sedum chingtungense, commonly known as the Ching Tung stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to China.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, clustered leaves that are typically green or reddish in color. The plant produces star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Sedum chingtungense is often used in rock gardens and as a decorative plant due to its hardy nature and aesthetic appeal.
