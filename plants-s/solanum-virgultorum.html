Solanum virgultorum

Solanum virgultorum, commonly known as the Virginia nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in the summer, followed by red, berry-like fruits. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a hairy texture.

Solanum virgultorum is part of the nightshade family, which includes many plants with toxic properties.
