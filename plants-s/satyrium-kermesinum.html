Satyrium kermesinum

Satyrium kermesinum, commonly known as the Kermes bug plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Fabaceae.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has trifoliate leaves and produces pods that contain seeds. It is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to thrive in arid environments.
