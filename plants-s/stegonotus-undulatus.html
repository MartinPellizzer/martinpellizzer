Stegonotus undulatus

Stegonotus undulatus, commonly known as the wavy leaf fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, undulating fronds that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its rhizome is creeping and produces multiple upright fronds with a feathery appearance.

The botanical features of Stegonotus undulatus make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
