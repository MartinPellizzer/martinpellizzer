Sprekelia spectabilis

Sprekelia spectabilis, commonly known as the showy lily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and features broad, strap-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Sprekelia spectabilis is known for its long blooming period, often extending from late spring through early summer.
