Syngonium vellozianum var. poeppigii

Syngonium vellozianum var. poeppigii, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a tropical species native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, arrowhead-shaped leaves that vary in color from bright green to variegated patterns. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers indirect sunlight.

This ornamental plant is popular for its aesthetic appeal and ability to purify indoor air.
