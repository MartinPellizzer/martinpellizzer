Solanum kavanayense

Solanum kavanayense, commonly known as the Kavanayen nightshade, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant features small, white or pale yellow flowers and produces berries that are typically green when mature. Its leaves are usually ovate with a rough texture, and it is often found in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its toxic properties, it is not commonly cultivated for agricultural purposes.
