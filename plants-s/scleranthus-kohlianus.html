Scleranthus kohlianus

Scleranthus kohlianus, commonly known as the Kashmir thorn, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Scleranthus kohlianus is often found in alpine meadows and is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil in mountainous regions.
