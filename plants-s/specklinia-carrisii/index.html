Specklinia Carrisii

Carr's specklinia, scientifically known as Specklinia carrisii, is a rare and exquisite orchid native to the mountainous regions of Borneo. This diminutive plant is celebrated for its striking, velvety green leaves and clusters of delicate, white or pale pink flowers. Despite its beauty, it remains a highly sought-after species due to its limited natural habitat and slow growth rate. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this fragile orchid from threats like habitat destruction and illegal collection. Its unique characteristics make it a favorite among orchid enthusiasts and a symbol of botanical rarity.
