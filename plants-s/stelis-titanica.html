Stelis titanica

Stelis titanica, commonly known as the giant stelis orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 centimeters in diameter. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, utilizing its aerial roots to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air. Its foliage consists of long, narrow leaves that help it thrive in humid, shaded environments.

Stelis titanica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
