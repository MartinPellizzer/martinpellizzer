Senna affinis

Senna affinis, commonly known as the yellow senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height, with pinnately compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, oblong pods that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine.

Senna affinis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been utilized in herbal remedies for its potential laxative properties.
