Senecio lorentziella

Senecio lorentziella, commonly known as the Lorentzella groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. It produces small yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by fluffy, white seed heads.
