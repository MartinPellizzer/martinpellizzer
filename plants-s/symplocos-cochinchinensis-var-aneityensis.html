Symplocos cochinchinensis var. aneityensis

Symplocos cochinchinensis var. aneityensis, commonly known as the Aneitynsian wax tree, is a shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are small, fleshy berries that turn red when mature.

This species is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and along stream banks.
