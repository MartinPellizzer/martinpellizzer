Specklinia malachantha

Specklinia malachantha, commonly known as the Malacantha Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and green. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on tree trunks or rocks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with a fine, velvety texture.

Specklinia malachantha is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
