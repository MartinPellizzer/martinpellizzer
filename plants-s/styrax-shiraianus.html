Styrax shiraianus

Styrax shiraianus, commonly known as the Japanese styrax, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Japan.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate to elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy. Its bark is smooth and reddish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

Styrax shiraianus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil.
