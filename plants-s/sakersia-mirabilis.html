Sakersia mirabilis

Sakersia mirabilis, commonly known as the miracle beauty, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, red, and white. The plant has a compact growth habit with glossy, dark green leaves that are lance-shaped. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Sakersia mirabilis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
