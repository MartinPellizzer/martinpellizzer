Stephanandra flexuosa var. chinensis

Stephanandra flexuosa var. chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese stephanandra, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Ericaceae and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and attractive, small, white flowers. The plant features elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces berries that range in color from red to black.

It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often used in ornamental horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and hardy nature.
