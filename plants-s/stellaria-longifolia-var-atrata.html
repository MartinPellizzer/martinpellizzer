Stellaria longifolia var. atrata

Stellaria longifolia var. atrata, commonly known as the black-stemmed starwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features slender, upright stems that are often tinged with a dark hue, giving it its common name. Its small, white flowers are arranged in clusters, and the leaves are lance-shaped with a hairy texture.

This variety is characterized by its distinctive appearance and is often found in rocky or disturbed soils.
