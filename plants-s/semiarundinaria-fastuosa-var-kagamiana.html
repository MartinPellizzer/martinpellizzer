Semiarundinaria fastuosa var. kagamiana

Semiarundinaria fastuosa var. kagamiana, commonly known as the Kagamiana bamboo, is a species native to Japan.

It is a clumping bamboo that grows in dense, compact clusters, forming a tall, slender appearance. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.

The bamboo is also appreciated for its resilience and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
