Spilotantha Leathersii

Leathers' spilotantha, scientifically known as Spilotantha leathersii, is a rare and enigmatic flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa. This unique species belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of violet and white. Despite its botanical significance, it remains poorly understood due to its limited distribution and elusive nature in the wild. Conservation efforts are underway to protect this fragile species from habitat loss and over-collection. Its discovery and study offer valuable insights into the biodiversity of desert ecosystems.
