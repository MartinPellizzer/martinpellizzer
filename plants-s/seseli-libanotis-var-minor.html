Seseli libanotis var. minor

Seseli libanotis var. minor, commonly known as the Lesser Lebanon Seseli, is a flowering plant in the family Apiaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, white to pale yellow umbellate flowers. This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
