Stellaria diversiflora f. robusta

Stellaria diversiflora f. robusta, commonly known as the robust starwort, is a variant of the starwort plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant features slender, branching stems and small, opposite leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and often overlooked member of the Caryophyllaceae family.
