Stelis caveata

Stelis caveata, commonly known as the cave orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single, lance-shaped leaf. Its flowers are usually pale green or white, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

Stelis caveata is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
