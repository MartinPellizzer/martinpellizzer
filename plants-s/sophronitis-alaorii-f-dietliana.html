Sophronitis alaorii f. dietliana

Sophronitis alaorii f. dietliana, commonly known as the Dietliana orchid, is a rare and captivating orchid species native to the Andean regions of South America.

The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with intricate floral structures, showcasing the genus Sophronitis's typical ornamental features.
