Senecio eurycephalus var. lewisrosei

Senecio eurycephalus var. lewisrosei, commonly known as Lewis's rose-gum, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy yellow to orange composite flowers. It thrives in rocky soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Its botanical structure supports its adaptation to arid environments, making it a resilient and visually appealing species.
