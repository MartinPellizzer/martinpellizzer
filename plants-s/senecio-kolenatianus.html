Senecio kolenatianus

Senecio kolenatianus, commonly known as the Kolenati's groundsel, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It is often found in mountainous regions and is known for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
