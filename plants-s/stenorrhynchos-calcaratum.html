Stenorrhynchos calcaratum

Stenorrhynchos calcaratum, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, spurred orchid flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that help it conserve moisture.

Stenorrhynchos calcaratum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
