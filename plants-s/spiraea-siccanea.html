Spiraea siccanea

Spiraea siccanea, commonly known as the dry spiraea, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of white or pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings. Its leaves are typically narrow and dark green, providing a striking contrast to the delicate flowers.

Spiraea siccanea is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in colder climates.
