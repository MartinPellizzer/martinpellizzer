Sutera divaricata

Sutera divaricata, commonly known as the Variegated Sutera, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub characterized by its trailing growth habit and distinctive variegated leaves with white and green patterns. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of cultivation.

Sutera divaricata thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade conditions.
