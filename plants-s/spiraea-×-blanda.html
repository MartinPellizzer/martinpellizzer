Spiraea × blanda

Spiraea × blanda, commonly known as meadow spiraea, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It features clusters of small, fragrant white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and has arching, slender stems. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a serrated edge.

Spiraea × blanda thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often used in ornamental landscaping for its attractive foliage and blooms.
