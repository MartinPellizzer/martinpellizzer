Stellaria holostea var. angustifolia

Stellaria holostea var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf starwort, is a flowering plant species native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family, characterized by its showy, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges. Its leaves are linear and narrow, while its flowers are white or pale pink with five petals.

It is a herbaceous perennial that blooms from late spring to early summer.
