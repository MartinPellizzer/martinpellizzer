Sideritis marianica

Sideritis marianica, commonly known as Mary's thyme, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants known for their medicinal properties. The plant features narrow, gray-green leaves and produces small, pink or purple flowers in summer. It thrives in rocky or limestone soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats.

Sideritis marianica is valued for its aromatic qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
