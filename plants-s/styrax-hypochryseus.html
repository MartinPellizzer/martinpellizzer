Styrax hypochryseus

Styrax hypochryseus, commonly known as the golden styrax, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces small, reddish-brown berries that are edible but not particularly flavorful. Its leaves are ovate, glossy, and turn yellow in autumn.

Styrax hypochryseus is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and is often used in gardens and landscapes.
