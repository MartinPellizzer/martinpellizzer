Schoenus lithospermus

Schoenus lithospermus, commonly known as the stone root sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes grass-like plants known as sedges. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in mountainous regions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a fibrous texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters.

The plant is adapted to arid conditions and is often found in rocky outcrops and along stream banks.
