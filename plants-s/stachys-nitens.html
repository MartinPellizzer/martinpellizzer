Stachys nitens

Stachys nitens, commonly known as the slender hedge nettle, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and streambanks. Its small, tubular flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom in late summer.

Stachys nitens is often used in native plant gardens due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
