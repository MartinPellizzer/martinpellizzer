Silene stellata

Silene stellata, commonly known as starflower, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, star-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or white. The plant grows in meadows, woodlands, and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soil. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while its stems are slender and erect.

Silene stellata is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
