Sterigmapetalum exappendiculatum

Sterigmapetalum exappendiculatum, commonly known as the appendiculate starpetal.

This rare plant is characterized by its unique floral structure, featuring elongated, appendiculate petals that extend from the base of the flower. The botanical description highlights its intricate inflorescence, which consists of dense clusters of small, star-shaped blooms. It is native to specific regions in South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments.
