Skimmia japonica var. arisanensis

Skimmia japonica var. arisanensis, commonly known as the Arisan skimmia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It features glossy, dark green elliptical leaves that remain throughout the year. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers in spring, which are followed by red-orange berries. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from its foliage, flowers, and berries, which add visual interest throughout the growing season.
