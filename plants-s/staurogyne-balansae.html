Staurogyne balansae

Staurogyne balansae, commonly known as the dwarf water starwort, is a small aquatic plant native to South America.

It typically grows in shallow, still or slow-moving freshwater habitats such as ponds and marshes. The plant has finely divided, ribbon-like leaves that are arranged in whorls around the stem. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or white in color.

Staurogyne balansae is often used in aquariums due to its ornamental value and ability to thrive in controlled environments.
