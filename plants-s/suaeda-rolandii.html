Suaeda rolandii

Suaeda rolandii, commonly known as desert spinach, is a halophytic plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It thrives in saline soils and is adapted to withstand extreme temperatures and drought conditions. The plant has succulent leaves that store water, and its flowers are typically small and yellow or greenish. It plays a role in stabilizing soil in desert environments.

Suaeda rolandii is also valued for its nutritional content, making it a potential food source in harsh climates.
