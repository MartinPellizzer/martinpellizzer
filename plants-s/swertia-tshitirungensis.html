Swertia tshitirungensis

Swertia tshitirungensis, commonly known as the Himalayan gentian, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Eastern Himalayas.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests and rocky slopes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with a fine layer of hair, while its stems are often woody at the base.

Swertia tshitirungensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
