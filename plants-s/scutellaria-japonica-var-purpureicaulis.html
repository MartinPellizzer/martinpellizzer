Scutellaria japonica var. purpureicaulis

Scutellaria japonica var. purpureicaulis, commonly known as Japanese skullcap, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features upright stems covered in fine hairs and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple, depending on the variety. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or rocky habitats.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
