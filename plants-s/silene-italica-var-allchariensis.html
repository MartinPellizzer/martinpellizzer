Silene italica var. allchariensis

Silene italica var. allchariensis, commonly known as the Allchar carnation, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan region.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink to red flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered flowers during the summer months.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens.
