Senecio dissidens

Senecio dissidens, commonly known as the narrow-leaved groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Senecio dissidens is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in dry conditions.
