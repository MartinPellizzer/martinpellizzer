Saxifraga × cadevallii

Saxifraga × cadevallii, commonly known as the rockfoil or rock saxifrage, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Saxifragaceae family.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit and fleshy, often succulent leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It is native to rocky and alpine environments in Europe, where it thrives in well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens with good drainage and partial sunlight.
