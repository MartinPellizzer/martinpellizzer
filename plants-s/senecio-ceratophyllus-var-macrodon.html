Senecio ceratophyllus var. macrodon

Senecio ceratophyllus var. macrodon, commonly known as the giant yellow ragwort, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, yellow-orange flowers and dense, woolly foliage. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are alternate, with a distinctive blue-green hue and a thick, fleshy texture.

It is often found in mountainous regions and is adapted to arid environments.
