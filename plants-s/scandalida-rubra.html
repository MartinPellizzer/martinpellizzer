Scandalida rubra

Scandalida rubra, commonly known as the red scented orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red petals and a pleasant, spicy fragrance. The plant typically grows in tropical rainforests, thriving in humid and shaded environments. Its flowers are borne on erect stems and bloom during the wet season.

Scandalida rubra is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its traditional medicinal uses in some cultures.
