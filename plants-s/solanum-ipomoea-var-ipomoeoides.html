Solanum ipomoea var. ipomoeoides

Solanum ipomoea var. ipomoeoides, commonly known as the purple morning glory, is a flowering plant belonging to the nightshade family (Solanaceae).

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in the early morning. The plant typically grows as a climbing vine with heart-shaped leaves and hairy stems. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
