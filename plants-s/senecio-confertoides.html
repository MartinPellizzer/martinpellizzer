Senecio confertoides

Senecio confertoides, commonly known as the dense-leaved groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features narrow, blue-green leaves arranged in a compact rosette. Its flowers are small, yellow, and clustered in dense inflorescences.

It is adapted to arid environments and is often found in fynbos vegetation.
