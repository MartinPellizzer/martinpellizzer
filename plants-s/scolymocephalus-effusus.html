Scolymocephalus effusus

Scolymocephalus effusus, commonly known as the woolly scolymus, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy foliage and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and is often found in open habitats. It has a compact growth habit and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.

Scolymocephalus effusus is a perennial species that thrives in full sun and well-drained conditions.
