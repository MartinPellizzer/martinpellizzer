Satureja ovata

Satureja ovata, commonly known as Spanish thyme, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant leaves and clusters of pink or purple flowers. The plant belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is known for its aromatic properties and medicinal uses. Satureja ovata thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its essential oils contain compounds like thymol and carvacrol, which contribute to its distinctive scent and potential health benefits.
