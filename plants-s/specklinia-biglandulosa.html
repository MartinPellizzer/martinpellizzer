Specklinia biglandulosa

Specklinia biglandulosa, commonly known as the hairy-leaved orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are often covered in fine, glandular hairs, giving it its common name.

Specklinia biglandulosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
