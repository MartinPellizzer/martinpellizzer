Stylagrostis elegans

Stylagrostis elegans, commonly known as the elegant orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, often branched stems and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant prefers warm, humid environments and is often found in forested areas with well-drained soil. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the air, a feature common in epiphytic orchids.

The flowers are pollinated primarily by insects, contributing to its ecological significance in its native habitat.
