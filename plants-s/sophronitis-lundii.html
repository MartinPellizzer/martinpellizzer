Sophronitis lundii

Sophronitis lundii, commonly known as the Lund's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in the understory of forests. Its roots are epiphytic, meaning they attach to tree bark rather than growing in soil.

Sophronitis lundii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its delicate beauty.
