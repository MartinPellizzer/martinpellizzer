Spiraeanthemum brongniartianum

Spiraeanthemum brongniartianum, commonly known as the African snowflake, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, characterized by its dense, rounded form. The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and covered in fine, silky hairs, giving them a soft texture.

The plant is well adapted to arid conditions and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
