Sutera welwitschii

Sutera welwitschii, commonly known as the Welwitschia mirabilis, is a unique and ancient plant native to the Namib Desert in Namibia.

It is characterized by its two large, leathery leaves that can grow up to 3 meters in length. The plant possesses a robust, woody trunk that can reach several meters in height. It is well adapted to arid environments, with a deep root system that allows it to access groundwater.

Sutera welwitschii is considered a living fossil, with a lineage dating back over 100 million years.
