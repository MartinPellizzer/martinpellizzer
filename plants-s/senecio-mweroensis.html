Senecio mweroensis

Senecio mweroensis, commonly known as the Mweru groundsel, is a flowering plant native to Tanzania.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small yellow flowers arranged in clusters.

Senecio mweroensis is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
