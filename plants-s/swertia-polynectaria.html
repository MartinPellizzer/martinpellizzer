Swertia polynectaria

Swertia polynectaria, commonly known as the Himalayan swertia, is a perennial herb native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, blue or violet flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

Swertia polynectaria is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
