Salvia sennenii var. acanthifolia

Salvia sennenii var. acanthifolia, commonly known as the Mexican sage or red sage, is a perennial herb native to Mexico.

It features upright stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces striking red or purple flower spikes that bloom in summer. Its foliage is often aromatic and has been used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
