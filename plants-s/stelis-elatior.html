Stelis elatior

Stelis elatior, commonly known as the tall stelis orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are usually elongated and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Stelis elatior is prized by horticulturists for its elegance and relatively easy care requirements.
