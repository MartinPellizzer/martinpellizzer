Staurogyne versteegii

Staurogyne versteegii, commonly known as the Amazonian water fern, is a species of aquatic fern native to South America.

It thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments, often found in swamps and marshes. The plant has finely divided, feather-like fronds that are adapted for underwater growth. Its rhizomes form dense mats, helping to stabilize sediment and improve water quality.

Staurogyne versteegii plays a vital role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
