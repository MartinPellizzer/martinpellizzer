Spiraea crenata f. homophylla

Spiraea crenata f. homophylla, commonly known as the smoothleaf weeping spirea, is a compact, shrub-like plant characterized by its dense, cascading clusters of white flowers.

It features finely cut, ovate leaves that are smooth and glossy, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fluffy seed heads that add interest in the fall. It is typically evergreen in colder climates but may lose some foliage in harsh winters.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to various garden conditions.
