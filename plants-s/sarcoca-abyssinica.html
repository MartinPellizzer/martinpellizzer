Sarcoca abyssinica

Sarcoca abyssinica, commonly known as the Abyssinian sarcoca, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, intricate flower structure. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous regions, adapting to high altitudes and arid conditions. Its flowers are often brightly colored, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
