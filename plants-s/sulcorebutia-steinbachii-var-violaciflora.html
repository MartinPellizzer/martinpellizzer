Sulcorebutia steinbachii var. violaciflora

Sulcorebutia steinbachii var. violaciflora, commonly known as the violet-flowered crassula, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy rosettes of pale green to blue-gray leaves that are covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces vibrant violet or purple flowers on tall, slender stems during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Due to its striking appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
