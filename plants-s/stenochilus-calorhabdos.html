Stenochilus calorhabdos

Stenochilus calorhabdos, commonly known as the fire-scales orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking, flame-colored pseudobulbs and showy, orange-red flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Stenochilus calorhabdos is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
