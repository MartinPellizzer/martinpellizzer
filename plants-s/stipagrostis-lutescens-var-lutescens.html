Stipagrostis lutescens var. lutescens

Stipagrostis lutescens var. lutescens, commonly known as the yellow speckled grass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, linear leaves that are typically yellow-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale yellow to golden flowers. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in grassland and savanna habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
