Sorbus keenanii

Sorbus keenanii, commonly known as the Chinese whitebeam, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and clusters of small, white flowers that appear in early spring. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible for birds but not for humans. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Sorbus keenanii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native habitats.
