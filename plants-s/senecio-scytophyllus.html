Senecio scytophyllus

Senecio scytophyllus, commonly known as the Cape York daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, yellow flowers that are typically arranged in dense, daisy-like clusters. It is a perennial herb that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Senecio scytophyllus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
