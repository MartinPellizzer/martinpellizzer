Syagrus × tostana

Syagrus × tostana, commonly known as the hybrid palm, is a naturally occurring hybrid between Syagrus romanzoffiana and Syagrus acuriçai.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that give it a striking, tropical appearance. The plant is native to Brazil and is often found in coastal areas with well-drained soils. Syagrus × tostana is valued for its ornamental qualities and is commonly used in landscaping.

It thrives in full sun and requires minimal maintenance once established.
