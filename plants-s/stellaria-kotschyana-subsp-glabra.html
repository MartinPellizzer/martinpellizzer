Stellaria kotschyana subsp. glabra

Stellaria kotschyana subsp.

glabra, commonly known as the smooth starwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in alpine or subalpine environments.

The plant features slender stems that are often branched and covered with small, white or pale flowers arranged in clusters. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slight pubescence, while the subspecies glabra is distinguished by its smooth, hairless surface.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
