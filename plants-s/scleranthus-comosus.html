Scleranthus comosus

It is a herbaceous annual plant characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is found in various parts of Europe and Asia. Scleranthus comosus is adapted to thrive in dry conditions and is often found along roadsides and in open fields.
