Schistogyne berroi

Schistogyne berroi, commonly known as the African rock fig, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Moraceae family, which includes figs and mulberries. The plant is characterized by its thick, leathery leaves and small, clustered greenish-white flowers. Schistogyne berroi produces edible berries that are typically consumed raw or cooked.

It is often found growing in rocky or sandy soils, thriving in arid and semi-arid environments.
