Saxifraga parisii

Saxifraga parisii, commonly known as the Paris saxifrage, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the forests of central and eastern Europe.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features a rosette of elliptical, dark green leaves with a hairy texture. It produces small, white to pale pink flowers in late spring to early summer.

Saxifraga parisii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with cool, humid conditions.
