Stipa plumosa var. micrura

Stipa plumosa var. micrura, commonly known as the silky feather grass, is a grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its finely textured, silver-blue foliage that forms dense, clumping growth. The plant produces slender, feathery inflorescences that give it a delicate, ornamental appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant once established.

This variety is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
