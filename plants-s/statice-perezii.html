Statice perezii

Statice perezii, commonly known as the dwarf statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, spike-like flowers. The plant typically produces small, colorful blooms in shades of pink, red, and white, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Statice perezii thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its drought tolerance and ability to reseed naturally contribute to its resilience in various environmental conditions.
