Solanum heteroclitum

Solanum heteroclitum, commonly known as the yellow nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and purple-black berries. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in open woodlands or disturbed areas. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Solanum heteroclitum is known for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
