Saxifraga klickitatensis

Saxifraga klickitatensis, commonly known as Klickitat saxifrage, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant features basal rosettes of oval, waxy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, pink to reddish-purple, and bloom in late spring to early summer.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky habitats.
