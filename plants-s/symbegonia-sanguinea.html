Symbegonia sanguinea

Symbegonia sanguinea, commonly known as the red-flowered begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Symbegonia sanguinea is often cultivated for its striking appearance and ease of care.
