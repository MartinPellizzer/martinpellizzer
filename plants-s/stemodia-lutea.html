Stemodia lutea

Stemodia lutea, commonly known as the yellow stemodia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands. The plant features a slender, upright stem covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a slightly hairy surface. It produces clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring.

The plant is known for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
