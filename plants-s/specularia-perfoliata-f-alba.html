Specularia perfoliata f. alba

Specularia perfoliata f. alba, commonly known as the white leaf everlasting, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its white, showy flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are often tinged with a bluish hue. It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and thrives in well-drained, sandy soils.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in rock gardens and as a cut flower.
