Stelis brevilabris

Stelis brevilabris, commonly known as the short-lipped stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often found in the understory of tropical forests. The plant features a slender, erect stem with several elongated, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small and usually pale green or yellowish, with a short, narrow lip.

Stelis brevilabris is noted for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in specialized horticulture.
