Selago ascendens

Selago ascendens, commonly known as the ascending selago, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal or inland areas. It has narrow, linear leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem.
