Stemona gloriosoides

Stemona gloriosoides, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It features slender, branching stems that are often covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Stemona gloriosoides is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits and is also cultivated for its ornamental appeal.
