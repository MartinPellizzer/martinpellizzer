Scutellaria scordiifolia

Scutellaria scordiifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved skullcap, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, while its flowers are small, tubular, and usually blue or violet in color.

Scutellaria scordiifolia is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
