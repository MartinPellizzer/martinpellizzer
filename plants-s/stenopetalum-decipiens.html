Stenopetalum decipiens

Stenopetalum decipiens, commonly known as the deceptive star-orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, star-shaped flowers that resemble other orchid species. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are small and fragrant, with a complex structure that aids in pollination by specific insect species.

Stenopetalum decipiens is notable for its deceptive appearance, which can lead to misidentification with other orchid genera.
