Specklinia delicatula

Specklinia delicatula, commonly known as the delicate specklinia, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, compact growth habit and its delicate, elongated leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in a loose inflorescence. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
