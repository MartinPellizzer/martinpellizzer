Stegolepis gleasoniana

Stegolepis gleasoniana, commonly known as the Florida starflower, is a rare flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its star-shaped white flowers and small, elliptical leaves. The plant typically grows in sandy soils along the edges of wetlands and in disturbed areas. It is a perennial herb that thrives in moist, acidic conditions.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and is protected in several states.
