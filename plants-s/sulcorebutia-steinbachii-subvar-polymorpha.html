Sulcorebutia steinbachii subvar. polymorpha

Sulcorebutia steinbachii subvar. polymorpha, commonly known as the variegated stone cactus, is a member of the Cactaceae family native to South America.

It is a compact, low-growing cactus with a short, thick stem that often branches into multiple offsets. The plant features distinctive, ribbed stems that are typically green with white or yellow variegation. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in clusters at the stem tips during the warmer months.

It is well-suited for rock gardens and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.
