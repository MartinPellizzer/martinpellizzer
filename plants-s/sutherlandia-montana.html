Sutherlandia montana

Sutherlandia montana, commonly known as cancer bush, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, yellowish-green flowers and woody stems that often have a distinctive red or purple coloration. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, typically growing in sandy or rocky soils. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain seeds.

Sutherlandia montana has been traditionally used in herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
