Sinoseris changii

Sinoseris changii, commonly known as the Chinese dandelion, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and riverbanks. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring and summer months.

Sinoseris changii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
