Solanum oblongifolium

Solanum oblongifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, featuring dark green, oval-shaped leaves and yellow-orange flowers. Its berries are small, round, and change color from green to red as they mature.

Solanum oblongifolium is often found in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
