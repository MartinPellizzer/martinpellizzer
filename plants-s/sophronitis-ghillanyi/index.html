Sophronitis Ghillanyi

Ghillany's orchid, scientifically known as Sophronitis ghillanyi, is a rare and exquisite species native to the cloud forests of Ecuador. This orchid is celebrated for its vibrant, colorful blooms and unique morphological features that set it apart from other orchid species. Despite its beauty, it remains a elusive plant, often found in remote and poorly studied habitats. Its conservation status is a growing concern due to habitat loss and climate change. Researchers and botanists are working tirelessly to better understand and protect this remarkable orchid.
