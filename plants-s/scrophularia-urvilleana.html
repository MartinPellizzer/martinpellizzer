Scrophularia urvilleana

Scrophularia urvilleana, commonly known as the meadow scrophularia, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae, which includes many species of flowering plants. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It produces showy, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple in color.

Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
