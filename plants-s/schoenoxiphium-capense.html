Schoenoxiphium capense

Schoenoxiphium capense, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow to orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, tubular florets arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

Schoenoxiphium capense is well adapted to Mediterranean climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
