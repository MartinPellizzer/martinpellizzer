Spiraeanthemum vitiense

Spiraeanthemum vitiense, commonly known as the Vitiense Spirea, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Oceania.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are typically elliptical and have a smooth, glossy surface.

Spiraeanthemum vitiense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its visual interest and fragrance.
