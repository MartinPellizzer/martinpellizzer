Silene beguinotii

Silene beguinotii, commonly known as the Canary Islands campion, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or coastal habitats. The plant produces showy pink to purple flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, adapting to the arid environment.

Silene beguinotii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
