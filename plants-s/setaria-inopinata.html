Setaria inopinata

Setaria inopinata, commonly known as the slender bristlegrass, is a flowering plant species belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia. Setaria inopinata is often considered a weed but has some ecological importance in certain environments.
