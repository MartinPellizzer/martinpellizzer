Stipa contracta

Stipa contracta, commonly known as slender needlegrass, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

Stipa contracta is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
