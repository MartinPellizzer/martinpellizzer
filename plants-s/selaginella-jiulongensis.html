Selaginella jiulongensis

It is native to China, specifically found in the Jiulongshan region of Yunnan Province. This plant exhibits a unique morphology with small, scale-like leaves arranged in a spiral pattern along its stems. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas.
