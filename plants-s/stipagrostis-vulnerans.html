Stipagrostis vulnerans

Stipagrostis vulnerans, commonly known as the vulnerable grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in conservation status.
