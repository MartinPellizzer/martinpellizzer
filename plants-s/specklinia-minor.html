Specklinia minor

Specklinia minor, commonly known as the miniature fern.

It is a small epiphytic fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. The plant has delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture and indirect light.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and terrariums.
