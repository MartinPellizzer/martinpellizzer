Spatularia petiolaris

Spatularia petiolaris, commonly known as the petiole spatula, is a rare and unique fern species characterized by its distinctive leaf morphology.

Its fronds are elongated and often have a spatula-like shape, giving the plant its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or stream banks. Spatularia petiolaris has a delicate, lace-like appearance with finely divided leaves that contribute to its ornamental value.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
