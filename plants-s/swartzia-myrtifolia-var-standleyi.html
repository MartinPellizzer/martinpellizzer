Swartzia myrtifolia var. standleyi

Swartzia myrtifolia var. standleyi, commonly known as the yellow wood or yellow ironwood, is a species of flowering plant in the Fabaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its dense, dark wood and striking yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as a small to medium-sized tree, reaching heights of up to 15 meters.
