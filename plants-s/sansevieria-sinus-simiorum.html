Sansevieria sinus-simiorum

Sansevieria sinus-simiorum, commonly known as the Spider Plant, is a succulent plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with light green or silver markings. The plant is well-known for its air-purifying properties and ability to thrive in low-light conditions. It produces small, white flowers on long, slender stalks during the growing season.

Its resilience and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
