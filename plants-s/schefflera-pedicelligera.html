Schefflera pedicelligera

Schefflera pedicelligera, commonly known as the Philippine umbrella tree, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its large, umbrella-like compound leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in diameter. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and landscape design.
