Syagrus gomesii

Syagrus gomesii, commonly known as the golden palm, is a species of palm native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant thrives in tropical climates and is often found in coastal areas and lowland forests. Its flowers are small and clustered, and it produces fruit that is edible for some bird species.

Syagrus gomesii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for landscaping purposes.
