Senecio tenuiculus

Senecio tenuiculus, commonly known as the narrowleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellow flowers in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
