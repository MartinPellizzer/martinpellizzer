Sinobambusa tootsik var. maeshimana

Sinobambusa tootsik var. maeshimana, commonly known as the Maeshimana bamboo, is a compact, ornamental bamboo species native to Japan.

It features narrow, dark green leaves that are slightly curled and covered in a fine, silvery down. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with pale yellow flowers, which appear in late spring. It is well-suited for container growing or as a decorative garden plant due to its upright growth habit and dense foliage.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and ease of care in both indoor and outdoor settings.
