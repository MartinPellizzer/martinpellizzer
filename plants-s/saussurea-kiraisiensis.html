Saussurea kiraisiensis

Saussurea kiraisiensis, commonly known as the Kirai's fleur-de-lis, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stem and numerous small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, thriving in cool and moist conditions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces a distinctive, aromatic scent.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
