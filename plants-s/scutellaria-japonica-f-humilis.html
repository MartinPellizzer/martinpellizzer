Scutellaria japonica f. humilis

Scutellaria japonica f. humilis, commonly known as the Japanese skullcap or small skullcap, is a low-growing perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular blue or violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its foliage is typically lance-shaped with a slightly hairy texture, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.
