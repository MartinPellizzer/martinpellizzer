Sarcostemma viminale subsp. thunbergii

Sarcostemma viminale subsp.

thunbergii, commonly known as the silver vine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia. It is a climbing shrub characterized by its slender, wiry stems and small, opposite leaves.

The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers, which are followed by small, red berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and greenhouses.
