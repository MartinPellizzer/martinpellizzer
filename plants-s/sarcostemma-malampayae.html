Sarcostemma malampayae

Sarcostemma malampayae, commonly known as the Malampaya starvine, is a climbing plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its slender, twining stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by follicles containing numerous seeds.
