Schinus polygama f. parviflora

Schinus polygama f. parviflora, commonly known as the dwarf flowering flame tree, is a compact shrub or small tree native to South America.

It belongs to the Anacardiaceae family and is characterized by its ornamental value, particularly during the flowering season. Its foliage provides dense shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
