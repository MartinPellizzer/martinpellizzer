Sison tenerum

Sison tenerum, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in spikes. Sison tenerum is known for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in both wet and dry conditions.

It is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
