Sida pulchra

Sida pulchra, commonly known as the beautiful sida, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States and parts of Mexico.

It is a herbaceous annual or biennial plant that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet. The plant produces small, pink to white flowers that bloom in clusters, and its leaves are ovate with a hairy texture. Sida pulchra is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides, thriving in full sun.

It is known for its adaptability and is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
