Senecio roberti-friesii

Senecio roberti-friesii, commonly known as the Friesii Senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance, it is often cultivated in arid and semi-arid regions.
