Smilax cyclophylla

Smilax cyclophylla, commonly known as the common smilax, is a vine-like plant native to the southeastern United States.

It features a robust, woody stem with sharp, recurved spines along its length. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a waxy texture with a distinctive, serrated margin.

Smilax cyclophylla is often found in moist, shaded areas such as forests and along stream banks.
