Sinocarum coloratum

Sinocarum coloratum, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.

The species is also noted for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
