Spilanthes ramosa

Spilanthes ramosa, commonly known as the hairy pincushion plant, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Spilanthes ramosa is adapted to thrive in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
