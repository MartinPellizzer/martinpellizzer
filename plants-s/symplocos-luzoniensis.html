Symplocos luzoniensis

Symplocos luzoniensis, commonly known as the Luzon waterapple, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are often clustered at the ends of branches. Symplocos luzoniensis is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as mountain forests and is valued for its ornamental and potential medicinal properties.
