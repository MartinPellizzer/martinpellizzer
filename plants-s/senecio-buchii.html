Senecio buchii

Senecio buchii, commonly known as the African snowball, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are usually narrow and slightly hairy, and it has a compact, bushy growth habit.

Senecio buchii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and ability to thrive in dry conditions.
