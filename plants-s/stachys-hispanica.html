Stachys hispanica

Stachys hispanica, commonly known as Spanish hedgehog.

It is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and western Asia. The plant features upright stems with dense clusters of small, pink to purple flowers. Its leaves are hairy and lance-shaped, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Stachys hispanica is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
