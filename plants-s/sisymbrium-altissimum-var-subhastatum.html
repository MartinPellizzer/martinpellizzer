Sisymbrium altissimum var. subhastatum

Sisymbrium altissimum var. subhastatum, commonly known as the tall shepherd's purse, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and distinctive triangular seed pods. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is found in various regions across Europe and Asia. It produces small white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.

The leaves are pinnately lobed and often have a rough texture, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.
