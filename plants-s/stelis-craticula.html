Stelis craticula

Stelis craticula, commonly known as the hairy stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia, including countries like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, with its roots anchored in the substrate. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, greenish-white flowers.

Stelis craticula thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture for optimal growth.
