Siphocampylus cordifolius

Siphocampylus cordifolius, commonly known as the heart-leaf siphocampylus, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and purple, often clustered in terminal inflorescences. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
