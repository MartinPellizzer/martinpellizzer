Spiraea sinobrahuica

Spiraea sinobrahuica, commonly known as the Chinese spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features slender, arching stems that bear clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers in early summer. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges, and its foliage turns yellow in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

Spiraea sinobrahuica is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature.
