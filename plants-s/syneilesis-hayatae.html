Syneilesis hayatae

Syneilesis hayatae, commonly known as the Japanese thistle or Hayata's thistle, is a species of flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Japan and is characterized by its composite flower heads and silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, purple to pinkish flowers that bloom in the summer months. Syneilesis hayatae typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
