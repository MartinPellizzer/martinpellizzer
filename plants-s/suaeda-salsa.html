Suaeda salsa

Suaeda salsa, commonly known as saltbush, is a halophyte plant adapted to saline environments.

It thrives in coastal areas and arid regions with high salt content in the soil. The plant has small, fleshy leaves that help reduce water loss and store moisture. Its flowers are typically yellow or greenish-yellow, arranged in clusters.

Suaeda salsa plays an important role in stabilizing soils and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
