Silene schmuckeri

Silene schmuckeri, commonly known as Schmucker's campion, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its pink to purple flowers and slender stems. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.
