Schaetzellia deckeri

Schaetzellia deckeri, commonly known as the Zulu lily, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep red, depending on the cultivar. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that form a dense rosette near the ground. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature contribute to its appeal among horticultural enthusiasts.
