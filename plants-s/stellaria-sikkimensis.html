Stellaria sikkimensis

Stellaria sikkimensis, commonly known as the Sikkim starwort, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to the Himalayan regions of India, Nepal, and Bhutan.

It typically thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky slopes. The plant features small, star-shaped white flowers with five petals, which bloom in early summer. Its leaves are narrow and lanceolate, arranged in a basal rosette, and it has a fibrous root system.

Stellaria sikkimensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
