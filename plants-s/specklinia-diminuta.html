Specklinia diminuta

Specklinia diminuta, commonly known as the miniature speckled orchid, is a small epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, speckled leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, typically during the warmer months. It thrives in high humidity and requires well-draining potting media to prevent root rot.

Due to its rarity and specific care requirements, it is often cultivated by experienced growers.
