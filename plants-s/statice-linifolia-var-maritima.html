Statice linifolia var. maritima

Statice linifolia var. maritima, commonly known as sea lavender, is a hardy, drought-tolerant plant native to coastal regions.

It features slender, linear leaves and spikes of small, showy blue or purple flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it ideal for rock gardens or coastal landscapes. Its salt tolerance allows it to flourish in harsh maritime environments.

It is often used in floral arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms and delicate appearance.
