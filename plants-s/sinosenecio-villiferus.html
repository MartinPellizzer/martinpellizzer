Sinosenecio villiferus

Sinosenecio villiferus, commonly known as the hairy leaf sinosenecio, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, hairy leaves that are often arranged in a basal rosette. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators.

Due to its ornamental value and rarity, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
