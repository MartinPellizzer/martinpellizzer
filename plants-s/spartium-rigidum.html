Spartium rigidum

Spartium rigidum, commonly known as rigid spartium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its erect, sparsely branched stems. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a rough texture.

Spartium rigidum is adapted to dry, rocky environments and is often found in open habitats such as scrublands and coastal areas.
