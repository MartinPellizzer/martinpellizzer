Solanum flavum humile

Solanum flavum humile, commonly known as the dwarf yellow nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants such as tomatoes and potatoes. The plant is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and smooth, green leaves. It typically grows to a height of less than 30 centimeters and thrives in warm, humid environments.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
