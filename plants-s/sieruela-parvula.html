Sieruela parvula

Sieruela parvula, commonly known as the small sieruela, is a small flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are usually pink or white in color. It is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to withstand salty air and strong winds.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
