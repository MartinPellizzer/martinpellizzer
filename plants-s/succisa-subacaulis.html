Succisa subacaulis

Succisa subacaulis, commonly known as the dwarf ice plant, is a low-growing succulent native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white, waxy powder, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, and purple during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact size and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
