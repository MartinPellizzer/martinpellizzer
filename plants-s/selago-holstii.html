Selago holstii

Selago holstii, commonly known as the blue ghost plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue and purple flowers. The plant features upright, branching stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.
