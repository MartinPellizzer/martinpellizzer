Scorzonera elongata

Scorzonera elongata, commonly known as the elongated scorzonera, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a robust, tuberous root system that can grow up to 30 cm in length, which is often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces basal leaves that are broad and heart-shaped, with a rough texture. Its flowers are yellow and daisy-like, typically appearing in late spring to early summer.

Scorzonera elongata thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.
