Senecio kuntzei

Senecio kuntzei, commonly known as the Kuntze's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. Its foliage is often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

The flowers are small and yellow, often arranged in a cluster at the top of the stems.
