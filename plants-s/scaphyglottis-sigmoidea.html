Scaphyglottis sigmoidea

Scaphyglottis sigmoidea, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, zigzag-shaped inflorescence. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of white and green, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is typically found in rainforests.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a valuable species in botanical studies.
