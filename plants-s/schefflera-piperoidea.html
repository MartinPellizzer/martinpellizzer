Schefflera piperoidea

Schefflera piperoidea, commonly known as the pepperleaf schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are typically palmately arranged with 5 to 7 leaflets. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by bright red, fleshy berries. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine and as a decorative plant in horticulture.

Schefflera piperoidea thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
