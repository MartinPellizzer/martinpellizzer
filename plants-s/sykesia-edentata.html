Sykesia edentata

Sykesia edentata, commonly known as the African snowball, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows up to 2 meters in height, featuring dense clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, round, green fruits that mature to a reddish color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Sykesia edentata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive blooms.
