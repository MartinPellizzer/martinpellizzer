Sulcorebutia losenickyana var. albispina

Sulcorebutia losenickyana var. albispina, commonly known as the white-spined crassula, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded rosettes of fleshy, pale green to blue-gray leaves with white spines along the edges. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped white flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, requiring minimal water to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
