Sida vallsii

Sida vallsii, commonly known as sand mat, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a low-growing annual or perennial herb with hairy, branched stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Sida vallsii thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to harsh, dry conditions.
