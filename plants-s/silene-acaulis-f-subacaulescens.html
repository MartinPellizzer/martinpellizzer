Silene acaulis f. subacaulescens

Silene acaulis f. subacaulescens, commonly known as the cushion stonecress, is a low-growing, mat-forming plant characterized by its dense, compact growth habit.

It features small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has a rosette of leaves that are typically fleshy and covered in a fine layer of white hairs. It is native to rocky or alpine environments and thrives in well-drained soils.

Its botanical structure is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions, making it a resilient species in its natural habitat.
