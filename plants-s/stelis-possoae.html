Stelis possoae

Stelis possoae, commonly known as the orchid of the genus Stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowering structures. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pink and purple.

Stelis possoae is characterized by its slender, fibrous roots and its ability to thrive in well-drained, nutrient-rich soils.
