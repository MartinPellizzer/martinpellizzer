Sanchezia conferta

Sanchezia conferta, commonly known as the velvetleaf stegolepis, is a tropical plant belonging to the family Lauraceae.

It is characterized by its broad, leathery leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, velvety hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by dark, spherical berries. Native to Central and South America, it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Its unique foliage and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and as a houseplant.
