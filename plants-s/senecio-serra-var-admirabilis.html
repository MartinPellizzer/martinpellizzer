Senecio serra var. admirabilis

Senecio serra var. admirabilis, commonly known as the mountain senecio, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of South Africa.

It features slender, upright stems with narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping.
