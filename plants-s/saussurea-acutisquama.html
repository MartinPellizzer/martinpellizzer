Saussurea acutisquama

Saussurea acutisquama, commonly known as the narrow-leaved yarrow, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Asia.

The plant typically grows in alpine and arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Saussurea acutisquama is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
