Spilanthes calva

Spilanthes calva, commonly known as the narrowleaf toothache plant, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in late summer.
