Stellaria nemorum var. montana

Stellaria nemorum var. montana, commonly known as mountain starwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It typically thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and rocky slopes. The plant features small, white or pale blue star-shaped flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

Stellaria nemorum var. montana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting insect life.
