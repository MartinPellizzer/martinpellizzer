Sacciolepis campestris

Sacciolepis campestris, commonly known as the broadleaf sacciolepis, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky soils. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. It is often found in open, sunny habitats such as fields and coastal areas.

Sacciolepis campestris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
