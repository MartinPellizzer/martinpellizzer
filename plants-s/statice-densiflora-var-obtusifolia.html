Statice densiflora var. obtusifolia

Statice densiflora var. obtusifolia, commonly known as sea lavender or salt grass, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and small, showy blue or purple flowers. The plant thrives in saline and sandy soils, making it well adapted to coastal environments. Its fibrous root system helps stabilize dunes and prevent erosion.

It is often used in coastal landscaping due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.
