Swertia schimperi

Swertia schimperi, commonly known as Ethiopian gentian, is a flowering plant native to the highlands of Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and branched.

Swertia schimperi is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional remedies for various ailments.
