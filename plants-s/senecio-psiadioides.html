Senecio psiadioides

Senecio psiadioides, commonly known as the Cape Town groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It has a compact growth habit and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.
