Stelis rimulata

Stelis rimulata, commonly known as the pink-stem orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, pale pink to white flowers with a distinct three-lobed lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Stelis rimulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
