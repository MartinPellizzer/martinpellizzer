Stelis umbonis

Stelis umbonis, commonly known as the Umbonata Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and elegant, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically features upright stems with lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. Stelis umbonis thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in tropical rainforests.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and role in orchid diversity studies.
