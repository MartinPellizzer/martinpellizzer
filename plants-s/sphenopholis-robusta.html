Sphenopholis robusta

Sphenopholis robusta, commonly known as the robust sphenophyll, is a member of the family Sphenophyllaceae.

It is a woody shrub characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, typically blooming in spring. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that release seeds through dehiscence.

Sphenopholis robusta is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments.
