Sambucus sieboldiana var. stenophylla

Sambucus sieboldiana var. stenophylla, commonly known as the Japanese elderberry, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Japan.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers. The species is also valued in traditional medicine and horticulture for its adaptability and aesthetic appeal.
