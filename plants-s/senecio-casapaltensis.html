Senecio casapaltensis

Senecio casapaltensis, commonly known as the Casapaltes senecio, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Argentina and Chile.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Senecio casapaltensis is also valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens due to its delicate appearance.
