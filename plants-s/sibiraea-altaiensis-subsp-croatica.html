Sibiraea altaiensis subsp. croatica

Sibiraea altaiensis subsp.

croatica, commonly known as the Altai Siberian tea, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It is characterized by its dense, aromatic foliage and small, yellowish-green leaves.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments.

Due to its unique chemical composition, it has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
