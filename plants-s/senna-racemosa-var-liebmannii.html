Senna racemosa var. liebmannii

Senna racemosa var. liebmannii, commonly known as the African senna, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow floral clusters. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional medicine.
