Swertia decumbens

Swertia decumbens, commonly known as Himalayan gentian, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes.

Swertia decumbens is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
