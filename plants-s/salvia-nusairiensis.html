Salvia nusairiensis

Salvia nusairiensis, commonly known as the Jordanian sage, is a flowering plant native to Jordan.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it well adapted to dry conditions.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal potential, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
