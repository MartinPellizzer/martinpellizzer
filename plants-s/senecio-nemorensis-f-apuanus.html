Senecio nemorensis f. apuanus

Senecio nemorensis f. apuanus, commonly known as the Apuan Alps groundsel, is a rare and striking plant native to the Apuan Alps in Tuscany, Italy.

The plant typically grows in rocky, calcareous soils and is adapted to the harsh, alpine conditions of its habitat.
