Styrax latifolius f. longiflorus

Styrax latifolius f. longiflorus, commonly known as the large-flowered styrax, is a cultivar of the styrax plant native to China.

It is characterized by its broad, glossy leaves and clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 feet. Its flowers emit a sweet, spicy fragrance, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

The wood of this plant is also valued for its aromatic properties and use in traditional medicine.
