Seymeria cualana

Seymeria cualana, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow and orange flowers that resemble flames. The plant has long, slender stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle. Seymeria cualana thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and botanical collections.
