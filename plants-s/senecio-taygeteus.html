Senecio taygeteus

Senecio taygeteus, commonly known as the Greek Skyrocket, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Greece.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in full sun. It has slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

Senecio taygeteus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
