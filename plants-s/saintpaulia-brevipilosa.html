Saintpaulia brevipilosa

Saintpaulia brevipilosa, commonly known as the short-stemmed African violet, is a compact, low-growing herbaceous plant native to the tropical rainforests of East Africa.

It features velvety, dark green leaves that are heart-shaped and often covered in a fine, waxy coating. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, which bloom in clusters at the ends of its stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.

Due to its compact size and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and container cultivation.
