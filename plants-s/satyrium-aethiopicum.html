Satyrium aethiopicum

Satyrium aethiopicum, commonly known as the African orchid or the Ethiopian orchid, is a species of orchid native to Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Satyrium aethiopicum thrives in humid, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.
