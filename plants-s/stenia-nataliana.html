Stenia nataliana

Stenia nataliana, commonly known as Natal stenia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are small and yellow, often appearing in clusters, and bloom during the warmer months.

Stenia nataliana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
