Schoenia

Schoenia, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Fabaceae.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its broad, silvery-green leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. Schoenia species are often used in traditional medicine for their various health benefits.
