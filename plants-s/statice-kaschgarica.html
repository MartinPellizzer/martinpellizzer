Statice kaschgarica

Statice kaschgarica, commonly known as the Karakhan Turk's Cap, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is well adapted to arid environments. It produces small, clustered flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Statice kaschgarica is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
