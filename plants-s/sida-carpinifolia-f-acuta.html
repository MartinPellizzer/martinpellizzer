Sida carpinifolia f. acuta

Sida carpinifolia f. acuta, commonly known as the sharpleaf sida, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its ovate to lanceolate leaves with serrated margins, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, typically blooming in the summer months. Its fibrous roots and robust growth make it a hardy species adapted to various environmental conditions.
