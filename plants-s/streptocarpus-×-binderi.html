Streptocarpus × binderi

Streptocarpus × binderi, commonly known as the hybrid African violet, is a hybrid plant resulting from crosses between Streptocarpus species.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped foliage and showy, tubular flowers that typically bloom in shades of violet, pink, and white. The plant is native to South Africa and is well adapted to warm, humid environments. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Streptocarpus × binderi is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and ease of care.
