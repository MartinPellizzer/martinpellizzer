Senecio brassiciformis

Senecio brassiciformis, commonly known as the cabbage tree or false cabbage tree, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa. The plant features a woody stem with a rosette of thick, fleshy, blue-green leaves. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in dense, daisy-like clusters.

Senecio brassiciformis is adapted to drought conditions and is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements.
