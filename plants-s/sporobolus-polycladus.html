Sporobolus polycladus

Sporobolus polycladus, commonly known as sand dropseed, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-green foliage and produces small, fluffy seed heads that are often seen in the wind. This plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is drought-tolerant, making it well-suited for arid environments. Its root system helps stabilize soil, preventing erosion in sandy areas.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
