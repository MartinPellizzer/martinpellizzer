Syagrus amicorum

Syagrus amicorum, commonly known as the friendly palm, is a species of palm tree native to the coastal regions of Brazil.

It is characterized by its slender trunk and a crown of feather-like leaves that give it a graceful appearance. The plant thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil. It produces small, yellowish flowers that eventually develop into clustered, edible fruits.

Syagrus amicorum is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
