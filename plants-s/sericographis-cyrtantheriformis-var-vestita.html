Sericographis cyrtantheriformis var. vestita

Sericographis cyrtantheriformis var. vestita, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage. The plant produces small, intricate flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variant is notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
