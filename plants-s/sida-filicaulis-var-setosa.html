Sida filicaulis var. setosa

Sida filicaulis var. setosa, commonly known as the hairy broadleaf sida, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the southeastern United States.

Its root system is fibrous, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soils, often found in wetlands or along stream banks.
