Senecio julietii

Senecio julietii, commonly known as Juliet's Senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It has slender, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves.

Senecio julietii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its delicate appearance and ease of care.
