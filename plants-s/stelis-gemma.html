Stelis gemma

Stelis gemma, commonly known as the gemma orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or branches, making it an epiphytic species. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Stelis gemma is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
