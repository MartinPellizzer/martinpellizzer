Syngonanthus venustus

Syngonanthus venustus, commonly known as the beautiful grass.

It is a perennial herb native to tropical regions of South America. The plant features slender, erect stems that bear small, clustered inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a slight sheen.

Syngonanthus venustus is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
