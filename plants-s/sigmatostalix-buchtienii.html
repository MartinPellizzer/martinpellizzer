Sigmatostalix buchtienii

Sigmatostalix buchtienii, commonly known as the Buchtienii cypress, is a rare and ancient conifer native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its distinctive, narrow, feather-like leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern along the branches. The plant produces small, scale-like cones that contain winged seeds, typical of its coniferous classification. Its slow growth rate and unique morphology make it a subject of interest in botanical research.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species requiring conservation efforts.
