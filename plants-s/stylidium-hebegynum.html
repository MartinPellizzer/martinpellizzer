Stylidium hebegynum

Stylidium hebegynum, commonly known as the pink-flowered sun-orchid, is a small, flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its unique, spurred petals and a distinctive, erect stem. The plant produces showy, pink or red flowers that bloom in spring, attracting various pollinators. It is often found in sandy or rocky soils in arid or semi-arid regions.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in some parts of its range.
