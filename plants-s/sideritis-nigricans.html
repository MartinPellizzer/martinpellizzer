Sideritis nigricans

Sideritis nigricans, commonly known as black horehound, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants known for their medicinal properties. The plant features slender, erect stems and small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, and it produces small, dark seeds.

Sideritis nigricans is often used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and antispasmodic effects.
