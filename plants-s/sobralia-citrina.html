Sobralia citrina

Sobralia citrina, commonly known as the golden sobralia, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features long, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle, aiding in moisture retention. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining potting media.

Sobralia citrina is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
