Sapphoa

Sapphoa, commonly known as the fairy lantern, is a striking member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from orange to red, often resembling small lanterns. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in warm, humid climates. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and glossy, providing a lush backdrop to its colorful blooms.

Sapphoa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
