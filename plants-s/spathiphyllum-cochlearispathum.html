Spathiphyllum cochlearispathum

Spathiphyllum cochlearispathum, commonly known as the peace lily, is a tropical evergreen plant native to the Philippines.

It features broad, dark green, heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces elegant, white spatulate flowers that are enclosed within a green, leaf-like bract. It is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its capacity to purify indoor air.

Spathiphyllum cochlearispathum is a popular ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.
