Sedum elegans

Sedum elegans, commonly known as the elegant stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of star-shaped, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The leaves are fleshy and often tinged with red or purple, especially in cooler temperatures. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

This hardy perennial is known for its ability to withstand drought and poor soil conditions.
