Semilta althaeifolia

Semilta althaeifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features delicate, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its aromatic foliage and long blooming period.
