Sonchus × toletanus

Sonchus × toletanus, commonly known as the common sowthistle, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its rosette of spoon-shaped, hairy leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in composite heads. The plant is typically found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of climates. It is an annual or biennial herb that spreads through seeds dispersed by wind.

Due to its rapid growth and invasive tendencies, it is often considered a weed in agricultural and garden settings.
