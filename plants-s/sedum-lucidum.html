Sedum lucidum

Sedum lucidum, commonly known as the shining stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is adapted to rocky and arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Sedum lucidum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
