Senecio karstenii

Senecio karstenii, commonly known as the Karsten's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in dense clusters.

Senecio karstenii is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
