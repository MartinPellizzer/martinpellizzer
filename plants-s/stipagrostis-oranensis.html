Stipagrostis oranensis

Stipagrostis oranensis, commonly known as the Oran grass, is a species of grass native to North Africa.

It is a perennial plant characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, showing adaptability to harsh climatic conditions. It has fibrous roots that help in stabilizing soil and preventing erosion.

Its botanical features make it an important component of local ecosystems and traditional land management practices.
