Stellaria radians var. ovato-oblonga

Stellaria radians var. ovato-oblonga, commonly known as the ovate-oblong starwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically features small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, and its leaves are ovate to oblong in shape with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors or stream sides. Its stems are often prostrate or ascending, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for ornamental purposes.
