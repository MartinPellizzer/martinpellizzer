Sesbania kapangensis

Sesbania kapangensis, commonly known as the Kapangensis sesbania, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in well-drained soils. It has a short life cycle and is known for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it valuable in soil enrichment.
