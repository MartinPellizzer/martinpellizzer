Selaginella versatilis

Selaginella versatilis, commonly known as the variable spikemoss, is a member of the Selaginellaceae family and is characterized by its small, scale-like leaves and spore-bearing structures.

It is a vascular plant that thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant exhibits a unique ability to survive desiccation, making it a resilient species in arid conditions. Its sporophyte stage is distinguished by a short, unbranched stem with whorled leaves.

Selaginella versatilis plays a role in soil stabilization and is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value.
