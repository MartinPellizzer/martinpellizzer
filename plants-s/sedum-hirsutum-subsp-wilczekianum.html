Sedum hirsutum subsp. wilczekianum

Sedum hirsutum subsp.

wilczekianum, commonly known as the woolly stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It features fleshy, upright stems covered in soft, woolly hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It is adapted to grow in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun.

Its drought tolerance makes it a hardy choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
