Scutellaria cordifrons

Scutellaria cordifrons, commonly known as the heart-leaved skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features opposite, heart-shaped leaves and produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers. It thrives in moist, wooded habitats and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Scutellaria cordifrons is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
