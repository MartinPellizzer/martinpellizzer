Sedum urvillei

Sedum urvillei, commonly known as the Urville's stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to China.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to rocky and arid environments, making it drought-tolerant and easy to care for.

Sedum urvillei is often used in rock gardens and as a decorative ground cover due to its hardy nature and aesthetic appeal.
