Solanum actaeabotrys

Solanum actaeabotrys, commonly known as the purple passionflower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, intricate flowers that resemble a cluster of small, star-shaped blooms. The plant belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is known for its vibrant purple and white coloration. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs.
