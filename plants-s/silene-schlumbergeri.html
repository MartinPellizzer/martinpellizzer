Silene schlumbergeri

Silene schlumbergeri, commonly known as the Schlumbergera, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in a variety of colors. The plant belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in well-drained soil and moderate light conditions. Its foliage consists of fleshy, dark green leaves that help store moisture.

Silene schlumbergeri is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its long blooming period and adaptability to indoor environments.
