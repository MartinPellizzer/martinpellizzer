Sarcochilus gracilentus

Sarcochilus gracilentus, commonly known as the slender spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to New Zealand.

It features a distinctive, narrow, and often curved leaf that is typically covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces a single, slender flower spike bearing a single, delicate, white or pale green flower with a prominent lip. It thrives in cool, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a protected species in New Zealand.
