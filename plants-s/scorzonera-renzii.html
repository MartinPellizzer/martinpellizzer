Scorzonera renzii

Scorzonera renzii, commonly known as the Alpine scorzonera, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features a rosette of dark green, hairy leaves that are typically covered in a dense layer of fine pubescence. The plant produces yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in late summer. Its root system is extensive and thick, often giving it a distinctive, tuberous appearance.

Scorzonera renzii is adapted to cold, alpine environments and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils.
