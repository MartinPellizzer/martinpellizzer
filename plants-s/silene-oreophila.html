Silene oreophila

Silene oreophila, commonly known as the mountain silene, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family, characterized by its showy, pink or red flowers that bloom in late summer. Silene oreophila plays an important ecological role in its native environment by providing nectar for pollinators and contributing to the biodiversity of high-altitude ecosystems.
