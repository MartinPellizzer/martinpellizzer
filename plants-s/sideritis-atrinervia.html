Sideritis atrinervia

Sideritis atrinervia, commonly known as dark-veined ironwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, narrow leaves with distinct dark veins. Its flowers are typically pale blue or purple, arranged in whorled clusters.

Sideritis atrinervia is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
