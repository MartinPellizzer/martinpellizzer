Scleranthus × polycarpoides

Scleranthus × polycarpoides, commonly known as the hybrid common thornweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant features erect stems with simple, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, pink or white flowers in clusters. Its flowers are actinomorphic, with five petals and numerous stamens, typical of the Caryophyllaceae family.

S. × polycarpoides is often found in temperate regions and can be invasive in certain environments.
