Stenorrhynchos petense

Stenorrhynchos petense, commonly known as the Natal orchid, is a species of orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically blooms during the summer months, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat. Its flowers are zygomorphic, with a specialized structure that facilitates pollination by specific insect species.

The orchid thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested or grassland areas.
