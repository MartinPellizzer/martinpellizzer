Specklinia unistriata

Specklinia unistriata, commonly known as the single-striped staghorn fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, leathery fronds that are typically green with a single, prominent midrib. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on tree trunks or rocks. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.

Specklinia unistriata requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture to flourish.
