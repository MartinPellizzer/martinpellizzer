Syzygium badescens

Syzygium badescens, commonly known as the golden apple or the red apple, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The fruit is a small, round berry that changes color from green to red or yellow as it matures.

Syzygium badescens is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
