Sideritis ilicifolia var. hirsuta

Sideritis ilicifolia var. hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy-leaved ironweed, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and aromatic foliage. The plant features small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in dense spikes. Its leaves are hairy and have a distinctive silvery-green hue, contributing to its ornamental value.

Sideritis species are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
