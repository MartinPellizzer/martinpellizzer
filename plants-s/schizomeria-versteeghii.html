Schizomeria versteeghii

Schizomeria versteeghii, commonly known as the Cape mountain thistle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or purple flower heads. The plant has narrow, silvery-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Schizomeria versteeghii is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
