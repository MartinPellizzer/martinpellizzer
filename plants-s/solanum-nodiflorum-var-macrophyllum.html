Solanum nodiflorum var. macrophyllum

Solanum nodiflorum var. macrophyllum, commonly known as the large-leaf nightshade, is a tropical plant native to regions of South America.

It is characterized by its broad, glossy leaves and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that bloom at night. The plant produces dark purple berries that are toxic to humans and animals. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in shaded, moist environments.

Due to its toxicity, it is important to handle the plant with care.
