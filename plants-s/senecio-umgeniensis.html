Senecio umgeniensis

Senecio umgeniensis, commonly known as the Umgeni groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats. It features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Senecio umgeniensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
