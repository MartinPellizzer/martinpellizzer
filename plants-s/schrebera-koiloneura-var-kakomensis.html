Schrebera koiloneura var. kakomensis

Schrebera koiloneura var. kakomensis, commonly known as the Kakomen's schrebera, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Democratic Republic of the Congo.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and distinctive, clustered inflorescences that bear small, fragrant white flowers. The leaves are typically elliptical with a leathery texture and a prominent midrib. It produces small, round fruits that are yellow when mature and contain a single seed.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a landscape plant.
