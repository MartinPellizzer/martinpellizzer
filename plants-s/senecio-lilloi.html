Senecio lilloi

Senecio lilloi, commonly known as the Lillo's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky, arid environments and has small, narrow leaves. Its flowers are yellow and bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees.

Senecio lilloi is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact growth and drought tolerance.
