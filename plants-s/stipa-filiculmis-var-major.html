Stipa filiculmis var. major

Stipa filiculmis var. major, commonly known as the giant feather grass, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and feathery, silver-green plumes that create a striking visual effect in gardens. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping. Its slender leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a fibrous texture.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to provide structure in mixed plantings.
