Scaphyglottis falcata

Scaphyglottis falcata, commonly known as the sickle-shaped orchid, is a tropical plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that support its flowering stems. Its flowers are typically greenish-yellow with a distinctive, elongated lip that resembles a sickle, giving it its common name.

Scaphyglottis falcata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm, humid environments.
