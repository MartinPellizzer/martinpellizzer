Sobolewskia caucasica

Sobolewskia caucasica, commonly known as Caucasian Sobolewskia, is a perennial herb native to the Caucasus region.

It typically grows in rocky or forested areas and features slender, upright stems covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pale green to yellowish in color. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly rough texture.

Sobolewskia caucasica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
