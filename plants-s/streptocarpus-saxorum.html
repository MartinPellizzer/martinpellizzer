Streptocarpus saxorum

Streptocarpus saxorum, commonly known as the rock streptocarpus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple and blue. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light. It is a popular choice for rock gardens and containers due to its ornamental value and ease of care.

Streptocarpus saxorum is also valued for its ability to bloom throughout the year under proper conditions.
