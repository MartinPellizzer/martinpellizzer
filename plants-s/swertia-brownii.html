Swertia brownii

Swertia brownii, commonly known as the Himalayan gentian, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, thriving in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Swertia brownii is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
