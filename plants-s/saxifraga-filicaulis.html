Saxifraga filicaulis

Saxifraga filicaulis, commonly known as the fern-leaved saxifrage, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves that are usually arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers in late spring or early summer. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Saxifraga filicaulis is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
