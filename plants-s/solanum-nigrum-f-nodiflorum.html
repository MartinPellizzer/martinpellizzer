Solanum nigrum f. nodiflorum

Solanum nigrum f. nodiflorum, commonly known as black nightshade var. nodiflorum, is a variant of the common black nightshade.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant belonging to the Solanaceae family, characterized by its hairy stems and alternate leaves. The flowers are typically clustered in umbels and are usually white or pale green, with five petals. The fruit is a globose berry that changes from green to black as it matures.

This form is notable for its compact growth habit and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.
