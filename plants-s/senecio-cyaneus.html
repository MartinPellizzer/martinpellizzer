Senecio cyaneus

Senecio cyaneus, commonly known as the blue-eyed daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It features showy blue or violet flowers surrounded by white ray florets, giving it a striking, daisy-like appearance. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its erect, branching stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Senecio cyaneus is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
