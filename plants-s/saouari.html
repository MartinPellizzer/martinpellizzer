Saouari

Saouari, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces bright yellow or orange flowers with a central disk. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

The Saouari is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
