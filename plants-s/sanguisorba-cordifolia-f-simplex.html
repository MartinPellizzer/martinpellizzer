Sanguisorba cordifolia f. simplex

Sanguisorba cordifolia f. simplex, commonly known as the simple-leaved rosebay rhododendron, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged alternately along its stems. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value lies in its attractive foliage and delicate floral display.
