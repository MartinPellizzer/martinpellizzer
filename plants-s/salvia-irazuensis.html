Salvia irazuensis

Salvia irazuensis, commonly known as the Irazu sage, is a flowering plant native to Costa Rica.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender stems, opposite leaves, and showy violet to purple flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
