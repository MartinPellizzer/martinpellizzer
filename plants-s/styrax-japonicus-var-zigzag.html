Styrax japonicus var. zigzag

Styrax japonicus var. zigzag, commonly known as zigzag styrax, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzagging stems and glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces fragrant, white or pale pink flowers in clusters during the spring. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, acidic soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
