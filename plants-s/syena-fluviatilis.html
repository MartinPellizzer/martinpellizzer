Syena fluviatilis

Syena fluviatilis, commonly known as the river senna, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in parts of Africa and Asia. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils along riverbanks and wetlands.

Syena fluviatilis is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
