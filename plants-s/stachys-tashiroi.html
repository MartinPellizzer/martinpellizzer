Stachys tashiroi

Stachys tashiroi, commonly known as Tashiro's hedge-nettle, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink to purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
