Stipa comata var. falcata

Stipa comata var. falcata, commonly known as the falcate feathergrass, is a variant of the tufted feathergrass.

It is a perennial grass native to the temperate regions of the northern hemisphere. The plant features slender, cylindrical stems and produces dense, fluffy panicles of seeds. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green color.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its graceful appearance and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
