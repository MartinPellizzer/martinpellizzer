Silene Colorata Var. Oliveriana

Silene colorata var. oliveriana is a rare and striking flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe. Known for its vibrant pink to purple blooms, this variant stands out among its relatives with its unique coloration and delicate structure. It thrives in rocky and well-drained soils, often found in alpine meadows and open woodlands. The plant's beauty has made it a subject of interest for botanists and gardeners alike. Its conservation status highlights the need for protecting its fragile habitats from environmental threats.
