Solanum hougasii

Solanum hougasii, commonly known as the Hougasii nightshade, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, followed by berries that change color from green to red as they mature. It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, and it thrives in dry, rocky environments.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
