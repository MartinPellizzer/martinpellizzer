Speculantha crebra

Speculantha crebra, commonly known as the dense speculantha, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form with dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale yellow to orange in color, blooming during the winter months. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is often cultivated in rock gardens and as a decorative plant in arid landscapes.
