Suregada nigricaulis

Suregada nigricaulis, commonly known as the black-stemmed orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, dark-colored stems. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
