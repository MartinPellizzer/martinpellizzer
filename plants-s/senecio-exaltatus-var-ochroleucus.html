Senecio exaltatus var. ochroleucus

Senecio exaltatus var. ochroleucus, commonly known as the yellow ragged robin, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are linear and often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its vibrant color and unique foliage.
