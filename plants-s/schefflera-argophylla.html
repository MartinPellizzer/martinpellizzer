Schefflera argophylla

Schefflera argophylla, commonly known as the silver schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy, dark green leaves that are arranged in a distinctive umbrella-like pattern at the ends of its branches. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red, fleshy fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as a ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.

Schefflera argophylla is relatively low-maintenance and adaptable to various indoor growing conditions.
