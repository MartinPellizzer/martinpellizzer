Spathodea mollis

Spathodea mollis, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a tropical tree native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that are typically orange or red in color. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets. It produces fruit that is a woody capsule containing numerous seeds.

The species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
