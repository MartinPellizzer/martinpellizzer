Stenocarpus forsteri var. billardierei

Stenocarpus forsteri var. billardierei, commonly known as the golden wattle, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are clustered in spherical inflorescences. The plant has compound leaves with ovate to lanceolate leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and heathland environments.

The species is notable for its ornamental value and is widely cultivated in gardens.
