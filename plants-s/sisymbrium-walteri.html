Sisymbrium walteri

Sisymbrium walteri, commonly known as Walter's brookweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many mustard plants. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils along streambanks and roadsides. It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring.

Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often have a slightly hairy texture.
