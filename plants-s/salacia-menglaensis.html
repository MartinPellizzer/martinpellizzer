Salacia menglaensis

Salacia menglaensis, commonly known as the Mengla salacia, is a species of flowering plant in the Salaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly in China, Vietnam, and Myanmar. The plant is characterized by its woody stems and opposite leaves with a glossy surface. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, and the plant is often found in lowland forests and along stream banks.
