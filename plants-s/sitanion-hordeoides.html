Sitanion hordeoides

Sitanion hordeoides, commonly known as the barley grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is a perennial grass that typically grows in temperate regions and is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets. It is often found in disturbed soils and is used in traditional medicine and as a nutritional supplement.

Sitanion hordeoides is also valued for its high content of chlorophyll, vitamins, and minerals.
