Streptocarpus mannii

Streptocarpus mannii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped foliage and showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, flat seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.
