Sedum dasyphyllum subsp. brevifolium

Sedum dasyphyllum subsp.

brevifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features fleshy, narrow leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it an attractive choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
