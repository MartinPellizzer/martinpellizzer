Sinosenecio baojingensis

Sinosenecio baojingensis, commonly known as the Baojing bellflower, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous regions, preferring well-drained soils. It features slender stems and small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
