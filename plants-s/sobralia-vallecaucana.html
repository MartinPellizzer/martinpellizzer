Sobralia vallecaucana

Sobralia vallecaucana, commonly known as the Vallecauca sobralia, is a species of orchid native to Colombia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Sobralia vallecaucana thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its beauty in horticultural collections.
