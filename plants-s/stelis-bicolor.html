Stelis bicolor

Stelis bicolor, commonly known as the two-colored orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking white and purple flowers, which bloom in clusters on tall, slender stems. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or branches, exhibiting an epiphytic growth habit. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Stelis bicolor is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
