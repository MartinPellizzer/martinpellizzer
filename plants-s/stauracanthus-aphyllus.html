Stauracanthus aphyllus

Stauracanthus aphyllus, commonly known as the leafless stauracanthus, is a unique plant characterized by its lack of foliage.

It belongs to the family Stauracanthaceae and is native to specific regions in Africa. The plant exhibits a compact, shrub-like growth form with small, often inconspicuous flowers. Its botanical structure is adapted to arid environments, with specialized features for water conservation.
