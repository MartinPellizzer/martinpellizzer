Strobilanthes procumbens

Strobilanthes procumbens, commonly known as the prostrate strobilanthes, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a low-growing herb that typically thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are often pale yellow or greenish in color. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

It is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits and is also studied for its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
