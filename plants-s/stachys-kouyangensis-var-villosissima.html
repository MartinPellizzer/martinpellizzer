Stachys kouyangensis var. villosissima

Stachys kouyangensis var. villosissima, commonly known as the hairy betony, is a perennial herb native to China.

It features upright stems covered in dense, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of purple or pink flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a fuzzy texture.

It is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forests or rocky slopes.
