Stapelia × maculosoides

Stapelia × maculosoides, commonly known as the spotted star cactus, is a hybrid succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow with red or purple markings. The plant features thick, ribbed stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. Its leaves are usually reduced to spines, and it produces showy, long-lasting blooms during the summer months.

Stapelia × maculosoides is popular among cactus enthusiasts for its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements.
