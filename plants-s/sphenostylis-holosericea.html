Sphenostylis holosericea

Sphenostylis holosericea, commonly known as the velvet tamarind, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces leguminous pods that contain edible seeds, which are used in traditional cuisines. Its botanical features include hairy stems and a distinctive, sericeous (silky) covering on its leaves and fruits.

Sphenostylis holosericea is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in its native habitat.
