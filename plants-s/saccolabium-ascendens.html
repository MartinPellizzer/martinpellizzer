Saccolabium ascendens

Saccolabium ascendens, commonly known as the ascending saccolabium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Leguminosae, also known as the legume or bean family. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.

The seeds are enclosed in pods that mature to a brownish color, typical of many leguminous species.
