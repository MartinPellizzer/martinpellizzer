Sedum jurgensenii

Sedum jurgensenii, commonly known as the Jurgensen's stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of North America.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow or orange. The plant is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water requirements. Its leaves are typically blue-green in color and covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

Sedum jurgensenii is often used in xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal.
