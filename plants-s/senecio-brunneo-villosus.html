Senecio brunneo-villosus

Senecio brunneo-villosus, commonly known as the brown hairy senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, gray-green leaves and yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with composite flower heads. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in grassland or shrubland habitats.

Senecio brunneo-villosus is typically perennial and can grow up to 30 centimeters in height.
