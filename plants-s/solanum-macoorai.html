Solanum macoorai

Solanum macoorai, commonly known as the Macoorai nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of northern Queensland, Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, characterized by its dark green, oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale blue, followed by berries that mature to a deep red color.

Solanum macoorai is part of the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades such as tomatoes and potatoes, and it is known for its ornamental value in horticulture.
