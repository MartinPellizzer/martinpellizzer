Stevia viminea

Stevia viminea, commonly known as silver leaf stevia, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Stevia viminea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
