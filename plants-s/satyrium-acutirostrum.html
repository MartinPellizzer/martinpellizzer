Satyrium acutirostrum

Satyrium acutirostrum, commonly known as the acute-beaked orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as rocky slopes and grasslands. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a single, lance-shaped leaf. Its flowers are small and pale greenish-yellow, with a distinctive elongated lip that resembles a beak.

This orchid is known for its unique morphology and is often found in dry, calcareous soils.
