Schuurmansia henningsii

Schuurmansia henningsii, commonly known as the Hennings' cycad, is a unique member of the Cycadaceae family.

It is native to southern Africa, specifically South Africa, where it thrives in coastal regions. This plant is characterized by its stout, unbranched trunk and a crown of pinnate, dark green leaves. Schuurmansia henningsii is known for its slow growth and ability to withstand harsh coastal conditions.
