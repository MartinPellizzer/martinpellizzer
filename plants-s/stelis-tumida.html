Stelis tumida

Stelis tumida, commonly known as the inflated stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, swollen pseudobulbs and long, slender leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Stelis tumida is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care in suitable conditions.
