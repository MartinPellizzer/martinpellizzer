Sciadotenia duckei

Sciadotenia duckei, commonly known as the African violet.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting to its arid environment. Its flowers are typically purple or blue, with five petals arranged in a showy cluster.

Sciadotenia duckei is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
