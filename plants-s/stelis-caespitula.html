Stelis caespitula

Stelis caespitula, commonly known as the clustered stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant forms dense clusters of pseudobulbs, which store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are small and often white or pale in color.

Stelis caespitula thrives in humid environments with indirect light and well-draining substrates.
