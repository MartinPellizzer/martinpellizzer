Spartium microphyllum

Spartium microphyllum, commonly known as lesser sparrow's nest, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and clusters of pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils. Its botanical features include a creeping stem system and small, leathery foliage.

Spartium microphyllum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance.
