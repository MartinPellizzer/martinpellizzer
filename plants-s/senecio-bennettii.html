Senecio bennettii

Senecio bennettii, commonly known as Bennett's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in mountainous regions. The plant features narrow, gray-green leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
