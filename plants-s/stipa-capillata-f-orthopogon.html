Stipa capillata f. orthopogon

Stipa capillata f. orthopogon, commonly known as the upright feather grass, is a variant of the slender wheatgrass.

It is a perennial grass characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and fine, feathery leaves. The plant typically reaches heights of 60 to 120 centimeters and produces fluffy, silver-gray seed heads. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in open grasslands and disturbed sites.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden landscapes and erosion control.
