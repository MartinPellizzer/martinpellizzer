Senna Hirsuta Var. Acuminata

Senna hirsuta var. acuminata is a striking legume native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia. Known for its vibrant yellow flowers and feathery leaves, this plant is a popular choice for ornamental gardening. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish. The variety acuminata is distinguished by its sharply pointed leaves and extended bloom period. Its ecological importance includes supporting pollinators and providing habitat for small wildlife.
