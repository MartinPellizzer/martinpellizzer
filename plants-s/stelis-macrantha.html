Stelis macrantha

Stelis macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its aerial roots and lack of soil. The plant produces showy, often fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Stelis macrantha thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for indoor cultivation and botanical gardens.
