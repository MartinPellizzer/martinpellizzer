Stelis compacta

Stelis compacta, commonly known as the compact orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees or rocks in tropical rainforests. The plant features a compact growth habit with clustered, hairy pseudobulbs and small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale green, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

Stelis compacta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
