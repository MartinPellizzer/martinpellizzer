Sulcorebutia arenacea var. kamiensis

Sulcorebutia arenacea var. kamiensis, commonly known as the Kamien's arenga cactus, is a compact, low-growing plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It features a globular or cylindrical stem with a ribbed structure, often adorned with white or pale green spines. The plant produces small, colorful flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
