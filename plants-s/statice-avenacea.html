Statice avenacea

Statice avenacea, commonly known as sea lavender, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant thrives in salty, coastal environments and is often found in sandy or gravelly soils. Its salt tolerance makes it a popular choice for coastal gardens and landscaping.

Statice avenacea is also valued for its use in floral arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms.
