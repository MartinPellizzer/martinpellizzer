Stellaria kingii

Stellaria kingii, commonly known as the King's starwort, is a small flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in damp, shaded areas such as forest floors and stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Stellaria kingii is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family, which includes other flowering plants with similar floral structures.
