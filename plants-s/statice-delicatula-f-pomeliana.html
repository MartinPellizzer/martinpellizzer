Statice delicatula f. pomeliana

Statice delicatula f. pomeliana, commonly known as the pink statice, is a flowering plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces small, showy flowers in shades of pink and purple. The plant is native to southern Europe and thrives in well-drained soils and full sun conditions. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are often used in ornamental gardening.

This cultivar is valued for its drought tolerance and ability to add color to gardens and floral arrangements.
