Side

Side, commonly known as the common sideplant, is a member of the Araliaceae family.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are typically palmately arranged with several leaflets. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The sideplant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is native to parts of Asia and Africa.
