Sison laciniatum

Sison laciniatum, commonly known as the shredded leaf plant, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, lobed leaves that give it a feathery appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Sison laciniatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
