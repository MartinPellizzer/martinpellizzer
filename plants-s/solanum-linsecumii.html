Solanum linsecumii

Solanum linsecumii, commonly known as the Linsecumii nightshade, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and tobacco. The plant typically features small, tubular flowers that are often white or purple in color. Its leaves are usually ovate and have a slightly hairy texture.

Solanum linsecumii is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
