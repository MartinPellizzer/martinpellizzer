Stylochaeton gabonicum

Stylochaeton gabonicum, commonly known as the Gabon velvet bean, is a climbing legume native to Central and Southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant produces pods that contain edible seeds, which are rich in protein and used in local cuisines.

Stylochaeton gabonicum is also valued for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil fertility in agricultural settings.
