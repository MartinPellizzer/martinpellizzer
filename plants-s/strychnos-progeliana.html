Strychnos progeliana

Strychnos progeliana, commonly known as the golden star tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a star-like appearance. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain seeds enclosed in a fleshy, yellowish pulp. Its leaves are typically alternate and ovate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Strychnos progeliana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
