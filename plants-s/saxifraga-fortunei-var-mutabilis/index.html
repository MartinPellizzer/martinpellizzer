Saxifraga Fortunei Var. Mutabilis

Saxifraga fortunei var. mutabilis is a striking plant known for its vibrant, showy flowers that change color throughout the blooming season. Native to the mountainous regions of China, this species thrives in cool, moist environments with well-drained soil. Its foliage ranges from dark green to silvery white, adding visual interest to any garden or landscape. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care. With its unique color transformations and delicate appearance, it remains a favorite among horticultural enthusiasts.
