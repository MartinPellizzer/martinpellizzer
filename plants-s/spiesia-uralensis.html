Spiesia uralensis

Spiesia uralensis, commonly known as the Ural spurge, is a flowering plant native to the Ural Mountains region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features alternate, ovate to lanceolate leaves with a hairy texture and produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and forest edges.

Spiesia uralensis is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its distinctive foliage and flowers.
