Solenopsis bicolor

Solenopsis bicolor, commonly known as the two-colored ant.

This species is a small, perennial herbaceous plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. It features slender, upright stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that are pale green to white in hue.

Solenopsis bicolor is often found in rocky soils and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.
