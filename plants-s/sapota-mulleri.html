Sapota mulleri

Sapota mulleri, commonly known as the Mexican sapote, is a tropical tree native to Central America and parts of Mexico.

It belongs to the family Sapotaceae and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The tree produces a smooth, yellowish fruit with a creamy, buttery flesh that is rich in nutrients. Its fruit is often used in desserts, beverages, and as a natural sweetener.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental qualities and potential medicinal properties.
