Sisymbrium stenophyllum

Sisymbrium stenophyllum, commonly known as narrowleaf mustard, is a member of the Brassicaceae family.

It is an annual herb that typically grows in dry, open habitats such as roadsides and disturbed soils. The plant has slender, branched stems and small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes. Its leaves are narrow and alternate, often with a slightly hairy texture.

The seeds are small and capsule-like, typical of the mustard family, and are dispersed by wind.
