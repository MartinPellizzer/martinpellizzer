Stanleya fruticosa

Stanleya fruticosa, commonly known as the yellow gentian, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its striking yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in mountainous regions. It has a compact, shrub-like structure with narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

Stanleya fruticosa is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
