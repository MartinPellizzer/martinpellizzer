Sartwellia

Sartwellia, commonly known as the yellow bellflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Campanulaceae.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sun. Sartwellia has lance-shaped leaves that are usually arranged oppositely along the stem.
