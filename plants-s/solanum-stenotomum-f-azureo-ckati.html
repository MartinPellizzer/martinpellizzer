Solanum stenotomum f. azureo-ckati

Solanum stenotomum f. azureo-ckati, commonly known as the Azure-leafed Nightshade, is a unique botanical variant characterized by its striking blue-green foliage.

This plant belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades such as tomatoes and potatoes. It is native to tropical regions of South America and thrives in humid, shaded environments. The leaves are ovate with a velvety texture, and the plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into berries.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
