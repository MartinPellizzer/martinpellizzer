Schlumbergeria brasiliensis

Schlumbergeria brasiliensis, commonly known as the Brazilian Christmas cactus, is a species native to Brazil.

It is a type of epiphytic cactus belonging to the Cactaceae family, characterized by its segmented, fleshy stems. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, pink, and white during the holiday season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Schlumbergeria brasiliensis is popular as a decorative plant due to its long blooming period and ease of care.
