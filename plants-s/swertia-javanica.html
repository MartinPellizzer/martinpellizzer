Swertia javanica

Swertia javanica, commonly known as the Java swertia, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its slender, branched stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Swertia javanica is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
