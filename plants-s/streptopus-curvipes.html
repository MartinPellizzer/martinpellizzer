Streptopus curvipes

Streptopus curvipes, commonly known as the zigzag lily, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzag-shaped leaves and striking blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies.

Streptopus curvipes is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local pollinators.
