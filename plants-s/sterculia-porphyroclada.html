Sterculia porphyroclada

Sterculia porphyroclada, commonly known as the red-leaf sterculia, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, palmate leaves that are often reddish in color, especially when young. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by woody capsules containing several seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
