Stemonacanthus coccineus

Stemonacanthus coccineus, commonly known as the red-stemmed orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking red stems and showy, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem.

The flowers are borne in clusters and can vary in color from pink to red, depending on the species.
