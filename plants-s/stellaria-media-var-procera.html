Stellaria media var. procera

Stellaria media var. procera, commonly known as hairy mouse-ear, is a variant of the common mouse-ear plant.

It is a low-growing annual herb that typically reaches heights of up to 20 centimeters. The plant features small, pale green, oval-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. Its flowers are small, white, and star-shaped, typically appearing in clusters.

It is often found in disturbed soils and is common in lawns and along paths.
