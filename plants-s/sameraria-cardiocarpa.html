Sameraria cardiocarpa

It is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped fruits, which are typically red or orange when mature. The plant is native to tropical regions of Africa and thrives in warm, humid climates. Its leaves are large, palmately lobed, and often have a velvety texture.

The flowers are usually pale yellow or green, with five petals, and are followed by the characteristic heart-shaped capsules.
