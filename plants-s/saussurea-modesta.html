Saussurea modesta

Saussurea modesta, commonly known as the Chinese yarrow, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in high-altitude grasslands and is adapted to cold, arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in fine hairs.

Saussurea modesta is valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
