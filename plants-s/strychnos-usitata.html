Strychnos usitata

Strychnos usitata, commonly known as the African strychnos, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Loganiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that contain seeds enclosed in a hard capsule. It is often found in rocky habitats and is adapted to arid or semi-arid conditions.
