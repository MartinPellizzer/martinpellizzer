Spiranthes africana

Spiranthes africana, commonly known as the African spiraling orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of flowers along the stem. The plant typically grows in grassland habitats and thrives in well-drained soils. Its inflorescence consists of numerous small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.

Spiranthes africana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
