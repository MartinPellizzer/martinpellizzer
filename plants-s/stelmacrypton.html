Stelmacrypton

Stelmacrypton, commonly known as the moonlight orchid, is a rare and exotic plant native to the misty highlands of South America.

It features striking, bioluminescent petals that glow faintly under moonlight, giving it its common name. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with dark green, waxy leaves that are slightly serrated. Its flowers bloom in late summer and are known for their unique, star-shaped structure.

Stelmacrypton requires high humidity and indirect light to thrive, making it a challenging but rewarding plant for experienced growers.
