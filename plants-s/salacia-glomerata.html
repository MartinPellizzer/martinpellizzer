Salacia glomerata

Salacia glomerata, commonly known as the clustered salacia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Salicaceae, which includes willows and poplars. The plant is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and heart-shaped leaves. It typically grows in moist, shady environments such as forest understories.

Salacia glomerata is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine.
