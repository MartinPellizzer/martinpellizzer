Spiraeopsis glabrescens

Spiraeopsis glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth spiraea, is a flowering plant native to eastern North America.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant features compound leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets that are smooth and glossy. Its showy inflorescences consist of clusters of small, white to pink flowers that bloom in early spring.

Spiraeopsis glabrescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and restoration projects.
