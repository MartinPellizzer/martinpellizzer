Sedum hangzhouense

Sedum hangzhouense, commonly known as the Hangzhou stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to China.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact size and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for landscaping in arid environments.
