Selago geniculata

Selago geniculata, commonly known as the zigzag grass or zigzag plant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its distinctive zigzag growth pattern. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny white or pale blue flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a bluish-gray hue due to a waxy coating.

Selago geniculata is often used in ornamental gardening for its unique appearance and adaptability to various soil conditions.
