Scutellaria texana

Scutellaria texana, commonly known as Texas skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is found in regions such as Texas and Oklahoma. It produces small, tubular flowers that are usually blue or violet in color.

The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.
