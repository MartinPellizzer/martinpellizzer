Senegalia montis-usti

Senegalia montis-usti, commonly known as the Montis-usti acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to the arid regions of North America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant is adapted to harsh environments, featuring deep root systems and waxy leaves to reduce water loss. It plays a vital role in stabilizing soils and providing habitat for desert wildlife.

Senegalia montis-usti is also valued for its hardy nature and use in traditional medicine.
