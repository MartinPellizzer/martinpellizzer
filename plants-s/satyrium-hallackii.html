Satyrium hallackii

Satyrium hallackii, commonly known as the Hallack's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its delicate, spurred flowers that typically range in color from white to pale pink. The plant has a slender, erect stem that supports a single or few flowers at the top. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Satyrium hallackii thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.
