Sansevieria javanica

Sansevieria javanica, commonly known as the snake plant, is a hardy, evergreen perennial plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with prominent yellow or white variegation. The plant is characterized by its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it an ideal choice for indoor environments. Sansevieria javanica is also noted for its air-purifying qualities, as it can absorb toxins and release oxygen during the night.

Its resilience and aesthetic appeal have made it a popular houseplant worldwide.
