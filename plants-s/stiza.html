Stiza

Stiza, commonly known as the African violet, is a popular ornamental plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically features velvety, dark green foliage with a waxy texture. Stiza thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its flowers come in a variety of colors, including purple, pink, white, and red, making it a favorite among indoor gardeners.
