Stemodia linophylla

Stemodia linophylla, commonly known as the woolly stemodia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-green foliage and compact growth habit. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
