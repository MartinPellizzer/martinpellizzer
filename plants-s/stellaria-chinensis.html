Stellaria chinensis

Stellaria chinensis, commonly known as Chinese starwort, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, white or pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Stellaria chinensis is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
