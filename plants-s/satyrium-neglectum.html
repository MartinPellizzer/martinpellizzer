Satyrium neglectum

Satyrium neglectum, commonly known as the neglected orchid, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to parts of southern Africa.

It typically grows in grassy areas and along forest margins, thriving in well-drained soil. The plant produces delicate, greenish-white flowers with a distinct lip that is often marked with purple or brown streaks. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often overlooked due to its modest appearance.

Satyrium neglectum is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is appreciated for its subtle beauty and ecological significance.
