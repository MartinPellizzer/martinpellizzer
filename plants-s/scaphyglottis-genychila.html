Scaphyglottis genychila

Scaphyglottis genychila, commonly known as the pale orchid, is a species of orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stem and relatively small, pale green to white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

The orchid has a complex flowering structure, with a single flower per node, and is known for its delicate, fragrant blooms.
