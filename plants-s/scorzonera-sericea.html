Scorzonera sericea

Scorzonera sericea, commonly known as the silky scorzonera, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a robust root system, which is often harvested for its edible tubers. The plant has dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs. Its flowers are yellow with brown markings, typically blooming in late spring to early summer.

Scorzonera sericea thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.
