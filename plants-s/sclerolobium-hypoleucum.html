Sclerolobium hypoleucum

Sclerolobium hypoleucum, commonly known as the white burr acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to Australia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers that appear in clusters. The plant produces woody pods that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showing adaptability to harsh conditions.

Its ecological importance includes providing habitat and food for various native animals.
