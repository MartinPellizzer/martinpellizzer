Salvia ottonis

Salvia ottonis, commonly known as Mexican bush sage, is a perennial herb native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and clusters of violet to purple flowers. The plant features narrow, gray-green leaves with a fuzzy texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Salvia ottonis is also valued for its aromatic foliage and attracts pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
