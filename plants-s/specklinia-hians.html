Specklinia hians

Specklinia hians, commonly known as the silver-veined orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are typically dark green with distinctive silver or white veins, giving it its common name. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
