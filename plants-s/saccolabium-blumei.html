Saccolabium blumei

Saccolabium blumei, commonly known as the yellow bursage.

It is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America, belonging to the Leguminosae family. The plant is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy, woody stems. It produces small, pod-like fruits that contain seeds covered in a fibrous coating.
