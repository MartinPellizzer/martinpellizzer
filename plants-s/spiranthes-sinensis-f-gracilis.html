Spiranthes sinensis f. gracilis

Spiranthes sinensis f. gracilis, commonly known as the slender spire orchid, is a rare and delicate orchid species native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and its unique spiral arrangement of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories or along stream banks. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in specialty gardens.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in the wild.
