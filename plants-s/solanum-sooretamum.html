Solanum sooretamum

Solanum sooretamum, commonly known as the sooretamum nightshade, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and hairy foliage. The plant typically grows as an erect shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate in shape and often have a reddish or purplish hue.

Solanum sooretamum is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and potential medicinal properties.
