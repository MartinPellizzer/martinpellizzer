Senecio exaltatus

Senecio exaltatus, commonly known as the African snowball, is a striking member of the Asteraceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of white to pale blue flowers. The plant features narrow, silver-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is also sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
