Strychnos urceolata

Strychnos urceolata, commonly known as the African snakeroot, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces small, round, woody fruits that contain seeds. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid conditions.
