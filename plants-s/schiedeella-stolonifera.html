Schiedeella stolonifera

Schiedeella stolonifera, commonly known as the stoloniferous schiedeella, is a unique member of the Schiedeellaceae family.

It is a perennial, mat-forming plant characterized by its creeping stolons that root at the nodes, allowing it to spread vegetatively. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments.

Schiedeella stolonifera is native to parts of Australia and is adapted to grow in sandy or rocky soils.
