Senecio erici-rosenii

Senecio erici-rosenii, commonly known as the ericaceous senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant features narrow, gray-green leaves and produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Senecio erici-rosenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
