Solanum chomatophilum f. sausianense

Solanum chomatophilum f. sausianense, commonly known as the Sausian dwarf nightshade, is a compact, herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Iran.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and tobacco. The plant features small, hairy leaves and produces clusters of pale yellow or white flowers. Its fruit is a small, green berry that turns red when mature.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
