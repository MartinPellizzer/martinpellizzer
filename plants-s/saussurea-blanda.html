Saussurea blanda

Saussurea blanda, commonly known as the slender Chinese yarrow, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China and Korea.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and fibrous root system. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky slopes, thriving in cool, dry climates. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are small and yellowish, forming a rounded cluster.

Saussurea blanda is valued in traditional medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
