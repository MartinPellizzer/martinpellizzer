Sphaerostigma orthocarpum

Sphaerostigma orthocarpum, commonly known as the yellow starling tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded crown and glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by woody, spherical fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.

The botanical features of Sphaerostigma orthocarpum make it an important species in both ecological and horticultural contexts.
