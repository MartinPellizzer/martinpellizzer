Sarcostemma areysianum

Sarcostemma areysianum, commonly known as the areysian star vine, is a climbing plant native to the tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its slender, woody stems and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by follicles containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and traditional herbal practices.
