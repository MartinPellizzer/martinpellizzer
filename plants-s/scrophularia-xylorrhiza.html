Scrophularia xylorrhiza

Scrophularia xylorrhiza, commonly known as the red-root scrophularia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in sunny to partially shaded environments. The plant features upright stems that are often reddish in color, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a rough texture. Its flowers are typically pink or purple, arranged in clusters, and bloom in the summer months.

Scrophularia xylorrhiza is part of the Scrophulariaceae family, known for its medicinal and ornamental value.
