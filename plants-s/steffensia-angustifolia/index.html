Steffensia Angustifolia

Steffensia angustifolia is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is known for its slender leaves and small, pale blue flowers. Due to habitat loss and climate change, this species is facing increasing threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are underway to protect and study this unique plant. Its existence highlights the importance of preserving biodiversity in fragile ecosystems.
