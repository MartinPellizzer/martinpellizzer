Sticherus paulistanus var. pseudopubescens

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are often covered in a dense, hairy layer, contributing to its varietal name.
