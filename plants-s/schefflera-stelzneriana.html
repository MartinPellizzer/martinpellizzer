Schefflera stelzneriana

Schefflera stelzneriana, commonly known as the Stelzner's umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like crown of large, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by black, berry-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and indoor spaces.
