Spilanthes barinensis

Spilanthes barinensis, commonly known as the Barinense stinging plant, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the genus Spilanthes within the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers. The plant is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense clusters, and it produces a distinctive, pungent odor. It is known for its ability to cause a tingling or stinging sensation upon contact with the skin, due to the presence of certain alkaloids.

This plant is often used in traditional medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
