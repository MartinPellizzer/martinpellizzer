Sideritis granatensis

Sideritis granatensis, commonly known as the Granada sida, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its small, pink or purple flowers and slender, branched stems. It thrives in rocky and dry environments, often found in mountainous areas.

Sideritis granatensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
