Senecio balbisianus

Senecio balbisianus, commonly known as the Balbisianus Groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a raceme. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
