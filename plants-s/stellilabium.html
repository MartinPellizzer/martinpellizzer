Stellilabium

Stellilabium, commonly known as the star-labium orchid, is a unique flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped labellum, which is a specialized petal that plays a role in pollination. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded, moist environments. Its flowers are usually colorful, with a complex arrangement that attracts specific pollinators.

Botanically, Stellilabium is noted for its intricate floral structure and adaptation to its ecological niche.
