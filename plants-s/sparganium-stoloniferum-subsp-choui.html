Sparganium stoloniferum subsp. choui

Sparganium stoloniferum subsp.

choui, commonly known as the Chinese bur reed, is a wetland plant native to East Asia. It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems and dense, spiky inflorescences that produce small, yellow flowers.

The plant thrives in shallow water or moist soils, often found in marshes and along the edges of ponds. Its subterranean stolons allow it to spread and form dense colonies, which can dominate wetland habitats.

The species is notable for its ecological role in stabilizing aquatic environments and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
