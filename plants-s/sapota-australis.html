Sapota australis

Sapota australis, commonly known as the Australian sapote, is a small evergreen tree native to Australia.

It produces small, round fruits with a smooth, yellowish skin and a sweet, creamy white pulp. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a glossy green surface. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its edible fruit.

The tree is also valued for its ornamental qualities and ability to provide shade in landscaped areas.
