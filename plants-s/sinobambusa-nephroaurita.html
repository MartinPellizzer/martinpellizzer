Sinobambusa nephroaurita

Sinobambusa nephroaurita, commonly known as the kidney-ear bamboo, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its distinctive, elongated leaves that resemble the shape of a kidney. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with pale yellow flowers, which bloom in the summer months. Its growth habit is typically clump-forming, with slender, bamboo-like stems that can reach heights of up to 3 meters.

Sinobambusa nephroaurita is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
