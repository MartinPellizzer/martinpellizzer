Stellaria media subsp. romana

Stellaria media subsp.

romana, commonly known as common chickweed, is a low-growing annual herb native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa. It features small, white star-shaped flowers and has a creeping habit with numerous stolons that root at the nodes.

The leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, while the stem is prostrate and often hairy. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in gardens, lawns, and along roadsides.

This plant is noted for its rapid growth and ability to spread easily through vegetative reproduction.
