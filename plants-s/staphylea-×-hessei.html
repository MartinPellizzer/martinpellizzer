Staphylea × hessei

Staphylea × hessei, commonly known as the hybrid European hazel, is a deciduous shrub native to Europe.

It belongs to the Betulaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and catkin-like flowers. The plant produces edible nuts enclosed in a spiny husk, similar to other hazel species. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges or hedgerows.

Staphylea × hessei is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
