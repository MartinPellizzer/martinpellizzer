Sauvagesia rubiginosa var. luxurians

Sauvagesia rubiginosa var. luxurians, commonly known as the red-leafed sauvagesia, is a variant of the sauvagesia plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking red foliage, which becomes more vivid during the cooler months. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique leaf coloration.
