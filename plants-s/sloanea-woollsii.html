Sloanea woollsii

Sloanea woollsii, commonly known as the Woolly Sloanea, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly found in regions of Brazil and Peru.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in humid, tropical environments. The plant is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage, which gives it its common name. Its flowers are usually small and white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters.

Sloanea woollsii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique appearance.
