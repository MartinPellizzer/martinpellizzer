Stachys paneiana

Stachys paneiana, commonly known as the broadleaf hedge nettle, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or pink, arranged in whorls along the stems.

Stachys paneiana is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its foliage and flowering display.
