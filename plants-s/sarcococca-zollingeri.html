Sarcococca zollingeri

Sarcococca zollingeri, commonly known as the fragrant wintergreen, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to southern China.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that remain throughout the year, providing visual interest in shaded gardens. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in late winter, which are followed by red berries that persist into winter. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it ideal for woodland gardens.

Sarcococca zollingeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging garden conditions.
