Sarcochilus phyllorhizus f. javanicus

Sarcochilus phyllorhizus f. javanicus, commonly known as the Javanese sword fern, is a unique epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a distinctive, sword-shaped leaf that is often used in traditional medicine and horticulture. The plant has a short, thick rhizome that supports its growth on trees or other structures. Its flowers are typically greenish-yellow with a faint fragrance, blooming in clusters.

It requires high humidity and indirect light to thrive, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
