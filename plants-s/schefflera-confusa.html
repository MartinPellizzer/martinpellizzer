Schefflera confusa

Schefflera confusa, commonly known as the broadleaf umbrella plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of large, leathery, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers followed by black, spherical berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its adaptability and aesthetic appeal.
