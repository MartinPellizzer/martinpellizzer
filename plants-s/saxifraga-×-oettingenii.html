Saxifraga × oettingenii

Saxifraga × oettingenii, commonly known as the hybrid saxifrage, is a species native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in high-altitude environments. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pink flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are usually basal, rosette-forming, and covered in a fine layer of hair.

Saxifraga × oettingenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens.
