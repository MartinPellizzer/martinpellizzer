Stenactis beyrichii

Stenactis beyrichii, commonly known as the narrow-leafed daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. Its narrow, linear leaves are arranged alternately along the stems, and it produces small, white to pale yellow flowers.

Stenactis beyrichii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
