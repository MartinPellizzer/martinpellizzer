Senecio darwinii f. falklandica

Senecio darwinii f. falklandica, commonly known as the Falkland Island groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the Falkland Islands.

It is a low-growing perennial that forms dense mats of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in coastal environments and is adapted to the harsh, windy conditions of its habitat.

Its botanical structure reflects its evolutionary response to the unique ecological pressures of the subantarctic region.
