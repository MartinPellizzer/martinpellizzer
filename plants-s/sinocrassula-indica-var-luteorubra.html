Sinocrassula indica var. luteorubra

Sinocrassula indica var. luteorubra, commonly known as the red-leaf sinocrassula, is a succulent plant native to China.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its compact, low-growing structure. The leaves are typically fleshy and arranged in a rosette pattern, often displaying a striking reddish or pinkish hue, especially under sunny conditions. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the spring or early summer.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-draining soil with minimal water.
