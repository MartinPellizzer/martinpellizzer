Sabicea floribunda var. floribunda

Sabicea floribunda var. floribunda, commonly known as the showy sabicea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, fragrant white or pink flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and produces small, woody fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
