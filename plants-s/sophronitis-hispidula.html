Sophronitis hispidula

Sophronitis hispidula, commonly known as the hairy orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Sophronitis hispidula is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
