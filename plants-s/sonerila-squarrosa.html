Sonerila squarrosa

Sonerila squarrosa, commonly known as the squarrosa orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the family Orchidaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including regions of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Sonerila squarrosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
