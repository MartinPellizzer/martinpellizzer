Stachyurus littoralis

Stachyurus littoralis, commonly known as the coastal stachyurus, is a deciduous shrub native to the coastal regions of China.

It features slender, arching branches with small, fragrant flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has dark green, oval-shaped leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy coastal areas.

Stachyurus littoralis is valued for its ornamental value and its ability to tolerate salt spray and coastal conditions.
