Stelis triapiculata

Stelis triapiculata, commonly known as the helmet orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. The plant features a pseudobulb with a single leaf and produces a raceme of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil, and it requires well-drained, nutrient-rich substrates.

Stelis triapiculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
