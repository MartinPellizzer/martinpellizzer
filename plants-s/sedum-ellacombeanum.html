Sedum ellacombeanum

Sedum ellacombeanum, commonly known as the blue sedum, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of the United States.

It features dense clusters of blue-green leaves that are fleshy and adapted for water storage. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its low maintenance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for landscaping in dry climates.
