Stachys germanica var. ehrenbergii

Stachys germanica var. ehrenbergii, commonly known as the German betony, is a perennial herb native to Central and Eastern Europe.

It features erect, branching stems that are often hairy and can grow up to 1 meter in height. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or blue in color, arranged in dense spikes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a rough, serrated margin.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.
