Sedum radiatum

Sedum radiatum, commonly known as the "sunstone" or "star sedum," is a succulent plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features fleshy, spoon-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for both gardeners and horticultural enthusiasts.
