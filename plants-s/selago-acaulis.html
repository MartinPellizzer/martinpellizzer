Selago acaulis

Selago acaulis, commonly known as the rockselaginella, is a low-growing, succulent plant native to arid and rocky environments.

It belongs to the family Selaginellaceae and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features small, scale-like leaves that help reduce water loss in harsh conditions. It is often found in coastal and inland desert regions, thriving in well-drained soils.

Selago acaulis is adapted to withstand extreme temperatures and limited moisture, making it a resilient species in challenging climates.
