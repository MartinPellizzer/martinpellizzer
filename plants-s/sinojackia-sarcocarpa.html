Sinojackia sarcocarpa

Sinojackia sarcocarpa, commonly known as the Chinese sand pear, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy white flowers. The plant produces small, round, yellowish fruits that are edible but not widely cultivated.

It thrives in temperate climates and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
