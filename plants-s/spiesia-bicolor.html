Spiesia bicolor

Spiesia bicolor, commonly known as the two-colored spurge, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive yellow and green bi-colored leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky or sandy soils.

Spiesia bicolor is often used in ornamental gardening due to its unique foliage and drought tolerance.
