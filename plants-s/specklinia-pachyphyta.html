Specklinia pachyphyta

Specklinia pachyphyta, commonly known as the thick-leafed orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting it to its often humid and shaded habitats. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically arranged in a raceme. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
