Sedum australe

Sedum australe, commonly known as the Australian stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Australia.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is often found growing in rocky or sandy soils, preferring full sun.

Sedum australe is a hardy perennial that is popular in xeriscaping due to its low maintenance requirements.
