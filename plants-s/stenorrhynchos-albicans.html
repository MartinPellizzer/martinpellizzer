Stenorrhynchos albicans

Stenorrhynchos albicans, commonly known as the white-flowered stenorrhynchos, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, white flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
