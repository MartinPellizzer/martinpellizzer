Stellaris scilla

Stellaris scilla, commonly known as the star-of-Bethlehem, is a bulbous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of bell-shaped, white or blue flowers. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Stellaris scilla is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
