Stylidium petiolare var. obtusatum

Stylidium petiolare var. obtusatum, commonly known as the blunt-leaf sun-orchid, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its striking, vividly colored flowers that resemble orchids. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are usually broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in arid environments.

The species is notable for its unique floral structure, which includes a spurred labellum that attracts specific pollinators.
