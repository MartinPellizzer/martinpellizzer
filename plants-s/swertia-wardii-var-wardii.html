Swertia wardii var. wardii

Swertia wardii var. wardii, commonly known as the Blue Swertia, is a member of the Gentianaceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender, upright stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or violet in color, which bloom in late summer. Native to the high-altitude regions of East Africa, it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after plant in horticulture, though it is not widely cultivated.
