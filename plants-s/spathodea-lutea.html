Spathodea lutea

Spathodea lutea, commonly known as the yellow flame tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a vibrant display to its environment. The plant has compound leaves with long, slender leaflets that provide a feathery appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Spathodea lutea is also valued for its ability to provide shade and support biodiversity in its ecosystem.
