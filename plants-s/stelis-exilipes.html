Stelis exilipes

Stelis exilipes, commonly known as the slender-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on tree trunks or rocks. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces clusters of fragrant blossoms during the warmer months.

Stelis exilipes is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
