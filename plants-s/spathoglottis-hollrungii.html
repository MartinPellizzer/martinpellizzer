Spathoglottis hollrungii

Spathoglottis hollrungii, commonly known as the Philippine orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant features a rhizome with fleshy roots and produces erect, leafy pseudobulbs. Its flowers are typically yellow or greenish-yellow with a distinctive lip, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

It is often found in lowland forests and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
