Sarothamnus rotundatus

Sarothamnus rotundatus, commonly known as roundleaf thornbush, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, round, gray-green leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rocky or arid environments.

Sarothamnus rotundatus is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental appeal.
