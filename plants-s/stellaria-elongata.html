Stellaria elongata

Stellaria elongata, commonly known as the slender starwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

It typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters and features slender, branching stems covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are elongated, narrow, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Stellaria elongata is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.
