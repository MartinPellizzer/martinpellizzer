Schefflera bodinieri var. membranifolia

Schefflera bodinieri var. membranifolia, commonly known as the paper-leaf schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that are thin and delicate, giving it a unique, almost fern-like appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded outdoor gardens.

Its ornamental foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor and landscape use.
