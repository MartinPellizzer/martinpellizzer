Scabiosa gramuntia var. alpestris

Scabiosa gramuntia var. alpestris, commonly known as the alpine scabious, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its compact, cushion-like growth habit and produces clusters of pink or purple flowers. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green in color. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to cold climates.

Scabiosa gramuntia var. alpestris is often found in meadows and slopes at high altitudes.
