Schanginia hortensis

Schanginia hortensis, commonly known as the garden schanginia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Schanginiaceae and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial with slender stems and lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually a mix of white and pink, with a subtle fragrance that attracts pollinators.

Schanginia hortensis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
