Scutellaria tashiroi

Scutellaria tashiroi, commonly known as Tashiro's skullcap, is a species of flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the genus Scutellaria in the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. It grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and mountainous regions.
