Stipa leptostachya

Stipa leptostachya, commonly known as slender panicle grass, is a perennial grass species native to temperate regions of Asia and Europe.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces dense, spike-like inflorescences that are typically pale green to golden in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and open woodlands.

Stipa leptostachya is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and ecological restoration projects.
