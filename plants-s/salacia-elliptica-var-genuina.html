Salacia elliptica var. genuina

Salacia elliptica var. genuina, commonly known as the narrowleaf salacia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Salicaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like India and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its elliptical, lanceolate leaves that are often glossy and dark green. It produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in catkin-like inflorescences.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
