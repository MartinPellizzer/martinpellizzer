Saccolabium koordersii

Saccolabium koordersii, commonly known as the koord's legume, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features trifoliate leaves with three oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in clusters. Its root system is deep and fibrous, allowing it to thrive in well-drained, sandy soils.

Saccolabium koordersii is often found in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.
