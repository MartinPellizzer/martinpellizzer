Solanum pynaertii

Solanum pynaertii, commonly known as the Pynaert's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the Democratic Republic of the Congo.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a rough texture, while its flowers are usually white or violet with five petals.

The fruit is a yellow or orange berry that contains several seeds.
