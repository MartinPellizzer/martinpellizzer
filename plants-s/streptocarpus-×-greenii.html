Streptocarpus × greenii

Streptocarpus × greenii, commonly known as the hybrid streptocarpus, is a hybrid plant resulting from crosses between different streptocarpus species.

It is native to tropical regions and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant features fleshy, succulent-like leaves that help it retain moisture in its native habitat. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Streptocarpus × greenii is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
