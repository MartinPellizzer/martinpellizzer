Stelis mnemonica

Stelis mnemonica, commonly known as the memory orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, linear leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers, which bloom in shades of white and pale pink, are often arranged in a raceme.

Stelis mnemonica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
