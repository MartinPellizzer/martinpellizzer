Saintpaulia ionantha var. diplotricha

Saintpaulia ionantha var. diplotricha, commonly known as the African violet with double petals, is a popular ornamental plant native to Tanzania.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant features vibrant, velvety flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, with a unique double-petaled structure. Its foliage consists of dark green, velvety leaves that are slightly hairy.

This variety is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its preference for indirect light and consistently moist, well-draining soil.
