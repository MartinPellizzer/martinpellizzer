Stelis fusilifera

Stelis fusilifera, commonly known as the slender-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale green in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Stelis fusilifera is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
