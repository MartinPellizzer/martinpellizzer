Schotia afra var. angustifolia

Schotia afra var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf silver tree, is a South African plant characterized by its silvery-blue foliage and distinctive, often twisted trunk.

It is a member of the Rutaceae family and is native to the Eastern and Western Cape provinces. The leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a waxy coating that gives them their characteristic appearance. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky, coastal areas.
