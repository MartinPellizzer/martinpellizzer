Silene araratica

Silene araratica, commonly known as the Armenian bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus and parts of Central Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces striking violet-blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Silene araratica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
