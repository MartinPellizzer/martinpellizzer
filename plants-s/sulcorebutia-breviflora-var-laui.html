Sulcorebutia breviflora var. laui

Sulcorebutia breviflora var. laui, commonly known as the Laui's short-flowered crassulaceae, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped stems covered in dense, woolly foliage that helps it retain moisture. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of yellow to orange, typically during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
