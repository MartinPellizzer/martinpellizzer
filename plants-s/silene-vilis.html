Silene vilis

Silene vilis, commonly known as the slender catchfly, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family and typically grows in grassy or open habitats. The plant features slender stems and small, pink or white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Silene vilis is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
