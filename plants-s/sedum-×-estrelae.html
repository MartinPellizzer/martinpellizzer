Sedum × estrelae

Sedum × estrelae, commonly known as the Star Sedum, is a hybrid succulent plant that belongs to the Crassulaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact, mound-like growth habit and clusters of star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink or red. The plant features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically blue-green in color, adapted to store water. It is native to hybrid forms developed in cultivation, often resulting from crosses between different sedum species.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens, container gardening, and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
