Strateuma militaris

Strateuma militaris, commonly known as the military strateuma, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Strateuma militaris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
