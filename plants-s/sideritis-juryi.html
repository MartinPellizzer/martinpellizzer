Sideritis juryi

Sideritis juryi, commonly known as the Balkan ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its slender stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that range in color from pale blue to purple. It thrives in rocky and dry habitats, often growing at higher elevations.

Sideritis juryi is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional remedies for its calming and anti-inflammatory effects.
