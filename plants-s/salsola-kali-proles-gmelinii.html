Salsola kali proles gmelinii

Salsola kali proles gmelinii, commonly known as Russian thistle or goatweed, is a flowering plant in the Amaranthaceae family.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its spiny stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa but has been introduced to many other regions, including North America. It thrives in dry, disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural fields and along roadsides.

Its rapid growth and ability to spread through seeds make it an invasive species in many areas.
