Schefflera gardneri

Schefflera gardneri, commonly known as the Hawaiian schefflera, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are divided into several leaflets arranged in a radiating pattern. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in shaded to partially sunny conditions.

Schefflera gardneri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant or in garden settings.
