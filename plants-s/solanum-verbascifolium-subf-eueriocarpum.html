Solanum verbascifolium subf. eueriocarpum

Solanum verbascifolium subf.

eueriocarpum, commonly known as the broadleaf nightshade, is a member of the Solanaceae family. It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, violet-blue flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its fruits are berries that turn from green to red as they mature, containing several seeds.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
