Spiraea nishimurae

Spiraea nishimurae, commonly known as the Nishimura weigela, is a species of flowering shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically blooms in late spring to early summer, adding a delicate ornamental value to gardens. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for a variety of garden settings.

Spiraea nishimurae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its compact size and seasonal floral display.
