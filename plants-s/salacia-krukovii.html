Salacia krukovii

Salacia krukovii, commonly known as the Krukovii salacia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Salaceae and is characterized by its woody stems and showy, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture.

Salacia krukovii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique floral display.
