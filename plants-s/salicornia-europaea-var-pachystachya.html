Salicornia europaea var. pachystachya

Salicornia europaea var. pachystachya, commonly known as sea beans or glasswort, is a halophyte plant adapted to saline environments.

It is characterized by its succulent, cylindrical stems that are often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, greenish flowers in dense clusters, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in coastal areas, salt marshes, and other brackish habitats.

Its ability to accumulate salt in its tissues makes it an important species in ecological and agricultural studies.
