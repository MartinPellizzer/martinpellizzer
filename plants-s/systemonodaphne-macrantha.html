Systemonodaphne macrantha

Systemonodaphne macrantha, commonly known as the large-fruit pandanus, is a member of the Pandanaceae family.

It is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its pungent, aromatic leaves and clusters of yellowish-green flowers. Its fruit, which is a fibrous, spiky structure, contains edible seeds.

Systemonodaphne macrantha is often used in traditional medicine and for its fragrant leaves in culinary applications.
