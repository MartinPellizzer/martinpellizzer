Schoenorchis mishmensis

Schoenorchis mishmensis, commonly known as the Mishmensis orchid, is a rare and endemic orchid species native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure and compact growth habit. The plant typically produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and green, which bloom during the spring season. It thrives in cool, moist environments with partial shade, often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species requiring conservation efforts.
