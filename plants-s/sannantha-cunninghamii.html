Sannantha cunninghamii

Sannantha cunninghamii, commonly known as the mountain everlasting, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in fine hairs.
