Stapelia pulchra

Stapelia pulchra, commonly known as the "beautiful stapelia," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are usually narrow and slightly fleshy, adapted for reducing water loss.

Stapelia pulchra is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and unique appearance.
