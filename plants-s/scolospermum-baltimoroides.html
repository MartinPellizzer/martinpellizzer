Scolospermum baltimoroides

Scolospermum baltimoroides, commonly known as the Baltimore morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous annual with trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are heart-shaped with serrated edges. Its flowers open in the morning and close by midday, hence its common name.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
