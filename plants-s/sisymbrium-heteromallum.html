Sisymbrium heteromallum

Sisymbrium heteromallum, commonly known as yellow rocket, is an annual flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many economically important crops such as cabbage and broccoli. The plant features slender, branching stems and bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are pinnately lobed and often have a slightly fuzzy texture.

Yellow rocket is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in disturbed soils, making it both a weed and a wildflower.
