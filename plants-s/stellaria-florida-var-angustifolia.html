Stellaria florida var. angustifolia

Stellaria florida var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf starwort, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
