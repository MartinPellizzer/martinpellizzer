Strelitzia reginae var. spathulata

Strelitzia reginae var. spathulata, commonly known as the Orange Sword or White Bird of Paradise, is a striking tropical plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, sword-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a reddish sheen. The plant produces striking inflorescences with vibrant orange and white petals that resemble a bird in flight. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
