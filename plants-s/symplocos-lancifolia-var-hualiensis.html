Symplocos lancifolia var. hualiensis

Symplocos lancifolia var. hualiensis, commonly known as the Hualien symplocos, is a shrub native to eastern Asia, particularly found in regions of China.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as mountain forests, where it thrives in partial to full shade.

Its fruit is a small, fleshy berry that contains several seeds, contributing to its role in local ecosystems.
