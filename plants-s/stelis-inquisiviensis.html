Stelis inquisiviensis

Stelis inquisiviensis, commonly known as the Inquisivi orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of Peru.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in rocky or mountainous habitats. The plant features a short, creeping rhizome and produces delicate, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive yellow lip. It is part of the Epidendroideae subfamily and is known for its specialized adaptations to high-altitude environments.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
