Spilanthes rugosa

Spilanthes rugosa, commonly known as the toothache plant, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

Spilanthes rugosa is often cultivated for its potential therapeutic properties, including its use in traditional medicine for treating toothaches and other ailments.
