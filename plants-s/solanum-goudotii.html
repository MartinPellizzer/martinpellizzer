Solanum goudotii

Solanum goudotii, commonly known as the Goudot's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring woody stems and hairy leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by berries that range in color from green to red. Solanum goudotii is part of the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers.

It is characterized by its unique morphology and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.
