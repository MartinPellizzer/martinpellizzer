Schwartzkopffia pumilio var. buettneriana

Schwartzkopffia pumilio var. buettneriana, commonly known as the dwarf blue haworthia, is a compact succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, blue-gray leaves with white markings, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale green flowers on slender stems during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Due to its slow growth and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for succulent collections.
