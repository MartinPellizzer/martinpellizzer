Senecio folidentatus

Senecio folidentatus, commonly known as the denseleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features dense, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It typically grows in rocky or gravelly habitats and is adapted to arid conditions.

Senecio folidentatus produces small yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators.
