Suaeda maritima

Suaeda maritima, commonly known as sea spurry, is a halophytic plant adapted to thrive in saline environments.

It is a shrub or perennial herb found in coastal areas, salt marshes, and estuaries. The plant has succulent leaves that store water and reduce water loss, aiding survival in arid conditions. Its small, yellow flowers bloom in summer, and it produces capsules containing numerous seeds.

Suaeda maritima plays a vital role in stabilizing coastal soils and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
