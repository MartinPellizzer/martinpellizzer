Scelochilus serrilabius

Scelochilus serrilabius, commonly known as the serrated labial orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, greenish-white flowers with distinctive serrated lips. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories or rocky crevices. Its roots are often specialized for absorbing nutrients from the substrate.

The species is notable for its unique floral structure and is found primarily in tropical and subtropical regions.
