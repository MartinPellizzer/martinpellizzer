Senecio kansuensis

Senecio kansuensis, commonly known as the Kansu groundsel, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine regions, preferring well-drained soils and cool climates. It produces yellow ray florets surrounded by tubular disc florets, typical of the genus Senecio.

The species is often found in rocky or grassy habitats at high altitudes.
