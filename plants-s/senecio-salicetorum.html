Senecio salicetorum

Senecio salicetorum, commonly known as the silverleaf senecio, is a perennial flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and daisy-like appearance. The plant features narrow, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive silvery appearance. It produces yellow flowers in clusters, typically blooming in late spring to early summer.

Senecio salicetorum thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
