Saccolabium kajewskii

Saccolabium kajewskii, commonly known as the Kajewskii's saccolabium, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Argentina and Paraguay.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats and has a deep root system that helps it survive in arid conditions.
