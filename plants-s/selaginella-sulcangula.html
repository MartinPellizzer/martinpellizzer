Selaginella sulcangula

Selaginella sulcangula, commonly known as the zigzag spikemoss, is a species of spikemoss belonging to the family Selaginellaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, zigzagging stems and small, scale-like leaves. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments. It possesses a unique ability to survive desiccation, making it a resilient member of its ecosystem.

The plant's reproductive structures, including strobila, are essential for its propagation and ecological role.
