Schkuhria pedata

Schkuhria pedata, commonly known as the Mexican sunflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

Schkuhria pedata thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners in warm climates.
