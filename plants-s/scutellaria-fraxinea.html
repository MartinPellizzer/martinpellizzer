Scutellaria fraxinea

Scutellaria fraxinea, commonly known as fraxinella skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue to purple in color, which bloom in late spring to early summer.

Scutellaria fraxinea is often found in moist, wooded areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
