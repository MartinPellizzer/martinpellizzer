Scrophularia leucoclada

Scrophularia leucoclada, commonly known as white scrophularia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands. The plant features upright stems that are often hairy, with opposite leaves that are lance-shaped and have a rough texture. Its flowers are clustered in racemes and are usually pale pink to white in color, blooming from late spring to early summer.

Scrophularia leucoclada is part of the Scrophulariaceae family, which includes many species with medicinal and ornamental value.
