Senecio rapistroides

Senecio rapistroides, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other composite flowers. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow ray flowers surrounded by a ring of pale green to white disk flowers. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Senecio rapistroides is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant blooms and drought tolerance.
