Senecio aristeguietae

Senecio aristeguietae, commonly known as the Aristeguieta's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Argentina.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with narrow, linear leaves and small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant thrives in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its botanical features include a fibrous root system and a compact growth habit.

It is often found in open grasslands and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
