Senecio sulphureus

Senecio sulphureus, commonly known as the golden groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and fuzzy, hairy leaves. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it an attractive addition to both wild and cultivated landscapes.
