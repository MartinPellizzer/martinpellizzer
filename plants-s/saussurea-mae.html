Saussurea mae

Saussurea mae, commonly known as the Chinese yarrow, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of China and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs, giving it a slightly fuzzy appearance. Saussurea mae is often found in alpine meadows and rocky habitats at high elevations.

Its botanical features make it an important species in traditional Chinese medicine and ecological studies.
