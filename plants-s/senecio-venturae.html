Senecio venturae

Senecio venturae, commonly known as the Ventura groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the western United States, particularly California.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Senecio venturae is considered a rare and endangered species.
