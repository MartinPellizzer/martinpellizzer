Scutellaria lanceolaria

Scutellaria lanceolaria, commonly known as the lanceleaf skullcap, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and has a compact growth habit. Its flowers are usually blue to purple in color and bloom from late spring to early summer.

Scutellaria lanceolaria is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its potential health benefits.
