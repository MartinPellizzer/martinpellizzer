Stevia commixta

Stevia commixta, commonly known as the sweetleaf plant, is a species native to Paraguay and Brazil.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its small, green, oval-shaped leaves. The plant is renowned for its intense sweetness, which comes from compounds called steviol glycosides. Stevia commixta is often cultivated as a natural sweetener and is used in herbal teas and food products.

Its leaves are typically harvested and dried for use in various culinary and medicinal applications.
