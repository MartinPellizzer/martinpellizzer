Sabicea entebbensis

Sabicea entebbensis, commonly known as the Uganda holly or Entebbe holly, is a shrub or small tree native to East Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces small, round pods that contain several seeds, typical of the legume family.

It is often found in moist, forested areas and is valued for its ornamental qualities and potential medicinal uses.
