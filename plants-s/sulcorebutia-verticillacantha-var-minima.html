Sulcorebutia verticillacantha var. minima

Sulcorebutia verticillacantha var. minima, commonly known as the miniature sun rose, is a compact cactus species native to South America.

It features short, cylindrical stems that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. The plant produces small, vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens and container cultivation due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements.

Its succulent leaves store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments.
