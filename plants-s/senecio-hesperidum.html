Senecio hesperidum

Senecio hesperidum, commonly known as the Desert Daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in summer, surrounded by narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for arid environments. Its growth habit is typically low and spreading, forming dense mats or clumps.

Senecio hesperidum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in xeriscaping.
