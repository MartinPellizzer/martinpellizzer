Spathantheum heterandrum

Spathantheum heterandrum, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, zigzagging inflorescence. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often arranged in a spiral pattern. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its intricate floral structure makes it a subject of interest for both horticulturists and botanists.
