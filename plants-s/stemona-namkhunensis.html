Stemona namkhunensis

Stemona namkhunensis, commonly known as the Namkhun stemona, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It possesses a distinctive, somewhat woody stem with a rough, ridged surface. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and white, arranged in clusters, and it produces red berries that are edible.

This species is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
