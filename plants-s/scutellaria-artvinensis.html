Scutellaria artvinensis

Scutellaria artvinensis, commonly known as Artvin skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the high-altitude regions of Turkey.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It is characterized by its distinctive scutellum, a structure that gives the genus its name.

Scutellaria artvinensis is often found in rocky and mountainous habitats, where it thrives in cool, moist environments.
