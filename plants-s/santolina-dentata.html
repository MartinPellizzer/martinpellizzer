Santolina dentata

Santolina dentata, commonly known as toothed santolina, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and compact, rounded form. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Santolina dentata is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its aromatic leaves and ornamental value make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
