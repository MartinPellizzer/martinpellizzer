Souza proregia

Souza proregia, commonly known as the purple coneflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to South America.

It features striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and a strong, somewhat aromatic scent. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, contributing to ecological biodiversity.
