Solanum burchellii subsp. horrescens

Solanum burchellii subsp.

horrescens, commonly known as the African nightshade, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, with woody stems and simple, alternate leaves.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by berries that change from green to red as they mature. It has a hairy texture on its stems and leaves, which helps to reduce water loss in arid environments.

The species is part of the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is known for its adaptability to various soil types.
