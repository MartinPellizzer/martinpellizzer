Solanum lima

Solanum lima, commonly known as the green bean or French bean, is a herbaceous annual plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its compound leaves and flowering clusters. The plant produces elongated, green pods that contain edible seeds surrounded by a fibrous membrane. Solanum lima thrives in temperate climates and is cultivated for its nutritious, crunchy pods.

It requires well-drained soil and full sun for optimal growth and yield.
