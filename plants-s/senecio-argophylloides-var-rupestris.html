Senecio argophylloides var. rupestris

Senecio argophylloides var. rupestris, commonly known as the rock senecio, is a perennial succulent native to South Africa.

It features slender, upright stems covered in dense, blue-gray foliage that is adapted to arid conditions. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
