Sauropus dunlopii

Sauropus dunlopii, commonly known as the African evergreen, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves that are often bright green or reddish in color. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that are followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for coastal and arid regions.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and seasonal color changes.
