Saxifraga diversifolia var. diversifolia

Saxifraga diversifolia var. diversifolia, commonly known as the variable saxifrage, is a perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are typically covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers during the summer months, which are clustered in a terminal inflorescence. It thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine environments.

Saxifraga diversifolia var. diversifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
