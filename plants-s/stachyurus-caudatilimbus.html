Stachyurus caudatilimbus

Stachyurus caudatilimbus, commonly known as the Chinese weeping honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its pendulous clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are dark green on top and pale underneath. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Stachyurus caudatilimbus is often used in ornamental landscaping for its elegant, cascading appearance.
