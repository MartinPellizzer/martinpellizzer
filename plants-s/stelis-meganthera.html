Stelis meganthera

Stelis meganthera, commonly known as the Meganthera orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, utilizing its aerial roots to absorb moisture and nutrients from the environment. Its leaves are usually strap-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water retention.

Stelis meganthera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
