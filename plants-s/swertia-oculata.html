Swertia oculata

Swertia oculata, commonly known as the eye-flower gentian, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky areas at high elevations. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Swertia oculata is known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
