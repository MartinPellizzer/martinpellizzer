Swartzia aptera

Swartzia aptera, commonly known as the smooth swartzia, is a species of flowering plant in the Fabaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub, characterized by its smooth, often grayish bark. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several pairs of oval-shaped leaflets.
