Stelis biserrula

Stelis biserrula, commonly known as the serrated stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments within tropical forests. The plant features a slender, erect stem with several nodes and produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a raceme. Its leaves are linear and often have a slightly serrated edge, giving it its common name.

Stelis biserrula is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
