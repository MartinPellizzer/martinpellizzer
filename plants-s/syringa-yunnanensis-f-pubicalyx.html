Syringa yunnanensis f. pubicalyx

Syringa yunnanensis f. pubicalyx, commonly known as the Yunnan lilac, is a unique cultivar of the Syringa genus native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are elliptical, with a smooth texture and a slightly pubescent underside.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its pleasant fragrance and visual beauty.
