Sinocarum acronemifolium

Sinocarum acronemifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf sinocarum, is a species of flowering plant in the family Polygonaceae.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers. Native to eastern Asia, particularly China, it thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides. The plant's rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
