Stelis aristocratica

Stelis aristocratica, commonly known as the aristocratic orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with narrow, linear leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often arranged in a raceme. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Stelis aristocratica is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
