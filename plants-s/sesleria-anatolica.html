Sesleria anatolica

Sesleria anatolica, commonly known as Anatolian feathergrass, is a perennial grass native to Turkey and the surrounding regions.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and produces delicate, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Sesleria anatolica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
