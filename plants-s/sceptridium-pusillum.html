Sceptridium pusillum

Sceptridium pusillum, commonly known as the dwarf moss fern, is a small, terrestrial fern that thrives in moist, shaded environments.

It belongs to the family Hymenophyllaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant has a unique reproductive system, producing spores in specialized structures called sporangia. Its leaves are typically narrow and closely arranged, forming a dense, carpet-like growth.
