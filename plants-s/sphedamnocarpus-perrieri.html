Sphedamnocarpus perrieri

Sphedamnocarpus perrieri, commonly known as the Perrier's cycad, is a rare and ancient plant species native to Madagascar.

The plant produces cones rather than flowers, with male and female cones occurring on separate plants. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered critically endangered, highlighting the need for conservation efforts.
