Sideritis brachystachys

Sideritis brachystachys, commonly known as the short-stalked ironwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of violet or blue. Its leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy surface.

Sideritis brachystachys is often found in rocky or dry habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.
