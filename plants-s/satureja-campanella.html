Satureja campanella

Satureja campanella, commonly known as sweet basil or campanula sage, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant leaves with a strong, aromatic scent reminiscent of basil and sage. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for Mediterranean gardens.

Satureja campanella is often used in culinary and medicinal applications due to its distinctive flavor and potential health benefits.
