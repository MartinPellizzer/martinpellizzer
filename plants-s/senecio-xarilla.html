Senecio xarilla

Senecio xarilla, commonly known as the orange daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, daisy-like blooms.

Senecio xarilla is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant colors and drought tolerance.
