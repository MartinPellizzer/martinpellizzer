Swertia punicea var. punicea

Swertia punicea var. punicea, commonly known as the scarlet gentian, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its striking red to purple flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine, silky hair.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is valued for its ornamental beauty and traditional medicinal uses.
