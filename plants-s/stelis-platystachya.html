Stelis platystachya

Stelis platystachya, commonly known as the broad-stalked orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest undergrowth. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with a rhizome that produces multiple shoots. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellow, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and delicate blooms.
