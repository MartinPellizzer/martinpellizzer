Senecio neowebsteri

Senecio neowebsteri, commonly known as the Webber's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun exposure. Its flowers are small and yellow, often arranged in dense clusters.

Senecio neowebsteri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
