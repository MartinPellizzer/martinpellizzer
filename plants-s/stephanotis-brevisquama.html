Stephanotis brevisquama

Stephanotis brevisquama, commonly known as the short-leaf stephanotis, is a climbing shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth surface and a short, velvety texture. Its flowers are typically clustered in terminal panicles and bloom during the warmer months.

Stephanotis brevisquama is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
