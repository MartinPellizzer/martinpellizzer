Satureja × preauxii

Satureja × preauxii, commonly known as hybrid savory, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Satureja hirsuta and Satureja montana.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with aromatic, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, pink or purple flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its essential oils contain compounds like thymol and carvacrol, which contribute to its distinctive aroma and potential medicinal properties.
