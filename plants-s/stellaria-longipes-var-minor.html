Stellaria longipes var. minor

Stellaria longipes var. minor, commonly known as the slender starwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically features slender, ascending stems covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Stellaria longipes var. minor is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors or stream banks.
