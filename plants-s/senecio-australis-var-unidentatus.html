Senecio australis var. unidentatus

Senecio australis var. unidentatus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in grassland habitats and is often found in open, sunny areas. It has a creeping growth habit and produces achenes that are dispersed by wind.

This variety is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
