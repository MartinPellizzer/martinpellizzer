Solanum gouakae var. latifolium

Solanum gouakae var. latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants such as tomatoes and potatoes. The plant is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into berries. It is often found in moist, wooded areas and is adapted to thrive in humid climates.

The species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and potential ecological significance.
