Schoenoplectus × juncohotarui

Schoenoplectus × juncohotarui, commonly known as the hybrid bulrush, is a wetland plant that belongs to the Cyperaceae family.

It is a sterile hybrid resulting from the cross between Schoenoplectus junceus and Schoenoplectus hotarui, two species native to Japan. The plant typically forms dense stands in shallow freshwater habitats, such as marshes and pond margins. Its culms are cylindrical and often have a bluish-green color, while the inflorescence consists of spikelets arranged in a panicle.

This hybrid is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for aquatic life.
