Spathiphyllum buntingianum

Spathiphyllum buntingianum, commonly known as the Bunting's peace lily, is a species of flowering plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

The plant produces elegant white spathes that enclose small, greenish-yellow flowers, which are typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.
