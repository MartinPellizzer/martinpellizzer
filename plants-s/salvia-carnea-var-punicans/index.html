Salvia Carnea Var. Punicans

Salvia carnea var. punicans is a strikingly beautiful plant known for its vibrant red flowers and lush green foliage. Native to the southern United States, it thrives in warm, sunny climates and well-drained soil. This variety of salvia is prized for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. Its compact growth habit makes it ideal for rock gardens, borders, or container planting. With its long blooming season and low maintenance requirements, it's a favorite among gardeners and landscapers alike.
