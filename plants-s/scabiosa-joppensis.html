Scabiosa joppensis

Scabiosa joppensis, commonly known as the Joppa scabious, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, pink or white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, contributing to its drought-resistant nature.

Scabiosa joppensis is often found in rocky habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
