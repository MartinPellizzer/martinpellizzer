Scutellaria wenshanensis

Scutellaria wenshanensis, commonly known as Wenshan skullcap, is a perennial herb native to China, specifically the Wenshan region of Yunnan Province.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, two-lipped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky slopes.

Scutellaria wenshanensis is valued for its traditional medicinal uses, particularly in Chinese herbal medicine.
