Stachys fontqueri

Stachys fontqueri, commonly known as the Sierra grassvetch, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats such as slopes and open woodlands. The plant features slender, erect stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are clustered in spikes and are usually pink to purple in color, blooming in late spring to early summer.
