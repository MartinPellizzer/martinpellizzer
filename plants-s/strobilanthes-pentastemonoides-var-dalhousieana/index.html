Strobilanthes Pentastemonoides Var. Dalhousieana

Strobilanthes pentastemonoides var. dalhousieana is a rare and striking plant native to the wetlands of southern India. It is known for its vibrant purple flowers that bloom in clusters during the monsoon season. This species belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is often found in swampy areas along riverbanks. Its unique floral structure makes it a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it remains poorly studied and is threatened by habitat loss.
