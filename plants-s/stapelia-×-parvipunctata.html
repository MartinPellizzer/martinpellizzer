Stapelia × parvipunctata

Stapelia × parvipunctata, commonly known as the miniature star-shaped stapelia, is a hybrid succulent native to South Africa.

It features small, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange with a distinctive, waxy texture. The plant has thick, fleshy stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in a fine, white powdery coating.

This species is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
