Sabicea composita

Sabicea composita, commonly known as the composite sabicea, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, and it produces small, flat pods that contain several seeds.

Sabicea composita is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
