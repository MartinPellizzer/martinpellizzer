Sabicea calophylla

Sabicea calophylla, commonly known as the glossy leaf sabicea, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage. The plant features trifoliate leaves with glossy, dark green surfaces and produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink. Its fruit is a legume pod that splits open to release seeds, aiding in its propagation.

Sabicea calophylla is often found in tropical and subtropical forests, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments.
