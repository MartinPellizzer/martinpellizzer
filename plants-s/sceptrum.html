Sceptrum

Sceptrum, commonly known as the crown vetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant is native to Europe, western Asia, and North Africa, and it has been introduced to other regions for erosion control. Sceptrum thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often forming dense colonies.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it beneficial for soil enrichment.
