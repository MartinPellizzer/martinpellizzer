Stapelia mcloughlinii

Stapelia mcloughlinii, commonly known as the Cape Town stapelia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed stems that store water. The plant produces striking, lantern-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. These flowers bloom in late winter to early spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and flies.

Stapelia mcloughlinii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
