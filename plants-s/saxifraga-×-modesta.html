Saxifraga × modesta

Saxifraga × modesta, commonly known as the hybrid saxifrage, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It typically forms dense mats of foliage with ovate, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.

Saxifraga × modesta is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in garden settings.
