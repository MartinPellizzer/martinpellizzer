Stachys madagascariensis var. goudotii

Stachys madagascariensis var. goudotii, commonly known as the Madagascar betony, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is a herbaceous perennial with upright stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces spikes of small, violet-blue flowers during the warm season. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long blooming period.
