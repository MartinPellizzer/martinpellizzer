Smilax corbularia var. corbularia

Smilax corbularia var. corbularia, commonly known as the arrowhead smilax, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a perennial vine characterized by its woody stems and climbing habit. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in umbels. Its leaves are arrow-shaped, with a distinctive notched base, and it has spiny stems that help in climbing.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
