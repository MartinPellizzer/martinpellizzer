Strobilanthes fimbriata var. majuscula

Strobilanthes fimbriata var. majuscula, commonly known as the giant fimbriated spurge, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences that resemble plumes, which are typically orange or red in color. The plant is herbaceous, with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral displays.
