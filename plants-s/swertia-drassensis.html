Swertia drassensis

Swertia drassensis, commonly known as the Drass gentian, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas and other mountainous regions.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, thriving in cold and arid conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in a fine layer of white pubescence.

Swertia drassensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
