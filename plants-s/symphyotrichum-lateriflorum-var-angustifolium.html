Symphyotrichum lateriflorum var. angustifolium

Symphyotrichum lateriflorum var. angustifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved heath aster, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and woodland edges. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered purple to pink flowers in the fall. It has a fibrous root system and is often found in well-drained soils.

This variety is distinguished by its narrower foliage compared to other varieties of the species.
