Stereocaryum symingtonianum

Stereocaryum symingtonianum, commonly known as the African snowball, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant produces clusters of white, fluffy flowers that resemble snowballs, blooming in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a waxy texture.

The African snowball is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
