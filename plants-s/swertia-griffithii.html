Swertia griffithii

Swertia griffithii, commonly known as the Himalayan gentian, is a perennial herb native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, thriving in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often branched.

Swertia griffithii is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its traditional medicinal uses in local cultures.
