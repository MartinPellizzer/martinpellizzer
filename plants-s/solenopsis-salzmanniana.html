Solenopsis salzmanniana

Solenopsis salzmanniana, commonly known as the Salzmann's antplant, is a unique species of flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its dense, hair-like structures that resemble ant colonies, providing it with its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, and its leaves are typically narrow and elongated. Solenopsis salzmanniana is a climbing plant that often grows on trees or shrubs, using its specialized structures to attach itself.
