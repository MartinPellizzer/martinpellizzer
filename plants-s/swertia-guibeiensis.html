Swertia guibeiensis

Swertia guibeiensis, commonly known as the Guibei gentian, is a flowering plant belonging to the Gentianaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of southern China, particularly in Yunnan Province. The plant is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Swertia guibeiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
