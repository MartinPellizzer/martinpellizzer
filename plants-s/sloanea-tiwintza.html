Sloanea tiwintza

Sloanea tiwintza, commonly known as the Mexican beautyberry, is a flowering plant native to Central America and northern South America.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features opposite, simple, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. Sloanea tiwintza produces clusters of small, fragrant, white flowers that bloom in the spring.

Its fruit is a small, dark purple berry that contains several seeds, making it an attractive plant for ornamental purposes.
