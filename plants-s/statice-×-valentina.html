Statice × valentina

Statice × valentina, commonly known as "Sea Shells" or "Pink Shells," is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and showy, long-lasting flowers that come in a range of colors, including pink, red, and white. The plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. It is often used in garden borders and as a cut flower due to its resilience and vibrant blooms.

Statice × valentina is drought-tolerant and requires minimal maintenance once established.
