Stobaea lanata

Stobaea lanata, commonly known as the woolly stobaea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive, soft appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens.
