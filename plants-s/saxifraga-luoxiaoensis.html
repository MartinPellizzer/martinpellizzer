Saxifraga luoxiaoensis

Saxifraga luoxiaoensis, commonly known as the Luoxiao saxifrage, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming growth habit. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
