Sauroglossum distans

Sauroglossum distans, commonly known as the Brazilian spider plant, is a perennial herb native to Brazil.

It belongs to the family Bromeliaceae and is characterized by its rosette of narrow, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces inflorescences with small, white flowers that are clustered in a panicle. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
