Smilax oldhamii var. ussuriensis

Smilax oldhamii var. ussuriensis, commonly known as the Ussuri arrowroot or Ussuri smilacina, is a perennial vine native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its woody stems and the presence of sharp, spiny tendrils used for climbing. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in loose inflorescences. Its berries are red when ripe and contain several seeds.

This species is often found in moist, shaded areas such as forests and thickets.
