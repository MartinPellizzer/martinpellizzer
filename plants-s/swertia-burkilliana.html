Swertia burkilliana

Swertia burkilliana, commonly known as the Burkill's gentian, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.
