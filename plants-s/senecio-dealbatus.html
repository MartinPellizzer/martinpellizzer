Senecio dealbatus

Senecio dealbatus, commonly known as the Whiteleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its pale green to white leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open grasslands. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Senecio dealbatus is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and blooms.
