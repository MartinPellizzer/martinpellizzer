Sulcorebutia hilmanii

Sulcorebutia hilmanii, commonly known as the Hilman's moon cactus, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to South America.

It features a swollen, spherical stem that often displays a variety of colorful hues, including green, red, and purple. The plant produces small, white flowers during the summer months, adding to its ornamental appeal. It is well-suited for rock gardens and indoor cultivation due to its drought tolerance and ease of care.

Its unique appearance and relatively simple care requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
