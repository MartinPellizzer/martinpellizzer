Scoparia dulcis var. tenuifolia

Scoparia dulcis var. tenuifolia, commonly known as sweet everlasting or sweet everlasting thyme, is a member of the Apiaceae family.

It is a low-growing herbaceous plant characterized by its slender, thread-like leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. The plant is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its aromatic foliage and long-lasting flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
