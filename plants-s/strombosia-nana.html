Strombosia nana

Strombosia nana, commonly known as the dwarf strombosia, is a small, herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along riverbanks. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are often elliptical in shape and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, usually with yellow or greenish petals.

Strombosia nana is known for its ability to thrive in humid climates and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.
