Sideritis × masferreri

Sideritis × masferreri, commonly known as the hybrid sainfoin, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that typically range in color from pink to purple. Native to the Mediterranean region, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its medicinal properties have been historically valued for their anti-inflammatory and digestive benefits.

Sideritis × masferreri is also noted for its ornamental value, making it a popular choice in gardens and landscaping.
