Solanum microspermum

Solanum microspermum, commonly known as the small-seed nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually purple or white in color. Its fruit is a fleshy berry that contains numerous small seeds.

Solanum microspermum belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae), which includes other plants like tomatoes and potatoes.
