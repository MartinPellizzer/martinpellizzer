Schoenus setiformis

Schoenus setiformis, commonly known as the slender bulrush, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to parts of Africa and is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are typically green or brown in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a slight sheen.

Schoenus setiformis is often found in wetland environments and is adapted to thrive in marshy or aquatic conditions.
