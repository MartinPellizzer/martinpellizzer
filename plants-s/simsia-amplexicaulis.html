Simsia amplexicaulis

Simsia amplexicaulis, commonly known as the clasping leaf simsia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Simsia amplexicaulis is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.
