Schoenodendron buecheri

Schoenodendron buecheri, commonly known as the Buecher's flame tree, is a tropical plant native to parts of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters during the rainy season. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, narrow leaflets that provide a lush green canopy. Its growth habit is typically shrub-like or small tree-like, with a dense, rounded form.

The botanical features of Schoenodendron buecheri make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm climates.
