Schoenus moorei

Schoenus moorei, commonly known as the Moore's sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and grass-like plants. The plant typically grows in wetland or marshy environments, thriving in moist, nutrient-rich soils. Its slender, grass-like stems are usually clustered and produce small, inconspicuous flowers in early spring.
