Schefflera stolleana

Schefflera stolleana, commonly known as the Stolleana umbrella plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like canopy of large, leathery, dark green leaves. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and thrives in well-draining soil. It produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by red berries.

Schefflera stolleana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor conditions.
