Solanum wittei

Solanum wittei, commonly known as the Witte's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale blue in color. Its berries are toxic and contain alkaloids, making it a plant with both botanical and medicinal significance.

Solanum wittei is often found in woodland and savanna habitats.
