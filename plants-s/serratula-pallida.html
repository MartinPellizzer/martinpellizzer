Serratula pallida

Serratula pallida, commonly known as the pale serratula, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in dry, rocky soils. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and serrated, giving it the common name.

Serratula pallida is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.
