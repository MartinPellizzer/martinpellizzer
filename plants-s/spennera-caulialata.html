Spennera caulialata

Spennera caulialata, commonly known as the silverleaf spennera, is a flowering plant native to the Andes region of South America.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, pubescent hairs. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers that bloom during the cooler months. Spennera caulialata thrives in high-altitude environments with cool, moist conditions.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
