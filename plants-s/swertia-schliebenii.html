Swertia schliebenii

Swertia schliebenii, commonly known as the Himalayan gentian, is a perennial herb native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, thriving in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Swertia schliebenii is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
