Spiraea salicifolia var. rubra

Spiraea salicifolia var. rubra, commonly known as redleaf weeping willow spirea, is a deciduous shrub characterized by its distinctive, cascading branches.

It features narrow, willow-like leaves that turn a striking red or orange in the fall. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to white flowers in early summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sun.

This variety is prized for its vibrant foliage and graceful form, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
