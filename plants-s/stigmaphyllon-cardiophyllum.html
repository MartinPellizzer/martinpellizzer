Stigmaphyllon cardiophyllum

Stigmaphyllon cardiophyllum, commonly known as the heartleaf stigmaphyllon, is a flowering plant native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters. Its leaves are heart-shaped, which is reflected in its scientific name.

Stigmaphyllon cardiophyllum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
