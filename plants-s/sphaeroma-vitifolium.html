Sphaeroma vitifolium

Sphaeroma vitifolium, commonly known as the grape-leaved sphaeroma, is a species of flowering plant native to certain regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, which are typically followed by small, round berries. Sphaeroma vitifolium thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.

Its unique foliage and compact growth habit make it an interesting subject for botanical study and horticultural cultivation.
