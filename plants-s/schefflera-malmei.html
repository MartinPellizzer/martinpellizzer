Schefflera malmei

Schefflera malmei, commonly known as the Malme's umbrella plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of large, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Schefflera malmei is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor conditions.
