Swertia teres

Swertia teres, commonly known as the purple gentian, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

Swertia teres is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
