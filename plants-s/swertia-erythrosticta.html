Swertia erythrosticta

Swertia erythrosticta, commonly known as the red-streaked gentian, is a perennial herb native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy surface.

Swertia erythrosticta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
