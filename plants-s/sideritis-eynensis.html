Sideritis eynensis

Sideritis eynensis, commonly known as the Eyn Mountain sideritis, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of eastern Turkey.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in rocky and limestone soils, often growing in open habitats at high elevations.

Sideritis eynensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
