Stemodia durantifolia var. durantifolia

Stemodia durantifolia var. durantifolia, commonly known as the everlasting stemodia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form with small, narrow leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale blue or white flowers that bloom in spring. Its stems are covered in fine, soft hairs, and it is known for its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.

This species is often cultivated in rock gardens and as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and hardy nature.
