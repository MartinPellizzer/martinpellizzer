Senna corymbosa

Senna corymbosa, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant produces long, slender pods that contain numerous seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

Senna corymbosa thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
