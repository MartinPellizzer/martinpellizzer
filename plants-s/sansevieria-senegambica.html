Sansevieria senegambica

Sansevieria senegambica, commonly known as the African spear or the Senegambian snake plant, is a species of succulent native to the tropical regions of West Africa.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with subtle variegation, often adorned with yellow or white markings. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its rosette growth habit and striking foliage make it both ornamental and resilient.

Sansevieria senegambica is also noted for its air-purifying properties, contributing to its reputation as a hardy and beneficial houseplant.
