Senecio prattii

Senecio prattii, commonly known as the pratt's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It features slender stems and small, daisy-like yellow flowers.

Senecio prattii is often found in open, sunny habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
