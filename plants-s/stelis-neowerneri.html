Stelis neowerneri

Stelis neowerneri, commonly known as the Werneri's spider orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically found growing on trees in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with a short, thick rhizome and a rosette of fleshy, elliptical leaves. Its flowers are small, usually greenish or yellowish, and are arranged in a dense spike.

Stelis neowerneri is noted for its specialized adaptations to its humid, shaded habitat.
