Stelis viridipurpurea

Stelis viridipurpurea, commonly known as the purple-leafed orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking foliage with a mix of green and purple tones, contributing to its ornamental value. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, well-lit environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Its unique coloration and flowering habits make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
