Sprekelia cybister var. subsexuncialis

Sprekelia cybister var. subsexuncialis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bellflower, is a striking perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a distinctive red or orange throat. The plant has strap-like, narrow leaves that are typically green with a slight blue or grayish hue. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, which can extend from late winter to early spring.
