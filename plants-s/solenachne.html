Solenachne

Solenachne, commonly known as the false millet, is a genus of grasses belonging to the family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spikelet-like inflorescences. The plants typically grow in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Solenachne species are often found in temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.
