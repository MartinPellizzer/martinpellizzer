Scabiosa rutifolia

Scabiosa rutifolia, commonly known as the Italian sainfoin, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, pink or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Scabiosa rutifolia is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
