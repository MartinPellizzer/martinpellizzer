Streptilon

Streptilon, commonly known as the blue mistflower, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the southeastern United States.

It features showy, blue-violet flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. Streptilon thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
