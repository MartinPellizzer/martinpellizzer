Sarcochilus scortechinii

Sarcochilus scortechinii, commonly known as the Hairy Sword Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its erect, sword-shaped leaves and clusters of pale green to yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. It has a rhizomatous growth habit and produces showy, fragrant blooms during late spring to early summer.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with proper humidity and light conditions.
