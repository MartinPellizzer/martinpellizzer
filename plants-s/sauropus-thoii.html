Sauropus thoii

Sauropus thoii, commonly known as the silverleaf sauropus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green leaves, which are covered in fine, fuzzy hairs. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are alternate and lance-shaped, with a waxy texture that helps reduce water loss.

Sauropus thoii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage.
