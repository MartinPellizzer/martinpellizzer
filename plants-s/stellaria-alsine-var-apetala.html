Stellaria alsine var. apetala

Stellaria alsine var. apetala, commonly known as the naked stellaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, white flowers that lack petals, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows and along pathways. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, with a hairy texture.

It is a short-lived perennial that produces seeds through its capsule-like fruit.
