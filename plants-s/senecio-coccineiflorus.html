Senecio coccineiflorus

Senecio coccineiflorus, commonly known as the red-flowered senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its vibrant, showy inflorescences. The plant features slender, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically bright red or orange, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
