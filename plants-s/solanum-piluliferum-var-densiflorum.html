Solanum piluliferum var. densiflorum

Solanum piluliferum var. densiflorum, commonly known as the denseflower nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and produces clusters of small, white to pale green flowers. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that are usually hairy and have a strong, often unpleasant odor. Its fruits are typically small, globose berries that change color as they mature.

It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is part of the nightshade family, Solanaceae.
