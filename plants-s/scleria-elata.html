Scleria elata

Scleria elata, commonly known as the tall bulrush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a hairy texture.

Scleria elata thrives in wetland environments and is often found in marshes and along the edges of water bodies.
