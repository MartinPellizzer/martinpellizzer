Senecio doria subsp. laderoi

Senecio doria subsp.

laderoi, commonly known as the Doria's ragged robin, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers and serrated leaves.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. It produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.
