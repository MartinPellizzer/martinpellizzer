Stelis oblongifolia

Stelis oblongifolia, commonly known as the broad-leafed stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is an epiphytic orchid, typically found growing on trees or rocks in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features elongated, ovate leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually in inflorescences that can be white, yellow, or greenish in color.

Stelis oblongifolia thrives in humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.
