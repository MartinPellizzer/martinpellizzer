Schefflera mamutiaca

Schefflera mamutiaca, commonly known as the Mamutia or Mamutiaca umbrella tree, is a species native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its large, umbrella-like compound leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in diameter. The plant has a woody stem with a smooth, greyish-brown bark. Its leaves are typically green on the upper surface and lighter in color on the underside.

Schefflera mamutiaca is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage and ability to thrive in various light conditions.
