Simsia hirsuta

Simsia hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy simsia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features hairy stems and leaves, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.
