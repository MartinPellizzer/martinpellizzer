Stellilabium tsipiriense

Stellilabium tsipiriense, commonly known as the Tsipiriense star-labium, is a rare flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

Its flowers bloom during the late spring to early summer season, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
