Scutellaria potosina var. platyphylla

Scutellaria potosina var. platyphylla, commonly known as the broad-leaved skullcap, is a perennial herb native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by square stems and opposite leaves. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture and produces small, two-lipped flowers in shades of blue or violet. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or shaded areas.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal potential, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
