Stylidium dilatatum

Stylidium dilatatum, commonly known as the broadleaf spurred orchid, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique spurred structure on the petals. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils in open habitats. It produces small, delicate flowers that are often yellow or greenish in color.

Stylidium dilatatum is a short-lived perennial, often found in coastal areas and is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
