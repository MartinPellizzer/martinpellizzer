Senecio erubescens

Senecio erubescens, commonly known as the red-hot poker, is a striking plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, spiky flower stalks that produce bright orange to red tubular flowers, which bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette at the base. It is a perennial succulent that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
