Syzygium rosulentum

Syzygium rosulentum, commonly known as the rose apple tree, is a tropical species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and white or pale pink, typically arranged in clusters.

The fruit is a spherical, smooth, yellow or orange berry with a sweet and aromatic flavor.
