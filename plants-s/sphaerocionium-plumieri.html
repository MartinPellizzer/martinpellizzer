Sphaerocionium plumieri

Sphaerocionium plumieri, commonly known as the Christmas star, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of small, waxy, white flowers that bloom in winter, giving it a star-like appearance. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its ornamental flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Sphaerocionium plumieri is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators and its resilience to coastal conditions.
