Spathium chinense

Spathium chinense, commonly known as the Chinese spathe, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, elongated bracts that enclose the small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, tropical environments and prefers well-drained soil. Its foliage consists of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often glossy in appearance.

Spathium chinense is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique floral structure.
