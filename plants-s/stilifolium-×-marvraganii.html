Stilifolium × marvraganii

Stilifolium × marvraganii, commonly known as the hybrid evergreen shrub, is a botanical hybrid resulting from the cross between Stilifolium and Marvraganii species.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage and compact growth habit, making it suitable for ornamental use in gardens. The plant typically produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by rounded, fleshy fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, preferring a temperate climate.

Due to its unique genetic makeup, it exhibits traits that are a blend of its parent species, contributing to its adaptability and aesthetic appeal.
