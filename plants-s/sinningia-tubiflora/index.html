Sinningia Tubiflora

Sinningia tubiflora is a striking member of the Gesneriaceae family native to the cloud forests of Ecuador. This species is known for its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. The plant's unique shape and color make it a popular choice among succulent and tropical plant enthusiasts. Its beauty and ease of care have made it a favorite for both indoor and outdoor gardens.
