Senecio perralderianus var. hosmariensis

Senecio perralderianus var. hosmariensis, commonly known as the Hosmariensis Groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun exposure. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring and summer, followed by fluffy, white seed heads.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
