Swertia porphyrantha

Swertia porphyrantha, commonly known as the purple gentian, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine regions, thriving in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and branched.

Swertia porphyrantha is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
