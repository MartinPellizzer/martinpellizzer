Sedum mucizonia subsp. abylaeum

Sedum mucizonia subsp.

abylaeum, commonly known as the desert stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of North America. It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are adapted to store water, allowing it to thrive in dry environments.

The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It is typically found in rocky soils and gravelly areas, often in desert or semi-desert habitats.

Its hardy nature makes it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
