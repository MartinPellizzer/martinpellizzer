Styphelia rotundifolia

Styphelia rotundifolia, commonly known as the wintergreen heath, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the boreal forests of North America and parts of Asia.

Styphelia rotundifolia thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in moist, shaded environments.
