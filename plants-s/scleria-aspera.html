Scleria aspera

Scleria aspera, commonly known as the rough sedges, is a perennial plant native to various regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and grass-like plants. The plant is characterized by its triangular, rough-textured stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It typically grows in wet or damp environments such as marshes and stream banks.
