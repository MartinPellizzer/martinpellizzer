Silene jeniseensis var. kodarensis

Silene jeniseensis var. kodarensis, commonly known as the Kodarensis Campion, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in temperate climates with moderate rainfall. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often prostrate or ascending.

This variety is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and limited distribution.
