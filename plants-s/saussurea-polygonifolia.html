Saussurea polygonifolia

Saussurea polygonifolia, commonly known as the Tibetan yellow gentian, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and fibrous root system. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky slopes, where it thrives in cold and arid conditions. Saussurea polygonifolia is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Tibetan medicine.
