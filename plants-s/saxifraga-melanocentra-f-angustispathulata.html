Saxifraga melanocentra f. angustispathulata

Saxifraga melanocentra f. angustispathulata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf saxifrage, is a rare and distinctive plant species belonging to the Saxifragaceae family.

Native to rocky alpine environments, this plant thrives in well-drained, calcareous soils.
