Solanum aurantium

Solanum aurantium, commonly known as the yellow bell pepper, is a member of the nightshade family (Solanaceae).

It is native to Central America and is characterized by its yellow, bell-shaped fruits. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with hairy stems and alternate leaves. Its flowers are usually yellow or green, and it produces small, bright yellow fruits that are edible.

The plant is cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of culinary ingredients.
