Strelitzia teretifolia

Strelitzia teretifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf bird-of-paradise, is a striking tropical plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its long, narrow, strap-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces dramatic, bird-of-paradise-like flowers with a blue-violet spathe and a yellow-orange hood. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Due to its unique foliage and showy blooms, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
