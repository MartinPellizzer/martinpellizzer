Sisyrinchium asheianum

Sisyrinchium asheianum, commonly known as Ashe's blue-eyed grass, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the genus Sisyrinchium, which includes various species of blue-eyed grasses. This plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It produces small, bright blue flowers that bloom in the spring, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.
