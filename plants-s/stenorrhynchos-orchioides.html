Stenorrhynchos orchioides

Stenorrhynchos orchioides, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, delicate flowers with a pale greenish-yellow color. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers a shady to semi-shaded environment. It has linear leaves and a short, stout stem that bears the floral spikes.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
