Statice catanensis

Statice catanensis, commonly known as the Catanese statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and dense clusters of small, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in full sunlight. Its flowers come in shades of pink, purple, and white, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Statice catanensis is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.
