Sterculia obtusifolia

Sterculia obtusifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved sterculia, is a tropical tree native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that are often rounded or oval in shape. The plant produces showy, yellow or orange flowers that are typically clustered in panicles. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
