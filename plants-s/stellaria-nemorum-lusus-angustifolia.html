Stellaria nemorum lusus angustifolia

Stellaria nemorum lusus angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf starwort, is a low-growing perennial herbaceous plant native to moist, shaded environments in temperate regions.

It features small, white or pale blue star-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, creeping stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It is typically found in woodlands, stream banks, and other damp habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to thrive in shaded, nutrient-rich soils.
