Statice amoena

Statice amoena, commonly known as sea lavender, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of Europe and North Africa.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant thrives in salty, well-drained soils and is often found in dunes and maritime habitats. Its sturdy stems and narrow leaves are adapted to withstand harsh coastal conditions.

Statice amoena is widely cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in dry, sunny environments.
