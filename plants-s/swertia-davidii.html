Swertia davidii

Swertia davidii, commonly known as the Chinese gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or forested areas at high altitudes, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a waxy coating, aiding in moisture retention.

Swertia davidii is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
