Satyrium parviflorum var. schimperi

Satyrium parviflorum var. schimperi, commonly known as the Schimper's small-flowered satyrium, is a flowering plant native to parts of East Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, delicate flowers. The plant typically produces pale yellow to greenish-white blooms, which are often fragrant. It thrives in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
