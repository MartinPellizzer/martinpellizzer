Schefflera humblotii

Schefflera humblotii, commonly known as the Madagascar schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of leaves, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant features compound leaves with multiple leaflets radiating from a central stem, and it produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers. Schefflera humblotii is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.
