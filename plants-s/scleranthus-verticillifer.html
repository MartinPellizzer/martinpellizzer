Scleranthus verticillifer

Scleranthus verticillifer, commonly known as the clustered sandspurry, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows in dry, sandy, or rocky soils. The plant features a prostrate or ascending stem with clusters of small, yellowish flowers. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture.

The fruit is a dry capsule that contains several small, oval seeds.
