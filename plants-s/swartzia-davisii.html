Swartzia davisii

Swartzia davisii, commonly known as the Davis's swartzia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Atlantic Forest region of Brazil.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant exhibits a distinctive appearance with its dark, glossy leaves and small, clustered white flowers. Swartzia davisii is valued for its hard, durable wood, which is used in local craftsmanship and furniture.
