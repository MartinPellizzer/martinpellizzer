Sonerila crassiuscula

Sonerila crassiuscula, commonly known as the broad-leaved sonerila, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, often clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
