Sedum abchasicum

Sedum abchasicum, commonly known as the Abchazian stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the Caucasus region.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, clustered leaves that are typically green or reddish in color. The plant produces star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to full sun exposure.

Due to its resilience and ornamental appeal, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and rockeries.
