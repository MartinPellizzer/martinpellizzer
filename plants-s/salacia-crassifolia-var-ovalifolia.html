Salacia crassifolia var. ovalifolia

Salacia crassifolia var. ovalifolia, commonly known as the oval-leaved salacia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, oval-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green in color. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, clustered in terminal inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in primary forest undergrowth.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses in local communities.
