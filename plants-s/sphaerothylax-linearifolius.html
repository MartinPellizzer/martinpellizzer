Sphaerothylax linearifolius

Sphaerothylax linearifolius, commonly known as the linear-leaved stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique morphology and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
