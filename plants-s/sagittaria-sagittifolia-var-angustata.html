Sagittaria sagittifolia var. angustata

Sagittaria sagittifolia var. angustata, commonly known as narrowleaf arrowhead, is a herbaceous aquatic plant native to North America.

It typically grows in shallow water, wetlands, and along the edges of ponds and lakes. The plant features arrow-shaped leaves that are narrow and pointed, giving it its common name. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, often appearing in clusters above the water.

It plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
