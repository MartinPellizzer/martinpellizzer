Senecio leucophyton

Senecio leucophyton, commonly known as the whiteleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky, arid environments. The plant features narrow, silvery-gray leaves that help reduce water loss in its harsh habitat. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in a cluster at the ends of the stems.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
