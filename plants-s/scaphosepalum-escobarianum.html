Scaphosepalum escobarianum

Scaphosepalum escobarianum, commonly known as the Escobaria orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in humid, tropical environments. The plant features slender, erect stems with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and typically white or pale yellow, arranged in a spike-like inflorescence.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
