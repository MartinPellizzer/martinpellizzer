Saxifraga giraldiana var. hupehensis

Saxifraga giraldiana var. hupehensis, commonly known as the Hupeh Saxifrage, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming growth habit. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers during the summer months. It thrives in rocky or well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and shady landscapes.
