Scutellaria nicaraguensis

Scutellaria nicaraguensis, commonly known as the Nicaraguan skullcap, is a flowering plant native to Central America, particularly Nicaragua.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Scutellaria nicaraguensis is noted for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
