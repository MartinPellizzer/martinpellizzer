Saussurea polycolea

Saussurea polycolea, commonly known as the Indian love grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the Himalayas and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and feathery foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the daisy family. It thrives in alpine and subalpine environments, often growing at high altitudes.
