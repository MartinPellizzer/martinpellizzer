Seriola aethnensis var. depressa

Seriola aethnensis var. depressa, commonly known as the Mediterranean seriola, is a variant of the seriola species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a flowering plant characterized by its low-growing habit and compact foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture.

This variety is often found in rocky or coastal areas, where it thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight.
