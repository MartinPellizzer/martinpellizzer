Siphoneugena densiflora

Siphoneugena densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered siphoneugena, is a herbaceous plant native to certain regions of South America.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of small, tubular flowers. The flowers are typically pale blue or violet in color and are arranged in dense inflorescences. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
