Senecio sareptanus

Senecio sareptanus, commonly known as the Sareptan groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. It features narrow, linear leaves and produces small yellow flowers arranged in clusters.
