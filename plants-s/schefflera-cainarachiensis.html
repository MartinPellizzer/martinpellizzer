Schefflera cainarachiensis

Schefflera cainarachiensis, commonly known as the Madagascar schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of large, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Schefflera cainarachiensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.
