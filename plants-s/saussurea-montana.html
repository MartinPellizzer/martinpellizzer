Saussurea montana

Saussurea montana, commonly known as Chinese yarrow or western yarrow, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, branched stems and clusters of small, pale yellow to white flowers. The plant has feathery, aromatic leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

Saussurea montana is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in herbal remedies for various health conditions.
