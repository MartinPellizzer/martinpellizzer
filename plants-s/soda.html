Soda

Soda, commonly known as *Solanum melongena*, is a flowering plant in the nightshade family.

It is native to warm temperate and tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas. The plant produces heart-shaped leaves and clusters of violet or white flowers. Its fruit, the eggplant, is a popular vegetable in many cuisines worldwide.

Botanically, it is a herbaceous perennial that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.
