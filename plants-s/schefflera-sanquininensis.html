Schefflera sanquininensis

Schefflera sanquininensis, commonly known as the Sanquininensis umbrella tree, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of large, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by orange-red berries, which are edible but not commonly consumed by humans. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant.

Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
