Sinopogonanthera

Sinopogonanthera, commonly known as the ghost orchid, is a rare and enigmatic flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, delicate appearance, with elongated, pale green leaves and a single, showy white or pale pink flower. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of trees without deriving nutrients from them. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
