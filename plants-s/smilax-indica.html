Smilax indica

Smilax indica, commonly known as the yellow arrowroot or Indian spinach, is a climbing shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its woody stems, which are often armed with sharp spines. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped with a rough texture.

Smilax indica is known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
