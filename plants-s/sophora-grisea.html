Sophora grisea

Sophora grisea, commonly known as the gray sophora, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Sophora grisea is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it useful in soil enrichment and ecological restoration projects.
