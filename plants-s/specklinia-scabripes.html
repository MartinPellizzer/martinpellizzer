Specklinia scabripes

Specklinia scabripes, commonly known as the hairy leaf orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, hairy foliage and compact, clustered growth habit. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Specklinia scabripes is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
