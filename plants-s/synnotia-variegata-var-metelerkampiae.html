Synnotia variegata var. metelerkampiae

Synnotia variegata var. metelerkampiae, commonly known as the variegated synnotia, is a striking evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with striking white or cream variegation, which adds visual interest to gardens. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of yellow and orange, attracting pollinators like birds and insects. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Its unique foliage and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for both landscape design and container gardening.
