Schrameckia madagascariensis

Schrameckia madagascariensis, commonly known as the Madagascan star orchid, is a unique orchid species native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insects.

Schrameckia madagascariensis is a rare and endangered species, highlighting the importance of conservation efforts in its native habitat.
