Solanum besserianum

Solanum besserianum, commonly known as the Besserianum nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and peppers. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale purple. Its fruit is a yellowish berry that ripens to orange, containing several seeds.

The plant is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, and it is often found in moist, tropical environments.
