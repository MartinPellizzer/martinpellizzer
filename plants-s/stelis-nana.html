Stelis nana

Stelis nana, commonly known as the dwarf stelis, is a small epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant has small, clustered pseudobulbs and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a delicate, fragrant scent.

Stelis nana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or terrariums.
