Stapelia × mutabilis

Stapelia × mutabilis, commonly known as the variable star cactus, is a hybrid plant that combines traits from its parent species, Stapelia and Mutabilis.

It is a succulent with thick, fleshy stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces striking, star-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a strong, unpleasant odor to attract pollinators. Its leaves are typically modified into spines, reducing water loss and providing protection.

Stapelia × mutabilis is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
