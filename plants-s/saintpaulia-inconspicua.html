Saintpaulia inconspicua

Saintpaulia inconspicua, commonly known as the African violet, is a species of flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and its velvety, waxy leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, typically blooming from spring to autumn. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular houseplant.

Saintpaulia inconspicua is prized for its ornamental value and ease of care in indoor environments.
