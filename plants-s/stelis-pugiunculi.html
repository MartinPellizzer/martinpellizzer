Stelis pugiunculi

Stelis pugiunculi, commonly known as the Pugilist Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure with a single, narrow leaf. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in a raceme. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Stelis pugiunculi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
