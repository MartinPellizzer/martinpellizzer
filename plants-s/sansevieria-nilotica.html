Sansevieria nilotica

Sansevieria nilotica, commonly known as the African spear or snake plant, is a succulent plant native to tropical Africa.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with prominent yellow or white margins. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering. It produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers on slender stalks during the growing season.

Sansevieria nilotica is popular as a houseplant due to its air-purifying properties and resilience.
