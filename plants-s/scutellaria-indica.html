Scutellaria indica

Scutellaria indica, commonly known as Indian skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks.

Scutellaria indica is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
