Sipolisia lanuginosa

Sipolisia lanuginosa, commonly known as the woolly sipolisia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and stems, which give it a fuzzy appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
