Sabazia longiradiata

Sabazia longiradiata, commonly known as the long-spurred orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking, showy flowers with elongated spurs that can reach several centimeters in length. Its leaves are typically broad and lance-shaped, arranged in a basal rosette.

Sabazia longiradiata thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
