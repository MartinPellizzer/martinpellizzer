Siebera baselloides

Siebera baselloides, commonly known as the silverleaf sievebird.

It is a small, evergreen shrub native to the coastal regions of southern Africa. The plant is characterized by its narrow, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs. It produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by edible, berry-like fruits.

Siebera baselloides is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to coastal conditions.
