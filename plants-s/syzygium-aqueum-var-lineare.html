Syzygium aqueum var. lineare

Syzygium aqueum var. lineare, commonly known as the linear water apple, is a variant of the water apple native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that typically grows in wet, tropical environments. The plant is characterized by its linear, elongated leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers. Its fruit, similar to the water apple, is edible and has a sweet, aromatic flavor.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential use in traditional medicine.
