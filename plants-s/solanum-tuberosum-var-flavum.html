Solanum tuberosum var. flavum

Solanum tuberosum var. flavum, commonly known as the yellow potato, is a variant of the potato plant characterized by its yellow or orange flesh.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family and is native to the Andes region of South America. The plant produces tubers that are typically smaller than regular potatoes and have a distinct, vibrant color. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its unique flavor and nutritional profile.
