Senecio megaglossus

Senecio megaglossus, commonly known as the giant leaf senecio, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, broad, and often hairy leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces showy yellow flowers in dense, branched inflorescences during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is also cultivated as an ornamental plant in many regions.
