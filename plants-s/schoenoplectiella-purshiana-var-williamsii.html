Schoenoplectiella purshiana var. williamsii

Schoenoplectiella purshiana var. williamsii, commonly known as the Williams' bulrush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, commonly known as the sedge family. The plant typically grows in wetland habitats, including marshes and ponds, where it forms dense stands. Its stems are triangular in cross-section and often have a reddish hue, while its leaves are narrow and grass-like.
