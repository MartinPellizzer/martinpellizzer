Scirpus mindorensis

Scirpus mindorensis, commonly known as Mindanao bulrush, is a species of flowering plant in the sedge family Cyperaceae.

It is native to the Philippines, particularly found in the island of Mindanao. This plant typically grows in wetland habitats such as marshes, swamps, and along the edges of water bodies. It has tall, cylindrical stems that are often used for thatching and basket weaving by local communities.

The plant also plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
