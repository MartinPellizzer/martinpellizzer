Silene tridentata var. coarctata

Silene tridentata var. coarctata, commonly known as the narrowleaf catchfly, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, pinkish-white flowers.
