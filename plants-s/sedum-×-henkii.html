Sedum × henkii

Sedum × henkii, commonly known as "Ghost Plant," is a hybrid succulent native to China.

It features fleshy, upright stems that often appear translucent or ghostly white, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique morphology and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
