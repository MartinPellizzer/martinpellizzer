Scyphogyne calcicola

Scyphogyne calcicola, commonly known as the limestone scyphogyne, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that thrives in calcareous soils, often found in rocky or limestone-rich habitats. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically narrow and linear, adapted to conserve water in its arid environment.

Scyphogyne calcicola is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in challenging soil conditions.
