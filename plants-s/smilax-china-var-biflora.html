Smilax china var. biflora

Smilax china var. biflora, commonly known as the Chinese arrowroot or twin-flowered smilax, is a climbing shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features a thick, woody stem with thorn-like projections and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in pairs, giving it its varietal name "biflora.". Its tuberous roots are often harvested for their starchy, edible qualities.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
