Scutellaria kiusiana var. discolor

Scutellaria kiusiana var. discolor, commonly known as the variegated skullcap, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its distinctive blue or purple flowers and variegated leaves with contrasting colors. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and botanical research.
