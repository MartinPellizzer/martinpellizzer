Stylosanthes erecta var. acuminata

Stylosanthes erecta var. acuminata, commonly known as the acuminata stylosanthes, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a legume, belonging to the Fabaceae family, and is characterized by its erect growth habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellow to orange flowers that are clustered in racemes, and its seeds are encased in pods.

It is often used in forage systems due to its high nutritional value and ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen.
