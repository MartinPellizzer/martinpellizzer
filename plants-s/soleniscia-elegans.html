Soleniscia elegans

Soleniscia elegans, commonly known as the silverleaf soleniscia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and small, clustered white flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to drought conditions. Its leaves are covered in fine, reflective hairs that help reduce water loss.

Soleniscia elegans is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
