Specklinia nigriflora

Specklinia nigriflora, commonly known as the black-flowered orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that grows on trees and rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces striking dark purple to black flowers that bloom during the rainy season. Its leaves are typically dark green and leathery, adapted to retain moisture.

Specklinia nigriflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouse settings due to its specific care requirements.
