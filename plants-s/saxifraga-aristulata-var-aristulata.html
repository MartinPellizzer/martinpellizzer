Saxifraga aristulata var. aristulata

Saxifraga aristulata var. aristulata, commonly known as the upright saxifrage, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or alpine environments. The plant features upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hair. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense inflorescences, and usually have five petals that range in color from white to pink.

It thrives in cool, moist conditions and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.
