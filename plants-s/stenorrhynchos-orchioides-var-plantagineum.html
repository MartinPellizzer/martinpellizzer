Stenorrhynchos orchioides var. plantagineum

Stenorrhynchos orchioides var. plantagineum, commonly known as the Plantagineum Orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in grassland habitats and prefers well-drained soils. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
