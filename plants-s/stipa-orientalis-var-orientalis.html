Stipa orientalis var. orientalis

Stipa orientalis var. orientalis, commonly known as oriental feathergrass, is a perennial grass native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and delicate, feathery plumes that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
