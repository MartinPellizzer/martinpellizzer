Spiranthes chiangii

Spiranthes chiangii, commonly known as the Chiang Mai spiraea, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, spiral-shaped flower clusters that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, and they bloom during the spring and early summer.

Spiranthes chiangii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
