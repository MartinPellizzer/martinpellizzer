Stylogyne micans

Stylogyne micans, commonly known as the silverleaf ironwood, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage that often has a silvery or whitish underside, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy fruits. Stylogyne micans thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
