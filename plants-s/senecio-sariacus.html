Senecio sariacus

Senecio sariacus, commonly known as the Sariacus groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Senecio sariacus is often used in rock gardens due to its hardy nature and ornamental appeal.
