Schefflera fantsipanensis

Schefflera fantsipanensis, commonly known as the Madagascar Schefflera, is a rare evergreen shrub native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of leaflets, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments within its native habitat. Its leaves are dark green and glossy, with a leathery texture.

Due to its limited distribution and unique morphology, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
