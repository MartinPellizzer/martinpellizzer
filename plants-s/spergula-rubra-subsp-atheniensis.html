Spergula rubra subsp. atheniensis

Spergula rubra subsp.

atheniensis, commonly known as the Athens red grass, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its slender, reddish stems and small, pinkish flowers.
