Saxifraga × splendida

Saxifraga × splendida, commonly known as the splendour saxifrage, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Saxifragaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Europe and is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of pink or red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and rockeries for its striking appearance.
