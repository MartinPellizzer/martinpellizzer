Stellaria litigiosa

Stellaria litigiosa, commonly known as the battle star, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, star-shaped white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in open, sunny habitats. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, while its stems are usually prostrate or ascending.
