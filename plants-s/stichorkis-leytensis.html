Stichorkis leytensis

Stichorkis leytensis, commonly known as the Leyte orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Stichorkis leytensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
