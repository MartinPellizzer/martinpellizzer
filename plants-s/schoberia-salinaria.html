Schoberia salinaria

Schoberia salinaria, commonly known as saltmarsh aster, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of North America.

It thrives in saline and brackish environments, typically found in salt marshes and tidal flats. The plant has slender, erect stems that bear showy blue to purple flowers in the late summer and early fall. Its leaves are narrow and linear, adapted to withstand salt spray and periodic flooding.

Schoberia salinaria plays an important ecological role by stabilizing soils and providing habitat for various insects and birds.
