Salix Rigida Subsp. Mackenzieana

Salix rigida subsp. mackenzieana is a hardy shrub native to the arctic and subarctic regions of north america. it thrives in harsh environments with extreme cold and limited nutrients. this subspecies is known for its dense growth and ability to stabilize soil in fragile ecosystems. its small, elliptical leaves and catkins make it easily identifiable in its natural habitat. it plays a vital role in supporting local wildlife and maintaining ecological balance in polar regions.
