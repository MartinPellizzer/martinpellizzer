Satyrium retusum

Satyrium retusum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and grasslands. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single or few flowers that are usually pale green or yellowish in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and the flowers have a distinctive lip that is often recurved.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, herbaceous structure.
