Sutera annua

Sutera annua, commonly known as the common blueweed, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, blue to purple flowers that bloom from late spring to early autumn. The plant has slender, branched stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are usually hairy. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

Sutera annua is valued for its ornamental appeal and has also been used in traditional herbal medicine.
