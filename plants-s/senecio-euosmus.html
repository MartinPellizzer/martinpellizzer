Senecio euosmus

Senecio euosmus, commonly known as the blue ice plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant has succulent leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. It produces small, daisy-like blooms that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Senecio euosmus is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
