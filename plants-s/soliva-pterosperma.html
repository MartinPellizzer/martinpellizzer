Soliva pterosperma

Soliva pterosperma, commonly known as the common soliva, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a rough texture, while its roots are fibrous and deep-reaching.

Soliva pterosperma is often used in traditional medicine and is also valued for its ornamental appeal.
