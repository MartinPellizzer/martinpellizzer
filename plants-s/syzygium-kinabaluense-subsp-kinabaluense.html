It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree with elliptical, leathery leaves that are dark green on the upper surface and lighter on the underside. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by edible, yellow-orange berries that are rich in vitamin C. It thrives in cool, moist climates and is adapted to the unique ecological conditions of its mountainous habitat.
