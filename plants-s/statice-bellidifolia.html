Statice bellidifolia

Statice bellidifolia, commonly known as sea lavender, is a flowering plant native to coastal areas of the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and spikes of small, showy blue or purple flowers. The plant thrives in saline and well-drained soils, making it well adapted to coastal environments. Its flowers are often used in floral arrangements due to their long-lasting nature.

Statice bellidifolia is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize dunes.
