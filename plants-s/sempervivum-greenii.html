Sempervivum greenii

Sempervivum greenii, commonly known as the Green Hens and Chicks, is a species of succulent plant native to the alpine regions of Central Asia.

It features dense, rosette-shaped foliage with fleshy, grass-like leaves that are typically green to blue-green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Sempervivum greenii is also known for its ability to propagate easily through offsets, which contribute to its hardiness and popularity among gardeners.
