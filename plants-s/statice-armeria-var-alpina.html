Statice armeria var. alpina

Statice armeria var. alpina, commonly known as the alpine statice, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and its clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically blooms in late summer to early autumn, producing delicate, grass-like foliage that is often used in floral arrangements. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Statice armeria var. alpina is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators such as butterflies and bees.
