Synaedrys cyclophora

Synaedrys cyclophora, commonly known as the cyclophora orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features striking, showy flowers with a unique, spiral-shaped lip that distinguishes it from other orchid species. Its roots are adapted for epiphytic growth, allowing it to thrive on tree bark in humid environments.

Synaedrys cyclophora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
