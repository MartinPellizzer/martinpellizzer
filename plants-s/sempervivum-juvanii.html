Sempervivum juvanii

Sempervivum juvanii, commonly known as the alpine houseleek, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped growth pattern, with fleshy, often blue-green leaves that store water. The plant produces tall flowering stems with small, star-shaped flowers, typically in shades of pink or white. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it well-suited for rock gardens or alpine settings.

Sempervivum juvanii is known for its hardiness and ability to survive in harsh, cold climates.
