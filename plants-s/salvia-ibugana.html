Salvia ibugana

Salvia ibugana, commonly known as the African sage, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and produces spikes of violet to blue flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Salvia ibugana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in traditional medicine.
