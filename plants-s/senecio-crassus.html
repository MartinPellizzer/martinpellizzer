Senecio crassus

Senecio crassus, commonly known as the thickstem senecio, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, typically during the warmer months. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.

Senecio crassus prefers well-draining soil and full sun exposure for optimal growth.
