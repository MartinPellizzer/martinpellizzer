Senecio hadrosomus

Senecio hadrosomus, commonly known as the Cape snowflake, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has a low-growing habit with narrow, silver-gray leaves that help it retain moisture in its arid habitat. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it an attractive and hardy addition to gardens in suitable climates.
