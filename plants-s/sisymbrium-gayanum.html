Sisymbrium gayanum

Sisymbrium gayanum, commonly known as the Sri Lankan winter cress, is a herbaceous plant native to Sri Lanka.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and features small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are alternate, oval-shaped, and have a slightly hairy texture.

The plant is often found in moist, open habitats such as grasslands and forest margins.
