Symbolanthus nebulosus

Symbolanthus nebulosus, commonly known as the cloud flower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its unique, showy inflorescences that resemble clouds, hence its common name. The plant produces small, white to pale blue flowers that are clustered in dense, rounded umbels. Symbolanthus nebulosus thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value has made it a sought-after plant in horticulture.
