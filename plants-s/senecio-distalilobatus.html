Senecio distalilobatus

Senecio distalilobatus, commonly known as the narrow-lobed groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid regions. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals, blooming during the cooler months. Its leaves are narrow and lobed, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes many other familiar flowering plants.
