Stachys memorabilis

Stachys memorabilis, commonly known as the purple flowered betony, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces spikes of violet or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Stachys memorabilis is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
