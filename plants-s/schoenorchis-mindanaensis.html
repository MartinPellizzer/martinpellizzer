Schoenorchis mindanaensis

Schoenorchis mindanaensis, commonly known as the Mindanao orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant typically produces a single, slender stem with few leaves and a single, showy flower. Its flowers are usually pale green to white with a distinctive yellow lip, and it blooms during the rainy season.

Schoenorchis mindanaensis is often found in moist, shaded forest understories, where it thrives in humid conditions.
