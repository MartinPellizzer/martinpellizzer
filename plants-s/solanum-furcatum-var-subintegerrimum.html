Solanum furcatum var. subintegerrimum

Solanum furcatum var. subintegerrimum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved nightshade, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along its stems. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into spherical, yellowish berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland or rocky habitats.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
