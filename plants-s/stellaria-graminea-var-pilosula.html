Stellaria graminea var. pilosula

Stellaria graminea var. pilosula, commonly known as silky starwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to July. The leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, giving the plant a distinctive silvery appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.

This variety is often found in damp meadows and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
