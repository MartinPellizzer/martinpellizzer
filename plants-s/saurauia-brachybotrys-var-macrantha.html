Saurauia brachybotrys var. macrantha

Saurauia brachybotrys var. macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered saurauia, is a species of flowering plant in the Ericaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form and produces small, clustered leaves. The plant's most distinguishing feature is its showy, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, typically found in mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
