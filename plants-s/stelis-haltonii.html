Stelis haltonii

Stelis haltonii, commonly known as the Halton's orchid, is a rare and delicate epiphytic orchid native to South Africa.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in rocky, well-drained environments. The plant features small, clustered pseudobulbs with narrow, linear leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale green, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

Stelis haltonii is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
