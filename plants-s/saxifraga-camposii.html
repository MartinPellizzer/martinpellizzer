Saxifraga camposii

Saxifraga camposii, commonly known as the Brazilian saxifrage, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pink, often with a subtle fragrance. Its leaves are usually succulent, adapted to thrive in rocky or arid environments.

Saxifraga camposii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand harsh conditions.
