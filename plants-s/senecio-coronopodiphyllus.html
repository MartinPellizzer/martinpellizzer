Senecio coronopodiphyllus

Senecio coronopodiphyllus, commonly known as the crownleaf senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small yellow flowers arranged in a dense, rounded cluster.
