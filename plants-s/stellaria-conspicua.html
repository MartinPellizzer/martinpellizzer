Stellaria conspicua

Stellaria conspicua, commonly known as the showy starwort, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like carnations and chickweed. The species is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are small, opposite, and often covered in fine hairs.

Stellaria conspicua typically grows in moist, shaded areas such as woodland floors and stream banks.
