Saccharum cayennense var. laxiusculum

Saccharum cayennense var. laxiusculum, commonly known as the dwarf sugarcane.

This variant is a grass species native to tropical regions, characterized by its shorter stature compared to other sugarcane varieties. It features tall, cylindrical stems covered in dense, fibrous leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that are typically inconspicuous.

It is adapted to grow in wet, swampy environments and is often used in ecological restoration projects.
