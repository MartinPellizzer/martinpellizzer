Stapelia × mutabilis var. discolor

Stapelia × mutabilis var. discolor, commonly known as the variegated starfish plant, is a hybrid succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems that are often variegated with yellow and green hues, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces large, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, resembling the texture of a starfish. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
