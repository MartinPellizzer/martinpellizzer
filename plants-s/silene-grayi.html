Silene grayi

Silene grayi, commonly known as Gray's catchfly, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Silene grayi is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.
