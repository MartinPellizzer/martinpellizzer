Sceptridium daucifolium var. kanaii

Sceptridium daucifolium var. kanaii, commonly known as the Kanai's goldenrod, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and streamside areas. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native habitat.
