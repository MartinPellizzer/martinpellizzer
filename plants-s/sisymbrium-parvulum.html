Sisymbrium parvulum

Sisymbrium parvulum, commonly known as dwarf shepherd's purse, is an annual flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant features small, triangular seed pods that are characteristic of the genus Sisymbrium. It typically grows in disturbed soils and thrives in sunny, open habitats.

Its botanical features make it a common weed in agricultural fields and gardens.
