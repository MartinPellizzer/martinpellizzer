Sterculia roxburghii

Sterculia roxburghii, commonly known as the Indian chestnut or Roxburgh's sterculia, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers that are typically pink or white in color. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release smooth, chestnut-like seeds.
