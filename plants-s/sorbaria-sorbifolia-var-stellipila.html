Sorbaria sorbifolia var. stellipila

Sorbaria sorbifolia var. stellipila, commonly known as the star-leaved whitebeam, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, particularly China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped leaf lobes. The plant produces small white flowers that appear in clusters, typically in early summer. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a serrated margin.

It is often found in rocky or woodland habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
