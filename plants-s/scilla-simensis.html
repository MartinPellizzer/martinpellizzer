Scilla simensis

Scilla simensis, commonly known as the Ethiopian squill, is a perennial bulbous plant native to the highland regions of Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and clusters of bell-shaped blue or violet flowers. Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in plant morphology and ecological adaptation.
