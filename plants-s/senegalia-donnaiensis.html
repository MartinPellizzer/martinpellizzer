Senegalia donnaiensis

Senegalia donnaiensis, commonly known as the Donnaiensis acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow or greenish-yellow flowers. The plant is well adapted to dry environments, with deep roots that help it access groundwater.

It plays an important ecological role by fixing nitrogen in the soil, supporting local biodiversity.
