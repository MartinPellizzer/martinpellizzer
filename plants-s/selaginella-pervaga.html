Selaginella pervaga

Selaginella pervaga, commonly known as the zigzag moss or stair-step spikemoss, is a species of spikemoss belonging to the family Selaginellaceae.

It is a small, herbaceous plant that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. The plant has a distinctive appearance with its spirally arranged leaves and branching stems that resemble stair steps. Selaginella pervaga is notable for its ability to survive in low-light conditions and its capacity for vegetative propagation through spores and rhizomes.

This plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
