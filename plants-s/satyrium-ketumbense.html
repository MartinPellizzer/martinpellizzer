Satyrium ketumbense

Satyrium ketumbense, commonly known as the African orchid, is a species of orchid native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, intricate floral structure and vibrant coloration, which attract specific pollinators. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers exhibit a complex morphology with specialized adaptations for pollination.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is noted for its ornamental value and ecological significance.
