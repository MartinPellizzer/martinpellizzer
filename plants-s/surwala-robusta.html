Surwala robusta

Surwala robusta, commonly known as the Indian chestnut, is a deciduous tree native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the family Fagaceae and is characterized by its thick, leathery leaves and smooth, grey bark. Its seeds are an important food source for various wildlife species.
