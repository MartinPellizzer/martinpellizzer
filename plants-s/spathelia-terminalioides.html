Spathelia terminalioides

Spathelia terminalioides, commonly known as the tropical orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant blooms. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with a unique spathelate structure that resembles a spatula.

Spathelia terminalioides requires high humidity and indirect light to thrive, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
