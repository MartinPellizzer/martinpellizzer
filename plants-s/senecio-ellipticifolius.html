Senecio ellipticifolius

Senecio ellipticifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaf Senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and showy inflorescences. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and elliptical, with a distinctive silver or grayish hue due to dense pubescence.

Senecio ellipticifolius is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
