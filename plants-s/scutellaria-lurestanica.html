Scutellaria lurestanica

Scutellaria lurestanica, commonly known as the Lorestan skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Iran.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, two-lipped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in rocky and well-drained soils, often found in alpine and subalpine habitats.

Scutellaria lurestanica is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
