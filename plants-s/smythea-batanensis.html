Smythea batanensis

Smythea batanensis, commonly known as the Batan Island orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant blooms. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments within tropical rainforests. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the forest floor, and it thrives in areas with high humidity and moderate temperatures.

Smythea batanensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
