Scaphosepalum swertiifolium

Scaphosepalum swertiifolium, commonly known as the Swertiifolium orchid, is a species native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its slender stems and lance-shaped leaves. The flowers are typically small and clustered, with a distinctive labellum that sets it apart from other orchid species. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
