Senna birostris var. totorae

Senna birostris var. totorae, commonly known as the African senna, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces yellow flowers in clusters. It is known for its ability to thrive in poor soils and is often used in traditional medicine.

The seeds of Senna birostris var. totorae are encased in a hard pod and contain compounds that have been studied for their medicinal properties.
