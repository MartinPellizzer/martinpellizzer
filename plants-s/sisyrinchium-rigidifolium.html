Sisyrinchium rigidifolium

Sisyrinchium rigidifolium, commonly known as the rigidleaf blue-eyed grass, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Iridaceae family, which includes irises and related plants. The plant features narrow, rigid leaves and produces small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open meadows and rocky areas.
