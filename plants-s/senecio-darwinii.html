Senecio darwinii

Senecio darwinii, commonly known as Darwin's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and grows in rocky or sandy soils. It is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to dry, sunny conditions.

Senecio darwinii is a perennial herb that can reach heights of up to 1 meter.
