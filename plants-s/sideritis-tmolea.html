Sideritis tmolea

Sideritis tmolea, commonly known as mountain tea, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Sideritis tmolea is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
