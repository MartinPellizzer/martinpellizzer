Silene chungtienensis

Silene chungtienensis, commonly known as the Chungtien Catchfly, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its slender stems and small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is found in mountainous regions. It has hairy leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small seeds.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
