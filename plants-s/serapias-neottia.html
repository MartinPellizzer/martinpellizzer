Serapias neottia

Serapias neottia, commonly known as the Neottian orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Orchidaceae.

It is characterized by its striking, brightly colored flowers that typically range from yellow to orange, with intricate patterns resembling a face. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it thrives in rocky or limestone soils. Serapias neottia is native to the Mediterranean region and is often found in sunny, well-drained habitats.

Its unique floral morphology and ecological adaptations make it a notable species in botanical studies.
