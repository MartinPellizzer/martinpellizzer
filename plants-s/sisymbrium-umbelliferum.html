Sisymbrium umbelliferum

Sisymbrium umbelliferum, commonly known as the field pennycress, is an annual flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant features small, yellow flowers arranged in an umbel, giving it its species name. Its leaves are pinnately divided, and it produces slender, hairy stems.

It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats.
