Stemodia verticillaris

Stemodia verticillaris, commonly known as the spiral stemplant, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, spirally arranged stems that give it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Stemodia verticillaris is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
