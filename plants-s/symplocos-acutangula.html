Symplocos acutangula

Symplocos acutangula, commonly known as the sharp-leaved inkberry, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its elliptical, glossy, dark green leaves that are sharply pointed. Symplocos acutangula is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
