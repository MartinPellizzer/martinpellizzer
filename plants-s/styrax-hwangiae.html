Styrax hwangiae

Styrax hwangiae, commonly known as the Hwang's styrax, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Styracaceae and is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, smooth, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. Its bark is smooth and reddish-brown, and it is often used in traditional medicine for its aromatic properties.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
