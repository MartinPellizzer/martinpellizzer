Stelis divaricans

Stelis divaricans, commonly known as the variable orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on the surface of other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant features a slender, branching stem with numerous small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive, fragrant scent.

Stelis divaricans is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
