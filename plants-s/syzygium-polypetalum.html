Syzygium polypetalum

Syzygium polypetalum, commonly known as the rose apple or Java apple, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, white or pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, round fruits with a sweet and acidic flavor, often used in culinary applications. Its leaves are elliptical, glossy, and arranged alternately along the stems.

Syzygium polypetalum is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its edible fruits and medicinal properties.
