Sedum lampusae var. microstachyum

Sedum lampusae var. microstachyum, commonly known as the dwarf stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tint. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange, often blooming in late summer. It is well adapted to dry conditions, storing water in its leaves and stems.

This variety is prized for its low maintenance and ornamental value in rock gardens and xeriscapes.
