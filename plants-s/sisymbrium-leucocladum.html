Sisymbrium leucocladum

Sisymbrium leucocladum, commonly known as white bladderpod, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants, and is characterized by its small, white flowers and pinnately compound leaves. It thrives in dry, disturbed soils and is often found in grasslands and along roadsides.
