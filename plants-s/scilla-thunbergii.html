Scilla thunbergii

Scilla thunbergii, commonly known as the Turk's cap lily, is a perennial bulbous plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It produces clusters of bell-shaped, blue or violet flowers in early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge from a bulbous base. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Scilla thunbergii is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and early blooming habit.
