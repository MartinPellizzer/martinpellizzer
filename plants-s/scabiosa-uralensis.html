Scabiosa uralensis

Scabiosa uralensis, commonly known as the Siberian scabious, is a perennial herb native to the colder regions of Eurasia.

It features upright stems covered in soft, silvery-green foliage. The plant produces clusters of pink or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its flowers are composed of numerous small petals arranged in a spherical shape, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Scabiosa uralensis is valued in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
