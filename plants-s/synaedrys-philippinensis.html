Synaedrys philippinensis

Synaedrys philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine star orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its flowers are often brightly colored, with a unique arrangement of petals and sepals that resemble a star.

Synaedrys philippinensis plays an important role in its ecosystem, providing nectar for pollinators and contributing to the biodiversity of its native habitat.
