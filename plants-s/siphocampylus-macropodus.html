Siphocampylus macropodus

Siphocampylus macropodus, commonly known as the giant water plantain, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland habitats in North America.

It features broad, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically submerged or floating, with a waxy surface to reduce water loss.
