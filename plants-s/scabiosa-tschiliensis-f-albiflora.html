Scabiosa tschiliensis f. albiflora

Scabiosa tschiliensis f. albiflora, commonly known as the whiteflower scabious, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caprifoliaceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant features finely divided, fern-like leaves that are often grayish-green in color. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Its delicate appearance makes it a popular choice for garden borders and cottage gardens.
