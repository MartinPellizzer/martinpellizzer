Seriphidium oranense

Seriphidium oranense, commonly known as the Oran sunflower, is a flowering plant native to North Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, branching stems and yellow flower heads. The plant produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky soils.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and potential agricultural applications.
