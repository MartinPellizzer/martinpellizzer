Scindapsus altissimus

Scindapsus altissimus, commonly known as the silver vine, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often marked with silvery or variegated patterns. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red berries, though it is primarily cultivated for its ornamental foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Scindapsus altissimus is a popular houseplant due to its ease of care and aesthetic appeal.
