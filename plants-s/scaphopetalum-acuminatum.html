Scaphopetalum acuminatum

Scaphopetalum acuminatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rockrose, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, which bloom in spring. The plant has small, narrow leaves that are covered in fine hairs, adapting to its arid environment. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens.

The plant's structure and appearance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and ornamental gardening.
