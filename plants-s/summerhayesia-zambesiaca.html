Summerhayesia zambesiaca

Summerhayesia zambesiaca, commonly known as the Zambesi grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open grasslands and along riverbanks, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant features erect, fibrous stems that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a prominent midrib, and it produces small, clustered flowers in late summer.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
