Sphaeropteris atrox var. inermis

Sphaeropteris atrox var. inermis, commonly known as the broad-leafed cycad, is a variant of the Sphaeropteris atrox species.

It is a member of the Cycadaceae family and is characterized by its large, robust, and often broad, leathery leaves. The plant is native to Southeast Asia and is typically found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its foliage is usually dark green and can grow to significant lengths, making it a striking addition to botanical gardens.

The plant produces cones rather than flowers, which are essential for its reproductive process.
