Seseli teres

Seseli teres, commonly known as the narrow-leaved seseli, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, greenish-white flowers that appear in umbels. The plant has finely divided, aromatic leaves that are often used in traditional herbal medicine. It thrives in rocky and dry environments, typically found in mountainous regions.
