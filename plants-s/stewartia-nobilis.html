Stewartia nobilis

Stewartia nobilis, commonly known as the noble stewartia, is a deciduous flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, urn-shaped flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant produces small, dark purple fruits that contain winged seeds. Its leaves are broad, heart-shaped, and turn vibrant yellow in autumn.

Stewartia nobilis is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
