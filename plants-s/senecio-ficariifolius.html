Senecio ficariifolius

Senecio ficariifolius, commonly known as the Ficaria leaf senecio, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, fleshy, spoon-shaped leaves that are often tinged with purple or red, especially in full sun. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
