Stelis palimmeces

Stelis palimmeces, commonly known as the orchid of the genus Stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually fleshy and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in moisture retention.

The flowers are typically small and clustered, displaying a variety of colors, often with intricate patterns.
