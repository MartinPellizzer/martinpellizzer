Spiraea henryi magnifica

Spiraea henryi magnifica, commonly known as the magnifica spiraea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features upright stems with dense clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has dark green, ovate leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sun, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and seasonal color changes.
