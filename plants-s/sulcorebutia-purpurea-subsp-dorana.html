Sulcorebutia purpurea subsp. dorana

Sulcorebutia purpurea subsp.

dorana, commonly known as the Dorana stonecress, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of South America. It features compact, low-growing rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often tinged with purple or red.

The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the growing season. It is well adapted to dry conditions, storing water in its thick leaves and stems.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation in rock gardens or as a container plant.
