Strychnos emarginata

Strychnos emarginata, commonly known as the African strychnos, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant produces yellowish flowers and small, red, fleshy fruits that contain seeds. Its leaves are simple and alternate, often with a serrated edge.

Strychnos emarginata is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its attractive appearance.
