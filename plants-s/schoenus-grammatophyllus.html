Schoenus grammatophyllus

Schoenus grammatophyllus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bulbous grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bulbous base and tufted foliage that resembles grass. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in dense spikes, typically during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental appeal and hardy nature, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
