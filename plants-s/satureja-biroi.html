Satureja biroi

Satureja biroi, commonly known as the Albanian thyme, is a perennial herb native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

Satureja biroi is valued for its aromatic leaves, which are used in traditional cuisine and herbal medicine.
