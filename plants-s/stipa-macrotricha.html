Stipa macrotricha

Stipa macrotricha, commonly known as the giant feather grass, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and feathery, silver-gray inflorescences that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine and subalpine environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue that helps it retain moisture in arid conditions.

Stipa macrotricha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique visual characteristics.
