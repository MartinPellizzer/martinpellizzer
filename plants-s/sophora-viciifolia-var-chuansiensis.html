Sophora viciifolia var. chuansiensis

Sophora viciifolia var. chuansiensis, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a leguminous plant native to China.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces elongated pods containing edible seeds, which are often used in traditional Chinese cuisine. It thrives in arid and semi-arid regions, demonstrating adaptability to poor soil conditions.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological restoration and agricultural use.
