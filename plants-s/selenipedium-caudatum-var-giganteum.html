Selenipedium caudatum var. giganteum

Selenipedium caudatum var. giganteum, commonly known as the giant selenipedium, is a striking member of the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescence, which consists of a spadix surrounded by a broad, colorful spathe. Native to the tropical forests of Central and South America, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are typically broad and strap-like, contributing to its dramatic appearance.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is a prized but challenging plant for cultivation.
