Sesleria phleoides var. orbelica

Sesleria phleoides var. orbelica, commonly known as the Orbelica sedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bright green and have a slightly rough texture. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, white to pale blue flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or swampy areas.

Due to its ornamental appeal, it is cultivated in gardens and conservation areas for its unique foliage and ecological value.
