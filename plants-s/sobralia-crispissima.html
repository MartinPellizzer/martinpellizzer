Sobralia crispissima

Sobralia crispissima, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, velvety purple flowers. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous small, fragrant blossoms that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Sobralia crispissima is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
