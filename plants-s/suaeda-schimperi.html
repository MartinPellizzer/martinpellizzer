Suaeda schimperi

Suaeda schimperi, commonly known as the Ethiopian sea blush, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Ethiopia.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and small, pinkish flowers. Suaeda schimperi plays a role in stabilizing soils in its native habitat and is of interest for its potential in phytoremediation.
