Specklinia condylata

Specklinia condylata, commonly known as the crested polypody, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries such as Indonesia, Malaysia, and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and distinctive, crested fronds that give it a unique appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on rocks or tree trunks.

Specklinia condylata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated as a houseplant.
