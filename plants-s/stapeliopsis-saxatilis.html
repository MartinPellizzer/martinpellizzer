Stapeliopsis saxatilis

Stapeliopsis saxatilis, commonly known as the rock stapeliopsis, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
