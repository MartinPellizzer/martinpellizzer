Senecio tanacetopsis

Senecio tanacetopsis, commonly known as the blue-eyed daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is often grown as an ornamental due to its attractive appearance. Senecio tanacetopsis is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as butterflies and bees.
