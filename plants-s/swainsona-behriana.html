Swainsona behriana

Swainsona behriana, commonly known as the red sandalwood, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces striking yellow flowers with purple veins, which bloom in summer. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

The wood of Swainsona behriana is highly valued for its durability and rich reddish color.
