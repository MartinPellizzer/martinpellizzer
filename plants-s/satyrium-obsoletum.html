Satyrium obsoletum

Satyrium obsoletum, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to the eastern United States.

It is a small, herbaceous plant that typically grows in moist, shaded forest understories. The plant has delicate, greenish-white flowers with a single lip that is often marked with purple or brown spots. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the rich, organic soil.

Satyrium obsoletum is characterized by its simple, linear leaves and its preference for humid, temperate climates.
