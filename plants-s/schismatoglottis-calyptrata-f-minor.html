Schismatoglottis calyptrata f. minor

Schismatoglottis calyptrata f. minor, commonly known as the dwarf schismatoglottis, is a rare and compact member of the Orchidaceae family.

It originates from the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features small, elongated leaves with a waxy texture and produces delicate, spider-like flowers in shades of white and pale pink. It is a low-maintenance plant that requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and botanists alike.
