Sickingia mollis

Sickingia mollis, commonly known as the velvet plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate sunlight.

Sickingia mollis is often used in rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact growth habit.
