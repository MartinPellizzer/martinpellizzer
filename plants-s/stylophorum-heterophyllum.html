Stylophorum heterophyllum

Stylophorum heterophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved cowslip, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, yellowish flowers that bloom in early spring. Stylophorum heterophyllum is often found in moist, acidic soils and is a common sight in the understory of deciduous forests.
