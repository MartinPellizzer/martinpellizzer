Spermadictyon suaveolens

Spermadictyon suaveolens, commonly known as the sweet-smelling starfruit plant, is a tropical species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped fruits, which are typically yellow when ripe and have a fragrant, mildly sweet aroma. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, and its leaves are broad and ovate with a smooth surface. The fruit is edible, though its texture can be fibrous, and it is often used in local cuisines.
