Soulangia microphylla

Soulangia microphylla, commonly known as the dwarf hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardens and tropical landscapes.
