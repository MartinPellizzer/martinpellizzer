Selenipedium nitidissimum var. clonius

Selenipedium nitidissimum var. clonius, commonly known as the white-veined selenipedium, is a rare and striking member of the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescence with a single, elongated spadix surrounded by a broad, white spathe. Native to the cloud forests of Central America, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and leathery, often with a distinctive white or pale green veining.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is highly sought after by botanists and conservationists.
