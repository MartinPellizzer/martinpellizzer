Schefflera poilaneana

Schefflera poilaneana, commonly known as the Madagascar schefflera, is a tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of leaves, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant features compound leaves with numerous small, elongated leaflets. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Schefflera poilaneana is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its lush foliage and adaptability to indoor conditions.
