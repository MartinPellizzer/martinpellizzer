Stipa bomanii

Stipa bomanii, commonly known as Boman's feathergrass, is a perennial grass native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and fine, silver-green foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are often adorned with silky, feathery bristles. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as steppes and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils.

Stipa bomanii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its graceful, wind-swaying form.
