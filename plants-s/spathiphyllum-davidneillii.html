Spathiphyllum davidneillii

Spathiphyllum davidneillii, commonly known as the David Neill's peace lily, is a tropical evergreen plant native to the Philippines.

It features broad, dark green, heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and slightly wavy. The plant produces striking white spathes that enclose small, greenish-yellow flowers. It thrives in low-light conditions and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Spathiphyllum davidneillii is popular as a houseplant due to its air-purifying qualities and ease of care.
