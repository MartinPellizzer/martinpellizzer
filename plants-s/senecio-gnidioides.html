Senecio gnidioides

Senecio gnidioides, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are usually narrow and lance-shaped, with a rough texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.
