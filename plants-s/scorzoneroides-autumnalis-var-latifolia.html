Scorzoneroides autumnalis var. latifolia

Scorzoneroides autumnalis var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf autumn scorzoneroides, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features basal rosettes of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. The plant produces tall, branching stems that bear small, pinkish-purple flowers in the autumn season. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value lies in its striking foliage and seasonal blooms.
