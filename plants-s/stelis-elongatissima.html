Stelis elongatissima

Stelis elongatissima, commonly known as the slender stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of delicate, white or pale green flowers that bloom in the summer. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Stelis elongatissima is often grown as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
