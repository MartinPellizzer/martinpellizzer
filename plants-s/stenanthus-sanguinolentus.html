Stenanthus sanguinolentus

Stenanthus sanguinolentus, commonly known as the crimson stenanthus, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, wooded areas and thrives in partial to full shade. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a slightly serrated edge.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
