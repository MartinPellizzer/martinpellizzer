Sarothamnus Multiflorus

Sarothamnus thyme, scientifically known as Sarothamnus multiflorus, is a hardy shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It is prized for its aromatic leaves and clusters of small, vibrant flowers. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. Its ability to withstand drought conditions makes it ideal for xeriscaping and low-maintenance landscapes. With its fragrant foliage and ornamental value, Sarothamnus multiflorus is both a practical and beautiful addition to any garden.
