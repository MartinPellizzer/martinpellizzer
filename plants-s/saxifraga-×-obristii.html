Saxifraga × obristii

Saxifraga × obristii, commonly known as the hybrid saxifrage, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It typically forms dense mats of foliage with rounded, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers during the late spring to early summer. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine environments.

Saxifraga × obristii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
