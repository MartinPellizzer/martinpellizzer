Stellaria media subsp. alsinoides

Stellaria media subsp.

alsinoides, commonly known as lesser starwort, is a low-growing annual plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It features small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming from late spring to early autumn.

The leaves are narrow, linear, and often hairy, with a distinctive appearance that resembles a star when viewed from above. It thrives in disturbed soils and can be found in gardens, fields, and along roadsides.

This subspecies is closely related to other members of the Stellaria genus, sharing similar botanical characteristics.
