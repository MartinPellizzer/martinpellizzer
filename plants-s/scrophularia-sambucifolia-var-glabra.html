Scrophularia sambucifolia var. glabra

Scrophularia sambucifolia var. glabra, commonly known as smooth leaf dog's mercury, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae and is characterized by its smooth, oval-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides. It has a fibrous root system and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds.

This variety is distinguished by its lack of pubescence on the leaves, giving it a smooth appearance.
