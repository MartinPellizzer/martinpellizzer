Sorghum saccharatum var. pulchrum

Sorghum saccharatum var. pulchrum, commonly known as the ornamental sorghum, is a striking plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stalks and vibrant, showy inflorescences that range in color from red to purple. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Its foliage is broad and lance-shaped, adding texture to garden landscapes.

This variety is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
