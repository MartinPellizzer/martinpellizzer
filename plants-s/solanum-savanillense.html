Solanum savanillense

Solanum savanillense, commonly known as the savanilla nightshade, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is native to Central and South America, particularly found in regions with tropical and subtropical climates. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate in shape, with a slightly hairy texture, and it produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into berries.

The berries are usually yellow or orange when mature and contain several seeds.
