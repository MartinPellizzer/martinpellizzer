Stelis microstigma

Stelis microstigma, commonly known as the miniature-stigma orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a small epiphytic orchid native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia. The plant typically grows on tree branches and derives its nutrients from the air and rain. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and often have a delicate white or pale yellow color.

It is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
