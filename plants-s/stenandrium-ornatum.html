Stenandrium ornatum

Stenandrium ornatum, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in dense clusters, creating a dramatic visual display. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant floral display. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Stenandrium ornatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a landscape plant.
