Solanum feuillei

Solanum feuillei, commonly known as the Feuille's nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that are typically green when mature and turn red or yellow when ripe.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
