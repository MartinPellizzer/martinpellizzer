Saxifraga cespitosa var. glabrata

Saxifraga cespitosa var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth saxifrage, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the northern hemisphere.

It typically thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine regions. The plant features compact rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves that are covered in a fine, powdery bloom. It produces small, white or pink flowers in late spring to early summer.

This variety is notable for its hardy nature and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
