Strychnos beddomei

Strychnos beddomei, commonly known as the beddome's strychnos, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by orange-red berries containing seeds. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a smooth texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
