Specklinia acicularis

Specklinia acicularis, commonly known as the needle-leaf lithops, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its small, fleshy, greenish-gray leaves that are shaped like needles, which help it conserve water in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal watering, making it ideal for rock gardens or as a houseplant.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
