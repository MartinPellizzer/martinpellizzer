Semenovia malcolmii

Semenovia malcolmii, commonly known as the Malcolm's daisy, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of South Africa.

Semenovia malcolmii is often cultivated in rock gardens and dry landscapes due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
