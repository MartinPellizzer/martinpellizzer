Stenanona

Stenanona, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Bignoniaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fiery orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and can grow as a tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its bark is smooth and gray, with a distinctive aromatic scent.
