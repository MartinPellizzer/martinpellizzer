Sesbania concolor

Sesbania concolor, commonly known as the white sesban, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a legume with compound leaves and produces yellow or white flowers. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stem and is often found in disturbed soils. It has small, elongated pods that contain several seeds.

Sesbania concolor is valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it useful in agricultural settings.
