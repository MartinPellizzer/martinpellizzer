Stellaria tetrandra

Stellaria tetrandra, commonly known as starwort, is a small flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is native to parts of Europe and Asia and typically grows in moist, grassy areas. The plant features delicate, star-shaped white or pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Stellaria tetrandra is often found in meadows and along stream banks, where it thrives in partial shade to full sun.
