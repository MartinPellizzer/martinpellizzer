Stellaria uchiyamana

Stellaria uchiyamana, commonly known as the Japanese starwort, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, star-shaped white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs.

Stellaria uchiyamana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
