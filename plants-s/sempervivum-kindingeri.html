Sempervivum kindingeri

Sempervivum kindingeri, commonly known as the Alpine houseleek, is a succulent plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It features rosettes of fleshy, pointed leaves that are typically blue-green in color, often with a bluish or purplish hue. The plant is well adapted to harsh, rocky environments and can thrive in full sun. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months.

Sempervivum kindingeri is a popular choice for rock gardens and containers due to its hardy nature and attractive appearance.
