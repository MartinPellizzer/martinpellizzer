Struthiola lateriflora

Struthiola lateriflora, commonly known as the side-flowered struthiola, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are narrow and grayish-green, often covered in a fine layer of hair.

Struthiola lateriflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
