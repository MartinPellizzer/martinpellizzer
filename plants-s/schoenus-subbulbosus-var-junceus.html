Schoenus subbulbosus var. junceus

Schoenus subbulbosus var. junceus, commonly known as the slender bulrush, is a perennial grass-like plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in wetland habitats such as marshes and along the edges of ponds. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikelets that are often green or brown in color. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in shallow water or moist soil.

This variety is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing wetland ecosystems.
