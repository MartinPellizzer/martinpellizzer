Scutellaria euboea

Scutellaria euboea, commonly known as Euboean skullcap, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Aegean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It is characterized by its distinctive calyx that resembles a skullcap, giving it its common name.

Scutellaria euboea is often found in rocky or sandy soils in open habitats.
