Swertia javanica subsp. sumatrensis

Swertia javanica subsp.

sumatrensis, commonly known as the Sumatran swertia, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia. It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its slender, branched stems and opposite leaves.

The plant produces small, blue to purple flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its medicinal properties have been studied for potential use in traditional medicine.
