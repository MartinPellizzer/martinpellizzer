Setaria speciosa

Setaria speciosa, commonly known as the ornamental dropseed, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, arching stems and delicate, silver-green foliage that adds visual interest to gardens. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are often adorned with fine, hair-like structures, giving it a unique appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full to partial sunlight, making it a versatile choice for landscaping.

Due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements, Setaria speciosa is a popular ornamental grass among gardeners.
