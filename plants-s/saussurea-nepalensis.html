Saussurea nepalensis

Saussurea nepalensis, commonly known as the Nepal fleur-de-lis, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stem and large, showy composite flower heads. The plant produces yellowish to brownish flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in alpine and subalpine environments, often growing at high elevations.

The plant has medicinal uses in traditional systems of medicine and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
