Senecio anthemoides

Senecio anthemoides, commonly known as the Cape marigold, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or subshrub, with narrow, linear leaves and a compact growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Senecio anthemoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its bright blooms.
