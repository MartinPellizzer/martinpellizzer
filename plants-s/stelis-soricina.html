Stelis soricina

Stelis soricina, commonly known as the orchid with silver-blue flowers, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and typically grows as an epiphyte on trees in tropical rainforests. The plant features a slender, branching stem with small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are clustered in inflorescences and are characterized by their pale blue or white color with a prominent yellow lip.

Stelis soricina is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
