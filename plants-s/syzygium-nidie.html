Syzygium nidie

Syzygium nidie, commonly known as the white flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has simple, opposite, and elliptic leaves that are glossy and dark green. Its fruit is a small, round capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Syzygium nidie is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to warm, humid climates.
