Stanhopea fonsecae

Stanhopea fonsecae, commonly known as the Fonseca's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Costa Rica.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, yellow and orange petals, often with a distinctive red lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Stanhopea fonsecae is a prized species among orchid enthusiasts due to its unique beauty and rarity.
