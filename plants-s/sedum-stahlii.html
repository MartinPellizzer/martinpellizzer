Sedum stahlii

Sedum stahlii, commonly known as the Stahl's stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, which help it store water and survive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and low maintenance requirements make it an attractive addition to succulent collections.
