Sonchus shzucinianus

Sonchus shzucinianus, commonly known as the Chinese dandelion, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to eastern Asia and is characterized by its basal rosette of serrated leaves and bright yellow composite flowers. The plant produces a distinctive, hollow, hairless stem that supports the flowering heads. It thrives in a variety of habitats, including disturbed soils and open fields, and is often found in temperate regions.

The species is noted for its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and vegetative reproduction.
