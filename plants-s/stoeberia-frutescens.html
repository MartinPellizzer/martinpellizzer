Stoeberia frutescens

Stoeberia frutescens, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its small, tubular flowers that are typically red or orange in color. The plant features opposite leaves that are usually ovate or elliptical in shape. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Stoeberia frutescens is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and birds.
