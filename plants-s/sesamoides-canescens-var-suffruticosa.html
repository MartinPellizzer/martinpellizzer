Sesamoides canescens var. suffruticosa

Sesamoides canescens var. suffruticosa, commonly known as the silverleaf sesamoid, is a shrub-like plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its gray-green, hairy leaves and small, inconspicuous yellow flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, black seeds. It is adapted to survive in dry conditions with minimal water requirements.

Its morphology and growth habit make it suitable for use in xeriscaping and soil stabilization in harsh environments.
