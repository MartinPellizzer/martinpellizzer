Solanum sublineatum

Solanum sublineatum, commonly known as the narrowleaf nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in dry, rocky environments. The plant features small, sparsely arranged leaves that are typically narrow and pointed. Its flowers are usually pale blue or violet, with a tubular shape that attracts pollinators like bees and moths.

The fruit is a small, berry-like capsule that contains several seeds.
