Senna spiniflora

Senna spiniflora, commonly known as the spiny senna, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with spiny tips and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in savanna habitats.

Senna spiniflora is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
