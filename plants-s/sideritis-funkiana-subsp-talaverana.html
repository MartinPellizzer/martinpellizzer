Sideritis funkiana subsp. talaverana

talaverana, commonly known as the Talaveran Heath, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs.

The plant is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in rocky and calcareous soils, often found in open habitats such as grasslands and shrublands.
