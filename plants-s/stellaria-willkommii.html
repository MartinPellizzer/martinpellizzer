Stellaria willkommii

Stellaria willkommii, commonly known as starwort, is a low-growing flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, white or pink star-shaped flowers. The plant typically has a prostrate habit, with slender stems and opposite leaves that are usually lanceolate in shape. Stellaria willkommii is known for its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
