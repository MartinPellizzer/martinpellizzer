Scrophularia bourgaeana

Scrophularia bourgaeana, commonly known as the Bourgae's figwort, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in dry, sunny habitats. The plant features upright stems with opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are usually hairy. Its flowers are tubular, with two lips, and are typically pink or purple in color.

It belongs to the Scrophulariaceae family, which includes many species with medicinal and ornamental value.
