Schaefferia angustifolia

Schaefferia angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved schaefferia, is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant features narrow, strap-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. Its flowers are typically white or pale blue, arranged in umbels, and bloom in late winter to early spring.

Schaefferia angustifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens or as a container plant.
