Stachys babunensis

Stachys babunensis, commonly known as the Babun's hedge nettle, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically arranged in whorls along the stem. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized species within its native range.
