Scheelea costaricensis

Scheelea costaricensis, commonly known as the Costa Rican palm, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves. The plant typically grows in humid, lowland areas with well-drained soil. Its flowers are small and clustered, and it produces edible fruits that are consumed by various wildlife.
