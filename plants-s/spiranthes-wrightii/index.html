Spiranthes Wrightii

Spiranthes wrightii is a rare and delicate member of the orchid family known for its unique spiral-shaped flowers. Native to the western United States, it thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. This plant is often overlooked due to its subtle appearance, yet it plays an important role in local ecosystems. Its bloom period typically occurs in late spring to early summer, adding a touch of beauty to the landscape. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this species from habitat loss and environmental changes.
