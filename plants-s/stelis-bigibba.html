Stelis bigibba

Stelis bigibba, commonly known as the two-flowered stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, pale green to white flowers with a distinctive two-lipped structure. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or branches, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, arranged in a rosette pattern.

Stelis bigibba is valued in horticulture for its unique floral appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
