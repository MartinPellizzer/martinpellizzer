Sansevieria polyrhytis

Sansevieria polyrhytis, commonly known as the "Striped Snake Plant," is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with white or yellow variegation along the edges. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers on slender stalks, which are usually white or green in color. It is a low-maintenance plant that thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Sansevieria polyrhytis is also noted for its air-purifying qualities and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor environments.
