Salsola kali var. angustifolia

Salsola kali var. angustifolia, commonly known as Russian thistle or goatweed, is a flowering plant in the family Amaranthaceae.

It is characterized by its spiny, bract-like leaves and small, greenish flowers that appear in dense clusters. The plant is highly drought-tolerant and thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. It has a sprawling growth habit and often forms dense mats or colonies.

This species is considered an invasive weed in many regions due to its rapid growth and ability to spread through wind-dispersed seeds.
