Sisymbrium irio var. maximum

Sisymbrium irio var. maximum, commonly known as the giant yellow rocket, is a biennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, branched stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are pinnately divided and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.
