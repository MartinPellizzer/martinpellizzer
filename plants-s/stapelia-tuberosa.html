Stapelia tuberosa

Stapelia tuberosa, commonly known as the starfish plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are usually small and rounded, often covered in a waxy coating.

Stapelia tuberosa is known for its unique appearance and is popular among succulent enthusiasts.
