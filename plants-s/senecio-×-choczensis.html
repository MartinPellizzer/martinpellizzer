Senecio × choczensis

Senecio × choczensis, commonly known as the hybrid African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows as a perennial shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long flowering period, which can last from spring to autumn.
