Stapelia × bella

Stapelia × bella, commonly known as the hybrid starry stapelia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed stems that store water. The plant produces large, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange with a distinctive, almost waxy texture. These flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and flies.

Stapelia × bella is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
