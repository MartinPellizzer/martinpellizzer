Senecio tylodis

Senecio tylodis, commonly known as the Cape snowflake, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, white or pale blue flowers that resemble snowflakes. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. It has a compact, bushy growth habit with narrow, linear leaves.

Senecio tylodis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and drought tolerance.
