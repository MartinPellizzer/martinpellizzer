Syneilesis palmata f. subconcolor

Syneilesis palmata f. subconcolor, commonly known as the smooth-leafed palm, is a rare and distinctive variant of the genus Syneilesis.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves that are typically smooth and glabrous on both surfaces. The plant is native to specific regions in Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, tropical environments. Its inflorescences are usually clustered and bear small, inconspicuous flowers.

This form is notable for its unique leaf morphology, which sets it apart from other members of the genus.
