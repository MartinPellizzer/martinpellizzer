Stelis janetiae

Stelis janetiae, commonly known as the Janet's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant typically produces small, showy flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a faintly fragrant aroma. Its pseudobulbs are short and stout, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments with good air circulation.

Stelis janetiae is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
