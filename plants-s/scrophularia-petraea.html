Scrophularia petraea

Scrophularia petraea, commonly known as rock scrophularia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to rocky and alpine regions of Europe.

It typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as gravelly soils and limestone outcrops. The plant features upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are tubular and usually pink or purple, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems.

Scrophularia petraea is part of the Scrophulariaceae family, known for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
