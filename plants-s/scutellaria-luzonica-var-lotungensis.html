Scutellaria luzonica var. lotungensis

Scutellaria luzonica var. lotungensis, commonly known as the Luzon scutellaria, is a rare flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. The plant has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.
