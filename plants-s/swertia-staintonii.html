Swertia staintonii

Swertia staintonii, commonly known as the Stainton's gentian, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine or subalpine regions, preferring well-drained soils and full sunlight. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Swertia staintonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
