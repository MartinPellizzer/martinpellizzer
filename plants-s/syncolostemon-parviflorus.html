Syncolostemon parviflorus

Syncolostemon parviflorus, commonly known as the small-flowered syncolostemon, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features simple, alternate leaves that are typically ovate to lanceolate in shape. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Syncolostemon parviflorus is often found in forest understories and is valued for its ornamental potential in horticulture.
