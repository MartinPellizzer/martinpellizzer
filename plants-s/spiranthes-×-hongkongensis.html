Spiranthes × hongkongensis

Spiranthes × hongkongensis, commonly known as the Hong Kong twayblade, is a hybrid orchid native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and intricate, spiral-shaped flower spikes. The plant typically blooms in late summer, producing delicate white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its unique floral structure and hybrid origin make it a subject of interest in botanical studies.
