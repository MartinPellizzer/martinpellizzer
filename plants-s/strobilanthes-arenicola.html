Strobilanthes arenicola

Strobilanthes arenicola, commonly known as the sand dune flameflower, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of Southeast Asia.

It thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and is often found in dune ecosystems. The plant produces striking, tubular flowers that are typically red or orange in color, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds.
