Stelis setacea

Stelis setacea, commonly known as the hairy stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. The plant features a short, creeping stem with numerous small, clustered leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive hairy appearance on the petals and sepals.

Stelis setacea is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and unique morphology.
