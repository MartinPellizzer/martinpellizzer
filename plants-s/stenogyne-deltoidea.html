Stenogyne deltoidea

Stenogyne deltoidea, commonly known as the delta shieldflower, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive deltoid-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and streamside areas.
