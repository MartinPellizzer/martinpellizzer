Scleria angustata

Scleria angustata, commonly known as narrowleaf sedges, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, linear leaves and small, clustered flowering spikes. Scleria angustata plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
