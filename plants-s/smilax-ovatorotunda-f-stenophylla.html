Smilax ovatorotunda f. stenophylla

Smilax ovatorotunda f. stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf smilax, is a variant of the smilax genus characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves.

It is a climbing shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas. The plant features stout, spiny stems and produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in spikes. Its leaves are usually ovate to elliptic in shape, with a smooth or slightly toothed margin.

The fruit is a bright red, fleshy berry that contains several seeds, contributing to its ecological role in seed dispersal.
