Scutellaria japonica

Scutellaria japonica, commonly known as Japanese skullcap, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. It is often found in moist, shaded areas such as woodlands and stream banks.

Scutellaria japonica is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal medicine.
