Senecio ptarianus

Senecio ptarianus, commonly known as the African Daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight. It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

Senecio ptarianus is also known for its ability to propagate through seeds and vegetative means.
