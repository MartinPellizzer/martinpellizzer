Sulcorebutia frankiana var. intermedia

Sulcorebutia frankiana var. intermedia, commonly known as the Intermediate Sulcorebutia, is a compact cactus native to South America.

It features short, ribbed stems that are typically green to bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often appearing in spring. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Due to its compact size and ornamental flowers, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container cultivation.
