Stelis alta

Stelis alta, commonly known as the tall stelis orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are usually elongated and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Stelis alta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in tropical gardens.
