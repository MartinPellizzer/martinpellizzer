Sedum brachystylum

Sedum brachystylum, commonly known as the dwarf stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of North America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, enabling it to thrive in dry conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers in late spring or early summer. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it an ideal choice for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its resilience and low maintenance, Sedum brachystylum is often used in landscaping to add texture and color to drought-tolerant gardens.
