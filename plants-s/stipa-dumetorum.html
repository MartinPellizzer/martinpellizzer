Stipa dumetorum

Stipa dumetorum, commonly known as purple needlegrass, is a perennial grass native to the western United States and parts of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and distinctive purple-tinged inflorescences. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a rough, hairy texture.

Stipa dumetorum plays an important role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife in its native ecosystems.
