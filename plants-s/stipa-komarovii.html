Stipa komarovii

Stipa komarovii, commonly known as Komarov's feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted clumps and fine, silver-green foliage. The plant produces delicate, feathery inflorescences that are often used in floral arrangements. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for dry landscapes.

Stipa komarovii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native plant gardens.
