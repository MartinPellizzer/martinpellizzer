Schwannia malmeana

Schwannia malmeana, commonly known as the Malme's schwannia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllis family (Amaryllidaceae) and is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage consists of strap-like leaves that are dark green and somewhat succulent.

Schwannia malmeana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
