Scutellaria dubia

Scutellaria dubia, commonly known as the blue skullcap, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture and produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers. Scutellaria dubia is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for wetland restoration and ornamental gardening.
