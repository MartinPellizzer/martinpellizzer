Senna socotrana

Senna socotrana, commonly known as Socotra's senna, is a flowering plant native to the Arabian Peninsula, particularly the island of Socotra.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters tall, characterized by its pinnately compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showing adaptations to the harsh conditions of its native habitat.
