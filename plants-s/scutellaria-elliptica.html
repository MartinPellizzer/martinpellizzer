Scutellaria elliptica

Scutellaria elliptica, commonly known as narrowleaf skullcap, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces small, tubular blue or violet flowers. Its genus, Scutellaria, is characterized by the presence of a scutellum, a specialized structure in the flower that aids in pollination.

It is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its potential calming and anti-inflammatory properties.
