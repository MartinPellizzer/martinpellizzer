Schizonepeta botryoides

Schizonepeta botryoides, commonly known as Chinese skullcap, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and clusters of small, tubular flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded woodland areas.

Schizonepeta botryoides is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
