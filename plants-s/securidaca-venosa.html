Securidaca venosa

Securidaca venosa, commonly known as the slender senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features palmate leaves with five to seven leaflets, and its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in racemes. It produces cylindrical, woody pods that contain several seeds.

The plant is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
