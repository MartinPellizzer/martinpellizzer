Satyrium stenopetalum var. brevicalcaratum

Satyrium stenopetalum var. brevicalcaratum, commonly known as the narrow-petaled satyrium, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers with a unique structure. The plant typically grows in wetland or swampy habitats, where it thrives in moist, acidic soils. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, and the flowers are usually pale green or yellowish, with a short spur.

This variety is notable for its limited distribution and is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
