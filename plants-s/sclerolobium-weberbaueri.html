Sclerolobium weberbaueri

Sclerolobium weberbaueri, commonly known as the Weberbauer's acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, featuring deep roots and waxy foliage to conserve water.
