Swartzia macrostachya var. glabrifolia

Swartzia macrostachya var. glabrifolia, commonly known as the smooth-leaved swartzia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and showy, clustered flowers. The plant produces elongated pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a landscape plant in warm regions.
