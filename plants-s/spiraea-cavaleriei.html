Spiraea cavaleriei

Spiraea cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese spiraea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features delicate, lace-like leaves and clusters of white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It prefers well-drained soil and is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and flowers.

Botanically, it belongs to the Rosaceae family and is valued for its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.
