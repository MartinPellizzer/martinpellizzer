Scutellaria resinosa var. brevifolia

Scutellaria resinosa var. brevifolia, commonly known as the short-leaved skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and rocky habitats. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, two-lipped blue or purple flowers. Its tubular flowers are adapted for pollination by bees and other insects.

The genus Scutellaria is characterized by its unique calyx that resembles a shield, giving the plant its common name.
