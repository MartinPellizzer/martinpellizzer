Saussurea simpsoniana

Saussurea simpsoniana, commonly known as Simpson's saffron.

It is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Tibetan Plateau and adjacent areas in China. The plant produces striking yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late summer. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves.
