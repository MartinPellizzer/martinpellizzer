Stelis monicae

Stelis monicae, commonly known as the Monica's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant typically produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. Its roots are adapted for aerial attachment to trees, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Stelis monicae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical botanical gardens.
