Selaginella circinalis var. aristata

Selaginella circinalis var. aristata, commonly known as the arrow fern, is a species of spore-bearing plant belonging to the family Selaginellaceae.

It is a small, evergreen fern that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features delicate, arching fronds with a distinctive arrowhead shape, giving it its common name. Its leaves are usually bright green and can be found in dense clusters, often forming a low-growing mat.

Selaginella circinalis var. aristata is known for its ability to thrive in humid conditions and is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens.
