Solanum tuberiferum

Solanum tuberiferum, commonly known as the nightshade or potato plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to South America.

It is characterized by its oval-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white or yellowish flowers. The plant produces tubers underground, which are the primary edible part and widely cultivated for food. It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other plants like tomatoes and peppers.

Solanum tuberiferum requires well-drained soil and moderate watering to thrive.
