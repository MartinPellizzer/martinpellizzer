Solanum myoxotrichum

Solanum myoxotrichum, commonly known as the hairy nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, and it produces small, yellowish flowers. Its fruits are berries that mature to a dark purple color, and they contain seeds.

Solanum myoxotrichum is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors.
