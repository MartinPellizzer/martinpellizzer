Sedum wettsteinii

Sedum wettsteinii, commonly known as the Wettstein's stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow to orange. The plant is adapted to rocky and arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves. It is often found growing in alpine meadows and on cliffs.

Sedum wettsteinii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
