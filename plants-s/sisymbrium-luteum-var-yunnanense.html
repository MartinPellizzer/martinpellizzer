Sisymbrium luteum var. yunnanense

Sisymbrium luteum var. yunnanense, commonly known as the Yunnan yellow mustard, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family and is characterized by its yellow flowers and slender, branched stems. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

It produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
