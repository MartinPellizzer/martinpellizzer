Sarracenia × swaniana

Sarracenia × swaniana, commonly known as the Swan Pitcher Plant, is a hybrid carnivorous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its striking, urn-shaped pitchers that are typically red with yellow veins, designed to lure and trap insects. The plant's leaves are modified into specialized structures that secrete digestive enzymes to break down prey. It thrives in wet, acidic soils and requires high humidity and bright light for optimal growth.

Sarracenia × swaniana is a popular choice among carnivorous plant enthusiasts due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
