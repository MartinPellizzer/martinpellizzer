Sisymbrium deltoideum

Sisymbrium deltoideum, commonly known as the deltoid shepherd's purse, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, heart-shaped pods that resemble a purse, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is native to Europe and parts of Asia. It has alternate, pinnately lobed leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters.

Sisymbrium deltoideum is often found in meadows, roadsides, and other open habitats.
