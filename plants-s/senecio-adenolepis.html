Senecio adenolepis

Senecio adenolepis, commonly known as the mountain pride or the yellow star, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellow ray florets surrounded by a ring of tubular flowers.

Senecio adenolepis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant color and drought tolerance.
