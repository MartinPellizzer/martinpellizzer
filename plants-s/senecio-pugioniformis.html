Senecio pugioniformis

Senecio pugioniformis, commonly known as the swordleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves and produces small, yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with composite flower heads. It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.

Senecio pugioniformis is often used in rock gardens due to its ornamental appearance and hardy nature.
