Saintpaulia ionantha subsp. rupicola

Saintpaulia ionantha subsp.

rupicola, commonly known as the rock Saintpaulia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of Africa. It features broad, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture.

The plant produces small, vibrant flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, often with a contrasting dark center. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a popular choice for both gardeners and plant enthusiasts.
