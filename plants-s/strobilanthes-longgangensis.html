Strobilanthes longgangensis

Strobilanthes longgangensis, commonly known as the Longgang flame flower, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking red bracts that resemble flames. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions. It produces small, tubular flowers that are pollinated by insects.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
