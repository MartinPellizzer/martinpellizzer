Sabicea gracilis var. gracilis

Sabicea gracilis var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender sabicea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, climbing habit. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Its leaves are simple and alternate, with a delicate, feathery appearance.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
