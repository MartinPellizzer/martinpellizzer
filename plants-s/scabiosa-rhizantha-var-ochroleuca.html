Scabiosa rhizantha var. ochroleuca

Scabiosa rhizantha var. ochroleuca, commonly known as the white scabious, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The leaves are lance-shaped and slightly hairy, with a distinct silvery underside. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in meadows and grasslands.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators.
