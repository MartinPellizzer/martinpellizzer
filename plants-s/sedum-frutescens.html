Sedum frutescens

Sedum frutescens, commonly known as stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and small, clustered leaves that are often gray-green in color. The plant produces star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Sedum frutescens is drought-tolerant and requires minimal maintenance once established.
