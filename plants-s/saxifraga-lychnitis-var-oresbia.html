Saxifraga lychnitis var. oresbia

Saxifraga lychnitis var. oresbia, commonly known as the Alpine saxifrage, is a perennial flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes, where it thrives in cool, moist environments. The plant features upright stems with small, clustered leaves and produces delicate, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink. It is well adapted to harsh mountain conditions, with a compact growth form that helps it resist wind and frost.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in alpine ecosystems.
