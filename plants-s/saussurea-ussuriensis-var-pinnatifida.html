Saussurea ussuriensis var. pinnatifida

Saussurea ussuriensis var. pinnatifida, commonly known as the Ussuri Himalayan yarrow, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and dense clusters of small, pale yellow to white flowers. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are pinnately arranged, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool, moist conditions.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in alpine flora studies.
