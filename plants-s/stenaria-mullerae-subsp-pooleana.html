Stenaria mullerae subsp. pooleana

Stenaria mullerae subsp.

pooleana, commonly known as the Poole's stenaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces small, daisy-like flowers with yellow central disk and white to pale pink ray florets.
