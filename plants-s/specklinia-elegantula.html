Specklinia elegantula

Specklinia elegantula, commonly known as the elegant miniature orchid, is a compact, epiphytic orchid native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features small, lance-shaped leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining potting media. It is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant.

Due to its slow growth and specific care requirements, it is considered a challenging but rewarding species for experienced growers.
