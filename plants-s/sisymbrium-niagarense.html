Sisymbrium niagarense

Sisymbrium niagarense, commonly known as the Niagaran mustard, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other mustard species. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed habitats such as stream banks and roadside ditches. It produces small, yellow flowers followed by slender, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.
