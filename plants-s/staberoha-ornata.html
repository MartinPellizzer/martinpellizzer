Staberoha ornata

Staberoha ornata, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange and yellow flowers, which bloom in clusters along its slender, branching stems. The plant has waxy, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to its colorful blooms. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Staberoha ornata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor gardens.
