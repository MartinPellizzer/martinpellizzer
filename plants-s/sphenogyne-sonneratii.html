Sphenogyne sonneratii

Sphenogyne sonneratii, commonly known as the golden thread orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, yellowish-green inflorescence and small, intricate flowers. Native to Southeast Asia, this orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments within tropical forests. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique floral structure and role in ecological systems.
