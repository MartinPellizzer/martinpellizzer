Solanum rufescens var. glabrescens

Solanum rufescens var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth red nightshade, is a shrub-like plant native to parts of South America.

It typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters and has smooth, glabrous stems and leaves. The leaves are ovate to elliptic in shape, with a slightly hairy underside in some varieties. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into red, fleshy berries.

The plant is part of the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its ornamental value and potential medicinal uses.
