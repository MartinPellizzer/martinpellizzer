Setcreasea

Setcreasea, commonly known as the "ice plant," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, water-storing leaves and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant thrives in arid environments and is adapted to store water in its thick, fleshy stems and leaves. It is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and drought tolerance.

Setcreasea is also popular as a decorative plant for its unique appearance and ability to thrive in rocky or sandy soils.
