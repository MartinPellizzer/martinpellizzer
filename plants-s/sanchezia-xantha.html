Sanchezia xantha

Sanchezia xantha, commonly known as the golden sanchezia, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad, leathery leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, yellow, and white. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white flowers that bloom in the spring. Sanchezia xantha is typically grown as a houseplant due to its preference for warm, humid environments.

Its unique appearance and air-purifying qualities make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
