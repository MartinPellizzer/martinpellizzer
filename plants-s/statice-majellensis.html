Statice majellensis

Statice majellensis, commonly known as the Mediterranean glassweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, often branched stems and small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and saline soils, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, and they bloom in late summer and early autumn.

Statice majellensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive, long-lasting blooms.
