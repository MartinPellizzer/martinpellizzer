Statice lingua

Statice lingua, commonly known as sea lavender.

It is a flowering plant native to coastal regions, characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and spikes of blue or purple flowers. The plant thrives in saline environments and is often found in dunes and salt marshes. Its flowers are typically arranged in dense clusters, and it blooms during the summer months.

Statice lingua is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in floral arrangements and as a decorative plant in gardens.
