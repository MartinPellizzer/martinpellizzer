Stachys alpina var. reinertii

Stachys alpina var. reinertii, commonly known as the Alpine betony, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces spikes of violet-blue flowers, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy perennial suitable for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms.
