Solanum madagascariense var. nitens

Solanum madagascariense var. nitens, commonly known as the Madagascan nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and tobacco. The plant is characterized by its hairy stems, oval-shaped leaves, and small, star-shaped white or purple flowers. Its fruits are typically berries that mature to a yellow or orange color.

This species is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to the unique ecological conditions of its native island.
