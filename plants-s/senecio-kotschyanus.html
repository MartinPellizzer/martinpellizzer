Senecio kotschyanus

Senecio kotschyanus, commonly known as the Kotschyan's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It features narrow, linear leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

Senecio kotschyanus is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and shrublands.
