Stachys beckeana

Stachys beckeana, commonly known as Betony or Red Stem Betony, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features slender, reddish stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are small, tubular, and typically purple or pink, arranged in whorled clusters.

Stachys beckeana is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and has been used traditionally in herbal medicine.
