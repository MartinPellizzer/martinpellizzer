Senecio rufohirsutus

Senecio rufohirsutus, commonly known as the red-hairy senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its hairy, reddish stems and fleshy, opposite leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens.
