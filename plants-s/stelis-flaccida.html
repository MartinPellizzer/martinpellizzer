Stelis flaccida

Stelis flaccida, commonly known as the limp orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, hanging stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Stelis flaccida is a popular choice for hanging baskets due to its elegant appearance and ease of cultivation.
