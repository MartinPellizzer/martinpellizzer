Solanum ovigerum var. ruber

Solanum ovigerum var. ruber, commonly known as the red devil's trumpet, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its striking red, tubular flowers that resemble small trumpets. The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that are typically red when mature. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
