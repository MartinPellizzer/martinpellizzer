Senecio filicalyculatus

Senecio filicalyculatus, commonly known as the Cape snowflake, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in clusters. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its delicate appearance and ability to thrive in well-drained soil.

Senecio filicalyculatus prefers full sun to partial shade and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.
