Stormia brasiliensis

Stormia brasiliensis, commonly known as the Brazilian storm tree, is a member of the Myrtaceae family.

It is a medium-sized evergreen tree native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil. The plant is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and lanceolate, glossy green leaves. It produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by elliptical, woody fruits.

The tree is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional medicine.
