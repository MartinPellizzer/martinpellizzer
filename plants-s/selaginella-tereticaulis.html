Selaginella tereticaulis

Selaginella tereticaulis, commonly known as the slender spike moss, is a species of spore-bearing plant in the family Selaginellaceae.

It is a small, herbaceous plant that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features a creeping stem with whorled leaves that are linear and often slightly curled. It is characterized by its ability to survive in arid conditions due to its specialized water-storing structures.

Selaginella tereticaulis plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various microorganisms.
