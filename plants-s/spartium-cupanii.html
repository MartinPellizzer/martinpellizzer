Spartium cupanii

Spartium cupanii, commonly known as needlegrass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and flowering structures. The plant features slender, needle-like leaves that are often arranged in a spiral pattern along the stems. Its small, clustered flowers are typically pink or purple, and it produces cylindrical, spiky seed pods.

Spartium cupanii is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and scrublands.
