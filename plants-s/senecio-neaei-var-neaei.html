Senecio neaei var. neaei

Senecio neaei var. neaei, commonly known as the Neaei Groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
