Salvia Sclareoides F. Lusitanica

Salvia sclareoides f. lusitanica is a rare and striking plant native to the Iberian Peninsula. Known for its vibrant purple flowers and silver-green foliage, it adds a unique touch to any garden or natural setting. This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils. It is also valued for its medicinal properties, though its use is less common today. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively obscure species among horticultural enthusiasts.
