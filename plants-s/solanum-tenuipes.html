Solanum tenuipes

Solanum tenuipes, commonly known as the slender nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale green, followed by berries that turn from green to red as they mature. Its leaves are ovate and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Solanum tenuipes belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers.
