Stachys moorei

Stachys moorei, commonly known as the Moore's hedge nettle, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic foliage. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a hairy texture and produces small, pale purple flowers in whorled clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and traditional herbal practices.
