Sekanama burkei

Sekanama burkei, commonly known as the Burkea tree, is a tropical tree native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and large, palmately compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by woody pods containing several seeds. It thrives in humid climates and is often found in forested areas.

The Burkea tree is valued for its hardy nature and is used in traditional medicine and as a source of timber.
