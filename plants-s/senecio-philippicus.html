Senecio philippicus

Senecio philippicus, commonly known as the Philippine senecio, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. It produces small, yellowish to brownish flowers that are arranged in a cluster.

Senecio philippicus is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
