Silene turbinatigemma

Silene turbinatigemma, commonly known as the turbinated bladder campion, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in alpine or arctic regions. Its leaves are linear and often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in harsh environments.

Silene turbinatigemma is a perennial species, with a rosette of basal leaves and a flowering stem that can reach up to 30 centimeters in height.
