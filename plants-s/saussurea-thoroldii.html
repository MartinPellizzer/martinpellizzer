Saussurea thoroldii

Saussurea thoroldii, commonly known as the Thorold's yomogi, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas and parts of East Asia.

The plant features a thick, fibrous root system and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a composite head. Its leaves are usually basal, with a rough, hairy texture, and the plant is known for its aromatic properties.

Saussurea thoroldii is valued in traditional medicine for its purported medicinal benefits.
