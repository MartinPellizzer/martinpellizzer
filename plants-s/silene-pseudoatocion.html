Silene pseudoatocion

Silene pseudoatocion, commonly known as the slender catchfly, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring.
