Senecio elatior

Senecio elatior, commonly known as the yellow oxeye daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers with white centers. The plant typically grows in grasslands and open areas, preferring well-drained soils. It produces small, woolly leaves and has a compact, bushy growth habit.

Senecio elatior is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and ease of care.
