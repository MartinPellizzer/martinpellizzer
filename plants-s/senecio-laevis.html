Senecio laevis

Senecio laevis, commonly known as the silverleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-white, woolly leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Senecio laevis is also known for its medicinal properties, traditionally used in herbal remedies.
