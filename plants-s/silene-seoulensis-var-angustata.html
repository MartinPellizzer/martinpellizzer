Silene seoulensis var. angustata

Silene seoulensis var. angustata, commonly known as the narrowleaf silene, is a flowering plant native to Korea.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its slender, angular stems and small, white to pinkish flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a hairy texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

This variant is notable for its distinct morphology and ecological significance in its native habitat.
