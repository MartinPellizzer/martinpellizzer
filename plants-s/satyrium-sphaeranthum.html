Satyrium sphaeranthum

Satyrium sphaeranthum, commonly known as the globe heath, is a flowering plant native to the southern regions of Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or red in color, which bloom during the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, and the plant is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Satyrium sphaeranthum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
