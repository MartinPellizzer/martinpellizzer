Senna cushina

Senna cushina, commonly known as African senna, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with small, oval leaflets and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, twisted pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Senna cushina is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
