Sarcopodium dearei

Sarcopodium dearei, commonly known as the Deare's snake plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with a prominent central vein. It is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that thrives in indirect light and well-draining soil.

Sarcopodium dearei is valued for its air-purifying properties and ability to thrive in indoor environments.
