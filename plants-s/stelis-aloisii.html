Stelis aloisii

Stelis aloisii, commonly known as the orchid of the genus Stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive lip that is often marked with purple or brown.

Stelis aloisii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in tropical gardens for its unique appearance.
