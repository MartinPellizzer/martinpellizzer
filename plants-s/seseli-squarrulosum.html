Seseli squarrulosum

Seseli squarrulosum, commonly known as rough seseli, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid conditions. The plant features hairy stems and leaves, which contribute to its rough texture. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in shades of white or pale blue.

Seseli squarrulosum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
