Strelitzia angustifolia

Strelitzia angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf bird-of-paradise, is a striking tropical plant native to South Africa.

It features vibrant orange and blue flowers that resemble a parrot's beak, making it a popular ornamental plant. The plant has long, strap-like leaves that are typically narrower than those of other Strelitzia species. It thrives in warm, sunny climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance and exotic flowers make it a favorite in gardens and floral arrangements.
