Strophocactus wittii

Strophocactus wittii, commonly known as the Witt's strophocactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are often ribbed and covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking visual contrast to its green foliage. It is a slow-growing, desert-adapted plant that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for succulent collectors and xeriscaping.
