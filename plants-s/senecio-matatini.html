Senecio matatini

Senecio matatini, commonly known as the Matatini groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Senecio matatini is often used in rock gardens due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
