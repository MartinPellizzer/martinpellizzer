Senecio grandiflorus

Senecio grandiflorus, commonly known as the grandiflorous groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its bright yellow flowers bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Senecio grandiflorus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its attractive foliage and vibrant blooms.
