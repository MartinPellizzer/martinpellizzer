Stenoptera acuta

Stenoptera acuta, commonly known as the narrow-leafed arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically narrow and pointed. Its rhizomes are used in traditional medicine and as a food source.

Stenoptera acuta is valued for its ornamental appeal and practical uses in both horticulture and agriculture.
