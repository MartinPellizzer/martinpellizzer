Stellaria holostea var. micropetala

Stellaria holostea var. micropetala, commonly known as the small-petaled starwort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a low-growing herb with slender stems and small, star-shaped flowers that typically bloom in early summer. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in alpine meadows.

This variety is noted for its delicate beauty and is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
