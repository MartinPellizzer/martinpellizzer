Sickingia maxonii

Sickingia maxonii, commonly known as the blue ghost plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, which help it store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring and summer months. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
