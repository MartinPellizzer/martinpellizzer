Stanhopea ruckeri var. speciosa

Stanhopea ruckeri var. speciosa, commonly known as the showy stanhopea, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature a vibrant pink or purple coloration. The plant produces large, fleshy pseudobulbs that store water, aiding in its survival in its natural habitat. Its flowers are borne on elongated stems and are often fragrant, attracting specific pollinators.

This orchid is appreciated by horticulturists for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
