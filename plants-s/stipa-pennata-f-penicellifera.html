Stipa pennata f. penicellifera

Stipa pennata f. penicellifera, commonly known as the narrow-leaved feathergrass, is a variant of the tall wheatgrass species.

It is a perennial grass characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces fluffy, feathery seed heads that are often used in floral arrangements. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and disturbed sites.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping and ecological restoration projects.
