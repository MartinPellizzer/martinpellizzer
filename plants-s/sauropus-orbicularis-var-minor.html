Sauropus orbicularis var. minor

Sauropus orbicularis var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf umbrella plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features dense, rounded clusters of elliptical, waxy leaves that give it a distinctive, umbrella-like appearance. The plant has a slow growth rate and is well-suited for container gardening or as a ground cover in shaded areas. It prefers moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it ideal for humid climates.

Its foliage remains lush throughout the year, offering ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor settings.
