Senecio luridus

Senecio luridus, commonly known as the yellow owl's clover, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has hairy, narrow leaves and grows in rocky or sandy soils. It is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and fynbos areas.

Senecio luridus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
