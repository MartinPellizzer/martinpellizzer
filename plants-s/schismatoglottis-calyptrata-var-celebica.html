Schismatoglottis calyptrata var. celebica

Schismatoglottis calyptrata var. celebica, commonly known as the Celebes Schismatoglottis, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Indonesia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome and produces elongated, strap-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a spike, which are typically short-lived. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, moist soil.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
