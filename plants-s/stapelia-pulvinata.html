Stapelia pulvinata

Stapelia pulvinata, commonly known as the Cape Town stapelia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces striking, lantern-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are small and often reduced to spines, a characteristic common in many stapelia species.

Stapelia pulvinata is known for its unique appearance and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
