Saintpaulia orbicularis

Saintpaulia orbicularis, commonly known as the African violet, is a tropical plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped foliage and vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. The leaves are typically velvety and dark green, with a rounded, orbicular shape that gives the plant its name. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
