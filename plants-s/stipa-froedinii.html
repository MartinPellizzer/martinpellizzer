Stipa froedinii

Stipa froedinii, commonly known as the Froedin's feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and delicate, feathery inflorescences that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue that becomes more prominent during the growing season.

Stipa froedinii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic and ecological benefits.
