Solanum marinasense var. dentifolium

Solanum marinasense var. dentifolium, commonly known as the Marinasense nightshade, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive serrated, toothed leaves. The plant produces small, typically purple or violet flowers that bloom in the evening, attracting nocturnal pollinators. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, and the plant is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its unique morphology and ecological niche, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
