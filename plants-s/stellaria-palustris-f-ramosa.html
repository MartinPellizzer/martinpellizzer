Stellaria palustris f. ramosa

Stellaria palustris f. ramosa, commonly known as the branched starwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit and numerous slender stems that often branch profusely, giving it a bushy appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Stellaria palustris f. ramosa is typically found in moist, sandy soils in wetlands and along streambanks.
