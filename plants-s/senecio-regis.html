Senecio regis

Senecio regis, commonly known as the royal senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as a shrub with woody stems and produces yellow to orange flowers in clusters. Its leaves are usually narrow and slightly hairy, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

Senecio regis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its attractive foliage and blooms.
