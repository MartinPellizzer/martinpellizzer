Sasaella hidaensis var. muraii

Sasaella hidaensis var. muraii, commonly known as the Hida bamboo, is a variant of the Hida bamboo species native to Japan.

It is a grass-like plant belonging to the Poaceae family, characterized by its slender, clustered growth habit. The plant features narrow, elongated leaves with a bluish-green hue and produces small, inconspicuous flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in mountainous regions.
