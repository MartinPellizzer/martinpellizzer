Swartzia panamensis

Swartzia panamensis, commonly known as the Panamanian swartzia, is a tropical tree native to Central America.

Its leaves are compound, with small, oval-shaped leaflets, and it produces pods that contain several seeds.

Swartzia panamensis is valued for its wood, which is used in local crafts and construction.
