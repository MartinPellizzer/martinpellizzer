Scrophularia tenuipes

Scrophularia tenuipes, commonly known as the slender scrophularia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or arid soils. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. It has slender, upright stems and narrow, linear leaves.

Scrophularia tenuipes is often found in dry, sunny habitats and is adapted to survive in harsh environmental conditions.
