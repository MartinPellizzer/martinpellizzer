Sobralia valida

Sobralia valida, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety purple flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are elongated and glossy, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Sobralia valida requires bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
