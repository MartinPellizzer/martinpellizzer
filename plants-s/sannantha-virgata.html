Sannantha virgata

Sannantha virgata, commonly known as the slender sannantha, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, white or pale blue flowers arranged in a spike. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Sannantha virgata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
