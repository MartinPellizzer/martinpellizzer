Stipa metatoris

Stipa metatoris, commonly known as the slender needlegrass, is a species of grass native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial plant that typically grows in dry, open habitats such as rocky soils and disturbed areas. The plant has slender, cylindrical stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a blue-green hue that helps it conserve water.

Stipa metatoris is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in arid conditions.
