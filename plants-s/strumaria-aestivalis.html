Strumaria aestivalis

Strumaria aestivalis, commonly known as the summer bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Europe.

It produces striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to blue or violet, depending on soil pH. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette, and it thrives in well-drained, sunny to partially shaded environments. Strumaria aestivalis is known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine beds.
