Scutellaria omeiensis var. serratifolia

Scutellaria omeiensis var. serratifolia, commonly known as the serrated scutellaria, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky slopes. The plant features opposite, lanceolate leaves with serrated margins, which contribute to its distinctive appearance. Its flowers are usually blue to purple, arranged in whorled clusters, and bloom during the summer months.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
