Sedum brunfelsii

Sedum brunfelsii, commonly known as the stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features fleshy, upright stems and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow to orange. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its hardy nature allows it to survive in a variety of climates with minimal water requirements.
