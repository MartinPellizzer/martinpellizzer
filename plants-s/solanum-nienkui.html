Solanum nienkui

Solanum nienkui, commonly known as the Nienkui nightshade, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate in shape, with a glossy green surface, and it produces small, white or pale yellow flowers.

The fruit is a globose berry that turns red when mature, containing several seeds.
