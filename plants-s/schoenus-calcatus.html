Schoenus calcatus

Schoenus calcatus, commonly known as the limestone sedge, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb with narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. The plant thrives in sandy or calcareous soils, often found in coastal or inland dune environments. Its flowers are typically greenish or brownish, and it blooms during the warmer months.

Schoenus calcatus is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing sandy soils and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
