Sagraea discoidea

Sagraea discoidea, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a unique flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, spiral-shaped inflorescence that gives it its common name. The plant features elongated, narrow leaves and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pink. Sagraea discoidea is typically found growing in humid, shaded environments within the rainforest canopy.

Its botanical significance lies in its distinctive morphology and ecological role in its native habitat.
