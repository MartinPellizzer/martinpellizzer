Sciaphila monticola

Sciaphila monticola, commonly known as the mountain shade plant, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, particularly in countries like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and dark green, velvety leaves that thrive in shaded environments. Sciaphila monticola is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
