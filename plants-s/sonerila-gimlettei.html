Sonerila gimlettei

Sonerila gimlettei, commonly known as the dwarf begonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its small, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests. It has a compact growth habit, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its ornamental value lies in its intricate foliage and long-lasting blooms.
