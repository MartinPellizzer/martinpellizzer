Stenochilus alternifolius

Stenochilus alternifolius, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a member of the Acanthaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzagging stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically clustered in terminal inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
