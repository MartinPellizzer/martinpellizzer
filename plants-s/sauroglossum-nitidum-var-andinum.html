Sauroglossum nitidum var. andinum

Sauroglossum nitidum var. andinum, commonly known as the Andean velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are velvety to the touch and have a dark green hue. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often found in shaded, moist areas within its native habitat.

Its botanical features make it a unique subject for horticultural study and conservation efforts.
