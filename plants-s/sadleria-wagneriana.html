Sadleria wagneriana

Sadleria wagneriana, commonly known as Wagner's sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and grasses, and is characterized by its triangular stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in wet or moist habitats such as marshes, swamps, and along the edges of ponds. Sadleria wagneriana is also valued for its ornamental appeal in water gardens and bog landscapes.
