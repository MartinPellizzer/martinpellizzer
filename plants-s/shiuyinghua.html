Shiuyinghua

Shiuyinghua, commonly known as the "rain flower," is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks. Its leaves are broad and oval, with a smooth texture.

Shiuyinghua is valued not only for its aesthetic appeal but also for its traditional use in herbal medicine.
