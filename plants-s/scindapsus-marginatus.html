Scindapsus marginatus

Scindapsus marginatus, commonly known as the silver vine, is a tropical evergreen vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with silver and green markings. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers moist, well-draining soil. It is known for its ability to climb and can be trained to grow on trellises or shelves.

Its attractive foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and hanging baskets.
