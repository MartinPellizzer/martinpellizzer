Solanum lyratum var. filamentaceum

Solanum lyratum var. filamentaceum, commonly known as the threadleaf nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, thread-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple, typically blooming in the summer months. Its fruit is a small, fleshy berry that turns from green to yellow or red as it matures.

This species is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and interesting floral display.
