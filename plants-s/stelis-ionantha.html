Stelis ionantha

Stelis ionantha, commonly known as the Christmas orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically blooms during the winter months, which is why it is often associated with the holiday season. Its leaves are usually dark green and arranged in a rosette pattern, providing a striking contrast to its delicate blossoms.

Stelis ionantha is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
