Stylosanthes mucronata

Stylosanthes mucronata, commonly known as the mucronate stylo, is a perennial leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and trifoliate leaves with small, pointed leaflets. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by oblong, woody pods containing several seeds. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a valuable forage crop.

Stylosanthes mucronata also has the ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, improving soil fertility.
