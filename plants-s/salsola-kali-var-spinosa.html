Salsola kali var. spinosa

Salsola kali var. spinosa, commonly known as Russian thistle or goatweed, is a flowering plant in the Amaranthaceae family.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its spiny stems and small, greenish flowers. The plant is native to Eurasia but has been introduced to many parts of the world, often becoming invasive. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, preferring well-drained soils.

Its seeds are dispersed by wind, contributing to its rapid spread and ecological impact.
