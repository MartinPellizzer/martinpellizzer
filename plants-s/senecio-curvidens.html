Senecio curvidens

Senecio curvidens, commonly known as the narrow-leaved groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Senecio curvidens is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its compact growth habit and attractive foliage.
