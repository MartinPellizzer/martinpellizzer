Syrenia cana

Syrenia cana, commonly known as the white sand mat, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that thrives in sandy, well-drained soils. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

Syrenia cana is often found in coastal dunes and disturbed areas, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environmental conditions.
