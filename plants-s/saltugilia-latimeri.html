Saltugilia latimeri

Saltugilia latimeri, commonly known as the goldenbush, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has small, narrow leaves that are adapted to reduce water loss in its harsh environment. Saltugilia latimeri is often found growing in rocky soils and along coastal areas.
