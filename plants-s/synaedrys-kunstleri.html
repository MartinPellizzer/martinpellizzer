Synaedrys kunstleri

Synaedrys kunstleri, commonly known as the desert star, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It features a compact, branching structure with fleshy, spiny leaves that help it store water and reduce transpiration. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the rainy season, adding a subtle ornamental appeal. Its adaptations to harsh environments make it highly drought-resistant and suitable for xeriscaping.

Synaedrys kunstleri is valued in horticulture for its resilience and unique appearance.
