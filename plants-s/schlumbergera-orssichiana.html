Schlumbergera orssichiana

Schlumbergera orssichiana, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its segmented, leaf-like stems that form a branching structure. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, pink, and white during the holiday season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Schlumbergera orssichiana is a popular ornamental plant due to its long blooming period and adaptability to indoor conditions.
