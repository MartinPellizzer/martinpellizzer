Spergula rubra var. marcelii

Spergula rubra var. marcelii, commonly known as red sandwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features slender, prostrate stems covered with small, oval-shaped leaves that are often reddish in color. Its flowers are typically pink or red, clustered in dense inflorescences at the ends of the stems.

It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in dry, open habitats such as dunes and rocky outcrops.
