Solanum orbiculatum

Solanum orbiculatum, commonly known as the globe tomato, is a flowering plant in the nightshade family.

It is native to South America and is characterized by its round, fleshy fruits that resemble small tomatoes. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial shrub, with hairy stems and oval-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually yellow or white, and the fruit ripens to a bright red color.

Solanum orbiculatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and edible fruits.
