Stelis delicata

Stelis delicata, commonly known as the delicate stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant has slender, wiry stems and produces clusters of small, white or pale green flowers. Its leaves are narrow and strap-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Stelis delicata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
