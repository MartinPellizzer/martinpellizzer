Solanum nigrum f. humile

Solanum nigrum f. humile, commonly known as the dwarf black nightshade, is a compact variant of the common nightshade plant.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features small, dark green leaves and produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. These flowers give way to berries that start off green and mature to a deep black color.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.
