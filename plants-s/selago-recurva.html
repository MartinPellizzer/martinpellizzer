Selago recurva

Selago recurva, commonly known as the zigzag grass.

It is a flowering plant native to southern Africa, characterized by its slender, zigzagging stems and small, narrow leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale blue or white flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Selago recurva is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique growth form.
