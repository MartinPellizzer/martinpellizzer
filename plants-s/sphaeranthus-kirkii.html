Sphaeranthus kirkii

Sphaeranthus kirkii, commonly known as the Kirk's globe thistle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its spherical, spiky inflorescence that resembles a globe, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in open grasslands.

Sphaeranthus kirkii is a short-lived perennial, typically growing to a height of about 30 centimeters.
