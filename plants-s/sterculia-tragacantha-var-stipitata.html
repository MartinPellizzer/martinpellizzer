Sterculia tragacantha var. stipitata

Sterculia tragacantha var. stipitata, commonly known as the stipitate sterculia, is a tropical plant native to regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, stipitate (stalked) inflorescences and large, palmate leaves. The plant produces yellowish-white flowers that are clustered in dense, branched spikes. Its fruits are woody capsules that contain several seeds.

This species is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
