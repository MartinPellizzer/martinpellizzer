Stenorrhynchos speciosum

Stenorrhynchos speciosum, commonly known as the rainbow orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful inflorescences that display a range of hues, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowers are borne on tall, slender stems and are known for their intricate, spurred shapes.

Stenorrhynchos speciosum is a popular ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
