Stelis reflexisepala

Stelis reflexisepala, commonly known as the reflexed sepal orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure, including reflexed sepals that give it its common name. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers are usually small and fragrant, with a variety of colors ranging from white to pink and purple.

Stelis reflexisepala is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
