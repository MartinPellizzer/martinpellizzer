Specklinia lineolata

Specklinia lineolata, commonly known as the streaked orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air, making it well-suited for climbing or hanging displays.

Specklinia lineolata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
