Scutellaria bolanderi var. austromontana

Scutellaria bolanderi var. austromontana, commonly known as Bolander's blue skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
