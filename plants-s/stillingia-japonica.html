Stillingia japonica

Stillingia japonica, commonly known as Japanese stillingia, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces distinctive, elongated fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

Stillingia japonica is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
