Sisyrinchium Graminifolium Subsp. Nanum

Littleleaf blue-eyed mary, scientifically known as Sisyrinchium graminifolium subsp. nanum, is a charming member of the Campanulaceae family native to the eastern United States. This diminutive perennial is prized for its delicate blue flowers and grass-like foliage that create a striking contrast in garden settings. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade, making it an excellent choice for woodland gardens. The plant's small size and vibrant blooms make it a favorite among native plant enthusiasts and ecological gardeners. Its ability to attract pollinators and support local biodiversity further enhances its value in sustainable landscaping.
