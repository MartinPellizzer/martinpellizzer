Sanchezia megalia

Sanchezia megalia, commonly known as the giant sanchezia, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in length, often with a glossy, dark green surface. The plant produces showy, clustered inflorescences with small, white to pale yellow flowers that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

Sanchezia megalia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its dramatic foliage and unique floral display.
