Scutellaria yangbiensis

Scutellaria yangbiensis, commonly known as the Yangbi skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple, typically blooming in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Scutellaria yangbiensis is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and potential medicinal properties.
