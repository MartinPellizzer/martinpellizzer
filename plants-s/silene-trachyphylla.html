Silene trachyphylla

Silene trachyphylla, commonly known as the roughleaf catchfly, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its hairy, rough-textured leaves and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in summer.
