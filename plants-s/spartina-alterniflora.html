Spartina alterniflora

Spartina alterniflora, commonly known as smooth cordgrass, is a perennial grass native to the Atlantic coast of North America.

It thrives in brackish and saltwater environments, particularly in tidal marshes and estuaries. This plant has long, flat leaves and produces dense clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. Its rhizomes form extensive underground networks that stabilize sediments and prevent erosion.

Spartina alterniflora plays a crucial role in coastal ecosystems by providing habitat for various wildlife species.
