Scabiosa columbaria var. viduiformis

Scabiosa columbaria var. viduiformis, commonly known as the mourning scabious, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces clusters of small, pink or purple flowers. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often found in dry, rocky soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.

The species is also known for its historical use in traditional medicine.
