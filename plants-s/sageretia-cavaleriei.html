Sageretia cavaleriei

Sageretia cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese sageretia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, white to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sun. Its fruit is a follicle that contains several seeds, and it is often used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Sageretia cavaleriei is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native landscapes.
