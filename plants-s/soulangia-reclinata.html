Soulangia reclinata

Soulangia reclinata, commonly known as the hanging lily, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its pendulous, showy flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest understories. Its leaves are ovate and glossy, with a slightly hairy texture.

Soulangia reclinata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
