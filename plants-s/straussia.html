Straussia

Straussia, commonly known as the "false orchid," is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It consists of a single species, Straussia sphaerocarpa, which is native to South Africa. The plant is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that resemble those of true orchids, with a distinctive white or pale pink coloration. Straussia has a short, underground stem and produces a single, large, rounded flower at the top of a tall stem.

It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to grow in arid or semi-arid environments.
