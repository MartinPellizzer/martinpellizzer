Syzygium boulindaense

Syzygium boulindaense, commonly known as the Boulinda apple.

It is a small evergreen tree native to the rainforests of northeastern Queensland, Australia. The plant produces small, edible fruits that are typically red or yellow when ripe. Its leaves are elliptical, glossy, and have a pleasant aroma.

Syzygium boulindaense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions.
