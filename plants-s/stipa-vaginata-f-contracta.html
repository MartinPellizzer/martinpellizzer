Stipa vaginata f. contracta

Stipa vaginata f. contracta, commonly known as the compact false oat, is a compact, grass-like plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color, and it produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to yellowish flowers. The plant is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit, which helps it conserve water in harsh environments. It is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental appearance.

This variety is a hardy, drought-tolerant plant that thrives in well-drained soils.
