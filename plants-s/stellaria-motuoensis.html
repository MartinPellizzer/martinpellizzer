Stellaria motuoensis

Stellaria motuoensis, commonly known as the Motuo starwort, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of southern China.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, star-shaped white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are lanceolate and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Stellaria motuoensis is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological significance in its native habitat.
