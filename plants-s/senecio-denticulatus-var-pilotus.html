Senecio denticulatus var. pilotus

Senecio denticulatus var. pilotus, commonly known as the Pilotus Groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with narrow, grass-like leaves and small yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate appearance and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
