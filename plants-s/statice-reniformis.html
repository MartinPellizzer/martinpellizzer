Statice reniformis

Statice reniformis, commonly known as the heart-shaped statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. The leaves are narrow and grass-like, while the stems are sturdy and often used in floral arrangements. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms, which remain vibrant even when dried.
