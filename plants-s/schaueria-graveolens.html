Schaueria graveolens

Schaueria graveolens, commonly known as the stinking nightshade, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters during the cooler months. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and often have a rough texture.

The plant is known for its strong, unpleasant odor, which is most noticeable when the flowers are in bloom.
