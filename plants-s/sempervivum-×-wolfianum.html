Sempervivum × wolfianum

Sempervivum × wolfianum, commonly known as the Woolly Bear or Chocolate Soldier, is a hybrid succulent plant belonging to the Crassulaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming growth habit and thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of white, woolly hairs. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of Central and Southern Europe, where it thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, or violet during the summer months.

Sempervivum × wolfianum is a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine displays due to its hardiness and ornamental appeal.
