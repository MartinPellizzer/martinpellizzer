Sieruela oxyphylla var. robusta

Sieruela oxyphylla var. robusta, commonly known as the robust sieruela, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, leathery leaves that are typically gray-green in color, providing a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for rocky or coastal environments.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
