Stichorkis inamoena

Stichorkis inamoena, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers with a distinctive star-shaped pattern on the petals. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil efficiently.

Stichorkis inamoena is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
