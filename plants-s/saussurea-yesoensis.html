Saussurea yesoensis

Saussurea yesoensis, commonly known as the Yesso saffron.

It is a perennial herb native to the high-altitude regions of the Korean Peninsula and surrounding areas. The plant is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems and large, pale yellow to orange inflorescences. Its flowers are typically arranged in a dense, conical shape, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
