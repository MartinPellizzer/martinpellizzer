Salacia lomensis

Salacia lomensis, commonly known as the Lome's salacia, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Salaceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its fruits are fleshy berries that contain several seeds.

Salacia lomensis is often found in moist, shaded forest environments.
