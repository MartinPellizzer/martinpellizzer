Scleranthus fussii

Scleranthus fussii, commonly known as the slender sickleweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, yellow flowers and linear leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to withstand arid conditions. Scleranthus fussii has a compact growth habit with a short, upright stem.
