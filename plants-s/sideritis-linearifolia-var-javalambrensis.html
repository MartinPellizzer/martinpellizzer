Sideritis linearifolia var. javalambrensis

Sideritis linearifolia var. javalambrensis, commonly known as the Linear-leaved Sideritis, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and small, pale blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is typically found in rocky or calcareous soils, thriving in dry and sunny environments. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes other aromatic herbs such as mint and sage.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its potential health benefits.
