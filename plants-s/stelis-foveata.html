Stelis foveata

Stelis foveata, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or mountainous regions. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a few leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and purple. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil.

Stelis foveata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with high humidity and partial shade.
