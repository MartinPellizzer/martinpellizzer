Senecio albiflorus

Senecio albiflorus, commonly known as the whiteflower senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, silver-green leaves and produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in summer. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Senecio albiflorus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
