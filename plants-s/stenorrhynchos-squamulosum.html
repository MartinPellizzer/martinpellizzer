Stenorrhynchos squamulosum

Stenorrhynchos squamulosum, commonly known as the velvet leaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and attractive, velvety leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically blooming during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright light for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
