Schefflera santosii

Schefflera santosii, commonly known as the Santos' umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of large, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by black, berry-like fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
