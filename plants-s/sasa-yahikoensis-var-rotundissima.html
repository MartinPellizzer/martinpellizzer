Sasa yahikoensis var. rotundissima

Sasa yahikoensis var. rotundissima, commonly known as the round-leaved bamboo, is a variant of the Sasa yahikoensis species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its dense, clumping growth habit and culms that are typically shorter and more robust than those of other bamboo species. The leaves are broad, rounded, and have a smooth texture, often with a bluish-green hue. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in temperate climates.
