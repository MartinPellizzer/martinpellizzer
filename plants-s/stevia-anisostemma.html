Stevia anisostemma

Stevia anisostemma, commonly known as bitter leaf stevia, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its opposite, lance-shaped leaves and small yellow flowers. The plant is notable for its high concentration of steviol glycosides, which are natural sweeteners. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for cultivation in temperate climates.

Due to its medicinal properties, it is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
