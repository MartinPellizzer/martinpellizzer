Statice × frostii

Statice × frostii, commonly known as the frost statice, is a hybrid perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense clusters of small, colorful flowers that range from white to pink and purple. The plant produces numerous tiny, thread-like leaves and has a strong, fibrous root system that allows it to thrive in well-drained soils. It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.

Statice × frostii is drought-tolerant and prefers full sun, making it a hardy choice for coastal and dry garden environments.
