Sedum praesidis

Sedum praesidis, commonly known as the Praesidis stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Its leaves are typically blue-gray in color, providing a striking visual contrast to its vibrant blooms.

Sedum praesidis is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
