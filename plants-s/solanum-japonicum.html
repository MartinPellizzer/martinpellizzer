Solanum japonicum

Solanum japonicum, commonly known as Japanese nightshade, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by berries that range in color from green to red. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Solanum japonicum belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae), which includes other economically important plants such as tomatoes and potatoes.
