Stelis reflexa

Stelis reflexa, commonly known as the reflexed stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with fleshy leaves that are often recurved, giving it its common name. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, typically blooming during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Stelis reflexa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is a popular species among orchid enthusiasts.
