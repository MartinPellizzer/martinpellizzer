Saxifraga fortunei f. rubra

Saxifraga fortunei f. rubra, commonly known as the red sandwort, is a flowering plant characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit.

It features succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically reddish or pinkish in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink, often blooming in late spring to early summer. Native to rocky and alpine environments, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
