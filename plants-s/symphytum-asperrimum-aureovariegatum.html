Symphytum asperrimum aureovariegatum

Symphytum asperrimum aureovariegatum, commonly known as the variegated comfrey, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

The plant is characterized by its hairy stems and its ability to thrive in moist, well-drained soils.
