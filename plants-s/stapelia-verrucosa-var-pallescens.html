Stapelia verrucosa var. pallescens

Stapelia verrucosa var. pallescens, commonly known as the pale-stemmed stapelia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems that are typically pale green to greyish in color, which help it store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are usually yellowish-white, with a distinctive scent reminiscent of decaying meat. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a hardy choice for xerophytic gardens.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular ornamental plant among succulent enthusiasts.
