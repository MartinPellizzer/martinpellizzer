Solanum nigrum var. dillenii

Solanum nigrum var. dillenii, commonly known as the black nightshade variant, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, dark purple to black berries that mature in late summer. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is native to Europe, Asia, and parts of Africa. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

It is considered a weed in agricultural settings due to its rapid growth and ability to thrive in various conditions.
