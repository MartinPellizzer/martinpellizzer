Semilta

Semilta, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features delicate, silvery-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Semilta thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its aromatic foliage and ornamental value make it a valued addition to botanical collections.
