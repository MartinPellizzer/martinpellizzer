Stigmaphyllon albiflorum

Stigmaphyllon albiflorum, commonly known as the white-flowered stigmaphyllon, is a species of flowering plant native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It is a shrub that typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often found in coastal areas. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, which are often accompanied by modified leaves called stipules. Its leaves are simple and alternate, with a leathery texture and a dark green color.
