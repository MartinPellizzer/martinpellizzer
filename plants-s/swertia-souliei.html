Swertia souliei

Swertia souliei, commonly known as the Chinese gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or forested areas at high altitudes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a velvety texture.

Swertia souliei is valued for its ornamental beauty and has traditional uses in Chinese medicine.
