Sickingia viridiflora

Sickingia viridiflora, commonly known as the green-flowered ice plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Sickingia viridiflora is known for its resilience and ability to survive in arid conditions.
