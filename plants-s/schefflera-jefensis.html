Schefflera jefensis

Schefflera jefensis, commonly known as the Jefense Schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to specific regions in Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of leaves, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by bright red berries, attracting various bird species. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
