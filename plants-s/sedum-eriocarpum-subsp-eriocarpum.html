Sedum eriocarpum subsp. eriocarpum

Sedum eriocarpum subsp.

eriocarpum, commonly known as the woolly stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves covered in a dense layer of white, woolly hairs.

The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
