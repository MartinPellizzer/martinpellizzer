Salvia schiedeana

Salvia schiedeana, commonly known as the Mexican sage, is a perennial herb native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and a strong, minty scent. Its flowers are typically violet to purple, clustered in whorls at the ends of the stems.

Salvia schiedeana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its vibrant colors and resilience.
