Scrophularia zaravschanica

Scrophularia zaravschanica, commonly known as the Zaravshan scrophularia, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing adaptations to survive in harsh conditions. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of tubular, pink or purple flowers. It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae, which includes many species with medicinal properties.

Its botanical characteristics make it an interesting subject for ecological and phytochemical studies.
