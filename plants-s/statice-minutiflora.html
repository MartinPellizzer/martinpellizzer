Statice minutiflora

Statice minutiflora, commonly known as dwarf statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its small, showy flowers that come in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens. Its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms make it an ideal candidate for rock gardens and borders.

Statice minutiflora is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
