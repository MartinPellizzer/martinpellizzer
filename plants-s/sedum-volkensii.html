Sedum volkensii

Sedum volkensii, commonly known as the Volkens' stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems and opposite, spoon-shaped leaves that are typically green or bluish-green. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the dry season. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and arid landscapes.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping.
