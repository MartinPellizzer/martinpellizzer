Sicyos longisepalus

Sicyos longisepalus, commonly known as the long-sepal bottle gourd, is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is characterized by its elongated, cylindrical fruit that resembles a bottle, hence its common name. The plant produces large, lobed leaves and yellow, fragrant flowers that are typically unisexual. Its vines can grow up to several meters in length, making it a climbing plant.

The fruit is used in various culinary and medicinal applications in its native regions.
