Sphaerostephanos cyrtocaulos

Sphaerostephanos cyrtocaulos, commonly known as the Cape bell, is a striking flowering plant native to South Africa.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
