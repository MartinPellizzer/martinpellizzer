Selenipedium longifolium var. hartwegii

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescence that consists of a spadix surrounded by a broad, white spathe. Native to the cloud forests of Central America, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments with high air humidity. Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered an endangered species and is highly sought after by botanists and conservationists.
