Sempervivum tenuifolium

Sempervivum tenuifolium, commonly known as the hen and chicks plant, is a succulent species native to alpine regions of Europe and western Asia.

It features rosettes of fleshy, pointed leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the summer months. It is well adapted to rocky and sunny environments, thriving in well-drained soil.

Sempervivum tenuifolium is popular in rock gardens and as a decorative indoor plant due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.
