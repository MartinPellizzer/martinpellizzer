Stelis prionota

Stelis prionota, commonly known as the purple-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and slender, often hairy stems. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Stelis prionota is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
