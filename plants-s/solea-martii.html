Solea martii

Solea martii, commonly known as the Mediterranean flounder, is a flatfish native to the eastern Atlantic Ocean.

It has a distinctive elongated body with both eyes located on the left side of its head. The species typically grows to a length of about 30 centimeters and has a silvery body covered in small scales. It thrives in sandy or muddy substrates and is often found in coastal waters.

Botanically, it is classified under the family Pleuronectidae, which includes other flatfish species.
