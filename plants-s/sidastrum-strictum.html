Sidastrum strictum

Sidastrum strictum, commonly known as the narrowleaf sida, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, white or pale pink flowers. It is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.

The species is noted for its adaptability to harsh conditions and its role in supporting local pollinators.
