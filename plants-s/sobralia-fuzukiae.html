Sobralia fuzukiae

Sobralia fuzukiae, commonly known as the Fuzukiae orchid, is a species native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking, showy inflorescences with vibrant, colorful flowers. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

The flowers bloom in late spring or early summer, adding a dramatic display to gardens and greenhouses.
