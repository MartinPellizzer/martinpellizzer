Stelis infundibulosa

Stelis infundibulosa, commonly known as the hooded orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are long and strap-like, often with a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

Stelis infundibulosa is valued in horticulture for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
