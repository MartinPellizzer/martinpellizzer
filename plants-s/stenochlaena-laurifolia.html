Stenochlaena laurifolia

Stenochlaena laurifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf arrowroot, is a tropical plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its large, broad, arrow-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green in color. The plant produces striking white to yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in an inflorescence called a spadix, surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is native to regions in Southeast Asia.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
