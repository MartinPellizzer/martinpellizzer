Schoenoplectus Erectus Subsp. Raynalii

Schoenoplectus erectus subsp. raynalii is a lesser-known aquatic plant native to specific regions of southern Africa. It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes a wide variety of grass-like plants. This subspecies is distinguished by its upright growth habit and unique morphological features. It thrives in wetland environments, often found in marshes and along the edges of lakes. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
