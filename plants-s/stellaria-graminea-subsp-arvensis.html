Stellaria graminea subsp. arvensis

Stellaria graminea subsp.

arvensis, commonly known as the field starwort, is a low-growing herbaceous plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family. It is characterized by its slender, prostrate stems and small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters.

The plant typically has narrow, linear leaves arranged in opposite pairs along the stem. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia and is often found in disturbed soils such as roadsides and fields. Stellaria graminea subsp.

arvensis is a common weed in agricultural areas and can be found in various habitats throughout its native range.
