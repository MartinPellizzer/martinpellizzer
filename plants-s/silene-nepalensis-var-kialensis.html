Silene nepalensis var. kialensis

Silene nepalensis var. kialensis, commonly known as the Kiala silene, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan regions of Nepal and India.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy pink to red flowers. The plant typically thrives in rocky or alpine environments, preferring well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while its stems are usually slender and branching.

This variant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
