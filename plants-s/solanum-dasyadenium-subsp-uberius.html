Solanum dasyadenium subsp. uberius

Solanum dasyadenium subsp.

uberius, commonly known as the silver bush or silverleaf nightshade, is a member of the Solanaceae family. It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its dense, silvery foliage that reflects light.

The plant produces small, white or pale green flowers followed by berries that range in color from yellow to red. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and hardy nature.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
