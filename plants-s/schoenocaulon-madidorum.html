Schoenocaulon madidorum

Schoenocaulon madidorum, commonly known as the Madidorum stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, adapted for water storage. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its compact size and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
