Streptocarpus mahonii

Streptocarpus mahonii, commonly known as the Mahon's streptocarpus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. The plant produces violet to purple flowers that bloom in clusters, often with contrasting yellow throats. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in a fine, fuzzy texture.
