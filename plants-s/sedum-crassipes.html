Sedum crassipes

Sedum crassipes, commonly known as the broad-leaved stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in open areas with full sun exposure.

Sedum crassipes is a low-maintenance plant that is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
