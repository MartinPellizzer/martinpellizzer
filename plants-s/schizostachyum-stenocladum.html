Schizostachyum stenocladum

Schizostachyum stenocladum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved palmyra palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and pinnate leaves that are narrow and elongated. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers arranged in branched inflorescences, which are typically borne on the lower part of the stem. Its fruit is a drupe that contains a single seed, and it is adapted to grow in humid, tropical climates.

Schizostachyum stenocladum is often found in lowland areas and is used for various purposes, including construction and traditional crafts.
