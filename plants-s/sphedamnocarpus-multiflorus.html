Sphedamnocarpus multiflorus

Sphedamnocarpus multiflorus, commonly known as the many-flowered spathiphyllum, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Sphedamnocarpus multiflorus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
