Solanum album

Solanum album, commonly known as the white nightshade, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 meter. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by egg-shaped berries that turn from green to yellow as they mature. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Solanum album contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
