Stelis sobrina

Stelis sobrina, commonly known as the pink-stemmed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers with pink and white petals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Stelis sobrina is appreciated for its ornamental value and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
