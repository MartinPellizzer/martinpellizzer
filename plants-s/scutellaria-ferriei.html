Scutellaria ferriei

Scutellaria ferriei, commonly known as the Sierra Madre skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and is often found in mountainous regions. Its small, tubular flowers are usually blue or purple, and it produces distinctive seed capsules that release tiny seeds when mature.

Scutellaria ferriei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
