Saxifraga cespitosa var. sponhemica

Saxifraga cespitosa var. sponhemica, commonly known as the Sponheim saxifrage, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe.

It features dense, mat-forming foliage with dark green, rounded leaves that are slightly hairy. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to colonize difficult garden spaces.
