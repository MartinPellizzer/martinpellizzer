Specklinia feuilletii

Specklinia feuilletii, commonly known as the feather-leaf orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated leaves that resemble feathers, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically during the rainy season. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found growing epiphytically on trees.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts for its unique appearance.
