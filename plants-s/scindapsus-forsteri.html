Scindapsus forsteri

Scindapsus forsteri, commonly known as the silver vine, is a climbing tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elongated leaves that often feature a silvery or variegated pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by red berries. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Scindapsus forsteri is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
