Swertia platyphylla

Swertia platyphylla, commonly known as the flat-leaved swertia, is a perennial herb native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and small, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes. Its leaves are arranged alternately along the stem, and it produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds.
