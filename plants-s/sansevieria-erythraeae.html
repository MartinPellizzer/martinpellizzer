Sansevieria erythraeae

Sansevieria erythraeae, commonly known as the Red Sea snake plant, is a succulent species native to the Arabian Peninsula.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with reddish or bronze hues along the edges. The plant is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its fleshy leaves and stems. It produces small, pale greenish-white flowers in clusters during the warmer months.

Sansevieria erythraeae is a low-maintenance plant that thrives in bright, indirect light and requires minimal watering.
