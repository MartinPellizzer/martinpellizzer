Sideritis stricta

Sideritis stricta, commonly known as narrowleaf ironwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, hairy leaves. Its flowers are usually pale blue or purple and are clustered in whorls along the stem.

Sideritis stricta is known for its medicinal properties, often used in traditional remedies for its purported calming and anti-inflammatory effects.
