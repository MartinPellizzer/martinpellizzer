Stellaria henryi

Stellaria henryi, commonly known as Henry's starwort, is a low-growing annual herb native to China.

It features slender stems that branch at the base and are often covered with fine hairs. The plant produces small, white flowers with five petals that form a star-like shape, giving it its common name. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stems.

Stellaria henryi is typically found in rocky or disturbed soils and is adapted to temperate climates.
