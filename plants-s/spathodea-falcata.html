Spathodea falcata

Spathodea falcata, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that are typically orange or red in color. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to lanceolate in shape. It produces woody pods that contain several seeds.

The tree is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can grow up to 20 meters tall.
