Senecio leuceria

Senecio leuceria, commonly known as the whiteleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, silver-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the summer months.

Senecio leuceria is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
