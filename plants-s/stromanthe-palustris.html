Stromanthe palustris

Stromanthe palustris, commonly known as the swamp stromanthe, is a tropical plant native to the wetlands of South America.

It features striking, variegated leaves with a mix of green, white, and pink hues, which are often adorned with a waxy bloom. The plant thrives in humid environments with indirect light and requires consistent moisture in its soil. Its foliage is often used in ornamental horticulture for its aesthetic appeal.

Stromanthe palustris is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.
