Sitanion latifolium

Sitanion latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf sitanion, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its large, broad leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
