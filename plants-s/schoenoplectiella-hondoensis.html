Schoenoplectiella hondoensis

Schoenoplectiella hondoensis, commonly known as Hondo's bulrush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, commonly known as the sedge family. This species typically grows in wetlands and marshy areas, thriving in moist, nutrient-rich soils. Its slender, grass-like stems are often clustered and can reach heights of up to 1 meter.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in spikelets, typical of the Cyperaceae family.
