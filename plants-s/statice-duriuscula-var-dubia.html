Statice duriuscula var. dubia

Statice duriuscula var. dubia, commonly known as the dwarf statice, is a low-growing, flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact habit and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically thrives in well-drained sandy or gravelly soils and full sun exposure. It is often used in ornamental gardening for its drought tolerance and long-lasting blooms.

Botanically, it belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is known for its fibrous root system and narrow, linear leaves.
