Sideritis eriocephala

Sideritis eriocephala, commonly known as the woolly ironweed, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its hairy, gray-green leaves and small, pale blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats.

Sideritis eriocephala is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
