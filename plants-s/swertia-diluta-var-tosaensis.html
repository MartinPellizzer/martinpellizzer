Swertia diluta var. tosaensis

Swertia diluta var. tosaensis, commonly known as Tosa Swertia, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.
