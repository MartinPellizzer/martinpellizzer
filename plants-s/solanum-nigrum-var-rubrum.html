Solanum nigrum var. rubrum

Solanum nigrum var. rubrum, commonly known as red black nightshade, is a variant of the common black nightshade species.

It is a herbaceous annual plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa. The plant produces small, dark red berries that are toxic to humans and animals. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in gardens and along roadsides.

Its botanical features include alternate, ovate leaves with serrated edges and flowers that are typically white or purple.
