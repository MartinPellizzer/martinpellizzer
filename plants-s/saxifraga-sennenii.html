Saxifraga sennenii

Saxifraga sennenii, commonly known as the Japanese saxifrage, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Saxifragaceae and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are usually rounded and slightly hairy, adapted to thrive in rocky or moist environments.

Saxifraga sennenii is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
