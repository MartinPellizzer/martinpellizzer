Sambucus rehderiana

Sambucus rehderiana, commonly known as the Rehder's elderberry, is a species of flowering plant in the Caprifoliaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of central and southern China, where it thrives in temperate climates. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves, which are typically opposite and have serrated edges. The berries are edible and have been used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.
