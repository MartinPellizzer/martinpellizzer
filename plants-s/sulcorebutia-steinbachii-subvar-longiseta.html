Sulcorebutia steinbachii subvar. longiseta

Sulcorebutia steinbachii subvar. longiseta, commonly known as the long-stemmed crassula, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tinge. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring or early summer. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
