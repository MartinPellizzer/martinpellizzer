Sasamorpha purpurascens var. psilostachys

Sasamorpha purpurascens var. psilostachys, commonly known as the purple-flowered sasamorpha, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking purple or violet blooms that appear in clusters, adding visual interest to gardens. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, with slender stems and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for tropical and subtropical climates.

Its ornamental value is heightened by its vibrant color and unique floral structure.
