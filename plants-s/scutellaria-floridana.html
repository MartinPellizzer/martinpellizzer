Scutellaria floridana

Scutellaria floridana, commonly known as Florida skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Scutellaria floridana is typically found in moist, sandy soils along stream banks and in wetlands.
