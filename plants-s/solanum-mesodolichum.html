Solanum mesodolichum

Solanum mesodolichum, commonly known as the mesodolichum nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale green. Its fruit is a berry that turns red when mature, containing several seeds.

Solanum mesodolichum is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and along stream banks.
