Sarcochilus calceolus

Sarcochilus calceolus, commonly known as the helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, helmet-shaped spathaceous bracts that enclose the inflorescence. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers with a distinct, fragrant scent. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and requires well-drained soil.

Sarcochilus calceolus is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
