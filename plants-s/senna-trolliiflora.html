Senna trolliiflora

Senna trolliiflora, commonly known as the African senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, which bloom during the rainy season. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with small, oval-shaped leaflets that are often covered in fine hairs.

The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it resilient to drought conditions.
