Sophronitis tereticaulis

Sophronitis tereticaulis, commonly known as the slender-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
