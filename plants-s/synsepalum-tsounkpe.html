Synsepalum tsounkpe

Synsepalum tsounkpe, commonly known as the African date plum, is a small shrub native to West Africa.

It produces small, round fruits that are typically yellow or orange when ripe. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The fruits are often consumed fresh or used in traditional remedies.

It thrives in tropical climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
