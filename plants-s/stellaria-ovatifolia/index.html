Stellaria Ovatifolia

Stellaria ovatifolia is a delicate flowering plant known for its star-shaped white blooms and slender, grass-like leaves. Native to temperate regions of Europe and western Asia, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. This low-growing species often carpets the forest floor, adding a subtle beauty to natural landscapes. Its common name, "White Star Flower," reflects its striking appearance and delicate nature. Despite its modest size, Stellaria ovatifolia plays an important role in supporting local insect populations and maintaining ecological balance.
