Scyphularia Pentaphylla

Five-leafed scyphularia, scientifically known as Scyphularia pentaphylla, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. This species is characterized by its distinctive five-leafed structure and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous habitats. Due to its limited range and specific environmental requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival in the face of climate change and habitat loss.
