Statice braunii

Statice braunii, commonly known as Braun's sea lavender, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its tall, spindly stems and clusters of small, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas, sandy soils, and saline environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its flowers are usually pink to purple in color, and they bloom during the summer months.

Statice braunii is often used in floral arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms and ornamental value.
