Stachys linearifolia

Stachys linearifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaved hedge nettle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and clusters of small, pale purple flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in open woodlands or along coastal areas. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is known for its aromatic herbs and shrubs.

Stachys linearifolia is generally hardy and can thrive in well-drained soils with full sun exposure.
