Swietenia cirrhata

Swietenia cirrhata, commonly known as the Honduras mahogany, is a tropical hardwood tree native to Central America and parts of Mexico.

It belongs to the Meliaceae family and is characterized by its tall, straight trunk and dense, dark reddish-brown wood. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by winged fruits that contain seeds. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly serrated margin.
