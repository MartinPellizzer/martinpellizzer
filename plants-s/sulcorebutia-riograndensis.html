Sulcorebutia riograndensis

Sulcorebutia riograndensis, commonly known as the Rio Grande cactus, is a species of flowering cactus native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular shape and spiny stems. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the rainy season, typically in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is often cultivated in arid gardens and botanical collections.
