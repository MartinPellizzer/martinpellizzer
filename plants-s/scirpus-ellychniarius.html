Scirpus ellychniarius

Scirpus ellychniarius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bulrush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland environments in the Northern Hemisphere.

Scirpus ellychniarius is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for various aquatic and terrestrial organisms.
