Skimmia japonica f. longifolia

Skimmia japonica f. longifolia, commonly known as the Japanese skimmia with long leaves, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It features glossy, dark green, elongated leaves that provide a dense, lush appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in the spring, followed by red berries that are ornamental and attract birds. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its foliage and seasonal flowers make it a valuable addition to shaded garden spaces.
