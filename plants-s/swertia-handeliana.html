Swertia handeliana

Swertia handeliana, commonly known as Himalayan gentian, is a perennial herb native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, thriving at elevations between 2,000 and 4,000 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered with a dense layer of fine hairs.

Swertia handeliana is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
