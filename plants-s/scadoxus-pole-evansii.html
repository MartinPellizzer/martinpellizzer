Scadoxus pole-evansii

Scadoxus pole-evansii, commonly known as the Cape lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stem and showy, white or pale pink flowers. The plant produces large, lance-shaped leaves that are often used in horticultural displays due to their ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Scadoxus pole-evansii is also valued for its ability to bloom reliably in colder climates when grown in containers.
