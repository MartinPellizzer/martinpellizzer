Specklinia nectarifera

Specklinia nectarifera, commonly known as the nectar-bearing orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit and its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, often with a distinctive nectar-producing structure. Its leaves are typically dark green and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in moisture retention.

Specklinia nectarifera is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique floral features.
