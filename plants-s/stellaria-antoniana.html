Stellaria antoniana

Stellaria antoniana, commonly known as the starwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its slender stems and small, white or pinkish flowers that resemble stars. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Stellaria antoniana is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
