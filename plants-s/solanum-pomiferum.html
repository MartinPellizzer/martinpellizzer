Solanum pomiferum

Solanum pomiferum, commonly known as the nightshade tomato, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like potatoes and peppers. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers that develop into red, fleshy fruits resembling tomatoes. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Solanum pomiferum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes grown in gardens for its vibrant fruit and foliage.
