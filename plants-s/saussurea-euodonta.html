Saussurea euodonta

Saussurea euodonta, commonly known as the Chinese yarrow or sweet everlasting, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and feathery leaves. The plant produces small, yellow to brown flowers that bloom in late summer. It is often found in alpine meadows and rocky habitats at high elevations.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for various health benefits.
