Silene Melzheimeri

Silene melzheimeri is a rare and elusive flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Tibetan Plateau. It is known for its striking pinkish-red flowers and unique adaptations to extreme environmental conditions. This species is classified as critically endangered due to habitat loss and climate change. Its limited distribution makes it a subject of great interest for botanists and conservationists. Studying Silene melzheimeri offers valuable insights into the resilience of plant life in harsh ecosystems.
