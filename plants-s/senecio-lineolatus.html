Senecio lineolatus

Senecio lineolatus, commonly known as the African marguerite, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features small, daisy-like yellow flowers with white centers, which bloom in clusters during the warmer months. Its leaves are generally gray-green with a fuzzy texture, and it thrives in well-drained, sandy soils.

Senecio lineolatus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.
