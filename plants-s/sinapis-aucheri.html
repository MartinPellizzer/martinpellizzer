Sinapis aucheri

Sinapis aucheri, commonly known as the Persian rocket or winter cress, is a biennial or annual plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of serrated, dark green leaves and clusters of small, yellow, cross-shaped flowers. The plant produces slender, branching stems that can grow up to 60 cm in height. Its flowers are typically arranged in racemes and bloom in early spring.

Sinapis aucheri is known for its rapid growth and is often used as a winter cover crop due to its hardy nature.
