Stelis acicularis

Stelis acicularis, commonly known as the needle orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, needle-like leaves and compact growth habit. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its unique appearance and specific habitat requirements, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
