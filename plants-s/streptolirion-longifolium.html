Streptolirion longifolium

Streptolirion longifolium, commonly known as the long-leaved streptolirion, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that bear small, tubular flowers in shades of pink and purple. The leaves are narrow and elongated, often with a slight waxy coating. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
