Selaginella distorta var. minor

Selaginella distorta var. minor, commonly known as the lesser zigzag moss.

It is a species of spikemoss belonging to the family Selaginellaceae, characterized by its delicate, branched stems and small, scale-like leaves. The plant is typically found in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its sporophyte produces a short, erect stem with a single spore-producing structure called a strobilus.

This variety is notable for its unique morphology and is often used in horticultural settings for its ornamental value.
