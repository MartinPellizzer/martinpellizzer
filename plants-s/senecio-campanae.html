Senecio campanae

Senecio campanae, commonly known as the bluebell of the Cape, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. Senecio campanae is a perennial shrub that can reach heights of up to 1 meter.

Its leaves are narrow and hairy, and it is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
