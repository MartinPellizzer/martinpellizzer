Strychnos macroacanthos

Strychnos macroacanthos, commonly known as the large-spined strychnos, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by capsule fruits containing seeds. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have prominent spines along the margins.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique appearance.
