Stellaria yunnanensis

Stellaria yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan starwort, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine regions, preferring moist, well-drained soils. Its leaves are opposite and linear, while its stems are often hairy.

Stellaria yunnanensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
