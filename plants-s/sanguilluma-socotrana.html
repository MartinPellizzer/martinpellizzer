Sanguilluma socotrana

Sanguilluma socotrana, commonly known as Socotra's blood vine, is a unique plant native to the island of Socotra.

It is characterized by its thick, woody stems and distinctive, spiraling leaves that curl tightly around the stem. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruit is a fleshy, red berry that contains several seeds.

Due to its rarity and unique adaptations, it is considered an important species for botanical research and conservation efforts.
