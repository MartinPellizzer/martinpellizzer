Senecio subdentatus var. simplex

Senecio subdentatus var. simplex, commonly known as the smoothleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are smooth and slightly lobed, while the stems are often branching and covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
