Specklinia bertoniensis

Specklinia bertoniensis, commonly known as the Berton's tillandsia, is a species of air plant belonging to the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. This epiphytic plant thrives in humid environments and does not require soil for growth. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a dense covering of trichomes that help in absorbing moisture from the air.

Specklinia bertoniensis is appreciated for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
