Sanguisorba praecox

Sanguisorba praecox, commonly known as early dock, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the rose family (Rosaceae) and is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, pinkish-red flowers. The plant features palmate leaves with serrated edges, which are typically bluish-green in color. Sanguisorba praecox thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, woodlands, and stream banks.
