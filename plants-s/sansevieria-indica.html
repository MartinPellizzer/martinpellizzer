Sansevieria indica

Sansevieria indica, commonly known as the snake plant, is a tropical flowering plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with distinct yellow or white edges. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color.

Sansevieria indica is also noted for its air-purifying properties, as it can help remove toxins from the environment.
