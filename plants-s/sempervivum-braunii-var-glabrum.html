Sempervivum braunii var. glabrum

Sempervivum braunii var. glabrum, commonly known as the smooth stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically fleshy and covered in a waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the summer months. It is well adapted to rocky soils and full sun exposure, making it a hardy and low-maintenance ornamental plant.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine landscapes.
