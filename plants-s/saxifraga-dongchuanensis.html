Saxifraga dongchuanensis

Saxifraga dongchuanensis, commonly known as the Dongchuan saxifrage, is a flowering plant native to the Yunnan province in China.

It is a member of the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming growth habit. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers during the summer months. It thrives in rocky, alpine environments and is adapted to high-altitude conditions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
