Senecio diclinus var. glabriusculus

Senecio diclinus var. glabriusculus, commonly known as the smoothleaf senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically smooth and hairless, giving it its varietal name. It produces small yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which attract various pollinators.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in rock gardens and dry landscapes.
