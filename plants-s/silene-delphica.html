Silene delphica

Silene delphica, commonly known as the Delphic Silene, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Silene delphica is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to sunny, well-drained environments.
