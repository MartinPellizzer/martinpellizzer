Senecio argenteus var. tillandsiifolius

Senecio argenteus var. tillandsiifolius, commonly known as the silverleaf senecio, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in rock gardens.
