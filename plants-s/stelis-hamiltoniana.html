Stelis hamiltoniana

Stelis hamiltoniana, commonly known as the Hamilton's stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant has a short, fleshy stem with a few leaves and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
