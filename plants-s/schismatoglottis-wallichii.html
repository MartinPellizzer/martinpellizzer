Schismatoglottis wallichii

Schismatoglottis wallichii, commonly known as the Himalayan arrowroot, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the Himalayas.

It is a herbaceous plant with fleshy, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It is adapted to grow in cool, moist environments at high altitudes.

Its rhizomes are edible and have been traditionally used in local cuisines.
