Stelis punicea

Stelis punicea, commonly known as the pink stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, erect growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or branches, making it an epiphytic species. Its leaves are usually clustered at the base and are often covered in a waxy coating.

Stelis punicea is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
