Searsia gracillima var. gracillima

Searsia gracillima var. gracillima, commonly known as the silver cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, oval leaves that are typically silver-white underneath, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers in spring, followed by red berries in autumn. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.

Searsia gracillima var. gracillima is relatively low-maintenance and thrives in full sun to partial shade.
