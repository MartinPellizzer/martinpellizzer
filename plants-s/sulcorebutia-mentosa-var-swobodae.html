Sulcorebutia mentosa var. swobodae

Sulcorebutia mentosa var. swobodae, commonly known as the Swobodae Pincushion Cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to the arid regions of Argentina.

It features a dense covering of white to pale blue spines, giving it a pincushion-like appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, requiring minimal water to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its unique shape and ornamental value in rock gardens and xeriscaping.
