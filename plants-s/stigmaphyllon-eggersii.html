Stigmaphyllon eggersii

Stigmaphyllon eggersii, commonly known as the golden star cactus, is a species of flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the family Cactaceae and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features a short, thick stem with spiny areoles, typical of many cacti. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing adaptations to withstand drought.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for collectors and gardeners in suitable climates.
