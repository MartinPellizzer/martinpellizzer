Senecio antipodus

Senecio antipodus, commonly known as the Antipodean groundsel, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or alpine environments. The plant features small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in a head, which are borne on slender stems. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture.
