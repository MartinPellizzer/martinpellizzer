Schnella alata

Schnella alata, commonly known as the winged balsam, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive wing-like structures on its stems, which give it its common name. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically red or orange in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Schnella alata is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been cultivated for its vibrant blooms.
