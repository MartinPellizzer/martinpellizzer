Shuteria vestita var. involucrata

Shuteria vestita var. involucrata, commonly known as the velvetleaf shuteria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, velvety leaves that are typically a deep green in color. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, often blooming during the rainy season. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

This variant is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
