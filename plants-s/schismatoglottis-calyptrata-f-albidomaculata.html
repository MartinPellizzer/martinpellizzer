Schismatoglottis calyptrata f. albidomaculata

Schismatoglottis calyptrata f. albidomaculata, commonly known as the white-spotted schismatoglottis, is a hybrid orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, broad, dark green leaves with distinctive white or pale spots. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink and white, typical of the Schismatoglottis genus. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
