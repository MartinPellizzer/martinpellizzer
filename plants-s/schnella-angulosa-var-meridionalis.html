Schnella angulosa var. meridionalis

Schnella angulosa var. meridionalis, commonly known as the southern angular schnella, is a rare flowering plant native to specific regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its angular, succulent leaves and produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink to white. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth. It is often found in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

Due to its limited distribution and unique morphology, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
