Saxifraga implicans var. weixiensis

Saxifraga implicans var. weixiensis, commonly known as the Weixian saxifrage, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive leaves. The plant typically produces small, white or pinkish flowers during the summer months. It thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine environments.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
