Stizolobium quadrialatum

Stizolobium quadrialatum, commonly known as the four-winged acacia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features bipinnate leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in dense, clustered inflorescences.

The seed pods are elongated and four-winged, which aid in wind dispersal of its seeds.
