Schaueria caduciflora

Schaueria caduciflora, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its attractive, velvety leaves and showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. It prefers well-drained soil and bright, indirect light to thrive.

Schaueria caduciflora is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
