Spiraea hypericifolia var. hupehensis

Spiraea hypericifolia var. hupehensis, commonly known as the Chinese spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features dense, compact growth with arching branches that bear clusters of small, white flowers in early summer. The foliage is typically dark green, turning reddish-purple in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to various garden settings.
