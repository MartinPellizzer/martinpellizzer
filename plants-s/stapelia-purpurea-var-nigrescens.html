Stapelia purpurea var. nigrescens

Stapelia purpurea var. nigrescens, commonly known as the Purple Star Stapelia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow with red markings, though the varietal form has a darker, almost black hue. Its leaves are usually reduced to small, spiny structures, and it blooms during the warmer months.

This species is valued for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
