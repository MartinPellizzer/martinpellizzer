Salicornia erectispica

Salicornia erectispica, commonly known as sea beans, is a halophytic plant adapted to saline environments.

It is characterized by its succulent, cylindrical stems that are often covered in a waxy cuticle to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in coastal areas, salt marshes, and other brackish habitats.

Salicornia erectispica plays a role in stabilizing coastal soils and providing habitat for various small organisms.
