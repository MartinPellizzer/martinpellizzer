Schefflera trevesioides

Schefflera trevesioides, commonly known as the African shield fern, is a tropical plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, palmate compound leaves that resemble a shield, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows as an understory shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its foliage is evergreen, with dark green, leathery leaves that provide a lush, tropical appearance.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant in warm climates.
