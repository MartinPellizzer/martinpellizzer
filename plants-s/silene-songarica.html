Silene songarica

Silene songarica, commonly known as the Mongolian catchfly, is a flowering plant native to the steppes of Mongolia and adjacent regions.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its slender stems and small, pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats and has a compact growth habit.
