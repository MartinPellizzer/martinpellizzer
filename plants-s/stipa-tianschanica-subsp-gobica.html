Stipa tianschanica subsp. gobica

Stipa tianschanica subsp.

gobica, commonly known as the Tianshan needlegrass, is a perennial grass species native to central Asia. It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and dense, spike-like inflorescences.

The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, often on rocky or sandy soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

This subspecies is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing forage for grazing animals.
