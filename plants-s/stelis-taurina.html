Stelis taurina

Stelis taurina, commonly known as the Taurine Stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic orchid, typically growing on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. The plant features a short, horizontal stem with clustered, small greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are usually arranged in a rosette pattern and are covered in a dense, velvety pubescence.

Stelis taurina is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
