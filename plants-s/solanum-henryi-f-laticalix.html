Solanum henryi f. laticalix

Solanum henryi f. laticalix, commonly known as the Laticalix nightshade, is a rare and lesser-known species within the Solanaceae family.

It is native to specific regions in China, where it thrives in temperate and subtropical climates. The plant features small, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture and produces clusters of bell-shaped, violet to purple flowers. Its fruits are typically small berries that change color as they mature, often from green to red.
