Sideritis hirsuta var. maroccana

Sideritis hirsuta var. maroccana, commonly known as Moroccan hirsute sida, is a perennial herb native to North Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its hairy, grayish-green leaves and small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in rocky and arid environments, often growing in mountainous regions.

Its botanical features make it an attractive addition to rock gardens and a subject of interest in botanical studies.
