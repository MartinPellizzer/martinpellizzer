Solanum macroscola

Solanum macroscola, commonly known as the giant nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, glossy leaves and showy, bell-shaped flowers that range from white to violet in color. The plant produces berries that are typically yellow or orange when ripe, containing toxic alkaloids. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its toxic properties, it is important to handle the plant with care.
