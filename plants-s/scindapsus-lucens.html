Scindapsus lucens

Scindapsus lucens, commonly known as the silver vine, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that often have a silvery or variegated appearance. Scindapsus lucens is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and ease of care.
