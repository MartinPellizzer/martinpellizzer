Siphoneranthemum heterophyllum

Siphoneranthemum heterophyllum, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped foliage and showy, violet-colored flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that help it store water, adapting to its native arid environment. It thrives in well-draining soil and partial shade, making it a popular houseplant.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and ecologically significant in its native habitat.
