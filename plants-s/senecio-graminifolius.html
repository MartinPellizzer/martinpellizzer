Senecio graminifolius

Senecio graminifolius, commonly known as the grass-leaved groundsels, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky or sandy soils. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Senecio graminifolius is often used in rock gardens due to its hardy nature and ornamental appeal.
