Schizopetalon walkeri var. brongniartii

Schizopetalon walkeri var. brongniartii, commonly known as the brongniartii variant of the walking fern, is a variant of the Schizopetalon walkeri species.

It is characterized by its distinctive, fiddlehead-like fronds that unfurl as they grow. The plant exhibits a creeping growth habit, often forming dense mats on the forest floor. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in temperate rainforests.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and ecological role in humid ecosystems.
