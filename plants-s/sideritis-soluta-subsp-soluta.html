Sideritis soluta subsp. soluta

Sideritis soluta subsp.

soluta, commonly known as the Greek violet or mountain violet, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants.

The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of small, violet-blue flowers. Its leaves are typically narrow and slightly hairy, and it thrives in rocky or sandy soils. Sideritis soluta subsp.

soluta is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
