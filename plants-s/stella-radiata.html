Stella radiata

Stella radiata, commonly known as the star radiata, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 feet. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

Stella radiata thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
