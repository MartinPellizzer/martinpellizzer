Senecio albaniae

Senecio albaniae, commonly known as the Albanian groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or grassy areas, often in mountainous regions. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a composite head. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Senecio albaniae is notable for its adaptability to harsh environments and its role in local ecosystems.
