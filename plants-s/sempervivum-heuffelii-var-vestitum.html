Sempervivum heuffelii var. vestitum

Sempervivum heuffelii var. vestitum, commonly known as the Velvet Hens and Chicks, is a succulent plant native to the Alps.

It features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves covered in a fine, velvety white bloom, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces upright flowering stalks that bear clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink to purple. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for both gardeners and collectors.
