Struthanthus patrisii

Struthanthus patrisii, commonly known as the desert star.

It is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. The plant is characterized by its clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It has a low-growing, mat-forming habit with hairy, succulent leaves adapted to arid environments.

Struthanthus patrisii is well-suited to rocky soils and full sun, making it a hardy species for xeriscaping.
