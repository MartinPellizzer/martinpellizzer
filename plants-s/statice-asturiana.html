Statice asturiana

Statice asturiana, commonly known as the Asturian statice, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of northern Spain.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and purple, which bloom during the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and gray-green, adapted to withstand salty sea air and coastal conditions.

Statice asturiana is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
