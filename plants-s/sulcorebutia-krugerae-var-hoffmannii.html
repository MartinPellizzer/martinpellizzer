Sulcorebutia krugerae var. hoffmannii

Sulcorebutia krugerae var. hoffmannii, commonly known as the Hoffmann's crassula, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped stems covered in short, woolly hairs. The plant produces small, pale green or blue-gray leaves that are often adorned with white or pale blue markings. It blooms with clusters of pale yellow flowers during the summer months.

This variety is well-suited for arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
