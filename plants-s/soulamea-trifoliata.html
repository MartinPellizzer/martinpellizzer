Soulamea trifoliata

Soulamea trifoliata, commonly known as the three-leaf soulamea, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which consist of three leaflets arranged symmetrically. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its botanical structure includes a woody stem with smooth, glossy bark.

Soulamea trifoliata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
