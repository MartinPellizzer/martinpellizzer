Stenaria umbratilis

Stenaria umbratilis, commonly known as the shadowy stenaria, is a flowering plant native to the shaded understories of tropical forests.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble single blooms. Stenaria umbratilis is often found growing on rocky substrates or in moist, shaded areas within its native habitat.
