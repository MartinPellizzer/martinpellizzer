Styphelia viridiflora

Styphelia viridiflora, commonly known as the green-flowered heath, is a flowering plant native to the wet heathlands of southeastern Australia.

Styphelia viridiflora thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and requires a humid, cool climate to flourish.
