Stanhopea stevensonii

Stanhopea stevensonii, commonly known as Stevenson's stanhopea, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange with intricate patterns. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in its natural habitat. It prefers warm, humid environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks.

Its unique floral structure and vibrant colors make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
