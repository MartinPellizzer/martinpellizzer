Sedum crassularia

Sedum crassularia, commonly known as the Jade Plant or Fat Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish tint, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in bright, indirect light with well-draining soil.

Sedum crassularia is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and aesthetic appeal.
