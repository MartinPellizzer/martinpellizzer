Stanhopea × spindleriana

Stanhopea × spindleriana, commonly known as the Spindler's orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant, and long-lasting flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, tropical environments. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to orange, with intricate patterns and a strong, spicy scent.

Stanhopea × spindleriana is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and rarity in cultivation.
