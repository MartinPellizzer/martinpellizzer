Sophora moorcroftiana

Sophora moorcroftiana, commonly known as the Chinese wisteria, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, pendulous flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant produces long, cylindrical pods that contain seeds, and it has a deep root system that helps stabilize soil.

Sophora moorcroftiana is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its ecological benefits.
