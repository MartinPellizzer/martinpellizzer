Sloanea calva

Sloanea calva, commonly known as the slender sloanea, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are often elliptical in shape. Its growth habit and leaf morphology make it an important species in ecological studies of tropical flora.
