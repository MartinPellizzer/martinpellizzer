Solanum stramoniifolium var. stramoniifolium

Solanum stramoniifolium var. stramoniifolium, commonly known as the purple leaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically purple or bronze in color. The plant produces small, violet to blue flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in arid or semi-arid environments.

The berries it produces are toxic and should not be consumed by humans or animals.
