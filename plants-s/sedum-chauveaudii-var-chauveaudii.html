Sedum chauveaudii var. chauveaudii

Sedum chauveaudii var. chauveaudii, commonly known as the Chauveaud's stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant is characterized by its ability to store water in its leaves, making it drought-tolerant. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often growing in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
