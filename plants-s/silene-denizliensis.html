Silene denizliensis

Silene denizliensis, commonly known as the Denizli Catchfly, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid conditions. Its leaves are linear and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Silene denizliensis is also noted for its small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.
