Senecio baurii

Senecio baurii, commonly known as the silver sword, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in fine white hairs, giving it a distinctive silvery appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers in spring, which are arranged in a cylindrical inflorescence. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
