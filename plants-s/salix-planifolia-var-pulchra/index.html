Salix Planifolia Var. Pulchra

Salix planifolia var. pulchra is a strikingly beautiful willow species known for its delicate, feathery foliage. Native to the mountainous regions of central Asia, this plant thrives in cool, moist environments. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and landscape design. The leaves are narrow, lance-shaped, and often display a soft, silvery-green hue. This variety is valued not only for its aesthetic appeal but also for its ecological benefits in supporting local wildlife.
