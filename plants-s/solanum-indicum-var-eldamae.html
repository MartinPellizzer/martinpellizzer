Solanum indicum var. eldamae

Solanum indicum var. eldamae, commonly known as the Eldamae nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and peppers. The plant features small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple, often blooming in spring. Its leaves are ovate and covered in fine hairs, while the fruit is a small, yellowish berry that ripens in summer.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
