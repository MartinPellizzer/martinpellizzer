Solanum ensifolium

Solanum ensifolium, commonly known as the swordleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and showy, violet-colored flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant produces small, toxic berries that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland or shrubland habitats.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
