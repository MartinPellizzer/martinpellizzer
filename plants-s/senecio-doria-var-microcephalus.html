Senecio doria var. microcephalus

Senecio doria var. microcephalus, commonly known as the small-headed doria senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in compact clusters. It is often found in rocky or sandy habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.
