Solanum caripense subsp. jamesonianum

Solanum caripense subsp.

jamesonianum, commonly known as the Jameson's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America. It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions.

The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are berries that change from green to red as they mature.

The leaves are usually ovate or elliptic with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.
