Strobilanthes medahinnensis

Strobilanthes medahinnensis, commonly known as the Medahinni flame flower, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences that resemble flames. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest understories and thrives in humid environments. Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
