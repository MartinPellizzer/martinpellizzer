Stephania brevipes

Stephania brevipes, commonly known as the short-stemmed stephania, is a climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Menispermaceae and is characterized by its slender, twining stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by distinctive, elongated berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical forests.
