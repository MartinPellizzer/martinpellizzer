Senecio schultzii

Senecio schultzii, commonly known as the Schultze's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters. The plant is characterized by its succulent stems and leaves, which help it conserve water in arid environments. It typically thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Senecio schultzii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
