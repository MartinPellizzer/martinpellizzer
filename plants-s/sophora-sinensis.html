Sophora sinensis

Sophora sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese scholar tree, is a deciduous tree native to East Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its bipinnate leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The tree produces elongated pods that contain flat, dark brown seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in Chinese medicine.

The plant is also notable for its role in ecological systems, providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
