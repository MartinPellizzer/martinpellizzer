Staurogyne amoena

Staurogyne amoena, commonly known as the pretty water plant, is a species of aquatic plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, floating leaves that are typically heart-shaped or ovate in form. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. Staurogyne amoena thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental ponds and aquariums.
