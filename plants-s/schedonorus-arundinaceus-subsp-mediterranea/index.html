Schedonorus Arundinaceus Subsp. Mediterranea

Schedonorus arundinaceus subsp. mediterranea is a grass species native to the Mediterranean region. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in open grasslands and disturbed soils. This plant is known for its hardy nature and ability to withstand drought conditions. Its tufted growth habit and fine leaves make it a common sight in southern Europe. It plays an important role in local ecosystems and is valued for its ornamental and ecological benefits.
