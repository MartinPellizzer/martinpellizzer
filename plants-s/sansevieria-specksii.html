Sansevieria specksii

Sansevieria specksii, commonly known as the Tiger Tail Snake Plant, is a striking member of the Asparagaceae family native to tropical Africa.

This plant is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are dark green with prominent, light green to yellowish stripes, giving it its distinctive appearance. It thrives in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it an ideal choice for indoor environments. The leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water, which helps the plant survive in arid conditions.

Sansevieria specksii is also known for its air-purifying qualities, contributing to improved indoor air quality.
