Solanum xylopiifolium

Solanum xylopiifolium, commonly known as the xylopiifolium nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It features woody stems and lanceolate, hairy leaves that are often gland-dotted. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically purple or violet in color. Its fruit is a globose berry that changes from green to yellow as it matures.

Solanum xylopiifolium is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water availability.
