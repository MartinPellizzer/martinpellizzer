Saintpaulia ulugurensis

Saintpaulia ulugurensis, commonly known as the Uluguru violet, is a species of African violet native to the Uluguru Mountains in Tanzania.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of vibrant violet or purple flowers. The leaves are typically dark green and velvety, with a slightly hairy texture. This plant thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
