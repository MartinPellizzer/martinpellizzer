Sulcorebutia brevispina

Sulcorebutia brevispina, commonly known as the short-spined crassulaceae, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to the arid regions of South America.

It features dense, fleshy rosettes of pale green to bluish-gray leaves that are covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the spring or early summer. It is well adapted to dry conditions, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems and leaves.

Due to its striking appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and succulent collections.
