Scrophularia chrysantha

Scrophularia chrysantha, commonly known as the golden scrophularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of bright yellow flowers in the summer months. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Scrophularia chrysantha is also valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
