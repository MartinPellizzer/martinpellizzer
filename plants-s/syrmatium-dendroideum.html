Syrmatium dendroideum

Syrmatium dendroideum, commonly known as the tree-like syrmatium, is a unique species belonging to the family Apiaceae.

It is characterized by its woody stem and branching structure, resembling a small tree or shrub. The plant features compound leaves with a distinctive arrangement of leaflets. Its flowers are small and clustered in umbels, typical of the Apiaceae family.
