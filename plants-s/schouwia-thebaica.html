Schouwia thebaica

Schouwia thebaica, commonly known as the Cape rose or the yellow orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family native to South Africa.

This herbaceous perennial features showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with intricate patterns resembling a rose. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its unique floral structure includes a specialized lip that aids in pollination by attracting specific insect pollinators.

Schouwia thebaica is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and adaptability to various garden conditions.
