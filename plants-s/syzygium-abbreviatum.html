Syzygium abbreviatum

Syzygium abbreviatum, commonly known as the small-fruited clove.

It is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family, native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its small, aromatic flowers and edible, aromatic fruits. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, often found in moist, shaded environments.

Syzygium abbreviatum is valued for its fragrant leaves and fruits, which are used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.
