Schizococcus nummularia

Schizococcus nummularia, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, silver-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps it conserve water in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a decorative touch to its appearance. It is well-suited for rock gardens or containers due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
