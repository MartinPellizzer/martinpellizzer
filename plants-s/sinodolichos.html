Sinodolichos

Sinodolichos, commonly known as the Chinese cowpea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and trifoliate leaves with stipules. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to purple. Its pods are typically hairy and contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Sinodolichos is adapted to grow in temperate climates and is valued for both its nutritional and medicinal properties.
