Selaginella densa var. densa

Selaginella densa var. densa, commonly known as the dense selaginella, is a species of spore-bearing plant belonging to the family Selaginellaceae.

It is a low-growing, evergreen herb that typically forms dense, mat-like colonies on rocky or sandy soils. The plant features closely packed, needle-like leaves that are usually green to bluish-green in color. It is characterized by its ability to survive in arid environments due to its efficient water retention mechanisms.

Selaginella densa var. densa is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
