Stillingia sinensis

Stillingia sinensis, commonly known as Chinese stellaria, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, white or pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

Stillingia sinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
