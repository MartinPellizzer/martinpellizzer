Swertia squamigera

Swertia squamigera, commonly known as the Himalayan gentian, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, thriving in cool and moist conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Swertia squamigera is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
