Scleronema grandiflorum

Scleronema grandiflorum, commonly known as the large-flowered rock heath, is a flowering plant native to the Cape region of South Africa.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its fleshy, evergreen leaves and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid conditions. Its flowers are typically visited by insects, contributing to its role in local pollination networks.

Scleronema grandiflorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens and xeriscapes.
