Saccolabium distichum var. pseudodistichum

Saccolabium distichum var. pseudodistichum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved screwbean, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and distinctive spiral arrangement of leaves around the stem. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense, spiral patterns along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.

Its unique morphology and adaptation to harsh environments make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
