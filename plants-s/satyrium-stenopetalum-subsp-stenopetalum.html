Satyrium stenopetalum subsp. stenopetalum

Satyrium stenopetalum subsp.

stenopetalum, commonly known as the narrow-petaled orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Africa. It typically grows in open grasslands and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils.

The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers with narrow petals and sepals, which are characteristic of the subgenus. It blooms during the summer months, and its flowering period is relatively short.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and is often found in association with other orchid species in its habitat.
