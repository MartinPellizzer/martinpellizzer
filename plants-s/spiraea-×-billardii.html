Spiraea × billardii

Spiraea × billardii, commonly known as the weeping spiraea, is a hybrid shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its arching, willow-like branches that bear clusters of small, fragrant white or pink flowers in early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are oval-shaped, with a matte green upper surface and a silvery underside.

Spiraea × billardii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in garden landscaping.
