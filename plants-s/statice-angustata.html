Statice angustata

Statice angustata, commonly known as narrowleaf glassweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and spikes of small, blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to coastal environments. It is often found in dunes and along the seashore, where it tolerates salt spray and strong winds.

Statice angustata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and drought resistance.
