Sambucus Pubens Var. Melanocarpa

Sambucus pubens var. melanocarpa is a strikingly unique shrub native to parts of eastern North America. Known for its dark purple berries and distinctive foliage, it stands out among other members of the honeysuckle family. This variety thrives in moist, wooded areas and is often found in the understory of forests. Its berries are not only visually appealing but also serve as a food source for various wildlife. Despite its beauty, it requires careful cultivation due to its specific environmental needs.
