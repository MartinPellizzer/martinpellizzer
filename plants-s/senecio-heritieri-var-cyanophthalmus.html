Senecio heritieri var. cyanophthalmus

Senecio heritieri var. cyanophthalmus, commonly known as the blue-eyed senecio, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
