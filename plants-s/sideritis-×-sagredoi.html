Sideritis × sagredoi

Sideritis × sagredoi, commonly known as the hybrid Greek ironweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. The plant features erect, branched stems covered with fine hairs and lanceolate, gray-green leaves. Its small, pale blue to violet flowers are clustered in whorls along the stem, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Sideritis × sagredoi is often cultivated for its ornamental value and traditional medicinal uses.
