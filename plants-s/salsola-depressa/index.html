Salsola Depressa

Salsola depressa is a hardy shrub that thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. It is commonly found in deserts and dry grasslands across the globe. This plant is known for its ability to survive in extreme conditions with minimal water. Its small, fleshy leaves help reduce water loss and store moisture efficiently. Salsola depressa plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for desert wildlife.
