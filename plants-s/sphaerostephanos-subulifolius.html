Sphaerostephanos subulifolius

Sphaerostephanos subulifolius, commonly known as the slender-leaved starflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit and produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue in color. The plant features narrow, needle-like leaves that are arranged in a dense rosette, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or alpine settings.

Its botanical features make it an attractive and unique addition to collections of succulent or alpine plants.
