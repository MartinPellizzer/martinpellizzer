Santolina oblongifolia subsp. obtusifolia

Santolina oblongifolia subsp.

obtusifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf santolina, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It features aromatic, silver-gray foliage composed of narrow, oblong leaves with rounded tips.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in compact, rounded clusters during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
