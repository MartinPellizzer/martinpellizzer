Stachys longiflora

Stachys longiflora, commonly known as purple flowered betony, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features upright stems that bear clusters of violet to purple flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and have a serrated edge. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting flowers and ability to attract pollinators.
