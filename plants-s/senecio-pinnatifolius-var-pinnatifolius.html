Senecio pinnatifolius var. pinnatifolius

Senecio pinnatifolius var. pinnatifolius, commonly known as the African Daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking yellow flowers that bloom in summer, surrounded by narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and ability to attract pollinators.
