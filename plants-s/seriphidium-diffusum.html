Seriphidium diffusum

Seriphidium diffusum, commonly known as the diffuse tansy, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and aromatic foliage. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun conditions. Its yellow flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting various pollinators.

Seriphidium diffusum is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory properties.
