Saussurea baoxingensis

Saussurea baoxingensis, commonly known as the Baoxing yellow gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Sichuan, China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers and robust, hairy stems. The plant typically grows in high-altitude grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in cool and moist environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, while its flowers bloom in late summer.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
