Sarracenia × exsculta

Sarracenia × exsculta, commonly known as the hybrid pitcher plant, is a fascinating carnivorous plant resulting from the cross between Sarracenia leucophylla and Sarracenia rubra.

It is characterized by its elongated, tubular leaves that form specialized pitfall traps to capture and digest insects. The plant's vibrant red and green colors, along with its intricate venation patterns, make it visually striking. It thrives in wet, acidic soils and requires ample sunlight to maintain its health and growth.

This hybrid is popular among carnivorous plant enthusiasts for its unique morphology and ease of cultivation.
