Stapelia albocastanea

Stapelia albocastanea, commonly known as the white castanea stapelia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, often with a faint scent to attract pollinators. Its leaves are usually small and rounded, and the plant is known for its slow growth and ease of care.

Stapelia albocastanea is a popular choice for collectors due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
