Statice pantoscekii

Statice pantoscekii, commonly known as the Balkan statice, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, bract-like structures that enclose the flowers.

Statice pantoscekii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking visual impact.
