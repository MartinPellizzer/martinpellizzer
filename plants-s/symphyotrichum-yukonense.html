Symphyotrichum yukonense

Symphyotrichum yukonense, commonly known as Yukon heath aster, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Yukon region of Canada.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy composite flowers. The plant typically features dark green, narrow leaves and produces clusters of small, white to pale purple flowers in the late summer to early autumn. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in alpine and subalpine environments.

Yukon heath aster is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local pollinators.
