Spiraea menziesii pyramidata

Spiraea menziesii pyramidata, commonly known as the pyramidal spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to the western United States.

It features dense, upright growth with small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has finely cut, bright green leaves that provide ornamental value throughout the growing season. It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

Its compact form and showy flowers make it a popular choice for borders and rock gardens.
