Stanhopea grandiflora

Stanhopea grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered stanhopea, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces pendulous blooms with a unique coloration, often featuring a deep purple or violet hue with yellow and white markings. Its roots are epiphytic, meaning it grows on other plants rather than in soil.

Stanhopea grandiflora is valued in horticulture for its beauty and fragrance, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
