Solanum tuberosum f. alkka-imilla

Solanum tuberosum f. alkka-imilla, commonly known as the Alkka-Imilla potato, is a botanical variety of the common potato.

It is characterized by its unique tuber morphology, which includes elongated and sometimes twisted shapes. The plant belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and exhibits typical solanum growth habits. It is adapted to specific climatic conditions, often found in cooler, temperate regions.

This variety is valued for its distinct flavor and potential use in specialty culinary applications.
