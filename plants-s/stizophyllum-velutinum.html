Stizophyllum velutinum

Stizophyllum velutinum, commonly known as the velvet sponge.

It is a type of marine sponge that belongs to the family Stizophyllidae. The plant exhibits a soft, velvety texture due to its dense covering of spongin fibers. It is typically found in shallow coastal waters and is characterized by its pale to dark brown coloration.

Stizophyllum velutinum plays a role in marine ecosystems by providing habitat for various small organisms.
