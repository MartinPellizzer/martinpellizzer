Sisymbrium nudum var. brachycarpum

Sisymbrium nudum var. brachycarpum, commonly known as the short-carpelled field bean, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant features simple, alternate leaves with a hairy surface and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes. Its fruits are short, pod-like silique containing several seeds.

This species is often found in temperate regions and is adapted to thrive in nutrient-poor environments.
