Styrax limprichtii

Styrax limprichtii, commonly known as the Chinese styrax, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Styracaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and showy, fragrant white or pink flowers. The plant produces a resinous sap that has been historically used in traditional medicine and as a source of perfume. It typically grows in mountainous regions and thrives in well-drained soils.

Styrax limprichtii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitat.
