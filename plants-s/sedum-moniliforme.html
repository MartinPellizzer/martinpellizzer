Sedum moniliforme

Sedum moniliforme, commonly known as the bead plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features jointed, cylindrical stems that resemble beads, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique morphology and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among gardeners.
