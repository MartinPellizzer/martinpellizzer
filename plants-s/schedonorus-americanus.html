Schedonorus americanus

Schedonorus americanus, commonly known as the tall fescue, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its coarse, dark green blades and tufted growth habit. The plant has a deep root system that allows it to thrive in a variety of soil types and environmental conditions. It is widely used in turfgrass applications due to its durability and resistance to drought and pests.

Schedonorus americanus also plays a role in preventing soil erosion and supporting local wildlife.
