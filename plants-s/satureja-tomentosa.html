Satureja tomentosa

Satureja tomentosa, commonly known as wooly betony, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, pink to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has hairy, gray-green leaves that are aromatic and often used in traditional herbal medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Satureja tomentosa is valued for its medicinal properties and is also used as a culinary herb in some regions.
