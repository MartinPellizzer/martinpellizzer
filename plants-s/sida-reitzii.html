Sida reitzii

Sida reitzii, commonly known as the African mallow, is a flowering plant native to sub-Saharan Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual or perennial plant that typically grows up to 1 meter in height. The plant features alternate, ovate to lanceolate leaves with hairy surfaces, and it produces small, pale yellow to white flowers arranged in clusters. Sida reitzii is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
