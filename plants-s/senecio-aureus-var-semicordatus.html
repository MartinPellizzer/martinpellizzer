Senecio aureus var. semicordatus

Senecio aureus var. semicordatus, commonly known as the heartleaf senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is often cultivated in arid and semi-arid regions.
