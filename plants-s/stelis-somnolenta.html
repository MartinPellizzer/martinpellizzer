Stelis somnolenta

Stelis somnolenta, commonly known as the sleepy orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant produces delicate, often fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale in color. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the surrounding soil.

Stelis somnolenta is known for its slow growth and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
