Senecio huaynaputinaensis

Senecio huaynaputinaensis, commonly known as the Huayna Putina groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of southern Peru.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant typically grows in rocky, arid environments, showcasing adaptations to harsh climatic conditions. Its yellow flowers are small and clustered, contributing to its ornamental value.

Due to its restricted habitat, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species.
