Senecio Capitatus Subsp. Campestris

Senecio capitatus subsp. campestris is a unique and lesser-known member of the Asteraceae family. Native to specific regions of southern Africa, this plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. Its compact growth habit and striking yellow flowers make it a visually appealing addition to gardens. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated and often overlooked by botanists and gardeners alike. Understanding its ecological role and horticultural potential is essential for its conservation and appreciation.
