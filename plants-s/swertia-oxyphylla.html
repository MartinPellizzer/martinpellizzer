Swertia oxyphylla

Swertia oxyphylla, commonly known as the Himalayan gentian, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine layer of white hair, which helps reduce water loss.

Swertia oxyphylla is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
