Syagrus elata

Syagrus elata, commonly known as the royal palm, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery, fan-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that develop into orange or red fruits, which are edible for some animals but not typically for humans. Syagrus elata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in landscaping in warm climates.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun exposure to thrive.
