Salacca graciliflora

Salacca graciliflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered bamboo palm, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and pinnate leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or white in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Salacca graciliflora is also valued for its ability to tolerate poor soil conditions and its air-purifying qualities.
