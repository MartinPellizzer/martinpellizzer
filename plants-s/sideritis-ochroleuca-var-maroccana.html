Sideritis ochroleuca var. maroccana

Sideritis ochroleuca var. maroccana, commonly known as Moroccan yellow heath, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region, particularly Morocco.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale yellow to white in color. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in high-altitude areas.

Due to its unique morphology and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
