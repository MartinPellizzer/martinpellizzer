Sinomenium heterophyllum

Sinomenium heterophyllum, commonly known as the Chinese climbing vine, is a member of the Rutaceae family.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and opposite, ovate leaves with serrated margins. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in axillary inflorescences. Its fruits are follicles that mature into red, fleshy berries.

Sinomenium heterophyllum is native to China and is often found in moist, shaded environments in forests.
