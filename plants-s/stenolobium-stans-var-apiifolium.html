Stenolobium stans var. apiifolium

Stenolobium stans var. apiifolium, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has narrow, strap-shaped leaves that are often arranged in a basal rosette, and it thrives in well-drained soils. It is commonly used in gardens for its ornamental value and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.

The variety *apiifolium* is distinguished by its finely divided, parsley-like foliage, which adds a unique visual appeal.
