Saxifraga stellaris subsp. robusta

Saxifraga stellaris subsp.

robusta, commonly known as the star saxifrage, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Arctic and alpine regions of Europe and Asia. It features dense clusters of small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant has dark green, slightly succulent leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous areas. Saxifraga stellaris subsp.

robusta is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in alpine ecosystems.
