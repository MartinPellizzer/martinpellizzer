Senecio magnicalyculatus

Senecio magnicalyculatus, commonly known as the large-leaved groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant features large, fleshy leaves and produces yellow ray flowers surrounded by tubular flowers. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological importance, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
