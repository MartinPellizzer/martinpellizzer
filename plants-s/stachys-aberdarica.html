Stachys aberdarica

Stachys aberdarica, commonly known as the Armenian hedgehog.

It is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus and Central Asia. The plant features upright stems covered in dense, spiny bracts that give it its distinctive appearance. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, often with a bluish-green hue.

It produces small, pale purple to pink flowers in clusters, typically blooming in late summer.
