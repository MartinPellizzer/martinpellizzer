Saxifraga meeboldii

Saxifraga meeboldii, commonly known as the Meebold's saxifrage, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It typically thrives in rocky and alpine environments, where it forms dense mats of foliage. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or white, blooming in late summer. Its leaves are thick and succulent, adapted to retain moisture in arid conditions.

Saxifraga meeboldii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in challenging, high-altitude habitats.
