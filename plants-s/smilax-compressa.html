Smilax compressa

Smilax compressa, commonly known as the smooth smilax, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a perennial vine characterized by its woody stems and spiny, compound leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into red, berry-like fruits. Smilax compressa is often found in woodland edges, thickets, and along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it an important part of the ecosystem, providing food for various wildlife species.
