Spinoliva ilicifolia

Spinoliva ilicifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf spurge, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
