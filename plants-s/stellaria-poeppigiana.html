Stellaria poeppigiana

Stellaria poeppigiana, commonly known as the pale starwort, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, star-shaped white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often prostrate or ascending.

Stellaria poeppigiana is often found in rocky or clay soils and is adapted to thrive in temperate climates.
