Spiranthes longispicata

Spiranthes longispicata, commonly known as the long-spurred spiral orchid, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its delicate, spiraling arrangement of flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and wet meadows. Its flowers are usually white or pale green, with a distinctive elongated spur that houses the nectar.

Spiranthes longispicata is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in supporting local pollinator populations.
