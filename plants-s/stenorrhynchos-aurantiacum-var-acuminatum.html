Stenorrhynchos aurantiacum var. acuminatum

Stenorrhynchos aurantiacum var. acuminatum, commonly known as the orange-stemmed orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant orange stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
