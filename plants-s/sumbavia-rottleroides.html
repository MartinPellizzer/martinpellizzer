Sumbavia rottleroides

Sumbavia rottleroides, commonly known as the African star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple star-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, dark blue berries that are edible for humans and animals. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Sumbavia rottleroides is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
