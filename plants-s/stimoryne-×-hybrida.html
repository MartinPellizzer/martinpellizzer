Stimoryne × hybrida

Stimoryne × hybrida, commonly known as the hybrid stimoryne, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apocynaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant is native to tropical regions and thrives in warm, humid climates. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces fleshy, berry-like fruits.

Stimoryne × hybrida is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
