Spiraea Densiflora Var. Splendens

Spiraea densiflora var. splendens is a striking ornamental shrub known for its vibrant, showy blooms. Native to the mountainous regions of western North America, it thrives in cooler climates and well-drained soils. This variety is particularly prized for its clusters of pink to white flowers that appear in late spring. Its compact growth habit makes it ideal for small gardens or as a border plant. With its elegant appearance and easy care requirements, it remains a popular choice among gardeners and landscapers alike.
