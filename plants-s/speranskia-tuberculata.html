Speranskia tuberculata

Speranskia tuberculata, commonly known as the mountain hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture and is often found in forested areas. It produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small seeds.

Speranskia tuberculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
