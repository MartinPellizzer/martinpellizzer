Stachytarpheta dichotoma var. neocaledonica

Stachytarpheta dichotoma var. neocaledonica, commonly known as the New Caledonia stachy.

It is a shrub native to the tropical regions of New Caledonia, characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, two-lipped flowers. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for tropical and subtropical gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant floral display, which attracts pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
