Stenostephanus putumayensis

Stenostephanus putumayensis, commonly known as the Andean mountain orchid, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, greenish-yellow blooms with intricate patterns that attract specific pollinators. It thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found in cloud forests and alpine meadows.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
