Scleranthus polybotrys

Scleranthus polybotrys, commonly known as the Chinese sandmat, is a flowering plant native to China and other parts of East Asia.

It is a herbaceous annual plant that typically grows in dry, sandy soils and arid environments. The plant features small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, and its leaves are narrow and hairy. Scleranthus polybotrys is characterized by its robust root system, which helps it survive in harsh, nutrient-poor conditions.
