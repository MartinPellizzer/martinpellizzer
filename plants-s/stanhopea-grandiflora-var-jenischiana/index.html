Stanhopea Grandiflora Var. Jenischiana

Large-flowered stanhopea jenischiana, scientifically known as Stanhopea grandiflora var. jenischiana, is a rare and striking orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador. This species is celebrated for its large, fragrant, and brightly colored flowers that bloom in late spring. It belongs to the Stanhopea genus, which is known for its unique floral structures and ecological significance. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific care to flourish. Due to its limited distribution and habitat threats, it is considered a vulnerable species in conservation efforts.
