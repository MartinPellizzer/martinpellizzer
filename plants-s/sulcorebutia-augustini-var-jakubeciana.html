Sulcorebutia augustini var. jakubeciana

Sulcorebutia augustini var. jakubeciana, commonly known as the Jakubec's sun rose, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rounded growth habit. The plant features fleshy, ribbed stems that store water, allowing it to survive in dry conditions. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, blooming in spring or early summer.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its hardiness and ornamental appeal.
