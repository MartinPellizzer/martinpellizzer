Serratula bornmuelleri

Serratula bornmuelleri, commonly known as the Sierra Nevada groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of California and Nevada.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high elevations. The plant features dark green, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers clustered in dense heads. It is adapted to cold, arid environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

Serratula bornmuelleri is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
