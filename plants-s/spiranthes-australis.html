Spiranthes australis

Spiranthes australis, commonly known as the Australian spiral orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its delicate, spiral-shaped inflorescence. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as wetlands and forest floors. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, and it produces small, fleshy seeds.

Spiranthes australis plays a role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators like bees and moths.
