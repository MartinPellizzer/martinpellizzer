Senecio taxifolius

Senecio taxifolius, commonly known as the African Daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, surrounded by white ray florets. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Senecio taxifolius is also valued for its ornamental beauty and ability to attract pollinators.
