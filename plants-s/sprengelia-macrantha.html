Sprengelia macrantha

Sprengelia macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered sprengelia, is a striking member of the Cactaceae family native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, columnar stem and produces large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has spiny areoles and is adapted to arid environments, making it a resilient species. Its foliage consists of reduced, spiny leaves that help reduce water loss.

Sprengelia macrantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
