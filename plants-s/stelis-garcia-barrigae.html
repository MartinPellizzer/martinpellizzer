Stelis garcia-barrigae

Stelis garcia-barrigae, commonly known as the Garcia-Barriga's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its epiphytic growth habit, meaning it grows on the surfaces of trees or other structures. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale green, with a subtle fragrance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media for optimal growth.

Stelis garcia-barrigae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
