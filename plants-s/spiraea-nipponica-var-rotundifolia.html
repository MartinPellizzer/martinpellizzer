Spiraea nipponica var. rotundifolia

Spiraea nipponica var. rotundifolia, commonly known as Japanese spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features upright, multi-stemmed growth with narrow, ovate leaves that are often rounded at the base. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers in early summer, which attract pollinators. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability in garden settings.
