Stachys hians

Stachys hians, commonly known as the silverweed or silverleaf stachys, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-gray leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces spikes of small, pale purple to blue flowers in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Stachys hians is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and modest flowering display.
