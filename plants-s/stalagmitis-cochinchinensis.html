Stalagmitis cochinchinensis

Stalagmitis cochinchinensis, commonly known as the Vietnamese star anise.

It is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly Vietnam. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, star-shaped fruit, which is used in traditional medicine and as a spice. Its botanical features include opposite leaves and small, fragrant flowers.

The fruit develops from a single ovary and is typically harvested when it turns dark brown.
