Sorbaria millefolium

Sorbaria millefolium, commonly known as false toadflax, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It features a slender, upright stem that can grow up to 1 meter in height. The plant has finely divided, feathery leaves that give it a delicate appearance. Its small, yellow flowers are arranged in racemes and bloom in early summer.

Sorbaria millefolium is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.
