Stilbe zeyheri

Stilbe zeyheri, commonly known as the Zeyher's broom.

It is a perennial shrub native to South Africa, characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, needle-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers in the spring, which are followed by small, woody pods containing seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Stilbe zeyheri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its distinctive appearance.
