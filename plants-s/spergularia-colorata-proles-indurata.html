Spergularia colorata proles indurata

Spergularia colorata proles indurata, commonly known as the hard-stemmed sandspurry, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that thrives in sandy or gravelly soils, often found in coastal and inland dunes.
