Secamone brachystigma f. densiflora

Secamone brachystigma f. densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered secamone, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apocynaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense clusters of small, tubular flowers. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptic, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. The flowers are usually yellowish-green to greenish-white, and the plant produces elliptic to globose berries.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and is often found in moist, shaded habitats.
