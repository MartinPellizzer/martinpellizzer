Scutellaria bambusetorum

Scutellaria bambusetorum, commonly known as the bamboo orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of tubular, violet-blue flowers. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slight pubescence.

Scutellaria bambusetorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic beauty.
