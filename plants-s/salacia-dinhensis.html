Salacia dinhensis

Salacia dinhensis, commonly known as the Dinh's salacia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Salaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Vietnam. The plant is characterized by its woody stems and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. Its leaves are typically elliptical and have a smooth, glossy surface.

Salacia dinhensis is often found in humid, shaded environments within tropical forests.
