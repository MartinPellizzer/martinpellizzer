Scrophularia hastata

Scrophularia hastata, commonly known as narrow-leaved figwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are tubular and usually pink or purple, arranged in clusters at the ends of stems.

Scrophularia hastata is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
