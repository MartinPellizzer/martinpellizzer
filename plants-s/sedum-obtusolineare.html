Sedum obtusolineare

Sedum obtusolineare, commonly known as the blunt-leaf stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to parts of North America.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically oval or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Sedum obtusolineare is known for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
