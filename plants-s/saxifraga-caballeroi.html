Saxifraga caballeroi

Saxifraga caballeroi, commonly known as the Sierra Madre saxifrage, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Mexico.

It is a low-growing perennial with fleshy, basal leaves that form a rosette. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to high-altitude environments.

Saxifraga caballeroi is often found in alpine meadows and is a characteristic species of the Sierra Madre mountain range.
