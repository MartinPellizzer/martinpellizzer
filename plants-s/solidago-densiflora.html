Solidago densiflora

Solidago densiflora, commonly known as denseflower goldenrod, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes.

Denseflower goldenrod is valued for its ecological role in supporting pollinators and its ornamental appeal in native plant gardens.
