Senecio calamifolius

Senecio calamifolius, commonly known as the deadly nightshade.

This plant is a member of the Asteraceae family and is native to South Africa. It is characterized by its striking, dark green leaves with a waxy coating and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant is toxic due to the presence of alkaloids, making it dangerous to both animals and humans.

It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
