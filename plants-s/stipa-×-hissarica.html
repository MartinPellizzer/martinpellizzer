Stipa × hissarica

Stipa × hissarica, commonly known as the Hissar grass, is a hybrid species within the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and tufted inflorescences that consist of spikelets. The plant typically grows in temperate regions and is often found in meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Stipa × hissarica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
