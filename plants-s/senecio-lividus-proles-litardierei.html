Senecio lividus proles litardierei

Senecio lividus proles litardierei, commonly known as the blue senecio, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features striking blue-gray foliage with a waxy texture, adapted to arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
