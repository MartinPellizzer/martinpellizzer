Siphocampylus platysiphon

Siphocampylus platysiphon, commonly known as the broad-siphon milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its legume fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.
