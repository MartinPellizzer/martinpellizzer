Solanum fonckii

Solanum fonckii, commonly known as the Fonck's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and peppers. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the evening, and its berries are typically red when ripe. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and shrublands.

Due to its toxic properties, it is important to handle the plant with care.
