Sideritis vagans

Sideritis vagans, commonly known as the wandering sainfoin, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of the Middle East.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

Sideritis vagans is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is valued for its medicinal properties.
