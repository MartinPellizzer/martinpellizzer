Sipapoa cordata

Sipapoa cordata, commonly known as the cordate bulbous orchid, is a species native to South Africa.

It is a bulbous plant that produces striking, showy flowers with a distinctive heart-shaped appearance. The plant features long, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a period of dormancy during the colder months.

Sipapoa cordata is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens with specific care requirements.
