Sorbus kiukiangensis var. crossotocalyx

Sorbus kiukiangensis var. crossotocalyx, commonly known as the Chinese whitebeam, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its small, white to pale yellow flowers that appear in clusters, followed by small, red or orange berries. The leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a serrated margin. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland or rocky habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
