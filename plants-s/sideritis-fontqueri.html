Sideritis fontqueri

Sideritis fontqueri, commonly known as the Iberian ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climates. It produces small, tubular flowers that are often pink or purple in color.

Sideritis fontqueri is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
