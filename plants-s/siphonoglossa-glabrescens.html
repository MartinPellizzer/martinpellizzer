Siphonoglossa glabrescens

Siphonoglossa glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth-tongued orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to certain regions of South America, particularly in Brazil and Paraguay. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and glabrous. Its flowers are typically small and greenish-white, with a subtle fragrance.

Siphonoglossa glabrescens is often found in shaded, moist habitats such as forest understories.
