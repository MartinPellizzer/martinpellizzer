Scutellaria oreophila

Scutellaria oreophila, commonly known as the mountain skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its rugged appearance.

Scutellaria oreophila thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.
