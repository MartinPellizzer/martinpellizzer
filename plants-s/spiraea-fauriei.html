Spiraea fauriei

Spiraea fauriei, commonly known as the weeping spiraea, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its cascading clusters of small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has arching branches that give it a graceful, weeping appearance, making it a popular ornamental plant. Its leaves are typically ovate and dark green, providing a striking contrast to the flowers.

Spiraea fauriei thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.
