Sideritis × costa-talensii

Sideritis × costa-talensii, commonly known as the hybrid sainfoin, is a flowering plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Sideritis costae and Sideritis talensii, characterized by its aromatic, glandular leaves and spikes of small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, often in mountainous regions of the Mediterranean. Its flowers are usually pale blue to purple in color, and it is known for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional herbal remedies.

Sideritis × costa-talensii is valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
