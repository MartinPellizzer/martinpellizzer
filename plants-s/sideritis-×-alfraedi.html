Sideritis × alfraedi

Sideritis × alfraedi, commonly known as the hybrid sainfoin, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. The plant features slender, upright stems covered in fine hairs and produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly aromatic scent when crushed.

Sideritis × alfraedi is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its potential health benefits.
