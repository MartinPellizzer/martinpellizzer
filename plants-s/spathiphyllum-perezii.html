Spathiphyllum perezii

Spathiphyllum perezii, commonly known as the Perez's peace lily, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy foliage and its striking, white, spatulate inflorescence. The plant produces small, green berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans. It thrives in low-light conditions and requires moderate watering, making it a popular houseplant.

Its air-purifying qualities also contribute to its widespread cultivation.
