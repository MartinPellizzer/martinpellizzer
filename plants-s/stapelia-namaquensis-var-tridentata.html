Stapelia namaquensis var. tridentata

Stapelia namaquensis var. tridentata, commonly known as the Tridentate Stapelia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its unique, waxy, and often ribbed stems. The plant produces striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in arid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
