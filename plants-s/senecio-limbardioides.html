Senecio limbardioides

Senecio limbardioides, commonly known as the Limbard's groundsel, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small yellow flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
