Senecio ibarii

Senecio ibarii, commonly known as the Cape Verde ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Cape Verde Islands.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in coastal areas. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, and its leaves are usually narrow and grayish-green.

Senecio ibarii is adapted to arid conditions and is often found in dry, sun-exposed habitats.
