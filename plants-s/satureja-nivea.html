Satureja nivea

Satureja nivea, commonly known as snow savory, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with small, narrow leaves and white or pale purple flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in alpine environments. Its aromatic leaves contain essential oils that contribute to its distinctive scent.

Satureja nivea is valued in traditional herbal medicine for its purported digestive and antiseptic properties.
