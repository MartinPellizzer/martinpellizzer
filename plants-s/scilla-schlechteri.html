Scilla schlechteri

Scilla schlechteri, commonly known as the Turkish bluebell, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It produces delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple, typically blooming in early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge from a basal rosette of bulbs. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and woodland settings.

Scilla schlechteri is also valued for its early blooming habit and ornamental appeal.
