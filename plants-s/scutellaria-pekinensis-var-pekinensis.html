Scutellaria pekinensis var. pekinensis

Scutellaria pekinensis var. pekinensis, commonly known as Peking skullcap, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shady environments such as forests and stream banks. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, violet-blue flowers in whorled clusters. Its flowering period occurs in late spring to early summer.

The genus Scutellaria is known for its medicinal properties, and this variety is no exception.
