Salacia divergens

Salacia divergens, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Salicaceae, which includes willows and poplars. The plant is characterized by its pendulous clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a serrated edge.

Salacia divergens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
