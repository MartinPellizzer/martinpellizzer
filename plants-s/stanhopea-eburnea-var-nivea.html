Stanhopea eburnea var. nivea

Stanhopea eburnea var. nivea, commonly known as the white-stemmed stanhopea, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, slender pseudobulbs and linear, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated by enthusiasts for its beauty and unique morphology.
