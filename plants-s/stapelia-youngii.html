Stapelia youngii

Stapelia youngii, commonly known as the Cape Town star.

It is a succulent plant native to South Africa, characterized by its distinctive star-shaped flowers. The plant produces bright yellow or orange blooms that are often covered in a fine, fuzzy coating. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in arid environments.

Stapelia youngii is a popular choice for collectors due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
