Scleranthus folliotii

Scleranthus folliotii, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sandparsley, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It typically grows in sandy or disturbed soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Scleranthus folliotii is an annual or biennial plant with a short life cycle adapted to harsh environmental conditions.
