Synotis reniformis

Synotis reniformis, commonly known as the kidney bean vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces pods that contain edible beans, similar to common beans but often smaller in size. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its botanical features make it both a useful crop and an ornamental plant in suitable environments.
