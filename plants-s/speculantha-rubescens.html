Speculantha rubescens

Speculantha rubescens, commonly known as the red-flowered daisy, is a herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, bright red petals that surround a yellow central disk, making it visually striking. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It typically blooms from late spring to early summer, thriving in full sun and well-drained soil.

Speculantha rubescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
