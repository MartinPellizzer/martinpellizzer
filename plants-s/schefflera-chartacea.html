Schefflera chartacea

Schefflera chartacea, commonly known as the paper shield plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that are thick and leathery, often with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by black, spherical berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular houseplant.

Schefflera chartacea is known for its ability to purify air and adapt well to indoor environments.
