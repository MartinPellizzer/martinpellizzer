Saintpaulia ionantha subsp. grandifolia

Saintpaulia ionantha subsp.

grandifolia, commonly known as the African violet, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is native to eastern Africa. It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and vibrant, velvety petals that come in a wide range of colors.

The plant produces showy, spurred flowers that bloom continuously throughout the growing season. Its leaves are typically dark green and slightly hairy, providing a striking contrast to the colorful blooms.

This species is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
