Sedum jahandiezii subsp. persicinum

Sedum jahandiezii subsp.

persicinum, commonly known as Persian stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the Middle East and Central Asia. It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink.

The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it both hardy and visually appealing.
