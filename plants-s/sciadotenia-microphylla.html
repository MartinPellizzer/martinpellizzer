Sciadotenia microphylla

Sciadotenia microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved sand mat, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that thrives in sandy and rocky soils, often found in arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and fleshy, adapted to conserve water in dry conditions.

Sciadotenia microphylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in xeriscapes due to its drought tolerance.
