Solanum hoehnei

Solanum hoehnei, commonly known as the Hoehne's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and peppers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a rough texture, while its flowers are usually white or purple.

The fruit is a fleshy berry that contains several seeds, and the plant is known for its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties.
