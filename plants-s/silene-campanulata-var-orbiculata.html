Silene campanulata var. orbiculata

Silene campanulata var. orbiculata, commonly known as the globe bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its orbicular, hairy leaves and showy, bell-shaped pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, preferring well-drained soils and full sun. It is a perennial herb that produces capsule fruits containing numerous small seeds.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine displays.
