Stellaria uda

Stellaria uda, commonly known as starwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Stellaria uda is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
