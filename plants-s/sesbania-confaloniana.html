Sesbania confaloniana

Sesbania confaloniana, commonly known as the tropical sesbania, is a fast-growing leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellow flowers. The plant is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen through symbiotic relationships with Rhizobium bacteria, enriching the soil. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often used for soil stabilization and as a forage crop.

Its rapid growth makes it a valuable species for ecological restoration projects.
