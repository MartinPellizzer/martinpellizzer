Solanum regnelii

Solanum regnelii, commonly known as the Regnell's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in summer, followed by berries that turn from yellow to red as they mature. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Solanum regnelii belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae), which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes.
