Smilax densiflora

Smilax densiflora, commonly known as the thickleaf smilax, is a perennial shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and heart-shaped, glossy leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into red, fleshy berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and thickets.

Smilax densiflora is known for its hardy nature and ability to spread through rhizomes.
