Senecio trachylepus

Senecio trachylepus, commonly known as the woolly groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique texture and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among gardeners.
