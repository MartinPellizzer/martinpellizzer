Sedum aoikon

Sedum aoikon, commonly known as the stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to arid and rocky environments.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in dry conditions. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red, typically in the summer months. It is often used in landscaping for its low maintenance and drought tolerance.

Sedum aoikon is also valued for its ability to prevent soil erosion in rocky or sandy soils.
