Solenostemon monostachyus var. marrubiifolius

Solenostemon monostachyus var. marrubiifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaf red stem plant, is a tropical perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving them a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red, arranged in dense spikes. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often grown as an ornamental plant for its striking foliage and flowering display.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
