Stellaria catalaunica

Stellaria catalaunica, commonly known as the Catalan starwort, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It typically reaches a height of about 10 to 20 centimeters and features small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has thin, branching stems covered with fine hairs and possesses opposite, narrow, ovate leaves. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows, stream banks, and woodland edges.

Stellaria catalaunica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
