Statice filicaulis proles marizii

Statice filicaulis proles marizii, commonly known as the Marizii filicaulis statice, is a flowering plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soils with full sun exposure.

This species is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
