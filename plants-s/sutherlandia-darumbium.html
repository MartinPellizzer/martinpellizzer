Sutherlandia darumbium

Sutherlandia darumbium, commonly known as the cancer bush, is a member of the Fabaceae family and is native to southern Africa.

It is a small shrub characterized by its yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant contains various bioactive compounds, including alkaloids and flavonoids, which have been studied for their potential medicinal properties. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.

Due to its traditional use in herbal medicine, it has attracted interest for its possible therapeutic applications.
