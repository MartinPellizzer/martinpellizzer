Stapelia italica

Stapelia italica, commonly known as the bird's nest plant, is a succulent native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, wrinkled, leaf-like structures that resemble a bird's nest, hence its common name. The plant produces vibrant orange to red flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
