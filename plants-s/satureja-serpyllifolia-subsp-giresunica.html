Satureja serpyllifolia subsp. giresunica

Satureja serpyllifolia subsp.

giresunica, commonly known as Giresun thyme, is a subspecies of the thyme plant native to Turkey. It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its aromatic, small, needle-like leaves.

The plant produces tiny, pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine regions.

This subspecies is valued for its culinary and medicinal uses, similar to other members of the Satureja genus.
