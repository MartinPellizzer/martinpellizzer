Stilbe ericoides

Stilbe ericoides, commonly known as the dwarf heath, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the western United States.

It typically thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions. The plant features small, leathery leaves that are dark green and densely packed, giving it a compact appearance. Its flowers are usually pink or red, appearing in clusters during the spring and early summer.

Stilbe ericoides is well adapted to harsh environmental conditions, making it a resilient plant in its native habitat.
