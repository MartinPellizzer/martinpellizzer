Saxifraga × opdalensis

Saxifraga × opdalensis, commonly known as the Opdal saxifrage, is a hybrid plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Norway.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense, mat-like colonies on rocky slopes and scree. The plant features broad, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. Its flowers are small, clustered in cymes, and usually display shades of pink or white.

Saxifraga × opdalensis is well adapted to harsh alpine conditions, thriving in full sun and well-drained soils.
