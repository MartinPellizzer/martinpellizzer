Senecio macrobotrys

Senecio macrobotrys, commonly known as the giant senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has tall, slender stems that can reach heights of up to 10 feet. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, and it thrives in well-drained, sandy soils.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
