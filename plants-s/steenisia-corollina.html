Steenisia corollina

Steenisia corollina, commonly known as the coral bell, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem.

Steenisia corollina is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
