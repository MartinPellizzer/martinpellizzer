Symphytum × hyerense

Symphytum × hyerense, commonly known as the hybrid wood sorrel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Brassicaceae and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and prefers well-drained soils in full sun to partial shade. It is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and flowers, and it can naturalize in suitable conditions.

The species is also valued for its historical use in traditional medicine.
