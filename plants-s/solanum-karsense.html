Solanum karsense

Solanum karsense, commonly known as the Kars nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the highlands of Turkey.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its tubular, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to temperate climates. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and slightly hairy, while its fruits are small, berry-like, and contain several seeds.

Solanum karsense is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and potential medicinal properties.
