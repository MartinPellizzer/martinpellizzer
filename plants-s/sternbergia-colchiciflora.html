Sternbergia colchiciflora

Sternbergia colchiciflora, commonly known as the autumn crocus, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Colchicaceae.

It is characterized by its striking violet or purple flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has narrow, strap-like leaves that emerge from a bulbous underground stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers sunny to partially shaded locations.

Sternbergia colchiciflora is native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.
