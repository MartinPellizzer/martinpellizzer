Sapium erythrospermum

Sapium erythrospermum, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red, lantern-shaped fruits that persist through winter, making it a popular ornamental plant. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly toothed margin. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Its botanical features make it both visually striking and ecologically beneficial in gardens.
