Stenorrhynchos pilosum

Stenorrhynchos pilosum, commonly known as the hairy stenorrhynchos, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, with a distinctive coloration that varies by species.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
