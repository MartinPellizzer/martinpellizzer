Stichorkis trachyglossa

Stichorkis trachyglossa, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rock heath, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in open habitats such as grasslands and shrublands. The plant features small, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers. It is adapted to arid or semi-arid conditions, showing resilience to drought.
