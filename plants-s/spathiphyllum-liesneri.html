Spathiphyllum liesneri

Spathiphyllum liesneri, commonly known as the Emerald Queen peace lily, is a tropical evergreen plant native to the Philippines.

It features broad, glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and can grow up to 12 inches in length. The plant produces elegant, white spathes that enclose small, greenish-white flowers, which are typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in low-light conditions and requires moderate watering, making it a popular houseplant.

Its air-purifying qualities and ease of care further enhance its appeal in indoor gardening.
