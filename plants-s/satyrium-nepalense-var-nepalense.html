Satyrium nepalense var. nepalense

Satyrium nepalense var. nepalense, commonly known as the Nepal orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Himalayan regions of Nepal and northern India.

It is a terrestrial herb with a short, upright stem and produces delicate, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinct lip. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest understories and thrives in cool, humid climates. Its flowering period occurs during the spring and early summer months.

The species is characterized by its unique floral structure and is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts due to its ecological and horticultural significance.
