Spartium pinastrifolium

Spartium pinastrifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf sicklebush, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant features small, compound leaves with a distinctive pinna arrangement, and its flowers are typically yellow or greenish-yellow. Spartium pinastrifolium produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is valued for its ornamental and ecological significance.
