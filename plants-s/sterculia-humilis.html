Sterculia humilis

It belongs to the Malvaceae family, which includes hibiscus and cotton plants. The plant is characterized by its simple, ovate leaves and small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.
