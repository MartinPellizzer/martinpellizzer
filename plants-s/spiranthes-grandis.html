Spiranthes grandis

Spiranthes grandis, commonly known as the showy lady's-tresses, is a species of orchid native to North America.

It is characterized by its delicate, spiraling arrangement of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and wet meadows. It has a slender, unbranched stem and produces small, oval-shaped leaves.

Spiranthes grandis is a perennial herb that blooms from late summer into early fall.
