Solanum chomatophilum f. angustifoliolum

Solanum chomatophilum f. angustifoliolum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf nightshade, is a botanical variant characterized by its slender, elongated leaves.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades, tomatoes, and potatoes. The plant typically features small, star-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale purple in color. Its fruit is a small, fleshy berry that matures to a yellow or orange hue.

This subspecies is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in shaded, moist environments.
