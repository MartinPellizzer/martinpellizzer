Salvia splendens

Salvia splendens, commonly known as the scarlet sage, is a tropical shrub native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant red, orange, or yellow tubular flowers that bloom in dense clusters. The plant has opposite, hairy, ovate leaves that are often grayish-green in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Its nectar-rich flowers attract bees, butterflies, and other pollinators.
