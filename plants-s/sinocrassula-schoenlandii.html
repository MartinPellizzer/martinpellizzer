Sinocrassula schoenlandii

Sinocrassula schoenlandii, commonly known as the Chinese Crassula, is a succulent plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Crassulaceae and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant features small, fleshy leaves that are typically green or reddish in color, often with a waxy coating. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the spring or early summer.

Sinocrassula schoenlandii is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
