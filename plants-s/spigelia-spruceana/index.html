Spigelia Spruceana

Spigelia spruceana is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the southeastern United States. It is known for its unique, tubular flowers that bloom in late spring. This species thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wetlands. Its striking red and yellow petals make it a standout among native flora. Due to habitat loss, Spigelia spruceana is now considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to survive.
