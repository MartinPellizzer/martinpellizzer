Senecio martirensis

Senecio martirensis, commonly known as the Martirensis Groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and ragworts. The plant is characterized by its low-growing habit, with narrow, grass-like leaves and small yellow flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
