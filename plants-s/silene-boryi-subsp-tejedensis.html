Silene boryi subsp. tejedensis

Silene boryi subsp.

tejedensis, commonly known as the Tejed mountain catchfly, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula. It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pinkish-purple flowers that bloom in early summer.

The plant typically grows in rocky or calcareous soils in mountainous regions. It has small, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and its stems are erect and branching.
