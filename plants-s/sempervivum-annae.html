Sempervivum annae

Sempervivum annae, commonly known as the Annae's houseleek, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe.

It features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the edges. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It is well adapted to rocky and dry environments, making it a hardy and drought-tolerant species.

Sempervivum annae is often grown as an ornamental plant for its compact growth and aesthetic appeal.
