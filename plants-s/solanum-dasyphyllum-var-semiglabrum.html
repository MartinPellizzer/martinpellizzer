Solanum dasyphyllum var. semiglabrum

Solanum dasyphyllum var. semiglabrum, commonly known as the roughleaf nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its hairy, ovate leaves and clusters of small, white to violet flowers. The plant produces berries that are typically yellow or orange when mature. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its toxic properties, it is important to handle the plant with care.
