Schismatoglottis mutata

Schismatoglottis mutata, commonly known as the dwarf lady's slipper, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, compact growth habit and features striking, showy flowers with a unique slipper-shaped pouch. The plant typically produces dark green, strap-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. Schismatoglottis mutata thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
