Senecio graciliflorus var. hookeri

Senecio graciliflorus var. hookeri, commonly known as the Hooker's slenderflower senecio, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow to orange. Its leaves are typically narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
