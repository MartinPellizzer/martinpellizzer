Sonchus asper f. hydrophilus

Sonchus asper f. hydrophilus, commonly known as the water prickly lettuce, is a variant of the prickly lettuce species.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its rough, hairy leaves and yellow flowers. This plant typically grows in moist environments such as wet meadows and stream banks. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped with a serrated margin.

The plant is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
