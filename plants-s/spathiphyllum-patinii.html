Spathiphyllum patinii

Spathiphyllum patinii, commonly known as the Patin's peace lily, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its dark green, waxy leaves and elegant white spatulate inflorescence. The plant produces a single, showy spathe that encloses a cluster of small, yellowish-white berries. It thrives in low-light conditions and is popular as a decorative indoor plant due to its air-purifying properties.

Spathiphyllum patinii is also valued for its ability to tolerate a wide range of environmental conditions.
