Saussurea sinuatoides var. glabrescens

Saussurea sinuatoides var. glabrescens, commonly known as the glabrous narrowleafed saussurea, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its narrow, glabrous (hairless) leaves and yellow flower heads. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
