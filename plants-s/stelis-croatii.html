Stelis croatii

Stelis croatii, commonly known as the Croatian stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant has a short, creeping rhizome with clustered, narrow leaves. Its flowers are small, white or pale green, and often fragrant, appearing in clusters.

Stelis croatii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
