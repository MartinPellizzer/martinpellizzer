Sedum weinbergii

Sedum weinbergii, commonly known as the Mexican stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, which help it store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for gardeners in dry climates.
