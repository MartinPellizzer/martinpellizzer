Stelis acutula

Stelis acutula, commonly known as the acute stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments within tropical forests. The plant features a slender, erect stem with several nodes, and its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged along the stem. Its flowers are small and often white or pale green, clustered in a raceme at the top of the stem.

Stelis acutula is valued in horticulture for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
