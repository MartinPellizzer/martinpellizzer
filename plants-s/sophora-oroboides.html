Sophora oroboides

Sophora oroboides, commonly known as the Chinese wisteria, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it beneficial for soil enrichment.
