Stanhopea × thienii

Stanhopea × thienii, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Stanhopea serena and Stanhopea tatei.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in warm, humid environments. The plant produces strikingly colorful, fragrant flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. Its pseudobulbs are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native habitat.

Stanhopea × thienii is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
