Semnostachya galeopsis

Semnostachya galeopsis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ironweed, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, pale yellow to brown flowers arranged in a dense spike. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
