Sempervivum marmoreum f. rubicundum

Sempervivum marmoreum f. rubicundum, commonly known as the Red-veined Houseleek, is a succulent plant native to southern Europe.

It features rosettes of fleshy, thick leaves that are typically green with red or purple veins. The plant is well adapted to rocky and arid environments, thriving in full sun and well-draining soil. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and ease of care, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
