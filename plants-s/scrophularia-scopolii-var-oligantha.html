Scrophularia scopolii var. oligantha

Scrophularia scopolii var. oligantha, commonly known as the slender scrophularia, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, often found in Mediterranean climates. The plant features upright stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are tubular and usually pink or purple, arranged in clusters at the ends of the branches.

It is part of the Scrophulariaceae family, known for its medicinal and ornamental value in horticulture.
