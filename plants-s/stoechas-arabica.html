Stoechas arabica

Stoechas arabica, commonly known as the Persian violet, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its distinctive violet-blue flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has small, narrow leaves that are often hairy to the touch. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Stoechas arabica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate in mild climates.
