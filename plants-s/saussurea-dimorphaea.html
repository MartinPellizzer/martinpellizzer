Saussurea dimorphaea

Saussurea dimorphaea, commonly known as the Himalayan arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive arrowhead-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in cold, mountainous environments with well-drained soil.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
