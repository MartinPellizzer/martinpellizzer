Senecio frondosus

Senecio frondosus, commonly known as the fronded groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and distinctive foliage. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators.

Senecio frondosus is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
