Senecio ellipticus

Senecio ellipticus, commonly known as the narrowleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped cluster. It is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Senecio ellipticus is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
