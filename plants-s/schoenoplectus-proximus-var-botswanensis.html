Schoenoplectus proximus var. botswanensis

Schoenoplectus proximus var. botswanensis, commonly known as the Botswana bulrush, is a perennial wetland plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in shallow water or damp soils, often found in marshes, lakes, and riverine habitats. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Its rhizomes help stabilize soil and prevent erosion in aquatic environments.

This variant is distinguished by its specific geographic distribution and morphological traits adapted to its native habitat.
