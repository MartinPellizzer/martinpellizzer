Schoenoxiphium ludwigii

Schoenoxiphium ludwigii, commonly known as Ludwig's spirea, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers in the spring, which are arranged in cylindrical inflorescences. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a serrated margin.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.
