Sophronitis militaris

Sophronitis militaris, commonly known as the Military Orchid, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, often purple or pinkish flowers. The plant typically produces a single, showy bloom per stem, which appears during the spring or early summer. Its botanical features include strap-like leaves and a pseudobulb structure typical of many orchid species.

Sophronitis militaris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
