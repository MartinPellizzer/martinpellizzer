Stelis fragilis

Stelis fragilis, commonly known as the fragile stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its delicate, slender stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its fragile nature, it requires careful handling and specific growing conditions to flourish.
