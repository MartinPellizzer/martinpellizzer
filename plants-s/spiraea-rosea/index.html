Spiraea Rosea

Spiraea rosea is a graceful shrub known for its delicate, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun. This plant is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators. Its compact size makes it ideal for borders, rock gardens, and as a foundation plant. With its charming appearance and low maintenance needs, Spiraea rosea is a favorite among gardeners and landscape designers.
