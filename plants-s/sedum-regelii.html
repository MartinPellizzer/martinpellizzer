Sedum regelii

Sedum regelii, commonly known as the silver rose, is a succulent plant native to Central Asia.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white wax, giving it a silvery appearance. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped, pink to red flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
