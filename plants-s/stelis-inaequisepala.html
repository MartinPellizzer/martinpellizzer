Stelis inaequisepala

Stelis inaequisepala, commonly known as the unequal-sepal stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions like Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant features a slender, wiry stem with numerous small, clustered pseudobulbs. Its flowers are typically white or pale yellow, with sepals of unequal size, giving it its scientific name.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, loose potting media for optimal growth.
