Syagrus itacambirana

Syagrus itacambirana, commonly known as the Itacambira palm, is a species native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It is a tall, slender palm with a straight trunk and pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces large, clustered inflorescences with yellowish flowers, which are followed by bright red fruits containing edible seeds. It thrives in humid, tropical conditions and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
