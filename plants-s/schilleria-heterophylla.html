Schilleria heterophylla

Schilleria heterophylla, commonly known as the variable leaf schilleria, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, often variegated leaves that can vary in shape and size depending on environmental conditions. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid climates. It produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Schilleria heterophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
