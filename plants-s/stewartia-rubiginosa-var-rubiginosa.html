Stewartia rubiginosa var. rubiginosa

Stewartia rubiginosa var. rubiginosa, commonly known as the red-stained stewartia, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, elongated leaves that turn a striking red or orange in the fall. The tree produces small, fragrant white flowers in early summer, followed by ornamental red or orange fruits. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
