Sapindus chinensis

Sapindus chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese soapberry tree, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Sapindaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The tree produces spherical, smooth, and spiny fruits that contain a single seed. Its bark is thick and fissured, and it is often found in dry, rocky areas.

The plant is noted for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine.
