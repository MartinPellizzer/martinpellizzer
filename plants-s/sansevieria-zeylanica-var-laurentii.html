Sansevieria zeylanica var. laurentii

Sansevieria zeylanica var. laurentii, commonly known as the Laurentii Snake Plant, is a striking evergreen perennial with upright, sword-shaped leaves that are dark green with prominent, light green to yellow variegation.

Native to tropical regions of Africa, it thrives in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it an excellent choice for indoor cultivation. The plant's foliage is adorned with intricate, wavy patterns that add a dramatic visual element to any space. It is also noted for its air-purifying qualities, capable of removing toxins such as formaldehyde and benzene from the air.

This variety is highly resilient, able to withstand neglect and thrive in a wide range of environmental conditions.
