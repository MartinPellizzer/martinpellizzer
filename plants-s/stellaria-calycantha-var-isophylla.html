Stellaria calycantha var. isophylla

Stellaria calycantha var. isophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf starwort, is a perennial herb native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The leaves are narrow and alternate, often with a slightly hairy surface. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments such as streambanks and forest floors.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in supporting local pollinators.
