Sophora pallida

Sophora pallida, commonly known as the white sophora, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces elongated seed pods that contain flat, winged seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or scrub habitats.

Sophora pallida is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine.
