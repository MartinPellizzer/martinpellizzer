Stelis zongoensis

Stelis zongoensis, commonly known as the Zongo orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It typically grows in high-altitude grasslands and rocky areas, where it thrives in cool, humid conditions. The plant has a slender stem with a few leaves and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pink. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the thin soil of its habitat.

Stelis zongoensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
