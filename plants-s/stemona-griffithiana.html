Stemona griffithiana

Stemona griffithiana, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a perennial herb native to China.

It features slender, erect stems that are often branched and covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue to purple in color, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly rough texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

The plant is valued not only for its aesthetic qualities but also for its traditional medicinal uses in Chinese herbal medicine.
