Stenocereus chacalapensis

Stenocereus chacalapensis, commonly known as the Chacalapense cactus, is a species native to the Sonoran Desert of North America.

It is a columnar cactus with a distinctive ribbed structure and spiny areoles. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by red, fleshy fruits. Its deep root system allows it to survive in arid environments with minimal water.

Stenocereus chacalapensis plays a vital role in its ecosystem by providing food and shelter for various desert animals.
