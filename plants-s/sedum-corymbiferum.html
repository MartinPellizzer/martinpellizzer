Sedum corymbiferum

Sedum corymbiferum, commonly known as the Autumn Sedum, is a perennial succulent native to the western United States.

It features upright, branching stems that bear clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange. The plant has fleshy, ribbed leaves that are typically blue-green in color, adapted for water storage. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its blooming period occurs in late summer to early fall, adding vibrant color to the landscape.
