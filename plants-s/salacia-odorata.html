Salacia odorata

Salacia odorata, commonly known as the fragrant salacia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Salicaceae, which includes willows and poplars. The plant is characterized by its ornamental leaves and clusters of small, fragrant yellow flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
