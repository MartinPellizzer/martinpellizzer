Sansevieria ensifolia

Sansevieria ensifolia, commonly known as the swordleaf snake plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with a subtle variegation of yellow or white. The plant is highly drought-tolerant, making it an excellent choice for low-maintenance indoor gardening. Its leaves can grow up to 2 meters in height, adding a striking vertical element to any space.

Sansevieria ensifolia is also known for its ability to purify indoor air, enhancing its appeal as both an ornamental and functional plant.
