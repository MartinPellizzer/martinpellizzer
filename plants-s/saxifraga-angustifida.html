Saxifraga angustifida

Saxifraga angustifida, commonly known as narrowleaf saxifrage, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight. The plant features upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white or gray hairs. Its small, white or pink flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, adding a delicate touch to its rugged habitat.

Saxifraga angustifida is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
