Sansevieria intermedia

Sansevieria intermedia, commonly known as the Snake Plant, is a hardy, evergreen perennial native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with yellow or white variegation. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it an ideal houseplant. Its leaves can grow up to 2 feet in length and are capable of absorbing carbon dioxide at night, improving indoor air quality.

Sansevieria intermedia is also valued for its resilience and ease of care, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced gardeners.
