Stellaria longipes var. laeta

Stellaria longipes var. laeta, commonly known as the cheerful starwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or meadow habitats. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs.

It is a low-growing perennial that spreads by rhizomes, forming dense mats in suitable environments.
