Samyda grewiifolia

Samyda grewiifolia, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a striking tropical plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling flames. The plant has compound leaves with long, narrow leaflets, and it thrives in full sun and well-drained soil. Samyda grewiifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and public spaces.

It is also valued for its ability to withstand drought and poor soil conditions.
