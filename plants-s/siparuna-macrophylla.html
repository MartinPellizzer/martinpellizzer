Siparuna macrophylla

Siparuna macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf siparuna, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Amazon Basin.

It is characterized by its glossy, elongated leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white flowers that bloom throughout the year, attracting various pollinators. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural use in warm climates.

Siparuna macrophylla thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.
