Schollera graminea

Schollera graminea, commonly known as the silver grass or silver thread grass, is a perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage and delicate, grass-like appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or brown in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Schollera graminea is often used in ornamental horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
