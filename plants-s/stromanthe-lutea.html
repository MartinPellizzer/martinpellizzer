Stromanthe lutea

Stromanthe lutea, commonly known as the Yellow Stromanthe, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with yellow and green streaks. The plant typically grows as an upright herb with broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Stromanthe lutea is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
