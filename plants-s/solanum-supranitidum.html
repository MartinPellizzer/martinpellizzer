Solanum supranitidum

Solanum supranitidum, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows in humid, tropical environments. The plant features small, white or pale green flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are usually shiny and have a distinctive silvery or pale underside.

Solanum supranitidum belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, which includes other plants like tomatoes and potatoes.
