Selenipedium × ainsworthii var. delicatum

Selenipedium × ainsworthii var. delicatum, commonly known as the delicate selenipedium, is a rare hybrid orchid native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, showy greenish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in mountainous forests. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a threatened species in the wild.
