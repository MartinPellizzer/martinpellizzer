Stipa glareosa var. nemegetica

Stipa glareosa var. nemegetica, commonly known as the Nemegetic feathergrass, is a grass species native to central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and dense, tufted growth habit. The plant produces narrow, grass-like leaves and panicles of small, spikelet-like flowers. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

Its ornamental value is appreciated in gardens for its delicate, feathery appearance.
