Senecio reissianus

Senecio reissianus, commonly known as the Reissian groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are usually linear and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are small and yellow.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
