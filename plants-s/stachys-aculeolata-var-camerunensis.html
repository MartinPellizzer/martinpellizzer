Stachys aculeolata var. camerunensis

Stachys aculeolata var. camerunensis, commonly known as Cameroon hedge-nettle, is a flowering plant native to Cameroon.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its hairy stems and opposite leaves with serrated edges. Its small, clustered flowers are typically pink or purple, and it produces small, dry fruits known as schizocarps.

This species is often found in moist, shaded habitats in the understory of tropical forests.
