Senecio polyanthemus

Senecio polyanthemus, commonly known as the Blue-Eyed Mary, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small blue or purple flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
