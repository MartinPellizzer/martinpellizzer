Staurogyne helferi

Staurogyne helferi, commonly known as the Helfer's water starwort, is a small aquatic plant native to South America.

It typically grows in slow-moving freshwater environments such as ponds and lakes. The plant has a rosette-like structure with floating leaves that are usually green and oval in shape. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, often appearing in clusters at the base of the plant.
