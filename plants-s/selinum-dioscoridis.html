Selinum dioscoridis

Selinum dioscoridis, commonly known as greater celandine, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Ranunculaceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of large, heart-shaped leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
