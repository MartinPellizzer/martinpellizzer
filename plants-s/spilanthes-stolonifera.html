Spilanthes stolonifera

Spilanthes stolonifera, commonly known as the stoloniferous bladderpod, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, open habitats such as prairies and wetlands. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often hairy, while its roots are tuberous and used in traditional medicine.

Spilanthes stolonifera is known for its peppery taste and has been used for various medicinal purposes by indigenous peoples.
