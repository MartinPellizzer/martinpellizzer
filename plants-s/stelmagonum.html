Stelmagonum

Stelmagonum, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its silver-green foliage and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in the evening. The plant produces berries that are typically green when immature and turn red when ripe. Stelmagonum is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in dry, sunny conditions.

It contains toxic compounds and is often considered a weed in agricultural settings.
