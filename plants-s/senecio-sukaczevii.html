Senecio sukaczevii

Senecio sukaczevii, commonly known as the Sukaczev's groundsel, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, thriving in arid and semi-arid environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and gray-green, with a rough texture, and it produces small yellow flowers in clusters.
