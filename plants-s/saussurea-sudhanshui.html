Saussurea sudhanshui

Saussurea sudhanshui, commonly known as the Sudhanshu saffron, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas, particularly in regions of India and Nepal. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stem and dense, feathery inflorescence that resembles a crown. Its flowers are typically pale yellow to white, and it produces small, dark brown seeds.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in alpine gardens.
