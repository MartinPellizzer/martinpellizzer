Sorghum rollii

Sorghum rollii, commonly known as the rolling sorghum, is a unique species within the grass family (Poaceae).

It is characterized by its distinctive, spirally twisted inflorescence, which gives it its common name. This plant is native to southern Africa and thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. Its botanical features include narrow, grass-like leaves and a compact growth habit.

Sorghum rollii is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its striking appearance.
