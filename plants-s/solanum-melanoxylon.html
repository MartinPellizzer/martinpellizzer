Solanum melanoxylon

Solanum melanoxylon, commonly known as the blackwood nightshade, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dark, woody stems and small, oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of white or pale purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its berries, which are typically black when mature, are toxic and should not be consumed.

Solanum melanoxylon is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
