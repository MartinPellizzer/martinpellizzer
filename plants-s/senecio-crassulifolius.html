Senecio crassulifolius

Senecio crassulifolius, commonly known as the thick-leaved senecio, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a compact inflorescence, which bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in dry climates.
