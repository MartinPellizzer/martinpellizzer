Schoberia dumortieri

Schoberia dumortieri, commonly known as the red-flowered bellflower, is a striking plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils. The plant features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to pink, often with a contrasting throat. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Schoberia dumortieri is valued in gardens for its vibrant blooms and adaptability to sunny, well-drained conditions.
