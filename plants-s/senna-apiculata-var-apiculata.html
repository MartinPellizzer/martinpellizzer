Senna apiculata var. apiculata

Senna apiculata var. apiculata, commonly known as the golden senna, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and the Americas.

It features compound leaves with alternating pairs of ovate to lanceolate leaflets, and it produces bright yellow flowers in clusters during the warmer months. The plant is characterized by its bipinnate leaves and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as savannas and disturbed soils.

It is also known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
