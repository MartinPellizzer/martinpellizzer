Stelis transversalis

Stelis transversalis, commonly known as the transverse stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is an epiphytic orchid, typically growing on trees or rocks in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features a short, stocky rhizome with clustered, linear leaves. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and usually white or pale pink, arranged in a loose inflorescence.

Stelis transversalis thrives in humid environments with high light exposure and well-draining substrates.
