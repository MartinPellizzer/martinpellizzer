Senecio heyneanus

Senecio heyneanus, commonly known as the yellow oxeye daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow ray florets and dark brown central disk, giving it a daisy-like appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. Its leaves are linear and often covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

Senecio heyneanus is known for its ability to thrive in arid conditions, making it a hardy and resilient species.
