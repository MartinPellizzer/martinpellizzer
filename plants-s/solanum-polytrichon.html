Solanum polytrichon

Solanum polytrichon, commonly known as the hairy nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, hairy leaves and clusters of violet or purple flowers. The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that are typically green when unripe and turn red when mature. Solanum polytrichon is native to regions of southern Europe and parts of Asia.

It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
