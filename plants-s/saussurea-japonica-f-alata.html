Saussurea japonica f. alata

Saussurea japonica f. alata, commonly known as the Japanese clouded gentian, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests. Its foliage consists of broad, lance-shaped leaves, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.

It is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
