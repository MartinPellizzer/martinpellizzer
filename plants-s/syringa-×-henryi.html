Syringa × henryi

Syringa × henryi, commonly known as Chinese lilac, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, clustered flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, elliptical leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Syringa × henryi is noted for its hardiness and adaptability to various climates.
