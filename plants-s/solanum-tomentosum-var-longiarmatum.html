Solanum tomentosum var. longiarmatum

Solanum tomentosum var. longiarmatum, commonly known as the woolly nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features hairy, glandular leaves that are typically ovate to elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into fleshy, orange to red berries. Its stems are often covered in a dense layer of white to grayish pubescence, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soil and is often found in disturbed habitats or along roadsides.
