Schradera korthalsiana subsp. robusta

Schradera korthalsiana subsp.

robusta, commonly known as the robust schradera, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa. It is a shrub characterized by its dense, woody structure and dark green, elliptical leaves.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in clusters. Its botanical features make it suitable for use in ornamental landscaping and traditional medicine.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires a humid climate for optimal growth.
