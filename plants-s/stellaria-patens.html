Stellaria patens

Stellaria patens, commonly known as common mouse-ear, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It features small, white or pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer, and its leaves are typically rounded and hairy. The plant has a compact growth habit with a rosette of leaves at the base, and it produces numerous stolons that root at the nodes. Stellaria patens is often found in disturbed soils, such as roadsides and fields, and is adapted to temperate climates.

It is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
