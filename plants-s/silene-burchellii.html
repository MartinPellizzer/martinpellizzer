Silene burchellii

Silene burchellii, commonly known as the Burchell's campion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces showy pink or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Silene burchellii is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and coastal areas.
