Sonerila robusta var. glabricaulis

Sonerila robusta var. glabricaulis, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed sonerila, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and glossy, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
