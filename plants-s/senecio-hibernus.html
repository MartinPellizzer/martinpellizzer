Senecio hibernus

Senecio hibernus, commonly known as the winter heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. It has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and thrives in well-drained, sandy soils.

Senecio hibernus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
