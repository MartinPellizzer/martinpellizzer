Stipa munroana

Stipa munroana, commonly known as Munro's feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-green foliage and slender, tufted inflorescences that resemble plumes. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it resilient to drought conditions.

Its ornamental value has led to its cultivation in gardens and landscapes for its delicate appearance.
