Senecio loayzanus

Senecio loayzanus, commonly known as the Loayzan's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the island of Socotra.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant typically grows in arid and rocky habitats, showcasing adaptations to its harsh environment. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in a fine layer of white hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Senecio loayzanus is also noted for its bright yellow flowers, which bloom in clusters and attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
