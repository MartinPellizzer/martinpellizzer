Spiraea assurgens

Spiraea assurgens, commonly known as the white spiraea, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It features arching stems that bear clusters of small, white flowers in early spring. The plant has pinnately compound leaves that are oval to lance-shaped with serrated edges. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Spiraea assurgens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and landscapes.
