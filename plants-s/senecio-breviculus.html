Senecio breviculus

Senecio breviculus, commonly known as the dwarf African daisy, is a small, herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, cushion-like growth with tightly packed, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and white, typically blooming in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its drought tolerance, it is a low-maintenance plant ideal for arid environments.
