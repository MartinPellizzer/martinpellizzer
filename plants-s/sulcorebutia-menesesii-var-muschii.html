Sulcorebutia menesesii var. muschii

Sulcorebutia menesesii var. muschii, commonly known as the Muschii Cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It features a short, thick stem with a ribbed structure that helps it store water efficiently. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers during the warmer months. It has a shallow root system adapted to rocky soils and requires minimal water.

Due to its slow growth and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
