Senecio crinitus

Senecio crinitus, commonly known as the curly dock, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its long, curly leaves that resemble the fronds of a dock plant. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, and it is often found growing in disturbed soils or along roadsides. Senecio crinitus is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

It is adapted to arid environments and can thrive in poor soil conditions.
