Schefflera salweenensis

Schefflera salweenensis, commonly known as the Salween Schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of leaves, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant features compound leaves with multiple leaflets arranged in a radiating pattern. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by berries that are typically red or orange in color.

Schefflera salweenensis is often cultivated as a ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
