Stelis allenii

Stelis allenii, commonly known as the Allen's stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a few leaves and produces small, clustered flowers. Its blooms are usually white or pale yellow, with a subtle fragrance.

Stelis allenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or terrariums.
