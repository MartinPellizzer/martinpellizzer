Statice bayonnensis

Statice bayonnensis, commonly known as the Bayonne statice, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically grows in coastal and sandy environments, showcasing its adaptability to saline and well-drained soils. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, while its inflorescence consists of tightly packed bracts that resemble small flowers.

Statice bayonnensis is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
