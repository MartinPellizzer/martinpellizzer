Stelis cylindrica

Stelis cylindrica, commonly known as the cylindrical stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic. The plant is characterized by its slender, cylindrical pseudobulbs and narrow, linear leaves. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, often in a raceme, with a distinctive coloration.

Stelis cylindrica is native to Southeast Asia and is cultivated for its ornamental value in tropical gardens.
