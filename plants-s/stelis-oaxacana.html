Stelis oaxacana

Stelis oaxacana, commonly known as the Oaxaca orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
