Specklinia miqueliana

Specklinia miqueliana, commonly known as the Miquel's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its compact growth habit and attractive, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, often with a greenish tinge. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media to prevent root rot.

Specklinia miqueliana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation under proper conditions.
