Selinum ranunculoides

Selinum ranunculoides, commonly known as the meadow garlic or false garlic, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apiaceae family.

Selinum ranunculoides is known for its strong, garlic-like odor, which is released when the leaves are crushed.
