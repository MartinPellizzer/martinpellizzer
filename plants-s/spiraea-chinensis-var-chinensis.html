Spiraea chinensis var. chinensis

Spiraea chinensis var. chinensis, commonly known as Chinese spiraea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and features dense, rounded clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers. The plant has arching stems with opposite, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and slightly hairy on the underside. It blooms in early summer, producing fragrant flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The plant is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and showy blooms.
