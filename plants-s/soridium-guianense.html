Soridium guianense

Soridium guianense, commonly known as the Guianan sorrel, is a herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Malvaceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
