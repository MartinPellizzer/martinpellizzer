Sorghum nigericum

Sorghum nigericum, commonly known as black sorghum, is a tall, perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black stem and leaves, which give it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, fluffy panicles of flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in warm climates and is often used for ornamental purposes in gardens.

Additionally, its seeds can be processed for food or fiber, making it a versatile plant with both aesthetic and practical value.
