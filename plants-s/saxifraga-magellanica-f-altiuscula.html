Saxifraga magellanica f. altiuscula

Saxifraga magellanica f. altiuscula, commonly known as the Magellan saxifrage, is a flowering plant native to the southern regions of South America.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its rosette of fleshy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, white or pinkish flowers. The plant thrives in rocky and coastal environments, often growing in well-drained soils. Its botanical features include a compact growth habit and adaptations to withstand harsh, windy conditions.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
