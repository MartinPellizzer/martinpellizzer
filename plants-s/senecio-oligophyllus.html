Senecio oligophyllus

Senecio oligophyllus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It features narrow, linear leaves and produces yellow daisy-like flowers.

Senecio oligophyllus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and resilience.
