Serapias grandiflora var. ensifolia

Serapias grandiflora var. ensifolia, commonly known as the swordleaf orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically sword-shaped, giving it its common name. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a distinctive central stripe. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
