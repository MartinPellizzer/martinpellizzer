Schubea heterophylla

Schubea heterophylla, commonly known as the variable leaf schubea, is a unique plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, often colorful leaves that vary in shape and size depending on environmental conditions. The plant is a succulent, meaning it stores water in its leaves and stems to survive in arid environments. It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Due to its adaptability and aesthetic value, it is popular among succulent enthusiasts and gardeners in suitable climates.
