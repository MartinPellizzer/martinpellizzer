Sinosenecio bodinieri

Sinosenecio bodinieri, commonly known as the Chinese ragged robin, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly serrated edge. Its flowers are typically pale blue to violet, arranged in clusters at the tips of the stems.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.
