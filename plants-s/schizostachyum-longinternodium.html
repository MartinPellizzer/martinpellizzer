Schizostachyum longinternodium

Schizostachyum longinternodium, commonly known as the long internode bamboo, is a member of the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and long internodes, which are the spaces between the nodes on the stem. This bamboo species is native to Southeast Asia and is often found in wetland or marshy environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, contributing to its overall appearance.

Schizostachyum longinternodium is valued for its strong, durable culms, which are used in construction and crafts.
