Stenochilus racemosus

Stenochilus racemosus, commonly known as the racemose stenochilus, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Orchidaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant features a terrestrial growth habit with long, slender pseudobulbs and linear leaves. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and typically arranged in a racemose inflorescence, giving it its common name.

Stenochilus racemosus is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
