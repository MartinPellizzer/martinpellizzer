Stenandrium lyonii

Stenandrium lyonii, commonly known as the Lyon's stenandrium, is a member of the Araliaceae family.

It is a small, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. The plant features simple, opposite, and ovate leaves with a smooth, glossy surface. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and clustered in umbels, which are characteristic of the Araliaceae family.

Stenandrium lyonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden environments.
