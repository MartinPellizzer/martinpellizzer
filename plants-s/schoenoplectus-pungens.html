Schoenoplectus pungens

Schoenoplectus pungens, commonly known as spiny bulrush, is a perennial wetland plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish or brownish in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with sharp, spiny margins.

Schoenoplectus pungens plays an important role in stabilizing wetland soils and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
