Solanum metrobotryon

Solanum metrobotryon, commonly known as the Botryon nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants such as tomatoes and potatoes. The plant is characterized by its small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or purple in color. Its fruit is a small, fleshy berry that turns red when mature.

Like many species in the Solanaceae family, it contains toxic alkaloids and should be handled with care.
