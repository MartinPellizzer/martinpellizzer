Stelis bicallosa

Stelis bicallosa, commonly known as the hairy stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a short, wiry stem with numerous small, clustered leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with a distinctive, fragrant scent.

Stelis bicallosa is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
