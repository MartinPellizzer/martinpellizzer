Sarcochilus unguiculatus

Sarcochilus unguiculatus, commonly known as the horned orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to New Guinea.

It features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with a single, upright leaf and a dense cluster of dark green, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a red or orange lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Sarcochilus unguiculatus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
