Salvia lavandulifolia subsp. amethystea

Salvia lavandulifolia subsp.

amethystea, commonly known as purple sage, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its aromatic, gray-green leaves and showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Its compact growth habit and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The essential oils found in its leaves are valued for their fragrance and potential medicinal properties.
