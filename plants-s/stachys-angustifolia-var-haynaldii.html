Stachys angustifolia var. haynaldii

Stachys angustifolia var. haynaldii, commonly known as narrowleaf betony, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces spikes of violet to purple flowers, which bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate appearance and ability to attract pollinators.
