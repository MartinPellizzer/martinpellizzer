Scutellaria topcuoglui

Scutellaria topcuoglui, commonly known as the Topcuoglu blueweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is native to Turkey and is characterized by its small, tubular blue or purple flowers. The plant has square stems and opposite leaves, typical of many members of its family. It thrives in rocky or dry habitats and is often found in mountainous regions.

Scutellaria topcuoglui is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and limited distribution.
