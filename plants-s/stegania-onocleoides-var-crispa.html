Stegania onocleoides var. crispa

Stegania onocleoides var. crispa, commonly known as the crinkled stegania, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Its unique leaf morphology, with a crinkled or wavy texture, distinguishes it from other species in the genus.
