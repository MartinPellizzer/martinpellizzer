Salacia nitida var. nitida

Salacia nitida var. nitida, commonly known as the slender salacia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Salicaceae, which includes willows and poplars. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, greenish-yellow flowers that appear in clusters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
