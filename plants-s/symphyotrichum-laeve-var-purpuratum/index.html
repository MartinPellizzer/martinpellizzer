Symphyotrichum Laeve Var. Purpuratum

Symphyotrichum laeve var. purpuratum is a unique variant of the blue mistflower known for its striking purple blooms. This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in prairies and open woodlands. Its flowers appear in late summer, adding vibrant color to the landscape. The variety is notable for its dense, fluffy petals that catch the sunlight beautifully. It is a hardy perennial that attracts pollinators and enhances biodiversity in native gardens.
