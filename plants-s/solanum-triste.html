Solanum triste

Solanum triste, commonly known as the sad tomato, is a species native to South America.

It is a flowering plant in the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its small, yellow or orange fruits. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded or moist environments. Its leaves are usually oval-shaped with a hairy texture, and it produces small, white flowers.

Solanum triste is not widely cultivated for food but is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
