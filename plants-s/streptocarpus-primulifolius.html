Streptocarpus primulifolius

Streptocarpus primulifolius, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette of velvety, waxy leaves and clusters of tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is known for its compact growth habit. It produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, dark seeds.

Streptocarpus primulifolius is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
