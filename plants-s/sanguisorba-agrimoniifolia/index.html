Sanguisorba Agrimoniifolia

Sanguisorba agrimoniifolia is a striking perennial plant known for its unique appearance and ecological value. Native to eastern Asia, it thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas. The plant features dark red stems and clusters of small, pinkish flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a distinctive, slightly serrated edge. This hardy species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to support local wildlife.
