Sideritis ocymastrum

Sideritis ocymastrum, commonly known as narrow-leaved ironwort, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically pale blue or violet, arranged in whorls along the stem.

It thrives in rocky or dry soils and is often found in open habitats such as hillsides and grasslands.
