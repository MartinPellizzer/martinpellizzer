Senecio arenarius

Senecio arenarius, commonly known as the sand pincushion, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and yellow flowers. The plant thrives in arid and rocky environments, showcasing its adaptation to harsh conditions. Its leaves are fleshy and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Senecio arenarius is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience.
