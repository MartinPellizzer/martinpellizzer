Statice nana

Statice nana, commonly known as dwarf statice, is a flowering plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is native to southern Europe and is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, showy flowers. The plant typically produces spikes of pink, red, or white flowers that bloom in summer. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Statice nana is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
