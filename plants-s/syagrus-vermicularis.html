Syagrus vermicularis

Syagrus vermicularis, commonly known as the golden fan palm, is a tropical plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, fan-shaped leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into red, fleshy fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Syagrus vermicularis is also valued for its ability to provide shade and its ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
