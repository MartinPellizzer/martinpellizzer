Sphaerostephanos tephrophyllus

Sphaerostephanos tephrophyllus, commonly known as the golden cup fern, is a rare and delicate fern species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

The plant has feathery fronds with a bright yellow hue, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil for optimal growth.
