Spilanthes ocymifolia f. radiifera

Spilanthes ocymifolia f. radiifera, commonly known as the radiate spilanthes, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive yellowish-orange flowers arranged in a radiate head, with both ray and disk florets. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of South America, particularly in Argentina and Chile. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure, often growing in dry, rocky habitats.

The species is noted for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic benefits.
