Stromanthe boliviana

Stromanthe boliviana, commonly known as the Bolivian flame plant, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, variegated foliage with shades of green, red, and white. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and can grow up to 30 cm in length, often displaying intricate patterns. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
