Solanum × indunii

Solanum × indunii, commonly known as the Indian nightshade, is a hybrid plant belonging to the nightshade family (Solanaceae).

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that are typically yellow or orange when ripe. It is native to the Indian subcontinent and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and traditional herbal practices.
