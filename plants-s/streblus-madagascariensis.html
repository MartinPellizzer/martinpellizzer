Streblus madagascariensis

Streblus madagascariensis, commonly known as the Madagascar figwood, is a deciduous tree native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Moraceae family, which includes figs and mulberries. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It produces small, clustered flowers that are followed by fruiting structures resembling figs.
