Solanum tuberosum f. jancko-pala

Solanum tuberosum f. jancko-pala, commonly known as the Jancko-Pala potato, is a botanical variant of the common potato.

It is characterized by its unique tuber morphology, which includes a distinctive coloration and texture. The plant belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and exhibits typical solanum growth habits. Its tubers are often smaller than standard potatoes and have a higher starch content.

This cultivar is of interest in botanical studies due to its genetic diversity and potential agricultural applications.
