Senecio deaniensis

Senecio deaniensis, commonly known as the Deani's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It features narrow, linear leaves and produces small yellow flowers arranged in a composite head.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
