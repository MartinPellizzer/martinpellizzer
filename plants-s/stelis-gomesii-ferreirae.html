Stelis gomesii-ferreirae

Stelis gomesii-ferreirae, commonly known as the Gomesii-Ferreirae orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with a distinct lip, and it produces tiny, capsule-like fruits.

This species is native to Brazil and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance.
