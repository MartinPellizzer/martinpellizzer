Stipa arechavaletae

Stipa arechavaletae, commonly known as the Arechavaleta's feathergrass, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is a perennial species characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and fine, needle-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. Native to the high Andean regions of South America, it thrives in cool, temperate climates with well-drained soils.

Its botanical features make it an important component of alpine ecosystems and a subject of interest in plant taxonomy.
