Statice hirta subsp. baetica

Statice hirta subsp.

baetica, commonly known as Spanish pink, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula. It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its dense, showy clusters of pink or red flowers.
