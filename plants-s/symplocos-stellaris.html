Symplocos stellaris

Symplocos stellaris, commonly known as starflower or starwood, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a smooth texture with a dark green upper surface. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically red or black when mature.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland habitats.
