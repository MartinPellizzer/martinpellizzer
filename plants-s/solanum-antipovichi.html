Solanum antipovichi

Solanum antipovichi, commonly known as the Antipovichi nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically purple or white in color. Its fruit is a fleshy berry that contains several seeds.

Due to its toxic compounds, it is not suitable for human consumption.
