Stelis dinoi

Stelis dinoi, commonly known as the Dinoi orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often found in forest undergrowth. The plant features a slender, upright stem with several fleshy, elliptical leaves. Its flowers are small, white to pale pink, and are arranged in a raceme, adding a delicate aesthetic to its appearance.

Stelis dinoi is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
