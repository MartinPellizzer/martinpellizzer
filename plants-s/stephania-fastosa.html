Stephania fastosa

Stephania fastosa, commonly known as the fast-growing stephania, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Menispermaceae and is characterized by its climbing habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by bright red, fleshy berries. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
