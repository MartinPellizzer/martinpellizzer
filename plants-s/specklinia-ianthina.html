Specklinia ianthina

Specklinia ianthina, commonly known as the silver-veined orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a silvery or grayish bloom, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale blue in color, arranged in a dense spike. Specklinia ianthina thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
