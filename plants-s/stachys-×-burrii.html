Stachys × burrii

Stachys × burrii, commonly known as the woolly betony, is a hybrid plant belonging to the mint family, Lamiaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive, fluffy appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in woodland areas or along stream banks. Its leaves are usually gray-green, and it produces small, violet or blue flowers in clusters.

Stachys × burrii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique texture and color.
