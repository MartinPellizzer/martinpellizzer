Statice bellidifolia var. divaricata

Statice bellidifolia var. divaricata, commonly known as the fringed statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and spikes of small, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers come in shades of pink, purple, and white, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

It is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.
