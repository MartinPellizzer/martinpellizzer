Sloanea sinensis

Sloanea sinensis, commonly known as the velvetleaf sloanea, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are typically clustered in terminal inflorescences. Sloanea sinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in warm climates for its attractive foliage.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for tropical and subtropical gardens.
