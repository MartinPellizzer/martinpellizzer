Scutellaria mocinoana

Scutellaria mocinoana, commonly known as the Mocino's skullcap, is a perennial herb native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features square stems and opposite leaves, typical of the mint family. Its small, tubular flowers are usually blue or purple, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Scutellaria mocinoana is often found in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid environments.
