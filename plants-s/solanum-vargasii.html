Solanum vargasii

Solanum vargasii, commonly known as the Vargas nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and tobacco. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a rough texture, while its flowers are usually white or purple.

The fruit is a small, spherical berry that turns from green to yellow or red when ripe.
