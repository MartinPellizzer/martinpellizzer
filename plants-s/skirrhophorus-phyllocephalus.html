Skirrhophorus phyllocephalus

Skirrhophorus phyllocephalus, commonly known as the leaf-headed skirrhophorus, is a unique plant species characterized by its distinctive leaf-like structures that resemble modified stems.

The plant's botanical features include a basal rosette of fleshy, broad leaves that serve both as photosynthetic organs and water storage structures.
