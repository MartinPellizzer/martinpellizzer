Sisymbrianthus sylvestris

Sisymbrianthus sylvestris, commonly known as the forest mustard, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in woodland and scrub habitats. The plant features small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes, and its leaves are alternate, pinnately lobed. It produces small, rounded pods that contain several seeds.

Sisymbrianthus sylvestris is often used in ecological restoration due to its ability to thrive in disturbed soils.
