Spiraea japonica var. ibukiensis

Spiraea japonica var. ibukiensis, commonly known as the Ibukiensis Weeping Spirea, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to Japan.

It is characterized by its dense, cascading clusters of white flowers that bloom in early summer. The foliage is typically dark green, providing a striking contrast to the delicate floral display. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden borders or containers.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to add a soft, flowing texture to landscapes.
