Sinocrassula stenostachya

Sinocrassula stenostachya, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to China.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit. The plant features fleshy, opposite leaves that are typically gray-green in color. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months.

Sinocrassula stenostachya is well adapted to rocky and arid environments, making it a hardy and drought-tolerant species.
