Smilax kraussiana var. dregei

Smilax kraussiana var. dregei, commonly known as the dwarf smilax, is a species of flowering plant in the family Smilacaceae.

It is native to parts of Africa, particularly South Africa, and is characterized by its climbing or trailing growth habit. The plant features small, heart-shaped leaves and produces clusters of yellowish-green flowers that are typically followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in woodland or forest understories.
