Stadtmannia bahiensis

Stadtmannia bahiensis, commonly known as the Brazilian star orchid, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in the understory of tropical rainforests. Its leaves are elongated and slightly fleshy, adapted to store water in its native habitat.

Stadtmannia bahiensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
