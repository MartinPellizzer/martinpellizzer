Solidago yokusaiana

Solidago yokusaiana, commonly known as the Yokosuka goldenrod, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and often branched.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
