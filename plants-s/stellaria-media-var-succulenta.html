Stellaria media var. succulenta

Stellaria media var. succulenta, commonly known as succulent mouse-ear chickweed, is a variant of the common chickweed species.

It is characterized by its fleshy, succulent leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small white flowers with five petals, which are often arranged in clusters. It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in moist, shady environments.

This variety is often found in gardens and along woodland paths, where it spreads through stolons and seeds.
