Sisymbrium orientale f. tenuisiliquum

Sisymbrium orientale f. tenuisiliquum, commonly known as the narrow-seeded babylonian cabbage, is a flowering plant in the family Brassicaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes. The leaves are alternate, pinnately lobed, and often have a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces elongated, narrow pods that contain several small, rounded seeds.
