Senecio trianthemos

Senecio trianthemos, commonly known as the Cape snowflake, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that resemble snowflakes, hence its common name. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Senecio trianthemos is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its delicate appearance and ability to attract pollinators.
