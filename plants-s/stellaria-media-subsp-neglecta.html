Stellaria media subsp. neglecta

Stellaria media subsp.

neglecta, commonly known as common chickweed, is a low-growing annual plant characterized by its prostrate or ascending growth habit. It features small, white, star-shaped flowers that typically bloom in early spring.

The leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with a hairy surface, and the plant produces small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in lawns, gardens, and along pathways.

This subspecies is closely related to other members of the Stellaria genus and is known for its rapid spread and adaptability.
