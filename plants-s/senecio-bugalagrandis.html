Senecio bugalagrandis

Senecio bugalagrandis, commonly known as the giant African daisy, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, daisy-like yellow flowers and its ability to thrive in arid conditions. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and thistles. Its leaves are typically gray-green and covered in a fine layer of white hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Senecio bugalagrandis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and hardy nature.
