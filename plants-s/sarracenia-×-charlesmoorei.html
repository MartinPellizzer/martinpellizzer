Sarracenia × charlesmoorei

Sarracenia × charlesmoorei, commonly known as the Charles Moore hybrid, is a fascinating carnivorous plant resulting from the cross between Sarracenia leucophylla and Sarracenia rubra.

It features tall, tubular leaves that form intricate traps to capture insects, with vibrant red and green hues that enhance its visual appeal. The plant thrives in wet, acidic soils and requires ample sunlight to maintain its health and growth. Its flowers are typically pink or purple, adding to its ornamental value.

This hybrid is prized by growers for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
