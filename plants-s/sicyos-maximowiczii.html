Sicyos maximowiczii

Sicyos maximowiczii, commonly known as the giant bottle gourd, is a climbing vine native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its large, swollen, bottle-shaped fruits that can grow up to 3 meters in length. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and produces yellow, fragrant flowers. Its stems are hairy and tend to climb using tendrils.

Sicyos maximowiczii is cultivated for its edible fruits, which are used in various culinary traditions.
