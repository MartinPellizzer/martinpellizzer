Scilla tingitana

Scilla tingitana, commonly known as the Gibraltar squill, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the Iberian Peninsula and North Africa.

It produces clusters of blue or violet flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found growing in rocky or coastal areas. It is adapted to dry, sunny environments and is drought-tolerant.

Scilla tingitana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
