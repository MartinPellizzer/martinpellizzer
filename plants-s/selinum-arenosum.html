Selinum arenosum

Selinum arenosum, commonly known as desert sandwort, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit and produces small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are usually narrow and linear. It is adapted to survive in sandy soils with low moisture.

Selinum arenosum is often found in deserts and coastal dunes, showcasing its resilience to harsh environmental conditions.
