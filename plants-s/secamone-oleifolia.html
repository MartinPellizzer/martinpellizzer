Secamone oleifolia

Secamone oleifolia, commonly known as the African snowball, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded clusters of white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has small, leathery leaves that are dark green in color and glossy in appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Secamone oleifolia is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
