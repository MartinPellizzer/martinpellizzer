Sideritis ozturkii

Sideritis ozturkii, commonly known as Turkish ironweed, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its upright stems and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Sideritis ozturkii is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is valued for its medicinal properties.
