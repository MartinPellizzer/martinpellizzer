Stellaria uliginosa var. alpicola

Stellaria uliginosa var. alpicola, commonly known as the alpine starwort, is a flowering plant native to alpine regions of Europe and Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with slender stems and small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is adapted to grow in rocky or sandy soils at high altitudes. It is often found in open habitats such as meadows and mountain slopes.

Its botanical features make it well-suited for harsh environmental conditions.
