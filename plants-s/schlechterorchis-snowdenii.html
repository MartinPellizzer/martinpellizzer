Schlechterorchis snowdenii

Schlechterorchis snowdenii, commonly known as the Snowden's twayblade, is a rare and delicate orchid species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a small, herbaceous plant with a short stem and two opposite leaves, typical of the Orchidaceae family. The flowers are inconspicuous, usually greenish or brownish, with a short spur at the base of the ovary. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes.
