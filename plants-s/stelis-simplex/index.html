Stelis Simplex

Stelis simplex is a small, delicate orchid native to the tropical regions of South America. It is known for its simple, elegant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale pink. This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires careful attention to humidity and light levels. Despite its modest appearance, it is highly valued by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and rarity. Its unique characteristics make it a fascinating subject for both botanists and plant lovers alike.
