Schizoglossum virgatum

Schizoglossum virgatum, commonly known as the Virgin's tongue, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces blue or violet petals that are typically arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. Schizoglossum virgatum is known for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional herbal remedies.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
