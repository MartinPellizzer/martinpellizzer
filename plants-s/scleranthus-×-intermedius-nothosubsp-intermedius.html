Scleranthus × intermedius nothosubsp. intermedius

Scleranthus × intermedius nothosubsp.

intermedius, commonly known as the intermediate sowthistle, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between S. arvenis and S. annuus. It is an annual herbaceous plant characterized by its prostrate to ascending growth habit and hairy stems.

The leaves are typically lanceolate or ovate, with serrated margins, and the flowers are small, yellow, and clustered in dense heads. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural fields and along roadsides.

This plant is noted for its adaptability and rapid growth, making it a common weed in various regions.
