Schefflera disparifolia

Schefflera disparifolia, commonly known as the Variegated Schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that often have striking white or yellow variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Schefflera disparifolia is known for its adaptability and ability to grow in a variety of indoor environments.
