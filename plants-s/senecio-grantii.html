Senecio grantii

Senecio grantii, commonly known as the Grant's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent species characterized by its fleshy, gray-green leaves and clusters of small yellow flowers. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky or sandy soils. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and ragworts.

Senecio grantii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and adaptability to dry conditions.
