Stelis mocoana

Stelis mocoana, commonly known as the Mocoan orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to the Caribbean.

It typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments, with its roots anchored in the bark of its host. The plant features a short, unbranched stem and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in white or pale pink. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Stelis mocoana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
