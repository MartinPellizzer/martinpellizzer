Spiraea thunbergii

Spiraea thunbergii, commonly known as Japanese spiraea, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It features slender, arching stems that bear clusters of small, white or pink flowers in early summer. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Spiraea thunbergii is relatively low maintenance and thrives in full sun to partial shade.
