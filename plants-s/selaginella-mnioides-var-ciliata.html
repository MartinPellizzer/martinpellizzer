Selaginella mnioides var. ciliata

Selaginella mnioides var. ciliata, commonly known as the ciliated spikemoss, is a vascular plant belonging to the family Selaginellaceae.

It is a low-growing, herbaceous plant that typically forms dense mats on rocky or sandy soils. The plant features small, scale-like leaves that are often covered with fine, ciliated hairs, giving it its varietal name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in temperate regions.

This species is notable for its ability to survive in harsh conditions, making it an important component of certain ecosystems.
