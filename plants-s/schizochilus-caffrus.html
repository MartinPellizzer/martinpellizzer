Schizochilus caffrus

Schizochilus caffrus, commonly known as the African rock orchid, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a slender, erect stem with few leaves and produces delicate, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive lip. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is well adapted to arid conditions.

Schizochilus caffrus is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance.
