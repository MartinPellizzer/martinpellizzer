Sprengelia aristata

Sprengelia aristata, commonly known as the coral tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking coral-pink to red flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a vibrant display to its foliage. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often glaucous, giving them a bluish-white hue. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Sprengelia aristata is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
