Specklinia cryptantha

Specklinia cryptantha, commonly known as the hidden specklinia, is a rare and delicate orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, dark green leaves and intricate, fragrant flowers that bloom in a striking shade of violet. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often nestled among moss and epiphytic on trees. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.

Due to habitat destruction, it is considered endangered and requires careful protection.
