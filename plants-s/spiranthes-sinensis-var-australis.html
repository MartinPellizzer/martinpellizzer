Spiranthes sinensis var. australis

Spiranthes sinensis var. australis, commonly known as the Australian lady's tresses, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive spiral arrangement of flowers along the stem. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its flowers are small, white to pale pink, and bloom in late summer to early autumn.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local pollinators.
