Sauromatum tentaculatum

Sauromatum tentaculatum, commonly known as the giant arrowroot, is a striking tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its large, showy, funnel-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant produces a starchy, edible tuberous root that is used in various cuisines. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, often reaching several feet in length.

Sauromatum tentaculatum is also known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in moist, humid environments.
