Senecio oflasii

Senecio oflasii, commonly known as the Cape mountain snowball, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its white, fluffy inflorescences that resemble snowballs. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. It is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.

Senecio oflasii is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
