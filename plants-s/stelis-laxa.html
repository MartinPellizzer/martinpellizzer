Stelis laxa

Stelis laxa, commonly known as the lax orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect or semi-erect growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with a delicate appearance.

Stelis laxa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
