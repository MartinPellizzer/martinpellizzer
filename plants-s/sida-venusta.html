Sida venusta

Sida venusta, commonly known as velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, velvety hairs, giving it a soft texture. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are typically clustered in racemes. It thrives in warm, moist environments and is often found in disturbed soils.

Sida venusta is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
