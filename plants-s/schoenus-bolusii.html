Schoenus bolusii

Schoenus bolusii, commonly known as the Bolus sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and grass-like plants. Schoenus bolusii is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
