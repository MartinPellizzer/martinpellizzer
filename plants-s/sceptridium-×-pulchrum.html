Sceptridium × pulchrum

Sceptridium × pulchrum, commonly known as the golden saxifrage, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Polygonaceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from March to May. The plant has upright, branching stems covered in soft, hairy foliage. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Sceptridium × pulchrum is often found in rocky or alpine environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.
