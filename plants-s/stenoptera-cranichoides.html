Stenoptera cranichoides

Stenoptera cranichoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved grasshopper orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It features a compact, hairy stem and narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive, spurred labellum. It thrives in shaded, moist environments such as rainforest understoreys.

Its unique morphology and habitat make it a specialized and ecologically significant species within its ecosystem.
