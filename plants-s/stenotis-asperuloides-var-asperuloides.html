Stenotis asperuloides var. asperuloides

Stenotis asperuloides var. asperuloides, commonly known as the roughleaf stenotis, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and lance-shaped leaves that are rough to the touch. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value lies in its foliage and modest floral display.
