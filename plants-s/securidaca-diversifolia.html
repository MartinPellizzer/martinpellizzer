Securidaca diversifolia

Securidaca diversifolia, commonly known as the African daisy or white daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
