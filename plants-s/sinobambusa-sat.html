Sinobambusa sat

Sinobambusa sat, commonly known as the silver bamboo, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems that often have a silvery or bluish hue due to a dense covering of fine hairs. Sinobambusa sat is native to eastern Asia and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and hardy nature.
