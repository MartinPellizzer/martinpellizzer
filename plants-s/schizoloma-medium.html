Schizoloma medium

Schizoloma medium, commonly known as the dwarf feather grass, is a low-growing perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It features fine, needle-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Schizoloma medium is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance in landscaping.
