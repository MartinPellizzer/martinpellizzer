Stelis siphonantha

Stelis siphonantha, commonly known as the Siphonantha orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are small and fragrant, often displaying a mix of white and green hues.

Stelis siphonantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
