Sida mollissima

Sida mollissima, commonly known as velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in spikes. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils.

Sida mollissima is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
