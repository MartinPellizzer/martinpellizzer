Swertia atroviolacea

Swertia atroviolacea, commonly known as the dark violet swertia, is a perennial herb native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking dark violet to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a reddish tint, while its stems are hairy.
