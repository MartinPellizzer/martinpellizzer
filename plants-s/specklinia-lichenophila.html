Specklinia lichenophila

Specklinia lichenophila, commonly known as the lichen-leaf fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Davalliaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that are often covered in a fine, lichen-like coating, giving it its common name. Native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, this fern thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant's unique morphology includes small, clustered rhizomes and intricate, pinnately divided leaves.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ornamental addition to botanical collections and conservatories.
