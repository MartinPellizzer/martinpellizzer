Selliguea violascens

Selliguea violascens, commonly known as the purple moss, is a species of liverwort belonging to the family Porellaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, cushion-like growth habit and vibrant purple to reddish coloration, which is most prominent in its gametophyte stage. Selliguea violascens plays a role in soil stabilization and provides microhabitats for various invertebrates.
