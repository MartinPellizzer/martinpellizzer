Stellularia media var. holosteoides

Stellularia media var. holosteoides, commonly known as the narrowleaf arrowhead, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetlands and marshy areas in North America.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically narrow and slightly notched, with a smooth texture. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers in clusters during the summer months. Its root system is fibrous and adapted to thrive in saturated soil conditions.

It plays an important role in wetland ecosystems by stabilizing soil and providing habitat for aquatic organisms.
