Sedum sajanense

Sedum sajanense, commonly known as the Sajanski stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and small, clustered leaves that are typically green or blue-green in color. The plant produces star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often used in xeriscaping and as an ornamental plant in arid landscapes.
