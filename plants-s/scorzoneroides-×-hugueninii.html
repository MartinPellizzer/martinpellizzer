Scorzoneroides × hugueninii

Scorzoneroides × hugueninii, commonly known as the hybrid evening primrose, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

The plant belongs to the Onagraceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure with four petals and stamens.
