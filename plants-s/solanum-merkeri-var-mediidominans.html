Solanum merkeri var. mediidominans

Solanum merkeri var. mediidominans, commonly known as the dwarf nightshade, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other notable plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 cm, with small, ovate leaves and clusters of violet or purple flowers. Its fruit is a small, berry-like structure that turns from green to yellow as it matures.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
