Streptocarpus caulescens var. pallescens

Streptocarpus caulescens var. pallescens, commonly known as the pale-stemmed streptocarpus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and attractive, tubular flowers. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in a fine, velvety texture. This variety is noted for its pale green stems and foliage, which contrast with its vibrant purple or blue flowers.

It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
