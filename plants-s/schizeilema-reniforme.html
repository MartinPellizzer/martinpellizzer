Schizeilema reniforme

Schizeilema reniforme, commonly known as the kidneyleaf, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive kidney-shaped leaves, which are typically hairy and have a lobed appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in early spring, adding a subtle ornamental value. Schizeilema reniforme thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

It is often used in native plant gardens for its ecological benefits and unique foliage.
