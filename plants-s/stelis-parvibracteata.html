Stelis parvibracteata

Stelis parvibracteata, commonly known as the narrow-bract stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with a few leaves and produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media. Its flowers are typically short-lived but attract various pollinators.

Stelis parvibracteata is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
