Sarcanthus porrigens

Sarcanthus porrigens, commonly known as the porridge thistle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features a robust stem with spiny leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in grasslands and shrublands.

Sarcanthus porrigens is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses.
