Senecio ghiesbreghtii

Senecio ghiesbreghtii, commonly known as the Ghiesbreght's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and gray-green, adapted to reduce water loss.

The flowers are yellow, with ray florets surrounding a central disk, and it blooms during the warmer months.
