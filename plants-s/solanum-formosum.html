Solanum formosum

Solanum formosum, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its trumpet-shaped, violet or blue flowers. The plant typically grows as an annual or biennial herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are ovate with a hairy surface, and it produces small, berry-like fruits that turn red when mature.

Solanum formosum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
