Stapelia atrata

Stapelia atrata, commonly known as the Black Thorn.

It is a succulent plant native to South Africa, characterized by its dark, spiny stems and fleshy, waxy leaves. The plant produces striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Stapelia atrata is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience.
