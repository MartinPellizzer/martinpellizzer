Salacia glabra

Salacia glabra, commonly known as the smooth salacia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Salicaceae, which includes willows and poplars. The plant is characterized by its smooth, slender stems and small, inconspicuous flowers. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a smooth texture.

Salacia glabra is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
