Saviniona suspensa

Saviniona suspensa, commonly known as the hanging savinea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Saviniona suspensa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
