Stemodia hassleriana

Stemodia hassleriana, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its flowers are small, daisy-like blooms that are usually white or pale yellow.

It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
