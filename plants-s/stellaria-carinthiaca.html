Stellaria carinthiaca

Stellaria carinthiaca, commonly known as Carinthian starwort, is a low-growing annual herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, white or pale blue flowers with five petals, arranged in a star-like pattern, giving it its common name. The plant has slender, branching stems and small, opposite leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.

Stellaria carinthiaca is often found in temperate regions and is known for its delicate appearance and ecological importance to pollinators.
