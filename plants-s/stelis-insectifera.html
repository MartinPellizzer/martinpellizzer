Stelis insectifera

Stelis insectifera, commonly known as the insect-like stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb and produces a single, showy flower with a distinctive, intricate lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually pale green to white, with a yellowish or reddish lip that resembles an insect.

Stelis insectifera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
