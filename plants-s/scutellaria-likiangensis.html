Scutellaria likiangensis

Scutellaria likiangensis, commonly known as the Yunnan skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants such as mint and sage. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Scutellaria likiangensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
