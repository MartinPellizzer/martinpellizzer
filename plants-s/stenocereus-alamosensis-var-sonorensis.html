Stenocereus alamosensis var. sonorensis

Stenocereus alamosensis var. sonorensis, commonly known as the Sonoran hedgehog cactus, is a species native to the Sonoran Desert.

It is a columnar cactus with a spiny, ribbed structure that helps it store water and reduce water loss. The plant produces white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its fruit is a small, edible, fleshy berry that attracts various wildlife.

This plant is adapted to arid environments and plays a role in the ecological balance of its habitat.
