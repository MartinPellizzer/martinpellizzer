Struthiola chrysantha var. dregei

Struthiola chrysantha var. dregei, commonly known as the Cape goldcup, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and has a bushy, compact growth habit. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often woody at the base.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
