Stachytarpheta crassifolia f. alba

Stachytarpheta crassifolia f. alba, commonly known as white stachytarpheta, is a variant of the Stachytarpheta crassifolia species.

It is a shrub native to the tropical regions of the Americas, particularly in countries like Brazil and Mexico. The plant is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and clusters of white to pale lavender flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.
