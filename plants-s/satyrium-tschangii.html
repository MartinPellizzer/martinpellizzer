Satyrium tschangii

Satyrium tschangii, commonly known as the Yunnan satyrium, is a rare orchid species native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with cool, moist climates, thriving in shaded forest environments. Its flowers exhibit a range of colors, including white, yellow, and green, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
