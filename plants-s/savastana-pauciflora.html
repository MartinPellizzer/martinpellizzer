Savastana pauciflora

Savastana pauciflora, commonly known as the sparse-flowered savastana, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, delicate flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant typically thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Due to its limited distribution and unique appearance, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
