Statice densiflora

Statice densiflora, commonly known as denseflower statice, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family, which includes many species known for their showy blooms and drought tolerance. The plant is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and numerous small, spike-like flowers that range in color from pink to purple. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Statice densiflora is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
