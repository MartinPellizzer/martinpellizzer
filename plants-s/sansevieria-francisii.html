Sansevieria francisii

Sansevieria francisii, commonly known as the Francisii Snake Plant, is a species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with distinct yellow or white variegation. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its leaves are stiff and fibrous, which is a common trait among the Sansevieria genus.

This plant is also noted for its air-purifying qualities, contributing to improved indoor air quality.
