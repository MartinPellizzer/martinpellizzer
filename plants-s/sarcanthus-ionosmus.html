Sarcanthus ionosmus

Sarcanthus ionosmus, commonly known as the silverleaf shrub, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features silvery-gray, fuzzy leaves that help reflect sunlight and reduce water loss. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

The silverleaf shrub is often used in ornamental gardening for its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements.
