Solenostemon thyrsiflorus

Solenostemon thyrsiflorus, commonly known as the flame bush, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically red or orange in color. These flowers are arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences, giving the plant its species name "thyrsiflorus.".

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.
