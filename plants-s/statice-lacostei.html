Statice lacostei

Statice lacostei, commonly known as the coastal statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant thrives in saline and well-drained soils, often found in coastal dunes and rocky areas. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, and it is known for its ability to tolerate drought and salty conditions.

Statice lacostei is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
