Sulcorebutia vasqueziana

Sulcorebutia vasqueziana, commonly known as the Vasqueziana Crassula, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to southern Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish tinge, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a favored plant among succulent enthusiasts.
