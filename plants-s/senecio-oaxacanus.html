Senecio oaxacanus

Senecio oaxacanus, commonly known as the Oaxaca groundsel, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are usually gray-green with a fuzzy texture, and it produces small yellow flowers.
