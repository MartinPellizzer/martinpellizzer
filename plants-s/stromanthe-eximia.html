Stromanthe eximia

Stromanthe eximia, commonly known as the velvetleaf stromanthe, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with shades of green, silver, and pink. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a velvety texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to maintain its vibrant appearance.

This plant is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its adaptability and low maintenance requirements.
