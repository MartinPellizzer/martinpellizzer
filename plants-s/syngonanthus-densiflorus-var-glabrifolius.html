Syngonanthus densiflorus var. glabrifolius

Syngonanthus densiflorus var. glabrifolius, commonly known as the smooth-leaved flamebush, is a shrub native to parts of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, showy inflorescences that display vibrant red or orange flowers, which appear in late summer. The leaves are typically narrow and smooth, giving the plant its varietal name. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
