Stellaria zolotuchinii

Stellaria zolotuchinii, commonly known as the Caucasian starwort, is a small annual herb native to the Caucasus region.

It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks. The plant features slender stems that branch near the base, supporting a profusion of small, star-shaped white flowers. Its leaves are narrow and lanceolate, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Stellaria zolotuchinii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
