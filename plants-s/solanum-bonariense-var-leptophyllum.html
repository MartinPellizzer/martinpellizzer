Solanum bonariense var. leptophyllum

Solanum bonariense var. leptophyllum, commonly known as the slender leaf nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, star-shaped white flowers. The plant produces dark purple to black berries that are edible but contain toxic compounds. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is cultivated in some regions for horticultural purposes.
