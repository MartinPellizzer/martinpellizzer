Sideritis ilorcitana

Sideritis ilorcitana, commonly known as the Ilorcitana ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its slender stems and small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or pink in color. It thrives in rocky or calcareous soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats.

Sideritis ilorcitana is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
