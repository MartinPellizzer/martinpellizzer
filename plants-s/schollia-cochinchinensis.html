Schollia cochinchinensis

Schollia cochinchinensis, commonly known as the Vietnamese bellflower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Schollia cochinchinensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
