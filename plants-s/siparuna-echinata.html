Siparuna echinata

Siparuna echinata, commonly known as the spiny beautyberry, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant produces small, dark blue berries that are edible for birds and some mammals. Siparuna echinata thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation in warm climates.
