Senecio cymbulifer

Senecio cymbulifer, commonly known as the woolly senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a unique, soft appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in some regions.
