Sanguisorba minor

Sanguisorba minor, commonly known as lesser dropwort, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features upright, branched stems that bear clusters of small, reddish-brown flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and have a slightly succulent texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

Sanguisorba minor is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in herbal medicine.
