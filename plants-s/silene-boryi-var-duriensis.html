Silene boryi var. duriensis

Silene boryi var. duriensis, commonly known as the Duriense campion, is a flowering plant species native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It is a low-growing herb with pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has a rosette of narrow, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in harsh environments. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in tundra or alpine habitats.

Silene boryi var. duriensis plays a role in pollination and is an indicator species for cold climates.
