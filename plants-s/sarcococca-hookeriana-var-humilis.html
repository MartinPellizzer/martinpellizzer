Sarcococca hookeriana var. humilis

Sarcococca hookeriana var. humilis, commonly known as littleleaf waxleaf box, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the eastern Himalayas.

It features glossy, dark green, oval-shaped leaves that remain evergreen throughout the year. The plant produces small, fragrant, white flowers in late winter or early spring, followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its compact size and year-round foliage make it an attractive addition to shaded landscapes.
