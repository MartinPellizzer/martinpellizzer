Shaniodendron subaequale

Shaniodendron subaequale, commonly known as the Chinese fan palm, is a species of palm native to southern China.

It is characterized by its pinnate leaves that are often used in traditional Chinese medicine. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that develop into clustered fruits containing edible seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in forested areas.

Its ornamental value has made it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation.
