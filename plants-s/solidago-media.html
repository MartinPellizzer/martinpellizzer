Solidago media

Solidago media, commonly known as tall goldenrod, is a perennial herb native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces showy, yellow inflorescences composed of numerous small disk flowers surrounded by bracts. Solidago media thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in wetlands, stream banks, and prairies.

It plays an important role in supporting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
