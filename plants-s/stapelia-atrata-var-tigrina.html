Stapelia atrata var. tigrina

Stapelia atrata var. tigrina, commonly known as the tiger-stem stapelia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems that are often ribbed and covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces striking, lantern-shaped flowers that are typically yellow with red markings, resembling a tiger's stripes. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Stapelia atrata var. tigrina is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
