Stellaria alsine f. atlantica

Stellaria alsine f. atlantica, commonly known as the Atlantic sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the coastal regions of the Atlantic coast.

It is characterized by its small, star-shaped white flowers and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and is often found in dune ecosystems. It has a compact growth habit and is well adapted to withstand salt spray and wind.

Its botanical features make it an important component of coastal vegetation.
