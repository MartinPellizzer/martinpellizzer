Stachys chinensis var. albiflora

Stachys chinensis var. albiflora, commonly known as white-flowered betony, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces showy white to pale lavender flowers arranged in whorls along the upper stems. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slight pubescence.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
