Senecio baccharoides

Senecio baccharoides, commonly known as the silverleaf senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its dense, gray-green foliage that reflects light, giving it a silvery appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in spring. It is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Senecio baccharoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance in landscaping.
