Schefflera sodiroi

Schefflera sodiroi, commonly known as the Sodiro's umbrella plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like crown of large, waxy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that are followed by bright red berries. Schefflera sodiroi thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

It has a compact growth habit, making it suitable for indoor cultivation with proper care.
