Stenochlaena kingii

Stenochlaena kingii, commonly known as the King's stenochlaena, is a perennial herb native to the wet tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Araceae, which includes plants such as philodendrons and peace lilies. The plant features a rhizomatous root system and produces striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a spathe, typical of the Araceae family.

Stenochlaena kingii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential pharmacological properties.
