Stenostephanus xanthothrix

Stenostephanus xanthothrix, commonly known as the yellow-hair grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and produces small, yellowish inflorescences. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils in arid or semi-arid regions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a hairy texture that gives it its common name.

It is often used in landscaping for its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
