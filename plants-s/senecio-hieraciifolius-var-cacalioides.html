Senecio hieraciifolius var. cacalioides

Senecio hieraciifolius var. cacalioides, commonly known as the silverleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, dense hairs. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
