Specklinia sibatensis

Specklinia sibatensis, commonly known as the Sibatensis orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that grows on trees in tropical rainforests. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
