Sida grayana

Sida grayana, commonly known as the grayana mallot, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Sida grayana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its foliage and floral display.
