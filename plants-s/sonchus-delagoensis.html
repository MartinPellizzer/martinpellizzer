Sonchus delagoensis

Sonchus delagoensis, commonly known as African sowthistle, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in grasslands and along roadsides. It has hairy stems and leaves with a distinctive bitter taste.

The species is often considered a weed but has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
