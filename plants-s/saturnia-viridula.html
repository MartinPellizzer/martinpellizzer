Saturnia viridula

Saturnia viridula, commonly known as the Green Saturn Moth, is a species of moth belonging to the family Saturniidae.

It is characterized by its striking green coloration and intricate wing patterns, which serve as camouflage in its natural habitat. The plant associated with this moth, *Saturnia viridula*, is a flowering plant known for its tall, erect stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and forest edges.

The plant plays a crucial role in the life cycle of the moth, providing both nectar for adult moths and a habitat for its larvae.
