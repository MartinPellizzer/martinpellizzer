Styrax cespedesii

Styrax cespedesii, commonly known as the Mexican snowbell, is a deciduous shrub native to Mexico.

It features showy, bell-shaped white flowers that bloom in early spring, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It produces small, dark purple berries that are edible but not particularly flavorful.

Styrax cespedesii thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sun, making it a versatile addition to native plant gardens.
