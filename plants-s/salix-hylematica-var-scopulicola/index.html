Salix Hylematica Var. Scopulicola

Rock willow, scientifically known as Salix hylematica var. scopulicola, is a resilient shrub adapted to harsh environments. It thrives in rocky and arid regions, often found in desert landscapes and mountainous terrains. This plant plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for local wildlife. Its unique characteristics make it an important species in ecological restoration efforts. Despite its tough conditions, the rock willow is a symbol of endurance and natural beauty.
