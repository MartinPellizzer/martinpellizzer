Senecio nordenskjoeldii

Senecio nordenskjoeldii, commonly known as the Arctic Senecio, is a flowering plant native to the Arctic regions of Europe and North America.

It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in cold, harsh environments, often found in alpine and tundra habitats. The plant features dense, woolly foliage that helps insulate it from extreme temperatures. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in compact clusters, and bloom in late summer.

Senecio nordenskjoeldii plays a vital role in its ecosystem by providing food and shelter for various Arctic wildlife species.
