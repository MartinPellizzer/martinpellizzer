Santiria rubiginosa var. latipetiolata

Santiria rubiginosa var. latipetiolata, commonly known as the broad-petioled santiria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its woody stems and large, heart-shaped leaves with broad petioles. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers that bloom during the wet season. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

This species is often found in moist, shaded habitats within rainforests.
