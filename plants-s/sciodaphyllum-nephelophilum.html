Sciodaphyllum nephelophilum

Sciodaphyllum nephelophilum, commonly known as the cloud forest fern, is a rare and unique plant species native to high-altitude cloud forests.

Its fronds are broad and leathery, allowing it to retain moisture and withstand frequent rainfall.
