Salvia argentea var. fontanesiana

Salvia argentea var. fontanesiana, commonly known as silver sage, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its gray-green, hairy leaves and spikes of pale blue to violet flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes. Its aromatic leaves and showy flowers make it an attractive addition to gardens.

It is also valued for its historical use in traditional medicine.
