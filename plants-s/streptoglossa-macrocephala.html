Streptoglossa macrocephala

Streptoglossa macrocephala, commonly known as the large-headed orchid, is a species of orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, violet to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has a compact growth habit with a single, upright stem that bears several flowering spikes. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, typically arranged in a rosette at the base.

Streptoglossa macrocephala is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil.
