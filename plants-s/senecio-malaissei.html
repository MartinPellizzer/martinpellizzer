Senecio malaissei

Senecio malaissei, commonly known as the Malaisian groundsel, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, often forming dense mats or low shrubs. Its leaves are generally lance-shaped with serrated edges, and it produces small yellow flowers arranged in composite heads.

Senecio malaissei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its foliage and flowering characteristics.
