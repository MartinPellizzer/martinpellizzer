Stenorrhynchos foliosum

Stenorrhynchos foliosum, commonly known as the broad-leaved stenorrhynchos, is a unique member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
