Sinocalamus stenoauritus

Sinocalamus stenoauritus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bamboo, is a species of grass belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in mountainous regions. Its culms are hollow and jointed, a common feature among bamboo species.

Sinocalamus stenoauritus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional crafts and construction.
