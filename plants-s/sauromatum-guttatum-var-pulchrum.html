Sauromatum guttatum var. pulchrum

Sauromatum guttatum var. pulchrum, commonly known as the red-veined arrowhead, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves with distinctive red veins that contrast sharply with its dark green foliage. The plant thrives in shallow, still waters and is often found in marshy environments. Its flowers are small, white, and clustered in an umbel, adding to its ornamental appeal.

It is popular in water gardens and is valued for its aesthetic beauty and ability to provide habitat for aquatic life.
