Stylidium lindleyanum var. subracemosum

Stylidium lindleyanum var. subracemosum, commonly known as the racemose spurred orchid, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive spurred flowers. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in racemose inflorescences. It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils in arid or semi-arid regions.

This variant is noted for its unique floral structure and is often found in coastal or inland heathlands.
