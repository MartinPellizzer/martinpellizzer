Scleria pubigera

Scleria pubigera, commonly known as the hairy sedges, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes grass-like plants known as sedges. Its inflorescence consists of small, clustered spikes that produce tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

Scleria pubigera is typically found in wet or damp environments such as marshes, stream banks, and swampy areas.
