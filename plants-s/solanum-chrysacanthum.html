Solanum chrysacanthum

Solanum chrysacanthum, commonly known as the golden star solanum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with hairy, ovate leaves. Its fruit is a small, fleshy berry that changes color from green to yellow as it matures.

Solanum chrysacanthum belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
