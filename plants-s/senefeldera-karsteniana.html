Senefeldera karsteniana

Senefeldera karsteniana, commonly known as the Karsten's senefelder, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and serrated edges. Its flowers are typically blue to purple in color, arranged in dense spikes, and bloom during the warmer months.

Senefeldera karsteniana is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
