Stellaria rigida

Stellaria rigida, commonly known as rigid starwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in temperate regions. The plant features slender, erect stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, white or pale pink, and arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems.

Stellaria rigida is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
