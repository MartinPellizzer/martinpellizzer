Sindora leiocarpa

Sindora leiocarpa, commonly known as the white-fruit sindora, is a species of flowering plant in the family Fabaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia, Malaysia, and Thailand. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruits are distinctive, being smooth and often used in traditional medicine.
