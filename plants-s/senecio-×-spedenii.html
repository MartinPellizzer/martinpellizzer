Senecio × spedenii

Senecio × spedenii, commonly known as the hybrid ice plant, is a member of the Asteraceae family and is native to South Africa.

It is a succulent perennial plant characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, demonstrating drought tolerance due to its thick, waxy leaves. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and ability to form dense mats.

Senecio × spedenii is also valued for its role in stabilizing soils in degraded landscapes.
