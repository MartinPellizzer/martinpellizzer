Sabazia liebmannii var. michoacana

Sabazia liebmannii var. michoacana, commonly known as the Michoacan sabazia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like appearance with yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences.

This variety is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and limited distribution.
