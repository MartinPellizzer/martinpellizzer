Sedum linearifolium var. dielsianum

Sedum linearifolium var. dielsianum, commonly known as the Diels' stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and fleshy, linear leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It is well adapted to rocky and arid environments, showcasing excellent drought tolerance.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
