Schoenus tuberosus

Schoenus tuberosus, commonly known as the tuberous bulrush, is a perennial wetland plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its rhizomatous growth and produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant's tuberous roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits. It thrives in shallow water or moist soil, making it a common sight in marshy areas.

Its structure and growth habits make it an important component of wetland ecosystems.
