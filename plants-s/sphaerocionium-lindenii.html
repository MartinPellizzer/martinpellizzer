Sphaerocionium lindenii

Sphaerocionium lindenii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compact rosette of velvety, dark green leaves and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth. It is a popular houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

Sphaerocionium lindenii is also known for its ability to bloom throughout the year under proper conditions.
