Spathiphyllum maldonadoanum

Spathiphyllum maldonadoanum, commonly known as the Maldonado's peace lily, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its broad, dark green leaves and its striking white spathe, which resembles a peace sign. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers inside the spathe, which are not showy but attract pollinators. It thrives in shady, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and air-purifying qualities.
