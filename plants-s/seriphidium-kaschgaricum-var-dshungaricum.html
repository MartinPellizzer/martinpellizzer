Seriphidium kaschgaricum var. dshungaricum

Seriphidium kaschgaricum var. dshungaricum, commonly known as the Dzungarian dwarf fleabane, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is a low-growing annual or biennial herb with yellow flowers and a hairy stem. The plant typically thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy or gravelly soils. Its leaves are narrow and linear, and it produces small, composite flower heads.

It plays a role in stabilizing soils in its native habitats and is of interest to botanists and ecologists.
