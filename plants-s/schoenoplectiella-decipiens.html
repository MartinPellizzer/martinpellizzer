Schoenoplectiella decipiens

Schoenoplectiella decipiens, commonly known as the deceptive spikerush, is a flowering plant species belonging to the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to parts of Europe and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with slender, ribbed stems. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in spikelets, typical of the Cyperaceae family. It thrives in wetland habitats such as marshes and fens, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting aquatic ecosystems.
