Seriphidium tridentatum subsp. vaseyanum

Seriphidium tridentatum subsp.

vaseyanum, commonly known as the Vaseyanum wormwood, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia. It features a compact, bushy growth habit with grey-green, softly hairy foliage.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in dense, flat-topped clusters, typical of the Asteraceae family. Its leaves are lanceolate and often have a strong, aromatic scent.

It is well adapted to dry, saline soils and is often found in steppes and semi-desert environments.
