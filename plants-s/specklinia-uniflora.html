Specklinia uniflora

Specklinia uniflora, commonly known as the single-flower orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, solitary flowers. The plant typically features dark green, strap-like leaves and produces small, fragrant blooms in shades of white or pale yellow. Specklinia uniflora is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
