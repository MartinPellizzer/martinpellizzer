Sorghum cernuum var. orbiculatum

Sorghum cernuum var. orbiculatum, commonly known as the roundleaf sorghum, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its broad, orbicular leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces panicles of small, spikelet-based flowers, which are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun for optimal growth.

This species is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various climates.
