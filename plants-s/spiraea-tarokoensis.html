Spiraea tarokoensis

Spiraea tarokoensis, commonly known as the Taroko spiraea, is a shrub native to Taiwan.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit and is often found in mountainous regions. The plant features small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are ovate to elliptic, with a smooth margin and a glossy surface.

Spiraea tarokoensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
