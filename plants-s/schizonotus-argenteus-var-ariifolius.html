Schizonotus argenteus var. ariifolius

Schizonotus argenteus var. ariifolius, commonly known as the silverleaf false heather, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and silver-blue foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.
