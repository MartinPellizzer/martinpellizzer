Silene pusilla subsp. candavica

Silene pusilla subsp.

candavica, commonly known as the dwarf campion, is a small flowering plant native to South Africa. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

The plant has slender stems and small, pink or white flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are narrow and linear, and it is adapted to withstand dry conditions.
