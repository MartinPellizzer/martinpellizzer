Spachea lactescens

Spachea lactescens, commonly known as the milkweed plant, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its milky sap, which is a distinguishing botanical feature. The plant typically grows as an erect shrub with opposite, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually clustered in umbels and are often pink or white in color.
