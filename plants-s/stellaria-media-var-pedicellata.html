Stellaria media var. pedicellata

Stellaria media var. pedicellata, commonly known as white chickweed, is a low-growing annual herb native to Europe and Asia.

It features small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom from late spring to early autumn. The plant has slender, hairy stems and toothed, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in gardens, meadows, and along pathways.

Its flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, making it a valuable plant for ecological gardens.
