Sopubia lanata var. lanata

Sopubia lanata var. lanata, commonly known as the woolly sopubia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, white, woolly covering on its stems and leaves, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
