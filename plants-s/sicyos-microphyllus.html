Sicyos microphyllus

Sicyos microphyllus, commonly known as the small-leaf bottle gourd, is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its slender stems and small, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that give way to smooth, bottle-shaped fruits. Its botanical features include a fibrous, hollow stem and a distinctive, elongated fruit with a thick, smooth rind.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions and is often cultivated for its edible fruit.
