Saxifraga nervosa subsp. cebennensis

Saxifraga nervosa subsp.

cebennensis, commonly known as the Cebenna saxifrage, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in mountainous areas with cool, moist conditions.

The plant features upright stems with alternate, ovate to elliptic leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are small, white or pale pink, and arranged in dense clusters at the tips of the stems.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in challenging environments.
