Sempervivum tectorum var. decoloratum

Sempervivum tectorum var. decoloratum, commonly known as the "Snowy Forester," is a low-growing succulent with a compact rosette structure.

It features pale green to white leaves that are often tinged with pink or red, giving it a distinctive, frosty appearance. The plant is native to the alpine regions of Central Europe and thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. It produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or lavender during the summer months.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to withstand harsh weather conditions.
