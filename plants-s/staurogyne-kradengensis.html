Staurogyne kradengensis

Staurogyne kradengensis, commonly known as the Kradengensis water plant, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its submerged, ribbon-like leaves that are typically narrow and elongated. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often submerged or floating, adapting to its wetland habitat. Staurogyne kradengensis thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments such as ponds and lakes.
