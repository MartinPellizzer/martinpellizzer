Statice pigadiensis

Statice pigadiensis, commonly known as the pink statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, spike-like inflorescences composed of numerous small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with silver-gray tones that add visual interest.

Statice pigadiensis is often used in floral arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms and vibrant color.
