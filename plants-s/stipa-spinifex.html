Stipa spinifex

Stipa spinifex, commonly known as needlegrass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, needle-like leaves and tufted growth habit. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its inflorescence consists of dense, spike-like clusters of small, spikelet-bearing branches.

Stipa spinifex is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in dryland landscaping.
