Smilax utilis

Smilax utilis, commonly known as catbriar or yellow smilax, is a perennial vine native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its woody stems, which are often covered in sharp, spiny projections. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into red, fleshy berries. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a rough texture.

Smilax utilis is often found in woodland areas and thrives in partial to full shade.
