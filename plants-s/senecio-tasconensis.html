Senecio tasconensis

Senecio tasconensis, commonly known as the Tasmanian groundsel, is a flowering plant native to Tasmania, Australia.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, blue-green leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.

Senecio tasconensis is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
