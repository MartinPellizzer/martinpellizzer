Specklinia cynocephala

Specklinia cynocephala, commonly known as the monkey head orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, tropical environments. The plant is characterized by its unique, rounded pseudobulbs and its striking, showy inflorescences that resemble a monkey's head. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow with intricate patterns, and it blooms during the rainy season.

Specklinia cynocephala is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
