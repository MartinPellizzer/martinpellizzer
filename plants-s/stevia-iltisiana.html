Stevia iltisiana

Stevia iltisiana, commonly known as sweetleaf, is a perennial shrub native to Paraguay and Brazil.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant is renowned for its intense sweetness, derived from steviol glycosides, which are naturally occurring compounds. Stevia iltisiana is often cultivated as a herbal plant for its sweetening properties and is also used in traditional medicine.

Its adaptability to various soil types makes it a versatile plant for both ornamental and functional purposes.
