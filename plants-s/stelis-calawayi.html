Stelis calawayi

Stelis calawayi, commonly known as the Calaway's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, greenish-white or pale yellow blossoms that are clustered in racemes. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Stelis calawayi is a relatively rare species, making it a sought-after plant among orchid enthusiasts.
