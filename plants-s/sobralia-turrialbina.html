Sobralia turrialbina

Sobralia turrialbina, commonly known as the Turrialba sobralia, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Central and South America, particularly found in the cloud forests of Costa Rica and Panama. This plant is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences that feature brightly colored, spurred flowers. The leaves are usually broad and evergreen, providing a lush backdrop to its ornamental blooms.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
