Senecio xantholeucus

Senecio xantholeucus, commonly known as the yellow ragwort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and hairy, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Senecio xantholeucus is often found in arid environments and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
