Stellaria franchetii var. japonica

Stellaria franchetii var. japonica, commonly known as Japanese starwort, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, star-shaped flowers that typically bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, branching stems and produces small, oval leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or grassy areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
