Saxifraga × bilekii

Saxifraga × bilekii, commonly known as the Bilek's saxifrage, is a hybrid plant species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white in color. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine environments. It has narrow, linear leaves that are adapted to withstand cold and arid conditions.

Saxifraga × bilekii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in rock gardens.
