Stanhopea bucephalus var. jenischiana

Stanhopea bucephalus var. jenischiana, commonly known as the Jenisch's stanhopea, is a species of orchid native to Ecuador.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, showy flowers with a complex structure. The plant typically produces pale yellow to greenish-white petals and sepals, with a distinctive, often reddish-brown lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
