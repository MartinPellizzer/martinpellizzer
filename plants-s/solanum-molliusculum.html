Solanum molliusculum

Solanum molliusculum, commonly known as the purple leaf nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its hairy, branched stems and broad, ovate leaves that are often tinged with purple. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that give way to spherical, fleshy berries containing numerous seeds. It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae), which includes other well-known plants such as tomatoes and potatoes.

Solanum molliusculum is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage and unique fruit.
