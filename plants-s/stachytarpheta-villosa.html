Stachytarpheta villosa

Stachytarpheta villosa, commonly known as velvetleaf or silkyleaf, is a tropical shrub native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its hairy, ovate leaves that give it a distinctive velvety appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to blue, attracting various pollinators. It thrives in moist, sunny environments and is often found in disturbed soils.

Stachytarpheta villosa is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
