Solanum hasslerianum var. aureomicans

Solanum hasslerianum var. aureomicans, commonly known as the golden leaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow variegation on its leaves, which adds to its ornamental value. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
