Solidago rugosa var. cronquistiana

Solidago rugosa var. cronquistiana, commonly known as the Cronquist's rough goldenrod, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. It is a perennial herb that plays an important role in supporting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
