Saxifraga litardierei

Saxifraga litardierei, commonly known as the Sierra Nevada saxifrage, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Sierra Nevada mountain range in California.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage, with small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to the harsh conditions of alpine environments. Its leaves are usually succulent, helping it retain moisture in arid conditions.

Saxifraga litardierei plays an important ecological role in its habitat by providing food and shelter for various invertebrates and small mammals.
