Statice langei

Statice langei, commonly known as the Lange's statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces spikes of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, purple, and white. The plant typically blooms in late summer and early autumn, adding vibrant color to gardens and natural landscapes. Statice langei prefers well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its ornamental value and drought tolerance contribute to its cultivation in horticulture.
