Sommera rivularis

Sommera rivularis, commonly known as the river sommera, is a rare aquatic plant found in fast-flowing streams and rivers.

It has long, slender leaves that are adapted to withstand strong water currents. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its roots help stabilize the riverbed and prevent erosion.

Sommera rivularis plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for various microorganisms and small aquatic life.
