Scorzonera yildirimlii

Scorzonera yildirimlii, commonly known as the Turkish Scorzonera, is a perennial herb native to Turkey.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and distinctive yellow flowers. The plant produces a fleshy, tuberous root that is often used in traditional medicine. Scorzonera yildirimlii thrives in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both ecological and medicinal studies.
