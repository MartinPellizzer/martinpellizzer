Schwenckia pallens

Schwenckia pallens, commonly known as the white coneflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, white ray flowers surrounding a central cluster of yellow disk flowers. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Schwenckia pallens typically grows in moist, sandy soils and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.
