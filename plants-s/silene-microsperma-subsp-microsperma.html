Silene microsperma subsp. microsperma

Silene microsperma subsp.

microsperma, commonly known as the small-seeded catchfly, is a flowering plant in the Caryophyllaceae family. It is a herbaceous perennial with slender, ascending stems and small, pink to reddish-purple flowers.

The plant produces numerous tiny, capsule-like fruits that contain minute seeds. It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as sandy soils and disturbed areas.
