Sibara grisea

Sibara grisea, commonly known as the gray sibara, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of East Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a bluish-gray hue on the upper surface. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are usually greenish-white in color.

Sibara grisea is noted for its delicate appearance and is often found in humid, temperate climates.
