Sulcorebutia horacekii

Sulcorebutia horacekii, commonly known as the Horacek's sun rose, is a species of flowering plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular shape and spiny stems. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It is well adapted to dry conditions, with thick, fleshy stems that store water.

Sulcorebutia horacekii is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal.
