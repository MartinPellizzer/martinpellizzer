Saxifraga tellimoides var. watanabei

Saxifraga tellimoides var. watanabei, commonly known as the Watanabei saxifrage, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant features small, clustered leaves that are usually gray-green in color, with a slightly woolly texture. Its flowers are pink to red, appearing in late spring to early summer, and are arranged in dense clusters.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens or as a ground cover.
