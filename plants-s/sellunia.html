Sellunia

Sellunia, commonly known as the moonflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, white, nocturnal blooms that open at night and close by dawn. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, green fruits that contain seeds. Sellunia thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Its unique blooming pattern makes it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections.
