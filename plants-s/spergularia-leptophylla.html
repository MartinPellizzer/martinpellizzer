Spergularia leptophylla

Spergularia leptophylla, commonly known as the slender leaf sandwort, is a low-growing flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, linear leaves and clusters of tiny, white or pinkish flowers. This species is typically found in sandy or gravelly soils, often in coastal or inland dune environments. It is adapted to harsh conditions, including high salinity and wind exposure.

Spergularia leptophylla plays a role in stabilizing sandy soils and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
