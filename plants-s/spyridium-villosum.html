Spyridium villosum

Spyridium villosum, commonly known as the silky spyridium, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of up to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually pale yellow to cream in color. Its leaves are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Spyridium villosum is often found in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.
