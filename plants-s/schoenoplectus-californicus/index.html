Schoenoplectus Californicus

Schoenoplectus californicus is a common wetland plant found throughout much of california. it thrives in marshy areas and shallow water environments. this plant is known for its tall, grass-like stems and fluffy, seed-producing heads. it plays an important role in stabilizing wetland soils and providing habitat for various wildlife. its presence is often an indicator of healthy aquatic ecosystems.
