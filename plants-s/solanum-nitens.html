Solanum nitens

Solanum nitens, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. The plant produces berries that are typically yellow or orange when mature, containing toxic alkaloids. Solanum nitens thrives in dry, arid environments and is often found in rocky soils.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the nightshade family, Solanaceae.
