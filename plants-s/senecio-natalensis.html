Senecio natalensis

Senecio natalensis, commonly known as Natal groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright, branched stems and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas. It has hairy leaves that help reduce water loss, adapting it to arid environments.

Senecio natalensis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
