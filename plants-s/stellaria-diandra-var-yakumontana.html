Stellaria diandra var. yakumontana

Stellaria diandra var. yakumontana, commonly known as Yakumontan starwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in early summer, and its leaves are typically narrow and linear. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky habitats. It is characterized by its low-growing habit and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
