Salacia diffusiflora

Salacia diffusiflora, commonly known as the spreading salacia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, fragrant yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the family Salaciacae and is known for its ornamental value due to its attractive appearance. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

The leaves are glossy and elliptical, providing a lush backdrop for its vibrant floral display.
