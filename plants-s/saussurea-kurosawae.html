Saussurea kurosawae

Saussurea kurosawae, commonly known as the Kurosawa's saffron.

It is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Japan, particularly in the northern and central parts of Honshu. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellowish-orange flower heads that resemble saffron. It typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, thriving in cool and moist environments.
