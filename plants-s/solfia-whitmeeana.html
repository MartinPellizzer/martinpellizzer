Solfia whitmeeana

Solfia whitmeeana, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, branching stems and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to purple. The leaves are typically rounded and glossy, providing a lush backdrop for the blooms. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

The plant is also valued for its ability to produce offsets, which can be propagated to create new plants.
