Stachys tenuifolia var. perlonga

Stachys tenuifolia var. perlonga, commonly known as slender leaf betony, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, hairy stems that often grow in dense clusters. The leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a hairy surface, and the plant produces spikes of small, pale blue to violet flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
