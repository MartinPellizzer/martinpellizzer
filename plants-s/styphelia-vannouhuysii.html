Styphelia vannouhuysii

Styphelia vannouhuysii, commonly known as the Cape Peninsula heath, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Western Cape region of South Africa.

The plant features small, needle-like leaves and produces delicate, bell-shaped white or pink flowers during the summer months. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal fynbos vegetation.

Styphelia vannouhuysii is well adapted to the Mediterranean climate of its native habitat, with drought-resistant properties and a slow growth rate.
