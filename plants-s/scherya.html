Scherya

Scherya, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

These plants are typically found in rocky or mountainous regions, where they thrive in well-drained soils. Scherya species are characterized by their small, delicate flowers that often have a unique coloration and structure.
