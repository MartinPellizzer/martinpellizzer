Stelis aphidifera

Stelis aphidifera, commonly known as the aphid-bearing stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on tree trunks or branches, thriving in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a pseudobulb with a single, linear leaf and produces delicate, white or pale pink flowers. It is notable for its association with aphids, which often reside on its stems and leaves.

This orchid is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and ease of cultivation.
