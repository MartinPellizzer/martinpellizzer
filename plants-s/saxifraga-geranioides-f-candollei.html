Saxifraga geranioides f. candollei

Saxifraga geranioides f. candollei, commonly known as the candollei saxifrage, is a flowering plant belonging to the Saxifragaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptic, with a slightly hairy surface. This variety is native to Europe and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

It is appreciated for its ornamental value in rock gardens and alpine settings.
