Scutellaria albida subsp. colchica

Scutellaria albida subsp.

colchica, commonly known as the white skullcap, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves.

The plant features showy blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically opposite and oval-shaped, with a hairy texture. Scutellaria albida subsp.

colchica is often found in rocky or woodland habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
