Smilax china var. yakushimensis

Smilax china var. yakushimensis, commonly known as the Japanese arrowroot or Japanese smilax, is a climbing shrub native to East Asia.

It features woody stems with sharp spines and compound leaves that are alternate and pinnately arranged. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in terminal panicles. Its fruit is a bright red, fleshy berry that contains several seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
