Sciodaphyllum octostylum

Sciodaphyllum octostylum, commonly known as the giant tree fern, is a towering member of the Cyatheaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, feather-like fronds that can reach lengths of up to 10 meters. The plant has a thick, fibrous trunk that supports its massive canopy. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical rainforests.

Its unique structure and slow growth make it a significant component of forest ecosystems.
