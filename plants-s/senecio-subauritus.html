Senecio subauritus

Senecio subauritus, commonly known as the auriculate senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Senecio subauritus is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its elegant appearance and adaptability to various growing conditions.
