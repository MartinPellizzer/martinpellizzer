Stanhopea oculata var. guttulata

Stanhopea oculata var. guttulata, commonly known as the Guatemalan stanhopea, is a striking orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its vivid, colorful flowers that often feature intricate patterns and a unique, elongated shape. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, tropical environments. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native habitat.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively manageable care requirements.
