Stellaria holostea var. minor

Stellaria holostea var. minor, commonly known as lesser starwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically features small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has slender, branching stems and produces tiny, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows, stream banks, and woodland edges.

Its delicate appearance and unique floral structure make it a notable species in botanical studies.
