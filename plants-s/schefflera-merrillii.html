Schefflera merrillii

Schefflera merrillii, commonly known as the Philippine umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of large, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by bright red berries. Schefflera merrillii thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

It has a shallow root system, making it suitable for container gardening.
