Schefflera regnelliana

Schefflera regnelliana, commonly known as the African nettle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant features pinnately compound leaves with a distinctive net-like venation pattern. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in umbels, and it produces red berries that are edible for some animals.

Schefflera regnelliana is often found in moist, wooded areas and is valued for its ornamental leaves and ecological importance.
