Scaphyglottis Bifida

Scaphyglottis bifida is a rare and striking orchid native to the wetlands of Central America. Its unique appearance features two distinct lobes on each flower, giving it a distinctive and almost otherworldly look. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires careful cultivation to flourish. Due to its limited distribution and delicate nature, it is considered a conservation concern. Despite its beauty, Scaphyglottis bifida remains a mysterious and underappreciated member of the orchid family.
