Stellaria longipes var. edwardsii

Stellaria longipes var. edwardsii, commonly known as the Edwards' starwort, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, star-shaped white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in meadows and woodland edges. It has slender stems with opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

This variety is distinguished by its elongated stems and is sometimes considered a rare or local species within its range.
