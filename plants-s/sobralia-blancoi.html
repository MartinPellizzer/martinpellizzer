Sobralia blancoi

Sobralia blancoi, commonly known as the white sobralia, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and its ability to produce multiple flower spikes during the growing season. The flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive, recurved lip that adds to its ornamental appeal. This plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
