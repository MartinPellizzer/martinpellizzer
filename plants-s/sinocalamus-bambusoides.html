Sinocalamus bambusoides

Sinocalamus bambusoides, commonly known as the Chinese bamboo or bamboo-like grass, is a member of the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks that can reach heights of up to 3 meters. The plant has long, narrow leaves that are typically green and have a smooth texture. It is native to China and thrives in moist, temperate climates.

Sinocalamus bambusoides is often cultivated for its ornamental value and its ability to form dense stands in suitable environments.
