Syzygium apodum

Syzygium apodum, commonly known as the slender satinash, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and Malaysia. Its fruit is a small, fleshy drupe that ripens to a dark red or purple color.

Syzygium apodum is often grown for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
