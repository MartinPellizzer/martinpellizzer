Satureja Insularis

Satureja insularis is a rare and unique plant native to specific island habitats. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, known for its aromatic and often medicinal properties. This species is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and delicate pink or white flowers. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts. Its presence highlights the importance of preserving biodiversity in isolated ecosystems.
