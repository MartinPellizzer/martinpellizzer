Solanum pseudocapsicum var. lancifolium

Solanum pseudocapsicum var. lancifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved nightshade, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and produces small, usually green or yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in temperate regions and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides. Its fruits are berries that can be toxic if ingested, containing alkaloids similar to those found in other nightshade species.

This variant is distinguished by its narrower foliage compared to other varieties of Solanum pseudocapsicum.
