Swertia pallasii

Swertia pallasii, commonly known as the Himalayan gentian, is a perennial herb native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, thriving at elevations between 2,000 and 4,500 meters. Its leaves are opposite, lanceolate, and often covered with a fine pubescence.

Swertia pallasii is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
