Stapelia macowanii var. conformis

Stapelia macowanii var. conformis, commonly known as the narrow-leafed starfish plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant has fleshy, ribbed stems and narrow, dark green leaves that help it store water. It thrives in arid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for succulent collections.
