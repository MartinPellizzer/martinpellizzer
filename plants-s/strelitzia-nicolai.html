Strelitzia nicolai

Strelitzia nicolai, commonly known as the giant yellow bird of paradise, is a striking tropical plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, banana-like leaves that can grow up to 3 meters in length, forming a dense rosette. The plant produces dramatic, bird-of-paradise-like inflorescences with bright yellow petals and a blue-violet center. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Strelitzia nicolai is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its unique and eye-catching appearance.
