Specklinia rinkei

Specklinia rinkei, commonly known as the silver-veined orchid, is a small epiphytic orchid native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. The plant produces delicate, white or pale yellow flowers with a distinctive, fragrant aroma. It thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements, it is a favored plant among orchid enthusiasts.
