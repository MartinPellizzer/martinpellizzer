Styrax officinalis var. californicus

Styrax officinalis var. californicus, commonly known as California fringetree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to coastal areas of California.

It features opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces fragrant, white, clustered flowers in spring, which are followed by elongated, woody fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
