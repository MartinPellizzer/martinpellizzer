Sideritis infernalis

Sideritis infernalis, commonly known as the Infernal Sideritis, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that range in color from pale blue to purple. Its leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Sideritis infernalis is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to thrive in dry, sunny environments.
