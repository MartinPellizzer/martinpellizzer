Scutellaria ussuriensis

Scutellaria ussuriensis, commonly known as Ussuri skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the Far East of Russia and parts of northern China.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces small, tubular flowers that are usually blue or purple in color. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs.

Scutellaria ussuriensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and antispasmodic effects.
