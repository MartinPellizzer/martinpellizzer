Silene vidaliana

Silene vidaliana, commonly known as the violet silene, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 20 to 40 centimeters. The plant features showy, violet-colored flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Silene vidaliana is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.
