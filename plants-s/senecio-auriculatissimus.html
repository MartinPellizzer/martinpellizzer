Senecio auriculatissimus

Senecio auriculatissimus, commonly known as the auriculate senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens, though it requires careful management to prevent it from becoming invasive.
