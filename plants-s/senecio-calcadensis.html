Senecio calcadensis

Senecio calcadensis, commonly known as the limestone groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white to pale blue petals. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.

Senecio calcadensis is adapted to withstand drought and high temperatures, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
