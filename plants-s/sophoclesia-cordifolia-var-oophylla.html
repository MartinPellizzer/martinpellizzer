Sophoclesia cordifolia var. oophylla

Sophoclesia cordifolia var. oophylla, commonly known as the heartleaf oophylla, is a rare and striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which are typically a vibrant green with a waxy texture. The plant produces striking purple or pink flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a dramatic visual element to its appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a botanical curiosity and is often cultivated in specialized gardens.
