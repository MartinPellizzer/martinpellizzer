Sonchus asper f. laevis

Sonchus asper f. laevis, commonly known as the smooth prickly lettuce, is a variant of the prickly lettuce species.

It is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its rough, hairy leaves and yellow flowers. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a distinctive waxy coating. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been introduced to other regions.

This plant often grows in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats.
