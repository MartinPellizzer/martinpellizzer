Stapelia × multiflora

Stapelia × multiflora, commonly known as the hybrid starfish plant, is a succulent perennial native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed stems and thick, waxy leaves. The plant produces striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange with a distinctive, almost fleshy appearance. These flowers are pollinated by specific insects, particularly flies, due to their strong scent and coloration.

Stapelia × multiflora is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
