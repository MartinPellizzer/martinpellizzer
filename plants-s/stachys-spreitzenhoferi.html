Stachys spreitzenhoferi

Stachys spreitzenhoferi, commonly known as the Spreitzenhofer's hedge-nettle, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves.
