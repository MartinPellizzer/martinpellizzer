Scutellaria kiusiana var. kuromidakensis

Scutellaria kiusiana var. kuromidakensis, commonly known as the Japanese skullcap, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

Its ornamental value and medicinal properties make it a notable species in botanical studies.
