Silene odessana

Silene odessana, commonly known as the Odessa campion, is a flowering plant native to the steppes of southern Russia and Ukraine.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are pink to purple in color, with five petals and numerous stamens, typical of the Caryophyllaceae family.

Silene odessana thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
