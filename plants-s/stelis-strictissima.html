Stelis strictissima

Stelis strictissima, commonly known as the rigid stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, forming a symbiotic relationship with mycorrhizal fungi. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette pattern, and it blooms during the rainy season.

Stelis strictissima is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
