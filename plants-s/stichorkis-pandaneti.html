Stichorkis pandaneti

Stichorkis pandaneti, commonly known as the giant pandanus, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It belongs to the family Pandanaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender stem and large, pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or green in color. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing near coastal areas.

Its leaves are used in traditional crafts and for making thatch, showcasing its cultural and practical significance.
