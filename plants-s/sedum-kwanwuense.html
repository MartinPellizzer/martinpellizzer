Sedum kwanwuense

Sedum kwanwuense, commonly known as the Kwanwuense stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to China.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in mountainous regions. Its small, star-shaped flowers are usually pink or white, and it blooms in the summer months.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
