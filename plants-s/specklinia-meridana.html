Specklinia meridana

Specklinia meridana, commonly known as the golden rosette.

It is a flowering plant native to Madagascar, belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae. The plant is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit and vibrant yellow flowers. Its leaves are fleshy and covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs.

Specklinia meridana is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
