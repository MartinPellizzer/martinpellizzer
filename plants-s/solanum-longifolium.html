Solanum longifolium

Solanum longifolium, commonly known as the long-leaved nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is native to Australia and is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, star-shaped white or pale blue flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that turn from green to yellow or red as they mature. It is often found in dry, open habitats such as woodlands and grasslands.

Due to its toxic properties, it is important to handle the plant with care.
