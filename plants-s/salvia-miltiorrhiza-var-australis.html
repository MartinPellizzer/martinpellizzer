Salvia miltiorrhiza var. australis

Salvia miltiorrhiza var. australis, commonly known as the Australian red sage, is a perennial herb native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and dark purple to reddish flowers. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
