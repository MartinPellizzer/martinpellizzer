Salacia duckei

Salacia duckei, commonly known as the golden banyan, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Salicaceae, which includes willows and poplars. The plant is characterized by its large, broad leaves and clusters of yellowish-green flowers. It thrives in moist, tropical environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Salacia duckei is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local medicine.
