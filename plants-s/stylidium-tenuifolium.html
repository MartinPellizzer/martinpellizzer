Stylidium tenuifolium

Stylidium tenuifolium, commonly known as the slender-leaf sun-orchid, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, sun-like appearance. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Stylidium tenuifolium is adapted to arid conditions and is considered a rare and delicate species.
