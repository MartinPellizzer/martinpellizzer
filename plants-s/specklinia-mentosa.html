Specklinia mentosa

Specklinia mentosa, commonly known as the Mentosa orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a rosette-like growth habit, featuring narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Specklinia mentosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
