Stolzia compacta

Stolzia compacta, commonly known as the dwarf stolzia, is a small, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded growth habit with dense clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has aromatic, dark green leaves that are slightly hairy beneath. It is well adapted to Mediterranean climates and thrives in well-drained soil.

Stolzia compacta is often used in rock gardens or as a container plant due to its ornamental appeal and compact size.
