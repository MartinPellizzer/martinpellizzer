Silviella Serpyllifolia

Silviella serpyllifolia is a small, resilient plant often found in rocky and coastal environments. It thrives in harsh conditions, demonstrating remarkable adaptability to salt and wind. Its narrow, grass-like leaves help it conserve water and withstand strong weather. The plant is commonly known as sea thrift and is a symbol of coastal resilience. Despite its delicate appearance, it plays an important role in stabilizing shoreline soils.
