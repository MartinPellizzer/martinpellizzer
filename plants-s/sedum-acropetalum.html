Sedum acropetalum

Sedum acropetalum, commonly known as the upright stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow to orange. The plant is adapted to rocky and well-drained soils, making it suitable for xerophytic gardens. Its leaves are typically opposite and covered in a waxy cuticle to reduce water loss.

Sedum acropetalum is often used in rock gardens and as a ground cover due to its hardiness and ornamental value.
