Stichorkis graciliscapa

Stichorkis graciliscapa, commonly known as the slender-stemmed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant with thin, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The flowers are typically pale green to white, with three sepals and three petals, and are arranged in a spike-like inflorescence. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
