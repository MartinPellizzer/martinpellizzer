Stelis viridiflava

Stelis viridiflava, commonly known as the green-flowered orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are usually ovate and dark green, while the inflorescence consists of a few showy flowers.

Stelis viridiflava is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
