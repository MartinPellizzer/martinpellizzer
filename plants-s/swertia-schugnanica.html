Swertia schugnanica

Swertia schugnanica, commonly known as the Schugnan gentian, is a perennial herb native to the high-altitude regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Swertia schugnanica is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
