Scutellaria lotienensis

Scutellaria lotienensis, commonly known as the lotienensis skullcap, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Scutellaria lotienensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
