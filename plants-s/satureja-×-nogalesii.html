Satureja × nogalesii

Satureja × nogalesii, commonly known as California mountain savory, is a hybrid shrub native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its small, tubular flowers are usually pink or purple, and it produces small, ovoid fruits.

Satureja × nogalesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal practices.
