Sobralia sanctorum

Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Sobralia sanctorum is often cultivated for its beauty and is valued in horticulture for its ability to thrive in shaded, humid environments.
