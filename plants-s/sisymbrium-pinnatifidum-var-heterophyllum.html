Sisymbrium pinnatifidum var. heterophyllum

Sisymbrium pinnatifidum var. heterophyllum, commonly known as the narrowleaf winter cress, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has pinnately divided leaves that are often narrow and linear, giving it a distinctive appearance. It is native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.

This species is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its role in supporting pollinators.
