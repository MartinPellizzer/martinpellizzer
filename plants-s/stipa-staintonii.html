Stipa staintonii

Stipa staintonii, commonly known as Stainton's needlegrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and produces narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant bears small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets, which are often wind-pollinated. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Stipa staintonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
