Struthanthus caribaeus

Struthanthus caribaeus, commonly known as the Caribbean starflower, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant produces striking yellow flowers with a star-shaped appearance, which bloom in the summer months. It has small, narrow leaves and is often found in tropical and subtropical climates.

Struthanthus caribaeus is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
