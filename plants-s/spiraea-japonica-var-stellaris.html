Spiraea japonica var. stellaris

Spiraea japonica var. stellaris, commonly known as star spiraea, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, clustered flowers that bloom in early summer. The foliage is typically dark green, providing a striking contrast to the white or pale pink blooms. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden borders or containers.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of maintenance in landscaping.
