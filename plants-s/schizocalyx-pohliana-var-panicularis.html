Schizocalyx pohliana var. panicularis

Schizocalyx pohliana var. panicularis, commonly known as the panicled schizocalyx, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen shrub characterized by its aromatic, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered in paniculate inflorescences, giving it its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its botanical features make it a valuable plant for ornamental and horticultural purposes.
