Sitanion californicum

Sitanion californicum, commonly known as the California buckwheat, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Polygonaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers. Sitanion californicum is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for native pollinators.
