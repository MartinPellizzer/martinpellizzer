Stelis suaveolens

Stelis suaveolens, commonly known as the sweet-scented orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, tropical environments. The plant features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink.

Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.
