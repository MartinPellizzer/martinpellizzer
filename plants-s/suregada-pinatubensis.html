Suregada pinatubensis

Suregada pinatubensis, commonly known as the Philippine native orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its elegant, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the soil.

It plays an important ecological role in its native habitat by supporting local biodiversity.
