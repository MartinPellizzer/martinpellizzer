Stelis juncea

Stelis juncea, commonly known as the slender stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features a slender, upright stem with few nodes and produces small, greenish-white flowers. Its roots are fibrous and adapted for anchoring in loose soil.

The botanical characteristics of Stelis juncea make it well-suited for epiphytic or lithophytic growth in suitable microhabitats.
