Sanseviella carnea

Sanseviella carnea, commonly known as the Crimson Sansevieria, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with red or pink variegation along the edges. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves to survive drought conditions. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, usually in shades of yellow or green.

Sanseviella carnea is a popular indoor plant due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
