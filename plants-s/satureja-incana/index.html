Satureja Incana

Satureja incana is a hardy perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia. It is commonly known as woodruff or lesser betony and is valued for its aromatic leaves and medicinal properties. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its small purple flowers bloom in the summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. Satureja incana has been used for centuries in traditional medicine and as a culinary herb.
