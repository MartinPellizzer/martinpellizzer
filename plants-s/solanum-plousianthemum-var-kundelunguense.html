Solanum plousianthemum var. kundelunguense

Solanum plousianthemum var. kundelunguense, commonly known as the Kundelungu nightshade, is a shrub native to the Democratic Republic of the Congo.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its dark green, ovate leaves and clusters of small, star-shaped white flowers. The plant produces violet-black berries that are toxic to humans but serve as a food source for certain wildlife.

It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in dense undergrowth in lowland forests.
