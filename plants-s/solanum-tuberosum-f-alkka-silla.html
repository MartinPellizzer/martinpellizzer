Solanum tuberosum f. alkka-silla

Solanum tuberosum f. alkka-silla, commonly known as the Alkka-Silla potato, is a botanical variety of the common potato.

It is characterized by its unique tuber morphology, featuring a distinctive waxy skin and a high starch content. This variety originates from Finland and is adapted to the cooler climate of the region. The plant exhibits typical solanaceous features, including compound leaves and solanum-type flowers.

It is valued for its culinary qualities and resilience to certain environmental conditions.
