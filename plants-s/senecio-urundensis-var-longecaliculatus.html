Senecio urundensis var. longecaliculatus

Senecio urundensis var. longecaliculatus, commonly known as the long-stemmed senecio, is a member of the Asteraceae family and is native to South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, cylindrical stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for gardeners seeking a distinctive and low-maintenance ornamental plant.
