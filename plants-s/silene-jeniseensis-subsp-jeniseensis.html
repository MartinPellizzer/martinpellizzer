Silene jeniseensis subsp. jeniseensis

Silene jeniseensis subsp.

jeniseensis, commonly known as the Jenisean catchfly, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia. It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes.

The plant produces striking pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, adapted to withstand cold and arid conditions.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitat.
