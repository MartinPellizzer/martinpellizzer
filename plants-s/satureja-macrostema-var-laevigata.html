Satureja macrostema var. laevigata

Satureja macrostema var. laevigata, commonly known as smoothleaf savory, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, aromatic leaves with a slightly minty and spicy scent, often used in culinary applications. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular choice for both medicinal and decorative purposes.
