Saccolabium orbiculare

Saccolabium orbiculare, commonly known as the roundleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, yellow disc flowers surrounded by white to pale pink ray florets. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Saccolabium orbiculare is often found in dry, open habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
