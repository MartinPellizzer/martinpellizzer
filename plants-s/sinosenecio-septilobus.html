Sinosenecio septilobus

Sinosenecio septilobus, commonly known as the seven-lobed sinosenecio, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features hairy, slender stems and lance-shaped leaves with distinct lobes. Its flowers are typically small and yellow, arranged in clustered inflorescences.

It is often found in rocky or mountainous habitats, thriving in temperate climates.
