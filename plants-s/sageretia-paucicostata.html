Sageretia paucicostata

Sageretia paucicostata, commonly known as the narrow-costate sageretia, is a member of the Rosaceae family.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in the spring. Its fruits are follicles that split open to release seeds.

Sageretia paucicostata is native to China and is often found in forested areas.
