Strobilanthes oligantha f. alba

Strobilanthes oligantha f. alba, commonly known as the white-flowered strobilanthes, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking white bracts that resemble flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture.

The species is noted for its ornamental value due to its unique and long-lasting floral display.
