Sideritis bolosiana

Sideritis bolosiana, commonly known as the Balkan ironweed, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants known for their medicinal properties. The plant features slender stems and small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported calming and antispasmodic effects.

Sideritis bolosiana is also valued for its role in local ecosystems, supporting pollinators and contributing to biodiversity.
