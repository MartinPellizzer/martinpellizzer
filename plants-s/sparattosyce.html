Sparattosyce

Sparattosyce, commonly known as the ghost orchid, is a rare and enigmatic flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, ethereal appearance, with long, slender stems and delicate, pale green or white flowers. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of trees without deriving nutrients from them. Sparattosyce has small, scale-like leaves and produces flowers that bloom in a single cluster at the top of the stem.
