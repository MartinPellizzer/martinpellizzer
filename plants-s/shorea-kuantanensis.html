Shorea kuantanensis

Shorea kuantanensis, commonly known as Kuantan ironwood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Dipterocarpaceae family and is characterized by its dense, heavy wood that is highly valued for its strength and durability. The tree can grow up to 50 meters tall and has a straight trunk with a smooth, dark grey bark. Its leaves are alternate, elliptical, and have a leathery texture, while its flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically green or yellowish in color.

The plant plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, contributing to biodiversity and soil stability.
