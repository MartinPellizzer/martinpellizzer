Smithiantha zebrina var. geroltiana

Smithiantha zebrina var. geroltiana, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a striking member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its vibrant, striped leaves that resemble a zebra's coat. The plant features clusters of tubular, bright red flowers that bloom in summer, adding dramatic visual interest. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
