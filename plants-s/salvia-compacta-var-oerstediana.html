Salvia compacta var. oerstediana

Salvia compacta var. oerstediana, commonly known as the compact sage, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features compact, upright growth with narrow, gray-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces spikes of violet to purple flowers during the summer months, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and garden design.
