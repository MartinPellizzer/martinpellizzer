Scutellaria megalaspis

Scutellaria megalaspis, commonly known as the large-leaf skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Scutellaria megalaspis is valued in traditional medicine for its potential medicinal properties.
