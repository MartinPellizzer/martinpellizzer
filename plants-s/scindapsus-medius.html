Scindapsus medius

Scindapsus medius, commonly known as the silver vine, is a tropical evergreen vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that often have a silvery or variegated pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to prevent root rot.

Scindapsus medius is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
