Sonchus asper subsp. nymanii

Sonchus asper subsp.

nymanii, commonly known as rough prickly lettuce, is a flowering plant in the daisy family (Asteraceae). It is characterized by its hairy, rough stems and leaves with serrated edges.

The plant produces small yellow flowers that are clustered in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been introduced to other regions.

It thrives in disturbed soils and can be found in fields, roadsides, and other open areas.
