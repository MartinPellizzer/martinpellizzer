Siphanthemum

Siphanthemum, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 feet. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a waxy texture that helps reduce water loss.

Siphanthemum is native to tropical regions and thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil.
