Stenophyllus capillaris var. nitidinux

Stenophyllus capillaris var. nitidinux, commonly known as the glossy thread fern, is a variant of the thread fern characterized by its slender, linear leaves.

The plant features a creeping rhizome that produces numerous small, clustered fronds. Its leaves are typically glossy and have a fine, silky texture, which distinguishes it from other similar species. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

The varietal name "nitidinux" refers to its shiny, smooth leaf surface.
