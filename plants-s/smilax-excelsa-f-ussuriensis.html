Smilax excelsa f. ussuriensis

Smilax excelsa f. ussuriensis, commonly known as the Ussuri arrowhead, is a variant of the arrowhead vine native to eastern Asia.

It is a climbing perennial shrub characterized by its distinctive arrowhead-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value and adaptability make it a popular choice for gardeners in temperate regions.
