Solanum tenuifolium

Solanum tenuifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves with a hairy texture, and its flowers are usually white or pale blue, appearing in clusters. The fruit is a small, green berry that turns red when ripe, containing several seeds.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae), which includes other plants like tomatoes and potatoes.
