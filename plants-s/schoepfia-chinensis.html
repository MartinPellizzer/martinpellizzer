Schoepfia chinensis

Schoepfia chinensis, commonly known as Chinese Schoepfia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright, herbaceous stems and clusters of small, yellowish to orange-red composite flowers. The plant produces small, oval-shaped fruits called cypselae, which are typically covered in fine hairs. Native to China, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Its botanical features make it a notable species for both ecological and horticultural studies.
