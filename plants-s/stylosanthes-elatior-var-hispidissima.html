Stylosanthes elatior var. hispidissima

Stylosanthes elatior var. hispidissima, commonly known as the hairy stylosanthes, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and trifoliate leaves with hairy surfaces. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink, followed by cylindrical seed pods. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in forage and soil stabilization.

Its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen makes it a valuable plant in agricultural systems.
