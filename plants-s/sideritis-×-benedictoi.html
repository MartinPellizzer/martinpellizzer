Sideritis × benedictoi

Sideritis × benedictoi, commonly known as the Benedict's ironweed, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils, often in mountainous regions of the Mediterranean. Its leaves are usually narrow and hairy, with a distinct aromatic scent.

Sideritis × benedictoi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
