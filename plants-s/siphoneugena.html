Siphoneugena

Siphoneugena, commonly known as the red-flowered orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety red petals and a unique, spurred lip that distinguishes it from other orchid species. Native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in warm, humid environments with well-drained soil. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is prized in horticultural collections for its vibrant color and elegance.
