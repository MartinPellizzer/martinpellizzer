Stapelia cylista

Stapelia cylista, commonly known as the cyclist stapelia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, waxy, and often ribbed stems that resemble the shape of a bicycle. The plant produces large, fleshy, and showy flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It is a member of the Apocynaceae family and is known for its ability to store water in its thick stems.

Stapelia cylista thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.
