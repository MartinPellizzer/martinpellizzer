Sedum floriferum

Sedum floriferum, commonly known as the flowering stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to South America.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a dense rosette pattern. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
