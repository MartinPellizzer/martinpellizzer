Sideritis glauca

Sideritis glauca, commonly known as blue beard, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically blue or purple, arranged in whorls along the stem.

Sideritis glauca is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
