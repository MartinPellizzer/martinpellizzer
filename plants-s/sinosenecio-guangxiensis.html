Sinosenecio guangxiensis

Sinosenecio guangxiensis, commonly known as Guangxi Senecio, is a flowering plant native to China, specifically the Guangxi region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and white.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
