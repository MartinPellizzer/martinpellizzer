Sedum cyrenaicum

Sedum cyrenaicum, commonly known as the desert stonecress, is a low-growing succulent plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, allowing it to store water efficiently. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in summer. It is well adapted to hot, dry environments and requires minimal water once established.

Sedum cyrenaicum is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
