Stellaria bungeana var. stubendorffii

Stellaria bungeana var. stubendorffii, commonly known as the Chinese starwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to China.

It features small, white or pale pink flowers that are typically arranged in clusters, giving it a star-like appearance. The plant has thin, branching stems and small, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.

Botanically, it is characterized by its distinctive floral morphology and its role in local ecosystems.
