Sium tenuifolium

Sium tenuifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved water pepper, is a herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the family Brassicaceae, which includes mustard and cabbages. The plant has slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are white or pale purple, arranged in clusters, and it produces small, rounded fruits.
