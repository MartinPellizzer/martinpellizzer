Solanum rufirameum

Solanum rufirameum, commonly known as the red-stemmed nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and peppers. The plant is characterized by its hairy, reddish stems and small, yellowish flowers. Its fruits are typically berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

Solanum rufirameum is often found in dry, open habitats and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
