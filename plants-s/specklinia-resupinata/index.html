Specklinia Resupinata

Specklinia resupinata is a unique epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. This plant is known for its striking appearance, featuring dark green leaves with distinctive white speckles. It thrives in humid environments and requires indirect light to flourish. Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is often sought after by plant enthusiasts. Its cultivation presents both a challenge and a reward for those dedicated to growing exotic flora.
