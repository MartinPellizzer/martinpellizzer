Schoenus monoicus

Schoenus monoicus, commonly known as the slender flat sedge, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wetland habitats such as marshes and swampy areas. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, greenish flowers in spikelets arranged in clusters. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense mats in its environment.

Schoenus monoicus plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
