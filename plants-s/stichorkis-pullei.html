Stichorkis pullei

Stichorkis pullei, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. The leaves are typically velvety and dark green, often with a waxy coating. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

The plant is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
