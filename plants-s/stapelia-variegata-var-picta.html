Stapelia variegata var. picta

Stapelia variegata var. picta, commonly known as the variegated starfish cactus, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a unique, star-shaped appearance with fleshy, ribbed stems that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer, adding a subtle ornamental touch. Its leaves are typically reduced to spines, and it thrives in well-draining soil with minimal water.

It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements.
