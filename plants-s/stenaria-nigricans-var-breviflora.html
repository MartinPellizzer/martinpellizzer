Stenaria nigricans var. breviflora

Stenaria nigricans var. breviflora, commonly known as the short-flowered stenaria, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open woodland or grassland habitats. The plant features slender, erect stems with small, dark green, oval-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense inflorescences, usually with pale blue or violet petals.

It is known for its ability to thrive in arid conditions and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
