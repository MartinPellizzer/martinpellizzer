Solanum filiforme var. subdentatum

Solanum filiforme var. subdentatum, commonly known as the slender nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial native to South America.

It features slender, branched stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by berry-like fruits that are typically red when mature. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with finely serrated margins characteristic of the varietal name subdentatum.

This species is often found in moist, shaded habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
