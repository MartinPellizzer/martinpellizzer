Sarcochilus mcgregorii

Sarcochilus mcgregorii, commonly known as the McGregor's sarcochilus, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a pseudobulb that supports its foliage and flowers. The plant produces a single, showy spike with several pale green to yellow flowers. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, often covered in a fine, hairy coating.

This orchid thrives in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
