Schefflera fargesii

Schefflera fargesii, commonly known as the Farges' umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded crown of large, glossy, dark green leaves that resemble an umbrella. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red berries, which are a food source for birds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Schefflera fargesii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor conditions.
