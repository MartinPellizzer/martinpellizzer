Spathoglottis papuana var. puberiflora

Spathoglottis papuana var. puberiflora, commonly known as the Papuan orchid, is a flowering plant native to New Guinea.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of white to pale pink flowers with a distinctive spatulate lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural collections.
