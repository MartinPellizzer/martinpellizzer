Specklinia soratana

Specklinia soratana, commonly known as the silver-veined orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive silvery-green coloration. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, typically blooming during the rainy season. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Specklinia soratana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
