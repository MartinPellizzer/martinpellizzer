Stylisma patens subsp. patens

Stylisma patens subsp.

patens, commonly known as the broadleaf stylisma, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Southeast Asia. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or wetlands.

The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and clustered in loose inflorescences.

It is characterized by its fibrous root system and is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
