Solanum nigrum f. glabrum

Solanum nigrum f. glabrum, commonly known as the smooth black nightshade, is a variant of the common black nightshade.

It is a herbaceous annual plant characterized by its smooth, hairless stems and leaves. The plant produces small, dark purple to black berries that are toxic to humans and animals. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in gardens and along roadsides.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and toxicology.
