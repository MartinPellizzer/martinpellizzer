Sarcocapnos crassifolia var. purpurascens

Sarcocapnos crassifolia var. purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-flowered sarcocapnos, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dark green, elliptical leaves with a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces showy, violet to purple flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms.
