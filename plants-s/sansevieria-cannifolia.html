Sansevieria cannifolia

Sansevieria cannifolia, commonly known as the sword leaf sansevieria, is a species of succulent plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its long, narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, white flowers on slender stalks, which bloom in the spring. It is highly drought-tolerant and thrives in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ability to purify the air and its low maintenance requirements further contribute to its popularity.
