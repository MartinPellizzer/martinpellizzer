Sphaeranthus ukambensis var. dauensis

Sphaeranthus ukambensis var. dauensis, commonly known as the Daueni grassleaf sunflower, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grasslands and open habitats, thriving in regions with moderate rainfall. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in clusters.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential ecological benefits in native plant communities.
