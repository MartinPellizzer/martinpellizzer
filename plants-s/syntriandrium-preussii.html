Syntriandrium preussii

Syntriandrium preussii, commonly known as the Preuss's syntriandrium, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial herbaceous plant characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces intricate floral structures with multiple whorls of petals and sepals, typical of the orchid family. It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a delicate and endangered species in its natural environment.
