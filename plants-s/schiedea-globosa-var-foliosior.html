Schiedea globosa var. foliosior

Schiedea globosa var. foliosior, commonly known as the Hawaiian leafy broom.

This variant is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family and is native to the Hawaiian Islands. It is characterized by its erect growth habit and dense clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments on rocky slopes or in coastal areas.

Its leaves are generally broader and more elongated compared to other Schiedea species, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
