Scabiosa atropurpurea var. mauritii

Scabiosa atropurpurea var. mauritii, commonly known as the purple scabious, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant features narrow, grey-green leaves that are lance-shaped and slightly hairy. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its nectar-rich flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
