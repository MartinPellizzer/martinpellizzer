Schollera limosa

Schollera limosa, commonly known as the limosa schollera, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, narrow leaves that are typically arranged in a spiral pattern along its stems. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers during the spring and summer months. Schollera limosa is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its compact form and attractive foliage.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.
