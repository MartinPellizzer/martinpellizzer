Sarcococca taiwaniana

Sarcococca taiwaniana, commonly known as Taiwan waxleaf, is a species of evergreen shrub native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage that remains throughout the year. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically blooming in late winter or early spring. Its compact growth habit and ornamental leaves make it a popular choice for garden use.

It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is relatively low maintenance once established.
