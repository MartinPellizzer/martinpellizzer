Schortia

Schortia, commonly known as the common vetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to purple. Schortia is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions. The plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.

It is widely distributed across Europe, Asia, and North Africa.
