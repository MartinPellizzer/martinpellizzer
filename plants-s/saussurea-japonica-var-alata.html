Saussurea japonica var. alata

Saussurea japonica var. alata, commonly known as the winged yarrow, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy, branched stems and basal rosette of narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow to orange flower heads that are surrounded by pale green bracts. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

Its botanical features make it an ornamental plant valued in horticulture for its unique appearance.
