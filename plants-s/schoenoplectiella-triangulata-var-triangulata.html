Schoenoplectiella triangulata var. triangulata

Schoenoplectiella triangulata var. triangulata, commonly known as the triangular bulrush, is a herbaceous plant native to wetland environments in North America.

It is characterized by its triangular stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish or brownish in color. It thrives in shallow water or moist soil, often found in marshes and along the edges of ponds.

Its structure and growth habits make it an important component of wetland ecosystems.
