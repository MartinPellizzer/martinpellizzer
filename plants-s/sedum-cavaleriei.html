Sedum cavaleriei

Sedum cavaleriei, commonly known as the rock sedum, is a succulent plant native to the rocky areas of southern Europe.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy choice for rock gardens.

Its compact growth habit and low maintenance requirements make it popular among gardeners seeking drought-tolerant plants.
