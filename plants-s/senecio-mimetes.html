Senecio mimetes

Senecio mimetes, commonly known as the mimic senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Senecio mimetes is notable for its ability to mimic the appearance of other plants, which may aid in its survival by deterring herbivores.
