Sansevieria livingstoniae

Sansevieria livingstoniae, commonly known as the "Livingstonia Snake Plant," is a striking member of the Asparagaceae family native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features tall, upright leaves that are dark green with prominent, light green transverse bands, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant is characterized by its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it highly adaptable. Its leaves are often described as resembling a snake's body, hence its common name.

This species is also noted for its air-purifying qualities, contributing to its popularity as an indoor plant.
