Soldanella × ganderi var. tubulosa

Soldanella × ganderi var. tubulosa, commonly known as the tubular soldanella, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Primulaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, tubular-shaped flowers that typically range in color from white to pale pink. The plant produces basal leaves that are ovate to elliptical in shape, often with a hairy texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland or rocky habitats.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is cultivated for its delicate floral display.
