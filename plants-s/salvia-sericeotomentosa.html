Salvia sericeotomentosa

Salvia sericeotomentosa, commonly known as silky hairy sage, is a perennial herb native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its gray-green, hairy leaves and spikes of violet to purple flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or arid environments. Its foliage is covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Salvia sericeotomentosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal practices.
