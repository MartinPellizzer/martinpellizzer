Sideritis edetana

Sideritis edetana, commonly known as the Edetan Heath, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open habitats such as hillsides and grasslands.

Sideritis edetana is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
