Scutellaria patonii

Scutellaria patonii, commonly known as Paton's skullcap, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered with fine hairs.

Scutellaria patonii is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats at high elevations.
