Stelis menippe

Stelis menippe, commonly known as the violet orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking violet or purple flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are elongated and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native humid environment.

The flowers bloom in clusters, adding to its ornamental value in horticultural settings.
