Sansevieria abyssinica var. sublaevigata

Sansevieria abyssinica var. sublaevigata, commonly known as the African Spear or Snake Plant, is a succulent plant native to Ethiopia.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are dark green with a subtle, wavy texture. The leaves are relatively narrow and have a slightly glossy surface, giving the plant a refined appearance. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in low-light conditions, making it an ideal houseplant.

The plant's unique foliage and easy care requirements have made it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
