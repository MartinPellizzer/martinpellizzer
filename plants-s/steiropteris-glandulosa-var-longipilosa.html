Steiropteris glandulosa var. longipilosa

Steiropteris glandulosa var. longipilosa, commonly known as the long-hair fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, compound fronds that can reach several meters in length. The fronds are covered with long, soft hairs, giving the plant its distinctive appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

The plant has a slow growth rate and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
