Styrax buchtienii

Styrax buchtienii, commonly known as the Chinese frankincense tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern China.

It belongs to the Styracaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, resinous bark and small, white or greenish flowers. The plant produces a valuable aromatic resin, which has been historically used in traditional medicine and incense. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a glossy appearance, while its fruits are capsule-like and contain several seeds.

Styrax buchtienii thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.
