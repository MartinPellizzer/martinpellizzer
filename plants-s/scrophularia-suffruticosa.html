Scrophularia suffruticosa

Scrophularia suffruticosa, commonly known as redstem dogbane, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the northeastern United States.

It typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring woody stems that become more prominent as the plant matures. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple, blooming from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a rough texture with serrated edges.

Scrophularia suffruticosa is often found in moist, open habitats such as woodlands and meadows.
