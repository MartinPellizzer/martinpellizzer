Symplocos itatiaiae

Symplocos itatiaiae, commonly known as the Itatiaia beautyberry, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It features opposite, elliptic leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by bright purple to blue berries that are edible for some bird species. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid tropical climates.

The berries are a key food source for various wildlife, contributing to its ecological importance in its native habitat.
