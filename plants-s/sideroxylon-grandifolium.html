Sideroxylon grandifolium

Sideroxylon grandifolium, commonly known as the large-leaf ironwood, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 20 meters tall, characterized by its dense, hard wood and thick, waxy leaves.
