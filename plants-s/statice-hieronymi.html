Statice hieronymi

Statice hieronymi, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers and silver-gray foliage. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens. Its hardy nature allows it to withstand salty air and coastal conditions.

Statice hieronymi is also valued for its use in dried floral arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms.
