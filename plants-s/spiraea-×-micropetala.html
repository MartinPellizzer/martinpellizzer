Spiraea × micropetala

Spiraea × micropetala, commonly known as the compact spiraea, is a deciduous shrub native to Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit and small, fragrant white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has narrow, dark green leaves that provide attractive foliage throughout the growing season. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil conditions.

Spiraea × micropetala is relatively low maintenance and resistant to pests and diseases.
