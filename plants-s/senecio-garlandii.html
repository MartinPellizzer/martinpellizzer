Senecio garlandii

Senecio garlandii, commonly known as the blue ice plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a succulent species characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves and clusters of small, star-shaped yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems and leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Senecio garlandii is known for its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements.
