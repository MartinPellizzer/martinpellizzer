Schizostege calocarpa

Schizostege calocarpa, commonly known as the red cedar, is a small evergreen tree native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, reddish-brown bark and small, scale-like leaves that are tightly clustered around the stems. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by fleshy, orange-red berries containing several seeds. Schizostege calocarpa is valued for its durable wood, which is used in traditional construction and crafting.
