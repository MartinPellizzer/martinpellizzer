Swertia duclouxii

Swertia duclouxii, commonly known as the Ducloux's gentian, is a perennial herb native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine, hairy texture, while its stems are usually unbranched and somewhat woody.

Swertia duclouxii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often found in botanical gardens specializing in alpine flora.
