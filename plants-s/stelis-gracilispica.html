Stelis gracilispica

Stelis gracilispica, commonly known as the slender-spiked stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, linear leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale green flowers arranged in a raceme, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Stelis gracilispica is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in terrariums or as a houseplant.
