Scutellaria holosericea

Scutellaria holosericea, commonly known as the silky skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its hairy, gray-green leaves and small, tubular blue or purple flowers. It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

Scutellaria holosericea is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
