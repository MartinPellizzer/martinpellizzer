Spiraea chinensis var. grandiflora

Spiraea chinensis var. grandiflora, commonly known as Chinese spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features showy, flat-topped clusters of white or pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has slender, arching branches and small, dark green leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil.

Spiraea chinensis var. grandiflora is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and hardiness.
