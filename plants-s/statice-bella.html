Statice bella

Statice bella, commonly known as sea lavender, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of Europe and the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its dense, spike-like clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from pink to violet. The plant thrives in salty, well-drained soils and is often found in dunes and coastal grasslands. Its foliage consists of narrow, gray-green leaves that are succulent and adapted to withstand harsh maritime conditions.

Statice bella is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in floral arrangements and garden landscaping.
