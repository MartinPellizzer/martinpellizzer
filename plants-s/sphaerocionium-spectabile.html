Sphaerocionium spectabile

Sphaerocionium spectabile, commonly known as the showy globe lily, is a striking flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy, globe-shaped inflorescence that consists of numerous small, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers come in a range of colors, including white, yellow, and orange, and bloom in late summer to early autumn.

Sphaerocionium spectabile is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
