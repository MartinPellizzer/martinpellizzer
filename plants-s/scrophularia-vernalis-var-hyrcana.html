Scrophularia vernalis var. hyrcana

Scrophularia vernalis var. hyrcana, commonly known as the spring scrophularia, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. The plant features upright stems covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a hairy surface. It produces clusters of tubular, two-lipped flowers that are usually pink or purple in color.

The species is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
