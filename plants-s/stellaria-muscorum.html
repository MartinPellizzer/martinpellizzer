Stellaria muscorum

Stellaria muscorum, commonly known as mouseear chickweed, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe, Asia, and North America.

It features small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from April to June. The plant has smooth, hairless stems and leaves that are heart-shaped with a distinctive notched base. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and garden beds.

Stellaria muscorum is often used in ornamental gardening due to its delicate appearance and ability to spread easily through runners.
