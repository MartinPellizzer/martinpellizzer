Syntherisma mariannensis

Syntherisma mariannensis, commonly known as the Marian thistle, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Pacific Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of numerous tiny florets. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters, with silver-gray foliage that reflects light. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in clusters at the ends of the stems.

Syntherisma mariannensis is adapted to coastal environments, thriving in sandy soils and saline conditions.
