Stigmatodactylus oxyglossus

Stigmatodactylus oxyglossus, commonly known as the narrow-leafed daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

Stigmatodactylus oxyglossus is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its delicate and attractive blooms.
