Senecio cissampelinus

Senecio cissampelinus, commonly known as the African Daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.

Senecio cissampelinus is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
