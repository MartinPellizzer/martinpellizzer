Senecio filaginoides var. filaginoides

Senecio filaginoides var. filaginoides, commonly known as the silverleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray, woolly leaves and small yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats. It is a perennial herb that spreads via rhizomes, forming dense mats.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
