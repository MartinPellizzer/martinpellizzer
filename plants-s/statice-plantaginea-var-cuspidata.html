Statice plantaginea var. cuspidata

Statice plantaginea var. cuspidata, commonly known as the spike statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense clusters of small, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to purple. The plant produces narrow, grass-like leaves and has a compact, bushy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden borders and coastal landscapes.

Its blooms are long-lasting, often persisting through the fall, and it is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
