Senna spinescens var. schultesiana

Senna spinescens var. schultesiana, commonly known as the Schultes' senna, is a shrub native to parts of South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its nitrogen-fixing ability and pod-like fruits. The plant features bipinnate leaves with small, rounded leaflets and produces yellow to orange flowers.

It is often found in dry, open habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal and medicinal properties.
