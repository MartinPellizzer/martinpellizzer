Sauropus orbicularis

Sauropus orbicularis, commonly known as the globe hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, round, waxy leaves that give it a unique appearance. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage and vibrant blooms.

Sauropus orbicularis prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
