Sedum aizoon var. yamatutae

Sedum aizoon var. yamatutae, commonly known as the Yamatotai stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Japan.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a powdery coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange, typically blooming in summer. It is well adapted to rocky and arid environments, making it a hardy and drought-tolerant species.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
