Scabiosa trenta

Scabiosa trenta, commonly known as the Trenta scabious, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caprifoliaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central and Southern Europe. The plant features upright stems that bear clusters of small, pink or purple flowers. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Scabiosa trenta is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
