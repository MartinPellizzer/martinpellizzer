Sedum valens

Sedum valens, commonly known as the "Purple Pearl" or "Ice Plant," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, clustered leaves that are often blue-green in color. The plant produces vibrant pink to purple flowers in late summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Sedum valens is drought-tolerant and requires minimal maintenance, ideal for low-water landscapes.
