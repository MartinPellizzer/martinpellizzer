Sinosenecio rotundifolius

Sinosenecio rotundifolius, commonly known as the round-leaf sinosenecio, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features basal rosettes of rounded, hairy leaves that are typically green with a bluish tinge. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in dense clusters, and it produces achene fruits.

It is often found in rocky or sandy soils in mountainous regions.
