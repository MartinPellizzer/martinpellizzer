Scrophularia lucida

Scrophularia lucida, commonly known as the bright scrophularia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, calcareous soils. The plant features upright stems with opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its showy, tubular flowers are typically pink or purple and bloom in late spring to early summer.

Scrophularia lucida is part of the Scrophulariaceae family, known for its medicinal and ornamental value.
