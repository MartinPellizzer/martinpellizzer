Sciodaphyllum umbellatum

Sciodaphyllum umbellatum, commonly known as the umbrella fern, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, arching fronds that resemble an umbrella, giving it its common name. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with a central stem that produces multiple branches. Its leaves are typically divided into segments, creating a feathery appearance.

Sciodaphyllum umbellatum thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.
