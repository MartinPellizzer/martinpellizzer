Stephanotis faulknerae

Stephanotis faulknerae, commonly known as the Falkner's jasmine, is a flowering plant native to the island of Socotra.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has compound leaves with narrow, elongated leaflets, and it produces small, green, fleshy fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing on rocky substrates.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
