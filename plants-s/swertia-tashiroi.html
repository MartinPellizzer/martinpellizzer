Swertia tashiroi

Swertia tashiroi, commonly known as the Japanese Swertia, is a flowering plant native to Japan and parts of East Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in the summer months. Swertia tashiroi is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides.
