Spiraea nervosa var. chinensis

Spiraea nervosa var. chinensis, commonly known as Chinese spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features slender, arching stems with small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in early summer. The leaves are elliptical and have a fine, hairy texture, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

This variety is valued for its compact growth and long-lasting floral display.
