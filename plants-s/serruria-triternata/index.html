Serruria Triternata

Serruria triternata is a striking member of the Geraniaceae family native to southern Africa. This small evergreen shrub is known for its vibrant blue and purple flowers that bloom throughout the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. The plant's unique foliage and long-lasting blooms make it a favorite among horticultural enthusiasts. Its resilience and beauty have earned it a special place in both natural and cultivated environments.
