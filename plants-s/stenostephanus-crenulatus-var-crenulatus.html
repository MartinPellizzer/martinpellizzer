Stenostephanus crenulatus var. crenulatus

Stenostephanus crenulatus var. crenulatus, commonly known as the coastal morning glory, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and produces vibrant blue or purple flowers that bloom in the early morning. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a slightly wavy or notched edge, giving the plant its species name "crenulatus.". It thrives in sandy or well-drained soils and is often found growing along beaches or in dune environments.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize coastal soils.
