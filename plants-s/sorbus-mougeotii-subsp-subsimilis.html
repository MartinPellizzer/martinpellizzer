Sorbus mougeotii subsp. subsimilis

Sorbus mougeotii subsp.

subsimilis, commonly known as the Chinese whitebeam, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China. It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its white to pale yellow flowers and persistent, red or orange berries.

The leaves are alternate, ovate to elliptic, and often have serrated edges. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or forested areas.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in its native habitat.
