Senecio quinqueradiatus

Senecio quinqueradiatus, commonly known as the blue chalk scale, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and features small, blue-green leaves covered in a powdery white coating. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in summer, adding visual interest to its appearance. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and rockeries.
