Stemodiopsis buchananii

Stemodiopsis buchananii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, hairy stems and dense, velvety foliage. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of violet, pink, and white. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Stemodiopsis buchananii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
