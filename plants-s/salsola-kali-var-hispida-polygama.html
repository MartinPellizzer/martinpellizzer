Salsola kali var. hispida-polygama

Salsola kali var. hispida-polygama, commonly known as Russian thistle or goatweed, is a flowering plant in the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and small, greenish flowers that appear in clusters. The plant is often found in arid and semi-arid regions, thriving in disturbed soils and open habitats. Its seeds are dispersed by wind, contributing to its invasive nature in many areas.

The species is adapted to survive in harsh environments with minimal water availability.
