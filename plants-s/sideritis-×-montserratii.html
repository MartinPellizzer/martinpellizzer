Sideritis × montserratii

Sideritis × montserratii, commonly known as the Corsican ironweed, is a hybrid plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, gray-green leaves. It produces clusters of purple or pink flowers that bloom in summer.

Sideritis × montserratii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and its historical use in traditional medicine.
