Stelis scutellifera

Stelis scutellifera, commonly known as the helmet orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, showy flowers with a distinctive helmet-shaped lip. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a prominent, spurred labellum.

Stelis scutellifera is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
