Stellaria laevis var. typica

Stellaria laevis var. typica, commonly known as white starwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, white or pale pink flowers that are typically star-shaped, giving it its common name. The plant has smooth, hairless stems and leaves that are usually oval or lance-shaped. It is native to temperate regions of Europe and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides.

Stellaria laevis var. typica is an annual or biennial herb that blooms from late spring to early summer.
