Sansevieria cylindrica

Sansevieria cylindrica, commonly known as the cylindrical snake plant, is a species of flowering plant in the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its tall, narrow, dark green leaves that are often curved or twisted. The plant produces small, white flowers on slender spikes during the growing season. It is highly drought-tolerant and thrives in low-light conditions, making it a popular indoor plant.

Its unique appearance and ease of care have contributed to its widespread cultivation.
