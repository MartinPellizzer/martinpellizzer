Satyrium pulchrum

Satyrium pulchrum, commonly known as the beautiful satyrium, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its delicate, grass-like leaves and a single, showy flower. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as woodlands and stream banks. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insects.

The species is native to parts of Asia and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its habitat.
