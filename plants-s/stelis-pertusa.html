Stelis pertusa

Stelis pertusa, commonly known as the violet orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or branches, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are narrow and arranged in a rosette pattern, often covered with a dense layer of fine hairs.

The flowers bloom in late spring or early summer, adding ornamental value to its natural habitat.
