Senecio melanandrus

Senecio melanandrus, commonly known as the blackfoot daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, with a dark green color.

Senecio melanandrus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
