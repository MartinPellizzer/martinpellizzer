Staphylea × elegans

Staphylea × elegans, commonly known as the European hornbeam, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Europe.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are small, white, and clustered in racemes. The plant produces small, leathery fruits that are typically green when mature. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or scrub habitats.

Staphylea × elegans is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native gardens.
