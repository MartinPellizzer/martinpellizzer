Senecio lithostaurus

Senecio lithostaurus, commonly known as the stonecrop senecio, is a member of the Asteraceae family and is native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent plant characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are usually thick and covered in a waxy cuticle to reduce water loss.

Senecio lithostaurus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
