Scrophularia exserta

Scrophularia exserta, commonly known as the slender scrophularia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a member of the Scrophulariaceae family, which includes many species with showy, often tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with two lips that are characteristic of the family.

It is an important plant for pollinators, particularly bees and butterflies.
