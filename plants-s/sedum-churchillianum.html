Sedum churchillianum

Sedum churchillianum, commonly known as the Churchillian stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant is adapted to rocky and arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves. It blooms in late summer, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat.

Sedum churchillianum is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and ornamental value.
