Schlumbergera bridgesii

Schlumbergera bridgesii, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a popular epiphytic cactus native to the tropical rainforests of Brazil.

It is characterized by its segmented, flat, leaf-like stems that are often brightly colored and arranged in a zigzag pattern. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, pink, orange, and white, typically during the holiday season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Schlumbergera bridgesii is known for its ability to bloom reliably when provided with the right care and environmental conditions.
