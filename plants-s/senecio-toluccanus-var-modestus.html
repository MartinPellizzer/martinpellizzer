Senecio toluccanus var. modestus

Senecio toluccanus var. modestus, commonly known as the Toluca senecio, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
