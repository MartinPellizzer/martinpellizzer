Skimmia japonica f. variegata

Skimmia japonica f. variegata, commonly known as the Japanese skimmia variegated form, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It features dark green, glossy leaves with striking white or yellow variegation, adding visual interest to gardens. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in spring, followed by red berries in late summer. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens or as a container plant.

Its ornamental value lies in its foliage and seasonal blooms, making it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
