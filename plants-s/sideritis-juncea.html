Sideritis juncea

Sideritis juncea, commonly known as narrow-leaved ironwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, narrow leaves. Its flowers are typically pale blue or violet, arranged in whorls along the stem.

Sideritis juncea is often found in rocky or limestone soils and is valued for its medicinal properties.
