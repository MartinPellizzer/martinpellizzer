Solidago rubricaulis

Solidago rubricaulis, commonly known as the redstem goldenrod, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes and swamps. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often have a reddish tint along the veins.

Solidago rubricaulis plays an important role in supporting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
