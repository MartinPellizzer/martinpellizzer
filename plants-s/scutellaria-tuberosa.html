Scutellaria tuberosa

Scutellaria tuberosa, commonly known as purple skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It features square, hairy stems and opposite leaves that are often lance-shaped. The plant produces showy, two-lipped blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. Its tuberous roots are a key feature, contributing to its name and traditional medicinal uses.

Scutellaria tuberosa is often found in moist woodlands and along stream banks.
