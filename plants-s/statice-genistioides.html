Statice genistioides

Statice genistioides, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking, spiky flowers that range in color from blue to purple, often with a yellow center. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and is typically found in coastal areas and sandy soils. It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and is known for its salt tolerance.

Statice genistioides is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and drought-resistant properties.
