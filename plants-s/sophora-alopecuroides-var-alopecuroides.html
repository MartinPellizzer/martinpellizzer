Sophora alopecuroides var. alopecuroides

Sophora alopecuroides var. alopecuroides, commonly known as Chinese wisteria, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive yellow flowers. The plant is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen through its root nodules, making it a valuable species in soil enrichment. The plant's botanical features make it both ornamental and ecologically significant.
