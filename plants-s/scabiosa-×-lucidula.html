Scabiosa × lucidula

Scabiosa × lucidula, commonly known as the clearleaf scabious, is a hybrid perennial plant native to Europe.

It features showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Scabiosa × lucidula is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as butterflies and bees.
