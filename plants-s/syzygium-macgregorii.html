Syzygium macgregorii

Syzygium macgregorii, commonly known as the MacGregor's apple.

It is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae, native to northern Australia. The plant is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub, typically reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are elliptical, glossy, and dark green, with a strong aromatic scent.
