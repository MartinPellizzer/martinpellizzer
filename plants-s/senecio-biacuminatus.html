Senecio biacuminatus

Senecio biacuminatus, commonly known as the African Sunset.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, characterized by its vibrant orange and yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its succulent leaves and compact growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its striking appearance and drought tolerance make it a unique addition to both natural and cultivated landscapes.
