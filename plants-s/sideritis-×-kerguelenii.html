Sideritis × kerguelenii

Sideritis × kerguelenii, commonly known as Kerguelen's sainfoin, is a hybrid shrub native to the southern hemisphere.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. The plant is characterized by its slender stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that are typically pale blue or purple in color. It thrives in rocky, coastal environments and is adapted to harsh climatic conditions.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
