Statice remotispicula

Statice remotispicula, commonly known as the slender glassweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in sandy or coastal soils and thrives in saline environments. Its small, blue to purple flowers bloom in summer, adding a touch of color to coastal landscapes.
