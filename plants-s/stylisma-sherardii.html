Stylisma sherardii

Stylisma sherardii, commonly known as the African buttercup, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a fibrous root system and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland and rocky habitats.

Stylisma sherardii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
