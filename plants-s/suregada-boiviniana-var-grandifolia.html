Suregada boiviniana var. grandifolia

Suregada boiviniana var. grandifolia, commonly known as the large-flowered suregada, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are usually a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, often with a red or orange tint at the base. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
