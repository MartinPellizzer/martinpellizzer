Spiraea aitchisonii

Spiraea aitchisonii, commonly known as the Aitchison's spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to the Himalayas.

It features upright, slender stems that bear clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers in early summer. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Spiraea aitchisonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in garden landscaping.
