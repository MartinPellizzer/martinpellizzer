Stenostegia congesta

Stenostegia congesta, commonly known as the clustered stenostegia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in grassland and rocky habitats, often forming dense, mat-like colonies. The plant features small, clustered inflorescences with numerous white to pale blue flowers. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered with fine hairs.

Stenostegia congesta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
