Stilaginella blanchetiana

Stilaginella blanchetiana, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, delicate flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. The plant thrives in rocky or coastal environments, often growing on cliffs or among stones. Its flowers are pollinated by insects, and it is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.

Stilaginella blanchetiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
