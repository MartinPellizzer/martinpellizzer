Scaphopetalum thonneri var. thonneri

Scaphopetalum thonneri var. thonneri, commonly known as the Cape snowflake, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant features woody stems and linear, gray-green leaves that are aromatic when crushed. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate appearance and ability to attract pollinators.
