Swertia tashiroi f. cruciata

Swertia tashiroi f. cruciata, commonly known as the cruciform swertia, is a rare and unique variant of the Swertia genus.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which are typically arranged in clusters. The plant is native to specific regions in East Asia, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are lanceolate and often have a slightly hairy texture.
