Schkuhria pinnata var. guatemalensis

Schkuhria pinnata var. guatemalensis, commonly known as the Guatemalan featherleaf, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its finely divided, feathery leaves that give it a delicate appearance. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is native to Central America and thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
