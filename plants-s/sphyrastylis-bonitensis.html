Sphyrastylis bonitensis

Sphyrastylis bonitensis, commonly known as the bonnet orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, showy inflorescence that resembles a bonnet or hat. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte or lithophyte, thriving on trees or rocks in humid environments. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, with a unique structure that includes a spurred lip.

Sphyrastylis bonitensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators.
