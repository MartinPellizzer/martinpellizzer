Senegalia hohenackeri

Senegalia hohenackeri, commonly known as the Hohenacker acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is well adapted to drought conditions, with a deep root system that allows it to access water from deeper soil layers.

It plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soils and providing forage for livestock in its native habitat.
