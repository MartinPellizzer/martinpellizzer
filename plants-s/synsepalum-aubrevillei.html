Synsepalum aubrevillei

Synsepalum aubrevillei, commonly known as the African eggplant or bignonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its woody stems and opposite leaves that are often ovate or lanceolate in shape. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that are clustered in racemose inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, smooth seeds.

Synsepalum aubrevillei is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
