Satyrium stolzianum

Satyrium stolzianum, commonly known as the Stolzian orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and green. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with each stem producing a new growth from the base. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

The orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
