Statice oxylepis

Statice oxylepis, commonly known as sea lavender, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant thrives in saline and well-drained soils, often growing in dunes and coastal sands. Its hardy nature makes it a popular choice for coastal gardens and landscaping.

Statice oxylepis is also valued for its ornamental use in floral arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms.
