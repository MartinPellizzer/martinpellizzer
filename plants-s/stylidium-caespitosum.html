Stylidium caespitosum

Stylidium caespitosum, commonly known as the tufted sun-orchid, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its compact, dense growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are often clustered together. It thrives in well-drained soils and is typically found in open habitats such as grasslands and heathlands.

Stylidium caespitosum is known for its ability to attract pollinators with its specialized floral structures.
