Selinum Galbanum

Selinum galbanum is a hardy perennial plant known for its distinctive yellow-green foliage and strong, sulfur-like odor. It thrives in dry, sunny environments and is commonly found in rocky soils across various regions. This plant has a long history of use in traditional medicine and is valued for its unique chemical properties. Its resilience and adaptability make it a notable species in ecological studies. Despite its pungent scent, it plays an important role in supporting local insect populations.
