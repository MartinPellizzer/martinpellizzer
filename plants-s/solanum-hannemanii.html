Solanum hannemanii

Solanum hannemanii, commonly known as the Hanneman's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple, often blooming in the evening. Its fruits are berries that are usually yellow or orange when mature.

Solanum hannemanii is part of the nightshade family, which includes many economically important plants.
