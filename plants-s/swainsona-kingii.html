Swainsona kingii

Swainsona kingii, commonly known as the King's swainsona, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces striking yellow flowers that are clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences. Its leaves are bipinnate, meaning they are divided into small, feather-like segments.

Swainsona kingii is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant blooms and unique foliage structure.
