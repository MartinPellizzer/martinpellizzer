Spennera uliginosa

It is a member of the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in dense clusters, with opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in peat bogs and marshes.
