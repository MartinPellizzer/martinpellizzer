Siphonochilus decorus

Siphonochilus decorus, commonly known as the elegant siphonochilus, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, ribbon-like leaves that float on the water surface, often forming dense mats. The plant thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and streams. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically appearing in clusters near the water's edge.

Siphonochilus decorus is valued in aquascaping for its aesthetic appeal and ability to provide habitat for aquatic life.
