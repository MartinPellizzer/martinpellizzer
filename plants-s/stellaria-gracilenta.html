Stellaria gracilenta

Stellaria gracilenta, commonly known as slender starwort, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

It typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters and features delicate, star-shaped white or pale blue flowers. The plant has slender, branching stems covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are small, ovate, and often hairy as well. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and disturbed areas.

Stellaria gracilenta is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
