Sherbournia bignoniiflora var. brazzaei

Sherbournia bignoniiflora var. brazzaei, commonly known as the Brazza's bignonia, is a striking climbing plant native to the tropical regions of Central Africa.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom in clusters during the rainy season. Its rapid growth and ornamental value make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm climates.

However, due to its invasive potential, it requires careful management to prevent ecological disruption.
