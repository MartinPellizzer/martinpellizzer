Schoenorchis subulata

Schoenorchis subulata, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features slender, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, greenish-yellow flowers with a subtle fragrance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or mountainous regions. Its root system is fibrous, allowing it to absorb nutrients from the soil efficiently.

It is valued in horticulture for its elegant appearance and ease of cultivation.
