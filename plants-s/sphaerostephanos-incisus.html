Sphaerostephanos incisus

Sphaerostephanos incisus, commonly known as the narrow-leafed star-twig, is a unique plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub characterized by its small, star-shaped leaves and clusters of white flowers. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils. Its botanical features include hairy stems and aromatic leaves, which contribute to its distinct appearance.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
