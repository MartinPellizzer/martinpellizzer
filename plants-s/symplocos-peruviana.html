Symplocos peruviana

Symplocos peruviana, commonly known as the Peruvian beautyberry, is a deciduous shrub native to the Andean regions of South America.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in mountainous areas with well-drained soil. Symplocos peruviana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in gardens for its unique fruit and foliage.
