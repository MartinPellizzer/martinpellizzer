Solanum latiflorum

Solanum latiflorum, commonly known as the broadflower nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, with a rough texture.

Solanum latiflorum is part of the nightshade family (Solanaceae), which includes other familiar plants like tomatoes and potatoes.
