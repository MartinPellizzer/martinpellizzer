Scutachne dura

Scutachne dura, commonly known as the rigid scutachne, is a grass species native to certain regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its stiff, upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of spikelets, which are often tightly packed.

Scutachne dura is adapted to arid conditions, making it a resilient plant in dry environments.
