Solanum morellifolium

Solanum morellifolium, commonly known as the Morellifolium nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and peppers. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically purple or white in color. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, with a rough texture.

Solanum morellifolium is often grown for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and unique flower structure.
