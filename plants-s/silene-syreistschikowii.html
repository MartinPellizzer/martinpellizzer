Silene syreistschikowii

Silene syreistschikowii, commonly known as the Khabarovsk catchfly, is a flowering plant native to eastern Siberia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pinkish-red flowers that bloom in early summer.
