Stelis rubens

Stelis rubens, commonly known as the red-stemmed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant red stems. The plant typically produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Stelis rubens is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
