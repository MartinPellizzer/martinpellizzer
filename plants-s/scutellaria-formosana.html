Scutellaria formosana

Scutellaria formosana, commonly known as the Formosan skullcap, is a herbaceous perennial native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features square stems and opposite leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are typically blue or purple and are arranged in whorls along the stem.

Scutellaria formosana is noted for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
