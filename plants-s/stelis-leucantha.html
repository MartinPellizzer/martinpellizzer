Stelis leucantha

Stelis leucantha, commonly known as the white-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments within forest understories. The plant features slender, upright stems that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. Its flowers are small and white, arranged in a loose inflorescence, and bloom during the warmer months.

Stelis leucantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
