Sesbania macrantha var. levis

Sesbania macrantha var. levis, commonly known as the broad-leaved sesbania, is a tropical leguminous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in full sun to partial shade. Its pods contain edible seeds that are used in some traditional cuisines.

Additionally, it plays a role in soil enrichment due to its nitrogen-fixing capabilities.
