Swainsona oroboides subsp. sericea

Swainsona oroboides subsp.

sericea, commonly known as silky swainsona, is a perennial legume native to parts of southern Africa. It is characterized by its slender, erect stems covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in dense, racemose inflorescences. Its trifoliate leaves are typically hairy and have a soft texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.
