Sorindeia zenkeri

Sorindeia zenkeri, commonly known as the African snowball, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 meters. The plant produces clusters of white, fluffy flowers that resemble snowballs, blooming during the rainy season. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a velvety texture.

Sorindeia zenkeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
