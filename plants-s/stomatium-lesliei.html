Stomatium lesliei

Stomatium lesliei, commonly known as the Leslii's bladderwort, is a carnivorous plant native to tropical regions.

It possesses specialized structures called bladders that trap small aquatic organisms. The plant's leaves are modified into bladder-like organs, which are used to capture prey. It thrives in nutrient-poor water environments, supplementing its diet with captured prey.

Its unique botanical features make it an intriguing subject for ecological and botanical studies.
