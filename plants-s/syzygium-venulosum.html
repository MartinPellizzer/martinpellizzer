Syzygium venulosum

Syzygium venulosum, commonly known as the glossy apple or satin leaf, is a tropical evergreen tree native to northern Australia.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark green, glossy leaves that provide a striking visual contrast to its white, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, round, edible fruits that are typically red or yellow when ripe. Syzygium venulosum thrives in humid climates and is often found in coastal and lowland rainforest areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation in warm regions.
