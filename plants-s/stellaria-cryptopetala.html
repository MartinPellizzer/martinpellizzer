Stellaria cryptopetala

Stellaria cryptopetala, commonly known as the cryptopetala starwort, is a flowering plant species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a low-growing annual herb with small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has slender, branched stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its fruit is a small, dry capsule that contains several tiny seeds.

Stellaria cryptopetala is adapted to harsh alpine environments and is often found in rocky soils at high elevations.
