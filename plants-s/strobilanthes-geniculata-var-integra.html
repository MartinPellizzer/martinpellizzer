Strobilanthes geniculata var. integra

Strobilanthes geniculata var. integra, commonly known as the mountain morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the rainy season. The plant is a herbaceous perennial with slender stems and heart-shaped leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value has made it a popular choice for horticulture in tropical regions.
