Sideritis foetens var. rivasgodayi

Sideritis foetens var. rivasgodayi, commonly known as the Rivasgodayi ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in rocky or calcareous soils and is often found in open habitats such as scrublands and woodland edges.
