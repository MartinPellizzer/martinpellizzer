Sapium oblongifolium

Sapium oblongifolium, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, elongated, heart-shaped leaves that are often red or purple in color. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by bright orange or red, lantern-shaped fruits. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Sapium oblongifolium is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
