Sulcorebutia albaoides

Sulcorebutia albaoides, commonly known as the white-flowered crassula, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that are typically green with a bluish tinge, and it produces clusters of small, star-shaped white flowers during the summer months. The plant is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it an ideal choice for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular ornamental plant among succulent enthusiasts.
