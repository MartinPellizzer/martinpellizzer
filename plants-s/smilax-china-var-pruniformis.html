Smilax china var. pruniformis

Smilax china var. pruniformis, commonly known as Chinese smilax, is a climbing vine native to eastern Asia.

It features woody stems with sharp spines and large, heart-shaped leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by red or orange berries. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.

Its ornamental value and medicinal uses have made it a subject of both horticultural and botanical interest.
