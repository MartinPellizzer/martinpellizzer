Schizangium durum

Schizangium durum, commonly known as the hardstem bladderwort, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, white to pinkish flowers. The plant is an aquatic or semi-aquatic species, often found in wetlands and along the edges of ponds. Its leaves are typically narrow and can be submerged or floating.
