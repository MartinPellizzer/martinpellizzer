Solenotus resupinatus

Solenotus resupinatus, commonly known as the prostrate solenotus, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and thickets. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. Its small, white or pale pink flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences.

Solenotus resupinatus is often found in low-lying areas and is adapted to thrive in humid, nutrient-rich soils.
