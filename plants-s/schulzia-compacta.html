Schulzia compacta

Schulzia compacta, commonly known as the dwarf ironwood, is a small evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and thick, leathery leaves that are dark green in color. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in umbels, adding a subtle floral display. Schulzia compacta is well adapted to arid environments, exhibiting drought tolerance and resistance to pests.

Its hardy nature makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
