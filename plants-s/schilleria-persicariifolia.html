Schilleria persicariifolia

Schilleria persicariifolia, commonly known as the pink root or pink orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking pink or purple flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling orchids in appearance. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette.

Schilleria persicariifolia is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
