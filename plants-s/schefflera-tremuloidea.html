Schefflera tremuloidea

Schefflera tremuloidea, commonly known as the trembling schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound, pinnately arranged leaves that give it a feathery appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by bright red berries. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil.

Schefflera tremuloidea is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor conditions.
