Solanum asiae-mediae

Solanum asiae-mediae, commonly known as the Asian media tomato, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its trailing growth habit and hairy stems. The leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a serrated margin. Its flowers are typically white or pale purple, with five petals and a prominent yellow stamen.

The fruit is a small, round berry that turns red when mature, containing numerous seeds.
