Sideritis violascens

Sideritis violascens, commonly known as violet scented germander, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, violet-blue flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, often with a faint minty scent.

Sideritis violascens is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional use in herbal medicine.
