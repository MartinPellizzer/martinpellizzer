Styrax leveillei

Styrax leveillei, commonly known as the Vietnamese styrax, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Styracaceae and is characterized by its simple, opposite, and elliptical leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers in the spring, which are followed by dark blue, waxy berries. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its aromatic properties.

The species is also noted for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its tolerance to partial shade.
