Spermaxyrum phyllanthi

Spermaxyrum phyllanthi, commonly known as the golden leaf vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests. Its leaves are used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The plant's rapid growth and ornamental value make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
