Sisymbrium pulchellum var. grandiflorum

Sisymbrium pulchellum var. grandiflorum, commonly known as the showy shepherd's purse, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its distinctive triangular seed pods and clusters of small, white or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, disturbed soils and thrives in full sun. It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other cruciferous plants.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and wildflower gardens.
