Sulcorebutia canigueralii var. applanata

Sulcorebutia canigueralii var. applanata, commonly known as the flat-topped crassula, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a dense rosette of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically pale green with a powdery blue coating. The plant produces tall, branched stems that bear clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container cultivation.
