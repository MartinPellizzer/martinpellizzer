Sibara deserti

Sibara deserti, commonly known as the desert brome, is a grass species native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets. Sibara deserti is adapted to survive in harsh desert conditions with minimal water.

Its growth patterns make it an important component of desert ecosystems.
