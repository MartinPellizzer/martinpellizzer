Statice insignis

Statice insignis, commonly known as sea lavender, is a flowering plant native to coastal areas of the Mediterranean and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and spikes of small, showy blue or purple flowers. The plant thrives in saline and well-drained soils, making it well adapted to coastal environments. It is often used in ornamental gardening for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.

Statice insignis is also valued in floral arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms and vibrant color.
