Stipa sodiroana

Stipa sodiroana, commonly known as the slender needlegrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. Its inflorescence consists of a single, narrow spikelet that is often slightly drooping.

Stipa sodiroana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its delicate appearance.
