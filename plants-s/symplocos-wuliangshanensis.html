Symplocos wuliangshanensis

Symplocos wuliangshanensis, commonly known as the Wuliangshan stoneberry, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces clusters of white flowers followed by small, red berries that are edible. It thrives in acidic soils and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
