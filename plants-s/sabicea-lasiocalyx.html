Sabicea lasiocalyx

Sabicea lasiocalyx, commonly known as the rough-leaved sabicea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are often hairy and have a rough texture. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually in racemes, with white or pale yellow petals.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its foliage and floral display.
