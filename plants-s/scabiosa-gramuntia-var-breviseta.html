Scabiosa gramuntia var. breviseta

Scabiosa gramuntia var. breviseta, commonly known as the short-hairy scabious, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It features erect, sparsely branched stems that are covered in short, stiff hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers with five petals, typical of the Caprifoliaceae family. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine, short hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and borders.
