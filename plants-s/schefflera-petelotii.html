Schefflera petelotii

Schefflera petelotii, commonly known as the Petelot's umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like crown of large, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Schefflera petelotii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to indoor environments.
