Solanum koniamboense

Solanum koniamboense, commonly known as the Koniambo nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, typically white or pale green flowers, which are followed by berries that mature to a yellow or orange color. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in forested areas with well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
