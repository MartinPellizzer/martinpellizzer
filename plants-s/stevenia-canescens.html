Stevenia canescens

Stevenia canescens, commonly known as the woolly stevia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its hairy, grayish-green foliage. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in dense, spherical heads. It is often cultivated for its aromatic leaves, which contain compounds used in herbal remedies.

Stevenia canescens thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking drought-resistant plants.
