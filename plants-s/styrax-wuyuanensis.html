Styrax wuyuanensis

Styrax wuyuanensis, commonly known as the Wuyuan styrax, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Styracaceae and is characterized by its fragrant, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces a sticky resin, which has been historically used in traditional medicine and perfumery. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a glossy surface.

Styrax wuyuanensis is typically found in moist, well-drained soils in mountainous regions.
