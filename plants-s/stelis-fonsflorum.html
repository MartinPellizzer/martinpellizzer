Stelis fonsflorum

Stelis fonsflorum, commonly known as the Fountain Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air and surrounding environment.

The flowers bloom during the rainy season, adding a delicate beauty to the forest canopy.
