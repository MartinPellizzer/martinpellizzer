Senega Incarnata

Senega incarnata is a rare and striking member of the nightshade family, known for its vibrant red flowers and unique leaf structure. Native to the southeastern united states, it thrives in moist, wooded areas and is often found in shaded understories. this plant has a long history of use in traditional medicine, valued for its purported healing properties. its delicate appearance contrasts with its hardy nature, making it both beautiful and resilient. due to habitat loss, senega incarnata is now considered endangered, highlighting the need for conservation efforts.
