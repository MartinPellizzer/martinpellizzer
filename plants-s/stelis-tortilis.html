Stelis tortilis

Stelis tortilis, commonly known as the twisted stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often twisted floral structures. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its flowers are usually small and fragrant, displaying a range of colors from white to pale yellow.

Stelis tortilis is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.
