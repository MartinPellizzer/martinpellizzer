Senecio moorei

Senecio moorei, commonly known as the Moore's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant features slender, branched stems and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are usually linear and covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Senecio moorei is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
