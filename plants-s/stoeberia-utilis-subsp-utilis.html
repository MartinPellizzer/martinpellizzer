Stoeberia utilis subsp. utilis

Stoeberia utilis subsp.

utilis, commonly known as the Cape daisies, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. It has a compact, bushy growth habit with narrow, silvery-gray leaves.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
