Sideritis spinulosa var. ilicifolia

Sideritis spinulosa var. ilicifolia, commonly known as the spiny sainfoin, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, narrow leaves with a leathery texture and a distinctive spiny appearance. The plant produces clusters of pale blue to violet flowers, which bloom in early summer. It thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats.

Sideritis spinulosa var. ilicifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional use in herbal medicine.
