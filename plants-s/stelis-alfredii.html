Stelis alfredii

Stelis alfredii, commonly known as the Alfred's stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is an epiphytic orchid, meaning it grows on the surface of other plants without being parasitic. This species typically thrives in tropical and subtropical regions, often found in shaded, humid environments. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors including white, yellow, and green.

Stelis alfredii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
