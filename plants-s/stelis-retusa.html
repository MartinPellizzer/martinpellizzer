Stelis retusa

Stelis retusa, commonly known as the slender stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant has a short, erect stem with dense clusters of small, white to pale green flowers. Its leaves are linear and arranged in two rows along the stem.

Stelis retusa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
