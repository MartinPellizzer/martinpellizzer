Socratea durissima

Socratea durissima, commonly known as the giant socratea, is a towering tree native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Areaceae and is characterized by its massive, buttressed roots and high growth rate. Its leaves are large, lanceolate, and often have a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

The tree produces small, white flowers followed by edible, round fruits that are an important food source for various wildlife species.
