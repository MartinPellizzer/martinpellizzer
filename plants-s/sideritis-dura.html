Sideritis dura

Sideritis dura, commonly known as hard ironwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its narrow, gray-green leaves and small, pale blue or purple flowers. It thrives in rocky or dry soils and is often found in mountainous areas.

Sideritis dura is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
