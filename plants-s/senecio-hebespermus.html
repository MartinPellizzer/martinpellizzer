Senecio hebespermus

Senecio hebespermus, commonly known as the African ironweed, is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

It is native to southern Africa and typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features narrow, gray-green leaves and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters. It is often used in ornamental gardening due to its hardy nature and attractive appearance.

Senecio hebespermus is also noted for its ability to thrive in arid conditions.
