Scytalia verticillata

Scytalia verticillata, commonly known as the spiral rush, is a flowering plant native to the wetlands of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive whorled arrangement of leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense spikes. It thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils and is often found in marshy areas.

Scytalia verticillata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in wetland ecosystems.
