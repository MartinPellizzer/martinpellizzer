Stenotus linearifolius var. interior

Stenotus linearifolius var. interior, commonly known as the interior linearleaf stenotus, is a perennial herb native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers clustered in dense, terminal inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and wetland conservation efforts.
