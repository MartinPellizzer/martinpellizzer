Syrmatium cytisoides

Syrmatium cytisoides, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its showy inflorescences. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Syrmatium cytisoides is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
