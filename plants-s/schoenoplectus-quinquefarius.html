Schoenoplectus quinquefarius

Schoenoplectus quinquefarius, commonly known as the narrowleaf bulrush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland environments in North America.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems that often reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that are typically greenish or brownish in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a distinctive sheathing base.

Schoenoplectus quinquefarius plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wetland species.
