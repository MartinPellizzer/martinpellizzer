Sparganium emersum f. submersum

Sparganium emersum f. submersum, commonly known as the submerged bur reed, is a rare aquatic plant characterized by its submersed growth habit.

It is a variant of the common bur reed, adapted to thrive in shallow, still waters such as ponds and marshes. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically submerged, with the inflorescence often floating at the water's surface. Its leaves are narrow and ribbon-like, forming dense underwater vegetation.
