Streptocarpus rungwensis var. latifolius

Streptocarpus rungwensis var. latifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf streptocarpus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of violet or purple, which bloom during the spring and summer months. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a suitable houseplant for indoor cultivation.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
