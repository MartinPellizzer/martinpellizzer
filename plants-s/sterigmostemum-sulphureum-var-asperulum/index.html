Sterigmostemum Sulphureum Var. Asperulum

Golden trumpet, scientifically known as Sterigmostemum sulphureum var. asperulum, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to tropical regions. Its most notable feature is its vibrant, golden-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dazzling display. This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its bold and showy appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish. Despite its beauty, it is important to note that some varieties may have mild toxicity, making it essential to handle with care.
