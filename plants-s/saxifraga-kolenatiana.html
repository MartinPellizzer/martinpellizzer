Saxifraga kolenatiana

Saxifraga kolenatiana, commonly known as the rock saxifrage, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It thrives in rocky and alpine environments, often growing in crevices and on slopes where soil is sparse. The plant has dark green, fleshy leaves that are adapted to conserve moisture in harsh conditions. Its small, white or pinkish flowers bloom in late summer, adding a delicate touch to its rugged habitat.

Saxifraga kolenatiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in rocky terrains.
