Scaphyglottis brachiata

Scaphyglottis brachiata, commonly known as the branched tongue orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, while its roots are fibrous and adapted for nutrient absorption.
