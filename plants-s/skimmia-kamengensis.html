Skimmia kamengensis

Skimmia kamengensis, commonly known as the Assam skimmia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to the eastern Himalayas.

It belongs to the family Rutaceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces red, fleshy berries that are an important food source for various bird species.

Skimmia kamengensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
