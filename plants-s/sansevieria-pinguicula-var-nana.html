Sansevieria pinguicula var. nana

Sansevieria pinguicula var. nana, commonly known as the Baby Chain Sansevieria, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to southern Africa.

It features rosettes of narrow, dark green leaves that are slightly undulating and often have a waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale green flowers on long, slender stems, which are typically followed by red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires minimal watering, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a popular choice for both novice and experienced plant enthusiasts.
