Senecio diclinus

Senecio diclinus, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and thistles. The plant features showy yellow flowers with white ray florets, arranged in a composite head. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Senecio diclinus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
