Sansevieria bracteata

Sansevieria bracteata, commonly known as the "Iron Sword" or "Mother of Thousands," is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, upright leaves that are typically dark green with white or yellowish margins, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is known for its adaptability to various environmental conditions.

Sansevieria bracteata is also noted for its air-purifying qualities and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
