Santiria mollissima

Santiria mollissima, commonly known as velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and agricultural fields.

Santiria mollissima has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
