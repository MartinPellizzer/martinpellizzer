Schaefferia obovata

Schaefferia obovata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf Schaefferia, is a perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its strap-like, obovate leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant produces clusters of white or pale pink flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Schaefferia obovata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
