Stellaria anomala

Stellaria anomala, commonly known as the strange starwort, is a small annual herb belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its star-shaped flowers, which typically range in color from white to pink, and its delicate, branching stems. The plant has oval, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is commonly found in meadows, along roadsides, and in woodland edges.

Stellaria anomala is known for its rapid growth and ability to colonize open areas, making it a common weed in various habitats.
