Senna rigidicaulis

Senna rigidicaulis, commonly known as the rigid senna, is a perennial shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It features woody stems that are often covered with a thick, yellowish or brownish bark. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are typically clustered in racemes, which bloom during the dry season. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with numerous small leaflets that are usually opposite in arrangement.

Senna rigidicaulis is adapted to arid environments and is often found in savannas and rocky soils.
