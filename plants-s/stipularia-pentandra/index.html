Stipularia Pentandra

Stipularia pentandra is a rare and delicate flowering plant known for its unique morphological features and ecological significance. Native to specific regions with particular environmental conditions, it thrives in moist, shaded habitats. Its scientific name reflects its distinctive structure, with "pentandra" hinting at its five-parted floral anatomy.
