Solanum nesiotes

Solanum nesiotes, commonly known as the Pacific island nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and disturbed soils. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom at night, attracting nocturnal pollinators. Its berries are toxic to humans but serve as a food source for certain wildlife.

Solanum nesiotes is also known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
