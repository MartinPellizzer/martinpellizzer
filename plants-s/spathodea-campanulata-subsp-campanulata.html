Spathodea campanulata subsp. campanulata

Spathodea campanulata subsp.

campanulata, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to yellow and orange.

The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
