Sciodaphyllum awa

Sciodaphyllum awa, commonly known as the shade-forest tree, is a species native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, straight trunk and broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant thrives in low-light conditions, making it well-suited for understory environments in dense forests.
