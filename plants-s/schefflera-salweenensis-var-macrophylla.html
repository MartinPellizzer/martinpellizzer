Schefflera salweenensis var. macrophylla

Schefflera salweenensis var. macrophylla, commonly known as the Salween Schefflera, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, palmate leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in diameter. The plant typically has a compact, shrub-like growth habit with multiple stems branching from the base. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Its striking foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
