Senecio argenteus

Senecio argenteus, commonly known as silver groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features silver-gray leaves covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its flowers are small, yellow, and clustered in dense heads, blooming in late spring to early summer.

Senecio argenteus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and ease of care.
