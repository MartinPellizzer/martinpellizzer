Scutellaria jaliscana

Scutellaria jaliscana, commonly known as the Jalisco skullcap, is a perennial herb native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features square stems and opposite leaves, typical of the mint family. Its flowers are usually blue or violet, with two lips, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Scutellaria jaliscana is often found in rocky or open habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.
