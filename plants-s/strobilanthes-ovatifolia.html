Strobilanthes ovatifolia

Strobilanthes ovatifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaf strobilanthes, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its ovate, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking clusters of tubular, two-lipped flowers that are typically purple or violet in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
