Sedum obtusatum var. retusum

Sedum obtusatum var. retusum, commonly known as the bluntleaf stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to parts of North America.

It is characterized by its fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it an ideal plant for arid environments.
