Stylidium nungarinense

Stylidium nungarinense, commonly known as the Nungarin spurred orchid, is a small, flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its distinctive spurred petals and compact growth habit. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils in arid or semi-arid environments. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, with a prominent spur that aids in pollination by specific insect species.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
