Siphoneranthemum detruncatum

Siphoneranthemum detruncatum, commonly known as the dwarf siphon, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is often cultivated in rock gardens and as a decorative ground cover.
