Stelis sanchoi

Stelis sanchoi, commonly known as the Sancho's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features slender, upright pseudobulbs that support its evergreen leaves. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and usually white or pale yellow, arranged in a spike.

Stelis sanchoi is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
