Schoenefeldia transiens

Schoenefeldia transiens, commonly known as the wandering lily, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces showy, white to pale pink flowers with a distinctive, fragrant scent. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves and grows from a rhizomatous root system. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its unique blooming pattern, where flowers open at night, makes it a fascinating subject for botanical study.
