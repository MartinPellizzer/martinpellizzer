Sebastiania cruenta

Sebastiania cruenta, commonly known as the crimson sebastiania, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its striking red-orange flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain seeds covered in a sticky resin. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a velvety texture.
