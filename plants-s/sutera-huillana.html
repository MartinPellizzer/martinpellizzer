Sutera huillana

Sutera huillana, commonly known as the Huillan silverleaf, is a shrub native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. Sutera huillana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
