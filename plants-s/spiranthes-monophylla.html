Spiranthes monophylla

Spiranthes monophylla, commonly known as the single-leaf ladies' tresses, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its dense, spiral arrangement of white to pale pink flowers.
