Sedum nanshanchunense

Sedum nanshanchunense, commonly known as the Nanshan sedum, is a succulent plant native to China.

It is characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping.
