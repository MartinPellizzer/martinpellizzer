Satureja sphaerophylla

Satureja sphaerophylla, commonly known as spherical-leaf savory, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, spherical leaves and compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces tiny, pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It prefers well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Satureja sphaerophylla is valued for its aromatic foliage and is often used in culinary and medicinal applications.
