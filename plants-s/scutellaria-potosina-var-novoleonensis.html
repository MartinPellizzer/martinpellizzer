Scutellaria potosina var. novoleonensis

Scutellaria potosina var. novoleonensis, commonly known as the Mexican skullcap, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. Its leaves are usually opposite and have a slightly serrated edge.

It is often found in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid environments.
