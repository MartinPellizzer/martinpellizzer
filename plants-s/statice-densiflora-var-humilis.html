Statice densiflora var. humilis

Statice densiflora var. humilis, commonly known as dwarf statice, is a low-growing, flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features compact, bushy growth with small, dense clusters of blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often cultivated as a ornamental for its long-lasting flowers and drought tolerance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its botanical characteristics make it a hardy and versatile choice for gardeners seeking resilient, decorative plants.
