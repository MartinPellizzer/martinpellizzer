Statice tunetana

Statice tunetana, commonly known as the Tunisian statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of pink, red, and purple. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and saline soils, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are often clustered in dense inflorescences.

Statice tunetana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and floral arrangements.
