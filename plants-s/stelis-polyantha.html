Stelis polyantha

Stelis polyantha, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive fragrance that attracts pollinators.

Stelis polyantha is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and unique scent.
