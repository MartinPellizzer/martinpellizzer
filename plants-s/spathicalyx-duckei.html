Spathicalyx duckei

Spathicalyx duckei, commonly known as the Borneo orchid, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique floral structure. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on tree trunks or branches.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species.
