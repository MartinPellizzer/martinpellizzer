Satyrium shirense

Satyrium shirense, commonly known as the Chinese orchid, is a species native to China and belongs to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its elegant, fragrant flowers that typically feature a combination of white, purple, and green hues. The plant grows as an epiphytic orchid, often found on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually long and strap-like, and it produces small, clustered flowers during the spring season.

Satyrium shirense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
