Senecio sykorae

Senecio sykorae, commonly known as the Sykorae groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by fluffy, seed-bearing structures.

Senecio sykorae is often found in mountainous regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
