Stenolobium stans var. castanifolium

Stenolobium stans var. castanifolium, commonly known as the yellow flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking yellow to orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. The leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptical, with a leathery texture and a glossy appearance. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its rapid growth and vibrant floral display make it a notable species in its native habitat.
