Sedum purdomii

Sedum purdomii, commonly known as Purdom's stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that store water, enabling it to thrive in dry environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the spring and summer months. It is often found growing on rocky outcrops and in sandy soils.

Due to its unique adaptations, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
