Selliguea Trisecta

Selliguea trisecta is a rare and intriguing moss species that thrives in humid, shaded environments. Found primarily in tropical and subtropical regions, it is known for its delicate, branching structure and unique leaf morphology. This plant plays a vital role in ecosystem stability by contributing to soil moisture retention and nutrient cycling. Despite its ecological importance, it remains under-studied and often overlooked in conservation efforts. Understanding Selliguea trisecta is key to preserving the biodiversity of its native habitats.
