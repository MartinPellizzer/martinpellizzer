Stelis kareniae

Stelis kareniae, commonly known as the Karenia orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, greenish-white or pale yellow flowers that bloom during the warm season. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Stelis kareniae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique beauty.
