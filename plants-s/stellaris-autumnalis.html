Stellaris autumnalis

Stellaris autumnalis, commonly known as autumn star.

It is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. The plant features delicate, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its unique appearance.

The plant prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens and alpine settings.
