Solanum ruvu

Solanum ruvu, commonly known as the African nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring simple, alternate leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are usually yellow or white, and it produces small, round berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

The plant is notable for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
