Sulcorebutia verticillacantha var. albispina

Sulcorebutia verticillacantha var. albispina, commonly known as the white-spined crested cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to South America.

It features a dense covering of white spines, which give it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking contrast to its green body. It is well-suited for rock gardens and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.

Its slow growth rate and unique appearance make it a prized specimen in horticulture.
