Shawia ramulosa

Shawia ramulosa, commonly known as the slender leaf shrub, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Shawia ramulosa thrives in well-drained soil and requires a warm, humid climate to flourish.

Its unique morphology makes it a desirable ornamental plant in botanical gardens and private collections.
