Stenocereus yunckeri

Stenocereus yunckeri, commonly known as the Yucca-like cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is a columnar cactus with a distinctive appearance, featuring a tall, spiny stem and clusters of pale yellow flowers. The plant has spines that are typically white to yellow in color, and its leaves are reduced to scale-like structures. It produces small, fleshy fruits that are edible and often consumed by local wildlife.

This plant is well adapted to survive in harsh desert conditions with minimal water.
