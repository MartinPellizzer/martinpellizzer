Sinocrassula stenosquamata

Sinocrassula stenosquamata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf sinocrassula, is a succulent plant native to China.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming growth habit. The plant features fleshy, narrow leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish or reddish tinge. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy leaves.

Sinocrassula stenosquamata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
