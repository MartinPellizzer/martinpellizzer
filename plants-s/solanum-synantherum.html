Solanum synantherum

Solanum synantherum, commonly known as the yellow nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often found in moist, shady environments. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, while its fruits are small, round, and yellow when mature.

Solanum synantherum is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive blooms.
