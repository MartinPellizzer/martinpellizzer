Solanum hygrothermicum

Solanum hygrothermicum, commonly known as the moisture-sensitive nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to tropical regions.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant is characterized by its small, egg-shaped leaves and clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. Its fruits are typically small berries that change color as they mature.

Due to its sensitivity to humidity, it thrives in moist, shaded environments.
