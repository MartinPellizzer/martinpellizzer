Stapelia montana var. grossa

Stapelia montana var. grossa, commonly known as the giant star stapelia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, fleshy, and often ribbed stems that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces showy, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, with a strong, unpleasant odor. These flowers bloom during the summer months and are pollinated by flies.

It is a hardy, drought-tolerant species that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.
