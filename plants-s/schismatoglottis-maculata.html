Schismatoglottis maculata

Schismatoglottis maculata, commonly known as the spotted lady's slipper, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, showy flowers with a distinctive pinkish-brown color and a prominent, pouch-like labellum. The plant has fleshy, dark green leaves that are often marked with spots or streaks, giving it its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, rich soil.

Schismatoglottis maculata is prized for its ornamental value in horticulture and is often cultivated for its unique and elegant blooms.
