Stelis comica

Stelis comica, commonly known as the fairy orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid tropical forests. The plant has a compact growth habit with a single stem and a few leaves. Its flowers are delicate, often with a pink or white coloration, and are arranged in a spike.

Stelis comica is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
