Spathiphyllum heliconiifolium

Spathiphyllum heliconiifolium, commonly known as the heliconiifolium peace lily, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or silver markings. The plant produces striking, white, spadix-shaped flowers surrounded by a broad, green spathe, which gives it its scientific name. It is a popular houseplant due to its air-purifying qualities and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and ecologically significant in its native habitat.
