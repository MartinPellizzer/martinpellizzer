Stelis asplundii

Stelis asplundii, commonly known as the Asplund's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often found in forest understories. The plant features a pseudobulb that supports its growth and stores water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually small and white or pale pink, with a distinctive lip that aids in pollination.

Stelis asplundii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
