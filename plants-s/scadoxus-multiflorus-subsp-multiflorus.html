Scadoxus multiflorus subsp. multiflorus

Scadoxus multiflorus subsp.

multiflorus, commonly known as the Natal flatleaf, is a bulbous plant native to southern Africa. It features a tall, erect stem with broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often spotted or streaked with purple.

The plant produces clusters of bright white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its striking foliage and long-lasting blooms.
