Styrax socialis

Styrax socialis, commonly known as the clustered styrax, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white, clustered flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

Styrax socialis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and is often used in gardens and landscapes.
