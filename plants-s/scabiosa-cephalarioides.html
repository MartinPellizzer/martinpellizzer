Scabiosa cephalarioides

Scabiosa cephalarioides, commonly known as the skyrocket scabious, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, pink or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found growing in rocky or gravelly soils. It is valued in gardens for its attractive appearance and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Scabiosa cephalarioides is relatively drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun conditions.
