Sphaerocionium wercklei

Sphaerocionium wercklei, commonly known as the golden star cactus, is a species of cactus native to South America.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are covered in dense, golden-yellow spines. The plant produces showy, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in arid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Sphaerocionium wercklei is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
