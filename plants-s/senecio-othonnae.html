Senecio othonnae

Senecio othonnae, commonly known as the Cape marigold, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. The plant produces yellow-orange flowers in summer, adding vibrant color to its habitat. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
