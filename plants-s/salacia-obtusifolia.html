Salacia obtusifolia

Salacia obtusifolia, commonly known as the blunt-leaf salacia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Salaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and oval-shaped leaves with a blunted appearance. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.

Salacia obtusifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique foliage and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
