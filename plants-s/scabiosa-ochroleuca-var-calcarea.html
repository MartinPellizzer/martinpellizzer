Scabiosa ochroleuca var. calcarea

Scabiosa ochroleuca var. calcarea, commonly known as the limestone scabious, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its pale yellow to white flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in calcareous soils, thriving in rocky or limestone-rich environments.
