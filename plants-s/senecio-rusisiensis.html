Senecio rusisiensis

Senecio rusisiensis, commonly known as the Rusisiense groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Tanzania.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in high-altitude environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and hairy, adapting to the harsh conditions of its habitat.

Senecio rusisiensis is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and potential ecological significance in its native ecosystem.
