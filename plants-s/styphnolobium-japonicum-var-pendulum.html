Styphnolobium japonicum var. pendulum

Styphnolobium japonicum var. pendulum, commonly known as the Chinese kerria, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are pinnately arranged and have a feathery appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by elongated, bean-like pods. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Its pendulous branches and attractive foliage make it a visually appealing addition to landscapes.
