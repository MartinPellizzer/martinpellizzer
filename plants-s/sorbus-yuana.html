Sorbus yuana

Sorbus yuana, commonly known as the Chinese whitebeam, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically red or yellow when mature. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a serrated margin, often turning yellow in autumn.

Sorbus yuana is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
