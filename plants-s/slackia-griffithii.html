Slackia griffithii

Slackia griffithii, commonly known as the Indian bluebell, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually blue or purple in color. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

Slackia griffithii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
