Senecio jungioides

Senecio jungioides, commonly known as the Jungioides groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright, branched stems and yellow-orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
