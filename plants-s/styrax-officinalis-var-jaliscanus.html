Styrax officinalis var. jaliscanus

Styrax officinalis var. jaliscanus, commonly known as Mexican styrax, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. The plant produces dark, glossy berries that are often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a leathery texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has historical uses in herbal remedies.
