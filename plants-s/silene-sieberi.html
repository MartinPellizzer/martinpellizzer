Silene sieberi

Silene sieberi, commonly known as the Sieber's campion, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. Silene sieberi is often found in alpine and subalpine regions, where it thrives in cooler, temperate climates.
