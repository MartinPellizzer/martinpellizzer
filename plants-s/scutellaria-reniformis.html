Scutellaria reniformis

Scutellaria reniformis, commonly known as the kidneyleaf skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is characterized by its distinctive kidney-shaped leaves and is often found in shaded or partially shaded environments.

Scutellaria reniformis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
