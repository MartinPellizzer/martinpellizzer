Salvia polystachia

Salvia polystachia, commonly known as the Chinese sage or large-leaf sage, is a perennial shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its striking, violet-blue tubular flowers that bloom in dense, terminal clusters. The plant features lance-shaped, aromatic leaves with a fuzzy texture, often tinged with silver or gray. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Salvia polystachia is also valued for its medicinal properties and use in traditional Chinese medicine.
