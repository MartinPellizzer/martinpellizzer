Sapium bodenbenderi

Sapium bodenbenderi, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its striking, papery orange to red bracts that resemble small lanterns, which are actually modified leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsule fruits that split open to release seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Its unique foliage makes it a favorite for adding color and texture to landscapes.
