Sideroxylon tenax

Sideroxylon tenax, commonly known as the ironwood tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, hard, and heavy wood, which is highly valued for its durability and strength. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in spikes, followed by woody, oblong fruits containing seeds.

The species is often found in lowland rainforests and is known for its ability to thrive in humid, tropical climates.
