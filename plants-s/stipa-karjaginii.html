Stipa karjaginii

Stipa karjaginii, commonly known as Karjagin's feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and produces delicate, feathery inflorescences. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as steppes and rocky soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Stipa karjaginii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in stabilizing soil in arid environments.
