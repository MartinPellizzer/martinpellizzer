Syncolostemon macrophyllus

Syncolostemon macrophyllus, commonly known as the large-leaf synclast.

It is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne on slender stalks. It thrives in humid, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Syncolostemon macrophyllus is valued for its ornamental leaves and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
