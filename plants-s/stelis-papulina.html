Stelis papulina

Stelis papulina, commonly known as the papular stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has a compact, pseudobulbous structure with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually small and white or pale green, with a distinct, hairy appearance.

Stelis papulina thrives in humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.
