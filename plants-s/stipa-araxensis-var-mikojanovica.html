Stipa araxensis var. mikojanovica

Stipa araxensis var. mikojanovica, commonly known as the Armenian feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of Armenia.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry culms and feathery inflorescences that give it a distinctive, ornamental appearance. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration that adds to its visual appeal.

This variety is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and its potential use in landscaping and restoration projects.
