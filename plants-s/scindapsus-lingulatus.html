Scindapsus lingulatus

Scindapsus lingulatus, commonly known as the silver vine, is a tropical evergreen vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with striking silvery veins that give it its common name. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is known for its ability to climb and can be trained to grow on trellises or shelves.

Scindapsus lingulatus is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
