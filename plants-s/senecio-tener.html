Senecio tener

Senecio tener, commonly known as the woolly groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
