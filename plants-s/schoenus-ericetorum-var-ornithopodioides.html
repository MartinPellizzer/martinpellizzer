Schoenus ericetorum var. ornithopodioides

Schoenus ericetorum var. ornithopodioides, commonly known as the ornithopodioides sedge, is a perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wet, acidic soils, often found in marshy areas or along the edges of water bodies. Its botanical features include fibrous roots and a clump-forming growth habit.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in wetland habitats.
