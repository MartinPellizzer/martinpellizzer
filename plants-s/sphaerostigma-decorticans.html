Sphaerostigma decorticans

Sphaerostigma decorticans, commonly known as the African snowball, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of white flowers that bloom in winter. The plant has small, leathery leaves and produces small, red berries that are edible for humans and animals. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in ornamental gardening.

The species is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators and its resilience to drought conditions.
