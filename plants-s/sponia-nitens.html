Sponia nitens

Sponia nitens, commonly known as the silver sponia, is a rare and delicate plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its finely textured, silvery leaves that reflect sunlight, aiding in its survival in harsh environments. The plant produces small, pale flowers that bloom in late summer, adding a subtle beauty to its alpine habitat. Sponia nitens thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and requires a cool, humid climate to flourish.

Due to its limited distribution and sensitivity to environmental changes, it is considered a vulnerable species.
