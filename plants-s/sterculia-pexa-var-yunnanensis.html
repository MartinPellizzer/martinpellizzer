Sterculia pexa var. yunnanensis

Sterculia pexa var. yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan sterculia, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree characterized by its smooth, dark bark and broad, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in axillary inflorescences, followed by woody capsules containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in secondary forests.

The species is valued for its ornamental potential and has limited use in traditional medicine.
