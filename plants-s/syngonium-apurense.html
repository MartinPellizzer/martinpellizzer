Syngonium apurense

Syngonium apurense, commonly known as the arrowhead vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, pale green flowers that are followed by bright red berries. Syngonium apurense thrives in humid, warm environments and is often used in ornamental horticulture.

Its rapid growth makes it an excellent choice for vertical gardening or as a houseplant.
