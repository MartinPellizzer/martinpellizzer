Schlumbergera truncata var. altensteinii

Schlumbergera truncata var. altensteinii, commonly known as the Altensteinii Christmas cactus, is a hybrid species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its segmented, fleshy stems that form a branching structure, typical of the Schlumbergera genus. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, pink, and white during the holiday season. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

This variety is popular for its ornamental value and ability to bloom reliably under proper care.
