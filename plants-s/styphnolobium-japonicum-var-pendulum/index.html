Styphnolobium Japonicum Var. Pendulum

Styphnolobium japonicum var. pendulum is a strikingly ornamental plant known for its cascading clusters of fragrant, golden-yellow flowers. Native to China, this variety of the Chinese honeylocust thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal. Its delicate, drooping inflorescences make it a popular choice for ornamental gardens and landscape design. The plant is also valued for its hardy nature and ability to tolerate a variety of soil conditions. With its elegant appearance and seasonal blooms, it adds a touch of beauty and fragrance to any garden setting.
