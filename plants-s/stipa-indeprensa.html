Stipa indeprensa

Stipa indeprensa, commonly known as the slender needlegrass, is a perennial grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open habitats.

Stipa indeprensa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in arid environments.
