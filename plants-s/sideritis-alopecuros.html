Sideritis alopecuros

Sideritis alopecuros, commonly known as Greek violet or violet rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and is characterized by its small, violet-blue flowers. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Sideritis alopecuros is valued for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
