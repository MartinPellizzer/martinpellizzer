Scabiosa columbaria subsp. eucolumbaria

Scabiosa columbaria subsp.

eucolumbaria, commonly known as the pink scabious, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia. It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.

The plant produces clusters of small, pink or purple flowers that bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are upright and often branched.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
