Senecio centropappus

Senecio centropappus, commonly known as the yellow starburster, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has slender, branched stems and narrow, gray-green leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
