Solanum tuberosum f. kunurana

Solanum tuberosum f. kunurana, commonly known as the purple potato, is a botanical variant of the common potato.

It is characterized by its distinctive violet to purple skin and flesh, which gives it a unique visual appeal. This variety is typically smaller than standard potatoes and has a slightly sweeter flavor. It thrives in cool climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The plant belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is cultivated for both ornamental and culinary purposes.
