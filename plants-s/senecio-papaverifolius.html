Senecio papaverifolius

Senecio papaverifolius, commonly known as the "papaver leaf senecio," is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
