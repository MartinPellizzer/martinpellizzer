Solanum tuberosum f. janccko-kkoyllu

Solanum tuberosum f. janccko-kkoyllu, commonly known as the Janccko-Kkoyllu potato, is a botanical variant of the common potato.

It is characterized by its unique tuber morphology, which includes distinct coloration and texture. This variety is native to the Andean region and is adapted to high-altitude growing conditions. The plant belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and exhibits typical solanum growth habits.

Its botanical significance lies in its genetic diversity and potential for agricultural improvement.
