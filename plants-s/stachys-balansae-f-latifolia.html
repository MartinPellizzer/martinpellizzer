Stachys balansae f. latifolia

Stachys balansae f. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf stachys, is a flowering plant belonging to the mint family, Lamiaceae.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and spikes of pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. Native to the Mediterranean region, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and floral display.

The species is also valued for its aromatic properties and historical use in traditional medicine.
