Stylidium petiolare var. pauciflorum

Stylidium petiolare var. pauciflorum, commonly known as the slender stickseed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Scrophulariaceae.

It is native to Australia and is characterized by its prostrate growth habit and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant produces a limited number of flowers per inflorescence, which is reflected in its varietal name "pauciflorum.". Its leaves are typically fleshy and succulent, adapted to arid environments.

This variety is often found in sandy or rocky soils in coastal regions.
