Stellaria diversiflora var. diandra

Stellaria diversiflora var. diandra, commonly known as the narrow-leaved starwort, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its small, white or pale pink star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a whorled pattern around the stem. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as forest floors or stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the Caryophyllaceae family.
