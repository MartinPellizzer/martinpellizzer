Sedum radiatum var. radiatum

Sedum radiatum var. radiatum, commonly known as the radiate stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features fleshy, upright stems and clusters of star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The leaves are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating that helps reduce water loss. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

This variety is known for its hardiness and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
