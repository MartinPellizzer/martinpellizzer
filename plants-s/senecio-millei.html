Senecio millei

Senecio millei, commonly known as the Blue Euphorbia, is a striking succulent native to South Africa.

It features a compact, rounded shape with dense clusters of blue-green leaves that give it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a subtle touch of color. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
