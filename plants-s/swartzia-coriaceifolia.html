Swartzia coriaceifolia

Swartzia coriaceifolia, commonly known as the African ebony, is a tropical tree native to Africa.

It is characterized by its dark, dense wood and smooth, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

Swartzia coriaceifolia is valued for its hard wood and is often used in furniture and musical instruments.
