Scaphyglottis Coriacea

Scaphyglottis coriacea is a unique and intriguing member of the orchid family known for its striking foliage and delicate flowers. Native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are thick and leathery, providing a striking contrast to its slender, showy blooms. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively obscure species among orchid enthusiasts. This article explores the characteristics, habitat, and cultivation of Scaphyglottis coriacea.
