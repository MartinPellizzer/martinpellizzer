Sibbaldia byssitecta

Sibbaldia byssitecta, commonly known as the Kashmir snowbell, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows at high altitudes, often in alpine meadows and rocky areas. The plant produces small, bell-shaped white flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often covered in a fine, silky hair.

Sibbaldia byssitecta is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens in cooler climates.
