Syngonium vellozianum

Syngonium vellozianum, commonly known as the arrowhead vine, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that often have a striking variegation of green, white, and sometimes pink. The plant is a member of the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like peace lilies and philodendrons. Syngonium vellozianum typically grows as a climbing or trailing vine, making it popular for hanging baskets or as a foliage plant in shaded areas.

Its rapid growth and attractive foliage make it a favored choice for indoor gardening.
