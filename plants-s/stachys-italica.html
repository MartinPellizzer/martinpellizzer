Stachys italica

Stachys italica, commonly known as Italian hedge nettle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges and produces small, pale purple to white flowers in whorled clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Its hairy stems and leaves give it a distinctive, somewhat fuzzy appearance.
