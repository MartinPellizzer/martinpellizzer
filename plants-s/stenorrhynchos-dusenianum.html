Stenorrhynchos dusenianum

Stenorrhynchos dusenianum, commonly known as the Duseni's orchid, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of West Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that display a range of colors including white, pink, and purple. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees without parasitizing them. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Due to its unique beauty and habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
