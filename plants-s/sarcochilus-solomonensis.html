Sarcochilus solomonensis

Sarcochilus solomonensis, commonly known as the Solomon Islands tree orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Solomon Islands.

It is characterized by its robust, woody rhizome and can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom in clusters along the stems. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and is adapted to the tropical climate of its native region.
