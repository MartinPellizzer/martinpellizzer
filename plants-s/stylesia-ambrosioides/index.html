Stylesia Ambrosioides

Stylesia ambrosioides is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of South America. Known for its vibrant, often yellow or orange flowers, it thrives in cool, humid climates. This species is closely related to the genus Eupatorium and shares similar ecological preferences. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Due to habitat loss, Stylesia ambrosioides faces increasing threats to its survival.
