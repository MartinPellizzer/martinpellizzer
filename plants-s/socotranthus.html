Socotranthus

Socotranthus, commonly known as the Socotra thistle, is a unique plant native to the island of Socotra.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, spiny leaves and yellow flowers. The plant thrives in the arid and rocky environments of Socotra, adapting to the island's harsh climate. Its morphology reflects evolutionary adaptations to the island's isolation and specific ecological conditions.

Socotranthus is a symbol of the island's unique biodiversity and is found in various habitats across Socotra's landscape.
