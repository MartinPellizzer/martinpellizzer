Saussurea chinensis

Saussurea chinensis, commonly known as Chinese yarrow, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, fibrous stems and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces a distinctive, aromatic essential oil. It thrives in cool, high-altitude environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for various health benefits.
