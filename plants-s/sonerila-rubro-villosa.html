Sonerila rubro-villosa

Sonerila rubro-villosa, commonly known as the red-hairy sonerila, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pink in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after species in horticulture.
