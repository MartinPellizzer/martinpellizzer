Stanhopea tigrina var. splendens

Stanhopea tigrina var. splendens, commonly known as the Splendour Orchid, is a species native to the tropical rainforests of Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of orange and yellow. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous fragrant blooms. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and unique floral structure.
