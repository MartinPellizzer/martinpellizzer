Setaria cylindrica

Setaria cylindrica, commonly known as slender bristlegrass, is a perennial grass native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, bristly spikelets. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and other open areas.

Setaria cylindrica is drought-tolerant and has a fibrous root system that helps stabilize soil.
