Senecio dimorphophyllus var. paysonii

Senecio dimorphophyllus var. paysonii, commonly known as the Payson's blue ice plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, covered in a fine layer of white hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it an attractive addition to arid landscapes.
