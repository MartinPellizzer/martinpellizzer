Specklinia aveniformis

Specklinia aveniformis, commonly known as the ridge-leafed tillandsia, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on the surfaces of other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant features long, narrow leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern, with a distinctive ridge running along the leaf surface. It produces small, white flowers that are typically borne in clusters near the base of the plant.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and terrariums.
