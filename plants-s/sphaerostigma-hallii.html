Sphaerostigma hallii

Sphaerostigma hallii, commonly known as the African orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to southern Africa.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive, showy flowers. The plant produces small, greenish-white to yellowish flowers that are typically arranged in a loose cluster. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade in its natural habitat.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
