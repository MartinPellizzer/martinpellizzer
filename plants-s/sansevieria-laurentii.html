Sansevieria laurentii

Sansevieria laurentii, commonly known as the snake plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with distinctive yellow or white variegation. The plant is highly drought-tolerant and thrives in low-light conditions, making it an ideal indoor plant. Its leaves are succulent, storing water to help it survive in arid environments.

Sansevieria laurentii is also noted for its air-purifying properties, improving indoor air quality by removing toxins.
