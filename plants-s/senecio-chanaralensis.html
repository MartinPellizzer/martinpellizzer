Senecio chanaralensis

Senecio chanaralensis, commonly known as the Chanaralensis Groundsel, is a flowering plant native to Chile.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, adapting to harsh environmental conditions. It features narrow, gray-green leaves and produces small yellow flowers.

Its botanical structure reflects its evolutionary adaptation to withstand drought and intense sunlight.
