Sonerila firma

Sonerila firma, commonly known as the golden flame or flame orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful inflorescences that resemble flames. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, utilizing its aerial roots to absorb moisture and nutrients from the environment. Its flowers are often brightly colored, with a mix of yellow, orange, and red hues, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Sonerila firma thrives in humid, warm climates and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.
