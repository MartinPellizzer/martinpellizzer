Stylosanthes procumbens

Stylosanthes procumbens, commonly known as匍匐三叶草, is a low-growing perennial legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features trifoliate leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a fibrous root system that helps stabilize soil and fix nitrogen, making it valuable for soil improvement. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in forage and erosion control.

Its rapid growth and hardiness make it a popular choice in agricultural and ecological restoration projects.
