Solanum wollastonii

Solanum wollastonii, commonly known as Wollaston's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and peppers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching up to 3 meters in height. Its leaves are oval-shaped with a rough texture, and it produces small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to violet.

The fruit is a small, round berry that turns from green to yellow or red when mature.
