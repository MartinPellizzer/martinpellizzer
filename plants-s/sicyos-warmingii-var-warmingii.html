Sicyos warmingii var. warmingii, commonly known as the golden bottle gourd, is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, spherical, yellowish-green fruits that are used in various culinary applications. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers that are typically unisexual. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
