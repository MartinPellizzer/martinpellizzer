Spartium baeticum

Spartium baeticum, commonly known as the Iberian spartium, is a perennial shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in rocky and sunny environments, often found in Mediterranean climates.

Spartium baeticum is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soils and supporting local biodiversity.
