Stachys ocymastrum

Stachys ocymastrum, commonly known as narrowleaf betony, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters, featuring opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture. Its flowers are small, tubular, and clustered in whorls, often in shades of violet or blue.

Stachys ocymastrum is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
