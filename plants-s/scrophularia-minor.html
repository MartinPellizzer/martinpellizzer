Scrophularia minor

Scrophularia minor, commonly known as lesser scrophularia, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Scrophulariaceae family, which includes many species with showy flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas and has lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with five petals and a distinctive tube shape.

It is often found in woodland habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
