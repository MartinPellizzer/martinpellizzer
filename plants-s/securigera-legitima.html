Securigera legitima

Securigera legitima, commonly known as the goat's rue, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and parts of southern Europe. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. It produces pods that contain small, round seeds.

Securigera legitima is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments.
