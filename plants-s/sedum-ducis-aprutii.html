Sedum ducis-aprutii

Sedum ducis-aprutii, commonly known as the Apulian stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Europe, particularly Italy.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
