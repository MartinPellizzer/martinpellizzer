Sauvagesia serpyllifolia

Sauvagesia serpyllifolia, commonly known as common sandmat, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with fleshy, succulent leaves that help it retain moisture in arid environments. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal areas or disturbed habitats.
