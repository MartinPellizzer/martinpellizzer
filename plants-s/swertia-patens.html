Swertia patens

Swertia patens, commonly known as the Himalayan gentian, is a perennial herb native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

Swertia patens is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
