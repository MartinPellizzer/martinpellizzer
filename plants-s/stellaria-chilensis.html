Stellaria chilensis

Stellaria chilensis, commonly known as Chile starwort, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Chile and Argentina.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, star-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pink. The plant has slender, branched stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, temperate climates and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

Stellaria chilensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for various purposes.
