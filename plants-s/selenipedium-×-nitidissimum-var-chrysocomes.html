Selenipedium × nitidissimum var. chrysocomes

Selenipedium × nitidissimum var. chrysocomes, commonly known as the golden-leaved selenipedium, is a hybrid orchid native to the cloud forests of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated leaves that are typically a vibrant yellow-green in color. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing numerous small, white to pale yellow flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-drained soil.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a conservation concern.
