Stenocactus cadaroyi

Stenocactus cadaroyi, commonly known as the Mexican fire cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, spineless cactus with a globular shape and bright green to reddish stems. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by small, red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and succulent collections.
