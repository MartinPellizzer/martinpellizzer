Saxifraga × salmonica

Saxifraga × salmonica, commonly known as the salmon pink saxifrage, is a hybrid plant that exhibits a range of botanical characteristics.

It typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers that are often pink or salmon-colored, adding ornamental value to its habitat. Its leaves are usually succulent and fleshy, adapted to retain moisture in arid conditions.

Saxifraga × salmonica is valued in horticulture for its hardiness and aesthetic appeal in garden settings.
