Sasaella leucorhoda var. kanayamensis

Sasaella leucorhoda var. kanayamensis, commonly known as the Kanayama white sasa, is a variant of the white sasa plant native to Japan.

It is a clump-forming bamboo that typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments. The plant features slender, hollow stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are usually a pale green to white color. It is valued for its ornamental appearance and is often used in horticultural displays.

This variety is relatively low maintenance and thrives in cool, humid climates.
