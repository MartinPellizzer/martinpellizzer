Stachys iberica var. congesta

Stachys iberica var. congesta, commonly known as Spanish hedgehog.

It is a perennial herb native to southern Europe, particularly Spain and Portugal. The plant features upright stems covered in dense, fuzzy hairs, and its leaves are ovate with a serrated margin. It produces spikes of violet to purple flowers in late spring to early summer.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soils.
