Solidago canadensis var. canadensis

Solidago canadensis var. canadensis, commonly known as Canadian goldenrod, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers in late summer, which are arranged in panicles. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Canadian goldenrod is often found in moist meadows, woodlands, and along stream banks.
