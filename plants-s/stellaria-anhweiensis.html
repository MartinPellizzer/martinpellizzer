Stellaria anhweiensis

Stellaria anhweiensis, commonly known as the Chinese starwort, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a smooth surface.

Stellaria anhweiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
