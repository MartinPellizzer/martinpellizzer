Solanum japonense

Solanum japonense, commonly known as Japanese nightshade, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, white or purple flowers that bloom in summer, followed by red, fleshy berries. Its leaves are typically ovate with a rough texture, and the plant is often found in moist, shaded environments.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
