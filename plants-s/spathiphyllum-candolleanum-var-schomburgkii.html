Spathiphyllum candolleanum var. schomburgkii

Spathiphyllum candolleanum var. schomburgkii, commonly known as the Schomburgk's peace lily, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green heart-shaped leaves and produces striking white spathes that enclose a cluster of small, yellowish flowers. The plant is a member of the Araceae family, known for its unique inflorescence structure. It thrives in humid, shady environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal.
