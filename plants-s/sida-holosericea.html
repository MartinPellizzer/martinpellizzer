Sida holosericea

Sida holosericea, commonly known as silky sida, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its soft, silky hairs that cover its stems and leaves, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

Sida holosericea is also known for its use in traditional medicine and as a source of natural fibers.
