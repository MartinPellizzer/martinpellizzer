Stellaria mukerjeeana

Stellaria mukerjeeana, commonly known as the Indian starwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is native to the Indian subcontinent and is characterized by its star-shaped flowers and delicate foliage. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, while its flowers are small and white or pale pink.

Stellaria mukerjeeana is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in partial shade.
