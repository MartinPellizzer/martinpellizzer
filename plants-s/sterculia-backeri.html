Sterculia backeri

Sterculia backeri, commonly known as the African silk tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves. The tree produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that are clustered in panicles, attracting various pollinators. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local communities.
