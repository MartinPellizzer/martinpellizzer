Senecio lucidus

Senecio lucidus, commonly known as the shining senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its bright yellow flowers bloom in spring and attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Senecio lucidus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
