Sulcorebutia mariana

Sulcorebutia mariana, commonly known as the Mariana crassula, is a species of succulent native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rounded growth habit. The plant features fleshy, ribbed stems that are typically green with a bluish or reddish tinge. Its small, star-shaped flowers are usually pink or purple, blooming in late spring or early summer.

Sulcorebutia mariana is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems.
