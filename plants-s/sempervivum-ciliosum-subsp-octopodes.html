Sempervivum ciliosum subsp. octopodes

Sempervivum ciliosum subsp.

octopodes, commonly known as the Eight-Footed Sempervivum, is a succulent plant native to the Alps. It features dense rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves with white margins.

The plant produces tall, branching flower stalks that bear clusters of pink or purple flowers. It is well adapted to rocky and sunny environments, thriving in well-drained soil.

Its hardy nature makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine displays.
