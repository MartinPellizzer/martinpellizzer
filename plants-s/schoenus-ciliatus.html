Schoenus ciliatus

Schoenus ciliatus, commonly known as the silky sedgeland, is a perennial grass-like plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads. It typically grows in wetland or marshy environments, thriving in moist, nutrient-rich soils.
