Senecio pyroloides

Senecio pyroloides, commonly known as the fireweed or redflower senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open grasslands. It produces small, bright red to orange flowers that bloom in late summer.

Senecio pyroloides is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
