Syringa carlsruhensis

Syringa carlsruhensis, commonly known as the Carlsbad lilac, is a deciduous shrub native to the Czech Republic.

It is characterized by its fragrant, clustered flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant produces small, oval leaves that are dark green and glossy on top. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Syringa carlsruhensis is valued for its ornamental value and adaptability to various climates.
