Statice × sennenii

Statice × sennenii, commonly known as sea lavender, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from pink to purple. Native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean, it thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and full sun. The plant is drought-tolerant and often used in garden landscapes for its ornamental value.

Its common name reflects its resemblance to true lavender and its frequent occurrence in coastal environments.
