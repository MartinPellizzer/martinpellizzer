Stenocactus grandicornis

Stenocactus grandicornis, commonly known as the horned cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stem that can grow up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers during the summer months. Its spines are long and often curved, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is a slow-growing cactus that thrives in well-draining soil and full sunlight.
