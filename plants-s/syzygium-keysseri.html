Syzygium keysseri

Syzygium keysseri, commonly known as the Keyser's apple.

It is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Philippines, characterized by its smooth, dark grey bark and elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by edible, round fruits with a sweet and tart flavor. Syzygium keysseri is valued for its ornamental appeal and culinary uses, particularly in local cuisines.

It thrives in humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
