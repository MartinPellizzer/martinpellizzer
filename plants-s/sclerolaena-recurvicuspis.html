Sclerolaena recurvicuspis

Sclerolaena recurvicuspis, commonly known as the African star grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features slender, wiry stems that grow close to the ground, often forming dense mats. The plant produces small, white to pale blue flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, with a distinctive recurved tip.

Sclerolaena recurvicuspis is adapted to arid environments and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.
