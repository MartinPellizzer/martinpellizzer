Swartzia brachyrhachis var. colombiana

Swartzia brachyrhachis var. colombiana, commonly known as the Colombian swartzia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the humid forests of Colombia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features a straight trunk with a dense crown and produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink.

Its wood is valued for its durability and is used in furniture and construction, although it is also threatened by deforestation.
