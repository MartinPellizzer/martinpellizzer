Salicornia duvalii

Salicornia duvalii, commonly known as glasswort, is a halophytic plant adapted to grow in saline environments.

It is characterized by its jointed, succulent stems that are often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It is commonly found in coastal areas, salt marshes, and other saline soils.

Salicornia duvalii plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various small organisms.
