Serapias purpurea

Serapias purpurea, commonly known as the purple orchid orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its striking purple and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies.

Serapias purpurea is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
