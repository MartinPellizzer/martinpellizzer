Selloa capensis

Selloa capensis, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow or orange daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils and saline conditions. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, wind-pollinated flowers.

Selloa capensis is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
