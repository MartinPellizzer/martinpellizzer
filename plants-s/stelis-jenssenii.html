Stelis jenssenii

Stelis jenssenii, commonly known as the Jenssen's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are narrow and leathery, adapted to retain moisture in its natural habitat.

Stelis jenssenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
