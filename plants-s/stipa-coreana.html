Stipa coreana

Stipa coreana, commonly known as Korean feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and fine, needle-like leaves that give it a delicate appearance. The plant produces fluffy, feathery plumes that are often used in floral arrangements. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Stipa coreana is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion in natural landscapes.
