Stellaria cuspidata

Stellaria cuspidata, commonly known as the needleleaf starwort, is a flowering plant native to regions of Europe and Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and pointed, giving it the common name "needleleaf.".

It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.
