Sideroxylon ligustrinum

Sideroxylon ligustrinum, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to southern Africa. The tree is valued for its hard, heavy wood, which is used in construction and as a source of timber.
