Senna leandrii

Senna leandrii, commonly known as the golden senna, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters tall, characterized by its bipinnate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces elongated pods that contain kidney-shaped seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

Senna leandrii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine.
