Salicornia herbacea var. conferta

Salicornia herbacea var. conferta, commonly known as sea beans or glassworts, is a halophytic plant adapted to saline environments.

It is a succulent shrub that thrives in coastal areas, marshes, and salt marshes, where it tolerates high levels of salt in the soil and water. The plant features fleshy, cylindrical stems that are often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are typically greenish or yellowish and appear in clusters.

Salicornia herbacea var. conferta is an important part of coastal ecosystems, providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
