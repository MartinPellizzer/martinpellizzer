Struthiola garciana

Struthiola garciana, commonly known as the Garcinia shrub, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant yellow flowers and elliptical leaves with a slightly rough texture. The plant produces small, orange-colored fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade.

Struthiola garciana is also valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
