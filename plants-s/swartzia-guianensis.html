Swartzia guianensis

Swartzia guianensis, commonly known as the Guianan ironwood, is a tropical tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, hard wood, which is highly valued for its strength and durability. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow.

The fruit is a woody pod containing several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.
