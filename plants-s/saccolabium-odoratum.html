Saccolabium odoratum

Saccolabium odoratum, commonly known as the sweet bush or sweet labium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its aromatic, fragrant leaves and yellow flowers.
