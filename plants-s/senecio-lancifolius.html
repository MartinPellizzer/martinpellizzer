Senecio lancifolius

Senecio lancifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved groundsel, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features upright, branched stems covered in fine, white hairs. The leaves are elongated, lance-shaped, and have a rough, hairy texture. Its flowers are small, yellow, and clustered in dense, spherical heads.

This plant is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
