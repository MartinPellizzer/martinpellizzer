Salacia membranacea

Salacia membranacea, commonly known as the membranous salacia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Salicaceae, which includes willows and poplars. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and heart-shaped leaves. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
