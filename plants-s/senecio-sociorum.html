Senecio sociorum

Senecio sociorum, commonly known as the social senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are usually narrow and grayish-green, with a fuzzy texture.
