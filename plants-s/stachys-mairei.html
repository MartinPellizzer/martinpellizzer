Stachys mairei

Stachys mairei, commonly known as the Maire's hedge nettle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture and produces small, pale purple to pink flowers in whorled clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Stachys mairei is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
