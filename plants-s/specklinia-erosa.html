Specklinia erosa

Specklinia erosa, commonly known as the rose-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are often speckled with dark markings, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, typically arranged in a loose inflorescence. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Specklinia erosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for in a tropical greenhouse setting.
