Scutellaria shikokiana var. pubicaulis

Scutellaria shikokiana var. pubicaulis, commonly known as the Shikoku skullcap, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native habitat.
