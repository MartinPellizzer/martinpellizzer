Saxifraga coriophylla var. karadzicensis

Saxifraga coriophylla var. karadzicensis, commonly known as the Karadzic's saxifrage, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Balkan region.

It thrives in rocky and alpine environments, often found in limestone soils. The plant features small, clustered leaves that are typically grayish-green in color. Its flowers are pink to red, appearing in late spring to early summer.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in harsh mountainous conditions.
