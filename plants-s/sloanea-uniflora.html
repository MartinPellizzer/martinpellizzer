Sloanea uniflora

Sloanea uniflora, commonly known as the single-flower sloanea, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its simple, elliptical leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often during the rainy season. Sloanea uniflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, thriving in humid climates.
