Symplocos cavaleriei

Symplocos cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese teaberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and pale underneath. Symplocos cavaleriei is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
