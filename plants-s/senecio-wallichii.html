Senecio wallichii

Senecio wallichii, commonly known as the Himalayan groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or alpine environments. The plant features a rosette of gray-green, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Senecio wallichii is adapted to cold climates and is often found in mountainous regions at elevations above 2,000 meters.
