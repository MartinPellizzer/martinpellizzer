Stemmadenia hannae

Stemmadenia hannae, commonly known as the African star grass, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, star-shaped flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open grasslands.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the local flora and an attractive plant for ornamental purposes.
