Sedum ocuilense

Sedum ocuilense, commonly known as the Mexican stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it both hardy and aesthetically pleasing.
