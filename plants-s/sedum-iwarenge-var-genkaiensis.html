Sedum iwarenge var. genkaiensis

Sedum iwarenge var. genkaiensis, commonly known as the Genkai stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Japan.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant is adapted to rocky and sunny environments, making it drought-tolerant and easy to care for. Its leaves are typically blue-green in color and arranged in a rosette pattern.

It is often used in rock gardens and as a decorative ground cover due to its ornamental value and hardy nature.
