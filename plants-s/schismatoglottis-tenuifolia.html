Schismatoglottis tenuifolia

Schismatoglottis tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender leaf schismatoglottis, is a member of the Araceae family, characterized by its unique floral structure.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe, typical of many aroid plants.

Schismatoglottis tenuifolia is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
