Scorzonera tunicata

Scorzonera tunicata, commonly known as the tunicate scorzonera, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and is characterized by its hairy, tubular leaves and yellow-orange composite flowers. Scorzonera tunicata is known for its resilience in harsh environments and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
