Scindapsus bipinnatifidus

Scindapsus bipinnatifidus, commonly known as the Shiny ZZ plant, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its glossy, dark green, heart-shaped leaves. The plant has a compact growth habit with a short, thick stem and produces small, inconspicuous white flowers. It is a popular indoor plant due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in low light conditions.

Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine in its native regions.
