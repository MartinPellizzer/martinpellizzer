Stachys myriotricha

Stachys myriotricha, commonly known as velvetleaf stachys, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a soft, velvety appearance. The plant produces spikes of small, pinkish to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Stachys myriotricha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique texture and color.
