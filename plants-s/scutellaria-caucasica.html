Scutellaria caucasica

Scutellaria caucasica, commonly known as Caucasian skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the Caucasus region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. The plant features square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It is often found in rocky or woodland areas and is valued for its potential medicinal properties.

Scutellaria caucasica is also noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
