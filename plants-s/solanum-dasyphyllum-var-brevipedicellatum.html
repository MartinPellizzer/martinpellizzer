Solanum dasyphyllum var. brevipedicellatum

Solanum dasyphyllum var. brevipedicellatum, commonly known as the short-stemmed nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and tobacco. The plant is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and elliptical, dark green leaves. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its toxic properties, it is not suitable for consumption.
