Stachys vestita

Stachys vestita, commonly known as velvetleaf sage, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its hairy, lance-shaped leaves and spikes of purple or pink flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Stachys vestita is also used in traditional herbal medicine for its potential health benefits.
