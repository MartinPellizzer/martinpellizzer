Salvia dombeyi

Salvia dombeyi, commonly known as Dombey's sage, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, gray-green leaves with a fuzzy texture and produces spikes of blue to violet flowers. The plant is known for its aromatic properties and is often used in traditional herbal medicine. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy addition to gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and drought tolerance.
