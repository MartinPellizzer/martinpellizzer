Stipagrostis brachyathera

Stipagrostis brachyathera, commonly known as the short-haired grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of spikelets, which are often tightly packed.

Stipagrostis brachyathera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping or as a ground cover in dry regions.
