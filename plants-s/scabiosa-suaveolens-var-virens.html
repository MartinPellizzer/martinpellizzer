Scabiosa suaveolens var. virens

Scabiosa suaveolens var. virens, commonly known as green scabious, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine hairs and produces clusters of small, five-petaled flowers in shades of white to pale blue. The leaves are lance-shaped, hairy, and arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in meadows and grasslands.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
