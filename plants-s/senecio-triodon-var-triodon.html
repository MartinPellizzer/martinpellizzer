Senecio triodon var. triodon

Senecio triodon var. triodon, commonly known as the narrowleaf senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions. It features slender, upright stems and narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

The flowers are yellow and produce small seeds that are dispersed by the wind.
