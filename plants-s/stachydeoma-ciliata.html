Stachydeoma ciliata

Stachydeoma ciliata, commonly known as the silky thyme or woolly thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It features small, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has aromatic, hairy leaves that are often used in culinary and medicinal applications. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
