Solanum lachneion var. protopyrrhotrichum

Solanum lachneion var. protopyrrhotrichum, commonly known as the red-leaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known species like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant is characterized by its hairy, reddish leaves and small, yellowish flowers. It produces berries that are typically green when immature and turn red when mature.

Due to its toxic properties, it is not suitable for consumption and is often found in shaded, moist environments.
