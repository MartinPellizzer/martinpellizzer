Sedum kiangnanense

Sedum kiangnanense, commonly known as the Kiangnan stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to China.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It is adapted to grow in rocky or arid environments, making it a hardy and drought-tolerant species.

Sedum kiangnanense is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
