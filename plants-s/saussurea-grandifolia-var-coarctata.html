Saussurea grandifolia var. coarctata

Saussurea grandifolia var. coarctata, commonly known as the narrowleaf Chinese yarrow, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of western China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, pale yellow to white flowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in high-altitude environments with well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

This variant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
