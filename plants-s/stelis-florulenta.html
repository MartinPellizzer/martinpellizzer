Stelis florulenta

Stelis florulenta, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, tropical environments. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Stelis florulenta is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or conservatories.
