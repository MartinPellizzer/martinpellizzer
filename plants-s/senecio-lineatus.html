Senecio lineatus

Senecio lineatus, commonly known as the linear senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves that give it its common name, and it produces small yellow flowers arranged in a corymb. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is typically found in rocky or sandy soils. It is often used in rock gardens due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
