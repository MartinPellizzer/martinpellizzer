Smithia blanda var. humilis

Smithia blanda var. humilis, commonly known as the lowland smilacine, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features delicate, trifoliate leaves that are typically light green with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers in umbels, which bloom in early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique addition to woodland gardens and conservation habitats.
