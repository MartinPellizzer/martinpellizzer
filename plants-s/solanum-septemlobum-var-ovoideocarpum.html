Solanum septemlobum var. ovoideocarpum

Solanum septemlobum var. ovoideocarpum, commonly known as the egg-shaped fruit nightshade, is a variant of the seven-lobed nightshade.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its distinctive, elongated, egg-shaped fruits. The plant typically features simple, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades such as tomatoes and potatoes.

This variant is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in humid, shaded environments.
