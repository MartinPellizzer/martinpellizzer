Spilanthes arnicoides var. intermedia

Spilanthes arnicoides var. intermedia, commonly known as the intermediate toothache plant, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the genus Spilanthes within the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant is notable for its small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn.
