Sciadotenia ramiflora

Sciadotenia ramiflora, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a tropical tree native to eastern and southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery red flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has compound leaves with long, slender leaflets that are often arranged in a feathery pattern. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and disturbed habitats.

Sciadotenia ramiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in reforestation projects.
