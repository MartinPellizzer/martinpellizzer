Spathulopetalum mogadoxense

Spathulopetalum mogadoxense, commonly known as the Mogadoxense Spatulate Petal Plant, is a unique species native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive spatulate-shaped petals, which give it its scientific name. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in tropical rainforests. Its leaves are broad and have a waxy texture, aiding in water retention.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a conservation concern.
