Solanum jasminoides var. boerhaviifolium

Solanum jasminoides var. boerhaviifolium, commonly known as the Boerhavia-leaved nightshade, is a variant of the nightshade family (Solanaceae) characterized by its unique foliage.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a distinct, fragrant scent. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, typically blooming in the evening. It is native to tropical regions and thrives in warm, humid climates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and greenhouses.
