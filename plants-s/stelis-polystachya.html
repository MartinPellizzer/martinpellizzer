Stelis polystachya

Stelis polystachya, commonly known as the orchid with multiple flowering stems, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees and deriving moisture and nutrients from the air. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense inflorescences, often displaying a range of colors from white to pink and purple.

Stelis polystachya is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
