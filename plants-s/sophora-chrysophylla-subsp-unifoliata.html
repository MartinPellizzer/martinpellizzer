Sophora chrysophylla subsp. unifoliata

Sophora chrysophylla subsp.

unifoliata, commonly known as the single-leaved golden sophora, is a flowering plant native to China. It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in forested areas and is known for its ornamental value. It has compound leaves with a single leaflet, giving it its subspecies name "unifoliata.".
