Solanum requienii

Solanum requienii, commonly known as the Requien's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and peppers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and have a rough texture, while its flowers are usually yellow with purple stamens.

The fruit is a fleshy berry that turns red when mature, containing several seeds.
