Sisymbrium elegans var. juniperorum

Sisymbrium elegans var. juniperorum, commonly known as juniper hill peppergrass, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and has small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring.
