Silene tuba

Silene tuba, commonly known as the tuberous silene, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its herbaceous stems and clusters of small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces rounded, tuberous roots that store nutrients, aiding in its survival in harsh environments. Silene tuba thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or rocky habitats.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
