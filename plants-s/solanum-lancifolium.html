Solanum lancifolium

Solanum lancifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and have a distinct, pointed tip. Its flowers are usually purple or blue, with five petals and a star-like appearance.

The fruit is a yellow or orange berry that contains several seeds.
