Senecio pinetorum

Senecio pinetorum, commonly known as the pine tree groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, branching stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Senecio pinetorum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
