Scilla festalis

Scilla festalis, commonly known as the spring squill, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It produces clusters of bell-shaped, blue to purple flowers in early spring. The plant has strap-like, grassy leaves that emerge from a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Scilla festalis is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and early blooming habit.
