Scutellaria Albida Subsp. Colchica

Scutellaria albida subsp. colchica is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus. It is known for its striking white blooms and unique ecological adaptations to high-altitude environments. This subspecies thrives in rocky and well-drained soils, often found in alpine meadows and forest edges. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered vulnerable to environmental changes. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this beautiful and ecologically important plant.
