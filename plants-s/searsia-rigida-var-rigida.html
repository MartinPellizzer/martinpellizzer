Searsia rigida var. rigida

Searsia rigida var. rigida, commonly known as the hard thornbush, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny branches and small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces yellowish-white flowers in clusters, which are followed by small, red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing on rocky soils.

Searsia rigida var. rigida is valued for its hardiness and is used in ornamental gardening and for its potential medicinal properties.
