Stemodiacra linearifolia

Stemodiacra linearifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved stemodia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and linear, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant in dry gardens.
