Senecio faberi

Senecio faberi, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to blue. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light. It is a popular houseplant due to its ease of care and ability to bloom throughout the year.

Senecio faberi is also valued for its ornamental appeal and air-purifying properties.
