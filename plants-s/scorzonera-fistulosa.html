Scorzonera fistulosa

Scorzonera fistulosa, commonly known as the tubular scorzonera, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a robust root system with thick, fleshy roots that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces yellow flowers with tubular corollas, typically arranged in clusters at the top of the stems. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Scorzonera fistulosa thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.
