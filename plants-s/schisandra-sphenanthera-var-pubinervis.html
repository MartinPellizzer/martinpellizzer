Schisandra sphenanthera var. pubinervis

Schisandra sphenanthera var. pubinervis, commonly known as the pubinervous five-flower schisandra, is a rare and endangered plant species native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Schisandraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive five-flowered umbels and aromatic berries. The plant has lanceolate leaves with a hairy underside, and its flowers are typically greenish-yellow with five sepals and petals. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest understories.
