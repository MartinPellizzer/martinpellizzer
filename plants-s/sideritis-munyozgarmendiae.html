Sideritis munyozgarmendiae

Sideritis munyozgarmendiae, commonly known as the Munyozgarmen sedge, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Pyrenees.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic foliage. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, arranged in dense spikes. It thrives in rocky and sandy soils, often growing in open habitats with full sun exposure.
