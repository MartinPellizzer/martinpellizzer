Schizoglossum aciculare

Schizoglossum aciculare, commonly known as the needleleaf schizoglossum, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its narrow, needle-like leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Schizoglossum aciculare thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure.

It is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid environments.
