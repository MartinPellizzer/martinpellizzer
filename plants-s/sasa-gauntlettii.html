Sasa gauntlettii

Sasa gauntlettii, commonly known as Gauntlett's bamboo, is a perennial grass-like plant native to China.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its dense, clumping growth habit. The plant features long, slender culms that are often used in traditional crafts and construction. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Sasa gauntlettii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in forest ecosystems.
