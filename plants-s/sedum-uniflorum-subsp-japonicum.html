Sedum uniflorum subsp. japonicum

Sedum uniflorum subsp.

japonicum, commonly known as Japanese stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to East Asia. It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow to orange.

The plant is adapted to rocky and well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Its leaves are typically blue-green in color and have a waxy texture to reduce water loss.

It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
