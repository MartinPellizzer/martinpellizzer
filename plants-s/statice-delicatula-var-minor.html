Statice delicatula var. minor

Statice delicatula var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf statice, is a compact, flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems covered in small, delicate spikes of blue to purple flowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically gray-green in color, giving it a silvery appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Due to its hardiness and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its subtle beauty and ease of care.
