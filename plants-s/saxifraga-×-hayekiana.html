Saxifraga × hayekiana

Saxifraga × hayekiana, commonly known as the hybrid saxifrage, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It typically forms dense mats of foliage with small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats. Its leaves are usually succulent, adapted to retain moisture in harsh environments.

Saxifraga × hayekiana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging garden conditions.
