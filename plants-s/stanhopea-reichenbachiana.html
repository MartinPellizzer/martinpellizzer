Stanhopea reichenbachiana

Stanhopea reichenbachiana, commonly known as the Reichenbach's stanhopea, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect pseudobulbs and elongated, narrow leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow with red and orange markings. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

Stanhopea reichenbachiana is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
