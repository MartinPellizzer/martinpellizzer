Schefflera burchellii

Schefflera burchellii, commonly known as the African umbrella tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, palm-like leaves that are arranged in a compact, umbrella-shaped crown. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Schefflera burchellii is also valued for its ability to tolerate indoor conditions, making it a popular choice for houseplants.
