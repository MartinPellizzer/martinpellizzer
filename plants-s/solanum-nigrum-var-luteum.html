Solanum nigrum var. luteum

Solanum nigrum var. luteum, commonly known as yellow nightshade, is a variant of the common nightshade plant.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, yellow berries that are toxic to humans and animals. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in gardens, roadsides, and waste areas.
