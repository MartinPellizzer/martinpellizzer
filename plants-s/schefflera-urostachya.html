Schefflera urostachya

Schefflera urostachya, commonly known as the mountain schefflera, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of leaves, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers that are followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and indoor plant cultivation.
