Scabiosa olivieri var. pinnatisecta

Scabiosa olivieri var. pinnatisecta, commonly known as the pinnatisect scabious, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like foliage with finely divided leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, pink or white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or borders.
