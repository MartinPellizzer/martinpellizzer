Shuteria pampaniniana

Shuteria pampaniniana, commonly known as the Pampas shuteria, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Argentina.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and shrublands. It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters.
