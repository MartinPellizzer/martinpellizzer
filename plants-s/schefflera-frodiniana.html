Schefflera frodiniana

Schefflera frodiniana, commonly known as the Frodin's umbrella tree, is a tropical plant native to the island of Madagascar.

It is characterized by its large, compound leaves that radiate from a central stem, resembling an umbrella. The plant features a sturdy, woody trunk with a smooth, grayish-brown bark. Its foliage is typically dark green and glossy, providing an attractive appearance.

Schefflera frodiniana is often cultivated as a ornamental plant due to its unique structure and visual appeal.
