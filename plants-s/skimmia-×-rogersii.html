Skimmia × rogersii

Skimmia × rogersii, commonly known as the variegated skimmia, is a hybrid evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with striking white or yellow variegation, adding visual interest to gardens. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in late winter or early spring, which are followed by red berries. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens or as a container plant.

Skimmia × rogersii is valued for its ornamental foliage and winter interest, though it may be sensitive to extreme weather conditions.
