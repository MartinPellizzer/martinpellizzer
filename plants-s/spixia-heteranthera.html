Spixia heteranthera

Spixia heteranthera, commonly known as the Brazilian star orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that display a vibrant combination of purple and white hues. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in the undergrowth of tropical rainforests.
