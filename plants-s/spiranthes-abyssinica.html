Spiranthes abyssinica

Spiranthes abyssinica, commonly known as the Abyssinian spiral orchid, is a species of orchid native to Ethiopia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and its delicate, spiral-shaped inflorescence of white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and grasslands. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is noted for its unique floral structure and pollination mechanisms.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
