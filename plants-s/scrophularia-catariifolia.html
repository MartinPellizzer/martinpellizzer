Scrophularia catariifolia

Scrophularia catariifolia, commonly known as the Sierra Madre scrophularia, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils at high elevations. The plant features upright stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that have a hairy texture. Its flowers are tubular, usually in shades of pink or purple, and bloom in late summer.

Scrophularia catariifolia is part of the Scrophulariaceae family, known for its medicinal and ornamental value in some species.
