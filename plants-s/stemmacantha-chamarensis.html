Stemmacantha chamarensis

Stemmacantha chamarensis, commonly known as the velvetleaf or Brazilian velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in shaded, moist environments.
