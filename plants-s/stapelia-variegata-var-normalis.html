Stapelia variegata var. normalis

Stapelia variegata var. normalis, commonly known as the Variegated Stapelia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems that are often tinged with green and white variegation. The plant produces striking, hooded flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
