Synotis yui

Synotis yui, commonly known as the silver leaf vine, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the warmer months. It thrives in humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Synotis yui is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying properties.
