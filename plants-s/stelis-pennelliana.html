Stelis pennelliana

Stelis pennelliana, commonly known as the orchid vine, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a climbing epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia, including regions such as Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant features long, wiry stems that support its pendant, showy flowers. Its leaves are typically ovate to lanceolate, with a waxy surface that aids in moisture retention.

The flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with a distinctive, spurred lip that attracts pollinators.
