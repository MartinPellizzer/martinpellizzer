Succisa pratensis var. ovalis

Succisa pratensis var. ovalis, commonly known as the oval-leaved mouse-ear.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe, characterized by its rosette of oval, hairy leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a spike, typically blooming in early summer. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.
