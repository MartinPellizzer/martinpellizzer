Sempervivum × christii

Sempervivum × christii, commonly known as the Christmas houseplant or firecrest, is a hybrid succulent belonging to the Crassulaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, rosette-shaped growth pattern with fleshy, thick leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white powder. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of Central and Southern Europe, where it thrives in rocky and well-drained soil. Its flowers, which appear in spring, are typically pink or red and are borne on tall, slender stems.

Sempervivum × christii is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that is well-suited for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
