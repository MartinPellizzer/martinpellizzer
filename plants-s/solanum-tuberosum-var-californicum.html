Solanum tuberosum var. californicum

Solanum tuberosum var. californicum, commonly known as the California potato, is a variant of the common potato native to California.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant that produces tubers underground, which are its primary edible part. The plant has hairy stems and broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slight purple tinge. It thrives in cool, moist climates and is adapted to the specific environmental conditions of California.

This variety is valued for its unique flavor and texture, making it a specialty crop in local agriculture.
