Sideritis spicata

Sideritis spicata, commonly known as spike heath, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its upright, branched stems and small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Sideritis spicata thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in dry, sunny environments.

Its leaves are narrow and hairy, and it is known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
