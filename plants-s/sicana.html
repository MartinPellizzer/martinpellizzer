Sicana

Sicana, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and chrysanthemums. The plant features showy, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center, often blooming in summer. Sicana is typically found in open grasslands and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soil.

It is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
