Swartzia cardiosperma

Swartzia cardiosperma, commonly known as the heartseed tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The tree produces distinctive seed pods that enclose hard, seed-like structures, giving it its common name. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional uses in local cultures.

The plant thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.
