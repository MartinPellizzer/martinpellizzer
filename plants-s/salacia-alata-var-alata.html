Salacia alata var. alata

Salacia alata var. alata, commonly known as the winged salacia, is a climbing shrub native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive winged stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in racemes. Its fruit is a fleshy, red berry containing several seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
