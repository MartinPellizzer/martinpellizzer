Stevia lucida var. opaca

Stevia lucida var. opaca, commonly known as the silver leaf stevia, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-white undersides on its leaves, which give it a distinct appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. It is known for its high concentration of steviol glycosides, making it a popular natural sweetener.

Stevia lucida var. opaca thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for cultivation in temperate climates.
