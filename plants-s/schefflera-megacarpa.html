Schefflera megacarpa

Schefflera megacarpa, commonly known as the giant umbrella plant, is a member of the Araliaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, umbrella-like compound leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in diameter. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in umbels, followed by bright red, berry-like fruits. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates.

Its striking foliage makes it a popular ornamental plant in both indoor and outdoor settings.
