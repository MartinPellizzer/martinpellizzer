Sciuris odoratissima

Sciuris odoratissima, commonly known as the sweet-scented orchid, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its fragrant, showy flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and dark green, and it produces small, white or pale yellow flowers with a strong, sweet fragrance.

Sciuris odoratissima is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its aromatic properties.
