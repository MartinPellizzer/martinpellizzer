Senecio nemiae

Senecio nemiae, commonly known as the Cape snowflake, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its delicate, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. Senecio nemiae is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and ability to thrive in dry conditions.
