Sarcochilus centipeda

Sarcochilus centipeda, commonly known as the centipede orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to New Zealand.

It features a rhizomatous growth habit with a cluster of fleshy, dark green leaves. The plant produces a single, erect stem that bears several pale yellow to greenish-white flowers. Its flowers have a distinctive, intricate structure with prominent sepals and petals.

Sarcochilus centipeda is adapted to grow in moist, shaded environments, often in forest undergrowth.
