Schnella obovata

Schnella obovata, commonly known as the broad-ovate schnella, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are typically hairy and have a rough texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid tropical regions.

Schnella obovata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
