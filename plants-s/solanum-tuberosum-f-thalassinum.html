Solanum tuberosum f. thalassinum

Solanum tuberosum f. thalassinum, commonly known as the purple potato, is a botanical variant of the common potato.

It is characterized by its striking violet or purple foliage and tubers, which are often more intensely colored than standard potato varieties. This form exhibits a unique leaf morphology with a bluish-green hue and a velvety texture. The plant belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is native to the Andes region of South America.

Its ornamental value has made it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections.
