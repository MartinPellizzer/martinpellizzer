Sideritis leucantha

Sideritis leucantha, commonly known as white heath, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are typically narrow and gray-green, with a slightly fuzzy texture.

Sideritis leucantha is often found in rocky or dry habitats and is valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
