Stenoglottis calcarata

Stenoglottis calcarata, commonly known as the rock lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a distinctive red or purple throat. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, preferring well-drained environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is often found in dry, open habitats.

Stenoglottis calcarata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
