Senecio cineraria var. plattii

Senecio cineraria var. plattii, commonly known as the Blueweed or Dusty Miller, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray, velvety foliage and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a favored ornamental plant.
