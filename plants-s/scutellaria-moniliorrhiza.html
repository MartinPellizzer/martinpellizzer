Scutellaria moniliorrhiza

Scutellaria moniliorrhiza, commonly known as Chinese violet or Indian violet, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or blue in color. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions.

Scutellaria moniliorrhiza is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
