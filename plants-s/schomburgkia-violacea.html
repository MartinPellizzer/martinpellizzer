Schomburgkia violacea

Schomburgkia violacea, commonly known as the violet orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking violet-blue flowers, which are typically clustered in racemes. The plant has strap-like leaves that are often dark green with a waxy texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Schomburgkia violacea is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
