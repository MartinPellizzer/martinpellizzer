Smilax megalophylla

Smilax megalophylla, commonly known as the giant arrowleaf smilax, is a perennial vine native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 12 inches in length. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by red, berry-like fruits. Smilax megalophylla is a woody plant with a strong, thorny stem that can climb or spread along the ground.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
