Sulcorebutia breviflora subsp. laui

Sulcorebutia breviflora subsp.

laui, commonly known as the Laui's short-flowered crassula, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to southern Africa. It features dense clusters of fleshy, spoon-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green with a subtle bluish tinge.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the summer months, which are borne on short, erect stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
