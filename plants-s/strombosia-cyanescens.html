Strombosia cyanescens

Strombosia cyanescens, commonly known as the blue-stemmed orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue-green stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom in clusters along the stems. Strombosia cyanescens thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a sought-after species in horticulture.
