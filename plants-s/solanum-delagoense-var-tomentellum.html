Solanum delagoense var. tomentellum

Solanum delagoense var. tomentellum, commonly known as the Delagoense nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant is characterized by its hairy leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a berry that turns from green to red as it matures.

This species is often found in woodland and savanna habitats, where it thrives in partial shade.
