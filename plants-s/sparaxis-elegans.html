Sparaxis elegans

Sparaxis elegans, commonly known as the "Rainbow Orchid," is a bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, tubular flowers that come in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often with a distinctive purple throat. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces underground bulbs that store nutrients for the next growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Sparaxis elegans is known for its vibrant blooms and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
