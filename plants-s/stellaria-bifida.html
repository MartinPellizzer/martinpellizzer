Stellaria bifida

Stellaria bifida, commonly known as the narrowleaf starwort, is a flowering plant native to Eurasia and North America.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like the common chickweed. The plant is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy surface.

Stellaria bifida is typically found in moist, disturbed soils such as roadsides, fields, and streambanks.
