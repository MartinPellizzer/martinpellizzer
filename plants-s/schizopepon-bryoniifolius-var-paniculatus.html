Schizopepon bryoniifolius var. paniculatus

Schizopepon bryoniifolius var. paniculatus, commonly known as the paniculate bitter melon, is a variant of the bitter melon species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a climbing vine with heart-shaped leaves and produces yellow-orange fruits that are typically harvested when immature. The plant belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes other cucurbits like cucumbers and pumpkins. Its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the same plant, and it relies on insects for pollination.
