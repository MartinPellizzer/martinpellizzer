Spiraea nipponica f. typica

Spiraea nipponica f. typica, commonly known as Japanese weeping spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features slender, arching branches that bear clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers in early summer. The foliage is typically dark green, providing an attractive contrast to the floral display. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
