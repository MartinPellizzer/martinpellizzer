Sulcorebutia taratensis

Sulcorebutia taratensis, commonly known as the Taratensis Cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular shape and dense covering of spines. The plant typically features small, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of yellow or orange, often during the warmer months. Its adaptations include thick, fleshy stems for water storage and a shallow root system to absorb moisture from the soil.

Sulcorebutia taratensis is well-suited for cultivation in dry, well-drained environments.
