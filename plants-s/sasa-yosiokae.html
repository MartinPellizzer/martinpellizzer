Sasa yosiokae

Sasa yosiokae, commonly known as the Yosioka bamboo, is a species of grass-like plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its dense, clumping growth habit. The plant features slender, hollow stems that are often used in traditional Japanese crafts. Its leaves are narrow and grassy, with a fine texture that adds to its ornamental appeal.

Sasa yosiokae thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.
