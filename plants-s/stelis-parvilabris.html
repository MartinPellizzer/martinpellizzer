Stelis parvilabris

Stelis parvilabris, commonly known as the small-lipped stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is an epiphytic orchid, typically growing on trees or rocks in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers with a distinctive lip. It thrives in humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts seeking a compact and ornamental species.
