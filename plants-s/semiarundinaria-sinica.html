Semiarundinaria sinica

Semiarundinaria sinica, commonly known as the Chinese bamboo or Chinese reed, is a member of the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, grass-like leaves. This plant is native to China and is often found in moist, shady environments. It has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread vegetatively.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental and ecological landscaping.
