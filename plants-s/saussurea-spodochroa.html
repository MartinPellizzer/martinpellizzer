Saussurea spodochroa

Saussurea spodochroa, commonly known as the alpine heliotrope, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, including the Himalayas and the Tibetan Plateau. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, yellowish to reddish flowers. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and well-drained soils.

Due to its ecological significance and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in alpine gardens and botanical collections.
