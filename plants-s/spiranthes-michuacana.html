Spiranthes michuacana

Spiranthes michuacana, commonly known as the Michuacan spiral orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Mexico.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces intricate, spiral-shaped inflorescences with small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories or along stream banks. Its roots are fibrous and adapted for nutrient absorption in acidic soils.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in specialty gardens.
