Sprekelia ringens

Sprekelia ringens, commonly known as the golden trumpet, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, with a distinctive red or orange throat. The plant produces a tall, erect stem that can reach up to 2 meters in height, adorned with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowering period occurs in late winter to early spring, making it a notable addition to gardens in mild climates.

Sprekelia ringens is well-suited for rock gardens or containers due to its compact growth habit and ornamental value.
