Sulcorebutia menesesii var. kamiensis

Sulcorebutia menesesii var. kamiensis, commonly known as the Kamien Mountain crested cactus, is a rare and distinctive species native to the high-altitude regions of central China.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and spiny, ribbed stems that often develop a crested or fasciated growth pattern. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking contrast to its green foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly sought after by cactus enthusiasts and collectors.
