Spiraea japonica f. fortunei

Spiraea japonica f. fortunei, commonly known as the Fortune's spirea, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in dense, flat clusters in early summer. The plant has slender, arching stems and dark green, ovate leaves that turn reddish in the fall. It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.

Spiraea japonica f. fortunei prefers partial to full sun and is relatively low maintenance once established.
