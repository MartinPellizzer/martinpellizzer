Spiraea mexicana

Spiraea mexicana, commonly known as Mexican spirea, is a shrub native to the United States and Mexico.

It features small, white to pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, arching stems and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Spiraea mexicana is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
