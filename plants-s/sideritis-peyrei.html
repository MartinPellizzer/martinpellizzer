Sideritis peyrei

Sideritis peyrei, commonly known as Peyre's rockrose, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, thriving in sunny, well-drained environments. Its small, tubular flowers are usually pink or purple, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds.

Sideritis peyrei is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
