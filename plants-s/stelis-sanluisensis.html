Stelis sanluisensis

Stelis sanluisensis, commonly known as the San Luis orchid, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, white or pale pink blossoms that bloom during the spring and early summer. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Stelis sanluisensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
