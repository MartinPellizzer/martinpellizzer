Schizachyrium yangambiense

Schizachyrium yangambiense, commonly known as the Yangambi grass, is a tall, perennial grass native to central Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, feathery flowers. It thrives in open, sunny habitats and is adapted to well-drained soils.

Schizachyrium yangambiense is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential use in ecological restoration projects.
