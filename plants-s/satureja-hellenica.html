Satureja hellenica

Satureja hellenica, commonly known as Greek thyme, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb with small, aromatic leaves and pink or white flowers. The plant belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes other aromatic herbs like mint and oregano. Its leaves are used in cooking and traditional medicine for their antiseptic and anti-inflammatory properties.

Satureja hellenica thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for Mediterranean climates.
