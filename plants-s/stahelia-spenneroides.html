Stahelia spenneroides

Stahelia spenneroides, commonly known as the silverleaf stahelia, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial plant characterized by its silvery-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in compact clusters, which bloom in the summer months. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in well-drained, rocky soils.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in xeriscaped gardens.
