Santolina terrestris

Santolina terrestris, commonly known as common santolina, is a low-growing, evergreen perennial native to southern Europe.

It features dense, silver-gray foliage with a strong, aromatic scent. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact size and ornamental leaves make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
