Solanum dasyphyllum var. natalense

Solanum dasyphyllum var. natalense, commonly known as Natal nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically yellow or orange when ripe. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland and coastal areas.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
