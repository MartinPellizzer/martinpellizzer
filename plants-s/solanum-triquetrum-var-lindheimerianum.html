Solanum triquetrum var. lindheimerianum

Solanum triquetrum var. lindheimerianum, commonly known as Lindheimer's nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades, tomatoes, and potatoes. The plant features small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, followed by dark blue to black berries. Its leaves are typically ovate with a slightly hairy texture.

It is often found in rocky soils and can thrive in arid environments.
