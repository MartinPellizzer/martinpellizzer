Stellaria ruscifolia

Stellaria ruscifolia, commonly known as the leafy starwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, white flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in early summer. The plant has thin, branched stems and leaves that are lance-shaped with a hairy texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream sides.

Stellaria ruscifolia is often found in association with other shade-tolerant flora in temperate regions.
