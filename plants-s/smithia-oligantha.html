Smithia oligantha

Smithia oligantha, commonly known as the slender leaved sandwort, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with small, fleshy leaves that help it conserve water in its harsh environment. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white or pale pink flowers during the spring and early summer. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to access moisture from deeper soil layers.

Smithia oligantha is well adapted to sandy and rocky soils, making it a common sight in desert landscapes.
