Straussia oncocarpa

Straussia oncocarpa, commonly known as the red-spored polypore, is a species of bracket fungus that belongs to the family Trametes.

It is characterized by its distinctive reddish-brown pores and a tough, leathery cap that often turns darker with age. Its spore-producing surface is smooth and remains exposed, facilitating spore dispersal.

Straussia oncocarpa plays a role in the decomposition of wood, contributing to nutrient cycling in forest ecosystems.
