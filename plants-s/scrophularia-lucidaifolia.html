Scrophularia lucidaifolia

Scrophularia lucidaifolia, commonly known as the glossy-leaved scrophularia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in woodland edges and stream banks. The plant features dark green, glossy, ovate leaves that are arranged oppositely along the stem. Its flowers are tubular and usually pink or purple, appearing in clusters during the summer months.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
