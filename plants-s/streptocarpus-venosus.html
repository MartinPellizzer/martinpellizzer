Streptocarpus venosus

Streptocarpus venosus, commonly known as the veined streptocarpus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves with prominent veins. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial shade.
