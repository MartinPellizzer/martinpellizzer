Sophora Japonica F. Pendula

Sophora japonica f. pendula is a striking ornamental tree known for its cascading, weeping foliage. Native to China, this variety of the Chinese scholar tree is prized for its unique, drooping branches that create a soft, elegant appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. The tree produces small, fragrant flowers in the spring, adding to its visual appeal. Its graceful form and adaptability make it a favorite among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts alike.
