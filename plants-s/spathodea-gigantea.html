Spathodea gigantea

Spathodea gigantea, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a tropical tree native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy bracts that resemble petals, which are actually modified leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a velvety texture.

The African flame tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often planted in gardens and parks for its striking appearance.
