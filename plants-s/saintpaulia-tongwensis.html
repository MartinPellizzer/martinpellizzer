Saintpaulia tongwensis

Saintpaulia tongwensis, commonly known as the Tongwens Island African violet, is a species native to the tropical rainforests of Tanzania.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, velvety foliage. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively rare occurrence in the wild, it is highly valued by botanists and horticultural enthusiasts.
