Specklinia fluminensis

Specklinia fluminensis, commonly known as the Rio de Janeiro fern, is a species of polypodioid fern native to Brazil.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in tropical rainforests. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce numerous small, lanceolate leaves.

Specklinia fluminensis is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
