Sicyos microcarpus var. kealiaensis

Sicyos microcarpus var. kealiaensis, commonly known as the Kealia bottle gourd, is a variant of the bottle gourd native to Hawaii.

This climbing vine produces large, smooth, bottle-shaped fruits that can grow up to 2 meters in length. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces yellow, fragrant flowers. It thrives in warm, tropical climates and requires well-drained soil.

The Kealia bottle gourd is valued for its culinary uses and traditional significance in Hawaiian culture.
