Senecio prionopterus

Senecio prionopterus, commonly known as the silver leaf senecio, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa, where it thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which helps it retain moisture in harsh conditions. Its flowers are typically small and yellow, arranged in daisy-like clusters.

Senecio prionopterus is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
