Sideritis virgata

Sideritis virgata, commonly known as mountain tea, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, pale blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Sideritis virgata is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
