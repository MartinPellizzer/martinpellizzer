Selaginella llanosii

Selaginella llanosii, commonly known as the llano spikemoss, is a member of the Selaginellaceae family and is native to South America.

It is a small, evergreen fern-like plant that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant has a distinct appearance with needle-like leaves that form dense, mat-like structures. Selaginella llanosii is known for its ability to thrive in humid conditions and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and ecological study.
