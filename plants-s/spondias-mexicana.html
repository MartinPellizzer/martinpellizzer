Spondias mexicana

Spondias mexicana, commonly known as the Mexican hog plum, is a small to medium-sized tree native to Central America and northern South America.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and oval-shaped leaves. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into edible, plum-like fruits with a sweet and tangy flavor.
