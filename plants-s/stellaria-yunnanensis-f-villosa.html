Stellaria yunnanensis f. villosa

Stellaria yunnanensis f. villosa, commonly known as the hairy starwort, is a flowering plant species belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the spring and early summer. The plant features hairy stems and leaves, which give it its specific epithet "villosa.". Native to regions in China, it thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky slopes.

Its botanical structure makes it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
