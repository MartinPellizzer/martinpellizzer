Stelis velaticaulis

Stelis velaticaulis, commonly known as the velvety-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems that are often covered in a soft, velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in tropical rainforests.

Stelis velaticaulis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance.
