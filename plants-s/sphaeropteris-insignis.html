Sphaeropteris insignis

Sphaeropteris insignis, commonly known as the giant cycad, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, feather-like leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces large, cone-like structures that contain both male and female reproductive organs. It thrives in humid, tropical climates with well-drained soil.

Due to its slow growth and long lifespan, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
