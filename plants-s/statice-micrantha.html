Statice micrantha

Statice micrantha, commonly known as sea lavender, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean and the eastern Mediterranean.

It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its compact, spiky growth habit and small, showy flowers. The plant thrives in salty, well-drained soils and is often found in dunes and salt marshes. Its flowers are typically pink or purple, and they bloom from late summer into early autumn.

Statice micrantha is often used in dried flower arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms and ornamental value.
