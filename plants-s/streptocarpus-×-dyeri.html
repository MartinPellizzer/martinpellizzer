Streptocarpus × dyeri

Streptocarpus × dyeri, commonly known as the hybrid African violet, is a hybrid plant resulting from crosses between Streptocarpus species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and clusters of showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to deep purple. The plant typically grows as a compact rosette, with leaves that are broad and often covered in a fine, fuzzy layer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This hybrid is popular among indoor gardeners for its ornamental value and ease of care.
