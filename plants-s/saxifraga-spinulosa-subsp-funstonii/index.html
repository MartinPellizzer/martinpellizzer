Saxifraga Spinulosa Subsp. Funstonii

Saxifraga spinulosa subsp. funstonii is a rare and striking plant native to the southwestern united states. it belongs to the saxifragaceae family and is known for its unique, spiky foliage. this subspecies is particularly adapted to rocky and arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils. its narrow, spinose leaves give it a distinctive appearance that sets it apart from other saxifrage species. due to its limited distribution, it is considered a valuable and vulnerable plant in its native habitat.
