Senna nomame

Senna nomame, commonly known as African senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters tall, characterized by its compound leaves and bright yellow flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Senna nomame is also valued for its medicinal properties and use in traditional herbal remedies.
