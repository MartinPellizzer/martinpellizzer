Scorzonera jacquiniana var. alpina

Scorzonera jacquiniana var. alpina, commonly known as the alpine scorzonera, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

Scorzonera jacquiniana var. alpina is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in alpine ecosystems.
