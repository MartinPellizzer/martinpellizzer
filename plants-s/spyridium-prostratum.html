Spyridium prostratum

Spyridium prostratum, commonly known as the prostrate spyridium, is a low-growing shrub native to southern Australia.

It features small, rounded leaves that are typically covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces clusters of tiny, yellowish-white flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and open woodlands.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
