Solanum simplicifolium var. mollifrons

Solanum simplicifolium var. mollifrons, commonly known as the soft-leaved nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It features ovate, softly hairy leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to violet, followed by berries that mature to a dark purple or black. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
