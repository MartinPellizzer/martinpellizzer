Sideritis × carrissoana

Sideritis × carrissoana, commonly known as the hybrid sainfoin, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, arranged in dense spikes. Sideritis × carrissoana is often found in rocky or sandy soils, thriving in sunny to partially shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it a valuable plant for both ecological and horticultural purposes.
