Stelis delhierroi

Stelis delhierroi, commonly known as the Delhierroi orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on the surface of other plants without being parasitic. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Stelis delhierroi is prized for its elegant appearance and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.
