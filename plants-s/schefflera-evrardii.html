Schefflera evrardii

Schefflera evrardii, commonly known as the African arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, palm-like leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by red, fleshy berries that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often grown as an ornamental plant.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
