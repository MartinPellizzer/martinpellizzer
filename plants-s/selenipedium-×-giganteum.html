Selenipedium × giganteum

Selenipedium × giganteum, commonly known as the giant spider-orchid, is a hybrid orchid native to the tropical rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that can reach several feet in height, featuring intricate, spider-like flowers in shades of yellow and green. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil to flourish. Its growth is slow, and it can take many years to reach maturity, making it a rare and valuable species in horticulture.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, conservation efforts are essential to protect this unique botanical wonder.
