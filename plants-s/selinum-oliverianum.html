Selinum oliverianum

Selinum oliverianum, commonly known as the Spanish flatvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in scrubland and grassland habitats. Its small, yellow flowers bloom in clusters and attract pollinators such as bees.

Selinum oliverianum is adapted to arid conditions and is often used in ecological restoration projects.
