Sideritis akmanii

Sideritis akmanii, commonly known as the Akmanii ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. Sideritis akmanii typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.

Its leaves are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance and aiding in water conservation.
