Senecio lijiangensis

Senecio lijiangensis, commonly known as the Yunnan Senecio, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically features slender, branching stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually yellow, with a central disc surrounded by ray florets.

Senecio lijiangensis is often found in rocky or alpine habitats, where it thrives in well-drained soils and cool climates.
