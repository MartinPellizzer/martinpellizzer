Sokinochloa perrieri

Sokinochloa perrieri, commonly known as the Perrier's grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family, Poaceae.

It is native to Madagascar and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit with narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
