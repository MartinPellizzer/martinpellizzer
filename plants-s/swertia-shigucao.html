Swertia shigucao

Swertia shigucao, commonly known as Chinese gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its roots are often used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.

Swertia shigucao is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its medicinal properties.
