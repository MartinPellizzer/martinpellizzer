Silene Himalayensis

Silene himalayensis is a rare flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas. It thrives in cold, rocky environments where few other species can survive. This plant is known for its striking pink or purple flowers that bloom during the short summer season. Its unique adaptations make it an important subject for botanical research. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this delicate species from habitat loss.
