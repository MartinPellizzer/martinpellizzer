Setulocarya

Setulocarya, commonly known as the spiky leaf plant, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, serrated or spiny leaves that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, preferring moist, well-drained soils. Setulocarya is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
