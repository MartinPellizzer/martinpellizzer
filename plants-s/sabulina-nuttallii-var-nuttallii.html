Sabulina nuttallii var. nuttallii

Sabulina nuttallii var. nuttallii, commonly known as Nuttall's sandwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the western United States.

It typically thrives in sandy or gravelly soils, often found in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features small, clustered leaves and produces clusters of tiny, white or pale pink flowers. It is characterized by its succulent stems and is well adapted to drought conditions.

Its botanical structure reflects its ability to survive in nutrient-poor, well-drained habitats.
