Schizachyrium bootanense

Schizachyrium bootanense, commonly known as little bluestem, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage that turns coppery in the fall. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and woodlands. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bloom in late summer.

Schizachyrium bootanense is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for wildlife.
