Sicyos aculeatus

Sicyos aculeatus, commonly known as the spiny leaf gourd, is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is a trailing or climbing vine characterized by its hairy stems and heart-shaped, spiny leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that give way to elongated, spiny fruits. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

Sicyos aculeatus is often cultivated for its edible fruits, which have a mild, slightly sweet flavor.
