Sideritis hirsuta var. provincialis

Sideritis hirsuta var. provincialis, commonly known as the Provincial Hairy Sideritis, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its hairy leaves and small, pale blue to violet flowers are adaptations to its arid environment.

It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and calming properties.
