Stemona japonica

Stemona japonica, commonly known as Japanese stemona, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features a slender, creeping stem that often grows along the ground or in dense vegetation. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in umbels. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly rough texture.

The roots of Stemona japonica are valued in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
