Stolzia repens var. obtusa

Stolzia repens var. obtusa, commonly known as the broad-leaved stolzia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features prostrate growth habit with fleshy, oval leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers with prominent yellow stamens. It thrives in well-drained sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is cultivated in gardens in mild climates.
