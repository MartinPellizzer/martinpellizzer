Scutellaria strigillosa var. yezoensis

Scutellaria strigillosa var. yezoensis, commonly known as Yezo skullcap, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky areas. It has hairy leaves and stems, which contribute to its common name.

This variant is often found in the Yezo region, which includes parts of Hokkaido.
