Syringa Amurensis Var. Mandshurica

Syringa amurensis var. mandshurica is a hardy shrub native to the northeastern regions of Asia. It is known for its fragrant, clusters of white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring. This variety is often favored for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil conditions. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is relatively low maintenance. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
