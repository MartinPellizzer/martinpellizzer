Statice ovalifolia var. pyramidata

Statice ovalifolia var. pyramidata, commonly known as the pyramid statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, spike-like inflorescence composed of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its drought-resistant nature. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

The flowers bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
