Sabicea orientalis

Sabicea orientalis, commonly known as the oriental sabicea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered, often in dense inflorescences, and are typically white or pale yellow in color.

Sabicea orientalis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
