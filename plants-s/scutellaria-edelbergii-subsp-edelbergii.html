Scutellaria edelbergii subsp. edelbergii

Scutellaria edelbergii subsp.

edelbergii, commonly known as the Edelberg's skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

The plant features slender, upright stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are blue to violet in color, forming whorled clusters at the ends of the stems.

This species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
