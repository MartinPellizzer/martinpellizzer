Schefflera chinensis

Schefflera chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese umbrella tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, umbrella-like compound leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in diameter. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by bright red berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Schefflera chinensis is also valued for its air-purifying properties and adaptability to indoor conditions.
