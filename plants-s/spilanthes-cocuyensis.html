Spilanthes cocuyensis

Spilanthes cocuyensis, commonly known as the Cocuy Mountain cactus, is a species of flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Mexico.

It belongs to the genus Spilanthes, which includes various species of cacti characterized by their spiny stems and showy flowers. This particular species is known for its yellowish to orange flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant typically grows in rocky and arid environments, adapting to harsh climatic conditions.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies in its native habitat.
