Saponaria ocymoides var. ruvenae

Saponaria ocymoides var. ruvenae, commonly known as the Ruvena carnation, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of small, showy pink or white flowers resembling carnations. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long flowering period, which extends from spring to early summer.
