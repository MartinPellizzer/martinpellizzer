Solanum kraussianum

Solanum kraussianum, commonly known as the Krussia nightshade, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its hairy, glandular stems and leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into round, fleshy berries. These berries are typically orange to red in color and contain several seeds.

Solanum kraussianum is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
