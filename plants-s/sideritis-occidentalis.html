Sideritis occidentalis

Sideritis occidentalis, commonly known as western ironweed, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants known for their medicinal properties. The plant features erect, branching stems covered in fine hairs and produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly aromatic scent when crushed.

Sideritis occidentalis is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is valued for its potential traditional medicinal uses.
