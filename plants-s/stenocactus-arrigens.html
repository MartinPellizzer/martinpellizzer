Stenocactus arrigens

Stenocactus arrigens, commonly known as the Desert Candelabra, is a species of cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stem that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Its leaves are reduced to spines, which help reduce water loss in its harsh environment.

Stenocactus arrigens is well adapted to extreme drought conditions, making it a resilient plant in desert ecosystems.
