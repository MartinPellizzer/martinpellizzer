Solanum tridynamum var. anoplocladum

Solanum tridynamum var. anoplocladum, commonly known as the smooth-leaved nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are typically smooth and oval in shape. Its flowers are usually yellow or white, with five petals, and it produces small, toxic berries that are often used in traditional medicine.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
