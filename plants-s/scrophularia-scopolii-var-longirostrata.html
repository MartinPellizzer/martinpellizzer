Scrophularia scopolii var. longirostrata

Scrophularia scopolii var. longirostrata, commonly known as the long-beaked scrophularia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It typically grows in rocky or calcareous soils and is found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges. The plant features slender stems that are often branched and covered with fine hairs. Its flowers are tubular and usually pink or purplish, with a distinctive elongated corolla that gives it its common name.

It is part of the Scrophulariaceae family, known for its medicinal and ornamental value in traditional herbal practices.
