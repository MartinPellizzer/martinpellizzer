Schefflera delavayi var. pubinervis

Schefflera delavayi var. pubinervis, commonly known as the pubescent nerve-veined schefflera, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that are typically lobed and have a glossy surface. The leaves often exhibit a distinctive pubescent (hairy) texture on the veins, giving it its varietal name. It produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by bright red berries.

This plant is often cultivated as an ornamental due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
