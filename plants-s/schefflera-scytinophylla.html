Schefflera scytinophylla

Schefflera scytinophylla, commonly known as the New Caledonian schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the island of New Caledonia.

It features a distinctive, umbrella-like crown composed of multiple leaflets arranged in a whorled pattern. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves that are typically ovate to elliptic in shape. It produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red, fleshy berries that are edible for birds.

Schefflera scytinophylla is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor conditions.
