Sium cicutifolium var. lineare

Sium cicutifolium var. lineare, commonly known as the slender waterparsley, is a herbaceous plant native to moist, temperate regions.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbel-like flower clusters. The plant typically grows in wetlands, stream banks, and other waterlogged habitats. The species is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and supporting pollinator populations.
