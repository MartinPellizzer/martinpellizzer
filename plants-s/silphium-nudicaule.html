Silphium nudicaule

Silphium nudicaule, commonly known as the slender silphium, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its tall, branching stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It is considered a rare and endangered species due to habitat loss and over-collection.
