Stelis odontopetala

Stelis odontopetala, commonly known as the toothed petal stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often fragrant flowers with elongated, tooth-like petals. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its roots form a symbiotic relationship with mycorrhizal fungi, aiding in nutrient absorption.

Stelis odontopetala is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
