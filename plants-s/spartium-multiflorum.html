Spartium multiflorum

Spartium multiflorum, commonly known as redstem filaree, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the western United States.

It features slender, branching stems that are often reddish in color, and it produces small, white or pinkish flowers in dense clusters. The plant has small, lance-shaped leaves that are hairy and arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils.

Spartium multiflorum is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
