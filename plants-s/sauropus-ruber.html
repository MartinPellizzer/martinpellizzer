Sauropus ruber

Sauropus ruber, commonly known as the red-leaved sauropus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange leaves, which are often variegated with green and yellow tones. The plant has a shrub-like growth habit and can reach heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a rough texture.

Sauropus ruber is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
