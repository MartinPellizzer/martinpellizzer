Satyrium compactum

Satyrium compactum, commonly known as the dwarf orchid or small orchid, is a species of orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a small, terrestrial herb with a short stem and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, often with a greenish tinge. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its small size and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and delicate species.
