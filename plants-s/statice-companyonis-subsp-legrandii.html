Statice companyonis subsp. legrandii

Statice companyonis subsp.

legrandii, commonly known as the Algerian statice, is a flowering plant native to North Africa. It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit.

The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and purple, often arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
