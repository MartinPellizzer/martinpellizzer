Sedum durisii

Sedum durisii, commonly known as the Desert Stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of North America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in dry conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy choice for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its resilience and low maintenance make it a popular plant among gardeners in desert and semi-arid climates.
