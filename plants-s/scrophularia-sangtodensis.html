Scrophularia sangtodensis

Scrophularia sangtodensis, commonly known as the Sangtoudong Scrophularia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae, which includes many species with medicinal and ornamental value. The plant typically features upright stems with opposite leaves that are often lance-shaped and hairy. Its flowers are usually tubular or bell-shaped, with a distinctive two-lipped structure, and are often pink or purple in color.

Scrophularia sangtodensis is valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
