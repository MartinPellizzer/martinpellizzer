Senecio densiflorus

Senecio densiflorus, commonly known as the denseflower senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. It produces small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a dense appearance.

Senecio densiflorus is often used in rock gardens due to its ornamental value and hardy nature.
