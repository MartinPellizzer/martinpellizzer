Stachys recta var. fragilis

Stachys recta var. fragilis, commonly known as slender betony, is a flowering plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright, herbaceous growth habit and hairy stems. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its fragility in the foliage gives it the varietal name "fragilis.".
