Scandix bulbosa

Scandix bulbosa, commonly known as the bulbous scurvygrass, is a flowering plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, sandy or rocky soils. The plant features a short, stocky stem with several narrow, grass-like leaves. Its small, white or pale blue flowers are clustered in a loose inflorescence.

Scandix bulbosa is adapted to cold climates and is often found in tundra and alpine environments.
