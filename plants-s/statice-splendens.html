Statice splendens

Statice splendens, commonly known as sea lavender, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces spikes of blue to purple flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its salt tolerance allows it to flourish in saline environments.

Statice splendens is often used in floral arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms and vibrant color.
