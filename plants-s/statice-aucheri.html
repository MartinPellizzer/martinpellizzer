Statice aucheri

Statice aucheri, commonly known as sea lavender, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and its showy spikes of blue, pink, or purple flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its leaves are narrow and gray-green, adding texture to its ornamental display.

Statice aucheri is also valued for its ability to tolerate salty air and harsh coastal conditions.
