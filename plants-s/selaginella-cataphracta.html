Selaginella cataphracta

Selaginella cataphracta, commonly known as the rock spikemoss, is a species of spore-bearing plant belonging to the family Selaginellaceae.

It is a small, evergreen plant that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid regions. The plant has a distinctive appearance with slender, grass-like leaves and a creeping stem system that allows it to spread across the ground.
