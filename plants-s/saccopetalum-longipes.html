Saccopetalum longipes

Saccopetalum longipes, commonly known as the longipes hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a yellow throat. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a rough texture with a distinctive scent.

Saccopetalum longipes thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and is often found in areas with high saline exposure.
