Stelis torrenticola

Stelis torrenticola, commonly known as the torrent orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rocky areas and stream banks. The plant has a short, rhizomatous stem with a few erect, linear leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered, often in a raceme, with a pale green to yellowish coloration.

Stelis torrenticola is adapted to high-altitude regions and is found primarily in tropical and subtropical areas.
