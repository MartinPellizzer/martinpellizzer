Schefflera cajambrensis

Schefflera cajambrensis, commonly known as the Bornean umbrella plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It is characterized by its distinctive, umbrella-like arrangement of large, leathery leaves that give it a striking appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and has a compact, bushy growth habit. Its leaves are usually dark green with a glossy surface and can reach lengths of up to 30 centimeters.

Schefflera cajambrensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
