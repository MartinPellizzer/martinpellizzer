Scutellaria ovata var. versicolor

Scutellaria ovata var. versicolor, commonly known as the variable skullcap, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple, depending on the variety. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or wetland habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
