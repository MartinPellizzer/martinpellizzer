Schefflera victoriae

Schefflera victoriae, commonly known as the Victoria's umbrella plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of leaves, which gives it a distinctive and ornamental appearance. The plant features compound leaves with multiple leaflets radiating from a central point, and it produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden areas.

Due to its attractive foliage, it is popular in horticulture for its aesthetic value.
