Stipagrostis hochstetteriana

Stipagrostis hochstetteriana, commonly known as the Hochstetter's grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open habitats.

Due to its resilience and ornamental appeal, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and landscaping projects.
