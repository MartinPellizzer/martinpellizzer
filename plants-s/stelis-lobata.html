Stelis lobata

Stelis lobata, commonly known as the spurred orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit and its ability to thrive in shaded, humid environments. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers with distinctive spurred petals, often in shades of white, pink, and purple. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding substrates.

Stelis lobata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
