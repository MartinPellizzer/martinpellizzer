Stellaria calycantha subsp. interior

Stellaria calycantha subsp.

interior, commonly known as the interior starwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the western United States. It features slender, prostrate stems that bear small, white flowers with five petals arranged in a star-like pattern.

The leaves are typically opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, sandy soils and is often found in open meadows and along streambanks.

This subspecies is noted for its ecological importance, providing nectar for pollinators and habitat for small invertebrates.
