Salvia sonklarii

Salvia sonklarii, commonly known as Sonklar's sage, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of eastern Turkey.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces showy violet to purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
