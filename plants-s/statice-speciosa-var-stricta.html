Statice speciosa var. stricta

Statice speciosa var. stricta, commonly known as the strict statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of pink, red, and purple. The plant produces small, bract-like leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden borders and coastal landscapes.

The flowers bloom in late summer to early autumn, adding color to gardens during the transitional seasons.
