Sarothamnus scoparius var. reverchonii

Sarothamnus scoparius var. reverchonii, commonly known as the Reverchon's thyme shrub, is a perennial evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. The plant features compact, aromatic foliage with small, needle-like leaves that are gray-green in color. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, appearing in clusters during the spring and summer months.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens or as a border plant.
