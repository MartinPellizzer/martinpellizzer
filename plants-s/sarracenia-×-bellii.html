Sarracenia × bellii

Sarracenia × bellii, commonly known as the hybrid pitcher plant, is a carnivorous plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a hybrid between Sarracenia leucophylla and Sarracenia rubra, resulting in a plant with distinctive, colorful, and often red-hued pitcher structures. The plant's pitchers are modified leaves that secrete digestive enzymes to trap and digest insects. It thrives in wet, acidic soils and requires ample sunlight for optimal growth.

Sarracenia × bellii is popular among carnivorous plant enthusiasts due to its striking appearance and ease of cultivation.
