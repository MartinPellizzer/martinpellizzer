Silene seguieri

Silene seguieri, commonly known as the Seguier's campion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils. The plant produces striking pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

Silene seguieri is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
