Silene laeta f. megalantha

Silene laeta f. megalantha, commonly known as the showy silene, is a striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family, characterized by its showy, often pink or red flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. Its flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, with petals that are often reflexed at the tips.

This subspecies is noted for its larger, more showy flowers compared to the typical form of Silene laeta.
