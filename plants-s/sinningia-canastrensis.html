Sinningia canastrensis

Sinningia canastrensis, commonly known as the Canastrensis Gloxinia, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its tuberous roots and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers.
