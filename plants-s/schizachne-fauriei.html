Schizachne fauriei

Schizachne fauriei, commonly known as the Faurie's grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and wetlands. The plant has narrow, elongated leaves with a prominent midrib and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Schizachne fauriei is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit, which allows it to spread and form dense mats.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the ecosystems it inhabits.
