Swartzia sprucei

Swartzia sprucei, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a tropical tree native to Central and Southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hard, dense wood, which is highly valued for its strength and durability. The plant has compound leaves with alternate, elliptical leaflets and produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in panicles. Swartzia sprucei is typically found in moist lowland forests and can grow up to 30 meters in height.

Its ecological importance includes providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
