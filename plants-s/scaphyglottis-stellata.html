Scaphyglottis stellata, commonly known as the star-shaped orchid, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests or stream sides. Its leaves are broad and ovate, often with a waxy texture, while its roots are fibrous and adapted for nutrient absorption.
