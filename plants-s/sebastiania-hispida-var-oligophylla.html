Sebastiania hispida var. oligophylla

Sebastiania hispida var. oligophylla, commonly known as the velvetleaf sebastiania, is a species of flowering plant in the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments.
