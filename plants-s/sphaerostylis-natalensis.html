Sphaerostylis natalensis

Sphaerostylis natalensis, commonly known as Natal orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, which bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Sphaerostylis natalensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
