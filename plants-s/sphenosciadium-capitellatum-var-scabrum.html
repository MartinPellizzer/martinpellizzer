Sphenosciadium capitellatum var. scabrum

Sphenosciadium capitellatum var. scabrum, commonly known as the rough sphenosciadium, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features a basal rosette of leaves that are rough and hairy, giving it its varietal name "scabrum.". The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a compact inflorescence at the top of the stem. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported anti-inflammatory properties.

The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.
