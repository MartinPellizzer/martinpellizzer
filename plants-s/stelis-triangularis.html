Stelis triangularis

Stelis triangularis, commonly known as the triangular orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and produces striking, triangular-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

Stelis triangularis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
