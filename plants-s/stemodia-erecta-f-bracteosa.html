Stemodia erecta f. bracteosa

Stemodia erecta f. bracteosa, commonly known as the bracteate stemodia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Acanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and distinctive bracts that often resemble leaves. The plant typically features opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves with serrated margins. Its inflorescence consists of dense clusters of small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple in color.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
