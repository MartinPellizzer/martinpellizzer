Scilla Scilloides

Scilla scilloides is a delicate flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is known for its striking blue-violet flowers that bloom in early spring. This species thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge in early spring. Scilla scilloides is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and easy care requirements.
