Salvia bungei

Salvia bungei, commonly known as Chinese sage, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces vibrant violet to blue flowers in whorled clusters, typically blooming in summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly fuzzy texture.

Salvia bungei is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well-suited to rock gardens and dry landscapes.
