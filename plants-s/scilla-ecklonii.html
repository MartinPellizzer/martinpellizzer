Scilla ecklonii

Scilla ecklonii, commonly known as the Cape bellflower, is a bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of bell-shaped blue or violet flowers in early spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders. Its bulbs are hardy and can be divided to propagate the plant.

Scilla ecklonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
