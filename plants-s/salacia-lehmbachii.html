Salacia lehmbachii

Salacia lehmbachii, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Salicaceae, which includes willows and poplars, though it is more closely related to the genus *Salacia*. The plant is characterized by its pendulous, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a striking appearance. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly serrated margin.

Salacia lehmbachii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its medicinal properties.
