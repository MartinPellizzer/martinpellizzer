Schefflera trineura

Schefflera trineura, commonly known as the three-veined schefflera, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with three main veins, giving it its scientific name. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters in its natural habitat. Its foliage is dense and glossy, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Schefflera trineura prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it relatively easy to care for.
