Sedum taquetii

Sedum taquetii, commonly known as the Taquet's stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow to orange. The plant has thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

Sedum taquetii is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance requirements.
