Serapias × todaroi

Serapias × todaroi, commonly known as the hybrid marsh orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange. The plant features a rosette of basal leaves and produces erect flowering stems with clustered blooms. It thrives in wet or marshy habitats, often growing in shallow water or along the edges of ponds.

Serapias × todaroi is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in wetland ecosystems.
