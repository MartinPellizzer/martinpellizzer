Sobralia veitchii

Sobralia veitchii, commonly known as the Veitch's sobralia, is a tropical orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its striking violet and yellow flowers, which bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has dark green, strap-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Sobralia veitchii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
