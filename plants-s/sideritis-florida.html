Sideritis florida

Sideritis florida, commonly known as Florida ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and small, clustered blue to purple flowers. Its leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy surface.

Sideritis florida is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
