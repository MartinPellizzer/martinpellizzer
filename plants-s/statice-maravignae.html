Statice maravignae

Statice maravignae, commonly known as the Canary Islands statice, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces showy, spike-like inflorescences composed of numerous small, often blue or purple flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is sometimes cultivated in horticultural settings.
