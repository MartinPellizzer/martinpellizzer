Stickmannia mosaica

Stickmannia mosaica, commonly known as the mosaic stickman plant, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage and unique, intricate leaf patterns. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Stickmannia mosaica is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
