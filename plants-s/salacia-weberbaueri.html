Salacia weberbaueri

Salacia weberbaueri, commonly known as the Weberbauer's salacia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Salaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its woody stems and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. Its leaves are typically elliptical and have a glossy surface, while its fruit is a small, fleshy berry.

Salacia weberbaueri is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and unique floral display.
