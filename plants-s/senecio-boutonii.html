Senecio boutonii

Senecio boutonii, commonly known as the Cape snowball, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of small white flowers that bloom in winter. The plant has silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine, hairy textures, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Senecio boutonii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique shape and floral display.
