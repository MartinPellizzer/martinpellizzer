Schollera erythrocarpa

Schollera erythrocarpa, commonly known as the red-fruited schollera, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, glossy leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white flowers in spring, which are followed by bright red, fleshy fruits. Schollera erythrocarpa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
