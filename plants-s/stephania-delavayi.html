Stephania delavayi

Stephania delavayi, commonly known as the Chinese herbaceous swallowwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Menispermaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of western China, particularly in Sichuan and Yunnan provinces. The plant features a climbing habit with tendrils and heart-shaped leaves that are often deeply lobed. Its flowers are small, greenish-yellow, and clustered in umbels, while the fruit is a distinctive, fleshy, three-lobed berry.

Stephania delavayi is valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
