Sideritis endressii f. laxespicata

Sideritis endressii f. laxespicata, commonly known as the lax spreading sideritis, is a flowering plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, pale blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in rocky or sandy soils. Its leaves are typically narrow and slightly hairy, contributing to its drought-resistant nature.

This subspecies is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and coastal areas.
