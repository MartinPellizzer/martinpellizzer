Schizachyrium shimadae

Schizachyrium shimadae, commonly known as the Japanese feather grass, is a perennial grass species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-green foliage and delicate, feathery plumes that bloom in the summer. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and forest edges. It has a clumping growth habit and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Schizachyrium shimadae is also valued for its ability to thrive in well-drained soils and its low maintenance requirements.
