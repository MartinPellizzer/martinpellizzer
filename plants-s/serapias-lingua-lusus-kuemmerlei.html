Serapias lingua lusus kuemmerlei

Serapias lingua lusus kuemmerlei, commonly known as the tongue-shaped serapias, is a rare and specialized orchid species native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.
