Statice tenoreana

Statice tenoreana, commonly known as the Sicilian statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, purple, and white. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy choice for coastal and rocky gardens.

Statice tenoreana is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
