Stachys sosnowskyi

Stachys sosnowskyi, commonly known as Sosnowsky's hedge nettle, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic foliage. The plant features erect, hairy stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture. Its flowers are small, clustered in whorls, and typically have purple or violet hues.

It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
