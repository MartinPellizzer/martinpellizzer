Spathiphyllum minahassae

Spathiphyllum minahassae, commonly known as the Minahassa peace lily, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green heart-shaped leaves and its striking white spathe that surrounds a yellow spadix, giving it a unique appearance. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments and prefers indirect sunlight. It is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Spathiphyllum minahassae is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
