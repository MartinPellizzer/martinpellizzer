Senecio keniophytum

Senecio keniophytum, commonly known as the Kenia leaf or Kenia plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

Senecio keniophytum is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its elegant appearance and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
