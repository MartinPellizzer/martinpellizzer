Swertia raveendrae

Swertia raveendrae, commonly known as the Raveendra's gentian, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, thriving in cool and moist conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, while its stems are slender and branched.

Swertia raveendrae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also of interest in botanical research due to its unique adaptations to high-altitude ecosystems.
