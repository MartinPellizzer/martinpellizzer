Satureja briquetii var. debilis

Satureja briquetii var. debilis, commonly known as slender mountain savory, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically features small, pink or purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are aromatic, often used in traditional herbal medicine for their mild antiseptic properties.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or limestone habitats.
