Stapelia ophiuncula

Stapelia ophiuncula, commonly known as the horned stapelia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, horn-shaped leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge. The plant produces striking, star-shaped flowers that are often yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Stapelia ophiuncula is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
