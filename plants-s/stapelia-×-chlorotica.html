Stapelia × chlorotica

Stapelia × chlorotica, commonly known as the hybrid star-shaped stapelia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, star-like flowers that are often yellow or greenish in color. The plant produces fleshy, ribbed stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. Its flowers are typically short-lived, opening in the early morning and closing by midday.

Stapelia × chlorotica is prized for its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for collectors and succulent enthusiasts.
