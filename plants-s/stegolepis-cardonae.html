Stegolepis cardonae

Stegolepis cardonae, commonly known as the prickly pear cactus, is a species native to the Canary Islands.

It is a succulent plant characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and spiny leaves. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the warmer months. Its fruit, known as a tuna, is edible and has a sweet, tangy flavor.

Stegolepis cardonae plays an important role in its native ecosystem, providing food and shelter for various desert animals.
