Senecio munozii

Senecio munozii, commonly known as the Munoz's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and is adapted to arid environments. It is often found in rocky soils and open habitats.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
