Stellaria sennii

Stellaria sennii, commonly known as the starwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

Stellaria sennii is often found in temperate regions of Europe and Asia, where it thrives in nutrient-rich soils.
