Sarcanthus amabilis

Sarcanthus amabilis, commonly known as the sweet-scented orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is known for its delicate, strap-like leaves. Sarcanthus amabilis thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for tropical gardens and indoor cultivation.
