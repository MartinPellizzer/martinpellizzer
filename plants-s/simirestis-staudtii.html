Simirestis staudtii

Simirestis staudtii, commonly known as the Staudt's milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a hairy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. It is a member of the Apocynaceae family, which includes many milkweed species.
