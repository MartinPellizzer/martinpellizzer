Stelis hypsitera

Stelis hypsitera, commonly known as the pink-stemmed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features a short, rhizomatous stem with a distinctive pink or reddish coloration. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in a rosette pattern.

The flowers are small, clustered in inflorescences, and often display a range of colors from white to pink.
