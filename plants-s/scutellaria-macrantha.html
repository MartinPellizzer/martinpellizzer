Scutellaria macrantha

Scutellaria macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features square stems and opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy. Its showy blue to violet flowers are borne in whorls along the stem, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Scutellaria macrantha is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
