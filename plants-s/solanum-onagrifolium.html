Solanum onagrifolium

Solanum onagrifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant features small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, and its leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and can thrive in arid environments.

The fruit is a small, berry-like structure that turns from green to red as it matures.
