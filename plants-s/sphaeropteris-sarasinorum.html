Sphaeropteris sarasinorum

Sphaeropteris sarasinorum, commonly known as the Sarasin's cycad, is a species of cycad native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its stout, unbranched trunk and a crown of large, feather-like leaves. The plant produces cones, with male and female cones typically occurring on separate plants. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with a distinctive, glossy green color and a waxy texture.

Sphaeropteris sarasinorum is adapted to tropical and subtropical climates, thriving in well-drained soils and receiving ample sunlight.
