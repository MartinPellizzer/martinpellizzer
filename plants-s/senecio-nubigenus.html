Senecio nubigenus

Senecio nubigenus, commonly known as the cloudland groundsel, is a striking flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant is noted for its tall, slender stems and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is a popular subject in botanical studies and conservation efforts.
