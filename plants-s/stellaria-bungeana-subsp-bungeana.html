Stellaria bungeana subsp. bungeana

Stellaria bungeana subsp.

bungeana, commonly known as the Bunge's starwort, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, including China. It is a low-growing herbaceous species with slender, upright stems that often branch near the base.

The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne on slender pedicels. Its leaves are opposite, ovate to lanceolate in shape, and have a soft, hairy texture.

This species is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.
