Sedum spathulisepalum

Sedum spathulisepalum, commonly known as the silver rose sedum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, which help it store water and survive in arid conditions. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped, pale pink or white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact size and ornamental appeal make it a versatile addition to both indoor and outdoor landscapes.
