Senecio subulatus var. erectus

Senecio subulatus var. erectus, commonly known as the upright ice plant, is a succulent perennial native to southern Africa.

It features compact, upright growth with dense clusters of narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in late summer, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
