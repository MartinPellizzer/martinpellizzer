Sempervivum vicentei subsp. paui

Sempervivum vicentei subsp.

paui, commonly known as the Alboraya stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Spain. It features fleshy, blue-green leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, which help it store water in arid environments.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white during the summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its hardy nature allows it to survive in harsh conditions, contributing to its resilience in Mediterranean climates.
