Stemmadenia pauciflora

Stemmadenia pauciflora, commonly known as the sparse-flowered stemmadenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its woody stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that are typically borne in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Stemmadenia pauciflora is primarily valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
