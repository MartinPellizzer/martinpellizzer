Solena floribunda

Solena floribunda, commonly known as the yellow bellvista, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and slightly hairy, while its stems are erect and branching.

Solena floribunda thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
