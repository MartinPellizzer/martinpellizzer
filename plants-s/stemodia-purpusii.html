Stemodia purpusii

Stemodia purpusii, commonly known as the New Zealand mistflower, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a perennial herb with slender, erect stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly rough texture.

Stemodia purpusii thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
