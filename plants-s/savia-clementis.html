Savia clementis

Savia clementis, commonly known as the Clementine vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, yellow-orange flowers. The plant produces small, edible fruits that are often used in culinary applications. Savia clementis thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
