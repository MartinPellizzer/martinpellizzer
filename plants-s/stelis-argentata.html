Stelis argentata

Stelis argentata, commonly known as the silver-stem orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-tinged stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
