Salvia paramiltiorrhiza

Salvia paramiltiorrhiza, commonly known as Chinese sage, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces vibrant violet or blue flowers that bloom in whorls along the upper stem. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Salvia paramiltiorrhiza is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
