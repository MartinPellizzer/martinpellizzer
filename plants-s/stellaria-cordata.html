Stellaria cordata

Stellaria cordata, commonly known as heartleaf starwort, is a small flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family, which includes other familiar plants like carnations and chickweed. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are heart-shaped, and it produces tiny white or pink star-shaped flowers.

Stellaria cordata is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
