Sideritis montana var. sericea

Sideritis montana var. sericea, commonly known as silverweed, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, hairy leaves that give it a silvery appearance. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale blue or violet flowers during the summer months. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine environments.

Sideritis montana var. sericea is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
