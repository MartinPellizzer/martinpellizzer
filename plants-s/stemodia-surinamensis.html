Stemodia surinamensis

Stemodia surinamensis, commonly known as Surinam stemodia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Suriname and neighboring countries. The plant is characterized by its erect, branching stems and opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are typically small, clustered in inflorescences, and exhibit a range of colors from white to pink.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
