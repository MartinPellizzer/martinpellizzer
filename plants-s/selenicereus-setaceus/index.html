Selenicereus Setaceus

The selenicereus setaceus, also known as the night blooming cereus, is a captivating member of the cacti family. This plant is famous for its large, fragrant flowers that open at night and close by dawn. Native to the tropical regions of the Americas, it thrives in warm, humid climates with well-draining soil. Its striking appearance and unique blooming cycle make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation. Despite its beauty, it requires specific care to ensure its health and longevity.
