Synsepalum zenkeri

Synsepalum zenkeri, commonly known as the African star apple, is a tropical plant native to West Africa.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub that produces edible, star-shaped fruits. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth texture. Its flowers are small and white, arranged in clusters, and it thrives in humid, tropical climates.

The fruit has a sweet, juicy pulp with a mild, slightly acidic flavor.
