Scutellaria javanica var. luzonica

Scutellaria javanica var. luzonica, commonly known as Luzon skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that typically bloom in shades of blue or purple. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
