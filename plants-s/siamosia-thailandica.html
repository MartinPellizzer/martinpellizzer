Siamosia thailandica

Siamosia thailandica, commonly known as the Thai orchid, is a species of orchid native to Thailand.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers that often feature intricate patterns and a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, tropical environments. Its leaves are usually broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native habitat.

Siamosia thailandica is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its cultural significance in Thai traditional medicine.
