Silvia duckei

Silvia duckei, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a species of epiphytic cactus native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its segmented, leaf-like stems that are often adorned with vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is a popular ornamental plant, especially during the holiday season due to its blooming habits.

Silvia duckei is relatively easy to care for, making it a favored choice for indoor gardening.
