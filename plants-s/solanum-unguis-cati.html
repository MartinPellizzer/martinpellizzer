Solanum unguis-cati

Solanum unguis-cati, commonly known as the cat's claw plant, is a member of the nightshade family (Solanaceae).

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its woody stems and distinctive, claw-like fruit. The plant produces small, white or purple flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with a slightly hairy texture.

Solanum unguis-cati is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and adaptability to various growing conditions.
