Senecio scrobicarioides

Senecio scrobicarioides, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow to orange daisy-like flowers. The plant has upright, branching stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Senecio scrobicarioides is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
