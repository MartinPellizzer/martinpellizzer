Sophora davidi var. chuansiensis

Sophora davidi var. chuansiensis, commonly known as the Chinese silk tree, is a deciduous leguminous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, fragrant yellow flowers. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for landscaping in temperate climates.

The species is also noted for its ecological benefits, providing food and habitat for various wildlife species.
