Stelis cyathochila

Stelis cyathochila, commonly known as the helmet orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated flower spike that bears a single, showy flower with a distinctive helmet-shaped lip. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its botanical features include fleshy, strap-like leaves and a compact, upright growth habit.

Stelis cyathochila is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in humid, shaded environments.
