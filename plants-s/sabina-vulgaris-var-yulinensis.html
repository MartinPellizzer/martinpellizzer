Sabina vulgaris var. yulinensis

Sabina vulgaris var. yulinensis, commonly known as the Yulin Juniper, is a shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Cupressaceae family and is characterized by its scale-like leaves and cone-bearing structure. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and thrives in well-drained soils. Its foliage is often used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
