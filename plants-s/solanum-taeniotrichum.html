Solanum taeniotrichum

Solanum taeniotrichum, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its striking red flowers with a spidery appearance. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine, hairy textures, while its flowers bloom in clusters during the summer months.

Solanum taeniotrichum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant, eye-catching blooms.
