Sphaerolobium euchilus

Sphaerolobium euchilus, commonly known as the yellow burr bush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, also known as the legume or pea family, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellow flowers. The plant produces distinctive, spherical, woody pods that enclose its seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
