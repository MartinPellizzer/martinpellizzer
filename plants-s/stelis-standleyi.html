Stelis standleyi

Stelis standleyi, commonly known as the Standley's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic. The plant typically produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Stelis standleyi is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in the Americas.
