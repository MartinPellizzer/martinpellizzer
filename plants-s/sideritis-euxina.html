Sideritis euxina

Sideritis euxina, commonly known as the Balkan ironwort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its slender stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to pink. It thrives in rocky and dry habitats, often found in alpine meadows.

Sideritis euxina is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional remedies for its purported health benefits.
