Sideritis bilgeriana

Sideritis bilgeriana, commonly known as Bilger's sainfoin, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in rocky or dry soils and is often found in mountainous areas.

Sideritis bilgeriana is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
