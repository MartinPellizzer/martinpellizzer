Sansevieria burdettii

Sansevieria burdettii, commonly known as the Burdet's snake plant, is a striking succulent native to South Africa.

It features tall, upright leaves that are dark green with prominent, light green vertical stripes. The plant is characterized by its ability to thrive in low light conditions and requires minimal watering. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water, making it highly drought-resistant.

Sansevieria burdettii is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and aesthetic appeal.
