Swartzia klugii

Swartzia klugii, commonly known as the Klug's ironwood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its hard, dense wood, which is highly valued for its strength and durability. The plant has compound leaves with long, narrow leaflets and produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow.

Swartzia klugii is also notable for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions, making it an important species in tropical forest ecosystems.
