Stelis cassidis

Stelis cassidis, commonly known as the Cassidis orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic nature. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

The flowers bloom in clusters, often appearing in late spring or early summer.
