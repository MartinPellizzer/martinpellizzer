Spiraea × constantia

Spiraea × constantia, commonly known as the constantia weeping spirea, is a hybrid shrub belonging to the Spiraeaceae family.

It is characterized by its graceful, cascading branches that produce clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers in early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, with dense, dark green foliage that provides an attractive backdrop to its floral display. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, fertile soil.

Spiraea × constantia is often used in landscape design for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
