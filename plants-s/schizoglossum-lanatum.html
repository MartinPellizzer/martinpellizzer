Schizoglossum lanatum

Schizoglossum lanatum, commonly known as the woolly schizoglossum, is a flowering plant native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-green leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Schizoglossum lanatum is often found in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions.

Its unique appearance and habitat make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
