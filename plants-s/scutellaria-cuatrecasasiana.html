Scutellaria cuatrecasasiana

Scutellaria cuatrecasasiana, commonly known as the Cuatrecasas's skullcap, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color, which bloom during the summer months.

Scutellaria cuatrecasasiana is notable for its ornamental value and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
