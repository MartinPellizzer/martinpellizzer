Swertia brevipedicellata

Swertia brevipedicellata, commonly known as the short-stemmed swertia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Himalayan regions of India and Nepal.

The plant typically grows at high altitudes, preferring cool and moist environments. Swertia brevipedicellata is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local medicine.
