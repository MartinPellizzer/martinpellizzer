Stewartia pseudocamellia

Stewartia pseudocamellia, commonly known as the Chinese Stewartia, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the family Theaceae and is characterized by its elegant, elongated leaves and showy, camellia-like flowers. The plant produces small, woody capsules that contain numerous seeds, typical of the genus Stewartia. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value lies in its foliage, floral display, and overall graceful form.
