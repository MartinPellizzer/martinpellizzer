Solanum lindenii

Solanum lindenii, commonly known as the Lindenhof nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its showy, violet to purple flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Solanum lindenii is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its striking floral display.
