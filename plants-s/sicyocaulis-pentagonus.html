Sicyocaulis pentagonus

Sicyocaulis pentagonus, commonly known as the pentagonal sea holly, is a unique marine flowering plant found in shallow coastal waters.

It is characterized by its distinctive pentagonal shape and vibrant blue or purple flowers. The plant has a short, compact growth habit with a fleshy, succulent stem. Its leaves are often covered in a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss in its saline environment.

Sicyocaulis pentagonus plays an important role in coastal ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various marine organisms.
