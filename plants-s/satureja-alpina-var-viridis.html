Satureja alpina var. viridis

Satureja alpina var. viridis, commonly known as alpine green savory, is a perennial herb native to mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils and full sun. Its leaves are small, oval, and have a minty, slightly peppery fragrance.

It is often used in traditional herbal medicine and as a culinary herb due to its unique flavor profile.
