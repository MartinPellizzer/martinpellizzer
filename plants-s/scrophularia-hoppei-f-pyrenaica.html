Scrophularia hoppei f. pyrenaica

Scrophularia hoppei f. pyrenaica, commonly known as the Pyrenean scrophularia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Pyrenees mountain range.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae, which includes many species with showy flowers and medicinal properties. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are lanceolate and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are tubular and usually pink or purple in color.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential pharmacological uses.
