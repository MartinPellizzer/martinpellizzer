Sium segetum

Sium segetum, commonly known as water parsnip, is a flowering plant in the family Apiaceae.

It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in the summer. Sium segetum plays an important role in wetland ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various insects and wildlife.
