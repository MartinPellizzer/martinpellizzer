Stanhopea jenischiana

Stanhopea jenischiana, commonly known as the Jenisch's stanhopea, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, tropical environments. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Stanhopea jenischiana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
