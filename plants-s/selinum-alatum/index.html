Selinum Alatum

Selinum alatum is a unique plant known for its distinctive, feather-like leaves that catch the light in a mesmerizing way. Native to arid and semi-arid regions, it has adapted to survive in harsh environments with minimal water. Its silvery-blue foliage gives it a striking appearance, making it a popular choice for collectors and gardeners. The plant's ability to thrive in poor soil conditions highlights its resilience and hardiness. Despite its beauty, it requires careful handling to avoid damaging its delicate structure.
