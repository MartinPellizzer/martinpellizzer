Sedum yunnanense var. forrestii

Sedum yunnanense var. forrestii, commonly known as the Yunnan stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to China.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves that store water, enabling it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly habitats, often in mountainous regions. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, ranging in color from white to pink, and bloom in late spring or early summer.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and ornamental value.
