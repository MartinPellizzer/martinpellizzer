Stylosanthes suborbiculata

Stylosanthes suborbiculata, commonly known as the small-leaf stylo, is a perennial legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its small, orbiculate leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers. The plant is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a valuable forage species. Its root system has the ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, improving soil fertility.

Stylosanthes suborbiculata is often used in pasture and land rehabilitation due to its drought tolerance and nutritional value for livestock.
