Sisymbrium contortum

Sisymbrium contortum, commonly known as twisted shepherd's purse, is an annual flowering plant native to Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant features small, yellow flowers and distinctive triangular seed pods that turn reddish as they mature. Its leaves are pinnately divided, and it often grows in disturbed soils such as roadsides and fields.

The species is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
