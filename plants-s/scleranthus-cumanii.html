Scleranthus cumanii

Scleranthus cumanii, commonly known as the Cumanii thornweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows in dry, disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant has slender, erect stems with small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense spikes, usually with five petals that range in color from white to pink.

Scleranthus cumanii is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
