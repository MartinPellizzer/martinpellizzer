Sonchus asper var. glanduliferus

Sonchus asper var. glanduliferus, commonly known as rough hawksbeard, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its hairy, rough stems and lobed, serrated leaves. The plant produces yellow composite flowers that are arranged in a single, showy head. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been introduced to other regions.

It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.
