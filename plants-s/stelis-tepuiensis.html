Stelis tepuiensis

Stelis tepuiensis, commonly known as the tepui orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tepuis of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically grows on rock surfaces or in crevices, adapted to the harsh, high-altitude conditions of the tepuis. Its flowers are usually small to medium in size, with intricate patterns and a sweet fragrance.

Stelis tepuiensis is a rare and specialized orchid, found primarily in the Guiana Shield region.
