Specklinia mirabilis

Specklinia mirabilis, commonly known as the silver-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage that ranges from dark green to silver in color. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, often clustered in racemes. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Specklinia mirabilis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of care in the right conditions.
