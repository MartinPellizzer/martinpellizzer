Statice virgata var. pumila

Statice virgata var. pumila, commonly known as dwarf statice, is a low-growing annual herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems and small, showy flowers that are typically pink to purple in color. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms make it a valuable addition to floral arrangements.
