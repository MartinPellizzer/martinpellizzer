Stapelia × glabriflora

Stapelia × glabriflora, commonly known as the hybrid star-shaped stapelia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and distinctive, star-shaped flowers. The plant produces yellowish-green blooms with a strong, unpleasant odor, which attracts pollinators like flies. It is a hybrid between Stapelia gummosa and Stapelia hirsuta, showcasing traits from both parent species.

Stapelia × glabriflora is relatively low-maintenance, requiring minimal water and well-draining soil to thrive.
