Sideritis mutica

Sideritis mutica, commonly known as narrow-leaved ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its slender stems and small, narrow leaves. Its flowers are typically pale blue or violet, arranged in whorled clusters.

Sideritis mutica is often found in rocky or dry habitats and is valued for its medicinal properties.
