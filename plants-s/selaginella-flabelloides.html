Selaginella flabelloides

Selaginella flabelloides, commonly known as the fan moss or slender spikemoss, is a species of spikemoss belonging to the family Selaginellaceae.

It is a small, evergreen plant that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features delicate, grass-like leaves arranged in whorls around its slender stems. Its reproductive structures, including microsporangia and megasporangia, are located on specialized leaves called sporophylls.

Selaginella flabelloides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
