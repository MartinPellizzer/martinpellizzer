Serratula multicaulis

Serratula multicaulis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spearthorn, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and typically herbaceous growth habit. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as meadows and slopes.

Serratula multicaulis is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
