Stoebe ensori

Stoebe ensori, commonly known as narrow-leaved betony, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Boraginaceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, blue or violet flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.
