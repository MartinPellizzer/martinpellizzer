Stachytarpheta lanata

Stachytarpheta lanata, commonly known as the woolly blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and clusters of blue to purple flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil. It is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
