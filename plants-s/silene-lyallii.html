Silene lyallii

Silene lyallii, commonly known as Lyall's campion, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its pink or white flowers and slender stems. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine regions, thriving in rocky and well-drained soils. Silene lyallii is a perennial herb that produces small, showy flowers during the summer months.
