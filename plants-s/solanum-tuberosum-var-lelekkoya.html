Solanum tuberosum var. lelekkoya

Solanum tuberosum var. lelekkoya, commonly known as the Lelekkoya potato, is a variety native to Turkey.

It is characterized by its medium to large tubers with a yellowish skin and pale yellow flesh. The plant belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is cultivated for its edible tubers. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its flavor and versatility in culinary applications.
