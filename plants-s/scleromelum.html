Scleromelum

Scleromelum, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a genus of flowering plants in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its hard, dense wood and is native to southern Africa. The plants typically have compound leaves and produce small, yellow or white flowers. Scleromelum species are often found in dry, rocky habitats and are adapted to arid conditions.

They play an important role in local ecosystems and have been used traditionally for construction and fuel.
