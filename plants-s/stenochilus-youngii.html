Stenochilus youngii

Stenochilus youngii, commonly known as the young's stenochilus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Orchidaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, fragrant orchid flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
