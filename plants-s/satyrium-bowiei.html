Satyrium bowiei

Satyrium bowiei, commonly known as the Bowei's orchid, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
