Stelis conmixta

Stelis conmixta, commonly known as the mixed-stem orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, fragrant flowers. The plant typically features two or more stems that arise from a single base, often with leaves that are lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette. Its flowers are usually small to medium-sized, with a complex structure typical of orchids, featuring a lip that is often brightly colored.

Stelis conmixta thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
