Specklinia gomesii-ferreirae

Specklinia gomesii-ferreirae, commonly known as the Ferreira's speckled orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to specific regions in Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, speckled foliage. The plant produces small, delicate flowers with a distinctive coloration that varies from greenish-white to pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil for optimal growth.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its natural range.
