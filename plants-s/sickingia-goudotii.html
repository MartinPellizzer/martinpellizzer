Sickingia goudotii

Sickingia goudotii, commonly known as the Red-veined Zebra Cactus, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, ribbed stems that are adorned with red veins, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Sickingia goudotii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements.
