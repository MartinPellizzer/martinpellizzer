Salacia patens

Salacia patens, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Salicaceae, which includes willows and poplars. The plant is characterized by its drooping clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate with a smooth margin.

Salacia patens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
