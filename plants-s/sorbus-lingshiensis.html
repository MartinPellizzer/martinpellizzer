Sorbus lingshiensis

Sorbus lingshiensis, commonly known as the Lingshi whitebeam, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its alternate, simple, and oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers in clusters, which are followed by small, red or orange berries. It is typically found in mountainous regions and thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soils.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
