Sideritis akkarensis

Sideritis akkarensis, commonly known as the Akkara Sideritis, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes plants known for their aromatic properties. The plant is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in rocky and well-drained soils, often found in alpine and subalpine habitats.

Sideritis akkarensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
