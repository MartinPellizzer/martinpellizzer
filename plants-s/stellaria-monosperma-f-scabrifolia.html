Stellaria monosperma f. scabrifolia

Stellaria monosperma f. scabrifolia, commonly known as the roughleaf starwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, ascending stems and small, star-shaped white or pale pink flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and covered with fine, stiff hairs, giving the plant a rough texture. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, streambanks, and woodland edges.

This subspecies is notable for its distinctive foliage and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
