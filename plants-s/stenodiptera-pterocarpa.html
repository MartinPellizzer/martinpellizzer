Stenodiptera pterocarpa

Stenodiptera pterocarpa, commonly known as the African wild bean, is a leguminous shrub native to tropical Africa.

It features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes. The plant bears pods that are typically hairy and contain several seeds. It is adapted to grow in dry, sandy soils and is often found in woodland savannas.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species for both ecological and agricultural purposes in its native habitat.
