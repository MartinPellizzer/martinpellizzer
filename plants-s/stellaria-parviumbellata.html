Stellaria parviumbellata

Stellaria parviumbellata, commonly known as the small-star flower, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It features small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the spring and early summer. The plant has delicate, hairy leaves and a sprawling growth habit, often forming dense mats on the ground. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and ornamental species within its native habitat.
