Sabicea perrottetii

Sabicea perrottetii, commonly known as the Perrottet's sabicea, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Vietnam. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
