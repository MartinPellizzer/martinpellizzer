Struthiola erecta var. angustifolia

Struthiola erecta var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed struthiola, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping.
