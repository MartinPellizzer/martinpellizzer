Sibbaldianthe semiglabra

Sibbaldianthe semiglabra, commonly known as the smoothleaf sibbaldianthe, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, well-drained soils. The plant features small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the rainy season. Its leaves are usually smooth and lance-shaped, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

It is often found in woodland and grassland habitats, where it plays a role in local ecosystems.
