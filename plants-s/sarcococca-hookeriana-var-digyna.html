Sarcococca hookeriana var. digyna

Sarcococca hookeriana var. digyna, commonly known as the fragrant evergreen winterberry, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that remain throughout the year. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in late winter, followed by bright red berries that persist through the winter months. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to provide color and texture in shaded garden settings.
