Sanicula crassicaulis var. tripartita

Sanicula crassicaulis var. tripartita, commonly known as the three-part sanicula, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its umbellate inflorescences and aromatic foliage. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are palmately compound, often divided into three parts, giving it its common name.
