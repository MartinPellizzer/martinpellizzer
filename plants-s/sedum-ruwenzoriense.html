Sedum ruwenzoriense

Sedum ruwenzoriense, commonly known as the Ruwenzori sedum, is a succulent plant native to the high-altitude regions of East Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid and rocky environments. The plant typically produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It is well adapted to cool temperatures and full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is often sought after by collectors and botanists.
