Stemmadenia pubescens

Stemmadenia pubescens, commonly known as the hairy stemmadenia, is a species of flowering plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and opposite leaves, which are usually elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, and it is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, and the plant is valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
