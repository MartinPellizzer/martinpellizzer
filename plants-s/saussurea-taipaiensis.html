Saussurea taipaiensis

Saussurea taipaiensis, commonly known as the Taiwan Saussurea, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stem and dense clusters of yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas at high altitudes, often in mountainous regions. It has fibrous roots and is known for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.

The leaves are usually linear and covered with fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
