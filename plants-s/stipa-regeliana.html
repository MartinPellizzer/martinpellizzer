Stipa regeliana

Stipa regeliana, commonly known as Regel's feathergrass, is a species of grass native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and tufted inflorescences that resemble delicate plumes. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as steppes and rocky slopes. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Stipa regeliana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its distinctive appearance.
