Simarouba berteroana

Simarouba berteroana, commonly known as the yellow mangle or yellow mangrove, is a tropical tree native to the Caribbean and northern South America.

It is a member of the Simarouba genus within the family Meliaceae, characterized by its aromatic wood and distinctive yellow flowers. The plant has compound leaves with alternate arrangement and produces small, yellowish-green fruits that contain winged seeds. It thrives in coastal and swampy areas, often forming dense thickets in brackish water environments.
