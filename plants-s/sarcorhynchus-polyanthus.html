Sarcorhynchus polyanthus

Sarcorhynchus polyanthus, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, multicolored foliage that often features shades of green, red, and yellow. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Sarcorhynchus polyanthus prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
