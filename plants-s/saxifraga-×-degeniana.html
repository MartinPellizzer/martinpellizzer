Saxifraga × degeniana

Saxifraga × degeniana, commonly known as the hybrid saxifrage, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are typically covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, pink or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Saxifraga × degeniana is often used in rock gardens due to its hardy nature and ornamental appeal.
