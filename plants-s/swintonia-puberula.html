Swintonia puberula

Swintonia puberula, commonly known as the velvetleaf or velvetleaf hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its hairy, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. Swintonia puberula is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
