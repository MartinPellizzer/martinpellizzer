Sarcochilus spathipetalus

Sarcochilus spathipetalus, commonly known as the helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to New Zealand.

It features a distinctive, helmet-like flower that is typically greenish-yellow with a red or purple lip. The plant has a pseudobulb and produces a single, showy flower per stem. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forested areas.

The orchid's unique morphology and habitat make it a notable species in New Zealand's flora.
