Scorzonera laciniata var. latifolia

Scorzonera laciniata var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved scorzonera, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features a rosette of broad, lobed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers with five petals, typically arranged in a loose inflorescence. Its roots are thick and fleshy, which help it survive in harsh, arid environments.

Scorzonera laciniata var. latifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
