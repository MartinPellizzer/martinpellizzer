Satureja × mixta

Satureja × mixta, commonly known as hybrid savory, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink to purple in color. Its aromatic leaves contain essential oils rich in thymol and carvacrol, contributing to its medicinal and culinary uses.

Satureja × mixta is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a spice in traditional recipes.
