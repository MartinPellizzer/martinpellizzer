Scabiosa japonica var. breviligula

Scabiosa japonica var. breviligula, commonly known as the Japanese scabious, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It features small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, blooming in late summer. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are lance-shaped with serrated edges. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate appearance and long bloom period.
