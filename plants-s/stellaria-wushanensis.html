Stellaria wushanensis

Stellaria wushanensis, commonly known as the Wushan starwort, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountainous regions. Its leaves are opposite and have a hairy texture, while its flowers are usually white or pale pink.

Stellaria wushanensis is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and limited distribution.
