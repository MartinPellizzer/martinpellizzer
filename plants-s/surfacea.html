Surfacea

Surfacea, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a species characterized by its distinctive silvery foliage.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is native to arid and semi-arid regions. The leaves are covered in fine, reflective hairs that give it its name and help reduce water loss. It produces small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in the spring.

The plant is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental value.
