Stelis quintella

Stelis quintella, commonly known as the violet orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces delicate, violet-colored flowers with intricate patterns, which are often fragrant. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Stelis quintella is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or terrariums.
