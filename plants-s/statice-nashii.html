Statice nashii

Statice nashii, commonly known as the narrowleaf statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are usually pink to purple in color and bloom in late summer.

Statice nashii is often cultivated as a ornamental plant due to its drought tolerance and long-lasting flowers.
