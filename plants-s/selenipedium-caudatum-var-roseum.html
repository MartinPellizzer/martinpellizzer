Selenipedium caudatum var. roseum

Selenipedium caudatum var. roseum, commonly known as the rose-flowered hooded orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to the tropical rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and rose. The plant produces a single, elongated flower spike with several large, hooded blooms that resemble a crown. Its botanical name reflects its distinctive appearance and its classification within the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

This species is known for its slow growth and requires specific environmental conditions to thrive.
