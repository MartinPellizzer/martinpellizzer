Senecio retusus

Senecio retusus, commonly known as the roughleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in open, rocky habitats. The plant features upright stems with rough, hairy foliage and produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes many other composite flowers.

Senecio retusus is often used in rock gardens due to its hardy nature and ornamental appeal.
