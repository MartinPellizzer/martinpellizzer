Stomatium leve

Stomatium leve, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, dense hairs. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Stomatium leve is also known for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
