Sideritis arborescens var. fontii

Sideritis arborescens var. fontii, commonly known as the Fontana ironwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branched stems that are typically covered in fine, hairy hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple in color, arranged in whorled clusters along the stem. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slightly hairy surface.

This variety is valued for its medicinal properties and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.
