Sophronitis alaorii

Sophronitis alaorii, commonly known as the Laorei orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a pseudobulb with a single leaf and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Its flowering period occurs during the warmer months, and it is prized for its ornamental value in horticulture.

The species is relatively rare and requires specific care to thrive in cultivation.
