Stapeliopsis khamiesbergensis

Stapeliopsis khamiesbergensis, commonly known as the Khamiesberg star, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rounded growth habit. The plant produces striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.
