Statice globulifera

Statice globulifera, commonly known as the globular statice, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and profuse display of small, colorful flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is often found in dune environments. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are adapted to withstand salty sea air and strong winds.

Statice globulifera is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize coastal soils.
