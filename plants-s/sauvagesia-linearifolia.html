Sauvagesia linearifolia

Sauvagesia linearifolia, commonly known as the slender-leaved sauvagesia, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are typically arranged in opposite pairs along the stems. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long-lasting foliage.
