Stelis trimeropetala

Stelis trimeropetala, commonly known as the three-petaled orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its flowers have three petals, which are usually white or pale pink, and they bloom during the wet season.

Stelis trimeropetala is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
