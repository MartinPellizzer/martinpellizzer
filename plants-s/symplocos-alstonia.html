Symplocos alstonia

Symplocos alstonia, commonly known as Alston's waxleaf, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a whitish wax layer, giving it a distinctive appearance.
