Setaria chevalieri

Setaria chevalieri, commonly known as the slender barnyardgrass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and spikelet-based inflorescence. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural fields. It has a fibrous root system and produces small, wind-dispersed seeds.

Its botanical features make it both a weed and a potential forage plant in certain environments.
