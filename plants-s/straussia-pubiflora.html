Straussia pubiflora

Straussia pubiflora, commonly known as the hairy-flowered straussia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of fleshy, pubescent leaves that help reduce water loss in its harsh environment. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense, terminal clusters, which bloom during the rainy season. Its succulent foliage and ability to store water make it well adapted to drought conditions.

Straussia pubiflora is often found in rocky soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience.
