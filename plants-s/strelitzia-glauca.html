Strelitzia glauca

Strelitzia glauca, commonly known as the blue strelitzia, is a striking herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features vibrant blue and green striped leaves that are lance-shaped and often have a waxy texture. The plant produces showy, bird-like flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which are arranged in a distinctive spiral pattern. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Strelitzia glauca is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
