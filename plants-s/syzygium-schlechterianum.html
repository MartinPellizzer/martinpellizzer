Syzygium schlechterianum

Syzygium schlechterianum, commonly known as the Schlechter's apple.

It is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family, native to the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its fragrant white flowers and red, edible fruits that resemble small apples. It thrives in tropical rainforests and is often found growing in shaded, humid environments.

The leaves are alternate, simple, and have a glossy surface, contributing to its ornamental value.
