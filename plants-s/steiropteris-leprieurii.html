Steiropteris leprieurii

Steiropteris leprieurii, commonly known as the Leprieur's tree fern, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in tropical rainforests. Its rhizome system allows it to absorb nutrients from the forest floor efficiently.

Steiropteris leprieurii plays a vital role in maintaining the ecological balance of its habitat.
