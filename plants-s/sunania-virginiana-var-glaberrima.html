Sunania virginiana var. glaberrima

Sunania virginiana var. glaberrima, commonly known as the smooth sunflower, is a variant of the sunflower species native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its smooth, hairless stems and leaves, which distinguish it from other varieties. The plant produces large, showy yellow flowers with a dark central disk, typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in open woodlands and prairies.

Its botanical features make it a valuable plant for both ecological and ornamental purposes.
