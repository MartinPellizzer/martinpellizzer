Spiraea media f. glabrescens

Spiraea media f. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth spiraea, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of central and eastern Europe.

It features narrow, upright stems with small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has smooth, glabrous bark that becomes more prominent as it ages, giving it its cultivar name. Its foliage is typically dark green and remains attractive throughout the growing season.

It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil conditions.
