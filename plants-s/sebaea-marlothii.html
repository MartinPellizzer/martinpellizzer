Sebaea marlothii

Sebaea marlothii, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow or orange flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and has a bushy, compact growth habit. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Sebaea marlothii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
