Statice florida

Statice florida, commonly known as sea lavender, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and its showy, spike-like inflorescences composed of numerous small, blue to purple flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens. Its foliage is typically gray-green, with silver-tinged leaves that add visual interest.

Statice florida is often used in floral arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms and ornamental value.
