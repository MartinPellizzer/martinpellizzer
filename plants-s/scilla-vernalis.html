Scilla vernalis

Scilla vernalis, commonly known as the spring squill, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It produces delicate, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue or violet in color, appearing in early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge from a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Scilla vernalis is often found in woodland areas and along rocky slopes.
