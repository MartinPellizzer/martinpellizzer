Synaedrys eichleri

Synaedrys eichleri, commonly known as the Eichler's synaedrys, is a rare and lesser-known species within the orchid family.

It is native to specific regions in South America, particularly in the Andean foothills of Ecuador. The plant features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and typically exhibit a delicate combination of white and green hues.
