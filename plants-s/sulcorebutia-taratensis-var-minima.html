Sulcorebutia taratensis var. minima

Sulcorebutia taratensis var. minima, commonly known as the miniature sun rose, is a compact cactus native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with a ribbed structure and produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months. The plant is characterized by its small size and ability to thrive in well-drained soil with minimal water. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in harsh environments.

Its botanical classification places it within the Cactaceae family, highlighting its adaptation to desert conditions.
