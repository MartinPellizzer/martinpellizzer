Swertia dichotoma var. rubrostriata

Swertia dichotoma var. rubrostriata, commonly known as the red-streaked gentian, is a perennial herb native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers with distinctive red streaks on the petals. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes. Its leaves are lanceolate and often have a hairy texture.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is valued for its ornamental beauty and traditional medicinal uses.
