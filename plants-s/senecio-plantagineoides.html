Senecio plantagineoides

Senecio plantagineoides, commonly known as the Blue Weed or Blueweed, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its blue or violet flowers that bloom in dense clusters. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in dry, open habitats. It is an annual or short-lived perennial, and its growth is adapted to arid conditions.

The species is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking flower color.
