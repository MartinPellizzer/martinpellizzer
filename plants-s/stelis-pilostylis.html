Stelis pilostylis

Stelis pilostylis, commonly known as the long-styled orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. Its flowers have a unique structure with elongated stamens that extend beyond the petals.

Stelis pilostylis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
