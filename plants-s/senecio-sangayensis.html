Senecio sangayensis

Senecio sangayensis, commonly known as the Sangay groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the Andean region of Ecuador.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Senecio sangayensis is known for its yellowish to orange-colored flowers, which bloom in spring and attract various pollinators.
