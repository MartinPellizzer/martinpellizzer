Schizachyrium condensatum var. intermedium

Schizachyrium condensatum var. intermedium, commonly known as intermediate threadgrass, is a perennial grass native to the eastern and central United States.

It typically grows in moist to wet soils in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks. The plant features slender, wiry stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. Its inflorescence consists of a cylindrical cluster of spikelets that are usually pale green to golden brown.

It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
