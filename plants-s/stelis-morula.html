Stelis morula

Stelis morula, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. The plant has a compact growth habit with short, stout stems and small, clustered flowers. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a delicate, fragrant scent.

Stelis morula is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its miniature size and ornamental value.
