Sedum lutzii

Sedum lutzii, commonly known as the Lutz's stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Sedum lutzii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
