Stelis greenwoodii

Stelis greenwoodii, commonly known as the Greenwood's stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in humid environments. The plant features a short, branching stem with numerous small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale green, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

Stelis greenwoodii is valued in horticulture for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
