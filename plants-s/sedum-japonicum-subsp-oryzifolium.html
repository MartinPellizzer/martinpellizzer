Sedum japonicum subsp. oryzifolium

Sedum japonicum subsp.

oryzifolium, commonly known as Japanese stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to East Asia. It features fleshy, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color.

The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
