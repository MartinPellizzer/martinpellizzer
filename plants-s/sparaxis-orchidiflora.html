Sparaxis orchidiflora

Sparaxis orchidiflora, commonly known as the orchid-flowered sparaxis, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant blooms in late winter to early spring, making it a notable addition to gardens during the cooler months. Its flowers are typically arranged in dense spikes, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Sparaxis orchidiflora prefers well-drained soil and full sun, thriving in Mediterranean climates.
