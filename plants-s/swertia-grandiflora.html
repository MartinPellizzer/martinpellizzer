Swertia grandiflora

Swertia grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered swertia, is a perennial herb native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It prefers cool, moist habitats and is often found in forested areas at high altitudes.

Swertia grandiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional medicinal uses in some Asian cultures.
