Scutellaria cochinchinensis

Scutellaria cochinchinensis, commonly known as Vietnamese skullcap, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides.

Scutellaria cochinchinensis is valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
