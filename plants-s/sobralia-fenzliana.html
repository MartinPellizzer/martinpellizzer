Sobralia fenzliana

Sobralia fenzliana, commonly known as the Fenzli's sobralia, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. The plant has strap-like, elongated leaves that are usually dark green and arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in humid, well-lit environments and requires good air circulation to prevent disease.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
