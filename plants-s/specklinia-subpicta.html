Specklinia subpicta

Specklinia subpicta, commonly known as the speckled orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and strikingly patterned leaves that display a speckled appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often arranged in a raceme. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Specklinia subpicta is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
