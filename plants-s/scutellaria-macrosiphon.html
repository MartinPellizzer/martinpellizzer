Scutellaria macrosiphon

Scutellaria macrosiphon, commonly known as the large-lipped skullcap, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants known for their medicinal properties. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces striking blue to violet tubular flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or wetland habitats.

Scutellaria macrosiphon is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
