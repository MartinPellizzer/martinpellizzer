Scutellaria iskanderi

Scutellaria iskanderi, commonly known as Iskander's skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants known for their medicinal properties. The plant features square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Due to its limited distribution and unique morphology, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
