Sertuernera

Sertuernera, commonly known as the Christmas star, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its star-shaped, brightly colored flowers that bloom in the winter season. The plant typically grows as a shrub with woody stems and glossy, dark green leaves. Sertuernera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a potted plant.
