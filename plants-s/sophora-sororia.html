Sophora sororia

Sophora sororia, commonly known as the Chinese honey locust, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to East Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive yellow flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it beneficial for soil enrichment.
