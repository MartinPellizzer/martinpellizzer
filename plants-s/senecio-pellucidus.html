Senecio pellucidus

Senecio pellucidus, commonly known as the translucent groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Senecio pellucidus is adapted to arid conditions and is often found in regions with well-drained soils.
