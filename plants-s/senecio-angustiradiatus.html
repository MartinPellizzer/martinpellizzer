Senecio angustiradiatus

Senecio angustiradiatus, commonly known as the narrow-rayed groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions. It features narrow, linear leaves and produces small yellow flowers in clusters.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it may be invasive in some areas.
