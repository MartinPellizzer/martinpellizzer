Saintpaulia ionantha subsp. velutina

Saintpaulia ionantha subsp.

velutina, commonly known as the velvet violet. This subspecies is characterized by its velvety, dark green foliage and vibrant, long-lasting flowers in shades of violet and pink.

It is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa. The plant thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

It is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
