Sauromatum horsfieldii

Sauromatum horsfieldii, commonly known as the Horsfield's arrowroot, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. The plant produces large, showy flowers that are often used in ornamental horticulture. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense clumps in suitable conditions.

The foliage is typically arrow-shaped, giving it a distinctive and exotic appearance.
