Sadiria erecta

Sadiria erecta, commonly known as the upright sadiria, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and clusters of small, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it well-suited for xerophytic environments. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, adapted to store water efficiently.

Sadiria erecta is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in harsh climates.
