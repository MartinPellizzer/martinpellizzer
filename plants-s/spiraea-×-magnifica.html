Spiraea × magnifica

Spiraea × magnifica, commonly known as the weeping spiraea, is a deciduous shrub native to Asia.

It is characterized by its cascading clusters of white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant features arching branches that create a graceful, weeping appearance. Its leaves are ovate and dark green, providing a striking contrast to the white blooms.

Spiraea × magnifica is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
