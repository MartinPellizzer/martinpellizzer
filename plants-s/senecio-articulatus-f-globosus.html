Senecio articulatus f. globosus

Senecio articulatus f. globosus, commonly known as the globular senecio, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded growth with densely packed, fleshy leaves that form a spherical shape. The plant produces yellow flowers in late summer, which are borne on slender, branched stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
