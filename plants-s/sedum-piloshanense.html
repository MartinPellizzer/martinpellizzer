Sedum piloshanense

Sedum piloshanense, commonly known as the Shandong stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to China.

It is characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions. It has a compact growth habit, making it suitable for rock gardens and container gardening.

Sedum piloshanense is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to bloom throughout the summer months.
