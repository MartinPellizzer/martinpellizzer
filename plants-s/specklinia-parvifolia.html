Specklinia parvifolia

Specklinia parvifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved specklinia, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dark green, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Specklinia parvifolia thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

It is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
