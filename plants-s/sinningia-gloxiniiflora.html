Sinningia gloxiniiflora

Sinningia gloxiniiflora, commonly known as the gloxinia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its tuberous roots and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of violet, pink, and white. It is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental value.

Sinningia gloxiniiflora prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil for optimal growth.
