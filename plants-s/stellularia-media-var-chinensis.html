Stellularia media var. chinensis

Stellularia media var. chinensis, commonly known as Chinese starflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces small, star-shaped white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species for wetland restoration and ecological studies.
