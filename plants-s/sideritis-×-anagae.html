Sideritis × anagae

Sideritis × anagae, commonly known as the hybrid cat's whiskers, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in whorls along the stem. Sideritis × anagae is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments.

Its aromatic foliage and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
