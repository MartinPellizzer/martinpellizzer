Stelis anisopetala

Stelis anisopetala, commonly known as the variable stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features slender, wiry stems that often branch and bear clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged spirally along the stem, and its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture from the air. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Stelis anisopetala is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its delicate appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
