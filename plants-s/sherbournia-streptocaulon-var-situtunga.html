Sherbournia streptocaulon var. situtunga

Sherbournia streptocaulon var. situtunga, commonly known as the Situtunga bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland or rocky habitats.

This variant is notable for its ornamental value and is cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
