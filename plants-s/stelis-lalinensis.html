Stelis lalinensis

Stelis lalinensis, commonly known as the Lalin's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant typically produces a single, erect stem with several lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pink.

Stelis lalinensis thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
