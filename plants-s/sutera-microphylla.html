Sutera microphylla

Sutera microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved lantana, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring small, aromatic leaves and clusters of small, showy flowers. The flowers are usually white or pale blue and bloom in the summer months. The plant has a woody stem and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soils.
