Stelis viamontis

Stelis viamontis, commonly known as the Viamontis orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments within forest understories. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pale yellow.

Stelis viamontis is valued in horticulture for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
