Senecio cyclotus

Senecio cyclotus, commonly known as the spiral senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves around the stem, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its striking form and hardy nature, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
