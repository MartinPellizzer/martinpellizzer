Scutellaria horsfieldiana

Scutellaria horsfieldiana, commonly known as Horsfield's skullcap, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, two-lipped, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or rocky habitats.

Scutellaria horsfieldiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine.
