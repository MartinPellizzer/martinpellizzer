Senecio nagensium

Senecio nagensium, commonly known as the Natal Senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its fleshy, hairy leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Senecio nagensium is adapted to withstand drought conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
