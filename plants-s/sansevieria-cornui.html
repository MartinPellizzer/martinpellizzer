Sansevieria cornui

Sansevieria cornui, commonly known as the horned snake plant, is a striking succulent native to southern Africa.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are often ribbed and can have a waxy, bluish-green sheen. The plant is characterized by its unique, spiky inflorescence that resembles a horn, giving it its common name. It is highly drought-tolerant and thrives in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

The leaves are also known for their ability to purify the air, adding to its appeal as a decorative and functional plant.
