Spiranthes itatiaiensis

Spiranthes itatiaiensis, commonly known as the Itatiaia spiral orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to South America.

This perennial herbaceous plant is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers that spiral around the stem. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. The plant has a rhizomatous root system and produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late summer.

It is noted for its unique floral structure and is often found in association with other orchid species in its native habitat.
