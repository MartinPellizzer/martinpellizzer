Scorzonera laciniata var. tenuifolia

Scorzonera laciniata var. tenuifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved scorzonera, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features basal rosettes of narrow, linear leaves and produces tall, branched stems with yellowish-green flowers. The plant is characterized by its tuberous roots, which are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to full sun exposure.

Scorzonera laciniata var. tenuifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
