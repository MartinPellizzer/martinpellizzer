Senecio rutshuruensis

Senecio rutshuruensis, commonly known as the Rutshuru groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the Democratic Republic of the Congo.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
