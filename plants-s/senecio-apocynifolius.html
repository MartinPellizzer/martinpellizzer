Senecio apocynifolius

Senecio apocynifolius, commonly known as the purple coneflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking purple to violet flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces a tall, spiky inflorescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens.
