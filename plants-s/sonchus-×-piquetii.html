Sonchus × piquetii

Sonchus × piquetii, commonly known as the hybrid groundsel, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is a sterile hybrid resulting from the cross between Sonchus arvensis and Sonchus oleraceus. The plant typically grows to a height of 20–60 cm and features alternate, heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family.

It is often found in disturbed soils and is considered an invasive species in some regions.
