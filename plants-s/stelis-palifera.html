Stelis palifera

Stelis palifera, commonly known as the feather-leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and elongated, lance-shaped leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers with prominent yellow markings. It typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories.

Stelis palifera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique foliage and delicate blooms.
