Silene guntensis subsp. vallicola

Silene guntensis subsp.

The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in well-drained soils. Its leaves are linear and often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in its harsh habitat. Silene guntensis subsp.

vallicola is an important species in its ecosystem, supporting pollinators and contributing to the biodiversity of high-altitude regions.
