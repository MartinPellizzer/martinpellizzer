Sansevieria sessiliflora

Sansevieria sessiliflora, commonly known as the snake plant, is a hardy succulent native to Africa.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with yellow or white variegations. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers on slender stalks, which are usually white or green in color. It is well adapted to low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it an ideal indoor plant.

Its ability to purify the air and thrive in various environments has made it a popular choice for both residential and commercial spaces.
