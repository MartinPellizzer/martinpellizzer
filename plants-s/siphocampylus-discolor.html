Siphocampylus discolor

Siphocampylus discolor, commonly known as the silverleaf cowpea, is a flowering plant native to Central America and parts of South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant produces cylindrical, seed-filled pods that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soils.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and ecological studies.
