Senna williamsii

Senna williamsii, commonly known as the yellow senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters tall, characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow, pea-like flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and disturbed sites.

Senna williamsii is also valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
