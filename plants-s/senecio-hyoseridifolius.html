Senecio hyoseridifolius

Senecio hyoseridifolius, commonly known as the yellow brittle bush, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow flowers and brittle, often hairy stems. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showing adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are usually narrow and linear, with a rough texture.

It is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental value.
