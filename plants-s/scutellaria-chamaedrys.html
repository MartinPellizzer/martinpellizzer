Scutellaria chamaedrys

Scutellaria chamaedrys, commonly known as mouse-ear scullcap, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or dry soils, often found in woodland edges and grassy areas. The plant features small, tubular blue or violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are rounded and fuzzy, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Scutellaria chamaedrys is valued in traditional medicine for its potential medicinal properties.
