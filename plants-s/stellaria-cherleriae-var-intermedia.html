Stellaria cherleriae var. intermedia

Stellaria cherleriae var. intermedia, commonly known as the intermediate starwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family, which includes many flowering plants with showy blooms. The plant features slender stems and small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are typically opposite and lance-shaped, with a soft, hairy texture.

It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.
