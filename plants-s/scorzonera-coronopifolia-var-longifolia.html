Scorzonera coronopifolia var. longifolia

Scorzonera coronopifolia var. longifolia, commonly known as the longleaf scorzonera, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features basal rosettes of narrow, gray-green leaves with a fuzzy texture. The plant produces tall, slender flowering stems that bear yellow flowers with reflexed petals. Its roots are thick and fleshy, often used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
