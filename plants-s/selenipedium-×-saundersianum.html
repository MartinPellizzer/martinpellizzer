Selenipedium × saundersianum

Selenipedium × saundersianum, commonly known as the hybrid giant spider-orchid, is a rare and endangered orchid species native to the rainforests of northern Queensland, Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its tall, strap-like leaves and striking, spider-like flowers. The plant is a hybrid between Selenipedium australianum and Selenipedium saundersianum, resulting in unique morphological traits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific soil conditions for successful growth.

Due to habitat loss and limited natural propagation, conservation efforts are crucial for its survival.
