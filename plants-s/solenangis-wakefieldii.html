Solenangis wakefieldii

Solenangis wakefieldii, commonly known as the Wakefield's spider orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family native to South Africa.

This plant is characterized by its unique, spider-like appearance, with long, slender inflorescences bearing intricate, colorful flowers. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions. The plant's morphology includes narrow leaves and a compact, pseudobulbous structure typical of many orchids.

Solenangis wakefieldii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated for its distinctive floral display.
