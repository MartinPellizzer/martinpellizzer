Satyrium amoenum var. tsaratananae

Satyrium amoenum var. tsaratananae, commonly known as the Tsaratanana orchid, is a rare and endemic orchid species native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded forest environments, often in rocky or mossy areas. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish with red or brown markings, and it has a short, erect stem.
