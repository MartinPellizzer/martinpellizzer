Statice maurocordatae

Statice maurocordatae, commonly known as the Maurocordata statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy blooms that range from pink to red. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in full sun. Its narrow, grass-like leaves are arranged in a compact, upright habit, while its flowers are clustered in dense, spike-like inflorescences.

Statice maurocordatae is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower due to its long-lasting blooms.
