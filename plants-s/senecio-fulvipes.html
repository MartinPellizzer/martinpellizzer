Senecio fulvipes

Senecio fulvipes, commonly known as the yellow owl's clover, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Senecio fulvipes is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
