Synotoma comosum var. pubescens

Synotoma comosum var. pubescens, commonly known as the hairy synotoma, is a variant of the synotoma comosum species.

It is characterized by its pubescent or hairy foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically has a compact growth habit with dense clusters of leaves. Its botanical features include small, narrow leaves with a fibrous texture.

It is often found in arid or semi-arid environments, adapting well to dry conditions.
