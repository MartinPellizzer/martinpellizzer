Stellaria ciliatosepala var. arctica

Stellaria ciliatosepala var. arctica, commonly known as Arctic starwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Arctic and alpine regions.

It features small, white star-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has slender, hairy stems and small, lance-shaped leaves with ciliate margins. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to cold climates.

Its botanical characteristics make it a hardy species suitable for alpine gardens.
