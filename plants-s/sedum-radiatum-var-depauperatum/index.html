Sedum Radiatum Var. Depauperatum

Sedum radiatum var. depauperatum is a rare and striking succulent native to the rocky regions of southern Africa. Known for its compact growth and vibrant blue-green foliage, this plant thrives in harsh, arid environments. Its unique appearance makes it a favorite among collectors and succulent enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it is often overlooked due to its limited distribution and subtle form. This article explores the characteristics, care, and significance of this intriguing plant.
