Silene demirizii

Silene demirizii, commonly known as the Demiriz's campion, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its pink or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Silene demirizii is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
