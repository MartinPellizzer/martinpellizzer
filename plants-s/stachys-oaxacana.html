Stachys oaxacana

Stachys oaxacana, commonly known as Oaxacan betony, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features square stems and opposite leaves that are often hairy and lance-shaped. Its flowers are typically blue or purple, arranged in whorls along the stem.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses in herbal medicine.
