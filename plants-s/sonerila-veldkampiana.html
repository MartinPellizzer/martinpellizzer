Sonerila veldkampiana

Sonerila veldkampiana, commonly known as the Veldkamp's orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers. Its flowering period usually occurs during the warmer months, and it is often found in lowland and montane rainforests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
