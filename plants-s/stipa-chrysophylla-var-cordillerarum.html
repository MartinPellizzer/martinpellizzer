Stipa chrysophylla var. cordillerarum

Stipa chrysophylla var. cordillerarum, commonly known as the Sierra Nevada feathergrass, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant produces delicate, golden-yellow inflorescences that are highly ornamental. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in alpine and subalpine regions.
