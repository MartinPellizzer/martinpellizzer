Swainsona magnifica

Swainsona magnifica, commonly known as the magnificent swainsona, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is a legume, belonging to the Fabaceae family, and features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in dry, open habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections.
