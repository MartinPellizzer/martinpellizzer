Stenorrhynchos lanuginosum

Stenorrhynchos lanuginosum, commonly known as the woolly stenorrhynchos, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its hairy, or lanuginous, stems and leaves. The plant produces small, delicate orchid flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized species.
