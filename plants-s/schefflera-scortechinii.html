Schefflera scortechinii

Schefflera scortechinii, commonly known as the Philippine umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It features a distinctive, umbrella-like crown composed of multiple leafy shoots that radiate from a central stem. The plant has compound, pinnately arranged leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It produces small, white, clustered flowers followed by orange-red berries.

Schefflera scortechinii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
