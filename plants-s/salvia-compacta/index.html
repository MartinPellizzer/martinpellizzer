Salvia Compacta

Salvia compacta is a compact, drought-tolerant shrub known for its vibrant blue to violet flowers. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it ideal for rock gardens and xeriscaping. This plant is native to southern Africa and is prized for its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements. Its small stature and long blooming period add charm to gardens throughout the summer. Salvia compacta is also a great choice for attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
