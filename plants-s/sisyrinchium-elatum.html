Sisyrinchium elatum

Sisyrinchium elatum, commonly known as the showy blue-eyed grass, is a herbaceous perennial native to the United States.

Sisyrinchium elatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
