Scutellaria microflora

Scutellaria microflora, commonly known as the small-flowered skullcap, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of North America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged oppositely along the stem.

Scutellaria microflora is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in association with other woodland flora.
