Stenocarpha filiformis var. genuina

Stenocarpha filiformis var. genuina, commonly known as the slenderleaf globemallow, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and showy, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. It has a compact growth habit with multiple stems arising from the base.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local pollinators.
