Solanum nigrum var. flavum

Solanum nigrum var. flavum, commonly known as the yellow nightshade, is a variant of the common black nightshade.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe, Asia, and Africa. The plant produces small, yellow berries that are toxic to humans and animals. It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other toxic plants like tomatoes and potatoes.

Its botanical features include simple, alternate leaves and a flowering structure typical of the nightshade family.
