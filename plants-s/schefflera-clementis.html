Schefflera clementis

Schefflera clementis, commonly known as the Hawaiian schefflera, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Pacific, particularly Hawaii.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of leaves, which give it a lush, full appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits. Schefflera clementis thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its adaptability to various soil types makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
