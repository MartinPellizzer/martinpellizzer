Senecio spellenbergii

Senecio spellenbergii, commonly known as the Desert Senecio, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing form and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has succulent leaves that help it store water, adapting it to survive in dry environments. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and resilience, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and drought-tolerant landscapes.
