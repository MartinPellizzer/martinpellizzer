Solanum megalospermum

Solanum megalospermum, commonly known as the large-seeded nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows in humid, tropical environments. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. Its berries are usually red when mature and contain toxic alkaloids.

The species is part of the nightshade family, Solanaceae, which includes other plants like tomatoes and potatoes.
