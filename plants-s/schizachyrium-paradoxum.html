Schizachyrium paradoxum

Schizachyrium paradoxum, commonly known as little bluestem, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green blades and distinctive silver-gray inflorescence. This plant is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in prairie ecosystems. It plays a vital role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for various insects and small animals.

Schizachyrium paradoxum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in native plant landscaping.
