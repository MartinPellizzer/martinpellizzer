Seddera capensis var. minor

Seddera capensis var. minor, commonly known as the Cape seddera, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that store water, enabling it to survive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. Its leaves are typically narrow and may be covered in a fine layer of white powder, which helps reduce water loss.

It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
