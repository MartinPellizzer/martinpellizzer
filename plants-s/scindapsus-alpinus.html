Scindapsus alpinus

Scindapsus alpinus, commonly known as the silver vine, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, waxy leaves that often display a striking silver or variegated pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Scindapsus alpinus is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and air-purifying properties.
