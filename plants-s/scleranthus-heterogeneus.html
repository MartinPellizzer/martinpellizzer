Scleranthus heterogeneus

Scleranthus heterogeneus, commonly known as the variable sickleweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, white or pink flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils, such as roadsides and fields, and is often found in dry, open habitats. Its leaves are linear and covered with fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid conditions.

Scleranthus heterogeneus is an annual plant that completes its life cycle within a single growing season.
