Solanum anomalostemon

Solanum anomalostemon, commonly known as the strange-stamen nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is native to certain regions of Southeast Asia, particularly in countries like Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its distinctive stamens, which are often longer than the petals, giving it its common name. Its flowers are typically small and white or pale green, with a subtle fragrance.

Solanum anomalostemon is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks.
