Seriphidium turanicum var. pallidum

Seriphidium turanicum var. pallidum, commonly known as the pale tansy, is a flowering plant native to central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellowish-green foliage and small, composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. It has a creeping growth habit and produces a strong, aromatic scent.

Its medicinal properties have been historically used in traditional herbal remedies.
