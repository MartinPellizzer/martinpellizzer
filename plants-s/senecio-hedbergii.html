Senecio hedbergii

Senecio hedbergii, commonly known as the "Blue Elixir" or "Blue Ice," is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has wiry, silver-green stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Senecio hedbergii is also known for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
