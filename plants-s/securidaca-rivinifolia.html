Securidaca rivinifolia

Securidaca rivinifolia, commonly known as the river cow-plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height, characterized by its pinnately compound leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, dry fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found near water sources, such as rivers and streams.
