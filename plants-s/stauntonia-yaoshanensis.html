Stauntonia yaoshanensis

Stauntonia yaoshanensis, commonly known as the Yaoshan sterculia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Sterculiaceae and is characterized by its showy, pale yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a velvety texture.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds, contributing to its role in local ecosystems.
