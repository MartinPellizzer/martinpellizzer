Styrax Carranzae

Styrax carranzae is a rare and enigmatic plant species native to the mountainous regions of Central America. It is known for its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in early spring. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests. Due to its limited distribution and habitat loss, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique botanical gem.
