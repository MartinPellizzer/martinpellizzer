Stelis hydroidea

Stelis hydroidea, commonly known as the water stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, often pendulous flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic growth habit. Its leaves are usually narrow and elongated, adapted for efficient water absorption.

The flowers are often white or pale pink, with intricate patterns that attract pollinators.
