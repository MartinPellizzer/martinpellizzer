Sideritis × engleriana

Sideritis × engleriana, commonly known as the Engler's ironwort, is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its aromatic, glandular leaves and small, tubular flowers that typically range in color from pink to purple. Native to the mountainous regions of the Mediterranean, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure. The plant is known for its medicinal properties, with the dried flowers used in traditional herbal remedies.

Its ornamental value also makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine displays.
