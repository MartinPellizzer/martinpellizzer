Spathiphyllum candolleanum

Spathiphyllum candolleanum, commonly known as the Candolleanum peace lily, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green heart-shaped leaves and its striking white spathified flower, which resembles a spatula. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its air-purifying qualities. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers inside the spathe, which are followed by bright red berries.

This species is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
