Stevia caracasana

Stevia caracasana, commonly known as sweet leaf or caracas stevia, is a perennial herb native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are rich in steviol glycosides, natural sweeteners. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and can reach heights of up to 1 meter. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in clusters, and it is often cultivated for its sweet-tasting leaves.

Stevia caracasana is valued for its use in herbal teas and as a natural sweetener alternative.
