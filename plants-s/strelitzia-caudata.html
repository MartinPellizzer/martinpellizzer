Strelitzia caudata

Strelitzia caudata, commonly known as the Orange Banana.

It is a striking tropical plant native to South Africa, characterized by its long, banana-like leaves and vibrant orange flowers. The plant belongs to the Strelitziaceae family and is known for its unique inflorescence, which resembles a bird's beak. Its foliage is broad and glossy, making it an attractive ornamental plant.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
