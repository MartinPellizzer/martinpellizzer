Solanum suaveolens subsp. microphyllidium

Solanum suaveolens subsp.

microphyllidium, commonly known as the small-leaved nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of South America. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

The plant features small, oval-shaped leaves that are usually hairy and have a distinct aromatic scent. Its flowers are typically white or pale purple, with five petals and a short corolla.

The fruit is a spherical berry that changes color from green to yellow or red as it matures.
