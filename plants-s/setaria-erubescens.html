Setaria erubescens

Setaria erubescens, commonly known as red panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a member of the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense, tufted foliage. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets. Its leaves are typically narrow and have a bluish-green hue, with a rough texture.

Setaria erubescens is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
