Stellaria xanthanthera

Stellaria xanthanthera, commonly known as the yellow starwort, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and the Pacific Islands.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to yellow. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a soft, hairy texture.

Stellaria xanthanthera is often used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and healing properties.
