Stellaria jacutica

Stellaria jacutica, commonly known as the narrowleaf starwort, is a low-growing annual herb native to Central Asia.

It features small, white or pale blue flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming from June to August. The plant has slender stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Stellaria jacutica is part of the Caryophyllaceae family, which includes other flowering plants with showy blooms.
