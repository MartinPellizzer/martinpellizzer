Statice × profusa

Statice × profusa, commonly known as sea lavender, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Plumbaginaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, spike-like inflorescences composed of numerous small, showy blue or purple flowers. Statice × profusa is widely cultivated as a ornamental plant for its long-lasting blooms and ability to thrive in dry, sunny conditions.
