Stemodiopsis buchananii var. buchananii

Stemodiopsis buchananii var. buchananii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, herbaceous stem and fleshy, basal leaves that form a rosette. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, typically during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This species is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
