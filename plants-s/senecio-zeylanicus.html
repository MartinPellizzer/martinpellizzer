Senecio zeylanicus

Senecio zeylanicus, commonly known as the Ceylon groundsel, is a flowering plant native to Sri Lanka.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial with lance-shaped leaves and yellow flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forests and along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the local flora and ecosystem.
