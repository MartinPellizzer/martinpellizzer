Sansevieria latifolia

Sansevieria latifolia, commonly known as the Snake Plant, is a succulent plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are broad and often variegated with light green and yellow patterns. The plant is well adapted to low-light conditions and requires minimal water, making it an ideal choice for indoor environments. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, storing water to help it survive in arid conditions.

Sansevieria latifolia is also noted for its ability to improve indoor air quality by filtering toxins.
