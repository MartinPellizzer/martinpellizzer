Sempervivum clusianum

Sempervivum clusianum, commonly known as the red-silver hens and chicks, is a succulent plant native to the Alps.

It features rosettes of fleshy, silver-tipped leaves that turn red in full sun. The plant produces small, pink or purple flowers in the spring. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil.

Sempervivum clusianum is often used in rock gardens and as a ground cover due to its hardy nature.
