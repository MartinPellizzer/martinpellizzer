Scutellaria rehderiana

Scutellaria rehderiana, commonly known as Rehder's skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants known for their medicinal properties. The plant features square stems, opposite leaves, and small, two-lipped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique appearance and potential medicinal value, it is of interest to botanists and herbalists alike.
