Syzygium neglectum

Syzygium neglectum, commonly known as the small-fruited tamarind.

It is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical to ovate in shape. Its small, yellowish-green flowers are followed by edible, sour-tasting fruits.

Syzygium neglectum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and traditional medicinal uses.
