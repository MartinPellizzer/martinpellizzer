Swertia turfosa

Swertia turfosa, commonly known as the Turf Gentian, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or grassy habitats at high altitudes. Its leaves are usually narrow and arranged in a basal rosette, while its stems are erect and branched.

Swertia turfosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
