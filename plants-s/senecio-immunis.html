Senecio immunis

Senecio immunis, commonly known as the Ice Plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. Its scientific name reflects its immunity to certain pests, which is a notable trait in its cultivation.

Senecio immunis is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
