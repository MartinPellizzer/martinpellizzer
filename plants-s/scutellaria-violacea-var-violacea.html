Scutellaria violacea var. violacea

Scutellaria violacea var. violacea, commonly known as violet scutellaria, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, particularly China.

It is a member of the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, violet to blue flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or shaded areas.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is cultivated in gardens and traditional herbal practices.
