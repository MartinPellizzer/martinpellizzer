Sansevieria grandicuspis

Sansevieria grandicuspis, commonly known as the footstool plant, is a species of snake plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are dark green with distinctive yellow or white variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It is highly drought-tolerant and thrives in low-light conditions, making it an ideal indoor plant.

Its air-purifying qualities and ease of care have contributed to its popularity among houseplant enthusiasts.
