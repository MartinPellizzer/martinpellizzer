Stellaria palustris f. minima

Stellaria palustris f. minima, commonly known as the dwarf starwort, is a low-growing, herbaceous plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, star-shaped white flowers and slender, ascending stems. This variety is typically found in moist, sandy soils in wetlands and along the edges of ponds. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slight pubescence.

The plant is noted for its ability to thrive in wet or waterlogged conditions, making it a common sight in marshy environments.
