Senecio lugens var. hookeri

Senecio lugens var. hookeri, commonly known as the Hooker's Senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces showy yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is often cultivated in arid and semi-arid regions.
