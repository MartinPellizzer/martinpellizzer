Saxifraga shennongii

Saxifraga shennongii, commonly known as the Shennong Mountain saxifrage, is a rare flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming growth habit. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers during the summer months. It thrives in rocky, alpine environments and is adapted to cold climates.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered an endangered species.
