Streptopus amplexifolius var. americanus

Streptopus amplexifolius var. americanus, commonly known as the American starwort, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It features a rosette of broad, heart-shaped leaves that clasp the stem, giving it its scientific name. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped white flowers in early spring. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its foliage is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported healing properties.
