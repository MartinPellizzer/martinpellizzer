Solanum microleprodes

Solanum microleprodes, commonly known as the small-leaved nightshade, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is native to parts of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features small, oval-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers. Its fruits are berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

The species is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in shaded, moist environments.
