Sesamoides canescens var. alpina

Sesamoides canescens var. alpina, commonly known as the alpine sesamoid, is a rare and specialized plant adapted to high-altitude environments.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically arranged in clusters, and it thrives in rocky or gravelly soils.
