Sabina gossainthanea

Sabina gossainthanea, commonly known as the Gossainthan hill juniper, is a rare and endemic species found in the Eastern Himalayas.

It is a small evergreen shrub or tree with dense, scale-like leaves that are typically blue-gray in color. The plant produces small, cone-like structures that contain the seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and well-drained soils.
