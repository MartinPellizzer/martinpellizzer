Spathiphyllum × hybridum

Spathiphyllum × hybridum, commonly known as the peace lily, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its dark green, waxy leaves and its showy, white spathaceous bract that encloses the small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant produces both male and female reproductive structures, with the spadix containing the actual flowers. It is native to tropical regions of the Americas and is widely cultivated as a decorative indoor plant.

The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its air-purifying qualities.
