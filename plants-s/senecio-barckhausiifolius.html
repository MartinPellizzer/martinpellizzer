Senecio barckhausiifolius

Senecio barckhausiifolius, commonly known as the Barckhaus's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and sometimes hairy, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in clusters.
