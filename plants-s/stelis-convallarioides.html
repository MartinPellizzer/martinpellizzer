Stelis convallarioides

Stelis convallarioides, commonly known as the coral vine, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas, particularly in countries like Brazil and Mexico. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, arranged in clusters and resembling those of the common ivy.

Stelis convallarioides thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in the wild.
