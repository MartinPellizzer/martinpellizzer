Saccharum officinarum var. fragile

Saccharum officinarum var. fragile, commonly known as the delicate sugarcane.

This variant is characterized by its thinner stems and reduced sugar content compared to standard sugarcane varieties. It exhibits a more tender structure, making it less suitable for industrial sugar production but ideal for ornamental purposes. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical climates, requiring ample sunlight and water.

Its botanical features include long, narrow leaves and a fibrous root system, which contribute to its unique ecological niche.
