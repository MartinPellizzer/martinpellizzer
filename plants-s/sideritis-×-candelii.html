Sideritis × candelii

Sideritis × candelii, commonly known as the Greek heather or candle heather, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. Sideritis × candelii is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

Its foliage is typically covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft, textured appearance.
