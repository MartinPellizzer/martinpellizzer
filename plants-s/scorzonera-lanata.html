Scorzonera lanata

Scorzonera lanata, commonly known as the woolly scorzonera, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a hairy, tuberous root system that is often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces showy, yellow or orange flowers with prominent, reflexed petals. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a woolly appearance.

Scorzonera lanata thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.
