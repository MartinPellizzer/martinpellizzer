Stelis tessmannii

Stelis tessmannii, commonly known as the orchid of the genus Stelis, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is an epiphytic orchid, meaning it grows on the surface of other plants without being parasitic. The plant typically has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and yellow. It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

Stelis tessmannii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
