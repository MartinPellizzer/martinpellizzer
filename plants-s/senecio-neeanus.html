Senecio neeanus

Senecio neeanus, commonly known as the Neenan's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid environments. It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small yellow flowers that bloom in the spring.

Senecio neeanus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its hardy nature and aesthetic appeal.
