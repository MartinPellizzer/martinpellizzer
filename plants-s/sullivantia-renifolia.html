Sullivantia renifolia

Sullivantia renifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaf sullivantia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, wooded areas and is often found in the understory of forests. Its botanical features include smooth, dark green foliage and a compact growth habit.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
