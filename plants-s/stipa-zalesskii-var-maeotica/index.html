Stipa Zalesskii Var. Maeotica

Stipa zalesskii var. maeotica is a rare and fascinating grass species native to the steppes of southern Russia and Crimea. This variant is distinguished by its unique morphological features and ecological adaptations to its harsh environment. It thrives in dry, open habitats and plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity. Due to habitat loss and climate change, it faces increasing threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are essential to protect this plant and preserve its ecological significance.
