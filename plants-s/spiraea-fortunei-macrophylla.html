Spiraea fortunei macrophylla

Spiraea fortunei macrophylla, commonly known as the golden spiraea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy, flat-topped clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has dark green, ovate leaves that are often larger than those of other Spiraea species. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Spiraea fortunei macrophylla is widely used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardiness.
