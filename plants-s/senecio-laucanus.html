Senecio laucanus

Senecio laucanus, commonly known as the yellow star flower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that resemble stars. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, daisy-like blooms.

Senecio laucanus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and drought tolerance.
