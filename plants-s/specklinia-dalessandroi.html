Specklinia dalessandroi

Specklinia dalessandroi, commonly known as the Dalessandro's Vanda, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is a terrestrial orchid with thick, fleshy roots adapted to its humid, tropical environment. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with a distinctive speckled pattern on the petals. It thrives in shaded, moist habitats and is prized for its ornamental value.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
