Scrophularia scopolii var. adenocalyx

Scrophularia scopolii var. adenocalyx, commonly known as the rock scrophularia, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments. The plant features erect stems that bear opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, arranged in whorls along the stem, and bloom in late spring to early summer.

The species is noted for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
