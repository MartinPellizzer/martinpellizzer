Scaphyglottis panamensis

Scaphyglottis panamensis, commonly known as the Panama hooded orchid, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central America.

Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, while its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences.

Scaphyglottis panamensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
