Sedum tectorum

Sedum tectorum, commonly known as the roof sedum, is a low-growing succulent native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped leaves that are typically green or reddish in color. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
