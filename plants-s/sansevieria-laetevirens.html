Sansevieria laetevirens

Sansevieria laetevirens, commonly known as the "Black Gold" snake plant, is a perennial evergreen plant native to tropical Africa.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are dark green with prominent, light green longitudinal stripes. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it highly adaptable to indoor environments. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, which helps store water, contributing to its resilience in arid conditions.

Sansevieria laetevirens is also noted for its air-purifying qualities, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
