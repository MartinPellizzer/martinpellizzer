Sauromatum nubicum

Sauromatum nubicum, commonly known as the giant arrowroot or arrowhead plant, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 2 meters in length. It produces striking, showy inflorescences with a spathe and spadix, typical of the Araceae family.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has also been used in traditional medicine.
