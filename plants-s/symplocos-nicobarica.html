Symplocos nicobarica

Symplocos nicobarica, commonly known as Nicobar beautyberry, is a flowering plant native to the Nicobar Islands in the Indian Ocean.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its fruit is a bright red berry that develops from these flowers and is edible for some bird species.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has limited use in traditional medicine.
