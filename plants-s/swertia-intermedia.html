Swertia intermedia

Swertia intermedia, commonly known as blue gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are tubular with prominent blue corollas, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Swertia intermedia is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
