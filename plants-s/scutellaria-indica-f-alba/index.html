Scutellaria Indica F. Alba

Scutellaria indica f. alba is a rare and lesser-known variant of the blue skullcap plant, native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Asia. This plant is characterized by its pale blue to white flowers, which contrast sharply with its dark green foliage. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest areas or along stream banks. Due to its limited distribution and unique appearance, it is considered a valuable species for botanical study and conservation efforts. Despite its beauty, Scutellaria indica f. alba remains underappreciated and poorly understood by the general public.
