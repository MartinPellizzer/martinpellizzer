Sarcopodium laurifolium

Sarcopodium laurifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf sarcopodium, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive spathe and spadix inflorescence. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
