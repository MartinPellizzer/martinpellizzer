Spiraea bracteata var. tosaensis

Spiraea bracteata var. tosaensis, commonly known as Tosa Spiraea, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features small, white to pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, arching branches and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for borders or as a specimen plant.
