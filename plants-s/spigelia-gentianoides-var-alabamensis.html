Spigelia gentianoides var. alabamensis

Spigelia gentianoides var. alabamensis, commonly known as the Alabama spigelia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red.
