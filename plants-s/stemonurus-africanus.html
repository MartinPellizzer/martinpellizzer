Stemonurus africanus

Stemonurus africanus, commonly known as the African stemless bellflower, is a member of the Campanulaceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its erect, leafless stems that bear clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers. Stemonurus africanus is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
