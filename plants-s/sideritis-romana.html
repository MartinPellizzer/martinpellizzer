Sideritis romana

Sideritis romana, commonly known as Roman catnip, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, tubular blue or violet flowers. Its leaves are often aromatic and can be used in traditional herbal remedies.

Sideritis romana thrives in rocky or limestone soils and is often found in dry, sunny environments.
