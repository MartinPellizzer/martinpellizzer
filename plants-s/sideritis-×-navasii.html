Sideritis × navasii

Sideritis × navasii, commonly known as the hybrid sainfoin, is a member of the Lamiaceae family.

It is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region, characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats.

Sideritis × navasii is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
