Santolina canescens

Santolina canescens, commonly known as silver woolly lavender, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and compact, rounded growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant yellow flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Santolina canescens is also valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
