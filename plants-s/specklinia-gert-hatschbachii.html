Specklinia gert-hatschbachii

Specklinia gert-hatschbachii, commonly known as the Hatzschbach's speckled fern, is a rare epiphytic orchid native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its compact, clustered growth habit and dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, often with a subtle fragrance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high air humidity and indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a valuable and delicate species in botanical collections.
