Sedum laxum subsp. retusum

Sedum laxum subsp.

retusum, commonly known as the smooth stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Europe and western Asia. It features fleshy, upright stems and small, fleshy leaves that are typically green or reddish in color.

The plant produces clusters of star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking low-maintenance, decorative plants.
