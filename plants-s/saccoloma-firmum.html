Saccoloma firmum

Saccoloma firmum, commonly known as the firm stem saccoloma, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Hymenophyllaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its reproductive structures, known as sori, are clustered on the underside of the leaves.

Saccoloma firmum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
