Stellaria yezoensis

Stellaria yezoensis, commonly known as Yezo starwort, is a flowering plant native to East Asia, particularly Japan and parts of Russia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, star-shaped white or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and meadows. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered with fine hairs, while its stems are slender and branching.
