Spartium linifolium

Spartium linifolium, commonly known as slender spartium, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, pale blue to purple flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

Spartium linifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
