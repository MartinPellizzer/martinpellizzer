Skimmia japonica f. yatabei

Skimmia japonica f. yatabei, commonly known as the Yatabei skimmia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters during late winter to early spring. These flowers are typically white or pale pink and are followed by orange-red berries.

It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
