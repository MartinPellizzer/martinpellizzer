Solanum retroflexum

Solanum retroflexum, commonly known as the purpleleaf nightshade, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is native to parts of South America, particularly Argentina and Chile, and is characterized by its distinctive purple foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by berries that mature to a dark purple color. Solanum retroflexum is an herbaceous perennial that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both ecological and horticultural studies.
