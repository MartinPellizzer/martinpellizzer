Spilanthes paniculata

Spilanthes paniculata, commonly known as the goatweed or stinkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the genus Spilanthes within the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers. The plant is characterized by its branched inflorescence and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.
