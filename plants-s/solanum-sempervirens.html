Solanum sempervirens

Solanum sempervirens, commonly known as evergreen nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and slightly hairy. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale green flowers that bloom in summer. Its berries, which are typically yellow or orange, are toxic and should not be consumed.

Solanum sempervirens is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
