Stipa leptothera var. atroviolacea

Stipa leptothera var. atroviolacea, commonly known as the dark violet feather grass, is a variant of the slender needlegrass species.

It is a perennial grass native to parts of southern Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant is characterized by its fine, wiry leaves and delicate, feathery inflorescences that give it a soft, elegant appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland habitats.

The dark violet coloration of its flower heads distinguishes it from other varieties within the Stipa genus.
