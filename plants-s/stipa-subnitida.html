Stipa subnitida

Stipa subnitida, commonly known as slender feathergrass, is a perennial grass native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and dense clusters of fine, needle-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are typically pale green to golden in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine and steppe environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
