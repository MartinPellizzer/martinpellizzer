Spiraea callosa var. robusta

Spiraea callosa var. robusta, commonly known as the robust spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features dense, upright growth with clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has finely toothed, dark green leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Spiraea callosa var. robusta is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in USDA hardiness zones 4 through 8.
