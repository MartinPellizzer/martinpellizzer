Stachys canariensis

Stachys canariensis, commonly known as Canary hedge or narrow-leaved hedge, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of pink or purple. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for hedges and ornamental gardens.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable plant for ecological and horticultural purposes.
