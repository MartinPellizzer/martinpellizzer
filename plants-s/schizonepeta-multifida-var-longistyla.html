Schizonepeta multifida var. longistyla

Schizonepeta multifida var. longistyla, commonly known as the long-styled fragrant germander, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant features small, tubular flowers that are typically pale blue to purple in color, arranged in dense spikes. Its leaves are often lanceolate and have a distinct, aromatic scent.

This variety is distinguished by its elongated staminodes, which contribute to its unique floral morphology.
