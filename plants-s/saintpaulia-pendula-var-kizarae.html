Saintpaulia pendula var. kizarae

Saintpaulia pendula var. kizarae, commonly known as the Kizarae African violet, is a rare and striking variant of the African violet genus.

It features pendulous flowers that dangle from the plant, creating a dramatic visual effect. The plant is characterized by its velvety, dark green foliage and vibrant, bi-color blooms. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and humidity.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in indoor gardens.
