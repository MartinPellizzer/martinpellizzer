Syngonanthus densifolius var. pilosior

Syngonanthus densifolius var. pilosior, commonly known as the hairy-leaved evergreen shrub, is a tropical plant characterized by its dense, dark green foliage.

It features small, oval leaves that are covered in a fine, hairy texture, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white flowers that bloom throughout the year, attracting various pollinators. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical rainforests.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm climates.
