Solanum minutum

Solanum minutum, commonly known as the small nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It is a small, herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 10-30 centimeters. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by red, berry-like fruits. Solanum minutum belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae), which includes other familiar plants such as tomatoes and potatoes.

It is often found in disturbed soils and can be invasive in certain regions.
