Solanum comptum

Solanum comptum, commonly known as the slender nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30-60 cm, featuring hairy stems and lobed leaves. Its small, yellowish flowers are followed by berries that mature to a dark purple color.

Solanum comptum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional uses in herbal medicine.
