Spiraea luxurians

Spiraea luxurians, commonly known as the weeping spiraea, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its dense, cascading clusters of white or pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has slender, arching branches that give it a graceful, weeping appearance. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and dark green, turning yellow in the fall.

Spiraea luxurians prefers moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
