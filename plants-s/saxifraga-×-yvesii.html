Saxifraga × yvesii

Saxifraga × yvesii, commonly known as the pink saxifrage, is a hybrid plant that belongs to the Saxifragaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of Europe, it thrives in cool, moist environments with well-drained soil. The plant is often cultivated in rock gardens and alpine settings due to its hardy nature and ornamental appeal.

Its foliage is typically dark green with a slightly hairy texture, adding visual interest throughout the growing season.
