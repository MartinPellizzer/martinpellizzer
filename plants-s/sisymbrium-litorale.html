Sisymbrium litorale

Sisymbrium litorale, commonly known as sea rocket, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It is an annual or biennial herb that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils along shorelines. The plant features slender, branched stems and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are pinnately divided and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Sisymbrium litorale plays a role in stabilizing coastal dunes and provides nectar for pollinators.
