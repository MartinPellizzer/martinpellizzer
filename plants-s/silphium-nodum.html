Silphium nodum

Silphium nodum, commonly known as the nodding silphium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features a tall, erect stem with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its most distinctive feature is its nodding, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer.

Silphium nodum is known for its medicinal properties and has been historically used in traditional herbal remedies.
