Stelis thomasiae

Stelis thomasiae, commonly known as the Thomasia stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on tree trunks or branches in humid, shaded forest environments. The plant has slender, branching stems and produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white and pink. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
