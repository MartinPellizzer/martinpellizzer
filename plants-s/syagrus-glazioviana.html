Syagrus glazioviana

Syagrus glazioviana, commonly known as the Glaziov's palm, is a striking tropical plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like leaves that give it a dramatic appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers followed by bright red fruits, which are an important food source for local wildlife. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas and lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is also cultivated in gardens and parks in tropical and subtropical regions.
