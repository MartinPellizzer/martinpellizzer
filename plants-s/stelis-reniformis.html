Stelis reniformis

Stelis reniformis, commonly known as the kidney orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, kidney-shaped pseudobulbs and dark green, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Stelis reniformis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a tropical or subtropical setting.
