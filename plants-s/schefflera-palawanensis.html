Schefflera palawanensis

Schefflera palawanensis, commonly known as the Palawan umbrella tree, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like crown of large, glossy leaves. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are usually leathery and arranged in a whorled pattern around the stems.

Schefflera palawanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
