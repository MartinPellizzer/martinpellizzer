Sphacele tenuiflora

Sphacele tenuiflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered sphacele, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Amaranthaceae.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or red. The plant has slender, branching stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa, where it thrives in sandy or rocky soils.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its ornamental value.
