Saxifraga bidens

Saxifraga bidens, commonly known as the common saxifrage, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in alpine or arctic regions. The plant features basal leaves that are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, with small, white to pinkish flowers arranged in clusters. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Saxifraga bidens is known for its resilience to harsh environmental conditions and is often used in rock gardens due to its ornamental value.
