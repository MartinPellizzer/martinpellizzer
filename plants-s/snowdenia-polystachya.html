Snowdenia polystachya

Snowdenia polystachya, commonly known as the Welsh mountain heath, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Wales.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming structure. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in late summer. It plays an important ecological role in supporting local insect and bird populations.
