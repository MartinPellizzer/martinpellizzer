Senecio tatsienensis

Senecio tatsienensis, commonly known as the Tatsienlung groundsel, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright, branched stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small yellow flowers arranged in composite heads, typical of the daisy family. It thrives in rocky or arid environments and is often found in alpine regions.
