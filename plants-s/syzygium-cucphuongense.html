Syzygium cucphuongense

Syzygium cucphuongense, commonly known as Cuc Phuong apple.

It is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family, native to Vietnam. The plant is characterized by its small, red, apple-like fruits that are typically harvested in the fall. The leaves are elliptical, glossy, and arranged alternately along the stems.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments.
