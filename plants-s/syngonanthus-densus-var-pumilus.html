Syngonanthus densus var. pumilus

Syngonanthus densus var. pumilus, commonly known as the dwarf evergreen hibiscus, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features small, glossy, dark green leaves and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and dense foliage.

Syngonanthus densus var. pumilus is relatively low-maintenance, making it suitable for gardens and indoor spaces.
