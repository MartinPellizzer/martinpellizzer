Smithia blanda

Smithia blanda, commonly known as the common smithia, is a perennial herb native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It typically grows to a height of 10 to 30 centimeters and features small, lance-shaped leaves arranged in a basal rosette. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white or pale pink flowers during the spring or early summer. Its flowering stems are usually unbranched and bear the inflorescence at the apex.

Smithia blanda is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
