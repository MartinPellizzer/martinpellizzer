Solanum campanulatum

Solanum campanulatum, commonly known as the bellflower nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to violet, typically blooming in summer. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to lanceolate in shape. It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae), which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes.

Solanum campanulatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
