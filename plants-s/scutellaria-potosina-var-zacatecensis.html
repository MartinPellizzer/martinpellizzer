Scutellaria potosina var. zacatecensis

Scutellaria potosina var. zacatecensis, commonly known as the Zacatecan skullcap, is a perennial herb native to Mexico.

It belongs to the mint family (Lamiaceae) and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue to purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or rocky habitats.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
