Senecio tournefortii var. aragonensis

Senecio tournefortii var. aragonensis, commonly known as the Aragon Senecio, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. This perennial shrub typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its yellow flowers bloom in summer, adding a vibrant touch to its natural habitat.

The plant is often found in Mediterranean regions, where it thrives in full sun and well-drained soils.
