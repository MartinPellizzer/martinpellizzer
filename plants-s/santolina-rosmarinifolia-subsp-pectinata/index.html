Santolina Rosmarinifolia Subsp. Pectinata

Santolina rosmarinifolia subsp. pectinata is a rare and charming plant native to the Canary Islands. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its compact, rounded growth habit. This subspecies features silvery-gray foliage that shimmers in the sun and small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. It is well-suited for rock gardens and containers due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance needs. Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a prized addition to any Mediterranean-style garden.
