Stelis uninervia

Stelis uninervia, commonly known as the narrow-veined orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its slender, epiphytic growth habit, often found growing on trees or rocks. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, with a distinctive three-lobed lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
