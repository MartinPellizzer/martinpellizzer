Statice limonoides

Statice limonoides, commonly known as lemon verbena, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, aromatic leaves and yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is often cultivated for its fragrant foliage and ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens and containers.

Its essential oils are also used in aromatherapy and natural remedies.
