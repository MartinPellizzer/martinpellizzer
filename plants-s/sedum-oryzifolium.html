Sedum oryzifolium

Sedum oryzifolium, commonly known as riceleaf stonecrop, is a low-growing succulent native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It features fleshy, narrow leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact size and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking easy-care, drought-resistant plants.
