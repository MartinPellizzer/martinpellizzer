Scrophularia heucheriiflora

Scrophularia heucheriiflora, commonly known as the Heucheriiflora Snapdragon, is a perennial herb native to Europe.

It features upright stems that bear opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces showy, two-lipped flowers in shades of pink, red, and purple, typically blooming from June to August. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
