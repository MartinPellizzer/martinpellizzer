Stemona versteegii

Stemona versteegii, commonly known as the Chinese starwort, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features slender, cylindrical stems that are often branched and covered in small, scale-like leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale green flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne on short pedicels. Its roots are notable for their medicinal properties, containing alkaloids used in traditional medicine.

Stemona versteegii thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.
