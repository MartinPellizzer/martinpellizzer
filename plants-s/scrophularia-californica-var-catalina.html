Scrophularia californica var. catalina

Scrophularia californica var. catalina, commonly known as Catalina dogbane, is a perennial herb native to the coastal regions of southern California.

It typically grows in rocky soils and coastal bluffs, thriving in full sun to partial shade. The plant features slender, branching stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its flowers are tubular, pale purple to pink, and bloom in late spring to early summer.

It is adapted to coastal climates and is often found in areas with mild, Mediterranean-type weather.
