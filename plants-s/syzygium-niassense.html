Syzygium niassense

Syzygium niassense, commonly known as the Niassense tamarind, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant produces edible fruits that are similar in appearance to tamarind but have a distinct flavor. Syzygium niassense is often found in moist, shaded environments and is valued for both its ornamental and potential medicinal properties.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in tropical botany.
