Schefflera dananensis

Schefflera dananensis, commonly known as the Danan Island Schefflera, is a species of flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like cluster of leaves, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically followed by bright red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Schefflera dananensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
