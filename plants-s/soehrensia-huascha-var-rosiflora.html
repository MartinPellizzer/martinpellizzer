Soehrensia huascha var. rosiflora

Soehrensia huascha var. rosiflora, commonly known as the pink-flowered huascha, is a species native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a shrub characterized by its woody stems and dense foliage. The plant produces small, showy flowers that are typically pink or red in color, which bloom in clusters. Its leaves are elliptical and have a leathery texture, often with a slight pubescence.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual interest.
