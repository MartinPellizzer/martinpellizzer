Scaligeria polyantha

Scaligeria polyantha, commonly known as the clustered scaleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its small, clustered inflorescences produce yellow or orange flowers that attract pollinators.
