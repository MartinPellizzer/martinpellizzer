Stipa joannis f. okensis

Stipa joannis f. okensis, commonly known as the Okongoni grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and produces dense, cylindrical spikelets that are typically pale green to brown in color. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas. It has a fibrous root system that helps stabilize soil and prevent erosion.

This grass is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local wildlife.
