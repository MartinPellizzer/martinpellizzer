Spilanthes exasperata var. cayennensis

Spilanthes exasperata var. cayennensis, commonly known as the Cayenne pepper plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces tiny, rounded fruits that contain seeds, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

The species is also noted for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
