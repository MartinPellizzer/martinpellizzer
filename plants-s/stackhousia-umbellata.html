Stackhousia umbellata

Stackhousia umbellata, commonly known as the mountain daisy or rock daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils, often in mountainous regions. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in an umbel, giving it its scientific name.
