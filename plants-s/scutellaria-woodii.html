Scutellaria woodii

Scutellaria woodii, commonly known as Wood's blueweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Scutellaria woodii is typically found in moist, shaded habitats such as woodland floors and stream banks.
