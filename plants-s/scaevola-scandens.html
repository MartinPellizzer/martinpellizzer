Scaevola scandens

Scaevola scandens, commonly known as the beach morning glory, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Americas.

It is a trailing shrub that produces showy, bilaterally symmetrical blue or violet flowers. The plant has small, ovate leaves and is adapted to sandy, well-drained soils. It thrives in full sun and is often found in dune environments.

Scaevola scandens is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize coastal soils.
