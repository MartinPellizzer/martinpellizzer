Sideritis salina

Sideritis salina, commonly known as saltbush or sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and small, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of blue and purple. Sideritis salina is adapted to saline and coastal environments, making it a hardy species that thrives in harsh conditions.

Its botanical features make it both ecologically significant and potentially useful in traditional medicine.
