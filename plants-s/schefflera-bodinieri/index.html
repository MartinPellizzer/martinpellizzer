Schefflera Bodinieri

Bodin's umbrella plant, scientifically known as Schefflera bodinieri, is a striking tropical evergreen native to Southeast Asia. With its lush, umbrella-like foliage, it is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening. This plant is characterized by its dense clusters of large, glossy leaves that give it a unique and elegant appearance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to maintain its vibrant health. Its adaptability and ornamental value make it a favorite among plant enthusiasts worldwide.
