Strophanthus speciosus

Strophanthus speciosus, commonly known as the African blood lily, is a striking plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that produces striking, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically arranged in dense, spike-like clusters, and it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
