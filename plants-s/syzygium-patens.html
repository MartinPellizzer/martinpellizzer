Syzygium patens

Syzygium patens, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, flame-colored flowers that bloom in clusters during the wet season. The plant belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptical, with a glossy green surface.

The fruit is a small, woody capsule that contains several seeds.
