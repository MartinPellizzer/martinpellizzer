Stephania papillosa

Stephania papillosa, commonly known as the hairy stephania, is a flowering plant belonging to the Menispermaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its distinctive hairy leaves and yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are palmately lobed, and it produces small, fleshy fruits that contain seeds.

Stephania papillosa is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
