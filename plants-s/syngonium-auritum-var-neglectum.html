Syngonium auritum var. neglectum

Syngonium auritum var. neglectum, commonly known as the "Neglectum arrowhead," is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive waxy texture and a deep green color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
