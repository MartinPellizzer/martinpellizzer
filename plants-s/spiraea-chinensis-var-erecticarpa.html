Spiraea chinensis var. erecticarpa

Spiraea chinensis var. erecticarpa, commonly known as Chinese spiraea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features slender, arching stems that bear clusters of small, white to pink flowers in early summer. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and produces small, elongated fruits that are typically red or black. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This species is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and showy blooms.
