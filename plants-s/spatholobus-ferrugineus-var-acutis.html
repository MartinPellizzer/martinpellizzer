Spatholobus ferrugineus var. acutis

Spatholobus ferrugineus var. acutis, commonly known as the sharp-leaved spatholobus, is a species of flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to several meters. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, and the fruit is a legume pod containing several seeds.

This plant is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, where it thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight.
