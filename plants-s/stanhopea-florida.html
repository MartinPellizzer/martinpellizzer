Stanhopea florida

Stanhopea florida, commonly known as the Christmas orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that bloom during the holiday season, typically from December to February. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous fragrant, white to pale pink blossoms. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Stanhopea florida is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
