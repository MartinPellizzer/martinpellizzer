Senecio rollandii

Senecio rollandii, commonly known as the Blue-Eyed Mary, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers with a white central disk. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has slender, branching stems and small, narrow leaves.

Senecio rollandii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.
