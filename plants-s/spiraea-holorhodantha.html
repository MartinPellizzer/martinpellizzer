Spiraea holorhodantha

Spiraea holorhodantha, commonly known as the golden spiraea, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It features showy, flat-topped clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, arching branches and dark green, serrated leaves. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
