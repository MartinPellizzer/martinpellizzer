Stenocalyx sulcatus var. ligustrinus

Stenocalyx sulcatus var. ligustrinus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved evergreen hibiscus, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red, typically blooming during the warm months. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
