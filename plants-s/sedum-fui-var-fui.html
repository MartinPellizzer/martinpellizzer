Sedum fui var. fui

Sedum fui var. fui, commonly known as the "Fui Sedum," is a succulent plant native to specific regions in China.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking visual element to its appearance. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it a hardy choice for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
