Sideritis hyssopifolia var. australis

Sideritis hyssopifolia var. australis, commonly known as the Australian heath, is a perennial herb native to southern Australia.

It features small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or violet in color, arranged in dense spikes. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.

Sideritis hyssopifolia var. australis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
