Simaba cavalcantei

Simaba cavalcantei, commonly known as the Brazilian mahogany or *marmelada*, is a tropical tree native to Brazil.

It belongs to the family Myristicaceae and is characterized by its aromatic, reddish-brown wood. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and often have a waxy texture. It produces small, yellowish flowers followed by bright red, fleshy berries that contain seeds.
