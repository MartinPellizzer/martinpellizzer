Scindapsus roseus

Scindapsus roseus, commonly known as the silver vine, is a member of the Araceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a trailing plant with distinctive waxy, dark green leaves that have silvery veins, giving it a striking appearance. The plant is known for its ability to climb or trail, making it suitable for hanging baskets or shelves. It prefers bright, indirect light and moderate humidity, thriving in warm, moist conditions.

Scindapsus roseus is also noted for its ability to purify indoor air, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
