Senecio crispatipilosus

Senecio crispatipilosus, commonly known as the woolly crinkled groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, crinkled leaves and yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Senecio crispatipilosus is often found in open grasslands and along riverbanks.
