Schefflera aquaverensis

Schefflera aquaverensis, commonly known as the aquatic schefflera, is a unique plant species native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its dense, palm-like foliage and its ability to thrive in moist or aquatic environments. The plant typically has large, glossy leaves arranged in a compact, umbrella-like pattern. It is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of slow-moving water bodies.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
