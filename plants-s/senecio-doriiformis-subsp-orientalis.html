Senecio doriiformis subsp. orientalis

Senecio doriiformis subsp.

orientalis, commonly known as the Eastern Doriiformis Groundsel, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It features fleshy, ribbed stems and leaves that are typically blue-green in color, adapted to arid environments.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its slow growth and compact form, it is a popular choice for container gardening.
