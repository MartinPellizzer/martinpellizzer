Scutellaria Velenovskyi

Velenovsky's blueweed, scientifically known as Scutellaria velenovskyi, is a rare and unique flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. This species belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its striking blue petals and compact growth habit. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats. Due to its limited distribution and ecological sensitivity, it is considered an endangered species. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this delicate and beautiful plant for future generations.
