Sameraria nummularia

Sameraria nummularia, commonly known as the silverleaf sedum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, round, silver-gray leaves that are densely packed along its stems. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Sameraria nummularia is often used in rock gardens and as a ground cover due to its low maintenance requirements.
