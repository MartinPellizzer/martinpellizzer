Swertia usambarensis var. usambarensis

Swertia usambarensis var. usambarensis, commonly known as the Usambara gentian, is a perennial herb native to the Usambara Mountains in Tanzania.

It features a slender, upright stem that can grow up to 1 meter in height, covered with opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces bright blue to violet flowers in clusters, which bloom during the rainy season. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in montane forests.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique floral structure and role in local ecosystems.
