Stenochlaena marginata var. phlebodes

Stenochlaena marginata var. phlebodes, commonly known as the narrow-leafed arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wet, lowland areas. The plant features long, narrow leaves with a distinctive waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture. Its rhizomes are the primary part used, valued for their starchy content.
