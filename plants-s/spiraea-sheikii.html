Spiraea sheikii

Spiraea sheikii, commonly known as the Kashmir spiraea, is a deciduous shrub native to the Himalayas.

It features slender, arching stems that bear clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers in early summer. The plant has simple, ovate leaves with serrated edges, and it thrives in well-drained, slightly acidic soils. It is often used in ornamental gardening for its delicate floral display.

Spiraea sheikii is also valued for its ecological role in supporting pollinators and stabilizing soil in mountainous regions.
