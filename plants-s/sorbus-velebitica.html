Sorbus velebitica

Sorbus velebitica, commonly known as the Velebit whitebeam, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Dinaric Alps, primarily found in Croatia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, rounded crown and small, elliptical leaves. The plant produces clusters of white flowers in spring, followed by small, red-orange berries in the fall.

It thrives in rocky soils and is often found in mountainous regions, contributing to the biodiversity of its native habitat.
