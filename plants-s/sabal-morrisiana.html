Sabal morrisiana

Sabal morrisiana, commonly known as the Morris's sabal, is a species of palm native to the Florida Keys.

It is a small, slow-growing palm that typically reaches a height of about 6 to 10 feet. The plant has a slender trunk and pinnately compound leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in coastal environments and is adapted to salty, windy conditions.

Sabal morrisiana plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
