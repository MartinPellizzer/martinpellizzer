Silene × urbanica

Silene × urbanica, commonly known as the hybrid campion, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is a sterile hybrid resulting from the cross between Silene dioica (bladder campion) and Silene latifolia (broad-leaved campion). The plant typically produces showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in summer. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats, including roadsides and gardens.

Its botanical characteristics make it a common sight in urban and rural environments alike.
