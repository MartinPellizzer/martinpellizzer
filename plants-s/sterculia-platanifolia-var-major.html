Sterculia platanifolia var. major

Sterculia platanifolia var. major, commonly known as the large-leaf sterculia, is a tropical tree native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces yellowish-white flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by woody pods containing flat, elliptical seeds. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local communities.
