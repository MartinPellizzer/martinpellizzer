Stelis acutiflora

Stelis acutiflora, commonly known as the sharp-flowered stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and usually pale green or white with a distinctive yellow lip.

Stelis acutiflora is appreciated for its delicate appearance and is occasionally cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
