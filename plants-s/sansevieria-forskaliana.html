Sansevieria forskaliana

Sansevieria forskaliana, commonly known as the snake plant, is a succulent plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with yellow or white variegation. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it an ideal houseplant. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, storing water to help it survive in arid environments.

Sansevieria forskaliana is also noted for its air-purifying properties, as it can remove toxins from indoor air.
