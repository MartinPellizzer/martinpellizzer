Seidenfadenia mitrata

Seidenfadenia mitrata, commonly known as the helmet orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique helmet-shaped flower, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in forested areas, often as an epiphyte on trees. Its botanical features include long, strap-like leaves and a complex inflorescence with showy, fragrant blooms.

Seidenfadenia mitrata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
