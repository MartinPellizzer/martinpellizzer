Serratula tianschanica

Serratula tianschanica, commonly known as the Tianshan serrated wormwood, is a perennial flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils. It produces small, achene-like fruits that are dispersed by wind.

Due to its habitat and appearance, it is often found in mountainous regions of Kazakhstan, China, and Kyrgyzstan.
