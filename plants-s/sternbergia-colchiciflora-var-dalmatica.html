Sternbergia colchiciflora var. dalmatica

Sternbergia colchiciflora var. dalmatica, commonly known as the Dalmatian Sternbergia, is a perennial bulbous plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and white. The plant typically blooms in late autumn to early winter, making it a unique addition to winter gardens. It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sun, thriving in temperate climates.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
