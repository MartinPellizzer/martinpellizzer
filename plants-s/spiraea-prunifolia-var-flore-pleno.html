Spiraea prunifolia var. flore-pleno

Spiraea prunifolia var. flore-pleno, commonly known as the double-flowered spiraea, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to Europe.

It features dense clusters of showy, double white flowers that bloom in early spring, adding a striking floral display. The plant has small, ovate leaves with serrated edges, and its stems are often reddish-brown in color. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
