Schoenoxiphium distinctum

Schoenoxiphium distinctum, commonly known as the distinctum spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant produces elliptical leaves that are typically arranged in a spiral pattern along the stems. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, loamy soils.

Its ornamental value lies in its spring bloom and its use in landscaping for its compact form and seasonal interest.
