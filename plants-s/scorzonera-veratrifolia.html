Scorzonera veratrifolia

Scorzonera veratrifolia, commonly known as the yellow scorzonera, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant features yellow daisy-like flowers with a central disk surrounded by ray florets. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Scorzonera veratrifolia is often found in dry grasslands and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
