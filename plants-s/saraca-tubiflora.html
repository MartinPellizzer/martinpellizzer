Saraca tubiflora

Saraca tubiflora, commonly known as the tuberous flame tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces long, slender pods that contain seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Saraca tubiflora is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
