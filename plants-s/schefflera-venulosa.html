Schefflera venulosa

Schefflera venulosa, commonly known as the veined schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that are deeply veined, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded to semi-shaded environments, making it well-suited for indoor cultivation. Its foliage is often a vibrant green, with some varieties displaying variegation.

Schefflera venulosa is relatively low maintenance, requiring moderate watering and well-draining soil to thrive.
