Sideritis briquetiana var. hilarii

Sideritis briquetiana var. hilarii, commonly known as the Hilary's ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. It typically thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated for both aesthetic and therapeutic purposes.
