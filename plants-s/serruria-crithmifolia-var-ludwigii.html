Serruria crithmifolia var. ludwigii

Serruria crithmifolia var. ludwigii, commonly known as the Ludwig's serruria, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, gray-green leaves with serrated edges, which help it conserve water in arid conditions. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for low-maintenance gardens.
