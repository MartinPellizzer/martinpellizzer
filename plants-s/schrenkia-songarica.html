Schrenkia songarica

Schrenkia songarica, commonly known as the Songarica lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Schrenkia songarica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens with well-draining soil.
