Saxifraga Geranioides Var. Obovata

Saxifraga geranioides var. obovata is a striking flowering plant known for its vibrant pink blooms and compact growth habit. Native to rocky and alpine regions, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to flourish in challenging environments. Its leaves are typically obovate, giving it a unique and elegant appearance. Gardeners often choose this plant for rock gardens and containers due to its hardiness and visual appeal.
