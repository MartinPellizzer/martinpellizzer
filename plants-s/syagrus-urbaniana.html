Syagrus urbaniana

Syagrus urbaniana, commonly known as the royal palm, is a striking tropical plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like leaves that give it a regal appearance. The plant produces yellowish-white flowers that develop into orange-red fruits, which are edible but not commonly consumed by humans. Syagrus urbaniana thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

Its growth habit makes it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical and subtropical regions.
