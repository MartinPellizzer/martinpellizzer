Stigmatosema lunulata

Stigmatosema lunulata, commonly known as the moon-leaf plant, is a species of flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, waxy, and often moon-shaped leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its botanical structure includes a fibrous root system and a short, herbaceous stem.

Stigmatosema lunulata is often found in shaded, moist environments such as forests or along riverbanks.
