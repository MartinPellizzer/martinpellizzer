Sphenodesme involucrata var. pubescens

Sphenodesme involucrata var. pubescens, commonly known as the pubescent sphenodesme, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, woody stems and small, clustered leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs.
