Symplocos rhamnifolia

It belongs to the family Ericaceae and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are rough to the touch due to their dense pubescence. Symplocos rhamnifolia is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
