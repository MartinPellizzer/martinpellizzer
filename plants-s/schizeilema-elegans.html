Schizeilema elegans

Schizeilema elegans, commonly known as the elegant schizeilema, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in raceme inflorescences, which bloom during the warmer months. Schizeilema elegans is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it drought-tolerant.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in the Asteraceae family.
