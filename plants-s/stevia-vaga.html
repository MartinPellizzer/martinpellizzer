Stevia vaga

Stevia vaga, commonly known as sweetleaf, is a perennial herb native to South America.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and small yellow flowers. The plant is renowned for its intense sweet taste, which comes from compounds called steviol glycosides. Stevia vaga is often cultivated as a natural sweetener and is used in herbal teas and dietary supplements.

Its low caloric content makes it a popular alternative to sugar in health-conscious diets.
