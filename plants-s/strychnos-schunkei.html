Strychnos schunkei

Strychnos schunkei, commonly known as the African strychnos, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Loganiaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces bright red berries that are attractive to birds, aiding in seed dispersal. It is often found in woodland and savanna habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.
