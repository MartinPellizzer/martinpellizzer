Saxifraga intricata f. nervosa-major

Saxifraga intricata f. nervosa-major, commonly known as the veined saxifrage, is a flowering plant belonging to the Saxifragaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, clustered stems and small, oval-shaped leaves that are often veined prominently. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white or pale pink flowers during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in mountainous regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and woodland settings.
