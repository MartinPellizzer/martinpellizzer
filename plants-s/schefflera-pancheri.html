Schefflera pancheri

Schefflera pancheri, commonly known as the Pancheri umbrella plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of compound leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by black, spherical berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-drained soil.

Schefflera pancheri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor conditions.
