Sobralia macrantha var. alba

Sobralia macrantha var. alba, commonly known as the white-flowered sobralia, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features strap-like, dark green leaves and can grow as an epiphyte or terrestrial plant. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright but indirect light, making it a popular choice for greenhouse cultivation.

Its ornamental value lies in its elegant appearance and unique floral structure.
