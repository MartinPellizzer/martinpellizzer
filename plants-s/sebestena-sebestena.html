Sebestena sebestena

Sebestena sebestena, commonly known as the European goosefoot, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features a rosette of broad, heart-shaped leaves with a waxy surface and a slightly serrated edge. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by distinctive, inflated, three-lobed fruits. It thrives in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and open woodlands.

The species is notable for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
