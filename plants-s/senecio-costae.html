Senecio costae

Senecio costae, commonly known as the Costate Senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically features narrow, ribbed leaves and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters. Senecio costae is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of cultivation.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
