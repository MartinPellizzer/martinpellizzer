Stelis andrei

Stelis andrei, commonly known as the Andes orchid, is a species of orchid native to the high altitudes of the Andes Mountains.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and strikingly colored flowers, often with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, adapting to the harsh mountain environment.

Stelis andrei is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
