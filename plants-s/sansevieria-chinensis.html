Sansevieria chinensis

Sansevieria chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese snake plant, is a perennial evergreen plant native to southern China.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with distinctive light green stripes. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal water, making it an ideal choice for indoor environments. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or greenish-white, followed by red berries.

Sansevieria chinensis is also valued for its air-purifying properties, as it is capable of removing toxins from the air.
