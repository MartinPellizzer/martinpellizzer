Sunipia purpurata

Sunipia purpurata, commonly known as the purple sunipia, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple-pink petals and lush green foliage, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 feet, with a dense branching structure. Its flowers bloom in clusters, adding a dramatic visual impact to the landscape.

Sunipia purpurata thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil and partial to full sun exposure for optimal growth.
