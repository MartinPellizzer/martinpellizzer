Schizostachyum pleianthemum

Schizostachyum pleianthemum, commonly known as the purple bamboo, is a species of grass native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often a striking purple or reddish color, especially when young. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Schizostachyum pleianthemum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticulture and landscaping.
