Stachys lazica

Stachys lazica, commonly known as the Caucasian betony, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or dry habitats. The plant features upright stems covered in fine hairs and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Stachys lazica is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
