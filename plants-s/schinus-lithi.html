Schinus lithi

Schinus lithi, commonly known as the Brazilian pepper tree, is a member of the Anacardiaceae family.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to South America. The plant features compound leaves with serrated edges and produces small, yellowish-green flowers that give way to red, fleshy berries. Its bark is rough and fissured, and it has a strong, aromatic scent.

Schinus lithi is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been introduced to other regions, sometimes becoming invasive.
