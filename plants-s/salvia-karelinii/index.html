Salvia Karelinii

Salvia karelinii is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of eastern Russia. Known for its vivid violet-blue flowers and silver-green foliage, it captivates botanists and gardeners alike. This perennial shrub thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers well-drained soil. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a sought-after species in conservation efforts. Despite its beauty, Salvia karelinii remains little known outside of specialized horticultural circles.
