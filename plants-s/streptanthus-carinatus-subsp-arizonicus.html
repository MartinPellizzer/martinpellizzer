Streptanthus carinatus subsp. arizonicus

Streptanthus carinatus subsp.

arizonicus, commonly known as Arizona ridgecress, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States. It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants.

This species is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and arid environments, showcasing adaptations to harsh, dry conditions.
