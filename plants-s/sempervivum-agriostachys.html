Sempervivum agriostachys

Sempervivum agriostachys, commonly known as the silver carpet sedum, is a low-growing succulent plant native to the Alps.

It features dense clusters of fleshy, silver-green leaves that form a compact rosette. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it ideal for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its hardy nature allows it to survive in a variety of climates, including cold and arid environments.
