Salacia polyantha

Salacia polyantha, commonly known as the white salacia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Salicaceae, which includes willows and poplars. The plant is characterized by its delicate white flowers and lance-shaped leaves. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments in tropical forests.

Salacia polyantha is valued for its ornamental beauty and has traditional medicinal uses in some cultures.
