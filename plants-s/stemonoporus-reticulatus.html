Stemonoporus reticulatus

Stemonoporus reticulatus, commonly known as the net-veined orchid, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in clusters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a distinct net-like venation pattern, giving it its common name.

Stemonoporus reticulatus is typically found in moist, shaded forest environments, where it thrives in the understory vegetation.
