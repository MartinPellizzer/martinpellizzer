Salsola kleinfonteini

Salsola kleinfonteini, commonly known as the Kleinfontein saltbush, is a hardy shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, fleshy leaves and produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. The plant is highly tolerant of arid conditions and can thrive in saline soils, making it well-suited for harsh environments. Its extensive root system helps stabilize soil and prevent erosion.

Salsola kleinfonteini plays an important role in desert ecosystems by providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
