Scelochilus obovatus

Scelochilus obovatus, commonly known as the broad-ovate scelochilus, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Orchidaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant features striking, showy flowers with a distinctive orange and yellow coloration, often with a dark red lip. Its leaves are broad and ovate, contributing to its common name.
