Silene karaczukuri

Silene karaczukuri, commonly known as the Karaczukuri catchfly, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender stems and small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions. Its leaves are linear and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.
