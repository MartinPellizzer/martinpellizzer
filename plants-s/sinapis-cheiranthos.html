Sinapis cheiranthos

Sinapis cheiranthos, commonly known as yellow wallflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and linear, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It produces small, round pods that contain several seeds.

Sinapis cheiranthos is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
