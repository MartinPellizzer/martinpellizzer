Stellaria mannii

Stellaria mannii, commonly known as white starwort, is a flowering plant native to Africa, particularly found in regions like Kenya and Tanzania.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in grasslands and open areas, often in sandy or disturbed soils. It has slender, branching stems and small, oval-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem.

Stellaria mannii is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
