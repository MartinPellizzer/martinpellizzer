Sisymbrium arundanum var. robustum

Sisymbrium arundanum var. robustum, commonly known as the robust field bean, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and pinnately divided leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes, which are followed by slender, elongated pods containing several seeds. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in disturbed soils and grasslands.

Its botanical features make it a common weed in agricultural fields and along roadsides.
