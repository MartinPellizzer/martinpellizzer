Serapias ensifolia

Serapias ensifolia, commonly known as the swordleaf orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the genus Serapias, which is part of the orchid family, Orchidaceae. The plant is characterized by its sword-shaped leaves and vibrant, often yellow or orange, flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Serapias ensifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
