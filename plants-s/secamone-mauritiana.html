Secamone mauritiana

Secamone mauritiana, commonly known as the Mauritius starflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mascarene Islands.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its star-shaped flowers, which are typically pink or white. The plant has a slender, branching stem and produces small, elliptical leaves. It thrives in coastal areas and is often found in sandy soils.
