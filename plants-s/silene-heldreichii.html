Silene heldreichii

Silene heldreichii, commonly known as the Balkan campion, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or mountainous environments. The plant produces showy pink or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Silene heldreichii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
