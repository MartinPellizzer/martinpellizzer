Smilacina yunnanensis

Smilacina yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan greenbrier, is a climbing vine native to China.

It belongs to the family Smilacaceae and is characterized by its woody stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal uses, it is sometimes cultivated in horticulture.
