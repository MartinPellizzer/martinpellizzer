Stelis mucronipetala

Stelis mucronipetala, commonly known as the narrow-lobed stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically found growing on trees in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has a compact growth habit with clustered, small pseudobulbs and narrow, linear leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with a distinctive lip that is often notched or spurred.

It thrives in humid environments with indirect light and well-draining substrates.
