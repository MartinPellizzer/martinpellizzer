Senna multifoliolata var. eurystegia

Senna multifoliolata var. eurystegia, commonly known as the broadleaf senna, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa and southern Asia.

Its seeds are encased in a long, slender pod, and the plant is known for its ornamental value and use in traditional medicine.
