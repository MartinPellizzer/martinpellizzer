Seaforthia caesia

Seaforthia caesia, commonly known as the blue seaforthia, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

Seaforthia caesia is well adapted to maritime climates and is often found in coastal dunes and cliffs.
