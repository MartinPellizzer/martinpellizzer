Swertia perennis subsp. obtusa

Swertia perennis subsp.

obtusa, commonly known as the blunt-leaved gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Asia. It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are broad and ovate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
