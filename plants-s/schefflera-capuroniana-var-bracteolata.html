Schefflera capuroniana var. bracteolata

Schefflera capuroniana var. bracteolata, commonly known as the Bracteolate Umbrella Tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features a distinctive, umbrella-like crown composed of large, palmately compound leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
