Stemonacanthus angustior var. microphyllus

Stemonacanthus angustior var. microphyllus, commonly known as the narrowleaf stemonacanthus, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, narrow leaves. The plant produces showy, two-lipped flowers that are typically purple or violet in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
