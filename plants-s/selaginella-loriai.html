Selaginella loriai

Selaginella loriai, commonly known as the Loria's spikemoss, is a species of spikemoss belonging to the family Selaginellaceae.

It is a small, evergreen plant that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant has a distinctive appearance with its creeping stems and dense clusters of small, scale-like leaves. Selaginella loriai is known for its ability to survive in low-light conditions and its resilience to environmental stress.

It plays a role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for various microorganisms and invertebrates.
