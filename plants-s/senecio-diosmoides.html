Senecio diosmoides

Senecio diosmoides, commonly known as the desert groundsel, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of South Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that typically reaches heights of about 30 centimeters. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. It is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water availability.

Senecio diosmoides is often found in rocky soils and is part of the Asteraceae family.
