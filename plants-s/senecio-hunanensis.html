Senecio hunanensis

Senecio hunanensis, commonly known as the Hunan senecio, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
