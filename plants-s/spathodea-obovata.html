Spathodea obovata

Spathodea obovata, commonly known as the African tulip, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to orange. The leaves are broad, obovate, and often have a waxy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

This plant is popular in landscaping due to its vibrant blooms and relatively low maintenance requirements.
