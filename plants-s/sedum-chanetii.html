Sedum chanetii

Sedum chanetii, commonly known as the Canary Islands stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its fleshy, erect stems and small, clustered leaves that are typically green or reddish in color. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months, adding a striking visual element to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
