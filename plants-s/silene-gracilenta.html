Silene gracilenta

Silene gracilenta, commonly known as slender pink, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, slender stems and small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in well-drained soils. Silene gracilenta is often found in open meadows and along slopes, where it forms dense clusters.

Its botanical significance lies in its role as a component of alpine flora and its potential for horticultural interest due to its elegant appearance.
