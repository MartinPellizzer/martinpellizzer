Styrax klotzschii

Styrax klotzschii, commonly known as the Chinese fringed styrax, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant produces small, dark purple berries that are edible but not particularly palatable. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a waxy texture.

Styrax klotzschii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and adaptability to various soil conditions.
