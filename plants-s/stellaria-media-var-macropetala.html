Stellaria media var. macropetala

Stellaria media var. macropetala, commonly known as the large-petaled chickweed, is a variant of the common chickweed.

It is a low-growing annual herb with slender stems and small, white flowers that have five petals. The leaves are typically oval-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in gardens and disturbed soils.

This variety is distinguished by its slightly larger petals compared to the standard Stellaria media.
