Schefflera stenomera

Schefflera stenomera, commonly known as the narrow-leaf umbrella plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like clusters of leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant has compound, pinnately arranged leaves that are typically narrow and pointed. It produces small, white flowers followed by bright red berries, which are attractive to birds.

Schefflera stenomera is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
