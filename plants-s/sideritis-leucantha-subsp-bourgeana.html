Sideritis leucantha subsp. bourgeana

Sideritis leucantha subsp.

bourgeana, commonly known as white heath, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs.

The plant is characterized by its slender stems, small gray-green leaves, and clusters of pale blue or white flowers. It thrives in rocky or dry habitats and is often found in open woodlands and scrublands.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
