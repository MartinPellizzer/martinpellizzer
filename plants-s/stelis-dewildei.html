Stelis dewildei

Stelis dewildei, commonly known as the Dewilde's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a short, erect stem with several fleshy, elliptical leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green or white, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

Stelis dewildei is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or terrariums.
