Stisseria tigridia

Stisseria tigridia, commonly known as the tiger lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, showy flowers that come in shades of orange, yellow, and red. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. Its flowers are typically borne in clusters at the top of the plant, and it produces small, dry berries that contain the seeds.

Stisseria tigridia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
