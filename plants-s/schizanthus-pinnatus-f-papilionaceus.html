Schizanthus pinnatus f. papilionaceus

Schizanthus pinnatus f. papilionaceus, commonly known as the butterfly pea or papilionaceous schizanthus, is a cultivar of the Schizanthus genus.

It is characterized by its striking, butterfly-like flowers that come in a variety of colors, including purple, blue, and white. The plant is a herbaceous annual with a trailing growth habit, making it suitable for hanging baskets or as a ground cover. Its foliage is finely divided, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.

The flowers are typically tubular with a distinctive bilabiate structure, typical of the Schizanthus genus.
