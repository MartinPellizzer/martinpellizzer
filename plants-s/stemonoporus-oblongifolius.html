Stemonoporus oblongifolius

Stemonoporus oblongifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf stemonoporus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or white in color. Its roots are fibrous and often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.
