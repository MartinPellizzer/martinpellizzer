Stelis globosa

Stelis globosa, commonly known as the globe orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, globular growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Stelis globosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
