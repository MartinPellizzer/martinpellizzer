Scutellaria andrachnoides

Scutellaria andrachnoides, commonly known as the Andean skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous areas.

Scutellaria andrachnoides is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
