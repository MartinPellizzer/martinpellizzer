Stenoglottis modesta

Stenoglottis modesta, commonly known as the dwarf orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its small, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers a sunny to partially shaded environment. Its botanical features include narrow, grass-like leaves and a compact growth habit.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its delicate appearance and ease of care.
