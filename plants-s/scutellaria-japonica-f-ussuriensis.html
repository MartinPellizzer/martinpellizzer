Scutellaria japonica f. ussuriensis

Scutellaria japonica f. ussuriensis, commonly known as the Ussuri scutellaria, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features square stems and opposite leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are typically blue or purple, arranged in whorls along the stem.

It is valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
