Scorpiurus sulcatus

Scorpiurus sulcatus, commonly known as the zigzag fumitory, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing annual herb with slender, branching stems that often have a zigzag appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in early spring. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Scorpiurus sulcatus is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides, thriving in sunny to partially shaded environments.
