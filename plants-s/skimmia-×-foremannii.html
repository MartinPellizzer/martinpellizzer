Skimmia × foremannii

Skimmia × foremannii, commonly known as the hybrid skimmia, is a compact evergreen shrub characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of fragrant, red berries.

It is a natural hybrid between Skimmia japonica and Skimmia reynoldsiana, resulting in a plant with enhanced ornamental features. The species thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens or as a container plant. Its evergreen foliage provides year-round interest, while the showy winter berries add color to the landscape.

Skimmia × foremannii is also valued for its resistance to pests and diseases, contributing to its popularity in horticulture.
