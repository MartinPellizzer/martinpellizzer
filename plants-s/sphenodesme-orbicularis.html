Sphenodesme orbicularis

Sphenodesme orbicularis, commonly known as the orbicular sphenodesme, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its orbicular, or circular, leaves that are typically smooth and glossy. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, which bloom during the warm season. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Its botanical structure makes it an interesting subject for study in plant morphology and ecology.
