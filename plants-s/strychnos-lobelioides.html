Strychnos lobelioides

Strychnos lobelioides, commonly known as the African strychnos, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in racemes, and its fruits are follicles that split open to release seeds. It is often found in forested areas and is adapted to humid, shaded environments.

The plant has been used traditionally in some regions for its medicinal properties.
