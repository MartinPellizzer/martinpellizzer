Senecio Gregatus

Senecio gregatus is a striking succulent native to South Africa known for its unique rosette shape and vibrant blue-green foliage. This plant thrives in arid environments and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or dry landscapes. Its ability to store water in its thick leaves allows it to survive in harsh conditions with little care. While it may appear delicate, Senecio gregatus is surprisingly resilient and hardy. Its beauty and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
