Sonchus acanthifolius

Sonchus acanthifolius, commonly known as the prickly lettuce, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its deeply lobed, spiny leaves and yellow daisy-like flowers. Native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa, it has been introduced to various parts of the world. The plant is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats.

Its life cycle includes a period of dormancy, allowing it to survive in different environmental conditions.
