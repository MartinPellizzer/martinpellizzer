Sutera annua var. laxa

Sutera annua var. laxa, commonly known as the laxa variant of the common suture plant, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and grows in a creeping or ascending habit. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
