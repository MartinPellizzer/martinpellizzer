Solidago camporum

Solidago camporum, commonly known as the prairie coneflower, is a flowering plant native to the central United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Solidago camporum is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in native plant gardens.
