Spathiphyllum montanum

Spathiphyllum montanum, commonly known as the mountain peace lily, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, waxy leaves and produces elegant, white spathes that enclose small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant is a member of the Araceae family, known for its unique inflorescence structure. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Its ability to purify indoor air makes it a popular choice for interior gardening.
