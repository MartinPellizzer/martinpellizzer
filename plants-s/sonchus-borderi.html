Sonchus borderi

Sonchus borderi, commonly known as the border sowthistle, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in fields, roadsides, and along riverbanks. It has smooth, hairy, or glandular stems and leaves that are often lobed or toothed.

The flowers are yellow and are surrounded by a series of bracts that give the plant its distinctive appearance.
