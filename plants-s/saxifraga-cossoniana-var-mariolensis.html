Saxifraga cossoniana var. mariolensis

Saxifraga cossoniana var. mariolensis, commonly known as the Mariolensian saxifrage, is a rare and endemic plant species native to the Pyrenees region of Europe.

The flowers are typically white or pale pink, clustered in dense inflorescences, and bloom in late spring to early summer. This plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.
