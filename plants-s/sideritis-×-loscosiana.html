Sideritis × loscosiana

Sideritis × loscosiana, commonly known as the hybrid sainfoin, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Sideritis × loscosiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
