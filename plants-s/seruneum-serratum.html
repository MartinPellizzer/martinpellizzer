Seruneum serratum

Seruneum serratum, commonly known as the serrated seruneum, is a flowering plant native to the temperate regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, serrated leaves and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation. Its ornamental value lies in its delicate foliage and subtle floral display.

Seruneum serratum is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory properties.
