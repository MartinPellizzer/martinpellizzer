Sideritis hirsuta var. bubanii

Sideritis hirsuta var. bubanii, commonly known as the Hirsute Sideritis, is a perennial herb native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils, often in mountainous regions. Its flowers are small, pink to purple, and clustered in whorls along the stem.

It is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional herbal remedies.
