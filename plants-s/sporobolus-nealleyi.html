Sporobolus nealleyi

Sporobolus nealleyi, commonly known as Nealley's threeawn, is a perennial grass native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and small, spiny spikelets that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small wildlife.

Sporobolus nealleyi is often found in grasslands and along the edges of deserts.
