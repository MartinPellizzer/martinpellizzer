Scleria steudeliana

Scleria steudeliana, commonly known as the purple rush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and rushes, and is characterized by its grass-like appearance. Scleria steudeliana is often found in moist, sandy soils and is used in ornamental gardening for its unique coloration and texture.
