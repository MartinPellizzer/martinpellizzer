Sasa togashiana f. asagishiana

Sasa togashiana f. asagishiana, commonly known as the asagi bamboo, is a variant of the sasa bamboo genus native to Japan.

It is characterized by its slender, upright culms and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often a vibrant green. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. It is a clumping bamboo that forms dense stands, contributing to the structure of its habitat.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ecological and ornamental purposes.
