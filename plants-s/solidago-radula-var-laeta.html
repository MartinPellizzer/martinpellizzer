Solidago radula var. laeta

Solidago radula var. laeta, commonly known as the happy goldenrod, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow inflorescences that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

This variety is noted for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
