Sonchella tibetica

Sonchella tibetica, commonly known as the Tibetan sowthistle, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Tibetan Plateau.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and milky sap. The plant typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing adaptations to harsh climatic conditions. Its leaves are hairy and lobed, while the flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat head.

Sonchella tibetica is valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Tibetan medicine.
