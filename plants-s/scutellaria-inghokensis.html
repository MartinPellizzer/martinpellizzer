Scutellaria inghokensis

Scutellaria inghokensis, commonly known as the Korean skullcap, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks.

Scutellaria inghokensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
