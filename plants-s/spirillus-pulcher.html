Spirillus pulcher

Spirillus pulcher, commonly known as the beautiful spiral plant, is a unique species characterized by its spiral-shaped leaves.

It belongs to the Spirillaceae family and is native to tropical regions. The plant exhibits a compact growth habit with a central stem that branches into spiraling patterns. Its leaves are typically narrow and have a glossy surface, enhancing its ornamental appeal.

Spirillus pulcher thrives in humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
