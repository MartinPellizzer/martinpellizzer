Sisymbrium virgatum

Sisymbrium virgatum, commonly known as yellow rocket, is an annual flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other mustard plants. The plant features slender, branching stems and bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are pinnately divided, with a distinctive mustard-like odor.

Sisymbrium virgatum is often found in disturbed soils and can be a weed in agricultural fields.
