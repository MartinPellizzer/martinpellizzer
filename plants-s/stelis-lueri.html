Stelis lueri

Stelis lueri, commonly known as the Luer's orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and clustered inflorescences. The plant typically produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with intricate patterns on the petals. Stelis lueri thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

It is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
