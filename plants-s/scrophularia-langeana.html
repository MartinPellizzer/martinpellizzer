Scrophularia langeana

Scrophularia langeana, commonly known as the Lange's figwort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae, which includes many species with showy, often two-lipped flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in temperate to cold climates. Its flowers are usually pink to purple in color and bloom in late spring to early summer.

Scrophularia langeana is often found in alpine meadows and is adapted to high-altitude environments.
