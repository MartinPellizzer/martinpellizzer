Solanum gracilescens

Solanum gracilescens, commonly known as the slender nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It typically grows as a low shrub with thin, branching stems and small, oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers, which are followed by small, smooth, berry-like fruits. It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae), which includes other plants like tomatoes and peppers.

Solanum gracilescens is often found in moist, shaded environments and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.
