Senecio lombokensis

Senecio lombokensis, commonly known as the Lombok Senecio, is a flowering plant native to the island of Lombok in Indonesia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid conditions. Its leaves are usually narrow and grey-green, with a fuzzy texture that helps reduce water loss.

Senecio lombokensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a succulent in gardens.
