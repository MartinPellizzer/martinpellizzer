Sphaerostephanos gregarius

Sphaerostephanos gregarius, commonly known as the clustered star-fruit, is a unique plant native to southern Africa.

The plant's botanical features include opposite, narrow leaves and a distinctive clustering pattern of its flowering stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.
