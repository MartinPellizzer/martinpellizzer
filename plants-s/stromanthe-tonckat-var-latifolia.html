Stromanthe tonckat var. latifolia

Stromanthe tonckat var. latifolia, commonly known as the Variegated Stromanthe, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with shades of green, white, and pink. The leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
