Scleria microcarpa var. foliosa

Scleria microcarpa var. foliosa, commonly known as the broad-leaved waterplantain, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland and marsh environments.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are often broad at the base, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers arranged in dense clusters. Its rhizomatous root system helps stabilize soil in aquatic habitats.

It is valued for its ecological role in filtering water and providing habitat for aquatic life.
