Solanum aureum var. latelanceolatum

Solanum aureum var. latelanceolatum, commonly known as the golden nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers and hairy, lance-shaped leaves. The plant belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is known for its ornamental value. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments and can reach a height of up to 1 meter.

Its fruits are small, orange berries that mature in late summer.
