Sarcochilus archytas

Sarcochilus archytas, commonly known as the red-stemmed tree orchid, is a species of orchid native to Australia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a distinctive reddish stem and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a warm, humid environment.

Sarcochilus archytas is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
