Schizachyrium ursulum

Schizachyrium ursulum, commonly known as stiff panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It typically grows in open woodlands, prairies, and disturbed sites, preferring well-drained soils. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces tall, slender flowering stalks that bear small, spike-like clusters of bracts. It is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
