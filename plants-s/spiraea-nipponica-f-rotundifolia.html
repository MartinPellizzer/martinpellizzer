Spiraea nipponica f. rotundifolia

Spiraea nipponica f. rotundifolia, commonly known as the Japanese spirea or round-leaf spirea, is a compact shrub native to Japan.

It features small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from April to May. The leaves are ovate to round in shape, giving the plant its distinguishing appearance. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

This ornamental plant is valued for its delicate foliage and showy floral display in gardens.
