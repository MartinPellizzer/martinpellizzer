Sinocalamus Affinis F. Viridiflavus

Green-flowered bamboo, scientifically known as Sinocalamus affinis f. viridiflavus, is a rare and visually striking variant of the bamboo family. This unique plant exhibits an unusual greenish-yellow hue in its flowers, setting it apart from typical bamboo species. Found primarily in the mountainous regions of southern China, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its ornamental value has made it a sought-after specimen among horticulturists and plant enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively obscure and under-studied plant in the botanical world.
