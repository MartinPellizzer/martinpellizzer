Sideritis gouanii

Sideritis gouanii, commonly known as the Greek ironwort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Greece.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Sideritis gouanii is characterized by its hairy, gray-green leaves and its ability to thrive in rocky, well-drained soils.

It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
