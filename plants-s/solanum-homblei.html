Solanum homblei

Solanum homblei, commonly known as the Hombli nightshade, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant features small, green, oval-shaped leaves and produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. Its fruit is a small, fleshy berry that turns red when mature.

Like many species in its family, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
