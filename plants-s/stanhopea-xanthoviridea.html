Stanhopea xanthoviridea

Stanhopea xanthoviridea, commonly known as the Yellow Viridian Stanhopea, is a rare and exquisite orchid native to the humid lowland forests of Costa Rica and Panama.

It is characterized by its striking yellow and green floral colors, which are highly unusual among orchids. The plant produces pendulous, fragrant flowers that are typically 5 to 7 centimeters in diameter, featuring intricate patterns and a velvety texture. It thrives in warm, moist environments with high humidity and partial shade, making it a challenging but rewarding species for orchid enthusiasts.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique coloration and role in the ecological diversity of tropical rainforests.
