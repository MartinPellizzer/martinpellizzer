Statice psiloclada var. albida

Statice psiloclada var. albida, commonly known as the white statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and small, clustered white or pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or coastal soils and thrives in full sunlight. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and the flowers bloom in late summer to early autumn.

It is often used in ornamental gardening for its delicate appearance and drought tolerance.
