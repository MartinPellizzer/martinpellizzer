Stelis abbreviata

Stelis abbreviata, commonly known as the short-stemmed orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a compact, short stem with few leaves and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pink. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air.

Stelis abbreviata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in terrariums or greenhouses.
