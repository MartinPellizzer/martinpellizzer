Salacicratea

Salacicratea, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that have a silvery underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its bark is smooth and often has a reddish or brownish hue.

Salacicratea is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
