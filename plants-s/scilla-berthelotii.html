Scilla berthelotii

Scilla berthelotii, commonly known as the Berthelot's scilla, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features grass-like basal leaves and produces clusters of bell-shaped blue or purple flowers in spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade. It is often found in rocky or limestone habitats.

Scilla berthelotii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
