Senecio venustus

Senecio venustus, commonly known as the beautiful Senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as a shrub or subshrub, featuring narrow, silver-green leaves and vibrant yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
