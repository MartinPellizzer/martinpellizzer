Schefflera polyandra

Schefflera polyandra, commonly known as the Umbrella Plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like clusters of leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant has compound, pinnate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It produces small, white, fragrant flowers followed by red, fleshy berries.

Schefflera polyandra is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in indoor environments.
