Statice dielsiana

Statice dielsiana, commonly known as the dielsian blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Statice dielsiana is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and drought-tolerant nature.
