Stefanoffia aurea

Stefanoffia aurea, commonly known as the golden stefanoffia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated leaves and clusters of small, bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in cloud forests. Its botanical features include a woody stem and opposite leaf arrangement.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
