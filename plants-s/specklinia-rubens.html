Specklinia rubens

Specklinia rubens, commonly known as the red-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit and its striking red stems that contrast with its dark green leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming during the warmer months. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining substrates, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often sought after by horticultural enthusiasts.
