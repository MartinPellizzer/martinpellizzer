Solanum nitidibaccatum var. robusticalyx

Solanum nitidibaccatum var. robusticalyx, commonly known as the robust calyx nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers. The plant produces berries that start off green and mature to a bright yellow or orange hue. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sun.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by horticulturists.
