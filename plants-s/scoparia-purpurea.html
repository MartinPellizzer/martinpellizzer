Scoparia purpurea

Scoparia purpurea, commonly known as purple scoparia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky soils. The plant features slender, branched stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are clustered in dense, spike-like inflorescences, with purple to pink petals that bloom in the summer months.

Scoparia purpurea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
