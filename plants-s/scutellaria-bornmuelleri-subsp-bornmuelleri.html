Scutellaria bornmuelleri subsp. bornmuelleri

Scutellaria bornmuelleri subsp.

bornmuelleri, commonly known as the Bornmueller's skullcap, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of southern China. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves.

The plant produces small, tubular blue or violet flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or forested habitats.
