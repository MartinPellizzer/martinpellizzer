Strombosia pustulata var. pustulata

Strombosia pustulata var. pustulata, commonly known as the pustulate strombosia, is a flowering plant species native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its woody structure and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The leaves are typically simple and alternate, with a rough texture and a distinct pubescent surface. The plant is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and horticultural contexts.
