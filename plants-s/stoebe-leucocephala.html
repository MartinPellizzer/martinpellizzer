Stoebe leucocephala

Stoebe leucocephala, commonly known as white-flowered evening-primrose, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in dry, sandy soils in open habitats. The plant produces showy, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in the spring and early summer. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.
