Stemonix nervosus

Stemonix nervosus, commonly known as the veined stemon.

It is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. The plant features slender, upright stems with prominent, parallel veins on its leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, typically displaying shades of white and yellow.

Stemonix nervosus is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and compact growth habit.
