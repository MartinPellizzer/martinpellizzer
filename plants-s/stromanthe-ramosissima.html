Stromanthe ramosissima

Stromanthe ramosissima, commonly known as the pink princess, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that display shades of pink, white, and green. The plant has a compact growth habit with a short, sturdy stem and can reach a height of up to 12 inches. Its foliage is often adorned with a waxy, glossy texture, enhancing its ornamental appeal.

Stromanthe ramosissima thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to maintain its vibrant appearance.
