Sphaerostigma tenuifolium

Sphaerostigma tenuifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved globular fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, globular growth habit and delicate, lanceolate leaves. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its fronds are usually arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its unique appearance.

Sphaerostigma tenuifolium is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in humid, low-light conditions.
