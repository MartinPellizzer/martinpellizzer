Schizostachyum yalyense

Schizostachyum yalyense, commonly known as the Yalyen bamboo, is a species of grass belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Vietnam, and Malaysia. This plant is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 10 meters. It thrives in moist, tropical environments and is often found in lowland forests or along riverbanks.

The plant is valued for its strong, durable wood, which is used in construction and traditional crafts.
