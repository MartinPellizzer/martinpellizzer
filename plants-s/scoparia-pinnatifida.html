Scoparia pinnatifida

Scoparia pinnatifida, commonly known as the narrow-leaved scrophularia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and opposite, pinnately lobed leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Scoparia pinnatifida is a member of the family Scrophulariaceae, which includes many species with similar floral structures.
