Schizaea incurvata

Schizaea incurvata, commonly known as the zigzag fern, is a member of the family Schizaeaceae.

It is a fern that exhibits a unique growth habit, characterized by its curved, spiraling fronds. The plant is native to tropical regions of the Americas and thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine, and its delicate, feathery fronds contribute to its ornamental appeal.

Schizaea incurvata plays an important ecological role in its habitat by providing shelter and food for various small organisms.
