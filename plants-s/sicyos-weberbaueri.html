Sicyos weberbaueri

Sicyos weberbaueri, commonly known as the Weberbaueri bottle gourd, is a climbing vine belonging to the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is characterized by its hollow, cylindrical stems and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces pale yellow flowers that give way to elongated, bottle-shaped fruits. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

Due to its unique morphology, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes or as a source of edible gourds.
