Sansevieria zanzibarica

Sansevieria zanzibarica, commonly known as the Zanzibar snake plant, is a species of succulent native to the island of Zanzibar.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with striking, dark green or silver variegation. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water, which is a characteristic feature of many succulents.

Sansevieria zanzibarica is also noted for its air-purifying qualities, contributing to its reputation as a hardy and beneficial houseplant.
