Sapium patens

Sapium patens, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves that are often bright green and glossy. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by orange or red berries, which are a food source for various birds and animals. Sapium patens thrives in moist, well-drained soil and is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive foliage.

It is also valued for its ability to tolerate partial shade and its relatively low maintenance requirements.
