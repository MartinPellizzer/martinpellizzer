Solanum tanysepalum

Solanum tanysepalum, commonly known as the slender nightshade, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous shrub with small, oval-shaped leaves and produces clusters of yellow flowers. Its fruit is a small, fleshy berry that turns from green to red as it matures.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its attractive foliage and flowers.
