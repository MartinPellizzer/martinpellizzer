Scrophularia czernjakowskiana

Scrophularia czernjakowskiana, commonly known as the Czernjakowska scrophularia, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in mountainous regions.
