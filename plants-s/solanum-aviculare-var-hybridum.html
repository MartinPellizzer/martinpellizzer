Solanum aviculare var. hybridum

Solanum aviculare var. hybridum, commonly known as the hybrid nightshade, is a member of the Solanaceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its hairy stems and alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or purple, followed by berries that range in color from yellow to red. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
