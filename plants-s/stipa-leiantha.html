Stipa leiantha

Stipa leiantha, commonly known as the slender feathergrass, is a grass species native to the mountainous regions of central and southern South America.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and delicate, feathery inflorescences that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are narrow and often have a bluish-gray hue, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments.

Stipa leiantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its graceful, airy foliage.
