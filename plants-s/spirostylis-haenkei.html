Spirostylis haenkei

Spirostylis haenkei, commonly known as the slender spike orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as understorey forests. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil efficiently.

Spirostylis haenkei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
