Solanum dichroandrum var. glabriusculum

Solanum dichroandrum var. glabriusculum, commonly known as the smoothleaf nightshade, is a species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree with dark green, glossy leaves that are typically smooth to the touch. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. Its fruits are berries that start green and mature to a deep red or black, often containing several seeds.

This species is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in moist, shaded environments.
