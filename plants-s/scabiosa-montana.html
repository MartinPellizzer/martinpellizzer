Scabiosa montana

Scabiosa montana, commonly known as mountain scabious, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of pink or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are typically gray-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Scabiosa montana is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
