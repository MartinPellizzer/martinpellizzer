Scaevola humifusa

Scaevola humifusa, commonly known as the carpet flower, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Australia.

It features dense clusters of small, blue or violet flowers that bloom throughout the year, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has fleshy, succulent leaves that help it retain moisture in dry conditions. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
