Scaevola goodeniacea

Scaevola goodeniacea, commonly known as the common scaevola, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a member of the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its showy, bilaterally symmetric flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or subshrub, with small, narrow leaves and bright blue or purple petals. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Scaevola goodeniacea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
