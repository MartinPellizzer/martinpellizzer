Sisymbrium maurum

Sisymbrium maurum, commonly known as the field rocket or wild mustard, is an annual herbaceous plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces slender, elongated pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Sisymbrium maurum is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been naturalized in various regions around the world.
