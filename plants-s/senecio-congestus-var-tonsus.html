Senecio congestus var. tonsus

Senecio congestus var. tonsus, commonly known as the dense senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features dense clusters of small yellow flowers atop stiff, upright stems. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
