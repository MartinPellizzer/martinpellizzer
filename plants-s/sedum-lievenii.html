Sedum lievenii

Sedum lievenii, commonly known as the Lieven's stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. The plant features fleshy, upright stems and small, often blue-green or yellowish leaves. Its flowers are usually star-shaped, ranging in color from white to pink, and bloom in late summer.

Sedum lievenii is drought-tolerant and requires minimal care, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
