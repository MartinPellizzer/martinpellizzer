Sansevieria phillipsiae

Sansevieria phillipsiae, commonly known as the Phillips' snake plant, is a species native to the tropical rainforests of West Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with light green or white variegation. The plant is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions. Its leaves are fleshy and succulent, adapted to store water, making it highly drought-tolerant.

Sansevieria phillipsiae is also noted for its air-purifying qualities, making it a popular houseplant.
