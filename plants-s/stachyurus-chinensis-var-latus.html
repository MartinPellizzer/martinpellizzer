Stachyurus chinensis var. latus

Stachyurus chinensis var. latus, commonly known as the broadleaf stachyurus, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that appear in late spring, typically in clusters of white or pale yellow. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are glossy and turn yellow in autumn. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

This ornamental plant is often used in gardens for its aesthetic appeal and seasonal interest.
