Syngonium podophyllum var. typicum

Syngonium podophyllum var. typicum, commonly known as arrowhead vine, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that often feature a striking variegation of green, white, and sometimes pink. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red berries, though it is primarily grown for its ornamental foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Syngonium podophyllum var. typicum is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its lush appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
