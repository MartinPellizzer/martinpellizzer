Sedum chilonense

Sedum chilonense, commonly known as the Chinese stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to China.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its hardy nature allows it to survive in a variety of climates, including USDA zones 5 through 9.
