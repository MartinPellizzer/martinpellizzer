Solanum etuberosum

Solanum etuberosum, commonly known as the tuberous nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant is characterized by its woody stems and clusters of small, white or pale purple flowers. Its fruit is a rounded berry that turns from green to yellow or red as it matures.

Solanum etuberosum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
