Stachys natalensis var. natalensis

Stachys natalensis var. natalensis, commonly known as Natal hedge nettle, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features upright stems that are often hairy and can grow up to 1 meter in height. The plant produces small, violet to purple flowers arranged in whorled clusters along the stem. Its leaves are lance-shaped with serrated edges and emit a mild, mint-like fragrance when crushed.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodland or grassland habitats.
