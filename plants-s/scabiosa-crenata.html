Scabiosa crenata

Scabiosa crenata, commonly known as the carpet bugloss, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features a low-growing, mat-forming structure with hairy stems and small, clustered flowers. The flowers are typically blue to purple in color and bloom in early summer. It has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

This plant is well adapted to rocky and dry soils, making it a hardy choice for garden landscapes.
