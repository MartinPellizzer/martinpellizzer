Serruria altiscapa

Serruria altiscapa, commonly known as the mountain serruria, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage and clusters of bright yellow, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is adapted to full sun exposure. Its unique floral structure attracts a variety of pollinators, including bees and birds.

Serruria altiscapa is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its resilience and aesthetic qualities.
