Sansevieria abyssinica

Sansevieria abyssinica, commonly known as the African spear or desert spear, is a species of flowering plant in the asparagus family.

It is native to East Africa and is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with light green vertical stripes. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. It produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in summer, followed by red berries.

Sansevieria abyssinica is also noted for its air-purifying properties, contributing to improved indoor air quality.
