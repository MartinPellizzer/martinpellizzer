Schizoglossum ciliatum

Schizoglossum ciliatum, commonly known as the velvetleaf or velvetleaf sunflower, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators. Schizoglossum ciliatum typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in supporting local wildlife.
