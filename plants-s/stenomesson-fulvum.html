Stenomesson fulvum

Stenomesson fulvum, commonly known as the golden cupid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange ray flowers that resemble those of a dandelion. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
