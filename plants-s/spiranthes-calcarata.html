Spiranthes calcarata

Spiranthes calcarata, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, spiral-shaped inflorescence. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its flowers are small, white to pale yellow, and bloom in late spring to early summer.
