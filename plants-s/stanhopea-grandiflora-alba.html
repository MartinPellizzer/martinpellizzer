Stanhopea grandiflora alba

Stanhopea grandiflora alba, commonly known as the white orchid of Costa Rica, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to the humid forests of Central America, particularly Costa Rica and Panama. The plant features a pseudobulb with fleshy leaves and produces large, showy white flowers with a distinctive yellow lip. Its blooms are typically fragrant and appear during the dry season.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
