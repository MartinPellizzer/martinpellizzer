Sesleria rigida f. leucostachys

Sesleria rigida f. leucostachys, commonly known as the white-stemmed sesleria, is a perennial grass-like plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant produces dense clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to form dense, low-growing mats.
