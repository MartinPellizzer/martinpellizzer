Stylidium luteum var. glandulosum

Stylidium luteum var. glandulosum, commonly known as the glandular sun-orchid, is a herbaceous perennial native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers with a distinctive, spurred labellum. The plant features hairy, glandular leaves that are often covered in fine, sticky secretions. It thrives in well-drained soils and is typically found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and coastal areas.

Its unique morphology and specialized pollination mechanisms make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
