Steenhammera dahurica

Steenhammera dahurica, commonly known as the Siberian bellflower, is a perennial herb native to the cold climates of Eastern Europe and Asia.

It typically grows to a height of about 30-60 cm, featuring lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. The plant produces striking blue to purple bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy choice for gardens in temperate regions.

Steenhammera dahurica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens or as a border plant.
