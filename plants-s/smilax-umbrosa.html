Smilax umbrosa

Smilax umbrosa, commonly known as dark smilax, is a perennial climbing shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and heart-shaped, dark green leaves. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and thickets.

Smilax umbrosa is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to tolerate partial shade.
