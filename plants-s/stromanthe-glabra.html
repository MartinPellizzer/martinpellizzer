Stromanthe glabra

Stromanthe glabra, commonly known as the velvet leaf plant, is a tropical herbaceous plant native to Central and South America.

It features striking, velvety, waxy leaves that are typically deep green with intricate veining patterns. The plant is noted for its compact growth habit and ability to thrive in low-light conditions. Its leaves are often variegated with shades of silver and green, adding visual interest to indoor spaces.

Stromanthe glabra is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
