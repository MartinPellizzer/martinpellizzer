Stewartia monadelpha

Stewartia monadelpha, commonly known as the Chinese stewartia, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its ornamental, elliptical leaves that turn yellow in autumn. The tree produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers in the spring. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

It is often cultivated for its graceful form and seasonal interest in gardens.
