Senecio macdougalii

Senecio macdougalii, commonly known as the MacDougal's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Senecio macdougalii is often found in desert scrub and chaparral habitats, contributing to the ecological diversity of these regions.
