Smilax siamensis

Smilax siamensis, commonly known as the Siam arrowroot or Siam smilax, is a climbing shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Araceae and is characterized by its woody stems and spiny growth habit. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by bright red berries. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped with a rough, waxy texture.

Smilax siamensis is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
