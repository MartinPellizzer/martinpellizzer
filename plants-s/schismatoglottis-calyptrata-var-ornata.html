Schismatoglottis calyptrata var. ornata

Schismatoglottis calyptrata var. ornata, commonly known as the ornate schismatoglottis, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
