Senecio behrianus

Senecio behrianus, commonly known as the blue-eyed daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue flowers and silver-green foliage. The plant produces small, daisy-like blooms with a central yellow disk surrounded by blue petals. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Senecio behrianus is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
