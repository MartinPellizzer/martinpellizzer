Scolymocephalus cordatus

Scolymocephalus cordatus, commonly known as the heartleaf scolymus, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its small, yellowish flowers bloom in clusters during the summer months.

Scolymocephalus cordatus is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
