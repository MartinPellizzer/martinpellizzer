Senecio ecalyculatus

Senecio ecalyculatus, commonly known as the yellowstar saxifrage, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer.

Senecio ecalyculatus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained conditions.
