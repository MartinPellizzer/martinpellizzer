Scabiosa argentea subsp. ucranica

Scabiosa argentea subsp.

ucranica, commonly known as the silver scabious, is a perennial herb native to the Caucasus region. It features silver-white, velvety foliage that gives it its common name.

The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and ability to attract pollinators.
