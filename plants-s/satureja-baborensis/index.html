Satureja Baborensis

Satureja baborensis is a rare and lesser-known species of savory native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe. This aromatic herb belongs to the Lamiaceae family, sharing characteristics with other members like oregano and thyme. Its small, fragrant leaves and delicate flowers make it a unique addition to both culinary and herbal traditions. Despite its potential, it remains underappreciated and often overlooked in mainstream horticulture. Understanding and cultivating Satureja baborensis could offer new opportunities for flavor and ecological benefit.
