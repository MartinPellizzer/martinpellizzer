Stelis ruprechtiana

Stelis ruprechtiana, commonly known as the Ruprecht's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on the surface of trees or other structures without deriving nutrients from them. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Its unique morphology and blooming habits make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
