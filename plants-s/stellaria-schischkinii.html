Stellaria schischkinii

Stellaria schischkinii, commonly known as the Siberian starwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has hairy, linear leaves that are often arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to cold, alpine environments.

Stellaria schischkinii is often found in meadows and slopes at high altitudes.
