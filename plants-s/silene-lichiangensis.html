Silene lichiangensis

Silene lichiangensis, commonly known as the Yunnan campion, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, adapting to high-altitude conditions with its compact growth form. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often hairy as well.

Silene lichiangensis is of interest to botanists due to its unique ecological adaptations and potential horticultural value.
