Silene italica subsp. salzmannii

Silene italica subsp.

salzmannii, commonly known as the Salzmann's campion, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family. It is characterized by its pink to purple flowers and hairy stems, which are typical features of the genus Silene.
