Sisyranthus fanniniae

Sisyranthus fanniniae, commonly known as the Fannin's spider orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its delicate, spider-like appearance with slender, wiry stems and small, pale green leaves. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive, narrow lip. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
