Senecio taiwanensis

Senecio taiwanensis, commonly known as the Taiwan senecio, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous areas, thriving in well-drained soils. It features slender stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, arranged in clusters, and bloom during the summer months.
