Stelis subinconspicua

Stelis subinconspicua, commonly known as the slender stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces delicate, white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in humid environments with indirect light and well-draining substrates.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for enthusiasts of miniature orchids.
