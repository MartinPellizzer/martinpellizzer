Smilax pekingensis

Smilax pekingensis, commonly known as the Peking arrowroot or Chinese smilax, is a perennial vine native to China.

It features a robust rhizomatous root system and produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant is characterized by its spiny stems and trifoliate leaves with a rough texture. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that contains several seeds.

Smilax pekingensis is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides.
