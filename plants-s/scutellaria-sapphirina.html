Scutellaria sapphirina

Scutellaria sapphirina, commonly known as Sapphire skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves and a square stem typical of the Lamiaceae family. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Scutellaria sapphirina is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color.
