Senecio multivenius var. oliganthus

Senecio multivenius var. oliganthus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved groundsel, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, bushy growth habit with fleshy, narrow leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in late spring or early summer. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of care, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
