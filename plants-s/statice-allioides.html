Statice allioides

Statice allioides, commonly known as the onion statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its flowers bloom in late summer, adding a touch of color to gardens during the warmer months.
