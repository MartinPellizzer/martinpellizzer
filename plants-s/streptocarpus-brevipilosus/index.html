Streptocarpus Brevipilosus

Streptocarpus brevipilosus is a rare and unique plant native to the tropical regions of Africa. It is known for its striking, velvety leaves and delicate, trumpet-shaped flowers. This species belongs to the Gesneriaceae family, which includes many popular houseplants. Its compact size and ornamental appeal make it a sought-after addition to greenhouses and botanical collections.
