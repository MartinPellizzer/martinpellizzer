Solanum vallegrandense

Solanum vallegrandense, commonly known as the Valle Grande nightshade, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and peppers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are ovate with serrated edges, and it produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into berries.

The berries are usually green when mature and contain a single seed, making it an interesting subject for botanical study.
