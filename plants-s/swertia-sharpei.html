Swertia sharpei

Swertia sharpei, commonly known as the Chinese gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or forested areas at elevations ranging from 1,500 to 3,000 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged oppositely along the stem, while its roots are often used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Swertia sharpei is valued for its ornamental beauty and medicinal properties.
