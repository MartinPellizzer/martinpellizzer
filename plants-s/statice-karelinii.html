Statice karelinii

Statice karelinii, commonly known as the Karakul statice, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is well adapted to dry, sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Statice karelinii is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
