Scandix nodosa

Scandix nodosa, commonly known as the nodding scented dill, is a herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features finely divided, feathery leaves that are often used as a culinary herb, similar to dill. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees. Its aromatic seeds are harvested for use in cooking and traditional medicine.

Scandix nodosa thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a versatile addition to gardens and herb collections.
