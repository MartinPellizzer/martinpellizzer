Syzygium filiflorum

Syzygium filiflorum, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery red flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth, glossy surface. Its fruit is a small, round drupe that turns from green to red as it matures.

Syzygium filiflorum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
