Sansevieria nitida

Sansevieria nitida, commonly known as the stiff leaf snake plant, is a tropical succulent native to Africa.

It features narrow, upright leaves that are typically a dark green with a silvery-blue hue along the edges. The plant is characterized by its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it highly adaptable. Its leaves are rigid and pointed, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Sansevieria nitida is also known for its air-purifying properties, contributing to indoor environmental quality.
