Stanhopea cavendishii

Stanhopea cavendishii, commonly known as the Cavendish Stanhopea, is a tropical orchid native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange with intricate patterns. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in its humid, shaded habitat. Its flowers are often fragrant and attract various pollinators, including bees and moths.

Stanhopea cavendishii is prized by orchid growers for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
