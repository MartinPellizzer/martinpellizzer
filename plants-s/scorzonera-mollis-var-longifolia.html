Scorzonera mollis var. longifolia

Scorzonera mollis var. longifolia, commonly known as the longleaf scorzonera, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a robust root system and produces clusters of violet-blue flowers with yellow centers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid habitats.

Scorzonera mollis var. longifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
