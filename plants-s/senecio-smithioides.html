Senecio smithioides

Senecio smithioides, commonly known as Smith's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers. It is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often found in mountainous regions and is considered a rare species.
