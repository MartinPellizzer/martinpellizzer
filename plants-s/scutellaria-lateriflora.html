Scutellaria lateriflora

Scutellaria lateriflora, commonly known as blue skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It features square, hairy stems and opposite, ovate to lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces spikes of blue to purple flowers, which bloom from late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sun.

Scutellaria lateriflora is often found in woodlands, stream banks, and moist meadows.
