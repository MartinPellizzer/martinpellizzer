Saxifraga × andrewsii

Saxifraga × andrewsii, commonly known as the hybrid saxifrage, is a flowering plant in the Saxifragaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant features dark green, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It produces clusters of small, white or pinkish flowers during the summer months.

Saxifraga × andrewsii is often found in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils.
