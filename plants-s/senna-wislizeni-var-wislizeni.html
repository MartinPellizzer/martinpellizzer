Senna wislizeni var. wislizeni

Senna wislizeni var. wislizeni, commonly known as the yellow senna, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

The plant is often used in traditional medicine and for soil enrichment due to its nitrogen-fixing ability.
