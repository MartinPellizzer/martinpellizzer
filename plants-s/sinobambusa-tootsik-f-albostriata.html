Sinobambusa tootsik f. albostriata

Sinobambusa tootsik f. albostriata, commonly known as the white-striped bamboo, is a clump-forming bamboo species native to China.

It features slender, arching culms that are often covered with a distinctive white or pale stripe along their length. The leaves are lance-shaped and have a smooth, glossy surface, contributing to its ornamental appeal. This variety is prized for its elegant appearance and is often used in landscaping and garden design.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a versatile choice for temperate climates.
