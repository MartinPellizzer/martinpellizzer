Sideritis vuralii

Sideritis vuralii, commonly known as Vural's ironweed, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, tubular flowers that are typically pale blue or purple in color. It thrives in rocky or limestone soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Sideritis vuralii is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
