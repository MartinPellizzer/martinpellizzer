Specklinia imraei

Specklinia imraei, commonly known as the Imraei's lithops, is a member of the Aizoaceae family, native to South Africa.

It is a small, succulent plant characterized by its fleshy, stone-like leaves that store water. The plant produces delicate, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple during the warmer months. Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
