Stizolobium capitatum

Stizolobium capitatum, commonly known as the yellow burr legume, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant produces distinctive, spiny pods that contain small, hard seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in arid environments due to its drought-resistant properties.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and potential agricultural applications.
