Setaria compacta

Setaria compacta, commonly known as slender false panicgrass, is a flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, two-lobed spikelets. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue when fresh.

Setaria compacta is often found in disturbed soils and is used in some traditional practices for its medicinal properties.
