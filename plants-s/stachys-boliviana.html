Stachys boliviana

Stachys boliviana, commonly known as the Bolivian hedge nettle, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features square stems and opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, tubular flowers are typically purple or pink, arranged in whorled clusters.

Stachys boliviana is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and flowers.
