Stelis inaequalis

Stelis inaequalis, commonly known as the unequal stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic orchid, typically growing on tree branches or rocks in humid tropical environments. The plant features a short, horizontal rhizome with clustered, small, pale green to yellowish leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

Stelis inaequalis is valued in horticulture for its delicate appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
