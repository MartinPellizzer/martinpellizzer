Scutellaria gaumeri

Scutellaria gaumeri, commonly known as Gaumer's bluebug.

It is a perennial herb native to the southwestern United States, particularly found in Texas and New Mexico. This species belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

Scutellaria gaumeri is often found in rocky soils and open woodlands, thriving in full sun to partial shade.
