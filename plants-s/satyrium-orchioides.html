Satyrium orchioides

Satyrium orchioides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved twayblade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance and small, greenish-yellow flowers. Native to regions of southern Africa, it thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky areas. The plant has a short, underground stem with a single leaf, and its flowers are typically arranged in a spike.

It plays a role in local ecosystems by providing nectar to various pollinators.
