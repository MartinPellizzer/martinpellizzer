Stellaria parviflora subsp. ignis

Stellaria parviflora subsp.

ignis, commonly known as the firestar starwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It features small, white to pale pink flowers that are star-shaped, giving it its common name.

The plant has delicate, branching stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and ornamental species within the Caryophyllaceae family.
