Stelis verecunda

Stelis verecunda, commonly known as the elegant orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and striking, showy flowers that often display a mix of white, pink, and purple hues. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often epiphytic on trees or rocks. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding substrate.

Stelis verecunda is valued for its ornamental beauty and is commonly cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
