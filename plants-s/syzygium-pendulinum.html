Syzygium pendulinum

Syzygium pendulinum, commonly known as the hanging star or Chinese starfruit, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen tree that produces small, star-shaped fruits with a distinctive yellow or orange color. The leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptic in shape, with a glossy surface. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its fruit is edible, though it has a slightly tart flavor and is often used in culinary applications.
