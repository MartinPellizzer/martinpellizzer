Scrophularia sikkimensis

Scrophularia sikkimensis, commonly known as Sikkim Scrophularia, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan region of India.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae, which includes many species with medicinal properties. The plant typically grows in moist, rocky habitats at high altitudes. It produces showy, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple in color.

Its leaves are opposite and often covered with fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
