Serratula radiata subsp. biebersteiniana

Serratula radiata subsp.

biebersteiniana, commonly known as the Alpine serrata, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces yellow to orange composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky slopes, thriving in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are serrated and often covered in a fine layer of white pubescence.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and seasonal blooms.
