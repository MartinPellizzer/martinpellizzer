Saxifraga aizoon var. neogaea

Saxifraga aizoon var. neogaea, commonly known as the rock saxifrage, is a perennial flowering plant native to arid and rocky regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in early summer. Its leaves are succulent, adapted to conserve water in harsh environments.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
