Sedum fanjingshanense

Sedum fanjingshanense, commonly known as the Fanjingshan stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to China.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit and fleshy, green leaves that are often adorned with a waxy coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It is adapted to rocky and arid environments, making it a hardy species suitable for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
