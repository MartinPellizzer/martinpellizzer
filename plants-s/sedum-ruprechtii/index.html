Sedum Ruprechtii

Sedum ruprechtii is a striking succulent native to the mountainous regions of China. It is known for its vibrant green leaves and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. This plant is highly adaptable and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance requirements. Its unique appearance and resilience make it a favorite among plant enthusiasts and gardeners alike.
