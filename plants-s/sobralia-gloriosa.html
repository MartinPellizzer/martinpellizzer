Sobralia gloriosa

Sobralia gloriosa, commonly known as the glorious sobralia, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to the Caribbean and Central America.

It features showy, pendulous inflorescences with brightly colored, spurred flowers that range from red to orange. The plant has thick, fleshy roots adapted for absorbing moisture from the air and rain. Its leaves are typically elongated and covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs.

Sobralia gloriosa thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.
