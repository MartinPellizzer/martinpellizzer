Scrophularia iranica

Scrophularia iranica, commonly known as Iranian figwort, is a flowering plant native to Iran.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically purple or pink in color. It thrives in rocky or arid environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Scrophularia iranica is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
