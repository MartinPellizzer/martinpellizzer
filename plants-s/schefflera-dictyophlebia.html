Schefflera dictyophlebia

Schefflera dictyophlebia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like crown of large, glossy, dark green leaves that are typically narrow and elongated. The plant produces small, white, clustered flowers followed by bright red berries that attract birds. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.

Schefflera dictyophlebia is relatively low-maintenance and suitable for indoor growing in suitable conditions.
