Stomatanthes tundavalaensis

Stomatanthes tundavalaensis, commonly known as the Tundavala sandveld grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. It plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
