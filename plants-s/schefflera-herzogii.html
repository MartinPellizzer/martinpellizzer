Schefflera herzogii

Schefflera herzogii, commonly known as the Herzog's umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like crown of large, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Schefflera herzogii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and compact growth habit.
