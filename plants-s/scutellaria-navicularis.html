Scutellaria navicularis

Scutellaria navicularis, commonly known as narrowleaf skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It produces small, blue or violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, giving it a distinctive appearance.
