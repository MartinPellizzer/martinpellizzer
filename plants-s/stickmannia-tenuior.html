Stickmannia tenuior

Stickmannia tenuior, commonly known as the slender stickman plant, is a small, herbaceous species native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, commonly referred to as the ice plant family, and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves. Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
