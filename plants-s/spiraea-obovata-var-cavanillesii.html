Spiraea obovata var. cavanillesii

Spiraea obovata var. cavanillesii, commonly known as the Cavanilles' spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It features upright, slender stems that bear clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers in early summer. The leaves are ovate to obovate in shape, with a glossy upper surface and a hairy underside. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

This variety is noted for its compact growth habit and ornamental value, particularly in spring and summer.
