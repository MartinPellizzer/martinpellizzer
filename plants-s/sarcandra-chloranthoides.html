Sarcandra chloranthoides

Sarcandra chloranthoides, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in clusters, which are not showy but attract pollinators. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor plants due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.
