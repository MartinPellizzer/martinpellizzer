Stylidium thyrsiforme

Stylidium thyrsiforme, commonly known as the thrysis or spikeless sun-orchid, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its unique, spike-like inflorescence. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers that are often clustered together, giving it a striking appearance. It thrives in sandy soils and is commonly found in heathland and coastal areas.

Stylidium thyrsiforme is known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and wasps due to its specialized floral structure.
