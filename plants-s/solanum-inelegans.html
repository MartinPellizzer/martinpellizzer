Solanum inelegans

Solanum inelegans, commonly known as the elegant nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue, followed by yellow berries that contain the seeds. Solanum inelegans belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and unique fruit.
