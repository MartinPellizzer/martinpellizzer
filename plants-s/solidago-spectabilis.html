Solidago spectabilis

Solidago spectabilis, commonly known as showy goldenrod, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It is characterized by its bright yellow inflorescences, which consist of numerous small, disk-like flowers surrounded by pale green to white bracts. The plant typically grows in moist to dry soils in open woodlands, meadows, and along roadsides. It thrives in full sun and is known for its ability to attract a variety of pollinators, including bees and butterflies.

Solidago spectabilis is a hardy species that is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
