Senecio rigens

Senecio rigens, commonly known as the rigid groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, branching stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy species suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Senecio rigens is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract beneficial insects.
