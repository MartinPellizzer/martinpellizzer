Salacia staudtiana var. leonensis

Salacia staudtiana var. leonensis, commonly known as the Leonese salacia, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Salaceae family and is characterized by its dense foliage and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in the understory of tropical rainforests. Its leaves are elliptical and dark green, with a smooth texture, while its flowers are usually yellow or orange in color.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique appearance.
