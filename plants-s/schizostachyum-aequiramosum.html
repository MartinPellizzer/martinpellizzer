Schizostachyum aequiramosum

Schizostachyum aequiramosum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bamboo, is a species of grass-like plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, linear leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. This plant is often found in moist, tropical environments and is adapted to thrive in humid, shaded conditions. Schizostachyum aequiramosum is valued for its strong, fibrous stalks, which are used in construction and crafts.
