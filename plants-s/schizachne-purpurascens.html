Schizachne purpurascens

Schizachne purpurascens, commonly known as the purple grass or purple panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, spike-like inflorescences that often display a striking purple hue. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves with a slightly rough texture, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soils. Schizachne purpurascens is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along riverbanks, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.

Its ornamental value has also made it a popular choice for landscaping in certain regions.
