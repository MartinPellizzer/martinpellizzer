Scorzoneroides carpetana subsp. nevadensis

Scorzoneroides carpetana subsp.

nevadensis, commonly known as the Nevada scorzonera, is a flowering plant native to the western United States. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of silver-gray, woolly leaves.

The plant produces showy yellow flowers with prominent orange centers, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine and subalpine environments.

This subspecies is adapted to cold climates and is an important component of high-elevation ecosystems.
