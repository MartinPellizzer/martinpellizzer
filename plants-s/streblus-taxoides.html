Streblus taxoides

Streblus taxoides, commonly known as the Indian rubber tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy upper surface. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by woody, spherical fruits containing several seeds. It is often cultivated for its latex, which has historical uses in rubber production.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its various therapeutic properties.
