Strumarium

Strumarium, commonly known as the common thistle, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple or pink flower heads composed of many small florets. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny environments such as meadows and wastelands. Its leaves are covered in a dense layer of fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

The plant is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
