Sideritis gineslopezii

Sideritis gineslopezii, commonly known as the Gineslopez's ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features slender, erect stems and small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or pink in color. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Sideritis gineslopezii is often found in rocky or limestone soils, thriving in dry, sunny environments.
