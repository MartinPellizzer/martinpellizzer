Symplocos martini

Symplocos martini, commonly known as the Indian snowberry, is a deciduous shrub native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. Symplocos martini thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas at high altitudes.

Its leaves are oval-shaped with a smooth margin and a glossy surface.
