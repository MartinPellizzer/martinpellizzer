Solenocentrum maasii

Solenocentrum maasii, commonly known as the Maas's spider plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a compact, evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 30 cm, featuring dense clusters of small, narrow leaves. The plant produces fragrant, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white and yellow, which bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Solenocentrum maasii is relatively low-maintenance and is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
