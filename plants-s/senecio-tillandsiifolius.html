Senecio tillandsiifolius

Senecio tillandsiifolius, commonly known as the Cape York Senecio, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, narrow leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hair, which helps reduce water loss in arid conditions. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, typically during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
