Sedum maximum subsp. caucasicum

Sedum maximum subsp.

caucasicum, commonly known as the Caucasian stonecrop, is a perennial succulent native to the Caucasus region. It features fleshy, upright stems and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink.

The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Its leaves are typically thick and green, adapted to store water in arid environments.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
