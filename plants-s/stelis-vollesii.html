Stelis vollesii

Stelis vollesii, commonly known as the Vollesii orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often found in forest understories. The plant features a rhizomatous stem with narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, clustered in inflorescences, and usually have a pale green or white color with a subtle fragrance.

Stelis vollesii is appreciated for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
