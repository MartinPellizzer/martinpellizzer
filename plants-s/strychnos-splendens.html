Strychnos splendens

Strychnos splendens, commonly known as the golden star or flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling stars. The plant produces small, round, green berries that turn red when mature, containing seeds enclosed in a fleshy pulp. Its leaves are typically palmately lobed, providing a lush green canopy.

Strychnos splendens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and parks for its vibrant floral display.
