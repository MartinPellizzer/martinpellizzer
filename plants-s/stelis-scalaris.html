Stelis scalaris

Stelis scalaris, commonly known as the scalloped orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on the surface of other plants without being parasitic. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, tropical environments. The plant features elongated, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers.

Its unique appearance and delicate blooms make it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
