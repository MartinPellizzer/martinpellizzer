Schefflera macgregorii

Schefflera macgregorii, commonly known as the MacGregor's umbrella plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features a distinctive, umbrella-like crown of large, glossy, dark green leaves that are arranged in a whorled pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid conditions and requires well-draining soil.

Schefflera macgregorii is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor environments.
