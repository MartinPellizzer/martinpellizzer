Sobralia liliastrum var. rosea

Sobralia liliastrum var. rosea, commonly known as the pink sobralia, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features strap-like, dark green leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil and bright indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance, it is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardens.
