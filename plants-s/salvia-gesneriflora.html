Salvia gesneriflora

Salvia gesneriflora, commonly known as the pink flamingo plant, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from pink to purple, resembling the feathers of a flamingo. The plant produces tubular flowers that are typically borne in clusters at the ends of its branches. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-draining soil.

Salvia gesneriflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
