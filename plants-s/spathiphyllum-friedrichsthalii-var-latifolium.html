Spathiphyllum friedrichsthalii var. latifolium

Spathiphyllum friedrichsthalii var. latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf peace lily, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, waxy, dark green leaves that are broad and lance-shaped, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces elegant, white spathes that enclose small, greenish-white flowers, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in low-light conditions and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

This variety is popular as a houseplant due to its air-purifying qualities and ornamental value.
