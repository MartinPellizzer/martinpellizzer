Sinapis elongata

Sinapis elongata, commonly known as the yellow rocket, is a biennial or annual plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and elongated, ribbed stems. The plant produces long, slender pods that contain small, rounded seeds. Sinapis elongata is often found in disturbed soils, such as roadsides and fields, and is known for its rapid growth.

It has been used historically in herbal medicine for its potential health benefits.
