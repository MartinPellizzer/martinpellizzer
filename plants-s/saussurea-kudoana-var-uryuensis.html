Saussurea kudoana var. uryuensis

Saussurea kudoana var. uryuensis, commonly known as the Uryuensis Hawk's Beard, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Japan and is characterized by its upright growth habit and composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow to orange composite flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
