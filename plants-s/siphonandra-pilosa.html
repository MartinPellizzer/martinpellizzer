Siphonandra pilosa

Siphonandra pilosa, commonly known as the hairy siphonandra, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and small, clustered white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage is often covered in fine, soft hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid environments.

Siphonandra pilosa is a popular choice for rock gardens due to its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
