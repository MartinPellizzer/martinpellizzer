Squilla sphaeroidea

Squilla sphaeroidea, commonly known as the globe squill, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is a bulbous perennial that produces striking blue or violet flowers during the spring. The plant belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its dense, clustered foliage. Its bulbs are often used in horticulture for their ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
