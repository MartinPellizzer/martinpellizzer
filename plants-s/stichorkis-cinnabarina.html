Stichorkis cinnabarina

Stichorkis cinnabarina, commonly known as the red-stemmed bellflower, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, reddish stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to deep purple, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Stichorkis cinnabarina thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
