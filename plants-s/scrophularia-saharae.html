Scrophularia saharae

Scrophularia saharae, commonly known as the Saharan figwort, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of North Africa.

It is a member of the Scrophulariaceae family, which includes many species adapted to dry environments. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is characterized by its hairy stems and opposite leaves. It produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple in color.

Scrophularia saharae is well adapted to withstand high temperatures and low water availability.
