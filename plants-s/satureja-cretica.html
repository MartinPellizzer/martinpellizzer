Satureja cretica

Satureja cretica, commonly known as Greek thyme, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. Its small, pink or white flowers bloom in summer, and the leaves are used in culinary and medicinal applications.

Satureja cretica is valued for its aromatic properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
