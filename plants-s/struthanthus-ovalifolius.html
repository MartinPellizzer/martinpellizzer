Struthanthus ovalifolius

Struthanthus ovalifolius, commonly known as the desert star.

It is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. The plant is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy, oval-shaped leaves. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy or rocky soils.
