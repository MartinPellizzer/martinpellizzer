Scorzonera intricata

Scorzonera intricata, commonly known as the alpine scorzonera, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

Scorzonera intricata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in alpine ecosystems.
