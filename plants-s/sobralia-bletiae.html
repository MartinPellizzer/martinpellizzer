Sobralia bletiae

Sobralia bletiae, commonly known as the Bletia Sobralia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually brightly colored, with a unique structure that includes a spurred lip.

Sobralia bletiae is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
