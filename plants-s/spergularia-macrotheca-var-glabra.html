Spergularia macrotheca var. glabra

Spergularia macrotheca var. glabra, commonly known as smooth sandspurry, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically thrives in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers with five petals, and its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy. It is often found in coastal dunes and open, disturbed habitats.

Its smooth, glabrous stems and leaves distinguish it from other varieties within the species.
