Spilanthes pseudogummifera

Spilanthes pseudogummifera, commonly known as the false gumweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the arid regions of North America.

It belongs to the genus Spilanthes, which is part of the family Asteraceae, commonly known as the sunflower family. The plant is characterized by its erect stems, narrow leaves, and small yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is often found in dry soils and can tolerate drought conditions due to its deep root system.
