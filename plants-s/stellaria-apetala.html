Stellaria apetala

Stellaria apetala, commonly known as the starwort, is a small flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, grassy areas and has slender, branching stems. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, often covered in fine hairs.

Stellaria apetala is known for its short blooming period, usually in late spring or early summer.
