Senecio insularis

Senecio insularis, commonly known as the Hawaiian groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in coastal areas and dry habitats. The plant features slender, branching stems and small, daisy-like yellow flowers. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in fine hairs.

Senecio insularis plays a role in the local ecosystem and is adapted to the unique environmental conditions of Hawaii.
