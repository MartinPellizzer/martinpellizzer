Svensonia

Svensonia, commonly known as the swamp willow, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the family Myrtaceae.

It is characterized by its woody stems and opposite, simple leaves that are often covered in a fine pubescence. The flowers are typically small and clustered in inflorescences, with showy bracts that attract pollinators.
