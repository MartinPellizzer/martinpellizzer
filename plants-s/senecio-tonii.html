Senecio tonii

Senecio tonii, commonly known as the Blue-green Senecio, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, which help it store water and withstand arid conditions. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
