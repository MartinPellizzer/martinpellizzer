Senecio pindilicensis

Senecio pindilicensis, commonly known as the Pindos groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, thriving in arid and semi-arid environments. It features small, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow.

Senecio pindilicensis is often found in open habitats such as coastal areas and mountainous regions.
