Seseli devenyense

Seseli devenyense, commonly known as the Transylvanian seseli, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apiaceae family.

It is native to the Carpathian region, particularly found in Hungary and Romania. The plant features a basal rosette of finely divided, fern-like leaves and produces small, clustered white flowers. It thrives in rocky or limestone soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.
