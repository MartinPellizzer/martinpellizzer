Senecio jacobaeoides

Senecio jacobaeoides, commonly known as the African Daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with narrow, gray-green leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Senecio jacobaeoides is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
