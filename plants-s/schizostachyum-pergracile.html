Schizostachyum pergracile

Schizostachyum pergracile, commonly known as the slender palm, is a species of palm native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and long, pinnate leaves that give it a graceful appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in panicles, which are followed by clustered, dark brown fruits. Schizostachyum pergracile thrives in tropical climates and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

Its unique morphology and adaptability make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
