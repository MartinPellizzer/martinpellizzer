Stelis zamorensis

Stelis zamorensis, commonly known as the Zamora orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the cloud forests of Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows on rocky substrates and thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to anchor in rocky crevices.

Stelis zamorensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
