Sedum painteri

Sedum painteri, commonly known as the painter's sedum, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is characterized by its ability to store water in its leaves, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. It is often found growing in rocky soils at high altitudes.

Sedum painteri is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in dry conditions.
