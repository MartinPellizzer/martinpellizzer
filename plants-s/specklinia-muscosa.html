Specklinia muscosa

Specklinia muscosa, commonly known as the mossy specklinia, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and Indonesia. This epiphytic plant typically grows on trees and rocks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its foliage is characterized by dark green, velvety leaves that are often covered in a fine, moss-like coating.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, which bloom during the wet season.
