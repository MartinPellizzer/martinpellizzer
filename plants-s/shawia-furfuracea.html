Shawia furfuracea

Shawia furfuracea, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. Shawia furfuracea is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.

It is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage.
