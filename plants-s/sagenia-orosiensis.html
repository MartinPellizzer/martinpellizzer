Sagenia orosiensis

Sagenia orosiensis, commonly known as the Canary Island sage, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, pale blue or purple flowers. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to the arid climate of its native habitat.

Sagenia orosiensis is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, where it contributes to the unique flora of the Canary Islands.
