Specklinia tubelliflora

Specklinia tubelliflora, commonly known as the tubular-flowered specklinia, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, tubular-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories where it relies on specific mycorrhizal fungi for nutrient uptake. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in a fine, velvety texture, enhancing its ornamental appeal.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and specialized orchid species.
