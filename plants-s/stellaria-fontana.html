Stellaria fontana

Stellaria fontana, commonly known as the starwort, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, white or pale blue flowers that are arranged in clusters, giving it a star-like appearance. Its leaves are opposite, narrow, and often covered in fine hairs.

Stellaria fontana thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides.
