Spiesia myriophylla

Spiesia myriophylla, commonly known as the Cape mountain heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant features small, needle-like leaves and produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. It is adapted to coastal and mountainous regions with a Mediterranean climate.

Spiesia myriophylla is often found in fynbos vegetation and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
