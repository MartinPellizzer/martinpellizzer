Swertia kouitchensis var. kouitchensis

Swertia kouitchensis var. kouitchensis, commonly known as Chinese swertia, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers during the summer months, which are clustered in racemes. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and traditional herbal practices.
