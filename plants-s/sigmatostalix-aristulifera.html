Sigmatostalix aristulifera

Sigmatostalix aristulifera, commonly known as the feather-leaved sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges, and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and wetland restoration projects.
