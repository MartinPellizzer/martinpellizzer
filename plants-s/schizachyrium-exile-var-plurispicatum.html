Schizachyrium exile var. plurispicatum

Schizachyrium exile var. plurispicatum, commonly known as slender false panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to the eastern United States.

It features slender, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, bract-like flowers. It thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in wetland habitats.
