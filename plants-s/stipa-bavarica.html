Stipa bavarica

Stipa bavarica, commonly known as the Bavarian feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to Central Europe.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and delicate, feathery inflorescences that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Stipa bavarica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and restoration projects.
