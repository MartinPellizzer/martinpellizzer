Sobralia tricolor

Sobralia tricolor, commonly known as the three-colored orchid, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its strikingly colored flowers. The plant typically produces flowers in shades of red, yellow, and white, which are arranged in a spiral pattern on the inflorescence. Sobralia tricolor thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant and unique floral display.
