Schoenus tristachyus

Schoenus tristachyus, commonly known as the spikegrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in wetland and marshy environments, preferring moist, well-drained soils. The plant features a dense, tufted growth habit with slender, cylindrical stems that often form compact clumps. Its inflorescence consists of a single, narrow spike that bears numerous small, spikelet-like flowers.

Schoenus tristachyus plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
