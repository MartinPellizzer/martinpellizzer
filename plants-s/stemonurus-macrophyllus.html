Stemonurus macrophyllus

Stemonurus macrophyllus, commonly known as the broadleaf stemonurus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are usually greenish-white and appear in clusters.

Stemonurus macrophyllus is valued for its ornamental foliage and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
