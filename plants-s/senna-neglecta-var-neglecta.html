Senna neglecta var. neglecta

Senna neglecta var. neglecta, commonly known as the small senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for cultivation in warm climates.

Its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators make it a popular choice for gardens.
