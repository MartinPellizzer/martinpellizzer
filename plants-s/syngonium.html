Syngonium

Syngonium, commonly known as arrowhead plant, is a tropical vine native to the Americas.

It features heart-shaped or arrowhead-shaped leaves that vary in color, often displaying variegation. The plant thrives in moist, well-draining soil and prefers indirect sunlight. Syngonium is known for its rapid growth and ability to climb or trail, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its botanical name, Syngonium podophyllum, reflects its unique leaf structure and growth habits.
