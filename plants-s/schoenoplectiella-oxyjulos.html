Schoenoplectiella oxyjulos

Schoenoplectiella oxyjulos, commonly known as the slender bulrush, is a wetland plant native to parts of Asia and North America.

It typically grows in shallow water or damp soil, often found in marshes and along the edges of ponds. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in late summer. Its rhizomes help stabilize soil and prevent erosion in aquatic environments.

It plays an important role in wetland ecosystems by providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
