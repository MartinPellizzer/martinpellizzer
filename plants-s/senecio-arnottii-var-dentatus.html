Senecio arnottii var. dentatus

Senecio arnottii var. dentatus, commonly known as the serrated senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, serrated leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
