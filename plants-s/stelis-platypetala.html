Stelis platypetala

Stelis platypetala, commonly known as the flat-lipped stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit and produces striking, showy flowers with a unique flat petal structure. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are often pale green or white with a subtle fragrance, attracting specific pollinators.

Stelis platypetala is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation under the right conditions.
