Schismatoglottis calyptrata f. multimarginata

Schismatoglottis calyptrata f. multimarginata, commonly known as the Multimarginate Schismatoglottis, is a rare and striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves with a distinctive wavy margin, which gives it its common name. The plant produces showy, white to pale pink flowers with a unique, spurred structure typical of orchids. It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is often cultivated by enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
