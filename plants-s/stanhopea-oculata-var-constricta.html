Stanhopea oculata var. constricta

Stanhopea oculata var. constricta, commonly known as the narrow-leafed stanhopea, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in late summer or early autumn. The plant features elongated, narrow leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. Its flowers are usually pale yellow with intricate patterns of darker hues, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and relatively ease of cultivation under suitable conditions.
