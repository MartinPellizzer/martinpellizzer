Sideritis rotundifolia

Sideritis rotundifolia, commonly known as round-leaved sainfoin, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are round and hairy, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Sideritis rotundifolia is often found in rocky or dry habitats and is valued for its medicinal properties.
