Solanum bill-hookeri

Solanum bill-hookeri, commonly known as the bill-hooker nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, with small, ovate leaves and clusters of white or pale purple flowers.

Its fruits are small berries that mature to a yellowish color, and the plant is often found in arid and semi-arid regions.
