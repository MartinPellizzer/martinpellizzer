Stelis megachlamys f. megachlamys

Stelis megachlamys f. megachlamys, commonly known as the showy stelis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers with a distinctive yellow and purple coloration. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its flowers are often pollinated by bees and other insects, contributing to its ecological role in tropical forests.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is commonly cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
