Stenospermation peripense

Stenospermation peripense, commonly known as the narrow-seed orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, slender pseudobulbs and narrow leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant orchid flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white and yellow. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
