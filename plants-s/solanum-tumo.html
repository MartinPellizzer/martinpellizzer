Solanum tumo

Solanum tumo, commonly known as the swollen nightshade, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, swollen, fleshy fruits that resemble small, inflated berries. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are ovate with a rough texture, and it produces small, white or pale yellow flowers.

Solanum tumo is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique fruit structure.
