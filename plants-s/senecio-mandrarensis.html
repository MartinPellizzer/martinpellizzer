Senecio mandrarensis

Senecio mandrarensis, commonly known as the Karoo groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, succulent stems and narrow, fleshy leaves. The plant produces small yellow flowers arranged in composite heads, typical of the daisy family. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils and open grasslands.
