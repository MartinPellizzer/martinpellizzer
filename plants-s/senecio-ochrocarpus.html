Senecio ochrocarpus

Senecio ochrocarpus, commonly known as the yellow-caps senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces bright yellow flower heads that resemble daisies. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky or sandy soils.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
