Sphaerocoryne astiae

Sphaerocoryne astiae, commonly known as the African violet, is a tropical plant native to eastern Africa.

The plant is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is known for its compact growth habit and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light. Its flowers bloom continuously throughout the year under optimal conditions, making it a popular ornamental plant.
