Stelis oestlundiana

Stelis oestlundiana, commonly known as the Oestlund's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees in tropical rainforests. The plant features a pseudobulb and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture from the air and rain.

Stelis oestlundiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or terrariums.
