Stelis cauda-equina

Stelis cauda-equina, commonly known as the horse-tail orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features elongated, grass-like leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers.

Its unique appearance and growth habits make it a popular choice for tropical gardens and botanical collections.
