Stanhopea avicula

Stanhopea avicula, commonly known as the avicula orchid, is a species of orchid native to Ecuador.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant, yellow and orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in its natural habitat. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, typically arranged in a fan shape.

Stanhopea avicula is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
