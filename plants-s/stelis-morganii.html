Stelis morganii

Stelis morganii, commonly known as the Morgan's stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of small, white or pale pink blossoms that bloom during the summer months. Stelis morganii thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
