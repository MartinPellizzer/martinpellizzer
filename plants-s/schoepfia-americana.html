Schoepfia americana

Schoepfia americana, commonly known as American schoepfia, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are usually small, elliptic, and have a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

The plant is adapted to withstand drought conditions, making it a resilient species in desert ecosystems.
