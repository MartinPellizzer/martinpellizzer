Senecio canaliculatus

Senecio canaliculatus, commonly known as the African marguerite, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and hairy stems. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in heads, giving it a daisy-like appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Senecio canaliculatus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its hardiness and low maintenance requirements.
