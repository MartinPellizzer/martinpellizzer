Senecio multnomensis

Senecio multnomensis, commonly known as the Desert Senecio, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, such as deserts and rocky soils. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish to greenish flowers arranged in dense, cylindrical clusters. It is adapted to survive in harsh, dry conditions with minimal water requirements.

Senecio multnomensis plays a role in local ecosystems by providing food and habitat for various insects and small animals.
