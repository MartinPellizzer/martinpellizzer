Senecio candidans

Senecio candidans, commonly known as the white ragwort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are usually hairy and have a distinctive grayish-green color.

Senecio candidans is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
