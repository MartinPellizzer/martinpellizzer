Staurogyne bullata

Staurogyne bullata, commonly known as the bullet staurogyne, is a species of aquatic plant native to the freshwater habitats of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth form and its ability to thrive in slow-moving or still water environments. The plant features small, rounded leaves that are typically green to reddish in color, depending on light conditions. Staurogyne bullata is often used in aquariums due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of maintenance.
