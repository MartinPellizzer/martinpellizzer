Sinobambusa sulcata

Sinobambusa sulcata, commonly known as the ridge bamboo, is a species of flowering bamboo native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, hollow stems that often exhibit distinct ridges or grooves along their surface. The plant typically grows in moist, temperate regions and prefers well-drained soil. Its leaves are long and narrow, with a glossy green color, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers.

Sinobambusa sulcata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
