Sansevieria sambiranensis

Sansevieria sambiranensis, commonly known as the Sambiranensis Snake Plant, is a species native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with distinctive light green or yellowish variegation. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its foliage is often adorned with small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters.

Sansevieria sambiranensis is also noted for its air-purifying properties, contributing to improved indoor air quality.
