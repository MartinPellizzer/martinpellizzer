Scutellaria wightiana var. microphylla

Scutellaria wightiana var. microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of India.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are typically small and covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are blue to purple, arranged in whorls, and are borne on tall, slender spikes.

It is often found in rocky or open habitats and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
