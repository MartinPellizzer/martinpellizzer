Symphyotrichum chilense var. invenustum

Symphyotrichum chilense var. invenustum, commonly known as the Chilean heath aster, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Chile.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its clustered growth habit and showy, typically purple or pink flowers. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and is adapted to grow in dry, rocky or sandy soils.

It is often found in coastal or mountainous regions where it thrives in full sun and well-drained conditions.
