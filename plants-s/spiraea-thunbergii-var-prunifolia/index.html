Spiraea Thunbergii Var. Prunifolia

Spiraea thunbergii var. prunifolia is a lesser-known but strikingly beautiful shrub native to eastern Asia. It is prized for its delicate, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade, making it a versatile addition to gardens. Its compact size and ornamental value make it a great choice for small spaces or containers. Despite its charm, it remains underappreciated compared to more common garden plants.
