Salacia lineolata

Salacia lineolata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf salacia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Salaceae family and is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are often marked with distinct white or pale veins, giving it a striking appearance.

Salacia lineolata is valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
