Senecio fuliginosus

Senecio fuliginosus, commonly known as the sooty senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It features narrow, gray-green leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers.
