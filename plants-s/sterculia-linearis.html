Sterculia linearis

Sterculia linearis, commonly known as the slender sterculia, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its elongated, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by woody, capsule-like fruits. Its bark is thick and fibrous, often used in traditional medicine.
