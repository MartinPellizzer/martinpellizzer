Styrax langkongensis

Styrax langkongensis, commonly known as the Langkong styrax, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Styracaceae and is characterized by its fragrant, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces a resinous sap, which can be used in traditional medicine and as a natural adhesive. It typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, shaded environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation.
