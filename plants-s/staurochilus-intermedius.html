Staurochilus intermedius

Staurochilus intermedius, commonly known as the intermediate starry orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest understories. Its flowers are usually a vibrant yellow or orange, with a complex structure that includes a spurred lip.

Staurochilus intermedius is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
