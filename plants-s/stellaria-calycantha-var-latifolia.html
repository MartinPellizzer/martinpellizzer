Stellaria calycantha var. latifolia

Stellaria calycantha var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf starwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

This variant is distinguished by its broader leaves compared to other varieties of Stellaria calycantha.
