Stelis coelochila

Stelis coelochila, commonly known as the violet lady's tresses, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, linear leaves and intricate floral structure. The plant produces small, violet-colored flowers arranged in a dense, cylindrical spike, which gives it its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on tree trunks or branches.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts worldwide.
