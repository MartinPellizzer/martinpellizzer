Sideritis ibanezii

Sideritis ibanezii, commonly known as the Ibanez's ironweed, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants known for their medicinal properties. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in fine hairs.

Sideritis ibanezii is valued for its potential medicinal uses, including its traditional role in herbal remedies.
