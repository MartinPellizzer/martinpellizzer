Senecio ameghinoi

Senecio ameghinoi, commonly known as the Ameghino's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Argentina.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and has a low, spreading growth habit. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in fine hairs, while its yellow flowers appear in clusters.

It is often found in arid or semi-arid regions, showing adaptations to survive in harsh environmental conditions.
