Stellaria chinensis var. ciliata

Stellaria chinensis var. ciliata, commonly known as Chinese starwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It features small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, branched stems covered with fine, hairy hairs, and its leaves are lance-shaped with serrated edges. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as forest floors or wet meadows.

The species is characterized by its delicate appearance and is often found in temperate regions of China and Korea.
