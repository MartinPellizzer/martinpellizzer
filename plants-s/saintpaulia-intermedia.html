Saintpaulia intermedia

Saintpaulia intermedia, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, waxy leaves and showy, long-lasting flowers that come in a wide range of colors. The plant typically grows in compact rosettes, with upright stems that bear clusters of tubular flowers. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Saintpaulia intermedia is known for its ability to bloom continuously throughout the year under proper care.
