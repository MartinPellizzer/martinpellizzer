Stellaria longifolia var. eciliata

Stellaria longifolia var. eciliata, commonly known as the slender starwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. This variant is native to Europe and is typically found in grassy areas and along roadsides.

It is a herbaceous perennial that thrives in well-drained soils and full to partial sunlight.
