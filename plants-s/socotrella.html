Socotrella

Socotrella, commonly known as the Socotra cactus, is a unique plant native to the island of Socotra in the Indian Ocean.

It belongs to the family Cactaceae and is characterized by its spiny, segmented stems and vibrant yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to the arid climate of its native habitat, with features that help it conserve water. Its structure resembles a tree, with a thick, woody trunk and branches that grow upward.

Socotrella is also notable for its medicinal properties, traditionally used in local remedies.
