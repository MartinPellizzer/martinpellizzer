Sphinctanthus maculatus var. macrophylla

Sphinctanthus maculatus var. macrophylla, commonly known as the spotted sphinctanthus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant features succulent leaves that are often spotted or mottled with contrasting colors. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and drought tolerance in landscaping.
