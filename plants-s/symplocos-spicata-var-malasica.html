Symplocos spicata var. malasica

Symplocos spicata var. malasica, commonly known as the Malasian evergreen holly, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, glossy evergreen leaves. The plant produces small, white, bell-shaped flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are small, red berries that mature in late summer.
