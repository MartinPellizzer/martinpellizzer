Sarosanthera excelsa

Sarosanthera excelsa, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, showy flowers that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is known for its complex inflorescence with multiple blooms. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
