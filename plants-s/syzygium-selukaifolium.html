Syzygium selukaifolium

Syzygium selukaifolium, commonly known as the Selukai leaf syzygium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. It produces small, round fruits that are typically edible and have a sweet, aromatic flavor.

Syzygium selukaifolium is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
