Sansevieria glauca

Sansevieria glauca, commonly known as the Variegated Snake Plant, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to southern Africa.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with distinctive white or yellow variegation. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it highly adaptable to indoor environments. Its air-purifying qualities make it a popular choice for both residential and commercial spaces.

Sansevieria glauca is also valued for its resilience, as it can tolerate a wide range of temperatures and soil conditions.
