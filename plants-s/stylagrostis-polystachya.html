Stylagrostis polystachya

Stylagrostis polystachya, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to eastern and southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery orange to red flowers that appear in dense clusters, giving it its common name. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often pubescent, and it produces small, round, woody fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Stylagrostis polystachya is also valued for its ability to withstand drought and its role in supporting local wildlife.
