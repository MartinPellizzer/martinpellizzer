Steris Atropurpurea

Steris atropurpurea is a striking member of the Asteraceae family known for its vibrant purple flowers and distinctive foliage. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. Its unique appearance and hardy nature have made it a favorite among gardeners and botanists alike. Native to parts of Europe and Asia, it has adapted to a variety of climates and environments. With its elegant blooms and resilience, Steris atropurpurea continues to captivate plant enthusiasts around the world.
