Solanum pulchrilobum var. paucilobum

Solanum pulchrilobum var. paucilobum, commonly known as the slender nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features angular, branched stems covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are ovate to lanceolate with serrated margins. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue, followed by yellow, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal potential, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
