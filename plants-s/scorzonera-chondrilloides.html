Scorzonera chondrilloides

Scorzonera chondrilloides, commonly known as the woolly scorzonera, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its hairy, gray-green foliage and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its root system is robust, allowing it to thrive in nutrient-poor soils.
