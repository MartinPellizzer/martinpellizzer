Swida poliophylla var. malifolia

Swida poliophylla var. malifolia, commonly known as the Malifolia Swida, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that are often adorned with a distinctive silvery or white underside. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, which bloom in clusters during the warmer months.
