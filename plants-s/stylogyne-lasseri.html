Stylogyne lasseri

Stylogyne lasseri, commonly known as the narrow-leafed grass.

It is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically arranged in dense clusters.

Stylogyne lasseri is adapted to dry, rocky environments and is often found in open grasslands and along rocky outcrops.
