Senecio fastigiatus var. layneae

Senecio fastigiatus var. layneae, commonly known as the Layne's upright groundsel, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, upright stems with dense, narrow leaves that are often covered in a fine white powdery coating. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or arid landscapes.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
