Solanum rigescens var. nanum

Solanum rigescens var. nanum, commonly known as dwarf silverleaf nightshade, is a small, herbaceous perennial plant native to South America.

It features hairy, glandular stems and leaves that are often covered in a silvery pubescence, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, violet to blue flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are berries that mature to a dark purple color, containing several seeds.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique appearance and adaptability to various growing conditions.
