Scutellaria tutensis

Scutellaria tutensis, commonly known as the Tutu Mountains skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It grows in rocky and well-drained soils, often in open grasslands or among shrubs.

Scutellaria tutensis is of interest to botanists due to its unique adaptations to its harsh, mountainous habitat.
