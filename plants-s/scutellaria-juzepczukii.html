Scutellaria juzepczukii

Scutellaria juzepczukii, commonly known as the Juzepczuk's skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. The plant features square stems, opposite leaves, and small, two-lipped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in rocky or dry soils and is often found in alpine environments.

Scutellaria juzepczukii is noted for its ornamental value and potential traditional medicinal uses.
