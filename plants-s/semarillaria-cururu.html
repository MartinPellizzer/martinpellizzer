Semarillaria cururu

Semarillaria cururu, commonly known as the Cururu orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its terrestrial growth habit. The plant typically produces small, delicate flowers with intricate patterns, often in shades of white and green. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
