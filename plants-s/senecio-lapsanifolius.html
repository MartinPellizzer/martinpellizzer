Senecio lapsanifolius

Senecio lapsanifolius, commonly known as the yellow ragwort, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It produces bright yellow flowers that bloom from summer to autumn, attracting various pollinators.

Senecio lapsanifolius is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in dry, sunny environments.
