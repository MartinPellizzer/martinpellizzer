Smithiantha × hybrida

Smithiantha × hybrida, commonly known as the hybrid flame flower, is a vibrant evergreen shrub native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its showy blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Botanically, it is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Smithiantha coccinea and Smithiantha fruticosa, showcasing genetic diversity in its floral structure and coloration.
