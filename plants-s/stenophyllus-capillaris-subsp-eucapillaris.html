Stenophyllus capillaris subsp. eucapillaris

Stenophyllus capillaris subsp.

eucapillaris, commonly known as the narrow-leafed grass, is a perennial grass species native to certain regions of Africa. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

The plant has slender, cylindrical stems and narrow, grass-like leaves with a fine texture. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers.

This subspecies is notable for its adaptability to arid conditions and its role in stabilizing soil in degraded environments.
