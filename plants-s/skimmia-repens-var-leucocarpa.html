Skimmia repens var. leucocarpa

Skimmia repens var. leucocarpa, commonly known as the white-fruited evergreen shrub, is a compact, low-growing plant native to parts of Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that remain throughout the year, providing evergreen coverage in shaded areas. The plant produces small, white berries that are distinctive and add ornamental value in the winter months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Skimmia repens var. leucocarpa is often used in gardens for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in woodland conditions.
