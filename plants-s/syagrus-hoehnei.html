Syagrus hoehnei

Syagrus hoehnei, commonly known as the Hoehne's palm, is a species of palm native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its slender, upright trunk and large, feather-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils and saline conditions. It produces small, yellowish flowers followed by orange or red fruits that attract various bird species.

Syagrus hoehnei is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
