Stipa pennata var. pulcherrima

Stipa pennata var. pulcherrima, commonly known as the purple feather grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to South America.

It features slender, arching stems that bear soft, feathery plumes of purple to pinkish flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant. Its delicate appearance adds a graceful touch to landscapes, and it is drought-tolerant once established.

The variety is prized for its vibrant color and airy foliage that enhances garden aesthetics.
