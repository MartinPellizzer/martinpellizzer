Scheelea dubia

Scheelea dubia, commonly known as the Brazilian palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that develop into bright red berries. Scheelea dubia is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its elegant appearance.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with well-drained soil and ample sunlight.
