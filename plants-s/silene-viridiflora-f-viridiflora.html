Silene viridiflora f. viridiflora

Silene viridiflora f. viridiflora, commonly known as the green-flowered catchfly, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, arranged alternately along the stem. This plant is native to certain regions of Europe and is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.

It is noted for its delicate appearance and is of interest to botanists and gardeners alike.
