Silene argentina

Silene argentina, commonly known as the Argentine campion, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in temperate climates. Silene argentina has linear leaves and produces capsule fruits containing numerous small seeds.

It is often found in alpine or mountainous regions, where it contributes to the local biodiversity.
