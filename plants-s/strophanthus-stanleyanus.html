Strophanthus stanleyanus

Strophanthus stanleyanus, commonly known as the Stanley's strophanthus, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub that produces striking, showy yellow or orange flowers in clusters. The plant has woody stems and pinnate leaves, and it thrives in sunny, well-drained soils. Its flowers bloom during the dry season, making it a notable ornamental plant in tropical and subtropical regions.

The plant is also valued for its medicinal properties, though its use requires caution due to the presence of toxic compounds.
