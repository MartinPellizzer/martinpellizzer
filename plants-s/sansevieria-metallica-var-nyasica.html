Sansevieria metallica var. nyasica

Sansevieria metallica var. nyasica, commonly known as the Nyasica snake plant, is a succulent plant native to East Africa.

It features long, narrow, dark green leaves with striking white and yellow variegation along the edges. The leaves are sword-shaped and can grow up to 2 feet in length, making it an attractive addition to indoor spaces. It is highly tolerant of low light and requires minimal watering, making it an ideal houseplant.

This variety is also noted for its air-purifying qualities, contributing to improved indoor air quality.
