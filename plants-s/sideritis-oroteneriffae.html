Sideritis oroteneriffae

Sideritis oroteneriffae, commonly known as the Cape Verde ironwort, is a flowering plant native to the island of Santiago in the Cape Verde archipelago.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with gray-green, hairy leaves and small, pale blue or violet flowers. The plant thrives in arid, rocky environments and is adapted to the island's unique climate. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs.

Sideritis oroteneriffae is noted for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional remedies.
