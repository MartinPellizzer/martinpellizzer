Scirpus lushanensis

Scirpus lushanensis, commonly known as the Lushan bulrush, is a perennial wetland plant native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces small, clustered spikelets that are typically green to brown in color. Scirpus lushanensis thrives in marshy environments and is often found in wetlands and along the edges of ponds.

Its rhizomes help stabilize soil and contribute to the ecological health of its habitat.
