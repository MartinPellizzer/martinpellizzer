Stapelia guttata

Stapelia guttata, commonly known as the bird's nest plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features a distinctive, fleshy, leaf-like structure that resembles a bird's nest, hence its common name. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers with a strong, unpleasant odor, which attracts pollinators like flies. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
