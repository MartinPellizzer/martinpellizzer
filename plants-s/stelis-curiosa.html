Stelis curiosa

Stelis curiosa, commonly known as the curious stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually small and fragrant, often appearing in clusters during the rainy season.

Stelis curiosa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
