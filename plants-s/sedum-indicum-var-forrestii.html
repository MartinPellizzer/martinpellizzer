Sedum indicum var. forrestii

Sedum indicum var. forrestii, commonly known as the Forest's stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to China.

It is characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and small, clustered leaves that are typically green or reddish in color. The plant produces star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of care, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping and drought-tolerant landscaping.
