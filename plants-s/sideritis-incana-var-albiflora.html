Sideritis incana var. albiflora

Sideritis incana var. albiflora, commonly known as whiteflower hedgehog.

It is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region, characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, hairy leaves. The plant produces clusters of white to pale yellow flowers, which bloom in summer. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats.

Sideritis incana var. albiflora is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
