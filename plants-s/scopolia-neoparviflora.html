Scopolia neoparviflora

Scopolia neoparviflora, commonly known as the New Small-flowered Scopolia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its fragrant, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows in woodland or shaded areas, preferring moist, well-drained soils. Scopolia neoparviflora is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique floral structure.
