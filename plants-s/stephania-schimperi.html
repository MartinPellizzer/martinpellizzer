Stephania schimperi

Stephania schimperi, commonly known as Ethiopian arrowroot, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia and parts of Eritrea.

It belongs to the Menispermaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and palmate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by distinctive, elongated fruits. Its roots are the primary part used, containing a starchy substance similar to arrowroot.

Stephania schimperi is valued both for its medicinal properties and as a traditional food source in its native regions.
