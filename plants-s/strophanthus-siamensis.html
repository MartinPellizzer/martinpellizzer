Strophanthus siamensis

Strophanthus siamensis, commonly known as the Siam arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant is noted for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
