Stylidium stowardii

Stylidium stowardii, commonly known as the Stoward's stickless sunflower, is a small, herbaceous plant native to Western Australia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its compact growth habit and bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are linear and often covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and slender.
