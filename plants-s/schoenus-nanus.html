Schoenus nanus

Schoenus nanus, commonly known as the dwarf bulrush, is a small, herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes and the edges of lakes and rivers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are often white or pale pink. Its roots help stabilize soil and improve water quality in aquatic ecosystems.

Schoenus nanus is an important species for both ecological and ornamental purposes.
