Senecio kjellmanii

Senecio kjellmanii, commonly known as the ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine, powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces yellow flowers in late spring, adding a vibrant touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or arid landscapes.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
