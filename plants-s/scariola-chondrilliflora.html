Scariola chondrilliflora

Scariola chondrilliflora, commonly known as the rock scariola, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky, arid environments, showcasing adaptations to harsh conditions. Its small, yellow flowers are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences.
