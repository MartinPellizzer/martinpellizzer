Spiraea media f. oblongifolia

Spiraea media f. oblongifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf spirea, is a compact shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features oval, dark green leaves that are slightly larger than those of the typical Spiraea media. The plant produces clusters of white, fluffy flowers in early summer, adding a delicate ornamental touch to gardens. It thrives in partial to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its foliage and floral display, making it a popular choice for landscaping.
