Sesleria robusta

Sesleria robusta, commonly known as the robust sesleria, is a perennial grass-like plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green with a slight blue or silver sheen. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, pale blue or white flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Sesleria robusta is often used in rock gardens and as a low-maintenance ornamental plant.
