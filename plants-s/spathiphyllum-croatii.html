Spathiphyllum croatii

Spathiphyllum croatii, commonly known as the Croatian peace lily, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of Central America.

It is characterized by its dark green, waxy leaves that are elliptical in shape and often have a slight curl. The plant produces striking white spathes that enclose a cluster of small, greenish-yellow flowers. It is a member of the Araceae family, which is known for its unique floral structures.
