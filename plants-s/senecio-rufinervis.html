Senecio rufinervis

Senecio rufinervis, commonly known as the red-veined senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking, woolly leaves with prominent red veins. The plant produces yellow daisy-like flowers, which bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in arid and semi-arid regions.
