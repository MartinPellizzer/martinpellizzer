Sutera filicaulis

Sutera filicaulis, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silvery-green, velvety leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in the spring. Sutera filicaulis thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a garden setting.
