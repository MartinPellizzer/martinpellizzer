Santolina Chamaecyparissus F. Squarrosa

Squarrosa santolina, scientifically known as Santolina chamaecyparissus f. squarrosa, is a unique and hardy evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region. Its distinguishing feature is the fuzzy, silver-gray foliage that gives it a soft, cloud-like appearance. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it an excellent choice for rock gardens or containers. With its low maintenance requirements and ornamental value, it is a popular selection among gardeners seeking drought-tolerant plants. Its compact growth habit and ability to withstand harsh conditions make it a versatile addition to any landscape.
