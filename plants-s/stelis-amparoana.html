Stelis amparoana

Stelis amparoana, commonly known as the Amparoan orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly in countries like Colombia and Venezuela. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded forest environments. Its flowers are small and fragrant, often displaying a mix of white, yellow, and green hues.

Stelis amparoana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
