Sinosenecio winklerianus

Sinosenecio winklerianus, commonly known as the Winkler's sinosenecio, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in alpine or rocky habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its foliage is often grayish-green, with narrow, hairy leaves that help reduce water loss.

The flowers are small and yellow, arranged in compact clusters, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.
