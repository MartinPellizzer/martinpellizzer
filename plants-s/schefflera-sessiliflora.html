Schefflera sessiliflora

Schefflera sessiliflora, commonly known as the broadleaf umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like crown of large, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Schefflera sessiliflora is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
