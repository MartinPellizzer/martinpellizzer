Sedum annuum subsp. gussonei

Sedum annuum subsp.

gussonei, commonly known as the Gussone's stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Europe. It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink.

The plant is adapted to rocky and arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves. It blooms in late summer, producing numerous flowers that attract pollinators.

This subspecies is often used in rock gardens due to its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
