Stephania abyssinica var. pubescens

Stephania abyssinica var. pubescens, commonly known as the Abyssinian stephania, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Menispermaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in umbels. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The variety pubescens is distinguished by its hairy or pubescent stems and leaves.
