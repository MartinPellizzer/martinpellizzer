Stelis tuerckheimii

Stelis tuerckheimii, commonly known as the Tuerckheim's orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinct lip that attracts pollinators.

Stelis tuerckheimii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
