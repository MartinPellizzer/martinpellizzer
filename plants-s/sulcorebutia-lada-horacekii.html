Sulcorebutia lada-horacekii

Sulcorebutia lada-horacekii, commonly known as the Lada-Horacekii Pincushion Cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It features a smooth, pale green body with prominent, ribbed tubercles that give it a pincushion-like appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a delicate touch of color to its otherwise muted palette. It is well adapted to dry climates and requires minimal water, making it an ideal choice for xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular specimen among succulent enthusiasts.
