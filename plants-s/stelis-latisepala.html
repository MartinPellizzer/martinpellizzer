Stelis latisepala

Stelis latisepala, commonly known as the broad-sepal stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clustered growth habit and long, narrow pseudobulbs. The plant produces showy, white to pale yellow flowers with broad, fleshy sepals and petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Stelis latisepala is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
