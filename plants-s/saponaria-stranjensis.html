Saponaria stranjensis

Saponaria stranjensis, commonly known as the Stranjensk saponaria, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of pink to purple flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, wind-dispersed seeds. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Saponaria stranjensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
