Stelis megahybos

Stelis megahybos, commonly known as the giant spurred orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers with prominent spurred petals. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with intricate patterns that attract specific pollinators.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value.
