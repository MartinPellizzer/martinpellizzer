Sedum filicaule

Sedum filicaule, commonly known as the common stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features fleshy, upright stems and leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Sedum filicaule is drought-tolerant and requires minimal maintenance once established.
