Stelis hansenacea

Stelis hansenacea, commonly known as the Hansen's stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or branches, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are usually fleshy and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water retention.

Stelis hansenacea is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
