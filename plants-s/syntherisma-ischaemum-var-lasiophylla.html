Syntherisma ischaemum var. lasiophylla

Syntherisma ischaemum var. lasiophylla, commonly known as the hairy-leaved syntherisma, is a variant of the syntherisma ischaemum species.

It is a flowering plant native to specific regions in southern Africa. The plant is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage, which gives it its common name. It produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color.
