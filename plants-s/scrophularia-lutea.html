Scrophularia lutea

Scrophularia lutea, commonly known as yellow scrophularia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Scrophulariaceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in grasslands and woodland edges. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and branching.

Yellow scrophularia is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
