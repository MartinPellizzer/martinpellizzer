Senecio sanctae-martae

Senecio sanctae-martae, commonly known as the Saint Martha's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid to semi-arid environments. Its flowers are yellow, and it produces small, oval-shaped seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
