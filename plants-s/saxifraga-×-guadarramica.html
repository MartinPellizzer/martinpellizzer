Saxifraga × guadarramica

Saxifraga × guadarramica, commonly known as the Guadarramica saxifrage, is a hybrid plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The leaves are succulent, often with a bluish-green hue and a waxy texture, adapted to arid conditions. It produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink to red, which bloom in late spring to early summer.

This species thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in mountainous regions.
