Salta

Salta, commonly known as the saltbush, is a hardy shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its small, scale-like leaves that reduce water loss. The plant is often found in saline soils, showcasing its ability to tolerate high levels of salt. Its flowering stems bear small, greenish-white flowers that attract pollinators.

Salta is valued for its resilience and is used in ecological restoration projects.
