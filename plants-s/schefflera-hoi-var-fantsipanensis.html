Schefflera hoi var. fantsipanensis

Schefflera hoi var. fantsipanensis, commonly known as the Fantsipanensis umbrella plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded crown of large, glossy, dark green leaves that give it a lush, umbrella-like appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by bright red berries, attracting birds and other wildlife. It thrives in warm, humid conditions with bright, indirect light and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and landscape design.
