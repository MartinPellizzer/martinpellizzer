Schefflera heterobotrya

Schefflera heterobotrya, commonly known as the African shield fern, is a tropical plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its large, shield-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant has a woody stem and can grow as an upright shrub or small tree. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in moist forests.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular ornamental plant in tropical gardens.
