Scaphyglottis cuneata var. major

Scaphyglottis cuneata var. major, commonly known as the large-leaf scaphyglottis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and showy, orchid-like flowers that typically bloom in summer. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking floral display.
