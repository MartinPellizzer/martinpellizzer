Saussurea darwasica

Saussurea darwasica, commonly known as the Darwasica gromwell, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is native to the high-altitude regions of the western Himalayas, particularly in Pakistan and India. The plant is characterized by its tall, branched stems and clusters of small, pale yellow to white flowers. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered with a dense layer of fine hairs.

Saussurea darwasica is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
