Senecio alvarezensis

Senecio alvarezensis, commonly known as the Alvarensis groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species.
