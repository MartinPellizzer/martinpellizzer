Scutellaria arabica

Scutellaria arabica, commonly known as Arabian skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the Arabian Peninsula.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky or sandy soils.

Scutellaria arabica is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
