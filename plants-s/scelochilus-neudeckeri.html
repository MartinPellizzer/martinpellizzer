Scelochilus neudeckeri

Scelochilus neudeckeri, commonly known as the Neudecker's scelochilus, is a rare and lesser-known plant species within the orchid family.

It is characterized by its unique floral structure, featuring specialized lip and column morphology typical of many orchids. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in tropical or subtropical regions. Its flowers are usually small and delicate, with a range of colors from white to pale yellow.
