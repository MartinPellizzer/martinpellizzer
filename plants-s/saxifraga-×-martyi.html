Saxifraga × martyi

Saxifraga × martyi, commonly known as the hybrid saxifrage, is a flowering plant belonging to the Saxifragaceae family.

It is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of Europe, it thrives in rocky and well-drained soils. The plant is often found in alpine environments, where it adapts to cold and harsh conditions.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine plant collections.
