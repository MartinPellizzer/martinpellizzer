Silene reiseri

Silene reiseri, commonly known as the Reiser's campion, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family, characterized by its showy, pink to white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, preferring well-drained soils and full sunlight. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and often branched.

Silene reiseri is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
