Shrirangia concanensis

Shrirangia concanensis, commonly known as the Bombay lily, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a herbaceous plant that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are usually pink or red in color. It has linear, grass-like leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Shrirangia concanensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
