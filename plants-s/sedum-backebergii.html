Sedum backebergii

Sedum backebergii, commonly known as the Cape sedum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant is adapted to arid environments, with thick leaves that store water. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its hardy nature allows it to survive in harsh conditions with minimal water.
