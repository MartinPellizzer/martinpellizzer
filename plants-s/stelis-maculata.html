Stelis maculata

Stelis maculata, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, spotted leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often arranged in a raceme. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Stelis maculata is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
