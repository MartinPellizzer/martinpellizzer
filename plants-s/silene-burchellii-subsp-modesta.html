Silene burchellii subsp. modesta

Silene burchellii subsp.

modesta, commonly known as the modest silene, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in open, sandy or rocky habitats.

The plant produces small, pink to purple flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slightly hairy surface.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
