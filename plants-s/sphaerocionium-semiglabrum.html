Sphaerocionium semiglabrum

Sphaerocionium semiglabrum, commonly known as the roughleaf sphaerocion, is a small, herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal or semi-arid regions. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, rough hairs, giving it a distinctive texture. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually in green or brownish clusters, and it blooms during the warmer months.

Sphaerocionium semiglabrum is known for its resilience to drought and poor soil conditions.
