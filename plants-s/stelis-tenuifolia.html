Stelis tenuifolia

Stelis tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, narrow leaves and small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and yellow. This epiphytic plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for with proper light and moisture conditions.

Stelis tenuifolia is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability.
