Salicornia Corticosa Var. Typica

Salicornia corticosa var. typica is a unique halophyte plant that thrives in coastal environments. It is commonly found in salt marshes and tidal flats where it tolerates high salinity levels. This plant has a succulent, cylindrical stem that often turns red or pink in color. Its leaves are scale-like and help reduce water loss in arid conditions. It plays an important role in stabilizing coastal soils and supporting local ecosystems.
