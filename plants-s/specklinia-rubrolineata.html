Specklinia rubrolineata

Specklinia rubrolineata, commonly known as the red-lined orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and striking, lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive red or purplish margin. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often arranged in clusters along the stems. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Specklinia rubrolineata is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
