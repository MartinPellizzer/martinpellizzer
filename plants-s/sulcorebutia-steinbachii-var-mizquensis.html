Sulcorebutia steinbachii var. mizquensis

Sulcorebutia steinbachii var. mizquensis, commonly known as the Mizquense Pincushion Cactus, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to Mexico.

It features a spherical or oblate stem that is typically covered in dense, white to pale green spines. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
