Salacia pallens

Salacia pallens, commonly known as the silverleaf salacia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Salaceae and is characterized by its small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence, giving it its common name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Salacia pallens is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
