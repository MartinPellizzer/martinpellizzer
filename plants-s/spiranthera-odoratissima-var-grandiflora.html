Spiranthera odoratissima var. grandiflora

Spiranthera odoratissima var. grandiflora, commonly known as the grandiflora lily, is a striking tropical plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and deep red, often with a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant features strap-like, lance-shaped leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is typically grown as a ornamental plant in gardens and greenhouses.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique floral structure and its role in horticultural diversity.
