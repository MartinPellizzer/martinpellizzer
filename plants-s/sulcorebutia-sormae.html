Sulcorebutia sormae

Sulcorebutia sormae, commonly known as the Sormae Crassula, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of fleshy, cylindrical leaves that are typically bluish-green with a waxy coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
