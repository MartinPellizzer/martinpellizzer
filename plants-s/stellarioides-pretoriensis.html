Stellarioides pretoriensis

Stellarioides pretoriensis, commonly known as the Pretoria starwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features a mat-forming habit with numerous slender, branching stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The leaves are opposite, linear, and slightly hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer.

This plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or disturbed habitats.
