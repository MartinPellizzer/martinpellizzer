Schoenus insolitus

Schoenus insolitus, commonly known as the slender bulrush, is a perennial grass-like plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are typically green or reddish in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a slight sheen.

Schoenus insolitus is often found in wetland habitats and is adapted to waterlogged soils.
