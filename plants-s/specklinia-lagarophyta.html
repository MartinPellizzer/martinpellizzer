Specklinia lagarophyta

Specklinia lagarophyta, commonly known as the silver-veined orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves with distinctive silvery veins, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Specklinia lagarophyta is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
