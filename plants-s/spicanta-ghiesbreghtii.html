Spicanta ghiesbreghtii

Spicanta ghiesbreghtii, commonly known as the Ghiesbreght's spiky plant, is a unique species native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, spiky stems that can grow up to two meters in height. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in late summer, adding a delicate contrast to its rugged appearance. Its leaves are narrow and covered in a fine layer of white hairs, which help reduce water loss in its harsh environment.

Spicanta ghiesbreghtii is often found growing in rocky outcrops and sandy soils, showcasing its adaptability to extreme conditions.
