Stipa eriocaulis subsp. austriaca

Stipa eriocaulis subsp.

austriaca, commonly known as Austrian feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to Central Europe. It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and fine, silver-green foliage.

The plant produces slender, spike-like inflorescences that are adorned with tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping and ecological restoration projects.
