Stellaria tschachanensis

Stellaria tschachanensis, commonly known as the Tschachan starwort, is a small flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs, while its stems are prostrate or ascending.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
