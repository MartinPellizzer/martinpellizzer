Stanhopea grossii

Stanhopea grossii, commonly known as the large-lobed orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a distinctive yellow and purple coloration. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Stanhopea grossii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
