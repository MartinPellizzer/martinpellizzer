Schismatoglottis latevaginata

Schismatoglottis latevaginata, commonly known as the broad sheath orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and compact, evergreen foliage. The plant produces striking inflorescences with showy, spurred flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in specialty gardens.
