Solanum melongena

Solanum melongena, commonly known as the eggplant, is a flowering plant in the nightshade family (Solanaceae).

It is native to India and is widely cultivated for its edible, fleshy fruits. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with hairy stems and lobed leaves. Its flowers are usually yellow or white, and the fruit can vary in color from purple to white or green depending on the variety.

The botanical features of Solanum melongena make it a versatile and economically important crop in many regions.
