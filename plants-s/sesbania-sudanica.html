Sesbania sudanica

Sesbania sudanica, commonly known as Sudan sesban, is a fast-growing leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers, which bloom in clusters. The plant is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it valuable in soil enrichment. It thrives in warm climates and is often used for forage, green manure, and windbreaks.

Sesbania sudanica is also recognized for its role in agroforestry and ecological restoration projects.
