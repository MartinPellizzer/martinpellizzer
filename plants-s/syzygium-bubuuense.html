Syzygium bubuuense

Syzygium bubuuense, commonly known as the Bubuense apple.

It is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae, native to the tropical rainforests of Queensland, Australia. The plant is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and lance-shaped leaves that are glossy on the upper surface. Its flowers are small, white, and fragrant, typically appearing in clusters.

The fruit is a round, red or yellow berry that contains a single seed, and it is often consumed by local wildlife.
