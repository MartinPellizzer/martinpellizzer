Sterculia nesogenes

Sterculia nesogenes, commonly known as the red-flowered sterculia, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters. The species is valued for its ornamental value and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
