Selago natalensis

Selago natalensis, commonly known as Natal grass or Natal silver grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-gray foliage and tufted growth habit, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has fibrous roots and produces small, inconspicuous flowers in late summer.

Selago natalensis is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal and drought tolerance.
