Smilax china f. gigantifolia

Smilax china f. gigantifolia, commonly known as Chinese smilax, is a variant of the smilax genus native to China.

It is a vigorous, climbing shrub characterized by its stout, thorny stems and large, palmately lobed leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into bright red, fleshy berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in both horticulture and ecological studies.
