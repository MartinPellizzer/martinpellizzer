Stelis latimarginata

Stelis latimarginata, commonly known as the broad-margin orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its upright, often branching stems. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive white to pale yellow coloration. Its leaves are typically broad and arranged in a rosette pattern, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Stelis latimarginata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in tropical gardens.
