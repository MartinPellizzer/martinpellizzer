Stachys willemsei

Stachys willemsei, commonly known as the Willemse's hedge nettle, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped with a hairy texture, and it produces small, pale purple flowers in whorled clusters.

Stachys willemsei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its foliage and modest blooms.
