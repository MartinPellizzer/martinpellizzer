Satureja canescens

Satureja canescens, commonly known as woolly thyme, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, gray-green leaves that are covered in fine, fuzzy hairs. The plant produces small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or dry habitats.

Satureja canescens is valued for its use in culinary and medicinal applications due to its strong, thyme-like fragrance.
