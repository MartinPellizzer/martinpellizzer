Silene burchellii subsp. burchellii

Silene burchellii subsp.

burchellii, commonly known as the Burchell's campion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and showy pink to red flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant typically grows in open grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are alternate, linear to lanceolate, and often have a slightly hairy texture. Silene burchellii subsp.
