Spiraea rosthornii var. rosthornii

Spiraea rosthornii var. rosthornii, commonly known as the Rosthorn spiraea, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features slender, arching stems that produce clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers in early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or alpine settings.

Its ornamental value comes from its delicate blooms and compact growth habit.
