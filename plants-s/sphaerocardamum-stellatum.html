Sphaerocardamum stellatum

Sphaerocardamum stellatum, commonly known as the star-shaped cardamum, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features distinctive, star-like flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. The plant has small, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Due to its unique floral structure, it is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes in botanical gardens.
