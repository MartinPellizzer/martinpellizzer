Skimmia japonica var. fragrans

Skimmia japonica var. fragrans, commonly known as the fragrant skimmia, is a flowering evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It features glossy, dark green leaves and produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink. The plant is noted for its subtle, spicy fragrance, which is most noticeable when the flowers are in bloom. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for shaded garden areas.

Skimmia japonica var. fragrans is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
