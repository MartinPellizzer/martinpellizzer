Sinocrassula luteorubra

Sinocrassula luteorubra, commonly known as the red-leaved stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to China.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically red or pink in color, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
