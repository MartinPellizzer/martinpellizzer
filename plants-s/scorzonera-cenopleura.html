Scorzonera cenopleura

Scorzonera cenopleura, commonly known as the alpine scorzonera, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy, upright stems and basal leaves that are often lobed or divided. Its root system is fibrous and may have medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
