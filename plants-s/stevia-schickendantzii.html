Stevia schickendantzii

Stevia schickendantzii, commonly known as the Amazonian stevia, is a species native to the rainforests of South America.

It is a perennial shrub that belongs to the sunflower family, Asteraceae. The plant is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and small yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves contain high concentrations of steviol glycosides, which are natural sweeteners.

Due to its unique chemical composition, it is valued for its intense sweetness and potential health benefits.
