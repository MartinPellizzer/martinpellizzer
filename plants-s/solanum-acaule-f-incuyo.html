Solanum acaule f. incuyo

Solanum acaule f. incuyo, commonly known as the orange-flowered nightshade, is a unique variant of the Solanum acaule species.

It is a shrub native to South America, particularly found in regions of Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking orange-yellow flowers that bloom at night. Its foliage consists of dark green, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm climates for its vibrant floral display.
