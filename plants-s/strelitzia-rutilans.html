Strelitzia rutilans

Strelitzia rutilans, commonly known as the orange bird of paradise, is a striking tropical plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange bracts that resemble a bird in flight, surrounding a cluster of yellowish-green flowers. The plant has narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Strelitzia rutilans is often cultivated as a houseplant or in gardens due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
