Schaueria hirta

Schaueria hirta, commonly known as the hairy schaueria, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, branching stems covered in fine, hairy texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Schaueria hirta thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for dry, rocky habitats.

It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
