Statice chazaliei

Statice chazaliei, commonly known as the pink statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its dense, spike-like inflorescences. The plant produces small, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red, often used in floral arrangements. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Statice chazaliei is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.
