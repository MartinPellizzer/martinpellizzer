Stelis amphigena

Stelis amphigena, commonly known as the mountain orchid, is a species of orchid native to the high-altitude regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows on rock surfaces or tree trunks, showcasing its epiphytic nature. Its leaves are narrow and fleshy, adapted to store water in its arid habitat.

Stelis amphigena is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in specialized horticultural settings.
