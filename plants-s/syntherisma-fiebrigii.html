Syntherisma fiebrigii

Syntherisma fiebrigii, commonly known as the fiery syntheris, is a rare flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange blossoms, which bloom during the cooler months. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with thick, waxy leaves that help retain moisture in arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.

Due to its limited distribution and unique appearance, it is a subject of interest in botanical research and conservation efforts.
