Salisburyodendron australis

Salisburyodendron australis, commonly known as the Australian ironwood, is a hardy evergreen tree native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and rugged, fibrous bark that provides protection against fire and pests. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing elliptical seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, coastal or inland regions.

Due to its resilience, it is valued for both ecological and ornamental purposes.
