Stellara lathraeoides

Stellara lathraeoides, commonly known as the silverleaf aster, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center, blooming in late summer to early fall. The plant has finely divided, silver-gray leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Stellara lathraeoides is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
