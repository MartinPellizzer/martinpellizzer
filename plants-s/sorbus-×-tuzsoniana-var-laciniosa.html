Sorbus × tuzsoniana var. laciniosa

Sorbus × tuzsoniana var. laciniosa, commonly known as the laciniate rowan, is a hybrid species within the genus Sorbus.

It is characterized by its distinctive, finely lobed leaves that give it a delicate, lace-like appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which are followed by bright red berries in the autumn. It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and thrives in temperate climates.

This ornamental shrub is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its seasonal color changes.
