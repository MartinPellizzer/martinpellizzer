Stelis mendietae

Stelis mendietae, commonly known as the Mendietta's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, delicate flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are usually arranged in a rosette and are covered with a dense layer of fine hairs.

Stelis mendietae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in specialized orchid collections.
