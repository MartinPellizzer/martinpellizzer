Salacia ledermannii

Salacia ledermannii, commonly known as the Ledermann's salacia, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Salaceae and is characterized by its woody structure and vibrant, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical rainforests, thriving in humid and shaded environments. Its leaves are usually elliptical with a smooth surface, and the flowers are often yellow or orange in color.

Salacia ledermannii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
