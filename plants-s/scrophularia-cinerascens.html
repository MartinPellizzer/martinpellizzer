Scrophularia cinerascens

Scrophularia cinerascens, commonly known as cinerascens scrophularia, is a flowering plant species native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or dry soils, often in open woodlands or along stream banks. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple in color, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Scrophularia cinerascens is part of the Scrophulariaceae family, known for its ornamental and sometimes medicinal properties.
