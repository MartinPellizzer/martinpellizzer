Setaria pycnocoma

Setaria pycnocoma, commonly known as bush bluestem, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and narrow, bluish-green leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It is well adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in prairie and coastal dune environments.

Setaria pycnocoma plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
