Stillingia bahiensis

Stillingia bahiensis, commonly known as Brazilian stellaria, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the family Stellariaceae and is characterized by its small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are opposite and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Stillingia bahiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
