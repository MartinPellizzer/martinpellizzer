Streptocarpus brevipilosus

Streptocarpus brevipilosus, commonly known as the short-hairy streptocarpus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, tubular flowers. The plant features hairy leaves and produces showy, often purple or pink flowers that attract pollinators. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Streptocarpus brevipilosus is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
