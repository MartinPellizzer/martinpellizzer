Senecio tenuicaulis

Senecio tenuicaulis, commonly known as the slender-stemmed groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and narrow, upright stems. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are linear and often covered in fine hairs, and it produces small yellow flowers in clusters.

Senecio tenuicaulis is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and shrublands.
