Senecio streptanthifolius var. platylobus

Senecio streptanthifolius var. platylobus, commonly known as the flatleaf senecio, is a perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage consists of narrow, gray-green leaves with a distinctive flat, spoon-shaped appearance.

It produces small yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, making it a striking addition to rock gardens.
