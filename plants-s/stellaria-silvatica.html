Stellaria silvatica

Stellaria silvatica, commonly known as wood star, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like carnations and chickweed. The plant features slender, branching stems and small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its delicate appearance.

Stellaria silvatica thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
