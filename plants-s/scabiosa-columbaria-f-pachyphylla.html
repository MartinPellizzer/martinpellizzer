Scabiosa columbaria f. pachyphylla

Scabiosa columbaria f. pachyphylla, commonly known as the thick-leaved scabious, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to southern Europe.

It features small, pink or white, five-petaled flowers arranged in dense, rounded clusters. The leaves are thick, leathery, and often covered in a fine, fuzzy coating, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
