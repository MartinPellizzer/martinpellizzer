Savia clusiifolia var. genuina

Savia clusiifolia var. genuina, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a striking ornamental plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow-orange foliage, which provides a striking contrast to its green stems and branches. The plant belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in tropical and subtropical climates. Its leaves are typically broad and lance-shaped, with a glossy texture that enhances its visual appeal.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a landscape plant.
