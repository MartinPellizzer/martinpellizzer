Swertia volkensii

Swertia volkensii, commonly known as the African gentian, is a perennial herb native to East Africa.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and branching.

Swertia volkensii is valued for its ornamental beauty and has traditional medicinal uses in some regions.
