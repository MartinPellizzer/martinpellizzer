Sorbus caloneura var. kwangtungensis

Sorbus caloneura var. kwangtungensis, commonly known as the Guangdong rowan, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, narrow leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. The fruit is a red, berry-like pome that matures in autumn, providing food for wildlife. It typically grows in moist, temperate climates and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.

This variant is often found in forested areas and is relatively rare in cultivation.
