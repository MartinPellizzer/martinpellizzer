Sophronitis kettieana

Sophronitis kettieana, commonly known as the Kettie's orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in late spring or early summer. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single or few branching inflorescence. Its flowers are usually a mix of white, yellow, and green, with a distinctive lip that is often marked with contrasting patterns.

Sophronitis kettieana is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is appreciated for its ornamental value and botanical uniqueness.
