Sphaeranthus kalahariensis

Sphaeranthus kalahariensis, commonly known as the Kalahari daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, annual herb that typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, which helps reduce water loss in dry conditions.

The Kalahari daisy is well adapted to survive in harsh environments with minimal water availability.
