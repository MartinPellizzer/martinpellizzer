Sedum nanifolium

Sedum nanifolium, commonly known as the miniature stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its small, fleshy, and often blue-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces delicate, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping and drought-tolerant landscaping.
