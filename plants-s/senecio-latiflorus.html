Senecio latiflorus

Senecio latiflorus, commonly known as the broadflower Senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically features showy yellow flowers surrounded by white ray florets, making it visually striking. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Senecio latiflorus is also noted for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
