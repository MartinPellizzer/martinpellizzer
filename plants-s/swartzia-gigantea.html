Swartzia gigantea

Swartzia gigantea, commonly known as the giant swartzia, is a tropical tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, compound leaves and showy, clustered flowers that range from white to pink. The plant belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and it has a woody structure with a straight trunk. Swartzia gigantea is often found in humid lowland forests and can grow up to 30 meters tall.
