Solidago flexicaulis

Solidago flexicaulis, commonly known as the flexible goldenrod, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that often bend or flex, giving it its common name. The plant produces bright yellow inflorescences composed of numerous small, clustered flowers. It thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks.

Solidago flexicaulis plays an important role in supporting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
