Sasa kurilensis f. tricolor

Sasa kurilensis f. tricolor, commonly known as the tricolor sasa, is a variant of the sasa plant native to the islands of the Kuril chain.

It is a clump-forming perennial bamboo that grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its striking tricolor foliage, featuring combinations of green, yellow, and white on its leaves. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense thickets.

This ornamental plant is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and landscapes.
