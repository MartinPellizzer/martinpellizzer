Schefflera Sphaerocoma

Schefflera sphaerocoma is a unique and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of New Guinea. It is known for its compact, rounded shape and its ability to thrive in a variety of indoor environments. This plant features glossy, dark green leaves that give it a lush, tropical appearance. Its slow growth rate makes it ideal for small spaces and container gardening. Schefflera sphaerocoma is a popular choice for those seeking an eye-catching, low-maintenance houseplant.
