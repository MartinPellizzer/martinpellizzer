Scrophularia haematantha

Scrophularia haematantha, commonly known as the bloodroot scrophularia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and open woodlands. The plant features erect stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are tubular and usually pink or purple, arranged in clusters at the top of the stem.

Scrophularia haematantha is part of the Scrophulariaceae family, known for its medicinal and ornamental value.
