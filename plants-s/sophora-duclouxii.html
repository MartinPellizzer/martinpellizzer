Sophora duclouxii

Sophora duclouxii, commonly known as the Ducloux's Sophora, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, woody pods that contain lens-shaped seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in dry forests and rocky areas.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
