Senecio arnottii

Senecio arnottii, commonly known as Arnott's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of narrow, gray-green leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
