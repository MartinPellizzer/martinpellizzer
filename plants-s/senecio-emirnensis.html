Senecio emirnensis

Senecio emirnensis, commonly known as the Emirnensis groundsel, is a flowering plant native to Iran.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing adaptations to dry conditions. It features narrow, linear leaves and produces small yellow flowers arranged in clusters.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species within its region.
