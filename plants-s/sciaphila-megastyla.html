Sciaphila megastyla

Sciaphila megastyla, commonly known as the giant shade orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It thrives in shaded, humid environments, typically growing on trees or rocks in forest canopies. The plant features broad, dark green leaves and produces large, showy flowers with a distinctive yellow and green coloration. Its inflorescence is long and racemose, bearing numerous fragrant blooms.

Sciaphila megastyla is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in the ecological dynamics of its native habitats.
