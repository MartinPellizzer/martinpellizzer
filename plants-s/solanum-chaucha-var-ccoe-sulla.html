Solanum chaucha var. ccoe-sulla

Solanum chaucha var. ccoe-sulla, commonly known as Ccoe-sulla, is a flowering plant belonging to the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is native to parts of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru, and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by oval, yellowish fruits. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a hairy texture.

It is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its unique appearance and adaptability to various soil conditions.
