Spiesia chiliophylla

Spiesia chiliophylla, commonly known as the red-leaved spurge, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking red to purple foliage, which contrasts with its green stems. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in cyathia, typical of the spurge family. It is native to southern Africa and thrives in well-drained soils.

Spiesia chiliophylla is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant leaf color and hardy nature.
