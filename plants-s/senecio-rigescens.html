Senecio rigescens

Senecio rigescens, commonly known as the rigid senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. It produces small yellow flowers that bloom in spring, and its stems are often stiff and upright.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens.
