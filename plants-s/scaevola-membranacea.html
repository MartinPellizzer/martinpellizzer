Scaevola membranacea

Scaevola membranacea, commonly known as the velvetleaf scaevola, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its small, showy flowers that are typically pink or white, arranged in clusters. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal regions.

Scaevola membranacea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly used in gardens and landscaping.
