Saxifraga sinomontana

Saxifraga sinomontana, commonly known as the Chinese mountain saxifrage, is a flowering plant native to high-altitude regions of China.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It produces small, white or pinkish flowers during the summer months.

Saxifraga sinomontana is adapted to cold, rocky environments and is often found in alpine meadows and mountainous areas.
