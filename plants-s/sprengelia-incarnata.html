Sprengelia incarnata

Sprengelia incarnata, commonly known as the pink orchid, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking pink or purple flowers. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its leaves are elliptical and glossy, while its roots are fibrous and adapted to its tropical habitat.

Sprengelia incarnata thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
