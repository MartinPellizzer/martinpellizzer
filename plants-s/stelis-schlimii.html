Stelis schlimii

Stelis schlimii, commonly known as the Schlim's spider orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a short, erect stem with a few clustered, linear leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with a distinctive spurred lip that aids in pollination.

Stelis schlimii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
