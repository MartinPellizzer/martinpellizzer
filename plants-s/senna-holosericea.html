Senna holosericea

Senna holosericea, commonly known as the golden senna, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters tall, characterized by its bipinnate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces long, slender pods that contain seeds covered in a silky, hairy coating. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

Senna holosericea is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some regions.
