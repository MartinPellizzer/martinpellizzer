Sonerila glabricaulis

Sonerila glabricaulis, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed sonerila, is a member of the Melastomataceae family.

It is a shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of India and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and opposite, elliptical leaves. It produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white, followed by capsule fruits.

Sonerila glabricaulis is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forest understories.
