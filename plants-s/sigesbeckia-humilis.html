Sigesbeckia humilis

Sigesbeckia humilis, commonly known as the lesser sunflower, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to Asia.

It features bright yellow ray flowers surrounded by numerous brown disk flowers, giving it a sunflower-like appearance. The plant has hairy, oval-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along its slender stem. It thrives in moist, sunny environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Sigesbeckia humilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
