Santia plumosa

Santia plumosa, commonly known as the plumose santia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery foliage and showy, pale yellow to white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and prefers well-drained, sandy soils. It is often found in rocky outcrops and is adapted to full sun exposure.

Santia plumosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for in a garden setting.
