Shuteria involucrata var. glabrata

Shuteria involucrata var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth shuteria, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features upright, branched stems covered in smooth, glabrous leaves. The plant produces small, white to pale blue flowers arranged in dense, terminal clusters. Its foliage is typically narrow and linear, with a grass-like appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
