Sicyos gracillimus

Sicyos gracillimus, commonly known as the slender gourd, is a climbing vine native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes other gourds and cucurbits. The plant produces elongated, cylindrical fruits that can grow up to 1 meter in length. Its leaves are palmately lobed and covered in fine hairs, while the stems are hairy and trailing.

Sicyos gracillimus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique fruit shape.
