Salvia granatensis

Salvia granatensis, commonly known as the granate sage, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright stems covered in dense, velvety gray-green foliage. The plant produces striking spikes of violet-blue flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its aromatic leaves and showy flowers make it both ornamental and useful in traditional herbal practices.
