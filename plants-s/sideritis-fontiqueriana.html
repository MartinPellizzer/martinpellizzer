Sideritis fontiqueriana

Sideritis fontiqueriana, commonly known as the Fontiquerian ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its slender stems and small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. Its leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy surface.

Sideritis fontiqueriana is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments.
