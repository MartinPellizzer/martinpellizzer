Senecio oerstedianus

Senecio oerstedianus, commonly known as the Oersted's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It features narrow, linear leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

Senecio oerstedianus is often used in rock gardens due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
