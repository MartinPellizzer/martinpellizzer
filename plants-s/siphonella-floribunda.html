Siphonella floribunda

Siphonella floribunda, commonly known as the golden thread plant, is a flowering species native to tropical regions.

It features slender, upright stems covered in soft, fuzzy hairs. Its small, clustered flowers are typically yellow or orange, giving it a vibrant appearance. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

It is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive foliage and prolonged blooming period.
