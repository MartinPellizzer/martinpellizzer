Sticherus boliviensis

It belongs to the family Peltasporaceae and is characterized by its small, leafy structure with a distinctive thallus. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rock crevices or forest floors. It has a simple morphology with a prostrate or ascending growth habit.
