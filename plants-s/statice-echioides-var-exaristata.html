Statice echioides var. exaristata

Statice echioides var. exaristata, commonly known as the spiked statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, and it produces numerous seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to withstand salt spray and coastal conditions.
