Spathiphyllum floribundum var. cuneatum

Spathiphyllum floribundum var. cuneatum, commonly known as the velvet leaf peace lily, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, dark green, velvety leaves that are lanceolate in shape. The plant produces striking white spathes that enclose small, greenish-white flowers, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in low-light conditions and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and air-purifying properties.
