Stellaria Glauca Var. Persica

Stellaria glauca var. persica is a lesser-known member of the Caryophyllaceae family known for its delicate blue flowers and sprawling growth habit. Native to parts of Europe and Asia, this variant of Stellaria glauca thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides. Its small, star-shaped petals and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a hardy, ornamental plant. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated compared to more common garden species. This plant offers both ecological value and aesthetic appeal, making it a worthy subject for further botanical study and appreciation.
