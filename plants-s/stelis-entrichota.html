Stelis entrichota

Stelis entrichota, commonly known as the richly veined orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often fragrant flowers with intricate veining patterns. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, showcasing its epiphytic nature. Its leaves are usually dense and dark green, providing a lush backdrop to its colorful blooms.

Stelis entrichota is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
