Syzygium cadetii

Syzygium cadetii, commonly known as the Kadetii apple or Kadetii fruit, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically red or yellow when mature, with a sweet and tangy flavor. Syzygium cadetii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its cultivation requires a warm, humid climate with well-drained soil to thrive.
