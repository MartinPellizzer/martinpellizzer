Solidago tenuissima

Solidago tenuissima, commonly known as narrowleaf goldenrod, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as meadows and sandy soils. It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn.

Its slender leaves and compact growth habit make it a distinctive and ecologically important species.
