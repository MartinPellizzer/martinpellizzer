Stachys elliptica var. aristata

Stachys elliptica var. aristata, commonly known as the upright hedge nettle, is a perennial herb native to the northeastern United States.

It features upright, branched stems that are covered in stiff, glandular hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in whorled clusters along the stems, typically blooming from June to August. Its leaves are elliptic to ovate, with a slightly hairy surface and a pointed tip.

This species is often found in moist, shaded woodlands and thrives in partial to full shade.
