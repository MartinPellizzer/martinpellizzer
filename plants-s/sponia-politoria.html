Sponia politoria

Sponia politoria, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. Sponia politoria thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in dry, rocky habitats.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
