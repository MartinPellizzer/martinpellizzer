Scoparia praedensa

Scoparia praedensa, commonly known as the common thistle.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple or pink flowers that bloom in summer. It grows in a variety of habitats, including meadows, wastelands, and disturbed soils.

Scoparia praedensa is often found in temperate regions and is known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
