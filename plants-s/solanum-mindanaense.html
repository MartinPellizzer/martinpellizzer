Solanum mindanaense

Solanum mindanaense, commonly known as Mindanao nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and peppers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching up to 3 meters in height. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a rough texture, while its flowers are usually white or pale blue.

The fruit is a capsule that contains numerous small, yellowish seeds.
