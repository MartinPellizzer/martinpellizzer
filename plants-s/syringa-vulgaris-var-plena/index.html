Syringa Vulgaris Var. Plena

Hydrangea plena, scientifically known as Syringa vulgaris var. plena, is a striking ornamental shrub prized for its vibrant, showy blooms. This variety of lilac is celebrated for its dense clusters of flowers that range from white to deep pink, depending on soil pH. Native to eastern Asia, it has become a popular choice in gardens across temperate regions worldwide. Its ability to thrive in various conditions makes it a versatile addition to both landscapes and containers. With its lush foliage and long-lasting floral display, Hydrangea plena remains a favorite among gardeners and floral enthusiasts alike.
