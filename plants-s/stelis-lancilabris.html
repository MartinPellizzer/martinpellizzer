Stelis lancilabris

Stelis lancilabris, commonly known as the slender-lipped stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically found growing on trees in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has long, slender stems and produces small, delicate flowers with a distinctive labellum. Its leaves are usually arranged in a rosette and are often covered in a fine, velvety texture.

Stelis lancilabris thrives in humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
