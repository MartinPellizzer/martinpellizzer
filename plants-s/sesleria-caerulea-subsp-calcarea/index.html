Sesleria Caerulea Subsp. Calcarea

Blue sesleria, scientifically known as Sesleria caerulea subsp. calcarea, is a striking ornamental grass native to southern Europe. This plant is prized for its vibrant blue-green foliage that adds a unique color palette to gardens and landscapes. It thrives in well-drained soils and full to partial sunlight, making it adaptable to a variety of garden settings. Its delicate, grass-like appearance and elegant growth habit make it a popular choice among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts. With its low maintenance requirements and aesthetic appeal, Blue sesleria is an excellent addition to both wildflower meadows and cultivated gardens.
