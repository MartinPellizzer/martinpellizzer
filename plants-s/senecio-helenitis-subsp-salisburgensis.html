Senecio helenitis subsp. salisburgensis

Senecio helenitis subsp.

salisburgensis, commonly known as the Salzburg helene's groundsel, is a member of the Asteraceae family. It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the alpine regions of Central Europe.

The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes. It features narrow, linear leaves and produces small yellow flowers arranged in composite heads.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to cold, harsh environments.
