Senecio moldenkei

Senecio moldenkei, commonly known as the Blue Euphorbia, is a striking succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often tinged with purple, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
