Spiraea hypericifolia var. besseriana

Spiraea hypericifolia var. besseriana, commonly known as the dwarf spiraea, is a compact shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant has narrow, dark green leaves that remain attractive throughout the growing season. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a border plant due to its compact size and hardy nature.

This variety is notable for its ability to thrive in poor soils and its resistance to common pests and diseases.
