Solidago yakusimensis

Solidago yakusimensis, commonly known as Yakushima goldenrod, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as mountain forests. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, daisy-like flowers.

Solidago yakusimensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
