Schismatoglottis calyptrata var. flavidomaculata

Schismatoglottis calyptrata var. flavidomaculata, commonly known as the golden-spotted schismatoglottis, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves with a waxy texture and a distinctive yellowish spot on the underside. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a spathe, typical of the Araceae family.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and unique appearance.
