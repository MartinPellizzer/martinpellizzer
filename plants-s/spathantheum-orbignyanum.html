Spathantheum orbignyanum

Spathantheum orbignyanum, commonly known as the orbignyanum orchid, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually brightly colored, with a unique spatulate shape that gives the genus its name.

Spathantheum orbignyanum requires careful cultivation due to its specific humidity and light requirements.
