Stelis parviflora

Stelis parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a short, stocky stem with clustered, small greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are usually fleshy and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Stelis parviflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in terrariums or as a houseplant.
