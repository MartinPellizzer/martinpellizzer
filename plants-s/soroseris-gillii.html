Soroseris gillii

Soroseris gillii, commonly known as the Gill's sorrel, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright, branched stems and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is known for its edible leaves, which have a tangy, sour flavor.
