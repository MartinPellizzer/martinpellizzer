Satureja teneriffae

Satureja teneriffae, commonly known as Canary Island thyme, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant leaves and clusters of pink or purple flowers. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Satureja teneriffae is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
