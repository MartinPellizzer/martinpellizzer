Scrophularia pseudoauriculata

Scrophularia pseudoauriculata, commonly known as the false earleaf scrophularia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or dry soils. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae, which includes many species with similar floral structures.
