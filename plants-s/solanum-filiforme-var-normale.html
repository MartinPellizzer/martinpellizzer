Solanum filiforme var. normale

Solanum filiforme var. normale, commonly known as the slender nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually violet or purple in color. Its fruit is a small, berry-like structure that turns red when mature.

The species is characterized by its thin, trailing stems and is often used in ornamental horticulture.
