Syagrus capitata

Syagrus capitata, commonly known as the royal palm, is a tropical plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a tall, slender palm with a slender trunk and a crown of long, pinnate leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that develop into clustered, orange-red fruits. Syagrus capitata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in landscaping and as a decorative plant.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
