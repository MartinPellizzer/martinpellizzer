Stelis elatissima

Stelis elatissima, commonly known as the skyrocket orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers along its stem, which bloom in the summer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Stelis elatissima is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
