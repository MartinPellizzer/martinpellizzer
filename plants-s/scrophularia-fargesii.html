Scrophularia fargesii

Scrophularia fargesii, commonly known as the Yunnan figwort, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of western China.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae and is characterized by its erect stems and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, often arranged in clustered inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas at high elevations.

Scrophularia fargesii is valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
