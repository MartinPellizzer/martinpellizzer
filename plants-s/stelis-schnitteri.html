Stelis schnitteri

Stelis schnitteri, commonly known as the Schneiter's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Mexico.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. The plant features elongated, narrow leaves and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pale pink. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate floral structure.

Stelis schnitteri is often found in cloud forests, where it thrives in the cool, moist conditions.
