Swertia mekongensis

Swertia mekongensis, commonly known as the Mekong swertia, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its slender stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, blue or violet flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth.

Its medicinal properties have been studied for their potential in traditional herbal remedies.
