Scutellaria tanakae

Scutellaria tanakae, commonly known as Tanaka's skullcap, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features square, hairy stems and opposite leaves with a serrated margin. Its small, tubular blue or violet flowers are arranged in whorls along the stem.

Scutellaria tanakae is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions.
