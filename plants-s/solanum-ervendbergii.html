Solanum ervendbergii

Solanum ervendbergii, commonly known as the Ervendberg nightshade, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants such as tomatoes and potatoes. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are ovate and often hairy, while its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences.

The fruit is a yellowish berry that matures in autumn, containing several seeds.
