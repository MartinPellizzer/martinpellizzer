Sideritis huber-morathii

Sideritis huber-morathii, commonly known as the Morath's ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that are typically pale blue or purple in color. It thrives in rocky and well-drained soils, often found in alpine and subalpine habitats.

Sideritis huber-morathii is known for its medicinal properties, with historical use in traditional herbal remedies.
