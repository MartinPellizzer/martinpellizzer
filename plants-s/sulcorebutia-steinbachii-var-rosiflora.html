Sulcorebutia steinbachii var. rosiflora

Sulcorebutia steinbachii var. rosiflora, commonly known as the Rosy Flora Cactus, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to South America.

It features short, cylindrical stems covered in white to pale green spines, which help reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant pink to red flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a striking contrast to its green foliage. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its slow growth and unique appearance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
