Stillingia Scutellifera

Stillingia scutellifera is a rare and striking plant native to the southeastern united states. it is known for its unique appearance and medicinal properties. the plant features bright red flowers and spiny leaves that make it easily identifiable. it thrives in moist, shaded environments along stream banks. due to its limited range and habitat destruction, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts.
