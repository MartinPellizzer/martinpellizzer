Siparuna cauliflora

Siparuna cauliflora, commonly known as the red-flowered siparuna, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that are often borne in clusters. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a glossy appearance.

Siparuna cauliflora is valued for its ornamental flowers and is often cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
