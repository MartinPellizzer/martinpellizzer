Spachea elegans var. obovata

Spachea elegans var. obovata, commonly known as the elegant spachea, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its obovate leaves, which are broad and rounded at the tips, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
