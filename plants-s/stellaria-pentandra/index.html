Stellaria Pentandra

Stellaria pentandra is a small flowering plant known for its delicate appearance and unique growth habits. Native to parts of Europe and Asia, it thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. This species belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family, which includes other familiar plants like carnations and chickweed. Its star-shaped white flowers bloom in spring, adding a subtle beauty to its natural habitat. Despite its modest size, Stellaria pentandra plays an important ecological role in supporting local wildlife and maintaining biodiversity.
