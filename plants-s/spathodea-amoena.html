Spathodea amoena

Spathodea amoena, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical flowering plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery orange to red bracts that surround its small, pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as a tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are large, pinnately compound, and its flowers bloom in clusters at the ends of branches.

Spathodea amoena is widely cultivated for its ornamental value and is often used in landscaping due to its vibrant floral display.
