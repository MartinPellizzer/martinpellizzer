Satureja plumosa

Satureja plumosa, commonly known as woolly savory, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its gray-green, fuzzy leaves and small, pink or purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes. Its aromatic foliage contains essential oils that contribute to its use in culinary and medicinal applications.

Satureja plumosa is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract beneficial insects.
