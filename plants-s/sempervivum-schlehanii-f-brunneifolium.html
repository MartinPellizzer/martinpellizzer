Sempervivum schlehanii f. brunneifolium

Sempervivum schlehanii f. brunneifolium, commonly known as the Brunneifolium houseleek, is a succulent plant native to the Pyrenees region.

It features rosettes of fleshy, bluish-gray leaves that are densely packed and often covered in a powdery coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

Its compact growth habit and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
