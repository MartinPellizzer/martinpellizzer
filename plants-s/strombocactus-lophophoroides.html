Strombocactus lophophoroides

Strombocactus lophophoroides, commonly known as the golden barrel cactus, is a species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its rounded, globular shape and a dense covering of yellow to golden spines. The plant features a thick, water-storing stem that helps it survive in arid conditions. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, blooming in spring and summer.

Strombocactus lophophoroides is a popular ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
