Spartium stylosum

Spartium stylosum, commonly known as narrowleaf sicklevetch, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, yellow pea-like flowers. The plant has trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets. It produces cylindrical, nodular pods that contain several small, dark brown seeds.

Spartium stylosum is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to thrive in arid environments.
