Solanum medianiviolaceum

Solanum medianiviolaceum, commonly known as the violetleaf nightshade, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its violet-colored leaves and small, clustered flowers that range from white to pale purple. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Solanum medianiviolaceum is not widely cultivated but is of interest to botanists due to its unique floral structure and potential medicinal properties.
