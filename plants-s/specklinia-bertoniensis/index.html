Specklinia Bertoniensis

Bertoni's specklinia, scientifically known as Specklinia bertoniensis, is a rare and exquisite member of the orchid family. Native to the mountainous regions of Ecuador, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its unique appearance features delicate, dark green leaves with intricate veining and small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink. Due to its limited habitat and threats from deforestation, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this botanical marvel for future generations.
