Senecio clarioneifolius

Senecio clarioneifolius, commonly known as the Clarion leaf senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators.

Due to its unique foliage and ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it prefers well-drained soils and full sun.
