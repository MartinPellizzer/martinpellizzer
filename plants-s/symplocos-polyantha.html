Symplocos polyantha

Symplocos polyantha, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or sweet pepper tree, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features small, fragrant white or pink flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by small, edible berries. The plant has elliptical leaves with a glossy surface and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Symplocos polyantha is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
