Stellaria pentastyla

Stellaria pentastyla, commonly known as the five-styled starwort, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges. Its small, white or pinkish flowers bloom in early summer, and the plant produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Stellaria pentastyla is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
