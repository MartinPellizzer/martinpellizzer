Salvia siyuanensis

Salvia siyuanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan sage, is a perennial flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is known for its aromatic herbs and ornamental plants. The plant features square stems and opposite leaves that are typically gray-green in color. It produces striking violet-blue flowers in whorled clusters, which bloom during the summer months.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
