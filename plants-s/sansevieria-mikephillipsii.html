Sansevieria mikephillipsii

Sansevieria mikephillipsii, commonly known as the Mike Phillips Sansevieria, is a unique species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-like leaves that are typically green with striking yellow or white variegation. The plant is well adapted to arid environments, making it highly drought-tolerant and easy to care for. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or greenish in color.

This species is prized for its ornamental value and air-purifying qualities, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
