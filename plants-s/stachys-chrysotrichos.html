Stachys chrysotrichos

Stachys chrysotrichos, commonly known as golden hedge-nettle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features upright stems covered in dense, soft hairs that give it a fuzzy appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pale yellow to greenish in color. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

Stachys chrysotrichos is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
