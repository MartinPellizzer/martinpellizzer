Sericographis cyrtantheriformis var. robustior

Sericographis cyrtantheriformis var. robustior, commonly known as the robust orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive yellow and green coloration. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native habitat.

The orchid's inflorescence produces multiple blooms, making it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation.
