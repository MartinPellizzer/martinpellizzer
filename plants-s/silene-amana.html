Silene amana

Silene amana, commonly known as the mountain catchfly, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its pink or white flowers that bloom in late summer. Silene amana has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.
