Sedum ermenekensis

Sedum ermenekensis, commonly known as the Ermenek stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.

Sedum ermenekensis is also valued for its low maintenance and ability to withstand drought conditions.
