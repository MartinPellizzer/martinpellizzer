Stenosiphonium confertum

Stenosiphonium confertum, commonly known as the narrow-sheath sedge, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, linear leaves and small, clustered flowers. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in wetland and marshy environments. The plant has a rhizomatous root system that helps it spread and colonize moist soils.

Its ecological role includes providing habitat for small aquatic organisms and contributing to soil stabilization in wetland ecosystems.
