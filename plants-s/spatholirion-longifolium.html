Spatholirion longifolium

Spatholirion longifolium, commonly known as the long-leaved spatholirion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, elongated leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pale blue or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Spatholirion longifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
