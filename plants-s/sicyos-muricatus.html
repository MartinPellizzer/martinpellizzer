Sicyos muricatus

Sicyos muricatus, commonly known as the spiny gourd, is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its rough, spiny stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow, fragrant flowers that give way to elongated, spiny fruits. Native to tropical regions of the Americas, it thrives in warm, humid climates.

Its unique morphology and growth habits make it a distinctive and sometimes invasive species in certain areas.
