Senecio mooreanus

Senecio mooreanus, commonly known as the Moorean groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the island of Mauritius.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its yellow flowers are surrounded by pale green bracts, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Senecio mooreanus is also noted for its medicinal properties, traditionally used in local remedies.
