Succisa ustulata

Succisa ustulata, commonly known as the fireweed or common fireweed, is a flowering plant native to the temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It is a member of the Onagraceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. Succisa ustulata is known for its ability to colonize areas after fires or other disturbances, hence its common name.
