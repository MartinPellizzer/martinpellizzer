Symplocos kwangsiensis

Symplocos kwangsiensis, commonly known as the Guangxi symplocos, is a deciduous shrub native to southern China.

It belongs to the family Ericaceae and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves with a leathery texture. The plant produces white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by small, woody fruits. It thrives in acidic soils and is often found in mountainous regions with high humidity.
