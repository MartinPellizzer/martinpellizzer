Stellaria media subsp. typica

Stellaria media subsp.

typica, commonly known as common chickweed, is a low-growing annual plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa. It features small, white star-shaped flowers and opposite, thin, hairy leaves.

The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in lawns, gardens, and along pathways. Its stems are prostrate or ascending, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

It is valued for its use in herbal medicine and as a nutritious leafy green.
