Solanum davidsoniae

Solanum davidsoniae, commonly known as the Davidson's nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant features small, white or pale green flowers that bloom in clusters, often at the tips of the stems. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with a slightly hairy texture.

Solanum davidsoniae is known for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in both sunny and shaded environments.
