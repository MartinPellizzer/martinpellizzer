Sisymbrium antarcticum

Sisymbrium antarcticum, commonly known as Antarctic winter cress, is a flowering plant native to the cold regions of the Southern Hemisphere.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and pinnately divided leaves. It thrives in harsh environments such as Antarctica and sub-Antarctic islands, where it plays a role in the local ecosystem.
