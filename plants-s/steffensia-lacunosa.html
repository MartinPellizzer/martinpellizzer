Steffensia lacunosa

Steffensia lacunosa, commonly known as the slender leaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, elongated leaves and produces small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in clusters. It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as grasslands and wetlands.

Steffensia lacunosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
