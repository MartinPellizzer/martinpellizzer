Scrophularia juratensis

Scrophularia juratensis, commonly known as the Juratian figwort, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae, which includes many species with medicinal and ornamental value. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are tubular and usually pink or purple in color.

Scrophularia juratensis is known for its resilience and is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
