Setaria seriata

Setaria seriata, commonly known as slender dropseed, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and sandy soils. It has a fibrous root system and is often used in ecological restoration projects.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to harsh environmental conditions.
