Senecio delbesianus

Senecio delbesianus, commonly known as the Delbesian groundseller, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Senecio delbesianus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and resilience.
