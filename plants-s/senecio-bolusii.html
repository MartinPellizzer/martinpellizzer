Senecio bolusii

Senecio bolusii, commonly known as Bolus's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky, arid environments and has small, narrow leaves. Its flowers are yellow and are arranged in dense clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Senecio bolusii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and hardiness.
