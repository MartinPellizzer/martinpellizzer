Schismatoglottis parvifolia

Schismatoglottis parvifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved schismatoglottis, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit. The plant features broad, leathery leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, often clustered in loose inflorescences.

Schismatoglottis parvifolia is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ease of cultivation.
