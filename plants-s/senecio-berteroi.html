Senecio berteroi

Senecio berteroi, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions. Its yellow-orange flower heads consist of numerous small florets, and it produces achenes as its seeds.

Senecio berteroi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
