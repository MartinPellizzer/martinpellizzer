Staehelina squarrosa

Staehelina squarrosa, commonly known as the squarrose starwort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils. The plant features small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of white or pale pink. Its leaves are lanceolate and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly rough texture.

Staehelina squarrosa is adapted to arid environments and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
