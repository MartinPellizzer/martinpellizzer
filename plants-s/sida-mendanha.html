Sida mendanha

Sida mendanha, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its rough, hairy leaves and small, pale yellow flowers. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides. Its seeds are small and can be dispersed by wind, contributing to its wide distribution.

Sida mendanha is also known for its potential medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
