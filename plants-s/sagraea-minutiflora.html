Sagraea minutiflora

Sagraea minutiflora, commonly known as the small-flowered sagraea, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, small flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has slender, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid tropical forests.

Due to its limited distribution and unique floral structure, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
