Solanum desvauxii

Solanum desvauxii, commonly known as the Desert Nightshade, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in dry, rocky soils. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale purple, blooming at night. Its fruit is a fleshy berry that turns red when mature, containing several seeds.

Solanum desvauxii is adapted to survive in harsh environments with limited water availability.
