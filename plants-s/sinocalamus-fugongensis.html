Sinocalamus fugongensis

Sinocalamus fugongensis, commonly known as the Fugong bamboo, is a species of grass belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to China, particularly found in the Yunnan and Sichuan provinces. This bamboo is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and can grow up to 15 meters in height. Its leaves are long and narrow, with a smooth texture and a slightly hairy surface.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
