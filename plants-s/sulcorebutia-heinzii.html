Sulcorebutia heinzii

Sulcorebutia heinzii, commonly known as the Heinz's sun rose, is a succulent plant native to South America.

It features a compact, rounded rosette of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it ideal for rock gardens or containers.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
