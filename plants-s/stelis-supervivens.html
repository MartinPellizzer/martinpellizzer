Stelis supervivens

Stelis supervivens, commonly known as the surviving orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on the surface of other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant typically has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in humid, tropical environments.

Stelis supervivens is known for its ability to survive in various conditions, making it a resilient species in its natural habitat.
