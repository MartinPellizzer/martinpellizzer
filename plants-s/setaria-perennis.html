Setaria perennis

Setaria perennis, commonly known as perennial dropseed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in prairies and open woodlands.

Setaria perennis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing habitat for pollinators.
