Solanum sericeum

Solanum sericeum, commonly known as silverleaf nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-gray foliage and small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically yellow or orange when ripe. Solanum sericeum is often found in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
