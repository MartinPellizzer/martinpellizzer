Senecio fastigiatus var. erosodentatus

Senecio fastigiatus var. erosodentatus, commonly known as the Blue Chalk Scale, is a striking succulent native to South Africa.

It features a compact, rounded growth habit with dense, blue-gray foliage that has a chalky texture. The leaves are arranged in a rosette pattern and are covered in a fine, powdery coating. It produces small, yellow flowers in summer, adding a subtle touch of color to its appearance.

This plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light.
