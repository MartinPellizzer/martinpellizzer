Stelis varicella

Stelis varicella, commonly known as the variable stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is an epiphytic orchid, typically found growing on trees or rocks in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has slender, often branching stems and produces small, greenish or white flowers. Its leaves are usually elongated and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Stelis varicella thrives in humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media for optimal growth.
