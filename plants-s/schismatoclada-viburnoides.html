Schismatoclada viburnoides

Schismatoclada viburnoides, commonly known as the African snowbush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white, clustered flowers that bloom in winter, followed by red, berry-like fruits. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showing drought tolerance.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
