Schizoloma ensifolium

Schizoloma ensifolium, commonly known as the swordleaf lichen, is a foliose lichen that thrives in moist, shaded environments.

It has a distinctive greenish-blue thallus with a leathery texture and is often found on bark or soil. The plant exhibits a complex structure with distinct upper and lower layers, which contribute to its ability to retain moisture. It plays a vital role in nutrient cycling in forest ecosystems.

Schizoloma ensifolium is also used in traditional medicine for its purported antifungal and anti-inflammatory properties.
