Stellaria monosperma var. taiwaniana

Stellaria monosperma var. taiwaniana, commonly known as the Taiwan starwort, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, star-shaped white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. It has slender stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

Its flowers bloom in early spring and attract various pollinators like bees and flies.
