Syndiclis chinensis

Syndiclis chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep red. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture.

Syndiclis chinensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various soil types.
