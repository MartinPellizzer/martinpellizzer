Spondias axillaris

Spondias axillaris, commonly known as the Brazilian red mombin, is a small to medium-sized tree native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Anacardiaceae family, which includes other economically important plants like the cashew and mango. The plant is also valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in folk medicine.
