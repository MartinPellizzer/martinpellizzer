Swertia juzepczukii

Swertia juzepczukii, commonly known as the Tibetan swertia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Gentianaceae family.

It is native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas, particularly in Nepal, India, and Bhutan. The plant is characterized by its slender stems and clusters of small, blue or violet flowers. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

Swertia juzepczukii is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
