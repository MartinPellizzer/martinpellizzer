Sabicea floribunda var. paucinervis

Sabicea floribunda var. paucinervis, commonly known as the slender sabicea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Leguminosae family and is characterized by its slender, often climbing stems. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pale green to white in color. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a distinct venation pattern.

It is often found in humid, lowland forest environments.
