Senniella

Senniella, commonly known as the African senna, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow-orange flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. Senniella is often found in dry, open habitats and is adapted to arid conditions.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional medicine.
