Sutera floribunda

Sutera floribunda, commonly known as the clustered bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its clusters of fragrant, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming a basal rosette.

Sutera floribunda is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
