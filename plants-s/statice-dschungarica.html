Statice dschungarica

Statice dschungarica, commonly known as the Central Asian glasswort, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its salt-tolerant nature, making it adaptable to arid and saline environments. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, which bloom during the summer months. Its foliage is succulent, and the stems are often covered in fine hairs.

Statice dschungarica is often used in ornamental gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and resilience.
