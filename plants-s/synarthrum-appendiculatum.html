Synarthrum appendiculatum

Synarthrum appendiculatum, commonly known as the slender water plantain, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetlands and marshy areas in North America.

It typically grows in shallow water or moist soil, often found in ponds, lakes, and along streams. The plant has long, narrow leaves that are often submerged or floating, and it produces small, white or greenish flowers in clusters. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense stands in its habitat.

Synarthrum appendiculatum plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various aquatic organisms.
