Sedum somenii

Sedum somenii, commonly known as the desert sedum, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of Central Asia.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow or orange. The plant is adapted to hot, dry environments and stores water in its thick leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy choice for xeriscaping.

Its low maintenance and ability to withstand drought make it a popular ornamental plant in rock gardens and dry landscapes.
