Sinapistrum giganteum

Sinapistrum giganteum, commonly known as the giant elephant's ear, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like peace lilies and calla lilies. Its leaves are typically a deep green with a velvety texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters.
