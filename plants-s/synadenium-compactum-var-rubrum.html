Synadenium compactum var. rubrum

Synadenium compactum var. rubrum, commonly known as the red torch cactus, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, cylindrical stems that are typically reddish in color, especially when exposed to full sun. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit and striking appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
