Sedum maximum subsp. fabaria

Sedum maximum subsp.

fabaria, commonly known as the rock sedum, is a perennial succulent native to rocky and arid environments. It features fleshy, upright stems and leaves that are typically green to bluish-green in color.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white, usually in late summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
