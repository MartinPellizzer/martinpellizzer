Sisyrinchium laxinervium

Sisyrinchium laxinervium, commonly known as the slenderleaf blue-eyed grass, is a herbaceous perennial native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for native plant gardens.
