Stenorrhynchos pubens

Stenorrhynchos pubens, commonly known as the hairy stenorrhynchos, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are typically broad and fleshy, adapted to arid conditions.

The flowers are showy, with a distinctive coloration and a fragrant scent, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
