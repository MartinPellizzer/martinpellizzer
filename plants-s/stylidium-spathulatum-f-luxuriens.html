Stylidium spathulatum f. luxuriens

Stylidium spathulatum f. luxuriens, commonly known as the luxuriant cushion-daisy, is a variant of the cushion-daisy species native to Australia.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming plant characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant features small, showy flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, which appear in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are fleshy and succulent, adapted to store water in arid environments.

This form is particularly noted for its lush, dense foliage, which contributes to its common name.
