Silene atsaensis

Silene atsaensis, commonly known as the Atsa mountainflower, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Andes in South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, pinkish-white flowers that bloom during the short growing season in its alpine habitat. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and is adapted to cold, harsh environmental conditions. Silene atsaensis has small, lance-shaped leaves that help reduce water loss in its arid environment.
