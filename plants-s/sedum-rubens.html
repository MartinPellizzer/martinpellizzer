Sedum rubens

Sedum rubens, commonly known as the red stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features fleshy, upright stems and clusters of small, bright red flowers that bloom in summer. The leaves are typically gray-green with a waxy coating, helping the plant store water efficiently. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy choice for rock gardens or containers.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant blooms make it a popular ornamental plant in landscaping.
