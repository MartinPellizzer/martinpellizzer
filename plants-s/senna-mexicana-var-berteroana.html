Senna mexicana var. berteroana

Senna mexicana var. berteroana, commonly known as the Mexican senna, is a shrub or small tree native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant is known for its yellow flowers, which are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soils.

Its leaves and pods contain compounds with potential medicinal properties.
