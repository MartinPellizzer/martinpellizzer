Sterculia kingii

Sterculia kingii, commonly known as the King's sterculia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of eastern Australia.

It is a large tree that can grow up to 30 meters in height, featuring a broad, spreading crown. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pale yellow or white, arranged in clusters. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds enclosed in a fleshy aril.

The leaves are large, alternate, and often have a rough, hairy texture.
