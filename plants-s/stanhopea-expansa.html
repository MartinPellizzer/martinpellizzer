Stanhopea expansa

Stanhopea expansa, commonly known as the broadleaf stanhopea, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 inches in diameter, with a distinctive, elongated lip. The plant typically grows as a sympodial orchid, forming pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

Stanhopea expansa is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
