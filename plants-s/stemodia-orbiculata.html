Stemodia orbiculata

Stemodia orbiculata, commonly known as the globe-leaf stemodia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its round, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It typically grows as a succulent shrub with a compact, upright form.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to survive in dry, rocky habitats.
