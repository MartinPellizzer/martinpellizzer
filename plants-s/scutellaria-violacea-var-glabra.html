Scutellaria violacea var. glabra

Scutellaria violacea var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth blue skullcap, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and aromatic foliage. The plant features showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in whorled clusters along the upper stems. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs, which are less prominent in the glabra variant.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
