Salsola monandra

Salsola monandra, commonly known as Russian thistle or goatweed, is a flowering plant in the family Amaranthaceae.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, characterized by its succulent stems and leaves. Salsola monandra is often considered a weed in agricultural areas due to its rapid growth and ability to spread easily.
