Sinosenecio cyclaminifolius

Sinosenecio cyclaminifolius, commonly known as the cyclaminifolius groundsel, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in clusters at the ends of stems.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or rocky habitats.
