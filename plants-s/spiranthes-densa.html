Spiranthes densa

Spiranthes densa, commonly known as the dense spiraea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact, rounded growth habit. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem, which is a distinctive feature of the genus Spiranthes.
