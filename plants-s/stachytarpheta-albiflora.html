Stachytarpheta albiflora

Stachytarpheta albiflora, commonly known as whiteflower ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its most distinctive characteristic is its clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in late summer.
