Senecio bidwillii

Senecio bidwillii, commonly known as the Bidwill's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a cluster. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is often cultivated in gardens.
