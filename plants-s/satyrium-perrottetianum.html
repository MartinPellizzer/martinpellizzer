Satyrium perrottetianum

Satyrium perrottetianum, commonly known as the perrottetianum orchid, is a species native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a slender, erect stem with several lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered, often displaying a mix of white, green, and purple hues.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
