Stellaria cuspidata subsp. cuspidata

Stellaria cuspidata subsp.

cuspidata, commonly known as the sharp-pointed starwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to parts of Europe and Asia. It typically features slender, branching stems covered in small, star-shaped flowers that are white or pale pink.

The leaves are narrow and lanceolate, often with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

This species is characterized by its small, delicate flowers and is often found in cool, temperate climates.
