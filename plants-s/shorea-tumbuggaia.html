Shorea tumbuggaia

Shorea tumbuggaia, commonly known as the Tumbuggaia ironwood, is a tropical tree native to the rainforests of northern Queensland, Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, heavy timber that is highly valued for its durability and strength. The tree has a straight trunk with a rounded crown, and its leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptical in shape. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by woody capsules containing several seeds.

Shorea tumbuggaia plays an important ecological role in its habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
