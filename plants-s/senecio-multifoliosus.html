Senecio multifoliosus

Senecio multifoliosus, commonly known as the many-leaved groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and features bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has hairy, narrow leaves that are often deeply lobed, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Senecio multifoliosus is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticulture for its hardy and low-maintenance nature.
