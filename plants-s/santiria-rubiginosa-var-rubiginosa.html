Santiria rubiginosa var. rubiginosa

Santiria rubiginosa var. rubiginosa, commonly known as the red-stemmed santiria, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with hairy, reddish stems and lance-shaped leaves. Its small yellow flowers appear in clusters, and it produces small, oval-shaped fruits.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and damp undergrowth.
