Swertia pianmaensis

Swertia pianmaensis, commonly known as the Pianma swertia, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Sichuan, China.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its slender stem and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Swertia pianmaensis is often found in moist, shaded habitats at high altitudes.

Its medicinal properties have been traditionally used in Chinese herbal medicine.
