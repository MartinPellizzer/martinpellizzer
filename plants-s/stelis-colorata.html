Stelis colorata

Stelis colorata, commonly known as the rainbow orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its compact growth habit and dense clusters of small, colorful flowers. The plant typically features strap-shaped leaves and produces showy inflorescences with flowers in a range of vibrant colors. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Stelis colorata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
