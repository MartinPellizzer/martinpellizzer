Stachyurus macrocarpus

Stachyurus macrocarpus, commonly known as the Chinese honeysuckle or large-fruited stachyurus, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features graceful, arching branches with clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces showy, red-orange berries that persist into winter, adding visual interest to the landscape. Its foliage is dark green and becomes particularly striking in the fall.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
