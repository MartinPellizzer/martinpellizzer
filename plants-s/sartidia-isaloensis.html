Sartidia isaloensis

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like fronds. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. Its leaves are bioluminescent, a rare trait among ferns, which helps it attract nocturnal pollinators.
