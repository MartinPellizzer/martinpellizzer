Staphylea bumalda var. latifolia

Staphylea bumalda var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf staphylea, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Staphyleaceae family and is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, wooded areas and thrives in partial to full shade. Its botanical features include smooth, dark green foliage and small, capsule-like fruits that mature in the fall.

It is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive appearance and ecological benefits.
