Sedum Telephium Var. Angustifolium

Sedum telephium var. angustifolium is a striking succulent known for its vibrant red and green foliage. Native to rocky and coastal areas, this plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. Its narrow, fleshy leaves form dense clusters that make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping. The plant's ability to store water makes it highly resilient in arid environments. With its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it's a favorite among gardeners seeking both beauty and durability.
