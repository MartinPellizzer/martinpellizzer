Serjania neei

Serjania neei, commonly known as the Brazilian serjania, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It belongs to the family Serjaniaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by yellowish-orange berries. Serjania neei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and flowering habit.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun exposure.
