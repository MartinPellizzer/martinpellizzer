Stelmation myrtifolium

Stelmation myrtifolium, commonly known as the silver myrtle, is a shrub native to the coastal regions of southern Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The leaves are elliptical in shape and have a distinctive aromatic scent when crushed. It produces small, white, fragrant flowers during the spring and summer months.

This plant is well adapted to sandy soils and salt-laden coastal environments.
