Seseli libanotis var. athamantoides

Seseli libanotis var. athamantoides, commonly known as the Lebanon sesame or Athamantoides sesame, is a perennial herb native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

Due to its ornamental value and historical significance, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
