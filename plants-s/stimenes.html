Stimenes

Stimenes, commonly known as the stimulant plant, is a species native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. Its botanical name, Stimenes, reflects its historical use in traditional medicine for its stimulating properties.

However, it is important to note that the plant contains psychoactive compounds and should be handled with caution.
