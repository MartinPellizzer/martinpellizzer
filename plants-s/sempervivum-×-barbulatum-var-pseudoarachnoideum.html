Sempervivum × barbulatum var. pseudoarachnoideum

Sempervivum × barbulatum var. pseudoarachnoideum, commonly known as the fairy moss or snowflake plant, is a hybrid succulent native to the Pyrenees region.

It features dense, rosette-shaped foliage with fleshy, silver-gray leaves that have a woolly texture. The plant produces star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it ideal for rock gardens or alpine settings.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for both gardeners and collectors.
