Senecio argaeus

Senecio argaeus, commonly known as the silverleaf senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its gray-green, fuzzy leaves that give it a distinctive silvery appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Senecio argaeus is also known for its resilience and ability to withstand drought conditions.
