Senecio transmarinus

Senecio transmarinus, commonly known as the Transmarinus groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its yellow flowers are surrounded by narrow, leaf-like bracts, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its hardy nature and aesthetic appeal.
