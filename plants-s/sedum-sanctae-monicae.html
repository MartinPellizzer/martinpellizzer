Sedum sanctae-monicae

Sedum sanctae-monicae, commonly known as the sacred Monica's sedum, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, succulent leaves and clusters of star-shaped flowers. Sedum sanctae-monicae is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
