Sisymbrium columnae var. villosissimum

Sisymbrium columnae var. villosissimum, commonly known as the woolly bladderpod, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants, and is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and pungent odor.
