Salvia orbignaei

Salvia orbignaei, commonly known as the Mexican sage, is a perennial shrub native to Mexico.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a distinctive bluish-green color. The plant produces striking spikes of violet-blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its aromatic foliage and showy blooms make it a popular choice among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts.
