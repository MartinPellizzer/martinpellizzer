Saussurea kinbuensis

Saussurea kinbuensis, commonly known as the Kinbu arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, often branched stem and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically arranged in a composite head, typical of the daisy family. It thrives in high-altitude environments and is adapted to cold, arid conditions.
