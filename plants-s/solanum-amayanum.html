Solanum amayanum

Solanum amayanum, commonly known as the African nightshade, is a flowering plant native to tropical and southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale green, followed by berries that mature to a bright red color. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Solanum amayanum belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae), which includes other plants like tomatoes and potatoes.
