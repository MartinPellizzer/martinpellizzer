Sincoraea ulei

Sincoraea ulei, commonly known as the creeping morning glory, is a flowering plant belonging to the Convolvulaceae family.

It is characterized by its vine-like growth habit and trumpet-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink to purple.
