Schefflera brevipes

Schefflera brevipes, commonly known as the short-stemmed umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of leaves, which are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by bright red berries, attracting birds and other wildlife. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for both ornamental and indoor gardening.
