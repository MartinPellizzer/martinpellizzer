Stachys sandersii

Stachys sandersii, commonly known as sanders' betony, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central Asia.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic foliage. The plant produces spikes of blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms.
