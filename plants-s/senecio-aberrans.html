Senecio aberrans

Senecio aberrans, commonly known as the yellow owl's eye, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy yellow ray flowers surrounding a central disk of smaller florets, giving it a distinctive appearance. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open habitats.

Senecio aberrans is also noted for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
