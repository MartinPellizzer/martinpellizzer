Swertia densifolia

Swertia densifolia, commonly known as the dense-leaved gentian, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in high-altitude regions, often in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are dense and arranged in whorls, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Swertia densifolia is valued both for its ornamental qualities and its traditional medicinal uses.
