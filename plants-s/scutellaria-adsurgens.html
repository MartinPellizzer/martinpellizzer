Scutellaria adsurgens

Scutellaria adsurgens, commonly known as the climbing skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, two-lipped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in rocky or dry habitats and is often found in alpine environments.

Scutellaria adsurgens is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine for various health purposes.
