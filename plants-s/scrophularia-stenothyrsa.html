Scrophularia stenothyrsa

Scrophularia stenothyrsa, commonly known as narrow-throat scrophularia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple, often attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
