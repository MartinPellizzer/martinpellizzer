Sedum reflexum var. cristatum

Sedum reflexum var. cristatum, commonly known as the Crown of Thorns or Christ's Crown, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, bushy growth habit with fleshy, blue-green leaves that form dense clusters. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers in late summer, adding ornamental value. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
