Schizanthus floribundus

Schizanthus floribundus, commonly known as the common lopsided flower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous annual that produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant features trifoliate leaves and is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. Its flowers are borne in clusters and bloom throughout the growing season.

Schizanthus floribundus is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and easy cultivation.
