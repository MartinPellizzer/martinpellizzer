Scheelea leandroana

Scheelea leandroana, commonly known as the Brazilian palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features a tall, slender trunk with a smooth, pale green surface and can grow up to 10 meters in height. Its leaves are large, pinnately compound, and often have a feathery appearance.
