Senecio uniserialis

Senecio uniserialis, commonly known as the narrowleaf groundsel, is a perennial flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant features narrow, linear leaves arranged in a spiral pattern along its stem. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

Senecio uniserialis is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
