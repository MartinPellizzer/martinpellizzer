Senecio sancti-sebastiani

Senecio sancti-sebastiani, commonly known as Saint Sebastian's Senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage is gray-green with a woolly texture, and it produces small yellow flowers in clusters.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
