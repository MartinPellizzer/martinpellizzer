Schefflera pentandra

Schefflera pentandra, commonly known as the Umbrella Tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like canopy of large, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Schefflera pentandra is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor environments.
