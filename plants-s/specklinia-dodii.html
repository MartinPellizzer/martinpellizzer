Specklinia dodii

Specklinia dodii, commonly known as the dwarf orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant has slender, dark green leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in terrariums or as a houseplant.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in the wild.
