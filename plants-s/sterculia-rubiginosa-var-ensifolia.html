Sterculia rubiginosa var. ensifolia

Sterculia rubiginosa var. ensifolia, commonly known as the swordleaf sterculia, is a tropical tree native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by woody, capsule-like fruits. Its bark is thick and often fissured, providing a rugged appearance.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
