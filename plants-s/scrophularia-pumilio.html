Scrophularia pumilio

Scrophularia pumilio, commonly known as the lesser scarlet pimpernel, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a small annual herb that typically grows to a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers with a distinctive red calyx. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

Scrophularia pumilio is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats such as roadsides and meadows.
