Sericanthe trilocularis subsp. paroissei

Sericanthe trilocularis subsp.

paroissei, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit.
