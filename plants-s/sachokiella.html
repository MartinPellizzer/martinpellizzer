Sachokiella

Sachokiella, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to Africa.

These plants are characterized by their velvety foliage and showy, clustered flowers that come in a variety of colors. They typically grow as compact shrubs with upright stems and broad, heart-shaped leaves. Sachokiella species are popular houseplants due to their ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

They thrive in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.
