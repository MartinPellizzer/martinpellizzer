Sarcopyramis napalensis var. delicata

Sarcopyramis napalensis var. delicata, commonly known as the delicate napalensis pyramidal, is a unique plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, pyramidal growth habit and small, scale-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers during the warmer months, which are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized species in horticulture.
