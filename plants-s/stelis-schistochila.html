Stelis schistochila

Stelis schistochila, commonly known as the stone orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical environments, often growing on tree trunks or branches. The plant features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with fleshy leaves that store water. Its flowers are typically small, clustered, and come in shades of white, pink, or purple.

It requires bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to flourish.
