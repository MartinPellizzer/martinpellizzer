Stelis perennis

Stelis perennis, commonly known as the everlasting orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia, including parts of India, China, and Indonesia. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and usually white or pale pink, arranged in a spike.

Stelis perennis is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
