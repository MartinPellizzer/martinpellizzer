Scutellaria polyphylla

Scutellaria polyphylla, commonly known as the many-leaved skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features a square stem and opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are blue to purple and are arranged in whorls along the stem.

Scutellaria polyphylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
