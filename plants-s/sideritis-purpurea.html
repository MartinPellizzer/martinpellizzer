Sideritis purpurea

Sideritis purpurea, commonly known as purple beardtongue, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features slender, upright stems and clusters of purple to pink flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, often with a minty scent.

Sideritis purpurea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
