Semnostachya antonii

Semnostachya antonii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant features velvety, dark green leaves and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of violet, pink, and white. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.
