Stenorrhynchos longicolle

Stenorrhynchos longicolle, commonly known as the long-headed orchid, is a species of orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the dry environment.

Stenorrhynchos longicolle is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral structure.
