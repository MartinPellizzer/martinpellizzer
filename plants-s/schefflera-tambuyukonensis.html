Schefflera tambuyukonensis

Schefflera tambuyukonensis, commonly known as the Tambuyukon schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of leaves. The plant features compound leaves with multiple leaflets arranged in a whorled pattern, giving it a lush, full appearance. It produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red berries, which are attractive to birds.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in warm climates as a houseplant or landscape specimen.
