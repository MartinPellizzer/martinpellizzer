Sphaerostigma contortum var. greenei

Sphaerostigma contortum var. greenei, commonly known as the Greene's twisted bladderpod, is a flowering plant in the family Brassicaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, inflated, bladder-like pods that enclose the seeds. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, often in rocky soils. It has small, green, lobed leaves and produces yellow flowers in early spring.
