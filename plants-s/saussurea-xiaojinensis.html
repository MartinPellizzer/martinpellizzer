Saussurea xiaojinensis

Saussurea xiaojinensis, commonly known as the Yunnan snow lotus, is a perennial herb native to the high-altitude regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stem and large, showy composite flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky areas, thriving in cold, dry climates. Its medicinal properties have been recognized in traditional Chinese medicine, where it is used to treat various ailments.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
