Solanum dillenianum

Solanum dillenianum, commonly known as the yellow nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant features palmate leaves with five to seven lobes and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruits are berries that turn from green to yellow or orange as they mature.

The plant is often found in moist, shaded areas and can be a weed in agricultural settings.
