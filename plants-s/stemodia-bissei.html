Stemodia bissei

Stemodia bissei, commonly known as the African milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features slender, erect stems that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, white to pale pink flowers are clustered in terminal umbels, attracting various pollinators.

The leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
