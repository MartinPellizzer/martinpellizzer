Silene parjumanensis

Silene parjumanensis, commonly known as the Parjumanensis campion, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid conditions.

Silene parjumanensis is of interest to botanists due to its unique ecological niche and limited distribution.
