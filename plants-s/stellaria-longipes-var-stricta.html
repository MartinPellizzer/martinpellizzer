Stellaria longipes var. stricta

Stellaria longipes var. stricta, commonly known as the compact starwort, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with slender stems that often form dense mats. The plant produces small, star-shaped white or pale blue flowers, which bloom in early summer. Its leaves are narrow and linear, arranged in a rosette pattern at the base.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and rocky soils.
