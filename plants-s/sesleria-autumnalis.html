Sesleria autumnalis

Sesleria autumnalis, commonly known as autumn sesleria, is a perennial grass-like plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are often silver or gray-green in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink or purple during the autumn season. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Sesleria autumnalis is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
