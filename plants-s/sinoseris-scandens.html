Sinoseris scandens

Sinoseris scandens, commonly known as the climbing aster, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically has slender, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are usually pink to purple in color and bloom in the summer months.

Sinoseris scandens is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
