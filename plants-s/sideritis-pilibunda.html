Sideritis pilibunda

Sideritis pilibunda, commonly known as the Greek violet or mountain violet, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features small, violet-blue flowers that bloom in summer, typically arranged in dense clusters. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, adapted to thrive in rocky or dry environments.

Sideritis pilibunda is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
