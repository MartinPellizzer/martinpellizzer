Senecio hieronymi

Senecio hieronymi, commonly known as the Blue Echeveria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, blue-gray foliage with a woolly texture, and its leaves are arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces striking blue or purple flowers during the cooler months, typically in spring or early summer. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
