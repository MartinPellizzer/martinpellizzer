Sondera preissii

Sondera preissii, commonly known as the desert lily, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has a deep root system that allows it to survive in dry conditions, making it well adapted to harsh desert environments. Its leaves are typically narrow and fleshy, reducing water loss through transpiration.

Sondera preissii plays an important ecological role by providing nectar for pollinators such as bees and birds.
