Stenocactus boedekerianus

Stenocactus boedekerianus, commonly known as the Boedeker's stenocactus, is a rare and compact cactus native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with a smooth, dark green surface that may have a slight bluish tint. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers during the cooler months, typically in spring or early summer. It has a shallow root system adapted to absorb moisture from the dry soil.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
