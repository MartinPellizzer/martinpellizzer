Skimmia euphlebia

Skimmia euphlebia, commonly known as the Japanese skimmia, is a evergreen shrub native to Japan and southern Korea.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and often have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters during late winter to early spring. These flowers are typically white or pale pink and are followed by red, fleshy berries.

Skimmia euphlebia is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
