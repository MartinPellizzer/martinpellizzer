Stengelia insignis

Stengelia insignis, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

The plant is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is known for its compact growth habit and ability to thrive in shaded environments. Stengelia insignis is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
