Stapelia × hanburyana

Stapelia × hanburyana, commonly known as the hybrid stapelia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed stems and modified leaves. The plant produces striking, waxy flowers that are typically yellow or orange with a strong scent, often resembling decaying flesh to attract pollinators. It is a hybrid between Stapelia hahndorfi and Stapelia subulata, resulting in unique floral characteristics.

Stapelia × hanburyana is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
