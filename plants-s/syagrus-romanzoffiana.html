Syagrus romanzoffiana

Syagrus romanzoffiana, commonly known as the royal palm, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, fan-shaped leaves that give it a regal appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into feather-like fruits, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal, it is widely used in landscaping across tropical and subtropical regions.
