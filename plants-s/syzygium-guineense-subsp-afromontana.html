Syzygium guineense subsp.

afromontana, commonly known as the African montane guineense, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the highland regions of eastern Africa. It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its elliptical, glossy leaves and small, fragrant white flowers.
