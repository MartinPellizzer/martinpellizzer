Sisymbrium pendulum

Sisymbrium pendulum, commonly known as the hanging brookweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils along streambanks and wet meadows. Its leaves are alternate, linear, and often have a slightly hairy texture.
