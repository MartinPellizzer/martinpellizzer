Scutellaria ikonnikovii

Scutellaria ikonnikovii, commonly known as the Ikonnikov's skullcap, is a species of flowering plant in the mint family, Lamiaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in Kazakhstan and Russia. The plant is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Scutellaria ikonnikovii is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to harsh, high-altitude environments.
