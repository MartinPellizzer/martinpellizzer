Syzygium toppingii

Syzygium toppingii, commonly known as the Topping's star apple, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically yellow or red when ripe, with a sweet and tangy flavor. Syzygium toppingii thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and edible fruits.
