Schrenkia pungens

Schrenkia pungens, commonly known as the spiny lily, is a striking member of the Amaryllidaceae family.

Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, it thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces tall, slender stems with showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white to pale pink. Its unique spiky bracts and fragrant blooms make it a distinctive ornamental plant.

Schrenkia pungens is adapted to cold climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
