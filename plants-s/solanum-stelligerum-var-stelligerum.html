Solanum stelligerum var. stelligerum

Solanum stelligerum var. stelligerum, commonly known as the Stelligerum nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades and tomatoes. The plant features small, white or pale green flowers and produces berries that range in color from yellow to red. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal regions.

Due to its toxic compounds, it should be handled with care.
