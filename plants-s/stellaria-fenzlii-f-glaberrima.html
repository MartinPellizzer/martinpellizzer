Stellaria fenzlii f. glaberrima

Stellaria fenzlii f. glaberrima, commonly known as the glabrous starwort, is a flowering plant species belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, star-shaped white flowers and slender, ascending stems. The plant typically grows in moist, sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are lanceolate and covered with a dense layer of fine hairs, which gives it a velvety texture.

This subspecies is notable for its horticultural value and is occasionally cultivated for ornamental purposes.
