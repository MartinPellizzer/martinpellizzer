Statice plantaginea

Statice plantaginea, commonly known as sea lavender, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, showy blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has slender, branching stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in coastal areas and saline environments.

Statice plantaginea is valued for its ornamental use in gardens and as a cut flower due to its long-lasting blooms.
