Senecio latiligulatus

Senecio latiligulatus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in dense clusters.

It is a perennial herb that can reach heights of up to 60 centimeters.
