Solanum deckenii

Solanum deckenii, commonly known as the Decken's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by small, globose berries that are usually red when mature. Its leaves are ovate to elliptic in shape, with a rough texture and a distinct, aromatic scent.

Solanum deckenii is often found in open woodland and grassland habitats, thriving in warm, humid climates.
