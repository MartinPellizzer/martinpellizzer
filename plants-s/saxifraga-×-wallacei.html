Saxifraga × wallacei

Saxifraga × wallacei, commonly known as the Wallisiean saxifrage, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Saxifragaceae family.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit and features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pink flowers during the summer months. It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and is adapted to rocky, well-drained soils.

Saxifraga × wallacei is often cultivated in rock gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
