Swartzia kaieteurensis

Swartzia kaieteurensis, commonly known as Kaieteure wood or Kaieteure rosewood, is a small to medium-sized tree native to central South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its dark, aromatic wood that is highly valued for furniture and musical instruments. The plant typically grows in humid lowland forests and has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets.

Its flowers are usually yellow or orange and are followed by elongated pods containing flat, kidney-shaped seeds.
