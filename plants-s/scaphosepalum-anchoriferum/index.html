Scaphosepalum Anchoriferum

Scaphosepalum anchoriferum is a rare and intriguing member of the Orchidaceae family. Native to the mountainous regions of South America, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its unique floral structure and striking coloration make it a subject of botanical interest. Despite its beauty, it remains under-studied and endangered due to habitat loss. This article explores its biology, habitat, and conservation status.
