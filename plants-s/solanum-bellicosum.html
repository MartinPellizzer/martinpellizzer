Solanum bellicosum

Solanum bellicosum, commonly known as the aggressive nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and dark green, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, white or purple flowers that bloom in clusters. Its berries, which are toxic, are typically yellow or orange when mature.

Solanum bellicosum is often found in disturbed habitats and can be invasive in certain regions.
