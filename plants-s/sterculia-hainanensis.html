Sterculia hainanensis

Sterculia hainanensis, commonly known as the Hainan sterculia, is a tropical tree native to China's Hainan Island.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by woody capsules containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
