Sideritis teucriifolia

Sideritis teucriifolia, commonly known as woolly ironweed, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its hairy, gray-green foliage and spikes of small, pale blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and is often found in mountainous areas. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs.

Sideritis teucriifolia is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
