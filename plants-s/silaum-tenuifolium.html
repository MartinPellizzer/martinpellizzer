Silaum tenuifolium

Silaum tenuifolium, commonly known as narrowleaf horsebean, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to regions of Europe and parts of Asia, often found in dry, rocky habitats. The plant has slender, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. Its flowers are typically pale yellow or greenish in color, and it is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.

Silaum tenuifolium is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
