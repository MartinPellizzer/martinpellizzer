Strophanthus dichotomus var. chinensis

Strophanthus dichotomus var. chinensis, commonly known as Chinese strophanthus, is a flowering plant native to China and parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height, characterized by its slender, often branched stems. The plant produces striking orange-red to yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and elliptical in shape, with a smooth surface and a slightly waxy texture.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential medicinal properties.
