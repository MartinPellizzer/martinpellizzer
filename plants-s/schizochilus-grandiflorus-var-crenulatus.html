Schizochilus grandiflorus var. crenulatus

Schizochilus grandiflorus var. crenulatus, commonly known as the crested flame orchid, is a striking tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or orange flowers with distinctive yellow lips and a unique crested structure on its petals. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, warm environments. Its foliage consists of long, narrow leaves that help it thrive in shaded, moist conditions.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
