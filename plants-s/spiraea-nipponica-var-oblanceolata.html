Spiraea nipponica var. oblanceolata

Spiraea nipponica var. oblanceolata, commonly known as the Japanese weeping spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features arching stems that produce clusters of small, white to pink flowers in early summer. The leaves are ovate to oblanceolate in shape, with a smooth or slightly serrated margin. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

This ornamental plant is valued for its graceful appearance and seasonal interest.
