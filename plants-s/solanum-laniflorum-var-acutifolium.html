Solanum laniflorum var. acutifolium

Solanum laniflorum var. acutifolium, commonly known as the woolly nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It features soft, hairy foliage and produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant has small, round berries that are typically yellow or red when ripe. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
