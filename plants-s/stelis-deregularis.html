Stelis deregularis

Stelis deregularis, commonly known as the irregular stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features clustered, linear leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture from the air and decaying organic matter.

Stelis deregularis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
