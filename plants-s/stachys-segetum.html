Stachys segetum

Stachys segetum, commonly known as field betony, is a flowering plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces spikes of blue or violet flowers, which bloom in summer. It is often found in meadows, fields, and along roadsides.

Stachys segetum is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
