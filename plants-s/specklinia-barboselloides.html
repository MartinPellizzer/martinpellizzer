Specklinia barboselloides

Specklinia barboselloides, commonly known as the Barbossa's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the rainforests of Ecuador.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid with a distinctive rosette-shaped growth habit and pale green to white foliage. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow with a contrasting lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
