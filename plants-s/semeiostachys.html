Semeiostachys

Semeiostachys, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to tropical Africa.

These plants are characterized by their upright stems and dense clusters of small, showy flowers. The leaves are typically velvety and heart-shaped, often with a deep green color. Semeiostachys species are popular houseplants due to their ornamental value and ease of care.

They thrive in bright, indirect light and require well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
