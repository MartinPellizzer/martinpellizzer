Sauvagesia setulosa

Sauvagesia setulosa, commonly known as the silky sauvagesia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, giving the plant a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various garden conditions.
