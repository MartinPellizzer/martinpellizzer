Schefflera manus-dei

Schefflera manus-dei, commonly known as the Manaus Schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like clusters of leaflets, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. Its leaves are typically glossy and dark green, with a leathery texture.

Schefflera manus-dei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
