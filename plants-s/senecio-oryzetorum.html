Senecio oryzetorum

Senecio oryzetorum, commonly known as the rice grass or riceflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in open, sunny environments and is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils. Senecio oryzetorum is also noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
