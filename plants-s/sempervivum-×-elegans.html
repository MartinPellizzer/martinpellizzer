Sempervivum × elegans

Sempervivum × elegans, commonly known as the 'Cape Hottentot' or 'Alpine Houseplant,' is a hybrid succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features rosettes of fleshy, spoon-shaped leaves that are typically green with red or purple margins. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in rocky, well-drained soils and full sun exposure. It produces clusters of star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, or purple during the summer months.

Sempervivum × elegans is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting due to its hardiness and low maintenance requirements.
