Senecio lucens

Senecio lucens, commonly known as the golden groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow ray florets and dark green, slightly hairy leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It produces small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in spring and summer.

Senecio lucens is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and ease of care.
