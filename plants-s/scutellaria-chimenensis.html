Scutellaria chimenensis

Scutellaria chimenensis, commonly known as the Chimen's skullcap, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. The plant features square stems, opposite leaves, and small, two-lipped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky slopes.

Scutellaria chimenensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
