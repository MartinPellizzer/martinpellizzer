Sonchus perennis

Sonchus perennis, commonly known as perennial sowthistle, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Europe, western Asia, and North Africa, and it has been introduced to other regions including North America. The plant has alternate, hairy, lobed leaves that are often bluish-green in color. It produces yellow flowers that are typically arranged in a composite head, surrounded by bracts.

Sonchus perennis is often found in disturbed soils, along roadsides, and in cultivated fields.
