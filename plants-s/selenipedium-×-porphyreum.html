Selenipedium × porphyreum

Selenipedium × porphyreum, commonly known as the Porphyry Cattail, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, erect inflorescence that resembles a cattail, with a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. The plant is native to Central and South America, where it thrives in wetland environments. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered on the spadix, typical of the Araceae family.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
