Syagrus inajai

Syagrus inajai, commonly known as the Inajai palm, is a species of palm native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in coastal areas with sandy soils. It produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into clustered fruits, which are an important food source for various wildlife.

Syagrus inajai is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for landscaping purposes.
