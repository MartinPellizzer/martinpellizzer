Solanum defensum

Solanum defensum, commonly known as the shieldleaf nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale purple, typically blooming in the evening. Its fruit is a yellow, fleshy berry that contains several seeds.

Solanum defensum is often found in shaded, moist environments and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
