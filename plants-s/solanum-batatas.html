Solanum batatas

Solanum batatas, commonly known as sweet potato, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its tuberous roots that are rich in carbohydrates. The plant typically has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, white or violet flowers. Its tubers come in a variety of colors, including orange, yellow, and purple, and are cultivated for their nutritional value.

Solanum batatas thrives in warm climates and is an important crop in many parts of the world.
