Sternbergia fischeriana subsp. hissarica

Sternbergia fischeriana subsp.

hissarica, commonly known as the Hissar Sternbergia, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of bell-shaped, yellow flowers during the spring season.

The plant thrives in well-drained soils and requires a cold winter dormancy period to bloom properly. It is often found in rocky or alpine habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens with suitable growing conditions.
