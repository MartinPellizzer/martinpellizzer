Statice cymulifera

Statice cymulifera, commonly known as sea lavender, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant thrives in saline and well-drained soils, often found in dunes and salt marshes. Its narrow, grass-like leaves are adapted to withstand harsh coastal conditions.

Statice cymulifera is widely cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in floral arrangements and as a decorative plant in gardens.
