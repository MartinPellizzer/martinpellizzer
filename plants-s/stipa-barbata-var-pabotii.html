Stipa barbata var. pabotii

Stipa barbata var. pabotii, commonly known as the Pabotii feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and fluffy, barbed seed heads that give it a distinctive, feathery appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as steppes and rocky slopes. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a blue-green hue when fresh.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in dry, well-drained soils.
