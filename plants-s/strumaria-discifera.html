Strumaria discifera

Strumaria discifera, commonly known as the disc-flowered strumaria, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its distinctive disc-shaped flower head. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in summer. Strumaria discifera is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
