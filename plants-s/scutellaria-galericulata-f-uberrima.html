Scutellaria galericulata f. uberrima

Scutellaria galericulata f. uberrima, commonly known as the broad-leaved scutellaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the mint family, Lamiaceae.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves and clusters of small, tubular blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It is native to parts of Europe and has been cultivated for its ornamental value.

The varietal form 'uberrima' is distinguished by its more robust and broader leaves compared to the typical form.
