Senecio linariifolius

Senecio linariifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which attract various pollinators.

Senecio linariifolius is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.
