Sedum palaestinum

Sedum palaestinum, commonly known as the Palestinian stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a hardy choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Sedum palaestinum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, supporting pollinators in its native habitat.
