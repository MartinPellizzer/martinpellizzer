Scutellaria litwinowii

Scutellaria litwinowii, commonly known as the Litwinow's skullcap, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with hairy surfaces and produces small, two-lipped flowers in shades of blue to purple. It typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in mountainous regions.

Scutellaria litwinowii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
