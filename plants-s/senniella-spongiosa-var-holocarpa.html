Senniella spongiosa var. holocarpa

Senniella spongiosa var. holocarpa, commonly known as the sponge tree, is a tropical plant native to parts of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spongy, and porous bark, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are typically alternate, simple, and have a rough texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
