Sloanea jamaicensis

Sloanea jamaicensis, commonly known as the Jamaican sloanea, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Caribbean, particularly Jamaica and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and smooth, greyish bark. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds.

It is often found in humid lowland forests and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
