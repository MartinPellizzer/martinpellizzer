Stelis cupuligera

Stelis cupuligera, commonly known as the helmet orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, helmet-shaped flower. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinctive, hood-like structure.

Stelis cupuligera thrives in shaded, moist conditions and is often found in mountainous regions.
