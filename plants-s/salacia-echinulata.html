Salacia echinulata

Salacia echinulata, commonly known as the spiny salacia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Salicaceae, which includes willows and poplars. The plant is characterized by its woody stems and small, clustered leaves. It produces yellow flowers that are followed by distinctive, spiny fruits.

Salacia echinulata is often found in moist, tropical forests and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
