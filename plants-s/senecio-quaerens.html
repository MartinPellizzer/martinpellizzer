Senecio quaerens

Senecio quaerens, commonly known as the searching senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Senecio quaerens is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and resilience in dry conditions.
