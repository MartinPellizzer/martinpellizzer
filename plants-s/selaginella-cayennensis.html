Selaginella cayennensis

Selaginella cayennensis, commonly known as the Cayenne spikemoss, is a species of spikemoss belonging to the family Selaginellaceae.

It is a small, herbaceous plant that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features closely packed, needle-like leaves that are usually green and sometimes reddish in color. Selaginella cayennensis reproduces via spores, and its sporophyte stage is characterized by a short, erect stem with clustered sporangia.

This plant is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, where it contributes to the biodiversity of its ecosystem.
