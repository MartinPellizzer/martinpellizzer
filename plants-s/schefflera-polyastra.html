Schefflera polyastra

Schefflera polyastra, commonly known as the mountain schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, clustered leaves that form a compact, umbrella-like shape. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. Schefflera polyastra thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental planting.
