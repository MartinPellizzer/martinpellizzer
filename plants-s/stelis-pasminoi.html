Stelis pasminoi

Stelis pasminoi, commonly known as the orchid of the genus Stelis, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pink and purple.

Stelis pasminoi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
