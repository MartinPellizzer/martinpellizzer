Scutellaria wuana

Scutellaria wuana, commonly known as the Chinese skullcap, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color, arranged in whorled clusters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly rough texture.

Scutellaria wuana is valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
