Smilax riparia subsp. ussuriensis

Smilax riparia subsp.

ussuriensis, commonly known as the Ussuri arrowleaf, is a climbing shrub native to eastern Asia. It features thin, woody stems with tendrils that allow it to climb and spread through vegetation.

The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically followed by spiny, three-lobed berries. Its leaves are arrow-shaped, with a pointed tip and a distinctive, serrated margin.

This subspecies is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and stream banks.
