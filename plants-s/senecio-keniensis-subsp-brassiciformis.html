Senecio keniensis subsp. brassiciformis

Senecio keniensis subsp.

brassiciformis, commonly known as the Kenyan senecio, is a flowering plant native to East Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers and linear, grass-like leaves.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and along riverbanks.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
