Sphyrospermum sessiliflorum

Sphyrospermum sessiliflorum, commonly known as the sessile-flowered sphaerosperm, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a leathery texture.

The species is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in arid conditions.
