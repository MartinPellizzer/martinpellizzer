Selaginella spirillum

Selaginella spirillum, commonly known as the spiral moss or zigzag spikemoss, is a member of the spikemoss family (Selaginellaceae).

It is a small, herbaceous plant that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or rocky crevices. The plant has a distinctive spiral arrangement of its leaves, which gives it its common name. Its life cycle includes both a gametophyte and a sporophyte stage, typical of vascular plants.

Selaginella spirillum is known for its ability to tolerate drought conditions, making it a resilient species in various habitats.
