Sedum sedoides subsp. ramosissimum

Sedum sedoides subsp.

ramosissimum, commonly known as the coral pine, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It features dense clusters of fleshy, blue-green leaves that form a compact rosette.

The plant produces vibrant orange to red flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
