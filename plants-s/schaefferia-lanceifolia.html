Schaefferia lanceifolia

Schaefferia lanceifolia, commonly known as the lanceleaf schaefferia, is a perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Schaefferia lanceifolia is also valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
