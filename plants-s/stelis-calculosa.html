Stelis calculosa

Stelis calculosa, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or limestone habitats, often on tree trunks or among rocks. The plant has a slender, upright stem with a few leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and green. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the mineral-rich environment.

Stelis calculosa is valued for its unique appearance and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
