Sedum oaxacanum

Sedum oaxacanum, commonly known as the Oaxacan stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant blooms make it an attractive addition to both outdoor and indoor landscapes.
