Spathiphyllum sagittifolium

Spathiphyllum sagittifolium, commonly known as the arrowleaf peace lily, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It features broad, arrow-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces elegant white spatulate flowers that resemble a peace sign, hence its common name. It thrives in low-light conditions and requires moderate watering, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its air-purifying qualities also contribute to its widespread cultivation and appreciation.
