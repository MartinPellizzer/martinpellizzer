Satureja × narentana

Satureja × narentana, commonly known as the Croatian savory, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its aromatic, small, oval-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional Mediterranean cuisine. The plant produces small, pale blue or purple flowers arranged in whorls along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or herb gardens.

Satureja × narentana is native to the Balkan Peninsula and is valued both for its culinary uses and ornamental appeal.
