Stachys japonica

Stachys japonica, commonly known as Japanese hedge nectar, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features hairy stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are clustered in dense spikes and are usually pink to purple in color.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
