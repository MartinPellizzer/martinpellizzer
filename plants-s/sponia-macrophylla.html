Sponia macrophylla

Sponia macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaved sponia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure.

Sponia macrophylla is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
