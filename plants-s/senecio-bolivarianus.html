Senecio bolivarianus

Senecio bolivarianus, commonly known as the Bolivian groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Bolivia and Peru.

Senecio bolivarianus is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
