Stelis molecula

Stelis molecula, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that grows on trees and rocks in tropical rainforests. The plant has delicate, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces tiny, fragrant blooms.

Stelis molecula is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
