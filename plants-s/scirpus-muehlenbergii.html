Scirpus muehlenbergii

Scirpus muehlenbergii, commonly known as Muhlenberg's bulrush, is a tall, herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, commonly referred to as the sedge family. This plant typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of ponds and lakes. Its tall, slender stems are often topped with clusters of small, spike-like flowers.
