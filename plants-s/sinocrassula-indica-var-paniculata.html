Sinocrassula indica var. paniculata

Sinocrassula indica var. paniculata, commonly known as the panicle sinocrassula, is a succulent plant native to China.

It features dense, compact growth with small, fleshy leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces delicate, branched inflorescences of tiny white flowers, which bloom in late spring or early summer. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its low maintenance requirements.

Its unique appearance and ability to thrive in well-drained soil make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
