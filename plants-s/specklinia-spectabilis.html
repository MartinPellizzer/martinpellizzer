Specklinia spectabilis

Specklinia spectabilis, commonly known as the showy specklinia, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant features slender, upright stems and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale green flowers with distinctive speckled markings. Its leaves are lanceolate and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Specklinia spectabilis thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
