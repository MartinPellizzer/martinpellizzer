Symplocos multipes

Symplocos multipes, commonly known as the clustered snowberry, is a deciduous shrub native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its small, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces red, fleshy berries that are edible for birds and small mammals. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a rough texture.
