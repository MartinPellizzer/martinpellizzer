Senecio flammeus

Senecio flammeus, commonly known as the flame flower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically blue-green in color. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Senecio flammeus is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant appearance and drought tolerance.
