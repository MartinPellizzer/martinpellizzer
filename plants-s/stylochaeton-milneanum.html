Stylochaeton milneanum

Stylochaeton milneanum, commonly known as the Malabar daisy, is a flowering plant native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are narrow and linear, and the plant is known for its ornamental value in horticulture.

It is also used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
