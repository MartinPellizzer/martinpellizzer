Silene mentagensis var. robusta

Silene mentagensis var. robusta, commonly known as the robust silene, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, pinkish to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils in arid or semi-arid regions. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, and it produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds.

This variety is notable for its resilience to harsh environmental conditions.
