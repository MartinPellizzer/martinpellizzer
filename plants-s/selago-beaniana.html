Selago beaniana

Selago beaniana, commonly known as the purple leafed selago, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive purple foliage and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are often aromatic and have a slightly hairy texture.

Selago beaniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique coloration and resilience.
