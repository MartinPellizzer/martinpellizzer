Solanum robustifrons

Solanum robustifrons, commonly known as the robust-fruited nightshade, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, peppers, and potatoes. The plant features simple, ovate leaves with a rough texture and produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. Its berries are typically yellow or orange when ripe, containing several seeds.

Solanum robustifrons is often found in humid, lowland areas and can grow up to several meters in height.
