Senecio helenitis

Senecio helenitis, commonly known as the Helen's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy, gray-green leaves and yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in mountainous regions and is adapted to dry, arid conditions.
