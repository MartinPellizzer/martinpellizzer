Statice sibthorpiana

Statice sibthorpiana, commonly known as sea lavender, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and spikes of small, showy blue or purple flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained, saline soils and is often found in dune environments. Its hardy nature makes it a popular choice for coastal gardens and landscaping.

Statice sibthorpiana is also valued for its use in floral arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms.
