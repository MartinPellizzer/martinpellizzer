Styrax loxensis

Styrax loxensis, commonly known as the Chinese styrax, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and opposite, ovate leaves that are dark green on top and pale underneath. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers in the spring, followed by small, woody fruits. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its visual interest.

The species is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
