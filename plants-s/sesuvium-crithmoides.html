Sesuvium crithmoides

Sesuvium crithmoides, commonly known as sea lavender, is a flowering plant native to coastal areas of the Mediterranean and parts of Africa.

The plant produces small, pink or purple flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
