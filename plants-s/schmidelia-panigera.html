Schmidelia panigera

Schmidelia panigera, commonly known as the golden sand mat, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, typically blooming in late spring to early summer.
