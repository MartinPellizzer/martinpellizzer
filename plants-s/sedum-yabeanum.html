Sedum yabeanum

Sedum yabeanum, commonly known as the Yabean sedum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often used in rock gardens or as a ground cover. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.

Its ability to store water in its leaves makes it drought-tolerant and suitable for xeriscaping.
