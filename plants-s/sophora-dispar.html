Sophora dispar

Sophora dispar, commonly known as the Chinese wisteria, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant produces fragrant, clustered flowers that are typically pale blue to purple in color. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can grow up to 10 meters in height.

The bark is smooth and grayish-brown, and the leaves are pinnately compound with ovate leaflets.
