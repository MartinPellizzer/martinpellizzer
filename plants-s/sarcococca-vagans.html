Sarcococca vagans

Sarcococca vagans, commonly known as the fragrant wintergreen, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features dense, dark green foliage that remains throughout the year, providing year-round interest in the garden. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers in late winter, followed by red berries that persist into spring. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens or as a ground cover.

Sarcococca vagans is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
