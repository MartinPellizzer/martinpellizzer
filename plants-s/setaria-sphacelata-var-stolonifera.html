Setaria sphacelata var. stolonifera

Setaria sphacelata var. stolonifera, commonly known as silver bluestem, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and distinctive, narrow leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, pale green to brown flowers. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a common species in prairie and open woodland habitats.

Silver bluestem is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in dry environments.
