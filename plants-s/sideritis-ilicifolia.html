Sideritis ilicifolia

Sideritis ilicifolia, commonly known as sweet cicely, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is known for its aromatic plants. The plant features slender, branching stems and small, opposite leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are typically pale blue or purple, arranged in whorls along the stems.

Sideritis ilicifolia is valued for its aromatic foliage and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
