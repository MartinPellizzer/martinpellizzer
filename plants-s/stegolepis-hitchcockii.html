Stegolepis hitchcockii

Stegolepis hitchcockii, commonly known as the Hitchcock's daisy.

It is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. The species belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves, and it produces showy yellow flowers with white ray florets.

Stegolepis hitchcockii is often found in rocky or sandy soils in arid environments.
