Stellaria brotheriana

Stellaria brotheriana, commonly known as the lesser starwort, is a low-growing flowering plant native to temperate regions of Europe and western Asia.

It typically thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and along stream banks. The plant features small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in early summer, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its delicate appearance.

Stellaria brotheriana is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family, known for its characteristic pink or white flowers and often hairy foliage.
