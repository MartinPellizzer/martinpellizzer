Swertia radiata var. maderensis

Swertia radiata var. maderensis, commonly known as the Mader's swertia, is a perennial herb native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its slender, branched stems and opposite, lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, typically arranged in racemes. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
