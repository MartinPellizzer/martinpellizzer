Securidaca ovalifolia f. microphylla

Securidaca ovalifolia f. microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved sandveld bean, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically less than 2 centimeters in length. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by long, slender pods containing several seeds. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

The species is noted for its adaptability to arid conditions and is used in traditional medicine.
