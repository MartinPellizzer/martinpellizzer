Stelis cyathiformis

Stelis cyathiformis, commonly known as the hooded orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its erect stem and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in a raceme, which are often covered by a hood-like structure. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Stelis cyathiformis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
