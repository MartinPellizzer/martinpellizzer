Stelis rabei

Stelis rabei, commonly known as the Rabei's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Stelis rabei is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
