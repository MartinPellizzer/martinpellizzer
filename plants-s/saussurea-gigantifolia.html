Saussurea gigantifolia

Saussurea gigantifolia, commonly known as the giant Himalayan yarrow, is a perennial herb native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its tall, branched stems that can reach up to 3 meters in height. The plant features large, gray-green, fern-like leaves and produces showy, yellow flower heads that bloom in summer. It thrives in cold, arid environments and is adapted to high-altitude conditions.

Saussurea gigantifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
