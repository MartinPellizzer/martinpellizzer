Sinocrassula Bergeri

Sinocrassula bergeri is a unique succulent native to the arid regions of China. This low-growing plant features compact, fleshy leaves that form a rosette shape. It is known for its ability to thrive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water. The plant's striking appearance makes it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts. Its resilience and beauty have led to increased interest in cultivating it in gardens and indoor spaces.
