Santolina Leucantha

Santolina leucantha is a hardy evergreen shrub known for its silver-gray foliage and compact growth habit. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. This plant is prized for its ornamental value and ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions. It is commonly used in rock gardens and as a border plant due to its resilience and low maintenance requirements. Santolina leucantha also attracts beneficial insects and adds a touch of elegance to any garden setting.
