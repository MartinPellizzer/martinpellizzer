Sinosenecio fanjingshanicus

Sinosenecio fanjingshanicus, commonly known as the Fanjingshan sinosenecio, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in the summer months.
