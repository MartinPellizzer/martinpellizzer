Strychnos cayennensis

It belongs to the family Loganiaceae and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces small, red berries that contain toxic seeds, which are rich in alkaloids such as strychnine and brucine. Its botanical features make it a notable species in the study of toxic plants and plant chemistry.
