Stachys algeriensis

Stachys algeriensis, commonly known as Algerian hedge nettle, is a flowering plant native to North Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or dry habitats. The plant features square stems and opposite leaves, which are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered in whorls, producing a distinctive appearance.

Stachys algeriensis is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
