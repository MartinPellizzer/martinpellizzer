Swertia lawii

Swertia lawii, commonly known as the Himalayan gentian, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows at high altitudes, where it thrives in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in a fine layer of white pubescence.

Swertia lawii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
