Saxifraga × akinfievii var. oettingenii

Saxifraga × akinfievii var. oettingenii, commonly known as the Oettingen saxifrage, is a hybrid plant species belonging to the Saxifragaceae family.

It is characterized by its rosette-forming growth habit and produces clusters of small, white or pinkish flowers during the summer months. Native to Central Asia, this plant thrives in rocky and alpine environments, often growing in well-drained soils. Its leaves are typically broad and slightly hairy, adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in cooler climates.
