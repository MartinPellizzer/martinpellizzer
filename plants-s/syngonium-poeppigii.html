Syngonium poeppigii

Syngonium poeppigii, commonly known as the arrowhead vine, is a tropical plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that often feature variegation, typically in shades of white, cream, and green. The plant produces small, pale green flowers that are not particularly showy, followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in humid environments with bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Syngonium poeppigii is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
