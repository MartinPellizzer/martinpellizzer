Schotia bergeri

Schotia bergeri, commonly known as the silver tree, is a distinctive evergreen shrub or small tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage, which gives it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in spherical inflorescences. Schotia bergeri thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.
