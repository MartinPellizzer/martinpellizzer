Senecio arboluco

Senecio arboluco, commonly known as the tree senecio, is a shrub-like plant native to South Africa.

It features woody stems that can grow up to several meters in height, giving it a tree-like appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the spring and summer seasons. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in a fine layer of white hairs, which helps reduce water loss.

Senecio arboluco is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and ability to thrive in dry conditions.
