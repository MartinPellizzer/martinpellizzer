Stachys kouyangensis var. tuberculata

Stachys kouyangensis var. tuberculata, commonly known as the Kouyang betony, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features dense, hairy foliage and produces spikes of pale blue to purple flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and ecological studies.
