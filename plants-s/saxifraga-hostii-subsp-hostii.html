Saxifraga hostii subsp. hostii

Saxifraga hostii subsp.

hostii, commonly known as the Alpine saxifrage, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It typically grows in rocky, alpine environments where it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

The plant produces showy, pink or white flowers in early summer, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are usually succulent, fleshy, and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water retention.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging garden conditions.
