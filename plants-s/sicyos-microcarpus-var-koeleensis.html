Sicyos microcarpus var. koeleensis

Sicyos microcarpus var. koeleensis, commonly known as the Koele's bottle gourd, is a variant of the bottle gourd species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a climbing vine with large, heart-shaped leaves and produces elongated, bottle-shaped fruits that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant thrives in warm climates with well-drained soil and requires full sun for optimal growth. Its fruits are typically harvested when immature for culinary use, though they can also be allowed to mature for ornamental purposes.

The plant is valued for its versatility in both agriculture and horticulture.
