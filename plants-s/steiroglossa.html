Steiroglossa

Steiroglossa, commonly known as the "dwarf orchid" or "bottle orchid," is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

The plants typically have a compact growth habit, with short, sturdy stems and small, strap-like leaves. Steiroglossa species are native to tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in Southeast Asia and the Pacific Islands.
