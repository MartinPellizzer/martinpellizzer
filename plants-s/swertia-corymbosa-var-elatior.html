Swertia corymbosa var. elatior

Swertia corymbosa var. elatior, commonly known as the Himalayan gentian, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky slopes, thriving in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged oppositely along the stem.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and has traditional medicinal uses in some regions.
