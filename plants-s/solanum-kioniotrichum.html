Solanum kioniotrichum

Solanum kioniotrichum, commonly known as the Kioniotrichum nightshade, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and peppers, and is characterized by its showy, often violet or blue flowers.
