Saussurea virescens

Saussurea virescens, commonly known as Greenhead Hawk's-bane, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems and large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant has rough, hairy leaves that are often grayish-green in color, and it thrives in alpine and subalpine environments. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal due to its striking appearance.
