Solanum rybinii var. boyacense

Solanum rybinii var. boyacense, commonly known as the Boyacense nightshade, is a species native to the Andean regions of Colombia.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its hairy stems and ovate, serrated leaves. The plant produces small, violet to purple flowers that are typically clustered in umbels. Its fruit is a berry that changes color from green to yellow as it matures.

This species is of interest in botanical studies due to its unique morphology and potential medicinal properties.
