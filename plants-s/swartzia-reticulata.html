Swartzia reticulata

Swartzia reticulata, commonly known as the reticulate swartzia, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in moist, shaded environments within tropical forests.
