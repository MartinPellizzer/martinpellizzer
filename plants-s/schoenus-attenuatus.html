Schoenus attenuatus

Schoenus attenuatus, commonly known as the slender bulrush, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It is typically found in wetland environments such as marshes and along the edges of lakes and rivers.

Schoenus attenuatus plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic and terrestrial organisms.
