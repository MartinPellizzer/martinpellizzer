Semecarpus merrillianus

Semecarpus merrillianus, commonly known as the Indian chestnut or horse chestnut, is a deciduous tree native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the family Sapindaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, yellowish-green flowers. The tree produces large, spiny fruits that contain a single, hard-shelled seed.
