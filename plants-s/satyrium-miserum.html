Satyrium miserum

Satyrium miserum, commonly known as the lesser satyr, is a species of orchid native to southern and eastern Africa.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid with a slender, leafless stem that produces a single spike of flowers. The flowers are typically pale yellow or greenish-white with a distinct, spurred lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and grasslands.

The plant is notable for its delicate appearance and its role in local ecosystems.
