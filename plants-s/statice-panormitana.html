Statice panormitana

Statice panormitana, commonly known as the Panormitana glassweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its erect, sparsely branched stems. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Statice panormitana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual interest.
