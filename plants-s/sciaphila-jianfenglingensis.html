Sciaphila jianfenglingensis

Sciaphila jianfenglingensis, commonly known as the Jianfengling ghost orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pale greenish-white flowers. This plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in mountainous regions with rich, moist soil. Its leaves are broad and leathery, adapted to conserve water in its natural habitat.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a critically endangered species.
