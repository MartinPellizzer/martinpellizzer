Solanum matthewsii

Solanum matthewsii, commonly known as the Matthews's nightshade, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that typically bloom in shades of purple or blue. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically yellow when mature. It is often found in rocky or sandy habitats and is adapted to arid conditions.

Solanum matthewsii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
