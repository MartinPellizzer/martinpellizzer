Senecio pungens

Senecio pungens, commonly known as the crown of thorns, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a distinctive rosette shape with thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the growing season, typically in spring or early summer. It is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

Senecio pungens is also known for its sharp spines, which serve as a defense mechanism against herbivores.
