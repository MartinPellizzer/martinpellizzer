Senecio ussanguensis

Senecio ussanguensis, commonly known as the Ussangua groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grassland and rocky habitats. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a cluster. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Senecio ussanguensis is characterized by its low-growing habit and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.
