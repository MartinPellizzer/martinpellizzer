Stelis simplicilabia

Stelis simplicilabia, commonly known as the simple-labied stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically has long, slender leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Stelis simplicilabia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
