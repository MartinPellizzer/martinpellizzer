Scutellaria taiwanensis

Scutellaria taiwanensis, commonly known as Taiwan skullcap, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
