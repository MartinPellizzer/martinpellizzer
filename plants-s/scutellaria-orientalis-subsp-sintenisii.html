Scutellaria orientalis subsp. sintenisii

Scutellaria orientalis subsp.

sintenisii, commonly known as the Eastern skullcap, is a perennial herb native to regions of Eastern Europe and Western Asia. It features square, hairy stems and opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges.

The plant produces small, tubular blue or violet flowers arranged in whorls along the stem. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species within the Scrophulariaceae family.
