Saxifraga aizoides f. aurantiaca

Saxifraga aizoides f. aurantiaca, commonly known as the orange-leaved saxifrage, is a compact perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming growth habit. The plant features striking orange-red leaves that contrast with its small, white or pinkish flowers. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal in rock gardens and alpine settings.
