Sophora albescens

Sophora albescens, commonly known as the white sophora, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy white or pale yellow flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.

The leaves and seeds contain various bioactive compounds, contributing to its medicinal significance.
