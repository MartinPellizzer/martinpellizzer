Stellera hedyotis

Stellera hedyotis, commonly known as the silverleaf stellera, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, woodlands or along stream banks, preferring partial to full shade. The plant features small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, arranged in clusters at the tips of the stems. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Stellera hedyotis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
