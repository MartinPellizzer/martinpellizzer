Svitramia minor

Svitramia minor, commonly known as the small green aphid, is a species of aphid belonging to the family Aphididae.

It is characterized by its small, green body and is typically found on the undersides of leaves. This plant pest feeds on the sap of various plants, particularly those in the grass family. Svitramia minor is often found in agricultural and garden settings where its host plants are abundant.

Its presence can lead to reduced plant growth and the spread of plant diseases.
