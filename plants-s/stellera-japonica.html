Stellera japonica

Stellera japonica, commonly known as Japanese starflower, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its star-shaped white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has alternate, ovate leaves with a hairy texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

Stellera japonica is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine.
