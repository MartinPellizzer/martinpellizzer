Sloanea myriandra

Sloanea myriandra, commonly known as the many-leaved sloanea, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy green foliage and can grow up to 30 meters in height. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by woody fruits containing several seeds. Sloanea myriandra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.

Its leaves are also used in traditional medicine by indigenous communities.
