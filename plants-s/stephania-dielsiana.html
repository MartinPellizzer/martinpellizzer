Stephania dielsiana

Stephania dielsiana, commonly known as the Chinese bittersweet, is a climbing plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Menispermaceae and is characterized by its woody stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by orange-red berries. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for various ailments.
