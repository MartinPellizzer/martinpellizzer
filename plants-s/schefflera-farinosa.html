Schefflera farinosa

Schefflera farinosa, commonly known as the starchy schefflera, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of leaves, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant has smooth, dark green foliage with a slightly waxy texture. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by yellowish berries.

Schefflera farinosa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
