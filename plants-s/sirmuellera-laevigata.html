Sirmuellera laevigata

Sirmuellera laevigata, commonly known as the smooth sirmuellera, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows in high-altitude environments, often in rocky or mountainous terrain. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pale pink to white in color. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a smooth, leathery texture.

Sirmuellera laevigata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable climatic conditions.
