Sarothra japonica f. robusta

Sarothra japonica f. robusta, commonly known as the Japanese spurge, is a variant of the Japanese spurge plant.

It is a succulent perennial that features thick, fleshy leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant is native to Japan and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It is known for its ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions.

Its ornamental value comes from its striking foliage and resilience in various climates.
