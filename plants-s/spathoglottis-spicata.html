Spathoglottis spicata

Spathoglottis spicata, commonly known as the spiky orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, spiky inflorescence and vibrant, often yellow or orange flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments such as forests and wetlands. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in tropical climates.

Spathoglottis spicata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
