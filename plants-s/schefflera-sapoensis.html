Schefflera sapoensis

Schefflera sapoensis, commonly known as the African umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to central and eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like canopy of large, leathery, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by bright red, berry-like fruits. Schefflera sapoensis thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
