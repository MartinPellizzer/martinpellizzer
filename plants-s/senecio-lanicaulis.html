Senecio lanicaulis

Senecio lanicaulis, commonly known as the woolly senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy composite flowers. The plant features dense, woolly foliage that helps it retain moisture in its arid habitat. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Senecio lanicaulis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
