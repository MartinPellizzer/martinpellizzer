Swertia pinifolia

Swertia pinifolia, commonly known as the pineleaf gentian, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in cool and moist conditions. Its leaves are narrow and needle-like, resembling pine needles, which is reflected in its common name.

Swertia pinifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
