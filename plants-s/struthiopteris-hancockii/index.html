Struthiopteris Hancockii

Struthiopteris hancockii is a rare and unique fern species native to the western United States. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as mountainous regions and forest understories. This plant is characterized by its distinctive fronds and intricate spore structures. Due to its limited habitat, it is considered endangered and faces threats from habitat loss and climate change. Studying Struthiopteris hancockii provides valuable insights into the biodiversity and conservation needs of rare plant species.
