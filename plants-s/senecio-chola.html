Senecio chola

Senecio chola, commonly known as the Chola groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are usually gray-green with a rough texture, and it produces small yellow flowers arranged in clusters.

Senecio chola is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
