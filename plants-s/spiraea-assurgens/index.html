Spiraea Assurgens

Spiraea assurgens is a deciduous shrub known for its delicate, lace-like foliage and showy flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, this plant thrives in cool, moist environments. It is often found in rocky soils and alpine meadows, where it forms dense thickets. The plant's unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Its ecological importance supports a variety of pollinators and wildlife.
