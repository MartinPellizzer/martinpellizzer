Salacca stolonifera

Salacca stolonifera, commonly known as the Chinese fan palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, fan-shaped leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or green in color. Its stolons, or horizontal stems, allow it to spread and form dense clusters.

Salacca stolonifera is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can thrive in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil.
