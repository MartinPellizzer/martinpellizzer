Schoenorchis brevirachis

Schoenorchis brevirachis, commonly known as the short-stemmed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a short, thick rhizome and a cluster of fleshy, linear leaves. The plant produces a single, erect stem bearing a few showy, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive hooded lip. It thrives in shaded, moist habitats and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique and sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
