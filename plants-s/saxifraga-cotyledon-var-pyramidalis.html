Saxifraga cotyledon var. pyramidalis

Saxifraga cotyledon var. pyramidalis, commonly known as the pyramidal saxifrage, is a succulent plant native to rocky environments in southern Europe.

It features fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge, especially under stress or in full sun. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and unique leaf shape make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
