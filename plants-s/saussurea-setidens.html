Saussurea setidens

Saussurea setidens, commonly known as the Chinese yarrow, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and dense clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant has feathery, aromatic leaves that are often used in traditional Chinese medicine. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ecological and medicinal purposes.
