Synaedrys luzoniensis

Synaedrys luzoniensis, commonly known as the Luzon climbing fern, is a species of fern native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its long, slender fronds that often climb or trail along the ground. The plant features a complex rhizome system that allows it to spread and establish itself in various habitats. Its leaves are typically bipinnate, with small, finger-like segments that give it a delicate appearance.

Synaedrys luzoniensis thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.
