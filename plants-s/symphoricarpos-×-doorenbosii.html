Symphoricarpos × doorenbosii

Symphoricarpos × doorenbosii, commonly known as the hybrid snowberry, is a deciduous shrub native to the western United States.

Its ornamental value comes from its showy flowers and colorful fruits, which contribute to its ecological and aesthetic appeal.
