Senecio dentatoalatus

Senecio dentatoalatus, commonly known as the toothed and broad-leaved groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features narrow, toothed leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Senecio dentatoalatus is often used in rock gardens due to its hardy nature and ornamental value.
