Scutellaria polymorpha

Scutellaria polymorpha, commonly known as the variable skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features upright, branched stems and lance-shaped leaves with hairy surfaces. Its flowers are blue to purple, tubular, and clustered in whorls along the stem.

Scutellaria polymorpha is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
