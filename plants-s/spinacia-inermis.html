Spinacia inermis

Spinacia inermis, commonly known as broadleaf spinach, is a leafy green plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are smooth and tender. The plant thrives in cool climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. It is rich in nutrients such as iron, vitamins A and C, and fiber.

Spinacia inermis is often cultivated for its edible leaves, which can be used in salads, soups, and other culinary dishes.
