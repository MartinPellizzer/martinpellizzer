Senecio campanulatus var. glabratus

Senecio campanulatus var. glabratus, commonly known as the smooth bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces showy, bell-shaped yellow flowers. The leaves are typically smooth and lance-shaped, with a somewhat succulent texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
