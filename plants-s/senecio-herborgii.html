Senecio herborgii

Senecio herborgii, commonly known as Herborg's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. It features narrow, linear leaves and produces small yellow flowers arranged in a cluster.
