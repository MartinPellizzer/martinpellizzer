Sinocrassula ambigua

Sinocrassula ambigua, commonly known as the ambiguous stonecrop, is a low-growing succulent native to China.

It features dense, compact rosettes of fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and white during the spring or early summer. It is well adapted to rocky and arid environments, thriving in full sun and well-draining soil.

Sinocrassula ambigua is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its hardiness and ornamental appeal.
