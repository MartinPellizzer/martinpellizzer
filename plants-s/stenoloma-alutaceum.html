Stenoloma alutaceum

Stenoloma alutaceum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf marsh grass, is a species of flowering plant in the family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets.
