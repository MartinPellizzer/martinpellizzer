Saussurea glandulosissima

Saussurea glandulosissima, commonly known as the woolly gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of blue to purple flowers. The plant has hairy, glandular leaves that give it its common name. It thrives in alpine and subalpine environments, often growing at high elevations.

Saussurea glandulosissima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
