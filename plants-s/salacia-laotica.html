Salacia laotica

Salacia laotica, commonly known as the Laotian salacia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly Laos and Vietnam.

It belongs to the Salaceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Salacia laotica is also valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
