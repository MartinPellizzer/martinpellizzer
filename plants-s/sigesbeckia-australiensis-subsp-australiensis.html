Sigesbeckia australiensis subsp. australiensis

Sigesbeckia australiensis subsp.

australiensis, commonly known as the Australian sigesbeckia, is a flowering plant native to Australia. It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils.

The plant features small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, often attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
