Satyrium leptopetalum

Satyrium leptopetalum, commonly known as the slender-petaled orchid, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid with delicate, pale green to white flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has a short, erect stem with few leaves and produces a single, slender inflorescence. Its flowers are characterized by their narrow petals and sepals, which are often adorned with subtle markings.

Satyrium leptopetalum is typically found in moist, shaded habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
