Styrax confusus var. superbus

Styrax confusus var. superbus, commonly known as the Chinese frankincense tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its glossy, ovate leaves and clusters of fragrant, white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces a resinous sap, which has been historically used in traditional medicine and incense. Its ornamental value is enhanced by its attractive foliage and showy flowers.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a suitable choice for garden landscapes.
