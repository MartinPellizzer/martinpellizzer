Schoenoplectiella × juncohotarui

Schoenoplectiella × juncohotarui, commonly known as the Japanese marsh reed, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems and dense clusters of small, greenish flowers. This plant typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes and along the edges of ponds. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread vegetatively, forming dense stands.

The plant plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic and terrestrial organisms.
