Satureja maritima

Satureja maritima, commonly known as coastal sagebrush, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region and parts of California.

It is a low-growing shrub that thrives in coastal and sandy soils, often found in coastal scrub ecosystems. The plant produces small, aromatic leaves and pink or white flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.

Satureja maritima is also an important component of the local flora, supporting various pollinators and wildlife.
