Scandix aucheri

Scandix aucheri, commonly known as the Persian shield, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Iran.

It features striking, velvety foliage with deep green veins and colorful, metallic-blue or purple markings. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for shade gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique leaf patterns and compact growth habit.
