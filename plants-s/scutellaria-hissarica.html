Scutellaria hissarica

Scutellaria hissarica, commonly known as Persian shield, is a herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. The plant is characterized by its striking, shield-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and purple markings. It produces small, tubular flowers in shades of blue and purple, typically blooming in late summer.

Scutellaria hissarica is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
