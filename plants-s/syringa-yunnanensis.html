Syringa yunnanensis

Syringa yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan lilac, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Syringa yunnanensis is also valued for its ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
