Schefflera pullei

Schefflera pullei, commonly known as the Philippine umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like crown of large, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red, fleshy berries that are edible for birds but toxic to humans. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
