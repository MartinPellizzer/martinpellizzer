Specklinia lewisiae

Specklinia lewisiae, commonly known as the Lewisi's orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to the high altitudes of the Andes in Peru.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and strikingly colored flowers, often featuring a combination of purple, pink, and white hues. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous environments, where it thrives in cool, humid conditions. Its leaves are usually elongated and grass-like, while its flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences.
