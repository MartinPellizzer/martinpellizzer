Stewartia yunnanensis

Stewartia yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan stewartia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Theaceae family and is characterized by its ornamental value, particularly its showy white to pink flowers. The tree has opposite, simple, ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, often with shallow fissures.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal and is relatively hardy in temperate climates.
