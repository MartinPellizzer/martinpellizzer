Suregada stenophylla

Suregada stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leafed suregada, is a perennial herb native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which is characterized by its composite flower heads and often aromatic foliage. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or along stream banks. Its narrow, lance-shaped leaves are covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
