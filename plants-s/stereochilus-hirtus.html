Stereochilus hirtus

Stereochilus hirtus, commonly known as the hairy root plant, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that are typically yellow or white in color. It is native to regions of southern Africa, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
