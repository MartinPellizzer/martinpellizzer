Swertia purpurascens var. ramosa

Swertia purpurascens var. ramosa, commonly known as the purple gentian, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is a member of the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking violet-blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged oppositely along the stem.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional medicinal uses in some regions.
