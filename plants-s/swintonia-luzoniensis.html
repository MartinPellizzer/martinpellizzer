Swintonia luzoniensis

Swintonia luzoniensis, commonly known as the Luzon starling tree, is a tropical tree native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by bright red, fleshy fruits. Swintonia luzoniensis is often found in lowland and montane rainforests, thriving in humid, shaded environments.

Its unique appearance and ecological role make it an important component of its native ecosystem.
