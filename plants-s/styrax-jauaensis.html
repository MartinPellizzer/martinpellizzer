Styrax jauaensis

Styrax jauaensis, commonly known as the Jauaena styrax, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Styracaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a leathery texture with a glossy appearance.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
