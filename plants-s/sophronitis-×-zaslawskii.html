Sophronitis × zaslawskii

Sophronitis × zaslawskii, commonly known as the Zaslawskii orchid, is a hybrid orchid species resulting from the cross between Sophronitis censata and Sophronitis rothschildiana.

It is native to the cloud forests of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces large, showy flowers with intricate patterns and a strong, spicy fragrance. Its pseudobulbs are thick and fleshy, adapted for storing water in its natural habitat.

Sophronitis × zaslawskii is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
