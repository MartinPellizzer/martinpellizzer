Senecio pamplonensis

Senecio pamplonensis, commonly known as the Pamplona groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Spain.

Senecio pamplonensis is often found in open, sunny habitats and is adapted to withstand cold and dry conditions.
