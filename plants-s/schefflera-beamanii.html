Schefflera beamanii

Schefflera beamanii, commonly known as the Beaman's umbrella plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the island of Guam.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of large, leathery, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.

Due to its ornamental value and unique foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor and outdoor landscaping.
