Saussurea uniflora var. uniflora

Saussurea uniflora var. uniflora, commonly known as the single-flower saussurea, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and a single, showy flower at the top, which typically blooms in late summer. The plant has dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in cold, arid environments and is adapted to withstand strong winds and low temperatures.

The flower is often pale yellow to cream in color, with a distinctive, elongated shape.
