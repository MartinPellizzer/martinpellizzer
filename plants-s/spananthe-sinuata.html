Spananthe sinuata

Spananthe sinuata, commonly known as the Sinuous Spananthe, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its slender, sinuous stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often marked with distinctive veining. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
