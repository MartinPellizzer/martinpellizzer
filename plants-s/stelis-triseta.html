Stelis triseta

Stelis triseta, commonly known as the three-branched stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in the Philippines and Indonesia. The plant has a compact, pseudobulbous structure with narrow, linear leaves. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, often in shades of white or yellow.

Stelis triseta thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, airy substrates for optimal growth.
