Saviniona reticulata

Saviniona reticulata, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a perennial flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It features striking red, spider-like flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are broad and often have a slightly wavy margin. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its dramatic foliage and unique floral display.
