Selaginella deflexa

Selaginella deflexa, commonly known as the flexible spikemoss, is a species of spikemoss belonging to the family Selaginellaceae.

It is a small, evergreen plant that typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features a creeping stem with whorled leaves that are often curved or twisted, giving it a distinctive appearance. Selaginella deflexa reproduces via spores rather than seeds, and it is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ability to tolerate low light and varying moisture levels makes it a resilient and adaptable species.
