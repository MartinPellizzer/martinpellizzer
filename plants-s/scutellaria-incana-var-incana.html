Scutellaria incana var. incana

Scutellaria incana var. incana, commonly known as blue skullcap, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. The plant features slender, upright stems covered in fine, silver-gray hairs. Its small, tubular blue or violet flowers appear in whorls along the stem, attracting various pollinators.

The leaves are lance-shaped and also covered in a dense layer of fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
