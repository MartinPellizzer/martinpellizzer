Sanguisorba officinalis var. carnea

Sanguisorba officinalis var. carnea, commonly known as crimson sanguisorba, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to eastern Asia.

It features tall, upright stems that bear clusters of dense, rose-purple to crimson bracts, which are not flowers but modified leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in panicles during the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or wetland habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
