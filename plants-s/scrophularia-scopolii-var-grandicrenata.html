Scrophularia scopolii var. grandicrenata

Scrophularia scopolii var. grandicrenata, commonly known as the large-crested scrophularia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or dry soils, often found in open woodlands and grasslands. The plant features slender stems that can reach up to 1 meter in height, adorned with opposite, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are tubular and usually pink or purple, blooming in late spring to early summer.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its distinctive foliage and blooms.
