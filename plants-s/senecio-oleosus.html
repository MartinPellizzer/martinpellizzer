Senecio oleosus

Senecio oleosus, commonly known as the yellow owl's eye, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces bright yellow ray florets surrounding a central disk of smaller, tubular flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Senecio oleosus is also noted for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
