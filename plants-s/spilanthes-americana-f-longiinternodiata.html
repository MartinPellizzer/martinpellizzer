Spilanthes americana f. longiinternodiata

Spilanthes americana f. longiinternodiata, commonly known as the long internode spilanthes, is a variant of the American spilanthes, characterized by its elongated internodes.

It is a herbaceous plant native to North America, typically found in dry, open habitats such as prairies and rocky soils. The plant features a branched stem with small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a rough, hairy texture.

This plant is notable for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
