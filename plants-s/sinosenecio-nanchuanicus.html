Sinosenecio nanchuanicus

Sinosenecio nanchuanicus, commonly known as the Nanchuan mountain sinosenecio, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous areas, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are small and clustered.
