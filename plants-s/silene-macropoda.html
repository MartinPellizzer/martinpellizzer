Silene macropoda

Silene macropoda, commonly known as the broad-leafed catchfly, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its upright, branching stems and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers.
