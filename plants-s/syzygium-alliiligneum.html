Syzygium alliiligneum

Syzygium alliiligneum, commonly known as the yellow apple or yellow mangosteen, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. The fruit is typically small, round, and has a yellowish skin with a sweet, aromatic pulp. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for its edible fruit.

The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
