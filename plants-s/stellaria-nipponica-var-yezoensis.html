Stellaria nipponica var. yezoensis

Stellaria nipponica var. yezoensis, commonly known as Yezo starweed, is a flowering plant native to Japan, particularly found on the island of Hokkaido.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, white or pinkish star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky areas. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
