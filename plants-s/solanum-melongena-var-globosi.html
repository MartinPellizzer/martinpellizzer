Solanum melongena var. globosi

Solanum melongena var. globosi, commonly known as the globe eggplant, is a cultivar of the eggplant species characterized by its round, compact fruit shape.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is native to Asia, particularly India. The plant produces large, glossy purple berries that are typically harvested when fully mature. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and is often used in both culinary and ornamental applications.
