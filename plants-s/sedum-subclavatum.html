Sedum subclavatum

Sedum subclavatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its fleshy, narrow leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Sedum subclavatum is drought-tolerant and requires minimal maintenance once established.
