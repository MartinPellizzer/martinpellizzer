Sesbania macroptera

Sesbania macroptera, commonly known as the giant sesbania, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, trifoliate leaves and showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces long, slender pods containing several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. Sesbania macroptera is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it valuable in agricultural systems.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas or along roadsides.
