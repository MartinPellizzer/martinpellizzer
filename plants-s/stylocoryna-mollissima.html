Stylocoryna mollissima

Stylocoryna mollissima, commonly known as the silky bark tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark-brown bark that feels silky to the touch. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by fleshy, red berries.

Stylocoryna mollissima is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
