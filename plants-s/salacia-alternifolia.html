Salacia alternifolia

Salacia alternifolia, commonly known as the zigzag vine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive alternating leaf arrangement and slender, twisting stems. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its leaves are usually elliptical with a slightly waxy texture.

Salacia alternifolia is often found in moist, shaded environments within tropical forests.
