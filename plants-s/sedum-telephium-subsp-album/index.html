Sedum Telephium Subsp. Album

Sedum telephium subsp. album is a striking member of the stonecrop family known for its pale blue to white flowers. Native to rocky and coastal areas of Europe, this plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. Its compact growth habit makes it an ideal choice for rock gardens and borders. The plant's ability to withstand drought and harsh weather conditions adds to its appeal. With its elegant appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice among gardeners and landscapers.
