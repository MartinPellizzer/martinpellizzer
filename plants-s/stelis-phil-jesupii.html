Stelis phil-jesupii

Stelis phil-jesupii, commonly known as the Jesup's stelis, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stem and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on forest floors or among mossy substrates. Its flowers are usually pale green or white with a faintly colored lip, and it blooms during the rainy season.

Stelis phil-jesupii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
