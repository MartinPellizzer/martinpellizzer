Stenopetalum lineare var. canescens

Stenopetalum lineare var. canescens, commonly known as the silverleaf stenopetalum, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery pubescence, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or sandy environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to arid conditions.
