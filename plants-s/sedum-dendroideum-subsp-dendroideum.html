Sedum dendroideum subsp. dendroideum

Sedum dendroideum subsp.

dendroideum, commonly known as the tree sedum, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and fleshy, blue-green leaves that form a dense, shrub-like structure.

The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a landscape plant.

Its ability to store water in its leaves allows it to survive in arid environments with minimal care.
