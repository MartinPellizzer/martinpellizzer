Spiraeanthemum katakata

Spiraeanthemum katakata, commonly known as the Katakata spirea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pink flowers during the spring season. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped and have a slightly serrated edge.

Spiraeanthemum katakata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
