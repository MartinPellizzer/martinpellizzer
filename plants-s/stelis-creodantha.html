Stelis creodantha

Stelis creodantha, commonly known as the orchid of the genus Stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as forest understories. Its roots are epiphytic, meaning they can grow on other plants without parasitizing them.

Stelis creodantha is native to parts of Southeast Asia and is valued for its ornamental beauty in horticulture.
