Senecio klinghardtianus

Senecio klinghardtianus, commonly known as the Klein's ragwort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. It has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Senecio klinghardtianus is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional African medicine.
