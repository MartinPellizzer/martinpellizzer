Stellariopsis

Stellariopsis, commonly known as common mouse-ear or mouse-ear chickweed, is a low-growing perennial plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its prostrate or ascending stems and small, rounded leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. Stellariopsis thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

It is often found in temperate regions of Europe, Asia, and North America.
