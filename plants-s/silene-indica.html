Silene indica

Silene indica, commonly known as the Indian catchfly, is a flowering plant native to Asia, including regions of India and China.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Silene indica is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
