Stapelia variegata var. atrata

Stapelia variegata var. atrata, commonly known as the Black Ghost Stapelia, is a unique succulent native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black, velvety leaves that are often tinged with green or purple. The plant produces striking, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange with a strong, unpleasant odor. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its rarity and distinctive appearance, it is a sought-after specimen among succulent enthusiasts.
