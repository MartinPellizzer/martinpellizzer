Stelis petropolitana var. latifolia

Stelis petropolitana var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved stelis, is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of South America, particularly in Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and lance-shaped leaves that are broader than the typical variety. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pink.

This orchid thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
