Stellaria pterosperma

Stellaria pterosperma, commonly known as the hairy starwort, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Stellaria pterosperma is often found in rocky or clay soils and is valued for its ornamental qualities in gardens.
