Stipularia mollis

Stipularia mollis, commonly known as the silky stipple, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. Stipularia mollis prefers full sun to partial shade and is drought-tolerant once established.
