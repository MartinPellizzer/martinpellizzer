Syzygium fenicis

Syzygium fenicis, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a striking appearance. The plant belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is known for its rapid growth and adaptability to various soil types. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a waxy texture.

Syzygium fenicis is also valued for its ornamental use in landscaping and its ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.
