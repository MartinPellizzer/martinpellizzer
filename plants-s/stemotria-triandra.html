Stemotria triandra

Stemotria triandra, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, herbaceous stems and rosette-like arrangement of fleshy, dark green leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of violet, pink, and white, typically in spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Stemotria triandra is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
