Specklinia fornicata

Specklinia fornicata, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant has slender, wiry stems and produces delicate, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinct, fragrant scent. Its leaves are narrow and strap-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Specklinia fornicata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
