Statice ovalifolia var. major

Statice ovalifolia var. major, commonly known as the broadleaf statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and spikes of small, showy flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

The flowers are used in floral arrangements and have a long vase life.
