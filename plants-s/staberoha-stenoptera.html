Staberoha stenoptera

Staberoha stenoptera, commonly known as the narrow-leafed evergreen, is a perennial plant native to the humid regions of Southeast Asia.

It features lanceolate leaves that are typically a deep green with a smooth, waxy surface. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers during the spring and summer months. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.
