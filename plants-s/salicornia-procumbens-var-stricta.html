Salicornia procumbens var. stricta

Salicornia procumbens var. stricta, commonly known as sea beans or glassworts, is a halophytic plant adapted to saline environments.

It is a low-growing, succulent species with fleshy, cylindrical stems that store water and nutrients. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are scale-like and often reduced, contributing to its unique appearance.

It is commonly found in coastal areas, salt marshes, and other brackish habitats.
