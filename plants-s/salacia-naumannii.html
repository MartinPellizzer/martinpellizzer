Salacia naumannii

Salacia naumannii, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Salicaceae and is characterized by its long, pendulous clusters of yellow flowers. The plant is deciduous, shedding its leaves seasonally, and can grow as a small tree or shrub. Its bark is smooth and greyish, while its leaves are opposite and ovate in shape.

The flowers are pollinated by insects and produce small, round fruits that are edible but not widely cultivated.
