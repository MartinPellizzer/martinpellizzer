Siccobaccatus dolichospermaticus

Siccobaccatus dolichospermaticus, commonly known as the desert balm, is a perennial shrub native to arid regions of North America.

It features narrow, gray-green leaves that are adapted to reduce water loss in hot, dry climates. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming in late spring. Its root system is deep and extensive, allowing it to access groundwater in nutrient-poor soils.

Siccobaccatus dolichospermaticus plays a vital role in stabilizing desert soils and providing habitat for native pollinators.
