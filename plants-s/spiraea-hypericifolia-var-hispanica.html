Spiraea hypericifolia var. hispanica

Spiraea hypericifolia var. hispanica, commonly known as the Spanish spiraea, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to southern Europe.

It features upright, slender stems covered in small, oval-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers in early spring, which attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden borders and rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
