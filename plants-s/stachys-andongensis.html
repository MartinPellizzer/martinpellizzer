Stachys andongensis

Stachys andongensis, commonly known as the Andong betony, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces spikes of purple or blue flowers, which bloom in the summer months. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests.
