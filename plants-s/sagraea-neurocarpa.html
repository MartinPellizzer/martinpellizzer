Sagraea neurocarpa

Sagraea neurocarpa, commonly known as the African starwood, is a small evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces round, woody fruits that contain several smooth, black seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
