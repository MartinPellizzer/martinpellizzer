Styrax japonicus var. nervillosus

Styrax japonicus var. nervillosus, commonly known as the Japanese styrax, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Japan.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with prominent veins, giving it a distinctive appearance. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from its showy flowers and attractive foliage.
