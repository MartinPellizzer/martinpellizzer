Scariola orientalis subsp. nuristanica

Scariola orientalis subsp.

nuristanica, commonly known as the Nuristan bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of eastern Afghanistan and northern Pakistan. It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer.
