Senecio barbareifolius

Senecio barbareifolius, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and showy, violet-colored flowers that bloom in spring. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Senecio barbareifolius is often cultivated as a popular ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.
