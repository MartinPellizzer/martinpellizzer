Scorzoneroides microcephala

Scorzoneroides microcephala, commonly known as the small-headed scorzonera, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

The small, yellowish flowers are arranged in a dense cluster, and the plant produces small, oval-shaped fruits known as cypsela.
