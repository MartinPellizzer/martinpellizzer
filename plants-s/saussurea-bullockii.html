Saussurea bullockii

Saussurea bullockii, commonly known as the Himalayan fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in rocky and arid environments, thriving at elevations between 2,500 and 5,000 meters. Its leaves are narrow and grayish-green, adapted to minimize water loss in harsh conditions.

The flowers, which bloom in summer, are yellow with a central disk, and the plant is known for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
