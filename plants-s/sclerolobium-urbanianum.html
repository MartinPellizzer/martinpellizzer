Sclerolobium urbanianum

Sclerolobium urbanianum, commonly known as the African bristlepod, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its woody stems and compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by cylindrical pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

Sclerolobium urbanianum is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showing resilience to drought conditions.
