Solanum schizandrum

Solanum schizandrum, commonly known as the Chinese nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to China.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and showy, bell-shaped flowers. The plant produces small, dark berries that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland or shaded areas.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
