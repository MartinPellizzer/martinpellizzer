Stelis dupliciformis

Stelis dupliciformis, commonly known as the double-form orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Thailand and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged in two rows along the stem.

Stelis dupliciformis thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil for optimal growth.
