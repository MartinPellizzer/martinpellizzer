Stellilabium atropurpureum

Stellilabium atropurpureum, commonly known as the purple-stemmed star-labium, is a striking member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple stems and striking, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture, adding to its ornamental appeal. Native to tropical regions, it thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a bold, exotic addition to their landscapes.
