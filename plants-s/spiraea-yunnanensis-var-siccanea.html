Spiraea yunnanensis var. siccanea

Spiraea yunnanensis var. siccanea, commonly known as the Yunnan spiraea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green in color and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pink flowers in early summer, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability in various garden settings.
