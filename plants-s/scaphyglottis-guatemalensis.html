Scaphyglottis guatemalensis

Scaphyglottis guatemalensis, commonly known as the Guatemalan scaphyglottis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial herb with a short, stocky rhizome and produces showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments in Central America, particularly in Guatemala. Its inflorescence is a raceme of resupinate flowers, and the sepals and petals are often similar in size and shape.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
