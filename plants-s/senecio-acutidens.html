Senecio acutidens

Senecio acutidens, commonly known as the sharp-leaved groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Senecio acutidens produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
