Sedum stenopetalum

Sedum stenopetalum, commonly known as the narrow-petaled stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to arid and rocky environments in southern Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, clustered leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and low maintenance requirements make it well-suited for rock gardens and containers.
