Smilax herbacea var. oblonga

Smilax herbacea var. oblonga, commonly known as the oblong leaf smilax, is a perennial vine native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its woody stems, which are covered in sharp, curved spines. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by red, berry-like fruits. Its leaves are typically oblong or elliptic in shape, with a glossy surface.

This species is often found in moist, wooded areas and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
