Swertia randaiensis

Swertia randaiensis, commonly known as the Japanese gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its leaves are opposite, lanceolate, and often covered with fine hairs.

Swertia randaiensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
