Sersalisia laurifolia

Sersalisia laurifolia, commonly known as the laurel leaf sersalisia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves that are arranged oppositely along the stems. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by small, round fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Sersalisia laurifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
