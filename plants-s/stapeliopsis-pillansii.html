Stapeliopsis pillansii

Stapeliopsis pillansii, commonly known as the Pillans's stapeliopsis, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, branching stems and waxy leaves. The plant produces striking, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xerophytic gardens.

Its unique appearance and ability to thrive in harsh conditions make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
