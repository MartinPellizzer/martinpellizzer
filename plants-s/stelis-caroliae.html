Stelis caroliae

Stelis caroliae, commonly known as the Caroline stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in tropical rainforests. The plant has a compact growth habit with narrow, linear leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in humid environments with indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Stelis caroliae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
