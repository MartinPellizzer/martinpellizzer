Schizachne Striata

Schizachne striata is a unique grass species known for its striking appearance and ecological significance. Native to tropical regions of South America, it thrives in wetland and marsh environments. Its narrow, linear leaves are often marked with distinct stripes, giving it a visually appealing look. This plant plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife. Due to its adaptability, it is also gaining attention for potential use in restoration projects.
