Schefflera gracilis

Schefflera gracilis, commonly known as the slender umbrella tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, feather-like leaves that form a compact, umbrella-shaped canopy. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters in its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually dark green and glossy, with a smooth texture.

Schefflera gracilis is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
