Stemonurus oblongifolius

Stemonurus oblongifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaf stemonurus, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, hairy texture. Its small, greenish-white flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom during the spring and early summer.

Stemonurus oblongifolius is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
