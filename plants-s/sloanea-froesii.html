Sloanea froesii

Sloanea froesii, commonly known as the Brazilian sloanea, is a small evergreen tree native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape.
