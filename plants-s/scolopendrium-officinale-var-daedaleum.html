Scolopendrium officinale var. daedaleum

Scolopendrium officinale var. daedaleum, commonly known as the noble fern, is a variant of the common holly fern.

It is a perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and parts of Asia. The plant features a distinctive, branched frond structure that gives it a unique, lace-like appearance. Its leaves are typically dark green and can grow up to several feet in length.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.
