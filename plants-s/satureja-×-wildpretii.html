Satureja × wildpretii

Satureja × wildpretii, commonly known as wild thyme, is a hybrid shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, blooming in summer. Its leaves are fragrant and often used in culinary applications, similar to other members of the thyme genus.

Satureja × wildpretii thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy and ornamental plant in gardens.
