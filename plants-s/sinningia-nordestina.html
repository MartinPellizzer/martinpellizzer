Sinningia nordestina

Sinningia nordestina, commonly known as the northeastern gloxinia, is a species of flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its tuberous roots and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant typically produces vibrant purple or pink blooms during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
