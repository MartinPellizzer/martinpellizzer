Serratula heterophylla subsp. nudicaulis

Serratula heterophylla subsp.

nudicaulis, commonly known as the nakedstalk sowthistle, is a herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in sunny environments.

The plant has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a loose, branched inflorescence.

It is known for its rapid growth and ability to colonize disturbed areas.
