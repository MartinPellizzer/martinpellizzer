Sedum sinicum

Sedum sinicum, commonly known as Chinese stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to China.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow or white. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Sedum sinicum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
