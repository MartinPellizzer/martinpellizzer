Schizachyrium hirtiflorum

Schizachyrium hirtiflorum, commonly known as hairyflower little bluestem, is a native grass species found in the eastern and central United States.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and its distinctive, hairy flower clusters that appear in late summer. This plant typically grows in full sun and thrives in well-drained soils, making it a common sight in prairies and open woodlands.
