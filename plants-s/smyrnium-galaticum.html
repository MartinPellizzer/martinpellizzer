Smyrnium galaticum

Smyrnium galaticum, commonly known as the galactic smyrnium, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feather-like leaves that resemble those of other umbelliferous plants. Due to its unique appearance, it is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens.
