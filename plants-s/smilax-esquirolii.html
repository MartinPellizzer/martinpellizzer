Smilax esquirolii

Smilax esquirolii, commonly known as the Florida smilax, is a perennial vine native to the southeastern United States.

It features thorned stems and heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into red berries, which are an important food source for wildlife. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forests and swamps.

Smilax esquirolii is characterized by its robust growth and ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent understory plant.
