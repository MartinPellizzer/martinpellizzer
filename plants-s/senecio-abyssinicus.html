Senecio abyssinicus

Senecio abyssinicus, commonly known as the Abyssinian groundsel, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its silver-gray foliage and yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in high-altitude regions.

Senecio abyssinicus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in arid gardens.
