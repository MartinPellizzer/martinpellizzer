Silene nardifolia

Silene nardifolia, commonly known as the alpine sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of up to 20 centimeters. The plant features small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, adapted to thrive in rocky or sandy soils.

Silene nardifolia is often found in alpine meadows and is well-suited to cold, high-altitude environments.
