Saxifraga × prossenii

Saxifraga × prossenii, commonly known as the Prossen's saxifrage, is a hybrid plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink, blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Saxifraga × prossenii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
