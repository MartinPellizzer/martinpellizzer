Stizolobium macrobotrys

Stizolobium macrobotrys, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red flowers that bloom in large clusters, creating a dramatic visual impact. The plant has bipinnate leaves composed of numerous small leaflets, and its trunk is often marked with distinctive fissures. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental species in warm climates.

The African flame tree is also valued for its rapid growth and ability to provide shade in landscaping.
