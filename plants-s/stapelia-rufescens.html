Stapelia rufescens

Stapelia rufescens, commonly known as the Red Stem Stapelia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed stems that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces striking, lantern-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. These flowers are adapted for pollination by flies and other insects.

Stapelia rufescens is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.
