Stevia velutinella

Stevia velutinella, commonly known as velvet leaf stevia, is a perennial herb native to South America.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its hairy, velvety leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and can reach heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves contain steviol glycosides, which are natural sweeteners used in food and beverages.

Stevia velutinella is valued for its medicinal properties and is also used in traditional herbal remedies.
