Saxifraga nishidae

Saxifraga nishidae, commonly known as the Nishida's saxifrage, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Saxifragaceae and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually white or pink, and it blooms during the summer months.

Saxifraga nishidae is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
