Spiesia caudata

Spiesia caudata, commonly known as the silky bristlegrass, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial grass that typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands. The plant features slender, erect stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs, giving it a silky appearance.

Spiesia caudata is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in horticulture.
