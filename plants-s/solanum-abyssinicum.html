Solanum abyssinicum

Solanum abyssinicum, commonly known as Abyssinian nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by red, fleshy berries. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with a slightly hairy texture.

Solanum abyssinicum is often found in arid and semi-arid regions, adapting well to harsh environmental conditions.
