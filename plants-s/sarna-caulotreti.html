Sarna caulotreti

Sarna caulotreti, commonly known as the tropical orchid, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful blooms that often resemble intricate patterns. The plant has a slender, upright stem with broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. Sarna caulotreti thrives in humid environments with high light exposure and well-draining soil.

Its unique floral structure makes it a popular choice for both ornamental gardening and botanical research.
