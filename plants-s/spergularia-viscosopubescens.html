Spergularia viscosopubescens

Spergularia viscosopubescens, commonly known as sticky pubescent sandwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its upright, herbaceous growth habit and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in early spring. It is commonly found in sandy or gravelly soils in arid or semi-arid environments.
