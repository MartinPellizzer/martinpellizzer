Scirpus thwaitesii

Scirpus thwaitesii, commonly known as the narrowleaf bulrush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland environments in North America.

Scirpus thwaitesii plays an important ecological role in wetland ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
