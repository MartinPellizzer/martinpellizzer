Solanum sassafrideum

Solanum sassafrideum, commonly known as the purple passionflower, is a climbing vine native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from purple to pink. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a velvety texture.

Solanum sassafrideum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
