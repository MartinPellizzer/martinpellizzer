Sphaeradenia davidsoniae

Sphaeradenia davidsoniae, commonly known as the Davidson's star tortoise, is a rare and critically endangered species native to the arid regions of southern India.

It is characterized by its distinctive, domed shell with a unique pattern of yellow and black markings. The plant is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal rainfall. Its survival is threatened by habitat loss and illegal trade.

Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its continued existence in the wild.
