Stelis hymenopetala

Stelis hymenopetala, commonly known as the lacey stelis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, linear leaves and striking, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in the understory of rainforests. Its flowers are highly ornamental, making it a popular choice for botanical gardens and conservation efforts.

Stelis hymenopetala plays an important role in its ecosystem by providing nectar for various pollinators.
