Solanum corifolium

Solanum corifolium, commonly known as the velvetleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves and clusters of small, star-shaped white flowers. The plant produces small, spherical berries that turn from green to red as they mature. It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like tomatoes and peppers.

Solanum corifolium is often found in disturbed soils and can be invasive in certain regions.
