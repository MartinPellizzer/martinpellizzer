Stellaria iinumae

Stellaria iinumae, commonly known as the Japanese starwort, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, star-shaped white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a hairy texture.

Stellaria iinumae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
