Solidago tenuifolia var. pycnocephala

Solidago tenuifolia var. pycnocephala, commonly known as narrowleaf goldenrod, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as wet meadows, marshes, and along stream banks. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, bright yellow flowers in late summer. It is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes many other flowering plants.

This variant is distinguished by its dense, compact flower heads compared to other goldenrod species.
