Stemodiacra subhastata

Stemodiacra subhastata, commonly known as the red-stemmed orchid, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive red-colored stems. The plant typically grows in humid, tropical environments and thrives in shaded or semi-shaded conditions. Its flowers are small and clustered, often displaying a mix of white and yellow hues.

Stemodiacra subhastata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
