Symphyotrichum patens var. patens

Symphyotrichum patens var. patens, commonly known as New England aster, is a flowering plant native to eastern North America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring stiff, upright stems covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, composite flower heads in shades of purple, pink, or white, with ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often have a rough texture with serrated edges.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and natural habitats.
