Spiraea laevigata var. tianschanica

Spiraea laevigata var. tianschanica, commonly known as the Tianshan spiraea, is a deciduous shrub native to Central Asia.

It features slender, arching stems that produce clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers in early summer. The leaves are oval-shaped, with a smooth texture and a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in well-drained soils and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
