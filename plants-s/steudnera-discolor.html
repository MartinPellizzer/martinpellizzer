Steudnera discolor

Steudnera discolor, commonly known as the Variegated Sword Plant, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its broad, dark green leaves with distinctive white or yellow variegation patterns. The plant typically grows as an upright, herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its leaves are often described as sword-shaped, giving it a dramatic and architectural appearance.

Steudnera discolor prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
