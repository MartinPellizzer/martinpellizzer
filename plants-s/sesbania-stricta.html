Sesbania stricta

Sesbania stricta, commonly known as the narrowleaf sesbania, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a legume belonging to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it valuable in soil enrichment. Its seeds are often used as a protein source for livestock.

Sesbania stricta is also cultivated for its ornamental value and in agroforestry systems.
