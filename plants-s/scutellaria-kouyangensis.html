Scutellaria kouyangensis

Scutellaria kouyangensis, commonly known as the Kouyang skullcap, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
