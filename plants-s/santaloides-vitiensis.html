Santaloides vitiensis

Santaloides vitiensis, commonly known as the Vitiensis Santol, is a hemiparasitic plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It belongs to the Santalaceae family and has a unique root system that allows it to absorb water and nutrients from its host plants. The plant has small, simple leaves and produces small, white or greenish flowers. It is often found in tropical rainforests, growing on the roots of other trees.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the ecological balance in its native habitat.
