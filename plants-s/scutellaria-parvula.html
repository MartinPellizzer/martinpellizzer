Scutellaria parvula

Scutellaria parvula, commonly known as small blue skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant produces small, tubular blue or violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and has lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture.

Scutellaria parvula is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
