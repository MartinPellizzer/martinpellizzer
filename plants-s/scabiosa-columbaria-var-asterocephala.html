Scabiosa columbaria var. asterocephala

Scabiosa columbaria var. asterocephala, commonly known as the starhead scabious, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers with a distinctive star-shaped appearance. The plant features finely divided, fern-like leaves that are often aromatic. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is noted for its long blooming period, typically from late spring to early autumn.
