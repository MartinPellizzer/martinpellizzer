Solanum medians f. neoweberbaueri

Solanum medians f. neoweberbaueri, commonly known as the narrow-leaved nightshade, is a variant of the Solanum medians species.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its slender stems and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, solitary flowers that are typically white or pale purple in color. Its fruit is a rounded berry that changes from green to red as it matures.

This plant is native to regions of South America and is often found in moist, shaded environments.
