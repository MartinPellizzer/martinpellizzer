Stellaria paniculigera

Stellaria paniculigera, commonly known as the panicled starwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, white or pink flowers that are arranged in panicle-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, grassy areas and is found in temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs.

Stellaria paniculigera is primarily a herbaceous perennial that thrives in partial shade to full sun.
