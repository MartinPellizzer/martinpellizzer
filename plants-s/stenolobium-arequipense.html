Stenolobium arequipense

Stenolobium arequipense, commonly known as the Arequipa acacia, is a shrub native to the highlands of southern Peru.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, exhibiting drought resistance through its deep root system. Its flowers are typically yellow or greenish-yellow, arranged in racemose inflorescences.

The species is of interest for its potential in ecological restoration and as a source of natural compounds.
