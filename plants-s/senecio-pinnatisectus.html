Senecio pinnatisectus

Senecio pinnatisectus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, daisy-like yellow flowers and finely divided, pinnatisected leaves that give it a feathery appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with composite flower heads.
