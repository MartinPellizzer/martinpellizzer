Senna Hebecarpa Var. Longipila

Senna hebecarpa var. longipila is a lesser-known yet fascinating plant native to parts of eastern Asia. It belongs to the legume family and is recognized for its striking yellow flowers and compound leaves. This variant is particularly noted for its elongated leaflets, which set it apart from other senna species. Though not widely cultivated, it holds ecological significance in its native habitats. Its unique features make it an intriguing subject for botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
