Solanum claviforme

Solanum claviforme, commonly known as the red tomato bush, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, branching structure and small, oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers that develop into bright red, berry-like fruits. Solanum claviforme belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, which includes other familiar plants like tomatoes and peppers.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and edible fruits.
