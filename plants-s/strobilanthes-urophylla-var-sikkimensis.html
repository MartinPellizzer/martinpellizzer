Strobilanthes urophylla var. sikkimensis

Strobilanthes urophylla var. sikkimensis, commonly known as the Sikkim flameflower, is a flowering plant native to the eastern Himalayas.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking red or orange tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest areas at elevations between 1,500 and 3,000 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered with a fine, silky hair.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
