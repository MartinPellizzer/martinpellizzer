Skimmia japonica var. kwangsiensis

Skimmia japonica var. kwangsiensis, commonly known as the Chinese skimmia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to China.

It features glossy, dark green elliptical leaves that provide a dense, lush appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers in early spring, which are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its year-round foliage and seasonal flowering.
