Sedum indicum var. densirosulatum

Sedum indicum var. densirosulatum, commonly known as the dense-leaved stonecrop, is a succulent plant characterized by its compact growth habit and fleshy, dark green leaves.

It produces clusters of star-shaped, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding ornamental value to gardens. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun conditions. The plant is drought-tolerant and requires minimal maintenance, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both horticultural and ecological landscapes.
