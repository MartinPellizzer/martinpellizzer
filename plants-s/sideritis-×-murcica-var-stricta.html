Sideritis × murcica var. stricta

Sideritis × murcica var. stricta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ironweed, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowering stems bear small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color.

Sideritis species are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
