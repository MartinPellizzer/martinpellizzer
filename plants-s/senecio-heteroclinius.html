Senecio heteroclinius

Senecio heteroclinius, commonly known as the African snowflake, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, bushy growth habit with fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white wax. The plant produces showy, daisy-like yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
