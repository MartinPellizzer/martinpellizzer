Stauropsis fasciata var. longifolia

Stauropsis fasciata var. longifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf stauropsis, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that bear opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive silver-white margin. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening for its striking color contrast.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
