Stelis gerontica

Stelis gerontica, commonly known as the old man's beard orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its long, hanging inflorescences that resemble a beard. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic growth habit. Its flowers are usually yellow or greenish-yellow, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

Stelis gerontica is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and adaptability to various growing conditions.
