Sclerorhachis rechingeri

Sclerorhachis rechingeri, commonly known as the Schomburgkia, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking inflorescences with a spadix and a spathe, typical of the arum family. It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil.

Sclerorhachis rechingeri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique foliage.
