Sinocrassula indica var. longistyla

Sinocrassula indica var. longistyla, commonly known as the long-stemmed stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to China.

It features compact, rosette-shaped foliage with thick, fleshy leaves that store water, typical of succulents. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
