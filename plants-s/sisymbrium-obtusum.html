Sisymbrium obtusum

Sisymbrium obtusum, commonly known as hoary salsify, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is an annual or biennial herb that typically grows in dry, disturbed soils. The plant features yellow flowers arranged in racemes and has pinnately divided leaves with a fuzzy, grayish appearance. Its seeds are small and capsule-like, containing several tiny seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Sisymbrium obtusum is often found in meadows, roadsides, and other open habitats.
