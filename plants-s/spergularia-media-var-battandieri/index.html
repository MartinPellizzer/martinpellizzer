Spergularia Media Var. Battandieri

Spergularia media var. battandieri is a lesser-known flowering plant that thrives in coastal environments. It is a variant of the common sea pink, known for its delicate pink flowers and salt-tolerant nature. This plant is often found in sandy or gravelly soils along the shores of the Mediterranean. Its unique adaptation to harsh coastal conditions makes it an important part of the local ecosystem. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated and rarely highlighted in botanical studies.
