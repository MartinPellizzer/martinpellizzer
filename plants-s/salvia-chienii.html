Salvia chienii

Salvia chienii, commonly known as the Chinese sage, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is known for its aromatic and often medicinal plants. The plant features square stems and opposite leaves that are often grayish-green in color. Its showy flowers, typically purple or blue, bloom in summer and attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Salvia chienii is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses in Chinese medicine.
