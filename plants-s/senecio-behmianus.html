Senecio behmianus

Senecio behmianus, commonly known as the Beihman's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid environments. It features narrow, linear leaves and produces small yellow flowers that attract pollinators.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
