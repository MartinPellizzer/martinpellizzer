Sedum rotundatum var. oblongatum

Sedum rotundatum var. oblongatum, commonly known as the round-leaf stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Europe.

It features fleshy, rounded leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tint. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
