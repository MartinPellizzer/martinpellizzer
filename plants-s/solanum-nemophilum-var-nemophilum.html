Solanum nemophilum var. nemophilum

Solanum nemophilum var. nemophilum, commonly known as the woodland nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial native to the temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other familiar plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant typically features small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple, often blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The fruit is a small, yellowish berry that matures in the fall, containing several seeds.
