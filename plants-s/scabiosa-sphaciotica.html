Scabiosa sphaciotica

Scabiosa sphaciotica, commonly known as the narrow-leaved scabious, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It is often found in dry, well-drained areas and is known for its ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Scabiosa sphaciotica is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
