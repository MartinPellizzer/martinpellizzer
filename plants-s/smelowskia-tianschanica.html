Smelowskia tianschanica

Smelowskia tianschanica, commonly known as the Tianshan bellflower, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple. Smelowskia tianschanica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
