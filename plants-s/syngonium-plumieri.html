Syngonium plumieri

Syngonium plumieri, commonly known as arrowhead vine, is a tropical perennial plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, arrowhead-shaped, or shield-shaped leaves that change color as the plant matures. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in humid, warm environments and is often grown as a houseplant or in shaded outdoor gardens.

Syngonium plumieri is also valued for its ability to purify air and its ornamental appeal.
