Solanum lycioides var. iodasterum

Solanum lycioides var. iodasterum, commonly known as the iodasterum nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and peppers. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are typically clustered in umbels. Its leaves are ovate with a slightly hairy texture, and it has a woody stem that becomes more branched with age.

The species is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
