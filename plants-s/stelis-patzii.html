Stelis patzii

Stelis patzii, commonly known as the Patazii orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its erect, leafy stems and clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in the undergrowth of tropical forests. Its flowers are usually small, fragrant, and come in shades of white, yellow, or green.

Stelis patzii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
