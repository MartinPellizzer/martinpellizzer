Specularia speculum-veneris var. calycina

Specularia speculum-veneris var. calycina, commonly known as the Venus' Looking Glass, is a striking member of the Boraginaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pale blue to violet, often with a white or pink throat. The plant produces hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in hue. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a sought-after addition to rock gardens and alpine displays.
