Sitanion × hansenii var. anomalum

Sitanion × hansenii var. anomalum, commonly known as the yellow bellflower, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow tubular flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun. It is native to the southeastern United States and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking colorful, native plants.
