Sedum platysepalum

Sedum platysepalum, commonly known as the flat-sepal sedum, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant's leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted for water storage, typical of the Crassulaceae family. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ability to survive in arid conditions makes it a hardy and low-maintenance plant.
