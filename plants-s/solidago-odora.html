Solidago odora

Solidago odora, commonly known as sweet goldenrod, is a flowering plant native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, bright yellow inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist soils along stream banks and wetlands. Its aromatic foliage and flowers attract various pollinators, including bees and butterflies.

Solidago odora is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological benefits in native plant gardens.
