Streptocarpus smithii

Streptocarpus smithii, commonly known as the Smith's streptocarpus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that typically bloom in shades of violet or purple. Streptocarpus smithii is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
