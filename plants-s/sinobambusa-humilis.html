Sinobambusa humilis

Sinobambusa humilis, commonly known as the dwarf bamboo, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its low-growing, clumping habit. The plant features narrow, elongated leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. Sinobambusa humilis is also valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small wildlife.
