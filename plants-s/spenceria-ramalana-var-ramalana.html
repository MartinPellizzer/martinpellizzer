Spenceria ramalana var. ramalana

Spenceria ramalana var. ramalana, commonly known as the Ramalana spider plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces delicate, pendulous inflorescences bearing small, white flowers. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Its compact growth habit makes it an ideal choice for rock gardens or container planting.
