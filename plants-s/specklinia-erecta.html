Specklinia erecta

Specklinia erecta, commonly known as the upright speckled orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, with a distinctive speckled appearance.

Specklinia erecta is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
