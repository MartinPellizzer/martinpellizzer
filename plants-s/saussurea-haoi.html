Saussurea haoi

Saussurea haoi, commonly known as the Hualing saussurea, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems and dense, woolly foliage. The plant produces yellow to brown composite flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
