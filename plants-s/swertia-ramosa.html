Swertia ramosa

Swertia ramosa, commonly known as the Himalayan gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in high-altitude grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in cool and moist environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Swertia ramosa is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
