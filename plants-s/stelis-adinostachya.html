Stelis adinostachya

Stelis adinostachya, commonly known as the Adinostachya orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often found in forest understories. The plant features a slender rhizome with several erect, linear leaves. Its flowers are clustered in a spike and are characterized by their pale green to yellowish coloration.

Stelis adinostachya is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
