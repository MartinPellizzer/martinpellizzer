Solanum stenotomum f. canastilla

Solanum stenotomum f. canastilla, commonly known as the Canary Island nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is a compact shrub characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically orange to red in color when mature. It is native to the Canary Islands and thrives in coastal and rocky environments.

Due to its ornamental value and potential toxicity, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens but requires careful handling.
