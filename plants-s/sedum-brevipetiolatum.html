Sedum brevipetiolatum

Sedum brevipetiolatum, commonly known as the short-petioled stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, rounded leaves that are typically green with a slight blue or silver hue. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped, yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its compact size and drought tolerance, it is well-suited for container gardening as well.
