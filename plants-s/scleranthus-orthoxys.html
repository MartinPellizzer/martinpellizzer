Scleranthus orthoxys

Scleranthus orthoxys, commonly known as the clustered thornweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, clustered flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and wastelands.

Scleranthus orthoxys is a herbaceous perennial that plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local pollinators.
