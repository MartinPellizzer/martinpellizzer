Scutellaria cyanocheila

Scutellaria cyanocheila, commonly known as the blue-lipped skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its striking blue-violet flowers and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Scutellaria cyanocheila is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
