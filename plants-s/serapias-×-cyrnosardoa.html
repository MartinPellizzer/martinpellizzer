Serapias × cyrnosardoa

Serapias × cyrnosardoa, commonly known as the hybrid orchidgrass, is a cross between Serapias and Cyrnosardoa species.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy, yellow-orange flowers with a distinctive hooded structure. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
