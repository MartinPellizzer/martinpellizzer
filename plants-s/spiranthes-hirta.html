Spiranthes hirta

Spiranthes hirta, commonly known as the hairy spire orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, spiraling spire of flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while the flowers are usually white or pale green with a faintly colored lip.

Spiranthes hirta is known for its unique floral structure and is often found in the eastern United States.
