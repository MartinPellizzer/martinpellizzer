Smilax binchuanensis

Smilax binchuanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan arrowroot or Chinese smilax, is a species of vine native to China.

It belongs to the family Smilacaceae and is characterized by its climbing habit and thorny stems. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by bright red berries that are edible. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped with a rough texture, and it thrives in shaded, moist environments.

Smilax binchuanensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and traditional medicinal uses.
