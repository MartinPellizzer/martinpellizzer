Stipa × kopetdaghensis

Stipa × kopetdaghensis, commonly known as the Kopetdag grass, is a hybrid grass species native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender culms and narrow, blade-like leaves. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in steppes and grasslands. Its inflorescence consists of a panicle with spikelets that are loosely arranged.

Stipa × kopetdaghensis is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing forage for livestock.
