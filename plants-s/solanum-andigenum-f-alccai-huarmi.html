Solanum andigenum f. alccai-huarmi

The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in cloud forests or rocky habitats at elevations above 3,000 meters. Its leaves are usually hairy and elliptical, while its fruits are small berries that contain toxic compounds typical of the Solanum genus.
