Smilax pallescens

Smilax pallescens, commonly known as the pale smilax, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the Smilacaceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and spiny growth. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in umbels. Its leaves are usually alternate, simple, and have a rough texture.
