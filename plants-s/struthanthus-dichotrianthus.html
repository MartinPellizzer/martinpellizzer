Struthanthus dichotrianthus

Struthanthus dichotrianthus, commonly known as the desert star.

It is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of South Africa. The plant produces striking yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are small and succulent, adapted to conserve water in dry environments.

Struthanthus dichotrianthus is often found growing in rocky soils and is well-suited to harsh, semi-desert conditions.
