Selinum phellandrium

Selinum phellandrium, commonly known as mountain savory, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, gray-green leaves with a pungent scent, and its small, yellowish flowers bloom in summer. The plant is often found in rocky or sandy soils, thriving in well-drained environments. It is valued for its medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects.

Selinum phellandrium is also used in traditional herbal remedies and as a culinary herb.
