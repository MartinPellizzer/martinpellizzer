Swertia javanica subsp. javanica

Swertia javanica subsp.

javanica, commonly known as the Java swertia, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions.

The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. Its flowers are often visited by bees and other pollinators, contributing to its ecological significance.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
