Scopolia carniolica

Scopolia carniolica, commonly known as the Carniolan bellflower, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink.
