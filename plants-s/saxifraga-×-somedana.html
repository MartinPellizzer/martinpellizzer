Saxifraga × somedana

Saxifraga × somedana, commonly known as the hybrid saxifrage, is a flowering plant that belongs to the Saxifragaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage, often found in rocky or alpine environments. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or red, which bloom in early summer. Its leaves are usually succulent and fleshy, adapted to retain moisture in harsh conditions.

Saxifraga × somedana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging garden settings.
