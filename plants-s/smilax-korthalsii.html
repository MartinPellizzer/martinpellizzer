Smilax korthalsii

Smilax korthalsii, commonly known as the Korthals's catbriar, is a perennial vine native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its woody stems and spiny, trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in umbels. Its fruits are berries that mature to a dark purple color, often containing several seeds.
