Sorghum caricosum

Sorghum caricosum, commonly known as the dwarf sorghum, is a shrub-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit, with short, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in poor, dry conditions.

Sorghum caricosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional landscaping.
