Stelis protracta

Stelis protracta, commonly known as the long-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on tree trunks or rocks. Its flowers are usually white or pale green, with a distinctive lip that is often spurred.

Stelis protracta is valued in horticulture for its elegant appearance and ease of cultivation.
