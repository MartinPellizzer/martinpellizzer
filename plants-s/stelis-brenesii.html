Stelis brenesii

Stelis brenesii, commonly known as the Brena's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is a sympodial epiphytic orchid, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic. The plant typically produces multiple pseudobulbs and possesses long, narrow leaves. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, often in shades of white, yellow, or green.

Stelis brenesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in humid, shaded environments.
