Saxifraga rizhaoshanensis

Saxifraga rizhaoshanensis, commonly known as the Rizhaoshan saxifrage, is a flowering plant species native to China.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming growth pattern. The plant typically produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white or pink during the summer months. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
