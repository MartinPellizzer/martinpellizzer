Stellera virescens

Stellera virescens, commonly known as the green starwort, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to eastern North America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features a basal rosette of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, white, and star-shaped, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems.

Stellera virescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local biodiversity.
