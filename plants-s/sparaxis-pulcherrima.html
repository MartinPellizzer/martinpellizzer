Sparaxis pulcherrima

Sparaxis pulcherrima, commonly known as the "Rainbow Sparaxis," is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

Sparaxis pulcherrima is also known for its ability to tolerate coastal conditions and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.
