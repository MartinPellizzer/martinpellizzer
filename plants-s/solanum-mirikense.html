Solanum mirikense

Solanum mirikense, commonly known as the Mirikense nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of East Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are ovate with a rough texture, and it produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters.

The fruit is a berry that turns from green to red as it matures, often containing several seeds.
