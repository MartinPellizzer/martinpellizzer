Stenophyllus tenuifolius

Stenophyllus tenuifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaf stenophyllus, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves that are typically less than 2 centimeters wide. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pale green to white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical or subtropical forests.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
