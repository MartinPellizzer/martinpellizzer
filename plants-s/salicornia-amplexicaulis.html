Salicornia amplexicaulis

Salicornia amplexicaulis, commonly known as sea beans or glassworts, is a halophyte plant adapted to saline environments.

The plant typically grows in coastal areas, salt marshes, and estuaries where it thrives in high-salinity conditions. Salicornia amplexicaulis plays an important ecological role in stabilizing coastal soils and providing habitat for various organisms.
