Stapelia × neglecta

Stapelia × neglecta, commonly known as the hybrid African milkwort, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed stems and distinctive, star-shaped flowers. The plant produces yellowish-green to orange flowers that emit a strong, unpleasant odor, which attracts pollinators like flies. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Stapelia × neglecta is often cultivated for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
