Saxifraga rivularis

Saxifraga rivularis, commonly known as the river saxifrage, is a perennial flowering plant native to northern regions of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, rocky habitats such as stream banks and mountainous areas. The plant features basal leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape, often with toothed margins. Its flowers are small, white or pale pink, and clustered in a loose inflorescence.

Saxifraga rivularis is adapted to cool, humid environments and is often found in alpine or subalpine regions.
