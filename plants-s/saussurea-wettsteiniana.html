Saussurea wettsteiniana

Saussurea wettsteiniana, commonly known as the Wettstein's yarrow, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Central Asia.

Saussurea wettsteiniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique appearance.
