Sarcophrynium leiogonium

Sarcophrynium leiogonium, commonly known as the smooth-leaved sarcophrynium, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green, heart-shaped leaves that are smooth to the touch. The plant produces striking inflorescences with white spadices and yellowish-green berries. It is native to tropical regions of Africa and thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated as a houseplant.
