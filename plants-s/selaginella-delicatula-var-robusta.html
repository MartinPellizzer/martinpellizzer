Selaginella delicatula var. robusta

Selaginella delicatula var. robusta, commonly known as the slender spikemoss, is a species of moss plant belonging to the family Selaginellaceae.

It is a small, terrestrial fern ally that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features delicate, linear leaves that are often arranged in whorls around the stems, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces spores in cone-like structures called strobila, which are borne on specialized branches.

This variety is noted for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
