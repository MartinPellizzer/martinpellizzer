Senecio nudiusculus

Senecio nudiusculus, commonly known as the naked stem senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. Its small yellow flowers appear in clusters and bloom during the warmer months of the year.
