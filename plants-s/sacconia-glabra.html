Sacconia glabra

Sacconia glabra, commonly known as the smooth sacconia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Malvaceae.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and heart-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
