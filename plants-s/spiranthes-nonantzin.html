Spiranthes nonantzin

Spiranthes nonantzin, commonly known as the nonantzinka or pink spirea, is a species of orchid native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its delicate, spiral-shaped inflorescences that consist of numerous small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wetlands. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces a fibrous root system.

Spiranthes nonantzin is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
