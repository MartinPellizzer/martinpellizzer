Senecio × kewensis

Senecio × kewensis, commonly known as the Kew's senecio, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, branched stems and large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Native to South Africa, this species is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking appearance. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice in gardens and botanical collections.
