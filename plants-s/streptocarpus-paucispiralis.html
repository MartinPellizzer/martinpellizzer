Streptocarpus paucispiralis

Streptocarpus paucispiralis, commonly known as the spiral-stem streptocarpus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

Streptocarpus paucispiralis prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
