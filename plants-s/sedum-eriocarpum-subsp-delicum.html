Sedum eriocarpum subsp. delicum

Sedum eriocarpum subsp.

delicum, commonly known as the Delicate Stonecrop, is a low-growing succulent plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink.

The leaves are typically blue-green, with a waxy coating that helps reduce water loss. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

This subspecies is noted for its ornamental value and ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
