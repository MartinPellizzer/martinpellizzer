Sisymbrium belangeri

Sisymbrium belangeri, commonly known as the California peppergrass, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other mustard plants. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by narrow, elongated pods containing seeds.
