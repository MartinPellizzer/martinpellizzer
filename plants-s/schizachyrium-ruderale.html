Schizachyrium ruderale

Schizachyrium ruderale, commonly known as eastern gamma grass, is a perennial grass native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are typically reddish or purplish in hue. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a suitable choice for native plant gardens.

Schizachyrium ruderale is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to support local wildlife.
