Streptanthus cutleri

Streptanthus cutleri, commonly known as the Sierra Nevada pincushion.

This plant is a member of the Brassicaceae family and is native to the Sierra Nevada mountains in California. It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and clusters of small, yellow flowers. Streptanthus cutleri typically grows in rocky soils at high elevations, often in open meadows or along streambanks.
