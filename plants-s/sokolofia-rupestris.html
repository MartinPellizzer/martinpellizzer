Sokolofia rupestris

Sokolofia rupestris, commonly known as the rock sokolofia, is a flowering plant native to rocky and arid regions.

It is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit and succulent leaves that store water. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its hardiness and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
