Saussurea chrysanthemoides

Saussurea chrysanthemoides, commonly known as the Chinese sandthorn, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine hairs to reduce water loss.

It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
