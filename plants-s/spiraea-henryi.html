Spiraea henryi

Spiraea henryi, commonly known as Henry's weeping spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features slender, arching branches that bear clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers in early summer. The plant has finely divided, feathery leaves that add to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to both landscape design and ecological restoration projects.
