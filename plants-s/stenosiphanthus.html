Stenosiphanthus

Stenosiphanthus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved orchid, is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, often fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors. Native to Southeast Asia, these plants typically thrive in humid, shaded environments within tropical forests. The leaves are usually narrow and strap-like, contributing to the plant's distinctive appearance.

Stenosiphanthus species are valued in horticulture for their ornamental value and ability to bloom reliably under proper care.
