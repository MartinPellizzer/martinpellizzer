Schismatoglottis hellwigiana var. subcordata

Schismatoglottis hellwigiana var. subcordata, commonly known as the heartleaf arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or silver markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Schismatoglottis hellwigiana var. subcordata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage.
