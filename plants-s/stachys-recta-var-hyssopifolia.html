Stachys recta var. hyssopifolia

Stachys recta var. hyssopifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf hedge nettle, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright stems covered in fine, soft hairs and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, pale purple to pink flowers in whorled clusters along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and ornamental species in garden settings.
