Swertia perfoliata

Swertia perfoliata, commonly known as the leafy gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers. The plant has opposite, often hairy leaves that are sometimes described as having a perfoliate arrangement, where the leaves appear to pass through the stems. Swertia perfoliata typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and visually appealing plant in horticultural settings.
