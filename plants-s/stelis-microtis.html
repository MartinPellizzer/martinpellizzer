Stelis microtis

Stelis microtis, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on tree trunks or branches in humid, tropical environments. The plant features a compact growth habit with narrow, strap-like leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Stelis microtis is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
