Statice insignis var. rossmaessleri

Statice insignis var. rossmaessleri, commonly known as the Desert Snapdragon, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and purple. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and is well adapted to dry, sandy soils. It blooms throughout the summer months, adding color to coastal and garden landscapes.

This variety is valued for its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal in xeriscaping.
