Senecio macrotus

Senecio macrotus, commonly known as the giant senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange composite flowers. Its flowers bloom in late summer to early autumn, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Senecio macrotus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to well-drained soils and full sun.
