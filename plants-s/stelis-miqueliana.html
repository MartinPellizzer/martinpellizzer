Stelis miqueliana

Stelis miqueliana, commonly known as the Miquel's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually small to medium in size, with a variety of colors ranging from white to pink and purple.

Stelis miqueliana thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, loose soil for optimal growth.
