Stipagrostis amabilis

Stipagrostis amabilis, commonly known as the beautiful grass, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences with narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or inland grasslands.

Due to its ornamental appeal, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
