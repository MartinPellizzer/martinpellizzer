Senecio silvascandens

Senecio silvascandens, commonly known as the mountain senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky, mountainous regions and prefers well-drained soils. Its leaves are usually gray-green with a fuzzy texture, and it produces small yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

Senecio silvascandens is often used in rock gardens due to its hardy nature and ornamental appeal.
