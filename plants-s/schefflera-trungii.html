Schefflera trungii

Schefflera trungii, commonly known as the Vietnamese umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of large, leathery, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden areas.

Schefflera trungii is valued for its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor conditions.
