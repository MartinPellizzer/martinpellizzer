Siphocampylus mollis

Siphocampylus mollis, commonly known as the slender milkweed, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It features upright, branched stems that are covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which attract various pollinators. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Siphocampylus mollis is adapted to dry, rocky habitats and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.
