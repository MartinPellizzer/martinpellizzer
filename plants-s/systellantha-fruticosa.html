Systellantha fruticosa

Systellantha fruticosa, commonly known as the coastal daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal areas, where it thrives in sandy soils and salty air. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom throughout the year. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, adapted to conserve water in arid conditions.

Systellantha fruticosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize coastal dunes.
