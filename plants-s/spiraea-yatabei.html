Spiraea yatabei

Spiraea yatabei, commonly known as the Japanese spiraea, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features slender, arching stems that produce clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers in early summer. The plant has finely cut, ovate leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

Spiraea yatabei is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
