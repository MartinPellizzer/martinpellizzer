Seriphidium tianschanicum

Seriphidium tianschanicum, commonly known as the Tian Shan wormwood, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

Its leaves are typically gray-green and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in its harsh habitat.
