Schumacheria raphanoides

Schumacheria raphanoides, commonly known as the Chinese mountain spinach, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features alternate, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its small, greenish-yellow flowers are arranged in umbels and bloom in the summer.

Schumacheria raphanoides is valued for its edible leaves, which are used in traditional Chinese cuisine.
