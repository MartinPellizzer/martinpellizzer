Stachys ramosissima var. elazigensis

Stachys ramosissima var. elazigensis, commonly known as Elazig hairy betony, is a perennial herb native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant features dense, hairy foliage and produces spikes of violet-blue flowers. It thrives in rocky or dry habitats and is adapted to Mediterranean climates.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
