Sitilias multicaulis

Sitilias multicaulis, commonly known as the mountain pincushion, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and vibrant purple to pink flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows. Its stems are often branched and covered in fine hairs, which help protect it from harsh weather conditions.

Sitilias multicaulis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable climates.
