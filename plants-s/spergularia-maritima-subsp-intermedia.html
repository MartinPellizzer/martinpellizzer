Spergularia maritima subsp. intermedia

Spergularia maritima subsp.

intermedia, commonly known as the intermediate sandspurry, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family. It is a low-growing perennial herb that thrives in coastal and sandy environments.

The plant produces small, pink or white flowers that bloom in summer, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous tiny seeds. Its leaves are typically narrow and linear, adapted to reduce water loss in arid conditions.

This subspecies is often found in dunes and salt marshes, showcasing its halophytic nature and ability to tolerate saline soils.
