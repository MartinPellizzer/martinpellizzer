Sedum orbiculatum

Sedum orbiculatum, commonly known as the roundleaf stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its fleshy, round leaves that are typically green or reddish in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Sedum orbiculatum is also known for its ability to survive in harsh conditions, including drought and poor soil.
