Senecio fistulosus var. fistulosus

Senecio fistulosus var. fistulosus, commonly known as the slender senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It has finely divided leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

This species is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and ability to thrive in well-drained conditions.
