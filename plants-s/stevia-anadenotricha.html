Stevia anadenotricha

Stevia anadenotricha, commonly known as the velvetleaf stevia, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant is known for its high concentration of steviol glycosides, which are natural sweeteners. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used traditionally for its calming and digestive benefits.
