Stelis jamesonii

Stelis jamesonii, commonly known as the Jameson's stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a pseudobulb and produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture from the air and rain.

Stelis jamesonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
