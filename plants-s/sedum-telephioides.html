Sedum telephioides

Sedum telephioides, commonly known as chalkweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a succulent species that thrives in rocky and sandy soils, often found in coastal and arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are fleshy and arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps it store water.

Sedum telephioides is well adapted to harsh conditions and is often used in xeriscaping due to its low maintenance requirements.
