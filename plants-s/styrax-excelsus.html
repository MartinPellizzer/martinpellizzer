Styrax excelsus

Styrax excelsus, commonly known as the Chinese frankincense tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Styracaceae and is characterized by its fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces a resinous sap that has been historically used in traditional medicine and incense. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a smooth texture, while its bark is smooth and reddish-brown.

Styrax excelsus is valued for its ornamental appeal and its historical significance in aromatic applications.
