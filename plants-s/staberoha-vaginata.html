Staberoha vaginata

Staberoha vaginata, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a tropical annual herb native to Central and South America. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a distinctive purple underside. It produces small, inconspicuous green flowers that develop into capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

Staberoha vaginata is often found in disturbed habitats and can become a weed in agricultural settings.
