Sedum tuberiferum

Sedum tuberiferum, commonly known as the tuberous sedum, is a succulent plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are typically green and slightly ribbed, adapting to reduce water loss.

Sedum tuberiferum is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and ornamental value.
