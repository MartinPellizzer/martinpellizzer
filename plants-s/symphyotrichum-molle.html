Symphyotrichum molle

Symphyotrichum molle, commonly known as silky aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and showy, purple to pink flowers that bloom in the fall. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodlands. It is a member of the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and has a bushy growth habit.

Silky aster is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
