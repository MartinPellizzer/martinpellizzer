Scutellaria albida subsp. velenovskyi

Scutellaria albida subsp.

velenovskyi, commonly known as the white skullcap, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by square stems and opposite leaves.

The plant produces small, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in late summer. It typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, preferring well-drained soils.

Its medicinal properties have been studied for potential use in traditional herbal remedies.
