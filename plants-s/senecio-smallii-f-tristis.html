Senecio smallii f. tristis

Senecio smallii f. tristis, commonly known as the silverleaf senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant is typically found in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. It is a low-growing perennial that forms dense mats, often used in rock gardens due to its ornamental appeal.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for xeriscaping in arid environments.
