Syringa Reticulata Var. Mandshurica

Syringa reticulata var. mandshurica is a striking ornamental shrub known for its fragrant, clustered flowers. Native to eastern Asia, it thrives in temperate climates and is prized for its vibrant blooms. This variety features a compact growth habit and is often used in gardens for its aesthetic appeal. Its leaves are dark green and provide a lush backdrop to its showy flowers. Despite its beauty, it requires well-drained soil and partial sun to flourish.
