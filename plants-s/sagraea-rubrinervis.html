Sagraea rubrinervis

Sagraea rubrinervis, commonly known as the red-veined sagraea, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking foliage, featuring deep green leaves with prominent red veins that add a vibrant visual contrast. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are usually elliptical or ovate in shape, with a smooth, waxy texture.

Sagraea rubrinervis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
