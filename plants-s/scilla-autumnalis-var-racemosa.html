Scilla autumnalis var. racemosa

Scilla autumnalis var. racemosa, commonly known as autumn squill, is a perennial bulbous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of bell-shaped, blue to purple flowers in the fall. The plant is adapted to well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade. It is often used in rock gardens and as a border plant due to its ornamental value.

Its foliage persists through winter, providing visual interest during the colder months.
