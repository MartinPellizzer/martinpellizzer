Senecio atkinsonii

Senecio atkinsonii, commonly known as Atkinson's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy habitats and is often found in coastal areas. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
