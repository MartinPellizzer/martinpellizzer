Schefflera junghuhniana

Schefflera junghuhniana, commonly known as the Hainan Schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to southern China and the island of Hainan.

It is characterized by its dense, compound leaves that are arranged in a distinctive umbrella-like pattern, giving it a unique visual appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by dark blue berries that are edible for some bird species. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.

Schefflera junghuhniana is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
