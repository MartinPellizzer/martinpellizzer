Scrophularia nodosa var. viridiflora

Scrophularia nodosa var. viridiflora, commonly known as green-flowered figwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in woodland edges and meadows. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are distinctive, with a greenish-yellow color and two lips, typical of the Scrophulariaceae family.

It produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, elliptical seeds.
