Schkuhria integrifolia var. oblongifolia

Schkuhria integrifolia var. oblongifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf yarrow, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It features basal leaves that are broadly ovate to elliptic in shape, with a smooth, uninterrupted margin. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in flat-topped clusters, typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in dry grasslands and rocky areas.
