Schedonorus

Schedonorus, commonly known as bentgrass, is a genus of grasses native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

These plants typically grow in low, dense tufts and are often found in moist, shaded environments such as lawns, meadows, and along stream banks. Schedonorus species are characterized by their fine, soft blades and compact growth habit, which make them popular in turfgrass applications.
