Silene fedtschenkoi

Silene fedtschenkoi, commonly known as the Fedtschenko's campion, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its erect stems and showy pink or white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are alternate and lance-shaped, often covered in fine hairs.
