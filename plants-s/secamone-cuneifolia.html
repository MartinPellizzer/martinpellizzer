Secamone cuneifolia

Secamone cuneifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf secamone, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, often with a red calyx. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
