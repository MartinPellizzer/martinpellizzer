Stipa smithii

Stipa smithii, commonly known as Smith's feathergrass, is a perennial grass native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and delicate, feathery inflorescences that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Stipa smithii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native plant gardens.
