Stephanostegia hildebrandtii

Stephanostegia hildebrandtii, commonly known as the African star grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a dense basal cluster. The plant produces small, white to pale blue flowers arranged in a star-like pattern, which gives it its common name. It is typically found in grasslands and along riverbanks, thriving in well-drained soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping in suitable climates.
