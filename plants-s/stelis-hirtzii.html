Stelis hirtzii

Stelis hirtzii, commonly known as the hairy stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are usually covered in fine, hairy structures, which contribute to its common name.

Stelis hirtzii is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
