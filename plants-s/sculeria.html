Sculeria

Sculeria, commonly known as the common sculeria, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its composite flower heads, which consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plants typically have narrow, grass-like leaves and are often found in open, sunny habitats. Sculeria species are native to various regions across Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

They are generally low-growing and can thrive in a variety of soil types.
