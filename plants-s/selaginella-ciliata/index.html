Selaginella Ciliata

Selaginella ciliata, commonly known as the basket moss, is a unique and ancient plant that has thrived for millions of years. This species belongs to the family Selaginellaceae and is often found in moist, shaded environments across various regions. Its delicate, feather-like fronds give it a distinctive appearance, making it a popular choice for terrariums and indoor gardens. Despite its small size, Selaginella ciliata plays an important role in ecosystems by contributing to soil stability and providing habitat for microorganisms. Its resilience and adaptability make it a fascinating subject for both botanical study and horticultural appreciation.
