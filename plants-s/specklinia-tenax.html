Specklinia tenax

Specklinia tenax, commonly known as the hardy orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome and produces a single, erect stem that bears several lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in a raceme, which are often fragrant. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Specklinia tenax is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in cultivation.
