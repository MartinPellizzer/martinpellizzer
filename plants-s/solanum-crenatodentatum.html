Solanum crenatodentatum

Solanum crenatodentatum, commonly known as the wavy-toothed nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features compound leaves with serrated, wavy edges, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, white or greenish flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are berries that mature to a yellow or orange color, often containing toxic compounds.

Solanum crenatodentatum is part of the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes.
