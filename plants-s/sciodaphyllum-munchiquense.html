Sciodaphyllum munchiquense

Sciodaphyllum munchiquense, commonly known as the Munchique cloud forest tree, is a rare evergreen species native to the cloud forests of Colombia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and can reach heights of up to 20 meters. The plant has a slow growth rate and thrives in high humidity and cool temperatures. It plays a crucial role in maintaining the biodiversity of its ecosystem.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to survive.
