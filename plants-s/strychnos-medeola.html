Strychnos medeola

Strychnos medeola, commonly known as the purple star or purple starling, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are often purple or white in color. Its leaves are simple and opposite, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.
