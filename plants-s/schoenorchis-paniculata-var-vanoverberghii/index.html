Schoenorchis Paniculata Var. Vanoverberghii

Schoenorchis paniculata var. vanoverberghii is a rare and exquisite orchid native to the montane regions of Southeast Asia. This unique variant is distinguished by its delicate, pendulous flowers and vibrant coloration that sets it apart from other members of the species. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires careful cultivation to flourish. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in the wild. Its beauty and rarity make it a prized addition to specialized botanical collections.
