Stachys taliensis

Stachys taliensis, commonly known as the Taliensis hedge nettle, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, particularly China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its leaves are hairy and have a distinctive, slightly fuzzy texture.
