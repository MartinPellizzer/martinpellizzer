Stevia schreiteri

Stevia schreiteri, commonly known as the red stevia, is a perennial shrub native to Brazil.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant produces sweet-tasting leaves that contain high levels of steviol glycosides, making it a natural sweetener. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Stevia schreiteri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of natural sweetener.
