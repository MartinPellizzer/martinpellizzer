Stelis vulpecula

Stelis vulpecula, commonly known as the fox fur orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees in tropical rainforests. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, resembling a fox's fur. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Stelis vulpecula is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or conservatories.
