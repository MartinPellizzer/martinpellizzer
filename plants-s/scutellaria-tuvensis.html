Scutellaria tuvensis

Scutellaria tuvensis, commonly known as the Tuven's skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants such as mint and sage. The plant is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Scutellaria tuvensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
