Sophora donihuensis

Sophora donihuensis, commonly known as the Chinese wisteria, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, pendulous flowers. The plant produces long, cylindrical pods that contain several seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can thrive in well-drained soil with full sun.

However, it requires careful management due to its vigorous growth habit.
