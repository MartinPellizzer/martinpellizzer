Sebaea amicorum

Sebaea amicorum, commonly known as the friendship vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and showy, tubular flowers. The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that are typically red or orange when mature. Its leaves are usually opposite and have a smooth, glossy surface.
