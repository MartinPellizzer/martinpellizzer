Sedum sparsiflorum

Sedum sparsiflorum, commonly known as the sparseflower stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink to red. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Its leaves are typically blue-green in color and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Sedum sparsiflorum is drought-tolerant and requires minimal maintenance once established.
