Sideritis flavovirens

Sideritis flavovirens, commonly known as yellow-green rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to greenish-yellow. Its leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Sideritis flavovirens thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in dry, sunny environments.
