Specklinia fulgens

Specklinia fulgens, commonly known as the golden speckled orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and its striking, dark green leaves with distinctive yellow or golden speckles. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange, often arranged in a raceme. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Specklinia fulgens is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
