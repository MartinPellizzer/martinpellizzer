Scorzonera karkasensis

Scorzonera karkasensis, commonly known as the Karkas mountain scorzonera, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Turkey.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer, followed by elongated, tubular fruits. Its leaves are typically basal, hairy, and narrow, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
