Senegalia ankokib

Senegalia ankokib, commonly known as the African burr acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers, which appear in clusters. The plant produces pod-like fruits that contain seeds, and it is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. Senegalia ankokib is often used in traditional medicine and as a source of timber and fodder.

Its ecological role includes soil stabilization and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
