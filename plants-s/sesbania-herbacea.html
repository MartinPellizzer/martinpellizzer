Sesbania herbacea

Sesbania herbacea, commonly known as the slender sesbania, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a legume, belonging to the Fabaceae family, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow, pod-like flowers. The plant is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions. Its seeds are encased in long, slender pods, which are typically hairy and contain several seeds.

Sesbania herbacea is also known for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it valuable in soil enrichment.
