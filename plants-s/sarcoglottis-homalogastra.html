Sarcoglottis homalogastra

Sarcoglottis homalogastra, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value and rarity, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
