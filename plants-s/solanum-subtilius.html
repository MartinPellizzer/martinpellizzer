Solanum subtilius

Solanum subtilius, commonly known as the delicate nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale purple in color. Its leaves are ovate and hairy, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

While not widely cultivated, it is sometimes studied for its potential medicinal properties.
