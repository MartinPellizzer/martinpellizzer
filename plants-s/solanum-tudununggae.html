Solanum tudununggae

Solanum tudununggae, commonly known as the Nigerian nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Nigeria.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant is characterized by its small, purple or blue flowers and hairy leaves. It produces small, round berries that are typically green when unripe and turn red when mature.

Solanum tudununggae is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in moist, shaded environments.
