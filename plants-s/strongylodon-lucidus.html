Strongylodon lucidus

Strongylodon lucidus, commonly known as the glossy ant plant, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and its unique ability to secrete a sticky substance that attracts ants. The plant's aerial roots help it cling to trees and other structures as it grows. Its flowers are small and clustered, often resembling the shape of an ant's body.

Strongylodon lucidus plays a role in its ecosystem by providing shelter and food for various insect species.
