Senecio dunensis

Senecio dunensis, commonly known as the Dun Mountain groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are generally narrow and covered in fine hairs, aiding in water conservation.

The showy yellow flowers appear in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
