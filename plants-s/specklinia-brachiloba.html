Specklinia brachiloba

Specklinia brachiloba, commonly known as the short-stemmed stonecress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors.
