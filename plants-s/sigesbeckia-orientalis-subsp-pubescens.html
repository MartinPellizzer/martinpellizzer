Sigesbeckia orientalis subsp. pubescens

Sigesbeckia orientalis subsp.

pubescens, commonly known as the hairy sigesbeckia, is a flowering plant native to East Asia. It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, sunny environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in ecological and horticultural contexts.
