Sibbaldia Compacta

Sibbaldia compacta is a low-growing, mat-forming plant native to the arctic and alpine regions of Asia. It thrives in harsh environments with poor soil and extreme temperatures. Its small, white flowers bloom in late summer, adding a delicate touch to rocky landscapes. The plant is known for its resilience and is often found in tundra and mountainous areas. It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and supporting local ecosystems.
