Senecio blattarioides

Senecio blattarioides, commonly known as the African snowflake, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a frosty appearance. The plant produces showy, daisy-like yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
