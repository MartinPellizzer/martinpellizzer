Stelis cubicularia

Stelis cubicularia, commonly known as the cubicle orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. The plant features slender, wiry stems and produces delicate, greenish-white flowers with a subtle fragrance. Its leaves are usually clustered and covered in a fine, velvety texture.

Stelis cubicularia is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
