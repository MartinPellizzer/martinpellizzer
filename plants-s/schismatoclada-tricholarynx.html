Schismatoclada tricholarynx

Schismatoclada tricholarynx, commonly known as the narrow-leaf schismatoclada, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy, narrow leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle to reduce water loss. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers during the summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Its unique morphology and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
