Strychnos croatii

Strychnos croatii, commonly known as the Croatian strychnos, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds.

The leaves are simple, alternate, and have a leathery texture with a distinctive serrated margin.
