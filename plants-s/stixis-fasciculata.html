Stixis fasciculata

Stixis fasciculata, commonly known as the clustered stixis, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, delicate flowers. The plant typically produces white or pale yellow blooms that are arranged in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often growing in dense tufts.

Stixis fasciculata is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or along stream banks.
