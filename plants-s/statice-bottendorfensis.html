Statice bottendorfensis

Statice bottendorfensis, commonly known as the Bottendorf statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically blooms in late summer, producing vibrant pink to red flowers that attract pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
