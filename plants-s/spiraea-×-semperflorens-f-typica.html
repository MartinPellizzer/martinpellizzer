Spiraea × semperflorens f. typica

Spiraea × semperflorens f. typica, commonly known as the compact spiraea, is a hybrid shrub valued for its ornamental qualities.

It features dense, upright growth with clusters of small, showy flowers that bloom repeatedly throughout the growing season. The plant is native to the United States and is often cultivated for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. Its foliage is typically dark green, providing a striking contrast to the bright pink or white flowers.

It is commonly used in gardens for its compact size and long-lasting bloom period.
