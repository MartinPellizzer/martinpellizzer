Spiraea henryi var. glabrata

Spiraea henryi var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth spiraea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features slender, arching branches and clusters of small, white to pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The leaves are typically oval-shaped and have a smooth texture, contributing to its ornamental value. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

This variety is valued for its compact growth habit and attractive floral display.
