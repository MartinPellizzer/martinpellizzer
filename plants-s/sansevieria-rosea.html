Sansevieria rosea

Sansevieria rosea, commonly known as the Tiger Plant, is a striking succulent native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features long, upright leaves with distinctive light green stripes and bright yellow margins. The plant is highly drought-tolerant, making it ideal for low-maintenance indoor environments. Its air-purifying qualities make it a popular choice for improving indoor air quality.

Sansevieria rosea thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.
