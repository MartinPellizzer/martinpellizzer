Solanum ivohibe

Solanum ivohibe, commonly known as the Madagascar nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically purple or white in color. Its fruit is a fleshy berry that contains several seeds.
