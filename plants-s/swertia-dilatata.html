Swertia dilatata

Swertia dilatata, commonly known as the Himalayan gentian, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in cool and moist conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in a fine layer of hair.

Swertia dilatata is valued for its ornamental beauty and has traditional medicinal uses in some Asian cultures.
