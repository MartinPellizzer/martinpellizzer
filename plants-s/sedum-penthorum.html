Sedum penthorum

Sedum penthorum, commonly known as the showy sedum, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, succulent leaves and clusters of star-shaped yellow flowers. Sedum penthorum is a perennial species that produces small, capsule-like fruits containing seeds.
