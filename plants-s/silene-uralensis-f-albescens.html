Silene uralensis f. albescens

Silene uralensis f. albescens, commonly known as the white-flowered mountain catchfly, is a botanical variant of the species Silene uralensis.

It is characterized by its pale or white flowers, which contrast with the typical pink or red hues of the species. This plant is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and is often found in alpine and rocky habitats. The foliage is typically narrow and linear, adapted to the harsh environmental conditions of its native range.

It is a perennial herb that produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds.
