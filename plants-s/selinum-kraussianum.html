Selinum kraussianum

Selinum kraussianum, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and silvery-green leaves, which are covered in fine hairs. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in dry, sandy soils. It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

Selinum kraussianum is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
