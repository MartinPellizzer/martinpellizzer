Sebastiania chamaelea var. africana

Sebastiania chamaelea var. africana, commonly known as the African Christmas tree, is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical Africa.

It features dense, leathery foliage with elliptical to ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by bright red, berry-like fruits that are visually striking. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
