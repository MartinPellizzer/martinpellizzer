Statice echioides var. segobricensis

Statice echioides var. segobricensis, commonly known as the Segobric Mountain statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant blue to purple flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, showy flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
