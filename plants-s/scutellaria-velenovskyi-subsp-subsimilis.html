Scutellaria velenovskyi subsp. subsimilis

Scutellaria velenovskyi subsp.

subsimilis, commonly known as the Velenovsky's skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and aromatic leaves.

The plant produces small, two-lipped flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in alpine environments.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
