Solanum ochranthum var. glabrifilamentum

Solanum ochranthum var. glabrifilamentum, commonly known as the silky nightshade, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces berries that transition from yellow to orange and finally to red as they mature. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in shaded, moist environments.

Due to its toxic properties, it is important to handle the plant with care.
