Stellaria dichotoma f. lanceolata

Stellaria dichotoma f. lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceolate starwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its erect or ascending stems and opposite, lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that are typically arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems. It thrives in moist, sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in disturbed or open habitats.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ecologically significant species in its native range.
