Schizoglossum theileri

Schizoglossum theileri, commonly known as the African orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, fragrant flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces spikes of white or pale pink flowers. Schizoglossum theileri is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
