Syngonium dodsonianum

Syngonium dodsonianum, commonly known as the dodsonian arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Syngonium dodsonianum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.
