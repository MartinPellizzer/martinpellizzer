Stephanotis rubicunda

Stephanotis rubicunda, commonly known as the red-flowered star jasmine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its fragrant, showy flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in shades of white to pink, with a reddish tinge. The plant has opposite, simple, and usually ovate or elliptic leaves that are glossy and dark green. It produces small, round, green fruits that contain seeds.

Stephanotis rubicunda is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in floral arrangements due to its pleasant aroma.
