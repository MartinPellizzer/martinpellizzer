Styrax ramirezii

Styrax ramirezii, commonly known as the Ramirez styrax, is a species of flowering plant in the family Styracaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of central Mexico, where it thrives in humid, temperate climates. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a glossy appearance.

Styrax ramirezii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
