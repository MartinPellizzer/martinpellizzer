Symphytum officinale var. patens

Symphytum officinale var. patens, commonly known as the broadleaf comfrey, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically broad and velvety in texture. The plant produces tall spikes of blue to purple flowers during the summer months. It is known for its deep root system, which allows it to thrive in moist, well-drained soils.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been historically used in herbal remedies.
