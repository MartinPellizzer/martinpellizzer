Selaginella martensii var. formosa

Selaginella martensii var. formosa, commonly known as the beautiful spikemoss, is a species of vascular plant belonging to the family Selaginellaceae.

It is characterized by its upright, branched stems covered with small, scale-like leaves. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in temperate regions. Its reproductive structures include sporophylls that bear spores, typical of the spikemoss group.

The varietal form, formosa, is distinguished by its more ornamental appearance and vibrant foliage.
