Sphaerolobium medium

Sphaerolobium medium, commonly known as the yellow everlasting pea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its legume fruits and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant produces small, yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

Sphaerolobium medium is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
