Stachys burgsdorffioides subsp. burgsdorffioides

Stachys burgsdorffioides subsp.

burgsdorffioides, commonly known as the Burgsdorff's hedge nettle, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

The plant features upright, branched stems covered in soft, hairy foliage. Its small, clustered flowers are usually pink to purple, blooming in late summer.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
