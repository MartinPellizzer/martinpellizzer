Senegalia Nitidifolia

Senegalia nitidifolia is a small, evergreen tree native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia. It is known for its striking white flowers and glossy, narrow leaves that give it a unique appearance. This plant is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
