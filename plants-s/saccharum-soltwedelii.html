Saccharum soltwedelii

Saccharum soltwedelii, commonly known as the Soltwedel grass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, blade-like leaves. The plant thrives in wetland environments and is often found near water bodies. Its flowering spikes produce small, clustered inflorescences that are typically pale green to brown in color.

Saccharum soltwedelii plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
