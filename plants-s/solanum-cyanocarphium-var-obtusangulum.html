Solanum cyanocarphium var. obtusangulum

Solanum cyanocarphium var. obtusangulum, commonly known as the broad-leaf nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves and showy, violet to blue flowers. The plant produces small, spherical berries that are typically green when immature and turn red when mature.

It is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and forest edges, and it has been used in traditional medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
