Senyumia minutiflora

Senyumia minutiflora, commonly known as the small-flowered senyumia, is a delicate flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It features small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. The plant has slender, upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique floral structure and ecological role in its native habitat.
