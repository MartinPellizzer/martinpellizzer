Schismatoglottis picta f. bivittata

Schismatoglottis picta f. bivittata, commonly known as the striped schismatoglottis, is a hybrid cultivar belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated leaves that display distinct green and white stripes. The plant produces small, delicate orchid-like flowers in shades of white and green, typically emerging from the base of the foliage. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This ornamental plant is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
