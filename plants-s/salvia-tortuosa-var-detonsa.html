Salvia tortuosa var. detonsa

Salvia tortuosa var. detonsa, commonly known as the zigzag sage, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its striking violet-blue flowers and gray-green, hairy foliage. The plant produces narrow, zigzagging stems that give it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long bloom period and aromatic leaves.
