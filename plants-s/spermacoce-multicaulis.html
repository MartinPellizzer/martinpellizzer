Spermacoce multicaulis

Spermacoce multicaulis, commonly known as the clustered starling bean, is a tropical plant belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface. Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and research settings.
