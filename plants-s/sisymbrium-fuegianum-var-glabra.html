Sisymbrium fuegianum var. glabra

Sisymbrium fuegianum var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth field pennycress, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly the southern regions.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many economically important crops like mustard and cabbage. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in grasslands and along roadsides.
