Swertia alata

Swertia alata, commonly known as the silver gentian, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky habitats at high elevations. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in a fine, silvery down.

Swertia alata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
