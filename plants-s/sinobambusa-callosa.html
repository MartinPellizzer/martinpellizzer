Sinobambusa callosa

Sinobambusa callosa, commonly known as the rough bamboo, is a species of flowering bamboo native to China.

It is characterized by its sturdy, straight culms that are often covered with a rough, scaly texture. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in mountainous regions. It produces small, clustered inflorescences with greenish-white flowers.

Sinobambusa callosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
