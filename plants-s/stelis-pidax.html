Stelis pidax

Stelis pidax, commonly known as the orchid spike orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial herb that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its upright, branching stems. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, often displaying a range of colors from white to pink.
