Sagittaria heterophylla

Sagittaria heterophylla, commonly known as arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves that float on the water surface, while its roots are submerged. This aquatic plant thrives in wetlands, marshes, and shallow water environments. It produces small, white flowers that bloom above the water, contributing to its ornamental value.

Sagittaria heterophylla is also an important food source for various aquatic animals and birds.
