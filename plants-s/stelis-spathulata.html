Stelis spathulata

Stelis spathulata, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant has linear to lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Stelis spathulata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
