Specklinia coeloglossa

Specklinia coeloglossa, commonly known as the silver-veined orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, tropical environments. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers with white petals and a yellow lip. Its unique silver-green veining on the leaves is a distinguishing characteristic.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity for optimal growth.
