Sicyos polyacanthos

Sicyos polyacanthos, commonly known as the spiny leaf gourd, is a climbing vine native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with spines along the margins, which serve as a defense mechanism against herbivores. The plant produces yellow-orange flowers followed by distinctive, spiny fruits that mature to a pale green color. Its fibrous stem and leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported anti-inflammatory properties.

Sicyos polyacanthos thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
