Spiraea chinensis

Spiraea chinensis, commonly known as Chinese spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features slender, arching branches that bear clusters of small, white or pink flowers in early summer. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Spiraea chinensis is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
