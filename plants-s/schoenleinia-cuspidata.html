Schoenleinia cuspidata

Schoenleinia cuspidata, commonly known as the mountain orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows at high altitudes, thriving in cool, humid environments with well-drained soil. Schoenleinia cuspidata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts of rare plants.
