Spiesia hirta

Spiesia hirta, commonly known as the hairy spurge, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features erect, hairy stems and produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Spiesia hirta is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
