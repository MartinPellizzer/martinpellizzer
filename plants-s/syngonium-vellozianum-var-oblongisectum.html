Syngonium vellozianum var. oblongisectum

Syngonium vellozianum var. oblongisectum, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a tropical perennial shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, arrowhead-like leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and indoor plant cultivation.
