Sisymbrium abyssinicum

Sisymbrium abyssinicum, commonly known as Ethiopian mustards, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other mustard plants. The plant features small, yellow flowers and has a taproot system that allows it to thrive in arid conditions. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped and have a slightly bitter taste.

Sisymbrium abyssinicum is often used in traditional medicine and as a source of edible seeds.
