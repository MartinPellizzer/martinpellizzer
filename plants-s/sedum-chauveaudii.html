Sedum chauveaudii

Sedum chauveaudii, commonly known as the Chauveaud's stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems and produces clusters of small, star-shaped yellow flowers. The plant has thick, water-storing leaves that help it survive in arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
