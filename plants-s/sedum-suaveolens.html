Sedum suaveolens

Sedum suaveolens, commonly known as the sweet-scented stonecrop, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Sedum suaveolens is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
