Senecio glabellus f. robustior

Senecio glabellus f. robustior, commonly known as the robust greater senecio, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces yellow flowers in summer, which are borne on long, branching stems. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
