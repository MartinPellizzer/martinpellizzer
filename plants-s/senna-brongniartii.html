Senna brongniartii

Senna brongniartii, commonly known as the African senna, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain seeds, and it is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Senna brongniartii is also valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for various health purposes.
