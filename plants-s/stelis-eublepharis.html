Stelis eublepharis

Stelis eublepharis, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with dark green, strap-like leaves. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers with a distinctive spider-like appearance, typically in shades of white and pink. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Stelis eublepharis requires well-draining potting media and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
