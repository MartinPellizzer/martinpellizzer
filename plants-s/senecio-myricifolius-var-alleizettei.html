Senecio myricifolius var. alleizettei

Senecio myricifolius var. alleizettei, commonly known as the Alleizette's goldenweed, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellow to orange flowers in dense clusters. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to low-water environments. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its medicinal properties in traditional use.
