Solidago virgaurea proles alpicola

Solidago virgaurea proles alpicola, commonly known as the alpine goldenrod, is a variant of the goldenrod species native to mountainous regions.

It is characterized by its yellow floral heads and narrow, linear leaves that are adapted to high-altitude environments. This plant typically grows in open, rocky habitats and thrives in well-drained soils. Its flowering period occurs in late summer to early autumn, attracting various pollinators.

The plant is known for its hardy nature and ability to withstand harsh climatic conditions.
