Senecio olgae

Senecio olgae, commonly known as the Olgae groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in fine hairs, while its yellow flowers bloom in clusters.

Senecio olgae is often found in fynbos vegetation and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
