Schoepfia vacciniiflora

Schoepfia vacciniiflora, commonly known as the redstem sage, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in rocky soils and arid environments. The plant features narrow, gray-green leaves and produces showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to purple. Its flowering period occurs primarily in the spring and early summer.

Schoepfia vacciniiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to harsh, dry conditions.
