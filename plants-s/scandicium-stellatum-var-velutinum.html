Scandicium stellatum var. velutinum

Scandicium stellatum var. velutinum, commonly known as the velvet star.

It is a unique variant of the star-shaped plant, characterized by its soft, velvety texture on the underside of its leaves. The plant exhibits a compact growth habit with small, star-like flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale pink. Its leaves are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

This variety is well-suited for rock gardens and dry, well-drained soil conditions.
