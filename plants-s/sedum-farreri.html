Sedum farreri

Sedum farreri, commonly known as the Farre's stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to arid and rocky environments.

It features fleshy, upright stems and clusters of star-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to red. The plant is adapted to survive in dry conditions, storing water in its thick leaves and stems. It typically blooms in summer, adding vibrant color to rocky landscapes.

Sedum farreri is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and ornamental value.
