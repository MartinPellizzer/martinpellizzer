Santolina chamaecyparissus var. rigidula

Santolina chamaecyparissus var. rigidula, commonly known as rigid santolina, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, silver-gray foliage with a pine-like fragrance, and its small yellow flowers appear in early summer. The plant has a compact growth habit, making it ideal for rock gardens or as a border plant. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, preferring full sun.

Its ornamental value comes from its aromatic leaves and long-lasting flowering period.
