Schefflera lucescens var. rigida

Schefflera lucescens var. rigida, commonly known as the rigid schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like clusters of palmate leaves, which are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil and partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor landscaping.
