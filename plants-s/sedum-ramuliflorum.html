Sedum ramuliflorum

Sedum ramuliflorum, commonly known as the leafy stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features fleshy, upright stems covered in small, clustered leaves that are typically blue-gray in color. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped, yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Sedum ramuliflorum is drought-tolerant and requires minimal maintenance once established.
