Sideritis incana var. matris-filiae

Sideritis incana var. matris-filiae, commonly known as mother's love, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, pale blue or violet flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used traditionally for its calming and anti-inflammatory effects.
