Stipa pennata var. tirsa

Stipa pennata var. tirsa, commonly known as the Tirsa feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and long, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces feathery, branched inflorescences that are typically pink to reddish in color. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as steppes and rocky soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in suitable climates.
