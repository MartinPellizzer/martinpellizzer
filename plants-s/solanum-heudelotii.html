Solanum heudelotii

Solanum heudelotii, commonly known as the African nightshade, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant features small, star-shaped white or purple flowers that bloom in clusters. Its berries are typically yellow or red when ripe and contain toxic alkaloids.

Solanum heudelotii is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
