Stellaria chlorotica

Stellaria chlorotica, commonly known as the green starwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically features small, star-shaped white or pale blue flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has slender, branching stems covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often hairy. Stellaria chlorotica thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
