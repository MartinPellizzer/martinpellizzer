Sitanion hystrix

Sitanion hystrix, commonly known as the spiny holly or holly fern, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves with prominent veins and a rough texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Sitanion hystrix is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant.
