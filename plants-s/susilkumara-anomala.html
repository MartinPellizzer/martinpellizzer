Susilkumara anomala

Susilkumara anomala, commonly known as the mountain orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil to flourish. Due to its limited habitat and sensitivity to environmental changes, it is considered a vulnerable species.
