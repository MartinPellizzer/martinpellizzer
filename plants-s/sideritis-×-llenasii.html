Sideritis × llenasii

Sideritis × llenasii, commonly known as the hybrid sainfoin, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, arranged in whorled clusters. Sideritis × llenasii is often found in dry, open habitats such as rocky slopes and grasslands.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and traditional herbal uses.
