Stelis alba

Stelis alba, commonly known as the white stelis orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, white or pale green flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on tree trunks or branches. Its leaves are narrow and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Stelis alba is prized in horticulture for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
