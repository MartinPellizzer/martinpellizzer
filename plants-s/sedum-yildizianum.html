Sedum yildizianum

Sedum yildizianum, commonly known as the Yildiz stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Turkey.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its hardy nature allows it to survive in USDA hardiness zones 5 through 9.
