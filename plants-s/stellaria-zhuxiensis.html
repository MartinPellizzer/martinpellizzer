Stellaria zhuxiensis

Stellaria zhuxiensis, commonly known as the Zhuxi starwort, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, star-shaped white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is found in mountainous regions. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
