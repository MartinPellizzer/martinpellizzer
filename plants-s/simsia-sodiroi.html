Simsia sodiroi

Simsia sodiroi, commonly known as the yellow bell, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy yellow ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers. Simsia sodiroi typically grows in coastal areas and thrives in well-drained soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
