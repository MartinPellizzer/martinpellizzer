Saxifraga khokhrjakovii

Saxifraga khokhrjakovii, commonly known as the Khokhrjakov's saxifrage, is a rare alpine plant native to the mountains of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils at high elevations. Its botanical features include hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with a waxy surface.
