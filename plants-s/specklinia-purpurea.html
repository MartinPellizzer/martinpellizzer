Specklinia purpurea

Specklinia purpurea, commonly known as the purple-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically growing on trees or rocks. The plant features dark green, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, violet-blue flowers with white markings. Its unique coloration and delicate appearance make it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Specklinia purpurea requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture to flourish.
