Senecio loureiroi

Senecio loureiroi, commonly known as the Cape marigold, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and grows as a perennial shrub. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and drought tolerance.
