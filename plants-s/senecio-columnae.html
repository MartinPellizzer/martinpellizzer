Senecio columnae

Senecio columnae, commonly known as the columnar senecio, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, spindly stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height. The plant features small, narrow leaves arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. It produces yellow flowers in the summer months, which are clustered in cylindrical inflorescences.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in arid and semi-arid regions.
