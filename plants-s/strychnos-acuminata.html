Strychnos acuminata

Strychnos acuminata, commonly known as the African strychnos, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a serrated margin.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.
