Sesleria haynaldiana var. leucostachys

Sesleria haynaldiana var. leucostachys, commonly known as the white-stemmed sesleria, is a perennial grass-like plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant produces slender, white to pale pink flower spikes that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Its ornamental value lies in its delicate foliage and subtle floral display.
