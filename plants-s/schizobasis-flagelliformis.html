Schizobasis flagelliformis

Schizobasis flagelliformis, commonly known as the flagellate sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets. Schizobasis flagelliformis plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic and terrestrial organisms.
