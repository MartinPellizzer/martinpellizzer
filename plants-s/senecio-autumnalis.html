Senecio autumnalis

Senecio autumnalis, commonly known as autumnal groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its yellow flowers bloom in late summer to early autumn, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Senecio autumnalis is often used in rock gardens due to its hardy nature and ornamental value.
