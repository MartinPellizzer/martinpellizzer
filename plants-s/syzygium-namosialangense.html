Syzygium namosialangense

Syzygium namosialangense, commonly known as the Namosialang mangosteen, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the island of Flores in Indonesia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant produces edible, segmented fruits that are similar in appearance to the common mangosteen but are less well-known.

Syzygium namosialangense is valued for its ornamental qualities and potential medicinal properties, though it remains relatively understudied in botanical research.
