Stellaria glauca lusus virens

Stellaria glauca lusus virens, commonly known as the green starwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, star-shaped white flowers and slender, hairy stems. The plant typically grows in moist, sandy soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Stellaria glauca lusus virens is an annual herb that blooms from late spring to early summer.
