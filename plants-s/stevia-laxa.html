Stevia laxa

Stevia laxa, commonly known as the slender stevia, is a perennial herb native to South America.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant produces tiny, sweet-tasting leaves that are often used as a natural sweetener. Stevia laxa thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its low maintenance requirements and ornamental value make it a popular choice among horticulturists.
