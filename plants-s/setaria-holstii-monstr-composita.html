Setaria holstii monstr. composita

Setaria holstii monstr.

composita, commonly known as the silverleaf grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its narrow, silver-gray blades and a compact growth habit.

The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland and savanna ecosystems.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and landscaping.
