Silene thunbergiana

Silene thunbergiana, commonly known as the Thunberg's campion, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces striking pink or red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

Silene thunbergiana is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and disturbed soils, and it thrives in full sun to partial shade.
