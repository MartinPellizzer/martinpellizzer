Stelis mucrouncata

Stelis mucrouncata, commonly known as the sticky stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments, utilizing its aerial roots to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually pale green or white, with a distinctive sticky secretion on the petals. Its leaves are elongated and fleshy, adapted to store water in its natural habitat.

Stelis mucrouncata is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
