Syagrus insignis

Syagrus insignis, commonly known as the royal palm, is a tropical plant native to Brazil.

It is a tall, slender palm with a smooth, unbranched trunk and a crown of large, feather-like leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that develop into orange-red fruits containing a single seed. Syagrus insignis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in landscaping in warm climates.

Its growth habit and striking appearance make it a popular choice for gardens and public spaces.
