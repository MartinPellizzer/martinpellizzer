Specklinia saundersiana

Specklinia saundersiana, commonly known as the Saunders' orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow blossoms that are often adorned with dark markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media to prevent root rot.

Specklinia saundersiana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
