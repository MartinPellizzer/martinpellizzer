Symphyotrichum graminifolium

Symphyotrichum graminifolium, commonly known as the slender aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and showy purple or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. It produces small, tubular flowers that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Symphyotrichum graminifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native plant gardens.
