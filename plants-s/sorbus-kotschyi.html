Sorbus kotschyi

Sorbus kotschyi, commonly known as the Kotschyi rowan, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, aggregate fruits known as pomes. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

Its flowers are usually white or pink, appearing in clusters, and it produces edible berries that are consumed by various wildlife species.
