Statice bonduellei

Statice bonduellei, commonly known as the Mediterranean statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and spikes of small, colorful flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy or rocky soils and is drought-tolerant. It blooms in summer, producing vibrant pink, red, or purple flowers that attract pollinators.

Statice bonduellei is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and can thrive in full sun conditions.
