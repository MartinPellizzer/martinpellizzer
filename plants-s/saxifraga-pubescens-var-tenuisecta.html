Saxifraga pubescens var. tenuisecta

Saxifraga pubescens var. tenuisecta, commonly known as the narrow-leaf saxifrage, is a low-growing perennial plant native to alpine and rocky environments.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white to pinkish flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in mountainous regions.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to harsh, cold climates.
