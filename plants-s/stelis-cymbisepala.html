Stelis cymbisepala

Stelis cymbisepala, commonly known as the cymbisepal stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, relying on its roots for moisture and nutrients. Its leaves are fleshy and arranged in a rosette, aiding in water retention.

Stelis cymbisepala is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
