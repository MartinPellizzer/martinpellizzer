Sempervivum gurgenidzeae

Sempervivum gurgenidzeae, commonly known as the Georgian houseleek, is a succulent plant native to the Caucasus region.

It belongs to the family Crassulaceae and is characterized by its rosette-forming growth pattern. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color. Sempervivum gurgenidzeae is also noted for its ability to produce offsets, which allows for easy propagation.
