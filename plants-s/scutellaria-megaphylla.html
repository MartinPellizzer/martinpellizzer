Scutellaria megaphylla

Scutellaria megaphylla, commonly known as the large-leaved skullcap, is a herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of central and southern China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic foliage. The plant features opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often hairy and have a distinct serrated margin. Its flowers are typically blue to purple, arranged in whorled clusters, and bloom during the summer months.

Scutellaria megaphylla is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional Chinese medicine.
