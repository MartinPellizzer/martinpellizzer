Statice armeria var. mixta

Statice armeria var. mixta, commonly known as mixed statice, is a flowering plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces dense clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun conditions. It is often used in garden landscapes for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

The foliage is typically gray-green, with hairy leaves that help reduce water loss.
