Staurogyne rubescens

Staurogyne rubescens, commonly known as redstem waterweed, is a perennial aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its bright red stems and dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are often submerged in water. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and lakes. Its dense growth habit makes it an effective competitor for light and nutrients in aquatic ecosystems.

Staurogyne rubescens plays a role in stabilizing sediments and providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
