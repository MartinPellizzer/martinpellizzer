Smithiantha incarnata

Smithiantha incarnata, commonly known as the pink fairy lantern, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique, lantern-like appearance and long blooming period.
