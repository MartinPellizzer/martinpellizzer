Sphagneticola × guangdongensis

Sphagneticola × guangdongensis, commonly known as the Guangdong ironleaf, is a hybrid plant native to southern China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and lance-shaped leaves. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and compact size.
