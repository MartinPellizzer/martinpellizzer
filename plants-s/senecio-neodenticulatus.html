Senecio neodenticulatus

Senecio neodenticulatus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and often have a distinctive serrated edge, while its flowers are yellow with a central disc.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and hardy nature.
