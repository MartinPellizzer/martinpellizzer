Smilax jauaensis

Smilax jauaensis, commonly known as the Jauaese smilax, is a climbing shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Smilacaceae and is characterized by its woody stems and spiny growth patterns. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in axillary inflorescences. Its berries are dark red to black when mature and serve as a food source for various wildlife.

Smilax jauaensis is often found in humid, shaded environments such as forest understories.
