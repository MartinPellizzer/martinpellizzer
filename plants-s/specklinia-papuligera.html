Specklinia papuligera

Specklinia papuligera, commonly known as the papular specklinia, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit and produces small, clustered flowers with a unique papillate texture on the petals. The plant typically thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories. Its leaves are usually dark green and somewhat leathery, adapted to retain moisture.

Specklinia papuligera is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
