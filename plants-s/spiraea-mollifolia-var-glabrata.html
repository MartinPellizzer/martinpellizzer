Spiraea mollifolia var. glabrata

Spiraea mollifolia var. glabrata, commonly known as smooth spiraea, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It features slender, arching stems that produce clusters of small, white to pink flowers in early spring. The leaves are ovate to elliptic, with a smooth texture and a dull green color. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a versatile addition to gardens.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
