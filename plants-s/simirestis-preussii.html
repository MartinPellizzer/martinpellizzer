Simirestis preussii

Simirestis preussii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires indirect sunlight to prevent leaf scorch. It is a popular houseplant due to its ease of care and ornamental value.

Simirestis preussii is often propagated through leaf cuttings or seeds, making it a favored choice for indoor gardening.
