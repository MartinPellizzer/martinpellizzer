Spermacoce compacta

Spermacoce compacta, commonly known as the small cowhead, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 3 meters. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by distinctive, spherical, red berries. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The plant is known for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
