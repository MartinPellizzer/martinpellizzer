Scyphocoronis

Scyphocoronis, commonly known as the crown-of-thorns sea star, is a marine echinoderm belonging to the family Scyphocoronidae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiny, crown-like structure that resembles a thorned crown, giving it its common name. The organism has a central disc surrounded by multiple arms, which are often adorned with small, calcareous spines. Scyphocoronis inhabits tropical and subtropical seas, typically found in shallow coastal waters.

Its unique morphology and ecological role make it an important species in marine ecosystems.
