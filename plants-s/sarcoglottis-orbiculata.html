Sarcoglottis orbiculata

Sarcoglottis orbiculata, commonly known as the globular orchid, is a tropical flowering plant native to regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, globular growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-drained soil.

Sarcoglottis orbiculata is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its unique appearance and ease of care.
