Sonerila suffruticosa var. lasiantha

Sonerila suffruticosa var. lasiantha, commonly known as the velvet tamarind, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small shrub characterized by its dense, leathery leaves and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. Its botanical features include a woody stem, opposite leaf arrangement, and showy inflorescences.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in forest understories.
