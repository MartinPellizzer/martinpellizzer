Senna lasseigniana var. canarensis

Senna lasseigniana var. canarensis, commonly known as the Brazilian senna, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant is known for its bright yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal regions.

Its leaves and pods are used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
