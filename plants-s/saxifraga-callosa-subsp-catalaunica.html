Saxifraga callosa subsp. catalaunica

Saxifraga callosa subsp.

catalaunica, commonly known as the Catalaunica saxifrage, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe. It typically thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

The plant features dark green, rounded leaves with a rough texture and small, clustered white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing rocky terrain.
