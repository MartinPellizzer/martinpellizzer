Senecio eriopus

Senecio eriopus, commonly known as the blue ice plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features a low-growing, mat-forming structure with fleshy, blue-gray leaves that store water. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for arid landscape designs.
