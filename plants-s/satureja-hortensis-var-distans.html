Satureja hortensis var. distans

Satureja hortensis var. distans, commonly known as distant savory, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, blooming in the summer months. Its aromatic leaves contain essential oils, giving it a sharp, peppery scent.

It is often used in culinary applications and traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
