Smilax mengmaensis

Smilax mengmaensis, commonly known as the Mengmaensis Smilax, is a climbing shrub belonging to the family Araceae.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Yunnan and Guizhou provinces. The plant features woody stems with thorns and heart-shaped leaves that are typically green on top and lighter underneath. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and clustered in spadices, while the fruit is a bright red, berry-like structure.

Smilax mengmaensis is valued for its ornamental potential and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
