Selago pulchra

Selago pulchra, commonly known as the beautiful star grass, is a flowering plant native to the southern regions of Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant produces small, yellowish-orange florets arranged in a central disk, surrounded by pale green to white ray flowers. Selago pulchra typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands, thriving in well-drained soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
