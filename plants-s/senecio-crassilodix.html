Senecio crassilodix

Senecio crassilodix, commonly known as the broad-leaf Senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in horticulture.
