Sideritis littoralis

Sideritis littoralis, commonly known as coastal ironweed, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in sunny and well-drained environments. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, gray-green leaves with a hairy texture. Its flowers are pale blue to purple and appear in clustered inflorescences, blooming from summer to early autumn.

Sideritis littoralis is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly for its use in traditional herbal remedies.
