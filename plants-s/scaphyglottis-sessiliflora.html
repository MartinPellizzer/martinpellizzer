Scaphyglottis sessiliflora

Scaphyglottis sessiliflora, commonly known as the sessile-flowered scaphyglottis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically pale green or white, with a unique, spurred structure. Its leaves are broad and ovate, often with a waxy surface to retain moisture.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to cultivated conditions.
