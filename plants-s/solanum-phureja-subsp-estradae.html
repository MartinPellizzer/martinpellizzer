Solanum phureja subsp. estradae

Solanum phureja subsp.

estradae, commonly known as the Estrada's nightshade, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family, Solanaceae. It is native to Central and South America, particularly found in regions of Colombia and Ecuador.

This subspecies is characterized by its small, usually purple or blue flowers and hairy, glandular leaves. It typically grows in humid, shaded environments such as forests and shrublands.

The plant is notable for its potential medicinal properties and has been studied for its bioactive compounds.
