Saxifraga funstonii

Saxifraga funstonii, commonly known as Funston's saxifrage, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically thrives in rocky or gravelly soils. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers during the spring and summer months. It is often found in alpine or subalpine environments, where it adapts to harsh climatic conditions.

Its leaves are usually basal, with a rosette-like structure that helps it conserve moisture.
