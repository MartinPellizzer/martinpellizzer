Stelis pichinchae

Stelis pichinchae, commonly known as the Pichincha orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andean regions of Ecuador.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments within cloud forests. The plant features a slender, upright stem with several fleshy, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, usually white or pale green, and are arranged in a loose raceme.

Stelis pichinchae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
