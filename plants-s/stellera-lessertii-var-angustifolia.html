Stellera lessertii var. angustifolia

Stellera lessertii var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf starflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It features a low-growing rosette of narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically blooming in early spring. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as forest floors and streambanks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable plant for ecological restoration and native plant gardens.
