Sphaerocionium horizontale

Sphaerocionium horizontale, commonly known as the horizontal sponge spore plant, is a unique member of the Sphaerocionaceae family.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found on the surfaces of other plants. The plant's most distinctive feature is its spherical, gelatinous spore sacs, which are embedded in a network of delicate, branching structures. These spore sacs release microscopic spores into the air, facilitating its reproduction.

Due to its specialized habitat and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species in tropical rainforests.
