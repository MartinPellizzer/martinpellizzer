Stelis boliviensis

Stelis boliviensis, commonly known as the Bolivian spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, spider-like floral structure. The plant typically produces small, pale green to yellowish flowers that are often adorned with intricate patterns. Stelis boliviensis thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

Its botanical features make it a unique and sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
