Stephanotis thouarsii

Stephanotis thouarsii, commonly known as the Madagascar jasmine, is a climbing evergreen vine native to Madagascar.

It features fragrant, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the warmer months. The plant has opposite, simple, and entire leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. Its stems are woody and can grow quite long, making it suitable for trellises or arbors.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and aromatic blossoms.
