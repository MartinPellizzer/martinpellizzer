Strombocactus denegrii

Strombocactus denegrii, commonly known as the Denegri's strombocactus, is a species of cactus native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical, ribbed stems that are typically covered in a layer of white, powdery bloom. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking contrast to its green foliage. Strombocactus denegrii is well adapted to harsh desert conditions, with deep roots that help it access water from underground sources.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
