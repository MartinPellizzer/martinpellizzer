Solanum arnezii

Solanum arnezii, commonly known as the Arnez's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and tobacco. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are ovate with a hairy texture, and it produces small, clustered white or purple flowers.

The fruit is a fleshy berry that turns from green to yellow or red as it matures.
