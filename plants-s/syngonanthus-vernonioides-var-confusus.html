Syngonanthus vernonioides var. confusus

Syngonanthus vernonioides var. confusus, commonly known as the tropical milkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, bright orange to red flowers. The leaves are opposite, elliptical, and covered in fine hairs, giving them a velvety texture. This plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and clearings.

It is a popular choice for gardens due to its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators like butterflies and bees.
