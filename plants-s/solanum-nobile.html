Solanum nobile

Solanum nobile, commonly known as the evergreen nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is native to the Canary Islands and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant produces small, toxic berries that are typically orange or yellow when mature. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for Mediterranean gardens.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
