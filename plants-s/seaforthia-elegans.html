Seaforthia elegans

Seaforthia elegans, commonly known as the elegant seaforthia, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sunlight. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming dense clumps.

Seaforthia elegans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and resilience.
