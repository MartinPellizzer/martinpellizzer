Saccoloma tenue

Saccoloma tenue, commonly known as the slender saccoloma, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated fronds that are typically less than 30 centimeters in length. Saccoloma tenue is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in humid, forested areas.
