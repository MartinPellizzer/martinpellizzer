Stellaria wushanensis var. trientaloides

Stellaria wushanensis var. trientaloides, commonly known as the Wushan starwort, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky slopes. Its leaves are linear to lanceolate, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
