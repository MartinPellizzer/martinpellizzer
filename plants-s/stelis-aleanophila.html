Stelis aleanophila

Stelis aleanophila, commonly known as the white-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows as an epiphytic plant, attaching itself to tree trunks or branches in tropical rainforests. The plant produces elegant, white or pale pink flowers that bloom during the rainy season. Its leaves are usually elongated and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water retention.

Stelis aleanophila is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and adaptability to certain greenhouse conditions.
