Syzygium johorense

Syzygium johorense, commonly known as the Javanese apple or white apple, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant produces edible, round fruits that are typically harvested when they turn red or yellow. Syzygium johorense thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Its ornamental value and fruit make it a popular species in horticulture and traditional medicine.
