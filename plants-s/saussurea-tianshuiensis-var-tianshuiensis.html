Saussurea tianshuiensis var. tianshuiensis

Saussurea tianshuiensis var. tianshuiensis, commonly known as the Tianshui saussurea, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems and yellowish to brownish flower heads. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky areas at high altitudes. It has fibrous roots and is known for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.

Its flowers are often visited by various pollinators, contributing to its ecological significance in its native habitat.
