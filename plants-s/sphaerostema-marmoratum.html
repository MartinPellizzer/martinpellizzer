Sphaerostema marmoratum

Sphaerostema marmoratum, commonly known as the marble-stemmed orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, fleshy, and often brightly colored stems. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in racemose inflorescences. Its leaves are usually opposite and have a smooth, waxy texture.

Sphaerostema marmoratum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
