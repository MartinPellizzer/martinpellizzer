Senecio sandersii

Senecio sandersii, commonly known as the sanders' groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky, arid environments. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Senecio sandersii is adapted to dry conditions and is often found in desert scrub and chaparral habitats.
