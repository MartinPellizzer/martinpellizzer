Stylosanthes figueroae

Stylosanthes figueroae, commonly known as the Figueroa stylo, is a perennial leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces small, elliptical pods containing several seeds. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a valuable forage species.

Stylosanthes figueroae is also known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, improving soil fertility.
