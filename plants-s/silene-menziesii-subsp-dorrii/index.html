Silene Menziesii Subsp. Dorrii

Silene menziesii subsp. dorrii is a rare and specialized flowering plant native to the coastal regions of Oregon and Washington. This subspecies is part of the Caryophyllaceae family, known for its showy blooms and adaptability to harsh environments. It thrives in sandy soils and is often found in dunes or coastal bluffs where it faces strong winds and salt spray. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered vulnerable to environmental changes and human disturbance. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique and ecologically important species.
