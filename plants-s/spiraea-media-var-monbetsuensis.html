Spiraea media var. monbetsuensis

Spiraea media var. monbetsuensis, commonly known as the Monbetsu spiraea, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features small, white to pinkish-pink flowers that bloom in clusters during late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, arching branches and elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a border plant due to its compact size and ornamental value.

Its foliage provides attractive fall color, adding seasonal interest to the landscape.
