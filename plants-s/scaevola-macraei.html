Scaevola macraei

Scaevola macraei, commonly known as the pink fairy fan, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its vibrant pink to purple flowers that bloom in clusters, forming a fan-like shape. The plant has small, narrow leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and sandy soils.

Scaevola macraei is a popular ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and drought tolerance.
