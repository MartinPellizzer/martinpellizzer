Silvianthus clerodendroides

Silvianthus clerodendroides, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae and is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture and produces small, fleshy fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.

Silvianthus clerodendroides is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
