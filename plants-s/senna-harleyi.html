Senna harleyi

Senna harleyi, commonly known as the yellow senna, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom during the dry season. Its leaves are bipinnate, meaning they are divided into small, feather-like segments.

Senna harleyi is also valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
