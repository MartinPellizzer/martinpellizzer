Solanum parvifolium subsp. tropicum

Solanum parvifolium subsp.

tropicum, commonly known as the tropical dwarf nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of South America. It typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters, featuring small, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture.

The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale green flowers that bloom during the warmer months. Its fruits are berries that mature to a deep red color, containing several seeds.

This species is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or rocky crevices.
