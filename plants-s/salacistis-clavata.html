Salacistis clavata

Salacistis clavata, commonly known as the clustered club-moss, is a small evergreen plant belonging to the family Lycopodiaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, clustered growth habit and small, scale-like leaves. The plant produces spore-bearing cones, which are typically found at the base of the foliage. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest undergrowth.
