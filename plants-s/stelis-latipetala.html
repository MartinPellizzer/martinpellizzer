Stelis latipetala

Stelis latipetala, commonly known as the broad-petalled stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments, with its roots anchored in the bark. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with broad petals and a distinctive lip. Its leaves are usually clustered at the base, and the stems are often short and unbranched.

Stelis latipetala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
