Solenostemon paniculatus

Solenostemon paniculatus, commonly known as the flame bush, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, brightly colored flowers that typically range from red to orange. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant blooms make it an attractive addition to gardens and landscapes.
