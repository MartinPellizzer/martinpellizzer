Salsola lutea

Salsola lutea, commonly known as bluestem or yellow gooseweed, is a flowering plant in the Amaranthaceae family.

It is a perennial shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of Europe, Asia, and North Africa. The plant has small, clustered leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It is adapted to survive in harsh environments with low water availability.

Salsola lutea is often found in disturbed soils and can be invasive in certain areas.
