Swertia calycina

Swertia calycina, commonly known as the blue gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has lance-shaped leaves with a smooth texture. Its flowers bloom in early summer and are often visited by pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Swertia calycina is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color.
