Sitanion hystrix var. hordeoides

Sitanion hystrix var. hordeoides, commonly known as the hordeoid bristlegrass, is a perennial grass species native to certain regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, bristly leaves and compact growth habit, which help it thrive in dry and semi-arid environments.
