Subpilocereus ottonis

Subpilocereus ottonis, commonly known as the Otto's night bloomer, is a cacti species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, spiny stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces striking, night-blooming flowers that are typically white or pale pink. These flowers open at night and emit a subtle fragrance, attracting nocturnal pollinators.

It is a popular choice for collectors due to its unique appearance and ornamental value.
