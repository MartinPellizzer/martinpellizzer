Salvia miltiorrhiza var. miltiorrhiza

Salvia miltiorrhiza var. miltiorrhiza, commonly known as Chinese sage or red sage, is a perennial herb native to China.

It is characterized by its square, hairy stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces vibrant purple to red flowers in whorled clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its roots are the primary part used in traditional medicine, known for their medicinal properties.

The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
