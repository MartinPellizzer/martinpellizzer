Selysia Smithii

Selysia smithii is a rare and fascinating species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. This delicate plant is known for its strikingly colorful flowers that bloom in vibrant shades of pink and white. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the undergrowth of dense rainforests. Due to habitat loss and over-collection, Selysia smithii is now considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique and beautiful orchid for future generations.
