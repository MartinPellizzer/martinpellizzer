Scutellaria orichalcea

Scutellaria orichalcea, commonly known as the golden skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its showy, violet to yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. It has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Scutellaria orichalcea is often found in regions with Mediterranean climates, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
