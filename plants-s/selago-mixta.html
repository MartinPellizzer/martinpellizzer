Selago mixta

Selago mixta, commonly known as the hybrid selago, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Selago mixta is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its hardy nature and attractive foliage.
