Stellularia media var. nemorum

Stellularia media var. nemorum, commonly known as the woodland aster, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern North America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and stream banks, preferring well-drained soils. The plant features slender, upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its showy, violet-blue flower heads appear in late summer to early autumn, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

It is valued in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
