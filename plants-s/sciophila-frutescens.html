Sciophila frutescens

Sciophila frutescens, commonly known as the dwarf morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, pale blue or white flowers that bloom in the early morning. Sciophila frutescens thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in shaded areas.
