Sideritis × viciosoi

Sideritis × viciosoi, commonly known as the hybrid Greek violet, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant produces small, violet-blue flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are typically narrow and slightly hairy, with a fragrant aroma.

Sideritis × viciosoi is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
