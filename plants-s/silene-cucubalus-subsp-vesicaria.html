Silene cucubalus subsp. vesicaria

Silene cucubalus subsp.

vesicaria, commonly known as the bladder bladder, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and clusters of small, pink to reddish-purple flowers.
