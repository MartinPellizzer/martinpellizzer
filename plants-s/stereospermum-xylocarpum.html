Stereospermum xylocarpum

Stereospermum xylocarpum, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Bignoniaceae family and is characterized by its thick, dark bark and dense, hard wood. Due to its slow growth and valuable timber, it is considered a vulnerable species in some regions.
