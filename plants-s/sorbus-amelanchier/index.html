Sorbus Amelanchier

Sorbus amelanchier, commonly known as the service tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to north america. it is known for its showy white flowers and bright red berries that attract birds and wildlife. the plant thrives in woodland edges and rocky soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. with its historical significance and ecological benefits, it plays an important role in both natural and cultivated environments.
