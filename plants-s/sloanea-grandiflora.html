Sloanea grandiflora

Sloanea grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered sloanea, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and showy, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces woody, elongated fruits that contain small, dark seeds. Sloanea grandiflora is often found in moist, shaded forest environments, where it thrives in humid climates.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in botanical gardens and ecological studies.
