Staavia verticillata

Staavia verticillata, commonly known as the clustered everlasting or spiral everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spirally arranged clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. The plant produces numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds, which contribute to its widespread distribution. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in grasslands and coastal areas.

Staavia verticillata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in floral arrangements.
