Salsola arbuscula var. laeviuscula

Salsola arbuscula var. laeviuscula, commonly known as sandmat, is a flowering plant in the Amaranthaceae family.

It is a low-growing shrub or perennial herb with small, fleshy, spine-tipped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy or gravelly soils.

Its succulent leaves help it retain water, making it well-suited for dry climates.
