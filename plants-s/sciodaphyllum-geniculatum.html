Sciodaphyllum geniculatum

Sciodaphyllum geniculatum, commonly known as the twisted-leaf fern, is a rare and unique plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, Sciodaphyllum geniculatum is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
