Statice minuta subsp. dissitiflora

Statice minuta subsp.

dissitiflora, commonly known as the dwarf statice, is a small flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, pink to red flowers.

The plant typically grows in dry, sandy or rocky soils and is well adapted to coastal environments. Its small stature and dense foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

It blooms from late spring to early autumn, adding vibrant color to gardens and natural landscapes.
