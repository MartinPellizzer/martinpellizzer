Schefflera wallichiana

Schefflera wallichiana, commonly known as the Himalayan umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of large, leathery, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in montane forests.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm climates.
