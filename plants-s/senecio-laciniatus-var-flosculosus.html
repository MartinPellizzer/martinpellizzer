Senecio laciniatus var. flosculosus

Senecio laciniatus var. flosculosus, commonly known as the Shiny Leaf Senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety blue-green foliage and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a desirable ornamental plant.
