Stomatium ermininum

Stomatium ermininum, commonly known as the silverleaf stoma, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It features small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling tiny stars. The plant has finely textured, silver-green leaves that give it its common name and help it reflect sunlight in its native habitat. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its limited distribution and vulnerability to habitat loss, it is considered a species of conservation concern.
