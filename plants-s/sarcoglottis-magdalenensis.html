Sarcoglottis magdalenensis

Sarcoglottis magdalenensis, commonly known as the Magdalena arrowroot, is a herbaceous plant native to Colombia.

It belongs to the family Araceae and is characterized by its large, arrowhead-shaped leaves. The plant produces a spadix inflorescence surrounded by a spathe, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its rhizomes are used in traditional medicine and as a source of starch.
