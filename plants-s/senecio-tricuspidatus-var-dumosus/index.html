Senecio Tricuspidatus Var. Dumosus

Senecio tricuspidatus var. dumosus is a unique and striking succulent native to southern Africa. Known for its dense, cushion-like growth habit, this plant forms compact clusters of fleshy, green leaves. Its foliage is often tinged with shades of blue or purple, adding a distinctive visual appeal. Thriving in well-drained soil and full sun, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping. This variety is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators and its resilience in arid environments.
