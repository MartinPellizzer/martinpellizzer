Sedum formosanum subsp. formosanum

Sedum formosanum subsp.

formosanum, commonly known as the Formosan stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Taiwan. It features fleshy, upright stems and opposite, ovate leaves that are typically green with a bluish tinge.

The plant produces clusters of star-shaped yellow flowers in the summer months. It is adapted to rocky and sunny environments, thriving in well-drained soils.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
