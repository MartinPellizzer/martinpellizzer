Sedum hametianum

Sedum hametianum, commonly known as the Desert Stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of Africa and the Middle East.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in dry conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its hardy nature and low maintenance requirements make it an ideal plant for drought-tolerant landscaping.
