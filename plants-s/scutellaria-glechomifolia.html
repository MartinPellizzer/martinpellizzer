Scutellaria glechomifolia

Scutellaria glechomifolia, commonly known as blueweed or blue skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features square, hairy stems and opposite, heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture. Its small, blue or violet flowers are clustered in whorls along the stem, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is often found in moist, shaded areas such as woodlands and stream banks.
