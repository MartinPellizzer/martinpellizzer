Stachys tenuifolia var. latidens

Stachys tenuifolia var. latidens, commonly known as narrowleaf betony, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces spikes of small, pinkish to violet flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate foliage and long blooming period.
