Solena triflora

Solena triflora, commonly known as the three-flowered solena, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky soils. The plant features slender, branching stems and small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters of three. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs.

Solena triflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
