Senecio rosei

Senecio rosei, commonly known as the roseless groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial with bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has hairy, fleshy leaves that help it conserve water in arid conditions. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and ragworts.

Senecio rosei is often cultivated in rock gardens and dry landscapes due to its ornamental value and hardy nature.
