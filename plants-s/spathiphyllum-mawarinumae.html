Spathiphyllum mawarinumae

Spathiphyllum mawarinumae, commonly known as the Mawarina peace lily, is a tropical evergreen plant native to the Philippines.

It features broad, dark green, heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces striking white spathes that enclose small, greenish-white flowers, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in low-light conditions and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Spathiphyllum mawarinumae is valued for its air-purifying qualities and ornamental appeal in indoor gardening.
