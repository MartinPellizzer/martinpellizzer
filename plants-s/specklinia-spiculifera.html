Specklinia spiculifera

Specklinia spiculifera, commonly known as the spiky specklinia, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to tree trunks or branches in its natural habitat. Its leaves are glossy and have a distinctive waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture in humid environments.

Specklinia spiculifera is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation under proper conditions.
