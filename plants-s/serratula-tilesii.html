Serratula tilesii

Serratula tilesii, commonly known as the Turkish groundsel, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Serratula tilesii is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
