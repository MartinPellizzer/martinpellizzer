Sideritis discolor

Sideritis discolor, commonly known as the white heath, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its small, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are typically narrow and gray-green, with a slightly hairy texture.

Sideritis discolor is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is valued for its medicinal properties.
