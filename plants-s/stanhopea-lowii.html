Stanhopea lowii

Stanhopea lowii, commonly known as the Low's stanhopea, is a rare and exquisite orchid native to the mountainous regions of Colombia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its pseudobulbs are relatively short and stout, supporting its evergreen foliage.

Stanhopea lowii is highly valued in horticulture for its beauty and rarity, making it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
