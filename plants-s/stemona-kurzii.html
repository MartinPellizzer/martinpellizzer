Stemona kurzii

Stemona kurzii, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a perennial herb native to China.

It features a slender, cylindrical stem that is often used in traditional medicine. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, bell-shaped, and typically white or pale blue in color.

Stemona kurzii is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly in treating respiratory and digestive ailments.
