Stanhopea panamensis

Stanhopea panamensis, commonly known as the Panamanian stanhopea, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Stanhopea panamensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
