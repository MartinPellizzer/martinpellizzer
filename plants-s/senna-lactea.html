Senna lactea

Senna lactea, commonly known as white senna, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces long, slender pods that contain numerous seeds. It is often found in sandy or coastal soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Senna lactea is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
