Scutia capensis var. parvifolia

Scutia capensis var. parvifolia, commonly known as the narrowleaf scutia, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, narrow leaves that are typically arranged opposite along the stems. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
