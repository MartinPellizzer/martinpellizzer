Sagraea columneifolia

Sagraea columneifolia, commonly known as the slender columnar plant, is a striking succulent native to southern Africa.

It features a tall, unbranched stem that can reach several meters in height, giving it a column-like appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. Its leaves are small, fleshy, and arranged in a rosette at the base, aiding in water storage.

Sagraea columneifolia is well-suited for arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
