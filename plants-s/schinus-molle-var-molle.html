Schinus molle var. molle

Schinus molle var. molle, commonly known as the velvet tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its smooth, grayish-brown bark and small, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety pubescence. The tree produces small, red, fleshy fruits that contain a single seed, which is dispersed by animals. It is widely cultivated for its ornamental value and for its use in traditional medicine.

The plant is also notable for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and climates.
