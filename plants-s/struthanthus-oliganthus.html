Struthanthus oliganthus

Struthanthus oliganthus, commonly known as the desert star, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial with fleshy, succulent stems and leaves adapted to arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Struthanthus oliganthus is often found in rocky soils and is well-suited to xerophytic conditions.
