Smilax fokiensis

Smilax fokiensis, commonly known as Fokiense arrowwood, is a species of flowering plant in the genus Smilax, native to Southeast Asia.

It is a climbing shrub characterized by its woody stems and spiny growth patterns. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in umbels. Its leaves are heart-shaped with a rough texture and are often covered in fine hairs.

Smilax fokiensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
