Stachys fragilis var. rhodopaea

Stachys fragilis var. rhodopaea, commonly known as the pink hedge nettle, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems covered in soft, glandular hairs. The plant produces small, pink to purple flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with serrated edges and a hairy surface.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or disturbed habitats.
