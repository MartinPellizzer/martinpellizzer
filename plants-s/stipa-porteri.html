Stipa porteri

Stipa porteri, commonly known as the Porteri's feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and dense, fluffy panicles that give it a distinctive, feathery appearance. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in cool, dry conditions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a slight bluish or grayish hue.

Stipa porteri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique visual characteristics.
