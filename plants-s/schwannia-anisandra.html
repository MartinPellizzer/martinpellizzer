Schwannia anisandra

Schwannia anisandra, commonly known as the anise-scented thistle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features silver-green, woolly leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Schwannia anisandra is valued for its ornamental appeal and has mild aromatic properties.
