Sideritis × bornmuelleri

Sideritis × bornmuelleri, commonly known as the hybrid sages, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants known for their medicinal properties. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. Its leaves are usually gray-green and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its drought-resistant nature.

Sideritis × bornmuelleri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and historical use in traditional herbal medicine.
