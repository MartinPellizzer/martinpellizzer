Selliguea × Khasiana

Selliguea × khasiana is a rare and fascinating hybrid fern native to the humid forests of the Eastern Himalayas. This plant is known for its delicate, feather-like fronds that display a striking combination of green and silver hues. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found growing in shaded, rocky crevices. Due to its limited distribution and unique characteristics, it is considered a valuable species for botanical study. Its beauty and rarity make it a subject of interest for both scientists and plant enthusiasts alike.
