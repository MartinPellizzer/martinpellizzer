Scindapsus coriaceus

Scindapsus coriaceus, commonly known as the satin pothos, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a silvery underside. The plant is characterized by its ability to thrive in low light conditions and its trailing growth habit. It belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other popular houseplants like peace lilies.

Scindapsus coriaceus is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
