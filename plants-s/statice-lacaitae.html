Statice lacaitae

Statice lacaitae, commonly known as the pink statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit. The plant produces showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

Statice lacaitae is often used in garden landscapes for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
