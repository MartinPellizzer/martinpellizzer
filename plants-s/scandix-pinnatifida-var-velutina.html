Scandix pinnatifida var. velutina

Scandix pinnatifida var. velutina, commonly known as velvetleaf, is a herbaceous annual plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, velvety hairs, giving it its common name. Its roots are fibrous and can become invasive, making it a problematic weed in agricultural settings.
