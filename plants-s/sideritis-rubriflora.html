Sideritis rubriflora

Sideritis rubriflora, commonly known as red-flowered ironweed, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright stems that are hairy and often branched, with opposite leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to deep red, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in rocky or limestone soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats.

Sideritis rubriflora is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly for its potential calming and anti-inflammatory effects.
