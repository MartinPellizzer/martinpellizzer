Stelis micacea

Stelis micacea, commonly known as the silver-stem orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, often silver-hued stems. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, tropical environments. Its flowers are usually small and white or pale green, with a delicate, fragrant aroma.

Stelis micacea prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
