Serissa gardneri

Serissa gardneri, commonly known as the silver leaf serissa, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the year, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant has dense, leathery foliage that gives it a compact and bushy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for container gardening.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor landscapes.
