Solanum tuberosum var. diemii

Solanum tuberosum var. diemii, commonly known as the Diemii potato, is a botanical variant of the common potato.

It is characterized by its unique tuber morphology, which often exhibits distinct coloration and texture compared to standard potato varieties. The plant belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and shares genetic traits with other Solanum species. Its tubers are typically smaller and have a higher starch content, making them suitable for specific culinary uses.

This variety is of interest to botanists and agricultural researchers due to its potential for genetic diversity and adaptation to different environmental conditions.
