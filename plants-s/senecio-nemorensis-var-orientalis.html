Senecio nemorensis var. orientalis

Senecio nemorensis var. orientalis, commonly known as the Oriental ragwort, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellowish-green foliage. The plant produces small, clustered flower heads that are typically yellow or brown in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
