Sedum maireanum

Sedum maireanum, commonly known as the Maire's stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a valuable addition to drought-tolerant landscapes.
