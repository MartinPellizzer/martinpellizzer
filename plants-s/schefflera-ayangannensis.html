Schefflera ayangannensis

Schefflera ayangannensis, commonly known as the Ayangannensis umbrella tree, is a species of flowering plant in the Araliaceae family.

It is native to Cameroon and is characterized by its large, compound leaves that resemble an umbrella. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by red berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements, it is popular among plant enthusiasts.
