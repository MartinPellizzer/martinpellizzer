Satureja florida

Satureja florida, commonly known as sweet basil or summer basil, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, pale blue to purple flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are often used in cooking for their aromatic, slightly sweet scent.

Satureja florida thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
