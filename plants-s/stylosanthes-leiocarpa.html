Stylosanthes leiocarpa

Stylosanthes leiocarpa, commonly known as the silky stylo, is a perennial leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a fibrous root system and is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. It is valued for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, improving soil fertility.

Stylosanthes leiocarpa is often used as forage for livestock due to its high nutritional value and drought tolerance.
