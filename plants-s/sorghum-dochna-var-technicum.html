Sorghum dochna var. technicum

Sorghum dochna var. technicum, commonly known as Sudan grass, is a tall, warm-season grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its robust growth habit and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types. The plant produces long, narrow leaves and can reach heights of up to 3 meters. It is often cultivated for forage, erosion control, and as a source of biomass.

Its botanical features make it adaptable to both temperate and tropical climates.
