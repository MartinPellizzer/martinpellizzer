Sedum kamtschaticum f. zokuriense

Sedum kamtschaticum f. zokuriense, commonly known as the Zokuri stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and fleshy, often blue-green leaves. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
