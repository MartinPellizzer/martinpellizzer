Saxifraga aizoon subvar. recta

Saxifraga aizoon subvar. recta, commonly known as the upright saxifrage, is a perennial flowering plant native to arctic and alpine regions of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, thriving in full sun to partial shade. The plant features compact, upright growth with small, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are usually white or pink, arranged in dense clusters at the ends of the stems.

It is valued for its hardiness and ability to withstand cold climates, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine plantings.
