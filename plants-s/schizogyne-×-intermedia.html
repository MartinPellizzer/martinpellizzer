Schizogyne × intermedia

Schizogyne × intermedia, commonly known as the hybrid spider plant, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Africa.

It features rosettes of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, spider-like flowers in clusters. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its tolerance to drought conditions. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.

Schizogyne × intermedia is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
