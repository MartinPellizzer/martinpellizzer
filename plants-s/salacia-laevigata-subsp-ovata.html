Salacia laevigata subsp. ovata

Salacia laevigata subsp.

ovata, commonly known as the smooth-leafed salacia, is a member of the Salaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its smooth, ovate leaves that are typically glossy and dark green in color.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by elliptical, orange-red berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
