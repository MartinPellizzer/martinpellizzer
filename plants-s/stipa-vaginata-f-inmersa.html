Stipa vaginata f. inmersa

Stipa vaginata f. inmersa, commonly known as the submerged false brome, is a variant of the false brome grass.

It is a perennial grass species native to certain regions of Europe and Asia. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

This form is distinguished by its habit of growing in a more submerged or low-lying position compared to the typical form.
