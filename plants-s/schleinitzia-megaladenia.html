Schleinitzia megaladenia

Schleinitzia megaladenia, commonly known as the giant Schlernia, is a striking member of the Asteraceae family.

Schleinitzia megaladenia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
