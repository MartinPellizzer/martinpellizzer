Smithorchis calceoliformis

Smithorchis calceoliformis, commonly known as the helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and thrives in acidic soils. The plant features a short, rhizomatous stem with a single, showy flower that is often pale green or yellowish in color. Its unique, helmet-shaped labellum is a distinguishing characteristic.

The species is notable for its specialized pollination mechanism involving specific insect species.
