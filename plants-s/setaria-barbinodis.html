Setaria barbinodis

Setaria barbinodis, commonly known as the hairy beardgrass, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands. The plant is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and spikelet-based inflorescences. Its seeds are small and typically dispersed by wind, contributing to its wide distribution.
