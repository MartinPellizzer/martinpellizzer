Swintonia floribunda

Swintonia floribunda, commonly known as the red-flowered orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy red blooms that appear in clusters, often resembling orchids in appearance. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are narrow and pointed, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Swintonia floribunda is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its unique and eye-catching flowers.
