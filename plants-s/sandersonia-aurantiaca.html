Sandersonia aurantiaca

Sandersonia aurantiaca, commonly known as the flame lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that bear large, showy flowers with bright orange petals and a red throat. The plant's botanical name reflects its vibrant coloration, with "aurantiaca" referring to its orange hue. It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is known for its dramatic, fleeting blooms.

The leaves are strap-like and often remain after the flowers have faded, contributing to its ornamental appeal.
