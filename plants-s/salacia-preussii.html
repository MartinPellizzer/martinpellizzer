Salacia preussii

Salacia preussii, commonly known as the Preuss's salacia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Salaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are often clustered in racemes. Its leaves are typically alternate, elliptical, and have a leathery texture.

Salacia preussii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
