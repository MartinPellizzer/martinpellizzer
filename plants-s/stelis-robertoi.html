Stelis robertoi

Stelis robertoi, commonly known as the Robert's spider orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, spider-like appearance with long, slender sepals and petals. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers are usually pale green or white, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insects.

Stelis robertoi is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
