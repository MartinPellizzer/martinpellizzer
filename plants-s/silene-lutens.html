Silene lutens

Silene lutens, commonly known as the sunset silene, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family, characterized by its showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often branched and upright.

Silene lutens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
