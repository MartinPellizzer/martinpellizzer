Stenocactus dichroacanthus var. violaciflorus

Stenocactus dichroacanthus var. violaciflorus, commonly known as the violet-flowered stenocactus, is a unique cactus native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and dense covering of spines. The plant produces striking violet or purple flowers that bloom in the spring. Its stems are typically a pale green color, and it is well adapted to arid environments.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
