Shawia

Shawia, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Gesneriaceae and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant features velvety, heart-shaped leaves and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. Shawia thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

It is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
