Salacia eurypetala

Salacia eurypetala, commonly known as the broad-leaved salacia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Salaceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and ecological studies.
