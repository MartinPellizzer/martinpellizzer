Stromanthe villosa

Stromanthe villosa, commonly known as the velvet leaf plant, is a tropical perennial herb native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety, waxy leaves that often display intricate patterns of silver and green. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 feet, with a compact, bushy habit. Its leaves are often variegated, featuring shades of white, cream, and green, which make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

It prefers bright, indirect light and consistently moist, well-draining soil.
