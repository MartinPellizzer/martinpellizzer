Solanum stellativillosum

Solanum stellativillosum, commonly known as the starry hairy nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its small, star-shaped flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, which contribute to its common name. It produces small, round berries that are usually green when unripe and turn red when mature.

Solanum stellativillosum is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in moist, shaded environments.
