Stellularia media var. vulgaris

Stellularia media var. vulgaris, commonly known as the broadleaf arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for wetland restoration and ornamental gardening.
