Stelis longiracemosa

Stelis longiracemosa, commonly known as the long-branch stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with erect, branching stems that can reach several centimeters in length. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming during the summer months. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding environment.

Stelis longiracemosa thrives in humid, shaded conditions and is often found growing on trees in tropical rainforests.
