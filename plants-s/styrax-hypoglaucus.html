Styrax hypoglaucus

Styrax hypoglaucus, commonly known as the Chinese frankincense tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its opposite, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, which are usually white or pale yellow. Its bark is aromatic and has been used in traditional medicine for its resinous properties.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in perfumery and medicinal applications.
