Stipa macroglossa var. kungeica

Stipa macroglossa var. kungeica, commonly known as the Kungei feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and dense, feathery inflorescences that give it a delicate, ornamental appearance. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue, and it produces small, spike-like clusters of florets.

This variety is valued for its ornamental use in landscaping and for its ecological role in stabilizing soil in arid environments.
