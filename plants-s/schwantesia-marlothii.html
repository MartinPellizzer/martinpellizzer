Schwantesia marlothii

Schwantesia marlothii, commonly known as the Marloth's dandelion, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow composite flowers that bloom in spring, followed by fluffy seed heads. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Schwantesia marlothii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to local pollinators.
