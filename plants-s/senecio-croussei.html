Senecio croussei

Senecio croussei, commonly known as the Crousse's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It features small, daisy-like flowers that are usually yellow or white in color.

Senecio croussei is often found in open, sunny habitats and is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable conditions.
