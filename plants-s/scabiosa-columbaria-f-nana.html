Scabiosa columbaria f. nana

Scabiosa columbaria f. nana, commonly known as the dwarf garden clover, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe.

It features small, delicate flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in clusters atop slender stems. The plant has hairy leaves that are trifoliate, meaning they consist of three leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its compact form and long blooming period make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
