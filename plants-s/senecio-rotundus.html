Senecio rotundus

Senecio rotundus, commonly known as the African Daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb with a bushy growth habit and produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has rounded, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its botanical name reflects its historical association with the genus Senecio, which includes many species with similar characteristics.
