Sphenostylis homblei

Sphenostylis homblei, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to its environment. The plant has compound leaves with elongated leaflets, and its bark is often smooth and grayish in appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release its seeds.
