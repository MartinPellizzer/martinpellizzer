Stellaria diversiflora f. yakumontana

Stellaria diversiflora f. yakumontana, commonly known as the Yakumontan starwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its small, star-shaped white flowers and delicate, linear leaves. Native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in cool, moist environments. Its flowers typically bloom in early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in rock gardens or shaded borders.
