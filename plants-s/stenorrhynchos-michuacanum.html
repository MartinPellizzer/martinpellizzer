Stenorrhynchos michuacanum

Stenorrhynchos michuacanum, commonly known as the Michuacan spider orchid, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, spider-like appearance due to its elongated, thin petals. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually small and white or pale green, with a distinctive, intricate structure.

Stenorrhynchos michuacanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
