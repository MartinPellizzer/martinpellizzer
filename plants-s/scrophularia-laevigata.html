Scrophularia laevigata

Scrophularia laevigata, commonly known as smooth scrophularia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. The plant features upright stems with opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are usually hairy. Its flowers are tubular and range in color from pink to purple, blooming in late spring to early summer.

It is often found in disturbed soils and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural areas.
