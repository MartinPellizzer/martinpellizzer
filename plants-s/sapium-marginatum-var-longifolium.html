Sapium marginatum var. longifolium

Sapium marginatum var. longifolium, commonly known as the broad-leaf Chinese lantern plant, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive red or orange coloration on the underside. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into bright red, lantern-like fruits. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
