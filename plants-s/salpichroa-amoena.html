Salpichroa amoena

Salpichroa amoena, commonly known as the purple morning glory, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in the early morning. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, round fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, sunny environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Salpichroa amoena is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
