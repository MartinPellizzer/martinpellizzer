Syringa koehneana

Syringa koehneana, commonly known as the Chinese lilac, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, clustered flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant produces small, oval leaves that are dark green and glossy on top. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens in temperate climates.

Syringa koehneana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in landscaping for its aromatic blooms.
