Siphonochilus bambutiorum

Siphonochilus bambutiorum, commonly known as the African water fern, is a species of aquatic fern native to Africa.

It is characterized by its finely divided, floating fronds that give it a delicate, lace-like appearance. The plant typically grows in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and lakes. Its rhizomes form dense mats on the water surface, helping to stabilize the substrate and provide habitat for aquatic life.

Siphonochilus bambutiorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in aquatic ecosystems.
