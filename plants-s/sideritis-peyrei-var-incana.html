Sideritis peyrei var. incana

Sideritis peyrei var. incana, commonly known as Peyre's ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its hairy, gray-green leaves and small, pale blue or violet flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in rocky or dry soils and is often found in mountainous areas.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
