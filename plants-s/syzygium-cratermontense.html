Syzygium cratermontense

Syzygium cratermontense, commonly known as the crater montane syzygium, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the highland regions of the Philippines, specifically on Mount Makiling. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in montane forests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
