Stenogyne kipahuluensis

Stenogyne kipahuluensis, commonly known as the Kipahulu ironwood, is a rare endemic species native to the island of Maui in Hawaii.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in moist, shaded habitats within the rainforest. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered, with five petals that are usually white or pale pink in color.
