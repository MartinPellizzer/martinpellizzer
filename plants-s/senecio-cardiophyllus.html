Senecio cardiophyllus

Senecio cardiophyllus, commonly known as the African snowflake, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
