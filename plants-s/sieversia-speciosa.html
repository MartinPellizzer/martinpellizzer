Sieversia speciosa

Sieversia speciosa, commonly known as the showy sieversia, is a striking plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of pink and purple. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a waxy texture, and its inflorescence consists of clustered, bell-shaped blooms. Sieversia speciosa thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value has led to its cultivation in botanical gardens and as a landscape plant.
