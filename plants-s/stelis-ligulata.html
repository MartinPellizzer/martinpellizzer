Stelis ligulata

Stelis ligulata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. Its leaves are narrow and elongated, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Stelis ligulata is prized for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
