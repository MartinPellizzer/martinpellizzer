Senecio molleri

Senecio molleri, commonly known as the Blue Dustplant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of tiny, blue-gray leaves that form a compact, bushy appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to its foliage. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
