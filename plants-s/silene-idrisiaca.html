Silene idrisiaca

Silene idrisiaca, commonly known as the Idrisi's campion, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its pink or white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in coastal areas. It has linear leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Due to its limited distribution, Silene idrisiaca is considered a rare and endemic species.
