Senna Smithiana

Senna smithiana is a strikingly beautiful shrub native to the coastal regions of southern Africa. Known for its vibrant yellow flowers and feathery foliage, it adds a dramatic touch to any garden or landscape. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a versatile choice for many climates. Its unique appearance and hardy nature have made it a popular subject among botanists and gardeners alike. Despite its beauty, Senna smithiana requires careful cultivation to ensure its health and longevity.
