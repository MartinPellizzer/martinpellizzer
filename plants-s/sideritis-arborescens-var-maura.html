Sideritis arborescens var. maura

Sideritis arborescens var. maura, commonly known as Maura shrub, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, hairy leaves and small, pale blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils, preferring full sun exposure. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs.

Its medicinal properties have been traditionally used in herbal remedies for digestive and respiratory ailments.
