Scutellaria vaniotiana

Scutellaria vaniotiana, commonly known as the Vaniot's skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color, arranged in whorls along the stem. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Scutellaria vaniotiana is valued in traditional medicine for its potential pharmacological properties.
