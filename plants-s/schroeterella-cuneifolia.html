Schroeterella cuneifolia

Schroeterella cuneifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed schroeterella, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged oppositely along the stems. Its flowers are small and clustered in whorls, often displaying vibrant colors such as red or purple.

Schroeterella cuneifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
