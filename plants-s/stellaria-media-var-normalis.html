Stellaria media var. normalis

Stellaria media var. normalis, commonly known as common chickweed, is a low-growing annual herbaceous plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It features small, white, star-shaped flowers that typically bloom in early spring to late summer. The plant has delicate, hairy stems and alternate, ovate leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in lawns, gardens, and along pathways.

Stellaria media var. normalis is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and stolons.
