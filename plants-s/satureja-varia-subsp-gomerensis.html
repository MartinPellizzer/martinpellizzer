Satureja varia subsp. gomerensis

Satureja varia subsp.

gomerensis, commonly known as Gomer's savory, is a perennial herb native to the Iberian Peninsula. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic leaves and square stems.

The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and open woodlands. Its leaves are lance-shaped and emit a strong, peppery scent when crushed.

It is valued for its culinary and potential medicinal uses.
