Stachys rossii

Stachys rossii, commonly known as the Texas betony, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features square stems, opposite leaves, and clusters of purple or pink flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.
