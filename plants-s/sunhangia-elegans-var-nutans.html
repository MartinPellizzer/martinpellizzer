Sunhangia elegans var. nutans

Sunhangia elegans var. nutans, commonly known as the drooping elegance plant, is a flowering species native to tropical regions.

It features slender, branching stems that often droop gracefully, giving it a cascading appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white and pink. Its leaves are elongated and lance-shaped, with a glossy surface that enhances its ornamental appeal.

It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.
