Senecio dubitabilis var. densicapitatus

Senecio dubitabilis var. densicapitatus, commonly known as the dense-headed senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features a low-growing, mat-forming habit with narrow, blue-green leaves. Its flowers are typically yellow, arranged in dense, spherical clusters.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
