Specklinia pertenuis

Specklinia pertenuis, commonly known as the slender speckled orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, delicate leaves and intricate, speckled patterns on its petals. This epiphytic plant typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually pale green or white with a subtle fragrance.

Specklinia pertenuis is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
