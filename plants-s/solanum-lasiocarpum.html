Solanum lasiocarpum

Solanum lasiocarpum, commonly known as the woolly nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant produces small, violet-colored flowers that bloom in summer. Its fruit is a globose berry that turns red when mature.

The leaves are covered in fine, white hairs, giving the plant its characteristic "woolly" appearance.
