Scilla welwitschii

Scilla welwitschii, commonly known as the Welwitsch scilla, is a bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It produces clusters of delicate, bell-shaped blue or violet flowers during the spring season. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge from a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its early blooming habit and striking floral display.
