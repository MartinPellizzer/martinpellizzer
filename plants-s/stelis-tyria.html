Stelis tyria

Stelis tyria, commonly known as the Tyrian stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Stelis tyria is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical botanical gardens.
