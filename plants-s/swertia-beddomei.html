Swertia beddomei

Swertia beddomei, commonly known as the Himalayan gentian, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows at high altitudes, where it thrives in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, while its stems are often hairy.

Swertia beddomei is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
