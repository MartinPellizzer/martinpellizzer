Skimmia japonica subsp. reevesiana

Skimmia japonica subsp.

reevesiana, commonly known as Japanese skimmia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to eastern Asia. It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and emit a subtle fragrance when touched.

The plant produces small, red berries that are ornamental and attract birds. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its slow growth and dense foliage make it an excellent choice for rock gardens or as a border plant.
