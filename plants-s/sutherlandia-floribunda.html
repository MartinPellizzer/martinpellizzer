Sutherlandia floribunda

Sutherlandia floribunda, commonly known as the "cancer plant," is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that grows in arid and semi-arid regions, often found in rocky soils. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer, followed by red, fleshy fruits. Its leaves are narrow and linear, and the plant has a distinctive, branched root system.

Sutherlandia floribunda is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in African herbal medicine.
