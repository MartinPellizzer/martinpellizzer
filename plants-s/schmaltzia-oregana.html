Schmaltzia oregana

Schmaltzia oregana, commonly known as Oregon thimbleweed, is a perennial herb native to the Pacific Northwest of the United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features a basal rosette of lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its small, white to pale pink flowers are arranged in a globular cluster at the top of the stem.

Schmaltzia oregana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native plant gardens.
