Stenotus latifolius

Stenotus latifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf stenotus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas. The plant features opposite, broad, lance-shaped leaves that are usually hairy. Its flowers are small and clustered in terminal inflorescences, often with a purple or pinkish hue.

Stenotus latifolius is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage.
