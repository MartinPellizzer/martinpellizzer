Saxifraga × churchillii

Saxifraga × churchillii, commonly known as the Churchill's saxifrage, is a hybrid plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or white, blooming in late summer. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to cold climates.

Saxifraga × churchillii is often found in tundra and alpine environments, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local ecosystems.
