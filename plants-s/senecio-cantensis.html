Senecio cantensis

Senecio cantensis, commonly known as the silver cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color, covered in a fine layer of white powder. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
