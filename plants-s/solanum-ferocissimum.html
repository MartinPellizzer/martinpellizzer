Solanum ferocissimum

Solanum ferocissimum, commonly known as the Brazilian nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its spiny stems and dark green, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that develop into spherical, yellowish berries. It is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its toxic properties, it is important to handle the plant with care.
