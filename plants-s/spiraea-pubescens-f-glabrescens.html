Spiraea pubescens f. glabrescens

Spiraea pubescens f. glabrescens, commonly known as the glabrous spirea, is a flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is a compact shrub characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and leaves, which are typically oval to elliptic in shape. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers in early summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

This cultivar is valued for its compact growth habit and showy floral displays.
