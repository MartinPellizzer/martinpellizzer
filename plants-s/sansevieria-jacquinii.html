Sansevieria jacquinii

Sansevieria jacquinii, commonly known as the Jacob's ladder sansevieria, is a species of flowering plant native to western Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a lighter midrib. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the summer months. It is a low-maintenance plant that thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Sansevieria jacquinii is also noted for its air-purifying qualities and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor environments.
