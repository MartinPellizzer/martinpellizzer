Stelis gratiosa

Stelis gratiosa, commonly known as the graceful stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, branching stem with numerous small, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often in clusters at the tips of the stems. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Stelis gratiosa is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
