Stenochasma ancolanum

Stenochasma ancolanum, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and daisy-like yellow flowers with a white center. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. It is often found in rocky or sandy habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Stenochasma ancolanum is a perennial shrub that is drought-tolerant and easy to maintain in gardens.
