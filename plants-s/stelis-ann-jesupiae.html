Stelis ann-jesupiae

Stelis ann-jesupiae, commonly known as the Jesup's stelis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are usually clustered at the base and are often covered in a dense, velvety pubescence.

The flowers, which bloom in spring, are typically white or pale pink and have a delicate, intricate structure.
