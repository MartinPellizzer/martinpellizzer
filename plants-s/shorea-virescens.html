Shorea virescens

Shorea virescens, commonly known as greenheart, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green heartwood, which is highly valued for its durability and resistance to decay. The plant belongs to the Dipterocarpaceae family and is typically found in lowland rainforests. Its leaves are alternate, elliptical, and have a leathery texture.

Shorea virescens plays an important ecological role in its habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
