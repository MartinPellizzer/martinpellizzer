Scrophularia shikokiana

Scrophularia shikokiana, commonly known as the Shikoku scrophularia, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae and is characterized by its upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or shaded areas.

Scrophularia shikokiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
