Sterculia Ivira

Sterculia ivira is a rare and ancient tree species native to the rainforests of western Africa. It is known for its unique, large, and heavy fruit that can weigh up to 10 kilograms. The plant plays a vital role in its ecosystem, providing food for various animals and contributing to soil fertility. Due to deforestation and habitat loss, it is now critically endangered. Conservation efforts are underway to protect and preserve this remarkable species for future generations.
