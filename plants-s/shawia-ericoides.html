Shawia ericoides

Shawia ericoides, commonly known as the heath shrub, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. The plant is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form and small, leathery leaves. Shawia ericoides produces clusters of bell-shaped, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in spring.

It prefers well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
