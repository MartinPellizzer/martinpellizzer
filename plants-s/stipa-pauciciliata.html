Stipa pauciciliata

Stipa pauciciliata, commonly known as the slender needlegrass, is a perennial grass species native to the temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands, meadows, and disturbed soils. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.

Stipa pauciciliata plays a role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for various small invertebrates.
