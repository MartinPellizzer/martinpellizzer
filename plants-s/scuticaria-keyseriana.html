Scuticaria keyseriana

Scuticaria keyseriana, commonly known as the keyser's scuticaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers arranged in a compact cluster. It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America, particularly in the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Scuticaria keyseriana is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a resilient species in harsh environments.
