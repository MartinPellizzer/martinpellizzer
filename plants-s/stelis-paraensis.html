Stelis paraensis

Stelis paraensis, commonly known as the Paraense Stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and clustered inflorescences. The plant typically produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, porous substrates for optimal growth.

Stelis paraensis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
