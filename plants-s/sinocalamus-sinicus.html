Sinocalamus sinicus

Sinocalamus sinicus, commonly known as the Chinese bamboo or golden bamboo, is a fast-growing grass species native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and dense foliage. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine. Its rhizomes are particularly valued for their medicinal properties.

Sinocalamus sinicus thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.
