Stellarioides ecklonii

Stellarioides ecklonii, commonly known as the Cape dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of fleshy, lobed leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is known for its resilience to drought conditions.
