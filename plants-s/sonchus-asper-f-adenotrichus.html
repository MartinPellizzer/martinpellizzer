Sonchus asper f. adenotrichus

Sonchus asper f. adenotrichus, commonly known as the rough hawkweed, is a variant of the common sowthistle.

It is a herbaceous annual plant characterized by its hairy, rough leaves and yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia. It typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in fields, roadsides, and waste areas.

Its botanical features make it a common weed in agricultural settings.
