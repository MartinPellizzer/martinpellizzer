Solanum assimile

Solanum assimile, commonly known as the small-flowered nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30-60 cm, featuring simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate or lanceolate in shape. Its flowers are small, usually white or pale green, and are followed by berries that mature to a dark purple color.

Solanum assimile is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
