Stelis callicentrum

Stelis callicentrum, commonly known as the golden stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a short, creeping stem with numerous small, clustered leaves. Its flowers are showy, with bright yellow petals and a distinctive greenish lip.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
