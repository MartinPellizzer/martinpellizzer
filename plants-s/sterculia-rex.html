Sterculia rex

Sterculia rex, commonly known as the royal sterculia, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is a large, evergreen tree that can grow up to 30 meters tall, characterized by its smooth, grey bark and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer, followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and horticultural contexts.
