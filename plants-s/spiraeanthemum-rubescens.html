Spiraeanthemum rubescens

Spiraeanthemum rubescens, commonly known as the red-flowered spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and its showy clusters of pink to red flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant features small, oval-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Spiraeanthemum rubescens is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of maintenance.
