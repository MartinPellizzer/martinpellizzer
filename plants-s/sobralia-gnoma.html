Sobralia gnoma

Sobralia gnoma, commonly known as the dwarf sobralia, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features compact, evergreen foliage with a rosette-like growth habit. Its flowers are typically small, fragrant, and come in shades of white, pink, and purple.

Sobralia gnoma is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
