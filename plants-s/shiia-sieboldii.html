Shiia sieboldii

Shiia sieboldii, commonly known as the Japanese white pine, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Pinaceae and is characterized by its slender, pyramidal shape and long, needle-like leaves. Shiia sieboldii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and parks.
