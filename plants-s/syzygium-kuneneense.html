Syzygium kuneneense

Syzygium kuneneense, commonly known as the Kunene apple, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to southern Africa, specifically found in Namibia and Angola. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and produces small, fragrant white flowers. The fruit is a small, round berry with a smooth, red or yellow skin and a sweet, aromatic pulp.

It is often grown for its ornamental value and has limited use in local cuisines.
