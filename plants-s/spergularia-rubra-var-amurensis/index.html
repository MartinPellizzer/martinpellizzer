Spergularia Rubra Var. Amurensis

Spergularia rubra var. amurensis is a unique variant of the common sea sandwort known for its distinct adaptations to coastal environments. This plant thrives in sandy and saline soils, often found along shorelines where it plays a vital role in stabilizing dunes. Its small, red-tinged flowers bloom in early spring, adding a splash of color to the otherwise barren coastal landscape. The species is notable for its ability to tolerate extreme conditions, including salt spray and strong winds. Despite its hardy nature, it remains a rare and specialized plant, highlighting the diversity of life in challenging ecosystems.
