Senecio namibensis

Senecio namibensis, commonly known as the Namib Desert groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the Namib Desert in Namibia.

It is a low-growing, succulent perennial that thrives in arid and coastal environments. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom during the rainy season. Its leaves are typically fleshy and covered in a fine layer of white wax, helping to reduce water loss.

Senecio namibensis is adapted to extreme conditions, making it a resilient species in its harsh habitat.
