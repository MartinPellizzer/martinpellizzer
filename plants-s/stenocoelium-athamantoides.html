Stenocoelium athamantoides

Stenocoelium athamantoides, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its striking, bright red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has compound leaves with numerous small leaflets and produces pods that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The tree is also valued for its hard wood and has traditional uses in some regions.
