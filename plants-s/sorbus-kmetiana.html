Sorbus kmetiana

Sorbus kmetiana, commonly known as the Kmetian whitebeam, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, clustered, white flowers that appear in early spring. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. Its leaves are ovate to elliptical, with serrated edges, and turn vibrant yellow in autumn.

Sorbus kmetiana is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in its native habitat.
