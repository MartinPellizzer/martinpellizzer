Salsola vermiculata var. frankenioides

Salsola vermiculata var. frankenioides, commonly known as Frankenioides saltbush, is a halophytic shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of central Asia.

It thrives in saline and alkaline soils, demonstrating remarkable adaptability to harsh environmental conditions. The plant has small, fleshy leaves that help reduce water loss, and its flowers are typically greenish-white, appearing in clusters. It plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and preventing erosion in degraded landscapes.

This variety is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its resilience and ability to colonize disturbed sites.
