Sobralia × intermedia

Sobralia × intermedia, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Sobralia and another orchid species, resulting in a unique blend of characteristics.

It is a tropical evergreen shrub that produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink, red, and orange. The plant has thick, leathery leaves that help it retain moisture in its native habitat. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a potted plant.
