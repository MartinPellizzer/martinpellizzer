Sonchus × maynari

Sonchus × maynari, commonly known as the hybrid groundsel, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant features lanceolate leaves with serrated edges and produces yellow composite flowers. It is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes.

Due to its invasive nature, it is often considered a weed in agricultural and natural settings.
