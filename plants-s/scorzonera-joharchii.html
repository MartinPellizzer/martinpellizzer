Scorzonera joharchii

Scorzonera joharchii, commonly known as the Kashmiri scorzonera, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan regions of Pakistan and India.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and yellow flowers. The plant produces a tuberous root that is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits. It thrives in high-altitude meadows and is adapted to cool, moist climates.

Scorzonera joharchii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in alpine ecosystems.
