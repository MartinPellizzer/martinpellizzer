Stichorkis nugentiae

Stichorkis nugentiae, commonly known as the African orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, often epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically blooming during the summer months. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
