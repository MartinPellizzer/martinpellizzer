Schoenoxiphium sparteum var. lehmannii

Schoenoxiphium sparteum var. lehmannii, commonly known as the Lehmann's sparteum, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Argentina.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in dense clusters, and bloom during the spring and early summer.

It is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.
