Schaefferia buxifolia

Schaefferia buxifolia, commonly known as the boxleaf schaefferia, is a striking evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a velvety texture, resembling the leaves of boxwood plants. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers during the spring and summer months. Schaefferia buxifolia thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental appeal make it a versatile addition to both landscapes and container gardens.
