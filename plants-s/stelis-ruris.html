Stelis ruris

Stelis ruris, commonly known as the rural stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically found growing on trees in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with narrow, linear leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered, often in a raceme, with a distinctive coloration that varies by species.

Stelis ruris thrives in humid environments with indirect light and well-draining substrates.
