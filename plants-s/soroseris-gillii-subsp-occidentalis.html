Soroseris gillii subsp. occidentalis

Soroseris gillii subsp.

occidentalis, commonly known as the western gillii sorrel, is a perennial herb native to the western United States. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, inconspicuous yellow flowers.

The plant produces oval, hairy leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

Its botanical features make it well-adapted to arid environments and it is often found in disturbed or marginal soils.
