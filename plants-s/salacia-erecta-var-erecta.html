Salacia erecta var. erecta

Salacia erecta var. erecta, commonly known as the climbing salacia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Salicaceae.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including India and Malaysia, where it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant is characterized by its woody stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in racemes, followed by small, fleshy berries.

The botanical features of Salacia erecta var. erecta make it an interesting subject for botanical study and horticultural cultivation.
