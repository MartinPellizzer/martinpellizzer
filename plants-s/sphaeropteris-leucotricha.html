Sphaeropteris leucotricha

Sphaeropteris leucotricha, commonly known as the white-haired cycad, is a species of cycad native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its stout, unbranched trunk and large, pinnately compound leaves that are covered in dense, white hairs. The leaves are typically arching and can reach lengths of up to 3 meters, giving the plant a distinctive appearance. The plant produces cone-like structures, with male and female cones occurring on separate plants.

It is a slow-growing species that thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with well-drained soil.
