Swertia mussotii var. mussotii

Swertia mussotii var. mussotii, commonly known as Indian snapdragon, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is a perennial herb with upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces vivid blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas.

Its medicinal properties have been utilized in traditional Ayurvedic practices for various health benefits.
