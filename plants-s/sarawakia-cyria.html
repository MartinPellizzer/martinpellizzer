Sarawakia cyria

Sarawakia cyria, commonly known as the Bornean flame orchid, is a tropical orchid native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery red and orange flowers that bloom during the wet season. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to tree bark and deriving moisture and nutrients from the air. Its leaves are broad and waxy, adapted to retain water in the humid environment.

Sarawakia cyria plays a vital role in the ecosystem, providing nectar for pollinators and contributing to the biodiversity of its habitat.
