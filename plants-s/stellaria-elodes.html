Stellaria elodes

Stellaria elodes, commonly known as the slender starwort, is a small flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family and typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features delicate, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are opposite, linear, and often covered in fine hairs.

Stellaria elodes is often found in low-light conditions and is adapted to thrive in nutrient-rich soils.
