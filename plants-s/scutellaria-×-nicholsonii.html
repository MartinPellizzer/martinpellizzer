Scutellaria × nicholsonii

Scutellaria × nicholsonii, commonly known as the hybrid blue skullcap, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or wetland habitats.

Scutellaria × nicholsonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, supporting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
