Sideritis incana var. virgata

Sideritis incana var. virgata, commonly known as silver hair vetch, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and small, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of blue to purple. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Sideritis species are known for their medicinal properties, and this variety is no exception.
