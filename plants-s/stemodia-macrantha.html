Stemodia macrantha

Stemodia macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered stemodia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring woody stems covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly serrated margin, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

The plant is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
