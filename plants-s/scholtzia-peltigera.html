Scholtzia peltigera

Scholtzia peltigera, commonly known as the desert dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Scholtzia peltigera produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom in early spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
