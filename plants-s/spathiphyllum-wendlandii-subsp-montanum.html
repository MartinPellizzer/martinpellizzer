Spathiphyllum wendlandii subsp. montanum

Spathiphyllum wendlandii subsp.

montanum, commonly known as the mountain peace lily, is a tropical plant native to Central America. It is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape.

The plant produces striking white spathes that enclose small, greenish-yellow flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both ornamental and horticultural purposes.
