Stenactis pilosa

Stenactis pilosa, commonly known as the hairy stenactis, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers arranged in compact clusters. It is typically found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments.

Stenactis pilosa is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental value.
