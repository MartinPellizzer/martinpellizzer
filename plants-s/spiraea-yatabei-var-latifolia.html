Spiraea yatabei var. latifolia

Spiraea yatabei var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf spiraea, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are typically broad and elliptical, with a smooth or slightly serrated margin. This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

It is also noted for its adaptability to partial shade to full sun.
