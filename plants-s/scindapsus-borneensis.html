Scindapsus borneensis

Scindapsus borneensis, commonly known as the Bornean prayer plant, is a tropical evergreen vine native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, waxy, heart-shaped leaves that often display intricate variegation. The plant is known for its ability to climb using aerial roots, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments with high air humidity. Its foliage changes color in response to light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Scindapsus borneensis is also noted for its ability to purify air, contributing to its popularity among plant enthusiasts.
