Senecio × telonensis

Senecio × telonensis, commonly known as the hybrid blueweed, is a flowering plant in the sunflower family, Asteraceae.

It is a perennial shrub native to South Africa, characterized by its distinctive blue or purple flowers and hairy, silver-green foliage. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening, though it can be invasive in some regions.

Senecio × telonensis is known for its ability to produce copious amounts of seed, contributing to its spread and ecological impact.
