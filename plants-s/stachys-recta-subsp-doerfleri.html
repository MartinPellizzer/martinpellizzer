Stachys recta subsp. doerfleri

Stachys recta subsp.

doerfleri, commonly known as the narrow-leaved betony, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It features upright, hairy stems that bear opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture.

The plant produces clusters of small, pale blue to purple flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long blooming period.
