Stelis bidentata

Stelis bidentata, commonly known as the two-toothed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and strikingly patterned flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish in color, with a distinctive two-toothed structure at the base of the lip.

Stelis bidentata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
