Sedum Stenopetalum Var. Monanthum

Blue sedum, scientifically known as Sedum stenopetalum var. monanthum, is a striking succulent native to the rocky habitats of southern Africa. This plant is celebrated for its vibrant blue-green foliage and compact growth habit, making it a popular choice among gardeners and collectors. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established. Its unique appearance and low maintenance needs have made it a favorite in modern xeriscaping and container gardening. With its ability to withstand harsh conditions, blue sedum is both a resilient and visually appealing addition to any landscape.
