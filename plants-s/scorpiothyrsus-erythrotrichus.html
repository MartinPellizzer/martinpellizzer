Scorpiothyrsus erythrotrichus

Scorpiothyrsus erythrotrichus, commonly known as the red-haired scorpiothyrsus, is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

It is native to southern Africa, where it thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features slender, erect stems covered in dense, reddish-brown hairs, giving it its common name. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in clusters, and it produces fluffy, wind-dispersed seeds.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
