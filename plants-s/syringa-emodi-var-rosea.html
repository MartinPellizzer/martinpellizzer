Syringa emodi var. rosea

Syringa emodi var. rosea, commonly known as the rose-scented lilac, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is a deciduous shrub that produces fragrant, pink or white flowers in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, dark green leaves and can grow up to 3 meters in height. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Syringa emodi var. rosea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its showy blooms.
