Solanecio mirabilis

Solanecio mirabilis, commonly known as the Marvelous Sunflower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers with a distinctive dark center. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas and has hairy stems and leaves. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.
