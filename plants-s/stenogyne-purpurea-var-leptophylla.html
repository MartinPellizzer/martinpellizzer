Stenogyne purpurea var. leptophylla

Stenogyne purpurea var. leptophylla, commonly known as the slender-leaved purple milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Hawaii.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its small, purple flowers and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. It is an endemic species, found only on the island of Hawaii, and plays a role in local ecosystems.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and is protected under conservation efforts.
