Solanum aureifolium

Solanum aureifolium, commonly known as the goldenleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of purple and white. The plant belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known species like tomatoes and potatoes. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

However, it contains toxic alkaloids and should be handled with care.
