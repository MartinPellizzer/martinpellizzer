Spilotantha ova-avis

Spilotantha ova-avis, commonly known as the bird's egg plant, is a member of the Malvaceae family.

It is a shrub-like plant native to South Africa, characterized by its distinctive, egg-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are typically covered in a fine, white pubescence, giving them a soft appearance.

While not widely cultivated, it is appreciated for its ornamental value and unique foliage structure.
