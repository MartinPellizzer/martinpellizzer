Strobilanthes rufocapitata

Strobilanthes rufocapitata, commonly known as the red-headed flame flower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red bracts that surround the small, yellowish flowers, which bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and shrublands. Its foliage consists of broad, lance-shaped leaves with a smooth texture.
