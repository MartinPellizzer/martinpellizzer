Stellaria nipponica

Stellaria nipponica, commonly known as Japanese starwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features slender, branching stems and small, white or pale pink flowers that resemble stars, giving it its common name. The plant typically has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Stellaria nipponica is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
