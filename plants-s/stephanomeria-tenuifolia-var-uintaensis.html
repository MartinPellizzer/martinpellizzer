Stephanomeria tenuifolia var. uintaensis

Stephanomeria tenuifolia var. uintaensis, commonly known as Uinta slenderleaf, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, such as sagebrush steppe and rocky slopes. It features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish to white flowers.

Its adaptations to dry conditions include a deep root system and reduced leaf surface area.
