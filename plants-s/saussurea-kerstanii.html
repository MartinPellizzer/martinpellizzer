Saussurea kerstanii

Saussurea kerstanii, commonly known as the Kashmir saffron or the Himalayan saffron, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stem and numerous yellow to orange flower heads. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically arranged in a dense cluster at the top of the stem. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slight hairy texture.

Saussurea kerstanii is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects.
