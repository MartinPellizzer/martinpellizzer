Sacoglottis guianensis f. sphaerocarpa

Sacoglottis guianensis f. sphaerocarpa, commonly known as the Guianan sacoglottis, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its unique, spherical, red berries that are a striking feature of the plant. The leaves are typically large, broad, and have a waxy surface, which helps in reducing water loss. This plant thrives in tropical wet environments, often found in the understory of dense rainforests.

Its botanical structure supports its role as a food source for various wildlife species.
