Salvia rosmarinoides

Salvia rosmarinoides, commonly known as Spanish sage, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, aromatic stems and lance-shaped leaves with a strong, minty fragrance. The plant produces spikes of blue to violet flowers, which bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a favored plant in both gardens and horticultural collections.
