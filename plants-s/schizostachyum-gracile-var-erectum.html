Schizostachyum gracile var. erectum

Schizostachyum gracile var. erectum, commonly known as the slender bamboo palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that give it a grass-like appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests. Its rapid growth rate makes it suitable for ornamental use in gardens and as a houseplant.

The varietal form 'erectum' distinguishes it from other varieties by its more upright growth habit.
