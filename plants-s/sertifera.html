Sertifera

Sertifera, commonly known as the Christmas star, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its star-shaped, bright yellow flowers that bloom during the winter months. The plant has a woody stem and produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Sertifera thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and seasonal blooms.
