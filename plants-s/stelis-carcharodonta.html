Stelis carcharodonta

Stelis carcharodonta, commonly known as the dragon's tongue orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, elongated floral structure. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are often brightly colored, with a distinctive shape that resembles a dragon's tongue.

Stelis carcharodonta prefers warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
