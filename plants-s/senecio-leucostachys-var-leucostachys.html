Senecio leucostachys var. leucostachys

Senecio leucostachys var. leucostachys, commonly known as the white-stemmed groundsel, is a herbaceous perennial native to South Africa.

It features slender, upright stems covered in dense, white to pale green hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers arranged in a cylindrical shape, typical of the Asteraceae family. Its leaves are linear and often covered with fine, white pubescence.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy environments.
