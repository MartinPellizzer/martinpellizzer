Senecio tsoongianus

Senecio tsoongianus, commonly known as the Tsoong's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped cluster. It is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
