Scutellaria leucodasys

Scutellaria leucodasys, commonly known as the white-hair skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and medicinal plants. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular blue or violet flowers. It typically grows in moist, wooded areas and thrives in partial shade.

Scutellaria leucodasys is often found in the understory of forests, where it plays a role in maintaining ecological balance.
