Syzygium tiumanense

Syzygium tiumanense, commonly known as the Tiumanense clove.

This evergreen shrub or small tree is native to the Philippines and belongs to the Myrtaceae family. It is characterized by its aromatic, pinkish-white flowers and elliptical, glossy leaves. The plant produces small, dark red berries that are edible but not commonly consumed.

Syzygium tiumanense is often cultivated for its ornamental value and its unique fragrance.
