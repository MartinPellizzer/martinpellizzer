Sedum flavidum

Sedum flavidum, commonly known as the yellow stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to North America.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, yellowish-green leaves that are arranged in a dense rosette. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped, yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Sedum flavidum is drought-tolerant and requires minimal maintenance once established.
