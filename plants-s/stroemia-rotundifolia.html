Stroemia rotundifolia

Stroemia rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leafed stroemia, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small, evergreen shrub characterized by its rounded, leathery leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, oval fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Stroemia rotundifolia is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forest understories and along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the local flora and a subject of interest in botanical studies.
