Sinocrassula vietnamensis

Sinocrassula vietnamensis, commonly known as the Vietnam stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Vietnam.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming growth habit. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color, adapted to store water. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the warmer months.

Sinocrassula vietnamensis is well-suited for rock gardens and arid environments due to its drought tolerance.
