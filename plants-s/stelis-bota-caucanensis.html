Stelis bota-caucanensis

Stelis bota-caucanensis, commonly known as the Cauca Orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of Colombia.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy flowers. The plant typically produces small, fragrant blooms in shades of white and pink, often with a yellow or green lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil for optimal growth.

Stelis bota-caucanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
