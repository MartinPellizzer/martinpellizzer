Sideritis euboea

Sideritis euboea, commonly known as Euboean ironwort, is a flowering plant native to the Greek island of Euboea.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Sideritis euboea is characterized by its hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves.

It is often found growing in rocky or sandy soils in open habitats.
