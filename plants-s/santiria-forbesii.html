Santiria forbesii

Santiria forbesii, commonly known as the African star of Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often branched.

Santiria forbesii is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
