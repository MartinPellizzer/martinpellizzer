Sedum aizoon var. austromanshuricum

Sedum aizoon var. austromanshuricum, commonly known as the Korean stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to East Asia.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is adapted to rocky and arid environments, making it drought-tolerant and easy to care for. Its leaves are typically opposite and can vary in shape depending on environmental conditions.

It is often used in rock gardens and as a ground cover due to its hardy nature and ornamental appeal.
