Senecio pseudaureus subsp. flavulus

Senecio pseudaureus subsp.

flavulus, commonly known as the yellow-flowered senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and ragworts.

The plant is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically tubular and arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or arid environments.
