Seriphidium junceum var. macrosciadium

Seriphidium junceum var. macrosciadium, commonly known as the broad-leaved wormwood, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and aromatic foliage. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces yellow flowers in late summer. It thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used historically in traditional herbal remedies.
