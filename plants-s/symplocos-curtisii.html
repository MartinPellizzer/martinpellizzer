Symplocos curtisii

Symplocos curtisii, commonly known as the Curtis's waxleaf.

It is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in India and Nepal. The plant is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and pale underneath. It produces small, white flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by edible, red berries.

Symplocos curtisii is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
