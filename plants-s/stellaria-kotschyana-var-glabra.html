Stellaria kotschyana var. glabra

Stellaria kotschyana var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth starwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia.

It features small, white or pale blue star-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The leaves are typically narrow and linear, with a smooth surface that distinguishes it from other varieties of Stellaria. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in challenging environments.
