Statice ebracteata var. laevis

Statice ebracteata var. laevis, commonly known as the smooth statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces dense clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for coastal and dry garden settings.

The variety 'laevis' is distinguished by its smooth, hairless stems and leaves, setting it apart from other statice species.
