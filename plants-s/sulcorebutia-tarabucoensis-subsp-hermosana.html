Sulcorebutia tarabucoensis subsp. hermosana

Sulcorebutia tarabucoensis subsp.

hermosana, commonly known as the Tarabuco beauty, is a compact cactus native to the Andes of Peru. It features a short, cylindrical stem with a distinctive reddish or brownish coloration.

The plant produces clusters of small, vibrant yellow flowers during the spring season. It is well adapted to arid and rocky environments, thriving in full sun.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for rock gardens and succulent collections.
