Sutera tenuis

Sutera tenuis, commonly known as narrowleaf sutera, is a perennial herb native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves with a smooth texture. Its flowers are typically pale blue or purple, arranged in whorls along the stem.

Sutera tenuis is often found in moist, open habitats such as woodlands and wetlands.
