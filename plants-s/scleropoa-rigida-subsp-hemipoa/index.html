Scleropoa Rigida Subsp. Hemipoa

Scleropoa rigida subsp. hemipoa is a resilient grass species native to the high-altitude regions of the European Alps. It thrives in harsh, rocky environments where few plants can survive. This subspecies is characterized by its rigid, upright growth and dense, dark green foliage. It plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and preventing erosion in its arid habitats. Due to its unique adaptations, it is considered an important indicator of alpine ecological health.
