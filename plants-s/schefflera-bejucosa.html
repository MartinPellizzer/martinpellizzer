Schefflera bejucosa

Schefflera bejucosa, commonly known as the Brazilian Schefflera, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of leaves, which gives it a distinctive and ornamental appearance. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters in its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually glossy and dark green, with a slightly waxy texture.

Schefflera bejucosa is often cultivated for its aesthetic value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
