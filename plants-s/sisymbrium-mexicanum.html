Sisymbrium mexicanum

Sisymbrium mexicanum, commonly known as Mexican shepherd's purse, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other mustard plants. The plant produces small, rounded pods that resemble a purse, giving it its common name. It typically grows in dry, disturbed soils and is often found in grasslands and along roadsides.
