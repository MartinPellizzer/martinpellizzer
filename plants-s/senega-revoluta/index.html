Senega Revoluta

Senega revoluta is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the southeastern united states. it belongs to the legume family and is known for its unique spiral-shaped leaves and small, pale purple flowers. this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks. due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is now classified as endangered. conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this remarkable species for future generations.
