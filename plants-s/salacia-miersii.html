Salacia miersii

Salacia miersii, commonly known as the Miers' salacia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Salaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are typically alternate and ovate, with a smooth texture.

Salacia miersii is often grown for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and unique floral structure.
