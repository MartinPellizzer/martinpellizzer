Stellaria aetnensis

Stellaria aetnensis, commonly known as the Aetna starwort, is a flowering plant native to the volcanic slopes of Mount Etna in Sicily.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with slender stems and small, star-shaped white flowers. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to the harsh conditions of a volcanic environment.

Stellaria aetnensis is of interest to botanists due to its unique ecological niche and potential medicinal properties.
