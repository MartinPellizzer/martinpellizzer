Sisymbrium arnottianum

Sisymbrium arnottianum, commonly known as the Arnott's mustard, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes other mustard plants. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It produces small, yellow flowers and has slender, branched stems.
