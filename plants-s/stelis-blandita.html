Stelis blandita

Stelis blandita, commonly known as the orchid of the genus Stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Stelis blandita is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
