Syzygium kanarense

Syzygium kanarense, commonly known as the white flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Mascarene Islands.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has smooth, greyish bark and elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green. Its fruit is a woody capsule containing several seeds, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Syzygium kanarense thrives in warm, humid climates and is well-suited for coastal areas.
