Schefflera diversifoliolata

Schefflera diversifoliolata, commonly known as the Variegated Schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which consist of multiple leaflets arranged in a distinctive, umbrella-like pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage.

Schefflera diversifoliolata is also valued for its adaptability to indoor conditions, making it a popular choice for houseplants.
