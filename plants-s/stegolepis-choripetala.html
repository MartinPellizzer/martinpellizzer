Stegolepis choripetala

Stegolepis choripetala, commonly known as the red-flowered orchid, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, vibrant red petals and a slender, erect stem. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as rainforests, where it thrives in the understory. Its flowers are often fragrant and attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Stegolepis choripetala is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
