Stachys riederi f. angustifolia

Stachys riederi f. angustifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaved betony, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine hairs and possesses lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with silver. The plant produces spikes of violet to blue flowers, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate foliage and long-lasting blooms.
