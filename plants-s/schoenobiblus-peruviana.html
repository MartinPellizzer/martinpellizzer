Schoenobiblus peruviana

Schoenobiblus peruviana, commonly known as the Peruvian feather grass, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance. The plant produces small, delicate inflorescences that resemble feathers, giving it its common name. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it requires well-drained soil and full sun.
