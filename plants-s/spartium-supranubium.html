Spartium supranubium

Spartium supranubium, commonly known as cloud spartium, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, typical of the legume family. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine environments.

Spartium supranubium is notable for its ability to tolerate harsh climatic conditions and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
