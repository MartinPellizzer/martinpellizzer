Stelis cajanumae

Stelis cajanumae, commonly known as the orchid vine, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a climbing plant that produces delicate, fragrant flowers in various colors, including white, yellow, and pink. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a waxy texture.

Stelis cajanumae thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
