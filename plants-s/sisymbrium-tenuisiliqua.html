Sisymbrium tenuisiliqua

Sisymbrium tenuisiliqua, commonly known as narrow-leaved babylon, is a herbaceous annual plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, branching stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The leaves are narrow and linear, often covered with fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

This plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
