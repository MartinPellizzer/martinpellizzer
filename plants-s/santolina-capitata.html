Santolina capitata

Santolina capitata, commonly known as Spanish santolina, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, needle-like leaves that are aromatic and gray-green in color. The plant produces compact, spherical clusters of yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to dry, sunny conditions and is often used in rock gardens or as a border plant.

Santolina capitata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
