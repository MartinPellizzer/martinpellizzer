Syringa pubescens var. potaninii

Syringa pubescens var. potaninii, commonly known as the Chinese lilac, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features fragrant, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of white to pale pink in the spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its ornamental value is further enhanced by its attractive bark and autumn foliage.
