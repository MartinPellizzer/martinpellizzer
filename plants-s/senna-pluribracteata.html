Senna pluribracteata

Senna pluribracteata, commonly known as the yellow senna, is a flowering plant native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters tall, characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in disturbed soils.

Senna pluribracteata is known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
