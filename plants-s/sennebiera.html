Sennebiera

Sennebiera, commonly known as the sacred tree of the Yoruba people, is a tropical plant native to West Africa.

It belongs to the family Anacardiaceae and is characterized by its large, palmate leaves and fragrant flowers. The tree produces a resin that has been traditionally used in religious and medicinal practices. Sennebiera is also valued for its ornamental appearance and is often cultivated in gardens.
