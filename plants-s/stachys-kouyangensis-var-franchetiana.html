Stachys kouyangensis var. franchetiana

Stachys kouyangensis var. franchetiana, commonly known as the Yunnan betony, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant features violet to purple flowers that bloom in whorled clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental value.

This variety is valued in horticulture for its attractive foliage and floral display.
