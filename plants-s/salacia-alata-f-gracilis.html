Salacia alata f. gracilis

Salacia alata f. gracilis, commonly known as the slender-winged salacia, is a unique variant of the Salacia alata species.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves and reduced, wing-like structures compared to the typical form. The plant is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its botanical features include small, clustered flowers that are often yellow or greenish in color.

This variety is of interest to botanists and horticulturists due to its distinct morphology and potential ornamental value.
