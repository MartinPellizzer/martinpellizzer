Stelis picta

Stelis picta, commonly known as the picta orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, brightly colored flowers that often feature intricate patterns. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its leaves are usually broad and strap-like, and it produces small, fragrant blooms throughout the year.

Stelis picta is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
