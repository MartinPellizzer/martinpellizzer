Syngonanthus gracilis var. olivaceus

Syngonanthus gracilis var. olivaceus, commonly known as the olive-green spikerush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetlands and marshy areas in the southeastern United States.

This variety is distinguished by its pale green to olive-colored inflorescences, which contrast with the darker green foliage of the typical varietal form.
