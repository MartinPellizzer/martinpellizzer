Stipularia

Stipularia, commonly known as the stipularia grass, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plants typically grow in wet or moist environments, such as marshes and wetlands. Stipularia species are often found in temperate regions and are known for their ability to thrive in waterlogged soils.
