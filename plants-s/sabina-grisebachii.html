Sabina grisebachii

Sabina grisebachii, commonly known as the Chinese thuja, is a species of conifer native to China.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub characterized by its dense, pyramidal shape and scale-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green female cones and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

The botanical features of Sabina grisebachii make it a hardy and adaptable plant for various climates.
