Salsola botschantzevii

Salsola botschantzevii, commonly known as Russian thornweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is a shrub-like plant characterized by its spiny stems and small, fleshy leaves. Native to Central Asia, it thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in steppes and deserts. The plant produces small greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences.

It is adapted to survive in harsh conditions with minimal water availability.
