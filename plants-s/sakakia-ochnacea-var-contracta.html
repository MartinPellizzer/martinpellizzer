Sakakia ochnacea var. contracta

Sakakia ochnacea var. contracta, commonly known as the African glorybower, is a shrub native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the family Ochnaceae and is characterized by its striking, showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are usually opposite and ovate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
