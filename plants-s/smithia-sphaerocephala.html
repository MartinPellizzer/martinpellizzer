Smithia sphaerocephala

Smithia sphaerocephala, commonly known as the globe-headed Smithia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, spherical inflorescence that gives it its common name. The plant features small, delicate flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to cold, alpine environments.

Smithia sphaerocephala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens and alpine settings.
