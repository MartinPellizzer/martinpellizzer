Schelveria

Schelveria, commonly known as the golden cup plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, yellow, cup-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges, and it typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet. Schelveria thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Its botanical name, *Schelveria*, is derived from the genus name honoring the botanist Schelver.
