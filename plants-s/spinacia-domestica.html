Spinacia domestica

Spinacia domestica, commonly known as spinach, is a leafy green vegetable belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its dark green, slightly serrated leaves and edible stems. The plant is native to the Mediterranean region but is now cultivated worldwide. Spinach is rich in nutrients such as iron, vitamins A and C, and antioxidants.

Its growth is typically rapid, with plants reaching maturity in about 40 to 50 days under optimal conditions.
