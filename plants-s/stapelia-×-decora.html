Stapelia × decora

Stapelia × decora, commonly known as the Decorated Stapelia, is a hybrid succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, waxy, and often ribbed leaves that are typically green with prominent purple or reddish markings. The plant produces striking, lantern-shaped flowers that are usually yellow or orange with a dark center, blooming in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight to flourish.

Due to its ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
