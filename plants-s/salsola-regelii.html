Salsola regelii

Salsola regelii, commonly known as Russian thistle or goatweed, is a flowering plant native to the steppes of Central Asia.

It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy stems and leaves. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense, spherical heads. It is highly adaptable, thriving in arid and saline environments, making it a common weed in disturbed soils.

Salsola regelii is also known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and vegetative reproduction.
