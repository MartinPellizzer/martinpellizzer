Sesbania uliginosa

Sesbania uliginosa, commonly known as swamp sesbania, is a tropical leguminous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its pinnately compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in wetland environments and is often found in marshy areas or along riverbanks. It has a deep root system that helps stabilize soil and prevent erosion.

Sesbania uliginosa is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it useful in agricultural and ecological restoration contexts.
