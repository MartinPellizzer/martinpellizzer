Schizachne purpurascens subsp. callosa

Schizachne purpurascens subsp.

callosa, commonly known as the scarlet grass or red panicgrass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas. It is characterized by its bright red or orange inflorescences, which are often used in ornamental horticulture.

The plant typically grows in moist, sunny environments such as wetlands and riverbanks. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a distinctive reddish hue on the lower surface.

It is a perennial species that spreads through rhizomes, forming dense clumps.
