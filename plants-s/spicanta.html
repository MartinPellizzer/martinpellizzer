Spicanta

Spicanta, commonly known as the spiky weed, is a flowering plant characterized by its distinctive spines and clustered leaves.

It typically grows in dry, arid environments, thriving in regions with low rainfall. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often overlooked due to the plant's defensive spines. Its root system is deep and robust, allowing it to access water from underground sources.

Spicanta is often considered a weed in agricultural areas due to its rapid growth and ability to colonize disturbed soils.
