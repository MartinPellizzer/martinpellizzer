Susilkumara

Susilkumara, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers with intricate patterns resembling flames. The plant has strap-like leaves that are broad and slightly curled at the edges, providing a lush green contrast to its vibrant blooms. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Susilkumara is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a popular choice in botanical gardens and private collections.
