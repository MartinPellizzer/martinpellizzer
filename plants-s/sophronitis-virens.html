Sophronitis virens

Sophronitis virens, commonly known as the green orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant greenish-yellow flowers and lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in the understory of forests. Its flowers are clustered in inflorescences and emit a mild fragrance.

Sophronitis virens is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts worldwide.
