Sabina chinensis f. albovariegata

Sabina chinensis f. albovariegata, commonly known as the variegated Chinese juniper, is a cultivar of the Chinese juniper tree.

It is characterized by its unique foliage, featuring white and yellow variegation on its needle-like leaves. This plant is evergreen and typically grows in a compact, bushy form. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

The variegation makes it a popular choice for ornamental purposes in gardens and landscapes.
