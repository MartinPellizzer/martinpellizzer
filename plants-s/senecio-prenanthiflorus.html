Senecio prenanthiflorus

Senecio prenanthiflorus, commonly known as the African marguerite, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features yellow flowers that are surrounded by white ray florets, giving it a daisy-like appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Senecio prenanthiflorus is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
