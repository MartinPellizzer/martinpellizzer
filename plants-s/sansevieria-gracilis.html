Sansevieria gracilis

Sansevieria gracilis, commonly known as the slender snake plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, upright leaves that are typically narrow and have a smooth, waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. Sansevieria gracilis is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that thrives in a variety of light conditions.

Its ability to purify indoor air makes it a popular choice for both residential and commercial spaces.
