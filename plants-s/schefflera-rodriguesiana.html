Schefflera rodriguesiana

Schefflera rodriguesiana, commonly known as the Rodrigues schefflera, is a tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of leaflets, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters in its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually glossy and dark green, with a smooth texture.

Schefflera rodriguesiana is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
