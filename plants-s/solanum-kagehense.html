Solanum kagehense

Solanum kagehense, commonly known as the Kagehense nightshade, is a species native to eastern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by berries that range in color from green to red. Its leaves are usually ovate or elliptic with a slightly hairy surface.

Solanum kagehense belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is known for its potential medicinal properties in traditional use.
