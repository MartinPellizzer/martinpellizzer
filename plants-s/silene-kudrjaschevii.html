Silene kudrjaschevii

Silene kudrjaschevii, commonly known as the Kudrjaschev's campion, is a species of flowering plant in the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in Kazakhstan and Uzbekistan. The plant features erect, branched stems and produces pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, typical of many Caryophyllaceae species.

Silene kudrjaschevii is adapted to rocky and arid environments, showcasing its resilience to harsh climatic conditions.
