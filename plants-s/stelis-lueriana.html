Stelis lueriana

Stelis lueriana, commonly known as the Lueriana orchid, is a species native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its epiphytic growth habit, often found growing on tree branches or rocks. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and yellow. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Stelis lueriana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
