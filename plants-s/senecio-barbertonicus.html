Senecio barbertonicus

Senecio barbertonicus, commonly known as the Barberton Daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Senecio barbertonicus is also noted for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
