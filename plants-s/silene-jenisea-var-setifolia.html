Silene jenisea var. setifolia

Silene jenisea var. setifolia, commonly known as the slender-flowered campion, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, pink to red flowers. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.

Silene jenisea var. setifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native environment.
