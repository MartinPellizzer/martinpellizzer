Schaefferia cuneifolia

Schaefferia cuneifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf schaefferia, is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family and is native to South Africa.

It is a bulbous perennial plant characterized by its strap-shaped, grass-like leaves that are typically narrow and pointed. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers during the summer months. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Schaefferia cuneifolia is relatively low maintenance and resistant to pests and diseases, making it a popular choice for gardeners.
