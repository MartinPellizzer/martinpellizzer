Senecio mitophyllus

Senecio mitophyllus, commonly known as the silverleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-white, woolly leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a cluster at the tip of its stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Senecio mitophyllus is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
