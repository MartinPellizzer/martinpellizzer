Salacia annettae

Salacia annettae, commonly known as the golden rain tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Salicaceae, which includes willows and poplars. The plant is characterized by its showy, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a serrated edge.

The tree can grow up to 15 meters tall and is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological benefits.
