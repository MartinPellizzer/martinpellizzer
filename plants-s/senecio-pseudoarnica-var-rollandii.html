Senecio pseudoarnica var. rollandii

Senecio pseudoarnica var. rollandii, commonly known as the Rolland's blueweed, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small blue or purple flowers. It thrives in dry, rocky environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
