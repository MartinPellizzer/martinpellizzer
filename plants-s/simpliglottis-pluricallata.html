Simpliglottis pluricallata

Simpliglottis pluricallata, commonly known as the rough-leaved daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, gray-green leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has a compact growth habit and is often used in rock gardens due to its ornamental value.
