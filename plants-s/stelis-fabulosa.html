Stelis fabulosa

Stelis fabulosa, commonly known as the fairy orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, pendulous inflorescences and small, brightly colored flowers that range from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic growth habit. Its leaves are narrow and arranged in a rosette, and it produces a fragrant bloom during the summer months.

Stelis fabulosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
