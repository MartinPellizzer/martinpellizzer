Stelis pyramidalis

Stelis pyramidalis, commonly known as the pyramidal orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on tree trunks or rocks. Its leaves are elongated and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Stelis pyramidalis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
