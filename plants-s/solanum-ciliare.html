Solanum ciliare

Solanum ciliare, commonly known as the silky nightshade, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant features small, star-shaped flowers that are typically purple or white in color. Its leaves are covered in fine hairs, giving them a silvery appearance.

Solanum ciliare is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
