Stylosanthes nunoi

Stylosanthes nunoi, commonly known as the smallflower stylosanthes, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its erect or prostrate growth habit and trifoliate leaves. The flowers are typically small, yellow or white, and arranged in racemes. It has a deep root system that helps in soil stabilization and nitrogen fixation.

This plant is often used in forage and soil conservation due to its hardiness and adaptability.
