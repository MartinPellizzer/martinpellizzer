Syzygium scandens

Syzygium scandens, commonly known as the climbing syzygium or the Chinese starfruit tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant is a vigorous climber, often found growing along fences, walls, or trees in its natural habitat. Its fruit, which resembles a star-shaped starfruit, is edible and has a sweet, tangy flavor.

Syzygium scandens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
