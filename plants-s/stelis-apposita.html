Stelis apposita

Stelis apposita, commonly known as the apposita orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant typically produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and green, often with a distinct color contrast between the sepals and petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Stelis apposita is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
