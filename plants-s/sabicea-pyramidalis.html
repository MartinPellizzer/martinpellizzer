Sabicea pyramidalis

Sabicea pyramidalis, commonly known as the pyramid tree, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its tall, straight trunk and dense, pyramidal crown, which gives it its common name. Its leaves are compound, with numerous small leaflets arranged in a feathery pattern.

Sabicea pyramidalis produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by pod-like fruits containing seeds.
