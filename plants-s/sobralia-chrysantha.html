Sobralia chrysantha

Sobralia chrysantha, commonly known as the golden sobralia, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, adding a bright splash of color to its foliage. The plant features strap-like leaves that form a rosette, creating a compact and elegant shape. Its flowers are borne on tall, branching inflorescences, which can reach up to two feet in height.

Sobralia chrysantha thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in tropical gardens.
