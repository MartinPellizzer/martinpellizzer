Sedum jurgensenii subsp. jurgensenii

Sedum jurgensenii subsp.

jurgensenii, commonly known as the Jurgensen's stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow to orange.

The plant is adapted to rocky and arid environments, with thick leaves that store water. It typically grows in open habitats such as alpine meadows and slopes.

Its compact growth habit and striking floral display make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
