Solanum umbelliferum var. glabrescens

Solanum umbelliferum var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smoothleaf nightshade, is a herbaceous annual plant native to the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its hairy stems and alternate leaves that are typically lance-shaped. The plant produces small, white or pale green flowers arranged in umbels, followed by berry-like fruits that are often yellow or red when mature.

It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.
