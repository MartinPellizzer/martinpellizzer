Salpianthus aequalis

Salpianthus aequalis, commonly known as the narrowleaf false indigo, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant is often found in rocky soils and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Salpianthus aequalis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in supporting local wildlife.
