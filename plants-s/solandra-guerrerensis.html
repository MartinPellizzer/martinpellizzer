Solandra guerrerensis

Solandra guerrerensis, commonly known as the Guerrerensis lantern plant, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its showy, lantern-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. It produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds.

Solandra guerrerensis thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
