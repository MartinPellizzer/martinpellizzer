Scleria triglomerata var. minor

Scleria triglomerata var. minor, commonly known as the small clustered bulbous plant, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family (Poaceae).

It is characterized by its compact, bulbous base and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like clusters of flowers that are typically white or pale blue in color. It is native to southern Africa and thrives in well-drained soils in full sun.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to form dense, attractive ground covers.
