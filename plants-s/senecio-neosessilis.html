Senecio neosessilis

Senecio neosessilis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun exposure. Its leaves are narrow and sessile, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It produces small yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.
