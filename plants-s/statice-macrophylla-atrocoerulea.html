Statice macrophylla atrocoerulea

Statice macrophylla atrocoerulea, commonly known as the blue statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, delicate blue or purple flowers. The plant produces numerous narrow, grass-like leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy perennial in suitable climates.

The flowers are also used in floral arrangements due to their long-lasting nature and vibrant color.
