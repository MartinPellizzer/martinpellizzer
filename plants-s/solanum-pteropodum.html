Solanum pteropodum

Solanum pteropodum, commonly known as the winged nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its distinctive winged stems and small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of white and purple. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The fruit is a small, berry-like structure that turns from green to red as it matures.
