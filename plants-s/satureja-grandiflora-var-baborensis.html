Satureja grandiflora var. baborensis

Satureja grandiflora var. baborensis, commonly known as sweet savory, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, lance-shaped leaves with a pungent, slightly peppery scent. The plant produces small, pale blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

This variety is valued for its culinary uses and ornamental appeal in gardens.
