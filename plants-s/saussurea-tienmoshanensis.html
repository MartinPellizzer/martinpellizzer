Saussurea tienmoshanensis

Saussurea tienmoshanensis, commonly known as the Tienmoshan saffron, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of China, particularly in the Tienmoshan area of Yunnan Province. The plant is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems and large, showy yellow-orange flower heads. Its botanical features include narrow, linear leaves and a fibrous root system adapted to high-altitude environments.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
