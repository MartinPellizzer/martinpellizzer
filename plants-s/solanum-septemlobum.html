Solanum septemlobum

Solanum septemlobum, commonly known as the seven-lobed nightshade, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant typically features palmately lobed leaves and produces small, violet or purple flowers. Its fruit is a fleshy berry that contains several seeds.

Due to its toxic properties, it is not commonly cultivated for agricultural purposes.
