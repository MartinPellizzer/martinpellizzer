Schizanthus Coccineus

Schizanthus coccineus is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant known for its vibrant, showy blooms. Native to the Andes mountains of South America, it thrives in cooler climates and well-drained soil. Its flowers come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white, making it a popular choice for gardeners. The plant is also called the "poet's flower" due to its delicate appearance and historical associations with poetry. Despite its charm, it requires careful cultivation to ensure optimal growth and blooming.
