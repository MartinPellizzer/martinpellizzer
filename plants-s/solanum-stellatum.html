Solanum stellatum

Solanum stellatum, commonly known as the star tomato, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its star-shaped flowers. The plant produces small, yellow or orange berries that are edible when ripe. It thrives in warm, sunny climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Solanum stellatum is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
