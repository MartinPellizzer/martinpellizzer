Sempervivum requienii

Sempervivum requienii, commonly known as the Roquefort Sempervivum, is a species of succulent plant native to the Pyrenees Mountains.

It is characterized by its dense, rosette-shaped growth pattern and fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the summer months. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun exposure, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Sempervivum requienii is also known for its resilience and ability to survive in harsh climates.
