Sinobambusa tootsik var. tenuifolia

Sinobambusa tootsik var. tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender bamboo, is a variant of the Tootsik bamboo species native to China.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and slender, upright culms that give it a graceful appearance. This plant thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil with partial to full sunlight. Its slow growth rate makes it suitable for small garden spaces or container cultivation.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and traditional Chinese gardens.
