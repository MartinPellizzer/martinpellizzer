Sedum aquilanum

Sedum aquilanum, commonly known as the alpine stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to high-altitude regions of Europe and Asia.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow to orange. The plant has thick, water-storing leaves that help it survive in arid and cold environments. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its resilience and unique appearance make it a valuable addition to alpine and xeriscaped landscapes.
