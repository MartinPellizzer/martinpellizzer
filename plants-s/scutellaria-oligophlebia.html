Scutellaria oligophlebia

Scutellaria oligophlebia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic foliage. The plant features small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or violet in color, arranged in whorled clusters along the stem. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered with fine hairs.

Scutellaria oligophlebia is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
