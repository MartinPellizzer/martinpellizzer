Sideritis congesta

Sideritis congesta, commonly known as the clustered ironwort, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants such as mint and lavender. The plant is characterized by its dense clusters of small, hairy leaves and pale blue to purple flowers. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in dry, sunny environments.

Sideritis congesta is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
