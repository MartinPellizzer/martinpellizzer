Sideritis oroteneriffae var. arayae

Sideritis oroteneriffae var. arayae, commonly known as the Arawak sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the island of Trinidad.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and along stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture, while its flowers are small and clustered in whorls.

This variety is distinguished by its unique floral structure and its role in the local flora of Trinidad.
