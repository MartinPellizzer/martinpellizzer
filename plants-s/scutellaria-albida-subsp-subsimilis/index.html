Scutellaria Albida Subsp. Subsimilis

Scutellaria albida subsp. subsimilis is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia. This subspecies belongs to the mint family, known for its aromatic properties and medicinal uses. Its small, white flowers bloom in early summer, adding a subtle beauty to its natural habitat. The plant is characterized by its narrow leaves and compact growth habit, making it a unique addition to botanical collections. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a species of conservation concern.
