Saintpaulia teitensis

Saintpaulia teitensis, commonly known as the Taiti African violet, is a species native to the tropical regions of Madagascar.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces striking, velvety flowers in shades of pink, purple, and white, often with intricate patterns. Its leaves are heart-shaped, glossy, and arranged in a rosette formation.

Saintpaulia teitensis is well-suited for indoor cultivation and thrives in bright, indirect light with consistent moisture.
