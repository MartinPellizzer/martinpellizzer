Schefflera auyantepuiensis

Schefflera auyantepuiensis, commonly known as the Angel's Tears, is a species of flowering plant native to the tepuis of Venezuela.

It is characterized by its striking, umbrella-like arrangement of leaves, which are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by red, fleshy fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
