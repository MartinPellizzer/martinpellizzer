Sanguisorba officinalis var. dilutiflora

Sanguisorba officinalis var. dilutiflora, commonly known as the pale flowered sweet everlasting, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It features upright, branched stems that bear clusters of pinkish-white to pale rose-colored flowers. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or wetland habitats.

The flowers are known for their delicate appearance and are used in floral arrangements and traditional herbal medicine.
