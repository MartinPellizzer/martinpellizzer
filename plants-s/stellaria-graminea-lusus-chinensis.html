Stellaria graminea lusus chinensis

Stellaria graminea lusus chinensis, commonly known as Chinese starwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, star-shaped white flowers. The plant typically thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or wet meadows. Its growth habit and morphology make it a common ground cover in its native habitat.

Botanically, it is noted for its delicate structure and ability to spread through stolons.
