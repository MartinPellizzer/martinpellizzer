Solanum tuberosum f. jancko-chockella

Solanum tuberosum f. jancko-chockella, commonly known as the Jancko-Chockella potato, is a botanical variant of the common potato.

It is characterized by its unique foliage and tuber morphology, which distinguish it from standard potato cultivars. This form exhibits distinct leaf patterns and often displays a range of colors in its foliage. The tubers of this form are typically smaller and may have a different texture or flavor profile.

It is primarily cultivated for its ornamental value and is appreciated in botanical gardens and specialty horticulture.
