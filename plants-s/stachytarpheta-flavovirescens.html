Stachytarpheta flavovirescens

Stachytarpheta flavovirescens, commonly known as the yellow-green verbena, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to greenish-white, which attract various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
