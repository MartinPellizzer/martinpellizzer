Saurauia pedunculata f. veranii

Saurauia pedunculata f. veranii, commonly known as the Variegated Saurauia, is a rare and unique botanical variant characterized by its striking variegated leaf patterns.

This form is a fasciation variant, which results in the fusion of plant tissues, leading to unusual growth forms and foliage. It is native to specific regions in China, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features small, elliptical leaves with a combination of green, white, and yellow tones, creating a visually appealing contrast.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is highly sought after by plant enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
