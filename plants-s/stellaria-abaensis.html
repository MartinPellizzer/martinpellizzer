Stellaria abaensis

Stellaria abaensis, commonly known as the ABA starwort, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, star-shaped white flowers and delicate, hairy leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, thriving in cool and moist environments. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized species.
