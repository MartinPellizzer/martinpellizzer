Spartium hispanicum

Spartium hispanicum, commonly known as Spanish broom, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, yellowish-green foliage and clusters of small, bright yellow flowers. The plant produces small, leathery pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in sunny, open habitats.

Spartium hispanicum is known for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
