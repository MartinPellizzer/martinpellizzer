Scindapsus beccarii

Scindapsus beccarii, commonly known as the silver vine, is a tropical evergreen vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive silvery sheen, which becomes more pronounced under indirect light. The plant is known for its ability to climb or trail, making it suitable for hanging baskets or shelves. It prefers warm, humid conditions and well-draining soil, and it is relatively low maintenance.

Scindapsus beccarii is also noted for its air-purifying qualities and ease of propagation through stem cuttings.
