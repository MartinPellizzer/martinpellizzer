Stipa gilliesii

Stipa gilliesii, commonly known as the gillies' feather grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and dense clusters of narrow, needle-like leaves. The plant produces fluffy, silver-gray seed heads that give it a distinctive appearance, especially when in bloom. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Stipa gilliesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in gardens and landscaping for its elegant, wind-blown foliage.
