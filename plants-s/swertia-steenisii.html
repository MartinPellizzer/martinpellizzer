Swertia steenisii

Swertia steenisii, commonly known as the yellow gentian, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Swertia steenisii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
