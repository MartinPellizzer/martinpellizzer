Suaeda hortensis

Suaeda hortensis, commonly known as garden sea-blush, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing, herbaceous perennial that thrives in saline and coastal environments. The plant features succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain water in arid conditions. Its small, yellowish flowers bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Suaeda hortensis is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to tolerate poor soil conditions.
