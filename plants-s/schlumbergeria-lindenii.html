Schlumbergeria lindenii

Schlumbergeria lindenii, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a popular epiphytic cactus native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its segmented, leaf-like stems that are often brightly colored and used for propagation. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, pink, and white during the holiday season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Schlumbergeria lindenii is a low-maintenance plant that is well-suited for indoor cultivation.
