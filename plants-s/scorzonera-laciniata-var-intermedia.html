Scorzonera laciniata var. intermedia

Scorzonera laciniata var. intermedia, commonly known as the intermediate scorzonera, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a stout, fleshy root system that is often harvested for its edible qualities. The plant has compound leaves with deeply lobed blades, and its flowers are typically yellow with a distinctive purple or brownish hue at the base. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Scorzonera laciniata var. intermedia is valued both for its culinary use and its ornamental appeal in gardens.
