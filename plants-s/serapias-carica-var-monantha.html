Serapias carica var. monantha

Serapias carica var. monantha, commonly known as the single-flowered carpet orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces a single, showy flower per stem. The flower is typically yellow with a distinctive dark red or purple blotch at the base of the petals. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or limestone habitats.

This variety is notable for its unique floral structure and is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal.
