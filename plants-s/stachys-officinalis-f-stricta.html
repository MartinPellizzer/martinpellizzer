Stachys officinalis f. stricta

Stachys officinalis f. stricta, commonly known as narrow-leaved betony, is a flowering plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright, compact growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces spikes of violet or blue flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is native to southern Europe and is often found in dry, sunny grasslands and rocky soils.

Due to its ornamental value and historical use in herbal medicine, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
