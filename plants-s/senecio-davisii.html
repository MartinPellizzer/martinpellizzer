Senecio davisii

Senecio davisii, commonly known as the Davis's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It features small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense clusters, and its leaves are usually narrow and hairy.
