Sedum sagittipetalum

Sedum sagittipetalum, commonly known as the arrowleaf stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water storage. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its drought tolerance and attractive appearance make it a versatile addition to xeriscapes.
