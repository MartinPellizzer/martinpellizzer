Santonica achilleoides

Santonica achilleoides, commonly known as narrowleaf sandthistle, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in desert or semi-desert environments. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring.

Santonica achilleoides is considered a perennial herb and is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
