Syzygium lissophyllum

Syzygium lissophyllum, commonly known as the smooth-leaved apple.

It is a member of the Myrtaceae family and is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia. The plant is characterized by its smooth, glossy leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. Its fruit, which resembles a small apple, is edible and has a sweet, aromatic flavor.

Syzygium lissophyllum is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive appearance and fragrance.
