Sulcorebutia tarabucoensis subsp. lindae

Sulcorebutia tarabucoensis subsp.

lindae, commonly known as the Tarabucoensis Lindae Cactus, is a species of flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America. It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular shape with ribbed stems.

The plant produces yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, thriving in full sun and well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is a subject of interest for botanists and cactus enthusiasts.
