Saururopsis chinensis

Saururopsis chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese saururaceae, is a member of the family Saururaceae.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, arranged in racemes.
