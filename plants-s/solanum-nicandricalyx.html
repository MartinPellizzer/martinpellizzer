Solanum nicandricalyx

Solanum nicandricalyx, commonly known as the Nicandria eggplant, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a herbaceous plant that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and deep green color. Its flowers are usually purple or violet, and it produces egg-shaped fruits that are typically yellow or orange when ripe.

The plant belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, which includes other well-known species like tomatoes and peppers.
