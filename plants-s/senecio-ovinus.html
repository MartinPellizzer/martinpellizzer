Senecio ovinus

Senecio ovinus, commonly known as the woolly sunflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fuzzy, silver-green foliage and bright yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Senecio ovinus is also known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant.
