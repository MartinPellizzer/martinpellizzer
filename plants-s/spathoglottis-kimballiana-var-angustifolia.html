Spathoglottis kimballiana var. angustifolia

Spathoglottis kimballiana var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed spathoglottis, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support strap-like leaves. The plant produces showy, spurred orchid flowers in shades of yellow and green, typically blooming during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
