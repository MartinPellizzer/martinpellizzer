Scaphyglottis rosea

Scaphyglottis rosea, commonly known as the pink orchid.

It is a herbaceous perennial native to the tropical regions of Central and South America. The plant features showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom during the summer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Scaphyglottis rosea is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blossoms and unique foliage.
