Sida tubulosa

Sida tubulosa, commonly known as the slender sida, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Sida tubulosa is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of light conditions.

Its fibrous roots and rapid growth make it a common weed in agricultural areas.
