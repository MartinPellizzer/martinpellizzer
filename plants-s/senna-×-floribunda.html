Senna × floribunda

Senna × floribunda, commonly known as the Chinese senna or yellow glorybower, is a hybrid shrub or small tree native to China and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its palmately compound leaves with 5–7 leaflets, and it produces bright yellow, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant is known for its rapid growth and is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its showy appearance.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and as a hedge plant.
