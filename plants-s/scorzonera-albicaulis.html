Scorzonera albicaulis

Scorzonera albicaulis, commonly known as the white-stemmed scorzonera, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant produces a tuberous root system that is sometimes used in traditional medicine. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, alpine environments.

Scorzonera albicaulis is often found in meadows and slopes at high elevations.
