Sedum spectabile

Sedum spectabile, commonly known as showy sedum, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of star-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a powdery coating. It is a hardy perennial that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil.

Sedum spectabile is popular in rock gardens and as a drought-tolerant ornamental plant.
