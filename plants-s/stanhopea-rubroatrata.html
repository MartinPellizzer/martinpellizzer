Stanhopea rubroatrata

Stanhopea rubroatrata, commonly known as the red-veined orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers with deep red veins contrasting against a white or pale pink background. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its floral structure includes a prominent lip that is often marked with intricate patterns.

Stanhopea rubroatrata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is a popular species among orchid enthusiasts.
