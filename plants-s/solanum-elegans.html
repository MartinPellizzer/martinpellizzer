Solanum elegans

Solanum elegans, commonly known as the elegant nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or purple in color. Its foliage is characterized by lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture.

Solanum elegans is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and unique floral structure.
