Scleria fujianensis

Scleria fujianensis, commonly known as the Fujian bulbous sedge, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes grass-like plants known for their rhizomatous growth. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, three-lobed flowers in clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species for ecological studies in its native habitat.
