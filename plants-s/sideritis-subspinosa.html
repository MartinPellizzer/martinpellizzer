Sideritis subspinosa

Sideritis subspinosa, commonly known as the thorny sainfoin, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its small, pink or purple flowers and hairy, often spiny stems. It thrives in rocky or dry soils and is often found in mountainous areas.

Sideritis subspinosa is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
