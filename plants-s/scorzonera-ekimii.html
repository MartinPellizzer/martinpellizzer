Scorzonera ekimii

Scorzonera ekimii, commonly known as the Turkestan scorzonera, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and basal leaves. The plant produces yellowish flowers with ray florets, typically blooming in late summer. Its tuberous roots are edible and have been used in traditional medicine.

Scorzonera ekimii thrives in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh climates.
