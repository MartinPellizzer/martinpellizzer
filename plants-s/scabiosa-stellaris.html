Scabiosa stellaris

Scabiosa stellaris, commonly known as starflower, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its star-shaped, pink or white petals that resemble miniature stars, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows to a height of 10 to 30 centimeters and produces small, fluffy seed heads that are often used in floral arrangements. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Scabiosa stellaris is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
