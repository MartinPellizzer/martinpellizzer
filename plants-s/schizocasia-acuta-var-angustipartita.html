Schizocasia acuta var. angustipartita

Schizocasia acuta var. angustipartita, commonly known as the narrow-lobed spurge, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically ovate to lanceolate in shape. The plant contains toxic compounds, making it potentially harmful to livestock and humans if ingested.
