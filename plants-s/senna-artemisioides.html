Senna artemisioides

Senna artemisioides, commonly known as yellow senna, is a shrub native to the arid and semi-arid regions of Africa, the Middle East, and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in dense, clustered inflorescences. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets, and it produces yellow to orange pods that contain seeds. It is often found in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Senna artemisioides is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine for various health purposes.
