Senecio Procerus

Senecio procerus is a unique and striking plant native to southern Africa. It is known for its tall, upright growth and distinctive, feather-like leaves. This succulent thrives in arid conditions and requires minimal water to survive. Its blooms are small but colorful, adding visual interest to any garden. Despite its beauty, it can be toxic if ingested, so caution is advised when handling it.
