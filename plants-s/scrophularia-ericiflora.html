Scrophularia ericiflora

Scrophularia ericiflora, commonly known as the heath scrophularia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Scrophulariaceae family, which includes many species with showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils in open woodlands and heathlands. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with five petals and a distinctive spurred structure.

Scrophularia ericiflora is often found in association with other heathland flora, such as heathers and dwarf shrubs.
