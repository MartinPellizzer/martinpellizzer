Silene jiningensis

Silene jiningensis, commonly known as the Jining catchfly, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive pink to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and has a low, spreading growth habit. Its leaves are linear and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are usually slender and branched.

Silene jiningensis is of interest to botanists due to its limited distribution and unique adaptations to its environment.
