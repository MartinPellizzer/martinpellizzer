Sempervivum ispartae

Sempervivum ispartae, commonly known as the Isparta houseleek, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Turkey.

It features fleshy, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces upright flowering stalks that bear small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red. It is well adapted to rocky, arid environments and thrives in full sun.

Sempervivum ispartae is a hardy, low-maintenance plant popular among rock garden enthusiasts.
