Statice plantaginea var. djurdjurae

Statice plantaginea var. djurdjurae, commonly known as the Djurdjura statice, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the Middle East and North Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and purple. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it well-suited for xeriscaping. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with silvery-gray tones that add visual interest.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
