Salacia verrucosa

Salacia verrucosa, commonly known as the rough salacia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its woody stems and dense clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that are often covered in a rough, bumpy texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Salacia verrucosa is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
