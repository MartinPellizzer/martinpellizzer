Setaria distans

Setaria distans, commonly known as slender false panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny spikelets. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Setaria distans is sometimes used in ecological studies due to its adaptability and role in soil stabilization.
