Seriphidium compactum

Seriphidium compactum, commonly known as the dwarf tansy, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a small annual herb with yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant has a compact growth habit, with gray-green, hairy leaves that are aromatic. It thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in disturbed soils.

Seriphidium compactum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has historical uses in traditional medicine.
