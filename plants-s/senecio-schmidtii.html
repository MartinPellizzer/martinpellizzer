Senecio schmidtii

Senecio schmidtii, commonly known as Schmidt's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid environments. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
