Scandentia cymosa

Scandentia cymosa, commonly known as the clustered scandentia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Scandentia cymosa is often cultivated as a decorative plant for hanging baskets or as a ground cover in warm climates.
