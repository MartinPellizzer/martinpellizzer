Senecio klattii

Senecio klattii, commonly known as Klatt's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent perennial that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features upright stems with narrow, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs. Its yellow flowers are borne in clusters and bloom during the warmer months.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in arid conditions.
