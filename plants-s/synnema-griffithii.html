Synnema griffithii

Synnema griffithii, commonly known as the Indian snowball tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth upper surface. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Synnema griffithii is valued for its ornamental flowers and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
