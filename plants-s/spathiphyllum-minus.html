Spathiphyllum minus

Spathiphyllum minus, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or the peace lily, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and its ability to thrive in low-light conditions. The plant produces striking white spathes that enclose small, greenish-yellow flowers. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its air-purifying qualities and ease of care.

Spathiphyllum minus is also valued for its resilience and adaptability to various indoor environments.
