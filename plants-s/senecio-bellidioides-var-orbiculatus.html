Senecio bellidioides var. orbiculatus

Senecio bellidioides var. orbiculatus, commonly known as the orbiculate groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its small, yellow flowers bloom in spring, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

The orbiculate groundsel is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its compact growth and drought tolerance.
