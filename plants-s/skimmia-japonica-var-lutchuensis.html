Skimmia japonica var. lutchuensis

Skimmia japonica var. lutchuensis, commonly known as the Lutchu skimmia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Ryukyu Islands of Japan.

It features glossy, dark green elliptical leaves that provide a dense, textured foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers in early spring, followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value lies in its attractive foliage and seasonal flowering.
