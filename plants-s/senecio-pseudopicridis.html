Senecio pseudopicridis

Senecio pseudopicridis, commonly known as the Cape York senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. It produces small, daisy-like flowers that are usually yellow or pale green in color.
