Syagrus × fairchildensis

Syagrus × fairchildensis, commonly known as the hybrid palm, is a naturally occurring hybrid between Syagrus romanzoffiana (the royal palm) and Syagrus oleracea (the buriti palm).

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of large, feather-like leaves. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in well-drained soils and full sunlight. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.

Syagrus × fairchildensis is also valued for its ecological role, providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
