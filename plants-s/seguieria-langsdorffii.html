Seguieria langsdorffii

Seguieria langsdorffii, commonly known as the Cape fennel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant features narrow, aromatic leaves and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters. It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Botanically, it belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
