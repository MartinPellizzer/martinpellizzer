Saxifraga × gaudinii

Saxifraga × gaudinii, commonly known as the hybrid saxifrage, is a low-growing perennial plant that typically forms dense mats.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Europe and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or white, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are generally fleshy and succulent, adapted to retain moisture in harsh environments.

Saxifraga × gaudinii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
