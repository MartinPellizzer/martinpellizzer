Siphocampylus giganteus f. brachyodon

Siphocampylus giganteus f. brachyodon, commonly known as the compact giant siphocampylus, is a unique variant of the giant siphocampylus.

It is characterized by its shorter, more compact growth habit compared to the standard form. The plant features broad, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and produces striking, showy flowers in shades of purple and pink. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to various garden settings.
