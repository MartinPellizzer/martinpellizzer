Scilla bifolia f. multiflora

Scilla bifolia f. multiflora, commonly known as the multibract scilla, is a flowering plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is a bulbous perennial that produces clusters of bell-shaped, blue or violet flowers in early spring. Its ornamental value lies in its delicate flowers and its ability to naturalize over time.
