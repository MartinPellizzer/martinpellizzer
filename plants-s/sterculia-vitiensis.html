Sterculia vitiensis

Sterculia vitiensis, commonly known as the Vanuatu Sterculia, is a tropical tree native to the Pacific Islands, particularly Vanuatu.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its large, palmate leaves and showy, pinkish-white flowers. The plant produces woody capsules that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its hard, durable wood.
