Scorzonera tuberosa var. sericeolanata

Scorzonera tuberosa var. sericeolanata, commonly known as the silky scorzonera, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features a robust root system with tuberous roots that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant has dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it a silky appearance. Its flowers are yellow with a distinctive black center, and they bloom in late summer.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments.
