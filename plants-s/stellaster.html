Stellaster

Stellaster, commonly known as the star-shaped aster, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its showy, star-like flowers that typically bloom in shades of purple, pink, or white. The plants are native to various regions in the Northern Hemisphere, including Europe, Asia, and North America. Stellaster species often thrive in meadows, woodlands, and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils.

They play an important role in supporting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
