Stipa lorentziana

Stipa lorentziana, commonly known as Lorentz's feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-gray leaves and delicate, feathery inflorescences that give it a graceful appearance. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal.

Botanically, it belongs to the Poaceae family and is known for its adaptability to various soil conditions.
