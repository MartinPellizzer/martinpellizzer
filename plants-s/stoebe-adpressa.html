Stoebe adpressa

Stoebe adpressa, commonly known as narrowleaf mouse-ear.

It is a low-growing perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States. The plant features small, hairy leaves that are typically less than 1 inch in length. Its flowers are pink to purple, forming in dense clusters at the ends of stems.

Stoebe adpressa thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.
