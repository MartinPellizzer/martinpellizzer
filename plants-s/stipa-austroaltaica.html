Stipa austroaltaica

Stipa austroaltaica, commonly known as the Altai needlegrass, is a perennial grass species native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and produces narrow, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically thrives in dry, open habitats such as steppes and mountain slopes. Its leaves are long and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Stipa austroaltaica plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity in its native range.
