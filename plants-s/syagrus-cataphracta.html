Syagrus cataphracta

Syagrus cataphracta, commonly known as the golden palm, is a species of palm native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like leaves that give it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by orange-red fruits that contain a single seed. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in coastal areas and sandy soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and landscaped areas.
