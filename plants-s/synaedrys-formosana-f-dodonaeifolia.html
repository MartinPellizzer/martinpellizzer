Synaedrys formosana f. dodonaeifolia

Synaedrys formosana f. dodonaeifolia, commonly known as the Taiwan synaedrys, is a rare horticultural form of the synaedrys genus.

It is characterized by its unique foliage, featuring leaves with a striking combination of green and red hues. The plant is native to Taiwan and thrives in shaded, humid environments. Its growth habit is compact, making it suitable for container gardening.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is highly sought after by plant enthusiasts.
