Stellaria muralis

Stellaria muralis, commonly known as mouse ear chickweed, is a small annual herbaceous plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or urban environments, such as walls, sidewalks, and cracks in pavement. The plant has small, oval leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers. It is characterized by its low-growing habit and is often found in disturbed soils.

Stellaria muralis is known for its ability to thrive in harsh conditions, making it a common weed in many regions.
