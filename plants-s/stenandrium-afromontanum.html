Stenandrium afromontanum

Stenandrium afromontanum, commonly known as the East African orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the high-altitude regions of East Africa, including Kenya, Tanzania, and Uganda. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte or lithophyte, attaching itself to trees or rocks in its natural habitat. Its flowers are small and clustered, often displaying a range of colors from white to pink and purple.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
