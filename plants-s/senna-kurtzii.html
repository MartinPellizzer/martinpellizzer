Senna kurtzii

Senna kurtzii, commonly known as the African senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 4 meters in height, characterized by its bipinnate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

Senna kurtzii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some regions.
