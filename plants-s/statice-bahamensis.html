Statice bahamensis

Statice bahamensis, commonly known as the Bahamian statice, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean region, particularly the Bahamas.

It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, spike-like inflorescences. The plant produces small, colorful flowers in shades of pink, red, and purple, which bloom throughout the growing season. Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Statice bahamensis is well adapted to coastal and sandy environments, making it a hardy and resilient plant in tropical climates.
