Sloanea sinemariensis var. melinonis

Sloanea sinemariensis var. melinonis, commonly known as the Melinon's sloanea, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its elliptical, leathery leaves that are typically dark green on the upper surface and lighter on the underside. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, which are often white or pale yellow in color. Its fruits are woody capsules that contain several seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
