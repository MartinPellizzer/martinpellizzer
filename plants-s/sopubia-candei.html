Sopubia candei

Sopubia candei, commonly known as the white-leafed euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are typically pale green to white in color. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Sopubia candei is often cultivated for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
