Senecio gibbiflorus

Senecio gibbiflorus, commonly known as the wooly milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, gray-green leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its milky sap. Senecio gibbiflorus thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy species in arid environments.
