Salacia madagascariensis f. minimifolia

Salacia madagascariensis f. minimifolia, commonly known as the miniature salacia, is a rare and unique plant endemic to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Salaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate leaves and unassuming appearance. The plant exhibits a compact growth habit, with foliage that is significantly smaller than its typical counterparts in the species. It thrives in shaded, humid environments, often found in the undergrowth of tropical forests.

Due to its limited distribution and distinctive features, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
