Stigmarota africana

Stigmarota africana, commonly known as the African star grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a dense cluster near the ground. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. Its root system is fibrous and helps it thrive in well-drained soils.

Stigmarota africana is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
