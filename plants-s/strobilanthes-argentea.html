Strobilanthes argentea

Strobilanthes argentea, commonly known as silver strobilanthes, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage due to a dense covering of fine hairs.
