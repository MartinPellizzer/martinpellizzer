Solanum capense var. wilmsii

Solanum capense var. wilmsii, commonly known as the Wilmsii nightshade, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring woody stems and thick, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually pale green to yellowish in color, followed by red, fleshy berries that are edible for humans and animals. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

The species is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
