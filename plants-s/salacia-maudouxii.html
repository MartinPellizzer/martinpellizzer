Salacia maudouxii

Salacia maudouxii, commonly known as the Maudoux's salacia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Salicaceae, which includes willows and poplars. The plant is characterized by its small, yellowish-green flowers and elongated, lance-shaped leaves. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth.
