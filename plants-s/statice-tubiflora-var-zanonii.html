Statice tubiflora var. zanonii

Statice tubiflora var. zanonii, commonly known as Zanonii statice, is a compact, flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, spiky flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with a white or pale pink base. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, tubular flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

This variety is noted for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
