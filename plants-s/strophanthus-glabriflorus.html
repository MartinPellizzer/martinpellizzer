Strophanthus glabriflorus

Strophanthus glabriflorus, commonly known as the smooth-flowered strophanthus, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a waxy texture.

The plant is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive blooms.
