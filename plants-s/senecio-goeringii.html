Senecio goeringii

Senecio goeringii, commonly known as the yellow owl's clover, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its yellow flowers and distinctive leaf structure. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and have a hairy texture, while its flowers are small and clustered in heads.

Senecio goeringii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience.
