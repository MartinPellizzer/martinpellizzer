Syzygium houttuynii

Syzygium houttuynii, commonly known as the Chinese star anise.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to southern China, Vietnam, and other parts of Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, star-shaped fruits that are commonly used in traditional Chinese medicine and as a spice. Its leaves are dark green and glossy, and it produces small white or pale pink flowers.

The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its aromatic seeds.
