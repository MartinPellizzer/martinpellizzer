Stachys italica var. albiflora

Stachys italica var. albiflora, commonly known as white-flowered wood betony, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features square, hairy stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly serrated edge. The plant produces clusters of small, showy white or pale pink flowers in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and ability to attract pollinators.
