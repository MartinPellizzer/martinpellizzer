Stephania hernandiifolia var. pubescens

Stephania hernandiifolia var. pubescens, commonly known as the hairy stephania, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Menispermaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves with a hairy texture. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in umbels. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The varietal form, pubescens, is distinguished by its dense pubescence, giving it a distinctive appearance.
