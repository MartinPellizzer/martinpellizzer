Sedum Smallii

Sedum smallii is a rare and striking succulent native to the mountainous regions of southern China. Known for its vibrant green leaves and clusters of star-shaped flowers, it thrives in rocky and arid environments. This plant is particularly valued for its ability to store water in its fleshy leaves, allowing it to survive in harsh conditions. Due to its limited distribution and habitat loss, Sedum smallii is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique species for future generations.
