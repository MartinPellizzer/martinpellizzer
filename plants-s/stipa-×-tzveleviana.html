Stipa × tzveleviana

Stipa × tzveleviana, commonly known as the hybrid feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to Central Asia.

It is a natural hybrid between Stipa capillata and Stipa trachysperma, exhibiting characteristics of both parent species. The plant is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and fine, needle-like leaves that give it a delicate appearance. Its inflorescence consists of narrow, open panicles, which are often adorned with silky, wind-dispersed seeds.

This grass is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient plant in harsh climates.
