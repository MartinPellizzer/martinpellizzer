Stylidium adenophorum

Stylidium adenophorum, commonly known as the hairy sun-orchid, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its compact, hairy stems and showy, yellow-orange flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils in arid or semi-arid regions. Its flowers are pollinated primarily by insects, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.
