Schleichera amoena

Schleichera amoena, commonly known as the Chinese fan palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that can reach up to two meters in diameter. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by orange-red berries. Schleichera amoena is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
