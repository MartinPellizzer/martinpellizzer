Stelis niessen-andreae

Stelis niessen-andreae, commonly known as the Niessen-Andreae orchid, is a species of orchid belonging to the Epidendroideae subfamily.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pink, depending on the specific variety.

Stelis niessen-andreae is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
