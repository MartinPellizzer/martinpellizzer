Senecio robertiifolius

Senecio robertiifolius, commonly known as the Robert's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features slender, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically yellow, with ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers.

Senecio robertiifolius is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
