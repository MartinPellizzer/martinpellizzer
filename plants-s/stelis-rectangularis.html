Stelis rectangularis

Stelis rectangularis, commonly known as the rectangular stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and the presence of clustered, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically arranged in a raceme. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Stelis rectangularis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care in suitable conditions.
