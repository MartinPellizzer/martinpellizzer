Senecio reflexifolius

Senecio reflexifolius, commonly known as the reflexedleaf senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, reflexed leaves that give it its common name, and it produces small yellow flowers in spring. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and drought tolerance.

Senecio reflexifolius prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.
