Sinapis ucranica

Sinapis ucranica, commonly known as the Ukrainian mustard, is an annual herbaceous plant native to Eastern Europe.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other mustard species. The plant features pinnately compound leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that attract pollinators. Its seed pods are slender and contain numerous small, black seeds.

Sinapis ucranica is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats.
