Sutera merxmuelleri

Sutera merxmuelleri, commonly known as the Mexican moonflower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its large, white, fragrant flowers that open at night. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, round berries that turn red when mature. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Sutera merxmuelleri is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
