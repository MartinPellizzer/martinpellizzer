Shirakia japonica

Shirakia japonica, commonly known as Japanese snowbell, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, bell-shaped white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are ovate to elliptical in shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Shirakia japonica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in landscape design.
