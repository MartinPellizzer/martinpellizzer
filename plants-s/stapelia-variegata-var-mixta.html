Stapelia variegata var. mixta

Stapelia variegata var. mixta, commonly known as the Variegated Stapelia, is a hybrid succulent native to South Africa.

It features distinctive, waxy, and often brightly colored flowers that resemble insect bodies, a trait common in the Stapeliinae subfamily. The plant has thick, fleshy stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. Its leaves are usually green with variegated markings, adding to its ornamental appeal.

It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
