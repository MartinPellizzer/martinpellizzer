Stenorrhynchos venustum

Stenorrhynchos venustum, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers, which are often adorned with intricate patterns and a sweet, spicy fragrance. Its leaves are lance-shaped and glossy, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Stenorrhynchos venustum thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its beauty and unique floral characteristics.
