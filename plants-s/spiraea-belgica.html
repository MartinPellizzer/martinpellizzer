Spiraea belgica

Spiraea belgica, commonly known as the Belgian spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to Europe.

It features small, white or pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, arching stems and is often used in gardens for its ornamental value. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun.

Spiraea belgica is relatively low maintenance and can thrive in a variety of garden conditions.
