Syzygium richii

Syzygium richii, commonly known as the Hainan sandalwood, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the island of Hainan in China and is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and smooth, pale bark. The plant typically grows as a small tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and have a slightly glossy surface.

Syzygium richii is valued for its aromatic wood, which is used in traditional Chinese medicine and for making incense.
