Stewartia × henryae

Stewartia × henryae, commonly known as the Chinese Stewartia, is a hybrid evergreen tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Theaceae and is characterized by its elegant, lace-like foliage and showy, camellia-like flowers. The leaves are ovate to elliptical, with a smooth, dark green upper surface and a silvery underside. It produces small, yellowish-green fruits that contain seeds, and it thrives in well-drained, acidic soils.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
