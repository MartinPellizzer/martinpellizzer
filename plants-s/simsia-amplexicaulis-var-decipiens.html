Simsia amplexicaulis var. decipiens

Simsia amplexicaulis var. decipiens, commonly known as the deceptive leaf simsia, is a unique species native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that wrap around the stem, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Due to its unusual morphology and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and interesting subject for botanical study.
