Syagrus flexuosa

Syagrus flexuosa, commonly known as the flexuous palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and pinnate leaves. The plant produces yellowish-pink flowers that are clustered in panicles, which are often pollinated by insects. Syagrus flexuosa is typically found in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical regions.
