Scutellaria hercegovinica

Scutellaria hercegovinica, commonly known as Hercegovenian skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan region, particularly Croatia and Bosnia and Herzegovina.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks.

Scutellaria hercegovinica is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its potential health benefits.
