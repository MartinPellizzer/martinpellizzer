Satureja umbrosa

Satureja umbrosa, commonly known as dark sage, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or shaded environments, thriving in well-drained soils. Its small, tubular flowers are usually purple or pink, and it produces small, aromatic seeds.

Satureja umbrosa is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
