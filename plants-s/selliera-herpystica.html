Selliera herpystica

Selliera herpystica, commonly known as the Cape snowbush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the heath family (Ericaceae) and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, white to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

It is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
