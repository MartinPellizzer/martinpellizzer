Statice plantaginea proles rigida

Statice plantaginea proles rigida, commonly known as rigid statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, spike-like inflorescences. The plant produces small, colorful flowers in shades of pink, purple, and white, which bloom throughout the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens.

Statice plantaginea proles rigida is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
