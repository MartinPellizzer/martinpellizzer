Solanum mairei

Solanum mairei, commonly known as the Maire's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale purple, followed by berries that range in color from yellow to red. Solanum mairei belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae), which includes other familiar plants like tomatoes and peppers.

It is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and forest edges.
