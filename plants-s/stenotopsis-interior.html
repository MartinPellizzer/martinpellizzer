Stenotopsis interior

Stenotopsis interior, commonly known as the interior stenotopsis, is a species of flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its compact growth habit and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of delicate, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade in its natural habitat.
