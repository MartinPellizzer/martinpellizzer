Sansevieria pinguicula

Sansevieria pinguicula, commonly known as the "Baby Chain" or "Mini Sansevieria," is a compact, low-maintenance succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, dark green leaves that are shorter and more slender than those of typical Sansevieria species. The plant produces small, white flowers on thin, upright stalks during the growing season, though it is primarily cultivated for its ornamental foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires minimal water, making it ideal for indoor environments.

Its unique appearance and ease of care have made it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts.
