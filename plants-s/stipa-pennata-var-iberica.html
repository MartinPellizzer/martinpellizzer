Stipa pennata var. iberica

Stipa pennata var. iberica, commonly known as the Iberian feathergrass, is a grass species native to southern Europe, particularly Spain and Portugal.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and delicate, feathery inflorescences that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and rocky areas. It is a perennial species that thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in Mediterranean climates.

Its ornamental value has led to its use in landscaping and restoration projects.
