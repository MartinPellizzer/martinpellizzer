Stenochilus maculatus

Stenochilus maculatus, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant features a slender, branching stem with numerous small, greenish-white flowers that have a distinctive spotted pattern. Its flowers are usually arranged in a raceme and bloom during the summer months.

Stenochilus maculatus is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
