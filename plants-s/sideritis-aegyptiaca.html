Sideritis aegyptiaca

Sideritis aegyptiaca, commonly known as Egyptian ironwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, tubular flowers that range in color from pale blue to violet. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in fine hairs.

Sideritis aegyptiaca is known for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
