Sauroglossum nitidum

Sauroglossum nitidum, commonly known as the glossy leaf plant, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking, elongated leaves that are often covered in a waxy, glossy coating. Its flowers are typically small and clustered in whorls, displaying shades of red, orange, and yellow.

Sauroglossum nitidum thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
