Sinningia × affinis

Sinningia × affinis, commonly known as the violet.

It is a hybrid plant belonging to the Gesneriaceae family, native to South America. The species is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including purple, pink, and white. Its foliage is typically heart-shaped and covered in a dense layer of fine, velvety hair.

This plant is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
