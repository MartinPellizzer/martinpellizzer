Solanum cobanense

Solanum cobanense, commonly known as the Coban nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and peppers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching up to 3 meters in height. Its leaves are ovate with a rough texture, and it produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale purple.

The fruit is a berry that turns from green to yellow when mature, and it contains several seeds.
