Salacia vitiensis

Salacia vitiensis, commonly known as the Vitiensis Salacia, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It belongs to the Salaciacae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and palmate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are berry-like, with a tart flavor, and are used in traditional medicine.

Salacia vitiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
