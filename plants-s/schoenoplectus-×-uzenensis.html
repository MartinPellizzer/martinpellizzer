Schoenoplectus × uzenensis

Schoenoplectus × uzenensis, commonly known as the Uzen reed, is a hybrid plant species belonging to the family Cyperaceae.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. This plant typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes and along the edges of ponds. It produces small, clustered spikelets that are often wind-pollinated.

The Uzen reed is known for its adaptability to varying water levels and is often used in ecological restoration projects.
