Scaevola basedowii

Scaevola basedowii, commonly known as the basedow's fanflower, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Western Australia.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers that bloom in dense, fan-like clusters. The plant has small, narrow leaves that are adapted to reduce water loss in its harsh environment. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Scaevola basedowii is a low-growing perennial that is well-suited to xerophytic conditions.
