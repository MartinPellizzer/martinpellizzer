Stipa grafiana f. leiantha

Stipa grafiana f. leiantha, commonly known as the silver feather grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is a perennial species characterized by its slender, silver-blue foliage and tufted inflorescences that resemble feathers. Native to high-altitude regions of South America, it thrives in cool, moist environments. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.

Its botanical structure includes narrow, cylindrical leaves and spikelet-based flowers that contribute to its distinctive appearance.
