Stenochilus ochroleucus

Stenochilus ochroleucus, commonly known as the yellow-stemmed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its slender, yellowish stems and lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small and typically white or pale yellow, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
