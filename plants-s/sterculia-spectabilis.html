Sterculia spectabilis

Sterculia spectabilis, commonly known as the beautiful sterculia, is a tropical tree native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that are typically white or pink in color. The plant produces woody capsules that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Its leaves are alternate and simple, with a rough texture and a serrated margin.

The tree is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and parks.
