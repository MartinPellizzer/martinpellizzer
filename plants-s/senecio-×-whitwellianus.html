Senecio × whitwellianus

Senecio × whitwellianus, commonly known as the hybrid groundsel, is a flowering plant in the sunflower family (Asteraceae).

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Senecio squalidus and Senecio jucundus, characterized by its yellow to orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and meadows. It has lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and produces numerous small florets in a disc-like arrangement.

Senecio × whitwellianus is often found in the British Isles and is considered a short-lived perennial.
