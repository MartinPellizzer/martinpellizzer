Samolus geniculatus

Samolus geniculatus, commonly known as the zigzag sedge, is a flowering plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges and related plants. Samolus geniculatus plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
