Spathoglottis daenikeri

Spathoglottis daenikeri, commonly known as the Daeniker's spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, intricate floral structure. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its flowers are usually yellow or greenish-yellow with intricate patterns, and it produces a single, elongated spathaceous bract that encloses the inflorescence.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
