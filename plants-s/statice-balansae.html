Statice balansae

Statice balansae, commonly known as the Balansae statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, showy flowers. The plant typically blooms in late summer, producing pink to purple flowers that attract pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Statice balansae is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to stabilize sandy soils.
