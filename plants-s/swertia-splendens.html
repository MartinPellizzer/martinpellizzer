Swertia splendens

Swertia splendens, commonly known as the golden gentian, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine regions, thriving in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged oppositely along the stem.

Swertia splendens is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
