Selenicereus inermis

Selenicereus inermis, commonly known as the Queen of the Night, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a climbing cactus that produces large, fragrant, white flowers that open only at night. The plant has a succulent stem with spines and is adapted to arid environments. Its flowers are ephemeral, blooming for just a few hours before closing.

The plant is also known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its striking nocturnal blooms.
