Stenochilus glaber

Stenochilus glaber, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its slender, unbranched stems. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Stenochilus glaber is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
