Swertia granvikii

Swertia granvikii, commonly known as the granvik's gentian, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and is adapted to cold, mountainous environments. Its leaves are opposite and lanceolate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.
