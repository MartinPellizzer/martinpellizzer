Sarothamnus patens

Sarothamnus patens, commonly known as the spreading thyme shrub, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing, evergreen shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features aromatic, narrow leaves and produces small, yellow flowers in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Sarothamnus patens is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to attract pollinators.
