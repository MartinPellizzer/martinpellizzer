Stylochaeton

Stylochaeton, commonly known as the African violets, is a genus of flowering plants native to tropical Africa.

These plants are characterized by their velvety, dark green leaves and showy, often violet-colored flowers. Stylochaeton species are herbaceous perennials that thrive in warm, humid environments. They are popular houseplants due to their ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

The flowers typically bloom in clusters and can vary in color, including shades of purple, pink, and white.
