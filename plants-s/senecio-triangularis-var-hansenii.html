Senecio triangularis var. hansenii

Senecio triangularis var. hansenii, commonly known as Hansen's triangular groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its triangular-shaped leaves and yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique morphology, it is a subject of interest in botanical studies and horticulture.
