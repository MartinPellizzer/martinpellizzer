Senecio achyrotrichus

Senecio achyrotrichus, commonly known as the woolly senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, known for its composite flower heads.

Senecio achyrotrichus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique texture and visual appeal.
