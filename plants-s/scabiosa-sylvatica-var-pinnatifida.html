Scabiosa sylvatica var. pinnatifida

Scabiosa sylvatica var. pinnatifida, commonly known as the woodland scabious, is a perennial herb native to Europe.

It features slender, upright stems and produces clusters of small, pink or purple flowers. The leaves are pinnately divided, giving the plant its specific variety name, pinnatifida. It thrives in moist, shaded woodland environments and is often found in forest understories.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators.
