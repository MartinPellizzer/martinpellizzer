Solanum elachophyllum

Solanum elachophyllum, commonly known as the dwarf nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous shrub characterized by its hairy stems and small, spiny leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several small, toxic berries.

It is native to regions of South America and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.
