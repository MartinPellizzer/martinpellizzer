Stachys lindenii var. aristulata

Stachys lindenii var. aristulata, commonly known as the purple flowered betony, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces spikes of violet or purple flowers, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
