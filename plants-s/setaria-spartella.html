Setaria spartella

Setaria spartella, commonly known as slender dropseed, is a grass species native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

Setaria spartella is often found in open areas, such as prairies and disturbed soils, and is valued for its ornamental and ecological benefits.
