Salvia nanchuanensis var. pteridifolia

Salvia nanchuanensis var. pteridifolia, commonly known as the Nanchuan sage, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture and produces spikes of violet to purple flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or rocky habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections.
