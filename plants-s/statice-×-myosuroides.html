Statice × myosuroides

Statice × myosuroides, commonly known as the sea lavender, is a hybrid plant that belongs to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, spike-like clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from pink to purple. Native to the coastal regions of the Mediterranean, it thrives in salty, well-drained soils and full sun. The plant is often used in ornamental gardening for its drought tolerance and ability to withstand coastal conditions.

Its fibrous root system helps stabilize sandy soils, making it a valuable plant in coastal restoration projects.
