Sideranthus turbinellus

The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three small, oval-shaped leaflets, and it produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Sideranthus turbinellus is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in full sun.
