Stenocoelium villosum

Stenocoelium villosum, commonly known as the silky senna, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety foliage and yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has compound leaves with small, hairy leaflets, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It is often found in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in sunny to partially shaded environments.

Stenocoelium villosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
