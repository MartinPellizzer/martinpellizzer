Sida Elongata Var. Balica

Longleaf sida, scientifically known as Sida elongata var. balica, is a rare and unique flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia. This variant of the longleaf sida exhibits distinctive morphological traits that set it apart from its relatives. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in dense tropical forests or along riverbanks. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, the species faces significant conservation challenges. Its ecological importance and botanical intrigue make it a subject of ongoing scientific interest.
