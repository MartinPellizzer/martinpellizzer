Setaria onurus

Setaria onurus, commonly known as slender false panicgrass, is a grass species native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is a perennial plant that typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes and riparian zones. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences. Its seeds are dispersed by wind, and it is often found in disturbed soils.

Botanically, it is characterized by its rhizomatous growth and ability to thrive in humid environments.
