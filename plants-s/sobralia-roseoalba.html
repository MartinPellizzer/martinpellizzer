Sobralia roseoalba

Sobralia roseoalba, commonly known as the white-flowered sobralia, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom during the summer months. The plant features a pseudobulb structure with strap-shaped leaves that are typically green and slightly hairy. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Sobralia roseoalba is often cultivated for its ornamental value in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
