Sempervivum Parlatorei

Sempervivum parlatorei is a unique succulent plant known for its striking rosettes of fleshy, vibrant leaves. Native to the alpine regions of Europe, it thrives in rocky and sunny environments. This plant is also called the "Himalayan houseleek" due to its hardy nature and ability to survive in harsh conditions. Its compact size and colorful foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively rare and less commonly cultivated species.
