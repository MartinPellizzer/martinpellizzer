Schollera Debilis

Schollera debilis is a rare and delicate plant native to the mountainous regions of South America. It is known for its small, elegant leaves and unique flowering patterns that make it a favorite among botanists and plant enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, this species is highly sensitive to environmental changes and is considered endangered in its natural habitat. Its cultivation requires careful attention to soil, light, and humidity levels. Schollera debilis remains a symbol of both botanical wonder and conservation challenges.
