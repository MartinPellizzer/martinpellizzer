Strobilanthes adenophora

Strobilanthes adenophora, commonly known as the "Christmas bell" or "firecracker plant," is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth.
