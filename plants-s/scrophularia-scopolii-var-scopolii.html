Scrophularia scopolii var. scopolii

Scrophularia scopolii var. scopolii, commonly known as the European figwort, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. The plant features square, hairy stems and opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are tubular, with two lips, and are usually pink or purple in color.

It is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges.
