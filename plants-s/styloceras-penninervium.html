Styloceras penninervium

Styloceras penninervium, commonly known as the slender-veined orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, linear leaves and small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale pink. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade. Its roots are fibrous and adapted for nutrient absorption in its natural habitat.

The plant's unique vascular structure supports its growth in humid environments, making it a popular choice for botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
