Sansevieria newtoniana

Sansevieria newtoniana, commonly known as the Newton's snake plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with prominent yellow or white variegation. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its foliage is often adorned with subtle stripes or patterns, adding visual interest to any space.

Sansevieria newtoniana is also noted for its air-purifying qualities, contributing to improved indoor air quality.
