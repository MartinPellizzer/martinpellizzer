Schaueria gonatistachya

Schaueria gonatistachya, commonly known as the African violet, is a perennial herb native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features upright stems that bear opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Schaueria gonatistachya is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
