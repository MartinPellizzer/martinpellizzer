Sciadotenia candicans

Sciadotenia candicans, commonly known as the shining star tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, elliptical leaves that are usually arranged in a spiral pattern along the stems. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by bright red or orange berries. Sciadotenia candicans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.

Its growth habit and foliage make it a distinctive feature in its natural habitat.
