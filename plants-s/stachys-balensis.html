Stachys balensis

Stachys balensis, commonly known as Balinese hedge nettle, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It features square, hairy stems and opposite, heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, pale purple to pinkish flowers arranged in whorled clusters along the stems. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or stream banks.

Stachys balensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
