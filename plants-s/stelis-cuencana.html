Stelis cuencana

Stelis cuencana, commonly known as the Cuencan Orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments within cloud forests. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with multiple pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pink and purple.

Stelis cuencana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
