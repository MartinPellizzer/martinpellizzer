Stomatanthes oblongifolius

Stomatanthes oblongifolius, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and clusters of showy, tubular flowers. The leaves are typically oval-shaped with a waxy surface, and the plant prefers bright, indirect light. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.

Stomatanthes oblongifolius thrives in humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
