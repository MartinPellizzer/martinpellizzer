Satureja vana

Satureja vana, commonly known as wild thyme, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. Its small, pink or white flowers bloom in summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Satureja vana is valued for its fragrant foliage, which is often used in traditional herbal remedies and culinary applications.
