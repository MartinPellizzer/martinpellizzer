Saurauia herthae

Saurauia herthae, commonly known as the Herth's evergreen mistletoe, is a hemiparasitic plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are typically visited by insects for pollination. Its berries, which are usually red or orange, contain seeds that are dispersed by birds.
