Smilax balearica

Smilax balearica, commonly known as the Balearic arrowhead, is a climbing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a robust root system and produces tendrils that help it climb and support its growth. The plant has compound leaves with a distinctive arrowhead shape, and it produces small, yellowish-green flowers. Its fruits are red berries that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by animals.

Smilax balearica is often found in rocky or coastal areas, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
