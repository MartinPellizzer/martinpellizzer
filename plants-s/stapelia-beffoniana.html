Stapelia beffoniana

Stapelia beffoniana, commonly known as the Ghost Plant, is a unique succulent native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, leaf-like structures that resemble small, ghostly shapes. The plant produces striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in arid environments and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for xerophytic conditions.

Its unusual appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for collectors and succulent enthusiasts.
