Stobaea microcephala

Stobaea microcephala, commonly known as the dwarf rock rose, is a low-growing shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, rounded leaves and produces clusters of pale yellow or white flowers. The plant is adapted to rocky and arid environments, showing drought tolerance. Its compact size makes it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in Mediterranean climates.
