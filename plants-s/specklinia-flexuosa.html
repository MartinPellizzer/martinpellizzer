Specklinia flexuosa

Specklinia flexuosa, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, sinuous leaf arrangement that gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are small and usually white or pale green, with a delicate, orchid-like appearance.

Specklinia flexuosa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
