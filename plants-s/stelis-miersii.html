Stelis miersii

Stelis miersii, commonly known as the Miers' orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, tropical environments. The plant features a slender, wiry stem with closely clustered, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

Stelis miersii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm, shaded conditions.
