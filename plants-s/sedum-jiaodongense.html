Sedum jiaodongense

Sedum jiaodongense, commonly known as the Jiaodong stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to China.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are typically borne on short, erect pedicels. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

Sedum jiaodongense is often used in rock gardens and as an ornamental plant due to its drought tolerance and aesthetic appeal.
