Salsola moquiniana

Salsola moquiniana, commonly known as sandmat or Russian thistle, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Eurasia and North America.

It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and small, greenish flowers.
