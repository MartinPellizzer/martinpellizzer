Senecio kingii var. kingii

Senecio kingii var. kingii, commonly known as the King's Senecio, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine, silky hairs. It produces showy yellow flowers arranged in dense, spherical clusters.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to grow in well-drained, sandy soils.
