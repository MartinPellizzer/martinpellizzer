Solanum balansae

Solanum balansae, commonly known as the Balansae nightshade, is a shrub native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and dark purple berries. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, often in rocky or mountainous terrain. Its foliage consists of ovate to lanceolate leaves with a rough texture.

Solanum balansae is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
