Stephania brachyandra

Stephania brachyandra, commonly known as the narrow-leaved stephania, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Menispermaceae family and is characterized by its slender stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in racemes. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Stephania brachyandra thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
