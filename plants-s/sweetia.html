Sweetia

Sweetia, commonly known as the sweet pea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that come in a wide range of colors, including pink, purple, and white. The plant typically grows as an annual vine, producing tendrils that help it climb supports. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three small, heart-shaped leaflets.

Sweetia thrives in cool climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
