Sedum moranii

Sedum moranii, commonly known as the Moran's stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and small, clustered leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping and ornamental gardening.
