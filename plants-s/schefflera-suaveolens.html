Schefflera suaveolens

Schefflera suaveolens, commonly known as the sweet-scented umbrella plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like clusters of leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are followed by bright red berries, attracting various wildlife. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its foliage is glossy and dark green, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
