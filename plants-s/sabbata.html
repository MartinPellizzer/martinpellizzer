Sabbata

Sabbata, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Nelumbonaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom on long, erect stalks. Sabbata is also noted for its symbolic significance in various cultures and religious traditions.
