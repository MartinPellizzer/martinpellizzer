Stelis ciliatissima

Stelis ciliatissima, commonly known as the feather-leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, hairy leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or branches, exhibiting an epiphytic growth habit. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination.

Stelis ciliatissima thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.
