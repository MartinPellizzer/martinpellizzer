Stemonurus australianus

Stemonurus australianus, commonly known as the Australian starwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Australia.

It features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink. The plant is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. It has a fibrous root system that helps it survive in harsh environmental conditions.

Stemonurus australianus is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
