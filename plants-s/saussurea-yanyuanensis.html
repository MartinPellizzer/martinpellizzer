Saussurea yanyuanensis

Saussurea yanyuanensis, commonly known as the Yanyuan safflower, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stem and yellowish flowers. The plant is adapted to cold, arid environments and thrives in mountainous areas with well-drained soil. Its medicinal properties have been recognized in traditional Chinese medicine, where it is used for its purported health benefits.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
