Scaphyglottis punctulata var. summersii

Scaphyglottis punctulata var. summersii, commonly known as the Summer's Sunset Orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its striking, showy flowers that resemble orchids. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or swampy areas. Its leaves are usually broad and heart-shaped, with a waxy texture.

The flowers bloom in late summer, displaying vibrant colors ranging from pink to purple, making it a visually appealing ornamental plant.
