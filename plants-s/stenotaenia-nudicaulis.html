Stenotaenia nudicaulis

Stenotaenia nudicaulis, commonly known as the naked-stemmed stenotaenia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grassland and rocky habitats. The plant has a slender, unbranched stem that is often leafless except for a few basal leaves. Its small, white or pale blue flowers are clustered in terminal spikes, and it blooms during the warmer months.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
