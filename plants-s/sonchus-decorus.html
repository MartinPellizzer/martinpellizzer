Sonchus decorus

Sonchus decorus, commonly known as the ornamental sowthistle, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, dandelion-like yellow flowers and finely divided, lobed leaves. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in disturbed areas, along roadsides, and in open fields.

Botanically, it belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with composite flower heads.
