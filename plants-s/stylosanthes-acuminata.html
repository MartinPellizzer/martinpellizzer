Stylosanthes acuminata

Stylosanthes acuminata, commonly known as the acacia leaf clover, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is well adapted to dry conditions, making it a valuable forage crop in arid environments. Its root system fixes nitrogen, improving soil fertility.

It is often used in pasture systems and for soil stabilization due to its hardy nature.
