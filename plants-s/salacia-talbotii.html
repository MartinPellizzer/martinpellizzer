Salacia talbotii

Salacia talbotii, commonly known as the Talbot's salacia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Salaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a glossy surface.

Salacia talbotii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique floral display.
