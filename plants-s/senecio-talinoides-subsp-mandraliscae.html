Senecio talinoides subsp. mandraliscae

Senecio talinoides subsp.

mandraliscae, commonly known as the Blue Echeveria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-gray leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments.

The plant produces showy yellow flowers in late summer, which attract pollinators. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, requiring minimal water.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
