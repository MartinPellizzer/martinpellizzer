Stelis exilis

Stelis exilis, commonly known as the slender stelis, is a member of the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a slender, wiry stem with a few leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. Its growth habit and floral characteristics make it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often challenging to grow outside its native range.
