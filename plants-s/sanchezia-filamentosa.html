Sanchezia filamentosa

Sanchezia filamentosa, commonly known as the threadleaf stromanthe, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence known as a spadix, surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. Sanchezia filamentosa requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture to flourish.
