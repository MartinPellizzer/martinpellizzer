Senecio germanicus var. karaulensis

Senecio germanicus var. karaulensis, commonly known as the Karaulenskaya Senecio, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers are yellow and arranged in a cluster.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in challenging climates.
