Stelis bractescens

Stelis bractescens, commonly known as the bracteate orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in summer. The plant features a sympodial growth pattern, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a fan-like structure.

Stelis bractescens thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
