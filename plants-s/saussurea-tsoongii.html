Saussurea tsoongii

Saussurea tsoongii, commonly known as the Tibetan yellow gentian, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas and Tibet.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers and robust, fibrous root system. The plant thrives in cold, arid environments and is often found in alpine meadows at elevations above 3,000 meters. Its medicinal properties have been recognized in traditional Chinese medicine, where it is used to treat various ailments.

The plant's resilience to harsh climates makes it an important species in ecological studies of high-altitude ecosystems.
