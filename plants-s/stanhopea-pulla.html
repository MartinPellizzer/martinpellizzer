Stanhopea pulla

Stanhopea pulla, commonly known as the pink stanhopea, is a species of orchid native to Costa Rica and Panama.

It is characterized by its striking pink and white flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are elongated and slightly fleshy, adapted to its tropical environment.

Stanhopea pulla is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
