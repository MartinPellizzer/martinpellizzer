Selaginella moritziana var. normalis

Selaginella moritziana var. normalis, commonly known as the common spikemoss, is a vascular plant belonging to the family Selaginellaceae.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices, and is native to regions in South America.
