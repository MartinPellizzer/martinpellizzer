Senecio hypochionaeus

Senecio hypochionaeus, commonly known as the African snowflake, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking white, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Senecio hypochionaeus is drought-tolerant and well-suited for xeriscaping due to its low water requirements.
