Stelis pholeoglossa

Stelis pholeoglossa, commonly known as the helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, humid environments such as forests and rocky areas. The plant has a slender, upright stem with a single or few flowers that are usually pale green or yellowish in color. Its roots are often clustered and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Stelis pholeoglossa is prized for its unique floral structure and is a popular subject in orchid cultivation.
