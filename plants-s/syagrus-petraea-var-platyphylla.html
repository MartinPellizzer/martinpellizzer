Syagrus petraea var. platyphylla

Syagrus petraea var. platyphylla, commonly known as the flat-leaf palm, is a variety of the royal palm native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its broad, flat leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils. It produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into orange fruits.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience to coastal conditions.
