Securidaca luschnathiana

Securidaca luschnathiana, commonly known as the African velvet bean, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to Africa and thrives in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. Its seeds are enclosed in pods and are used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant also has a distinctive, fragrant aroma that contributes to its ornamental value.
