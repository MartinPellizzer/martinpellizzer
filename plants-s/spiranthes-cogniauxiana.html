Spiranthes cogniauxiana

Spiranthes cogniauxiana, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spiral orchid, is a species of orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and its distinctive spiral arrangement of flowers, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as pine flatwoods and wetlands. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color and bloom from late spring to early summer.

The species is part of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its delicate appearance and ecological significance in its native habitat.
