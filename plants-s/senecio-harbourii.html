Senecio harbourii

Senecio harbourii, commonly known as the Harbour's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that forms dense mats of silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice among gardeners.
