Stachys dinteri

Stachys dinteri, commonly known as the Dinter's hedge nettle, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, pale purple to pink flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to hot, dry climates.

Stachys dinteri is often found in rocky or sandy habitats, and it plays a role in supporting local pollinators.
