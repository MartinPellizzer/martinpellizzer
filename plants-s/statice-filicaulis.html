Statice filicaulis

Statice filicaulis, commonly known as the featherleaf statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and showy, spike-like flower heads. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its flowers, which range in color from pink to red, bloom in late summer and early autumn.

Statice filicaulis is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
