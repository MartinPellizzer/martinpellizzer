Stenorrhynchos robustum

Stenorrhynchos robustum, commonly known as the robust orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its thick, fleshy rhizomes and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically orange or yellow with purple markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
