Scilla peruviana var. flaveola

Scilla peruviana var. flaveola, commonly known as the Peruvian squill, is a bulbous perennial plant native to South America.

It produces striking blue to violet flowers with a yellow center, which bloom in early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Scilla peruviana var. flaveola is relatively hardy and can thrive in a variety of garden conditions.
