Sinopteris hopeiensis

Sinopteris hopeiensis, commonly known as the Hope Island water fern, is a rare aquatic fern species native to the freshwater habitats of Hope Island in the Philippines.

It is characterized by its delicate, floating fronds that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving waters with high humidity and moderate light conditions. Its reproductive structures are small and inconspicuous, contributing to its limited propagation.

Due to habitat degradation, Sinopteris hopeiensis is considered critically endangered and is of significant interest for conservation efforts.
