Sedum cuspidatum

Sedum cuspidatum, commonly known as the spike sedum, is a succulent plant native to North America.

It features fleshy, upright stems that produce clusters of small, star-shaped yellow flowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically blue-green in color. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Sedum cuspidatum is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
