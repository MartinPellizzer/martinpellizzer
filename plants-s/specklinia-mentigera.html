Specklinia mentigera

Specklinia mentigera, commonly known as the Mentigera orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on tree bark in humid, shaded environments. The plant has slender, wiry stems and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in high humidity with indirect light.

Specklinia mentigera is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and ease of cultivation.
