Sedum franchetii

Sedum franchetii, commonly known as the Franchet's stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to China.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink to purple. The leaves are typically thick and green, adapted to store water, which helps the plant survive in arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

This species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
