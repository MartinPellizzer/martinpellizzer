Serjania dura

Serjania dura, commonly known as the Brazilian rosewood or Brazilian cherry, is a tropical evergreen tree native to South America.

Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a leathery texture, contributing to its ornamental value.

Serjania dura is also valued for its hard, durable wood, which has been used in furniture and musical instruments.
