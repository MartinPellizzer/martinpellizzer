Stelis rhomboglossa

Stelis rhomboglossa, commonly known as the rhomboid-lipped orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. The plant features a slender, upright stem with several fleshy, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, greenish-white with a distinctive rhomboid-shaped lip.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
