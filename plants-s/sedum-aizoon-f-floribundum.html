Sedum aizoon f. floribundum

Sedum aizoon f. floribundum, commonly known as the Crimson Chief, is a succulent plant native to the southwestern United States.

It features fleshy, upright stems and clusters of bright red flowers that bloom in summer. The leaves are typically blue-green and arranged in a rosette pattern. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its striking color and compact growth make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
