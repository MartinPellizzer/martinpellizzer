Stipa tenuis

Stipa tenuis, commonly known as slender feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It typically grows in open habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant has slender, wiry stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are often tinged with silver or gray. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a distinctive bluish-green color.

Stipa tenuis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
