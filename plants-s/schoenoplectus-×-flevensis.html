Schoenoplectus × flevensis

Schoenoplectus × flevensis, commonly known as the Dutch reed, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and dense, spike-like inflorescences that bloom in late summer. This plant is native to the Netherlands and is often found in wetland habitats such as marshes and pond edges. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense stands, which can stabilize soil and provide habitat for various wildlife.

Schoenoplectus × flevensis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in water gardens.
