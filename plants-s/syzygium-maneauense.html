Syzygium maneauense

Syzygium maneauense, commonly known as the white flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has smooth, greyish bark and lance-shaped leaves that are glossy on top and pale underneath. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking floral display.

The fruit is a small, woody capsule that contains several seeds.
