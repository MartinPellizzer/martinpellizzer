Sebaea crassulifolia var. stricta

Sebaea crassulifolia var. stricta, commonly known as the compact sebaea, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a low-growing, dense rosette of fleshy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white powder. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a houseplant.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
