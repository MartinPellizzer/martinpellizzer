Sophora prazeri var. mairei

Sophora prazeri var. mairei, commonly known as the Maire's Sophora, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to temperate climates and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and nitrogen-fixing capabilities.

The species is also valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
