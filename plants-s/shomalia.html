Shomalia

Shomalia, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that have a silvery underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Shomalia thrives in humid climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its wood is valued for its durability and is used in traditional construction and crafts.
