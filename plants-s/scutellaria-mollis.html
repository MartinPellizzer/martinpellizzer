Scutellaria mollis

Scutellaria mollis, commonly known as smooth blueweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant produces small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine, soft hairs.

Scutellaria mollis is often found in meadows and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils.
