Senecio shikokianus

Senecio shikokianus, commonly known as the Shikoku Senecio, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically produces yellow-orange ray flowers surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

Senecio shikokianus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
