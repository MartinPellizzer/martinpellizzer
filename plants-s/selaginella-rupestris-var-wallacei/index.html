Selaginella Rupestris Var. Wallacei

Selaginella rupestris var. wallacei is a unique and resilient plant species known for its ability to thrive in harsh environments. This variety of Selaginella is often found in rocky and arid regions where other plants struggle to survive. Its small, scale-like leaves help it conserve water and withstand extreme temperatures. The plant's adaptability makes it a fascinating subject for botanists and ecologists. Despite its tough conditions, it plays an important role in stabilizing soil and supporting local ecosystems.
