The serapias x dupuyana is a hybrid orchid known for its striking floral display and unique appearance. This plant combines traits from its parent species, serapias acaulis and serapias grandiflora, resulting in a rare and beautiful bloom. Native to the alpine regions of the pyrenees, it thrives in cool, moist environments with well-drained soil. Its flowers feature a vibrant mix of yellow and orange hues, often with intricate patterns that mimic the appearance of insects. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, the serapias x dupuyana is considered a rare and valuable addition to botanical collections.
