Sulcorebutia frankiana var. rectispina

Sulcorebutia frankiana var. rectispina, commonly known as the straight-spined sun rose, is a succulent plant native to South America.

It features a short, thick stem with clusters of fleshy, spiny leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding ornamental value. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
