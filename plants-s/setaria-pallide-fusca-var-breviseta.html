Setaria pallide-fusca var. breviseta

Setaria pallide-fusca var. breviseta, commonly known as slender false panicgrass, is a grass species native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, pale green to brown spikelets that are typically arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

Its botanical features make it a common indicator plant in arid and semi-arid environments.
