Swertia gracilescens

Swertia gracilescens, commonly known as the slender swertia, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional Chinese medicine, where it is used to treat ailments related to the liver and gallbladder.

Swertia gracilescens is also noted for its ornamental value due to its delicate floral display.
