Sedum nanlingense

Sedum nanlingense, commonly known as the Nanling stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and fleshy, blue-green leaves. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers in late summer, which attract pollinators. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

Sedum nanlingense is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance requirements.
