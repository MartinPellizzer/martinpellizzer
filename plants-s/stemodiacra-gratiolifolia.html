Stemodiacra gratiolifolia

Stemodiacra gratiolifolia, commonly known as the golden star fern, is a delicate and elegant plant native to tropical regions.

It features a creeping rhizome system that supports its growth and helps anchor it in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces small, clustered leaves that are typically bright green with a subtle metallic sheen. Its flowers are inconspicuous, but the plant is prized for its ornamental foliage rather than its blooms.

It thrives in humid conditions and requires consistent moisture to maintain its lush appearance.
