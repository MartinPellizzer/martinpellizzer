Solanum kitivuense subsp. ukerewense

Solanum kitivuense subsp.

ukerewense, commonly known as the Ukerewe tomato, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Ukerewe Island in Lake Victoria, Tanzania. It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants such as potatoes and peppers. The plant typically features hairy stems and lobed leaves, with small, yellowish flowers that develop into berries containing edible, red or orange fruits.

It is adapted to wetland habitats and plays a role in local ecosystems and traditional agriculture.
