Solanum indicum f. album

Solanum indicum f. album, commonly known as the white nightshade, is a variant of the common nightshade plant.

It is characterized by its white flowers and foliage, which distinguish it from the typical purple-flowered form. This plant belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other familiar plants like tomatoes and peppers. It is native to parts of Asia and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

The plant is known for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
