Schoenorchis scolopendria

Schoenorchis scolopendria, commonly known as the dragon's tail orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, creeping rhizomes and linear, grass-like leaves. The plant produces striking, spurred orchid flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
