Stelis luteria

Stelis luteria, commonly known as the Luteria orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and elegant, fragrant flowers. The plant typically features upright stems with clusters of small, white or pale yellow blooms. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Stelis luteria is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both indoor and greenhouse settings.
