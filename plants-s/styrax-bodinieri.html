Styrax bodinieri

Styrax bodinieri, commonly known as the Chinese styrax, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. Its bark is smooth and grayish, with a subtle aromatic scent.

Styrax bodinieri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
