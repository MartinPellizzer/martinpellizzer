Sphenostylis marginata subsp. obtusifolia

Sphenostylis marginata subsp.

obtusifolia, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa. It is characterized by its velvety, waxy leaves that are typically heart-shaped and have a deep green color.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, which bloom throughout the year under proper care. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

This subspecies is particularly valued for its ornamental beauty and ease of cultivation in home environments.
