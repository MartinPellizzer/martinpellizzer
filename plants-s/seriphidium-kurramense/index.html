Seriphidium Kurramense

Seriphidium kurramense is a rare and unique plant species native to the rugged mountainous regions of Pakistan. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing remarkable adaptations to survive in harsh conditions. This plant is known for its distinctive yellow flowers and robust, hairy stems that help it retain moisture. Due to its limited distribution and ecological significance, it is considered an important part of the local biodiversity. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this plant from habitat loss and overharvesting.
