Scorzonera libanotica

Scorzonera libanotica, commonly known as the Lebanon scorzonera, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a robust, fleshy root system that is often used in traditional medicine. The plant has dark green, hairy leaves that are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem. Its flowers are yellow with purple veins, typically blooming in early summer.

Scorzonera libanotica is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical uses in herbal remedies.
