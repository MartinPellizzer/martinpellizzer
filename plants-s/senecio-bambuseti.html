Senecio bambuseti

Senecio bambuseti, commonly known as the bamboo sedum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves that resemble bamboo shoots, giving it its common name. The plant produces yellow flowers in summer, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
