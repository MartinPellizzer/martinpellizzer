Senecio telembinus

Senecio telembinus, commonly known as the Cape Hottentot, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as a shrub with narrow, blue-green leaves and produces yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value comes from its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms.
