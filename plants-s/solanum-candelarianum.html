Solanum candelarianum

Solanum candelarianum, commonly known as the candle plant, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. Its fruit is a fleshy, yellow or orange berry that contains numerous seeds.

The leaves are ovate, hairy, and have a distinctive glandular surface.
