Smilax chinensis

Smilax chinensis, commonly known as Chinese smilax, is a perennial vine native to East Asia.

It features a robust, woody stem with spiny nodes and is characterized by its compound leaves with three to five leaflets. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into red, fleshy berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Smilax chinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional medicinal uses in Chinese herbal medicine.
