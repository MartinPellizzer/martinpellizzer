Scorzonera graminifolia var. minor

Scorzonera graminifolia var. minor, commonly known as the lesser scorzonera, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features a robust, branched root system that can reach significant depths, often used in traditional medicine. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, arid environments.

Due to its medicinal properties and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
