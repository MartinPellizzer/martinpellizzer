Saxifraga × jenkinsii

Saxifraga × jenkinsii, commonly known as the hybrid saxifrage, is a flowering plant belonging to the Saxifragaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage, often found in rocky or alpine environments. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white, pink, or red, depending on the variety. It is well adapted to harsh conditions, with succulent leaves that store water and aid in survival.

Saxifraga × jenkinsii is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
