Sansevieria bhitalae

Sansevieria bhitalae, commonly known as the Bhitari Snake Plant, is a species of flowering plant in the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to southern Africa, specifically found in South Africa's Eastern Cape province. The plant is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with dark green variegation. It is a low-maintenance plant that thrives in indirect light and well-draining soil.

Sansevieria bhitalae is also noted for its ability to purify indoor air, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
