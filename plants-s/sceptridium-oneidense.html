Sceptridium oneidense

Sceptridium oneidense, commonly known as the Oneida feathercup, is a rare and endangered species of sedges native to the northeastern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, clustered flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in wetland habitats, such as bogs and marshes, where it thrives in moist, acidic soils.
