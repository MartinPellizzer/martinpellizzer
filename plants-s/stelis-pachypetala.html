Stelis pachypetala

Stelis pachypetala, commonly known as the showy stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that are typically white or pale yellow with intricate patterns. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its leaves are usually broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native habitat.

Stelis pachypetala is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and ease of cultivation.
