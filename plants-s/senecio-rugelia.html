Senecio rugelia

Senecio rugelia, commonly known as the rugel's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Senecio rugelia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its compact size and vibrant blooms.
