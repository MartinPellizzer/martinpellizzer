Simirestis klaineana

Simirestis klaineana, commonly known as the Klainean star vine, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The leaves are usually alternate, simple, and have a glossy surface. This species is often found in forest understories where it relies on support structures to grow.

Its ornamental value has led to its cultivation in some horticultural settings.
