Staberoha Cernua

Staberoha cernua is a rare and delicate plant known for its unique, drooping foliage. Native to the misty forests of Southeast Asia, it thrives in cool, humid environments. Its leaves, which hang gracefully from slender stems, create a soft, cascading effect. Despite its beauty, it remains a lesser-known species among plant enthusiasts. Cultivating Staberoha cernua requires careful attention to light and moisture levels.
