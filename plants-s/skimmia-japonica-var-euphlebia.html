Skimmia japonica var. euphlebia

Skimmia japonica var. euphlebia, commonly known as the Japanese skimmia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It features glossy, dark green elliptical leaves with a leathery texture. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers in spring, followed by red berries in autumn. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and seasonal flowering.
