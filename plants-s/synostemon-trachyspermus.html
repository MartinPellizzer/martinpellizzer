Synostemon trachyspermus

Synostemon trachyspermus, commonly known as wild thyme, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with small, fragrant leaves and pink or purple flowers. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves, which contain essential oils responsible for its distinctive scent. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry environments.

Synostemon trachyspermus is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
