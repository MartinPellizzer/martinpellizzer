Sansevieria scimitariformis

Sansevieria scimitariformis, commonly known as the Scimitar Sword Plant, is a species of flowering plant in the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to tropical Africa and is characterized by its long, narrow, sword-shaped leaves that curve gracefully at the tips. Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
