Spermacoce perpusilla

Spermacoce perpusilla, commonly known as the small false coffee.

It is a small annual herb native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas. The plant features small, white or pale pink flowers that are clustered in umbels. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that split open to release tiny, smooth seeds.

Spermacoce perpusilla is often found in disturbed soils and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
