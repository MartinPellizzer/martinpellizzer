Senecillis potaninii

Senecillis potaninii, commonly known as the Potanin's senecio, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically features narrow, linear leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by horticulturists.
