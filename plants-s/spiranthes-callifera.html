Spiranthes callifera

Spiranthes callifera, commonly known as the pink spiraea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, spiral-shaped inflorescence. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wetlands. Its flowers are small, pink to white, and bloom in late summer to early fall.

Spiranthes callifera is known for its ornamental value and is often found in botanical gardens and natural habitats.
