Sideritis × fernandezii

Sideritis × fernandezii, commonly known as the hybrid sida, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or coastal environments.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
