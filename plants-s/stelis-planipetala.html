Stelis planipetala

Stelis planipetala, commonly known as the flat-leaved stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and broad, flat leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, often clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Stelis planipetala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
