Spilanthes hirta

Spilanthes hirta, commonly known as the hairy pippal, is a flowering plant native to regions of Asia, including India and Nepal.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features hairy stems and leaves, which contribute to its common name. Spilanthes hirta produces small yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.
