Stanhopea martiana var. bicolor

Stanhopea martiana var. bicolor, commonly known as the bicolor stanhopea, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers that typically feature a combination of white, yellow, and purple hues. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in its natural habitat. Its flowers are highly fragrant, attracting nocturnal pollinators such as moths.

This orchid is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its beauty and rarity.
