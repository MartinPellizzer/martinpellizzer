Scolymocephalus scolymus

Scolymocephalus scolymus, commonly known as the mountain hollyhock, is a perennial plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant features palmate leaves with a rough, hairy texture, and its stems are often woody at the base. Scolymocephalus scolymus thrives in cool, moist environments and is adapted to harsh alpine conditions.
