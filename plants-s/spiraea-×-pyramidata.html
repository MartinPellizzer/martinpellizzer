Spiraea × pyramidata

Spiraea × pyramidata, commonly known as the weeping willow spirea, is a deciduous shrub that features elegant, cascading branches.

It produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of white to pink, typically blooming in early summer. The plant has a compact, rounded form with dark green foliage that turns red in the fall. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Spiraea × pyramidata is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and adaptability to various growing conditions.
