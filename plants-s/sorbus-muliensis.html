Sorbus muliensis

Sorbus muliensis, commonly known as the Muli rowan, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and red, berry-like fruits. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and is adapted to temperate climates. Its leaves are alternate and ovate, with a serrated margin.

Sorbus muliensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
