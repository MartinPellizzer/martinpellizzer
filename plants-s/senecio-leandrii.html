Senecio leandrii

Senecio leandrii, commonly known as the Leandrii groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions. It features narrow, linear leaves and produces small yellow flowers that attract pollinators.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
