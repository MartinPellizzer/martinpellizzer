Sorbus kongboensis

Sorbus kongboensis, commonly known as the Bhutanese whitebeam, is a deciduous tree native to the Eastern Himalayas.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive white to pale yellow fruits. The plant typically grows at high altitudes, preferring cool and moist environments. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a serrated margin.

The species is valued for its ornamental value and plays a role in local ecosystems.
