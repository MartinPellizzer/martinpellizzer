Sophora alpina

Sophora alpina, commonly known as the alpine sophora, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, Europe, and North Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows in rocky or alpine environments. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are compound, with several pairs of oval-shaped leaflets.

Sophora alpina is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
