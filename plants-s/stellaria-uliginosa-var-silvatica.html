Stellaria uliginosa var. silvatica

Stellaria uliginosa var. silvatica, commonly known as woodland starwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to moist, shaded environments in temperate regions.

It features small, star-shaped white flowers with five petals, typically blooming in early spring. The plant has slender, branching stems and opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in rich, loamy soils and is often found in woodlands, stream banks, and wet meadows.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important component of moist forest ecosystems.
