Struthiopteris doodioides, commonly known as the Doodie's brake, is a fern belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its large, dark green, and leathery fronds that are typically divided into numerous pinnae. Struthiopteris doodioides is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ornamental appearance and ecological role in forest ecosystems.
