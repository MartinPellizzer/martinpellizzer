Schefflera rigida

Schefflera rigida, commonly known as the stiff schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, compound leaves that are typically arranged in a radiating pattern around the stems. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Schefflera rigida is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
