Schefflera brevipedicellata

Schefflera brevipedicellata, commonly known as the short-stemmed umbrella tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like crown of large, glossy green leaves. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters in its natural habitat. Its leaves are arranged in a compact, circular pattern at the ends of its branches, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Schefflera brevipedicellata is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
