Senecio campestris var. rupicola

Senecio campestris var. rupicola, commonly known as the rock Senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and produces clusters of small, yellow flowers. This variety is typically found growing on rocky outcrops and in arid environments. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

The plant is adapted to withstand drought and poor soil conditions, making it a hardy species.
