Specklinia lasioglossa

Specklinia lasioglossa, commonly known as the velvet leaf orchid, is a unique epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, velvety leaves that give it its common name, and it typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, often appearing in the spring. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air and surrounding substrates, making it well-suited for its natural habitat.

Specklinia lasioglossa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
