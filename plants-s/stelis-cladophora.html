Stelis cladophora

Stelis cladophora, commonly known as the clustered orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are usually clustered at the base, and the stems are short and often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Stelis cladophora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
