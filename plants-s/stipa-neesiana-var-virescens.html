Stipa neesiana var. virescens

Stipa neesiana var. virescens, commonly known as the green feather grass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, arching blades that are typically green to bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

This variety is noted for its drought tolerance and ability to form dense, low-growing clumps.
