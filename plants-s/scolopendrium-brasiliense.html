Scolopendrium brasiliense

Scolopendrium brasiliense, commonly known as the Brazilian clubmoss, is a member of the Lycopodiaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America.

Its unique morphology and ecological adaptations make it an important component of its native ecosystem.

Scolopendrium brasiliense is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in horticultural settings.
