Sesbania Sesban Var. Picta

Sesbania sesban var. picta is a striking leguminous plant known for its vibrant, colorful flowers. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in warm, humid climates. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardens and ecological restoration projects. The plant also plays a vital role in nitrogen fixation, enriching the soil. With its beauty and ecological benefits, it stands out as a valuable addition to diverse landscapes.
