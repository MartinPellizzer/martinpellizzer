Stelis coarctata

Stelis coarctata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with a distinctive spurred lip.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in tropical gardens.
