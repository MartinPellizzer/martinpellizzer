Solenogyne brachycomoides

Solenogyne brachycomoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved swamp everlasting, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wetland or swampy environments. The plant features small, compact flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it has a fibrous root system adapted to waterlogged soils.

Solenogyne brachycomoides is often found in coastal areas and is considered an important species for ecological studies in wetland ecosystems.
