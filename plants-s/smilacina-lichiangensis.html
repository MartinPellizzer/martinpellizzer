Smilacina lichiangensis

Smilacina lichiangensis, commonly known as the Yunnan greenbriar, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Smilacaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and tendrils. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a waxy coating.

Smilacina lichiangensis is adapted to moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
