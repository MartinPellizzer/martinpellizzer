Scrophularia tenuis

Scrophularia tenuis, commonly known as slender scrophularia, is a perennial herb native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in woodland edges or along stream banks. The plant features slender, erect stems that are often branched, with opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are hairy on both surfaces. Its flowers are tubular and usually pink or purple, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems.

Scrophularia tenuis is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
