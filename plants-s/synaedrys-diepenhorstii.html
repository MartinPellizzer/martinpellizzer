Synaedrys diepenhorstii

Synaedrys diepenhorstii, commonly known as the Diepenhorst's ghost orchid, is a rare and enigmatic orchid species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, ghost-like appearance, with translucent white flowers that resemble spectral forms. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in rocky or forested areas. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.

Due to habitat loss and its elusive nature, it is considered endangered and requires careful protection.
