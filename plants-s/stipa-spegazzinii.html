Stipa spegazzinii

Stipa spegazzinii, commonly known as the Spegazzini's grass, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and fine, silver-green foliage. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats.

Stipa spegazzinii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in harsh environments.
