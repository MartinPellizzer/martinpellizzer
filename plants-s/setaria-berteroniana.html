Setaria berteroniana

Setaria berteroniana, commonly known as slender false panicgrass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Its botanical features make it a common weed in agricultural and natural settings.
