Strumaria watermeyeri subsp. botterkloofensis

Strumaria watermeyeri subsp.

botterkloofensis, commonly known as the Botterkloof lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa. It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
