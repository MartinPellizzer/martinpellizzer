Sacciolepis aurita

Sacciolepis aurita, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking yellow and green foliage. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are often yellow or greenish in color. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Sacciolepis aurita is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
