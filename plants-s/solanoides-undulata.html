Solanoides undulata

Solanoides undulata, commonly known as the wavy leaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant is characterized by its undulating, wavy leaves and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white and purple. It typically grows in moist, tropical environments and is often found in shaded areas within its native range.

Solanoides undulata is not widely cultivated but is of interest to botanists due to its unique leaf morphology and potential medicinal properties.
