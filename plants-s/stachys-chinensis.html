Stachys chinensis

Stachys chinensis, commonly known as Chinese betony, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are arranged in whorled clusters, typically purple or violet in color, and bloom in summer.

Stachys chinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses in herbal remedies.
