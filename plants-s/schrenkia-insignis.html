Schrenkia insignis

Schrenkia insignis, commonly known as the Turk's cap lily, is a perennial bulbous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a distinctive red or purple throat. The plant produces a tall, erect stem with narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Schrenkia insignis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking blooms.
