Sida tomentella var. brevicalyx

Sida tomentella var. brevicalyx, commonly known as the woolly sida, is a herbaceous annual plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features small, yellow flowers and is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is often found in open habitats such as roadsides and fields. It has a shallow root system and is adapted to thrive in warm, humid climates.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional medicine.
