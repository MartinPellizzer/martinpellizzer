Scrophularia foliosa

Scrophularia foliosa, commonly known as the leafy scrophularia, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It is a member of the Scrophulariaceae family, which includes many species with showy, often tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or disturbed soils and has lance-shaped, often hairy leaves. Its flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom in early summer.

It is often found in open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.
