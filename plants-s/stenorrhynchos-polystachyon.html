Stenorrhynchos polystachyon

Stenorrhynchos polystachyon, commonly known as the narrow-leafed grass.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves arranged in a spiral pattern. Its inflorescence consists of dense, spike-like clusters of small, pale green to white flowers.

Stenorrhynchos polystachyon is often found in open, disturbed habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
