Spaniopappus hygrophilus

Spaniopappus hygrophilus, commonly known as the marsh hound, is a flowering plant native to wetland environments in the southeastern United States.

The plant typically grows in marshy areas, swamps, and along the edges of ponds, where it thrives in moist, sandy soils.
