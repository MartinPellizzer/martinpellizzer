Silene triflora

Silene triflora, commonly known as the three-flowered catchfly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family, characterized by its showy, pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. Its flowers are arranged in a raceme and bloom in late spring to early summer.

Silene triflora is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in disturbed or marginal environments.
