Sophora velutina var. dolichopoda

Sophora velutina var. dolichopoda, commonly known as the longpod Chinese wisteria, is a deciduous leguminous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are pinnately arranged and covered with a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces long, drooping clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. Its seed pods are elongated and cylindrical, giving the plant its common name.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and floral displays.
