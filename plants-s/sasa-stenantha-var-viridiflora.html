Sasa stenantha var. viridiflora

Sasa stenantha var. viridiflora, commonly known as the green-flowered bamboo, is a variant of the Sasa stenantha species native to East Asia.

It is a clump-forming evergreen bamboo that typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments. The plant features slender, hollow stems that are often green or yellow-green in color. Its leaves are lance-shaped, smooth, and have a glossy surface.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural settings for its unique foliage.
