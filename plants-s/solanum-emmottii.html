Solanum emmottii

Solanum emmottii, commonly known as the Emott's nightshade, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is native to parts of Africa, particularly Kenya and Tanzania, where it grows in woodland and forested areas. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. Its fruits are typically berries that mature to a deep purple or black, containing several seeds.

Solanum emmottii is often found in shaded environments and is adapted to thrive in humid, tropical conditions.
