Schefflera bourdillonii

Schefflera bourdillonii, commonly known as the African laceleaf, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, intricate foliage composed of small, leathery, dark green leaves arranged in a whorled pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Schefflera bourdillonii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor conditions.
