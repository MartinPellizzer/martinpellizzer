Scrophularia scopolii var. burdurensis

Scrophularia scopolii var. burdurensis, commonly known as Burdurensis Scrophularia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in rocky or limestone soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in some areas.
