Sirdavidia solannona

Sirdavidia solannona, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, fragrant, yellow flowers that bloom in long, hanging clusters, resembling a shower of golden rain. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The tree is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
