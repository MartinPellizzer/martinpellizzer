Senecio stenocephalus

Senecio stenocephalus, commonly known as the narrow-headed senecio, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is often cultivated in xeriscaped environments.
