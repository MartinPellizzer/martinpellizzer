Schnella hymenaeifolia

Schnella hymenaeifolia, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often found in moist lowland forests.

Schnella hymenaeifolia is known for its rapid growth and is sometimes considered an invasive species in certain regions.
