Solanum × juzepczukii f. ck'oyu-ckaisalla

Solanum × juzepczukii f. ck'oyu-ckaisalla, commonly known as the hybrid nightshade, is a botanical hybrid species within the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive foliage and flowers, which typically exhibit a range of colors from white to purple. The plant is native to regions in the Andes and is often found in high-altitude environments. It possesses medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.

Due to its hybrid nature, it displays genetic variability and is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
