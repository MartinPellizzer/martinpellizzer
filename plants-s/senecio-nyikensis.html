Senecio nyikensis

Senecio nyikensis, commonly known as the Nyika groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the Nyika Plateau in Malawi.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and foliage with a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in rocky and grassland habitats, thriving in regions with moderate rainfall. Its leaves are usually narrow and slightly hairy, while its yellow flowers bloom in clusters.

Senecio nyikensis plays a role in local ecosystems by providing food and shelter for various insect and bird species.
