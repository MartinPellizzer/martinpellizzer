Scilla amoena

Scilla amoena, commonly known as the beautiful scilla, is a flowering plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to southern Europe and western Asia, often found in woodland areas and along forest edges. The plant produces clusters of delicate, bell-shaped blue or purple flowers in early spring. Its leaves are grass-like, basal, and evergreen, contributing to its ornamental value.

Scilla amoena is valued in gardens for its early blooming and elegant appearance.
