Saxifraga × costei

Saxifraga × costei, commonly known as the hybrid saxifrage, is a flowering plant belonging to the Saxifragaceae family.

It is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves and clusters of small, often pink or white flowers. Native to mountainous regions of Europe, it thrives in cool, moist environments. The plant typically grows to a height of 10–30 cm and prefers well-drained, acidic soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine displays.
