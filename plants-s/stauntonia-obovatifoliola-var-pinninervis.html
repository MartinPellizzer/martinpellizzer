Stauntonia obovatifoliola var. pinninervis

Stauntonia obovatifoliola var. pinninervis, commonly known as the narrow-veined oval-leaf sterculia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, ovate leaves with a distinct pinnate venation pattern. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pale yellow or greenish-white. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

This variant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique foliage structure.
