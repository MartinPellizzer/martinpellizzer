Sicyos angulatus f. ohtanus

Sicyos angulatus f. ohtanus, commonly known as the Ohtani bottle gourd, is a variant of the bottle gourd plant native to Japan.

It is characterized by its elongated, bottle-shaped fruits that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant is a climbing vine with hairy stems and large, heart-shaped leaves. It thrives in warm climates and requires full sun for optimal growth.

The fruit is typically harvested when mature, and it is valued for its use in traditional Japanese cuisine and crafts.
