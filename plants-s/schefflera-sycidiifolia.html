Schefflera sycidiifolia

Schefflera sycidiifolia, commonly known as the Philippine umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like crown of large, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant has compound leaves with multiple leaflets arranged in a whorled pattern, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red, fleshy berries.

Schefflera sycidiifolia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
