Strumaria tenella subsp. tenella

Strumaria tenella subsp.

tenella, commonly known as the narrowleaf star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a small, herbaceous perennial that produces delicate, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink.

The plant has narrow, linear leaves and prefers well-drained soil in full sun to partial shade. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.

The species is also noted for its ability to thrive in arid conditions, making it a drought-tolerant choice for xeriscaping.
