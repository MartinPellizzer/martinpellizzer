Salicornia decussata

Salicornia decussata, commonly known as sea beans, is a halophytic plant adapted to thrive in saline environments.

It is a flowering plant in the Amaranthaceae family, characterized by its succulent, jointed stems and small, scale-like leaves. The plant produces tiny, green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It is often found in coastal areas, salt marshes, and other saline habitats where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small organisms.

Salicornia decussata is also used in some regions for culinary and medicinal purposes.
