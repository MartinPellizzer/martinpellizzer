Stelis kuijtii

Stelis kuijtii, commonly known as the Kuijt's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features a slender, upright stem with several fleshy, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, white to pale green, and arranged in a spike, often with a distinctive lip.

Stelis kuijtii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
