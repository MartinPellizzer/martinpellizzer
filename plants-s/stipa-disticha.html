Stipa disticha

Stipa disticha, commonly known as the slender feather grass, is a perennial grass native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage and slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are often used in floral arrangements. Stipa disticha thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and rocky areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping and garden design.
