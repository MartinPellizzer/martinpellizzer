Serapias orientalis var. spaethiae

Serapias orientalis var. spaethiae, commonly known as the Eastern Serapias, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features striking, brightly colored flowers that range from yellow to orange, often with a distinctive central pattern. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are basal, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
