Striolaria amazonica

Striolaria amazonica, commonly known as the Amazonian starfruit, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant produces small, edible fruits that resemble miniature starfish, hence its common name. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, often with a waxy surface that helps retain moisture in humid environments.

Striolaria amazonica is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its potential medicinal properties.
