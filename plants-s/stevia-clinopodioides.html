Stevia clinopodioides

Stevia clinopodioides, commonly known as sweetleaf or honey plant, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its aromatic, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves contain high concentrations of steviol glycosides, which are natural sweeteners.

Stevia clinopodioides is often cultivated for its medicinal and culinary uses due to its intense sweetness and health benefits.
