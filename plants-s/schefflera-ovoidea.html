Schefflera ovoidea

Schefflera ovoidea, commonly known as the eggplant schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, umbrella-like clusters of leaves that give it a unique appearance. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, ovoid leaflets that are glossy and dark green. It produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers followed by red, fleshy berries.

Schefflera ovoidea is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and adaptability to indoor environments.
