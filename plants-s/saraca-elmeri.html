Saraca elmeri

Saraca elmeri, commonly known as the Indian flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Indian subcontinent.

It is characterized by its striking orange to red flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptical in shape. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Saraca elmeri is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Ayurvedic practices.
