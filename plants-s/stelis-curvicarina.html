Stelis curvicarina

Stelis curvicarina, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pale yellow. The plant prefers epiphytic growth, often found on trees in humid, tropical environments. Its leaves are linear and arranged spirally along the stem, contributing to its unique appearance.

Stelis curvicarina is valued in horticulture for its delicate beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
