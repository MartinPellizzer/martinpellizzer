Scutellaria popovii

Scutellaria popovii, commonly known as Popov's blueweed, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. The plant features square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular blue or purple flowers. It thrives in rocky or well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Scutellaria popovii is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
