Stelis pendulata

Stelis pendulata, commonly known as the pendant stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees or rocks in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features a slender, pendulous stem with clustered flowers that are usually white or pale yellow. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Stelis pendulata thrives in humid environments with bright, indirect light.
