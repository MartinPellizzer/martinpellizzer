Swartzia cabrerae

Swartzia cabrerae, commonly known as the Cabrera's ironwood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its dense, dark-colored wood, which is highly valued for its strength and durability. The plant has compound leaves with elliptical leaflets and produces small, fragrant yellow flowers. Swartzia cabrerae thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in secondary forest growth.

Its ecological importance includes providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
