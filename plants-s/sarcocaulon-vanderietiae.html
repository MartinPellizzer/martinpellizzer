Sarcocaulon vanderietiae

Sarcocaulon vanderietiae, commonly known as the Vanderiet's rock cress, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family and is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in spring. Its leaves are basal, rosette-like, and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Sarcocaulon vanderietiae is adapted to rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.
