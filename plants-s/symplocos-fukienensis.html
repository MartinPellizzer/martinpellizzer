Symplocos fukienensis

Symplocos fukienensis, commonly known as the Fukien tea plant, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. Its fruit is a capsule containing several small, dark brown seeds.
