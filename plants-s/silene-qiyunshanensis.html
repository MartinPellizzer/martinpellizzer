Silene qiyunshanensis

Silene qiyunshanensis, commonly known as the Qiyunshan catchfly, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pinkish-purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or grassy areas at high altitudes. Its leaves are linear and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and branching.
