Solenandra ixoroides subsp. eggersii

Solenandra ixoroides subsp.

eggersii, commonly known as the Christmas star, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red.

The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and glossy, providing a lush green backdrop to its vibrant blooms.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
