Streptanthus Pilosus

Streptanthus pilosus is a unique flowering plant native to the western united states. it belongs to the brassicaceae family and is known for its striking purple and white blooms. this plant thrives in rocky and coastal environments, often found in california and oregon. its hairy leaves and delicate flowers make it a distinctive addition to native plant gardens. due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
