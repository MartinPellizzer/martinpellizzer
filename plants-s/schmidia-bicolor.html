Schmidia bicolor

Schmidia bicolor, commonly known as the silver stripe plant, is a member of the Marantaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves with silver and green stripes. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Schmidia bicolor is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
