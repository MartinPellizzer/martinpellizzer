Sorghum pohlianum

Sorghum pohlianum, commonly known as the red sorghum, is a species of grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect growth and striking red or purple flower heads. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant color and unique appearance. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil.

Sorghum pohlianum is also valued for its potential use in traditional medicine and as a source of forage for livestock.
