Strychnos toxifera var. acuminata

Strychnos toxifera var. acuminata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved poisonwood, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Loganiaceae and is characterized by its narrow, acuminate leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. However, due to its potent toxins, it is generally avoided for cultivation and handling.
