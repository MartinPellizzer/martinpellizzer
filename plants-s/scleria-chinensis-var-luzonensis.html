Scleria chinensis var. luzonensis

Scleria chinensis var. luzonensis, commonly known as the Luzon flat sedge, is a perennial herb native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, linear leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountainous regions and forest floors. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The species is part of the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants.
