Senecio tablensis

Senecio tablensis, commonly known as the Table Mountain groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage is usually gray-green with a fuzzy texture, and it produces small yellow flowers.

Senecio tablensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and hardy nature.
