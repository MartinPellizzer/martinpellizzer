Stenomesson splendens

Stenomesson splendens, commonly known as the splendour grass, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its erect, slender stems and narrow leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, two-lobed flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
