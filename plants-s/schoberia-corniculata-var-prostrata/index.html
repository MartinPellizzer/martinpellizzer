Schoberia Corniculata Var. Prostrata

Schoberia corniculata var. prostrata is a low-growing perennial plant native to rocky and sandy soils in arid regions. It features tiny, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white, adding subtle beauty to its surroundings. The plant's prostrate habit allows it to spread across the ground, forming dense mats that help prevent soil erosion. It is well adapted to harsh climates, thriving in full sun and requiring minimal water. This resilient species is often overlooked but plays an important ecological role in its native habitat.
