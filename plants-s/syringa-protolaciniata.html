Syringa protolaciniata

Syringa protolaciniata, commonly known as the Chinese lilac, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Syringa protolaciniata is also valued for its adaptability and resistance to pests and diseases.
