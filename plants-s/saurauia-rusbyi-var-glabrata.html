Saurauia rusbyi var. glabrata

Saurauia rusbyi var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth saurauia, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are typically smooth and glossy. The plant produces clusters of white or pale pink flowers during the rainy season, which are followed by small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in primary forests.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
