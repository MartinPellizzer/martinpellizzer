Senecio hollandii

Senecio hollandii, commonly known as the Dutch Senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, daisy-like blooms throughout the growing season.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens, though it may be considered invasive in some regions.
