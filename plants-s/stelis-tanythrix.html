Stelis tanythrix

Stelis tanythrix, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a small epiphytic or lithophytic orchid, typically found growing on trees or rocks in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has slender, wiry stems and produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are narrow and arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps in water retention.

The species is characterized by its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
