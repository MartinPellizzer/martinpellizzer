Sorghum nutans subsp. avenaceum

Sorghum nutans subsp.

avenaceum, commonly known as dwarf sorghum, is a grass species native to Africa. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and shorter stature compared to other sorghum species.

The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny grains. It thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated for its grain or as a forage crop.

Its botanical features make it adaptable to various soil types and environmental conditions.
