Senna tuhovalyana

Senna tuhovalyana, commonly known as the Tuhovalyana senna, is a species of flowering plant in the Fabaceae family.

It is native to Madagascar and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces elongated, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to the arid conditions of its native environment.

Senna tuhovalyana is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local communities.
