Statice lepturoides

Statice lepturoides, commonly known as the slender sea lavender, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and spikes of small, showy blue or purple flowers. The plant thrives in saline and well-drained soils, often found in dunes and salt marshes. It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family, which includes other salt-tolerant species.

Statice lepturoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize coastal soils.
