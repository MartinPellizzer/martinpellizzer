Senega Graebiana

Senega graebiana is a rare and elusive plant native to the southeastern United States. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as coves and stream banks. This species is known for its unique, delicate flowers that bloom in late spring. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this rare botanical gem.
