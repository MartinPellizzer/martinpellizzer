Senecio pulcher f. pulcher

Senecio pulcher f. pulcher, commonly known as the beautiful groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of yellow flowers with prominent central disks. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value comes from its delicate appearance and ability to attract pollinators.
