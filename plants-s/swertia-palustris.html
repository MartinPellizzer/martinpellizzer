Swertia palustris

Swertia palustris, commonly known as marsh gentian, is a perennial herb native to wetland environments in North America.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist soils, often near streams or in swampy areas. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are slender and branching.

Swertia palustris plays an important role in supporting local wildlife and maintaining wetland ecosystems.
