Scutellaria javanensis

Scutellaria javanensis, commonly known as Java skullcap, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas.

Scutellaria javanensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
