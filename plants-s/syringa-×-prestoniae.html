Syringa × prestoniae

Syringa × prestoniae, commonly known as the Chinese lilac, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, showy flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, oval-shaped leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Syringa × prestoniae is valued for its hardiness and ability to withstand urban conditions.
