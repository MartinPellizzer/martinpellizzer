Statice taubertii

Statice taubertii, commonly known as Sea Lavender, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and its ability to thrive in coastal and saline environments. The plant produces small, dense clusters of blue to purple flowers, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and silver-gray, adapted to reduce water loss in arid conditions.

Statice taubertii is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
