Sesbania triflora

Sesbania triflora, commonly known as the three-flowered sesbania, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces elongated, cylindrical pods that contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in various climatic conditions.

Sesbania triflora is valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it useful in agricultural settings.
