Sterculia colombiana

Sterculia colombiana, commonly known as the Colombian sterculia, is a tropical tree native to the Andean regions of Colombia.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves. The tree is valued for its ornamental qualities and has cultural significance in its native habitat.
