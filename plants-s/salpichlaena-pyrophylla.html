Salpichlaena pyrophylla

Salpichlaena pyrophylla, commonly known as the flame fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, bright red or orange spore-bearing fronds, which give it its common name. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests. Its fronds are typically leathery and have a unique, almost fiery appearance.

Salpichlaena pyrophylla is valued both for its ornamental qualities and its ecological role in forest ecosystems.
