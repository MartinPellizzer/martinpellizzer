Salicornia prostrata subsp. simonkaiana

Salicornia prostrata subsp.

simonkaiana, commonly known as sea beans or glassworts, is a halophytic plant adapted to saline environments. It is a low-growing, succulent species with fleshy, cylindrical stems that are often tinged with red or purple.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. It thrives in coastal areas, salt marshes, and other saline soils, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various organisms.

Its ability to tolerate high salinity makes it an important component of coastal ecosystems.
