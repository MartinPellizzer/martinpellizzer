Satureja barosma

Satureja barosma, commonly known as Greek thyme, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 cm and produces small, pink or white flowers. Its leaves are fragrant, containing essential oils that contribute to its medicinal and culinary uses.

Satureja barosma is often used in traditional medicine for its antiseptic and digestive properties.
