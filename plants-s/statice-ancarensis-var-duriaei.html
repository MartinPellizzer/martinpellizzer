Statice ancarensis var. duriaei

Statice ancarensis var. duriaei, commonly known as the Cape Statice, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, long-lasting blooms that range in color from pink to purple. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is well adapted to saline and windy coastal environments. Its narrow, grass-like leaves and clustered flowers make it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in harsh conditions.
