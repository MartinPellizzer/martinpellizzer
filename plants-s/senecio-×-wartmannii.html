Senecio × wartmannii

Senecio × wartmannii, commonly known as the Wartmann's ragwort, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy, often glandular leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is native to southern Africa. It exhibits a variety of morphological traits due to its hybrid origin, making it a subject of interest in botanical studies.

Its ecological role includes providing nectar for pollinators and contributing to local biodiversity.
