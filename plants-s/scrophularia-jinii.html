Scrophularia jinii

Scrophularia jinii, commonly known as the Chinese figwort, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Scrophulariaceae family and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces showy, two-lipped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Scrophularia jinii is also used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
