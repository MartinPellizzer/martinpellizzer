Stellaria souliei

Stellaria souliei, commonly known as the Chinese starwort, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, star-shaped white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, with a hairy texture.

Stellaria souliei is often used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
