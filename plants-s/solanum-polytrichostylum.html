Solanum polytrichostylum

Solanum polytrichostylum, commonly known as the hairy nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant features hairy stems and leaves, and its flowers are typically white or pale purple. Its berries are small and often contain toxic compounds, making it potentially harmful to humans and animals.

It is often found in woodland areas and thrives in moist, shaded environments.
