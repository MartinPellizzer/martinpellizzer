Smilax elegans

Smilax elegans, commonly known as the elegant smilax, is a climbing vine native to the southeastern United States.

It features woody stems with sharp, recurved spines and heart-shaped, glossy dark green leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Smilax elegans is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing food for wildlife.
