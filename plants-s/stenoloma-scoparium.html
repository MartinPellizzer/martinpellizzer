Stenoloma scoparium

Stenoloma scoparium, commonly known as the narrow-leafed grass.

It is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands, woodlands, and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and often have a slight sheen, while its inflorescence consists of spikelets arranged in a loose panicle.

Stenoloma scoparium is valued for its resilience and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
