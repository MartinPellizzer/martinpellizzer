Solanum tenerum

Solanum tenerum, commonly known as the slender nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, and its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often hairy. Solanum tenerum belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae), which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes.

It is often found in wetlands and along stream banks, where it thrives in humid conditions.
