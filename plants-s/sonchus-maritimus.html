Sonchus maritimus

Sonchus maritimus, commonly known as sea lettuce or common sowthistle, is a flowering plant in the dandelion family.

It is an annual herb that typically grows in coastal areas, salt marshes, and disturbed soils. The plant has alternate, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, and it produces yellow flowers that are surrounded by pale green bracts. It is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in saline environments.

The plant is often used in traditional medicine and has been studied for its potential nutritional and medicinal properties.
