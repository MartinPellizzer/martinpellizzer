Simirestis dewildemaniana

Simirestis dewildemaniana, commonly known as the African violet, is a tropical perennial plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette of velvety, waxy leaves and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

Simirestis dewildemaniana is also valued for its ability to bloom throughout the year under optimal conditions.
