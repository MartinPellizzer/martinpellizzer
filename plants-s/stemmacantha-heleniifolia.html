Stemmacantha heleniifolia

Stemmacantha heleniifolia, commonly known as the spiral leaf succulent, is a unique plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it a distinctive and ornamental appearance. The plant has a short, thick stem that supports its fleshy, waxy leaves. It thrives in arid conditions and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for xeriscaping.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for rock gardens and as a decorative indoor plant.
