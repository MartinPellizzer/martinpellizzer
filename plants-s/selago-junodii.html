Selago junodii

Selago junodii, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and thistles. The plant produces small, yellow flowers surrounded by white ray florets, giving it a daisy-like appearance. It is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Selago junodii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its vibrant blooms and drought tolerance.
