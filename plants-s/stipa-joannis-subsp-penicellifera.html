Stipa joannis subsp. penicellifera

Stipa joannis subsp.

penicellifera, commonly known as the Penicellifera grass, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves.

The plant produces dense, spike-like inflorescences that are typically pale green to golden in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as rocky slopes and grasslands.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in supporting local wildlife.
