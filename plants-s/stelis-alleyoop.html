Stelis alleyoop

Stelis alleyoop, commonly known as the orchid vine, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink. The plant has fleshy, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Stelis alleyoop thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
