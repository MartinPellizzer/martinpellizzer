Sophora biflora

Sophora biflora, commonly known as the two-flowered sophora, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, yellowish-white flowers. The plant produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Sophora biflora is often found in moist, shady environments and is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological benefits.

It also has traditional medicinal uses in Chinese herbal medicine.
