Scleranthus dissitiflorus

Scleranthus dissitiflorus, commonly known as the scattered-flowered thornweed, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Europe and western Asia.

The plant typically grows as a low, sprawling herb with hairy stems and small, greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in fine hairs, helping to reduce water loss.
