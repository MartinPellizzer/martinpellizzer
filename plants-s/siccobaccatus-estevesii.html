Siccobaccatus estevesii

Siccobaccatus estevesii, commonly known as the São Paulo burr.

It is a flowering plant native to Brazil, specifically the state of São Paulo. The plant is characterized by its woody stems and small, spiny fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in scrubland and savanna environments.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
