Solanum caniarense

Solanum caniarense, commonly known as the Canary Islands nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 meter. The plant produces small, violet flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its berries are toxic and should not be consumed by humans or animals.

Solanum caniarense is often found in rocky or coastal areas, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
