Solanum conraui

Solanum conraui, commonly known as the Conraui nightshade, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is native to parts of Central and South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its erect, branched stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a hairy texture. Its flowers are typically white or pale blue, with five petals and a prominent yellow stamen.

The fruit is a berry that turns from green to red when mature, containing several seeds.
