Sium nipponicum

Sium nipponicum, commonly known as Japanese water dropwort, is a herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, hollow stems and distinctive, umbrella-shaped inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy habitats, often near water bodies. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, with a rough texture.

It produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late summer.
