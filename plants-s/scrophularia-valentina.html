Scrophularia valentina

Scrophularia valentina, commonly known as Valentine's scrophularia, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae, which includes many species with showy flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer, are usually pink or purple and have a distinctive two-lipped structure.
