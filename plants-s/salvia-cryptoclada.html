Salvia cryptoclada

Salvia cryptoclada, commonly known as the cryptic sage, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branched stems covered in dense, grayish-green foliage. The plant produces striking violet-blue flowers in whorls along the stem, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy addition to rock gardens.

Its aromatic leaves and showy blooms make it a popular choice for both ornamental and medicinal purposes.
