Schefflera osyana

Schefflera osyana, commonly known as the Osyana umbrella tree, is a species native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its distinctive, umbrella-like crown of large, glossy green leaves. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters in its natural habitat. Its leaves are typically arranged in a whorled pattern, giving it a lush, full appearance.

Schefflera osyana is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
