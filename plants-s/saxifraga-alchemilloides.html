Saxifraga alchemilloides

Saxifraga alchemilloides, commonly known as the alpine saxifrage, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It typically thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats. The plant features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, often blooming in early summer. Its leaves are usually basal, rosette-shaped, and covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

Saxifraga alchemilloides is known for its hardiness and ability to withstand cold temperatures and harsh environmental conditions.
