Silene ertekinii

Silene ertekinii, commonly known as the Ertekin's campion, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its simple, erect stems and showy pink or red flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its leaves are opposite and linear, often covered in fine hairs.

Silene ertekinii is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and limited distribution.
