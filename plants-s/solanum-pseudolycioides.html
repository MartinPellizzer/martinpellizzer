Solanum pseudolycioides

Solanum pseudolycioides, commonly known as the hairy nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific. The plant features hairy stems and leaves, with small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruits are berries that turn from green to yellow or red as they mature.

It is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and agricultural fields.
