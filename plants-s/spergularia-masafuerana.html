Spergularia masafuerana

Spergularia masafuerana, commonly known as the Masafuera sandspurry, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically thrives in sandy or rocky soils. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by small, round fruits. It is adapted to arid environments and is often found in coastal areas.

Its botanical features make it a unique species within the Caryophyllaceae family.
