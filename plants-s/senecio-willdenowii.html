Senecio willdenowii

Senecio willdenowii, commonly known as the African marguerite, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features silver-gray foliage with a woolly texture, and its flowers are typically yellow with white ray florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun exposure.

Senecio willdenowii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and hardy nature.
