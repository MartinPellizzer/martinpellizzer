Stromanthe setosa

Stromanthe setosa, commonly known as the velvet leaf plant, is a member of the Marantaceae family.

It is native to the tropical rainforests of Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that often feature a mix of green, silver, and pink hues. Its foliage is velvety to the touch, giving it its common name.

Stromanthe setosa thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.
