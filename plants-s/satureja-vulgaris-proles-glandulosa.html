Satureja vulgaris proles glandulosa

Satureja vulgaris proles glandulosa, commonly known as glandular thyme, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, small, green leaves and pink or white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces glandular secretions that contribute to its strong, minty fragrance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its essential oils are valued for their culinary and medicinal properties.
