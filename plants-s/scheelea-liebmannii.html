Scheelea liebmannii

Scheelea liebmannii, commonly known as the Liebmann's palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and pinnate leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by triangular, red fruits.
