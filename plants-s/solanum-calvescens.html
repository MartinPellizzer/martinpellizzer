Solanum calvescens

Solanum calvescens, commonly known as the wild tomato or small-fruited nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its hairy stems and alternate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into berries containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal uses, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
