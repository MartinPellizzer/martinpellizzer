Senna tropica

Senna tropica, commonly known as the tropical senna, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its palmate leaves, which are typically divided into five to seven leaflets. The plant produces yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes, and it is known for its ability to thrive in dry, sunny environments. Senna tropica is also notable for its medicinal properties, with various parts of the plant used in traditional medicine.

Its seeds contain compounds that have been studied for their potential health benefits.
