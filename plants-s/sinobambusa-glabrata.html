Sinobambusa glabrata

Sinobambusa glabrata, commonly known as the smooth bamboo, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems that are covered in a smooth, glabrous surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are long and narrow, with a smooth texture and a slightly pointed tip.

Sinobambusa glabrata is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its elegant appearance and adaptability to various growing conditions.
