Schismatoglottis calyptrata f. dahlii

Schismatoglottis calyptrata f. dahlii, commonly known as the Dahl's schismatoglottis, is a tropical plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia, and is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white spadices surrounded by a colorful spathe, which is a typical feature of the Araceae family. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This ornamental plant is often cultivated for its unique foliage and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
