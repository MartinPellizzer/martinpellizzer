Schouwia semiserrata

Schouwia semiserrata, commonly known as the serrated schouwia, is a striking member of the Araceae family native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces inflorescences consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, which adds to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil.

Schouwia semiserrata is valued in horticulture for its unique foliage and dramatic floral display.
