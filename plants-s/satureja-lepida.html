Satureja lepida

Satureja lepida, commonly known as narrowleaf savory, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, aromatic leaves and clusters of pink to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is well adapted to Mediterranean climates. Its leaves are often used in cooking for their pungent, slightly peppery flavor.

Satureja lepida is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
