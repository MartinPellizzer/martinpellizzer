Stillingia melanosticta

Stillingia melanosticta, commonly known as black-stained stillingia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its woody stems and simple, alternate leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by distinctive, elongated seed pods. Stillingia melanosticta thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.
