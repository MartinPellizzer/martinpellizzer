Senecio spathulifolius

Senecio spathulifolius, commonly known as the broad-leaf senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, featuring elongated, lance-shaped leaves and yellow-orange flowers that bloom in summer. Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often cultivated in regions with a Mediterranean climate.
