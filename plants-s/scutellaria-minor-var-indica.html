Scutellaria minor var. indica

Scutellaria minor var. indica, commonly known as Indian skullcap, is a perennial herb native to parts of Asia, including India and China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants such as mint and lavender. The plant features square, hairy stems and opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. Its small, tubular flowers are typically blue or violet, arranged in whorls along the stem.

It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported calming and anti-inflammatory properties.
