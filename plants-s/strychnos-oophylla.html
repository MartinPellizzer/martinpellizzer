Strychnos oophylla

Strychnos oophylla, commonly known as the "eggplant tree," is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub with smooth, dark green leaves that are often clustered at the base of the stems. The plant produces small, fragrant white or yellowish flowers that are followed by distinctive, egg-shaped fruits. Its fruits are typically yellow or orange when mature and contain a single large seed.

Strychnos oophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage and fruit structure.
