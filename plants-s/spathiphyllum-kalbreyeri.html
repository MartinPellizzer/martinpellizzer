Spathiphyllum kalbreyeri

Spathiphyllum kalbreyeri, commonly known as the Kalbreyer's peace lily, is a tropical evergreen plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It features broad, dark green, heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and upright, giving it a lush, ornamental appearance. The plant produces striking white spathes that enclose small, greenish-yellow flowers, which are typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in low-light conditions and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its air-purifying qualities and ease of care.
