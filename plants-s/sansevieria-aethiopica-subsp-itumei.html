Sansevieria aethiopica subsp. itumei

Sansevieria aethiopica subsp.

itumei, commonly known as the African spear or tiger tail plant, is a compact, upright succulent native to South Africa. It features narrow, dark green leaves with white or yellowish variegations that resemble tiger stripes, giving it its common name.

The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. It produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers on slender stalks under optimal growing conditions.

Its foliage is characterized by a rigid, upright growth habit and a distinctive, almost sword-like appearance.
