Senecio succulentus

Senecio succulentus, commonly known as the Narrow-leaved Senecio, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, narrow leaves that are typically blue-green in color, adapted to store water and reduce transpiration. The plant produces yellow flowers in late spring, which are clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping in arid climates.
