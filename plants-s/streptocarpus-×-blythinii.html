Streptocarpus × blythinii

Streptocarpus × blythinii, commonly known as the hybrid African violet, is a hybrid plant resulting from crosses between Streptocarpus species and African violets (Saintpaulia).

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, violet-like flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant typically has fleshy, dark green leaves that are often marked with silver or white veins. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This hybrid is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and ease of care.
