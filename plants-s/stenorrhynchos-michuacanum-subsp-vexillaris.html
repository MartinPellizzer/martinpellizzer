Stenorrhynchos michuacanum subsp. vexillaris

Stenorrhynchos michuacanum subsp.

The plant typically grows in rocky or open habitats, often in areas with well-drained soil. It produces showy blooms during the spring and summer months, making it a notable ornamental species.
