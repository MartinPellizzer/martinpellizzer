Senecio austromontanus

Senecio austromontanus, commonly known as the southern mountain groundsel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions. The plant features wiry stems and narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are yellow, arranged in a composite head, and bloom in late summer.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
