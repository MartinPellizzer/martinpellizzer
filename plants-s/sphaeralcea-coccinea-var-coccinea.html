Sphaeralcea coccinea var. coccinea

Sphaeralcea coccinea var. coccinea, commonly known as the showy milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments.
