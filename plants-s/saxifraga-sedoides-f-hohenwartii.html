Saxifraga sedoides f. hohenwartii

Saxifraga sedoides f. hohenwartii, commonly known as the Hohenwart's saxifrage, is a flowering plant belonging to the Saxifragaceae family.

It is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves and clusters of small, white or pinkish flowers. Native to the alpine regions of Central Europe, this plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. It is a perennial species that typically blooms in late spring to early summer.

The varietal form f. hohenwartii is distinguished by its specific leaf morphology and flowering habits.
