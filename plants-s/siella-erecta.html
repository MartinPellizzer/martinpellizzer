Siella erecta

Siella erecta, commonly known as the upright siella, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Apocynaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its erect growth habit and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, with five petals and a prominent corona.

Siella erecta is often found in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
