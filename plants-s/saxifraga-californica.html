Saxifraga californica

Saxifraga californica, commonly known as the California saxifrage, is a perennial flowering plant native to the western United States.

It features rosettes of fleshy, waxy leaves that are typically green with a bluish or reddish tinge. The plant produces small, pink to white flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Saxifraga californica is adapted to dry environments and is drought-tolerant once established.
