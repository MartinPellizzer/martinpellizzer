Sedum kagamontanum

Sedum kagamontanum, commonly known as Kagamontan stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Japan.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink or white. The plant has thick, water-storing leaves that help it survive in arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long blooming period.
