Senecio multiceps

Senecio multiceps, commonly known as the woolly senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
