Sutherlandia foliolata

Sutherlandia foliolata, commonly known as cancer bush, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and woody stems. The plant produces small, bean-like pods that contain seeds, and it thrives in sandy or rocky soils.

It is often found in grassland and savanna habitats, and it plays a role in local ecosystems by providing food for various animals.
