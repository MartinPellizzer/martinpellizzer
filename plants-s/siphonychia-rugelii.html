Siphonychia rugelii

Siphonychia rugelii, commonly known as the roughleaf siphonychia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in a fine, rough texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pale green to yellow in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands or rocky areas.

Siphonychia rugelii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
