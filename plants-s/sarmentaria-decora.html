Sarmentaria decora

Sarmentaria decora, commonly known as the Decorative Wreath vine, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for hanging baskets and trellises.
