Senecio reinoldii

Senecio reinoldii, commonly known as the Reinold's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions. It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small yellow flowers that bloom in the spring.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
