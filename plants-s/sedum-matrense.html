Sedum matrense

Sedum matrense, commonly known as Matrena's stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow to orange. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Its leaves are typically green with a waxy coating, aiding in water retention.

Sedum matrense is often used in landscaping for its low maintenance and ornamental value.
