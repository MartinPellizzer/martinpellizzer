Scholtzia parviflora

Scholtzia parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered scholtzia, is a flowering plant native to California and Baja California.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellow to orange flowers in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Scholtzia parviflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
