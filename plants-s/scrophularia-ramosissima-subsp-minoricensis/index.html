Scrophularia Ramosissima Subsp. Minoricensis

Scrophularia ramosissima subsp. minoricensis is a rare and lesser-known plant species native to the mountainous regions of the Iberian Peninsula. It belongs to the family Plantaginaceae and is characterized by its delicate, hairy leaves and clusters of pale pink to white flowers. This subspecies thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded, moist microhabitats. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered vulnerable to environmental changes and human encroachment. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this unique botanical gem from extinction.
