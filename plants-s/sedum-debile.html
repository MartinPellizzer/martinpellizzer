Sedum debile

Sedum debile, commonly known as weak stonecrop, is a low-growing succulent native to the Mediterranean region.

It features fleshy, spoon-shaped leaves that are typically pale green to blue-gray in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Sedum debile is drought-tolerant and requires minimal care once established.
