Spiraea jiangxiensis

Spiraea jiangxiensis, commonly known as Jiangxi spiraea, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its clusters of small, white or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its foliage is dense and dark green, providing ornamental value in gardens.

It is valued for its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
