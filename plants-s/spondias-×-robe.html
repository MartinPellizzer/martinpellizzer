Spondias × robe

Spondias × robe, commonly known as the red mangosteeno, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Spondias mombinensis and Spondias pinnata.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in warm, humid climates. The plant produces small, round fruits with a thick, red rind and a sweet, aromatic pulp. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a distinctive glossy appearance.

The botanical characteristics of Spondias × robe make it a valuable species for both ornamental and culinary purposes.
