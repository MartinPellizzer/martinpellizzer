Spathoglottis erectiflora

Spathoglottis erectiflora, commonly known as the upright-flowered orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant with a rhizomatous growth habit, typically found in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Spathoglottis erectiflora is valued in horticulture for its beauty and ability to thrive in tropical climates.
