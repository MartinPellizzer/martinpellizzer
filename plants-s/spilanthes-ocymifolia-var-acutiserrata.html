Spilanthes ocymifolia var. acutiserrata

Spilanthes ocymifolia var. acutiserrata, commonly known as the acutely serrated spilanthes, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its compact, herbaceous growth habit and features small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense clusters. The plant possesses a distinctive, pungent odor, which is often described as similar to that of a mustard plant. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped with acute or serrated margins, contributing to its common name.

This species is valued in traditional medicine for its potential medicinal properties.
