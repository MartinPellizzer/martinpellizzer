Solanum tuberosum var. brevidens

Solanum tuberosum var. brevidens, commonly known as the dwarf potato, is a botanical variant of the common potato.

It is characterized by its shorter stems and smaller tubers compared to standard potato varieties. This plant belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is native to the Andes region of South America. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its compact size makes it suitable for smaller gardens and container cultivation.
