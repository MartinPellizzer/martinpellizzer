Solanum nigrum f. uliginosum

Solanum nigrum f. uliginosum, commonly known as black nightshade in a swampy habitat, is a variant of the common black nightshade.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant belonging to the nightshade family, Solanaceae. This form is adapted to wet or marshy environments, exhibiting characteristics suited for such conditions. Its leaves are typically larger and more elongated compared to the standard species.

The plant produces small, dark purple berries that are toxic to humans and animals.
