Senna monilifera

Senna monilifera, commonly known as the yellow senna, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It is often found in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Senna monilifera is also noted for its use in traditional medicine and as a source of dye.
