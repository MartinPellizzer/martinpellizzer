Senecio nimborum

Senecio nimborum, commonly known as the cloudland groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowers are small, yellow, and arranged in dense clusters, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Senecio nimborum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
