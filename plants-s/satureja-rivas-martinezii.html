Satureja rivas-martinezii

Satureja rivas-martinezii, commonly known as Rivas-Martinez savory, is a rare and endemic species native to the mountainous regions of central Spain.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is known for its essential oils and aromatic properties. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to high-altitude environments.
