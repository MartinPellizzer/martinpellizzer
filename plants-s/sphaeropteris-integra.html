Sphaeropteris integra

Sphaeropteris integra, commonly known as the shield fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its sturdy, erect trunk and large, pinnate fronds that can reach up to 3 meters in length. The fronds are typically dark green and have a smooth, leathery texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
