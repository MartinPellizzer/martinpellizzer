Sabal haitensis

Sabal haitensis, commonly known as the Haitian cabbage palm, is a species of palm native to the Caribbean.

It is a tall, slender palm with a slender trunk and large, feather-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into clusters of berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a common sight in coastal areas.

Its resilience and adaptability have made it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical and subtropical regions.
