Symplocos graveolens

Symplocos graveolens, commonly known as the sweet pepper tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring.
