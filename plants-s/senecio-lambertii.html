Senecio lambertii

Senecio lambertii, commonly known as Lambert's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright, branched stems and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
