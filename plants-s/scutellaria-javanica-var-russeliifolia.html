Scutellaria javanica var. russeliifolia

Scutellaria javanica var. russeliifolia, commonly known as the Russel's skullcap, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks.

Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a velvety texture, while its roots are used in traditional medicine.
