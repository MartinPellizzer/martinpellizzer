Sesbania hirticalyx

Sesbania hirticalyx, commonly known as the hairy sesbania, is a tropical leguminous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with hairy surfaces and small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces elongated, pod-like fruits containing several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in moist, tropical environments.

Sesbania hirticalyx is known for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it useful in soil enrichment and agroforestry systems.
