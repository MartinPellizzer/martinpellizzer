Stellaria xanthospora

Stellaria xanthospora, commonly known as the yellow starwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features small, white or yellow star-shaped flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has slender, branching stems covered with fine hairs and produces small, capsule-like fruits. Its leaves are typically oval to lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Stellaria xanthospora is often found in moist, calcareous soils in meadows and woodland edges.
