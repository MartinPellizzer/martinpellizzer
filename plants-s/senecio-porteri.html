Senecio porteri

Senecio porteri, commonly known as the African Daisy, is a perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow-orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, gray-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Senecio porteri is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
