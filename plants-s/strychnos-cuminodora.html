Strychnos cuminodora

Strychnos cuminodora, commonly known as the African strychnos, is a deciduous shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Loganiaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces woody, angular fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in dry, open woodlands and is adapted to arid conditions.
