Syringodea coronata

Syringodea coronata, commonly known as the crown fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and stream banks. Its rhizome is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
