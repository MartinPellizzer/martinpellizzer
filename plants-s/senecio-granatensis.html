Senecio granatensis

Senecio granatensis, commonly known as the Granate Senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in a head, surrounded by pale green bracts.

Senecio granatensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and hardy nature.
