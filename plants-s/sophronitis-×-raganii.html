Sophronitis × raganii

Sophronitis × raganii, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a naturally occurring hybrid between Sophronitis censata and S. phalaenopsis.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically feature a white or pale pink lip with a contrasting yellow or orange center. The plant is epiphytic, growing on trees or other structures without deriving nutrients from them. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and consistent moisture.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and unique hybrid characteristics.
