Sedum × nicaeense

Sedum × nicaeense, commonly known as the stonecrop hybrid, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is well adapted to rocky and arid environments, thriving in full sun and well-drained soil. It is often used in rock gardens and as a decorative ground cover due to its hardy nature and ornamental appeal.

Sedum × nicaeense is also valued for its ability to survive in harsh conditions with minimal water.
