Sesleria Varia F. Elongata

Elongated sesleria, scientifically known as Sesleria varia f. elongata, is a striking ornamental grass native to southern Africa. This unique plant features tall, slender blades that create a graceful, flowing appearance in gardens or landscapes. Its foliage ranges from deep green to bluish-gray, adding visual interest throughout the growing season. Often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements, it thrives in well-drained soils and full to partial sun. With its elegant form and adaptability, elongated sesleria is a popular choice among gardeners seeking both beauty and resilience.
