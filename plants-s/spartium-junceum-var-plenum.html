Spartium junceum var. plenum

Spartium junceum var. plenum, commonly known as the full-blown spartium, is a variant of the spartium plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the summer months. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with slender, linear leaflets, and it often grows in dry, rocky soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in arid environments.
