Secamone tenuifolia

Secamone tenuifolia, commonly known as slenderleaf secamone, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and southern Asia.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and small, opposite leaves.
