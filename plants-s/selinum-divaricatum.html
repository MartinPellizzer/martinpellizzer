Selinum divaricatum

Selinum divaricatum, commonly known as the zigzag fennel, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its distinctive, zigzag-shaped leaves and a delicate, feathery appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in umbels, typical of the Apiaceae family. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas.
