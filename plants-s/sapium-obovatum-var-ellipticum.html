Sapium obovatum var. ellipticum

Sapium obovatum var. ellipticum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf Chinese lantern plant, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features elliptic to obovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy on the upper surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by red, inflated seed pods that resemble lanterns, giving it its common name. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

The species is often cultivated for its ornamental seed pods and is relatively low maintenance.
