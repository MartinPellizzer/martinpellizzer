Sedum spathulifolium var. spathulifolium

Sedum spathulifolium var. spathulifolium, commonly known as the broad-leaf stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features fleshy, broad leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water storage. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its hardy nature allows it to survive in a variety of climates, including arid and coastal environments.
