Sulcorebutia Vasqueziana Subsp. Alba

Sulcorebutia vasqueziana subsp. alba is a rare and striking cactus native to the arid regions of northern Chile. This succulent is known for its unique white-pink coloration and compact, globular shape. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice among experienced growers. The subspecies alba is particularly prized for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation. Its delicate beauty and resilience make it a standout in the world of desert flora.
