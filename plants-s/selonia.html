Selonia

Selonia, commonly known as the blue starflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Boraginaceae and is characterized by its striking blue or purple star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or well-drained soils and thrives in temperate climates. Its foliage consists of hairy, lance-shaped leaves that help it retain moisture in arid environments.

Selonia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
