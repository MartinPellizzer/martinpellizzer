Statice brasiliensis var. antarctica

Statice brasiliensis var. antarctica, commonly known as the Antarctic statice, is a flowering plant native to the southern regions of South America.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. This variety thrives in coastal environments and is adapted to withstand harsh, saline conditions. The plant is often found in sandy or rocky soils along the shoreline.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners in coastal areas.
