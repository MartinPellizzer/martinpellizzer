Schoenus Bifidus

Schoenus bifidus is a unique grass species known for its distinctive bifid leaves. It thrives in wetland environments, often found in marshes and along water edges. This plant plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and supporting local ecosystems. Its delicate structure makes it an interesting subject for botanical study. Despite its modest appearance, Schoenus bifidus has significant ecological value.
