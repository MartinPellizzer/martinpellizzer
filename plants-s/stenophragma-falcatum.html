Stenophragma falcatum

Stenophragma falcatum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed piper, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Piperaceae, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and small, greenish-white flowers. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.
