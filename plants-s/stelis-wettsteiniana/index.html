Stelis Wettsteiniana

Stelis wettsteiniana is a rare orchid species native to the cloud forests of Central America. This delicate plant is known for its striking white and green floral structure. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with high altitude. Due to habitat loss, it faces significant conservation challenges. Its unique beauty makes it a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
