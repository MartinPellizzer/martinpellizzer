Sisymbrium macrorrhizum var. meyenii

Sisymbrium macrorrhizum var. meyenii, commonly known as the meyenii variant of the broadroot mustard, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has compound leaves with a serrated edge and produces pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields and along roadsides.
