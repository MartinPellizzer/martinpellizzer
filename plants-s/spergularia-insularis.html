Spergularia insularis

Spergularia insularis, commonly known as the island sandwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae.

It is a small, herbaceous annual plant characterized by its delicate, linear leaves and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. Its presence is an indicator of stable, sandy coastal environments with minimal vegetation cover.
