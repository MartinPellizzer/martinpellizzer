Sloanea chingiana var. integrifolia

Sloanea chingiana var. integrifolia, commonly known as the Chinese sloanea, is a shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its simple, entire leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its bark is smooth and grayish, with a fragrant scent.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments in mountainous regions.
