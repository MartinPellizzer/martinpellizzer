Sphaeropteris pulcherrima

Sphaeropteris pulcherrima, commonly known as the golden tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant has a robust rhizome system that helps it thrive in humid, tropical environments. Its fronds are typically bright green and can grow up to several meters in length.

Sphaeropteris pulcherrima is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
