Stravadium macrophyllum

Stravadium macrophyllum, commonly known as the giant leaf fern, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its large, broad, and leathery fronds.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to the humid forests of Southeast Asia. The plant's fronds can grow up to three meters in length, making it a dramatic addition to any botanical garden or greenhouse. It thrives in high humidity and indirect light, requiring well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for both ornamental and horticultural purposes.
