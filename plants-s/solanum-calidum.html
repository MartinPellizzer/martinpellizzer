Solanum calidum

Solanum calidum, commonly known as the hot tomato, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant produces small, yellowish-green fruits that are edible when ripe. Its leaves are typically ovate with a rough texture, and it thrives in warm, humid climates.

Solanum calidum is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its potential culinary uses.
