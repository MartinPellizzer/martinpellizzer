Specklinia githaginea

Specklinia githaginea, commonly known as the Githagine orchid, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that grows on trees and rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers with intricate patterns of white and pink. Its leaves are typically narrow and arranged in a rosette shape.

Specklinia githaginea is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in tropical greenhouses.
