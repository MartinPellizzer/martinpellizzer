Sceletium dejagerae

Sceletium dejagerae, commonly known as the "Kaffir lime" or "sceletium," is a member of the Aizoaceae family, native to South Africa.

It is a small, succulent shrub characterized by its fleshy, aromatic leaves and yellow-orange flowers. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, exhibiting adaptations such as reduced leaf surface area to minimize water loss. Its unique morphology and chemical composition have made it a subject of interest in botanical and pharmacological research.

Sceletium dejagerae is also valued for its traditional uses in herbal medicine.
