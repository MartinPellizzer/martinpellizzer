Scabiosa leptopoda

Scabiosa leptopoda, commonly known as the slender scabious, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features upright, branched stems that are covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers that bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow, lance-shaped, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Scabiosa leptopoda is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
