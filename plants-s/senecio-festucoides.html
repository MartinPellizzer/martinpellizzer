Senecio festucoides

Senecio festucoides, commonly known as the silverleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
