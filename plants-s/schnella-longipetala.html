Schnella longipetala

Schnella longipetala, commonly known as the long-petioled waterplantain, is a aquatic herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It typically grows in slow-moving freshwater environments such as ponds and marshes. The plant features narrow, ribbon-like leaves that are often submerged or floating. Its flowers are small and clustered, with three sepals and three petals, typically in shades of white or pale pink.

Schnella longipetala plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
