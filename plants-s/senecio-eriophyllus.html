Senecio eriophyllus

Senecio eriophyllus, commonly known as the silverleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Senecio eriophyllus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and drought tolerance.
