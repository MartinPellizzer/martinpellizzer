Sedum formosanum subsp. miyakojimense

Sedum formosanum subsp.

miyakojimense, commonly known as the Miyakojima sedum, is a succulent plant native to Japan. It features fleshy, upright stems and small, clustered leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tint.

The plant produces clusters of star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to rocky and sunny environments, thriving in well-drained soil.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
