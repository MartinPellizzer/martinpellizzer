Simirestis luteoviridis

Simirestis luteoviridis, commonly known as the yellow-green fern, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that display a unique combination of yellow and green hues. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests. Its leaves are typically narrow and elongated, with a soft texture and a subtle, fern-like appearance.

The botanical features of Simirestis luteoviridis make it a visually striking addition to botanical gardens and a subject of interest for plant enthusiasts.
