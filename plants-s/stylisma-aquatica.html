Stylisma aquatica

Stylisma aquatica, commonly known as water starwort, is a small aquatic plant found in freshwater environments.

It typically grows in shallow, slow-moving waters such as ponds and streams. The plant has delicate, branching stems with small, opposite leaves that are often submerged. Its flowers are tiny and clustered, producing a distinctive star-like appearance.

Stylisma aquatica plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for microorganisms and invertebrates.
