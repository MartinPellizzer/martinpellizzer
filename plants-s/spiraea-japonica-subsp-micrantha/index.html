Spiraea Japonica Subsp. Micrantha

Spiraea japonica subsp. micrantha is a compact, deciduous shrub known for its delicate, clusters of white flowers. Native to eastern Asia, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade. This species is prized for its ornamental value and easy maintenance in garden settings. Its small leaves and fragrant blooms make it a favorite among horticulturists. It is also valued for its role in ecological restoration projects.
