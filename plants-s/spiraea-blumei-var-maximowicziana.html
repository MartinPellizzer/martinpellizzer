Spiraea blumei var. maximowicziana

Spiraea blumei var. maximowicziana, commonly known as the Korean weeping spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features arching stems with small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has alternate, ovate leaves that are often tinged with red or purple in the fall. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its compact size and graceful appearance make it ideal for borders or as a specimen plant.
