Syringa emodi var. elegantissima

Syringa emodi var. elegantissima, commonly known as the Himalayan lilac, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Nepal and northern India.

It features fragrant, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath, providing an attractive contrast. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a suitable choice for gardens in temperate climates.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its showy flowers and compact growth habit.
