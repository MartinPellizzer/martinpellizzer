Sicyos debilis

Sicyos debilis, commonly known as the weak cucumber or creeping cucumber, is a herbaceous vine native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the family Cucurbitaceae, which includes other cucurbits like pumpkins and squash. The plant produces small, green, edible fruits that are often used in traditional medicine.
