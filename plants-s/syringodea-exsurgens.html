Syringodea exsurgens

Syringodea exsurgens, commonly known as the climbing orchid, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, with a distinctive spurred lip. The plant has a climbing habit, using its tendrils to ascend supports in its natural habitat. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a waxy texture.

Syringodea exsurgens is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm, humid environments.
