Senecio × oyensis

Senecio × oyensis, commonly known as the hybrid groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows as a low-growing shrub with hairy, gray-green foliage. Its flowers are yellow and bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Senecio × oyensis is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
