Solanum plousianthemum var. commixtum

Solanum plousianthemum var. commixtum, commonly known as the hybrid nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically purple or violet in color. The plant has simple, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.

However, like many members of its family, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
