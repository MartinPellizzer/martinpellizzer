Selinum Sisarum

Selinum sisarum is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It is known for its small, pale yellow blooms that appear in late spring. This plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas. Due to its limited habitat and slow growth, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique species for future generations.
