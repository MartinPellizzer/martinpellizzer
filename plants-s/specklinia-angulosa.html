Specklinia angulosa

Specklinia angulosa, commonly known as the angular speckled orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including countries like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with dark speckles, giving it its common name. It grows as an epiphyte, often found on trees in humid, shaded environments.

Specklinia angulosa is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
