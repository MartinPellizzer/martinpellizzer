Statice montana

Statice montana, commonly known as sea lavender, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, showy blue or purple flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy perennial suitable for coastal and arid environments. Its foliage is usually gray-green, with narrow, linear leaves that help reduce water loss.

Statice montana is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and its ability to attract pollinators.
