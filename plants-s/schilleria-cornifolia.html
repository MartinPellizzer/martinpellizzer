Schilleria cornifolia

Schilleria cornifolia, commonly known as the horned dandelion, is a striking plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, horn-like bracts that surround the flower head, giving it its common name. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. Schilleria cornifolia is a herbaceous perennial that thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

Its unique appearance and seasonal blooming make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
