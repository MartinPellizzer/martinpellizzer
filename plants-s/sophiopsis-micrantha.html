Sophiopsis micrantha

Sophiopsis micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered sophia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid regions. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a delicate appearance. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Sophiopsis micrantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
