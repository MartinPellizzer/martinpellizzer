Sesleria feretrana subsp. mariculensis

Sesleria feretrana subsp.

mariculensis, commonly known as the Mariculensis bent grass, is a perennial grass species native to South Africa. It belongs to the family Poaceae and is characterized by its compact, tufted growth habit.

The plant typically features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
