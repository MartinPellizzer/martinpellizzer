Senecio subdiscoideus

Senecio subdiscoideus, commonly known as the silverleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and daisy-like appearance. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs, giving it a silvery appearance.

It produces small yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.
