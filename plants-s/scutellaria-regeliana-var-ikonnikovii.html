Scutellaria regeliana var. ikonnikovii

Scutellaria regeliana var. ikonnikovii, commonly known as the Ikonnikov's blue skullcap, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular blue or violet flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after plant in specialty gardens.
