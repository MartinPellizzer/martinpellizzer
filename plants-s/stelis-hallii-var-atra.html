Stelis hallii var. atra

Stelis hallii var. atra, commonly known as the black stelis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dark, velvety foliage and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of purple and violet. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat requirements, it is a sought-after specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
