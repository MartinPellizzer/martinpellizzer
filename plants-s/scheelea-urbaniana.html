Scheelea urbaniana

Scheelea urbaniana, commonly known as the urbaniana palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to parts of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. Scheelea urbaniana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to grow in well-drained soil with ample sunlight.
