Saxifraga geranioides f. obovata

Saxifraga geranioides f. obovata, commonly known as the broad-leafed saxifrage, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to rocky and alpine regions.

It features a low-growing habit with fleshy, succulent leaves that are obovate in shape, often with a waxy coating. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine displays.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact form and vibrant floral display.
