Saxifraga oreophila var. dapaoshanensis

It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in rocky, well-drained soils at high altitudes. The plant features small, clustered leaves and produces delicate white or pinkish flowers during the summer months. It is adapted to cold and humid environments, often found in alpine meadows.
