Saussurea nipponica subsp. yakusimensis

Saussurea nipponica subsp.

yakusimensis, commonly known as the Yakushima saussurea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely leafed stem and large, showy composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments such as mountain forests. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to white, with prominent central disc florets.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often found in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
