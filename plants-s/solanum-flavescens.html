Solanum flavescens

Solanum flavescens, commonly known as the yellow nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, with hairy stems and alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. Its flowers are yellow, and it produces small, green berries that turn red when mature.

The plant is characterized by its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
