Scrophularia silaifolia

Scrophularia silaifolia, commonly known as the Silaifolia figwort, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or alpine environments. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

Scrophularia silaifolia is part of the Scrophulariaceae family, which includes many species with medicinal and ornamental value.
