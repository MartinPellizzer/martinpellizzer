Swainsona greyana

Swainsona greyana, commonly known as the Grey's swainsona, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, also known as the legume or pea family, characterized by its distinctive pod fruits. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces showy, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Swainsona greyana is known for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in full sun.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and nitrogen-fixing capabilities.
