Syzygium toddalioides

Syzygium toddalioides, commonly known as the white-flowered tamarind.

It is a flowering plant species native to tropical regions of Asia and Australia. The plant is characterized by its small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a glossy appearance.
