Smithia geminiflora var. conferta

Smithia geminiflora var. conferta, commonly known as the dense-flowered smithia, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the alpine regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are densely packed, giving it a mat-like appearance. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or mountainous terrain.
