Stelis ophioceps

Stelis ophioceps, commonly known as the Ophioceps Epidendrum, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, branching growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often arranged in racemes. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Stelis ophioceps is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
