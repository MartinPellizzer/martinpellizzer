Senna hayesiana

Senna hayesiana, commonly known as the yellow senna, is a shrub or small tree native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, flat pods that contain seeds, which are often dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Senna hayesiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
