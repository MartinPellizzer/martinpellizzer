Strelitzia alba subsp. nicolai

Strelitzia alba subsp.

nicolai, commonly known as the White Bird of Paradise, is a striking tropical plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its large, white, bird-of-paradise-like flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant has broad, sword-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. It prefers full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for warm climates.

This subspecies is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
