Solanum chilliasense

Solanum chilliasense, commonly known as the Chillia's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and peppers. The plant features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically purple or violet in color. Its leaves are broad and lobed, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Solanum chilliasense is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
