Sciodaphyllum archeri

Sciodaphyllum archeri, commonly known as the Kauai ʻōhiʻa.

It is a rare and endemic shrub found exclusively on the island of Kauai in Hawaii. The plant is characterized by its small, leathery leaves and clusters of pale yellow or greenish-white flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically in the understory of native forests.

Due to habitat loss and invasive species, Sciodaphyllum archeri is considered critically endangered.
