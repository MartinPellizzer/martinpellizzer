Salacia obovatilimba

Salacia obovatilimba, commonly known as the broad-leaf salacia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Salaceae and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in the study of tropical flora and medicinal plants.
