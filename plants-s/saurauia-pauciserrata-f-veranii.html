Saurauia pauciserrata f. veranii

Saurauia pauciserrata f. veranii, commonly known as the Variegated Saurauia, is a unique variant of the Saurauia pauciserrata species.

It is a shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its striking variegated leaves that display a combination of green, white, and yellow hues. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value.
