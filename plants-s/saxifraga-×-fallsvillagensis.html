Saxifraga × fallsvillagensis

Saxifraga × fallsvillagensis, commonly known as the Fallsvillen saxifrage, is a hybrid plant native to the northeastern United States.

It is a low-growing perennial with succulent, fleshy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a hardy, ornamental ground cover.
