Specklinia transparens

Specklinia transparens, commonly known as the transparent orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, translucent leaves that allow light to pass through, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, typically blooming during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled environments.
