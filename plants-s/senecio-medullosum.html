Senecio medullosum

Senecio medullosum, commonly known as the woolly senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, white, woolly covering on its stems and leaves, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, typically during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and prefers full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
