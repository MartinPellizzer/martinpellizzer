Stelis pycnochila

Stelis pycnochila, commonly known as the wrinkled orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant has a slender, erect stem and produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, and the flowers have a delicate, fragrant aroma.

Stelis pycnochila is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
