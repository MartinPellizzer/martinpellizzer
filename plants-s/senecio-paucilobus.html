Senecio paucilobus

Senecio paucilobus, commonly known as the few-lobed groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are usually narrow and linear, with a rough texture.

Senecio paucilobus is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
