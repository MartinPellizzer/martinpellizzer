Stellaria roughii

Stellaria roughii, commonly known as mouse-ear chickweed, is a low-growing flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, white flowers with five petals and hairy, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, stiff hairs. The plant typically thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds.

Stellaria roughii is a perennial herb that is sometimes cultivated as a ornamental plant due to its delicate appearance.
