Stellaria sachalinensis

Stellaria sachalinensis, commonly known as the Sakhalin starwort, is a flowering plant native to the Far East, including parts of Russia and China.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, star-shaped white flowers and slender stems. The plant typically grows in moist, temperate environments such as wet meadows and riverbanks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged oppositely along the stem, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Stellaria sachalinensis is often found in regions with cool, humid climates and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
