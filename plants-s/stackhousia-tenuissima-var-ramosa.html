Stackhousia tenuissima var. ramosa

Stackhousia tenuissima var. ramosa, commonly known as the slender stackhousia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows in coastal or rocky habitats, preferring well-drained soils. It is often found in the fynbos vegetation type, which is known for its rich biodiversity.

The varietal form, ramosa, distinguishes itself by its more branched growth habit compared to the typical variety.
