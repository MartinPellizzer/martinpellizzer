Satureja pulegium

Satureja pulegium, commonly known as pennyroyal, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes other aromatic plants like mint and basil. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant leaves and clusters of pink or white flowers. It is often grown for its medicinal properties and use in traditional remedies.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
