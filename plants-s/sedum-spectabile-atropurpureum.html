Sedum spectabile atropurpureum

Sedum spectabile atropurpureum, commonly known as the showy sedum, is a perennial succulent native to North America.

It features upright, branching stems that produce dense clusters of star-shaped flowers in shades of pink to purple. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in well-drained soils and full sun. It is often used in rock gardens or as a border plant due to its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.

The species is known for its ability to bloom from late summer into early fall.
