Sarothra drummondii

Sarothra drummondii, commonly known as Drummond's sage, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, pale blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
