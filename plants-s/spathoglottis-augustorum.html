Spathoglottis augustorum

Spathoglottis augustorum, commonly known as the August orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its striking floral display. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

The flowers are pollinated primarily by bees and other insects, contributing to its ecological significance.
