Stereocaryum watsonianum

Stereocaryum watsonianum, commonly known as the African snowball, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of white flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and glossy, with a leathery texture.

The botanical features of S. watsonianum make it a popular ornamental plant in warm climates.
