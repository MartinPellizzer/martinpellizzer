Sarracenia × willmottiae

Sarracenia × willmottiae, commonly known as the Willmott's pitcher plant, is a hybrid carnivorous plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its elongated, tubular leaves that form deep, colorful pitchers to trap and digest insects. The plant's pitchers are typically red or orange with intricate patterns, which help attract prey. It thrives in wet, acidic soils and requires bright light for optimal growth.

Sarracenia × willmottiae is a popular choice for carnivorous plant enthusiasts due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
