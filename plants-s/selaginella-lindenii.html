Selaginella lindenii

Selaginella lindenii, commonly known as the ladder brake, is a species of spore-bearing plant belonging to the family Selaginellaceae.

It is a small, evergreen plant that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rock crevices. The plant has a distinctive appearance with its segmented, scale-like leaves that form a dense, mat-like structure. It is known for its ability to survive in low-light conditions and is often found in temperate regions.

Selaginella lindenii plays a role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for various small organisms.
