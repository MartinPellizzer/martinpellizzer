Sisymbrium reboudianum

Sisymbrium reboudianum, commonly known as the French rocket or wild radish, is an annual flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbage and mustard. The plant features basal leaves that are lobed or pinnately divided, with a distinctive peppery aroma. Its yellow flowers are arranged in racemes and bloom from spring to early summer.

The plant produces small, round pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
