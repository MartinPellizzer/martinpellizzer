Shorea flemmichii

Shorea flemmichii, commonly known as the Bornean ironwood, is a tropical tree native to the islands of Borneo.

It belongs to the family Dipterocarpaceae and is characterized by its heavy, durable wood that is highly valued for its strength and resistance to decay. The tree can grow up to 50 meters tall and has a straight trunk with a dense, buttressed root system. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptical, with a leathery texture and dark green color.

Shorea flemmichii plays an important ecological role in its native rainforests, providing habitat and contributing to biodiversity.
