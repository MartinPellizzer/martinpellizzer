Swertia curtioides

Swertia curtioides, commonly known as Himalayan gentian, is a perennial herb native to the alpine regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows at high altitudes, where it thrives in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged oppositely along the stem.

Swertia curtioides is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
