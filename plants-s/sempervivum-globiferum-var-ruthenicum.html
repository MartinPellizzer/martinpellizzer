Sempervivum globiferum var. ruthenicum

Sempervivum globiferum var. ruthenicum, commonly known as the Ruthenic Rosette, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features a compact, rosette-shaped growth pattern with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the summer months. It is well adapted to rocky and arid environments, thriving in full sun and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and hardiness in cold climates.
