Scrophularia buergeriana var. buergeriana

Scrophularia buergeriana var. buergeriana, commonly known as Buerger's scrophularia, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and streambanks. The plant features upright stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are tubular and usually pink or purple, arranged in spikes.

It is valued in traditional medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
