Stylidium yilgarnense

Stylidium yilgarnense, commonly known as the Yilgarn Sun-orchid, is a striking member of the orchid family native to Western Australia.

It features a slender, erect stem with a single, showy yellow or orange flower that resembles a sunflower. The plant has a short, fleshy root system adapted to survive in arid conditions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in fine hairs to reduce water loss.

Stylidium yilgarnense is typically found in rocky outcrops and sandy soils, thriving in full sun.
