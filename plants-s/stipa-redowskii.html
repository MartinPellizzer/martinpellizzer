Stipa redowskii

Stipa redowskii, commonly known as Redowski's feathergrass, is a grass species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth and fine, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to cold and arid conditions. Its inflorescence consists of narrow, spike-like clusters of small, pale florets.

Stipa redowskii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate, wind-swaying foliage.
