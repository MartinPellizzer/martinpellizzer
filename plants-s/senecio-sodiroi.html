Senecio sodiroi

Senecio sodiroi, commonly known as the Sodiro's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single bloom. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. It produces small, yellowish to white flowers that are often clustered in dense inflorescences.

Senecio sodiroi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
