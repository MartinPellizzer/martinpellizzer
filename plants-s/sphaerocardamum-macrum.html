Sphaerocardamum macrum

Sphaerocardamum macrum, commonly known as the giant sphaerocardamum, is a striking plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its unique morphology makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

However, it requires specific humidity and light conditions to thrive.
