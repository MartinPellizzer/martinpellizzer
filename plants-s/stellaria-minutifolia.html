Stellaria minutifolia

Stellaria minutifolia, commonly known as mouse ear chickweed, is a small annual herb native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It features tiny, star-shaped white flowers and is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, gardens, and along pathways. Its leaves are rounded and succulent, with a distinctive, soft texture.

Stellaria minutifolia is often used in traditional medicine and is also a common weed in cultivated areas.
