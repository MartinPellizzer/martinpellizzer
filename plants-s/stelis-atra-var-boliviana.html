Stelis atra var. boliviana

Stelis atra var. boliviana, commonly known as the Black Orchid of Bolivia, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andean regions of Bolivia.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black, inflorescence and velvety, dark green leaves. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in cloud forests or high-altitude grasslands. Its flowers are small and clustered, with a unique combination of dark purple and deep brown hues.

This variety is valued for its striking appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
