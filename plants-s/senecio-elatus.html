Senecio elatus

Senecio elatus, commonly known as the African Daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers and linear, grass-like leaves. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and thistles. Senecio elatus thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ability to produce a large number of small flowers makes it an attractive and easy-to-grow plant for gardeners.
