Sclerostemma

Sclerostemma, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family.

These plants are characterized by their unique, often showy flowers that are adapted to thrive in rocky or arid environments. Sclerostemma species typically have thick, fleshy leaves that help them conserve water in dry conditions.
