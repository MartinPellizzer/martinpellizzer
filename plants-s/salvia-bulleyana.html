Salvia bulleyana

Salvia bulleyana, commonly known as Chinese sage, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces striking violet to blue flowers in whorled clusters, typically blooming in summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a soft texture.

Salvia bulleyana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well-suited for gardens with well-drained soil.
