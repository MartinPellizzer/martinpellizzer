Sotoa

Sotoa, commonly known as the "tree of life," is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, straight trunk and dense canopy of broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to yellow, followed by edible, orange-colored fruits. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The sotoa tree is also valued for its durable wood and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
