Senecio malaisei

Senecio malaisei, commonly known as the Malaise's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils. It produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a cluster, and its leaves are usually narrow and succulent.

Senecio malaisei is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
