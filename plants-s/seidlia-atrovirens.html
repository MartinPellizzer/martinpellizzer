Seidlia atrovirens

Seidlia atrovirens, commonly known as the dark green orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the temperate regions of Europe.

It features slender, upright stems that bear lance-shaped leaves with a dark green hue. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of green and white, typically blooming in late spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Seidlia atrovirens is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
