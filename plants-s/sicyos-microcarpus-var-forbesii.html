Sicyos microcarpus var. forbesii

Sicyos microcarpus var. forbesii, commonly known as the Forbes' bottle gourd, is a variant of the bottle gourd plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a climbing herbaceous vine that produces large, elongated fruits with a smooth, thick rind. The plant has palmate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in warm climates with well-drained soil and requires full sun for optimal growth.

The fruit is often used in culinary and traditional medicinal applications due to its versatile properties.
