Sisymbrium officinale var. parvifolium

Sisymbrium officinale var. parvifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved mustard, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is found in various regions across Europe and Asia. It produces small, round pods containing several seeds, which are often dispersed by wind.

This variety is known for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
