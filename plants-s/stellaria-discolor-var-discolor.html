Stellaria discolor var. discolor

Stellaria discolor var. discolor, commonly known as white sandplant, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia and the Pacific Islands.

It typically features small, white or pale blue flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the spring and summer months. The plant has slender, branching stems and small, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or sandy environments.

Its botanical name reflects its disc-like appearance and its distinct coloration.
