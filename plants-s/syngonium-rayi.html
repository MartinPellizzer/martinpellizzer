Syngonium rayi

Syngonium rayi, commonly known as the arrowhead vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped to arrowhead-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or cream markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by red berries. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Syngonium rayi is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
