Silene arguta var. sisianica

Silene arguta var. sisianica, commonly known as the Sisian pink, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive pink to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous areas, preferring well-drained soils. It has a compact growth habit with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves.
