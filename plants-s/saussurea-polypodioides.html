Saussurea polypodioides

Saussurea polypodioides, commonly known as the Himalayan swamp gentian, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas, where it thrives in moist, cool environments. The plant features a slender, herbaceous stem with alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its showy, blue or violet flowers bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators.
