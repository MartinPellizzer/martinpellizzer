Sacciolepis striata

Sacciolepis striata, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, ribbed leaves that are typically green with prominent white or yellow stripes. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
