Silene fetleri

Silene fetleri, commonly known as the Turkish catchfly, is a flowering plant native to Turkey and parts of the Balkans.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pinkish-purple flowers that bloom in late summer. It is often found in open habitats such as meadows and forest edges.
