Schefflera porphyranthera

Schefflera porphyranthera, commonly known as the Porphyranthera umbrella plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of large, leathery, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by bright red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Schefflera porphyranthera is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor conditions.
