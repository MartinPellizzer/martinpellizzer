Solanum microdontum subsp. gigantophyllum

Solanum microdontum subsp.

gigantophyllum, commonly known as the giantleaf nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the tropical regions of South America. It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length.

The plant produces clusters of small, white or yellowish flowers followed by berry-like fruits that are typically yellow or orange when ripe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
