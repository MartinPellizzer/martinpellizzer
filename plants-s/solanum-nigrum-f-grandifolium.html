Solanum nigrum f. grandifolium

Solanum nigrum f. grandifolium, commonly known as the large-leaved black nightshade, is a variant of the common black nightshade.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its large, dark green, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into black, spherical berries. It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like tomatoes and potatoes.

This variety is often found in disturbed soils and thrives in temperate climates.
