Spiraea × gieseleriana

Spiraea × gieseleriana, commonly known as the weeping spiraea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its cascading clusters of white or pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has a compact, rounded shape with fine, dark green foliage that turns yellow in the fall. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.

Spiraea × gieseleriana is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
