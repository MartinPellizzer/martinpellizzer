Scutellaria tortumensis

Scutellaria tortumensis, commonly known as the Tortum skullcap, is a perennial herb native to specific regions of Turkey.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color, arranged in whorls along the stem. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a rough texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Scutellaria tortumensis is often found in rocky or dry habitats, reflecting its adaptability to arid environments.
