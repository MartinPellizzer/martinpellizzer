Sheadendron pisoniiflorum var. macrostachyum

Sheadendron pisoniiflorum var. macrostachyum, commonly known as the giant flame tree, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its large, showy inflorescences.

It produces vibrant red or orange flowers that are clustered in long, pendulous spikes, making it a visually dramatic specimen. The plant is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil. Its foliage consists of large, glossy, dark green leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its flamboyant blooms.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and public spaces due to its striking appearance and seasonal flowering.
