Scorzoneroides carpetana

Scorzoneroides carpetana, commonly known as the carpet anemone, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, white or pale pink flowers in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a hardy and ornamental species suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
