Stevia diversipapposa f. longiaristata

Stevia diversipapposa f. longiaristata, commonly known as the longistyle stevia, is a variant of the stevia plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, slender bracts and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant is a perennial herb that thrives in temperate climates and is often found in dry, sunny environments. Its leaves are used in traditional medicine for their sweet-tasting compounds, similar to other stevia species.

This form is particularly noted for its unique floral structure and potential medicinal properties.
