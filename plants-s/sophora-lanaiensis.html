Sophora lanaiensis

Sophora lanaiensis, commonly known as Lanai Sophora, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant is typically found in dry, coastal habitats and is adapted to the unique environmental conditions of its native range. It plays a role in local ecosystems and has been the subject of conservation efforts due to its limited distribution.

The species is also of interest for its potential ecological and botanical significance in tropical regions.
