Syzygium euonymifolium

Syzygium euonymifolium, commonly known as the flame tree or Australian flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fiery red flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and often have a glossy texture. Syzygium euonymifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often planted in gardens and urban landscapes for its vibrant floral display.

It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
