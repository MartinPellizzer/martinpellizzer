Stelis vagans

Stelis vagans, commonly known as the wandering orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale green in color. It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia and is often found on tree trunks or branches.

Its unique growth habit and delicate appearance make it a popular specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
