Stellaria pentagyna var. arenaria

Stellaria pentagyna var. arenaria, commonly known as sand starwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to sandy and coastal environments.

It features small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to August. The plant has thin, branching stems and narrow, linear leaves that are adapted to withstand harsh, windy conditions. It is often found in dunes, gravelly soils, and other arid habitats.

Its ecological importance includes stabilizing sandy soils and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
