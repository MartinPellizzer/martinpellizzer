Scytalia informis

Scytalia informis, commonly known as the slender false nettle, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Urticaceae family, which includes stinging nettles, and typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features slender, branching stems with opposite, ovate leaves that have a soft, hairy texture. Its small, greenish-white flowers are arranged in clusters and are pollinated by insects.

Scytalia informis is not known for producing a stinging reaction, unlike its close relatives.
