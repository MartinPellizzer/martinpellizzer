Satureja rotundifolia var. micrantha

Satureja rotundifolia var. micrantha, commonly known as roundleaf savory, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of the Mediterranean.

It features small, rounded leaves that are often aromatic and used in traditional cooking. The plant produces tiny, pale blue or purple flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

The essential oils found in its leaves contribute to its distinctive scent and potential medicinal properties.
