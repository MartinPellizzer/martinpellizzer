Solanum jelskii

Solanum jelskii, commonly known as the Jelskii nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale purple in color. Its fruit is a fleshy berry that contains several seeds.

Solanum jelskii is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
