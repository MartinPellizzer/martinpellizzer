Syzygium okudai

Syzygium okudai, commonly known as the Okudai apple or mountain apple, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in the Philippines and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and produces small, fragrant white or pink flowers. Its fruit is a round, edible berry with a sweet, tart flavor, often used in local cuisines.

Syzygium okudai is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its medicinal properties.
