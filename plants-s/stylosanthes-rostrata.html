Stylosanthes rostrata

Stylosanthes rostrata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf stylo, is a perennial legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is known for its deep root system, which helps it thrive in poor soil conditions. It is widely used as a forage crop due to its high nutritional value and drought tolerance.

Additionally, it plays a role in soil nitrogen fixation, making it beneficial for agricultural systems.
