Sempervivum Saundersii

Sempervivum saundersii is a hardy succulent known for its striking rosettes of fleshy leaves. It thrives in rocky and alpine environments, often found in mountainous regions across Europe. This plant is also called the "Hens and Chicks" due to its ability to produce offsets. Its vibrant colors range from green to red and purple, making it a popular choice for rock gardens. It requires minimal water and is ideal for gardeners seeking low-maintenance, resilient plants.
