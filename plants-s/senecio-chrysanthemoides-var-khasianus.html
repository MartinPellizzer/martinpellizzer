Senecio chrysanthemoides var. khasianus

Senecio chrysanthemoides var. khasianus, commonly known as the Khasi Senecio, is a flowering plant native to the hill regions of northeastern India.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties.
