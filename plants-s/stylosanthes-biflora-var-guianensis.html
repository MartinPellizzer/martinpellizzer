Stylosanthes biflora var. guianensis

Stylosanthes biflora var. guianensis, commonly known as the Guianan stylosanthes, is a leguminous plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its small, trifoliate leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is typically found in tropical and subtropical regions, often in disturbed soils or along roadsides. It has a deep root system that helps it thrive in nutrient-poor environments.

This species is also valued for its potential in soil stabilization and as a forage crop.
