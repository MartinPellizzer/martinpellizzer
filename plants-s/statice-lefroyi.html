Statice lefroyi

Statice lefroyi, commonly known as the desert statice, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, spiky flowers. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, colorful bracts that resemble flowers. It is well adapted to harsh environments, with deep roots and a high tolerance for drought.

Statice lefroyi is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental value.
