Sonchus ecklonianus

Sonchus ecklonianus, commonly known as the Ecklon's sowthistle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is an annual or biennial herb that typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant features alternate, toothed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces yellow flowers arranged in a composite head. It belongs to the dandelion family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its milky sap.

While it is sometimes considered a weed, it can also serve as a food source for certain insects and animals.
