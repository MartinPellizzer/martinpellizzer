Senecio sipoccruncus

Senecio sipoccruncus, commonly known as the Cape snowflake, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb with a distinctive rosette of silver-green, fleshy leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers during the winter months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and cold-hardiness make it a striking addition to alpine and Mediterranean-style gardens.
