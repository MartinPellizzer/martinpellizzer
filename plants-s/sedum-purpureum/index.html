Sedum Purpureum

Sedum purpureum is a striking succulent native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa. It is known for its vibrant purple foliage and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers. This plant thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Its ability to store water in its thick leaves allows it to survive in arid conditions. Sedum purpureum is not only ornamental but also has historical uses in traditional medicine.
