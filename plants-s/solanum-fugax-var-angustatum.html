Solanum fugax var. angustatum

Solanum fugax var. angustatum, commonly known as the narrowleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, angular stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale green flowers that bloom in summer. Its fruits are typically berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

This species is native to parts of southern Africa and is often found in open, disturbed habitats.
