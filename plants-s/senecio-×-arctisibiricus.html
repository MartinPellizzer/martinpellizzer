Senecio × arctisibiricus

Senecio × arctisibiricus, commonly known as the Arctic Senecio, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and narrow, grass-like leaves. Native to the Arctic and subarctic regions, it thrives in cold, high-altitude environments. The plant is often found in alpine tundra and rocky habitats, where it adapts to harsh climatic conditions.

Its botanical features make it an important component of polar ecosystems.
