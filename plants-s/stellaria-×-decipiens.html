Stellaria × decipiens

Stellaria × decipiens, commonly known as the hybrid starwort, is a perennial plant that belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit and produces small, white or pinkish flowers that resemble stars, hence its common name. The plant typically thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and disturbed sites. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture, while its stems are prostrate or ascending.

Stellaria × decipiens is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in various habitats.
