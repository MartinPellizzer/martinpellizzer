Stelis thymochila

Stelis thymochila, commonly known as the violet orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial epiphyte, typically found growing on trees in humid tropical environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually small, fragrant, and come in shades of white, pink, and purple.

Stelis thymochila is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
