Sedum transvalense

Sedum transvalense, commonly known as the Transvaal sedum, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Its leaves are typically blue-gray, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant flowers.

Sedum transvalense is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
