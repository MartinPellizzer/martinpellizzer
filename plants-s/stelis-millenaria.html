Stelis millenaria

Stelis millenaria, commonly known as the millenaria orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a slender, upright stem with several leaves arranged in a rosette pattern. Its flowers are small, white, and clustered in a spike, often with a faintly fragrant aroma.

Stelis millenaria is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
