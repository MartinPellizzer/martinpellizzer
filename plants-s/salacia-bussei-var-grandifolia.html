Salacia bussei var. grandifolia

Salacia bussei var. grandifolia, commonly known as the giant salacia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruit is a fleshy, yellowish berry that contains numerous seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in humid, shaded environments.
