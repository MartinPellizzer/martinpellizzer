Senecio brachyglossus var. major

Senecio brachyglossus var. major, commonly known as the giant ice plant, is a succulent perennial native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, which store water and aid in drought survival. The plant produces yellow flowers in summer, which are clustered in dense, branched inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
