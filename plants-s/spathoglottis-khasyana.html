Spathoglottis khasyana

Spathoglottis khasyana, commonly known as the Khasi orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the northeastern regions of India.

It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that are typically pale green to yellowish in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Spathoglottis khasyana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine.
