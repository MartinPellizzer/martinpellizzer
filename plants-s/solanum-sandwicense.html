Solanum sandwicense

Solanum sandwicense, commonly known as the Hawaiian nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a hairy texture.

The flowers are usually purple or white, and the fruit is a fleshy berry that contains several seeds.
