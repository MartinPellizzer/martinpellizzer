Specklinia deltoglossa

Specklinia deltoglossa, commonly known as the velvet leaf orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, velvety leaves that are often a deep green or reddish-brown in color. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with intricate patterns on the petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil for optimal growth.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in specialized botanical gardens.
