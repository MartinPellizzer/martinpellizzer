Styrax macrophyllus

Styrax macrophyllus, commonly known as the large-leaf styrax, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that can grow up to 10 centimeters in length. The plant produces fragrant, white or pale pink flowers in clusters during the spring. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, with a distinctive aromatic scent when cut.

Styrax macrophyllus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
