Stellaria parviflora subsp. parviflora

Stellaria parviflora subsp.

parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered starwort, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It typically features slender stems and small, white or pale blue flowers arranged in clusters.

The leaves are usually narrow and lance-shaped, with a hairy texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and disturbed areas.

This species is known for its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
