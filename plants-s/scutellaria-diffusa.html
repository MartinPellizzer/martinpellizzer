Scutellaria diffusa

Scutellaria diffusa, commonly known as blue skullcap, is a perennial herb native to eastern North America.

It features square, hairy stems and opposite leaves that are lance-shaped with serrated edges. The plant produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Scutellaria diffusa is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
