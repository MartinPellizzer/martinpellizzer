Stemodia hyptoides var. stricta

Stemodia hyptoides var. stricta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved stemodia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Australia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. The plant has slender, upright stems and small, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense inflorescences, usually appearing in shades of white or pale yellow.

This variant is distinguished by its more compact growth habit compared to other varieties of Stemodia hyptoides.
