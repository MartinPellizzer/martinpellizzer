Schlegelia roseiflora

Schlegelia roseiflora, commonly known as the pink-flowered schlegelia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems covered in soft, hairy foliage. The plant produces clusters of showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early summer. Schlegelia roseiflora thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
