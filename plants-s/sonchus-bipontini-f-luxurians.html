Sonchus bipontini f. luxurians

Sonchus bipontini f. luxurians, commonly known as the luxuriant sow-thistle, is a variant of the common sow-thistle.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant features alternate, rough, hairy leaves that are typically lobed or toothed. It produces yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family.

The species is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats.
