Sedum linifolium

Sedum linifolium, commonly known as the silverleaf stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white wax, giving it a silvery appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to harsh, cold climates.

Sedum linifolium is often used in rock gardens and as a ground cover in alpine settings.
