Schefflera macrostachya

Schefflera macrostachya, commonly known as the giant umbrella tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, umbrella-like canopy of palmately compound leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clustered inflorescences. Its growth habit includes a tall, slender trunk with a dense crown of leaves.

Schefflera macrostachya is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and adaptability to various growing conditions.
