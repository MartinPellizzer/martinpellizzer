Solanum lucidum

Solanum lucidum, commonly known as the shining nightshade, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is native to Australia and is characterized by its smooth, often hairy leaves and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. The plant produces berries that are typically yellow or orange when ripe, containing seeds that are dispersed by animals. Solanum lucidum is adapted to arid environments and can thrive in sandy or rocky soils.

It is often found in coastal regions and is known for its resilience to harsh climatic conditions.
