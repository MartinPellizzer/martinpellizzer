Spinifex sericeus

Spinifex sericeus, commonly known as the silky spinifex, is a hardy, evergreen shrub native to the arid regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, wiry foliage that forms a low-growing mat, providing excellent ground cover. The plant has small, needle-like leaves covered in a fine, silvery-white coating, which helps reduce water loss in harsh environments. It produces small, pale yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a subtle floral element to its appearance.

Spinifex sericeus is highly drought-tolerant and thrives in sandy or gravelly soils, making it well-suited for xeriscaping and coastal areas.
