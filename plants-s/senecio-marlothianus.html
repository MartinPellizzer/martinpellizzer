Senecio marlothianus

Senecio marlothianus, commonly known as the Marloth's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. It is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is often found in arid and semi-arid regions.
