Streptopus amplexifolius subsp. americanus

Streptopus amplexifolius subsp.

americanus, commonly known as the American starwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America. It features a basal rosette of broad, waxy leaves that wrap around the flowering stems.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale green, arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.

Its foliage and flowers are often used in native plant gardens for their ornamental value and ecological benefits.
