Shortia × intertexta

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, blooming in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and covered in a fine, waxy coating.
