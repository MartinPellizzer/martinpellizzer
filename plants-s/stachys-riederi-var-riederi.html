Stachys riederi var. riederi

Stachys riederi var. riederi, commonly known as the Rieder's hedge nettle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features upright stems that are often hairy and bear opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, pale purple to pinkish flowers arranged in whorled clusters along the upper stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
