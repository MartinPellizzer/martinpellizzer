Schlechteranthus subglobosus

Schlechteranthus subglobosus, commonly known as the globular dandelion, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact, globe-shaped inflorescence composed of numerous small yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, sunny habitats such as grasslands and stream banks. Its leaves are basal, rosette-like, and covered in fine hairs.

Schlechteranthus subglobosus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
