Scytalis catjang

Scytalis catjang, commonly known as the catjang orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are long and strap-like, and it produces fragrant, often yellow or greenish-yellow blooms.

Scytalis catjang is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
