Spartium lanigerum

Spartium lanigerum, commonly known as blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has feathery, bipinnate leaves and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. Spartium lanigerum is often found in dry, rocky soils and can thrive in full sun.

It is sometimes used in herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
