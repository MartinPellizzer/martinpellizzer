Sphaerostigma cheiranthifolium

Sphaerostigma cheiranthifolium, commonly known as the yellow star-of-Bethlehem, is a member of the Myrtaceae family and is native to eastern Australia.

It is a low-growing shrub with distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are usually covered in a fine, white pubescence. Its flowers are typically arranged in clusters and have five petals that are fused at the base.

The plant is often found in coastal and sandy soils, and it plays a role in supporting local pollinators.
