Saurauia pauciserrata var. kegeliana

Saurauia pauciserrata var. kegeliana, commonly known as the Kegel's saurauia, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
