Schizaea pectinata

Schizaea pectinata, commonly known as the feather moss, is a species of moss that belongs to the family Schizaeaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, branching stems and small, overlapping leaves that give it a feathery appearance. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest floors and on rocky surfaces. Schizaea pectinata plays an important role in soil stabilization and moisture retention in its habitat.

Its simple yet elegant structure makes it a popular subject in botanical studies and ecological research.
