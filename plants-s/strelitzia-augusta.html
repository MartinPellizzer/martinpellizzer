Strelitzia augusta

Strelitzia augusta, commonly known as the Orange Strelitzia, is a striking member of the Strelitzia genus native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, banana-like leaves that are typically bright green with a reddish tinge along the edges. The plant produces distinctive orange and white flowers that resemble a bird in flight, making it a popular ornamental species. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, requiring moderate watering to prevent root rot.

Strelitzia augusta is relatively low maintenance and suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation in warm climates.
