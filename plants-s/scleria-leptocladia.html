Scleria leptocladia

Scleria leptocladia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bulrush, is a member of the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, linear leaves and cylindrical flower clusters. Native to parts of Africa, this plant typically grows in wetland environments such as marshes and swampy areas.
