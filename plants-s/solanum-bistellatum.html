Solanum bistellatum

Solanum bistellatum, commonly known as the starry nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale blue, which bloom in clusters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

The fruit is a yellow or orange berry that contains several seeds.
