Schefflera umbellata

Schefflera umbellata, commonly known as the umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of large, leathery, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that are typically followed by bright red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Schefflera umbellata is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
