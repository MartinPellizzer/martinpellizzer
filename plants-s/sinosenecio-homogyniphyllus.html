Sinosenecio homogyniphyllus

Sinosenecio homogyniphyllus, commonly known as the Yunnan ghost flower, is a member of the Asteraceae family and is native to China.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its finely divided, grass-like leaves and small, clustered yellow flowers. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions. Its unique floral structure and habitat preferences make it a subject of interest in botanical studies.

It is also noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in specialized gardens.
