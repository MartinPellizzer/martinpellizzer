Synotis alata

Synotis alata, commonly known as the winged ant plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive winged leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded areas.

Synotis alata is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its ecological role in supporting pollinators.
