Strophanthus schultzei

Strophanthus schultzei, commonly known as the red devil's trumpet, is a striking flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height, characterized by its long, pendulous, tubular flowers that are typically red or orange in color. The plant produces small, woody pods that contain seeds, and its leaves are simple and alternate.

Strophanthus schultzei is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
