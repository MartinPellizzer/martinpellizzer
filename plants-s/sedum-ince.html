Sedum ince

Sedum ince, commonly known as Ice Plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Crassulaceae.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its fleshy, succulent leaves and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers. Sedum ince is also valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
