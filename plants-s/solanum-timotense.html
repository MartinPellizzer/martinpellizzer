Solanum timotense

Solanum timotense, commonly known as the Timotense nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its flowers are usually white or pale green, with five petals, and it produces small, yellow berries that contain the seeds.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
