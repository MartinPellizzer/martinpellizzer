Solanum robecchii

Solanum robecchii, commonly known as the Robecchii's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and peppers. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, with hairy stems and oval, pointed leaves. Its flowers are small, white or purple, and it produces small, yellow berries that are edible but contain toxic compounds.

Solanum robecchii is often found in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in the unique microclimates of its native habitat.
