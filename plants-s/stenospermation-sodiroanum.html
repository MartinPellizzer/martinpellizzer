Stenospermation sodiroanum

Stenospermation sodiroanum, commonly known as the narrow-seeded grass.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Andean region of South America. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow leaves. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, typical of the grass family.

Its botanical significance lies in its role within local ecosystems and its potential for ecological research.
