Stromanthe jacquinii

Stromanthe jacquinii, commonly known as the velvet leaf plant, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with shades of green, silver, and pink. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.

Stromanthe jacquinii is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
