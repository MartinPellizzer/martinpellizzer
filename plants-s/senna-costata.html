Senna costata

Senna costata, commonly known as the coastal senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with serrated margins and yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. Senna costata thrives in coastal areas and sandy soils, often growing in disturbed habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
