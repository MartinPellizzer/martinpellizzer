Syzygium campanulatum var. longistylum

Syzygium campanulatum var. longistylum, commonly known as the long-styled apple orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its pendant, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and glossy, with a lanceolate shape.

This variant is distinguished by its elongated stamen, which contributes to its unique botanical appearance.
