Stapelia × fuscata

Stapelia × fuscata, commonly known as the brown starfish plant, is a hybrid succulent native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and distinctive, star-shaped flowers. The plant produces waxy, yellowish-green flowers with a strong, unpleasant odor, which attracts pollinators like flies. Its leaves are typically small and oval, and it is often grown as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance.

Stapelia × fuscata is relatively easy to care for, requiring minimal water and well-draining soil.
