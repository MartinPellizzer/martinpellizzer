Senecio gerardi

Senecio gerardi, commonly known as the Gerard's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features dark green, lobed leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. It is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Senecio gerardi is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its striking foliage and floral display.
