Sunipia grandiflora

Sunipia grandiflora, commonly known as the grandiflora sunipia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its striking, large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant features broad, waxy leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Sunipia grandiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
