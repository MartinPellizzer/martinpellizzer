Stelis glossulicles

Stelis glossulicles, commonly known as the orchid with clustered flowers, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, often epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale in color, with intricate patterns on the petals. Its roots are adapted for attachment to tree trunks or other surfaces, allowing it to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

Stelis glossulicles is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
