Stylogyne perpunctata

Stylogyne perpunctata, commonly known as the punctate stylogyne, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, delicate leaves and clusters of tiny, white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its unique leaf patterns and compact growth habit make it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Stylogyne perpunctata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
