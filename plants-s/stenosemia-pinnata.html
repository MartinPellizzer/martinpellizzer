Stenosemia pinnata

Stenosemia pinnata, commonly known as the broadleaf arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are often veined with a striking pattern, making it visually distinctive. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as rainforests and along stream banks. Its leaves are usually large and broad, with a smooth, glossy surface.

Stenosemia pinnata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
