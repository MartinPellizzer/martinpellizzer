Silene kermesina

Silene kermesina, commonly known as the scarlet pimpernel, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, often changing with the light. The plant typically grows in meadows, woodlands, and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture, while its stems are slender and branching.

Silene kermesina is an annual or biennial plant, blooming in spring and early summer.
