Solanum cayeuxi

Solanum cayeuxi, commonly known as the Cayuxi nightshade, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is native to Central America, particularly found in countries like Guatemala and Belize. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often pubescent, while its flowers are small and typically white or pale green.

The fruit is a globose berry that contains several seeds, and the plant is known for its potential medicinal properties.
