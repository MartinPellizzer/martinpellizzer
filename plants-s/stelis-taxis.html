Stelis taxis

Stelis taxis, commonly known as the orchid spike orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features a rhizomatous root system and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and green. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Stelis taxis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
