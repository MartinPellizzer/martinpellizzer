Sisymbrium glandulosum var. linearifolium

Sisymbrium glandulosum var. linearifolium, commonly known as the slender bladderpod, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, cruciform flowers and capsule fruits. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has linear, glandular leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
