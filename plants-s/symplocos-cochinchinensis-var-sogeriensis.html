Symplocos cochinchinensis var. sogeriensis

Symplocos cochinchinensis var. sogeriensis, commonly known as the Sogeriensian waxtree, is a shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its small, glossy, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, white to pinkish flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are small, fleshy berries that turn red when mature.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
