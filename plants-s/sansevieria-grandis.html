Sansevieria grandis

Sansevieria grandis, commonly known as the foot-stool plant or African spear, is a striking succulent native to tropical Africa.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 meters in height, often with a dark green coloration and prominent yellowish or white margins. The plant is characterized by its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal water, making it highly adaptable. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, storing water and enabling it to survive in arid environments.

Sansevieria grandis is also noted for its air-purifying qualities, contributing to its popularity as an indoor plant.
