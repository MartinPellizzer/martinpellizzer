Senecio hochstetteri

Senecio hochstetteri, commonly known as the Hochstetter's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant is often grown as an ornamental due to its unique appearance and ability to thrive in well-drained soil. It prefers full sun to partial shade and is drought-tolerant once established.

Senecio hochstetteri is also known for its ability to form dense mats, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.
