Silene laxiflora

Silene laxiflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered catchfly, is a herbaceous plant native to arctic and alpine regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in cool, moist environments. The plant features simple, alternate leaves and produces clusters of small, pink or white flowers. It is often found in tundra and mountain meadow habitats.

Silene laxiflora is adapted to withstand harsh climatic conditions and is an important component of its ecological community.
