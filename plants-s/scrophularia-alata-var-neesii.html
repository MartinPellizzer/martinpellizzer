Scrophularia alata var. neesii

Scrophularia alata var. neesii, commonly known as the broadleaf scrophularia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of East Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a distinctive wavy margin and a rough texture. Its flowers are tubular, with a two-lipped structure, and are usually pink or purple in color.

It belongs to the Scrophulariaceae family, which includes many species with medicinal and ornamental value.
