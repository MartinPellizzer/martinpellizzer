Solanum lanceifolium

Solanum lanceifolium, commonly known as the lanceleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are usually hairy and have a slightly serrated edge. Its flowers are usually violet or purple and bloom in clusters, while the fruit is a small, toxic berry.

Botanically, it belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like tomatoes and peppers.
