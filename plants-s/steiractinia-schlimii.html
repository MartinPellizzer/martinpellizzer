Steiractinia schlimii

Steiractinia schlimii, commonly known as the Schlim's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the high altitudes of South America, particularly in the Andes.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, delicate flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically blooms in the spring and summer months, showcasing a range of colors including white, yellow, and pink. Steiractinia schlimii thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.
