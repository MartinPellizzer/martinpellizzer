Stanhopea Lyncea

Stanhopea lyncea is a rare and exquisite orchid native to the cloud forests of Costa Rica. Known for its striking violet and white petals, it captivates botanists and enthusiasts alike with its beauty. This species thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific conditions to bloom. Its delicate flowers often appear in late winter or early spring. Due to habitat loss, Stanhopea lyncea is considered endangered, making conservation efforts crucial for its survival.
