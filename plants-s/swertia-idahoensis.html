Swertia idahoensis

Swertia idahoensis, commonly known as Idaho gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Idaho and western Montana.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, rocky habitats and thrives in high-altitude environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly rough texture.

Swertia idahoensis is known for its medicinal properties and has been used by Indigenous peoples for various healing purposes.
