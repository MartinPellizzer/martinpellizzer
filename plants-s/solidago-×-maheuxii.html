Solidago × maheuxii

Solidago × maheuxii, commonly known as the hybrid goldenrod, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the genus Solidago and is a cross between Solidago decurrens and Solidago nemoralis. The plant is characterized by its tall, sturdy stems and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Solidago × maheuxii is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.
