Senecio fedifolius

Senecio fedifolius, commonly known as the golden ragwort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and fuzzy, lobed leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is known for its showy inflorescence.

Senecio fedifolius is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant color and unique foliage.
