Stelis decipiens

Stelis decipiens, commonly known as the deceptive stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees in tropical rainforests, where it derives moisture and nutrients from the air and rain. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, often during the rainy season. Its roots are specialized for absorbing water and nutrients from the surrounding environment.

Stelis decipiens is known for its delicate appearance and is a popular species among orchid enthusiasts.
