Scolosanthus multiflorus subsp. hirticalyx

Scolosanthus multiflorus subsp.

hirticalyx, commonly known as the hairy leaved scolosanthus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils.

The plant features narrow, hairy leaves and produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. It is often found in open woodland or grassland habitats.

The subspecies is distinguished by its dense pubescence and compact growth habit.
