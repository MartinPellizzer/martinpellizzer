Syagrus × nabonnandii

Syagrus × nabonnandii, commonly known as the royal palm, is a hybrid palm native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of long, feather-like leaves that give it a regal appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by clusters of orange-red fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

Syagrus × nabonnandii is also valued for its ability to tolerate urban environments, making it a popular choice for landscaping.
