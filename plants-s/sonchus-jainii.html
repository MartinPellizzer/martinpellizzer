Sonchus jainii

Sonchus jainii, commonly known as Indian sowthistle, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the Indian subcontinent and thrives in a variety of habitats, including roadsides and disturbed soils. The plant features basal leaves that are smooth and oval-shaped, with a hairy texture. It produces yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the daisy family.

Sonchus jainii is often considered a weed but has traditional medicinal uses in some regions.
