Senecio anethifolius subsp. brevibracteolatus

Senecio anethifolius subsp.

brevibracteolatus, commonly known as the narrow-bract senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, daisy-like flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. It has a compact growth habit and is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance.
