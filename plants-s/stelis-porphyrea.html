Stelis porphyrea

Stelis porphyrea, commonly known as the purple-stem orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, often branched stems that are typically covered in a purplish hue. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale pink, arranged in clusters along the stems. Stelis porphyrea thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing on trees or rocks in tropical forests.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a sought-after plant among orchid enthusiasts.
