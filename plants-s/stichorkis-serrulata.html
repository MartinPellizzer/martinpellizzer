Stichorkis serrulata

Stichorkis serrulata, commonly known as the serrated stichorkis, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges, which give it its common name. Its small, white or pale blue flowers are clustered in racemes and bloom in the spring.

Stichorkis serrulata is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
