Sogerianthe versicolor

Sogerianthe versicolor, commonly known as the variable orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its strikingly colorful and often fragrant blooms. The plant typically features slender, erect stems with broad, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and purple, and often have a complex, ornate structure.

Sogerianthe versicolor thrives in warm, humid environments and is often found in forest understories or near water sources.
