Spiraea japonica var. bumalda

Spiraea japonica var. bumalda, commonly known as Japanese weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It features showy, clustered flowers that range from pink to red, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and turn reddish in the fall. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This ornamental plant is often used in gardens for its vibrant floral display and compact growth habit.
