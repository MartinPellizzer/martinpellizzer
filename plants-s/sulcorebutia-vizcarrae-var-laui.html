Sulcorebutia vizcarrae var. laui

Sulcorebutia vizcarrae var. laui, commonly known as the Laui's Sulcorebutia, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to the arid regions of Argentina.

It features a short, cylindrical stem that is often covered in dense, white to pale green spines. The plant produces clusters of small, showy yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to dry conditions, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
