Sulcorebutia tarabucoensis subsp. rauschii

Sulcorebutia tarabucoensis subsp.

rauschii, commonly known as the Rauschii crassula, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to the Andes of Bolivia. It features dense clusters of small, rounded, pale green to blue-green leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base.

The plant produces delicate, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the spring or early summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This species is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
