Scabiosa monspeliensis var. lineariloba

Scabiosa monspeliensis var. lineariloba, commonly known as the linear-leaved scabious, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of small, pink to purple flowers. The leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slight hairy texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
