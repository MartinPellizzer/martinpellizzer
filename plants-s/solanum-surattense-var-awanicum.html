Solanum surattense var. awanicum

Solanum surattense var. awanicum, commonly known as the awanicum nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its woody stems and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers. The plant produces berries that are typically yellow or orange when ripe, and they are often used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
