Stephania japonica var. discolor

Stephania japonica var. discolor, commonly known as Japanese stephania, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with a distinctive reddish or purple discoloration on the underside. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by oval, woody fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique leaf coloration and seasonal foliage changes.
