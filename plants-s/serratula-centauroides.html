Serratula centauroides

Serratula centauroides, commonly known as the centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Serratula centauroides is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
