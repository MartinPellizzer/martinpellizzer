Senecio Candidans

Senecio candidans is a striking plant known for its unique appearance and adaptability. It features thick, fleshy leaves that often display a pale or whitish color, giving it a distinctive look. Native to southern Africa, this species thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. Its ability to store water makes it highly drought-resistant. Despite its beauty, it can be invasive in some regions, requiring careful management.
