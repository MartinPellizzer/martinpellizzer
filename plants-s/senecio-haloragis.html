Senecio haloragis

Senecio haloragis, commonly known as the silverleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant features narrow, silvery-gray leaves with a waxy coating, which help reduce water loss in arid conditions. Its small, yellow flowers appear in clusters and bloom during the warmer months.

It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to withstand drought and high temperatures.
