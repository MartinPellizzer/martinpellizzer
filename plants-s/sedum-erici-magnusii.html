Sedum erici-magnusii

Sedum erici-magnusii, commonly known as the Ericus Magnusii stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color, adapted to store water. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, often blooming in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy choice for rock gardens or alpine settings.

Its botanical characteristics make it well-suited for arid environments and low-maintenance gardening.
