Scutellaria potosina var. kaibabensis

Scutellaria potosina var. kaibabensis, commonly known as Kaibab skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its unique genetic variation distinguishes it from other Scutellaria species.
