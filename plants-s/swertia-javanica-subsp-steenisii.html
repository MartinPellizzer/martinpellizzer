Swertia javanica subsp. steenisii

Swertia javanica subsp.

steenisii, commonly known as the Javan gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia. It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its blue to violet flowers that bloom in early summer.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
