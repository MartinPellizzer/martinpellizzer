Spiraeanthemum vitiense var. macrophyllum

Spiraeanthemum vitiense var. macrophyllum, commonly known as the giant leaved spiraeanthemum, is a species native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, elliptical leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers during the flowering season. It thrives in humid, tropical climates with well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for tropical gardens and greenhouses.
