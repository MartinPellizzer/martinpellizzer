Stenolobium quinquejugum

Stenolobium quinquejugum, commonly known as the five-lobed acacia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant is notable for its dense, spreading growth habit and its ability to thrive in arid and semi-arid environments.

Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, and it is often used in traditional medicine and as a source of timber.
