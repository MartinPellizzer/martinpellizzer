Stobaea gaertneri

Stobaea gaertneri, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often hairy as well.

Stobaea gaertneri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
