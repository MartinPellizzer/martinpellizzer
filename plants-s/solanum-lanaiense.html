Solanum lanaiense

Solanum lanaiense, commonly known as the Lanai nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian island of Lanai.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring woody stems and dark green, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by berries that are initially green and turn red as they mature.

Solanum lanaiense is part of the nightshade family (Solanaceae), which includes other well-known plants such as tomatoes and potatoes.
