Spiraea crenifolia var. pallasii

Spiraea crenifolia var. pallasii, commonly known as the Pallas's spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features slender, arching stems that produce clusters of small, white or pinkish flowers in early summer. The leaves are narrow, serrated, and have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
