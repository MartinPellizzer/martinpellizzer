Senna hebecarpa

Senna hebecarpa, commonly known as the horse bean tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces long, slender pods that contain seeds used as a source of protein in some cultures.

Senna hebecarpa is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in various soil types.
