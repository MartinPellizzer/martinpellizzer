Solanum crotonoides

Solanum crotonoides, commonly known as the African nightshade, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant features small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by orange or red berries. Its leaves are ovate and hairy, and the plant is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Solanum crotonoides is used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
