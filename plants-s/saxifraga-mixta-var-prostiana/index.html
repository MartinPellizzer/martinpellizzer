Saxifraga Mixta Var. Prostiana

Saxifraga mixta var. prostiana is a striking plant native to the alpine regions of Asia. It thrives in harsh, rocky environments where few other species can survive. The plant is known for its vibrant pink or red flowers that bloom in late summer. Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a favorite among horticulturists and gardeners. This variety is also valued for its ability to add color to difficult-to-landscape areas.
