Specklinia gonzalezii

Specklinia gonzalezii, commonly known as the Gonzalez's speckled orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments within montane forests. The plant features a slender, upright stem with several fleshy, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, pale green, and adorned with distinctive speckles, giving it its common name.

Specklinia gonzalezii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
