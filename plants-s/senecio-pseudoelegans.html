Senecio pseudoelegans

Senecio pseudoelegans, commonly known as the purple coneflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking purple to blue flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces a distinctive conical flower head.

Senecio pseudoelegans is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
