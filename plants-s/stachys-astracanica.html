Stachys astracanica

Stachys astracanica, commonly known as the Astracanica hedge nettle, is a perennial herb native to regions of Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with hairy surfaces and produces spikes of small, blue to purple flowers. It is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and stream banks.

Stachys astracanica is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
