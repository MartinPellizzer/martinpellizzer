Scutellaria attenuifolia

Scutellaria attenuifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaved skullcap, is a flowering plant belonging to the mint family, Lamiaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and small, tubular blue or purple flowers. Native to regions of eastern Asia, including China and Japan, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often found in woodland or rocky habitats.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and horticultural interest.
