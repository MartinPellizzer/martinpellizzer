Swertia speciosa var. perfoliata

Swertia speciosa var. perfoliata, commonly known as the leafy gentian, is a perennial herb native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has opposite, often lance-shaped leaves that are sometimes fused around the stems, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

Its medicinal properties have been studied for their potential anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects.
