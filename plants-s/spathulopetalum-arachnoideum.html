Spathulopetalum arachnoideum

Spathulopetalum arachnoideum, commonly known as the spider-petaled orchid, is a rare and exotic flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, spider-like petals that give it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, often in shades of white and pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a delicate and endangered species.
