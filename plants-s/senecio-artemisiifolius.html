Senecio artemisiifolius

Senecio artemisiifolius, commonly known as the blueweed or blue thistle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in open grasslands and rocky areas. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a bluish-green hue and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Senecio artemisiifolius is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
