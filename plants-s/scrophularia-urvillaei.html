Scrophularia urvillaei

Scrophularia urvillaei, commonly known as the Urville's figwort, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, often at high altitudes where conditions are harsh. The plant features erect stems covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are lance-shaped with serrated edges. It produces clusters of tubular, two-lipped flowers that are usually pink or purple in color.

Scrophularia urvillaei is part of the Scrophulariaceae family, which includes many species known for their medicinal properties.
