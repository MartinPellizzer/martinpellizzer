Smilax ovatorotunda f. maximowiczii

Smilax ovatorotunda f. maximowiczii, commonly known as the broad-leaf smilax, is a variant of the smilax genus characterized by its distinctive, broad, ovate leaves.

This plant is a climbing shrub with a woody stem and is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia. Its leaves are typically smooth and have a waxy texture, while its stems are covered in spines. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by bright red berries.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along streambanks.
