Swertia lastii

Swertia lastii, commonly known as the Himalayan gentian, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, alpine environments and is adapted to cold, harsh climates. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Swertia lastii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
