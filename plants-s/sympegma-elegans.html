Sympegma elegans

Sympegma elegans, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, often during the cooler months. Its leaves are covered in a fine layer of white, waxy hairs, giving it a silvery appearance.

The fruit is a small, fleshy berry that turns red when mature.
