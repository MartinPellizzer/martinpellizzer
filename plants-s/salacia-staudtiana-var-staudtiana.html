Salacia staudtiana var. staudtiana

Salacia staudtiana var. staudtiana, commonly known as the Staudt's salacia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Salicaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth surface. Its small, yellowish-green flowers are typically clustered in dense inflorescences.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden environments.
