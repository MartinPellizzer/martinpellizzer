Sonerila tuberculifera

Sonerila tuberculifera, commonly known as the warty sonerila, is a member of the Rubiaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, evergreen shrub characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine, waxy hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a small, three-lobed capsule that releases seeds when mature.

Sonerila tuberculifera is valued in traditional medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
