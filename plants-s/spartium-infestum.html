Spartium infestum

Spartium infestum, commonly known as goat's beard, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, arching stems that bear clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats.

Spartium infestum is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
