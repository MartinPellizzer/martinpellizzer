Senecio goodspeedii

Senecio goodspeedii, commonly known as Goodspeed's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Senecio goodspeedii is often found in disturbed habitats and is considered a species of conservation concern in some regions.
