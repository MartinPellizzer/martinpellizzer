Strelitzia parvifolia var. juncea

Strelitzia parvifolia var. juncea, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bird-of-paradise, is a rare and unique variant of the bird-of-paradise plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves that are typically a deep green with a subtle variegation. The plant produces striking, colorful inflorescences that resemble a bird in flight, featuring vibrant orange and blue petals. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in specialized gardens and botanical collections.
