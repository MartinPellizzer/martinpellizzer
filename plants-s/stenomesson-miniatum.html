Stenomesson miniatum

Stenomesson miniatum, commonly known as the miniature sunflower, is a small annual plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that resemble those of the sunflower. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in dry conditions.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
