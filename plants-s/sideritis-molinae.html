Sideritis molinae

Sideritis molinae, commonly known as Spanish heather, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are narrow and aromatic, often used in traditional herbal remedies.

Sideritis molinae thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climates.
