Solanum ovigerum var. insanum

Solanum ovigerum var. insanum, commonly known as the crazy nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant is characterized by its dark purple berries, which are toxic to humans and animals. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in disturbed soils.

Its rapid growth and invasive tendencies make it a problematic weed in some regions.
