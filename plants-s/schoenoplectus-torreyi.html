Schoenoplectus torreyi

Schoenoplectus torreyi, commonly known as Torrey's bulrush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that often reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that bloom in late summer, typically from July to September. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, forming dense clumps at the base.

Schoenoplectus torreyi is commonly found in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of ponds and lakes.
