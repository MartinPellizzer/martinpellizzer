Smilacina tatsienensis

Smilacina tatsienensis, commonly known as the Tatsienliao greenbrier, is a climbing vine native to eastern Asia, particularly China.

It belongs to the Smilacaceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in umbels. Its fruits are berries that mature to a red color, attracting various bird species.

Smilacina tatsienensis is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides.
