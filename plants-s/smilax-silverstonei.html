Smilax silverstonei

Smilax silverstonei, commonly known as silverleaf smilax, is a perennial vine native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its thick, spiny stems and heart-shaped, glossy green leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into red, fleshy berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its attractive foliage and unique fruiting structures.
