Sesbania multijuga

Sesbania multijuga, commonly known as the yellow sesbania, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers, which bloom in clusters. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it useful in agricultural settings. It thrives in warm climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.

Sesbania multijuga is also valued for its ornamental appeal and potential as a forage crop.
