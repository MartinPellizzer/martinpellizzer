Stephanomeria tenuifolia

Stephanomeria tenuifolia, commonly known as the narrowleaf stephanomeria, is a flowering plant native to California and southern Oregon.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and typically showy blooms. The plant has slender, linear leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal scrub and chaparral habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
