Schefflera mangiferifolia var. panatarana

Schefflera mangiferifolia var. panatarana, commonly known as the Panatarana schefflera, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of large, leathery leaves. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters in its natural habitat. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, with veins that are prominent and visible.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm, humid environments.
