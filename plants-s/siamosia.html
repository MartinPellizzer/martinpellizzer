Siamosia

Siamosia, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the genus Saintpaulia and is characterized by its compact, mound-like growth habit. The plant features velvety, heart-shaped leaves and produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of purple, blue, and white. Siamosia thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

It is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
