Senecio verbascifolius

Senecio verbascifolius, commonly known as the woolyleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-gray leaves and yellow ray flowers that resemble daisies. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with composite flower heads. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Senecio verbascifolius is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and blooms.
