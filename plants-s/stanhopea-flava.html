Stanhopea flava

Stanhopea flava, commonly known as the yellow stanhopea, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers with contrasting purple and green markings. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, tropical environments. Its flowers bloom in late summer and are known for their fragrant, spicy scent.

Stanhopea flava is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and unique floral characteristics.
