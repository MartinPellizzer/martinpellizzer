Sauvagesia adima var. minor

Sauvagesia adima var. minor, commonly known as the lesser sauvagesia, is a rare and lesser-known variant of the sauvagesia genus.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. This plant is typically found in specific microhabitats, often in rocky or shaded areas. Its botanical features include a compact growth habit and a preference for well-drained soils.
