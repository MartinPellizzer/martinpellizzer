Steris javanica

Steris javanica, commonly known as the Javanese Steris, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae, commonly known as the orchid family. The plant is characterized by its tall, erect stem and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Steris javanica is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
