Swertia papuana

Swertia papuana, commonly known as the Papuan swertia, is a flowering plant native to the highlands of New Guinea.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its erect, herbaceous stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, blue to violet flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the cooler months. Its medicinal properties have been of interest to traditional healers in the region.
