Stemonocoleus

Stemonocoleus, commonly known as the star-tipped orchid, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

Stemonocoleus species have fibrous roots and are adapted to nutrient-poor soils, often forming symbiotic relationships with mycorrhizal fungi.
