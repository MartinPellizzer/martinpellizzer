Stylosanthes flavicans

Stylosanthes flavicans, commonly known as the yellow stylo, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its prostrate or semi-erect growth habit and produces small, yellow flowers. The plant has trifoliate leaves with three oval-shaped leaflets. It is often used as a forage crop due to its high nutritional value for livestock.

Stylosanthes flavicans is also valued for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, improving soil fertility.
