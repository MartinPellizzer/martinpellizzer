Spergularia campestris var. alpina

Spergularia campestris var. alpina, commonly known as the alpine sandspurry, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically thrives in sandy or rocky soils in alpine and subalpine environments. The plant produces small, yellow or orange flowers that bloom in summer, and its leaves are usually narrow and succulent.
