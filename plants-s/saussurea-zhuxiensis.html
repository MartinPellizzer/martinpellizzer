Saussurea zhuxiensis

Saussurea zhuxiensis, commonly known as the Zhuxi saussurea, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stem and large, showy composite flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky habitats at high elevations. Its leaves are linear and often covered with a dense layer of fine hairs.
