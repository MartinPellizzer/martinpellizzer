Stelis dentata

Stelis dentata, commonly known as the toothed stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Stelis dentata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a tropical or subtropical setting.
