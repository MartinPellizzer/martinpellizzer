Scelochilus lindenii

Scelochilus lindenii, commonly known as the African orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern around the stem.

Scelochilus lindenii thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
