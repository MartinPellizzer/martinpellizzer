Saxifraga forrestii

Saxifraga forrestii, commonly known as the Himalayan saxifrage, is a perennial flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or alpine regions.

Due to its unique habitat and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
