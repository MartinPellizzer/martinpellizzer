Stephanomeria lactucina

Stephanomeria lactucina, commonly known as the milkweed aster, is a flowering plant native to California.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has hairy stems and leaves, and its flowers bloom from late spring to early summer.

The plant is also noted for its milky sap, which is a distinguishing feature of the genus Stephanomeria.
