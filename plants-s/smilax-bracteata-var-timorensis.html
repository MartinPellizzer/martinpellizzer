Smilax bracteata var. timorensis

Smilax bracteata var. timorensis, commonly known as the Timor arrowroot, is a climbing shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features a robust, woody stem with thorns and produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in axillary inflorescences. The plant bears yellowish berries that mature into a dark purple color, containing several seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its leaves are heart-shaped with a rough texture and are often used in traditional medicine.
