Seriphidium skorniakovii

Seriphidium skorniakovii, commonly known as the Skorniakov's tarragon, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is often found in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.

Its unique morphology and ecological adaptability make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
