Syngonium vellozianum var. riedelianum

Syngonium vellozianum var. riedelianum, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a tropical climbing vine native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, arrowhead-like leaves that vary in color from bright green to variegated white and green patterns. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth. Its aerial roots allow it to climb and attach to supports, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets or trained trellises.

The foliage is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
