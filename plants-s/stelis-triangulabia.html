Stelis triangulabia

Stelis triangulabia, commonly known as the triangle orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its unique triangular-shaped pseudobulbs. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its flowers are usually small and white or pale green, with intricate patterns.

Stelis triangulabia is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions.
