Sansevieria brevifolia

Sansevieria brevifolia, commonly known as the short-leaf snake plant, is a succulent species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features upright, grass-like leaves that are shorter and more compact compared to other Sansevieria varieties. The leaves are typically a deep green with a slightly waxy texture and may have subtle variegation. It is well adapted to low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

The plant is also known for its air-purifying qualities, contributing to improved indoor air quality.
