Sabicea africana

Sabicea africana, commonly known as the African hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, pentagonal-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a velvety texture.

Sabicea africana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
