Satureja straussii

Satureja straussii, commonly known as Strauss's savory, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and thrives in full sun. Its small, tubular flowers are usually pink or violet, and it produces aromatic leaves that are used in traditional herbal medicine.

Satureja straussii is valued for its culinary and medicinal properties, similar to other members of the savory genus.
