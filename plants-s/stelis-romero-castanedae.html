Stelis romero-castanedae

Stelis romero-castanedae, commonly known as the Romero-Castaneda orchid, is a terrestrial orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the high-altitude regions of South America, particularly in Ecuador and Peru. The plant typically grows in rocky or forested areas, where it thrives in cool, humid conditions. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pink and purple.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and is often found in association with other epiphytic orchids in its native habitat.
