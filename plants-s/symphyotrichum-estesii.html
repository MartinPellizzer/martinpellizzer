Symphyotrichum estesii

Symphyotrichum estesii, commonly known as Estes' New England Aster, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and showy blooms. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodlands. It produces vibrant purple to pink flowers that bloom in the late summer to early fall.

Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture.
