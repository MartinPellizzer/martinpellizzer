Silene zhongbaensis

Silene zhongbaensis, commonly known as the Zhongba sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the Qinghai-Tibet Plateau in China.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its upright stems and small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant thrives in high-altitude environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils. Silene zhongbaensis is adapted to cold climates and has a short growing season.

Its limited distribution makes it a rare and ecologically significant species in its native habitat.
