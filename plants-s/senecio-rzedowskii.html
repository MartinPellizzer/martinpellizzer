Senecio rzedowskii

Senecio rzedowskii, commonly known as the Rzedowskii's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its yellow flowers are arranged in a single head, with bracts that are often mistaken for petals.

Senecio rzedowskii is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
