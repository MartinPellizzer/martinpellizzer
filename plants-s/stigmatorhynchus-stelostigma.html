Stigmatorhynchus stelostigma

Stigmatorhynchus stelostigma, commonly known as the tropical orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature a complex structure with a labellum and column. The plant is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in warm, humid climates. Its roots are epiphytic, meaning they grow on other plants without being parasitic.

The botanical features of S. stelostigma make it a popular choice for both horticultural and conservation purposes.
