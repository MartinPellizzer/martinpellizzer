Stellaria alpestris

Stellaria alpestris, commonly known as alpine starwort, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to alpine and arctic regions.

It features small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to August. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils. It is adapted to cold climates and can tolerate harsh environmental conditions.

Stellaria alpestris plays a role in stabilizing soil in mountainous areas and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
