Sobralia kalbreyeri

Sobralia kalbreyeri, commonly known as the Kalbreyer's Sobralia, is a striking orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in varying environmental conditions. Its leaves are long, strap-like, and often have a waxy texture, contributing to its aesthetic appeal.

Sobralia kalbreyeri is well-suited for greenhouse cultivation due to its preference for warm, humid climates and bright indirect light.
