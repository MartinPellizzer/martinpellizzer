Stachys herrerae

Stachys herrerae, commonly known as Herrera's hedge nettle, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces small, clustered flowers with purple to pink petals, which bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, giving them a fuzzy texture.

Stachys herrerae is often found in rocky or disturbed soils and is adapted to high-altitude environments.
