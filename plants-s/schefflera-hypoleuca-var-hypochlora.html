Schefflera hypoleuca var. hypochlora

Schefflera hypoleuca var. hypochlora, commonly known as the white-veined schefflera, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with white veins against a dark green background. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal.
