Stipa stricta

Stipa stricta, commonly known as slender needlegrass, is a perennial grass species native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant has narrow, upright leaves and produces dense, cylindrical spikelets that are often tinged with red or purple. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.

Stipa stricta is also known for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions and its role in stabilizing soil in ecological restoration projects.
