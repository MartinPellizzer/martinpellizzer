Strobilanthes fauriei

Strobilanthes fauriei, commonly known as the golden flame or flame flower, is a striking member of the Acanthaceae family.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

Strobilanthes fauriei is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is appreciated for its unique floral display.
