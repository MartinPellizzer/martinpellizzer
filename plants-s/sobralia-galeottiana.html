Sobralia galeottiana

Sobralia galeottiana, commonly known as the Galeotti's sobralia, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences that feature a mix of violet and white petals. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its leaves are usually long and narrow, adapted for efficient water absorption in its natural habitat.

Sobralia galeottiana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique floral structure.
