Swertia pulchella

Swertia pulchella, commonly known as the Himalayan gentian, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in cool and moist conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in a fine pubescence.

Swertia pulchella is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
