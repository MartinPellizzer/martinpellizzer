Senecio onae var. leonis

Senecio onae var. leonis, commonly known as the Lion's Head Groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of yellow flowers. The plant has silver-green, woolly leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is widely cultivated in arid and semi-arid regions.
