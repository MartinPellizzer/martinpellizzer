Stellaria stellatopilosa

Stellaria stellatopilosa, commonly known as the starry chickweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the spring and summer months. The plant has slender, hairy stems and opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. Native to temperate regions of Asia, it thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and rocky areas.

Its delicate appearance and unique floral structure make it a subject of interest in botanical studies and garden cultivation.
