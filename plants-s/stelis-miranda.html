Stelis miranda

Stelis miranda, commonly known as the Miranda stelis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structure. The plant typically produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, often with a distinctive lip. Stelis miranda thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both horticultural collections and botanical research.
