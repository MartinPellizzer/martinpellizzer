Syncarpha mucronata

Syncarpha mucronata, commonly known as the narrowleaf synbranch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, showing a preference for well-drained soils. Its narrow, linear leaves are arranged alternately along the stem, and it produces small yellow flowers in clusters.
