Solanum cyclophyllum

Solanum cyclophyllum, commonly known as the cyclophyll bellflower, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its distinctive bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with hairy stems and opposite leaves. Its flowers are usually pale blue or purple, and it produces small, yellow berries that contain the seeds.

Solanum cyclophyllum is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
