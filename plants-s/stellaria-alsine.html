Stellaria alsine

Stellaria alsine, commonly known as common mouse-ear, is a low-growing annual herb belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, rounded leaves and clusters of star-shaped white flowers. The plant typically thrives in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas. Stellaria alsine is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in various habitats.

Its botanical features make it adaptable to a wide range of environmental conditions.
