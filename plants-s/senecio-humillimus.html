Senecio humillimus

Senecio humillimus, commonly known as the dwarf ironweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of less than 30 centimeters. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact size and long-lasting blooms.
