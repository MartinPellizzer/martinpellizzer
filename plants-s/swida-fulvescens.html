Swida fulvescens

Swida fulvescens, commonly known as the golden swida, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its showy, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in late spring. Swida fulvescens is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
