Silene Transalpina

Alpine campion, scientifically known as Silene transalpina, is a striking flower that thrives in the harsh environments of mountainous regions. This resilient plant is native to the alpine zones of Europe and parts of Asia, where it adapts to cold temperatures and thin air. Its delicate pink or white petals and vibrant foliage make it a notable sight in high-altitude ecosystems. Despite its beauty, Silene transalpina is often overlooked due to its modest size and remote habitat. It plays a vital role in supporting local biodiversity and is a symbol of the unique flora found in alpine landscapes.
