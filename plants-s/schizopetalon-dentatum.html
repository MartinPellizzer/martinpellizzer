Schizopetalon dentatum

Schizopetalon dentatum, commonly known as the toothed petal orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, toothed petals and sepals, which give it its scientific name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and streamside areas. It produces small, fragrant flowers in a range of colors, including white, yellow, and green.

Schizopetalon dentatum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
