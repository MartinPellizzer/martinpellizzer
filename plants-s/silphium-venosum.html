Silphium venosum

Silphium venosum, commonly known as the veiny silphium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, branching stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves with prominent veins. Silphium venosum is known for its ornamental value and has been historically used in traditional medicine.
