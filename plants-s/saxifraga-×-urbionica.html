Saxifraga × urbionica

Saxifraga × urbionica, commonly known as the hybrid saxifrage, is a low-growing perennial plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically forms dense mats of foliage with small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and rock crevices. Its leaves are usually broad and fleshy, adapted to retain moisture in its natural habitat.

Saxifraga × urbionica is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in challenging conditions.
