Schkuhria multiflora var. aristata

Schkuhria multiflora var. aristata, commonly known as the arrowleaf dandelion, is a perennial herb native to South America.

It features basal leaves that are arrow-shaped, with a distinctive central vein, and a flowering head composed of numerous yellow ray florets. The plant produces a capsule fruit that contains numerous small, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species for ecological studies and herbal medicine.
