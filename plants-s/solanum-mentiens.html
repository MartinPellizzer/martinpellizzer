Solanum mentiens

Solanum mentiens, commonly known as the Mentient Nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is characterized by its small, bell-shaped flowers that typically range in color from white to pale purple. The plant produces small, round berries that turn from green to yellowish as they mature. Native to certain regions of South America, it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Like many members of its family, it contains toxic alkaloids, making it potentially harmful to humans and animals.
