Swertia Obtusa Var. Cuspidata

Swertia obtusa var. cuspidata is a lesser-known member of the gentian family known for its striking blue flowers and medicinal properties. Native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas, this plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas. Its unique appearance and potential health benefits have made it a subject of interest among botanists and herbalists alike. Despite its beauty and utility, it remains underappreciated and rarely cultivated outside its natural habitat. This article explores the characteristics, habitat, and significance of this remarkable plant.
