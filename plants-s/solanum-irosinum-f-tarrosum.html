Solanum irosinum f. tarrosum

Solanum irosinum f. tarrosum, commonly known as the Tarrosum nightshade, is a botanical variant of the species Solanum irosinum.

It is a perennial herb native to certain regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia. The plant features dark green, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture and produces small, violet flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a yellowish, fleshy berry that ripens to a red color, containing several seeds.

This variant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage and flowering characteristics.
