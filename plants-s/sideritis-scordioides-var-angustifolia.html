Sideritis scordioides var. angustifolia

Sideritis scordioides var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaved ironwort, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color, arranged in whorled clusters. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

This species is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
