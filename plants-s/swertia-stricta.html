Swertia stricta

Swertia stricta, commonly known as the Chinese gentian, is a perennial herb native to Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and thrives in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Swertia stricta is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
