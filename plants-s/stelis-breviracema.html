Stelis breviracema

Stelis breviracema, commonly known as the short-stemmed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in tropical rainforests. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in greenhouses and botanical gardens.
