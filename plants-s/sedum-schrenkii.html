Sedum schrenkii

Sedum schrenkii, commonly known as the Schrenk's stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow to orange. The plant is well adapted to arid and rocky environments, storing water in its thick leaves. It typically grows in mountainous regions and is often found in dry, well-drained soils.

Sedum schrenkii is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in harsh conditions.
