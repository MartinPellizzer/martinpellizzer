Samara laeta

Samara laeta, commonly known as the shining samara, is a unique plant species characterized by its distinctive winged seeds.

The plant produces small, elongated fruits that are equipped with feathery wings, aiding in wind dispersal. Its leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a smooth, waxy surface. Samara laeta thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

This plant plays an important role in local ecosystems by providing food for various insects and birds.
