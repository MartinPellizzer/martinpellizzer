Statice franchetii

Statice franchetii, commonly known as the blue statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, blue to purple flowers. The plant produces small, narrow leaves and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It is often cultivated as a ornamental plant for its long-lasting blooms and drought tolerance.

Statice franchetii is also used in floral arrangements due to its vibrant color and resilience.
