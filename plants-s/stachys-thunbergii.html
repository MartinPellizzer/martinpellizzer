Stachys thunbergii

Stachys thunbergii, commonly known as narrowleaf hedge nettle, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Stachys thunbergii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
