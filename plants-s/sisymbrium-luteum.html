Sisymbrium luteum

Sisymbrium luteum, commonly known as yellow rocket, is an annual flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many cruciferous plants. The plant features basal leaves that are lobed and hairy, with yellow flowers arranged in elongated racemes. Its small, pod-like fruits are typically hairy and contain several seeds.

It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats, including roadsides and fields.
