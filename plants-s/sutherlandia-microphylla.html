Sutherlandia microphylla

Sutherlandia microphylla, commonly known as cancer bush, is a small, evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, red, fleshy fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has gained attention for its potential health benefits.
