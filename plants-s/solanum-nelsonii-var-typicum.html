Solanum nelsonii var. typicum

Solanum nelsonii var. typicum, commonly known as Nelson's nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades such as tomatoes and potatoes. The plant features small, white or pale green flowers and produces berries that change color from green to red as they mature. It is adapted to survive in dry conditions with deep roots and thick, waxy leaves.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
