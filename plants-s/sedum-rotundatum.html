Sedum rotundatum

Sedum rotundatum, commonly known as the roundleaf stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features fleshy, rounded leaves that are typically blue-green in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Sedum rotundatum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
