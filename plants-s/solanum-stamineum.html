Solanum stamineum

Solanum stamineum, commonly known as the stamen leaf or stamen plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its distinctive foliage, which resembles stamens due to its narrow, thread-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often followed by berries that change color as they mature.

It is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique leaf structure and can thrive in warm, humid environments.
