Sculeria geminata

Sculeria geminata, commonly known as the twinleaf sculeria, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, white or pale pink flowers appear in clusters during the spring.

Sculeria geminata is noted for its aromatic foliage and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
