Stachygynandrum sanguinolentum

Stachygynandrum sanguinolentum, commonly known as the blood-red orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, vividly red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments within tropical forests. Its leaves are elongated and glossy, often arranged in a compact, upright cluster.

The orchid's unique coloration makes it a popular choice for both ornamental gardening and botanical study.
