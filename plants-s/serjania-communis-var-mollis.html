Serjania communis var. mollis

Serjania communis var. mollis, commonly known as the velvet serjania, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its smooth, velvety leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by edible, orange-yellow fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and shaded landscapes.
