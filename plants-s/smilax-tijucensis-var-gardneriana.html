Smilax tijucensis var. gardneriana

Smilax tijucensis var. gardneriana, commonly known as Gardner's smilax, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with woody stems and sharp, spiny leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered in spadices, typical of the Araceae family.

It is often found in humid, shaded environments such as forests and undergrowth.
