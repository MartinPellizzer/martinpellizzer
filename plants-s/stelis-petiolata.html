Stelis petiolata

Stelis petiolata, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, often pendulous stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as forest undergrowth. Its leaves are usually elongated and fleshy, adapted to store water.

Stelis petiolata is prized in horticulture for its delicate appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
