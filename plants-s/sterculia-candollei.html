Sterculia candollei

Sterculia candollei, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy, flame-colored flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces woody pods containing flat, winged seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The leaves are typically large and palmately lobed, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
