Spodiopogon angustifolius

Spodiopogon angustifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spikerush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland environments in Asia.

It features slender, cylindrical stems that are often reddish in color and bear small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically arranged in two rows along the stem. Its flowers are unisexual, with male and female flowers occurring on separate spikes.

Spodiopogon angustifolius plays an important ecological role in stabilizing wetland soils and providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
