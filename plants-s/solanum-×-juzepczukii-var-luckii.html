Solanum × juzepczukii var. luckii

Solanum × juzepczukii var. luckii, commonly known as the Luckii nightshade, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and oval-shaped leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into berries, which are typically red when mature. It is native to parts of Central America and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses for its unique foliage and flowering habit.
