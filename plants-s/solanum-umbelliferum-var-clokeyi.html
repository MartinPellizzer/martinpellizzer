Solanum umbelliferum var. clokeyi

Solanum umbelliferum var. clokeyi, commonly known as the Clokey's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Australia.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades and tomatoes. The plant is characterized by its small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue in color. It produces small, toxic berries that are often mistaken for edible fruits.

Due to its toxic properties, it is important to handle the plant with care.
