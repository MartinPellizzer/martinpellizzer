Senecio tredecimsquamosus

Senecio tredecimsquamosus, commonly known as the thirteen-scaly senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features a stout, upright stem covered in dense, woolly hairs. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and also covered in a thick layer of fine, white pubescence.

The flowers are yellow and arranged in a cluster, making it an attractive addition to rock gardens.
