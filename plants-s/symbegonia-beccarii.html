Symbegonia beccarii

Symbegonia beccarii, commonly known as the Bornean arrowhead, is a species of flowering plant native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a glossy surface, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Symbegonia beccarii is typically found growing along riverbanks and in dense undergrowth.

Its ornamental value has led to its cultivation in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
