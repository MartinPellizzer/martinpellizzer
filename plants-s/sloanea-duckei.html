Sloanea duckei

Sloanea duckei, commonly known as the white sloanea, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers clustered in cyathia, which are typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. Sloanea duckei is also known for its ornamental value, particularly due to its attractive, waxy white flowers.
