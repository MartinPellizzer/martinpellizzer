Staurogyne athroantha

Staurogyne athroantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved waterplantain, is a species of aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in shallow freshwater habitats such as ponds, lakes, and slow-moving streams. The plant has narrow, ribbon-like leaves that are often submerged, with small, inconspicuous flowers that appear in clusters. Staurogyne athroantha plays a role in water filtration and supports biodiversity in its ecosystem.
