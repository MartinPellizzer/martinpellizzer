Schinus huigan var. subtridentata

Schinus huigan var. subtridentata, commonly known as the Brazilian pepper tree, is a flowering plant in the Anacardiaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, red berries.
