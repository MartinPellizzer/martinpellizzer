Sanicula officinalis

Sanicula officinalis, commonly known as European cow-wheat, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in umbels. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas such as woodlands and stream banks. It has a long taproot and is often found in temperate regions of Europe and western Asia.

Sanicula officinalis is known for its medicinal properties and has been used historically in traditional herbal remedies.
