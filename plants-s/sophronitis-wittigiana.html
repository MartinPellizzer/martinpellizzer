Sophronitis wittigiana

Sophronitis wittigiana, commonly known as the Wittig's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Sophronitis wittigiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
