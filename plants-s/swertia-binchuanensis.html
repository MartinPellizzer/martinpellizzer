Swertia binchuanensis

Swertia binchuanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan swertia, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan, China.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its slender stems and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Swertia binchuanensis is valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
