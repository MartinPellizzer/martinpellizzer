Salsola kopetdaghensis

Salsola kopetdaghensis, commonly known as the Kopetdagh saltbush, is a flowering plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and small, greenish flowers. Native to arid and semi-arid regions, this plant is well adapted to harsh, saline environments. It thrives in sandy soils and is often found in desert and coastal areas.

Salsola kopetdaghensis plays a role in stabilizing soils and providing forage for livestock in its native habitat.
