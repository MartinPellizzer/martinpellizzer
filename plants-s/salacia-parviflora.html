Salacia parviflora

Salacia parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered salacia, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Salaciacae and is characterized by its delicate, small white or yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments within tropical rainforests. Its leaves are usually ovate or elliptical with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Salacia parviflora is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its attractive foliage and fragrant blooms.
