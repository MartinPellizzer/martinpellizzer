Scutellaria speciosa

Scutellaria speciosa, commonly known as the showy skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers and heart-shaped leaves. It typically grows in moist woodlands and thrives in partial shade.

Scutellaria speciosa is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
