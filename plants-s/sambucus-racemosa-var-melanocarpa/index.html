Sambucus Racemosa Var. Melanocarpa

Sambucus racemosa var. melanocarpa is a striking native plant known for its vibrant red berries and ornamental value. This variety of elderberry thrives in moist woodlands and is commonly found across eastern North America. Its clusters of dark purple fruit make it a favorite among birds and wildlife. The plant is also valued for its medicinal properties and historical use by indigenous peoples. With its attractive foliage and seasonal fruit, it adds both beauty and ecological benefit to the landscape.
