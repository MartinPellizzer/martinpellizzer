Schefflera bequaertii

Schefflera bequaertii, commonly known as the Bequaert's umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers arranged in panicles, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in warm, humid conditions and requires well-draining soil.

Schefflera bequaertii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and compact growth habit.
