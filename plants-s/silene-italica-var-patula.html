Silene italica var. patula

Silene italica var. patula, commonly known as Italian catchfly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pinkish-red flowers that bloom in summer. Its flowers are arranged in dense, spike-like clusters and are pollinated primarily by insects.

Silene italica var. patula is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides, thriving in full sun to partial shade.
