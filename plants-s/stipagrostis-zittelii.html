Stipagrostis zittelii

Stipagrostis zittelii, commonly known as the Zittel's grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environmental conditions. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its botanical structure is characterized by a robust root system that aids in water retention.

Stipagrostis zittelii plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local ecosystems in its native habitat.
