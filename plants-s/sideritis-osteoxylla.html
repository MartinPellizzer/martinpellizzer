Sideritis osteoxylla

Sideritis osteoxylla, commonly known as Greek violet or mountain violet, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, violet-blue flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Sideritis osteoxylla is often found in rocky or limestone soils and is valued for its medicinal properties.
