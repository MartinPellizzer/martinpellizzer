Sisymbrium fluviatile

Sisymbrium fluviatile, commonly known as water pennycress, is an annual herbaceous plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It typically grows in moist environments such as riverbanks, wet meadows, and marshes. The plant has alternate, pinnately divided leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. Its fruit is a flat, pod-like structure containing several seeds.

Water pennycress is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in disturbed or water-logged soils.
