Senecio triangularis

Senecio triangularis, commonly known as the triangleweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features narrow, triangular leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in spring and summer.

Senecio triangularis is adapted to arid environments and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions.
