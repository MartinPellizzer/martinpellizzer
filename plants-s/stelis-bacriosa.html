Stelis bacriosa

Stelis bacriosa, commonly known as the Bacriosa orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features a short, rhizomatous stem with erect, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

Stelis bacriosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
