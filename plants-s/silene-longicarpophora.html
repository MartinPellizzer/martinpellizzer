Silene longicarpophora

Silene longicarpophora, commonly known as the long-fruited campion, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is native to regions of Europe and Asia, typically found in grassy areas and woodland edges. The plant produces showy pink or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its fruit is a distinctive, elongated capsule that splits open to release seeds.
