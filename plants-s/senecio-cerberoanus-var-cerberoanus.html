Senecio cerberoanus var. cerberoanus

Senecio cerberoanus var. cerberoanus, commonly known as the Cerberus groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial with succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically green or bluish-gray in color. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in spring and early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
