Sinobambusa laeta

Sinobambusa laeta, commonly known as the silver bamboo, is a species of grass belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to eastern China and is characterized by its distinctive silvery-white sheathes on its stems. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions. Its slender, hollow culms can reach heights of up to 3 meters, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in early summer.

Sinobambusa laeta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
