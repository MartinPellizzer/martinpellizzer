Sideritis hyssopifolia f. elongata

Sideritis hyssopifolia f. elongata, commonly known as the elongated heath, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that are often branched and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or violet in color, arranged in dense spike-like clusters. Its leaves are narrow, linear, and aromatic, with a slightly fuzzy texture.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens or as a border plant.
