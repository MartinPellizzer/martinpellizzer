Schismatoglottis caulescens

Schismatoglottis caulescens, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its upright, slender stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, typical of many orchid species. It is often grown as a decorative plant due to its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.

Schismatoglottis caulescens prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
