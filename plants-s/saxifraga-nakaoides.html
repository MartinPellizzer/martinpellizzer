Saxifraga nakaoides

Saxifraga nakaoides, commonly known as the Japanese saxifrage, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Saxifragaceae and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white, with five petals. It thrives in rocky or shady environments, often found in mountainous regions.

Saxifraga nakaoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
