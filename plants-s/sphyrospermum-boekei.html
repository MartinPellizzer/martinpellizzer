Sphyrospermum boekei

Sphyrospermum boekei, commonly known as the African milk tree, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a thick, woody stem with smooth, grayish bark and produces small, white, star-shaped flowers. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that store water, making it well adapted to arid environments. It is also known for its milky sap, which is toxic and can cause skin irritation.

Sphyrospermum boekei is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience.
