Schefflera maguireorum

Schefflera maguireorum, commonly known as the Maguire's umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of large, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Schefflera maguireorum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor growing conditions.
