Stachys bikramii

Stachys bikramii, commonly known as the Nepal hedge-nettle, is a perennial herb native to the Himalayan regions of Nepal and northern India.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has hairy, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, clustered in whorls, and are usually pink or purple in color.

Stachys bikramii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
