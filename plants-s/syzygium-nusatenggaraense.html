Syzygium nusatenggaraense

Syzygium nusatenggaraense, commonly known as the Nusatenggara apple.

It is a small evergreen tree native to the Moluccas in Indonesia, characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits with a sweet and tart flavor, often used in local cuisines. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Syzygium nusatenggaraense is also valued for its ornamental qualities and potential medicinal uses.
