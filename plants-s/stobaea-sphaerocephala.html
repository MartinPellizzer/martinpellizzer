Stobaea sphaerocephala

Stobaea sphaerocephala, commonly known as the globe-headed everlasting.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, characterized by its dense, spherical inflorescence composed of numerous small, showy flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its long-lasting blooms, which can persist for several months. Its compact growth habit and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
