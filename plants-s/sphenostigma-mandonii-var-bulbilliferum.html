Sphenostigma mandonii var. bulbilliferum

Sphenostigma mandonii var. bulbilliferum, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette of velvety, waxy leaves that are typically green or purple in color. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of violet, pink, and white, which bloom from the center of the leaf rosette. It is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
