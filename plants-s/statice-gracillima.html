Statice gracillima

Statice gracillima, commonly known as slender sea lavender, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of southern Europe and North Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, blue to purple flowers and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant thrives in salty, well-drained soils and is often found in dunes and coastal grasslands. It blooms from late summer to early autumn, adding a splash of color to the shoreline.

Statice gracillima is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate harsh coastal conditions.
