Stachys truncata

Stachys truncata, commonly known as narrow-leaved betony, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features square stems and opposite leaves that are typically narrow and truncate at the tip. Its flowers are arranged in whorls along the stem and are usually pink or purple in color.

Stachys truncata is often found in grasslands, woodland edges, and disturbed soils.
