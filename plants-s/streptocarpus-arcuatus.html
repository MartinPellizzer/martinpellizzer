Streptocarpus arcuatus

Streptocarpus arcuatus, commonly known as the Cape bell, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. Streptocarpus arcuatus is a popular choice for hanging baskets and as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
