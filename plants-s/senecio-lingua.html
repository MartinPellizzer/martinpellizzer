Senecio lingua

Senecio lingua, commonly known as the tongue plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, tongue-shaped leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Senecio lingua is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements.
