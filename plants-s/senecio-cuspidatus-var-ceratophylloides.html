Senecio cuspidatus var. ceratophylloides

Senecio cuspidatus var. ceratophylloides, commonly known as the horned senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, sharply pointed leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces yellow flowers in dense clusters, typically during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a notable addition to botanical collections.
