Sedum tsugaruense

Sedum tsugaruense, commonly known as the Tsugaru sedum, is a succulent plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped yellow flowers in the summer months. It is well adapted to rocky and sunny environments, often growing in coastal areas.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
