Specklinia cordilabia

Specklinia cordilabia, commonly known as the heart-leaf staghorn fern, is a unique epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Polypodiaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped fronds that resemble a heart. Specklinia cordilabia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
