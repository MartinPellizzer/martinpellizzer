Stauracanthus genistoides f. spartioides

Stauracanthus genistoides f. spartioides, commonly known as the spiny broom.

It is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region, characterized by its small, yellow flowers and thorny stems. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for rocky or arid landscapes.

Its botanical features make it a hardy and ornamental plant often used in xeriscaping.
