Stelis marioi

Stelis marioi, commonly known as the Mario's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces strikingly beautiful flowers with a white and pink coloration. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its flowers are usually arranged in racemes and bloom during the summer months.

Stelis marioi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
