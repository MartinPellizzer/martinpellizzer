Scottia

Scottia, commonly known as the African daisy, is a genus of flowering plants native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other similar plants. The genus comprises several species, most of which are perennial herbs with showy, daisy-like flowers. Scottia plants typically have basal leaves and are often cultivated for their ornamental value.

They thrive in well-drained soil and full sun, making them popular in gardens and landscapes.
