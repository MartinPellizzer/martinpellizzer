Setaria nigrirostris

Setaria nigrirostris, commonly known as black-beaked foxtail, is a grass species native to Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous spikelets. Its seeds are typically dark brown and have a distinctive, elongated beak-like structure.

Setaria nigrirostris is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
