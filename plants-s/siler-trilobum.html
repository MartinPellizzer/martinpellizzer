Siler trilobum

Siler trilobum, commonly known as blue cohosh, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, blue or purple flowers. The plant produces berries that are typically red or black when mature. It thrives in woodland habitats and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.
