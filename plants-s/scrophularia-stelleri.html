Scrophularia stelleri

Scrophularia stelleri, commonly known as Steller's scrophularia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central Asia.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and showy, two-lipped flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, often at high elevations. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, and it blooms during the summer months.

Scrophularia stelleri is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
