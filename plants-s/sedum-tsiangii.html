Sedum tsiangii

Sedum tsiangii, commonly known as the Chinese stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to China.

It features fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that are typically green or reddish in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It is adapted to grow in rocky or arid environments, making it drought-tolerant and easy to care for.

Sedum tsiangii is often used in rock gardens and as a decorative plant due to its hardy nature and aesthetic appeal.
