Setaria penicillata

Setaria penicillata, commonly known as slender dropseed, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as prairies, roadsides, and disturbed soils. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are often tinged with red or purple. It is characterized by its ability to thrive in dry conditions and its tolerance to various soil types.

Setaria penicillata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in supporting local wildlife.
