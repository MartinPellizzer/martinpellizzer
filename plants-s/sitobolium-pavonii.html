Sitobolium pavonii

Sitobolium pavonii, commonly known as the rainbow grass, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and vibrant, colorful inflorescences that range from red to purple. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for ornamental gardening.

Sitobolium pavonii is also valued for its ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators and supporting local biodiversity.
