Setaria polystachya

Setaria polystachya, commonly known as yellow foxtail, is a grass species native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and dense, spike-like inflorescences that bear small, golden-yellow flowers. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in sunny, well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Setaria polystachya is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
