Sedum quinquefidum

Sedum quinquefidum, commonly known as the five-lobed stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow to orange. The leaves are typically green and arranged in a rosette pattern, helping the plant store water efficiently. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

This hardy species is often used in landscaping due to its low maintenance and ornamental value.
