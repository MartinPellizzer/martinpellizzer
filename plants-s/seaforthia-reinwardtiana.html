Seaforthia reinwardtiana

Seaforthia reinwardtiana, commonly known as the African orchid, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has a short, stout stem with narrow, grass-like leaves, and its flowers are borne in racemes. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Seaforthia reinwardtiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
