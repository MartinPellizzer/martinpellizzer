Stellaria autumnalis

Stellaria autumnalis, commonly known as autumn starwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in the fall, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, temperate regions and has a low-growing, spreading habit. Its leaves are usually oval or lance-shaped, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Stellaria autumnalis is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and along streambanks, where it thrives in well-drained soil.
