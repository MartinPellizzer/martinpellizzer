Swertia alternifolia

Swertia alternifolia, commonly known as the alternate leaf swertia, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Gentianaceae family.

It is characterized by its alternate arrangement of leaves, which are typically lanceolate and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from blue to yellow, often with prominent blue or yellow veins. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, commonly found in mountainous regions of Asia.

Swertia alternifolia is noted for its medicinal properties, traditionally used in herbal remedies for its purported health benefits.
