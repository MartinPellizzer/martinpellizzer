Senecio salzmannii

Senecio salzmannii, commonly known as Salzmann's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It produces small yellow flowers that are surrounded by leaf-like structures called phyllaries.

Senecio salzmannii is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and shrublands.
