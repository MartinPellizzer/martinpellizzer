Spergularia rubra

Spergularia rubra, commonly known as sea sandspurry, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that thrives in coastal and sandy environments. The plant produces small, pink to red flowers that bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Spergularia rubra is adapted to saline and well-drained soils, making it a common sight in dune ecosystems.
