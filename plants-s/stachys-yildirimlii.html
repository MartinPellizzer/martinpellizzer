Stachys yildirimlii

Stachys yildirimlii, commonly known as the Turkish hedge parsley, is a perennial herb native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale purple or white, arranged in whorls along the stem. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture.

Stachys yildirimlii is often found in rocky or dry habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.
