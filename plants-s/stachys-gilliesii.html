Stachys gilliesii

Stachys gilliesii, commonly known as the gillies' hedge parsley, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture and produces spikes of small, white to pale purple flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.

Stachys gilliesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
