Steudnera griffithii

Steudnera griffithii, commonly known as the golden arrowroot, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

Steudnera griffithii thrives in humid, shady conditions and requires well-drained, fertile soil to flourish.
