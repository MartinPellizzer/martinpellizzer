Salacia nipensis

Salacia nipensis, commonly known as the golden rain tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a vibrant appearance. The plant is a deciduous shrub or small tree, typically growing up to 10 meters in height. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly waxy texture.

Salacia nipensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its showy floral display.
