Sideritis × varoi subsp. berniensis

Sideritis × varoi subsp.

berniensis, commonly known as the Balkan germander, is a perennial herb native to the Balkan Peninsula. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and aromatic leaves.

The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils, often in open woodlands or grasslands. Its small, tubular flowers are usually pink or violet, and it blooms from late spring to early summer. Sideritis × varoi subsp.

berniensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
