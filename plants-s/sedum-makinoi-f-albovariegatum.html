Sedum makinoi f. albovariegatum

Sedum makinoi f. albovariegatum, commonly known as the white-variegated stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Japan.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically green with white or yellow markings, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the summer months. It is well adapted to rocky and sunny environments, making it a hardy ornamental plant.

Its variegation pattern adds visual interest, making it popular among gardeners and plant enthusiasts.
