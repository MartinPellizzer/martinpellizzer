Stylogyne ardisioides

Stylogyne ardisioides, commonly known as the red-flowered false ardisia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that are followed by bright red berries, which are attractive to birds. Stylogyne ardisioides thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm climates.
