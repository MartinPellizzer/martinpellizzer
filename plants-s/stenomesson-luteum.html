Stenomesson luteum

Stenomesson luteum, commonly known as the yellow stenomesson, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces bright yellow ray florets surrounded by a ring of tubular disk florets. It typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in full sun.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to both ecological and horticultural landscapes.
