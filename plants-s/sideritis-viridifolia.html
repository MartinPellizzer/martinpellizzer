Sideritis viridifolia

Sideritis viridifolia, commonly known as greenleaf sainfoin, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its upright stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Sideritis viridifolia is known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
