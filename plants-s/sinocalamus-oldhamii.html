Sinocalamus oldhamii

Sinocalamus oldhamii, commonly known as the Chinese bamboo or oldham's bamboo, is a tall, slender grass-like plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its hollow, jointed stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. This plant is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its rhizomes form dense clumps, allowing it to spread vegetatively.

Sinocalamus oldhamii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic qualities in gardens.
