Sempervivum simonkaianum

Sempervivum simonkaianum, commonly known as the Simonkaian houseleek, is a succulent plant native to the Alps.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically fleshy and covered in a thin layer of white wax. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it ideal for rock gardens or container planting.

Its hardy nature allows it to survive in a variety of climates, including colder regions.
