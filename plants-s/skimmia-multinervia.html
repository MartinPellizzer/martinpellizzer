Skimmia multinervia

Skimmia multinervia, commonly known as the Chinese skimmia, is a evergreen shrub native to China and Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green elliptical leaves with prominent veins, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters during late winter to early spring. These flowers are followed by red berries that persist through the winter, adding visual interest.

Skimmia multinervia is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
