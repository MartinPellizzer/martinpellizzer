Scutellaria spectabilis

Scutellaria spectabilis, commonly known as showy skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the central and eastern United States.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant produces striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs.

Scutellaria spectabilis is typically found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.
