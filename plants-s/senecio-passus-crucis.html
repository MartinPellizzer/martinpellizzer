Senecio passus-crucis

Senecio passus-crucis, commonly known as the Cross-leaved Groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features linear, cross-shaped leaves that give it its common name. It produces small yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer.

Senecio passus-crucis is typically found in open, sandy or rocky habitats.
