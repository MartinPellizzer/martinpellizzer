Swainsona rigida

Swainsona rigida, commonly known as the rigid swainsona, is a perennial shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its woody stems and compound leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, pea-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Swainsona rigida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
