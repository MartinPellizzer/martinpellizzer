Scrophularia bianoris

Scrophularia bianoris, commonly known as the white scrophularia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae, which includes many species with showy, often tubular flowers. Scrophularia bianoris is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for various purposes.
