Syzygium tenuiflorum var. brevipes

Syzygium tenuiflorum var. brevipes, commonly known as the slender-flowered clove.

It is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The flowers are typically pink or white and are known for their aromatic properties.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic benefits.
