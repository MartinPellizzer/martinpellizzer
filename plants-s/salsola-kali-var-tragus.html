Salsola kali var. tragus

Salsola kali var. tragus, commonly known as Russian thistle or goatweed, is a flowering plant in the family Amaranthaceae.

It is an annual or biennial herb that typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions. The plant has a robust, sprawling growth habit with spiny stems and small, fleshy leaves. Its flowers are clustered in dense, pink to purplish inflorescences, and it produces numerous seeds that are dispersed by wind.

It is often considered a weed but has adapted well to disturbed soils and harsh environments.
