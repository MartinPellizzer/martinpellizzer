Schmaltzia trilobata var. pilosissima

Schmaltzia trilobata var. pilosissima, commonly known as the velvety schmaltzia, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has hairy, glandular leaves that give it a distinctive, velvety texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and flowers.
