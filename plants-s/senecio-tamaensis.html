Senecio tamaensis

Senecio tamaensis, commonly known as Tama's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Senecio tamaensis is often found in alpine meadows and is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
