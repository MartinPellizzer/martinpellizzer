Spartium multiflorum var. incarnatum

Spartium multiflorum var. incarnatum, commonly known as crimson tawny, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial shrub that features slender, woody stems and small, clustered flowers. The flowers are typically pink to reddish in color, which gives the plant its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
