Solanum splendens

Solanum splendens, commonly known as the golden trumpet vine, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a member of the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped yellow flowers. The plant is a vigorous climber with long, trailing stems that can reach several meters in length. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Solanum splendens thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
