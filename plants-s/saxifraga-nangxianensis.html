Saxifraga nangxianensis

Saxifraga nangxianensis, commonly known as the Nangxian saxifrage, is a rare flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, mountainous environments, where it thrives in well-drained soil. Its flowering period occurs in the spring, producing white or pale pink blooms.
