Scutellaria platensis

Scutellaria platensis, commonly known as the Mexican skullcap, is a perennial herb native to Central America and parts of the southern United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces violet or blue flowers in whorled clusters. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Scutellaria platensis is valued for its potential medicinal properties, though further research is needed to confirm its therapeutic uses.
