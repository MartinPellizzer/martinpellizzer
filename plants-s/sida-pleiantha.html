Sida pleiantha

Sida pleiantha, commonly known as velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Sida pleiantha is also known for its ability to spread rapidly, making it a potential weed in agricultural settings.
