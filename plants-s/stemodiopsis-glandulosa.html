Stemodiopsis glandulosa

Stemodiopsis glandulosa, commonly known as the glandular stemodia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems covered in small, glandular hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters along the stems. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy surface.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland habitats.
