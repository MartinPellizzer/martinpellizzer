Sparganium barrerae

Sparganium barrerae, commonly known as the Barrera's bur reed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to wetland habitats in Central America, particularly in Mexico. The plant is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems that are often covered in spiny bracts. Its flowers are small and clustered in spikelets, typical of the Cyperaceae family.

Sparganium barrerae plays a role in stabilizing wetland soils and providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
