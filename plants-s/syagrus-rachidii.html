Syagrus rachidii

Syagrus rachidii, commonly known as the royal palm, is a species of palm native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that give it a striking appearance. The plant produces clusters of yellowish-white flowers, which are followed by elongated, spiky fruits. Syagrus rachidii thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, requiring well-drained soil and full sunlight.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its elegant form and resilience.
