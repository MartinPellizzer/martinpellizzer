Sesamum macranthum

Sesamum macranthum, commonly known as the large-flowered sesame, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain small, elliptical seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Sesamum macranthum is primarily cultivated for its ornamental value rather than its seeds.
