Salvia keitaoensis

Salvia keitaoensis, commonly known as Keitao sage, is a species of flowering plant in the Lamiaceae family.

It is native to China, specifically the Yunnan Province, where it grows in moist, forested areas. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit, hairy stems, and showy violet to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are typically opposite, ovate, and covered in fine pubescence.

Salvia keitaoensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
