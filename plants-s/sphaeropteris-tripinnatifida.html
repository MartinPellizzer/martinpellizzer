Sphaeropteris tripinnatifida

Sphaeropteris tripinnatifida, commonly known as the three-pinnate cycad, is a member of the Cycadaceae family.

It is a tropical plant characterized by its stout, unbranched trunk and large, pinnately compound leaves. The leaves are typically dark green and have a feathery appearance, with multiple levels of division. This species is native to Southeast Asia and is often found in humid, shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ornamental plant in horticulture.
