Serjania acuta

Serjania acuta, commonly known as the sharpserjania, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Serjaniae and is characterized by its woody structure and elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that contains numerous seeds, and it is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Serjania acuta is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
