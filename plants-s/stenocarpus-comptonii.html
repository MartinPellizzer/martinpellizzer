Stenocarpus comptonii

Stenocarpus comptonii, commonly known as the New Zealand tree fern, is a member of the Cyatheaceae family.

It is native to New Zealand and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds. Stenocarpus comptonii plays an important role in the ecological systems of New Zealand's forests.
