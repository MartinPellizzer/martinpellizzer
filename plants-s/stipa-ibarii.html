Stipa ibarii

Stipa ibarii, commonly known as the Ibari needlegrass, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

Stipa ibarii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in stabilizing soils in arid environments.
