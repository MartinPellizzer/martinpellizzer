Solanum demissum f. longifilamentosum

Solanum demissum f. longifilamentosum, commonly known as the long-stemmed nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue. The leaves are ovate to elliptical, with a hairy texture, and the plant is native to South America. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance.

The fruit is a small, globose berry that turns red when mature.
