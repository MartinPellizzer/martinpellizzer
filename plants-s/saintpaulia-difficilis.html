Saintpaulia difficilis

Saintpaulia difficilis, commonly known as the Shiny Leaf African Violet, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green foliage and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in indoor gardening. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice for plant enthusiasts.
