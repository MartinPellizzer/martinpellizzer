Sauromatum ferox

Sauromatum ferox, commonly known as the giant arrowroot or devil's tongue, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, dark green, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces striking, red-orange flowers that are often surrounded by long, spiky bracts. It is a member of the Araceae family, known for its unique inflorescence structure.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is cultivated in tropical gardens and botanical collections.
