Senecio rudbeckiifolius

Senecio rudbeckiifolius, commonly known as the roughleaf senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

Senecio rudbeckiifolius is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and drought tolerance.
