Stenogonum flexum

Stenogonum flexum, commonly known as the flexible stenogonum, is a species of flowering plant in the family Acanthaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
