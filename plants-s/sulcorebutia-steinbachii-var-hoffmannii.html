Sulcorebutia steinbachii var. hoffmannii

Sulcorebutia steinbachii var. hoffmannii, commonly known as the Hoffmann's moon cactus, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It features a globular or cylindrical stem with a distinctive blue-gray or bluish-green coloration. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It has shallow, fibrous roots adapted to absorb moisture from the dry soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental appearance and relatively easy care requirements, making it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
