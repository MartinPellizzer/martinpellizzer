Sporobolus patulus var. stenothyrsus

Sporobolus patulus var. stenothyrsus, commonly known as the narrow-spike dropseed, is a perennial grass native to the United States.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils. The plant features slender, cylindrical stems with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its inflorescence is a loose panicle composed of small, spikelet-like flowers.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in dry conditions.
