Stelis smaragdina

Stelis smaragdina, commonly known as the emerald stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking emerald-green leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically blooming during the summer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Stelis smaragdina is often cultivated for its ornamental value in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
