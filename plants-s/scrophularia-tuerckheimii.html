Scrophularia tuerckheimii

Scrophularia tuerckheimii, commonly known as the Tuerckheim's figwort, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae, which includes many species with showy flowers and medicinal uses. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high elevations, often in alpine environments.
