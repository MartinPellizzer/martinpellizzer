Sideritis linearifolia

Sideritis linearifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaved ironwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats.

Sideritis linearifolia is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
