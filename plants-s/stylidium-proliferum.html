Stylidium proliferum

Stylidium proliferum, commonly known as the prolific spurred orchid, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique spurred petals. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils in arid or semi-arid regions. Its small, delicate flowers are usually pink or purple, and it produces numerous seed capsules.

Stylidium proliferum is known for its ability to thrive in harsh environments, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
