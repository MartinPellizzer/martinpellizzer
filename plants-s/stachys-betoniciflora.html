Stachys betoniciflora

Stachys betoniciflora, commonly known as betony leaf hedge parsley, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and have a distinctive minty aroma. Its flowers are small, clustered in whorls, and range in color from white to pale purple.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges and rocky habitats.
