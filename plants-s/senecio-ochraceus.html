Senecio ochraceus

Senecio ochraceus, commonly known as the Orange Milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers and linear, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Senecio ochraceus is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
