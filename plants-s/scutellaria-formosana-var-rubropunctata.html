Scutellaria formosana var. rubropunctata

Scutellaria formosana var. rubropunctata, commonly known as the red-spotted skullcap, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of blue to purple tubular flowers. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture.

This variety is distinguished by the presence of red spots on its leaves, which give it its common name.
