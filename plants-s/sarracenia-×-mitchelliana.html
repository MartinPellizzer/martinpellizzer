Sarracenia × mitchelliana

Sarracenia × mitchelliana, commonly known as Mitchell's Sarracenia, is a hybrid carnivorous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its tall, upright pitchers that can reach up to 30 inches in height, often adorned with red and green striped patterns. The plant thrives in wet, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth. Its pitchers are capable of trapping and digesting insects, providing essential nutrients in nutrient-poor environments.

This species is popular among carnivorous plant enthusiasts for its striking appearance and hardy nature.
