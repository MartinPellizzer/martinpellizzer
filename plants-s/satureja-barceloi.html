Satureja barceloi

Satureja barceloi, commonly known as Spanish thyme, is a perennial herb native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or calcareous soils and thrives in sunny environments. Its small, pink or purple flowers bloom in summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Satureja barceloi is valued for its fragrant leaves, which are used in culinary and traditional medicinal applications.
