Solanum peikuoense

Solanum peikuoense, commonly known as the Peikuo nightshade, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and oval-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions. Its berries are toxic and contain alkaloids, making it a plant of both botanical and ecological interest.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare species within its native habitat.
