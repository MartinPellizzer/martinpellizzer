Senna dariensis var. smaragdina

Senna dariensis var. smaragdina, commonly known as the Emerald Senna, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its pinnately compound leaves, which are often bright green and glossy. The plant produces yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes, typical of the Fabaceae family. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed or open habitats.

The leaves and flowers are used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
