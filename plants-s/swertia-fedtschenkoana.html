Swertia fedtschenkoana

Swertia fedtschenkoana, commonly known as the Himalayan gentian, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, thriving at elevations between 3,000 and 5,000 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in a fine layer of hair, while its stems are erect and branching.

Swertia fedtschenkoana is also noted for its medicinal properties, traditionally used in local herbal remedies.
