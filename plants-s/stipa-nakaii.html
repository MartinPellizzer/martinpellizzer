Stipa nakaii

Stipa nakaii, commonly known as the Nakai's feathergrass, is a perennial grass native to eastern Asia, particularly found in regions of China, Korea, and Japan.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and dense, feathery plumes that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue, and it produces small, spike-like inflorescences.

Stipa nakaii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in landscaping and restoration projects.
