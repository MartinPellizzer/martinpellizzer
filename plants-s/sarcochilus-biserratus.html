Sarcochilus biserratus

Sarcochilus biserratus, commonly known as the serrated helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It features a dense, leafy stem with broad, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces striking, helmet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which are typically borne on a single inflorescence. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Sarcochilus biserratus is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
