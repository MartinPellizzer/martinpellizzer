Salvia hasankeyfensis

Salvia hasankeyfensis, commonly known as the Hasankeyf sage, is a perennial flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces vibrant blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
