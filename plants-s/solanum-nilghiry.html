Solanum nilghiry

Solanum nilghiry, commonly known as the Nilgiri tomato, is a herbaceous plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its hairy stems and ovate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into red, cherry-like fruits. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas at higher altitudes.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
