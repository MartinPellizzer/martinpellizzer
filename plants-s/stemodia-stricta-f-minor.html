Stemodia stricta f. minor

Stemodia stricta f. minor, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed stemodia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, daisy-like flowers that typically bloom in the summer months. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It is native to parts of southern Africa and thrives in well-drained soils.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its delicate appearance and ease of care.
