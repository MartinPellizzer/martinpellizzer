Senecio prionites

Senecio prionites, commonly known as the Cape York Senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas. It is a perennial herb that thrives in full sun and is relatively drought-tolerant.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
