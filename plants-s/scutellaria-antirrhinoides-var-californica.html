Scutellaria antirrhinoides var. californica

Scutellaria antirrhinoides var. californica, commonly known as California skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is found in regions such as California and Oregon. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, tubular blue or violet flowers. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic foliage.

This species is often used in native gardens due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
