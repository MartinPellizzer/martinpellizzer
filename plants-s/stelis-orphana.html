Stelis orphana

Stelis orphana, commonly known as the Orchidine Stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest understories. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. Its flowers are usually small and delicate, with a distinctive coloration that varies by species.

Stelis orphana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
