Sphaerolobium grandiflorum

Sphaerolobium grandiflorum, commonly known as the largeflower burr legume, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces showy, yellow to orange flowers. Its fruit is a hairy, spiny pod that contains several seeds.
