Solanum stylesinum

Solanum stylesinum, commonly known as the purple leaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its striking purple foliage and showy, violet to blue flowers. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, with hairy stems and alternate leaves. Its fruit is a small, spherical berry that turns from green to red as it matures, containing several seeds.

Solanum stylesinum is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant appearance and unique foliage.
