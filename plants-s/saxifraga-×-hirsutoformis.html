Saxifraga × hirsutoformis

Saxifraga × hirsutoformis, commonly known as the wooly saxifrage, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Saxifragaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. Native to the mountainous regions of Europe, it thrives in rocky and well-drained soils. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in early summer.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
