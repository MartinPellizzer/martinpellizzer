Stellaria turkestanica

Stellaria turkestanica, commonly known as the Turkestan starwort, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, star-shaped white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, with a hairy texture.

Stellaria turkestanica is noted for its adaptability to harsh environmental conditions.
