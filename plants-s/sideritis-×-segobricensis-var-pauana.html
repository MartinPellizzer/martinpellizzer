Sideritis × segobricensis var. pauana

Sideritis × segobricensis var. pauana, commonly known as the Pauan Sideritis, is a hybrid shrub native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic leaves and showy flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in temperate climates. Its flowering period occurs in late summer, producing clusters of pink or purple tubular flowers.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
