Staurogyne setigera

Staurogyne setigera, commonly known as the slender waterwheel plant, is a species of aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its whorled arrangement of leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant typically grows in shallow, still or slow-moving freshwater habitats. Its leaves are narrow and often have a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss.
