Stachytarpheta gesnerioides var. simplex

Stachytarpheta gesnerioides var. simplex, commonly known as the narrowleaf ironweed, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It features slender, opposite leaves that are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pale violet, attracting various pollinators. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value lies in its delicate foliage and showy floral displays.
