Selinum ammoides

Selinum ammoides, commonly known as goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or dry soils. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in a dense, feathery cluster. It is often used in ornamental gardening due to its delicate appearance.
