Solena heterophylla subsp. heterophylla

Solena heterophylla subsp.

heterophylla, commonly known as the variable leaf solena, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its variable leaf morphology, with leaves ranging from ovate to linear in shape depending on environmental conditions.

The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowers are small, clustered in inflorescences, and are usually pale blue or white in color.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to various garden conditions.
