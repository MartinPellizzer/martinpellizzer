Scolymocephalus acerosus

Scolymocephalus acerosus, commonly known as the hard-leaved scolymus, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branched stems covered in stiff, glandular hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense clusters at the ends of the branches. Its leaves are narrow, leathery, and have a distinctive serrated margin.

Scolymocephalus acerosus is adapted to rocky and well-drained soils, often growing in dry, sunny environments.
