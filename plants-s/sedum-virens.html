Sedum virens

Sedum virens, commonly known as the green sedum, is a succulent plant native to North America.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped yellow flowers in late spring to early summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Sedum virens is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion in arid environments.
