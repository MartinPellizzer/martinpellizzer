Sedum Telephium Var. Praeruptorum

Sedum telephium var. praeruptorum is a unique and striking plant known for its vibrant red flowers and succulent foliage. Native to rocky and alpine environments, it thrives in harsh conditions where few other plants can survive. This variant of Sedum telephium is particularly prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to different climates. Its ability to store water in its thick leaves makes it a low-maintenance choice for gardeners. With its bold appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular selection for rock gardens and drought-tolerant landscapes.
