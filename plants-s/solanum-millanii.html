Solanum millanii

Solanum millanii, commonly known as the millanii nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are often followed by red, fleshy berries. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with a slightly hairy texture.

The species is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive appearance.
