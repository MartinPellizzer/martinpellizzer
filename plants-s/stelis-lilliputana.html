Stelis lilliputana

Stelis lilliputana, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a small epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on tree branches or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant has slender, wiry stems that are often covered in a fine layer of white or pale green hairs. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and usually white or pale yellow, appearing in clusters.

Due to its delicate nature and specific habitat requirements, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
