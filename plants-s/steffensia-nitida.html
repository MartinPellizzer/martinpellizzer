Steffensia nitida

Steffensia nitida, commonly known as the silverleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, silvery-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by fluffy, white seed heads. Steffensia nitida is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
