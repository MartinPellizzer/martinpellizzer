Spathodea pisoniana

Spathodea pisoniana, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a dramatic appearance. The plant has large, compound leaves that are alternate and often have a rough texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Spathodea pisoniana is also known for its rapid growth and ability to adapt to various soil types.
