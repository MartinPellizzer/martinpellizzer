Senecio hortensiae

Senecio hortensiae, commonly known as the blue-eyed daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers surrounded by white ray florets. The plant typically grows as a low, compact shrub with silvery-gray foliage. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Senecio hortensiae is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
