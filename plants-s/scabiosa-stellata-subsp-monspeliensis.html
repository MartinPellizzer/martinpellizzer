Scabiosa stellata subsp. monspeliensis

Scabiosa stellata subsp.

monspeliensis, commonly known as the Alpine scabious, is a perennial herb native to mountainous regions of Europe. It features slender, upright stems and produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, white, and purple.

The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are hairy and grayish in appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or alpine environments.

This species is valued in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
