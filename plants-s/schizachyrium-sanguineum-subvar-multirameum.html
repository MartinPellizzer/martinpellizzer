Schizachyrium sanguineum subvar. multirameum

Schizachyrium sanguineum subvar. multirameum, commonly known as red false panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It features slender, upright stems that grow up to 1 meter in height, with narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green to bluish-green in color. The plant produces showy, plume-like inflorescences that range in color from pink to red, depending on the season and environmental conditions. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in prairies and open woodlands.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing habitat and food for various wildlife.
