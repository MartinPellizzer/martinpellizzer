Stevia vaccinioides

Stevia vaccinioides, commonly known as the evergreen stevia, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its small, pale green leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is notable for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its tolerance to drought conditions. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its compact growth habit and attractive foliage.

Stevia vaccinioides is also valued for its potential medicinal properties, though further research is needed to confirm its efficacy.
