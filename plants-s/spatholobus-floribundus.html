Spatholobus floribundus

Spatholobus floribundus, commonly known as the sweet senna, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and bright yellow flowers. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions and full sun.

Spatholobus floribundus is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
