Stelis satyrella

Stelis satyrella, commonly known as the dwarf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that grows on trees and rocks in tropical rainforests. The plant has a compact growth habit with narrow, linear leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique inflorescence structure.

Due to its limited habitat and threatened status, it is often sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists.
