Stellaria monosperma var. japonica

Stellaria monosperma var. japonica, commonly known as Japanese starwort, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a low-growing herb with small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has slender, branching stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or meadow habitats.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the Caryophyllaceae family.
