Stizophyllum perforatum

It is characterized by its leafy shoots and the presence of perforations in its thallus, which are small holes that aid in gas exchange. Its structure consists of a central stem with closely packed, spirally arranged leaves.

Stizophyllum perforatum plays a role in nutrient cycling and provides habitat for microorganisms in its ecosystem.
