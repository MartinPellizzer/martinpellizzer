Scabiosa japonica subsp. tschiliensis

Scabiosa japonica subsp.

tschiliensis, commonly known as the Chinese ladies' mantle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia. It features a rosette of velvety, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers with a distinctive, slightly hairy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and delicate floral display.
