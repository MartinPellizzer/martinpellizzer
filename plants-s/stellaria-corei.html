Stellaria corei

Stellaria corei, commonly known as the Korean starwort, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, temperate regions and has slender, branching stems. Its leaves are opposite and lanceolate, with a slightly hairy surface.

Stellaria corei is often found in meadows and along stream banks, where it thrives in partial shade to full sun.
