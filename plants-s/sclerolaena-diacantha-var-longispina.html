Sclerolaena diacantha var. longispina

Sclerolaena diacantha var. longispina, commonly known as the longispine sclerolaena, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features woody stems and lance-shaped leaves with a rough, hairy texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds.

It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
