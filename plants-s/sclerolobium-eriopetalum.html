Sclerolobium eriopetalum

Sclerolobium eriopetalum, commonly known as the silky acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving the plant its common name. The flowers are typically yellow or orange and are clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
