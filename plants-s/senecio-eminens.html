Senecio eminens

Senecio eminens, commonly known as the African iron plant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features dense, fleshy leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, and it produces small, yellowish flowers in late summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it well-suited for arid environments.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.
