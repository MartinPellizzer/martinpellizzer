Sedum droserifolium

Sedum droserifolium, commonly known as the dewy sedum, is a succulent plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a layer of fine, hair-like structures. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is adapted to withstand harsh climatic conditions.

Its unique morphology makes it an interesting subject for botanical study.
