Solidago milleriana

Solidago milleriana, commonly known as the Miller's goldenrod, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Solidago milleriana is valued for its ecological role in supporting local wildlife and its ornamental appeal in wetland gardens.
