Scabiosa holosericea

Scabiosa holosericea, commonly known as the silky scabious, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, pink or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features finely hairy stems and leaves, which contribute to its distinctive, fluffy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Scabiosa holosericea is also valued for its role in supporting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
