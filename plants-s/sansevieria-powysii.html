Sansevieria powysii

Sansevieria powysii, commonly known as the Powys sword, is a striking succulent native to South Africa.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are dark green with prominent white or yellow marginal bands. The plant is highly drought-tolerant and thrives in low-light conditions, making it an ideal indoor plant. It produces small, inconspicuous white flowers on slender stalks during the growing season.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements have made it a popular choice for both ornamental and decorative purposes.
