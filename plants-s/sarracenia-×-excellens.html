Sarracenia × excellens

Sarracenia × excellens, commonly known as the hybrid pitcher plant, is a carnivorous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a hybrid between Sarracenia leucophylla and Sarracenia rubra, resulting in a plant with distinctive, colorful, and often large pitcher-shaped leaves. The pitchers are typically red or orange with intricate patterns, designed to lure and trap insects. These plants thrive in wet, acidic soils and require bright sunlight to maintain their vibrant colors and efficient trapping mechanisms.

They are popular among carnivorous plant enthusiasts for their striking appearance and unique ecological adaptations.
