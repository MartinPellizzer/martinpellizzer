Symplocos oranjeensis

Symplocos oranjeensis, commonly known as the Orange Island beauty, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features small, elliptical leaves with a glossy green surface and produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. Its fruits are small, round berries that turn red when mature, attracting various bird species.

Symplocos oranjeensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
