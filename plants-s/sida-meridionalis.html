Sida meridionalis

Sida meridionalis, commonly known as the southern sida, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions. The plant features small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, and its leaves are generally oval with hairy surfaces. It has a fibrous root system and is often found in disturbed soils.

Sida meridionalis is known for its adaptability to harsh environmental conditions.
