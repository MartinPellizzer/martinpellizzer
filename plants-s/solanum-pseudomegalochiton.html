Solanum pseudomegalochiton

Solanum pseudomegalochiton, commonly known as the giant nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom at night. The plant features broad, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and its fruit is a fleshy, berry-like structure that changes color as it matures. Solanum pseudomegalochiton is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
