Sasa kaihunourana

Sasa kaihunourana, commonly known as the Japanese bamboo, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family.

It is native to Japan and is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can reach up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Sasa kaihunourana is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides.
