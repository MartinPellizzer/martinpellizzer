Swertia makinoana

Swertia makinoana, commonly known as Japanese gentian, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and thrives in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged oppositely along the stem.

Swertia makinoana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
