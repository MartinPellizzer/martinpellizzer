Stelis wendtii

Stelis wendtii, commonly known as the Wendt's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, showy flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Stelis wendtii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
