Stemona pierrei

Stemona pierrei, commonly known as the Chinese stemona, is a perennial herb native to China.

It features slender, upright stems that are often branched and covered with small, ovate leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. Its roots are valued in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.
