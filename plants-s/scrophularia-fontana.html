Scrophularia fontana

Scrophularia fontana, commonly known as the spring scrophularia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and thrives in partial to full shade. The plant features erect, hairy stems that bear opposite, ovate leaves with toothed edges. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, arranged in whorled clusters along the stem.

Scrophularia fontana is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
