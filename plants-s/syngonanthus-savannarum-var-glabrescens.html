Syngonanthus savannarum var. glabrescens

Syngonanthus savannarum var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth sand-verbena, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink.
