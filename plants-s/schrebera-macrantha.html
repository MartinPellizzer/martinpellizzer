Schrebera macrantha

Schrebera macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered schrebera, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a deciduous tree or shrub that can grow up to 10 meters in height, characterized by its smooth, grey bark and simple, alternate leaves. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, which bloom in the summer months.

Schrebera macrantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm climates for its attractive foliage and floral display.
