Senecio linearifolius var. gariwerdensis

Senecio linearifolius var. gariwerdensis, commonly known as the Gariwerden senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a dense inflorescence, which bloom during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often cultivated in arid regions.
