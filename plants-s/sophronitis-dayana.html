Sophronitis dayana

Sophronitis dayana, commonly known as the Dayana orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, pendulous flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually elongated and fleshy, adapted to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

Sophronitis dayana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
