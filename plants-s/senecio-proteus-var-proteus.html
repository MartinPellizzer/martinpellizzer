Senecio proteus var. proteus

Senecio proteus var. proteus, commonly known as the Cape York daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal regions. It is a hardy perennial that thrives in full sun and is relatively drought-tolerant.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
