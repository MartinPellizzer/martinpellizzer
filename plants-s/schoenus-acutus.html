Schoenus acutus

Schoenus acutus, commonly known as the sharp spike grass, is a species of grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in wetland areas and is often found in marshy or swampy environments. Its flowering stems produce small, spike-like clusters of flowers, which are usually green or pale in color.

Schoenus acutus plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
