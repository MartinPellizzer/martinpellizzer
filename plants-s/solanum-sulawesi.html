Solanum sulawesi

Solanum sulawesi, commonly known as the Sulawesi nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the Indonesian island of Sulawesi.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are ovate and hairy, while its flowers are usually white or purple, with five petals.

The fruit is a yellow or orange berry that contains several seeds.
