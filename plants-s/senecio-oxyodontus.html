Senecio oxyodontus

Senecio oxyodontus, commonly known as the toothed groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features narrow, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters. It is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Senecio oxyodontus is often found in open, sunny habitats and is adapted to arid conditions.
