Salacia memecyloides

Salacia memecyloides, commonly known as the golden star vine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Salicaceae, which includes willows and poplars. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling stars. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a slightly serrated edge.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
