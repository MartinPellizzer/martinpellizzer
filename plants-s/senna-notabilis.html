Senna notabilis

Senna notabilis, commonly known as the golden senna, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with serrated edges and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom during the dry season. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in coastal areas and sandy soils, where it thrives in full sun.

Senna notabilis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional medicine.
