Smilax trachypoda

Smilax trachypoda, commonly known as the rough smilax, is a perennial vine native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its woody stems and spiny, trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into red, fleshy berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and swamps.

Smilax trachypoda is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
