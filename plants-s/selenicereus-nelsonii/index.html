Selenicereus Nelsonii

Selenicereus nelsonii is a rare and striking climbing cactus known for its large, fragrant white flowers. Native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, it thrives in warm, humid climates with plenty of sunlight. This plant is often referred to as the "Queen of the Night" due to its nocturnal blooming habit. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a prized specimen among plant enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it requires careful cultivation to flourish in non-native environments.
