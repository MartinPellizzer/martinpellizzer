Stelis longicuspis

Stelis longicuspis, commonly known as the long-crested stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is an epiphytic orchid, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic. The plant is characterized by its long, slender stems and clustered, linear leaves. Its flowers are typically small and pale green or white, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is often found in humid, shaded environments.
