Scrophularia patriniana

Scrophularia patriniana, commonly known as the narrow-leaved figwort, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branching stems that can grow up to 1 meter in height. The plant produces clusters of tubular, two-lipped flowers that are typically pink to purple in color. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a rough, hairy texture.

Scrophularia patriniana is often found in rocky soils and open habitats, thriving in full sun to partial shade.
