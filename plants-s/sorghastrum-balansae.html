Sorghastrum balansae

Sorghastrum balansae, commonly known as the Balansae bluestem, is a perennial grass native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its blue-green foliage and tufted inflorescence, which consists of spikelets arranged in a panicle. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. It plays an important role in stabilizing soils and providing forage for wildlife.

Sorghastrum balansae is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
