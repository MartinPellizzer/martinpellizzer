Stemonurus Vitiensis

Stemonurus vitiensis is a rare and fascinating plant native to the island of Vanuatu. It belongs to the family Gesneriaceae and is known for its striking violet-colored flowers. This species thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests. Due to its limited distribution and habitat loss, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival in the wild.
