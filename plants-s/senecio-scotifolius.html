Senecio scotifolius

Senecio scotifolius, commonly known as the darkleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
