Schefflera asymmetrica

Schefflera asymmetrica, commonly known as the umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, umbrella-like crown formed by its pinnate leaves, which are typically bright green and glossy. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by orange-red berries that attract birds. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil and indirect sunlight.

Schefflera asymmetrica is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and adaptability to indoor conditions.
