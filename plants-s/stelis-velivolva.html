Stelis velivolva

Stelis velivolva, commonly known as the velvety stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pink.

The plant thrives in humid environments with bright, indirect light and well-draining substrates.
