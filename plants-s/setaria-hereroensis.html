Setaria hereroensis

Setaria hereroensis, commonly known as the Herero panicgrass, is a species of grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and savannas.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often found in areas with poor soil and limited water availability.
