Stellaria cuspidata var. alsiniformis

Stellaria cuspidata var. alsiniformis, commonly known as the Alsike starwort, is a low-growing, herbaceous perennial native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to July. The plant has narrow, linear leaves arranged in a basal rosette, and its stems are often hairy. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and meadows.

The species is characterized by its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
