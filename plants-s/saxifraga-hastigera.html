Saxifraga hastigera

Saxifraga hastigera, commonly known as the rock saxifrage, is a perennial flowering plant native to mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. The plant features basal leaves that are broad and lobed, with upright stems that bear small, white or pinkish flowers. Its flowers are usually arranged in clusters and bloom in late spring to early summer.

Saxifraga hastigera is valued for its hardiness and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
