Stylodon carolinianum

Stylodon carolinianum, commonly known as the Carolina grassvetch, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. The plant features slender, erect stems with alternate, linear leaves that are often hairy. Its small, clustered flowers are usually pink or purple, appearing in late spring to early summer.

Stylodon carolinianum is characterized by its fibrous root system and its ability to thrive in nutrient-poor soils.
