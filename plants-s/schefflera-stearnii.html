Schefflera stearnii

Schefflera stearnii, commonly known as the African net leaf.

It is a tropical plant native to Madagascar, characterized by its distinctive, palm-like foliage with large, lobed leaves arranged in a radiating pattern. The plant features a stout, woody stem with a smooth, greyish-brown bark. It thrives in humid, warm environments and is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant.

Schefflera stearnii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to adapt to various light conditions.
