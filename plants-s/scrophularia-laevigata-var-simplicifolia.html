Scrophularia laevigata var. simplicifolia

Scrophularia laevigata var. simplicifolia, commonly known as the smooth scrophularia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in woodland edges and meadows. The plant features erect stems that are hairy and can reach up to 1 meter in height. Its leaves are lance-shaped, with a smooth texture and prominent veins, while its flowers are tubular and usually pink or purple in color.

It is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
