Senna prostrata

Senna prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate senna, is a low-growing shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets. Its seeds are encased in pods and contain compounds that have been used in traditional medicine.

Senna prostrata is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and flowering habit.
