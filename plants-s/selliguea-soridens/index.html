Selliguea Soridens

Selliguea soridens is a rare and intriguing species of moss that thrives in humid, shaded environments. It belongs to the family Pottiaceae and is known for its delicate, branching structure and vibrant green color. This plant is often found on rock surfaces or in moist soil, showcasing its adaptability to challenging conditions. Despite its small size, Selliguea soridens plays an important role in ecosystem stability and biodiversity. Studying this moss offers insights into the resilience and ecological significance of cryptogamic life forms.
