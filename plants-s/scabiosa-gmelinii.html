Scabiosa gmelinii

Scabiosa gmelinii, commonly known as the mountain scabious, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its clusters of small, pink or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Scabiosa gmelinii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
