Scabiosa olivieri var. longiseta

Scabiosa olivieri var. longiseta, commonly known as the long-hairy scabious, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features slender, upright stems that are densely covered with soft, silky hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, pink or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and also covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
