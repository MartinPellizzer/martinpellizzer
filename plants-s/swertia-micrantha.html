Swertia micrantha

Swertia micrantha, commonly known as the Himalayan gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, often in moist, shaded areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, while its stems are erect and branched.

Swertia micrantha is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
