Stipa caduciseta

Stipa caduciseta, commonly known as the slender feathergrass, is a perennial grass native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are often tinged with silver or gray. The plant produces delicate, feathery inflorescences that are typically pink or purplish in color, adding a subtle ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine environments.

Stipa caduciseta is valued in horticulture for its drought tolerance and aesthetic appeal in dry garden settings.
