Stramonium vulgare

Stramonium vulgare, commonly known as jimsonweed, is an annual herbaceous plant native to Europe, North Africa, and parts of Asia.

Stramonium vulgare is often found in disturbed soils, along roadsides, and in waste areas, where it can thrive in full sun.
