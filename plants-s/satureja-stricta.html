Satureja stricta

Satureja stricta, commonly known as narrowleaf savory, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, aromatic leaves and produces clusters of pink to purple flowers. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often found in rocky or dry soils. Its leaves are used in cooking and traditional medicine for their pungent, slightly peppery flavor.

Satureja stricta is valued for its ornamental appeal and medicinal properties.
