Senecio Hewrensis

Senecio hewrensis is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Namibia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, known for its diverse and often showy flowers. This species is characterized by its spiny stems and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. Due to its limited distribution and unique appearance, it is highly sought after by botanists and collectors. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this vulnerable plant from habitat loss and overharvesting.
