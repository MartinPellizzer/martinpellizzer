Symphonia gymnoclada

Symphonia gymnoclada, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark grey bark and dense, rounded crown. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a glossy green upper surface. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by woody, spherical fruits containing several seeds.

The tree is valued for its hard, durable wood and is often used in construction and furniture-making.
