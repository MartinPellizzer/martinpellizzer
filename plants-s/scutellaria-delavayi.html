Scutellaria delavayi

Scutellaria delavayi, commonly known as Delavay's skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of western China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants known for their medicinal properties. The plant features square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular blue or purple flowers. It is often found in rocky or forested areas at high elevations.

Scutellaria delavayi is valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
