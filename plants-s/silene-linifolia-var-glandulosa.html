Silene linifolia var. glandulosa

Silene linifolia var. glandulosa, commonly known as the glandular flaxvetch, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, erect stems that are often hairy and bear small, pink to purple flowers. The leaves are linear and covered with glandular dots, giving the plant its specific epithet. It thrives in dry, sunny habitats and is often found in rocky soils.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
