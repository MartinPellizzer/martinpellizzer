Sideritis paniculata

Sideritis paniculata, commonly known as the panicled ironwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Sideritis paniculata thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or limestone habitats.

Its foliage is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
