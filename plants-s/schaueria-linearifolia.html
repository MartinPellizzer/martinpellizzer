Schaueria linearifolia

Schaueria linearifolia, commonly known as the linear-leaved Schaueria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
