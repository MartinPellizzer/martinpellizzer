Schomburgkia heidii

Schomburgkia heidii, commonly known as the Heidii's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature vibrant colors such as red, orange, and yellow. The plant has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, enabling it to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually elongated and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Schomburgkia heidii is prized by horticulturists for its beauty and adaptability in cultivation.
