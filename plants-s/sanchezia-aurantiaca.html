Sanchezia aurantiaca

Sanchezia aurantiaca, commonly known as the red-leaf staghorn plant, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety, reddish-brown leaves that are often variegated with green and yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its unique foliage.

Sanchezia aurantiaca is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.
