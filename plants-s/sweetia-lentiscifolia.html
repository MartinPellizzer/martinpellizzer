Sweetia lentiscifolia

Sweetia lentiscifolia, commonly known as the rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, often appearing in clusters. Its leaves are small, leathery, and evergreen, contributing to its hardy nature.

Sweetia lentiscifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
