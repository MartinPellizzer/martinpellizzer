Solenopsis balearica

Solenopsis balearica, commonly known as the Balearic antplant, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, bushy growth habit and produces small, yellowish flowers. The plant has a symbiotic relationship with ants, providing them with shelter in exchange for protection from herbivores. Its leaves are opposite and oval-shaped, often covered in fine hairs.

Solenopsis balearica is adapted to rocky and coastal environments, showing resilience to salt spray and drought.
