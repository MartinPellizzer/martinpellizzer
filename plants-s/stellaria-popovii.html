Stellaria popovii

Stellaria popovii, commonly known as the Popov's starwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It typically forms dense mats of foliage with small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant has slender, branched stems and leaves that are usually opposite and lance-shaped. It thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine environments.

Stellaria popovii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in rock gardens.
