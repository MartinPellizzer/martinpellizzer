Stenolobium galactioides

Stenolobium galactioides, commonly known as the yellow milkwood, is a deciduous tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and compound leaves. The plant produces yellowish-white flowers that are clustered in racemes, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. Its fruit is a legume pod that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.

The tree is adapted to arid conditions and plays an important role in local ecosystems.
