Stevia Purpurea

Stevia purpurea is a striking perennial plant native to South America known for its vibrant purple flowers and sweet leaves. It belongs to the sunflower family and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking low-maintenance beauty. Its unique color palette adds a dramatic touch to any landscape. Despite its ornamental appeal, it also has potential uses in natural sweeteners and herbal remedies.
