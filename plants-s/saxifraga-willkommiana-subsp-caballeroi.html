Saxifraga willkommiana subsp. caballeroi

Saxifraga willkommiana subsp.

caballeroi, commonly known as the Sierra Nevada saxifrage, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America. It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage.

The plant features small, clustered white or pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are usually spoon-shaped and covered in a fine, silvery coating.

It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.
