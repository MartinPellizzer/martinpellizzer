Senecio nelumbifolius

Senecio nelumbifolius, commonly known as the Chinese spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture. Its flowering period occurs in the summer months, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Senecio nelumbifolius is also noted for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
