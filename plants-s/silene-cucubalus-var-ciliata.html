Silene cucubalus var. ciliata

Silene cucubalus var. ciliata, commonly known as the silky bladder campion, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its pink to purple flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in temperate climates. Its leaves are opposite and often have soft, hairy margins, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
