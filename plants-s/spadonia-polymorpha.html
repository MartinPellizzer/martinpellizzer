Spadonia polymorpha

Spadonia polymorpha, commonly known as the variable spadix plant, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its unique inflorescence, which consists of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant typically grows in wetlands and marshy areas, thriving in moist, nutrient-rich soils. Its leaves are broad and arrow-shaped, often with a waxy surface.

Spadonia polymorpha is known for its adaptability to varying environmental conditions, making it a common sight in tropical and subtropical regions.
