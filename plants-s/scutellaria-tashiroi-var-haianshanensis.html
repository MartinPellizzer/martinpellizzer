Scutellaria tashiroi var. haianshanensis

Scutellaria tashiroi var. haianshanensis, commonly known as the Hai'an mountain skullcap, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in rocky and mountainous habitats, often in areas with well-drained soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
