Styphelia nutans var. arfakensis

Styphelia nutans var. arfakensis, commonly known as the Arfaken sandpink, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the coastal dunes of northern Florida.

The plant has narrow, leathery leaves that are adapted to withstand salt spray and sandy soil conditions.
