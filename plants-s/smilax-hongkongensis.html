Smilax hongkongensis

Smilax hongkongensis, commonly known as Hong Kong smilax, is a climbing shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features a woody stem with thorns and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in umbels. The plant bears red berries that are edible but have a tart flavor. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Smilax hongkongensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and traditional medicinal uses.
