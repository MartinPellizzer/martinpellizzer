Sobralia decora

Sobralia decora, commonly known as the decorative sobralia, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences that feature a mix of vibrant red, yellow, and orange hues. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in arid conditions.

Sobralia decora blooms during the warmer months, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in suitable climates.
