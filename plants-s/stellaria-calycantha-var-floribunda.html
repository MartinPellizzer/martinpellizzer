Stellaria calycantha var. floribunda

Stellaria calycantha var. floribunda, commonly known as the showy starwort, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped white or pale blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often hairy, with a slightly serrated edge.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
