Senecio xenostylus

Senecio xenostylus, commonly known as the mountain senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. It features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, daisy-like inflorescences.

Senecio xenostylus is often found in mountainous regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
