Selaginella picta f. viridis

Selaginella picta f. viridis, commonly known as the green-stemmed spikemoss, is a variant of the spikemoss genus Selaginella.

It is a low-growing, evergreen plant that typically thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, scale-like leaves arranged in a spiral pattern along its stems. Its foliage is characterized by a vibrant green color, which distinguishes it from other forms of the species.

This variety is often appreciated for its ornamental value in horticultural settings.
