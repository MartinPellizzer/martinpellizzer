Senecio werneriifolius var. malmstenoides

Senecio werneriifolius var. malmstenoides, commonly known as the Malmsten-like senecio, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy foliage arranged in a rosette pattern, with each leaf being narrow and slightly curled. The plant produces yellow flowers in summer, which are clustered in cylindrical inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in arid gardens.
