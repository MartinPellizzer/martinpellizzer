Scrophularia xylobasis

Scrophularia xylobasis, commonly known as the woodruff scrophularia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy, and it produces clusters of small, pale yellow or greenish flowers. Its flowers are usually tubular and are borne on slender stems, which can reach up to 60 cm in height.

Scrophularia xylobasis is part of the Scrophulariaceae family, known for its medicinal and ornamental value in horticulture.
