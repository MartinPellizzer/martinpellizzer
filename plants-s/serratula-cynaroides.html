Serratula cynaroides

Serratula cynaroides, commonly known as the spearthistle, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, spiny stems and yellow composite flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has deeply lobed, silvery-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It is often found in disturbed soils and can become invasive in some regions.

Serratula cynaroides is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
