Senecio pleniauritus

Senecio pleniauritus, commonly known as the woolly Senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its unique texture and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
