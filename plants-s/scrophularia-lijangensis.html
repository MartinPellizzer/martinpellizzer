Scrophularia lijangensis

Scrophularia lijangensis, commonly known as the Korean figwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of East Asia.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Scrophularia lijangensis is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
