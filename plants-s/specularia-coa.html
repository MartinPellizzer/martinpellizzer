Specularia coa

Specularia coa, commonly known as the silverleaf sunflower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique foliage and long-lasting blooms.
