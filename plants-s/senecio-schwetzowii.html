Senecio schwetzowii

Senecio schwetzowii, commonly known as the Schwetzow's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid regions. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a cluster at the end of its stems. Its leaves are usually narrow and linear, with a rough texture.
