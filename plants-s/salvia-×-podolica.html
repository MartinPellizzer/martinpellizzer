Salvia × podolica

Salvia × podolica, commonly known as the hybrid sage, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branching stems covered in dense, fuzzy gray-green foliage. The plant produces spikes of showy, violet-blue to purple flowers during the summer months. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its hardiness and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.

Salvia × podolica is also used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
