Solenostemon robustus

Solenostemon robustus, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and striking, velvety leaves that are often variegated. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, typically blooming from late winter to early summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Solenostemon robustus is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
