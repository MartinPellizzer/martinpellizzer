Smilax nipponica var. higoensis

Smilax nipponica var. higoensis, commonly known as the Japanese arrowroot or Higoensis smilax, is a climbing shrub native to Japan.

It features woody stems with sharp, recurved spines and heart-shaped, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and ecologically important species in its native habitat.
