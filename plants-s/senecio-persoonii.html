Senecio persoonii

Senecio persoonii, commonly known as the Blue Echeveria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, which store water to help it survive in arid conditions. The leaves are typically blue-gray in color, with a waxy coating that reduces water loss. It produces yellow flowers in summer, adding a striking contrast to its foliage.

This plant is popular in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and aesthetic appeal.
