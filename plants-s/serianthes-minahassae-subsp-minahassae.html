Serianthes minahassae subsp. minahassae

Serianthes minahassae subsp.

minahassae, commonly known as the Minahassa ironwood, is a deciduous tree native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its dense, hard wood that is highly valued for its durability and strength.

The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape and have a smooth surface. It produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by small, round fruits.

This species is often found in lowland rainforests and is known for its ability to thrive in nutrient-poor soils.
