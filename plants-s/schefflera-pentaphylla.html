Schefflera pentaphylla

Schefflera pentaphylla, commonly known as the five-leaf schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, palm-like foliage, with multiple leaflets radiating from a single point on each leaf. The plant typically grows to a height of 3 to 5 meters, featuring a short, woody stem and a broad, rounded crown. Its leaves are usually dark green and glossy, with a leathery texture.

Schefflera pentaphylla thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant or in tropical gardens.
