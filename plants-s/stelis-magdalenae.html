Stelis magdalenae

Stelis magdalenae, commonly known as the Magdalena orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structure. The plant typically produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often arranged in a raceme. Stelis magdalenae thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a prized species among orchid enthusiasts.
