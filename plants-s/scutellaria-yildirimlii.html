Scutellaria yildirimlii

Scutellaria yildirimlii, commonly known as the Yildirimli Scutellaria, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

Scutellaria yildirimlii is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
