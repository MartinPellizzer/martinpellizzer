Sponia glomerata

Sponia glomerata, commonly known as the clustered sponia, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are arranged in dense clusters, giving it its common name. Sponia glomerata typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments.

It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
