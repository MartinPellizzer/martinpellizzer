Scabiosa crenata var. breviseta

Scabiosa crenata var. breviseta, commonly known as the dwarf crested scabious, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Europe.

It features delicate, nodding flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, often with a distinctive crested center. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its botanical characteristics make it both ornamental and ecologically valuable, supporting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
