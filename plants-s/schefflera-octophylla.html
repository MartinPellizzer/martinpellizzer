Schefflera octophylla

Schefflera octophylla, commonly known as the octopus tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, umbrella-like crown of large, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant has a short, thick trunk and can grow up to several meters in height. Its leaves are arranged in a radial pattern, giving it a unique and striking appearance.

Schefflera octophylla is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant due to its ornamental value and adaptability to various growing conditions.
