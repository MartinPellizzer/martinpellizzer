Senna auriculata

Senna auriculata, commonly known as the earleaf senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces elongated pods that contain small, kidney-shaped seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry habitats.

Senna auriculata is also valued for its medicinal properties and use in traditional medicine.
