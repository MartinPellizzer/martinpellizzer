Saxifraga pedemontana

Saxifraga pedemontana, commonly known as the Alpine saxifrage, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically thrives in rocky and alpine environments. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are usually basal, rosette-shaped, and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Saxifraga pedemontana is well adapted to cold climates and can survive in poor soil conditions.
