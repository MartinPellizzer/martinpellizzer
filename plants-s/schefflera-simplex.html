Schefflera simplex

Schefflera simplex, commonly known as the umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of large, leathery, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in gardens.

Schefflera simplex is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor landscaping.
