Specklinia Pertenuis

Specklinia pertenuis is a rare and delicate orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. This small, epiphytic plant thrives in humid, shaded environments where it attaches itself to trees. Its unique appearance features intricate patterns on its leaves and delicate, fragrant flowers. Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is now critically endangered. Conservation efforts are essential to ensure its survival in the wild.
