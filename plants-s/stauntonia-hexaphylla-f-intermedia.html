Stauntonia hexaphylla f. intermedia

Stauntonia hexaphylla f. intermedia, commonly known as the variegated star jasmine, is a cultivar of the star jasmine plant.

It is a flowering shrub native to tropical regions, characterized by its fragrant, star-shaped white flowers. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves with striking variegation, often displaying yellow or cream patterns. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

This hybrid is valued for its compact growth and continuous blooming throughout the growing season.
