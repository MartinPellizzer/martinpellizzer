Senecio nebrodensis var. glabratus

Senecio nebrodensis var. glabratus, commonly known as the Nebrodes Senecio, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and may have a slightly hairy texture. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

It is also noted for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
