Schizocodon ilicifolius var. nankaiensis

Schizocodon ilicifolius var. nankaiensis, commonly known as the Nankai hollyleaf, is a shrub native to southern China.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves that resemble those of the holly, with a glossy, dark green surface. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters, which are typically followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in forested or rocky habitats.

This variant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its attractive foliage and fruit.
