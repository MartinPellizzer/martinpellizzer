Spiraea cantoniensis f. plena

Spiraea cantoniensis f. plena, commonly known as the Chinese spirea or double-flowered spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit and its profuse display of double white to pink flowers in early spring. The foliage is typically bright green, providing a striking contrast to the showy blooms. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
