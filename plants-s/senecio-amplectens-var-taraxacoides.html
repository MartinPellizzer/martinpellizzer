Senecio amplectens var. taraxacoides

Senecio amplectens var. taraxacoides, commonly known as the yellow ragwort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single bloom. The plant features yellow ray florets surrounded by a dense cluster of yellow disk florets, giving it a striking appearance. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions.

The species is noted for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
