Schefflera vidaliana

Schefflera vidaliana, commonly known as the Philippine umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like crown of large, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Schefflera vidaliana is also valued for its ability to tolerate partial shade and its air-purifying qualities.
