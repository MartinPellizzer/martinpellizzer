Sporobolus cynosuroides

Sporobolus cynosuroides, commonly known as sand dropseed, is a flowering plant native to the arid and semi-arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a perennial grass characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant has a deep root system that enables it to thrive in sandy and drought-prone soils. It is well adapted to harsh environments and is often found in dunes, grasslands, and along coastal areas.

Its botanical features make it an important component of native plant communities in these regions.
