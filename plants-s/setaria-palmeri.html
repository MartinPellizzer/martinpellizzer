Setaria palmeri

Setaria palmeri, commonly known as Palmer's dropseed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, pale green to golden brown flowers. Its leaves are narrow and often have a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments.
