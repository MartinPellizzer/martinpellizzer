Setaria berlandieri

Setaria berlandieri, commonly known as buffalo grass, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted foliage and small, spike-like inflorescences. This plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. It has a fibrous root system that helps stabilize soil and prevent erosion.

Setaria berlandieri is often used in landscaping and forage due to its hardiness and low maintenance requirements.
