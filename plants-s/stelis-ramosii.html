Stelis ramosii

Stelis ramosii, commonly known as the branched stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are usually opposite and arranged in two rows along the stem.

Stelis ramosii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and relatively easy care requirements.
