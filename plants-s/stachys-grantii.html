Stachys grantii

Stachys grantii, commonly known as the Grant's hedge nettle, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. Stachys grantii is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
