Scutellaria saxatilis var. arguta

Scutellaria saxatilis var. arguta, commonly known as the rock skullcup, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, thriving in well-drained environments. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves and produces blue to violet flowers in racemes. It is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats, where it tolerates cold and harsh conditions.

Its botanical characteristics make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
