Silene roemeri f. macrocarpa

Silene roemeri f. macrocarpa, commonly known as the large-fruited silene, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the fruit is a capsule that contains numerous small seeds.
