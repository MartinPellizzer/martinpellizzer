Sempervivum × aizoides

Sempervivum × aizoides, commonly known as the "Hens and Chicks," is a hybrid succulent plant belonging to the Crassulaceae family.

It is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are typically fleshy and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of Europe and is well adapted to rocky, well-drained soils. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, or white during the summer months.

Sempervivum × aizoides is a low-maintenance plant that thrives in full sun and is popular in rock gardens and as a houseplant.
