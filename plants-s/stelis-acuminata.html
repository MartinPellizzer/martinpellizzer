Stelis acuminata

Stelis acuminata, commonly known as the acuminata orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit, often found growing on trees in humid environments. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically arranged in a raceme. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette pattern, and it thrives in bright, indirect light with high humidity.

Stelis acuminata is appreciated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
