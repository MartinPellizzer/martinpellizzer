Stylosanthes biflora var. elatior

Stylosanthes biflora var. elatior, commonly known as the tall stylosanthes, is a perennial legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and trifoliate leaves with hairy surfaces. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, followed by flat, elliptical pods containing several seeds. It is well adapted to arid environments and is often used for forage and soil stabilization.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it valuable in agricultural systems.
