Symphytum icaricum

Symphytum icaricum, commonly known as Icaric Meadow Plant or Icaric Comfrey, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Boraginaceae family and is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and spikes of blue or purple flowers. The plant is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in moist to moderately dry conditions.
