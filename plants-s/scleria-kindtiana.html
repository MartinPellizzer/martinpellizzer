Scleria kindtiana

Scleria kindtiana, commonly known as the African sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy habitats, often forming dense clumps with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowering stems produce small, three-lobed flowers that are usually pale green or brown.

Scleria kindtiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
