Stachytarpheta gesnerioides var. hirsuta

Stachytarpheta gesnerioides var. hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy bearded sages, is a shrub native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its hairy, lance-shaped leaves and spikes of showy, two-lipped flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in moist woodlands or along stream banks.
