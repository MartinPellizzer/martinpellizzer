Schefflera capitulifera

Schefflera capitulifera, commonly known as the clustered umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features a distinctive, compact growth habit with a dense crown of leafy branches. The plant produces small, white, clustered flowers that are followed by bright red, fleshy fruits. Its leaves are typically glossy and oval-shaped, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
