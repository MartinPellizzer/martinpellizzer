Solanum galactites

Solanum galactites, commonly known as the purple potato vine, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant purple foliage. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that develop into berry-like fruits containing toxic seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
