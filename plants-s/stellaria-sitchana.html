Stellaria sitchana

Stellaria sitchana, commonly known as the Siberian starwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its small, star-shaped white flowers and slender, branching stems. The plant typically thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, forest edges, and wetlands. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered with fine hairs, while its fruit is a dry capsule that releases tiny seeds.

Stellaria sitchana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
