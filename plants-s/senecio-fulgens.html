Senecio fulgens

Senecio fulgens, commonly known as the golden ragwort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are usually blue-green in color and have a slightly fuzzy texture.
