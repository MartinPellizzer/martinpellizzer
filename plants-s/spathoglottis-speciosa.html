Spathoglottis speciosa

Spathoglottis speciosa, commonly known as the showy orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are usually broad and oval, while its flowers are clustered in a spike-like inflorescence.

Spathoglottis speciosa is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
