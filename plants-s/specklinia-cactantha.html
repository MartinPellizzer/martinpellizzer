Specklinia cactantha

Specklinia cactantha, commonly known as the spiky orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

This plant is characterized by its unique, spiky appearance, with elongated, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, which bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant is adapted to grow in shaded, humid environments, often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and visually striking ornamental plant.
