Spiranthes tovarensis

Spiranthes tovarensis, commonly known as the Tovarensis spider orchid, is a species of orchid native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, spiral-shaped inflorescence, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as cloud forests and mountain meadows. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with intricate, lip-like structures that aid in pollination.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in specialized gardens.
