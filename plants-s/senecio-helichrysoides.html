Senecio helichrysoides

Senecio helichrysoides, commonly known as the golden ragwort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that bloom throughout the warmer months. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Senecio helichrysoides is a perennial species that can reach heights of up to 60 centimeters.
