Symphyotrichum ericoides

Symphyotrichum ericoides, commonly known as heath aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that bloom in the fall. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or heathlands. It has hairy, narrow leaves and produces purple to pinkish flowers that attract pollinators.

Symphyotrichum ericoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native plant gardens.
