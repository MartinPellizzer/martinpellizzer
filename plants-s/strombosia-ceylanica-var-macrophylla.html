Strombosia ceylanica var. macrophylla

Strombosia ceylanica var. macrophylla, commonly known as the giant strombosia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by woody capsules containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its foliage.
