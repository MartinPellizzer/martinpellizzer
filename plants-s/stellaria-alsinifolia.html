Stellaria alsinifolia

Stellaria alsinifolia, commonly known as the slender starwort, is a low-growing annual herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, giving it a star-like appearance. The plant has slender, branching stems and small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

Stellaria alsinifolia is known for its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for ornamental purposes.
