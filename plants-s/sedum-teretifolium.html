Sedum teretifolium

Sedum teretifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and fleshy, narrow leaves that are often blue-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Sedum teretifolium is also valued for its low maintenance requirements and ability to tolerate drought conditions.
