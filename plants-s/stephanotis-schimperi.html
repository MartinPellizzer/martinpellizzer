Stephanotis schimperi

Stephanotis schimperi, commonly known as the African sweet pea, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that are often clustered in racemes. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves and is characterized by its rapid growth habit. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Stephanotis schimperi is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and is often used in garden landscapes.
