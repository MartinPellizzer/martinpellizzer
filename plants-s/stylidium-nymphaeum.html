Stylidium nymphaeum

Stylidium nymphaeum, commonly known as the water lily orchid, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its showy, often violet or blue flowers. Stylidium nymphaeum is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in the ecological balance of its wetland environment.
