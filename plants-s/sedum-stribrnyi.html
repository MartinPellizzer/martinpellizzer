Sedum stribrnyi

Sedum stribrnyi, commonly known as the narrow-leaf stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of Central Asia.

It features fleshy, narrow leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, allowing it to store water efficiently. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its hardiness and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for gardeners in dry climates.
