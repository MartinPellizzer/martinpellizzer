Stelis tonsoria

Stelis tonsoria, commonly known as the hairy stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia, including regions like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, attaching itself with its aerial roots. Its leaves are usually dark green and somewhat leathery, while its flowers are small and often white or pale pink.

Stelis tonsoria is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
