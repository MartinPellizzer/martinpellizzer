Scutellaria androssovii

Scutellaria androssovii, commonly known as the Androsowicz skullcap, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous areas.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
