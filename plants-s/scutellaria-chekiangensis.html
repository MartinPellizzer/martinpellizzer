Scutellaria chekiangensis

Scutellaria chekiangensis, commonly known as the Chinese skullcap, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides.

Scutellaria chekiangensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
