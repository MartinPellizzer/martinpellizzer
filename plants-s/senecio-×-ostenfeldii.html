Senecio × ostenfeldii

Senecio × ostenfeldii, commonly known as the hybrid African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows as a shrub with wiry, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

Senecio × ostenfeldii is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
