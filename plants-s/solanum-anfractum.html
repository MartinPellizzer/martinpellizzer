Solanum anfractum

Solanum anfractum, commonly known as the warty nightshade, is a flowering plant species native to South America.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive warty stems and leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into berries containing toxic seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its toxic properties, it is important to handle the plant with care.
