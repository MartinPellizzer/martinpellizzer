Setaria yunnanensis

Setaria yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan millet, is a grass species native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It is often found in arid or semi-arid regions, thriving in well-drained soils.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
