Spartium congestum

Spartium congestum, commonly known as clustered spartium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky soils and sunny habitats. The plant features slender, branched stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often gray-green in color. Its flowers are borne in dense clusters and are usually pink or purple, attracting various pollinators.

Spartium congestum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
