Salvia miltiorrhiza

Salvia miltiorrhiza, commonly known as Chinese red sage, is a perennial herb native to China.

It is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and striking red or purple flowers. The plant is renowned for its medicinal properties, particularly the roots, which contain tanshinones and other bioactive compounds. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Salvia miltiorrhiza is widely cultivated for both traditional medicine and ornamental purposes.
