Solanum nigrum f. stenopetalum

Solanum nigrum f. stenopetalum, commonly known as the narrow-petaled black nightshade, is a variant of the common black nightshade.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant features small, dark purple to black berries that are toxic to humans and animals. Its flowers are typically smaller and more narrow-petaled compared to the typical species.

It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in gardens and along roadsides.
