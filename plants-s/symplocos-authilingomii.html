Symplocos authilingomii

Symplocos authilingomii, commonly known as the Philippine tea tree, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves and small, white or pinkish flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically red when mature. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in secondary forests.
