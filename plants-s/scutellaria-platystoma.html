Scutellaria platystoma

Scutellaria platystoma, commonly known as the broad-leaved skullcap, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a velvety texture and produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. Scutellaria platystoma is often found in moist, woodland habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.

Its structure and growth habits make it a distinctive member of the genus Scutellaria.
