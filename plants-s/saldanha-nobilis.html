Saldanha nobilis

Saldanha nobilis, commonly known as the noble saldanha, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its evergreen shrub form, with glossy, dark green leaves that are often aromatic. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
