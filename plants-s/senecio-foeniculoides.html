Senecio foeniculoides

Senecio foeniculoides, commonly known as the African Daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces bright yellow ray flowers surrounded by pale yellow disk flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Senecio foeniculoides is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
