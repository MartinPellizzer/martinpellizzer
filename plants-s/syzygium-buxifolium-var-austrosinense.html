Syzygium buxifolium var. austrosinense

Syzygium buxifolium var. austrosinense, commonly known as the Australian wax plum, is a small evergreen shrub native to eastern Australia.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in spring, followed by edible, waxy fruits that are typically red or yellow when mature. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and conservation efforts.
