Serruria aitonii var. multifida

Serruria aitonii var. multifida, commonly known as the multifid serruria, is a striking South African shrub belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are deeply divided into multiple lobes, giving it a feathery appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to white, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and unique foliage, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
