Senecio caryophyllus

Senecio caryophyllus, commonly known as the red-hot poker, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, spiky inflorescences that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow, giving it its common name. The plant has rough, hairy leaves that are typically gray-green in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Senecio caryophyllus is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
