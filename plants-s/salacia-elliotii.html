Salacia elliotii

Salacia elliotii, commonly known as Elliot's salacia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Salaciacae and is characterized by its climbing habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a waxy surface.

Salacia elliotii is valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
