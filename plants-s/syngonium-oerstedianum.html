Syngonium oerstedianum

Syngonium oerstedianum, commonly known as the arrowhead vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, arrowhead-shaped, or kidney-shaped leaves that vary in color from solid green to variegated patterns. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Its rapid growth makes it suitable for trellises or as a ground cover in humid climates.
