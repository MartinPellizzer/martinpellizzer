Sansevieria rorida

Sansevieria rorida, commonly known as the "Coral Snake Plant," is a succulent perennial native to tropical Africa.

It features stiff, upright leaves that are typically green with red or pink margins, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, pale green flowers on slender stalks, which bloom in the summer months. It is highly drought-tolerant and thrives in low-light conditions, making it an ideal indoor plant.

Its ability to purify air and require minimal care has contributed to its popularity among houseplant enthusiasts.
