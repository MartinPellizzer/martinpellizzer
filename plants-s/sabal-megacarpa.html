Sabal megacarpa

Sabal megacarpa, commonly known as the giant cabbage palm, is a species native to the Florida Keys.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, fan-shaped leaves that can reach up to 10 feet in diameter. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into large, edible fruits resembling cabbage. These fruits are an important food source for various wildlife species.

Its unique morphology and ecological significance make it a notable plant in coastal ecosystems.
