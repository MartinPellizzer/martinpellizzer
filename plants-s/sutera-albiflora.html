Sutera albiflora

Sutera albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered sutera, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers. The plant produces small, oval-shaped fruits that contain seeds covered in a sticky substance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Sutera albiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
