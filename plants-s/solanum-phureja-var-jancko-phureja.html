Solanum phureja var. jancko-phureja

Solanum phureja var. jancko-phureja, commonly known as the Jancko phureja tomato, is a botanical variety native to Central America.

It is a member of the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its small, cherry-like fruits. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with hairy stems and lobed leaves. Its flowers are usually yellow with five petals, and it produces fruits that range in color from red to orange.
