Solanum luteum var. calvum

Solanum luteum var. calvum, commonly known as the smooth yellow nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of Central and South America.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, followed by orange to red berries that contain toxic seeds. The plant has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in disturbed habitats.

Due to its toxic properties, it is important to handle the plant with care.
