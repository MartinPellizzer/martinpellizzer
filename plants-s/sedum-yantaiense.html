Sedum yantaiense

Sedum yantaiense, commonly known as the Yantai stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to China.

It is characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its hardy nature allows it to survive in USDA hardiness zones 5 through 9.
