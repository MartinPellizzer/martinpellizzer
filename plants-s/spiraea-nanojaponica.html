Spiraea nanojaponica

Spiraea nanojaponica, commonly known as Japanese spirea, is a compact shrub native to Japan.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming in early spring. The plant has fine, dark green foliage that provides a dense, ornamental appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden borders or containers.

Its compact size and showy flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
