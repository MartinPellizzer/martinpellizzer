Syngonium auritum

Syngonium auritum, commonly known as the ear-shaped arrowhead plant, is a tropical perennial native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped to arrowhead-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or cream markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Syngonium auritum is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
