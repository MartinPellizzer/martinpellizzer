Schoberia corniculata

Schoberia corniculata, commonly known as the horned poppy, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking red-orange flowers with distinctive horn-like structures at the base of the petals. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in fine hairs.

Schoberia corniculata is a herbaceous perennial that blooms from late spring to early summer.
