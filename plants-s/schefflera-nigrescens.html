Schefflera nigrescens

Schefflera nigrescens, commonly known as the black-leaf schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, compound leaves that are dark green and often have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by bright red berries that are attractive to birds. Schefflera nigrescens thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
