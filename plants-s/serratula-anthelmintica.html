Serratula anthelmintica

Serratula anthelmintica, commonly known as the helminth fighter, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant is known for its medicinal properties, particularly its anthelmintic effects, which help in expelling parasitic worms. It typically grows in meadows and grasslands, reaching heights of up to 1 meter.

Its yellow flowers bloom in summer, and the plant has hairy stems and serrated leaves.
