Sychinium

Sychinium, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in dense clusters during the rainy season. The plant has compound leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. Its bark is smooth and dark in color, often with a slight sheen.

Sychinium is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often planted in gardens and as a street tree in warm climates.
