Stanhopea tigrina var. superba

Stanhopea tigrina var. superba, commonly known as the tiger orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that display a mix of red, orange, and yellow hues, often with a distinctive tiger-like pattern on the petals. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, shaded environments with high air circulation. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in seasonal droughts.

The species is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
