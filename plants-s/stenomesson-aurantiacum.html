Stenomesson aurantiacum

Stenomesson aurantiacum, commonly known as the yellow evening primrose, is a herbaceous plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its striking yellow-orange flowers that bloom in the evening, attracting nocturnal pollinators such as moths. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, black seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Botanically, it belongs to the genus *Stenomesson* within the family *Onagraceae*, which includes other evening primroses.
