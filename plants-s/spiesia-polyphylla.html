Spiesia polyphylla

Spiesia polyphylla, commonly known as the many-leaved spurge, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant has smooth, often hairy stems and leaves that are usually opposite or whorled. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Spiesia polyphylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
