Scutellaria tenax var. tenax

Scutellaria tenax var. tenax, commonly known as blue skullcap, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces spikes of blue to purple flowers. Its genus, Scutellaria, is characterized by a distinctive cap-like structure beneath the flower, known as a calyx.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
