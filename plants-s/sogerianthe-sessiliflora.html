Sogerianthe sessiliflora

Sogerianthe sessiliflora, commonly known as the sessile-flowered orchid, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The plant has narrow, strap-like leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette. It prefers humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Sogerianthe sessiliflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
