Senecio icoglossus var. icoglossus

Senecio icoglossus var. icoglossus, commonly known as the blueweed or bluecup, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, blue or purple flowers arranged in a dense, cup-like cluster. It is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
