Symplocos bidana

Symplocos bidana, commonly known as the narrow-leaved evergreen.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia. The plant features elliptical, glossy, dark green leaves with a smooth texture. Its flowers are small, white, and clustered in umbels, while the fruit is a small, yellowish drupe.

Symplocos bidana is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions.
