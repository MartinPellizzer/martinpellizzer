Senecio kirkii var. angustior

Senecio kirkii var. angustior, commonly known as narrowleaf senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically features narrow, linear leaves and produces yellow to orange composite flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in some regions.
