Stachys arrecta

Stachys arrecta, commonly known as upright hedge nettle, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features square stems and opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, clustered in whorls, and typically have purple or pink hues.
