Spiesia platonychia

Spiesia platonychia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spurge, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender stems and small, alternate leaves that are typically lance-shaped. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

Spiesia platonychia is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
