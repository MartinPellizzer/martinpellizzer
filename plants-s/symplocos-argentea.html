Symplocos argentea

Symplocos argentea, commonly known as silverleaf evergreen, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, silver-gray leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a small, round drupe that turns red when mature.

Symplocos argentea is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions.
