Staurogyne shanica

Staurogyne shanica, commonly known as the Shanica waterweed, is a species of aquatic plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a submerged herbaceous plant characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, oval-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in still or slow-moving freshwater environments such as ponds and lakes. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, often clustered in dense inflorescences.
