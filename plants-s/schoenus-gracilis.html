Schoenus gracilis

Schoenus gracilis, commonly known as slender bulrush, is a grass-like plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems that often grow in wetland environments. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. Its roots help stabilize soil and improve water quality in marshy areas.

Schoenus gracilis is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its adaptability and environmental benefits.
