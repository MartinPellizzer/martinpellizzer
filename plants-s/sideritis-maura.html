Sideritis maura

Sideritis maura, commonly known as the Sicilian ironwort, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to violet. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in fine hairs.

Sideritis maura is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly for its potential anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects.
