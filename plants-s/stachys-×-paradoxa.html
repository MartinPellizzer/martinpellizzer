Stachys × paradoxa

Stachys × paradoxa, commonly known as the purple deadnettle, is a hybrid plant in the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces small, two-lipped flowers in shades of purple or pink, which bloom in whorls along the stem. Native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia, it is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
