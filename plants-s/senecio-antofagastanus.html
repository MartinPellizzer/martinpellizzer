Senecio antofagastanus

Senecio antofagastanus, commonly known as the Antofagasta groundsel, is a flowering plant native to Chile.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow-orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions. It has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.
