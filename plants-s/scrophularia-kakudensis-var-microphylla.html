Scrophularia kakudensis var. microphylla

Scrophularia kakudensis var. microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved scarlet root, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae, which includes many ornamental and medicinal plants. The plant is characterized by its slender stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically pink or purple, arranged in clusters, and bloom in late spring to early summer.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodland areas and stream banks.
