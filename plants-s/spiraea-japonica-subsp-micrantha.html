Spiraea japonica subsp. micrantha

Spiraea japonica subsp.

micrantha, commonly known as Japanese spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia. It features small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring.

The plant has slender, arching branches and dark green leaves that turn red in the fall. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value comes from its showy flowers and compact growth habit.
