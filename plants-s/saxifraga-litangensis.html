Saxifraga litangensis

Saxifraga litangensis, commonly known as the Litang saxifrage, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of China.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and clustered foliage. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers during the summer months, typically in June or July. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, arid environments.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
