Selaginella densa

Selaginella densa, commonly known as the dense spikemoss, is a species of spikemoss belonging to the family Selaginellaceae.

It is a low-growing, evergreen plant that typically forms dense mats on rocky or sandy soils. The plant has slender, branching stems covered in small, scale-like leaves. Its reproductive structures, called strobila, are cone-like and produce spores.

Selaginella densa is often found in arid or semi-arid environments and is adapted to survive in harsh conditions.
