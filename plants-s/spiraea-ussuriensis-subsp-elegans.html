Spiraea ussuriensis subsp. elegans

Spiraea ussuriensis subsp.

elegans, commonly known as the Ussuri Spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia. It features upright, arching branches covered in clusters of small, white to pink flowers in early summer.

The plant has oval, serrated leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This ornamental plant is valued for its showy blooms and compact growth habit.
