Schismatoglottis bogneri

Schismatoglottis bogneri, commonly known as the Bogner's schismatoglottis, is a rare and unusual orchid native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of trees without deriving nutrients from them.
