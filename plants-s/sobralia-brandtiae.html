Sobralia brandtiae

Sobralia brandtiae, commonly known as the Brandt's sobralia, is a striking tropical orchid native to Colombia.

It features vibrant, showy flowers that are typically red or orange with contrasting yellow sepals. The plant has a compact growth habit with a pseudobulb and produces pendulous inflorescences. Its leaves are elliptical and slightly fleshy, adapted to store water in its native habitat.

Sobralia brandtiae is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its bold coloration and unique floral structure.
