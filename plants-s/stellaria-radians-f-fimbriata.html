Stellaria radians f. fimbriata

Stellaria radians f. fimbriata, commonly known as the fringed starwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its star-shaped flowers with white petals and prominent stamens. The foliage consists of opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.

Its fimbriated (fringed) leaf margins are a distinctive botanical feature that aids in its identification.
