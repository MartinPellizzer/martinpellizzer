Schefflera monosperma

Schefflera monosperma, commonly known as the African umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, umbrella-like compound leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in diameter, with numerous leaflets radiating from a central point. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by bright red, fleshy berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage.

Schefflera monosperma is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
