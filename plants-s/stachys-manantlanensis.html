Stachys manantlanensis

Stachys manantlanensis, commonly known as the Mexican hedge nettle, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features square stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. Its small, pinkish-white flowers are clustered in whorls along the stems.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.
