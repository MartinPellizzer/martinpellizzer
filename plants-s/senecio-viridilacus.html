Senecio viridilacus

Senecio viridilacus, commonly known as the green coneflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces yellow to orange flower heads that resemble a cone. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Senecio viridilacus is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
