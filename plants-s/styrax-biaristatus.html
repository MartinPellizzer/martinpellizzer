Styrax biaristatus

Styrax biaristatus, commonly known as the Chinese frankincense tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Styracaceae and is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces a resinous sap that has been historically used in traditional medicine and perfumery. Its leaves are alternate, elliptical, and have a smooth, glossy surface.

Styrax biaristatus thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a suitable ornamental plant in gardens.
