Sideritis Lotsyi

Sideritis lotsyi is a rare and lesser-known plant species native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, known for its aromatic and often medicinal properties. This plant is characterized by its delicate, hairy leaves and small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. Due to its limited distribution and fragile habitat, Sideritis lotsyi is considered vulnerable to environmental changes and human encroachment. Its unique botanical features make it an intriguing subject for botanical research and conservation efforts.
