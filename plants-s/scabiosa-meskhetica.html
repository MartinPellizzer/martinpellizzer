Scabiosa meskhetica

Scabiosa meskhetica, commonly known as the Caucasian scabious, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its delicate, pink or white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and is often found in mountainous areas. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Scabiosa meskhetica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
