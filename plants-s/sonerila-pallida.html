Sonerila pallida

Sonerila pallida, commonly known as the pale sonerila, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The pale sonerila is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
