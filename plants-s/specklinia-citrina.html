Specklinia citrina

Specklinia citrina, commonly known as the golden speckled orchid, is a small epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, branching growth habit and vibrant, speckled green leaves that often have a yellowish tinge. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining potting media.

Due to its ornamental value, it is a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
