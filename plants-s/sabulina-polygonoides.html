Sabulina polygonoides

Sabulina polygonoides, commonly known as the sand snakeroot, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils, often in arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it has a fibrous root system adapted to its harsh habitat.
