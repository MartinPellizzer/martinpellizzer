Saprosma lowiana

Saprosma lowiana, commonly known as the dwarf pittosporum, is a small evergreen shrub native to New Zealand.

It features dense, dark green foliage and produces small, fragrant white flowers in spring. The plant has smooth, greyish bark that becomes more rugged with age. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Saprosma lowiana is relatively disease-resistant and requires minimal maintenance once established.
