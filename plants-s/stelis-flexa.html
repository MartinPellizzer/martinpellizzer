Stelis flexa

Stelis flexa, commonly known as the flexed stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant has slender, erect stems that bear clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water retention.

Stelis flexa is valued in horticulture for its delicate appearance and ease of cultivation.
