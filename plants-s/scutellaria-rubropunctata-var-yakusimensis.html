Scutellaria rubropunctata var. yakusimensis

Scutellaria rubropunctata var. yakusimensis, commonly known as the Yakushima skullcup, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its small, tubular blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as the forests of Yakushima Island. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

This variant is distinguished by its red-brown spots on the leaves, which give it its scientific name.
