Silene acuta

Silene acuta, commonly known as sharp sandwort, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of about 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant features small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are usually fleshy and succulent, adapted to survive in dry, sandy environments.

Silene acuta is often found in rocky or coastal areas, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
