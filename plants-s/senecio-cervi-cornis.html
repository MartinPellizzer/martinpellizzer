Senecio cervi-cornis

Senecio cervi-cornis, commonly known as the horned lettuce, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features distinctive, horn-like structures on its stems, which give it its common name. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid environments.

Senecio cervi-cornis is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique appearance.
