Senna stipulacea var. stipulacea

Senna stipulacea var. stipulacea, commonly known as the yellow senna, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with small stipules and bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits containing several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
