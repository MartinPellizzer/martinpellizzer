Swertia tubulosa

Swertia tubulosa, commonly known as the Himalayan gentian, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, thriving in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and branched.

Swertia tubulosa is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
