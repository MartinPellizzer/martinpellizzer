Solanum nocturnum

Solanum nocturnum, commonly known as nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is a climbing vine that produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that open at night. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas. Its berries, which turn from green to black as they mature, are toxic but have been used in traditional medicine.

Solanum nocturnum thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed habitats.
