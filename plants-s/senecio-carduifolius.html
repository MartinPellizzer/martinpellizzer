Senecio carduifolius

Senecio carduifolius, commonly known as the African Daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Senecio carduifolius is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
