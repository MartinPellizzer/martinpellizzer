Stachys kouyangensis var. leptodon

Stachys kouyangensis var. leptodon, commonly known as the narrow-leaved betony, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and aromatic foliage. The plant features lanceolate leaves with a hairy surface and produces spikes of violet or purple flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded forest environments.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
