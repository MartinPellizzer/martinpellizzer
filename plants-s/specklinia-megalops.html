Specklinia megalops

Specklinia megalops, commonly known as the giant speckled orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, showy flowers that are often speckled with contrasting colors. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, such as forests or rocky areas. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to anchor in loose soil or moss.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its striking floral display.
