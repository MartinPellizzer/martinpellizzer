Sedum creticum var. hierapetrae

Sedum creticum var. hierapetrae, commonly known as the Cretan stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and small, clustered leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping.
