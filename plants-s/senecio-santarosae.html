Senecio santarosae

Senecio santarosae, commonly known as the Santa Rosa groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, succulent habit. The plant features narrow, gray-green leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
