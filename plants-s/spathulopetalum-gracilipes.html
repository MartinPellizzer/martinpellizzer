Spathulopetalum gracilipes

Spathulopetalum gracilipes, commonly known as the slender-petaled orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a herbaceous plant with delicate, elongated leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant's most distinctive feature is its spatulate-shaped petals, which give it its scientific name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized orchid species.
