Stipa fusiformis

Stipa fusiformis, commonly known as the slender feathergrass, is a grass species native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical spikelets that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth texture and a bluish-green color.

Stipa fusiformis is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
