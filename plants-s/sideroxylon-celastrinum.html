Sideroxylon celastrinum

Sideroxylon celastrinum, commonly known as the yellow ironwood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Celastraceae and is characterized by its dense, hard wood that is highly valued for its durability. The plant typically grows in lowland rainforests and has compound leaves with opposite arrangement. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, while the fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds.

The tree is also noted for its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.
