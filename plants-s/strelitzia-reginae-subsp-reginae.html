Strelitzia reginae subsp. reginae

Strelitzia reginae subsp.

reginae, commonly known as the African giant lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its large, banana-like leaves that are broad and dark green, often with a waxy texture.

The plant produces showy, orchid-like flowers in vibrant shades of yellow and orange, encased in a striking blue or purple bract. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires plenty of sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and indoor cultivation.

This subspecies is known for its resilience and ability to bloom reliably under proper care.
