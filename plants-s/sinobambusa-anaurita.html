Sinobambusa anaurita

Sinobambusa anaurita, commonly known as the Chinese dwarf bamboo, is a species of grass in the family Poaceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its compact growth habit and slender, green culms. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests and stream sides. Sinobambusa anaurita is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a decorative plant.
