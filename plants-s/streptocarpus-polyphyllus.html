Streptocarpus polyphyllus

Streptocarpus polyphyllus, commonly known as the broad-leaf streptocarpus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

Streptocarpus polyphyllus is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
