Sesleria rigida

Sesleria rigida, commonly known as the rigid sesleria, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa, where it thrives in rocky and sandy soils. Sesleria rigida is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
