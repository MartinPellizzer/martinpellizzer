Saribus jeanneneyi

Saribus jeanneneyi, commonly known as the Jeanne's palm, is a species of palm native to Madagascar.

It is a tall, slender palm with a single, unbranched stem and large, pinnate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by elongated, three-angled fruits. Its leaves are used in traditional crafts, and the plant is valued for its ornamental appearance.

Saribus jeanneneyi is also of interest to botanists due to its unique genetic makeup and ecological adaptations.
