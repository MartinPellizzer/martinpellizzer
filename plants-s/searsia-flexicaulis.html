Searsia flexicaulis

Searsia flexicaulis, commonly known as the yellow wild pear, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Rosaceae and is characterized by its slender, often branching stems. Searsia flexicaulis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
