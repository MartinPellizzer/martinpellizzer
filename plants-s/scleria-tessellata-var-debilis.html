Scleria tessellata var. debilis

Scleria tessellata var. debilis, commonly known as the slender basket grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a prominent midrib. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing tiny white or pale blue flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its botanical structure makes it an attractive addition to water gardens and wetland restoration projects.
