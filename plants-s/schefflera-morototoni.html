Schefflera morototoni

Schefflera morototoni, commonly known as the Morototi Schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Madagascar.

It features a dense, rounded canopy with large, palmately lobed leaves that are dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red, fleshy fruits containing black seeds. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor landscaping.
