Scutellaria amana

Scutellaria amana, commonly known as the blue skullcap, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, particularly China and Japan.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular blue or violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Scutellaria amana is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

It is valued in traditional medicine for its potential calming and anti-inflammatory properties.
