Stellaria media subsp. pallida

Stellaria media subsp.

pallida, commonly known as white chickweed, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa. It features small, white flowers with five petals arranged in a star-like pattern, which gives it its common name.

The leaves are typically oval or lance-shaped, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. This subspecies is often found in disturbed soils, such as along roadsides and in gardens.

It is characterized by its delicate appearance and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
