Solanum atrovirens

Solanum atrovirens, commonly known as the black nightshade, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into berries, which turn from green to dark purple as they mature. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Solanum atrovirens belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae), which includes other plants like tomatoes and potatoes.
