Sansevieria varians

Sansevieria varians, commonly known as the Variegated Snake Plant, is a striking member of the Asparagaceae family native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are often variegated with shades of yellow, white, and green. The plant is highly adaptable, thriving in low-light conditions and requiring minimal water, making it an excellent choice for indoor cultivation. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, storing water to help it survive in arid environments.

Additionally, Sansevieria varians is known for its air-purifying properties, improving indoor air quality by filtering toxins.
