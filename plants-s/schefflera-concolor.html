Schefflera concolor

Schefflera concolor, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like cluster of dark green, glossy leaves. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters in its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually leathery and have a smooth texture, with a slightly waxy surface.

Schefflera concolor is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its adaptability and aesthetic appeal.
