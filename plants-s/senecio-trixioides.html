Senecio trixioides

Senecio trixioides, commonly known as the silverleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It has a compact growth habit with hairy leaves that help reduce water loss.

Senecio trixioides is often used in rock gardens and as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
