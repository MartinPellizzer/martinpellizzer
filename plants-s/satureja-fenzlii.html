Satureja fenzlii

Satureja fenzlii, commonly known as Fenzli's savory, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine and culinary applications due to their pungent, aromatic properties.

Satureja fenzlii thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to harsh, alpine environments.
