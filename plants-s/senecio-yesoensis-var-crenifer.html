Senecio yesoensis var. crenifer

Senecio yesoensis var. crenifer, commonly known as the Yesso's Senecio, is a perennial flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant features narrow, serrated leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
