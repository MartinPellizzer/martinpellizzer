Schoenorchis latifolia

Schoenorchis latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of white or pale pink flowers. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique rhizomatous growth habit.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a threatened species in many regions.
