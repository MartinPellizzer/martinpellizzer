Senegalia Hainanensis

Senegalia hainanensis is a rare and unique plant native to the island of Hainan in China. It belongs to the legume family and is known for its distinctive appearance and ecological significance. This species thrives in specific tropical and subtropical environments, often found in coastal areas and lowland forests. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered vulnerable and requires conservation efforts. Its study provides valuable insights into biodiversity and plant adaptation in southern China.
