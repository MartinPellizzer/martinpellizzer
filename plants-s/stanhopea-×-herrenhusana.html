Stanhopea × herrenhusana

Stanhopea × herrenhusana, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Stanhopea and Herrenhusa orchids.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors, often with a unique, intricate pattern. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This orchid is popular among horticulturists for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
