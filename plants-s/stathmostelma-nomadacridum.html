Stathmostelma nomadacridum

Stathmostelma nomadacridum, commonly known as the desert grasshopper, is a species of grasshopper native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa.

The plant aspect of this species refers to its interaction with the vegetation in its habitat, as it primarily feeds on grasses and other low-growing plants.
