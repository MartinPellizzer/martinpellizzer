Schkuhria greenmanii

Schkuhria greenmanii, commonly known as the Greenman's silverweed, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, silver-gray leaves covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid environments. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in dense clusters, and bloom in the spring.

Schkuhria greenmanii is adapted to rocky soils and high altitudes, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
