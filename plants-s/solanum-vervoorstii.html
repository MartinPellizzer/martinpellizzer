Solanum vervoorstii

Solanum vervoorstii, commonly known as the Vervorst's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and peppers. The plant is characterized by its small, hairy leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its fruit is a small, spherical berry that turns from green to red as it matures.

Solanum vervoorstii is typically found in rocky or sandy habitats and is adapted to arid conditions.
