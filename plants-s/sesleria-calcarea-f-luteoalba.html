Sesleria calcarea f. luteoalba

Sesleria calcarea f. luteoalba, commonly known as the yellow-white sesleria, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, clustered inflorescences. The plant produces pale yellow to white florets, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. It is native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa, and thrives in rocky, well-drained soils.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
