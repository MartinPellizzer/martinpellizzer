Sida cavaleriei

Sida cavaleriei, commonly known as the Hawaiian sida, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to the arid conditions of its native habitat. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a rough texture.

Sida cavaleriei is notable for its ecological significance in supporting native insect and bird populations.
