Salvia omissa

Salvia omissa, commonly known as the slender sage, is a perennial herb native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It features narrow, gray-green leaves and produces spikes of blue to purple flowers. The plant is adapted to arid environments and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often found in rocky habitats and open woodlands.

Salvia omissa is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance in landscaping.
