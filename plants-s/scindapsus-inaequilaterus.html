Scindapsus inaequilaterus

Scindapsus inaequilaterus, commonly known as the unequal-veined arrowhead plant, is a species of flowering plant in the Araceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Scindapsus inaequilaterus is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
