Syzygium microbotryum

Syzygium microbotryum, commonly known as the small-fruited clove.

It is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces small, reddish-brown fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes other aromatic plants like eucalyptus and guava.

Syzygium microbotryum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has limited use in traditional medicine.
