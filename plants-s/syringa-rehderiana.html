Syringa rehderiana

Syringa rehderiana, commonly known as the Chinese lilac, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, clustered flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant produces small, oval leaves that are dark green and glossy on top. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Syringa rehderiana is valued for its ornamental beauty and adaptability to various garden conditions.
