Saxifraga varians var. delphinensis

Saxifraga varians var. delphinensis, commonly known as the blue saxifrage, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features dense clusters of small, sky-blue flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has dark green, succulent-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Saxifraga varians var. delphinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
