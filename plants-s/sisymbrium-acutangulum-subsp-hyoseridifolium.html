Sisymbrium acutangulum subsp. hyoseridifolium

Sisymbrium acutangulum subsp.

hyoseridifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leafed winter cress, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family. It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, greenish-yellow flowers that appear in early spring.

The leaves are narrow and pinnately divided, giving the plant a delicate appearance. It is typically found in disturbed soils and open areas, often along roadsides and in fields.
