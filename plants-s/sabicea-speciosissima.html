Sabicea speciosissima

Sabicea speciosissima, commonly known as the beautiful sabicea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Leguminosae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white to pale yellow flowers, which bloom in spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation.
