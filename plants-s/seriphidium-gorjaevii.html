Seriphidium gorjaevii

Seriphidium gorjaevii, commonly known as the Kyzylkum hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, thriving in sandy or gravelly soils. It has small, narrow leaves and produces numerous seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Due to its resilience, it is often found in regions with extreme temperature fluctuations.
