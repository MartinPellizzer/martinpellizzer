Syngonium steyermarkii

Syngonium steyermarkii, commonly known as the arrowhead vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically variegated with white or cream markings, giving it a striking appearance. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which is characterized by its unique inflorescence structure known as a spadix surrounded by a spathe. Syngonium steyermarkii thrives in humid, shady environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Its rapid growth and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
