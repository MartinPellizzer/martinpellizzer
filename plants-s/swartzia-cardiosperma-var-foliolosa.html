Swartzia cardiosperma var. foliolosa

Swartzia cardiosperma var. foliolosa, commonly known as the leafy swartzia, is a deciduous tree native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant exhibits a distinctive bark that is smooth and often fissured, with a dark brown to greyish color. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with small, oval-shaped leaflets that give it a lush, green appearance.

The tree produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by elongated, woody pods containing flat, kidney-shaped seeds.
