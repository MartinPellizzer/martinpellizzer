Saussurea wernerioides

Saussurea wernerioides, commonly known as the Himalayan yellowhead, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellowish-orange flowers and robust, hairy stems. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, thriving at elevations between 2,500 and 5,000 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in its harsh habitat.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral display.
