Scaphyglottis pauciflora

Scaphyglottis pauciflora, commonly known as the few-flowered scaphyglottis, is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is a terrestrial herb with a short, creeping rhizome and produces a single, erect stem bearing a few flowers. The flowers are typically greenish-white with a yellowish lip, and they bloom during the wet season. Native to tropical regions of South America, it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it a unique and relatively rare orchid species.
