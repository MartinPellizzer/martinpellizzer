Solanecio harennensis

Solanecio harennensis, commonly known as the Hainan solanecio, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and has hairy foliage. It is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it an important species in the study of alpine flora.
