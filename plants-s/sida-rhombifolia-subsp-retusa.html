Sida rhombifolia subsp. retusa

Sida rhombifolia subsp.

retusa, commonly known as the narrow-leaf sida, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa, Asia, and the Americas. It is a herbaceous annual or perennial species that typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats.
