Schnella hymenaeifolia var. stuebeliana

Schnella hymenaeifolia var. stuebeliana, commonly known as the Stuebel's Schnella, is a herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in clusters. The leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, giving the plant a soft appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with appropriate conditions.
