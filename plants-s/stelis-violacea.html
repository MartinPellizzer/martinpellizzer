Stelis violacea

Stelis violacea, commonly known as the violet orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces striking violet-blue flowers that bloom during the rainy season. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic nature. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

The leaves are usually dark green and have a velvety texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal.
