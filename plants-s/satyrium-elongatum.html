Satyrium elongatum

Satyrium elongatum, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its slender, erect stem and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically arranged in a loose spike. It is native to regions of southern Africa, particularly South Africa, where it thrives in grassland habitats.

Due to its delicate appearance and specific ecological requirements, it is often found in shaded, moist environments.
