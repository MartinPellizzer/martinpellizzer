Stapelia concinna var. paniculata

Stapelia concinna var. paniculata, commonly known as the panicle stapelia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed stems that store water. The plant produces striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange with a strong, unpleasant odor. These flowers are arranged in a panicle inflorescence, giving the plant its common name.

It is a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
