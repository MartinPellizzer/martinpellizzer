Senecio dewildemanianus

Senecio dewildemanianus, commonly known as the Dewildeman's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and protected species.
