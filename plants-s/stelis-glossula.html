Stelis glossula

Stelis glossula, commonly known as the hooded orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, humid environments such as forest understories. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with a short rhizome and a single, fleshy stem. Its flowers are small, clustered, and usually pale green to yellowish in color.

Stelis glossula is characterized by its unique hood-like structure on the flower petals, which gives it its common name.
