Stipecoma ovata

Stipecoma ovata, commonly known as the oval-stemmed orchid, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

Its leaves are usually elongated and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Stipecoma ovata thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its aesthetic qualities in horticulture.
