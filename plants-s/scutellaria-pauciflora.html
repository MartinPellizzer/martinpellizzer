Scutellaria pauciflora

Scutellaria pauciflora, commonly known as the few-flowered skullcap, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, particularly China and Korea.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It has a compact growth habit with hairy leaves and is often found in rocky or woodland habitats.

Scutellaria pauciflora is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal medicine.
