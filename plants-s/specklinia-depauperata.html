Specklinia depauperata

Specklinia depauperata, commonly known as the poor speckled orchid, is a rare and delicate orchid species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, strap-like leaves and its unique, pale green to white flowers with intricate speckling. This epiphytic plant typically grows on tree trunks or branches in humid, shaded environments. It has a short flowering period, usually during the rainy season, and requires specific conditions to thrive.

Due to its rarity and habitat specificity, it is often difficult to cultivate outside its natural range.
