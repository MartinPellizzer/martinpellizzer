Senecio molinarius

Senecio molinarius, commonly known as the Molin's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas. It produces small yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by fluffy seed heads.

Senecio molinarius is known for its hardy nature and is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
