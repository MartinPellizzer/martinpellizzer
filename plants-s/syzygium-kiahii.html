Syzygium kiahii

Syzygium kiahii, commonly known as the Kiahii apple.

It is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Pacific Islands, particularly Samoa and Tonga. The plant produces small, round, red or yellow fruits that are edible and have a sweet, tangy flavor. Its leaves are glossy and dark green, providing a dense canopy.

Syzygium kiahii is valued for both its ornamental appeal and its fruit, which is used in local cuisines.
