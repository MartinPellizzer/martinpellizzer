Senecio panduriformis

Senecio panduriformis, commonly known as the African buttercup, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or arid environments.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
