Spiraea prunifolia var. hupehensis

Spiraea prunifolia var. hupehensis, commonly known as the Hupeh Spiraea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, white to pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. The plant has slender, arching branches and lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

This variety is relatively low maintenance and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.
