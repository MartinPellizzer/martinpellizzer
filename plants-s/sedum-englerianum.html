Sedum englerianum

Sedum englerianum, commonly known as the Engler's stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of Central Asia.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow to orange. The plant is adapted to survive in dry conditions, storing water in its thick leaves and stems. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its resilience and ornamental value make it a desirable plant for low-maintenance landscaping.
