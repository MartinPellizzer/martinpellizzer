Solanum mellobarretoi

Solanum mellobarretoi, commonly known as the Mellobarreto's nightshade, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is native to parts of South America, particularly Brazil, and is typically found in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant is characterized by its small, often hairy leaves and clusters of yellow or white flowers. Its fruits are berries that mature to a deep red color, containing several seeds.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and research settings.
