Syzygium prasiniflorum

Syzygium prasiniflorum, commonly known as the green-flowered syzygium, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are predominantly green, which is a unique feature among Syzygium species. Its fruit is a small, round berry that turns from green to red when mature.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic value.
