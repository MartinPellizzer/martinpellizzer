Senecio taitoensis

Senecio taitoensis, commonly known as the Taitoense Groundsel, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its foliage is usually gray-green, with serrated leaves that help reduce water loss.

Senecio taitoensis is notable for its small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, contributing to its ornamental value.
