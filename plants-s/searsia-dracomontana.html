Searsia dracomontana

Searsia dracomontana, commonly known as the red hakea, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and striking red or orange flowers that appear in spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and sandy environments. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, hard seeds.
