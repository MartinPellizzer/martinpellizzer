Stelis caesariata

Stelis caesariata, commonly known as the Caesar's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows on tree branches or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

The orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded, humid environments.
