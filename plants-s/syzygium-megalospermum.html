Syzygium megalospermum

Syzygium megalospermum, commonly known as the large-seeded clove.

It is a flowering plant native to the Moluccas, Indonesia, and belongs to the Myrtaceae family. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, red-brown flowers and is closely related to the clove tree, Syzygium aromaticum. Its fruit is a drupe that contains a single large seed, which is used in traditional medicine.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been cultivated for its unique appearance and fragrance.
