Sideritis peloponnesiaca

Sideritis peloponnesiaca, commonly known as the Peloponnesian ironwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region, particularly Greece.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its upright stems, narrow leaves, and small blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous areas.

Sideritis peloponnesiaca is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
