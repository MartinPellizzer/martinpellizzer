Senecio corymbosus var. walkeri

Senecio corymbosus var. walkeri, commonly known as the African marguerite, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers surrounded by white ray florets. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is a perennial herb with a compact, bushy growth habit.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
