Scutellaria indica var. yakusimensis

Scutellaria indica var. yakusimensis, commonly known as Yakushimense Blueweed, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and opposite leaves. The plant features small, tubular blue or violet flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are typically oval and slightly hairy, with a strong aromatic scent.

It is often found in moist, shaded areas such as forests and streamside habitats.
