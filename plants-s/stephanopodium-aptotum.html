Stephanopodium aptotum

Stephanopodium aptotum, commonly known as the slender tansy, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small yellow flowers arranged in clusters. Native to the temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils. The plant is often found in meadows, along stream banks, and in woodland edges.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and habitat restoration.
