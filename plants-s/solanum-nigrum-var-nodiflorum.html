Solanum nigrum var. nodiflorum

Solanum nigrum var. nodiflorum, commonly known as black nightshade variant with clustered flowers, is a variant of the common black nightshade plant.

It is a herbaceous annual or perennial plant native to Europe, Asia, and Africa. The plant produces small, dark purple to black berries that are toxic to humans and animals. It has alternate, ovate leaves with a hairy texture and clusters of small white or pale yellow flowers.

This variant is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides, thriving in temperate climates.
