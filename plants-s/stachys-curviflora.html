Stachys curviflora

Stachys curviflora, commonly known as curledflower hedge-nettle, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with hairy surfaces and produces small, pale blue to purple flowers arranged in whorled clusters. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Stachys curviflora is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its potential health benefits.
