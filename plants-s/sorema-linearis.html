Sorema linearis

Sorema linearis, commonly known as the slender sorema, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Amaranthaceae.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of red or yellow. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of water bodies.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and aquatic landscapes.
