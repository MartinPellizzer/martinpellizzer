Solanum reflexum var. chloropetalon

Solanum reflexum var. chloropetalon, commonly known as the chloropetalon nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, oval leaves that are usually hairy and have a distinctive bluish-green hue. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers, which are typically bell-shaped and borne in dense inflorescences. Its fruit is a fleshy, yellow or orange berry that contains several seeds.

This species is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
