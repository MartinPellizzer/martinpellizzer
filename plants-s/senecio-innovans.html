Senecio innovans

Senecio innovans, commonly known as the Cape snowflake, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, white to pale blue flowers arranged in a star-like pattern, giving it its common name. It has a low-growing habit with hairy, silver-green leaves.

Senecio innovans is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
