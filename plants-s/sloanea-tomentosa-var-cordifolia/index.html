Sloanea Tomentosa Var. Cordifolia

Cordate sloanea, scientifically known as Sloanea tomentosa var. cordifolia, is a unique and striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America. It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of rainforests. Its ornamental value has made it a popular choice for botanical gardens and conservation efforts. Despite its beauty, it remains a lesser-known species within the Sloanea genus.
