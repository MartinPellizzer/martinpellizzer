Stromanthe sanguinea f. spectabilis

Stromanthe sanguinea f. spectabilis, commonly known as the velvet leaf plant, is a tropical evergreen herbaceous plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with deep green veins and bright red or pinkish margins. The plant has strap-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture, giving it its common name. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

This ornamental plant is popular in indoor gardening for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
