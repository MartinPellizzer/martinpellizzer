Sulcorebutia croceareolata

Sulcorebutia croceareolata, commonly known as the yellow areola cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to southern Africa.

It features a smooth, pale green to bluish-green stem with a distinctive yellow areola, which is a central area where spines and flowers emerge. The plant produces yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a striking contrast to its foliage. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.

Its slow growth rate and unique appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
