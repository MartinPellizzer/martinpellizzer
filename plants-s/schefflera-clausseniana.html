Schefflera clausseniana

Schefflera clausseniana, commonly known as the Philippine schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of leaves, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant has compound leaves with multiple leaflets arranged in a whorled pattern around a central stem. It produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by red, fleshy berries.

Schefflera clausseniana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
