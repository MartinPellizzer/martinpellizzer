Stelis apiculata

Stelis apiculata, commonly known as the apiculate stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit, often found growing on trees in tropical rainforests. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in moisture retention.

Stelis apiculata is valued in horticulture for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
