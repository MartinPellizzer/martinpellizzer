Stenostomum mucronatum

Stenostomum mucronatum, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, often with a red or orange throat. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant.

The species is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
