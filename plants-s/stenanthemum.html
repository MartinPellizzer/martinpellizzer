Stenanthemum

Stenanthemum, commonly known as the coastal holly or sea holly, is a genus of flowering plants native to Australia.

These plants are typically shrubs or small trees, characterized by their evergreen foliage and showy, often blue or purple, flower clusters. Stenanthemum species are adapted to coastal environments, tolerating salt spray and sandy soils. They play an important role in stabilizing dunes and providing habitat for native wildlife.
