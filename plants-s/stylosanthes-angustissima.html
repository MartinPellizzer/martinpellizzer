Stylosanthes angustissima

Stylosanthes angustissima, commonly known as narrowleaf stylo, is a perennial legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers. The plant has a deep root system that helps it survive in poor soil conditions and drought. It is widely used as a forage crop due to its high nutritional value and ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen.

Stylosanthes angustissima also plays a role in soil conservation and land rehabilitation efforts.
