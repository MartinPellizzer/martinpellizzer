Spiranthes plantaginea

Spiranthes plantaginea, commonly known as the pink spire orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, spiral-shaped inflorescence of pink to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wetlands. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, and it produces small, fleshy roots.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
