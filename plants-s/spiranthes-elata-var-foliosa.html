Spiranthes elata var. foliosa

Spiranthes elata var. foliosa, commonly known as the showy ladies' tresses, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the orchid family and is characterized by its dense, spiral arrangement of pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as wetlands and forests. It produces small, greenish-yellow berries that contain the seeds.

This variety is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
