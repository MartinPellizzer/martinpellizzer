Spiraea ogawae

Spiraea ogawae, commonly known as the Japanese spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early summer, creating clusters that resemble spires. The plant has slender, arching branches and dark green foliage that turns reddish in the fall. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

Spiraea ogawae is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
