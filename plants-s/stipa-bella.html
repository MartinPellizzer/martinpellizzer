Stipa bella

Stipa bella, commonly known as the beautiful feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Asia and Europe.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and delicate, silver-gray foliage that gives it a soft, feathery appearance. The plant produces fluffy, spherical seed heads that are often used in ornamental gardening for their aesthetic appeal. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Stipa bella is valued for its low maintenance requirements and ability to add texture and interest to garden designs.
