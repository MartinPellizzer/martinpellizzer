Symplocos laurina subsp. cochinchinensis

Symplocos laurina subsp.

cochinchinensis, commonly known as the Vietnamese sweet pepper tree, is a shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia. It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, white flowers.
