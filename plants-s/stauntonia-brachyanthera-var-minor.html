Stauntonia brachyanthera var. minor

Stauntonia brachyanthera var. minor, commonly known as the small-flowered stauntonia, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves and produces clusters of small, fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit and is often found in moist, shaded forest environments. Its flowers are typically followed by small, round, yellow fruits.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to humid climates.
