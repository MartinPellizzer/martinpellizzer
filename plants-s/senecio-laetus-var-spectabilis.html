Senecio laetus var. spectabilis

Senecio laetus var. spectabilis, commonly known as the showy groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow daisy-like flowers and linear, grass-like leaves. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other composite flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, often found in rocky or arid environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
