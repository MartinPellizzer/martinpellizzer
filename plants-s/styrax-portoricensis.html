Styrax portoricensis

Styrax portoricensis, commonly known as the Puerto Rican styrax, is a flowering plant native to the island of Puerto Rico.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded areas of the forest understory. The plant produces fragrant, white, waxy flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. Its leaves are elliptical and have a smooth, glossy surface.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
