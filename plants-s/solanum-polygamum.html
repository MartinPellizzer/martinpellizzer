Solanum polygamum

Solanum polygamum, commonly known as the purple mullein, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are hairy and have a bluish-gray hue. Its flowers are violet to purple in color, with five petals and a star-shaped appearance.

Solanum polygamum produces small, yellow, berry-like fruits that contain seeds.
