Stellaria diversiflora var. leptophylla

Stellaria diversiflora var. leptophylla, commonly known as the slenderleaf starwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It features small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The leaves are narrow and lanceolate, often covered in fine hairs, and the plant typically thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or streambanks. It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family, characterized by its delicate structure and clustered inflorescences.

This variant is distinguished by its smaller, more elongated leaves compared to other varieties within the species.
