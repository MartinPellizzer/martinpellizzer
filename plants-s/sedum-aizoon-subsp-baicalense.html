Sedum aizoon subsp. baicalense

Sedum aizoon subsp.

baicalense, commonly known as the Siberian stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the cold regions of Asia. It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant is adapted to rocky and arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves. It prefers full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its hardy nature allows it to thrive in USDA hardiness zones 4 to 8.
