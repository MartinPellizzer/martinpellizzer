Syagrus allenii

Syagrus allenii, commonly known as the Allen's palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its slender, unbranched trunk and feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to saline and sandy soils. It produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by elongated, spiny fruits.

Syagrus allenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
