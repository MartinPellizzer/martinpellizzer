Spiranthes falcata

Spiranthes falcata, commonly known as the twisted spire orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and its striking, spiral-shaped inflorescence of white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and wetlands. It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is known for its intricate floral structure.
