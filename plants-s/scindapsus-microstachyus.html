Scindapsus microstachyus

Scindapsus microstachyus, commonly known as the silver vine, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features lance-shaped leaves with a striking silver or variegated pattern that contrasts with its dark green base. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by red berries, though it is primarily cultivated for its ornamental foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity.

Scindapsus microstachyus is a popular houseplant due to its ease of care and aesthetic appeal.
