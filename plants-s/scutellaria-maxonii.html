Scutellaria maxonii

Scutellaria maxonii, commonly known as the Mexican skullcap, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes other aromatic herbs such as mint and sage. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or shaded areas.

Scutellaria maxonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
