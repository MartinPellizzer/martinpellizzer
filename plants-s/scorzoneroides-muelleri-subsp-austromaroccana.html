Scorzoneroides muelleri subsp. austromaroccana

Scorzoneroides muelleri subsp.

austromaroccana, commonly known as the Australian scorzonera, is a perennial herb native to Australia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of lance-shaped leaves.

The plant produces yellow flowers in a single head, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.

This subspecies is notable for its ornamental value and adaptability to harsh environmental conditions.
