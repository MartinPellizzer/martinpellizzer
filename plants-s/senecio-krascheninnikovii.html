Senecio krascheninnikovii

Senecio krascheninnikovii, commonly known as the Antarctic hair grass, is a flowering plant native to the cold, arid regions of southern Africa and Antarctica.

The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the short growing season in its harsh environment.
