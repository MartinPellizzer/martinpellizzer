Schoenus apogon var. laxiflorus

Schoenus apogon var. laxiflorus, commonly known as the slender-flowered bulrush, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wetland habitats such as marshes and along the edges of water bodies. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, wind-pollinated flowers.

This variant is distinguished by its lax, open inflorescence structure compared to other members of the species.
