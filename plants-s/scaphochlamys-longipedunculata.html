Scaphochlamys longipedunculata

Scaphochlamys longipedunculata, commonly known as the long-stemmed hooded orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded, moist habitats. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pale pink.
