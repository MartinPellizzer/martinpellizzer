Sansevieria longiflora var. fernandopoensis

Sansevieria longiflora var. fernandopoensis, commonly known as the Fernandopoensis Snake Plant, is a striking succulent native to Madagascar.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are dark green with prominent white or silver variegations. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or green in color. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy leaves.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and air-purifying qualities, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
