Sida allemanii

Sida allemanii, commonly known as the purpleleaf sida, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet. The plant features alternate, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often tinged with purple or reddish hues. Its small, pale purple flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom in the summer months.

Sida allemanii is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed soils and is often found in fields and along roadsides.
