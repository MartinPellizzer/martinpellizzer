Senecio polygaloides

Senecio polygaloides, commonly known as the blue ice plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. The plant is adapted to arid environments, featuring a shallow root system and water-storing tissues. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its striking color and drought tolerance.
