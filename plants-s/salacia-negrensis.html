Salacia negrensis

Salacia negrensis, commonly known as the Negrense Salacia, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Salaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and woody stems. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically clustered in racemes. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a rough texture with a serrated margin.

Salacia negrensis is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental and potential medicinal properties.
