Senecio garcibarrigae var. cataractarum

Senecio garcibarrigae var. cataractarum, commonly known as the Cataract Falls Senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant is known for its striking yellow-orange inflorescences that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is often cultivated in specialized gardens.
