Stemodia lathraia

Stemodia lathraia, commonly known as the blackwood tree, is a woody shrub or small tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black bark, which gives it its common name. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. It produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, followed by spherical, woody fruits.

Stemodia lathraia is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its hard, durable wood.
