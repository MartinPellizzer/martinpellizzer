Stipa juncoides

Stipa juncoides, commonly known as the tufted feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Asia, including China and the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and fluffy, feathery inflorescences that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Stipa juncoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and garden design.
