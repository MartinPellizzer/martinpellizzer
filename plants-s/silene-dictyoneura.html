Silene dictyoneura

Silene dictyoneura, commonly known as the fairy lantern, is a striking member of the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is a biennial or short-lived perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia. Silene dictyoneura thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.
