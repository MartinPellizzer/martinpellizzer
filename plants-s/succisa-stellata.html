Succisa stellata

Succisa stellata, commonly known as starry bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its star-shaped, bell-like flowers that range in color from white to blue or purple. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain water in arid environments. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Succisa stellata is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ease of care.
