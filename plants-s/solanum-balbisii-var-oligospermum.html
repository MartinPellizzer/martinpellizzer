Solanum balbisii var. oligospermum

Solanum balbisii var. oligospermum, commonly known as the Balbisii nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other notable plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant is characterized by its simple, ovate leaves and clusters of small, star-shaped white flowers. It produces berries that are typically yellow or orange when ripe, containing numerous seeds.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in warm climates.
