Scaphosepalum anchoriferum

Scaphosepalum anchoriferum, commonly known as the anchoring scaphosepalum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features elongated, strap-like leaves and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. Its unique floral structure includes a specialized lip that aids in pollination by specific insect species.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
