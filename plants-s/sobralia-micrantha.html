Sobralia micrantha

Sobralia micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered sobralia, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are elliptical and glossy, providing a lush backdrop to its delicate blooms.

Sobralia micrantha is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation under suitable conditions.
