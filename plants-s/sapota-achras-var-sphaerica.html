Sapota achras var. sphaerica

Sapota achras var. sphaerica, commonly known as the chikoo or sapodilla, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and produces a fruit with a thick, bumpy skin. The fruit contains a sweet, starchy pulp that is rich in nutrients and has a unique flavor. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated for both fruit production and landscaping.
