Scabiosa columbaria var. guadarramica

Scabiosa columbaria var. guadarramica, commonly known as the Guadarramica milkweed, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It features delicate, pink to purple flower heads that resemble tiny umbrellas, surrounded by white, fluffy bracts. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Its flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, making it a valuable addition to native gardens.
