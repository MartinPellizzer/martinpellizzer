Sida abscissa

Sida abscissa, commonly known as the common sida, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Sida abscissa is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and is often found in disturbed areas.
