Solanum dasyadenium

Solanum dasyadenium, commonly known as the starry nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in clusters, often during the cooler months. Its leaves are oval-shaped with a slightly hairy texture, and the fruits are small, green berries that turn red when mature.

The plant belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like tomatoes and potatoes.
