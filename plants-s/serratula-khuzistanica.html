Serratula khuzistanica

Serratula khuzistanica, commonly known as Khuzestan ragwort, is a flowering plant native to Iran.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellowish to greenish flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky, or semi-arid environments, showing adaptability to harsh conditions. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species in its native habitat.
