Serratula cetinjensis

Serratula cetinjensis, commonly known as the Cetinje serretula, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky and mountainous areas, showcasing adaptability to harsh environments. Its yellow-orange flowers bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators.
