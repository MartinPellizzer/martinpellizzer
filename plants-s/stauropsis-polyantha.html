Stauropsis polyantha

Stauropsis polyantha, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides. It belongs to the Campanulaceae family, which includes other bellflowers.

Stauropsis polyantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can thrive in a variety of soil types when provided with adequate moisture.
