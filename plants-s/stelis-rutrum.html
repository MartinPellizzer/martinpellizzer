Stelis rutrum

Stelis rutrum, commonly known as the red-stemmed orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its erect, branched stems that are typically reddish in color. Its flowers are small, clustered in inflorescences, and often have a yellowish or greenish hue with purple or red markings.

Stelis rutrum thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in the wild.
