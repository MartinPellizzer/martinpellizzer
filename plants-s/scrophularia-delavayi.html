Scrophularia delavayi

Scrophularia delavayi, commonly known as Delavay's figwort, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Himalayas and parts of China.

It typically grows in moist, rocky habitats at high altitudes. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with hairy surfaces and produces clusters of tubular, pink to purple flowers. Its flowering period occurs in late spring to early summer.

Scrophularia delavayi is valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
