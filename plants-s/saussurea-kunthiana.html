Saussurea kunthiana

Saussurea kunthiana, commonly known as the Yunnan Saussurea, is a perennial herb native to the high-altitude regions of China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems and large, showy composite flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky slopes, thriving in cool and moist environments. Its leaves are usually gray-green with a rough texture, and it produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer.

Saussurea kunthiana is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional Chinese medicine.
