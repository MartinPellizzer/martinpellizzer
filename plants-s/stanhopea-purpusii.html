Stanhopea purpusii

Stanhopea purpusii, commonly known as the Purpus's stanhopea, is a tropical orchid native to the wetlands of Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange and yellow flowers, which are often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in seasonal conditions.

Stanhopea purpusii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts worldwide.
