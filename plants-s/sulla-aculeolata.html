Sulla aculeolata

Sulla aculeolata, commonly known as prickly sowthistle, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been introduced to other regions. It typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in fields, roadsides, and waste areas.

Sulla aculeolata is an annual herb that spreads through seeds dispersed by wind and animals.
