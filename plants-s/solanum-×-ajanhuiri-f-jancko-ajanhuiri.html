Solanum × ajanhuiri f. jancko-ajanhuiri

Solanum × ajanhuiri f. jancko-ajanhuiri, commonly known as the Jancko-ajanhuiri tomato, is a hybrid plant belonging to the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is characterized by its small, round fruits that typically range in color from red to orange, depending on the variety. The plant is native to the Andean regions of South America, where it thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil. It exhibits a compact growth habit with hairy leaves and flowers that are usually white or pale yellow.

This variety is valued for its unique flavor and is often cultivated for both culinary and ornamental purposes.
