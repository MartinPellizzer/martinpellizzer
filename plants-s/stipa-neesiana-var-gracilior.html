Stipa neesiana var. gracilior

Stipa neesiana var. gracilior, commonly known as slender needlegrass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. The plant features slender, cylindrical stems that are often branched and covered with fine, silky hairs. Its inflorescence consists of a dense, spike-like cluster of spikelets, which are characteristic of the Poaceae family.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
