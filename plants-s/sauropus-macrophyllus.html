Sauropus macrophyllus

Sauropus macrophyllus, commonly known as the large-leaf sauropus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its leaves are glossy and can vary in color from dark green to reddish-purple.

It prefers moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
