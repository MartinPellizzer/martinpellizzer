Syngramma luzonica

Syngramma luzonica, commonly known as the Luzon bamboo.

It is a member of the grass family, Poaceae, and is native to the Philippines. This plant is characterized by its slender, hollow stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests.

Syngramma luzonica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in horticultural settings.
