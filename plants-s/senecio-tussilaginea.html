Senecio tussilaginea

Senecio tussilaginea, commonly known as the coughweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid to semi-arid environments. It features narrow, blue-green leaves and produces yellowish to orange flower heads.

Senecio tussilaginea is often used in traditional medicine for its potential respiratory benefits.
