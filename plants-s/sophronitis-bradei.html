Sophronitis bradei

Sophronitis bradei, commonly known as the Brade's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded environments at high elevations. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with a rhizome that produces multiple pseudobulbs. Its flowers are usually small, with a distinctive coloration that ranges from white to pale pink.

Sophronitis bradei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
