Scutellaria pekinensis var. transitra

Scutellaria pekinensis var. transitra, commonly known as the Peking skullcap, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue to purple in color, arranged in whorled clusters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered with fine hairs.

This species is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
