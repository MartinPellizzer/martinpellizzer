Schrankia macrostachya

Schrankia macrostachya, commonly known as the giant marsh orchid, is a herbaceous plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces large, showy inflorescences with numerous pale yellow to greenish flowers. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Schrankia macrostachya thrives in wetland environments and is adapted to tolerate waterlogged soils.
