Silene yunnanensis

Silene yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan campion, is a flowering plant native to China, particularly in the Yunnan province.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its pink to purple flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and is adapted to cool, moist environments. Silene yunnanensis is often found in meadows and rocky slopes at elevations ranging from 2,000 to 3,500 meters.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in the study of alpine flora.
