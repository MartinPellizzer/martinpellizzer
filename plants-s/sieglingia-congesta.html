Sieglingia congesta

Sieglingia congesta, commonly known as the clustered Sieglingia, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. Sieglingia congesta is often found in open woodlands and along the edges of grasslands.
