Sphaerostichum abbreviatum

Sphaerostichum abbreviatum, commonly known as the dwarf sphaerostichum, is a small, moss-like plant belonging to the family Sphaerostichaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, cushion-like growth form and is typically found in moist, shaded environments. The plant has short, upright stems with leaf-like structures that are often curved or twisted. Its spore-producing structures, called sporophytes, are borne on short stalks at the tips of the stems.

Sphaerostichum abbreviatum plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing microhabitats for other organisms in its ecosystem.
