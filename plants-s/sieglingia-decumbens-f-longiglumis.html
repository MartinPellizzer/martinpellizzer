Sieglingia decumbens f. longiglumis

Sieglingia decumbens f. longiglumis, commonly known as the long glume sieglingia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is a perennial grass characterized by its low-growing habit and dense foliage. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences. Its inflorescence is notable for the elongated glumes that give the variety its specific name.

This variety is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands.
