Stelis synsepala

Stelis synsepala, commonly known as the clustered lady's tresses, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, compact growth habit with a rosette of fleshy, dark green leaves. The plant produces a single, branched inflorescence bearing numerous small, white to pale pink flowers. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding substrates.

Stelis synsepala thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in tropical rainforests.
