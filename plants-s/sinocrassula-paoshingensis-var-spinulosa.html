Sinocrassula paoshingensis var. spinulosa

Sinocrassula paoshingensis var. spinulosa, commonly known as the Shaoxing crassula, is a succulent plant native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and fleshy, bluish-green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white powder. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a potted plant.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
