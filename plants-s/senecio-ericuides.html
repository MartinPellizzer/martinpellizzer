Senecio ericuides

Senecio ericuides, commonly known as the silverleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that typically reaches heights of about 30 centimeters. The plant features dense, silver-gray foliage with hairy leaves that help reduce water loss in arid conditions. Its flowers are yellow and occur in clusters, blooming in spring and early summer.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
