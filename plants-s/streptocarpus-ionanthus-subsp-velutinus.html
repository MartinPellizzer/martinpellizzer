Streptocarpus ionanthus subsp. velutinus

Streptocarpus ionanthus subsp.

velutinus, commonly known as the velvet streptocarpus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, hairy leaves that give it its common name.

The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.
