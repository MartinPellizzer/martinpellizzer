Scheelea degranvillei

Scheelea degranvillei, commonly known as the De Granville's pampas grass, is a perennial grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces panicles of small, fluffy flowers that are often white or pale in color. It thrives in open, sunny environments and is adapted to arid or semi-arid climates.

Scheelea degranvillei is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
