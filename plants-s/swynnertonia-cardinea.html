Swynnertonia cardinea

Swynnertonia cardinea, commonly known as the Brazilian nettle, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It is a member of the Urticaceae family, which includes other nettle species. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in moist forests.

Swynnertonia cardinea is noted for its stinging hairs, which can cause irritation upon contact with the skin.
