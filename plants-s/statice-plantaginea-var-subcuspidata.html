Statice plantaginea var. subcuspidata

Statice plantaginea var. subcuspidata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting flowers, which are often used in dried floral arrangements.
