Sedum yunnanense var. valerianoides

Sedum yunnanense var. valerianoides, commonly known as the Yunnan sedum, is a succulent plant native to China.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It is often found growing in rocky or mountainous regions, where it can tolerate harsh conditions.

Due to its hardy nature and ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens.
