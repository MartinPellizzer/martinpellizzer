Salvia souliei

Salvia souliei, commonly known as the Chinese sage, is a perennial shrub native to China.

It features striking violet-blue to purple flowers that bloom in whorls along the stems. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green in color, with a fuzzy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Salvia souliei is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
