Sphaeranthus mossambiquensis

Sphaeranthus mossambiquensis, commonly known as the African starflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and small, star-shaped yellow flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky habitats.

Sphaeranthus mossambiquensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
