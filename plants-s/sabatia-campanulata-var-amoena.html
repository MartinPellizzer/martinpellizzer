Sabatia campanulata var. amoena

Sabatia campanulata var. amoena, commonly known as the showy sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils, often in open habitats such as fields and dunes. It has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves, and it blooms from late spring to early summer.
