Specklinia llamachoi

Specklinia llamachoi, commonly known as the Llama Orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in high-altitude grasslands and rocky areas. The plant has a slender, wiry stem with a few leaves, and it produces delicate, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive yellow lip. It is known for its resilience to harsh environmental conditions and is often found in areas with limited soil.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
