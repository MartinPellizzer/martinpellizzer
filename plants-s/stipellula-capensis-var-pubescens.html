Stipellula capensis var. pubescens

Stipellula capensis var. pubescens, commonly known as the Cape stipple, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its distinctive stipitate (bead-like) inflorescences. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered with fine pubescence, giving the plant its varietal name. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color.

This plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.
