Stelis capitata

Stelis capitata, commonly known as the helmet orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its unique, helmet-shaped inflorescence. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte or lithophyte, attaching itself to trees or rocks. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinct, elongated spur.

Stelis capitata thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, loose soil for optimal growth.
