Senecio thomasii

Senecio thomasii, commonly known as Thomas's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is a low-growing perennial with narrow, gray-green leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and ragworts. It thrives in rocky soils and arid environments, often found in desert regions.

Its structure and growth pattern make it well adapted to survive in harsh, dry conditions.
