Statice owerinii

Statice owerinii, commonly known as sea lavender, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and vibrant blue to purple flower heads. The plant thrives in salty, well-drained soils and is often found in dunes and coastal grasslands. Its flowers are typically arranged in dense spikes and bloom in late summer.

Statice owerinii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize coastal soils.
