Siphonochilus kilimanensis

Siphonochilus kilimanensis, commonly known as the Kilimanjaro siphonochilus, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of East Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its terrestrial growth habit. The plant produces small, delicate flowers with a unique coloration that varies depending on environmental conditions. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine by local communities.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
