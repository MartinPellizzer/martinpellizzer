Sida Littoralis

Sida littoralis is a flowering plant commonly found in coastal areas around the world. It is known for its ability to thrive in salty and humid environments. This plant has small, pale green leaves and produces clusters of tiny white flowers. Sida littoralis is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its hardy nature. Its adaptability makes it an important species for stabilizing shorelines.
