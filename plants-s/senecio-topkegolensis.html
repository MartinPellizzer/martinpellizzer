Senecio topkegolensis

Senecio topkegolensis, commonly known as the Kgalagadi senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense clusters. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, showcasing adaptations to drought conditions. Its leaves are usually narrow and succulent, helping to conserve water.

It plays a role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators and habitat for small fauna.
