Senecio kuekenthalii

Senecio kuekenthalii, commonly known as the Kuekenthal's groundsels, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky, arid environments and has a low, spreading growth habit. Its leaves are linear and covered in fine hairs, while its yellow flowers bloom in clusters.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
