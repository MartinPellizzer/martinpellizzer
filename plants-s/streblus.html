Streblus

Streblus, commonly known as the ironwood tree, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Moraceae.

It is characterized by its hard, dense wood, which is highly valued for construction and tool-making. The plants typically have simple, alternate leaves and produce small, clustered flowers. Streblus species are native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, Africa, and the Pacific Islands.
