Scabiosa columbaria var. lucida

Scabiosa columbaria var. lucida, commonly known as the shining heart's-ease, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features delicate, pink to purple flower clusters that resemble small hearts, which give it its common name. The plant has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine, soft hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its short stature and charming blooms make it a valuable addition to pollinator-friendly landscapes.
