Stapeliopsis ballyi

Stapeliopsis ballyi, commonly known as the Bally's milkweed, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed stems and clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers. Stapeliopsis ballyi is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience.
