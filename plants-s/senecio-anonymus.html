Senecio anonymus

Senecio anonymus, commonly known as the ghost plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its small, inconspicuous flowers. Senecio anonymus is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
