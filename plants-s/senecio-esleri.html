Senecio esleri

Senecio esleri, commonly known as the Blue-Eyed Mary, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers that resemble eyes, hence its common name. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its showy inflorescence. Senecio esleri typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant color and unique appearance.
