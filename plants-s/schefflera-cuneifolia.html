Schefflera cuneifolia

Schefflera cuneifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf umbrella tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green, elliptical leaves that are arranged in a distinctive umbrella-like pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by red berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Schefflera cuneifolia is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
