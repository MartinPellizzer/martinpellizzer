Spiranthes macrantha

Spiranthes macrantha, commonly known as the showy spiranthes, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its delicate, spiraling arrangement of white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and wetlands. Its roots form a dense network that helps stabilize soil and support its growth.

Spiranthes macrantha is also valued for its ecological role in supporting pollinators like bees and moths.
