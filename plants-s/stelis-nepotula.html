Stelis nepotula

Stelis nepotula, commonly known as the orchid vine, is a tropical orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant features long, slender stems with opposite, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a fine pubescence. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Its unique morphology and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for greenhouse cultivation.
