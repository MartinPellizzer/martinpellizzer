Sabicea discolor var. laxothrysa

Sabicea discolor var. laxothrysa, commonly known as the variable sabbatia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptic in shape, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

This variant is distinguished by its variable leaf morphology and floral structure compared to other Sabicea species.
