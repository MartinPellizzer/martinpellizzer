Solanum hastatilobum

Solanum hastatilobum, commonly known as the starry nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with hairy stems and broad, ovate leaves. Its flowers are usually violet or blue, with five petals that are prominently lobed.

Solanum hastatilobum is often found in moist, shaded areas and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
