Smilax randaiensis

Smilax randaiensis, commonly known as the Japanese arrowroot or Japanese smilax, is a climbing shrub native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the family Araceae and is characterized by its woody stems and spiny structures. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped with a smooth or slightly serrated margin.
