Spermacoce affinis

Spermacoce affinis, commonly known as the small cowpea or velvet bean, is a tropical annual herb native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous ellipsoidal seeds. It is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and agricultural fields.
