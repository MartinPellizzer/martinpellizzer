Sorbaria stellipila var. incerta

Sorbaria stellipila var. incerta, commonly known as the uncertain star-leaf sorbaria, is a variant of the star-leaf sorbaria, a member of the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, star-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a whorled pattern along the stems. The plant produces small, white to pinkish flowers in early summer, which are clustered in racemes. Its foliage is often a deep green, with a slightly serrated margin.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique leaf structure and moderate growth habit.
