Scutellaria Laeteviolacea Var. Maekawae

Scutellaria laeteviolacea var. maekawae is a rare and lesser-known flowering plant native to specific regions of East Asia. This variant of Scutellaria is distinguished by its unique floral structure and vibrant violet hues. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth. Due to habitat loss, it faces increasing threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this botanical treasure.
