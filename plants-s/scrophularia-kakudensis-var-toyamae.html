Scrophularia kakudensis var. toyamae

Scrophularia kakudensis var. toyamae, commonly known as Toyama Scrophularia, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae, which includes many species with medicinal and ornamental value. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are tubular and usually pink or purple in color.

This variant is distinguished by its specific geographic distribution and morphological characteristics compared to other varieties of Scrophularia.
