Stipa occidentalis var. minor

Stipa occidentalis var. minor, commonly known as western needlegrass dwarf form, is a grass species native to western North America.

It is a low-growing perennial with slender, wiry stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

Its botanical features make it an important component of native plant communities in arid regions.
