Scrophularia alaschanica

Scrophularia alaschanica, commonly known as the Alaska Scrophularia, is a flowering plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions of North America.

The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Scrophularia alaschanica is part of the Scrophulariaceae family, known for its medicinal and ornamental value in some species.
