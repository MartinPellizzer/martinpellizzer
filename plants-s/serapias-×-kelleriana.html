Serapias × kelleriana

Serapias × kelleriana, commonly known as the hybrid orchid gentian, is a naturally occurring hybrid between Serapias aucubifolia and Serapias grandiflora.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe. The plant features striking, showy flowers with a deep blue or violet color, often with contrasting yellow markings. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, typically arranged in a basal rosette.

Serapias × kelleriana thrives in well-drained, calcareous soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.
