Sabina chinensis var. sargentii

Sabina chinensis var. sargentii, commonly known as the Sargent's Chinese thuja, is a cultivar of the Chinese thuja, native to China.

It is a slow-growing evergreen conifer with a compact, pyramidal shape and dark green, needle-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green female cones and is often used in ornamental horticulture for its dense foliage and hardy nature. It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a specimen plant.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to both landscape design and botanical collections.
