Solanum calvum

Solanum calvum, commonly known as the smooth nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by berries that mature to a dark purple color. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and often covered with a fine layer of hair.

Solanum calvum belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae), which includes other well-known plants such as tomatoes and potatoes.
