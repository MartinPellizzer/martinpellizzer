Statice leonis

Statice leonis, commonly known as the lion's statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are usually pink, red, or purple, and they bloom from late summer to early autumn.

Statice leonis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.
