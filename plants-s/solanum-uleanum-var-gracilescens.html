Solanum uleanum var. gracilescens

Solanum uleanum var. gracilescens, commonly known as the slender nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 meter, featuring hairy stems and alternate leaves with serrated edges. Its small, white flowers are followed by berries that mature to a dark purple color.

This species is often found in moist, shaded environments and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.
