Syzygium mumbwaense

Syzygium mumbwaense, commonly known as the Mumbwa apple.

It is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family, native to the tropical regions of Africa. The plant is characterized by its attractive, fragrant white flowers and smooth, glossy leaves. It produces small, edible fruits that are typically yellow or red when ripe.

Syzygium mumbwaense is often grown for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and potential medicinal uses.
