Sphaeranthus tenuis

Sphaeranthus tenuis, commonly known as slender globeweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its small, spherical flower heads are often surrounded by narrow, grass-like leaves.

Sphaeranthus tenuis is known for its resilience and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
