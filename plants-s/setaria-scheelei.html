Setaria scheelei

Setaria scheelei, commonly known as the slender dropseed, is a perennial grass native to parts of North America.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and sandy soils. The plant has slender, erect stems and small, spike-like inflorescences that produce numerous tiny grains. Its leaves are narrow and often have a bluish-green hue, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Setaria scheelei is valued for its drought tolerance and ability to stabilize soil in restoration projects.
