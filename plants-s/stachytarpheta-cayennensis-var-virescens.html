Stachytarpheta cayennensis var. virescens

Stachytarpheta cayennensis var. virescens, commonly known as the greenleaf ironweed, is a perennial shrub native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to pink, attracting various pollinators. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, sandy soils.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
