Stelis chasme

Stelis chasme, commonly known as the mountain orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic growth habit. The plant features linear, grass-like leaves and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white, yellow, or green. It thrives in humid environments with indirect light and well-draining substrates.

Stelis chasme is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitats.
