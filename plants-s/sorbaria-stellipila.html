Sorbaria stellipila

Sorbaria stellipila, commonly known as the starry sorbaria, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features compound leaves with slender, serrated leaflets that turn yellow in autumn. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers in clusters during late spring to early summer. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds.

Sorbaria stellipila is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
