Sagittaria Pugioniformis Var. Rhombifolia

Water arrowhead, scientifically known as Sagittaria pugioniformis var. rhombifolia, is a striking aquatic plant native to wetland environments. Its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves and bright green color make it a popular choice for water gardens and pond landscapes. This variety thrives in shallow waters, often forming dense mats that provide habitat for aquatic life. Its ability to filter water and stabilize sediment makes it ecologically valuable. With its elegant appearance and functional benefits, it is both a decorative and practical addition to aquatic ecosystems.
