Swertia tetrapetala f. albiflora

Swertia tetrapetala f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered swertia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Gentianaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.
