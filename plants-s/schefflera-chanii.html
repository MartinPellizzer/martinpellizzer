Schefflera chanii

Schefflera chanii, commonly known as the Philippine umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of large, leathery, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by bright red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Schefflera chanii is also valued for its ability to tolerate indoor conditions, making it a popular choice for houseplants.
