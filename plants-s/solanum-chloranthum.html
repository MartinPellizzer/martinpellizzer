Solanum chloranthum

Solanum chloranthum, commonly known as the green-flowered nightshade, is a member of the Solanaceae family.

It is a herbaceous plant native to South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, and its leaves are typically oval with a hairy texture. Solanum chloranthum produces small, round berries that turn from green to yellow or red as they mature.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests or wetlands.
