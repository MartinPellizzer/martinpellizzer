Schistostemon densiflorus

Schistostemon densiflorus, commonly known as the denseflower schistostemon, is a member of the Campanulaceae family.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white to pale blue. Due to its ornamental value and rarity, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
