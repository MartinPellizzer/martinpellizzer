Spathiphyllum schlechteri

Spathiphyllum schlechteri, commonly known as the Schlechter's peace lily, is a tropical evergreen plant native to the Philippines.

It features broad, dark green, heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces striking white spathe flowers that are surrounded by a modified leaf called a spadix. It is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its air-purifying properties.

Spathiphyllum schlechteri is popular as a decorative indoor plant due to its elegant appearance and low maintenance requirements.
