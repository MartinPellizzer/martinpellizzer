Sticherus hirtus

Sticherus hirtus, commonly known as the hairy sticherus, is a small, perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. The plant features a creeping rhizome with opposite, ovate leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense inflorescences, and are usually greenish-white in color.

Sticherus hirtus is valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
