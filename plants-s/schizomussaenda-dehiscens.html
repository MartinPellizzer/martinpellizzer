Schizomussaenda dehiscens

Schizomussaenda dehiscens, commonly known as the African buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features showy yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer, often arranged in umbel-like clusters. The plant has alternate, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, white pubescence. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens or borders.

Schizomussaenda dehiscens is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
