Statice armeria var. hortensis

Statice armeria var. hortensis, commonly known as sea thrift, is a hardy perennial plant native to coastal regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant thrives in well-drained, sandy or gravelly soils and is highly tolerant of salt spray and coastal conditions. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating.

Statice armeria var. hortensis is widely cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in challenging environments.
