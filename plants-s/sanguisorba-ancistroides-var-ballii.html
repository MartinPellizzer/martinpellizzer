Sanguisorba ancistroides var. ballii

Sanguisorba ancistroides var. ballii, commonly known as Ball's false spurge, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its upright, branched stems and clusters of small, reddish-brown flowers. The plant produces distinctive, translucent, red-tinged leaves that are often used in ornamental gardening.
