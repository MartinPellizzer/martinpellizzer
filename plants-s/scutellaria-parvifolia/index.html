Scutellaria Parvifolia

Scutellaria parvifolia is a delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of East Asia. It is known for its small, pale blue or purple flowers and its unique, lance-shaped leaves. This species thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in woodland or rocky habitats. Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is increasingly sought after by botanists and gardeners. Its medicinal properties have also sparked interest in traditional herbal medicine.
