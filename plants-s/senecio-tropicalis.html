Senecio tropicalis

Senecio tropicalis, commonly known as the tropical groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features alternate, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in some regions.
