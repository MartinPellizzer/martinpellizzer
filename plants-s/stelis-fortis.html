Stelis fortis

Stelis fortis, commonly known as the hardy orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features a slender, upright stem with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and usually white or pale pink, arranged in a spike.

Stelis fortis is known for its resilience and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
