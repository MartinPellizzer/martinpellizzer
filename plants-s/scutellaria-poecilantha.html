Scutellaria poecilantha

Scutellaria poecilantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color, arranged in whorls along the stem. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Scutellaria poecilantha is typically found in moist, open habitats such as woodlands and wetlands.
