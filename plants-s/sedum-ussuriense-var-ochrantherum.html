Sedum ussuriense var. ochrantherum

Sedum ussuriense var. ochrantherum, commonly known as the Ussuri sedum, is a succulent plant native to eastern Asia.

It features fleshy, upright stems and produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange. The leaves are typically green and succulent, adapted to store water, making it drought-tolerant. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging environments.
