Silene turkestanica

Silene turkestanica, commonly known as the Turkestan campion, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while its stems are upright and branched.

Silene turkestanica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
