Scrophularia × costei

Scrophularia × costei, commonly known as the hybrid scarlet pimpernel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae, which includes many ornamental and medicinal plants. The plant features upright, branching stems covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often slightly hairy. It produces clusters of small, two-lipped flowers that are typically pink or red, blooming from late spring to early autumn.

Scrophularia × costei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
