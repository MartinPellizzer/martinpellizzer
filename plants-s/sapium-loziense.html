Sapium loziense

Sapium loziense, commonly known as African senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant has compound leaves with three to seven pairs of leaflets, and it produces yellow flowers in clusters. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

The plant is adapted to grow in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in riverine forests.
