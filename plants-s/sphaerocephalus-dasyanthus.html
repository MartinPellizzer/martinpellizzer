Sphaerocephalus dasyanthus

Sphaerocephalus dasyanthus, commonly known as the globular-headed grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical inflorescence that gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered at the top of the plant.

Sphaerocephalus dasyanthus is often used in ornamental gardening for its unique, rounded appearance.
