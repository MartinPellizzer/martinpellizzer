Scorzonera dincii

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and basal rosette of leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers with ray florets, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine environments.
