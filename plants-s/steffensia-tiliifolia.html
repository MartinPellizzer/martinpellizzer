Steffensia tiliifolia

Steffensia tiliifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf tiliifolia, is a member of the Malvaceae family and is native to the southeastern United States.

It is a shrub that typically grows in wetlands and along waterways, thriving in moist to wet soil conditions. Its flowers are small and clustered, producing pale yellow to greenish blooms that appear in late spring.

Steffensia tiliifolia plays a role in supporting local wildlife, particularly insects, due to its nectar-rich flowers.
