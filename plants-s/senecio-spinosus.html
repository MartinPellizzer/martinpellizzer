Senecio spinosus

Senecio spinosus, commonly known as the spiny groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and leaves, which provide a defense mechanism against herbivores. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in dry, rocky environments and is often found in coastal areas.

Senecio spinosus is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
