Stachys montenegrina

Stachys montenegrina, commonly known as the Montenegrin hedge-nettle, is a perennial herb native to the Balkan region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features square stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture. Its small, tubular flowers are typically pink or purple, arranged in whorls along the stem.

Stachys montenegrina is often found in rocky or dry habitats and is valued for its ornamental and potential medicinal properties.
