Saxifraga × gentyana

Saxifraga × gentyana, commonly known as the hybrid saxifrage, is a perennial plant belonging to the Saxifragaceae family.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit, with clustered stems and small, often white or pinkish flowers. The plant typically thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are usually succulent, with a waxy texture that helps retain moisture.

Saxifraga × gentyana is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal in garden settings.
