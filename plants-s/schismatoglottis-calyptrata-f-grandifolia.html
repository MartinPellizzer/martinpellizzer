Schismatoglottis calyptrata f. grandifolia

Schismatoglottis calyptrata f. grandifolia, commonly known as the large-flowered schismatoglottis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a rhizomatous herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in the Philippines. The plant features broad, strap-like leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. Its most distinguishing characteristic is the showy, fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in tropical gardens.
