Stellaria patens var. patens

Stellaria patens var. patens, commonly known as common starwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

It features small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to July. The plant has slender, branching stems covered with fine hairs and produces small, oval-shaped leaves arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along stream banks.

Its flowering display attracts various pollinators, including bees and flies.
