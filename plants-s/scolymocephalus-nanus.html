Scolymocephalus nanus

Scolymocephalus nanus, commonly known as the dwarf scolymus, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows to a height of less than 10 centimeters, with delicate, hairy stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, aiding in water retention. Its flowers are usually pale blue or purple, and it blooms in the summer months.

Scolymocephalus nanus is well adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in sunny, well-drained habitats.
