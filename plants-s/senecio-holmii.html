Senecio holmii

Senecio holmii, commonly known as Holm's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in dry, open habitats. It produces small, yellowish to white flowers that bloom in the spring.

Senecio holmii is generally low-growing, with narrow, gray-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs.
