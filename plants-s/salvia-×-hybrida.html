Salvia × hybrida

Salvia × hybrida, commonly known as the hybrid sage, is a perennial plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that come in a wide range of colors, including blue, purple, red, and white. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Salvia × hybrida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly used in gardens and landscapes.
