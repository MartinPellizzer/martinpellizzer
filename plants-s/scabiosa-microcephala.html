Scabiosa microcephala

Scabiosa microcephala, commonly known as the dwarf scabious, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink, purple, and white. The plant features slender, hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its delicate appearance and extended blooming period make it a valuable addition to pollinator-friendly landscapes.
