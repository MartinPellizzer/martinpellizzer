Sideritis scythica

Sideritis scythica, commonly known as Scythian woundwort, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in sunny environments. Its small, tubular flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom in summer.

Sideritis scythica is known for its medicinal properties, traditionally used to treat respiratory and digestive ailments.
