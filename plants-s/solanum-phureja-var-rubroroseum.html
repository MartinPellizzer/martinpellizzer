Solanum phureja var. rubroroseum

Solanum phureja var. rubroroseum, commonly known as the red rose tomato, is a variety of the nightshade family native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red to pinkish-orange fruit, which develops a striking coloration as it matures. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with hairy stems and broad, ovate leaves. Its flowers are yellow with purple veins, and it produces small, oval-shaped fruits that are prized for their unique color and flavor.

This variety is often cultivated for ornamental purposes and in specialty tomato gardens.
