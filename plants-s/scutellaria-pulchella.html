Scutellaria pulchella

Scutellaria pulchella, commonly known as blue skullcap, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, blue to violet flowers clustered in whorls. It thrives in moist, woodland environments and is often found in shaded areas.

Scutellaria pulchella is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
