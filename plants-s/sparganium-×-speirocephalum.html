Sparganium × speirocephalum

Sparganium × speirocephalum, commonly known as the hybrid bur rush, is a wetland plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is a sterile hybrid between Sparganium emersum and Sparganium angustifolium, resulting in unique morphological characteristics. The plant forms dense stands in shallow water, with tall, cylindrical stems that bear spadices of yellowish-brown spikelets. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, typically ranging from 10 to 30 cm in length.

Sparganium × speirocephalum plays an important role in stabilizing shorelines and providing habitat for aquatic organisms.
