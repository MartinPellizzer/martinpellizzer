Syringa × dubia f. rubra

Syringa × dubia f. rubra, commonly known as the red flowered weeping bush honeysuckle, is a cultivar of the weeping bush honeysuckle.

It is characterized by its striking red flowers that appear in clusters, adding vibrant color to gardens. The plant has long, drooping branches that create a weeping effect, making it visually striking. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and seasonal interest.
