Stelis concaviflora

Stelis concaviflora, commonly known as the concave-flowered stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a lithophytic plant, meaning it grows on rocks, often in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a short, creeping stem with clustered, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, white or pale green, with a distinctive concave shape and a strong, spicy fragrance.

It is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
