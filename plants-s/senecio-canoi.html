Senecio canoi

Senecio canoi, commonly known as the narrowleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats. Its leaves are narrow and linear, with a rough texture, and it produces small yellow flowers in clusters.

Senecio canoi is often used in rock gardens due to its hardy nature and ornamental appeal.
