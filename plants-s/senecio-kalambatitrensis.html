Senecio kalambatitrensis

Senecio kalambatitrensis, commonly known as the Kalambatiti Senecio, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Tanzania.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in high-altitude environments. Due to its limited distribution and unique habitat, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
