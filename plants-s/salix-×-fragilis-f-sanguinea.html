Salix × fragilis f. sanguinea

Salix × fragilis f. sanguinea, commonly known as the weeping willow with red stems, is a cultivar of the weeping willow tree.

It is characterized by its delicate, cascading branches that create a striking, flowing appearance. The plant features slender, reddish-brown stems that contrast beautifully with its fresh green foliage. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This ornamental tree is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal and ability to provide shade in garden settings.
