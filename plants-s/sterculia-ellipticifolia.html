Sterculia ellipticifolia

Sterculia ellipticifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaf sterculia, is a tropical tree native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers that are followed by woody pods containing edible seeds. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The tree is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in some indigenous cultures.
