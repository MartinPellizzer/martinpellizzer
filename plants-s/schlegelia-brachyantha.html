Schlegelia brachyantha

Schlegelia brachyantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved schlegelia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color, arranged in whorls along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Schlegelia brachyantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
