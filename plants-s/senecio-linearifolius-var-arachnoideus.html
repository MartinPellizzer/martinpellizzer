Senecio linearifolius var. arachnoideus

Senecio linearifolius var. arachnoideus, commonly known as the silky sandmat, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features elongated, narrow leaves that are covered in a fine, silky white hair, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small yellow flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained sandy soils and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
