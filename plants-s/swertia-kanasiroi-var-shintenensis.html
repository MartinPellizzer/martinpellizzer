Swertia kanasiroi var. shintenensis

Swertia kanasiroi var. shintenensis, commonly known as the Tibetan gentian, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in cool and moist conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

The species is noted for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional Tibetan medicine.
