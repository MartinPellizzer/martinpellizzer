Senecio alloeophyllus var. alloeophyllus

Senecio alloeophyllus var. alloeophyllus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb with a compact, cushion-like growth habit, often forming dense colonies. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine, white hairs. Its flowers are small, yellow, and clustered in heads, blooming from spring to early summer.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
