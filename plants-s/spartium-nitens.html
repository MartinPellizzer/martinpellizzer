Spartium nitens

Spartium nitens, commonly known as the silver spartium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its seeds are enclosed in pods and are adapted for dispersal by wind.

Spartium nitens is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its hardy nature and ability to stabilize soil.
