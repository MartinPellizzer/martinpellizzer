Saxifraga gemmuligera

Saxifraga gemmuligera, commonly known as the gemmuligera saxifrage, is a flowering plant belonging to the Saxifragaceae family.

It is native to the alpine regions of Europe and is characterized by its rosette-forming growth habit. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are typically succulent and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Saxifraga gemmuligera is adapted to harsh, rocky environments and is often found in mountainous areas.
