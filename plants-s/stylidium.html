Stylidium

Stylidium, commonly known as the sticky seed plant, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Commelinaceae.

These plants are characterized by their unique seed structure, where the seeds are attached to a fleshy, sticky stalk that aids in seed dispersal. The genus includes over 100 species, many of which are native to Australia and South America.
