Satyrium volkensii

Satyrium volkensii, commonly known as the Volkens's orchid, is a species of orchid native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its unique and intricate floral structure, featuring a spurred labellum and showy petals. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its flowers are often fragrant and attract various pollinators, including bees and moths.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
