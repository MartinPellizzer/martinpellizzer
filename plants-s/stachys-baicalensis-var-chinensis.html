Stachys baicalensis var. chinensis

Stachys baicalensis var. chinensis, commonly known as Chinese betony, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features square, hairy stems and opposite, heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces spikes of violet-blue flowers in late summer, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value and traditional medicinal uses make it a popular choice in gardens and herbal medicine.
