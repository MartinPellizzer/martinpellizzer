Solanum buddlejifolium

Solanum buddlejifolium, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and small, star-shaped flowers that are typically purple or white. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in shaded, moist environments.

Due to its toxic properties, it is important to handle the plant with care.
