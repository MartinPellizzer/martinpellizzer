Stemmadenia insignis

Stemmadenia insignis, commonly known as the African star gooseberry, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, with woody stems that are often branched. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, followed by bright red, fleshy berries. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a rough, hairy texture.

The plant is adapted to arid conditions and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
