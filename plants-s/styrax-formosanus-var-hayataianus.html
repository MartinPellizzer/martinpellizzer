Styrax formosanus var. hayataianus

Styrax formosanus var. hayataianus, commonly known as the Hayata's styrax, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the family Styracaceae and is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
