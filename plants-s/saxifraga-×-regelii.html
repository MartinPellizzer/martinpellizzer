Saxifraga × regelii

Saxifraga × regelii, commonly known as the alpine saxifrage, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Saxifragaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and is characterized by its rosette-forming growth habit. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers in early summer, typically arranged in clusters. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky areas.

Saxifraga × regelii is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in garden settings.
