Stelis duckei

Stelis duckei, commonly known as the Duckei orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. The plant features slender, upright stems and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Stelis duckei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or terrariums.
