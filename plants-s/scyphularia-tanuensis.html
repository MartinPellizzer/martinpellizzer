Scyphularia tanuensis

Scyphularia tanuensis, commonly known as the slender scyphularia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, yellow, and arranged in clusters, typically blooming in late summer.

Scyphularia tanuensis is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
