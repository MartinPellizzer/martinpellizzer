Styrax suberifolius var. caloneurus

Styrax suberifolius var. caloneurus, commonly known as the California styrax, is a deciduous shrub native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has ovate to elliptic leaves with a glossy, dark green upper surface and a lighter underside. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its aromatic foliage and showy blooms.
