Schefflera aurata

Schefflera aurata, commonly known as the golden schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like cluster of palmate leaves, which are typically glossy and bright green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in outdoor gardens.

Schefflera aurata is valued for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to various light conditions.
