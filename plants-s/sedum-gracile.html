Sedum gracile

Sedum gracile, commonly known as the slender stonecrop, is a low-growing succulent native to arid and rocky environments.

It features fleshy, narrow leaves that are typically blue-green in color, adapted to store water efficiently. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Sedum gracile is a hardy perennial that is relatively easy to care for, requiring minimal water once established.
