Statice delicatula var. subrotundifolia

Statice delicatula var. subrotundifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, showy flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The leaves are narrow and slightly rounded, giving the plant a delicate appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and drought tolerance.
