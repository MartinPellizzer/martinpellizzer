Stelis acrisepala

Stelis acrisepala, commonly known as the elegant orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often found in forest undergrowth. The plant features a slender, upright stem with several elongated, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and usually white or pale yellow, arranged in a loose spike.

Stelis acrisepala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
