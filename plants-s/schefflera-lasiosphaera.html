Schefflera lasiosphaera

Schefflera lasiosphaera, commonly known as the woolly schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded crown and thick, leathery leaves that are often covered in a fine, woolly pubescence. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a ornamental plant.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
