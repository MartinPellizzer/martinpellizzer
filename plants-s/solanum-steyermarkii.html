Solanum steyermarkii

Solanum steyermarkii, commonly known as the Steyermark's nightshade, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, particularly in countries like Brazil, Colombia, and Peru. The plant typically grows as an erect shrub with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale purple, with five petals, and it produces small, globose berries that contain the seeds.

Solanum steyermarkii is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its attractive foliage and flowers.
