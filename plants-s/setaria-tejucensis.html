Setaria tejucensis

Setaria tejucensis, commonly known as the slender barnyardgrass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and agricultural research.
