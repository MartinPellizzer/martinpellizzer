Sedum definitum

Sedum definitum, commonly known as the "Dragon's Blood" or "Fireplant," is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically red or purple, especially when exposed to full sun. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its striking foliage and unique appearance make it a popular choice for both ornamental and low-maintenance landscaping.
