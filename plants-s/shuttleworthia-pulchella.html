Shuttleworthia pulchella

Shuttleworthia pulchella, commonly known as the beautiful shuttleworthia, is a small, flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its delicate, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in moist, sandy soils along stream banks and wetlands. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Shuttleworthia pulchella is often found in shaded to partially shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
