Sarcopodium leopardinum

Sarcopodium leopardinum, commonly known as the leopard's ear, is a striking plant native to the Mediterranean region.

The plant belongs to the family Gesneriaceae and is known for its vibrant, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
