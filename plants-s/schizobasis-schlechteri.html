Schizobasis schlechteri

Schizobasis schlechteri, commonly known as the Schizobasis orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features a compact rhizome with a few leaves and produces delicate, greenish-white flowers. Its flowers are usually arranged in a raceme and have a unique structure with spurred petals.

Schizobasis schlechteri is native to Southeast Asia and is valued for its ornamental qualities in horticulture.
