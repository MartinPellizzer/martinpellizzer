Stipa villifolia

Stipa villifolia, commonly known as slender feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted clumps of fine, silver-green foliage and produces delicate, feathery panicles of pale blue to purple flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine and steppe environments. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping for its elegant appearance.

Stipa villifolia plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small wildlife in its native habitat.
