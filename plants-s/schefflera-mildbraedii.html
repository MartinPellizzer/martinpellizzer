Schefflera mildbraedii

Schefflera mildbraedii, commonly known as the African shield fern, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves that resemble a shield, with multiple leaflets radiating from a central point. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Schefflera mildbraedii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
