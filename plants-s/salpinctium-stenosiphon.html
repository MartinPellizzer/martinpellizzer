Salpinctium stenosiphon

Salpinctium stenosiphon, commonly known as the narrow-siphon grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. The plant has slender, erect stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a distinctive blue-green color.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
