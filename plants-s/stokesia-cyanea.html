Stokesia cyanea

Stokesia cyanea, commonly known as the blue stokesia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, blue to purple flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sunlight. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.
