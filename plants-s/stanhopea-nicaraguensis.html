Stanhopea nicaraguensis

Stanhopea nicaraguensis, commonly known as the Nicaraguan stanhopea, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant, and showy flowers that typically bloom in late spring or early summer. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous blooms, each featuring a unique combination of colors and patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its vibrant floral display.
