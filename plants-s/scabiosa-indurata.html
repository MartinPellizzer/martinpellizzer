Scabiosa indurata

Scabiosa indurata, commonly known as the hardy scabious, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, pink or purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has hairy, gray-green leaves that are often covered in fine, white hairs. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or borders.

Scabiosa indurata is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
