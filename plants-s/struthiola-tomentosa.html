Struthiola tomentosa

Struthiola tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly struthiola, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features soft, hairy foliage that gives it its common name, and it produces small, yellow to orange flowers. Struthiola tomentosa thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
