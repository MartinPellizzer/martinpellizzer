Scrophularia canescens var. glabrata

Scrophularia canescens var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth scrophularia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and open woodlands. The plant features upright stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, arranged in clusters, and bloom in late spring to early summer.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained conditions.
