Saxifraga kruhsiana

Saxifraga kruhsiana, commonly known as the Himalayan saxifrage, is a perennial flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. The plant features compact, rosette-forming leaves that are usually covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. Its flowers are small, white or pink, and clustered in umbels, adding a delicate touch to its habitat.

Saxifraga kruhsiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens or alpine settings.
