Sarcochilus cladostachys

Sarcochilus cladostachys, commonly known as the clustered helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to New Zealand.

It features a dense, hairy rhizome and produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers with prominent, helmet-like sepals. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky areas. Its leaves are narrow and strap-shaped, often covered in a fine, white pubescence.

This species is notable for its unique floral structure and is part of the orchid family, Orchidaceae.
