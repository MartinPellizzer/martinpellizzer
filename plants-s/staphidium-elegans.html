Staphidium elegans

Staphidium elegans, commonly known as the elegant staphidium, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the temperate regions of the northern hemisphere.

It features delicate, alternate leaves that are typically oval to lance-shaped with a smooth margin. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in early summer. Its root system is fibrous and often used in traditional herbal medicine.

Staphidium elegans prefers moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges or shaded gardens.
