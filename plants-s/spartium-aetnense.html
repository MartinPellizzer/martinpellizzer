Spartium aetnense

Spartium aetnense, commonly known as the Aetna thistle, is a flowering plant native to the volcanic regions of Sicily.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to the harsh conditions of its native habitat. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
