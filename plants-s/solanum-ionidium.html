Solanum ionidium

Solanum ionidium, commonly known as the Ionidium nightshade, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant typically features small, white or pale purple flowers and produces berries that contain toxic compounds. Its leaves are usually ovate with a rough texture, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its toxic properties, it is often avoided by herbivores and requires careful handling.
