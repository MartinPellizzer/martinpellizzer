Silene mushaensis

Silene mushaensis, commonly known as the Musha's catchfly, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of eastern Turkey.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pinkish-purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in well-drained, sunny environments. Its conservation status is currently listed as vulnerable due to habitat loss and limited population size.
