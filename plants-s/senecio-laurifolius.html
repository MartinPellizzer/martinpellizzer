Senecio laurifolius

Senecio laurifolius, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a compact shrub, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Senecio laurifolius is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
