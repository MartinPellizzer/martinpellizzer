Scutellaria andamanica

Scutellaria andamanica, commonly known as Andaman skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the Andaman Islands.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually blue or purple and are borne in whorled clusters.

Scutellaria andamanica is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
