Stelis serrulata

Stelis serrulata, commonly known as the serrated stelis, is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is a small epiphytic or lithophytic orchid native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, with narrow, grass-like leaves and a compact growth habit. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, often in shades of white or pale yellow, with intricate patterns on the petals.

Stelis serrulata is appreciated for its ornamental value and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its delicate appearance.
