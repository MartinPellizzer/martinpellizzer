Senecio tulinensis

Senecio tulinensis, commonly known as the Tulinensis groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It produces small, yellow flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Senecio tulinensis is often used in rock gardens due to its hardy nature and ornamental value.
