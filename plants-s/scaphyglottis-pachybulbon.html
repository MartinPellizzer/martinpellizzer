Scaphyglottis pachybulbon

Scaphyglottis pachybulbon, commonly known as the thick-rooted orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories where it thrives on decomposing organic matter. Its leaves are long and strap-like, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences with colorful, lip-shaped petals.
