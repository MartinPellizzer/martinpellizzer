Salix × boulayi

Salix × boulayi, commonly known as the Chinese weeping willow, is a hybrid willow species native to China.

It is characterized by its long, cascading branches that create a distinctive weeping appearance. The plant has slender, light green leaves that are typically oval in shape. It thrives in moist soils and is often found near water bodies such as rivers and ponds.

Salix × boulayi is valued for its ornamental beauty and is commonly used in landscaping and garden design.
