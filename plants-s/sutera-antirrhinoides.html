Sutera antirrhinoides

Sutera antirrhinoides, commonly known as the Cape jasmine, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Sutera antirrhinoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
