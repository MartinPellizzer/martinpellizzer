Swertia barbata

Swertia barbata, commonly known as the Himalayan gentian, is a perennial herb native to the alpine regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows at high altitudes, thriving in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, while its stems are often hairy.

Swertia barbata is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
