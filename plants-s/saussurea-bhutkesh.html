Saussurea bhutkesh

Saussurea bhutkesh, commonly known as Bhutkesh, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, spindly stems and dense, feathery foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish to brownish flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in cold, arid environments and is often found growing at elevations above 3,000 meters.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species in its native range.
