Schoberia dendroides

Schoberia dendroides, commonly known as the tree spurge, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright, branching structure that resembles a small tree, hence its common name. The plant features smooth, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement and often have a waxy coating. Its flowers are small, greenish-yellow, and appear in clusters during the spring.

Schoberia dendroides is adapted to dry, sunny conditions and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils.
