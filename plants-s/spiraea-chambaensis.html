Spiraea chambaensis

Spiraea chambaensis, commonly known as the Chamba spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, showy flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments and is often found in forested areas. Its leaves are oval-shaped and have a slightly serrated edge.

Spiraea chambaensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
