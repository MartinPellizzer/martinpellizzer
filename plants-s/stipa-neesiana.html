Stipa neesiana

Stipa neesiana, commonly known as the slender feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage and tufted inflorescences that resemble plumes. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. Its slender stems and delicate appearance make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Stipa neesiana is also valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
