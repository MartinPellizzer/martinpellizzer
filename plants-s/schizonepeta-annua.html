Schizonepeta annua

Schizonepeta annua, commonly known as annual schizonepeta, is an herbaceous plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is native to Asia and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, pale blue or purple flowers arranged in whorls along the stem. It is often found in moist habitats such as meadows and riverbanks.

Schizonepeta annua is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
