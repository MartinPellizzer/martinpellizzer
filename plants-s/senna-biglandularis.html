Senna biglandularis

Senna biglandularis, commonly known as the yellow senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals. Senna biglandularis is known for its medicinal properties, with various parts of the plant used in traditional medicine for their therapeutic effects.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and pharmaceutical contexts.
