Schistostephium artemisiifolium subsp. artemisiifolium

Schistostephium artemisiifolium subsp.

artemisiifolium, commonly known as the narrowleaf ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves.

The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. It features tall, slender stems with dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Its showy purple to pink composite flowers bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators.
