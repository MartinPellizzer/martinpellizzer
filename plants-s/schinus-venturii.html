Schinus venturii

Schinus venturii, commonly known as the California buckthorn, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the western United States.

It has compound leaves with three to five elliptic leaflets, and its flowers are small, greenish-yellow, and arranged in clusters. The plant produces small, bright red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife.

It is often found in chaparral and coastal scrub habitats, and its ornamental value makes it a popular garden plant.
