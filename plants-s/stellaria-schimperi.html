Stellaria schimperi

Stellaria schimperi, commonly known as the Ethiopian starwort, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to the highlands of Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, star-shaped white flowers. The plant typically has slender stems and opposite, lanceolate leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
