Stylosanthes guianensis var. microcephala

Stylosanthes guianensis var. microcephala, commonly known as the small-headed stylo, is a leguminous plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its small, rounded leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a valuable forage species for livestock. It has a deep root system that helps in soil stabilization and nitrogen fixation.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it suitable for use in pasturelands and erosion control.
