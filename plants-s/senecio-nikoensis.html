Senecio nikoensis

Senecio nikoensis, commonly known as the Niko's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in rocky, arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. It has narrow, silvery leaves that help reduce water loss through transpiration.

Senecio nikoensis is often found in fynbos vegetation, contributing to the biodiversity of its ecosystem.
