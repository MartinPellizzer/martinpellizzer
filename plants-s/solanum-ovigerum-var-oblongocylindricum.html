Solanum ovigerum var. oblongocylindricum

Solanum ovigerum var. oblongocylindricum, commonly known as the long-cylindrical nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, cylindrical fruits that resemble small, green capsules. The plant produces small, white flowers with five petals, typical of the Solanaceae family. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a hairy texture.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in humid, shaded environments.
