Swertia decussata

Swertia decussata, commonly known as the Himalayan gentian, is a perennial herb native to the alpine regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous terrain, thriving in cool and moist conditions. Its leaves are opposite and arranged in a decussate pattern, giving it its scientific name.

Swertia decussata is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine systems.
