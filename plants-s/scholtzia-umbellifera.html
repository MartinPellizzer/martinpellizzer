Scholtzia umbellifera

Scholtzia umbellifera, commonly known as the umbrella plant, is a flowering plant native to California.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has a basal rosette of leaves and produces a tall, branched stem with numerous flower heads. Scholtzia umbellifera thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a valuable addition to native plant landscapes.
