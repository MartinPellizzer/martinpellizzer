Sedum argunense

Sedum argunense, commonly known as the Argun stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and small, clustered leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces yellow flowers in late summer, which are arranged in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it adaptable to rocky and arid environments.

Sedum argunense is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and ornamental value.
