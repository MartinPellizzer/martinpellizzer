Salacia mennegana

Salacia mennegana, commonly known as the red salacia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Salaciacae and is characterized by its striking red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a velvety texture.

Salacia mennegana is valued not only for its ornamental appeal but also for its potential medicinal properties.
