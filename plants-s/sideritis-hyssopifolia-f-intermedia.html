Sideritis hyssopifolia f. intermedia

Sideritis hyssopifolia f. intermedia, commonly known as the intermediate hyssop-leaved ironweed, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a hairy surface. The plant produces small, pale blue to violet flowers arranged in spikes, which bloom from late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Its aromatic foliage and medicinal properties make it a valued plant in traditional herbal practices.
