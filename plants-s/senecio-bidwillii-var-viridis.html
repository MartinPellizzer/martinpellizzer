Senecio bidwillii var. viridis

Senecio bidwillii var. viridis, commonly known as the Emerald Green Senecio, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant features bright green, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white, powdery wax. It produces yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer, adding a subtle ornamental value.

This variety is well-suited for rock gardens and dry landscapes due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
