Synadenium compactum

Synadenium compactum, commonly known as the compact milkweed, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a fine, white pubescence. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It is adapted to arid environments and has a deep root system for water storage.

Synadenium compactum is also noted for its milky sap, which is a characteristic feature of the milkweed genus.
