Swartzia acutifolia var. ynesiana

Swartzia acutifolia var. ynesiana, commonly known as the Ynesian ironwood, is a tropical tree native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its dense, hard wood and evergreen foliage, which contributes to its use in furniture and construction. The plant belongs to the Fabaceae family, known for its nitrogen-fixing abilities, which enhance soil fertility. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, often appearing in shades of white or pale yellow.

The species is valued for both its ecological role and economic importance in local communities.
