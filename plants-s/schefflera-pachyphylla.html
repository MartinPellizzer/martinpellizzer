Schefflera pachyphylla

Schefflera pachyphylla, commonly known as the thick-leaved umbrella plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the island of Madagascar.

It is characterized by its large, clustered leaves that give it a distinctive umbrella-like appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by yellowish fruits containing three seeds. It thrives in humid, warm climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Schefflera pachyphylla is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
