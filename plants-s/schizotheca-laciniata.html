Schizotheca laciniata

Schizotheca laciniata, commonly known as the laceleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its finely dissected, feather-like leaves that give it a delicate, lace-like appearance. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in a compact head, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Schizotheca laciniata thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its unique foliage and modest flowers make it an attractive addition to botanical collections and cultivated gardens.
