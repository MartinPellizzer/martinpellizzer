Saussurea ceterach

Saussurea ceterach, commonly known as the Himalayan swamp gentian, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

The plant typically grows in moist, rocky habitats and is adapted to the cold, alpine climate of its native range.
