Schefflera kuntzei

Schefflera kuntzei, commonly known as the Kuntze schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like crown of large, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Schefflera kuntzei is often cultivated as a ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor growing conditions.
