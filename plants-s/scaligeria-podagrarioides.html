Scaligeria podagrarioides

Scaligeria podagrarioides, commonly known as the rock daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in spring. Its leaves are typically gray-green and covered in fine hairs, adapted to reduce water loss in arid environments.
