Seriphidium santolinum var. simplicifolium

Seriphidium santolinum var. simplicifolium, commonly known as the simple-leaved tansy.

It is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region, characterized by its aromatic, finely divided leaves and yellow ray flowers.
