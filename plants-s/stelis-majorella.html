Stelis majorella

Stelis majorella, commonly known as the giant stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows on trees in tropical rainforests, where it receives ample light and humidity. Its leaves are long and strap-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern around the stem.

Stelis majorella is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
