Senecio tournefortii var. granatensis

Senecio tournefortii var. granatensis, commonly known as the Granatense Senecio, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, silver-gray leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and arid landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is often cultivated in xeriscaped environments.
