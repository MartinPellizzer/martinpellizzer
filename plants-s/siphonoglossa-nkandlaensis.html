Siphonoglossa nkandlaensis

Siphonoglossa nkandlaensis, commonly known as the Nkandla orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in summer. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with a rhizomatous stem that produces new shoots from the base. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water retention.

Siphonoglossa nkandlaensis thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or rocky habitats.
