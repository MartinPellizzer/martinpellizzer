Senecio pauperculus var. flavovirens

Senecio pauperculus var. flavovirens, commonly known as the Yellow-green Senecio, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that are typically green with a yellowish or pale green hue. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and white, which bloom in spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
