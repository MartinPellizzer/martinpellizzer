Stelis macrolemma

Stelis macrolemma, commonly known as the large bract stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its clustered growth habit and slender, often branched stems. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with a distinct lip. Its leaves are typically long and narrow, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Stelis macrolemma is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
