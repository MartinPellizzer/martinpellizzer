Saglorithys menthoides

Saglorithys menthoides, commonly known as the Menthol Leaf Plant, is a unique species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, menthol-like scent, which is derived from its essential oils. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface, and it produces small, white flowers during the flowering season. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Due to its distinct fragrance and ornamental value, it is often used in both horticulture and aromatherapy.
