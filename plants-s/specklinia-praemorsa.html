Specklinia praemorsa

Specklinia praemorsa, commonly known as the death's-head orchid, is a rare and striking epiphytic orchid native to the rainforests of Queensland, Australia.

It is characterized by its unique, dark purple to black flowers that resemble a skull, giving it its ominous common name. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific conditions to bloom.

Due to its rarity and distinctive appearance, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and botanists alike.
