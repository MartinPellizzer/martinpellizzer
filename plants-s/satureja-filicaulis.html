Satureja filicaulis

Satureja filicaulis, commonly known as the slender savory, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its aromatic leaves are often used in cooking, similar to other members of the sage family.

Satureja filicaulis thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and herbal gardens.
