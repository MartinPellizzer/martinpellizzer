Salacia dimidia

Salacia dimidia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved salacia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Salaciacae and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and yellowish-orange flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as tropical forests. Its fruit is small and contains a single seed, which is often dispersed by animals.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
