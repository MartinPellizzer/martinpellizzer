Spathoglottis plicata f. alba

Spathoglottis plicata f. alba, commonly known as the white spathoglottis, is a flowering plant belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its broad, dark green leaves and a single, showy white flower with a distinctive spatulate lip. The plant is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in moist, shaded environments. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its elegant appearance.

The white variant, f. alba, is distinguished by its pale, almost white petals and sepals, setting it apart from other forms of the species.
