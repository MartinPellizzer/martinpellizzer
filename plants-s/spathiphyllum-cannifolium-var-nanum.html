Spathiphyllum cannifolium var. nanum

Spathiphyllum cannifolium var. nanum, commonly known as the dwarf cannalily, is a compact variety of the spatiphyllum genus native to tropical regions.

It features broad, waxy leaves that are typically a deep green with a subtle variegation. The plant produces elegant white spathes that enclose small, fragrant flowers, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Due to its compact size and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
