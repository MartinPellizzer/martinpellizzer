Satureja densiflora

Satureja densiflora, commonly known as denseflower savory, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, gray-green leaves and small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes. Its foliage is often used in culinary applications for its pungent, thyme-like scent.

Satureja densiflora is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties.
