Schoenus oligostachyus

Schoenus oligostachyus, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

Schoenus oligostachyus is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for various wildlife species.
