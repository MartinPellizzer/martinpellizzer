Senecio angelensis

Senecio angelensis, commonly known as the Angel's Senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun, making it well-suited for arid environments. It features narrow, linear leaves and produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late summer.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens.
