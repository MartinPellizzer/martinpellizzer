Sykesia rosea

Sykesia rosea, commonly known as the pink flame orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Queensland, Australia.

It is characterized by its vibrant pink and red floral spikes that resemble flames, making it a visually captivating species. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that bloom during the warmer months, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. Sykesia rosea is a terrestrial orchid with a rhizomatous root system, and its leaves are typically broad and lance-shaped.

Due to its ornamental value and rarity, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
