Sempervivum webbii

Sempervivum webbii, commonly known as the Webbi's stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming growth pattern. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish or reddish tinge, depending on environmental conditions. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months.

Sempervivum webbii is well adapted to rocky and arid environments, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
