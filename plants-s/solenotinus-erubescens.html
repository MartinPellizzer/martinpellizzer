Solenotinus erubescens

Solenotinus erubescens, commonly known as the red-flowered orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking red petals and a unique, intricate floral structure typical of the Orchidaceae family. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by horticulturists.
