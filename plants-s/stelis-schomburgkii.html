Stelis schomburgkii

Stelis schomburgkii, commonly known as the Schomburgk's stelis, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or branches, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are usually dark green and arranged in a rosette pattern.

The flowers, which bloom in the summer, come in shades of white and yellow, adding to its ornamental appeal.
