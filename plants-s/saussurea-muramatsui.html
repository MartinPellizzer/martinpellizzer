Saussurea muramatsui

Saussurea muramatsui, commonly known as the Japanese yomogi, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, branched stems and yellow-orange flowers. The plant produces small, oval-shaped seeds that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its leaves are sometimes used in local cuisine and herbal remedies.
