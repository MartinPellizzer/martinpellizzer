Syzygium caudatum

Syzygium caudatum, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, bright red flowers that appear in clusters, often giving the tree a fiery appearance. The plant belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is known for its ornamental value due to its showy blooms. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate, with a glossy green surface.

The fruit is a small, round drupe that turns from green to yellow as it matures.
