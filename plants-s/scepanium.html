Scepanium

Scepanium, commonly known as the giant water lily, is a striking aquatic plant native to the Amazon Basin.

It features large, broad leaves that can reach up to three meters in diameter, providing a unique habitat for various aquatic life. The plant has a thick, spongy stem that allows it to float on the water's surface. Its flowers are typically white or pink, blooming at night and emitting a sweet fragrance.

Scepanium plays an important role in its ecosystem by oxygenating the water and supporting biodiversity.
