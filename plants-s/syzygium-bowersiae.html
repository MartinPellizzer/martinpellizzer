Syzygium bowersiae

Syzygium bowersiae, commonly known as the Bowers's Syzygium, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical rainforests of northern Queensland, Australia. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a glossy green upper surface.

Syzygium bowersiae is typically found in moist, shaded habitats and is often associated with other rainforest flora.
