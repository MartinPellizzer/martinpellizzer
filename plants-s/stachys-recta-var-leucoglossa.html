Stachys recta var. leucoglossa

Stachys recta var. leucoglossa, commonly known as white-lipped betony, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features upright, hairy stems that bear opposite, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces spikes of small, tubular flowers in shades of blue to purple, which are attractive to pollinators. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and naturalized areas.
