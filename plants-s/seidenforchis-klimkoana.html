Seidenforchis klimkoana

Seidenforchis klimkoana, commonly known as the Klimko's spider orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories where it relies on mycorrhizal fungi for nutrient uptake.
