Serjania hatschbachii

Serjania hatschbachii, commonly known as the Brazilian serjania, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the family Serjaniaceae and is characterized by its woody structure and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a rough texture.

The flowers are clustered in inflorescences and are pollinated primarily by insects.
