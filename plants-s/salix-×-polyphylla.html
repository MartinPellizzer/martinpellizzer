Salix × polyphylla

Salix × polyphylla, commonly known as the Chinese weeping willow, is a hybrid species of willow native to China.

It is characterized by its long, drooping branches that create a cascading effect, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant has narrow, elongated leaves that are typically a bright green on the upper surface and a lighter green underneath. It thrives in moist environments and is often found near rivers, lakes, and wetlands.

Salix × polyphylla is valued for its ornamental beauty and is commonly used in landscaping and garden design.
