Sesbania geminiflora

Sesbania geminiflora, commonly known as the twin-flower sesbania, is a tropical leguminous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive yellow flowers that often appear in pairs. The plant is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it valuable in soil enrichment. It thrives in moist, tropical climates and is often found in disturbed habitats.

Sesbania geminiflora is also used in traditional medicine and as a forage crop in some regions.
