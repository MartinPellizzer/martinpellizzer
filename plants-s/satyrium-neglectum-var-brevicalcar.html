Satyrium neglectum var. brevicalcar

Satyrium neglectum var. brevicalcar, commonly known as the narrow-spurred orchid, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or along stream banks. Its flowers are usually pale green to white, with a distinctive spur that is shorter than the standard form of the species.

The plant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral structure.
