Saintpaulia ionantha subsp. nitida

Saintpaulia ionantha subsp.

nitida, commonly known as the African violet, is a compact, evergreen perennial shrub native to eastern Africa. It features dense, dark green foliage with a velvety texture, and its flowers are typically violet or purple, with a showy, clustered appearance. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers consistently moist, well-draining soil.

It is widely cultivated as a popular ornamental plant due to its attractive blooms and ease of care.
