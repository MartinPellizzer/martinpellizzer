Satyrium amoenum

Satyrium amoenum, commonly known as the pretty orchid, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are linear and often covered in a fine, waxy coating.

Satyrium amoenum thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical forests.
