Schefflera mindanaensis

Schefflera mindanaensis, commonly known as the Mindanao umbrella plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like canopy of large, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by spherical, red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its adaptability makes it suitable for both indoor and outdoor gardening in suitable conditions.
