Solanum fulvum

Solanum fulvum, commonly known as the yellow nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its fruits are berries that start green and ripen to a bright orange or yellow color.

Solanum fulvum is part of the nightshade family, which includes other plants like tomatoes and peppers.
