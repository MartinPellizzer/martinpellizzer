Stephanochilus omphalodes

Stephanochilus omphalodes, commonly known as the star orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant typically grows in high-altitude regions with cool, moist climates. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming a dense rosette at the base.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
