Saussurea pantlingiana

Saussurea pantlingiana, commonly known as the Tibetan yellow gentian, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers and robust, branched stems. The plant typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Saussurea pantlingiana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
