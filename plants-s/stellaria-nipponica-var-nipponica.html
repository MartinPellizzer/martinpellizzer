Stellaria nipponica var. nipponica

Stellaria nipponica var. nipponica, commonly known as Japanese starwort, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a low-growing herbaceous species with slender stems and small, white or pinkish flowers. The plant typically thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodland floors and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, often with hairy surfaces.

The flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom in early summer, attracting pollinators like bees and flies.
