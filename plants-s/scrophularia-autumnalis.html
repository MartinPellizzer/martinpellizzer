Scrophularia autumnalis

Scrophularia autumnalis, commonly known as autumnal figwort, is a flowering plant in the family Scrophulariaceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it typically grows in moist meadows and woodland edges. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are ovate to lance-shaped, with a rough texture, and it often thrives in partial shade.

The plant is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
