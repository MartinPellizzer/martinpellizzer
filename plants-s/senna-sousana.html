Senna sousana

Senna sousana, commonly known as golden senna, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its bipinnate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savannas and rocky areas.

Senna sousana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
