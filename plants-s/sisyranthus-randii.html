Sisyranthus randii

Sisyranthus randii, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange, often with intricate patterns. The plant is a perennial herb with a short, underground rhizome and erect, slender stems. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a basal rosette, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Sisyranthus randii thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
