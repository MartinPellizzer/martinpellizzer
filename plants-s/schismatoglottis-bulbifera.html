Schismatoglottis bulbifera

Schismatoglottis bulbifera, commonly known as the bulb-bearing arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces striking, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green. One of its notable features is the formation of bulb-like structures at the base of the stems, which can be used for propagation.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
