Sinobambusa exaurita

Sinobambusa exaurita, commonly known as the golden bamboo, is a species of flowering bamboo native to China.

It is characterized by its striking golden-yellow culms and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. This plant belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is valued for its ornamental qualities. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.
