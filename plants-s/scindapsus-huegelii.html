Scindapsus huegelii

Scindapsus huegelii, commonly known as the satin pothos, is a climbing evergreen vine native to tropical regions of Asia.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically a deep green with a silvery underside, giving it a distinctive, satiny appearance. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity. It is known for its ability to climb using aerial roots, often reaching lengths of several feet in optimal conditions.

Scindapsus huegelii is a popular houseplant due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal.
