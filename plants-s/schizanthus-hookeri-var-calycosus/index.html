Schizanthus Hookeri Var. Calycosus

Hookeri calycosus, scientifically known as Schizanthus hookeri var. calycosus, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to South America. It belongs to the genus Schizanthus, which is part of the nightshade family, Solanaceae. This variant is distinguished by its vibrant, double-petaled flowers that come in a range of colors including pink, purple, and white. The plant thrives in cooler climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses. Its unique appearance and relatively easy care make it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts.
