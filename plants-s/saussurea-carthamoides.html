Saussurea carthamoides

Saussurea carthamoides, commonly known as the Chinese yarrow or garden saussurea, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, branched stems and clusters of small, yellowish or pale green flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine environments.

Saussurea carthamoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
