Salacia elliptica

Salacia elliptica, commonly known as the elliptic salacia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Salaceae family and is characterized by its elliptical, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Salacia elliptica is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
