Scorzonera sinensis f. plantaginifolia

Scorzonera sinensis f. plantaginifolia, commonly known as the Chinese scorzonera, is a variant of the Scorzonera genus native to China.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its hairy stems and basal leaves that are lanceolate in shape. The flowers are yellow with a central dark brown disc, typical of the Asteraceae family. It produces oval, fleshy tubers that are sometimes used in traditional Chinese medicine.

This plant thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.
