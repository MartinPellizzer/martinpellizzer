Sedum nuttallii

Sedum nuttallii, commonly known as Nuttall's stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.

Sedum nuttallii is a hardy species that requires minimal water and is well-suited for xeriscaping.
