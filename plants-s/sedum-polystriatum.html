Sedum polystriatum

Sedum polystriatum, commonly known as the stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow to orange. The plant is adapted to rocky and arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Sedum polystriatum is also valued for its low maintenance requirements and ability to tolerate drought conditions.
