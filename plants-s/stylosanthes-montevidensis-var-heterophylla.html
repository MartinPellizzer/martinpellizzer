Stylosanthes montevidensis var. heterophylla

Stylosanthes montevidensis var. heterophylla, commonly known as the variable stylosanthes, is a leguminous plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant is often found in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It has a bushy growth habit and can reach heights of up to 30 centimeters.

This species is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in agricultural and ecological restoration contexts.
