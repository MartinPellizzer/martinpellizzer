Sinocalamus bacthaiensis

Sinocalamus bacthaiensis, commonly known as the Bactrian bamboo, is a species of grass bamboo native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 10 meters. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically green and covered in a fine layer of hair. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Sinocalamus bacthaiensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
