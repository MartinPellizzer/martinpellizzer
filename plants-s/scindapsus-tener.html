Scindapsus tener

Scindapsus tener, commonly known as the silver molly or silver spider plant, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with distinctive silver or gray veins. The plant is evergreen and typically grows as a trailing vine, making it suitable for hanging baskets or shelves. Its leaves are often variegated, adding visual interest to any indoor space.

Scindapsus tener prefers bright, indirect light and moderate humidity, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
