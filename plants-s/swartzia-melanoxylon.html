Swartzia melanoxylon

Swartzia melanoxylon, commonly known as blackwood, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark, dense wood that is highly valued for its strength and durability. The plant produces small, white or yellowish flowers that are typically arranged in clusters. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.

Swartzia melanoxylon is also known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its role in local ecosystems.
