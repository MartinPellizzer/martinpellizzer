Statice limonia

Statice limonia, commonly known as lemon verbena, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, lemon-scented leaves. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.
