Sedum stelliforme

Sedum stelliforme, commonly known as star sedum, is a succulent plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features dense clusters of star-shaped, yellow-green flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has fleshy, upright stems and leaves that are typically blue-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
