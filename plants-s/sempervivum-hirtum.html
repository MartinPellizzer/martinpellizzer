Sempervivum hirtum

Sempervivum hirtum, commonly known as the woolly stonecress, is a perennial succulent native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features compact rosettes of fleshy, hairy leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Sempervivum hirtum is also known for its ability to survive in harsh, cold climates.
