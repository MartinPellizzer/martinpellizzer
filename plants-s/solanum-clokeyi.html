Solanum clokeyi

Solanum clokeyi, commonly known as the Clokey's nightshade, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is native to the eastern coast of Australia, particularly in Queensland and New South Wales. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are ovate to elliptic in shape, with a rough texture and a strong, often aromatic scent.

Solanum clokeyi produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries, which are edible but contain toxic compounds.
