Scutellaria pekinensis

Scutellaria pekinensis, commonly known as Peking skullcap, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features square stems and opposite leaves that are usually lanceolate in shape. Its flowers are typically blue to purple and are clustered in whorls along the stem.

It is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional Chinese medicine.
