Senecio subumbellatus var. subumbellatus

Senecio subumbellatus var. subumbellatus, commonly known as the subumbellate groundsels, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features upright, branched stems with narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in clusters, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.
