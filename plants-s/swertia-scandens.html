Swertia scandens

Swertia scandens, commonly known as the creeping gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its delicate blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and has a trailing or climbing habit. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

Swertia scandens is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
