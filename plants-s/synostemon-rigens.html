Synostemon rigens

Synostemon rigens, commonly known as the rigid synostemon, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 feet. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale yellow. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value comes from its compact growth habit and delicate floral display.
