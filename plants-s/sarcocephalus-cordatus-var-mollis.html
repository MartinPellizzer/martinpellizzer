Sarcocephalus cordatus var. mollis

Sarcocephalus cordatus var. mollis, commonly known as the soft-leaved starfish tree, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by woody, star-shaped fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or sandy habitats.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses.
