Senna shaferi

Senna shaferi, commonly known as the golden senna, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bipinnate leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is known for its leguminous properties. Senna shaferi produces small, pod-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine.
