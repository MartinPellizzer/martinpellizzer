Senecio parvifolius

Senecio parvifolius, commonly known as the small-leaved groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellow flowers in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
