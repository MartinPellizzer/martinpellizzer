Solidago bonaerensis

Solidago bonaerensis, commonly known as the Buenos Aires goldenrod, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Argentina.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow inflorescences. The plant typically grows in grasslands and open habitats, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and often branched.

Solidago bonaerensis plays a role in supporting local pollinators and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
