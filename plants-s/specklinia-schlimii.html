Specklinia schlimii

Specklinia schlimii, commonly known as the Schlim's polka dot plant, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive, waxy leaves that often display a polka-dotted pattern of white and green. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming in late winter or early spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Specklinia schlimii is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
