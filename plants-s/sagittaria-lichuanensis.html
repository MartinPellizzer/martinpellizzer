Sagittaria lichuanensis

Sagittaria lichuanensis, commonly known as the Lichuan arrowhead, is a species of aquatic plant native to China.

It is characterized by its distinctive arrowhead-shaped leaves that float on the water's surface. The plant typically grows in shallow, freshwater habitats such as ponds and marshes. Its flowering stems rise above the water, producing small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in spikes.

Sagittaria lichuanensis plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various aquatic organisms.
