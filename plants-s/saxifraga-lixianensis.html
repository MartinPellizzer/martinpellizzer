Saxifraga lixianensis

Saxifraga lixianensis, commonly known as the Lixian saxifrage, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and succulent-like leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its flowers are usually pink or white, and it blooms during the summer months.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
