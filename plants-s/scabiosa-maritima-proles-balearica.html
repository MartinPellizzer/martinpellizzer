Scabiosa maritima proles balearica

Scabiosa maritima proles balearica, commonly known as the Balearic rock scabious, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region, particularly the Balearic Islands.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or coastal environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. The plant features slender stems and clusters of small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, aiding in water retention.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
