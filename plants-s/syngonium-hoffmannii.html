Syngonium hoffmannii

Syngonium hoffmannii, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped or arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in humid, bright indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Syngonium hoffmannii is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and air-purifying properties.
