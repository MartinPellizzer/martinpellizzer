Solanum moestum

Solanum moestum, commonly known as the purple nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its striking purple foliage and small, yellow flowers. The plant produces berries that change color from green to red as they mature, often attracting various wildlife. It is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forests or thickets.

Due to its toxic properties, it is important to handle the plant with care.
