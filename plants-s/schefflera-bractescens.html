Schefflera bractescens

Schefflera bractescens, commonly known as the white bract schefflera, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palm-like leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by distinctive bract-like structures. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a ornamental plant.

Schefflera bractescens is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
