Solidago vulgaris var. paniculata

Solidago vulgaris var. paniculata, commonly known as the panicled goldenrod, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem that can grow up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces showy, cone-shaped inflorescences composed of numerous small yellow flowers. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a common sight in meadows and along roadsides.
