Synaedrys calathiformis

Synaedrys calathiformis, commonly known as the basket-shaped fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, sprawling growth habit and distinctive, fan-like fronds that resemble a basket. The plant's fronds are typically bright green, with a smooth, leathery texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Synaedrys calathiformis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
