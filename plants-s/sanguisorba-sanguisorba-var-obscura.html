Sanguisorba sanguisorba var. obscura

Sanguisorba sanguisorba var. obscura, commonly known as the dark sanguisorba, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It features upright, branching stems that are covered in dense, dark purple to blackish-red hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-red to purple flowers in late summer, which are arranged in dense, spherical inflorescences. Its leaves are lance-shaped, serrated, and have a slightly hairy texture.

This variety is valued for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to thrive in moist, well-drained soils.
