Schefflera pentagyra

Schefflera pentagyra, commonly known as the five-lobed umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like crown of large, leathery, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits. Schefflera pentagyra is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.

It thrives in warm, humid environments and is relatively easy to care for indoors.
