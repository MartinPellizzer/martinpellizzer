Senna ligustrina

Senna ligustrina, commonly known as the yellow senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters tall, characterized by its pinnately compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas or disturbed sites.

Senna ligustrina is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
