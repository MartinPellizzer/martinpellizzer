Stellaria filicaulis f. jaluana

Stellaria filicaulis f. jaluana, commonly known as the narrow-leaved starwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The leaves are typically narrow and lanceolate, arranged alternately along the stem. This plant is native to regions of southern Europe and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in well-drained environments.
