Stemona tuberosa var. tuberosa

Stemona tuberosa var. tuberosa, commonly known as the Chinese starwort, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It features a rhizomatous root system and slender, erect stems that produce opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant is characterized by its small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its tuberous roots are valued in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
