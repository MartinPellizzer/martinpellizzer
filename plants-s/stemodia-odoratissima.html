Stemodia odoratissima

Stemodia odoratissima, commonly known as the sweet-smelling stemodia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, often fragrant foliage. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered in heads, producing a pleasant scent that attracts pollinators.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
