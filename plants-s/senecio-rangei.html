Senecio rangei

Senecio rangei, commonly known as the Range's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb with slender, branched stems and small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are usually linear and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.
