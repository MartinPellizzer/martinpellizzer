Stelis micrantha

Stelis micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are narrow and arranged in a rosette pattern, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Stelis micrantha is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
