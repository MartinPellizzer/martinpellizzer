Semnostachya longespicatus

Semnostachya longespicatus, commonly known as the long-spurred orchid, is a species of orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in late summer. The plant produces intricate, fragrant blooms with long, slender petals and a distinctive spur. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological importance, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
