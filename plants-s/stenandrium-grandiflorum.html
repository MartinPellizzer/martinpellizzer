Stenandrium grandiflorum

Stenandrium grandiflorum, commonly known as the large-flowered stenandrium, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. Its flowering period usually occurs during the warmer months, making it a popular ornamental species in gardens and botanical collections.

Stenandrium grandiflorum is also valued for its air-purifying properties and ability to thrive in humid environments.
