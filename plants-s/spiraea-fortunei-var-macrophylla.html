Spiraea fortunei var. macrophylla

Spiraea fortunei var. macrophylla, commonly known as the Japanese spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features clusters of small, fragrant white or pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has dark green, ovate leaves that turn red or orange in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it suitable for borders, rock gardens, and as a foundation plant.
