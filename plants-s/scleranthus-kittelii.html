Scleranthus kittelii

Scleranthus kittelii, commonly known as the Kashmir thorn, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan regions of India and Pakistan.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows in rocky and arid environments. The plant features small, pink or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, dry fruits that contain a single seed.

Scleranthus kittelii is adapted to withstand harsh climatic conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
