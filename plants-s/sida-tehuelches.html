Sida tehuelches

Sida tehuelches, commonly known as the Tehuelche mallow, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Argentina and Chile.

It is a shrub that can grow up to 1.5 meters tall, with hairy, lance-shaped leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in coastal and mountainous regions. Its flowers are typically clustered in dense inflorescences, and it produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds.
