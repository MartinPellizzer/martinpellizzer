Sida palmensis

Sida palmensis, commonly known as the palmleaf sida, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central and South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are palmately lobed, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Sida palmensis is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
