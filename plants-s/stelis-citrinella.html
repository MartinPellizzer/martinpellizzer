Stelis citrinella

Stelis citrinella, commonly known as the lemon orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often found in forest understories. The plant features a slender, branching stem with small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually yellow or cream-colored, with a distinctive, intricate pattern.

Stelis citrinella is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
