Stellera lessertii var. afghanica

Stellera lessertii var. afghanica, commonly known as the Afghan starflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, linear leaves and produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant is characterized by its succulent, fleshy stems and leaves, which help it retain moisture in its arid habitat. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or alpine environments.

Its unique floral structure and adaptability to harsh climates make it a notable species in its native range.
