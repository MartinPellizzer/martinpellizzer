Solenostemon porpeodon

Solenostemon porpeodon, commonly known as the red-leaf begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety red foliage that contrasts beautifully with its deep green stems. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Solenostemon porpeodon is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its aesthetic value and relatively easy care requirements.
