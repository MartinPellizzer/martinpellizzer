Swertia hispidicalyx var. hispidicalyx

Swertia hispidicalyx var. hispidicalyx, commonly known as the hairy swertia, is a perennial herb native to the Himalayas.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes. The plant features lanceolate leaves with a rough, hairy texture on both sides. Its flowers are small, blue to purple in color, and arranged in clusters.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
