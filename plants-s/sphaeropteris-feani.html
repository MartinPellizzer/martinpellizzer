Sphaeropteris feani

Sphaeropteris feani, commonly known as the Philippine cycad, is a unique cycad species native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its stout, unbranched trunk and a crown of large, pinnately compound leaves. The plant has a slow growth rate and can live for many decades, making it a long-lived species. Its leaves are typically dark green and can reach lengths of up to 2 meters.

Sphaeropteris feani plays an important role in its native ecosystem, providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
