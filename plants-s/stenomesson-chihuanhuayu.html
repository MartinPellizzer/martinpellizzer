Stenomesson chihuanhuayu

Stenomesson chihuanhuayu, commonly known as the Chihuanhuayu sunflower, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stem is upright and branching.
