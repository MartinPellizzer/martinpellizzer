Shiia

Shiia, commonly known as the Chinese chestnut, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the genus *Castanea* in the family Fagaceae, which includes oaks and beeches. The plant produces spiny husks that enclose edible nuts, which are often harvested in the autumn. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and typically have serrated edges.

Shiia trees are valued for their ornamental beauty and the nutritional value of their nuts.
