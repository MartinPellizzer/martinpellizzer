Shantzia garckeana

Shantzia garckeana, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are clustered in racemes with a distinctive violet or purple hue.

Shantzia garckeana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
