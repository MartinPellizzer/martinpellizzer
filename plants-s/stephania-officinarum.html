Stephania officinarum

Stephania officinarum, commonly known as the Indian snakeroot, is a climbing plant native to the tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Menispermaceae and is characterized by its woody stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by distinctive, winged fruits. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along riverbanks.
