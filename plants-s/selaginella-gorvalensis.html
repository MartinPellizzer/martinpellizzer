Selaginella gorvalensis

Selaginella gorvalensis, commonly known as the Gorvalens spike moss, is a small, evergreen fern-like plant belonging to the family Selaginellaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand and Malaysia. The plant features delicate, needle-like leaves arranged in whorls around its branching stems. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Selaginella gorvalensis is noted for its ability to survive in low-light conditions and its unique reproductive structures.
