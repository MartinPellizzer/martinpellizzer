Silene anastomosans

Silene anastomosans, commonly known as the common red campion, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Silene anastomosans is often found in temperate regions of Europe and parts of Asia.
