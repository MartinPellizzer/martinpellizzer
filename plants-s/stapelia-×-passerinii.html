Stapelia × passerinii

Stapelia × passerinii, commonly known as the hybrid African star.

It is a succulent plant native to South Africa, characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and distinctive star-shaped flowers. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange blooms that emit a strong, unpleasant odor, attracting pollinators like flies. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Stapelia × passerinii is often cultivated for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
