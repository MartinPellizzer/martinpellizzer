Schizobasis dinteri

Schizobasis dinteri, commonly known as the Dinter's milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family, which includes many milkweed species. The plant is characterized by its hairy stems and showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.
