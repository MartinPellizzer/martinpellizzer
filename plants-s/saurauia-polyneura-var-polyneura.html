Saurauia polyneura var. polyneura

Saurauia polyneura var. polyneura, commonly known as the broad-leafed saurauia, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree, typically reaching heights of up to 10 meters. The plant features elliptical, glossy dark green leaves that are often leathery in texture. Its flowers are small, white, and clustered in inflorescences, while the fruit is a small, fleshy drupe.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
