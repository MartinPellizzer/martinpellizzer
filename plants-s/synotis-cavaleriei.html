Synotis cavaleriei

Synotis cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments and has lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.
