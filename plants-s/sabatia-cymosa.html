Sabatia cymosa

Sabatia cymosa, commonly known as clustered ironweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy purple to pink flowers. Sabatia cymosa is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
