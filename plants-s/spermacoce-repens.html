Spermacoce repens

Spermacoce repens, commonly known as wandering jew, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that spreads via stolons, forming dense mats on the ground. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers that are clustered in cymes. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas.
