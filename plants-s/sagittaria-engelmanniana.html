Sagittaria engelmanniana

Sagittaria engelmanniana, commonly known as arrowhead, is a herbaceous aquatic plant native to North America.

It features arrow-shaped leaves that float on the water's surface, with submerged leaves that are narrower and more elongated. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom above the water, typically in late summer. It thrives in shallow, freshwater environments such as ponds, lakes, and wetlands.

Sagittaria engelmanniana plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various aquatic organisms.
