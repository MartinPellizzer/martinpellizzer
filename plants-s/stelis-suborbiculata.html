Stelis suborbiculata

Stelis suborbiculata, commonly known as the orchid with orbiculate leaves, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant features a sympodial growth pattern, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually ovate to orbiculate in shape, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Stelis suborbiculata thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
