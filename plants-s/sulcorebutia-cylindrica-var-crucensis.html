Sulcorebutia cylindrica var. crucensis

Sulcorebutia cylindrica var. crucensis, commonly known as the Crucensis Candelabra Cactus, is a compact, columnar cactus native to the rocky habitats of southern Africa.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with a distinctive, branched appearance that resembles a candlestick. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. Its leaves are reduced to spines, and it has a shallow root system adapted to arid conditions.

This variety is valued for its unique shape and ease of care, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
