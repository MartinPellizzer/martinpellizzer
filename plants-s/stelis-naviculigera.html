Stelis naviculigera

Stelis naviculigera, commonly known as the boat-shaped orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, boat-shaped pseudobulbs and elongated, narrow leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a raceme, typically blooming during the wet season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates.

Stelis naviculigera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical orchid collections.
