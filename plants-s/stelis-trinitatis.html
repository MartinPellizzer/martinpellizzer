Stelis trinitatis

Stelis trinitatis, commonly known as the Trinidad orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Caribbean island of Trinidad.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic growth habit. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Stelis trinitatis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
