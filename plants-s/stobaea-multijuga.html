Stobaea multijuga

Stobaea multijuga, commonly known as the mountain holly.

It is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, clustered white flowers. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in temperate regions.
