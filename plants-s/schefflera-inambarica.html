Schefflera inambarica

Schefflera inambarica, commonly known as the Inambar Island schefflera, is a tropical plant native to the Indo-Pacific region.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like crown of large, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers that are followed by bright red berries, which are an important food source for local wildlife. It thrives in humid, warm environments and is often found in coastal forests and lowland areas.

Schefflera inambarica is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to adapt to various soil types.
