Salacia integrifolia

Salacia integrifolia, commonly known as the smoothleaf salacia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Salaciacae and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are usually smooth-edged. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, and it is often found in moist, shaded environments. Its fruit is a small, fleshy berry that contains several seeds.

Salacia integrifolia is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
