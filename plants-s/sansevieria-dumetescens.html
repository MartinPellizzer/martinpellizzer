Sansevieria dumetescens

Sansevieria dumetescens, commonly known as the snake plant or mother-in-law's tongue, is a hardy succulent native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with distinct white or yellow variegation. The plant is well adapted to low light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it an excellent choice for indoor environments. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, storing water to help it survive in arid conditions.

Sansevieria dumetescens is also known for its air-purifying properties, enhancing indoor air quality.
