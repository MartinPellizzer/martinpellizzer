Systellantha serratifolia var. serratifolia

Systellantha serratifolia var. serratifolia, commonly known as the serrated leaf systellantha, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features compound leaves with serrated margins, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its botanical structure includes opposite leaf arrangement and woody stems.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments within its native habitat.
