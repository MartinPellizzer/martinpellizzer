Salacia klossii

Salacia klossii, commonly known as the Kloss's salacia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Salaceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland and hill forest areas.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and somewhat elusive species in botanical collections.
