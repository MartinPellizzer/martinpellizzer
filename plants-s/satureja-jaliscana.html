Satureja jaliscana

Satureja jaliscana, commonly known as Mexican thyme, is a perennial herb native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes other aromatic plants like mint and oregano. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant leaves and clusters of pink or purple flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or arid environments.

Satureja jaliscana is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
