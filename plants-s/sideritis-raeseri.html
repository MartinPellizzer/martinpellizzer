Sideritis raeseri

Sideritis raeseri, commonly known as the Greek mountain tea, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes plants known for their aromatic properties. The plant features slender, hairy stems and small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to violet. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Sideritis raeseri is valued for its medicinal properties, often used in traditional herbal remedies for its calming and anti-inflammatory effects.
