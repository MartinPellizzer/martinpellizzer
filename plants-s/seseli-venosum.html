Seseli venosum

Seseli venosum, commonly known as veined seseli, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its finely veined, compound leaves and small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it often grows in grassy areas and along roadsides. The plant is typically herbaceous, with a slender stem and a long flowering period.

It is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
