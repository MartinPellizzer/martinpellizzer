Stenorrhynchos aphyllum

Stenorrhynchos aphyllum, commonly known as the ghost orchid, is a rare and elusive flowering plant native to the wet tropical forests of Southeast Asia.

The plant lacks true leaves, relying instead on its specialized roots to absorb nutrients from the air and surrounding environment.
