Stemonurus corniculatus

Stemonurus corniculatus, commonly known as the horned grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, commonly known as the grass family, and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, often hairy stems. Stemonurus corniculatus is often found in rocky or sandy areas and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
