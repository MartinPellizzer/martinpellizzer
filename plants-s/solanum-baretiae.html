Solanum baretiae

Solanum baretiae, commonly known as the Baretia nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows in high-altitude regions, often in rocky or alpine environments. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually purple or violet in color. Its fruit is a globose berry that contains several seeds.

Solanum baretiae is part of the nightshade family (Solanaceae), which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes.
