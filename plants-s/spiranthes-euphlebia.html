Spiranthes euphlebia

Spiranthes euphlebia, commonly known as the pink spire orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and its striking, spiral-shaped inflorescence of pale pink to white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and wetlands. It produces small, greenish-brown capsules as fruit, which contain the seeds.

Spiranthes euphlebia is a perennial species that blooms from late spring to early summer.
