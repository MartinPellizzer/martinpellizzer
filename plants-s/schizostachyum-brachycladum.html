Schizostachyum brachycladum

Schizostachyum brachycladum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bamboo, is a species of grass belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant is typically found in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. Schizostachyum brachycladum is often used in landscaping due to its ornamental value and ability to form dense clumps.

Its rapid growth rate makes it a popular choice for erosion control and as a decorative plant in gardens.
