Sideritis cretica var. eriocephala

Sideritis cretica var. eriocephala, commonly known as Cretan ironwort, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky and dry environments, showcasing its adaptability to arid conditions. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by aromatic leaves and square stems.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional use in herbal medicine.
