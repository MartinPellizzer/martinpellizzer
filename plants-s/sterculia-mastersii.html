Sterculia mastersii

Sterculia mastersii, commonly known as the African rosewood, is a tropical tree native to eastern and southern Africa.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its large, glossy leaves and showy, pinkish-white flowers. The tree produces woody pods that contain flat, elongated seeds. It is valued for its hard, heavy wood, which is used in traditional crafts and musical instruments.
