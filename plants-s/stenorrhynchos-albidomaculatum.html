Stenorrhynchos albidomaculatum

Stenorrhynchos albidomaculatum, commonly known as the white-spotted stenorrhynchos, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, showy flowers with white spots on the dorsal sepal and petals. The plant typically produces inflorescences that bear several blooms, each with a distinctive coloration pattern. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts of rare orchids.
