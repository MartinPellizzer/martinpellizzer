Sisymbrium sinuatum

Sisymbrium sinuatum, commonly known as the narrowleaf tansy, is an annual herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, branching stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has pinnately divided leaves with a distinctive, slightly serrated edge. It is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides, thriving in full sun.

Its seeds are dispersed by wind, contributing to its widespread distribution.
