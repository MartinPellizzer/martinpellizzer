Specklinia dimidia

Specklinia dimidia, commonly known as the streaked pigmy orchid, is a small epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. The plant produces compact inflorescences with small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale green with distinctive markings. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its compact size and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
