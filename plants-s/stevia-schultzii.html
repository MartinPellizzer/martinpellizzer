Stevia schultzii

Stevia schultzii, commonly known as the yellow stevia, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and yellowish flowers. The plant is known for its high concentration of steviol glycosides, which are natural sweeteners. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for both ornamental and medicinal use.

Stevia schultzii is often cultivated for its sweet leaves and potential health benefits.
