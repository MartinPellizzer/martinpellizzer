Specklinia peperomioides

Specklinia peperomioides, commonly known as the peperomia-like orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a small, epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in the Philippines. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, typically in shades of white or pink.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
