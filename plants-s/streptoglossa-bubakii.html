Streptoglossa bubakii

Streptoglossa bubakii, commonly known as the Bubak's spider orchid, is a rare and striking member of the orchid family native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, spider-like appearance, with long, slender petals and a distinctive, elongated lip. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in semi-arid environments. Its flowering period occurs during the summer months, producing vibrant yellow or orange blooms.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
