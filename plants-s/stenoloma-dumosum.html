Stenoloma dumosum

Stenoloma dumosum, commonly known as the bushy stenoloma, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories or along stream banks. The plant features a compact, bushy growth habit with numerous small, lance-shaped leaves arranged in whorls around the stem. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually greenish-white in color, and it produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Stenoloma dumosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its dense foliage and unique appearance.
