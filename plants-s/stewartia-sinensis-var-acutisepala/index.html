Stewartia Sinensis Var. Acutisepala

Chinese stewartia, scientifically known as Stewartia sinensis var. acutisepala, is a rare and ornamental tree native to eastern China. It is prized for its elegant form, fragrant flowers, and striking autumn foliage that transforms into vibrant shades of red and yellow. This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade, making it a popular choice for shaded gardens and woodland settings. Its slow growth and long lifespan make it a valuable addition to conservation efforts and botanical collections. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively unknown outside of specialized horticultural circles.
