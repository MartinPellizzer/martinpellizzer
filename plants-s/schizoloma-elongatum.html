Schizoloma elongatum

Schizoloma elongatum, commonly known as the elongated split-leaf plant, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated leaves that are deeply divided into narrow segments. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Schizoloma elongatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its distinctive foliage.
