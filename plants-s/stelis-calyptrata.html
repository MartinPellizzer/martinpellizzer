Stelis calyptrata

Stelis calyptrata, commonly known as the helmet orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a compact growth habit with a single, erect stem and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish, with a distinctive hood-like structure at the base of the petals.

Stelis calyptrata is valued for its unique appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
