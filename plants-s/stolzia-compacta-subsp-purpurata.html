Stolzia compacta subsp. purpurata

Stolzia compacta subsp.

purpurata, commonly known as the purple stolzia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a shrub characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage.

The plant produces showy, clustered flowers that are typically pink to purple in color, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
