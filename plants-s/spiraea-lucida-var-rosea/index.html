Spiraea Lucida Var. Rosea

Spiraea lucida var. rosea is a striking shrub known for its vibrant pink blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun. This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes. Its delicate flowers appear in early summer, adding a touch of elegance. It is a popular choice for both novice and experienced gardeners alike.
