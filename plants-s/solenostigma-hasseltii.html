Solenostigma hasseltii

Solenostigma hasseltii, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple, often with a contrasting throat. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Botanically, it belongs to the family Campanulaceae and is characterized by its unique floral structure and adaptability to arid environments.
