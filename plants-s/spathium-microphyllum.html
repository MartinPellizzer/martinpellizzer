Spathium microphyllum

Spathium microphyllum, commonly known as the small-leaved spathium, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or white in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
