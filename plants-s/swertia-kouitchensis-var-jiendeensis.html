Swertia kouitchensis var. jiendeensis

Swertia kouitchensis var. jiendeensis, commonly known as the Jiende gentian, is a flowering plant native to the Yunnan province in China.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly rough texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
