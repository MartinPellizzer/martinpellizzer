Sciodaphyllum peruvianum

Sciodaphyllum peruvianum, commonly known as the Peruvian sunspider, is a rare and unique plant native to the high-altitude regions of Peru.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, adding to its ornamental appeal. Its growth habit is low and sprawling, with a tendency to form dense mats on rocky soils.
