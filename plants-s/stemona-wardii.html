Stemona wardii

Stemona wardii, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, herbaceous stems and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down.

Stemona wardii is valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits and is also cultivated for ornamental purposes.
