Saxifraga ganeshii

Saxifraga ganeshii, commonly known as Ganesh's saxifrage, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Nepal and India.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves and delicate, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, thriving in cool, moist conditions. Its flowering period occurs during the spring and early summer months.

Saxifraga ganeshii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
