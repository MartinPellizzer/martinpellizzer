Sedum cymatopetalum

Sedum cymatopetalum, commonly known as the rock sedum, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that form dense, compact rosettes. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Sedum cymatopetalum is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
