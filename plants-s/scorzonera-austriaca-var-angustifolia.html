Scorzonera austriaca var. angustifolia

Scorzonera austriaca var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved scorzonera, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe.

It features a robust, fleshy root system that is often used in traditional medicine. The plant has finely divided, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. Its flowers are yellow and bloom in early summer, producing small, round fruits that contain numerous seeds.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and medicinal properties.
