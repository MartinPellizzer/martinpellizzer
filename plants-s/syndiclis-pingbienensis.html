Syndiclis pingbienensis

Syndiclis pingbienensis, commonly known as the Pingbienensis Begonia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy bracts that often resemble petals. The plant typically features opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and a distinct reddish or purple hue. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Syndiclis pingbienensis is a relatively rare species, making it a sought-after addition to botanical collections.
