Smelowskia calycina var. integrifolia

Smelowskia calycina var. integrifolia, commonly known as the smooth-leafed smelowskia, is a rare and unique plant species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, smooth-edged leaves that are typically a deep green color, providing a striking visual contrast to its pale yellow flowers. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in the spring, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
