Senecio richii var. latior

Senecio richii var. latior, commonly known as the broadleaf Senecio, is a species of flowering plant in the sunflower family, Asteraceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its robust, upright growth habit and showy yellow composite flowers. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

This variety is noted for its hardiness and ability to withstand drought conditions.
