Sisymbrium solidagineum

Sisymbrium solidagineum, commonly known as yellow rocket, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and slender, branching stems. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and woodland edges. Its leaves are alternate, pinnately lobed, and often have a distinctive hairy texture.

The fruit is a short, cylindrical silique that contains several small, elongated seeds.
