Sophora philippinensis

Sophora philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine locust tree, is a deciduous tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The tree can grow up to 15 meters tall, with a broad, spreading canopy. Its flowers are typically yellow or greenish-yellow, arranged in racemose inflorescences.

The plant is adapted to tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests and secondary growth areas.
