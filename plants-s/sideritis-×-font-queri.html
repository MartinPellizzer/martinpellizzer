Sideritis × font-queri

Sideritis × font-queri, commonly known as the hybrid sainfoin, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in whorled clusters. Sideritis × font-queri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often grown in gardens for its attractive foliage and flowering display.

It prefers well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to Mediterranean-style landscapes.
