Sphenostigma gramineum

Sphenostigma gramineum, commonly known as the grassy sphenostigma, is a member of the family Sphenostigmataceae.

It is a small, herbaceous plant characterized by its simple, linear leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shady environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and ecological role in its native habitats.
