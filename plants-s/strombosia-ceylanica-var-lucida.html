Strombosia ceylanica var. lucida

Strombosia ceylanica var. lucida, commonly known as the shining strombosia, is a tropical shrub native to Sri Lanka.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

This species is often found in moist, shaded areas of tropical forests.
