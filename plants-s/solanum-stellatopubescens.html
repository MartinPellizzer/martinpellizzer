Solanum stellatopubescens

Solanum stellatopubescens, commonly known as the star-hairy nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades such as tomatoes and potatoes. The plant is characterized by its hairy, star-shaped leaves and small, yellowish flowers. It produces berries that are typically green when immature and turn red when mature.

Solanum stellatopubescens is often found in moist, shaded environments and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.
