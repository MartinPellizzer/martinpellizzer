Succisa centauroides

Succisa centauroides, commonly known as the centaur's foot, is a striking succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a unique and eye-catching appearance. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a splash of color to its foliage. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its architectural form and ornamental value make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
