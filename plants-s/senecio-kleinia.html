Senecio kleinia

Senecio kleinia, commonly known as the Kleinia succulent, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves and vibrant yellow flowers. The plant is drought-tolerant, making it well-suited for arid environments. Its leaves are often covered in a fine layer of white powder, which helps reduce water loss.

Senecio kleinia is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
