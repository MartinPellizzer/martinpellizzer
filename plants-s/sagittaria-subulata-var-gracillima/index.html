Sagittaria Subulata Var. Gracillima

Sagittaria subulata var. gracillima is a striking aquatic plant known for its slender, arrow-shaped leaves that float just above the water's surface. This variety thrives in shallow, still waters and is often found in marshes and ponds throughout its native range. Its delicate foliage creates a soft, shimmering effect when illuminated by sunlight or moonlight. The plant is prized by water gardeners for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care. With its unique form and adaptability, Sagittaria subulata var. gracillima adds both beauty and ecological value to aquatic environments.
