Senecio valderramae

Senecio valderramae, commonly known as the Valderrama groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It features narrow, linear leaves and produces small yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
