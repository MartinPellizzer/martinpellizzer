Saussurea diffusa

Saussurea diffusa, commonly known as Chinese yarrow, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, pale yellow to white flowers. The plant has feathery, aromatic leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in high altitudes and is adapted to cold, dry climates.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in the Asteraceae family.
