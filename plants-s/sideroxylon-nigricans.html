Sideroxylon nigricans

Sideroxylon nigricans, commonly known as black ironwood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, heavy wood that is highly valued for its durability and strength. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by woody, oval-shaped fruits.

The species is also notable for its ability to thrive in coastal and saline environments.
