Senecio leucanthemoides

Senecio leucanthemoides, commonly known as the African Daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers with white ray florets, which resemble daisies. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and has linear, gray-green leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Senecio leucanthemoides is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
