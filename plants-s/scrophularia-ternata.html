Scrophularia ternata

Scrophularia ternata, commonly known as narrow-leaved figwort, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Scrophulariaceae family, which includes many species with showy flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, and it produces clusters of tubular, pink or purple flowers.

Scrophularia ternata is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats.
