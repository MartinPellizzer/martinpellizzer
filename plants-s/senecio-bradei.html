Senecio bradei

Senecio bradei, commonly known as the Brade's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in clusters.

Senecio bradei is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in arid conditions.
