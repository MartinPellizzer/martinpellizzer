Solidago moseleyi

Solidago moseleyi, commonly known as Moseley's goldenrod, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Solidago moseleyi plays an important role in supporting pollinators like bees and butterflies during the autumn season.
