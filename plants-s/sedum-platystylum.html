Sedum platystylum

Sedum platystylum, commonly known as the flatstem stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to parts of Asia and the Middle East.

It features fleshy, upright stems that are often covered in a fine layer of white powder, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are typically blue-green in color and arranged in opposite pairs along the stems.

Sedum platystylum is well adapted to arid environments and is often used in rock gardens or as an ornamental plant in dry landscapes.
