Specklinia stenophylla

Specklinia stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leafed staghorn fern, is a species of epiphytic fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including regions of Malaysia, Indonesia, and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit and dark green, leathery fronds that are typically narrow and elongated. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on tree trunks or rocks in tropical rainforests.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and conservation efforts.
