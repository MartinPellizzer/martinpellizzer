Stelis venosa

Stelis venosa, commonly known as the veined stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are elongated and covered with a dense layer of fine, white hairs.

Stelis venosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
