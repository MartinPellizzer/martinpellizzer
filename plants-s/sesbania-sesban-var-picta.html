Sesbania sesban var. picta

Sesbania sesban var. picta, commonly known as the painted sesbania, is a tropical leguminous plant characterized by its striking, colorful foliage.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is native to parts of Africa and Asia. The leaves are typically trifoliate, with each leaflet displaying vibrant hues of red, orange, and yellow, making it visually distinctive. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

The plant also has nitrogen-fixing capabilities, contributing to soil fertility.
