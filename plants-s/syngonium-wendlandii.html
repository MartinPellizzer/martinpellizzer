Syngonium wendlandii

Syngonium wendlandii, commonly known as the arrowhead vine, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, arrowhead-like leaves that vary in color from bright green to variegated patterns. The plant is a climbing vine that thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light. Its stems are slender and can trail or climb, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets or as a trailing plant.

Syngonium wendlandii is also valued for its ability to purify indoor air and its aesthetic appeal in ornamental gardening.
