Sideritis hirsuta var. emporitana

Sideritis hirsuta var. emporitana, commonly known as the hairy beard vetch, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Sideritis species are known for their aromatic properties and have been used in traditional herbal medicine.
