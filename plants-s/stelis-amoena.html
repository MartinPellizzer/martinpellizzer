Stelis amoena

Stelis amoena, commonly known as the elegant orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant blooms that come in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air and rain, allowing it to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

Stelis amoena is prized in horticulture for its beauty and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
