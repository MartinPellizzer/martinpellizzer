Swartzia obscura

Swartzia obscura, commonly known as the blackwood tree, is a species of flowering plant in the family Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its dark, heavy wood and compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. Swartzia obscura produces yellowish-white flowers arranged in racemes, which are followed by flat, pod-like fruits.

It is often valued for its hard, durable wood used in furniture and construction.
