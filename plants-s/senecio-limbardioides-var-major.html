Senecio limbardioides var. major

Senecio limbardioides var. major, commonly known as the giant limbed senecio, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces yellow flowers in dense, branched inflorescences during the summer months. Its leaves are typically narrow and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
