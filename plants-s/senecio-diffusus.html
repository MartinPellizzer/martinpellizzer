Senecio diffusus

Senecio diffusus, commonly known as the spreading groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. The plant features wiry stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its yellow flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom in the summer months.

Senecio diffusus is known for its ability to thrive in poor soils and its use in traditional medicine in some regions.
