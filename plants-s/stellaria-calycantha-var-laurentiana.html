Stellaria calycantha var. laurentiana

Stellaria calycantha var. laurentiana, commonly known as Laurentian starwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the northeastern United States and eastern Canada.

It features delicate, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves arranged in a basal rosette, and its stems are usually prostrate or ascending. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

The species is characterized by its small, hairy fruits and is often found in acidic soils.
