Securidaca decora

Securidaca decora, commonly known as the ornamental senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and showy blooms. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Securidaca decora is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
