Sagenia grandifolia

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain a single seed. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland edges and disturbed soils.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
