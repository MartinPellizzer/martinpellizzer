Scutellaria compressa

Scutellaria compressa, commonly known as narrowleaf skullcap, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. It is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.

Scutellaria compressa is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
