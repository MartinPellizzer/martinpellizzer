Sarcochilus minutiflorus

Sarcochilus minutiflorus, commonly known as the miniature flower orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It features a compact, low-growing plant with a pseudobulb and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and green, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its delicate nature, it is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a potted plant.
