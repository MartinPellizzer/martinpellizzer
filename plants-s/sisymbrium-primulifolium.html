Sisymbrium primulifolium

Sisymbrium primulifolium, commonly known as yellow rocket, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, branched stems and pinnately divided leaves with a fern-like appearance. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and along stream banks.

Sisymbrium primulifolium is an annual herb that plays a role in supporting pollinators and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
