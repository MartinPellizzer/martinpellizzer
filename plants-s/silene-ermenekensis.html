Silene ermenekensis

Silene ermenekensis, commonly known as the Armenian catchfly, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Armenia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pinkish-purple flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in alpine or subalpine habitats. It has a low-growing habit with slender stems and small, ovate leaves.
