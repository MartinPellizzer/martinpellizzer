Sonchus dregeanus

Sonchus dregeanus, commonly known as the African sowthistle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in grasslands and along roadsides. Its leaves are hairy and have a distinctive yellowish-green color, while the flowers are small and yellow, forming a cluster at the top of the stem.

It is often considered a weed but has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
