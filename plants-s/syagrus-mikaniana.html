Syagrus mikaniana

Syagrus mikaniana, commonly known as the royal palm, is a tropical plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, fan-shaped leaves that give it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into red, fleshy fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

Syagrus mikaniana is also valued for its ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
