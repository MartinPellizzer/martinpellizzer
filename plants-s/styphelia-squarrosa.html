Styphelia squarrosa

Styphelia squarrosa, commonly known as the squarrose stylewort, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Australia.

It is a low-growing, evergreen shrub that typically reaches heights of up to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and leathery, adapted to withstand the harsh coastal conditions.

Styphelia squarrosa is often found in sandy soils and is well-suited to maritime climates.
