Senecio solitarius

Senecio solitarius, commonly known as the solitary groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features upright stems with narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a cluster at the top of the stem. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and ragworts.

Senecio solitarius is known for its resilience and ability to thrive in harsh conditions.
