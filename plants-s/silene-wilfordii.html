Silene wilfordii

Silene wilfordii, commonly known as Wilford's campion, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats. The plant produces showy pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

Silene wilfordii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native environment.
