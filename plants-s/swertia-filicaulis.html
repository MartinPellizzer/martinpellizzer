Swertia filicaulis

Swertia filicaulis, commonly known as the slender swertia, is a perennial herb native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Swertia filicaulis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
