Stramonium vulgatum

Stramonium vulgatum, commonly known as thornapple, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

The plant has a strong, unpleasant odor and contains toxic alkaloids, making it potentially harmful to humans and animals. Stramonium vulgatum is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats.
