Sansevieria coleana

Sansevieria coleana, commonly known as the African spear or snake plant, is a succulent perennial native to tropical Africa.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with subtle variegation. The plant is characterized by its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering. It produces small, white flowers on slender stalks during the growing season.

Sansevieria coleana is well-suited for indoor environments due to its air-purifying properties and low maintenance requirements.
