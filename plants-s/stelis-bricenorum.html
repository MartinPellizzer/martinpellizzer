Stelis bricenorum

Stelis bricenorum, commonly known as the Bricen's orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, particularly found in the cloud forests of Colombia and Ecuador. This epiphytic orchid typically grows on tree branches or rocks, anchoring itself with its root system while absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air. Its flowers are usually small, with a delicate appearance, and come in shades of white, pink, and purple.

Stelis bricenorum is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
