Saxifraga carpetana var. aliciana

Saxifraga carpetana var. aliciana, commonly known as the Alpine saxifrage, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the high alpine regions of Europe.

It features dense clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to August. The plant has dark green, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating, known as a bloom. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, harsh environments.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in rock gardens and alpine settings.
