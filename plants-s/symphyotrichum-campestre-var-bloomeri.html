Symphyotrichum campestre var. bloomeri

Symphyotrichum campestre var. bloomeri, commonly known as Bloomer's New England aster, is a variant of the New England aster native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial with showy, typically purple or pink flower heads that bloom in the fall. The plant features slender, branched stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in swampy areas or along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for wetland gardens and native plant collections.
