Sansevieria humiflora

Sansevieria humiflora, commonly known as the "humiflora snake plant," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with a bluish tint and can grow up to 2 feet in height. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers on slender stalks during the summer months. It is highly drought-tolerant and thrives in low-light conditions, making it an excellent choice for indoor gardening.

Its ability to purify the air and its low maintenance requirements have contributed to its popularity as a houseplant.
