Stephania hypoglauca

Stephania hypoglauca, commonly known as the blue-green stephania, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Menispermaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and heart-shaped leaves. Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in horticultural settings.
