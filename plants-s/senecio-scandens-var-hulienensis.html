Senecio scandens var. hulienensis

Senecio scandens var. hulienensis, commonly known as the Hulien's spider flower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and showy yellow flowers. The plant features narrow, slightly hairy leaves and produces small, daisy-like blooms that attract pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and hanging baskets.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
