Scutellaria lutescens

Scutellaria lutescens, commonly known as golden beard or yellow beard, is a perennial herb native to southern China.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Scutellaria lutescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
