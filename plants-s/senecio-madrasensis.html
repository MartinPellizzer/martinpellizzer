Senecio madrasensis

Senecio madrasensis, commonly known as the Madras groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters. It is often found in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to dry conditions.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
