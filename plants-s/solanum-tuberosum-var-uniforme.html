Solanum tuberosum var. uniforme

Solanum tuberosum var. uniforme, commonly known as the uniform potato, is a cultivated variety of the common potato.

It is characterized by its relatively uniform tuber size and shape, making it ideal for commercial processing. The plant belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and produces edible underground stems called tubers. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its consistent yield and resistance to certain diseases.
