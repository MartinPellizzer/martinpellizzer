Sinorchis simplex

Sinorchis simplex, commonly known as the simple orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single, lance-shaped leaf. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and lack the typical showy petals of many orchids.

It is a rare and endangered species, with limited geographic distribution and specific habitat requirements.
