Sansevieria aureovariegata

Sansevieria aureovariegata, commonly known as the Gold Variiegata Snake Plant, is a striking member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that display a vibrant mix of green and yellow hues. The plant's leaves are marked with distinct, horizontal bands of light yellow, giving it a unique and eye-catching appearance. It is highly resilient, thriving in low-light conditions and requiring minimal watering, making it an ideal choice for indoor gardening.

Additionally, it is noted for its air-purifying qualities, contributing to improved indoor air quality.
