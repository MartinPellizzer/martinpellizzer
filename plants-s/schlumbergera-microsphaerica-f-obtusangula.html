Schlumbergera microsphaerica f. obtusangula

Schlumbergera microsphaerica f. obtusangula, commonly known as the obtuse-angled Christmas cactus, is a hybrid cultivar of the Schlumbergera genus.

It is characterized by its thick, segmented stems that form a compact, bushy shape. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, pink, and white during the holiday season. It is a popular ornamental plant due to its long blooming period and adaptability to indoor conditions.

Its foliage is typically a deep green with a slightly waxy texture.
