Serapias Orientalis Subsp. Feldwegiana

Serapias orientalis subsp. feldwegiana is a rare and elegant orchid native to the mountainous regions of Turkey. This subspecies is known for its striking yellow and green flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in rocky and well-drained soils, often found in alpine and subalpine habitats. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this unique plant from environmental threats.
