Satureja × orjenii

Satureja × orjenii, commonly known as the Orjen thyme, is a hybrid shrub native to the Balkan region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, small, ovate leaves. The plant produces pink to purple flowers in spikes, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or dry garden environments.

Due to its ornamental value and aromatic properties, it is often cultivated in horticulture and traditional herbal practices.
