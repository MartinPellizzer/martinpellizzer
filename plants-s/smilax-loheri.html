Smilax loheri

Smilax loheri, commonly known as the Loher's catbriar, is a perennial vine native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its woody stems and compound leaves with three to five leaflets. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits. Smilax loheri is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and thickets.
