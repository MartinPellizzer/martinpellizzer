Stylidium amoenum var. caulescens

Stylidium amoenum var. caulescens, commonly known as the slender sun-orchid, is a rare and striking member of the orchid family native to Australia.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and produces small, yellowish-green flowers with a distinctive, spurred labellum. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils, often in arid or semi-arid regions. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable and requires protection to ensure its survival.
