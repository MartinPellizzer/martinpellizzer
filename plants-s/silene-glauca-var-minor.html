Silene glauca var. minor

Silene glauca var. minor, commonly known as the lesser bladder campion, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its pale blue to pink flowers and hairy stems. Native to arctic and alpine regions of Europe and Asia, it thrives in rocky and sandy soils. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

It is often found in open habitats such as tundra, heath, and rocky slopes.
