Stelis megachlamys subsp. teotepecensis

Stelis megachlamys subsp.

teotepecensis, commonly known as the Teotepec Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, fragrant flowers.

The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its inflorescence consists of several showy, white to pale pink flowers with a distinctive lip.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
