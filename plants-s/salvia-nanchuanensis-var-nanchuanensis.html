Salvia nanchuanensis var. nanchuanensis

Salvia nanchuanensis var. nanchuanensis, commonly known as the Nanchuan sage, is a perennial herb native to China.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces striking violet-blue flowers in whorled clusters, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value and adaptability make it a popular choice among horticulturists.
