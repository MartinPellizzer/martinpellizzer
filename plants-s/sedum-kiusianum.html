Sedum kiusianum

Sedum kiusianum, commonly known as the Kiusianum stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers during the summer months, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for both gardeners and horticultural enthusiasts.
