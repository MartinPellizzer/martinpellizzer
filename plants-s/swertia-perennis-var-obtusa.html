Swertia perennis var. obtusa

Swertia perennis var. obtusa, commonly known as the broad-leaf gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky slopes. Its leaves are broad and ovate, often with a slightly wavy margin.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
