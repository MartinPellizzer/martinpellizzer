Senna cana var. cana

Senna cana var. cana, commonly known as the yellow senna, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and clusters of bright yellow flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and dry woodland habitats.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine.
