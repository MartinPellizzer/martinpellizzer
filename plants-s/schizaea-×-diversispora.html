Schizaea × diversispora

Schizaea × diversispora, commonly known as the hybrid common spike moss, is a fern ally belonging to the family Schizaeaceae.

It is a terrestrial, epiphytic, or lithophytic plant that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant exhibits a dichotomous branching pattern, with small, scale-like leaves that are often arranged in pairs. Schizaea × diversispora is characterized by its sporangia, which are clustered in spore-bearing structures called sporangia, and it reproduces via spores rather than seeds.

This species is of interest in botanical studies due to its unique morphology and ecological adaptability.
