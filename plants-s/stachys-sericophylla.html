Stachys sericophylla

Stachys sericophylla, commonly known as the silverleaf hedge nettle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray, woolly leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale purple flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in dry, coastal areas.

Stachys sericophylla is a perennial shrub that is relatively low-maintenance and drought-tolerant.
