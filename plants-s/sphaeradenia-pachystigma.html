Sphaeradenia pachystigma

Sphaeradenia pachystigma, commonly known as the broad-leaved sphaeradenia, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and striking inflorescence. Native to tropical regions of Africa, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces small, white flowers surrounded by a spadix, typical of the Araceae family.

Its rhizomatous growth habit makes it suitable for cultivation in tropical and subtropical gardens.
