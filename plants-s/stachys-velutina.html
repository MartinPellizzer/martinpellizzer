Stachys velutina

Stachys velutina, commonly known as velvet hedge-nettle, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and stems, which give it a velvety texture. The plant produces small, pale purple to blue flowers arranged in whorls along the stems. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and streambanks.

Stachys velutina is often used in herbal medicine for its potential anti-inflammatory properties.
