Sherbournia batesii subsp. batesii

Sherbournia batesii subsp.

batesii, commonly known as the Bates' sherbournia, is a species native to the western United States. It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its botanical features make it an attractive choice for xeriscaping and native plant gardens.
