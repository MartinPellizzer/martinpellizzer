Stachys obscura

Stachys obscura, commonly known as the narrow-leaved betony, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its small, tubular flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom in the summer months.

Stachys obscura is noted for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
