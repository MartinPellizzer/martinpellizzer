Styrax esquirolii

Styrax esquirolii, commonly known as the California styrax, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. It produces small, reddish-brown berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans.

Styrax esquirolii thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal scrub and chaparral habitats.
