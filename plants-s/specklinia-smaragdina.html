Specklinia smaragdina

Specklinia smaragdina, commonly known as the emerald rosette.

This epiphytic orchid is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit. It features glossy, dark green leaves that are often marked with subtle speckles, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and green, typically during the summer months.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media for optimal growth.
