Senecio cygnorum

Senecio cygnorum, commonly known as the Swan-necked groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its yellow flowers are surrounded by pale green bracts, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Senecio cygnorum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
