Schoenoxiphium lanceum

Schoenoxiphium lanceum, commonly known as the lanceleaf phlox, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features upright stems that bear opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to purple, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its botanical characteristics make it an attractive and hardy addition to gardens with suitable growing conditions.
