Sida planiflora

Sida planiflora, commonly known as the flatleaf sida, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features alternate, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and clustered in dense inflorescences.

Sida planiflora is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides, thriving in full sun.
