Scutellaria durangensis

Scutellaria durangensis, commonly known as the Durango skullcap, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in rocky or dry habitats and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
