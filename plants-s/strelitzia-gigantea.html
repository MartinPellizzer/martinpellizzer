Strelitzia gigantea

Strelitzia gigantea, commonly known as the giant bird-of-paradise, is a striking tropical plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, brightly colored inflorescences that resemble a bird in flight, with vibrant blue and yellow petals. The plant features broad, sword-shaped leaves that are glossy and can grow up to several feet in length. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil and ample sunlight.

Strelitzia gigantea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
