Saccolabium minahassae

Saccolabium minahassae, commonly known as the Minahassa saccolabium, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive pod-like fruits. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are trifoliate, and the flowers are usually small and clustered in racemes.
