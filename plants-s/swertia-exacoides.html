Swertia exacoides

Swertia exacoides, commonly known as Himalayan gentian, is a perennial herb native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, thriving at elevations between 2,000 and 4,500 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered with a dense layer of fine hairs.

Swertia exacoides is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional Ayurvedic medicine for various health benefits.
