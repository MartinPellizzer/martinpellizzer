Statice filicaulis var. maroccana

Statice filicaulis var. maroccana, commonly known as the Moroccan statice, is a compact, flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces spikes of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant blooms and ability to attract pollinators.

It is often used in floral arrangements due to its long-lasting flowers and delicate appearance.
