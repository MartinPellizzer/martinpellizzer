Sansevieria ceylonica

Sansevieria ceylonica, commonly known as the Ceylon snake plant, is a species of succulent native to Sri Lanka.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with distinctive yellow or white variegation. The plant is characterized by its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal water, making it highly adaptable. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, storing water to help it survive in arid environments.

Sansevieria ceylonica is also noted for its air-purifying qualities, contributing to indoor air quality improvement.
