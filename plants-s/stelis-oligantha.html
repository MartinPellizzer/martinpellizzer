Stelis oligantha

Stelis oligantha, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant has a slender, upright stem with few nodes and produces small, greenish-white flowers. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Stelis oligantha is valued for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
