Stelis mandonii

Stelis mandonii, commonly known as the Mandon's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a short, erect stem with clustered, narrow leaves. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and usually white or pale yellow, arranged in a spike.

Stelis mandonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
