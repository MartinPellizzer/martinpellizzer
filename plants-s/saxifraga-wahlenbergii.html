Saxifraga wahlenbergii

Saxifraga wahlenbergii, commonly known as the mountain saxifrage, is a flowering plant belonging to the Saxifragaceae family.

It is native to the Arctic and alpine regions of Europe and Asia, where it thrives in rocky and well-drained soils. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to July. Its leaves are basal, forming a rosette, and are covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Saxifraga wahlenbergii is often found in open habitats such as tundra, meadows, and mountain slopes.
