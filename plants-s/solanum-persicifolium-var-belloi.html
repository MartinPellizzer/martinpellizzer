Solanum persicifolium var. belloi

Solanum persicifolium var. belloi, commonly known as the pink bell pepper plant, is a variant of the nightshade family (Solanaceae).

It is characterized by its vibrant pink to red fruit, which resembles small bell peppers. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with sprawling stems and hairy leaves. Its flowers are usually yellow with white markings, and it thrives in warm, sunny conditions.

The fruit is edible and often used in culinary applications, though it may have a milder flavor compared to typical peppers.
