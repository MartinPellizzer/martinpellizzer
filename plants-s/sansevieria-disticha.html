Sansevieria disticha

Sansevieria disticha, commonly known as the snake plant, is a hardy, evergreen perennial plant native to Africa.

It features tall, upright leaves that are typically dark green with lighter green vertical stripes. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted for storing water, making it drought-tolerant.

Sansevieria disticha is also noted for its air-purifying qualities, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
