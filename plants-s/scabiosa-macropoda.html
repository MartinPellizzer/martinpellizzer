Scabiosa macropoda

Scabiosa macropoda, commonly known as the Greater Scabious, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, spiky stems and clusters of small, pink or purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has palmately divided leaves that are hairy and slightly serrated. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and wildflower meadows.

Scabiosa macropoda is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
