Specklinia polygonoides

Specklinia polygonoides, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its leaves are broad and leathery, adapted to retain moisture in its natural habitat.

Specklinia polygonoides is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
