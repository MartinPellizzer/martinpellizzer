Solanum kollastrum

Solanum kollastrum, commonly known as the Kollastrum nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats and produces small, yellow flowers. Its fruits are fleshy berries that turn red when mature.

Solanum kollastrum is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its attractive foliage and flowering display.
