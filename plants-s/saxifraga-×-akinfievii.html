Saxifraga × akinfievii

Saxifraga × akinfievii, commonly known as the Akinfiev's saxifrage, is a hybrid plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage, with dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers during the summer months, which are arranged in a compact inflorescence. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine environments.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
