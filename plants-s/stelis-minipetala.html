Stelis minipetala

Stelis minipetala, commonly known as the miniature-petaled orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small epiphytic orchid that typically grows on tree branches or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Its flowers are usually clustered in racemes and bloom during the warmer months.

Stelis minipetala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
