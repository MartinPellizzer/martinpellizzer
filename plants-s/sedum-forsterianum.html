Sedum forsterianum

Sedum forsterianum, commonly known as the Forster's stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is adapted to rocky and arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves. It typically blooms in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Sedum forsterianum is a low-maintenance plant that thrives in full sun and well-draining soil.
