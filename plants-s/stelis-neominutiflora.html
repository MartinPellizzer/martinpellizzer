Stelis neominutiflora

Stelis neominutiflora, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. The plant features a slender stem with clustered, tiny white or pale green flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, adapted to conserve moisture in its natural habitat.

Stelis neominutiflora is valued for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
