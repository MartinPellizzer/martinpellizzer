Smilax odontolama

Smilax odontolama, commonly known as the Florida arrowleaf smilax, is a perennial vine native to the southeastern United States.

It features woody stems that are often thorny and can climb or trail along the ground. The plant has heart-shaped, arrowhead-like leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. Its flowers are small, greenish-yellow, and appear in clusters, followed by red or orange berries that are edible for birds.

Smilax odontolama thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.
