Salacia preussii var. preussii

Salacia preussii var. preussii, commonly known as the Preuss's salacia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Salicaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its woody stems, simple leaves, and small, inconspicuous yellow flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The plant has been studied for its potential medicinal properties, though further research is needed to confirm its uses.
