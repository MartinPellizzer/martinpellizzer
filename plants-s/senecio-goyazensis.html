Senecio goyazensis

Senecio goyazensis, commonly known as the Goyazensis groundsel, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It features narrow, linear leaves and produces small yellow flowers arranged in clusters.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
