Sida longipes var. canescens

Sida longipes var. canescens, commonly known as the hairy sida, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems covered in fine, whitish hairs. The leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a pubescent surface. The flowers are small, yellowish, and clustered in dense inflorescences.

It is adapted to grow in dry, disturbed soils and is often found in open habitats.
