Senecio adenophyllus var. angustipinnatus

Senecio adenophyllus var. angustipinnatus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf senecio, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, narrow leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white wax, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil to thrive.

Its unique morphology makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
