Solms-laubachia pulcherrima var. latifolia

Solms-laubachia pulcherrima var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leafed flame tree, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, flame-colored bracts that surround its small, clustered flowers. The leaves are broad and lance-shaped, providing a lush green backdrop to the vibrant floral display. This species thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its dramatic visual impact in gardens and landscapes.
