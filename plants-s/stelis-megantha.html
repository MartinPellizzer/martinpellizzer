Stelis megantha

Stelis megantha, commonly known as the showy orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a few leaves and produces clusters of showy, fragrant flowers. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to orange with dark markings, attracting pollinators such as bees and wasps.

Stelis megantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
