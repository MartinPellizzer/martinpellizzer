Stemodia lanceolata f. latifolia

Stemodia lanceolata f. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf stemodia, is a flowering plant species belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves, which are broader than the typical variety of the species. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in composite heads, typical of the daisy family. It is native to southern Africa and thrives in well-drained soils.
