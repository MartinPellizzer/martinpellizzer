Specularia speculum-veneris f. plena

Specularia speculum-veneris f. plena, commonly known as the Venus's Looking-Glass, is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, double blooms that resemble intricate, mirror-like patterns, giving it its evocative common name. The plant produces showy, pinkish-white flowers with a distinctive, almost translucent appearance. It thrives in cool climates and prefers well-drained soil, often growing in rocky or alpine environments.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and its unique floral structure.
