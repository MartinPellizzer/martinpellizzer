Stipa pennata valida

Stipa pennata valida, commonly known as the slender feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It is characterized by its fine, slender leaves and distinctive, feathery panicles that give it a delicate appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.

Stipa pennata valida plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
