Serratula mollis

Serratula mollis, commonly known as the smooth serratula, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features basal rosettes of narrow, gray-green leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.

Serratula mollis is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
