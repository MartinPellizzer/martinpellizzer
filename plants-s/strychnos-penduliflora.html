Strychnos penduliflora

Strychnos penduliflora, commonly known as the hanging-flower strychnos, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that hang in pendulous inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds covered in a sticky, fleshy coating.
