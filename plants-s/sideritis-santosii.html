Sideritis santosii

Sideritis santosii, commonly known as the Santos' sainfoin, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Sideritis santosii is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
