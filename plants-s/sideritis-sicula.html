Sideritis sicula

Sideritis sicula, commonly known as Sicilian ironwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region, particularly Sicily.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features slender, hairy stems and small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Sideritis sicula is often found in rocky or limestone soils and is valued for its medicinal properties.
