Silene ikonnikovii

Silene ikonnikovii, commonly known as the Ikonnikov's campion, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats. The plant produces showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

Silene ikonnikovii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
