Stachys tenuifolia

Stachys tenuifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaved hedge-nettle, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features slender, hairy stems and small, opposite leaves that are typically narrow and lance-shaped. Its flowers are clustered in whorls and are usually pale blue or violet in color.

Stachys tenuifolia is often found in moist, disturbed soils and can thrive in woodland or meadow environments.
