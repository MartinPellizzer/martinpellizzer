Scutellaria iyoensis f. albiflora

Scutellaria iyoensis f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered blueweed, is a rare and specialized plant species native to Japan.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant is distinguished by its pale to white flowers, which contrast with the typical blue or purple hues of its species. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution and unique floral characteristics, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
