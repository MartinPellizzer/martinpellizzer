Stelis niesseniae

Stelis niesseniae, commonly known as the Niessen's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow with intricate patterns, and it blooms during the rainy season.

Stelis niesseniae is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
