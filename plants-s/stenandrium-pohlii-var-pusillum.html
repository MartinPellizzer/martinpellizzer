Stenandrium pohlii var. pusillum

Stenandrium pohlii var. pusillum, commonly known as the dwarf stenandrium, is a compact, low-growing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically a deep green color with a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces delicate, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
