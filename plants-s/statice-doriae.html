Statice doriae

Statice doriae, commonly known as Doria's sea thrift, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Europe.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and produces showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in well-drained, sandy or rocky soils and is often found in maritime environments. Its ability to tolerate salt spray and strong winds makes it a hardy species suitable for coastal gardens.

Statice doriae is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in stabilizing dune ecosystems.
