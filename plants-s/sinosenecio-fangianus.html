Sinosenecio fangianus

Sinosenecio fangianus, commonly known as the Fang Mountains sinosenecio, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with cool, moist conditions. It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small yellow flowers arranged in clusters.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
