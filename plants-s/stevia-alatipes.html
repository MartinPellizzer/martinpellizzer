Stevia alatipes

Stevia alatipes, commonly known as the broadleaf stevia, is a perennial herb native to South America.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and small yellow flowers. The plant is renowned for its intense sweet taste, which comes from steviol glycosides, making it a natural sweetener. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often growing in humid tropical climates.

Stevia alatipes is widely cultivated for both ornamental and commercial purposes.
