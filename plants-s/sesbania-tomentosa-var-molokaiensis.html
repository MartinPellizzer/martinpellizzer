Sesbania tomentosa var. molokaiensis

Sesbania tomentosa var. molokaiensis, commonly known as Molokai sesbania, is a shrub native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in coastal areas and is adapted to saline and dry conditions. It plays a role in stabilizing sand dunes and providing habitat for native wildlife.

Its seeds are often dispersed by wind, contributing to its spread along shorelines.
