Senecio deltoideus

Senecio deltoideus, commonly known as the deltoid senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, deltoid-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense, conical inflorescences. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and compact growth habit.

Senecio deltoideus thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
