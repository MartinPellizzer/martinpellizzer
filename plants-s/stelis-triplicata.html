Stelis triplicata

Stelis triplicata, commonly known as the three-lobed orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a slender, branching stem with small, clustered leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green or white, with three distinct lobes, giving it its common name.

Stelis triplicata is valued for its unique appearance and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
