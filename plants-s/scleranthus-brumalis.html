Scleranthus brumalis

Scleranthus brumalis, commonly known as winter spleenwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Polygonaceae.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, calcareous soils, often found in wet meadows and along stream banks. Scleranthus brumalis plays a role in stabilizing soil in wetland ecosystems and provides nectar for early pollinators.
