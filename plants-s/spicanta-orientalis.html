Spicanta orientalis

Spicanta orientalis, commonly known as the Eastern Spiky Grass, is a perennial herb native to the temperate regions of eastern Asia.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, spiny hairs, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, typically blooming in the spring. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slightly rough texture.

Spicanta orientalis is often found in meadows and woodland edges, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
