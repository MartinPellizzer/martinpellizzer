Sicyos sertulifer

Sicyos sertulifer, commonly known as the clustered bottle gourd, is a vine native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes other gourds and cucurbits. The plant produces large, lobed leaves and yellow flowers that eventually develop into thick, bottle-shaped fruits. Its roots and stems are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
