Securidaca fragilis

Securidaca fragilis, commonly known as the delicate securidaca, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and southern Asia.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant produces pods that contain seeds, which are often dispersed by wind. It is typically found in open woodlands and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils.
