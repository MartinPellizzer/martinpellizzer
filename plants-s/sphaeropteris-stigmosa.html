Sphaeropteris stigmosa

Sphaeropteris stigmosa, commonly known as the bamboo fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a distinctive crown of arching fronds that give it a graceful appearance. Its rhizome is thick and fleshy, supporting the growth of the plant in humid, tropical environments.

Sphaeropteris stigmosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
