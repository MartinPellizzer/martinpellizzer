Silene turbinata

Silene turbinata, commonly known as the turban bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces striking, bell-shaped flowers that are usually pink or purple in color, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Silene turbinata is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to thrive in well-drained environments.
