Stelis crenata

Stelis crenata, commonly known as the crested stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on tree trunks or rocks. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Stelis crenata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
