Scabiosa columbaria var. tenuisecta

Scabiosa columbaria var. tenuisecta, commonly known as the slenderleaf garden clover, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of western Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of small, pink or white flowers resembling clover. The plant has delicate, narrow leaves that are finely divided, giving it a feathery appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in grassy areas and along roadsides.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
