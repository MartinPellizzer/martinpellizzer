Saxifraga llonakhensis

Saxifraga llonakhensis, commonly known as the Llonakhensis saxifrage, is a rare and endemic plant species found in the high-altitude regions of the Caucasus.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers during the summer months, typically blooming in June and July. Its leaves are usually succulent and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs, which help to retain moisture in its harsh environment.
