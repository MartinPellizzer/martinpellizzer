Specklinia petiolaris

Specklinia petiolaris, commonly known as the leafless orchid, is a unique member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its lack of leaves, relying instead on its specialized roots for photosynthesis. The plant produces small, green, photosynthetic roots that are often covered in a dense, velvety coating. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in the understory of tropical rainforests.

Due to its delicate nature, it is often cultivated in controlled environments to ensure its survival.
