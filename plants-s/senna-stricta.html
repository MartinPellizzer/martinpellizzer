Senna stricta

Senna stricta, commonly known as the narrowleaf senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are narrow and pinnately compound, with small, oval-shaped leaflets.

Senna stricta is known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for its anti-inflammatory and laxative effects.
