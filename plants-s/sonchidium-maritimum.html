Sonchidium maritimum

Sonchidium maritimum, commonly known as sea holly, is a coastal plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous annual with striking blue-violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has a robust root system that helps stabilize sandy or saline soils. Its leaves are typically gray-green with a waxy coating, aiding in water retention.

It is often found growing in dunes and salt marshes, demonstrating its adaptability to harsh maritime environments.
