Strychnos gnetifolia

Strychnos gnetifolia, commonly known as the African strychnos, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Gnetaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant produces woody capsules that contain seeds, and it typically grows in moist, shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it an important species in the study of gymnosperm-like characteristics in angiosperms.
