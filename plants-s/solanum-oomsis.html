Solanum oomsis

Solanum oomsis, commonly known as the Oomsis nightshade, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its flowers are usually white or pale blue, with five petals, and it produces small, round berries that are toxic to humans and animals.

The leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.
