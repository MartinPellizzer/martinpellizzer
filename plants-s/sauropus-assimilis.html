Sauropus assimilis

Sauropus assimilis, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or silver evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with a silvery underside, which give it a striking visual appearance. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite and elliptical, often with a waxy texture.

Sauropus assimilis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is commonly cultivated as a houseplant or in gardens.
