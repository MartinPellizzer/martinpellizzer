Scutellaria luzonica var. playfairii

Scutellaria luzonica var. playfairii, commonly known as Luzon scutellaria, is a perennial herb native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It is often found growing in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists.
