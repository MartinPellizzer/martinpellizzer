Stelis uniflora

Stelis uniflora, commonly known as the single-flower orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces a single, showy flower per stem. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow, with a distinctive spurred lip.

Stelis uniflora is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
