Susarium andinum

Susarium andinum, commonly known as the Andean lily, is a striking flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Andes Mountains.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, showy white or pale pink flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern at the base. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
