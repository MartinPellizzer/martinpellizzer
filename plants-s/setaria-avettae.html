Setaria avettae

Setaria avettae, commonly known as the Aventea Panicgrass, is a species of grass native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and agricultural fields. Its leaves are narrow and often have a bluish-green hue, and it produces wind-dispersed seeds.

Setaria avettae is adapted to a variety of soil types and can thrive in both dry and moist conditions.
