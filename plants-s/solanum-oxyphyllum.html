Solanum oxyphyllum

Solanum oxyphyllum, commonly known as the purple passionflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped and have a velvety texture.

Solanum oxyphyllum is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant blooms and unique foliage.
