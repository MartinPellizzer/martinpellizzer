Spananthe angulosa

Spananthe angulosa, commonly known as the angular orchid, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, angular-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
