Satureja simensis

Satureja simensis, commonly known as Ethiopian thyme, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant leaves and clusters of pink or purple flowers. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Satureja simensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for its antiseptic and anti-inflammatory effects.
