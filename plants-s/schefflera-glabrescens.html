Schefflera glabrescens

Schefflera glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth-leaf schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like clusters of leaves, which are typically glossy and leathery in texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Schefflera glabrescens is often cultivated as a ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor conditions.
