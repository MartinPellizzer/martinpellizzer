Saxifraga vvedenskyi

Saxifraga vvedenskyi, commonly known as the Vvedensky's saxifrage, is a flowering plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats on rocky or barren ground. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers during the short growing season. Its leaves are usually rosette-shaped and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Saxifraga vvedenskyi is adapted to harsh environmental conditions, including cold temperatures and strong winds.
