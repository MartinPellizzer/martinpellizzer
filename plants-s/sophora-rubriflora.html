Sophora rubriflora

Sophora rubriflora, commonly known as the red-flowered Sophora, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and striking red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is known for its leguminous properties. It produces elongated pods that contain edible seeds, which are often used in traditional Chinese cuisine.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
