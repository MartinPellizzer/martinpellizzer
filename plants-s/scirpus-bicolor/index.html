Scirpus Bicolor

Scirpus bicolor is a unique wetland plant known for its striking color variations. It thrives in marshy environments and is commonly found in temperate regions. The plant features slender stems with distinctive blue and white stripes. Its ability to absorb excess water makes it valuable for ecological restoration. This species plays an important role in maintaining wetland biodiversity.
