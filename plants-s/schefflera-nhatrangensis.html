Schefflera nhatrangensis

Schefflera nhatrangensis, commonly known as Nhatrang umbrella tree, is a tropical plant native to Vietnam.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like crown of large, glossy green leaves. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are usually arranged in a compact, circular pattern at the ends of its branches.

Schefflera nhatrangensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
