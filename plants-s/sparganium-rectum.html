Sparganium rectum

Sparganium rectum, commonly known as the narrow-stem bur-reed, is a herbaceous aquatic plant native to wetlands and shallow waters.

It has slender, cylindrical stems that are often covered in small, spiny structures. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and strap-like, typically floating or partially submerged.

Sparganium rectum plays a role in stabilizing wetland soils and providing habitat for aquatic organisms.
