Spathodea dolichandra

Spathodea dolichandra, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a striking visual display. The plant has compound leaves composed of several ovate to lanceolate leaflets. Its bark is smooth and greyish, with a tendency to flake off in small pieces.

Spathodea dolichandra is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
