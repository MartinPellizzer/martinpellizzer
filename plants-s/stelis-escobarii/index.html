Stelis Escobarii

Stelis escobarii is a rare orchid native to the cloud forests of Central America. It is known for its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale pink. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific conditions to survive. Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and is protected in many regions. Its beauty and rarity make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
