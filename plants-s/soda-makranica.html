Soda makranica

Soda makranica, commonly known as the coastal spurge, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant thrives in saline and well-drained soils, often found along rocky shores and dunes. It has a low-growing habit with a branching stem that produces numerous small leaves.

Soda makranica is adapted to withstand harsh coastal conditions, including high salinity and strong winds.
