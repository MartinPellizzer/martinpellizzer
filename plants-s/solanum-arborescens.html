Solanum arborescens

Solanum arborescens, commonly known as the tree tomato or carambola tree, is a shrub or small tree native to South America.

It produces yellow to orange fruits that are often used in culinary applications. The plant has simple, alternate leaves with a rough texture and a distinct aroma. Its flowers are typically yellow or green and are followed by the characteristic five-lobed fruits.

Solanum arborescens is valued for its ornamental qualities and edible fruit in various regions.
