Stephania concinna

Stephania concinna, commonly known as the Indian snakeroot, is a perennial climbing shrub native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the family Menispermaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive yellow flowers. The plant produces a milky sap that contains various alkaloids, some of which have medicinal properties.
