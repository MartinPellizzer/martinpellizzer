Schefflera vantsilana

Schefflera vantsilana, commonly known as the Madagascar schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Madagascar.

It features a distinctive, umbrella-like crown composed of large, glossy, dark green leaves arranged in a whorled pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellowish-green flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in warm, humid conditions with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Schefflera vantsilana is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
