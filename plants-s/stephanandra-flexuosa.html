Stephanandra flexuosa

Stephanandra flexuosa, commonly known as the flexible stephanandra, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. The plant has opposite, simple, and often serrated leaves that turn red in the autumn. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
