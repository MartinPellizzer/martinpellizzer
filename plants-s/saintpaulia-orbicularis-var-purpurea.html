Saintpaulia orbicularis var. purpurea

Saintpaulia orbicularis var. purpurea, commonly known as the purple African violet, is a hybrid cultivar prized for its vibrant, velvety purple foliage and showy, tubular flowers.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa. The plant features compact, upright growth with broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often darker in color than the flowers. Its flowers are typically pink to purple in hue, with a distinctive pattern of darker veins.

This variety is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in indoor environments.
