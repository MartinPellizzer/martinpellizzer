Senna petersiana

Senna petersiana, commonly known as the yellow senna, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. Its leaves are bipinnate, consisting of numerous small leaflets, and the plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in various soil types.

Senna petersiana is also valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
