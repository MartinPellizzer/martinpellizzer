Sterculia megalocarpa

Sterculia megalocarpa, commonly known as the large-seeded sterculia, is a tropical tree native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, woody fruits that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces showy, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in panicles. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a rough texture.

The tree is valued for its durable wood and is often found in lowland rainforests.
