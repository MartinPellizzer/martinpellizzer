Sorindeia schweinfurthii

Sorindeia schweinfurthii, commonly known as the African velvet tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is known for its unique, velvety texture on its leaves, which gives it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Sorindeia schweinfurthii is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
