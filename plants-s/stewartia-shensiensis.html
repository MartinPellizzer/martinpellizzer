Stewartia shensiensis

Stewartia shensiensis, commonly known as the Chinese stewartia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Theaceae and is characterized by its ornamental value, particularly its showy white to pale pink flowers. The leaves are elliptical, glossy, and often have a prominent midrib. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.
