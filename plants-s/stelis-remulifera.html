Stelis remulifera

Stelis remulifera, commonly known as the lace orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, linear leaves and intricate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its flowers are often white or pale pink, with a distinctive, lace-like pattern on the petals.

Stelis remulifera is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
