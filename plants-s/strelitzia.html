Strelitzia

Strelitzia, commonly known as the bird of paradise, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the ginger family (Zingiberaceae) and is characterized by its unique, colorful inflorescences that resemble a bird in flight. The plant features long, narrow, strap-like leaves that are typically a deep green color. Its flowers consist of three vivid blue petals and three orange-yellow sepals, creating a dramatic and exotic appearance.

Strelitzia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and thrives in well-drained soil and bright, indirect light.
