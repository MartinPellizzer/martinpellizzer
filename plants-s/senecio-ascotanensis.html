Senecio ascotanensis

Senecio ascotanensis, commonly known as the Ascotan groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South America, specifically found in Peru and Bolivia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, thriving in rocky soils and high altitudes.
