Stachys officinalis subsp. velebitica

Stachys officinalis subsp.

velebitica, commonly known as the Velebit betony, is a perennial herb native to the Balkan Peninsula. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves.

The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats. Its leaves are lanceolate and covered with fine hairs, while its flowers are arranged in whorls along the stem.

The species is valued for its medicinal properties and has been traditionally used in herbal remedies.
