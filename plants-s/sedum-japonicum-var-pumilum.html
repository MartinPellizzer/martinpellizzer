Sedum japonicum var. pumilum

Sedum japonicum var. pumilum, commonly known as Japanese stonecrop, is a low-growing succulent plant native to Japan.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, clustered leaves that are typically green or reddish in color. The plant produces star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It is well adapted to rocky and sunny environments, making it a hardy and drought-tolerant species.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
