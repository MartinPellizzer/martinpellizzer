Stachys elata

Stachys elata, commonly known as tall hedge-nettle, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, opposite leaves with a hairy texture. It produces clusters of pale blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer.
