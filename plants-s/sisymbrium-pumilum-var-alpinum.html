Sisymbrium pumilum var. alpinum

Sisymbrium pumilum var. alpinum, commonly known as the alpine wallflower, is a flowering plant native to alpine regions of Europe.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, bright yellow flowers in early spring. The plant has slender, branched stems and narrow, linear leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous habitats.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to cold, high-altitude environments.
