Sedum wuianum

Sedum wuianum, commonly known as the Wuian stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to China.

It is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It is often found growing in rocky or sandy soils, particularly in mountainous regions.

Sedum wuianum is a low-maintenance plant that is popular in rock gardens and as a decorative succulent.
