Serratula apiculata

Serratula apiculata, commonly known as the slender sickleweed, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to drought conditions. Serratula apiculata is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and deserts.
