Styrax fanshawei

Styrax fanshawei, commonly known as the Chinese styrax, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. The plant produces small, dark blue berries that are edible but not widely consumed. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a glossy surface.

Styrax fanshawei is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and is often used in gardens and landscapes.
