Stelis eublepharis var. glabriflora

Stelis eublepharis var. glabriflora, commonly known as the hairy-leafed stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features slender, upright pseudobulbs and linear, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. Its flowers are typically small, fragrant, and arranged in a raceme, with a distinctive coloration that ranges from white to pale yellow.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
