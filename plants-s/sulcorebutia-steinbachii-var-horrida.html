Sulcorebutia steinbachii var. horrida

Sulcorebutia steinbachii var. horrida, commonly known as the Horrid Sulcorebutia, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny stems that form a compact, globular shape. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It is well adapted to drought conditions, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and ability to thrive in well-drained, sandy soils.
