Stelis myriantha

Stelis myriantha, commonly known as the clustered orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees in tropical rainforests. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive, elongated shape.

Stelis myriantha is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.
