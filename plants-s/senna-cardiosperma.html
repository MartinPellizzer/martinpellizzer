Senna cardiosperma

Senna cardiosperma, commonly known as the heartseed senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces distinctive seed pods that contain seeds with a heart-shaped structure, giving it its common name. Senna cardiosperma is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

It is also valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
