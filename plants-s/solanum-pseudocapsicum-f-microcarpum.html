Solanum pseudocapsicum f. microcarpum

Solanum pseudocapsicum f. microcarpum, commonly known as the dwarf nightshade, is a compact variant of the nightshade family.

It is characterized by its small, rounded leaves and clusters of small, violet flowers. The plant produces tiny, round berries that are typically less than 1 cm in diameter. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
