Stropholirion californicum

Stropholirion californicum, commonly known as the California flax.

It is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of California and Baja California. The plant produces small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in sandy or gravelly soils.

Stropholirion californicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in coastal climates.
