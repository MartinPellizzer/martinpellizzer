Silene cardiopetala var. deqenensis

Silene cardiopetala var. deqenensis, commonly known as the Deqen's lady's mantle, is a flowering plant native to high-altitude regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its rosette growth habit and showy pink or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, thriving in cool and moist conditions. Its leaves are usually hairy and arranged in a compact rosette, while its flowers are borne on slender stalks.

This variety is of interest to botanists due to its unique adaptations to its harsh habitat.
