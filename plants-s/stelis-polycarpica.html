Stelis polycarpica

Stelis polycarpica, commonly known as the many-fruited orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces numerous small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Stelis polycarpica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
