Stapelia × cupularis

Stapelia × cupularis, commonly known as the hooded carrion flower, is a hybrid succulent native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed stems and its striking, hooded花朵 that resemble decaying flesh. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers with a strong, unpleasant odor to attract pollinators. It is a member of the Apocynaceae family and is known for its ability to store water in its thick, fleshy stems.
