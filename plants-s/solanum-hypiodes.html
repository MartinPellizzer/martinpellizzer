Solanum hypiodes

Solanum hypiodes, commonly known as the purple-flowered nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically purple or violet in color. Its leaves are ovate and have a slightly hairy texture.

Solanum hypiodes is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can thrive in a variety of soil types.
