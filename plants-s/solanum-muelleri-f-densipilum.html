Solanum muelleri f. densipilum

Solanum muelleri f. densipilum, commonly known as the dense-piliated nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to Australia.

It features glandular, hairy leaves that are typically dark green and oval in shape. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale purple, which bloom in summer. Its fruit is a smooth, yellowish berry that matures in late winter.

This species is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions of southern Australia.
