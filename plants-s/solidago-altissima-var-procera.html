Solidago altissima var. procera

Solidago altissima var. procera, commonly known as the tall goldenrod, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers in late summer and early fall. Its leaves are lance-shaped, alternate, and covered in fine, downy hairs.

It thrives in full sun and is often found in moist soils along stream banks and in open woodlands.
