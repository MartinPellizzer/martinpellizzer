Selaginella nutans

Selaginella nutans, commonly known as the nodding spikemoss, is a species of spikemoss belonging to the family Selaginellaceae.

It is a small, herbaceous plant that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features slender, upright stems covered with small, scale-like leaves. Its reproductive structures, called strobila, are cone-like and contain spores, which are released when mature.

Selaginella nutans is known for its ability to survive in low-light conditions and is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value.
