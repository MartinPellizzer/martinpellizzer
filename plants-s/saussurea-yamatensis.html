Saussurea yamatensis

Saussurea yamatensis, commonly known as the Yama-take, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems and large, showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and thrives in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, with a fibrous texture.
