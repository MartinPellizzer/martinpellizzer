Shuteria

Shuteria, commonly known as the desert lily, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range from white to deep purple. Shuteria blooms in spring and is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements.
