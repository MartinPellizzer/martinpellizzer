Stelis immersa

Stelis immersa, commonly known as the immersed stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or mountainous regions. The plant has slender, upright stems that often bear a single or few leaves, and its flowers are small, fragrant, and usually white or pale yellow. Stelis immersa is characterized by its unique growth habit and is often found in epiphytic or lithophytic conditions.
