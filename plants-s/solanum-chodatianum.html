Solanum chodatianum

Solanum chodatianum, commonly known as the Chodat's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant typically grows as an annual or biennial herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

It produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into berries containing numerous seeds.
