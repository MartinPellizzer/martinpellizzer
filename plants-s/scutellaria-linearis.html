Scutellaria linearis

Scutellaria linearis, commonly known as narrowleaf skullcap, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features slender, linear leaves and produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers. It typically grows in moist, wooded areas and is often found in the eastern United States.

Scutellaria linearis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
