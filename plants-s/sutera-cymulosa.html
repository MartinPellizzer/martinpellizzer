Sutera cymulosa

Sutera cymulosa, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are velvety to the touch, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered white or yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is often found in moist, shady environments such as forests and wetlands.

Sutera cymulosa is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
