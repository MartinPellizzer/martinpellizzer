Sansevieria yumbiensis

Sansevieria yumbiensis, commonly known as the Yumbi snake plant, is a species native to the humid lowland forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its tall, narrow leaves that are often adorned with distinctive white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that thrives in a variety of indoor conditions.

Its ability to purify air and tolerate poor soil makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
