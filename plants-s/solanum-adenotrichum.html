Solanum adenotrichum

Solanum adenotrichum, commonly known as the hairy nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into round, berry-like fruits. Its leaves are hairy, which gives it its common name, and it belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae).

Solanum adenotrichum is often found in disturbed soils and open areas, where it thrives in full sun.
