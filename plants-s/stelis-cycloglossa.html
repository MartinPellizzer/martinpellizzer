Stelis cycloglossa

Stelis cycloglossa, commonly known as the spiral-lip orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique spiral-shaped labellum, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, tropical environments. Its flowers are usually small and white or pale yellow, arranged in a compact inflorescence.

Stelis cycloglossa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
