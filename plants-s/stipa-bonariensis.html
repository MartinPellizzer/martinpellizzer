Stipa bonariensis

Stipa bonariensis, commonly known as the Argentine feathergrass, is a perennial grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-gray foliage and delicate, feathery plumes that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and can thrive in a variety of soil types. It is drought-tolerant and prefers full sun, making it a hardy choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for landscape design.
