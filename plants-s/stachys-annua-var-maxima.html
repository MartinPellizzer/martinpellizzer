Stachys annua var. maxima

Stachys annua var. maxima, commonly known as giant betony, is a herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a fuzzy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.
