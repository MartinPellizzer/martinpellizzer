Silene nutans var. glabra

Silene nutans var. glabra, commonly known as the naked stellaria, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in meadows and grassy areas. The plant features small, white or pink flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, with a smooth surface due to the absence of hairs.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
