Syngonium vellozianum var. decipiens

Syngonium vellozianum var. decipiens, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a tropical species native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that often have a distinctive arrowhead shape, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees or other structures in its natural habitat. Its foliage is usually a vibrant green, with some varieties displaying variegation or reddish veins.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to flourish.
