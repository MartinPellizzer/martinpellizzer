Stelis villosa

Stelis villosa, commonly known as the hairy stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and clustered, small greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually elliptical and covered with fine, soft hairs.

Stelis villosa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
