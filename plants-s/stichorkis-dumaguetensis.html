Stichorkis dumaguetensis

Stichorkis dumaguetensis, commonly known as the Dumaguetan orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil, and it relies on specific pollinators for reproduction.
