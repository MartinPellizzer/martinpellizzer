Salvia dorrii var. clokeyi

Salvia dorrii var. clokeyi, commonly known as Clokey's sage, is a rare and endemic species native to the southwestern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial with gray-green, lance-shaped leaves and spikes of violet-blue flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils in arid or semi-arid environments. It is adapted to withstand drought and heat, making it a hardy species for xeriscaping.
