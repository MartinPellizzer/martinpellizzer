Sandersonia

Sandersonia, commonly known as the flame lily, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, strap-like leaves that can grow up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces a dramatic inflorescence with vibrant red and orange flowers that resemble flames. Sandersonia is a perennial herb that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
