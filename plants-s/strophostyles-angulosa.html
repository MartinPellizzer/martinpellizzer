Strophostyles angulosa

Strophostyles angulosa, commonly known as the angular strophostyle, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive angular appearance. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, often displaying a range of colors from white to pink.

The species is noted for its adaptability to moist, shady environments and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
