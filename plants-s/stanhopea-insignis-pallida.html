Stanhopea insignis pallida

Stanhopea insignis pallida, commonly known as the pale stanhopea, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically display a range of colors from white to pale yellow. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, adapting to its humid, shaded habitats.

The orchid thrives in warm, moist environments and is often found in forest understories where it can receive indirect sunlight.
