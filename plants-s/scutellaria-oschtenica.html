Scutellaria oschtenica

Scutellaria oschtenica, commonly known as the narrow-leaved skullcap, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, tubular blue or violet flowers. Its genus, Scutellaria, is characterized by the presence of a scutellum, a specialized structure in the seed coat.

The plant is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its potential anti-inflammatory and antispasmodic properties.
