Sinningia glazioviana

Sinningia glazioviana, commonly known as the Glaziovian gloxinia, is a striking tropical plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often a deep green with a slight sheen. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Sinningia glazioviana is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation indoors.
