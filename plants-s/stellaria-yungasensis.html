Stellaria yungasensis

Stellaria yungasensis, commonly known as the Yungas starwort, is a flowering plant native to the Andean region of South America.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in high-altitude grasslands and rocky habitats, thriving in cool and moist conditions. Its leaves are usually narrow and arranged in a basal rosette, while its stems are often prostrate or ascending.

Stellaria yungasensis is of interest to botanists due to its unique adaptations to its alpine environment.
