Sedum pseudospectabile

Sedum pseudospectabile, commonly known as the showy stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun exposure, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a versatile and low-maintenance plant for gardeners.
