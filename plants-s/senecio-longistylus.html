Senecio longistylus

Senecio longistylus, commonly known as the long-styled senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces yellow flowers with prominent, elongated styles. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
