Senecio pentapterus

Senecio pentapterus, commonly known as the Cape rose or purple iceplant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its ability to thrive in arid environments. Senecio pentapterus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and drought tolerance.

Its growth habit includes both upright and prostrate forms, making it adaptable to various garden settings.
