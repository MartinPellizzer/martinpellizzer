Sophronitis × wyattiana

Sophronitis × wyattiana, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Sophronitis cattleyana and Sophronitis × sanderiana.

It is a sympodial epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant features long, narrow pseudobulbs that support its evergreen leaves. Its flowers are typically fragrant and come in a variety of colors, including yellow, orange, and red.

It is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and ease of cultivation.
