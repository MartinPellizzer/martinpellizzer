Sprekelia stenopetala

Sprekelia stenopetala, commonly known as the narrow-petaled lily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant typically grows in coastal regions and is adapted to sandy, well-drained soils. It produces a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and has a short, erect stem.

Sprekelia stenopetala is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in Mediterranean climates.
