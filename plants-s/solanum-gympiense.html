Solanum gympiense

Solanum gympiense, commonly known as the Gympie nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its erect growth habit and showy, violet-blue flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically yellow or orange when ripe. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and rainforests.

Due to its toxic properties, it is not recommended for cultivation or consumption.
