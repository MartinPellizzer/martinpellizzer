Swertia duemmeriana

Swertia duemmeriana, commonly known as the Himalayan gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows at high altitudes, often in rocky or alpine habitats. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged oppositely along the stem.

Swertia duemmeriana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
