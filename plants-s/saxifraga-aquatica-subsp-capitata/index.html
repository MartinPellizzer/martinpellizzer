Saxifraga Aquatica Subsp. Capitata

Saxifraga aquatica subsp. capitata is a rare aquatic plant found in specific wetland habitats. It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and narrow leaves adapted to water environments. This subspecies thrives in shallow, still waters with high light exposure. Its presence indicates healthy ecosystems with stable water conditions. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this delicate species from habitat loss.
