Smilacina nervulosa

Smilacina nervulosa, commonly known as greenbriar, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern North America.

It features climbing stems with tendrils that allow it to ascend trees and shrubs. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a distinctive veining pattern.

The berries that follow the flowers are dark blue and edible, providing food for various wildlife species.
