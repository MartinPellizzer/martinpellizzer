Syngonium ruizii

Syngonium ruizii, commonly known as the arrowhead vine, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that often have a distinctive arrowhead pattern, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits. Syngonium ruizii thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

It is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
