Shiia cuspidata var. muratae

Shiia cuspidata var. muratae, commonly known as the Murata's sharp-leafed shiia, is a rare variant of the shiia plant characterized by its distinctive, pointed leaves.

It is native to specific regions in Japan, where it thrives in shaded, moist forest environments. The plant's foliage is typically dark green with a glossy texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers. Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and limited distribution, making it a subject of interest for botanists.

Conservation efforts are ongoing to protect this rare species from habitat loss and environmental changes.
