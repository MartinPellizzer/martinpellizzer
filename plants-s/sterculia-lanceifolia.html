Sterculia lanceifolia

Sterculia lanceifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sterculia, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 20 meters tall, characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces woody pods that contain flat, edible seeds, which are sometimes used in local cuisines.
