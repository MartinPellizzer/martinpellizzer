Selenipedium palmifolium

Selenipedium palmifolium, commonly known as the giant spider-orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, palm-like leaves and its unique, spider-like flower structure. The plant is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is known for its striking appearance and rarity in the wild. Selenipedium palmifolium thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific conditions to grow successfully.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists alike.
