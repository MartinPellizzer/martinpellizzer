Stelis Melanopus

Stelis melanopus is a rare orchid species native to the tropical regions of South America. This delicate plant is known for its striking dark purple flowers and compact growth habit. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific conditions to flourish. Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and faces significant conservation challenges. Understanding and protecting Stelis melanopus is crucial for preserving biodiversity in its native ecosystems.
