Spathodea vernicosa

Spathodea vernicosa, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a tropical tree native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy, flame-colored inflorescences that appear in clusters at the ends of branches. The plant has compound leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. Its flowers are typically yellow to orange in color, with a distinctive red or purple calyx.

The tree is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can grow up to 20 meters in height.
