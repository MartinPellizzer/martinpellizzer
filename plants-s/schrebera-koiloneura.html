Schrebera koiloneura

Schrebera koiloneura, commonly known as the zigzag tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, zigzagging branches and dense, leathery foliage. The leaves are typically elliptical and have a rough, textured surface. It produces small, clustered white flowers followed by yellowish fruits containing flat, winged seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm, humid climates.
