Stelis jimenezii

Stelis jimenezii, commonly known as the Jimenez's spider orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, erect stem and small, greenish-white flowers. Native to the high-altitude regions of Central and South America, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and is often found in cloud forests.

Its unique floral structure and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
