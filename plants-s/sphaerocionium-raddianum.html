Sphaerocionium raddianum

Sphaerocionium raddianum, commonly known as the red sandalwood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark reddish-brown heartwood, which is highly valued for its durability and fine grain. The plant has a straight trunk with smooth, gray bark that becomes fissured with age. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate, with a glossy surface.

Sphaerocionium raddianum is also known for its fragrant flowers and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
