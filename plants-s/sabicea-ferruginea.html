Sabicea ferruginea

Sabicea ferruginea, commonly known as the rusty sabicea, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of India, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a legume pod that contains several seeds.

Sabicea ferruginea is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along riverbanks.
