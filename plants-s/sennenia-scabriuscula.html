Sennenia scabriuscula

Sennenia scabriuscula, commonly known as the roughleaf senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant has a woody stem and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain seeds. It is often found in savannas and woodland areas, thriving in well-drained soils.
