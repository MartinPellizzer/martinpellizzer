Sedum subtile

Sedum subtile, commonly known as the slender stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to arid and rocky environments.

It features fleshy, linear leaves that help it store water, adapting to its harsh habitat. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the summer months. It is often found growing in crevices or on rocky slopes, thriving in full sun.

Sedum subtile is a hardy, low-maintenance plant popular in xeriscaping and rock gardens.
