Sloanea robusta

Sloanea robusta, commonly known as the robust sloanea, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, dark bark and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by orange-red, fleshy fruits. Sloanea robusta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm climates for its aesthetic qualities.

Its wood is also used in local crafts and construction due to its durability.
