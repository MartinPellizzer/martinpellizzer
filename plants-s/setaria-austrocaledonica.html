Setaria austrocaledonica

Setaria austrocaledonica, commonly known as the African silver grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-gray leaves and compact, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are often tinged with green or brown. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Its ornamental value is appreciated in gardens for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
