Scilla Ensifolia

Scilla ensifolia is a striking bulbous plant known for its vibrant blue or purple flowers. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in well-drained soils and sunny to partially shaded areas. This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes. Its slender leaves and delicate blossoms make it a popular choice among horticultural enthusiasts. Scilla ensifolia is also valued for its ability to naturalize and return year after year.
