Stevia karwinskyana

Stevia karwinskyana, commonly known as Karwinsky's stevia, is a perennial herb native to the Andean region of South America.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and small yellow flowers. The plant is notable for containing steviol glycosides, natural sweeteners that are significantly sweeter than table sugar. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its medicinal and culinary potential, it has gained interest in herbal and organic gardening circles.
