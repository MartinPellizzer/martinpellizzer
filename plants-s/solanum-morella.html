Solanum morella

Solanum morella, commonly known as the Morella nightshade, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in umbels. Its fruits are berries that mature to a dark purple color, containing seeds inside.

Botanically, it is characterized by its hairy stems and alternate leaves with serrated edges.
