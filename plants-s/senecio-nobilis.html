Senecio nobilis

Senecio nobilis, commonly known as the noble groundsels, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its bright yellow flowers bloom in late summer and early autumn, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Senecio nobilis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
