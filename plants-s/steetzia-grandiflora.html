Steetzia grandiflora

Steetzia grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered steetzia, is a species of flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Mexico.

The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters, with dark green, narrow leaves. Its flowers, which bloom in the spring, consist of both ray and disk florets, producing a striking yellow-orange color.

Steetzia grandiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and resilience.
