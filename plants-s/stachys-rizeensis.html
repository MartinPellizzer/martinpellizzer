Stachys rizeensis

Stachys rizeensis, commonly known as Rize hedge-nettle, is a perennial herb native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and clusters of small, pinkish-purple flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
