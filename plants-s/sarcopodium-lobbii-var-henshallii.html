Sarcopodium lobbii var. henshallii

Sarcopodium lobbii var. henshallii, commonly known as the Henshall's snake plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves that are often marked with silver or white stripes. The plant is known for its ability to store water in its leaves, making it drought-tolerant. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers on tall, slender stalks under suitable conditions.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ease of care in indoor gardening.
