Swertia Swertopsis

Swertia swertopsis is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas. It belongs to the gentian family, known for its striking blue or purple flowers. This species thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas. Due to its limited habitat and threats from climate change, it is considered endangered. Its beauty and ecological significance make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
