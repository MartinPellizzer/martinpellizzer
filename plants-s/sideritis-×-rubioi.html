Sideritis × rubioi

Sideritis × rubioi, commonly known as the hybrid sainfoin, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. The plant is characterized by its slender stems, small opposite leaves, and clusters of pink or purple flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Sideritis × rubioi is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
