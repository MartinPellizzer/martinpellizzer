Solanecio kanzibiensis

Solanecio kanzibiensis, commonly known as the Kanzibi endemic sunflower, is a flowering plant native to the island of Unguja in Zanzibar.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white to pale blue petals. The plant typically grows in coastal and sandy soils, thriving in the tropical climate of its native habitat.

Solanecio kanzibiensis is noted for its ornamental value and is often found in dry, open habitats along the coastline.
