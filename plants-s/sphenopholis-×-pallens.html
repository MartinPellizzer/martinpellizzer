Sphenopholis × pallens

Sphenopholis × pallens, commonly known as the hybrid sphenophyllum, is a rare fern that exhibits characteristics of both its parent species.

It is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features lance-shaped, serrated fronds that are often a deep green in color. Its reproductive structures, including sporangia, are clustered on the underside of the fronds.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
