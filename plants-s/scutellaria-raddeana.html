Scutellaria raddeana

Scutellaria raddeana, commonly known as blue skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular blue or purple flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Scutellaria raddeana is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
