Schizostachyum pingbianense

Schizostachyum pingbianense, commonly known as the Pingbian bamboo, is a tropical grass species native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, grass-like leaves. This plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in lowland areas with well-drained soil. Schizostachyum pingbianense is valued for its strong, durable culms, which are used in construction and crafts.

Its rapid growth rate makes it an important resource in sustainable forestry and reforestation projects.
