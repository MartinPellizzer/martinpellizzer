Solenostemon gracilifolius

Solenostemon gracilifolius, commonly known as the slender-leaf threadleaf, is a small herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. The plant features elongated, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a delicate appearance. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually in spikes, and are typically pale green or white.

Solenostemon gracilifolius is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
