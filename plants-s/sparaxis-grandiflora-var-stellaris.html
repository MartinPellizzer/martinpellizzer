Sparaxis grandiflora var. stellaris

Sparaxis grandiflora var. stellaris, commonly known as the star sparaxis, is a bulbous perennial native to South Africa.

It features striking, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow, often with a distinctive orange or red center. The plant produces sword-like leaves that are narrow and grassy in appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its blooming period typically occurs in late spring to early summer, adding a dramatic floral display to gardens.
