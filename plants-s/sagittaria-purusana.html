Sagittaria purusana

Sagittaria purusana, commonly known as arrowhead plant, is a herbaceous aquatic plant native to South America.

It features arrow-shaped leaves that float on the water's surface, with roots submerged in the mud. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom above the water, typically in late summer. It thrives in shallow, still waters and is often found in wetlands and marshes.

Sagittaria purusana plays a vital role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various aquatic organisms.
