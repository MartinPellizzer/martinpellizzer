Swertia jiendeensis

Swertia jiendeensis, commonly known as the Jiende gentian, is a flowering plant native to the Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a smooth texture, while its stems are upright and branched.

Swertia jiendeensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
