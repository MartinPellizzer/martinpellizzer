Spiraeanthemum pulleanum

Spiraeanthemum pulleanum, commonly known as the white-flowered spirea, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features dense clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in early spring, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant has alternate, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and slightly hairy. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for temperate climates.

Its compact growth habit and showy flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
