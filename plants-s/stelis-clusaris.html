Stelis clusaris

Stelis clusaris, commonly known as the orchid spike orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia, Malaysia, and the Philippines. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic, often on trees in tropical rainforests. Its flowers are typically small and clustered in spikes, with a variety of colors ranging from white to pink and purple.

Stelis clusaris is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique and delicate floral display.
