Stevia pulcherrima

Stevia pulcherrima, commonly known as the flame flower or red stevia, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red, orange, and yellow tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is known for its aromatic foliage and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Stevia pulcherrima is also valued for its potential medicinal properties, though it is not as widely used for its sweet compounds as other stevia species.
