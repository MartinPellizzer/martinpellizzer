Scutellaria parvula var. australis

Scutellaria parvula var. australis, commonly known as the Australian skullcap, is a small herbaceous plant native to Australia.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, tubular flowers that are usually blue or purple in color. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in regions with a Mediterranean climate.

The species is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
