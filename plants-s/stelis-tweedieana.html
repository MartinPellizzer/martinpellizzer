Stelis tweedieana

Stelis tweedieana, commonly known as the Tweedie's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small epiphytic plant that typically grows on tree trunks or branches in humid, tropical environments. The plant features a slender stem with a few leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Stelis tweedieana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
