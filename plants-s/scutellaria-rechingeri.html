Scutellaria rechingeri

Scutellaria rechingeri, commonly known as the Rechinger's skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the high-altitude regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a hairy texture.

Scutellaria rechingeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
