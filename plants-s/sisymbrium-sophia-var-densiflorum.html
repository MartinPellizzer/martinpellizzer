Sisymbrium sophia var. densiflorum

Sisymbrium sophia var. densiflorum, commonly known as the denseflower sweet rocket, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, branching stems and pinnately divided leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and ecological landscapes.
