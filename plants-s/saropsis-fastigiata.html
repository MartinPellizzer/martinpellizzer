Saropsis fastigiata

Saropsis fastigiata, commonly known as the upright saropsis, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It features narrow, upright growth with small, leathery leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that appear in clusters during the warmer months. Its woody stems are characterized by a smooth, greyish bark.

Saropsis fastigiata is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient plant in harsh conditions.
