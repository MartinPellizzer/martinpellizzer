Spiraea kiusiana

Spiraea kiusiana, commonly known as the Japanese spiraea, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters of white or pale pink in early spring. The plant has slender, arching stems and finely cut, serrated leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

Spiraea kiusiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
