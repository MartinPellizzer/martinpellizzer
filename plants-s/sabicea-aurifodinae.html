Sabicea aurifodinae

Sabicea aurifodinae, commonly known as goldenleaf sabicea, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its striking golden-yellow foliage, which gives it its common name. It produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pale yellow to greenish-white in color.

Sabicea aurifodinae is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
