Schotia rubriflora

Schotia rubriflora, commonly known as the red-flowered glory bush, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves and clusters of striking red flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens. Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant flowers and compact growth habit.

Schotia rubriflora is also valued for its ability to attract birds and butterflies.
