Stachys aspera subsp. chinensis

Stachys aspera subsp.

chinensis, commonly known as Chinese hedge nettle, is a perennial herb native to East Asia. It is characterized by its hairy, erect stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves.

The plant produces small, pinkish to purple flowers in whorled clusters, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in the Lamiaceae family.
