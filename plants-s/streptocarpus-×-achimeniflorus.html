Streptocarpus × achimeniflorus

Streptocarpus × achimeniflorus, commonly known as the hybrid African violet, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Streptocarpus normanii and Streptocarpus hirtellus.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South Africa, characterized by its thick, velvety leaves and showy, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant typically grows to a height of 10–30 cm, with a compact rosette structure that makes it well-suited for indoor cultivation.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
