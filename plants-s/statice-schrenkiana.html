Statice schrenkiana

Statice schrenkiana, commonly known as the desert statice, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces showy, spike-like flower heads in shades of pink, red, and purple. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is well adapted to dry conditions, with deep roots that help it survive in sandy soils. It is often used in ornamental gardening for its drought tolerance and vibrant blooms.

Statice schrenkiana is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
