Senecio chrysanthemum var. chrysanthemum

Senecio chrysanthemum var. chrysanthemum, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and thistles. The plant features upright, branching stems and produces clusters of small, bright yellow to orange flowers. Its leaves are typically narrow, linear, and covered in a fine, white powdery coating.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant blooms and drought tolerance.
