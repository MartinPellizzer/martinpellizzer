Solanum citrullifolium var. citrullifolium

Solanum citrullifolium var. citrullifolium, commonly known as the yellow leaf nightshade, is a member of the Solanaceae family.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its yellowish-green leaves and small, bell-shaped flowers. The plant produces berries that are typically yellow or orange when mature. It is native to parts of South America and thrives in warm, humid climates.

Due to its toxic compounds, it is often considered a weed in agricultural settings.
