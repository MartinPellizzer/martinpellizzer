Silene cucubalus proles stenocalyx

Silene cucubalus proles stenocalyx, commonly known as the narrow calyx silene, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or rocky habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections.
