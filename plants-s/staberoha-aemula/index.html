Staberoha Aemula

Staberoha aemula is a rare and enigmatic plant species found in remote, high-altitude regions. It thrives in nutrient-poor soils and requires specific microclimatic conditions to flourish. Due to its elusive nature, little is known about its full biological makeup and ecological role.
