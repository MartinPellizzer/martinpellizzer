Stelis tricuspis

Stelis tricuspis, commonly known as the three-lobed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, three-lobed flowers that often display vivid colors. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Stelis tricuspis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
