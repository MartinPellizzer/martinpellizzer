Sarcocapnos crassifolia var. stenoceras

Sarcocapnos crassifolia var. stenoceras, commonly known as the narrow-capsuled sarcocapnos, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, tubular flowers that bloom in spring. The leaves are typically narrow and leathery, adapted to the arid conditions of its native habitat. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
