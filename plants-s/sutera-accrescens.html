Sutera accrescens

Sutera accrescens, commonly known as the evergreen spongeplant, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and small, fragrant white flowers. The plant thrives in coastal areas and is adapted to salty air and strong winds. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, helping it retain moisture in arid conditions.

Sutera accrescens is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardiness.
