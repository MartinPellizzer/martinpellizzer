Silene gangotriana

Silene gangotriana, commonly known as the Gangotri silene, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Indian Himalayas.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, often at elevations above 3,000 meters. The plant features simple, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, pink or white flowers arranged in a raceme. It is adapted to cold climates and has a short growing season.

Silene gangotriana is of interest to botanists due to its unique ecological niche and limited distribution.
