Siler zernyi

Siler zernyi, commonly known as narrowleaf siler, is a flowering plant native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features slender, upright stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. Its small, clustered flowers are typically pink or purple, blooming in summer.
