Selaginella carinata

Selaginella carinata, commonly known as the zigzag spike moss, is a species of spore-bearing plant belonging to the family Selaginellaceae.

It is a small, evergreen plant that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has a distinctive appearance with its segmented, leafy stems and small, scale-like leaves arranged in a zigzag pattern. It produces microspores and megaspores on separate structures, which are essential for its reproductive cycle.

Selaginella carinata is notable for its ability to survive in arid conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitats.
