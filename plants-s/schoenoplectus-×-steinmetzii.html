Schoenoplectus × steinmetzii

Schoenoplectus × steinmetzii, commonly known as the hybrid bulrush, is a wetland plant that thrives in shallow water and moist soils.

It is a sterile hybrid between Schoenoplectus mucronatus and Schoenoplectus pungens, resulting in unique morphological characteristics. The plant has tall, cylindrical stems with narrow leaves that are often covered in a fine, white bloom. Its flowers are small and clustered in spike-like inflorescences, typically appearing in late summer.

Schoenoplectus × steinmetzii plays an important role in stabilizing wetland ecosystems and providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
