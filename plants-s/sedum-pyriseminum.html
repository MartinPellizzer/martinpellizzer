Sedum pyriseminum

Sedum pyriseminum, commonly known as the rock sedum, is a succulent plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow or orange. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous areas. Its leaves are typically blue-green in color and are arranged in a compact, cushion-like growth form.

Sedum pyriseminum is drought-tolerant and is often used in rock gardens or as a ground cover in xeriscaping.
