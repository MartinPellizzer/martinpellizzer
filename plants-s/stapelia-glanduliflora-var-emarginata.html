Stapelia glanduliflora var. emarginata

Stapelia glanduliflora var. emarginata, commonly known as the margined stapelia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, ribbed stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces striking, waxy flowers that are typically yellow or orange with a strong, unpleasant odor. Its leaves are usually small and fleshy, and it blooms during the summer months.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
