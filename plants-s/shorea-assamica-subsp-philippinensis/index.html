Shorea Assamica Subsp. Philippinensis

Shorea assamica subsp. philippinensis is a rare and unique species of dipterocarp tree native to the Philippines. It belongs to the family Dipterocarpaceae and is known for its valuable timber and ecological importance. This subspecies is characterized by its distinct morphology and habitat preferences, often found in lowland and montane forests. Due to deforestation and habitat loss, it faces significant conservation challenges. Studying this plant provides insight into the biodiversity and ecological dynamics of Philippine forests.
