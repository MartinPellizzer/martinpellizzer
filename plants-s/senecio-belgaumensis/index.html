Senecio Belgaumensis

Senecio belgaumensis is a rare and unique flowering plant native to the western ghats of india. it belongs to the asteraceae family and is known for its striking yellow flowers and succulent leaves. this plant is particularly adapted to arid and rocky environments, making it a resilient species. due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered endangered. conservation efforts are crucial to protect this botanical gem from extinction.
