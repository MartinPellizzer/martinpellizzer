Silene ligulata

Silene ligulata, commonly known as the narrowleaf catchfly, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. Its leaves are narrow and linear, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Silene ligulata is often found in the western United States and parts of Canada.
