Souroubea aubletii

Souroubea aubletii, commonly known as the African star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that produces striking blue or violet star-shaped flowers during the summer months. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Souroubea aubletii is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging conditions.
