Sedum linearifolium

Sedum linearifolium, commonly known as the linear-leaf stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to arid and rocky environments.

It features fleshy, linear leaves that help it store water, adapting to drought conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its resilience and low maintenance make it an ideal plant for xeriscaping and desert landscapes.
