Stylosanthes floridana

Stylosanthes floridana, commonly known as Florida stylo, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a legume, belonging to the family Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant is often found in sandy soils and can thrive in coastal areas due to its salt tolerance.
