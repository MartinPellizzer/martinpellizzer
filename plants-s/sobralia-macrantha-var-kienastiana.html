Sobralia macrantha var. kienastiana

Sobralia macrantha var. kienastiana, commonly known as the Kienast's sobralia, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its showy, pendulous inflorescences. The flowers are typically vibrant in color, with a unique structure that features a prominent lip or labellum. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in warm, humid environments.

It requires well-draining soil and bright, indirect light to thrive.
