Soroseris

Soroseris, commonly known as the slender milkweed, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to various regions of Africa, particularly in the southern and eastern parts. The plants typically have hairy stems and produce small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters. They are often found in grasslands and open woodlands, thriving in well-drained soils.
