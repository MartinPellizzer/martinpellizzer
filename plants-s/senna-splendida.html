Senna splendida

Senna splendida, commonly known as the golden senna, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are clustered in racemes and bloom during the dry season. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with small, ovate leaflets, and its bark is smooth and often fissured. Senna splendida is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in challenging climates.

It is also valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
