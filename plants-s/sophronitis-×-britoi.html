Sophronitis × britoi

Sophronitis × britoi, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a naturally occurring cross between Sophronitis coccinea and Sophronitis orchidiflora.

It is a sympodial orchid with a compact growth habit and produces strikingly colorful flowers. The plant typically features showy, spurred blossoms that come in shades of red, pink, and white. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained, loose soil.

Sophronitis × britoi is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
