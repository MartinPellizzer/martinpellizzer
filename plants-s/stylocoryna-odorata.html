Stylocoryna odorata

Stylocoryna odorata, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are often white or pale pink in color. Its roots are fibrous and help it anchor in the soil while absorbing nutrients.

Stylocoryna odorata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its aromatic properties.
