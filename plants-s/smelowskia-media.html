Smelowskia media

Smelowskia media, commonly known as the blue-eyed grass, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It features delicate, grass-like foliage and produces showy, blue-violet flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows to a height of 6 to 12 inches and prefers moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks.

Smelowskia media is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
