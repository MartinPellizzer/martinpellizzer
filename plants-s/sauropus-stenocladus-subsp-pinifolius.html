Sauropus stenocladus subsp. pinifolius

Sauropus stenocladus subsp.

pinifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sauropus, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its small, needle-like leaves that are arranged in a dense, compact manner.

The plant typically grows in coastal or swampy areas, thriving in humid and saline conditions. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

The species is also valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and adaptability to various soil types.
