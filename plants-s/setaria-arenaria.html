Setaria arenaria

Setaria arenaria, commonly known as sand dropseed, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open, disturbed areas. The plant has slender, erect stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green color.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize sandy soils.
