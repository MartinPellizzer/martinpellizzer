Streptocarpus × watsonii

Streptocarpus × watsonii, commonly known as the hybrid streptocarpus, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Streptocarpus section Streptocarpus and Streptocarpus section Rhombifolius. This plant typically produces vibrant, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, often with contrasting markings. It is native to regions of southern Africa, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated as a houseplant.
