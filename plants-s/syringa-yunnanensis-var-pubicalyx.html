Syringa yunnanensis var. pubicalyx

Syringa yunnanensis var. pubicalyx, commonly known as the Yunnan lilac, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has narrow, elliptical leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for gardens in temperate climates.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its showy blooms and its ability to attract pollinators.
