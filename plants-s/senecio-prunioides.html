Senecio prunioides

Senecio prunioides, commonly known as the orange tree dandelion, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that resemble dandelions. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits containing seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Senecio prunioides is also known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
