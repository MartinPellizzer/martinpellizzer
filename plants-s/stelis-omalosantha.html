Stelis omalosantha

Stelis omalosantha, commonly known as the Omalosantha orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or branches, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are usually dark green and glossy, and the flowers are often white or pale pink in color.

Stelis omalosantha is appreciated for its ornamental value and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and unique appearance.
