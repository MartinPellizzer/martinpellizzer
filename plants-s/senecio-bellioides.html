Senecio bellioides

Senecio bellioides, commonly known as the bell-shaped senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, bell-shaped yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Senecio bellioides is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and drought tolerance.
