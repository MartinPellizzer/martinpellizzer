Strelitzia reginae subsp. juncea

Strelitzia reginae subsp.

juncea, commonly known as the white-veined bird of paradise, is a striking tropical plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its large, sword-shaped leaves that are dark green with prominent white veins.

The plant produces vibrant orange and blue flowers that resemble a bird in flight, making it a popular ornamental species. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires plenty of sunlight to flourish.

This subspecies is often cultivated for its unique appearance and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
