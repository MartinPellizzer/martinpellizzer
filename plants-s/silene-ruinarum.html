Silene ruinarum

Silene ruinarum, commonly known as the ruins campion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or arid environments. The plant produces showy, pink or red flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Silene ruinarum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
