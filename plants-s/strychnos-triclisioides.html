Strychnos triclisioides

Strychnos triclisioides, commonly known as the African strychnos, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Loganiaceae and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain toxic seeds, which contain the alkaloids strychnine and brucine.
