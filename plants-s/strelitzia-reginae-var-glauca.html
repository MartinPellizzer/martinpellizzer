Strelitzia reginae var. glauca

Strelitzia reginae var. glauca, commonly known as the Blue Strelitzia, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, banana-like leaves that are a deep blue-green color, with a waxy texture and prominent veins. The plant produces vibrant orange flowers that are surrounded by large, boat-shaped blue-purple bracts, creating a dramatic visual display. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its exotic appearance and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
