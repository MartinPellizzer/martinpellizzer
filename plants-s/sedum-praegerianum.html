Sedum praegerianum

Sedum praegerianum, commonly known as the Cape Town sedum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, adapted to store water and thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It is typically found in rocky or gravelly soils and is drought-tolerant.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
