Sinobambusa acutiligulata

Sinobambusa acutiligulata, commonly known as the sharp-leafed bamboo, is a species of flowering bamboo native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with a pale green color. This plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as mountain forests and stream sides. Its culms are strong and flexible, making it suitable for traditional crafts and construction.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in its native habitat.
