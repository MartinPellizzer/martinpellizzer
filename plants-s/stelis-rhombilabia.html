Stelis rhombilabia

Stelis rhombilabia, commonly known as the rhomboid-labiate stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of white, woolly hair. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, with a distinctive rhomboidal labellum. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Stelis rhombilabia is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
