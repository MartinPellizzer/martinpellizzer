Senecio stenoglossus

Senecio stenoglossus, commonly known as the narrow-lipped groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are yellow with a distinctive central disc.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
