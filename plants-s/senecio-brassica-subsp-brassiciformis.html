Senecio brassica subsp. brassiciformis

Senecio brassica subsp.

brassiciformis, commonly known as the African Daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow daisy-like flowers and silver-green foliage.

The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
