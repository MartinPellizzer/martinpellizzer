Swintonia helferi

Swintonia helferi, commonly known as the Helferi's spider plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of small, narrow leaves that give it a bushy appearance, often with a dark green to bluish-green hue. The plant produces delicate, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale pink, typically blooming in late spring or early summer. It is well-suited for container gardening and can thrive in partial shade to full sun, depending on the climate.

Swintonia helferi is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
