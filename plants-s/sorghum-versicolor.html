Sorghum versicolor

Sorghum versicolor, commonly known as the variegated sorghum, is a strikingly colorful grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, multicolored foliage, which can range from yellow and red to green and purple. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in warm, sunny climates. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and landscape design.

Sorghum versicolor is also valued for its resilience to drought and poor soil conditions.
