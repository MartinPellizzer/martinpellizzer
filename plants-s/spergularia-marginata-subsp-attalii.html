Spergularia marginata subsp. attalii

Spergularia marginata subsp.

attalii, commonly known as the coastal sandspurry, is a flowering plant native to sandy coastal areas of the Mediterranean. It is a low-growing perennial herb with small, pinkish to white flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant has fleshy, succulent leaves that help it retain moisture in arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dunes or on rocky shores.
