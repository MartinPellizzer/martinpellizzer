Sideritis tunetana

Sideritis tunetana, commonly known as the Tunisian ironwort, is a flowering plant native to North Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, pale blue or violet flowers. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Sideritis tunetana is often found in rocky or sandy soils, thriving in dry and sunny environments.
