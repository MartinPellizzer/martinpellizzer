Schismatoglottis minor

Schismatoglottis minor, commonly known as the small schismatoglottis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial herb with narrow, strap-like leaves that form a rosette. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Schismatoglottis minor is often cultivated for its ornamental foliage rather than its flowers.
