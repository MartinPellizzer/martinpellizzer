Statice bollei

Statice bollei, commonly known as Bolle's sea thrift, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and salt-tolerant nature. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, which bloom throughout the summer months. Its fibrous root system helps stabilize sandy or coastal soils, making it a valuable plant for erosion control.

Statice bollei thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in dunes and rocky shores.
