Sinobambusa yixingensis

Sinobambusa yixingensis, commonly known as the Yixing bamboo, is a rare and unique species native to China.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, elongated culms that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant exhibits a distinct growth habit with a clumping or spreading form, depending on environmental conditions. Its leaves are narrow and linear, with a smooth surface and a slightly hairy underside.

This bamboo is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
