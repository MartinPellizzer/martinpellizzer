Sobralia granitica

Sobralia granitica, commonly known as the granite sobralia, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has a compact growth habit with thick, fleshy leaves that help it store water in its natural habitat. Its flowers are borne on branching inflorescences, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Sobralia granitica is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
