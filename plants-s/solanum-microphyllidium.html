Solanum microphyllidium

Solanum microphyllidium, commonly known as the small-leaved nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the evening, attracting nocturnal pollinators. Its leaves are small and often covered in a fine, white pubescence.

The berries it bears are toxic and should not be consumed by humans or animals.
