Stachys recta var. glabrescens

Stachys recta var. glabrescens, commonly known as narrow-leaved betony, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features upright, square stems and lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs, though the variety glabrescens has a smoother texture. The plant produces spikes of violet to purple flowers, which bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This species is often used in herbal medicine and as an ornamental plant in gardens.
