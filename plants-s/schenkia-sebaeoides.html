Schenkia sebaeoides

Schenkia sebaeoides, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant typically grows as a compact shrub, with leaves arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Schenkia sebaeoides is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
