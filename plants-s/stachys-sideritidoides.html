Stachys sideritidoides

Stachys sideritidoides, commonly known as the ironroot hedge-nettle, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features hairy, opposite leaves and produces small, two-lipped flowers that are typically purple or pink in color. It thrives in rocky or dry soils and is often found in alpine environments.

Stachys sideritidoides is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
