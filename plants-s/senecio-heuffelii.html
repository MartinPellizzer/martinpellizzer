Senecio heuffelii

Senecio heuffelii, commonly known as the African snowflake, is a perennial plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its showy inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance and hardiness.
