Sorbus prattii var. tatsienensis

Sorbus prattii var. tatsienensis, commonly known as the Tatsienlung rowan, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in mountainous regions with well-drained soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation.
