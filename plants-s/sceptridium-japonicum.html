Sceptridium japonicum

Sceptridium japonicum, commonly known as the Japanese umbrella fern, is a species of fern native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, umbrella-like fronds that are typically light green with a leathery texture. The plant has a short, thick rhizome that supports its upright growth habit. It prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Sceptridium japonicum is valued for its ornamental appearance and is commonly cultivated as a houseplant.
