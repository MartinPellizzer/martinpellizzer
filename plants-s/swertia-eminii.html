Swertia eminii

Swertia eminii, commonly known as the Himalayan gentian, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, thriving at elevations between 2,000 and 4,000 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered with a fine layer of white pubescence.

Swertia eminii is known for its medicinal properties, traditionally used in herbal remedies for digestive and respiratory ailments.
