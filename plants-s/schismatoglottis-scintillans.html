Schismatoglottis scintillans

Schismatoglottis scintillans, commonly known as the sparkling schismatoglottis, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

Its leaves are typically broad and glossy, with a waxy texture that helps it retain moisture in its natural habitat.
