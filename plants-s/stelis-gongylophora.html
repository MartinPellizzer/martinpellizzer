Stelis gongylophora

Stelis gongylophora, commonly known as the orchid with the striking white and green flowers, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features a sympodial growth pattern, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually arranged in a raceme and are characterized by their intricate, often fragrant structure.

Stelis gongylophora thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
