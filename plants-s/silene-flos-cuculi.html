Silene flos-cuculi

Silene flos-cuculi, commonly known as the cuckoo flower, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its striking pink or purple flowers that bloom in early summer. Silene flos-cuculi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
