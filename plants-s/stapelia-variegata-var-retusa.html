Stapelia variegata var. retusa

Stapelia variegata var. retusa, commonly known as the Variegated Stapelia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems that are often green with distinct yellow or white markings. The plant produces striking, lantern-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It is a slow-growing species that thrives in well-draining soil and bright, indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
