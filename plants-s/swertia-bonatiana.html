Swertia bonatiana

Swertia bonatiana, commonly known as the Himalayan gentian, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky and alpine environments. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces striking blue to purple flowers. It belongs to the family Gentianaceae, which is known for its showy and often blue or violet flowers.

Swertia bonatiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in folk medicine.
