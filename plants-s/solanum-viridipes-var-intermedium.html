Solanum viridipes var. intermedium

Solanum viridipes var. intermedium, commonly known as the intermediate green nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its upright growth habit and hairy stems. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by round, yellow berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or shrubland habitats.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
