Scutellaria multicaulis var. gandomanensis

Scutellaria multicaulis var. gandomanensis, commonly known as the Gandomanensis skullcap, is a perennial herb native to specific regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple, arranged in whorls along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Its medicinal properties have been of interest in traditional herbal practices.
