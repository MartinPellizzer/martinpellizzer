Satureja baborensis

Satureja baborensis, commonly known as mountain savory, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments. Its small, tubular flowers are usually pink to purple in color, and it produces small, aromatic seeds.

Satureja baborensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
