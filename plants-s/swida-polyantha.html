Swida polyantha

Swida polyantha, commonly known as the Chinese snowbell, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, bell-shaped white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are typically ovate to elliptical in shape. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Swida polyantha is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and adaptability to various garden conditions.
