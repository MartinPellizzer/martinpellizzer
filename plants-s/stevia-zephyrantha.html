Stevia zephyrantha

Stevia zephyrantha, commonly known as the cloud stevia, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant is notable for its high concentration of steviol glycosides, which are natural sweeteners. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value and potential health benefits, it is increasingly cultivated in gardens and research settings.
