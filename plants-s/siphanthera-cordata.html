Siphanthera cordata

Siphanthera cordata, commonly known as the heartleaf piper, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a member of the Piperaceae family, which includes other species known for their aromatic properties. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences.
