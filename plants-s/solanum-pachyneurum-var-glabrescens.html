Solanum pachyneurum var. glabrescens

Solanum pachyneurum var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smoothleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its smooth, glabrous leaves and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers. The plant produces berries that are typically yellow or orange when ripe, and it is often found in dry, open habitats. It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers.

Due to its toxic properties, it is not typically cultivated for agricultural purposes.
