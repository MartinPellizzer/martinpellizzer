Sarcochilus brevirhachis

Sarcochilus brevirhachis, commonly known as the short-stemmed helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It features a rhizomatous growth habit with a short, thick stem and a single, lance-shaped leaf. The plant produces a single, showy greenish-yellow flower with a distinctive helmet-shaped lip. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
