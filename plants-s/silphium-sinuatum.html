Silphium sinuatum

Silphium sinuatum, commonly known as the zigzag goldenroot, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its distinctive zigzagging stems and bright yellow flowers. The plant thrives in moist, sunny habitats such as wetlands and stream banks. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often have a rough texture.

Silphium sinuatum is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally for its purported health benefits.
