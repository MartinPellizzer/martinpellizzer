Serrastylis modesta

Serrastylis modesta, commonly known as the modest lady's tears, is a species of orchid native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its delicate, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in rainforest or woodland areas. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Serrastylis modesta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with appropriate conditions.
