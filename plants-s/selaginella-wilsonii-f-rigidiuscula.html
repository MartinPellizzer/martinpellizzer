Selaginella wilsonii f. rigidiuscula

Selaginella wilsonii f. rigidiuscula, commonly known as the rigid-stemmed spikemoss, is a member of the Selaginellaceae family.

It is a small, evergreen fern ally characterized by its closely packed, needle-like leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its stems are short and robust, with a dense arrangement of microphylls.

This variety is noted for its compact growth habit and is often found in subtropical and tropical regions.
