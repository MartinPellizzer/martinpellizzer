Schizoglossum biflorum var. gwelense

Schizoglossum biflorum var. gwelense, commonly known as the Gwelens mountain bellflower, is a rare and endemic plant species found in specific alpine regions.

The plant has a compact growth habit with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence.
