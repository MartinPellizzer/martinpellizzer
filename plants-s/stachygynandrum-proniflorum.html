Stachygynandrum proniflorum

Stachygynandrum proniflorum, commonly known as the pink-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking pink stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers with white petals and a distinctive pink lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some regions for horticultural purposes.
