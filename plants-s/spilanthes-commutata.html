Spilanthes commutata

Spilanthes commutata, commonly known as the narrowleaf pippeweed, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other mustard plants. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer to early fall.
