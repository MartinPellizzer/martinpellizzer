Senecio sandersianus

Senecio sandersianus, commonly known as the sandersian sunflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its yellow flowers are typically arranged in a single head, giving it a sunflower-like appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.
