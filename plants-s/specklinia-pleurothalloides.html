Specklinia pleurothalloides

Specklinia pleurothalloides, commonly known as the pleurothalloides orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and the presence of pseudobulbs, which store water and nutrients. The plant produces small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellowish, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

Specklinia pleurothalloides thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining potting media.
