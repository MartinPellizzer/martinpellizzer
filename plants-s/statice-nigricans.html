Statice nigricans

Statice nigricans, commonly known as black statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, spike-like inflorescence composed of small, dark purple to black flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, often found in coastal areas and sandy environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and the flowers are typically arranged in dense clusters.

Statice nigricans is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
