Sedum venustum

Sedum venustum, commonly known as the showy sedum, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the family Crassulaceae and is characterized by its fleshy, succulent leaves and vibrant, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in prairies, open woodlands, and along roadsides.
