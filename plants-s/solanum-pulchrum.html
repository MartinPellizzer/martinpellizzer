Solanum pulchrum

Solanum pulchrum, commonly known as the beautiful nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant is characterized by its vibrant purple flowers and hairy stems. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments and can reach heights of up to 2 meters.

Solanum pulchrum is also noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
