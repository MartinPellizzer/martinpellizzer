Selaginella zartmanii

The plant has a distinctive appearance with its whorled leaves and spore-bearing structures called strobila. It is characterized by its ability to survive in low-light conditions and its unique reproductive system involving both spores and seeds.

Selaginella zartmanii plays a role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for small invertebrates in its native ecosystem.
