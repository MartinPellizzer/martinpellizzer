Sphaeroma bonariense

Sphaeroma bonariense, commonly known as the Brazilian velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its botanical features make it an attractive ornamental plant, though it can be invasive in certain regions.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.
