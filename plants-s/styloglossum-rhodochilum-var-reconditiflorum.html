Styloglossum rhodochilum var. reconditiflorum

Styloglossum rhodochilum var. reconditiflorum, commonly known as the hidden-flower orchid, is a rare and delicate orchid species native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It is characterized by its compact, hairy stems and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant thrives in cool, shaded environments with high humidity, typically found in forest understories. Its unique floral structure and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is considered endangered and requires careful protection.
