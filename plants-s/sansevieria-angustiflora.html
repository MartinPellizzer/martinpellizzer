Sansevieria angustiflora

Sansevieria angustiflora, commonly known as the Snake Plant, is a hardy, evergreen perennial native to Africa.

It features tall, upright leaves that are typically dark green with light green stripes, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it an ideal choice for indoor environments. Its air-purifying qualities have also made it popular in homes and offices.

Sansevieria angustiflora is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is valued for its ornamental beauty and low maintenance requirements.
