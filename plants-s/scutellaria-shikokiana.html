Scutellaria shikokiana

Scutellaria shikokiana, commonly known as the Shikoku skullcap, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Scutellaria shikokiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
