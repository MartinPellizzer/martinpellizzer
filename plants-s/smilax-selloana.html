Smilax selloana

Smilax selloana, commonly known as the red-berried smilax, is a species of flowering plant in the family Smilacaceae.

It is a climbing shrub characterized by its thorny stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into bright red berries. Native to parts of Central and South America, it thrives in humid, tropical environments.

Smilax selloana is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive foliage and berries.
