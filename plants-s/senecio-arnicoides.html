Senecio arnicoides

Senecio arnicoides, commonly known as the African Daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features bright yellow ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers, giving it a daisy-like appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or sandy environments.

Senecio arnicoides is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
