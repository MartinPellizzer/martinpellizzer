Sonchus tenerrimus var. dianae

Sonchus tenerrimus var. dianae, commonly known as the slender sickleweed, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are lanceolate and often covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in a cluster.

It is a perennial herb that can reach heights of up to 60 centimeters.
