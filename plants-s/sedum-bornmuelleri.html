Sedum bornmuelleri

Sedum bornmuelleri, commonly known as the Bornmueller's stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it an ideal plant for xeriscaping.
