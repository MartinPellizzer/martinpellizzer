Symphytum sylvaticum var. hordokopii

Symphytum sylvaticum var. hordokopii, commonly known as the Hordokopii comfrey, is a variant of the comfrey plant native to Central Europe.

The plant belongs to the Boraginaceae family and is known for its thick, succulent stems and roots.
