Spartium japonicum

Spartium japonicum, commonly known as Japanese needlegrass, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant features slender, erect stems and small, spike-like clusters of blue to purple flowers. Its leaves are narrow and linear, and it produces small, three-angled seeds.

Spartium japonicum is often used in traditional medicine and is also valued for its ornamental qualities.
