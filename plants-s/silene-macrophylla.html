Silene macrophylla

Silene macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaved catchfly, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has large, broad leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

Silene macrophylla is a perennial species that is valued for its ornamental qualities in gardens.
