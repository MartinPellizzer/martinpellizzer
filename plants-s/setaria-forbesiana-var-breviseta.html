Setaria forbesiana var. breviseta

Setaria forbesiana var. breviseta, commonly known as the narrowsettu grass, is a perennial grass native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny grains of seeds. It thrives in moist to wet environments, often found in marshy areas or along waterways.

Due to its adaptability, it is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects to stabilize soil and prevent erosion.
