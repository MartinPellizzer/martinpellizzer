Senecio chilensis var. chilensis

Senecio chilensis var. chilensis, commonly known as the Chilean ragged robin, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Chile.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or coastal environments. The plant features slender, branched stems and produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
