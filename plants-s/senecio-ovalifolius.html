Senecio ovalifolius

Senecio ovalifolius, commonly known as the oval-leaved groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, wind-dispersed seeds.

Senecio ovalifolius is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
