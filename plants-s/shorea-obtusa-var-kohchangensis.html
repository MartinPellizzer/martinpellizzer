Shorea obtusa var. kohchangensis

It is native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand. The tree is characterized by its dense, heavy timber, which is highly valued for its strength and durability. It typically grows in lowland and hill forest areas, reaching heights of up to 40 meters.
