Silene swertiifolia var. stenophylla

Silene swertiifolia var. stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf catchfly, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or white. The leaves are narrow and linear, often covered with fine hairs, giving the plant a delicate appearance. Native to regions of Asia, including China and the Himalayas, it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

This variant is distinguished by its narrower leaves compared to the typical form of Silene swertiifolia.
