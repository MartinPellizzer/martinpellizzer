Senna urmenetae

Senna urmenetae, commonly known as the yellow senna, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for dry climates.

Senna urmenetae is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
