Solanum preussii

Solanum preussii, commonly known as the Preuss's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, with smooth, often hairy stems and alternate leaves. It produces small, fleshy berries that are yellow when ripe, containing numerous seeds.

Solanum preussii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
