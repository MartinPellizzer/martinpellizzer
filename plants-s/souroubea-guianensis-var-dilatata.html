Souroubea guianensis var. dilatata

Souroubea guianensis var. dilatata, commonly known as the broadleaf souroubea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in cyathia, a unique floral structure typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. Its bark and leaves contain various chemical compounds, making it of interest in traditional medicine.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
