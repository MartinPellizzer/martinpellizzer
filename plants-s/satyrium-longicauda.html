Satyrium longicauda

Satyrium longicauda, commonly known as the long-tailed orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stem and its distinctive, elongated floral structure. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
