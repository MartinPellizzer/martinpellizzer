Seriphidium ifranensis

Seriphidium ifranensis, commonly known as the Moroccan tansy, is a flowering plant native to North Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, yellowish-green foliage. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

Seriphidium ifranensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
