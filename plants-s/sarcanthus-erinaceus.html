Sarcanthus erinaceus

It is characterized by its distinctive spiny calyx that encloses the small, pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showing adaptations to drought conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.
