Serapias pallens

Serapias pallens, commonly known as the pale orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its delicate, pale yellow to white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and the flowers are arranged in a loose raceme.

Serapias pallens is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to withstand coastal conditions such as salt spray and strong winds.
