Schefflera heteroclada

Schefflera heteroclada, commonly known as the African laceleaf, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, palmately compound leaves that are deeply lobed and resemble a lace-like pattern. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and thrives in humid climates. Its foliage is evergreen, providing a lush, tropical appearance throughout the year.

Schefflera heteroclada is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique leaf structure and adaptability to indoor conditions.
