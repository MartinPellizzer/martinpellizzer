Stenandrium lindenii

Stenandrium lindenii, commonly known as the Lindenberg's stenandrium, is a rare and striking orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, colorful flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in the understory of dense rainforests. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, and the flowers feature intricate patterns of spots and stripes.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
