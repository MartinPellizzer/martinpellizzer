Strongylocaryum

Strongylocaryum, commonly known as the spider plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is characterized by its rosette of fleshy, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with a white stripe down the center. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. After flowering, it develops red or purple berries that contain seeds.

Strongylocaryum is a hardy, low-maintenance plant often used in landscaping and as a houseplant due to its adaptability and aesthetic appeal.
