Stephania andamanica

Stephania andamanica, commonly known as the Andaman stephania, is a climbing plant native to the Andaman Islands.

It belongs to the Menispermaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, yellowish-white flowers. The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that contain seeds enclosed in a fleshy aril. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species in its native habitat.
