Scabiosa atropurpurea f. glabra

Scabiosa atropurpurea f. glabra, commonly known as the smooth scabious, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its rounded, velvety leaves and clusters of pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant is a member of the Caprifoliaceae family and thrives in well-drained soil in full sun. It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and attracts pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
