Schefflera kivuensis

Schefflera kivuensis, commonly known as the Kivu umbrella plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Congo region.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of leaflets, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant has compound leaves with multiple pairs of opposite leaflets, and its flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically greenish-white in color. Schefflera kivuensis thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
