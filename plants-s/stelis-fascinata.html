Stelis fascinata

Stelis fascinata, commonly known as the orchid spike orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on tree branches or rocks in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features a short, erect stem with several clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink in color. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Stelis fascinata thrives in humid environments with bright, indirect light.
