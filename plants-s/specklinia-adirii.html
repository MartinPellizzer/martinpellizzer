Specklinia adirii

Specklinia adirii, commonly known as the Adirii's orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate leaf structure. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming during the wet season. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding substrates.

Specklinia adirii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
