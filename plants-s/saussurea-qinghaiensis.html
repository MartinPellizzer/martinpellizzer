Saussurea qinghaiensis

Saussurea qinghaiensis, commonly known as the Qinghai saussurea, is a flowering plant native to the Qinghai region of China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stem and large, showy composite flowers. The plant thrives in high-altitude environments, often found in alpine meadows and rocky areas. Its roots are valued in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Due to its unique habitat and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
