Sisymbrium tilesii

Sisymbrium tilesii, commonly known as the Turkish mustard or Turkish wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is an annual herb that typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. The plant features small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, and its leaves are pinnately lobed with a slightly hairy texture.
