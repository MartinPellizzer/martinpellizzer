Squilla numidica

Squilla numidica, commonly known as the Algerian squill, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that form a rosette near the ground. The plant produces white or pale blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure.

Squilla numidica is often used in ornamental gardening for its elegant appearance and hardy nature.
