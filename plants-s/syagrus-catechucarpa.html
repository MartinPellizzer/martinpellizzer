Syagrus catechucarpa

Syagrus catechucarpa, commonly known as the golden palm, is a tropical plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, fan-shaped leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellowish-white flowers that develop into small, round fruits. Its botanical features make it well-suited for warm, humid climates and partial shade.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and hardy nature.
