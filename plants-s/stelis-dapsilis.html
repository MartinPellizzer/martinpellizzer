Stelis dapsilis

Stelis dapsilis, commonly known as the silver spider orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, spider-like appearance and produces small, pale green or white flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually linear and covered in a fine, silvery pubescence, giving it a distinctive look.

Stelis dapsilis prefers warm, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
