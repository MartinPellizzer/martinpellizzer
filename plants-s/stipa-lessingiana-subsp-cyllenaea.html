Stipa lessingiana subsp. cyllenaea

Stipa lessingiana subsp.

cyllenaea, commonly known as the slender wheatgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Central Asia. It is characterized by its slender, erect culms and narrow, grass-like leaves.

The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
