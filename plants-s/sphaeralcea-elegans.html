Sphaeralcea elegans

Sphaeralcea elegans, commonly known as the elegant globemallow, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, giving them a fuzzy texture.
