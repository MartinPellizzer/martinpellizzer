Stelis lehmanneptis

Stelis lehmanneptis, commonly known as the Lehman's stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees in humid, tropical environments, where it thrives in partial shade. The plant features a pseudobulb with a single leaf and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air and decaying organic matter.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique growth habit.
