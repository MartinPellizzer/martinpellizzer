Sonchus gandogeri

Sonchus gandogeri, commonly known as the Ethiopian sunflower, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and other composite flowers. The plant features a basal rosette of leaves and produces yellow flowers with a central disk. It is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
