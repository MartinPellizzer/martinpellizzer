Stachygynandrum obliquum

Stachygynandrum obliquum, commonly known as the zigzag grass, is a species of grass native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzag pattern in the arrangement of its leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in wetland or marshy environments, thriving in moist, nutrient-rich soils. Its flowering stems produce small, inconspicuous flowers that are often clustered in dense inflorescences.

Stachygynandrum obliquum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
