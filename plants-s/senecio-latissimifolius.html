Senecio latissimifolius

Senecio latissimifolius, commonly known as the broad-leaved groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, fleshy leaves and yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes many common garden flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
