Senecio brassii

Senecio brassii, commonly known as the yellow star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its bright yellow flowers bloom in spring and are surrounded by narrow, grass-like leaves.
