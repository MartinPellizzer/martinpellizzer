Swertia pseudochinensis f. grandiflora

Swertia pseudochinensis f. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered swertia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Gentianaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which are typically arranged in dense clusters. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of southwestern China and is adapted to cool, moist environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
