Stelis humilis

Stelis humilis, commonly known as the small-stemmed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is a small epiphytic orchid, typically growing on trees or rocks, and thrives in humid environments. The plant has slender, branching stems and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green. It requires bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to flourish.

Stelis humilis is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
