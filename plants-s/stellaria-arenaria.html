Stellaria arenaria

Stellaria arenaria, commonly known as sandstar, is a low-growing annual herb native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It features small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom from spring to early summer. The plant has thin, branching stems and small, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open, disturbed habitats.

Stellaria arenaria is known for its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
