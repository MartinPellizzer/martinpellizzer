Stenocline ferruginea

Stenocline ferruginea, commonly known as the rusty stenocline, is a species of flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories or wetlands. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a reddish or rusty-colored underside, giving it its common name.

The flowers are small and yellow, arranged in a composite head, and bloom during the spring and early summer.
