Searsia blanda f. exelliana

Searsia blanda f. exelliana, commonly known as the Cape snowball, is a small evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, rounded foliage that provides a lush green appearance throughout the year. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers in late winter to early spring, which are highly attractive to pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value and adaptability make it a popular choice for landscaping in temperate climates.
