Solanum multivenosum

Solanum multivenosum, commonly known as the Tasmanian nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to Tasmania.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and peppers. The plant features small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in spring, followed by purple or red berries. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

The plant contains toxic alkaloids, making it potentially harmful to humans and animals.
