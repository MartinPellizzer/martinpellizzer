Solenocentrum

Solenocentrum, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to southern Africa.

These plants are characterized by their attractive, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including purple, pink, and white. Solenocentrum species typically have thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting them to arid environments. They are popular as houseplants due to their ease of care and ornamental value.

The genus includes several species, with Solenocentrum villosum being one of the most commonly cultivated varieties.
