Smilacina tatsienensis var. stenoloba

Smilacina tatsienensis var. stenoloba, commonly known as the narrow-leaved smilacina, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and heart-shaped leaves that are typically smaller than those of the typical species. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in loose inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and ecological studies.
