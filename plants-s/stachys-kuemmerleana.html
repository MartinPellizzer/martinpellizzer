Stachys kuemmerleana

Stachys kuemmerleana, commonly known as the velvetleaf betony, is a flowering plant native to southern China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic foliage. The plant produces clusters of purple or pink flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are hairy and often have a velvety texture, contributing to its common name.

Stachys kuemmerleana is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
