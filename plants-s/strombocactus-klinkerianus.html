Strombocactus klinkerianus

Strombocactus klinkerianus, commonly known as the Mexican hat cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stem that often develops a ribbed structure as it matures. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Strombocactus klinkerianus is a slow-growing cactus that requires well-draining soil and minimal water to thrive.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in arid gardens and rockeries.
