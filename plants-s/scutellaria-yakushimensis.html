Scutellaria yakushimensis

Scutellaria yakushimensis, commonly known as the Yakushimen skullcap, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Scutellaria yakushimensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
