Sarcostemma tomentellum

Sarcostemma tomentellum, commonly known as the hairy nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its trailing or climbing growth habit and produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or violet in color. The plant has hairy, glandular leaves and is native to regions of southern Africa. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rocky or disturbed soils.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
