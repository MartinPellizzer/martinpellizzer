Sonchus tenerrimus var. perennis

Sonchus tenerrimus var. perennis, commonly known as the perennial sowthistle, is a herbaceous plant native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

It features upright, hairy stems that can grow up to 1 meter in height, often branching near the base. The plant produces basal leaves that are spoon-shaped with wavy margins and a rough texture. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family.

It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and waste areas.
