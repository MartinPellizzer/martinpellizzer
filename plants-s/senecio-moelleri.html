Senecio moelleri

Senecio moelleri, commonly known as the blue-green ice plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white powder, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
