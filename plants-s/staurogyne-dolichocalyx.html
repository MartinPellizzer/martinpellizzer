Staurogyne dolichocalyx

Staurogyne dolichocalyx, commonly known as the long-stalked waterwheel plant, is a species of aquatic plant native to tropical regions of South America.

The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, often found in marshes and ponds.
