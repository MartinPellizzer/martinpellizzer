Stipa aristoides

Stipa aristoides, commonly known as the aristoid grass, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and dense, spike-like inflorescences that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight. Its leaves are narrow and wiry, often covered in fine hairs, while its seeds are small and wind-dispersed.

Stipa aristoides is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its elegant, grass-like foliage.
