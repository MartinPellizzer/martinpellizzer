Specklinia pristeoglossa

Specklinia pristeoglossa, commonly known as the velvet leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of trees or other plants without deriving nutrients from them. It thrives in humid, tropical environments with indirect sunlight.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
