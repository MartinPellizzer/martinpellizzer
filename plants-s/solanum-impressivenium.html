Solanum impressivenium

Solanum impressivenium, commonly known as the impressive nightshade, is a member of the Solanaceae family, characterized by its showy, often colorful flowers.

It typically features smooth, glandular-stemmed foliage and produces berries that range in color from yellow to red. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions and thrives in warm, humid climates. It has small, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture.

Due to its toxic properties, it is important to handle the plant with care.
