Senecio anisophyllus

Senecio anisophyllus, commonly known as the African Daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features bright yellow ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers, creating a daisy-like appearance. Its leaves are typically linear and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Senecio anisophyllus thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
