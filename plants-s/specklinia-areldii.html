Specklinia areldii

Specklinia areldii, commonly known as the golden rosette.

It is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family, Orchidaceae, native to the Philippines. This epiphytic plant forms dense, compact rosettes of dark green, shiny leaves with a golden hue. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters during the wet season.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
