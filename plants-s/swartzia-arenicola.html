Swartzia arenicola

Swartzia arenicola, commonly known as the coastal swartzia, is a small evergreen tree native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is well adapted to coastal conditions, including salt spray and strong winds. Its wood is valued for its durability and is used in local crafts and construction.

Swartzia arenicola plays an important ecological role in stabilizing coastal dunes and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
