Scutellaria altaica

Scutellaria altaica, commonly known as the Altai skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue to purple in color, arranged in whorls along the stem. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to high-altitude environments.

Scutellaria altaica is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
