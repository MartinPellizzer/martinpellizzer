Sedum erici-magnusii subsp. erici-magnusii

Sedum erici-magnusii subsp.

erici-magnusii, commonly known as the Ericus Magnusii stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions. It is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are typically blue-green in color and covered in a fine, white powder.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its hardy nature allows it to survive in cold climates with minimal care.
