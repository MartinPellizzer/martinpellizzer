Schismatoglottis latifolia var. viridis

Schismatoglottis latifolia var. viridis, commonly known as the Emerald Leaf Schismatoglottis, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features broad, waxy, dark green leaves that are often tinged with a vibrant green hue, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. It is a herbaceous perennial that thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-draining soil.

Due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical plant collections.
