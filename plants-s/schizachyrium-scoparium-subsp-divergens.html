Schizachyrium scoparium subsp. divergens

Schizachyrium scoparium subsp.

divergens, commonly known as eastern grass bluestem, is a perennial grass native to eastern North America. It typically grows in moist to wet soils in open woods, wetlands, and prairies.

The plant has narrow, bluish-green leaves and produces panicles of small, fluffy seeds in late summer. It is a valuable component of native plant communities due to its ecological benefits.

Its adaptability makes it suitable for restoration projects and wildlife habitat enhancement.
