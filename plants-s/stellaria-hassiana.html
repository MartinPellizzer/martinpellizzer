Stellaria hassiana

Stellaria hassiana, commonly known as the lesser starwort, is a small annual herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist meadows, grasslands, and along stream banks. The plant features slender stems that are often branched and covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, white or pale blue, and arranged in clusters, giving it a star-like appearance.

Stellaria hassiana is known for its delicate foliage and is often found in disturbed soils or areas with partial shade.
