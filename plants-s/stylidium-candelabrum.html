Stylidium candelabrum

Stylidium candelabrum, commonly known as the candlestick grass.

This plant is a member of the Commelinaceae family and is native to Australia. It is a small, herbaceous plant that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant is characterized by its upright, candle-like appearance with multiple stems emerging from a single base.

It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often followed by distinctive, club-shaped seed pods.
