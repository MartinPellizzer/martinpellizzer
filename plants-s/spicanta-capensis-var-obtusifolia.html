Spicanta capensis var. obtusifolia

Spicanta capensis var. obtusifolia, commonly known as the Cape spurge, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically from spring to early summer. The plant has smooth, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly waxy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.

Its adaptability and ornamental value make it a popular choice for native gardens.
