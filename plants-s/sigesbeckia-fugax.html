Sigesbeckia fugax

Sigesbeckia fugax, commonly known as the Chinese ironweed, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy, herbaceous stems and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces small, spherical fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Sigesbeckia fugax is noted for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
