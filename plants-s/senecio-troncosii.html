Senecio troncosii

Senecio troncosii, commonly known as the African daisy, is a striking succulent native to South Africa.

It features a thick, branching stem with dense clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a valuable addition to xeriscaping and drought-tolerant landscapes.
