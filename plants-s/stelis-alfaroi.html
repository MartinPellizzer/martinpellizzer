Stelis alfaroi

Stelis alfaroi, commonly known as the Alfaroi orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clustered, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
