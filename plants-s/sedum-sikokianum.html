Sedum sikokianum

Sedum sikokianum, commonly known as the Korean stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to East Asia.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is adapted to rocky and arid environments, making it drought-tolerant and easy to care for. Its leaves are typically blue-green in color and can store water efficiently.

Sedum sikokianum is often used in rock gardens and as a decorative plant due to its hardy nature and aesthetic appeal.
