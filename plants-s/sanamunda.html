Sanamunda

Sanamunda, commonly known as the African violet, is a popular ornamental plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the genus Saintpaulia and is characterized by its vibrant, velvety flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant thrives in well-draining soil and requires indirect sunlight to prevent leaf burn. Its foliage consists of thick, waxy leaves that help retain moisture.

Sanamunda is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ability to bloom continuously under proper care.
