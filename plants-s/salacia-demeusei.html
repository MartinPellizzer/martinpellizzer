Salacia demeusei

Salacia demeusei, commonly known as the Demeuse's salacia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Salaceae family and is characterized by its striking, pendulous inflorescences that display vibrant colors. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a waxy texture, while its flowers are fragrant and attract various pollinators.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, Salacia demeusei is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
