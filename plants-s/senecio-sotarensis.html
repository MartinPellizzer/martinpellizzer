Senecio sotarensis

Senecio sotarensis, commonly known as the Sotara Senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It produces yellow daisy-like flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens for its ornamental value.
