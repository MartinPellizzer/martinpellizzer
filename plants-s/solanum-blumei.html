Solanum blumei

Solanum blumei, commonly known as the Blume's nightshade, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is native to parts of Central and South America, particularly in regions with tropical climates. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters, with simple, ovate leaves and showy, violet-blue flowers. Its fruits are small, globose berries that change color from green to yellow as they mature.

Solanum blumei is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and open woodlands.
