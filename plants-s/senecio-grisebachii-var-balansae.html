Senecio grisebachii var. balansae

Senecio grisebachii var. balansae, commonly known as the Balansae groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are generally hairy and grayish-green, while its flowers are yellow and occur in clustered inflorescences.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
