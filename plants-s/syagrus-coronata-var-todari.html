Syagrus coronata var. todari

Syagrus coronata var. todari, commonly known as the Todari palm, is a tropical plant native to the coastal regions of Brazil.

It is characterized by its slender, upright trunk and a crown of narrow, feather-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that eventually develop into ornamental fruits with a spiky exterior. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires ample sunlight to flourish.

Due to its unique beauty and adaptability, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in warm climates.
