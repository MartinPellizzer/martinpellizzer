Stemonurus polymorphus var. ovalifolius

Stemonurus polymorphus var. ovalifolius, commonly known as the oval-leaved stemonurus, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The leaves are oval-shaped and have a smooth texture, contributing to its ornamental value. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade conditions.

It is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and as a decorative plant.
