Styloglossum stella

Styloglossum stella, commonly known as the star orchid, is a rare and delicate orchid species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of white and purple. The plant has a short, erect stem with a single or few leaves, and its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the forest floor. Styloglossum stella thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires careful cultivation due to its specific ecological needs.

Its unique floral structure and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
