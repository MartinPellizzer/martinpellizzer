Solanum rigescens var. rubetorum

Solanum rigescens var. rubetorum, commonly known as the red-stemmed nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its glandular, hairy stems that often turn reddish in color, and its small, white or purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces berries that range in color from yellow to red, containing toxic alkaloids. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its toxic properties, it is important to handle the plant with care.
