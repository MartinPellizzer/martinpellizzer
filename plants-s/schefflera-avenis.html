Schefflera avenis

Schefflera avenis, commonly known as the umbrella tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of leaflets, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its adaptability and ornamental value.

Schefflera avenis is also valued for its ability to purify air and tolerate low light conditions.
