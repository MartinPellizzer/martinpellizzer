Sinocrassula bergeri

Sinocrassula bergeri, commonly known as the Berger's stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to China.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its compact, low-growing form. The plant features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the spring or early summer.

Sinocrassula bergeri is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
