Solanum cuencanum

Solanum cuencanum, commonly known as the Cuencanum nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and tobacco. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are ovate with a hairy texture, and it produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into berries.

The berries are usually green when mature and contain several seeds.
