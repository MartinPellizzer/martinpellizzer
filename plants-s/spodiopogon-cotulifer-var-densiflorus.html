Spodiopogon cotulifer var. densiflorus

Spodiopogon cotulifer var. densiflorus, commonly known as the denseflower spodiopogon, is a perennial grass species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and dense clusters of small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or wetlands. Its leaves are narrow, grass-like, and often have a bluish-green hue.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral structure.
