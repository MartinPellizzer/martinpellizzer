Scutellaria × minkwitziae

Scutellaria × minkwitziae, commonly known as the hybrid bluebeard, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces spikes of showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its botanical features include glandular hairs on the foliage and a distinctive, spurred corolla in its flowers.

Scutellaria × minkwitziae is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
