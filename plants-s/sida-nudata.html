Sida nudata

Sida nudata, commonly known as smallflower sida, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with a hairy surface, and it has a fibrous root system.

Sida nudata is often found in disturbed soils and is adapted to a variety of environmental conditions.
