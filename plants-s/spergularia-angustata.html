Spergularia angustata

Spergularia angustata, commonly known as narrowleaf sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of the Americas.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family, characterized by its succulent leaves and showy, pink to red flowers.
