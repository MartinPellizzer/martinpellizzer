Sebastiania discolor

Sebastiania discolor, commonly known as the red-seeded sycamore, is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

Sebastiania discolor is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in warm, humid climates.
