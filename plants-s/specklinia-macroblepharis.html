Specklinia macroblepharis

Specklinia macroblepharis, commonly known as the large-eyed speckled orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit and its distinctive, speckled green leaves. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a yellow or orange lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, loose substrates for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and delicate beauty.
