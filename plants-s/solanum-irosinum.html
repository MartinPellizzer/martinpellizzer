Solanum irosinum

Solanum irosinum, commonly known as the Irosinum nightshade, is a shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves and clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that turn from green to red as they mature. It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae), which includes other economically important plants like tomatoes and potatoes.

Solanum irosinum is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and riverbanks.
