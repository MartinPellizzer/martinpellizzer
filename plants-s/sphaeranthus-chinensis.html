Sphaeranthus chinensis

Sphaeranthus chinensis, commonly known as Chinese globe thistle, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its spherical, spiny seed heads that resemble a globe, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is part of the Asteraceae family. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are small and yellow.

Sphaeranthus chinensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance.
