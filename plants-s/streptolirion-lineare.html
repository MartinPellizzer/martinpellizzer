Streptolirion lineare

Streptolirion lineare, commonly known as the linear leaf streptolirion, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of pink or white, which bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact size and prolonged flowering period.
