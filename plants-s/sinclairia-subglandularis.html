Sinclairia subglandularis

Sinclairia subglandularis, commonly known as the silver leaf sinclairia, is a striking evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that have a silvery underside, giving it a unique visual appeal. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Sinclairia subglandularis is valued for its ornamental qualities and relatively low maintenance requirements.
