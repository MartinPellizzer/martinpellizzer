Silene firma

Silene firma, commonly known as the rigid catchfly, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family, characterized by its showy, pink or white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are linear and slightly hairy, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Silene firma is known for its resilience and is often found in disturbed or marginal soils.
