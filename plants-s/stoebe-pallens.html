Stoebe pallens

Stoebe pallens, commonly known as lesser cow-wheat, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its small, yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

Stoebe pallens is often found in disturbed soils and can spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in some areas.
