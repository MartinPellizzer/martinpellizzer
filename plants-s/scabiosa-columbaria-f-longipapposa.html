Scabiosa columbaria f. longipapposa

Scabiosa columbaria f. longipapposa, commonly known as the long-pappous scabious, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It features finely divided, fern-like leaves and produces clusters of pink to purple flowers with long, slender bracts. The plant is characterized by its distinctive, fluffy pappus that aids in seed dispersal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in meadows and rocky habitats.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
