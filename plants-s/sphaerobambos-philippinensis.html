Sphaerobambos philippinensis

Sphaerobambos philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine bamboo, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family (Poaceae).

It is characterized by its dense, clumping growth habit and is native to the Philippines. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often used in traditional crafts and as a source of fiber.
