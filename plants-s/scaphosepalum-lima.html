Scaphosepalum lima

Scaphosepalum lima, commonly known as the Lima orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in horticulture.
