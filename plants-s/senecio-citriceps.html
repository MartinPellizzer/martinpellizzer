Senecio citriceps

Senecio citriceps, commonly known as the yellow pincushion, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers. The plant features dense, woolly foliage that gives it a pincushion appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Senecio citriceps is also noted for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
