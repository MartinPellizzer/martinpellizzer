Sansevieria downsii

Sansevieria downsii, commonly known as the Snake Plant, is a hardy, evergreen perennial plant native to tropical Africa.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with yellow or white variegation. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it an ideal indoor plant. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water, which contributes to its resilience.

Sansevieria downsii is also noted for its air-purifying properties, improving indoor air quality.
