Stellaria madagascariensis

Stellaria madagascariensis, commonly known as the Madagascar starwort, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is a low-growing herb that typically reaches heights of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, white or pale blue flowers that are arranged in clusters, giving it a star-like appearance. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

Stellaria madagascariensis is adapted to thrive in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories or along stream banks.
