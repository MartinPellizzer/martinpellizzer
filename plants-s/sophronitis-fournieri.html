Sophronitis fournieri

Sophronitis fournieri, commonly known as the Fournier's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique and striking floral structure. The plant typically features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with intricate patterns and a distinct scent.

Sophronitis fournieri is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and rarity in cultivation.
