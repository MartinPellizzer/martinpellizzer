Siphoneranthemum bicolor

Siphoneranthemum bicolor, commonly known as the two-colored siphoneranthemum, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking bi-colored flowers, typically featuring a purple upper lip and a white lower lip. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
