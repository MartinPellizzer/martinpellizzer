Styrax weberbaueri

Styrax weberbaueri, commonly known as the Chinese styrax, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces small, dark blue berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

Styrax weberbaueri is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and is often used in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
