Stelis cracens

Stelis cracens, commonly known as the Cracens orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid tropical environments. The plant has a compact growth habit with a single stem and a few leaves. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, featuring a variety of colors including white, pink, and purple.

Stelis cracens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or shaded gardens.
