Syagrus harleyi

Syagrus harleyi, commonly known as the harley palm, is a species of palm native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of pinnate leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into orange-red fruits, which are edible for some bird species. Syagrus harleyi is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
