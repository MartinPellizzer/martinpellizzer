Scutellaria drummondii

Scutellaria drummondii, commonly known as Drummond's blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often covered with fine hairs.

Scutellaria drummondii is typically found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments.
