Swertia manshurica

Swertia manshurica, commonly known as the Siberian gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its blue or violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shady habitats such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged oppositely along the stem.

Swertia manshurica is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
