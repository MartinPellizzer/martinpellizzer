Sphenopholis obtusata f. purpurascens

Sphenopholis obtusata f. purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-stemmed sphenophyllum, is a rare variant of the sphenophyllum genus.

It is characterized by its distinctive purplish stems and leaves that are typically shorter and broader than those of the typical form. This fern-like plant is native to specific regions in South America, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its botanical features include a creeping rhizome and small, ovate leaves with a smooth margin.
