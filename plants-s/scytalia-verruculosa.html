Scytalia verruculosa

Scytalia verruculosa, commonly known as the warty scytalia, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, warty tubers that are often covered in a rough, uneven surface. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and can vary in size depending on environmental conditions.

Scytalia verruculosa is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and adaptability to various soil types.
