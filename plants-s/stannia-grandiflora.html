Stannia grandiflora

Stannia grandiflora, commonly known as the grandiflora stannia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has smooth, dark green leaves that are elongated and arranged alternately along the stem. Stannia grandiflora thrives in humid environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
