Stelis revoluta

Stelis revoluta, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually in shades of white and green.

Stelis revoluta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
