Stenoglottis fimbriata

Stenoglottis fimbriata, commonly known as the lace-leaf orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, intricately veined leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, white or pale green flowers that are clustered in racemes. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Stenoglottis fimbriata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a garden setting.
