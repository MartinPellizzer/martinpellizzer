Senecio leucostachys var. schreiteri

Senecio leucostachys var. schreiteri, commonly known as the Schreiter's blueweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its blue to purple flowers and silver-gray foliage. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. It produces small, tubular flowers arranged in a cylindrical inflorescence, which attracts various pollinators.
