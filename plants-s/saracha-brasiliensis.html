Saracha brasiliensis

Saracha brasiliensis, commonly known as the Brazilian starfruit, is a tropical plant native to Brazil.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the family Solanaceae, which includes nightshades. The plant produces small, star-shaped fruits that are typically yellow or orange when ripe. Its leaves are ovate and have a slightly hairy texture.
