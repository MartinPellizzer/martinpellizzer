Sedum mixtum

Sedum mixtum, commonly known as the stonecrop mix, is a hardy succulent plant native to Europe.

It features compact, upright growth with fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens, containers, and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance requirements.

Sedum mixtum is also valued for its ability to tolerate frost and poor soil conditions.
