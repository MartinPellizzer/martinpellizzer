Solandra odorifera

Solandra odorifera, commonly known as the sweet sarsaparilla, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a woody shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a glossy appearance.

Solandra odorifera is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for its unique fragrance.
