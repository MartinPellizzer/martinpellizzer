Schizachyrium fragile

Schizachyrium fragile, commonly known as slender false panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes, swales, and wet meadows. The plant has slender, erect stems and produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are often reddish in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Schizachyrium fragile is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in wetland restoration.
