Stenospermation mathewsii var. stipitatum

Stenospermation mathewsii var. stipitatum, commonly known as the narrow-fruit orchid, is a rare and delicate orchid species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

The plant produces distinctive, narrow fruits that are often covered in a fine, hairy texture. Its growth is slow and requires specific environmental conditions, such as high humidity and well-drained soil, to thrive.
