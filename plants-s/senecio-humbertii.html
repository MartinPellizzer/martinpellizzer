Senecio humbertii

Senecio humbertii, commonly known as the Humbert's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid environments. It features narrow, linear leaves and produces small yellow flowers that attract pollinators.
