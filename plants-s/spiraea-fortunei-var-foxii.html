Spiraea fortunei var. foxii

Spiraea fortunei var. foxii, commonly known as the Foxii weeping spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its cascading clusters of white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has arching stems that create a graceful, weeping appearance, making it popular in ornamental gardening. Its foliage turns a striking red in the fall, adding seasonal interest.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is relatively low maintenance once established.
