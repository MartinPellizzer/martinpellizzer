Scutellaria benthamiana

Scutellaria benthamiana, commonly known as the bentham's blueweed, is a herbaceous plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. It is often used in botanical research due to its genetic simplicity and ease of cultivation.

Scutellaria benthamiana is also valued for its medicinal properties, though further studies are needed to fully explore its potential.
