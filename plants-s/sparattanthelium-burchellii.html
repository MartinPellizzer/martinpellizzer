Sparattanthelium burchellii

Sparattanthelium burchellii, commonly known as the Burchell's grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open grasslands and rocky outcrops, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant has slender, wiry stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, which are characteristic of the grass family.

Sparattanthelium burchellii is valued for its hardiness and is sometimes used in restoration ecology projects.
