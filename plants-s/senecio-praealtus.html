Senecio praealtus

Senecio praealtus, commonly known as the tall senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features upright, branching stems and narrow, gray-green leaves. It produces showy yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens, though it requires well-drained soil and full sun.
