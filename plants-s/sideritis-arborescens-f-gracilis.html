Sideritis arborescens f. gracilis

Sideritis arborescens f. gracilis, commonly known as the slender wood sage, is a flowering plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is a shrub-like perennial that typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. The plant features small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple in color, arranged in dense spikes. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in a fine layer of hair.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
