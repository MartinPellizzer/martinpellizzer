Solanum desmodioides

Solanum desmodioides, commonly known as the Mexican nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of about 3 to 5 meters. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by yellowish berries. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a waxy coating.

Solanum desmodioides belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is known for its potential medicinal properties.
