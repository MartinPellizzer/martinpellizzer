Saurauia aspera f. delessertiana

Saurauia aspera f. delessertiana, commonly known as the Delessert's roughleaf saurauia, is a variant of the saurauia genus native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its rough, textured leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, often as an epiphyte on trees. Its botanical features include elliptical to ovate leaves with a leathery texture and a preference for high humidity.

This fern-like plant is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to indoor conditions.
