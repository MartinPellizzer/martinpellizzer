Stemona sulensis

Stemona sulensis, commonly known as the Southern stemona, is a perennial herb native to China.

It features slender, cylindrical stems that are typically covered with a smooth, pale green to yellowish bark. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional Chinese medicine.
