Stapelia scylla

Stapelia scylla, commonly known as the "Cape Town star" or "Stapelia star," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its unique, star-shaped flowers that resemble a crown. The plant produces fleshy, green stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. Its flowers are typically yellow with red markings, and they bloom in late winter to early spring.

Stapelia scylla is often cultivated for its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements.
