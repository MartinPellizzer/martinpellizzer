Steffensia Citrifolia

Steffensia citrifolia is a rare and fascinating plant native to the high altitudes of southern South America. Known for its striking yellow flowers and unique citrus-scented leaves, it has captured the attention of botanists and plant enthusiasts alike. This species is notable for its ability to thrive in extreme conditions, making it a subject of scientific interest. Despite its beauty, it remains poorly studied due to its limited distribution and elusive habitat. Its discovery and conservation are crucial for understanding biodiversity in mountain ecosystems.
