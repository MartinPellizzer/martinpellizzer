Senecio turczaninovii

Senecio turczaninovii, commonly known as the Turczaninov's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. It features narrow, linear leaves and produces small yellow flowers that bloom in the spring.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
