Solanum callobotrys

Solanum callobotrys, commonly known as the Australian nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are usually purple or blue in color. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, and the plant is part of the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

Due to its toxic properties, it is often considered a weed in agricultural settings.
