Statice bonnetii

Statice bonnetii, commonly known as the pink statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces dense clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically blooms from late summer to early autumn, adding vibrant color to gardens and natural landscapes. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Statice bonnetii is well-suited for coastal gardens due to its tolerance of salty air and well-drained soils.
