Senecio ionophyllus var. bernardinus

Senecio ionophyllus var. bernardinus, commonly known as the Desert Carpetweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features narrow, gray-green leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters. It is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturalists.
