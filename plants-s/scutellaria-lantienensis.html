Scutellaria lantienensis

Scutellaria lantienensis, commonly known as the Lantien skullcap, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features square stems and opposite leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are typically blue or violet, with two-lipped corollas characteristic of the genus.

It is valued in traditional medicine for its potential medicinal properties.
