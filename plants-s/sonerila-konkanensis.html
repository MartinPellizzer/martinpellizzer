Sonerila konkanensis

Sonerila konkanensis, commonly known as the Konkan Sonerila, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white. Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
