Scorzonera sublanata

Scorzonera sublanata, commonly known as the woolly scorzonera, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a robust, fleshy root system that is often used in traditional medicine. The plant has hairy, gray-green leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine pubescence. Its flowers are yellow and typically appear in clusters, blooming from late spring to early summer.

Scorzonera sublanata thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or limestone habitats.
