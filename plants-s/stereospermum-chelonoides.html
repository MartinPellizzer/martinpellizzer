Stereospermum chelonoides

It belongs to the Bignoniaceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, white, clustered flowers that resemble snowballs. Its rapid growth and ability to tolerate various soil conditions contribute to its adaptability and widespread cultivation.
