Stellaria media subsp. eliezeri

Stellaria media subsp.

eliezeri, commonly known as Eliezer's mouse-ear, is a low-growing perennial herb native to southern Europe. It features small, white flowers with five petals, typically arranged in clusters at the ends of stems.

The leaves are oval-shaped, hairy, and often have a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges and disturbed areas.

This subspecies is closely related to the common chickweed but exhibits distinct morphological characteristics.
