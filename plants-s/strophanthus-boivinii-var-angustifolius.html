Strophanthus boivinii var. angustifolius

Strophanthus boivinii var. angustifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leafed strophanthus, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Africa.

It features lanceolate, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces striking yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom in late winter to early spring. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its cardiac glycoside compounds.
