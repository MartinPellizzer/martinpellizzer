Sedum aizoon subsp. maximowiczii

Sedum aizoon subsp.

maximowiczii, commonly known as the stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to arid and rocky environments. It features fleshy, upright stems and opposite, spoon-shaped leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color.

The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
