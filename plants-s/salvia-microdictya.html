Salvia microdictya

Salvia microdictya, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sage, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces spikes of blue to violet flowers. The plant is characterized by its aromatic foliage and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Salvia microdictya is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
