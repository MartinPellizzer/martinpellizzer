Saccopetalum carnosum

Saccopetalum carnosum, commonly known as the sweet-scented saccopetalum, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in dune environments. Its unique aroma is attributed to the presence of essential oils in its foliage.

Saccopetalum carnosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic and aromatic qualities.
