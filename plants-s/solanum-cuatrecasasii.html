Solanum cuatrecasasii

Solanum cuatrecasasii, commonly known as the Cuatrecasas's nightshade, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central America.

The plant is characterized by its small, bell-shaped flowers that typically bloom at night, a trait common among many nightshade species.
