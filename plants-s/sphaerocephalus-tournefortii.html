Sphaerocephalus tournefortii

Sphaerocephalus tournefortii, commonly known as the globular rock rose, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, rounded growth habit and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it well-suited for rock gardens and dry landscapes. Its leaves are narrow and gray-green, often covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

Sphaerocephalus tournefortii is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
