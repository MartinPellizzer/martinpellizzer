Scutellaria cordifolia

Scutellaria cordifolia, commonly known as heartleaf skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, tubular blue or purple flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and stream banks.

Scutellaria cordifolia is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported calming and sedative properties.
