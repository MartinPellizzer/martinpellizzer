Solanum demissum f. calycotrichum

Solanum demissum f. calycotrichum, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a variant of the Solanum demissum species.

It is a herbaceous perennial native to South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, dense hairs. It produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale purple, followed by yellow berries.

This cultivar is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and ease of cultivation.
