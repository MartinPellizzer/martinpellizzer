Sprekelia

Sprekelia, commonly known as the crown imperial, is a striking bulbous plant native to the Cape Province of South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with a distinctive crown-like arrangement at the base. The plant has fleshy, underground bulbs that store nutrients and water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. Its leaves are long, strap-like, and grassy, emerging from the base of the plant.

Sprekelia is often cultivated for its vibrant, eye-catching blooms and is well-suited to rock gardens and well-drained soils.
