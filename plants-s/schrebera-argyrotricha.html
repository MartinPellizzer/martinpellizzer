Schrebera argyrotricha

Schrebera argyrotricha, commonly known as the silver-haired schrebera, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green leaves that have a velvety texture and a waxy coating. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by edible, fleshy fruits. Schrebera argyrotricha thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
