Silene inflata lusus scabra

Silene inflata lusus scabra, commonly known as the scarlet pimpernel, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, spurred petals that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Silene inflata lusus scabra is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in disturbed soils.
