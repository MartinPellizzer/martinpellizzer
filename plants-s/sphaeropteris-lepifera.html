Sphaeropteris lepifera

Sphaeropteris lepifera, commonly known as the lace fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds that are covered in fine, silky hairs. The fronds are typically bright green and can reach lengths of up to 3 meters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

The plant has a slow growth rate and is valued for its ornamental appearance and ecological significance.
