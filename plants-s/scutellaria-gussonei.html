Scutellaria gussonei

Scutellaria gussonei, commonly known as the Gussone's skullcap, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of southern Italy.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color, arranged in whorled clusters along the stems. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs.

Scutellaria gussonei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
