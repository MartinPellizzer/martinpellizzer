Smilax austrosinensis

Smilax austrosinensis, commonly known as the Chinese smilax, is a perennial vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features a robust rhizomatous root system and produces woody stems covered in sharp, spiny projections. The plant has alternate, simple, and often hairy leaves that are elliptical to ovate in shape. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and clustered in umbels, followed by red, berry-like fruits.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
