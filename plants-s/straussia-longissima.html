Straussia longissima

Straussia longissima, commonly known as the long-stemmed lily, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant features strap-like leaves that are long and narrow, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and blooming habits, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
