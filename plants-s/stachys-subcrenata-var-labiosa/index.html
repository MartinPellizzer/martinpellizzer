Stachys Subcrenata Var. Labiosa

Stachys subcrenata var. labiosa is a unique and striking plant known for its vibrant purple flowers and ornamental value. Native to specific regions in Europe, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade. Its foliage features serrated edges and a compact growth habit, making it a popular choice for gardeners. The plant is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. With its distinctive appearance and low maintenance requirements, it stands out as a versatile addition to any landscape.
