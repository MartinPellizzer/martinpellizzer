Stipa aristella

Stipa aristella, commonly known as the aristate grass or slender needlegrass, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of Eurasia, including parts of Central Asia and the Middle East. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its inflorescence consists of a panicle of spikelets, which are small clusters of flowers.

Stipa aristella is adapted to survive in harsh environments, making it an important component of steppe and desert ecosystems.
