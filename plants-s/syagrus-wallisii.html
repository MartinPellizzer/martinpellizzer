Syagrus wallisii

Syagrus wallisii, commonly known as the royal palm, is a tropical plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, fan-shaped leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that eventually develop into feather-like fruits. Syagrus wallisii thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and landscapes.
