Sedum fischeri

Sedum fischeri, commonly known as Fischer's stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Central Asia.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically blue-green in color, adapted for storing water. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the summer months. It thrives in rocky or arid environments and prefers full sun.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping.
