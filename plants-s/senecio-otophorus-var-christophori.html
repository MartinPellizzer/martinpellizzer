Senecio otophorus var. christophori

Senecio otophorus var. christophori, commonly known as the Christophori's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Namibia and Angola.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and fleshy, helping it conserve water in harsh conditions.
