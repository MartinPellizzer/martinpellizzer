Stellina platycarpa

Stellina platycarpa, commonly known as the starfruit tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped fruits, which are typically yellow when ripe. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually oval or elliptical in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered, often in inflorescences, and it produces small, round berries.

Stellina platycarpa is valued not only for its edible fruit but also for its ornamental appeal.
