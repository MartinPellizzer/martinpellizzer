Schlegelia axillaris

Schlegelia axillaris, commonly known as the axillary schlegelia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as a shrub with small, narrow leaves and produces clusters of yellow to orange flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Schlegelia axillaris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
