Spinacia oleracea var. inermis

Spinacia oleracea var. inermis, commonly known as the smooth-leafed spinach, is a variant of the common spinach plant.

It is characterized by its smooth, hairless leaves as opposed to the typical serrated edges of standard spinach. This variety is often cultivated for its tender foliage and mild flavor. It thrives in cool climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The plant is rich in nutrients, making it a valuable addition to both culinary and nutritional contexts.
