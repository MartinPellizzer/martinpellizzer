Sansevieria bagamoyensis

Sansevieria bagamoyensis, commonly known as the Bagamoyo snake plant, is a species native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with a subtle variegation. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or greenish in color.

Sansevieria bagamoyensis is also valued for its air-purifying properties and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
