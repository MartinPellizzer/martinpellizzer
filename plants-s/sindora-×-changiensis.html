Sindora × changiensis

Sindora × changiensis, commonly known as the hybrid flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree belonging to the family Bombacaceae.

It is a natural hybrid between Sindora obovata and Sindora siamensis, characterized by its striking, fiery orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand and Malaysia, and thrives in warm, humid climates. Its foliage consists of large, palmate leaves, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and urban landscapes.

The tree is also valued for its durable timber and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
