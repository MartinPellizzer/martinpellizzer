Schismatoglottis edanoi

Schismatoglottis edanoi, commonly known as the Edanoi's slipper orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, slipper-shaped flowers that are often brightly colored. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, often with a waxy texture, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Schismatoglottis edanoi is prized for its ornamental value and is cultivated by enthusiasts for its exotic appearance.
