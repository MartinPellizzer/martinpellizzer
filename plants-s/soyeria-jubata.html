Soyeria jubata

Soyeria jubata, commonly known as the golden star lily, is a striking herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that bear clusters of bright yellow, star-shaped flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette at the base. Soyeria jubata thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and unique foliage structure.
