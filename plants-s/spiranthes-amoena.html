Spiranthes amoena

Spiranthes amoena, commonly known as the pink fairy-orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its delicate, spiral arrangement of flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and wetlands. Its inflorescence consists of numerous small, pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

Spiranthes amoena is a low-growing plant with narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in clusters.
