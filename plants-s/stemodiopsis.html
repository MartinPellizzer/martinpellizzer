Stemodiopsis

Stemodiopsis, commonly known as the spider plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is characterized by its rosette of fleshy, sword-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by clusters of red berries. It is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-draining soil and indirect sunlight.

Stemodiopsis is popular as a houseplant due to its ability to propagate easily through its offsets.
