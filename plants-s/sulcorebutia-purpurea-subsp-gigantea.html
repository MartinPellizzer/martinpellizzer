Sulcorebutia purpurea subsp. gigantea

Sulcorebutia purpurea subsp.

gigantea, commonly known as the giant purple crassula, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa. It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green with a reddish or purple tinge, especially under direct sunlight.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

Due to its slow growth and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container cultivation.
