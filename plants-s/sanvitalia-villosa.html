Sanvitalia villosa

Sanvitalia villosa, commonly known as the hairy sanvitalia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its hairy, gray-green foliage and showy, bright yellow flowers. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. Its blooms typically appear in spring and summer, adding vibrant color to the landscape.

Sanvitalia villosa is also valued for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
