Specklinia kuhniae

Specklinia kuhniae, commonly known as the Kuhniae's lithops, is a species of flowering plant in the Aizoaceae family.

It is native to South Africa, specifically the Western Cape region, where it grows in rocky, well-drained soils. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for xerophytic gardens.
