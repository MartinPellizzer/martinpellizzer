Solanum stenopterum

Solanum stenopterum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant features small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by oval, yellowish berries that mature in the fall. Its leaves are typically narrow and have a slightly hairy texture.

Solanum stenopterum is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
