Sideritis scordioides var. guillonii

Sideritis scordioides var. guillonii, commonly known as the Guillon's ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in dry, sunny environments.

Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a mild, herbaceous scent, contributing to its use in traditional herbal medicine.
