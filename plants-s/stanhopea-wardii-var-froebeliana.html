Stanhopea wardii var. froebeliana

Stanhopea wardii var. froebeliana, commonly known as the Froebel's stanhopea, is a rare and striking orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, pendant-like inflorescence bearing numerous fragrant, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration pattern. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers are highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts due to their beauty and rarity.

The variety is noted for its intricate floral structure and unique scent, which plays a role in attracting specific pollinators.
