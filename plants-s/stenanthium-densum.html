Stenanthium densum

Stenanthium densum, commonly known as dense-stemmed grass of heaven, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the family Melanthiaceae and is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and showy, white to pale pink flowers. Stenanthium densum is often found in shaded areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
