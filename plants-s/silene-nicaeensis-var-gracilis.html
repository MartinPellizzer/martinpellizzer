Silene nicaeensis var. gracilis

Silene nicaeensis var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender lady's mantle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright, slender stems and delicate, fern-like leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Silene nicaeensis var. gracilis is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
