Sitobolium apiifolium

Sitobolium apiifolium, commonly known as the common yarrow.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its feathery, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, white or pinkish flowers. The plant is native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and disturbed areas.

Sitobolium apiifolium is known for its medicinal properties, with historical use in traditional medicine for its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic qualities.
