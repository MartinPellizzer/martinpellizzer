Selenicereus boeckmannii

Selenicereus boeckmannii, commonly known as the night blooming cereus, is a climbing cactus native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and large, showy white flowers that open at night. The plant produces fleshy, cylindrical fruits that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in warm, sunny environments with well-draining soil.

Selenicereus boeckmannii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique nocturnal blooming habit.
