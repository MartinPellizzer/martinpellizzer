Schoberia heterophylla

Schoberia heterophylla, commonly known as the variable leaf sandverbena, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid climates. It produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or purple, blooming in late spring to early summer.
