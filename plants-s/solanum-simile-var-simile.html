Solanum simile var. simile

Solanum simile var. simile, commonly known as the common nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other familiar plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring hairy stems and alternate, ovate leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are usually violet or blue, and it produces small, yellow berries that contain toxic alkaloids.

Botanically, it is characterized by its simple structure and adaptability to a variety of soil types.
