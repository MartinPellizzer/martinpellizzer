Solanum esuriale f. rubro-aurantiacum

Solanum esuriale f. rubro-aurantiacum, commonly known as the red-orange-flowered nightshade, is a variant of the Solanum esuriale species.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its striking red and orange flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are ovate and have a slightly hairy texture, while its stems are erect and branching.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its vibrant floral display.
