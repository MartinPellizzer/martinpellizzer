Scaphosepalum dalstroemii

Scaphosepalum dalstroemii, commonly known as the Cape mountain orchid, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in a raceme, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
