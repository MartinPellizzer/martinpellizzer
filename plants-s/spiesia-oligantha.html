Spiesia oligantha

Spiesia oligantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spurge, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features erect, branched stems that are often covered in a milky sap, a characteristic common among the Euphorbiaceae family. The plant has narrow, elongated leaves that are usually arranged oppositely along the stem. Its small, greenish-yellow flowers are clustered in cyathia, typical of the spurge family.

Spiesia oligantha is often found in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils.
