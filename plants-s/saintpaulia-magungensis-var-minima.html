Saintpaulia magungensis var. minima

Saintpaulia magungensis var. minima, commonly known as the dwarf African violet, is a compact, low-growing cultivar of the African violet genus.

It features small, velvety leaves and produces clusters of delicate, tubular flowers in shades of pink, purple, and white. Native to the tropical regions of East Africa, this variety thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light. Its small size makes it ideal for container gardening and as a decorative plant in home settings.

The plant is prized for its ornamental value and ease of care, making it a popular choice among indoor gardeners.
