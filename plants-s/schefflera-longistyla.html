Schefflera longistyla

Schefflera longistyla, commonly known as the longistyle umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like crown of large, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by bright red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as a decorative plant.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and landscape design.
