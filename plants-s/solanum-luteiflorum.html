Solanum luteiflorum

Solanum luteiflorum, commonly known as the yellow flowered nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant produces striking yellow flowers that bloom in summer, followed by small, oval-shaped berries. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Solanum luteiflorum is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.
