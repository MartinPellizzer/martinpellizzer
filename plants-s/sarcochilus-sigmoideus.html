Sarcochilus sigmoideus

Sarcochilus sigmoideus, commonly known as the zigzag hibiscus, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It features a distinctive, zigzag-shaped stem and produces showy, yellow-orange flowers with a prominent red lip. The plant has a fibrous root system and thrives in shaded, moist environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, contributing to its unique appearance.

Sarcochilus sigmoideus is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
