Stachys macilenta

Stachys macilenta, commonly known as the slender hedge nettle, is a herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and pairs of opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, pale purple to white flowers arranged in whorled clusters along the stems. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland edges and stream banks.

Stachys macilenta is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
