Solanum luteum f. miniatibaccatum

Solanum luteum f. miniatibaccatum, commonly known as the miniature yellow nightshade, is a compact variant of the yellow nightshade plant.

It is characterized by its small, clustered leaves and yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like tomatoes and peppers. It is native to southern Europe and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
