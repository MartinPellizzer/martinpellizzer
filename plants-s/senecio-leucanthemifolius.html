Senecio leucanthemifolius

Senecio leucanthemifolius, commonly known as the white thistle.

This perennial plant is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its silvery-green, woolly foliage and yellow flowers. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its showy composite flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to rock gardens or dry landscapes.

It is also noted for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
