Sorbus hemsleyi

Sorbus hemsleyi, commonly known as the Hemsley's rowan, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the Himalayas and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and red berries. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and thrives in temperate climates. Its leaves are simple and alternate, with a serrated margin.

Sorbus hemsleyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
