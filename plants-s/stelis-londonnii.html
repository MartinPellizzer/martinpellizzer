Stelis londonnii

Stelis londonnii, commonly known as the London orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often in clusters. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding substrate.

Stelis londonnii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
