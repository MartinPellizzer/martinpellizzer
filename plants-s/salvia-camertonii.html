Salvia camertonii

Salvia camertonii, commonly known as Camerton's sage, is a perennial herb native to the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in rocky soils and full sun, making it well-suited for xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for native plant gardens.
