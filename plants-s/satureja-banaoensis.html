Satureja banaoensis

Satureja banaoensis, commonly known as Banao thyme, is a perennial herb native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants known for their essential oils. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, exhibiting compact growth with small, narrow leaves. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, arranged in whorls along the stems.

Satureja banaoensis is valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
