Selbyana consobrina

Selbyana consobrina, commonly known as the Selbyana rose.

It is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family, characterized by its thorny stems and showy pink to red flowers. Native to the southern United States, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant blooms and unique foliage.

It is also used in horticulture for its ability to provide both floral display and structural interest in gardens.
