Senecio × lamottei

Senecio × lamottei, commonly known as the hybrid African violet, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves and showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 10–30 cm, with hairy stems and bright green, velvety foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Senecio × lamottei is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact size and attractive appearance.
