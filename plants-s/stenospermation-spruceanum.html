Stenospermation spruceanum

It is characterized by its slender, unbranched trunk and a crown of feathery, pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow cones that are typically unisexual, with separate male and female cones on the same plant. It is native to a limited geographic region in Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments.
