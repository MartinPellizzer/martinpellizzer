Siphoneranthemum tricolor

Siphoneranthemum tricolor, commonly known as the three-colored siphoneranthemum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its strikingly colorful, tri-colored flowers that typically display red, yellow, and white hues. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture and emits a mild, aromatic scent.
