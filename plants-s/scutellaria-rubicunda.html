Scutellaria rubicunda

Scutellaria rubicunda, commonly known as red beardroot, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is known for its aromatic herbs and medicinal properties. The plant features a basal rosette of hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces spikes of violet to purple flowers. Its tuberous roots are distinctive and often used in traditional medicine.

Scutellaria rubicunda thrives in rocky, arid environments and is adapted to full sun exposure.
