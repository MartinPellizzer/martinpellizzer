Salmasia ×

Salmasia ×, commonly known as the hybrid salvia.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces showy, two-lipped flowers in shades of blue, purple, and white. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Salmasia × hybrids are typically perennial and thrive in well-drained soil and full sun.
