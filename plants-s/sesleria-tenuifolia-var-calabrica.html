Sesleria tenuifolia var. calabrica

Sesleria tenuifolia var. calabrica, commonly known as the narrowleaf sesleria, is a perennial grass-like plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color, with a delicate, feathery appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, often in shades of pale blue or white. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
