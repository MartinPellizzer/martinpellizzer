Silene meyeri var. persica

Silene meyeri var. persica, commonly known as Persian campion, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking pink to purple flowers with five petals. The plant has alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to rock gardens and alpine collections.
