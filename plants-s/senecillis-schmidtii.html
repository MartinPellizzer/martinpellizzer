Senecillis schmidtii

Senecillis schmidtii, commonly known as the Schmidt's senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically features hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with a grayish-green hue. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in shades of yellow to orange.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or dry landscapes.
