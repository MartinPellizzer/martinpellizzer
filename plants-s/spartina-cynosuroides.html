Spartina cynosuroides

Spartina cynosuroides, commonly known as salt marsh cordgrass, is a perennial grass native to coastal regions of North America.

It thrives in brackish and saline environments, often found in tidal marshes and estuaries. The plant has slender, erect stems that can grow up to 2 meters tall, with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its inflorescence consists of dense, spike-like clusters of small, pale green to brownish flowers.

Spartina cynosuroides plays a crucial role in stabilizing coastal soils and providing habitat for various wetland species.
