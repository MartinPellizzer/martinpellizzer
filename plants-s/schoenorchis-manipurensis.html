Schoenorchis manipurensis

Schoenorchis manipurensis, commonly known as the Manipur orchid, is a rare terrestrial orchid native to the northeastern region of India.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, elongated leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and grasslands. Its unique botanical features include a pseudobulb structure and intricate floral morphology.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
