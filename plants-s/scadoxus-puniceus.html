Scadoxus puniceus

Scadoxus puniceus, commonly known as the red-flowered African lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, strap-like leaves that are often reddish in color, adding to its ornamental appeal. The plant produces showy, red or orange flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. Its bulbs are adapted to store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions.

Scadoxus puniceus is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant appearance and resilience.
