Solanum robinsonianum

Solanum robinsonianum, commonly known as the Robust nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters tall, characterized by its thick, woody stems and dark green, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into berries, which are typically red when mature. It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae), which includes other familiar plants like tomatoes and potatoes.

Solanum robinsonianum is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its attractive foliage and fruit.
