Senecio alliariifolius

Senecio alliariifolius, commonly known as the garlic leaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Senecio alliariifolius is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
