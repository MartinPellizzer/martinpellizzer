Sobralia aspera

Sobralia aspera, commonly known as the rough sobralia, is a striking orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of vibrant, orange-red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features lance-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly rough texture, giving it its common name. Sobralia aspera thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
