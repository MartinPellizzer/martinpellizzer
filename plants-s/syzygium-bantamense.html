Syzygium bantamense

Syzygium bantamense, commonly known as the Bantam apple or Java apple, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves and fragrant white flowers. The fruit is a small, round, yellowish-green berry with a sweet and aromatic flavor. It is often cultivated for its edible fruit and ornamental value.

The plant thrives in humid climates and is commonly found in coastal regions and lowland forests.
