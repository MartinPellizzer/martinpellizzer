Sempervivum × schnittspahnii

Sempervivum × schnittspahnii, commonly known as the Schnittspahnii houseleek, is a hybrid succulent plant belonging to the Crassulaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming growth habit and fleshy, often blue- or silver-colored leaves. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It is native to the Alpine regions of Europe and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
