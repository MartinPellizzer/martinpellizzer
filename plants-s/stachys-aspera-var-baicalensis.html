Stachys aspera var. baicalensis

Stachys aspera var. baicalensis, commonly known as the Siberian hedge-nettle, is a perennial herb native to the temperate regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, two-lipped flowers in shades of purple or pink. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges or stream banks.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
