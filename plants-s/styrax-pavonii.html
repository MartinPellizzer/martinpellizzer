Styrax pavonii

Styrax pavonii, commonly known as the Chinese frankincense tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by dark red, waxy berries. The plant produces a resinous sap that has been historically used in traditional medicine and as a fragrance. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a glossy appearance.

Styrax pavonii thrives in well-drained soil and is often found in forested areas.
