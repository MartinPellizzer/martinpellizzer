Senecio lenensis

Senecio lenensis, commonly known as the Lenensis groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are usually linear or oblanceolate, and it produces small, yellowish flowers.

Senecio lenensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its compact growth habit and distinctive foliage.
