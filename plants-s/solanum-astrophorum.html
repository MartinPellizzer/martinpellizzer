Solanum astrophorum

Solanum astrophorum, commonly known as the starfruit nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant produces striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue. Its fruits are small, yellow berries that contain a high concentration of alkaloids.

Due to its toxic properties, it is not suitable for human consumption.
