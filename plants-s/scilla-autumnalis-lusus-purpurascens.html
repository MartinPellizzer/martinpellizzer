Scilla autumnalis lusus purpurascens

Scilla autumnalis lusus purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-flowered squill, is a perennial bulbous plant native to southern Europe.

It features striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge from a bulbous base. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
