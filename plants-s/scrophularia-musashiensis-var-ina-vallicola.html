Scrophularia musashiensis var. ina-vallicola

Scrophularia musashiensis var. ina-vallicola, commonly known as the Ina Valley Scrophularia, is a rare flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Scrophulariaceae family, characterized by its showy, often two-lipped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountainous regions and forested areas. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged oppositely along the stem.
