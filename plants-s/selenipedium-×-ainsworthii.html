Selenipedium × ainsworthii

Selenipedium × ainsworthii, commonly known as the hybrid giant spider-orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to the tropical rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, showy flowers that resemble spiders, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and requires specific conditions to thrive. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with intricate patterns, and it produces a single, elongated inflorescence.

Due to its rarity and habitat specificity, it is considered an endangered species in its native range.
