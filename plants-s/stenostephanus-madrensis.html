Stenostephanus madrensis

Stenostephanus madrensis, commonly known as the Mexican sunflower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange to yellow flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and chrysanthemums. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in dry, rocky habitats.
