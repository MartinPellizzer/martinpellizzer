Sondera zonaria

Sondera zonaria, commonly known as the zebra orchid, is a flowering plant native to the eastern regions of Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, zebra-like pattern on its leaves. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow, with a distinctive three-lobed structure.

Sondera zonaria is a perennial herb that is often found in open woodlands and heathlands.
