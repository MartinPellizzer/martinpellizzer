Stenostephanus azureus

Stenostephanus azureus, commonly known as the Azure Starflower, is a strikingly beautiful plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant blue and purple flowers that bloom in star-like clusters, attracting a variety of pollinators. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with narrow, grass-like leaves that help it conserve water in its harsh environment. Its flowers are typically arranged in dense inflorescences, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Stenostephanus azureus is well-suited for xeriscaping and requires minimal water once established.
