Sideritis microstegia

Sideritis microstegia, commonly known as the small-stemmed ironwort, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features slender, upright stems covered with fine hairs and produces small, pale blue or violet flowers. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slight pubescence.

Sideritis microstegia is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
