Stenophyllus capillaris

Stenophyllus capillaris, commonly known as the slender feather grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically less than 1 centimeter in width. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are arranged in spikelets, which are clustered along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and rocky areas.

Its delicate appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in suitable climates.
