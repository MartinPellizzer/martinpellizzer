Stellaria boraeana

Stellaria boraeana, commonly known as the Arctic star, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to the Arctic and alpine regions.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically has slender stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are adapted to cold climates. It thrives in well-drained, rocky or sandy soils and is often found in tundra and mountainous environments.

Stellaria boraeana plays a role in stabilizing soil in harsh environments and is an indicator species for Arctic ecosystems.
