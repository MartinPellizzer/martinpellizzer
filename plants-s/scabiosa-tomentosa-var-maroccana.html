Scabiosa tomentosa var. maroccana

Scabiosa tomentosa var. maroccana, commonly known as the Moroccan hedgehog flower, is a perennial herb native to North Africa.

It features dense clusters of small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has hairy, gray-green foliage that gives it a distinctive, fuzzy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a valuable addition to both gardens and wildflower landscapes.
