Staehelina arborescens

Staehelina arborescens, commonly known as the tree hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant features large, showy flowers with five petals, often in shades of yellow, orange, or red. Its leaves are ovate and have a smooth, waxy surface, while the stems are woody and branching.

Staehelina arborescens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
