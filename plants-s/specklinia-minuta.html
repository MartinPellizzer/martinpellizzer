Specklinia minuta

Specklinia minuta, commonly known as the miniature fern, is a small, epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on tree trunks or branches in humid, shaded environments. The plant has delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually in shades of white or pale green.

Specklinia minuta is valued in horticulture for its compact size and ornamental appeal.
