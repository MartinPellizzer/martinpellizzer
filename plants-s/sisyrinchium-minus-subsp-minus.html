Sisyrinchium minus subsp. minus

Sisyrinchium minus subsp.

minus, commonly known as the lesser blue-eyed grass, is a low-growing perennial herb native to North America. It features slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, blue to violet flowers with yellow centers.
