Senecio evacoides

Senecio evacoides, commonly known as the African Daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers that resemble daisies. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and thrives in well-drained soil. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and drought tolerance.

Senecio evacoides is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
