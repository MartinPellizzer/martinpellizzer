Senecio neaei

Senecio neaei, commonly known as the Neaei groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky, arid environments and has small, narrow leaves. Its yellow flowers bloom in late spring and are followed by small, spherical seed heads.

Senecio neaei is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact growth and unique appearance.
