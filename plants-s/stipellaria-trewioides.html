Stipellaria trewioides

Stipellaria trewioides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved stipellaria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, linear leaves that are often slightly hairy. Its flowers are small and white, clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences.

It is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to arid or semi-arid conditions.
