Swainsona greyana var. bracteata

Swainsona greyana var. bracteata, commonly known as the bracteate swainsona, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It features compound leaves with oval to lance-shaped leaflets, and its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in clusters. The plant produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

It is often found in dry, open habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
