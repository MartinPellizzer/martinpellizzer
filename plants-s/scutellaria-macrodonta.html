Scutellaria macrodonta

Scutellaria macrodonta, commonly known as the large-leaved skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture and produces small, blue to purple flowers in whorls along the stems. It thrives in moist, wooded habitats and is often found in the understory of forests.

Scutellaria macrodonta is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
