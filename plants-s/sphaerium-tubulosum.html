Sphaerium tubulosum

Sphaerium tubulosum, commonly known as the tubular snailweed, is a species of aquatic plant native to freshwater environments in Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, tubular leaves that are typically submerged or floating. The plant has small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually borne in clusters near the water surface. Its root system helps stabilize sediment and improve water quality.

Sphaerium tubulosum plays a role in providing habitat for aquatic invertebrates and is often found in slow-moving streams and ponds.
