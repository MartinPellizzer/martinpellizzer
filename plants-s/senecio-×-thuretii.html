Senecio × thuretii

Senecio × thuretii, commonly known as the hybrid Senecio, is a perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored, composite flowers. The plant typically features narrow, linear leaves and can grow in a variety of forms, including shrubs or subshrub structures. Senecio × thuretii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
