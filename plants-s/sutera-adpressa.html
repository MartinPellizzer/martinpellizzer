Sutera adpressa

Sutera adpressa, commonly known as the carpetweed, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters, often throughout the growing season. The plant has prostrate stems that spread along the ground, forming a dense mat. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with a slightly hairy texture.

Sutera adpressa is often found in moist, shaded areas such as woodlands and stream banks.
