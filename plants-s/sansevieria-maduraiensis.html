Sansevieria maduraiensis

Sansevieria maduraiensis, commonly known as the Madurai snake plant, is a species of succulent native to southern India.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-like leaves that are typically green with distinctive yellow or white margins. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water, which is a common feature among succulents.

Sansevieria maduraiensis is also noted for its air-purifying properties, contributing to improved indoor air quality.
