Sorbus Pluripinnata

Sorbus pluripinnata is a rare and striking member of the Rosaceae family known for its unique foliage and ornamental value. Native to the mountainous regions of southern China, this plant thrives in cool, humid climates with well-drained soil. Its leaves are deeply lobed and often display a striking variegation of green and white, making it a standout in any landscape. While not widely cultivated, it has gained attention among horticulturists for its aesthetic appeal and ecological benefits. This plant exemplifies the diversity and beauty found within the Sorbus genus.
