Strumaria gigantea

Strumaria gigantea, commonly known as the giant strumaria, is a striking member of the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom in summer. The plant features strap-like, grassy leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive, narrow corolla that gives it its common name.

Strumaria gigantea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
