Sideritis macrostachyos

Sideritis macrostachyos, commonly known as the large-stalked ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its slender stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

Sideritis macrostachyos is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
