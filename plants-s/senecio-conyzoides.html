Senecio conyzoides

Senecio conyzoides, commonly known as the African dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in open grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant features alternate, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that develop into fluffy, white seed heads. It has a fibrous root system and is adapted to arid conditions.

Senecio conyzoides is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
