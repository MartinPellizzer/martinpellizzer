Scutellaria longituba

Scutellaria longituba, commonly known as the long-tube skullcap, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and opposite leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are typically blue to purple in color, with a distinctive two-lipped structure.

Scutellaria longituba is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.
