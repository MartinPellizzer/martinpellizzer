Senecio schumannianus

Senecio schumannianus, commonly known as Schumann's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils. The plant features basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. Its yellow flowers are arranged in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family.

It is often found in open, sunny habitats and is adapted to arid conditions.
