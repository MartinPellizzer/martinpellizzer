Siparuna guianensis var. nitens

Siparuna guianensis var. nitens, commonly known as the glossy siparuna, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, glossy dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white flowers that bloom in the summer months. These flowers are followed by bright red berries that contain seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and unique flowering display.
