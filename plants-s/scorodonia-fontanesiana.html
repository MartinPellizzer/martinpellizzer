Scorodonia fontanesiana

Scorodonia fontanesiana, commonly known as the Fontanesian lily, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, showcasing adaptations to its arid environment. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it has a fibrous root system that helps it retain moisture.
