Senecio tchihatchewii

Senecio tchihatchewii, commonly known as the Tchihatchew's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in compact clusters. The plant has hairy, often silver-gray leaves that help reduce water loss in its arid environment.
