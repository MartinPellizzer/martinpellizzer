Solanum mankiense

Solanum mankiense, commonly known as the Manki nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a rough texture.

The flowers are usually white or pale blue, and the fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds.
