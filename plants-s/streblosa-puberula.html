Streblosa puberula

Streblosa puberula, commonly known as the hairy streblosa, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Streblosaceae.

It is characterized by its small, hairy leaves and clusters of tiny, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant is native to certain regions of Southeast Asia, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

Streblosa puberula is relatively rare and has limited distribution in the wild.
