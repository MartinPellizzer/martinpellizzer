Syagrus guimaraesensis

Syagrus guimaraesensis, commonly known as the Guimaraes palm, is a species of palm native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of pinnate leaves that are typically bright green. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with both male and female flowers, making it a dioecious species. Its fruits are drupes that mature to a reddish color, containing a single seed.

Syagrus guimaraesensis is often found in coastal and inland forested areas, where it contributes to the local biodiversity.
