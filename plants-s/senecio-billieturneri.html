Senecio billieturneri

Senecio billieturneri, commonly known as the Desert Candelabra, is a striking succulent native to South Africa.

It features a branched stem that can grow up to 3 meters tall, with dense clusters of blue-green, fleshy leaves. The plant produces showy yellow flowers in summer, which attract pollinators like bees and birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid climates.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
