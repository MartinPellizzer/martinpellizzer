Swertia stapfii

Swertia stapfii, commonly known as Stapf's gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments at higher elevations. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often covered with fine hairs.

Swertia stapfii is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
