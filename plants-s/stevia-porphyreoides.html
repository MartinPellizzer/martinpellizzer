Stevia porphyreoides

Stevia porphyreoides, commonly known as red stevia, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its vibrant red flowers and lance-shaped leaves. The plant is known for its high concentration of steviol glycosides, which are natural sweeteners. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for both garden cultivation and ornamental purposes.

Its unique coloration and medicinal properties have made it a popular choice in herbal remedies and culinary applications.
