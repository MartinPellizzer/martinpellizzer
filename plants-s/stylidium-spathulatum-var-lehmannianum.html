Stylidium spathulatum var. lehmannianum

Stylidium spathulatum var. lehmannianum, commonly known as the Lehmann's spurred orchid, is a small, herbaceous plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, spurred flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid regions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white with a distinctive spur.

This variety is valued for its unique morphology and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
