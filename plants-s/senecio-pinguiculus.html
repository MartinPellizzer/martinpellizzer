Senecio pinguiculus

Senecio pinguiculus, commonly known as the dwarf ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers during the summer months. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact size and drought tolerance.

Its botanical classification places it in the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
