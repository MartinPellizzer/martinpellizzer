Scabiosa tomentosa var. fallax

Scabiosa tomentosa var. fallax, commonly known as the Woolly Scabious or Autumn Scabious, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features a basal rosette of soft, hairy, gray-green leaves that are covered in fine white pubescence. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer to autumn. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a hardy addition to gardens.

Its unique appearance and extended blooming period make it a popular choice for attracting pollinators.
