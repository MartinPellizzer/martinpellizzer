Sutera rotundifolia

Sutera rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leaved sutera, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its rounded, waxy leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their mild sedative properties.

Sutera rotundifolia is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
