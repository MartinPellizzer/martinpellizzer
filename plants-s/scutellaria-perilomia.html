Scutellaria perilomia

Scutellaria perilomia, commonly known as the blue skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square, hairy stems and opposite, ovate leaves. Its small, tubular blue or violet flowers bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Scutellaria perilomia is often found in moist, woodland habitats and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal potential.
