Styrax annamensis

Styrax annamensis, commonly known as Annam styrax, is a species of flowering plant in the family Styracaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Vietnam and Laos, where it grows in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. Its bark and flowers have been used in traditional medicine for their aromatic properties.

Styrax annamensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitats.
