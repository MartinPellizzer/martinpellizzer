Selas lanceolatum

Selas lanceolatum, commonly known as the lanceleaf selas, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Campanulaceae.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. The plant is native to certain regions of South America, particularly in the Andean foothills. Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate appearance and seasonal blooming period.
