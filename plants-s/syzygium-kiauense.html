Syzygium kiauense

Syzygium kiauense, commonly known as the Kiau tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and fragrant white flowers. The tree produces small, round fruits that are typically red or yellow when ripe. It thrives in humid climates and is often found in lowland forests.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and has traditional uses in local cultures.
