Stelis propinqua

Stelis propinqua, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that grows on trees in tropical rainforests. The plant has a compact growth habit with short, wiry stems and small, linear leaves. Its flowers are typically white or pale green, with a delicate, fragrant aroma.

Stelis propinqua is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
