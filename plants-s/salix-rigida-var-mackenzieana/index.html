Salix Rigida Var. Mackenzieana

Salix rigida var. mackenzieana is a hardy shrub native to arctic and alpine regions of north america. it thrives in harsh environments with poor soil and extreme cold. this variant is known for its dense growth and ability to stabilize soil in sensitive ecosystems. its small, oval leaves and catkins make it a distinctive member of the willow family. it plays an important role in supporting local wildlife and preserving fragile habitats.
