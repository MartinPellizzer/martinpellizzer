Sedum sediforme var. brevirostratum

Sedum sediforme var. brevirostratum, commonly known as the narrow-beaked stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Europe.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, clustered leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
