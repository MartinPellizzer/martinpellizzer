Salacia leonensis var. cucumerella

Salacia leonensis var. cucumerella, commonly known as the cucumber tree, is a species native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Salicaceae family, characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces small, cucumber-like fruits that are edible and have a mild, sweet flavor. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its unique appearance and edible fruits, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
