Solanum sandwichense

Solanum sandwichense, commonly known as the Sandwich Islands tomato, is a species native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a herbaceous plant with hairy stems and alternate, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into red, fleshy berries. It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is adapted to the tropical climate of Hawaii.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
