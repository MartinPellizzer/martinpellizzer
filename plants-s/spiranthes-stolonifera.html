Spiranthes stolonifera

Spiranthes stolonifera, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive spiral arrangement of flowers along the stem. The plant typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and other moist habitats, thriving in partial to full shade. Its leaves are grass-like and linear, while its flowers are small, white or greenish-white, and arranged in a tight spiral.

The species is known for its stoloniferous growth habit, which allows it to spread vegetatively through runners.
