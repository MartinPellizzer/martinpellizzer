Specklinia angustilabia

Specklinia angustilabia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. It has a short rhizome and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a unique and attractive addition to orchid collections.
