Solanum missimense

Solanum missimense, commonly known as the Missimense nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is native to parts of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically purple or violet in color. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Solanum missimense is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage and floral display.
