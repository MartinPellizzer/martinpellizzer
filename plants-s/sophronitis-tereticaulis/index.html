Sophronitis Tereticaulis

Sophronitis tereticaulis is a rare and fascinating orchid native to the cloud forests of South America. This delicate plant is known for its unique, elongated green leaves and striking white flowers with a subtle purple hue. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific conditions to flourish. Due to its limited habitat and threats from deforestation, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival for future generations.
