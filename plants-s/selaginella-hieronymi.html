Selaginella hieronymi

Selaginella hieronymi, commonly known as the Hieronymi's spikemoss, is a species of spore-bearing plant belonging to the family Selaginellaceae.

It is a small, evergreen plant that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features a creeping stem with whorled leaves that are often needle-like in shape. It produces microspores and megaspores on separate cones, which is a characteristic feature of the spore plants.

Selaginella hieronymi is known for its resilience and ability to survive in nutrient-poor soils.
