Schoenus festucoides

Schoenus festucoides, commonly known as silvergrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically silver-gray in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It is often found in moist, sandy soils along coastal areas and estuaries.

Schoenus festucoides is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
