Simsia canescens

Simsia canescens, commonly known as the silverleaf sunflower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and bright yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in open areas such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has a taproot system and produces small, wind-dispersed seeds.

Simsia canescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
