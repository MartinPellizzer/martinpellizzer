Silene willdenowii subsp. marginata

Silene willdenowii subsp.

marginata, commonly known as the marginated catchfly, is a herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its pink to purple flowers and linear, hairy leaves.

The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky slopes and grasslands. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. Silene willdenowii subsp.

marginata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.
