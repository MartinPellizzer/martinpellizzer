Sonchus integrifolius var. schlechteri

Sonchus integrifolius var. schlechteri, commonly known as the Schlechter's leaf lettuce, is a variant of the common sowthistle.

It is a herbaceous plant with a rosette of broad, lobed, and often hairy leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in a composite inflorescence, typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural fields and along roadsides.

Its rapid growth and invasive potential make it a common weed in various regions.
