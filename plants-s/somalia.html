Somalia

Somalia, commonly known as the "Land of the Somali," is a country located in the Horn of Africa, renowned for its diverse and unique botanical landscape.

The region features a variety of plant species adapted to its arid and semi-arid climate, including acacia trees, frankincense, and myrrh, which have been historically significant for their economic and medicinal value. The coastal areas support mangrove forests, while the interior is characterized by savannas and shrublands. Somalia's flora also includes several endemic species, contributing to its ecological uniqueness.

However, the country faces challenges such as desertification and deforestation, which threaten its botanical diversity.
