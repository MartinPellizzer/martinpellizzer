Suaeda densiflora

Suaeda densiflora, commonly known as coastal saltbush, is a halophyte plant adapted to saline environments.

It thrives in coastal areas, salt marshes, and other arid or semi-arid regions with high salinity. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that store water and reduce transpiration. Its small, greenish flowers are typically arranged in clusters and bloom in the spring.

Suaeda densiflora plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for coastal wildlife.
