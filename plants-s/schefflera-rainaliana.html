Schefflera rainaliana

Schefflera rainaliana, commonly known as the Rain Island umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of large, leathery, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Schefflera rainaliana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor environments.
