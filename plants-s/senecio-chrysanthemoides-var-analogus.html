Senecio chrysanthemoides var. analogus

Senecio chrysanthemoides var. analogus, commonly known as the African Daisy, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features bright yellow ray flowers surrounding a central disk, giving it a daisy-like appearance. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its flowering period occurs in spring and summer, adding vibrant color to gardens.
