Saxifraga sheqilaensis

Saxifraga sheqilaensis, commonly known as the Sheqila saxifrage, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Eastern Mediterranean.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. It is adapted to cold and dry conditions, often found in alpine meadows and mountain slopes.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
