Sericographis cordifolia

Sericographis cordifolia, commonly known as the heartleaf sericographis, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding ornamental value. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Sericographis cordifolia is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
