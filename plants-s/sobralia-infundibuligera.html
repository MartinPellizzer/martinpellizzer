Sobralia infundibuligera

Sobralia infundibuligera, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a species of flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the family Cactaceae and is characterized by its thick, segmented stems that store water. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically during the holiday season. Its leaves are usually arranged in a zigzag pattern along the stems.

Sobralia infundibuligera is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ability to thrive in indoor conditions.
