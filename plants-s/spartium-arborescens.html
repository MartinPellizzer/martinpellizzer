Spartium arborescens

Spartium arborescens, commonly known as tree spartium, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its woody stems and small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has serrated leaves and produces small, three-lobed fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Spartium arborescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
