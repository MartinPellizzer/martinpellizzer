Senecio cymbalarioides var. borealis

Senecio cymbalarioides var. borealis, commonly known as the Arctic groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It is a low-growing perennial that forms dense mats, often found in rocky or sandy soils. The plant has small, yellowish-green leaves and produces daisy-like flowers with yellow petals and a central disk. It is adapted to cold climates and can survive in harsh, wind-swept environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important component of tundra ecosystems.
