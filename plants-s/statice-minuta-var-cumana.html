Statice minuta var. cumana

Statice minuta var. cumana, commonly known as dwarf statice, is a compact annual plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, delicate flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, often resembling miniature chrysanthemums. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It is often used in floral arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms and ornamental value.

Statice minuta var. cumana is drought-tolerant and suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.
