Stapelia × micrantha

Stapelia × micrantha, commonly known as the starry stapeliad, is a hybrid succulent native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces striking, star-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a strong, carrion-like odor to attract pollinators. Its leaves are typically small and rounded, and it is often cultivated for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.

Stapelia × micrantha is a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts due to its resilience and ornamental value.
