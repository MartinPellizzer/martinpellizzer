Salvia japonica var. fortunei

Salvia japonica var. fortunei, commonly known as Chinese sage, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features upright, branching stems covered in dense, velvety foliage. The plant produces striking violet to purple flowers in whorled clusters, which bloom during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value and adaptability contribute to its widespread cultivation in temperate regions.
