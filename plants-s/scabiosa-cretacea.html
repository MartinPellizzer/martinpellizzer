Scabiosa cretacea

Scabiosa cretacea, commonly known as the Crete scabious, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or dry soils, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. The plant features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of small, pink or white flowers with five petals. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Scabiosa cretacea is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators.
