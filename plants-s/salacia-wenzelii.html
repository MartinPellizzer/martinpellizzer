Salacia wenzelii

Salacia wenzelii, commonly known as the Wenzel's salacia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Salicaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically greenish-yellow with four petals and are borne in clusters.

Salacia wenzelii is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its delicate appearance and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
