Scrophularia olgae

Scrophularia olgae, commonly known as the Olgan figwort, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky soils and sunny habitats. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple in color, arranged in clusters along the stems. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Scrophularia olgae is part of the Scrophulariaceae family, known for its diverse and often ornamental species.
