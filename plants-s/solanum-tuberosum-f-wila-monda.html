Solanum tuberosum f. wila-monda

Solanum tuberosum f. wila-monda, commonly known as the Wila-Monda potato, is a botanical variant of the common potato characterized by its unique morphological features.

This cultivar exhibits a distinct leaf shape and a higher concentration of alkaloids compared to standard potato varieties. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. The tubers of this variety are typically smaller and have a slightly different skin texture.

Botanically, it is classified under the Solanaceae family, sharing traits with other nightshade plants.
