Scutellaria albida f. perhispida

Scutellaria albida f. perhispida, commonly known as the white skullcap.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States. The plant features opposite, lanceolate leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs. Its flowers are tubular and typically white or pale blue, with a distinctive two-lipped structure.

The species belongs to the mint family (Lamiaceae), characterized by aromatic foliage and showy flowers.
