Sansevieria sulcata

Sansevieria sulcata, commonly known as the African spear or snake plant, is a succulent plant native to Africa.

It features long, narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are dark green with light green longitudinal stripes. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering. Its leaves are adapted to store water, making it highly drought-tolerant.

Sansevieria sulcata is also noted for its air-purifying qualities, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
