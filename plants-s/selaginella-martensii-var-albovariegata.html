Selaginella martensii var. albovariegata

Selaginella martensii var. albovariegata, commonly known as the variegated spikemoss, is a member of the Selaginellaceae family and is native to parts of Asia.

It is a small, evergreen fern-like plant that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features delicate, needle-like leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. It is characterized by its ability to reproduce through spores and its unique ability to survive desiccation.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in horticulture due to its attractive foliage.
