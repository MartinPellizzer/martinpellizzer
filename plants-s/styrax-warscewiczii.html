Styrax warscewiczii

Styrax warscewiczii, commonly known as the Chinese fringed styrax, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white or pale pink flowers that appear in clusters, often with a distinctive fringed appearance. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It produces small, woody capsules that contain aromatic seeds.

Styrax warscewiczii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its showy blooms.
