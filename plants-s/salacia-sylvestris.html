Salacia sylvestris

Salacia sylvestris, commonly known as the wild salacia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Salaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, greenish-white blooms that are often clustered in terminal inflorescences. Its leaves are typically elliptical with a smooth surface and a slightly waxy texture.

Salacia sylvestris is valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
