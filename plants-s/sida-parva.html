Sida parva

Sida parva, commonly known as smallleaf sida, is a small annual herb native to the Americas.

It typically grows in dry, disturbed soils and is often found in grasslands and along roadsides. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. Its small, pale yellow flowers are clustered in loose inflorescences, and it produces small, round seeds.

Sida parva is known for its adaptability to various environmental conditions.
