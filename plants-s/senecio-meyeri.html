Senecio meyeri

Senecio meyeri, commonly known as the Blue Echeveria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, blue-gray leaves that store water, aiding in its survival in arid conditions. The plant produces striking spikes of bright yellow flowers during the summer months. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.

Senecio meyeri thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.
