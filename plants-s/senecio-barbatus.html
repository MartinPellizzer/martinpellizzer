Senecio barbatus

Senecio barbatus, commonly known as the blue ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-gray leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
