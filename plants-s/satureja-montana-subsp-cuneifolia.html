Satureja montana subsp. cuneifolia

Satureja montana subsp.

cuneifolia, commonly known as alpine savory, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia. It typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils and full sun.

The plant features aromatic, narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, pink to purple flowers appear in terminal clusters during the summer months. Satureja montana subsp.

cuneifolia is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
