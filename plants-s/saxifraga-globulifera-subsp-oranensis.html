Saxifraga globulifera subsp. oranensis

Saxifraga globulifera subsp.

oranensis, commonly known as the Algerian saxifrage, is a perennial herb native to the rocky areas of Algeria. It features small, clustered leaves that are typically succulent and adapted to arid conditions.

The plant produces delicate, pink or white flowers that bloom in spring, adding a subtle ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a desirable plant for xeriscaping.
