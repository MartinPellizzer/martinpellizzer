Scutellaria jishouensis

Scutellaria jishouensis, commonly known as the Jishou skullcap, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and unique morphology, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
