Stellaria alsine var. phaenopetala

Stellaria alsine var. phaenopetala, commonly known as the showy sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a distinctive star-like appearance. The plant has slender, branching stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or alpine environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in ecological studies and horticultural gardens.
