Synostemon umbrosus

Synostemon umbrosus, commonly known as the shadowy synostemon, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage and clusters of small, fragrant white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a leathery texture.

Synostemon umbrosus thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
