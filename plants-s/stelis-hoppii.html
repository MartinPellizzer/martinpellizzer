Stelis hoppii

Stelis hoppii, commonly known as the Hoppi's orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a short, wiry stem with clustered, lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often arranged in a raceme. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining potting media.

Stelis hoppii is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
