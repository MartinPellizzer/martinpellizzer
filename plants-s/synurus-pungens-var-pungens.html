Synurus pungens var. pungens

Synurus pungens var. pungens, commonly known as the spiny synurus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Africa.

It features upright, branching stems that are covered in sharp, spiny projections, giving it a distinctive appearance. The leaves are narrow, lance-shaped, and often have a hairy texture. It produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in the spring.

This plant is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
