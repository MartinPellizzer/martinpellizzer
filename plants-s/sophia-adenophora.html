Sophia adenophora

Sophia adenophora, commonly known as the woolly sophia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, white, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens.
