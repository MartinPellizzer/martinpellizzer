Spartium ellipticum

Spartium ellipticum, commonly known as narrowleaf sicklevetch, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, elliptical leaves and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in spikes. The plant is characterized by its stout, spiny stems and is often found in dry, rocky habitats. It plays a role in soil stabilization and is used in some traditional herbal remedies.

Spartium ellipticum is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, benefiting surrounding vegetation.
