Senna rostrata

Senna rostrata, commonly known as the yellow senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters tall, characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow-orange flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. Senna rostrata is known for its ability to thrive in well-drained soils and full sun, making it adaptable to various environmental conditions.

It is also valued for its medicinal properties and use in traditional herbal remedies.
