Salvia unicostata

Salvia unicostata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf sage, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It features slender, upright stems and narrow, gray-green leaves with a fuzzy texture. The plant produces spikes of blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Salvia unicostata is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
