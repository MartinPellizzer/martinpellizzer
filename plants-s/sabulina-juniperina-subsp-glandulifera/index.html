Sabulina Juniperina Subsp. Glandulifera

Sabulina juniperina subsp. glandulifera is a rare and intriguing plant species found in specific alpine environments. It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is known for its unique morphological features. This subspecies is characterized by its glandular leaves and small, bell-shaped flowers. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a conservation concern. Understanding its ecology and habitat is crucial for its preservation.
