Spiranthes weirii

Spiranthes weirii, commonly known as the Weir's spiral orchid, is a species of orchid native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its delicate, spiral-shaped inflorescence composed of small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of ponds, where it thrives in moist, acidic soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is often found in shaded environments.

Spiranthes weirii is a rare and endangered species, requiring specific habitat conditions for successful growth and reproduction.
