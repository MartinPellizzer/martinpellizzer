Strychnos decorsei

Strychnos decorsei, commonly known as the golden star tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Loganiaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that resemble stars. The plant has compound leaves with lance-shaped leaflets and produces small, woody fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.
