Serianthes melanesica var. yunckeri

Serianthes melanesica var. yunckeri, commonly known as the Yunkeri ironwood, is a slow-growing, evergreen tree native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and heavy, hard wood that is highly valued for its durability. The plant has a fibrous root system that helps stabilize soil in its native habitat. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically greenish-white in color, and it produces small, woody fruits.

Due to its ecological importance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
