Scabrethia

Scabrethia, commonly known as the rough thistle, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny, rough stems and leaves, which are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellow or purple composite flowers, typical of the daisy family. Scabrethia is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in dry, open habitats.

It is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
