Saxifraga callosa subsp. australis

Saxifraga callosa subsp.

australis, commonly known as the Australian saxifrage, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia. It is a low-growing perennial with fleshy, succulent leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating.

The plant produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

Its botanical features make it well-suited for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
