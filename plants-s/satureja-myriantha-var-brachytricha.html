Satureja myriantha var. brachytricha

Satureja myriantha var. brachytricha, commonly known as narrow-leaved savory, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, lance-shaped leaves that are densely packed along the stems. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink to purple in color, arranged in spikes. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for rocky or dry garden environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable plant for both ornamental and culinary purposes.
