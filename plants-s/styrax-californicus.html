Styrax californicus

Styrax californicus, commonly known as the California styrax, is a deciduous shrub native to the western United States.

It features opposite, elliptical leaves that are dark green and have a slightly pubescent underside. The plant produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers in clusters during late spring to early summer. Its bark is smooth and reddish-brown, often exuding a sticky resin when damaged.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal scrub and chaparral habitats.
