Senecio bacopoides

Senecio bacopoides, commonly known as the silver leaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and small yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often used in rock gardens due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

Senecio bacopoides is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
