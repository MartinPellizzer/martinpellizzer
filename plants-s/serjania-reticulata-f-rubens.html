Serjania reticulata f. rubens

Serjania reticulata f. rubens, commonly known as the red-flowered serjania, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Serjaniaceae and is characterized by its striking red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture.

The flowers are showy and attract various pollinators, contributing to its ecological significance in its native habitat.
