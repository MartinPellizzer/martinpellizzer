Sedum Jahandiezii Var. Battandieri

Sedum jahandiezii var. battandieri is a rare and striking succulent native to the mountainous regions of Iran. This variant is known for its vibrant blue-green foliage and clusters of star-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a sought-after plant among succulent enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively unknown outside of specialized horticultural circles.
