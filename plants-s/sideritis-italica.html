Sideritis italica

Sideritis italica, commonly known as Italian ironwort, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its small, pink or purple flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Sideritis italica is often found in rocky or dry habitats and is valued for its medicinal properties.
