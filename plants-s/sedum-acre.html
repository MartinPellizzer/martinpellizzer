Sedum acre

Sedum acre, commonly known as common stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped yellow flowers in the summer. It is often found growing on rocky or gravelly soils in sunny locations.

Sedum acre is a hardy perennial that is easy to care for and suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.
