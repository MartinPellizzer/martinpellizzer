Scrophularia altaica

Scrophularia altaica, commonly known as the Altai figwort, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or mountainous regions, preferring well-drained soils. The plant features erect stems that can reach up to 1 meter in height, adorned with opposite, ovate leaves. Its flowers are tubular and usually pink or purple, blooming in late summer.

Scrophularia altaica is known for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
