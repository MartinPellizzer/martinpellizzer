Scutellaria tournefortii

Scutellaria tournefortii, commonly known as blue skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing herb with square stems and opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, tubular blue or violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Scutellaria tournefortii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
