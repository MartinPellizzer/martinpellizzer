Spiloxene pusilla

Spiloxene pusilla, commonly known as the small spiloxene, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its delicate, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid environments. It has small, linear leaves and produces tiny, capsule-like fruits that contain its seeds.

Spiloxene pusilla is adapted to withstand drought and is often found in open, sun-exposed areas.
