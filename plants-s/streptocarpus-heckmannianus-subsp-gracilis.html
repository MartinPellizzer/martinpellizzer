Streptocarpus heckmannianus subsp. gracilis

Streptocarpus heckmannianus subsp.

gracilis, commonly known as the slender streptocarpus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and tubular flowers.

The subspecies gracilis features elongated, narrow leaves and produces showy, bell-shaped blooms in shades of violet or purple. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

This plant prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
