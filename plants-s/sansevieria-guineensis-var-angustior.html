Sansevieria guineensis var. angustior

Sansevieria guineensis var. angustior, commonly known as the narrow-leaf African spearplant, is a succulent plant native to West Africa.

It features upright, grass-like leaves that are typically narrow and dark green with a slight sheen. The plant is characterized by its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation. Its leaves often have a subtle variegation pattern, adding visual interest to its appearance.

This variety is prized for its air-purifying qualities and resilience in various environmental conditions.
