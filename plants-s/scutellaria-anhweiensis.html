Scutellaria anhweiensis

Scutellaria anhweiensis, commonly known as the Anhwei skullcap, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, two-lipped flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments in forested areas.

Scutellaria anhweiensis is valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
