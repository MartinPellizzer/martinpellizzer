Scandix balansae

Scandix balansae, commonly known as the Balansae water plant, is a unique aquatic plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, ribbon-like leaves that float on the water's surface, often forming dense mats. The plant has small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, emerging from the water. Scandix balansae thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, preferring nutrient-rich substrates.

Its growth pattern and appearance make it an interesting subject for botanical study and aquatic gardeners alike.
