Sommera

Sommera, commonly known as the summer rose, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Rosaceae.

It is characterized by its showy, often fragrant flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plants typically have simple, alternate leaves and thornless stems, making them suitable for ornamental gardening. Sommera species are native to various regions in Europe and Asia, where they thrive in temperate climates.

They are valued for their aesthetic appeal and are often cultivated in gardens for their vibrant floral displays.
