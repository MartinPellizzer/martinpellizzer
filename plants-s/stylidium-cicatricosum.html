Stylidium cicatricosum

Stylidium cicatricosum, commonly known as the scarred sun-orchid, is a striking member of the orchid family native to Australia.

This plant is characterized by its vivid orange or red flowers, which are often adorned with dark markings. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. The plant's unique feature is its spurred labellum, which is modified to aid in pollination by specific insects.

Stylidium cicatricosum plays an important role in its ecosystem, supporting pollinators and contributing to the biodiversity of its native habitat.
