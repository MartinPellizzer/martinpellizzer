Stelis dimidia

Stelis dimidia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Stelis dimidia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a tropical or subtropical setting.
