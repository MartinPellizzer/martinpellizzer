Smilax oldhamii var. higoensis

Smilax oldhamii var. higoensis, commonly known as the Higoensis Smilax, is a species of vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and the presence of spiny stems. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by bright red berries. Its leaves are typically alternate and have a rough, waxy texture.

This variety is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in shaded or semi-shaded environments.
