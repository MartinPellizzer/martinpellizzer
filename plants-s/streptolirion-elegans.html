Streptolirion elegans

Streptolirion elegans, commonly known as the elegant streptolirion, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of small, delicate flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its unique floral display and compact growth habit make it an attractive addition to both cultivated and natural landscapes.
