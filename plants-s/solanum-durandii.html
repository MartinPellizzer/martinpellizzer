Solanum durandii

Solanum durandii, commonly known as the Durandii tomato, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its vibrant red, orange, and yellow fruits. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are ovate with a hairy texture, and the flowers are usually yellow with a white corolla.

The fruit is edible, though it is less commonly cultivated compared to other tomato species.
