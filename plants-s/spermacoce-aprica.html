Spermacoce aprica

Spermacoce aprica, commonly known as the African star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual that grows up to 30 cm tall, featuring simple, alternate leaves and small white flowers arranged in clusters. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny, smooth seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
