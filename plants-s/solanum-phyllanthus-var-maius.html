Solanum phyllanthus var. maius

Solanum phyllanthus var. maius, commonly known as the giant nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant features large, velvety leaves and produces showy, violet or purple flowers. Its fruits are typically berries that contain toxic alkaloids, making them harmful to humans and animals.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forests or along stream banks.
