Spiloxene curculigoides

Spiloxene curculigoides, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant features a compact, rhizomatous growth habit with fleshy, dark green leaves. Its most distinctive characteristic is its showy, yellow and orange-colored flowers that resemble stars.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
