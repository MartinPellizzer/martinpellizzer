Scutellaria potosina var. tessellata

Scutellaria potosina var. tessellata, commonly known as the tessellated skullcap, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its distinctive, small, blue to purple flowers and serrated leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland areas.

Its unique appearance and medicinal properties make it a notable species in botanical studies.
