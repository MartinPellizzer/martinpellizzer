Sobralia kermesina

Sobralia kermesina, commonly known as the Kermesina orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually brightly colored, with a variety of hues including red, orange, and yellow.

Sobralia kermesina thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
