Stephanomeria hitchcockii

Stephanomeria hitchcockii, commonly known as Hitchcock's globemallow, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its globular shape and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits.

This species is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and slopes.
