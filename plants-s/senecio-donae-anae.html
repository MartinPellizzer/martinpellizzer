Senecio donae-anae

Senecio donae-anae, commonly known as the African Daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces bright yellow to orange ray flowers surrounded by a ring of smaller disk flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Senecio donae-anae is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
