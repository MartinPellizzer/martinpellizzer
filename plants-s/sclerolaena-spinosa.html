Sclerolaena spinosa

Sclerolaena spinosa, commonly known as the spiny sclerolaena, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its woody stems and small, narrow leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland areas.

Sclerolaena spinosa is adapted to arid conditions and is relatively drought-tolerant.
