Solanum swartzianum var. chrysophyllum

Solanum swartzianum var. chrysophyllum, commonly known as the golden star apple, is a member of the Solanaceae family.

It is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of South America. The plant produces yellow-orange fruits that are often used in local cuisines. Its leaves are ovate with serrated edges, and it has hairy stems.

The flowers are typically white or pale green, with five petals.
