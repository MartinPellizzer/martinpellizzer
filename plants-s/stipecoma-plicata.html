Stipecoma plicata

Stipecoma plicata, commonly known as the pleated stipe.

It is a flowering plant native to southern Africa, characterized by its unique pleated leaves that give it its scientific name. The plant produces small, clustered white or yellow flowers, which are typically pollinated by insects. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Stipecoma plicata thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in coastal forests.
