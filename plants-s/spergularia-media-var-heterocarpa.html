Spergularia media var. heterocarpa

Spergularia media var. heterocarpa, commonly known as the narrowleaf sand-verbena, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of the western United States.

Its reproductive strategy involves producing small, wind-dispersed seeds that allow it to colonize new areas effectively.
