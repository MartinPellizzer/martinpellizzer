Syagrus allagopteroides

Syagrus allagopteroides, commonly known as the golden palm, is a species of palm native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by clustered fruits that are edible for some bird species. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in coastal regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
