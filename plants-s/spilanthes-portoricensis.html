Spilanthes portoricensis

Spilanthes portoricensis, commonly known as the Puerto Rican stinkcress, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean island of Puerto Rico.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cress plants, and is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and pungent odor. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks. Spilanthes portoricensis is an important part of the local flora and plays a role in supporting native insect populations.
