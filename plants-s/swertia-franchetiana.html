Swertia franchetiana

Swertia franchetiana, commonly known as the Chinese gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or forested areas at high altitudes, preferring cool and moist environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Swertia franchetiana is valued both for its ornamental beauty and traditional medicinal uses in Chinese herbal medicine.
