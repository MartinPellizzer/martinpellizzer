Suaeda vera

Suaeda vera, commonly known as saltbush, is a halophytic plant adapted to saline environments.

It is characterized by its succulent leaves and small, greenish flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant thrives in arid and coastal regions, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and preventing erosion. Its fleshy stems store water, allowing it to survive in dry conditions.

Suaeda vera is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
