Sonderothamnus speciosus

Sonderothamnus speciosus, commonly known as the showy thamnus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flower heads that are typically arranged in clusters. Its foliage consists of narrow, leathery leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
