Schefflera cabalionii

Schefflera cabalionii, commonly known as the African shield fern, is a tropical evergreen plant native to southern Africa.

It features a distinctive, upright growth habit with a thick, fibrous root system that helps it thrive in well-drained soils. The plant produces large, shield-shaped leaves that are glossy and deeply veined, giving it a striking appearance. It is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.

Schefflera cabalionii prefers bright, indirect light and moderate watering, making it suitable for indoor environments.
