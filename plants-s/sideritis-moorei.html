Sideritis moorei

Sideritis moorei, commonly known as Moore's ironweed, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants such as mint and sage. The plant features hairy, square stems and small, tubular flowers that are typically pale blue or violet in color. Its leaves are often gray-green and have a slightly minty fragrance.

Sideritis moorei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
