Specklinia barbulata

Specklinia barbulata, commonly known as the hairy fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing on trees or rocks in tropical rainforests. Its rhizomes are covered in fine, hairy structures, giving it its common name.

Specklinia barbulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
