Succisa trichotocephala

Succisa trichotocephala, commonly known as the hairy-headed succisa, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous annual with opposite, hairy, ovate leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
