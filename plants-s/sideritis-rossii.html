Sideritis rossii

Sideritis rossii, commonly known as the Greek violet or mountain sainfoin, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant produces small, violet-blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in fine hairs.

Sideritis rossii is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
