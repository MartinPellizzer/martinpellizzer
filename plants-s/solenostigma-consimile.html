Solenostigma consimile

Solenostigma consimile, commonly known as the small-stigma sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant is characterized by its slender, triangular stems and small, clustered inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a fibrous structure typical of many Cyperaceae species.

Solenostigma consimile is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as wetlands and forest understories.
