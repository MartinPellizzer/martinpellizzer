Syagrus weddelliana var. cinereus

Syagrus weddelliana var. cinereus, commonly known as the pink palm, is a striking species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, fan-shaped leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by edible, coconut-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its unique coloration and structure make it a popular choice for tropical gardens and landscapes.
