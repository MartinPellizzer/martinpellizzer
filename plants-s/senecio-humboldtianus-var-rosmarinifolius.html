Senecio humboldtianus var. rosmarinifolius

Senecio humboldtianus var. rosmarinifolius, commonly known as the rosmarinifolius senecio, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features compact, upright growth with fleshy, gray-green leaves that have a distinctive aromatic scent. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
