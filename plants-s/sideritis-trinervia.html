Sideritis trinervia

Sideritis trinervia, commonly known as the three-veined ironwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features slender, erect stems and small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are opposite and have a distinctive three-veined structure, contributing to its scientific name.

Sideritis trinervia is often found in rocky or dry habitats and is valued for its medicinal properties.
