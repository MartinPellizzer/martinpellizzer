Senecio vernalis

Senecio vernalis, commonly known as the spring senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces bright yellow flowers in early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is adapted to dry conditions and can thrive in full sun.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
