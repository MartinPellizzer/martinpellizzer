Syzygium roseum

Syzygium roseum, commonly known as the rose apple or Java apple, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, white or pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, round fruits with a sweet, aromatic flavor, often resembling apples in appearance. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a glossy surface, while its bark is smooth and greyish in color.

Syzygium roseum is valued for its ornamental beauty and culinary uses in various regions.
