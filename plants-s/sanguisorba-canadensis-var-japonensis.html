Sanguisorba canadensis var. japonensis

Sanguisorba canadensis var. japonensis, commonly known as Japanese dropwort, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features upright, branched stems that bear clusters of small, reddish-purple flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and have a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Its ornamental value comes from its striking flower spikes and ability to attract pollinators.
