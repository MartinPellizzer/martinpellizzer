Stipa ilorcitana

Stipa ilorcitana, commonly known as the Ilorci grass, is a perennial grass species native to Central Asia.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as steppes and rocky slopes. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is adapted to arid conditions and has a deep root system for water absorption.

Stipa ilorcitana plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing forage for livestock in its native range.
