Sparganium glomeratum var. natans

Sparganium glomeratum var. natans, commonly known as floating bur-reed, is a water plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its floating stems that bear clusters of spiky, brownish flowers. The plant thrives in shallow, freshwater habitats such as ponds and lakes. Its leaves are narrow and strap-like, often submerged or floating on the water surface.

It plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
