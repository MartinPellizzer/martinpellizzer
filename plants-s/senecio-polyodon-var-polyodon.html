Senecio polyodon var. polyodon

Senecio polyodon var. polyodon, commonly known as the narrow-leaved groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters. It is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
