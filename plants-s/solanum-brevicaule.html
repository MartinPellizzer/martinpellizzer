Solanum brevicaule

Solanum brevicaule, commonly known as the dwarf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by berries that turn from green to red as they mature. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Solanum brevicaule is often found in moist, shaded areas and is part of the nightshade family, Solanaceae.
