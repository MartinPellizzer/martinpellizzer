Solanum phillyreoides

Solanum phillyreoides, commonly known as the purpleleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. Its flowers are small, bell-shaped, and typically white or purple in color.

The fruit is a globose berry that turns red when mature, containing several seeds.
