Sanchezia cyathibractea

Sanchezia cyathibractea, commonly known as the hooded arrowroot, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces inflorescences consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, which adds to its ornamental appeal. Sanchezia cyathibractea thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique foliage and dramatic floral displays make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor cultivation.
