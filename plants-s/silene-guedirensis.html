Silene guedirensis

Silene guedirensis, commonly known as the Guedira catchfly, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of North Africa.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pinkish-purple flowers that bloom in late summer.
