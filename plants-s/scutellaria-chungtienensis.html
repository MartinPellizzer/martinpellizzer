Scutellaria chungtienensis

Scutellaria chungtienensis, commonly known as Chungtien skullcap, is a flowering plant species native to China.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
