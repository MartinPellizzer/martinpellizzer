Saussurea sinuatoides

Saussurea sinuatoides, commonly known as the Chinese yarrow, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

Saussurea sinuatoides is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
