Shivparvatia ciliolata var. pendula

Shivparvatia ciliolata var. pendula, commonly known as the hanging leaf plant, is a variant of the Shivparvatia ciliolata species, characterized by its pendulous growth habit.

It is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of India, where it thrives in cool, moist environments. The plant features delicate, ciliate (fringed) leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often overlooked. Its unique morphology makes it a sought-after ornamental plant in horticulture.
