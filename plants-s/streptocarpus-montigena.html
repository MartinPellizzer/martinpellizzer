Streptocarpus montigena

Streptocarpus montigena, commonly known as the mountain streptocarpus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically produces violet or purple flowers with a distinctive spurred structure at the base. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Streptocarpus montigena is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
