Schuurmansia parviflora

Schuurmansia parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered Schuurmansia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and white, typically blooming in spring. Schuurmansia parviflora is often found in rocky or gravelly soils, thriving in full sun.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
