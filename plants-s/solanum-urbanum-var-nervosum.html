Solanum urbanum var. nervosum

Solanum urbanum var. nervosum, commonly known as the urban nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features slender, branching stems covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are ovate with prominent veins, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into rounded, yellowish berries. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in urban environments, gardens, and along roadsides.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
