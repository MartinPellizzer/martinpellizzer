Stenotus stenophyllus

Stenotus stenophyllus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved stenotus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and white. It is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Stenotus stenophyllus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
