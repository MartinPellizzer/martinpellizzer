Senegalia Giganticarpa

Senegalia giganticarpa is a strikingly tall leguminous tree native to arid regions of Africa and the Middle East. It is known for its impressive height, often reaching over 20 meters, making it one of the tallest acacia species. The tree has long, slender leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its hardy nature allows it to thrive in harsh environments with minimal water. This plant plays a vital role in its ecosystem, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
