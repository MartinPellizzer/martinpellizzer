Sphaerostigma andinum var. hilgardii

Sphaerostigma andinum var. hilgardii, commonly known as the Andean mountain orchid, is a species native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration pattern, often featuring a mix of white, yellow, and green hues. It thrives in cool, humid environments and is typically found in cloud forests and alpine meadows.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
