Solanum demissum f. atrocyaneum

Solanum demissum f. atrocyaneum, commonly known as the dark purple nightshade, is a fascinating botanical specimen belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its striking dark purple to almost black foliage and flowers. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptical in shape, with a velvety texture and a deep, rich hue. Its flowers are small, bell-shaped, and usually appear in clusters, adding to its ornamental appeal.

This cultivar is valued for its unique coloration and is often grown in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
