Senecio sodirscandens

Senecio sodirscandens, commonly known as the Desert Senecio, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb with narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, aiding in water retention. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in a dense, cylindrical inflorescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand high temperatures and low rainfall.

Due to its resilience, it is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
