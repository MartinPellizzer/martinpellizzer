Solanum lycopersicum var. oviforme

Solanum lycopersicum var. oviforme, commonly known as the oval tomato, is a botanical variant of the domesticated tomato.

It is characterized by its elongated, oval-shaped fruits that are typically smaller than standard tomatoes. The plant belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and shares the same genus as other tomato species. Its foliage consists of alternate, heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture.

This variety is often cultivated for its unique fruit shape and can be used in both culinary and ornamental applications.
