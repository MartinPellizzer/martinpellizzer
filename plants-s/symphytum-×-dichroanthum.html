Symphytum × dichroanthum

Symphytum × dichroanthum, commonly known as the hybrid wood betony, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are hairy and often have a bluish-green hue. The plant produces showy, two-toned flowers in shades of purple and white, typically blooming from June to August. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is cultivated in gardens and traditional herbal practices.
