Schnella kunthiana

Schnella kunthiana, commonly known as the desert bell, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is well adapted to drought conditions. Its leaves are usually small and succulent, helping to conserve water.

Schnella kunthiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in xeriscapes and dry gardens.
