Securinega flexuosa

Securinega flexuosa, commonly known as the creeping nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It features woody stems that grow prostrate or ascending, often forming dense mats. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by dark purple to black berries. Its leaves are ovate to elliptic, with a slightly hairy texture.

It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in harsh conditions.
