Scrophularia obtusa

Scrophularia obtusa, commonly known as broad-leaved figwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks. The plant features opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are tubular and usually pink or purple, arranged in clusters at the ends of stems.

Scrophularia obtusa is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
