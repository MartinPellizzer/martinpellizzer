Seseli libanotis subsp. leiocarpum

Seseli libanotis subsp.

leiocarpum, commonly known as the Lebanon sesame or white sesame, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apiaceae family. It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in umbels.

Due to its habitat preferences, it is often found in dry, sunny environments with well-drained soil.
