Sapium integerrimum

Sapium integerrimum, commonly known as the Chinese flowering maple, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its showy, pink to red flowers that appear in clusters during the spring. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Sapium integerrimum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
