Siphocampylus quindioros

Siphocampylus quindioros, commonly known as the Quindio rock orchid, is a rare and endemic orchid species native to the Andean region of Colombia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive floral morphology, featuring showy, lip-like petals. The plant typically thrives in rocky, well-drained soils at high altitudes, often in cloud forest ecosystems. Its flowers are pollinated by specific insects, highlighting its ecological significance within its native habitat.
