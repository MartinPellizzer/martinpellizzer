Symphyotrichum texanum

Symphyotrichum texanum, commonly known as Texas fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It is a member of the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its yellow to orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and roadsides. It has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves, and it blooms from late summer into early fall.

Texas fleabane is often used in native plant gardens due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
