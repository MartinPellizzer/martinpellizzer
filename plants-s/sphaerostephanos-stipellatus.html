Sphaerostephanos stipellatus

Sphaerostephanos stipellatus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved star-fruit, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped flowers, which are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that are edible and have a sweet, tangy flavor. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value and unique fruit, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
