Scutellaria javanica var. javanica

Scutellaria javanica var. javanica, commonly known as Java skullcap, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value and medicinal properties make it a notable species in botanical studies.
