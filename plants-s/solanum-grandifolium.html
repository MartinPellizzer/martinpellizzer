Solanum grandifolium

Solanum grandifolium, commonly known as the large-leaf nightshade, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in warm, humid climates. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, violet to purple flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruits are berries that change from green to yellow or red as they mature.

Solanum grandifolium is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage and floral display.
