Sarcopodium beccarianum

Sarcopodium beccarianum, commonly known as the Beccari's arrowhead, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its arrowhead-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white, followed by spadix-like inflorescences. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread rapidly in suitable moist environments.

It is often found in dense tropical forests where it climbs trees to reach sunlight.
