Sedum daigremontianum var. macrosepalum

Sedum daigremontianum var. macrosepalum, commonly known as the "Cape Hottentot," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems that often branch out, covered in small, rounded leaves. The plant produces vibrant, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, or orange, typically during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

This variety is known for its resilience and ability to withstand drought conditions.
