Staurogyne subrosulata

Staurogyne subrosulata, commonly known as the pink water starwort, is a perennial aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in still or slow-moving freshwater environments such as ponds and marshes. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often submerged, with small, pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters. Its root system helps stabilize sediments, contributing to water clarity.

Staurogyne subrosulata is valued in aquatic gardens for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
