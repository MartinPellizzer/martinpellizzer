Senecio foliolosus

Senecio foliolosus, commonly known as the leafy groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or arid environments. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a head. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Senecio foliolosus is often used in rock gardens due to its hardy nature and ornamental value.
