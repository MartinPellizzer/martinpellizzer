Statice pyramidata

Statice pyramidata, commonly known as the desert statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, spindly stems and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from pink to white. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it well-suited for arid and semi-arid environments. Its foliage is typically narrow and grass-like, contributing to its drought-resistant nature.

Statice pyramidata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and hardy growth habits.
