Stipa ukranensis

Stipa ukranensis, commonly known as the Ukrainian needlegrass, is a perennial grass species native to Eastern Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.

Stipa ukranensis is also known for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions and withstand drought.
