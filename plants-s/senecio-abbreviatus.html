Senecio abbreviatus

Senecio abbreviatus, commonly known as the dwarf ragwort, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and narrow, linear leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. It produces small yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months, and its leaves are often covered in fine hairs.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens.
