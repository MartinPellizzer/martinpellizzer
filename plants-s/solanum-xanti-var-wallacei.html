Solanum xanti var. wallacei

Solanum xanti var. wallacei, commonly known as Wallace's nightshade, is a member of the Solanaceae family.

It is a shrub-like plant native to parts of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia. The plant features small, egg-shaped leaves and produces clusters of violet to purple flowers. Its fruits are typically small berries that turn from green to yellow when mature.

This species is often found in forested areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses.
