Senecio pseudomairei

Senecio pseudomairei, commonly known as the Cape York senecio, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, spiny stems and small, yellowish-green leaves. The plant produces daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange, typically during the warmer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-drained soil and minimal water.

Senecio pseudomairei is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience.
