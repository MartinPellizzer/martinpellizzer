Serjania caracasana f. elliptica

Serjania caracasana f. elliptica, commonly known as the elliptical serjania, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its elliptical-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces woody, elongated fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in forested areas.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in tropical botany.
