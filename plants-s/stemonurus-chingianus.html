Stemonurus chingianus

Stemonurus chingianus, commonly known as the Chinese starwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and streambanks. The plant features a basal rosette of lance-shaped leaves with a hairy surface. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in a spike-like inflorescence.

Stemonurus chingianus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
