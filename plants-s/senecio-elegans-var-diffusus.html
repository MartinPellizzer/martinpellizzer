Senecio elegans var. diffusus

Senecio elegans var. diffusus, commonly known as the African marguerite, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its fuzzy, silver-green foliage and daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers. It produces small, edible seeds that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its spread.

Due to its drought tolerance, it is often cultivated as a low-maintenance ornamental plant.
