Syzygium kosteri

Syzygium kosteri, commonly known as the Koster's apple.

It is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Philippines, characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and showy, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically red or yellow when mature. Syzygium kosteri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
