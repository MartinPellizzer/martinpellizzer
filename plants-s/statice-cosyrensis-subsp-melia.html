Statice cosyrensis subsp. melia

Statice cosyrensis subsp.

melia, commonly known as the coastal statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to purple.

The plant typically thrives in well-drained, sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal environments. Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves that are usually covered in a fine, powdery coating.

This subspecies is valued in horticulture for its drought tolerance and ability to add color to garden landscapes.
