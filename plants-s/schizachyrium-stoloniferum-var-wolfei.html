Schizachyrium stoloniferum var. wolfei

Schizachyrium stoloniferum var. wolfei, commonly known as the eastern woodland panicum, is a perennial grass native to the eastern United States.

It features slender, upright stems that produce fluffy, silver-gray seed heads in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. It thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in wetlands, swamps, and along stream banks.

Its botanical structure makes it well-suited for wetland restoration and ecological landscaping.
