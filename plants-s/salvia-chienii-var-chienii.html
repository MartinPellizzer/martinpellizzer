Salvia chienii var. chienii

Salvia chienii var. chienii, commonly known as Chinese sage, is a perennial herb native to China.

It features square, hairy stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. The plant produces showy, violet-blue flowers in whorled clusters during the summer months. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value and adaptability make it a popular choice among gardeners.
