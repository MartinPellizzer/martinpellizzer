Spathoglottis vanvuurenii

Spathoglottis vanvuurenii, commonly known as the Van Vuuren's lady's slipper, is a rare and striking orchid native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in areas with high humidity and partial shade.
