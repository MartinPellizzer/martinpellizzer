Solanum cyrtopodium

Solanum cyrtopodium, commonly known as the Cyrtopodium nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, such as tropical rainforests. It features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, bell-shaped flowers that bloom at night.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
