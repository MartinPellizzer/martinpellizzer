Styrax pefrit

Styrax pefrit, commonly known as the Chinese frankincense tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the family Styracaceae and is characterized by its fragrant, resinous bark and leaves. The plant produces small, white to pinkish flowers in clusters, followed by ornamental, berry-like fruits. Its bark and leaves are used in traditional medicine for their aromatic and anti-inflammatory properties.

Styrax pefrit is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and cultural significance.
