Stephania cambodica

Stephania cambodica, commonly known as Cambodian stephania, is a climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Menispermaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive yellow flowers. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow berries that contain seeds. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and traditional uses, it is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
