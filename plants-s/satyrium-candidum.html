Satyrium candidum

Satyrium candidum, commonly known as the white marsh orchid, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wet or marshy environments. The plant features a slender stem with a single, showy white or pale pink flower. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, greenish flowers in the early spring.

The orchid is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in swampy or seasonally flooded areas.
