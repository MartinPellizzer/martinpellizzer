Senecio linearifolius var. latifolius

Senecio linearifolius var. latifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves that are broader than the typical variety, giving it a distinct appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

This variant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
