Stelis catenata

Stelis catenata, commonly known as the linked stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically growing on tree trunks or branches. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually small and white or pale pink, arranged in a raceme.

Stelis catenata is valued in horticulture for its delicate appearance and adaptability to indoor cultivation.
