Streptocarpus ionanthus subsp. occidentalis

Streptocarpus ionanthus subsp.

occidentalis, commonly known as the western streptocarpus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and showy, tubular flowers.

The plant produces vibrant, trumpet-shaped blooms in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with contrasting dark markings. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This subspecies is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
