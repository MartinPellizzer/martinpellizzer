Spiraea cantoniensis var. jiangxiensis

Spiraea cantoniensis var. jiangxiensis, commonly known as the Jiangxi spiraea, is a deciduous shrub native to southern China.

It is characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and its showy clusters of small, white to pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are ovate to elliptic, with a smooth margin and a glossy green surface. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a suitable ornamental plant for gardens.

This variety is noted for its compact size and ornamental value, often used in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal.
