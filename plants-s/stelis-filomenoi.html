Stelis filomenoi

Stelis filomenoi, commonly known as the Filomena orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, pendulous flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, and it produces small, fragrant blooms in shades of white and pink.

Stelis filomenoi is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or terrariums.
