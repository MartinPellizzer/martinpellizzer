Silene kremeri

Silene kremeri, commonly known as the Kramer's campion, is a flowering plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of up to 20 centimeters. The plant produces small, pink or white flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Silene kremeri is adapted to cold climates and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
