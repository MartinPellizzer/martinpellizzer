Stachys natalensis var. galpinii

Stachys natalensis var. galpinii, commonly known as Natal hedge nettle, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and pairs of opposite, lance-shaped leaves with hairy surfaces. The plant produces small, clustered spikes of purple to reddish flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ornamental plant in its native habitat.
