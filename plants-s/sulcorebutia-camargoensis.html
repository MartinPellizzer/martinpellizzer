Sulcorebutia camargoensis

Sulcorebutia camargoensis, commonly known as the Camargo's crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped growth habit. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slight blue or purple tint. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the warmer months.

Sulcorebutia camargoensis is well-suited for arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
