Solanum jasminoides subvar. pubinerve

Solanum jasminoides subvar. pubinerve, commonly known as the pubinerve nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by yellow berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
