Saintpaulia goetzeana

Saintpaulia goetzeana, commonly known as the Goetze's African violet, is a species native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, waxy leaves and showy, clustered flowers that come in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in indoor gardening. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for both pots and hanging baskets.
