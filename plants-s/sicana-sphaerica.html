Sicana sphaerica

Sicana sphaerica, commonly known as the globe senna, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its spherical, yellowish fruit that resembles a small globe, hence its common name. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom during the dry season. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three oval-shaped leaflets.

Sicana sphaerica is often found in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments.
