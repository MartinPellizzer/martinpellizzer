Senecio helenitis subsp. arvernensis

Senecio helenitis subsp.

arvernensis, commonly known as the French Senecio, is a member of the Asteraceae family. It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender stems and small, yellowish-green leaves.

The plant produces daisy-like flowers with yellow disc florets surrounded by pale green to white ray florets. Native to southern Europe, it thrives in rocky or gravelly soils in sunny, open habitats.

It is often found in alpine or subalpine regions, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environmental conditions.
