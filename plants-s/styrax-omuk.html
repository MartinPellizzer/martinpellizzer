Styrax omuk

Styrax omuk, commonly known as the Chinese frankincense tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the family Styracaceae and is characterized by its fragrant, resinous bark that exudes a strong, aromatic scent. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers in clusters, which are often followed by dark, leathery fruits. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties and historical use in traditional Chinese medicine.
