Striga thunbergii

Striga thunbergii, commonly known as the yellow striga, is an annual herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, yellowish-green leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or along stream banks.

Striga thunbergii is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
