Silene trichocalycina

Silene trichocalycina, commonly known as the purple fringed catchfly, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its striking, violet-pink flowers with fringed petals. Its botanical features make it a notable species in ecological and horticultural contexts.
