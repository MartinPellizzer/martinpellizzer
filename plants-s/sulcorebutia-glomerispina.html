Sulcorebutia glomerispina

Sulcorebutia glomerispina, commonly known as the clustered spine cactus, is a species native to South America, particularly Argentina and Bolivia.

It is a small, globular cactus with a compact growth habit and typically features a single stem that may branch slightly. The plant has cylindrical, ribbed stems covered in short, white spines, and it produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a favored plant among succulent enthusiasts.
