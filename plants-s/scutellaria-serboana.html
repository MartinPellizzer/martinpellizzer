Scutellaria serboana

Scutellaria serboana, commonly known as Serbian skullcap, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas and has square stems and opposite leaves. Its small, tubular flowers are usually purple or blue, and it produces distinctive, helmet-shaped seed capsules.

It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
