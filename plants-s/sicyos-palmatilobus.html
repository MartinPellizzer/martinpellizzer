Sicyos palmatilobus

Sicyos palmatilobus, commonly known as the broad-lobed gourd, is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that are followed by large, edible, green fruits. Sicyos palmatilobus is native to Central and South America.
