Stellaria groenlandica

Stellaria groenlandica, commonly known as Arctic star, is a small flowering plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its star-shaped white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in cold, harsh environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and arranged in a basal rosette, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Stellaria groenlandica is adapted to survive in low-light conditions and has a short growing season.
