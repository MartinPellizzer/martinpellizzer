Sideritis murgetana

Sideritis murgetana, commonly known as the Murgean ironwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region, particularly found in southern Italy and parts of Greece. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and hairy, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, clustered in whorls, and typically have a pale blue or violet color.

Sideritis murgetana is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its potential health benefits.
