Stemodia muraria

Stemodia muraria, commonly known as the wallstem daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial that thrives in rocky and coastal environments. The plant features wiry, branching stems covered in small, white to pale yellow flowers. Its leaves are narrow, linear, and often covered in fine hairs.

It is well adapted to dry conditions and is often found growing on cliffs and sandy soils.
