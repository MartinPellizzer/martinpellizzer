Salacia chlorantha subsp. dalzielii

Salacia chlorantha subsp.

dalzielii, commonly known as the Dalziel's salacia, is a shrub native to the western regions of India. It belongs to the family Salicaceae and is characterized by its slender, woody stems and opposite, lanceolate leaves.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in racemose inflorescences. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that contain several seeds.

This species is noted for its potential medicinal properties and is often found in moist, shaded forest environments.
