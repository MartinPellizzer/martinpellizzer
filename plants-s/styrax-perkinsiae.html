Styrax perkinsiae

Styrax perkinsiae, commonly known as the Chinese frankincense tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and clusters of fragrant, white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces small, dark red berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans. Its leaves are opposite, oval-shaped, and have a glossy texture.

Styrax perkinsiae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
