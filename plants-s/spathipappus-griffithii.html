Spathipappus griffithii

Spathipappus griffithii, commonly known as the Himalayan spathipappus, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

Spathipappus griffithii is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological significance in alpine ecosystems.
