Strobilanthes acrocephala

Strobilanthes acrocephala, commonly known as the "Cape of Good Hope flower," is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in dense, terminal clusters. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions. It has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it is known for its short blooming period.

Strobilanthes acrocephala is a popular ornamental plant due to its vibrant color and unique floral structure.
