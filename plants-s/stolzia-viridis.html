Stolzia viridis

Stolzia viridis, commonly known as the green stolzia, is a South African plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and trifoliate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in racemes, which are typically borne on long peduncles. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Stolzia viridis is a hardy, drought-tolerant species that is well adapted to the arid conditions of its native environment.
