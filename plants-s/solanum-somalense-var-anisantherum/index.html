Solanum Somalense Var. Anisantherum

Somalense nightshade, scientifically known as Solanum somalense var. anisantherum, is a rare and enigmatic plant native to the arid regions of Somalia. This variant of the nightshade genus is characterized by its striking purple flowers and dark green foliage, which thrive in harsh desert conditions. Despite its beauty, it remains poorly studied due to its remote habitat and limited distribution. The plant holds cultural significance in local communities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine. Its survival is threatened by climate change and habitat degradation, making conservation efforts crucial for its future.
