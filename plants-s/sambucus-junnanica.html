Sambucus junnanica

Sambucus junnanica, commonly known as the Chinese elderberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers. The plant produces dark purple berries that are edible but should be consumed in moderation. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Sambucus junnanica is valued for its ornamental qualities and traditional medicinal uses.
