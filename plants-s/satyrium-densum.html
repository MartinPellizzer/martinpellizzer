Satyrium densum

Satyrium densum, commonly known as the dense satyrium, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact, often densely packed inflorescence. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest undergrowth. Its flowers are usually small and colorful, with a variety of hues including yellow, orange, and red.

Satyrium densum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid, tropical conditions.
