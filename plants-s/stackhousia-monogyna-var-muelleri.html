Stackhousia monogyna var. muelleri

Stackhousia monogyna var. muelleri, commonly known as the Mueller's stackhousia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Proteaceae and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white wax. Its flowers are typically pink to red in color, appearing in small, clustered inflorescences.
