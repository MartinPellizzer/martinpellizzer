Spergularia media var. latifolia

Spergularia media var. latifolia, commonly known as broadleaf sandspurry, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically thrives in sandy or gravelly soils, often found in coastal or arid environments. The plant produces small, pink or white flowers with five petals, which bloom in summer. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to conserve water in dry conditions.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
