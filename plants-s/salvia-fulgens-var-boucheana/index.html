Salvia Fulgens Var. Boucheana

Salvia fulgens var. boucheana is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant known for its vibrant red and orange bracts. Native to the mountainous regions of Mexico, this variety belongs to the mint family and is prized for its ornamental value. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners in warm climates. The plant produces clusters of showy flowers that attract pollinators like bees and hummingbirds. Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a standout addition to any landscape.
