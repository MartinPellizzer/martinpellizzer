Sorghastrum incompletum var. africanum

Sorghastrum incompletum var. africanum, commonly known as African dropseed, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and fine, silver-green foliage. The plant produces small, pale blue to purple flower clusters that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for drought-tolerant landscapes.

Its compact size and attractive appearance make it a popular choice for native plant gardens and erosion control.
