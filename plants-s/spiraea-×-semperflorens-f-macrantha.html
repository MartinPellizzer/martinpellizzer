Spiraea × semperflorens f. macrantha

Spiraea × semperflorens f. macrantha, commonly known as the compact spiraea, is a hybrid shrub valued for its ornamental qualities.

It features dense, upright growth with clusters of small, showy flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant is characterized by its dark green foliage and showy pink or white blossoms that appear in panicles. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.

This cultivar is often used in gardens for its compact size and long-lasting floral display.
