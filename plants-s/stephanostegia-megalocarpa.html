Stephanostegia megalocarpa

Stephanostegia megalocarpa, commonly known as the giant cupuaçu, is a woody shrub native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers that are followed by large, edible fruits resembling a cupuaçu. The fruit has a thick, fibrous outer layer and a sweet, creamy pulp.

It is valued for its unique flavor and is used in various culinary and traditional applications.
