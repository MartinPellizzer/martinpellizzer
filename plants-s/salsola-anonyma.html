Salsola anonyma

Salsola anonyma, commonly known as desert goosefoot, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It is a low-growing shrub or perennial herb with succulent leaves that help it conserve water. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters, which are typically inconspicuous. Its seeds are dispersed by wind, allowing it to colonize disturbed soils and thrive in harsh environments.

Salsola anonyma is adapted to high temperatures and low precipitation, making it a common species in desert ecosystems.
