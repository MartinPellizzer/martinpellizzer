Solanum zelenetzkii

Solanum zelenetzkii, commonly known as the Zelenetzki's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers and red berries that are toxic to humans and animals. It is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in shaded, moist environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it an interesting subject for study in plant taxonomy and ecological research.
