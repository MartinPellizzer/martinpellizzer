Sonchus brassicifolius

Sonchus brassicifolius, commonly known as the curly dock, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa but has been naturalized in many parts of the world. The plant features alternate, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces small yellow flowers arranged in a composite head. It thrives in moist soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

The plant is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
