Styloglossum caulodes

Styloglossum caulodes, commonly known as the red-flowered milkweed, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking red and orange flowers, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. The plant has a milky sap that is toxic and can cause skin irritation. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and have a rough texture.

Styloglossum caulodes thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.
