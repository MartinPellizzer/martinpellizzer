Sarcostemma resiliens

Sarcostemma resiliens, commonly known as the resilient milkweed, is a climbing plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It is adapted to arid environments and has a succulent nature, allowing it to store water efficiently.

Sarcostemma resiliens is often found in rocky areas and is known for its resilience to drought and poor soil conditions.
