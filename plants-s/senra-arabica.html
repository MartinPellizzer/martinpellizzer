Senra arabica

Senra arabica, commonly known as Arabica coffee.

It is a species of the Rubiaceae family, native to East Africa. The plant is an evergreen shrub that can grow up to 10 feet tall, with dark green, oval-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small and white, followed by red berries containing two seeds, which are the coffee beans.
