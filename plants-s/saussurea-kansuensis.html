Saussurea kansuensis

Saussurea kansuensis, commonly known as the Kansu yarrow, is a perennial herb native to the high altitudes of China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems and narrow, gray-green leaves. The plant produces showy, yellow-to-orange flower heads that bloom in late summer. It thrives in arid, rocky environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
