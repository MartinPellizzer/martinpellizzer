Scaphosepalum pleurothallodes

Scaphosepalum pleurothallodes, commonly known as the pleurothallid orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to the high-altitude cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, pendulous inflorescences and showy, often brightly colored flowers. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its unique morphology, including specialized sepals and petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific conditions to flourish.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is often cultivated by enthusiasts and conservationists.
