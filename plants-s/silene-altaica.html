Silene altaica

Silene altaica, commonly known as the Altai catchfly, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or arid environments. The plant features simple, linear leaves and produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. It is adapted to cold climates and has a long flowering period in the summer.

Silene altaica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
