Swainsona laxa

Swainsona laxa, commonly known as the slender swainsona, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Australia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its woody stems and bipinnate leaves. The plant produces yellow to orange flowers in racemes, which bloom during the warmer months. Swainsona laxa is adapted to drought conditions, with deep roots and a low water requirement.
