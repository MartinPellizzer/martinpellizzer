Stelis dolichopus

Stelis dolichopus, commonly known as the long-stemmed stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically arranged in a raceme. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Stelis dolichopus is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
