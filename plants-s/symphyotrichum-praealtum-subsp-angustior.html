Symphyotrichum praealtum subsp. angustior

Symphyotrichum praealtum subsp.

angustior, commonly known as the narrowleaf blueweed, is a flowering plant native to North America. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit.

The plant produces small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a grayish-green hue.

It thrives in dry, open habitats such as prairies and rocky slopes.
