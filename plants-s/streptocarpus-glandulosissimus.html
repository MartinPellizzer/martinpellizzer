Streptocarpus glandulosissimus

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to blue. The plant features hairy, glandular leaves that give it its scientific name, and it typically grows in rocky or sandy soils.
