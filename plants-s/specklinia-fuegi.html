Specklinia fuegi

Specklinia fuegi, commonly known as the Fuegian fern.

It is a species of epiphytic fern native to the southern Andes and Patagonia. The plant has delicate, pinnately divided fronds that are often covered in fine, hairy structures. Its rhizomes are typically short and thick, anchoring it to tree trunks or branches.

Specklinia fuegi thrives in humid, shaded environments, making it a characteristic component of cloud forests.
