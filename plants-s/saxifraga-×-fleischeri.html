Saxifraga × fleischeri

Saxifraga × fleischeri, commonly known as the hybrid saxifrage, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It typically forms dense mats of fleshy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces small, pink or white flowers in late summer, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
