Saussurea aster

Saussurea aster, commonly known as the Chinese yarrow, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet, with hairy, lance-shaped leaves and purple or pinkish flowers. Its root system is fibrous and often used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Saussurea aster thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy perennial in suitable climates.
