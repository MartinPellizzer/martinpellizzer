Salacia rehmannii var. baumii

Salacia rehmannii var. baumii, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year, giving it its common name. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth surface and a slightly waxy texture. Its fruit is a small, fleshy drupe that contains a single seed.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
