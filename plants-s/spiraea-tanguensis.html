Spiraea tanguensis

Spiraea tanguensis, commonly known as the Tanguis spiraea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Rosaceae and is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pink flowers in early spring, which are followed by red berries. Spiraea tanguensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscape design.

It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight.
