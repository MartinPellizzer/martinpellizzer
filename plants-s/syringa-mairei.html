Syringa mairei

Syringa mairei, commonly known as the Chinese lilac, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple in the spring. The plant has dark green, ovate leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Syringa mairei is also valued for its hardiness and ability to withstand urban environments.
