Syngramma alta

Syngramma alta, commonly known as the highland feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and delicate, feathery plumes that give it a graceful appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, contributing to its distinctive texture.

Syngramma alta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic value.
