Senna roemeriana

Senna roemeriana, commonly known as the yellow senna, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with small, oval leaflets and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, flat pods that contain several seeds. It is often found in dry, open habitats and is adapted to thrive in arid conditions.

Senna roemeriana is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some cultures.
