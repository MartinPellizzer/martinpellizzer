Scindapsus decursivus

Scindapsus decursivus, commonly known as the silver vine, is a tropical evergreen climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that often have a silvery or variegated appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Scindapsus decursivus is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
