Schismatoglottis viridissima

Schismatoglottis viridissima, commonly known as the Emerald Orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial with a rhizomatous root system and produces striking, lance-shaped leaves that are often a vibrant green. The plant features small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in a raceme, typical of many orchid species. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

Schismatoglottis viridissima is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its elegant foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
