Senecio × baxteri

Senecio × baxteri, commonly known as the hybrid groundsel, is a perennial flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It produces small, oval-shaped leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Senecio × baxteri is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
