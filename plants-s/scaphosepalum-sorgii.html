Scaphosepalum sorgii

Scaphosepalum sorgii, commonly known as the African orchid, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a short, hairy stem and produces striking, showy flowers with a distinctive hooded labellum. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its flowers are pollinated by insects, particularly bees and flies, which are attracted by their vibrant colors and scent.
