Synostemon pinifolius

Synostemon pinifolius, commonly known as the pineleaf synostemon, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its narrow, needle-like leaves that resemble pine needles, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Synostemon pinifolius is often grown as an ornamental plant for its unique foliage and modest floral display.
