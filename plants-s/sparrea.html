Sparrea

Sparrea, commonly known as the white campion, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, pink or white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas and has a trailing or ascending growth habit. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Sparrea is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
