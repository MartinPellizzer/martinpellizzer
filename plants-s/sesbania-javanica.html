Sesbania javanica

Sesbania javanica, commonly known as the Javan sesban, is a leguminous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces elongated, pod-like fruits containing several seeds. It is often found in moist, tropical environments and is used for forage and soil enrichment.

Its rapid growth makes it a valuable species for ecological restoration projects.
