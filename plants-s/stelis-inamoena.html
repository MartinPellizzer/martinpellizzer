Stelis inamoena

Stelis inamoena, commonly known as the elegant orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a few leaves and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Stelis inamoena is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
