Solanum squamuliferum

Solanum squamuliferum, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-tomentose leaves and small, violet-colored flowers. The plant belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is known for its toxic properties. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

Solanum squamuliferum is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance.
