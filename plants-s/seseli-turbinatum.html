Seseli turbinatum

Seseli turbinatum, commonly known as the Turban Seseli, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers. Native to Central Asia, it thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in mountainous regions. The plant's distinctive turban-shaped inflorescence gives it its common name.

It is valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
