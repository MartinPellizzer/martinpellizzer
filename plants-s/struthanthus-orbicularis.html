Struthanthus orbicularis

Struthanthus orbicularis, commonly known as the roundleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its orbicular, waxy leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually white or pale purple, blooming at night. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils and open habitats.

The species is adapted to withstand drought conditions, making it a hardy plant in desert ecosystems.
