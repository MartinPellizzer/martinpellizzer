Schizoloma walkerae

Schizoloma walkerae, commonly known as the Cape Town rock orchid, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to South Africa.

It typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single or few flowers that are usually pale yellow or greenish-white. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it blooms during the warmer months.

Schizoloma walkerae is valued for its unique appearance and is often cultivated in rock gardens due to its ornamental qualities.
