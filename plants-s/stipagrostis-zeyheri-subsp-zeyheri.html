Stipagrostis zeyheri subsp. zeyheri

Stipagrostis zeyheri subsp.

zeyheri, commonly known as Zeyher's grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves.

The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy or rocky soils.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
