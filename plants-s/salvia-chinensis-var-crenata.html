Salvia chinensis var. crenata

Salvia chinensis var. crenata, commonly known as Chinese sage or broad-leaf sage, is a perennial herb native to China.

It features upright, branched stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with a serrated margin and a bluish-green hue. The flowers are typically violet to purple, arranged in whorled clusters at the ends of the branches.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
