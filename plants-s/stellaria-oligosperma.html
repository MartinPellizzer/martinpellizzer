Stellaria oligosperma

Stellaria oligosperma, commonly known as lesser starwort, is a small annual herbaceous plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its star-shaped flowers, which are typically white or pale pink, and its prostrate or ascending growth habit. The plant has slender, branched stems and small, oval leaves arranged in a whorled pattern. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

Stellaria oligosperma is known for its delicate appearance and is sometimes used in ornamental gardening.
