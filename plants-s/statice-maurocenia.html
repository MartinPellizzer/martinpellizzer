Statice maurocenia

Statice maurocenia, commonly known as the purple gullwing, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces vibrant purple or pink flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Statice maurocenia is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
