Schoenoplectus aberrans

Schoenoplectus aberrans, commonly known as the swamp reed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Cyperaceae.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and dense clusters of small, spike-like inflorescences. This species typically grows in wetland environments such as marshes and shallow water areas. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread vegetatively and form dense vegetation.
