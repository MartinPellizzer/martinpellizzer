Sciaphila tuberculata

Sciaphila tuberculata, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dark green, hairy leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest understories.

Sciaphila tuberculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
