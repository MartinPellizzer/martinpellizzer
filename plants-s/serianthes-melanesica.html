Serianthes melanesica

Serianthes melanesica, commonly known as the Melanesian ironwood, is a tropical tree native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its dense, hard wood and can grow up to 30 meters tall. The tree has a straight trunk and a broad, umbrella-shaped crown. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a rough texture.

It produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody, spherical fruits containing several seeds.
