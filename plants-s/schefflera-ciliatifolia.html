Schefflera ciliatifolia

Schefflera ciliatifolia, commonly known as the silky schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded crown and large, palmate leaves that are soft to the touch. The leaves are typically light green with a distinctive silvery or fuzzy underside, giving it its common name. It produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red, berry-like fruits.

This plant is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its adaptability and attractive foliage.
