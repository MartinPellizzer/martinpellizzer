Sesbania sinuocarinata

Sesbania sinuocarinata, commonly known as the scarlet sesbania, is a leguminous plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red flowers and trifoliate leaves, which are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces elongated pods that contain edible seeds, which are rich in protein and nutrients. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.

Its rapid growth makes it useful for soil stabilization and as a forage crop.
