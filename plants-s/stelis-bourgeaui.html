Stelis bourgeaui

Stelis bourgeaui, commonly known as the Bourgeau's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Stelis bourgeaui is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
