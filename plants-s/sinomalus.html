Sinomalus

Sinomalus, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to Africa.

These plants are characterized by their dense, velvety foliage and showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. They typically grow in moist, shaded environments and prefer well-draining soil. Sinomalus species are popular as ornamental plants due to their compact size and long-lasting blooms.

The genus includes several species, with Sinomalus africanus being one of the most widely cultivated.
