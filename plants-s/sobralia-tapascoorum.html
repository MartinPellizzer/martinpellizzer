Sobralia tapascoorum

Sobralia tapascoorum, commonly known as the Tapasco orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually broad and strap-shaped, arranged along the stem.

Sobralia tapascoorum thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
