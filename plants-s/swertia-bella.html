Swertia bella

Swertia bella, commonly known as blue gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and branched.

Swertia bella is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
