Scrophularia nervosa

Scrophularia nervosa, commonly known as narrowleaf figwort, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. The plant features slender, hairy stems and opposite, heart-shaped leaves with prominent veins. Its flowers are tubular and usually pink or purple, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems.

Scrophularia nervosa is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
