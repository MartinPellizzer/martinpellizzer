Stenochilus curvipes

Stenochilus curvipes, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique spiral-shaped inflorescence, which gives it its common name. The plant features long, narrow leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in shaded, moist environments.

Stenochilus curvipes is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
