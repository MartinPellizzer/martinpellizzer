Sicyos microcarpus var. obovoideis

Sicyos microcarpus var. obovoideis, commonly known as the narrow-leafed bottle gourd, is a variant of the bottle gourd species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a climbing vine with hairy, lobed leaves and produces large, cylindrical fruits that mature to a pale green or yellowish color. The plant thrives in warm climates with well-drained soil and requires full sun for optimal growth. Its fruits are used in various culinary and medicinal applications due to their high water content and mild flavor.

The variety is distinguished by its narrower, obovate leaf shape compared to the standard bottle gourd.
