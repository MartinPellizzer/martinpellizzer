Serpocaulon crystalloneuron

Serpocaulon crystalloneuron, commonly known as the crystal fern, is a unique epiphytic plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that resemble the structure of a fern, though it is not closely related to true ferns. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in tropical rainforests. Its most striking feature is the presence of small, translucent, crystalline structures on its leaves, which give it its common name.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, Serpocaulon crystalloneuron is considered a conservation concern in many areas.
