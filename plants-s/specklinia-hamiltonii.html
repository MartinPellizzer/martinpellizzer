Specklinia hamiltonii

Specklinia hamiltonii, commonly known as the Hamilton's speckled orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-white with speckled patterns. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Specklinia hamiltonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
