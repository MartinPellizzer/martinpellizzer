Salacia triplinervis

Salacia triplinervis, commonly known as the three-veined salacia, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Salaceae and is characterized by its distinctive three-veined leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are fleshy berries that contain several seeds.

Salacia triplinervis is often found in humid, tropical lowland forests.
