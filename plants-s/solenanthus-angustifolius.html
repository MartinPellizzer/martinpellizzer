Solenanthus angustifolius

Solenanthus angustifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved solenanthus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils. The plant features small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are narrow and elongated, often covered in fine hairs.

It is known for its ability to thrive in harsh, coastal environments.
