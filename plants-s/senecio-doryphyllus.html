Senecio doryphyllus

Senecio doryphyllus, commonly known as the giant senecio, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, woolly leaves and showy yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its robust growth habit. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is often cultivated in arid regions.
