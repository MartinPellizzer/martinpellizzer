Senecio bodinieri var. parcepilosa

Senecio bodinieri var. parcepilosa, commonly known as the Cape snowflake, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and clusters of small, star-shaped white flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.
