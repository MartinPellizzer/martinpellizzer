Scutellaria vacillans subsp. colchica

Scutellaria vacillans subsp.

colchica, commonly known as the Caucasian skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces small, tubular blue or violet flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, rocky habitats and is often found in mountainous areas. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is known for its aromatic properties and medicinal uses.

The subspecies colchica is distinguished by its slightly different morphology compared to the nominate form of Scutellaria vacillans.
