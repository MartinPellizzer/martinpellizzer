Siebera valida

Siebera valida, commonly known as the golden star holly, is a hardy evergreen shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features dense, dark green foliage with striking yellow or orange berries that persist through winter. The plant has a compact, rounded growth habit and is well-suited for use as a hedge or foundation plant. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and long-lasting berries, making it a popular choice in landscaping.
