Scilla globosa

Scilla globosa, commonly known as globe squill, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features striking blue or purple flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from March to May. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge from a central bulb. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Scilla globosa is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in floral arrangements.
