Satureja montana var. stenophylla

Satureja montana var. stenophylla, commonly known as narrow-leaved savory, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes. Its essential oils contain compounds like carvacrol and thymol, which contribute to its medicinal and culinary uses.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and historical use in traditional herbal remedies.
