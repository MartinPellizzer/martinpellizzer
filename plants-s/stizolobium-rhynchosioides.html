Stizolobium rhynchosioides

Stizolobium rhynchosioides, commonly known as the slender legume, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in racemes, which are followed by elongated, bean-like pods. Native to tropical regions of Africa, it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional herbal practices.
