Specklinia recula

Specklinia recula, commonly known as the miniature orchid or the dwarf orchid, is a small epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in tropical rainforests. The plant has delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces tiny, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It is prized for its compact size and ornamental value in horticulture.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and sensitive species.
