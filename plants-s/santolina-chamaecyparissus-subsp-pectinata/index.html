Santolina Chamaecyparissus Subsp. Pectinata

Santolina chamaecyparissus subsp. pectinata is a lesser-known variant of the santolina plant. It is native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa. This subspecies is characterized by its dense, silver-green foliage and compact growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun conditions. Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is gaining popularity among gardeners.
