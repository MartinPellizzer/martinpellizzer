Stanhopea oculata var. conspicua

Stanhopea oculata var. conspicua, commonly known as the showy stanhopea, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, often with contrasting darker markings. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its pseudobulbs are cylindrical and clustered, supporting its evergreen foliage.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively manageable care requirements.
