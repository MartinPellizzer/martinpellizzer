Silene platyphylla f. paniculifera

Silene platyphylla f. paniculifera, commonly known as the paniculate silene, is a flowering plant species native to certain regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, branched inflorescences. The plant typically produces small, pink or white flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are linear and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
