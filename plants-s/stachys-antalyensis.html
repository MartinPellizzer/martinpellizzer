Stachys antalyensis

Stachys antalyensis, commonly known as Antalya hedge nettle, is a perennial herb native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features square stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture. Its small, clustered flowers are typically pink or purple, blooming in summer.

It is often found in rocky or dry habitats, showing adaptability to harsh environments.
