Senecio banksii

Senecio banksii, commonly known as the Banks' groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small yellow flowers arranged in a cluster. It is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Senecio banksii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
