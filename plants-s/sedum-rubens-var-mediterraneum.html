Sedum rubens var. mediterraneum

Sedum rubens var. mediterraneum, commonly known as Mediterranean stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, clustered leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces vibrant pink to red flowers in the summer, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

This variety is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in full sun.
