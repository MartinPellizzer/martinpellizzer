Statice echinus

Statice echinus, commonly known as sea lavender, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean and the eastern Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its compact, spiky appearance and vibrant blue or purple flowers. The plant thrives in saline and well-drained soils, often found in dunes and rocky shores. Its narrow, grass-like leaves are adapted to withstand harsh coastal conditions.

Statice echinus is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in dried flower arrangements.
