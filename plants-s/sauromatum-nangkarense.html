Sauromatum nangkarense

Sauromatum nangkarense, commonly known as the Nangkaren swamp arrowroot, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
