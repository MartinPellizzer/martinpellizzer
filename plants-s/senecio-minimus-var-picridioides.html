Senecio minimus var. picridioides

Senecio minimus var. picridioides, commonly known as the dwarf senecio, is a small, herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a compact inflorescence. It is often found in arid or semi-arid environments, demonstrating a high degree of drought tolerance.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological adaptations, it is occasionally cultivated as an ornamental plant.
