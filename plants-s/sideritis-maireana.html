Sideritis maireana

Sideritis maireana, commonly known as Maire's sainfoin, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Sideritis maireana is often found in rocky or dry habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.
