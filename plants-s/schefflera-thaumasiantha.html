Schefflera thaumasiantha

Schefflera thaumasiantha, commonly known as the Philippine umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of large, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated as a houseplant.
