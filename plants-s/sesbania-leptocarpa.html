Sesbania leptocarpa

Sesbania leptocarpa, commonly known as the slender cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellow pea-like flowers. The plant produces elongated pods that contain several seeds, which are often used as a food source. Sesbania leptocarpa is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it valuable in agricultural systems.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used for forage and soil improvement.
