Strobilanthes atropurpurea var. stenophylla

Strobilanthes atropurpurea var. stenophylla, commonly known as the purple flame or purple strobilanthes, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking purple or reddish-purple inflorescences, which are clustered in dense, spike-like formations. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
