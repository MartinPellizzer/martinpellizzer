Schoenoplectiella humillima

Schoenoplectiella humillima, commonly known as the slender bulrush, is a small, herbaceous plant native to wetland environments in North America.

It typically grows in shallow water or moist soil, often found in marshes and along the edges of ponds. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in dense spikes. Its root system helps stabilize soil and improve water filtration in wetland ecosystems.

Schoenoplectiella humillima plays an important role in maintaining the ecological balance of its habitat.
