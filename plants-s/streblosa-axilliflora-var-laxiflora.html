Streblosa axilliflora var. laxiflora

Streblosa axilliflora var. laxiflora, commonly known as the laxiflorous streblosa, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, tubular flowers that typically bloom in spring. The leaves are usually opposite and elliptical, with a slightly hairy texture. The plant prefers well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.
