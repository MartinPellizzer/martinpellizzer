Syngonium albolineatum

Syngonium albolineatum, commonly known as the White-veined Arrowhead, is a tropical perennial plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves with distinctive white veins, giving it a striking visual appearance. The plant thrives in moist, well-draining soil and prefers indirect sunlight. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and air-purifying qualities.

Syngonium albolineatum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
