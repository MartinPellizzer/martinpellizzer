Stelis asseris

Stelis asseris, commonly known as the purple-stemmed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

The orchid's blooming period occurs during the rainy season, producing fragrant, violet-colored flowers.
