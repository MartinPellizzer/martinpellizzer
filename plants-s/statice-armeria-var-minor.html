Statice armeria var. minor

Statice armeria var. minor, commonly known as sea pink, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces dense clusters of small, showy pink or purple flowers. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to salt-laden environments. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in a fine layer of white powder.

It is valued in gardens for its hardiness and ability to thrive in coastal or maritime conditions.
