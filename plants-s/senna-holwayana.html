Senna holwayana

Senna holwayana, commonly known as the golden senna, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters tall, characterized by its bipinnate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces pods that contain seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. Senna holwayana thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and medicinal contexts.
