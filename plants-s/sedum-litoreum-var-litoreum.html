Sedum litoreum var. litoreum

Sedum litoreum var. litoreum, commonly known as sea sedum, is a succulent plant native to coastal regions of Europe and Asia.

It thrives in saline and sandy environments, often found along shores and cliffs. The plant features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water retention. It produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months.

This hardy species is well adapted to withstand harsh maritime conditions and is often used in coastal landscaping.
