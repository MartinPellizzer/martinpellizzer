Sansevieria pinguicula subsp. nana

Sansevieria pinguicula subsp.

nana, commonly known as the dwarf snake plant, is a compact, low-maintenance succulent native to South Africa. It features short, stiff, dark green leaves that are slightly undulating and often have a pale green or yellowish margin.

The plant produces small, white to pale green flowers on slender stalks under optimal conditions. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, requiring minimal water.

Its compact size and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and rock gardens.
