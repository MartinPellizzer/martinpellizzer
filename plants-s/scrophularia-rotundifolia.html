Scrophularia rotundifolia

Scrophularia rotundifolia, commonly known as round-leaved figwort, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features heart-shaped, hairy leaves and produces spikes of purple or violet flowers. Its flowers are tubular and attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Scrophularia rotundifolia is part of the Scrophulariaceae family, known for its medicinal and ornamental value.
