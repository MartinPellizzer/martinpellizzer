Sacciolepis polymorpha

Sacciolepis polymorpha, commonly known as the variable sacciolepis, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in dune environments. It has a short lifespan, typically completing its growth cycle within a single growing season.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
