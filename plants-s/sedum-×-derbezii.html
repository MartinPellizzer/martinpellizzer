Sedum × derbezii

Sedum × derbezii, commonly known as the hybrid stonecrop, is a succulent plant characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and clusters of star-shaped flowers.

It is native to regions in the Middle East and Central Asia, where it thrives in rocky and arid environments. The plant typically grows to a height of 10 to 30 centimeters and produces vibrant yellow or orange blooms during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and prefers well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Sedum × derbezii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
