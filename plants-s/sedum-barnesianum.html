Sedum barnesianum

Sedum barnesianum, commonly known as the Barnsfield stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the British Isles.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy choice for rock gardens.

Its ability to store water in its leaves allows it to survive in dry conditions.
