Solanum ornatum

Solanum ornatum, commonly known as the showy nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known species like tomatoes and potatoes. Its leaves are typically ovate with a slightly hairy texture, and the fruit is a small, fleshy berry that turns red when mature.

Solanum ornatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its attractive foliage and flowers.
