Stellaria ovalifolia

Stellaria ovalifolia, commonly known as the oval-leaved starwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, white or pinkish star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, temperate environments, such as meadows and woodland edges. Its leaves are oval-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Stellaria ovalifolia is often found in Europe and parts of Asia, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
