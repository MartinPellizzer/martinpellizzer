Stachys tenuifolia var. platyphylla

Stachys tenuifolia var. platyphylla, commonly known as narrowleaf betony, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright stems covered in soft, hairy foliage and produces spikes of pale blue to purple flowers. The leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
