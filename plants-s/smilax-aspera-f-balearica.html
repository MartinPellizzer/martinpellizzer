Smilax aspera f. balearica

Smilax aspera f. balearica, commonly known as the Balearic smilax, is a variant of the rough smilax native to the Balearic Islands.

It is a climbing shrub characterized by its spiny stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in coastal and limestone areas, often found in shaded, moist environments.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ecologically important species in its native habitat.
