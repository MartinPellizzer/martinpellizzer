Shawia linifolia

Shawia linifolia, commonly known as the silky leaf shrub, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a warm, humid climate to flourish.

Shawia linifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
