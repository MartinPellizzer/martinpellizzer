Setaria trinii

Setaria trinii, commonly known as slender dropseed, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny grains, typical of the grass family. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in prairies and open fields.

Setaria trinii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing habitat and food for various wildlife.
