Stelis trimera

Stelis trimera, commonly known as the miniature orchid or the dwarf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. The plant has slender, upright stems and produces clusters of tiny, white or pale green flowers. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding substrate.

Stelis trimera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
