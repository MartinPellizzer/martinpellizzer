Sideritis germanicopolitana subsp. viridis

Sideritis germanicopolitana subsp.

viridis, commonly known as green Germanicopolitan sideritis, is a flowering plant species native to the Balkan Peninsula. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves.

The plant produces small, tubular blue or violet flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in rocky or limestone soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
