Sansevieria hyacinthoides

Sansevieria hyacinthoides, commonly known as the Variegated Snake Plant, is a striking member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to tropical Africa and is characterized by its tall, upright stems and sword-shaped leaves that exhibit a distinctive variegation of green, white, and yellow. The plant is known for its air-purifying qualities and ability to thrive in low-light conditions. It produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months.

Its hardy nature makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and easy care.
