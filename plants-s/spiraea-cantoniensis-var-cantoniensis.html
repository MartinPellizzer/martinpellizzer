Spiraea cantoniensis var. cantoniensis

Spiraea cantoniensis var. cantoniensis, commonly known as the Chinese spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features small, white to pink flowers that appear in clusters along the stems in early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and has serrated, ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This ornamental plant is valued for its compact growth and showy floral display.
