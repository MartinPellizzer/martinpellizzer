Solanum salzmannii

Solanum salzmannii, commonly known as Salzmann's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale green. Its fruit is a yellow or orange berry that contains several seeds.

Solanum salzmannii is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments.
