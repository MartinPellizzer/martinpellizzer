Sticherus hirtus var. ornamentalis

Sticherus hirtus var. ornamentalis, commonly known as the ornamented sticherus, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant features hairy stems and leaves, which contribute to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact form and delicate floral display.
