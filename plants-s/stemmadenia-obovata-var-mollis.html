Stemmadenia obovata var. mollis

Stemmadenia obovata var. mollis, commonly known as the soft-leaved stemmadenia, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, velvety leaves that are obovate in shape, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, which are typically borne on the upper parts of the stems. Its stems are often covered in a soft, pubescent coating, contributing to its common name.

This species is valued in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
