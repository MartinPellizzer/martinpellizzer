Senecio fimbrillifer

Senecio fimbrillifer, commonly known as the fibrous senecio, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa, and thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. Senecio fimbrillifer is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
