Salvia wuana

Salvia wuana, commonly known as Chinese sage, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly serrated edge, often used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Salvia wuana is valued for its ornamental beauty and medicinal properties.
