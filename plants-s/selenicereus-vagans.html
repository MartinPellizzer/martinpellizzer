Selenicereus vagans

Selenicereus vagans, commonly known as the night blooming cereus, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

Its stems are fleshy and succulent, adapted to store water in arid environments.

Selenicereus vagans is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a popular choice for night-blooming gardens.
