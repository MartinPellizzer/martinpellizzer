Suteria

Suteria, commonly known as golden ragwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and hairy, lance-shaped leaves. Native to North America, it thrives in dry, disturbed soils and is often found in prairies and open woodlands. The plant produces small, wind-dispersed seeds, aiding in its spread across various habitats.

Suteria is also noted for its medicinal properties, historically used by indigenous peoples for its healing benefits.
