Streptanthus oliganthus

Streptanthus oliganthus, commonly known as the few-flowered whitlow grass, is a member of the Brassicaceae family.

It is a herbaceous annual plant native to the western United States, particularly California. The plant features small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often covered in a fine layer of hairs.

Streptanthus oliganthus typically grows in rocky soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climates.
