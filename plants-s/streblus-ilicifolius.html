Streblus ilicifolius

Streblus ilicifolius, commonly known as the Indian rubber vine, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are typically composed of several small, oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing several seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine.

However, it can become invasive in certain environments if not managed properly.
