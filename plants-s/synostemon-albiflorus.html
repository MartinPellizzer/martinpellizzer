Synostemon albiflorus

Synostemon albiflorus, commonly known as the whiteflower synostemon, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its most distinctive characteristic is the clusters of small, white, tubular flowers that bloom in the summer.
