Strychnos yapurensis

Strychnos yapurensis, commonly known as the Yapurensis tree, is a species native to South America, particularly found in the Amazon rainforest.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 meters. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate or elliptical in shape. Its flowers are small and yellow, clustered in inflorescences, and it produces berries that contain the seeds.

The plant is notable for its potential medicinal properties and has been studied for its chemical composition.
