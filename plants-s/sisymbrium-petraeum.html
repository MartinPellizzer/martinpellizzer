Sisymbrium petraeum

Sisymbrium petraeum, commonly known as the rock pepper or mountain mustard, is a flowering plant native to rocky and arid regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and linear, hairy leaves adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
