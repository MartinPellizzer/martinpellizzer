Sulcorebutia mentosa subsp. flavissima

Sulcorebutia mentosa subsp.

flavissima, commonly known as the Orange Globular Cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to southern Africa. It features a pale green to reddish stem with prominent areoles and spines that are often reddish or orange in color.

The plant produces showy, yellow or orange flowers during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
