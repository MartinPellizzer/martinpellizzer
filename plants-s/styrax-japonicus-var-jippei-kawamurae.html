Styrax japonicus var. jippei-kawamurae

Styrax japonicus var. jippei-kawamurae, commonly known as the Japanese styrax, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, oval-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. It typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and prefers well-drained, acidic soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its floral display.
