Stelis tonduziana

Stelis tonduziana, commonly known as the Tonduziana orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Stelis tonduziana is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
