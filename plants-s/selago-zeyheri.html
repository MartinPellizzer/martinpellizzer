Selago zeyheri

Selago zeyheri, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a South African plant belonging to the Acanthaceae family.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal regions, characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowering stems are often branched and bear clusters of small, tubular flowers.

Selago zeyheri is well adapted to sandy soils and is often found in dune environments.
