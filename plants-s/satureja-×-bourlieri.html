Satureja × bourlieri

Satureja × bourlieri, commonly known as hybrid savory, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in spike-like clusters. Its aromatic leaves are used in cooking and traditional medicine for their pungent, slightly minty flavor.

Satureja × bourlieri thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy addition to gardens.
