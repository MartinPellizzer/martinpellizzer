Spartium junceum ochroleucum

Spartium junceum ochroleucum, commonly known as yellow spartium, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and traditional herbal uses.
