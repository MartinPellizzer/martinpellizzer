Scaphosepalum fimbriatum

Scaphosepalum fimbriatum, commonly known as the fringed lip orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in grassland and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant features a slender, upright stem with several fleshy, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are showy, with a distinctive lip that has a fringed edge, giving it its common name.

The orchid blooms in late summer, producing clusters of pale yellow to orange flowers.
