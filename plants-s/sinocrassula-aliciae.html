Sinocrassula aliciae

Sinocrassula aliciae, commonly known as the Alice's stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to China.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its compact, low-growing form. The plant features fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a powdery coating. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring.

Sinocrassula aliciae is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
