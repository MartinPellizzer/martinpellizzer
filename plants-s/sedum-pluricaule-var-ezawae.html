Sedum pluricaule var. ezawae

Sedum pluricaule var. ezawae, commonly known as the Ezawa's stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped, yellow flowers during the summer months. It is adapted to grow in rocky or gravelly soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.
