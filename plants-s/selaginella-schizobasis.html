Selaginella schizobasis

Selaginella schizobasis, commonly known as the common spikemoss, is a vascular plant belonging to the family Selaginellaceae.

It is a small, herbaceous plant that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests or rocky crevices. The plant has a distinctive appearance with upright, branching stems and small, scale-like leaves. It reproduces via spores, which are released from specialized structures called strobila.

Selaginella schizobasis is known for its ability to survive in harsh conditions, making it a resilient and adaptable species.
