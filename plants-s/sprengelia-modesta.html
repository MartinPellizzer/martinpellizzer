Sprengelia modesta

Sprengelia modesta, commonly known as the modest spurge, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features smooth, pale green stems that are often tinged with red or purple. The plant produces small, yellow flowers clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a waxy texture.

Sprengelia modesta is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
