Siphanthera arenaria var. cordifolia

Siphanthera arenaria var. cordifolia, commonly known as the heartleaf sandplant, is a rare and specialized succulent native to arid regions of southern Africa.

The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers during the warmer months, which are typically short-lived but attract pollinators like bees and moths.
