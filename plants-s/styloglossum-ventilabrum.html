Styloglossum ventilabrum

Styloglossum ventilabrum, commonly known as the flame flower, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, resembling flames. The plant belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is known for its showy, long-lasting blooms. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a smooth texture and a glossy appearance.

Styloglossum ventilabrum thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
