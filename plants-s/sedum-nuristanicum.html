Sedum nuristanicum

Sedum nuristanicum, commonly known as the Nuristan stonecrop, is a perennial succulent native to the mountainous regions of eastern Afghanistan and northern Pakistan.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to high-altitude environments.

Due to its unique appearance and resilience, it is often cultivated in rock gardens and alpine landscapes.
