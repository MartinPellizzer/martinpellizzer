Satureja × alboviridis

Satureja × alboviridis, commonly known as silverleaf savory, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its aromatic, gray-green foliage that gives it a distinctive silvery appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, blooming in late spring to early summer. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Satureja × alboviridis is often used in herbal teas and as a culinary herb due to its pungent, slightly minty flavor.
