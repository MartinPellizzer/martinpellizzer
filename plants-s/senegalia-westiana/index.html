Senegalia Westiana

Senegalia westiana is a unique and resilient plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it well-suited for harsh climates. This shrub or small tree is known for its hardy nature and ability to withstand drought conditions. Its small, leathery leaves and distinctive yellow flowers make it a striking addition to desert landscapes. Despite its tough exterior, Senegalia westiana plays an important role in local ecosystems and has potential uses in traditional medicine and land restoration.
