Senecio obtectus

Senecio obtectus, commonly known as the woolly groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly gray foliage and small yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to arid conditions.

Senecio obtectus is a popular choice for rock gardens due to its ornamental appearance and low maintenance requirements.
