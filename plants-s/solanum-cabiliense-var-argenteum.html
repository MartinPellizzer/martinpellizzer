Solanum cabiliense var. argenteum

Solanum cabiliense var. argenteum, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and peppers. The plant is characterized by its silver-white foliage, which gives it its common name. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters.

The fruit is a small, egg-shaped berry that turns red when mature.
