Sinabraca

Sinabraca, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a striking visual display. The plant belongs to the genus Brachylecithum and is part of the legume family, Fabaceae. Its leaves are typically compound, with multiple leaflets arranged in a feathery pattern.

The bark of Sinabraca is smooth and often exhibits a reddish or brownish hue, which adds to its ornamental appeal.
