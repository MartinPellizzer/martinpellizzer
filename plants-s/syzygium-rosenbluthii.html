Syzygium rosenbluthii

Syzygium rosenbluthii, commonly known as the roseapple tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and produces fragrant white or pale pink flowers. Its fruit, the roseapple, is a small, round, greenish-yellow fruit with a sweet and aromatic flavor.

Syzygium rosenbluthii is valued for its ornamental beauty and edible fruit in its native region.
