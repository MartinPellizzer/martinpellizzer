Solanum volkensii

Solanum volkensii, commonly known as the African nightshade, is a shrub native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and broad, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that give way to orange-red berries, which are edible but have a bitter taste. Solanum volkensii belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is known for its ornamental value.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.
