Silene grisebachii

Silene grisebachii, commonly known as the Sierra Nevada silene, is a flowering plant native to the Sierra Nevada mountain range in California.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky, alpine environments. The plant produces showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in a fine, silvery coating.

Silene grisebachii is adapted to cold, high-altitude conditions and plays a role in the local ecosystem by providing nectar for pollinators.
