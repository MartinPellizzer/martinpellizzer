Synaedrys lucida

Synaedrys lucida, commonly known as the brightleaf spurge, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Africa.

It features opposite, simple, and usually lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by bead-like fruits. It thrives in dry, open habitats and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Synaedrys lucida is noted for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
