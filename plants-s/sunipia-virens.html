Sunipia virens

Sunipia virens, commonly known as the green flame plant, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant green foliage and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 3 feet. Its leaves are ovate in shape with a smooth texture and a slightly waxy appearance.

Sunipia virens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in warm, humid environments.
