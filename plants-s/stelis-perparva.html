Stelis perparva

Stelis perparva, commonly known as the small-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, tropical environments. The plant features slender, wiry stems and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pink. Its leaves are usually narrow and arranged in a rosette pattern, and it thrives in partial shade with high humidity.

Stelis perparva is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
