Syzygium maire

Syzygium maire, commonly known as kahikatea, is a flowering plant species native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen leaves and showy white flowers. The plant typically grows as a tall tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 20 meters. Its fruit is a woody capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
