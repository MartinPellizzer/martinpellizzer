Statice ovalifolia var. minor

Statice ovalifolia var. minor, commonly known as the narrow-leaf statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and produces showy, pink to purple flower heads. The leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs, while the stems are sturdy and branched. This variety is valued in horticulture for its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in sandy or well-drained soils.

It blooms from late summer into early autumn, adding vibrant color to coastal and garden landscapes.
