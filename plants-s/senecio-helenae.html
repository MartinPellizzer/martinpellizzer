Senecio helenae

Senecio helenae, commonly known as Helen's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are usually gray-green with a woolly texture, and it produces yellow flowers in spring.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and hardy nature.
