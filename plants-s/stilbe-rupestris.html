Stilbe rupestris

Stilbe rupestris, commonly known as the rock bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of blue or violet flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often found growing on rocky slopes and in dry habitats.

Stilbe rupestris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
