Sophora unifoliata var. elliptica

Sophora unifoliata var. elliptica, commonly known as the Chinese wisteria, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are elliptic in shape and have a glossy green appearance. The plant produces long, pendulous clusters of fragrant, pale yellow flowers in late spring to early summer. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.

The seeds contain toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
