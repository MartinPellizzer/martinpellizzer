Stellaria glauca var. virens

Stellaria glauca var. virens, commonly known as green starwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

It features small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to August. The plant has slender, branching stems covered in fine, silky hairs, and its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.

The species is characterized by its delicate appearance and is often found in disturbed or semi-natural habitats.
