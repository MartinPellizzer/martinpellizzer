Saccolabium escritorii

Saccolabium escritorii, commonly known as the writer's thorn, is a shrub native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its spiny stems and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by pod-like fruits containing several seeds. It is adapted to dry environments and is often found in savannas and rocky areas.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
