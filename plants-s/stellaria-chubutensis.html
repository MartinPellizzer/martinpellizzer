Stellaria chubutensis

Stellaria chubutensis, commonly known as the Chubut starwort, is a flowering plant native to the Patagonian region of Argentina.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to the arid and cold conditions of its native habitat. It has small, hairy leaves and produces white or pale pink blooms.

Stellaria chubutensis is of interest to botanists due to its unique adaptations and limited distribution.
