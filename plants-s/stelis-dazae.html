Stelis dazae

Stelis dazae, commonly known as the Dazae orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, tropical environments. Its flowers are usually small, with a unique coloration that ranges from white to pale pink.

Stelis dazae thrives in well-drained soil and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.
