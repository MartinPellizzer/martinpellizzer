Sinningia harleyi

Sinningia harleyi, commonly known as the Harley's gloxinia, is a species of flowering plant native to the Andean region of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. Sinningia harleyi is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
