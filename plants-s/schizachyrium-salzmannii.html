Schizachyrium salzmannii

Schizachyrium salzmannii, commonly known as little bluestem, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage that turns coppery in the fall. This plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for native plant gardens. It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife.

Schizachyrium salzmannii is also valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological benefits in prairie ecosystems.
