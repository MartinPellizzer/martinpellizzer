Scabiosa loscosii

Scabiosa loscosii, commonly known as the alpine scabious, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils. It is a perennial species that blooms from late spring to early summer.

The leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, with a hairy texture that helps reduce water loss in its harsh habitat.
