Sibiraea tianschanica

Sibiraea tianschanica, commonly known as the Tian Shan spruce, is a coniferous tree native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and dense, pyramidal crown. The tree has needle-like leaves that are typically dark green and arranged spirally along the branches. It produces small, cone-like structures that contain its seeds.

This species is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments and plays an important role in the ecological systems of its native habitat.
