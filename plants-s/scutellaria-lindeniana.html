Scutellaria lindeniana

Scutellaria lindeniana, commonly known as Lindenberg's skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. The plant features square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that are typically blue to purple in color. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
