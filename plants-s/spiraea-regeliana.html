Spiraea regeliana

Spiraea regeliana, commonly known as the Regel's spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to Central Asia.

It features dense, compact growth with clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has finely cut, dark green leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Spiraea regeliana prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
