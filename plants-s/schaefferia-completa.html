Schaefferia completa

Schaefferia completa, commonly known as the white bellflower, is a perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, white flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. Schaefferia completa is noted for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
