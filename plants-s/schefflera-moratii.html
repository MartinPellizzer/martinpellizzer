Schefflera moratii

Schefflera moratii, commonly known as the Moratii umbrella tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive umbrella-shaped cluster of leaves at the ends of its branches. The plant has large, leathery, dark green leaves that are often glossy and arranged in a whorl pattern. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Schefflera moratii is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
