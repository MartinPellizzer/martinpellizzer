Stachys burchelliana

Stachys burchelliana, commonly known as Burchell's hedge-nettle, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features upright, branched stems covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in dense clusters at the tips of the branches. Its leaves are opposite, ovate to elliptic in shape, and have a slightly serrated margin.

Stachys burchelliana is often grown as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
