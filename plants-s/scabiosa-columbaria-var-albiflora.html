Scabiosa columbaria var. albiflora

Scabiosa columbaria var. albiflora, commonly known as the whitehead scabious, is a flowering plant native to Europe.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically blooms in late spring to early summer, producing a delicate, lace-like appearance. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and wildflower meadows.

Its flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, contributing to ecological biodiversity.
