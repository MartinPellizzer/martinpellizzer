Senecio uralensis

Senecio uralensis, commonly known as the Siberian groundsel, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It typically grows in alpine and subalpine environments, often found in rocky or gravelly soils. The plant features a basal rosette of leaves that are usually gray-green in color, with a fuzzy texture. Its flowering stems bear yellow composite flowers, which are arranged in a daisy-like pattern.

Senecio uralensis is known for its resilience to harsh environmental conditions, including cold temperatures and limited moisture.
