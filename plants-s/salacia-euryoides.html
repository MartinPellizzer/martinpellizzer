Salacia euryoides

Salacia euryoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved salacia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Salaciacae and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are elliptical and glossy, with a dark green coloration.

The flowers are usually white or pale yellow, and the plant produces small, fleshy fruits that are edible but not widely consumed.
