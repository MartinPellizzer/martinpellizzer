Satureja linearifolia

Satureja linearifolia, commonly known as slender mountain savory, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe and western Asia.

It features upright, branched stems that are often hairy, and its leaves are narrow, linear, and aromatic. The plant produces small, pale blue or purple flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Satureja linearifolia is valued for its fragrant leaves, which are used in culinary and medicinal applications.
