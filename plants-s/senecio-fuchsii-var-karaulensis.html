Senecio fuchsii var. karaulensis

Senecio fuchsii var. karaulensis, commonly known as the Karaulenskaya Senecio, is a perennial flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and narrow, linear leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, arranged in dense clusters, and bloom during the summer months.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and resilience in dry climates.
