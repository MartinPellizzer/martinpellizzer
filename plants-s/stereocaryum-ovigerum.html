Stereocaryum ovigerum

Stereocaryum ovigerum, commonly known as the African snowball, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of white, fluffy flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

The African snowball is often used in ornamental gardening for its unique and showy floral display.
