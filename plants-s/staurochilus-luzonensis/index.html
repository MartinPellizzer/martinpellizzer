Staurochilus Luzonensis

Luzon starling fern, scientifically known as Staurochilus luzonensis, is a unique and rare plant species native to the Philippines. This fern is characterized by its delicate, star-like fronds that give it its distinctive name. Found primarily in the mountainous regions of Luzon, it thrives in humid and shaded environments. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts. Its beauty and ecological importance make it a subject of interest for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike.
