Schefflera nephelophila

Schefflera nephelophila, commonly known as the cloud forest schefflera, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the high-altitude cloud forests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in diameter, giving it a tropical, lush appearance. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish. Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and as a statement plant in tropical landscapes.

Schefflera nephelophila is also valued for its ability to purify the air and adapt well to various growing conditions.
