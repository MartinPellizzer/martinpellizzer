Stegolepis terramarensis

Stegolepis terramarensis, commonly known as the Terramarensis rockrose, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae.

It is native to the rocky habitats of southern Africa, particularly in Namibia and South Africa. The plant is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability to arid environments, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
