Stemona tuberosa var. ternatensis

Stemona tuberosa var. ternatensis, commonly known as the Ternate stemona, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and dark green, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in umbels. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

It prefers moist, well-drained soils and is typically found in tropical and subtropical regions.
