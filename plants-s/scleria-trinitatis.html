Scleria trinitatis

Scleria trinitatis, commonly known as the Trinidad screwbean, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and small, pale blue to purple flowers. Scleria trinitatis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
