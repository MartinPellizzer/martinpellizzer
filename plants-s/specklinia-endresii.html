Specklinia endresii

Specklinia endresii, commonly known as the Endres' orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia. This epiphytic orchid grows on trees and thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its foliage consists of narrow, leathery leaves, and it produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
