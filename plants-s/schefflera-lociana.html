Schefflera lociana

Schefflera lociana, commonly known as the Philippine umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like canopy of large, palmate leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments within the understory of tropical forests. Its leaves are usually dark green and glossy, with a smooth texture.

Schefflera lociana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
