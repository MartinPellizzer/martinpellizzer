Stanhopea chironii

Stanhopea chironii, commonly known as the Chironii Stanhopea, is a species of orchid native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that often display a vibrant mix of purple, pink, and white hues. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to tree trunks or branches in its natural habitat. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Stanhopea chironii is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and rarity in cultivation.
