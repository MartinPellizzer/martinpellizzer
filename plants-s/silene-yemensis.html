Silene yemensis

Silene yemensis, commonly known as Yemen starflower, is a flowering plant native to Yemen.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, while its stems are erect and branched.

Silene yemensis plays a role in local ecosystems and is of interest to botanists studying plant adaptation in arid regions.
