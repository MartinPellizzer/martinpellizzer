Stelis yanganensis

Stelis yanganensis, commonly known as the Yangan orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to China.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its erect, leafy stems and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest understories. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with a distinctive lip that is often adorned with intricate patterns.

Stelis yanganensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
