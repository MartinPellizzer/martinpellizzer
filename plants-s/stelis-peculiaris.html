Stelis peculiaris

Stelis peculiaris, commonly known as the peculiar orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are usually dark green and glossy, and it produces white or pale yellow blooms.

Stelis peculiaris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
