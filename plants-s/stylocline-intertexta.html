Stylocline intertexta

Stylocline intertexta, commonly known as the lace fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, lacy fronds that are typically divided into narrow segments. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in tropical rainforests. Its rhizomes are fibrous and help anchor it to the forest floor.

Stylocline intertexta is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
