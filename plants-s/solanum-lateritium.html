Solanum lateritium

Solanum lateritium, commonly known as the rock tomato, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a member of the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its flowers are usually yellow or white, and it produces small, edible berries that are often used in local cuisines.

The species is valued not only for its culinary uses but also for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance.
