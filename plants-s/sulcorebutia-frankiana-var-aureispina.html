Sulcorebutia frankiana var. aureispina

Sulcorebutia frankiana var. aureispina, commonly known as the golden spined sun cactus, is a compact, spineless cactus native to South America.

It features a globular or cylindrical stem that is typically green to yellowish in color. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
