Spartium procerum

Spartium procerum, commonly known as slender spartium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds, typical of the Fabaceae family. Spartium procerum is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to thrive in arid environments.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
