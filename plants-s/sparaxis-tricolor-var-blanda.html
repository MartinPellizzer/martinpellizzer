Sparaxis tricolor var. blanda

Sparaxis tricolor var. blanda, commonly known as the "Rainbow Sparaxis," is a South African bulbous plant characterized by its striking, tri-colored flowers.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of three-colored, bell-shaped blooms in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers. It is known for its drought tolerance and ability to bloom reliably in summer.

This variety is a popular choice for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
