Stephanostachys martiana

Stephanostachys martiana, commonly known as the mountain bamboo, is a perennial herb native to the high-altitude regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered inflorescences. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous habitats, where it forms dense clumps. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a bluish-green hue.
