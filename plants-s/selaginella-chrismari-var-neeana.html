Selaginella chrismari var. neeana

Selaginella chrismari var. neeana, commonly known as the Christmas moss, is a rare and unique species of spore-bearing plant belonging to the family Selaginellaceae.

This variety is native to specific regions in South America, particularly in Brazil, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments.
