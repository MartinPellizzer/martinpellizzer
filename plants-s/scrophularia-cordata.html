Scrophularia cordata

Scrophularia cordata, commonly known as heart-leaved scrophularia, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves and spikes of tubular, violet or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It belongs to the family Plantaginaceae and is often found in temperate regions.
