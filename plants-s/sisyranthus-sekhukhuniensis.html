Sisyranthus sekhukhuniensis

Sisyranthus sekhukhuniensis, commonly known as the Sekhukhune orchid, is a rare and endemic species found in South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils in mountainous regions. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, colorful blooms.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
