Strobilanthes fruticosa

Strobilanthes fruticosa, commonly known as the flame flower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, often during the rainy season. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a rough texture.

Strobilanthes fruticosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
