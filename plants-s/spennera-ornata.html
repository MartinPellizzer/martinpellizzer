Spennera ornata

Spennera ornata, commonly known as the golden star plant, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that resemble stars, surrounded by colorful bracts. Spennera ornata typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 1 meter.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
