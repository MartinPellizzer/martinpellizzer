Scopolia carniolica var. violacea

Scopolia carniolica var. violacea, commonly known as the purple scopolia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Alps and Carpathian regions.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, fuzzy texture. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its fragrant blooms and unique foliage.
