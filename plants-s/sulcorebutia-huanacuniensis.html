Sulcorebutia huanacuniensis

Sulcorebutia huanacuniensis, commonly known as the Huanacuniensis Cactus, is a species native to the Andes Mountains of Peru.

It is a small, compact cactus with a globular shape and typically features a crown of spines. The plant has thick, fleshy stems that store water, allowing it to survive in arid conditions. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing during the warmer months.

It is well adapted to high-altitude environments and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.
