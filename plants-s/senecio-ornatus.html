Senecio ornatus

Senecio ornatus, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other composite flowers. The plant features showy yellow flowers surrounded by white ray florets, giving it a daisy-like appearance. It is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Senecio ornatus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
