Silphium asteriscus var. pumilum

Silphium asteriscus var. pumilum, commonly known as the dwarf silphium, is a herbaceous perennial native to the central and eastern United States.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and features narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, loamy soils.

Silphium asteriscus var. pumilum is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical significance in native plant gardens.
