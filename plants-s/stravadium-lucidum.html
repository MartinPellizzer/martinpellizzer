Stravadium lucidum

Stravadium lucidum, commonly known as the silverleaf sunspire, is a rare flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green leaves that reflect sunlight, giving it a shimmering appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in cool, moist environments with well-drained soil.

Due to its limited distribution and unique appearance, it is considered a botanical curiosity and is often studied for its potential medicinal properties.
