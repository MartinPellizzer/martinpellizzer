Solanum umbelliferum var. umbelliferum

Solanum umbelliferum var. umbelliferum, commonly known as the common nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant produces berries that start green and mature to a deep purple or black color, often containing toxic compounds such as solanine.

It thrives in disturbed soils and can be found in gardens, wastelands, and along roadsides.
