Saussurea subulisquama

Saussurea subulisquama, commonly known as the Chinese yarrow, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stem and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant features fibrous roots and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered with fine hairs. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

Due to its medicinal properties, it is valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
