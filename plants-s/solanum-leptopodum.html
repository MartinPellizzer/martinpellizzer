Solanum leptopodum

Solanum leptopodum, commonly known as the slender nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants such as tomatoes and potatoes. The plant typically features small, green leaves and produces clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in the evening. Its fruit is a small, green berry that turns red when mature.

Solanum leptopodum is often found in moist, shaded environments and is characterized by its rapid growth and short lifespan.
