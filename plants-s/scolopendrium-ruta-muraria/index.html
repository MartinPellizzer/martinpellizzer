Scolopendrium Ruta-Muraria

Scolopendrium ruta-muraria is a rare and ancient fern that thrives in shaded, moist environments. Native to parts of Europe and western Asia, it has survived through millennia of environmental change. Its unique, shield-like fronds and historical significance make it a subject of both botanical and cultural interest. Often found in rocky crevices and forest floors, it requires specific conditions to flourish. This plant holds a special place in the study of prehistoric flora and ecological resilience.
