Stelis euspatha

Stelis euspatha, commonly known as the Elegant Stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, showcasing its epiphytic nature. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

The flowers often display a mix of white and green hues, making it a visually striking addition to botanical collections.
