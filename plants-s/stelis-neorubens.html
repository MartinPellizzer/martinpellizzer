Stelis neorubens

Stelis neorubens, commonly known as the red-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems that are often reddish in color, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive lip that is often marked with purple or brown markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on tree trunks or branches in the canopy of rainforests.

Stelis neorubens is a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts due to its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
