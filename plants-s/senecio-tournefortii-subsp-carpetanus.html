Senecio tournefortii subsp. carpetanus

Senecio tournefortii subsp.

carpetanus, commonly known as the carpet sedum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a succulent shrub that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments.

The plant features fleshy, blue-green leaves arranged in a dense, carpet-like formation. Its flowers are small, yellowish, and appear in clusters during the warmer months.

It is well adapted to drought conditions and requires minimal water once established.
