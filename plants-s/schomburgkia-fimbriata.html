Schomburgkia fimbriata

Schomburgkia fimbriata, commonly known as the pink lady's slipper, is a striking orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant pink and white flowers, which resemble a lady's slipper, hence its common name. The plant features a single, erect stem that bears a single, showy flower with a distinctive pouch-like lip. Schomburgkia fimbriata thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
