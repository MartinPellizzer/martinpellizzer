Sideritis erythrantha var. cedretorum

Sideritis erythrantha var. cedretorum, commonly known as the red-flowered sida, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and sunny to partially shaded environments.

Sideritis species are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
