Syspone sagittalis

Syspone sagittalis, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a tropical species native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. The plant thrives in moist, well-draining soil and prefers partial to full shade. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale yellow.

Syspone sagittalis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor conditions.
