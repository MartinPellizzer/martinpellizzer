Senecio mesogrammoides

Senecio mesogrammoides, commonly known as the yellow owl's clover, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for ornamental purposes.
