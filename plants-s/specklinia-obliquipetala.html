Specklinia obliquipetala

Specklinia obliquipetala, commonly known as the oblique-leafed lithops, is a species of succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a layer of fine, white wool. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for collectors and gardeners.
