Sedum shengkuangense

Sedum shengkuangense, commonly known as the Shengkuang sedum, is a succulent plant native to China.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, leafy stems and compact growth habit. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves to survive drought conditions.

Sedum shengkuangense is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
