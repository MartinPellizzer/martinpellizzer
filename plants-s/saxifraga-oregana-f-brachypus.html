Saxifraga oregana f. brachypus

Saxifraga oregana f. brachypus, commonly known as the short-stemmed Oregon saxifrage, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It typically thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky habitats. The plant features compact, rosette-forming leaves that are usually hairy and have a bluish-green hue. Its flowers are small, white or pink, and are borne on short, erect stems.

This variety is distinguished by its shorter stems compared to the typical form of Saxifraga oregana.
