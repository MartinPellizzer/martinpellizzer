Solanum plousianthemum var. ugandae

Solanum plousianthemum var. ugandae, commonly known as the Uganda nightshade, is a shrub native to East Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and peppers. The plant features small, yellow flowers and produces berries that range in color from green to red. It is often found in woodland and savanna habitats.

Due to its toxic properties, it is not typically cultivated for agricultural purposes.
