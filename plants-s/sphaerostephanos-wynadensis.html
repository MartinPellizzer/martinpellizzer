Sphaerostephanos wynadensis

Sphaerostephanos wynadensis, commonly known as the Wynad starflower, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a herbaceous plant with distinctive star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant features a short, erect stem with lance-shaped leaves arranged in a basal rosette. Its flowers are actinomorphic, with five petals that are often adorned with a central yellow spot.

Sphaerostephanos wynadensis is adapted to grow in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental beauty.
