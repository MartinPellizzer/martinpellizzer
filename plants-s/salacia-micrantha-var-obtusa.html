Salacia micrantha var. obtusa

Salacia micrantha var. obtusa, commonly known as the blunt-leaf salacia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, fragrant yellow flowers. The plant produces elliptical, fleshy fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal potential, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and traditional herbal practices.
