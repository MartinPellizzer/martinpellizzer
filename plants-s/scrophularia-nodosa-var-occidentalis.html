Scrophularia nodosa var. occidentalis

Scrophularia nodosa var. occidentalis, commonly known as western nodding scrophularia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae, which includes many species with showy, often tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous regions. Its flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom in late spring to early summer.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging environments.
