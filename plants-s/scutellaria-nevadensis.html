Scutellaria nevadensis

Scutellaria nevadensis, commonly known as the Sierra sky pilot, is a small, herbaceous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of California and Nevada.

It typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils at high elevations, often in open meadows or along stream banks. The plant features slender, upright stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are small, tubular, and typically blue or violet, arranged in whorls along the stem.

Scutellaria nevadensis is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and medicinal plants.
