Solanum thonningianum

Solanum thonningianum, commonly known as the African nightshade, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is an annual herb that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features alternate, ovate leaves with a hairy surface and produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. Its fruits are berries that mature to a dark purple color, containing several seeds.

The plant belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known species such as tomatoes and potatoes.
