Solanum schlimii

Solanum schlimii, commonly known as the Schlim's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring woody stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale blue, which bloom during the cooler months. Its fruit is a fleshy berry that turns red when mature, containing several seeds.

Solanum schlimii is part of the nightshade family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes.
