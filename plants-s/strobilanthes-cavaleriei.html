Strobilanthes cavaleriei

Strobilanthes cavaleriei, commonly known as the "Cavalerie's flameflower," is a striking member of the Acanthaceae family native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

This plant is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, often displaying shades of red, orange, and yellow. Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
