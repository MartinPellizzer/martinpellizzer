Synaedrys rassa

Synaedrys rassa, commonly known as the narrow-leaved synaedrys, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the temperate regions of southern Africa.

Synaedrys rassa is often found in grassy areas and along the edges of woodlands, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
