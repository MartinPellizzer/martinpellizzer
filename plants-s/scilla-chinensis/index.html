Scilla Chinensis

Scilla chinensis, also known as Chinese squill, is a striking bulbous plant native to eastern Asia. It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of bell-shaped blue or violet flowers. This perennial plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens. Its ornamental value has led to its cultivation in many parts of the world. However, it is important to note that some species within the Scilla genus can be toxic, requiring caution when handling.
