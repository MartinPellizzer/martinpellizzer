Stenoglottis fimbriata var. saxicola

Stenoglottis fimbriata var. saxicola, commonly known as the rock lily, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, strap-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, fibrous coating. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and resilience, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
