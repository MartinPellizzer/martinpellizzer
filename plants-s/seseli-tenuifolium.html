Seseli tenuifolium

Seseli tenuifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved seseli, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and small, clustered white to pale yellow flowers. Its leaves are narrow and often have a rough texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
