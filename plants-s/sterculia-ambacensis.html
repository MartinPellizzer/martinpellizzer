Sterculia ambacensis

Sterculia ambacensis, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a tropical tree native to regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and large, palmately lobed leaves. The species also plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various wildlife.
