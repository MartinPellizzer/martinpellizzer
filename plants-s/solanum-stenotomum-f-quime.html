Solanum stenotomum f. quime

Solanum stenotomum f. quime, commonly known as the Quime tomato, is a botanical variant of the nightshade family.

It is characterized by its small, cherry-like fruits that are typically red when mature. The plant is native to South America and thrives in warm, sunny climates. Its leaves are broad and ovate, with a slightly hairy texture.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique fruit shape.
