Sarcochilus moorei

Sarcochilus moorei, commonly known as the Moore's sarcochilus, is a terrestrial orchid native to New Zealand.

It features a robust, fleshy rhizome and produces tall, erect stems with broad, strap-shaped leaves. The plant blooms with showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Sarcochilus moorei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is a popular choice for cultivation in cool, humid climates.
