Solanum taulisense

Solanum taulisense, commonly known as the Taulisense nightshade, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale purple, and its leaves are usually ovate with a slightly hairy texture. Solanum taulisense is characterized by its ability to thrive in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in shaded, moist environments.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and less-studied species within the nightshade family.
