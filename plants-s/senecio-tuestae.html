Senecio tuestae

Senecio tuestae, commonly known as the Tuestae groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and grayish-green, with a fuzzy texture.

Senecio tuestae produces small yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting various pollinators.
