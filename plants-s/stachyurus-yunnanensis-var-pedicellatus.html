Stachyurus yunnanensis var. pedicellatus

Stachyurus yunnanensis var. pedicellatus, commonly known as the Yunnan stachyurus, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features gracefully drooping clusters of fragrant, white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that turn yellow in autumn, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its unique floral display and seasonal color changes make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
