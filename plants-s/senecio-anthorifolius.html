Senecio anthorifolius

Senecio anthorifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a composite head. It is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Senecio anthorifolius is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and along rocky outcrops.
