Sicyos microcarpus subsp. puberulus

Sicyos microcarpus subsp.

puberulus, commonly known as the hairy false cucumber, is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family. It is a trailing vine characterized by its hairy, pubescent stems and leaves.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by oval, smooth-skinned fruits. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

Its botanical features make it adaptable to various soil types and climates.
