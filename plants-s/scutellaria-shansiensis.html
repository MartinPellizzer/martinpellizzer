Scutellaria shansiensis

Scutellaria shansiensis, commonly known as the Shanxi skullcap, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. The plant features square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Scutellaria shansiensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
