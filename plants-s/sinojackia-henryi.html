Sinojackia henryi

Sinojackia henryi, commonly known as the Chinese hickory, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Betulaceae family and is characterized by its slender, gray-barked trunk and alternate, simple leaves. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
