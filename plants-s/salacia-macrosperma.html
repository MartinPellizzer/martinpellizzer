Salacia macrosperma

Salacia macrosperma, commonly known as the giant salacia, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. The plant has thick, waxy leaves that help it retain moisture in its humid, tropical habitat. Its fruit is a fleshy, orange-red berry that contains several seeds.

Salacia macrosperma is valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
