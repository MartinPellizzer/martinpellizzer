Salacia ulei

Salacia ulei, commonly known as the yellow salacia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Salicaceae, which includes willows and poplars. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and elongated, slender leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The bark and leaves have been used in traditional medicine for their potential health benefits.
