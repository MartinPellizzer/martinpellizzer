Silene coeli-rosa

Silene coeli-rosa, commonly known as the celestial rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are linear and slightly hairy, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds.

Silene coeli-rosa is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and historical significance in herbal medicine.
