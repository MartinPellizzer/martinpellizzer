Sprekelia howardii

Sprekelia howardii, commonly known as the golden trumpet, is a bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features striking yellow flowers that resemble trumpets, with a vibrant orange throat. The plant produces large, fleshy leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is complemented by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
