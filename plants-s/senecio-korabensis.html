Senecio korabensis

Senecio korabensis, commonly known as the Korab groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and is found in high-altitude areas. It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small yellow flowers that bloom in the summer.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
