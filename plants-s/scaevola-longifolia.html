Scaevola longifolia

Scaevola longifolia, commonly known as the common scaevola, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a low-growing shrub that features small, clustered flowers in shades of blue, purple, and white. The plant has small, narrow leaves and produces numerous stamens that give it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in coastal areas and is often found in sandy soils.

Scaevola longifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in gardens and landscaping.
