Senecio pfisteri

Senecio pfisteri, commonly known as the Pfister's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small yellow flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in dry, rocky environments and is often found in coastal areas.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
