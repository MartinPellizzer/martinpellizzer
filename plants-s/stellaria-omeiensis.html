Stellaria omeiensis

Stellaria omeiensis, commonly known as the Chinese starwort, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and often branched.

Stellaria omeiensis is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and limited distribution.
