Scutellaria sintenisii

Scutellaria sintenisii, commonly known as the Sintenis's skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants known for their medicinal properties. The plant typically grows in rocky or forested habitats, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its distinctive features include small, tubular flowers that are usually blue or violet in color.

Scutellaria sintenisii is valued for its potential traditional medicinal uses, though further research is needed to confirm its efficacy.
