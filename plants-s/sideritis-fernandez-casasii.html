Sideritis fernandez-casasii

Sideritis fernandez-casasii, commonly known as the Canary Islands ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

Sideritis fernandez-casasii thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions.
