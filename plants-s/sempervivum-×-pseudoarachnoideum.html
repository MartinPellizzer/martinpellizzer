Sempervivum × pseudoarachnoideum

Sempervivum × pseudoarachnoideum, commonly known as the snowflake plant, is a hybrid succulent belonging to the Crassulaceae family.

It is characterized by its rosette-forming growth pattern and dense, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a powdery white coating. The plant is native to the Alpine regions of Europe and is adapted to cold, rocky environments. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the summer months.

Due to its hardiness and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine arrangements.
