Schomburgkia carinata

Schomburgkia carinata, commonly known as the ridged orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, dark green leaves with prominent longitudinal ridges. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing numerous fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Schomburgkia carinata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
