Schismatoglottis matangensis

Schismatoglottis matangensis, commonly known as the Matangensis Schismatoglottis, is a tropical plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in the Philippines and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its sympodial growth habit, with elongated, fleshy rhizomes and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, with a distinctive structure that includes a spurred lip.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique foliage and delicate floral display.
