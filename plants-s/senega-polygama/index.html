Senega Polygama

Senega polygama is a rare and endangered plant species native to the southeastern united states. it is commonly known as the broad-leafed goldenroot and is characterized by its unique yellow flowers and medicinal properties. this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rich, organic soils. due to habitat loss and overharvesting, its population has significantly declined over the years. conservation efforts are now underway to protect and preserve this valuable botanical species.
