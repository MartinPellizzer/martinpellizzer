Schoenorchis phitamii f. alba

Schoenorchis phitamii f. alba, commonly known as the white-flowered orchid, is a rare and exquisite member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, white petals and a unique floral structure that sets it apart from other orchid species. Native to specific regions in Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-drained soil. Its growth is slow, requiring careful cultivation to maintain its health and beauty.

Due to its rarity and aesthetic appeal, it is highly valued in botanical gardens and among orchid enthusiasts.
