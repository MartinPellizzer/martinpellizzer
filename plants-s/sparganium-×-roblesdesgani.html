Sparganium × roblesdesgani

Sparganium × roblesdesgani, commonly known as the hybrid bur reed, is a wetland plant characterized by its robust, cylindrical stems and dense, spiky inflorescences.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Sparganium emersum and Sparganium japonicum, exhibiting traits from both parents. The plant thrives in shallow water or moist soil, often found in marshes, ponds, and along the edges of lakes. Its showy, yellowish-green flowers are followed by distinctive, spiny bur-like fruits that aid in seed dispersal.

Sparganium × roblesdesgani plays an important ecological role by providing habitat and food for various aquatic and terrestrial organisms.
