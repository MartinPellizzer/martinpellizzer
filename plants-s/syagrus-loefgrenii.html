Syagrus loefgrenii

Syagrus loefgrenii, commonly known as the Loefgren's palm, is a species native to Brazil, specifically found in the northeastern region.

It is a tall, slender palm with a single trunk and pinnate leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. The plant produces yellow flowers followed by small, round fruits that are edible but not commonly consumed. Syagrus loefgrenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.

Its growth habit and foliage make it a distinctive addition to the palm family.
