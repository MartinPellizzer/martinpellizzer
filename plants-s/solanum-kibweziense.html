Solanum kibweziense

Solanum kibweziense, commonly known as Kibwezi nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the Kibwezi area in Kenya.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and peppers. The plant is characterized by its hairy stems, oval-shaped leaves, and small, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruits are typically berries that change color from green to yellow as they mature.

Due to its limited distribution and unique characteristics, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
