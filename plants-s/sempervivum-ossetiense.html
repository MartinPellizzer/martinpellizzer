Sempervivum ossetiense

Sempervivum ossetiense, commonly known as the Ossetian houseleek, is a hardy succulent belonging to the Crassulaceae family.

It is native to the Caucasus region, particularly Georgia and parts of Russia. The plant features rosettes of fleshy, pointed leaves that are typically blue-green in color. It is well adapted to rocky and alpine environments, thriving in full sun and well-drained soil.

Sempervivum ossetiense is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its compact, decorative appearance.
