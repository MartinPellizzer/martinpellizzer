Stelis minutissima

Stelis minutissima, commonly known as the miniature stelis, is a small epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees in humid, tropical environments. The plant has delicate, wiry stems and produces small, white or pale green flowers. Its leaves are narrow and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Due to its tiny size, it is often overlooked but is valued by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance.
