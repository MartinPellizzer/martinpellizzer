Scleranthus annuus var. delortii

Scleranthus annuus var. delortii, commonly known as the Delort's sickleweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is an annual herb characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, often with a hairy surface. It thrives in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.
