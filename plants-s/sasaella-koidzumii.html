Sasaella koidzumii

Sasaella koidzumii, commonly known as the Koidzumi sasa, is a flowering plant species belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to Japan and is characterized by its dense, clumping growth habit and slender, arching stems. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, greenish-white flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.
