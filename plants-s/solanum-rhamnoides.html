Solanum rhamnoides

Solanum rhamnoides, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its silver-gray leaves and showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces small, toxic berries that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in a variety of soil types and is adaptable to different climates.

Due to its toxic properties, it is important to handle the plant with care.
