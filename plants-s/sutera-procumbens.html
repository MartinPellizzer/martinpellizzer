Sutera procumbens

Sutera procumbens, commonly known as the prostrate sutera, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of only a few centimeters. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are elliptical, covered in fine hairs, and have a slightly aromatic scent.

Sutera procumbens is often found in rocky or coastal areas, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
