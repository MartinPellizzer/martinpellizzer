Syngonium angustatum

Syngonium angustatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that often have a striking variegation of green, white, and sometimes pink. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or greenish in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Syngonium angustatum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
