Seriphidium santonicum var. patens

Seriphidium santonicum var. patens, commonly known as the Patens wormwood, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and aromatic properties. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and gray-green, with a distinctive, pungent odor.

It produces small yellow flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
