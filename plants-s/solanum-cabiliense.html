Solanum cabiliense

Solanum cabiliense, commonly known as the Bicol nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades, tomatoes, and potatoes. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching up to 3 meters in height. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a rough texture, while its flowers are usually white or pale purple.

The fruit is a berry that turns from green to red when mature, containing several seeds.
