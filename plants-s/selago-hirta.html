Selago hirta

Selago hirta, commonly known as hairy selago, is a flowering plant native to various parts of Africa, Asia, and Australia.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and small, often clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to dry conditions. Selago hirta is also known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
