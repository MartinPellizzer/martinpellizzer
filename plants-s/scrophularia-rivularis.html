Scrophularia rivularis

Scrophularia rivularis, commonly known as water scrophularia, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shady environments such as stream banks and woodland edges. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple in color, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Scrophularia rivularis is part of the Scrophulariaceae family, which includes other genera with similar floral structures and ecological preferences.
