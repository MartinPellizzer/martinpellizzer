Struchium africanum

Struchium africanum, commonly known as African goatweed, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in grasslands and along roadsides. It produces small, yellowish flowers that attract various pollinators.

Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.
