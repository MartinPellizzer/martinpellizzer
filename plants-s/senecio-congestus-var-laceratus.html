Senecio congestus var. laceratus

Senecio congestus var. laceratus, commonly known as the shredded senecio, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, cascading clusters of narrow, blue-green leaves that are often tinged with purple. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a distinctive, shredded appearance along their edges. It produces small yellow flowers in late spring to early summer, adding a delicate touch to its ornamental display.

This plant thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
