Senecio loeseneri

Senecio loeseneri, commonly known as the Cape snowflake, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features a low-growing, mat-forming structure with silver-gray foliage. Its flowers are small and white, arranged in a star-like pattern, giving it a delicate appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
