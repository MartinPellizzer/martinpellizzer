Schizaea kikuzatonis

Schizaea kikuzatonis, commonly known as the Japanese spike moss, is a member of the family Schizaeaceae and is native to Japan.

It is a small, terrestrial fern that typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. The plant has a distinctive appearance with its spore-bearing spikes, which are often clustered at the tips of its stems. Its leaves are simple and linear, contributing to its overall delicate and fern-like appearance.

Schizaea kikuzatonis plays a role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for various small organisms in its native ecosystem.
