Stachytarpheta gesnerioides var. glabra

Stachytarpheta gesnerioides var. glabra, commonly known as the velvetleaf or white stachy.

It is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of the Americas. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs, giving it a velvety texture. Its flowers are clustered in terminal panicles and are typically blue or purple in color.

It is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and forest edges.
