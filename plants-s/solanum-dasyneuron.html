Solanum dasyneuron

Solanum dasyneuron, commonly known as the hairy nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, and it produces small, yellowish flowers. Its fruit is a spherical berry that turns from green to red as it matures.

Solanum dasyneuron is often found in dry, open habitats and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
