Statice aragonensis

Statice aragonensis, commonly known as the Aragon carnation, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, which bloom from summer to autumn. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Statice aragonensis is also valued for its ornamental use and is sometimes cultivated for its drought tolerance.
