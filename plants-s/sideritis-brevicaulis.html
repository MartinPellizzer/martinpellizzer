Sideritis brevicaulis

Sideritis brevicaulis, commonly known as the short-stemmed ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments. Its small, tubular flowers are usually pink or purple, and it produces small, hairy seeds.

Sideritis brevicaulis is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
