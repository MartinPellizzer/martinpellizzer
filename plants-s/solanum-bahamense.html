Solanum bahamense

Solanum bahamense, commonly known as the Bahamian nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its small, showy flowers that bloom at night. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are oval-shaped with a rough texture, and it produces small, round berries that are yellow when mature.

The plant is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to saline environments.
