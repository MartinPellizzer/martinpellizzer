Stenolobium fulvum

Stenolobium fulvum, commonly known as the yellow flame tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree with a broad, rounded crown. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow, flame-like flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with numerous small, oval-shaped leaflets.

Stenolobium fulvum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
