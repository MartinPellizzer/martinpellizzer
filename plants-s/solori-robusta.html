Solori robusta

Solori robusta, commonly known as the robust sunflower, is a hardy annual plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features large, showy yellow flowers with a dark central disk, typically reaching heights of 2 to 4 feet. The plant has sturdy, upright stems covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are deeply lobed with a rough texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Solori robusta is known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
