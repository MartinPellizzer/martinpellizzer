Senecio adenophyllus

Senecio adenophyllus, commonly known as the yellow starburst, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fuzzy foliage that is typically bright yellow to green in color. The plant produces striking yellow flowers in late summer, which are arranged in a star-like pattern. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
