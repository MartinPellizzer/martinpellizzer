Stellaria diversiflora

Stellaria diversiflora, commonly known as the diverse-starflower, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and the Pacific Islands.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family, which includes other flowering plants such as carnations and pinks. The plant is characterized by its small, star-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Its delicate appearance makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in suitable climates.
