Stipa kieslingii

Stipa kieslingii, commonly known as the Kiesling's feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and dense, feathery plumes that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is well adapted to harsh environmental conditions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration that helps reduce water loss.

Stipa kieslingii is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and thrive in low-nutrient environments.
