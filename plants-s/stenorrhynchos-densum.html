Stenorrhynchos densum

Stenorrhynchos densum, commonly known as the dense-stemmed orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and dense clusters of leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically orange or yellow in color, arranged in a spike-like inflorescence. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts of tropical flora.
