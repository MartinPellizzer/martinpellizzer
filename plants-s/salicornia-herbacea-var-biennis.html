Salicornia herbacea var. biennis

Salicornia herbacea var. biennis, commonly known as sea bean or glasswort, is a halophytic plant adapted to saline environments.

It is a biennial species, typically growing in coastal areas and salt marshes. The plant features succulent, cylindrical stems that are often red or pink in color. Its leaves are scale-like and reduce in size as the plant matures.

It produces small, green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, contributing to its ecological importance in stabilizing coastal soils.
