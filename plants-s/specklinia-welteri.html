Specklinia welteri

Specklinia welteri, commonly known as the Welter's orchid, is a species of terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, delicate flowers that are typically greenish-white with intricate markings. The plant thrives in shaded, humid environments and prefers well-drained, nutrient-rich soil. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Specklinia welteri is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
