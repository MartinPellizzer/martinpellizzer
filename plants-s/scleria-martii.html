Scleria martii

Scleria martii, commonly known as the African flat sedge, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and grass-like plants. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy environments, showcasing its adaptability to moist soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its inflorescence consists of small, clustered spikelets.
