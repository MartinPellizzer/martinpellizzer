Specklinia puttemansii

Specklinia puttemansii, commonly known as the Puttemans' staghorn fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its unique, tree-like appearance with a thick, woody rhizome. The plant features a dense frond with a broad, shield-shaped base and a long, narrow, feather-like structure called a stipe. Its leaves are typically dark green and glossy, with a velvety texture.

Specklinia puttemansii thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance.
