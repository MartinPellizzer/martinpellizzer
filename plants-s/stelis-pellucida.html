Stelis pellucida

Stelis pellucida, commonly known as the glassy orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, translucent petals that give it its common name. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its flowers are usually small and white or pale pink, with a fragrant aroma.

Stelis pellucida is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
