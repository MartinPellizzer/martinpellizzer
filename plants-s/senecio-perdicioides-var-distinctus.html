Senecio perdicioides var. distinctus

Senecio perdicioides var. distinctus, commonly known as the distinct dwarf groundsel, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in dense, spherical clusters, typical of the Asteraceae family. The plant has a compact growth habit with narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
