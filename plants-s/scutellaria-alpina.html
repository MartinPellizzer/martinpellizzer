Scutellaria alpina

Scutellaria alpina, commonly known as alpine skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, thriving in well-drained, rocky soils. The plant features a square, hairy stem and opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are blue to violet, forming whorled clusters at the top of the stem.

Scutellaria alpina is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
