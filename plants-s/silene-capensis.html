Silene capensis

Silene capensis, commonly known as the Cape bellflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Silene capensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
