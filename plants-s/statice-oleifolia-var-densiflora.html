Statice oleifolia var. densiflora

Statice oleifolia var. densiflora, commonly known as denseflower statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces numerous small, showy flowers in shades of pink, purple, and white. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in full sun and well-drained soil. It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

Statice oleifolia var. densiflora is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
