Scorzonera juncea

Scorzonera juncea, commonly known as the common scorzonera, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its hairy, upright stems and basal leaves that are typically heart-shaped. The plant produces yellow flowers with ray florets, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Scorzonera juncea is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and rocky habitats.

It is sometimes cultivated for its edible roots, which have a slightly sweet and nutty flavor.
