Senebiera

Senebiera, commonly known as *Piper aduncum*, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other species like black pepper. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with heart-shaped leaves and small, greenish-yellow flowers. Its fruit is a berry that turns red when mature, containing several seeds.

Senebiera is valued for its aromatic properties and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
