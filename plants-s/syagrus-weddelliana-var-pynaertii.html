Syagrus weddelliana var. pynaertii

Syagrus weddelliana var. pynaertii, commonly known as the Pynaert's palm, is a species of palm native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant thrives in tropical climates and requires well-drained soil and ample sunlight. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage.

The fruit of this plant is edible, though it is not widely consumed by humans.
