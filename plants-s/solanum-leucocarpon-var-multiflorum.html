Solanum leucocarpon var. multiflorum

Solanum leucocarpon var. multiflorum, commonly known as the white-flowered nightshade, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly serrated margin. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its fruit is a globose, yellowish berry that ripens in the fall.

This species is native to southern Europe and is often found in disturbed soils and rocky areas.
