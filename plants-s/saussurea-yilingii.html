Saussurea yilingii

Saussurea yilingii, commonly known as Yiling yarrow, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its tall, branched stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense, spherical clusters. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and traditional herbal practices.
