Styloncerus pygmaeus

Styloncerus pygmaeus, commonly known as the dwarf ant-like beetle, is a small insect belonging to the family Stylonuridae.

It is characterized by its elongated, segmented body and a distinctive head structure that resembles that of an ant. Due to its small size and cryptic behavior, it is rarely observed and studied in detail.
