Solanum ellipticum var. mollibaccalis

Solanum ellipticum var. mollibaccalis, commonly known as the smooth-leaved nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to parts of South America.

It is characterized by its elliptical, smooth, and waxy leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by berries that mature to a deep purple or black color. It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades such as tomatoes and potatoes.

This species is often found in moist, shaded environments and is known for its ornamental value in horticulture.
