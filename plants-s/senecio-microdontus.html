Senecio microdontus

Senecio microdontus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. It has small, narrow leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
