Stelis perpusilla

Stelis perpusilla, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a small epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant has slender, wiry stems that are often covered in a layer of fine, white hair. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and usually appear in clusters, displaying a range of colors from white to pale pink.

Stelis perpusilla is prized for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
