Sobralia macra

Sobralia macra, commonly known as the giant sobralia, is a striking tropical orchid native to the Caribbean and northern South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 inches in diameter, with vibrant orange and red hues. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its foliage consists of long, narrow leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern.

Sobralia macra is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its dramatic appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
