Scleria verticillata

Scleria verticillata, commonly known as the spiral sedge, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wetlands and marshy areas. The plant features a robust rhizome system and produces clusters of small, three-ranked leaves. Its flowers are arranged in spikelets, with male and female flowers often found on separate spikelets.

Scleria verticillata is notable for its ornamental value and ecological importance in wetland habitats.
