Statice smithii

Statice smithii, commonly known as the sea lavender, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant thrives in saline and well-drained soils, often found in dunes and coastal grasslands. Its narrow, gray-green leaves are adapted to withstand salty air and strong winds.

Statice smithii is valued in horticulture for its long-lasting blooms and ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
