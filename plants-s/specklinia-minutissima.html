Specklinia minutissima

Specklinia minutissima, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a small epiphytic orchid native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tiny, clustered growth habit and delicate, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically arranged in a spike. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its size and beauty, it is a popular choice for miniature orchid collections.
