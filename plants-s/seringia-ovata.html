Seringia ovata

Seringia ovata, commonly known as the oval seringia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
