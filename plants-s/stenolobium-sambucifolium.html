Stenolobium sambucifolium

Stenolobium sambucifolium, commonly known as the blackthorn acacia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows in dry, rocky soils. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are arranged in dense clusters. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a waxy coating.

The fruit is a pod that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.
