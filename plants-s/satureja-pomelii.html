Satureja pomelii

Satureja pomelii, commonly known as lemon sage, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, fuzzy leaves that emit a strong lemony scent. The plant produces small, pale purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

Its essential oils, rich in monoterpene compounds, are valued for their culinary and medicinal uses.
