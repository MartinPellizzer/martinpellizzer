Semiarundinaria albostriata

Semiarundinaria albostriata, commonly known as the white-striped bamboo, is a member of the grass family (Poaceae) and is native to Southeast Asia.

Semiarundinaria albostriata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens and landscapes for its aesthetic beauty.
