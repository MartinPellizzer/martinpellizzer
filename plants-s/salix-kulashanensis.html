Salix kulashanensis

Salix kulashanensis, commonly known as the Kulashan willow, is a species of flowering plant in the genus Salix.

It is native to the mountainous regions of central Asia, particularly in China. This deciduous shrub or small tree typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. Its leaves are elliptical, with a smooth margin and a dark green upper surface.

The plant produces catkin-like inflorescences, which are characteristic of the Salix genus and contain both male and female flowers.
