Saccolabium praemorsum

Saccolabium praemorsum, commonly known as the narrowleaf false thorn.

It is a perennial shrub native to the arid and semi-arid regions of Australia. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, adapted to reduce water loss. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in dense spikes, and bloom during the warmer months.

The plant has a deep root system, allowing it to survive in harsh, dry environments.
