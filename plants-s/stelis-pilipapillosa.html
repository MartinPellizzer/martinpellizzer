Stelis pilipapillosa

Stelis pilipapillosa, commonly known as the pilose stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with fleshy leaves that store water. Its flowers are usually small and white or pale yellow, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

It thrives in humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.
