Sisymbrium ceratophyllum

Sisymbrium ceratophyllum, commonly known as the horned poppy, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its distinctive, horn-like seed pods that give it its common name. The plant produces showy, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in disturbed habitats.

Sisymbrium ceratophyllum is an annual plant that completes its life cycle within a single growing season.
