Stenophyllus antillanus

Stenophyllus antillanus, commonly known as the Caribbean fan palm, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean region.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, fan-shaped leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant belongs to the Arecaceae family, which includes various palm species. Its leaves are often used in traditional crafts and for thatching.
