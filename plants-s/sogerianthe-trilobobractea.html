Sogerianthe trilobobractea

Sogerianthe trilobobractea, commonly known as the three-lobed bract orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, three-lobed bracts that encase the inflorescence, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories where it can receive indirect light. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with a complex structure typical of orchids, featuring specialized petals and sepals.
