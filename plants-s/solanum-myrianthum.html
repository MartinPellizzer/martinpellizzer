Solanum myrianthum

Solanum myrianthum, commonly known as the purple leaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features showy, violet or purple flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are often deeply lobed and have a distinctive purple hue, adding to its ornamental value.

The fruit is a small, berry-like structure that contains several seeds.
