Setaria gussonei

Setaria gussonei, commonly known as the Gussone's millet, is a species of grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are typically green or brown in color. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in sandy or well-drained soils.

Setaria gussonei is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.
