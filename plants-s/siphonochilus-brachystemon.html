Siphonochilus brachystemon

Siphonochilus brachystemon, commonly known as the broad-leaved siphonochilus, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive yellow-green coloration. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
