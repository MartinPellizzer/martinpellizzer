Satureja Grandiflora Var. Baborensis

Satureja grandiflora var. baborensis is a unique and lesser-known member of the Lamiaceae family. Native to the mountainous regions of southern Italy, this plant thrives in rocky and well-drained soils. Known for its aromatic leaves and showy purple flowers, it adds both visual and culinary value to gardens. Often mistaken for its more common relatives, it possesses distinct botanical characteristics. Its rarity and ornamental appeal make it a fascinating subject for botanists and gardeners alike.
