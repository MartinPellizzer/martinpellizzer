Stachys villosissima

Stachys villosissima, commonly known as woolly betony, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy, gray-green leaves and clusters of purple or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.

Stachys villosissima is also used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
