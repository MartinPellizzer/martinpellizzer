Spathiphyllum solomonense

Spathiphyllum solomonense, commonly known as the Solomon Island peace lily, is a flowering plant native to the Solomon Islands.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces an attractive, white spathe that encloses a yellow spadix, typical of the genus Spathiphyllum. It thrives in humid, shady environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Its ability to tolerate low light and moderate watering makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
