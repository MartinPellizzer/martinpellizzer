Spennera sileniflora

Spennera sileniflora, commonly known as the silver leaf spennera, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage and clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Spennera sileniflora is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
