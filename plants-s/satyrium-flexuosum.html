Satyrium flexuosum

Satyrium flexuosum, commonly known as the flexible satyrium, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including countries like Thailand and Vietnam. The plant is characterized by its slender, often climbing stems and small, greenish-white flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.

Due to its delicate appearance and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
