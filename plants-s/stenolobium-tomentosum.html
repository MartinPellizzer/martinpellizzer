Stenolobium tomentosum

Stenolobium tomentosum, commonly known as the woolly legume, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, and it is often found in rocky or sandy soils. Stenolobium tomentosum is adapted to arid conditions and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

Its botanical features make it an important component of local ecosystems.
