Sulcorebutia torancaniana

Sulcorebutia torancaniana, commonly known as the Torancanian crassula, is a species of flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular shape and thick, fleshy leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It is a succulent that thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
