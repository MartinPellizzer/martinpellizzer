Silene haenkeana

Silene haenkeana, commonly known as the New Zealand Christmas tree, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking red or pink flowers during the summer months. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest margins and rocky areas.

Silene haenkeana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also significant in Māori culture.
