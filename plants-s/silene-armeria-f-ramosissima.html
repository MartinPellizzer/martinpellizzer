Silene armeria f. ramosissima

Silene armeria f. ramosissima, commonly known as the flowering sandmat, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright, branched stems and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. Its leaves are linear and often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.
