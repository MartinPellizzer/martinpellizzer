Sisymbrium carolinense

Sisymbrium carolinense, commonly known as Carolina mustards, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is an annual herb that typically grows in disturbed soils, such as roadsides and fields. The plant features slender stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

It plays a role in supporting pollinators and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
