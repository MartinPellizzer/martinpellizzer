Swertia kamoonensis

Swertia kamoonensis, commonly known as Kashmiri gentian, is a flowering plant belonging to the Gentianaceae family.

It is native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas, particularly in regions of Pakistan and India. The plant is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers, which bloom during the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine meadows.

Swertia kamoonensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and has traditional medicinal uses in some cultures.
