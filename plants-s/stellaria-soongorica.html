Stellaria soongorica

Stellaria soongorica, commonly known as the Tibetan starwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has slender, branching stems and narrow, lanceolate leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to harsh, semi-desert environments.

Stellaria soongorica is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats at elevations ranging from 2,000 to 5,000 meters.
