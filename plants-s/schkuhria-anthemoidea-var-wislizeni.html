Schkuhria anthemoidea var. wislizeni

Schkuhria anthemoidea var. wislizeni, commonly known as the wislizeni goldenweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, silvery-green leaves. The plant produces yellow ray florets surrounded by a dense cluster of yellow disk florets, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

Its botanical features make it a valuable plant for ecological restoration and native landscaping.
