Stelis larsenii

Stelis larsenii, commonly known as the Larsen's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, greenish-white or pale yellow blooms with intricate patterns. It thrives in cool, moist environments and requires well-drained, humus-rich soil.

Stelis larsenii is often found in cloud forests and mountainous areas, where it grows among rocks and shaded undergrowth.
