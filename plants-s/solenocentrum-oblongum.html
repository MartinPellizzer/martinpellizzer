Solenocentrum oblongum

Solenocentrum oblongum, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its thick, segmented stems and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically produces flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, often blooming around the holiday season. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in a zigzag pattern along the stems.

Solenocentrum oblongum is a popular ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
