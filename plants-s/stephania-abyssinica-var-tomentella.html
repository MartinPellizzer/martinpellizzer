Stephania abyssinica var. tomentella

Stephania abyssinica var. tomentella, commonly known as the Abyssinian stephania, is a herbaceous plant native to the highlands of Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Menispermaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and compound leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by distinctive, fleshy berries. Its hairy, tomentose leaves and stems are notable botanical features, contributing to its identification.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments at higher elevations.
