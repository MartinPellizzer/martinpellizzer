Scheelea bassleriana

Scheelea bassleriana, commonly known as the bassler's palm, is a species of palm native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by dark blue, spherical berries. Scheelea bassleriana is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Its unique morphology and habitat make it an important component of tropical ecosystems.
