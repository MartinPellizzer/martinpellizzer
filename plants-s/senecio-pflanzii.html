Senecio pflanzii

Senecio pflanzii, commonly known as Pflanz's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small yellow flowers.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
