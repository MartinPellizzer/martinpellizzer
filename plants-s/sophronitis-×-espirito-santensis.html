Sophronitis × espirito-santensis

Sophronitis × espirito-santensis, commonly known as the Espirito Santo orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically display a mix of purple, pink, and white hues. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of other plants without being parasitic, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are often fragrant and bloom during the warmer months, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

This hybrid is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
