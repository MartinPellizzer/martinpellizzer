Sulcorebutia pulchra var. albiareolata

Sulcorebutia pulchra var. albiareolata, commonly known as the White-veined Zebra Cactus, is a succulent native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It features a compact, rounded stem with a distinctive white or pale green pattern that contrasts with its green base. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a vibrant touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight to maintain its unique coloration.

Due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
