Sideritis candida

Sideritis candida, commonly known as white heather, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, often with a minty scent.

Sideritis candida is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is valued for its ornamental beauty and traditional medicinal uses.
