Smilax nebelii

Smilax nebelii, commonly known as the misty smilax, is a climbing shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its woody stems and the presence of sharp, spiny tendrils used for climbing. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits. Its leaves are typically arrow-shaped with a distinctive, waxy surface.

Smilax nebelii thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
