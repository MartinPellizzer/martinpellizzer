Strobilanthes barisanensis

Strobilanthes barisanensis, commonly known as the Barisan flame flower, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Sumatra, Indonesia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Strobilanthes barisanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in the local biodiversity of its native habitat.
