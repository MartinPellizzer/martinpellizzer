Scutia pauciflora

Scutia pauciflora, commonly known as the sparse-flowered scutia, is a member of the Rutaceae family.

It is a small, evergreen shrub native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant features simple, elliptical leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and clustered in inflorescences, often appearing in spring.

Scutia pauciflora is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to various soil conditions.
