Stapelia atropurpurea var. pan

Stapelia atropurpurea var. pan, commonly known as the Purple Natal Stapelia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces striking, lantern-shaped flowers that are typically reddish-purple in color. Its leaves are usually small and may be covered in fine hairs.

This variant is prized for its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
