Saxifraga numidica

Saxifraga numidica, commonly known as the Algerian saxifrage, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features fleshy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, pink or purple flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Saxifraga numidica is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
