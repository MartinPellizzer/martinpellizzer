Stromanthe popolucana

Stromanthe popolucana, commonly known as the Velvet Leaf Stromanthe, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with deep green veins and cream or white margins. The leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, with a velvety texture that gives the plant its common name. It produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season.

This plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to maintain its lush appearance.
