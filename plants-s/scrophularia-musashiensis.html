Scrophularia musashiensis

Scrophularia musashiensis, commonly known as the Japanese figwort, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. The plant features square, hairy stems and opposite leaves that are often lance-shaped. Its flowers are usually purple or pink, clustered in whorls along the stems.

Scrophularia musashiensis is part of the Scrophulariaceae family, which includes many species with medicinal and ornamental value.
