Solanum eitenii

Solanum eitenii, commonly known as the Eiten's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with hairy stems and small, green leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale purple, and it produces berries that contain toxic compounds.

Due to its toxic nature, it is not commonly cultivated for agricultural purposes.
