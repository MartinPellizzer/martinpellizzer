Solanum orthocarpum

Solanum orthocarpum, commonly known as the slender nightshade, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its upright growth habit and hairy stems. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by berries that change from green to red as they mature. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
