Senna wislizeni

Senna wislizeni, commonly known as yellow senna, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters tall, characterized by its bipinnate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces pod-like fruits that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Senna wislizeni is known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for its therapeutic effects.
