Saxifraga tatsienluensis

Saxifraga tatsienluensis, commonly known as the Tatsienlu saxifrage, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the family Saxifragaceae and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves and clusters of small, white or pink flowers. The plant thrives in rocky and alpine environments, often growing in well-drained soils. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in rock gardens.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
