Statice akkensis

Statice akkensis, commonly known as the Akkens statice, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the Middle East and parts of North Africa.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its compact, sparsely branched stems and showy, spike-like inflorescences. The flowers are typically blue to purple in color and are borne on short, stiff pedicels. This plant is adapted to dry, saline soils and is often found in coastal or desert environments.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
