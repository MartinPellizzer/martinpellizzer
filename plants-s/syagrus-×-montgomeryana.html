Syagrus × montgomeryana

Syagrus × montgomeryana, commonly known as the pink palm, is a hybrid palm species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking pink bracts that surround the inflorescence, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows as a tall, slender tree with pinnate leaves that are often used in horticultural displays. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The fruit of Syagrus × montgomeryana is small and edible, though not as widely consumed as other palm fruits.
