Satureja rupestris

Satureja rupestris, commonly known as rock sage or mountain thyme, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub with small, aromatic leaves and pink or white flowers. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in dry, sunny environments. Its essential oils contain compounds like thymol and carvacrol, which contribute to its medicinal and aromatic properties.

Satureja rupestris is valued in both traditional herbal medicine and as a culinary herb.
