Stellamaris

Stellamaris, commonly known as the starflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its star-shaped petals, which give it its common name. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Stellamaris has small, lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow or orange flowers in the spring.

It is often used in ornamental gardening for its delicate and attractive blooms.
