Sulcorebutia krugerae

Sulcorebutia krugerae, commonly known as the Kruger's sun rose, is a compact, flowering cactus native to southern Africa.

It features a globular to cylindrical stem that is typically greenish-gray with a powdery coating. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding vibrant color to its appearance. It is well adapted to arid environments, with succulent stems that store water.

Due to its slow growth and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
