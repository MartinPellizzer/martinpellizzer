Sesbania cavanillesii

Sesbania cavanillesii, commonly known as the yellow sesbania, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are pinnately compound, consisting of several pairs of oval-shaped leaflets.

Sesbania cavanillesii is also known for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it valuable in soil enrichment and ecological restoration.
