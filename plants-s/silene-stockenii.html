Silene stockenii

Silene stockenii, commonly known as the Sierra Nevada silene, is a flowering plant native to the Sierra Nevada mountain range in California.

It is a perennial herb with showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or open habitats, often at high elevations. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and branching.

Silene stockenii plays a role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators and contributing to plant biodiversity in alpine environments.
