Senecio crepidineus

Senecio crepidineus, commonly known as the crested groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing herb with hairy, gray-green leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically forms dense mats and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Senecio crepidineus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
