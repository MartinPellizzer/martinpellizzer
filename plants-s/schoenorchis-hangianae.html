Schoenorchis hangianae

Schoenorchis hangianae, commonly known as the Hangi orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to the mountainous regions of Central Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, terrestrial growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.
