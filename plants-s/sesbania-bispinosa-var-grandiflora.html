Sesbania bispinosa var. grandiflora

Sesbania bispinosa var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered sesbania, is a perennial legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features compound leaves with elliptical leaflets and is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces elongated, slender pods containing several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in agroforestry and as a forage crop.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it beneficial for soil enrichment.
