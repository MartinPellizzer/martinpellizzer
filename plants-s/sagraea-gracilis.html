Sagraea gracilis

Sagraea gracilis, commonly known as the slender sagraea, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30-60 centimeters. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, fragrant, blue to purple flowers during the spring and early summer. Sagraea gracilis thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

It is often used in rock gardens and as a ground cover due to its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
