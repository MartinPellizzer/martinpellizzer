Stapelia variegata var. rugosa

Stapelia variegata var. rugosa, commonly known as the woolly stapelia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems covered in a dense layer of white, woolly hairs that give it its common name. The plant produces striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
