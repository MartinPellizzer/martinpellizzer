Schefflera poomae

Schefflera poomae, commonly known as the Poomae umbrella plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of large, leathery, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.

Schefflera poomae is valued for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to various light conditions.
