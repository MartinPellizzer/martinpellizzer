Saurauia minutiflora

Saurauia minutiflora, commonly known as the dwarf flame tree, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and arranged oppositely on the stems. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. These flowers are followed by bright orange or red berries that contain the seeds.

Saurauia minutiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
