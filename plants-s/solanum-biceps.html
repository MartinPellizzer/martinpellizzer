Solanum biceps

Solanum biceps, commonly known as the two-armed nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its fruits are berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

The leaves are ovate with a rough texture and often have a glandular surface.
