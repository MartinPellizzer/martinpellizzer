Sinosenecio hainanensis

Sinosenecio hainanensis, commonly known as Hainan Senecio, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Hainan Island in China.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has finely divided, grass-like leaves. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators.
