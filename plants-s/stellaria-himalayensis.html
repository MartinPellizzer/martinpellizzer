Stellaria himalayensis

Stellaria himalayensis, commonly known as the Himalayan starwort, is a small flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped white flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, where it thrives in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a hairy texture that helps retain moisture.

Stellaria himalayensis is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to the harsh conditions of high-altitude regions.
