Scopolia tubiflora

Scopolia tubiflora, commonly known as the tuberous scopolia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tuberous roots, which are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Scopolia tubiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical medicinal uses.
