Schefflera ridleyi var. polyandra

Schefflera ridleyi var. polyandra, commonly known as the Philippine umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like clusters of leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant has compound, pinnate leaves with numerous small leaflets arranged in a whorled pattern. It produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by red, fleshy berries that are edible for some bird species.

This species is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
