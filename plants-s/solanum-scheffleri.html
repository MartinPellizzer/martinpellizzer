Solanum scheffleri

Solanum scheffleri, commonly known as the yellow nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by berries that mature to a dark purple color. It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae), which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes.

Solanum scheffleri is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
