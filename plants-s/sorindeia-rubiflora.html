Sorindeia rubiflora

Sorindeia rubiflora, commonly known as the red-flowered sorindeia, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking red blooms that appear during the spring season. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its leaves are elliptical and have a glossy surface, while its stems are slender and often covered in fine hairs.

Sorindeia rubiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
