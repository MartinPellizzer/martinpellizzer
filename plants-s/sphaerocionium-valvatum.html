Sphaerocionium valvatum

Sphaerocionium valvatum, commonly known as the valve sponge or valve plant, is a unique species of aquatic plant characterized by its distinctive, sponge-like structure.

The plant's morphology features a central axis with specialized, valve-like structures that aid in buoyancy and nutrient absorption.
