Sulcorebutia krugerae var. hoffmanniana

Sulcorebutia krugerae var. hoffmanniana, commonly known as the Hoffmann's stone cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to South America.

It features a dense covering of white to pale blue spines, which provide protection from herbivores and intense sunlight. The plant produces yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
