Scolymocephalus spectabilis

Scolymocephalus spectabilis, commonly known as the showy scolymus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to purple, depending on the variety. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Scolymocephalus spectabilis is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
