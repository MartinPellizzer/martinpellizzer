Stelis covilleana

Stelis covilleana, commonly known as the Covilleana orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, tropical environments. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale pink in color. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Stelis covilleana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
