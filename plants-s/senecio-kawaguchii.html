Senecio kawaguchii

Senecio kawaguchii, commonly known as Kawaguchii's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its upright stems and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils in mountainous regions. It produces small, oval-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture.

Senecio kawaguchii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
