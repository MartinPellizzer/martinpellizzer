Senecio crassifolius var. latisectus

Senecio crassifolius var. latisectus, commonly known as the broad-leaved groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its robust, upright stems and large, heart-shaped to broadly ovate leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in dense, branched inflorescences, typically blooming in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

This variant is noted for its distinctive foliage and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
