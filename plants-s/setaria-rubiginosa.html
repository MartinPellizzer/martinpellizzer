Setaria rubiginosa

Setaria rubiginosa, commonly known as red panicgrass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Its leaves are often covered in fine hairs, giving them a rough texture.

Setaria rubiginosa is adaptable to various soil types and is often found in disturbed areas or along roadsides.
