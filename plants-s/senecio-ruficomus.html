Senecio ruficomus

Senecio ruficomus, commonly known as the red-stemmed groundsel, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features slender, hairy stems that often turn reddish in color, particularly in full sun. The plant produces small, yellow composite flowers arranged in clusters, typical of the Asteraceae family. Its leaves are usually narrow and slightly lobed, with a rough texture.

Senecio ruficomus thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
