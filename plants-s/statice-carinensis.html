Statice carinensis

Statice carinensis, commonly known as the Carpathian statice, is a flowering plant native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its delicate, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to harsh alpine conditions. Its small, colorful flowers range from pink to purple, adding visual interest to its natural environment.

Statice carinensis is often used in floral arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms and aesthetic appeal.
