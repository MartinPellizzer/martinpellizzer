Spiraea × fontenaysii f. rosea

Spiraea × fontenaysii f. rosea, commonly known as the roseflower weigela, is a hybrid shrub prized for its ornamental value.

It belongs to the Spiraeaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant floral display. The plant produces showy clusters of pink or rose-colored flowers in early summer, which are borne on slender, arching stems. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, fertile soil.

This cultivar is popular in gardens for its long blooming period and low maintenance requirements.
