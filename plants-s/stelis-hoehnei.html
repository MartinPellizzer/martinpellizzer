Stelis hoehnei

Stelis hoehnei, commonly known as the Hoehne's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It typically grows on tree branches or rocks, relying on its roots for moisture and nutrients rather than soil. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are often white or pale pink, with a distinctive appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires careful cultivation to mimic its natural habitat.

Stelis hoehnei is valued for its ornamental beauty and is a subject of interest in botanical gardens and orchid enthusiasts.
