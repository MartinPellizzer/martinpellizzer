Sphagneticola Calendulacea

Sphagneticola calendulacea is a unique flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and South America. It is known for its vibrant yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a striking visual display. This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sun exposure. It is often used in ornamental gardening for its beauty and ability to attract pollinators. Its scientific name reflects its historical use in traditional medicine and its ecological significance in native habitats.
