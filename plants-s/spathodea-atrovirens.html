Spathodea atrovirens

Spathodea atrovirens, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a tropical tree native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red and orange flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual impact. The plant has large, palmate leaves that are dark green and glossy, providing a lush backdrop to its vibrant blossoms. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

The tree is also valued for its fast growth and ability to provide shade in urban and rural landscapes.
