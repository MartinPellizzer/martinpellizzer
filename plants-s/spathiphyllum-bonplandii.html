Spathiphyllum bonplandii

Spathiphyllum bonplandii, commonly known as the Bonplandian peace lily, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its spathe and spadix inflorescence structure. The plant features broad, dark green, heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and often variegated. It produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that are enclosed by a large, showy leaf-like structure called a spathe.

Spathiphyllum bonplandii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
