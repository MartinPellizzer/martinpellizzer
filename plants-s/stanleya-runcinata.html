Stanleya runcinata

Stanleya runcinata, commonly known as the Colorado larkspur, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the family Ranunculaceae and is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers. The plant has palmately lobed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It typically grows in moist, rocky habitats at high elevations.
