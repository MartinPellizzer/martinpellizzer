Stipa sczerbakovii

Stipa sczerbakovii, commonly known as the Sczerbakovii needlegrass, is a species of grass native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical spikelets and fine, wiry leaves. This plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as steppes and rocky slopes. Its flowering period occurs in the summer months, and it produces small, pale green to brown flowers.

Stipa sczerbakovii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in arid environments.
