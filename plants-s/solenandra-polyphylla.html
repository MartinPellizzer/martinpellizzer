Solenandra polyphylla

Solenandra polyphylla, commonly known as the silver sword, is a striking tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It features a rosette of broad, silver-green leaves that are stiff and sword-shaped, giving it its common name. The plant produces striking orange or red flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a notable species in horticulture and botany.
