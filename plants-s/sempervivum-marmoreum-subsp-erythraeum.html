Sempervivum marmoreum subsp. erythraeum

Sempervivum marmoreum subsp.

erythraeum, commonly known as the Red Sandstone Sempervivum, is a succulent plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features compact rosettes of fleshy, bluish-green leaves with a distinctive red or pinkish hue on the upper surfaces.

The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its hardy nature allows it to survive in harsh conditions, including drought and frost.
