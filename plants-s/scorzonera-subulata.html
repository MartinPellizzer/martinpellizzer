Scorzonera subulata

Scorzonera subulata, commonly known as the slender scorzonera, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a perennial species that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils at high altitudes. The plant features narrow, needle-like leaves and produces yellow flowers with five petals. Its root system is fibrous and often used in traditional medicine.

Scorzonera subulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in alpine environments.
