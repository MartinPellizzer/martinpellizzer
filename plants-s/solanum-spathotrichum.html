Solanum spathotrichum

Solanum spathotrichum, commonly known as the purple leaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive purple leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces berries that are typically green when unripe and turn red when mature. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in shaded, moist environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
