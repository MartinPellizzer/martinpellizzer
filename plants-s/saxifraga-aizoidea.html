Saxifraga aizoidea

Saxifraga aizoidea, commonly known as the ice plant or coastal saxifrage, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Aizoaceae, also known as the ice plant family, and is characterized by its succulent leaves and vibrant flowers. The plant thrives in sandy, coastal environments and is adapted to withstand salt spray and strong winds. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, and yellow, typically blooming from late spring to early summer.

Saxifraga aizoidea is a hardy, low-maintenance plant often used in rock gardens and coastal landscaping.
