Schefflera polita

Schefflera polita, commonly known as the smooth schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, palmate leaves that are smooth and glossy, typically arranged in a compact, umbrella-like pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by black, spherical berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.

Schefflera polita is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
