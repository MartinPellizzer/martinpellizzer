Syringa Vulgaris F. Plena

Plena lilac, scientifically known as Syringa vulgaris f. plena, is a striking ornamental shrub prized for its dense, fragrant blooms. This cultivar of the common lilac is celebrated for its abundant clusters of rich purple flowers that appear in late spring. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for small gardens, borders, or as a specimen plant. The plant is hardy and adaptable, thriving in a variety of soil types and climates. With its showy display and pleasant fragrance, plena lilac is a popular choice for adding color and charm to landscapes.
