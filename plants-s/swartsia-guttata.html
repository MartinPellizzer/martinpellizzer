Swartsia guttata

Swartsia guttata, commonly known as the yellow star orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow petals and sepals. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in late summer. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Swartsia guttata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
