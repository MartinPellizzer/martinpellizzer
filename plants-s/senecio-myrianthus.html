Senecio myrianthus

Senecio myrianthus, commonly known as the blue ice plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. It produces small, wind-pollinated flowers that develop into capsule fruits.

Senecio myrianthus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
