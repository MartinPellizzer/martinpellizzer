Selaginella kittyae var. aeneifolia

Selaginella kittyae var. aeneifolia, commonly known as the bronze spikemoss, is a rare and unique species of spikemoss native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its striking bronzy foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance compared to other members of the genus. The plant typically grows in moist, acidic soils in shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are elongated and spiral around the stems, forming dense, low-growing mats.
