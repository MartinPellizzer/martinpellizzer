Sorbus aria var. danubialis

Sorbus aria var. danubialis, commonly known as the Danube rowan, is a variant of the European rowan tree.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Central and Eastern Europe. The plant features small, white flowers that appear in clusters, followed by red, berry-like fruits. Its leaves are oval with serrated edges, and it typically grows in moist, woodland habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
