Scrophularia provincialis

Scrophularia provincialis, commonly known as the provincial scrophularia, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in sunny to partially shaded environments. The plant features slender, erect stems that bear opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves with hairy surfaces. Its flowers are usually pink to purple, arranged in clusters, and bloom from late spring to early summer.

Scrophularia provincialis is part of the Scrophulariaceae family, known for its medicinal and ornamental value in some regions.
