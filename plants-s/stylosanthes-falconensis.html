Stylosanthes falconensis

Stylosanthes falconensis, commonly known as the Falcon's wing or velvet bean, is a tropical leguminous plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces cylindrical pods containing kidney-shaped seeds, which are often used in animal feed and soil improvement. Stylosanthes falconensis is known for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it a valuable plant in sustainable agriculture.

It thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated in regions with poor soil conditions.
