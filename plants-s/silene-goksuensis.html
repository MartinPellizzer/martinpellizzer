Silene goksuensis

Silene goksuensis, commonly known as the Goksu rockflower, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pinkish-purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Silene goksuensis has small, lance-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
