Senecio blinii

Senecio blinii, commonly known as the Blue Senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs.

Senecio blinii is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant color and ease of care.
