Stelis clipeus

Stelis clipeus, commonly known as the helmet orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, helmet-shaped flower that gives it its common name. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in tropical rainforests, exhibiting an epiphytic growth habit. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow with a darkened lip, and it blooms during the wet season.

Stelis clipeus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
