Scabiosa australis

Scabiosa australis, commonly known as the Australian lady's mantle, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of silvery-green, hairy leaves and produces clusters of pink or white flowers. The plant is typically found in dry, sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is a perennial species that is drought-tolerant and well adapted to Mediterranean climates.

The flowers attract a variety of pollinators, including bees and butterflies.
