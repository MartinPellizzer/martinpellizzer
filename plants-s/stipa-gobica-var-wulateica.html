Stipa gobica var. wulateica

Stipa gobica var. wulateica, commonly known as the Wulateica feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and fluffy, silver-gray panicles that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as steppes and rocky slopes. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique visual characteristics.
