Scindapsus anomalus

Scindapsus anomalus, commonly known as the silver vine, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with striking silver veins. The plant is a climbing species that produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by bright red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Scindapsus anomalus is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and ease of care.
