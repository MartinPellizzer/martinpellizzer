Stevia Eupatoria

Stevia eupatoria is a rare and lesser-known plant belonging to the Scrophulariaceae family. Native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe and parts of North Africa, it thrives in rocky and arid environments. This plant is known for its small, fragrant flowers and its ability to withstand harsh conditions. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated and often overlooked in botanical discussions. Its unique characteristics make it a fascinating subject for both researchers and nature enthusiasts.
