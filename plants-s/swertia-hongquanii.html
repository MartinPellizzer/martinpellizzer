Swertia hongquanii

Swertia hongquanii, commonly known as Hongquan gentian, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and is adapted to cool, moist environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often branched.

Swertia hongquanii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
