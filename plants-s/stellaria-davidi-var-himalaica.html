Stellaria davidi var. himalaica

Stellaria davidi var. himalaica, commonly known as Himalayan starwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas, where it thrives in cold, rocky environments. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, giving it a star-like appearance. Its leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, adapted to withstand harsh mountain conditions.

Stellaria davidi var. himalaica is often found in alpine meadows and is valued for its ornamental qualities in horticulture.
