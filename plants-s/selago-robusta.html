Selago robusta

Selago robusta, commonly known as the robust selago, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Selago robusta thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
