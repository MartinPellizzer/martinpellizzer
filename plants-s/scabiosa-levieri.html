Scabiosa levieri

Scabiosa levieri, commonly known as the Italian spring gentian, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, blue to purple flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. It prefers well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Scabiosa levieri is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
