Sanguisorba officinalis f. auriculata

Sanguisorba officinalis f. auriculata, commonly known as the auriculate sanguisorba, is a compact, herbaceous perennial with distinctive, auriculate (ear-shaped) leaves.

It produces upright spikes of bright red to orange flowers in late summer, which are clustered in dense, showy inflorescences. The plant is native to Europe and thrives in moist, well-drained soils, often found in woodland or meadow habitats. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in gardens for its vibrant fall color and ecological benefits.

The species is also noted for its historical use in traditional medicine.
