Scutellaria hunanensis

Scutellaria hunanensis, commonly known as Hunan skullcap, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features square stems and opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, tubular flowers are typically blue or purple, arranged in whorls along the stem.

Hunan skullcap is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
