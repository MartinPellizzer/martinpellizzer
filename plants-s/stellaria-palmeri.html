Stellaria palmeri

Stellaria palmeri, commonly known as Palmer's starwort, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a low-growing annual herb with small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender stems and lance-shaped leaves, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.

Its name refers to the star-like appearance of its flowers, which are arranged in a cluster.
