Smilax nilagirensis

Smilax nilagirensis, commonly known as the Nilagiri smilax, is a climbing shrub native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is characterized by its woody stems and spiny, arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by bright red, fleshy berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in dense undergrowth.

Smilax nilagirensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local medicine.
