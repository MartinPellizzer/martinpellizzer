Stellaria maximowiczii

Stellaria maximowiczii, commonly known as mouse-ear chickweed, is a low-growing perennial plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It features small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters, typically from spring to early summer. The plant has hairy, spoon-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, white pubescence. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and rocky slopes.

Stellaria maximowiczii is often found in disturbed soils and is considered a common weed in agricultural and garden settings.
