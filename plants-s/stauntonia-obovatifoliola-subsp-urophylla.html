Stauntonia obovatifoliola subsp. urophylla

Stauntonia obovatifoliola subsp.

urophylla, commonly known as the broad-leafed stauntonia, is a species native to Southeast Asia. It is a climbing plant characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy leaves and fragrant, white flowers.

The plant thrives in humid, tropical climates and requires well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.

Its growth habit and foliage make it a popular choice for vertical gardening and shaded areas.
