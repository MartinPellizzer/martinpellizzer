Syagrus drudei

Syagrus drudei, commonly known as the "palm of the Amazon," is a species of palm tree native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of long, feather-like leaves. The plant typically grows in humid, tropical climates and requires well-drained soil to thrive. Syagrus drudei is often found in lowland areas and is adapted to the dense canopy of the rainforest.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular ornamental plant in horticulture.
