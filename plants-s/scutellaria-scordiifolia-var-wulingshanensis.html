Scutellaria scordiifolia var. wulingshanensis

Scutellaria scordiifolia var. wulingshanensis, commonly known as the Wulingshan skullcap, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

Its leaves and flowers are used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.
