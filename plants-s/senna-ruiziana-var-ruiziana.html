Senna ruiziana var. ruiziana

Senna ruiziana var. ruiziana, commonly known as the yellow senna, is a tropical shrub native to regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with serrated edges and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine.

The botanical features of this species make it well-suited for warm, humid climates.
