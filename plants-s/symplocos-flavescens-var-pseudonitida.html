Symplocos flavescens var. pseudonitida

Symplocos flavescens var. pseudonitida, commonly known as the glossy flameberry, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green on top with a pale underside. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical forests.

The berries are edible but are typically consumed by birds and other wildlife.
