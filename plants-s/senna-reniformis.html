Senna reniformis

Senna reniformis, commonly known as the sickle senna, is a flowering plant native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters tall, characterized by its pinnately compound leaves with sickle-shaped leaflets. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which are followed by pod-like fruits containing seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in dry, open areas.

Senna reniformis is also known for its use in traditional medicine and as a source of natural dyes.
