Sideritis getula

Sideritis getula, commonly known as the Greek ironwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and small, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to pink. Sideritis getula typically thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats.

It is also valued for its medicinal properties, traditionally used to treat respiratory and digestive ailments.
