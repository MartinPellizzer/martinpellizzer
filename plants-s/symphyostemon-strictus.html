Symphyostemon strictus

Symphyostemon strictus, commonly known as the rigid symphyostemon, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often slightly hairy. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually in inflorescences, and can be white or pale yellow.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its foliage and floral display.
