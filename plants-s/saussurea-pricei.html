Saussurea pricei

Saussurea pricei, commonly known as the Himalayan fleur-de-lis, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking, pale blue to violet ray florets and yellow disk florets. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in cool and moist conditions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

Saussurea pricei is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens that mimic its natural habitat.
