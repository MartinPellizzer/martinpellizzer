Stelis ovatilabia

Stelis ovatilabia, commonly known as the ovate-labial stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with fleshy leaves that are ovate in shape. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically arranged in a raceme. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Stelis ovatilabia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
