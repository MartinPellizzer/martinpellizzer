Sonerila calaminthifolia

Sonerila calaminthifolia, commonly known as the violet-leaved sonerila, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

Sonerila calaminthifolia is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
