Sparattanthelium amazonum subsp. guatemalense

Sparattanthelium amazonum subsp.

guatemalense, commonly known as the Guatemalan spathalium, is a member of the Araceae family. It is a herbaceous plant native to the humid lowland regions of Central America, particularly Guatemala.

The plant features a short, rhizomatous stem with broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of white, waxy hairs. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a spathe, typical of the Araceae family.

It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
