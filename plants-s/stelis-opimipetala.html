Stelis opimipetala

Stelis opimipetala, commonly known as the pink-stemmed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, often displaying shades of white, pink, and purple.

Stelis opimipetala thrives in humid environments with bright, indirect light.
