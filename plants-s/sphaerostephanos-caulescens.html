Sphaerostephanos caulescens

Sphaerostephanos caulescens, commonly known as the globular starflower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a notable species in the Asteraceae family.
