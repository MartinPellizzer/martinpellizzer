Styrax rufopilosus

Styrax rufopilosus, commonly known as the red-hair styrax, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its distinctive red-pubescent stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. Its bark is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

Styrax rufopilosus is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique foliage.
