Scorzonera austriaca subsp. bupleurifolia

Scorzonera austriaca subsp.

bupleurifolia, commonly known as Austrian scorzonera, is a perennial herb native to Central Europe. It features a robust root system and produces basal leaves that are deeply lobed and hairy.

The plant blooms with yellow flowers that have five petals and a distinctive tubular throat. It prefers well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and historical use in traditional medicine.
