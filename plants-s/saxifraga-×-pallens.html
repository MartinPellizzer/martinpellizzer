Saxifraga × pallens

Saxifraga × pallens, commonly known as the pink saxifrage, is a hybrid plant that exhibits a range of flower colors, from white to deep pink.

It is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe and parts of Asia. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, where it thrives in well-drained, acidic soils. Its leaves are usually basal, with a rosette structure, and the flowers are clustered in racemes.

Saxifraga × pallens is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to flourish in challenging environments.
