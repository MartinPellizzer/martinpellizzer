Sarcoyucca mohavensis

Sarcoyucca mohavensis, commonly known as the Mohave yucca, is a perennial plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its tall, sword-shaped leaves and produces large, showy white flowers in the spring. The plant is adapted to arid environments and has a deep root system for water absorption. It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is closely related to other yucca species.

Its botanical features make it well-suited for xeriscaping and desert gardens.
