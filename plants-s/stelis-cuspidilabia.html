Stelis cuspidilabia

Stelis cuspidilabia, commonly known as the hooded orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit and produces striking, hood-shaped flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to orange, with a prominent lip that is often darker in hue.

Stelis cuspidilabia is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
