Schizeilema ranunculus

Schizeilema ranunculus, commonly known as the water buttercup, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, bright yellow flowers with five petals and a hairy, lobed leaf structure. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy environments, thriving in moist soils near water bodies. Its leaves are often floating or submerged, adapted to aquatic conditions.
