Salsola dzhungarica

Salsola dzhungarica, commonly known as the Dzungarian saltbush, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in desert and steppe regions. The plant has succulent leaves and small, greenish flowers that appear in clusters. It is adapted to withstand extreme temperatures and low water availability.

Salsola dzhungarica plays a role in stabilizing soils and providing forage for livestock in its native habitat.
