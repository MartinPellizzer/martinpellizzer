Stachyurus praecox subsp. lancifolius

Stachyurus praecox subsp.

lancifolius, commonly known as the lanceleaf stachyurus, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia. It features attractive, lance-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and turn yellow in autumn.

The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in early spring, which are often white or pale pink. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

This species is valued in gardens for its ornamental foliage and early blooming habit.
