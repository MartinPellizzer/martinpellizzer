Schefflera clarkeana

Schefflera clarkeana, commonly known as the Clarke's umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, umbrella-like arrangement of large, leathery, glossy leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in umbels, followed by bright red berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.

Schefflera clarkeana is valued for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to various light conditions.
