Senecio × kleiniiformis

Senecio × kleiniiformis, commonly known as the African daisy, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its daisy-like flowers, which typically feature yellow central cones surrounded by white to pale blue ray florets. The plant is native to southern Africa and thrives in well-drained soils, often found in rocky or sandy habitats. It is a perennial shrub that can grow up to 30 cm in height, with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves.

Senecio × kleiniiformis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
