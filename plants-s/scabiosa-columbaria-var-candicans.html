Scabiosa columbaria var. candicans

Scabiosa columbaria var. candicans, commonly known as the showy scabious, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features finely divided, fern-like leaves that are aromatic and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its nectar-rich flowers attract bees and other pollinators, contributing to its ecological value.
