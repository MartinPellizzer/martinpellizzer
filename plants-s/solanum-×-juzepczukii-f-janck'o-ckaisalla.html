Solanum × juzepczukii f. janck'o-ckaisalla

Solanum × juzepczukii f. janck'o-ckaisalla, commonly known as the Janck'o-ckaisalla nightshade, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and oval, dark green leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically purple or white in color. Its fruits are berries that turn from green to red as they mature, containing several seeds.

This species is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ornamental qualities.
