Stelis saavedrensis

Stelis saavedrensis, commonly known as the Saavedra's spider orchid, is a rare orchid species native to the mountainous regions of Ecuador.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structure. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale green in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Stelis saavedrensis is considered a vulnerable species.
