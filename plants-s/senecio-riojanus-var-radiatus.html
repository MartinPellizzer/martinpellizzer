Senecio riojanus var. radiatus

Senecio riojanus var. radiatus, commonly known as the Radiate Senecio, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of northern Spain.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically features a low-growing, mat-forming structure with narrow, silvery-green leaves. Its flowers consist of both ray and disk florets, producing a striking yellow-orange coloration.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy ornamental plant in suitable climates.
