Stelis digitifera

Stelis digitifera, commonly known as the digitate stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. The plant features a short, branched stem with numerous leaf-like structures that are often mistaken for true leaves. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and usually white or pale yellow, arranged in a loose cluster.

Stelis digitifera is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
