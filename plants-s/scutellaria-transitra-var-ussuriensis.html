Scutellaria transitra var. ussuriensis

Scutellaria transitra var. ussuriensis, commonly known as Ussuri scullion, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern regions of Russia and parts of eastern China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky slopes. Its flowers are small, tubular, and usually blue or purple in color, arranged in whorled clusters.

The species is noted for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
