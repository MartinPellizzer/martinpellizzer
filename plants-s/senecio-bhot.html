Senecio bhot

Senecio bhot, commonly known as the Himalayan groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, thriving in cool and moist conditions. It has slender stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Senecio bhot is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate white and yellow flowers.
