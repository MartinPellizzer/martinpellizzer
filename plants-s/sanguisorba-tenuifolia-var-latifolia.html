Sanguisorba tenuifolia var. latifolia

Sanguisorba tenuifolia var. latifolia, commonly known as broadleaf sanguisorba, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It features basal leaves that are broad and lance-shaped, with a distinctive reddish or purple hue on the underside. The plant produces upright, branched inflorescences of small, pinkish-red flowers in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native gardens.
