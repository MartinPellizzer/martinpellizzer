Salvia Officinalis Var. Variegata

Salvia officinalis var. variegata is a striking variant of the common sage plant known for its ornamental value and unique foliage. This plant features vibrant, variegated leaves that display shades of white, yellow, and green, making it a popular choice for gardens and indoor spaces. While it shares the aromatic properties of its parent species, it is often cultivated more for its visual appeal than for medicinal use. Its adaptability to various growing conditions makes it a versatile addition to both novice and experienced gardeners. Despite its beauty, it requires proper care to thrive and maintain its distinctive appearance.
