Sclerostemma altissimum

Sclerostemma altissimum, commonly known as the giant rock orchid, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, showy flowers that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its unique morphology includes fleshy leaves and a complex inflorescence that attracts various pollinators.

Sclerostemma altissimum is a member of the orchid family and is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological significance.
