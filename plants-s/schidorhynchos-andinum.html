Schidorhynchos andinum

Schidorhynchos andinum, commonly known as the Andean dragon tree, is a unique plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

Schidorhynchos andinum is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments and thrives in well-drained soils.
