Spathiphyllum monachinoi var. perangustum

Spathiphyllum monachinoi var. perangustum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf peace lily, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves that are dark green and glossy. The plant produces striking white spathes that enclose small, greenish-white flowers. It is a popular houseplant due to its air-purifying qualities and low maintenance requirements.

Its botanical features make it well-suited for both indoor and shaded outdoor environments.
