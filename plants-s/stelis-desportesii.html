Stelis desportesii

Stelis desportesii, commonly known as the Desportes's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and usually white or pale pink, arranged in a raceme.

Stelis desportesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
