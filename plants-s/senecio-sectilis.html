Senecio sectilis

Senecio sectilis, commonly known as the narrowleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open habitats. It features narrow, linear leaves and produces small yellow flowers that attract pollinators.

Senecio sectilis is generally hardy and can thrive in well-drained conditions.
