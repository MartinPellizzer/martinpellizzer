Scabiosa dubia

Scabiosa dubia, commonly known as the alpine scabious, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, pink to white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, often forming a basal rosette.

Scabiosa dubia is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
