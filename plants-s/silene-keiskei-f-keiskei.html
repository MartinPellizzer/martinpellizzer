Silene keiskei f. keiskei

Silene keiskei f. keiskei, commonly known as the desert sandflower, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to withstand extreme temperatures and drought conditions.
