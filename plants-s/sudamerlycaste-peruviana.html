Sudamerlycaste peruviana

Sudamerlycaste peruviana, commonly known as the Peruvian flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting a variety of pollinators. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement, and its bark is smooth and often reddish in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The tree can grow up to 20 meters tall and is well-suited for ornamental landscaping in tropical and subtropical regions.
