Senecio kawakamii

Senecio kawakamii, commonly known as Kawakami's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its delicate, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky slopes. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are slender and branching.

Senecio kawakamii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its subtle beauty.
