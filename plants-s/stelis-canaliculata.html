Stelis canaliculata

Stelis canaliculata, commonly known as the channel-ribbed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, pendulous inflorescence and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic growth habit. Its leaves are usually narrow and arranged in a rosette pattern, and the flowers often display a combination of white, pink, and purple hues.

Stelis canaliculata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
