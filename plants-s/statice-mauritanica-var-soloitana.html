Statice mauritanica var. soloitana

Statice mauritanica var. soloitana, commonly known as the solitary statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and produces dense clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically gray-green in color.

This variety is prized for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
