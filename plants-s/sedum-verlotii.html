Sedum verlotii

Sedum verlotii, commonly known as the Verlot's stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow to orange. The plant is adapted to rocky and arid environments, making it highly drought-tolerant. Its leaves are typically blue-green in color and arranged in a compact rosette pattern.

Sedum verlotii is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
