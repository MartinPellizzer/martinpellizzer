Strobilanthes flexicaulis var. flexicaulis

Strobilanthes flexicaulis var. flexicaulis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved flameflower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often resembling flames. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in moist, well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a smooth surface and a slightly waxy texture.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral displays.
