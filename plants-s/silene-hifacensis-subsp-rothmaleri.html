Silene hifacensis subsp. rothmaleri

Silene hifacensis subsp.

rothmaleri, commonly known as the Hifafu Hottentot, is a flowering plant native to the island of Socotra. It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive pink or purple flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.
