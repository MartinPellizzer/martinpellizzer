Senecio karstenii var. lanceolata

Senecio karstenii var. lanceolata, commonly known as the Karsten's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It produces yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, spiny fruits.

This species is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
