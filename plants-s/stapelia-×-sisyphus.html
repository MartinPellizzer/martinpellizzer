Stapelia × sisyphus

Stapelia × sisyphus, commonly known as the hybrid stapelia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its unique, fleshy, and often tubular-shaped stems. The plant produces striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, which attract pollinators like flies and beetles. It is a hybrid between Stapelia hirtella and Stapelia sisyphus, resulting in a plant that combines traits from both parent species.

Stapelia × sisyphus is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
