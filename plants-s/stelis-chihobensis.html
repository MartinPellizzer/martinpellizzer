Stelis chihobensis

Stelis chihobensis, commonly known as the Chihobensis orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows on tree branches or rocks, showcasing its epiphytic nature. Its leaves are usually narrow and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water retention.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is noted for its ornamental value in horticulture.
