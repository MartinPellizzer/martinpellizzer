Stellaria longipes var. humilis

Stellaria longipes var. humilis, commonly known as the dwarf starwort, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features small, white star-shaped flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

The species is characterized by its delicate appearance and is often found in damp, calcareous soils.
