Spilanthes tinctoria

Spilanthes tinctoria, commonly known as yellow rocket, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes plants like mustard and cabbage. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and pinnately compound leaves. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.

Spilanthes tinctoria is also known for its potential medicinal properties.
