Stipa nitens

Stipa nitens, commonly known as the silver grass, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and narrow, silver-gray leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fluffy seed heads that are often used in ornamental gardening due to their aesthetic appeal. Stipa nitens thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid climates.

It plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various small organisms.
