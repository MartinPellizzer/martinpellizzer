Scrophularia rockii

Scrophularia rockii, commonly known as the rock scrophularia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of central and eastern China.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in alpine or subalpine environments. The plant features upright stems with opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are showy, with tubular corollas that are typically pink or purple in color, and it blooms from late spring to early summer.

Scrophularia rockii is valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
