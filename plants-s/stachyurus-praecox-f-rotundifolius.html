Stachyurus praecox f. rotundifolius

Stachyurus praecox f. rotundifolius, commonly known as the rounded-leaf stachyurus, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its ovate, dark green leaves that are rounded at the base, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in early spring, typically in shades of white or pale yellow, which are clustered in pendulous racemes. Its foliage remains attractive throughout the growing season, making it a valuable ornamental plant in gardens.

It thrives in partial shade to full sun and is relatively low maintenance, suitable for smaller spaces.
