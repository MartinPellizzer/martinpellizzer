Schismatoglottis rizalensis

Schismatoglottis rizalensis, commonly known as the Rizal's slit-lip orchid, is a rare and endangered species native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, narrow leaves and delicate, spurred flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in montane forests. Its botanical significance lies in its limited distribution and the challenges it faces due to habitat loss.

Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this rare botanical gem.
