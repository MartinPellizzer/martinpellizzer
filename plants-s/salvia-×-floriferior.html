Salvia × floriferior

Salvia × floriferior, commonly known as the hybrid sage, is a perennial plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant blue to purple flowers and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly fuzzy texture. This plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and for its aromatic properties.

Salvia × floriferior is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
