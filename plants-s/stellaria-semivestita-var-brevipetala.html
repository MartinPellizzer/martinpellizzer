Stellaria semivestita var. brevipetala

Stellaria semivestita var. brevipetala, commonly known as the short-petioled starwort, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family, characterized by its small, delicate flowers and often hairy foliage. The plant typically grows in moist, grassy habitats and has a low, spreading growth habit. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with five petals arranged in a star-like pattern, giving it its common name.

The leaves are opposite and often have a short petiole, distinguishing it from other varieties of Stellaria.
