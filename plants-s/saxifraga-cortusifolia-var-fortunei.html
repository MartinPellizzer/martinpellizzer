Saxifraga cortusifolia var. fortunei

Saxifraga cortusifolia var. fortunei, commonly known as Fortune's saxifrage, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It features dense, mat-forming foliage with dark green, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine pubescence. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers in late spring to early summer, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging garden conditions.
