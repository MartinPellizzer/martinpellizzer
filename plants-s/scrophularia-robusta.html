Scrophularia robusta

Scrophularia robusta, commonly known as the rough horse gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. The plant features upright, branched stems covered in short, rough hairs. Its flowers are blue to violet, tubular in shape, and bloom in late summer.

Scrophularia robusta is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
