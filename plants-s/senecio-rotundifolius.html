Senecio rotundifolius

Senecio rotundifolius, commonly known as the round-leaved groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its rounded, silvery-gray leaves and yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.

Senecio rotundifolius is also known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
