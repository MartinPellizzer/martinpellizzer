Schefflera pedicellata

Schefflera pedicellata, commonly known as the clustered umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of large, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Schefflera pedicellata is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and compact growth habit.
