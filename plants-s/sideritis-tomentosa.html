Sideritis tomentosa

Sideritis tomentosa, commonly known as woolly ironwort, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-gray foliage and small, pale blue or violet flowers. The plant thrives in rocky or dry soils and is often found in mountainous areas. Its leaves are covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Sideritis tomentosa is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
