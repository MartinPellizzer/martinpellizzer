Schizostachyum elegantissimum

Schizostachyum elegantissimum, commonly known as the elegant bamboo or golden bamboo, is a tall, fast-growing grass native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, hollow stems and long, narrow leaves. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its graceful appearance and ability to thrive in various soil conditions. It is also valued for its use in construction and as a source of fiber.

Schizostachyum elegantissimum is well-suited for tropical and subtropical climates, making it a popular choice in landscaping and agroforestry.
