Statice armeria var. major

Statice armeria var. major, commonly known as sea thrift, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and spikes of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant thrives in salty, well-drained soils and is often found in dunes and tidal zones. Its ability to tolerate harsh maritime conditions makes it a hardy and resilient species.

Statice armeria var. major is also valued for its ornamental use in gardens and as a cut flower.
