Spirillus obtusifolius

Spirillus obtusifolius, commonly known as the broad-leaved spirillum, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it a unique and ornamental appearance. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its flowers are small and clustered, often displaying shades of white or pale yellow.

Spirillus obtusifolius is valued in horticulture for its distinctive foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
