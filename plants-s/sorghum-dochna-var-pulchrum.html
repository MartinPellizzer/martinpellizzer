Sorghum dochna var. pulchrum

Sorghum dochna var. pulchrum, commonly known as the purpletop sorghum, is a striking ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features tall, slender stalks with vibrant purple and green striped foliage that adds visual interest to gardens. The plant produces small, feathery panicles of pale green to yellow flowers, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping and drought-tolerant landscapes.

Its unique coloration and upright growth habit make it a popular choice for both ornamental and ecological gardening.
