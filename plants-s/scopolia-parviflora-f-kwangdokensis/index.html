Scopolia Parviflora F. Kwangdokensis

Scopolia parviflora f. kwangdokensis is a rare and intriguing plant species native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia. Known for its delicate, bell-shaped flowers and unique foliage, it captivates botanists and plant enthusiasts alike. This particular form of Scopolia is distinguished by its compact growth habit and subtle coloration. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in the botanical world due to its limited distribution. Its study offers valuable insights into the adaptation strategies of alpine flora.
