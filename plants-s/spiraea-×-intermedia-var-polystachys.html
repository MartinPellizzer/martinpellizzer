Spiraea × intermedia var. polystachys

Spiraea × intermedia var. polystachys, commonly known as the weeping spiraea, is a deciduous shrub native to Asia.

It features cascading clusters of white to pink flowers that bloom in early summer, creating a dramatic visual display. The plant has slender, arching branches that give it a weeping appearance, and its foliage is typically dark green. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and hardiness in temperate climates.
