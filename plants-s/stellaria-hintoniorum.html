Stellaria hintoniorum

Stellaria hintoniorum, commonly known as the Hinton's starwort, is a small flowering plant native to specific regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its star-shaped white flowers and delicate, hairy leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in grassy areas or along stream banks. Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
