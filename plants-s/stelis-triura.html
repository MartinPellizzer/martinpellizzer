Stelis triura

Stelis triura, commonly known as the orchid with three-lobed leaves, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia, including countries like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. Its leaves are typically elliptical or ovate, with a waxy texture, and it produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and green.

Stelis triura is often cultivated for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
