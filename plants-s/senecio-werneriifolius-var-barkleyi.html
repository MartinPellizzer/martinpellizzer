Senecio werneriifolius var. barkleyi

Senecio werneriifolius var. barkleyi, commonly known as the Barkley's senecio, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, compact growth with small, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color. The plant produces yellow flowers in summer, which are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in rock gardens and arid landscapes.
