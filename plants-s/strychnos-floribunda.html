Strychnos floribunda

Strychnos floribunda, commonly known as the red-flowered strychnos, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of bright red flowers that attract pollinators. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain seeds. It is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and riverine areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it should be handled with care.
