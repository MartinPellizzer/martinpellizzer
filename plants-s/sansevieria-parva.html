Sansevieria parva

Sansevieria parva, commonly known as the miniature snake plant, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with subtle variegation. The plant is characterized by its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it highly adaptable. Its small size, usually reaching only a few inches in height, makes it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Sansevieria parva is also noted for its air-purifying qualities, contributing to improved indoor air quality.
