Sphaerocionium gaudichaudii

Sphaerocionium gaudichaudii, commonly known as the golden star vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its yellow, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it its common name. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural gardens.
