Stenogyne konaensis

Stenogyne konaensis, commonly known as Kona ironwood, is a rare endemic species native to the island of Hawaii.

It is a small, evergreen shrub or tree that typically grows in moist, shaded habitats within the mesic to wet forest zones. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a leathery texture and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink.
