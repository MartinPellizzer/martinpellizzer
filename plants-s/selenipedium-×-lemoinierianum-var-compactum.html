Selenipedium × lemoinierianum var. compactum

Selenipedium × lemoinierianum var. compactum, commonly known as the compact selenipedium, is a hybrid orchid native to the tropical rainforests of Central America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments with rich, well-drained soil. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
