Salacia laxiflora var. oblongifolia

Salacia laxiflora var. oblongifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf salacia, is a climbing shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features compound leaves with elongated, oblong-shaped leaflets, giving it its varietal name. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruits are fleshy berries that turn red when mature, containing numerous seeds.

This species is valued in traditional medicine for its potential pharmacological properties.
