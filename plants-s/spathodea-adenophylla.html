Spathodea adenophylla

Spathodea adenophylla, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery orange-red flowers that appear in clusters, making it a visually striking plant. The leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate, with a smooth surface. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain seeds covered in a fleshy, red aril.

It thrives in full sun and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in warm climates.
