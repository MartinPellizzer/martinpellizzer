Stenolobium arboreum

Stenolobium arboreum, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant is notable for its striking, bright red flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its bark is smooth and grey, often exfoliating to reveal a lighter inner layer.

Stenolobium arboreum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in various soil types.
