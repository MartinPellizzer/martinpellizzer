Senecio foliatilis

Senecio foliatilis, commonly known as the Blueweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, blue-green foliage and produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Senecio foliatilis is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
