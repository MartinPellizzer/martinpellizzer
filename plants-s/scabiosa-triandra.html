Scabiosa triandra

Scabiosa triandra, commonly known as the fairy ring or honeycomb flower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant features a basal rosette of hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces numerous small, clustered flower heads on slender stems. Scabiosa triandra thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its nectar-rich flowers attract a variety of pollinators, including bees and butterflies.
