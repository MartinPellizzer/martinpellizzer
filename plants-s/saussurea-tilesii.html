Saussurea tilesii

Saussurea tilesii, commonly known as the Tibetan saffron.

It is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas, including parts of Tibet, Nepal, and Bhutan. The plant is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stem and dark purple to blackish flowers. It thrives in cold, arid environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

Due to its unique habitat and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
