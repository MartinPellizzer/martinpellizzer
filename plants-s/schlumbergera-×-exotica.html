Schlumbergera × exotica

Schlumbergera × exotica, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a hybrid plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its segmented, fleshy stems that form a branching structure, and its flowers come in a variety of colors including red, pink, orange, and white. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is a popular indoor plant, especially during the holiday season, due to its vibrant blooms and ability to flower in winter.

Its unique appearance and seasonal flowering make it a favorite among plant enthusiasts.
