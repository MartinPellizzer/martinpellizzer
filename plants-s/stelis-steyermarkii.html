Stelis steyermarkii

Stelis steyermarkii, commonly known as the Steyermark's spider orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Stelis steyermarkii is a relatively rare species, making it a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
