Senecio sepium

Senecio sepium, commonly known as the upright groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features upright stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, yellow, and clustered in dense, conical inflorescences.

Senecio sepium thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.
