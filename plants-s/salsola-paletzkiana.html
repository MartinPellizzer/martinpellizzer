Salsola paletzkiana

Salsola paletzkiana, commonly known as the palezkian saltbush, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and small, greenish flowers. The plant is adapted to arid and saline environments, making it a hardy species found in desert and semi-desert regions. It has a low-growing, shrub-like structure with extensive root systems that help it survive in harsh conditions.

Salsola paletzkiana plays a role in stabilizing soil and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
