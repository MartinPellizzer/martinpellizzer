Sonchus tuberifer

Sonchus tuberifer, commonly known as the edible groundsel, is a herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features a rosette of dark green, hairy leaves with a slightly serrated edge. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family. Its root system includes fleshy, tuber-like structures that store nutrients.

It is often found in disturbed soils and can be invasive in certain regions.
