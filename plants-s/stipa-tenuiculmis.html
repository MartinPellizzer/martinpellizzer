Stipa tenuiculmis

Stipa tenuiculmis, commonly known as slender threadgrass, is a species of grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its fine, wiry leaves and slender, cylindrical flower spikes. The plant typically grows in open, dry habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It is a perennial species that thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soils.

Stipa tenuiculmis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and conservation efforts.
