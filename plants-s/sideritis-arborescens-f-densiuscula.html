Sideritis arborescens f. densiuscula

Sideritis arborescens f. densiuscula, commonly known as the dense-leaved ironweed, is a compact shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, gray-green leaves that are densely packed, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of pale blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It is often found in rocky or dry soils, thriving in sunny to partially shaded environments.

Its aromatic foliage and ornamental value make it a desirable plant for gardens and ecological restoration projects.
