Stelis septicola

Stelis septicola, commonly known as the dark-stem orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, often dark-colored stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or branches, exhibiting an epiphytic growth habit. Its flowers are usually greenish-white or yellowish, with a distinctive spurred lip.

Stelis septicola is valued for its unique appearance and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
