Soliva anthemifolia var. acaulis

Soliva anthemifolia var. acaulis, commonly known as slender hillside grass, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, hairless stems that often form a dense mat on the ground. The leaves are narrow, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It produces small, yellowish flowers in early summer, which are clustered in loose inflorescences.

This plant is well adapted to rocky and dry soils, making it a hardy choice for landscaping in arid environments.
