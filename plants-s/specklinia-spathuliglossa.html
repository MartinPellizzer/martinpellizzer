Specklinia spathuliglossa

Specklinia spathuliglossa, commonly known as the broad-lipped orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, lanceolate leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive spatulate lip, which is a key feature in its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on tree trunks or branches.

Due to its ornamental value, it is a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
