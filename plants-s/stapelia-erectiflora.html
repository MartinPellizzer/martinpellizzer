Stapelia erectiflora

Stapelia erectiflora, commonly known as the erect-flowered stapelia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems that store water. The plant produces striking, meaty flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. These flowers are designed to attract pollinators such as flies and beetles.

Stapelia erectiflora is a slow-growing plant that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.
