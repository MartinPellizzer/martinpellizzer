Sophia multifida

Sophia multifida, commonly known as the multifid leaf plant, is a member of the Apocynaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that are deeply divided into multiple lobes, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pink in color. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions and thrives in warm, humid environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in gardens.
