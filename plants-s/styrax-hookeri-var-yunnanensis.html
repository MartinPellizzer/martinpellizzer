Styrax hookeri var. yunnanensis

Styrax hookeri var. yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan styrax, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Styracaceae and is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

The fruit is a small, woody capsule that contains aromatic seeds.
