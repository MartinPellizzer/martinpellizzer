Scutellaria sibthorpii var. geraniana

Scutellaria sibthorpii var. geraniana, commonly known as the geranium scutellaria, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long flowering period.
