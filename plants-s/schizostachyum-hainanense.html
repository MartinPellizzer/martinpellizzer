Schizostachyum hainanense

Schizostachyum hainanense, commonly known as Hainan bamboo, is a species of grass belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to the island of Hainan in China and is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 10 meters. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters. It is often found in moist, well-drained soils in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its hardiness and ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated for landscaping purposes.
