Stenolobium stans

Stenolobium stans, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a tropical tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, bright orange to red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces legume pods that contain flat, winged seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular ornamental plant in warm climates.

Its rapid growth and showy flowers make it a notable species in ecological and horticultural contexts.
