Senecio violifolius

Senecio violifolius, commonly known as the violet-leaved groundsels, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Senecio violifolius is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
