Scrophularia longifolia var. intermedia

Scrophularia longifolia var. intermedia, commonly known as the intermediate scrophularia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in woodland edges or grassy areas. The plant features lanceolate leaves with a rough texture and produces spikes of violet or purple flowers. Its flowering period occurs in late spring to early summer, attracting various pollinators.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
