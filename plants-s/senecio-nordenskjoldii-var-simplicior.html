Senecio nordenskjoldii var. simplicior

Senecio nordenskjoldii var. simplicior, commonly known as the simpleleaf senecio, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tint. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a cluster, which bloom during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
