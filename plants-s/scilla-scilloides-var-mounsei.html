Scilla scilloides var. mounsei

Scilla scilloides var. mounsei, commonly known as the Mounse's scilla, is a perennial bulbous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers in early spring. The plant is often found in rocky or gravelly soils, thriving in well-drained environments. It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to naturalize in suitable climates.

Its botanical characteristics make it a hardy and resilient choice for gardens and woodland settings.
