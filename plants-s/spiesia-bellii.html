Spiesia bellii

Spiesia bellii, commonly known as the white sand dandelion, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is adapted to drought conditions.

Spiesia bellii is often found in coastal and inland sand dunes, where it plays a role in stabilizing the soil.
