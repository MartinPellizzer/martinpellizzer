Stellaria tetragona

Stellaria tetragona, commonly known as four-angled starwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is a low-growing annual herb characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, star-shaped flowers. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy surface. The flowers are typically white or pale pink, with five petals that spread out like a star.

This plant is commonly found in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as fields and roadsides.
