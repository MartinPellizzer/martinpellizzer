Stelis purpurea

Stelis purpurea, commonly known as the purple stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, often pendulous growth habit. The plant features linear, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. Its flowers are small, clustered in inflorescences, and often display a range of colors from white to purple.

Stelis purpurea thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.
