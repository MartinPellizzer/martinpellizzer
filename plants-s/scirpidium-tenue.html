Scirpidium tenue

Scirpidium tenue, commonly known as the slender scirpus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family, commonly referred to as the sedge family.

Scirpidium tenue plays a role in stabilizing wetland soils and providing habitat for various aquatic and terrestrial organisms.
