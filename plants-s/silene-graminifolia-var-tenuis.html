Silene graminifolia var. tenuis

Silene graminifolia var. tenuis, commonly known as slenderleaf catchfly, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family, characterized by its showy, pink or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and marshes. It has thin, grass-like leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits.
