Sedum eriocarpum subsp. orientale

Sedum eriocarpum subsp.

orientale, commonly known as the Eastern stonecrop, is a perennial succulent native to parts of Asia. It features fleshy, upright stems and small, clustered leaves that are typically green or reddish in color.

The plant produces star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange, which bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its hardy nature allows it to survive in a variety of environmental conditions.
