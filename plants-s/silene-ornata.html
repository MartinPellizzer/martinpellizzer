Silene ornata

Silene ornata, commonly known as the showy lady's slipper, is a striking plant native to the western United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or open habitats. The plant produces vibrant pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

Silene ornata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
