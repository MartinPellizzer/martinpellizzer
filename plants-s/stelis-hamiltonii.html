Stelis hamiltonii

Stelis hamiltonii, commonly known as the Hamilton's stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and clustered pseudobulbs. The plant produces small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with a distinctive spurred lip.

Stelis hamiltonii thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.
