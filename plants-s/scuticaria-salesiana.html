Scuticaria salesiana

Scuticaria salesiana, commonly known as the Florida scutellaria, is a perennial herb native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features square stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are typically violet or purple, arranged in whorls along the stem.

Scuticaria salesiana is often found in moist, sandy soils in pine flatwoods and wetlands.
