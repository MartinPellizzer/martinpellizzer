Swertia pedicellata

Swertia pedicellata, commonly known as the Himalayan gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows at high altitudes, often in rocky or alpine environments. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and covered with fine hairs.

Swertia pedicellata is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
