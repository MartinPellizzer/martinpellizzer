Stenospermation adsimile

Stenospermation adsimile, commonly known as the narrow-seed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Indonesia. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically clustered in spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

The species is noted for its unique seed structure, with seeds that are extremely small and adapted for wind dispersal.
