Stenochlaena longicaudata

Stenochlaena longicaudata, commonly known as the long-tailed fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, narrow fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with creeping underground stems that help it spread and establish new colonies. Its leaves are typically bi- or tri-pinnate, giving it a delicate, lacy appearance.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
