Sida lomageiton

Sida lomageiton, commonly known as the coastal sida, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the southeastern United States.

It is an annual herb that typically grows in sandy or disturbed soils along the shoreline. The plant features small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, and its leaves are ovate with a rough texture. It has a fibrous root system that helps stabilize coastal dunes.
