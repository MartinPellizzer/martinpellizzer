Shorea Assamica F. Philippinensis

Shorea assamica f. philippinensis is a rare and lesser-known variant of the Shorea assamica species. It is native to the Philippines and is characterized by its unique morphological features. This plant is often found in lowland dipterocarp forests, thriving in warm and humid climates. Due to habitat loss and limited research, its conservation status remains uncertain. Understanding this plant is crucial for preserving biodiversity in Southeast Asian ecosystems.
