Specklinia Luis-Diegoi

Specklinia luis-diegoi is a rare and fascinating orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador. This delicate plant is known for its unique foliage and striking floral display. It thrives in humid, shaded environments where it clings to tree trunks and branches. Due to its limited habitat and threats from deforestation, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this rare species for future generations.
