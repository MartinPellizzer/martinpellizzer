Saxifraga × ursina

Saxifraga × ursina, commonly known as the bear's breeches, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Saxifragaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of white or pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Native to the mountainous regions of Europe, it thrives in cool, moist environments. The plant is often found in rocky or woodland habitats, where it can tolerate partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
