Specklinia steinbuchiae

Specklinia steinbuchiae, commonly known as the Steinbuch's cryptanthus, is a rare and unique orchid native to the high-altitude regions of Ecuador.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pale green to brown in color, with intricate patterns on the petals. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires specific conditions to flourish.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
