Senna Lasseigniana

Senna lasseigniana is a unique species of legume native to the arid regions of northern Africa. This plant is known for its striking yellow flowers and its ability to thrive in harsh, dry environments. It plays an important role in local ecosystems by providing food and habitat for various wildlife. The plant's resilience makes it a valuable resource for ecological restoration projects. Its study offers insights into plant adaptation strategies in extreme climates.
