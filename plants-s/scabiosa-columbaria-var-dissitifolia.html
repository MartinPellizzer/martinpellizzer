Scabiosa columbaria var. dissitifolia

Scabiosa columbaria var. dissitifolia, commonly known as the "Snowball Scabious," is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe.

It features a compact, rounded growth habit with dense clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, slightly hairy leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its delicate flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, contributing to its ecological value.
