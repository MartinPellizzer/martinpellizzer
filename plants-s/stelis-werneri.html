Stelis werneri

Stelis werneri, commonly known as the Werneri's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. The plant features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with narrow, linear leaves. Its flowers are small, clustered in inflorescences, and often have a distinct coloration with a greenish or brownish hue.

Stelis werneri is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
