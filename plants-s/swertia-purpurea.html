Swertia purpurea

Swertia purpurea, commonly known as purple gentian, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has lance-shaped leaves with a smooth texture. Its flowers bloom in late summer and are known for their vibrant color and nectar-rich petals.

Swertia purpurea is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
