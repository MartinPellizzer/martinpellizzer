Saxifraga × baccii

Saxifraga × baccii, commonly known as the red-scented saxifrage, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Saxifragaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and produces clusters of small, pinkish-red flowers in late summer. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded to semi-shaded environments.

Saxifraga × baccii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in woodland gardens.
