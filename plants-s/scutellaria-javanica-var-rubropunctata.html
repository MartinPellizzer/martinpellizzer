Scutellaria javanica var. rubropunctata

Scutellaria javanica var. rubropunctata, commonly known as the red-spotted skullcap, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. Its leaves are often marked with red or purple spots, giving it a distinctive appearance.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
