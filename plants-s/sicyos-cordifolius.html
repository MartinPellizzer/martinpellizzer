Sicyos cordifolius

Sicyos cordifolius, commonly known as the heartleaf gourd, is a tropical vine native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces edible fruits that are typically harvested when they are still green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Sicyos cordifolius is often cultivated for its ornamental value and culinary uses.
