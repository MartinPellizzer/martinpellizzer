Stellaria krylovii

Stellaria krylovii, commonly known as the Krylov's starwort, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has slender, branching stems and opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine meadows.

Stellaria krylovii is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
