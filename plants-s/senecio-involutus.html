Senecio involutus

Senecio involutus, commonly known as the curledleaf senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. Senecio involutus is also known for its ability to produce a milky sap, which can be toxic to some animals.
