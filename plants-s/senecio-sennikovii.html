Senecio sennikovii

Senecio sennikovii, commonly known as the Sennikovii groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing a low, compact growth habit. Its leaves are often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in its harsh habitat.

Senecio sennikovii is noted for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in folk remedies.
