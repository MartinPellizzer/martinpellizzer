Sideritis Hyssopifolia F. Intermedia

Sideritis hyssopifolia f. intermedia is a rare and intriguing plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family. It is a flowering plant known for its delicate purple blooms and aromatic leaves. This particular form of Sideritis is often found in rocky and mountainous regions across southern Europe. Its unique characteristics make it a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists alike. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in both research and cultivation.
