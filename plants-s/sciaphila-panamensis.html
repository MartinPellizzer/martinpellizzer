Sciaphila panamensis

Sciaphila panamensis, commonly known as the Panama shade plant, is a species of flowering plant in the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central America, particularly Panama. The plant is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves and produces small, fragrant white flowers. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in the understory of tropical forests.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
