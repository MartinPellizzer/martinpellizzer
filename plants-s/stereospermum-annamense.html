Stereospermum annamense

Stereospermum annamense, commonly known as the Annam tallow tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Bignoniaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds.
