Solanum hainanense var. griffithii

Solanum hainanense var. griffithii, commonly known as Hainan nightshade, is a flowering plant native to southern China.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves and clusters of violet or purple flowers. The plant produces small, yellow berries that are typically edible but may contain toxic compounds.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, shaded environments.
