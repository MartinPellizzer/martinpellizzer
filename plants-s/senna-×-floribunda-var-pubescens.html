Senna × floribunda var. pubescens

Senna × floribunda var. pubescens, commonly known as the silky senna, is a hybrid shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with small, hairy stipules, giving it a distinctive silvery appearance. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in dense clusters, which are typical of the Fabaceae family. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
