Sprekelia formosissima

Sprekelia formosissima, commonly known as the golden trumpet, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, with a distinctive red or orange throat. The plant produces a single stem that can reach up to 2 meters in height, bearing clusters of large, fragrant blooms. Its leaves are long, narrow, and grass-like, often appearing after the flowers have bloomed.

Sprekelia formosissima is well-suited for gardens with well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
