Senecio muninensis

Senecio muninensis, commonly known as the Munin's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils. It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by spherical seed heads.

Senecio muninensis is often found in coastal and inland regions, showcasing adaptability to various environmental conditions.
