Stellaria capillipes

Stellaria capillipes, commonly known as the slender starwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is native to parts of Europe, Asia, and North Africa, often found in grassy areas and along roadsides. The plant has small, white or pale pink flowers that are typically arranged in clusters, giving it a star-like appearance. Its leaves are slender and grass-like, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Stellaria capillipes is generally a low-growing herb that thrives in well-drained soils.
