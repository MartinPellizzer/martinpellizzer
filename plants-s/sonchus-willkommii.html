Sonchus willkommii

Sonchus willkommii, commonly known as the hairy sowthistle, is an annual herbaceous plant native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

It features lobed leaves that are typically hairy and covered in fine, white pubescence. The plant produces bright yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in disturbed soils and can often be found in fields, roadsides, and waste areas.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a common weed in agricultural settings.
