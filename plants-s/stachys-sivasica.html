Stachys sivasica

Stachys sivasica, commonly known as the Sivas betony, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and clusters of small, tubular flowers. It typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
