Sorghum centroplicatum var. tricolor

Sorghum centroplicatum var. tricolor, commonly known as the tri-color sorghum, is a striking ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features vibrant, tri-colored bracts that display shades of red, orange, and yellow, creating a visually appealing display. The plant typically grows to a height of 2 to 4 feet, with slender, grass-like leaves that are often a deep green. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its unique coloration and compact growth habit make it a popular choice for ornamental use in temperate climates.
