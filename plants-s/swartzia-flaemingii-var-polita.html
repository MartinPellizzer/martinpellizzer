Swartzia flaemingii var. polita, commonly known as the velvetwood, is a species of flowering plant in the Fabaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its smooth, dark, almost velvety bark. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters.
