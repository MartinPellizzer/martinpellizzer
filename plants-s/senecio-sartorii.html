Senecio sartorii

Senecio sartorii, commonly known as the Sartorii Groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It features linear, grass-like leaves and produces small yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Senecio sartorii is often used in rock gardens due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
