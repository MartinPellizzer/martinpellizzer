Sajanella monstrosa

Sajanella monstrosa, commonly known as the giant spider lily, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that resemble spider-like structures, hence its common name. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often near water sources. Its leaves are broad and dark green, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Sajanella monstrosa is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and ability to thrive in tropical climates.
