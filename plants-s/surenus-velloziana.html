Surenus velloziana

Surenus velloziana, commonly known as the velvetleaf suranus, is a tropical plant native to regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in loose inflorescences. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its rapid growth and invasive tendencies, it is considered a problematic species in some regions.
