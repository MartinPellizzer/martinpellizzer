Statice cancellata

Statice cancellata, commonly known as sea lavender, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its fluffy, pink or purple flower heads that resemble small clouds. The plant thrives in salty, coastal environments and is often found in dunes and saline soils. Its narrow, grass-like leaves are adapted to withstand harsh maritime conditions.

Statice cancellata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging conditions.
