Setaria viridis var. stenostachya

Setaria viridis var. stenostachya, commonly known as slender green millet, is a variant of the green millet species native to Africa.

It is a small annual grass with narrow, grass-like leaves and produces slender, spike-like inflorescences. The plant is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to thrive in dry, disturbed soils. It is often used as a forage crop and has potential applications in sustainable agriculture.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and agricultural research.
