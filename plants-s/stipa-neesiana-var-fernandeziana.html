Stipa neesiana var. fernandeziana

Stipa neesiana var. fernandeziana, commonly known as the Fernandezian feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and delicate, feathery inflorescences that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its foliage is often silver-gray due to a dense covering of fine hairs, which helps reduce water loss in arid conditions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its elegant, airy growth habit.
