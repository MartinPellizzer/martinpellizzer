Schizostachyum acutiflorum

Schizostachyum acutiflorum, commonly known as the narrow-flowered bamboo, is a tropical grass species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender culms and narrow, linear leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with pale green to yellowish flowers. It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and can thrive in well-drained soils.

Schizostachyum acutiflorum is also valued for its lightweight and durable bamboo-like material, making it useful in crafts and construction.
