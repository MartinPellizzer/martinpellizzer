Symphysia orosiensis

Symphysia orosiensis, commonly known as the Canary Island rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a shrub characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to the island's Mediterranean climate.

Symphysia orosiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.
