Solanum pulverulentifolium

Solanum pulverulentifolium, commonly known as the dusty-leaf nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, hairy leaves that give it a dusty appearance, as well as its star-shaped, violet-blue flowers. Native to arid and semi-arid regions, it thrives in dry, well-drained soils. The plant produces small, orange berries that are edible but contain toxic alkaloids.

Due to its unique morphology and ecological adaptations, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
