Statice terekiensis

Statice terekiensis, commonly known as the Terekei statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its erect, branching stems and dense clusters of small, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, and they bloom from late summer to early autumn.

Statice terekiensis is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its hardiness and long-lasting flowers.
