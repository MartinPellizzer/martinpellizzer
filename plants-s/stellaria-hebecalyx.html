Stellaria hebecalyx

Stellaria hebecalyx, commonly known as the slender-stemmed starwort, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically features small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has thin, branching stems and small, opposite leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors or stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the Caryophyllaceae family.
