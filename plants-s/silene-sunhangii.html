Silene sunhangii

Silene sunhangii, commonly known as the Sunhang's campion, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its simple, often hairy stems and clusters of small, pink or white flowers. Silene sunhangii is a rare species, making it of interest to botanists and conservationists.
