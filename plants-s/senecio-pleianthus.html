Senecio pleianthus

Senecio pleianthus, commonly known as the blue ice plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its striking appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
