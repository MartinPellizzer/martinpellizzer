Scutellaria quadrilobulata

Scutellaria quadrilobulata, commonly known as four-lobed skullcap, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color, arranged in whorled clusters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Scutellaria quadrilobulata is valued in traditional medicine for its potential medicinal properties.
