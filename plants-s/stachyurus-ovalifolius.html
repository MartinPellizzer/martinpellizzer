Stachyurus ovalifolius

Stachyurus ovalifolius, commonly known as the oval-leaf stachyurus, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that appear in early spring. The leaves are oval-shaped and have a glossy green surface, providing ornamental value throughout the growing season. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

This plant is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
