Saxifraga adenodes

Saxifraga adenodes, commonly known as the rockfoil saxifrage, is a flowering plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, often blooming in late summer. Its leaves are usually succulent, adapted to retain moisture in harsh climates.

Saxifraga adenodes is a perennial species, often found in tundra and mountainous areas across northern Europe, Asia, and North America.
