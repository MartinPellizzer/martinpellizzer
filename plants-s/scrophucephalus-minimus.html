Scrophucephalus minimus

Scrophucephalus minimus, commonly known as the dwarf scrophularia, is a small herbaceous plant native to certain regions of Europe.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features small, opposite leaves that are usually hairy and have a distinct heart-shaped base. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, arranged in clusters, and bloom in the spring.

Scrophucephalus minimus is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
