Schoepfia cubensis

Schoepfia cubensis, commonly known as the Cuban silverleaf, is a shrub native to the Caribbean, particularly Cuba.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in composite heads, typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Schoepfia cubensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
