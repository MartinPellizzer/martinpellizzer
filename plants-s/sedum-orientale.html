Sedum orientale

Sedum orientale, commonly known as the oriental stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to regions of Europe and Asia.

It features fleshy, upright stems and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Its leaves are typically blue-green in color and have a slightly waxy texture.

Sedum orientale is known for its hardiness and ability to survive in arid conditions.
