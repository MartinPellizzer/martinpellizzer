Salacia nitida

Salacia nitida, commonly known as the silverleaf salacia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Salaciacae and is characterized by its slender, climbing habit and glossy, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically borne in clusters. Its fruits are fleshy, berry-like structures that contain multiple seeds.

Salacia nitida is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and unique floral display.
