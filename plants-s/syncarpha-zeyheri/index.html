Syncarpha Zeyheri

Syncarpha zeyheri is a small, hardy shrub native to southern Africa. It is known for its delicate white flowers that bloom in spring. This plant thrives in rocky and sandy soils, often found in coastal regions. Its compact size makes it popular for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Despite its modest appearance, Syncarpha zeyheri plays an important role in local ecosystems.
