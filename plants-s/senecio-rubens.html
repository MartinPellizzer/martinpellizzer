Senecio rubens

Senecio rubens, commonly known as the red sandwort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant features small, fleshy leaves and produces clusters of yellow flowers. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or arid environments.

Senecio rubens is well adapted to dry conditions and can tolerate high levels of sunlight.
