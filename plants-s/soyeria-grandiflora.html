Soyeria grandiflora

Soyeria grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered everlet, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a waxy texture.

Soyeria grandiflora is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
