Sisyranthus imberbis

Sisyranthus imberbis, commonly known as the smooth sisyranthus, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, delicate flowers. The plant produces showy, spurred orchid blooms that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of rainforests.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is considered a rare and specialized species within its native range.
