Swartzia polita

Swartzia polita, commonly known as the velvetwood or yellowwood, is a tropical tree native to South America.

It belongs to the Leguminosae (Fabaceae) family and is characterized by its smooth, dark bark and dense, heavy wood. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by flat, bean-like pods containing seeds. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland and hill forests.

Swartzia polita is valued for its hard, durable wood, which is used in furniture and construction.
