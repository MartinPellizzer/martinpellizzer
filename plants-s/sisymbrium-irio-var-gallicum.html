Sisymbrium irio var. gallicum

Sisymbrium irio var. gallicum, commonly known as the French yellow rocket, is an annual herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features slender, branching stems covered in fine hairs and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in loose clusters. The plant has pinnately divided leaves with serrated edges, and its seed pods are slender, elongated, and turn from green to brown as they mature. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and waste areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
