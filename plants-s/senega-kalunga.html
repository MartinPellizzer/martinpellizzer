Senega kalunga

Senega kalunga, commonly known as the African arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the genus Maranta and is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by red, fleshy berries. Its rhizomes are used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The leaves are also used as ornamental plants due to their vibrant appearance.
