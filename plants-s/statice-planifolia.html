Statice planifolia

Statice planifolia, commonly known as flat statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces dense clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, white, and purple. The plant is part of the Amaranthaceae family and is known for its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in well-drained soils. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves, and it is often used in ornamental gardening for its long-lasting blooms.

Statice planifolia is also valued for its use in dried flower arrangements due to its sturdy structure and vibrant color retention.
