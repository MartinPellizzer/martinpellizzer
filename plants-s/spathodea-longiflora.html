Spathodea longiflora

Spathodea longiflora, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a tropical tree native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters, making it a striking ornamental plant. The plant has palmate leaves that are deeply lobed, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and parks.

Spathodea longiflora is also valued for its rapid growth and ability to provide shade in tropical landscapes.
