Satureja despreauxii

Satureja despreauxii, commonly known as the Corsican savory, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region, particularly Corsica and Sardinia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants known for their essential oils. The plant features small, pink or purple flowers and narrow, aromatic leaves that are often used in traditional cuisine. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to Mediterranean gardens.

Its medicinal properties have been studied for their potential anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects.
