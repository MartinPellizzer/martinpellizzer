Sulcorebutia tarabucoensis var. callecallensis

Sulcorebutia tarabucoensis var. callecallensis, commonly known as the Tarabucoensis Cactus, is a species of flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains of Peru.

It is a small, globular cactus with a compact, rounded shape and dense, pale green to blue-gray foliage. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, thriving in full sun and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and resilience, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
