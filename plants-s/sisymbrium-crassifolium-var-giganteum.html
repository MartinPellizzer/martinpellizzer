Sisymbrium crassifolium var. giganteum

Sisymbrium crassifolium var. giganteum, commonly known as giant yellow rocket, is a biennial plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can reach heights of up to 2 meters. It is often found in open areas such as roadsides and fields.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and invasive plant management.
