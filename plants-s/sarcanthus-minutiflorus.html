Sarcanthus minutiflorus

Sarcanthus minutiflorus, commonly known as the dwarf starflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
