Silene cerastoides var. dunensis

Silene cerastoides var. dunensis, commonly known as the Dun Mountain catchfly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with hairy, linear leaves and small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats. Its flowers are typically arranged in a dense cluster at the tip of the stem.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
