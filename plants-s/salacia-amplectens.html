Salacia amplectens

Salacia amplectens, commonly known as the climbing salacia, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its scrambling growth habit and is often found in humid, lowland forests. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and typically white or pale yellow, appearing in clusters.

Salacia amplectens is valued in traditional medicine for its potential medicinal properties.
