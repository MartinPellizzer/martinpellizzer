Saxifraga × chrysoleuca

Saxifraga × chrysoleuca, commonly known as the golden-leaved saxifrage, is a hybrid plant that exhibits striking botanical features.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow foliage, which contrasts beautifully with its dark green stems and pinkish-white flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of Europe, this plant thrives in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are typically succulent, adapted to retain moisture in harsh alpine conditions.

The plant's ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and shady borders.
