Sideroxylon celebicum

Sideroxylon celebicum, commonly known as the Celebes ironwood, is a tropical tree native to the Maluku Islands of Indonesia.

It is characterized by its dense, hard wood that is highly valued for its durability and strength. The plant typically grows in lowland rainforests and has a tall, straight trunk with a smooth, dark bark. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptical, often with a glossy surface.

Sideroxylon celebicum also produces small, white or pale yellow flowers followed by woody fruits containing seeds.
