Stelis alloinfundibulosa

Stelis alloinfundibulosa, commonly known as the broad-leafed stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on trees or rocks. Its leaves are broad and elliptical, with a glossy surface.

The flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with a unique, funnel-shaped lip that distinguishes it from other orchid species.
