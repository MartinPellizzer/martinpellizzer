Sherbournia kiliotricha

Sherbournia kiliotricha, commonly known as the Kiliotricha grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils. The plant has slender, erect stems with narrow, linear leaves that are often hairy along the edges. Its inflorescence consists of a loose cluster of small, spikelet-like flowers, which are wind-pollinated.

Sherbournia kiliotricha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
