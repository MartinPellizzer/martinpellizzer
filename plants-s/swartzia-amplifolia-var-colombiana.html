Swartzia amplifolia var. colombiana

Swartzia amplifolia var. colombiana, commonly known as the Colombian swartzia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments within the rainforest canopy. Its wood is valued for its strength and durability, making it useful in construction and furniture-making.

The species also plays an important ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
