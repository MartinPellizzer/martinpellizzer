Selaginella Pallescens F. Aurea

Selaginella pallescens f. aurea is a rare and captivating fern-like plant known for its striking golden foliage. Native to specific regions with high humidity and cool temperatures, this plant thrives in shaded, moist environments. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts and collectors. The variegated leaves add a touch of elegance to any indoor or outdoor garden. With proper care, this plant can flourish and bring a sense of natural beauty to its surroundings.
