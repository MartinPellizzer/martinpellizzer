Silene lindtneri

Silene lindtneri, commonly known as the Lindtner's catchfly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or dry soils. The plant produces small, pink or white flowers that bloom in summer, and its leaves are linear and covered in fine hairs. Silene lindtneri is part of the Caryophyllaceae family, which includes many species with showy flowers and succulent leaves.

It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges.
