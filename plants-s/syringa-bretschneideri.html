Syringa bretschneideri

Syringa bretschneideri, commonly known as Chinese lilac, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, clustered flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Syringa bretschneideri is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in landscaping for its showy blooms and hardy nature.
