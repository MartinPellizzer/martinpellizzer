Sida Rhombifolia Var. Canariensis

Sida rhombifolia var. canariensis is a unique variant of the sida plant known for its striking appearance and adaptability. Native to the Canary Islands, this plant thrives in coastal and rocky environments. It features heart-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, pale flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Its resilience to salt and wind makes it a notable species in maritime ecosystems. This plant is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
