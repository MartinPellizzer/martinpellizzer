Smilax china f. variegata

Smilax china f. variegata, commonly known as the variegated Chinese smilax, is a hybrid form of the Chinese smilax plant.

It is a perennial vine characterized by its distinctive variegated leaves with white and green patterns. The plant produces small, yellowish-green berries that are typically borne in clusters. It thrives in shaded to partially sunny environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking unique foliage.
