Stellaria petraea

Stellaria petraea, commonly known as the rock starwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to rocky and arid environments.

It has small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from March to May. The plant features slender, spreading stems and narrow, linear leaves that are adapted to withstand harsh conditions. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in limestone areas.

Stellaria petraea is a hardy species that plays a role in stabilizing soil in rocky habitats.
