Sciaphila elata

Sciaphila elata, commonly known as the moonflower orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking white, night-blooming flowers. The plant thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot. Its leaves are broad and glossy, often with a waxy texture that helps retain moisture.

Sciaphila elata is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
