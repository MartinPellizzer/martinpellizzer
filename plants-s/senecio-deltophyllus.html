Senecio deltophyllus

Senecio deltophyllus, commonly known as the Cape marigold, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and rockeries.
