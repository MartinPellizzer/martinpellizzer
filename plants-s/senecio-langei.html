Senecio langei

Senecio langei, commonly known as the Lange's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Senecio langei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
