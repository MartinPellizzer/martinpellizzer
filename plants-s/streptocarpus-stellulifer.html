Streptocarpus stellulifer

Streptocarpus stellulifer, commonly known as the star-leaved streptocarpus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. The plant produces distinctive star-shaped leaves, which give it its common name, and blooms in shades of violet or purple. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Streptocarpus stellulifer is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
