Stelis confusa

Stelis confusa, commonly known as the cloud orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in tropical rainforests. The plant has a compact growth habit with a single, upright stem and a few leaves. Its flowers are usually small and white or pale pink, clustered in a spike.

Stelis confusa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or shaded gardens.
