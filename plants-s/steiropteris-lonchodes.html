Steiropteris lonchodes

Steiropteris lonchodes, commonly known as the longleaf fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical rainforests. Its fronds are typically bright green and have a smooth, leathery texture.

Steiropteris lonchodes plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
