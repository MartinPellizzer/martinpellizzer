Solenocentrum costaricense

Solenocentrum costaricense, commonly known as the Costa Rican star cactus, is a species of cactus native to Costa Rica.

It is a small, branching plant with cylindrical stems that are covered in spines. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers during the dry season. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
