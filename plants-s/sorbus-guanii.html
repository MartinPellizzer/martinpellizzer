Sorbus guanii

Sorbus guanii, commonly known as the Guangxi rowan, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant produces bright red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas with well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
