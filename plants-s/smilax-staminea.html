Smilax staminea

Smilax staminea, commonly known as the yellow smilax, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a perennial vine that produces small, yellowish-green flowers clustered in umbels. The plant has a distinctive appearance with spiny stems and heart-shaped leaves. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that ripens in the fall.

Smilax staminea is often found in moist, wooded areas and is known for its ornamental value and ecological importance.
