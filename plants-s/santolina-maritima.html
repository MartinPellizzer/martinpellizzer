Santolina maritima

Santolina maritima, commonly known as maritime savory, is a low-growing shrub native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It features aromatic, silver-gray foliage with a strong, pungent scent reminiscent of thyme and sage. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in dense clusters, typically blooming in summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it ideal for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.

Santolina maritima is also valued for its ornamental appeal and use in herbal remedies.
