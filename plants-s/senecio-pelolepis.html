Senecio pelolepis

Senecio pelolepis, commonly known as the blue ice plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers. The plant has succulent leaves that are covered in a fine, powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Senecio pelolepis is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
