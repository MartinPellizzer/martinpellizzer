Schoenoplectus juncoides subsp. ohwianus

Schoenoplectus juncoides subsp.

ohwianus, commonly known as the Ohwian bulrush, is a perennial wetland plant native to parts of East Asia. It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters.

The plant produces small, clustered brownish flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in shallow water or moist soils, often found in marshes and along the edges of ponds.

Its rhizomes help stabilize soil and provide habitat for various aquatic organisms.
