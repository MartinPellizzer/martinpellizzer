Strophanthus longicalyx

Strophanthus longicalyx, commonly known as the red milkweed tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red flowers and woody, tree-like structure. The plant produces capsules that contain toxic seeds, which are used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a smooth texture.

The plant is part of the Apocynaceae family, known for its milky sap and medicinal properties.
