Schefflera pueckleri

Schefflera pueckleri, commonly known as the Pueckler's umbrella tree, is a tropical plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its distinctive, umbrella-like arrangement of leaves, which are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Schefflera pueckleri is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
