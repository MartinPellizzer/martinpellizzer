Senecio westermanii

Senecio westermanii, commonly known as Westerman's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are usually gray-green with a fuzzy texture, and it produces small yellow flowers.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and hardy nature.
