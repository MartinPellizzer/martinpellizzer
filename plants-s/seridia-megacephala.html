Seridia megacephala

Seridia megacephala, commonly known as the giant helmet orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, showy flowers that resemble a helmet, hence its common name. It typically grows in humid, shaded environments within the rainforest canopy.

The botanical features of Seridia megacephala include its complex inflorescence and specialized pollination mechanisms adapted to its ecological niche.
