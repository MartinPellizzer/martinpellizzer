Selaginella posewitzii

Selaginella posewitzii, commonly known as the Christmas moss, is a species of spore-bearing plant belonging to the family Selaginellaceae.

It is a small, evergreen plant that typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant has a distinctive appearance with its intricate, moss-like foliage and tiny, spirally arranged leaves.
