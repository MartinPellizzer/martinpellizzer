Scleria millespicula

Scleria millespicula, commonly known as the many-flowered bulrush, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces dense clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers, which bloom in summer. Its leaves are long, narrow, and often have a waxy coating.

Scleria millespicula is typically found in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of water bodies.
