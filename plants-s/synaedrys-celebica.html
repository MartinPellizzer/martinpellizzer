Synaedrys celebica

Synaedrys celebica, commonly known as the Celebes starling tree, is a tropical plant native to the Indonesian archipelago.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and distinctive star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that are often consumed by local wildlife. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
