Sabicea stellaris

Sabicea stellaris, commonly known as the starleaf acacia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Sabicea stellaris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
