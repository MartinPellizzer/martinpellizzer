Scutellaria orientalis subsp. orientalis

Scutellaria orientalis subsp.

orientalis, commonly known as oriental skullcap, is a perennial herb native to regions of Central Asia and the Middle East. It features upright, branched stems that bear opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves with hairy surfaces.

The plant produces showy blue to violet flowers in whorled clusters, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
