Scabiosa nivea

Scabiosa nivea, commonly known as the snowy scabious, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has slender, upright stems and basal leaves that are usually hairy and lance-shaped. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy perennial suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its nectar-rich flowers attract bees and other pollinators, contributing to its ecological value.
