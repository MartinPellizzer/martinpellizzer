Stelis ipialesensis

Stelis ipialesensis, commonly known as the Ipialesensis orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the cloud forests of Colombia.

This epiphytic plant typically grows on trees and thrives in humid, shaded environments. It features a compact growth habit with lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant is characterized by its pseudobulbs and is often found in high-altitude regions with cool temperatures.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
