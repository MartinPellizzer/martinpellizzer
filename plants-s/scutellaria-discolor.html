Scutellaria discolor

Scutellaria discolor, commonly known as blueweed or blue skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It features square, hairy stems and opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its root system is fibrous and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported calming properties.

Scutellaria discolor thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
