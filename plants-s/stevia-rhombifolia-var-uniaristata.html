Stevia rhombifolia var. uniaristata

Stevia rhombifolia var. uniaristata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf stevia, is a perennial herb native to South America.

It is characterized by its rhombic-shaped leaves and is often cultivated for its sweet-tasting leaves, which contain high levels of steviol glycosides. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for both garden and commercial cultivation. Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular choice for landscaping.

Due to its low maintenance requirements, it is also favored in sustainable gardening practices.
