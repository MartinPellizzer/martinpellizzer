Stipagrostis uniplumis var. intermedia

Stipagrostis uniplumis var. intermedia, commonly known as the intermediate speargrass, is a perennial grass native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It is adapted to survive in harsh environments with low rainfall and high temperatures.

Its fibrous roots help prevent soil erosion, making it ecologically significant in its habitat.
