Senecio caloxanthus

Senecio caloxanthus, commonly known as the yellow oxeye daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow ray florets and dark brown central disk. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It produces small, tubular flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Senecio caloxanthus is often used in rock gardens due to its hardy nature and ornamental value.
