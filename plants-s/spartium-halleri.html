Spartium halleri

Spartium halleri, commonly known as Haller's sparrowgrass, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges.
