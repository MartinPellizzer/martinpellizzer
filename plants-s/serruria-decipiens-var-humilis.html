Serruria decipiens var. humilis

Serruria decipiens var. humilis, commonly known as the dwarf serruria, is a small, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom in spring. It is adapted to rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
