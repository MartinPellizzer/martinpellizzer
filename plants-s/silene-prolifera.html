Silene prolifera

Silene prolifera, commonly known as the clustered campion, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its pink to red flowers and succulent-like stems. Silene prolifera is often found in open habitats such as sagebrush steppe and rocky slopes.
