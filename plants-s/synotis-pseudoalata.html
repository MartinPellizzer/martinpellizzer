Synotis pseudoalata

Synotis pseudoalata, commonly known as the false winged bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, often with a hairy texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its fruit is a pod that contains several elliptical seeds.

Synotis pseudoalata is valued for its nutritional content and is used in traditional agriculture and local cuisines.
