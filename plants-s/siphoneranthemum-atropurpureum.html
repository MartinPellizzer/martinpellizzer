Siphoneranthemum atropurpureum

Siphoneranthemum atropurpureum, commonly known as the purple siphoneranthemum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking purple to violet flowers. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are clustered in whorls along the stems, creating a distinctive appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
