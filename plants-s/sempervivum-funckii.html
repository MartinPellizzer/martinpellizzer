Sempervivum funckii

Sempervivum funckii, commonly known as the Alpine houseleek, is a low-growing succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe.

It features rosettes of fleshy, pointed leaves that are typically green with reddish or purple hues, especially in full sun. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It is well adapted to rocky and arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

Sempervivum funckii is a popular choice for rock gardens and containers due to its hardiness and ornamental appeal.
