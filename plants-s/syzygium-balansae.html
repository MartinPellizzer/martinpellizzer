Syzygium balansae

Syzygium balansae, commonly known as the Balansae apple.

It is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Philippines, belonging to the Myrtaceae family. The plant produces small, round fruits with a smooth, red or yellowish skin. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a glossy surface.

Syzygium balansae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic beauty.
