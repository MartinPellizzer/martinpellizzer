Spergularia battandieri

Spergularia battandieri, commonly known as the coastal sandspurry, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that thrives in sandy and coastal environments. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are usually narrow and succulent, adapted to conserve water in arid conditions.
