Sedum hutchisonii

Sedum hutchisonii, commonly known as the Hutchison's stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of central China.

It is characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Its leaves are typically green and often have a slightly waxy texture, aiding in water retention.

Sedum hutchisonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
