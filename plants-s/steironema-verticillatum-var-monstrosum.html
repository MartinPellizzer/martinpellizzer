Steironema verticillatum var. monstrosum

Steironema verticillatum var. monstrosum, commonly known as the giant feather plant, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, branched stem and clusters of long, feathery leaves that give it a dramatic appearance. The plant has a fibrous root system and thrives in moist, well-drained soil. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and ability to grow rapidly.

Steironema verticillatum var. monstrosum prefers bright, indirect light and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
