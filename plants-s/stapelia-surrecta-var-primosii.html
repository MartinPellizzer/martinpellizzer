Stapelia surrecta var. primosii

Stapelia surrecta var. primosii, commonly known as the Ghost Plant, is a succulent belonging to the Apocynaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its unique, fleshy, and often ghost-like appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its striking appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
