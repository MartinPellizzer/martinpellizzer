Saxifraga mengtzeana var. foliolata

Saxifraga mengtzeana var. foliolata, commonly known as the leafy saxifrage, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and rosette-forming leaves. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers during the summer months, typically in June or July. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in horticulture.
