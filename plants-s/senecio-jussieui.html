Senecio jussieui

Senecio jussieui, commonly known as the Jussieui groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, followed by fluffy, seed-filled heads.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
