Solanum lasiophyllum var. lasiophyllum

Solanum lasiophyllum var. lasiophyllum, commonly known as the purple leaf nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It features striking violet to purple foliage, with leaves that are often velvety to the touch. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its fruits are berries that transition from green to red as they mature.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique leaf color and compact growth habit.
