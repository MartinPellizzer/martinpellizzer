Scutellaria indica var. elliptica

Scutellaria indica var. elliptica, commonly known as Indian skullcap, is a perennial herb native to South Asia.

It features opposite, elliptic to ovate leaves that are typically hairy and have a rough texture. The plant produces blue to purple tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional medicinal uses in Ayurvedic practices.
