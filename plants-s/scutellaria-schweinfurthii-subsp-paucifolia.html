Scutellaria schweinfurthii subsp. paucifolia

Scutellaria schweinfurthii subsp.

paucifolia, commonly known as the slender blue skullcap, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States. It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as woodland edges and stream banks.

The plant features slender, hairy stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are blue to purple, arranged in whorls along the stem, and bloom in late spring to early summer.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native plant gardens.
