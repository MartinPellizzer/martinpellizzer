Schefflera acuminatissima

Schefflera acuminatissima, commonly known as the giant umbrella plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, umbrella-like crown composed of numerous leaflets arranged in a whorled pattern. The plant has dark green, glossy, and leathery leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. It produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by black, berry-like fruits.

Schefflera acuminatissima is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and adaptability to various growing conditions.
