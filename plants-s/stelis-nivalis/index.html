Stelis Nivalis

Stelis nivalis is a rare and delicate orchid found in high-altitude regions of the Andes. It thrives in cold, moist environments where few plants can survive. Its flowers are small, white, and often covered in a fine, frost-like coating. This species is known for its unique adaptation to extreme climates. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this fragile beauty from habitat loss.
