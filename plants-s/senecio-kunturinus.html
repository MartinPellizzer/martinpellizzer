Senecio kunturinus

Senecio kunturinus, commonly known as the Andean groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Senecio kunturinus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens in temperate climates.
