Spathiphyllum humboldtii

Spathiphyllum humboldtii, commonly known as the Humboldt peace lily, is a tropical evergreen plant native to the Caribbean and Central America.

It features broad, dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces striking white spathes that enclose small, greenish-yellow flowers, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in low-light conditions and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Spathiphyllum humboldtii is popular as a houseplant due to its air-purifying qualities and ease of care.
