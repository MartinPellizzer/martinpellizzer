Scutellaria alexeenkoi

Scutellaria alexeenkoi, commonly known as the Russian skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color, arranged in whorls along the stem. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Scutellaria alexeenkoi is often found in rocky or gravelly soils at elevations above 1,500 meters.
