Senecio crassulus

Senecio crassulus, commonly known as the silver rose or ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white wax, giving it a silvery appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, which are clustered in umbels. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
