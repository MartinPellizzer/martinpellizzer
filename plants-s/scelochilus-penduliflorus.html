Scelochilus penduliflorus

Scelochilus penduliflorus, commonly known as the hanging flower plant, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its pendulous inflorescences. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in humid environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Scelochilus penduliflorus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique floral display.
