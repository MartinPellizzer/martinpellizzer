Sorbus teodori f. elegans

Sorbus teodori f. elegans, commonly known as the elegant whitebeams, is a compact, ornamental shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features small, dark green leaves that turn vibrant red in the fall, adding seasonal interest to the landscape. The plant produces clusters of white, apple-like fruits that are edible but not particularly flavorful. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and small landscapes.

Its slow growth rate and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a hardy, decorative plant.
