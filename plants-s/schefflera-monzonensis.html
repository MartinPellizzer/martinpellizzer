Schefflera monzonensis

Schefflera monzonensis, commonly known as the Monzon's umbrella tree, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like crown of large, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by black, berry-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Schefflera monzonensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor environments.
