Sanguisorba minor var. virescens

Sanguisorba minor var. virescens, commonly known as green sandwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern North America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features upright stems covered in dense clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a reddish or purple hue, especially when young.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native plant gardens.
