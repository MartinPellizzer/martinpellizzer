Syringa × chinensis f. bicolor

Syringa × chinensis f. bicolor, commonly known as the weeping white lilac, is a hybrid shrub that exhibits unique botanical features.

It is characterized by its pendulous, fragrant flowers that bloom in early summer, typically in shades of white and pink. The plant has dark green, lance-shaped leaves that provide a striking contrast to its floral display. It is a compact, ornamental shrub that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
