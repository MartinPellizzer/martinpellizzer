Senecio ucranicus

Senecio ucranicus, commonly known as the Ucranicus groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid to semi-arid environments. Its leaves are generally narrow and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Senecio ucranicus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and resilience.
