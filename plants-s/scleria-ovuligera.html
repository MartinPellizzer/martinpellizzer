Scleria ovuligera

Scleria ovuligera, commonly known as the African bulrush, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes grass-like plants known as sedges. The plant is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Scleria ovuligera thrives in wetland environments and is often found in marshy areas and along water bodies.
