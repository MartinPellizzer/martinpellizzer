Senecio schischkinianus

Senecio schischkinianus, commonly known as the Schischkin's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by fluffy, white seed heads.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
