Silene korshinskyi

Silene korshinskyi, commonly known as the Korshinsky's catchfly, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

Silene korshinskyi is often found in alpine meadows and is adapted to withstand cold temperatures and strong winds.
