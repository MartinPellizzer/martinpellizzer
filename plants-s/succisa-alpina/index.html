Succisa Alpina

Succisa alpina is a delicate flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. It thrives in rocky and sandy soils at high elevations where few other plants can survive. Known for its small, bright yellow flowers, it adds a splash of color to the mountain landscape. This species is often found in meadows and grasslands, blooming in late spring and early summer. Its resilience and beauty make it a symbol of the harsh yet beautiful alpine environment.
