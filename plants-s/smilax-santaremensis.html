Smilax santaremensis

Smilax santaremensis, commonly known as the Santa Remensis arrowhead vine, is a climbing plant native to the Caribbean region.

It belongs to the family Smilacaceae and is characterized by its woody stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically borne in clusters. Its fruits are red berries that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by animals.

Smilax santaremensis is often found in moist, shaded areas of tropical forests.
