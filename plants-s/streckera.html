Streckera

Streckera, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to Africa.

These plants are characterized by their compact, bushy growth habit and showy, velvety flowers that come in a variety of colors. The leaves are typically thick, fleshy, and arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps the plant conserve moisture. Streckera species are popular houseplants due to their ability to thrive in low-light conditions and their relatively easy care requirements.

They belong to the Gesneriaceae family and are prized for their ornamental value.
