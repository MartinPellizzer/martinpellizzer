Satyrium rupestre

Satyrium rupestre, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows on rocky or limestone soils. The plant features a slender, wiry stem with small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinctive lip that is often darker.

It blooms in early spring and is adapted to thrive in arid or semi-arid environments.
