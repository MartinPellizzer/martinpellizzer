Sisymbrium ragoneseanum

Sisymbrium ragoneseanum, commonly known as the Ragonese mustard, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and linear, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in various regions of Europe. It produces slender, elongated pods that contain numerous small, round seeds.

Sisymbrium ragoneseanum is often considered a weed but has ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
