Saintpaulia velutina

Saintpaulia velutina, commonly known as the velvet violet.

It is a species of African violet native to the tropical regions of East Africa. The plant features velvety, dark green leaves with a prominent central vein. Its flowers are typically violet or purple, with a showy, clustered appearance.

Saintpaulia velutina is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
