Specklinia acanthodes

Specklinia acanthodes, commonly known as the velvety pigmy orchid, is a small epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, velvety foliage. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, typically blooming during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Specklinia acanthodes is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
