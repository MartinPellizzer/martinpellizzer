Scilla picta

Scilla picta, commonly known as the painted scilla, is a flowering plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to southern Europe and western Asia, where it thrives in woodland and rocky habitats. Scilla picta is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and ability to naturalize in gardens.
