Sideritis nutans

Sideritis nutans, commonly known as the drooping ironwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, tubular flowers that are typically blue to violet in color. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in fine hairs.

Sideritis nutans is known for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
