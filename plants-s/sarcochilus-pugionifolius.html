Sarcochilus pugionifolius

Sarcochilus pugionifolius, commonly known as the sword-leafed or spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a dense, wiry root system and produces a single, erect, leafy stem. The plant bears small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive, narrow, spathaceous bract. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained, humus-rich soil.

Sarcochilus pugionifolius is known for its unique appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
