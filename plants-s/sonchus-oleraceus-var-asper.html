Sonchus oleraceus var. asper

Sonchus oleraceus var. asper, commonly known as prickly lettuce, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its finely toothed, lobed leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving them a rough texture. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the daisy family. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been naturalized in various regions around the world.

Prickly lettuce is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats, including fields and roadsides.
