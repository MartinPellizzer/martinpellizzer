Sedum dasyphyllum var. congestum

Sedum dasyphyllum var. congestum, commonly known as the denseleaf stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green to reddish in color, arranged in a dense rosette formation. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that range from white to pale pink, usually blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its adaptability and low maintenance requirements make it an attractive addition to arid landscapes.
