Sinocarum sikkimense

Sinocarum sikkimense, commonly known as the Sikkim sinocarum, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Polygonaceae.

It is characterized by its upright, branched stems and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. Native to the eastern Himalayas, particularly in regions of India and Nepal, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate in shape, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The plant is often found in forest understories and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitat.
