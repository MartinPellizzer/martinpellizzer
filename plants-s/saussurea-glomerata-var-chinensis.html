Saussurea glomerata var. chinensis

Saussurea glomerata var. chinensis, commonly known as Chinese yarrow or Chinese bloodwort, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tufted, fibrous root system and dense clusters of small, pale yellow to white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing adaptability to harsh conditions. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
