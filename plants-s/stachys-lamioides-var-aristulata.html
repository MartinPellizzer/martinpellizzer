Stachys lamioides var. aristulata

Stachys lamioides var. aristulata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved betony, is a perennial herb native to the United States.

It features upright, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with a serrated margin. The plant produces spikes of violet-blue flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sun.

Its foliage and flowers are often used in traditional herbal medicine.
