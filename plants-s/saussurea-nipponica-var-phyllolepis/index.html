Saussurea Nipponica Var. Phyllolepis

Saussurea nipponica var. phyllolepis is a rare and striking plant native to the high altitudes of Japan. Known for its large, showy yellow flowers, it thrives in cold, mountainous environments. This variety is distinguished by its unique leaf structure and is often found in rocky soils. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a delicately endangered species. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving its natural habitat and ensuring its survival.
