Stormesia bifurcata

Stormesia bifurcata, commonly known as the bifurcated stormesia, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bi-lobed flowers that bloom in clusters, often displaying vibrant colors such as pink and purple. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and it thrives in humid, tropical environments. It is typically found in moist forest understories, where it receives filtered sunlight.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
