Sphaerocodon natalense

Sphaerocodon natalense, commonly known as Natal globe thistle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spherical, blue to purple flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, daisy-like flowers.

Sphaerocodon natalense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
