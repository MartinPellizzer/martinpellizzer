Statice ornata

Statice ornata, commonly known as the ornate statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its dense, spike-like clusters of small, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy or rocky soils and is drought-tolerant. Its flowers come in a variety of colors, including pink, red, and white, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Statice ornata is also used in floral arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms.
