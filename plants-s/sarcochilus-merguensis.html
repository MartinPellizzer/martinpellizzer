Sarcochilus merguensis

Sarcochilus merguensis, commonly known as the Merguens Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It features a pseudobulb with a single leaf and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant typically blooms during the dry season, and its inflorescence is a raceme of relatively small flowers. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Sarcochilus merguensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
