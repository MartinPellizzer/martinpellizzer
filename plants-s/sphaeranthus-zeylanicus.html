Sphaeranthus zeylanicus

Sphaeranthus zeylanicus, commonly known as the Ceylon globe thistle, is a flowering plant native to Sri Lanka.

It is a herbaceous annual with a compact, globe-shaped inflorescence composed of numerous small, violet-blue flowers. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are lance-shaped and often covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to tropical and subtropical climates.

The flowers bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
