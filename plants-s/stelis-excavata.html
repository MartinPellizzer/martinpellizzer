Stelis excavata

Stelis excavata, commonly known as the hollow stem orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its unique, hollow stems that give it its common name. Native to Southeast Asia, this plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The flowers are usually small and white or pale green, clustered in racemes.

Stelis excavata is often found in forest undergrowth and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
