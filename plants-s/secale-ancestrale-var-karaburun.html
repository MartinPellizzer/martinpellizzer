Secale ancestrale var. karaburun

Secale ancestrale var. karaburun, commonly known as Karaburun ryegrass, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to yellowish flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and rocky habitats.

Due to its hardy nature, it is occasionally used in ecological restoration projects.
