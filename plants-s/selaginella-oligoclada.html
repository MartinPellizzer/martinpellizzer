Selaginella oligoclada

Selaginella oligoclada, commonly known as the slender spikemoss, is a species of spikemoss belonging to the family Selaginellaceae.

It is a small, evergreen plant that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in whorls around the stems, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces small, cone-like structures called strobila, which contain the reproductive organs.

Selaginella oligoclada is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
