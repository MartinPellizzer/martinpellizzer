Scrophularia landroveri

Scrophularia landroveri, commonly known as the Landrover scrophularia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to pink. The plant typically thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats. It produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, dark seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many areas.
