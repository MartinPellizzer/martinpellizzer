Stelis parvipetala

Stelis parvipetala, commonly known as the small-petaled orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit and produces small, delicate flowers with a unique coloration. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with intricate patterns on the petals.

Stelis parvipetala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
