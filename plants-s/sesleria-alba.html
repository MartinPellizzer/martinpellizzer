Sesleria alba

Sesleria alba, commonly known as the white sesleria, is a perennial grass-like plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, white to pale blue florets that bloom in summer, adding a delicate floral display. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Sesleria alba is also valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
