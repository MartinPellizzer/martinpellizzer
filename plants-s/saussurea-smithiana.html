Saussurea smithiana

Saussurea smithiana, commonly known as the Alpine Goatsbeard, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, white to pale yellow ray florets. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its fibrous root system helps it anchor in steep, nutrient-poor soils.

Saussurea smithiana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique appearance.
