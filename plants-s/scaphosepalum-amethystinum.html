Scaphosepalum amethystinum

Scaphosepalum amethystinum, commonly known as the purple-stemmed orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, violet-hued stems that are often adorned with waxy, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
