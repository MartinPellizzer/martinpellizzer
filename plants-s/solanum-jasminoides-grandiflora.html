Solanum jasminoides grandiflora

Solanum jasminoides grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia and is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that bloom at night. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces berries that turn from green to red as they mature. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

However, like many members of the nightshade family, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
