Senecio cinerarioides

Senecio cinerarioides, commonly known as the blueweed or silverleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-gray leaves and clusters of small, blue or purple flowers. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky or sandy soils.

Senecio cinerarioides is also valued as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and flowers.
