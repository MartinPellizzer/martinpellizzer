Sinarundinaria yunnanensis

Sinarundinaria yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan bamboo, is a tall, slender grass-like plant native to China.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its long, hollow stems and narrow, blade-like leaves. This species is often found in moist, mountainous regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Sinarundinaria yunnanensis is also notable for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
