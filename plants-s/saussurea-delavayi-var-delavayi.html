Saussurea delavayi var. delavayi

Saussurea delavayi var. delavayi, commonly known as the Yunnan gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of western China.

It features a thick, rhizomatous root system and upright stems that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant produces large, showy blue to purple flowers in early summer, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are lanceolate and covered with a fine, white pubescence.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in high-altitude gardens.
