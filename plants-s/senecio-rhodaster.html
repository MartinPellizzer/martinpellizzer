Senecio rhodaster

Senecio rhodaster, commonly known as the red starflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, pink to reddish-orange ray florets surrounding a central disk of yellow tubular flowers. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Senecio rhodaster is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its striking appearance.
