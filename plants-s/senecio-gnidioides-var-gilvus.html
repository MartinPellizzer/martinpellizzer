Senecio gnidioides var. gilvus

Senecio gnidioides var. gilvus, commonly known as the golden senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and gray-green in color, with a distinctive fuzzy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

This plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
