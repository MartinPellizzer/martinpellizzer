Scilla idaea

Scilla idaea, commonly known as the snowflake squill, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that resemble snowflakes, typically blooming in early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge from a basal rosette of bulbs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Scilla idaea is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ease of care.
