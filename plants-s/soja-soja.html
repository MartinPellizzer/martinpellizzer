Soja soja

Soja soja, commonly known as Chinese soybean, is a leguminous plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pod-like fruits. The plant produces edible seeds that are rich in protein and used in various culinary and industrial applications. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Soja soja is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, benefiting agricultural ecosystems.
