Swartzia fanshawei

Swartzia fanshawei, commonly known as the Guangxi ironwood, is a tropical tree native to China.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its dense, dark-colored wood. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellowish flowers. It is valued for its hardiness and is often used in ornamental horticulture.

The species is also noted for its slow growth and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
