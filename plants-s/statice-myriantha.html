Statice myriantha

Statice myriantha, commonly known as blue statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and its clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. The plant produces narrow, grass-like leaves and has a long flowering period, typically from summer to autumn. It is often used in dried flower arrangements due to its durability and vibrant color.

Botanically, it belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is valued for its ornamental qualities in gardens.
