Stapelia × divergens

Stapelia × divergens, commonly known as the hybrid star-shaped stapelia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its unique, star-shaped, fleshy leaves that resemble the petals of a flower. The plant produces striking, red-orange flowers with a strong, unpleasant odor, which attracts pollinators like flies. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its hybrid nature, Stapelia × divergens exhibits a mix of traits from its parent species, making it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
