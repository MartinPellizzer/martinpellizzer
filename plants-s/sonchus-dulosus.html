Sonchus dulosus

Sonchus dulosus, commonly known as the common sowthistle, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is an annual herb that typically grows in disturbed soils, such as roadsides and fields. The plant has alternate, rough-hairy leaves that are usually heart-shaped at the base. Its yellow flowers are arranged in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family.

Sonchus dulosus is often considered a weed but can be found in various regions across Europe and parts of Asia.
