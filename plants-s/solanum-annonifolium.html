Solanum annonifolium

Solanum annonifolium, commonly known as the tropical nightshade, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades like tomatoes and peppers. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruits are berries that change from green to red as they mature.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its attractive foliage and flowers.
