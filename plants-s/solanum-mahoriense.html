Solanum mahoriense

Solanum mahoriense, commonly known as the Mahori nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its hairy, oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, violet-colored flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by berries that change from green to red as they mature.

Solanum mahoriense is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth.
