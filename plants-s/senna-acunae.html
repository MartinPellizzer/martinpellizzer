Senna acunae

Senna acunae, commonly known as the golden senna, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Its leaves are trifoliate, with each leaflet being oval and slightly hairy.

Senna acunae is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
