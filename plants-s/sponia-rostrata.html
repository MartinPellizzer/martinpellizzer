Sponia rostrata

Sponia rostrata, commonly known as the rostrated sponia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive rosette of fleshy, spoon-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the daisy family. It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
