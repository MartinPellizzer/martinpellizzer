Schefflera panamensis

Schefflera panamensis, commonly known as the Panamanian umbrella plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Central America.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like clusters of large, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters, with a broad, spreading habit. Its leaves are usually leathery and have a smooth surface, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Schefflera panamensis thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
