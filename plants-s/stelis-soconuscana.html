Stelis soconuscana

Stelis soconuscana, commonly known as the Soconusco stelis, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant typically produces clustered inflorescences with small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Its leaves are usually ovate to elliptic, with a waxy surface that helps reduce water loss.

Stelis soconuscana thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.
