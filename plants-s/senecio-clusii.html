Senecio clusii

Senecio clusii, commonly known as the Clusius's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features dark green, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a cluster. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in horticulture.
