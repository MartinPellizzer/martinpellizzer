Senecio cymbalariifolius var. rotundifolius

Senecio cymbalariifolius var. rotundifolius, commonly known as the round-leaf senecio, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, rounded leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in composite heads, typical of the daisy family. It is native to South Africa and thrives in well-drained, sandy soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain environments.
