Scutellaria ossethica

Scutellaria ossethica, commonly known as the Ossetian skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features square stems and opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. Its small, tubular flowers are typically blue or purple, arranged in whorls along the stem.

Scutellaria ossethica is often found in rocky or dry habitats and is valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
