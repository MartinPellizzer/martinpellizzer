Sphenodesme pentandra var. wallichiana

Sphenodesme pentandra var. wallichiana, commonly known as the Himalayan rose.

This plant is a perennial shrub characterized by its woody stems and dense, dark green foliage. It produces showy, pink or white flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The leaves are typically elliptical with serrated edges, and the plant thrives in cool, moist environments.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Asia, particularly in the Himalayas.
