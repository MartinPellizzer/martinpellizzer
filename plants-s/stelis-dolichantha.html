Stelis dolichantha

Stelis dolichantha, commonly known as the long-stemmed spider orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on tree trunks or rocks. Its roots are adapted for epiphytic growth, allowing it to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air.

Stelis dolichantha is valued for its unique appearance and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
