Solanum depilatum

Solanum depilatum, commonly known as the hairy nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 meter. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it its common name. Its flowers are small, star-shaped, and usually white or pale green, appearing in clusters.

The fruit is a smooth, fleshy berry that turns from green to red as it matures.
