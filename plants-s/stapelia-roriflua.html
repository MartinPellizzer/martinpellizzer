Stapelia roriflua

Stapelia roriflua, commonly known as the "Cape Thorn" or "Cape Flax," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and spiny leaves. The plant produces striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange with a strong, unpleasant odor. These flowers bloom during the summer months and attract pollinators such as flies and beetles.

Stapelia roriflua is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
