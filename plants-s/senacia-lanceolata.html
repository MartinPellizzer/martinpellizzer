Senacia lanceolata

Senacia lanceolata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small yellow flowers arranged in clusters. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens, though it requires well-drained soil and minimal water.
