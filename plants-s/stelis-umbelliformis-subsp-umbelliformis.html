Stelis umbelliformis subsp. umbelliformis

Stelis umbelliformis subsp.

umbelliformis, commonly known as the umbellate stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and small, clustered flowers that often display a range of colors from white to pale pink.

The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
