Scaphosepalum delhierroi

Scaphosepalum delhierroi, commonly known as the Delhierroi orchid, is a species native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive lip that is often brightly colored. It typically grows in shaded, moist environments within forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a notable subject for both horticultural and scientific study.
