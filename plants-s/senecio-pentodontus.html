Senecio pentodontus

Senecio pentodontus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small yellow flowers arranged in clusters. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
