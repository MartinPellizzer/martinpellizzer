Stenandrium humile

Stenandrium humile, commonly known as the dwarf stenandrium, is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and glossy in appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in coastal forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in tropical gardens.
