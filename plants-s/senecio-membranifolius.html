Senecio membranifolius

Senecio membranifolius, commonly known as the paperleaf senecio, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, narrow, and often bluish-green leaves that are covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
