Statice

Statice, commonly known as sea lavender, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region and coastal areas.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its showy, spike-like inflorescences. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. Statice is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its ability to grow in saline environments also makes it a valuable plant for coastal landscaping.
