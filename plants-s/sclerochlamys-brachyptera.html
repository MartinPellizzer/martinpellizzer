Sclerochlamys brachyptera

Sclerochlamys brachyptera, commonly known as the short-winged rock orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive, spurred orchid flowers that are typically pale green to brown in color. The plant thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers that bloom during the cooler months.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many regions.
