Senecio snowdenii

Senecio snowdenii, commonly known as Snowden's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by papery, white bracts.

Senecio snowdenii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and hardy nature.
