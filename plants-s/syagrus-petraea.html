Syagrus petraea

Syagrus petraea, commonly known as the royal palm, is a tropical plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellowish-white flowers that develop into bright red fruits, which are edible for some bird species. Syagrus petraea thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its elegant foliage and resilience in warm climates.
