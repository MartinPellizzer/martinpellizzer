Stapelia × luxurians

Stapelia × luxurians, commonly known as the hybrid star-shaped stapelia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is a hybrid between Stapelia rubra and Stapelia hirsuta, resulting in a plant with unique floral characteristics. The flowers are typically yellow with a distinctive star-shaped pattern and emit a strong, unpleasant odor to attract pollinators. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

This plant is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unusual appearance and low maintenance requirements.
