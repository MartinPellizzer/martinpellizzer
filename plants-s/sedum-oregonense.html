Sedum oregonense

Sedum oregonense, commonly known as Oregon stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow to red. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in alpine or arid environments. It has a compact growth habit and is well adapted to withstand drought and cold temperatures.

Oregon stonecrop is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements in landscaping.
