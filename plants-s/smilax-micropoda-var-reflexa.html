Smilax micropoda var. reflexa

Smilax micropoda var. reflexa, commonly known as the reflexed smilax, is a perennial vine native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into red, fleshy berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and wetlands.

Its botanical features make it an important component of understory vegetation in its native habitat.
