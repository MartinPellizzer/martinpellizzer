Schinus gracilipes

Schinus gracilipes, commonly known as the Brazilian pepper tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to South America.

Schinus gracilipes is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its attractive appearance and adaptability to different climates.
