Sophora stenophylla

Sophora stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf sophora, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for soil enrichment. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas or along riverbanks.

The seeds of Sophora stenophylla are encased in a hard pod and are dispersed by wind or animals.
