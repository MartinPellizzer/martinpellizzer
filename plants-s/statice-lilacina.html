Statice lilacina

Statice lilacina, commonly known as the lilac statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink to lilac, which bloom in late summer. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically gray-green in color.

Statice lilacina is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
