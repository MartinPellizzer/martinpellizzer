Statice ruizii

Statice ruizii, commonly known as the pink statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions. Its flowers come in shades of pink, red, and white, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Statice ruizii is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.
