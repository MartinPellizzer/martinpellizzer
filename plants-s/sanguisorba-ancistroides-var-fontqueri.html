Sanguisorba ancistroides var. fontqueri

Sanguisorba ancistroides var. fontqueri, commonly known as the Fontqueri's sweetflag, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a bluish-green hue, while its roots are fibrous and can form dense colonies.
