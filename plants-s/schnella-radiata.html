Schnella radiata

Schnella radiata, commonly known as the radiate schnella, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking radial symmetry, with flowers that radiate outward in a vibrant display. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, with a smooth texture and a deep green color.

Schnella radiata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual impact.
