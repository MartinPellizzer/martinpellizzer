Strobilanthes rufescens subsp. parvibracteata

Strobilanthes rufescens subsp.

parvibracteata, commonly known as the red-flowered strobilanthes, is a species native to Southeast Asia. It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its distinctive red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The subspecies parvibracteata is distinguished by its smaller bracts compared to the typical form of *S. rufescens*.
