Sicyos erostratus var. paucipilosus

Sicyos erostratus var. paucipilosus, commonly known as the slender gourd, is a variant of the common gourd species native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a vine that produces small, elongated fruits with a smooth, pale green exterior. The plant has palmate leaves with lobed margins and is typically found in moist, tropical environments. Its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the same plant.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been cultivated for both decorative and culinary purposes.
