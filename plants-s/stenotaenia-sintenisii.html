Stenotaenia sintenisii

Stenotaenia sintenisii, commonly known as the Sintenisi's orchid, is a rare and endemic orchid species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves and a striking inflorescence of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, where it relies on mycorrhizal fungi for nutrient absorption. Its flowers exhibit a unique coloration, often displaying a combination of white, yellow, and green hues.

Due to habitat destruction, Stenotaenia sintenisii is considered endangered, making conservation efforts crucial for its survival.
