Sarothamnus balansae

Sarothamnus balansae, commonly known as the Balansae thyme, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, aromatic leaves that are often used in culinary and medicinal applications. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pink to purple flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
