Senecio ceratophylloides

Senecio ceratophylloides, commonly known as the horned everlasting or horned senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

Senecio ceratophylloides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique visual characteristics.
