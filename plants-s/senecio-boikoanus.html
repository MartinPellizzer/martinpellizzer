Senecio boikoanus

Senecio boikoanus, commonly known as the Boikoan ragwort, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and ragworts, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun exposure. It produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
