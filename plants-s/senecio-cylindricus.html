Senecio cylindricus

Senecio cylindricus, commonly known as the cylindrical groundsel, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a distinctive, cylindrical stem that is often covered in fine, hairy hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a conical shape, typical of the Asteraceae family. Its leaves are linear and may have a slightly woolly texture.

It is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
