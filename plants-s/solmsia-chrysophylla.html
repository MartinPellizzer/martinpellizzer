Solmsia chrysophylla

Solmsia chrysophylla, commonly known as the golden-leaved solmsia, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow foliage and clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance, it is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in horticultural settings.
