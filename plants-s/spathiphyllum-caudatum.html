Spathiphyllum caudatum

Spathiphyllum caudatum, commonly known as the snake plant, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Central and South America.

It features tall, upright leaves that are typically variegated with green and white stripes. The plant produces clusters of small, white spathes that enclose bright yellow flowers, which bloom infrequently. It is known for its ability to thrive in low light conditions and requires minimal watering.

Spathiphyllum caudatum is also popular for its air-purifying qualities and ease of care.
