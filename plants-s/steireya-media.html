Steireya media

Steireya media, commonly known as the silverleaf spurge, is a perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Steireya media is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
