Scabiosa dumetorum

Scabiosa dumetorum, commonly known as mouse-ear scabious, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. The plant features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a distinctive blue or purple hue. Its leaves are usually hairy and heart-shaped, contributing to its characteristic appearance.

Scabiosa dumetorum is known for its resilience and ability to thrive in poor soils.
