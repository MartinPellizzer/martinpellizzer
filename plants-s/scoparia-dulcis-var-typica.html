Scoparia dulcis var. typica

Scoparia dulcis var. typica, commonly known as sweet alyssum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant white or pink flowers and linear, aromatic leaves. The plant is often grown as an ornamental due to its delicate appearance and pleasant scent. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Scoparia dulcis var. typica is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
