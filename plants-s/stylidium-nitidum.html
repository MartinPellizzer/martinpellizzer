Stylidium nitidum

Stylidium nitidum, commonly known as the glossy sun-orchid, is a small, herbaceous plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its striking, sun-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils in open habitats such as heathlands and grasslands. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered with a waxy coating that helps reduce water loss.

Stylidium nitidum is a short-lived perennial, often flowering in spring and early summer.
