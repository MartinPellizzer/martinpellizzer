Schizostachyum durie

Schizostachyum durie, commonly known as the Durie's bamboo, is a species of grass native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 10 meters. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of hairs. It thrives in moist, tropical environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Schizostachyum durie is valued for its strong, lightweight timber and is used in construction and handicrafts.
