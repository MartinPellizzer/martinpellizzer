Scutellaria anatolica

Scutellaria anatolica, commonly known as Anatolian skullcap, is a perennial herb native to Turkey and parts of the Middle East.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. The plant features square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Scutellaria anatolica is valued for its potential medicinal properties, though further research is needed to confirm its therapeutic uses.
