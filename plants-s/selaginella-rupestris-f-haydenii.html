Selaginella rupestris f. haydenii

Selaginella rupestris f. haydenii, commonly known as the Hayden's spikemoss, is a rare and distinctive member of the Selaginellaceae family.

It is a vascular plant characterized by its upright, spiky stems and small, scale-like leaves. Native to specific regions of the southwestern United States, it thrives in rocky, arid environments. The plant exhibits a unique reproductive system, producing microspores and megaspores on separate structures.

Its limited distribution and specialized habitat make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
