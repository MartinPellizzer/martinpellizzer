Senecio sparsilobatus

Senecio sparsilobatus, commonly known as the sparse-lobed groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant produces small, yellow composite flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are usually narrow and have a distinctive, sparse lobing pattern.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
