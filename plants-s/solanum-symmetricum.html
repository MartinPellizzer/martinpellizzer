Solanum symmetricum

Solanum symmetricum, commonly known as the symmetric nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant is characterized by its distinctive, symmetrical flowers that are typically purple or blue in color. Its leaves are usually oval-shaped with a slightly hairy texture.

Solanum symmetricum is often found in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
