Senecio antaisaka

Senecio antaisaka, commonly known as the Antaisaka groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils. It features upright stems with narrow, hairy leaves and produces yellow daisy-like flowers.

Senecio antaisaka is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
