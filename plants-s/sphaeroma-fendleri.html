Sphaeroma fendleri

Sphaeroma fendleri, commonly known as the desert hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its woody stem and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to red. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water availability.
