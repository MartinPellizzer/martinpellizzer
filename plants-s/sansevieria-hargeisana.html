Sansevieria hargeisana

Sansevieria hargeisana, commonly known as the Hargeisa snake plant, is a striking succulent native to the arid regions of southern Arabia.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are dark green with prominent, light green vertical stripes. The plant is characterized by its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it highly suitable for indoor environments. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water efficiently in its native habitat.

Sansevieria hargeisana is also noted for its air-purifying properties, contributing to improved indoor air quality.
