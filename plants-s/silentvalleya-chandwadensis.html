Silentvalleya chandwadensis

Silentvalleya chandwadensis, commonly known as the Chandwad Valley orchid, is a rare flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is characterized by its delicate, strap-like leaves and small, pale pink or white flowers that bloom during the monsoon season. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in the dense undergrowth of tropical forests. Its unique morphology and restricted habitat make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered vulnerable and requires careful protection.
