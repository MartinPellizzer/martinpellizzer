Sesleria bielzii

Sesleria bielzii, commonly known as the woolly pink grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and its striking pinkish to purple inflorescences. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Sesleria bielzii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in well-drained conditions.
