Senecio solanifolius

Senecio solanifolius, commonly known as the purpleleaf senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy, composite flower heads. The plant features lance-shaped, often hairy leaves that are typically green with a purple or reddish tinge on the underside. It produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.

Senecio solanifolius is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
