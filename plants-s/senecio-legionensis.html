Senecio legionensis

Senecio legionensis, commonly known as the Cape Verde iron plant, is a flowering plant native to the Cape Verde Islands.

It is a shrub characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and yellow-orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is adapted to arid environments, featuring a deep root system for water absorption. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Senecio legionensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in dry climates.
