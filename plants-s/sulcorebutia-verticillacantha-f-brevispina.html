Sulcorebutia verticillacantha f. brevispina

Sulcorebutia verticillacantha f. brevispina, commonly known as the short-spined stone cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to South America.

It features a dense covering of short, cylindrical spines, giving it a unique and rugged appearance. The plant has a ribbed stem that can range from light green to pale blue in color. It produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months.

This species is well-suited for rock gardens and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
