Sturmia reflexa

Sturmia reflexa, commonly known as the reflexed sturmia, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Andes in South America.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with five petals and prominent stamens. Sturmia reflexa thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or alpine regions.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest for botanists studying alpine flora.
