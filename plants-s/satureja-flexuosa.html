Satureja flexuosa

Satureja flexuosa, commonly known as flexible savory, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and has small, lance-shaped leaves with a pungent, savory scent. Its flowers are usually pink or purple and are arranged in whorls along the stems.

Satureja flexuosa is often used in traditional herbal medicine and as a culinary herb due to its aromatic properties.
