Staphylea shweliensis

Staphylea shweliensis, commonly known as the Shweli sycamore, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Hippocastanaceae family and is characterized by its palmately lobed leaves and showy, bell-shaped flowers. The tree produces woody, spiny capsules that contain smooth, glossy seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.
