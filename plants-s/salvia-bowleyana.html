Salvia bowleyana

Salvia bowleyana, commonly known as the Christmas sage, is a striking evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from deep red to purple, blooming profusely during the winter months. The plant has aromatic, gray-green foliage that adds visual interest throughout the year. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value and adaptability make it a favored plant among gardeners seeking winter color.
