Sansevieria vanillosa

Sansevieria vanillosa, commonly known as the Vanillosa Snake Plant, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a subtle yellowish tinge. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear at the ends of its stems. It is highly drought-tolerant and thrives in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ability to purify the air and its low maintenance requirements further contribute to its popularity.
