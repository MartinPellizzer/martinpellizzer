Stemmadenia pauli

Stemmadenia pauli, commonly known as the African star gooseberry, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It has small, elliptical leaves that are opposite and often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by small, berry-like fruits. Its stem is woody and branched, with a somewhat succulent texture.

The plant is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
