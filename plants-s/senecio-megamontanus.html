Senecio megamontanus

Senecio megamontanus, commonly known as the giant montane senecio, is a striking member of the Asteraceae family native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, woolly leaves and showy yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils at high altitudes. It is adapted to cool, moist climates and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
