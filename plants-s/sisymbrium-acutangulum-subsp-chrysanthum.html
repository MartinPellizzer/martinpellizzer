Sisymbrium acutangulum subsp. chrysanthum

Sisymbrium acutangulum subsp.

chrysanthum, commonly known as the yellow rocket, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family. It is characterized by its yellow, cruciform flowers and hairy, pinnately divided leaves.

The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is found in temperate regions of Europe and Asia. It produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and used in ecological restoration projects.
