Sprekelia clintiae

Sprekelia clintiae, commonly known as the golden trumpet, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking orange-yellow tubular flowers that bloom in spring. The plant features succulent, ribbed leaves that are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Sprekelia clintiae is often cultivated for its vibrant, showy blooms and drought-resistant nature.
