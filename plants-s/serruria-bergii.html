Serruria bergii

Serruria bergii, commonly known as the Cape snowbush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of white or pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is adapted to arid conditions, with small, waxy leaves that reduce water loss. It is often found in rocky outcrops and mountainous regions, thriving in full sun.

Serruria bergii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
