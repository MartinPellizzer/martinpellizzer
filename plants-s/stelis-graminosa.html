Stelis graminosa

Stelis graminosa, commonly known as the grassy stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, delicate flowers. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant typically grows in low-lying areas with rich, moist soil.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and ecological study.
