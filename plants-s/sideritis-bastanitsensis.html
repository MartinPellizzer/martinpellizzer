Sideritis bastanitsensis

Sideritis bastanitsensis, commonly known as the Bastanitsa sida, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Turkey.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in rocky and dry environments, often growing at high altitudes.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
