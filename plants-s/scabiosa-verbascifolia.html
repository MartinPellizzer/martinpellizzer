Scabiosa verbascifolia

Scabiosa verbascifolia, commonly known as the tall scabious, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of pink or purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are hairy and grayish-green in appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Scabiosa verbascifolia is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
