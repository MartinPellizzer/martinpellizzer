Sophora davidi var. liangshanensis

Sophora davidi var. liangshanensis, commonly known as the Liangshan Sophora, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive yellow flowers. The plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in mountainous regions with well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural use in gardens and landscapes.
