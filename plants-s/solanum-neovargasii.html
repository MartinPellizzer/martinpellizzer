Solanum neovargasii

Solanum neovargasii, commonly known as the Vargasii nightshade, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, specifically Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its small, hairy leaves and clusters of white or pale blue flowers. Its fruits are typically berries that mature to a yellow or orange color.

Solanum neovargasii is often found in humid, shaded environments within cloud forests.
