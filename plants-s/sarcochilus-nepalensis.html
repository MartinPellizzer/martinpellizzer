Sarcochilus nepalensis

Sarcochilus nepalensis, commonly known as the Nepal stone orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Himalayan regions of Nepal and northern India.

It features a pseudobulb with a single leaf and produces pendulous inflorescences bearing showy, fragrant flowers. The flowers are typically yellow with green and brown markings, and they bloom during the spring and early summer. This orchid thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found growing on rocky slopes or in forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
