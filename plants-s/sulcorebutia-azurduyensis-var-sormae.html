Sulcorebutia azurduyensis var. sormae

Sulcorebutia azurduyensis var. sormae, commonly known as the Sorma's blue bulb, is a species of flowering plant native to the Andean region of South America.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular shape and blue-gray foliage. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is a slow-growing plant that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun exposure.
