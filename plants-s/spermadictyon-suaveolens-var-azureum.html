Spermadictyon suaveolens var. azureum

Spermadictyon suaveolens var. azureum, commonly known as the Azure Blue Trumpet.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical regions, characterized by its striking blue-violet flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and emits a sweet, fragrant aroma. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its unique coloration and floral structure make it a notable species in botanical gardens and horticultural collections.
