Satureja tenella

Satureja tenella, commonly known as narrowleaf mountain savory, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and small, pale purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in full sun. It is valued for its culinary and medicinal uses, particularly in traditional herbal remedies.

Satureja tenella is also noted for its hardy nature and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
