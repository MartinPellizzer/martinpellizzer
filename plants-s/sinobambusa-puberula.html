Sinobambusa puberula

Sinobambusa puberula, commonly known as the hairy bamboo, is a species of flowering bamboo native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems that are often covered in short, fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with tiny, greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Sinobambusa puberula is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and as a landscape plant.
