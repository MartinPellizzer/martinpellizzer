Senecio lessonii

Senecio lessonii, commonly known as the lesson's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late summer. It has hairy stems and leaves that are usually gray-green in color.

Senecio lessonii is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
