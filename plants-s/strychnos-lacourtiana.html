Strychnos lacourtiana

Strychnos lacourtiana, commonly known as the African strychnos, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Loganiaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. Due to its toxic properties, it is generally avoided for ornamental use but is of interest in botanical and ecological studies.
