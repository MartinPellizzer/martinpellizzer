Stelis aeolica

Stelis aeolica, commonly known as the Aeolian stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and green. The plant thrives in humid, tropical environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in a fine, hairy texture.

Stelis aeolica is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
