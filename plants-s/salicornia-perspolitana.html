Salicornia perspolitana

Salicornia perspolitana, commonly known as Persian glasswort, is a halophytic plant native to arid and saline environments.

It thrives in coastal and inland salt marshes, where it tolerates high soil salinity. The plant exhibits a succulent, cylindrical stem covered in minute, scale-like leaves. Its flowering stems produce small, greenish flowers that bloom in summer.

Salicornia perspolitana is valued for its nutritional content and is used in traditional cuisine and as a forage plant.
