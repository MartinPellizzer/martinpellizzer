Sisymbrium irio subsp. costei

Sisymbrium irio subsp.

costei, commonly known as the yellow rocket or winter cress, is a herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and basal leaves that are lobed or divided.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom from late winter to early spring. Its fruits are short, cylindrical pods that split open to release the seeds.

This species is often found in disturbed soils and is known for its rapid growth and ecological adaptability.
