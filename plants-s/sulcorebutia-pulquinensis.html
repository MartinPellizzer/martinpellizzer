Sulcorebutia pulquinensis

Sulcorebutia pulquinensis, commonly known as the Pachyveria, is a succulent plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular shape and thick, fleshy stems. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its stems and leaves.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
