Sonchus zollikoferioides

Sonchus zollikoferioides, commonly known as the narrowleaf sowthistle, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is an annual herb that typically grows in disturbed soils, such as roadsides and fields. The plant has alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and have a distinct yellowish-green color. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family.
