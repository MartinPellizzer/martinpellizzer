Smilax tsaii

Smilax tsaii, commonly known as the Chinese arrowroot or Chinese smilax, is a perennial vine native to eastern Asia.

It features a robust, woody stem with thorny nodes and produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant bears bright yellow berries that are typically eaten by birds and other wildlife. Its leaves are heart-shaped with a rough texture and often have a reddish or purple underside.

Smilax tsaii is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
