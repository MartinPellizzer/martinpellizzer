Senecio vernalis subsp. sosnowskyi

Senecio vernalis subsp.

The plant typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing adaptations to harsh climates. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by cylindrical seed heads.
