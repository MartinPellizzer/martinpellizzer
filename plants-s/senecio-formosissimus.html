Senecio formosissimus

Senecio formosissimus, commonly known as the Orange Sword or Orange Candelabra, is a striking member of the Asteraceae family native to South Africa.

It features a tall, upright stem with large, fleshy, orange-colored leaves that resemble a sword, giving it its common name. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers in dense, cylindrical clusters, which bloom during the summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and thrives in full sun with well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
