Sedum moranense var. arboreum

Sedum moranense var. arboreum, commonly known as the tree sedum, is a succulent plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and fleshy, cylindrical leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a landscape plant.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
