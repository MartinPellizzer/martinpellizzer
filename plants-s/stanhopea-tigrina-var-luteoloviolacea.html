Stanhopea tigrina var. luteoloviolacea

Stanhopea tigrina var. luteoloviolacea, commonly known as the Tigrina orchid, is a rare and exquisite orchid species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful flowers that display a striking combination of yellow, violet, and orange hues. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, typical of many orchid species. Its leaves are elongated and fleshy, adapted to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

This variety is highly valued by orchid enthusiasts for its unique floral display and challenging cultivation requirements.
