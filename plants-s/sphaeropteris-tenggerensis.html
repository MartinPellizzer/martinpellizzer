Sphaeropteris tenggerensis

Sphaeropteris tenggerensis, commonly known as the Tengger cycad, is a unique cycad species native to Indonesia.

It is characterized by its stout, unbranched trunk and a crown of large, feather-like leaves. The plant produces both male and female cones, which are essential for its reproductive process. Its leaves are typically a deep green and can grow up to several meters in length.

Sphaeropteris tenggerensis is adapted to thrive in tropical and subtropical climates with well-drained soil.
