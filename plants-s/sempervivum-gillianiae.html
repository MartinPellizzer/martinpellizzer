Sempervivum gillianiae

Sempervivum gillianiae, commonly known as the Gillian's houseleek, is a species of succulent plant native to the Pyrenees region.

It is characterized by its compact rosettes of fleshy, pointed leaves that are typically green with red or purple hues. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It is well adapted to rocky and alpine environments, thriving in full sun and well-drained soil.

Sempervivum gillianiae is a hardy perennial that is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in rock gardens and containers.
