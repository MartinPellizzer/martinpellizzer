Styrophyton caudatum

Styrophyton caudatum, commonly known as the long-tailed broom.

It is a flowering plant native to southern Africa, characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of yellow flowers. Styrophyton caudatum thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and shrublands.
