Sarocalamus faberi

Sarocalamus faberi, commonly known as the Chinese bamboo or Faberi's bamboo, is a species of grass belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.
