Sesuvium edule

Sesuvium edule, commonly known as edible sandplant, is a halophytic plant adapted to thrive in saline and coastal environments.

Sesuvium edule is native to regions such as southern Africa and the Arabian Peninsula, where it plays a role in stabilizing coastal soils.
