Syzygium yunnanense

Syzygium yunnanense, commonly known as the Yunnan apple or Chinese apple, is a small evergreen tree native to China.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces edible, fleshy berries that are typically red or orange when ripe. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fruit.

Syzygium yunnanense is also valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
