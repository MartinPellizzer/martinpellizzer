Scleranthus arrectifolius

Scleranthus arrectifolius, commonly known as the upright-leaved sand-plant, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

The plant has erect, branching stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, appearing in clusters at the ends of the branches.
