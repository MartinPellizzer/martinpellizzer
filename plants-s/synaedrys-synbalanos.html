Synaedrys synbalanos

Synaedrys synbalanos, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flower heads, which are surrounded by spiny, leaf-like bracts. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in maritime environments.
