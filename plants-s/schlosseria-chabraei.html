Schlosseria chabraei

Schlosseria chabraei, commonly known as the Chabraei's orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant blooms. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers a sunny to partially shaded environment. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to orange, with intricate patterns that attract pollinators.

Schlosseria chabraei is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by horticulturists.
