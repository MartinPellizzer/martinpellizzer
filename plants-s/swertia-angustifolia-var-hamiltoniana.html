Swertia angustifolia var. hamiltoniana

Swertia angustifolia var. hamiltoniana, commonly known as the narrow-leaf gentian, is a perennial herb native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

Its medicinal properties have been traditionally used in Ayurvedic practices for their purported health benefits.
