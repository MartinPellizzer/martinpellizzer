Selinum cryptotaenium

Selinum cryptotaenium, commonly known as the alpine tansy, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, fern-like leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in high-altitude environments, often growing in rocky or alpine soils. It is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly its use in traditional herbal remedies.

Selinum cryptotaenium is also noted for its ability to tolerate harsh climatic conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
