Sulcorebutia vargasii

Sulcorebutia vargasii, commonly known as the Vargasian crassula, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, covered in a fine layer of white wax. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
