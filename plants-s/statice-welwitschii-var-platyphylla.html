Statice welwitschii var. platyphylla

Statice welwitschii var. platyphylla, commonly known as the flatleaf statice, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces small, showy flowers in shades of pink, purple, and white. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

The species is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.
