Stemona phyllantha

Stemona phyllantha, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It features a rhizomatous stem system that allows it to spread and form dense colonies. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. Its flowers are small, bell-shaped, and usually white or pale blue in color.

The plant is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits and is also cultivated as an ornamental plant.
