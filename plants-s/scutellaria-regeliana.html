Scutellaria regeliana

Scutellaria regeliana, commonly known as Regel's skullcap, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or grassland habitats.

Scutellaria regeliana is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
