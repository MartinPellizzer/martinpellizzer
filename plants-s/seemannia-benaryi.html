Seemannia benaryi

Seemannia benaryi, commonly known as the Benary's star orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that typically range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant has a compact growth habit with fleshy, strap-like leaves that help it store water in its native arid environment. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
