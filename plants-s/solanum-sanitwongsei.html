Solanum sanitwongsei

Solanum sanitwongsei, commonly known as the Thai nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom at night, a characteristic that gives it its common name. Its leaves are typically ovate with a rough texture, and it grows as an erect shrub.

Solanum sanitwongsei is often found in forested areas and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.
