Senecio subrubriflorus

Senecio subrubriflorus, commonly known as the red-flowered senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features upright, branched stems covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are typically narrow and linear. Its most striking feature is the bright red to orange flowers that bloom in late summer.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
