Schefflera horsfieldii

Schefflera horsfieldii, commonly known as the Horsfield's umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of large, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by orange-red berries that are toxic to humans and animals. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated as a ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.

Schefflera horsfieldii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
