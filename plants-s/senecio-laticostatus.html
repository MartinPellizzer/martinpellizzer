Senecio laticostatus

Senecio laticostatus, commonly known as the broad-costate senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Senecio laticostatus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
