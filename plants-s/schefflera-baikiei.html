Schefflera baikiei

Schefflera baikiei, commonly known as the Chinese umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to southern China.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of large, leathery, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that are followed by bright red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Schefflera baikiei is relatively low maintenance and adaptable to various indoor growing conditions.
