Sebaea schimperiana

Sebaea schimperiana, commonly known as the African moonflower, is a climbing plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its milky sap and opposite leaves. Sebaea schimperiana is also valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens due to its unique floral display.
