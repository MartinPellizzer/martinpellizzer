Staurogyne Palawanensis

Staurogyne palawanensis is a rare aquatic plant native to the freshwater ecosystems of Palawan, Philippines. It is known for its unique morphology and ecological significance within its habitat. This species thrives in slow-moving waters and is often found in swampy or marshy areas. Its presence indicates a healthy aquatic environment and contributes to biodiversity. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a valuable subject for conservation and scientific study.
