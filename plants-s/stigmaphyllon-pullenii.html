Stigmaphyllon pullenii

Stigmaphyllon pullenii, commonly known as the Brazilian star cactus, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. Its stems are succulent, allowing it to store water, while its leaves are modified into spines.

The flowers are usually yellow or orange and bloom during the dry season.
