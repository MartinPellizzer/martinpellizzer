Scrophularia stelleriana

Scrophularia stelleriana, commonly known as Steller's figwort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae, which includes many species with showy, often tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly habitats and is characterized by its upright stems and pairs of opposite leaves. Its flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators.

Scrophularia stelleriana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
