Suaeda liaotungensis

Suaeda liaotungensis, commonly known as the Liaotung sea blite, is a halophytic plant native to coastal regions of northern China.

It thrives in saline and alkaline soils, often found in estuaries and tidal flats. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting to its arid and salty environment. Its small, greenish flowers are typically clustered in dense inflorescences.

Suaeda liaotungensis plays a role in stabilizing coastal soils and is of interest for its ecological and potential agricultural value.
