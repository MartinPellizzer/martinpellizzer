Silene jugora

Silene jugora, commonly known as the mountain catchfly, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, including countries like Kazakhstan and Kyrgyzstan. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with five petals that bloom in late summer.
