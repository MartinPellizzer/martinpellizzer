Stelis flexuosissima

Stelis flexuosissima, commonly known as the flexible stelis orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries such as Thailand and Malaysia. This epiphytic plant typically grows on trees or rocks, relying on atmospheric moisture rather than soil. Its slender, pendant stems are often covered in small, lance-shaped leaves.

The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
