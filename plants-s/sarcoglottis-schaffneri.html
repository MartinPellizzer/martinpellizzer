Sarcoglottis schaffneri

Sarcoglottis schaffneri, commonly known as the Schaffner's sarcoglottis, is a climbing herbaceous plant belonging to the family Gesneriaceae.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Native to tropical regions of South America, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouses.
