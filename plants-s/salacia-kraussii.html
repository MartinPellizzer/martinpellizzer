Salacia kraussii

Salacia kraussii, commonly known as the yellow salacia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Salicaceae, which includes willows and poplars, though it is not closely related to them. The plant is characterized by its attractive yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often in late spring or early summer. It typically grows in moist, wooded areas and can reach heights of up to 5 meters.

The leaves are simple, alternate, and have a smooth texture with a slightly waxy appearance.
