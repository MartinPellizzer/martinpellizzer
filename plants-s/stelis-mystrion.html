Stelis mystrion

Stelis mystrion, commonly known as the Mystrion orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. The plant features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with a single, upright stem and several lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered, often displaying a mix of white, yellow, and green hues.

Stelis mystrion is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
