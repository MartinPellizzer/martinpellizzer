Sedum hypericifolium

Sedum hypericifolium, commonly known as the ice plant or winter sedum, is a succulent plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ability to survive in harsh conditions makes it both hardy and low-maintenance.
