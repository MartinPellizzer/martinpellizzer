Sedum latifolium

Sedum latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its fleshy, broad leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy species suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Sedum latifolium is also valued for its ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions.
