Solanum scandens

Solanum scandens, commonly known as the creeping nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the nightshade family (Solanaceae).

It is a trailing or climbing vine native to tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in parts of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or purple, followed by berries that range in color from yellow to red. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and often have a hairy texture.

Solanum scandens is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and flowers.
