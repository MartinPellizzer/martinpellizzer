Sisymbrium strictissimum

Sisymbrium strictissimum, commonly known as hoary salsify, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant is characterized by its tall, erect stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a distinctive silvery appearance.
