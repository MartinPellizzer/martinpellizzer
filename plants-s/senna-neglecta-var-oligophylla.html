Senna neglecta var. oligophylla

Senna neglecta var. oligophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaved senna, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, pinnate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, ribbed pods containing seeds with a distinctive blackish-brown color. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
