Stachys keerlii

Stachys keerlii, commonly known as the Keerli's hedge nettle, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features upright stems covered with fine hairs and produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a rough texture, often with serrated edges.

Stachys keerlii is adapted to rocky and well-drained soils, thriving in temperate to cold climates.
