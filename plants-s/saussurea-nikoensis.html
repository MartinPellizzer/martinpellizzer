Saussurea nikoensis

Saussurea nikoensis, commonly known as the Niko saffron.

It is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas, particularly in regions of Nepal and northern India. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stem and dense clusters of yellow flowers that resemble saffron threads. It thrives in cold, arid environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
