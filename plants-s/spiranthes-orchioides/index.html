Spiranthes Orchioides

Spiranthes orchioides, also known as the narrow-leaved ladies' tresses, is a delicate orchid native to the eastern united states. this plant is characterized by its spiraling, grass-like inflorescence that resembles a twisted ribbon. it thrives in moist, shaded environments such as wet meadows and along stream banks. spiranthes orchioides plays an important role in pollination and is a favorite among native pollinators. its unique appearance and ecological significance make it a notable species in the orchid family.
