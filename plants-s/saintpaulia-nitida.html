Saintpaulia nitida

Saintpaulia nitida, commonly known as the African violet, is a species of flowering plant native to Tanzania.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and velvety, dark green foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Saintpaulia nitida is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
