Senecio × eversii

Senecio × eversii, commonly known as the Ever's groundsel, is a hybrid plant native to the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid environments. Its foliage is usually gray-green, with rough, hairy surfaces that help reduce water loss.

Senecio × eversii exhibits a compact growth habit and produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.
