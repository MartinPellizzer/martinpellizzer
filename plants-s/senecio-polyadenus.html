Senecio polyadenus

Senecio polyadenus, commonly known as the woolly goldenrod, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-green foliage and clusters of small, bright yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its showy inflorescences. Senecio polyadenus thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in suitable climates.
