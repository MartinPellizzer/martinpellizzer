Sanchezia pennellii

Sanchezia pennellii, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a member of the Araceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its unique, zigzagging stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. It thrives in humid, warm environments and is often grown as a decorative houseplant.

Sanchezia pennellii is valued for its attractive foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
