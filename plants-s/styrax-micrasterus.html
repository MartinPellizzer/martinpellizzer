Styrax micrasterus

Styrax micrasterus, commonly known as the small-flowered styrax, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia, particularly China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds. Its leaves are elliptical, with a glossy surface and a serrated margin.

Styrax micrasterus is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and adaptability to various soil types.
