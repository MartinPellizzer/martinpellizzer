Stelis aspergilliformis

Stelis aspergilliformis, commonly known as the rough-hairy stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and hairy, often reddish stems. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in racemes. Its leaves are typically arranged in two rows along the stem and are covered with a fine, pubescent layer.

Stelis aspergilliformis thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.
