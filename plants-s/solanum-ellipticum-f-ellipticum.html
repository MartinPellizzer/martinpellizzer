Solanum ellipticum f. ellipticum

Solanum ellipticum f. ellipticum, commonly known as the elliptic nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and peppers. The plant features elliptical, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its small, white flowers bloom in clusters and are followed by berries that change from green to red as they mature.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and unique flowering habit.
