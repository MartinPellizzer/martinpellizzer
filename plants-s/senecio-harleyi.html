Senecio harleyi

Senecio harleyi, commonly known as the Harley's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Senecio harleyi is generally drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a hardy plant suited to xeriscaping.
