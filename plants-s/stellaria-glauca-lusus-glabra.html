Stellaria glauca lusus glabra

Stellaria glauca lusus glabra, commonly known as white starwort, is a low-growing annual herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to August. The plant has thin, branched stems and produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are arranged in clusters, and it is often found in disturbed soils such as roadsides and fields.

The plant is known for its delicate appearance and is sometimes used in floral arrangements.
