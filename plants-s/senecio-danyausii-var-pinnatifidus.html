Senecio danyausii var. pinnatifidus

Senecio danyausii var. pinnatifidus, commonly known as the Pinnatifid Senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small yellow flowers in clusters.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is often cultivated in rock gardens or as an ornamental plant.
