Succisa inflexa

Succisa inflexa, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves and clusters of showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is well adapted to humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, which helps it store water, making it a succulent.

Succisa inflexa is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
