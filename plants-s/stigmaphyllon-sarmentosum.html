Stigmaphyllon sarmentosum

Stigmaphyllon sarmentosum, commonly known as the creeping stigmaphyllon, is a climbing shrub native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its trailing stems and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.
