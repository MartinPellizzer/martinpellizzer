Senecio brachyanthus

Senecio brachyanthus, commonly known as the "blue ice" or "blue snow" plant, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its striking blue-gray foliage and clusters of small, star-shaped white flowers. The plant has a compact, cushion-like growth form, which helps it retain moisture in its arid native habitat. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a desirable ornamental plant.
