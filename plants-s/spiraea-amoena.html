Spiraea amoena

Spiraea amoena, commonly known as the meadow spiraea, is a deciduous shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, pink to white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically reaches heights of 1 to 3 meters and has finely cut, serrated leaves that are dark green in color. Spiraea amoena thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers moist, well-drained soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
