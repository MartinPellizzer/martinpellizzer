Serjania micrantha

Serjania micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered serjania, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Serjaniae, which includes several species of shrubs and trees. The plant is characterized by its small, white flowers that are often clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are typically alternate and ovate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.
