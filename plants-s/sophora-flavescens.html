Sophora flavescens

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellowish flowers. The plant is notable for its roots, which contain bioactive compounds such as oxymatrine and sophoracoside. These compounds have been studied for their potential medicinal properties, including antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory effects.
