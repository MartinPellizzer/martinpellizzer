Schefflera comptonii

Schefflera comptonii, commonly known as the Compton's umbrella plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of leaflets, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant has compound leaves with numerous small, oval-shaped leaflets arranged in a whorl at the end of each stem. It produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by red berries.

Schefflera comptonii is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its lush foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
