Stipa brachychaeta

Stipa brachychaeta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved needlegrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and fine, needle-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Stipa brachychaeta is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in stabilizing soil in arid environments.
