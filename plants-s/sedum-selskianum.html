Sedum selskianum

Sedum selskianum, commonly known as the Selskian stonecrop, is a perennial succulent native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, clustered leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
