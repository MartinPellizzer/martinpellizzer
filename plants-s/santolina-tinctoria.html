Santolina tinctoria

Santolina tinctoria, commonly known as Spanish santolina, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, silver-gray foliage with a strong, aromatic scent. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in late spring to early summer. Its compact growth habit makes it ideal for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Santolina tinctoria is also valued for its use in herbal medicine and as a source of natural dye.
