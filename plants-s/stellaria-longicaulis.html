Stellaria longicaulis

Stellaria longicaulis, commonly known as the long-stemmed starwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, ascending stems and small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, sandy soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, narrow, and slightly hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Stellaria longicaulis is a perennial herb that plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
