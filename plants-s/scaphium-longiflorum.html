Scaphium longiflorum

Scaphium longiflorum, commonly known as the long-flowered scaphium, is a striking member of the Araceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its large, showy inflorescence. The plant features broad, arrow-shaped leaves that are often bright green with a waxy texture. Its flowers are clustered in a spadix surrounded by a spathe, typical of the Araceae family.

Scaphium longiflorum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in warm climates.
