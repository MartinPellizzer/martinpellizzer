Stachys recta subsp. anisochila

Stachys recta subsp.

anisochila, commonly known as narrowleaf betony, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features upright, branched stems covered with soft, hairy foliage.

The leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly serrated edge, while the flowers are small, tubular, and typically violet or blue in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or borders.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
