Stellaria atrata var. eciliata

Stellaria atrata var. eciliata, commonly known as the blackstar starwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a bluish-green hue.

It is a perennial herb that thrives in temperate climates and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
