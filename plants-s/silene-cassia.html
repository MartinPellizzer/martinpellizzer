Silene cassia

Silene cassia, commonly known as the scarlet silene, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red or pink flowers that bloom in dense clusters. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry conditions.

Silene cassia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
