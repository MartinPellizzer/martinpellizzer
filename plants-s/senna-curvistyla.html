Senna curvistyla

Senna curvistyla, commonly known as the slender senna, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features bipinnate leaves with small, oval leaflets and produces yellow flowers in clusters. It is often found in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Senna curvistyla is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
