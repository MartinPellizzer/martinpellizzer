Senecio degiensis

Senecio degiensis, commonly known as the Degien's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It produces small yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
