Selago swynnertonii var. swynnertonii

Selago swynnertonii var. swynnertonii, commonly known as the Swynnerton's selago, is a low-growing, succulent plant native to the arid regions of South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in dry conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, adding a striking visual contrast to its green foliage. Its leaves are narrow and pointed, often covered in a fine, powdery coating that helps reduce water loss.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
