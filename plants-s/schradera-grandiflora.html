Schradera grandiflora

Schradera grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered schradera, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically bright yellow or orange. The plant has smooth, green, and sometimes hairy leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in moist forest understories.

Schradera grandiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
