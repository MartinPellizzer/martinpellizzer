Solanum cornigerum

Solanum cornigerum, commonly known as the red-berry nightshade, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, white or pale green flowers that are followed by bright red berries. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and have a rough texture.

The berries are toxic to humans and animals, making the plant a potential hazard in gardens.
