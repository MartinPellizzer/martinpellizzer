Silene portensis subsp. rigidula

Silene portensis subsp.

rigidula, commonly known as the rigid leaf catchfly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its upright, hairy stems and small, pinkish-purple flowers that bloom in late spring.

The subspecies is distinguished by its rigid, linear leaves and compact growth habit. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal and inland habitats.

This plant is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and limited distribution.
