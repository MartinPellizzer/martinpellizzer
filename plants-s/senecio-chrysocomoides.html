Senecio chrysocomoides

Senecio chrysocomoides, commonly known as the yellow-flowered senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces bright yellow ray florets that are highly ornamental. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its striking appearance, it is often cultivated in horticulture for its aesthetic value.
