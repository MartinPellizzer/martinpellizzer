Sideroxylon littorale

Sideroxylon littorale, commonly known as the coastal ironwood, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to coastal regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, hard wood and its ability to thrive in saline and sandy soils. Sideroxylon littorale is often found in mangrove and coastal forest ecosystems, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various species.
