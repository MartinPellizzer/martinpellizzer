Statice plantaginea subsp. choulettiana

Statice plantaginea subsp.

choulettiana, commonly known as the Choulett's statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit.

The plant produces dense clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, which bloom in summer. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

This subspecies is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
