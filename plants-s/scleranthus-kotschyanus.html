Scleranthus kotschyanus

Scleranthus kotschyanus, commonly known as the Kotschyan's thornweed, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is a herbaceous annual with slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant has hairy leaves and produces small, dry fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in disturbed soils.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies of desert flora.
