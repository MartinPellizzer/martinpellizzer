Sedum cavaleriense

Sedum cavaleriense, commonly known as the Cape Hottentot, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its resilience and ornamental value make it a favored plant among succulent enthusiasts.
