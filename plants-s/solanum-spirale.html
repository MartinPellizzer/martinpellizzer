Solanum spirale

Solanum spirale, commonly known as the spiral nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, violet-colored flowers that bloom in clusters. It is a herbaceous perennial that thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Solanum spirale belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
