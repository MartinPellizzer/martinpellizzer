Scolopendrium Cordatum

Scolopendrium cordatum is a unique and rare fern native to the eastern United States. Known for its distinctive heart-shaped fronds, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments. It is often found in rocky woodlands and stream banks where it contributes to the biodiversity of its habitat. Despite its delicate appearance, Scolopendrium cordatum is highly resilient and adaptable to various soil conditions. This fascinating plant is a valuable addition to both natural ecosystems and botanical gardens.
