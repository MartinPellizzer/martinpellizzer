Sudamerlycaste reichenbachii var. viridis

Sudamerlycaste reichenbachii var. viridis, commonly known as the green leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its lush, green foliage and delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as tropical forests. Its botanical features include strap-like leaves and small, clustered blooms in shades of white and green.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
