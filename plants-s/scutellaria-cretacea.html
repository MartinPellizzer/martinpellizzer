Scutellaria cretacea

Scutellaria cretacea, commonly known as the Mediterranean skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Scutellaria cretacea is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
