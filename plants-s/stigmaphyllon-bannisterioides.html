Stigmaphyllon bannisterioides

Stigmaphyllon bannisterioides, commonly known as the Bannister's stigmaphyllon, is a flowering plant native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

The plant has a compact growth habit and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
