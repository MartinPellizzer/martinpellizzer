Solanum nigrum f. luridum

Solanum nigrum f. luridum, commonly known as the yellow nightshade, is a variant of the common black nightshade.

It is a herbaceous annual plant native to Europe, western Asia, and North Africa. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to lanceolate in shape. Its flowers are typically yellow or yellowish-white, with five petals, and it produces small, spherical berries that change from green to red as they mature.

This form is notable for its distinct coloration and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
