Sericotheca schaffneri

It is characterized by its distinctive, velvety, pod-like fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant is native to tropical regions of Africa and thrives in humid, warm climates. Its leaves are trifoliate, and it produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters.

The botanical features of Sericotheca schaffneri make it an important species in both ecological and medicinal contexts.
