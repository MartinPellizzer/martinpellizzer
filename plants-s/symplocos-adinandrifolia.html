Symplocos adinandrifolia

Symplocos adinandrifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf evergreen shrub, is a member of the Ericaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features small, elliptical leaves with a smooth upper surface and a hairy underside. Its flowers are white or pale pink, clustered in inflorescences, and it produces small, fleshy berries.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
