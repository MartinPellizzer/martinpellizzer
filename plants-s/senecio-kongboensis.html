Senecio kongboensis

Senecio kongboensis, commonly known as the Kongbo Groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Tibet.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are generally narrow and covered in a fine layer of white hairs, which help reduce water loss.

The flowers, which appear in late summer, are yellow and attract various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
