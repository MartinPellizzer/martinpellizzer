Stelis embreei

Stelis embreei, commonly known as the Embree's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, clustered growth habit. The plant typically produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, often with intricate patterns on the petals. Stelis embreei thrives in shaded, moist environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its unique floral structure and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
