Secale cereale var. vernum

Secale cereale var. vernum, commonly known as spring ryegrass, is a perennial grass species native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tinge. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, pastures, and along stream banks.
