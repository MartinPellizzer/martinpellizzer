Senecio pallens var. khasianus

Senecio pallens var. khasianus, commonly known as the Khasi Senecio, is a flowering plant native to the Eastern Himalayas.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes. Its leaves are generally lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disc.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
