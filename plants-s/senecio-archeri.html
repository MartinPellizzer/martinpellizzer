Senecio archeri

Senecio archeri, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rosette growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically features velvety, dark green leaves and produces vibrant purple or blue flowers that bloom in spring. It is a popular ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.

Senecio archeri thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.
