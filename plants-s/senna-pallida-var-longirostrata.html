Senna pallida var. longirostrata

Senna pallida var. longirostrata, commonly known as the long-beaked senna, is a shrub native to parts of Africa and the Arabian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

Senna pallida var. longirostrata is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some regions.
