Steiropteris densisora

Steiropteris densisora, commonly known as the dense-veined tree fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a unique rhizome system that allows it to anchor itself in moist, shaded environments. Its fronds are typically dark green with prominent, dense veins that give it its common name.

Steiropteris densisora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
