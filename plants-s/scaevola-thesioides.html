Scaevola thesioides

Scaevola thesioides, commonly known as the silver cotyledon, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that features dense, silver-gray foliage with a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, showy blue or purple flowers that bloom throughout the year. It is well adapted to arid and coastal environments, making it drought-tolerant and suitable for xeriscaping.

Scaevola thesioides is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
