Strychnos dekindtiana

Strychnos dekindtiana, commonly known as the African strychnos, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows in dry, rocky soils. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by orange-red berries containing seeds. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture.

The species is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and fruit.
