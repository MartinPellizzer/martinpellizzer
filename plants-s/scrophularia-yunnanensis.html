Scrophularia yunnanensis

Scrophularia yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan scrophularia, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae and is characterized by its upright stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces clusters of tubular flowers, typically in shades of purple or pink, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested or rocky habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and traditional medicinal uses, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and herbal practices.
