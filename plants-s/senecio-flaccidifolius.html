Senecio flaccidifolius

Senecio flaccidifolius, commonly known as the flaccid-leaf senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.
