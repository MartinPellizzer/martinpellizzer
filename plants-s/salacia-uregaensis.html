Salacia uregaensis

Salacia uregaensis, commonly known as the yellow salacia, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Salaciacae and is characterized by its climbing habit and vibrant yellow flowers. The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are usually opposite and ovate, with a glossy surface.

Salacia uregaensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
