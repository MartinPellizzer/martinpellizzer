Splitgerbera

Splitgerbera, commonly known as the African daisy, is a striking member of the Asteraceae family.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its vibrant, daisy-like flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features a sturdy, upright stem with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, fuzzy texture. Its flowers are typically arranged in a single, showy head, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Splitgerbera is known for its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in full sun, making it a hardy and low-maintenance plant.
