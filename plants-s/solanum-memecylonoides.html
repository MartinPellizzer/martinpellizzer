Solanum memecylonoides

Solanum memecylonoides, commonly known as the Indian nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its erect or prostrate growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into berries, which are typically green when immature and turn red when ripe. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.

Due to its toxic compounds, it is important to handle the plant with care.
