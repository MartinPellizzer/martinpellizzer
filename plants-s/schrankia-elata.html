Schrankia elata

Schrankia elata, commonly known as the tall sand mat, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of North America.

It is characterized by its low-growing, sprawling habit with fleshy, succulent stems and leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Schrankia elata thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to withstand drought and high temperatures.

It is often found in desert scrub and grassland habitats.
