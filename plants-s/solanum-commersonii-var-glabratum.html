Solanum commersonii var. glabratum

Solanum commersonii var. glabratum, commonly known as the Commerson's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial with hairy stems and alternate, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to violet, typically blooming in the evening. Its fruit is a fleshy berry that turns red when mature, containing several seeds.

This species is often found in dry, disturbed habitats and is adapted to arid environments.
