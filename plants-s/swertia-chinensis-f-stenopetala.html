Swertia chinensis f. stenopetala

Swertia chinensis f. stenopetala, commonly known as the narrow-petaled swertia, is a perennial herb native to the Himalayas and parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers during the summer months, which are typically tubular in shape. Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and traditional herbal practices.
