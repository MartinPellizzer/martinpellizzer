Solanum chrysobotrys

Solanum chrysobotrys, commonly known as the golden star solanum, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its showy, yellow star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, while its fruits are small, yellow berries that mature in late summer.

Solanum chrysobotrys is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its vibrant floral display.
