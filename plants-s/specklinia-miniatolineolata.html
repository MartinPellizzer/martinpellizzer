Specklinia miniatolineolata

Specklinia miniatolineolata, commonly known as the miniature line-leafed orchid, is a compact and elegant epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, often arranged in clusters along the stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media to prevent root rot.

Due to its rarity and specific care requirements, it is a prized specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
