Senecio hieraciifolius var. carduifolius

Senecio hieraciifolius var. carduifolius, commonly known as the "Hairy Groundsel," is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, lobed leaves and bright yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in grasslands and along rocky outcrops.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ornamental species in its native habitat.
