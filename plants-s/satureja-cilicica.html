Satureja cilicica

Satureja cilicica, commonly known as Cilician thyme, is a perennial herb native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic leaves and square stems. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and is often found in mountainous areas. Its small, tubular flowers are usually pink or purple, and it produces small, oval-shaped fruits.

Satureja cilicica is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
