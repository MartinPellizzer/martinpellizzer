Statice × coincyi

Statice × coincyi, commonly known as sea lavender, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and showy, blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in saline and well-drained soils, making it well adapted to coastal environments. Its narrow, grass-like leaves are typically gray-green in color, and it is often used in coastal landscaping for its hardiness and ornamental value.

Statice × coincyi is also popular in floral arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms.
