Sedum coeruleum

Sedum coeruleum, commonly known as blue sedum, is a succulent plant native to North America.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, allowing it to store water efficiently. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of blue and purple during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it an ideal plant for arid environments.
