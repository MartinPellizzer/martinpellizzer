Specklinia paranaensis

Specklinia paranaensis, commonly known as the Parana orchid, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant features pseudobulbs that support its evergreen leaves, which are typically narrow and leathery. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and often appear in clusters, showcasing a range of colors from white to pale pink.

Specklinia paranaensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions.
