Stanleya pinnata var. typica

Stanleya pinnata var. typica, commonly known as the showy lady's slipper, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and meadows. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with elongated, slender leaflets.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
