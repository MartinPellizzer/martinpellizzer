Salacia obovata

Salacia obovata, commonly known as the broad-leaf salacia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Salaciacae and is characterized by its obovate leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical rainforests and thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its fruit is edible and has a tart, citrus-like flavor, making it a valued species in local cuisines.

Salacia obovata is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
