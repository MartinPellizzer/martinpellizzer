Scrophularia macrorrhyncha

Scrophularia macrorrhyncha, commonly known as the large-flowered figwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in woodland edges, meadows, and along stream banks. The plant features square, hairy stems and opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. Its showy, tubular flowers, which range in color from pink to purple, bloom in late spring to early summer.

Scrophularia macrorrhyncha is part of the Scrophulariaceae family, known for its medicinal and ornamental value.
