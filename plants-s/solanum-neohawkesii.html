Solanum neohawkesii

Solanum neohawkesii, commonly known as the Neohawkes's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains in South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, white or pale green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are berries that change from green to red as they mature.

The species is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which have a rough texture.
