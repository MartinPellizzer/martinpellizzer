Stelis kroemeri

Stelis kroemeri, commonly known as the Kroemeri's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are narrow and strap-like, and it prefers bright, indirect light with high humidity.

Stelis kroemeri is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
