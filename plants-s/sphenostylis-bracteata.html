Sphenostylis bracteata

Sphenostylis bracteata, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, often forming a low, bushy shrub. Its flowers are borne on long, slender stalks and are known for their vibrant colors and long-lasting blooms.

Sphenostylis bracteata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
