Sebestena glomerata

Sebestena glomerata, commonly known as the clustered sebestena, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides. The plant features a basal rosette of lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its small, clustered flowers are usually white or pale yellow and appear in late summer.

Sebestena glomerata is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
