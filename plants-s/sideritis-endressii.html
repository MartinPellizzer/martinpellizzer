Sideritis endressii

Sideritis endressii, commonly known as the Endress's ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its slender stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that range in color from pale blue to violet. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in high-altitude environments.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
