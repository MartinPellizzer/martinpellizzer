Stelis discrepans

Stelis discrepans, commonly known as the Discrepant Stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It thrives in humid, shaded forest environments, often growing on tree trunks or branches. The plant features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically small, fragrant, and come in shades of white and green, arranged in a dense inflorescence.

Stelis discrepans is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
