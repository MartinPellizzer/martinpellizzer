Spiranthes × stellata subsp. perexilis

Spiranthes × stellata subsp.

perexilis, commonly known as the slender spirea, is a species of orchid native to North America. It is characterized by its delicate, spiraling inflorescences that consist of small, white to pale yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wet meadows. It has linear, grass-like leaves and produces seeds in capsules.

This subspecies is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
