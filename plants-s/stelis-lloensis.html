Stelis lloensis

Stelis lloensis, commonly known as the Lloens Lattice Orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andean region of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments at high elevations. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with a short rhizome and several vertical pseudobulbs. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pink and purple.

Stelis lloensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
