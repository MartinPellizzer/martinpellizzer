Scaevola hainanensis

Scaevola hainanensis, commonly known as the Hainan sandveld, is a flowering plant native to the island of Hainan in China.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its showy, bilaterally symmetrical flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant is shrub-like, with narrow, linear leaves and produces small, fluffy seed capsules that aid in wind dispersal.
