Sorbus prattii var. aestivalis

Sorbus prattii var. aestivalis, commonly known as the early whitebeams, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its white to pale yellow flowers that appear in early spring, typically before the leaves emerge. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits known as pomes, which are typically red or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in its native habitat.
