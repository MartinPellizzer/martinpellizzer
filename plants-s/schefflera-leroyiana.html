Schefflera leroyiana

Schefflera leroyiana, commonly known as the African schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of leaflets, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by orange-red berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Schefflera leroyiana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its lush foliage and adaptability to indoor environments.
