Symbolanthus argyreus

Symbolanthus argyreus, commonly known as the silverleaf morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its striking white to pale blue flowers that open in the early morning. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it its common name. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Symbolanthus argyreus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can be found in gardens and natural habitats.
