Stapelia stellaris

Stapelia stellaris, commonly known as the star stapelia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed stems and its distinctive star-shaped flowers. The flowers are typically yellow or orange with a waxy texture and emit a strong, unpleasant odor to attract pollinators. The plant is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
