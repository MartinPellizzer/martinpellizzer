Solanum bigeminatum var. zeylanicum

Solanum bigeminatum var. zeylanicum, commonly known as the Zeylanic nightshade, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant produces berries that are typically green when immature and turn red when ripe. It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its toxic compounds, it is important to handle the plant with care.
