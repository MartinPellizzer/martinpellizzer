Sedum Rosea Var. Roanense

Rosy stonecrop, scientifically known as Sedum rosea var. roanense, is a striking succulent native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe. With its vibrant pink to rose-colored foliage, it stands out among other sedums for its unique and eye-catching appearance. This variety thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers. Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it an ideal plant for low-maintenance landscaping. Whether grown for its ornamental value or its resilience, Rosy stonecrop is a favorite among gardeners and plant enthusiasts alike.
