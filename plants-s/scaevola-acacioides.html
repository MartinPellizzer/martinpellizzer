Scaevola acacioides

Scaevola acacioides, commonly known as the coastal morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils. The plant produces showy, bilaterally symmetrical flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Scaevola acacioides is well adapted to arid and saline environments, making it a resilient species in coastal ecosystems.
