Scutellaria elegans

Scutellaria elegans, commonly known as the elegant skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and blooms from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs, while its flowers range in color from blue to purple.

Scutellaria elegans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
