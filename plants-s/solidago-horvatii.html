Solidago horvatii

Solidago horvatii, commonly known as Horvat's goldenrod, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands. Horvat's goldenrod is known for its ecological importance in supporting local wildlife and its adaptability to arid environments.
