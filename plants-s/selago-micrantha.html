Selago micrantha

Selago micrantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved selago, is a flowering plant native to parts of Australia.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae, which includes eucalyptus and other aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in a fine, silvery or grayish pubescence.

Selago micrantha typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal or inland heathlands.
