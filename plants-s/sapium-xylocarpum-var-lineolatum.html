Sapium xylocarpum var. lineolatum

Sapium xylocarpum var. lineolatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf Chinese tallowtree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It features alternate, elliptic to ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly serrated margin. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in clusters, followed by orange to red berries that contain several seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been introduced to various parts of the world.

However, it can be invasive in some regions, outcompeting native vegetation.
