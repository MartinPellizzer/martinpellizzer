Stylochaeton tubulosum

Stylochaeton tubulosum, commonly known as the slender legume, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant has slender, cylindrical stems and small, trifoliate leaves. Its flowers are typically pink or purple, arranged in racemes, and it produces elongated pods containing several seeds.

Stylochaeton tubulosum is often found in grasslands and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils.
