Selenothamnus squamatus

Selenothamnus squamatus, commonly known as the silverleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray, fuzzy leaves and yellow composite flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is well adapted to arid conditions. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
