Schefflera gracilipes

Schefflera gracilipes, commonly known as the slender umbrella plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant features compound leaves with multiple leaflets radiating from a central stem, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by red berries. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated as a ornamental plant.

Its adaptability makes it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation in suitable environments.
