Sasa arakii

Sasa arakii, commonly known as Japanese bamboo or black bamboo, is a perennial grass native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 3 meters. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are usually a deep green color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Sasa arakii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional Japanese crafts.
