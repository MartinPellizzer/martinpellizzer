Statice Oleifolia

Statice oleifolia is a striking flowering plant known for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms that range from pink to purple. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in coastal and sandy soils where it withstands salty winds and harsh conditions. This plant is often used in floral arrangements due to its ability to retain color and form for extended periods. Its slender stems and delicate flowers make it a popular choice among gardeners and florists alike. With its hardy nature and aesthetic appeal, Statice oleifolia is a valuable addition to any landscape or bouquet.
