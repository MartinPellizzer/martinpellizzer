Sphaerostigma bistorta

Sphaerostigma bistorta, commonly known as broadleaf bistort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features a basal rosette of lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive purple-tinged underside. Its flowering stems bear small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color.

The plant is characterized by its unique, swollen base and is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value.
