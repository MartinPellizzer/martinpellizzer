Searsia dumetorum

Searsia dumetorum, commonly known as the silver tree, is a shrub or small tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray bark and small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to coastal and inland regions with a Mediterranean climate.

Searsia dumetorum is often used in landscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appearance.
