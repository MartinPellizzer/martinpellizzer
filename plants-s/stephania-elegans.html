Stephania elegans

Stephania elegans, commonly known as the elegant stephania, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, particularly China and Japan.

It belongs to the Menispermaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by distinctive, winged fruits. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Stephania elegans is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its ecological role in its native habitats.
