Sutera cordata

Sutera cordata, commonly known as the heartleaf suture or heartleaf suture plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and the Americas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. Sutera cordata thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

It is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its use in traditional medicine.
