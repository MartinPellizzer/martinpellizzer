Saxifraga jotanii

Saxifraga jotanii, commonly known as the Tibetan saxifrage, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, arid environments. Its flowers, which range in color from white to pink, bloom in late summer.

Saxifraga jotanii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in alpine gardens.
