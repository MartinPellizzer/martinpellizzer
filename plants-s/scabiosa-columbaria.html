Scabiosa columbaria

Scabiosa columbaria, commonly known as the dove's foot or bird's foot scabious, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, branching stems and clusters of small, delicate blue or purple flowers. The leaves are typically lobed and resemble the shape of a bird's foot, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in meadows and grasslands.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
