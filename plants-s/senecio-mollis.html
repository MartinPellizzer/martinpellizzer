Senecio mollis

Senecio mollis, commonly known as the softleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves and yellow composite flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. It has a hairy stem and produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Senecio mollis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and easy care requirements.
