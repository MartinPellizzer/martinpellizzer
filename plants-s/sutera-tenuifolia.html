Sutera tenuifolia

Sutera tenuifolia, commonly known as slenderleaf mallow, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, pale pink to white flowers in the summer months. Its botanical name reflects its slender leaves and the mallow family to which it belongs.

Sutera tenuifolia is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
