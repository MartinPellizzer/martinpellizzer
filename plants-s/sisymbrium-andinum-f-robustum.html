Sisymbrium andinum f. robustum

Sisymbrium andinum f. robustum, commonly known as the Andean rocket, is a perennial herb native to the Andes region of South America.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features pinnately compound leaves with serrated edges, and its stems are often hairy. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some South American countries.
