Silene maheshwarii

Silene maheshwarii, commonly known as the Maheshwari campion, is a flowering plant native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its pink to purple flowers and linear leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid environments. Silene maheshwarii is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
