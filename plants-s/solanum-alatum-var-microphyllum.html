Solanum alatum var. microphyllum

Solanum alatum var. microphyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It features slender, branching stems covered in fine, glandular hairs. The leaves are typically narrow and ovate, with a slightly pubescent surface. Its flowers are small, star-shaped, and usually white or pale green, blooming in clusters.

The plant produces small, round berries that are typically yellow or red when mature.
