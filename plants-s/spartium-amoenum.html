Spartium amoenum

Spartium amoenum, commonly known as lesser sparrowgrass, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and open woodlands. The plant has slender, branching stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are pink to purple in color and are arranged in dense, spike-like clusters.

Spartium amoenum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
