Solanum jasminoides argentia

Solanum jasminoides argentia, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking silver-white variegated leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in the evening. The plant belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades such as tomatoes and peppers. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and unique blooming pattern.

However, it contains toxic alkaloids and should be handled with care.
