Senecio kaempferi

Senecio kaempferi, commonly known as the Blue Echeveria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, which are clustered in inflorescences. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in rock gardens.
