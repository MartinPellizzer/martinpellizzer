Sabicea colombiana

Sabicea colombiana, commonly known as the Colombian sabicea, is a flowering plant native to the Andes region of South America.

Sabicea colombiana is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as cloud forests, where it thrives in the cool, humid climate.
