Sullivania brockmanii

Sullivania brockmanii, commonly known as the rock daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit. The plant produces showy, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center, blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its ornamental value and adaptability make it a popular choice among gardeners.
