Salvia nilotica

Salvia nilotica, commonly known as blue sage, is a perennial shrub native to Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers and silver-gray foliage. The plant belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and medicinal plants. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
