Stellaria hypericifolia

Stellaria hypericifolia, commonly known as common chickweed, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It features small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming from spring to early summer. The plant has slender, branched stems covered with fine hairs and produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are often hairy as well. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in gardens, lawns, and along woodland paths.

Stellaria hypericifolia is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and runners.
