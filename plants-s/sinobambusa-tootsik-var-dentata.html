Sinobambusa tootsik var. dentata

Sinobambusa tootsik var. dentata, commonly known as the toothed bamboo, is a variant of the Sinobambusa tootsik species.

It is characterized by its distinctive serrated leaf margins, which give it its common name. This bamboo is native to China and is often found in mountainous regions with well-drained soils. The plant exhibits a clump-forming growth habit, producing tall, slender culms that are often used in ornamental horticulture.

Its foliage is typically a vibrant green, adding aesthetic value to gardens and landscapes.
