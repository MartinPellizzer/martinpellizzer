Sideritis cilicica

Sideritis cilicica, commonly known as Cilician ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region, particularly Turkey and Cyprus.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, pale blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Sideritis cilicica is often found in rocky or dry habitats and is valued for its medicinal properties in traditional remedies.
