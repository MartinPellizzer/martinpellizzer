Statice welwitschii

Statice welwitschii, commonly known as the desert star or welwitsch's statice, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, spiky flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves that help reduce water loss.

Statice welwitschii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
