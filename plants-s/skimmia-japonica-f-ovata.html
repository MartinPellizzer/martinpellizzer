Skimmia japonica f. ovata

Skimmia japonica f. ovata, commonly known as the oval-leaf Japanese skimmia, is a compact evergreen shrub characterized by its glossy, ovate-shaped leaves.

It produces clusters of small, fragrant, white flowers in early spring, which are followed by red berries. The plant is native to Japan and thrives in partial shade with well-drained soil. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in woodland gardens.

Its foliage remains evergreen throughout the year, providing visual interest in the garden.
