Schizostachyum helferi

Schizostachyum helferi, commonly known as the Helfer's bamboo, is a species of grass belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Indonesia and the Philippines. This plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional basket weaving.

Schizostachyum helferi thrives in tropical climates and requires well-drained soil and ample sunlight.
