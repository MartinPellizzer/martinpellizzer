Solanum floccosistellatum

Solanum floccosistellatum, commonly known as the starry nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial native to South America.

It features small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in shades of white and purple. The plant has hairy, glandular leaves that are often covered in a fine, cotton-like bloom. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

The fruits are small berries that turn from green to yellow or red as they mature.
