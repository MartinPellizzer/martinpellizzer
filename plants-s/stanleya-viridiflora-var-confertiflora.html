Stanleya viridiflora var. confertiflora

Stanleya viridiflora var. confertiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered green-flowered larkspur, is a herbaceous perennial native to the western United States.

It features tall, slender stems that bear clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant has alternate, lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and a distinct, bitter taste. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species for ecological studies and botanical classification.
