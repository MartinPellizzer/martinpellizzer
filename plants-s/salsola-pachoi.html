Salsola pachoi

Salsola pachoi, commonly known as Russian thistle or goatweed, is a flowering plant in the family Amaranthaceae.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, characterized by its succulent stems and leaves. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Salsola pachoi is also known for its ability to spread rapidly, making it a common weed in agricultural and disturbed areas.
