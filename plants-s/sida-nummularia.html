Sida nummularia

Sida nummularia, commonly known as little mallow, is a low-growing annual plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, round, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

Sida nummularia is also used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
