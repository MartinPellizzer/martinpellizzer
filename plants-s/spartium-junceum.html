Spartium junceum

Spartium junceum, commonly known as goat's rue, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant produces small, pale blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. Its slender, wiry stems and hairy foliage are typical of its arid habitat.

Spartium junceum is often found in dry grasslands and rocky soils, thriving in full sun.
