Straussia grandiflora

Straussia grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered straussia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. Its blooming period occurs in the spring and early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Straussia grandiflora is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
