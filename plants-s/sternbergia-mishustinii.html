Sternbergia mishustinii

Sternbergia mishustinii, commonly known as the Caucasian starflower, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the Caucasus region.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of star-shaped, yellowish-white flowers. The plant produces narrow, grass-like leaves and has a short, stout stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Sternbergia mishustinii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its early spring blooms.
