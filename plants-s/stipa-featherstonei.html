Stipa featherstonei

Stipa featherstonei, commonly known as the feathergrass, is a species of grass native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and distinctive, feathery seed heads that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to harsh, arid environments. It is often found in alpine meadows and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.

Stipa featherstonei plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small wildlife in its native range.
