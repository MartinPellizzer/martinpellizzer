Spiranthes chilensis

Spiranthes chilensis, commonly known as the Chilean spiral orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, spiral-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces a single, slender stem.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in its native habitat.
