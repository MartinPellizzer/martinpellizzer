Streblocarpus pubescens

Streblocarpus pubescens, commonly known as the hairy strobilanthes, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves. The plant produces striking, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
