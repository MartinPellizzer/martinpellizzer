Sedum ibicense

Sedum ibicense, commonly known as the Canary Island stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features fleshy, upright stems and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy choice for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular ornamental plant.
