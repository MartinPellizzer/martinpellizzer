Scabiosa superba f. nana

Scabiosa superba f. nana, commonly known as the dwarf scabious, is a compact, flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, rounded leaves and produces clusters of pink or purple flowers with a distinctive, globular shape. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in well-drained soil and full sunlight. It is often used in rock gardens and as a border plant due to its ornamental value.

Its short stature makes it ideal for containers and small garden spaces.
