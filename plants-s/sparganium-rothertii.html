Sparganium rothertii

Sparganium rothertii, commonly known as the narrowleaf bur-reed, is a perennial aquatic plant native to North America.

It typically grows in shallow freshwater habitats such as ponds, lakes, and wetlands. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces distinctive, spiky, yellowish-brown bur-like structures that contain small seeds. Sparganium rothertii is characterized by its ability to thrive in muddy or sandy substrates.

It plays a role in stabilizing aquatic environments and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
