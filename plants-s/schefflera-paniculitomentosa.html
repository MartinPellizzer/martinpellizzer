Schefflera paniculitomentosa

Schefflera paniculitomentosa, commonly known as the woolly umbrella plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded clusters of leaves that resemble an umbrella, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by orange-red berries. Its foliage is thick and leathery, with a dark green color and a slightly waxy texture.

Schefflera paniculitomentosa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
