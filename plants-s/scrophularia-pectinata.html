Scrophularia pectinata

Scrophularia pectinata, commonly known as the yellow scrophularia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features opposite, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are tubular, yellow or orange in color, and are arranged in clusters at the tips of the stems.

Scrophularia pectinata is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
