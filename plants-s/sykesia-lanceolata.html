Sykesia lanceolata

Sykesia lanceolata, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, clustered flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows as a compact shrub, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Sykesia lanceolata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care.
