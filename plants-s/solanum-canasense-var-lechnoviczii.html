Solanum canasense var. lechnoviczii

Solanum canasense var. lechnoviczii, commonly known as the Lechnoviczii nightshade, is a shrub native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant features small, yellow flowers and produces berries that are typically orange to red in color. It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves with a slightly serrated edge.

This species is often found in moist, shaded environments at higher elevations.
