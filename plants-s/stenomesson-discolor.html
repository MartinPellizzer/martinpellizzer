Stenomesson discolor

Stenomesson discolor, commonly known as the pale morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its trumpet-shaped, pale blue to white flowers that open in the morning and close by midday. The plant has heart-shaped, velvety leaves and produces capsule fruits containing numerous small, brown seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in arid or semi-arid environments.
