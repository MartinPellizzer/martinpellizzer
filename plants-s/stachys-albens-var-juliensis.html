Stachys albens var. juliensis

Stachys albens var. juliensis, commonly known as white betony, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces spikes of pale blue to white flowers, which bloom from late spring to early summer. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and wildflower displays.
