Stenocactus dichroacanthus subsp. violaciflorus

Stenocactus dichroacanthus subsp.

violaciflorus, commonly known as the violet-flowered stenocactus, is a succulent cactus native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its cylindrical stem covered in white to pale blue spines.

The plant produces striking violet or purple flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, requiring minimal water.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
