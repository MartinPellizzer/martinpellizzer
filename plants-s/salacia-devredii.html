Salacia devredii

Salacia devredii, commonly known as the golden flame vine, is a striking tropical climbing plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a dramatic visual display to its environment. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and emit a mild fragrance, enhancing its ornamental value. Its rapid growth habit makes it suitable for trellises or as a ground cover in warm climates.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by horticulturists.
