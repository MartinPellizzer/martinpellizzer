Schismatoglottis rubiginosa

Schismatoglottis rubiginosa, commonly known as the red-stemmed orchid, is a tropical plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its dark red or purple stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the warmer months.

Schismatoglottis rubiginosa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
