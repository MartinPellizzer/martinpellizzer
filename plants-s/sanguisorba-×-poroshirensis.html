Sanguisorba × poroshirensis

Sanguisorba × poroshirensis, commonly known as the ornamental dock, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and its clusters of pink to red bracts that resemble flowers. The plant produces small, white to yellow flowers in panicles, which are arranged in a branching pattern. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a serrated edge, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Sanguisorba × poroshirensis is typically found in moist, well-drained soils and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic value.
