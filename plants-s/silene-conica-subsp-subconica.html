Silene conica subsp. subconica

Silene conica subsp.

subconica, commonly known as the narrow-coned catchfly, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, pink or white flowers.
