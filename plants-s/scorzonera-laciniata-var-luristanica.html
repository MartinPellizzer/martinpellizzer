Scorzonera laciniata var. luristanica

Scorzonera laciniata var. luristanica, commonly known as the Luristan scorzonerina, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Iran.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of lanceolate, lobed leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers with ray florets, typically blooming in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and traditional herbal practices.
