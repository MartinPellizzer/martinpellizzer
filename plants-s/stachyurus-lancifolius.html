Stachyurus lancifolius

Stachyurus lancifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaf stachyurus, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in whorls along the stems. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers in early spring, which are often followed by reddish-brown, papery fruits. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a suitable ornamental plant in gardens.

Stachyurus lancifolius is valued for its elegant appearance and adaptability to various garden conditions.
