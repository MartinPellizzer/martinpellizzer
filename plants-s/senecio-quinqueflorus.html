Senecio quinqueflorus

Senecio quinqueflorus, commonly known as the five-flowered groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils. The plant features small, yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, which bloom from late winter to early summer. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Senecio quinqueflorus is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and along rocky outcrops.
