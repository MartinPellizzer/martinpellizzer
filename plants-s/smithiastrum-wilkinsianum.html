Smithiastrum wilkinsianum

Smithiastrum wilkinsianum, commonly known as the Wilkins' sedge, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to the wetlands of southern Africa, particularly in South Africa and Namibia. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in marshy areas or along the edges of water bodies.

Due to its unique ecological role and limited distribution, it is considered a valuable species for wetland conservation.
