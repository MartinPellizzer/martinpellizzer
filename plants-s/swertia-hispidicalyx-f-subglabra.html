Swertia hispidicalyx f. subglabra

Swertia hispidicalyx f. subglabra, commonly known as the smooth swertia, is a variant of the swertia plant characterized by its reduced pubescence.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is native to regions of Asia, particularly China. The plant typically features lanceolate leaves with a smooth surface, distinguishing it from its more hairy relatives. Its flowers are usually blue or purple, arranged in racemose inflorescences, and are known for their ornamental value.

The species is valued in traditional medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
