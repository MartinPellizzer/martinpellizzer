Senecio massaiensis

Senecio massaiensis, commonly known as the Massai's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is a succulent shrub that typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions. The plant features woolly, gray-green leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters. It is adapted to withstand drought conditions due to its thick, fleshy stems and leaves.

Senecio massaiensis is often found in rocky soils and is part of the Asteraceae family.
