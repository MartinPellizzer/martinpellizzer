Solanum tuberosum f. chiar-pala

Solanum tuberosum f. chiar-pala, commonly known as the Chiar-Pala potato, is a botanical variant of the common potato characterized by its unique foliage and tuber morphology.

This variety exhibits a striking contrast in leaf coloration, with dark green veins against a lighter green background. The tubers are typically elongated and have a smooth skin, often displaying a range of colors from white to pale yellow. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and mild flavor, making it suitable for both culinary and decorative purposes.

The plant thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
