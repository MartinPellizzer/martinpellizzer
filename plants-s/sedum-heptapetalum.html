Sedum heptapetalum

Sedum heptapetalum, commonly known as the seven-petaled stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red. The plant has thick, water-storing leaves that help it survive in arid conditions. It blooms during the summer months and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Sedum heptapetalum is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and ornamental value.
