Sporobolus exilis

Sporobolus exilis, commonly known as slender spikerush, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes, swales, and along the edges of ponds. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, wind-pollinated flowers.

Sporobolus exilis is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in both full sun and partial shade.
