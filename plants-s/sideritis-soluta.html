Sideritis soluta

Sideritis soluta, commonly known as the Greek violet or mountain violet, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous areas, preferring well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its small, violet-blue flowers bloom in summer, and the leaves are often covered in fine hairs.

Sideritis soluta is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
