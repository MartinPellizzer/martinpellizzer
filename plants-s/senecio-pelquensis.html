Senecio pelquensis

Senecio pelquensis, commonly known as the Pelquens Senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
