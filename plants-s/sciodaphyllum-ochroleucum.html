Sciodaphyllum ochroleucum

Sciodaphyllum ochroleucum, commonly known as the yellow-leafed sunshade, is a unique plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage that forms a compact, low-growing shrub. The leaves are typically broad and leathery, with a yellowish-green hue that gives the plant its common name. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is a rare and ecologically significant species.
