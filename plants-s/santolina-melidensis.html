Santolina melidensis

Santolina melidensis, commonly known as Spanish santolina, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and small yellow flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for Mediterranean climates.

Santolina melidensis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract beneficial insects.
