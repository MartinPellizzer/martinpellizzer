Senna occidentalis

Senna occidentalis, commonly known as the sickle senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, twisted pods that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and waste lands.

Senna occidentalis is also known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for its laxative effects.
