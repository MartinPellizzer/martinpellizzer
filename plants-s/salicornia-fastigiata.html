Salicornia fastigiata

Salicornia fastigiata, commonly known as sea bean or glasswort, is a halophyte plant adapted to grow in saline environments.

It is a succulent plant characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems and small, scale-like leaves. The plant produces tiny, green flowers that are typically clustered in dense spikes. It is often found in coastal areas, salt marshes, and other saline soils.

Salicornia fastigiata is valued for its ability to thrive in harsh conditions and is sometimes used in culinary or medicinal applications.
