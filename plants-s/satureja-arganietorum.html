Satureja arganietorum

Satureja arganietorum, commonly known as Arganietum sage, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and thrives in full sun. Its small, tubular flowers are usually purple or pink, and it produces tiny, oval-shaped fruits.

Satureja arganietorum is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
