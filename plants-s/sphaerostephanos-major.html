Sphaerostephanos major

Sphaerostephanos major, commonly known as the giant starflower, is a striking perennial plant native to the high altitudes of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy, star-shaped flowers that can reach up to 10 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces clusters of these flowers on tall, branching stems, which often exceed two meters in height. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine layer of white hair, giving them a silvery appearance.

Sphaerostephanos major is adapted to harsh, arid environments and thrives in full sun with well-drained soil.
