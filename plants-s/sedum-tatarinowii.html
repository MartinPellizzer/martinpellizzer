Sedum tatarinowii

Sedum tatarinowii, commonly known as the Tatarian stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow to orange. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens and arid landscapes. Its leaves are typically blue-green in color and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Sedum tatarinowii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
