Stapelia × lunata

Stapelia × lunata, commonly known as the hybrid star cactus, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed stems that store water. The plant produces striking, lantern-shaped flowers that are often yellow or orange in color. These flowers emit a strong, unpleasant odor to attract pollinators like flies.

Stapelia × lunata is a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
