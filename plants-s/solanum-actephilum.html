Solanum actephilum

Solanum actephilum, commonly known as the purple potato vine, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant purple foliage. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that develop into berries containing glossy, dark purple seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
