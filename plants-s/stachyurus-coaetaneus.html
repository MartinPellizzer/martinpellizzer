Stachyurus coaetaneus

Stachyurus coaetaneus, commonly known as the Chinese weeping honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features slender, arching branches that bear small, fragrant flowers in early spring. The plant produces yellowish-white to pale pink blossoms that hang in clusters, creating a weeping effect. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture.

Stachyurus coaetaneus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscape design for its visual interest.
