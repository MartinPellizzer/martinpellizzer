Senecio octophyllus

Senecio octophyllus, commonly known as the eight-leaf senecio, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant is characterized by its rosette growth habit and hairy, lance-shaped leaves. It produces small yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the daisy family.
