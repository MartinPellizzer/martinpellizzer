Spergularia Colorata Var. Indurata

Spergularia colorata var. indurata is a lesser-known plant species that thrives in specific coastal environments. It is characterized by its compact growth and distinctive reddish-pink flowers. This variety is often found in sandy or gravelly soils along the shores of the Atlantic coast. Its hardy nature allows it to withstand harsh maritime conditions. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated in botanical studies and conservation efforts.
