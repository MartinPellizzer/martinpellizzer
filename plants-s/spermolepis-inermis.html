Spermolepis inermis

Spermolepis inermis, commonly known as the smooth sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of up to 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom during the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, adapted to store water in its native dry environment.

Spermolepis inermis is often found in sandy or gravelly soils and is well-suited to drought conditions.
