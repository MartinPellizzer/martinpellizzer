Sarcolaena multiflora var. latifolia

Sarcolaena multiflora var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf flame bush, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

The leaves are broad and ovate, often with a slightly hairy texture, and the plant is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.
