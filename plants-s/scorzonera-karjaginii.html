Scorzonera karjaginii

Scorzonera karjaginii, commonly known as Karjagin's scorzonera, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and lobed leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers with a distinctive central disk, typically blooming in the summer months. Scorzonera karjaginii is adapted to arid and rocky environments, showcasing resilience to harsh climatic conditions.

Its root system is often thick and fleshy, which helps it store water and nutrients.
