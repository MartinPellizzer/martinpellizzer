Specklinia tricolor

Specklinia tricolor, commonly known as the three-colored orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its strikingly patterned leaves and flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually small and come in a combination of green, white, and yellow hues.

Specklinia tricolor is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
