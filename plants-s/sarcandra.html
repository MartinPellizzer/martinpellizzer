Sarcandra

Sarcandra, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Campanulaceae.

It consists of a single species, Sarcandra glabra, which is native to China and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Sarcandra is relatively low-maintenance, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
