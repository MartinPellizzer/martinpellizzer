Sedum bouvieri

Sedum bouvieri, commonly known as the blue sedum, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features dense, compact growth with small, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped, pale blue or purple flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Sedum bouvieri is often used in rock gardens and as a ground cover due to its hardiness and ornamental appeal.
