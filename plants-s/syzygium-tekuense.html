Syzygium tekuense

Syzygium tekuense, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. The tree produces small, round fruits that are typically red or yellow when mature. Syzygium tekuense is valued for its hard, heavy wood, which is used in furniture and carving.

It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.
