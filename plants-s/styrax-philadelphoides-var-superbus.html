Styrax philadelphoides var. superbus

Styrax philadelphoides var. superbus, commonly known as the Chinese frankincense tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces a resinous sap that has been historically used in traditional medicine and incense. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a glossy surface.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in its native habitat.
