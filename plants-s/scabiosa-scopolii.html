Scabiosa scopolii

Scabiosa scopolii, commonly known as the rock scabious, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, pink or purple flowers. The plant has hairy, narrow leaves that are often bluish-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Scabiosa scopolii is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
