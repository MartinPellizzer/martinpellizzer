Senecio corymbosus

Senecio corymbosus, commonly known as the clustered groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant features wiry stems and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense clusters. Its leaves are usually gray-green and covered in fine hairs.

Senecio corymbosus is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
