Schefflera oligodon

Schefflera oligodon, commonly known as the African shield fern, is a tropical evergreen plant native to parts of Africa.

It features a distinctive, upright growth habit with a thick, fibrous trunk and large, shield-shaped leaves that are deeply lobed. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often used in ornamental horticulture.

Its foliage provides a lush, tropical appearance, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
