Seriphidium poljakovii

Seriphidium poljakovii, commonly known as the Transcaucasian wormwood, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is known for its aromatic foliage. It produces small yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.
