Seriphidium fedtschenkoanum

Seriphidium fedtschenkoanum, commonly known as the Fedtschenko's wormwood, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in dense heads. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or gravelly soils.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
