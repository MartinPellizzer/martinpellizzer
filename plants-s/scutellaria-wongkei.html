Scutellaria wongkei

Scutellaria wongkei, commonly known as Wongkei skullcap, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that typically bloom in shades of blue or purple. Its leaves are opposite and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Scutellaria wongkei is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
