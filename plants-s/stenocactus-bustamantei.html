Stenocactus bustamantei

Stenocactus bustamantei, commonly known as the Pachyveria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its dense, cylindrical stems that are covered in short, white spines. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
