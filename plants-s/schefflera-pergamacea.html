Schefflera pergamacea

Schefflera pergamacea, commonly known as the African shield fern, is a tropical plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, shield-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. The plant has a woody stem and can grow as an upright shrub or small tree. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Schefflera pergamacea is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
