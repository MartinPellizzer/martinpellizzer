Solanum macranthemum

Solanum macranthemum, commonly known as the large-flowered nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant features showy, violet to purple flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a hairy texture.

Solanum macranthemum is typically found in moist, shaded environments and can thrive in garden settings with proper care.
