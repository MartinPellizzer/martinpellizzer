Solanum plousianthemum var. rhodesianum

Solanum plousianthemum var. rhodesianum, commonly known as the Rhodesian nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its hairy stems and ovate, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer, followed by spherical, berry-like fruits that turn from green to red as they mature.

It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
