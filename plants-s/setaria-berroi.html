Setaria berroi

Setaria berroi, commonly known as the narrowleaf foxtail, is a grass species native to Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces dense, spike-like inflorescences that are typically green to reddish in color. It thrives in warm, dry climates and is often found in disturbed soils.

Setaria berroi is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings due to its rapid growth and adaptability.
