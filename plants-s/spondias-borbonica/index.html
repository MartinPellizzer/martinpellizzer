Spondias Borbonica

Spondias borbonica is a small, ornamental tree native to the tropical regions of South America. It is known for its striking yellow flowers and edible fruits that are often used in local cuisines. This plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for gardeners and landscapers. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively unknown to many outside its native habitat.
