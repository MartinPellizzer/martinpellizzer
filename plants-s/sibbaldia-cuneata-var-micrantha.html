Sibbaldia cuneata var. micrantha

Sibbaldia cuneata var. micrantha, commonly known as the narrowleaf sibbaldia, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Rosaceae and is characterized by its slender, cuneate leaves and small, white to pinkish flowers. The plant typically thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to cold, alpine environments.
