Securidaca trianae

Securidaca trianae, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its dense, hard wood that is highly valued for its durability and strength. Due to its slow growth and limited natural range, it is considered a vulnerable species in some regions.
