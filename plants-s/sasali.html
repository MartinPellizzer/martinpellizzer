Sasali

Sasali, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, waxy leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a small shrub or herb, often with a compact, bushy appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Sasali is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
