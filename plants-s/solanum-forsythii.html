Solanum forsythii

Solanum forsythii, commonly known as the Chinese nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant features alternate, ovate leaves with a hairy surface and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers. Its fruit is a globose berry that turns red when mature, containing several seeds.

Solanum forsythii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in Chinese medicine.
