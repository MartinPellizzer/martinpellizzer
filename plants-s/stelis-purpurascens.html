Stelis purpurascens

Stelis purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking purple stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or white with a distinctive red or purple lip.

Stelis purpurascens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
