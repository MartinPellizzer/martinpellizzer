Symphytum × wettsteinii

Symphytum × wettsteinii, commonly known as the hybrid comfrey, is a perennial plant belonging to the Boraginaceae family.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves and clusters of blue or violet flowers. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often cultivated for its medicinal properties and as an ornamental plant.

Due to its vigorous growth, it can become invasive if not properly managed.
