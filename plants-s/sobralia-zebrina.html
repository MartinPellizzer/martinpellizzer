Sobralia zebrina

Sobralia zebrina, commonly known as the Zebra Cactus, is a striking epiphytic plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, zebra-striped stems that range in color from green to blue and purple. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically blooming during the warmer months. Its unique appearance and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Sobralia zebrina is relatively low maintenance, requiring well-draining soil and moderate watering to flourish.
