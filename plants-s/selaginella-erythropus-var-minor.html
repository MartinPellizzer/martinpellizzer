Selaginella erythropus var. minor

Selaginella erythropus var. minor, commonly known as the lesser red spore moss, is a species of spikemoss belonging to the family Selaginellaceae.

It is a small, herbaceous plant that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features intricate, branching stems covered in tiny, scale-like leaves. Its reproductive structures include cone-like strobila that produce spores, a characteristic feature of its evolutionary lineage.

This variety is notable for its delicate, reddish coloration, which becomes more pronounced under certain environmental conditions.
