Stachys subcrenata var. labiosa

Stachys subcrenata var. labiosa, commonly known as the pink hedgehog.

This perennial herb is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soils. It features dense, woolly foliage with a distinctive pinkish hue, and its flowers are small, tubular, and typically pink or purple. The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in rock gardens or as a ground cover.

It is drought-tolerant and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
