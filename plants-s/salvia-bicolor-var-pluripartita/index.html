Salvia Bicolor Var. Pluripartita

Blue mistflower, scientifically known as Salvia bicolor var. pluripartita, is a striking perennial plant native to the eastern United States. It is celebrated for its delicate, sky-blue flowers that bloom in dense clusters, creating a mist-like appearance. This plant thrives in partial to full sunlight and is well-suited for garden landscapes due to its hardy nature. Its unique color and form make it a popular choice among gardeners seeking both beauty and biodiversity. With its ability to attract pollinators like butterflies and bees, it plays an important role in supporting local ecosystems.
