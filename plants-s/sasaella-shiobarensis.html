Sasaella shiobarensis

Sasaella shiobarensis, commonly known as the Shiobara bamboo, is a shrub-like plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its dense, clumping growth habit. The plant features narrow, elongated leaves that are typically dark green in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Sasaella shiobarensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in forest ecosystems.
