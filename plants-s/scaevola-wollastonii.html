Scaevola wollastonii

Scaevola wollastonii, commonly known as the silver bush, is a perennial shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and silver-gray foliage. Scaevola wollastonii is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.
