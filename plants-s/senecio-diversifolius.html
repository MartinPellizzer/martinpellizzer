Senecio diversifolius

Senecio diversifolius, commonly known as the variable leaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its diverse leaf shapes, which range from linear to lobed, and its yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open habitats. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its medicinal properties in traditional use.

Senecio diversifolius is also valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens due to its unique foliage and vibrant blooms.
