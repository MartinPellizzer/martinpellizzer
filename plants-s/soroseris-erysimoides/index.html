Soroseris Erysimoides

Soroseris erysimoides is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern China. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, known for its composite flower heads and ecological importance. This species is characterized by its small, daisy-like blooms and finely divided leaves. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts. Its unique appearance and ecological role make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
