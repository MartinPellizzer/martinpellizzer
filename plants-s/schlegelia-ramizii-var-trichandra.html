Schlegelia ramizii var. trichandra

Schlegelia ramizii var. trichandra, commonly known as the trichandra variant of Schlegelia ramizii, is a rare and specialized plant species found in specific alpine environments.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, mat-forming foliage. The leaves are typically small, hairy, and adapted to withstand harsh, cold climates. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often clustered near the base of the plant.
