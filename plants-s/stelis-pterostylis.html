Stelis pterostylis

Stelis pterostylis, commonly known as the slender spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests and understoreys. Its flowers have a unique structure with a narrow, spurred lip that is adapted for pollination by specific insect species.

Stelis pterostylis is often found in clusters and is known for its delicate appearance and ecological significance in its habitat.
