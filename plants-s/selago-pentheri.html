Selago pentheri

Selago pentheri, commonly known as the mountain balm, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous habitats.

Selago pentheri is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
