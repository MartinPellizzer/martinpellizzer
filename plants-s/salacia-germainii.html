Salacia germainii

Salacia germainii, commonly known as the African mountain thorn, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height, characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruit is a small, round capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Salacia germainii is often found in rocky or sandy soils in woodland or savanna habitats.
