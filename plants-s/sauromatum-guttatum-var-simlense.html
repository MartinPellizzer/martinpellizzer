Sauromatum guttatum var. simlense

Sauromatum guttatum var. simlense, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red flowers with a dark purple center, which resemble spiders, giving it its common name. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, fertile soil.

This variant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a popular choice in tropical gardens.
