Stenophyllus hirtellus f. humilis

Stenophyllus hirtellus f. humilis, commonly known as the low-growing form of the genus Stenophyllus, is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its compact growth habit.

It typically features slender, upright stems covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. The leaves are narrow and linear, often arranged in a rosette pattern at the base of the plant.
