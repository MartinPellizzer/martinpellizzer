Solanum cerasiferum var. parcestellatum

Solanum cerasiferum var. parcestellatum, commonly known as the dwarf cherry tomato, is a compact variant of the cherry tomato plant.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like potatoes and peppers. The plant produces small, round, red fruits that are typically harvested when fully ripe. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its compact size makes it ideal for container gardening and small spaces.
