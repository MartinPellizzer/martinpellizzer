Spiraeanthemum samoense var. lanutooi

Spiraeanthemum samoense var. lanutooi, commonly known as the Samoan spirea, is a shrub native to the islands of Samoa.

It features small, showy flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in shades of white and pink. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges, and its stems are often covered in a fine, fuzzy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular ornamental plant in tropical gardens.

The variety lanutooi is distinguished by its unique foliage and floral characteristics compared to other variants of Spiraeanthemum samoense.
