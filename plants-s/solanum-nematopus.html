Solanum nematopus

Solanum nematopus, commonly known as the slender nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants such as tomatoes and peppers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, with hairy stems and small, oval-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale purple, and it produces small, round berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

The plant is often found in moist, open habitats such as woodlands and fields.
