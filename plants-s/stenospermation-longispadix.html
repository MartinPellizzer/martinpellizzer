Stenospermation longispadix

Stenospermation longispadix, commonly known as the narrow-seed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces elongated, spadix-like inflorescences that bear small, greenish-white flowers. It is native to tropical regions of South America and is often found in shaded, humid environments.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a delicate and less commonly cultivated orchid.
