Scilla numidica subsp. ebusitana

Scilla numidica subsp.

ebusitana, commonly known as the Ebusi scilla, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region. It typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and is often found in coastal areas.

The plant produces clusters of blue or purple flowers in early spring, which are attractive to pollinators. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, emerging from the base of the plant.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
