Steffensia lhotzkyana

Steffensia lhotzkyana, commonly known as the Lhotzkyana dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, thriving in cool, moist conditions. It features a basal rosette of leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Steffensia lhotzkyana is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
