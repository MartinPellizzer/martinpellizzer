Sideritis subatlantica

Sideritis subatlantica, commonly known as the Atlantic sedge, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Sideritis subatlantica is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
