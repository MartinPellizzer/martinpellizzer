Saxifraga fortunei var. jotanii

Saxifraga fortunei var. jotanii, commonly known as the stonecrop or rockfoil, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, succulent leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, blooming in the summer months. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to harsh alpine environments.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil and full sun.
