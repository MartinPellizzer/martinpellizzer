Selaginella ovalis

Selaginella ovalis, commonly known as the common spikemoss, is a species of spikemoss found in various regions, including parts of Asia, Africa, and the Americas.

It is a small, evergreen plant that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. The plant has a distinctive appearance with its upright, branching stems and small, scale-like leaves. Selaginella ovalis reproduces via spores, which are produced in sporangia located on the underside of its leaves.

This species is notable for its ability to survive in harsh conditions, making it an important component of many ecosystems.
