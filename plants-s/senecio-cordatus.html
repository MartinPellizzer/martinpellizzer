Senecio cordatus

Senecio cordatus, commonly known as the heartleaf senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long blooming period.
