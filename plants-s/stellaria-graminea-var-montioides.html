Stellaria graminea var. montioides

Stellaria graminea var. montioides, commonly known as the alpine starwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to mountainous regions of Europe.

It features small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to July. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in well-drained, rocky soils. It is often found in alpine meadows and dry slopes at elevations above 1,000 meters.

Due to its delicate structure and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
