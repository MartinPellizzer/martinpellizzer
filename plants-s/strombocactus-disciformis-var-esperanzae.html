Strombocactus disciformis var. esperanzae

Strombocactus disciformis var. esperanzae, commonly known as Esperanza's disc-shaped cactus, is a rare and striking member of the Cactaceae family.

It is native to the arid regions of Mexico, where it thrives in well-drained, sandy soils.
