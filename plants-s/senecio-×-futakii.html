Senecio × futakii

Senecio × futakii, commonly known as the hybrid blueweed, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows as a perennial herb with slender, branching stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. Senecio × futakii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its vibrant color and ease of care.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
