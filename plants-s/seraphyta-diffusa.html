Seraphyta diffusa

Seraphyta diffusa, commonly known as the wandering angel, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, velvety leaves that range in color from deep green to dark purple, often with intricate patterns of silver or white. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom throughout the year in humid conditions. Seraphyta diffusa thrives in high-light environments and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil to flourish.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
