Senecio chinotegensis

Senecio chinotegensis, commonly known as the Chinese Senecio, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. It features narrow, linear leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
