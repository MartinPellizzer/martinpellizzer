Silene bipartita var. stenophylla

Silene bipartita var. stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf twinflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pinkish-purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in alpine environments. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, contributing to its common name.

Silene bipartita var. stenophylla is often found in open, sunny habitats at high elevations.
