Solidago ulmifolia var. palmeri

Solidago ulmifolia var. palmeri, commonly known as Palmer's goldenrod, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow inflorescences. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a rough texture.

Palmer's goldenrod plays an important role in supporting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
