Statice rumelica

Statice rumelica, commonly known as the Rumelian statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with the flowers often appearing in dense clusters at the top of the stems.

Statice rumelica is known for its long-lasting blooms, which make it a favorite among florists and gardeners alike.
