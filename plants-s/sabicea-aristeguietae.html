Sabicea aristeguietae

Sabicea aristeguietae, commonly known as the Aristeguieta's sabicea, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows in high-altitude environments, often in rocky or mountainous terrain. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink in color. Its leaves are simple and alternate, with a slightly hairy texture.

Sabicea aristeguietae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
