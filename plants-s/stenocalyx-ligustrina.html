Stenocalyx ligustrina

Stenocalyx ligustrina, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or the silver thread plant, is a flowering shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Oleaceae family and is characterized by its small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. It is commonly cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
