Senecio betonicifolius

Senecio betonicifolius, commonly known as the African ironweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant features upright, branched stems covered in fine, hairy hairs, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a rough texture. It produces small yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer.

Senecio betonicifolius is often found in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils.
