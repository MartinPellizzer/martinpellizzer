Stelis grossilabris

Stelis grossilabris, commonly known as the large-lipped stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and produces clusters of showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically has long, narrow leaves and thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are often pale yellow or greenish-white with contrasting purple or red markings.

Stelis grossilabris is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
