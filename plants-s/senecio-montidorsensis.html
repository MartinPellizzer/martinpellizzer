Senecio montidorsensis

Senecio montidorsensis, commonly known as the mountain daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other composite flowers. The plant is characterized by its dense, cushion-like growth habit and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
