Stigmatopteris rotundata var. trinidadensis

Stigmatopteris rotundata var. trinidadensis, commonly known as the Trinidad fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, clustered growth habit and dark green, leathery fronds. The fronds are typically divided into lanceolate segments with a distinctive, recurved shape. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
