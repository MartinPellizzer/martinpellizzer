Sulcorebutia sanguineotarijensis

Sulcorebutia sanguineotarijensis, commonly known as the Tarija blood cactus, is a succulent plant native to Bolivia.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular shape and bright red stems. The plant features spiny areoles and produces yellow flowers during the growing season. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its fleshy stems and leaves.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
