Senefeldera chiribiquetensis

Senefeldera chiribiquetensis, commonly known as the Chiribiquet lily, is a rare and endemic plant species native to the Chiribiquet National Park in Colombia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded habitats within the tropical rainforest. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces striking white or pale pink flowers with a distinctive, elongated shape. Its botanical significance lies in its unique adaptations to the specific ecological conditions of its habitat.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species requiring conservation efforts.
