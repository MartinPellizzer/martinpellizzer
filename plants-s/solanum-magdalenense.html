Solanum magdalenense

Solanum magdalenense, commonly known as the Magdalena nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and tobacco. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are ovate with a rough texture, and it produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters.

The fruit is a berry that turns from green to red when mature, containing several seeds.
