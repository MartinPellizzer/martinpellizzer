Senecillis yunnanensis

Senecillis yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan Senecio, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. The plant is characterized by its erect stem and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. It typically grows in mountainous regions and is adapted to cool, moist environments.
