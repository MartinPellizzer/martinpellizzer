Sanguisorba × kishinamii

Sanguisorba × kishinamii, commonly known as the hybrid bloodwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the rose family (Rosaceae) and is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, reddish-purple flowers. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a slightly serrated edge, and its roots are fibrous and often used in traditional medicine.

Sanguisorba × kishinamii thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.
