Sansevieria bacularis

Sansevieria bacularis, commonly known as the Bacularis Snake Plant, is a species native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with subtle variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Sansevieria bacularis is also known for its air-purifying qualities and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor environments.
