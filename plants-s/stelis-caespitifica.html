Stelis caespitifica

Stelis caespitifica, commonly known as the clustered stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a short, compact stem with numerous small, clustered leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, arranged in a dense inflorescence.

Stelis caespitifica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in terrariums or as a houseplant.
