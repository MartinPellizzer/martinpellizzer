Stelis distantiflora

Stelis distantiflora, commonly known as the distant-flowered stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, white or pale green flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, deriving moisture and nutrients from the air and rain. Its roots are specialized for absorbing water and nutrients from the environment.

Stelis distantiflora is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and adaptability to various growing conditions.
