Spiesia chionobia

Spiesia chionobia, commonly known as the silverleaf spurge, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its succulent, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
