Senecio arnottianus

Senecio arnottianus, commonly known as the Arnott's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are small and yellow.

It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and shrublands.
