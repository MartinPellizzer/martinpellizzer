Solanum patulum f. album

Solanum patulum f. album, commonly known as the white-flowered nightshade, is a variant of the Solanum patulum species.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves and produces showy, white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a small, round berry that turns from green to yellow as it matures.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking floral display.
