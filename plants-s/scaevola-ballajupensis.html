Scaevola ballajupensis

Scaevola ballajupensis, commonly known as the Ballajup scaly fanflower, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and distinctive blue to purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has small, scale-like leaves that are tightly arranged around the stems, giving it a unique appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
