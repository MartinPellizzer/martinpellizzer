Smithia blanda var. racemosa

Smithia blanda var. racemosa, commonly known as the racemose smithia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Ericaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers. The leaves are typically evergreen, glossy, and elliptical in shape. Native to regions with acidic soils, it thrives in moist, well-drained environments.
