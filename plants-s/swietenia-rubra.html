Swietenia rubra

Swietenia rubra, commonly known as the red siamese wood, is a tropical hardwood tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Meliaceae family and is characterized by its heavy, durable, and highly valued timber. The tree can grow up to 40 meters tall, with a straight trunk and a dense canopy. Its leaves are alternate, pinnate, and have a glossy appearance.

The flowers are small and inconspicuous, while the fruit is a woody capsule containing several seeds.
