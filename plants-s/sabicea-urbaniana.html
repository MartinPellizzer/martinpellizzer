Sabicea urbaniana

Sabicea urbaniana, commonly known as the urbaniana acacia, is a shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its compound leaves and legume fruits. The plant features bipinnate leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets, and it produces yellowish to orange flowers arranged in racemes. Its fruit is a flat, dehiscent pod that contains several seeds.

Sabicea urbaniana is often found in moist, tropical environments and is valued for its ornamental qualities.
