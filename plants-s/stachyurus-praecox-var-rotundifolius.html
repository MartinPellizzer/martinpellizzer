Stachyurus praecox var. rotundifolius

Stachyurus praecox var. rotundifolius, commonly known as the round-leaf stachyurus, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features attractive, rounded leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white flowers in early spring, which are followed by red berries. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to woodland gardens and mixed borders.
