Senecio eriopodus

Senecio eriopodus, commonly known as the woolyleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-green leaves and yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It thrives in full sun and is relatively drought-tolerant.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
