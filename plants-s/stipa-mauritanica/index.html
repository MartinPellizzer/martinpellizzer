Stipa Mauritanica

Stipa mauritanica is a hardy grass species native to northern Africa and the Mediterranean region. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing on rocky soils and slopes. Known for its tufted growth habit and silver-blue foliage, this plant is both ornamental and ecologically valuable. Its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion makes it an important species in restoration projects. Despite its resilience, Stipa mauritanica remains a lesser-known plant in the world of botany and landscaping.
