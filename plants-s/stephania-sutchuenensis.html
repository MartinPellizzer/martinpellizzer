Stephania sutchuenensis

Stephania sutchuenensis, commonly known as the Sichuan stephania, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Menispermaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in umbels. Its tuberous roots are notable for their medicinal properties and are used in traditional Chinese medicine.
