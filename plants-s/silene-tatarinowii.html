Silene tatarinowii

Silene tatarinowii, commonly known as the Tatarian campion, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or arid environments. The plant features showy pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are alternate and often have a rough texture.

Silene tatarinowii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
