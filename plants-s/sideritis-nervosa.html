Sideritis nervosa

Sideritis nervosa, commonly known as narrow-leaved ironweed, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features slender, hairy stems and small, pale blue or violet flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are narrow and serrated, often with a distinct veining pattern.

Sideritis nervosa is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
