Scutellaria tashiroi var. tomentosa

Scutellaria tashiroi var. tomentosa, commonly known as the tomentose skullcap, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its hairy, grayish foliage. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
