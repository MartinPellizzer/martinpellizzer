Schefflera balansana

Schefflera balansana, commonly known as the Philippine umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like crown of large, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that are followed by bright red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Schefflera balansana is also valued for its ability to tolerate partial shade and its relatively low maintenance requirements.
