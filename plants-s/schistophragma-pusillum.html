Schistophragma pusillum

Schistophragma pusillum, commonly known as the small-leaved rock vine, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, particularly China and India.

It is a climbing shrub that typically grows in rocky or mountainous habitats. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow in color. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a velvety texture.

Schistophragma pusillum is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
