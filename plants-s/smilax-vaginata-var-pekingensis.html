Smilax vaginata var. pekingensis

Smilax vaginata var. pekingensis, commonly known as the Peking arrowleaf, is a climbing shrub native to eastern China.

It features dark green, arrow-shaped leaves with a waxy texture and prominent veins. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into spiny, berry-like fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value and adaptability make it a popular choice for horticultural use in temperate climates.
