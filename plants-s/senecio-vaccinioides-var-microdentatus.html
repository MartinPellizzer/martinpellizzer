Senecio vaccinioides var. microdentatus

Senecio vaccinioides var. microdentatus, commonly known as the dwarf Senecio or miniature Senecio, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, upright growth with fleshy, blue-green leaves that are densely packed and often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in terminal clusters during the summer months. It is adapted to rocky, well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
