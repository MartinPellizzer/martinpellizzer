Schizachyrium sanguineum var. oligostachyum

Schizachyrium sanguineum var. oligostachyum, commonly known as red panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It features slender, upright stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant produces fluffy, red-tipped inflorescences that give it its common name, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soils.

This variety is often used in native plant gardens for its drought tolerance and attractive fall color.
