Stelis perbona

Stelis perbona, commonly known as the Perbona orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, hanging inflorescences. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to tree trunks or branches in tropical rainforests. Its flowers are usually small and fragrant, displaying a range of colors from white to pale pink.

Stelis perbona thrives in humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media for optimal growth.
