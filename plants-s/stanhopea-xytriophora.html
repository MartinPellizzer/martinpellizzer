Stanhopea xytriophora

Stanhopea xytriophora, commonly known as the yellow stanhopea, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers with contrasting purple and green markings. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in the understory of tropical forests. Its pseudobulbs are relatively short and clustered, supporting its epiphytic growth habit.

Stanhopea xytriophora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
