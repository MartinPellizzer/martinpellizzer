Sarcanthus discolor

Sarcanthus discolor, commonly known as the pale orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, pale pink to white flowers. The plant typically grows in high-altitude regions with cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, while its inflorescence consists of a single, showy flower.

Sarcanthus discolor is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
