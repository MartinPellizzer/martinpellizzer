Senecio megaphyllus

Senecio megaphyllus, commonly known as the giant leaf senecio, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its large, broad, lobed leaves. The plant produces yellow daisy-like flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun.

Due to its striking foliage, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
