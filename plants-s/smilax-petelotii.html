Smilax petelotii

Smilax petelotii, commonly known as the Mexican catclaw, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Smilacaceae.

It is characterized by its woody stems and spiny, climbing or trailing growth habit. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in axillary inflorescences. Its leaves are usually alternate, simple, and have a rough, textured surface.
