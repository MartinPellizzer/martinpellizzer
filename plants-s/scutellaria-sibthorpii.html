Scutellaria sibthorpii

Scutellaria sibthorpii, commonly known as the Sibthorp's skullcap, is a flowering plant belonging to the mint family, Lamiaceae.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils in arid or semi-arid regions. Its leaves are opposite, ovate to lanceolate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Scutellaria sibthorpii is native to regions of Asia, particularly China and the Indian subcontinent.
