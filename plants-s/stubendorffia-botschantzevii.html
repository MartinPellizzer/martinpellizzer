Stubendorffia botschantzevii

Stubendorffia botschantzevii, commonly known as the mountain orchid, is a species of orchid native to the high altitudes of the Caucasus region.

It is a terrestrial herb with a slender, upright stem and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in a loose spike. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
