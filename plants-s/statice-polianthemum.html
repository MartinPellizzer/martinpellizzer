Statice polianthemum

Statice polianthemum, commonly known as the garden statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, spike-like inflorescences composed of numerous small, colorful flowers. The plant produces tiny, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its long-lasting flowers make it a favored plant for dried floral arrangements.
