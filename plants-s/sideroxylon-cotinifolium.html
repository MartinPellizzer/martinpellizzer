Sideroxylon cotinifolium

Sideroxylon cotinifolium, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its hard, dense wood, which is highly valued for its strength and durability. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by small, round fruits containing a single seed.

The tree is often found in dry forest and woodland habitats, where it plays an important ecological role.
