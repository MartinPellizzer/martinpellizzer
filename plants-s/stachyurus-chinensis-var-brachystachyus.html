Stachyurus chinensis var. brachystachyus

Stachyurus chinensis var. brachystachyus, commonly known as the Chinese weeping maple, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its distinctive, pendulous clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are ovate to elliptic, with a smooth margin and a dark green upper surface. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Its unique floral display and graceful form make it a valuable addition to landscape designs.
