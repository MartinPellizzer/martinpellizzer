Stipa avenacioides

Stipa avenacioides, commonly known as the slender needlegrass, is a grass species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are often clustered at the tips of the stems. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.

Stipa avenacioides plays a role in stabilizing soil in arid and semi-arid environments.
