Scutellaria brevifolia

Scutellaria brevifolia, commonly known as the short-leaved skullcap, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are ovate and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Scutellaria brevifolia is often found in moist, shaded woodlands and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
