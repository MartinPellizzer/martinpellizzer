Stelis trianae

Stelis trianae, commonly known as the Trianas orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow blossoms that bloom during the dry season. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Stelis trianae is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
