Solanum nigrum var. judaicum

Solanum nigrum var. judaicum, commonly known as the black nightshade, is a variant of the common black nightshade plant.

It is a herbaceous annual or biennial plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa. The plant features small, greenish-white flowers that develop into dark purple to black berries. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like tomatoes and potatoes.
