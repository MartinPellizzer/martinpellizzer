Stellaria latifolia

Stellaria latifolia, commonly known as white starwort, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is a low-growing annual herb that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, white, five-petaled flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it its common name. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a hairy texture.

Stellaria latifolia thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.
