Sonchus massauensis

Sonchus massauensis, commonly known as the African sowthistle, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to sub-Saharan Africa and is characterized by its hairy, sprawling growth habit. The plant features alternate, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. Its yellow flowers are arranged in composite heads, typical of the daisy family.

Sonchus massauensis is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
