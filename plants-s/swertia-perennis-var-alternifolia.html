Swertia perennis var. alternifolia

Swertia perennis var. alternifolia, commonly known as the alternate leaf gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has alternate, lance-shaped leaves. It produces small, tubular flowers that attract pollinators such as butterflies and bees.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional medicinal uses in some cultures.
