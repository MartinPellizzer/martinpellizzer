Setaria viridis var. prostrata

Setaria viridis var. prostrata, commonly known as prostrate green millet, is a low-growing grass species native to Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant is characterized by its prostrate growth habit, often spreading along the ground. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in poor soil conditions.

This variety is sometimes used in ornamental gardening or as a ground cover due to its hardy nature.
