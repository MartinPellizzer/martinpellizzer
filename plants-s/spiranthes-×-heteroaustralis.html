Spiranthes × heteroaustralis

Spiranthes × heteroaustralis, commonly known as the hybrid lady's tresses, is a perennial orchid native to North America.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Spiranthes australis and Spiranthes romanzoffiana. The plant produces dense, spiral-shaped inflorescences with pale yellow to white flowers that bloom in late summer. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as wetlands and forest floors.

The botanical features of this species include its rhizomatous growth habit and its ability to thrive in nutrient-poor soils.
