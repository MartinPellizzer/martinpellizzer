Swertia yunnanensis

Swertia yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan swertia, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its slender stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest understories.

Swertia yunnanensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
