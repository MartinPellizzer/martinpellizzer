Swertia wolfgangiana

Swertia wolfgangiana, commonly known as the Himalayan gentian, is a perennial herb native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and alpine meadows, thriving in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in a fine, white pubescence.

Swertia wolfgangiana is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
