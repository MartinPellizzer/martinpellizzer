Senecio guicanensis

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Its flowers are yellow and occur in clusters, attracting various pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Senecio guicanensis is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
