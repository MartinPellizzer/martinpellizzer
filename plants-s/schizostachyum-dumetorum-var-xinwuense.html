Schizostachyum dumetorum var. xinwuense

Schizostachyum dumetorum var. xinwuense, commonly known as the Xinwu bamboo, is a variant of the dwarf bamboo species native to China.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and slender, greenish-brown culms that are often used in traditional Chinese medicine. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with pale yellow to white flowers, which are typical of the grass family. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, providing a dense foliage that makes it suitable for ornamental purposes.

This variety is also valued for its ability to thrive in shaded environments, making it a popular choice for garden landscaping.
