Stellaria venezuelana

Stellaria venezuelana, commonly known as the Venezuelan starwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly Venezuela, and is characterized by its small, star-shaped white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are slender and often branched.

Stellaria venezuelana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
