Stelis oligoblephara

Stelis oligoblephara, commonly known as the orchid with few eyes, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, loose substrate for optimal growth.

Stelis oligoblephara is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
