Stelis buxiflora

Stelis buxiflora, commonly known as the boxleaf orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage that resembles the leaves of boxwood plants. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media to prevent root rot.

Stelis buxiflora is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
