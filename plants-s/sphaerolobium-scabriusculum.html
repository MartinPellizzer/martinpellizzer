Sphaerolobium scabriusculum

Sphaerolobium scabriusculum, commonly known as the rough-leaved burr legume, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small shrub characterized by its woody stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers followed by distinctive, spiny pods that enclose its seeds. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological role, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
