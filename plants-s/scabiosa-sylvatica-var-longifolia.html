Scabiosa sylvatica var. longifolia

Scabiosa sylvatica var. longifolia, commonly known as the long-flowered wood scabious, is a perennial herb native to Europe.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of small, pink or white flowers with five petals. The leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture. This plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, loamy soils.

It is often found in woodland edges and meadows, contributing to the biodiversity of its habitat.
