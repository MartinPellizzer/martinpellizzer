Sedum × affomarcoi

Sedum × affomarcoi, commonly known as the Affomarco stonecrop, is a hybrid succulent plant native to the southwestern United States.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped yellow flowers in the spring. It is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance requirements.

Its botanical characteristics make it well-suited for USDA hardiness zones 8 through 10.
