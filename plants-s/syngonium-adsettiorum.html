Syngonium adsettiorum

Syngonium adsettiorum, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a tropical species native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, arrowhead-like leaves that vary in color from bright green to shades of red and variegated patterns. The plant is a climbing vine that can grow up to several meters in height, often requiring a support structure for optimal growth. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Syngonium adsettiorum is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
