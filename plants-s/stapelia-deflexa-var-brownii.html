Stapelia deflexa var. brownii

Stapelia deflexa var. brownii, commonly known as the Brown's Stapelia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and unique, waxy flowers. The plant produces yellowish-green to orange flowers that are often covered in a sticky, resinous substance. It is a slow-growing species that thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water.

Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for collectors and succulent enthusiasts.
