Scutellaria weishanensis

Scutellaria weishanensis, commonly known as the Weishan skullcap, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions.

Scutellaria weishanensis is valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
