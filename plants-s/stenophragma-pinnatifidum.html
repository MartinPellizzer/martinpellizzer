Stenophragma pinnatifidum

Stenophragma pinnatifidum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are often deeply divided, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by small, berry-like fruits. It thrives in moist, temperate climates and is often found in forested areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in native habitats.
