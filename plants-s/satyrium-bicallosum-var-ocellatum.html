Satyrium bicallosum var. ocellatum

Satyrium bicallosum var. ocellatum, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, including China and Japan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or rocky slopes. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, while the flowers display a distinctive pattern of spots and markings.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
