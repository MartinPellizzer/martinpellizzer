Santolina pinnata

Santolina pinnata, commonly known as silver thread, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its finely divided, silvery-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Santolina pinnata is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

It is also valued for its aromatic properties and is often used in herbal remedies.
