Styrax andinus

Styrax andinus, commonly known as the Andean styrax, is a species of flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces a resinous sap, which is used in traditional medicine and for making incense. Its leaves are typically elliptical and have a glossy surface.

Styrax andinus is often found in moist, shaded environments at higher elevations.
