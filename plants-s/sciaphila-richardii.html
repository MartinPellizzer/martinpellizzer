Sciaphila richardii

Sciaphila richardii, commonly known as the shadow orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high levels of atmospheric moisture to survive.
