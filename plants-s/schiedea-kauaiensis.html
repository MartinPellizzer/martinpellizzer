Schiedea kauaiensis

Schiedea kauaiensis, commonly known as the Kauai starling or Kauai starflower, is an endemic species native to the Hawaiian island of Kauai.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its compact, cushion-like growth habit. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming during the cooler months. Schiedea kauaiensis thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories at higher elevations.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species.
