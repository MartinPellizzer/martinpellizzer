Stachys sideritis

Stachys sideritis, commonly known as mountain betony, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or limestone soils. The plant features upright stems with opposite leaves that are often hairy and lance-shaped. Its flowers are clustered in whorls and are usually blue or purple in color.

Stachys sideritis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
