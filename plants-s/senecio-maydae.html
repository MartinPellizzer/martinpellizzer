Senecio maydae

Senecio maydae, commonly known as the yellow starbursts, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features yellow ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers, creating a starburst-like appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Senecio maydae is also known for its ability to produce a milky sap, which can be toxic if ingested.
