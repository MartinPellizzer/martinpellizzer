Stellaria divnogorskajae var. pilosa

Stellaria divnogorskajae var. pilosa, commonly known as the hairy starwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, star-shaped white flowers and hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. Native to the mountainous regions of Eastern Europe, this plant thrives in rocky and sandy soils. It typically grows in alpine or subalpine habitats, where it withstands cold temperatures and strong winds.

The species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and limited distribution.
