Schollia chinensis

Schollia chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese schollia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Schollaceae and is characterized by its small, showy flowers that typically bloom in the spring. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Schollia chinensis is often grown in gardens for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for.
