Silene cossoniana

Silene cossoniana, commonly known as the Cosson's campion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family, characterized by its showy, pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in sunny environments. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often branched.
