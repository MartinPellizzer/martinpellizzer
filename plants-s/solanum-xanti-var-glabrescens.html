Solanum xanti var. glabrescens

Solanum xanti var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smoothleaf nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and smooth, oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. Its fruit is a globose berry that turns red when mature, containing several seeds.

It is often found in arid and semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils.
