Scutellaria daghestanica

Scutellaria daghestanica, commonly known as the Caucasian skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the Caucasus region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous areas.

Scutellaria daghestanica is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
