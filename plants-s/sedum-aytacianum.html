Sedum aytacianum

Sedum aytacianum, commonly known as the Aytacian stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Turkey.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a vibrant touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it a hardy choice for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value, it is a popular plant among gardeners in arid regions.
