Stelis melanoxantha

Stelis melanoxantha, commonly known as the black-stemmed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stem and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate white or pale yellow flowers with a distinctive, spurred lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered an endangered species in many regions.
