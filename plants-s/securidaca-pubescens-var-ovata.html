Securidaca pubescens var. ovata

Securidaca pubescens var. ovata, commonly known as the white thorn or white securidaca, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters tall, characterized by its thorny branches and compound leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, often followed by long, slender seed pods. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to semi-arid to arid environments.

The species is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
