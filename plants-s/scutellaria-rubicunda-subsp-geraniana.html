Scutellaria rubicunda subsp. geraniana

Scutellaria rubicunda subsp.

geraniana, commonly known as the purple skullcap, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves.

The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks. Scutellaria rubicunda subsp.

geraniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
