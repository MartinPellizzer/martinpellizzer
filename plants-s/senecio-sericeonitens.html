Senecio sericeonitens

Senecio sericeonitens, commonly known as the silky senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, hairy leaves that give it a soft, velvety appearance. The plant produces clusters of tiny, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its delicate foliage and unique appearance, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
