Specklinia involuta

Specklinia involuta, commonly known as the wrinkled orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit and is often found growing on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features long, narrow leaves that are often covered in a distinctive, waxy coating. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically in shades of white and green, with a subtle fragrance.

Specklinia involuta is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
