Sideritis kuegleriana

Sideritis kuegleriana, commonly known as Kuegler's sainfoin, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers sunny to partially shaded environments.

Sideritis kuegleriana is often found in rocky or grassy habitats at elevations ranging from 800 to 2,000 meters.
