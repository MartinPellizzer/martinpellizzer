Schizachyrium obliquiberbe

Schizachyrium obliquiberbe, commonly known as blue grama, is a native grass species of North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green blades and tufted seed heads that turn brown in the fall. This perennial grass is drought-tolerant and thrives in sandy or loamy soils. It plays an important role in stabilizing soils and providing forage for wildlife.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for native plant gardens.
