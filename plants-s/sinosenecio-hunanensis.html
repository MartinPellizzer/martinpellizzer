Sinosenecio hunanensis

Sinosenecio hunanensis, commonly known as the Hunan sinosenecio, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to China, specifically the Hunan province, and is characterized by its slender stems and daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories or rocky slopes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are small and clustered in composite heads.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
