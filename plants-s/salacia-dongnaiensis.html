Salacia dongnaiensis

Salacia dongnaiensis, commonly known as the Dong Nai salacia, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Salaceae and is characterized by its woody stems and dense foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in racemes, often blooming during the rainy season. Its leaves are typically elliptical with a smooth surface and a slightly waxy texture.

Salacia dongnaiensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
