Scabiosa tomentosa f. giennensis

Scabiosa tomentosa f. giennensis, commonly known as the Giennensis carpet flower, is a compact, low-growing perennial plant native to southern Europe.

It features dense clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early summer, creating a carpet-like appearance. The plant has hairy, gray-green foliage that is both drought-tolerant and heat-resistant. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its unique floral structure and ornamental value make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a charming, hardy plant.
