Scrophularia caesia

Scrophularia caesia, commonly known as the blue scrophularia, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in woodland edges or along stream banks. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are usually hairy, and its flowers are tubular with a distinctive blue or violet hue. It blooms from late spring to early summer, producing nectar that attracts various pollinators.

Scrophularia caesia is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
