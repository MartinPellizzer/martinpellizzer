Stelis superbiens

Stelis superbiens, commonly known as the noble stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with a distinctive lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Stelis superbiens is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
