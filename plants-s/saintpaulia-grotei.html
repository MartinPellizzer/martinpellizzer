Saintpaulia grotei

Saintpaulia grotei, commonly known as the Giant African Violet, is a striking member of the Gesneriaceae family native to Tanzania.

It is characterized by its large, velvety leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in a range of colors including pink, white, and purple. Saintpaulia grotei is popular among collectors for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
