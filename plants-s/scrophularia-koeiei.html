Scrophularia koeiei

Scrophularia koeiei, commonly known as Japanese scrophularia, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae and is characterized by its erect, herbaceous stems and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or meadow habitats.

Scrophularia koeiei is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
