Senecio doryphoroides

Senecio doryphoroides, commonly known as the African Daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping. It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.

Senecio doryphoroides is also known for its resilience to drought and mild frost.
