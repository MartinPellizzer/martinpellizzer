Syzygium tawahense

Syzygium tawahense, commonly known as the Tawah apple.

It is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family, native to the tropical rainforests of Borneo. The plant is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom during the wet season. Its fruit, resembling a small apple, is edible and has a sweet, slightly tart flavor.

Syzygium tawahense is often found in lowland and hill forest areas, growing as a shrub or small tree.
