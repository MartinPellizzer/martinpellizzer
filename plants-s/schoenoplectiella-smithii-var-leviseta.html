Schoenoplectiella smithii var. leviseta

Schoenoplectiella smithii var. leviseta, commonly known as the slender bulrush, is a wetland plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems that grow in dense stands in shallow water or moist soils. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish or brownish in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often floating on the water surface.

This variety is adapted to tolerate fluctuating water levels and is an important component of wetland ecosystems.
