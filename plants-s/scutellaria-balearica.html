Scutellaria balearica

Scutellaria balearica, commonly known as the Balearic skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Scutellaria balearica is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
