Syringa robusta

Syringa robusta, commonly known as the Chinese lilac, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, clustered flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, lance-shaped leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil.

Syringa robusta is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
