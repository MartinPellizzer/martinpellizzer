Sparaxis grandiflora var. liliago

Sparaxis grandiflora var. liliago, commonly known as the Orange lily or the Yellow lily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange or yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has strap-like, grassy leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Sparaxis grandiflora var. liliago is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
