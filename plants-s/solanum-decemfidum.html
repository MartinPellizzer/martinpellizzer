Solanum decemfidum

Solanum decemfidum, commonly known as the devil's fig, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades, and is characterized by its distinctive, spiny fruit. Its flowers are usually yellow or greenish-yellow, and the fruit is a fleshy, berry-like structure that turns red when mature.
