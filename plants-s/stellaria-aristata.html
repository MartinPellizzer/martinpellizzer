Stellaria aristata

Stellaria aristata, commonly known as the starwort, is a flowering plant native to the northern hemisphere.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, star-shaped white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and stream banks. Stellaria aristata has slender, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

It blooms from late spring to early summer, attracting various pollinators with its delicate floral display.
