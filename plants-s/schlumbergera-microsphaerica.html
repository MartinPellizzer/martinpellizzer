Schlumbergera microsphaerica

Schlumbergera microsphaerica, commonly known as the mini holiday cactus, is a species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its compact, rounded stems that form a dense, bushy shape. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, pink, and white during the holiday season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Schlumbergera microsphaerica is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and festive blooming display.
