Striga ledermannii

Striga ledermannii, commonly known as the desert striga, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Africa.

It is a small annual herb with a fibrous root system adapted to survive in dry environments. The plant produces small, white or pale purple flowers, and its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in desert or semi-arid habitats.

Striga ledermannii plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small desert organisms.
