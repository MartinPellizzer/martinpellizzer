Senecio yungningensis

Senecio yungningensis, commonly known as the Yungning Senecio, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous regions, preferring well-drained soils and full sunlight. Its foliage is usually narrow and grass-like, with small, serrated leaves.

The flowers are yellow, with a central disk surrounded by ray florets, contributing to its ornamental appeal.
