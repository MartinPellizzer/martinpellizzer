Sisyrinchium guatemalense

Sisyrinchium guatemalense, commonly known as the Guatemalan blue-eyed grass, is a herbaceous perennial native to Central America.

Sisyrinchium guatemalense is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
