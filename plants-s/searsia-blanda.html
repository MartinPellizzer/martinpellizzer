Searsia blanda

Searsia blanda, commonly known as the silver cotoneaster, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and small, glossy, dark green leaves that turn reddish in autumn. The plant produces clusters of white flowers in spring, followed by bright red berries that are an important food source for birds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Searsia blanda is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate coastal conditions.
