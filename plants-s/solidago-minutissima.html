Solidago minutissima

Solidago minutissima, commonly known as the dwarf goldenrod, is a small flowering plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as wet meadows and pond edges. The plant features delicate, narrow leaves and produces tiny, bright yellow flowers in late summer. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes many other flowering plants.

Solidago minutissima plays an important role in supporting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
