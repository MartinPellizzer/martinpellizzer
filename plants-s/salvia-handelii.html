Salvia handelii

Salvia handelii, commonly known as the Mexican blue sage, is a perennial shrub native to Mexico.

It features slender, upright stems and produces striking violet-blue flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.

Salvia handelii is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
