Skapanthus Oreophilus

Skapanthus oreophilus is a rare and enigmatic plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded, forested areas. This species is known for its striking, tubular flowers that bloom in late spring. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Due to habitat loss, Skapanthus oreophilus is now considered endangered.
