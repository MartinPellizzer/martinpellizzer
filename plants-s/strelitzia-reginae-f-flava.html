Strelitzia reginae f. flava

Strelitzia reginae f. flava, commonly known as the yellow strelitzia, is a striking hybrid of the royal strelitzia.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow bracts that contrast with its deep green, sword-shaped leaves. The plant is native to South Africa and thrives in warm, humid climates. It produces striking orange flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
