Saussurea leptolepis

Saussurea leptolepis, commonly known as the slender Chinese yarrow, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stem and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a dense, spike-like inflorescence. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool, dry climates.

Due to its medicinal properties, it is sometimes harvested for traditional herbal use.
