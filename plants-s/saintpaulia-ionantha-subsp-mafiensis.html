Saintpaulia ionantha subsp. mafiensis

Saintpaulia ionantha subsp.

mafiensis, commonly known as the Mafiensis African violet, is a compact, low-growing herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of Africa. It features dark green, velvety leaves that are heart-shaped and covered in a fine, white pubescence.

The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, purple, and white, typically blooming throughout the year under proper care. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
