Solenangis longipes

Solenangis longipes, commonly known as the long-stemmed spider orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, spider-like appearance with long, slender stems and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in areas with moderate to high rainfall. Its flowers, which bloom in spring, feature a combination of white and green hues with intricate patterns.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
