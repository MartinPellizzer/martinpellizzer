Synaedrys jordanae

Synaedrys jordanae, commonly known as the Jordanian starflower, is a rare flowering plant native to the arid regions of Jordan.

It is characterized by its small, star-shaped white petals and adapted to thrive in harsh, dry environments. The plant has a compact growth habit with short, hairy stems and narrow leaves. It blooms during the cooler months, typically between November and February.
