Stylidium notabile

Stylidium notabile, commonly known as the showy sun-orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family native to Australia.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers with red markings, which are highly attractive to pollinators. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils in arid or semi-arid regions. Its unique structure includes a short, erect stem and a single, showy flower that opens in full sun.

Stylidium notabile is adapted to survive in harsh environments, showcasing remarkable resilience and beauty.
