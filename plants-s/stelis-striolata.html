Stelis striolata

Stelis striolata, commonly known as the striped orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a sympodial epiphyte, typically growing on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a slender, wiry stem with numerous nodes and produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white and purple. Its leaves are linear and arranged in two rows along the stem.

Stelis striolata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
