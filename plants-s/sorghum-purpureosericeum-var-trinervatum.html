Sorghum purpureosericeum var. trinervatum

Sorghum purpureosericeum var. trinervatum, commonly known as the purple-leafed sorghum, is a striking ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features tall, slender stalks with vibrant purple and silver hues, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. The plant produces small, feathery panicles that add a delicate texture to its appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, requiring minimal water once established.

Its unique coloration and graceful form make it a valuable addition to both wild and cultivated settings.
