Senecio milanjianus

Senecio milanjianus, commonly known as the Milanjian's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers sunny to partially shaded environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and hairy, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Senecio milanjianus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
