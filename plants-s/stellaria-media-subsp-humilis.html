Stellaria media subsp. humilis

Stellaria media subsp.

humilis, commonly known as common chickweed, is a low-growing annual herb native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa. It features prostrate or ascending stems that are often covered in fine, soft hairs.

The leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with a pointed tip and a hairy surface, and they are arranged alternately along the stem. Small white flowers with five petals are borne in clusters, typically blooming from spring to early summer.

This plant thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in gardens, lawns, and along pathways.
