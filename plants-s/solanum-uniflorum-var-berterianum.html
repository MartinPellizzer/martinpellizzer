Solanum uniflorum var. berterianum

Solanum uniflorum var. berterianum, commonly known as the Berterianum nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant typically features small, solitary flowers that are often white or purple in color. Its leaves are usually oval-shaped with a slightly hairy surface.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
