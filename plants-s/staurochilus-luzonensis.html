Staurochilus luzonensis

Staurochilus luzonensis, commonly known as the Luzon star orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to the Philippines.

It features striking, star-shaped flowers with a vivid purple and yellow coloration. The plant has a compact growth habit and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air and surrounding substrates.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments typical of tropical rainforests.
