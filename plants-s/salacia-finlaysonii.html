Salacia finlaysonii

Salacia finlaysonii, commonly known as the Bornean salacia, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It belongs to the Salaceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and vibrant yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a glossy appearance.

Salacia finlaysonii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral display.
