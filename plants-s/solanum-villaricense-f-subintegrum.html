Solanum villaricense f. subintegrum

Solanum villaricense f. subintegrum, commonly known as the Villarica nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It features dark green, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and its flowers are typically white or pale blue, clustered in small inflorescences. The plant produces small, round berries that are yellow when mature, containing numerous seeds. It is adapted to cool, moist environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Botanically, it is notable for its unique leaf morphology and potential medicinal properties.
