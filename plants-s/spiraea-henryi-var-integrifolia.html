Spiraea henryi var. integrifolia

Spiraea henryi var. integrifolia, commonly known as the Chinese spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features slender, arching branches that bear clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers in early summer. The leaves are ovate to elliptic, with smooth margins, giving it a neat appearance. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
