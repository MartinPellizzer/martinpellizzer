Sideritis bourgeana

Sideritis bourgeana, commonly known as the Balkan ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant produces small, pale blue or violet flowers that bloom in summer, and its leaves are often covered in fine hairs. Sideritis bourgeana is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional herbal remedies.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.
