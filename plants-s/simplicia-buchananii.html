Simplicia buchananii

Simplicia buchananii, commonly known as the Buchanan's simplicity plant, is a species native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous plant characterized by its simple, undivided leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and have a smooth texture, while its flowers are usually greenish-white in color.

It is valued in traditional medicine for its potential medicinal properties.
