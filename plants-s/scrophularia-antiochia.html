Scrophularia antiochia

Scrophularia antiochia, commonly known as the Antioch scrophularia, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or dry soils, often found in mountainous areas. The plant features upright stems covered in hairy leaves that are lance-shaped and often have a bluish tinge. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, arranged in spikes, and bloom in late spring to early summer.

Scrophularia antiochia is part of the Scrophulariaceae family, known for its medicinal and ornamental value.
