Selinopsis foetida

Selinopsis foetida, commonly known as the stinking nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

The plant is characterized by its dark green, hairy leaves and small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple. Selinopsis foetida is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to thrive in arid conditions.
