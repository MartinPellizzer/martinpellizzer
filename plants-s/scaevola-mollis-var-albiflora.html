Scaevola mollis var. albiflora

Scaevola mollis var. albiflora, commonly known as the White-Flowered Fanflower, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial that produces clusters of small, showy white flowers. The plant features narrow, silver-blue leaves that are typically arranged in a fan-like pattern. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.

The flowers bloom throughout the spring and summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
