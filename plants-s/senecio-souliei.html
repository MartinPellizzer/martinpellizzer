Senecio souliei

Senecio souliei, commonly known as the Soulie's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It features narrow, linear leaves and produces small yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is often cultivated in gardens.
