Securidaca uniflora

Securidaca uniflora, commonly known as the single-flower securidaca, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces yellow flowers in clusters. Its flowers are followed by elongated, woody pods that contain several seeds.

The plant is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, arid environments.
