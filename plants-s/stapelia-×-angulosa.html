Stapelia × angulosa

Stapelia × angulosa, commonly known as the angular stapelia, is a hybrid succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its unique, waxy, and often ribbed stems. The plant produces striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically orange or yellow in color, which bloom in late spring or early summer. Its leaves are usually small, fleshy, and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stems.

Stapelia × angulosa is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for succulent gardens.
