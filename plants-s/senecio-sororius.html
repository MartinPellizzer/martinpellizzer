Senecio sororius

Senecio sororius, commonly known as the African Daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Senecio sororius is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
