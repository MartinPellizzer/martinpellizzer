Stipellaria mollis

Stipellaria mollis, commonly known as the slender stippleleaf, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features simple, elliptical leaves with a distinctive stippling pattern on the upper surface. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Stipellaria mollis is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and delicate floral display.
