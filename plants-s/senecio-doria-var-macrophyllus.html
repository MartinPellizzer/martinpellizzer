Senecio doria var. macrophyllus

Senecio doria var. macrophyllus, commonly known as the large-leaf senecio, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The leaves are typically broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

This plant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage.
