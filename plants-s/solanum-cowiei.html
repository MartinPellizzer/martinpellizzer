Solanum cowiei

Solanum cowiei, commonly known as the Cowie nightshade, is a shrub native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in areas with moderate to high rainfall. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale purple, followed by berries that range in color from yellow to red. Solanum cowiei is part of the nightshade family, which includes many economically important plants.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
