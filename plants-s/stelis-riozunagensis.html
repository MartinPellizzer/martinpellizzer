Stelis riozunagensis

Stelis riozunagensis, commonly known as the Riozunangensis Orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features elongated, lance-shaped leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure and symbiotic relationship with specific mycorrhizal fungi.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
