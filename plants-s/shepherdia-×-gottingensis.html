Shepherdia × gottingensis

Shepherdia × gottingensis, commonly known as the hybrid sea buckthorn, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its yellow-orange berries, which are rich in vitamins and antioxidants. The plant has narrow, gray-green leaves and produces small, yellow flowers in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

The species is also valued for its ecological benefits, providing food for birds and wildlife.
