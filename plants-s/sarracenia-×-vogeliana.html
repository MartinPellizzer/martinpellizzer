Sarracenia × vogeliana

Sarracenia × vogeliana, commonly known as the Vogeliana pitcher plant, is a hybrid carnivorous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its tall, robust growth and distinctive, elongated pitcher-shaped leaves that secrete a sticky liquid to trap insects. The plant's pitchers are typically red or orange with dark veins, creating a striking visual appearance. It thrives in wet, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This species is popular among carnivorous plant enthusiasts due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.
