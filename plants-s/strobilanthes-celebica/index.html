Strobilanthes Celebica

Strobilanthes celebica is a rare and striking flowering plant native to the Indonesian island of Sulawesi. Known for its vibrant purple and yellow blooms, it captivates botanists and nature enthusiasts alike. This species belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is closely related to other strobilanthes plants found in Southeast Asia. Due to its limited habitat and unique appearance, it is considered a conservation concern. Its beauty and ecological significance make it a subject of ongoing scientific study and admiration.
