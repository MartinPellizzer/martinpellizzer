Sisymbrium reticulatum

Sisymbrium reticulatum, commonly known as the nettleleaf shepherd's purse, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive triangular seed pods and hairy, alternate leaves. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is found in various regions across Europe and western Asia. It produces small white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer.
