Senecio munitus

Senecio munitus, commonly known as the yellow star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has slender, branching stems and narrow, grass-like leaves.

Senecio munitus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.
