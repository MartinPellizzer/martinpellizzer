Strempelia articulicymosa

Strempelia articulicymosa, commonly known as the articulated thyme, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its prostrate growth habit and aromatic, small, opposite leaves. The plant produces clusters of pink to purple flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact size and fragrant foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
