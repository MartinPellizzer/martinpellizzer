Stefaninia telephiifolia

Stefaninia telephiifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rosette of silver-gray leaves and bright yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid environments, with succulent leaves that store water. It produces small, wind-dispersed seeds, aiding in its propagation across dry landscapes.

Stefaninia telephiifolia is often found in rocky soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role.
