Stapelia revoluta

Stapelia revoluta, commonly known as the African milkweed, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces striking, lantern-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange with a distinctive, waxy texture. Its leaves are usually small and covered in a fine, powdery coating.

Stapelia revoluta is often cultivated for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
