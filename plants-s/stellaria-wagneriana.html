Stellaria wagneriana

Stellaria wagneriana, commonly known as Wagner's starwort, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It typically features small, star-shaped flowers that are white or pale pink, with five petals arranged radially. The plant has slender, branching stems and small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, rocky or sandy soils and is often found in tundra and alpine habitats.

Stellaria wagneriana is adapted to cold climates and is an important component of the vegetation in its native ecosystems.
