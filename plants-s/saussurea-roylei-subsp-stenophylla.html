Saussurea roylei subsp. stenophylla

Saussurea roylei subsp.

stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leafed snow lotus, is a perennial herb native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas. It is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stem and large, showy blue to purple flowers.

The plant has fibrous roots and is adapted to cold, arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and often covered with a dense layer of fine hairs.

It plays an important role in the alpine ecosystems where it grows.
