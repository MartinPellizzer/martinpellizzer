Sida theezans

Sida theezans, commonly known as the Hawaiian sida, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and dry habitats. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Sida theezans is adapted to the unique ecological conditions of Hawaii and plays a role in local biodiversity.
