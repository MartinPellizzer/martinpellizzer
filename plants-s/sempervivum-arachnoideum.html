Sempervivum arachnoideum

Sempervivum arachnoideum, commonly known as the spider plant or snowy sedum, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe.

It features dense, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green with a powdery coating, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its hardy nature allows it to survive in a variety of climates, including colder regions.
