Sicyos palmatus

Sicyos palmatus, commonly known as the giant dayflower, is a member of the Convolvulaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial vine characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Sicyos palmatus is native to the tropical regions of the Americas and thrives in warm, humid climates.
