Spiraea luxuriosa

Spiraea luxuriosa, commonly known as the Chinese spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features showy, fluffy pink or white flower clusters that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has arching stems and lance-shaped leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Spiraea luxuriosa is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
