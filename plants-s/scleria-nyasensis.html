Scleria nyasensis

Scleria nyasensis, commonly known as the Nyasense bulbous sedge, is a perennial herb native to the eastern African region.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and grass-like plants. The plant is characterized by its bulbous base and narrow, grass-like leaves. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters.

Scleria nyasensis typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.
