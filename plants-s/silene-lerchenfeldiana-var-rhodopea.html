Silene lerchenfeldiana var. rhodopea

Silene lerchenfeldiana var. rhodopea, commonly known as the Rhodopean campion, is a flowering plant native to the Rhodope Mountains in Bulgaria.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer.
