Senecio eruciformis

Senecio eruciformis, commonly known as the Wooly Senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
