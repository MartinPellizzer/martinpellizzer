Senecio glabellus

Senecio glabellus, commonly known as the smoothleaf groundsels, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. Its leaves are generally smooth and glabrous, giving it its common name.

Senecio glabellus is known for its yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer.
