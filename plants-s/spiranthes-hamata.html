Spiranthes hamata

Spiranthes hamata, commonly known as the slender spirea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, spiraling inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wetlands. Its flowers are small, white to pale pink, and bloom in late spring to early summer.

Spiranthes hamata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance as a nectar source for pollinators.
