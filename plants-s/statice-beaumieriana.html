Statice beaumieriana

Statice beaumieriana, commonly known as the sea lavender or coastal statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and its ability to thrive in saline and coastal environments. The plant produces vibrant blue, purple, or white flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. Its foliage consists of narrow, silver-blue leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of salt.

Statice beaumieriana is valued in horticulture for its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal in garden settings.
