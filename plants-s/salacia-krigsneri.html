Salacia krigsneri

Salacia krigsneri, commonly known as the Krigsner's salacia, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Salicaceae, which includes willows and poplars, though it is more closely related to other members of the Salacia genus. The plant is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and elongated, woody stems. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
