Swertia patula

Swertia patula, commonly known as blue gentian, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas and parts of Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged oppositely along the stem.

Swertia patula is valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
