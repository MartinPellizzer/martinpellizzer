Siparuna Lagopus

Siparuna lagopus is a unique and lesser-known plant species native to the rainforests of South America. It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes well-known plants like avocado and cinnamon. Despite its ecological importance, it remains under-studied and often overlooked in conservation efforts.
