Spathoglottis breviscapa

Spathoglottis breviscapa, commonly known as the short-capped orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive, short-capped floral structure. The plant typically produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

Spathoglottis breviscapa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
