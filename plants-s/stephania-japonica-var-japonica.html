Stephania japonica var. japonica

Stephania japonica var. japonica, commonly known as Japanese stephania, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a member of the Menispermaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by distinctive, spiny fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value and medicinal properties have made it a subject of interest in both horticulture and traditional medicine.
