Spatholobus palawanensis

Spatholobus palawanensis, commonly known as the Palawan legume, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers.

Its root system plays a vital role in soil enrichment and ecological balance in its native habitat.
