Spadactis flava

Spadactis flava, commonly known as the yellow spadix plant, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its striking yellow spadix and a broad, heart-shaped leaf. The plant is native to tropical regions of Africa and thrives in warm, humid environments. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are enclosed within the spathe.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant color and unique foliage.
