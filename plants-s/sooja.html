Sooja

Sooja, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a tropical aquatic plant native to Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom on long, slender stems rising from water. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that float on the water's surface. Sooja is known for its symbolic significance in various cultures, representing purity and spiritual enlightenment.

Its botanical name is *Nelumbo nucifera*, and it thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.
