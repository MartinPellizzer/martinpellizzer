Synaedrys pulchra

Synaedrys pulchra, commonly known as the beautiful spurge, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, ribbed stems and leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water to thrive.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
