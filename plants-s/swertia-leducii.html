Swertia leducii

Swertia leducii, commonly known as the Tibetan gentian, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in cool and moist conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and branched.

Swertia leducii is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Tibetan medicine.
