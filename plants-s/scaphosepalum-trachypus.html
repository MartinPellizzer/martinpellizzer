Scaphosepalum trachypus

Scaphosepalum trachypus, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in moist forest understories.

Due to its delicate structure and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many areas.
