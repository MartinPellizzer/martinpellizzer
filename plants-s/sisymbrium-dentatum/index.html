Sisymbrium Dentatum

Sisymbrium dentatum is a small flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, commonly known as the mustard family. This species is often found in grassy areas and disturbed soils where it thrives in sunny conditions. Its distinguishing features include small white or pale yellow flowers and finely toothed leaves. Despite its modest appearance, it plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and maintaining biodiversity.
