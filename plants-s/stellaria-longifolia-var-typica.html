Stellaria longifolia var. typica

Stellaria longifolia var. typica, commonly known as starwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically features slender, branching stems that are covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers arranged in clusters, giving it a star-like appearance. Its leaves are opposite, narrow, and often have a slightly hairy surface.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and damp meadows.
