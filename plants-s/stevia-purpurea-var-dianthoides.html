Stevia purpurea var. dianthoides

Stevia purpurea var. dianthoides, commonly known as purple leaf stevia, is a perennial herb native to South America.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its vibrant purple foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant is known for its high concentration of steviol glycosides, which are natural sweeteners. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its botanical features make it both aesthetically pleasing and potentially valuable for natural sweetener production.
