Statice spachii

Statice spachii, commonly known as sea lavender, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and its profusion of small, showy blue to purple flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens. Its flowers are often used in floral arrangements due to their long-lasting nature.

Statice spachii is also valued for its ability to tolerate salty air and sandy soils, which makes it well-suited for maritime environments.
