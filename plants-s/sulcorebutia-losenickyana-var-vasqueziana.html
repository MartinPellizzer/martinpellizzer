Sulcorebutia losenickyana var. vasqueziana

Sulcorebutia losenickyana var. vasqueziana, commonly known as the Vasquez's Sulcorebutia, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular shape and dense clusters of small, ribbed stems. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring, which are typically borne on short, erect stems. It has a slow growth rate and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability to harsh environments, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
