Solanum orthacanthum

Solanum orthacanthum, commonly known as the African nightshade, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by bright red, fleshy berries. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a rough texture.

Solanum orthacanthum belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and contains toxic alkaloids in its berries and other parts.
