Sedum dasyphyllum var. rifanum

Sedum dasyphyllum var. rifanum, commonly known as the Rif sedum, is a succulent plant native to the rocky areas of North Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
