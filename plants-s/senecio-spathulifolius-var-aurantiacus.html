Senecio spathulifolius var. aurantiacus

Senecio spathulifolius var. aurantiacus, commonly known as the Orangeleaf Senecio, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often tinged with orange or red hues, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique coloration and compact growth habit make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
