Solanum yabari

Solanum yabari, commonly known as the Yabari nightshade, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into berries containing numerous seeds. It is typically found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and open woodlands.

Due to its toxic compounds, it is not recommended for consumption or cultivation.
