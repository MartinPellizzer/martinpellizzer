Streptocarpus fenestra-dei

Streptocarpus fenestra-dei, commonly known as the window leaf streptocarpus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves with a distinctive white window-like marking on the upper surface. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, violet-blue flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. It is a perennial herb that thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its unique foliage and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and rockeries.
