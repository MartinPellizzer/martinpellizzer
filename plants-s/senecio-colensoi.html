Senecio colensoi

Senecio colensoi, commonly known as the Colenso's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky, alpine environments, thriving in cool and moist conditions. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
