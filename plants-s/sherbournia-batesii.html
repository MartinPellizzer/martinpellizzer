Sherbournia batesii

Sherbournia batesii, commonly known as the Cape York lily, is a flowering plant native to northern Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, showy white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in wetland or swampy areas, preferring moist, acidic soils. It has narrow, strap-like leaves that are often submerged or partially submerged in water.

Sherbournia batesii is an important component of the wetland flora and plays a role in supporting local wildlife.
