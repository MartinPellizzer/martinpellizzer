Senecio solandri

Senecio solandri, commonly known as the Solander's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by small, fluffy seeds.

Senecio solandri is often used in rock gardens due to its hardy nature and ornamental value.
