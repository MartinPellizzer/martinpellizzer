Sideritis giennensis

Sideritis giennensis, commonly known as the Iberian ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its slender stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in Mediterranean climates.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
