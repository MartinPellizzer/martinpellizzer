Silene otites var. calcicola

Silene otites var. calcicola, commonly known as the limestone campion, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Europe and Asia.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family, characterized by its showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in calcareous soils, often found on rocky or limestone outcrops. It has small, lance-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soils in harsh environments.
