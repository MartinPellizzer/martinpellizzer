Senecio doronicum subsp. lagascanus

Senecio doronicum subsp.

lagascanus, commonly known as the Lagascanus groundsel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid environments.

The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. It is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
