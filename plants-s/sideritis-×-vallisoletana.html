Sideritis × vallisoletana

Sideritis × vallisoletana, commonly known as the Spanish heath, is a perennial herb native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs.

Sideritis × vallisoletana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
