Stellaria decipiens var. angustata

Stellaria decipiens var. angustata, commonly known as narrowleaf starwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, angular stems and small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The leaves are typically narrow and opposite, with a slightly hairy surface. Native to regions of Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in moist, calcareous soils in meadows and woodland edges.

This plant is often found in open habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
