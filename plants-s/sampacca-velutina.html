Sampacca velutina

Sampacca velutina, commonly known as the velvet leaf.

It is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its thick, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant belongs to the Malvaceae family, which includes cotton and hibiscus. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Sampacca velutina is also valued as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage.
