Stauropsis philippinensis

Stauropsis philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine star orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers are usually brightly colored, with a complex structure that includes a spurred lip.

Stauropsis philippinensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
