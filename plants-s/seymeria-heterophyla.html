Seymeria heterophyla

Seymeria heterophyla, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. Seymeria heterophyla thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has also been used in traditional medicine.
