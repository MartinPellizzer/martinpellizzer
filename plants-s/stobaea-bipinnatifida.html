Stobaea bipinnatifida

Stobaea bipinnatifida, commonly known as the giant fern, is a striking member of the Cyatheaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, feather-like fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant has a robust, unbranched trunk that supports its massive canopy of compound leaves. Its fronds are bipinnately divided, giving it a delicate yet imposing appearance.

This species is native to tropical regions and thrives in humid, shaded environments.
