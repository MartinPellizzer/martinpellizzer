Senecio bathiaei

Senecio bathiaei, commonly known as Bathia's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers a dry, sunny environment. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Senecio bathiaei is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in arid conditions.
