Sinobaijiania smitinandii

Sinobaijiania smitinandii, commonly known as the Smitinandii cycad, is a rare and ancient plant species native to China.

It belongs to the Cycadaceae family and is characterized by its stout, unbranched trunk and feathery, palm-like leaves. The plant produces cones, with male and female cones occurring on separate plants, a feature typical of cycads.
