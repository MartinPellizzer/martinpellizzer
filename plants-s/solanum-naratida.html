Solanum naratida

Solanum naratida, commonly known as the Naratida nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and peppers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are ovate with a rough texture, and it produces small, white or pale yellow flowers.

The fruit is a globose berry that turns red when mature, containing numerous seeds.
