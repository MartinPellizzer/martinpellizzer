Saccharum cylindricum var. contractum

Saccharum cylindricum var. contractum, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed sugarcane.

This variant is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stalks and reduced leaf surface area compared to other sugarcane varieties. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and is often cultivated for its high sucrose content. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires a warm climate with ample sunlight.

Its botanical features make it suitable for both commercial sugar production and ornamental gardening.
