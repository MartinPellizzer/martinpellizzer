Sambucus intermedia f. neomexicana

Sambucus intermedia f. neomexicana, commonly known as the New Mexico elderberry, is a shrub native to the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, white flowers.
