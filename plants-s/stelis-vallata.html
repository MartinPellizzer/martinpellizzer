Stelis vallata

Stelis vallata, commonly known as the valley stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a few leaves and produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white and pink. Its roots are specialized for absorbing nutrients from the surrounding soil.

Stelis vallata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or tropical gardens.
