Siphocampylus hypsophilus

Siphocampylus hypsophilus, commonly known as the highland bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

The plant features showy blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
