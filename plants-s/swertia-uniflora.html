Swertia uniflora

Swertia uniflora, commonly known as the single-flower swertia, is a perennial herb native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its slender stems and small, blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Swertia uniflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional medicinal uses in some regions.
