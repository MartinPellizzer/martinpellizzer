Stelis fissurosa

Stelis fissurosa, commonly known as the crevice orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in rocky or crevice habitats, where it anchors itself with its fibrous roots. Its leaves are usually narrow and arranged in a rosette pattern, helping to conserve moisture.

Stelis fissurosa is a terrestrial orchid that prefers partial shade and well-drained soil conditions.
