Sedum platyphyllum var. longisepalum

Sedum platyphyllum var. longisepalum, commonly known as the long-sepal sedum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, typical of the Crassulaceae family. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its unique floral structure and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
