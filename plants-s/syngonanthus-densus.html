Syngonanthus densus

Syngonanthus densus, commonly known as the dense thimbleweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and swamps.
