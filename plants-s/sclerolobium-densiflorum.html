Sclerolobium densiflorum

Sclerolobium densiflorum, commonly known as the denseflower acacia, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, feathery foliage and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has compound leaves with numerous small, needle-like leaflets, and its woody, thorny stems provide structural support. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions with a Mediterranean climate.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a garden plant.
