Stelis cypripedioides

Stelis cypripedioides, commonly known as the cypripedioid orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid environments. The plant features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with narrow, strap-like leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive, spurred lip that is characteristic of the Orchidaceae family.

Stelis cypripedioides is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouse settings due to its specific humidity and light requirements.
