Senecio tenuifolius

Senecio tenuifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and small, yellow composite flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in open grasslands. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.

Senecio tenuifolius is also noted for its ability to thrive in arid conditions, making it a resilient species in its native environment.
