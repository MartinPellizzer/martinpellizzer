Sobralia alstroemerioides

Sobralia alstroemerioides, commonly known as the yellow sobralia, is a striking tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a bold splash of color to gardens and greenhouses. The plant has pendulous, hairy leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Sobralia alstroemerioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its showy, long-lasting blooms.
