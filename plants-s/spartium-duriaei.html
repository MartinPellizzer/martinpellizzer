Spartium duriaei

Spartium duriaei, commonly known as the Kashmir spartia, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in racemes, followed by narrow, bean-like pods. Spartium duriaei is typically found in rocky and alpine environments, thriving at high altitudes.

Its botanical features make it an important species in the study of alpine flora and ecological adaptations.
