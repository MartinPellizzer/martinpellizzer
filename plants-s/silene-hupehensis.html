Silene hupehensis

Silene hupehensis, commonly known as the Chinese catchfly, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pinkish-purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has lance-shaped leaves. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Silene hupehensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
