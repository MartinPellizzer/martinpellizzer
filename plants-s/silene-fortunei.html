Silene fortunei

Silene fortunei, commonly known as the Fortune's campion, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or alpine environments. The plant features showy pink or red flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, adapting to harsh climatic conditions.

Silene fortunei is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable growing conditions.
