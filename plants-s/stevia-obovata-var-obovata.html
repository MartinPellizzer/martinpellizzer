Stevia obovata var. obovata

Stevia obovata var. obovata, commonly known as the broad-leaf stevia, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its lanceolate to obovate leaves, which are typically green and have a slightly serrated margin. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the summer months. Stevia obovata var. obovata is known for its high concentration of steviol glycosides, making it a popular natural sweetener.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often used in both ornamental and culinary applications.
