Santolina marchii

Santolina marchii, commonly known as the Spanish santolina, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features silver-gray foliage with a distinctive aromatic scent, and its small, yellow flowers bloom in summer. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Its compact form and ornamental value make it a popular choice for landscaping.

Santolina marchii is also valued for its use in essential oil extraction and traditional herbal medicine.
