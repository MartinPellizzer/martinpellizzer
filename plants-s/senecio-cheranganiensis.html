Senecio cheranganiensis

Senecio cheranganiensis, commonly known as the African Daisy, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and has a rosette of basal leaves. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, with a central disk surrounded by ray florets.

Senecio cheranganiensis is also known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
