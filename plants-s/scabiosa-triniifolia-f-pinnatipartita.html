Scabiosa triniifolia f. pinnatipartita

Scabiosa triniifolia f. pinnatipartita, commonly known as the ferny scabious, is a perennial flowering plant characterized by its finely divided, fern-like foliage.

It produces clusters of small, pink or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders. Its delicate appearance and extended blooming period make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The foliage's unique pinnate structure adds visual interest and supports a variety of pollinators.
