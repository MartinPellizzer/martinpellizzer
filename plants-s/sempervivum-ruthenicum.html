Sempervivum ruthenicum

Sempervivum ruthenicum, commonly known as the Ruthenian houseleek, is a succulent plant native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It is characterized by its rosettes of fleshy, thick leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces pink or purple flowers on tall stems during the summer months. It is well adapted to rocky and alpine environments due to its ability to store water.

Sempervivum ruthenicum is a hardy species that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil.
