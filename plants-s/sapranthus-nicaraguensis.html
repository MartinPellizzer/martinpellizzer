Sapranthus nicaraguensis

Sapranthus nicaraguensis, commonly known as the Nicaraguan bellflower, is a flowering plant native to Central America, specifically Nicaragua.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
