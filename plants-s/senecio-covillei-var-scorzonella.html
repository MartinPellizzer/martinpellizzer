Senecio covillei var. scorzonella

Senecio covillei var. scorzonella, commonly known as the Coville's senecio, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and aromatic foliage. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid environments.
