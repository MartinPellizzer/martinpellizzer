Satureja hieronymi

Satureja hieronymi, commonly known as Hieronymi thyme, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils, thriving in sunny environments. Its small, pink or white flowers appear in summer, followed by small, oval-shaped berries.

The leaves are often used in cooking for their subtle, thyme-like fragrance.
