Sparaxis tricolor var. versicolor

Sparaxis tricolor var. versicolor, commonly known as the rainbow lily, is a bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, tubular flowers that come in a range of colors, including pink, yellow, and orange, creating a vibrant display. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It is known for its drought tolerance and ability to bloom reliably in mild climates.

The foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge in early spring.
