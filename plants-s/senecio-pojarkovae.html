Senecio pojarkovae

Senecio pojarkovae, commonly known as the Pojarkov's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It features narrow, linear leaves and produces small yellow flowers arranged in a cluster.
