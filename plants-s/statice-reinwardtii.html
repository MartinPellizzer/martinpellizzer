Statice reinwardtii

Statice reinwardtii, commonly known as the blue statice, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, spike-like inflorescences. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Statice reinwardtii is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.
