Saussurea kunthiana var. filicifolia

Saussurea kunthiana var. filicifolia, commonly known as the fernleaf saussurea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features a robust root system and produces tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. The leaves are deeply lobed and resemble fern foliage, giving the plant its common name. It blooms with yellow to orange composite flowers in the summer months.

This species is adapted to high-altitude environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes.
