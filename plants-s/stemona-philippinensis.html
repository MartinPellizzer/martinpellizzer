Stemona philippinensis

Stemona philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine stemona, is a perennial herb native to the Philippines.

It features a rhizomatous stem system that allows it to spread and form dense colonies. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters. Its roots are valued for their medicinal properties, containing alkaloids used in traditional remedies.

The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in forest understories.
