Swertia pumila

Swertia pumila, commonly known as the Himalayan gentian, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is a member of the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and alpine environments, thriving in cold and moist conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in a fine layer of hairs.

Swertia pumila is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
