Sideritis briquetiana f. dissitiflora

Sideritis briquetiana f. dissitiflora, commonly known as the scattered-flowered ironwort, is a flowering plant species native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, hairy leaves. Its flowers are typically pale blue or violet, arranged in whorls along the stem.

It thrives in rocky or dry habitats and is often found in mountainous areas.
