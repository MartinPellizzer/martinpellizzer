Saxifraga xiaojinensis

Saxifraga xiaojinensis, commonly known as the Xiaojin saxifrage, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves and clusters of small, white or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine or rocky habitats, thriving in cool, moist environments. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often found in botanical gardens and conservation areas.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
