Senna Acuta

Senna acuta is a strikingly beautiful shrub native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia. Known for its bright yellow flowers and feathery leaves, it adds a vibrant touch to any landscape. This plant thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive appearance. Despite its beauty, it can be toxic if ingested, making it important to handle with care.
