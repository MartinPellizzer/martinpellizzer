Scrophularia nankinensis

Scrophularia nankinensis, commonly known as Chinese toadflax, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces showy, two-lipped flowers in shades of purple or pink. Its root system is fibrous and often used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Scrophularia nankinensis is part of the Scrophulariaceae family, known for its ornamental and medicinal value.
