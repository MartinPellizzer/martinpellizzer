Stemmacantha uniflora

Stemmacantha uniflora, commonly known as the single-flowered stemmacantha, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stem and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pale yellow. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate or elliptical in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.
