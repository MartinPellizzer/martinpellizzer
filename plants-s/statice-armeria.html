Statice armeria

Statice armeria, commonly known as sea thrift, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and its clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from pink to white. The plant thrives in salty, well-drained soils and is often found in dunes and rocky shores. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and its stems are sturdy, allowing it to withstand harsh coastal conditions.

Statice armeria is also valued for its ornamental use in gardens and its ability to stabilize coastal soils.
