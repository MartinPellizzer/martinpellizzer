Scilla fallax

Scilla fallax, commonly known as the spring squill, is a perennial bulbous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It produces clusters of bell-shaped, blue to purple flowers in early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge from a bulbous base. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Scilla fallax is often used in rock gardens and as a ground cover due to its ornamental value.
