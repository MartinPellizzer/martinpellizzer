Senecio tmoleus

Senecio tmoleus, commonly known as the Cape mountain daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and hairy, narrow leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It produces small, wind-dispersed seeds, which aid in its propagation across its native range.

Senecio tmoleus is often found in mountainous regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
