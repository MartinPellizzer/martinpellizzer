Senecio argyreus

Senecio argyreus, commonly known as the silver sword, is a striking succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, silver-green leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a frosty appearance. The plant produces tall, branched stems that bear yellow flowers in the spring. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
