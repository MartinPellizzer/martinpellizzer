Sphaeritis subulata

Sphaeritis subulata, commonly known as the slender scaleleaf, is a small, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and compact, rounded growth habit. The leaves are typically narrow, pointed, and covered in a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments. It produces small, yellowish flowers during the spring and summer months.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appearance.
