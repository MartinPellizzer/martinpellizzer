Schippia concolor

Schippia concolor, commonly known as the smoothleaf schippia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Acanthaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, where it typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and glossy, with a striking contrast between its deep green foliage and vibrant purple flowers. Schippia concolor is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.

Its blooming period extends throughout the growing season, adding color and interest to its surroundings.
