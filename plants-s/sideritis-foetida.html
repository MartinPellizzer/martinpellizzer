Sideritis foetida

Sideritis foetida, commonly known as the stinking rock rose, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its hairy stems and small, pale purple or pink flowers. Its leaves are aromatic and often used in traditional herbal remedies.

Sideritis foetida is adapted to rocky and arid environments, showcasing its resilience to harsh conditions.
