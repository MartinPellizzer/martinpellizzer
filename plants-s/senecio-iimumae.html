Senecio iimumae

Senecio iimumae, commonly known as the Hottentot's beard, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy, upright stems and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun exposure. It is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.

Senecio iimumae is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
