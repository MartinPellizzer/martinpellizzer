Stelis lesliegarayi

Stelis lesliegarayi, commonly known as the Leslie Garay's orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often clustered in a raceme. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Stelis lesliegarayi thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.
