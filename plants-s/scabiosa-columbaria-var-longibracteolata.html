Scabiosa columbaria var. longibracteolata

Scabiosa columbaria var. longibracteolata, commonly known as the long-bract scabious, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features slender stems that are often hairy, and it produces clusters of small, pink or purple flowers with a distinctive, flattened shape. The leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy surface. This variety is characterized by its elongated bracts, which give it its specific name.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and meadows.
