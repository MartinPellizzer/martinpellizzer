Sarracenia × catesbaei

Sarracenia × catesbaei, commonly known as the flame pitcher, is a hybrid carnivorous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a hybrid between Sarracenia leucophylla and Sarracenia rubra, resulting in a plant with unique characteristics. The plant produces striking red to orange-colored pitchers that are often adorned with white veins, which help in attracting prey. Its leaves are typically narrow and elongated, forming a spiral arrangement that aids in trapping insects.

Sarracenia × catesbaei thrives in wet, acidic soils and requires high humidity and bright light to flourish.
