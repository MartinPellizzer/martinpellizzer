Swertia mussotii

Swertia mussotii, commonly known as Indian gentian, is a perennial herb native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and have a slightly hairy surface.

Swertia mussotii is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional Ayurvedic and Tibetan medicine.
