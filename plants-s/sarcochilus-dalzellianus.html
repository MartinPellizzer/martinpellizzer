Sarcochilus dalzellianus

Sarcochilus dalzellianus, commonly known as the Dalzell's sarcochilus, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It features a short, stocky rhizome with a dense cluster of fleshy, dark green leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green, typically blooming in spring. Its flowers are borne on a single, erect stem and have a distinctive spurred lip.

Sarcochilus dalzellianus is often found in heathlands and open forests, thriving in well-drained soils.
