Sabazia multiradiata

Sabazia multiradiata, commonly known as the radiate sabazia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, yellow-orange ray flowers surrounding a central disk of smaller florets. It typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils.

Sabazia multiradiata is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant and distinctive blooms.
