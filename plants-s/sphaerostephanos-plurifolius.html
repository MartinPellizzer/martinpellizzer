Sphaerostephanos plurifolius

Sphaerostephanos plurifolius, commonly known as the many-leaved globe-stephanos, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, globular inflorescence composed of numerous small, white to pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub with woody stems and small, elliptical leaves that are arranged in a compact, bushy form. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
