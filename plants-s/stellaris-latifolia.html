Stellaris latifolia

Stellaris latifolia, commonly known as broadleaf starflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features showy, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring, typically in shades of white, pink, or purple. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

Stellaris latifolia is often found in woodland edges, wet meadows, and stream banks.
