Scandix bulbocastanum

Scandix bulbocastanum, commonly known as the bulbocastanum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers. The plant produces distinctive, bulbous fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun exposure.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
