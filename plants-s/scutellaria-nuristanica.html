Scutellaria nuristanica

Scutellaria nuristanica, commonly known as the Nuristan skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of northern Pakistan and Afghanistan.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. It thrives in high-altitude environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

Due to its potential medicinal properties, it has attracted interest from botanists and herbalists alike.
