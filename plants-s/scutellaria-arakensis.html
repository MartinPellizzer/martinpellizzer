Scutellaria arakensis

Scutellaria arakensis, commonly known as the Arakensis skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color, arranged in whorls along the stem. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a hairy texture.

Scutellaria arakensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
