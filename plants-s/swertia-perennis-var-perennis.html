Swertia perennis var. perennis

Swertia perennis var. perennis, commonly known as the perennial gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged oppositely along the stem.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
