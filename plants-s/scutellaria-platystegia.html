Scutellaria platystegia

Scutellaria platystegia, commonly known as the broad-stemmed skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and has lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. Its small, tubular flowers are usually blue or purple, arranged in whorls along the stem.

Scutellaria platystegia is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
