Scrophularia formosana

Scrophularia formosana, commonly known as the Formosan figwort, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Scrophularia formosana is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
