Stizolobium bennettii

Stizolobium bennettii, commonly known as Bennett's legume, is a shrub native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes many leguminous plants known for their nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. Its fruit is a legume pod that contains several seeds.

Stizolobium bennettii is often found in arid or semi-arid environments, adapting well to poor soil conditions.
