Senecio cacaliifolius var. cebennensis

Senecio cacaliifolius var. cebennensis, commonly known as the Cebenna Senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white to pale blue petals. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

This variety is noted for its compact growth habit and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens.
