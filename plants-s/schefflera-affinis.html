Schefflera affinis

Schefflera affinis, commonly known as the umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of large, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellowish-green flowers followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.

Schefflera affinis is valued for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
