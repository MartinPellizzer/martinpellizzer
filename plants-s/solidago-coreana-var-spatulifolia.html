Solidago coreana var. spatulifolia

Solidago coreana var. spatulifolia, commonly known as the Korean goldenrod, is a perennial herb native to Korea.

It features upright, branching stems that bear clusters of small, bright yellow flowers. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine, soft hairs. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant color and ability to attract pollinators.
