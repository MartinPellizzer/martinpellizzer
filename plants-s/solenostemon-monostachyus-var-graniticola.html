Solenostemon monostachyus var. graniticola

Solenostemon monostachyus var. graniticola, commonly known as the granite stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to rocky habitats in southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, adapted to store water and reduce transpiration. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or purple, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
