Sasa cernua var. nebulosa

Sasa cernua var. nebulosa, commonly known as the clouded sasa, is a variant of the sasa bamboo, native to eastern Asia.

It is a clump-forming grass-like plant that typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and stream banks. The plant features slender, arching culms with a bluish-green hue, and its leaves are narrow and often have a waxy coating. It is characterized by its distinctive variegated foliage, which gives it its common name.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appearance and is often used in horticultural settings for erosion control and landscape design.
