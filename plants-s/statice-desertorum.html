Statice desertorum

Statice desertorum, commonly known as desert statice, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, spike-like inflorescences. The plant produces small, brightly colored flowers, typically in shades of pink, red, or purple, which attract pollinators. Its leaves are narrow and fleshy, adapted to conserve water in harsh environments.

Statice desertorum is often used in dried floral arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms and ornamental value.
