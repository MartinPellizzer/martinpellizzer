Salvia bidens

Salvia bidens, commonly known as ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of small, violet or blue flowers. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Salvia bidens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
