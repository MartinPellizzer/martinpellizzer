Stemodia multifida

Stemodia multifida, commonly known as the multifid stemodia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered leaves that are deeply lobed, giving the plant its common name. The flowers are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in dense, terminal clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.
