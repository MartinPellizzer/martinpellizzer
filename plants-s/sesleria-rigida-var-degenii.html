Sesleria rigida var. degenii

Sesleria rigida var. degenii, commonly known as the Degen's rigid sedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, pale green to brownish flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Sesleria rigida var. degenii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
