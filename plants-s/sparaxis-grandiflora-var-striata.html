Sparaxis grandiflora var. striata

Sparaxis grandiflora var. striata, commonly known as the stripe-flowered sparaxis, is a perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, tubular flowers that are typically bright yellow with red or orange markings, arranged in dense spikes. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often striped or streaked with contrasting colors. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, usually in late winter to early spring.
