Silene oreina

Silene oreina, commonly known as the alpine campion, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its pink or white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils and cool climates. Silene oreina has slender stems and small, lance-shaped leaves, and it is often found in open, sunny habitats at high altitudes.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens that mimic its natural environment.
