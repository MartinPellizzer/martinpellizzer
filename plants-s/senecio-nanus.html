Senecio nanus

Senecio nanus, commonly known as dwarf African daisy, is a small, flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant yellow flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of only a few inches, making it ideal for rock gardens or as a container plant. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves, and it thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight.

Senecio nanus is drought-tolerant and requires minimal maintenance once established.
