Senecio cacaliaster var. radiatus

Senecio cacaliaster var. radiatus, commonly known as the Radiate Senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant features showy yellow ray florets surrounding a central disk of smaller, tubular flowers. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Its botanical structure includes narrow, hairy leaves and a branched stem that supports its distinctive floral display.
