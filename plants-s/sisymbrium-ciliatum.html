Sisymbrium ciliatum

Sisymbrium ciliatum, commonly known as the hoary cress, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is an annual or biennial herb that typically grows in disturbed soils, such as roadsides and fields. The plant features small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes, and its leaves are covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a frosted appearance.
