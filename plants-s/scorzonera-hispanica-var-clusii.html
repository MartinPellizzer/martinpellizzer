Scorzonera hispanica var. clusii

Scorzonera hispanica var. clusii, commonly known as Spanish scorzonera, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy, upright stems and yellow flowers. The plant produces a tuberous root that is often used in traditional medicine and cuisine. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.
