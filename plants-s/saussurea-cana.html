Saussurea cana

Saussurea cana, commonly known as the Chinese yarrow or white saussurea, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, branched stems and pale yellow to white flowers. The plant produces small, narrow leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in alpine and arctic environments, often growing at high elevations with poor soil conditions.

Saussurea cana is valued in traditional medicine for its purported therapeutic properties.
