Stelis rhomboidea

Stelis rhomboidea, commonly known as the rhomboid stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features a short, erect stem with several leaves arranged in a rosette pattern. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with a distinctive rhomboid shape that gives it its common name.

The orchid thrives in humid environments and requires well-drained, loose substrate for optimal growth.
