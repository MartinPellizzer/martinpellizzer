Symplocos hualiensis

Symplocos hualiensis, commonly known as the Yunnan waxleaf holly, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, glossy evergreen foliage that remains throughout the year. The plant produces small, white, bell-shaped flowers in clusters, which are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Symplocos hualiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate urban environments.
