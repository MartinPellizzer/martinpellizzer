Specklinia dryadum

Specklinia dryadum, commonly known as the dryad's speckled orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a small, epiphytic plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. The plant features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with narrow, linear leaves that are often speckled with dark spots. Its flowers are typically white or pale yellow, with a distinctive, intricate pattern on the petals.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, loose soil for optimal growth.
