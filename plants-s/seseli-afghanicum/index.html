Seseli Afghanicum

Seseli afghanicum is a rare and lesser-known plant native to the mountainous regions of Afghanistan. It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes familiar plants like carrots and parsley. This species is characterized by its delicate white flowers and finely divided leaves. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered vulnerable to environmental changes. Studying Seseli afghanicum offers insights into the biodiversity of high-altitude ecosystems.
