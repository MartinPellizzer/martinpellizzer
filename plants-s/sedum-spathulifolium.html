Sedum spathulifolium

Sedum spathulifolium, commonly known as the broad-leaf stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features fleshy, broad leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish or reddish tinge. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Sedum spathulifolium is drought-tolerant and requires minimal maintenance once established.
