Senecio ganpinensis

Senecio ganpinensis, commonly known as the Ganpin's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Senecio ganpinensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
