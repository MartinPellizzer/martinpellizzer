Senecio × decipiens

Senecio × decipiens, commonly known as the hybrid groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow-orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows as a low, spreading shrub with narrow, linear leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens despite its potential invasiveness in certain regions.
