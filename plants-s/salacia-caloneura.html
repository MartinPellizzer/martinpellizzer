Salacia caloneura

Salacia caloneura, commonly known as the slender salacia, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Salaceae and is characterized by its climbing habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, with five petals and a prominent central column.

Salacia caloneura is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
