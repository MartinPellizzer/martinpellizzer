Sasa paniculata var. harae

Sasa paniculata var. harae, commonly known as the Harae bamboo, is a clumping bamboo native to Japan.

It is characterized by its slender, erect culms that are often covered with a whitish bloom. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in ornamental gardening and landscape design.
