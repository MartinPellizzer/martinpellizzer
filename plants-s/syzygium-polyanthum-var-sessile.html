Syzygium polyanthum var. sessile

Syzygium polyanthum var. sessile, commonly known as the sessile apple or yellow apple, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia, Malaysia, and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its small, round, yellow fruits that are typically harvested when ripe. It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and edible fruit.

The leaves are simple, opposite, and have a glossy surface, while the flowers are small and fragrant.
