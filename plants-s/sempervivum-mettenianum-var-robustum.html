Sempervivum mettenianum var. robustum

Sempervivum mettenianum var. robustum, commonly known as the Chocolate Soldier, is a succulent plant native to the Pyrenees region.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with a reddish or bronze hue, especially under full sun. The leaves are usually arranged in a compact, upright formation, giving the plant a distinctive, almost armored appearance. It produces upright, branched flower stalks that bear clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple.

This hardy plant is well-suited for rock gardens and alpine settings, thriving in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight.
