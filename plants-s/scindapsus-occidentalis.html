Scindapsus occidentalis

Scindapsus occidentalis, commonly known as the silver vine, is a tropical evergreen vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its waxy, dark green leaves with prominent silver veins, which give it its distinctive appearance. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.

Scindapsus occidentalis can be propagated through stem cuttings, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
