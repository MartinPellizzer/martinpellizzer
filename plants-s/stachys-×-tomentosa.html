Stachys × tomentosa

Stachys × tomentosa, commonly known as velvetleaf hemp.

It is a hybrid plant in the mint family, Lamiaceae, characterized by its soft, fuzzy leaves that give it a velvety texture. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and produces small, pale blue to purple flowers. It is native to the eastern United States and thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Its hairy foliage and compact growth make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
