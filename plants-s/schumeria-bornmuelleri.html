Schumeria bornmuelleri

Schumeria bornmuelleri, commonly known as the pink dwarf orchid, is a small, flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking pink or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, fragrant blooms during the warmer months.

Schumeria bornmuelleri is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
