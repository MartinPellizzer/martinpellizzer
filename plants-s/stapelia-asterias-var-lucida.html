Stapelia asterias var. lucida

Stapelia asterias var. lucida, commonly known as the star-shaped stapelia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant has thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are adapted to store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. It produces its flowers during the warmer months, often in late spring or early summer.

The varietal form 'lucida' is distinguished by its particularly bright and glowing appearance, enhancing its ornamental value.
