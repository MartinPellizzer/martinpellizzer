Schistocarpha calzadana

Schistocarpha calzadana, commonly known as the Calzadana thistle, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe, particularly in Spain and southern France. The plant is characterized by its spiny, upright stems and large, showy purple to pink flower heads. Its leaves are typically gray-green with a rough, hairy texture.

Schistocarpha calzadana is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
