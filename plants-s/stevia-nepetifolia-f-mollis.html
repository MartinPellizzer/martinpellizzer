Stevia nepetifolia f. mollis

Stevia nepetifolia f. mollis, commonly known as the velvet leaf stevia, is a variant of the stevia plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves that are often used as a natural sweetener. The plant is a perennial shrub that thrives in warm, humid climates. Its leaves contain high levels of steviol glycosides, which are responsible for its intense sweetness.

It is widely cultivated for both ornamental and culinary purposes.
