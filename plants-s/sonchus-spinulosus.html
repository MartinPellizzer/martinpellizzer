Sonchus spinulosus

Sonchus spinulosus, commonly known as prickly lettuce, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in fields, roadsides, and wastelands. It has a taproot system and produces seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Botanically, it belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
