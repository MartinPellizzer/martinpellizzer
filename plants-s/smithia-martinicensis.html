Smithia martinicensis

Smithia martinicensis, commonly known as the Martinique starling or Martinique starfruit, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean.

It is a small, flowering shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers that are often visited by pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a smooth texture with a slightly glossy appearance.

The plant is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its attractive foliage and floral display.
