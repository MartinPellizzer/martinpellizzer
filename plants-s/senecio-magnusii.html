Senecio magnusii

Senecio magnusii, commonly known as Magnusii's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. It produces daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to yellow, often with a central disc.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
