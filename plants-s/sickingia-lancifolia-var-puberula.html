Sickingia lancifolia var. puberula

Sickingia lancifolia var. puberula, commonly known as the velvety sickingia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically a deep green with a soft, velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
