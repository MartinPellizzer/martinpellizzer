Senecio ruwenzoriensis

Senecio ruwenzoriensis, commonly known as the Ruwenzori senecio, is a striking plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Ruwenzori Mountains in Uganda and the Democratic Republic of the Congo.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and distinctive foliage. The plant features narrow, sword-shaped leaves with a silvery underside, adapted to the cool, moist conditions of its mountainous habitat. It produces yellow daisy-like flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a vibrant touch to its alpine environment.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical studies.
