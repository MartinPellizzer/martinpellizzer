Scaevola obovata

Scaevola obovata, commonly known as the fairy fan flower, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has narrow, obovate leaves that are often hairy to the touch. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Scaevola obovata is a popular choice for gardens due to its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
