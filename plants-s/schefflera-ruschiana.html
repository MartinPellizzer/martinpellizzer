Schefflera ruschiana

Schefflera ruschiana, commonly known as the Schultze's umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, umbrella-like arrangement of leaves, which are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage.

Schefflera ruschiana is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
