Statice urgellensis

Statice urgellensis, commonly known as the Urgellensis sea lavender, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, often resembling those of other statice species. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas or dry, sunny environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and used in floral arrangements.
