Serruria florida

Serruria florida, commonly known as the Cape rose, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are narrow and often have a serrated edge, giving the plant its scientific name.

Serruria florida is well adapted to arid environments and thrives in full sun.
