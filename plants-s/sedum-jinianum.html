Sedum jinianum

Sedum jinianum, commonly known as the Chinese stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to China.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple, blooming in summer. It is often used in rock gardens and as a ground cover due to its low maintenance and ornamental value.

Sedum jinianum prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
