Senecio paberensis

Senecio paberensis, commonly known as the Paber's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It features slender stems and small, daisy-like flowers that range in color from yellow to white.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species.
