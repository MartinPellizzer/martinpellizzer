Stachys foucauldiana

Stachys foucauldiana, commonly known as the purple flowered hedgehog.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, belonging to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of tubular, purple to violet flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture.

Stachys foucauldiana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant color and unique appearance.
