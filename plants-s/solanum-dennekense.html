Solanum dennekense

Solanum dennekense, commonly known as the Dennekens nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its hairy stems and alternate leaves. The plant produces small, white or purple flowers that are typically clustered in the axils of the leaves. Its fruit is a globose berry that turns red when mature, containing several seeds.

Solanum dennekense is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
