Sinapidendron rupestre var. gymnocalyx

Sinapidendron rupestre var. gymnocalyx, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its upright stems and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slight pubescence on the underside.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in rock gardens or as a houseplant.
