Spathiphyllum beccarii

Spathiphyllum beccarii, commonly known as the Beccari's peace lily, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves that are broad and lance-shaped. The plant produces elegant, white spathes that enclose small, greenish-white flowers. It thrives in low-light conditions and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Spathiphyllum beccarii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
