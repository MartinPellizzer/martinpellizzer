Schismatoglottis crypta

Schismatoglottis crypta, commonly known as the hidden schismatoglottis, is a rare and enigmatic member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, particularly in the Philippines, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Due to its elusive nature and limited distribution, it is considered a challenging subject for horticultural cultivation.
