Scorzonera safievii

Scorzonera safievii, commonly known as the Turkmenian scorzonera, is a perennial plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and basal leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers with ray florets, typically blooming in the summer months. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Scorzonera safievii is noted for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
