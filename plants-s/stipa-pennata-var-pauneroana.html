Stipa pennata var. pauneroana

Stipa pennata var. pauneroana, commonly known as the Pauneroana feathergrass, is a grass species native to southern South America.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces dense, cylindrical spikelets that are often adorned with silky, feathery bristles. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in arid environments.
