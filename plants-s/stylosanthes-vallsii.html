Stylosanthes vallsii

Stylosanthes vallsii, commonly known as the yellow stylo, is a perennial legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a deep root system, which helps it thrive in poor soils and arid conditions. It is often used for forage in livestock farming due to its high nutritional value.

Additionally, it plays a role in soil nitrogen fixation, making it beneficial for sustainable agriculture.
