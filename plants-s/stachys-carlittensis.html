Stachys carlittensis

Stachys carlittensis, commonly known as the Carlitt's hedge nettle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or dry soils, often found in coastal areas. The plant features opposite, hairy leaves and produces small, clustered flowers with purple or pink petals. Its blooming period occurs during the spring and early summer, attracting various pollinators.

Stachys carlittensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
