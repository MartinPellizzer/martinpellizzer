Salvia chinensis

Salvia chinensis, commonly known as Chinese sage, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces striking violet or blue flowers in whorled clusters, which attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. Its aromatic leaves and showy blooms make it a popular choice in ornamental gardening.

Salvia chinensis is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
