Sigmatostalix peruviana

Sigmatostalix peruviana, commonly known as the Peruvian star-twig, is a rare and unique plant native to the Andean region of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, star-shaped twigs and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool, moist conditions.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
