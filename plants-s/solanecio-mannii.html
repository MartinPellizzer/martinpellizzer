Solanecio mannii

Solanecio mannii, commonly known as the African bluebell, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in grasslands and along riverbanks, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are upright and branching.

Solanecio mannii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
