Scabiosa rufescens f. elata

Scabiosa rufescens f. elata, commonly known as the tall scabious, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that can grow up to 1 meter in height, making it a striking addition to gardens. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy and low-maintenance choice for gardeners.
