Spathoglottis wariana

Spathoglottis wariana, commonly known as the Bornean orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical rainforests of Borneo.

It is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and produces striking, showy inflorescences with brightly colored flowers. The plant typically has lance-shaped leaves and is often found in shaded, moist environments. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
