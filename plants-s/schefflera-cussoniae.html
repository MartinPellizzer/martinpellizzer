Schefflera cussoniae

Schefflera cussoniae, commonly known as the Cussonia schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of palmate leaves, which are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Schefflera cussoniae is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor environments.
