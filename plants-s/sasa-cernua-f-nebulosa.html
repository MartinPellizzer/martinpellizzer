Sasa cernua f. nebulosa

Sasa cernua f. nebulosa, commonly known as the clouded fern sasa, is a variant of the common sasa plant.

It is a clump-forming, evergreen perennial that belongs to the grass family (Poaceae). The plant is characterized by its dense, mat-like growth habit and its distinctive, pale, cloud-like appearance on the underside of its leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in temperate forests.

This fern sasa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural settings for ground cover.
