Solanum tenuisectum

Solanum tenuisectum, commonly known as the slenderleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant produces berries that are generally green when immature and turn red when ripe. It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers.

Solanum tenuisectum is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
