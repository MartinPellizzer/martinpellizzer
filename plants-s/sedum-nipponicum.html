Sedum nipponicum

Sedum nipponicum, commonly known as Japanese stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Japan.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, which help it store water and survive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers in late summer, adding ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Sedum nipponicum is also valued for its low maintenance and ability to tolerate poor soil conditions.
