Stipa donetzica

Stipa donetzica, commonly known as the Donetska needlegrass, is a species of grass native to eastern Europe and western Siberia.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth and long, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in dry, sandy or rocky soils and is often found in steppe and grassland habitats.

Stipa donetzica is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various insects and small animals.
