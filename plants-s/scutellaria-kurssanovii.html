Scutellaria kurssanovii

Scutellaria kurssanovii, commonly known as Kurssanov's blueweed, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants known for their medicinal properties. The plant features square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular blue or violet flowers. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils at high altitudes.

Scutellaria kurssanovii is valued for its potential medicinal uses and is often found in alpine environments.
