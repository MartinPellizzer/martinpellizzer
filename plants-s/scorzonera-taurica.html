Scorzonera taurica

Scorzonera taurica, commonly known as the Taurian scorzonera, is a perennial herb native to the Caucasus region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers with ray florets, typically blooming in late summer. Its tuberous roots are notable for their medicinal properties and are used in traditional herbal remedies.

Scorzonera taurica thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous areas.
