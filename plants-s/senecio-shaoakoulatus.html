Senecio shaoakoulatus

Senecio shaoakoulatus, commonly known as the Shaoakoulatus groundsel, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features narrow, silver-green leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in composite inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it requires careful management to prevent it from becoming invasive.
