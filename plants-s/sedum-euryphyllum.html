Sedum euryphyllum

Sedum euryphyllum, commonly known as the broadleaf stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It is often found growing on rocky slopes or in well-drained soils.

Sedum euryphyllum is a hardy, low-maintenance plant suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
