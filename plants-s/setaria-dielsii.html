Setaria dielsii

Setaria dielsii, commonly known as yellow foxtail, is a flowering plant native to Africa, Asia, and Australia.

It is a member of the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its erect, cylindrical spikelets that are often tinged with yellow. Setaria dielsii is also known for its ability to thrive in arid conditions, making it a common weed in agricultural areas.
