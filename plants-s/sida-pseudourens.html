Sida pseudourens

Sida pseudourens, commonly known as the coastal sida, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows in coastal areas and moist soils. The plant features small, pale yellow to green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate, often with a hairy texture, and it has a fibrous root system.

Sida pseudourens plays a role in stabilizing coastal dunes and providing habitat for various insects.
