Stellaria sanjuanensis

Stellaria sanjuanensis, commonly known as the San Juan starwort, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, star-shaped white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils in open habitats such as meadows and slopes. It has thin, hairy stems and small, oval-shaped leaves.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in some regions.
