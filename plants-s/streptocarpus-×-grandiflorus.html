Streptocarpus × grandiflorus

Streptocarpus × grandiflorus, commonly known as the hybrid streptocarpus, is a hybrid species resulting from crosses between Streptocarpus species.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to white. The plant typically grows as a compact herb with fleshy, dark green leaves. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its blooming period usually occurs in spring and summer, adding vibrant color to gardens and greenhouses.
