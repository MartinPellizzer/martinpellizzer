Serratula caulescens

Serratula caulescens, commonly known as the tall spearthistle, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer, and its leaves are often covered in fine hairs.

Serratula caulescens is known for its upright growth habit and can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters.
