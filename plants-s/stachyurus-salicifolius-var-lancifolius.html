Stachyurus salicifolius var. lancifolius

Stachyurus salicifolius var. lancifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved stachyurus, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features finely serrated, lance-shaped leaves that are typically bright green during the growing season. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, yellowish-white flowers in early spring, which are followed by distinctive, winged fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a suitable choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique foliage and delicate blooms make it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and parks.
