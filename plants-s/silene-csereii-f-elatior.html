Silene csereii f. elatior

Silene csereii f. elatior, commonly known as the alpine campion, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its pink to purple flowers and hairy, succulent leaves adapted to cold and arid environments. This subspecies is typically found in alpine and subalpine regions of Central Asia, including the Tien Shan and Pamir Mountains. The plant has a compact growth habit and produces small, clustered inflorescences.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in high-altitude ecosystems.
