Sansevieria pearsonii

Sansevieria pearsonii, commonly known as the Pearson's snake plant, is a species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, narrow leaves that are typically green with distinct yellow or white margins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-draining soil and bright, indirect light.

Sansevieria pearsonii is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its ability to tolerate low light and infrequent watering.
