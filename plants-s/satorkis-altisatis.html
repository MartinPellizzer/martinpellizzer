Satorkis altisatis

Satorkis altisatis, commonly known as the tall sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland environments.

It typically grows in marshy areas and along the edges of ponds, where it thrives in moist, well-drained soils. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves that are generally dark green and have a slightly rough texture. Its flowering stems produce small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences.

Satorkis altisatis plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic and terrestrial organisms.
