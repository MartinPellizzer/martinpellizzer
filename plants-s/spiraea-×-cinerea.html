Spiraea × cinerea

Spiraea × cinerea, commonly known as the silver weeping spiraea, is a deciduous shrub prized for its ornamental value.

It features gracefully drooping branches that bear clusters of fragrant, white flowers in early summer. The plant has dark green, oval leaves that turn yellow in the fall, adding to its seasonal appeal. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Spiraea × cinerea is often used in landscaping for its elegant form and long blooming period.
