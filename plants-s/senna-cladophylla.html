Senna cladophylla

Senna cladophylla, commonly known as the clustered senna, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, clustered foliage and yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Senna cladophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
