Solanum vieillardii

Solanum vieillardii, commonly known as the Vieillard's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and oval-shaped leaves. It typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in scrub or forested areas.

Due to its limited distribution and specific ecological requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
