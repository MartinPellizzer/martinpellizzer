Sterculia bracteata

Sterculia bracteata, commonly known as the broad-leaved sterculia, is a tropical tree native to regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, pale yellow flowers. The plant produces woody capsules that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.
