Stelis bevilacquana

Stelis bevilacquana, commonly known as the Bevilacqua's stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and clustered inflorescences. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctively shaped lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, airy substrates for optimal growth.

Stelis bevilacquana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
