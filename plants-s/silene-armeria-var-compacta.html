Silene armeria var. compacta

Silene armeria var. compacta, commonly known as the compact stonecrop, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically narrow and densely arranged, forming a compact, cushion-like structure.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine environments.
