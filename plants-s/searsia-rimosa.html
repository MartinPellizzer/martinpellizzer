Searsia rimosa

Searsia rimosa, commonly known as the common glorybower, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Rosaceae and is characterized by its dense, thorny growth habit. The plant produces small, white to yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture.
