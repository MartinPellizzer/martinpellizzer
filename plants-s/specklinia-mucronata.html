Specklinia mucronata

Specklinia mucronata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and produces small, pale green or white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Specklinia mucronata is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
