Symphyotrichum robynsianum

Symphyotrichum robynsianum, commonly known as Robins' weed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as roadsides and meadows. It produces small, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in the fall.

Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a rough texture.
