Salacia oliveriana var. oliveriana

Salacia oliveriana var. oliveriana, commonly known as the Oliver's salacia, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Salaceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and dense foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are typically elliptical with a leathery texture and a glossy surface.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique appearance.
