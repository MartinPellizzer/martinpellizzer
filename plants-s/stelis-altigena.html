Stelis altigena

Stelis altigena, commonly known as the orchid vine, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia and is characterized by its climbing habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant features long, trailing stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hair. Its flowers are typically white or pale yellow, with a distinctive three-lobed lip that attracts pollinators.

Stelis altigena is often cultivated for its ornamental value in greenhouses and as a houseplant.
