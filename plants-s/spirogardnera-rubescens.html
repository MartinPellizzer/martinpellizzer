Spirogardnera rubescens

Spirogardnera rubescens, commonly known as the red-flowered spirogardnera, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its striking red petals and its ability to thrive in moist, well-drained soil. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color.

Spirogardnera rubescens is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
