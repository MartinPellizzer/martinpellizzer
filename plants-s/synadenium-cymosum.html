Synadenium cymosum

Synadenium cymosum, commonly known as the African milk tree, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its milky sap, which is toxic. The plant has small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in cymes, giving it its scientific name. Its leaves are alternate and often have a waxy coating.

Synadenium cymosum is adapted to arid environments and is often used in traditional medicine.
