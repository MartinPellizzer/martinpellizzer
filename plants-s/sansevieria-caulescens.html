Sansevieria caulescens

Sansevieria caulescens, commonly known as the upright sansevieria, is a species of flowering plant in the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its tall, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in late spring or early summer. It is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that thrives in a variety of light conditions and is popular as a houseplant.

Its ability to purify the air makes it a favored choice for indoor environments.
