Stewartia villosa var. kwangtungensis

Stewartia villosa var. kwangtungensis, commonly known as the Kwangtung Stewartia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Theaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces white, fragrant flowers in late spring to early summer, followed by elliptical, red-orange fruits. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
