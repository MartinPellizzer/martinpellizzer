Stenostephanus densiflorus

Stenostephanus densiflorus, commonly known as the denseflower stephanus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. It produces small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
