Sideritis ochroleuca var. denticulata

Sideritis ochroleuca var. denticulata, commonly known as the denticulate ironwort, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing adaptability to harsh environments. Its flowers are small, pink to purple, and clustered in whorls, adding ornamental value.

The species is noted for its medicinal properties, traditionally used in herbal remedies.
