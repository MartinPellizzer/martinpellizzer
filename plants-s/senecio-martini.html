Senecio martini

Senecio martini, commonly known as the coastal senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom during the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and gray-green, often covered in fine hairs.

Senecio martini is adapted to withstand salt spray and strong winds, making it a resilient species in its natural habitat.
