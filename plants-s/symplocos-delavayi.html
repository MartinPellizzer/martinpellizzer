Symplocos delavayi

Symplocos delavayi, commonly known as Delavay's waxleaf, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, glossy evergreen foliage. The plant produces small, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. Its fruits are small, red berries that mature in the fall.
