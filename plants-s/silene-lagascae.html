Silene lagascae

Silene lagascae, commonly known as the mountain catchfly, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe and western Asia.

Silene lagascae is known for its resilience to harsh climatic conditions, making it a hardy species adapted to mountainous terrain.
