Solanum lydgatei

Solanum lydgatei, commonly known as the Lydgate's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically purple or violet in color. Its fruit is a yellowish berry that ripens in late summer.

Solanum lydgatei is often found in coastal and lowland areas, growing in a variety of soil types.
