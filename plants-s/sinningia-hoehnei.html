Sinningia hoehnei

Sinningia hoehnei, commonly known as the Hoehne's gloxinia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant typically grows to a height of 15-30 cm, with thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Sinningia hoehnei is also known for its compact growth habit and ability to bloom throughout the year under proper care.
