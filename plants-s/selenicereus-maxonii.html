Selenicereus maxonii

Selenicereus maxonii, commonly known as the night-blooming cereus, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It is a member of the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stem covered in spines. Its flowers typically bloom only once a year, making it a rare and striking sight.
