Staurogyne dasystachya

Staurogyne dasystachya, commonly known as the broad-stemmed water plant, is a perennial aquatic plant native to the Americas.

It features floating leaves that are typically oval to lance-shaped with a waxy surface. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Its dense growth helps in stabilizing water ecosystems and providing habitat for aquatic life.
