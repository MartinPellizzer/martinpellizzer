Steffensia eximia

Steffensia eximia, commonly known as the desert lily, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky soils and dry habitats. The plant produces striking white or pale purple flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, adapted to conserve water in harsh environments.

Steffensia eximia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
