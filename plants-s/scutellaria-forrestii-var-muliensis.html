Scutellaria forrestii var. muliensis

Scutellaria forrestii var. muliensis, commonly known as the Mulien skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or forested areas and has small, tubular flowers that are usually blue or violet in color. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture, while its flowers are arranged in whorls along the stem.

This variant is noted for its ornamental value and is occasionally cultivated for its unique appearance and potential medicinal properties.
