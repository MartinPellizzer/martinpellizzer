Stellaria strictiflora

Stellaria strictiflora, commonly known as the upright starwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Stellaria strictiflora is a herbaceous perennial that thrives in temperate climates and is often found in the understory of forests.
