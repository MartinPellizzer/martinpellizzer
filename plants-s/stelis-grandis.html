Stelis grandis

Stelis grandis, commonly known as the large-stemmed stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is an epiphytic orchid, typically found growing on trees in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has long, slender stems that can reach several meters in length. Its leaves are elongated and often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

The flowers are usually white or pale pink and are arranged in a spike, producing a striking visual display.
