Saxifraga gouanii var. porophylla

Saxifraga gouanii var. porophylla, commonly known as the rock saxifrage, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Central Asia.

It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments. The plant features dense, mat-forming growth with small, fleshy leaves that help conserve moisture. Its flowers are typically pink or purple, blooming in early summer.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in harsh conditions.
