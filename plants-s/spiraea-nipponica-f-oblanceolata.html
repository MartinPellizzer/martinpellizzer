Spiraea nipponica f. oblanceolata

Spiraea nipponica f. oblanceolata, commonly known as the Japanese spirea, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features small, fragrant white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The leaves are ovate to oblanceolate in shape, with a smooth or slightly serrated margin. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
