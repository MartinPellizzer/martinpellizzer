Scutellaria karkaralensis

Scutellaria karkaralensis, commonly known as the Karkaral skullcup, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Turkey.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Scutellaria karkaralensis is often found in rocky or limestone soils, thriving in well-drained, sunny environments.
