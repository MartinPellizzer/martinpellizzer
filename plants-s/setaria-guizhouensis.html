Setaria guizhouensis

Setaria guizhouensis, commonly known as the Guizhou Panicgrass, is a grass species native to China.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit. The plant typically has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and along riverbanks.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
