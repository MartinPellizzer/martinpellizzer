Schaefferia frutescens

Schaefferia frutescens, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 meter. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that are usually yellow or orange in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Schaefferia frutescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and birds.
