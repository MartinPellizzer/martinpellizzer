Senecio tschabanicus

Senecio tschabanicus, commonly known as the Tschabani's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its botanical features make it a hardy and resilient species adapted to arid environments.
