Syngonium churchillii

Syngonium churchillii, commonly known as the Churchill's arrowhead, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that often have a striking variegation of white, pink, and green. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments and prefers indirect sunlight. Its aerial roots allow it to climb and trail gracefully, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets or shelves.

Syngonium churchillii is relatively easy to care for, requiring consistent moisture and well-draining soil.
