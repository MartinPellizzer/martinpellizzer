Sommera fusca

It is native to the tropical regions of Africa, particularly found in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
