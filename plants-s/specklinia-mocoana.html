Specklinia mocoana

Specklinia mocoana, commonly known as the Mocoan speckled orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, speckled green leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, typically blooming during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
