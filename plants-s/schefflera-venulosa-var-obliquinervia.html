Schefflera venulosa var. obliquinervia

Schefflera venulosa var. obliquinervia, commonly known as the oblique-veined schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with a distinctive oblique venation pattern, which sets it apart from other schefflera species. The plant features a dense crown of feathery foliage, making it an attractive ornamental plant. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
