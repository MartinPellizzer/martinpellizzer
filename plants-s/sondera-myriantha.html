Sondera myriantha

Sondera myriantha, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense, velvety texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that contain numerous tiny seeds.

Sondera myriantha is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
