Stephania brevipedunculata

Stephania brevipedunculata, commonly known as the short-stemmed stephania, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Menispermaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and compound leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by berries that contain the seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional medicine.
