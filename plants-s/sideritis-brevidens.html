Sideritis brevidens

Sideritis brevidens, commonly known as narrowleaf ironwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and clusters of pink or purple flowers. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in sunny, dry environments.

Sideritis brevidens is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
