Saccharum cayennense var. genuinum

Saccharum cayennense var. genuinum, commonly known as the Cayenne cane.

It is a variety of sugarcane native to the Caribbean and parts of South America. This plant is characterized by its tall, robust stalks and high sugar content, making it valuable for commercial sugar production. It thrives in warm, tropical climates with ample sunlight and moist soil.

Its cultivation has played a significant role in the economic history of the regions where it is grown.
