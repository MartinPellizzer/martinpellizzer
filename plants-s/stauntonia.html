Stauntonia

Stauntonia, commonly known as the Chinese hibiscus, is a genus of flowering plants in the mallow family.

These plants are native to Southeast Asia and are characterized by their showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The leaves are typically large, heart-shaped, and have a velvety texture. Stauntonia species are often cultivated for their ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
