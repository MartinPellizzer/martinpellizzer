Scaphosepalum dodsonii

Scaphosepalum dodsonii, commonly known as the Cape Town orchid, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically blooms in spring, producing showy, pale yellow to orange flowers with a distinctive, hood-like structure. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
