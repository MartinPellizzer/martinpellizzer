Sideritis regimontana subsp. edetana

Sideritis regimontana subsp.

edetana, commonly known as the Edetan Heath, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves.

The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments. Its flowering period occurs in late summer, producing small, pale blue or violet flowers in whorls along the stems.

The species is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
