Scalesia atractyloides var. atractyloides

Scalesia atractyloides var. atractyloides, commonly known as the giant lobelias, is a striking plant native to the Galápagos Islands.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet. The plant has a rosette-like growth habit with thick, fleshy leaves that help it retain water in its arid environment. It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is known for its unique adaptations to high-altitude conditions.
