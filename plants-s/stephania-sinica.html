Stephania sinica

Stephania sinica, commonly known as Chinese goldthread, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Menispermaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and compound leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by elliptical berries. Its roots are used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported medicinal properties.

Stephania sinica thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.
