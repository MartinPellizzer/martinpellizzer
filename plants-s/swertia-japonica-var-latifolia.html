Swertia japonica var. latifolia

Swertia japonica var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved swertia, is a herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its lanceolate to ovate leaves and bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting various pollinators.
