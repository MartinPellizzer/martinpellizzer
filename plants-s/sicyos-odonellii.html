Sicyos odonellii

Sicyos odonellii, commonly known as the Mexican bottle gourd, is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is a trailing vine that produces large, bottle-shaped fruits with a smooth, waxy surface. The plant is native to Mexico and parts of Central America, where it thrives in warm, tropical climates.
