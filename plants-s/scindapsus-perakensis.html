Scindapsus perakensis

Scindapsus perakensis, commonly known as the silver vine, is a tropical evergreen vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves that often display a striking silver or variegated pattern. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity to flourish. Its aerial roots allow it to climb and attach to surfaces, making it an ideal choice for hanging baskets or trellises.

Scindapsus perakensis is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular houseplant among indoor gardeners.
