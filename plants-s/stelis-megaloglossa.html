Stelis megaloglossa

Stelis megaloglossa, commonly known as the large-lipped stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stem and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink with a distinctive, elongated lip.

Stelis megaloglossa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
