Satyria nitida

Satyria nitida, commonly known as the slender sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Satyria nitida plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native habitat and provides nectar for local pollinators.
