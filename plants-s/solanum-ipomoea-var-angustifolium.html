Solanum ipomoea var. angustifolium

Solanum ipomoea var. angustifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of South America.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. These flowers give way to berries that are typically yellow or orange when mature.

It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and unique flowering habit.
