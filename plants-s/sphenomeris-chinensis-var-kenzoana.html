Sphenomeris chinensis var. kenzoana

Sphenomeris chinensis var. kenzoana, commonly known as the Kenzoan stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to China.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, clustered leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
