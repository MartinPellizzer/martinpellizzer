Specklinia rabei

Specklinia rabei, commonly known as the Rabei's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, dense growth habit and strikingly colored inflorescences. The plant typically produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, which bloom during the wet season. Its leaves are usually dark green and somewhat fleshy, adapted to store water in its natural habitat.

Specklinia rabei is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
