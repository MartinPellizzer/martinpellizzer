Saxifraga decipiens f. uniflora

Saxifraga decipiens f. uniflora, commonly known as the single-flowered rock saxifrage, is a flowering plant belonging to the Saxifragaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial with fleshy, often succulent leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette. The plant produces a single, showy flower that is usually pink or white, which blooms in late spring or early summer. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to harsh environments.
