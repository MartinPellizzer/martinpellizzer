Syagrus purusana

Syagrus purusana, commonly known as the "palm of the Amazon," is a species of palm native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like leaves that form a dense crown. The plant produces yellowish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, round fruits that are edible. Syagrus purusana thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its ecological importance includes providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
