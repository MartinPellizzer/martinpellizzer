Steris aquatica

Steris aquatica, commonly known as water aster, is a herbaceous plant native to wetland environments.

It features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers during the summer months. Its root system helps stabilize soil in aquatic habitats.

Steris aquatica is often used in wetland restoration projects due to its ability to thrive in moist conditions.
