Skimmia × foremannii var. rogersii

Skimmia × foremannii var. rogersii, commonly known as the variegated skimmia, is a hybrid evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with striking yellow or white variegation, which adds ornamental value to gardens. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that persist into winter. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens or as a container plant.

Its compact growth habit and year-round visual interest make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
