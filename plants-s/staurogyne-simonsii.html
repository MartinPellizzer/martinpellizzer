Staurogyne simonsii

Staurogyne simonsii, commonly known as the dwarf water lettuce, is a floating aquatic plant native to South America.

It has broad, waxy leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern on the water surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. It thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments and is often found in ponds and marshes.

Staurogyne simonsii is valued in aquascaping for its aesthetic appeal and ability to provide habitat for aquatic life.
