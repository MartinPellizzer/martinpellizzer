Schrebera excelsa var. somnifera

Schrebera excelsa var. somnifera, commonly known as the African velvet tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, leathery leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces striking yellow flowers followed by edible, fleshy fruits that are consumed by both humans and animals. Its bark contains compounds with potential medicinal properties, though further research is needed.

The tree thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.
