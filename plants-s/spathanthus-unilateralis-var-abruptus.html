Spathanthus unilateralis var. abruptus

Spathanthus unilateralis var. abruptus, commonly known as the abrupt-flowered spathanthus, is a unique species within the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, unilateral inflorescence, where flowers are clustered on one side of the stem. Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical study.
