Stelis umbelliformis subsp. brevipedunculata

Stelis umbelliformis subsp.

brevipedunculata, commonly known as the short-stemmed stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family. It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its compact growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves.

The plant produces a single, short pedunculate inflorescence bearing several small, white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and relatively rare orchid species in its native habitat.
