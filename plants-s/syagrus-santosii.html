Syagrus santosii

Syagrus santosii, commonly known as the Santos palm, is a species of palm native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils and salt spray. It produces yellowish-pink flowers that develop into coconut-like fruits.

Syagrus santosii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
