Stelis lutea

Stelis lutea, commonly known as the yellow stelis, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant yellow flowers, which bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are linear and arranged in two rows, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Stelis lutea is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
