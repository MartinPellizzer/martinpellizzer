Sykesia paniculata

Sykesia paniculata, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red flowers that appear in panicles during the summer months. The plant has compound leaves with small, ovate leaflets and produces woody pods that contain seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental species.

Sykesia paniculata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in landscaping for its vibrant floral display.
