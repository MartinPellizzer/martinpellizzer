Stapelia variegata var. horizontalis

Stapelia variegata var. horizontalis, commonly known as the horizontal stapeliina, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems that grow horizontally along the ground, often forming dense mats. The leaves are typically small, oval, and covered in a fine, powdery coating. Its flowers are large, star-shaped, and emit a strong odor resembling decaying flesh, a trait common among stapeliina species.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for succulent collectors.
