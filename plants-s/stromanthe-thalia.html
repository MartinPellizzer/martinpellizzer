Stromanthe thalia

Stromanthe thalia, commonly known as the silver queen or white stromanthe, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that display shades of silver, white, and green. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family, known for its ornamental foliage. Stromanthe thalia thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.
