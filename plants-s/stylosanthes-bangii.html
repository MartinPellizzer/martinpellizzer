Stylosanthes bangii

Stylosanthes bangii, commonly known as the African stylo, is a perennial legume native to Africa and widely used in tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow or orange flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has a deep root system that helps it survive in poor soil conditions and drought. Stylosanthes bangii is valued for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, improving soil fertility.

It is often used as a forage crop for livestock due to its high nutritional value and adaptability.
