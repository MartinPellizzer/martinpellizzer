Specklinia gracilenta

Specklinia gracilenta, commonly known as the slender speckled orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are often speckled with darker markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is typically found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated as a houseplant.
