Sauroglossum corymbosum

Sauroglossum corymbosum, commonly known as the Brazilian star orchid, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with multiple stems emerging from a central rhizome. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in warm, humid environments.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
