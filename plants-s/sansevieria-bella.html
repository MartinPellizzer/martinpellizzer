Sansevieria bella

Sansevieria bella, commonly known as the "Bella Snake Plant," is a species of succulent plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with subtle variegation. The plant is highly drought-tolerant, making it well-suited for low-maintenance indoor environments. Its leaves are stiff and fibrous, containing a high concentration of water-storing cells.

Sansevieria bella is also noted for its ability to purify indoor air, contributing to its popularity as a houseplant.
