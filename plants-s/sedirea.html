Sedirea

Sedirea, commonly known as the silverleaf sedum, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of North America.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in dry conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. Its root system is shallow but extensive, enabling it to absorb moisture from the soil efficiently.

Sedirea is often used in xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and drought tolerance.
