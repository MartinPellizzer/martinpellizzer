Sonchus beltraniae

Sonchus beltraniae, commonly known as the Beltrania sowthistle, is a flowering plant native to the Andean region of South America.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and ragworts, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. It is often considered a weed but has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
