Schilleria lucaeana

Schilleria lucaeana, commonly known as the Lucaeana grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to specific regions of South America.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow leaves. Schilleria lucaeana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
