Scrophularia modesta

Scrophularia modesta, commonly known as the modest scrophularia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Scrophulariaceae.

It is native to parts of Europe and is often found in woodland and grassland habitats. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves, which help it adapt to its natural environment.

Scrophularia modesta is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
