Senecio ngoyanus

Senecio ngoyanus, commonly known as the Ngoyi's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in dry, open habitats. It features narrow, linear leaves and produces small yellow flowers arranged in clusters.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
