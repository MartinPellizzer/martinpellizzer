Spiraea splendens var. splendens

Spiraea splendens var. splendens, commonly known as the weeping spiraea, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its cascading clusters of fragrant, white to pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, arching stems that create a graceful, weeping appearance. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and dark green, turning yellow in autumn.

It prefers partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden borders and landscape design.
