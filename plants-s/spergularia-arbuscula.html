Spergularia arbuscula

Spergularia arbuscula, commonly known as the red sand-rat, is a flowering plant in the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically thrives in sandy or gravelly soils, often found in arid or semi-arid regions. Spergularia arbuscula is also known for its ability to colonize disturbed soils and is sometimes considered a weed in certain areas.
