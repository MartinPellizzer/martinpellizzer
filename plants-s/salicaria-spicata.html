Salicaria spicata

Salicaria spicata, commonly known as spike rush, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Cyperaceae.

It is characterized by its cylindrical, grass-like stems and small, spike-like clusters of flowers. Salicaria spicata plays an important ecological role by providing habitat and food for various aquatic and terrestrial organisms.
