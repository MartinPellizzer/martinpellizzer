Sideritis boissieriana

Sideritis boissieriana, commonly known as the Balkan ironwort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are often hairy and have a strong, mint-like scent.

Sideritis boissieriana is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
