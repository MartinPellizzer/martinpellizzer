Serjania lineariifolia

Serjania lineariifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf serjania, is a tropical evergreen tree native to South America.

It belongs to the family Serjaniae and is characterized by its simple, alternate, and often elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. Serjania lineariifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.

Its growth habit and foliage make it a distinctive addition to tropical landscapes.
