Solanum lycioides

Solanum lycioides, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits. Its leaves are covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs, giving it a silvery appearance.

Solanum lycioides contains toxic compounds and is not suitable for consumption by humans or animals.
