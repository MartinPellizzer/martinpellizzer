Synsiphon crociflorus

Synsiphon crociflorus, commonly known as the broad-leaved synsiphon, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many species with characteristic cruciform flowers. The plant features alternate, ovate leaves with serrated edges and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes. Its fruit is a dry pod that splits open to release seeds.
