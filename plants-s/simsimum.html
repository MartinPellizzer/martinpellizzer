Simsimum

Simsimum, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other similar plants. The plant features showy yellow flowers with a distinctive white center, often surrounded by narrow, grass-like leaves. Simsimum is typically found in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual interest.
