Solanum demissum f. stenantherum

Solanum demissum f. stenantherum, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant is characterized by its hairy, silver-gray foliage and small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue. It produces berries that mature to a dark purple color, containing toxic seeds.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique appearance and ability to thrive in various soil conditions.
