Syzygium odoardoi

Syzygium odoardoi, commonly known as the fragrant apple or red apple, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and red, apple-like fruits that are edible. The leaves are elliptical, glossy, and arranged alternately along the stems.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland rainforests.
