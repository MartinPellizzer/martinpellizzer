Stellaria ciliatosepala var. angustifolia

Stellaria ciliatosepala var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf starwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

It features slender, ascending stems that bear opposite, lance-shaped leaves with finely toothed margins. The flowers are small, white or pale pink, and star-like, typically arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems. It thrives in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and stream banks.

The plant is characterized by its delicate appearance and is often found in moist, calcareous soils.
