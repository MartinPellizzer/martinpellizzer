Sitanion cinereum

Sitanion cinereum, commonly known as the silverleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and yellow composite flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a rosette growth habit, with leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Sitanion cinereum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
