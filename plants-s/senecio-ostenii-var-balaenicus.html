Senecio ostenii var. balaenicus

Senecio ostenii var. balaenicus, commonly known as the Balaenicus groundsel, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, woolly foliage that is typically green to blue-gray in color. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
