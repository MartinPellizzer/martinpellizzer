Salacia wardii

Salacia wardii, commonly known as the golden apple or yellow apple, is a species of flowering plant in the family Salicaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, particularly in countries like Thailand and Vietnam. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow, fleshy fruits that resemble small apples. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a smooth texture.

Salacia wardii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique fruit.
