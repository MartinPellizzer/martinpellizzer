Scelochilus tungurahuae

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure and compact growth habit. The plant typically grows in high-altitude cloud forests, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and often display a range of colors, including white, green, and yellow.
