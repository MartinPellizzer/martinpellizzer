Stachys arenaria var. maroccana

Stachys arenaria var. maroccana, commonly known as Moroccan sand betony, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the arid regions of North Africa.

It features small, pale blue to violet flowers that bloom in spikes during the summer months. The plant has hairy, narrow leaves that help reduce water loss in its harsh environment. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
