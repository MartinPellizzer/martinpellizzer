Schoenus filiculmis

Schoenus filiculmis, commonly known as the feather sedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and rushes. The plant features slender, grass-like stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its inflorescence consists of spikelets arranged in a loose, cylindrical cluster, typical of the sedge family.

Schoenus filiculmis is often found in wetland habitats, including marshes and along the edges of water bodies.
