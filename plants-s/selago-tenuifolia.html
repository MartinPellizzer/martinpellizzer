Selago tenuifolia

Selago tenuifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved selago, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant features small, needle-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its flowers are usually pale blue or white, appearing in clusters along the stems.

Selago tenuifolia is adapted to rocky and sandy soils, making it a hardy species suited to coastal and arid environments.
