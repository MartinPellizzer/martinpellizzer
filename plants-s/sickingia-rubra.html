Sickingia rubra

Sickingia rubra, commonly known as the red-flowered kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, ribbed leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often blooming in winter. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Sickingia rubra is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and vibrant floral display.
