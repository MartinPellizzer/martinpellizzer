Spathoglottis rivularis

Spathoglottis rivularis, commonly known as the river orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and along stream banks. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome and several pseudobulbs. Its flowers are usually yellow or greenish-yellow, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

Spathoglottis rivularis is known for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
