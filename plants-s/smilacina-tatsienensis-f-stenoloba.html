Smilacina tatsienensis f. stenoloba

Smilacina tatsienensis f. stenoloba, commonly known as the narrow-leaved smilacina, is a variant of the smilacina genus native to China.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest understories. Its botanical features include a climbing habit and a distinct floral structure.

This subspecies is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and limited distribution.
