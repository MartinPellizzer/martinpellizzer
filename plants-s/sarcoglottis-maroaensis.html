Sarcoglottis maroaensis

Sarcoglottis maroaensis, commonly known as the Maroaensis orchid, is a tropical plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the Caribbean, particularly found in the Lesser Antilles. This herbaceous plant typically grows as a rhizomatous vine, with fleshy, dark green leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered, often with a distinctive coloration that aids in pollination by specific insects.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
