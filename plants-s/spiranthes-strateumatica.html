Spiranthes strateumatica

Spiranthes strateumatica, commonly known as the pink lady's tresses, is a species of orchid native to North America.

It is a terrestrial herb with a distinctive spiral arrangement of its flowering stems, which gives it its common name. The plant produces delicate, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows.

Spiranthes strateumatica is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
