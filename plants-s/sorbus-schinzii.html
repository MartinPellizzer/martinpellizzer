Sorbus schinzii

Sorbus schinzii, commonly known as the Schinz's whitebeams, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, including parts of China, Kazakhstan, and Mongolia. The plant is characterized by its small, white flowers that appear in clusters, followed by small, persistent fruits. Sorbus schinzii is often found in rocky or alpine habitats, where it thrives in well-drained soils and cooler climates.
