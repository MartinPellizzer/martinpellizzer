Sobralia macrantha var. purpurea

Sobralia macrantha var. purpurea, commonly known as the purple-flowered Sobralia, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences. The plant features elongated, pendulous flowers with a distinctive purple hue, often with contrasting yellow or white markings. Its leaves are broad, lance-shaped, and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

This variety is valued for its vibrant blooms and is often cultivated in warm, humid climates.
