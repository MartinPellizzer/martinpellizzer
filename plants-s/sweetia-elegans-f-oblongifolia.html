Sweetia elegans f. oblongifolia

Sweetia elegans f. oblongifolia, commonly known as the oblong-leaved sweetia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves that are typically a glossy dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. It prefers partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens or shaded rockeries.

Its ornamental value lies in its attractive foliage and delicate floral display.
