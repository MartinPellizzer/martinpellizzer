Spergularia rubra var. dillenii

Spergularia rubra var. dillenii, commonly known as the red sandplain star, is a flowering plant native to sandy soils in the southeastern United States.

It is a small annual herb with narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of bright red flowers. The plant is adapted to harsh, nutrient-poor environments and is often found in coastal dunes and open fields. Its flowers are typically visited by bees and other pollinators, contributing to local biodiversity.
