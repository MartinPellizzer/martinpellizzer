Schefflera laeve

Schefflera laeve, commonly known as the smooth schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, palm-like foliage composed of large, glossy, dark green, palmate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by yellowish-orange berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor landscaping.
