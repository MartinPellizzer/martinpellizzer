Strobilanthes paniculata var. alata

Strobilanthes paniculata var. alata, commonly known as the winged strobilanthes, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive winged bracts that enclose the flower clusters, giving it a unique appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its flowers are usually small and clustered in paniculate inflorescences, and it produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
