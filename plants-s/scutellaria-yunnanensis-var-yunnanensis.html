Scutellaria yunnanensis var. yunnanensis

Scutellaria yunnanensis var. yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color, arranged in whorls along the stem. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its medicinal properties, it is of interest in traditional Chinese medicine.
