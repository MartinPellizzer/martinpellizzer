Sedum hemsleyanum

Sedum hemsleyanum, commonly known as the Hemsley's stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to China.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink or red. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a visually appealing addition to gardens.
