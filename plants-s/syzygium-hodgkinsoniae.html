Syzygium hodgkinsoniae

Syzygium hodgkinsoniae, commonly known as the Hodgkinson's apple.

It is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae, native to northern Australia. The plant is a small to medium-sized tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall, with smooth, greyish bark and lance-shaped leaves. It produces small, white or pale pink flowers followed by edible, apple-like fruits.
