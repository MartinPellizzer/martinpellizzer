Silphium equatoriale

Silphium equatoriale, commonly known as the African giant hydra.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is native to parts of Africa. The plant is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers and robust, upright stems. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

Silphium equatoriale is also noted for its medicinal properties, traditionally used in local remedies.
