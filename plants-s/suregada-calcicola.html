Suregada calcicola

Suregada calcicola, commonly known as the calcicola suregada, is a rare and specialized plant adapted to grow in calcium-rich soils.

It is native to specific regions in South Africa, particularly in the Western Cape, where it thrives in rocky and arid environments. The plant has small, narrow leaves and produces delicate, white or pale blue flowers during the spring season. Its unique habitat requirements make it highly sensitive to environmental changes and habitat degradation.

Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this rare botanical species from extinction.
