Seridia latifolia

Seridia latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf seridia, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa and the Middle East.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. Seridia latifolia thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water once established.
