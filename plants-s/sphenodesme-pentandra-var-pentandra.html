Sphenodesme pentandra var. pentandra

Sphenodesme pentandra var. pentandra, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red, spider-like flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections.
