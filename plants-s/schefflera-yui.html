Schefflera yui

Schefflera yui, commonly known as the Yui umbrella plant, is a member of the Araliaceae family native to Japan.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of dark green, leathery leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. Schefflera yui is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its adaptability and low maintenance requirements.
