Schefflera microphylla

Schefflera microphylla, commonly known as the silver schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of small, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage.

Schefflera microphylla is relatively low-maintenance and tolerant of indoor conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
