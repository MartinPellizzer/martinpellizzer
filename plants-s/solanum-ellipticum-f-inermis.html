Solanum ellipticum f. inermis

Solanum ellipticum f. inermis, commonly known as the smooth-leaved nightshade, is a variant of the elliptic nightshade species.

It is a herbaceous plant native to parts of South America, particularly in Brazil. The plant features elliptical, smooth leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale green. Its fruit is a fleshy berry that changes color as it matures, often from green to yellow or red.

This plant is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and unique appearance.
