Sansevieria metallica

Sansevieria metallica, commonly known as the Black Gold Snake Plant, is a striking member of the Asparagaceae family native to tropical regions of Africa.

This plant is characterized by its dark green, upright leaves that have a metallic sheen, giving it its common name. The leaves are typically long and narrow, with a distinctive zigzag pattern along their length. It is a low-maintenance plant that thrives in indirect light and requires minimal watering.

Sansevieria metallica is also noted for its air-purifying properties, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
