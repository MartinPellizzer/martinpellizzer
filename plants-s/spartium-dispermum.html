Spartium dispermum

Spartium dispermum, commonly known as red bupleurum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its small, fragrant flowers are usually pink or purple, blooming in the spring.

Spartium dispermum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
