Sericostoma verrucosum

Sericostoma verrucosum, commonly known as the spiny leaf orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, spiky leaves and intricate floral structure. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with a distinctive coloration that attracts specific pollinators.

Due to its habitat specificity and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
