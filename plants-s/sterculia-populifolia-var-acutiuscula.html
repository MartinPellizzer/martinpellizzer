Sterculia populifolia var. acutiuscula

Sterculia populifolia var. acutiuscula, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sterculia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine pubescence. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local communities.
