Spiranthes elytra

Spiranthes elytra, commonly known as the narrow-leaf ladies' tresses, is a flowering plant belonging to the orchid family.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and its distinctive spiral arrangement of flowers, which give it its common name.
