Saussurea glomerata

Saussurea glomerata, commonly known as the Chinese yarrow or sweet coltsfoot, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It features a thick, fleshy rhizome and produces dense clusters of small, pale yellow to white flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species in traditional medicine and horticulture.
