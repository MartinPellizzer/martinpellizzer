Stewartia sinensis var. shensiensis

Stewartia sinensis var. shensiensis, commonly known as the Sichuan Stewartia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Theaceae family and is characterized by its elliptical, dark green leaves that turn yellow in autumn. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries. It is valued for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.

This variety is also noted for its adaptability to different soil types and its ability to thrive in partial shade.
