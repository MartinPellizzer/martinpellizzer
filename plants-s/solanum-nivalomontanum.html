Solanum nivalomontanum

Solanum nivalomontanum, commonly known as the snow mountain nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or alpine environments. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale blue in color. Its fruits are berries that mature to a dark purple or black color, containing several seeds.

Solanum nivalomontanum is adapted to cold climates and has a unique ecological role in its native habitat.
