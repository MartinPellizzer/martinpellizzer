Scilla barba-caprae

Scilla barba-caprae, commonly known as the goat's beard, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It produces striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that form a basal rosette. Its bulbs are adapted for survival in rocky and well-drained soils.

Scilla barba-caprae is often found in coastal and mountainous habitats.
