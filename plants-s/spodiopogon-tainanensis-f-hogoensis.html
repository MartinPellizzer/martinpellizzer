Spodiopogon tainanensis f. hogoensis

Spodiopogon tainanensis f. hogoensis, commonly known as the Hogoensis form of Tainanensis spodiopogon, is a rare grass species native to Taiwan.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and compact growth habit. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are narrow and have a distinct blue-green hue, which helps in identifying it from other similar species.

This form is of interest to botanists due to its unique genetic variation and ecological significance.
