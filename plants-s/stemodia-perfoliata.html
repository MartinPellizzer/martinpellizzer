Stemodia perfoliata

Stemodia perfoliata, commonly known as the perfoliate stemodia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features distinctive leaves with a perfoliate arrangement, where the leaves are joined at the base and pass through the stem. Its small, white or pale blue flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, often blooming in late summer.

The plant is adapted to arid conditions and is often found in open grasslands or along rocky outcrops.
