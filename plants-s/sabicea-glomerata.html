Sabicea glomerata

Sabicea glomerata, commonly known as the clustered sabicea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive floral display.
