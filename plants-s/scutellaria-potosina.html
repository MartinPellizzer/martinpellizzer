Scutellaria potosina

Scutellaria potosina, commonly known as Potosino bluebug.

It is a member of the mint family (Lamiaceae) and is native to Mexico. The plant is characterized by its square, hairy stems and opposite, ovate leaves. Its flowers are typically blue or purple, with two-lipped corollas typical of the genus.

Scutellaria potosina is often found in rocky or open habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
