Sulcorebutia alba

Sulcorebutia alba, commonly known as the white moon cactus, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular shape and pale green to white-colored stems. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, which are typically borne in clusters. Sulcorebutia alba is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
