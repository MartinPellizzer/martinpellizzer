Schismatoglottis wallichii var. oblongata

Schismatoglottis wallichii var. oblongata, commonly known as the broad-leaved schismatoglottis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of India and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its broad, elliptical leaves that are often slightly curled at the edges. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color.

This species is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
