Senecio soukupii

Senecio soukupii, commonly known as the Cape Peninsula groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky, coastal areas. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces daisy-like yellow flowers. It is adapted to arid conditions and thrives in well-drained soils.

Senecio soukupii is often found in the fynbos biome, where it plays a role in local ecosystems.
