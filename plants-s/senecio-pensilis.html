Senecio pensilis

Senecio pensilis, commonly known as the hanging senecio, is a perennial flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and hanging baskets.

Senecio pensilis is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
