Stellaria borealis f. macrantha

Stellaria borealis f. macrantha, commonly known as the Arctic starflower, is a low-growing perennial herb native to arctic and alpine regions.

It features small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to August. The plant has a compact rosette of narrow, hairy leaves that help it withstand harsh cold and strong winds. It thrives in well-drained, rocky or sandy soils and is often found in tundra and mountainous habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it well-adapted to extreme environmental conditions.
