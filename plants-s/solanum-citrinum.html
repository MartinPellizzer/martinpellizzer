Solanum citrinum

Solanum citrinum, commonly known as the yellow nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 meter. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by orange or yellow berries. Its leaves are ovate with a rough texture and often have a slight pubescence.

Solanum citrinum contains toxic compounds, making it potentially harmful to humans and animals.
