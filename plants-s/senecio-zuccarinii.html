Senecio zuccarinii

Senecio zuccarinii, commonly known as the yellow star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, spiny fruits.

Senecio zuccarinii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
