Senegalia Torta

Senegalia torta is a unique and resilient plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa and the Middle East. Known for its twisted, gnarled branches and distinctive appearance, it has adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments. This shrub or small tree is often found in rocky soils and can thrive with minimal water, making it a hardy species. Its ability to withstand extreme temperatures and drought has made it a symbol of endurance in many cultures. Despite its tough exterior, Senegalia torta plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing shelter for wildlife.
