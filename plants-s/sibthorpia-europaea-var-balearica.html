Sibthorpia europaea var. balearica

Sibthorpia europaea var. balearica, commonly known as the Balearic sibthorpia, is a variant of the European sibthorpia, native to the Balearic Islands.

It is a shrub characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy or rocky soils. It has a compact growth habit and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and relatively rare species within the Sibthorpia genus.
