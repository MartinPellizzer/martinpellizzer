Schefflera thorelii

Schefflera thorelii, commonly known as the African net leaf, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, palmate leaves that are deeply lobed and resemble a net, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by red, fleshy berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as a ornamental plant.

Schefflera thorelii is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
