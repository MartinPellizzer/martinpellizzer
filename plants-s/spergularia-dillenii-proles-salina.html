Spergularia dillenii proles salina

Spergularia dillenii proles salina, commonly known as salt marsh starwort, is a halophytic plant adapted to grow in saline environments.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically thrives in coastal salt marshes and tidal flats, where it tolerates high soil salinity. Its leaves are often succulent, helping to store water in arid conditions.

Spergularia dillenii proles salina plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various small organisms in its ecosystem.
