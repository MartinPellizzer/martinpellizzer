Solanum tuntula

Solanum tuntula, commonly known as the golden apple snake plant, is a striking member of the Solanaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features dark green, waxy leaves with a distinctive golden or yellowish border, which gives it its common name. It is a perennial herb that thrives in well-drained soil and bright, indirect light.

Solanum tuntula is valued for its ornamental appeal and air-purifying qualities.
