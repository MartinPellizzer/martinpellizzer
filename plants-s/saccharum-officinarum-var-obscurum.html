Saccharum officinarum var. obscurum

Saccharum officinarum var. obscurum, commonly known as the dark sugarcane, is a variant of the sugarcane plant characterized by its darker pigmentation and unique biochemical composition.

This variety is native to tropical regions and thrives in warm, humid climates with abundant sunlight. It possesses a robust root system and tall, slender stalks that are rich in sucrose content. The plant's leaves are broad and sword-shaped, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Due to its unique traits, it is sometimes used in specialized agricultural or horticultural applications.
