Solanum tjamoel

Solanum tjamoel, commonly known as the Tjamoel nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically purple or white in color. Its leaves are ovate and have a slightly hairy texture.

Solanum tjamoel is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to the island's unique climate.
