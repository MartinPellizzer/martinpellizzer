Selaginella Rupestris Var. Bolanderi

Selaginella rupestris var. bolanderi is a rare and fascinating plant native to the western United States. It thrives in rocky, arid environments where few other species can survive. This variety of Selaginella is known for its unique, delicate foliage and resilience in harsh conditions. Its ability to retain moisture in its leaves makes it a hardy choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Despite its tough exterior, this plant holds a special place in the world of succulent enthusiasts and botanists alike.
