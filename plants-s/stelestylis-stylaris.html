Stelestylis stylaris

Stelestylis stylaris, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking golden-yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest undergrowth. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the surrounding soil.

Stelestylis stylaris is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
