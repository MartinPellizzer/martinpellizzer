Senecio formosanus

Senecio formosanus, commonly known as the Taiwan Senecio, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and has hairy, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a compact cluster, often surrounded by bracts.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
