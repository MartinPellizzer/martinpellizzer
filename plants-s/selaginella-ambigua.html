Selaginella ambigua

Selaginella ambigua, commonly known as the zigzag spikemoss, is a species of spore-bearing plant belonging to the family Selaginellaceae.

It is a small, terrestrial, and evergreen plant that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant has a distinctive appearance with its zigzag-shaped stems and small, scale-like leaves. It produces spores in specialized structures called strobila, which are located at the tips of the stems.

Selaginella ambigua is known for its ability to survive in various environmental conditions, making it a resilient and adaptable species.
