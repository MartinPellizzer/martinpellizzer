Sagraea microphylla

Sagraea microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved sagraea, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are showy, with tubular corollas that range in color from white to pink, attracting various pollinators.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
