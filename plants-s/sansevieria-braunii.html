Sansevieria braunii

Sansevieria braunii, commonly known as the Black Gold Sansevieria, is a striking succulent native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features dark green, lance-shaped leaves with a prominent central vein, often adorned with a golden or bronze border. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it an excellent choice for indoor environments. Its foliage remains relatively compact, rarely exceeding a few feet in height.

Sansevieria braunii is also noted for its air-purifying qualities, contributing to improved indoor air quality.
