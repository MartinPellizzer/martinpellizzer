Sinobambusa incana

Sinobambusa incana, commonly known as the silver bamboo, is a species of grass in the Poaceae family.

It is native to eastern Asia, particularly China, where it grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its slender, silver-gray stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its elegant appearance.

Sinobambusa incana is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
