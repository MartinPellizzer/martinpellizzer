Stipa zuvantica

Stipa zuvantica, commonly known as the Zuvant grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to certain regions of Central Asia and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Stipa zuvantica is often found in dry, open habitats and is adapted to arid conditions.
