Solanum testaceum

Solanum testaceum, commonly known as the pale tomato or yellow nightshade, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family.

It is native to Central and South America and is characterized by its small, yellowish-orange fruits that resemble tomatoes. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with hairy, branched stems and alternate leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale green, and the fruit matures to a bright yellow or orange color.

Solanum testaceum is often found in disturbed soils and can be cultivated as a ornamental or for its edible fruits.
