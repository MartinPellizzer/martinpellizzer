Senecio lanifer

Senecio lanifer, commonly known as the woolly senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. Senecio lanifer typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
