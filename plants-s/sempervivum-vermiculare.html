Sempervivum vermiculare

Sempervivum vermiculare, commonly known as the hen and chicks plant, is a succulent species native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It features rosettes of fleshy, pointed leaves that are typically green with red or purple hues, especially in cooler temperatures. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, or white during the summer months. It is well adapted to rocky and arid environments, thriving in full sun and well-draining soil.

Sempervivum vermiculare is a low-maintenance plant that is popular in rock gardens and as a decorative houseplant.
