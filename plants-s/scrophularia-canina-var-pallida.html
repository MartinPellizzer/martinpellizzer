Scrophularia canina var. pallida

Scrophularia canina var. pallida, commonly known as the pale dog's mercury, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae, which includes many species with medicinal and ornamental uses. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and streambanks. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are pale yellow and clustered in spikes.

It is characterized by its delicate appearance and its preference for calcareous soils.
