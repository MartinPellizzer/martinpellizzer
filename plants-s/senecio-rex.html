Senecio rex

Senecio rex, commonly known as the "King of the Senecios," is a striking succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, triangular-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers during the summer months, adding a delicate contrast to its bold foliage. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
