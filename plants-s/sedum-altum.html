Sedum altum

Sedum altum, commonly known as the tall stonecrop, is a flowering plant native to Eurasia.

It is a succulent species characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and clusters of star-shaped yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It produces small, rounded leaves that help it store water efficiently.

Sedum altum is often used in landscaping for its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
