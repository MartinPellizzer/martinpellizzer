Schomburgkia lyonsii f. immaculata

Schomburgkia lyonsii f. immaculata, commonly known as the immaculate schomburgkia, is a rare and striking orchid species native to the tropical rainforests of Central America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in vibrant shades of yellow and orange. The plant features a pseudobulb structure, which helps it store water and nutrients, adapting to its humid, shaded habitat. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, providing a lush green backdrop to its colorful blooms.

This cultivar is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
