Stipa aliena var. macrocarpa

Stipa aliena var. macrocarpa, commonly known as the giant feather grass, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and long, narrow leaves that resemble feathers, giving it a delicate appearance. The plant produces fluffy, seed-bearing spikes that are often used in ornamental gardening due to their aesthetic appeal. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for dry, rocky environments.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for landscaping and ecological restoration projects.
