Solanum deltoideum

Solanum deltoideum, commonly known as the deltoid nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 meter, featuring woody stems and opposite leaves that are ovate to deltoid in shape. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by berries that mature to a dark purple color. It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae), which includes other notable plants like tomatoes and potatoes.

Solanum deltoideum is often found in dry, open habitats and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.
