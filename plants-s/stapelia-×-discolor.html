Stapelia × discolor

Stapelia × discolor, commonly known as the hybrid starry stapelia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed stems and distinctive star-shaped flowers. The plant produces large, showy blooms that are typically yellow or orange with a strong, unpleasant odor, which attracts pollinators like flies. Stapelia × discolor is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.

It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.
