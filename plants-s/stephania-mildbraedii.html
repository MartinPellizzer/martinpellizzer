Stephania mildbraedii

Stephania mildbraedii, commonly known as the mildbraed's stephania, is a climbing plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Menispermaceae family and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and distinctive yellow flowers. The plant produces small, dark blue berries that are often used in traditional medicine. Its roots contain alkaloids, which have been studied for potential medicinal properties.
