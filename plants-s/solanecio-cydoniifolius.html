Solanecio cydoniifolius

Solanecio cydoniifolius, commonly known as the pineappleweed, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rosette of broad, waxy leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that resemble a pineapple, hence its common name. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in gardens and disturbed areas.

Solanecio cydoniifolius is also known for its edible leaves and has been used in traditional medicine.
