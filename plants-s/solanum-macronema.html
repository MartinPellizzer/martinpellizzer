Solanum macronema

Solanum macronema, commonly known as the large-fruited nightshade, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its woody stems and broad, ovate leaves. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically violet or blue in color. Its fruit is a fleshy berry that turns red when ripe, containing several seeds.

Solanum macronema is often found in disturbed habitats and can be invasive in certain areas.
