Spanizium ocymoides

Spanizium ocymoides, commonly known as the narrowleaf spaniard, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its small, pale blue or violet flowers bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months.

Spanizium ocymoides is often found in rocky soils and dry habitats, making it well adapted to arid environments.
