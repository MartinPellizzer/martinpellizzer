Stenomesson vitellinum

Stenomesson vitellinum, commonly known as the yellow star grass, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically bright yellow to green in color. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually yellow or orange, which bloom during the warmer months. It is often found in open grasslands and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils.

Stenomesson vitellinum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in gardens for its vibrant color and low maintenance requirements.
