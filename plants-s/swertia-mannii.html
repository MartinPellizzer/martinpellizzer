Swertia mannii

Swertia mannii, commonly known as African yellow gentian, is a flowering plant native to various regions of Africa.

It is a member of the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a rough texture.

Swertia mannii is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
