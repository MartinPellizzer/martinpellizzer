Sarracenia × osterrathii

Sarracenia × osterrathii, commonly known as the hybrid pitcher plant, is a fascinating carnivorous plant resulting from the cross between Sarracenia leucophylla and Sarracenia rubra.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular-shaped pitchers that are often brightly colored, typically in shades of red, orange, or yellow. These pitchers secrete a digestive enzyme-laden fluid to trap and digest insects, providing the plant with essential nutrients. The plant thrives in wet, acidic environments such as bogs and marshes, where it can maintain the necessary humidity and soil conditions.

Its unique morphology and ecological role make it a popular subject in both horticulture and botanical research.
