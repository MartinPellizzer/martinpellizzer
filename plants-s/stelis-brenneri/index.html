Stelis Brenneri

Stelis brenneri is a rare orchid species native to the cloud forests of Central America. It is known for its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in the early spring. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific soil conditions to flourish. Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and is the subject of conservation efforts. Its unique characteristics make it a fascinating subject for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike.
