Setaria dura

Setaria dura, commonly known as hardset grass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa, Asia, and Australia.

It is characterized by its erect, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny seeds. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in harsh conditions.

Setaria dura is often found in disturbed soils and can be invasive in some regions.
