Solanum hypoleucum

Solanum hypoleucum, commonly known as the white nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is native to parts of Africa and is characterized by its small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or subshrub, with hairy stems and oval-shaped leaves. Its fruit is a small, spherical berry that turns from green to red as it matures.

Solanum hypoleucum is often found in disturbed habitats and can thrive in a variety of soil types.
