Selenipedium × Conchiferum

Selenipedium × conchiferum is a rare and striking hybrid orchid known for its unique appearance and elusive nature. Native to the cloud forests of Central and South America, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. This plant is prized by botanists and collectors for its dramatic, elongated inflorescence and vibrant, showy flowers. Its hybrid origin makes it a fascinating subject in the study of plant genetics and evolution. Despite its beauty, Selenipedium × conchiferum remains a challenging plant to cultivate and a mystery to many in the botanical world.
