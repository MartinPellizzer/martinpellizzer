Senecio ionophyllus

Senecio ionophyllus, commonly known as the Ionophyllus groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its yellow flowers are surrounded by pale green bracts, creating a striking visual contrast.

Senecio ionophyllus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and hardy nature.
