Sorghum bipennatum

Sorghum bipennatum, commonly known as the two-feather sorghum, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, feather-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in warm climates and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Sorghum bipennatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in traditional landscaping.
