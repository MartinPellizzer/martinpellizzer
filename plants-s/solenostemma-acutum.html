Solenostemma acutum

Solenostemma acutum, commonly known as the arrowhead vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant has a vigorous growth habit and is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive foliage. It thrives in humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Solenostemma acutum is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
