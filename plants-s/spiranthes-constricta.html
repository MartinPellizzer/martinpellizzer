Spiranthes constricta

Spiranthes constricta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ladies' tresses, is a species of orchid native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, twisting flower spikes that bear small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as bogs and wet meadows. It has strap-like leaves that are narrow and grass-like in appearance.

Spiranthes constricta is a perennial herb that blooms from late spring to early summer.
