Salsola soda

Salsola soda, commonly known as Russian thistle or goatweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is a shrub-like perennial that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant has small, spiny leaves and produces clusters of greenish-yellow flowers. Its seeds are dispersed by wind, allowing it to spread rapidly in disturbed soils.

Salsola soda is often considered a weed but is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to stabilize soil.
