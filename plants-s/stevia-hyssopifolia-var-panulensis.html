Stevia hyssopifolia var. panulensis

Stevia hyssopifolia var. panulensis, commonly known as the panular stevia, is a perennial shrub native to parts of South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, pale green to yellowish flowers. The plant produces aromatic leaves that are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits. Its botanical features include opposite, lance-shaped leaves and a robust root system.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for cultivation in temperate climates.
