Siphyalis nitida

Siphyalis nitida, commonly known as the silverleaf lily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers. The plant produces a distinctive, fragrant scent, which attracts pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Siphyalis nitida is also valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
