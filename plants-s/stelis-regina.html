Stelis regina

Stelis regina, commonly known as the royal stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family, native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small epiphytic orchid, typically growing on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a slender, wiry stem with clustered, lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green in color. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and often white or pale pink, arranged in a loose inflorescence.

Stelis regina is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
