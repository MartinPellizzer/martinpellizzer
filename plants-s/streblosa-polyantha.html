Streblosa polyantha

Streblosa polyantha, commonly known as the Chinese rose or the everblooming rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are elliptic to ovate in shape and have a smooth, glossy surface. The plant produces fragrant, single or double flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, which bloom continuously throughout the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Streblosa polyantha is also valued for its ornamental value and is often used in traditional Chinese horticulture.
