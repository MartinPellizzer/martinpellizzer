Specklinia trulla

Specklinia trulla, commonly known as the spiral fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, spiraling fronds that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant has delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil.

Specklinia trulla is popular among fern enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of care.
