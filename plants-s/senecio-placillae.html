Senecio placillae

Senecio placillae, commonly known as the coastal senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in coastal areas, where it thrives in sandy soils and salty air. Its small, yellow flowers are arranged in dense clusters, and it has narrow, blue-green leaves.
