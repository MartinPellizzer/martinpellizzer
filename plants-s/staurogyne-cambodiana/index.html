Staurogyne Cambodiana

Staurogyne cambodiana is a rare aquatic plant native to the freshwater ecosystems of Cambodia. It is known for its unique morphology, featuring dense clusters of small, bright green leaves. This species thrives in slow-moving waters with rich sediment, often found in marshy areas and backwaters. Its ecological importance lies in providing habitat and food for various aquatic organisms. Due to habitat loss and pollution, Staurogyne cambodiana is now considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
