Schizocasia regnieri

Schizocasia regnieri, commonly known as the African star fruit or yellow star fruit, is a tropical shrub native to Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive yellow, star-shaped fruits that are edible and often used in local cuisines. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and produces small, fragrant white flowers. Its growth habit includes a woody stem with thorn-like projections, which helps in its identification.

Schizocasia regnieri is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for its unique fruit and foliage.
