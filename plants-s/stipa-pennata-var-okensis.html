Stipa pennata var. okensis

Stipa pennata var. okensis, commonly known as the Okinawan feather grass, is a perennial grass species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and delicate, feathery inflorescences that resemble plumes. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. It produces small, spikelet-based flowers that are wind-pollinated.

Stipa pennata var. okensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and garden design.
