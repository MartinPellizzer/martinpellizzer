Scutellaria kuramensis

Scutellaria kuramensis, commonly known as Kuram skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of northeastern Turkey.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. The plant features square stems, opposite leaves, and small, two-lipped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It is often found in rocky or forested habitats at elevations ranging from 1,500 to 3,000 meters.

Scutellaria kuramensis is noted for its potential medicinal properties, though further research is needed to fully understand its botanical and pharmacological significance.
