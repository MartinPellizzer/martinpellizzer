Senecio insignis

Senecio insignis, commonly known as the yellow starflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has narrow, linear leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette.

Senecio insignis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and drought tolerance.
