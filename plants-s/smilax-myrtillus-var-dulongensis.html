Smilax myrtillus var. dulongensis

Smilax myrtillus var. dulongensis, commonly known as the dwarf smilax, is a variant of the smilax genus native to the southeastern United States.

This plant is a climbing vine characterized by its spiny stems and heart-shaped leaves. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into red, berry-like fruits. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
