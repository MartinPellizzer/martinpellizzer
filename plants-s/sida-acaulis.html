Sida acaulis

Sida acaulis, commonly known as the silverleaf sida, is a herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which results from dense pubescence covering the leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Sida acaulis is often found in disturbed soils and arid environments due to its adaptability.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and taxonomic studies.
