Specklinia millipeda

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its finely divided, feathery fronds that resemble the legs of a millipede. Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in specialized botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
