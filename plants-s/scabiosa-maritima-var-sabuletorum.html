Scabiosa maritima var. sabuletorum

Scabiosa maritima var. sabuletorum, commonly known as sea scabious, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its pink to purple, daisy-like flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun, making it well adapted to coastal environments. Its leaves are narrow and gray-green, often covered in fine hairs to reduce water loss.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize dune soils.
