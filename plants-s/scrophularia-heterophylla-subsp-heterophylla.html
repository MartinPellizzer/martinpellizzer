Scrophularia heterophylla subsp. heterophylla

Scrophularia heterophylla subsp.

heterophylla, commonly known as the variable scrophularia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Scrophulariaceae. It is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its variable leaf morphology, which can range from basal to opposite or whorled arrangements.

The plant typically produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of pink or purple, often with a distinctive two-lipped structure. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.

This subspecies is noted for its adaptability and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.
