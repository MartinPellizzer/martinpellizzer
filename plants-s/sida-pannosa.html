Sida pannosa

Sida pannosa, commonly known as rough sida, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a herbaceous annual or perennial plant that typically grows in disturbed soils and arid environments. The plant has hairy, ovate leaves with a rough texture, and it produces small, yellowish flowers clustered in dense inflorescences. Sida pannosa is known for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
