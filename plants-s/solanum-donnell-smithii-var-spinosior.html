Solanum donnell-smithii var. spinosior

Solanum donnell-smithii var. spinosior, commonly known as the spiny nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades, tomatoes, and potatoes. The plant is characterized by its spiny stems and leaves, which serve as a defense mechanism against herbivores. Its small, white or pale yellow flowers are followed by berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
