Stelis alboviolacea

Stelis alboviolacea, commonly known as the white-violet orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers with white and violet hues. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic nature. Its flowers bloom in clusters, often in late spring or early summer.

The orchid's botanical features make it a popular choice for both horticultural collections and botanical studies.
