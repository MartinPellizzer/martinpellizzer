Segurola chanal

Segurola chanal, commonly known as the blue bell.

It is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region, characterized by its striking blue or violet bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is often found in coastal areas.

Segurola chanal is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
