Solanum knoblochii

Solanum knoblochii, commonly known as the Knobloch's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants such as tomatoes and potatoes. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate in shape, with a rough texture, and it produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters.

The fruit is a fleshy berry that turns red when mature, containing several seeds.
