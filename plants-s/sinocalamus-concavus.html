Sinocalamus concavus

Sinocalamus concavus, commonly known as the Yunnan bamboo, is a tall perennial grass native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, hollow stems that are often used in traditional Chinese medicine and as a building material. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically green and covered in a fine layer of hair. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Sinocalamus concavus plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
