Stelis hypsela

Stelis hypsela, commonly known as the highland stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees in humid, shaded forest environments. The plant features a short, upright stem with clustered, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, white or pale green, and arranged in a raceme.

Stelis hypsela is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical greenhouses.
