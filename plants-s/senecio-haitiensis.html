Senecio haitiensis

Senecio haitiensis, commonly known as Haitian groundsel, is a flowering plant native to Haiti.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or disturbed habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. It has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
