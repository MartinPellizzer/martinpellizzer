Senecio brachyatus

Senecio brachyatus, commonly known as the Wooly Senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers in compact clusters, which bloom during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in arid and semi-arid regions.
