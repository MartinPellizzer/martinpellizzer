Stelis imraei

Stelis imraei, commonly known as the Imraei orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with multiple stems that produce leaves and flowers along their length. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors including white, yellow, and green.

Stelis imraei is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to cultivated conditions.
