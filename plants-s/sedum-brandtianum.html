Sedum brandtianum

Sedum brandtianum, commonly known as the Brandt's stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow to orange. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often used in rock gardens or as a ground cover due to its drought tolerance. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it a hardy choice for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
