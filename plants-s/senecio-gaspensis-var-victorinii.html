Senecio gaspensis var. victorinii

Senecio gaspensis var. victorinii, commonly known as the Victorinii Senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow-orange flowers. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, daisy-like blooms that attract pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
