Sloanea curatellifolia

Sloanea curatellifolia, commonly known as the velvetleaf sloanea, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine, velvety hairs. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by elliptical, woody fruits containing several seeds. Sloanea curatellifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in warm climates for its dense foliage.

It thrives in humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
