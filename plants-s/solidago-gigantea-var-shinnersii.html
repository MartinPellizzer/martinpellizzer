Solidago gigantea var. shinnersii

Solidago gigantea var. shinnersii, commonly known as Shinners' giant goldenrod, is a variant of the giant goldenrod species native to the eastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its tall, erect stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant produces showy, bright yellow inflorescences composed of numerous small, clustered flowers. Its leaves are lance-shaped, alternate, and covered in fine, silky hairs.

This variety is often found in open woods, meadows, and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils.
