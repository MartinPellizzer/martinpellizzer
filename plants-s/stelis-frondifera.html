Stelis frondifera

Stelis frondifera, commonly known as the leafy orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and numerous, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often blooming during the rainy season. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both horticultural and conservation purposes.
