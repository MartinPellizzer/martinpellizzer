Sideritis vulgaris

Sideritis vulgaris, commonly known as common ironwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically pink or purple, forming whorls along the stem.

Sideritis vulgaris is known for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
