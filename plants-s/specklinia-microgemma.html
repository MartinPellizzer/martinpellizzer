Specklinia microgemma

Specklinia microgemma, commonly known as the miniature speckled orchid, is a small epiphytic orchid native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, speckled leaf patterns. The plant typically produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a contrasting lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, loose soil.

Due to its unique appearance and rarity, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
