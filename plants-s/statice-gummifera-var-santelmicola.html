Statice gummifera var. santelmicola

Statice gummifera var. santelmicola, commonly known as the Santelmicola statice, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of southern Europe.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, showy flowers in shades of pink and purple. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its foliage is typically gray-green, with hairy leaves that help reduce water loss.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
