Syngonanthus xeranthemoides f. brevifolius

Syngonanthus xeranthemoides f. brevifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved synogonanthus, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, narrow leaves that are densely packed, giving it a dense, bushy appearance. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers in late winter to early spring, which are clustered in umbels. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its unique foliage and seasonal blooms make it a desirable ornamental plant in Mediterranean climates.
