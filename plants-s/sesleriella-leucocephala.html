Sesleriella leucocephala

Sesleriella leucocephala, commonly known as the white-headed sesleria, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Xanthorrhoeaceae and is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and striking white flower heads. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are small and clustered, forming a spherical or rounded inflorescence.
