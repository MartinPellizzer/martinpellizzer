Spathiphyllum candicans

Spathiphyllum candicans, commonly known as the peace lily, is a tropical evergreen plant native to the understory of forests in Central and South America.

It features broad, dark green, heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces striking white spatulate flowers that are surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. It is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its air-purifying properties.

Spathiphyllum candicans is popular as a houseplant due to its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
