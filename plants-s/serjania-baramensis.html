Serjania baramensis

Serjania baramensis, commonly known as the Baramensis serjania, is a species of flowering plant in the family Serjania.

It is native to the rainforests of northern South America, particularly in regions of Venezuela and Colombia. The plant is characterized by its woody structure and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. Its leaves are typically alternate and ovate, with a smooth or slightly serrated margin.

Serjania baramensis is often found in moist, shaded environments and plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various insects and birds.
