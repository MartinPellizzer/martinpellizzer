Solanum dunalii

Solanum dunalii, commonly known as the Dunal's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of purple and white. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Solanum dunalii is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or dry soils.
