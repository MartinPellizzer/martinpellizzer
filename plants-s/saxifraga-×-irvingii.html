Saxifraga × irvingii

Saxifraga × irvingii, commonly known as the hybrid saxifrage, is a flowering plant in the Saxifragaceae family.

It is a hybrid between Saxifraga oppositifolia and Saxifraga hirculus, resulting in a plant with distinctive white to pink flowers. The species is native to the Arctic and alpine regions of Europe and North America. It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in cool, moist environments.

Saxifraga × irvingii is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
