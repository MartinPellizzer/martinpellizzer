Shawia salicina

Shawia salicina, commonly known as the Christmas star, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit and produces small, fleshy fruits that contain seeds. Shawia salicina thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
