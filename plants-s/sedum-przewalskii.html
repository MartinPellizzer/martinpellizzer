Sedum przewalskii

Sedum przewalskii, commonly known as the Przewalski's stonecrop, is a perennial succulent native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features fleshy, upright stems and clusters of star-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to red. The plant is adapted to harsh, arid environments and can thrive in well-drained soils. Its leaves are typically thick and covered in a waxy cuticle to reduce water loss.

Sedum przewalskii is often cultivated in rock gardens and xeriscapes due to its hardiness and ornamental value.
