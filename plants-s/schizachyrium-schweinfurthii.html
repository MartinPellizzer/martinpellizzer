Schizachyrium schweinfurthii

Schizachyrium schweinfurthii, commonly known as little bluestem, is a native North American grass species that thrives in a variety of soil types, including sandy and clay soils.

It is characterized by its blue-green foliage that turns coppery in the fall, and its slender, branched seed heads that appear in late summer. This perennial plant is well adapted to drought conditions and is often found in prairie ecosystems and open woodlands. Schizachyrium schweinfurthii is also valued for its ornamental appeal in native plant gardens.
