Schizochilus transvaalensis

Schizochilus transvaalensis, commonly known as the Transvaal orchid, is a species of orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Schizochilus transvaalensis thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested or rocky habitats.
