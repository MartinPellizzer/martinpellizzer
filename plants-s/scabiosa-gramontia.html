Scabiosa gramontia

Scabiosa gramontia, commonly known as the mountain scabious, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are usually narrow and hairy, adapting to harsh climatic conditions.

Scabiosa gramontia is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
