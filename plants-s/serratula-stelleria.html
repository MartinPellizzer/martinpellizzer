Serratula stelleria

Serratula stelleria, commonly known as Steller's fleabane, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow to orange composite flowers and hairy, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It is often found in disturbed areas and can spread through both seeds and vegetative reproduction.

Serratula stelleria is sometimes considered a weed due to its rapid growth and adaptability.
