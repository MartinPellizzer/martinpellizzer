Solanum actinocalyx

Solanum actinocalyx, commonly known as the white star apple, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its showy, white to pale yellow flowers that resemble stars. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically green when unripe and turn red when mature. Its leaves are ovate with a slightly hairy surface, and the plant often grows as a shrub or small tree.

Solanum actinocalyx is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been cultivated for its unique floral display.
