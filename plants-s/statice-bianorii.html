Statice bianorii

Statice bianorii, commonly known as the pink statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its dense, spike-like inflorescences. The flowers are typically pink or purple, with narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

This plant is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators and its use in floral arrangements.
