Sphaerolobium alatum

Sphaerolobium alatum, commonly known as the winged burr legume, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features distinctive winged pods that enclose its seeds, aiding in wind dispersal. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions.

Its ornamental value and ability to fix nitrogen make it ecologically and horticulturally significant.
