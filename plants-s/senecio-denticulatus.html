Senecio denticulatus

Senecio denticulatus, commonly known as the toothed groundsel, is a perennial flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy, gray-green foliage and small yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It produces numerous small seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Senecio denticulatus is often used in rock gardens due to its hardiness and ornamental value.
