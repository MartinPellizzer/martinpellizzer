Scrophularia talassica

Scrophularia talassica, commonly known as the coastal scrophularia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in coastal areas, sandy soils, and saline environments, showing a high tolerance for salt and drought. The plant features erect stems that can reach up to 60 cm in height, covered with fine hairs. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, arranged in clusters, and bloom from spring to early summer.

Scrophularia talassica is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
