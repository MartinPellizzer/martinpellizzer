Spiesia chionophylla

Spiesia chionophylla, commonly known as the snow-leaf plant, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-white foliage that gives it its common name. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers during the spring and early summer. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in high-altitude grasslands.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
