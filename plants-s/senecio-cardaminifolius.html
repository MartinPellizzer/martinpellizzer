Senecio cardaminifolius

Senecio cardaminifolius, commonly known as the heartleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of dark green, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a cluster at the end of each stem. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is occasionally cultivated as an ornamental plant.
