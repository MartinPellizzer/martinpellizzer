Sorbus Arranensis Subsp. Neglecta

Sorbus arranensis subsp. neglecta is a rare and elusive plant native to the Scottish islands. It belongs to the rose family and is known for its distinctive yellow berries. This subspecies is often overlooked due to its small size and inconspicuous appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is a subject of interest for botanists. Conservation efforts are underway to protect this unique species from habitat loss.
