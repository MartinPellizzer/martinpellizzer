Streptogyna crinita

Streptogyna crinita, commonly known as the silky orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, hair-like petals that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually pale green to white, with a subtle fragrance.

Streptogyna crinita is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
