Solanum hainanense

Solanum hainanense, commonly known as Hainan nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of China, particularly Hainan Island.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and tobacco. The plant typically grows as an erect shrub with simple, ovate leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers. Its fruits are berries that mature to a dark purple color, containing several seeds.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and studied for its chemical compounds.
