Saxifraga unguiculata var. auctiflora

Saxifraga unguiculata var. auctiflora, commonly known as the rock saxifrage, is a perennial flowering plant native to alpine and arctic regions.

It is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and clusters of small, white or pink flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in high-altitude environments. It has a compact growth habit and is adapted to cold climates with short growing seasons.

Saxifraga unguiculata var. auctiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in harsh environments.
