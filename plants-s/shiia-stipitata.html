Shiia stipitata

Shiia stipitata, commonly known as the slender bamboo orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are elongated and narrow, with a smooth texture.

Shiia stipitata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
