Schaefferia frutescens var. buxifolia

Schaefferia frutescens var. buxifolia, commonly known as the boxleaf schaefferia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a leathery texture, resembling the leaves of boxwood plants. The plant produces clusters of small, white, bell-shaped flowers in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a versatile choice for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
