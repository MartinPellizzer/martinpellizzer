Senna dariensis var. dariensis

Senna dariensis var. dariensis, commonly known as the Darien senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its bipinnate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces elongated pods that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories or along riverbanks.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
