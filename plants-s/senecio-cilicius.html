Senecio cilicius

Senecio cilicius, commonly known as the Cilician groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region, particularly Turkey and Cyprus.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with narrow, linear leaves and small yellow flowers arranged in a cluster. The plant thrives in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its fibrous roots help it retain moisture, making it resilient in dry climates.

It is often found in open habitats such as cliffs and slopes.
