Sudamerlycaste barbifrons

Sudamerlycaste barbifrons, commonly known as the Brazilian orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

Its leaves are broad and strap-like, adapted to capture as much sunlight as possible in its low-light habitat.
