Setaria brownii

Setaria brownii, commonly known as brown foxtail, is a grass species native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, blade-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in warm, dry climates and is often found in disturbed soils.

Setaria brownii is a resilient plant that can spread rapidly in agricultural fields and pastures.
