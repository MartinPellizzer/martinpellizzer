Sulcorebutia purpurea subsp. horacekii

Sulcorebutia purpurea subsp.

horacekii, commonly known as the Horacek's purple cushion cactus, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to Mexico. It features dense, rounded clusters of pale green to bluish-gray stems that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating.

The plant produces showy, violet to purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a distinctive addition to succulent collections.
