Satureja capitata

Satureja capitata, commonly known as summer savory, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant leaves and produces clusters of tiny, pinkish-white flowers. The plant is characterized by its aromatic foliage, which contains essential oils responsible for its distinctive scent. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, often growing in rocky or dry environments.

Satureja capitata is widely used in culinary and traditional medicinal applications due to its flavorful and therapeutic properties.
