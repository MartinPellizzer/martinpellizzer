Scutellaria yunnanensis var. subsessilifolia

Scutellaria yunnanensis var. subsessilifolia, commonly known as Yunnan skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically features lance-shaped leaves with a hairy surface and produces small, tubular flowers in shades of blue or purple. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or rocky slopes.

Due to its medicinal properties, it is of interest in traditional Chinese medicine and botanical research.
