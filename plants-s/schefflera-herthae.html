Schefflera herthae

Schefflera herthae, commonly known as the Herth's umbrella plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to New Guinea.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of large, waxy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil.

Schefflera herthae is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various light conditions.
