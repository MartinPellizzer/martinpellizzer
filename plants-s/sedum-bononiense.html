Sedum bononiense

Sedum bononiense, commonly known as the Bononiense stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to South America.

It features fleshy, upright stems and produces clusters of small, star-shaped yellow flowers. The plant has thick, waxy leaves that help it store water, making it well adapted to arid environments. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, preferring warm climates.

Sedum bononiense is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and ease of care.
