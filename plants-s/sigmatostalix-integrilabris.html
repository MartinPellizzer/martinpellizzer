Sigmatostalix integrilabris

Sigmatostalix integrilabris, commonly known as the broad-leafed sigmatostalix, is a species of flowering plant native to specific regions in Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically smooth and glossy. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pale green to yellow in color. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
