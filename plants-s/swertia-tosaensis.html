Swertia tosaensis

Swertia tosaensis, commonly known as Tosa gentian, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Swertia tosaensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
