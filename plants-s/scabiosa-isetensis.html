Scabiosa isetensis

Scabiosa isetensis, commonly known as the Alpine Scabious, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils. It has small, hairy leaves that help it retain moisture in harsh climates.

Scabiosa isetensis is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
