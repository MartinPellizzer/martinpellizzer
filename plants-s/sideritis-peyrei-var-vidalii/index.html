Sideritis Peyrei Var. Vidalii

Sideritis peyrei var. vidalii is a rare and lesser-known member of the Lamiaceae family. Native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe, it thrives in rocky and arid environments. This plant is distinguished by its delicate, violet-colored flowers and aromatic leaves. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated and poorly documented in botanical studies. Its unique characteristics make it a fascinating subject for further research and conservation efforts.
