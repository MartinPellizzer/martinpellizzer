Senna leiophylla

Senna leiophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaved senna, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, leathery leaves that are often clustered at the base of the stems. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the warmer months. Senna leiophylla is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showing drought resistance.

It is also known for its medicinal properties, with various parts of the plant used in traditional medicine.
