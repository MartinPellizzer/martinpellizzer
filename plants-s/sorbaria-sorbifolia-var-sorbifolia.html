Sorbaria sorbifolia var. sorbifolia

Sorbaria sorbifolia var. sorbifolia, commonly known as the whitebeard sorbaria, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant produces oval, reddish-brown fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
