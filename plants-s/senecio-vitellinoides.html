Senecio vitellinoides

Senecio vitellinoides, commonly known as the yellow owl's clover, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in some regions.
