Stellaria stagnalis

Stellaria stagnalis, commonly known as lesser starwort, is a small, herbaceous plant native to wetland and marsh environments.

It typically grows to a height of about 10-30 centimeters and produces small, white or pale pink flowers with five petals. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils and is often found in areas with standing water.

This species is part of the Caryophyllaceae family, which includes other flowering plants with similar floral structures.
