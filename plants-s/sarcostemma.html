Sarcostemma

Sarcostemma, commonly known as the milkweed vine, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asclepiadaceae.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and the presence of milky sap, which is a distinguishing botanical feature. The leaves are typically opposite, ovate, and have a rough texture. The flowers are showy, with a distinctive crown of hooked filaments surrounding the reproductive structures.
