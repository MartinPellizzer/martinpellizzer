Seriphidium kurramense

Seriphidium kurramense, commonly known as the Kurram sandthorn, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Pakistan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny stems and yellow flowers. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils. It has a deep root system that allows it to survive in harsh, dry conditions.

Its ecological role includes providing habitat and food for local wildlife.
