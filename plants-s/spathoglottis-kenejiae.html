Spathoglottis kenejiae

Spathoglottis kenejiae, commonly known as the Kenejia's spathoglottis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red and orange. The plant has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, enabling it to thrive in its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually broad and fleshy, adapted to reduce water loss.

Spathoglottis kenejiae is often found in shaded, humid environments such as forests and along stream banks.
