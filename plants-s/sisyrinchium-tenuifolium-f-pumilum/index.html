Sisyrinchium Tenuifolium F. Pumilum

Sisyrinchium tenuifolium f. pumilum is a compact variant of the slenderleaf blue-eyed grass. It is known for its delicate, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. This plant thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland gardens. Its small stature makes it ideal for rock gardens or as a ground cover. It is a popular choice among gardeners seeking low-maintenance, ornamental plants.
