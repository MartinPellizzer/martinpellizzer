Strongyleria pannea

Strongyleria pannea, commonly known as the African snowball, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces clusters of white, fluffy flowers that resemble small balls, giving it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

The botanical features of Strongyleria pannea include its aromatic leaves and showy inflorescences, which make it a popular ornamental plant.
