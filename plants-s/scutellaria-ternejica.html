Scutellaria ternejica

Scutellaria ternejica, commonly known as the Mexican skullcap, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Scutellaria ternejica is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.
