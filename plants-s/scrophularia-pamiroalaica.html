Scrophularia pamiroalaica

Scrophularia pamiroalaica, commonly known as the Pamir Scrophularia, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas and Central Asia.

The plant is characterized by its upright stems, lance-shaped leaves, and clusters of tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple.
