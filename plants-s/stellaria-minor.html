Stellaria minor

Stellaria minor, commonly known as lesser starwort, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It typically reaches a height of 5 to 20 centimeters and features small, delicate white or pink flowers arranged in star-like clusters. The plant has slender, branching stems covered with fine hairs, and its leaves are narrow and opposite, often with a slightly hairy texture. Stellaria minor thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and along stream banks.

It is a perennial species that blooms from late spring to early summer.
