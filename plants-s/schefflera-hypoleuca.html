Schefflera hypoleuca

Schefflera hypoleuca, commonly known as the white-veined umbrella tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, umbrella-like leaves that are typically a deep green with prominent white veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by bright red berries. Schefflera hypoleuca thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
