Statice articulata var. strictissima

Statice articulata var. strictissima, commonly known as sea lavender or desert lavender, is a flowering plant native to arid and coastal regions.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, showy blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is well adapted to salty, windy environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dunes or along shorelines.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize sandy soils.
