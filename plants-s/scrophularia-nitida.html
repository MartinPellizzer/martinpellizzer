Scrophularia nitida

Scrophularia nitida, commonly known as the slender dog's mercury, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae, which includes many species with showy flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are small, clustered, and usually pink or purple in color.

Scrophularia nitida is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
