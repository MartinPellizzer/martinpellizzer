Stelis adrianae

Stelis adrianae, commonly known as Adriana's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial epiphytic plant, typically growing on tree trunks or branches in humid tropical forests. The plant features a slender, wiry stem with numerous small, clustered leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

Stelis adrianae is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or shaded gardens.
