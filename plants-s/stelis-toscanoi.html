Stelis toscanoi

Stelis toscanoi, commonly known as the Toscana orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features slender, wiry stems and produces clusters of small, white to pale green flowers. Its leaves are usually arranged in a rosette pattern and are covered in fine, hairy structures.

Stelis toscanoi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or tropical gardens.
