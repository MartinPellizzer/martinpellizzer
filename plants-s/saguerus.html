Saguerus

Saguerus, commonly known as the sacred fig, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant produces edible fruits that are widely used in traditional medicine and cuisine. Its roots, bark, and leaves contain various bioactive compounds with medicinal properties.

Saguerus is also valued for its ecological role in supporting biodiversity in its native habitats.
