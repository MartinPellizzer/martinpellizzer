Stipa kirghisorum var. ikonnikovii

Stipa kirghisorum var. ikonnikovii, commonly known as the Ikonnikov's feathergrass, is a grass species native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and dense, fluffy panicles that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as steppes and rocky slopes. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in landscaping and ecological restoration projects.
