Sparattanthelium glabrum subsp. angustatum

Sparattanthelium glabrum subsp.

angustatum, commonly known as the narrowleaf spathelium, is a perennial herb native to specific regions of South America. It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves that are typically narrow and glabrous.

The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with pale green to white flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical or subtropical woodlands.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
