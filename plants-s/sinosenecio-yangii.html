Sinosenecio yangii

Sinosenecio yangii, commonly known as the Yang's sinosenecio, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or forested areas and has hairy, narrow leaves. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in a cluster, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.
