Stelis benzingii

Stelis benzingii, commonly known as the Benzing's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments at high altitudes. The plant features a slender, erect stem with several fleshy, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, white to pale green, and have a distinctive spurred lip.

Stelis benzingii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
