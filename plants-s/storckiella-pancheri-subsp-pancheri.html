Storckiella pancheri subsp. pancheri

Storckiella pancheri subsp.

pancheri, commonly known as the Kashmir bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas. It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique floral display.
