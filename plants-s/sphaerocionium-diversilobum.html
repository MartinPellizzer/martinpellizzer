Sphaerocionium diversilobum

Sphaerocionium diversilobum, commonly known as the California starflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit and its distinctive star-shaped flowers. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow blooms that typically appear in the spring. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a lobed or serrated margin.

Sphaerocionium diversilobum thrives in rocky soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climates with dry summers and wet winters.
