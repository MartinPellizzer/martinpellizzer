Scleranthus delortii

Scleranthus delortii, commonly known as Delort's thornweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and typically grows in dry, rocky or sandy soils. The plant features slender, erect stems with small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are usually pink or red, clustered in dense spikes, and bloom during the summer months.

Scleranthus delortii is adapted to arid environments and is often found in open habitats such as coastal dunes and scrublands.
