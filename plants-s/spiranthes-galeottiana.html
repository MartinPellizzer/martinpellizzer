Spiranthes galeottiana

Spiranthes galeottiana, commonly known as the prairie ladies' tresses, is a perennial orchid native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and its distinctive spiral arrangement of fragrant, pale pink to white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and prairies. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Spiranthes galeottiana is known for its ornamental value and is often found in native plant gardens.
