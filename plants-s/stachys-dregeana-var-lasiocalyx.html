Stachys dregeana var. lasiocalyx

Stachys dregeana var. lasiocalyx, commonly known as the woolly betony, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features dense, fuzzy silver-green foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces spikes of violet-blue flowers in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique texture and color contrast.
