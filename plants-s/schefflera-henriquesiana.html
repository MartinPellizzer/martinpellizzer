Schefflera henriquesiana

Schefflera henriquesiana, commonly known as the Henriques' umbrella plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of large, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by black, berry-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Schefflera henriquesiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in indoor settings.
