Salacca dolicholepis

Salacca dolicholepis, commonly known as the long-stemmed bamboo palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Arecaceae family, which includes palms, and is characterized by its slender, clustered stems and large, pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces inflorescences that bear small, fragrant flowers, which are typically followed by edible, sweet fruits.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor landscaping in warm climates.
