Salacia germainii var. germainii

Salacia germainii var. germainii, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery red flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant belongs to the family Salicaceae, which includes willows and poplars, though it is not closely related to them. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate, with a glossy green surface.

The tree can grow up to 10 meters tall and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and parks.
