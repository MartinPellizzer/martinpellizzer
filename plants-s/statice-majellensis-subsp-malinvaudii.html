Statice majellensis subsp. malinvaudii

Statice majellensis subsp.

malinvaudii, commonly known as the Malinvaud's statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, spike-like inflorescences composed of numerous small, brightly colored flowers.

The species typically blooms in late summer, producing flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
