Saccolabium epichysiochilum

Saccolabium epichysiochilum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved silverleaf.

It is a perennial shrub native to South Africa, characterized by its silver-gray foliage and compact growth habit. The plant features small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale blue, blooming in spring. Its leaves are narrow and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs, contributing to its silvery appearance.

Saccolabium epichysiochilum is well adapted to arid conditions and is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance.
