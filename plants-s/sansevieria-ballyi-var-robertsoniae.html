Sansevieria ballyi var. robertsoniae

Sansevieria ballyi var. robertsoniae, commonly known as the Robertson's snake plant, is a succulent perennial native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with subtle yellowish markings. The plant is known for its air-purifying properties and ability to thrive in low-light conditions. It has a rosette growth habit and produces clusters of small, white flowers under optimal conditions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
