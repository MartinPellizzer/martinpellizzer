Scleranthus holanthus

Scleranthus holanthus, commonly known as the common scarlet pimpernel, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is an annual herb that grows up to 30 centimeters tall, characterized by its slender stems and small, bright yellow flowers. The leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and waste areas.

The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to bloom throughout the summer months.
