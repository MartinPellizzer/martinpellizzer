Socratea altissima

Socratea altissima, commonly known as the royal palm, is a tall, slender palm native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, arching fronds that give it a distinctive, elegant appearance. The plant has a robust root system that allows it to thrive in a variety of soil types, including those with poor drainage. Its trunk is often covered in a fibrous, spongy material that helps it withstand strong winds.

The royal palm is also notable for its ability to grow in urban environments, making it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical cities.
