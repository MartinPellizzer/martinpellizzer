Sonchus nigricans

Sonchus nigricans, commonly known as black sowthistle, is a herbaceous annual plant native to temperate regions of Europe, Asia, and North America.

It features a basal rosette of spiny, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, which are followed by spherical, prickly seed heads. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and gardens.

Botanically, it belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its rapid growth and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
