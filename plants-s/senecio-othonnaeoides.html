Senecio othonnaeoides

Senecio othonnaeoides, commonly known as the Cape Harebell, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
