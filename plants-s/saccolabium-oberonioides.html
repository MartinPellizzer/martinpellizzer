Saccolabium oberonioides

Saccolabium oberonioides, commonly known as the pink burr bush, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Argentina and Chile.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its woody stems and compound leaves. The plant produces showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Its fruit is a dry, dehiscent pod that splits open to release small, hairy seeds.
