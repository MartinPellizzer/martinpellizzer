Sporobolus × bolanderi

Sporobolus × bolanderi, commonly known as Bolander's needlegrass, is a hybrid grass species native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and fine, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a layer of fine hairs. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are typically green or brown in color. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

Sporobolus × bolanderi is often used in restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for native wildlife.
