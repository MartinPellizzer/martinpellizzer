Syringa philemon

Syringa philemon, commonly known as the Chinese lilac, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, clustered flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant produces small, oval leaves that are dark green and glossy on top. Syringa philemon typically grows to a height of 6 to 10 feet and thrives in well-drained soil.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its showy floral display.
