Solanum urbanum var. subtomentosum

Solanum urbanum var. subtomentosum, commonly known as the woolly nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features hairy, glandular leaves that are typically ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by berries that range in color from yellow to red. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
