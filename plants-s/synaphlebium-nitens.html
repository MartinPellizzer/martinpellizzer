Synaphlebium nitens

Synaphlebium nitens, commonly known as the silverleaf thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, aromatic leaves that are typically covered in a fine, silvery bloom, giving it its common name. The plant produces tiny, pink or white flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a valuable addition to both garden and horticultural collections.
