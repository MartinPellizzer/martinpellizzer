Schefflera ischyrocephala

Schefflera ischyrocephala, commonly known as the African shield fern, is a tropical plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of leaves, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant features compound leaves with multiple leaflets, often arranged in a radiating pattern. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its foliage is evergreen, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
