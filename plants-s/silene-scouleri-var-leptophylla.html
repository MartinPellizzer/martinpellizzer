Silene scouleri var. leptophylla

Silene scouleri var. leptophylla, commonly known as the slenderleaf catchfly, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, clustered inflorescences and narrow, linear leaves.
