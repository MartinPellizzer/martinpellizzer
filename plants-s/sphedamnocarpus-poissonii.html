Sphedamnocarpus poissonii

Sphedamnocarpus poissonii, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking yellow petals and a distinctive golden center, giving it its common name. The plant has a compact, pseudobulbous structure with fleshy leaves that store water, aiding in its survival in humid, tropical environments. Its flowers bloom in clusters, often in late spring or early summer, and are highly fragrant.

The species is prized by horticulturists for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
