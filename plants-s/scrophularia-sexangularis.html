Scrophularia sexangularis

Scrophularia sexangularis, commonly known as the angular scrophularia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features square, hairy stems and opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are tubular, usually pink or purple, and are arranged in spikes at the top of the stems.

It is part of the Scrophulariaceae family, known for its medicinal and ornamental value.
