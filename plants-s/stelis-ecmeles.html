Stelis ecmeles

Stelis ecmeles, commonly known as the golden spike orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow and green coloration. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are clustered in racemes and feature a unique, spike-like structure.

Stelis ecmeles thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained, loose soil for optimal growth.
