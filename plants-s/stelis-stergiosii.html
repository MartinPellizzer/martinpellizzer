Stelis stergiosii

Stelis stergiosii, commonly known as the Serpentine Stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows on tree branches or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are elongated and arranged in a rosette pattern, and it has a delicate, branched root system.

Stelis stergiosii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
