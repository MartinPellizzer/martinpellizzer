Schumanniophyton klaineanum

Schumanniophyton klaineanum, commonly known as the Klaineanum cycad, is a member of the Cycadaceae family and is native to Madagascar.

It is a dioecious plant, meaning it has separate male and female plants, and produces cones for reproduction. The plant has a thick, unbranched trunk that is covered with smooth, waxy leaves. Its foliage consists of large, pinnately compound leaves that can reach up to several meters in length.

This species is valued for its ornamental appearance and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
