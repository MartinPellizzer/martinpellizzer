Sindora echinocalyx

Sindora echinocalyx, commonly known as the spiny-lobed sindora, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-bearing fruits. The plant features prominently thorned stems and leaves with serrated edges, contributing to its distinctive appearance. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, with a yellowish or greenish hue.

The fruit is a legume, containing several seeds that are dispersed by wind or animals.
