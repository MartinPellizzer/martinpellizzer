Scutellaria rumicifolia

Scutellaria rumicifolia, commonly known as blueweed or heartleaf blueweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular blue or violet flowers that bloom in late summer. Scutellaria rumicifolia thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
