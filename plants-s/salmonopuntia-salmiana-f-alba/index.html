Salmonopuntia Salmiana F. Alba

White ghost cactus, scientifically known as Salmonopuntia salmiana f. alba, is a striking and rare plant native to the arid regions of Mexico. Its name derives from its pale, ghostly appearance, which gives it an ethereal and otherworldly look. This cactus is characterized by its large, fleshy stems that are covered in soft, white spines, creating a unique visual effect. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for desert gardens and succulent collections. Due to its beauty and rarity, the White ghost cactus is highly sought after by plant enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
