Stelis amilotensis

Stelis amilotensis, commonly known as the Amilotens orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces strikingly colorful flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or branches, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink with dark markings, and it blooms during the rainy season.

Stelis amilotensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
