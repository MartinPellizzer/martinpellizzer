Sadleria fauriei

Sadleria fauriei, commonly known as the Faurie's spiderwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features slender, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, delicate blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Sadleria fauriei is relatively low maintenance and resistant to pests and diseases.
