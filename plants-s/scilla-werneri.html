Scilla werneri

Scilla werneri, commonly known as Werneri's scilla, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of delicate, bell-shaped blue or violet flowers in early spring. The plant is often found in rocky soils and open woodlands, thriving in well-drained conditions. Its bulbs are perennial, allowing it to return year after year.

Scilla werneri is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and ease of cultivation.
