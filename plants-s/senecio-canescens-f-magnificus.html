Senecio canescens f. magnificus

Senecio canescens f. magnificus, commonly known as the showy senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features bright yellow ray flowers surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers, creating a striking visual display. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
