Stelis dalstroemii

Stelis dalstroemii, commonly known as the Dalsborg orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant prefers shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the surrounding soil, and it has a symbiotic relationship with mycorrhizal fungi.

Stelis dalstroemii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its delicate floral display.
