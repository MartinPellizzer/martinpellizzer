Silene candicans

Silene candicans, commonly known as the showy campion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking pink to red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

Silene candicans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
