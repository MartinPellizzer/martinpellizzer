Schefflera maduraiensis

Schefflera maduraiensis, commonly known as the Madurai schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to southern India.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of leaves, which gives it a distinctive and ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by spherical, berry-like fruits that turn red when mature. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Schefflera maduraiensis is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor conditions.
