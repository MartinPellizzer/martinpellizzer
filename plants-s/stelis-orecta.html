Stelis orecta

Stelis orecta, commonly known as the erect stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Stelis orecta is valued in horticulture for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
