Swartzia viridiflora

Swartzia viridiflora, commonly known as the green-flowered swartzia, is a tropical tree native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in moist, lowland forests and is valued for its hard, durable wood.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable in some regions.
