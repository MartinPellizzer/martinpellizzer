Senecio cinerascens

Senecio cinerascens, commonly known as the silverleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name, and its small, yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Senecio cinerascens is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and along rocky outcrops.
