Senna sophera

Senna sophera, commonly known as the yellow senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in some cultures.

Senna sophera thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun for optimal growth.
