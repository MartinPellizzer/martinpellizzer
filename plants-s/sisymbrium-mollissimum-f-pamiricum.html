Sisymbrium mollissimum f. pamiricum

Sisymbrium mollissimum f. pamiricum, commonly known as the Pamir hedge mustard, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming structure with soft, hairy leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, including the Pamir Mountains.

This species is adapted to harsh, alpine environments and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils.
