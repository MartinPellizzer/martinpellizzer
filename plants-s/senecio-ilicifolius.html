Senecio ilicifolius

Senecio ilicifolius, commonly known as the silverleaf senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray, fuzzy leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow daisy-like flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is often cultivated in gardens.
