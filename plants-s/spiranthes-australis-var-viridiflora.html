Spiranthes australis var. viridiflora

Spiranthes australis var. viridiflora, commonly known as the green-flowered ladies' tresses, is a perennial orchid native to Australia.

It features slender, grass-like leaves and produces dense, spiral arrangements of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as wetlands and forest floors. Its flowering period occurs in late winter to early spring, and it relies on specific pollinators for reproduction.

The species is characterized by its unique floral structure and is often found in coastal and inland regions of southern Australia.
