Swartzia lamellata var. kaieteurensis

Swartzia lamellata var. kaieteurensis, commonly known as the Kaieteure Swartzia, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its pod-like fruits and often nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant typically grows as a small to medium-sized tree or shrub, with compound leaves and showy, clustered flowers. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its timber or as a landscape plant.
