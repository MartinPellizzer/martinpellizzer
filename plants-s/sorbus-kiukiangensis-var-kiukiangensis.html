Sorbus kiukiangensis var. kiukiangensis

Sorbus kiukiangensis var. kiukiangensis, commonly known as the Chinese whitebeam, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and alternate, simple, ovate leaves that are typically reddish in color. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters, followed by bright red berries that are an important food source for birds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or mountainous regions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
