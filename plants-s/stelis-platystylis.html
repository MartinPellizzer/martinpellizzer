Stelis platystylis

Stelis platystylis, commonly known as the broad-styled orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or branches, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture from the air.

Stelis platystylis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
