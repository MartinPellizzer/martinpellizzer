Stoebe squarrosa

Stoebe squarrosa, commonly known as roughleaf betony, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. The plant features hairy, square stems and opposite, heart-shaped leaves. Its flowers are blue to purple, forming whorls along the stems, and it blooms from June to August.

It is valued in native gardens for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
