Satureja calamintha subsp. officinalis

Satureja calamintha subsp.

officinalis, commonly known as mountain savory, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It features small, pinkish-white flowers and aromatic, lance-shaped leaves that are often used in culinary and medicinal applications.

The plant thrives in well-drained soils and sunny to partially shaded environments. Its essential oils contain compounds like thymol and carvacrol, which contribute to its antimicrobial properties.

It is also valued in traditional herbal medicine for its digestive and anti-inflammatory benefits.
