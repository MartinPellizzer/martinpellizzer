Stachys assoana

Stachys assoana, commonly known as the Ethiopian violet, is a perennial herb native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces spikes of purple or violet flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
