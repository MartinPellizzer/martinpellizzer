Schollera erecta

Schollera erecta, commonly known as the silver tree, is a striking evergreen shrub or small tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-gray foliage that provides a dramatic visual contrast against its dark bark. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers in clusters, which are followed by orange-red berries that attract birds. Schollera erecta thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a notable addition to gardens and landscapes.
