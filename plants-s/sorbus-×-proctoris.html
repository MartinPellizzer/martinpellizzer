Sorbus × proctoris

Sorbus × proctoris, commonly known as the proctor's rowan, is a hybrid species within the genus Sorbus, native to the United Kingdom.

It is characterized by its small, yellowish-orange berries that mature in the autumn. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have serrated edges.

The flowers are white and appear in clusters, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.
