Statice argentea

Statice argentea, commonly known as silver statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, delicate flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant has narrow, silver-gray leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for garden borders and coastal landscapes.

Statice argentea is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in dried flower arrangements.
