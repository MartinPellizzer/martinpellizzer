Sisymbrium philippianum

Sisymbrium philippianum, commonly known as the Philippian winter cress, is an annual flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other mustard plants. Sisymbrium philippianum is known for its rapid growth and ability to colonize open areas quickly.
