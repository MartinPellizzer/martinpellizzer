Stachys tlaxiacana

Stachys tlaxiacana, commonly known as Mexican hedge nettle, is a herbaceous plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and stream banks. Its small, clustered flowers are usually pink or purple, and it produces small, hairy seeds.

Stachys tlaxiacana is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
