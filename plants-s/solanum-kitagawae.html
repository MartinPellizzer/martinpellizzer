Solanum kitagawae

Solanum kitagawae, commonly known as the Japanese nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale purple. Its fruit is a yellow or orange berry that ripens in the fall.

Solanum kitagawae is often found in woodland areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
