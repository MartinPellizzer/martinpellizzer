Spermacoce diversistyla

Spermacoce diversistyla, commonly known as the African morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are usually white or pink. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, black, smooth seeds.

The plant is often found in disturbed soils and is adapted to thrive in warm, humid climates.
