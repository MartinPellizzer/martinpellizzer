Smelowskia ovalis var. typica

Smelowskia ovalis var. typica, commonly known as the oval leaved smelowskia, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elliptical, smooth, and glossy leaves that range in color from dark green to deep purple. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, blooming during the warmer months. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for cultivation in tropical and subtropical climates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes grown in botanical gardens and private collections.
