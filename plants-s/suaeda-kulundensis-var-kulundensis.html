Suaeda kulundensis var. kulundensis

Suaeda kulundensis var. kulundensis, commonly known as the Kulunda saltbush, is a halophytic shrub adapted to saline and arid environments.

It is native to Central Asia, particularly found in regions with high salinity and low precipitation. The plant has small, fleshy leaves that help reduce water loss, and it produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to access deep water sources in nutrient-poor soils.

This species plays a role in stabilizing soils in coastal and desert areas.
