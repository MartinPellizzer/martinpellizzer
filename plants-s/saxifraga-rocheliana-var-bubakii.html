Saxifraga rocheliana var. bubakii

Saxifraga rocheliana var. bubakii, commonly known as the Balkan saxifrage, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

It is a perennial herb with succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette form. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers during the summer months, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in rocky or limestone soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Due to its specific ecological requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
