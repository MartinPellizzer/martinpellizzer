Scrophularia elegantissima

Scrophularia elegantissima, commonly known as the elegant scrophularia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Scrophulariaceae.

It is native to parts of Europe and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically blooms in late spring to early summer, and its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens and wildflower meadows.

Scrophularia elegantissima is also valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional herbal medicine.
