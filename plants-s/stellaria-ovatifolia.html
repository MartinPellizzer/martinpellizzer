Stellaria ovatifolia

Stellaria ovatifolia, commonly known as the ovateleaf starwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like carnations and chickweed. The plant is characterized by its slender stems and small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are ovate in shape and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Stellaria ovatifolia typically thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
