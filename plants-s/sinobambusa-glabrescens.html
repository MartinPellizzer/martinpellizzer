Sinobambusa glabrescens

Sinobambusa glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth bamboo, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family.

It is native to eastern Asia, particularly China, where it grows in moist, temperate regions. The plant is characterized by its slender, smooth stalks and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in panicles.

Sinobambusa glabrescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a decorative plant.
