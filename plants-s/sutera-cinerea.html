Sutera cinerea

Sutera cinerea, commonly known as silverleaf sage, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, pale blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer. Sutera cinerea is often found in rocky or dry soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and aromatic properties.
