Scutellaria hsiehii

Scutellaria hsiehii, commonly known as the Hsieh's skullcap, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It is often found in rocky or mountainous regions, growing in well-drained soils.

Scutellaria hsiehii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for potential medicinal properties.
