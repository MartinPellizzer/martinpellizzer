Statice formosa

Statice formosa, commonly known as the Chinese statice, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Poaceae, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks. Statice formosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional Chinese medicine.
