Stelis meerenbergensis

Stelis meerenbergensis, commonly known as the Meerenberg orchid, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its epiphytic growth habit, often found growing on trees or rocks in humid environments. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, with intricate patterns on the petals. It thrives in shaded, moist conditions and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
