Sphaerocoma hookeri

Sphaerocoma hookeri, commonly known as the Hooker's globe-mallow, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant features showy, globe-shaped flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are palmately divided, giving it a distinctive appearance.
