Senecio paulsenii

Senecio paulsenii, commonly known as the Paulsen's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. Senecio paulsenii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and resilience in dry conditions.
