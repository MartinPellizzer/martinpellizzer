Solanum heudesii

Solanum heudesii, commonly known as the African nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into spherical, red berries. Solanum heudesii is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

It contains toxic alkaloids and is not typically cultivated for agricultural purposes.
