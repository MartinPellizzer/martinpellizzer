Salacia conferta var. genuina

Salacia conferta var. genuina, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, pendulous clusters of yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has elliptical, glossy leaves and produces small, fleshy fruits that are edible. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The species is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
