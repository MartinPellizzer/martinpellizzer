Sedum bucharicum

Sedum bucharicum, commonly known as the Bukhara stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Sedum bucharicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
