Sisymbrium blennodia

Sisymbrium blennodia, commonly known as the narrowleaf winter cress, is a member of the Brassicaceae family.

It is a herbaceous annual plant characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is native to Europe and parts of Asia. Its leaves are alternate, pinnately divided, and often have a slightly hairy texture.
