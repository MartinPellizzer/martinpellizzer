Spiraeopsis celebica

Spiraeopsis celebica, commonly known as the Celebes spiraeoid, is a flowering plant native to the Maluku Islands of Indonesia.

Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a serrated margin, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Spiraeopsis celebica is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and as a decorative plant in tropical regions.
