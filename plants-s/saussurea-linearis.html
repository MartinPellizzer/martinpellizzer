Saussurea linearis

Saussurea linearis, commonly known as the linear aster, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central and eastern Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and showy yellow to orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, often at high elevations where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Saussurea linearis is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies for various ailments.

Its ecological role includes providing nectar for pollinators and contributing to the biodiversity of its mountainous environment.
