Sorbus prattii var. prattii

Sorbus prattii var. prattii, commonly known as the Chinese whitebeam, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its rounded, often multi-stemmed form. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptical in shape, with a smooth or slightly serrated margin. Its small, white to pale yellow flowers appear in clusters, followed by small, berry-like fruits that are typically red or orange in color.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and seasonal interest.
