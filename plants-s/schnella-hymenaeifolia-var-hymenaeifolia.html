Schnella hymenaeifolia var. hymenaeifolia

Schnella hymenaeifolia var. hymenaeifolia, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are velvety to the touch.

The leaves are typically dark green with a dense covering of fine hairs, giving the plant its distinctive texture. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or yellow, followed by kidney-shaped seeds enclosed in a capsule. The plant is native to Central and South America and thrives in warm, humid environments.

It is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and agricultural fields.
