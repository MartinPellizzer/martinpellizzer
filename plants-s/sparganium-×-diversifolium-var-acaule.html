Sparganium × diversifolium var. acaule

Sparganium × diversifolium var. acaule, commonly known as the slender bur-reed, is a hybrid plant native to wetlands and marshy areas in North America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems that lack a distinct stem base, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces elongated, cylindrical spikes of yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming dense clumps at the base.

It plays an important ecological role in wetland habitats by providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
