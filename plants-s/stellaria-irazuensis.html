Stellaria irazuensis

Stellaria irazuensis, commonly known as the Irazu starflower, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Costa Rica.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in cloud forests. Its leaves are ovate and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are slender and branching.

Stellaria irazuensis is notable for its ornamental value and ecological significance in its native habitat.
