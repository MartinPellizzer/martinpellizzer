Soldanella × transsilvanica

Soldanella × transsilvanica, commonly known as the Transylvanian sandwort, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Montiaceae.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit and features small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
