Stelis lancipetala

Stelis lancipetala, commonly known as the narrow-leaved stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is an epiphytic orchid, typically found growing on trees in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has slender, linear leaves that are often clustered at the base. Its flowers are usually small and white or pale green, with a distinctive lip.

Stelis lancipetala thrives in humid environments with high light exposure and well-draining substrates.
