Schwenckia patens

Schwenckia patens, commonly known as the common ironweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features upright, branching stems covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are lance-shaped with serrated edges. It produces showy purple to pink flower heads that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Schwenckia patens is often found in moist to wet habitats, including marshes and stream banks.
