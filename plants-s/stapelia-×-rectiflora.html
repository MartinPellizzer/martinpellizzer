Stapelia × rectiflora

Stapelia × rectiflora, commonly known as the starry stapelia, is a hybrid succulent native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems that store water, typical of succulent plants. The plant produces striking, star-shaped flowers that are often pale yellow or orange with a distinctive central marking. These flowers are designed to attract pollinators such as flies and beetles.

Stapelia × rectiflora is relatively easy to care for, requiring bright light and well-draining soil.
