Solanum luridum

Solanum luridum, commonly known as the yellow nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 meter. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its berries are initially green and turn red when ripe, often attracting birds.

Solanum luridum contains toxic alkaloids and should be handled with care.
