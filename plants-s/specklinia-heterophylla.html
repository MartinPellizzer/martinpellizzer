Specklinia heterophylla

Specklinia heterophylla, commonly known as the variable speckled fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, variegated foliage with a mix of green and white or pale yellow markings. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest undergrowth. Its fronds are usually pinnate, with a delicate, feathery appearance.

Specklinia heterophylla is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid, low-light conditions.
