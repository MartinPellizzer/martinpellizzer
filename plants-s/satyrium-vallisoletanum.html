Satyrium vallisoletanum

Satyrium vallisoletanum, commonly known as the Spanish orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny to partially shaded environments. Its flowers are usually pink or purple and have a unique, intricate structure typical of orchids.

It is often found in Mediterranean regions and is valued for its ornamental beauty.
