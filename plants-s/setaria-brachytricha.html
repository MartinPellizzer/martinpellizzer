Setaria brachytricha

Setaria brachytricha, commonly known as slender barnyardgrass, is an annual grass species native to Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences with pale green to brown bracts. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural fields.

Its botanical features make it a common weed in various crops, including rice and corn.
