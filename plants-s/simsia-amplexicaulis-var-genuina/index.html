Simsia Amplexicaulis Var. Genuina

Simsia amplexicaulis var. genuina is a rare and unique plant native to specific regions of South America. It is known for its distinctive foliage and subtle floral characteristics. This variant stands out due to its compact growth habit and striking leaf arrangement. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively unknown to the general public. Its cultivation requires careful attention to environmental conditions to thrive.
