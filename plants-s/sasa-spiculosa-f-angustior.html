Sasa spiculosa f. angustior

Sasa spiculosa f. angustior, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bamboo, is a variant of the Sasa spiculosa species native to East Asia.

It is a clump-forming shrub that belongs to the grass family Poaceae, though it is often classified under the bamboo subfamily. The plant is characterized by its slender, narrow leaves that are typically a deep green in color. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its growth habit makes it suitable for use in ornamental landscaping and erosion control.
