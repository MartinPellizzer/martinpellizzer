Sideritis spinosa

Sideritis spinosa, commonly known as the spiny sainfoin, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

Sideritis spinosa is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
