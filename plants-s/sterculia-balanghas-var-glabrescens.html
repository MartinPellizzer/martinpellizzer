Sterculia balanghas var. glabrescens

Sterculia balanghas var. glabrescens, commonly known as the Philippine tamarind tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and large, heart-shaped leaves. The tree produces yellowish flowers followed by elongated, woody pods containing edible seeds.
