Senecio sophioides

Senecio sophioides, commonly known as the African violet or African violet-like plant, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant features silver-green, hairy leaves that are often mistaken for those of true African violets. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Senecio sophioides is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
