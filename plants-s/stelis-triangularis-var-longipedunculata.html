Stelis triangularis var. longipedunculata

Stelis triangularis var. longipedunculata, commonly known as the long-pedunculate stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, often branched growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on tree trunks or rocks. Its leaves are linear to lanceolate, and the inflorescence consists of multiple flower spikes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
