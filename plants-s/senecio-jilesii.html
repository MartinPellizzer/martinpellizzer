Senecio jilesii

Senecio jilesii, commonly known as the Karoo Senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant is typically shrub-like, with hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is often cultivated in rock gardens and arid landscapes.
