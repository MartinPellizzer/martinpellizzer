Stelis microcaulis

Stelis microcaulis, commonly known as the miniature stem orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, tropical environments. The plant has slender, wiry stems and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and yellow. Its leaves are narrow and arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps it conserve moisture.

Stelis microcaulis is valued for its unique appearance and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
