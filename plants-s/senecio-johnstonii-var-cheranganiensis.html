Senecio johnstonii var. cheranganiensis

Senecio johnstonii var. cheranganiensis, commonly known as the Cherangani's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to Kenya.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, adapting to harsh environmental conditions. It has small, linear leaves and produces numerous small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in the spring.

This variety is notable for its resilience and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
