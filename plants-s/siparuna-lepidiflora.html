Siparuna lepidiflora

Siparuna lepidiflora, commonly known as the velvetleaf siparuna, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the wet season. Its fruit is a capsule containing several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Siparuna lepidiflora is valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
