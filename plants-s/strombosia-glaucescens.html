Strombosia glaucescens

Strombosia glaucescens, commonly known as the blue-green strombosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is a woody shrub or small tree that typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant features simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered, often with a fragrant aroma, and it produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

Strombosia glaucescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
