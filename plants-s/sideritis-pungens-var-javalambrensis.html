Sideritis pungens var. javalambrensis

Sideritis pungens var. javalambrensis, commonly known as the Javalambrense Sideritis, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, pale blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used traditionally for its calming and antispasmodic effects.
