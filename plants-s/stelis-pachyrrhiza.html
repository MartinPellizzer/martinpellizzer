Stelis pachyrrhiza

Stelis pachyrrhiza, commonly known as the thick-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its robust, often hairy stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Stelis pachyrrhiza is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in tropical gardens.
