Sedum mingjinianum

Sedum mingjinianum, commonly known as the Mingjinian stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to China.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

Due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping.
