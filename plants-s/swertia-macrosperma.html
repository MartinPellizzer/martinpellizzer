Swertia macrosperma

Swertia macrosperma, commonly known as Himalayan gentian, is a perennial herb native to the Himalayan region.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows at high altitudes, often in rocky or alpine environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Swertia macrosperma is valued for its ornamental beauty and has traditional medicinal uses in some cultures.
