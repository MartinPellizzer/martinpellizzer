Sida stenopetala

Sida stenopetala, commonly known as the narrow-petioled sida, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions. The plant features small, pale green leaves with a hairy surface, and its flowers are usually pale yellow or white. Sida stenopetala is known for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions and withstand drought.

It is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
