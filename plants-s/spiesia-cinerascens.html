Spiesia cinerascens

Spiesia cinerascens, commonly known as the ash-gray spurge, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in coastal and sandy habitats. The plant features small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, and its leaves are usually narrow and slightly hairy. It has a distinctive ash-gray coloration on its stems and young leaves.

Spiesia cinerascens is known for its resilience to coastal conditions and is often used in ecological restoration projects.
