Scorzonera laciniata f. subulata

Scorzonera laciniata f. subulata, commonly known as the slender leaf scorzonera, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Compositae (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its finely divided, needle-like leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers with ray florets, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is cultivated in some gardens and naturalized areas.
