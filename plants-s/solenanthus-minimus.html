Solenanthus minimus

Solenanthus minimus, commonly known as the small solenanthus, is a flowering plant native to the island of Tongatapu in the Kingdom of Tonga.

It is a small shrub that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters, characterized by its narrow, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom during the warmer months.
