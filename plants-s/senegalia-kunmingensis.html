Senegalia kunmingensis

Senegalia kunmingensis, commonly known as the Kunming acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its feathery, bipinnate leaves and yellow, globular flower heads. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it well adapted to dry climates. It is often used in landscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.

The seeds of Senegalia kunmingensis are encased in pods and can be used for various traditional purposes.
