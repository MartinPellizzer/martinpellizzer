Spodiopogon sibiricus var. purpurascens

Spodiopogon sibiricus var. purpurascens, commonly known as the Siberian spikerush, is a perennial grass-like plant native to the colder regions of Asia.

It features slender, cylindrical stems that are often reddish or purplish in color, giving it its varietal name. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in wet or moist environments such as marshes and wet meadows.

Its botanical structure is adapted to tolerate cold climates and waterlogged soils.
