Salmia guineensis

Salmia guineensis, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to West Africa.

It belongs to the family Gesneriaceae and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of violet, pink, and white, often with contrasting markings. Its leaves are typically velvety and heart-shaped, contributing to its ornamental appeal.
