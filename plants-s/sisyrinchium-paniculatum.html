Sisyrinchium paniculatum

Sisyrinchium paniculatum, commonly known as the blue-eyed grass, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

Sisyrinchium paniculatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native plant gardens.
