Sabicea multibracteata

Sabicea multibracteata, commonly known as the many-bract sabicea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features compound leaves with alternate arrangement and small, clustered flowers. Its inflorescence consists of multiple bracts, which are modified leaves that often resemble petals.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in horticultural settings.
