Suregada Mindanaensis

Suregada mindanaensis is a rare and endemic flowering plant found only in the Philippines, specifically in the mountainous regions of Mindanao. This plant belongs to the Malvaceae family and is known for its unique appearance and ecological significance. Its delicate pink and white flowers bloom during the dry season, making it a striking feature of its native habitat. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is classified as endangered, highlighting the need for conservation efforts. Studying Suregada mindanaensis offers valuable insights into the biodiversity and ecological balance of the region.
