Stemmodontia brachycarpa

Stemmodontia brachycarpa, commonly known as the short-fruited stemmodontia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its woody stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruit is a small, capsule-like structure that contains several seeds.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.
