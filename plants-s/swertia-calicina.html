Swertia calicina

Swertia calicina, commonly known as the Himalayan gentian, is a perennial herb native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in cool and moist conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while its stem is erect and branched.

Swertia calicina is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
