Stemmacantha

Stemmacantha, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, woody stems and dense, velvety foliage. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Stemmacantha is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
