Satyrium cernuum

Satyrium cernuum, commonly known as the nodding orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, drooping inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its flowers are small, greenish-yellow, and often have a faint fragrance.

The leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette, contributing to its low-growing habit.
