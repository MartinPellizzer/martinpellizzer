Stelechocarpus nitidus

Stelechocarpus nitidus, commonly known as the glossy ironwood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, hard, and heavy wood, which is highly valued for its durability and strength. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are typically glossy and dark green in color. Its flowers are small and clustered, and it produces woody, spiny fruits that contain seeds.

The species is also noted for its ecological importance in forest ecosystems.
