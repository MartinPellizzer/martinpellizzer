Senecio foliosus

Senecio foliosus, commonly known as the silverleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray leaves that are covered in fine, dense hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Senecio foliosus is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
