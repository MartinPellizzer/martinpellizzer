Solanum monanthemon

Solanum monanthemon, commonly known as the white nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the summer months. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

The fruit is a small, oval berry that changes color from green to red as it matures.
