Schefflera mannii var. lancifolia

Schefflera mannii var. lancifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved umbrella plant, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of large, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters in its natural habitat. Its foliage is often glossy and dark green, providing a striking visual appeal.

It thrives in humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant in tropical and subtropical gardens.
