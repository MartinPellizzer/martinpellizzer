Salacia chlorion

Salacia chlorion, commonly known as the green salacia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Salicaceae, which includes willows and poplars. The plant is characterized by its small, yellowish-green flowers and elongated, narrow leaves. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth.

Salacia chlorion is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
