Stemodia tenuiflora

Stemodia tenuiflora, commonly known as narrow-flowered stemodia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in coastal and sandy environments, showing adaptations to arid conditions. Its slender stems and small, tubular flowers are typical of the genus.

It is often found in open vegetation and is valued for its ornamental potential in gardens.
