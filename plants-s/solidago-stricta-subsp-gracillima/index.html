Solidago Stricta Subsp. Gracillima

Solidago stricta subsp. gracillima is a delicate member of the sunflower family native to the eastern united states. it thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. its slender stems and small yellow flowers make it a subtle yet striking addition to natural landscapes. this subspecies is often overlooked due to its modest appearance but plays an important role in supporting local ecosystems. it is a valuable plant for conservation efforts and wildflower gardens alike.
