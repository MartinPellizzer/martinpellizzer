Stelis convoluta

Stelis convoluta, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or branches, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are elliptical and arranged in a spiral pattern, giving it its common name.

Stelis convoluta thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, loose soil for optimal growth.
