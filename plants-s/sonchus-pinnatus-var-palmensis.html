Sonchus pinnatus var. palmensis

Sonchus pinnatus var. palmensis, commonly known as the palmleaf sowthistle, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its palmately lobed leaves and yellow composite flowers. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in moist, disturbed soils. The plant is often found in wetlands, along stream banks, and in cultivated areas.

It has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
