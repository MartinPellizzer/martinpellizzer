Stachys baicalensis f. intermedia

Stachys baicalensis f. intermedia, commonly known as the intermediate Chinese skullcap, is a perennial herb native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture and produces clusters of small, pale blue to violet flowers. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky slopes.

This plant is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly for its potential anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects.
