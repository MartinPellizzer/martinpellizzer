Schefflera hainanensis

Schefflera hainanensis, commonly known as Hainan umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the island of Hainan in China.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of large, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by bright red berries, which are attractive to birds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Due to its unique foliage, it is popular in horticulture for indoor and outdoor use.
