Senecio khorasanicus

Senecio khorasanicus, commonly known as the Persian groundsel, is a flowering plant native to Iran.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showing a high degree of drought tolerance. Its leaves are usually gray-green with a fuzzy texture, and it produces small, wind-dispersed seeds.

It is often used in rock gardens and as an ornamental plant due to its hardy nature and aesthetic appeal.
