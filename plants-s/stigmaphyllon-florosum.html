Stigmaphyllon florosum

Stigmaphyllon florosum, commonly known as the Brazilian morning glory, is a climbing vine native to South America.

It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. Its rapid growth and ornamental value make it a popular choice for horticultural use.
