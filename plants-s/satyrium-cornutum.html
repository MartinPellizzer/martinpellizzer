Satyrium cornutum

Satyrium cornutum, commonly known as the horned orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to the wetlands of eastern Australia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a distinctive appearance, featuring a single, showy orchid-like flower on a slender stem. The plant produces a single, erect, pale yellow or greenish-yellow flower with a hooded labellum. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in swampy or marshy habitats.

Satyrium cornutum is a protected species due to its limited distribution and sensitivity to habitat disturbance.
