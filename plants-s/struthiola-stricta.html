Struthiola stricta

Struthiola stricta, commonly known as the rigid struthiola, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. Its small, yellowish flowers bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months.

Struthiola stricta is known for its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in poor soils.
