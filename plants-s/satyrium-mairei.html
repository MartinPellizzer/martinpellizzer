Satyrium mairei

Satyrium mairei, commonly known as the Maire's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated flowers that typically range in color from white to pale pink. The plant grows in moist, shaded environments, often in mountainous regions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its aesthetic appeal.

The orchid is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
