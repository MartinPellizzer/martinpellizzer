Stapelia variegata var. trisulca

Stapelia variegata var. trisulca, commonly known as the Trisulcate Stapelia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces striking, odorless, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange. Its leaves are usually small and may be reduced to spines in some varieties.

It is a popular choice for succulent gardens due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
