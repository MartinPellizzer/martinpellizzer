Syngonium podophyllum var. albolineatum

Syngonium podophyllum var. albolineatum, commonly known as the White-veined arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves with striking white veins that contrast with its dark green foliage. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth. Its rapid growth rate makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and as a houseplant.

The variegation in its leaves adds aesthetic value, making it a desirable ornamental plant.
