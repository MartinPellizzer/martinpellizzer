Sloanea mexicana

Sloanea mexicana, commonly known as the Mexican sloanea, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Mexico.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are often elliptical in shape.
