Stipagrostis plumosa var. brachypoda

Stipagrostis plumosa var. brachypoda, commonly known as the short-stemmed feather grass, is a perennial grass species native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted, feather-like inflorescence that gives it a delicate, ornamental appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sandy soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions. Its narrow, grass-like leaves are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its unique texture.

This variety is valued for its ornamental use in dryland gardens and its role in stabilizing soil in degraded landscapes.
