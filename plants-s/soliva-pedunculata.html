Soliva pedunculata

Soliva pedunculata, commonly known as slender bedstraw, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its prostrate growth habit and small, white to pinkish flowers. The plant produces tiny, oval fruits called achenes, which are typically dispersed by wind. It thrives in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.

Soliva pedunculata is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported healing properties.
