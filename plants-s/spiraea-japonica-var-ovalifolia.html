Spiraea japonica var. ovalifolia

Spiraea japonica var. ovalifolia, commonly known as Japanese spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features small, white or pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has oval-shaped leaves that are arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is often used in ornamental gardening for its compact growth and attractive floral displays.
