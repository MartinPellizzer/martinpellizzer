Syringa dilatata f. rubra

Syringa dilatata f. rubra, commonly known as the red-flowered weeping lilac, is a unique cultivar of the weeping lilac plant.

It is characterized by its pendulous, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink to deep red. The plant features dark green, lance-shaped leaves that provide a striking contrast to its vibrant floral display. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This ornamental shrub is often used in gardens for its aesthetic appeal and seasonal interest.
