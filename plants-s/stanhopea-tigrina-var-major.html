Stanhopea tigrina var. major

Stanhopea tigrina var. major, commonly known as the tiger orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that feature intricate patterns resembling a tiger's stripes. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, tropical environments. Its floral structure includes a long, slender column and prominent, spurred petals.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
