Statice mauritanica var. safiensis

Statice mauritanica var. safiensis, commonly known as the Safien statice, is a flowering plant native to North Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, fluffy, pink or white flowers. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or arid environments. It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family, which includes other drought-tolerant plants.

The species is noted for its hardy nature and ability to withstand harsh climatic conditions.
