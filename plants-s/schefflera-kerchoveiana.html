Schefflera kerchoveiana

Schefflera kerchoveiana, commonly known as the Kerchove's umbrella tree, is a tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like crown of large, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Schefflera kerchoveiana is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
