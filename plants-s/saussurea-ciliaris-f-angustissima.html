Saussurea ciliaris f. angustissima

Saussurea ciliaris f. angustissima, commonly known as the narrow-leaved saussurea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, angular stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish to brownish composite flowers arranged in a compact inflorescence. It is typically found in alpine and subalpine regions, often growing in rocky or gravelly soils.

This subspecies is adapted to cold climates and is often used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
