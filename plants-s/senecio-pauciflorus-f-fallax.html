Senecio pauciflorus f. fallax

Senecio pauciflorus f. fallax, commonly known as the variable senecio, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to South Africa.

It features dense, silver-gray foliage with a fuzzy texture, which helps it retain moisture in arid conditions. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters during the summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens or containers due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
