Sclerosciadium humile

Sclerosciadium humile, commonly known as the small hard-stemmed scrophularia, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features small, hairy leaves and produces clusters of pale yellow to greenish-yellow flowers. The plant is characterized by its woody stems and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae, which includes many species with medicinal properties.
