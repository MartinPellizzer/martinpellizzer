Scabiosa gramuntia var. calcicola

Scabiosa gramuntia var. calcicola, commonly known as the limestone scabious, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It thrives in calcareous soils, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats. The plant produces clusters of pink or white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and gray-green, adapted to withstand dry and sunny conditions.

It is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
