Stephania japonica

Stephania japonica, commonly known as Japanese stephania, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Menispermaceae and is characterized by its climbing habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically followed by orange-red berries. Its roots are used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Stephania japonica thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
