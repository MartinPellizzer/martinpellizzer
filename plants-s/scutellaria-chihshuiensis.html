Scutellaria chihshuiensis

Scutellaria chihshuiensis, commonly known as the Chihshui skullcap, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, two-lipped flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
