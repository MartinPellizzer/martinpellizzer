Sideroxylon Bonianum

Sideroxylon bonianum is a unique and striking plant native to the island of Mauritius. It is known for its distinctive appearance, featuring large, waxy leaves and clusters of yellow flowers. This species thrives in coastal environments and is often found growing on rocky outcrops. Due to its limited habitat and threats from invasive species, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this rare and beautiful plant.
