Smilax helferi var. maingayana

Smilax helferi var. maingayana, commonly known as the Maingay's smilax, is a climbing shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features woody stems with sharp, spiny projections that aid in its climbing habit. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically clustered in umbels. Its berries are red or black when mature and serve as a food source for various wildlife.

This species is often found in dense vegetation near streams or in moist forest understories.
