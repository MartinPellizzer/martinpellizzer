Senecio divergens

Senecio divergens, commonly known as the mountain ragwort, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has serrated, alternate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Senecio divergens is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
