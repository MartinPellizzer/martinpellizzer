Stenanthium gramineum

Stenanthium gramineum, commonly known as the grassy false hellebore, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the family Melanthiaceae and is characterized by its tall, grass-like foliage and clusters of small, white or pale green flowers. Stenanthium gramineum is noted for its ornamental value and ecological importance, providing habitat and nectar for various pollinators.
