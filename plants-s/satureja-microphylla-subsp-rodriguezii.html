Satureja microphylla subsp. rodriguezii

Satureja microphylla subsp.

rodriguezii, commonly known as Rodriguez's savory, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It features small, aromatic leaves and produces tiny, pinkish-white flowers in the spring.

The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or limestone habitats. Its leaves are used in culinary applications for their pungent, thyme-like flavor.

It is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
