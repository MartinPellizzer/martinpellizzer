Spiraea japonica var. fortunei

Spiraea japonica var. fortunei, commonly known as Fortune's weeping spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its cascading clusters of white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has fine, serrated leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil.

This ornamental plant is often used in gardens for its elegant, flowing form and seasonal interest.
