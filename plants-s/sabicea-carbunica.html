Sabicea carbunica

Sabicea carbunica, commonly known as the Carpathian hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Carpathian Mountains in Europe.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are usually pink or red in color. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Sabicea carbunica is valued for its ornamental appeal and contributes to the biodiversity of its native habitat.
