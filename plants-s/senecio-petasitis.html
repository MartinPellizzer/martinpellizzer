Senecio petasitis

Senecio petasitis, commonly known as the Cape snowflake, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its fluffy, white, daisy-like flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has a rosette of silver-gray, hairy leaves that help it retain moisture in its arid native habitat. Senecio petasitis is an evergreen perennial that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
