Stachys ajugoides var. rigida

Stachys ajugoides var. rigida, commonly known as rigid betony, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features square, hairy stems and opposite, heart-shaped leaves with a slightly serrated margin. The plant produces spikes of blue to purple flowers in the summer months, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and borders.
