Stelis octavioi

Stelis octavioi, commonly known as the Octavio's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small to medium-sized epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale green with intricate patterns. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Stelis octavioi is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
