Stipa oligostachya

Stipa oligostachya, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and dense, feathery plumes that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Stipa oligostachya is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in landscaping and restoration projects.
