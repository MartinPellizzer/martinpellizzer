Stelis macilenta

Stelis macilenta, commonly known as the slender stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, linear leaves. The plant produces delicate, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It typically grows in shaded, moist habitats such as forest floors or rocky crevices.

Stelis macilenta is native to Southeast Asia and is often found in humid tropical and subtropical regions.
