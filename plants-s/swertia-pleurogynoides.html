Swertia pleurogynoides

Swertia pleurogynoides, commonly known as the Himalayan gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and often branched.

Swertia pleurogynoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
