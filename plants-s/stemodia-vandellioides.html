Stemodia vandellioides

Stemodia vandellioides, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, branching stems and dense, velvety foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Stemodia vandellioides is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
