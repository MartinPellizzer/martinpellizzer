Stevia gilliesii

Stevia gilliesii, commonly known as New Zealand stevia, is a perennial herb native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its upright growth habit and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves contain high concentrations of steviol glycosides, which are natural sweeteners.

Stevia gilliesii is often cultivated for both ornamental purposes and its potential medicinal uses.
