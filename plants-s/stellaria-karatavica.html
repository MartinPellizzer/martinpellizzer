Stellaria karatavica

Stellaria karatavica, commonly known as the Karatavica starwort, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, star-shaped white flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in steppe and semi-desert environments. Its leaves are narrow and opposite, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Stellaria karatavica is of interest to botanists due to its unique adaptations to arid conditions.
