Sterculia obovata

Sterculia obovata, commonly known as the broad-leaf sterculia, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, obovate leaves that are often leathery in texture. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in panicles, followed by woody pods containing several seeds. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The tree is valued for its hard, heavy wood and is also used in traditional medicine.
