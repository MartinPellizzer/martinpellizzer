Scrophularia oldhamii

Scrophularia oldhamii, commonly known as Oldham's figwort, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae and is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces showy, two-lipped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, blooming from summer to autumn. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Scrophularia oldhamii is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
