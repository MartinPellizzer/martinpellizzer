Sideritis peyrei subsp. guillonii

Sideritis peyrei subsp.

guillonii, commonly known as Peyre's sainfoin, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs.

The plant is characterized by its slender stems and small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. It thrives in rocky and arid environments, often found in mountainous areas.

Its leaves are hairy and have a distinctive, slightly minty scent.
