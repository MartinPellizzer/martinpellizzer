Sericostoma strigosa

Sericostoma strigosa, commonly known as the hairy leaf fern, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds. The plant has a creeping rhizome with numerous short, erect branches that bear small, lance-shaped leaves. Its leaves are covered in fine, hairy trichomes, giving it a distinctive appearance.
