Senecio × reisachii

Senecio × reisachii, commonly known as the hybrid African daisy, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Senecio serotinus and Senecio cruentus. This species is characterized by its striking blue to violet flower heads, which are surrounded by narrow, grass-like leaves. It is native to South Africa and thrives in well-drained soils with full sun exposure.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
