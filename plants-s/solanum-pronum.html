Solanum pronum

Solanum pronum, commonly known as the purple nightshade, is a member of the nightshade family (Solanaceae).

It is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant produces small, violet-colored berries that are toxic to humans and animals. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with a slightly hairy texture.

Solanum pronum thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in woodland or shrubland habitats.
