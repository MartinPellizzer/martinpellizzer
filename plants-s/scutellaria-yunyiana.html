Scutellaria yunyiana

Scutellaria yunyiana, commonly known as the Yunnan skullcap, is a perennial herb native to China, specifically the Yunnan province.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. The plant features square stems and opposite leaves, typical of the mint family. Its small, tubular flowers are usually blue or purple, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Scutellaria yunyiana is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional Chinese medicine.
