Specklinia xyloura

Specklinia xyloura, commonly known as the Xyloura's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Madagascar.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that thrives in humid, shaded forest environments. The plant features intricate, lance-shaped leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It is characterized by its unique inflorescence, which consists of multiple clustered blooms.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a conservation concern.
