Stephanotis maingayi

Stephanotis maingayi, commonly known as the African jasmine, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Stephanotis maingayi is a climbing vine that can grow quite long, making it suitable for trellises or as a houseplant.
