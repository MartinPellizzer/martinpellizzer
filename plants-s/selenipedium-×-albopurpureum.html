Selenipedium × albopurpureum

Selenipedium × albopurpureum, commonly known as the purple-flowered selenipedium, is a hybrid orchid belonging to the family Orchidaceae.

It is characterized by its striking, large, and showy inflorescences that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces pendulous, white or purple flowers with intricate patterns, often resembling those of its parent species. It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in shaded, humid environments.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is often cultivated by enthusiasts and conservationists.
