Sphenodesme

Sphenodesme, commonly known as the "buttercup fern," is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the family Sphenostomataceae.

Sphenodesme species are valued for their ornamental appeal and are sometimes cultivated in gardens for their vibrant floral display.
