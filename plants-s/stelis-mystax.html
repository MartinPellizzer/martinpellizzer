Stelis mystax

Stelis mystax, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit and produces intricate, showy inflorescences with dark purple to brown flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its leaves are linear and arranged in two rows, while the roots are fibrous and adapted for nutrient absorption.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
