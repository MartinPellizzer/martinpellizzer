Schefflera exaltata

Schefflera exaltata, commonly known as the Umbrella Tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, umbrella-like crown of large, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant features a single, straight trunk with a dense cluster of leaves at the top, giving it a unique appearance. It produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red berries, though these are not typically noticeable in cultivated settings.

Schefflera exaltata is often grown as a houseplant or in gardens due to its adaptability and ornamental value.
