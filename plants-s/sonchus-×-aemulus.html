Sonchus × aemulus

Sonchus × aemulus, commonly known as the common sowthistle, is a hybrid plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, lobed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the daisy family. It is often found in disturbed soils and can become invasive in certain regions.

Its botanical features make it adaptable to various environments, contributing to its widespread distribution.
