Symbegonia geraniifolia

Symbegonia geraniifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved begonia, is a herbaceous perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, typically in late spring to early summer. It thrives in partial shade and moist, well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for shaded garden beds.

Symbegonia geraniifolia is valued for its ornamental foliage and is relatively easy to care for in a home garden.
