Stelis congesta

Stelis congesta, commonly known as the clustered stelis orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces white or pale yellow blooms that are arranged in dense inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Stelis congesta is often cultivated for its ornamental value in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
