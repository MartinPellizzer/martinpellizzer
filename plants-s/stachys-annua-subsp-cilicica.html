Stachys annua subsp. cilicica

Stachys annua subsp.

cilicica, commonly known as the Cilician hedge-nettle, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region. It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is characterized by its erect stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or purplish in color, blooming from late spring to early summer. It is often found in grasslands, woodland edges, and disturbed areas.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
