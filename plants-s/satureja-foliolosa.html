Satureja foliolosa

Satureja foliolosa, commonly known as narrow-leaf savory, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and features small, pink or purple flowers. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a strong, pungent scent.

Satureja foliolosa is often used in traditional medicine and as a culinary herb due to its aromatic properties.
