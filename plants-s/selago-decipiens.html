Selago decipiens

Selago decipiens, commonly known as the deceptive grass.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, belonging to the family Poaceae. The plant is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with small, inconspicuous flowers. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal or arid regions.

Due to its deceptive appearance, it can be easily mistaken for other grass species, hence its common name.
