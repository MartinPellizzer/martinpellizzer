Stemodia grandiflora

Stemodia grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered stemodia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its erect, herbaceous stems and opposite, ovate leaves with hairy surfaces. The plant produces showy, yellowish-white flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
