Stelis escobarii

Stelis escobarii, commonly known as the Escobarii orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Mexico.

It typically grows in shaded, humid environments such as forests and rocky slopes. The plant features a rhizomatous root system and produces clustered, showy flowers in shades of white and pink. Its leaves are elongated and often covered in a waxy coating, which helps retain moisture.

Stelis escobarii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
