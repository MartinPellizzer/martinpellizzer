Specklinia heliconiscapa

Specklinia heliconiscapa, commonly known as the spiral fern, is a unique epiphytic plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it a visually striking appearance. The plant has elongated, leathery fronds that are typically dark green with a slightly glossy surface. It thrives in humid environments with high light exposure and requires well-draining soil.

Specklinia heliconiscapa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
