Sutera elegantissima

Sutera elegantissima, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Campanulaceae.

It is native to the Caribbean and is characterized by its silvery-white, velvety leaves and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers. Sutera elegantissima is also valued for its ability to purify indoor air, making it a popular choice for interior landscaping.
