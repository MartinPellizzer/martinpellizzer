Scaevola pedunculata var. mollis

Scaevola pedunculata var. mollis, commonly known as the velvet scabiosa, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety foliage and clusters of small, pink to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in a compact, bushy form, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its prolonged blooming period, which extends from spring through early autumn.
