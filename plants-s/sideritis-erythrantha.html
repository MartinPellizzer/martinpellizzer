Sideritis erythrantha

Sideritis erythrantha, commonly known as red-flowered ironweed, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red, typically blooming in summer. Sideritis erythrantha is often found in rocky or dry habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.

Its aromatic foliage and medicinal properties have made it a subject of interest in traditional herbal practices.
