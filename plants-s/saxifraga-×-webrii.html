Saxifraga × webrii

Saxifraga × webrii, commonly known as the hybrid saxifrage, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features dense, compact foliage with waxy, ovate leaves that are typically green to reddish in color. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers in late spring to early summer, arranged in clusters at the ends of its stems. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.

Saxifraga × webrii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate shade and poor soil conditions.
