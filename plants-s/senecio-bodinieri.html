Senecio bodinieri

Senecio bodinieri, commonly known as the silver ragwort, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and clusters of small yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.
