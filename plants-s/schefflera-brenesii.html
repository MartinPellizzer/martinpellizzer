Schefflera brenesii

Schefflera brenesii, commonly known as the Guanacaste Schefflera, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are typically arranged in a whorled pattern around the stem. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant or in gardens.
