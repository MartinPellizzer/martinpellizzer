Spiraea nipponica

Spiraea nipponica, commonly known as Japanese spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in early spring. The plant has slender, arching branches and narrow, serrated leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Spiraea nipponica is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and easy maintenance.
