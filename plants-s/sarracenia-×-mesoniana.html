Sarracenia × mesoniana

Sarracenia × mesoniana, commonly known as the hybrid pitcher plant, is a fascinating carnivorous plant resulting from the cross between Sarracenia leucophylla and Sarracenia rubra.

It features elongated, tubular leaves that form specialized pitfall traps to capture insects. The plant's pitchers are typically red or orange with a distinctive white or yellow stripe, enhancing their visual appeal. These plants thrive in moist, acidic soils and require full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Sarracenia × mesoniana is popular among carnivorous plant enthusiasts due to its striking appearance and effective trapping mechanism.
