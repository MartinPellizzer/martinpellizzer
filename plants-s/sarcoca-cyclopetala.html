Sarcoca cyclopetala

Sarcoca cyclopetala, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Sarcoca cyclopetala is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
