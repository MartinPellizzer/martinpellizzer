Sarcolaena grandiflora

Sarcolaena grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered cholla, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions. The plant features small, spiny stems and produces clusters of showy, yellow or orange flowers. Its leaves are usually small and scale-like, adapted to reduce water loss.

Sarcolaena grandiflora is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
