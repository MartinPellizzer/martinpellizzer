Stelis panamensis

Stelis panamensis, commonly known as the Panamanian orchid, is a terrestrial orchid belonging to the Epidendroideae subfamily.

It is native to Central America, particularly Panama, and thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are typically small, clustered, and come in shades of white, pink, or yellow.

Stelis panamensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
