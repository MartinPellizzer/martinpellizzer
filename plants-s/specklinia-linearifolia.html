Specklinia linearifolia

Specklinia linearifolia, commonly known as the slender leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, linear leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically arranged in a spike-like inflorescence. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Specklinia linearifolia is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
