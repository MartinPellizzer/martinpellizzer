Stipa molfinoi

Stipa molfinoi, commonly known as Molfino's feathergrass, is a species of grass native to southern South America.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and produces feathery, light green to golden yellow inflorescences. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It has narrow, linear leaves and is often used in ornamental gardening due to its delicate appearance.

Stipa molfinoi is also valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
