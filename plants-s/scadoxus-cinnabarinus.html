Scadoxus cinnabarinus

Scadoxus cinnabarinus, commonly known as the red-stalked gladiolus, is a bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant features a tall, erect stem with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its bulbs are tuberous and store nutrients for the growing season.

Scadoxus cinnabarinus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
