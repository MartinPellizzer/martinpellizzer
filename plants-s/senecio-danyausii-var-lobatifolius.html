Senecio danyausii var. lobatifolius

Senecio danyausii var. lobatifolius, commonly known as the lobate-leaved groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive lobed leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in dry, sunny environments. It is a perennial herb that produces small, daisy-like blooms, making it a visually appealing addition to gardens.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in arid or semi-arid regions.
