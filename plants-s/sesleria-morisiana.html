Sesleria morisiana

Sesleria morisiana, commonly known as the Morisian pink grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and produces clusters of pink to purple flowers during the summer months. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Sesleria morisiana is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive floral display and low maintenance requirements.
