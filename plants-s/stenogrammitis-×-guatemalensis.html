Stenogrammitis × guatemalensis

Stenogrammitis × guatemalensis, commonly known as the Guatemalan stenogrammitis, is a hybrid fern species native to Central America.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are typically bi- or tri-pinnate. The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
