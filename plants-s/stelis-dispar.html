Stelis dispar

Stelis dispar, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including India, China, and Southeast Asia. The plant typically grows on rocks or tree trunks, exhibiting an epiphytic or lithophytic growth habit. Its flowers are usually small, fragrant, and come in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with intricate patterns.

Stelis dispar thrives in humid environments with partial shade, requiring well-drained soil and consistent moisture.
