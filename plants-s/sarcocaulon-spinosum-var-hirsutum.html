Sarcocaulon spinosum var. hirsutum

Sarcocaulon spinosum var. hirsutum, commonly known as the hairy spiny broom, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring, attracting various pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in coastal and rocky habitats.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a distinctive addition to botanical collections.
