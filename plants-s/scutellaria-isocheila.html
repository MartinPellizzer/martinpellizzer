Scutellaria isocheila

Scutellaria isocheila, commonly known as the narrow-leaved skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, tubular blue or violet flowers. Its scientific name reflects its distinctive skullcap-like calyx and uniform leaf structure.

Scutellaria isocheila is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often found in native plant gardens.
