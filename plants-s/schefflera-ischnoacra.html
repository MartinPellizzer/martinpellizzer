Schefflera ischnoacra

Schefflera ischnoacra, commonly known as the Bornean umbrella plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of leaflets, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Schefflera ischnoacra is valued for its ornamental foliage and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
