Sinningia richii

Sinningia richii, commonly known as the silver leaf gloxinia, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant features velvety, waxy leaves that are typically a deep green with a silvery underside. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Sinningia richii is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
