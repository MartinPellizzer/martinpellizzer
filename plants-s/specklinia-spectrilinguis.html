Specklinia spectrilinguis

Specklinia spectrilinguis, commonly known as the rainbow fern, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its intricate, lace-like fronds that display a striking range of colors. Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
