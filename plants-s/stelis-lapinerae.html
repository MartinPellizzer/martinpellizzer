Stelis lapinerae

Stelis lapinerae, commonly known as the Lapinerae orchid, is a species of orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia and the Philippines. This epiphytic plant typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are small and clustered, often displaying a variety of colors including white, pink, and purple.

The plant's botanical features include pseudobulbs and linear leaves, which help it store water and nutrients in its natural habitat.
