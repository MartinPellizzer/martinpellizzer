Sideritis × sennenii

Sideritis × sennenii, commonly known as the hybrid senna or Greek catnip, is a flowering plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its aromatic, hairy leaves and purple or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and is often found in open woodland or scrub habitats. Sideritis × sennenii is valued for its medicinal properties, with its leaves used in traditional remedies.

It is also cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and blooms.
