Sciothamnus collinus

Sciothamnus collinus, commonly known as silverleaf sage, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, such as sagebrush steppe and rocky slopes. It has a deep root system that allows it to survive in dry conditions.

Silverleaf sage is often used in traditional herbal medicine and is also valued for its ornamental qualities.
