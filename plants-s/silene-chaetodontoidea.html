Silene chaetodontoidea

Silene chaetodontoidea, commonly known as the fringed catchfly, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pinkish-purple flowers that bloom in summer. Silene chaetodontoidea is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
