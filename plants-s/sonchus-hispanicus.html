Sonchus hispanicus

Sonchus hispanicus, commonly known as Spanish sowthistle, is a flowering plant in the sunflower family, Asteraceae.

It is an annual herb that typically grows in disturbed soils, such as roadsides and fields. The plant features alternate, hairy, heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture. Its yellow flowers are arranged in a composite head, with ray florets surrounding disk florets.

It is native to Europe and has been introduced to other regions, sometimes becoming invasive.
