Silene rigens

Silene rigens, commonly known as the rigid catchfly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in dry, rocky soils. The plant features upright stems covered in fine, silky hairs, and it produces small, pink or purple flowers in clusters. Its leaves are linear and often have a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss.

Silene rigens is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
