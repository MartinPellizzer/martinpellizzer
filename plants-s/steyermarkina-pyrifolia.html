Steyermarkina pyrifolia

Steyermarkina pyrifolia, commonly known as the Chinese white pear, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and alternate, simple leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers in spring, followed by edible, sweet fruits that are similar to pears.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fruit production in temperate climates.
