Stenorrhynchos macranthum

Stenorrhynchos macranthum, commonly known as the large-flowered stenorrhynchos, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in a basal rosette.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
