Staflinus setifolius

Staflinus setifolius, commonly known as the slender-leaved starflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of hair. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy addition to alpine gardens.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
