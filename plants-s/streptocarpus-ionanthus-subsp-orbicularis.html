Streptocarpus ionanthus subsp. orbicularis

Streptocarpus ionanthus subsp.

orbicularis, commonly known as the African violet, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is native to South Africa. It is characterized by its oval, velvety leaves and showy, spurred flowers that come in a range of colors including purple, pink, and white.

The plant is evergreen and typically grows as a compact, bushy perennial. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its popularity as a houseplant is due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
