Schefflera pseudobrassaia

Schefflera pseudobrassaia, commonly known as the giant umbrella plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, umbrella-like compound leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in diameter. The plant has a dense, bushy growth habit with multiple stems emerging from the base. Its leaves are typically dark green and glossy, with a prominent central stem and several leaflets radiating outward.

Schefflera pseudobrassaia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and ability to thrive in warm, humid environments.
