Sporobolus iocladoides

Sporobolus iocladoides, commonly known as blue grama, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and compact growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a common component of grasslands and shrublands.

Blue grama is also valued for its ecological role in soil stabilization and as forage for wildlife and livestock.
