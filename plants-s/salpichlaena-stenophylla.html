Salpichlaena stenophylla

Salpichlaena stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf huperzia, is a species of fern belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its diminutive, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Southeast Asia, including countries like Thailand and Malaysia. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest undergrowth.

The sporophylls are clustered at the tips of the stems, producing spores for reproduction.
