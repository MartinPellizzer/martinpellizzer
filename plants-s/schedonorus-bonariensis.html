Schedonorus bonariensis

Schedonorus bonariensis, commonly known as Argentine bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and dark green, narrow leaves. This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands and grassy areas. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.

Schedonorus bonariensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and turfgrass applications.
