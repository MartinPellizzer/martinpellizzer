Symphyotrichum pilosum

Symphyotrichum pilosum, commonly known as the rough blueweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodlands. It produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in the fall, attracting various pollinators.

Symphyotrichum pilosum is often found in the eastern and central United States.
