Stelis pluriracemosa

Stelis pluriracemosa, commonly known as the many-branched orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with elongated, jointed stems that often hang from trees. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically blooming during the rainy season. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding environment.

Stelis pluriracemosa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique growth form.
