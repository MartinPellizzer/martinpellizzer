Stachys balbisii

Stachys balbisii, commonly known as the Balbis sedge, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is known for its aromatic plants. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, tubular flowers in spikes. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.

Stachys balbisii thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is typically found in wetlands or along stream banks.
