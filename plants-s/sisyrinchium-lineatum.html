Sisyrinchium lineatum

Sisyrinchium lineatum, commonly known as the slender blue-eyed grass, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features slender, grass-like stems and produces delicate, showy blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it blooms from late spring to early summer.

Sisyrinchium lineatum is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
