Scabiosa palaestina var. microcephala

Scabiosa palaestina var. microcephala, commonly known as the Palestinian scabious, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, rounded flower heads that are typically pink or white in color. The plant features slender, hairy stems and narrow, serrated leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical significance in traditional herbal medicine.
