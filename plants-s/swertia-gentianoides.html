Swertia gentianoides

Swertia gentianoides, commonly known as the blue gentian, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky areas. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Swertia gentianoides is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
