Schoenus brevisetis var. subimberbis

Schoenus brevisetis var. subimberbis, commonly known as the short-stemmed grass, is a variant of the Schoenus genus native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its compact growth habit and short, dense inflorescences. The leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a smooth texture and a tendency to curl at the edges. This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetland or marshy environments.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and wetland restoration projects.
