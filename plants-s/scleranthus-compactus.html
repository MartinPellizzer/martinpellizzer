Scleranthus compactus

Scleranthus compactus, commonly known as the compact thornweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Amaranthaceae.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are often pink or red in color. Its leaves are usually narrow and hairy, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Scleranthus compactus is known for its ability to thrive in arid and semi-arid environments.
