Saussurea tenuis

Saussurea tenuis, commonly known as the narrowleafed yarrow, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its slender, aromatic leaves and small yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine and arid environments, thriving at high altitudes. Its root system is deep and fibrous, allowing it to withstand harsh climatic conditions.
