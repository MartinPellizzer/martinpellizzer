Sansevieria roxburghiana

Sansevieria roxburghiana, commonly known as the snake plant, is a hardy, evergreen perennial native to tropical Africa.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with subtle variegation. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering. Its air-purifying qualities make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Sansevieria roxburghiana is also valued for its resilience and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
