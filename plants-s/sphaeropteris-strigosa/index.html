Sphaeropteris Strigosa

Sphaeropteris strigosa is a unique fern species known for its distinctive appearance and ecological importance. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments within tropical forests. Its fronds are characterized by a rugged, hairy texture that sets it apart from other ferns. This plant plays a vital role in maintaining biodiversity and soil stability in its habitat. Due to habitat loss, it faces threats that highlight the need for conservation efforts.
