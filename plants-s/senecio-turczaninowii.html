Senecio turczaninowii

Senecio turczaninowii, commonly known as the Turczaninow's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that typically reaches heights of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a cluster at the ends of its stems. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to survive in harsh, dry conditions.
