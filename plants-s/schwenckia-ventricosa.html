Schwenckia ventricosa

Schwenckia ventricosa, commonly known as the bladderleaf or bladderleaf aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its showy, violet-blue to purple flowers that bloom in the fall. The plant has alternate, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as wetlands and meadows.
