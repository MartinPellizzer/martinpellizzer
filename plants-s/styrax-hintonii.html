Styrax hintonii

Styrax hintonii, commonly known as Hinton's styrax, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the family Styracaceae and is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant produces small, leathery berries that are typically red or black when mature. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Styrax hintonii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and hardy nature in cooler climates.
