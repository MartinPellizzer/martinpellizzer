Soroseris teres

Soroseris teres, commonly known as the spherical dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its round, fleshy leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces a unique seed head that resembles a small sphere, which is a distinguishing feature. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
