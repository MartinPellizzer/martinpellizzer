Steinbachiella

Steinbachiella, commonly known as the narrow-leafed dandelion, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces yellow composite flowers that develop into fluffy seed heads.
