Solanum styracifolium

Solanum styracifolium, commonly known as the styracifolium nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features woody stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. Its fruit is a smooth, yellow or orange berry that ripens in late summer.

Solanum styracifolium is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has mild toxic properties in its leaves and berries.
