Strobilanthes japonica

Strobilanthes japonica, commonly known as Japanese flame flower, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant produces vibrant orange to red bracts that resemble flowers, although they are modified leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Strobilanthes japonica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its visual impact.
