Serapias longipetala subsp. pallidiflora

Serapias longipetala subsp.

pallidiflora, commonly known as the pale-flowered orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family. It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

The plant features showy, yellowish-orange flowers with a distinctive hooded lip. Its leaves are basal, lance-shaped, and often covered in a fine layer of white hair.

It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is typically found in alpine or subalpine habitats.
