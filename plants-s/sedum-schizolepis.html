Sedum schizolepis

Sedum schizolepis, commonly known as the stonecrop or ice plant, is a succulent plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, allowing it to store water efficiently. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions makes it a hardy and low-maintenance plant.
