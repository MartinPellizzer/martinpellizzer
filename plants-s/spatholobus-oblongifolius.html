Spatholobus oblongifolius

Spatholobus oblongifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaf wattle, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas. It plays a role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for various insects and small animals.
