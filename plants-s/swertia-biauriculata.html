Swertia biauriculata

Swertia biauriculata, commonly known as the two-lobed gentian, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy surface.

Swertia biauriculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
