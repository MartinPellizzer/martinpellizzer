Scutellaria hartwegii

Scutellaria hartwegii, commonly known as Hartweg's skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It belongs to the mint family (Lamiaceae) and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue to purple in color, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Scutellaria hartwegii is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.

Its medicinal properties have been studied for potential use in traditional herbal remedies.
