Selaginella Tereticaulos

Selaginella tereticaulis is a fascinating species of spikemoss that thrives in moist, shaded environments. This ancient plant belongs to the family Selaginellaceae and is known for its unique morphology and resilience. Its small, scale-like leaves and branching stems make it a striking addition to botanical gardens and natural habitats. The plant's ability to survive in harsh conditions has made it a subject of scientific interest. Studying Selaginella tereticaulis offers insights into the evolution of early land plants and their ecological roles.
