Sedum chazaroi

Sedum chazaroi, commonly known as the Armenian stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Armenia and Turkey.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It is often found growing on rocky substrates and in dry, well-drained soils.

Sedum chazaroi is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in xerophytic gardens.
