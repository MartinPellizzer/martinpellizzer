Silene stenocalycina

Silene stenocalycina, commonly known as the narrow calyx catchfly, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Mexico.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its pink to purple flowers and slender stems. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It has small, ovate leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds.
