Symplocos arfakensis

Symplocos arfakensis, commonly known as the Arfak mountain tea, is a shrub native to the highlands of Indonesia.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves with a leathery texture. Symplocos arfakensis is adapted to cool, moist environments and is often found in cloud forests at elevations above 1,500 meters.
