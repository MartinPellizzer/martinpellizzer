Saxifraga cotyledon var. glabrata

Saxifraga cotyledon var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth-leaved stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are usually green with a waxy texture, and it produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white. The plant is adapted to rocky and dry environments, storing water in its leaves for survival.

It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
