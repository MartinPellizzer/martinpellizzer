Sisyrinchium flagellum

Sisyrinchium flagellum, commonly known as the slender blue-eyed grass, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the western United States.

It features slender, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. The plant typically blooms in spring and early summer, often in open meadows and rocky areas. It is part of the Iridaceae family, which includes many flowering plants with showy blooms.
