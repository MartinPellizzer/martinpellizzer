Saxifraga notkana

Saxifraga notkana, commonly known as the California saxifrage, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the western United States.

It typically thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions. The plant features small, clustered leaves and produces delicate white or pink flowers in the spring. It is adapted to harsh environments, with a compact growth form that helps it conserve moisture.

Saxifraga notkana is an important component of alpine and coastal ecosystems, contributing to biodiversity in these unique habitats.
