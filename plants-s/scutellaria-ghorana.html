Scutellaria ghorana

Scutellaria ghorana, commonly known as the Ghoran skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Afghanistan and Pakistan.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple, typically blooming in the summer months.

Scutellaria ghorana is often found in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes, and it is valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
