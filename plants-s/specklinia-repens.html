Specklinia repens

Specklinia repens, commonly known as the creeping speckled fern, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia and Malaysia. This terrestrial herb typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest undergrowth. The plant features delicate, linear leaves that are often speckled with dark spots, giving it its common name.

Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in inflorescences and bloom during the rainy season.
