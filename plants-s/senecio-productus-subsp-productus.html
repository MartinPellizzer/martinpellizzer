Senecio productus subsp. productus

Senecio productus subsp.

productus, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a perennial herb characterized by its upright growth habit and showy yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers in dense clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and drought tolerance.
