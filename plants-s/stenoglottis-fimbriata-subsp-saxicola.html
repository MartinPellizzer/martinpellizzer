Stenoglottis fimbriata subsp. saxicola

Stenoglottis fimbriata subsp.

saxicola, commonly known as the rock lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa. It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale purple.

The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions. It is adapted to withstand periods of drought and is well-suited for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its unique floral structure and resilience make it a desirable addition to xeriscapes and native plant collections.
