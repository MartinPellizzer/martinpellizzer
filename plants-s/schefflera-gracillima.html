Schefflera gracillima

Schefflera gracillima, commonly known as the slender umbrella tree, is a member of the Araliaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. Schefflera gracillima is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor conditions.
