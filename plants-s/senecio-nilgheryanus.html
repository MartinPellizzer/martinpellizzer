Senecio nilgheryanus

Senecio nilgheryanus, commonly known as the Nilgiri groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Western Ghats in India.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky and open habitats. The plant features slender stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, yellow, and clustered in inflorescences, contributing to its ornamental value.

It plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and nectar for pollinators.
