Sisymbrium siliquosum

Sisymbrium siliquosum, commonly known as the yellow rocket, is an annual herbaceous plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It belongs to the family Brassicaceae, which includes many economically important crops such as cabbage and mustard. The plant features lobed leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. Its fruit is a long, slender pod called a silique, typical of the Brassicaceae family.

Sisymbrium siliquosum is often found in disturbed soils and can be invasive in some regions.
