Stellaria ciliata

Stellaria ciliata, commonly known as hairy starwort, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, star-shaped white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas such as woodland floors and stream banks. Its leaves are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive, fuzzy appearance.

Stellaria ciliata is often found in temperate climates and is relatively easy to cultivate in gardens with suitable conditions.
