Scytalis retusa

Scytalis retusa, commonly known as the slender scytalis, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and the Pacific Islands.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, elongated leaves and small, fragrant flowers. Scytalis retusa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
