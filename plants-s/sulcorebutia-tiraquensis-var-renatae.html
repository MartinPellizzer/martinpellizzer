Sulcorebutia tiraquensis var. renatae

Sulcorebutia tiraquensis var. renatae, commonly known as the Renata's Pincushion Cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to the arid regions of Argentina.

It features a dense arrangement of cylindrical, ribbed stems that are typically pale green to reddish in color. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers during the spring and early summer. It is well adapted to dry, rocky environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its slow growth rate and unique appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and cactus collections.
