Scindapsus maclurei

Scindapsus maclurei, commonly known as the silver mottled philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a striking silver and green mottled pattern, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its aerial roots that help it climb toward light. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Scindapsus maclurei is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
