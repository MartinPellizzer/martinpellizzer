Sisyrinchium bermudiana var. albidum

Sisyrinchium bermudiana var. albidum, commonly known as the white sandflower, is a flowering plant native to Bermuda.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. This variety is noted for its ornamental value and its ability to stabilize sandy soils.
