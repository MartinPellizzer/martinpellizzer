Stachys cilicica

Stachys cilicica, commonly known as Cilician hedge-nettle, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is known for its aromatic plants. The plant features square stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. It produces small, two-lipped flowers in shades of purple or blue, typically blooming in late spring to early summer.

Stachys cilicica is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
