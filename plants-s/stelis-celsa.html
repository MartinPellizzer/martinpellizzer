Stelis celsa

Stelis celsa, commonly known as the Elegant Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, showy flowers that often have a distinctive color pattern. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as forest understories. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged along the stem.

Stelis celsa is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
