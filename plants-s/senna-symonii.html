Senna symonii

Senna symonii, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular ornamental and medicinal plant.

Senna symonii is also used in traditional medicine for its potential laxative properties.
