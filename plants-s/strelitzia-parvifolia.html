Strelitzia parvifolia

Strelitzia parvifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved bird-of-paradise, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the ginger family (Zingiberaceae) and is characterized by its unique, flamboyant inflorescences that resemble a bird in flight. The plant features narrow, strap-like leaves that are typically a deep green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant flowers. Strelitzia parvifolia thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for tropical gardens and indoor plant collections.
