Sedum pyramidale

Sedum pyramidale, commonly known as the pyramidale stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to North America.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow to orange. The plant has a compact growth habit, forming dense mats or low shrubs. Its leaves are typically blue-green in color and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Sedum pyramidale is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
