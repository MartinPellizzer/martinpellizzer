Spiranthes australis var. sinensis

Spiranthes australis var. sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese ladies' tresses, is a species of orchid native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and its striking, spiral arrangement of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
