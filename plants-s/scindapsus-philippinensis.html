Scindapsus philippinensis

Scindapsus philippinensis, commonly known as the silver mister or Philippine arrowroot, is a tropical climbing plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive silver or variegated pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white, followed by spathes that enclose the true flowers. It thrives in humid, shady environments and is often grown as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage.

Scindapsus philippinensis is relatively easy to care for, requiring moderate watering and indirect light.
