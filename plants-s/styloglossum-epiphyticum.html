Styloglossum epiphyticum

Styloglossum epiphyticum, commonly known as the epiphytic orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species that thrives as an epiphyte on the trunks and branches of trees.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, where it prefers humid and shaded environments. The plant has a distinctive appearance with its narrow, grass-like leaves and delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Its growth is slow, and it requires specific conditions to flourish, making it a challenging plant for cultivation.
