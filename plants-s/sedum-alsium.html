Sedum alsium

Sedum alsium, commonly known as Armenian sedum, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus and Central Asia.

It features fleshy, upright stems and clusters of star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has small, translucent leaves that help it store water, adapting it to arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Sedum alsium is also valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
