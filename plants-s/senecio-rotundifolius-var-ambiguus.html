Senecio rotundifolius var. ambiguus

Senecio rotundifolius var. ambiguus, commonly known as the ambiguous round-leaved senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features succulent, rounded leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. It produces small yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, which attract various pollinators.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
