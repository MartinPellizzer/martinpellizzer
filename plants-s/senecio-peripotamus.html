Senecio peripotamus

Senecio peripotamus, commonly known as the Cape mountain ice plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves to survive dry conditions. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Senecio peripotamus is also noted for its ability to thrive in full sun and its low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
