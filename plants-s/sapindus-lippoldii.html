Sapindus lippoldii

Sapindus lippoldii, commonly known as the Chinese soapberry tree, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Sapindaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, yellowish-green flowers. The tree produces spherical, spiny fruits that contain a single seed, and its bark is often fissured and rough.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
