Stellaria Filiformis

Stellaria filiformis, commonly known as threadleaf starwort, is a small flowering plant native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia. It is characterized by its delicate, slender stems and tiny white flowers that bloom in clusters. This plant thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, along roadsides, and in woodland edges. Despite its modest appearance, it plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and maintaining ecological balance. Its simple yet elegant structure makes it a fascinating subject for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike.
