Schismatoglottis calyptrata var. calyptrata

Schismatoglottis calyptrata var. calyptrata, commonly known as the Calyptrata Schismatoglottis, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces striking, showy inflorescences with white or pale pink flowers that bloom in summer. Its botanical features include a short rhizome and small, strap-shaped leaves with a waxy texture.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
