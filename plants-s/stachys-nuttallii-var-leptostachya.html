Stachys nuttallii var. leptostachya

Stachys nuttallii var. leptostachya, commonly known as Nuttall's hedge-nettle, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. It produces small, pale purple to white flowers arranged in whorled clusters along the stems.

Its leaves are usually hairy and have a distinctive heart-shaped base.
