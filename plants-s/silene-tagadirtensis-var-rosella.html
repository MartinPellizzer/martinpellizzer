Silene tagadirtensis var. rosella

Silene tagadirtensis var. rosella, commonly known as the Rosella Silene, is a flowering plant native to the highlands of Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are upright and branched.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
