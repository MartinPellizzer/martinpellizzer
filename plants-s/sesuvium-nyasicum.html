Sesuvium nyasicum

Sesuvium nyasicum, commonly known as the rock saltbush, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent shrub that thrives in arid and coastal environments, often growing on rocky soils. The plant features fleshy, blue-green leaves that help it retain moisture in harsh conditions. Its small, yellow flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, typically blooming in spring.

Sesuvium nyasicum is adapted to tolerate high salinity and is often found in areas with strong coastal winds.
