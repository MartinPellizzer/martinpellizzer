Senecio desfontainei

Senecio desfontainei, commonly known as the desert groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features upright stems and small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are usually gray-green and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Senecio desfontainei is adapted to survive in harsh conditions, making it a hardy species in dry climates.
