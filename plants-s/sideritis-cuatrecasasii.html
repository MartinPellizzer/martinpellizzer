Sideritis cuatrecasasii

Sideritis cuatrecasasii, commonly known as the Cuatrecasas' ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to pink. It thrives in rocky and dry habitats, often growing at high altitudes.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
