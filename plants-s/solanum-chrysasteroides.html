Solanum chrysasteroides

Solanum chrysasteroides, commonly known as the golden star solanum, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers that resemble stars. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often hairy, while its fruits are small, berry-like, and contain several seeds.

Solanum chrysasteroides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
