Stebbinsia

Stebbinsia, commonly known as the desert dandelion, is a genus of flowering plants in the sunflower family, Asteraceae.

It is native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico, where it thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plants are characterized by their basal rosettes of narrow, gray-green leaves and yellow ray flowers that bloom in spring. Stebbinsia species are typically annual or biennial, with small, capsule-like fruits that disperse seeds by wind.
