Silene glareosa subsp. marginata

Silene glareosa subsp.

marginata, commonly known as the marginated catchfly, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late summer.
