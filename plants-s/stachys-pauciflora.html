Stachys pauciflora

Stachys pauciflora, commonly known as the few-flowered hedge-nettle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features upright stems covered in soft, hairy foliage and produces small, pale purple flowers arranged in whorls. It thrives in rocky or dry soils and is often found in sunny, open habitats.
