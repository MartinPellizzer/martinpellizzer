Sideritis curvidens

Sideritis curvidens, commonly known as the curved beard vetch, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, fragrant flowers that range in color from pink to purple. Its leaves are opposite and oval-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Sideritis curvidens is typically found in rocky or dry habitats and is valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
