Solanum eschweilerianum

Solanum eschweilerianum, commonly known as the yellow bell pepper plant, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its yellow, bell-shaped flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically yellow when ripe. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant flowers and unique fruit.

The leaves are ovate with a slightly hairy texture, and the plant prefers well-drained soil and full sun.
