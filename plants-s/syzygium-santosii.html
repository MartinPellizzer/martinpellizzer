Syzygium santosii

Syzygium santosii, commonly known as the Santosii apple or red apple tree, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in the Philippines and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and red, edible fruits that resemble small apples. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a glossy appearance, while the bark is smooth and pale in color.

Syzygium santosii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of edible fruit in local communities.
