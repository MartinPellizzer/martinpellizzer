Sciodaphyllum rotundifolium

Sciodaphyllum rotundifolium, commonly known as the round-leaved shadow plant, is a unique species native to specific tropical regions.

It is characterized by its broad, rounded leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in the understory of dense forests.

Due to its specialized habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
