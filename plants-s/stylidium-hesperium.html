Stylidium hesperium

Stylidium hesperium, commonly known as the Western sun-orchid, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its striking, spurred flowers that resemble orchids. The plant has a compact growth habit with hairy stems and linear, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange with a distinctive, forward-facing spur.

Stylidium hesperium is adapted to arid environments and is often found in sandy or rocky soils.
