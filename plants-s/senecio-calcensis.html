Senecio calcensis

Senecio calcensis, commonly known as the limestone groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters. It is adapted to arid conditions and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Senecio calcensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in rock gardens.
