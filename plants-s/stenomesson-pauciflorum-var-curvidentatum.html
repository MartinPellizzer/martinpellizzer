Stenomesson pauciflorum var. curvidentatum

Stenomesson pauciflorum var. curvidentatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sunflower, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive yellow ray florets and robust, upright stems. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slightly waxy coating to reduce water loss.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
