Sanchezia macrocnemis

Sanchezia macrocnemis, commonly known as the giant arrowroot or arrowleaf arrowroot, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces clusters of small, white, spadix-like flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe. Its rhizomatous root system is notable for its ability to store starch, making it a valuable crop for food and traditional medicine.

Sanchezia macrocnemis thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
