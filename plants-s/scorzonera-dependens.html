Scorzonera dependens

Scorzonera dependens, commonly known as the hanging scorzonera, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces a thick, fleshy root that is often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Scorzonera dependens is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
