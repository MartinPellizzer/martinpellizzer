Specklinia trichostoma

Specklinia trichostoma, commonly known as the hairy leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, hairy leaves that give it its common name. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its flowers are small and white, often appearing in clusters along the stems.

Specklinia trichostoma is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
