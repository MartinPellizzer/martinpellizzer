Scutellaria atrocyanea

Scutellaria atrocyanea, commonly known as black-flowered skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically dark blue or purple, which give it its common name.

Scutellaria atrocyanea is often found in rocky or woodland habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
