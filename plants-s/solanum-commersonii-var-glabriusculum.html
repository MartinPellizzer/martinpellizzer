Solanum commersonii var. glabriusculum

Solanum commersonii var. glabriusculum, commonly known as the Commerson's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant features small, oval leaves with a slightly hairy surface, and its flowers are usually purple or violet in color. The fruit is a small, yellow or orange berry that contains several seeds.

It is part of the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers.
