Sempervivum dicranocladon

Sempervivum dicranocladon, commonly known as the snowflake plant, is a succulent species native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white, woolly hair. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine displays.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for horticultural enthusiasts.
