Sabicea vogelii

Sabicea vogelii, commonly known as the Vogel's sabicea, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Leguminosae family, also known as the legume or pea family, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. Its botanical features make it an important component of its native ecosystems.
