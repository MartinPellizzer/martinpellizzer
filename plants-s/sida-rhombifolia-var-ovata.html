Sida rhombifolia var. ovata

Sida rhombifolia var. ovata, commonly known as the broadleaf sida, is a flowering plant in the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its ovate, broad leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that are clustered in loose inflorescences.
