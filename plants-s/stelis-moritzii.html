Stelis moritzii

Stelis moritzii, commonly known as the Moritz's spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid belonging to the Epidendroideae subfamily.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a few leaves and produces delicate, spider-like flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. Its flowers are typically borne on a single inflorescence and have a distinctive, intricate structure.

Stelis moritzii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
