Saxifraga corsica subsp. ebusitana

ebusitana, commonly known as the Ebusi saxifrage, is a rare and endemic plant species found primarily on the island of Corsica. It is a low-growing perennial herb with succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically rosette-shaped.
