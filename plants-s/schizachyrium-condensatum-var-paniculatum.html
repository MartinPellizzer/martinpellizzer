Schizachyrium condensatum var. paniculatum

Schizachyrium condensatum var. paniculatum, commonly known as little bluestem, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage that turns coppery in the fall. The plant produces panicles of small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in prairies and open woodlands.

Schizachyrium condensatum var. paniculatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in ecological restoration.
