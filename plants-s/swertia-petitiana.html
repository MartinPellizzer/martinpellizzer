Swertia petitiana

Swertia petitiana, commonly known as the Himalayan gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine environments, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, while its stems are usually erect.

Swertia petitiana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often found in botanical gardens and conservation sites.
