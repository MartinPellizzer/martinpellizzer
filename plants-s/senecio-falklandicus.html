Senecio falklandicus

Senecio falklandicus, commonly known as Falkland Island groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the Falkland Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to the harsh maritime climate of its native habitat. It features small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, with narrow, grass-like leaves.

Senecio falklandicus is a hardy species that thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sandy environments.
