Schefflera grandigemma

Schefflera grandigemma, commonly known as the giant umbrella plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, umbrella-like crown composed of many leaflets radiating from a central stem. The plant features compound, pinnately arranged leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It produces small, inconspicuous yellowish-white flowers followed by orange-red berries.

Schefflera grandigemma is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in warm, humid environments.
