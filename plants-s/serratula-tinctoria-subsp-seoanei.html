Serratula tinctoria subsp. seoanei

Serratula tinctoria subsp.

seoanei, commonly known as the Canary Island serretula, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow to orange-yellow composite flowers.
