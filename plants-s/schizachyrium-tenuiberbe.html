Schizachyrium tenuiberbe

Schizachyrium tenuiberbe, commonly known as slender threadgrass, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It typically grows in open habitats such as prairies, meadows, and disturbed soils. The plant features slender, grass-like blades with a fine, thread-like texture. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

Schizachyrium tenuiberbe is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting native pollinators.
