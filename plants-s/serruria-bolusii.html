Serruria bolusii

Serruria bolusii, commonly known as the Bolus serruria, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the Western Cape of South Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, needle-like leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern along its stems. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a dramatic visual contrast to its foliage. Serruria bolusii thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun exposure.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and unique architectural form.
