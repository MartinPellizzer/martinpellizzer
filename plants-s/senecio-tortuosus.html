Senecio tortuosus

Senecio tortuosus, commonly known as the twisted groundsel, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is a perennial herb native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant features wiry, branched stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and arranged in a composite head, typical of the daisy family.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
