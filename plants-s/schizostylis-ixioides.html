Schizostylis ixioides

Schizostylis ixioides, commonly known as the yellow rain lily, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30-60 cm, with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its rhizomatous root system allows it to spread and form dense clumps.

Schizostylis ixioides thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.
