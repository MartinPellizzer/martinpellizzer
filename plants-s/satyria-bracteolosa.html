Satyria bracteolosa

Satyria bracteolosa, commonly known as the bracteolosa orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, showy floral structures. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its flowers are usually small to medium in size, with vibrant colors ranging from white to pink and purple.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by horticultural enthusiasts.
