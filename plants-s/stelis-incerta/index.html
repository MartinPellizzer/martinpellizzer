Stelis Incerta

Stelis incerta is a rare orchid species native to the cloud forests of Southeast Asia. It is known for its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in the spring. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-drained soil. Its unique appearance makes it a favorite among orchid enthusiasts. Due to habitat loss, Stelis incerta is now considered endangered.
