Stemona involuta

Stemona involuta, commonly known as the twisted stemona, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, twisted stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Stemona involuta thrives in moist, shaded environments and is commonly found in forest undergrowth.
