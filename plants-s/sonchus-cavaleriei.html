Sonchus cavaleriei

Sonchus cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese sowthistle, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features a basal rosette of leaves that are typically lobed or toothed, with a hairy surface. Its yellow flowers bloom in the summer, producing small seeds that are dispersed by wind.

It is often found in disturbed soils and can be invasive in certain regions.
