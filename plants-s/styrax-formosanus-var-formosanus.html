Styrax formosanus var. formosanus

Styrax formosanus var. formosanus, commonly known as the Formosan styrax, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Taiwan.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. The plant has simple, opposite, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. Its bark is smooth and reddish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

The Formosan styrax is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and floral display.
