Spiranthes thelymitra

Spiranthes thelymitra, commonly known as the pink lady's tresses, is a member of the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is native to the eastern United States.

The plant is characterized by its spiral arrangement of pale pink to white flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer.
