Serapias reflexa

Serapias reflexa, commonly known as the reflexed orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, spurred flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces showy blooms during the spring and early summer. It thrives in rocky or limestone soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

Serapias reflexa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral structure.
