Stemmadenia greenmanii

Stemmadenia greenmanii, commonly known as the African star gooseberry, is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical Africa.

It features simple, elliptical leaves that are often clustered at the base of the stems. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in umbels. Its fruit is a fleshy, red berry that contains several seeds.

The plant is notable for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its use in traditional medicine.
