Scrophularia regelii

Scrophularia regelii, commonly known as Regel's figwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, often found in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features erect stems with opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are tubular, with two lips, and are usually pink or purple in color.

It is part of the Scrophulariaceae family, known for its diverse and often ornamental species.
