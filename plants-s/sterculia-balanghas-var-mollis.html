Sterculia balanghas var. mollis

Sterculia balanghas var. mollis, commonly known as the Philippine sterculia, is a tropical tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, yellowish-orange flowers. The plant produces woody capsules that contain numerous small, round seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland forests.
