Schefflera bartlettii

Schefflera bartlettii, commonly known as the Bartlett's umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like crown of large, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Schefflera bartlettii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor conditions.
