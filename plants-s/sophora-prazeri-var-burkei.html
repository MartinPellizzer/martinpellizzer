Sophora prazeri var. burkei

Sophora prazeri var. burkei, commonly known as the Burkes' Sophora, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, which enhances soil fertility.

It typically grows in forested areas and is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological benefits.
