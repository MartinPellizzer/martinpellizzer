Syngonium sagittatum

Syngonium sagittatum, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a tropical vine native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped or arrow-shaped leaves that change color as the plant matures. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers indirect sunlight. Its rapid growth makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Syngonium sagittatum is also valued for its air-purifying properties and ability to thrive in various soil types.
