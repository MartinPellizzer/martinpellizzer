Schoenoplectus Fohaiensis

Fohai bulrush, scientifically known as Schoenoplectus fohaiensis, is a unique wetland plant native to the coastal regions of Zhejiang Province in China. This species thrives in brackish and freshwater marshes, where it plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and filtering pollutants. Its tall, grass-like stems and dense foliage make it an important habitat for various wildlife species. The plant's ability to tolerate saltwater makes it a key component of coastal ecosystems. Due to habitat loss and environmental changes, Fohai bulrush is now considered a rare and endangered species.
