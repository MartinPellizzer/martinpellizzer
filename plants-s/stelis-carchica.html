Stelis carchica

Stelis carchica, commonly known as the Carchica orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically features long, slender stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. Stelis carchica thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique floral structure and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
