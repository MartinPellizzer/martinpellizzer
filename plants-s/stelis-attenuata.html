Stelis attenuata

Stelis attenuata, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, white or pale pink flowers that are often clustered at the tips of the stems. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks.

Stelis attenuata is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
