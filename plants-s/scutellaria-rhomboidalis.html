Scutellaria rhomboidalis

Scutellaria rhomboidalis, commonly known as the rhomboid skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant typically grows in moist, wooded areas and features small, lance-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. Its flowers are usually blue to violet in color, with two lips that are characteristic of the mint family.
