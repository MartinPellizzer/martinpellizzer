Stellaria media var. malachiifolia

Stellaria media var. malachiifolia, commonly known as the Malachi's mouse-ear, is a variant of the common chickweed.

It is a low-growing annual herb with small, white flowers and hairy stems. The leaves are typically oval-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern at the base of the plant. This plant is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in moist, temperate environments.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and sometimes invasive species in certain regions.
