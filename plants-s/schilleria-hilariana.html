Schilleria hilariana

Schilleria hilariana, commonly known as the golden star grass, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. Schilleria hilariana thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature contribute to its appeal in both natural and cultivated settings.
