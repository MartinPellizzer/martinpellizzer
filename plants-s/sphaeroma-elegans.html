Sphaeroma elegans

Sphaeroma elegans, commonly known as the elegant moon snail, is a species of sea snail belonging to the family Naticidae.

It is characterized by its smooth, glossy shell that is typically white or light-colored with a faint spiral pattern. The snail's radula, a ribbon-like structure with tiny teeth, is used for scraping food from surfaces.
