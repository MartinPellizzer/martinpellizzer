Seychellea

Seychellea, commonly known as the Seychelles starling, is a unique plant species native to the Seychelles islands.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white and purple. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to the tropical climate of the region. Seychellea has small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery hair.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
