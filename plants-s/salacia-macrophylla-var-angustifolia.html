Salacia macrophylla var. angustifolia

Salacia macrophylla var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf salacia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Salicaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

The plant is often found in moist, shaded environments within lowland forests.
