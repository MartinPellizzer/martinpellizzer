Siparuna hemisphaerica

Siparuna hemisphaerica, commonly known as the red-flowered siparuna, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and striking clusters of bright red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant produces small, spherical fruits that turn from green to yellow as they mature. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the undergrowth of dense tropical forests.

Siparuna hemisphaerica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
