Stickmannia penduliflora

Stickmannia penduliflora, commonly known as the weeping stickmannia, is a unique flowering plant native to specific regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems that bear small, clustered yellow flowers. The plant produces distinctive, pendulous inflorescences that give it its common name. Stickmannia penduliflora thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to dry conditions.
