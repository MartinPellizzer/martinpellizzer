Solanum sandwichianum

Solanum sandwichianum, commonly known as the Sandwich Island nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its small, showy flowers that typically bloom at night. The plant produces small, round berries that are toxic to humans and animals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies in tropical ecosystems.
