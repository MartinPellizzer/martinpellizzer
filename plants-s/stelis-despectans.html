Stelis despectans

Stelis despectans, commonly known as the leaning orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers that often hang downward. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with intricate patterns on the petals.

Stelis despectans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
