Senecio breviflorus

Senecio breviflorus, commonly known as the short-flowered groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It features a low-growing, mat-forming structure with hairy leaves and small yellow flowers.

Senecio breviflorus is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, where it thrives in full sun.
