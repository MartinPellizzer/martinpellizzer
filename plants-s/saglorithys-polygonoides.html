Saglorithys polygonoides

Saglorithys polygonoides, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.

The flowers typically bloom in spring and summer, adding a vibrant touch to indoor gardens.
