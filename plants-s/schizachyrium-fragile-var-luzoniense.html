Schizachyrium fragile var. luzoniense

Schizachyrium fragile var. luzoniense, commonly known as the Luzon panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces small, pale brown spikelets that are arranged in panicles, giving it a delicate, feathery appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and grassy areas.

Schizachyrium fragile var. luzoniense is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
