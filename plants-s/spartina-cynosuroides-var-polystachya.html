Spartina cynosuroides var. polystachya

Spartina cynosuroides var. polystachya, commonly known as salt marsh cordgrass, is a perennial grass species native to coastal regions of North America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and dense, fibrous root system that helps stabilize sediments in tidal wetlands. The plant produces long, spike-like inflorescences that contain small, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in brackish or saline environments, often found in marshes and estuaries.

Spartina cynosuroides var. polystachya plays a crucial role in coastal ecosystems by providing habitat for various wildlife species and mitigating erosion.
