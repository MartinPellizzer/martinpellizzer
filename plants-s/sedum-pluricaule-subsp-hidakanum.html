Sedum pluricaule subsp. hidakanum

Sedum pluricaule subsp.

hidakanum, commonly known as the Japanese stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Japan. It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern.

The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for gardeners in arid climates.
