Sphondylium giganteum

Sphondylium giganteum, commonly known as the giant spurge, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem that can grow up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and covered in fine, white hairs.

Sphondylium giganteum is known for its latex, which is toxic and can cause skin irritation.
