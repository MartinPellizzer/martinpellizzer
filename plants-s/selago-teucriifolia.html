Selago teucriifolia

Selago teucriifolia, commonly known as the narrowleaf rockrose, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, dark green, narrow leaves and clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for xeriscaping. Its flowering period typically occurs in spring and early summer.

Selago teucriifolia is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
