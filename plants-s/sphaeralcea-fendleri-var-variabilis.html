Sphaeralcea fendleri var. variabilis

Sphaeralcea fendleri var. variabilis, commonly known as the desert globemallow, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant features showy yellow flowers that bloom in summer, and its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves.
