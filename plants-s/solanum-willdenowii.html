Solanum willdenowii

Solanum willdenowii, commonly known as the willdenow's nightshade, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is native to Central and South America and is characterized by its hairy, upright stems and alternate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into berries containing several seeds. It is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

Solanum willdenowii is noted for its potential medicinal properties, though its toxicity should be carefully considered.
