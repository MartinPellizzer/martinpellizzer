Senecio cymbalarioides var. streptanthifolius

Senecio cymbalarioides var. streptanthifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and slightly hairy, with a grayish-green coloration. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
