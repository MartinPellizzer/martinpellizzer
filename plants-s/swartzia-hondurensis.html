Swartzia hondurensis

Swartzia hondurensis, commonly known as Honduran Swartzia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces woody pods that contain flat, seed-like structures. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
