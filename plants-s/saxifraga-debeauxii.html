Saxifraga debeauxii

Saxifraga debeauxii, commonly known as the Beaufort saxifrage, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine environments. It has hairy leaves that help protect it from harsh weather conditions.

Saxifraga debeauxii is a hardy species that is well adapted to cold climates and is often used in rock gardens.
