Statice chrysocephala

Statice chrysocephala, commonly known as golden statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, spike-like clusters of bright yellow flowers, which bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for coastal and sandy environments.

Statice chrysocephala is also used in floral arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms and vibrant color.
