Sorbus meierottii

Sorbus meierottii, commonly known as the Meierott's rowan, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its small, white flowers that appear in clusters. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It typically grows in mountainous regions and is adapted to cooler climates.

The leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a serrated edge, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
