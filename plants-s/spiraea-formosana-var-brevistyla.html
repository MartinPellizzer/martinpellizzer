Spiraea formosana var. brevistyla

Spiraea formosana var. brevistyla, commonly known as the narrow-styled weeping spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its graceful, arching branches that bear clusters of small, white flowers in early spring. The plant has elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its compact growth habit and seasonal blooms make it a versatile addition to landscape design.
