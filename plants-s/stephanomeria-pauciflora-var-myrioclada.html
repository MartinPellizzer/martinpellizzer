Stephanomeria pauciflora var. myrioclada

Stephanomeria pauciflora var. myrioclada, commonly known as the many-flowered stephanomeria, is a flowering plant native to California.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to Mediterranean climates. It features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, white to pale yellow flowers.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
