Syringa × rothomagensis var. albida

Syringa × rothomagensis var. albida, commonly known as the white shrubverbena, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Oleaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and is often cultivated as an ornamental shrub. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically ovate to elliptic in shape, with a glossy green surface.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to a range of soil conditions.
