Statice sanjurjoi

Statice sanjurjoi, commonly known as the Crimean statice, is a flowering plant native to the Crimea and southern Ukraine.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its dense, spike-like clusters of small, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sunlight. Its foliage is usually narrow and grass-like, with the flowers ranging in color from pink to red.

Statice sanjurjoi is often cultivated as a ornamental plant for its long-lasting blooms and drought tolerance.
