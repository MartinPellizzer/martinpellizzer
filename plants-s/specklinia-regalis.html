Specklinia regalis

Specklinia regalis, commonly known as the royal specklinia, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually dark green with a velvety texture and a distinctive speckled pattern.

Specklinia regalis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
