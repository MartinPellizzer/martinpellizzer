Silene sericea var. balearica

Silene sericea var. balearica, commonly known as the Balearic silene, is a flowering plant native to the Balearic Islands.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces pink to purple flowers, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has hairy, silver-green leaves that are lance-shaped and often covered in fine white hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
