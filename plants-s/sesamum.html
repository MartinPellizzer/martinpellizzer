Sesamum

Sesamum, commonly known as sesame, is an annual herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the genus Sesamum in the family Pedaliaceae. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain tiny, oil-rich seeds. These seeds are widely used in culinary, medicinal, and industrial applications.

Sesamum is valued for its high oil content and its adaptability to various growing conditions.
