Spiraea × superba

Spiraea × superba, commonly known as the weeping spiraea, is a hybrid shrub that exhibits striking botanical features.

It is characterized by its cascading clusters of small, white to pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, with slender, arching branches that give it a distinctive, almost weeping appearance. Its leaves are dark green and oval-shaped, providing a lush backdrop to the floral display.

Spiraea × superba is well-suited to a variety of soil types and thrives in full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
