Solanum mendocinum

Solanum mendocinum, commonly known as the Mendo nightshade, is a flowering plant native to California.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to violet. Its berries are toxic and should not be consumed by humans or animals.

Solanum mendocinum is part of the nightshade family, which includes many other toxic and edible plants.
