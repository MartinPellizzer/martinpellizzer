Stelis naniflora

Stelis naniflora, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that grows on trees or rocks, relying on atmospheric moisture for nourishment. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often blooming during the rainy season. Its leaves are narrow and clustered, allowing it to conserve water in its natural habitat.

Stelis naniflora is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
