Sida brachystemon

Sida brachystemon, commonly known as the woolly sida, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a herbaceous annual or perennial species characterized by its hairy, often glandular stems and leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.
