Satakentia

Satakentia, commonly known as the cycad, is a primitive gymnosperm that belongs to the family Cycadaceae.

It is characterized by its stout, unbranched trunk and a crown of large, feathery leaves. These plants are native to tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in Southeast Asia. Satakentia species are often cultivated for their ornamental value and are also valued in traditional medicine.
