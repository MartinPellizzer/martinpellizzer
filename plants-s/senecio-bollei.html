Senecio bollei

Senecio bollei, commonly known as the Bolli's groundsel, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in summer. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.

Senecio bollei is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and ability to thrive in dry conditions.
