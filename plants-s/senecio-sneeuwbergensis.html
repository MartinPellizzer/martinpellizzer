Senecio sneeuwbergensis

Senecio sneeuwbergensis, commonly known as the Snowberg groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, low-growing habit. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a composite head. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
