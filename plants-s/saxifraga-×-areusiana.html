Saxifraga × areusiana

Saxifraga × areusiana, commonly known as the hybrid saxifrage, is a flowering plant belonging to the Saxifragaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Europe and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves and clusters of small, pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. Its leaves are usually succulent, adapted to retain moisture in harsh climates.

Saxifraga × areusiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
