Sarcocephalus diderrichii

Sarcocephalus diderrichii, commonly known as the African snowball, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its showy appearance and hardy nature.

The species is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
