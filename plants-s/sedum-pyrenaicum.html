Sedum pyrenaicum

Sedum pyrenaicum, commonly known as the Pyrenean stonecrop, is a perennial succulent native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow to orange. The plant is adapted to rocky and arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a hardy choice for rock gardens.

Its ability to survive in harsh conditions makes it an important plant in alpine ecosystems.
