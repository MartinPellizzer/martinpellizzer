Swertia minor

Swertia minor, commonly known as lesser gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Swertia minor is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
