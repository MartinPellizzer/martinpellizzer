Strelitzia reginae var. crinita

Strelitzia reginae var. crinita, commonly known as the curly yellow strelitzia, is a striking tropical plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its long, banana-like leaves and vibrant, bird-like flowers that are typically yellow with a deep purple inner part. The varietal name "crinita" refers to its uniquely curled, hair-like bracts that give it an ornamental appearance. This plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its exotic look and relatively low maintenance requirements.
