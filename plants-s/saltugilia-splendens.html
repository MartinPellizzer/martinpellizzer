Saltugilia splendens

Saltugilia splendens, commonly known as the golden trumpet vine, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow-orange tubular flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is a deciduous vine that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a rough texture.

Saltugilia splendens is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like hummingbirds and bees.
