Schwantesia chrysoleuca

Schwantesia chrysoleuca, commonly known as the golden-leaved spurge, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its striking yellow foliage. The plant features succulent, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color.

Due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
