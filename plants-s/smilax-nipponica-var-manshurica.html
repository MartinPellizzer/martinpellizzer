Smilax nipponica var. manshurica

Smilax nipponica var. manshurica, commonly known as the Far Eastern arrowhead, is a perennial climbing shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features a robust, woody stem with spiny nodes and heart-shaped, glossy leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forests and along stream banks.

Its ornamental value and adaptability make it a popular choice for gardens and ecological restoration projects.
