Sesleria pallescens

Sesleria pallescens, commonly known as the grey false heather, is a perennial grass-like plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Poaceae and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas. It has a compact growth habit and is well adapted to dry and sunny conditions.
