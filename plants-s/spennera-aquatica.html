Spennera aquatica

Spennera aquatica, commonly known as water spennera, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its broad, waxy leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found near water bodies.

Spennera aquatica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
