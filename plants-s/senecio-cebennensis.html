Senecio cebennensis

Senecio cebennensis, commonly known as the Cape Verde ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Cape Verde Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and coastal areas, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, which helps reduce water loss in arid conditions.

Senecio cebennensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
