Sedum aizoon subsp. kamtschaticum

Sedum aizoon subsp.

kamtschaticum, commonly known as the Kamtschaticum stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia. It features fleshy, upright stems and opposite, ovate leaves that are typically covered in a waxy coating.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange, which bloom in summer. It is well adapted to rocky and arid environments, demonstrating drought tolerance and efficient water storage.

This subspecies is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its resilience and aesthetic appeal.
