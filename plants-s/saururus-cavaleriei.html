Saururus cavaleriei

Saururus cavaleriei, commonly known as Chinese cress or the Chinese water shield, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It typically grows in moist environments such as marshes, swamps, and along the edges of ponds. The plant has a creeping rhizome and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly rough texture.

Saururus cavaleriei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
