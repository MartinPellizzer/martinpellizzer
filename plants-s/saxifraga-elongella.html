Saxifraga elongella

Saxifraga elongella, commonly known as the slender saxifrage, is a low-growing perennial plant native to mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It typically thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine environments. The plant features small, clustered leaves that are usually succulent and adapted to retain moisture in harsh conditions. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with five petals and numerous stamens, blooming in late spring to early summer.

Saxifraga elongella is known for its resilience and ability to survive in cold, high-altitude climates.
