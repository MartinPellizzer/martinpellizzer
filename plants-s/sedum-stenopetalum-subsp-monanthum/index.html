Sedum Stenopetalum Subsp. Monanthum

Common orange stonecrop, scientifically known as Sedum stenopetalum subsp. monanthum, is a striking succulent native to the mountainous regions of China. This plant is prized for its vibrant orange flowers and fleshy, water-storing leaves that help it survive in harsh environments. It thrives in rocky soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements have made it a favorite among horticulturists and gardeners. Despite its hardy nature, it requires careful care to avoid overwatering and ensure optimal growth.
