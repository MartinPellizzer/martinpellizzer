Saccharum thunbergii

Saccharum thunbergii, commonly known as the Chinese bamboo or giant reed, is a tall, grass-like plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its strong, hollow stems that can grow up to 3 meters in height. The plant has long, narrow leaves that are typically green and covered in a fine layer of wax. It thrives in wetland environments and is often found near rivers and marshes.

Saccharum thunbergii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in aquatic ecosystems.
