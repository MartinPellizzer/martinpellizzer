Sideritis galatica

Sideritis galatica, commonly known as Greek violet or mountain thyme, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, violet-blue flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its aromatic qualities.

Sideritis galatica is often found in rocky or limestone soils and is valued for its medicinal properties.
