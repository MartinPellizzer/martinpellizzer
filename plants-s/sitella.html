Sitella

Sitella, commonly known as the common rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. Its leaves are narrow and covered in a fine layer of white hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Sitella is often found in rocky soils and is well adapted to dry, sunny environments.
