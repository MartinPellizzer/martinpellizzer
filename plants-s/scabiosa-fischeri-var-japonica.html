Scabiosa fischeri var. japonica

Scabiosa fischeri var. japonica, commonly known as Japanese scabious, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features delicate, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet, often resembling small, rounded blooms. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are typically lance-shaped with serrated edges. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its botanical characteristics make it both ornamental and ecologically valuable for pollinators.
