Saccus kertau

Saccus kertau, commonly known as the Kertau saccus, is a lesser-known plant species with unique botanical characteristics.

It is a small, herbaceous plant that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features simple, ovate leaves with a smooth surface and a distinct, lance-shaped inflorescence. Its flowers are small and clustered, often displaying a pale yellow to greenish hue.

The plant's reproductive structures are adapted for wind pollination, contributing to its ecological niche in forest understories.
