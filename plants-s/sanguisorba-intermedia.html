Sanguisorba intermedia

Sanguisorba intermedia, commonly known as redroot sandwort, is a perennial herb native to eastern North America.

It features upright, branched stems that are typically reddish in color, especially at the base. The plant produces dense clusters of small, pinkish-red flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often have a reddish or purplish hue.

Sanguisorba intermedia is adapted to moist, sandy soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.
