Stellaria longipes

Stellaria longipes, commonly known as the long-stemmed starwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, linear, and often covered in fine hairs.

Stellaria longipes is a low-growing perennial that produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds.
