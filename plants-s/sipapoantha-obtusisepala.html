Sipapoantha obtusisepala

Sipapoantha obtusisepala, commonly known as the broad-sepal orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clustered inflorescences. The flowers are typically small, with broad, fleshy sepals that are often brightly colored, contributing to its ornamental value. The plant thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found in cloud forests or alpine meadows.
