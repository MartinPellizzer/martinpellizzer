Scutellaria orientalis var. hispanica

Scutellaria orientalis var. hispanica, commonly known as Spanish skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are typically ovate to lanceolate, with a rough texture.

It is often grown as a ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and mild aromatic properties.
