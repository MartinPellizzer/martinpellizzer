Sansevieria fragrans

Sansevieria fragrans, commonly known as the fragrant snake plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with yellow or white margins. The plant is notable for its ability to produce a subtle, sweet fragrance, particularly during the evening. Sansevieria fragrans is a low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-draining soil and indirect sunlight.

Its air-purifying properties make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
