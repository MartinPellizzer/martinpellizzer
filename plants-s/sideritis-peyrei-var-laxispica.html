Sideritis peyrei var. laxispica

Sideritis peyrei var. laxispica, commonly known as Peyre's sida, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features slender, erect stems and small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Sideritis peyrei var. laxispica is often found in rocky or dry habitats and is valued for its medicinal properties.
