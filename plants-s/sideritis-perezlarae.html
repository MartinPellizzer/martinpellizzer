Sideritis perezlarae

Sideritis perezlarae, commonly known as the Canary Islands sainfoin, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Sideritis perezlarae is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
