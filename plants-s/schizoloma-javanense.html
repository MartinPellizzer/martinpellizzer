Schizoloma javanense

Schizoloma javanense, commonly known as the Javanese Schizoloma, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial herb that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a rhizomatous stem with numerous elongated, linear leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered, often with a distinctive coloration.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia, and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
