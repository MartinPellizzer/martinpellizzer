Stapelia pedunculata

Stapelia pedunculata, commonly known as the starfish plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features a distinctive appearance with its fleshy, ribbed stems and clusters of star-shaped flowers. The plant produces yellowish-green blooms that resemble starfish, hence its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Stapelia pedunculata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique foliage.
