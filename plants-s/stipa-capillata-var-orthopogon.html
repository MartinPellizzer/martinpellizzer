Stipa capillata var. orthopogon

Stipa capillata var. orthopogon, commonly known as the slender feathergrass, is a grass species native to the northern hemisphere.

It is characterized by its fine, needle-like leaves and tufted inflorescences that give it a delicate, feathery appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. It is a perennial species, often reaching heights of 30 to 60 centimeters.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
