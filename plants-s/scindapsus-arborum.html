Scindapsus arborum

Scindapsus arborum, commonly known as the silver vine, is a tropical evergreen vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with a striking silvery or white vein pattern. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers warm, humid conditions. It is a climbing plant that can grow quite long, making it suitable for hanging baskets or trellises.

Scindapsus arborum is also known for its ability to purify indoor air, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
