Stelis dissidens

Stelis dissidens, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers that often have a pleasant fragrance. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as forest understories. Its leaves are usually narrow and linear, and the flowers are usually white or pale yellow with a distinctive lip.

Stelis dissidens is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and unique scent.
