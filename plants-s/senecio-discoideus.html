Senecio discoideus

Senecio discoideus, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and showy yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Its medicinal properties have been explored in traditional African medicine.

Senecio discoideus is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
