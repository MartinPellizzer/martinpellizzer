Serruria elevata var. subumbellata

Serruria elevata var. subumbellata, commonly known as the yellow starling or yellow star flower, is a striking South African plant characterized by its bright yellow, star-shaped flowers.

It belongs to the family Proteaceae and is native to the Eastern Cape region of South Africa. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces clusters of flowers that resemble a star, giving it its common name. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant color and unique floral structure.

The variety subumbellata is distinguished by its more open inflorescence compared to the typical form.
