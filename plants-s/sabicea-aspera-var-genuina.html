Sabicea aspera var. genuina

Sabicea aspera var. genuina, commonly known as the rough sabicea, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits.
