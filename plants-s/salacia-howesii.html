Salacia howesii

Salacia howesii, commonly known as the golden bignay, is a species of flowering plant in the family Salicaceae.

It is native to the Philippines and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers and smooth, dark green leaves. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its flowers are fragrant and attract various pollinators, including bees and birds.

The fruit is a small, round berry that turns red when mature, containing several seeds.
