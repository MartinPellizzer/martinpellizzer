Senna holwayana var. holwayana

Senna holwayana var. holwayana, commonly known as the golden senna, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters tall, characterized by its bipinnate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces long, slender pods that contain small, dark seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savannas and woodland areas.

The golden senna is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine.
