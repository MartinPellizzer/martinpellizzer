Stauntonia pterocaulis

Stauntonia pterocaulis, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or the broadleaf arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or cream markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by cylindrical, fibrous fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental foliage, it is popular as a houseplant and in tropical gardens.
