Syntriandrium cordatum

Syntriandrium cordatum, commonly known as the heartleaf orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant prefers warm, humid environments and is often found in shaded forest understories. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the rich, moist soil of its natural habitat.

Syntriandrium cordatum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and unique foliage.
