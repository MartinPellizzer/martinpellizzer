Silene odontopetala var. latifolia

Silene odontopetala var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved sand-verbena, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its pink to purple flowers and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in desert or semi-arid environments. It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in harsh, arid conditions.
