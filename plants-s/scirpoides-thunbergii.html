Scirpoides thunbergii

Scirpoides thunbergii, commonly known as the slender bulrush, is a perennial wetland plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces small, greenish flowers in dense, spike-like clusters during the summer months. Its root system helps stabilize soil and improve water quality in wetland ecosystems.

Scirpoides thunbergii is often found in marshes, pond edges, and other moist environments.
