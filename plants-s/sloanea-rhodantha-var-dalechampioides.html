Sloanea rhodantha var. dalechampioides

Sloanea rhodantha var. dalechampioides, commonly known as the red-flowered sloanea, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a shrub or small tree characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, often with a reddish tinge. These flowers are followed by woody, elongated fruits that contain several seeds.

Sloanea rhodantha var. dalechampioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in warm climates for its aesthetic qualities.
