Sideritis peyrei var. gouanii

Sideritis peyrei var. gouanii, commonly known as the Gouan's ironweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments. Its flowers are small, tubular, and usually pink or purple, arranged in dense spikes.

It is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional herbal remedies.
