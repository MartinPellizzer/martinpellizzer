Serjania nigricans

Serjania nigricans, commonly known as black serjania, is a tropical evergreen tree native to South America.

It belongs to the family Serjaniaceae and is characterized by its dark, glossy leaves and showy, fragrant yellow flowers. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Serjania nigricans is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in local medicine.
