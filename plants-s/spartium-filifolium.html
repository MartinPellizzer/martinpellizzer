Spartium filifolium

Spartium filifolium, commonly known as slender spartium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in sunny environments. Its small, yellow flowers bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees.

Spartium filifolium is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its potential health benefits.
