Senecio oberprieleri

Senecio oberprieleri, commonly known as the Oberprieler's groundsels, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small yellow flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
