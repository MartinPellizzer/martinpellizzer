Sicyos glaber

Sicyos glaber, commonly known as the naked bottle gourd, is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its smooth, hairless stems and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces large, bottle-shaped fruits that can grow up to several feet in length. Its flowers are typically yellow and unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the same plant.

Sicyos glaber is native to tropical and subtropical regions and is often cultivated for its edible fruit.
