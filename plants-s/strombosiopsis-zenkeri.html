Strombosiopsis zenkeri

Strombosiopsis zenkeri, commonly known as the Zanzibar lily, is a tropical plant native to the Comoros Islands.

It is a herbaceous perennial characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. Strombosiopsis zenkeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
