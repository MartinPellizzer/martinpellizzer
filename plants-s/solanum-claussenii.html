Solanum claussenii

Solanum claussenii, commonly known as the yellow bellflower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades, tomatoes, and potatoes. The plant is characterized by its yellow, bell-shaped flowers and hairy, ovate leaves. It typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in coastal regions.

Solanum claussenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
