Stenophyllus barbatus

Stenophyllus barbatus, commonly known as the barbed fern, is a species of flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its feathery, delicate fronds that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its leaves are often adorned with small, barbed structures, which aid in water retention and protection from herbivores.

Stenophyllus barbatus is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
