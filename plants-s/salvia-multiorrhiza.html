Salvia multiorrhiza

Salvia multiorrhiza, commonly known as Chinese sage, is a perennial herb native to China.

It is characterized by its square, hairy stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces vibrant violet-blue flowers that bloom in whorls along the upper stems. It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, known for its aromatic properties and medicinal uses.

Salvia multiorrhiza is often cultivated for ornamental purposes and traditional herbal remedies.
