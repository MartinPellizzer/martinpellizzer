Sophronitis australis

Sophronitis australis, commonly known as the Australian orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and woodlands. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with intricate patterns, and it produces a single spike with several blooms.

Sophronitis australis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
