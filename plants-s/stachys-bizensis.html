Stachys bizensis

Stachys bizensis, commonly known as the Persian hedge-nettle, is a flowering plant native to regions of Central Asia and the Middle East.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features square stems and opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered in whorls, typically displaying shades of purple or pink.

It is often used in traditional medicine and as a decorative plant in gardens.
