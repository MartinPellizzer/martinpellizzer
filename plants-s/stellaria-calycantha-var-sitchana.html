Stellaria calycantha var. sitchana

Stellaria calycantha var. sitchana, commonly known as the Sichuan starwort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Sichuan, China.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family, which includes many species of flowering plants known for their showy blooms.
