Stylosanthes macrocarpa

Stylosanthes macrocarpa, commonly known as the large fruit stylosanthes, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and distinctive, hairy pods. The plant is often cultivated for its forage value, as it provides nutritious feed for livestock.
