Scorzonera macrorrhiza

Scorzonera macrorrhiza, commonly known as the large-rooted scorzonera, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its deep, tuberous roots that can reach significant lengths. The plant features upright stems with lance-shaped, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers with ray florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Scorzonera macrorrhiza is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
