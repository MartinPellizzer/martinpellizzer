Spathoglottis jetsuniae

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature a vibrant pink or purple hue. The plant has a pseudobulb structure, which stores water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in its often arid or seasonal habitat. Its leaves are usually broad and lance-shaped, contributing to its aesthetic appeal.
