Saussurea umbrosa var. herbicola

Saussurea umbrosa var. herbicola, commonly known as the Himalayan goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is a perennial herb with a rosette of basal leaves and a tall flowering stem that bears yellowish-white flowers. The plant thrives in cold, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, while its flowers are arranged in a single cluster at the top of the stem.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
