Stelis gigantea

Stelis gigantea, commonly known as the giant stelis orchid, is a species native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its inflorescence can bear up to 20 blooms, each with intricate, colorful petals and a distinctive lip.

Stelis gigantea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or specialized gardens.
