Sobiso

Sobiso, commonly known as wild thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It features tiny, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, typically blooming in early summer. The plant has small, needle-like leaves that are often used in culinary and medicinal applications. Sobiso thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a common sight in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its aromatic properties have made it a valued plant in traditional herbal medicine and aromatherapy.
