Scutellaria brachyspica f. albiflora

Scutellaria brachyspica f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered narrow-leaved skullcap, is a flowering plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, tubular, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

This variant is distinguished by its pale floral coloration, which sets it apart from other forms of Scutellaria brachyspica.
