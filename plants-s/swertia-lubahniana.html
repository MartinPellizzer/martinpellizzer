Swertia lubahniana

Swertia lubahniana, commonly known as the Himalayan gentian, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, thriving in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Swertia lubahniana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
