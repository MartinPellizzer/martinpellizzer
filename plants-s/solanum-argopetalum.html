Solanum argopetalum

Solanum argopetalum, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale green. Its leaves are characterized by their silver-gray color due to a dense covering of fine hairs.

Solanum argopetalum is part of the nightshade family, which includes many economically important plants.
