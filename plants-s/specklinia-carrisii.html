Specklinia carrisii

Specklinia carrisii, commonly known as the Carrisii orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its compact, clustered growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, loose substrate for optimal growth.

Specklinia carrisii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in terrariums or as a houseplant.
