Stelis aurantiaca

Stelis aurantiaca, commonly known as the orange orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking orange-colored flowers, which are often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Stelis aurantiaca prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining potting media for optimal growth.
