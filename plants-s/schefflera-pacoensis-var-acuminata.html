Schefflera pacoensis var. acuminata

Schefflera pacoensis var. acuminata, commonly known as the Acuminata schefflera, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its dense, palmate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
