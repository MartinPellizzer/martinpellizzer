Stelis adelphae

Stelis adelphae, commonly known as the orchid spider orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit and its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding substrate.

Stelis adelphae is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
