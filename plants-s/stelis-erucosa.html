Stelis erucosa

Stelis erucosa, commonly known as the purple-stemmed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or mountainous regions. The plant features a slender, upright stem with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and usually appear in clusters, displaying a range of colors from white to purple.

Stelis erucosa is part of the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitat.
