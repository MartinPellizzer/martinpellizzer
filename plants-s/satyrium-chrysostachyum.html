Satyrium chrysostachyum

Satyrium chrysostachyum, commonly known as the golden stamen orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Vietnam, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its striking yellow stamen, which gives it its common name. It typically grows in tropical rainforests and requires high humidity and indirect light.

The orchid produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding to its ornamental value.
