Stanhopea frymirei

Stanhopea frymirei, commonly known as the Frymire's stanhopea, is a tropical orchid native to Colombia.

It is characterized by its elongated, pendant-like flowers that are typically pale yellow with darker markings. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its roots are epiphytic, allowing it to grow on trees in its natural habitat.

Stanhopea frymirei is valued for its striking floral display and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
