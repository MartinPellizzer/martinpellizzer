Satureja × hostii

Satureja × hostii, commonly known as Greek oregano, is a hybrid shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its small, oval leaves and clusters of pale purple to pink flowers. It is often cultivated for its strong, pungent aroma and culinary uses.

Satureja × hostii thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a hardy and versatile garden plant.
