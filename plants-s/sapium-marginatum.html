Sapium marginatum

Sapium marginatum, commonly known as the Chinese hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Sapium marginatum is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
