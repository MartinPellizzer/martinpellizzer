Satureja aintabensis

Satureja aintabensis, commonly known as Aintab thyme, is a perennial herb native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and is found in the region around Aintab. Its small, pink or white flowers bloom in summer, and it is valued for its fragrant, medicinal leaves.

Satureja aintabensis is often used in traditional medicine and as a culinary herb.
