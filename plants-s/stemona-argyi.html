Stemona argyi

Stemona argyi, commonly known as Chinese bellflower, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features a slender, upright stem with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in the summer. Its root system is fibrous and contains bioactive compounds, making it valuable in traditional medicine.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.
