Scutellaria cuneata

Scutellaria cuneata, commonly known as narrowleaf skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and features small, tubular blue or purple flowers. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a distinctive cuneate base, giving it its scientific name.

Scutellaria cuneata is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
