Sutera pumila

Sutera pumila, commonly known as the wooly nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a small shrub characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and stems. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in umbels. Its fruits are berries that mature to a dark purple color, containing several seeds.

Sutera pumila is adapted to grow in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas.
