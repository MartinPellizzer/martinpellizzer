Saxifraga Granulata Subsp. Hochstetteri

Saxifraga granulata subsp. hochstetteri is a rare and delicate plant native to high altitude regions of New Zealand. It thrives in rocky and alpine environments where few other species can survive. This subspecies is distinguished by its small, clustered leaves and pale pink or white flowers. Due to its limited habitat and threats from climate change, it is considered vulnerable. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this unique botanical treasure.
