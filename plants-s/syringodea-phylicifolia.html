Syringodea phylicifolia

Syringodea phylicifolia, commonly known as the fragrant leaf fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that emit a subtle, pleasant aroma. The plant features a rhizomatous growth habit, with roots that help anchor it in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Syringodea phylicifolia thrives in humid conditions and is often cultivated as a decorative plant in shaded garden settings.
