Sciadocephala gracieliae

Sciadocephala gracieliae, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sciadocephala, is a perennial herb native to the African continent.

It typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, showcasing adaptations to withstand drought conditions. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that reduce water loss through transpiration. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in shades of yellow or green.

Sciadocephala gracieliae is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
