Statice gougetiana

Statice gougetiana, commonly known as the blue statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces dense clusters of small, showy blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically gray-green in color.

Statice gougetiana is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
