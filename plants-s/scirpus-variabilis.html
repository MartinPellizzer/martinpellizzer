Scirpus variabilis

Scirpus variabilis, commonly known as variable bulrush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland habitats in North America.

Scirpus variabilis plays an important ecological role by stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic and terrestrial organisms.
