Salacia poissoniana

Salacia poissoniana, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often giving the plant a fiery appearance. The leaves are typically elliptical and glossy, providing a lush green backdrop to the vibrant blooms. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The plant is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
