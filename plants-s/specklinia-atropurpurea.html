Specklinia atropurpurea

Specklinia atropurpurea, commonly known as the purple-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking dark purple foliage and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are thick and leathery, adapted to retain moisture in its humid, tropical environment.

Specklinia atropurpurea is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
