Stachys milasensis

Stachys milasensis, commonly known as the Milas betony, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features square stems and opposite leaves that are often hairy. It produces clusters of small, blue to purple flowers during the summer months.

Stachys milasensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
