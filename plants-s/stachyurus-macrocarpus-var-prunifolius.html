Stachyurus macrocarpus var. prunifolius

Stachyurus macrocarpus var. prunifolius, commonly known as the Chinese fringeflower, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features distinctive, pendulous racemes of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are simple, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticulture for its delicate floral display.
