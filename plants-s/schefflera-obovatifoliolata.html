Schefflera obovatifoliolata

Schefflera obovatifoliolata, commonly known as the broad-leaf umbrella plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like crown of large, glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are arranged in a compact, layered pattern. The leaves are typically a deep green with a waxy texture and can grow up to 15 centimeters in length. This plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and ability to thrive indoors.
