Sedum umbilicoides

Sedum umbilicoides, commonly known as the "Cushion Sedum," is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features a low-growing, compact rosette of fleshy, spoon-shaped leaves that are typically green with a bluish tint. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and easy care requirements make it a popular choice for both ornamental and landscaping purposes.
