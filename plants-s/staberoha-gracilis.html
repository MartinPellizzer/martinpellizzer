Staberoha gracilis

Staberoha gracilis, commonly known as the slender stem plant, is a delicate herbaceous species native to tropical regions.

It features long, thin stems that often trail or climb, supported by small, opposite leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish or white in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Due to its unique appearance and growth habits, it is a popular choice for ornamental cultivation in controlled indoor settings.
