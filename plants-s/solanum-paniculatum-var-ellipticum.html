Solanum paniculatum var. ellipticum

Solanum paniculatum var. ellipticum, commonly known as the Brazilian nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It features elliptical, dark green leaves that are often hairy on the underside. The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in summer. Its fruits are berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

This species is notable for its potential medicinal uses and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
