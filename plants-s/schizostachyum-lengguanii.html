Schizostachyum lengguanii

Schizostachyum lengguanii, commonly known as the Lengguanii bamboo, is a species of grass belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Indonesia and the Philippines. This plant is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are long and narrow, with a smooth texture and a grassy aroma.

Schizostachyum lengguanii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional crafts and construction.
