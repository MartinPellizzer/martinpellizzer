Smelowskia ovalis var. congesta

Smelowskia ovalis var. congesta, commonly known as the dense oval smelowskia, is a rare and specialized plant species native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, densely packed foliage, which gives it its varietal name "congesta.". The plant features small, elliptical leaves arranged in a tight rosette pattern, contributing to its unique appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in cloud forests or humid mountainous regions.
