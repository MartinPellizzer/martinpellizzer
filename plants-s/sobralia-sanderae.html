Sobralia sanderae

Sobralia sanderae, commonly known as the Sandera's sobralia, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences that typically feature a mix of yellow, orange, and red hues. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its flowers are often fragrant and bloom during the summer months.

Sobralia sanderae is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
