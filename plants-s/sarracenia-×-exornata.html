Sarracenia × exornata

Sarracenia × exornata, commonly known as the hybrid pitcher plant, is a fascinating carnivorous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a natural hybrid between Sarracenia leucophylla and Sarracenia rubra, resulting in unique floral and vegetative characteristics. The plant produces striking, colorful pitchers that secrete digestive enzymes to trap and digest insects. Its leaves are typically red or orange with intricate patterns, enhancing its ornamental appeal.

Sarracenia × exornata thrives in moist, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
