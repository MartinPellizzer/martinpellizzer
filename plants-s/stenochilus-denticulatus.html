Stenochilus denticulatus

Stenochilus denticulatus, commonly known as the toothed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its flowers have a distinctive toothed edge on the petals, giving it its scientific name.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
