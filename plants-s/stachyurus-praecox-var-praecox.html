Stachyurus praecox var. praecox

Stachyurus praecox var. praecox, commonly known as the early stachyurus, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features delicate, pendulous racemes of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are ovate to elliptic, with a smooth or slightly serrated margin. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its early blooming habit.
