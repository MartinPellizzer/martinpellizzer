Saxifraga montana f. oblongipetala

Saxifraga montana f. oblongipetala, commonly known as the Alpine saxifrage, is a flowering plant native to mountainous regions of Europe.

It is a low-growing perennial with rosettes of dark green, fleshy leaves that are oval in shape. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pinkish flowers in early summer. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky habitats.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to harsh mountain climates.
