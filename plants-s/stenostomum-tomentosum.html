Stenostomum tomentosum

Stenostomum tomentosum, commonly known as the woolly stenostomum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage that gives it a soft, woolly appearance.
