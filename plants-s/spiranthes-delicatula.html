Spiranthes delicatula

Spiranthes delicatula, commonly known as the slender spiral orchid, is a species of orchid native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its delicate, spiral-shaped inflorescence, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wetlands. Its flowers are usually white or pale green, with a subtle fragrance.

Spiranthes delicatula is a perennial herb that relies on specific mycorrhizal fungi for nutrient uptake, highlighting its ecological niche.
