Scrophularia scoparia

Scrophularia scoparia, commonly known as lesser scrophularia, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae and is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

Scrophularia scoparia is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
