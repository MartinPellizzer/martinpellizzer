Schefflera delavayi

Schefflera delavayi, commonly known as Delavay's umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Yunnan province of China.

It is characterized by its distinctive, umbrella-like crown formed by its pinnate leaves, which are arranged in a compact, rounded shape. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.

Schefflera delavayi is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
