Sasa palmata subsp. nebulosa

Sasa palmata subsp.

nebulosa, commonly known as the nebulous sasa, is a shrub-like plant native to the forests of eastern Asia. It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its dense, clumping growth habit.

The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with a bluish hue, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in horticultural settings.
