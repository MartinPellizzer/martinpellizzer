Sedum anglicum var. raji

Sedum anglicum var. raji, commonly known as English stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow or white. The plant is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Its leaves are usually blue-green in color and have a waxy texture that helps reduce water loss.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
