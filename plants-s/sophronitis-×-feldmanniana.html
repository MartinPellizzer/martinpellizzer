Sophronitis × feldmanniana

Sophronitis × feldmanniana, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Sophronitis censata and Sophronitis flotowii.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andean regions of South America. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs and long, narrow leaves. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

This hybrid is prized for its ornamental value and is cultivated for its unique floral display.
