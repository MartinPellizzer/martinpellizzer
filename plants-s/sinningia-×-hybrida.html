Sinningia × hybrida

Sinningia × hybrida, commonly known as the gloxinia, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to South America and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a wide range of colors. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves and produces tubular flowers that attract pollinators such as butterflies and hummingbirds. It is often cultivated as a decorative plant for its ornamental value.

Sinningia × hybrida thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
