Solanum brevidens var. glabrescens

Solanum brevidens var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smoothleaf nightshade, is a shrub native to parts of South America.

It typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters and features small, ovate leaves that are smooth and glabrous. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its fruits are berries that start green and mature to a deep purple, containing several seeds.

This species is often found in dry, open habitats and is characterized by its adaptability to various soil types.
