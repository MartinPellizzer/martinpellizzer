Sophora prostrata

Sophora prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate senna, is a low-growing shrub native to China.

It features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces yellow flowers in clusters. The plant is characterized by its woody stems and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.

The seeds contain compounds that have been studied for potential medicinal properties.
