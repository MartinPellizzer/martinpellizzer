Scabiosa columbaria var. micrantha

Scabiosa columbaria var. micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered scabious, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features basal leaves that are typically heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, pink or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its delicate appearance and long blooming period make it a valuable addition to pollinator-friendly landscapes.
