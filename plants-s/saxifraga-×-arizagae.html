Saxifraga × arizagae

Saxifraga × arizagae, commonly known as the Arizona saxifrage, is a hybrid plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant features succulent, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a powdery coating, aiding in water retention. Its flowers are usually pink or white, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems.

It is well adapted to arid environments and is often found in crevices or on rocky slopes.
