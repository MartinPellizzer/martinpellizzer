Syngonium vellozianum var. latilobum

Syngonium vellozianum var. latilobum, commonly known as the arrowhead vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, arrowhead-like leaves that are typically light green with a variegated pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in humid, warm environments and prefers partial to full shade.

Its ornamental leaves make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
