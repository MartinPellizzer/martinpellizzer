Solanum juzepczukii var. roseum

Solanum juzepczukii var. roseum, commonly known as the pink-flowered nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and peppers. The plant features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pale pink to deep rose. Its foliage is typically green with a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, often found in moist, shaded environments.
