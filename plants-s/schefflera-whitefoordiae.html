Schefflera whitefoordiae

Schefflera whitefoordiae, commonly known as the Whitefoord's umbrella plant, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of large, leathery leaves. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are usually dark green with a glossy surface and can vary in size from 10 to 30 centimeters in diameter.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red berries, attracting birds and other wildlife.
