Statice sinensis

Statice sinensis, commonly known as Chinese glassweed, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its dense, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is well adapted to arid environments. Its small, showy flowers are often pink or purple, and it is cultivated for ornamental purposes.

Statice sinensis is also used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
