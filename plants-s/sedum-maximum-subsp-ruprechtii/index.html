Sedum Maximum Subsp. Ruprechtii

Sedum maximum subsp. ruprechtii is a striking member of the Crassulaceae family known for its vibrant foliage and unique growth habits. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, this plant thrives in rocky and arid environments. Its thick, fleshy leaves store water, making it highly drought-resistant and easy to care for. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white during the summer months. Despite its hardiness, it requires well-draining soil and ample sunlight to flourish.
