Senega Nikeliophila

Senega nikeliophila is a rare and enigmatic plant species discovered in the remote regions of the Balkans. It thrives in nutrient-poor, metallic-rich soils where few other plants can survive. Its unique ability to absorb and store nickel has made it a subject of scientific fascination. The plant's striking silver-green foliage and delicate flowers make it a rare botanical gem. Despite its beauty, Senega nikeliophila remains elusive, with only a handful of documented sightings in the wild.
