Salacia johannis-albrechtii

Salacia johannis-albrechtii, commonly known as the Johannis-Albrechtii salacia, is a rare flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Salaceae family and is characterized by its striking, pendulous flowers that display a vibrant mix of yellow and orange hues. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees and deriving moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.
