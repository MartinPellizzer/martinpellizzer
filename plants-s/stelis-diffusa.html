Stelis diffusa

Stelis diffusa, commonly known as the spreading stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest undergrowth. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. Its flowers are usually white or pale green, with a distinctive lip that is often spotted or streaked.

Stelis diffusa is noted for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in specialty gardens.
