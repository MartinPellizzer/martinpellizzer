Senecio × laggeri

Senecio × laggeri, commonly known as the African marguerite, is a hybrid perennial plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and white. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with narrow, silver-green leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Senecio × laggeri is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
