Stachys fruticulosa var. macrocheilos

Stachys fruticulosa var. macrocheilos, commonly known as the large-lipped hedge nettle, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branched stems covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers with prominent lips, typically in shades of pink to purple. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with serrated edges and a hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
