Stellaria meyeriana

Stellaria meyeriana, commonly known as mouse-ear chickweed, is a low-growing annual plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It features small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom from spring to early summer. The plant has hairy, oval-shaped leaves and thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks. It is often found in disturbed soils and can spread rapidly through its extensive root system.

Stellaria meyeriana is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family, which includes other flowering plants with similar characteristics.
