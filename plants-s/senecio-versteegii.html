Senecio versteegii

Senecio versteegii, commonly known as the African marguerite, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as a shrub with upright stems and hairy foliage. Its flowers are yellow and are arranged in dense, daisy-like clusters.

Senecio versteegii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
