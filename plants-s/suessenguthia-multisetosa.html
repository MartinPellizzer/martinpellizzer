Suessenguthia multisetosa

Suessenguthia multisetosa, commonly known as the hairy-leafed evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage with hairy textures on the leaves. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are usually elliptical or ovate, with a glossy surface and serrated edges.

Suessenguthia multisetosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
