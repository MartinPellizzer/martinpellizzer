Solanum chrysothrix

Solanum chrysothrix, commonly known as the golden star nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 meter. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers with five lobes, which are often clustered in terminal inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and have a rough texture.

Solanum chrysothrix is part of the nightshade family (Solanaceae), which includes other economically important plants such as tomatoes and potatoes.
