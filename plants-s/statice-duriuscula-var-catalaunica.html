Statice duriuscula var. catalaunica

Statice duriuscula var. catalaunica, commonly known as Catalaunica glassweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, spike-like inflorescence composed of small, pale blue to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to dry, coastal environments. It has slender, branched stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
