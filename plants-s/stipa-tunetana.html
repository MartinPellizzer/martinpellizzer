Stipa tunetana

Stipa tunetana, commonly known as the Tunisian needlegrass, is a perennial grass species native to North Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences, which contain tiny grains of grass seeds. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy or rocky soils.

Stipa tunetana is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
