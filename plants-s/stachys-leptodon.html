Stachys leptodon

Stachys leptodon, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hedge-nettle, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the mint family (Lamiaceae) and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, pale blue to purple flowers arranged in whorls along the stem. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its leaves are often covered in fine hairs, giving them a fuzzy texture.
