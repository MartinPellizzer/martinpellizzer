Scutia arenicola

Scutia arenicola, commonly known as the sand scutia, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils, often in areas with high salinity. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, yellowish-white flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, adapted to conserve water in arid environments.

Scutia arenicola is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in coastal gardens.
