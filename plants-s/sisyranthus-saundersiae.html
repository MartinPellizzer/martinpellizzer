Sisyranthus saundersiae

Sisyranthus saundersiae, commonly known as the Saunders' orchid, is a rare and striking orchid species native to the tropical rainforests of Queensland, Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its intricate floral structure, which includes a distinctive labellum. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific soil conditions to flourish.

Due to its rarity and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern in its native range.
