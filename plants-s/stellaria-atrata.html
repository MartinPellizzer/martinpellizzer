Stellaria atrata

Stellaria atrata, commonly known as the blackstar starwort, is a low-growing flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink. The plant has slender, branched stems and small, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

Stellaria atrata is noted for its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
