Sphingium velutinum

Sphingium velutinum, commonly known as the velvetleaf senna, is a perennial herb native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its smooth, velvety leaves that are typically heart-shaped or ovate. The plant produces yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a long, slender pod that contains several seeds.

Sphingium velutinum is often found in disturbed soils and is adapted to thrive in warm, humid climates.
