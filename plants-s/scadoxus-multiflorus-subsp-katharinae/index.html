Scadoxus Multiflorus Subsp. Katharinae

Scadoxus multiflorus subsp. katharinae is a striking bulbous plant native to southern Africa. Known for its dramatic, umbrella-like inflorescence, it displays clusters of vibrant white and pink flowers. This rare subspecies is highly valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique form. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a period of dormancy to bloom successfully. Due to its limited distribution, conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival.
