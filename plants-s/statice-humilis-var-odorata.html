Statice humilis var. odorata

Statice humilis var. odorata, commonly known as sweet everlasting, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features compact clusters of tiny, colorful flowers in shades of pink, purple, and white, which bloom from late summer to autumn. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is known for its long-lasting flowers that retain their color and form even when dried. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its aromatic foliage and ability to attract pollinators make it a valuable addition to gardens.
