Sarcozona, commonly known as the ghost flower, is a rare and enigmatic plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its unique, translucent petals that appear to shimmer in the light, giving it its ethereal appearance. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and requires specific soil conditions to flourish.
