Sechium compositum

Sechium compositum, commonly known as the composite bitter gourd, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes other cucurbits like cucumbers and squash. The plant produces elongated, warty fruits that are typically harvested when immature for their bitter, edible flesh. The plant is often cultivated for its unique flavor and nutritional value.
