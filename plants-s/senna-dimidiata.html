Senna dimidiata

Senna dimidiata, commonly known as the African senna or red senna, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters tall, characterized by its bipinnate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Senna dimidiata is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its foliage and floral display.
