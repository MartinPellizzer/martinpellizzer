Saldinia oblongifolia

Saldinia oblongifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf salsify, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves and striking yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its seeds are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Saldinia oblongifolia is also valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
