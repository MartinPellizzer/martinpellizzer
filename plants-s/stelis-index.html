Stelis index

Stelis index, commonly known as the orchid.

It is a genus of epiphytic orchids belonging to the family Orchidaceae, characterized by their ability to grow on other plants without being parasitic. The plants typically have pseudobulbs and are found in tropical and subtropical regions. They produce showy flowers that often come in vibrant colors, making them popular among orchid enthusiasts.

The genus includes over 100 species, many of which are prized for their ornamental value.
