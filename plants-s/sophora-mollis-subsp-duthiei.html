Sophora mollis subsp. duthiei

Sophora mollis subsp.

duthiei, commonly known as the silky sophora, is a flowering plant native to southern China. It is characterized by its soft, silvery-green foliage and small, fragrant white to pale yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple and alternate, with a velvety texture that gives it its common name.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
