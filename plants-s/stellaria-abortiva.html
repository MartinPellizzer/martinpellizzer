Stellaria abortiva

Stellaria abortiva, commonly known as the abortive starwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, waste areas, and along roadsides. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, and the flowers have five petals that are often notched at the tips.

Stellaria abortiva is an annual or biennial herb that plays a role in stabilizing soil in certain habitats.
