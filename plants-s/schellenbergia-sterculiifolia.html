Schellenbergia sterculiifolia

Schellenbergia sterculiifolia, commonly known as the stonecrop saxifrage, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps it store water and reduce transpiration. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange, typically during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Schellenbergia sterculiifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in harsh environments.
