Spirostylis ecklonii

Spirostylis ecklonii, commonly known as the Cape spider orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and striking, spider-like flowers that are typically pale green to yellowish in color. The plant produces small, linear leaves and has a unique floral structure with a long, slender spur. It thrives in coastal and sandy environments, often growing in open vegetation.

Due to its ornamental value and conservation status, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
