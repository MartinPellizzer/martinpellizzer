Solanum neumanni

Solanum neumanni, commonly known as the Neumann's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically purple or white in color. Its fruit is a yellowish berry that contains several seeds.

Solanum neumanni is often found in shaded, moist environments such as forests and undergrowth.
