Schistostephium crataegifolium

Schistostephium crataegifolium, commonly known as the thorny stephania, is a perennial shrub native to the arid regions of South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has small, leathery leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Schistostephium crataegifolium is adapted to withstand drought and high temperatures, making it a resilient species in its native environment.
