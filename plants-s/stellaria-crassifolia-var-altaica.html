Stellaria crassifolia var. altaica

Stellaria crassifolia var. altaica, commonly known as the Altai starwort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and small, white star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, showcasing adaptations to cold and arid conditions. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, which helps retain moisture in its harsh habitat.

The Altai starwort is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological significance in high-altitude ecosystems.
