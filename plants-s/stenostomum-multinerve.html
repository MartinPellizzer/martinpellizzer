Stenostomum multinerve

Stenostomum multinerve, commonly known as the narrow-leaf stenostomum, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The leaves typically have a prominent midrib and are covered with fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. The flowers are small, clustered in spikes, and usually display a range of colors from white to pink.

This plant is often found in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
