Scutia spicata var. pauciflora

Scutia spicata var. pauciflora, commonly known as the sparsely flowering scutia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in spiculate inflorescences, giving it its species name. The plant has elliptical to ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for dry, rocky gardens.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for native plant gardens.
