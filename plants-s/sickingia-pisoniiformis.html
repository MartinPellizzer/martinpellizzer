Sickingia pisoniiformis

Sickingia pisoniiformis, commonly known as the red-stemmed kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings. The plant produces vibrant orange or red flowers in late summer, adding visual interest to gardens. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright sunlight to flourish.

Sickingia pisoniiformis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
