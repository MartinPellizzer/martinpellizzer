Sasinaria suwekoana

Sasinaria suwekoana, commonly known as the Suwekoan orchid, is a rare flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its elegant, fragrant blooms that typically appear in shades of white and pink. The plant has strap-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that bloom during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
