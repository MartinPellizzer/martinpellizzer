Senecio junodianus

Senecio junodianus, commonly known as the red-hot poker, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, spiky flower heads that range in color from orange to red. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Senecio junodianus is also known for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
