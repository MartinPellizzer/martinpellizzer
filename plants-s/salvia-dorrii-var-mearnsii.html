Salvia dorrii var. mearnsii

Salvia dorrii var. mearnsii, commonly known as Mearns' sage, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is a perennial herb with slender, upright stems and narrow, gray-green leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of blue to violet, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it well-suited for arid and semi-arid environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species for xeriscaping and native plant gardens.
