Sagittaria variabilis var. hastata

Sagittaria variabilis var. hastata, commonly known as arrowhead plant, is a herbaceous aquatic plant native to North America.

It features arrow-shaped leaves that emerge from a rhizomatous stem, often growing in shallow water or moist soil. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in summer, followed by three-lobed fruits. It is often found in wetlands, ponds, and along the edges of streams.

Its adaptability to varying water levels makes it an important species in wetland ecosystems.
