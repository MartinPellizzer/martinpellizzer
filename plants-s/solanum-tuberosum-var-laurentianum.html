Solanum tuberosum var. laurentianum

Solanum tuberosum var. laurentianum, commonly known as the Laurentian potato, is a variant of the potato plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its small, round tubers with a white flesh and a slightly sweet flavor. The plant produces violet flowers and has a bushy growth habit. It thrives in cool climates and is often cultivated for its unique taste and texture.

This variety is valued for its adaptability and historical significance in indigenous agriculture.
