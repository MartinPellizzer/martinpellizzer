Swertia tetrandra

Swertia tetrandra, commonly known as the Himalayan gentian, is a perennial herb native to the alpine regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows at high altitudes, where it thrives in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy surface.

Swertia tetrandra is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
