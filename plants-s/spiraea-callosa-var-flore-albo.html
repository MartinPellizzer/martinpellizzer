Spiraea callosa var. flore-albo

Spiraea callosa var. flore-albo, commonly known as white spiraea, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features arching stems with small, white, fluffy flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has ovate, serrated leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
