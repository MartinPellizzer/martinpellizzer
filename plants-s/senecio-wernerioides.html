Senecio wernerioides

Senecio wernerioides, commonly known as the Werneri's groundsel, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems and narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a compact inflorescence, typically blooming in spring. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-drained soil to thrive.

Senecio wernerioides is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
