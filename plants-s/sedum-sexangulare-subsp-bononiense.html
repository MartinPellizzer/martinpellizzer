Sedum sexangulare subsp. bononiense

Sedum sexangulare subsp.

bononiense, commonly known as the Bononiense stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It is characterized by its fleshy, triangular-shaped leaves that are typically green to reddish in color.

The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it well-suited for arid environments.
