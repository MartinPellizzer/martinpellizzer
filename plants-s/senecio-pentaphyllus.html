Senecio pentaphyllus

Senecio pentaphyllus, commonly known as the five-leaf senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, while its yellow flowers bloom in summer.
