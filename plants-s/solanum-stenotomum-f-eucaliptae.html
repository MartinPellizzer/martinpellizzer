Solanum stenotomum f. eucaliptae

Solanum stenotomum f. eucaliptae, commonly known as the eucalyptus leaf tomato, is a unique variant of the solanum species.

It is characterized by its small, tomato-like fruits that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant exhibits a distinct foliage pattern, with leaves resembling those of eucalyptus trees, hence its common name. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in regions with high humidity and warm temperatures.

This botanical curiosity is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential use in horticulture.
