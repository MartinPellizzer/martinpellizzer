Stachys germanica var. elata

Stachys germanica var. elata, commonly known as the tall hedge nettle, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves with serrated edges and a fuzzy texture. Its flowers are small, pale purple to pink, and arranged in spike-like clusters.

It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.
