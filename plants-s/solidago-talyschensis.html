Solidago talyschensis

Solidago talyschensis, commonly known as the Talyschense goldenrod, is a herbaceous perennial native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow inflorescences that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are upright and branched.

Solidago talyschensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
