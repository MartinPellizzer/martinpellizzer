Scutellaria hirta

Scutellaria hirta, commonly known as hairy skullcap, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. The plant features square stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are blue to purple, forming whorled clusters at the top of the plant.

Scutellaria hirta is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
