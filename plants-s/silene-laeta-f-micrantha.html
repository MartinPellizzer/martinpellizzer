Silene laeta f. micrantha

Silene laeta f. micrantha, commonly known as the pale silene or the dwarf silene, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright, herbaceous growth habit and features small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white in color. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or shaded habitats.

This subspecies is notable for its compact size and delicate floral structure, making it a distinctive member of its genus.
