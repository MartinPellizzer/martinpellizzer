Stellaria eriantha

Stellaria eriantha, commonly known as the starwort, is a small annual herbaceous plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pale pink. The plant has slender stems and opposite, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, along roadsides, and in wetlands.

Stellaria eriantha is known for its ability to spread through seeds and is considered a common weed in many regions.
