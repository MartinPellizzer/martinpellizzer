Senecillis veitchiana

Senecillis veitchiana, commonly known as the Veitch's senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and white. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.

Senecillis veitchiana prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.
