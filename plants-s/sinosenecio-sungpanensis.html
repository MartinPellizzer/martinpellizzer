Sinosenecio sungpanensis

Sinosenecio sungpanensis, commonly known as the Sungpan Senecio, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically features slender stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in dense clusters, giving it a delicate appearance.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.
