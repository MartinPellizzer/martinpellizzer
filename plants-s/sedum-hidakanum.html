Sedum hidakanum

Sedum hidakanum, commonly known as the Japanese stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Japan.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a tight rosette pattern. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for landscaping in temperate climates.
