Stellaria biflora

Stellaria biflora, commonly known as the two-flowered starwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features slender, branching stems that bear small, star-shaped white flowers, typically arranged in clusters. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Stellaria biflora is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in early successional habitats.
