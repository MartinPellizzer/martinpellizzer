Sonchus welwitschii

Sonchus welwitschii, commonly known as the African sowthistle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and lobed leaves. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in grasslands and along roadsides. It has a creeping growth habit with a hairy stem and serrated leaves.

The flowers are yellow and bloom in the summer months, attracting various pollinators.
