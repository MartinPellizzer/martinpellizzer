Stelis fimbriata

Stelis fimbriata, commonly known as the fringed stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features slender, branching stems and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates. Its leaves are typically elongated and covered in a fine, hairy texture.

Stelis fimbriata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
