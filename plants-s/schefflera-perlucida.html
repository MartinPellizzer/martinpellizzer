Schefflera perlucida

Schefflera perlucida, commonly known as the translucent schefflera, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often veined with a lighter color, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in umbels, which are typically followed by round, fleshy fruits. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.

Schefflera perlucida is also valued for its ability to purify the air and enhance indoor environments.
