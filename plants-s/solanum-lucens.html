Solanum lucens

Solanum lucens, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other familiar plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant is characterized by its hairy, silver-green foliage and small, star-shaped flowers. It produces berries that are typically yellow or orange when ripe.

Solanum lucens is often found in disturbed soils and can be invasive in certain regions.
