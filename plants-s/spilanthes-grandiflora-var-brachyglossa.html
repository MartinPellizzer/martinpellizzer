Spilanthes grandiflora var. brachyglossa

Spilanthes grandiflora var. brachyglossa, commonly known as the narrow-lipped prickly lettuce, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its pungent, peppery scent.
