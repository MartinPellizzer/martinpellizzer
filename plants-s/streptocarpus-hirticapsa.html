Streptocarpus hirticapsa

Streptocarpus hirticapsa, commonly known as the hairy-capsule streptocarpus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered with fine, hairy texture, giving it its common name.
