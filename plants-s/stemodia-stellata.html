Stemodia stellata

Stemodia stellata, commonly known as starstemmed stemodia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its upright growth habit and small, narrow leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in spring. Its stems are often branched and covered with fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
