Sarcochilus tridentatus var. erectus

Sarcochilus tridentatus var. erectus, commonly known as the upright sword fern, is a terrestrial orchid native to New Zealand.

It features a short, creeping rhizome with dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are often stiff and upright. The plant produces pendulous, greenish-yellow flowers with three prominent lobes, typical of the Sarcochilus genus. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in coastal areas.

Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a distinctive and sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
