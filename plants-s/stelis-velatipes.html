Stelis velatipes

Stelis velatipes, commonly known as the velvet lip orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, velvety-textured flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its flowers are usually pale green to white with intricate patterns, and they bloom during the warmer months.

Stelis velatipes is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
