Stanhopea nigripes

Stanhopea nigripes, commonly known as the black-stemmed orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and purple. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic growth habit. Its flowers are often clustered in racemes and bloom during the dry season.

Stanhopea nigripes is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
