Scorzonera graminifolia var. intermedia

Scorzonera graminifolia var. intermedia, commonly known as the intermediate scorzonera, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features a robust root system and produces yellow flowers with a distinctive, elongated corolla. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are basal, lanceolate, and often covered with fine hairs.

Scorzonera graminifolia var. intermedia is noted for its ornamental value and has been cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens.
