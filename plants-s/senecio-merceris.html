Senecio merceris

Senecio merceris, commonly known as the Blue Echeveria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of blue-gray, fuzzy leaves that store water, adapting to arid environments. The plant produces showy yellow flowers in late summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, requiring minimal water once established.

Its compact size and striking appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
