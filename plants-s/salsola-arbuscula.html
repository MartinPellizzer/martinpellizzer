Salsola arbuscula

Salsola arbuscula, commonly known as Russian thistle or goatweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides. The plant has a bushy appearance with spiny leaves and produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its seeds are dispersed by wind, allowing it to spread rapidly in new areas.

Salsola arbuscula is adapted to survive in harsh conditions, making it a common invasive species in many regions.
