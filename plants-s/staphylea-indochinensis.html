Staphylea indochinensis

Staphylea indochinensis, commonly known as the Indochina Staphylea, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Staphyleaceae and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly serrated edge.

Staphylea indochinensis is adapted to humid, tropical climates and is often found in forest understories.
