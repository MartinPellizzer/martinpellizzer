Swertia deflexa

Swertia deflexa, commonly known as the bent-flower gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly serrated edge.

Swertia deflexa is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
