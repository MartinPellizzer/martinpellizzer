Solanum fraxinellum

Solanum fraxinellum, commonly known as the yellow nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is native to parts of Africa and is characterized by its yellowish flowers and distinctive, spiny fruit. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often pubescent, while the flowers are solitary and borne in the axils of the leaves.

The fruit is a globose berry that turns from green to yellow when ripe, containing several seeds.
