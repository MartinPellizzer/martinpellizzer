Stelis sphaerochila

Stelis sphaerochila, commonly known as the globe orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air.

The flowers bloom during the rainy season, adding to its ecological significance in tropical rainforests.
