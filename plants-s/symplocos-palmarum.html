Symplocos palmarum

Symplocos palmarum, commonly known as the palmate evergreen, is a small tree or shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its palmately lobed leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that are typically clustered in umbels. Its fruits are red, fleshy drupes that contain a single seed.

Symplocos palmarum is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and along stream banks.
