Senecio altus

Senecio altus, commonly known as the tall groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, preferring well-drained soils. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer, and its height can reach up to two meters.

Senecio altus is often used in landscaping due to its striking appearance and hardy nature.
