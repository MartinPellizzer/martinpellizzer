Selenipedium × ainsworthii var. robustum

Selenipedium × ainsworthii var. robustum, commonly known as the robust selenipedium, is a hybrid orchid species native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil, often found in cloud forests. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange with a distinctive greenish tinge, and it produces a unique, elongated fruit capsule.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
