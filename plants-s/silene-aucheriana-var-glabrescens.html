Silene aucheriana var. glabrescens

Silene aucheriana var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth silene, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its upright stems and clusters of pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny to partially shaded environments. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, while the variety 'glabrescens' is distinguished by its smoother foliage compared to other varieties.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
