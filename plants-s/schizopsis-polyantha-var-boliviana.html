Schizopsis polyantha var. boliviana

Schizopsis polyantha var. boliviana, commonly known as the Bolivian orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of Bolivia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that often display a vibrant mix of colors. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, thriving in cool and humid conditions. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs, which help protect it from harsh weather.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated for its beauty and unique floral structure.
