Syena

Syena, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Nymphaeaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom on long, erect stems. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that float on the surface of still water. Syena's flowers are typically pink or white, with a prominent central stigma and numerous stamens.

It is revered in many cultures for its symbolic and medicinal properties.
