Specklinia bacillaris

Specklinia bacillaris, commonly known as the speckled orchid, is a small, epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, speckled green leaves. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow with purple or red markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, loose soil.

Specklinia bacillaris is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
