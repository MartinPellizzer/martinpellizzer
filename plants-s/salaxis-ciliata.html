Salaxis ciliata

Salaxis ciliata, commonly known as the silky salaxis, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and delicate, hairy leaves. Its unique appearance and adaptability contribute to its popularity among horticulturists.
