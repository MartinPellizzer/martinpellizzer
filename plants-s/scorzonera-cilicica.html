Scorzonera cilicica

Scorzonera cilicica, commonly known as the Cilician scorzonera, is a perennial herb native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and leaf structures. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid soils, showing adaptability to harsh environmental conditions. Its leaves are usually basal, rough, and hairy, while its flowers are yellow with ray florets.

Scorzonera cilicica is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
