Swertia sarmentosa

Swertia sarmentosa, commonly known as Himalayan gentian, is a perennial herb native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its slender, creeping stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, blue or purple flowers during the summer months. It thrives in high-altitude environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

Swertia sarmentosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional medicinal uses in some regions.
