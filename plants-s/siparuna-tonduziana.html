Siparuna tonduziana

Siparuna tonduziana, commonly known as the pink flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking pink and white flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling flames. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate to elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green. It produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds.

Siparuna tonduziana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and is often used in landscaping in warm climates.
