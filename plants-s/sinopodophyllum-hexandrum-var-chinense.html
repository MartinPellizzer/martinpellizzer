Sinopodophyllum hexandrum var. chinense

Sinopodophyllum hexandrum var. chinense, commonly known as Chinese mayapple, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

The plant contains toxic compounds, particularly in its roots and berries, which require caution during handling or consumption.
