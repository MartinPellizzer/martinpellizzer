Silene rubra

Silene rubra, commonly known as red campion, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in summer, typically arranged in racemes. The plant has hairy, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in a variety of habitats, including grasslands, rocky soils, and disturbed areas.

Silene rubra is often found in open environments and is considered a hardy, adaptable species.
