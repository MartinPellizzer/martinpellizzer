Sebestena dillenii

Sebestena dillenii, commonly known as the African star apple, is a tropical plant native to Africa, particularly found in countries like Nigeria and Ghana.

It is a small tree or shrub that typically grows to a height of about 5 to 8 meters. The plant produces small, yellow or orange fruits that are edible and have a sweet, tangy flavor. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a rough texture, while its flowers are small and clustered in panicles.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and its fruit, which is used in local cuisines and traditional medicine.
