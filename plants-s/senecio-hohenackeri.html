Senecio hohenackeri

Senecio hohenackeri, commonly known as the Hohenacker's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming growth habit. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a composite head. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Due to its ornamental appeal, it is sometimes cultivated as a succulent in gardens.
