Stephania kwangsiensis

Stephania kwangsiensis, commonly known as the Guangxi stephania, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Menispermaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and palmate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically clustered in umbels. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Stephania kwangsiensis is valued for its ornamental appearance and ecological role in its native habitat.
