Stigmatodon multifoliatus

Stigmatodon multifoliatus, commonly known as the multileaf stigmatodon, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, lobed leaves that vary in shape and size depending on environmental conditions. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in inflorescences. Its botanical significance lies in its unique leaf morphology and its role in local ecosystems.
