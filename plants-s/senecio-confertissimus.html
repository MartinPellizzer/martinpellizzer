Senecio confertissimus

Senecio confertissimus, commonly known as the denseleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. It has a low, spreading growth habit and produces small, daisy-like blooms.

Senecio confertissimus is adapted to withstand drought conditions and is often used in rock gardens or as an ornamental plant.
