Solanum verbascifolium f. hebecarpum

Solanum verbascifolium f. hebecarpum, commonly known as the woolly nightshade, is a rare and distinctive variant of the nightshade family.

It is characterized by its hairy, woolly leaves that give it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer, followed by berries that range in color from green to red. It is native to southern Europe and thrives in rocky, well-drained soils.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in specialized botanical gardens.
