Scrophularia decora

Scrophularia decora, commonly known as the showy scrophularia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae, which includes many species with showy, often tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its flowers are usually pink to purple, with two lips that are characteristic of the genus.

Scrophularia decora is often found in association with other xerophytic plants, adapting to arid environments with its hairy leaves and deep root system.
