Spathium monostachyum

Spathium monostachyum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spathiphyllum, is a tropical plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves that grow in a rosette pattern, providing a striking visual appearance. The plant produces an inflorescence known as a spathe, which is typically green or white in color, encasing a cluster of small, inconspicuous flowers. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
