Selbyana suaveolens

Selbyana suaveolens, commonly known as the sweet-smelling orchid, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand and Malaysia, where it thrives in tropical rainforests. The plant is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. Its leaves are broad and glossy, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Selbyana suaveolens is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological role in supporting local pollinators.
