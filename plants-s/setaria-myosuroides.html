Setaria myosuroides

Setaria myosuroides, commonly known as yellow foxtail, is a grass species native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, cylindrical spikelets that are typically yellow to golden in color. The plant grows in a variety of habitats, including disturbed soils and agricultural fields. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, wind-pollinated flowers.

Setaria myosuroides is often considered a weed but is also used in some traditional medicinal practices.
