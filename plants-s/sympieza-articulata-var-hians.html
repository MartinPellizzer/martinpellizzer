Sympieza articulata var. hians

Sympieza articulata var. hians, commonly known as the Hairy Sympieza, is a species native to certain regions of South America.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and taxonomic studies.
