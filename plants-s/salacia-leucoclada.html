Salacia leucoclada

Salacia leucoclada, commonly known as the white-leaved salacia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Salaceae family and is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, often blooming during the rainy season. Its leaves are typically covered in a fine, whitish pubescence, which gives it its common name.

Salacia leucoclada is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forest understories.
