Schismatoglottis longispatha

Schismatoglottis longispatha, commonly known as the long-spurred schismatoglottis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant features broad, elliptical leaves with a smooth, waxy surface. Its flowers are distinctive, with long, slender spurs and showy, pale green to white petals.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to grow in shaded, humid environments.
