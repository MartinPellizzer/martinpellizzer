Sphaeranthus suaveolens var. abyssinicus

Sphaeranthus suaveolens var. abyssinicus, commonly known as the Abyssinian globe flower, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It is characterized by its spherical, fleshy flower heads that resemble small globes, typically ranging in color from yellow to orange. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky soils. Its leaves are usually narrow and succulent, adapted to conserve water.

This species is of interest in botany for its unique morphology and potential medicinal properties.
