Syntherisma adusta

Syntherisma adusta, commonly known as the twilight orchid, is a rare flowering plant native to the misty highlands of South America.

It is characterized by its striking violet and cream-colored petals that resemble the colors of dawn and dusk. The plant thrives in cool, humid environments and requires partial shade for optimal growth. Its unique blooming pattern occurs during the early morning and late evening, hence its common name.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its delicate, fragrant flowers.
