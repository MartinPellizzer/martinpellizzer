Stephanachne

Stephanachne, commonly known as the "false millet," is a genus of grasses belonging to the family Poaceae.

These plants are characterized by their compact, tufted growth habit and small, spikelet-based inflorescences. The leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth surface and parallel venation.

Stephanachne species are sometimes used in ecological restoration projects due to their ability to stabilize soil in wetland environments.
