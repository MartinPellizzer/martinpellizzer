Senecio muirii

Senecio muirii, commonly known as the Muir's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and ragworts. The plant is characterized by its upright stems and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.
