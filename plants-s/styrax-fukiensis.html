Styrax fukiensis

Styrax fukiensis, commonly known as the Japanese styrax, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Styracaceae and is characterized by its fragrant, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces glossy, ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn, adding to its ornamental value. Its bark is smooth and reddish-brown, and it is often used in traditional Japanese gardens.

Styrax fukiensis is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
