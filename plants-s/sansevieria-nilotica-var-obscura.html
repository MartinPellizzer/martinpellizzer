Sansevieria nilotica var. obscura

Sansevieria nilotica var. obscura, commonly known as the Black Sansevieria, is a unique variant of the Sansevieria genus native to Africa.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black, lance-shaped leaves that are often adorned with subtle green or yellow margins. This plant thrives in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it an ideal choice for indoor environments. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water, which is a common trait among succulents.

The plant is also noted for its air-purifying qualities, contributing to improved indoor air quality.
