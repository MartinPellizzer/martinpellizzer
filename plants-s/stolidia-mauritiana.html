Stolidia mauritiana

Stolidia mauritiana, commonly known as the Mauritius star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to the island of Mauritius.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in spring.
