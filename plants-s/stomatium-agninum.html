Stomatium agninum

Stomatium agninum, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to the subtropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage and delicate, fragrant blooms that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its unique leaf structure, which features a waxy coating that helps reduce water loss. Stomatium agninum typically grows in shaded, moist environments and requires well-drained soil to thrive.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and specialized horticultural collections.
