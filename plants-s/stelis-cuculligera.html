Stelis cuculligera

Stelis cuculligera, commonly known as the hooded orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, hood-like flower structure, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its flowers are usually pale green or white, with a distinctive hood-shaped lip.

Stelis cuculligera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouse environments.
