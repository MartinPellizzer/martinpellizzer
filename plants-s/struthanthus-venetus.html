Struthanthus venetus

Struthanthus venetus, commonly known as the silverleaf globemallow, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and showy yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments.
