Stipa pulcherrima f. pubiflora

Stipa pulcherrima f. pubiflora, commonly known as the silky feather grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, arching stems and dense clusters of fine, silky green leaves. The plant produces delicate, fluffy flower heads that are often pink or purple in color, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This cultivar is noted for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
