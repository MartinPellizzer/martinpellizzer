Sarracenia × umlauftiana

Sarracenia × umlauftiana, commonly known as the hybrid pitcher plant, is a fascinating carnivorous plant resulting from the cross between Sarracenia leucophylla and Sarracenia rubra.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems and vibrant, tubular red or orange-colored pitchers that secrete a sticky liquid to trap insects. The plant's leaves are modified into specialized trapping structures, with the inner surface coated in a waxy, slippery layer that prevents prey from escaping. Sarracenia × umlauftiana thrives in boggy, nutrient-poor soils and requires consistent moisture and acidic conditions.

It is a popular choice among carnivorous plant enthusiasts due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
