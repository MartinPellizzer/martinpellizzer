Solanum diffusum subsp. miozygum

Solanum diffusum subsp.

miozygum, commonly known as the creeping nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South America. It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and hairy stems.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by berries that change from green to red as they mature. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
