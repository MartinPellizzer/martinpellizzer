Serjania Platyptera

Brazilian cherry, scientifically known as Serjania platyptera, is a striking tropical plant native to South America. It is prized for its vibrant red flowers and ornamental value, making it a popular choice in horticulture. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal. Its unique foliage and seasonal blooms add visual interest to gardens and landscapes. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to soil and water conditions for optimal growth.
