Stemodia macrotricha

Stemodia macrotricha, commonly known as the hairy stemodia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems covered in dense, woolly hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale blue in color. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in shrubland habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
