Stipa pulchra

Stipa pulchra, commonly known as the purple grass or purple needlegrass, is a species of grass native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are often tinged with purple. The plant produces small, spikelike inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Stipa pulchra thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops.

Its ornamental value has led to its cultivation in gardens, where it adds a delicate, colorful touch to landscapes.
