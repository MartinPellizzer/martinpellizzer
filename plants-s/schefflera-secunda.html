Schefflera secunda

Schefflera secunda, commonly known as the silver schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like clusters of leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant has glossy, dark green foliage with a silvery underside, contributing to its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.

Schefflera secunda is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor landscaping.
