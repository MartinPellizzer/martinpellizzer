Solena longiflora

Solena longiflora, commonly known as the long-flowered solena, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in moist, sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and branched.

Solena longiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
