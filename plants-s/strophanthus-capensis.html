Strophanthus capensis

Strophanthus capensis, commonly known as the Cape arrowroot, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its striking orange-red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces cylindrical, yellowish fruits that contain numerous seeds. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, often with a waxy texture.

Strophanthus capensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
