Senecio lugens var. parryi

Senecio lugens var. parryi, commonly known as Parry's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its yellow, daisy-like flowers and linear, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. This species is often found in open, disturbed habitats such as gravelly slopes and dry grasslands.
