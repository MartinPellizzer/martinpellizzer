Selinum coloratum

Selinum coloratum, commonly known as golden thread, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Selinum coloratum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
