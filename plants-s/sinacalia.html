Sinacalia

Sinacalia, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to Africa.

These plants are characterized by their compact, bushy growth habit and showy, colorful flowers. Sinacalia species typically have thick, fleshy leaves that help them retain moisture in their native arid environments. The flowers are usually arranged in dense clusters and come in a variety of colors, including purple, pink, and white.

They are popular houseplants due to their ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
