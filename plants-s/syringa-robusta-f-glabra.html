Syringa robusta f. glabra

Syringa robusta f. glabra, commonly known as the smooth-flowered lilac, is a cultivar of the common lilac that features smooth, hairless stems and leaves.

It is characterized by its fragrant, clustered flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant is native to China and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This cultivar is often used in landscaping for its showy display and hardy nature.
