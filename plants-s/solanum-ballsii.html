Solanum ballsii

Solanum ballsii, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant produces berries that start green and mature to a dark blue or black color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Solanum ballsii is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
