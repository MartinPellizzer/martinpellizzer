Senecio dissimulans

Senecio dissimulans, commonly known as the African snowflake, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a low-growing, mat-forming structure with fleshy, gray-green leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces delicate, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
