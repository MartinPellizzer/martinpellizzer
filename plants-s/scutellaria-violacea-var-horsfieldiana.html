Scutellaria violacea var. horsfieldiana

Scutellaria violacea var. horsfieldiana, commonly known as the Horsfield's blue skullcap, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, violet-blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners interested in native plants.
