Sedum hengduanense

Sedum hengduanense, commonly known as the Hengduan Mountain stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and small, clustered leaves that are typically green or reddish in color. The plant produces star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange, which bloom in late summer. It is adapted to rocky and well-drained soils, making it a hardy species suitable for rock gardens.

Sedum hengduanense is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in arid conditions.
