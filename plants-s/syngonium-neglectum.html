Syngonium neglectum

Syngonium neglectum, commonly known as arrowhead vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves that vary in color from bright green to variegated patterns of white and pink. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments and prefers indirect sunlight. Its rapid growth habit makes it popular as a houseplant or for outdoor landscaping.

Syngonium neglectum is also valued for its ability to purify indoor air and its ornamental appeal.
