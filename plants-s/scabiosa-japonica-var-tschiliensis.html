Scabiosa japonica var. tschiliensis

Scabiosa japonica var. tschiliensis, commonly known as the Chinese spring gentian, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of blue to purple flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
