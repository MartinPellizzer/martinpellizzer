Solanum shirleyanum

Solanum shirleyanum, commonly known as Shirley's nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and peppers. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its berries are typically blue or black when mature and contain toxic compounds.

It is often found in moist, shady environments and has been used in traditional medicine.
