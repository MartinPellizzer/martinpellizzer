Senecio baumii

Senecio baumii, commonly known as the African snowflake, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its rosette growth habit. The plant features narrow, silver-blue leaves and produces delicate, star-shaped yellow flowers. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soil.

Senecio baumii is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
