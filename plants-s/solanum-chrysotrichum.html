Solanum chrysotrichum

Solanum chrysotrichum, commonly known as the golden hairleaf nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South America.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its hairy, yellow-green foliage. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that develop into red, fleshy berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
