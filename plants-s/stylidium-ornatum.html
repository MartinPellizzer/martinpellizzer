Stylidium ornatum

Stylidium ornatum, commonly known as the ornate sun-orchid, is a small, herbaceous plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its unique, spurred flowers that are often brightly colored. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is found in arid or semi-arid regions. Its flowering period usually occurs during the warmer months, and it relies on specific pollinators for reproduction.

Stylidium ornatum is also notable for its ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
