Stellaria caroliniana

Stellaria caroliniana, commonly known as Carolina starweed, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the eastern United States.

It features small, white star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring, giving it its common name. The plant has slender, branching stems and opposite leaves that are typically oval to lance-shaped. It thrives in moist, sandy soils and is often found in open woodlands and along streambanks.

Botanically, it is characterized by its delicate appearance and its role as a food source for various insect species.
