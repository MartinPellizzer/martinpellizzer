Scutellaria pekinensis var. grandiflora

Scutellaria pekinensis var. grandiflora, commonly known as the Peking skullcap, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features square stems and opposite leaves that are often hairy. Its showy blue to purple flowers bloom in summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

It is valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
