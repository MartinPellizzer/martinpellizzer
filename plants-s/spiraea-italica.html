Spiraea italica

Spiraea italica, commonly known as Italian spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Europe.

It features upright, multi-branched stems and is characterized by its clusters of small, white to pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are typically oval-shaped and have a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

This plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
