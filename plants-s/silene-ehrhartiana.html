Silene ehrhartiana

Silene ehrhartiana, commonly known as the Ehrhart's catchfly, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Europe and is characterized by its delicate, pinkish-purple flowers that bloom in late summer.
