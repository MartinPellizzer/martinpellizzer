Saribus chinensis

Saribus chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese bamboo palm, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and pinnate leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Saribus chinensis is also valued for its use in traditional medicine and as a source of fiber.
