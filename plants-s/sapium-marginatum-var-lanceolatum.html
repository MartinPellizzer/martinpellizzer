Sapium marginatum var. lanceolatum

Sapium marginatum var. lanceolatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf Chinese lantern plant, is a species of flowering plant in the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiny, orange-red fruits that resemble small lanterns, which are often used in ornamental gardening. Native to Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, well-drained soils.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and landscapes.
