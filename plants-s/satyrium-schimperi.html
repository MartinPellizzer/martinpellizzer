Satyrium schimperi

Satyrium schimperi, commonly known as the Ethiopian orchid, is a flowering plant native to the highlands of Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous environments, adapting to the harsh conditions of its native habitat. Its flowers have a complex structure with specialized pollination mechanisms.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
