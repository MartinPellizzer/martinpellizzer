Senecio remyanus f. eradiatus

Senecio remyanus f. eradiatus, commonly known as the Eradiatus Senecio, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It is native to southern Africa and thrives in well-drained soils.

This succulent plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
