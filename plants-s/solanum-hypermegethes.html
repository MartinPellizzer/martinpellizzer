Solanum hypermegethes

Solanum hypermegethes, commonly known as the giant nightshade, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is native to parts of Central and South America, where it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant is characterized by its large, showy flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. Its leaves are broad and ovate, and the fruit is a fleshy, berry-like structure that can be toxic if consumed.

Solanum hypermegethes is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
