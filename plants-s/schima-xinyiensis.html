Schima xinyiensis

Schima xinyiensis, commonly known as the Chinese schima, is a deciduous evergreen shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces small, bell-shaped white flowers in clusters, which are followed by bright red berries that are an important food source for birds. Schima xinyiensis is often found in moist, acidic soils and is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological benefits.

It is also used in traditional Chinese medicine for its various therapeutic properties.
