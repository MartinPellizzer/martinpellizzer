Senecio decorticans

Senecio decorticans, commonly known as the African daisy or rock daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems and dense clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often found in rocky or arid environments. Its leaves are typically blue-gray in color, and it produces showy, daisy-like blooms during the summer months.

Senecio decorticans is well adapted to dry conditions and is often used in rock gardens due to its ornamental value.
