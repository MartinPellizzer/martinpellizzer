Scrophularioides arborea

Scrophularioides arborea, commonly known as the tree scrophularia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Scrophulariaceae.

It is characterized by its woody stems and showy, tubular flowers that typically range in color from pink to purple. The plant is native to certain regions of southern Africa, where it thrives in woodland and rocky habitats. Its leaves are usually opposite and lance-shaped, with a rough texture.

Scrophularioides arborea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
