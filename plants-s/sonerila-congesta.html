Sonerila congesta

Sonerila congesta, commonly known as the clustered sonerila, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its dense clusters of small, showy flowers. The plant typically grows as an erect shrub with opposite, ovate leaves that are often gland-dotted. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Sonerila congesta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
