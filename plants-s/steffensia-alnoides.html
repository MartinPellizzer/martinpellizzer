Steffensia alnoides

Steffensia alnoides, commonly known as the alderleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and has a shrub-like structure with woody stems. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often resemble those of alder trees, hence its common name.

Steffensia alnoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
