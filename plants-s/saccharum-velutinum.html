Saccharum velutinum

Saccharum velutinum, commonly known as velvet grass, is a tall, perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its dense, fibrous foliage and smooth, silky texture, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in wetland areas and can reach heights of up to 2 meters. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine and as a source of fiber.

Saccharum velutinum is also valued for its ornamental appeal in landscaping and garden design.
