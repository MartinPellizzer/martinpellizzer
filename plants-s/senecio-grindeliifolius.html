Senecio grindeliifolius

Senecio grindeliifolius, commonly known as the woolly senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It thrives in full sun and has a compact growth habit.

Due to its unique appearance, it is occasionally cultivated as an ornamental plant.
