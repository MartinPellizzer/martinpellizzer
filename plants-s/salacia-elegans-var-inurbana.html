Salacia elegans var. inurbana

Salacia elegans var. inurbana, commonly known as the urban salacia, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Salaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and showy, yellow-orange flowers. The leaves are typically alternate, ovate, and have a smooth texture. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in urban green spaces due to its adaptability.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens and parks.
