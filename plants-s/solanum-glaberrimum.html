Solanum glaberrimum

Solanum glaberrimum, commonly known as the woolly nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions. The plant features small, greenish-white flowers and produces berries that turn from green to red as they mature. Its leaves are usually covered in a dense layer of fine hairs, giving it a woolly appearance.

Solanum glaberrimum is part of the nightshade family, which includes many plants with toxic properties.
