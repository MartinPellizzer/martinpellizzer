Sesleria argentea subsp. cylindrica

Sesleria argentea subsp.

cylindrica, commonly known as the silver tufted grass, is a low-growing perennial grass native to southern Africa. It features narrow, silver-tipped leaves that give it a distinctive, ornamental appearance.

The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are typically pale green to pinkish in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact size and striking foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
