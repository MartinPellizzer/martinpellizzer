Sinobambusa sichuanensis

Sinobambusa sichuanensis, commonly known as the Sichuan bamboo, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to China, particularly found in the Sichuan province, where it thrives in mountainous regions. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are long and narrow, typically with a bluish-green hue, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers.

This bamboo is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
