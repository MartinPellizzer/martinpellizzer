Symbegonia Fulvovillosa

Symbegonia fulvovillosa is a unique flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of South America. It is known for its striking orange-red bracts that resemble flowers, though they are actually modified leaves. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture to flourish. Its velvety texture and vibrant color make it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Due to its rarity and specific care requirements, it is often sought after by plant enthusiasts.
