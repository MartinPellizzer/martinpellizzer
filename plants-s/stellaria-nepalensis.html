Stellaria nepalensis

Stellaria nepalensis, commonly known as Himalayan starwort, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its star-shaped flowers and slender stems. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh climates. Its small, white or pink flowers bloom in the summer months, attracting various pollinators.

Stellaria nepalensis is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
