Schefflera albidobracteata

Schefflera albidobracteata, commonly known as the white bract schefflera, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that are typically green with a slightly glossy surface. The plant produces small, white bracts that surround its tiny yellow flowers, giving it a unique appearance. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.

Its growth habit includes a short trunk with multiple branching stems, making it an attractive addition to indoor or outdoor landscapes.
