Sonchus arvensis var. angustifolius

Sonchus arvensis var. angustifolius, commonly known as narrow-leaved sowthistle, is an annual herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in composite inflorescences, which are typical of the dandelion family. It is commonly found in disturbed soils and agricultural fields, often considered a weed.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a widespread species in temperate regions.
