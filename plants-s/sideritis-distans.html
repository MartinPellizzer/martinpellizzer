Sideritis distans

Sideritis distans, commonly known as the Greek oregano or mountain thyme, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its small, tubular flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom in summer.

Sideritis distans is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
