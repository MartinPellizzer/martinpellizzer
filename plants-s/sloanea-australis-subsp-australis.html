Sloanea australis subsp. australis

Sloanea australis subsp.

australis, commonly known as the Australian sloanea, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia. It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green in color.

The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, often blooming in spring. Its botanical features include a woody stem with smooth bark and a preference for well-drained soils in sunny to partially shaded environments.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in coastal regions.
