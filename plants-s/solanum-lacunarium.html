Solanum lacunarium

Solanum lacunarium, commonly known as the lacunaria nightshade, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale green, often with a slight purple tint. Its fruit is a globose berry that turns from green to yellow or red when mature.

The leaves are simple, ovate, and have a rough texture with prominent veins.
