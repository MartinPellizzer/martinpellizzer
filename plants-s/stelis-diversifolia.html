Stelis diversifolia

Stelis diversifolia, commonly known as the variegated orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in humid, tropical environments. The plant features slender, upright stems and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a distinctive variegation pattern.

Stelis diversifolia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
