Stanhopea deltoidea

Stanhopea deltoidea, commonly known as the delta orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. The plant has a pseudobulb structure and produces long, arching inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Stanhopea deltoidea is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
