Senecio mojandensis

Senecio mojandensis, commonly known as the Mojanda groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are small and yellow.

It plays a role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators.
