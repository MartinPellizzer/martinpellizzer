Sideritis hirta

Sideritis hirta, commonly known as woolly ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its hairy, gray-green leaves and small, pale blue or violet flowers. It thrives in rocky or dry soils and is often found in mountainous areas.

Sideritis hirta is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
