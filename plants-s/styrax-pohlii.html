Styrax pohlii

Styrax pohlii, commonly known as the Chinese snowbell, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring, often before the leaves emerge. The plant has opposite, elliptical leaves that turn yellow in the fall. Its fragrant flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Styrax pohlii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and adaptability to various soil types.
