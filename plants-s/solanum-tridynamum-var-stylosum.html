Solanum tridynamum var. stylosum

Solanum tridynamum var. stylosum, commonly known as the slender nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 meter, featuring simple, alternate leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are yellow with five petals and are followed by small, round berries that mature to a dark blue color.

This species is often found in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils.
