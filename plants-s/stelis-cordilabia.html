Stelis cordilabia

Stelis cordilabia, commonly known as the heart-leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, heart-shaped leaves. The plant typically produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often arranged in a raceme. Stelis cordilabia thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation.
