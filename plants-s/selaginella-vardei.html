Selaginella vardei

Selaginella vardei, commonly known as the Varde's spikemoss, is a vascular plant belonging to the family Selaginellaceae.

It is a small, evergreen plant that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features a short, upright stem with whorled leaves that are often spirally arranged. Its reproductive structures include microsporangia and megasporangia, which are borne on specialized leaves called sporophylls.
