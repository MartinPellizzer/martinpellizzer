Stellaria neogaea

Stellaria neogaea, commonly known as the New Zealand starwort, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to New Zealand.

It features small, white or pale blue flowers that are star-shaped, giving it its common name. The plant has slender, branching stems and produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks.

Stellaria neogaea is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family, which includes other flowering plants with similar morphological characteristics.
