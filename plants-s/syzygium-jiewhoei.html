Syzygium jiewhoei

The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The fruit is a small, fleshy drupe that turns from green to yellow when ripe, and it is often used in local cuisines for its tangy flavor.
