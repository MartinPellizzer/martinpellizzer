Schistogyne pentaseta

Schistogyne pentaseta, commonly known as the purple sandparsley, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces delicate, pale purple or white flowers that bloom in spring. Schistogyne pentaseta typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.

It is often found in coastal areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
