Sponia orientalis var. commersonii

Sponia orientalis var. commersonii, commonly known as the Commerson's sponia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to saline environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are small and yellow.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing sandy soils.
