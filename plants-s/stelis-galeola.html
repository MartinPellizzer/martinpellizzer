Stelis galeola

Stelis galeola, commonly known as the pale orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in humid tropical environments. The plant features slender, wiry stems and produces small, pale green or white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are usually elongated and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Stelis galeola is valued for its unique appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
