Scleria reticularis var. obscura

Scleria reticularis var. obscura, commonly known as the dark nettle sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dark green, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with pale green to brownish flowers. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it an important component of wetland ecosystems.
