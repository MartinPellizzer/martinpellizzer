Scleria microcarpa var. latifolia

Scleria microcarpa var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved arrowhead, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically broad and glossy, often emerging from a rhizomatous root system. The plant produces small, white to pale blue flowers in dense clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for water gardens and bog landscapes.
