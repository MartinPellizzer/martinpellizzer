Silene czerei

Silene czerei, commonly known as the alpine campion, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or alpine environments. The plant produces showy pink or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in a fine, white pubescence.

Silene czerei is adapted to cold climates and is often found at high altitudes where few other plants can survive.
