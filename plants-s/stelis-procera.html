Stelis procera

Stelis procera, commonly known as the tall orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged along the stem.

Stelis procera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
