Sorbus karpatii

Sorbus karpatii, commonly known as the Carpathian rowan, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces bright red berries that are a food source for various birds and mammals. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas with well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in cooler regions.
