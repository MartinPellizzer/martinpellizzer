Salsola Praecox

Salsola praecox, commonly known as Russian thistle or goatweed, is a hardy annual plant native to Central Asia. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides. Its distinctive spiny leaves and fluffy seed heads make it easily recognizable. Despite its invasive tendencies in many regions, it plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and providing forage for wildlife. This resilient plant has adapted to survive in some of the harshest conditions on earth.
