Solanum inopinum

Solanum inopinum, commonly known as the Australian nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in winter. The plant produces berries that change from green to yellowish-red as they mature. It is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Solanum inopinum contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
