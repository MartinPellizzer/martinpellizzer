Stellaria bertolae

Stellaria bertolae, commonly known as the Sierra starflower, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, star-shaped white or pale blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Stellaria bertolae is often found in rocky soils and open habitats, such as meadows and slopes.
