Scrophularia muliensis

Scrophularia muliensis, commonly known as the Yunnan scrophularia, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
