Selinum Rhodopetalum

Selinum rhodopetalum is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of southeastern Europe. It is known for its vibrant purple and pink flowers that bloom in late spring. This species thrives in rocky soils and harsh climates, making it a resilient addition to alpine ecosystems. Its unique appearance has made it a subject of interest for botanists and gardeners alike. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated and often overlooked in the wild.
