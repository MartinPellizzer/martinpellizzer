Solidago doronicum

Solidago doronicum, commonly known as the dwarf goldenrod, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes and bogs. The plant features upright, slender stems that bear clusters of small, bright yellow flowers. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

Solidago doronicum plays an important role in supporting pollinators and maintaining wetland ecosystems.
