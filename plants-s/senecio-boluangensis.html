Senecio boluangensis

Senecio boluangensis, commonly known as the Boluang's groundsels, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are usually narrow and hairy, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

It is often used in traditional medicine and is also valued for its ornamental qualities.
