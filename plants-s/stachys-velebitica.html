Stachys velebitica

Stachys velebitica, commonly known as the Velebit betony, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, pale blue to purple flowers arranged in whorls along the stem. It thrives in rocky and calcareous soils, typically in mountainous regions.

Stachys velebitica is often found in open habitats such as meadows and forest edges.
