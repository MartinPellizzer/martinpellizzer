Streptocarpus boinensis

Streptocarpus boinensis, commonly known as the Bornean streptocarpus, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that typically bloom in shades of violet or blue. The plant features succulent, fleshy leaves that help it conserve moisture in its humid, shaded habitat. Streptocarpus boinensis is often grown as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
