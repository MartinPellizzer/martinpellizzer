Sondaria

Sondaria, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Sondaria is a member of the Malvaceae family, which includes cotton and hibiscus.

It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in disturbed soils.
