Spathiphyllum cannifolium

Spathiphyllum cannifolium, commonly known as the cannifoliate peace lily, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, waxy leaves that are often dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces elegant white spathes that enclose small, greenish-white flowers. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular houseplant.

Its ability to purify indoor air and its low maintenance requirements contribute to its widespread cultivation.
