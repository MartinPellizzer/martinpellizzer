Strobilanthes malabarica

Strobilanthes malabarica, commonly known as the Malabar starflower, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of India and Sri Lanka.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and shrublands, where it thrives in partial to full shade. The species is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and botanical collections.
