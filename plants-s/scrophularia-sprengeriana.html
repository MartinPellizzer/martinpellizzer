Scrophularia sprengeriana

Scrophularia sprengeriana, commonly known as the Sprenger's figwort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae, which includes many species with medicinal and ornamental value. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments.
