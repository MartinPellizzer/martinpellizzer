Satyrium elatum

Satyrium elatum, commonly known as the tall orchid, is a species of orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant produces a single, elongated flower spike that can reach heights of up to two meters. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, often arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Satyrium elatum thrives in humid, tropical environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
