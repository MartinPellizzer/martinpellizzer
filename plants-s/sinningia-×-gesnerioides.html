Sinningia × gesnerioides

Sinningia × gesnerioides, commonly known as the flamingo flower, is a hybrid plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the Andes Mountains of South America and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. Its foliage consists of broad, oval-shaped leaves with a waxy texture, and it thrives in bright, indirect light.

Sinningia × gesnerioides is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
