Stelis obtecta

Stelis obtecta, commonly known as the hooded orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, humid environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single or few erect flowers. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive hood-like structure over the lip.

Stelis obtecta is prized for its elegant appearance and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
