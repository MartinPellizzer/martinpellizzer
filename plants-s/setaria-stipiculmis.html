Setaria stipiculmis

Setaria stipiculmis, commonly known as slender false panicgrass, is a grass species native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions. It has a fibrous root system and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Botanically, it is classified within the Poaceae family, which includes many other grass species.
