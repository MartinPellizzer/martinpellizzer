Stenospermum ericifolium

Stenospermum ericifolium, commonly known as the African snowball or white thorn, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Africa.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has compound leaves with stiff, needle-like leaflets, and its bark is often rough and fissured. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savannas and woodland areas.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in some cultures.
