Senecio perezioides

Senecio perezioides, commonly known as the African snowflake, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rosette growth habit and pale blue to white flowers. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically arranged in a dense, star-like cluster. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Senecio perezioides is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
