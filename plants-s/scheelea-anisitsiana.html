Scheelea anisitsiana

Scheelea anisitsiana, commonly known as the Anisitsi palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. It produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in panicles, followed by bright red, berry-like fruits.

Scheelea anisitsiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to grow in well-drained soils with ample sunlight.
