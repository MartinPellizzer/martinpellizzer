Stelis crassilabia

Stelis crassilabia, commonly known as the broad-lipped stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its flowering stems. Its flowers are typically white or pale yellow, with a distinctive broad lip that gives it its common name.

Stelis crassilabia thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.
