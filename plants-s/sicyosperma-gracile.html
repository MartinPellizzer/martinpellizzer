Sicyosperma gracile

Sicyosperma gracile, commonly known as the slender bottle gourd, is a climbing vine native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes other gourds and cucurbits. The plant produces elongated, bottle-shaped fruits that are typically green when mature. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

Sicyosperma gracile is valued for its edible fruits and is cultivated for both culinary and ornamental purposes.
