Solanum kwebense var. chondropetalum

Solanum kwebense var. chondropetalum, commonly known as the Kwebense nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of Central Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale purple in color. Its leaves are ovate with a slightly hairy surface, and it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates.

The fruit is a small, fleshy berry that contains seeds, though the plant is not widely cultivated for agricultural purposes.
