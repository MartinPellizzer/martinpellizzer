Stenostephanus laxus

Stenostephanus laxus, commonly known as the narrow-stephanus grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open grasslands and rocky outcrops, preferring well-drained soils. The plant features slender, upright stems that bear narrow, grass-like leaves with a bluish-green hue. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, often appearing in late spring to early summer.

Stenostephanus laxus is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in arid conditions.
