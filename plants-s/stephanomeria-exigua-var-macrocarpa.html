Stephanomeria exigua var. macrocarpa

Stephanomeria exigua var. macrocarpa, commonly known as the giant tansy or largeleaf tansy, is a flowering plant native to California.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, bracted flower heads.

This variety is distinguished by its larger leaves and more robust growth compared to the typical form of Stephanomeria exigua.
