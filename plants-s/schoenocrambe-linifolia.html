Schoenocrambe linifolia

Schoenocrambe linifolia, commonly known as slender leafed bladderwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, delicate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, this plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils. It is an annual herb that often thrives in wet meadows and along stream banks.

The plant's botanical features make it a unique and interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
