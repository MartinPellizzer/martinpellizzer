Scaevola Ramosissima

Scaevola ramosissima is a hardy shrub native to the coastal regions of southern Australia. It is known for its vibrant blue or purple flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it ideal for coastal gardens. Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for landscaping. This resilient species is also valued for its ability to stabilize sand dunes and prevent erosion.
