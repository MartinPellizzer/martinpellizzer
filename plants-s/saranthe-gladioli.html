Saranthe gladioli

Saranthe gladioli, commonly known as the sword lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, strap-like leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant typically blooms in late spring to early summer, producing vibrant white, yellow, or orange flowers. Saranthe gladioli prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Its botanical features make it both visually appealing and relatively easy to cultivate in suitable climates.
