Streptanthus major

Streptanthus major, commonly known as the showy streptanthus, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The species is characterized by its showy, yellow or orange flowers that bloom in late summer. It typically grows in rocky soils and open habitats, such as coastal bluffs and mountain slopes.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking floral display.
