Salix Delavayana Var. Medogensis

Salix delavayana var. medogensis is a rare and unique willow species native to the high altitudes of western China. This variant is known for its distinctive morphological features and ecological adaptations to its harsh environment. It thrives in cold, mountainous regions where few plants can survive. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species facing threats from habitat loss and climate change. Studying this plant offers valuable insights into plant resilience and conservation strategies in alpine ecosystems.
