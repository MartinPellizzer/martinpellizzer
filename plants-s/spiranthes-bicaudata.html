Spiranthes bicaudata

Spiranthes bicaudata, commonly known as the twocauda spire orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, spiral-shaped inflorescence. The plant typically grows in moist, acidic habitats such as bogs and wetlands. Its flowers are usually white or pale green, with a distinct two-lobed lip.

Spiranthes bicaudata is known for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions and is often found in association with other wetland flora.
