Stegnogramma diplazioides

Stegnogramma diplazioides, commonly known as the dwarf ironwood, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small shrub characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and woody stems that provide structural support. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, adding a delicate aesthetic to its appearance. Stegnogramma diplazioides is valued for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in shaded, moist environments.
