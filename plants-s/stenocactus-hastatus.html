Stenocactus hastatus

Stenocactus hastatus, commonly known as the spiked hedge cactus, is a member of the Cactaceae family native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a ribbed stem that can grow up to 30 centimeters in height. The plant features prominent, sharp spines that provide protection from herbivores. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, appearing at the top of the plant during the summer months.

Stenocactus hastatus is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.
