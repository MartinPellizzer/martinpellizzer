Strychnos leiosepala

Strychnos leiosepala, commonly known as the smooth-sepal strychnos, is a shrub native to parts of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Loganiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces small, red, fleshy fruits that contain seeds surrounded by a white, cottony aril. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and undergrowth.
