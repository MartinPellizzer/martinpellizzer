Stelis tippenhaueri

Stelis tippenhaueri, commonly known as the Tippenhauer's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
