Saccharum hookeri

Saccharum hookeri, commonly known as the Hooker's grass.

It is a perennial grass species native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its tall, erect stems and broad, blade-like leaves. It thrives in wetland environments and is often found near rivers and swampy areas.

Saccharum hookeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional landscaping and ecological restoration projects.
