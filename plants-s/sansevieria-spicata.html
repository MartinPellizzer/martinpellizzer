Sansevieria spicata

Sansevieria spicata, commonly known as the spineless snake plant, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with subtle yellowish or white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers on slender stalks, which are usually white or greenish in color. It is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Sansevieria spicata is popular as a houseplant due to its air-purifying properties and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor conditions.
