Spathiphyllum friedrichsthalii var. brevifolium

Spathiphyllum friedrichsthalii var. brevifolium, commonly known as the short-leaved peace lily, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its dark green, broad, and short leaves that are typically less than 30 cm in length. The plant produces elegant white spatulate flowers that emerge from a leaf-like bract. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Due to its unique foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
