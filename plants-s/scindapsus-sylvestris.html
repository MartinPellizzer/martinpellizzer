Scindapsus sylvestris

Scindapsus sylvestris, commonly known as the silver vine, is a tropical evergreen climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that often have a silvery or variegated pattern. The plant produces small, pale green flowers followed by red berries, though it is primarily grown for its ornamental foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Scindapsus sylvestris is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
