Sansevieria itumei

Sansevieria itumei, commonly known as the "Coral Fang" plant, is a species of snake plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with red or pink variegation along the edges. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers on slender stalks during the growing season. It is highly resilient, requiring minimal water and thriving in low-light conditions.

Its ability to purify indoor air makes it a popular choice for interior landscaping.
