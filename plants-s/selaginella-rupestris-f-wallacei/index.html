Selaginella Rupestris F. Wallacei

Selaginella rupestris f. wallacei is a unique and rare plant species known for its distinctive appearance and hardy nature. It belongs to the family Selaginellaceae and is often found in rocky or arid environments where it thrives in harsh conditions. This fern-like plant is prized by collectors for its ornamental value and ability to survive with minimal care. Its foliage displays a striking contrast of colors, making it a standout in any collection. Despite its resilience, it remains a sought-after specimen due to its limited distribution and ecological significance.
