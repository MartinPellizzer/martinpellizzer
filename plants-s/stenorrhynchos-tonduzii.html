Stenorrhynchos tonduzii

Stenorrhynchos tonduzii, commonly known as the Tonduzii orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of pale yellow to orange flowers with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
