Schouwia purpurea f. albiflora

Schouwia purpurea f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered schouwia, is a striking member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky hair. Its flowers, which are predominantly white with a hint of pink, bloom in clusters during the summer months.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
