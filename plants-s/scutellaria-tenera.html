Scutellaria tenera

Scutellaria tenera, commonly known as the slender skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the United States and parts of Mexico.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its small, tubular blue or violet flowers and opposite, narrow leaves. It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.

Scutellaria tenera is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
