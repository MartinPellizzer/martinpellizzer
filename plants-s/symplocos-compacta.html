Symplocos compacta

Symplocos compacta, commonly known as the Indian hawthorn, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features dense, compact growth with small, oval leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters, which are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It is well adapted to tropical and subtropical climates and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for hedges and landscape design.
