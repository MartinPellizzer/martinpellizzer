Sponia willdenowiana

Sponia willdenowiana, commonly known as the willdenow's sponia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as grasslands and riverbanks. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.
