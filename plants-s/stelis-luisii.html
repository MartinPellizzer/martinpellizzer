Stelis luisii

Stelis luisii, commonly known as the Luisii Orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, white or pale yellow blossoms that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. Stelis luisii thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
