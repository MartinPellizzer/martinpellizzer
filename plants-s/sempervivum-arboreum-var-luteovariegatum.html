Sempervivum arboreum var. luteovariegatum

Sempervivum arboreum var. luteovariegatum, commonly known as the Lemon Beauty hens and chicks, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features rosettes of fleshy, crinkled leaves that are typically green with yellow or cream variegation. The plant produces upright flowering stems with small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.

Its compact growth habit and striking foliage make it an attractive addition to any succulent collection.
