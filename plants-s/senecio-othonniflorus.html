Senecio othonniflorus

Senecio othonniflorus, commonly known as the Cape fritillary, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, gray-green leaves with a woolly texture, and its flowers are typically yellow with a central dark center. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Senecio othonniflorus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
