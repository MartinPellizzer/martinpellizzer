Sideritis hyssopifolia var. relegata

Sideritis hyssopifolia var. relegata, commonly known as lesser woundwort, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, slender stems covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, pale blue or purple flowers arranged in whorls along the stem. The leaves are lance-shaped, slightly hairy, and have a strong, minty aroma. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or limestone habitats.

It is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
