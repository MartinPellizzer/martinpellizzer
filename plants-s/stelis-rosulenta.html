Stelis rosulenta

Stelis rosulenta, commonly known as the Rosulate Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit, with leaves arranged in a tight, circular pattern. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, often with a slight blush of color. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Stelis rosulenta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
