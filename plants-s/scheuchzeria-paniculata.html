Scheuchzeria paniculata

Scheuchzeria paniculata, commonly known as the water sedge, is a rare aquatic plant native to the wetlands of northern Europe and North America.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous greenish flowers arranged in a panicle. The plant typically grows in shallow water or moist soil, often in areas with slow-moving water. Its rhizomes help stabilize the sediment, contributing to the ecological health of its habitat.
