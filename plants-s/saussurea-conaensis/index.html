Saussurea Conaensis

Saussurea conaensis is a rare and striking alpine plant native to the high mountains of western China. It is known for its tall, slender stems and large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. This species thrives in cold, rocky environments and is often found in remote, high-altitude regions. Due to its limited distribution and fragile habitat, it is considered a vulnerable species. Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
