Satyrium nyassense

Satyrium nyassense, commonly known as the Nyassa orchid, is a species of orchid native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature a combination of white, pink, and purple hues. The plant has a compact growth habit with a pseudobulb that stores water and nutrients. Its flowers are often clustered in a raceme, and it thrives in shaded, moist environments.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
