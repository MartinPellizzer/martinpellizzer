Sceptridium Dissectum

Sceptridium dissectum is a rare and delicate fern native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. Known for its intricate, finely divided fronds, this plant exhibits a unique and elegant structure that sets it apart from other fern species. It thrives in cool, shaded environments and requires high humidity to flourish. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its natural range. Its striking appearance makes it a prized addition to botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
