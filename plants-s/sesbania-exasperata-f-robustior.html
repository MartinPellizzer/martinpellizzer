Sesbania exasperata f. robustior

Sesbania exasperata f. robustior, commonly known as the robust sesbania, is a variant of the sesbania genus native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a fast-growing leguminous plant characterized by its large, trifoliate leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant is notable for its ability to fix nitrogen through symbiotic relationships with rhizobia bacteria, enhancing soil fertility. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in agroforestry and as a forage crop.

Its rapid growth and ecological benefits make it valuable in ecological restoration projects.
