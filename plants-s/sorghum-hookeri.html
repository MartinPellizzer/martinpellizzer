Sorghum hookeri

Sorghum hookeri, commonly known as the Hooker's sorghum, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tall, erect growth habit. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that are typically green or reddish in color. Its leaves are narrow and linear, with a prominent midrib.
