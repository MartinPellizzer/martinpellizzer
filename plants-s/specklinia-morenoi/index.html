Specklinia Morenoi

Moreno's specklinia, scientifically known as Specklinia morenoi, is a rare and captivating orchid native to the mountainous regions of Ecuador. This plant is prized for its striking, intricate foliage and delicate, fragrant blooms that appear in shades of white and pink. Found primarily in cloud forests, it thrives in humid, shaded environments with high rainfall. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this endangered species from habitat loss and over-collection.
