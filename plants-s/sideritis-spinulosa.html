Sideritis spinulosa

Sideritis spinulosa, commonly known as the prickly ironwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features slender, branched stems covered in small, spiny projections. Its small, tubular flowers are typically pink or purple and bloom in summer.

Sideritis spinulosa is often found in rocky or dry habitats and is valued for its medicinal properties.
