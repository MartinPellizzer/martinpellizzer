Sparaxis stellaris

Sparaxis stellaris, commonly known as the star lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that bear clusters of bright yellow, star-shaped flowers, which bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often overlooked due to their subtle appearance. Sparaxis stellaris thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its unique floral structure and vibrant color make it a standout addition to any botanical collection.
