Stelis micropetala

Stelis micropetala, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant grows as an epiphyte, often found on trees in tropical rainforests. Its leaves are narrow and arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem.

Stelis micropetala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
