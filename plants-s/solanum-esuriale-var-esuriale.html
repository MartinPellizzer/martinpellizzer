Solanum esuriale var. esuriale

Solanum esuriale var. esuriale, commonly known as the slender nightshade, is a species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. The plant features alternate, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small, star-shaped, and usually white or pale green, blooming in clusters.

The fruit is a globose berry that turns red when mature, containing several small seeds.
