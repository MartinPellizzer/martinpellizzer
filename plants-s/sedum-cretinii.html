Sedum cretinii

Sedum cretinii, commonly known as the Cretin's stonecrop, is a low-growing succulent native to the Mediterranean region.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal, Sedum cretinii is a popular choice for gardeners seeking easy-to-maintain plants.
