Stachys brantii

Stachys brantii, commonly known as Persian shield, is a flowering plant native to the Middle East and South Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from violet to white. The plant belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and ornamental plants. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a low-maintenance, decorative plant.

Its unique appearance and adaptability contribute to its popularity in both ornamental and ecological landscapes.
