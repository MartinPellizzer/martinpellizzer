Sedum yvesii

Sedum yvesii, commonly known as the Yves' stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, clustered leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces yellow flowers in late summer, which are arranged in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Sedum yvesii is drought-tolerant and requires minimal maintenance once established.
