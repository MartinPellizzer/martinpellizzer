Sansevieria arborescens

Sansevieria arborescens, commonly known as the snake plant tree or the mother-in-law's tongue, is a striking succulent native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are dark green with yellow edges, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers on long, slender stalks during the growing season. It is highly drought-tolerant and thrives in low light conditions, making it an ideal indoor plant.

Its ability to purify the air and its low maintenance requirements further enhance its popularity among plant enthusiasts.
