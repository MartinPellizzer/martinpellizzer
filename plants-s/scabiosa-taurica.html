Scabiosa taurica

Scabiosa taurica, commonly known as the Caucasian scabious, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or dry soils. The plant features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. Its foliage consists of narrow, hairy leaves that are often grayish-green in appearance.

Scabiosa taurica is valued in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
