Stenorrhynchos lanceolatum

Stenorrhynchos lanceolatum, commonly known as the narrow-lobed orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, brightly colored flowers that are often clustered in racemes. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat preferences, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
