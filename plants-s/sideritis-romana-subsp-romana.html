Sideritis romana subsp. romana

Sideritis romana subsp.

romana, commonly known as Roman catnip, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs.

The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are aromatic and have been traditionally used in herbal remedies.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or limestone habitats.
