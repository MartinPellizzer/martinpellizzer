Stapelia verrucosa var. robusta

Stapelia verrucosa var. robusta, commonly known as the rough-stemmed stapelia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems covered in irregular, waxy tubercles that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers with a strong, unpleasant odor, which attracts pollinators like flies. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique morphology and adaptability make it a popular choice for cactus and succulent enthusiasts.
