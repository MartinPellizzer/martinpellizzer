Spathodea fenzliana

Spathodea fenzliana, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery orange to red inflorescences that appear in clusters, making it a visually dramatic species. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered within long, tubular bracts. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a smooth surface with a slightly waxy texture.

Spathodea fenzliana is often cultivated as an ornamental tree due to its showy blooms and adaptability to various climates.
