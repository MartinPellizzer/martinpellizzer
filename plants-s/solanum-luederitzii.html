Solanum luederitzii

Solanum luederitzii, commonly known as the Luederitz's nightshade, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes potatoes, tomatoes, and tobacco. The plant is characterized by its small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to violet. Its berries are typically yellow or orange when ripe and contain toxic compounds.

Due to its toxicity, it is not commonly cultivated but is sometimes found in wild habitats.
