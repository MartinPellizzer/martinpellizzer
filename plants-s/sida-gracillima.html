Sida gracillima

Sida gracillima, commonly known as slender sida, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is a low-growing annual herb that typically reaches heights of up to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, and its leaves are ovate with a slightly hairy texture. Sida gracillima is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides, thriving in full sun.

It is valued in some regions for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine.
