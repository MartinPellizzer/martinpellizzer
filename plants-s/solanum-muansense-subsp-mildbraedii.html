Solanum muansense subsp. mildbraedii

Solanum muansense subsp.

mildbraedii, commonly known as the Muansense nightshade, is a member of the Solanaceae family. It is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

The plant typically features elliptical leaves with a smooth or slightly pubescent surface. Its flowers are usually white or pale blue, with five petals and stamens that are often hairy.

The fruit is a globose berry that turns red when mature, containing numerous small seeds.
