Syzygium pallens

Syzygium pallens, commonly known as the white apple or white Syzygium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including regions of India, Indonesia, and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and oval-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. Its flowers are small, white, and fragrant, typically appearing in clusters.

The fruit is a small, round, yellowish drupe that ripens in late summer.
