Sesleria ujhelyii

Sesleria ujhelyii, commonly known as the Hungarian feather grass, is a perennial grass species native to the Carpathian Basin.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and delicate, feathery inflorescences that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full shade. It is often found in woodland edges, meadows, and along streams.

Sesleria ujhelyii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
