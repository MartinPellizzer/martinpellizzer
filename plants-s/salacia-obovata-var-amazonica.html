Salacia obovata var. amazonica

Salacia obovata var. amazonica, commonly known as the Amazonian salacia, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its elliptical, leathery leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces small, red berries that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is adapted to nutrient-poor soils.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies of tropical flora.
