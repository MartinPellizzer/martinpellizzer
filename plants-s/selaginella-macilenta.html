Selaginella macilenta

Selaginella macilenta, commonly known as the slender spikemoss, is a species of spikemoss belonging to the family Selaginellaceae.

It is a small, evergreen plant that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in rocky or forested areas. The plant has a distinctive appearance with its delicate, thread-like leaves and erect, spore-bearing cones. Its leaves are usually a dark green color and are arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem.

Selaginella macilenta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
