Senecio × lulioi

Senecio × lulioi, commonly known as the hybrid groundsels, is a perennial flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Senecio × lulioi is known for its ability to thrive in well-drained soils and its tolerance to drought conditions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
