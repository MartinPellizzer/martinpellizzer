Sitanion × leckenbyi

Sitanion × leckenbyi, commonly known as the hybrid sitanion, is a naturally occurring hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Sitanion hirsutum and Sitanion longifolium.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and is native to the arid regions of southern Africa. The plant produces small, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators. Its succulent leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native environment.

Sitanion × leckenbyi is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.
