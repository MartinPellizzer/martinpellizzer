Senecio setulosus

Senecio setulosus, commonly known as the silky sandwort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial that thrives in rocky and sandy soils. The plant features slender, hairy stems and small, clustered yellow flowers. Its leaves are often covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and drought tolerance.
