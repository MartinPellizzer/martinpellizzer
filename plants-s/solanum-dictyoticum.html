Solanum dictyoticum

Solanum dictyoticum, commonly known as the laceleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, deeply lobed leaves that give it a lace-like appearance. The plant produces small, violet-colored flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its fruit is a smooth, globose berry that ripens to a dark purple color.

Like other members of the Solanaceae family, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
