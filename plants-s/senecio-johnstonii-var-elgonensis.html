Senecio johnstonii var. elgonensis

Senecio johnstonii var. elgonensis, commonly known as the Elgon Senecio, is a succulent shrub native to the high-altitude regions of East Africa.

It features dense, compact growth with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It is adapted to rocky and well-drained soils, thriving in full sun.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in arid and semi-arid environments.
