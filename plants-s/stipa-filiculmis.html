Stipa filiculmis

Stipa filiculmis, commonly known as the feather grass, is a perennial grass species native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and delicate, feathery plumes that give it a graceful appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and woodland edges. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with leaves that are often slightly hairy.

Stipa filiculmis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and restoration projects.
