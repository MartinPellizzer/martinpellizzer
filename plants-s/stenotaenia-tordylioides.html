Stenotaenia tordylioides

Stenotaenia tordylioides, commonly known as the woolly stenotaenia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and leaves covered in a dense layer of white, woolly hair. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in dry, rocky environments and is often found in grassland and shrubland habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
