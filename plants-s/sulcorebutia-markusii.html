Sulcorebutia markusii

Sulcorebutia markusii, commonly known as the Markusii Pincushion Cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of South America.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular shape and dense clusters of spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a vibrant touch to its otherwise desolate appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for xerophytic gardens.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
