Saxifraga oranensis

Saxifraga oranensis, commonly known as the Oran saxifrage, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or limestone soils, often in dry and sunny environments. The plant features basal rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its small, white or pinkish flowers bloom in early spring, adding a delicate touch to its habitat.

Saxifraga oranensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens or alpine settings.
