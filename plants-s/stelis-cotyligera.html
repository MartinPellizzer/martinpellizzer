Stelis cotyligera

Stelis cotyligera, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its flowers are small and usually white or pale green, arranged in a loose inflorescence.

Stelis cotyligera is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
