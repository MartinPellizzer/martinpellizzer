Schizoglossum pumilum

Schizoglossum pumilum, commonly known as the dwarf schizoglossum, is a small, annual herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows to a height of less than 10 centimeters, with slender, branching stems covered in fine, hairy hairs. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often arranged alternately along the stem.

Schizoglossum pumilum is adapted to rocky and well-drained soils, thriving in full sun conditions.
