Schoenoplectiella chuana

Schoenoplectiella chuana, commonly known as the Chuana bulrush, is a perennial wetland plant native to South America.

It typically grows in shallow water or moist soil, often found in marshes and along the edges of lakes and rivers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in the summer. Its rhizomes help stabilize soil and prevent erosion in wetland environments.

Schoenoplectiella chuana plays an important role in maintaining the ecological balance of its habitat.
