Smilax china var. yanagitae

Smilax china var. yanagitae, commonly known as the Japanese arrowroot or Japanese smilax, is a perennial vine native to East Asia.

It features a climbing habit with woody stems and is characterized by its compound leaves with spiny margins. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by bright red berries. It is often found in forest understories and thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Botanically, it is notable for its rhizomatous growth and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.
