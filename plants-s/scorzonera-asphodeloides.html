Scorzonera asphodeloides

Scorzonera asphodeloides, commonly known as the asphodel-like scorzonera, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features basal rosettes of narrow, gray-green leaves and produces tall, branched stems with yellow flowers in the summer. The plant is characterized by its fibrous root system, which is often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or dry garden environments.

Scorzonera asphodeloides is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in supporting pollinators.
