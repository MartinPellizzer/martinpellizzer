Stellera diffusa

Stellera diffusa, commonly known as starwort, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, woodland habitats. The plant features small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Stellera diffusa is often found in shaded areas and is valued for its ecological role in supporting pollinators.
