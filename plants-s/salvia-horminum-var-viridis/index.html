Salvia Horminum Var. Viridis

Salvia horminum var. viridis is a unique and striking plant known for its vibrant green foliage and delicate blue flowers. Native to the Mediterranean region, this variety thrives in sunny environments with well-drained soil. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and ability to attract pollinators. Its compact growth habit makes it ideal for rock gardens and containers. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to watering and pruning to maintain its health and vigor.
