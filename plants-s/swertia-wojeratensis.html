Swertia wojeratensis

Swertia wojeratensis, commonly known as the Himalayan gentian, is a perennial herb native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, thriving in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Swertia wojeratensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
