Succisa integrifolia

Succisa integrifolia, commonly known as the slender leaf everlasting, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, clustered yellow flowers. The plant is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is drought-tolerant and often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
