Stapletonia arunachalensis

Stapletonia arunachalensis, commonly known as the Arunachal Pradesh orchid, is a rare and endemic orchid species found in the eastern Himalayas.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate floral structure and unique morphological features. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, often on tree trunks or rocks. Its flowers are usually small, with vibrant colors that attract specific pollinators.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical research and conservation efforts.
