Sedum pluricaule var. yezoense

Sedum pluricaule var. yezoense, commonly known as the Yezo sedum, is a succulent plant native to Japan.

It features fleshy, upright stems that often branch out, forming a compact, shrub-like structure. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange during the summer months. It is adapted to rocky and sunny environments, showcasing drought tolerance and cold resistance.

Its leaves are typically thick and waxy, aiding in water retention and survival in harsh conditions.
