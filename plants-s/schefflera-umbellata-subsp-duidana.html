Schefflera umbellata subsp. duidana

Schefflera umbellata subsp.

duidana, commonly known as the Duidana umbrella tree, is a tropical plant native to Madagascar. It is characterized by its distinctive umbrella-like arrangement of leaves, which are typically large and glossy.

The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

This subspecies is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and ease of care.
