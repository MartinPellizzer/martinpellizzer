Sageraea reticulata

Sageraea reticulata, commonly known as the Chinese hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Sageraea reticulata is also valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its potential health benefits.
