Schefflera fengii

Schefflera fengii, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to southern China.

It features a dense, bushy growth habit with glossy, dark green, palmately compound leaves. The plant typically grows up to 3 meters tall, with a compact and upright form. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Schefflera fengii is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
