Solenospermum aquatile

Solenospermum aquatile, commonly known as the water solenospermum, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its fruits are fleshy berries that contain numerous seeds.

Solenospermum aquatile thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
