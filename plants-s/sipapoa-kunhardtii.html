Sipapoa kunhardtii

Sipapoa kunhardtii, commonly known as the Kunhardt's orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Sipapoa kunhardtii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a controlled environment.
