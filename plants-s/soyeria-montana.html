Soyeria montana

Soyeria montana, commonly known as mountain soyeria, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

Soyeria montana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate foliage and flowering habit.
