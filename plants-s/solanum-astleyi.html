Solanum astleyi

Solanum astleyi, commonly known as the Astley's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue in color. Its fruit is a dry, capsule-like structure that contains several seeds.

Solanum astleyi is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid environments.
