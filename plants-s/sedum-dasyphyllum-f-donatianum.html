Sedum dasyphyllum f. donatianum

Sedum dasyphyllum f. donatianum, commonly known as the Donatian's stonecrop, is a succulent plant characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and clustered leaves.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is native to arid and rocky environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It is well adapted to drought conditions, storing water in its thick leaves and stems.

This cultivar is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance requirements.
