Stellaria sabulosa

Stellaria sabulosa, commonly known as sandstar, is a low-growing annual plant native to sandy and coastal environments.

It features small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to August. The plant has slender, branched stems and small, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dunes, gravelly slopes, and disturbed areas.

Stellaria sabulosa is an important component of coastal ecosystems, providing habitat and forage for various insects and small animals.
