Sasamorpha uinuizoana

Sasamorpha uinuizoana, commonly known as the Madagascar star orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that display a vibrant mix of colors. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in mountainous regions with rich, well-drained soil. Its floral structure includes a complex arrangement of petals and sepals, with a distinctive labellum that aids in pollination by specific insect species.
