Senna organensis var. heterandra

Senna organensis var. heterandra, commonly known as the African senna, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its bipinnate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces pods that contain small, flat, dark brown seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savannas and woodland areas.

The botanical features of this species make it an important component of its native ecosystems.
