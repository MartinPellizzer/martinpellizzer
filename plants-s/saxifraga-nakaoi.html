Saxifraga nakaoi

Saxifraga nakaoi, commonly known as Nakao's saxifrage, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its rosette of fleshy, oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in rocky or alpine environments, preferring well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
