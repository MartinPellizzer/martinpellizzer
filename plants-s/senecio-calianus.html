Senecio calianus

Senecio calianus, commonly known as the blue ice plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, compact growth with fleshy, blue-gray leaves that store water, adapting to arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
