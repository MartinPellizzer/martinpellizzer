Sabicea glabrescens

Sabicea glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth sabicea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often found in forest understories. The plant features simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate to elliptical in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, often with a distinctive white or pale pink color.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
