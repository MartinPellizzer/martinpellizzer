Sedum obtusipetalum subsp. dandyanum

Sedum obtusipetalum subsp.

dandyanum, commonly known as the Dandyan sedum, is a succulent plant native to southern China. It features fleshy, upright stems and small, clustered leaves that are typically green to bluish-green in color.

The plant produces star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and as a decorative ground cover.
