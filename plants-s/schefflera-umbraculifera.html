Schefflera umbraculifera

Schefflera umbraculifera, commonly known as the Brazilian Schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like canopy of large, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant has compound leaves with multiple leaflets arranged in a whorled pattern, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries.

Schefflera umbraculifera is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
