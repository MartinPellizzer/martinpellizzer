Spiranthes elata var. ovata

Spiranthes elata var. ovata, commonly known as the showy lady's tresses, is a perennial orchid native to North America.

It is characterized by its distinctive spiraling arrangement of pale green to white flowers along a slender stem. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and wetlands. It has strap-like leaves and produces small, fragrant blossoms that bloom in late spring to early summer.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
