Sideritis virgata var. fontanesii

Sideritis virgata var. fontanesii, commonly known as the Alpine sainfoin, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in spikes. It thrives in rocky or dry habitats and is often found in alpine meadows.

Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.
