Scabiosa columbaria var. pyrenaica

Scabiosa columbaria var. pyrenaica, commonly known as the Pyrenean scabious, is a flowering plant native to the Pyrenees region.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of pink to purple flowers. The plant features basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the upper leaves are smaller and more upright. Its flowers are typically arranged in dense, spherical inflorescences, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to rock gardens and alpine landscapes.
