Senecio bergii

Senecio bergii, commonly known as the Berg's groundsel, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage is gray-green with hairy surfaces, and it produces small yellow flowers in spring.

Senecio bergii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
