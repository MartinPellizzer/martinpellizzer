Sedum polytrichoides var. setouchiense

Sedum polytrichoides var. setouchiense, commonly known as the Setouchi sedum, is a succulent plant native to Japan.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a powdery coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is a popular choice for gardeners seeking low-maintenance, drought-tolerant plants.
