Sarcomphalium

Sarcomphalium, commonly known as the common hawksbeard, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers that resemble a beak, hence the common name. The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. Sarcomphalium has a basal rosette of leaves and can reach heights of up to 60 cm.

It is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
