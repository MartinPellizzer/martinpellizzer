Scutellaria grossa

Scutellaria grossa, commonly known as broad-leaved skullcap, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture and distinctive blue to violet flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and streambanks.

Scutellaria grossa is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
