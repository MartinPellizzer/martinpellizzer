Stenocactus confusus

Stenocactus confusus, commonly known as the confused stenocactus, is a species of cactus native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are often covered in white or yellowish spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the rainy season, typically in winter. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
