Sinocalamus jianshuiensis

Sinocalamus jianshuiensis, commonly known as the JianShui bamboo, is a species native to China, specifically found in Yunnan Province.

It is a tall, slender grass-like plant that belongs to the grass family, Poaceae. The plant is characterized by its long, hollow stems and narrow, linear leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
