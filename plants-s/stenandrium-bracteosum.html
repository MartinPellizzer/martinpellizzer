Stenandrium bracteosum

Stenandrium bracteosum, commonly known as the velvetleaf stenandrium, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Stenandrium bracteosum is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in ecological studies and horticultural practices.
