Synotis cappa var. cappa

Synotis cappa var. cappa, commonly known as the Cape mountain cabbage, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are typically grayish-green in color. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in a dense inflorescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
