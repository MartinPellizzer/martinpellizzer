Sansevieria trifasciata

Sansevieria trifasciata, commonly known as the snake plant, is a hardy, evergreen perennial native to Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with distinct light and dark vertical stripes. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it an ideal houseplant. Its foliage is often used in interior design due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying properties.

Sansevieria trifasciata is also notable for its resilience, surviving in a wide range of environmental conditions.
