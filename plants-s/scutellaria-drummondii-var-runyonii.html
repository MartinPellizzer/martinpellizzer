Scutellaria drummondii var. runyonii

Scutellaria drummondii var. runyonii, commonly known as Drummond's blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the mint family (Lamiaceae) and is characterized by its small, tubular blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and has lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture. It is a perennial herb that produces a basal rosette of leaves and flowering stems.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ecologically important species in its native range.
