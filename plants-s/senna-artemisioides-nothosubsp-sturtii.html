Senna artemisioides nothosubsp. sturtii

Senna artemisioides nothosubsp.

sturtii, commonly known as Sturt's senna, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of Australia. It is characterized by its compound leaves with serrated margins and bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters.

The plant is adapted to withstand drought conditions, with deep root systems that allow it to access water from deeper soil layers. It plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soils and providing nectar for pollinators.

The nothosubspecies designation indicates it is a hybrid form between S. artemisioides and S. sturtii.
