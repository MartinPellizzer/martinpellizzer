Sedum donatianum

Sedum donatianum, commonly known as the Donatian stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves. It prefers full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long blooming period.
