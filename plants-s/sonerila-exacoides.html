Sonerila exacoides

Sonerila exacoides, commonly known as the broadleaf sonerila, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its opposite, ovate to elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white, arranged in clusters. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.
