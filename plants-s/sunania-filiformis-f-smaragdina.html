Sunania filiformis f. smaragdina

Sunania filiformis f. smaragdina, commonly known as the Emerald Sunania, is a rare and striking variant of the Sunania filiformis species.

It is characterized by its vibrant green foliage, which is more intense and lush compared to the typical form. The plant exhibits a compact growth habit with dense, curling leaves that give it a unique, ornamental appearance. It thrives in shaded, humid environments, making it a sought-after specimen for indoor cultivation.

Its botanical interest lies in its distinct coloration and rarity, making it a valuable addition to specialized plant collections.
