Saxifraga japonica variegata

Saxifraga japonica variegata, commonly known as the Japanese stonecrop, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Japan.

It features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with striking white and yellow variegation. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Its compact size and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
