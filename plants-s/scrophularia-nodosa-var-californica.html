Scrophularia nodosa var. californica

Scrophularia nodosa var. californica, commonly known as California figwort, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It features erect, hairy stems that can grow up to 1 meter in height, often branching near the base. The plant produces showy, two-lipped flowers in shades of purple or pink, typically clustered in whorls along the stem. Its leaves are opposite, ovate to lanceolate, and covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.
