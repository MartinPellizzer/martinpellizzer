Senecio hakeifolius var. vidalii

Senecio hakeifolius var. vidalii, commonly known as the Vidal's groundsels, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features upright, branched stems covered in fine, white hairs. The leaves are typically alternate, with a distinctive lobed or serrated margin. Its small, daisy-like yellow flowers bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators.

This plant prefers well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.
