Schomburgkia brysiana var. intermedia

Schomburgkia brysiana var. intermedia, commonly known as the Intermediate Schomburgkia, is a hybrid orchid species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, often fragrant flowers that come in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are broad and leathery, adapted to retain moisture in its humid environment.

The orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is cultivated by growers for its vibrant blooms and unique appearance.
