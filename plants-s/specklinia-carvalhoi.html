Specklinia carvalhoi

Specklinia carvalhoi, commonly known as the Carvalho's tillandsia, is a species of air plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped growth form. The plant produces striking, showy inflorescences with brightly colored bracts that attract pollinators. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires minimal soil, as it is an epiphytic species.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
