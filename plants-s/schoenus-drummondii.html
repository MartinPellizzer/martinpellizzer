Schoenus drummondii

Schoenus drummondii, commonly known as Drummond's bulrush, is a perennial grass-like plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in wetland environments such as marshes, lakeshores, and riverbanks. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like clusters of flowers. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Schoenus drummondii plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
