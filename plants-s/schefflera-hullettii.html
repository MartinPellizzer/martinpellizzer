Schefflera hullettii

Schefflera hullettii, commonly known as the Hullett's schefflera, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of leaves, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Schefflera hullettii is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
