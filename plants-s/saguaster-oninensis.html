Saguaster oninensis

Saguaster oninensis, commonly known as the Oninense Saguar, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
