Scutellaria orthocalyx

Scutellaria orthocalyx, commonly known as narrow-leaved skullcap, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers. It typically grows in moist, wooded areas and is often found in the understory of forests.

Scutellaria orthocalyx is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
