Stigmatodon plurifolius

Stigmatodon plurifolius, commonly known as the many-leaved stigmatodon, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, clustered growth habit and its distinctive, palmately lobed leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique foliage and ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
