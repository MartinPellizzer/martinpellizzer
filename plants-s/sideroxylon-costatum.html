Sideroxylon costatum

Sideroxylon costatum, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a tropical tree native to Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, hard wood and distinctive fluted trunk. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody, capsule-like fruits.

Sideroxylon costatum is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in harsh climates.
