Sophora prazeri subsp. mairei

Sophora prazeri subsp.

mairei, commonly known as the Maire Sophora, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China. It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is notable for its ability to fix nitrogen, which enriches the soil and supports ecological balance.

It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas with well-drained soils.
