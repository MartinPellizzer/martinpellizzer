Swertia phragmitiphylla

Swertia phragmitiphylla, commonly known as the reed leaf swertia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Himalayan regions of Nepal and India.

The plant produces small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer, typically arranged in raceme-like clusters.
