Schefflera clusietorum

Schefflera clusietorum, commonly known as the African nettle, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of leaves, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by black, spherical berries. Its leaves are typically leathery and have a rough texture, while its bark is smooth and grayish in color.

Schefflera clusietorum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
