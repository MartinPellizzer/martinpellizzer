Scleria spicata var. castanea

Scleria spicata var. castanea, commonly known as the redstem sedges, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related grass-like plants. The plant is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. Its inflorescence consists of spikelets arranged in a dense, spikelike cluster, typical of the sedge family.
