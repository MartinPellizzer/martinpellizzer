Schizochilus flexuosus

Schizochilus flexuosus, commonly known as the flexible schizochilus, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Schizochilus flexuosus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care in tropical gardens.
