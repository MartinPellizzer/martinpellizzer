Satyrium niloticum

Satyrium niloticum, commonly known as the Nile orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Africa.

It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive hooded labellum. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Satyrium niloticum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitats.
