Specklinia barbosana

Specklinia barbosana, commonly known as the velvet fern, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The fronds are typically small, leathery, and covered in a fine, velvety texture that gives the plant its common name. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
