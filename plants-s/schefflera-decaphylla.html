Schefflera decaphylla

Schefflera decaphylla, commonly known as the umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of leaves, which are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by bright red berries that are attractive to birds. Schefflera decaphylla thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant.

Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for both ornamental and horticultural purposes.
