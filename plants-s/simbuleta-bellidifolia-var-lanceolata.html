Simbuleta bellidifolia var. lanceolata

Simbuleta bellidifolia var. lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceleaf simbuleta, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs, giving them a soft texture. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate foliage and prolonged flowering period.
