Sansevieria zeylanica

Sansevieria zeylanica, commonly known as the upright snake plant, is a striking succulent native to Sri Lanka.

It features tall, upright leaves that are typically green with distinctive silver or grayish-white variegation. The plant is characterized by its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it highly adaptable. Its leaves are rigid and sword-shaped, often growing in a rosette pattern.

Sansevieria zeylanica is also noted for its air-purifying qualities, contributing to indoor air quality.
