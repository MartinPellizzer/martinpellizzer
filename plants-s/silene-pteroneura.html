Silene pteroneura

Silene pteroneura, commonly known as the winged catchfly, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, hairy stems and showy pink or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and thrives in open habitats such as grasslands and heaths. Silene pteroneura is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
