Stipa plumosa var. gracilis

Stipa plumosa var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to temperate regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery plumes that give it a distinctive, airy appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
