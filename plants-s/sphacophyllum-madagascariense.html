Sphacophyllum madagascariense

Sphacophyllum madagascariense, commonly known as the Madagascar spiky moss, is a unique member of the Sphacelobryaceae family.

It is a non-vascular plant characterized by its dense, spiky growth form and is typically found in moist, shaded environments. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species in Madagascar.
