Satyrium conopseum var. densiflorum

Satyrium conopseum var. densiflorum, commonly known as the dense-flowered satyrium, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, herbaceous growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in dense clusters. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a slightly hairy texture.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in tropical gardens.
