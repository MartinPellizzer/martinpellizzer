Sedum verticillatum var. nipponicum

Sedum verticillatum var. nipponicum, commonly known as Japanese stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Japan.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
