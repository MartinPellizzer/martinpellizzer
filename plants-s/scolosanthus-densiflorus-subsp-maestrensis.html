Scolosanthus densiflorus subsp. maestrensis

Scolosanthus densiflorus subsp.

maestrensis, commonly known as the Maestrense scolosanthus, is a member of the Iridaceae family. It is a perennial herb characterized by its dense, showy flower clusters that bloom in late spring.

The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils in arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while the flowers are usually blue or purple in color.

This subspecies is native to southern Africa, particularly in regions of Namibia and Angola.
