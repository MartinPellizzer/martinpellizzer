Senecio samnitum

Senecio samnitum, commonly known as the Sicilian groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small yellow flowers arranged in clusters.

Senecio samnitum is often found in mountainous regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
