Stelis tenuissima

Stelis tenuissima, commonly known as the slender stelis orchid, is a small epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant has narrow, strap-like leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It is prized for its compact size and ornamental value in botanical gardens.

Stelis tenuissima requires careful cultivation, with high humidity and indirect light for optimal growth.
