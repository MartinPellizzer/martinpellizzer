Swertia gentianifolia

Swertia gentianifolia, commonly known as the blue gentian, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas and parts of East Asia.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine regions, preferring moist, well-drained soils. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs.

Swertia gentianifolia is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
