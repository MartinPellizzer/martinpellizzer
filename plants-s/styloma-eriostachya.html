Styloma eriostachya

Styloma eriostachya, commonly known as the silky stalked grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves with a silvery sheen. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and rocky areas.

Styloma eriostachya is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local wildlife.
