Spilanthes urens f. ciliatifolia

Spilanthes urens f. ciliatifolia, commonly known as the fringed false mustards, is a variant of the common spilanthes plant.

It is a flowering plant native to various regions in the Americas, particularly in tropical and subtropical areas. The plant features small, yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, and its leaves are typically lance-shaped with finely toothed margins. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

The species is noted for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
