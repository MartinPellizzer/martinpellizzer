Senna Confinis

Senna confinis is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East. It belongs to the legume family and is known for its striking yellow flowers. This plant is often found in dry, arid environments where it thrives with minimal water. Its leaves are bipinnate, giving it a unique and delicate appearance. Senna confinis is also valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
