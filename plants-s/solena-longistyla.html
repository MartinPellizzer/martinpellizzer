Solena longistyla

Solena longistyla, commonly known as the slender spurge, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom from late spring to early autumn. Solena longistyla is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
