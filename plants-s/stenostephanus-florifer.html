Stenostephanus florifer

Stenostephanus florifer, commonly known as the showy stephanus, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences that consist of numerous small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub, with woody stems and narrow, linear leaves. Its flowers are usually brightly colored, ranging from yellow to orange, and bloom during the warmer months.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its visual impact.
