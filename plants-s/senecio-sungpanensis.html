Senecio sungpanensis

Senecio sungpanensis, commonly known as the Sungpan Senecio, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. It thrives in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
