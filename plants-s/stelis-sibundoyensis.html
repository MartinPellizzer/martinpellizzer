Stelis sibundoyensis

Stelis sibundoyensis, commonly known as the Sibundoy orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of Colombia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insects.

Stelis sibundoyensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
