Silene paghmanica

Silene paghmanica, commonly known as the Paghman's campion, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its upright stems and showy pink or white flowers. Silene paghmanica is a perennial species that blooms during the summer months, contributing to the floral diversity of its native habitat.
