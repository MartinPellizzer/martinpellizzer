Scutellaria indica var. parvifolia

Scutellaria indica var. parvifolia, commonly known as small-leaved skullcap, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of blue to violet tubular flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Scutellaria indica var. parvifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional medicinal uses in some cultures.
