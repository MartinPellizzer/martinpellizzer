Swertia assamensis

Swertia assamensis, commonly known as Assam gentian, is a flowering plant native to the northeastern regions of India.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and shrublands. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Swertia assamensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
