Silene jenisea var. multiflora

Silene jenisea var. multiflora, commonly known as the multiflora silene, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of pink or white flowers.
