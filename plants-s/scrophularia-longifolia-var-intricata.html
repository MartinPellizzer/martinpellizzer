Scrophularia longifolia var. intricata

Scrophularia longifolia var. intricata, commonly known as the intricate scrophularia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that are often branched and covered with fine hairs. The leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with serrated edges and a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are typically pale pink to purple, arranged in whorls along the stem, and bloom in late spring to early summer.

This variety is noted for its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for ornamental purposes.
