Schefflera golip

Schefflera golip, commonly known as the golden schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound, palmate leaves that are typically bright green with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red, berry-like fruits. Schefflera golip thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
