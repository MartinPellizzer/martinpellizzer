Stellaria sessiliflora var. japonica

Stellaria sessiliflora var. japonica, commonly known as Japanese starwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features small, white or pale pink flowers that are arranged in clusters, giving it a star-like appearance. The plant has slender, branching stems that often lie close to the ground, and its leaves are typically ovate to lanceolate in shape. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
