Stelis scaberula

Stelis scaberula, commonly known as the rough stelis, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its compact growth habit and slender, erect stems. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in a spike. Stelis scaberula thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its unique morphology and ornamental flowers make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
