Socotranthus socotranus

Socotranthus socotranus, commonly known as the Socotra star grass, is a unique plant endemic to the island of Socotra.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped leaves. The plant thrives in the arid and rocky environments of Socotra, adapting to the harsh climatic conditions. It plays an important role in the local ecosystem and is a symbol of the island's unique biodiversity.

Due to its restricted distribution and habitat fragility, it is considered a vulnerable species.
