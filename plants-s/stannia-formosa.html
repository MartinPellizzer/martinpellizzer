Stannia formosa

Stannia formosa, commonly known as the beautiful stannia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a smooth, waxy texture.

Stannia formosa thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded forest environments.
