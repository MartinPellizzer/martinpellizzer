Swartzia brachyrhachis var. snethlageae

Swartzia brachyrhachis var. snethlageae, commonly known as the African yellowwood, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, aromatic wood and dark green, glossy leaves. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, followed by woody, spherical fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value and durable timber, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a decorative plant.
