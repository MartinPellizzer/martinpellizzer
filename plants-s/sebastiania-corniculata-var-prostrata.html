Sebastiania corniculata var. prostrata

Sebastiania corniculata var. prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate sebastiania, is a low-growing shrub native to the Caribbean.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are usually covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces clusters of yellowish-green flowers, which are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in coastal areas and is often found in sandy or limestone soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
