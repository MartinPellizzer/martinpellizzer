Scrophularia californica var. floribunda

Scrophularia californica var. floribunda, commonly known as California smooth betony, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of California.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils along the coast. The plant produces showy, violet-blue flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, and it has a fibrous root system.

It is often found in disturbed habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
