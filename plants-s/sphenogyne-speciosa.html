Sphenogyne speciosa

Sphenogyne speciosa, commonly known as the beautiful sphenogyne, is a rare and striking orchid species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, showy flowers that display a combination of vivid colors and intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories where it receives filtered sunlight. Its botanical features include slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Sphenogyne speciosa is considered a conservation concern.
