Specklinia exilis

Specklinia exilis, commonly known as the slender pigmy orchid, is a small, epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. The plant has narrow, dark green leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It is prized for its compact size and ornamental value in terrariums and indoor gardens.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled environments to ensure its survival.
