Schoenus Neovillosus

Schoenus neovillosus is a unique grass species native to specific regions of South America. It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth and distinctive, soft-hairy leaves. This plant thrives in wetland environments, often found in marshes and swampy areas. Its ecological importance lies in its role as a habitat for various small organisms. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species.
