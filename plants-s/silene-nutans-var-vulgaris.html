Silene nutans var. vulgaris

Silene nutans var. vulgaris, commonly known as the nodding catchfly, is a flowering plant native to the northern regions of the United States and Canada.

Silene nutans var. vulgaris is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 10 to 30 centimeters.
