Sonerila verticillata

Sonerila verticillata, commonly known as the clustered flame flower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite and elliptical, with a smooth surface and a glossy appearance.

Sonerila verticillata thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in lowland forests.
