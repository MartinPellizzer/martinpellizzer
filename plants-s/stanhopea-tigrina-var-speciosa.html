Stanhopea tigrina var. speciosa

Stanhopea tigrina var. speciosa, commonly known as the tiger orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful flowers that often feature intricate patterns resembling a tiger's stripes. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in its natural habitat.

The species is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
