Senecio pinnulatus

Senecio pinnulatus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved groundsel, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South Africa. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small yellow flowers arranged in composite heads, typical of the daisy family.

Senecio pinnulatus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
