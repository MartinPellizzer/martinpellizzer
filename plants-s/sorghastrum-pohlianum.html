Sorghastrum pohlianum

Sorghastrum pohlianum, commonly known as the Pohlian's bluestem, is a perennial grass native to the southern United States.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and prairies. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and small, blue-green foliage. Its inflorescence consists of a dense spike of pale blue to purple flowers, which appear in late summer.

Sorghastrum pohlianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in stabilizing soil in native plant landscapes.
