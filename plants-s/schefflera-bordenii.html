Schefflera bordenii

Schefflera bordenii, commonly known as the Borden's umbrella tree, is a tropical plant native to the island of Madagascar.

It is characterized by its distinctive, umbrella-like arrangement of large, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits. Schefflera bordenii thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

It is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
