Sobralia bradeorum

Sobralia bradeorum, commonly known as the Brade's sobralia, is a striking orchid species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically display a mix of yellow, orange, and red hues, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowering structure. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a fan-like pattern, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Sobralia bradeorum thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to flourish.
