Smilacina canadensis var. trifolia

Smilacina canadensis var. trifolia, commonly known as Canadian greenbriar, is a flowering plant native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, white, spadix-like flowers surrounded by a green spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.
