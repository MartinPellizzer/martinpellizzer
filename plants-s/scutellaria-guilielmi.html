Scutellaria guilielmi

Scutellaria guilielmi, commonly known as the Italian skullcap, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features square stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. Its small blue to purple flowers bloom in late spring, attracting pollinators like bees.

It is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported calming properties.
