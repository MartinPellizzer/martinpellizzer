Stelis bradei

Stelis bradei, commonly known as the Brade's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically features a pseudobulb with a single leaf and produces clusters of small, white or pale pink blossoms. Stelis bradei thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and ecological role within its native habitat.
