Spiranthes nebulorum

Spiranthes nebulorum, commonly known as the nebulous spikeroot, is a member of the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is native to the southeastern United States.

It is a perennial herb with a distinctive spiral arrangement of its white to pale pink flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as pine flatwoods and wetlands. Its roots are tuberous, and it reproduces through seeds and vegetative propagation.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and ecological importance in its native habitat.
