Schefflera latianulata

Schefflera latianulata, commonly known as the giant umbrella plant, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, umbrella-shaped leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in diameter. The plant has a robust, woody stem with a smooth, grayish-brown bark. Its leaves are typically a deep green with a waxy texture and are arranged in a compact, circular pattern at the ends of the branches.

Schefflera latianulata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor environments.
