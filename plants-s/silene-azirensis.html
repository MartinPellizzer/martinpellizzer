Silene azirensis

Silene azirensis, commonly known as the Azirian catchfly, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pinkish-purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Silene azirensis is of interest to botanists due to its unique ecological adaptations and limited distribution.
