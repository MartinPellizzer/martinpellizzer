Struthiola volkensii

Struthiola volkensii, commonly known as the African goldenbush, is a shrub native to eastern and southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, yellowish-green foliage and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has a compact growth habit and is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance.

Struthiola volkensii is also valued for its resilience to drought and poor soil conditions.
