Souroubea lepidota

Souroubea lepidota, commonly known as the African snowball, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Africa.

It features clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring, giving it a snowball-like appearance. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually covered in a fine, powdery coating. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its botanical characteristics make it well-suited for coastal and dry climates.
