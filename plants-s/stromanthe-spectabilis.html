Stromanthe spectabilis

Stromanthe spectabilis, commonly known as the Flame Plant, is a tropical foliage plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that display shades of green, white, and pink, often with a reddish tint on the underside. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to maintain its vibrant appearance. Its leaves are typically broad and elongated, with a waxy texture that enhances its ornamental value.

Stromanthe spectabilis is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
