Senecio parkeri

Senecio parkeri, commonly known as the Desert Star, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its yellow flowers and succulent leaves. The plant has a compact, low-growing habit, often forming a dense mat to conserve moisture. Its leaves are typically fleshy and covered in a fine, white powder, which helps reduce water loss.

Senecio parkeri is well adapted to harsh desert conditions, making it a resilient and ornamental plant in dry environments.
