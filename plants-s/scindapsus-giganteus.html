Scindapsus giganteus

Scindapsus giganteus, commonly known as the giant arrowhead plant, is a striking member of the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 inches in length, often with a glossy, dark green surface. Native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in bright, indirect light and humid environments. Its aerial roots allow it to climb and attach to surfaces, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets or shelves.

The plant is also noted for its ability to purify indoor air, adding both aesthetic and functional value to any space.
