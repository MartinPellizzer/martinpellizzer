Sonerila mamei

Sonerila mamei, commonly known as the Mamei orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, pendulous inflorescences with bright yellow or orange petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
