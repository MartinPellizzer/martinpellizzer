Sedum leveilleanum

Sedum leveilleanum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, narrow leaves that are typically blue-green in color, adapted for water storage. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
