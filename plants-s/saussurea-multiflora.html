Saussurea multiflora

Saussurea multiflora, commonly known as the Chinese yarrow or the Himalayan saffron.

It is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Asia, including China, India, and Nepal. The plant features a tall, leafless stem that can reach up to 2 meters in height, with dense clusters of yellow flowers at the top. Its roots are known for their medicinal properties and are used in traditional Chinese medicine.

The plant thrives in high-altitude environments and is adapted to cold, dry climates.
