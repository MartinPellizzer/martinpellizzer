Specklinia pyrsodes

Specklinia pyrsodes, commonly known as the golden rosette orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact rosette growth habit and bright green leaves with a distinctive golden hue. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Specklinia pyrsodes is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
