Sida olens

Sida olens, commonly known as velvetleaf, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a velvety texture. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by three-lobed seed pods. It is often found in disturbed soils and can be invasive in agricultural areas.

Botanically, it belongs to the Malvaceae family, which includes other plants like cotton and hibiscus.
