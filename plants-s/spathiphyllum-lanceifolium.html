Spathiphyllum lanceifolium

Spathiphyllum lanceifolium, commonly known as the lanceleaf peace lily, is a tropical flowering plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are glossy and often have a waxy texture. The plant produces white, spathaceous flowers that are enclosed within a modified leaf called a spathe. It thrives in low-light conditions and is popular as a houseplant due to its air-purifying properties and ease of care.

Spathiphyllum lanceifolium is also valued for its ability to tolerate a wide range of environmental conditions.
