Sassafras loureiroi

Sassafras loureiroi, commonly known as the Brazilian sassafras, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a distinctive wavy margin.

The fruit is a small, dark-colored drupe that contains a single seed.
