Schefflera borneensis

Schefflera borneensis, commonly known as the Bornean umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It is characterized by its distinctive, umbrella-like arrangement of large, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Schefflera borneensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor environments.
