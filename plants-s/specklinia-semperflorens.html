Specklinia semperflorens

Specklinia semperflorens, commonly known as the everblooming stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically blooming throughout the year. It prefers well-draining soil and full sun to partial shade.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
