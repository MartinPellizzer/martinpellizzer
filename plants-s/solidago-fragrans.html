Solidago fragrans

Solidago fragrans, commonly known as sweet goldenrod, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, bright yellow inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist soils along stream banks and wetlands. Its fragrant foliage and flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Solidago fragrans is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
