Scutellaria californica

Scutellaria californica, commonly known as California bluehead, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It belongs to the mint family (Lamiaceae) and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

Scutellaria californica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
