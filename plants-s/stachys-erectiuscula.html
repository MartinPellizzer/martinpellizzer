Stachys erectiuscula

Stachys erectiuscula, commonly known as the upright hedge nettle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features slender, upright stems covered in fine hairs and produces small, tubular flowers arranged in whorls along the stem. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate in shape, with a rough, hairy texture.

Stachys erectiuscula is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to thrive in sunny, well-drained environments.
