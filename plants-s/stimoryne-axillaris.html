Stimoryne axillaris

Stimoryne axillaris, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and dark green color. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in humid environments and is often found in disturbed soils near rivers or in open woodlands.

Due to its rapid growth, it can become invasive in certain areas.
