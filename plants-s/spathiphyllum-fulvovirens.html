Spathiphyllum fulvovirens

Spathiphyllum fulvovirens, commonly known as the golden torch peace lily, is a tropical evergreen plant native to the Philippines.

It features broad, dark green leaves with a distinctive golden-yellow margin, giving it its common name. The plant produces elegant, white spathes that enclose small, greenish-white flowers. It thrives in low-light conditions and requires consistent moisture and moderate humidity.

Spathiphyllum fulvovirens is popular as a houseplant due to its air-purifying properties and ornamental value.
