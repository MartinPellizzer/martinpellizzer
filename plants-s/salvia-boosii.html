Salvia boosii

Salvia boosii, commonly known as the African sage, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It features lance-shaped leaves with a silvery underside, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces spikes of violet to purple flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Salvia boosii is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
