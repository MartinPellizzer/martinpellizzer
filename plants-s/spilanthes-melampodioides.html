Spilanthes melampodioides

Spilanthes melampodioides, commonly known as the yellow rocket or false rocket, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States and parts of Mexico.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants, and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as prairies and woodland edges. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
