Satureja tenuis var. soriae

Satureja tenuis var. soriae, commonly known as Soria thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features small, narrow leaves that are aromatic and often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces pink to purple flowers in spikes during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its essential oils contain thymol and other compounds, giving it a strong, spicy fragrance.
