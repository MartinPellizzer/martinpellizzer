Sempervivum marmoreum subsp. marmoreum

Sempervivum marmoreum subsp.

marmoreum, commonly known as the Marble Cactus, is a succulent plant native to the Pyrenees Mountains. It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves with distinctive white or pinkish markings.

The plant produces tall, flowering stalks that bear clusters of small, pink or purple flowers. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its compact size and ornamental appearance make it a desirable addition to both indoor and outdoor gardens.
