Saussurea poophylla

Saussurea poophylla, commonly known as the desert globemallow, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of North America.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that helps it conserve moisture in harsh environments. The plant produces showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
