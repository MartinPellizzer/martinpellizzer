Scolymus perennis

Scolymus perennis, commonly known as everlasting daisy, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features sturdy, upright stems that bear clusters of small, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, gray-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Scolymus perennis is known for its long-lasting flowers, which remain intact even when dried, hence its common name.
