Senecio Picridioides

Senecio picridioides is a striking succulent native to South Africa. It features elongated, fleshy leaves that range in color from green to reddish-brown. This plant is known for its ability to thrive in arid environments with minimal water. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts. Cultivating Senecio picridioides requires careful attention to light and soil conditions.
