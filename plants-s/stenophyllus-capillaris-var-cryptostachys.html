Stenophyllus capillaris var. cryptostachys

Stenophyllus capillaris var. cryptostachys, commonly known as the cryptostachys sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in wetland or marshy environments, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences that are often inconspicuous. Its botanical structure is characterized by a rhizomatous root system that aids in its spreading growth habit.

This variety is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing wetland soils and providing habitat for aquatic organisms.
