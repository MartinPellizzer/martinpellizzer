Strobilorhachis

Strobilorhachis, commonly known as the "bearded orchid," is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

Strobilorhachis species are native to tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in Southeast Asia and the Pacific Islands.
