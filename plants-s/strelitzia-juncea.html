Strelitzia juncea

Strelitzia juncea, commonly known as the narrow-leaf bird-of-paradise, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the ginger family (Zingiberaceae) and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, colorful inflorescences that resemble a bird of paradise. The plant features broad, sword-shaped leaves that are usually a deep green, and its flowers are typically orange or yellow with a blue or purple hood.
