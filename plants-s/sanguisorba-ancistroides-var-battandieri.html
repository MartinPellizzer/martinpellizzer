Sanguisorba ancistroides var. battandieri

Sanguisorba ancistroides var. battandieri, commonly known as the purple fringed sanguisorbe, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features upright stems that bear dense clusters of small, pinkish-purple flowers with fringed petals. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges or meadows.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
