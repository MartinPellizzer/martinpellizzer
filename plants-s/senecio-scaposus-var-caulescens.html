Senecio scaposus var. caulescens

Senecio scaposus var. caulescens, commonly known as the slender senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, branched stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense, terminal clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
