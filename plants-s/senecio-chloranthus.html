Senecio chloranthus

Senecio chloranthus, commonly known as the green-flowered senecio, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
