Sulcorebutia crispata var. muelleri

Sulcorebutia crispata var. muelleri, commonly known as the Mueller's crassula, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, ribbed stems that are often tinged with red or purple, especially in cooler temperatures. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. Its leaves are typically blue-gray in color, with a slightly wrinkled texture.

It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its drought tolerance and ease of care.
