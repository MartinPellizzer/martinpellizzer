Scutellaria shikokiana f. pubicaulis

Scutellaria shikokiana f. pubicaulis, commonly known as the pubescent scutellaria, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its hairy or pubescent foliage. The plant typically features small, tubular flowers that are usually blue or purple in color. It grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions.

Its unique foliage and floral structure make it a notable species in botanical studies.
