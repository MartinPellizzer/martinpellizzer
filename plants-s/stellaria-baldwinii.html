Stellaria baldwinii

Stellaria baldwinii, commonly known as Baldwin's starwort, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically reaches a height of about 10 to 20 centimeters and has small, oval leaves arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in moist, rocky soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

Stellaria baldwinii is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family, which includes other flowering plants with similar morphological traits.
