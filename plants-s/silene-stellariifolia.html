Silene stellariifolia

Silene stellariifolia, commonly known as starwort, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges. Silene stellariifolia has slender, branching stems and small, lance-shaped leaves.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in supporting local pollinator populations.
