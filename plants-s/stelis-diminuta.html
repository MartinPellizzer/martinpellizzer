Stelis diminuta

Stelis diminuta, commonly known as the miniature stelis orchid, is a small epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. The plant has short, wiry stems and produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white and pink. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, adapted for efficient water absorption.

Stelis diminuta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or terrariums.
