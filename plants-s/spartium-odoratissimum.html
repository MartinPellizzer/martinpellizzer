Spartium odoratissimum

Spartium odoratissimum, commonly known as sweet everlasting, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its small, pea-like flowers. The plant produces fluffy, spherical seed pods that release tiny seeds when mature. Its aromatic foliage and showy blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Spartium odoratissimum is also valued for its historical use in herbal medicine.
