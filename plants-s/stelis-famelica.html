Stelis famelica

Stelis famelica, commonly known as the fairy orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, white or pale pink blooms that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, loose soil.

Stelis famelica is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
