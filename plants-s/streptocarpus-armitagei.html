Streptocarpus armitagei

Streptocarpus armitagei, commonly known as the Cape umbrella plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, umbrella-like inflorescence composed of numerous tubular flowers. The plant produces showy, violet to blue flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a waxy texture.

Streptocarpus armitagei is commonly cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
