Salacia gilgiana

Salacia gilgiana, commonly known as the yellow salacia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Salaceae and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a glossy surface.

Salacia gilgiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
