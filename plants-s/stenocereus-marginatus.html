Stenocereus marginatus

Stenocereus marginatus, commonly known as the organ pipe cactus, is a columnar cactus native to the Sonoran Desert.

It features a tall, spiny stem with ribbed surfaces and often branches near the top. The plant produces white or pink flowers during the spring and summer months. Its fruits are small, red berries that contain seeds.

Stenocereus marginatus is adapted to arid environments and plays a role in supporting local wildlife.
