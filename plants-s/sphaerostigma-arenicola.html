Sphaerostigma arenicola

Sphaerostigma arenicola, commonly known as the sandstone bladderwort, is a unique aquatic plant found in shallow, still waters with sandy or rocky substrates.

The plant typically grows in regions with low nutrient levels, adapting to its environment through specialized morphological features. Its reproductive structures are simple, often consisting of small, floating fruits that disperse via water currents.
