Syngonium standleyanum

Syngonium standleyanum, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a climbing vine native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, arrowhead-shaped, or shield-like leaves that vary in color from bright green to deep red or maroon, depending on the variety. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Syngonium standleyanum is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and air-purifying qualities.
