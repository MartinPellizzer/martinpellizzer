Santolina heterophylla

Santolina heterophylla, commonly known as silverleaf santolina, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring and early summer. It is a hardy, drought-tolerant shrub that thrives in well-drained soils.

Santolina heterophylla is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
