Serruria lacunosa

Serruria lacunosa, commonly known as the lace-leaf serruria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves with prominent leaf gaps, giving it a lacy appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in summer. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions.

Serruria lacunosa is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
