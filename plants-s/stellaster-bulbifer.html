Stellaster bulbifer

Stellaster bulbifer, commonly known as the star-shaped bulbous plant, is a unique species characterized by its intricate, star-like structure.

It features a central bulb from which multiple branches emerge, resembling a star with radiating arms. The plant's botanical structure includes fleshy, modified stems that serve as storage organs, supporting its survival in arid environments. Its leaves are often reduced to small scales, while its flowers are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences.

This plant is native to specific regions and is adapted to thrive in nutrient-poor soils.
