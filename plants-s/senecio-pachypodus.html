Senecio pachypodus

Senecio pachypodus, commonly known as the woolly blueweed, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Senecio pachypodus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique texture and color.
