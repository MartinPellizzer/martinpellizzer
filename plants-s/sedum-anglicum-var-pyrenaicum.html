Sedum anglicum var. pyrenaicum

Sedum anglicum var. pyrenaicum, commonly known as the Pyrenean stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the Pyrenees region.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink to white. The plant has thick, water-storing leaves that help it survive in arid conditions. It thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate harsh climates.
