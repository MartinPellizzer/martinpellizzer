Senna durangensis var. durangensis

Senna durangensis var. durangensis, commonly known as the Durango senna, is a shrub or small tree native to Mexico.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing drought tolerance. It produces pods containing seeds that are often used in traditional medicine.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and local flora.
