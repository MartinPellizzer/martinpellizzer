Skimmia wallichii

Skimmia wallichii, commonly known as Chinese skimmia, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves and produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers in the spring. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release bright red berries.

Skimmia wallichii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and winter interest.
