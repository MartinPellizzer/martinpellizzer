Setaria hochstetteri

Setaria hochstetteri, commonly known as the spikegrass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Africa, Asia, and Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear minute, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

Setaria hochstetteri is often found in disturbed areas and is adapted to both temperate and tropical climates.
