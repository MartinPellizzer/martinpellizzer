Scutellaria strigillosa f. albiflora

Scutellaria strigillosa f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered skullcap, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are ovate with a rough texture, and the stems are often hairy.

This variety is distinguished by its pale flowers, which contrast with the more common blue or purple hues of the species.
