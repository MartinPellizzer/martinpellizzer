Specklinia bupleurifolia

Specklinia bupleurifolia, commonly known as the bupleurum orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant has strap-like leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found growing in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some horticultural collections.
