Sinocrassula densirosulata

Sinocrassula densirosulata, commonly known as the dense-leaf sinocrassula, is a succulent species native to China.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming growth habit. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish tinge, adapted for water storage. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of care, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
