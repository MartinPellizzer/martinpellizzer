Senecio caloneotes

Senecio caloneotes, commonly known as the blue ice plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white powder, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in late winter to early spring, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its drought tolerance and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
