Scorzonera neapolitana

Scorzonera neapolitana, commonly known as the Naples scorzonera, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and yellow-orange composite flowers. The plant produces a tuberous root that is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and grassy slopes.
