Spondias purpurea var. munita

Spondias purpurea var. munita, commonly known as the purple mangosteen, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking purple flowers and dark purple, segmented fruit that is highly prized for its flavor and medicinal properties. The leaves are alternate, simple, and glossy, with a deep green color. The plant thrives in humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species in both ornamental horticulture and traditional medicine.
