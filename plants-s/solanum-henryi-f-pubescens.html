Solanum henryi f. pubescens

Solanum henryi f. pubescens, commonly known as the pubescent henry nightshade, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and peppers. The plant is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale purple.

The fruit is a yellowish berry that ripens in late summer, though it is not typically cultivated for food.
