Stipa extremiorientalis var. pubicalyx

Stipa extremiorientalis var. pubicalyx, commonly known as the Eastern Asian feather grass, is a perennial grass species native to regions of East Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, slender leaves and panicle-like inflorescences that are often adorned with silky, hair-like structures. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its botanical features include narrow, grass-like leaves and a distinctive, feathery appearance that contributes to its ornamental value.

This variety is noted for its resilience and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
