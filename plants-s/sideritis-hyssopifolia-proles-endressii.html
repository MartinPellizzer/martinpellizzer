Sideritis hyssopifolia proles endressii

Sideritis hyssopifolia proles endressii, commonly known as the Endress's ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or blue in color, arranged in dense clusters. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, with a distinct minty aroma.

This species is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun conditions.
