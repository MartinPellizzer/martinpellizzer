Stenandriopsis leptostachys

Stenandriopsis leptostachys, commonly known as the slender-stemmed orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its slender stems and elongated leaves, which are typically green and lance-shaped. Its flowers are small and clustered, often with a delicate white or pale pink coloration.

Stenandriopsis leptostachys thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.
