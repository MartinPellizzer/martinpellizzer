Saurauia leucocarpa var. smithiana

Saurauia leucocarpa var. smithiana, commonly known as Smith's white-fruited saurauia, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen shrub characterized by its elliptical, glossy dark green leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are fleshy, white or yellow, and contain several seeds.

It is often found in shaded, moist forest environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
