Stranvaesia impressivena

Stranvaesia impressivena, commonly known as the impressivena starfish flower, is a unique and striking plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, star-shaped flowers that resemble the pattern of a starfish, giving it its common name. The plant belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in gardens. Its foliage is lance-shaped and often features a striking contrast to its colorful blooms.

Stranvaesia impressivena thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.
