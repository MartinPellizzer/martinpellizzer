Santiria trimera

Santiria trimera, commonly known as the African star grass, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant produces small, yellow to orange flowers that are often clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Santiria trimera is often found in open grasslands and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils.
