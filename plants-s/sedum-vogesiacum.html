Sedum vogesiacum

Sedum vogesiacum, commonly known as the Voges sedum, is a succulent plant native to Europe.

It is characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine regions. It has adapted to harsh environments with its ability to store water in its leaves.

Sedum vogesiacum is also valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens and its role in supporting local pollinators.
