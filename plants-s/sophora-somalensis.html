Sophora somalensis

Sophora somalensis, commonly known as the Somali Sophora, is a flowering plant native to the Horn of Africa.

It is a small, deciduous tree or shrub that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant produces yellow or greenish flowers arranged in racemes, which are borne on slender peduncles. Its leaves are pinnately compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.

The seeds are enclosed in elongated pods and are often dispersed by wind or animals.
