Sterculia crassiramea

Sterculia crassiramea, commonly known as the broad-leafed sterculia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release winged seeds.

Sterculia crassiramea is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forest understories.
