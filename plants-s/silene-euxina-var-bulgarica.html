Silene euxina var. bulgarica

Silene euxina var. bulgarica, commonly known as the Bulgarian campion, is a flowering plant native to parts of Eastern Europe and the Balkans.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its pink to red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky areas. It has simple, alternate leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits.
