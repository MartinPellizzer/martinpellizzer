Silphium speciosum

Silphium speciosum, commonly known as the showy silphium, is a perennial herb native to the eastern and central United States.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its striking yellow-orange composite flowers. The plant has a robust, branched stem and lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. Silphium speciosum thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical use in traditional medicine.
