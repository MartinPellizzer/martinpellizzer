Sesbania leptocarpa var. minimiflora

Sesbania leptocarpa var. minimiflora, commonly known as the small-flower sesbania, is a variant of the sesbania plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a leguminous species characterized by its small, delicate flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces elongated, slender pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and agricultural applications.
