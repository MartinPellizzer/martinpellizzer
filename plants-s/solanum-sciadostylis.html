Solanum sciadostylis

Solanum sciadostylis, commonly known as the dark-styled nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant is characterized by its distinctive dark purple style in its flowers, which sets it apart from other species in the genus. It typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded or humid environments.

Solanum sciadostylis produces small, fleshy berries that are toxic to humans but may be consumed by certain wildlife.
