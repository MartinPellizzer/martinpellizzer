Swartzia parviflora

Swartzia parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered swartzia, is a tropical tree native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant produces legumes as its fruit, which contain several seeds. It is often found in humid lowland forests and is valued for its timber.

Swartzia parviflora is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
