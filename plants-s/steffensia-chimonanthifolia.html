Steffensia chimonanthifolia

Steffensia chimonanthifolia, commonly known as the Chinese lantern tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its showy, orange-red flowers that resemble lanterns, which bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Steffensia chimonanthifolia is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
