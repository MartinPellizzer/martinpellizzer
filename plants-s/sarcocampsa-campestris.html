Sarcocampsa campestris

Sarcocampsa campestris, commonly known as the field gentian, is a flowering plant belonging to the Gentianaceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of western Asia, typically found in meadows and grasslands. The plant produces striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. It has lance-shaped leaves and grows up to 60 cm tall.

Sarcocampsa campestris is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens.
