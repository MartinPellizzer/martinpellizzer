Sempervivum holochrysum

Sempervivum holochrysum, commonly known as the golden lion's tail, is a succulent plant native to the Pyrenees Mountains.

It features dense, fleshy rosettes of leaves that are typically green with a striking golden or coppery hue. The plant produces vibrant red or orange flowers in late summer, which are borne on tall, upright stems. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Sempervivum holochrysum is drought-tolerant and hardy, suitable for USDA hardiness zones 4 through 8.
