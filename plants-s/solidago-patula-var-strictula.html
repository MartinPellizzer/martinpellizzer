Solidago patula var. strictula

Solidago patula var. strictula, commonly known as the narrowleaf goldenrod, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as meadows, wetlands, and stream banks. The plant features upright, narrow leaves and produces bright yellow inflorescences in late summer to early fall. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

This variety is distinguished by its more compact growth habit compared to other goldenrod species.
