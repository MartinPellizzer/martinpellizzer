Schizachyrium domingense

Schizachyrium domingense, commonly known as dwarf panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist to wet soils, often found in swamps, marshes, and along stream banks. The plant has slender, erect stems that produce small, spike-like inflorescences adorned with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowering period occurs in late summer to early fall, and it is well adapted to wetland environments.

Schizachyrium domingense plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
