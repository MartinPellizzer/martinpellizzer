Spiranthes polyantha

Spiranthes polyantha, commonly known as the pink spire orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its delicate, spiraling spires of pink to lavender flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wetlands. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, forming a basal rosette.

Spiranthes polyantha is a member of the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is known for its unique floral structure and ecological importance to pollinators.
