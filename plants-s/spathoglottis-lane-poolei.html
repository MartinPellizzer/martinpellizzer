Spathoglottis lane-poolei

Spathoglottis lane-poolei, commonly known as the Lane-Poole's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces showy, resupinate flowers with a distinctive spathe and a single, erect lip. Its leaves are typically linear and arranged in a rosette pattern.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
