Santolina tomentosa

Santolina tomentosa, commonly known as woolly santolina, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features silver-gray, fuzzy foliage that is dense and compact, giving it a soft, woolly appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in late spring to early summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Santolina tomentosa is also valued for its aromatic properties and use in herbal medicine.
