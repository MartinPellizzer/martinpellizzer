Setaria glaziovii

Setaria glaziovii, commonly known as Glaziov's panicgrass, is a flowering plant species native to arid and semi-arid regions of Mexico and Central America.

It is a member of the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or disturbed habitats.

Due to its resilience and ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in dryland gardens.
