Stromanthe bahiensis

Stromanthe bahiensis, commonly known as the Bahamian prayer plant, is a tropical flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with contrasting colors. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale pink. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Stromanthe bahiensis is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental leaves and relatively easy care requirements.
