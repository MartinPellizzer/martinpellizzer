Suaeda salina

Suaeda salina, commonly known as saltbush, is a halophyte plant adapted to saline environments.

It thrives in coastal areas, salt marshes, and arid regions with high salinity. The plant has succulent leaves that store water and reduce transpiration. Its small, green or yellowish flowers are often clustered in dense inflorescences.

Suaeda salina plays an important role in stabilizing soils and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
