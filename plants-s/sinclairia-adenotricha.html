Sinclairia adenotricha

Sinclairia adenotricha, commonly known as the hairy leaf sinclairia, is a member of the Araliaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are covered in fine, hairy structures, giving it its common name. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels.

Sinclairia adenotricha is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
