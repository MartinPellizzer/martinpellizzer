Sida duumviratii

Sida duumviratii, commonly known as the two-faced sida, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It is an annual herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features alternate, lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture, and it produces small, pale yellow flowers in clusters. Sida duumviratii is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides, thriving in arid and semi-arid environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it an interesting subject for ecological and agricultural studies.
