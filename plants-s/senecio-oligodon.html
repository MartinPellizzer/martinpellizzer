Senecio oligodon

Senecio oligodon, commonly known as the narrowleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats. Its leaves are narrow and linear, and it produces small yellow flowers that bloom in the spring.
