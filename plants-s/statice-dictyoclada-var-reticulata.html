Statice dictyoclada var. reticulata

Statice dictyoclada var. reticulata, commonly known as the lace statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery foliage and showy, spike-like flower heads that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its flowers are often used in floral arrangements due to their long-lasting nature and elegant appearance.

The species is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract beneficial insects such as butterflies and bees.
