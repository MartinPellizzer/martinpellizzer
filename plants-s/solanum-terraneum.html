Solanum terraneum

Solanum terraneum, commonly known as the African nightshade, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 30 cm, with small, dark green leaves and showy, violet-blue flowers. The plant produces small, yellow berries that are toxic to humans and animals. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Solanum terraneum is part of the nightshade family, which includes other plants with similar characteristics.
