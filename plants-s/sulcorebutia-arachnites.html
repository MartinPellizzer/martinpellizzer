Sulcorebutia arachnites

Sulcorebutia arachnites, commonly known as the spider-shaped crassula, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features a dense rosette of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine, fuzzy coating. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and succulent collections.
