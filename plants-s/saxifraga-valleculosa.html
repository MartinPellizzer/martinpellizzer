Saxifraga valleculosa

Saxifraga valleculosa, commonly known as the rockfoam, is a perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in alpine or woodland habitats. The plant features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with a waxy texture. Its flowers are small, white or pale pink, and appear in clusters during the spring.

Saxifraga valleculosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging environments.
