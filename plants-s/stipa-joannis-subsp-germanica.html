Stipa joannis subsp. germanica

Stipa joannis subsp.

germanica, commonly known as the German feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe. It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and fluffy, feathery plumes that give it a distinctive appearance.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in landscaping and restoration projects.
