Spathiphyllum brent-berlinii

Spathiphyllum brent-berlinii, commonly known as the Berlinii Peace Lily, is a tropical evergreen plant native to the rainforests of Central America.

It features broad, waxy, dark green leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces elegant, white spathes that enclose small, greenish-white flowers, giving it a striking appearance. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and ability to thrive in humid environments.

The plant is also noted for its air-purifying properties, making it a popular choice for interior spaces.
