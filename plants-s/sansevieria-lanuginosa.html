Sansevieria lanuginosa

Sansevieria lanuginosa, commonly known as the woolly snake plant, is a succulent native to southern Africa.

It features long, narrow leaves covered in soft, white, woolly hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its fleshy leaves. It produces small, greenish-white flowers on slender stems during the growing season.

Sansevieria lanuginosa is a low-maintenance plant that thrives in indirect light and well-draining soil.
