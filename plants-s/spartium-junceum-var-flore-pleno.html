Spartium junceum var. flore-pleno

Spartium junceum var. flore-pleno, commonly known as the full-flowered rush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its distinctive pod-like fruits and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces dense clusters of small, pale yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Its ornamental value comes from its compact growth habit and showy floral display.
