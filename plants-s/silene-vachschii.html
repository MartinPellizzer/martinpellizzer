Silene vachschii

Silene vachschii, commonly known as the Vachschii campion, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, including parts of Kazakhstan and Tajikistan. The plant is characterized by its upright stems, narrow leaves, and clusters of pink or white flowers. It thrives in rocky and arid environments, often growing at high altitudes.

Silene vachschii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
