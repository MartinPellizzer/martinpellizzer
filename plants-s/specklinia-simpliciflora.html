Specklinia simpliciflora

Specklinia simpliciflora, commonly known as the simple-flowered orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces delicate, greenish-white flowers arranged in a spike, which bloom during the wet season. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in its natural habitat.

Specklinia simpliciflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in terrariums or as a houseplant.
