Sinarundinaria pubescens var. bicolor

Sinarundinaria pubescens var. bicolor, commonly known as the bicolor bamboo, is a striking ornamental plant native to China.

It is characterized by its distinctive two-toned foliage, with a combination of green and silver-white variegation on its leaves. The plant belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is valued for its elegant appearance and hardy nature. It typically grows to a height of 3 to 5 meters and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and landscapes.

Its leaves are narrow, lance-shaped, and have a soft, pubescent texture that adds to its visual interest.
