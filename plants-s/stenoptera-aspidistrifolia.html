Stenoptera aspidistrifolia

Stenoptera aspidistrifolia, commonly known as the velvetleaf arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture, giving it its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its rhizomes are also used in traditional medicine and as a food source in some regions.
