Saccoloma inaequale var. costaricense

Saccoloma inaequale var. costaricense, commonly known as the Costa Rican saccoloma, is a fern native to Central America.

It belongs to the family Hymenophyllaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds. The plant typically grows on moist, shaded rock surfaces or tree trunks in humid tropical environments. Its leaves are small, with a distinctive veining pattern and a pale green to silvery appearance.
