Scrophularia ehrhartii

Scrophularia ehrhartii, commonly known as Ehrhart's figwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges. The plant features square stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture. Its flowers are tubular and usually pink or purple, arranged in spikes, and bloom in late spring to early summer.

It is part of the Scrophulariaceae family, known for its ornamental and medicinal uses.
