Strombosia fleuryana

Strombosia fleuryana, commonly known as the African star orchid, is a rare and striking orchid species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow with intricate patterns. The plant has a compact growth habit, with fleshy, dark green leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. Strombosia fleuryana is often found in rocky outcrops and sandy soils, thriving in full sun.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is highly sought after by botanists and horticulturists.
