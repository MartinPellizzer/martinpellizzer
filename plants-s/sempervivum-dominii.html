Sempervivum dominii

Sempervivum dominii, commonly known as the Albanian houseleek, is a succulent plant native to the alpine regions of southern Europe.

It features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with a bluish tint, often covered in a fine layer of white wax. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink to purple during the summer months. It is well adapted to rocky and arid environments, thriving in full sun and well-drained soil.

Sempervivum dominii is a low-maintenance plant that is popular among rock garden enthusiasts.
