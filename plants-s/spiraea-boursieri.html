Spiraea boursieri

Spiraea boursieri, commonly known as the golden spiraea, is a deciduous shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, showy yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has finely toothed, dark green leaves that turn reddish in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Spiraea boursieri is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
