Sabicea dewevrei var. glabra

Sabicea dewevrei var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth sabicea, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous bark and simple, opposite leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
