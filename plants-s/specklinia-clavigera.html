Specklinia clavigera

Specklinia clavigera, commonly known as the clustered feather-leaf orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, clumping growth habit and its distinctive, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, typically blooming during the summer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
