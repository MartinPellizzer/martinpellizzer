Stelis pelycophora

Stelis pelycophora, commonly known as the orchid with the distinctive yellow and green flowers, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is typically found in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually borne on terminal inflorescences and are characterized by their elongated, spurred petals.

Stelis pelycophora prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
