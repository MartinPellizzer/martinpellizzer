Stemodia stricta var. paucidentata

Stemodia stricta var. paucidentata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved stemodia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale green to yellow in color, arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
