Solanum marquesii

Solanum marquesii, commonly known as the Marquesas nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the tropical Pacific islands of the Marquesas.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale purple, which bloom in clusters. Its fruits are berries that change from green to yellow or red as they mature.

Solanum marquesii is part of the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants such as tomatoes and potatoes.
