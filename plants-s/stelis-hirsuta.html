Stelis hirsuta

Stelis hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy stelis orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia and the Pacific Islands.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a distinctive appearance, featuring hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique and relatively rare orchid species in its native habitat.
