Scutellaria baldshuanica

Scutellaria baldshuanica, commonly known as the Balkan skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular blue or violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or woodland habitats.

Scutellaria baldshuanica is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
