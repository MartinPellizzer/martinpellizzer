Senecio perezii

Senecio perezii, commonly known as the Perez's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and woolly foliage. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It has small, narrow leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Senecio perezii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
