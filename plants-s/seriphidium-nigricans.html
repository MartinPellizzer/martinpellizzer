Seriphidium nigricans

Seriphidium nigricans, commonly known as blackstem wormwood, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, often branched stems that are typically dark green to black in color. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in dense, spherical clusters called capitula. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas and open woodlands.

Seriphidium nigricans is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
