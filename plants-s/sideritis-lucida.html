Sideritis lucida

Sideritis lucida, commonly known as the white heath, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its slender stems and small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are often hairy and have a mint-like scent, contributing to its medicinal uses.

Sideritis lucida is often grown for ornamental purposes and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
