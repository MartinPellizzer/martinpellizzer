Sorghum centroplicatum var. incertum

Sorghum centroplicatum var. incertum, commonly known as the uncertain sorghum, is a lesser-known variant within the sorghum genus.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically produces small, clustered flowers that are often inconspicuous compared to other sorghum species. It is native to regions of Africa and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Due to its limited cultivation and unclear taxonomic status, it remains a subject of botanical interest and study.
