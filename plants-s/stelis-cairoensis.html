Stelis cairoensis

Stelis cairoensis, commonly known as the Cairo orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a slender, branching stem with dense clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture from the air, and it thrives in high humidity and indirect light.

Stelis cairoensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
