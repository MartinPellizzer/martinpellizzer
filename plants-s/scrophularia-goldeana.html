Scrophularia goldeana

Scrophularia goldeana, commonly known as the golden scrophularia, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that can grow up to 60 centimeters in height. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.
