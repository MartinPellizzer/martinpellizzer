Sansevieria volkensii

Sansevieria volkensii, commonly known as the African spear or snake plant, is a succulent plant native to East Africa.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are often variegated with green, white, and yellow patterns. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low light conditions and requires minimal watering. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water, making it highly drought-tolerant.

Sansevieria volkensii is also noted for its air-purifying qualities, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
