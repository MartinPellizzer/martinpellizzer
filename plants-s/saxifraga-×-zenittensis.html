Saxifraga × zenittensis

Saxifraga × zenittensis, commonly known as the hybrid saxifrage, is a flowering plant in the Saxifragaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It is native to rocky or alpine environments in Europe and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and rockeries.
