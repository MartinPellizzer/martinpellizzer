Styrax burchellii var. longifolius

Styrax burchellii var. longifolius, commonly known as the long-leaved styrax, is a species native to southern Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils. The plant is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and clusters of fragrant, white flowers that bloom in spring. Its fruit is a small, woody capsule that contains several seeds.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticulture for its unique foliage and floral display.
