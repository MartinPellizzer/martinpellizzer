Saxifraga vulcanica

Saxifraga vulcanica, commonly known as the volcanic saxifrage, is a flowering plant native to the volcanic regions of Iceland.

It thrives in harsh, rocky environments, often growing in crevices and on lava fields. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are typically succulent, adapted to retain moisture in arid conditions.

Saxifraga vulcanica is a hardy species that demonstrates remarkable resilience to extreme environmental conditions.
