Sedum cognense

Sedum cognense, commonly known as the showy sedum, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a perennial succulent that thrives in rocky or sandy soils, often found in dry, sunny environments. The plant features fleshy, upright stems and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are typically blue-green in color and are arranged in a rosette pattern.

Sedum cognense is valued for its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal in gardens and landscapes.
