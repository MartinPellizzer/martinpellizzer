Senecio mustersii var. mustersii

Senecio mustersii var. mustersii, commonly known as the Desert Senecio, is a perennial herb native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions. Its foliage is gray-green with a fuzzy texture, and it produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring.

This species is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental value.
