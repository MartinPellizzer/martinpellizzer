Senecio palmatifidus

Senecio palmatifidus, commonly known as the African marguerite, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features soft, gray-green leaves that are palmately divided, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces bright yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months.

Senecio palmatifidus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
