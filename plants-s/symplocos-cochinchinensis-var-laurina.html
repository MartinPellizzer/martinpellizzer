Symplocos cochinchinensis var. laurina

Symplocos cochinchinensis var. laurina, commonly known as the laurina boxwood, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage. The leaves are typically elliptical, glossy, and have a leathery texture. It produces small, white flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by red, fleshy berries.
