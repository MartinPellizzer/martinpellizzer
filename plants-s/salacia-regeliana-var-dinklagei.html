Salacia regeliana var. dinklagei

Salacia regeliana var. dinklagei, commonly known as the Dinklage's salacia, is a rare and unique species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in spikes, followed by red, fleshy berries that serve as a food source for various wildlife.
