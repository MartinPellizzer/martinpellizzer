Scaphyglottis dubia

Scaphyglottis dubia, commonly known as the slender sand orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

It is a small, terrestrial herb with a short, wiry stem and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces delicate, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically clustered in loose inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand periods of drought.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
