Stelis tempestuosa

Stelis tempestuosa, commonly known as the storm orchid, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and strikingly colorful flowers that often feature vivid red, orange, or yellow hues. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle, and thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are short-lived but highly fragrant, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Stelis tempestuosa is valued not only for its ornamental appeal but also for its ecological role in supporting biodiversity in its native habitat.
