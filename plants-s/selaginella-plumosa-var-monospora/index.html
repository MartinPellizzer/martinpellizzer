Selaginella Plumosa Var. Monospora

Selaginella plumosa var. monospora is a unique and resilient fern-like plant known for its striking, feathery appearance. Native to arid and rocky environments, it thrives in conditions where many other plants struggle to survive. This variant of Selaginella is particularly noted for its ability to withstand extreme temperatures and limited water availability. Its delicate, moss-like foliage forms intricate patterns that make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Despite its delicate look, it possesses remarkable survival mechanisms that have allowed it to endure in harsh climates for centuries.
