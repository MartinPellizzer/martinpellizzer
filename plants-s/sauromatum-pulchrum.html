Sauromatum pulchrum

Sauromatum pulchrum, commonly known as the golden arrow or the arrowhead plant, is a striking tropical species native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy, yellow-orange flowers that resemble arrows, hence its common name. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in humid, warm environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and greenhouse collections.
