Senecio minesinus

Senecio minesinus, commonly known as the Mexican ragwort, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, often in rocky soils. It has lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Senecio minesinus is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
