Strobus Koraiensis

Strobus koraiensis is a unique plant species native to the mountainous regions of East Asia. It belongs to the family Pinaceae and is known for its distinctive conical shape and dense foliage. This evergreen tree thrives in cool, humid climates and is often found in forested areas. Its ecological importance includes providing habitat and food for various wildlife species. Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, Strobus koraiensis is increasingly being studied for conservation and sustainable use.
