Senecio sanmarcosensis

Senecio sanmarcosensis, commonly known as San Marcos groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, hot environments. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months.
