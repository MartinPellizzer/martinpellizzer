Sarcoyucca schottii

Sarcoyucca schottii, commonly known as the Schott's yucca, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its rosette of sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in coastal or dry forest environments.
