Senecio auricomus

Senecio auricomus, commonly known as the golden groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and hairy, lobed leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Senecio auricomus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and hardiness.
