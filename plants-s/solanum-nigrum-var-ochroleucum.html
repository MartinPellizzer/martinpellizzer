Solanum nigrum var. ochroleucum

Solanum nigrum var. ochroleucum, commonly known as the white-leaf black nightshade, is a variant of the common nightshade plant.

It is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant features oval, pale green leaves with a distinctive white or pale green hue. Its flowers are typically yellow with a purple calyx, and it produces small, black berries that are toxic to humans and animals.

This variant is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides, thriving in temperate climates.
