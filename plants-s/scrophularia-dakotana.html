Scrophularia dakotana

Scrophularia dakotana, commonly known as the Dakota figwort, is a flowering plant native to the central United States.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, open habitats such as prairies and wetlands. The plant features square, hairy stems and opposite leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are clustered in whorls and are usually pink or purple in color.

Scrophularia dakotana plays a role in supporting local pollinators and is part of the Scrophulariaceae family.
