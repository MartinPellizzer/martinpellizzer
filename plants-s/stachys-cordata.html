Stachys cordata

Stachys cordata, commonly known as heartleaf plant or heart-leaf hedge.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to temperate regions of Europe and parts of Asia. The plant features opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are typically hairy and have a rough texture. It produces spikes of violet or purple flowers in the summer months.

Stachys cordata is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
