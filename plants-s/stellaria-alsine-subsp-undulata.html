Stellaria alsine subsp. undulata

Stellaria alsine subsp.

undulata, commonly known as the wavy sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to sandy and disturbed soils in temperate regions. It features small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in early summer.

The leaves are narrow and often have a wavy or undulating margin, giving the species its subspecific name. It is often found in coastal dunes, grasslands, and along roadsides.

The plant has a compact growth habit and is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
