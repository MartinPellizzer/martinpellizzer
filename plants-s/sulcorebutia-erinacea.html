Sulcorebutia erinacea

Sulcorebutia erinacea, commonly known as the erinacea cactus, is a species of flowering cactus native to South America.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular shape and spiny, segmented stems. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, blooming during the warmer months. It is a low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-draining soil and full sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for succulent collections and rock gardens.
