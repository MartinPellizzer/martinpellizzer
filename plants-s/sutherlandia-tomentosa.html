Sutherlandia tomentosa

Sutherlandia tomentosa, commonly known as cancer bush, is a small shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, greyish-green leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces red, berry-like fruits that contain seeds, which are dispersed by animals. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in grassland and rocky habitats.

Due to its traditional use in herbal medicine, it has gained attention for its potential health benefits.
