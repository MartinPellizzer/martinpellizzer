Stenoloma chinense

Stenoloma chinense, commonly known as the Chinese stenoloma, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the family Poaceae, or grass family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, elongated leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, bisexual flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Stenoloma chinense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate foliage.
