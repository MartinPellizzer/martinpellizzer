Stapelianthus insignis subsp.

tongoboryensis, commonly known as the Tongoboryensis stapelioid, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically during the warmer months. It is a member of the Apocynaceae family, which includes many other succulent species.
