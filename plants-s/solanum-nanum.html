Solanum nanum

Solanum nanum, commonly known as the dwarf nightshade, is a small shrub native to Brazil.

It features simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into berries containing several seeds. It is a member of the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes.

Solanum nanum is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in shaded, moist environments.
