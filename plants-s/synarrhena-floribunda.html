Synarrhena floribunda

Synarrhena floribunda, commonly known as the fragrant starflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in shades of yellow and orange. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sunlight to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
