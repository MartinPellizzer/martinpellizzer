Scindapsus suffruticosus

Scindapsus suffruticosus, commonly known as the silver vine, is a tropical evergreen vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features waxy, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with silver and green patterns. The plant is known for its ability to climb using aerial roots and can grow quite long if given support. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Its ornamental foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and hanging baskets.
