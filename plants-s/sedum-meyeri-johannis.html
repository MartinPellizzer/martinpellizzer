Sedum meyeri-johannis

Sedum meyeri-johannis, commonly known as the "Johannis Sedum," is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow or orange. The plant has a compact growth habit, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.

Its ability to store water in its leaves allows it to survive in arid conditions.
