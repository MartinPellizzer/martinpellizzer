Saussurea Triangulata Subsp. Manshurica

It is known for its striking yellow flowers and its ability to thrive in cold, high-altitude environments. This subspecies is particularly rare and faces threats from habitat loss and climate change. It plays an important role in the local ecosystem and is a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
