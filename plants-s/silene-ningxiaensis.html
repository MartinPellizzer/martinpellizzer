Silene ningxiaensis

Silene ningxiaensis, commonly known as the Ningxia campion, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its pink to red flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Silene ningxiaensis has small, lance-shaped leaves and produces capsules as its fruit.

It is of interest to botanists due to its unique ecological niche and potential horticultural value.
