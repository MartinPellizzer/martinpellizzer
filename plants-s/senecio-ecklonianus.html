Senecio ecklonianus

Senecio ecklonianus, commonly known as the yellow oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that resemble those of the oxalis genus. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, daisy-like blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Senecio ecklonianus is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
