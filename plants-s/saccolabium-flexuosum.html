Saccolabium flexuosum

Saccolabium flexuosum, commonly known as the zigzag acacia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzagging stems and bipinnate leaves. The plant produces yellow to orange flowers in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

Saccolabium flexuosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
