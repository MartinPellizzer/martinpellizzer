Sarcogonum complexum

Sarcogonum complexum, commonly known as the common sarcogonum, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its composite flower heads, which consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as wetlands and marshes. Its leaves are alternate, lanceolate, and often have hairy surfaces.
