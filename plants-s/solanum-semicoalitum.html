Solanum semicoalitum

Solanum semicoalitum, commonly known as the black nightshade, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades such as tomatoes and peppers. The plant produces small, dark purple berries that are toxic to humans and animals. Its leaves are ovate with a rough texture, and it typically grows in shaded, moist environments.

Solanum semicoalitum is often found in disturbed soils and can be invasive in certain areas.
