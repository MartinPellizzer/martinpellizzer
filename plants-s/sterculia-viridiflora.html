Sterculia viridiflora

Sterculia viridiflora, commonly known as the green-flowered sterculia, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and distinctive greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces woody pods that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and medicinal contexts.
