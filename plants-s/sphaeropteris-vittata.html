Sphaeropteris vittata

Sphaeropteris vittata, commonly known as the ribbon fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, narrow, and often arching fronds that give it a graceful appearance. The plant features a creeping rhizome that allows it to spread and form dense mats. Its leaves are typically dark green with a slightly glossy texture and may have a subtle variegation.

Sphaeropteris vittata thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
