Statice hohenackeri

Statice hohenackeri, commonly known as the Hohenacker's sea lavender, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit. The plant produces showy, spike-like clusters of small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in a fine layer of white powder.

Statice hohenackeri is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
