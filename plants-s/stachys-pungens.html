Stachys pungens

Stachys pungens, commonly known as garden betony, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the mint family (Lamiaceae) and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces spikes of purple or pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are often aromatic and have a slightly rough texture.

Stachys pungens is valued in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
