Scalesia stewartii var. euryphylla

Scalesia stewartii var. euryphylla, commonly known as the broad-leaved scalesia, is a variant of the scalesia genus native to the Galápagos Islands.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its large, broad leaves and showy yellow-orange flower heads. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to the unique climate of the Galápagos. It plays a significant role in the local ecosystem, providing habitat and food for various native species.

Due to its restricted range and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species.
