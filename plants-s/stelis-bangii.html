Stelis bangii

Stelis bangii, commonly known as the Bangi orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are usually dark green and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Stelis bangii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
