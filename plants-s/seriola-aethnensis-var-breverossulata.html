Seriola aethnensis var. breverossulata

Seriola aethnensis var. breverossulata, commonly known as the Aethnian seriola, is a rare and unique variant of the seriola genus.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive foliage, which features small, tightly packed leaves. The plant exhibits a preference for well-drained, calcareous soils and thrives in Mediterranean climates. Its flowers are typically small and inconspicuous, with a short blooming period.

Due to its limited distribution and specific ecological requirements, it is considered a botanical curiosity and of interest to horticultural enthusiasts.
