Stylidium debile var. paniculatum

Stylidium debile var. paniculatum, commonly known as the paniculate sun-orchid, is a small, herbaceous plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its delicate, often yellow or greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils, often in arid or semi-arid regions. Its flowers are clustered in a paniculate inflorescence, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The species is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in disturbed or open habitats.
