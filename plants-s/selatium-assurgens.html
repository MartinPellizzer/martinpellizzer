Selatium assurgens

Selatium assurgens, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences.
