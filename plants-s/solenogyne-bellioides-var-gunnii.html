Solenogyne bellioides var. gunnii

Solenogyne bellioides var. gunnii, commonly known as the Gunn's flax-lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Australia.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are often blue-green in color, and it produces small, clustered white or pale pink flowers. The plant is typically found in sandy or gravelly soils in open habitats. It is well adapted to dry conditions and can thrive in full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for native garden landscaping.
