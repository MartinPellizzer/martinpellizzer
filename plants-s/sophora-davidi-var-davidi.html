Sophora davidi var. davidi

Sophora davidi var. davidi, commonly known as the Chinese wisteria, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are pinnately arranged and have a distinctive blue-green color. The plant produces long, pendulous clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in early summer. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to a variety of soil types.

However, it can become invasive in some regions if not properly managed.
