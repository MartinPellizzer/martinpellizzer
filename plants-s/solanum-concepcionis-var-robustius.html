Solanum concepcionis var. robustius

Solanum concepcionis var. robustius, commonly known as the robust giant nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its large, ovate leaves and showy, violet-pink flowers. The plant produces small, yellowish berries that are toxic to humans and animals. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in open forests and disturbed areas.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
