Scindapsus peepla

Scindapsus peepla, commonly known as the Silver Pothos.

It is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its waxy, heart-shaped leaves that often display a striking silver or variegated pattern. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions. Its aerial roots allow it to climb and attach to surfaces, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets or shelves.

Scindapsus peepla is relatively easy to care for, requiring minimal watering and occasional pruning to maintain its shape.
