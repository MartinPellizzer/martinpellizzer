Santiria apiculata var. apiculata

Santiria apiculata var. apiculata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved santiria, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of pale yellow to greenish-yellow flowers, which are typically borne on short pedicels. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Santiria apiculata var. apiculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
