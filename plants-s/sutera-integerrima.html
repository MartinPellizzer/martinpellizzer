Sutera integerrima

Sutera integerrima, commonly known as the hardy hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its robust, upright growth habit and large, showy flowers that range from white to pink or red. The plant has heart-shaped, serrated leaves that are typically covered in a fine, downy hair. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners in temperate climates.

Sutera integerrima is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
