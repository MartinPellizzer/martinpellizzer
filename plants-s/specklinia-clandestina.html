Specklinia clandestina

Specklinia clandestina, commonly known as the hidden specklinia, is a rare and elusive orchid species native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, compact growth habit and striking, dark green leaves with a glossy texture. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, often hidden among the dense foliage. Specklinia clandestina thrives in shaded, humid environments with high air circulation.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is highly sought after by botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
