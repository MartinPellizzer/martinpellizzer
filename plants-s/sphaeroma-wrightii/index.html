Sphaeroma Wrightii

Sphaeroma wrightii is a small crustacean commonly found in coastal areas around the world. It belongs to the family Sphaeromatidae and is known for its unique, spherical shape. This species thrives in intertidal zones, where it can be found hiding under rocks or in crevices. Its ability to survive in harsh marine environments makes it an important part of the ecosystem. Despite its small size, Sphaeroma wrightii plays a significant role in nutrient cycling and habitat structure.
