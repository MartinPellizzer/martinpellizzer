Schoberia calceoliformis

Schoberia calceoliformis, commonly known as the fairy lantern, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has small, narrow leaves and produces seed pods that split open to release its seeds.

Schoberia calceoliformis is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
