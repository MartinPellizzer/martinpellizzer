Sideritis grandis

Sideritis grandis, commonly known as the large beard vetch, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its hairy stems and small, pink or purple flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Sideritis grandis is valued for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
