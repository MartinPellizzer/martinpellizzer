Stemona jinshajiangensis

Stemona jinshajiangensis, commonly known as the Jinshajiang stemona, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the family Piperaceae and is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers and has lance-shaped leaves with a smooth texture. Its roots are the primary part used medicinally, known for their bitter taste and various pharmacological properties.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.
