Scutellaria laevis

Scutellaria laevis, commonly known as the smooth skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland areas.

Scutellaria laevis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
