Schoberia pannonica

Schoberia pannonica, commonly known as the Pannonian bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to Central Europe.

It is characterized by its blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves and grows up to 30 cm in height. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Schoberia pannonica is often found in meadows and grasslands, contributing to the biodiversity of these habitats.
