Schismatoglottis scortechinii

Schismatoglottis scortechinii, commonly known as the Malagasy orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a raceme, typical of many orchid species. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and requires well-draining soil.

Schismatoglottis scortechinii is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
