Stoeberia utilis

Stoeberia utilis, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and bright yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Stoeberia utilis is also valued for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
