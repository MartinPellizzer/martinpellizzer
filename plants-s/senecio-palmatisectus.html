Senecio palmatisectus

Senecio palmatisectus, commonly known as the palmate senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive palm-like leaves, which are deeply lobed and arranged in a radiating pattern. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often cultivated in arid and semi-arid regions.
