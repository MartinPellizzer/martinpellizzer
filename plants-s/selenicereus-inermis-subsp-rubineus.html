Selenicereus inermis subsp. rubineus

Selenicereus inermis subsp.

rubineus, commonly known as the red-flowered night bloomer cactus, is a species native to parts of Central America. It is a climbing cactus that produces large, showy flowers that open at night.

The plant has a segmented stem with spines and is adapted to arid environments. Its flowers are typically a vibrant red or pink color and emit a strong fragrance.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and nocturnal blooming habit.
