Strophopappus speciosus

Strophopappus speciosus, commonly known as the showy pappus, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Strophopappus speciosus is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal in native plant gardens.
