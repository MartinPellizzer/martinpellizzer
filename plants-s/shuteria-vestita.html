Shuteria vestita

Shuteria vestita, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of the Americas. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, velvety hairs, giving it its common name. It produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.

Shuteria vestita is known for its adaptability and is often found in disturbed soils and agricultural areas.
