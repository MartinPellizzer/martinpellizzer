Solanum amethystinum

Solanum amethystinum, commonly known as the amethyst starfruit, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking purple and violet flowers, which contrast with its green foliage. The plant produces small, yellow-orange fruits that resemble star-shaped citrus fruits. It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
