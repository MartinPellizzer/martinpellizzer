Salacia zeyheri

Salacia zeyheri, commonly known as the Zeyher's salacia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Salaciacae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of rainforests.

The genus Salacia is known for its diverse species, many of which are used in traditional medicine.
