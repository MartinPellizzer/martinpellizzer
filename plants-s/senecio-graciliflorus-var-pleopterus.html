Senecio graciliflorus var. pleopterus

Senecio graciliflorus var. pleopterus, commonly known as the slenderflower senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, branching stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are typically yellow and occur in clusters, adding ornamental value.

It is often grown in gardens for its delicate appearance and drought tolerance.
