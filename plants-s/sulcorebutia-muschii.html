Sulcorebutia muschii

Sulcorebutia muschii, commonly known as the Muschii Candelabra Cactus, is a compact, branching cactus native to Argentina.

It features short, cylindrical stems that often branch out into a candelabra-like shape, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a vibrant touch to its structure. It is well adapted to arid environments, with thick, fleshy stems that store water.

Sulcorebutia muschii is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections due to its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
