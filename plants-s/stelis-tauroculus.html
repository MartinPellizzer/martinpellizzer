Stelis tauroculus

Stelis tauroculus, commonly known as the golden stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees in humid, shaded forest environments. The plant features a compact, branching structure with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and often yellow or orange in color.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
