Sedum jiulungshanense

Sedum jiulungshanense, commonly known as the Jiulungshan stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to China.

It is characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and small, clustered leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
