Staphylodendron pinnatum

Staphylodendron pinnatum, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a tropical plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, flame-like inflorescences that display vibrant red, orange, and yellow hues. The plant has compound leaves with pinnate arrangements, giving it a distinctive and ornamental appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its rapid growth and seasonal flowering make it a notable species in both horticultural and ecological contexts.
