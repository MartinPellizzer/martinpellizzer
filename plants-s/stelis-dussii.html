Stelis dussii

Stelis dussii, commonly known as the Dussi's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces spike-like inflorescences with multiple blooms, each featuring intricate, often colorful petals and sepals. Stelis dussii prefers shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical significance lies in its ornamental value and its role in the ecological diversity of its native habitats.
