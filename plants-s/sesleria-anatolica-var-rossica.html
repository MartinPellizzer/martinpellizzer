Sesleria anatolica var. rossica

Sesleria anatolica var. rossica, commonly known as the Anatolian rossica sedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to Turkey.

The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.
