Specklinia claviculata

Specklinia claviculata, commonly known as the club-stem orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive club-shaped stems, which are often covered in a dense layer of brownish or reddish hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, typically blooming during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in its natural habitat.

Due to its unique morphology and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
