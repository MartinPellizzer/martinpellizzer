Senecio dictyophyllus

Senecio dictyophyllus, commonly known as the African snowflake, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and finely divided leaves. The plant features a succulent, branching stem with small, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are narrow, serrated, and often covered in a fine layer of white hair.

Senecio dictyophyllus is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its fleshy stems and leaves.
