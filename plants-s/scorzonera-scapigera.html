Scorzonera scapigera

Scorzonera scapigera, commonly known as the scarlet pimpernel, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and covered in fine pubescence. Scorzonera scapigera is a biennial or short-lived perennial, typically reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters.
