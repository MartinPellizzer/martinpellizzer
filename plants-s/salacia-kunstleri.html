Salacia kunstleri

Salacia kunstleri, commonly known as the yellow salacia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Salicaceae, which includes willows and poplars. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and is often found in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are alternate and ovate, with a smooth texture.

Salacia kunstleri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
