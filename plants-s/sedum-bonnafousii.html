Sedum bonnafousii

Sedum bonnafousii, commonly known as the Bonnafous' stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink or purple. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it an attractive addition to xeriscapes.
