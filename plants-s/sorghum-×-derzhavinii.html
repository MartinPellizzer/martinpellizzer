Sorghum × derzhavinii

Sorghum × derzhavinii, commonly known as the hybrid sorghum, is a cross between sorghum and a related species, often used for its unique traits.

It is a tall, robust grass that can grow up to several meters in height, with narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces large, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple, which are attractive to pollinators. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions and thrives in warm, sunny climates.

Its botanical features make it suitable for both ornamental and agricultural purposes.
