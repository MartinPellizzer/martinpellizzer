Senecio erisithalifolius

Senecio erisithalifolius, commonly known as the purple coneflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking purple to violet flowers. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. It produces a tall, spiky flower head that attracts pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Senecio erisithalifolius is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
