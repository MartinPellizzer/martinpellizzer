Symplocos longiflora var. moyobambensis

Symplocos longiflora var. moyobambensis, commonly known as the Moyobamba wax tree, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Peru.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows in humid, lowland forests. The plant is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that contain several small, hard seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local communities.
