Senecio baberka

Senecio baberka, commonly known as the Baberka's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a cluster. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance.
