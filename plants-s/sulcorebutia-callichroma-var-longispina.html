Sulcorebutia callichroma var. longispina

Sulcorebutia callichroma var. longispina, commonly known as the Longispine Crassula, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, cylindrical stems covered in small, rounded leaves that give it a unique, almost spiky appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Its slow growth and striking foliage make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
