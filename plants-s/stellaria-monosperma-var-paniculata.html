Stellaria monosperma var. paniculata

Stellaria monosperma var. paniculata, commonly known as the panicled starwort, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It features small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a delicate and intricate appearance. The plant has slender, branching stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that help it thrive in open, disturbed habitats. It is often found in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

The species is noted for its ability to spread through seeds dispersed by wind, contributing to its wide distribution across its native range.
