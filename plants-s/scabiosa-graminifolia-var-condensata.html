Scabiosa graminifolia var. condensata

Scabiosa graminifolia var. condensata, commonly known as the dense scabious, is a low-growing perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically gray-green in color and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, pink or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or borders.

Its compact growth habit and attractive floral display make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens.
