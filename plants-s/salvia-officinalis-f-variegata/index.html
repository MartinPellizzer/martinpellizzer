Salvia Officinalis F. Variegata

Salvia officinalis f. variegata is a striking variant of the common sage plant known for its vibrant, variegated leaves. This ornamental cultivar features striking white and green foliage that adds visual interest to any garden or indoor space. While it shares the aromatic qualities of its parent plant, it is primarily grown for its aesthetic appeal rather than medicinal use. Its compact growth habit makes it ideal for container gardening or as a border plant. This unique form of sage is a popular choice among horticulturists and plant enthusiasts alike.
