Spergularia borealis

Spergularia borealis, commonly known as the Arctic willow, is a low-growing, mat-forming plant that thrives in cold, arctic and alpine environments.

It is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and pink or red flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant is adapted to harsh conditions, with a deep root system that helps it survive in nutrient-poor soils. It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small arctic organisms.
