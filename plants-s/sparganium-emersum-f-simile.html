Sparganium emersum f. simile

Sparganium emersum f. simile, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bur-reed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and spikes of yellowish-green flowers that appear in late summer. The plant typically grows in shallow water or moist soil, often found in wetlands and along the edges of ponds. Its fruits are small, spiky structures that aid in seed dispersal by attaching to animals or clothing.

This variety is distinguished by its narrower leaves compared to the typical form of Sparganium emersum.
