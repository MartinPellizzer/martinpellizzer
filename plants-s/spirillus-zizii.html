Spirillus zizii

Spirillus zizii, commonly known as the Chinese spirilla, is a unique plant species characterized by its spiral-shaped leaves.

It is native to parts of East Asia, particularly China, where it thrives in temperate climates. The plant's foliage exhibits a striking spiral pattern, which is a distinctive botanical feature. Spirillus zizii is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unusual leaf structure.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial sunlight, making it a suitable choice for garden landscapes.
