Senna paposana

Senna paposana, commonly known as the golden senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and the Middle East.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters tall, characterized by its bipinnate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. Senna paposana is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its bright, showy blooms.

It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun for optimal growth.
