Succisa glabrata

Succisa glabrata, commonly known as the smooth succis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and glossy. It produces small, tubular flowers in shades of purple and white, typically blooming in spring.

Succisa glabrata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
