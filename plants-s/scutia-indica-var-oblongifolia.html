Scutia indica var. oblongifolia

Scutia indica var. oblongifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf scutia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Asia.

It features compound leaves with elongated, elliptical leaflets that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically white or pale yellow, which bloom in the spring. Its growth habit is compact, making it suitable for ornamental use in gardens.

The species is valued for its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
