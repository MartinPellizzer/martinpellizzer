Scaphosepalum globosum

Scaphosepalum globosum, commonly known as the globular orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, rounded growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant has fleshy, strap-like leaves that are adapted to retain moisture in its native habitat. Its flowers are typically clustered in a dense inflorescence, and it blooms during the wet season.

Scaphosepalum globosum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
