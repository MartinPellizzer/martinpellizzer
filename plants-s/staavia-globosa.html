Staavia globosa

Staavia globosa, commonly known as the globe lily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped blooms that are often fragrant. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Staavia globosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively low maintenance once established.
