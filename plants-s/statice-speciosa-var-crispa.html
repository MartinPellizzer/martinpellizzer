Statice speciosa var. crispa

Statice speciosa var. crispa, commonly known as the spiked statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its dense, spike-like clusters of small, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with the flowers often displaying shades of pink, red, or white.

Statice speciosa var. crispa is also valued for its long-lasting blooms, which make it a favorite in floral arrangements.
