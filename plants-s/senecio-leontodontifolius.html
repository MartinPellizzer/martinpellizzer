Senecio leontodontifolius

Senecio leontodontifolius, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas. The plant features upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are bright yellow and are arranged in dense, daisy-like clusters.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
