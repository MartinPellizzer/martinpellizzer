Scilla lanceolata

Scilla lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceleaf scilla, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It produces clusters of bell-shaped, blue to purple flowers in early spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically evergreen in mild climates. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a suitable addition to rock gardens or woodland settings.

Scilla lanceolata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural displays.
