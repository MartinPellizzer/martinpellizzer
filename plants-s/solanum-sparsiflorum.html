Solanum sparsiflorum

Solanum sparsiflorum, commonly known as the sparsiflorum nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant is characterized by its hairy stems and clusters of small, white or yellowish flowers. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with a rough texture.

The fruit is a small, berry-like structure that ripens to a dark purple color.
