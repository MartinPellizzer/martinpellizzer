Sabia parviflora var. nitidissima

Sabia parviflora var. nitidissima, commonly known as the glossy sabia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are typically narrow and have a glossy, waxy surface, which gives the plant its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
