Stapelia kougabergensis

Stapelia kougabergensis, commonly known as the Kougaberg Stapelia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its unique, fleshy, and often ribbed stems. The plant produces striking, lantern-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy leaves and stems.

Stapelia kougabergensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and interesting morphology.
