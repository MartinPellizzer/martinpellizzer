Sinarundinaria delicata

Sinarundinaria delicata, commonly known as the delicate bamboo, is a perennial grass-like plant native to East Asia.

It features slender, hollow stems that are often used in traditional Chinese medicine. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically green and slightly hairy. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Sinarundinaria delicata is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in ecological restoration projects.
