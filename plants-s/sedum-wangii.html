Sedum wangii

Sedum wangii, commonly known as the Wang's stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to China.

It is characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and small, clustered leaves that are typically green or reddish in color. The plant produces yellow flowers in the summer, which are borne on short pedicels. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Sedum wangii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care.
