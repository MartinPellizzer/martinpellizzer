Scutellaria punctata

Scutellaria punctata, commonly known as the spotted skullcap, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and a distinctive blue or violet flower spike. Its flowers are small and tubular, often clustered in whorls along the stem.

Scutellaria punctata is valued for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
