Stephania yunnanensis var. trichocalyx

Stephania yunnanensis var. trichocalyx, commonly known as Yunnan stephania, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Menispermaceae and is characterized by its climbing habit and palmate leaves. The plant produces yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in terminal umbels. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The varietal name *trichocalyx* refers to the hairy calyx that surrounds its flowers.
