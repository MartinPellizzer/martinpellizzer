Satyrium wilmsianum

Satyrium wilmsianum, commonly known as the Wilmsian orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant has a slender, upright stem with a few leaves, and its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
