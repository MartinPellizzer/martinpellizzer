Silene drummondii var. striata

Silene drummondii var. striata, commonly known as Drummond's catchfly, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring.
