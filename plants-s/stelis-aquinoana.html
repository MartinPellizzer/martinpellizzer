Stelis aquinoana

Stelis aquinoana, commonly known as the Aquino's spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stem and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces striking, spider-like flowers with a distinctive coloration pattern. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
