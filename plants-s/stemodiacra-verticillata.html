Stemodiacra verticillata

Stemodiacra verticillata, commonly known as the spiral stem plant, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves around its stem, a feature that gives it its scientific name. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments and has small, inconspicuous flowers. Its stem is sturdy and often exhibits a reddish or purple hue.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and ease of cultivation.
