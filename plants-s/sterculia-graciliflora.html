Sterculia graciliflora

Sterculia graciliflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered sterculia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous tree or shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate or lanceolate in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, often with five petals.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release its seeds, which are often dispersed by wind.
