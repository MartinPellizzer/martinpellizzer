Synoecia diversifolia

Synoecia diversifolia, commonly known as the diverse-leaved synoecia, is a unique plant species characterized by its distinctive foliage.

It is native to specific regions in Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, tropical climates. The plant features compound leaves with varying shapes and sizes along its stems, contributing to its name. Its flowers are small and clustered, often producing a subtle fragrance.

Synoecia diversifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
