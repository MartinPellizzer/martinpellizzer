Sphaerocaryum elegans

Sphaerocaryum elegans, commonly known as the elegant globular thornapple, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its spherical, spiny fruit that resembles a small, ornamental apple. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Sphaerocaryum elegans is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
