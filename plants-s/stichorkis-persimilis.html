Stichorkis persimilis

Stichorkis persimilis, commonly known as the slender-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, often pendulous inflorescences and small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in humid, shaded environments. It is often found in forest understories and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
