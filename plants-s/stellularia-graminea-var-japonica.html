Stellularia graminea var. japonica

Stellularia graminea var. japonica, commonly known as Japanese starwort, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, and it produces small, star-shaped flowers in clusters. The plant is often found in moist environments such as wet meadows and stream banks. It has a fibrous root system and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique addition to aquatic and bog garden landscapes.
