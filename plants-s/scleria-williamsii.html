Scleria williamsii

Scleria williamsii, commonly known as the Williams' speargrass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences.
