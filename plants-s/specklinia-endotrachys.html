Specklinia endotrachys

Specklinia endotrachys, commonly known as the golden rosette.

This epiphytic orchid is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit. It features lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of pale yellow to white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green, typically arranged in a spike.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value.
