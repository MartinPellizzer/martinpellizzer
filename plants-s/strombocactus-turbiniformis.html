Strombocactus turbiniformis

Strombocactus turbiniformis, commonly known as the Turbiniform Strombocactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems that are typically green to blue-green in color. The plant produces large, showy yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It has a spiny exterior with areoles that bear both spines and flowers.

Strombocactus turbiniformis is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.
