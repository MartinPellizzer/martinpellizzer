Silene ungeri

Silene ungeri, commonly known as the Unger's campion, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family, characterized by its showy, pink or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
