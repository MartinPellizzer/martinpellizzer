Spinacia oleracea subsp. orientalis

Spinacia oleracea subsp.

orientalis, commonly known as the oriental spinach, is a subspecies of the common spinach plant. It is characterized by its broad, dark green leaves that are slightly heart-shaped and have a mild, slightly sweet flavor.

This plant is native to the Middle East and is well adapted to warmer climates. It thrives in cool seasons and requires consistent moisture for optimal growth.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both home gardens and commercial cultivation.
