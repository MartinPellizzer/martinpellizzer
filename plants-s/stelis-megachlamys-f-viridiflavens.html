Stelis megachlamys f. viridiflavens

Stelis megachlamys f. viridiflavens, commonly known as the green-yellow stelis, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers with a unique coloration pattern. The plant typically features linear, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette formation. Native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
