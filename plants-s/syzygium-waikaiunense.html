Syzygium waikaiunense

Syzygium waikaiunense, commonly known as the Kauai apple or Kauai hibiscus, is a rare endemic species native to the island of Kauai in Hawaii.

It is a small evergreen shrub or tree that typically grows in moist, shaded habitats within the native forests of the island. The plant features elliptical to ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy on the upper surface. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with five petals and a prominent central stigma.

The fruit is a small, edible berry that is typically red or orange when mature.
