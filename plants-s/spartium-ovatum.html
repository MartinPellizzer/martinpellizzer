Spartium ovatum

Spartium ovatum, commonly known as narrowleaf spartium, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its seed pods are slender and hairy, containing several oblong seeds.

Spartium ovatum is often found in dry, rocky soils and is used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
