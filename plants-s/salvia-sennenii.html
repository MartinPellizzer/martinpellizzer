Salvia sennenii

Salvia sennenii, commonly known as the Mexican sage, is a perennial shrub native to Mexico.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and it thrives in well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Salvia sennenii is also valued for its aromatic properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
