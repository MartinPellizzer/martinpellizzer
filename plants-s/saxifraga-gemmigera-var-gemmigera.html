Saxifraga gemmigera var. gemmigera

Saxifraga gemmigera var. gemmigera, commonly known as the gemmy saxifrage, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. The plant features upright, branching stems with opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, pinkish-white, and clustered in dense inflorescences, blooming in early summer.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in alpine gardens.
