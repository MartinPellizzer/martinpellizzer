Stellarioides nathoana

Stellarioides nathoana, commonly known as the Natal starwort, is a small, herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices. The plant features delicate, star-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and white, arranged in clusters, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Stellarioides nathoana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
