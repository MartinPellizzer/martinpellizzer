Solanum dasyphyllum var. transiens

Solanum dasyphyllum var. transiens, commonly known as the variable nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant features hairy stems and oval-shaped leaves with a slightly serrated edge. Its flowers are typically yellow or greenish-yellow, and it produces small, round berries that are toxic to humans and animals.

Due to its variability in appearance, it is often challenging to identify without specialized knowledge.
