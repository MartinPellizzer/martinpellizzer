Scrophularia exilis

Scrophularia exilis, commonly known as slender scrophularia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Scrophulariaceae.

It is native to regions of Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple in color, which attract various pollinators like bees and butterflies. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Scrophularia exilis is generally a herbaceous perennial, growing to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters.
