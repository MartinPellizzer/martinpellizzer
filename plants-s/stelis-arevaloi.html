Stelis arevaloi

Stelis arevaloi, commonly known as the Arevalo's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of small, white to pale pink blooms that are adorned with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Stelis arevaloi is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
