Scrophularia eriocalyx var. foliosa

Scrophularia eriocalyx var. foliosa, commonly known as the broad-leaved scrophularia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in woodland edges or along stream banks. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy and have a distinctive basal rosette. Its flowers are tubular, with two lips, and are usually pink or purple in color.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae, which includes many species with similar floral structures and ecological preferences.
