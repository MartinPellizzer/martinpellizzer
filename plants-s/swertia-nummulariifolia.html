Swertia nummulariifolia

Swertia nummulariifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its silver-gray, waxy leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, blue or violet flowers during the summer months, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.

Swertia nummulariifolia is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
