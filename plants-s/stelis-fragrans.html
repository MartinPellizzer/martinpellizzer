Stelis fragrans

Stelis fragrans, commonly known as the fragrant stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, pendulous flower spikes. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic growth habit. Its flowers are fragrant and usually white or pale yellow, with a pleasant scent that attracts pollinators.

Stelis fragrans is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and unique fragrance.
