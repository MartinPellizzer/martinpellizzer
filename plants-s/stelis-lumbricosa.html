Stelis lumbricosa

Stelis lumbricosa, commonly known as the ground orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to tropical regions of Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features a short, upright stem with a single or few flowers that are usually white or pale pink. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to anchor in loose soil, while its leaves are narrow and grass-like.

Stelis lumbricosa is known for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated in botanical gardens for its ornamental value.
