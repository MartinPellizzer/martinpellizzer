Syzygium ixoroides

Syzygium ixoroides, commonly known as the Java rose or red clydesdale, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and showy, fragrant flowers that range from white to pink. The plant produces small, round fruits that are edible and often used in traditional medicine. Syzygium ixoroides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and as a hedge plant.

Its growth habit and adaptability make it a popular choice in warm, humid climates.
