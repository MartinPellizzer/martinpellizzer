Sternbergia angustifolia

Sternbergia angustifolia, commonly known as the narrowleaf star of Persia, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of star-shaped, yellow flowers in the spring. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and ability to thrive in well-drained soils. It is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats, preferring full sun to partial shade.

Sternbergia angustifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its delicate, early blooming flowers.
