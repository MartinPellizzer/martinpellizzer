Solanum indicum var. multiflorum

Solanum indicum var. multiflorum, commonly known as the Indian nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, white or greenish-white flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a rough texture.

The fruit is a small, fleshy berry that turns red when mature, containing several seeds.
