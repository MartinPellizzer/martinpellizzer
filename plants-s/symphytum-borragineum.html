Symphytum borragineum

Symphytum borragineum, commonly known as purple bugloss, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of blue to purple, hairy leaves and tall spikes of blue or violet flowers. The plant belongs to the Boraginaceae family, known for its medicinal and ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for gardens and ecological restoration projects.
