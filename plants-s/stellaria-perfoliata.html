Stellaria perfoliata

Stellaria perfoliata, commonly known as mouse ear or starwort, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches heights of 5 to 30 centimeters. The plant features opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that often have a hairy texture. Its small white or pink flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom from spring to early summer.

Stellaria perfoliata is often found in grassy areas, along roadsides, and in disturbed soils.
