Stipagrostis uniplumis var. uniplumis

Stipagrostis uniplumis var. uniplumis, commonly known as the narrowleaf grass, is a perennial grass species native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically covered in a fine layer of silky hairs. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It is adapted to survive in harsh environments with low rainfall and high temperatures.

Its botanical features make it an important component of grassland ecosystems in its native range.
