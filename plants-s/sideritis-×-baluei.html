Sideritis × baluei

Sideritis × baluei, commonly known as the hybrid sainfoin, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. The plant features square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It is often found in rocky or dry habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.

Sideritis × baluei is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
