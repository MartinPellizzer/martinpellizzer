Sulcorebutia menesesii

Sulcorebutia menesesii, commonly known as the Menesesii Pincushion Cactus, is a small, compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem with a ribbed structure that helps it store water efficiently. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a vibrant touch to its desert environment. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid climates.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
