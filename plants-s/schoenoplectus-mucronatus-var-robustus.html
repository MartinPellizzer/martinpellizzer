Schoenoplectus mucronatus var. robustus

Schoenoplectus mucronatus var. robustus, commonly known as the robust bulrush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland environments in North America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters, with narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in late summer, typically in shades of greenish-white. It thrives in shallow water or saturated soil, making it an important species for wetland ecosystems.

Its dense growth helps stabilize soil and provides habitat for various aquatic and terrestrial organisms.
