Senecio tubicaulis

Senecio tubicaulis, commonly known as the tubular senecio, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a dense inflorescence, which attracts pollinators like bees and butterflies. Its scientific name reflects its unique tubular structure, and it is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance.

Senecio tubicaulis requires well-draining soil and minimal water to prevent root rot.
