Stemonacanthus macrophyllus

Stemonacanthus macrophyllus, commonly known as the giant leaf stemonacanthus, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to several feet in diameter. The plant produces small, white flowers arranged in clusters, which are often followed by elongated, spiny fruits. Its foliage is thick and waxy, which helps it retain moisture in its humid, tropical habitat.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its dramatic leaf size and unique appearance.
