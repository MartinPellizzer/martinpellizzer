Spiniluma discolor

Spiniluma discolor, commonly known as the Variable Spineflower, is a unique plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its spiny, succulent stems and small, often colorful, fleshy leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that can range in color from white to pink or purple. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for xerophytic environments.

Due to its striking appearance and adaptability, it is often cultivated in rock gardens and as an ornamental plant.
