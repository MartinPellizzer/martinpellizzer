Solanum compressibaccatum

Solanum compressibaccatum, commonly known as the compressed leathery nightshade, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically green when immature and turn red when ripe. It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments.

Due to its toxic properties, it is important to handle the plant with care.
