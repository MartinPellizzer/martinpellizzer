Saccharum kanashiroi

Saccharum kanashiroi, commonly known as the Japanese sugarcane.

This plant is a member of the grass family Poaceae and is native to Japan. It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and can grow up to several meters in height. The leaves are long and narrow, with a bluish-green color.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional Japanese gardens.
