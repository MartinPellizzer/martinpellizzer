Stapelia variegata var. planiflora

Stapelia variegata var. planiflora, commonly known as the Variegated Stapelia, is a unique succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems that are often covered in a waxy, pale green or yellowish coating. The plant produces striking, lantern-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are typically small, narrow, and arranged in a rosette pattern.

This variety is well adapted to arid environments, making it a low-maintenance choice for succulent enthusiasts.
