Scaphyglottis sessilis

Scaphyglottis sessilis, commonly known as the sessile lip orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, lip-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Scaphyglottis sessilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its delicate floral display.
