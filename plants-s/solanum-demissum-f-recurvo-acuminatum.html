Solanum demissum f. recurvo-acuminatum

Solanum demissum f. recurvo-acuminatum, commonly known as the curly nightshade, is a variant of the Solanum demissum species.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features distinctive, curling leaves that give it its common name. It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters.

The botanical characteristics of this plant include its unique leaf morphology and its classification within the Solanaceae family.
