Schivereckia imeretica

Schivereckia imeretica, commonly known as the Imeretian bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region, particularly Georgia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or mountainous habitats. The plant features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are usually blue or violet in color. Its leaves are lanceolate and often covered in fine hairs.

Schivereckia imeretica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
