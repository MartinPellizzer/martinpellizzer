Swertia cuneata var. asterocalyx

Swertia cuneata var. asterocalyx, commonly known as the star-flowered swertia, is a perennial herb native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, lanceolate leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers with five petals, which are often arranged in a star-like pattern, giving it its common name. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is typically found at high altitudes.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential pharmacological properties.
