Scaphyglottis tenuis

Scaphyglottis tenuis, commonly known as the slender-lipped orchid, is a herbaceous perennial native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers with elongated petals. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as under forest canopies, where it thrives in partial to full shade. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, while its flowers are usually pale green to white in color, with a distinctive elongated lip.

Scaphyglottis tenuis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
