Solenopsis mothiana

Solenopsis mothiana, commonly known as the moth orchid, is a species of orchid native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in humid, well-drained environments.

Solenopsis mothiana is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
