Schefflera huachamacarii

Schefflera huachamacarii, commonly known as the Huachamacarii umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant.

Its foliage is glossy and dark green, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
