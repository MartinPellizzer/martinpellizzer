Stenolobium velutinum

Stenolobium velutinum, commonly known as the velvet senna, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its typical pod fruit and compound leaves. The plant features hairy, ovate leaves that are often arranged alternately along the stems. Its yellow flowers are clustered in racemes and attract various pollinators.

The seeds are enclosed in elongated pods and are dispersed by wind or animals.
