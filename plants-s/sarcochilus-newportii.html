Sarcochilus newportii

Sarcochilus newportii, commonly known as the Newport's sarcochilus, is a terrestrial orchid native to New Zealand.

It features a thick, fleshy rhizome and produces striking, dark green to purple flowers with yellow lips. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rock crevices. Its flowers are usually borne on erect, hairy stems and bloom in late summer to early autumn.

Sarcochilus newportii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with proper humidity and drainage.
