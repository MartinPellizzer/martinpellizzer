Stenorrhynchos berroanum

Stenorrhynchos berroanum, commonly known as the Berroa orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly found in regions of Brazil, Peru, and Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stem and small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in montane forests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
