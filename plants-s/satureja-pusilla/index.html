Satureja Pusilla

Satureja pusilla is a small, herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, known for its aromatic and often medicinal properties. This species is characterized by its tiny, compact stature and distinctive, fragrant leaves. Despite its modest size, it plays an important ecological role in its native habitat. Its unique features make it a fascinating subject for botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
