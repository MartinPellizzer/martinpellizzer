Specklinia bowmanni

Specklinia bowmanni, commonly known as the Bowmann's cycad, is a unique member of the Cycadaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia. This plant is characterized by its stout, unbranched trunk and a crown of pinnate, feather-like leaves. The leaves are typically a deep green color and can grow quite large, adding a striking visual presence to its habitat.

Specklinia bowmanni is known for its slow growth and is often cultivated as a ornamental plant due to its distinctive appearance.
