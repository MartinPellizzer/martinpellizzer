Scaevola dielliana

Scaevola dielliana, commonly known as the coastal soapplant, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of Australia.

It is a low-growing shrub with small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has silver-gray foliage and is adapted to sandy, well-drained soils. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to withstand salt spray and strong winds.
