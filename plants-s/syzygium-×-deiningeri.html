Syzygium × deiningeri

Syzygium × deiningeri, commonly known as the hybrid satinwood, is a species within the Myrtaceae family.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows to a height of 5–10 meters. The plant is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and fragrant white or pink flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a small, smooth, red or yellow drupe that contains a single seed.

Syzygium × deiningeri is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia and is valued for its ornamental qualities and timber.
