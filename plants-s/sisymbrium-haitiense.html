Sisymbrium haitiense

Sisymbrium haitiense, commonly known as Haitian shepherd's purse, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean island of Haiti.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant features small, yellow flowers and heart-shaped leaves that are characteristic of the genus Sisymbrium. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.
