Sophora longicarinata

Sophora longicarinata, commonly known as the long-leaf sophora, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is known for its ornamental value, with its delicate foliage and showy blooms. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal.

The seeds of Sophora longicarinata are encased in pods and can be dispersed by wind or animals.
