Salaxis octandra

Salaxis octandra, commonly known as the yellow salaxis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its showy, orange-yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are typically oval in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.
