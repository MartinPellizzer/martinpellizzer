Sulcorebutia canigueralii subsp. brevispina

Sulcorebutia canigueralii subsp.

brevispina, commonly known as the short-spined sun rose, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to the arid regions of Argentina. It features a cylindrical stem with a ribbed structure that helps it store water efficiently.

The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a striking visual element to its appearance. Its spines are short and white, providing protection while maintaining an aesthetic appeal.

This species is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
