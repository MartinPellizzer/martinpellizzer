Scalesia pedunculata var. svensonii

Scalesia pedunculata var. svensonii, commonly known as the Santa Cruz giant hibiscus, is a striking member of the Malvaceae family native to the Galápagos Islands.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is a key species in the Galápagos ecosystem.
