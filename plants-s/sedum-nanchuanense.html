Sedum nanchuanense

Sedum nanchuanense, commonly known as the Nanchuan stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to China.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in rocky or well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens and rockeries.
