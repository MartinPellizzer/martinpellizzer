Schefflera marcellana

Schefflera marcellana, commonly known as the Marcellana umbrella plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of leaves, which are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by bright red berries, attracting birds and other wildlife. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Its adaptability to various light conditions makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
