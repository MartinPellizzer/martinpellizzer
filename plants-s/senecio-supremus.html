Senecio supremus

Senecio supremus, commonly known as the giant senecio, is a striking herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, branching stems and large, serrated leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom in late summer and early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
