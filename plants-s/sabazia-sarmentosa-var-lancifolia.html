Sabazia sarmentosa var. lancifolia

Sabazia sarmentosa var. lancifolia, commonly known as the slender-stemmed sabazia, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
