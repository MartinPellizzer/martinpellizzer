Satureja × delpozoi

Satureja × delpozoi, commonly known as Greek thyme, is a hybrid shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic leaves and square stems. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are often used in culinary applications due to their pungent, thyme-like aroma.

Satureja × delpozoi thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy perennial suitable for rock gardens and borders.
