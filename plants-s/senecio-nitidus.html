Senecio nitidus

Senecio nitidus, commonly known as the smoothleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features narrow, smooth, dark green leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a cluster. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Senecio nitidus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
