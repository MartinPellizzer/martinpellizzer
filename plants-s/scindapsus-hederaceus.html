Scindapsus hederaceus

Scindapsus hederaceus, commonly known as the silver vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that often have a silvery or variegated pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Scindapsus hederaceus is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
