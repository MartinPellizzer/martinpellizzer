Solena rigida

Solena rigida, commonly known as rigid solena, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as a perennial herb with erect, branching stems. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in clusters.

Solena rigida is known for its ability to thrive in well-drained soils and full sun exposure.
