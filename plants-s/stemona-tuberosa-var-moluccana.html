Stemona tuberosa var. moluccana

Stemona tuberosa var. moluccana, commonly known as the Moluccan stemona, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and clusters of small, white to pale green flowers. The plant produces tuberous roots that are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
