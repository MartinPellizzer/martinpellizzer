Senecio geniculipes

Senecio geniculipes, commonly known as the zigzag groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its unique zigzagging stem and dark green, hairy leaves. The plant produces small yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom from late spring to early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is widely cultivated in arid regions around the world.
