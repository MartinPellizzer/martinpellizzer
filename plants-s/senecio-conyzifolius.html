Senecio conyzifolius

Senecio conyzifolius, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and thistles. The plant features showy yellow flower heads surrounded by white ray florets, making it visually striking. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
