Stachys germanica var. boissieri

Stachys germanica var. boissieri, commonly known as the German hedge nettle, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Its leaves are covered in fine hairs, giving them a fuzzy texture and aiding in water retention.
