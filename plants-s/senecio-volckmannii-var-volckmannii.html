Senecio volckmannii var. volckmannii

Senecio volckmannii var. volckmannii, commonly known as the Volckmann's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow daisy-like flowers and linear, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is known for its compact growth habit and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

The species is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
