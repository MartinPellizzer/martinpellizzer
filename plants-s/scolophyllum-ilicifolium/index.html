Scolophyllum Ilicifolium

Scolophyllum ilicifolium is a rare and intriguing plant native to the Canary Islands. It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is known for its unique appearance and ecological significance. The plant features leathery leaves and small, clustered flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in rocky and coastal environments, showcasing remarkable adaptability. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a species of conservation concern.
