Scutellaria barbata

Scutellaria barbata, commonly known as the hairy skullcap, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of North America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular blue or purple flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and stream banks.

Scutellaria barbata is noted for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its potential health benefits.
