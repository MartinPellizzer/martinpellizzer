Specklinia cycesis

Specklinia cycesis, commonly known as the golden rosette.

This rare orchid is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, where it thrives in cool, humid environments. It is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit and striking golden-green foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, typically during the spring and early summer.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, Specklinia cycesis is considered a delicate and sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
