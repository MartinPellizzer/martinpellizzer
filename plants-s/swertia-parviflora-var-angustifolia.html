Swertia parviflora var. angustifolia

Swertia parviflora var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved gentian, is a flowering plant belonging to the Gentianaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in summer. Native to the Himalayas, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes. Its medicinal properties have been traditionally used in herbal remedies.

The plant's botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and pharmacological studies.
