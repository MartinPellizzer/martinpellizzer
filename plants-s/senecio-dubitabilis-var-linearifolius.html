Senecio dubitabilis var. linearifolius

Senecio dubitabilis var. linearifolius, commonly known as the linear-leaved groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and small yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its distinctive foliage and delicate blooms.
