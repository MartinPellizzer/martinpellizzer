Scabiosa graminifolia var. pyrenaica

Scabiosa graminifolia var. pyrenaica, commonly known as the Pyrenean ladies' mantle, is a perennial herb native to the Pyrenees region.

It features basal leaves that are fern-like and covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft, velvety appearance. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers in clustered umbels, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained, calcareous soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate foliage and flowers.
