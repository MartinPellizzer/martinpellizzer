Spilanthes arnicoides f. grisea

Spilanthes arnicoides f. grisea, commonly known as the gray-flowered spilanthes, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features grayish-green, hairy leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer. It is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks.

The species is noted for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
