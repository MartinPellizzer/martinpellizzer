Senecio purpurascens

Senecio purpurascens, commonly known as the purple ragwort, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant purple to yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has serrated, hairy leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of down. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Due to its striking appearance, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
