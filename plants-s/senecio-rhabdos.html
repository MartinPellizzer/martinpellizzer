Senecio rhabdos

Senecio rhabdos, commonly known as the woolly senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly gray foliage and produces small, yellow flowers in clusters. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy succulent suitable for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
