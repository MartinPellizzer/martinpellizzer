Syzygium burkillianum

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom during the wet season. Its fruit is a small, round, yellowish drupe that contains a single seed.
