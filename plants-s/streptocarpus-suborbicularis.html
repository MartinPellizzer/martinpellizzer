Streptocarpus suborbicularis

Streptocarpus suborbicularis, commonly known as the broad-leaf streptocarpus, is a species native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its broad, rounded leaves and clusters of tubular flowers. The leaves are typically dark green and slightly hairy, while the flowers range in color from white to pale purple. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This plant is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
