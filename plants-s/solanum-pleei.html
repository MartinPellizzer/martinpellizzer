Solanum pleei

Solanum pleei, commonly known as the plei nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or vine, featuring hairy stems and alternate leaves. Its flowers are small and usually white or purple, followed by berries that mature to a yellow or orange color.

Solanum pleei is often found in moist, shaded environments and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.
