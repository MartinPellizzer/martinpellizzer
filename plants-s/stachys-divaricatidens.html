Stachys divaricatidens

Stachys divaricatidens, commonly known as the variable hedge nettle, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, pale purple to white flowers arranged in whorls along the stem. Its leaves are typically opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

Stachys divaricatidens is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of light conditions.
