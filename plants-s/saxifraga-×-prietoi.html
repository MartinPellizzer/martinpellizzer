Saxifraga × prietoi

Saxifraga × prietoi, commonly known as the hybrid saxifrage, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pinkish flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are typically opposite, ovate, and covered in a fine layer of white hair.

Saxifraga × prietoi thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine environments.
