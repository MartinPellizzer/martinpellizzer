Sideritis hyssopifolia f. angustifolia

Sideritis hyssopifolia f. angustifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaved ironwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and spikes of small, pale blue to violet flowers. The plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in rocky or sandy soils. It is often found in dry, sunny habitats such as cliffs and slopes.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ornamental species within its native range.
