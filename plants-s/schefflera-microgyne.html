Schefflera microgyne

Schefflera microgyne, commonly known as the small-leaf umbrella plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of palmate leaves, which are typically small and glossy. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellowish-green flowers followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in humid, warm environments and is often cultivated as a ornamental plant.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and landscape design.
