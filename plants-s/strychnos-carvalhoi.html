Strychnos carvalhoi

Strychnos carvalhoi, commonly known as the Carvalho's bitter apple, is a small evergreen shrub native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Loganiaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and showy, yellowish-white flowers. Its ornamental value and medicinal potential have led to interest in its cultivation and study.
