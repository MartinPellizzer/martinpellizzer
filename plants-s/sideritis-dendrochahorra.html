Sideritis dendrochahorra

Sideritis dendrochahorra, commonly known as the tree senna, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or dry habitats.

Sideritis dendrochahorra is valued for its medicinal properties and ornamental appeal.
