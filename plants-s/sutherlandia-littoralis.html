Sutherlandia littoralis

Sutherlandia littoralis, commonly known as coastal cancer bush, is a South African plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is a shrub characterized by its yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant thrives in coastal and sandy environments, often found along the Atlantic coastline. Its roots are used in traditional medicine for their purported anti-cancer properties.

However, it is important to note that scientific evidence supporting these claims remains limited.
