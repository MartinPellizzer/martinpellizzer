Sophora nuttalliana

Sophora nuttalliana, commonly known as the Nuttall's Sophora, is a deciduous leguminous shrub or small tree native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are pinnately arranged and have a feathery appearance. The plant produces showy, pea-like flowers that are typically blue or purple in color, blooming in early summer. Its fruit is a legume pod containing several seeds, and it has a deep root system that helps prevent soil erosion.

Sophora nuttalliana is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological benefits in native landscapes.
