Stellaria media var. ovalifolia

Stellaria media var. ovalifolia, commonly known as common chickweed or mouse-ear chickweed, is a low-growing annual herbaceous plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It features small, white star-shaped flowers and has oval or ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, gardens, and along paths. Its stems are prostrate or ascending, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

It is often considered a weed but has historical uses in herbal medicine.
