Scorzoneroides × lannesii

Scorzoneroides × lannesii, commonly known as the hybrid sunflower.

This plant is a natural hybrid between Scorzoneroides glabra and Scorzoneroides lannesii, resulting in a species with unique botanical characteristics. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. The plant features tall, slender stems with large, daisy-like yellow flowers.

Its hybrid nature contributes to its adaptability and resilience in various environmental conditions.
