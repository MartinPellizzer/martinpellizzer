Sipapoa steyermarkii

Sipapoa steyermarkii, commonly known as the Steyermark's torch cactus, is a species native to southern Africa.

It is a small, spineless cactus that typically grows to a height of about 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant features a short, thick stem with a single, elongated flowering stem that produces striking yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
