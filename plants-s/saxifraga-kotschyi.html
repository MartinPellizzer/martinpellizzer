Saxifraga kotschyi

Saxifraga kotschyi, commonly known as the Kotschyi's saxifrage, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing habit and fleshy, often succulent leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, which bloom in the spring. It thrives in rocky or alpine environments, where it is adapted to harsh climatic conditions.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and specialized species.
