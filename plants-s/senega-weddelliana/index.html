Senega Weddelliana

Senega weddelliana is a rare and striking plant native to the high altitudes of South America. It belongs to the Apiaceae family, known for its aromatic and often edible members. This species is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and small, clustered white flowers. Due to its limited habitat and threatened status, it is highly sought after by botanists and conservationists. Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it a fascinating subject for study and protection.
