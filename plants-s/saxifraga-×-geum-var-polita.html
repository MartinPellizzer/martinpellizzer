Saxifraga × geum var. polita

Saxifraga × geum var. polita, commonly known as the variegated garden saxifrage, is a hybrid plant that exhibits a variety of leaf colors, including green, white, and pink.

It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in moist, well-drained soils, often found in woodland or shady garden environments. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink or red during the summer months. Its foliage is characterized by a distinctive variegation pattern, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

It is relatively hardy and can tolerate partial to full shade, making it versatile for different garden settings.
