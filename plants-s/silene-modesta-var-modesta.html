Silene modesta var. modesta

Silene modesta var. modesta, commonly known as the modest silene, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in temperate climates. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are often hairy and have a bluish-green hue. Its flowers are small, pink to purple in color, and are clustered in dense racemes.

Silene modesta var. modesta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
