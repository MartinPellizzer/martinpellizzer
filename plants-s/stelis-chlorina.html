Stelis chlorina

Stelis chlorina, commonly known as the green stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, erect stem and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered at the base of the stem. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Stelis chlorina is appreciated for its unique appearance and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value.
