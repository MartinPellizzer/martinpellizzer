Selago bilacunosa

Selago bilacunosa, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia and Australia.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, reflective hairs. Its small, white or pale blue flowers bloom in clusters, often during the spring and summer months.
