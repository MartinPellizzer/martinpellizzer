Swertia deltoidea

Swertia deltoidea, commonly known as the delta gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or forested areas and prefers cool, moist environments. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette.

Swertia deltoidea is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
