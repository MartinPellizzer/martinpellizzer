Stenorrhynchos calophyllum

Stenorrhynchos calophyllum, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a fiery appearance. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually oval or elliptic in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Stenorrhynchos calophyllum is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
