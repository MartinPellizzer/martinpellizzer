Solanum platycypellon

Solanum platycypellon, commonly known as the broad-leaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant features small, yellow flowers and produces berries that are typically green when immature and turn red when ripe. Its leaves are broad and ovate, with a slightly hairy texture.

Solanum platycypellon is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.
