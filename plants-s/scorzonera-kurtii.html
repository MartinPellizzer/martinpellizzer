Scorzonera kurtii

Scorzonera kurtii, commonly known as the Kashmiri scorzonera, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, thriving in cool and moist conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a distinctive hairy texture.
