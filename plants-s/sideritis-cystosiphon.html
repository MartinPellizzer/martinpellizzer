Sideritis cystosiphon

Sideritis cystosiphon, commonly known as Greek catnip, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that are typically pale blue or violet in color. Its leaves are often aromatic and have been used in traditional herbal medicine for their calming properties.

Sideritis cystosiphon thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous areas.
