Solanum longipilum

Solanum longipilum, commonly known as the hairy nightshade, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is native to Australia and is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into berries containing toxic seeds. Solanum longipilum thrives in a variety of habitats, including grasslands and disturbed soils.

Due to its toxic properties, it is important to handle the plant with care.
