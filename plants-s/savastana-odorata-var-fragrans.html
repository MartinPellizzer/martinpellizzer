Savastana odorata var. fragrans

Savastana odorata var. fragrans, commonly known as the fragrant savastana, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its aromatic, showy flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, greenish-white petals with a strong, sweet fragrance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant scent.
