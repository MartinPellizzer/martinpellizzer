Suregada ceratophora

Suregada ceratophora, commonly known as the horned violets, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Violaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its distinctive, hairy leaves and small, violet-colored flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
