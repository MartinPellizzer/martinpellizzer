Simira wurdackii

Simira wurdackii, commonly known as the Amazonian white cedar, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to the rainforests of South America.

Due to its slow growth and limited range, Simira wurdackii is considered a valuable but rare species in its native habitat.
