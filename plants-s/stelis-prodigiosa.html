Stelis prodigiosa

Stelis prodigiosa, commonly known as the magnificent stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing on tree trunks or branches. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, which bloom in clusters. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Stelis prodigiosa is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts worldwide.
