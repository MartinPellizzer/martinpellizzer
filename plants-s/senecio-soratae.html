Senecio soratae

Senecio soratae, commonly known as the Desert Senecio, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub with silver-gray foliage that helps it retain moisture in harsh environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to full sun, making it a hardy choice for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it an interesting addition to succulent gardens.
