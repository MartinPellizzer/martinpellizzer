Saxifraga longshengensis

Saxifraga longshengensis, commonly known as the Longsheng saxifrage, is a flowering plant species native to China.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming growth habit. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pink in color. It thrives in rocky or alpine environments, preferring well-drained soils and cool climates.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
