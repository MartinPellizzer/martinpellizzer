Sideritis hirsuta var. flaviflora

Sideritis hirsuta var. flaviflora, commonly known as the yellowflowered ironwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and aromatic foliage. The plant features small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to yellow, typically blooming in summer. Its leaves are hairy and gray-green, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Sideritis species are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
