Stachytarpheta oblongifolia

Stachytarpheta oblongifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf purpletop, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with purple or violet flowers, which attract various pollinators. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
