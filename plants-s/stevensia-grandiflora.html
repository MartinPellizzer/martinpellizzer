Stevensia grandiflora

Stevensia grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered stevensia, is a flowering plant native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its leaves are elliptical, leathery, and have a glossy texture.

Stevensia grandiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses due to its striking blooms and hardy nature.
