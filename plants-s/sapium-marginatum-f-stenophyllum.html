Sapium marginatum f. stenophyllum

Sapium marginatum f. stenophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Chinese tallowtree, is a variant of the Chinese tallowtree characterized by its narrower leaves.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia, particularly China. The plant features simple, alternate leaves with a distinctive yellowish-green margin. Its flowers are small and clustered in catkins, and it produces woody, capsule-like fruits.

This cultivar is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various soil types.
