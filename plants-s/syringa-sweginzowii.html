Syringa sweginzowii

Syringa sweginzowii, commonly known as the Chinese lilac, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, clustered flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant produces small, oval leaves that are typically dark green and have a slightly hairy texture. Syringa sweginzowii thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its showy blossoms and adaptability to various climates.
