Symplocos Macrocarpa Subsp. Kanarana

Symplocos macrocarpa subsp. kanarana is a unique plant species native to the tropical regions of India and Sri Lanka. It belongs to the Ericaceae family, commonly known as the heath or evergreen shrub family. This subspecies is distinguished by its large, showy berries and dense, evergreen foliage. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it has gained interest among botanists and horticulturalists alike.
