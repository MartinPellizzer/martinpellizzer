Sobralia wercklei

Sobralia wercklei, commonly known as the Werckle's sobralia, is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. This plant is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The leaves are typically broad and glossy, providing a lush green backdrop to the vibrant blossoms.

It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to flourish.
