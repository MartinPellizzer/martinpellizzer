Stelis ascendens

Stelis ascendens, commonly known as the ascending stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with a distinctive lip that aids in pollination.

Stelis ascendens is valued in horticulture for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
