Sutera dubia

Sutera dubia, commonly known as the slender mallow, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, pale pink or white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. Sutera dubia is adapted to dry, sunny environments and has a deep root system to access water.

It is also known for its aromatic foliage, which has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
