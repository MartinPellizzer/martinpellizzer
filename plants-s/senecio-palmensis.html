Senecio palmensis

Senecio palmensis, commonly known as the desert palm, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a tall, unbranched stem that resembles a palm, covered in dense, spiny leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, followed by distinctive, spherical seed heads. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its fleshy stem.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
