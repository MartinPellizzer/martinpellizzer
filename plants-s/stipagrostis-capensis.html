Stipagrostis capensis

Stipagrostis capensis, commonly known as the Cape grass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, linear leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous tiny florets. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal and inland grassland areas.

Stipagrostis capensis is adapted to arid conditions and is resilient to drought and salt spray.
