Stelis aernbyae

Stelis aernbyae, commonly known as the Aernby's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. The plant features a slender, unbranched stem with a few leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Stelis aernbyae is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or shaded gardens.
