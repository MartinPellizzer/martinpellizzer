Scutellaria modesta

Scutellaria modesta, commonly known as the small skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist woodlands and shaded areas, reaching a height of about 1 to 2 feet. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with hairy surfaces and produces blue to violet flowers in whorled clusters. Its flowers are tubular with two lips, a characteristic feature of the Scutellaria genus.

The plant is known for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
