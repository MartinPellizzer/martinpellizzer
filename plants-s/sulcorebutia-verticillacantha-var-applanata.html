Sulcorebutia verticillacantha var. applanata

Sulcorebutia verticillacantha var. applanata, commonly known as the flat-topped crassula, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a slight blue or purple tint. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. Its unique morphology and ability to thrive in arid conditions make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.

It requires minimal water and well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
