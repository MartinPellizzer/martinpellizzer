Silene inconspicua

Silene inconspicua, commonly known as the inconspicuous campion, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family, characterized by its showy, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes.
