Solanum kleinii

Solanum kleinii, commonly known as the Klein's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes potatoes, tomatoes, and peppers. The plant typically grows as an erect shrub with smooth, often hairy stems and ovate leaves. Its small, white or pale purple flowers are followed by berries that start green and mature to a deep red.

Solanum kleinii is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and flowers.
