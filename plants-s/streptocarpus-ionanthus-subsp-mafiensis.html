Streptocarpus ionanthus subsp. mafiensis

Streptocarpus ionanthus subsp.

mafiensis, commonly known as the Mafui streptocarpus, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of violet and white.

The plant has elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing in forest understories.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
