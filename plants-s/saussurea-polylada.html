Saussurea polylada

Saussurea polylada, commonly known as the Chinese yarrow or the Himalayan saffron, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, including parts of China, India, and Tibet. The plant is characterized by its tall, branched stems and dense clusters of small, yellowish flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in fine hairs.

Saussurea polylada is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
