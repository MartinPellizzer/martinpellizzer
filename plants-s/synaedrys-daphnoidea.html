Synaedrys daphnoidea

Synaedrys daphnoidea, commonly known as the slender daphne, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a slightly hairy texture.

The bark is smooth and grayish, with a distinctive scent when crushed.
