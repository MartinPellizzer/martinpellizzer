Stellaria stephaniana

Stellaria stephaniana, commonly known as the stephanian starwort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, where it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its stems are erect and often branched.

Stellaria stephaniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its charming appearance.
