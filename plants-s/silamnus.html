Silamnus

Silamnus, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The leaves are typically compound, with small, oval-shaped leaflets. Silamnus species are native to various regions in Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands.
