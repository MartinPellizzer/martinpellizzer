Scalia gracilis

Scalia gracilis, commonly known as the slender scalia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, arranged in spikes. Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
