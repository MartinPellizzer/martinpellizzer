Solanum spectabile var. echinaceum

Solanum spectabile var. echinaceum, commonly known as the Echinaceum Solanum, is a striking plant native to South America.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range from purple to red. The plant features velvety, lance-shaped leaves and produces berries that are typically yellow or orange when ripe. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance make it a desirable addition to both cultivated and natural landscapes.
