Stenocarpus angustifolius

Stenocarpus angustifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hakea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces spherical, woody fruits that remain on the plant for several years. It is typically found in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Its floral structure consists of small, clustered flowers that are often pink or white in color.
