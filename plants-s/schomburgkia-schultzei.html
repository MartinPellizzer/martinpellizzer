Schomburgkia schultzei

Schomburgkia schultzei, commonly known as the Schomburgkia orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, pendulous flowers that are typically bright yellow or orange in color. The plant has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, a common feature in many orchid species. Its leaves are long, narrow, and arranged in a rosette pattern around the pseudobulbs.

Schomburgkia schultzei thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.
