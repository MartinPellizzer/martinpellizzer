Salvia × doganii

Salvia × doganii, commonly known as the Mexican hybrid sage, is a perennial shrub native to Mexico.

It features striking violet-blue to purple flowers that bloom in whorls along the stems. The plant has lance-shaped, aromatic leaves with a fuzzy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and ability to attract pollinators.
