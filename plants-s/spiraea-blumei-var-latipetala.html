Spiraea blumei var. latipetala

Spiraea blumei var. latipetala, commonly known as the broad-petaled spiraea, is a deciduous shrub native to the Andean regions of South America.

It features dense, rounded growth with multiple stems that often branch profusely. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pink flowers in early spring, which are borne on elongated, cylindrical inflorescences. Its leaves are ovate to elliptic, with a glossy upper surface and a hairy underside.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
