Schomburgkia marginata var. moyobambae

Schomburgkia marginata var. moyobambae, commonly known as the Moyobamba orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to the cloud forests of Peru.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with vibrant red and yellow hues, often featuring intricate patterns. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its pseudobulbs are elongated and covered in a thick, fibrous sheath, which helps store water.

This variety is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts due to its rarity and unique floral display.
