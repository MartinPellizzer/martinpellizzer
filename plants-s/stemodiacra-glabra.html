Stemodiacra glabra

Stemodiacra glabra, commonly known as the smoothstemmed orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, smooth stems and lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. It is native to certain regions of Africa and thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its unique morphology and ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
