Senecio bornmuelleri

Senecio bornmuelleri, commonly known as the Bornmueller's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in dry gardens.
