Syringantha

Syringantha, commonly known as the sweet flag, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its aromatic, grass-like leaves and produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. The plant is native to wetland areas and thrives in moist, swampy environments. Syringantha is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its rhizomes are particularly valued for their medicinal properties.
