Solidago Virgaurea Subsp. Kurilensis

Solidago virgaurea subsp. kurilensis is a unique variant of the goldenrod plant native to the Kuril Islands. This subspecies thrives in coastal and mountainous regions where it adapts to harsh environmental conditions. Its yellow flowers bloom in late summer, adding vibrant color to the landscape. The plant is known for its resilience and ability to grow in poor soil conditions. It plays an important role in local ecosystems by supporting pollinators and contributing to biodiversity.
