Sansevieria lavranii

Sansevieria lavranii, commonly known as the Lavranii Snake Plant, is a species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with distinctive silver or white markings. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or green, followed by red berries.

Sansevieria lavranii is also valued for its air-purifying properties and resilience in various environmental conditions.
