Stipa koelzii

Stipa koelzii, commonly known as the Koelz's feathergrass, is a species of grass native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted clumps of narrow, gray-green leaves and its delicate, feathery inflorescence. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to high-altitude environments. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its elegant appearance.

Stipa koelzii is also of interest to botanists due to its unique morphological features and ecological adaptations.
