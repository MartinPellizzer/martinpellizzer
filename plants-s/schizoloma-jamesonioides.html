Schizoloma jamesonioides

Schizoloma jamesonioides, commonly known as the Jameson's lichen, is a foliose lichen that thrives in humid, shaded environments.

It is characterized by its delicate, leaf-like thallus with a pale green to yellowish upper surface and a darker, granular lower surface. The lichen forms a symbiotic relationship between a fungus and a green alga, which together carry out photosynthesis. It is typically found on tree bark, rocks, and soil in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a sensitive indicator of environmental health.
