Sonchus wilmsii

Sonchus wilmsii, commonly known as the Wilms' sowthistle, is a herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters, with hairy stems and lobed leaves. Its flowers are yellow and occur in clusters, resembling dandelions.

Sonchus wilmsii is often found in disturbed soils and is considered an invasive species in some regions.
