Soldanella dimoniei

Soldanella dimoniei, commonly known as the violet bellflower, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from violet to white, typically blooming in early spring. The plant has basal leaves that are heart-shaped and covered in a fine layer of white or grayish down. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a valued addition to botanical collections.
