Strukeria oppugnata

Strukeria oppugnata, commonly known as the battle-stricken orchid, is a rare and enigmatic flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Andes.

The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and requires specific soil conditions to flourish. Its leaves are elongated and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs, which help protect it from harsh weather.

Due to its limited habitat and vulnerability to environmental changes, Strukeria oppugnata is considered endangered and is the subject of ongoing conservation efforts.
