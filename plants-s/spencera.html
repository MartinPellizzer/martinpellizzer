Spencera

Spencera, commonly known as the Spencera plant, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its daisy-like flowers, which typically feature yellow centers surrounded by white or pale pink petals. The plant is native to certain regions of South America and thrives in well-drained soil with ample sunlight. Spencera is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive blooms and relatively low maintenance requirements.

It is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
