Stanhopea graveolens var. straminea

Stanhopea graveolens var. straminea, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow and green floral coloration, which is unique among orchid species. The plant produces intricate, spurred flowers that are often fragrant, attracting specific pollinators. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
