Stemodia damaziana

Stemodia damaziana, commonly known as the Madagascar star vine, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and produces small, star-shaped flowers. The plant has opposite, simple leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically white or pale yellow, and it blooms during the rainy season.

Stemodia damaziana is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
