Senecio haworthii

Senecio haworthii, commonly known as the blue chalk scale, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of blue-gray, scale-like leaves that form a compact rosette. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
