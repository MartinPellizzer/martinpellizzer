Staphylea franchetii

Staphylea franchetii, commonly known as the Chinese lantern vine, is a deciduous climbing plant native to China.

It produces striking, lantern-shaped fruits that are yellow to orange in color, often persisting through winter. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptical in shape. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for ornamental gardens.

Its unique fruiting display makes it a popular choice for adding visual interest to landscapes.
