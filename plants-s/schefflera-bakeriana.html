Schefflera bakeriana

Schefflera bakeriana, commonly known as the Baker's umbrella plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like crown of leaves, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant has compound, pinnate leaves that are glossy and dark green, with a leathery texture. It produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red, berry-like fruits.

Schefflera bakeriana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
