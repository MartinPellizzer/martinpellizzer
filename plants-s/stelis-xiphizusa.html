Stelis xiphizusa

Stelis xiphizusa, commonly known as the orchid with sword-like leaves, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of pale yellow to orange flowers with a distinctive lip. Its leaves are sword-shaped, stiff, and often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Stelis xiphizusa is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of trees or rocks without being parasitic.
