Syringa oblata f. alba

Syringa oblata f. alba, commonly known as the Chinese lilac, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Syringa oblata f. alba is relatively low maintenance and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.
