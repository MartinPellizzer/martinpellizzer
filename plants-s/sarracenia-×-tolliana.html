Sarracenia × tolliana

Sarracenia × tolliana, commonly known as the hybrid pitcher plant, is a fascinating carnivorous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a hybrid between Sarracenia leucophylla and Sarracenia rubra, resulting in unique morphological traits. The plant produces striking red and green pitchers that secrete a sticky digestive fluid to trap insects. Its leaves are modified into tubular structures that serve both as traps and as solar collectors.

Sarracenia × tolliana thrives in wet, acidic soils and requires specific care to maintain its health and vigor.
