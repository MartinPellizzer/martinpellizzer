Staurogyne cremostachya

Staurogyne cremostachya, commonly known as the slender water fern, is a aquatic plant native to the Americas.

It typically grows in shallow freshwater habitats such as ponds and streams. The plant has long, narrow leaves that are often submerged, with a feathery appearance. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are pollinated by wind.

Staurogyne cremostachya is valued for its ability to provide habitat for aquatic life and improve water quality.
