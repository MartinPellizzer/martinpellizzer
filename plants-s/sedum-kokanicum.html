Sedum kokanicum

Sedum kokanicum, commonly known as the Kashmir sedum, is a succulent plant native to the Himalayas.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, green to blue-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped, yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its drought tolerance and attractive appearance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
