Stenolepia speciosissima

Stenolepia speciosissima, commonly known as the showy stenolepia, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers that typically feature a mix of purple, pink, and white hues. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in seasonal environments. Its leaves are elongated and lance-shaped, often with a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

Stenolepia speciosissima is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
