Saxifraga geranioides

Saxifraga geranioides, commonly known as the rockfoil geranium, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and clustered foliage. The plant produces small, showy flowers that are typically pink or red, often with a distinctive dark center. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers sunny to partially shaded locations.

Saxifraga geranioides is often used in rock gardens due to its compact size and ornamental value.
