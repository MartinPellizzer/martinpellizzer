Sitanion × flexuosum

Sitanion × flexuosum, commonly known as the hybrid sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, linear leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist or wet environments such as marshes, swamps, and along stream banks. It has a rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread and form dense stands.

Sitanion × flexuosum is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
