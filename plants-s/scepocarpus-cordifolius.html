Scepocarpus cordifolius

Scepocarpus cordifolius, commonly known as the heart-leaved scepocarpus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are often white or pale yellow. Its fruits are fleshy and contain seeds that are dispersed by animals.

Scepocarpus cordifolius is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
