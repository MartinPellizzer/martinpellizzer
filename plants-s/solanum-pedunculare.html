Solanum pedunculare

Solanum pedunculare, commonly known as the slender nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually purple or violet in color. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Solanum pedunculare belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades such as tomatoes and potatoes.
