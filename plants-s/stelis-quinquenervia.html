Stelis quinquenervia

Stelis quinquenervia, commonly known as the five-veined orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often found in forest understories. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its flowering stems. Its flowers are usually small and fragrant, arranged in clusters along the stem.

The leaves are linear and often have a distinctive five-veined pattern, which is a key identifying feature.
