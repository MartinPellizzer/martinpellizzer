Stipa panicoides

Stipa panicoides, commonly known as the panicled feather grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and dense, feathery plumes that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue, and it produces small, wind-pollinated flowers in summer.

Stipa panicoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its elegant, airy foliage.
