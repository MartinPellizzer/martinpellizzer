Sinningia magnifica

Sinningia magnifica, commonly known as the Flame Violet, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features velvety, dark green leaves with a waxy texture, and its stems are often covered in fine, white hairs. Sinningia magnifica thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is a popular choice for indoor gardens due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
