Spiraea pubescens var. glabrescens

Spiraea pubescens var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth spiraea, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features slender, arching stems that produce clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers in early spring. The leaves are ovate to elliptic, with a smooth texture and a dark green upper surface. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers moist, well-drained soils.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is often used in gardens for its delicate floral display.
