Salsola glomerulata

Salsola glomerulata, commonly known as Russian thistle or goatweed, is a flowering plant in the family Amaranthaceae.

It is a halophyte, meaning it thrives in saline or coastal environments. The plant is characterized by its spiny, leafless stems and small, clustered greenish flowers. It is often found in disturbed soils and is considered an invasive species in many regions.

Salsola glomerulata is adapted to arid conditions and can tolerate extreme temperatures.
