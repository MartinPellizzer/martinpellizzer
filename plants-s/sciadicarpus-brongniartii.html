Sciadicarpus brongniartii

Sciadicarpus brongniartii, commonly known as the giant water fern, is a tropical aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, floating leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in diameter. The plant has a complex root system that helps stabilize the water ecosystem. It is often found in slow-moving or still freshwater habitats.

Sciadicarpus brongniartii plays a vital role in providing shelter for aquatic life and improving water quality.
