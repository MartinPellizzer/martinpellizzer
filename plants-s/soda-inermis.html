Soda inermis

Soda inermis, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features silvery-gray foliage with small, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value comes from its striking foliage and compact growth habit.
