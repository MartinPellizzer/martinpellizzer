Solanum pruinosum var. phyllolophum

Solanum pruinosum var. phyllolophum, commonly known as the velvet nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its velvety, glandular leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple, followed by dark blue or black berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
