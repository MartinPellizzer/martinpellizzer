Sarcocapnos crassifolia var. dissidens

Sarcocapnos crassifolia var. dissidens, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rock rose, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant has small, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to rocky or coastal environments.

This variety is valued for its hardiness and ability to withstand drought conditions.
