Salacia petiolata

Salacia petiolata, commonly known as the broadleaf salacia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Salaceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal due to its striking appearance.
