Strigilia obliquinervia

Strigilia obliquinervia, commonly known as the narrow-veined grass.

It is a perennial herb native to the wetlands and marshy areas of southern Africa. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves with a distinct, narrow venation pattern. Its flowering stems produce small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or brown.

Strigilia obliquinervia plays a role in stabilizing wetland soils and provides habitat for various invertebrates.
