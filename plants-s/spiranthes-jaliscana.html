Spiranthes jaliscana

Spiranthes jaliscana, commonly known as the Jalisco ladies' tresses, is a perennial orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its delicate, spiraling arrangement of pale green to white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its flowering period occurs in late spring to early summer.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological significance in its native habitat.
