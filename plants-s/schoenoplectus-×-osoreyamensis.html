Schoenoplectus × osoreyamensis

Schoenoplectus × osoreyamensis, commonly known as the Osoreyama bulrush, is a hybrid plant species native to Japan.

The plant typically grows in wetland environments such as marshes and along the edges of ponds. Its botanical features make it a notable species in the study of wetland flora and hybridization in plants.
