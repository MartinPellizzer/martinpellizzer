Seriola brasiliensis var. parviflora

Seriola brasiliensis var. parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered seriola, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of both ray and disk florets. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic value.
