Specklinia deborana

Specklinia deborana, commonly known as the Deborana orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and attractive, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, green or yellowish leaves and has a rhizome that helps anchor it to trees or other surfaces. Its flowers are usually borne in clusters and can range in color from white to pink or purple, depending on the variety.

Specklinia deborana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
