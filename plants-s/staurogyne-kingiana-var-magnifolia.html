Staurogyne kingiana var. magnifolia

Staurogyne kingiana var. magnifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf water clover, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often bright green with a waxy texture, enhancing its ornamental value in aquariums. The plant thrives in low to moderate light conditions and requires stable water parameters to flourish. It is known for its ability to oxygenate water and provide shelter for aquatic life.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for both freshwater and brackish water setups.
