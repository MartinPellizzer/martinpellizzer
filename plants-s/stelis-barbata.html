Stelis barbata

Stelis barbata, commonly known as the feathered stelis, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are elongated and often have a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss.

Stelis barbata is appreciated for its delicate appearance and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
