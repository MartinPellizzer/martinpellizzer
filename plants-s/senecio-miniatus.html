Senecio miniatus

Senecio miniatus, commonly known as the miniature senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has narrow, gray-green leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens.
