Schefflera eurhyncha

Schefflera eurhyncha, commonly known as the Hawaiian schefflera, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of leaves, which are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in shaded to semi-shaded conditions.

Schefflera eurhyncha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant or in gardens.
