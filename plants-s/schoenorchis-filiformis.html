Schoenorchis filiformis

Schoenorchis filiformis, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, thread-like leaves and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil.

Schoenorchis filiformis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
