Senecio abrotanifolius

Senecio abrotanifolius, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has small, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Senecio abrotanifolius is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
