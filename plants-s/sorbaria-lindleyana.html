Sorbaria lindleyana

Sorbaria lindleyana, commonly known as the Chinese spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of white to pink flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and is often found in moist, well-drained soils. Its botanical features include stipulate stipules and achenes as its fruit type.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden settings.
