Swartzia cuspidata var. brevistyla

Swartzia cuspidata var. brevistyla, commonly known as the narrow-styled swartzia, is a tropical tree native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant features a straight trunk with a dense crown and produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with oval-shaped leaflets that are often hairy.

The species is valued for its hard wood and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
