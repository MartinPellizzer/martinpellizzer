Symphoricarpos sinensis

Symphoricarpos sinensis, commonly known as Chinese snowberry, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features small, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant produces bright red, berry-like fruits that are edible for birds and small mammals. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
