Searsia blanda f. blanda

Searsia blanda f. blanda, commonly known as the coastal hakea, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in winter, followed by red, berry-like fruits. The plant has small, leathery leaves that are arranged in a dense, compact form. It thrives in coastal environments and is adapted to sandy, well-drained soils.

Searsia blanda f. blanda is often used in coastal landscaping due to its hardiness and ornamental value.
