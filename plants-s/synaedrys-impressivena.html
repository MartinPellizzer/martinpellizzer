Synaedrys impressivena

Synaedrys impressivena, commonly known as the impressive velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, velvety hairs. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.

Synaedrys impressivena is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as rainforests and along riverbanks.
