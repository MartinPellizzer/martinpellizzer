Satureja flacca

Satureja flacca, commonly known as slender mountain savory, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and thrives in full sun. Its small, tubular flowers are usually pink or purple, and it produces small, oblong fruits.

Satureja flacca is valued for its fragrant leaves, which are used in culinary and traditional medicinal applications.
