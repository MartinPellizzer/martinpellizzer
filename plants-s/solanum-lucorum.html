Solanum lucorum

Solanum lucorum, commonly known as the common nightshade, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to lanceolate in shape. The plant produces small, white or pale green flowers that are typically clustered in racemes. Its fruits are berries that mature to a deep red color, containing numerous seeds.

Solanum lucorum is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.
