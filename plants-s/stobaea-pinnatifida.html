Stobaea pinnatifida

Stobaea pinnatifida, commonly known as the broadleaf star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Stobaea pinnatifida is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and public spaces.
