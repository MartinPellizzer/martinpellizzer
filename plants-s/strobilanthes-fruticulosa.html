Strobilanthes fruticulosa

Strobilanthes fruticulosa, commonly known as the "Flower of the Moon," is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in clusters, often in shades of red, orange, and yellow. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and elliptical in shape.

Strobilanthes fruticulosa is known for its short blooming period, which occurs during the rainy season.
