Specklinia ghillanyi

Specklinia ghillanyi, commonly known as the golden rosette, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped growth habit and vibrant yellow-green foliage. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom during the dry season. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable specimen in the orchid world.
