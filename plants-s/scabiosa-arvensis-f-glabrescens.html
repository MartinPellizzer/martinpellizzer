Scabiosa arvensis f. glabrescens

Scabiosa arvensis f. glabrescens, commonly known as the glabrous field scabious, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caprifoliaceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, pink or white flowers. This variety is distinguished by its reduced pubescence, giving it a smoother appearance compared to other forms of Scabiosa arvensis.

It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and disturbed habitats.
