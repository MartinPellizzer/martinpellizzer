Swertia balansae

Swertia balansae, commonly known as the Himalayan gentian, is a perennial herb native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, thriving at elevations between 2,000 and 4,000 meters. Its leaves are lanceolate and arranged oppositely along the stem, while its roots are often used in traditional medicine.

Swertia balansae is valued for its ornamental beauty and medicinal properties in local herbal practices.
