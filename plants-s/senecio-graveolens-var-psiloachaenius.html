Senecio graveolens var. psiloachaenius

Senecio graveolens var. psiloachaenius, commonly known as the narrow-achene senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and small yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It produces small, dry fruits called achenes, which are used for seed dispersal.

This variant is distinguished by its reduced achene structure compared to other varieties of Senecio graveolens.
