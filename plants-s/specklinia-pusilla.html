Specklinia pusilla

Specklinia pusilla, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a small epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact size and delicate foliage. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, relying on its roots to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air. Its flowers are small, usually white or pale green, and bloom in clusters.

Specklinia pusilla is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its low maintenance requirements.
