Sinobambusa fimbriata

Sinobambusa fimbriata, commonly known as the fringed bamboo, is a species of flowering bamboo native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive fringed edge. The plant produces small, pale green to white flowers in panicles, which are typically borne in the axils of the leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Sinobambusa fimbriata is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
