Sedum likiangense

Sedum likiangense, commonly known as the Likiang stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to China.

It is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It is often found growing on rocky surfaces or in mountainous regions.

Sedum likiangense is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance in landscaping.
