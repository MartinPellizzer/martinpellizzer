Schefflera selloi

Schefflera selloi, commonly known as the Sello's umbrella plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the rainforests of Madagascar.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like crown of large, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits. Schefflera selloi thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
