Sobralia undatocarinata

Sobralia undatocarinata, commonly known as the wavy-ribbed sobralia, is a striking orchid native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange, with intricate patterns on the petals. The plant features strap-like leaves that are often wavy-edged, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Sobralia undatocarinata is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its vibrant blooms and unique foliage.
