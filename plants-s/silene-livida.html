Silene livida

Silene livida, commonly known as the pale campion, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink or white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Silene livida has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

It is often found in alpine or coastal environments due to its tolerance for harsh conditions.
