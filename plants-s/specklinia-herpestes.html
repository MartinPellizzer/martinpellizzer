Specklinia herpestes

Specklinia herpestes, commonly known as the coral spike orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, branching structure with a pseudobulb that supports its growth. The plant produces clusters of small, pale pink to white flowers with a distinctive coral-like appearance. Its leaves are thick, fleshy, and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water retention.

Specklinia herpestes thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media for optimal growth.
