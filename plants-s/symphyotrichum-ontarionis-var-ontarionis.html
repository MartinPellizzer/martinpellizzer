Symphyotrichum ontarionis var. ontarionis

Symphyotrichum ontarionis var. ontarionis, commonly known as the Ontario aster, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern North America.

It typically grows in open woodlands and moist meadows, reaching heights of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features showy purple to pink flower heads that bloom in late summer to early autumn. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often reddish in color.

It plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and is valued for its ornamental qualities in gardens.
