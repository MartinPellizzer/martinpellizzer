Spinifex Littoreus Var. Longifolius

Spinifex littoreus var. longifolius is a hardy coastal plant known for its unique adaptations to harsh environments. It thrives in sandy soils and salty air, making it a common sight along shorelines. Its long, grass-like leaves help it retain moisture in arid conditions. This variety is often used in dune stabilization efforts due to its strong root system. It plays a vital role in preventing erosion and supporting local ecosystems.
