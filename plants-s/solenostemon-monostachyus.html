Solenostemon monostachyus

Solenostemon monostachyus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf plectranthus, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy underneath. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale blue or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Solenostemon monostachyus is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
