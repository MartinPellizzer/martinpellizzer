Stachys ahmetsavranii

Stachys ahmetsavranii, commonly known as the Turkish betony, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces spikes of purple to blue flowers, typically blooming in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
