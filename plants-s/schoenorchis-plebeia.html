Schoenorchis plebeia

Schoenorchis plebeia, commonly known as the common orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. The plant has a slender, hairy stem and produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a spike. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil.

Schoenorchis plebeia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
