Scleria subulata

Scleria subulata, commonly known as the slender speargrass, is a member of the Cyperaceae family, commonly referred to as the sedge family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, clustered flowering spikes. Scleria subulata is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing wetland soils.
