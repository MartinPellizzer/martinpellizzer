Spathelia cubensis

Spathelia cubensis, commonly known as the Cuban spathelia, is a flowering plant native to Cuba.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests or along stream banks. Its flowers are usually pale green to white, with a distinctive spatulate shape.

Spathelia cubensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
