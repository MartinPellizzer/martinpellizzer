Spartium grandiflorum

Spartium grandiflorum, commonly known as largeflower sicklegrass, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

The plant produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Spartium grandiflorum typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils, and it is adapted to thrive in arid environments.
