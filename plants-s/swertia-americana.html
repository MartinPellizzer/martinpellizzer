Swertia americana

Swertia americana, commonly known as American gentian, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and have a rough texture.

Swertia americana is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
