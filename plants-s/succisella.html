Succisella

Succisella, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flower heads and silver-gray foliage. Native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean, it thrives in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant has a compact, low-growing habit and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Its ability to tolerate salty air makes it well-suited for coastal landscapes.
