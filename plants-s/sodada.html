Sodada

Sodada, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and chrysanthemums. The plant features showy, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white to pink petals. Sodada typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun.

It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
