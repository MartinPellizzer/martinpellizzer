Spicantopsis niponica var. reflexipinnula

Spicantopsis niponica var. reflexipinnula, commonly known as the Japanese spicantopsis, is a rare and delicate fern native to Japan.

It features intricate, finely divided fronds that are reflexed, giving it a distinctive, cascading appearance. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments, typically found in shaded forest understories. Its leaves are characterized by their slender, arching structure and delicate, lace-like texture.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a valuable and often endangered species in its native range.
