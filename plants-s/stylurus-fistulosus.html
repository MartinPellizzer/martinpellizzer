Stylurus fistulosus

Stylurus fistulosus, commonly known as the hollow-stemmed grass.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Africa and Asia. The plant is characterized by its slender, hollow stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as wetlands and marshes.

Its flowers are small and clustered, often producing light-colored bracts that aid in wind pollination.
