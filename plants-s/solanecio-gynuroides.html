Solanecio gynuroides

Solanecio gynuroides, commonly known as the golden crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow-orange inflorescence, which resembles a crown. The plant typically grows in grasslands and open areas, thriving in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its stems are erect and branching.

Solanecio gynuroides is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking floral display.
