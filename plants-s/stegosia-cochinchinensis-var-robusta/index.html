Stegosia Cochinchinensis Var. Robusta

Stegosia cochinchinensis var. robusta is a striking plant native to southern China and Southeast Asia. It is known for its vibrant purple flowers and unique, spiky bracts that add visual interest to any garden. This variety is a popular choice among horticulturists for its ornamental value and adaptability to various growing conditions. Its foliage is lush and evergreen, providing year-round beauty. Despite its aesthetic appeal, it requires well-drained soil and full sun to thrive optimally.
