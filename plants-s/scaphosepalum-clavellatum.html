Scaphosepalum clavellatum

Scaphosepalum clavellatum, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow petals and sepals. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with a distinctive shape that sets it apart from other orchid species.

Scaphosepalum clavellatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
