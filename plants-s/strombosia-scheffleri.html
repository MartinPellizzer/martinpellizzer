Strombosia scheffleri

Strombosia scheffleri, commonly known as the African moonflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

The plant belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types. Strombosia scheffleri is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique night-blooming display.
