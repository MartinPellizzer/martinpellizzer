Sedum divaricatum

Sedum divaricatum, commonly known as the zigzag sedum, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features fleshy, upright stems that often curve in a zigzag pattern, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It is adapted to rocky and arid environments, making it drought-tolerant and easy to care for.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
