Smilax sailenii

Smilax sailenii, commonly known as the Florida arrowroot or swamp arrowhead, is a perennial vine native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its stout, thorned stems and heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive waxy texture. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that develop into red, berry-like fruits. Smilax sailenii thrives in moist, shaded environments such as swamps and thickets.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies in its natural habitat.
