Stelis alajuelensis

Stelis alajuelensis, commonly known as the Alajuela orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and green. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are epiphytic, meaning they attach to other plants without parasitizing them.

Stelis alajuelensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
