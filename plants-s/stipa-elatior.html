Stipa elatior

Stipa elatior, commonly known as the tall feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted clumps of long, silver-gray leaves that form a distinctive, feathery appearance. The plant produces slender, spike-like inflorescences that are often adorned with silky, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Stipa elatior is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to stabilize soil in arid environments.
