Solanum calleryanum

Solanum calleryanum, commonly known as Chinese lantern, is a member of the nightshade family (Solanaceae).

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China and is characterized by its distinctive orange-red, balloon-shaped berries. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale green. Its leaves are ovate to elliptical, with a slightly hairy texture.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant fruits and adaptability to various climates.
