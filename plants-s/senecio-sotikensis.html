Senecio sotikensis

Senecio sotikensis, commonly known as the Sotik groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It features narrow, linear leaves and produces small yellow flowers arranged in a clustered inflorescence.

Senecio sotikensis is often found in fynbos vegetation and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
