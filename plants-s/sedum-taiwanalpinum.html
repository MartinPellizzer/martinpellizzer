Sedum taiwanalpinum

Sedum taiwanalpinum, commonly known as the Taiwan alpine stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to high-altitude regions of Taiwan.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, clustered leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange during the summer months. It is well adapted to rocky and arid environments, thriving in full sun.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
