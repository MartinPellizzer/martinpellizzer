Stelis minuta

Stelis minuta, commonly known as the miniature stelis orchid, is a small epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. The plant has slender, wiry stems and small, clustered leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a delicate, fragrant scent.

Stelis minuta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or terrariums.
