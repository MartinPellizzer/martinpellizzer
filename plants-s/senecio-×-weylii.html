Senecio × weylii

Senecio × weylii, commonly known as the hybrid groundsel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. Its leaves are usually narrow and slightly hairy, with a grayish-green color.

Senecio × weylii is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its delicate floral display.
