Solenangis liberica var. villiersii

Solenangis liberica var. villiersii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, waxy leaves and clusters of showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers warm, humid environments. It is a popular houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

Its botanical features make it a favorite among both novice and experienced gardeners.
