Specklinia mazei

Specklinia mazei, commonly known as the Malayan orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the rainy season. Its leaves are usually ovate and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in moisture retention.

Specklinia mazei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
