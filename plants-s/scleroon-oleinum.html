Scleroon oleinum

Scleroon oleinum, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which bloom in spring and summer. The plant has a woody stem and produces small, linear leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Scleroon oleinum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
