Sedum grisebachii subsp. flexuosum

Sedum grisebachii subsp.

flexuosum, commonly known as the flexible stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Central Asia. It is characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical leaves that are often covered in a powdery bloom, which helps reduce water loss.

The plant produces clusters of star-shaped, yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its adaptability to arid conditions makes it a hardy and low-maintenance plant for gardeners in dry climates.
