Sphaerostylis tulasneana

Sphaerostylis tulasneana, commonly known as the African violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical Africa.

It features a compact rosette of fleshy, dark green leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces showy, violet-colored flowers that bloom in clusters at the ends of its stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated as a houseplant.
