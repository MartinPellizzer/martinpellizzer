Scindapsus aruensis

Scindapsus aruensis, commonly known as the silver vine, is a tropical evergreen vine native to Indonesia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with prominent silver veins, which give it its distinctive appearance. The plant is known for its ability to climb and can reach lengths of several meters in its natural habitat. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Scindapsus aruensis is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
