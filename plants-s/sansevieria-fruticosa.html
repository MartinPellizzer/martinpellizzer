Sansevieria fruticosa

Sansevieria fruticosa, commonly known as the snake plant, is a hardy succulent native to Africa.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with yellow or white margins. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low light conditions and requires minimal watering. It produces small, white flowers on slender stalks during the growing season.

Sansevieria fruticosa is popular as a houseplant due to its air-purifying properties and low maintenance requirements.
