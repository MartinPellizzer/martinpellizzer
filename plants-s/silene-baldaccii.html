Silene baldaccii

Silene baldaccii, commonly known as the Baldaccii campion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or arid environments. The plant produces striking pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it has a fibrous root system adapted to dry conditions.

Silene baldaccii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil.
