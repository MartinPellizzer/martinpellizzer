Stapelia schinzii

Stapelia schinzii, commonly known as the star-shaped corpse flower, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed stems and its unique, star-shaped flowers that resemble decaying flesh. The plant produces large, waxy blooms in shades of yellow to orange, often with a strong, unpleasant odor to attract pollinators. It is a member of the Apocynaceae family and is known for its ability to store water in its thick stems.

Stapelia schinzii is often cultivated for its striking appearance and unusual floral characteristics.
