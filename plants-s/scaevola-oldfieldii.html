Scaevola oldfieldii

Scaevola oldfieldii, commonly known as Oldfield's sandmat, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of South Africa.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming shrub with small, showy blue or violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has silver-gray foliage and is adapted to sandy, well-drained soils. It is often found in dune environments and is relatively drought-tolerant.

Scaevola oldfieldii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in stabilizing coastal sands.
