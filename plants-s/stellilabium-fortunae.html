Stellilabium fortunae

Stellilabium fortunae, commonly known as the star-labium fortune plant, is a unique member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped floral structure, which gives it its common name. Native to specific regions in Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are typically a vibrant mix of white and yellow, with intricate patterns that resemble celestial motifs.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in specialized botanical gardens.
