Stelis rodriguesii

Stelis rodriguesii, commonly known as the Rodrigueses orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Madagascar.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a short, creeping rhizome with a few pairs of opposite, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive spurred lip that aids in pollination by insects.

Stelis rodriguesii is noted for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
