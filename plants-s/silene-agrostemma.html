Silene agrostemma

Silene agrostemma, commonly known as the field catchfly, is a flowering plant in the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides. The plant features slender stems with alternate, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically pink or purple, with five petals and a prominent central style.

Silene agrostemma produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.
