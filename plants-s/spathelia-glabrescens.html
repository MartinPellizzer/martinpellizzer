Spathelia glabrescens

Spathelia glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth spathelia, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features slender, erect stems and produces showy, violet-colored flowers that are clustered in racemes. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in a fine, velvety texture.

Spathelia glabrescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
