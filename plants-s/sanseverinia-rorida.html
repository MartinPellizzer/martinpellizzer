Sanseverinia rorida

Sanseverinia rorida, commonly known as the water sansevieria, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features stiff, upright leaves that are typically dark green with light green or white markings. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in low light conditions, making it an excellent choice for indoor environments. Its ability to purify the air and store water in its leaves contributes to its popularity as a houseplant.

Sanseverinia rorida is also known for its resilience and ease of care, requiring minimal maintenance.
