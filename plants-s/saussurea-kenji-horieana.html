Saussurea kenji-horieana

Saussurea kenji-horieana, commonly known as the Kenji-Horiean saffron plant, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

The plant is noted for its ability to thrive in cold, arid environments, often growing at elevations above 3,000 meters.
