Solanum polyaster

Solanum polyaster, commonly known as the starflower or wild starflower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has small, oval-shaped leaves. Solanum polyaster produces small, yellowish fruits that contain seeds.

It is often found in regions with temperate climates and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
