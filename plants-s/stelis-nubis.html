Stelis nubis

Stelis nubis, commonly known as the cloud orchid, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, pendulous flowers that often bloom in clusters, displaying shades of white and pale pink. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments, showcasing its epiphytic nature. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Stelis nubis is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological role in supporting biodiversity in its native habitat.
