Saccolabium ochraceum

Saccolabium ochraceum, commonly known as the yellow sicklepod, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is well adapted to drought conditions, with deep roots and a low, spreading growth habit.
