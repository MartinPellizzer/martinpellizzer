Stenocalyx lucidus

Stenocalyx lucidus, commonly known as the glossy flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery orange to red flowers that bloom in clusters during the wet season. The plant has a smooth, glossy bark and elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, round seeds.
