Spergula fontenellei

Spergula fontenellei, commonly known as Fontenelle's sedge, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Cyperaceae.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, linear leaves and small, clustered flowers. It is often found in temperate climates and is valued for its ornamental qualities in garden settings.
