Semiarundinaria fortis f. nebulosa

Semiarundinaria fortis f. nebulosa, commonly known as the nebulous bamboo, is a unique variant of the giant bamboo species.

It is characterized by its distinctive, fluffy, and often white or pale-colored inflorescences that give it a misty appearance. This plant is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. Its botanical features include tall, slender culms and large, lance-shaped leaves.

The nebulosa form is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
