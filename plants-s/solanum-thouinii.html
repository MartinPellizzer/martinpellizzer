Solanum thouinii

Solanum thouinii, commonly known as the Thouin's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant is characterized by its small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. It produces small, green berries that turn red when mature, containing the seeds.
