Strychnos holstii f. laxiuscula

Strychnos holstii f. laxiuscula, commonly known as the dwarf strychnos, is a compact shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically glaucous in appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by orange-red berries. Its botanical characteristics include a woody stem and a preference for well-drained, sandy soils.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in coastal environments.
