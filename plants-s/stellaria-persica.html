Stellaria persica

Stellaria persica, commonly known as Persian starwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is native to regions of southern Europe and western Asia, including parts of Turkey and the Caucasus. The plant features small, white or pinkish flowers arranged in a star-like pattern, which gives it its common name. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Stellaria persica typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
