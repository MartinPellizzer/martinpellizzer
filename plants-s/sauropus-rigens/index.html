Sauropus Rigens

Blue bottle plant, scientifically known as Sauropus rigens, is a striking member of the Euphorbiaceae family native to Southeast Asia. Its unique appearance, characterized by its vibrant blue-green leaves and bottle-shaped stems, makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to maintain its lush foliage. Despite its ornamental appeal, it is also valued for its air-purifying properties and ability to thrive in low-light conditions. With proper care, the Blue bottle plant can become a stunning addition to any home or office space.
