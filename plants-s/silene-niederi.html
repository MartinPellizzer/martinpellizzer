Silene niederi

Silene niederi, commonly known as the Nieder's campion, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its pink to purple flowers and succulent leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, thriving in cool, well-drained soils. Silene niederi is a perennial herb that produces capsules as its fruit, containing numerous small seeds.
