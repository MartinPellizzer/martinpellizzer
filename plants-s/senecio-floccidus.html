Senecio floccidus

Senecio floccidus, commonly known as the woolly senecio, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive, fluffy appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
