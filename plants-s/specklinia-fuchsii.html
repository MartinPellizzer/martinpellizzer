Specklinia fuchsii

Specklinia fuchsii, commonly known as the Fuchs's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are dark green with a slightly waxy texture. Its flowers are usually pale green or white, with a subtle fragrance, and bloom in clusters during the rainy season.

Specklinia fuchsii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
