Sideritis Scordioides Var. Hyssopifolia

Sideritis scordioides var. hyssopifolia is a lesser-known yet remarkable plant species native to the Mediterranean region. Known for its aromatic leaves and delicate flowers, it thrives in rocky and dry environments. This variety is often mistaken for common hyssop due to its similar appearance. Its unique fragrance and medicinal properties have intrigued botanists and herbalists alike. Despite its beauty and potential, it remains relatively underappreciated in modern horticulture.
