Scalia nutans

Scalia nutans, commonly known as the nodding scalia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that droop downward, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.
