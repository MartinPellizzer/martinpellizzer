Stachys balansae

Stachys balansae, commonly known as Balansae hedge-nettle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its hairy stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, pale purple to white flowers arranged in whorls along the stem. It thrives in rocky or dry habitats and is often found in coastal areas.

Stachys balansae is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
