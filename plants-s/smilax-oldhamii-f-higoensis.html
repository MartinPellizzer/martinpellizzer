Smilax oldhamii f. higoensis

Smilax oldhamii f. higoensis, commonly known as the Higoensis Smilax, is a variant of the Smilax genus native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a climbing shrub characterized by its stout, spiny stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into red, fleshy berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for horticultural study and conservation efforts.
