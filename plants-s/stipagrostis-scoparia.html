Stipagrostis scoparia

The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Stipagrostis scoparia is well adapted to harsh environments, with deep root systems that allow it to survive in low-nutrient soils and extreme temperatures.
