Syntherisma ciliaris

Syntherisma ciliaris, commonly known as the silverleaf spurge, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features succulent, bluish-gray leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a distinctive silvery appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the spring. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
