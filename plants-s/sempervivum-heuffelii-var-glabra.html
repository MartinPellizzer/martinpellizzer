Sempervivum heuffelii var. glabra

Sempervivum heuffelii var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth stonecrop, is a low-growing perennial succulent native to the Alps.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, smooth leaves that are typically green with a bluish or reddish tinge. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It is well adapted to rocky and sunny environments, thriving in well-drained soil.

Its hardy nature makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine displays.
