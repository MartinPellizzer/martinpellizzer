Stipa tenuissima f. colorata

Stipa tenuissima f. colorata, commonly known as the pink feather grass, is a variant of the slender wheatgrass species.

It is characterized by its fine, grass-like foliage that produces delicate, fluffy seed heads. The plant is native to regions of Europe and Asia, where it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. Its unique pinkish hue in the seed heads makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

This cultivar is often used in landscaping for its visual appeal and low maintenance requirements.
