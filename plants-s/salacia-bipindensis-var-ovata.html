Salacia bipindensis var. ovata

Salacia bipindensis var. ovata, commonly known as the ovate salacia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Salaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Africa, particularly found in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are ovate in shape and have a smooth, glossy surface. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, and it is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal.

The plant is also valued for its potential medicinal properties, though further research is needed to confirm its therapeutic uses.
