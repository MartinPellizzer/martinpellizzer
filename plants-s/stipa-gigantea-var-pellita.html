Stipa gigantea var. pellita

Stipa gigantea var. pellita, commonly known as the giant feather grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 2 meters, adorned with feathery, silver-green plumes that sway gracefully in the wind. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are both visually appealing and attract various pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a valuable addition to both natural and cultivated environments.
