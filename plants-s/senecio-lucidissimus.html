Senecio lucidissimus

Senecio lucidissimus, commonly known as the shining senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, daisy-like blooms.

Senecio lucidissimus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
