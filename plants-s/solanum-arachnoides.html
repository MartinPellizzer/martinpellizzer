Solanum arachnoides

Solanum arachnoides, commonly known as the hairy nightshade, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 meter. The plant features hairy stems and leaves with a rough texture, and its flowers are usually purple or violet in color. Its fruit is a small, spherical berry that turns from green to yellow when mature.

Solanum arachnoides belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes.
