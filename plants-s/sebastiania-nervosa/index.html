Sebastiania Nervosa

Sebastiania nervosa is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America. It is known for its vibrant yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. This species thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens. Its unique leaf structure, featuring prominent veins, adds to its visual appeal. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively obscure plant among botanists and gardeners alike.
