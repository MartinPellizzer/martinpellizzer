Spiraea Prunifolia Var. Typica

Spiraea prunifolia var. typica is a deciduous shrub known for its delicate pink flowers and compact growth habit. Native to parts of Asia, it thrives in temperate climates and well-drained soils. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in partial shade. Its small, oval leaves provide a lush green backdrop to its showy spring blooms. It is a popular choice for gardeners seeking low-maintenance yet visually appealing plants.
