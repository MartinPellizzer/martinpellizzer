Selaginella caudata var. commersonii

Selaginella caudata var. commersonii, commonly known as the Commerson's spike moss, is a member of the Selaginellaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like appearance.

It is a small, evergreen plant that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features whorled, linear leaves that are often reddish or bronze in color, especially when young. It reproduces via spores, with sporophylls forming tight, cone-like structures at the tips of its stems.

This variety is notable for its adaptability and is often used in horticultural settings for its ornamental value.
