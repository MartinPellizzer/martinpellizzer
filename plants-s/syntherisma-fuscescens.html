Syntherisma fuscescens

Syntherisma fuscescens, commonly known as the black orchid, is a rare and striking plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dark purple to blackish flowers, which are densely clustered in inflorescences. The plant has a compact, herbaceous growth habit with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Due to its limited distribution and unique appearance, it is highly sought after by botanists and plant collectors.
