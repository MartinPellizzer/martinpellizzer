Senna multifoliolata var. multifoliolata

Senna multifoliolata var. multifoliolata, commonly known as the many-leaved senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with numerous small leaflets, giving it a feathery appearance. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

The plant thrives in warm, sunny environments and is adaptable to various soil types.
