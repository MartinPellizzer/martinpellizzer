Sopropis

Sopropis, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its long, drooping clusters of yellow flowers that resemble a shower of golden beads. The plant produces small, leguminous pods that contain seeds used in traditional medicine. Sopropis thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its leaves are trifoliate, with each leaflet being oval and slightly serrated.
