Serjania morii

Serjania morii, commonly known as the Brazilian serjania, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Brazil.

It belongs to the family Serjaniaceae and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the summer, followed by red, fleshy berries.

Serjania morii is often found in humid forests and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance.
