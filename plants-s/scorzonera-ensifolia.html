Scorzonera ensifolia

Scorzonera ensifolia, commonly known as the swordleaf scorzonera, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its narrow, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers with ray florets, typically blooming in the summer months. Scorzonera ensifolia is often found in rocky or dry soils, thriving in well-drained environments.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
