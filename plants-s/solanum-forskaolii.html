Solanum forskaolii

Solanum forskaolii, commonly known as the African nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale purple, followed by berries that mature to a dark red or black color. It has hairy stems and leaves that are generally ovate or elliptical in shape.

Solanum forskaolii is part of the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like tomatoes and potatoes.
