Strelitzia reginae var. ovata

Strelitzia reginae var. ovata, commonly known as the Orange Banana.

It is a striking plant native to South Africa, characterized by its large, banana-like leaves that are typically a vibrant orange to yellow-green in color. The plant produces unique, bird-of-paradise-like flowers in shades of orange and yellow, which bloom on tall, erect stalks. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

This ornamental plant is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and is relatively easy to care for in warm, humid environments.
