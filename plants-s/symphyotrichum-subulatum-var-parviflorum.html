Symphyotrichum subulatum var. parviflorum

Symphyotrichum subulatum var. parviflorum, commonly known as the slenderleaf aster, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as meadows and woodlands. The plant features narrow, needle-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers in the fall. Its flowers are usually pink to purple in color and attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native plant gardens.
