Senecio talinoides subsp. aizoides

Senecio talinoides subsp.

aizoides, commonly known as the Aizoides groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing succulent leaves adapted to conserve water. Its yellow flowers are arranged in dense clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
