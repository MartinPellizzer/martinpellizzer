Scutellaria tenasserimensis

Scutellaria tenasserimensis, commonly known as the Tenasserim skullcap, is a herbaceous perennial native to the northeastern region of India.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color, which bloom during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
