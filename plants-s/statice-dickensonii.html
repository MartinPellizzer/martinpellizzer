Statice dickensonii

Statice dickensonii, commonly known as the desert statice, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family, which includes many species adapted to dry environments. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, which bloom in late summer and early fall. It has a compact, bushy growth habit with narrow, grass-like leaves that help reduce water loss.

Statice dickensonii is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
