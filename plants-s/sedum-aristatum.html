Sedum aristatum

Sedum aristatum, commonly known as the noble sedum, is a species of flowering plant in the family Crassulaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Europe, including the Alps and the Carpathians. Sedum aristatum is often cultivated in rock gardens and as a decorative plant due to its hardy nature and attractive appearance.
