Spergularia marginata var. intermedia

Spergularia marginata var. intermedia, commonly known as the intermediate sandspurry, is a flowering plant native to sandy and coastal environments.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with small, fleshy leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dunes or disturbed areas. Its flowers are typically clustered in racemes and are pollinated by insects.

The species is noted for its adaptability to harsh, arid conditions.
