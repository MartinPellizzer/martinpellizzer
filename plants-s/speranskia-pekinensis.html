Speranskia pekinensis

Speranskia pekinensis, commonly known as the Chinese flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy red or orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has compound leaves that are opposite and pinnate, with small, ovate leaflets. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental tree in warmer climates.

The fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release its seeds.
