Stelis flava

Stelis flava, commonly known as the Yellow Stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Stelis flava is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
