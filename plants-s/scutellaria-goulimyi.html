Scutellaria goulimyi

Scutellaria goulimyi, commonly known as the Chinese skullcap, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. Scutellaria goulimyi is often found in woodland areas and along stream banks, where it thrives in moist, well-drained soil.

It is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for centuries.
