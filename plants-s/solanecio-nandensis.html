Solanecio nandensis

Solanecio nandensis, commonly known as the Nandensian sunflower, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow to orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous terrain, where it thrives in well-drained soils. Its leaves are usually narrow and hairy, adapted to reduce water loss in arid conditions.

Solanecio nandensis plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing nectar for pollinators and supporting local biodiversity.
