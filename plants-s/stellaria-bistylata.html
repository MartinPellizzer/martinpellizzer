Stellaria bistylata

Stellaria bistylata, commonly known as the two-styled starwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its delicate, star-shaped white flowers with two distinct styles. The plant typically thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, lanceolate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Stellaria bistylata is known for its small, inconspicuous flowers that bloom in early spring, contributing to its ecological role in supporting pollinators.
