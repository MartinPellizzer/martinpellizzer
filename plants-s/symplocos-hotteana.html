Symplocos hotteana

Symplocos hotteana, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or red-flowered evergreen, is a deciduous shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, depending on the variety. Its fruits are small, capsule-like structures that split open to release seeds.

Symplocos hotteana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adaptable to a variety of soil types.
