Senecio speciosus

Senecio speciosus, commonly known as the showy groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in mountainous regions. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are usually alternate, with a rough texture and a slightly hairy surface.

Senecio speciosus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
