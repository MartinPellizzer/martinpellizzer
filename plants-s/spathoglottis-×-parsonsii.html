Spathoglottis × parsonsii

Spathoglottis × parsonsii, commonly known as the Parson's lady's tresses, is a hybrid orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, spike-like inflorescence composed of numerous small, pale pink to white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or mountainous regions. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
