Senecio lamarckianus

Senecio lamarckianus, commonly known as the yellow starbursts, is a member of the Asteraceae family and is native to South Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features upright, branching stems covered in fine, hairy foliage. Its flowers are typically arranged in dense clusters, creating a striking visual display.

Senecio lamarckianus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
