Sedum mucizonia subsp. urceolatum

Sedum mucizonia subsp.

urceolatum, commonly known as the urn-shaped stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It features fleshy, upright stems and small, clustered leaves that are typically gray-green in color.

The plant produces compact, urn-shaped flower clusters in shades of yellow to orange, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a versatile addition to arid landscapes.
