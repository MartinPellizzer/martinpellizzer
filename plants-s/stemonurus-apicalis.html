Stemonurus apicalis

Stemonurus apicalis, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking yellow flowers with a distinctive star-shaped pattern. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments within tropical forests. Its leaves are broad and slightly fleshy, adapted to retain moisture in its native habitat.

Stemonurus apicalis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
