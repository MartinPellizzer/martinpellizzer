Senna bauhinioides

Senna bauhinioides, commonly known as the golden senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and parts of Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters tall, characterized by its pinnately compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Senna bauhinioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
