Stelis subtilis

Stelis subtilis, commonly known as the slender stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate appearance. The plant typically features slender, upright stems with small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a subtle fragrance, and are arranged in a loose inflorescence.

Stelis subtilis thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in montane forests.
