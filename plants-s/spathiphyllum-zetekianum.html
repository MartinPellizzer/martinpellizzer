Spathiphyllum zetekianum

Spathiphyllum zetekianum, commonly known as the Zetekian peace lily, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green heart-shaped leaves and produces striking white spathes that enclose bright yellow spadices. The plant is a member of the Araceae family, known for its unique inflorescence structure. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Spathiphyllum zetekianum is also valued for its air-purifying properties and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
