Scutellaria cavaleriei

Scutellaria cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese skullcap, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or rocky habitats.

Scutellaria cavaleriei is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
