Spiraea lemoinei

Spiraea lemoinei, commonly known as the Japanese spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features clusters of small, fragrant white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, arching stems and dark green, oval-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Spiraea lemoinei is valued for its ornamental appeal and easy maintenance.
