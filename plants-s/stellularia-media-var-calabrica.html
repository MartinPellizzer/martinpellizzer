Stellularia media var. calabrica

Stellularia media var. calabrica, commonly known as the California starflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to the western United States.

It features delicate, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring, typically in shades of white and yellow. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in wetlands, stream banks, and other moist habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, supporting pollinators and native wildlife.
