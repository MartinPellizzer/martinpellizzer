Syngonium decipiens

Syngonium decipiens, commonly known as the arrowhead vine, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that often have a variegated pattern of green, white, and sometimes pink. The plant is a climbing vine that can grow up to 10 meters in length, making it suitable for hanging baskets or trellises. Its leaves are typically glossy and emit a mild scent when crushed.

Syngonium decipiens thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.
