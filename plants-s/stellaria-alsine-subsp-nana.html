Stellaria alsine subsp. nana

Stellaria alsine subsp.

nana, commonly known as dwarf starwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia. It typically reaches a height of only a few centimeters, with small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in early summer.

The plant has a mat-forming growth habit, with slender stems and opposite, oval-shaped leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.

Its small flowers are often visited by pollinators like bees and flies.
