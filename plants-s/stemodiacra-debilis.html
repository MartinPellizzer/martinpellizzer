Stemodiacra debilis

Stemodiacra debilis, commonly known as the weakstemmed orchid, is a delicate flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, often blooming during the spring and summer seasons. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a conservation concern in many areas.
