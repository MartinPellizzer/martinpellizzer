Schefflera crassilimba

Schefflera crassilimba, commonly known as the broad-leafed schefflera, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by spherical, red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Schefflera crassilimba is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
