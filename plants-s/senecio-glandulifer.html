Senecio glandulifer

Senecio glandulifer, commonly known as the glandular senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are usually covered in small, glandular dots, which give it its scientific name.

It produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators.
