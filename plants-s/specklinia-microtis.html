Specklinia microtis

Specklinia microtis, commonly known as the miniature pigmy orchid, is a small epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in terrariums.
