Silybum marianum var. albiflorum

Silybum marianum var. albiflorum, commonly known as the white-flowered milk thistle, is a variant of the common milk thistle.

It is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region and belongs to the Asteraceae family. The plant is characterized by its spiny leaves and white or pale yellow flowers. It is known for its medicinal properties, particularly in supporting liver health.

The variety is often cultivated for its seeds, which are used in herbal remedies and as a source of the compound silymarin.
