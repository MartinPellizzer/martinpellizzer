Scutellaria pamirica

Scutellaria pamirica, commonly known as the Pamir skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, thriving at high altitudes. Its small, tubular flowers are usually blue or violet, and it produces distinctive nutlets as fruits.

Scutellaria pamirica is valued for its potential medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
