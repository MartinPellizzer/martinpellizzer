Saussurea riederi var. elongatum

Saussurea riederi var. elongatum, commonly known as the elongated saussurea, is a perennial herb native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It features a tall, slender stem that can reach up to 2 meters in height, adorned with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a panicle inflorescence, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, arid environments.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
