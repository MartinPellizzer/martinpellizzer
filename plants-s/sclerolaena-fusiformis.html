Sclerolaena fusiformis

Sclerolaena fusiformis, commonly known as the narrowleaf everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant features slender, cylindrical stems and small, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in shades of pink or purple.

It is known for its ability to thrive in arid conditions and is sometimes used in ornamental gardening.
