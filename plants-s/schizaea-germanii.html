Schizaea germanii

Schizaea germanii, commonly known as the German clubmoss, is a member of the family Schizaeaceae.

It is a small, evergreen fern that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant has a distinctive appearance with its creeping rhizomes and erect, leafy shoots. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and have a serrated margin.

Schizaea germanii is often found in forest floors and is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological role in soil stabilization.
