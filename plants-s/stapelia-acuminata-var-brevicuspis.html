Stapelia acuminata var. brevicuspis

Stapelia acuminata var. brevicuspis, commonly known as the short-spined stapelia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems that are typically green or yellowish in color. The plant produces striking, waxy flowers that are often orange or yellow with a distinctive, almost meaty appearance. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

This variety is valued for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements in cultivation.
