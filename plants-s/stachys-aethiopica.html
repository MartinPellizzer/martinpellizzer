Stachys aethiopica

Stachys aethiopica, commonly known as Ethiopian hedgehog.

It is a flowering plant native to the highlands of Ethiopia and is characterized by its unique, spiky bracts that resemble a hedgehog. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Stachys aethiopica is often grown as an ornamental plant for its striking foliage and interesting floral structure.
