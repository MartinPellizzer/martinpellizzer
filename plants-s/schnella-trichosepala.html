Schnella trichosepala

Schnella trichosepala, commonly known as the hairy-sepaled orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, hairy sepals and petals, which give it its common name. Native to tropical regions of Africa, this plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments. Its flowers are usually small and fragrant, often appearing in clusters.

The plant's botanical structure includes a sympodial growth pattern and a rhizomatous root system.
