Senecio joharchii

Senecio joharchii, commonly known as the Kashmir wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan regions of Pakistan and India.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and vibrant yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its showy inflorescences. Senecio joharchii thrives in rocky, alpine environments and is adapted to cold, high-altitude climates.
