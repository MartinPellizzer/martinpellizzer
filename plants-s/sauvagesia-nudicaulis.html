Sauvagesia nudicaulis

Sauvagesia nudicaulis, commonly known as the slender sandwort, is a low-growing, perennial herb native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pink. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in dry environments. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in desert scrub and coastal dunes.

Due to its adaptability, it is considered an important species for stabilizing loose soils and supporting local biodiversity.
