Sesleria Caerulea Var. Albicans

Albicans sesleria, scientifically known as Sesleria caerulea var. albicans, is a striking ornamental grass native to southern Africa. This plant is prized for its elegant, silver-blue foliage that adds a unique texture to gardens and landscapes. Its delicate, feathery inflorescences bloom in shades of pink and purple, creating a soft, ethereal effect during the growing season. Easy to care for, it thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a versatile choice for both novice and experienced gardeners. With its refined appearance and low maintenance requirements, Albicans sesleria is a valuable addition to modern botanical collections.
