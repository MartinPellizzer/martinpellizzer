Solanum tuberosum var. maglia

Solanum tuberosum var. maglia, commonly known as the Maglia potato, is a cultivated variety of the common potato.

It is characterized by its high yield and excellent tuber quality, making it popular in agricultural settings. The plant is a herbaceous perennial that produces large, oval-shaped tubers with a smooth skin. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its adaptability and resistance to certain diseases, contributing to its widespread use in farming.
