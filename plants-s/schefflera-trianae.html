Schefflera trianae

Schefflera trianae, commonly known as the Colombian umbrella plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Colombia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of large, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by red, fleshy berries that are edible for birds. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-drained soil.

Schefflera trianae is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor conditions.
