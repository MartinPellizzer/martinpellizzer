Sphaeranthus cochinchinensis

Sphaeranthus cochinchinensis, commonly known as the Vietnamese daisy, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular shape and bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often hairy and branching.

Sphaeranthus cochinchinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
