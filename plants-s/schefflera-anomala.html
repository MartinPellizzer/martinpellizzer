Schefflera anomala

Schefflera anomala, commonly known as the umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of palmate leaves, which are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by black, berry-like fruits. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
