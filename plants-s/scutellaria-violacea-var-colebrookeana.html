Scutellaria violacea var. colebrookeana

Scutellaria violacea var. colebrookeana, commonly known as the blue scutellaria, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces showy blue or violet flowers in whorls along the stem, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade, making it a suitable candidate for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate foliage and long-lasting blooms.
