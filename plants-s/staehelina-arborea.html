Staehelina arborea

Staehelina arborea, commonly known as the tree hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and large, showy flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and red. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a waxy coating.
