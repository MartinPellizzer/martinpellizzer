Sedum oreganum var. oreganum

Sedum oreganum var. oreganum, commonly known as Oregon sedum, is a perennial succulent native to the western United States.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink to white. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Its leaves are typically blue-green and succulent, storing water to help it survive in arid conditions.

It is also valued for its low maintenance and ability to attract pollinators.
