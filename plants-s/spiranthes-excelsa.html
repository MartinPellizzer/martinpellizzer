Spiranthes excelsa

Spiranthes excelsa, commonly known as the pink lady's tresses, is a species of orchid native to North America.

It is characterized by its delicate, spiraling inflorescence that resembles a twisted ribbon, with pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wetlands. It has rhizomatous growth and produces small, elongated leaves.

Spiranthes excelsa is a perennial herb that blooms from late spring to early summer.
