Scrophularia libanotica var. sivasica

Scrophularia libanotica var. sivasica, commonly known as the Sivas scrophularia, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in rocky or dry habitats and is often found in mountainous regions.

This variant is distinguished by its unique geographical distribution and specific morphological traits.
