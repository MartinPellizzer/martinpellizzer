Schoutensia monophylla

Schoutensia monophylla, commonly known as the single-leaf Schoutensia, is a rare and unique plant species native to the Western Cape of South Africa.

It is characterized by its singular, broad, waxy leaf that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires a Mediterranean climate with mild, wet winters.
