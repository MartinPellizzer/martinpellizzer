Scaphospermum asiaticum

Scaphospermum asiaticum, commonly known as the Asian bubblegum tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant features palmate leaves with five to seven lobes and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a distinctive, inflated capsule that splits open to release seeds.

The plant is notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique foliage and colorful blooms.
