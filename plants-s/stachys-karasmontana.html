Stachys karasmontana

Stachys karasmontana, commonly known as Karasmontan hedge-nettle, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic foliage. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color, arranged in whorls along the stem. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Stachys karasmontana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
