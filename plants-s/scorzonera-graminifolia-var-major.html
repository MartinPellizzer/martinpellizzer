Scorzonera graminifolia var. major

Scorzonera graminifolia var. major, commonly known as the tall scorzonera, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features a robust root system and produces basal rosettes of lanceolate, hairy leaves. The plant bears yellow flowers arranged in a loose cluster, with five petals and prominent yellow stamens. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional herbal medicine.
