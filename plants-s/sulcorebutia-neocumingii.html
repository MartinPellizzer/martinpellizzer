Sulcorebutia neocumingii

Sulcorebutia neocumingii, commonly known as the Neocuming's stone cactus, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem covered in dense, white to pale green spines. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red during the spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for rock gardens and succulent collections.
