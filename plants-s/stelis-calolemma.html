Stelis calolemma

Stelis calolemma, commonly known as the helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including regions such as Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant typically grows on forest floors or in shaded areas, preferring humid and well-drained environments. Its flowers are small, usually white or pale pink, and are clustered in racemes.

Stelis calolemma has a short, thick rhizome and is often found in association with mycorrhizal fungi, which aid in nutrient absorption.
