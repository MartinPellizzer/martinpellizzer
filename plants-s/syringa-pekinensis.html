Syringa pekinensis

Syringa pekinensis, commonly known as Chinese lilac, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, clustered flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Syringa pekinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its showy floral display.
