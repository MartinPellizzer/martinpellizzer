Steganotaenia araliacea var. araliacea

Steganotaenia araliacea var. araliacea, commonly known as the African waterleaf, is a member of the Araliaceae family.

It is a perennial herb native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often deeply veined and have a distinctive waxy texture. Its flowers are small, white, and clustered in umbels, which are characteristic of the Araliaceae family.

The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found near water sources.
