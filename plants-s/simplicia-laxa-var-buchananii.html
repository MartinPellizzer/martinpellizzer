Simplicia laxa var. buchananii

Simplicia laxa var. buchananii, commonly known as the slender simplicia, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated leaves and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or along stream banks. Its botanical structure includes a rhizomatous root system and herbaceous stems that often lie close to the ground.
