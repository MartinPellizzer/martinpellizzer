Sophora mollis subsp. griffithii

Sophora mollis subsp.

griffithii, commonly known as the purple wisteria, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China. It is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in early spring, often in pendulous clusters.

The plant has pinnately compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens in temperate climates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated for its showy floral display.
