Saxifraga cotyledon var. oederiana

Saxifraga cotyledon var. oederiana, commonly known as the "Laceleaf Saxifrage," is a striking succulent native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, crinkled leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, especially in full sun. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
