Suttonia lessertiana

Suttonia lessertiana, commonly known as the yellow star orchid, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow petals and a distinctive star-shaped appearance. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments within the rainforest canopy. It produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom during the warmer months.

Suttonia lessertiana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
