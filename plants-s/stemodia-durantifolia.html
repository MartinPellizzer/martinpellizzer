Stemodia durantifolia

Stemodia durantifolia, commonly known as the everlasting stemodia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form with small, leathery leaves that remain green throughout the year. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale blue or white flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in poor soils make it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

The plant's resilience and ornamental value have contributed to its cultivation in various horticultural settings.
