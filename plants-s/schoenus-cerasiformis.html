Schoenus cerasiformis

Schoenus cerasiformis, commonly known as the red rush, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.
