Spiraea miyabei

Spiraea miyabei, commonly known as the Japanese weeping spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its cascading clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, arching branches that give it a graceful, weeping appearance. Its leaves are oval-shaped and turn vibrant shades of red in the fall.

Spiraea miyabei thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.
