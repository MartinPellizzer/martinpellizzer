Solanum didymum var. subglabrum

Solanum didymum var. subglabrum, commonly known as the smooth nightshade, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its smooth, glabrous leaves and small, purple to red berries that contain toxic alkaloids. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and shrublands.

Due to its toxic properties, it is often avoided by livestock and humans alike.
