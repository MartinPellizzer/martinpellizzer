Sideritis sventenii

Sideritis sventenii, commonly known as the Balkan ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features upright stems covered in fine hairs and produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. Sideritis sventenii is characterized by its medicinal properties, often used in traditional remedies.

Its botanical structure supports its role in both ecological and pharmacological contexts.
