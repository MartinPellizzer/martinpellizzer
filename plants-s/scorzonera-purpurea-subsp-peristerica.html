Scorzonera purpurea subsp. peristerica

Scorzonera purpurea subsp.

peristerica, commonly known as the purple scorzonera, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features a robust, tuberous root system that is often used in traditional medicine.

The plant has dark green, hairy leaves that are basal and rosette-like in growth habit. Its flowers are typically purple or pink, with five petals and a distinctive yellow center.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.
