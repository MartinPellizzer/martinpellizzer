Syngonium willdenowii

Syngonium willdenowii, commonly known as arrowhead vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, arrowhead-like leaves that are typically variegated with white or cream patterns. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments and prefers indirect sunlight. Its rapid growth habit makes it an excellent choice for hanging baskets or trained on trellises.

Syngonium willdenowii is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and is popular in indoor gardening.
