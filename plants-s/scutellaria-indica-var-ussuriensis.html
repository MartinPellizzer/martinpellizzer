Scutellaria indica var. ussuriensis

Scutellaria indica var. ussuriensis, commonly known as the Ussuri skullcap, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features square stems, opposite leaves, and small, two-lipped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its medicinal properties have been studied for their potential anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects.
