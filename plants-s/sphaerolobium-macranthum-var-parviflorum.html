Sphaerolobium macranthum var. parviflorum

Sphaerolobium macranthum var. parviflorum, commonly known as the small-flowered ironwood, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in dry, rocky soils. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, and it is known for its hard, woody stems. It has small, elliptical leaves and is often used in traditional medicine.
