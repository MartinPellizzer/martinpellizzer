Solidago cantoniensis

Solidago cantoniensis, commonly known as the Canton goldenrod, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are arranged in dense, spherical clusters.

Solidago cantoniensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
