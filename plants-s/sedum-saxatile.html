Sedum saxatile

Sedum saxatile, commonly known as the alpine stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to rocky and alpine environments.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil.

Sedum saxatile is often used in rock gardens and as a ground cover in xeriscapes.
