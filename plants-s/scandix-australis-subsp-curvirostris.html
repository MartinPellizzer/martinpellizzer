Scandix australis subsp. curvirostris

Scandix australis subsp.

curvirostris, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sandbur, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia. It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by spiny, bur-like fruits. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slight curl at the edges.

It is adapted to arid environments and is often found in coastal and inland regions.
