Satureja × tagananensis

Satureja × tagananensis, commonly known as the hybrid savory, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its aromatic, small, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of pink to purple flowers. The plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soils. It is valued for its culinary and medicinal uses, particularly for its pungent, thyme-like scent.

Satureja × tagananensis is often used in cooking and traditional herbal remedies.
