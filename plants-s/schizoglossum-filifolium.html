Schizoglossum filifolium

Schizoglossum filifolium, commonly known as the thread-leaved schizoglossum, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are finely divided, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the cooler months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.
