Strobilanthopsis hypericifolia

Strobilanthopsis hypericifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf strobilanthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and showy, clustered inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine, white hairs that give it a distinctive appearance.
