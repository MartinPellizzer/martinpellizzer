Statice hybrida

Statice hybrida, commonly known as the sea thrift, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and spikes of tiny, brightly colored flowers that range from white to pink and purple. The plant thrives in salty, well-drained soils and is often found in dunes and coastal grasslands. Statice hybrida is valued for its drought tolerance and ability to stabilize sandy soils.

Its flowers are popular in floral arrangements due to their long-lasting nature and vibrant hues.
