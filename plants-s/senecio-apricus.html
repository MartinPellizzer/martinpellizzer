Senecio apricus

Senecio apricus, commonly known as the yellow pincushion.

This plant is native to South Africa and belongs to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its dense, yellow-orange flower heads that resemble small pincushions. The leaves are typically narrow and slightly hairy, with a grayish-green hue.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
