Sempervivum × fauconnetii

Sempervivum × fauconnetii, commonly known as the 'Fauconnetii' or 'Crown of Thorns,' is a hybrid succulent plant native to the Alps.

It features dense, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces tall, branched flowering stems that bear small, pink or red flowers in the summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its hardy nature allows it to survive in a variety of climates, though it prefers full sun.
