Stachys macraei

Stachys macraei, commonly known as the Sierra foothill betony, is a flowering plant native to California.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces spikes of purple to pink flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.
