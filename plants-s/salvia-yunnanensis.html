Salvia yunnanensis

Salvia yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan sage, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces striking violet to blue flowers that bloom in late summer. It is often found in mountainous regions with well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and used in traditional medicine.
