Strobilanthes radicans

Strobilanthes radicans, commonly known as the creeping strobilanthes, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and a distinct reddish or purple underside. Its small, clustered inflorescences produce showy, five-petaled flowers in shades of pink to violet.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to form dense ground covers.
