Saxifraga staminosa

Saxifraga staminosa, commonly known as the pink saxifrage, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Arctic and alpine regions.

It typically features dense, mat-forming growth with small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in harsh environments. It thrives in rocky soils and is often found in mountainous or tundra-like habitats.

Saxifraga staminosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in stabilizing soil in extreme climates.
