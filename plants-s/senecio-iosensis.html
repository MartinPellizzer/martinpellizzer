Senecio iosensis

Senecio iosensis, commonly known as the Io Island Groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the island of Io in the Greek Aegean Sea.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky and coastal habitats. The plant produces daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals, blooming in the summer months. It has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Senecio iosensis is notable for its limited distribution and is considered a rare endemic species.
