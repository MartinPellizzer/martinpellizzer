Solanum tonsum

Solanum tonsum, commonly known as the African nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae), which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring alternate, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its flowers are usually yellow or white, and it produces small, round berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
