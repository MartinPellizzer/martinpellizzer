Stigmaphyllon anomalum

Stigmaphyllon anomalum, commonly known as the Brazilian starfruit or yellow starvine, is a climbing shrub native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It is characterized by its distinctive yellow, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, elliptical leaves with a smooth texture and a slightly waxy appearance. Its fruit is a small, fleshy berry that turns from green to yellow as it matures.

Stigmaphyllon anomalum is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant flowers and unique foliage.
