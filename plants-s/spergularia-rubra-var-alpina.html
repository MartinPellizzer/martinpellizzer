Spergularia rubra var. alpina

Spergularia rubra var. alpina, commonly known as alpine sandspurry, is a flowering plant native to alpine and arctic regions.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with small, fleshy leaves adapted to cold and dry environments. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pink or red flowers that bloom in the summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its botanical features make it well-suited for survival in harsh, high-altitude climates.
