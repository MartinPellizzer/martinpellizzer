Sophora yunnanensis

Sophora yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan Sophora, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it ecologically significant.

It is often found in mountainous regions and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
