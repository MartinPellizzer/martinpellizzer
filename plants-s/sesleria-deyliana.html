Sesleria deyliana

Sesleria deyliana, commonly known as the Deyliana false orchid, is a species of grass-like plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Poaceae, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth form. Sesleria deyliana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
