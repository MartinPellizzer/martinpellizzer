Shorea malaanonan

Shorea malaanonan, commonly known as the Malaanonan ironwood, is a tropical tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Dipterocarpaceae family and is characterized by its dense, hard wood that is highly valued for its strength and durability. The tree can grow up to 40 meters tall, with a straight trunk and a broad, spreading crown. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptic in shape, often with a leathery texture.

The plant plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing habitat for various flora and fauna.
