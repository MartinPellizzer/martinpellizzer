Siphocampylus cylindricus

Siphocampylus cylindricus, commonly known as the cylindrical milkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southern United States.

It features slender, upright stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink to purple in color. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy surface.
