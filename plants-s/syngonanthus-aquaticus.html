Syngonanthus aquaticus

Syngonanthus aquaticus, commonly known as the water hyacinth, is a free-floating aquatic plant native to South America.

The plant is known for its rapid growth rate, which can lead to dense mats covering water bodies. Syngonanthus aquaticus has a complex root system that helps it anchor in water while absorbing nutrients.
