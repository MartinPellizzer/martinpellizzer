Stenomesson variegatum

Stenomesson variegatum, commonly known as the variegated grassvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are alternate and trifoliate, with a distinctive variegation pattern.

Stenomesson variegatum is often found in grasslands and open habitats, where it plays a role in soil stabilization and supports local biodiversity.
