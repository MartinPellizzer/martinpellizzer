Sticherus Revolutus

Sticherus revolutus is a rare and delicate moss species native to specific regions of Asia. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found on rocks or decaying wood. Its unique appearance features intricate, rolled leaves that give it its name. This plant plays an important role in its ecosystem by contributing to soil stability and providing habitat for small invertebrates. Due to habitat loss, Sticherus revolutus is increasingly threatened, making conservation efforts crucial for its survival.
