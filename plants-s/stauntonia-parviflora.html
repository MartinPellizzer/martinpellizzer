Stauntonia parviflora

Stauntonia parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered stauntonia, is a deciduous shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features slender, branching stems and is characterized by its clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant produces elliptical, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Stauntonia parviflora is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
