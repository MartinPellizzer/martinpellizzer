Senna Hilariana

Senna hilariana is a striking tropical shrub known for its vibrant yellow flowers and feathery foliage. Native to regions of South America, it thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil. This plant is often cultivated as an ornamental due to its eye-catching blooms and compact growth habit. Its name, meaning "joyful" in Latin, reflects its cheerful appearance and positive energy. Senna hilariana is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing abilities, making it a useful plant in sustainable gardening practices.
