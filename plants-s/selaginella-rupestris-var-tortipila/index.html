Selaginella Rupestris Var. Tortipila

Selaginella rupestris var. tortipila is a unique and resilient plant known for its striking appearance and adaptability. Often found in rocky and arid environments, it thrives in conditions where few other plants can survive. Its foliage features a distinct, twisted pattern that gives it its name, making it a standout in any collection. This variety is prized by horticulturists for its ornamental value and ease of care. Despite its delicate look, it possesses a strong survival instinct, making it a fascinating subject for both plant enthusiasts and researchers.
