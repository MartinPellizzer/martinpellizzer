Stelis atra

Stelis atra, commonly known as the black stelis orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a single, lance-shaped leaf. Its flowers are small, dark purple to black, and are arranged in a spike.

Stelis atra is known for its striking coloration and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
