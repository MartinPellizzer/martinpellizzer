Senecio rahmeri var. rahmeri

Senecio rahmeri var. rahmeri, commonly known as the Kahurangi Senecio, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It is typically found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.
