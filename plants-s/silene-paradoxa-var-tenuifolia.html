Silene paradoxa var. tenuifolia

Silene paradoxa var. tenuifolia, commonly known as the slenderleaf catchfly, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

Silene paradoxa var. tenuifolia is a short-lived perennial that produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds.
