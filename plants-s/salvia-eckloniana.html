Salvia eckloniana

Salvia eckloniana, commonly known as the Cape sage, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It features striking violet to purple flower spikes that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has lance-shaped, aromatic leaves that are gray-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Salvia eckloniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
