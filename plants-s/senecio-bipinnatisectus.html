Senecio bipinnatisectus

Senecio bipinnatisectus, commonly known as the African Daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking yellow-orange flower heads surrounded by white ray florets. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that give it a delicate appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Senecio bipinnatisectus is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
