Silene hoggariensis

Silene hoggariensis, commonly known as the Hogg's campion, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy pink to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, adapting to the harsh environmental conditions of its habitat. Silene hoggariensis is of interest to botanists due to its unique adaptations and limited distribution.
