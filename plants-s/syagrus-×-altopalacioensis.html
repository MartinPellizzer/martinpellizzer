Syagrus × altopalacioensis

Syagrus × altopalacioensis, commonly known as the hybrid palm, is a natural hybrid between Syagrus romanzoffiana and Syagrus oleracea.

It is a tall, slender palm with a slender trunk and a crown of long, narrow leaves. The plant is native to Brazil and is often found in coastal areas and sandy soils. It produces small, yellowish-white flowers that develop into orange-red fruits.

The hybrid is valued for its ornamental qualities and is used in landscaping and horticulture.
