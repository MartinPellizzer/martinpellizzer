Saxifraga rocheliana subsp. velebitica

Saxifraga rocheliana subsp.

velebitica, commonly known as the Velebit saxifrage, is a flowering plant native to the Dinaric Alps, particularly in Croatia. It is a perennial herb with fleshy, basal leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence.

The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters, typically blooming in early summer. It thrives in rocky, calcareous habitats and is adapted to high-altitude environments.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
