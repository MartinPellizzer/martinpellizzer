Stipa austriaca

Stipa austriaca, commonly known as Austrian feather grass, is a perennial grass species native to Central Europe.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-gray blades and fluffy, feathery seed heads that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in landscaping and restoration projects.

Stipa austriaca is also known for its ability to thrive in poor soils and withstand drought conditions.
