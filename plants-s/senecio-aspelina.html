Senecio aspelina

Senecio aspelina, commonly known as the aspelina groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Senecio aspelina is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its drought tolerance and unique appearance.
