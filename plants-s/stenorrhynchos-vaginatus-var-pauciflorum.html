Stenorrhynchos vaginatus var. pauciflorum

Stenorrhynchos vaginatus var. pauciflorum, commonly known as the slender-stemmed orchid, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest understories, where it relies on mycorrhizal fungi for nutrient uptake. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, while its inflorescence bears few, small, colorful blooms.
