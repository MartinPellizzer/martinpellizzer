Sedum telephium var. dumeticola

Sedum telephium var. dumeticola, commonly known as red stonecrop, is a low-growing succulent native to southern Europe.

It features fleshy, upright stems covered in small, rounded leaves that are typically reddish or purple in color. The plant produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant foliage and drought tolerance.
