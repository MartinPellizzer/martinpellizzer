Strelitzia quensonii

Strelitzia quensonii, commonly known as the Orange Bird of Paradise, is a striking member of the Strelitziaceae family native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange flowers, which are surrounded by long, narrow green bracts that resemble a bird in flight. The plant has narrow, strap-like leaves that are bright green and often have a waxy texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Strelitzia quensonii is a popular ornamental plant due to its unique and eye-catching appearance.
