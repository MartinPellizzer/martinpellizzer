Scaevola revoluta subsp. stenostachya

Scaevola revoluta subsp.

stenostachya, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed fanflower, is a perennial shrub native to Australia. It features compact, rounded foliage and produces clusters of bright blue or violet flowers.

The plant has a prostrate to ascending growth habit, often forming dense mats. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for coastal and sandy environments.

Its flowers are typically borne on short pedicels, adding a decorative touch to gardens and natural landscapes.
