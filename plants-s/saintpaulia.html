Saintpaulia

Saintpaulia, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to tropical Africa.

These plants are characterized by their velvety foliage and showy, tubular flowers that come in a wide range of colors. They are typically grown as houseplants due to their preference for warm, humid environments. Saintpaulia species are popular for their compact growth and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Their botanical features make them a favorite among indoor gardeners and plant enthusiasts.
