Stapelia × anemoniflora

Stapelia × anemoniflora, commonly known as the Anemone Stapelia, is a hybrid succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems that store water, adapting it to arid environments. The plant produces striking, lantern-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange with prominent veins, resembling anemones. Its leaves are usually green and somewhat fleshy, and it thrives in bright, indirect light with minimal water.

Stapelia × anemoniflora is a popular choice for collectors due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
