Satyrium bifolium

Satyrium bifolium, commonly known as the two-leaved orchid, is a small terrestrial orchid native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as rocky soils and grasslands. The plant features a slender, upright stem with two leaves, from which emerge a few flowers. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellowish, with a distinctive hooded labellum.

Satyrium bifolium is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in alpine or subalpine environments.
