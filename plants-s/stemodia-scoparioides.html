Stemodia scoparioides

Stemodia scoparioides, commonly known as the narrow-leafed stemodia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pale green to yellowish in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
