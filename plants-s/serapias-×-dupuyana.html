Serapias × dupuyana

Serapias × dupuyana, commonly known as the hybrid orchid orchid, is a cross between Serapias parviflora and Serapias aucubefolia.

It is a hybrid perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe. The plant produces showy, brightly colored flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically with a distinctive central spot. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its botanical interest lies in its hybrid origin and ornamental value.
