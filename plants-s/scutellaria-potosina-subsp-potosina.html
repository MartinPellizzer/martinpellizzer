Scutellaria potosina subsp. potosina

Scutellaria potosina subsp.

potosina, commonly known as Mexican skullcap, is a herbaceous perennial native to Mexico. It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves.

The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue to violet in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or open habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
