Syngramma campyloneuroides

Syngramma campyloneuroides, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, spiral-shaped inflorescence. The plant features showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration pattern, often in shades of yellow and orange. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, adapted to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

Syngramma campyloneuroides is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its ecological role in supporting pollinators.
