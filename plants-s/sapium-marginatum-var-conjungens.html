Sapium marginatum var. conjungens

Sapium marginatum var. conjungens, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive red or purple margin, which give it its ornamental appeal. The plant produces small, inconspicuous green flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its attractive foliage, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
