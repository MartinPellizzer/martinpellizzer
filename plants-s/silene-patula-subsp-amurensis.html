Silene patula subsp. amurensis

Silene patula subsp.

amurensis, commonly known as Amur lady's mantle, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia, particularly Russia and China. It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and clusters of pink or white flowers.

amurensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
