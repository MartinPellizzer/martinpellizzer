Sedum euxinum

Sedum euxinum, commonly known as the Transylvanian sedum, is a succulent plant native to Central and Eastern Europe.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, clustered leaves that are typically green or reddish in color. The plant produces star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It is adapted to grow in rocky or dry soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Sedum euxinum is a hardy species that can thrive in full sun and is relatively low maintenance.
