Scheeria mexicana var. purpurea

Scheeria mexicana var. purpurea, commonly known as the purple-leafed scheeria, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking violet to purple foliage. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or white, arranged in whorled clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.
