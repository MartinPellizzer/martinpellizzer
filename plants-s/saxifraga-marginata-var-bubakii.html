Saxifraga marginata var. bubakii

Saxifraga marginata var. bubakii, commonly known as the Balkan saxifrage, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils and full sun. The plant features basal rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with a distinctive white or pale margin. It produces small, pinkish-white flowers in early summer, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems.

This variety is noted for its hardiness and ability to withstand harsh mountain climates.
