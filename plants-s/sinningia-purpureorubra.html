Sinningia purpureorubra

Sinningia purpureorubra, commonly known as the purple-red gloxinia, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range from deep purple to reddish hues. The plant features broad, velvety leaves that are often a rich green with a slightly waxy texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is typically grown as a houseplant or in greenhouse settings.

Sinningia purpureorubra is prized for its ornamental value and is a popular choice among plant enthusiasts.
