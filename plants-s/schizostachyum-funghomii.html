Schizostachyum funghomii

Schizostachyum funghomii, commonly known as the Hainan bamboo, is a species of grass native to southern China, particularly Hainan Island.

It is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 10 meters. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in panicles, typical of the grass family. Its leaves are long and narrow, with a smooth texture and a bluish-green color.

Schizostachyum funghomii is often used in traditional Chinese medicine and as a source of sustainable building materials.
