Solanum cyathophorum

Solanum cyathophorum, commonly known as the purple bellflower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, bell-shaped purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has hairy, glandular leaves. Its fruit is a fleshy, orange-red berry that contains several seeds.

Solanum cyathophorum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
