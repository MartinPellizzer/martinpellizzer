Specularia speculum-veneris var. racemosa

Specularia speculum-veneris var. racemosa, commonly known as the Venus' Looking Glass, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, translucent, papery bracts that resemble a mirror, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy perennial suitable for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and cold hardiness make it a prized addition to alpine gardens.
