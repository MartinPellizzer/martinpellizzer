Sorghum cabanisii

Sorghum cabanisii, commonly known as the Cape York sorghum, is a perennial grass native to Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that are typically green or reddish in color. Sorghum cabanisii is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it resilient to drought conditions.

It is often found in open woodlands and grasslands, contributing to the ecological diversity of its native habitat.
