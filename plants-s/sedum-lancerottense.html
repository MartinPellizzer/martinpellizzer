Sedum lancerottense

Sedum lancerottense, commonly known as the Lancerottense stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy choice for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular ornamental plant.
