Stomatium duthieae

Stomatium duthieae, commonly known as the Duthie's bladderwort, is a carnivorous plant native to the wetlands of northern Scotland.

It is characterized by its small, bladder-like structures that trap and digest small aquatic organisms. The plant has thin, ribbon-like leaves that float on the water's surface, facilitating photosynthesis. Its unique morphology allows it to thrive in nutrient-poor environments by supplementing its diet with captured prey.
