Sterculia reticulata

Sterculia reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined sterculia, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves with a distinctive net-like venation pattern. The plant produces showy, cream-colored flowers that are typically clustered in panicles. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release flat, winged seeds.

The tree is often valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local medicine and crafts.
