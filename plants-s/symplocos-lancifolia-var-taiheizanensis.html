Symplocos lancifolia var. taiheizanensis

Symplocos lancifolia var. taiheizanensis, commonly known as the Taiheizan evergreen holly, is a shrub native to eastern Asia, particularly found in China.

It belongs to the family Ericaceae and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by bright red berries that are an important food source for birds.
