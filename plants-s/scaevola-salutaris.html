Scaevola salutaris

Scaevola salutaris, commonly known as the beach morning glory, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of Australia.

It is a low-growing shrub with bright blue or violet flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves and is well adapted to sandy, saline soils. It is often found in dunes and along shorelines, where it helps stabilize the soil.

Scaevola salutaris is also known for its ability to thrive in harsh coastal conditions.
