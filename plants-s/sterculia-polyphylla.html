Sterculia polyphylla

Sterculia polyphylla, commonly known as the broad-leaf sterculia, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing several seeds. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The tree is valued for its hard, durable wood and is also used in traditional medicine.
