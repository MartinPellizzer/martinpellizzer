Sempervivum atlanticum

Sempervivum atlanticum, commonly known as the Atlantic houseleek, is a succulent plant native to the coastal regions of the British Isles and the Iberian Peninsula.

It features rosettes of fleshy, thick leaves that are typically green with reddish or purplish hues, especially in full sun. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It is well adapted to rocky, well-drained soils and can tolerate coastal winds and salt spray.

Sempervivum atlanticum is a low-maintenance plant that thrives in full sun and is often used in rock gardens or as a container plant.
