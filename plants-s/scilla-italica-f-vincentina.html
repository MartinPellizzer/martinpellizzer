Scilla italica f. vincentina

Scilla italica f. vincentina, commonly known as the Vincentine squill, is a perennial bulbous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from March to April. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge from a basal rosette of bulbs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its early blooming habit and delicate appearance.
