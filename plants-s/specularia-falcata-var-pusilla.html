Specularia falcata var. pusilla

Specularia falcata var. pusilla, commonly known as the dwarf fairy lantern, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It produces delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pale yellow to deep orange, resembling small lanterns. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, white pubescence. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and compact size, it is often cultivated in rock gardens and alpine settings.
