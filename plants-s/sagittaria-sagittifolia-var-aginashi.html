Sagittaria sagittifolia var. aginashi

Sagittaria sagittifolia var. aginashi, commonly known as the Japanese arrowhead, is a striking aquatic plant native to East Asia.

It features arrow-shaped leaves that emerge from submerged stems, creating a distinctive visual appearance in water gardens. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom above the water surface, adding ornamental value. It thrives in shallow freshwater environments, preferring full sun to partial shade.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread easily, making it a popular choice for pond and aquarium decoration.
