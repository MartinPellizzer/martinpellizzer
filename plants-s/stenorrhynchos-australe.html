Stenorrhynchos australe

Stenorrhynchos australe, commonly known as the Australian grass tree, is a unique plant native to the coastal regions of southern Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, grass-like leaves that form a dense rosette around a central stem. The plant produces striking, bottle-shaped inflorescences that bear small, white or cream-colored flowers. Stenorrhynchos australe is adapted to sandy soils and can tolerate drought conditions.
