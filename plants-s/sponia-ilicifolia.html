Sponia ilicifolia

Sponia ilicifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf sponia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy ornamental plant.

Sponia ilicifolia is often used in gardens for its aesthetic appeal and drought tolerance.
