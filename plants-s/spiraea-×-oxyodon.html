Spiraea × oxyodon

Spiraea × oxyodon, commonly known as the weeping spiraea, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its cascading clusters of white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, arching branches that give it a graceful, weeping appearance. Its leaves are narrow and dark green, providing a striking contrast to the pale flowers.

Spiraea × oxyodon is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil conditions.
