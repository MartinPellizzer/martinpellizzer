Syringodea montana

Syringodea montana, commonly known as the mountain syringa, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of central and eastern Asia.

Syringodea montana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its showy blossoms.
