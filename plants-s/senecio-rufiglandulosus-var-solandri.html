Senecio rufiglandulosus var. solandri

Senecio rufiglandulosus var. solandri, commonly known as the Solander's ragged robin, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink to purple ray flowers and serrated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. It produces small, spiky seed heads that are adapted for wind dispersal.

Its botanical features make it an attractive and ecologically significant species in its native environment.
