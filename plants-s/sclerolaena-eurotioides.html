Sclerolaena eurotioides

Sclerolaena eurotioides, commonly known as the African dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers develop into small, puffball-like seed heads.
