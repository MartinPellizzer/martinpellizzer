Stapelia kwebensis

Stapelia kwebensis, commonly known as the Kwebeni Stapelia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed stems that store water. The plant produces striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. These flowers are often borne on long, slender stems and bloom during the warmer months.

Stapelia kwebensis is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
