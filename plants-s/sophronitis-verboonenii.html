Sophronitis verboonenii

Sophronitis verboonenii, commonly known as the Verboonen's orchid, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature a vibrant purple or pink coloration. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are borne on terminal inflorescences and are often fragrant, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Sophronitis verboonenii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
