Senecio ragonesei

Senecio ragonesei, commonly known as the Ragonese's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the island of Socotra.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in arid and rocky environments, showcasing its adaptation to harsh conditions.

Due to its unique appearance and restricted habitat, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
