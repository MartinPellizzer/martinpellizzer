Schefflera diguana

Schefflera diguana, commonly known as the Philippine umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like crown of large, leathery, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by bright red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as a ornamental plant.

Schefflera diguana is also valued for its air-purifying properties and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
