Streptostachys lanciflora

Streptostachys lanciflora, commonly known as the sharp-leaved streptostachys, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often serrated along the edges. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, which bloom during the warm season. Streptostachys lanciflora thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticultural and ecological studies.
