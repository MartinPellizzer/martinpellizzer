Sarcochilus pusillus

Sarcochilus pusillus, commonly known as the dwarf rock orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its small, compact growth habit and produces delicate, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly habitats, often in coastal or mountainous regions. Its leaves are fleshy and succulent, adapted to retain moisture in arid environments.
