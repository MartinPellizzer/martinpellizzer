Sideritis × candelii var. rigida

Sideritis × candelii var. rigida, commonly known as rigid sidereal, is a hybrid shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is cultivated in gardens and traditional herbal practices.
