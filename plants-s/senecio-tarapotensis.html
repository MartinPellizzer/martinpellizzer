Senecio tarapotensis

Senecio tarapotensis, commonly known as the Andean groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the high Andes of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Senecio tarapotensis is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and shrublands at elevations above 3,000 meters.
