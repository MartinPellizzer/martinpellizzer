Sciodaphyllum crassilimbum

Sciodaphyllum crassilimbum, commonly known as the thick-leafed shade plant, is a unique species native to the dense, humid undergrowth of tropical rainforests.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white, which are pollinated by insects rather than wind. Its roots are fibrous and help stabilize the soil in its natural habitat.
