Sedum dasyphyllum var. suendermannii

Sedum dasyphyllum var. suendermannii, commonly known as the Suendermann's stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves to survive drought conditions.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
