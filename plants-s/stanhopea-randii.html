Stanhopea randii

Stanhopea randii, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a tropical orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers with intricate patterns and a distinctive, elongated shape. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers bloom during the dry season, often in clusters of several blooms.

Stanhopea randii is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
