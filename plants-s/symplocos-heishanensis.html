Symplocos heishanensis

Symplocos heishanensis, commonly known as the Heishan evergreen hawthorn, is a shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces edible berries that are typically red or yellow when mature. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
