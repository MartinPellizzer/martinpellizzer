Scutellaria alaschanica

Scutellaria alaschanica, commonly known as the Altai skullcap, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular blue or violet flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Scutellaria alaschanica is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
