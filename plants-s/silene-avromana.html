Silene avromana

Silene avromana, commonly known as the Avroman's campion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its pink to purple flowers and slender, branching stems. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Silene avromana is part of the Caryophyllaceae family, known for its showy, often pink or red flowers.
