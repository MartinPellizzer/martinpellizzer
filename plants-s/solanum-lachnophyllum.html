Solanum lachnophyllum

Solanum lachnophyllum, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its small, silver-gray leaves that reflect light, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its fruit is a spherical berry that turns from green to red as it matures.

Solanum lachnophyllum is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is part of the nightshade family, which includes both edible and toxic species.
