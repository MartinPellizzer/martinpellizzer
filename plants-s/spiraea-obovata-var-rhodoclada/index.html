Spiraea Obovata Var. Rhodoclada

Spiraea obovata var. rhodoclada is a striking shrub known for its vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in early summer. This variant of Spiraea obovata is particularly prized for its dense clusters of pink or white blossoms that create a dramatic visual impact in gardens. Native to parts of Asia, it thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sun conditions. Its compact growth habit makes it ideal for small spaces, borders, or as a foundation plant. Despite its ornamental appeal, it also offers ecological benefits by attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
