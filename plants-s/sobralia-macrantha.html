Sobralia macrantha

Sobralia macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered Sobralia, is a tropical plant belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences that bear large, colorful flowers with a distinct lip. Its leaves are typically broad and ovate, arranged in a rosette pattern.

Sobralia macrantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses due to its vibrant blooms and unique appearance.
