Stellaria zangnanensis

Stellaria zangnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan starwort, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky areas, thriving in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are opposite and lanceolate, while its stems are usually hairy.

Stellaria zangnanensis is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and limited distribution.
