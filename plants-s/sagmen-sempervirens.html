Sagmen sempervirens

Sagmen sempervirens, commonly known as the evergreen sagmen, is a hardy plant native to specific regions with a preference for well-drained soils.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy, providing an attractive visual element in gardens. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often overlooked but play a role in its reproductive cycle. Its evergreen nature allows it to maintain foliage year-round, making it a valuable addition to ornamental landscapes.

Due to its resilience, it is often used in rock gardens or as a border plant in temperate climates.
