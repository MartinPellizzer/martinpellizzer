Scabiosa ucranica var. eburnea

Scabiosa ucranica var. eburnea, commonly known as the white scabious, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Ukraine.

It features delicate, pinkish-white flower clusters that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden borders.

Its flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, contributing to ecological biodiversity.
