Senna ruiziana

Senna ruiziana, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall, characterized by its pinnate leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces long, cylindrical pods that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Senna ruiziana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral displays.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
