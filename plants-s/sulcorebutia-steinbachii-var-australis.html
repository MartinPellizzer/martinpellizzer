Sulcorebutia steinbachii var. australis

Sulcorebutia steinbachii var. australis, commonly known as the Australian rock rose, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of Australia.

It features a compact, rounded growth habit with thick, ribbed stems that store water, enabling it to thrive in dry conditions. The plant produces clusters of small, vibrant pink or red flowers during the warmer months. Its leaves are typically green and fleshy, reducing water loss through transpiration.

It is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and drought tolerance.
