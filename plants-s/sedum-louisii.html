Sedum louisii

Sedum louisii, commonly known as the Louisiana stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the southeastern United States.

It features fleshy, upright stems and opposite, spoon-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped, yellow flowers in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Sedum louisii is drought-tolerant and requires minimal maintenance once established.
