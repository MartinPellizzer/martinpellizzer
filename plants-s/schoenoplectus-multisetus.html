Schoenoplectus multisetus

Schoenoplectus multisetus, commonly known as the many-bracted sedge, is a flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and shallow water areas. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.
