Schefflera chinpingensis

Schefflera chinpingensis, commonly known as the Chinese umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to southern China.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of large, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellowish-white flowers followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Schefflera chinpingensis is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
