Solanum melongena var. depressum

Solanum melongena var. depressum, commonly known as the dwarf eggplant, is a compact variety of the eggplant species.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its small, round fruits that are typically purple in color. The plant has a bushy growth habit with dark green, lobed leaves and hairy stems. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value as well as for its edible fruits.

This variety is well-suited for container gardening due to its compact size.
