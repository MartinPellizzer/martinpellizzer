Staurogyne bella

Staurogyne bella, commonly known as the small water plant or water starwort, is a small aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in shallow freshwater environments such as ponds, lakes, and slow-moving streams. The plant has small, narrow leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern, and it produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers. Staurogyne bella is often used in aquariums due to its hardiness and ability to thrive in various water conditions.
