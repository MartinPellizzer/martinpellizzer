Stipa trollii

Stipa trollii, commonly known as the Troll's grass, is a species of grass native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and produces dense, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically thrives in cold, arid environments and is adapted to withstand harsh climatic conditions. Its leaves are narrow and often have a bluish-green hue, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Stipa trollii plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native habitat and is of interest to botanists studying alpine flora.
