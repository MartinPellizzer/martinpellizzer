Solanum lasiocarpum var. domesticum

Solanum lasiocarpum var. domesticum, commonly known as the common nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into berries containing toxic compounds. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with a slightly hairy texture.

It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
