Scindapsus mamillifer

Scindapsus mamillifer, commonly known as the velvet plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features waxy, dark green leaves with a velvety texture, often adorned with intricate patterns of silver or cream. The plant is part of the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence structure consisting of a spadix and spathe. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity to maintain its lush foliage.

Due to its ornamental appeal, it is popular as a houseplant and is relatively easy to care for.
