Sohnreyia excelsa

Sohnreyia excelsa, commonly known as the giant flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a striking visual display. The plant has pinnate leaves and produces woody pods containing seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Its botanical features make it a popular ornamental plant in tropical gardens.
