Stelis ovatipetala

Stelis ovatipetala, commonly known as the ovate-petaled stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features slender, wiry stems that often hang from trees, and its leaves are elongated and somewhat fleshy. The flowers are small, fragrant, and typically white or pale pink, arranged in a loose inflorescence. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
