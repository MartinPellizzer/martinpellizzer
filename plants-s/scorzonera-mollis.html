Scorzonera mollis

Scorzonera mollis, commonly known as the meadow scorzonera, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and rocky areas. The plant features a robust root system, which is often edible and has a nutty flavor. Its flowers are yellow with five petals and are arranged in a cluster at the top of the stem.

Scorzonera mollis is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
