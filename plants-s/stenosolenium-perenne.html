Stenosolenium perenne

Stenosolenium perenne, commonly known as the Narrow-leaved Lithops, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, fleshy leaves that are often mistaken for stones, a feature that aids in its survival in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the winter months, adding a delicate touch of color to its rocky habitat. Its leaves are typically narrow and ribbed, storing water efficiently to withstand drought conditions.

Stenosolenium perenne is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
