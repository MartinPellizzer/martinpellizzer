Schefflera paniculata

Schefflera paniculata, commonly known as the umbrella tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of large, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers arranged in panicles, which are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden areas.

Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor landscaping.
