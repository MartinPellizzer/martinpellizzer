Steudnera colocasiifolia var. discolor

Steudnera colocasiifolia var. discolor, commonly known as the variegated arrowhead plant, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its distinctive, arrow-shaped leaves.

The leaves are typically a mix of green, white, and yellow, creating a variegated appearance that adds visual interest to any setting. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil. This plant is native to Central and South America and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Its foliage is not only aesthetically pleasing but also demonstrates the plant's adaptability to various growing conditions.
