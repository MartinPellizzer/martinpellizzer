Sideritis cretica subsp. cretica

Sideritis cretica subsp.

cretica, commonly known as Cretan sainfoin, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region, particularly Crete. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs.

The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit, narrow leaves, and clusters of small, pale blue to violet flowers. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats. Sideritis cretica subsp.

cretica is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
