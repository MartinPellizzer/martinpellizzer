Sinosassafras

Sinosassafras, commonly known as Chinese sassafras, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and distinctive, often clustered flowers. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that are typically followed by small, orange-red fruits. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a serrated margin, often with a fragrant scent.

Sinosassafras is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
