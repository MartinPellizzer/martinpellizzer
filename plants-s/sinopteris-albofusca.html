Sinopteris albofusca

Sinopteris albofusca, commonly known as the pale-brown tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk covered in a dense layer of pale brown, fibrous roots. The fronds are pinnately compound, with a feathery appearance and a pale green coloration. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical forests.

This plant plays a significant role in its ecosystem by providing habitat and contributing to soil stability.
