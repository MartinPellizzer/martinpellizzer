Serratula hsinganensis

Serratula hsinganensis, commonly known as the Hsianan goldenweed, is a flowering plant native to northern China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its yellow flowers bloom in the summer, adding a striking visual element to its natural habitat.

Serratula hsinganensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
