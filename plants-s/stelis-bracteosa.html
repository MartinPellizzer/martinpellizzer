Stelis bracteosa

Stelis bracteosa, commonly known as the bracteosa orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant typically produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are strap-like and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in moisture retention.

Stelis bracteosa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
