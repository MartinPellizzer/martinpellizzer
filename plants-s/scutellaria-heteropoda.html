Scutellaria heteropoda

Scutellaria heteropoda, commonly known as the Chinese skullcap, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are typically oval and have a slightly hairy texture.

Scutellaria heteropoda is valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
