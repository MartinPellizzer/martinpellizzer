Spicanta fraxinea

Spicanta fraxinea, commonly known as the fragrant ash.

It is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, particularly China and Japan. The plant is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are simple and opposite, with a smooth texture and a slight sheen.

Spicanta fraxinea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant scent.
