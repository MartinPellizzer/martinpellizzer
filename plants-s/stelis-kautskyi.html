Stelis kautskyi

Stelis kautskyi, commonly known as the Kautsky's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant has a fibrous root system and prefers well-drained, nutrient-rich soil. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Stelis kautskyi is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
