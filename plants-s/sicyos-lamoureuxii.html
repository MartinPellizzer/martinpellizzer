Sicyos lamoureuxii

Sicyos lamoureuxii, commonly known as the creeping gourd, is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its trailing stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that give way to elongated, spiny fruits. Native to tropical regions of South America, it thrives in warm, humid climates.
