Stelis catharinensis

Stelis catharinensis, commonly known as the Brazilian orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its upright, leafy stems and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

Stelis catharinensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
