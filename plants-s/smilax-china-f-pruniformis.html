Smilax china f. pruniformis

Smilax china f. pruniformis, commonly known as the Chinese devil's backbone, is a climbing shrub native to China.

It features prominently spined stems and heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive waxy texture. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that develop into red, fleshy berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique foliage and persistent berries.
