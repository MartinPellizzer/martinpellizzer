Sphaerocarya wallichiana

Sphaerocarya wallichiana, commonly known as the Himalayan rose apple, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the Himalayas and is characterized by its large, showy white or pink flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces edible, fragrant fruits that are often used in traditional cuisines. It thrives in temperate climates and is valued for both its ornamental and culinary uses.

Sphaerocarya wallichiana is also noted for its hardy nature and ability to grow in a variety of soil conditions.
