Statice Maritima Var. Pubigera

Statice maritima var. pubigera is a resilient coastal plant known for its ability to thrive in harsh, saline environments. This variant of Statice maritima is commonly found along sandy shores and dunes where it withstands strong winds and salt spray. Its small, colorful flowers add a vibrant touch to the rugged landscape of maritime regions. The plant's fibrous roots help stabilize sand dunes, making it an important species for coastal erosion control. Its unique adaptations make it a valuable subject for ecological and botanical study.
