Saxifraga lamashanensis

Saxifraga lamashanensis, commonly known as the Lamashan saxifrage, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming growth habit. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers during the summer months. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to high-altitude environments.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
