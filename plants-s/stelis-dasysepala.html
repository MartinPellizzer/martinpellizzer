Stelis dasysepala

Stelis dasysepala, commonly known as the showy stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in the Philippines and Indonesia. The plant features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with fleshy leaves that store water. Its flowers are typically white or pale green, with a distinctive sepals and petals arrangement.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media for optimal growth.
