Streptocarpus rhodesianus subsp. rhodesianus

Streptocarpus rhodesianus subsp.

rhodesianus, commonly known as the Rhodesian streptocarpus, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa. It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple.
