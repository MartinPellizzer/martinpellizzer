Solidago caucasica

Solidago caucasica, commonly known as Caucasian goldenrod, is a perennial herb native to the Caucasus region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features upright, branching stems covered in fine, hairy foliage. Its flowers are yellow, arranged in dense, fluffy clusters at the tips of the stems.

It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and forest edges.
