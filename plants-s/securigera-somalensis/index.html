Securigera Somalensis

Securigera somalensis is a rare and unique flowering plant native to the arid regions of Somalia. It belongs to the legume family and is adapted to thrive in harsh, dry environments. This plant is known for its distinctive yellow flowers and ability to fix nitrogen in the soil. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species. Studying Securigera somalensis provides valuable insights into plant resilience in extreme climates.
