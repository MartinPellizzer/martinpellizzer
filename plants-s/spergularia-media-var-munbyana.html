Spergularia media var. munbyana

Spergularia media var. munbyana, commonly known as the narrowleaf sand mat, is a flowering plant native to sandy and coastal environments.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit with small, narrow leaves. The plant produces tiny, pinkish to reddish flowers arranged in dense clusters, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dunes or disturbed coastal areas.

This variety is adapted to harsh environmental conditions, making it an important component of coastal vegetation.
