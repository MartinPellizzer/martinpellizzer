Secamone dewevrei

Secamone dewevrei, commonly known as the African moonflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that open at night. The plant has a woody stem and produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Its leaves are opposite and elliptical, often with a slightly hairy surface.
