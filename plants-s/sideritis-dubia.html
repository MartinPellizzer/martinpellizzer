Sideritis dubia

Sideritis dubia, commonly known as the Greek violet, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its small, violet-blue flowers and hairy, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in rocky and dry environments, often found in mountainous areas.

Sideritis dubia is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
