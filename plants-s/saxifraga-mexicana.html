Saxifraga mexicana

Saxifraga mexicana, commonly known as the Mexican saxifrage, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves and clusters of small, white or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. It is often found in high-altitude regions, where it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Saxifraga mexicana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
