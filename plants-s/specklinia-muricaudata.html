Specklinia muricaudata

Specklinia muricaudata, commonly known as the hairy-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a slight blush of purple. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is typically found in forest understories.

Specklinia muricaudata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
