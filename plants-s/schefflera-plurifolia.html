Schefflera plurifolia

Schefflera plurifolia, commonly known as the Brazilian Schefflera, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like clusters of leaves, which give it a lush, full appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by bright red berries, attracting various bird species. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Schefflera plurifolia is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
