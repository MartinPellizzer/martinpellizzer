Stenocactus lancifer

Stenocactus lancifer, commonly known as the lancifer cactus, is a species of flowering cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are often ribbed and covered in a layer of white, waxy hair. The plant produces yellow or orange flowers that bloom in summer, adding a striking visual contrast to its green stems. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it well-suited for desert gardens.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
