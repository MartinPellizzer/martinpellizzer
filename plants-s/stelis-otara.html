Stelis otara

Stelis otara, commonly known as the Otara orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant typically features slender pseudobulbs and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Stelis otara thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
