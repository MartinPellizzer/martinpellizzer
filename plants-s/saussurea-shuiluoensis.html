Saussurea shuiluoensis

Saussurea shuiluoensis, commonly known as the Shuiluo saussurea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the high-altitude regions of China, particularly in the Tibetan Plateau and adjacent areas. The plant is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems and dense clusters of yellow to orange flowers. Its leaves are usually linear and covered with a fine layer of hair, adapting to the harsh environmental conditions.
