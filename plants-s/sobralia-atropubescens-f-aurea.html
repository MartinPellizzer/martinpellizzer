Sobralia atropubescens f. aurea

Sobralia atropubescens f. aurea, commonly known as the golden sobralia, is a hybrid orchid species native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers, which contrast with its dark green foliage. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. It produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous small, fragrant blooms.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
