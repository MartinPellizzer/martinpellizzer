Silene integripetala subsp. integripetala

Silene integripetala subsp.

integripetala, commonly known as the smooth-petaled catchfly, is a flowering plant native to parts of North America. It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and showy, pink to red flowers.
