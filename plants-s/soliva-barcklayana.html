Soliva barcklayana

Soliva barcklayana, commonly known as the slender soliva, is a flowering plant native to the grasslands of southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, pea-like flowers. The plant produces trifoliate leaves, which are compound leaves consisting of three leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.

Soliva barcklayana is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
