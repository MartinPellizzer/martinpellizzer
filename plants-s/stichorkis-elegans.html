Stichorkis elegans

Stichorkis elegans, commonly known as the elegant orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in shaded, moist environments within the undergrowth of forests. The plant features slender, erect stems with opposite, ovate leaves that have a velvety texture. Its flowers are small, clustered in racemes, and are usually pale green to white in color.

Stichorkis elegans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
