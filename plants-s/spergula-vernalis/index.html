Spergula Vernalis

Spergula vernalis is a small, herbaceous plant that thrives in moist, shaded environments. It is commonly found in woodland areas and along streambanks during the spring season. Known for its delicate blue or purple flowers, it adds a subtle beauty to the early spring landscape. This plant plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and maintaining ecological balance. Despite its modest appearance, Spergula vernalis is a valuable component of diverse plant communities.
