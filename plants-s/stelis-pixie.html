Stelis pixie

Stelis pixie, commonly known as the pixie orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in tropical rainforests. The plant has a compact growth habit with narrow, dark green leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Stelis pixie is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in shaded, humid environments.
