Scabiosa velutina f. verbascifolia

Scabiosa velutina f. verbascifolia, commonly known as the velvet scabious, is a perennial flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It features dense, woolly foliage that gives it a soft, velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, pink or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its unique appearance and long blooming period make it a popular choice among gardeners.
