Sericocoma avolans

Sericocoma avolans, commonly known as the silky bristlegrass, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant features fine, silky hairs on its stems and leaves, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its inflorescence consists of a loose cluster of spikelets, which are often pale green to brown in color.
