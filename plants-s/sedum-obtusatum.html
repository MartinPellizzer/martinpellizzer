Sedum obtusatum

Sedum obtusatum, commonly known as the bluntleaf stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically gray-green in color, adapted to store water. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
