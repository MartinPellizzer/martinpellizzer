Sideritis × garrigae

Sideritis × garrigae, commonly known as the hybrid germander, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Sideritis × garrigae is characterized by its hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves.

It is often used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
