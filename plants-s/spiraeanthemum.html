Spiraeanthemum

Spiraeanthemum, commonly known as the snowbell plant, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Rosaceae.

It is characterized by its small, bell-shaped flowers that often appear in clusters, typically white or pale pink in color. The leaves are usually simple, alternate, and have a slightly serrated edge. Native to regions of Asia, including China and Japan, it thrives in temperate climates.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in garden settings.
