Sorbus rockii

Sorbus rockii, commonly known as the Chinese whitebeam, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant produces small, yellowish fruits that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans. It is often found in mountainous regions and is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological benefits.

Sorbus rockii is also used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
