Satureja sphaciotica

Satureja sphaciotica, commonly known as mountain thyme, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, small, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of pink or white flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or mountainous areas. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes other aromatic herbs like mint and oregano.

Satureja sphaciotica is valued for its culinary and medicinal uses, particularly in traditional herbal remedies.
