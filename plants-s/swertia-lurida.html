Swertia lurida

Swertia lurida, commonly known as the blue gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, thriving in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged oppositely along the stem.

Swertia lurida is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
