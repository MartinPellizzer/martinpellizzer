Staurogyne humilis

Staurogyne humilis, commonly known as small-leaved water clover, is a species of aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its small, ovate leaves that are often arranged in a floating or submerged manner. The plant typically grows in still or slow-moving freshwater environments such as ponds and lakes. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, usually appearing in clusters near the water surface.

Staurogyne humilis plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for microorganisms and invertebrates.
