Seaforthia appendiculata

Seaforthia appendiculata, commonly known as the slender seaforthia, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has a compact growth habit with narrow, grass-like leaves that help it conserve water in its arid environment. It thrives in well-drained sandy soils and is often found growing near the shoreline.

Seaforthia appendiculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in coastal gardens.
