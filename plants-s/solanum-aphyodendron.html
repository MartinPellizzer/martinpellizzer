Solanum aphyodendron

Solanum aphyodendron, commonly known as the leafless nightshade, is a member of the Solanaceae family.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its lack of leaves, which is a unique botanical feature. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruits are typically berries that contain toxic compounds, making it potentially harmful to animals and humans.

This species is native to parts of Africa and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.
