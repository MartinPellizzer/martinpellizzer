Setaria luxurians

Setaria luxurians, commonly known as slender false panicgrass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Setaria luxurians is a hardy perennial that is sometimes used in landscaping for its ornamental appeal.
