Seseli rigidum subsp. hirtulum

Seseli rigidum subsp.

hirtulum, commonly known as hairy rigid seseli, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its umbellate inflorescence and aromatic foliage.
