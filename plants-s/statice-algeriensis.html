Statice algeriensis

Statice algeriensis, commonly known as Algerian glassweed, is a flowering plant native to North Africa.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its dense, spike-like inflorescence. The plant produces small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. It thrives in saline and coastal environments, often found in dunes and sandy soils.

Statice algeriensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is used in dried floral arrangements.
