Syzygium roemeri

Syzygium roemeri, commonly known as the Hawaiian hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and elliptical in shape, often with a slightly waxy texture.

Syzygium roemeri thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.
