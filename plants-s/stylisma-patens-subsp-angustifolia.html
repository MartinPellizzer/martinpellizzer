Stylisma patens subsp. angustifolia

Stylisma patens subsp.

angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved stylisma, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its slender, angular stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
