Sinocalamus variostriatus

Sinocalamus variostriatus, commonly known as the variable-striped bamboo, is a species of grass belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its distinctive variegated leaf patterns, which include alternating light and dark green stripes. The plant typically grows in moist, temperate climates and is often found in mountainous regions. Its culms are hollow and jointed, with a smooth, sometimes woody texture.

Sinocalamus variostriatus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens and parks for its aesthetic foliage.
