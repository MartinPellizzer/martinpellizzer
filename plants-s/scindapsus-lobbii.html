Scindapsus lobbii

Scindapsus lobbii, commonly known as the silver vine, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that display shades of silver and green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Scindapsus lobbii is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
