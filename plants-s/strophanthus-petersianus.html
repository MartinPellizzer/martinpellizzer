Strophanthus petersianus

Strophanthus petersianus, commonly known as the Petersianus arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking orange-red flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant is a deciduous shrub or small tree, typically growing up to 6 meters in height. Its leaves are compound, with elongated leaflets that give it a distinctive appearance.

The plant is also notable for its medicinal properties, though it contains toxic compounds that require careful handling.
