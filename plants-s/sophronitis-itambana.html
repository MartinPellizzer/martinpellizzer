Sophronitis itambana

Sophronitis itambana, commonly known as the Itambana orchid, is a rare and striking species native to the high altitudes of Tanzania.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, showy flowers with a unique coloration pattern. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, clinging to tree trunks in humid, shaded forest environments.
