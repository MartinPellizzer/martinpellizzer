Stachys recta var. sideritidoides

Stachys recta var. sideritidoides, commonly known as the silver carpet plant, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, mat-forming foliage with silvery-gray leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces spikes of pale purple to pink flowers in the summer months, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and ground cover.
