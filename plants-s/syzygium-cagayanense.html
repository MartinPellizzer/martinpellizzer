Syzygium cagayanense

Syzygium cagayanense, commonly known as the Cagayan apple, is a species of flowering plant in the myrtaceae family.

It is native to the Philippines, specifically the Cagayan Valley region. The plant is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall. Its leaves are elliptical, glossy, and have a distinct aromatic scent.

The fruit is a small, round, and edible berry with a sweet and tangy flavor.
