Stelis montserratii

Stelis montserratii, commonly known as theMontserrat orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Caribbean islands.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant has a slender, upright stem with several fleshy, elliptical leaves. Its flowers are small, white or pale pink, and are clustered in a loose inflorescence.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or tropical gardens.
