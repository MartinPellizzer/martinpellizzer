Spenceria parviflora

Spenceria parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered spenceria, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, rounded foliage composed of small, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white to pale pink, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal make it a favored plant among succulent enthusiasts.
