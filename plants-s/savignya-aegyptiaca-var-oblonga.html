Savignya aegyptiaca var. oblonga

Savignya aegyptiaca var. oblonga, commonly known as the Egyptian savignya, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and historical use in traditional medicine.
