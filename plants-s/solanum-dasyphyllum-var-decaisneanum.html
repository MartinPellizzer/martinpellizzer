Solanum dasyphyllum var. decaisneanum

Solanum dasyphyllum var. decaisneanum, commonly known as the Decaisne's nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features dark green, ovate leaves with a velvety texture and produces small, clustered flowers that range from white to pale yellow. The plant bears purple-black berries that are toxic to humans but serve as a food source for certain wildlife. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Botanically, it is characterized by its distinctive floral structure and unique leaf morphology.
