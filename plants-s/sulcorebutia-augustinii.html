Sulcorebutia augustinii

Sulcorebutia augustinii, commonly known as the Augustine's crassula, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that are often tinged with red or purple, especially in full sun. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. Its leaves are thick and waxy, adapted to store water in arid environments.

Sulcorebutia augustinii is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its drought tolerance and compact growth habit.
