Schoenocephalium martianum

Schoenocephalium martianum, commonly known as the Martian beauty, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy, yellow-orange flower clusters that bloom in the summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms.
