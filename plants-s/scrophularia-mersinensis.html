Scrophularia mersinensis

Scrophularia mersinensis, commonly known as the Mersin scrophularia, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in rocky or dry habitats and is often found in mountainous regions.

Scrophularia mersinensis is of interest to botanists due to its unique adaptations and limited distribution.
