Stonesia gracilis

Stonesia gracilis, commonly known as the slender stoneia, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, wooded areas and thrives in partial to full shade. The plant features slender, upright stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its small, greenish-white flowers appear in clusters during late spring to early summer.

Stonesia gracilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local wildlife.
