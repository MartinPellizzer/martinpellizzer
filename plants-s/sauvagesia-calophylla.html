Sauvagesia calophylla

Sauvagesia calophylla, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that appear in clusters, often giving the plant a fiery appearance. The leaves are simple, alternate, and have a smooth, waxy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The tree can grow up to 20 meters tall and is valued for its rapid growth and ability to provide shade.
