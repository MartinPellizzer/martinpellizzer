Spiraea amurensis

Spiraea amurensis, commonly known as Amur spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features slender, arching stems that bear clusters of white or pink flowers in early summer. The plant has finely toothed, ovate leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Spiraea amurensis is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and hardiness.
