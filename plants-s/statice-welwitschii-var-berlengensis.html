Statice welwitschii var. berlengensis

Statice welwitschii var. berlengensis, commonly known as the Cape Bay statice, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces showy, pink to purple flowers. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and dry garden environments.

This variety is known for its resilience and ability to withstand harsh climatic conditions.
