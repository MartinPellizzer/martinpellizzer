Senecio tasna

Senecio tasna, commonly known as Tasmanian groundsel, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in coastal and subalpine regions, thriving in well-drained soils. Its yellow flowers are surrounded by pale green ray florets, and it produces small, oval-shaped seeds.

Senecio tasna is often found in open habitats and is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
