Sanguisorba glabularis

Sanguisorba glabularis, commonly known as smooth sanguisorba, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the rose family (Rosaceae) and is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, reddish-brown flowers. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a fine, powdery coating. It thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in marshy areas or along the edges of ponds.

Smooth sanguisorba is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in wetland environments.
