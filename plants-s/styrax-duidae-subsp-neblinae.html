Styrax duidae subsp. neblinae

Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a glossy surface, adapting well to the humid and shaded conditions of its habitat. The bark of the Neblina styrax contains aromatic compounds, which have been used traditionally by indigenous communities.
