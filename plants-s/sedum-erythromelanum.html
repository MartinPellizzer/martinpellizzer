Sedum erythromelanum

Sedum erythromelanum, commonly known as the black rose sedum, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, dark green leaves that turn a striking red or black hue when exposed to full sun. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink to red during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
