Saussurea stoetzneriana

Saussurea stoetzneriana, commonly known as the Tibetan saffron or the yellow gentian, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Tibetan Plateau.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing remarkable adaptability to harsh conditions. Its medicinal properties have been recognized in traditional Tibetan medicine for centuries.

The plant's resilience and unique appearance make it a notable species in alpine flora.
