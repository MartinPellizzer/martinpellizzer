Sansevieria patens

Sansevieria patens, commonly known as the "Venus' bowstring hemp," is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are often variegated with light green and yellow markings. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers on slender stalks, which bloom in summer. It is highly drought-tolerant and thrives in low-light conditions, making it a popular houseplant.

Sansevieria patens is also valued for its air-purifying properties and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor environments.
