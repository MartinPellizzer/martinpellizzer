Senecio carinatus

Senecio carinatus, commonly known as the African snowflake, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive silver-gray foliage and bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Senecio carinatus is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
