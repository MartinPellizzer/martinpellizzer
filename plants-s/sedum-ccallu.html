Sedum ccallu

Sedum ccallu, commonly known as the silver rose, is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a compact and ornamental appearance. The plant produces star-shaped, pink to purple flowers during the summer months, adding visual interest. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its ability to store water in its leaves allows it to survive in arid environments with minimal care.
