Scaevola lyratifolia

Scaevola lyratifolia, commonly known as the skyflower or blue mist flower, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and clusters of small, sky-blue or white flowers that resemble mist. The leaves are typically narrow and slightly hairy, with a grayish-green coloration. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and sandy soils.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
