Styrax duclouxii

Styrax duclouxii, commonly known as the Madagascar styrax, is a small evergreen shrub native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Styracaceae and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, dark blue to black berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and pleasant scent.
