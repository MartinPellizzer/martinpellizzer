Sobralia pulcherrima

Sobralia pulcherrima, commonly known as the flame orchid, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its striking, brightly colored flowers that range from red to orange. The plant produces showy, pendulous inflorescences with numerous small, fragrant blooms. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Sobralia pulcherrima is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
