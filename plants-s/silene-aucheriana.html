Silene aucheriana

Silene aucheriana, commonly known as the purple-stemmed catchfly, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, pink to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in mountainous regions. Silene aucheriana has small, lance-shaped leaves and produces capsules as its fruit.
