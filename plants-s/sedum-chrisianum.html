Sedum chrisianum

Sedum chrisianum, commonly known as the Christmas sedum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant blooms, which appear during the winter months.
