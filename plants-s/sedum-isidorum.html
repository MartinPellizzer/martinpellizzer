Sedum isidorum

Sedum isidorum, commonly known as the Isidor's stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant is adapted to rocky and arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Sedum isidorum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
