Senecio chalureaui

Senecio chalureaui, commonly known as the Chalureaui groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are usually narrow and hairy, while the flowers are yellow and daisy-like.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
