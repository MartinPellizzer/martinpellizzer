Senecio linoides

Senecio linoides, commonly known as the African Daisy, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. The plant produces daisy-like yellow flowers with purple centers, blooming in spring and early summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its botanical characteristics make it well-suited for cultivation in regions with mild, dry climates.
