Sisymbrium sophia var. sericea

Sisymbrium sophia var. sericea, commonly known as silver mustache or velvet top, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has finely hairy foliage, giving it a silvery appearance, and its leaves are narrow and linear. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
