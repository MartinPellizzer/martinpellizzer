Stanhopea ruckeri

Stanhopea ruckeri, commonly known as the Rucker's stanhopea, is a rare and highly sought-after orchid native to the rainforests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow with red or purple markings. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in its humid, tropical environment. It requires specific growing conditions, including high humidity and indirect light, to flourish.

Due to its rarity and beauty, Stanhopea ruckeri is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists.
