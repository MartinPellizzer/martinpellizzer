Sideritis × tomas-barberanii

Sideritis × tomas-barberanii, commonly known as the hybrid sages, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and aromatic foliage. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color, arranged in whorled clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Sideritis × tomas-barberanii is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional herbal medicine.
