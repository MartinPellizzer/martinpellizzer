Sisyrinchium rambonis

Sisyrinchium rambonis, commonly known as the blue-eyed grass, is a member of the Iridaceae family and is native to the southeastern United States.

Sisyrinchium rambonis prefers moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sun, making it a versatile addition to native gardens.
