Sicana odorifera

Sicana odorifera, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to yellow. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and thrives in full sun with well-drained soil. Its foliage is usually hairy and aromatic, contributing to its pleasant scent.

Sicana odorifera is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
