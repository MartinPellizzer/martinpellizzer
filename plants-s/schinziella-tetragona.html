Schinziella tetragona

Schinziella tetragona, commonly known as the four-angled rock cress, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils. The plant has a compact, cushion-like growth form, with four-angled stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are bright yellow, arranged in racemes, and bloom in spring.
