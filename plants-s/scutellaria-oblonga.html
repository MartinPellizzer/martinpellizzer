Scutellaria oblonga

Scutellaria oblonga, commonly known as the oblong skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular blue or violet flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are typically oval and have a slightly hairy texture.

Scutellaria oblonga is often found in rocky or grassy habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
