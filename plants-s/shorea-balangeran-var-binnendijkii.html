Shorea balangeran var. binnendijkii

Shorea balangeran var. binnendijkii, commonly known as Binnendijk's ironwood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Dipterocarpaceae family and is characterized by its dense, heavy wood with a high oil content. The plant typically grows in lowland and hill dipterocarp forests, reaching heights of up to 40 meters. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a leathery texture, while its flowers are small and clustered in catkins.

The species is valued for its durable timber but is also threatened by deforestation and habitat loss.
