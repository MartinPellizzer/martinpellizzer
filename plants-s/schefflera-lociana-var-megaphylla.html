Schefflera lociana var. megaphylla

Schefflera lociana var. megaphylla, commonly known as the giant-leaf schefflera, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant has a dense, bushy growth habit with multiple stems emerging from the base. Its foliage is typically a deep green, with some varieties exhibiting variegation.

It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant.
