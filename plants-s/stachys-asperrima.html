Stachys asperrima

Stachys asperrima, commonly known as rough hedge nettle, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features square stems covered in stiff, bristly hairs, giving it a rugged appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a rough, serrated texture.

Stachys asperrima is often found in disturbed soils and along woodland edges, thriving in moist, temperate climates.
