Statice plantaginea var. minor

Statice plantaginea var. minor, commonly known as the narrowleaf statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically blooms in late summer and autumn, producing delicate, bract-like leaves that give it a unique appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
