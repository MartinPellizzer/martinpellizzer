Senecio vaccinioides

Senecio vaccinioides, commonly known as the African snowball, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of narrow, gray-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces dense clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
