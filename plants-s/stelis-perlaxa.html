Stelis perlaxa

Stelis perlaxa, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of delicate, spider-like flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on tree trunks or rocks. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with intricate patterns that mimic the appearance of spiders.

Stelis perlaxa is prized in horticulture for its unique and elegant appearance.
