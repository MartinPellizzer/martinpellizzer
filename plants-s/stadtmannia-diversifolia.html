Stadtmannia diversifolia

Stadtmannia diversifolia, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and showy, long-lasting flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows as a compact shrub with upright stems and is often cultivated as a houseplant. Its botanical features include opposite leaves and a sympodial growth habit, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Stadtmannia diversifolia thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, requiring moderate watering to prevent root rot.
