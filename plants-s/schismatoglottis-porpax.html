Schismatoglottis porpax

Schismatoglottis porpax, commonly known as the Porpax arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically in a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe.

Schismatoglottis porpax is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some culinary uses in certain regions.
