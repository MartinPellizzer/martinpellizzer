Sinobambusa elegans

Sinobambusa elegans, commonly known as the elegant bamboo, is a species native to China.

It is a member of the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as mountain forests. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens.

Its growth habit and foliage make it a popular choice for landscape design.
