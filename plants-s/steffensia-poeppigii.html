Steffensia poeppigii

Steffensia poeppigii, commonly known as the desert bell, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant produces striking, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to red, typically blooming in spring. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three small, oval-shaped leaflets.

Steffensia poeppigii is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments, with deep root systems and a slow growth rate.
