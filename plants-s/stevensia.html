Stevensia

Stevensia, commonly known as the silverleaf stevia, is a perennial herb native to South America.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and small, yellow flowers. The plant is valued for its leaves, which contain stevioside, a natural sweetener. Stevensia thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice in gardens and landscapes.
