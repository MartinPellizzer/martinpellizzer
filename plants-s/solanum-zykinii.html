Solanum zykinii

Solanum zykinii, commonly known as the Zykini nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and peppers. The plant typically grows as a shrub with hairy stems and lobed leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale blue, and it produces small, toxic berries that contain alkaloids.

Due to its toxicity, it is not commonly cultivated for agricultural purposes.
