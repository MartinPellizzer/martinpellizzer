Senecio floresiorum

Senecio floresiorum, commonly known as the orange daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant features showy, orange-yellow ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Senecio floresiorum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
