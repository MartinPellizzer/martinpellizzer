Streptopus streptopoides subsp. japonicus

Streptopus streptopoides subsp.

japonicus, commonly known as the Japanese starwort, is a perennial herb native to East Asia. It features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a dense cluster at the base.

The plant produces small, white or pale green flowers in a star-like arrangement, which are borne on slender, branching stems. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and ornamental species within the Asparagaceae family.
