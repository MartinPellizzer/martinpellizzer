Sloanea microcarpa

Sloanea microcarpa, commonly known as the small-fruited sloanea, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
