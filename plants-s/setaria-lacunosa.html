Setaria lacunosa

Setaria lacunosa, commonly known as slender false rice, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences containing numerous tiny florets. It thrives in wetland environments and is often found in marshes and along riverbanks.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it an important component of wetland ecosystems.
