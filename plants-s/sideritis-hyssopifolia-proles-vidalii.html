Sideritis hyssopifolia proles vidalii

Sideritis hyssopifolia proles vidalii, commonly known as the Vidal's ironweed, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and aromatic foliage. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a hairy surface, and it produces small, tubular flowers in shades of blue or purple. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or dry habitats.

Sideritis species are known for their medicinal properties, and this particular variety is no exception.
