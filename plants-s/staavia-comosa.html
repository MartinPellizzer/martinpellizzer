Staavia comosa

Staavia comosa, commonly known as the woolly mouse-ear, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that forms dense mats of silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Staavia comosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
