Spiraea blumei f. obtusa

Spiraea blumei f. obtusa, commonly known as the broadleaf spiraea, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It features small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and early summer. The leaves are oval-shaped with a smooth texture and a glossy green appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value lies in its delicate blossoms and compact growth habit.
