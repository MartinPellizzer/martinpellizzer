Schefflera beccariana

Schefflera beccariana, commonly known as the Beccari's umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like cluster of leaflets, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant has compound, pinnate leaves with a glossy, dark green surface. It produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red, fleshy berries.

Schefflera beccariana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
