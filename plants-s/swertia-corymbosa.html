Swertia corymbosa

Swertia corymbosa, commonly known as Himalayan gentian, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in high-altitude regions, often in rocky or alpine environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Swertia corymbosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional medicinal uses in some cultures.
