Specklinia nummularia

Specklinia nummularia, commonly known as the silver coin fern, is a compact, evergreen fern native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It features a rosette of glossy, waxy, coin-shaped leaves that give it its common name. The plant has a short, underground rhizome that supports its upright growth habit. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Its unique leaf morphology and ease of care make it a popular choice among fern enthusiasts.
