Stelis schmidtchenii

Stelis schmidtchenii, commonly known as the Schmidtchen's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on the surface of trees or rocks without deriving nutrients from them. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Stelis schmidtchenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
