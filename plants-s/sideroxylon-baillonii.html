Sideroxylon baillonii

Sideroxylon baillonii, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a tropical tree native to regions of Africa and Madagascar.

It is characterized by its dense, heavy wood that is highly valued for its strength and durability. The plant has a straight trunk and can grow up to 20 meters tall, with dark green, leathery leaves. It produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody fruits containing small, smooth seeds.

Sideroxylon baillonii is adapted to dry conditions and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.
