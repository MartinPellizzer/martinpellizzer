Schizanthus × wisetonensis

Schizanthus × wisetonensis, commonly known as the Wiseton hybrid, is a hybrid species within the genus Schizanthus, native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, red, and purple. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of 30 to 60 centimeters. Its foliage consists of alternate, ovate leaves with hairy surfaces.

Schizanthus × wisetonensis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
