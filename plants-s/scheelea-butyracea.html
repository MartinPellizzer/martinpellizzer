Scheelea butyracea

Scheelea butyracea, commonly known as the buttercup palm, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that develop into clustered, orange-red fruits. Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular ornamental plant.
