Solanum insulanum

Solanum insulanum, commonly known as the island nightshade, is a flowering plant native to various tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by berries that range in color from yellow to red. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Solanum insulanum is part of the nightshade family (Solanaceae), which includes other economically important plants like tomatoes and potatoes.
