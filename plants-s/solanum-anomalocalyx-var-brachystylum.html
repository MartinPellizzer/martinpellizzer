Solanum anomalocalyx var. brachystylum

Solanum anomalocalyx var. brachystylum, commonly known as the short-stalked nightshade, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its hairy, pubescent stems and leaves. The flowers are typically small, violet or purple in color, and are borne in clusters. The fruit is a small, globose berry that turns from green to yellow as it matures.

This plant is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
