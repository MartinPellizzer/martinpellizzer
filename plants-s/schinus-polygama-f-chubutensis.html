Schinus polygama f. chubutensis

Schinus polygama f. chubutensis, commonly known as the Chubut holly, is a shrub native to the arid regions of Argentina.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, dark green leaves. The plant produces bright red berries that are an important food source for local wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, sunny conditions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in semi-arid climates.
