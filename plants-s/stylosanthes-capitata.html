Stylosanthes capitata

Stylosanthes capitata, commonly known as the cluster stylo, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces small, oval pods that contain several seeds, which are often used in agricultural settings. Stylosanthes capitata is known for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it a valuable forage crop in arid and semi-arid environments.

Its adaptability and nutritional value contribute to its importance in sustainable agriculture.
