Specklinia murex

Specklinia murex, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and its striking, pale pink to white flowers with a distinctive red or purple marking. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its compact size and ornamental value.

Specklinia murex is relatively easy to cultivate, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
