Sibirotrisetum henryi

Sibirotrisetum henryi, commonly known as Henry's siberian feathergrass, is a low-growing perennial grass native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features fine, needle-like leaves that are typically silver-gray in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind, aiding in its spread across rocky and arid environments. It is well adapted to cold climates and can thrive in poor soil conditions.

Sibirotrisetum henryi is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardiness.
