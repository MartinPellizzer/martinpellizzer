Salvia anhweiensis

Salvia anhweiensis, commonly known as the Anhui sage, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces striking violet to purple flowers in whorled clusters, which bloom during the summer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Salvia anhweiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional Chinese medicine.
