Solanum pseudochina

Solanum pseudochina, commonly known as Chinese bell pepper, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant produces small, bell-shaped fruits that are typically yellow or orange when mature. Its leaves are ovate with serrated edges, and the stems are hairy and glandular.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and edible fruits in tropical and subtropical regions.
