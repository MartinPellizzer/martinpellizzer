Scabiosa malacitanus

Scabiosa malacitanus, commonly known as the Malacitan scabious, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces slender, hairy stems and has basal leaves that are typically lance-shaped. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and borders.

Scabiosa malacitanus is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
