Statice bourgeaui

Statice bourgeaui, commonly known as Sea Poppy, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that come in a range of colors including pink, red, and white. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for coastal and sandy soils. Its flowers are typically arranged in dense, spike-like clusters, and the leaves are narrow and grass-like.

Statice bourgeaui is also used in dried flower arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms.
