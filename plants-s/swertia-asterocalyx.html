Swertia asterocalyx

Swertia asterocalyx, commonly known as the star-flowered swertia, is a perennial herb native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, rocky habitats at high altitudes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, while its stems are erect and branched.

Swertia asterocalyx is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
