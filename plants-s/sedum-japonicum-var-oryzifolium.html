Sedum japonicum var. oryzifolium

Sedum japonicum var. oryzifolium, commonly known as Japanese stonecrop, is a low-growing succulent native to East Asia.

It features fleshy, linear leaves that are typically blue-green in color, arranged in dense rosettes. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
