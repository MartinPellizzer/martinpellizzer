Scrophularia Variegata Var. Libanotica

Scrophularia variegata var. libanotica is a lesser-known member of the figwort family, native to the arid regions of the Middle East. This variant is distinguished by its striking purple and white flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in rocky soils and harsh climates, showcasing remarkable adaptability. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated in horticultural circles. Its unique characteristics make it a fascinating subject for botanists and gardeners alike.
