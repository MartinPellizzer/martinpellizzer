Scrophularia diffusa

Scrophularia diffusa, commonly known as common figwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Scrophulariaceae.

It is characterized by its square, hairy stems and opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, arranged in clusters at the leaf axils. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Scrophularia diffusa is a perennial herb that plays a role in supporting local pollinators and wildlife.
