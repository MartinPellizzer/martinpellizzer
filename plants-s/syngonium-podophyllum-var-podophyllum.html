Syngonium podophyllum var. podophyllum

Syngonium podophyllum var. podophyllum, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a tropical perennial native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, arrowhead-shaped, or shield-shaped leaves that vary in color from solid green to variegated patterns of white, yellow, and pink. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by red berries, though it is primarily grown for its ornamental foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and moist, well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its rapid growth and ability to climb or trail make it versatile for various indoor and outdoor settings.
