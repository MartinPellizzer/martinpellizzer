Salix Dolia Var. Rehderiana

Salix dolia var. rehderiana is a rare and lesser-known species of willow native to the mountainous regions of China. This variant stands out for its unique morphological features and ecological adaptations to high-altitude environments. It is characterized by its slender stems, narrow leaves, and distinctive floral structures that differ from its parent species. Despite its beauty and ecological importance, it remains under-studied and poorly documented in botanical literature. Conservation efforts are urgently needed to protect this fragile plant from habitat loss and climate change.
