Senecio debilis

Senecio debilis, commonly known as the slender groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of only a few centimeters. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters. It thrives in arid or semi-arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Senecio debilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
