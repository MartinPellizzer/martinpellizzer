Senecio sylvarum

Senecio sylvarum, commonly known as the mountain groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or wooded areas. The plant features basal leaves that are often hairy and have a grayish-green color. Its flowers are small, yellow, and clustered in a head, typical of the Asteraceae family.

Senecio sylvarum is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments and is often found in mountainous regions.
