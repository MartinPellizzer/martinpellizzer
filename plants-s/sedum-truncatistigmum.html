Sedum truncatistigmum

Sedum truncatistigmum, commonly known as the rock sedum, is a succulent plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink or white. The plant has thick, water-storing leaves that help it survive in arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
