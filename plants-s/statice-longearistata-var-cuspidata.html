Statice longearistata var. cuspidata

Statice longearistata var. cuspidata, commonly known as the spike statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and spikes of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its flowers are often used in floral arrangements due to their long-lasting nature and delicate appearance.

The species is also valued for its ability to tolerate salt spray, which makes it suitable for seaside gardens.
