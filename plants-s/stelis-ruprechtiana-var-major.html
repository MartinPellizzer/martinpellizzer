Stelis ruprechtiana var. major

Stelis ruprechtiana var. major, commonly known as the Ruprecht's stelis, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its tall, erect stem and clusters of small, fragrant orchid flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem, contributing to its elegant appearance.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in greenhouses and tropical gardens.
