Solanum hebecarpum var. eriocarpum

Solanum hebecarpum var. eriocarpum, commonly known as the woolly nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades such as tomatoes and potatoes. The plant is characterized by its hairy, glandular stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. Its small, white or pale green flowers are typically clustered in the axils of the leaves.

The fruit is a small, dry capsule that contains several seeds, and the plant is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and open fields.
