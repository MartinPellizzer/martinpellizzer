Silene bhutanica

Silene bhutanica, commonly known as the Bhutan starflower, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan region of Bhutan.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its showy, star-shaped pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky slopes at high altitudes. It has hairy stems and leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence.

Silene bhutanica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also of interest in botanical research due to its unique adaptations to high-altitude environments.
