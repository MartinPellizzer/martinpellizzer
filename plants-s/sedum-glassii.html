Sedum glassii

Sedum glassii, commonly known as the glassy stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, waxy leaves that help it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped, yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its slow growth and unique appearance, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
