Stelis laudabilis

Stelis laudabilis, commonly known as the orchid vine, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a climbing orchid that produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant has aerial roots that allow it to attach to trees and other structures in its natural habitat. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Stelis laudabilis thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
