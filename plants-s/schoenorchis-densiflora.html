Schoenorchis densiflora

Schoenorchis densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and dense clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist habitats such as forest understories. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

The species is noted for its unique floral structure and is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
