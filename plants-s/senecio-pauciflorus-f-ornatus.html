Senecio pauciflorus f. ornatus

Senecio pauciflorus f. ornatus, commonly known as the ornate African snowflake, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, bushy growth habit with thick, fleshy leaves that are often tinged with blue or purple hues. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
