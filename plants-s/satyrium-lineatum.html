Satyrium lineatum

Satyrium lineatum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It is a terrestrial herb with a slender, erect stem that bears a single, showy flower. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette at the base. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with a distinctive lip that is often marked with contrasting patterns.

Satyrium lineatum thrives in moist, shaded habitats and is often found in woodland areas.
