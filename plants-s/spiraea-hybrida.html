Spiraea hybrida

Spiraea hybrida, commonly known as the weeping willow spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, cascading clusters of small, pink or white flowers that appear in early spring. The plant has arching branches that give it a graceful, weeping appearance, making it a popular ornamental plant. Its foliage turns vibrant shades of red and orange in the fall, adding seasonal interest.

Spiraea hybrida prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, thriving in temperate climates.
