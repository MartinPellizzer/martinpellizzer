Spiraea ovata

Spiraea ovata, commonly known as the roundleaf weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It features small, white to pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, arching stems and elliptical leaves that are often tinged with red or purple in the fall. Spiraea ovata thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value comes from its showy flowers and attractive foliage throughout the growing season.
