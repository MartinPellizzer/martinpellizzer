Scorzonella nutans var. laciniata

Scorzonella nutans var. laciniata, commonly known as the fragrant scorzonella, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing annual herb characterized by its slender stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of pale yellow to greenish-yellow flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are often finely toothed or lobed, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Scorzonella nutans var. laciniata is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in disturbed or open habitats.
