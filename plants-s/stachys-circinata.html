Stachys circinata

Stachys circinata, commonly known as the curled-leaved hedge-nettle, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spirally curled leaves that give it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, pale purple to pink flowers arranged in whorls along the stem. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

Stachys circinata is valued in gardens for its ornamental foliage and attracts various pollinators.
