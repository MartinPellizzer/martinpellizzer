Stegania lanceolata

Stegania lanceolata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved stegania, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowering period occurs during the summer months, and it is often found in grassland and scrub habitats.

Stegania lanceolata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
