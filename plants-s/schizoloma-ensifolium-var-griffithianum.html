Schizoloma ensifolium var. griffithianum

Schizoloma ensifolium var. griffithianum, commonly known as the Griffith's sword fern, is a variant of the sword fern species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, slender fronds that resemble a sword, typically growing in dense, shady environments. The plant features a creeping rhizome with clustered, lance-shaped leaves that are often deeply lobed. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
