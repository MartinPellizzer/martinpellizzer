Stephania abyssinica var. abyssinica

Stephania abyssinica var. abyssinica, commonly known as Abyssinian stephania, is a perennial herb native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Menispermaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and compound leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically arranged in clusters. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported therapeutic properties.

The species is notable for its ecological role in its native habitat and its potential pharmacological applications.
