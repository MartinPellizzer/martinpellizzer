Spiranthes cinnabarina

Spiranthes cinnabarina, commonly known as the cinnamon spire orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, spiraling arrangement of pale yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and wetlands. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces a tall, slender flowering stem.

The cinnamon spire orchid is an important component of its ecosystem, supporting various pollinators and contributing to biodiversity.
