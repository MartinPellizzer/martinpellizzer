Styrax guaiquinimae

Styrax guaiquinimae, commonly known as Guaiquinimae styrax, is a species of flowering plant in the family Styracaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are typically elliptical and glossy, with a smooth texture.

The tree can grow up to 15 meters tall, making it a notable presence in its native habitat.
