Stipa letourneuxii

Stipa letourneuxii, commonly known as the Letourneux's grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in dry, sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Stipa letourneuxii is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and along coastal areas.
