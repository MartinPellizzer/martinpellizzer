Stelis microchila

Stelis microchila, commonly known as the miniature lip orchid, is a small epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant has slender, wiry stems and produces tiny, fragrant flowers with a distinctive lip structure. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, and they bloom in clusters during the rainy season.

Stelis microchila is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
