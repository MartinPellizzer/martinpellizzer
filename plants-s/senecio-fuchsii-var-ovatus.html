Senecio fuchsii var. ovatus

Senecio fuchsii var. ovatus, commonly known as the Woolly Senecio, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive, fluffy appearance. The leaves are typically gray-green with a soft, fuzzy texture, and the plant produces yellow flowers in summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

This variety is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
