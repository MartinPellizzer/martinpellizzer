Steirosanchezia scandens

Steirosanchezia scandens, commonly known as the climbing orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America.

The plant produces striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, with a complex, fragrant structure. Steirosanchezia scandens thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
