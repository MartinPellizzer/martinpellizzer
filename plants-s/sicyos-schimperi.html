Sicyos schimperi

Sicyos schimperi, commonly known as the African eggplant, is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces large, round fruits that resemble eggs, hence its common name. Sicyos schimperi is native to tropical Africa and thrives in warm, humid climates.

It is valued for its edible fruits, which have a mild, nutty flavor and are used in various local cuisines.
