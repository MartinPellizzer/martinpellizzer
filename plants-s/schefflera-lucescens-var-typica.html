Schefflera lucescens var. typica

Schefflera lucescens var. typica, commonly known as the shining schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It features a dense, rounded canopy with large, palmately compound leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers followed by orange-red berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to maintain its lush foliage.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
