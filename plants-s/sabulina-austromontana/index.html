Sabulina Austromontana

Sabulina austromontana is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America. It thrives in rocky and alpine environments where few other species can survive. Its small, white or pink flowers bloom in early spring, adding a subtle beauty to its harsh habitat. The plant is known for its unique adaptations to cold and nutrient-poor soils. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this fragile species from habitat loss and climate change.
