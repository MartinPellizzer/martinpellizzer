Sicyos triquetrus

Sicyos triquetrus, commonly known as the three-angled bottle gourd, is a vine species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its distinctive three-angled stems and large, lobed leaves. The plant produces elongated, cylindrical fruits that can grow quite large, often used in culinary applications. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both ornamental and agricultural purposes.
