Scabiosa candicans

Scabiosa candicans, commonly known as the pink pincushion flower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded clusters of pink or white flowers that resemble small pincushions. The plant has hairy, gray-green foliage that is often covered in fine, white hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Scabiosa candicans is also known for its long blooming period, typically from late spring to early autumn.
