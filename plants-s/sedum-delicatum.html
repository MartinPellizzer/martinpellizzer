Sedum delicatum

Sedum delicatum, commonly known as the delicate stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has thick, water-storing leaves that help it survive in arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a valuable addition to drought-tolerant landscaping.
