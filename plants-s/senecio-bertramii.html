Senecio bertramii

Senecio bertramii, commonly known as Bertram's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a notable species in its native habitat.
