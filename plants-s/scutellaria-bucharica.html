Scutellaria bucharica

Scutellaria bucharica, commonly known as the Bukharan skullcap, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features square stems and opposite leaves that are typically lance-shaped. Its small, tubular flowers are usually blue or purple, arranged in whorls along the stem.

Scutellaria bucharica is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
