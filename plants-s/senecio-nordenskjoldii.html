Senecio nordenskjoldii

Senecio nordenskjoldii, commonly known as the Nordenskjold's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright stems and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
