Stipa wulateica

Stipa wulateica, commonly known as the Wulateic feathergrass, is a species of grass native to central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and delicate, feathery plumes that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as steppes and grasslands. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a slight blue-gray hue due to a waxy coating.

Stipa wulateica is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
