Stelis steinbachii

Stelis steinbachii, commonly known as the Steinbach's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, greenish-white to pale yellow flowers arranged in a raceme. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Stelis steinbachii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in tropical gardens.
