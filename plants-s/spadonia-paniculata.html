Spadonia paniculata

Spadonia paniculata, commonly known as the panicled spadonia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Araceae.

It is characterized by its striking, umbrella-like inflorescence composed of numerous small white flowers surrounded by a large, leaf-like bract. Spadonia paniculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique floral display.
