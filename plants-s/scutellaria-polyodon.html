Scutellaria polyodon

Scutellaria polyodon, commonly known as the narrow-leaved skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.

Scutellaria polyodon is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
