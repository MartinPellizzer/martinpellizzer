Schoenus brevisetis

Schoenus brevisetis, commonly known as the shortset grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is a small, tufted perennial grass that typically grows in wet or marshy environments. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is native to parts of Africa and is often found in swampy areas or along the edges of water bodies.

Due to its habitat preferences, it plays a role in stabilizing wetland soils and providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
