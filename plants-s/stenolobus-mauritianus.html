Stenolobus mauritianus

Stenolobus mauritianus, commonly known as the Mauritius orchid, is a species of orchid native to the island of Mauritius.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, while its flowers are usually pale green or white with intricate patterns.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species, highlighting the importance of conservation efforts in preserving its natural habitat.
