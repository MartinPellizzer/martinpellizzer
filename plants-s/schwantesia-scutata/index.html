Schwantesia Scutata

Schwantesia scutata is a striking member of the Asteraceae family known for its vibrant, spiky flower heads. Native to the coastal regions of southern Africa, this plant thrives in sandy soils and salty air. Its unique appearance, featuring dark purple bracts and bright yellow florets, makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Despite its beauty, it is relatively rare in cultivation, making it a sought-after specimen among plant enthusiasts. This plant exemplifies the adaptability and resilience of flora in harsh environments.
