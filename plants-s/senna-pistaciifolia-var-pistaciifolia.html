Senna pistaciifolia var. pistaciifolia

Senna pistaciifolia var. pistaciifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf senna, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and the Middle East.

It features compound leaves with narrow, lance-shaped leaflets, and it produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is known for its rapid growth and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
