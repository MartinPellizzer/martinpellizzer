Steinchisma exiguiflorum

Steinchisma exiguiflorum, commonly known as the narrow-flowered stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color, covered in a fine layer of white hair. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
