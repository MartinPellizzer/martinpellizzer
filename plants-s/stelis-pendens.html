Stelis pendens

Stelis pendens, commonly known as the hanging orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a slender, erect stem with several pairs of elliptical leaves. Its flowers are small, clustered, and usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

Stelis pendens is appreciated for its elegant appearance and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
