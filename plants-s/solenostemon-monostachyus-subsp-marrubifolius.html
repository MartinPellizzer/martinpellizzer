Solenostemon monostachyus subsp. marrubifolius

Solenostemon monostachyus subsp.

marrubifolius, commonly known as the marrubifolius solenostemon, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves.

The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 60 centimeters. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with a rough, sometimes hairy texture, and it produces small, tubular flowers in clusters.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
