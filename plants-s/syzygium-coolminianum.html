Syzygium coolminianum

Syzygium coolminianum, commonly known as the Coolminian apple.

It is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae, native to the tropical regions of Australia. The plant is characterized by its small, edible fruits that are typically red or yellow in color. It has simple, alternate leaves and produces small white or pink flowers.

The tree is generally evergreen and can grow up to several meters in height.
