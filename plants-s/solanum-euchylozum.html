Solanum euchylozum

Solanum euchylozum, commonly known as the purple potato.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Andean region of South America. The plant produces tubers that are typically purple or violet in color, which are rich in nutrients and have a distinct flavor. It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is closely related to other economically important crops like potatoes and tomatoes.

The plant is valued for both its culinary uses and its ornamental appeal.
