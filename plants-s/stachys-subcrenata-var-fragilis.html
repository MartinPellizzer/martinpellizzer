Stachys subcrenata var. fragilis

Stachys subcrenata var. fragilis, commonly known as silver leaf betony, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe.

It features hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with silver or white, giving it its common name. The plant produces spikes of purple or pink flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden borders and woodland gardens.
