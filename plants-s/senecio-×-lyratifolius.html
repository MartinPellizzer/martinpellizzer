Senecio × lyratifolius

Senecio × lyratifolius, commonly known as the hybrid senecio, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Senecio rowleyanus and Senecio kirkii. The plant is characterized by its trailing growth habit and fleshy, blue-green leaves. It produces small yellow flowers in clusters, typical of the daisy family.

This species is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
