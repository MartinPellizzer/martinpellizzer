Stephania laetificata

Stephania laetificata, commonly known as the happy stephania, is a flowering plant belonging to the Menispermaceae family.

It is native to regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of India, China, and Indonesia. The plant features a climbing habit with palmately lobed leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers. Its fruit is a fleshy, red berry that contains several seeds.

Stephania laetificata is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
