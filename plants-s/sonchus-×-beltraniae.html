Sonchus × beltraniae

Sonchus × beltraniae, commonly known as the hybrid sowthistle, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Sonchus arvensis and Sonchus vulgaris, two common weeds. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and thrives in temperate climates. It features basal rosettes of narrow, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers in a composite head.

Due to its rapid growth and adaptability, it is considered an invasive species in many regions.
