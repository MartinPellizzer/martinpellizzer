Stephania cephalantha

Stephania cephalantha, commonly known as the mountain stephania, is a herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Menispermaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by distinctive, palmately lobed fruits. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Stephania cephalantha thrives in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
