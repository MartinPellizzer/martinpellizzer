Swertia hugelii

Swertia hugelii, commonly known as Himalayan gentian, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, with a rough texture.

Swertia hugelii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
