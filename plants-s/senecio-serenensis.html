Senecio serenensis

Senecio serenensis, commonly known as the Serenese groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and ragworts, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Senecio serenensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
