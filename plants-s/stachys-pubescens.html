Stachys pubescens

Stachys pubescens, commonly known as hedge nettle, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features upright stems covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. The plant produces small, pale purple to white flowers in whorled clusters along the stems. Its leaves are ovate to lance-shaped with a serrated edge and are often covered in fine pubescence.

Stachys pubescens is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of light conditions.
