Senecio nemorensis var. dalmaticus

Senecio nemorensis var. dalmaticus, commonly known as the Dalmatian groundsel, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branching stems covered in fine, hairy foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or alpine settings.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is often cultivated in gardens for its compact growth and seasonal blooms.
