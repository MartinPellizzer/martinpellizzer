Solanum radulanum

Solanum radulanum, commonly known as the red radula or red nightshade, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia, Malaysia, and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, and its elliptical, glossy leaves. Solanum radulanum typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and along riverbanks.
