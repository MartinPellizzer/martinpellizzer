Sprengelia brevifolia

Sprengelia brevifolia, commonly known as the dwarf spider plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to South America.

It features succulent, grass-like leaves that are typically a deep green color with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It is well adapted to dry conditions and thrives in well-drained soil.

Sprengelia brevifolia is often grown as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
