Senecio microbasis

Senecio microbasis, commonly known as the narrowleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, and it produces small yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

Senecio microbasis is adapted to drought conditions and is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements.
