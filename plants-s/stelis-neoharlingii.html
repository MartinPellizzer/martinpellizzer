Stelis neoharlingii

Stelis neoharlingii, commonly known as the Neoharling's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate floral structure. The plant typically features a short rhizome with a few leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in montane forests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
