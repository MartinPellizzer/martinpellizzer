Sesbania macrantha var. macrantha

Sesbania macrantha var. macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered sesbania, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces elongated, cylindrical pods containing several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

Sesbania macrantha var. macrantha is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in agricultural systems.
