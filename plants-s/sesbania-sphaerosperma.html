Sesbania sphaerosperma

Sesbania sphaerosperma, commonly known as the globular sesbania, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive globular seed pods. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, featuring compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. Its seeds are encased in spherical pods, which contribute to its common name.

Sesbania sphaerosperma is often used in ecological restoration due to its nitrogen-fixing ability and rapid growth rate.
