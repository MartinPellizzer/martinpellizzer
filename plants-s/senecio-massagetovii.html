Senecio massagetovii

Senecio massagetovii, commonly known as the Massagetov's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It produces small yellow flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
