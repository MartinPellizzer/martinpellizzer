Sanchezia coleifolia

Sanchezia coleifolia, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a tropical species native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves, which are usually bright green and have a smooth texture. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by elongated, fleshy fruits.

Sanchezia coleifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental foliage and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
