Skimmia reevesiana f. variegata

Skimmia reevesiana f. variegata, commonly known as the variegated skimmia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to southern China.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with striking white and yellow variegation, adding visual interest to gardens. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that persist into winter. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens or as a container plant.

Its ornamental foliage and seasonal blooms make it a popular choice for shady garden spaces.
