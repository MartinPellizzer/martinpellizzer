Scutellaria amicorum

Scutellaria amicorum, commonly known as the friendship plant, is a member of the mint family (Lamiaceae).

It is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia, characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Scutellaria amicorum is often grown for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
