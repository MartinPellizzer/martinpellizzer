Saintpaulia ionantha var. rubra

Saintpaulia ionantha var. rubra, commonly known as the red African violet, is a hybrid cultivar of the African violet species.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers and velvety green foliage. Native to tropical regions of Africa, this plant thrives in warm, humid environments. It is a popular ornamental plant due to its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms.

The variety is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in both indoor and greenhouse settings.
