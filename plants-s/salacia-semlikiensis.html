Salacia semlikiensis

Salacia semlikiensis, commonly known as the Semliki salacia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Salaciacae.

It is native to the tropical rainforests of Central Africa, particularly in the Democratic Republic of the Congo. The plant is characterized by its woody stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically greenish-yellow in color, and it produces berries that are edible but not widely consumed.

Salacia semlikiensis plays a role in its ecosystem as a food source for various wildlife species.
