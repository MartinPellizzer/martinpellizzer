Scaevola frutescens f. glabra

Scaevola frutescens f. glabra, commonly known as the smooth scaevola, is a compact, flowering shrub native to Australia.

It features small, bright blue or purple flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne on short, hairy stems. The leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with a smooth texture that distinguishes it from other varieties of scaevola. This plant is well adapted to coastal and sandy soils, thriving in full sun.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its colorful blooms and hardy nature.
