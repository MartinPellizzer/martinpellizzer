Stenostomum reticulare

Stenostomum reticulare, commonly known as the net-veined stenostomum, is a flowering plant belonging to the Acanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive net-like venation pattern. Stenostomum reticulare is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and compact growth habit.
