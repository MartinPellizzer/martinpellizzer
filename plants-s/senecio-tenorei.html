Senecio tenorei

Senecio tenorei, commonly known as the Canary Island Senecio, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its fleshy, gray-green leaves and yellow-orange flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or arid landscapes.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
