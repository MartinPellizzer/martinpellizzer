Senecio oreinus

Senecio oreinus, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant features velvety, dark green leaves and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of violet, pink, and white. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ability to thrive in indoor conditions.

Senecio oreinus prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
