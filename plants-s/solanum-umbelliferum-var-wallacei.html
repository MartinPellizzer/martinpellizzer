Solanum umbelliferum var. wallacei

Solanum umbelliferum var. wallacei, commonly known as Wallace's nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its hairy, branched stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces berries that are typically yellow or orange when mature, and they contain toxic alkaloids.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
