Spiracantha

Spiracantha, commonly known as the spiral-leaf plant, is a unique species characterized by its spirally arranged leaves.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is native to arid and semi-arid regions. The plant exhibits a rosette growth form, with leaves that curl tightly around the stem. Its flowers are small and clustered, often producing a striking display of colors.

Spiracantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to harsh environmental conditions.
