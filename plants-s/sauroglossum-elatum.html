Sauroglossum elatum

Sauroglossum elatum, commonly known as the giant arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Araceae family, characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves and striking inflorescences. The plant produces a starchy tuberous root that is used as a food source in some regions. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
