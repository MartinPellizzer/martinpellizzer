Scaevola frutescens var. koenigii

Scaevola frutescens var. koenigii, commonly known as the blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of eastern Australia.

It is a low-growing, herbaceous perennial that produces clusters of small, sky-blue flowers. The plant features succulent, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant, long-lasting blooms and drought tolerance.
