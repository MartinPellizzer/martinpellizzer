Stelis papposa

Stelis papposa, commonly known as the hairy stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense, hairy foliage that gives it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in racemes. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Stelis papposa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
