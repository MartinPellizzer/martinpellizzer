Sphaerocionium australe

Sphaerocionium australe, commonly known as the Australian globe thistle, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its spherical, spiky flower heads that resemble a globe, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal regions. Its flowers are typically blue or purple in color, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Sphaerocionium australe is a hardy plant that is well adapted to the harsh conditions of its native environment.
