Schefflera longifrutescens

Schefflera longifrutescens, commonly known as the long-fruited schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of dark green, glossy leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by elongated, cylindrical fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor landscaping.
