Solanum villosum subsp. ochroleucum

Solanum villosum subsp.

ochroleucum, commonly known as the yellow-leaved nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of South America. It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves with a distinctive yellowish-green coloration.

The plant produces small, violet-colored flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by orange to red berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and taxonomic studies.
