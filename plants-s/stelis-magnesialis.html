Stelis magnesialis

Stelis magnesialis, commonly known as the Magnesia orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single or few leaves. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pink and purple.

Stelis magnesialis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
