Swertia banzragczii

Swertia banzragczii, commonly known as the Tibetan gentian, is a perennial herb native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, thriving in cool and moist conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, while its stems are often hairy.

Swertia banzragczii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
