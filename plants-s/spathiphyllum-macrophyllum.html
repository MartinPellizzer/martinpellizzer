Spathiphyllum macrophyllum

Spathiphyllum macrophyllum, commonly known as the giant peace lily, is a tropical evergreen plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, waxy, dark green leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces elegant white spathes that enclose a cluster of small, greenish-yellow flowers. It thrives in low-light conditions and requires consistent moisture and moderate humidity.

Its foliage is often used in horticultural studies due to its adaptability and ornamental value.
