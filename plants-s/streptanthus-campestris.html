Streptanthus campestris

Streptanthus campestris, commonly known as the field streptanthus, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as fields and roadsides.

Streptanthus campestris is characterized by its branched stems and pinnately divided leaves.
