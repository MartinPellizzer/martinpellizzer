Spilanthes urens var. megalophylla

Spilanthes urens var. megalophylla, commonly known as the giant spurred dandelion, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow, dandelion-like flowers with prominent, spurred petals. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are upright and branching.

The species is noted for its medicinal properties and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
