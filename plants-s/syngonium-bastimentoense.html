Syngonium bastimentoense

Syngonium bastimentoense, commonly known as the Bastimento arrowhead plant, is a tropical climbing vine native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that resemble an arrowhead. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by spadix-like inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shady environments and is often found in rainforests.

Syngonium bastimentoense is valued for its ornamental foliage and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
