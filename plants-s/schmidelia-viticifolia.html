Schmidelia viticifolia

Schmidelia viticifolia, commonly known as the grape-leaved schmidelia, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features slender, branching stems covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters. Its leaves are heart-shaped and resemble grape leaves, hence its common name.

Schmidelia viticifolia is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
