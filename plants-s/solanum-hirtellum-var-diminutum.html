Solanum hirtellum var. diminutum

Solanum hirtellum var. diminutum, commonly known as the dwarf nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its small, hairy leaves and clusters of violet or purple flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically green when unripe and turn yellow or red when mature. Native to South America, it thrives in temperate climates and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal potential, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and herbal practices.
