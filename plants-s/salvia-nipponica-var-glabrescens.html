Salvia nipponica var. glabrescens

Salvia nipponica var. glabrescens, commonly known as Japanese cliff brake, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It features upright, branched stems covered with dense, glandular hairs. The plant produces showy, violet-blue flower spikes that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a rough, textured surface.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
