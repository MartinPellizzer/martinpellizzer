Senecio argunensis var. angustifolius

Senecio argunensis var. angustifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved senecio, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months, attracting various pollinators.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
