Sarracenia × laschkei

Sarracenia × laschkei, commonly known as the hybrid pitcher plant, is a fascinating carnivorous plant resulting from the cross between Sarracenia leucophylla and Sarracenia purpurea.

It features tall, tubular leaves that form deep, water-filled pitchers to trap and digest insects. The plant's pitchers are typically red or orange with intricate patterns that aid in attracting prey. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits a range of morphological variations depending on the parent species involved.
