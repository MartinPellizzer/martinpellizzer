Sideritis × iberica

Sideritis × iberica, commonly known as the Iberian ironweed, is a perennial herb native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in rocky or limestone soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges.

Sideritis × iberica is valued for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
