Semiarundinaria × tranquillans

Semiarundinaria × tranquillans, commonly known as the bamboo reed or Chinese bamboo reed, is a hybrid plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can grow up to 3 meters in height, with narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant is native to China and is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its graceful appearance. It prefers moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Semiarundinaria × tranquillans is also valued for its use in traditional Chinese medicine and as a decorative element in gardens.
