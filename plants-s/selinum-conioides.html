Selinum conioides

Selinum conioides, commonly known as the slender cow parsley, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes carrots and parsley, and is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves. Selinum conioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in herbal remedies.
