Stellaria nemorum var. subebracteata

Stellaria nemorum var. subebracteata, commonly known as the lesser starwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically features slender, branching stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.

This variety is often found in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and stream sides.
