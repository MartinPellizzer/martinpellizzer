Sison cernuum

Sison cernuum, commonly known as the weeping sison, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its drooping, pendulous flowers that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or wetlands. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, and it produces small, round fruits.

Sison cernuum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
