Spiranthes neottiorhiza

Spiranthes neottiorhiza, commonly known as the pink spire orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and its striking, spiral-shaped inflorescence of pink to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wetlands. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and ecological importance in supporting local pollinators.
