Stenorrhynchos laxum

Stenorrhynchos laxum, commonly known as the slender-stemmed orchid, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, typically arranged in a loose inflorescence. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
