Schefflera chimantensis var. iluensis

Schefflera chimantensis var. iluensis, commonly known as the Iluensis umbrella plant, is a species native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of large, dark green leaves. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its adaptability and aesthetic qualities.
