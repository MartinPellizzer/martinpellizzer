Silene gracillima

Silene gracillima, commonly known as the slender catchfly, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family, characterized by its showy, pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It has small, narrow leaves and produces numerous tiny seeds in its fruiting structures.
