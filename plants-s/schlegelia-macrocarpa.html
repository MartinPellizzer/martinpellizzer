Schlegelia macrocarpa

Schlegelia macrocarpa, commonly known as the large-fruited schlegelia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Polygonaceae.

It is native to the eastern United States, particularly found in moist, wooded areas. The plant is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a reddish tint when mature.
