Senecio speculicola

Senecio speculicola, commonly known as the rock daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish to white flowers arranged in dense clusters, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Senecio speculicola is often used in rock gardens due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
