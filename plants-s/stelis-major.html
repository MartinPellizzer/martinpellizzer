Stelis major

Stelis major, commonly known as the large-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments. The plant has erect, often branching stems that can reach several feet in height. Its leaves are usually long and narrow, and it produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow.

Stelis major is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
