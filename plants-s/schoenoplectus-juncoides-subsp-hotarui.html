Schoenoplectus juncoides subsp. hotarui

Schoenoplectus juncoides subsp.

hotarui, commonly known as the Hotarui bulrush, is a herbaceous plant native to Japan. It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems and dense, spike-like inflorescences that bloom in late summer.

The plant thrives in wetland environments, such as marshes and the edges of ponds. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a slight sheen.

It plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic and terrestrial organisms.
