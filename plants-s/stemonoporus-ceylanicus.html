Stemonoporus ceylanicus

Stemonoporus ceylanicus, commonly known as the Ceylon star anise, is a tropical shrub native to Sri Lanka.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, star-shaped fruits that resemble those of the true star anise. The plant produces small, white flowers and has glossy, elliptical leaves with a strong, spicy fragrance. Its fruit is harvested when fully mature and dried, often used in traditional medicine and as a spice.

The botanical features of Stemonoporus ceylanicus make it a valuable plant in both ecological and economic contexts.
