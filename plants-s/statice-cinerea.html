Statice cinerea

Statice cinerea, commonly known as silverweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and small, delicate flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas. It has a compact growth habit and is well adapted to arid conditions.

Statice cinerea is often used in rock gardens and as a border plant due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
