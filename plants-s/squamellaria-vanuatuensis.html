Squamellaria vanuatuensis

Squamellaria vanuatuensis, commonly known as the Vanuatu starfish plant, is a unique member of the Malvaceae family.

It is native to the island of Vanuatu in the South Pacific and is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white, star-like flowers that bloom in clusters, adding to its ornamental appeal. Its leaves are often covered in a fine, waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss in its tropical environment.

Squamellaria vanuatuensis is typically grown as a houseplant due to its adaptability to indoor conditions and low maintenance requirements.
