Saurauia sakoembangensis var. sakoembangensis

Saurauia sakoembangensis var. sakoembangensis, commonly known as the Sakoembang tree, is a species native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows up to 15 meters in height. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth, glossy surface. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, and it produces small, fleshy berries that are an important food source for local wildlife.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional medicine.
