Scabiosa atropurpurea var. villosa

Scabiosa atropurpurea var. villosa, commonly known as the fuzzy scabious, is a flowering plant native to Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy foliage and clusters of pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 12 to 24 inches and prefers full sun to partial shade. It thrives in well-drained soil and is often used in gardens for its ornamental value.

The fuzzy texture of its leaves and the delicate appearance of its flowers make it a distinctive addition to the landscape.
