Stelis tricula

Stelis tricula, commonly known as the three-leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, clustered pseudobulbs. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a yellow or greenish throat. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Stelis tricula is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
