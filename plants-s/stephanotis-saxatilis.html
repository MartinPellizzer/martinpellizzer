Stephanotis saxatilis

Stephanotis saxatilis, commonly known as the rock stephanotis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a climbing shrub that produces fragrant, white, waxy flowers arranged in clusters. The plant has woody stems and heart-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

The flowers bloom in summer and are pollinated primarily by insects.
