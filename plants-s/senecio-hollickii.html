Senecio hollickii

Senecio hollickii, commonly known as Hollick's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. Senecio hollickii is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and shrublands.
