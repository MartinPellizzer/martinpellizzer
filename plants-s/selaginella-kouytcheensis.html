Selaginella kouytcheensis

Selaginella kouytcheensis, commonly known as the Kouytcheena spikemoss, is a rare and ancient vascular plant belonging to the family Selaginellaceae.

It is characterized by its small, scale-like leaves and sporophylls that bear microspores and megaspores. Native to the arid regions of Central Asia, this plant is adapted to extreme environmental conditions. Its reproductive structures are highly specialized, contributing to its survival in harsh climates.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
