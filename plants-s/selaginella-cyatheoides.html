Selaginella cyatheoides

Selaginella cyatheoides, commonly known as the clubmoss or resurrection plant, is a unique vascular plant belonging to the family Selaginellaceae.

It is characterized by its small, scale-like leaves and ability to survive extreme desiccation, often reviving when water becomes available. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant exhibits a distinctive branching pattern and has a long history of use in traditional medicine.

Its resilience and unique physiology make it an interesting subject in botanical research.
