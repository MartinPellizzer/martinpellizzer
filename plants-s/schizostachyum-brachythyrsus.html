Schizostachyum brachythyrsus

Schizostachyum brachythyrsus, commonly known as the short-thorned bamboo, is a species of grass belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. This plant is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and small, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, well-drained soils.

Schizostachyum brachythyrsus is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional landscaping and as a source of sustainable materials.
