Specklinia dichotoma

Specklinia dichotoma, commonly known as the dichotomous fern, is a species of fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, bipinnate fronds that divide into two equal parts, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in forest understories. Its rhizomes are thick and creeping, allowing it to spread slowly across the forest floor.

Specklinia dichotoma plays a role in maintaining soil stability and providing habitat for small organisms in its native ecosystem.
