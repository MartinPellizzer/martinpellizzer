Scabiosa vestina

Scabiosa vestina, commonly known as the pink scabious, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its delicate, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has a compact, upright growth habit with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Scabiosa vestina is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
