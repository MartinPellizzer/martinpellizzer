Sinobambusa pulchella

Sinobambusa pulchella, commonly known as the elegant bamboo, is a deciduous bamboo species native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and gracefully arching foliage. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with pale green to yellowish flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Sinobambusa pulchella is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
