Stoechas pinnata

Stoechas pinnata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved stoechas, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, pinnately divided leaves. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Stoechas pinnata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
