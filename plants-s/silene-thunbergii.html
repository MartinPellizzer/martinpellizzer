Silene thunbergii

Silene thunbergii, commonly known as the Thunberg's campion, is a flowering plant native to northern Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family and typically grows in moist, woodland habitats. The plant features showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and somewhat hairy.

Silene thunbergii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
