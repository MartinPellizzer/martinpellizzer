Stanhopea graveolens var. major

Stanhopea graveolens var. major, commonly known as the giant orchid or the yellow stanhopea, is a striking orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically feature a yellow or orange corolla with a distinctive red or purple lip. The plant grows as an epiphyte, often found on trees in humid, shaded environments. Its botanical features include long, narrow leaves and a complex inflorescence with multiple flowers.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is a popular species among orchid enthusiasts.
