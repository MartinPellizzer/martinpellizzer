Senecio morenonis

Senecio morenonis, commonly known as the Moreton Bay ragwort, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in coastal and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained environments. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers are yellow and arranged in clusters.

Senecio morenonis is often found in open habitats such as heathlands and coastal dunes.
