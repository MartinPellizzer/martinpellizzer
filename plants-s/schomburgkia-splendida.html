Schomburgkia splendida

Schomburgkia splendida, commonly known as the splendid spider orchid, is a striking tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, spider-like flowers that come in shades of red, yellow, and orange, often with intricate patterns. The plant has a pseudobulb structure typical of many orchids, which helps it store water and nutrients. Its leaves are long and strap-like, adapted to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

Schomburgkia splendida is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and unique floral display.
