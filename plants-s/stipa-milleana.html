Stipa milleana

Stipa milleana, commonly known as the Millean needlegrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are usually brown or reddish in hue. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops.

Stipa milleana is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.
