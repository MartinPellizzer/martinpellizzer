Schefflera glabra

Schefflera glabra, commonly known as the smooth schefflera, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like cluster of leaves, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white or greenish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, often followed by bright red berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its adaptability makes it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation in suitable climates.
