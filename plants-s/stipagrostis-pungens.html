Stipagrostis pungens

Stipagrostis pungens, commonly known as the spikegrass, is a perennial grass native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical spike-like inflorescence that rises from a clump of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water availability. Its leaves are often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Stipagrostis pungens is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping in dry climates.
