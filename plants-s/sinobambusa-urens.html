Sinobambusa urens

Sinobambusa urens, commonly known as the narrowleaf bamboo, is a species native to eastern Asia.

It is a perennial grass that grows in dense clumps, often found in moist, shady environments. The plant features slender, hollow stems that are typically green to reddish in color. Its leaves are narrow and elongated, with a smooth texture and a slightly pointed tip.

Sinobambusa urens is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
