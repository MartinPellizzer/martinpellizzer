Strophocactus chontalensis

Strophocactus chontalensis, commonly known as the Chontalensis Cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its cylindrical stem and spiny appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking visual element to its structure. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, requiring well-draining soil and minimal water.

Due to its unique features, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in cactus collections.
