Spiranthes standleyi

Spiranthes standleyi, commonly known as the Standley's spiral orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial herb with a distinctive spiral arrangement of its flowers, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its inflorescence consists of a single, tightly coiled spike of white or pale green flowers.

Spiranthes standleyi is native to parts of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, tropical climates.
