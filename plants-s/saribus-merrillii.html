Saribus merrillii

Saribus merrillii, commonly known as the Borneo palm, is a species of palm native to the island of Borneo.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by round, fibrous fruits. Saribus merrillii thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its ecological importance includes providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
