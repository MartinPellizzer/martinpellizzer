Silene parnassica subsp. parnassica

Silene parnassica subsp.

parnassica, commonly known as the Parnassian catchfly, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Central Asia. It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its striking pink to purple flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high elevations. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits.
