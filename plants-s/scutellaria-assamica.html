Scutellaria assamica

Scutellaria assamica, commonly known as Assam skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the northeastern regions of India.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and features opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are small, tubular, and usually violet or blue in color, arranged in whorled clusters.

Scutellaria assamica is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
