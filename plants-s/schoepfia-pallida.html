Schoepfia pallida

Schoepfia pallida, commonly known as the pale schoepfia, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in rocky or arid environments. The plant features small, pale green leaves and produces clusters of yellowish-white flowers. It is adapted to drought conditions and has a deep root system for water absorption.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
