Suprago

Suprago, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to parts of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, pendulous clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in abundance during the rainy season. The plant has compound leaves with multiple leaflets and produces small, winged seeds that are dispersed by wind. Suprago is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
