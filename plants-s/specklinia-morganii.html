Specklinia morganii

Specklinia morganii, commonly known as the Morgan's speckled orchid, is a small epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dark green, leathery leaves that are often marked with speckles or spots. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, typically during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Specklinia morganii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
