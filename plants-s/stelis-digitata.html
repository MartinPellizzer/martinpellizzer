Stelis digitata

Stelis digitata, commonly known as the digitate stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of small, showy blooms in shades of white, yellow, or pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Stelis digitata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both greenhouse and tropical garden settings.
