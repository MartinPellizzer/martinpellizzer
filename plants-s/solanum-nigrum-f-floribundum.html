Solanum nigrum f. floribundum

Solanum nigrum f. floribundum, commonly known as the black nightshade, is a variant of the common nightshade plant.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, dark purple berries that are edible but contain toxic compounds. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.
