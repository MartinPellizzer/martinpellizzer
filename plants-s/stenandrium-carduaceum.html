Stenandrium carduaceum

Stenandrium carduaceum, commonly known as the thistleweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower. The plant typically grows in grasslands and disturbed soils, exhibiting a rosette of basal leaves with spiny margins. Its flowers are usually yellow or white, appearing in clusters at the ends of stems.

Stenandrium carduaceum is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
