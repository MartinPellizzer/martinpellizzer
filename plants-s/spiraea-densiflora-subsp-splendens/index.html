Spiraea Densiflora Subsp. Splendens

Spiraea densiflora subsp. splendens is a striking shrub known for its vibrant floral displays. Native to the mountainous regions of western North America, it thrives in cool, moist environments. This subspecies is characterized by its dense clusters of showy pink or white flowers that bloom in early spring. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to soil and moisture conditions for optimal growth.
