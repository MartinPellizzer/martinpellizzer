Solanum ochrandrum

Solanum ochrandrum, commonly known as the yellow nightshade, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by berries that change color as they mature. Its leaves are typically ovate with a slightly hairy texture, and the plant is often found in shaded, moist environments.

Solanum ochrandrum is noted for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
