Senecio doria subvar. intermedius

Senecio doria subvar. intermedius, commonly known as the Doria's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in clusters.

It is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, showcasing adaptability to various environmental conditions.
