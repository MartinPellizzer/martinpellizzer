Saccharum polydactylon

Saccharum polydactylon, commonly known as purple fountain grass, is a tall, ornamental grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features tall, slender stalks with long, feathery plumes that turn a striking purple hue as the plant matures. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. Its rapid growth and ability to spread through rhizomes make it both a desirable ornamental and a potential invasive species in some areas.

Botanically, it belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its fibrous roots and dense, clumping growth habit.
