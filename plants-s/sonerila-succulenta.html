Sonerila succulenta

Sonerila succulenta, commonly known as the succulent sonerila, is a member of the Melastomataceae family and is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fleshy, water-storing leaves that help it survive in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of pink and purple, which bloom during the wet season. Its succulent foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Due to its unique adaptations, it is often cultivated in xeriscapes and rock gardens.
