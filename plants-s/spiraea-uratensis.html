Spiraea uratensis

Spiraea uratensis, commonly known as the Japanese spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming in early spring. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and has finely cut, serrated leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Botanically, it belongs to the Rosaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability.
