Scorzonera Decumbens

Scorzonera, scientifically known as Scorzonera decumbens, is a lesser-known yet fascinating member of the Asteraceae family. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, this plant thrives in rocky and arid environments. Its distinctive appearance includes hairy stems and yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months. Despite its rugged habitat, Scorzonera decumbens has been used in traditional medicine for centuries. This resilient plant offers both ecological and historical significance worth exploring.
