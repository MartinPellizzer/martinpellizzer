Scindapsus officinalis

Scindapsus officinalis, commonly known as the silver vine, is a tropical evergreen vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy, dark green leaves that often have a silvery or white vein pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers moist, well-draining soil.

Scindapsus officinalis is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and air-purifying qualities.
