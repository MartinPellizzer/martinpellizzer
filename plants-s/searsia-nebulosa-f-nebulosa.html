Searsia nebulosa f. nebulosa

Searsia nebulosa f. nebulosa, commonly known as the clouded glory bush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, ovate leaves that are typically pale green with a bluish or greyish hue, giving it its common name. The plant produces yellow flowers in winter, which are clustered in umbels and attract pollinators like bees and birds. Its fruit is a small, spherical drupe that turns red when mature.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in coastal and sandy soils.
