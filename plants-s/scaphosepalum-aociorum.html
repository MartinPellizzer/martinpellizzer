Scaphosepalum aociorum

Scaphosepalum aociorum, commonly known as the Aociorum orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and clusters of fragrant, violet-blue flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically blooms during the spring and early summer, showcasing its ornamental value. It thrives in cool, moist environments with well-drained soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
