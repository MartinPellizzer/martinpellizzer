Silene langshanensis

Silene langshanensis, commonly known as the Langshan catchfly, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pinkish-purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils in mountainous regions. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Silene langshanensis is of interest to botanists due to its limited distribution and unique ecological adaptations.
