Satyrium × fibeckii

Satyrium × fibeckii, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a fascinating plant that belongs to the Orchidaceae family.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between two species, Satyrium orchidum and Satyrium pusillum, which are both native to South America. This hybrid plant is characterized by its intricate and often colorful flowers, which typically feature a combination of purple, pink, and white hues. The botanical features of Satyrium × fibeckii include its sympodial growth habit and its ability to produce showy, fragrant blooms.

Due to its unique genetic makeup, it is a subject of interest in both horticulture and botanical research.
