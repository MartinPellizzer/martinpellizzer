Stelis grandiflora

Stelis grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and clusters of showy, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged along the stem.

Stelis grandiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
