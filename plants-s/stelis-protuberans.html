Stelis protuberans

Stelis protuberans, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on rocky outcrops and in shaded forest environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with a pseudobulb that supports its flowering structure. Its flowers are usually white or pale green with a distinctive lip, and it produces small, fleshy roots adapted to its rocky habitat.

Stelis protuberans is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil.
