Saxifraga androsacea f. pyrenaica

Saxifraga androsacea f. pyrenaica, commonly known as the Pyrenean saxifrage, is a flowering plant belonging to the Saxifragaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or white, blooming in late spring to early summer. Native to the mountainous regions of the Iberian Peninsula and southern France, it thrives in rocky, well-drained soils.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species for alpine and subalpine habitats.
