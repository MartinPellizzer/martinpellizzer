Senecio cotyledonis

Senecio cotyledonis, commonly known as the Cape York senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and yellow-orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. It is often found in mountainous regions and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
