Senecio morettii

Senecio morettii, commonly known as the Morettii groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of less than 10 centimeters. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are usually gray-green, with a fuzzy texture, and it thrives in rocky or sandy soils.

Senecio morettii is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and chaparral.
