Salomonia

Salomonia, commonly known as the crown of thorns, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apocynaceae family.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its distinctive, spiny stems and clusters of small, tubular flowers. The plant produces red or orange flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects. Salomonia is adapted to arid environments, with succulent leaves that store water.

Its botanical features make it a hardy and visually striking plant in both natural and cultivated settings.
