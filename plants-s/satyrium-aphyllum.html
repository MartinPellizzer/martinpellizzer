Satyrium aphyllum

Satyrium aphyllum, commonly known as the leafless orchid, is a unique member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its lack of leaves, a trait that sets it apart from most other orchids. The plant produces a single, showy flower with a distinctive lip that is often brightly colored. It thrives in specific habitats, typically in shaded, moist environments within its native range.

Due to its specialized growth requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
