Swertia macrosepala subsp. macrosepala

Swertia macrosepala subsp.

macrosepala, commonly known as the Himalayan gentian, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan regions of Asia. It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers and lance-shaped leaves.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes. It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.

Its botanical features make it an important species in alpine ecosystems.
