Sempervivum laetevirens

Sempervivum laetevirens, commonly known as the "Alpine Houseplant" or "Hens and Chicks," is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features rosettes of fleshy, pointed leaves that are typically green with reddish or bronze hues, especially in cooler temperatures. The plant produces vibrant red or pink flowers on tall stalks during the summer months. It is well adapted to rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun.

Sempervivum laetevirens is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that is popular in rock gardens and as a houseplant.
