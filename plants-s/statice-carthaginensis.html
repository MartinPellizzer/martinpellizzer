Statice carthaginensis

Statice carthaginensis, commonly known as the Carthage glasswort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, spindly stems and small, delicate flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in saline or coastal environments, often found in dunes or along the seashore. Its leaves are succulent and fleshy, adapted to retain moisture in arid conditions.

Statice carthaginensis is also valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate harsh, coastal climates.
