Scheelea attaleoides

Scheelea attaleoides, commonly known as the tropical orchid, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, tropical environments. Its leaves are elongated and often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs.

Scheelea attaleoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated for its vibrant, fragrant blooms.
