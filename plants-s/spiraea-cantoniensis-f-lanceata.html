Spiraea cantoniensis f. lanceata

Spiraea cantoniensis f. lanceata, commonly known as the lanceolate spiraea, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to China.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green, providing an attractive foliage display throughout the growing season. The plant produces clusters of delicate, white to pale pink flowers in early summer, which bloom along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden borders or as a specimen plant.

Its ornamental value lies in its foliage and the subtle, fragrant blooms that add a refined touch to landscapes.
