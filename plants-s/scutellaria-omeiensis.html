Scutellaria omeiensis

Scutellaria omeiensis, commonly known as the Omei skullcap, is a rare flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.
