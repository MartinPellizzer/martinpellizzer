Scutellaria multiflora

Scutellaria multiflora, commonly known as blue skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, sky-blue to violet flowers that bloom from late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Scutellaria multiflora is valued in herbal medicine for its potential calming and anti-inflammatory properties.
