Staavia dregeana

Staavia dregeana, commonly known as the Cape dandelion, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that resemble dandelions. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and often branched.

Staavia dregeana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
