Senecio rudis

Senecio rudis, commonly known as the rock dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, branched stems and bright yellow composite flowers that bloom in summer. The leaves are typically linear and covered in fine hairs, giving the plant a slightly fuzzy appearance. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Senecio rudis is a hardy plant that is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
