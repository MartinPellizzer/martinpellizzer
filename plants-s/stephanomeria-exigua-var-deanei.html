Stephanomeria exigua var. deanei

Stephanomeria exigua var. deanei, commonly known as the Deane's tansy, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its small, yellowish flowers are arranged in clusters, and it has narrow, gray-green leaves.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native plant gardens.
