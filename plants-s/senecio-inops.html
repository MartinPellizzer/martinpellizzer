Senecio inops

Senecio inops, commonly known as the ghost plant, is a member of the Asteraceae family and is native to South Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous plant characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and delicate, yellowish-green flowers. Senecio inops is often found in coastal regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
