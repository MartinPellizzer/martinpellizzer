Stelis cucullata

Stelis cucullata, commonly known as the hooded orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, humid environments such as forest floors and mountainous regions. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive hood-like structure over the lip.

Stelis cucullata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or shade gardens.
