Sterculia rubiginosa var. glabrescens

Sterculia rubiginosa var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed sterculia, is a tropical tree native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous bark and large, palmately lobed leaves. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that are clustered in panicles, attracting various pollinators. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local medicine and crafts.
