Stellaria valida

Stellaria valida, commonly known as the starwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, white or pinkish flowers that are arranged in clusters, giving it a star-like appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, with a hairy texture.

Stellaria valida is often found in temperate regions of Europe and Asia, where it thrives in nutrient-rich soils.
