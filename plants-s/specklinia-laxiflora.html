Specklinia laxiflora

Specklinia laxiflora, commonly known as the slender-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, thin leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as forest understories. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive spurred lip.

Specklinia laxiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
