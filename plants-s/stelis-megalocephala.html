Stelis megalocephala

Stelis megalocephala, commonly known as the giant-headed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 centimeters in diameter. Its inflorescence typically consists of multiple blooms arranged in a raceme, with bright yellow petals and a distinctive, elongated lip.

Stelis megalocephala thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in the understory of tropical forests.
