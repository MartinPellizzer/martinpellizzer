Shorea balangeran

Shorea balangeran, commonly known as the Balangeran tree, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Dipterocarpaceae family and is characterized by its tall stature and buttressed roots. The tree produces heavy, aromatic wood that is highly valued for its durability and resistance to termites. Its leaves are alternate, elliptical, and have a smooth surface.

Shorea balangeran plays an important role in the ecological systems of its native habitats.
