Salvia Aucheri Subsp. Canescens

Salvia aucheri subsp. canescens is a hardy perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Turkey. It is known for its striking violet-blue flowers that bloom in late spring and early summer. The plant features silvery-gray foliage that adds visual interest throughout the growing season. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. This subspecies is valued for its ornamental beauty and adaptability to various garden conditions.
