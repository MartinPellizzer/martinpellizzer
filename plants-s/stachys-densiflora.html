Stachys densiflora

Stachys densiflora, commonly known as denseflower betony, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright stems covered in dense, woolly hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically blue to purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its foliage is aromatic and can be used in herbal teas, adding to its botanical and practical value.
