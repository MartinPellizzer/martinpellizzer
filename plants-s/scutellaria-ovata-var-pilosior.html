Scutellaria ovata var. pilosior

Scutellaria ovata var. pilosior, commonly known as the hairy oval skullcap, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of North America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features oval, hairy leaves and produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

This variant is distinguished by its dense, hairy foliage, which gives it its specific epithet "pilosior.".
