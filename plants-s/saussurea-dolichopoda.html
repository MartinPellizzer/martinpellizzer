Saussurea dolichopoda

Saussurea dolichopoda, commonly known as the long-stemmed saussurea, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky areas, thriving in cold, arid conditions. Saussurea dolichopoda is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
