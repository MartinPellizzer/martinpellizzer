Stelis buchtienii

Stelis buchtienii, commonly known as the Buchtien's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its compact growth habit and clustered pseudobulbs. The plant produces narrow, grass-like leaves and bears small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale pink. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it a unique and sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
