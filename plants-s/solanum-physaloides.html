Solanum physaloides

Solanum physaloides, commonly known as the bladderleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas, particularly in Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. Its fruit is a distinctive, inflated, greenish capsule that turns red when mature.

Solanum physaloides is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats, including forests and open fields.
