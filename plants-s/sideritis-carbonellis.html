Sideritis carbonellis

Sideritis carbonellis, commonly known as Carbonell's ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its slender stems, opposite leaves, and small, pale blue or violet flowers. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats.

Sideritis carbonellis is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
