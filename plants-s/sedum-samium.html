Sedum samium

Sedum samium, commonly known as the stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped yellow flowers in the summer months. It is often found growing on rocky soils or in dry habitats.

Sedum samium is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance in gardens.
