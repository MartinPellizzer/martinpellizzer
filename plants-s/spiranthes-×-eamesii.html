Spiranthes × eamesii

Spiranthes × eamesii, commonly known as the Eames' spiral orchid, is a hybrid orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its delicate, spiral-shaped inflorescence composed of numerous small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as pine flatwoods and wetlands. It has a rhizomatous root system and produces showy, fragrant blooms during the late spring and early summer.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with appropriate moisture and light conditions.
