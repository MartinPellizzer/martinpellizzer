Schlumbergera kautskyi

Schlumbergera kautskyi, commonly known as the Kautsky holiday cactus, is a species native to Brazil.

It is a type of epiphytic cactus that belongs to the Cactaceae family. The plant is characterized by its segmented, leaf-like stems that are often brightly colored. It produces showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white during the holiday season.

Schlumbergera kautskyi is popular as a houseplant due to its ease of care and long blooming period.
