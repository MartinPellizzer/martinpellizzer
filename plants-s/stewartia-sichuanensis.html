Stewartia sichuanensis

Stewartia sichuanensis, commonly known as the Sichuan Stewartia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Theaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, elongated leaves and showy, fragrant white flowers. The plant is noted for its ornamental value, particularly in gardens and landscapes due to its attractive foliage and floral display. It typically grows in mountainous regions and prefers well-drained, acidic soils.

Stewartia sichuanensis is also valued for its slow growth and longevity, making it a desirable specimen in horticulture.
