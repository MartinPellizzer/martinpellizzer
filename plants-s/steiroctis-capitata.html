Steiroctis capitata

Steiroctis capitata, commonly known as the clustered starflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches a height of about 10-20 centimeters. The plant features a cluster of small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming a dense mat on the ground.

Steiroctis capitata is well adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in sunny, coastal areas.
