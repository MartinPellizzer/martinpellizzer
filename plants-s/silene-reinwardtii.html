Silene reinwardtii

Silene reinwardtii, commonly known as the Reinwardt's catchfly, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, pink or white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are linear and often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Silene reinwardtii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
