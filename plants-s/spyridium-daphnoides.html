Spyridium daphnoides

Spyridium daphnoides, commonly known as the silverleaf spyridium, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal regions. The plant features small, oval leaves with a silvery underside, giving it its common name. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow and are arranged in clusters.

Spyridium daphnoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native garden landscapes.
