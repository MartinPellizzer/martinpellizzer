Seriphidium validum

Seriphidium validum, commonly known as the narrowleaf tansy.

It is a flowering plant in the sunflower family, Asteraceae, native to the arid regions of Central Asia. The plant features slender, branching stems covered in fine, grayish hairs. Its leaves are linear and often have a strong, aromatic scent.

It produces small yellow flowers arranged in composite heads, typical of the Asteraceae family.
