Statice globulariifolia var. majus

Statice globulariifolia var. majus, commonly known as globe amaranth, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, globular inflorescence composed of numerous small, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant produces small, triangular seeds that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its propagation. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant blooms.
