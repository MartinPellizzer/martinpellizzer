Saxifraga hornungii

Saxifraga hornungii, commonly known as the hornung's saxifrage, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves and produces small, white or pinkish flowers during the summer months. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats. It has adapted to harsh environmental conditions, including cold temperatures and strong winds.

Saxifraga hornungii is a member of the Saxifragaceae family, which includes a variety of hardy, often alpine species.
