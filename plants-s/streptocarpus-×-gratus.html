Streptocarpus × gratus

Streptocarpus × gratus, commonly known as the hybrid streptocarpus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces showy, spurred flowers in shades of violet, pink, and white, often in clusters. Its foliage consists of glossy, dark green, ovate leaves that are arranged oppositely along the stem.

Streptocarpus × gratus is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
