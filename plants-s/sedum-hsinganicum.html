Sedum hsinganicum

Sedum hsinganicum, commonly known as the Hsingan sedum, is a succulent plant native to China.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It produces vibrant pink or purple flowers during the summer months, making it a visually striking addition to gardens.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in regions with mild climates.
