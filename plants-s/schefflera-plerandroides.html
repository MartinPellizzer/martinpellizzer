Schefflera plerandroides

Schefflera plerandroides, commonly known as the giant umbrella tree, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, umbrella-like foliage composed of multiple leaflets radiating from a central stem. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters in its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually glossy and dark green, with a slightly waxy texture.

Schefflera plerandroides is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its adaptability and ability to thrive in a variety of light conditions.
