Sarcochilus merrillii

Sarcochilus merrillii, commonly known as the Borneo helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Borneo.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique helmet-shaped pseudobulbs. The plant produces striking, pale yellow to orange flowers with a distinctive hood-like structure. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Sarcochilus merrillii is a rare and endangered species, making it a subject of conservation interest.
