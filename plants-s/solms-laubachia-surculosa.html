Solms-laubachia surculosa

It is characterized by its dense, velvety fronds that are often used in horticulture for their ornamental value. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as rainforests or along stream banks. Its rhizomes are fibrous and can spread to form dense colonies.
