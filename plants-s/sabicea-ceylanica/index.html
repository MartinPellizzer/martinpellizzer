Sabicea Ceylanica

Sabicea ceylanica is a lesser-known yet fascinating plant species native to the tropical regions of Asia. It belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes over 13,000 species of flowering plants. This plant is characterized by its delicate leaves and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom throughout the year. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Despite its beauty, Sabicea ceylanica remains relatively understudied and is not widely cultivated.
