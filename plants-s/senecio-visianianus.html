Senecio visianianus

Senecio visianianus, commonly known as the Blue Echeveria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, blue-gray leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. The plant produces striking clusters of yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal make it a favored plant among succulent enthusiasts.
