Sauropus stenocladus

Sauropus stenocladus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sauropus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually narrow and elliptical in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in green or red bracts.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
