Scutellaria rojasii

Scutellaria rojasii, commonly known as the Rojas' skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple, often arranged in whorls along the stem. Its leaves are typically oval and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Scutellaria rojasii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
