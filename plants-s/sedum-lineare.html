Sedum lineare

Sedum lineare, commonly known as linear sedum, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a succulent species characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and clusters of star-shaped yellow flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it adaptable to arid and rocky environments. It is often found in prairies, woodlands, and along roadsides.

Sedum lineare is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in erosion-prone areas.
