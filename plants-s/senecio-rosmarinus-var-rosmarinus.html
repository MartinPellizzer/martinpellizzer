Senecio rosmarinus var. rosmarinus

Senecio rosmarinus var. rosmarinus, commonly known as upright everlasting.

This plant is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. It features narrow, gray-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense, spherical heads.

It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
