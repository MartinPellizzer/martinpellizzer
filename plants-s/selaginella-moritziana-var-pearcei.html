Selaginella moritziana var. pearcei

Selaginella moritziana var. pearcei, commonly known as the pearce's spikemoss, is a species of spikemoss belonging to the family Selaginellaceae.

It is a small, evergreen herbaceous plant characterized by its creeping rhizomes and dense, compact foliage. The leaves are typically narrow, linear, and arranged in two rows along the stems. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in rocky crevices or forest floors.

This variety is noted for its ability to tolerate low light conditions and is appreciated for its ornamental value in horticulture.
