Schefflera subavenis var. eurhyncha

Schefflera subavenis var. eurhyncha, commonly known as the broadleaf schefflera, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palm-like leaves that are typically arranged in a compact, umbrella-shaped cluster. The plant features a smooth, dark green stem with a woody texture and can grow up to several meters in height under optimal conditions. Its leaves are thick and leathery, providing a striking visual appeal.

This variety is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its adaptability and air-purifying qualities.
