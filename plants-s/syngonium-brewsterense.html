Syngonium brewsterense

Syngonium brewsterense, commonly known as the Brewster's arrowhead, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its distinctive arrowhead-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically surrounded by a spathaceous bract. Syngonium brewsterense thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid tropical forests.

Its foliage is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to indoor growing conditions.
