Selbyana javieri

Selbyana javieri, commonly known as the Java orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, shaded environments. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Selbyana javieri is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
