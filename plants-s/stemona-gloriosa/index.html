Stemona Gloriosa

Golden stemona, scientifically known as Stemona gloriosa, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. With its vibrant yellow flowers and delicate, lance-shaped leaves, it captivates botanists and gardeners alike. This species thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories where it contributes to the biodiversity of its habitat. Its ornamental value has made it a popular choice for cultivation in tropical and subtropical gardens. Despite its beauty, the golden stemona remains relatively obscure compared to more commonly cultivated plants.
