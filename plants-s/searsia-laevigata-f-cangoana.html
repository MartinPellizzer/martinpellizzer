Searsia laevigata f. cangoana

Searsia laevigata f. cangoana, commonly known as the Cango white thorn, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its pale yellow to white flowers that appear in clusters. The plant has small, elliptical leaves with a smooth texture and a distinctive thorny structure on its branches. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in coastal and sandy habitats.

The Cango white thorn is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
