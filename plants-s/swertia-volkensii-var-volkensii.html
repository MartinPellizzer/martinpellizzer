Swertia volkensii var. volkensii

Swertia volkensii var. volkensii, commonly known as the African gentian, is a perennial herb native to eastern and southern Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and rocky areas. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a bluish-green hue and produces striking blue or violet flowers during the spring and summer months. Its flowering stems are often branched and bear clusters of small, tubular flowers.

Swertia volkensii var. volkensii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
