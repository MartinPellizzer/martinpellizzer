Sophronitis cernua var. alagoensis

Sophronitis cernua var. alagoensis, commonly known as the Alagoas orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive three-lobed lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
