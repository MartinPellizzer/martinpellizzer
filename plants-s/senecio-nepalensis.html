Senecio nepalensis

Senecio nepalensis, commonly known as the Nepal groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan regions of Nepal and northern India.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a composite head. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Senecio nepalensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
