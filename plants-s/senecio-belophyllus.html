Senecio belophyllus

Senecio belophyllus, commonly known as the blue ice plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and its ability to store water in its fleshy, blue-green leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a subtle ornamental touch. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
