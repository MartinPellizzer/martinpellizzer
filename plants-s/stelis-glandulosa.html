Stelis glandulosa

Stelis glandulosa, commonly known as the glandular stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are usually elliptical and covered with fine, glandular hairs.

Stelis glandulosa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
