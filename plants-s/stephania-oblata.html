Stephania oblata

Stephania oblata, commonly known as the Chinese twinflower, is a climbing plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Menispermaceae and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its roots have been used in traditional medicine for their potential health benefits.
