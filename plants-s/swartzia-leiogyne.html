Swartzia leiogyne

Swartzia leiogyne, commonly known as the smooth swartzia, is a tropical tree native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its straight, cylindrical trunk and smooth, grayish bark. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Its leaves are bipinnate, consisting of numerous small, oval-shaped leaflets.

Swartzia leiogyne is valued for its timber, which is used in furniture and construction due to its durability and fine grain.
