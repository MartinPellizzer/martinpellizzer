Sariava

Sariava, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in bright, indirect light. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
