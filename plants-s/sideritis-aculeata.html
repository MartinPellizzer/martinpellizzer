Sideritis aculeata

Sideritis aculeata, commonly known as prickly ironwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its hairy, upright stems and small, gray-green leaves. It produces clusters of pale blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer.

Sideritis aculeata is often found in rocky or dry habitats and is valued for its medicinal properties.
