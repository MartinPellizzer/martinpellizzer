Seseli libanotis subsp. pyrenaicum

Seseli libanotis subsp.

pyrenaicum, commonly known as the Pyrenean seseli, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Iberian Peninsula. It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its umbellate inflorescences and aromatic foliage.
