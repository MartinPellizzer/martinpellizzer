Syngonium foreroanum

Syngonium foreroanum, commonly known as the Forero's arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that often exhibit variegation with white or cream patterns. The plant thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its rapid growth makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and as a houseplant.

Syngonium foreroanum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to purify indoor air.
