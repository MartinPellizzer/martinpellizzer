Stanhopea oculata var. flava

Stanhopea oculata var. flava, commonly known as the Yellow Orchid, is a striking orchid species native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow petals and a distinctive, intricate floral structure typical of the Stanhopea genus. The plant produces fragrant blooms that are often long-lasting, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its beauty and unique coloration.
