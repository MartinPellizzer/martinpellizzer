Smilax laevis var. vanchingshanensis

Smilax laevis var. vanchingshanensis, commonly known as the Vanchingshan Smilax, is a variant of the smooth smilax, native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is a perennial vine characterized by its woody stems and spiny, alternate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically clustered in axillary inflorescences. Its berries are dark red to black when mature and are an important food source for various wildlife.

This variant is valued for its ornamental qualities and traditional medicinal uses in local communities.
