Stevia elatior var. austrina

Stevia elatior var. austrina, commonly known as the Australian stevia, is a perennial shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils.

Stevia elatior var. austrina is also valued for its use in traditional medicine and as a natural sweetener.
