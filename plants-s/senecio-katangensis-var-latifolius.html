Senecio katangensis var. latifolius

Senecio katangensis var. latifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf senecio, is a flowering plant native to the Democratic Republic of the Congo.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and produces yellow flowers in a cluster. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
