Seseli cyclolobum

Seseli cyclolobum, commonly known as the Texas sesame.

It is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States, particularly Texas. The plant has hairy, branched stems and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late spring. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a rough texture.

Seseli cyclolobum is adapted to arid conditions and thrives in well-drained soils.
