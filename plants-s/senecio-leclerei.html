Senecio leclerei

Senecio leclerei, commonly known as the Cape snowbush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces showy, daisy-like flowers in shades of white and yellow, which bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping.
