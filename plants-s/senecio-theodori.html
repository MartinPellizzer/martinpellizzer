Senecio theodori

Senecio theodori, commonly known as the Theodore's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky, arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in a fine layer of white hairs, which help reduce water loss.

The yellow flowers appear in clusters, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.
