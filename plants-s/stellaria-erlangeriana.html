Stellaria erlangeriana

Stellaria erlangeriana, commonly known as the Erlanger's starwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family, which includes other flowering plants such as carnations and chickweed. The plant features small, white or pale pink flowers that are typically clustered in star-like formations, giving it its common name. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered with fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Stellaria erlangeriana thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.
