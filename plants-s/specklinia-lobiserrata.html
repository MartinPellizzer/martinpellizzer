Specklinia lobiserrata

Specklinia lobiserrata, commonly known as the serrated leaf orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, often appearing during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique foliage and subtle floral display.
