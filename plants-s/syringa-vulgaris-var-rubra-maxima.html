Syringa vulgaris var. rubra-maxima

Syringa vulgaris var. rubra-maxima, commonly known as the Red Giant weigela, is a cultivar of the weigela plant known for its striking floral display.

It features large, showy flowers that are typically a deep red or crimson color, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has upright, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves that are a glossy green throughout the growing season. It is a deciduous shrub that can reach heights of up to 6 feet, making it a prominent feature in gardens.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in well-drained soil and full sun.
