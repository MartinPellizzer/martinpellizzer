Solanum grossularia var. subunijugum

Solanum grossularia var. subunijugum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved nightshade, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant features ovate to lanceolate leaves with a hairy surface, and its flowers are typically white or pale blue, clustered in umbels. The fruit is a small, yellowish berry that contains several seeds.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks.
