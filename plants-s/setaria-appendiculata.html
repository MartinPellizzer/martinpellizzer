Setaria appendiculata

Setaria appendiculata, commonly known as slender dropseed, is a grass species native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and fine, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in prairies and open woodlands.

Setaria appendiculata is a hardy perennial that plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife.
