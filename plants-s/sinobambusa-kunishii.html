Sinobambusa kunishii

Sinobambusa kunishii, commonly known as the Japanese bamboo or Sinobambusa bamboo, is a species native to East Asia.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its slender, hollow stems and dense foliage. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in forested areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Sinobambusa kunishii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic qualities in gardens.
