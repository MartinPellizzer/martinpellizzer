Stachys germanica subsp. heldreichii

Stachys germanica subsp.

heldreichii, commonly known as the Heldreich's betony, is a perennial herb native to the Balkan Peninsula. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by square stems and opposite leaves.

The plant features violet to purple flowers arranged in whorls along the upper stems. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.
