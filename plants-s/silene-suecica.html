Silene suecica

Silene suecica, commonly known as the Baltic bellflower, is a rare flowering plant native to the Baltic region.

It is characterized by its striking pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in coastal dunes and sandy areas. Silene suecica is a herbaceous perennial with slender, erect stems and narrow leaves.
