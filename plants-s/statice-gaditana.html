Statice gaditana

Statice gaditana, commonly known as sea lavender, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, spiky appearance and its ability to thrive in saline and coastal environments. The plant produces small, fluffy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, adapted to retain moisture in arid conditions.

Statice gaditana is often used in floral arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms and ornamental value.
