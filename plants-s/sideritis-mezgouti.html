Sideritis mezgouti

Sideritis mezgouti, commonly known as the Greek ironwort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Greece.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of blue and purple. It thrives in rocky and limestone soils, often found in high-altitude environments.

Sideritis mezgouti is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Greek herbal remedies.
