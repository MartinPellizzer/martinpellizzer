Sterculia neriifolia

Sterculia neriifolia, commonly known as the black-leaf sterculia, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green on the upper surface and lighter on the underside. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by woody pods containing flat, winged seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in some indigenous cultures.
