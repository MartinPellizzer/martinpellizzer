Stylosanthes gloiodes

Stylosanthes gloiodes, commonly known as the silverleaf stylo, is a tropical legume native to Africa and the Americas.

It is characterized by its small, trifoliate leaves and clusters of white or pale pink flowers. The plant has a deep root system that helps it thrive in poor soils and arid conditions. It is often used as forage for livestock due to its high nutritional value and drought tolerance.

Additionally, it plays a role in soil nitrogen fixation, making it beneficial for agricultural systems.
