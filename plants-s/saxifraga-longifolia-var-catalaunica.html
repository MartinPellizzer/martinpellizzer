Saxifraga longifolia var. catalaunica

Saxifraga longifolia var. catalaunica, commonly known as the Catalan saxifrage, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It typically grows in rocky or limestone soils, often in mountainous regions. The plant features rosettes of basal leaves and produces clusters of small, white or pinkish flowers in the spring. It is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in harsh, alpine conditions.

The variety catalaunica is distinguished by its specific geographic distribution and slightly different floral characteristics compared to the typical form of Saxifraga longifolia.
