Specklinia scariosa

Specklinia scariosa, commonly known as the leathery fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery fronds that are often covered in a fine, waxy layer. Native to Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. The fronds are typically divided into segments with a distinctive, serrated edge.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a decorative plant in tropical gardens.
