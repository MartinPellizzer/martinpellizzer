Schismatoglottis plurivenia

Schismatoglottis plurivenia, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a rare and striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

Native to the mountainous regions of Central and South America, it is characterized by its unique, spider-like inflorescence and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its native habitat. It features broad, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of small, colorful blossoms that attract pollinators.
