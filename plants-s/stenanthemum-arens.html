Stenanthemum arens

Stenanthemum arens, commonly known as the coastal stenanthemum, is a shrub native to the coastal regions of southern Australia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, narrow leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers in late spring to early summer, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to withstand saline and windy conditions.

Stenanthemum arens plays an important role in stabilizing coastal dunes and providing habitat for native wildlife.
