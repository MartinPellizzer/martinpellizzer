Sedum Telephium Var. Vogesiacum

Vogesia sedum, scientifically known as Sedum telephium var. vogesiacum, is a resilient succulent native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe. This plant is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves and vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in rocky soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine landscapes. Its ability to store water in its leaves allows it to survive in harsh, arid conditions. Vogesia sedum is not only an ornamental plant but also plays a vital role in supporting local biodiversity.
