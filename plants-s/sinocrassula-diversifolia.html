Sinocrassula diversifolia

Sinocrassula diversifolia, commonly known as the variegated stonecrop, is a low-growing succulent native to China.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are often variegated with white, yellow, or green markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

Its unique foliage and hardy nature make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
