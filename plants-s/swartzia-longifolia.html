Swartzia longifolia

Swartzia longifolia, commonly known as the long-leaved swartzia, is a tropical tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, pinnate leaves and small, clustered flowers that range from white to pink in color. The tree produces woody pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Swartzia longifolia is valued for its hard, durable wood, which is used in furniture and construction.
