Spergularia patens

Spergularia patens, commonly known as the sea sandspurry, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of Europe and North America.

It is a low-growing herbaceous plant that thrives in sandy and saline environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are typically succulent and adapted to retain moisture in arid conditions.

Spergularia patens plays a role in stabilizing coastal soils and is often found in dune ecosystems.
