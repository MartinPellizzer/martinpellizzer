Schimperella

Schimperella, commonly known as the moonflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that open at night and close during the day. The plant belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is known for its aromatic scent. Schimperella thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
