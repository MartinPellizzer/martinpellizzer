Sideritis Lurida Subsp. Borgiae

Sideritis lurida subsp. borgiae is a rare and lesser-known plant species native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe. This subspecies is distinguished by its unique floral structure and adaptability to harsh, high-altitude environments. It thrives in rocky soils and is often found in alpine meadows where it plays a vital role in local ecosystems. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensuring its survival in the face of climate change and habitat loss.
