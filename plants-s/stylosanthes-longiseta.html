Stylosanthes longiseta

Stylosanthes longiseta, commonly known as the long-hair stylo, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its prostrate growth habit and trifoliate leaves with hairy surfaces. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by cylindrical, pod-like fruits containing several seeds. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a valuable forage crop.

Its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen improves soil fertility, enhancing its agricultural utility.
