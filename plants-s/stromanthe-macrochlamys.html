Stromanthe macrochlamys

Stromanthe macrochlamys, commonly known as the Variegated Stromanthe, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. The plant features striking, variegated foliage with shades of green, white, and pink, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Stromanthe macrochlamys is known for its ability to purify the air and add aesthetic value to any indoor space.
