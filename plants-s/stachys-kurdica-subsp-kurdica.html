Stachys kurdica subsp. kurdica

Stachys kurdica subsp.

kurdica, commonly known as Kurdish hedgehog. It is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of the Middle East and Central Asia.

The plant features upright, hairy stems and produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a rough, textured surface.

It is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
