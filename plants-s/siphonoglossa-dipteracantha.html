Siphonoglossa dipteracantha

Siphonoglossa dipteracantha, commonly known as the flame violet, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It features showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in opposite pairs along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.
