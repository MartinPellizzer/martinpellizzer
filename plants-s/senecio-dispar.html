Senecio dispar

Senecio dispar, commonly known as the ragged Robin, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Senecio dispar thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
