Sansevieria rugosifolia

Sansevieria rugosifolia, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a striking member of the Asparagaceae family native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves with distinctive dark green stripes and a slightly wavy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, white, bell-shaped flowers during the growing season. It is highly drought-tolerant and thrives in low-light conditions, making it an ideal indoor plant.

Its unique appearance and easy care requirements have made it a popular choice for both novice and experienced plant enthusiasts.
