Stellaria kotschyana var. intermedia

Stellaria kotschyana var. intermedia, commonly known as the Intermediate starwort, is a flowering plant native to parts of Central Asia and the Caucasus.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its star-shaped white flowers and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically thrives in moist, rocky or sandy soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine environments. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
