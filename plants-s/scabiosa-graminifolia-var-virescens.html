Scabiosa graminifolia var. virescens

Scabiosa graminifolia var. virescens, commonly known as greenleaf scabious, is a perennial herb native to Europe.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically a vibrant green, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens or borders.

Its botanical characteristics make it an attractive addition to both wild and cultivated landscapes.
