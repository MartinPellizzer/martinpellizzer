Syngonium llamasii

Syngonium llamasii, commonly known as the arrowhead vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, variegated leaves that change color with age, often transitioning from light green to reddish-brown. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth. Its rapid growth habit makes it ideal for hanging baskets or as a climbing plant in greenhouses.

Syngonium llamasii is also popular for its ornamental value and air-purifying qualities.
