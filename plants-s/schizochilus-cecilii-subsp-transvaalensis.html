Schizochilus cecilii subsp. transvaalensis

Schizochilus cecilii subsp.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in areas with high humidity and partial shade. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinctive lip that is often darker and more prominent.
