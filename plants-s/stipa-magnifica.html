Stipa magnifica

Stipa magnifica, commonly known as the magnificent feathergrass, is a species of grass native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and feathery plumes that give it a delicate, elegant appearance. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in sunny, open environments. Its foliage is often silver-blue in color, adding a striking visual contrast to its surroundings.

Stipa magnifica is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in rocky or arid conditions.
