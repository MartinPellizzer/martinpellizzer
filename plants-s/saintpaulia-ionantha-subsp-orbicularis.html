Saintpaulia ionantha subsp. orbicularis

Saintpaulia ionantha subsp.

orbicularis, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa. It is characterized by its compact, velvety foliage and clusters of showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors.

The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a waxy texture, which helps reduce water loss. This subspecies is prized for its ornamental value and is widely cultivated as a houseplant.

Its botanical features make it well-suited for indoor growing conditions.
