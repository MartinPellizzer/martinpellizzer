Senecio apulus

Senecio apulus, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and thistles. The plant is characterized by its showy yellow flowers surrounded by white ray florets, which bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Senecio apulus is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
