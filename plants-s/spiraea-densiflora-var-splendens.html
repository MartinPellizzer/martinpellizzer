Spiraea densiflora var. splendens

Spiraea densiflora var. splendens, commonly known as the showy spiraea, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features dense clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has a compact, mounding growth habit with dark green, serrated leaves. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden borders or foundation plantings.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its showy flowers and attractive foliage throughout the growing season.
