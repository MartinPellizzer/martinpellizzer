Sulipa

Sulipa, commonly known as the African violet, is a popular ornamental plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the genus Saintpaulia and is characterized by its velvety, tubular leaves and showy, clustered flowers. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Sulipa is often cultivated for its vibrant colors, which range from purple and pink to white and blue.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for both indoor and outdoor gardens.
