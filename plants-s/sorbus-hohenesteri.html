Sorbus hohenesteri

Sorbus hohenesteri, commonly known as the Hohenester's whitebeam, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of central and eastern Europe, particularly in the Alps. The plant is characterized by its small, white flowers that appear in clusters, followed by small, red berries that ripen in the fall. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with a smooth margin and a slightly serrated edge.

Sorbus hohenesteri is often found in rocky or forested areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance.
