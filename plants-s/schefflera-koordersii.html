Schefflera koordersii

Schefflera koordersii, commonly known as the Koorders' umbrella plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of large, leathery, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage.

Schefflera koordersii is valued for its ornamental appearance and adaptability to indoor growing conditions.
