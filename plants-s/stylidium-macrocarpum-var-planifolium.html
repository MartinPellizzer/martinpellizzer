Stylidium macrocarpum var. planifolium

Stylidium macrocarpum var. planifolium, commonly known as the flat-leaf spurred orchid, is a member of the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is native to Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive spurred flowers, which are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant has a short, erect stem with a few leaves that are broad and flat, giving it its common name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

This variety is noted for its compact growth habit and is a popular species among orchid enthusiasts.
