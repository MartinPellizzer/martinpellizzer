Sulcorebutia canigueralii

Sulcorebutia canigueralii, commonly known as the Canary Island rock rose, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to the Canary Islands.

It features dense, woolly spines and rounded, ribbed stems that store water efficiently. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
