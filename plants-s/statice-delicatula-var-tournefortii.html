Statice delicatula var. tournefortii

Statice delicatula var. tournefortii, commonly known as the velvetblue statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, delicate blue to violet flowers. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

This variety is valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.
