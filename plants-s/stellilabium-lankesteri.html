Stellilabium lankesteri

Stellilabium lankesteri, commonly known as the star-labium orchid, is a unique flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Colombia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped floral structure. The plant produces small, fragrant blooms that are typically pale yellow or greenish in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, adapted to the cool, moist conditions of its natural habitat.

Stellilabium lankesteri is a rare and endangered species, making it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
