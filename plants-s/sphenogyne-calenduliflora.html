Sphenogyne calenduliflora

Sphenogyne calenduliflora, commonly known as the golden star lily, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has narrow, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, white down. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Sphenogyne calenduliflora is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
