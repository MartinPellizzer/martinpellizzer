Stelis umbriae

Stelis umbriae, commonly known as the Umbrian spider orchid, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, intricate flowers that often feature a striking combination of colors. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are usually arranged in a rosette pattern and are covered in fine, soft hairs.

Stelis umbriae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
