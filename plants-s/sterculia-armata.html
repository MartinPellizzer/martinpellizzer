Sterculia armata

Sterculia armata, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its dense, heavy wood that is highly valued for its durability. The tree is adapted to dry conditions and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.
