Sideritis spathulata

Sideritis spathulata, commonly known as broad-leafed ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its hairy stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. Its small, pale blue to purple flowers bloom in clusters during the summer months.

Sideritis spathulata is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is valued for its medicinal properties.
