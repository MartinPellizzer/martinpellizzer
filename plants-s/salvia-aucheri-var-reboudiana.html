Salvia aucheri var. reboudiana

Salvia aucheri var. reboudiana, commonly known as the Rebound sage, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It features upright, slender stems that bear opposite pairs of lance-shaped, gray-green leaves with a fuzzy texture. The plant produces clusters of violet-blue, tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its aromatic foliage and long-lasting blooms.
