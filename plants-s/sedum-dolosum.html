Sedum dolosum

Sedum dolosum, commonly known as the deceptive sedum, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, allowing it to store water efficiently. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its common name reflects its ability to mimic other plants, making it difficult to identify without close examination.
