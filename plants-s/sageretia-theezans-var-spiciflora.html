Sageretia theezans var. spiciflora

Sageretia theezans var. spiciflora, commonly known as the Chinese hibiscus or the Chinese spider flower, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate with a slightly hairy surface.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.
