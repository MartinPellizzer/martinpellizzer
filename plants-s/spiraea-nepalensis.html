Spiraea nepalensis

Spiraea nepalensis, commonly known as the Nepal spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, white to pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are opposite in arrangement and often have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Spiraea nepalensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
