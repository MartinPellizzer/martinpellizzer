Streblosa glabrata

Streblosa glabrata, commonly known as the smoothleaf streblosa, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to specific regions of southern Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or along stream banks. The plant features slender, smooth-stemmed leaves that are often arranged alternately along the stem. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are usually greenish-white in color and are borne in clusters.

Streblosa glabrata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate foliage.
