Statice frondosa

Statice frondosa, commonly known as sea lavender, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, showy blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that help it withstand salty air and strong winds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dunes and saline environments.

Statice frondosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly used in floral arrangements.
