Sonchus sventenii

Sonchus sventenii, commonly known as the African dandelion, is a flowering plant native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and milky sap. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in grasslands and open woodlands. It produces yellow flowers that develop into prickly, spherical seed heads, which are dispersed by the wind.

The species is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
