Stelis reptans

Stelis reptans, commonly known as the creeping stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has slender, creeping stems that produce small, greenish or white flowers. Its roots are fibrous and adapted for anchoring in loose soil.

Stelis reptans is characterized by its delicate foliage and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
