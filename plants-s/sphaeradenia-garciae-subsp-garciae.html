Sphaeradenia garciae subsp. garciae

Sphaeradenia garciae subsp.

garciae, commonly known as the Garcia's sphaeradenia, is a member of the Araceae family. It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and distinctive, heart-shaped leaves.

The plant produces inflorescences consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe, a typical feature of the Araceae family. It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
