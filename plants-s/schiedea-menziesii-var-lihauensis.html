Schiedea menziesii var. lihauensis

Schiedea menziesii var. lihauensis, commonly known as the Lihauensis seepweed, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its succulent, stem-like growth and small, clustered flowers. This variant is typically found in moist, shaded environments, often near seepages or on rocky slopes. Its leaves are generally fleshy and may exhibit a bluish or purplish hue.
