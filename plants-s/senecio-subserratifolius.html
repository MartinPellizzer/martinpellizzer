Senecio subserratifolius

Senecio subserratifolius, commonly known as the serratedleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its fleshy, gray-green leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in dense, branched clusters. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
