Solidago heterotricha

Solidago heterotricha, commonly known as the rough goldenrod, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes and roadside ditches. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, giving it a rough texture.

Solidago heterotricha plays an important role in supporting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
