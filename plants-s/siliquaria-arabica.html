Siliquaria arabica

Siliquaria arabica, commonly known as the Arabian siliquaria, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the Middle East.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant produces small, elongated pods called siliques, which are characteristic of the family. It has small, narrow leaves and white or pale yellow flowers.

Siliquaria arabica is adapted to survive in dry climates, with deep roots and a waxy coating on its leaves to reduce water loss.
