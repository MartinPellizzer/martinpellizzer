Spathelia yumuriensis

Spathelia yumuriensis, commonly known as the Yumuriensis orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually violet or purple in color, with a distinctive spathe-like structure that encloses the actual flowers.

Spathelia yumuriensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
