Spatalla cylindrica

Spatalla cylindrica, commonly known as the cylindrical spathiphyllum, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, narrow leaves and a striking, elongated spadix surrounded by a spathe. Native to tropical regions, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its botanical features include a rhizomatous root system and a unique reproductive structure known as a synandrium.

Spatalla cylindrica is often cultivated for its ornamental value and air-purifying properties.
