Sophia brachycarpon

Sophia brachycarpon, commonly known as the short-stemmed sophia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its small, erect growth habit and trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It is native to certain regions of southern Africa and thrives in well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and natural habitats.
