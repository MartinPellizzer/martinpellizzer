Silene venosa proles maritima

Silene venosa proles maritima, commonly known as the maritime bladder campion, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions.

It is characterized by its pink to purple flowers and hairy, succulent stems that help it retain moisture in salty, windy environments. Silene venosa proles maritima is adapted to harsh maritime conditions, making it a resilient and hardy species.
