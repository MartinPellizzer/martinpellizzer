Senecio segethi

Senecio segethi, commonly known as the field senecio, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and hairy leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish to brownish flowers arranged in composite inflorescences. It typically grows in grassy areas and is often found in disturbed soils.

Senecio segethi is a hardy species that thrives in temperate climates and is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
