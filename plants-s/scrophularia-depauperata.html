Scrophularia depauperata

Scrophularia depauperata, commonly known as the poor man's scrophularia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a member of the Scrophulariaceae family and typically grows in dry, rocky soils. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.

Scrophularia depauperata is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
