Saussurea likiangensis

Saussurea likiangensis, commonly known as the Yunnan gentian, is a perennial herb native to the high-altitude regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems and large, showy blue or purple flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant has fibrous roots and is adapted to cold, arid environments, often growing in alpine meadows. Its flowers are typically visited by pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Saussurea likiangensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
