Scabiosa lucida f. subalpina

Scabiosa lucida f. subalpina, commonly known as the Alpine Scabious, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caprifoliaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters, often appearing in late spring to early summer. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are typically hairy, providing a distinctive texture. Native to the alpine regions of Europe, it thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to cold climates.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine arrangements.
