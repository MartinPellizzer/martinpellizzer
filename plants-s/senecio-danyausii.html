Senecio danyausii

Senecio danyausii, commonly known as the Danyausii groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. It features narrow, linear leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
