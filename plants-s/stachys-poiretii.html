Stachys poiretii

Stachys poiretii, commonly known as the French hedge nettle, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It features upright, branched stems covered in fine hairs and produces clusters of small, pinkish to purple flowers. The plant has serrated, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy on both sides. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry grasslands or along roadsides.

Stachys poiretii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
