Selaginella kriegeriana

Selaginella kriegeriana, commonly known as the Krieger's spikemoss, is a species of spore-bearing plant belonging to the family Selaginellaceae.

It is a low-growing, evergreen plant that typically forms dense mats or cushions on rocky or sandy soils. The plant has small, scale-like leaves that are arranged in whorls around the stems, giving it a distinctive appearance. It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa, where it thrives in well-drained, mineral-rich soils.

Selaginella kriegeriana is notable for its ability to survive in harsh environments, making it an important component of desert ecosystems.
