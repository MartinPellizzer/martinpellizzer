Senecio caledoniae

Senecio caledoniae, commonly known as the Caledonia groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Senecio caledoniae is often found in fynbos vegetation, contributing to the biodiversity of its native habitat.
