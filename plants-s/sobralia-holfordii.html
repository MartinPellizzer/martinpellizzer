Sobralia holfordii

Sobralia holfordii, commonly known as the Holford's sobralia, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant typically features vibrant orange or red flowers with a distinctive, elongated shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Sobralia holfordii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
