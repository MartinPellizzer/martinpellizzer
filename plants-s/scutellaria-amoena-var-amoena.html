Scutellaria amoena var. amoena

Scutellaria amoena var. amoena, commonly known as the blue skullcap, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 12 to 24 inches. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

Botanically, it is characterized by its square stems and distinctive two-lipped flowers.
