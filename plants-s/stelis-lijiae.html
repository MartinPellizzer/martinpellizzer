Stelis lijiae

Stelis lijiae, commonly known as the Lijia orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features a pseudobulb structure, which stores water and nutrients, and produces clustered, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette pattern, facilitating efficient light capture.

Stelis lijiae thrives in humid environments with high air circulation and partial shade.
