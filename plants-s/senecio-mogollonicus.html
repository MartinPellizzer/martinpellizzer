Senecio mogollonicus

Senecio mogollonicus, commonly known as the Mogollon goldenweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Senecio mogollonicus is adapted to drought conditions and plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native habitat.
