Schizachyrium delavayi

Schizachyrium delavayi, commonly known as Delavay's switchgrass, is a perennial grass native to China.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, blade-like leaves. The plant produces panicles of small, spikelet-like flowers that are typically golden to brown in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or grassy slopes.

Schizachyrium delavayi is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native plant landscapes.
