Stauntonia hexaphylla

Stauntonia hexaphylla, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual display. The plant has compound leaves with multiple leaflets, and its bark is smooth and reddish in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Stauntonia hexaphylla is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
