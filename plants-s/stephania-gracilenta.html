Stephania gracilenta

Stephania gracilenta, commonly known as slender stephania, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Menispermaceae and is characterized by its slender, twining stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by distinctive, narrow, winged fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal potential, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
