Scutellaria monterreyana

Scutellaria monterreyana, commonly known as Monterrey blueweed, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular blue or purple flowers. It typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showing adaptations to arid environments.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in ecological and horticultural contexts.
