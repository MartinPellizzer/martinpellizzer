Silene ionica

Silene ionica, commonly known as the Ionian campion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features showy pink or red flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

Silene ionica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
