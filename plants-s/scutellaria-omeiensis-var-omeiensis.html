Scutellaria omeiensis var. omeiensis

Scutellaria omeiensis var. omeiensis, commonly known as the Omei mountain skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Sichuan, China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and aromatic foliage. The plant typically grows in rocky or forested habitats at high altitudes. Its flowers are small, tubular, and usually blue or violet in color, arranged in whorled clusters.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses in local herbal practices.
