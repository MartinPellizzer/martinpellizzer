Solanum naucinum

Solanum naucinum, commonly known as the purple nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the nightshade family (Solanaceae).

It is a herbaceous annual or biennial plant native to southern Europe and western Asia. The plant produces small, violet to purple flowers that are typically clustered in umbels. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with a rough, hairy texture.

The fruit is a small, globose berry that turns from green to red as it matures.
