Stenorrhynchos exaltatum

Stenorrhynchos exaltatum, commonly known as the tall spike orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and forest margins. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, with a distinctive spurred lip that aids in pollination by insects.

Stenorrhynchos exaltatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
