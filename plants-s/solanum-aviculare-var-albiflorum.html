Solanum aviculare var. albiflorum

Solanum aviculare var. albiflorum, commonly known as the white-flowered nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other notable plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant features small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, often at the base of the leaves. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with a slightly hairy texture.

It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and ease of cultivation.
