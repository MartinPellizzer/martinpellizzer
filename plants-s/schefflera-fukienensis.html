Schefflera fukienensis

Schefflera fukienensis, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a tropical plant native to China and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-shaped clusters of dark green, leathery leaves. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters in its natural habitat. Its foliage provides a lush, tropical appearance, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.

Schefflera fukienensis is also valued for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its relatively low maintenance requirements.
