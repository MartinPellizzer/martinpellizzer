Synnotia bicolor

Synnotia bicolor, commonly known as the two-colored daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking white and pink petals that form a distinctive daisy-like flower. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun conditions. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Synnotia bicolor is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
