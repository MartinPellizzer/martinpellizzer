Silene nodulosa var. peduncularis

Silene nodulosa var. peduncularis, commonly known as the clustered catchfly, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its pink or purple flowers that bloom in summer. Silene nodulosa var. peduncularis is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
