Streptocarpus integrifolius

Streptocarpus integrifolius, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is known for its compact growth habit. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Streptocarpus integrifolius is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
