Spiranthes decora

Spiranthes decora, commonly known as the decorated spire orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its delicate, spiraling arrangement of flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wet meadows. Its leaves are grass-like and linear, while its flowers are small, white or pale pink, and fragrant.

It plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and is often found in prairie and wetland ecosystems.
