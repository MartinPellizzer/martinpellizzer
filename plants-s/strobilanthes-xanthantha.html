Strobilanthes xanthantha

Strobilanthes xanthantha, commonly known as the golden flame or yellow flame plant, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

Strobilanthes xanthantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
