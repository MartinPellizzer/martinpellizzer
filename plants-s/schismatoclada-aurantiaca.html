Schismatoclada aurantiaca

Schismatoclada aurantiaca, commonly known as the red-flowered ice plant, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, ribbed stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to orange-red. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems. It produces small, rounded fruits that contain seeds dispersed by wind.

Schismatoclada aurantiaca is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
