Statice nogalensis

Statice nogalensis, commonly known as the Canary Islands statice, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically produces vibrant pink to red flowers, which bloom in summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in fine hairs.

Statice nogalensis is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and rocky habitats.
