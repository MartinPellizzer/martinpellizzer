Syzygium glomeruliferum

It is native to northern Australia, particularly Queensland and the Northern Territory, and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. Syzygium glomeruliferum is often found in rainforest and coastal habitats, where it plays a role in supporting local biodiversity.
