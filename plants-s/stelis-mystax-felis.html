Stelis mystax-felis

Stelis mystax-felis, commonly known as the cat's whiskers orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its roots are fibrous and adapted for nutrient absorption from the surrounding soil.

Stelis mystax-felis is known for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
