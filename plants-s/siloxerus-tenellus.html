Siloxerus tenellus

Siloxerus tenellus, commonly known as the slender silversword, is a rare and unique plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rosette of narrow, silver-green leaves. The plant produces a single, tall flower stalk that bears small, yellowish flowers. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to the dry, sunny conditions of its native habitat.

Due to its limited distribution and threats from habitat loss, Siloxerus tenellus is considered endangered.
