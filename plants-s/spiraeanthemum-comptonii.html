Spiraeanthemum comptonii

Spiraeanthemum comptonii, commonly known as the Compton's spirea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded shrub form and its clusters of small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant features narrow, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long-lasting floral display.
