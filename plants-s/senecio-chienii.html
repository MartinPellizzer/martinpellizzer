Senecio chienii

Senecio chienii, commonly known as the Chinese groundsel, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Senecio chienii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
