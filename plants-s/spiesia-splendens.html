Spiesia splendens

Spiesia splendens, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the rainy season. The plant has compound leaves with long, narrow leaflets and produces small, round fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Spiesia splendens is also valued for its ability to provide shade and support local wildlife.
