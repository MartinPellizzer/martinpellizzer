Sebastiania Integra

Sebastiania integra is a striking evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Central and South America. It is known for its vibrant, showy yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves that provide a lush backdrop for its bright blossoms. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens. Its unique appearance and easy care requirements have made it a favorite among plant enthusiasts.
