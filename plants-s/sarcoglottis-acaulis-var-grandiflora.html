Sarcoglottis acaulis var. grandiflora

Sarcoglottis acaulis var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered sarcoglottis, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, upright growth habit with a short, often woody stem and lacks a true rhizome. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, arranged in dense, terminal clusters. Its leaves are alternate, ovate to lanceolate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded forest environments.
