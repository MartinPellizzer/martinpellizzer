Silene montbretiana

Silene montbretiana, commonly known as the Montbretian catchfly, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

Silene montbretiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
