Specklinia duplooyi

Specklinia duplooyi, commonly known as the Pearl Orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, clustered inflorescences that bear small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are usually thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native humid environment.

Specklinia duplooyi is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
