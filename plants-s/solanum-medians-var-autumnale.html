Solanum medians var. autumnale

Solanum medians var. autumnale, commonly known as the autumn bellflower, is a variant of the Solanum medians species.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Asia, particularly China. The plant features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often appearing in clusters. Its foliage is typically broad and lobed, with a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
