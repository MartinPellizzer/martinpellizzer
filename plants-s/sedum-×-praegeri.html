Sedum × praegeri

Sedum × praegeri, commonly known as the Jade Plant or Crassula ovata, is a hybrid succulent plant that belongs to the Crassulaceae family.

It is characterized by its fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a bluish or reddish tinge, depending on light conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, or white during the spring or early summer. It is native to South Africa and is widely cultivated as a popular houseplant due to its low maintenance and aesthetic appeal.

Sedum × praegeri thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.
