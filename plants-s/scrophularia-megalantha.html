Scrophularia megalantha

Scrophularia megalantha, commonly known as the giant scrophularia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features tall, erect stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height, covered with lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are clustered in spikes and are usually pink or purple, with five petals and a distinctive spurred structure.

Scrophularia megalantha is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
