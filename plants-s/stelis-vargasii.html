Stelis vargasii

Stelis vargasii, commonly known as the Vargas orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, epiphytic growth habit. The plant typically produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Stelis vargasii is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
