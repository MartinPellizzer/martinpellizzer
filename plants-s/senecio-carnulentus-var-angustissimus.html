Senecio carnulentus var. angustissimus

Senecio carnulentus var. angustissimus, commonly known as narrowleaf Senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and ragworts.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
