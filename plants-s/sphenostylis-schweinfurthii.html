Sphenostylis schweinfurthii

Sphenostylis schweinfurthii, commonly known as the African star grass, is a perennial herb native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its erect, leafy stems and produces small, white or pink flowers arranged in dense clusters. The plant has fibrous roots and is often found in moist, well-drained soils in open woodlands and grasslands. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses.

The species is also known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
