Stenopetalum nutans

Stenopetalum nutans, commonly known as the nodding stenopetalum, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered inflorescences. The plant produces white to pale pink flowers that droop downward, giving it its common name. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a smooth upper surface and a hairy underside.

Stenopetalum nutans is often found in dry, open habitats and is adapted to withstand arid conditions.
