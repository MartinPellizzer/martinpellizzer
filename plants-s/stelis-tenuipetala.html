Stelis tenuipetala

Stelis tenuipetala, commonly known as the slender-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, narrow leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on forest floors or along stream banks. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the soil, while its flowering stems produce clusters of white or pale yellow blooms.

Stelis tenuipetala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
