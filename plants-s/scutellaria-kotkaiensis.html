Scutellaria kotkaiensis

Scutellaria kotkaiensis, commonly known as the Kotkai skullcap, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and distinctive two-lipped flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky areas.

Scutellaria kotkaiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for potential medicinal properties.
