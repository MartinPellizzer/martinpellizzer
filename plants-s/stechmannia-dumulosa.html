Stechmannia dumulosa

Stechmannia dumulosa, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety pubescence. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid lowland forests.

Stechmannia dumulosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been cultivated for its unique foliage.
