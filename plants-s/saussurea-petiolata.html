Saussurea petiolata

Saussurea petiolata, commonly known as the Chinese yarrow, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stem and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically hairy and arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in cool, arid environments and is often found at high elevations.

Saussurea petiolata is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
