Stromanthe sanguinea

Stromanthe sanguinea, commonly known as the Red-veined Stromanthe, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that feature deep red veins against a glossy green background. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal in indoor gardening. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Stromanthe sanguinea is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced plant enthusiasts.
