Sageretia Cordiformis

Heartleaf sageretia, scientifically known as Sageretia cordiformis, is a unique and striking plant native to the southeastern United States. Its heart-shaped leaves and delicate appearance make it a standout species in the Asteraceae family. This plant thrives in moist, shady environments, often found in woodlands and along stream banks. With its elegant foliage and subtle floral display, it adds a touch of natural beauty to its habitat. Despite its charm, it remains relatively obscure in horticultural circles, making it a rare gem for plant enthusiasts.
