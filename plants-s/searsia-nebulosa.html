Searsia nebulosa

Searsia nebulosa, commonly known as the cloud tree, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rutaceae.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its distinctive, white, waxy flowers that appear in clusters. The cloud tree is also valued for its ecological role, providing habitat and food for various insects and birds.
