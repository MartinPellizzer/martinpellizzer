Sitilias grandiflora

Sitilias grandiflora, commonly known as the giant flameflower, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 inches in diameter, typically displaying vibrant red or orange hues. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot. Its leaves are broad and glossy, often with a slightly waxy texture.

Sitilias grandiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
