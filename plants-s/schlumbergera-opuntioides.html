Schlumbergera opuntioides

Schlumbergera opuntioides, commonly known as the holiday cactus, is a species native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its segmented, flat stems that resemble leaves. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically blooming during the winter months. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Schlumbergera opuntioides is a popular ornamental plant due to its long-lasting blooms and ease of care.
