Satureja alpina var. purpurascens

Satureja alpina var. purpurascens, commonly known as alpine savory, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its small, aromatic leaves and clusters of pale pink to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils and full sun. Its leaves contain essential oils that contribute to its distinctive scent and medicinal properties.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal remedies.
