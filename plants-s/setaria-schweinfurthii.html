Setaria schweinfurthii

Setaria schweinfurthii, commonly known as the African foxtail, is a perennial grass native to sub-Saharan Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted clumps of slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces distinctive spikelets that are often tinged with red or brown, giving it a striking appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping and erosion control.

Setaria schweinfurthii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract wildlife.
