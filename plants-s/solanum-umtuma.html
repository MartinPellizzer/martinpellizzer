Solanum umtuma

Solanum umtuma, commonly known as the Umtuma tomato, is a species native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant belonging to the nightshade family, Solanaceae. The plant produces small, round, red berries that are edible and often used in local cuisines. Its leaves are alternate and ovate, with a slightly hairy texture.

Solanum umtuma is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient crop in regions with limited water resources.
