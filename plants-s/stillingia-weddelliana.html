Stillingia weddelliana

Stillingia weddelliana, commonly known as Weddell's stellaria, is a flowering plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It is a low-growing herb with small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has opposite, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, nutrient-poor soils and is often found in tundra and alpine environments.

Stillingia weddelliana plays a role in stabilizing soil in its harsh habitats and supports local pollinators.
