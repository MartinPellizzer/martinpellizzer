Sataria linearis

Sataria linearis, commonly known as the slender sataria, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as grasslands and wetlands. It is often found in regions with warm, humid climates.

Due to its delicate appearance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its ornamental value.
