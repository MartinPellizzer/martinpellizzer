Scabiosa arvensis var. pinnatifida

Scabiosa arvensis var. pinnatifida, commonly known as the slender scabious, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry meadows and grasslands. The plant features delicate, pink or purple flowers arranged in spherical clusters, which bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are pinnately lobed, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is valued in gardens for its ornamental value and its role in supporting pollinators.
