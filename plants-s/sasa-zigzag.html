Sasa zigzag

Sasa zigzag, commonly known as zigzag bamboo, is a perennial grass native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, zigzagging stems that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a bluish-green hue.

Sasa zigzag is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and traditional crafts.
