Sulcorebutia callichroma

Sulcorebutia callichroma, commonly known as the red-tipped crassula, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that are often tinged with red or purple at the tips, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. Its leaves are thick and waxy, adapted to store water in arid environments.

It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact size and drought tolerance.
