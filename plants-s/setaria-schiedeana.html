Setaria schiedeana

Setaria schiedeana, commonly known as slender false panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific.

It is a grass species belonging to the Poaceae family, characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets, typical of many grasses. Setaria schiedeana is sometimes considered a weed but has been studied for its potential in ecological restoration and as a model species in plant biology.
