Solanum rupestre

Solanum rupestre, commonly known as the rock nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to violet, often blooming in spring. Its leaves are generally hairy and grayish-green, which helps reduce water loss.

Solanum rupestre is part of the nightshade family, which includes many other plants with medicinal and toxic properties.
