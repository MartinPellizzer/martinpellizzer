Stewartia

Stewartia, commonly known as the pagoda tree, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Theaceae.

It is native to eastern Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea, and is known for its ornamental value. The plants typically have alternate, simple, and ovate to elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green.
