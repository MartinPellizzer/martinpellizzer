Stelis sodiroi

Stelis sodiroi, commonly known as the Sodiro's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and typically produces small, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is often found growing on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are epiphytic, meaning they attach to other plants without parasitizing them.

The flowers of Stelis sodiroi are often fragrant and attract various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
