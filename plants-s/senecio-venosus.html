Senecio venosus

Senecio venosus, commonly known as the veiny groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and ragworts. The plant features slender, branching stems and small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters. Its leaves are typically narrow and have prominent veins, giving it its common name.
