Stelis pauciflora

Stelis pauciflora, commonly known as the few-flowered orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often found in forests or along stream banks. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a few small, green leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green or white, with a delicate, spurred lip.

Stelis pauciflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
