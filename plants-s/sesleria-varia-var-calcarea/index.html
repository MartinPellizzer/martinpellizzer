Sesleria Varia Var. Calcarea

Calcarea sesleria, scientifically known as Sesleria varia var. calcarea, is a rare and lesser-known member of the grass family. Native to the mountainous regions of Europe, it thrives in cool, moist environments with well-drained soil. Its delicate, grass-like appearance belies its ecological importance as a habitat for specialized invertebrates. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated and often overlooked in botanical discussions. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this unique species from habitat loss and climate change.
