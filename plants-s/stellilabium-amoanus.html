Stellilabium amoanus

Stellilabium amoanus, commonly known as the star-labium orchid, is a rare flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories where it relies on specific mycorrhizal fungi for nutrient uptake.
