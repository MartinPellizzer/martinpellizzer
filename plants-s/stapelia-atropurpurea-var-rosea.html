Stapelia atropurpurea var. rosea

Stapelia atropurpurea var. rosea, commonly known as the purple star stapelia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed stems and distinctive, star-shaped flowers that range from purple to pink in color. The plant produces its flowers in late spring to early summer, often in clusters along the stems. Its leaves are typically short and cylindrical, adapted for water storage in arid environments.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
