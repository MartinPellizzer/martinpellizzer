Sulcorebutia nigrofuscata

Sulcorebutia nigrofuscata, commonly known as the Blackish Pincushion Cactus, is a species of flowering cactus native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and dense clusters of spines. The plant produces bright yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking contrast to its dark green foliage. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
