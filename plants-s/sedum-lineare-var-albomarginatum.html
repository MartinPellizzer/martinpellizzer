Sedum lineare var. albomarginatum

Sedum lineare var. albomarginatum, commonly known as the white-margin stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features fleshy, linear leaves that are typically green with distinct white or pale margins. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal contribute to its use in both ornamental horticulture and ecological landscaping.
