Sprekelia heisteri

Sprekelia heisteri, commonly known as the yellow bell, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has strap-like, grassy leaves that are dark green and often variegated with silver markings. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Sprekelia heisteri is known for its resilience and ability to naturalize over time in suitable climates.
