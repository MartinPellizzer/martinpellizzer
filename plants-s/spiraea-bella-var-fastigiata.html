Spiraea bella var. fastigiata

Spiraea bella var. fastigiata, commonly known as the compact spiraea or upright spiraea, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to the eastern United States.

It features narrow, upright growth habit with small, white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy, providing an attractive appearance throughout the growing season. It is well-suited for small garden spaces and can be used as a border plant or in containers.

Spiraea bella var. fastigiata is relatively low maintenance and resistant to most pests and diseases.
