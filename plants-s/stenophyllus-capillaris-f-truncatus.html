Stenophyllus capillaris f. truncatus

Stenophyllus capillaris f. truncatus, commonly known as the truncate stenophyllus, is a rare and specialized plant species characterized by its unique leaf morphology.

Its leaves are typically narrow and linear, with a distinctive truncate or notched tip, which sets it apart from other members of the genus. The plant exhibits a low-growing habit, often forming dense mats on rocky or gravelly substrates. It is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments, showing a high degree of drought tolerance.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
