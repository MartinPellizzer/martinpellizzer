Sobralia liliastrum f. maior

Sobralia liliastrum f. maior, commonly known as the giant lily orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that resemble lilies, with a pinkish-white coloration and a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and can grow up to several feet in height, making it a striking addition to gardens. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil and partial shade.

This ornamental plant is highly valued for its beauty and is often cultivated for its dramatic floral display.
