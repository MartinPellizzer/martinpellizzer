Stipularia rotundifolia

Stipularia rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leaf stipularia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features opposite, round or ovate leaves that are often hairy, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a clustered head, which attract various pollinators.

Stipularia rotundifolia typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
