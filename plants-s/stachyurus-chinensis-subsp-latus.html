Stachyurus chinensis subsp. latus

Stachyurus chinensis subsp.

latus, commonly known as the broadleaf stachyurus, is a deciduous shrub native to China. It is characterized by its showy, pendulous clusters of white or pale yellow flowers that appear in early spring.

The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. It prefers partial shade to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for ornamental landscaping.

Its compact growth habit and seasonal blooms make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a low-maintenance, decorative shrub.
