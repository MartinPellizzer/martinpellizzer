Sinningia kautskyi

Sinningia kautskyi, commonly known as the Kautsky's gesnerian, is a species of flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically produces violet or purple flowers with a distinctive yellow or orange throat. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Sinningia kautskyi is a popular choice among succulent and tropical plant enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
