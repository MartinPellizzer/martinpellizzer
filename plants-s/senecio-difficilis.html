Senecio difficilis

Senecio difficilis, commonly known as the hard-to-grow senecio, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is known for its difficulty in cultivation, requiring specific conditions to thrive. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
