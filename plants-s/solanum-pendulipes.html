Solanum pendulipes

Solanum pendulipes, commonly known as the hanging nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually purple or violet in color. Its fruit is a yellowish, fleshy berry that contains several seeds.

Like other species in the Solanaceae family, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
