Stellaria monosperma f. paniculata

Stellaria monosperma f. paniculata, commonly known as the paniculate starwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit and produces small, white or pinkish flowers arranged in a paniculate inflorescence. The plant typically thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs.

The species is notable for its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
