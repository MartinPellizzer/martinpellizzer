Specklinia Unicornis

Specklinia unicornis is a rare and striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. Known for its unique, single-spiked inflorescence and vibrant, colorful flowers, it stands out among other orchid species. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific care to flourish. Its name, meaning "unicorn-like," reflects its unusual appearance and mythical allure. Due to habitat loss, Specklinia unicornis is now considered endangered, making it a subject of conservation interest.
