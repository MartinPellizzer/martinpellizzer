Senecio elamutensis

Senecio elamutensis, commonly known as the Elamutensis groundsels, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, showcasing adaptations to harsh environmental conditions. It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small yellow flowers arranged in clusters.
