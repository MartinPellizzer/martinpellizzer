Sedum polytrichoides

Sedum polytrichoides, commonly known as the mossy stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to rocky and arid environments.

It features fleshy, upright stems that are often covered in a dense layer of moss-like growth, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It is adapted to thrive in poor soil conditions and requires minimal water, making it drought-tolerant.

Its ability to store water in its leaves helps it survive in harsh, sun-exposed habitats.
