Salvia × telekiana

Salvia × telekiana, commonly known as the flame violet, is a hybrid perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking violet to red flowers that bloom in dense clusters during the summer months. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and features narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its botanical name reflects its hybrid origin, combining species from the Salvia genus.
