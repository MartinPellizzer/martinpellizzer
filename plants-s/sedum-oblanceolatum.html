Sedum oblanceolatum

Sedum oblanceolatum, commonly known as the broadleaf stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for landscaping in arid climates.
