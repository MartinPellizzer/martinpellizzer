Scutellaria rubicunda subsp. brevibracteata

Scutellaria rubicunda subsp.

brevibracteata, commonly known as the short-bracted blue skullcap, is a member of the Lamiaceae family. It is a perennial herb characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves.

The plant produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. It is native to the western United States, particularly in California and Oregon.

Its habitat includes moist meadows and woodlands, where it thrives in partial shade.
