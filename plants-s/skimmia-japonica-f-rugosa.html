Skimmia japonica f. rugosa

Skimmia japonica f. rugosa, commonly known as the Japanese skimmia variegated form, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with distinctive white or yellow variegation, adding visual interest to gardens. The plant produces small, fragrant, white flowers in early spring, followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens or as a container plant.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its slow growth and ability to tolerate frost.
