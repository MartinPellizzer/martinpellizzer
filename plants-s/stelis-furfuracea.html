Stelis furfuracea

Stelis furfuracea, commonly known as the hairy stelis, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, hairy appearance on its stems and leaves. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pale yellow.

Stelis furfuracea is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
