Steleocodon gracilis

Steleocodon gracilis, commonly known as the slender tamarisk, is a species of flowering plant in the family Tamaricaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, scale-like leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in dense spikes. It is typically found in arid and semi-arid regions, often growing in sandy or saline soils.
