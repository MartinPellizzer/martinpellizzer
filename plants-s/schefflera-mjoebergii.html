Schefflera mjoebergii

Schefflera mjoebergii, commonly known as the African umbrella plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, palm-like leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, white flowers arranged in umbel-like clusters, which are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Schefflera mjoebergii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
