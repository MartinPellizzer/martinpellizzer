Schismatoclada psychotrioides

Schismatoclada psychotrioides, commonly known as the African milk bush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and hairy, aromatic leaves. The plant produces a milky sap that is often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in arid and semi-arid regions, adapting well to poor soil conditions.

Its unique morphology and chemical composition make it an interesting subject for botanical and pharmacological research.
