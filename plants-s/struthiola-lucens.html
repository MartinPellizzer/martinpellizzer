Struthiola lucens

Struthiola lucens, commonly known as the shining struthiola, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form and produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers. The plant has silvery-green foliage that helps it conserve water in its harsh environment. Struthiola lucens is adapted to full sun and well-drained soils, making it a hardy choice for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a popular ornamental plant in drought-tolerant gardens.
