Stevia elatior var. dissoluta

Stevia elatior var. dissoluta, commonly known as the dwarf stevia, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its small, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant is noted for its high concentration of steviol glycosides, which are natural sweeteners. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for both ornamental and medicinal use.

Its compact growth habit makes it an excellent choice for container gardening and small garden spaces.
