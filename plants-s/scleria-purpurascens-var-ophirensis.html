Scleria purpurascens var. ophirensis

Scleria purpurascens var. ophirensis, commonly known as the Purple-eyed Scleria, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its distinctive purple-tinged flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in grassland or woodland habitats. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of spikelets.
