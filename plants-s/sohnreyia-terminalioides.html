Sohnreyia terminalioides

Sohnreyia terminalioides, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters during the rainy season. The plant has compound leaves with numerous small leaflets and produces woody pods that contain seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it a striking addition to gardens and landscapes.
