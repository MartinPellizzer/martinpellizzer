Syncolostemon floccosus

Syncolostemon floccosus, commonly known as the fluffball morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, fluffy seed pods that resemble small clouds, giving it its common name. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Syncolostemon floccosus is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its role in traditional medicine.
