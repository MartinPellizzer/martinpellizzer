Solanum nigrum f. uniflorum

Solanum nigrum f. uniflorum, commonly known as the single-flowered black nightshade, is a variant of the common black nightshade.

It is a herbaceous annual plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant features small, dark purple to black berries that are typically toxic to humans and animals. Its leaves are ovate with a slightly hairy texture, and it produces a single flower per cluster.

This form is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its distinctive appearance.
