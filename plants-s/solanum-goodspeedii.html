Solanum goodspeedii

Solanum goodspeedii, commonly known as the Goodspeed's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or arid environments. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to violet, often blooming at night. Its fruits are berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

Solanum goodspeedii belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae), which includes other plants like tomatoes and peppers.
