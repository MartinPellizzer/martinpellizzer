Scutellaria glabriuscula subsp. laxa

Scutellaria glabriuscula subsp.

laxa, commonly known as the slender skullcap, is a flowering plant native to parts of North America. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves.

The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. It produces small, tubular blue or violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
