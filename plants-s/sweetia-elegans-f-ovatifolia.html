Sweetia elegans f. ovatifolia

Sweetia elegans f. ovatifolia, commonly known as the ovate-leaf sweetia, is a rare and unique variant of the sweetia plant.

It is characterized by its ovate-shaped leaves, which are broader and more elongated compared to the typical form. This fern-like plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its foliage displays a striking combination of deep green and silver hues, making it an ornamental favorite among plant enthusiasts.

Due to its limited distribution and specific growing requirements, it is considered a challenging plant to cultivate.
