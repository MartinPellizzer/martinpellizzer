Stellaria miahuatlana

Stellaria miahuatlana, commonly known as the Mexican starwort, is a flowering plant native to high-altitude regions of Mexico.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, star-shaped white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in cool, temperate climates. Its leaves are usually narrow and arranged in a basal rosette, while its stems are slender and often branched.

Stellaria miahuatlana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
