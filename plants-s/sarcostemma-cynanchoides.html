Sarcostemma cynanchoides

Sarcostemma cynanchoides, commonly known as the climbing milkweed or devil's milkweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.
