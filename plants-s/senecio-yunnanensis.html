Senecio yunnanensis

Senecio yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan Senecio, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in alpine or subalpine regions, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Senecio yunnanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
