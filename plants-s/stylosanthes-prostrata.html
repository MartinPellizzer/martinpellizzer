Stylosanthes prostrata

Stylosanthes prostrata, commonly known as prostrate stylo, is a low-growing perennial legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features prostrate or semi-prostrate growth habit with trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a valuable forage species. It has a deep root system that enhances soil stability and nitrogen fixation.

Stylosanthes prostrata is often used in pasture mixes and land rehabilitation due to its resilience and nutritional value.
