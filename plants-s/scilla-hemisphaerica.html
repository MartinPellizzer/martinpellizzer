Scilla hemisphaerica

Scilla hemisphaerica, commonly known as the hemispherical scilla, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It produces clusters of delicate, bell-shaped blue or purple flowers in early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge from a basal rosette of bulbs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Scilla hemisphaerica is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and early blooming habit.
