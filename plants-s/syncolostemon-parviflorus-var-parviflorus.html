Syncolostemon parviflorus var. parviflorus

Syncolostemon parviflorus var. parviflorus, commonly known as the small-flowered syncolostemon, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces delicate, tubular flowers that are typically pale yellow to orange in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in horticulture for its unique floral display.
