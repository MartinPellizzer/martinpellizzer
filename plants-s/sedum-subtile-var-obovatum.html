Sedum subtile var. obovatum

Sedum subtile var. obovatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features small, fleshy, obovate leaves that are typically blue-green in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange, often blooming in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking hardy, ornamental plants.
