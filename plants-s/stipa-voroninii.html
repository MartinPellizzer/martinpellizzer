Stipa voroninii

Stipa voroninii, commonly known as the Voronin's feathergrass, is a species of grass native to Central Asia.

It is a perennial plant that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant has slender, cylindrical leaves and produces fluffy, panicle-like inflorescences. It is characterized by its fine, hair-like leaves and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.

Stipa voroninii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in harsh conditions.
