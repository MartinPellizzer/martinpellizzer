Stylidium neurophyllum

Stylidium neurophyllum, commonly known as the nervous sun-orchid, is a small, herbaceous plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, sun-like flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils in arid or semi-arid regions. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange with a distinctive, spurred lip.

Stylidium neurophyllum is known for its rapid growth and short flowering period, often blooming after rainfall.
