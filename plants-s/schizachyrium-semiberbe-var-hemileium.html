Schizachyrium semiberbe var. hemileium

Schizachyrium semiberbe var. hemileium, commonly known as little bluestem, is a native grass species found in North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage that turns coppery in the fall. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as prairies and sandy soils. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are often reddish-brown in color.

This grass is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
