Scadoxus × hybridus

Scadoxus × hybridus, commonly known as the hybrid calla lily, is a hybrid plant resulting from crosses between species of the genus Scadoxus.

It is characterized by its striking, spatulate-shaped leaves and showy, white or colored spatheae that surround the small, clustered flowers. The plant is native to southern Africa and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a potted plant. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Scadoxus × hybridus is a member of the Araceae family, which includes other popular ornamental plants such as peace lilies and calla lilies.
