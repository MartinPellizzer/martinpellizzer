Stipa Pulcherrima F. Pubiflora

Stipa pulcherrima f. pubiflora is a striking ornamental grass known for its elegant, feathery foliage and vibrant summer blooms. This variety features soft, silver-blue leaves that create a striking contrast against its fluffy, pinkish-white seed heads. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a favorite among gardeners and designers. This plant adds both texture and color to any outdoor space, enhancing the visual appeal of the landscape.
