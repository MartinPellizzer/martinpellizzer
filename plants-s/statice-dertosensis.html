Statice dertosensis

Statice dertosensis, commonly known as the Dertos statice, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Western Australia.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant blue to purple flowers. The plant is well adapted to drought conditions, featuring succulent leaves and a deep root system. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal and inland dunes.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
