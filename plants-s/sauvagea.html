Sauvagea

Sauvagea, commonly known as the sauvagea orchid, is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

The genus includes several species, such as Sauvagea alba and Sauvagea rufescens, which are often found in humid, tropical environments. Sauvagea plants typically have long, slender stems and produce showy, often fragrant blooms that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
