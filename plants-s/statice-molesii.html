Statice molesii

Statice molesii, commonly known as the Sicilian statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and purple, which bloom in late summer. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a fine, powdery coating.

Statice molesii is often cultivated as a ornamental plant for its vibrant colors and drought tolerance.
