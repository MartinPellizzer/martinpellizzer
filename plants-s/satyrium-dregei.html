Satyrium dregei

Satyrium dregei, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive floral structure. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in coastal regions. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow with a subtle fragrance, and it produces small, round seeds.

Satyrium dregei is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
