Salacia articulata

Salacia articulata, commonly known as the climbing salacia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Salaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that contains numerous tiny seeds.

Salacia articulata is often found in moist, tropical forests and is valued for its ornamental qualities.
