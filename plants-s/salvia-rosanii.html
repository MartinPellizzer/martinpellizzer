Salvia rosanii

Salvia rosanii, commonly known as the rose sage, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, gray-green leaves and showy, violet to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is part of the Lamiaceae family, known for its square stems and fragrant foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is complemented by its use in traditional herbal remedies.
