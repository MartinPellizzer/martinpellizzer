Syzygium macrocalyx

Syzygium macrocalyx, commonly known as the large-flowered clove.

It is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, white to pinkish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are alternate, elliptical, and have a leathery texture.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, dark brown seeds.
