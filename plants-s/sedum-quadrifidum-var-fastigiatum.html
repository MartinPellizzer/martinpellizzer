Sedum quadrifidum var. fastigiatum

Sedum quadrifidum var. fastigiatum, commonly known as the narrowleaf stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of North America.

It features upright, branched stems covered in small, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped, yellowish flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to rocky and sandy soils, thriving in full sun and requiring minimal water.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
