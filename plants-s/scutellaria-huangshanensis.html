Scutellaria huangshanensis

Scutellaria huangshanensis, commonly known as Huangshan skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China, particularly around Huangshan Mountain.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or violet in color, which bloom during the summer months.

Scutellaria huangshanensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional Chinese medicine.
