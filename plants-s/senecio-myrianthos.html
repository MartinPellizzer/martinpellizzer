Senecio myrianthos

Senecio myrianthos, commonly known as the African Daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy composite flowers. The plant features a basal rosette of silver-gray, fuzzy leaves and produces yellow disk flowers surrounded by white to pale blue ray florets. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Senecio myrianthos is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.
