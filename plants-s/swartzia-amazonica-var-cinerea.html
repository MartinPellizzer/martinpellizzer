Swartzia amazonica var. cinerea

Swartzia amazonica var. cinerea, commonly known as the cinerea swartzia, is a tropical tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black bark and dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. Its wood is valued for its durability and is used in traditional crafts and construction.

The species is also noted for its role in the ecological dynamics of its habitat, supporting a diverse array of flora and fauna.
