Sonchus mairei

Sonchus mairei, commonly known as Maire's sowthistle, is a flowering plant in the sunflower family, Asteraceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its basal rosette of lance-shaped leaves and yellow ray florets. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in mountainous regions. It produces a tall, branching stem with numerous small flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and traditional herbal practices.
