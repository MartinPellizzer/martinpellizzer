Syzygium luzonense

Syzygium luzonense, commonly known as the Luzon flame tree, is a tropical tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its striking red-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a glossy surface. It produces woody capsules containing small, round seeds.

Syzygium luzonense is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
