Silene nivea

Silene nivea, commonly known as the snowflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in well-drained soil. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in a fine, silvery down.

Silene nivea is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
