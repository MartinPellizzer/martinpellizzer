Stelis sotoarenasii

Stelis sotoarenasii, commonly known as the Soto Arenas orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andes of South America.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils at high altitudes, often in humid environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its leaf structure. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pink and purple.

Stelis sotoarenasii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in specialty orchid collections.
