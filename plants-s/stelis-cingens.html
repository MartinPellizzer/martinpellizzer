Stelis cingens

Stelis cingens, commonly known as the Ceylon stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments. The plant features a slender rhizome and produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Stelis cingens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
