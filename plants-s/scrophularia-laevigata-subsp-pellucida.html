Scrophularia laevigata subsp. pellucida

Scrophularia laevigata subsp.

pellucida, commonly known as the smooth scrophularia, is a perennial herb native to parts of Europe and Asia. It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in woodland or meadow habitats.

The plant features erect stems that are usually hairless or only slightly hairy. Its flowers are tubular and usually pink or purple, arranged in whorls along the stem.

It belongs to the family Plantaginaceae and is known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
