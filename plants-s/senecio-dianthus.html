Senecio dianthus

Senecio dianthus, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces bright yellow or orange flowers that bloom in summer. Senecio dianthus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
