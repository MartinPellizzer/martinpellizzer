Seris reflexa

Seris reflexa, commonly known as the reflexed seris, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellow flowers in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Seris reflexa is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
