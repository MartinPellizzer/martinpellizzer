Solanum esuriale f. esuriale

Solanum esuriale f. esuriale, commonly known as the narrow-leaved nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, sparsely hairy leaves and clusters of small, violet-blue flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces smooth, spherical berries that turn from green to yellow or red as they mature. Native to tropical regions of South America, it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its toxic alkaloids, it is important to handle the plant with care.
