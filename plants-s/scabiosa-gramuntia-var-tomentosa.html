Scabiosa gramuntia var. tomentosa

Scabiosa gramuntia var. tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly scabious, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features upright stems covered in a dense layer of soft, white hairs, giving it a fluffy appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, pink or purple flowers that bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine, fuzzy hairs, contributing to its distinctive texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
