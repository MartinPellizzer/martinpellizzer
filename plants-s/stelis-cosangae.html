Stelis cosangae

Stelis cosangae, commonly known as the orchid of the genus Stelis, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale yellow. Stelis cosangae thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in the wild.

Its botanical features make it a popular subject for horticultural study and conservation efforts.
