Specklinia garayi

Specklinia garayi, commonly known as the Garay's Vanda, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Vanda genus, which is characterized by its epiphytic growth habit and showy, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media to prevent root rot.

Specklinia garayi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
