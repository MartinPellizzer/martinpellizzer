Scutellaria kansuensis

Scutellaria kansuensis, commonly known as Kansu skullcap, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Scutellaria kansuensis is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.
