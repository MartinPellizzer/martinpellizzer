Saxifraga magellanica f. lineariloba

Saxifraga magellanica f. lineariloba, commonly known as the Magellan saxifrage, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.

Its foliage and flowering structure make it a notable species in botanical studies and horticulture.
