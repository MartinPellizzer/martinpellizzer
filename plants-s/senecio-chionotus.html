Senecio chionotus

Senecio chionotus, commonly known as the snowflake dandelion, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble dandelions. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It produces yellow ray florets surrounded by a dense cluster of tubular disk florets.

Senecio chionotus is known for its compact growth habit and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
