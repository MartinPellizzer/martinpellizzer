Scrophularia yoshimurae

Scrophularia yoshimurae, commonly known as Yoshimura's figwort, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae, which includes many species of flowering plants. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky slopes. It produces showy, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple in color.

Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with a hairy surface that helps retain moisture in its native habitat.
