Statice catalaunica

Statice catalaunica, commonly known as the Catalan statice, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces showy, spike-like inflorescences composed of numerous small, brightly colored flowers, typically in shades of pink, red, and white. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Statice catalaunica is also valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
