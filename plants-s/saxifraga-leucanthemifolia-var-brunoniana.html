Saxifraga leucanthemifolia var. brunoniana

Saxifraga leucanthemifolia var. brunoniana, commonly known as the purple saxifrage, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Arctic and alpine regions.

It features dense, compact foliage with dark green, velvety leaves that are often covered in a fine white bloom. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to cold climates.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and resilience in harsh environments.
