Schaetzellia

Schaetzellia, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to Africa.

These plants are characterized by their compact rosettes of velvety, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of showy, tubular flowers. Schaetzellia species typically thrive in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making them popular houseplants. They belong to the Gesneriaceae family and are valued for their ornamental beauty and ease of care.
