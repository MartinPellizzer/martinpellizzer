Senecio toromarensis

Senecio toromarensis, commonly known as the Toromarensis Groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid regions, showcasing adaptations to drought conditions. It features narrow, linear leaves and produces small yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species within its native habitat.
