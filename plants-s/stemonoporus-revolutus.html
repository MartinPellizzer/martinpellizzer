Stemonoporus revolutus

Stemonoporus revolutus, commonly known as the curled-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spirally twisted stems that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its flowers are small and fragrant, usually appearing in clusters along the stems.

Stemonoporus revolutus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
