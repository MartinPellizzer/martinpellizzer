Schkuhria coquimbana

Schkuhria coquimbana, commonly known as the Coquimbo dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of Chile.

Schkuhria coquimbana produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by fluffy seed heads that aid in wind dispersal.
