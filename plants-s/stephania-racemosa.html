Stephania racemosa

Stephania racemosa, commonly known as the clustered arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Menispermaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces berries that are typically red or orange when mature, containing several seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
