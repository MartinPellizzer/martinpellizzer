Serapias patens

Serapias patens, commonly known as the showy orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with distinctive markings. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are basal, narrow, and often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Serapias patens is a perennial herb that blooms in late spring to early summer, adding vibrant color to its natural environment.
