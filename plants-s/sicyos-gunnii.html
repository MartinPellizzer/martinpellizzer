Sicyos gunnii

Sicyos gunnii, commonly known as the giant horsebean vine, is a climbing plant native to the southern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant produces elongated, bean-like fruits that contain several seeds. Sicyos gunnii is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and open woodlands.

It has been used in traditional medicine by some indigenous communities for its purported health benefits.
