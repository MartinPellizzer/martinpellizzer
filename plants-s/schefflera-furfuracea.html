Schefflera furfuracea

Schefflera furfuracea, commonly known as the woolly schefflera, is a tropical plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a unique, soft appearance. The leaves are typically thick and leathery, with a pale green to grayish-white underside. It produces small, inconspicuous yellowish-white flowers followed by red, fleshy berries.

This plant is often cultivated as an ornamental due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
