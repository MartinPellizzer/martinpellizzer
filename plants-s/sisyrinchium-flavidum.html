Sisyrinchium flavidum

Sisyrinchium flavidum, commonly known as the yellow gaura, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Sisyrinchium flavidum is often used in native plant gardens due to its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
