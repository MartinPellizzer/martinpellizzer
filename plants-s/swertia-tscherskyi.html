Swertia tscherskyi

Swertia tscherskyi, commonly known as the Tibetan gentian, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky areas, thriving in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while its stem is erect and branched.

Swertia tscherskyi is valued for its ornamental beauty and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
