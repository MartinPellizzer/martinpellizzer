Senecio pavlovii

Senecio pavlovii, commonly known as the Pajaro Desert Senecio, is a flowering plant native to the Pajaro Desert in California.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its fleshy, gray-green leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant thrives in arid environments and is adapted to withstand drought conditions. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Senecio pavlovii is also known for its striking appearance and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
