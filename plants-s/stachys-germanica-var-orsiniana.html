Stachys germanica var. orsiniana

Stachys germanica var. orsiniana, commonly known as the German violet or the violet wood sorrel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive violet or purple coloration, often marked with silver or white veins. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple, blooming in the spring. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
