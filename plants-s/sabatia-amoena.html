Sabatia amoena

Sabatia amoena, commonly known as the pink evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and rocky slopes. The plant features showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in the evening, attracting nocturnal pollinators like moths. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often branched and sturdy.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
