Stellaria longipes var. monantha

Stellaria longipes var. monantha, commonly known as the slender starwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pale pink. The plant has slender, branching stems and small, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It is native to regions of Europe and Asia and thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and natural habitats.
