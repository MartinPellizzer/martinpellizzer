Swertia pahalgamensis

Swertia pahalgamensis, commonly known as the Pahalgam gentian, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan region of India.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in high-altitude meadows and rocky areas, thriving in cool and moist environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged oppositely along the stem.

Swertia pahalgamensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
