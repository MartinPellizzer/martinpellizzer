Seseli jinanense

Seseli jinanense, commonly known as the Jinan seseli, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apiaceae family.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Yunnan and Sichuan provinces. The plant is characterized by its slender stems and small, clustered white flowers. Its leaves are finely divided and have a fern-like appearance, contributing to its ornamental value.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.
