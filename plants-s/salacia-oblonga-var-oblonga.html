Salacia oblonga var. oblonga

Salacia oblonga var. oblonga, commonly known as the longleaf salacia, is a member of the Salaceae family.

It is a shrub or small tree native to parts of Southeast Asia, including India and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its elongated, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. It produces small, fleshy fruits that are typically green when mature.

The species is noted for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in tropical and subtropical climates.
