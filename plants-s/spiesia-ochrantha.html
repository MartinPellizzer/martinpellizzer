Spiesia ochrantha

Spiesia ochrantha, commonly known as the yellow bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Spiesia ochrantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
