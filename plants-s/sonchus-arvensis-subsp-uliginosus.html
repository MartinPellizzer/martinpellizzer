Sonchus arvensis subsp. uliginosus

Sonchus arvensis subsp.

uliginosus, commonly known as swamp sowthistle, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and alternate, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a composite inflorescence, typical of the daisy family. It thrives in moist, often boggy habitats, such as wet meadows and marshes.

Its presence is often indicative of nutrient-rich, waterlogged soils.
