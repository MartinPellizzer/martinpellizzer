Scorpioides muricata

Scorpioides muricata, commonly known as the rough scorpion plant, is a member of the family Acanthaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its striking, spiny leaves and showy, tubular flowers. Scorpioides muricata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can thrive in well-drained soils with partial to full sun.
