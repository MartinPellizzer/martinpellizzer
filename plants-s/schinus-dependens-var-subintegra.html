Schinus dependens var. subintegra

Schinus dependens var. subintegra, commonly known as the Brazilian peppertree or "pepper tree," is a species of flowering plant in the Anacardiaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, red, edible berries. The species is known for its ornamental value and has been introduced to other regions, sometimes becoming invasive.
