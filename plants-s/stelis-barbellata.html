Stelis barbellata

Stelis barbellata, commonly known as the barred stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. The plant features a short, erect stem with a few leaves and produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white and pink. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Stelis barbellata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and relatively easy care requirements.
