Sphaeranthus keniensis

Sphaeranthus keniensis, commonly known as Kenyan globe thistle, is a flowering plant native to Kenya.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spherical, spiky flower heads. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, adapting to harsh environmental conditions. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers range in color from white to pink.

Sphaeranthus keniensis is often found in rocky soils and is a common sight in dry savanna habitats.
