Solanum laciniatum f. australiense

Solanum laciniatum f. australiense, commonly known as the Australian potato vine, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that are typically green when immature and turn red when ripe.

It thrives in warm, sunny conditions and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various soil types.
