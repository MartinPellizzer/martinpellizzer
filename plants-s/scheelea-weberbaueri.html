Scheelea weberbaueri

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread vegetatively, forming extensive colonies in suitable habitats.

Scheelea weberbaueri is native to regions of South America, particularly in countries like Peru and Bolivia, where it thrives in humid, tropical climates.
