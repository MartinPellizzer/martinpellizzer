Schlumbergeria donnellsmithii

Schlumbergeria donnellsmithii, commonly known as the Donnellsmith holiday cactus, is a species native to Brazil.

It is a type of epiphytic cactus that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its segmented, fleshy stems that form a tree-like structure. Its flowers are typically bright red or orange, appearing during the holiday season.

It requires well-draining soil and moderate watering to flourish.
