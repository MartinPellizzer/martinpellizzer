Sideritis hirsuta var. glabrior

Sideritis hirsuta var. glabrior, commonly known as the smooth-haired ironweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, hairy leaves that are typically gray-green in color. Its flowers are pale blue to violet, arranged in whorls along the stem, and bloom in early summer.

This variety is noted for its aromatic foliage and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
