Symphyotrichum ericoides var. stricticaule

Symphyotrichum ericoides var. stricticaule, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed heath aster, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in dry, acidic soils in open woodlands and rocky areas. The plant features upright, slender stems that are often covered with fine hairs. Its small, clustered flowers are usually purple to pink in color and bloom in the fall.

It is part of the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy inflorescences.
