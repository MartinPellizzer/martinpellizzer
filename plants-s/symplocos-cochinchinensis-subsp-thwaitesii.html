Symplocos cochinchinensis subsp. thwaitesii

Symplocos cochinchinensis subsp.

thwaitesii, commonly known as the Thwaites' boxwood, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia. It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage.
