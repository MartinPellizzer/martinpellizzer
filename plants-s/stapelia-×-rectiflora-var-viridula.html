Stapelia × rectiflora var. viridula

Stapelia × rectiflora var. viridula, commonly known as the green star stapelia, is a hybrid succulent native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems that are typically green to reddish in color. The plant produces large, star-shaped flowers that are often yellow or orange with a strong, unpleasant odor. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
