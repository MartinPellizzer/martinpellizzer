Spiranthes bonariensis var. bombylifera

Spiranthes bonariensis var. bombylifera, commonly known as the Bombylifera spiral orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features delicate, spiraling inflorescences composed of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or wetlands. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming a basal rosette.

The species is noted for its unique floral structure and is often found in association with other orchid species in its habitat.
