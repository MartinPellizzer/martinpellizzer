Saxifraga montana

Saxifraga montana, commonly known as the mountain saxifrage, is a perennial plant native to the alpine regions of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils at high elevations. The plant features upright stems with alternate, ovate leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. Its small, white or pale pink flowers bloom in late spring to early summer.

Saxifraga montana is known for its hardiness and ability to thrive in harsh, cold environments.
