Symplocos vatteri

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, glossy evergreen foliage. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.
