Solanum reflexiflorum

Solanum reflexiflorum, commonly known as the reflexed-flower nightshade, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant produces small, white or pale green flowers that are often clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are usually ovate or elliptical with a rough texture.

The fruit is a berry that turns from green to yellow or red when mature.
