Strychnos cinnamomifolia

Strychnos cinnamomifolia, commonly known as the cinnamon tree or Indian cinnamon, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces small, white or greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in panicles. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds, which are small and often covered in a fleshy coating.
