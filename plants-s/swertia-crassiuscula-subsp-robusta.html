Swertia crassiuscula subsp. robusta

Swertia crassiuscula subsp.

robusta, commonly known as the Himalayan gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Asia. It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture, while its stems are erect and branched. Swertia crassiuscula subsp.

robusta is valued for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
