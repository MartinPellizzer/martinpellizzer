Schefflera shweliensis

Schefflera shweliensis, commonly known as the Shweliensis umbrella tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of large, glossy green leaves. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters in its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually leathery and have a smooth, waxy texture.

Schefflera shweliensis thrives in humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
