Scorzonera turkestanica

Scorzonera turkestanica, commonly known as the Turkistan scorzonera, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and basal leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers with a distinctive tubular corolla, typically blooming in late summer. Its root system is fibrous and often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Scorzonera turkestanica thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils.
