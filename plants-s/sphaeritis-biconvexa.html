Sphaeritis biconvexa

Sphaeritis biconvexa, commonly known as the two-convex sphere plant, is a unique species characterized by its spherical, bi-convex shape.

It belongs to the family Sphaeritidaceae and is native to specific regions in South America. The plant's structure consists of a dense, layered arrangement of cells that contribute to its distinctive form. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its rare occurrence, it is of particular interest to botanists studying plant morphology and adaptation.
