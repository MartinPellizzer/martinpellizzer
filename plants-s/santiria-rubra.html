Santiria rubra

Santiria rubra, commonly known as the red santiria, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, bright red flowers. The plant produces oval, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Santiria rubra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
