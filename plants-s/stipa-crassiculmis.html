Stipa crassiculmis

Stipa crassiculmis, commonly known as the thick-stemmed feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and slender, wiry stems that often form clumps. The plant produces slender, spike-like inflorescences that are typically green to golden in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a rough texture, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

Stipa crassiculmis is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
