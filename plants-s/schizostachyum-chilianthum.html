Schizostachyum chilianthum

Schizostachyum chilianthum, commonly known as the Chilianthum rattan, is a climbing palm native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its pinnate leaves and long, flexible stems. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional crafts. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Schizostachyum chilianthum is an important species in both ecological and economic contexts.
