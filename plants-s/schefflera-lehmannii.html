Schefflera lehmannii

Schefflera lehmannii, commonly known as the Lehmann's umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It features a distinctive, umbrella-like crown composed of multiple leaflets arranged in a whorled pattern. The plant has compound, pinnately arranged leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by orange-red berries.

Schefflera lehmannii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
