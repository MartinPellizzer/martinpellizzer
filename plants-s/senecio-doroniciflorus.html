Senecio doroniciflorus

Senecio doroniciflorus, commonly known as the Doronicifolium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features striking, bright yellow ray florets surrounded by a ring of tubular disc florets. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Senecio doroniciflorus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its vibrant blooms and unique foliage.
