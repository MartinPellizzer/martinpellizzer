Spathiphyllum atrovirens

Spathiphyllum atrovirens, commonly known as the black-veined peace lily, is a tropical evergreen plant native to the Philippines.

It features broad, dark green leaves with striking black veins, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces elegant white spathes that enclose small, greenish-yellow flowers. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and ability to thrive in humid environments.

Spathiphyllum atrovirens is also valued for its air-purifying properties and ability to tolerate occasional neglect.
