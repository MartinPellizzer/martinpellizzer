Shuteria hirsuta

Shuteria hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy shuteria, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are often grayish-green in color. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in clusters, and it produces achene fruits.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.
