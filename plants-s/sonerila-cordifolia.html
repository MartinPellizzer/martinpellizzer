Sonerila cordifolia

Sonerila cordifolia, commonly known as the heart-leaf sonerila, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, often pink or red flowers. Sonerila cordifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
