Scutellaria sessilifolia f. ramiflora

Scutellaria sessilifolia f. ramiflora, commonly known as the ramiflorous purplehead, is a flowering plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive violet-blue flowers. The plant features lanceolate, sessile leaves that are often clustered at the base of the stems. Its inflorescence consists of whorled clusters of small, two-lipped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

This variety is noted for its compact growth and is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
