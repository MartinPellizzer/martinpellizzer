Senecio jacottetianus

Senecio jacottetianus, commonly known as the Jacottet's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It features small, yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, often surrounded by narrow, grass-like leaves.
