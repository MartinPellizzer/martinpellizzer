Schefflera violea

Schefflera violea, commonly known as the violet schefflera, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically arranged in a compact, umbrella-like pattern. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and violet, which bloom in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Schefflera violea is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ornamental flowers.
