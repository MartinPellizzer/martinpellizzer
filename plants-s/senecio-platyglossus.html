Senecio platyglossus

Senecio platyglossus, commonly known as the silverleaf groundsel, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a low-growing, mat-forming habit with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically grayish-green in color. The leaves are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs, giving the plant its characteristic silvery appearance. It produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, which are clustered in dense inflorescences.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
