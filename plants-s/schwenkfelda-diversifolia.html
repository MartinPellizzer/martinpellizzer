Schwenkfelda diversifolia

Schwenkfelda diversifolia, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette of velvety, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a bluish tint. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, which bloom from the center of the leaf rosette. Schwenkfelda diversifolia thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires indirect sunlight.

It is a popular houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
