Sinocalamus yentuensis

Sinocalamus yentuensis, commonly known as Yentu bamboo, is a species of grass in the Poaceae family.

It is native to China, particularly found in the Yunnan and Sichuan provinces. This bamboo is characterized by its tall, slender stems and dense foliage, often growing in moist, mountainous environments. The plant is valued for its strong, lightweight culms, which have been used in traditional crafts and construction.

Its ecological role includes providing habitat for various wildlife species and contributing to soil stabilization in its native habitat.
