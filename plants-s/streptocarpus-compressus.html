Streptocarpus compressus

Streptocarpus compressus, commonly known as the compact streptocarpus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and attractive, tubular flowers. The plant typically features broad, glossy leaves and produces clusters of violet or blue flowers during the spring and summer seasons. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Streptocarpus compressus is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
