Sedum stellariifolium

Sedum stellariifolium, commonly known as starleaf stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a star-like pattern, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in late summer, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Sedum stellariifolium is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
