Sisymbrium montanum

Sisymbrium montanum, commonly known as mountain shepherd's purse, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other mustard family plants. The plant produces small, yellow flowers and distinctive triangular seed pods that resemble a shepherd's purse, hence its common name. It is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in open, sunny habitats.

Sisymbrium montanum is typically herbaceous, with hairy stems and serrated leaves.
