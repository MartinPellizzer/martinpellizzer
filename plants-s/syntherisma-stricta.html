Syntherisma stricta

Syntherisma stricta, commonly known as the rigid syntherism, is a unique plant species characterized by its tightly compacted growth habit.

It features small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a spiral pattern along its stem. The plant produces minute, inconspicuous flowers that are often overlooked due to their diminutive size. Its root system is fibrous and shallow, allowing it to thrive in well-drained soils.

Syntherisma stricta is native to arid regions and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
