Sisyrinchium vaginatum subsp. restioides

Sisyrinchium vaginatum subsp.

restioides, commonly known as the narrowleaf blue-eyed grass, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its grass-like foliage and showy blue or violet flowers.
