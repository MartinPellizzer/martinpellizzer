Sarcanthus armiger

Sarcanthus armiger, commonly known as the yellow starburster, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers with a star-like appearance. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Sarcanthus armiger is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
