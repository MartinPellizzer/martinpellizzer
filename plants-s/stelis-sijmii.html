Stelis sijmii

Stelis sijmii, commonly known as the Sijm's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of white or pale pink blooms, which are often adorned with intricate patterns. Stelis sijmii thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
