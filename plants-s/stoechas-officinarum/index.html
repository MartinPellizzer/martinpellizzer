Stoechas Officinarum

Stoechas officinarum is a rare and striking plant known for its unique blue-violet flowers. Native to the Canary Islands, it thrives in rocky and coastal environments. This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been cultivated by horticulturists worldwide. Its delicate appearance makes it a favorite among plant enthusiasts. However, it requires careful attention to grow successfully in different climates.
