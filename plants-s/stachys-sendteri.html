Stachys sendteri

Stachys sendteri, commonly known as the Sierra stonecrop, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It is a low-growing perennial with succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in its arid habitat. The plant produces clusters of small, violet to purple flowers during the summer months.
