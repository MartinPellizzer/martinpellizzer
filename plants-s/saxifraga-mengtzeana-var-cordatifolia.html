Saxifraga mengtzeana var. cordatifolia

Saxifraga mengtzeana var. cordatifolia, commonly known as the broad-leafed saxifrage, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are broad and heart-shaped. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
