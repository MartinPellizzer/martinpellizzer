Sedum anatolicum

Sedum anatolicum, commonly known as Anatolian stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Turkey.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has thick, water-storing leaves that help it survive in arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Sedum anatolicum is valued for its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
