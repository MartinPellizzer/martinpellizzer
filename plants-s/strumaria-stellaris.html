Strumaria stellaris

Strumaria stellaris, commonly known as the star flower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the high altitudes of the Andes in South America.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple, typically during the spring and early summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers well-drained soil.

Strumaria stellaris is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
