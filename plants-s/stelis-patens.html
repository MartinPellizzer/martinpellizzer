Stelis patens

Stelis patens, commonly known as the spreading stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic. The plant produces delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are often clustered at the base. Its flowers are small and typically white or pale green, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

Stelis patens is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia and is appreciated for its ornamental value in cultivation.
