Senecio × dominii

Senecio × dominii, commonly known as the hybrid African violet, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Senecio serotinus and Senecio macroglossus.

It is characterized by its rosette growth habit and produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant is native to South Africa and thrives in well-draining soil with moderate watering. It is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

Senecio × dominii is often cultivated as a houseplant or in rock gardens due to its adaptability and aesthetic appeal.
