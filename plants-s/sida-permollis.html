Sida permollis

Sida permollis, commonly known as the silky sida, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It features slender, branching stems covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically pale green to white in color. Its leaves are alternate, ovate to lanceolate, and often have a soft, hairy texture.

Sida permollis is adapted to a variety of soil types and is commonly found in disturbed areas and along roadsides.
