Stelis anchorilabia

Stelis anchorilabia, commonly known as the anchoring stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid tropical environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pink and purple.

Stelis anchorilabia is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
