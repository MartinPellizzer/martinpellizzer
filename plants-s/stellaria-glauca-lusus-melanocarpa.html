Stellaria glauca lusus melanocarpa

Stellaria glauca lusus melanocarpa, commonly known as the blackstem starwort, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in grasslands and woodland edges. Its leaves are lanceolate and slightly hairy, while the stems are slender and often tinged with a dark color.

It is an herbaceous perennial that blooms from late spring to early summer.
