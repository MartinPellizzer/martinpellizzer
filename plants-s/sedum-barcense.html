Sedum barcense

Sedum barcense, commonly known as the Barcense stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the southwestern United States.

It features fleshy, upright stems and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow to orange. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Sedum barcense is also valued for its low maintenance and ability to attract pollinators.
