Silene berthelotiana

Silene berthelotiana, commonly known as the Berthelot's campion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb with a rosette of basal leaves and produces pink or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It has a taproot system that helps it survive in dry conditions.

Silene berthelotiana is often found in mountainous regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
