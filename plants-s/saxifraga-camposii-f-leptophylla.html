Saxifraga camposii f. leptophylla

Saxifraga camposii f. leptophylla, commonly known as the slender-leafed saxifrage, is a rare and delicate plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its small, clustered leaves that are typically narrow and elongated, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white or pale pink flowers during the summer months. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.
