Sinningia × pronopes

Sinningia × pronopes, commonly known as the violet.

It is a hybrid plant in the Gesneriaceae family, native to South America. The plant is characterized by its velvety, waxy leaves and showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Sinningia × pronopes is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
