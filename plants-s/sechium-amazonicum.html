Sechium amazonicum

Sechium amazonicum, commonly known as the chayote, is a tropical vine native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes cucumbers and squash. The plant produces large, pear-shaped fruits with a white, edible flesh and a mild, sweet flavor. Its leaves are heart-shaped and can be used in traditional medicine.
