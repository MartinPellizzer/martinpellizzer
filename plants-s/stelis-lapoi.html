Stelis lapoi

Stelis lapoi, commonly known as the Lapoi orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with a distinct three-lobed lip.

Stelis lapoi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
