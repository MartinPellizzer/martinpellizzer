Solanum tettense var. renschii

Solanum tettense var. renschii, commonly known as the Rensch's nightshade, is a perennial herb native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades, tomatoes, and potatoes. The plant features hairy stems and alternate leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are typically yellow or white, with five petals, and it produces small, berry-like fruits that are toxic to humans.

This variety is often found in humid, shaded environments in its native habitat.
