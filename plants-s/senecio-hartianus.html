Senecio hartianus

Senecio hartianus, commonly known as Hart's groundsel, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.
