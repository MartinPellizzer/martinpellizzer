Stanhopea insignis var. flava

Stanhopea insignis var. flava, commonly known as the Yellow Stanhopea, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers, which are typically borne on long, pendulous inflorescences. The plant features strap-like, dark green leaves that are arranged in a fan shape. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its striking appearance, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for both its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
