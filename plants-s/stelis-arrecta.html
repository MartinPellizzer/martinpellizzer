Stelis arrecta

Stelis arrecta, commonly known as the upright stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically has dark green, lance-shaped leaves and thrives in humid, shaded environments. It prefers well-draining soil and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Stelis arrecta is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its resilience in tropical climates.
