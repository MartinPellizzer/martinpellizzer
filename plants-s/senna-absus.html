Senna absus

Senna absus, commonly known as the yellow senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its feathery, bipinnate leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, triangular pods that contain several seeds. Senna absus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
