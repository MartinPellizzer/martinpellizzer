Senecio paucidentatus var. limbardioides

Senecio paucidentatus var. limbardioides, commonly known as the Limbardioides senecio, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa.

It features cylindrical, ribbed stems that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the cooler months. Its leaves are typically narrow and pointed, with a slightly hairy texture.

This species is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
