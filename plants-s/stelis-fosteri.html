Stelis fosteri

Stelis fosteri, commonly known as the Foster's stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on tree trunks or branches, deriving moisture and nutrients from the air and rain. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are often white or pale pink in color. Its roots are adapted to absorb water and nutrients efficiently in its natural habitat.

Stelis fosteri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
