Scilla talosii

Scilla talosii, commonly known as the Talosii squill, is a perennial bulbous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It produces striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.

Scilla talosii is also noted for its ability to thrive in full sun to partial shade conditions.
