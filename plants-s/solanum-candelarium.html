Solanum candelarium

Solanum candelarium, commonly known as the candle bush, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Its fruit is a yellow, fleshy berry that contains several seeds.

The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.
