Sideritis vestita var. virens

Sideritis vestita var. virens, commonly known as green-haired ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open habitats such as slopes and meadows.

Sideritis vestita var. virens is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
