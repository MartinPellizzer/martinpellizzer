Seriphium tenuifolium

Seriphium tenuifolium, commonly known as slenderleaf hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the northern regions of North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and linear, and it produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

Seriphium tenuifolium is often found in regions with cool, temperate climates and is adapted to thrive in low-nutrient environments.
