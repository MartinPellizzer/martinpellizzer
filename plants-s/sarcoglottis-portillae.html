Sarcoglottis portillae

Sarcoglottis portillae, commonly known as the Portilla's sarcoglottis, is a member of the Dioscoreaceae family.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its slender stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that contain several seeds.

Native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, it is often found in humid, shaded environments.
