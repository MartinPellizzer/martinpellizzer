Sanguisorba stipulata var. kishinamii

Sanguisorba stipulata var. kishinamii, commonly known as the Japanese dropwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It features upright, branched stems that bear clusters of small, reddish-purple flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with prominent veins and stipules, which are leaf-like structures at the base of the leaf stalks. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

This variant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and flowering display.
