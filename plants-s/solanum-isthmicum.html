Solanum isthmicum

Solanum isthmicum, commonly known as the Isthmus nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its small, violet-blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

Solanum isthmicum produces small, round berries that are toxic to humans and animals.
