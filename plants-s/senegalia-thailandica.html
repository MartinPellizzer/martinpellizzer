Senegalia thailandica

Senegalia thailandica, commonly known as Thai acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are typically bipinnate and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, followed by long, slender pods that contain seeds. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it adaptable to various soil types.

Senegalia thailandica is often used in traditional medicine and for its hardy, drought-resistant properties.
