Sphenomeris chinensis var. tenuifolia

Sphenomeris chinensis var. tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender-leafed sphenomeris, is a species native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is a climbing or trailing plant with slender, lanceolate leaves that are usually covered in fine pubescence. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in racemes, which are typically borne along the stems. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.
