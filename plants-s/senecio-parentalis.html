Senecio parentalis

Senecio parentalis, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other composite flowers. The plant features showy yellow flowers surrounded by white ray florets, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open grasslands.

Senecio parentalis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
