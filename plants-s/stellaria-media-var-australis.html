Stellaria media var. australis

Stellaria media var. australis, commonly known as Australian chickweed, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Australia.

It features small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom throughout the year, typically from spring to autumn. The plant has slender, branching stems covered in fine, soft hairs, and its leaves are opposite, ovate, and slightly hairy. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as gardens, lawns, and along pathways.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
