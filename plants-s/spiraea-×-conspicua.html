Spiraea × conspicua

Spiraea × conspicua, commonly known as the weeping spiraea, is a deciduous shrub native to Asia.

It is characterized by its cascading clusters of white or pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has slender, arching branches that give it a graceful, weeping appearance. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Spiraea × conspicua is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardiness.
