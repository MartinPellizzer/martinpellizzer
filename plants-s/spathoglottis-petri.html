Spathoglottis petri

Spathoglottis petri, commonly known as the petri's orchid, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its erect, leafy stems and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of white or pale pink flowers with a distinctive spathaceous bract. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forest understories.

Spathoglottis petri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
