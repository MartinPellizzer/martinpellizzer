Scutellaria darriensis

Scutellaria darriensis, commonly known as the Darri's skullcap, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. The plant features square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool, moist conditions.

Scutellaria darriensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
