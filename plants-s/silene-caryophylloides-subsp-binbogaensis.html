Silene caryophylloides subsp. binbogaensis

Silene caryophylloides subsp.

binbogaensis, commonly known as the Binboga carnation, is a flowering plant native to Turkey. It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and slopes. It is a perennial herb with a compact growth habit and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Its botanical features make it a unique and interesting species within its native flora.
