Scabiosa Orientalis

Scabiosa orientalis, also known as the oriental ladies' mantle, is a striking perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Turkey and the Caucasus. With its delicate, clustered blooms in shades of pink, white, and purple, it adds a touch of elegance to gardens and wildflower displays. This plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, loamy soil for optimal growth. Its unique foliage, featuring silvery-white veins on deep green leaves, enhances its ornamental appeal. Often used in rock gardens or as a border plant, Scabiosa orientalis is both a beauty and a hardy addition to any landscape.
