Schlegelia dressleri

Schlegelia dressleri, commonly known as the desert hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a contrasting red or purple throat. The plant has a woody stem and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires minimal water, making it well adapted to dry environments.

Schlegelia dressleri is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
