Saussurea microdeltoidea

The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, preferring well-drained soils and full sunlight. Saussurea microdeltoidea is known for its medicinal properties, traditionally used in herbal remedies for its anti-inflammatory and analgesic effects.
