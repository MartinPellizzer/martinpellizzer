Stelis litensis

Stelis litensis, commonly known as the Litens orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, often clustered growth habit. The plant features slender, wiry stems that bear small, elongated leaves. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a distinct three-lobed lip.

Stelis litensis thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in tropical rainforests.
