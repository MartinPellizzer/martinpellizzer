Stephania hernandiifolia var. abyssinica

Stephania hernandiifolia var. abyssinica, commonly known as the Abyssinian stephania, is a climbing plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Menispermaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in montane forests.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
