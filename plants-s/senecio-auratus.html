Senecio auratus

Senecio auratus, commonly known as the golden ragwort, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers and silver-green foliage. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. Senecio auratus typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its vibrant appearance.
