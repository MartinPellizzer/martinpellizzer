Siumis

Siumis, commonly known as water pepper, is a small flowering plant belonging to the family Berberidaceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that grow in clusters along the stem. Siumis is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodland areas and stream banks.

It is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
