Senecio brachychaetus var. discoideus

Senecio brachychaetus var. discoideus, commonly known as the African marguerite, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its compact growth habit and dense clusters of small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant features silver-gray leaves with a fuzzy texture, which help reduce water loss in arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and ability to attract pollinators.
