Stelis Harlingii

Stelis harlingii is a rare and delicate orchid native to the mountainous regions of South America. It is known for its striking white and purple flowers that bloom during the cooler months. This epiphytic plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires careful cultivation. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this endangered species from habitat loss.
