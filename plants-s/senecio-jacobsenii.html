Senecio jacobsenii

Senecio jacobsenii, commonly known as the Jacob's beard or Jacob's plant, is a striking succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a tufted, rosette-like structure. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring and summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
