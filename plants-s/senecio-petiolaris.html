Senecio petiolaris

Senecio petiolaris, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rosette-like growth pattern and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue, purple, or white in color. It has hairy leaves and thrives in well-drained soil with bright, indirect light.

Senecio petiolaris is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
