Sphaerocionium venustum

Sphaerocionium venustum, commonly known as the glittering globe moss, is a small, delicate liverwort that thrives in moist, shaded environments.

It has a distinctive spherical shape, with a dense covering of tiny, overlapping leaves that give it a globular appearance. The plant's leaves are usually a vibrant green, and they often have a waxy coating that helps retain moisture. It is typically found on rocks or tree trunks in temperate regions.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat preferences, it is a subject of interest for botanists studying bryophyte diversity.
