Spathicalyx

Spathicalyx, commonly known as the orchid vine, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and the presence of showy, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically features long, slender stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually arranged in clusters and can be found in various colors, including white, yellow, and pink.
