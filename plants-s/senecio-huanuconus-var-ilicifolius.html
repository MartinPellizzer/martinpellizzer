Senecio huanuconus var. ilicifolius

Senecio huanuconus var. ilicifolius, commonly known as the silverleaf senecio, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It features narrow, silvery-gray leaves that are densely covered with fine hairs, giving it a distinctive, almost frost-like appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
