Sinocrassula stenostachya var. integrifolia

Sinocrassula stenostachya var. integrifolia, commonly known as the rock rose or stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to China.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
