Stelis pleistantha

Stelis pleistantha, commonly known as the showy stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with fleshy leaves that store water. Its flowers are clustered in inflorescences and are characterized by their vibrant colors and intricate patterns.

Stelis pleistantha is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
