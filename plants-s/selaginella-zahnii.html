Selaginella zahnii

Selaginella zahnii, commonly known as the common spike moss, is a species of spore-bearing plant in the genus Selaginella.

It is a low-growing, evergreen plant that typically forms dense mats on rocky or sandy soils. The plant has a unique life cycle involving both a gametophyte and a sporophyte stage. Its leaves are typically linear and arranged in whorls around the stems.

Selaginella zahnii is often found in arid or semi-arid environments and is adapted to survive in harsh conditions.
