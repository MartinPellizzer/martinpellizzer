Sedum annuum subsp. litoreum

Sedum annuum subsp.

litoreum, commonly known as sea purslane, is a succulent plant native to coastal regions of Europe and western Asia. It thrives in saline and sandy soils, often found along shores and dunes.

The plant has fleshy, blue-green leaves and produces small yellow flowers in summer. It is adapted to withstand salt spray and strong winds.

Its ability to store water in its leaves makes it drought-tolerant and well-suited to harsh maritime environments.
