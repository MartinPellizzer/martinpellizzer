Sigmoidala kityana

Sigmoidala kityana, commonly known as the Kityana spiral plant, is a unique species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive spiral growth pattern, which gives it an ornamental appearance. The plant features narrow, elongated leaves that spiral around its central stem, creating a visually striking form. Its botanical structure includes a woody stem with a fibrous root system, adapted to thrive in moist, shaded environments.

Sigmoidala kityana is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in horticultural settings.
