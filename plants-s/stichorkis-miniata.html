Stichorkis miniata

Stichorkis miniata, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant has narrow, strap-like leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Its flowers are clustered in racemes and bloom during the warmer months.

Stichorkis miniata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
