Stachys burgsdorffioides

Stachys burgsdorffioides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hedge-nettle, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, soft hairs. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in whorled clusters along the stems.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.
