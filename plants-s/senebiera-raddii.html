Senebiera raddii

Senebiera raddii, commonly known as the giant senebiera, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves that can grow up to 3 meters in length. The plant produces striking red and yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom during the wet season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Senebiera raddii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in local medicine.
