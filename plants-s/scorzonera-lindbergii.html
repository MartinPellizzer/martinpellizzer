Scorzonera lindbergii

Scorzonera lindbergii, commonly known as Lindberg's scorzonera, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Compositae (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its hairy, erect stems and yellow flowers with prominent central disk. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
