Senna itatiaiae

Senna itatiaiae, commonly known as Brazilian senna, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant is known for its bright yellow flowers, which bloom in clusters and attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are pinnately compound, and the plant can grow as a shrub or small tree.

Senna itatiaiae is also valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional remedies.
