Saprosma verrucosa

Saprosma verrucosa, commonly known as the warty saprosma, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its rough, bark-like stems covered in warty protuberances. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Saprosma verrucosa is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
