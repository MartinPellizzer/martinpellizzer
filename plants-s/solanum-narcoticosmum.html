Solanum narcoticosmum

Solanum narcoticosmum, commonly known as the narcotic nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its dark green, lobed leaves and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. The plant produces berries that start off green and mature to a deep red, containing toxic compounds such as solanine. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland areas or along the edges of forests.

Due to its toxic properties, it is important to handle the plant with care.
