Solanum linearisepalum

Solanum linearisepalum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are berries that mature to a dark purple color, containing several seeds.

Solanum linearisepalum is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.
