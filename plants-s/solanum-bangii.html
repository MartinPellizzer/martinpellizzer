Solanum bangii

Solanum bangii, commonly known as the Bangi nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and peppers. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with hairy stems and oval-shaped leaves. Its small, yellowish flowers bloom in clusters and are followed by berries that mature to a dark purple color.

Solanum bangii is often found in forested areas and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
