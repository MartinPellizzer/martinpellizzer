Statice alleizettei

Statice alleizettei, commonly known as the Canary Islands statice, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and purple, which bloom from summer to autumn. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, adapted to the arid conditions of its native habitat.

Statice alleizettei is often cultivated as a ornamental plant for its long-lasting blooms and drought tolerance.
