Stellaria borealis var. sitchana

Stellaria borealis var. sitchana, commonly known as the Arctic starwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Arctic and alpine regions.

It features small, white or pinkish star-shaped flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant has slender, hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in well-drained, rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to cold climates.

Stellaria borealis var. sitchana plays a role in stabilizing soil in its harsh habitat and is often found in tundra and mountainous areas.
