Scindapsus ceramensis

Scindapsus ceramensis, commonly known as the Ceram Island ZZ plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Indonesia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with silver or white markings. The plant is noted for its slow growth and ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements have contributed to its popularity among plant enthusiasts.

Scindapsus ceramensis is also valued for its air-purifying properties and ability to adapt to a variety of indoor environments.
