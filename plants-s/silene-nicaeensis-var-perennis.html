Silene nicaeensis var. perennis

Silene nicaeensis var. perennis, commonly known as the perennial lady's mantle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of broad, velvety leaves. The plant produces clusters of pink or white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance, it is often cultivated in gardens across temperate climates.
