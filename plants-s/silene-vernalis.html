Silene vernalis

Silene vernalis, commonly known as the spring beauty, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its delicate, pink or white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, rocky soils and is often found in woodland areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and appear after the flowers, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Silene vernalis is valued for its early blooming habit and its role in supporting pollinators.
