Sideritis × linearifolia

Sideritis × linearifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ironweed, is a hybrid species belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and small, pale blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. Native to the Mediterranean region, this plant thrives in rocky and well-drained soils. It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and scrublands.

The plant is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
