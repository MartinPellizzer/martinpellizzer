Solanum phureja var. erlansonii

Solanum phureja var. erlansonii, commonly known as the Erlansonii nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes many economically important plants such as tomatoes and potatoes. The plant is characterized by its dark green, lobed leaves and clusters of small, white to pale purple flowers. It produces small, round berries that are typically yellow or orange when ripe.

This species is often grown for its ornamental value and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
