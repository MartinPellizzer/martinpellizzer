Sphaeranthus kirkii var. kirkii

Sphaeranthus kirkii var. kirkii, commonly known as the Kirk's globe thistle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, globe-shaped inflorescence composed of small, bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
