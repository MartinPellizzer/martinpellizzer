Schefflera parvifolia

Schefflera parvifolia, commonly known as the small-leaf umbrella plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, bushy growth habit with thick, waxy leaves that are small and glossy. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red, berry-like fruits. Its foliage is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in indoor environments.

Schefflera parvifolia prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil for optimal growth.
