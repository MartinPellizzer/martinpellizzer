Senecio psychrophilus

This plant is adapted to extreme cold and has a low-growing, cushion-like growth form to minimize heat loss. Its leaves are typically covered in a dense layer of fine hairs, which helps reduce water loss and protect against freezing temperatures.

Senecio psychrophilus plays a role in the sparse flora of its environment, contributing to the ecological dynamics of polar and alpine regions.
