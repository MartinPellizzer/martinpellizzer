Saussurea bomiensis

Saussurea bomiensis, commonly known as the Yunnan snow lotus, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stem and large, showy yellow flowers. The plant thrives in cold, arid environments and is adapted to the harsh conditions of mountainous regions. Its roots are valued in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat vulnerability, it is considered an endangered species.
