Stachys recta var. karstiana

Stachys recta var. karstiana, commonly known as Karstian stonecrop, is a perennial succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

It features compact, upright growth with fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink or purple during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine displays.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a popular choice among gardeners seeking low-maintenance, decorative plants.
