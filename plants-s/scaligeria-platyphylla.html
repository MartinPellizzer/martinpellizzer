Scaligeria platyphylla

Scaligeria platyphylla, commonly known as the flat-leaved scaligeria, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette, giving it a compact appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is often cultivated in xeriscapes and Mediterranean-style gardens.
