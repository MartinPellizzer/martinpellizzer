Schwartzia pterosara

Schwartzia pterosara, commonly known as the narrow-leafed sandalwood, is a member of the Burseraceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows in dry, sandy soils.
