Streblorrhiza speciosa

Streblorrhiza speciosa, commonly known as the beautiful orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature a combination of purple, pink, and white hues. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern around the pseudobulbs.

Streblorrhiza speciosa thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
