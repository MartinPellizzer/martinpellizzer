Senecio reedii

Senecio reedii, commonly known as the African marguerite, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has narrow, linear leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Senecio reedii is drought-tolerant and prefers well-drained soil conditions.
