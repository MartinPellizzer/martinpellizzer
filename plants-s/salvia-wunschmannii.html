Salvia wunschmannii

Salvia wunschmannii, commonly known as Wunschmann's sage, is a species of flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces striking violet to blue flowers that bloom in late summer. It is often found in rocky or limestone soils in its natural habitat.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
