Sunhangia elegans var. elegans

Sunhangia elegans var. elegans, commonly known as the elegant sunhangia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with woody stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
