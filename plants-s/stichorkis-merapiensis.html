Stichorkis merapiensis

Stichorkis merapiensis, commonly known as the Merapi orchid, is a rare and endemic species found in the mountainous regions of Java, Indonesia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structure.
