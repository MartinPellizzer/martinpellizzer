Stelis eustylis

Stelis eustylis, commonly known as the elegant stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically has linear, grass-like leaves and is often found growing on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow with a subtle fragrance, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Stelis eustylis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
