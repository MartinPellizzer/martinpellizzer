Struthiola erecta var. vulgaris

Struthiola erecta var. vulgaris, commonly known as the common struthiola, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Brassicaceae.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and small, clustered flowers that typically range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is native to regions of southern Europe and is often found in rocky or disturbed soils.
