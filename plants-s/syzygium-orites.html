Syzygium orites

Syzygium orites, commonly known as the red jungleberry, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, reddish-brown bark and elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, white flowers that are followed by edible, red, fleshy berries. Syzygium orites thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its fruits are valued for their flavor and are used in local cuisines and traditional medicine.
