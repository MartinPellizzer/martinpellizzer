Steirachne barbata

Steirachne barbata, commonly known as the hairy grass or barbed grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of India, China, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. Its inflorescence consists of spikelets that are often hairy, giving it its common name.

Steirachne barbata is typically found in moist, open habitats such as grasslands and forest margins.
