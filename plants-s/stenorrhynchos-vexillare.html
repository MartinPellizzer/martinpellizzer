Stenorrhynchos vexillare

Stenorrhynchos vexillare, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, showy floral structures. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and has a compact, low-growing habit. Its flowers are often pale blue or violet, with a distinctive hood-like shape.

Stenorrhynchos vexillare is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
