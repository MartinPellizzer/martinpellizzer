Scaphosepalum erinaceum

Scaphosepalum erinaceum, commonly known as the spiny orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive spiny sepals. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid forest environments. Its flowers are small and often white or greenish-white, with a unique, spiky appearance.

Scaphosepalum erinaceum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
