Spiraea kwangsiensis var. dongvanensis

Spiraea kwangsiensis var. dongvanensis, commonly known as the Dong Van spiraea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features slender, arching stems that bear clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers in early summer. The plant has finely serrated leaves that are typically green on top and paler underneath. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in garden landscapes for its delicate floral display.
