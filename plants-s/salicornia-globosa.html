Salicornia globosa

Salicornia globosa, commonly known as sea beans or marsh samphre, is a halophyte plant adapted to saline environments.

It is a flowering plant in the family Amaranthaceae, characterized by its succulent, fleshy stems and small, inconspicuous flowers.
