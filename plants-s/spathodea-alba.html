Spathodea alba

Spathodea alba, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking white petals and a prominent yellow center, giving it a daisy-like appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of white and yellow. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

Spathodea alba is also valued for its ability to tolerate a range of soil conditions and its use in traditional medicine.
