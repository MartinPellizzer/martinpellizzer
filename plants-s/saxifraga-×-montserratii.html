Saxifraga × montserratii

Saxifraga × montserratii, commonly known as the Montserrat saxifrage, is a hybrid plant native to the volcanic island of Montserrat in the Caribbean.

It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in rocky, well-drained soils, often found in coastal and mountainous regions. The plant features dense clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of broad, waxy leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tint.

Saxifraga × montserratii is well adapted to harsh environmental conditions, making it a resilient and hardy species.
