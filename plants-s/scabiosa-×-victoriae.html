Scabiosa × victoriae

Scabiosa × victoriae, commonly known as the Victoria scabious, is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the Caprifoliaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to white, often with a distinctive central boss. The plant typically grows to a height of 12 to 24 inches, featuring hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Botanically, it is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
