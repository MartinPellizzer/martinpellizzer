Strelitzia × kewensis

Strelitzia × kewensis, commonly known as the Kew banana.

It is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Strelitzia reginae and Strelitzia nicolai. This species is characterized by its large, banana-like leaves and distinctive orange and blue flowers. It is native to South Africa and thrives in warm, humid climates.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
