Spachia Fulgens

Spachia fulgens is a rare and striking plant native to the high altitudes of the Andes. It is known for its vibrant, fiery orange flowers that bloom in late summer. This species thrives in cool, moist environments with well-drained soil. Its unique appearance makes it a favorite among botanists and gardeners alike. Despite its beauty, Spachia fulgens remains elusive and is often difficult to cultivate outside its natural habitat.
