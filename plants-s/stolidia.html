Stolidia

Stolidia, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Gesneriaceae.

These plants are native to tropical regions of Africa and are characterized by their velvety, waxy leaves and showy, tubular flowers. They thrive in bright, indirect light and require well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
