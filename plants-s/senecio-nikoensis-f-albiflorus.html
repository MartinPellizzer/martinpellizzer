Senecio nikoensis f. albiflorus

Senecio nikoensis f. albiflorus, commonly known as the white-flowered senecio, is a rare and striking plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features upright, hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its most distinctive trait is the bright white flowers that bloom in clusters, contrasting sharply with its green foliage.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a unique addition to specialized botanical collections.
