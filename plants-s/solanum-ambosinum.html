Solanum ambosinum

Solanum ambosinum, commonly known as the Brazilian nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant features small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale purple in color. Its leaves are usually oval-shaped with a smooth texture and a slightly hairy surface.

Solanum ambosinum is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in moist, shaded environments.
