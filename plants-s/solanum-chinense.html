Solanum chinense

Solanum chinense, commonly known as Chinese nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features simple, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface and produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. Its berries are initially green and turn red when mature, containing seeds that are dispersed by animals.

Botanically, it belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like tomatoes and potatoes.
