Statice speciosa var. multicaulis

Statice speciosa var. multicaulis, commonly known as the multicaulis statice, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, spiky flower heads that range in color from white to pink and purple, depending on the cultivar. The plant features a compact growth habit with multiple stems arising from the base, giving it a bushy appearance. Its foliage consists of narrow, gray-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs, contributing to its drought-tolerant nature.

This variety is often cultivated in gardens for its long-lasting blooms and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
