Statice psiloclada var. gracilis

Statice psiloclada var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, feathery foliage and small, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions. It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.

The species is also noted for its resilience to salt spray and drought.
