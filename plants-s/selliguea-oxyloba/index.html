Selliguea Oxyloba

Selliguea oxyloba is a unique and lesser-known fern species that thrives in moist, shaded environments. Found primarily in tropical and subtropical regions, this plant is known for its delicate, feathery fronds that add a soft texture to its surroundings. Its scientific name highlights its distinct morphological features, making it a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, Selliguea oxyloba remains relatively obscure in popular horticulture circles. Understanding this plant offers insights into the diversity of ferns and their ecological importance.
