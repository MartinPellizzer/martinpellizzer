Saprosma glomerata var. glomerata

Saprosma glomerata var. glomerata, commonly known as the clustered saprosma, is a shrub native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit and small, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its fruit is a capsule that contains numerous tiny seeds.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in coastal areas and disturbed soils.
