Sarga

Sarga, commonly known as the African violet, is a popular ornamental plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Gesneriaceae and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers. Its flowers come in a variety of colors, including purple, pink, white, and red.
