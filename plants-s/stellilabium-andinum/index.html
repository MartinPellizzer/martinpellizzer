Stellilabium Andinum

Stellilabium andinum is a rare and striking plant native to the high altitudes of the Andes. It is known for its unique star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. This species thrives in cold, arid environments where few plants can survive. Its delicate beauty makes it a sought-after specimen among botanists and plant enthusiasts. Due to habitat loss, Stellilabium andinum is now considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
