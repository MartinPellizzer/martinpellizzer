Spathiphyllum bariense

Spathiphyllum bariense, commonly known as the Bariense peace lily, is a tropical evergreen plant native to parts of Central and South America.

It features broad, dark green, waxy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces striking white spatulate flowers that emerge from a leaf-like bract, giving it its scientific name. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and ability to thrive in moderate humidity.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and air-purifying qualities.
