Serratula donetzica

Serratula donetzica, commonly known as the Donetz thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Eastern Europe and Central Asia.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant features a robust stem that is often branched and covered in stiff, spiny leaves. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, typical of the Asteraceae family.

Serratula donetzica is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
