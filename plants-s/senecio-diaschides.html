Senecio diaschides

Senecio diaschides, commonly known as the blue ice plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, adapted for water storage and reducing transpiration. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or arid landscapes.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
