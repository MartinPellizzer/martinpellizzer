Stisseria stellaris

Stisseria stellaris, commonly known as the starry stisseria, is a flowering plant native to the temperate regions of the northern hemisphere.

It is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters and prefers well-drained soil in partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Stisseria stellaris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic beauty.
