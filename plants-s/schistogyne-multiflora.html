Schistogyne multiflora

Schistogyne multiflora, commonly known as the African milk bush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its woody stems and small, often clustered leaves. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that are typically arranged in umbels. It is known for its milky sap, which can be toxic if ingested.

Schistogyne multiflora is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions.
