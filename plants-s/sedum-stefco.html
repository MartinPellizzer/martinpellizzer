Sedum stefco

Sedum stefco, commonly known as "Stefco Stonecrop," is a succulent plant native to southern Europe.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is adapted to rocky and well-drained soils, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping. It blooms in late spring to early summer, adding a touch of color to arid landscapes.

Sedum stefco is drought-tolerant and requires minimal maintenance once established.
