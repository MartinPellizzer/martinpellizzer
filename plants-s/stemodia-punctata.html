Stemodia punctata

Stemodia punctata, commonly known as the dotted stemodia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its unique botanical profile.

Stemodia punctata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
