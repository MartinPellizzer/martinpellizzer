Stylocoryna barbertonensis

Stylocoryna barbertonensis, commonly known as the Barberton starlight, is a striking member of the Asteraceae family.

It is native to South Africa, specifically the Eastern Cape province, where it thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers, which bloom in late summer and early autumn. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down.

Stylocoryna barbertonensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
