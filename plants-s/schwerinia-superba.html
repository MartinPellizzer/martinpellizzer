Schwerinia superba

Schwerinia superba, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a rare and striking orchid species native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers, which are arranged in a star-like pattern, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are long and strap-like, often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs.

Schwerinia superba is prized by botanists and horticulturists for its unique appearance and ecological significance in its native environment.
