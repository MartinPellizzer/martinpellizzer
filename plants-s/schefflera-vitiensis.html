Schefflera vitiensis

Schefflera vitiensis, commonly known as the Vitiensis umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like canopy of large, leathery, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscaping.
