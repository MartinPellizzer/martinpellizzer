Stera conocephala

Stera conocephala, commonly known as the mountain orchid, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its roots are adapted to thrive in rocky, well-drained soils, and it is often found in alpine or subalpine environments.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
