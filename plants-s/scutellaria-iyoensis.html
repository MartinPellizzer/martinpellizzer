Scutellaria iyoensis

Scutellaria iyoensis, commonly known as the Iyo Island skullcap, is a flowering plant native to Japan, specifically the island of Iyo.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that typically bloom in shades of purple or blue. It has hairy stems and leaves, and its flowering period occurs during the summer months.

Scutellaria iyoensis is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky areas.
