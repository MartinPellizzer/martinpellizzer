Sedum cineritium

Sedum cineritium, commonly known as silver stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its fleshy, bluish-gray leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers in the summer, adding a delicate touch to rocky or sandy habitats. It prefers full sun and well-draining soil, making it an ideal choice for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among gardeners seeking hardy, ornamental plants.
