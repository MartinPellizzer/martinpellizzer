Solanum delagoense subsp. baumii

Solanum delagoense subsp.

baumii, commonly known as the Delagoense nightshade, is a shrub native to eastern Africa. It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades and tomatoes.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas and features small, greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are ovate with serrated edges, and it produces berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

The species is notable for its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties.
