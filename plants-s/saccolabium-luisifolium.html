Saccolabium luisifolium

Saccolabium luisifolium, commonly known as the Luisi leaf scolopendrum, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of South America.

Saccolabium luisifolium is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens in suitable climates.
