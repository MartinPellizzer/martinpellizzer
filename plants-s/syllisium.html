Syllisium

Syllisium, commonly known as the desert lily, is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is a perennial herb that thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features a robust, fibrous root system that allows it to store water and nutrients efficiently. Its leaves are usually broad and fleshy, adapted to reduce water loss.

Syllisium produces striking, showy flowers that are typically white or pale pink, often blooming after rainfall.
