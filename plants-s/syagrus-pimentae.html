Syagrus pimentae

Syagrus pimentae, commonly known as the pink palm, is a striking palm species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, fan-shaped leaves that display a distinctive pinkish hue when young. The plant thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in coastal areas and humid forests. Its unique coloration makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Syagrus pimentae plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
