Stemona affinis

Stemona affinis, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a perennial herb native to China and Southeast Asia.

It features erect, branched stems that are often covered with fine hairs. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue in color. Its leaves are opposite, lanceolate, and have a slightly rough texture.

Stemona affinis is valued in traditional medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
