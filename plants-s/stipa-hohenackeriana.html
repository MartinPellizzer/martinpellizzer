Stipa hohenackeriana

Stipa hohenackeriana, commonly known as the Hohenacker's feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and produces delicate, silver-gray spikelets that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is well adapted to arid conditions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue, and it blooms in late summer.

Stipa hohenackeriana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique visual characteristics.
