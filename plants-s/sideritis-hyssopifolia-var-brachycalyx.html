Sideritis hyssopifolia var. brachycalyx

Sideritis hyssopifolia var. brachycalyx, commonly known as narrow-calyx ironweed, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright stems that are often covered in soft, greyish-white hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale blue to violet in color. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly aromatic scent.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens or as a border plant.
