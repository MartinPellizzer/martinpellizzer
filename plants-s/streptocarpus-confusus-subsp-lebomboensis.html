Streptocarpus confusus subsp. lebomboensis

Streptocarpus confusus subsp.

lebomboensis, commonly known as the Lebombo streptocarpus, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form with tubular, brightly colored flowers that range from violet to pink.

The leaves are typically ovate to elliptic, with a glossy surface and a slightly hairy texture. This species thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated as a houseplant in many regions.
