Stelis sarae

Stelis sarae, commonly known as the Sarae Epidendrum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is an epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in the Philippines. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pink and purple.

Stelis sarae requires bright, indirect light and consistent moisture to thrive in cultivation.
