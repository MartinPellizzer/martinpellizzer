Senecillis Dux

Senecio dux, scientifically known as Senecillis dux, is a striking member of the Asteraceae family native to South Africa. This plant is celebrated for its unique, cascading foliage and vibrant, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for hanging baskets and rock gardens. With its ability to thrive in well-drained soil and full sun, Senecio dux is both hardy and easy to care for. Whether grown indoors or outdoors, it adds a touch of elegance and color to any garden space.
