Silene lucida subsp. lucida

Silene lucida subsp.

lucida, commonly known as the showy campion, is a herbaceous plant native to North America. It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its striking pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky areas. It has alternate, lance-shaped leaves and produces capsules as its fruit. Silene lucida subsp.

lucida is often found in the eastern and central United States.
