Solanum purpusii

Solanum purpusii, commonly known as the Purpus's nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant features small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically purple or white in color. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and slightly hairy, with a distinct scent.

Solanum purpusii produces small, yellowish berries that are edible but contain toxic compounds.
