Strumaria hardyana

Strumaria hardyana, commonly known as the hardy bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue or violet in color, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, upright stems that are often branched, and its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped. It prefers well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it a versatile addition to gardens.

Strumaria hardyana is valued for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of garden conditions.
