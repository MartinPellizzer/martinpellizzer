Serruria stellata

Serruria stellata, commonly known as the star-flowered serruria, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a contrasting central stripe. Serruria stellata is a popular choice for rock gardens due to its ornamental value and adaptability to dry conditions.
