Solidago × snarskisii

Solidago × snarskisii, commonly known as the Snarskisii goldenrod, is a hybrid perennial herb native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, bright yellow inflorescences that bloom in late summer to early fall. The plant typically grows in moist to wet soils in open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. It has lance-shaped to linear leaves and a robust root system that helps it thrive in diverse environmental conditions.

Solidago × snarskisii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
