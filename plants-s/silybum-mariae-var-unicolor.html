Silybum mariae var. unicolor

Silybum mariae var. unicolor, commonly known as the yellow milk thistle, is a variant of the common milk thistle.

It is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region and belongs to the Asteraceae family. The plant is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and yellow flowers, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in dry, disturbed soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.

The plant contains bioactive compounds, making it of interest in herbal medicine.
