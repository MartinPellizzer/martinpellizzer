Specklinia platystylis

Specklinia platystylis, commonly known as the broad-styled speckled orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often speckled foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pale green to yellowish in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Specklinia platystylis is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
