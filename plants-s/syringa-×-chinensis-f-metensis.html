Syringa × chinensis f. metensis

Syringa × chinensis f. metensis, commonly known as the Chinese lilac or Metens hybrid.

This plant is a deciduous shrub or small tree characterized by its fragrant, clustered flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It has dark green, oval-shaped leaves that turn yellow in the fall. The bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

It is native to China and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
