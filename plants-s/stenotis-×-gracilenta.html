Stenotis × gracilenta

Stenotis × gracilenta, commonly known as the slender stenotis, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Acanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often grown for its vibrant blooms and unique foliage.
