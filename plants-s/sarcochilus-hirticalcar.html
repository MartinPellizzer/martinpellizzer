Sarcochilus hirticalcar

Sarcochilus hirticalcar, commonly known as the hairy-stemmed sarcochilus, is a terrestrial orchid native to New Zealand.

It features a distinctive, hairy stem that distinguishes it from other members of the genus. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers with a pleasant fragrance. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique morphology and habitat make it a rare and ecologically significant species.
