Stelis elegantissima

Stelis elegantissima, commonly known as the elegant stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are elongated and glossy, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Stelis elegantissima is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
