Stelis alata

Stelis alata, commonly known as the feathered stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated inflorescence that bears numerous small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Stelis alata is valued in horticulture for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
