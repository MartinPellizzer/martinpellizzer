Selaginella frondosa var. ciliata

Selaginella frondosa var. ciliata, commonly known as the ciliated spikemoss, is a species of spikemoss belonging to the family Selaginellaceae.

It is a low-growing, herbaceous plant that typically forms dense mats on rocky or sandy soils. The plant features small, scale-like leaves that are often adorned with cilia, giving it its common name. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing remarkable drought tolerance.

This variety is often found in regions with Mediterranean climates, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
