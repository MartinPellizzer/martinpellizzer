Schefflera klossii

Schefflera klossii, commonly known as the Kloss schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Indonesia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of compound leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows to a height of 3 to 5 meters and features smooth, grayish-brown bark. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, with leaflets that are oval and pointed.

Schefflera klossii prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
