Spiranthes chloroleuca

Spiranthes chloroleuca, commonly known as the greenish spire orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its delicate, spiraling arrangement of flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as wetlands and forest floors. Its leaves are grass-like and linear, while its flowers are small, greenish-white, and often clustered in a spiral pattern.

Spiranthes chloroleuca is known for its unique morphology and ecological importance in supporting local biodiversity.
