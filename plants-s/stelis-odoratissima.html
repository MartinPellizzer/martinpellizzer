Stelis odoratissima

Stelis odoratissima, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically produces showy, colorful blooms in shades of pink, white, and yellow. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, requiring high humidity and indirect sunlight.

Stelis odoratissima is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and delicate fragrance.
