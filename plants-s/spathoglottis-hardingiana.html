Spathoglottis hardingiana

Spathoglottis hardingiana, commonly known as the harding's spathoglottis, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is a terrestrial herb with a sympodial growth habit, characterized by its pseudobulbs and fleshy roots. The plant produces showy, lanceolate leaves and bears inflorescences with white to pale pink flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
