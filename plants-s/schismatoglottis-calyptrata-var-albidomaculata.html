Schismatoglottis calyptrata var. albidomaculata

Schismatoglottis calyptrata var. albidomaculata, commonly known as the white-spotted schismatoglottis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial herb with a rhizomatous root system and produces striking, lance-shaped leaves with a waxy texture. The plant is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, typically with a greenish-white coloration and a distinctive, hood-like structure.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a humid, well-drained environment.
