Spiranthes oaxacana

Spiranthes oaxacana, commonly known as the Oaxaca ladies' tresses, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, spiraling arrangement of flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, and it produces small, greenish seed pods.

Spiranthes oaxacana is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in its native habitat.
