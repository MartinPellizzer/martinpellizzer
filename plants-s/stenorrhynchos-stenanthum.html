Stenorrhynchos stenanthum

Stenorrhynchos stenanthum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rock orchid, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features linear, narrow leaves and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It is often found in mountainous regions and is known for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.

The species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological adaptations.
