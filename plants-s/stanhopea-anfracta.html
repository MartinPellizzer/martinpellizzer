Stanhopea anfracta

Stanhopea anfracta, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that display a complex, zigzag pattern in their arrangement. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are elongated and often have a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

Stanhopea anfracta is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and delicate fragrance.
