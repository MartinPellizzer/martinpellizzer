Schefflera metcalfiana var. langbianensis

Schefflera metcalfiana var. langbianensis, commonly known as the Langbian Schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Vietnam.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of compound leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland evergreen forests.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping in warm climates.
