Stapelia chinensis

Stapelia chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese Stapelia, is a succulent plant native to China.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed stems and waxy leaves. The plant produces striking, lantern-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. These flowers are highly fragrant and attract pollinators such as flies and beetles.

Stapelia chinensis is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
