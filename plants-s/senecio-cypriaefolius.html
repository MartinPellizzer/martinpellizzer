Senecio cypriaefolius

Senecio cypriaefolius, commonly known as the Cyprian leaf senecio, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, upright growth with narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil to thrive.

Due to its ornamental appeal, it is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens.
