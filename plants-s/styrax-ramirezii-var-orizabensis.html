Styrax ramirezii var. orizabensis

Styrax ramirezii var. orizabensis, commonly known as the Orizaba styrax, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the family Styracaceae and is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are glossy and elliptical, providing a striking contrast to its delicate blossoms.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic and aromatic qualities.
