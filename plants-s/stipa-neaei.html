Stipa neaei

Stipa neaei, commonly known as the Neaei feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and fluffy, silver-gray plumes that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with fine, delicate leaves that contribute to its ornamental value.

Stipa neaei is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
