Stenolobium glabrum

Stenolobium glabrum, commonly known as the smooth-branched legume, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in dry, rocky soils. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, which are followed by elongated, pod-like fruits. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a smooth texture.

Stenolobium glabrum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
