Senecio flagellisectus

Senecio flagellisectus, commonly known as the whipstick senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, whip-like stems that are often covered in fine hairs. Its leaves are linear and arranged alternately along the stem.

It produces small yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators.
