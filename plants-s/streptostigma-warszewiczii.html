Streptostigma warszewiczii

Streptostigma warszewiczii, commonly known as the Warszewicz's streptostigma, is a rare and distinctive member of the orchid family.

It is characterized by its unique, spirally twisted inflorescence and small, delicate flowers with intricate petal structures. Native to the mountainous regions of Central and South America, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant's botanical features make it a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.

Its conservation status is vulnerable due to habitat loss and limited distribution.
