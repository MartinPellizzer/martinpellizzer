Senecio dewildeorum

Senecio dewildeorum, commonly known as the Dewilde's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
