Spathiphyllum gracile

Spathiphyllum gracile, commonly known as the slender peace lily, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its narrow, dark green leaves and produces elegant, white spathes that enclose small, greenish-white flowers. The plant is evergreen and thrives in humid, shaded environments. It is popular as a houseplant due to its air-purifying qualities and low maintenance requirements.

Its botanical features make it a desirable addition to both indoor and outdoor gardens.
