Sasa variegata f. glabra

Sasa variegata f. glabra, commonly known as the smooth bamboo or variegated bamboo, is a variant of the Sasa variegata species.

It is a clump-forming shrub-like plant native to East Asia, particularly Japan. The plant features slender, smooth stems with a distinctive variegated leaf pattern, often showing white or yellow markings. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged in a compact, upright growth habit.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
