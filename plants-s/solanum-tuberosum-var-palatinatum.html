Solanum tuberosum var. palatinatum

Solanum tuberosum var. palatinatum, commonly known as the Purple Perennial Potato, is a botanical variant of the common potato.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple foliage and tubers that range in color from deep purple to red. This variety is typically grown as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal rather than for culinary use. The plant belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshade plants.

It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
