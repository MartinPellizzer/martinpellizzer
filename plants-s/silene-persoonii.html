Silene persoonii

Silene persoonii, commonly known as the Persoon's campion, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its showy pink or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a basal rosette, while its stems are upright and branched.

Silene persoonii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
