Schefflera yatesii

Schefflera yatesii, commonly known as the Yate's schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of compound leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments within its native habitat. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, with leaves that are usually arranged in whorls at the ends of the stems.

Schefflera yatesii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is occasionally cultivated as a houseplant.
