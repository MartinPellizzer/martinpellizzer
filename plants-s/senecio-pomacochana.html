Senecio pomacochana

Senecio pomacochana, commonly known as the Andean groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Senecio pomacochana is often used in traditional medicine by local communities for its potential therapeutic properties.
