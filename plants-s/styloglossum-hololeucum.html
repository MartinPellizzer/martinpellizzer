Styloglossum hololeucum

Styloglossum hololeucum, commonly known as the white-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, white stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a spike, which bloom during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Styloglossum hololeucum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
