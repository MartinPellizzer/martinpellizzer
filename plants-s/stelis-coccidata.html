Stelis coccidata

Stelis coccidata, commonly known as the red-tipped orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit and produces vibrant, showy flowers with red-tipped petals. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to green with a distinctive red or orange tip.

Stelis coccidata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions.
