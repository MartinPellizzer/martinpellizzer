Strombocactus schmiedickeanus

Strombocactus schmiedickeanus, commonly known as the yellow star cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a vibrant splash of color to its desert habitat. The plant has a cylindrical stem with areoles that bear clusters of spines, typical of the Cactaceae family. Its leaves are modified into spines, and it stores water in its stem, adapting to its harsh environment.

Strombocactus schmiedickeanus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and hardy nature.
