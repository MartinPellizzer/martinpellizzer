Senecio rydbergii

Senecio rydbergii, commonly known as Rydberg's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright stems and yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are usually gray-green and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Senecio rydbergii is often found in open areas such as grasslands and sagebrush steppe.
