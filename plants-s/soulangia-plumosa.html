Soulangia plumosa

Soulangia plumosa, commonly known as the feathered bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. The plant has slender, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

Soulangia plumosa is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
