Saxifraga diversifolia f. alpina

Saxifraga diversifolia f. alpina, commonly known as the alpine saxifrage, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It thrives in rocky and alpine environments, where it forms dense mats of foliage. The plant features small, clustered leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tint. Its flowers are white or pale pink, appearing in early summer and adding a delicate touch to its habitat.

Saxifraga diversifolia f. alpina is well adapted to cold climates and is often found in high-altitude meadows and slopes.
