Serialbizzia attopeuensis

Serialbizzia attopeuensis, commonly known as the Attopeuensis cycad, is a unique plant species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Cycadaceae family and is characterized by its stout, unbranched trunk and large, feather-like leaves. The plant produces cones rather than flowers, which are typical of cycads. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires a warm, humid climate.

Due to its restricted distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
