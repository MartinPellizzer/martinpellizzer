Senecio subulatus var. salsus

Senecio subulatus var. salsus, commonly known as the Salsus Senecio, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of narrow, needle-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a subtle ornamental touch. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
