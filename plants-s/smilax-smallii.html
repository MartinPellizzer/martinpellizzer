Smilax smallii

Smilax smallii, commonly known as the Florida smilax, is a climbing vine native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its woody stems and spiny, arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by bright red berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and swampy areas.

Smilax smallii is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its hardy nature and unique appearance.
