Scutellaria javanica

Scutellaria javanica, commonly known as the Java skullcap, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and shrublands.

Scutellaria javanica is valued for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
