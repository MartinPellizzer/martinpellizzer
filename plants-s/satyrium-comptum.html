Satyrium comptum

Satyrium comptum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved orchid, is a species native to the southeastern United States.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid with slender stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive hooded lip. It typically blooms in late spring to early summer.

Satyrium comptum thrives in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and wetlands.
