Sprekelia cybister

Sprekelia cybister, commonly known as the desert bell, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that help it conserve water in arid environments. It produces showy, clustered blooms that attract pollinators such as bees and birds.

Sprekelia cybister is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
