Solanum cundinamarcae

Solanum cundinamarcae, commonly known as the Cundinamarca tomato, is a species native to Colombia.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its hairy stems and lobed leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into red, round fruits resembling tomatoes. It is adapted to grow in tropical and subtropical climates.

Due to its ornamental value and potential culinary uses, it is occasionally cultivated in home gardens.
