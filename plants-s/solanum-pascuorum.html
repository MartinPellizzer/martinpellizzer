Solanum pascuorum

Solanum pascuorum, commonly known as the meadow nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to lanceolate in shape. Its small, yellowish-green flowers are clustered in umbels and bloom in the summer months.

The fruit is a spherical berry that turns from green to red as it matures, containing several seeds.
