Saxifraga aizoon

Saxifraga aizoon, commonly known as the rock saxifrage, is a perennial flowering plant native to arctic and alpine regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and clusters of small, white or pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to cold climates. It has a compact growth habit, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Saxifraga aizoon is also known for its ornamental value and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
