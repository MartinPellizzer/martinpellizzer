Strelitzia regalis

Strelitzia regalis, commonly known as the Bird of Paradise, is a striking tropical plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, sword-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. The plant produces showy, colorful inflorescences that resemble a bird in flight, with vibrant orange and blue petals. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Strelitzia regalis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a symbol of resilience and beauty in botanical gardens.
