Solanum martiusianum

Solanum martiusianum, commonly known as the Martius nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or purple in color. Its fruit is a small, yellowish berry that contains several seeds.

Solanum martiusianum is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in shaded, moist environments.
