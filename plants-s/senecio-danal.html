Senecio danal

Senecio danal, commonly known as the Danal groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It produces small, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow.

Senecio danal is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, where it contributes to local biodiversity.
