Stellilabium bullpenense

Stellilabium bullpenense, commonly known as the star-labium peninsular plant, is a unique species native to the arid regions of the Peninsular region.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of violet and white. The plant has narrow, lanceolate leaves that are adapted to reduce water loss in its harsh environment. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to access deep underground water sources.

Stellilabium bullpenense plays a vital role in the local ecosystem by providing nectar for pollinators and habitat for small desert organisms.
