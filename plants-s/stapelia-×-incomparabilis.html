Stapelia × incomparabilis

Stapelia × incomparabilis, commonly known as the giant star stapelia, is a hybrid succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, star-shaped flowers that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter, which are typically yellow with reddish-brown markings. The plant produces fleshy, greenish-gray leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water retention. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight to flourish.

This unique species is often cultivated for its striking appearance and is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
