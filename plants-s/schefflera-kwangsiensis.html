Schefflera kwangsiensis

Schefflera kwangsiensis, commonly known as the Guangxi schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to southern China.

It is characterized by its compound, palmate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its adaptability makes it suitable for both indoor and outdoor environments.
