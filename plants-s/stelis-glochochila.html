Stelis glochochila

Stelis glochochila, commonly known as the glochilah orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small epiphytic plant that grows on trees in tropical rainforests. The plant has linear, dark green leaves and produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate floral structure.

Stelis glochochila is often found in humid, shaded environments and requires specific conditions to thrive.
