Stelis tomcroatii

Stelis tomcroatii, commonly known as the Tomcroatii orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant has a compact, pseudobulbous structure with narrow, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in high humidity and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich substrates for optimal growth.

Stelis tomcroatii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
