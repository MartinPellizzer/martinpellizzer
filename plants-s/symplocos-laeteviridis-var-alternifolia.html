Symplocos laeteviridis var. alternifolia

Symplocos laeteviridis var. alternifolia, commonly known as the alternate-leaf green symplocos, is a variant of the green symplocos species.

It is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions, characterized by its alternate leaf arrangement. The leaves are typically elliptical or ovate, with a smooth or slightly serrated margin. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible berries that are often consumed by wildlife.

Its botanical features make it an important component of its native ecosystems.
