Sagittipetalum palawanense

Sagittipetalum palawanense, commonly known as the Palawan arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Araceae and is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking inflorescences with a spathe and spadix, typical of the arum family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
