Sulcorebutia tunariensis

Sulcorebutia tunariensis, commonly known as the Tunariensis Pincushion Cactus, is a species of flowering cactus native to Bolivia.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its dense, spiny stems that form a spherical or cylindrical shape. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a desirable addition to succulent collections.
