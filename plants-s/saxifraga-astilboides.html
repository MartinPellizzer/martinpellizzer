Saxifraga astilboides

Saxifraga astilboides, commonly known as the white saxifrage, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It features upright, branched stems that bear clusters of small, white, showy flowers. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.

Saxifraga astilboides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
