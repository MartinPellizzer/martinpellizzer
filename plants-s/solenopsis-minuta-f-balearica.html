Solenopsis minuta f. balearica

Solenopsis minuta f. balearica, commonly known as the Balearic tiny fire ant, is a variant of the fire ant species Solenopsis minuta.

It is a small, aggressive ant species native to the Balearic Islands in the Mediterranean. The plant aspect of this species refers to its role as a pest in agricultural and horticultural settings. It is characterized by its dark reddish-brown color and small, segmented body.

This ant is known for its ability to form large colonies and can cause significant damage to crops and native plant life.
