Spiraea orbicularis

Spiraea orbicularis, commonly known as the roundleaf spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features small, white to pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. The plant has ovate, dark green leaves that are rounded at the base, giving it its common name. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Spiraea orbicularis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
