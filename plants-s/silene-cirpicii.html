Silene cirpicii

Silene cirpicii, commonly known as the Cirpicii catchfly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous species that typically grows in rocky or limestone soils. The plant features small, pink or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Silene cirpicii is often found in dry, open habitats such as cliffs and slopes.
