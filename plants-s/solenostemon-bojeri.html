Solenostemon bojeri

Solenostemon bojeri, commonly known as the flame bush, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often resembling flames. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually hairy and have a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its striking appearance.

Solenostemon bojeri is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
