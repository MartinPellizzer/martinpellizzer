Sempervivum marmoreum subsp. ballsii

Sempervivum marmoreum subsp.

ballsii, commonly known as the Rosy Glow Stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. It features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with a distinctive pink or red hue along the edges.

The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white during the summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant color make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
