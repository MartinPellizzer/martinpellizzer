Sansevieria hallii

Sansevieria hallii, commonly known as the Hall's snake plant, is a species of flowering plant in the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to the island of Madagascar and is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with subtle variegation. The plant produces small, white, tubular flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, which are often hidden among the foliage. Sansevieria hallii is highly drought-tolerant and thrives in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ability to purify indoor air has also contributed to its widespread cultivation and appreciation.
