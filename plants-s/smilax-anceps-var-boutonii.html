Smilax anceps var. boutonii

Smilax anceps var. boutonii, commonly known as the yellow smilax, is a climbing shrub native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its yellowish stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in suitable environments.
