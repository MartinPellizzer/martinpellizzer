Solanum × juzepczukii f. lucki-pechuma

Solanum × juzepczukii f. lucki-pechuma, commonly known as the Lucki-Pechuma tomato, is a hybrid cultivar within the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its medium-sized, red-fruited tomatoes that are typically used for both fresh consumption and culinary purposes. The plant exhibits a bushy growth habit with hairy stems and lobed leaves typical of the Solanum genus. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its flavor and versatility in various cooking applications.
