Stapelia asterias

Stapelia asterias, commonly known as the star-shaped stapelia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, star-like flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant has thick, fleshy stems that store water, allowing it to survive in arid conditions. Its flowers are often fragrant and attract pollinators such as flies and beetles.

Stapelia asterias is a popular choice for succulent gardens due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
