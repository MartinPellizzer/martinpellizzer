Santolina erecta

Santolina erecta, commonly known as Spanish santolina, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features compact, upright growth with silvery-gray foliage that is aromatic and slightly fuzzy to the touch. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Santolina erecta is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to attract beneficial insects.
