Scutellaria ovalifolia subsp. mollis

Scutellaria ovalifolia subsp.

mollis, commonly known as soft ovalleaf skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the southeastern United States. It typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and features opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often softly hairy.

The plant produces blue to violet flowers in whorled clusters, which bloom from late spring to early summer. Its root system is fibrous, and it is often found in shaded or partially shaded environments.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
