Stelis herbiola

Stelis herbiola, commonly known as the orchid vine, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a climbing epiphytic plant that thrives in humid, tropical environments. The plant features thin, wiry stems with lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green. Its flowers are small and fragrant, typically appearing in clusters along the stems.

Stelis herbiola is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to climb various structures.
