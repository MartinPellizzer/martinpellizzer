Silene koycegizensis

Silene koycegizensis, commonly known as the Koycegiz silene, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its pink to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils in mountainous regions. It has slender stems and small, lance-shaped leaves.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
