Scabiosa repens

Scabiosa repens, commonly known as baby blue eyes, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe and western Asia.

It features small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. The plant has a mat-forming habit with wiry stems and fuzzy, heart-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Scabiosa repens is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
