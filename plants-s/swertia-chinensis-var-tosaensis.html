Swertia chinensis var. tosaensis

Swertia chinensis var. tosaensis, commonly known as Tosa Swertia, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its slender stems and dark green, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, blue to purple flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and traditional herbal practices.
