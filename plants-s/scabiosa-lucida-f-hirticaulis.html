Scabiosa lucida f. hirticaulis

Scabiosa lucida f. hirticaulis, commonly known as the hairy scabious, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caprifoliaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and clusters of small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Native to the Mediterranean region, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.
