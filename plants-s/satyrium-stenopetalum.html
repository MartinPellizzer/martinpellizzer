Satyrium stenopetalum

Satyrium stenopetalum, commonly known as the narrow-petaled orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, narrow petals and sepals, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments within rainforests. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, often displaying a range of colors from white to pale pink.

Satyrium stenopetalum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
