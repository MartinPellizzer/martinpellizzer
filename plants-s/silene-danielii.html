Silene danielii

Silene danielii, commonly known as Daniel's campion, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Alps.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds.
