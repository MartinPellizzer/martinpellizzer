Serruria arenaria

Serruria arenaria, commonly known as the sandstone daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub with small, fleshy leaves adapted to arid conditions. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Due to its resilience and ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens in regions with similar climates.
