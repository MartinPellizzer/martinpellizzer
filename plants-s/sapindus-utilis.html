Sapindus utilis

Sapindus utilis, commonly known as the Chinese soapberry tree, is a deciduous tree native to China and Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Sapindaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellowish flowers. The fruit is a spherical, spiny capsule that turns red when mature, containing a single seed. The tree is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used traditionally for its fruit's mild soap-like properties.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
