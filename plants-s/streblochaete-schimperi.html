Streblochaete schimperi

Streblochaete schimperi, commonly known as the African orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical Africa.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its slender, erect stems and linear, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in a raceme. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some horticultural collections.
