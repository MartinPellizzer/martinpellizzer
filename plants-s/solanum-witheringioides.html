Solanum witheringioides

Solanum witheringioides, commonly known as the Withering's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with hairy stems and small, greenish-white flowers. Its fruits are berries that mature to a deep purple color, and the plant is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in horticultural settings.
