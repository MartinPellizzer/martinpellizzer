Simarubopsis kerstingii

Simarubopsis kerstingii, commonly known as Kersting's simarouba, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Simaroubaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces fruit that contains several seeds, which are often dispersed by animals.

It is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential anti-inflammatory and antiparasitic effects.
