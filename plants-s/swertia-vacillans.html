Swertia vacillans

Swertia vacillans, commonly known as the trembling swertia, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its slender stems and small, blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest understories. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture, while its flowers bloom in clusters.

Swertia vacillans is valued in traditional medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
