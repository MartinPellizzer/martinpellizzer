Smithia setosissima

Smithia setosissima, commonly known as the woolly smithia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine environments. Its unique appearance makes it a subject of interest in botanical studies and horticulture.

Smithia setosissima is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
