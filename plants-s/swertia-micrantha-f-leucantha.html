Swertia micrantha f. leucantha

Swertia micrantha f. leucantha, commonly known as the white-flowered swertia, is a variant of the Swertia micrantha species.

It is a perennial herb native to the Himalayan regions of India and Nepal. The plant features slender stems and lance-shaped leaves with a bluish-green hue. Its most distinguishing feature is the white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
