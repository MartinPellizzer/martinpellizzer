Salacia erythroxyloides

Salacia erythroxyloides, commonly known as redwood salacia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Salicaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including countries like India, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its small, woody shrub form and produces yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture.

The plant is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value and has been studied for potential medicinal properties.
