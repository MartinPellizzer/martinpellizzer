Sarcostemma grandiflorum

Sarcostemma grandiflorum, commonly known as the large-flowered milkweed, is a climbing plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with a distinctive waxy coating. Sarcostemma grandiflorum is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and waste areas.
