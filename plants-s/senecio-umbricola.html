Senecio umbricola

Senecio umbricola, commonly known as the shade-loving groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces small, yellow composite flowers. The plant has dark green, velvety leaves that are often glandular and can emit a slight odor. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

Senecio umbricola is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
