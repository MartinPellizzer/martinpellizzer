Stelis strigosa

Stelis strigosa, commonly known as the hairy stelis, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and hairy stems, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically arranged in clusters. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks.

Stelis strigosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
