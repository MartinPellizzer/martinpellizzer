Solanum adoense var. schweinfurthii

Solanum adoense var. schweinfurthii, commonly known as the Schweinfurth's eggplant, is a tropical species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and dark green color. Its flowers are yellow with purple veins, and it produces small, round fruits that are often used in local cuisines.

This variant is notable for its ornamental value and culinary versatility in its native regions.
