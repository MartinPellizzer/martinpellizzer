Stipa Zalesskii Subsp. Maeotica

Stipa zalesskii subsp. maeotica is a unique grass species native to the steppes of southern Russia and Ukraine. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing remarkable adaptability to harsh conditions. This subspecies is distinguished by its tall, slender stems and fluffy seed heads that sway gracefully in the wind. Its ecological significance lies in its role as a pioneer species in degraded soils and its importance to local wildlife. Despite its hardiness, it remains a relatively understudied plant in botanical research.
