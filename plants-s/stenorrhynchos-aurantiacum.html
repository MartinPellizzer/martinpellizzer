Stenorrhynchos aurantiacum

Stenorrhynchos aurantiacum, commonly known as the orange-flowered orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its striking orange-red flowers that bloom during the warm season. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its unique floral structure and vibrant color make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
