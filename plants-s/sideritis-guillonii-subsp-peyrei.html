Sideritis guillonii subsp. peyrei

Sideritis guillonii subsp.

peyrei, commonly known as Peyre's ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs.

The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It is characterized by its hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

This subspecies is often found in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments.
