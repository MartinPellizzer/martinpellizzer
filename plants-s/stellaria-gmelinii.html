Stellaria gmelinii

Stellaria gmelinii, commonly known as lesser starwort, is a low-growing annual or biennial herbaceous plant native to Eurasia.

It features small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically from late spring to early summer. The plant has thin, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, along roadsides, and in wetlands.

Stellaria gmelinii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
