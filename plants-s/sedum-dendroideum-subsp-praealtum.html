Sedum dendroideum subsp. praealtum

Sedum dendroideum subsp.

praealtum, commonly known as the tall stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Europe. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and fleshy, lance-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color.

The plant produces clusters of star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for gardeners in arid climates.
