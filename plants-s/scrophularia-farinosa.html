Scrophularia farinosa

Scrophularia farinosa, commonly known as the hoary scrophularia, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. The plant features hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. Its flowers are tubular, pink to purple in color, and appear in clusters during the summer months.

Scrophularia farinosa is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
