Seseli Montanum Subsp. Tommasinii

Alpine seseli, scientifically known as Seseli montanum subsp. tommasinii, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high altitude regions of the Alps. This subspecies is characterized by its small, white flowers and finely divided leaves that adapt to the harsh mountain environment. It thrives in rocky soils and cool climates, often found in alpine meadows and slopes. Due to its limited distribution and sensitivity to environmental changes, it is considered a vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are essential to protect this unique plant from habitat loss and climate impacts.
