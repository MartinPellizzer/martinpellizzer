Scutellaria minor var. strailii

Scutellaria minor var. strailii, commonly known as the narrow-leaved skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by square stems and aromatic foliage. The plant produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.
