Senecio fortunatus

Senecio fortunatus, commonly known as Fortune's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with narrow, linear leaves and produces small yellow flowers in clusters. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
