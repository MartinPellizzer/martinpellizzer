Sphaeradenia fosbergii

Sphaeradenia fosbergii, commonly known as the Fosberg's shield fern, is a member of the Polypodiaceae family.

It is a small, terrestrial fern characterized by its compact, cushion-like growth habit. The plant features rhizomatous stems with closely spaced, lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. Its fronds are typically bipinnate, with a delicate, lace-like appearance.

Native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, it thrives in moist, shaded environments.
