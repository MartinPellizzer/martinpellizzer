Salvia cavaleriei var. cavaleriei

Salvia cavaleriei var. cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese sage, is a perennial herb native to China.

It features upright stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of violet to purple flowers during the summer months, which are attractive to pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens or as a border plant.
