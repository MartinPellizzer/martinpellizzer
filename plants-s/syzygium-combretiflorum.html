Syzygium combretiflorum

Syzygium combretiflorum, commonly known as the African star fruit or yellow mangosteen, is a tropical plant native to Africa.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, white or pink flowers. The plant produces small, yellow fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are evergreen and have a serrated edge, while its bark is rough and fibrous.

Syzygium combretiflorum is often found in moist, shaded areas within tropical forests.
