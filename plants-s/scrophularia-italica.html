Scrophularia italica

Scrophularia italica, commonly known as Italian figwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and open woodlands. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are tubular and usually pink or purple, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems.

Scrophularia italica is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
