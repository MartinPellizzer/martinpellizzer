Stenaria umbratilis var. umbratilis

Stenaria umbratilis var. umbratilis, commonly known as the shade-loving aster, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette, adapted for low-light conditions. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of purple or blue, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
