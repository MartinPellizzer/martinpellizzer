Stellaria lycopodioides

Stellaria lycopodioides, commonly known as the starwort, is a small flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit, with slender stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are alternate and linear, often covered in fine hairs.

Stellaria lycopodioides is known for its delicate appearance and its ability to spread through rhizomes, forming dense mats in suitable habitats.
