Senecio sylvicola

Senecio sylvicola, commonly known as the forest senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and woodlands. It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters.

Senecio sylvicola is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in partially shaded conditions.
