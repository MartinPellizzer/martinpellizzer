Senecio mindoroensis

Senecio mindoroensis, commonly known as Mindoro groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small yellow flowers arranged in clusters.

Due to its restricted distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
