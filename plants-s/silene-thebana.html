Silene thebana

Silene thebana, commonly known as the Theban campion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous annual or biennial plant that typically grows in rocky or dry soils. The plant produces clusters of pink or white flowers with five petals, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

Silene thebana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
