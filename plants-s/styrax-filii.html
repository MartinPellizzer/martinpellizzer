Styrax filii

Styrax filii, commonly known as the Chinese frankincense tree, is a deciduous flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Styracaceae and is characterized by its fragrant, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces a resinous sap, which has been historically used in traditional medicine and incense. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a glossy appearance.

Styrax filii typically grows in forested areas and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
