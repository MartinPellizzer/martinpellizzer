Senecio kandambren

Senecio kandambren, commonly known as the Kandambren groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage is generally gray-green with a slightly hairy texture, and it produces small yellow flowers in the summer months.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
