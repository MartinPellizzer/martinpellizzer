Sphaeranthus dauensis

Sphaeranthus dauensis, commonly known as the Dau's globe flower, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.
