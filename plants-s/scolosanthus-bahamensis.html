Scolosanthus bahamensis

Scolosanthus bahamensis, commonly known as the Bahamian starflower, is a flowering plant native to the Bahamas.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale yellow. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it has a compact growth habit.

Scolosanthus bahamensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in local ecosystems.
