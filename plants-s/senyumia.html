Senyumia

Senyumia, commonly known as the "laughing plant," is a unique species native to certain tropical regions.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of red, yellow, and orange. The plant has a compact growth habit with broad, waxy leaves that are slightly serrated along the edges. Senyumia thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its botanical name reflects its cheerful appearance, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
