Statice globulariifolia var. glauca

Statice globulariifolia var. glauca, commonly known as blue glass statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, globular flower heads that are typically blue to purple in color. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are tightly clustered, giving it a distinctive, glass-like appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its drought tolerance and long-lasting blooms make it a favored ornamental plant in horticulture.
