Steirachne Deccanensis

Steirachne deccanensis is a rare grass species native to the Deccan region of India. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in arid and semi-arid environments. This plant is part of the Poaceae family and is known for its unique morphological features. Due to habitat loss and climate change, it faces increasing threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this rare and ecologically significant species.
