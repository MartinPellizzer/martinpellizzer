Spiraea media subsp. sericea

Spiraea media subsp.

sericea, commonly known as silver weeping spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States. It is characterized by its slender, arching branches that cascade with clusters of white flowers in early summer.

The plant has finely textured, silver-gray foliage that adds ornamental value throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil. Spiraea media subsp.

sericea is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal and easy maintenance.
