Sulcorebutia verticillacantha var. verticosior

Sulcorebutia verticillacantha var. verticosior, commonly known as the tall-stemmed crassula, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features a short, thick stem with dense clusters of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with a slight blue or silver hue. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring or early summer, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. Its slow growth rate and ability to thrive in well-drained soil make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.

The variety is distinguished by its taller stem and more elongated leaves compared to other Sulcorebutia species.
