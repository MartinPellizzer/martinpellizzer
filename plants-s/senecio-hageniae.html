Senecio hageniae

Senecio hageniae, commonly known as Hagenia's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in dense clusters.

Senecio hageniae is often used in rock gardens due to its hardy nature and ornamental appeal.
