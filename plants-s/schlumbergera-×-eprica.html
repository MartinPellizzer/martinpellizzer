Schlumbergera × eprica

Schlumbergera × eprica, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a hybrid epiphytic cactus native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its segmented, branching stems that bear vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is popular as a decorative houseplant, especially during the holiday season.

Schlumbergera × eprica is known for its ability to bloom multiple times a year under proper care.
