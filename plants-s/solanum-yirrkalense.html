Solanum yirrkalense

Solanum yirrkalense, commonly known as the Yirrkalense nightshade, is a flowering plant native to northern Australia.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and oval-shaped, hairy leaves. It typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, often in sandy soils.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
