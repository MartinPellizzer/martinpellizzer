Spathoglottis portus-finschii

Spathoglottis portus-finschii, commonly known as the Finsch's spathoglottis, is a herbaceous orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, humid environments such as forest undergrowth. The plant features a pseudobulb with a single, lance-shaped leaf and produces a tall, branched inflorescence. Its flowers are showy, with a distinctive spatulate lip and vibrant colors, often in shades of yellow and orange.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and requires specific care to thrive.
