Sida flexuosa

Sida flexuosa, commonly known as the flexible sida, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a herbaceous annual or perennial that typically grows in moist to wet habitats. The plant features alternate, ovate leaves with hairy surfaces and small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. Its root system is fibrous, and it often thrives in disturbed soils.

Sida flexuosa is known for its ability to spread rapidly, making it both a weed and a potential ecological indicator species.
