Silene saldanhensis

Silene saldanhensis, commonly known as the Saldanha's campion, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its pink to purple flowers and slender stems. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny environments. It is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Silene saldanhensis is also valued for its ornamental qualities in horticulture.
