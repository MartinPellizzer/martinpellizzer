Sideritis × mauritii

Sideritis × mauritii, commonly known as the hybrid mint shrub, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically pink or purple, arranged in whorled clusters, and bloom during the summer months.

Sideritis × mauritii is valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
