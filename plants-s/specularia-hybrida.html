Specularia hybrida

Specularia hybrida, commonly known as the evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow or orange flowers that open in the late afternoon and close at dawn. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. Its leaves are heart-shaped and hairy, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

Specularia hybrida is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine.
