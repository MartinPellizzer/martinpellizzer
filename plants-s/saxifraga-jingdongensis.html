Saxifraga jingdongensis

Saxifraga jingdongensis, commonly known as the Jingdong saxifrage, is a rare flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, clustered flowers. The plant typically features waxy, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of hair. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to high-altitude environments.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
