Savia andringitrana

Savia andringitrana, commonly known as the Madagascar milkweed, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family, which includes many plants with milky sap. The plant features opposite, oval-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Its milky latex is a characteristic feature, often used in traditional medicine.
