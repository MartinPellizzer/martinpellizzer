Serjania communis var. glabra

Serjania communis var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth serjania, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually smooth and glossy in texture. Its flowers are small and clustered, often with a yellowish or greenish hue.

The fruit is a woody capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.
