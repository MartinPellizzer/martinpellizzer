Sauropus delavayi

Sauropus delavayi, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or Delavay's evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit and produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by orange-red fruits. It thrives in humid, warm environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
