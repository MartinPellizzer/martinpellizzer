Scrophularia decomposita subsp. latifolia

Scrophularia decomposita subsp.

latifolia, commonly known as broadleaf scrophularia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States. It is a member of the Scrophulariaceae family, which includes many species with showy, often tubular flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. It produces violet to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

Its leaves are broad and ovate, with a hairy texture that helps it retain moisture in its native environment.
