Sideritis hirsuta var. granatensis

Sideritis hirsuta var. granatensis, commonly known as Granatense Heath, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in spikes. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or limestone habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and herbal gardens.
