Scaphyglottis Graminifolia

Scaphyglottis graminifolia is a rare and fascinating flowering plant native to the wetlands of southern Mexico. This member of the orchid family is known for its delicate, grass-like leaves and striking, pale pink blooms. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found near streams and in swampy areas. Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and is the subject of conservation efforts. Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it a valuable subject for botanical study.
