Swertia rosea

Swertia rosea, commonly known as the rose gentian, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Swertia rosea is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
