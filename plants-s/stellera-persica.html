Stellera persica

Stellera persica, commonly known as Persian starflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

Stellera persica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate, fragrant blooms.
