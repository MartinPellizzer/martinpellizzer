Spinacia oleracea var. moenchii

Spinacia oleracea var. moenchii, commonly known as the broad-leafed spinach, is a cultivated variety of spinach characterized by its large, robust leaves.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is valued for its high nutritional content, including vitamins A, C, and K. The plant thrives in cool climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. Its leaves are typically harvested before flowering to ensure maximum flavor and nutrient retention.

This variety is often used in salads and cooked dishes due to its mild, earthy taste.
