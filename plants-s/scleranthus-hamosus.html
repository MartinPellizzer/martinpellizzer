Scleranthus hamosus

Scleranthus hamosus, commonly known as the common scarlet pimpernel, is an annual herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine hairs and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers with five petals, typically blooming from late spring to early autumn. It thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and waste areas.

Scleranthus hamosus is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to colonize various environments.
