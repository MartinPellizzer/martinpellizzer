Sickingia tinctoria

Sickingia tinctoria, commonly known as the velvet plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a velvety texture. The plant produces striking, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Sickingia tinctoria is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
