Silene Grisebachii

Silene grisebachii is a rare and striking flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Tibetan Plateau. It is known for its unique pinkish-purple blooms that appear during the short summer season. This species thrives in harsh, arid environments where few plants can survive. Due to its limited distribution and habitat threats, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this delicate and beautiful species.
