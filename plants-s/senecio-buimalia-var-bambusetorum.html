Senecio buimalia var. bambusetorum

Senecio buimalia var. bambusetorum, commonly known as the bamboo sedum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, grass-like leaves that are typically green with a bluish tinge. The plant produces tall, branched stems that bear yellow flowers in the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with full sun exposure.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
