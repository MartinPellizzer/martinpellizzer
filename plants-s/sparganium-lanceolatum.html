Sparganium lanceolatum

Sparganium lanceolatum, commonly known as narrowleaf burr rush, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is characterized by its tall, grass-like stems and produces spike-like clusters of small, spadix-like flowers. Its seeds are enclosed in spiky, burr-like structures that aid in seed dispersal.

Sparganium lanceolatum is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support diverse wildlife.
