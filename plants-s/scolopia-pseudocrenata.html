Scolopia pseudocrenata

Scolopia pseudocrenata, commonly known as the pink-rooted senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant has a distinctive reddish-brown root system, which is a key feature in its identification. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.
