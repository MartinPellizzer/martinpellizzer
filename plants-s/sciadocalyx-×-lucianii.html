Sciadocalyx × lucianii

Sciadocalyx × lucianii, commonly known as the hybrid flame vine, is a flowering plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is a hybrid between Sciadocalyx morio and Sciadocalyx stamineus, resulting in a unique combination of traits from both parent species. The plant is characterized by its vibrant red to orange tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees. It thrives in warm, tropical climates and requires well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes in suitable regions.
