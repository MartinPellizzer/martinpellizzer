Sansevieria fischeri

Sansevieria fischeri, commonly known as the Fischer's snake plant, is a species of succulent native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with light green or yellowish margins. The plant produces small, white flowers on slender stalks during the growing season. It is highly drought-tolerant and thrives in low-light conditions, making it a popular houseplant.

Its ability to purify indoor air has also contributed to its widespread cultivation.
