Scutellaria angrenica

Scutellaria angrenica, commonly known as the Armenian skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Armenia and Turkey.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes plants known for their aromatic properties. The plant features square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in rocky or woodland habitats and is often found in high-altitude environments.

Scutellaria angrenica is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
