Schizocodon soldanelloides var. ilicifolius

Schizocodon soldanelloides var. ilicifolius, commonly known as the spurgeleaf spurge, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spurge-like appearance with yellow flowers and milky sap. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

This variant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
