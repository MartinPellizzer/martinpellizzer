Sinobambusa intermedia

Sinobambusa intermedia, commonly known as the intermediate bamboo, is a species of grass-like flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, hollow stems and dense foliage. This plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests and stream sides. Its leaves are elongated and narrow, often with a bluish-green hue, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters.

Sinobambusa intermedia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
