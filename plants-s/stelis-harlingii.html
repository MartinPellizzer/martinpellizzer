Stelis harlingii

Stelis harlingii, commonly known as the Harling's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic nature. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Stelis harlingii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
