Spathodea nilotica

Spathodea nilotica, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy, orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a striking appearance. The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, and its bark is smooth and grayish in color. It thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant floral display.

The tree is also valued for its fast growth and ability to provide shade in urban and rural settings.
