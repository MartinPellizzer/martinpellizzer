Statice cancellata var. japygica

Statice cancellata var. japygica, commonly known as the pink statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, showy pink or purple flowers. The plant produces numerous slender, spike-like inflorescences that bloom in late summer to early autumn. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs.

It is a hardy perennial that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens and floral arrangements.
