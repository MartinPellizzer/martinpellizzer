Stemona mairei

Stemona mairei, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a perennial herb native to China.

It features slender, upright stems that are often covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue, arranged in clusters. Its leaves are lanceolate, with a slightly serrated edge, and are arranged alternately along the stem.

Stemona mairei is valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
