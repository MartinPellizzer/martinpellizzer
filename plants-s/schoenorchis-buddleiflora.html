Schoenorchis buddleiflora

Schoenorchis buddleiflora, commonly known as the butterfly orchid, is a rare and delicate orchid species native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that resemble butterflies. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in mountainous regions. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the forest floor.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species.
