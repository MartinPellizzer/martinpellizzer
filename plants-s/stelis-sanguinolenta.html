Stelis sanguinolenta

Stelis sanguinolenta, commonly known as the blood-red orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, red or orange flowers with a distinctive lip that attracts pollinators. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
