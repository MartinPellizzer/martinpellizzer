Stelis polycarpa

Stelis polycarpa, commonly known as the many-fruited orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant produces multiple fruits, which are typically ellipsoid and contain numerous seeds. Stelis polycarpa thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing on trees or rocks.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
