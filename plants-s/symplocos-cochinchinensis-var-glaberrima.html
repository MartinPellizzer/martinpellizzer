Symplocos cochinchinensis var. glaberrima

Symplocos cochinchinensis var. glaberrima, commonly known as the smooth-skin symplocos, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate to elliptical in shape. Its flowers are small and white, often clustered in inflorescences, and it produces small, fleshy berries as fruit.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional medicinal uses in local communities.
