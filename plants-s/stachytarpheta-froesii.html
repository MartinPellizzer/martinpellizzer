Stachytarpheta froesii

Stachytarpheta froesii, commonly known as the Brazilian blue mistflower, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue-violet bract-like flowers that resemble mist. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and features opposite, ovate leaves with hairy surfaces. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique appearance and adaptability contribute to its popularity in both landscape design and horticultural collections.
