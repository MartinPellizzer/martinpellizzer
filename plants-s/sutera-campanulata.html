Sutera campanulata

Sutera campanulata, commonly known as the blue bellflower, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, bell-shaped blue or violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves and grows up to 1 meter tall. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Sutera campanulata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
