Stachys jijigaensis

Stachys jijigaensis, commonly known as the Ethiopian betony, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features square stems and opposite leaves, typical of the mint family. Its flowers are usually blue or purple, arranged in whorls along the stem.

It is often found in rocky or arid environments, showing adaptability to harsh conditions.
