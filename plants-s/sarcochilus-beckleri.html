Sarcochilus beckleri

Sarcochilus beckleri, commonly known as the Hairy Sword Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its tall, sword-shaped leaves and produces striking, greenish-yellow flowers with prominent red and purple markings. The plant thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest undergrowth. Its unique appearance and habitat make it a distinctive member of the Orchidaceae family.

Sarcochilus beckleri is also noted for its ability to form symbiotic relationships with mycorrhizal fungi, aiding in nutrient absorption.
