Solanum hoplopetalum

Solanum hoplopetalum, commonly known as the hoplopetalum nightshade, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and tobacco. The plant features small, green leaves with a hairy texture and produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its berries are typically red or yellow when ripe and contain toxic compounds similar to other nightshades.

Due to its toxicity, it is not recommended for consumption or cultivation in gardens.
