Sorbus liljeforsii

Sorbus liljeforsii, commonly known as the Chinese whitebeam, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, white or yellowish flowers. The plant produces small, red or orange berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas or along stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value, it is also cultivated in gardens and parks.
