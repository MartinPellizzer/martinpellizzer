Stenandrium venatum

Stenandrium venatum, commonly known as the white-flowered stenandrium, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous species with slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers with prominent yellow stamens, which bloom during the warm season. Its foliage and floral structure make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in humid climates.

Stenandrium venatum is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
