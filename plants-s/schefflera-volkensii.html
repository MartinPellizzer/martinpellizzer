Schefflera volkensii

Schefflera volkensii, commonly known as the African umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like canopy of large, dark green, glossy leaves. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Schefflera volkensii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor conditions.
