Salacia madagascariensis

Salacia madagascariensis, commonly known as the Madagascar salacia, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Salaceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its unique appearance and restricted habitat, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
