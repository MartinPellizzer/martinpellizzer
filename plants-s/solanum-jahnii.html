Solanum jahnii

Solanum jahnii, commonly known as the Jahn's nightshade, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and dark purple berries. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Solanum jahnii is often found in regions of Brazil, Argentina, and Paraguay.
