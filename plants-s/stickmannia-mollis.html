Stickmannia mollis

Stickmannia mollis, commonly known as the soft stickman plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, pale green to blue-gray leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Stickmannia mollis is often cultivated for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
