Seriphidium fragrans var. phyllostachys

Seriphidium fragrans var. phyllostachys, commonly known as the fragrant seriphidium, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flower heads that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia and is adapted to thrive in dry, well-drained soils.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in harsh environmental conditions.
