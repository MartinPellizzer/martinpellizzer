Schefflera bifurcata

Schefflera bifurcata, commonly known as the split-leaf schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, palm-like foliage composed of large, lobed leaves that often split into multiple segments. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by yellowish berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Schefflera bifurcata is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
