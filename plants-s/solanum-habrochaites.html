Solanum habrochaites

Solanum habrochaites, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive silver-gray foliage and small, star-shaped flowers. The plant belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae), which includes other well-known species such as tomatoes and potatoes. Solanum habrochaites is often found in dry, arid environments and can thrive in poor soil conditions.

It is also notable for its use in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance.
