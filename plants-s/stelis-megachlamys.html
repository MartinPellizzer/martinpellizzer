Stelis megachlamys

Stelis megachlamys, commonly known as the giant stelis orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, showy flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. The plant produces numerous small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in a spike, which bloom during the warm season. Stelis megachlamys thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
