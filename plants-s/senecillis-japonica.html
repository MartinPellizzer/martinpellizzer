Senecillis japonica

Senecillis japonica, commonly known as the Japanese senecio, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom in summer. Senecillis japonica is often grown as an ornamental plant in gardens due to its attractive foliage and flowers.

It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight.
