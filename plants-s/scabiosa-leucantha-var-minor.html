Scabiosa leucantha var. minor

Scabiosa leucantha var. minor, commonly known as the lesser white scabious, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features delicate, pinkish-white flowers arranged in flat, rounded clusters, which bloom from late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, sandy or loamy soils.

This species is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators.
