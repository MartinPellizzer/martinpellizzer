Stachys germanica var. italica

Stachys germanica var. italica, commonly known as Italian hedge nettle, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It features upright, hairy stems that bear opposite, heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, pale purple to pink flowers in whorled clusters along the stem. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it identifiable by its distinctive foliage and flowering pattern.
