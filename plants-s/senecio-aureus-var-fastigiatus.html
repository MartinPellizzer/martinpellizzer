Senecio aureus var. fastigiatus

Senecio aureus var. fastigiatus, commonly known as the golden ragwort.

It is a striking evergreen shrub characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in early summer, which are clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences. Native to South Africa, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
