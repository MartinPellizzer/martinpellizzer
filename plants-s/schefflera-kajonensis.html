Schefflera kajonensis

Schefflera kajonensis, commonly known as the Kajonensis umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of palmate leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments and is often found in coastal areas. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, with a leathery texture.

Schefflera kajonensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
