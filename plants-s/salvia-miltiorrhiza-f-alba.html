Salvia miltiorrhiza f. alba

Salvia miltiorrhiza f. alba, commonly known as white salvia, is a variant of the Chinese herb salvia miltiorrhiza.

It is characterized by its pale or white flowers, which contrast with the typical red or purple hues of the main species. The plant is native to China and is often found in woodland areas and rocky soils. Its roots are used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Botanically, it belongs to the Lamiaceae family and exhibits similar growth habits to its parent species.
