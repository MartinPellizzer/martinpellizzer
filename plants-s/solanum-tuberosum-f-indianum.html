Solanum tuberosum f. indianum

Solanum tuberosum f. indianum, commonly known as the Indian potato, is a botanical variant of the common potato.

It is characterized by its smaller, elongated tubers that are typically yellow or red in color. This variety is native to the Indian subcontinent and thrives in temperate climates. It exhibits a unique foliage structure with hairy leaves and purple-tinged stems.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities as well as its edible tubers.
