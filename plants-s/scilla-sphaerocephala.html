Scilla sphaerocephala

Scilla sphaerocephala, commonly known as the globe squill, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It produces striking, spherical blue or purple flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge from a basal rosette of bulbs. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Scilla sphaerocephala is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ease of care.
