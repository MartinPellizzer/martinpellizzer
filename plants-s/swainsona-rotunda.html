Swainsona rotunda

Swainsona rotunda, commonly known as the round-leaved swainsona, is a flowering plant native to parts of Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive round leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Swainsona rotunda is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
