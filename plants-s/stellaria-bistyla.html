Stellaria bistyla

Stellaria bistyla, commonly known as the two-styled starwort, is a small flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in moist meadows and woodland edges. The plant has delicate, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Stellaria bistyla is known for its short stature and is often overlooked due to its modest appearance.
