Symphyotrichum Novae-Angliae

Symphyotrichum novae-angliae, also known as New England aster, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to eastern North America. This species is celebrated for its vibrant purple to pink blossoms that appear in late summer and early fall. It thrives in a variety of soil types and is often found in meadows, woodlands, and along roadsides. The plant plays an important role in supporting pollinators like bees and butterflies. Its resilience and aesthetic appeal make it a popular choice for gardens and conservation efforts.
