Stelis molleturoi

Stelis molleturoi, commonly known as the Molleturoi orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with fleshy leaves that store water, adapting to its natural habitat. Its flowers are small and often clustered, displaying a range of colors including white, pink, and purple.

Stelis molleturoi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
