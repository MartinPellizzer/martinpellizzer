Sanguisorba minor var. maroccana

Sanguisorba minor var. maroccana, commonly known as the Moroccan dropwort, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of small, reddish-purple flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or woodland areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
