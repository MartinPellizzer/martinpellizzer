Stellaria sylvatica

Stellaria sylvatica, commonly known as woodstar, is a flowering plant native to Europe, western Asia, and North Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with slender stems and small, star-shaped white flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Its leaves are typically opposite and have a rounded or ovate shape with hairy surfaces.

Stellaria sylvatica thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
