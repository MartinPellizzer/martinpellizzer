Sorbus hupehensis var. laxiflora

Sorbus hupehensis var. laxiflora, commonly known as the Chinese whitebeam, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It features compound leaves that are alternate and pinnately arranged, with serrated margins. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers in clusters during the spring. Its fruit is a spherical, red berry that ripens in the fall.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
