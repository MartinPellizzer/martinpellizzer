Scabiosa maritima f. tenuis

Scabiosa maritima f. tenuis, commonly known as the slender sea scabious, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of Europe and Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial with hairy, linear leaves and small, clustered flowers that range from white to pink. The plant thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to salty, windy environments. Its flowers bloom in early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

It is often used in coastal gardens for its hardiness and ornamental value.
