Sideritis gracilis

Sideritis gracilis, commonly known as slender beardgrass, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and small, pale blue or purple flowers. Sideritis gracilis typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny environments.

It is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
