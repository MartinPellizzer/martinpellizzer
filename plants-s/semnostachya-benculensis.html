Semnostachya benculensis

Semnostachya benculensis, commonly known as the Benkulensis spikegrass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves with a smooth texture. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodland or grassland habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
