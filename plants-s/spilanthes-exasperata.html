Spilanthes exasperata

Spilanthes exasperata, commonly known as the toothache plant, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the genus Spilanthes in the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant is known for its pungent, peppery scent and is often used in traditional medicine for its analgesic properties.
