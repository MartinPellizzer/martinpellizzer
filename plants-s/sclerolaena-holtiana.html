Sclerolaena holtiana

Sclerolaena holtiana, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, shrub-like tree that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant is characterized by its dense, hard wood, which is highly valued for its durability and resistance to decay. Its small, white flowers appear in clusters and are followed by small, round fruits.

Sclerolaena holtiana plays an important ecological role in its native environment, providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
