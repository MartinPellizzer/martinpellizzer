Stachys thirkei var. condensata

Stachys thirkei var. condensata, commonly known as the dense-stemmed betony, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and aromatic foliage. The plant features small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in whorled clusters along the stem. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with a hairy texture and a strong, mint-like scent.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.
