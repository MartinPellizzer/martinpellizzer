Scutellaria sedelmeyerae

Scutellaria sedelmeyerae, commonly known as the Andean skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes in South America.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces seed capsules that contain numerous tiny seeds.

It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or mountainous terrain.
