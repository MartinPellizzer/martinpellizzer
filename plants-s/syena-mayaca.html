Syena mayaca

Syena mayaca, commonly known as the yellow bell.

It is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America. The plant features showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Syena mayaca is typically found in moist, sunny habitats such as coastal areas and wetlands.
