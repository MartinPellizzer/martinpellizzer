Seseli hypoleucum

It is characterized by its slender stems and small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in umbels. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is found in various regions of Europe. It has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are often covered in a soft, hairy texture.

Seseli hypoleucum is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
