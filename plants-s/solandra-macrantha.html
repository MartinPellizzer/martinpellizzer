Solandra macrantha

Solandra macrantha, commonly known as the giant solandra, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is a shrub or small tree, often reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a rough texture.

Solandra macrantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
