Synsepalum tomentosum

Synsepalum tomentosum, commonly known as the velvet bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a small shrub that produces small, yellowish flowers and bean-like fruits. The plant is characterized by its hairy, tomentose leaves and stems, which give it its common name. The fruits contain seeds that are used in traditional medicine and as a source of protein.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often grown for its nutritional value.
