Stipagrostis masirahensis

Stipagrostis masirahensis, commonly known as the Masirah grass, is a perennial grass species native to the Arabian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. This plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy or gravelly soils. Its growth habit is low and spreading, making it well adapted to withstand harsh climatic conditions.

Stipagrostis masirahensis plays a role in stabilizing soils and providing forage for wildlife in its native habitat.
