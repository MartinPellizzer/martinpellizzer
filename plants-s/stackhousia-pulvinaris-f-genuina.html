Stackhousia pulvinaris f. genuina

Stackhousia pulvinaris f. genuina, commonly known as the narrow-leaved stackhousia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental value and drought tolerance in garden settings.
