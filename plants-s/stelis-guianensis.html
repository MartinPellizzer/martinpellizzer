Stelis guianensis

Stelis guianensis, commonly known as the Guianan stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically found growing on trees in tropical rainforests. The plant has a slender, wiry stem with multiple nodes and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

The species is native to South America, particularly in regions such as Guyana and Venezuela.
