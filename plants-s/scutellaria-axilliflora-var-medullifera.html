Scutellaria axilliflora var. medullifera

Scutellaria axilliflora var. medullifera, commonly known as the medicinal skullcap, is a perennial herb native to parts of Asia.

It features upright stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers arranged in whorls along the stem. It is known for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional Chinese medicine.

Its genus, Scutellaria, is characterized by its unique seed structure called a scutellum.
