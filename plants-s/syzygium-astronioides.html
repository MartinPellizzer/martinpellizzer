Syzygium astronioides

Syzygium astronioides, commonly known as the Java apple or *Pomelo*, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and large, glossy leaves. The plant produces fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by edible, citrus-like fruits with a sweet and acidic flavor. Syzygium astronioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and its fruit, which is used in both culinary and medicinal applications.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
