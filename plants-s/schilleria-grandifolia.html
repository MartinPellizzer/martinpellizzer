Schilleria grandifolia

Schilleria grandifolia, commonly known as the giant schilleria, is a striking member of the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy white or pale blue flowers. The plant features strap-shaped, evergreen leaves that are broad and often waxy in texture. Schilleria grandifolia thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens in temperate climates.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and ability to attract pollinators.
