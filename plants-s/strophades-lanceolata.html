Strophades lanceolata

Strophades lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceleaf strophades, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs. Its small, yellowish flowers bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months.

Strophades lanceolata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
