Sarcanthus taiwanianus

Sarcanthus taiwanianus, commonly known as Taiwan's flame vine, is a climbing plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its striking red-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and is often found in forested areas. It is noted for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens despite its invasive tendencies.
