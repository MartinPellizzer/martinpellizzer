Stachyurus praecox f. bicolor

Stachyurus praecox f. bicolor, commonly known as the two-colored early stachyurus, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its striking, bicolor flowers that typically display a combination of white and pink hues. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that turn yellow in the fall, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, loamy soil.

This cultivar is valued for its early blooming habit and unique floral display in gardens.
