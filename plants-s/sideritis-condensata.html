Sideritis condensata

Sideritis condensata, commonly known as Greek oregano, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are aromatic and often used in culinary applications.

Sideritis condensata is also valued for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional herbal remedies.
