Solanum vestissimum

Solanum vestissimum, commonly known as the velvet nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its hairy, velvety foliage. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or purple in color. Its fruit is a berry that turns from green to yellow or red as it matures.

Solanum vestissimum is often found in moist, shaded environments and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.
