Soehrensia tarijensis

Soehrensia tarijensis, commonly known as the Tarijensis euphorbia, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Bolivia and Peru.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, characterized by its unique floral structures and milky sap. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a rough texture, while its flowers are clustered in complex inflorescences.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in dry or semi-arid environments.
