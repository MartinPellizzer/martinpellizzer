Salicornia perennans subsp. perennans

Salicornia perennans subsp.

perennans, commonly known as sea beans or glassworts, is a halophytic plant adapted to saline environments. It is a perennial species that thrives in coastal areas, salt marshes, and other saline habitats.

The plant features succulent, fleshy stems and leaves that store water, aiding survival in arid conditions. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically arranged in clusters. Salicornia perennans subsp.

perennans plays a vital role in stabilizing coastal soils and supporting diverse ecosystems.
