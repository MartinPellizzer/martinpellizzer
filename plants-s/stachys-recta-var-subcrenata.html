Stachys recta var. subcrenata

Stachys recta var. subcrenata, commonly known as narrow-leaved hedge nettle, is a flowering plant in the mint family, Lamiaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, opposite, heart-shaped leaves with a slightly wavy margin. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale blue to purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is native to parts of Europe and is often found in moist, shaded woodland areas.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in partially shaded conditions.
