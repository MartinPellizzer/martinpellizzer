Schismatoglottis crispata purpurea

Schismatoglottis crispata purpurea, commonly known as the Purple Crisp Crinum, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

Schismatoglottis crispata purpurea is often cultivated for its dramatic floral display and unique foliage.
