Sophronitis isopetala

Sophronitis isopetala, commonly known as the pink-flowered orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, pale pink to white flowers with a distinctive violet lip. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are elongated and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water retention.

Sophronitis isopetala is valued in horticulture for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
