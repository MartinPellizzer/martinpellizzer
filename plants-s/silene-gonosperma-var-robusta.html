Silene gonosperma var. robusta

Silene gonosperma var. robusta, commonly known as the robust silene, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Silene gonosperma var. robusta is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
