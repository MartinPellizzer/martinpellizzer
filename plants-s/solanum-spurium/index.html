Solanum Spurium

Solanum spurium is a lesser-known member of the nightshade family known for its striking appearance and unique growth habits. Native to South America, this plant thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with ample sunlight and well-draining soil. Its small, bell-shaped flowers often display vibrant colors, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Despite its beauty, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care. This article explores its characteristics, cultivation tips, and ecological significance.
