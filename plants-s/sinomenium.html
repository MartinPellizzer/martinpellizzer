Sinomenium

Sinomenium, commonly known as Chinese wisteria, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Menispermaceae.

It comprises several species, most notably Sinomenium acutum, which is native to eastern Asia. These plants are characterized by their climbing habit, compound leaves, and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. The fruits are typically capsule-like and contain several seeds.

Sinomenium species are often cultivated for their ornamental value and have also been used in traditional medicine.
