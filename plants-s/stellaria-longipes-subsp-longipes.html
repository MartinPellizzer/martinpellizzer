Stellaria longipes subsp. longipes

Stellaria longipes subsp.

longipes, commonly known as the long-stemmed starwort, is a flowering plant species native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its slender, ascending stems and small, star-shaped white flowers.

The leaves are typically opposite, lanceolate, and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

This subspecies is notable for its delicate appearance and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
