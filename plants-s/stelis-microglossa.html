Stelis microglossa

Stelis microglossa, commonly known as the miniature lip orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in tropical forests. The plant features slender, upright stems and produces small, delicate flowers with a distinct lip. Its botanical characteristics include narrow leaves and a compact growth habit.

It is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
