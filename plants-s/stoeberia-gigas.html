Stoeberia gigas

Stoeberia gigas, commonly known as the giant stoeberia, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant produces large, showy flowers that are typically blue or purple in color, arranged in dense, terminal clusters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Stoeberia gigas is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a hardy plant in suitable climates.
