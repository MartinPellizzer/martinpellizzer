Syzygium filicaudum

Syzygium filicaudum, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in summer, creating a vibrant display. The plant is a deciduous tree, shedding its leaves during the dry season. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, with a glossy green surface.

Syzygium filicaudum is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its showy flowers and adaptability to various climates.
