Stelis pachyglossa

Stelis pachyglossa, commonly known as the broad-lipped stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and pseudo-bulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces strikingly colorful flowers with a distinctive broad lip, often in shades of pink or purple. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Stelis pachyglossa is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
