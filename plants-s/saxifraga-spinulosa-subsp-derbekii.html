Saxifraga spinulosa subsp. derbekii

Saxifraga spinulosa subsp.

derbekii, commonly known as the Armenian saxifrage, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus. It typically grows in rocky and alpine habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments.

The plant features upright stems with small, clustered leaves and produces delicate pink or white flowers. Its botanical characteristics include hairy foliage and a compact growth habit.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and resilience in challenging conditions.
