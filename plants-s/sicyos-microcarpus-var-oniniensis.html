Sicyos microcarpus var. oniniensis

Sicyos microcarpus var. oniniensis, commonly known as the Oniniensis Bottle Gourd, is a variant of the bottle gourd belonging to the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is characterized by its elongated, bottle-shaped fruits that are typically green when mature. The plant is a climbing vine with palmately lobed leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. It thrives in warm, tropical climates and is often cultivated for its edible fruits.

The variety is noted for its relatively small fruit size compared to other bottle gourd cultivars.
