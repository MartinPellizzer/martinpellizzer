Synsepalum stipulatum

Synsepalum stipulatum, commonly known as the African star apple, is a small evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by edible, round fruits with a sweet, white pulp. Its ornamental value and edible fruits make it a valuable species in both horticulture and agriculture.
