Syringa × Henryi Var. Eximia

Syringa × henryi var. eximia is a striking deciduous shrub known for its fragrant, clustered flowers. Native to China, it has become a popular ornamental plant in gardens around the world. This variety is prized for its long blooming period and adaptability to various soil conditions. Its vibrant lilac blooms add a splash of color to landscapes in late spring. With its elegant form and enduring beauty, it remains a favorite among horticultural enthusiasts.
