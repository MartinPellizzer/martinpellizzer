Swartzia myrtifolia var. guatemalensis

Swartzia myrtifolia var. guatemalensis, commonly known as the Guatemalan Swartzia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It produces long, flat pods containing small, dark seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its timber and medicinal properties.
