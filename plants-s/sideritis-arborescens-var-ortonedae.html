Sideritis arborescens var. ortonedae

Sideritis arborescens var. ortonedae, commonly known as the Ortone's rockrose, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing a compact, sprawling growth habit. The plant features small, aromatic leaves and produces clusters of pale blue to violet flowers. It is known for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional herbal remedies.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to harsh, sun-exposed habitats.
