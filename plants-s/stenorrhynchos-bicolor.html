Stenorrhynchos bicolor

Stenorrhynchos bicolor, commonly known as the two-colored orchid, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers that display two distinct hues, typically pink and white. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its compact growth habit. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Stenorrhynchos bicolor thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
