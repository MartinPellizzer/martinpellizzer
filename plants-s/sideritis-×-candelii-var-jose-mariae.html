Sideritis × candelii var. jose-mariae

Sideritis × candelii var. jose-mariae, commonly known as the Jose Maria Sideritis, is a hybrid shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, branched stems covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are narrow, lance-shaped, and often hairy. The plant produces small, pale blue to violet flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional herbal medicine.
