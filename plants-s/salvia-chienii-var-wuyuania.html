Salvia chienii var. wuyuania

Salvia chienii var. wuyuania, commonly known as the Wuyuan salvia, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

This variety is often cultivated for its vibrant floral display and ecological benefits in gardens.
