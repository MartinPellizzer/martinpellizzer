Sorghum tuberculatum

Sorghum tuberculatum, commonly known as the knobstick sorghum, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in warm climates and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.

Sorghum tuberculatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional landscaping.
