Stylidium aquaticum

Stylidium aquaticum, commonly known as the water pincushion, is a unique flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, spiky inflorescence that resembles a pincushion, with bright yellow flowers surrounded by red bracts. The plant is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and exhibits specialized adaptations for aquatic environments. Its roots are often submerged, allowing it to thrive in wetland habitats.

Stylidium aquaticum plays an important ecological role in its native ecosystems by providing habitat and nectar for various pollinators.
