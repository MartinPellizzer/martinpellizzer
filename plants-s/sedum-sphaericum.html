Sedum sphaericum

Sedum sphaericum, commonly known as the globe sedum, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, spherical shape, formed by dense clusters of fleshy, green leaves. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
