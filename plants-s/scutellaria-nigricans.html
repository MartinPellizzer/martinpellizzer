Scutellaria nigricans

Scutellaria nigricans, commonly known as black scutellaria, is a species of flowering plant in the mint family, Lamiaceae.

It is native to the southeastern United States, particularly Florida and the Caribbean. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.
