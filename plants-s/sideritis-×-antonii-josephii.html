Sideritis × antonii-josephii

Sideritis × antonii-josephii, commonly known as the hybrid sainfoin, is a perennial herb native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its small, tubular flowers are usually purple or pink, arranged in whorled clusters along the stem.

Sideritis species are known for their medicinal properties, and this hybrid is no exception, often used in traditional remedies.
