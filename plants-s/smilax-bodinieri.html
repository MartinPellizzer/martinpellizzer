Smilax bodinieri

Smilax bodinieri, commonly known as the Bodinier smilax, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a climbing shrub characterized by its woody stems and spiny structures. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are usually opposite, ovate, and have a rough texture.

Smilax bodinieri is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in dense vegetation and along forest edges.
