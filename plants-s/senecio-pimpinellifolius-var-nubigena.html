Senecio pimpinellifolius var. nubigena

Senecio pimpinellifolius var. nubigena, commonly known as the cloud cushion, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive, fluffy appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, adding a subtle ornamental touch. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
