Stachyurus chinensis subsp. brachystachyus

Stachyurus chinensis subsp.

brachystachyus, commonly known as the Chinese weeping honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China. It is characterized by its pendulous clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in early spring.

The plant has finely toothed, ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It prefers moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sun.

Its ornamental value comes from its elegant flowering display and attractive foliage.
