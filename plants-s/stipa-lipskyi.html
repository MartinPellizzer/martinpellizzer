Stipa lipskyi

Stipa lipskyi, commonly known as the Liskyi's feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to the temperate regions of Eurasia.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and produces delicate, airy panicles of flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Stipa lipskyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
