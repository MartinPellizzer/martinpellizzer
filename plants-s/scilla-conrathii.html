Scilla conrathii

Scilla conrathii, commonly known as the Conrath's squill, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of blue to violet, bell-shaped flowers in early spring. The plant is characterized by its hardy nature and ability to thrive in well-drained soils. It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and early blooming habit.

Scilla conrathii is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
