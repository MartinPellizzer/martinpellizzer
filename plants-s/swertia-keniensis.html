Swertia keniensis

Swertia keniensis, commonly known as Kenyan gentian, is a flowering plant native to the highland regions of Kenya.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments at elevations above 2,000 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Swertia keniensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
