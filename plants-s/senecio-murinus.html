Senecio murinus

Senecio murinus, commonly known as the coastal groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its succulent, hairy leaves and yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant thrives in coastal environments, tolerating salty air and sandy soils. It is often found in open, sun-exposed areas along the coastline.

Senecio murinus is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes many familiar flowering plants.
