Sarcoglottis neuroptera var. camposnovensis

Sarcoglottis neuroptera var. camposnovensis, commonly known as the Brazilian arrowhead vine, is a climbing plant native to the Atlantic Forest region of Brazil.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
