Schizostemma solanoides

Schizostemma solanoides, commonly known as the rock nightshade, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades such as tomatoes and potatoes. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and has small, lobed leaves with a hairy texture. Its flowers are usually purple or violet, and it produces small, berry-like fruits that contain toxic seeds.

Due to its toxic properties, it is not recommended for cultivation or consumption.
