Sinocrassula longistyla

Sinocrassula longistyla, commonly known as the Long-styled Crassula, is a species of succulent plant native to southern China.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming growth habit. The plant features fleshy, ovate leaves that are typically green with a bluish tint, and it produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
