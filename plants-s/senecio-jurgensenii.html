Senecio jurgensenii

Senecio jurgensenii, commonly known as the Jurgensen's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, such as rocky soils and desert scrublands.
