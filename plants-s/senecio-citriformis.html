Senecio citriformis

Senecio citriformis, commonly known as the lemon leaf senecio, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, dark green leaves that are shaped like lemon slices, giving it its common name. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping.
