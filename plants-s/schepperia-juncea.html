Schepperia juncea

Schepperia juncea, commonly known as the slender Schepperia, is a species of flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains in South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, which bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Schepperia juncea is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
