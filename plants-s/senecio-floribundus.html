Senecio floribundus

Senecio floribundus, commonly known as the Cape gold or yellow star, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers. The plant features upright, branched stems covered in fine hairs and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in clusters. Its leaves are typically narrow and gray-green, with a fuzzy texture.

Senecio floribundus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
