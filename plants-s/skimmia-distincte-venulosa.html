Skimmia distincte-venulosa

Skimmia distincte-venulosa, commonly known as the veined skimmia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with prominent veins that give it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming in late winter or early spring. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Skimmia distincte-venulosa is relatively low maintenance and thrives in partial shade with well-drained soil.
