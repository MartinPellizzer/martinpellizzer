Senecio backii

Senecio backii, commonly known as the Cape mountain sandthorn, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring, and its leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs. It is adapted to arid environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Senecio backii is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
