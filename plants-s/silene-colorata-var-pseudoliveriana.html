Silene colorata var. pseudoliveriana

Silene colorata var. pseudoliveriana, commonly known as the Liverleaf or Liverworts, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its small, pink or white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has a rosette of fleshy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, white coating. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.

This variety is closely related to other members of the Caryophyllaceae family, sharing similar morphological features.
