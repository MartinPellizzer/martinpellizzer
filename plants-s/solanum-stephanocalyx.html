Solanum stephanocalyx

Solanum stephanocalyx, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, white or pale green flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences. Its leaves are ovate to elliptic in shape, with a silver-gray underside that gives it its common name.

The fruit is a globose berry that turns red when ripe, containing several small seeds.
