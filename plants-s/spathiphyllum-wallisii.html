Spathiphyllum wallisii

Spathiphyllum wallisii, commonly known as the Wallisii Peace Lily, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy, and elongated leaves that grow in a rosette pattern. The plant produces striking white spathes that enclose small, greenish-white flowers, giving it a dramatic appearance. It thrives in low-light conditions and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Spathiphyllum wallisii is popular as a houseplant due to its air-purifying qualities and elegant foliage.
