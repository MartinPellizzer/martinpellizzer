Sedum subgaleatum

Sedum subgaleatum, commonly known as the rock sedum, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow to orange. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a hardy choice for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular addition to drought-tolerant landscapes.
