Sideritis scordioides var. elongata

Sideritis scordioides var. elongata, commonly known as long-stemmed ironweed, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, tubular flowers that are typically blue to purple in color. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a mild, aromatic scent.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.
