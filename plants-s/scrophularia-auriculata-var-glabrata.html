Scrophularia auriculata var. glabrata

Scrophularia auriculata var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth bellflower, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in sunny to partially shaded environments. The plant features upright stems with opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy but become smooth in this variety. Its showy, tubular flowers, which range in color from pink to purple, bloom in late spring to early summer.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is occasionally cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
