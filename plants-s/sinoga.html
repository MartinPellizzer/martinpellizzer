Sinoga

Sinoga, commonly known as the "Chinese evergreen," is a popular ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers surrounded by a spadix, which is typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in low-light conditions and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its air-purifying properties.

Sinoga is also valued for its ability to tolerate a wide range of environmental conditions.
