Senecio mishmi

Senecio mishmi, commonly known as the Mishmi Senecio, is a flowering plant native to the Eastern Himalayas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high elevations. Its foliage consists of serrated, lance-shaped leaves, and it produces small yellow flowers arranged in clusters.

Due to its restricted distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
