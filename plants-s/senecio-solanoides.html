Senecio solanoides

Senecio solanoides, commonly known as the silverleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces yellow flowers with a distinctive silver-velvet appearance. The plant features narrow, silver-tomentose leaves that help reduce water loss in arid environments. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Senecio solanoides is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage and ease of care.
