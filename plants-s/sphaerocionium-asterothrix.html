Sphaerocionium asterothrix

Sphaerocionium asterothrix, commonly known as the star-thorned cactus, is a member of the Cactaceae family and is native to arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its globular shape and spiny, segmented stems that store water efficiently. The plant produces yellow flowers during the warmer months, which are clustered in a star-like pattern, giving it its common name. Its root system is shallow and adapted to rapid water absorption after infrequent rainfall.

Sphaerocionium asterothrix is well-suited to harsh desert environments and is often used in xeriscaping due to its low maintenance requirements.
