Strophanthus holosericeus

Strophanthus holosericeus, commonly known as the silky strophanthus, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a climbing shrub that produces striking orange to red flowers, which bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has palmate leaves with long, silky hairs that give it its common name. Its flowers are often used in traditional medicine, though it contains toxic compounds.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
