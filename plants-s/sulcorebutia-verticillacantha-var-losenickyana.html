Sulcorebutia verticillacantha var. losenickyana

Sulcorebutia verticillacantha var. losenickyana, commonly known as the Losenickyana Cactus, is a rare and striking member of the Cactaceae family native to the arid regions of central Argentina.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and dense clusters of short, cylindrical stems that often bear white to pale blue spines. The plant produces showy, funnel-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red during the spring and early summer. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, requiring minimal water to prevent root rot.
