Scutellaria indica var. subacaulis

Scutellaria indica var. subacaulis, commonly known as the slender blue skullcap, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. The plant features square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular blue or purple flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species for ecological studies and traditional herbal medicine.
