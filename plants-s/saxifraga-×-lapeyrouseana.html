Saxifraga × lapeyrouseana

Saxifraga × lapeyrouseana, commonly known as the Lapeyrouse's saxifrage, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Saxifragaceae family.

It is characterized by its rosette-forming growth habit and produces showy, pink to white flowers during the summer months. Native to the mountainous regions of Europe, it thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and cool climates. The plant is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats, where it adapts to harsh environmental conditions.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine plant collections.
