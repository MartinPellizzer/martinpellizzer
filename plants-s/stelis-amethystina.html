Stelis amethystina

Stelis amethystina, commonly known as the amethystina orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking violet to purple floral coloration. The plant typically produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. Stelis amethystina thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, loose soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance and relatively easy care make it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
