Sphaerostigma micranthum var. jonesii

Sphaerostigma micranthum var. jonesii, commonly known as the Jones's globe thistle, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features striking spherical blue to purple flower heads that are surrounded by spiny bracts, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant has a basal rosette of narrow, gray-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and unique morphology, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
