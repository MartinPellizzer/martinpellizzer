Senecio moreliae

Senecio moreliae, commonly known as the Morelia's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. It produces small, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow.

Senecio moreliae is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
