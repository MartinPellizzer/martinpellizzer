Siliquastrum arbor-judae

Siliquastrum arbor-judae, commonly known as the Judas tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It features fragrant, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a serrated margin.

The Judas tree is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
