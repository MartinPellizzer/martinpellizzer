Senna dariensis var. gatunensis

Senna dariensis var. gatunensis, commonly known as the Malayan senna, is a shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bipinnate leaves, which are often divided into numerous small leaflets. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in dense, cylindrical clusters, typical of the Fabaceae family. Its fruit is a slender, dehiscent pod that splits open to release seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional medicinal uses.
