Sansevieria forestii

Sansevieria forestii, commonly known as the African spear or snake plant, is a succulent plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with subtle variegation, often displaying light green or yellowish stripes. The plant is highly drought-tolerant, making it ideal for low-maintenance indoor environments. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water efficiently.

Sansevieria forestii is also known for its ability to purify indoor air, enhancing its appeal as a decorative and functional houseplant.
