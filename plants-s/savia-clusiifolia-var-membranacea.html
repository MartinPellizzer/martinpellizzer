Savia clusiifolia var. membranacea

Savia clusiifolia var. membranacea, commonly known as the membranous clusiifolia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Clusiaceae.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that are often covered in a thin, waxy layer, giving them a distinctive appearance.
