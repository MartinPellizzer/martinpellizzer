Styrax macrospermus

Styrax macrospermus, commonly known as the large-seeded styrax, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. The plant produces small, dark red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a glossy appearance.

Styrax macrospermus is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and adaptability to various soil types.
