Stellera cyanea

Stellera cyanea, commonly known as blue flax, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its delicate blue to purple flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, branched stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodlands or along stream banks.

Stellera cyanea is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators.
