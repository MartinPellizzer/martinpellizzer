Saxifraga × eudoxiana

Saxifraga × eudoxiana, commonly known as the alpine saxifrage, is a hybrid plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in rocky and alpine environments, often found in subalpine and alpine zones. The plant features dense, mat-forming growth with succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in harsh conditions. Its flowers are typically pink or white, arranged in clusters, and bloom in late summer.

Saxifraga × eudoxiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
