Securinega schweinfurthii

Securinega schweinfurthii, commonly known as the African moonseed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small shrub characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers. The plant is known for its fruit, which contains a single, hard, black seed. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in rocky habitats.
