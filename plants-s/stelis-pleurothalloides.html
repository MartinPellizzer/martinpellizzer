Stelis pleurothalloides

Stelis pleurothalloides, commonly known as the pleurothalloides orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces delicate flowers with intricate patterns, typically in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Stelis pleurothalloides is often found in forest understories and is valued for its ornamental beauty in horticulture.
