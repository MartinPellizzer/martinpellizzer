Solori thorelii

Solori thorelii, commonly known as the golden groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a soft texture.

Solori thorelii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
