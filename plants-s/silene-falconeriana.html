Silene falconeriana

Silene falconeriana, commonly known as the Falconer's campion, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family, characterized by its showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer.
