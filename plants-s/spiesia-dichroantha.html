Spiesia dichroantha

Spiesia dichroantha, commonly known as the two-colored spurge, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of about 30 centimeters. The plant features small, yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a distinctive two-colored appearance, with a lighter green upper surface and a darker green underside.

The species is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
