Stachys huetii

Stachys huetii, commonly known as the Huet's hedge nettle, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the mint family (Lamiaceae) and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, pink to purple flowers in whorled clusters, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded woodland areas.

Stachys huetii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local pollinators.
