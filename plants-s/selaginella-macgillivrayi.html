Selaginella macgillivrayi

Selaginella macgillivrayi, commonly known as the MacGillivray's spikemoss, is a species of spikemoss native to the southeastern United States.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
