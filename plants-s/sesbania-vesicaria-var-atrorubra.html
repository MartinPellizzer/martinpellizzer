Sesbania vesicaria var. atrorubra

Sesbania vesicaria var. atrorubra, commonly known as the red-seeded sesbania, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces elongated pods containing reddish-brown seeds, which are often used in traditional practices. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

Sesbania vesicaria var. atrorubra is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it useful in agricultural settings.
