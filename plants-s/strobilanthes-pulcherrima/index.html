Strobilanthes Pulcherrima

Strobilanthes pulcherrima is a rare and striking flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. Known for its vibrant purple and white blooms, it captivates botanists and nature lovers alike with its unique beauty. This species belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is often referred to as the "Queen of the Orchids" due to its ornamental value. Despite its allure, it faces threats from habitat loss and over-collection. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival in the wild.
