Saxifraga cuneata var. valentina

Saxifraga cuneata var. valentina, commonly known as the Valentina saxifrage, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features rosettes of dark green, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery bloom. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pinkish flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens and alpine settings.
