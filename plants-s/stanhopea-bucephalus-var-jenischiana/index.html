Stanhopea Bucephalus Var. Jenischiana

Jenisch's stanhopea, scientifically known as Stanhopea bucephalus var. jenischiana, is a rare and exquisite orchid native to the cloud forests of Colombia. This captivating species is prized for its vibrant, fragrant flowers that bloom in a striking display of color and form. Despite its beauty, it faces significant threats from habitat loss and over-collection. Its unique characteristics make it a subject of fascination for botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival in the wild.
