Stelis longipetala

Stelis longipetala, commonly known as the long-petalled stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, erect stem and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive lip that is elongated and spurred.

Stelis longipetala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
