Schefflera straminea

Schefflera straminea, commonly known as the silver schefflera, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, palmate leaves that are typically light green with a silvery underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Schefflera straminea is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
