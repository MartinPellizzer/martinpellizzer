Scrophularia filicifolia var. minor

Scrophularia filicifolia var. minor, commonly known as the lesser willowleaf figwort, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae, which includes many species with medicinal and ornamental value. The plant is characterized by its slender, willow-like leaves and spikes of tubular, two-lipped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, wooded habitats and is often found in shaded or partially sunny areas.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
