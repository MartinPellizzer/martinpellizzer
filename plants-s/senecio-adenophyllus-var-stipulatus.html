Senecio adenophyllus var. stipulatus

Senecio adenophyllus var. stipulatus, commonly known as the stipulate senecio, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-forming growth habit and fleshy, narrow leaves that are typically green with a bluish tint. The plant produces yellow flowers in summer, which are arranged in a compact inflorescence. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
