Scrophularia vernalis

Scrophularia vernalis, commonly known as spring scrophularia, is a herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features upright stems with opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are tubular and range in color from pink to purple, appearing in clusters at the ends of the stems.

Scrophularia vernalis blooms in early spring, making it one of the earliest flowering plants in its habitat.
