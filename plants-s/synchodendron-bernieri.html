Synchodendron bernieri

Synchodendron bernieri, commonly known as the Madagascar star apple, is a tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is a small to medium-sized tree with a dense, rounded canopy and smooth, grayish bark. The leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptical, with a glossy green surface. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically greenish-yellow, while the fruit is a spherical, yellow or orange berry with a sweet, aromatic pulp.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appearance and edible fruit, which is often consumed fresh or used in local cuisines.
