Saugetia pleiostachya

Saugetia pleiostachya, commonly known as the broad-leaved grass, is a perennial herb native to Africa.

It is characterized by its robust, upright growth habit and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces inflorescences of dense, spike-like clusters that bear small, pale blue to white flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value and resilience, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
