Stellaria macropetala

Stellaria macropetala, commonly known as the large-petaled starwort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. The plant is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem, and it produces small, oval seeds in its fruiting structures.

Stellaria macropetala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
