Saxifraga cuneifolia subsp. umbrosa

Saxifraga cuneifolia subsp.

umbrosa, commonly known as the shadow saxifrage, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It typically thrives in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices.

The plant features dark green, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. Its flowers are small, pinkish-white, and clustered in dense inflorescences, blooming in early summer.

It is well adapted to cool, humid climates and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.
