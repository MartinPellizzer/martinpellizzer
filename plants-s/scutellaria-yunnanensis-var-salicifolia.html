Scutellaria yunnanensis var. salicifolia

Scutellaria yunnanensis var. salicifolia, commonly known as the willow-leaved skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan, China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants known for their medicinal properties. The plant is characterized by its lanceolate, willow-like leaves and small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in the summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique morphology and potential pharmacological properties, it is of interest to botanists and herbalists alike.
