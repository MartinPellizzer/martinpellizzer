Silene otites proles pseudotites

Silene otites proles pseudotites, commonly known as the false mouse-ear or nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its pink or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often glandular.
