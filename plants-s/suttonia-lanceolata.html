Suttonia lanceolata

Suttonia lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceleaf suttonia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom in summer. Suttonia lanceolata is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
