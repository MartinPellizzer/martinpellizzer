Senna surattensis

Senna surattensis, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical leguminous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of yellow, fragrant flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant produces long, slender pods containing small, dark seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and medicinal properties.

Senna surattensis is also used in traditional medicine for its laxative and anti-inflammatory effects.
