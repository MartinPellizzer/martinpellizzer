Salaciopsis sparsiflora var. jugifera

Salaciopsis sparsiflora var. jugifera, commonly known as the slender salacii, is a rare and distinctive member of the Salaciacae family.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, clustered leaves that are typically dark green and leathery in texture. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or yellow-green in color, followed by small, fleshy fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical rainforests.
