Stachytarpheta amplexicaulis

Stachytarpheta amplexicaulis, commonly known as the creeping redweed, is a herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that often wrap around the stems, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple, arranged in clusters at the ends of the branches. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.
