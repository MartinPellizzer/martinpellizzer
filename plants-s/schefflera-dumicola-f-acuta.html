Schefflera dumicola f. acuta

Schefflera dumicola f. acuta, commonly known as the sharp-leafed umbrella plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features a dense, bushy growth habit with glossy, dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are sharply pointed. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a versatile ornamental plant for both indoor and outdoor settings.
