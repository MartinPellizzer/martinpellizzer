Spiesia macrobotrys

Spiesia macrobotrys, commonly known as the giant spider lily, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers with prominent yellow stamens that resemble spider legs, giving it its common name. The plant is a member of the Amaryllis family and is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in moist, shady environments. Its leaves are broad, lance-shaped, and often reach several feet in length.

Spiesia macrobotrys is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
