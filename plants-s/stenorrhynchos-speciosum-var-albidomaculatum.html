Stenorrhynchos speciosum var. albidomaculatum

Stenorrhynchos speciosum var. albidomaculatum, commonly known as the white-spotted stenorrhynchos, is a striking member of the orchid family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its elegant, pendulous inflorescences. The flowers are typically pale pink or white with contrasting yellow markings, making it a popular choice for horticultural collections. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is noted for its relatively easy care and long-lasting blooms, making it a favored plant among orchid enthusiasts.
