Senecio gracillimus

Senecio gracillimus, commonly known as the slender groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its slender stems and small, yellowish flowers make it a distinctive member of the Senecio genus.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and hardy nature.
