Spathoglottis obovata

Spathoglottis obovata, commonly known as the broad-ovate orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its ovoid, fleshy pseudobulbs. The plant produces showy, spurred flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Spathoglottis obovata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
