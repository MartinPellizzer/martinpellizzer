Solanum gandarillasii

Solanum gandarillasii, commonly known as the Gándara or Gándarilla, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is native to the arid regions of South America, particularly Argentina and Chile. The plant is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and hairy, glandular leaves. It typically grows as a shrub or subshrub, often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Solanum gandarillasii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
