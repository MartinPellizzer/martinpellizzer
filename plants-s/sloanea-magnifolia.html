Sloanea magnifolia

Sloanea magnifolia, commonly known as the magnolia sloanea, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy white or pale pink flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Sloanea magnifolia is often found in humid, shaded environments such as rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation in warm climates.
