Satureja rigida

Satureja rigida, commonly known as rigid savory, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and thrives in full sun. Its small, tubular flowers are usually pink or purple, and it produces small, oval-shaped seeds.

Satureja rigida is valued for its fragrant foliage and is often used in herbal medicine and culinary applications.
