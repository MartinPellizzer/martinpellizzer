Schizostachyum subcordatum

Schizostachyum subcordatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf palma.

It is a species of palm native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its slender, arching leaves and robust trunk. The plant typically grows in humid, tropical environments and is often found in lowland areas with well-drained soil. Its leaves are long and narrow, often used in traditional thatching and basket-making.

Schizostachyum subcordatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and sustainable use in local crafts.
