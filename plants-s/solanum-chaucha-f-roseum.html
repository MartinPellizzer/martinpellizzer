Solanum chaucha f. roseum

Solanum chaucha f. roseum, commonly known as the pink-flowered nightshade, is a variant of the Solanum chaucha species.

It is a herbaceous plant native to South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features ovate, hairy leaves and produces small, showy flowers that are typically pink or violet in color. Its fruit is a globose berry that turns red when mature.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often grown in gardens for its unique floral display.
