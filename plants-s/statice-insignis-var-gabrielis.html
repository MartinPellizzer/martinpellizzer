Statice insignis var. gabrielis

Statice insignis var. gabrielis, commonly known as Gabriel's statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces spikes of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, purple, and white. The plant typically blooms in late summer to early autumn, adding vibrant color to gardens and landscapes. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in poor soils further enhance its appeal among horticultural enthusiasts.
