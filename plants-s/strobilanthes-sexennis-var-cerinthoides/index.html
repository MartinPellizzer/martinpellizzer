Strobilanthes Sexennis Var. Cerinthoides

Strobilanthes sexennis var. cerinthoides is a rare and striking flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. Known for its vibrant purple and blue petals, it captivates botanists and gardeners alike with its unique beauty. This variant of Strobilanthes is particularly noted for its short blooming period, which occurs only once every few years. Its delicate structure and fleeting display make it a sought-after species in specialized horticulture.
