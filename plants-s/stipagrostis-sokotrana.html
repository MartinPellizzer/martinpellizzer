Stipagrostis sokotrana

Stipagrostis sokotrana, commonly known as the Socotra dragon's blood grass, is a unique plant endemic to the Socotra Archipelago.

It is a perennial grass characterized by its distinctive, sword-like leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, pale yellow flowers. It thrives in the arid and rocky environments of its native habitat, showcasing remarkable adaptations to withstand extreme conditions.

Due to its limited distribution and ecological significance, it is considered a valuable species for botanical research and conservation efforts.
