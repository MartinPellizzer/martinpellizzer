Sedum stenostachyum

Sedum stenostachyum, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, clustered leaves that are typically green or reddish in color. The plant produces delicate, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Sedum stenostachyum is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
