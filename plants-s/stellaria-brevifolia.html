Stellaria brevifolia

Stellaria brevifolia, commonly known as the short-leaved starwort, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically features small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has delicate, branching stems and small, oval leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Stellaria brevifolia is often found in association with other moisture-loving plants in its native habitat.
