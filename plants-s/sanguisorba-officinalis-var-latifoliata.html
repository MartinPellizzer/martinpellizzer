Sanguisorba officinalis var. latifoliata

Sanguisorba officinalis var. latifoliata, commonly known as broad-leaved sorrel, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It features upright, branched stems that bear clusters of bright red, urn-shaped flowers. The leaves are broad, heart-shaped, and have a distinctive reddish or purple hue on the underside. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
