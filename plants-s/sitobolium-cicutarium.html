Sitobolium cicutarium

Sitobolium cicutarium, commonly known as the yellow brome, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is an annual grass that typically grows in disturbed soils, such as roadsides and fields. The plant has slender, erect stems and produces small, yellowish-green spikelets that contain numerous tiny grains. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, and it is often found in dry, open habitats.

The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
