Sida ciliosa

Sida ciliosa, commonly known as silky sida, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant features small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are ovate with hairy surfaces, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Sida ciliosa is known for its ability to thrive in arid conditions, making it an important species in ecological studies of drought-resistant flora.
