Spinifex elegans

Spinifex elegans, commonly known as the slender spinifex, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, white to pale blue flowers. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal and arid regions.

Spinifex elegans is well adapted to harsh environmental conditions, making it a resilient component of its ecosystem.
