Scrophularia verticillata

Scrophularia verticillata, commonly known as blueweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe, North Africa, and parts of Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. The plant features upright stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are clustered in whorls along the stem, producing striking blue or violet petals.

Scrophularia verticillata is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
