Smilax china var. trinervula

Smilax china var. trinervula, commonly known as the Chinese arrowroot or Chinese smilax, is a perennial vine native to eastern Asia.

It features a woody stem with thorny nodes and produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which are typically three-lobed and have a rough texture. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that contains several seeds.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
