Sedum tsonanum

Sedum tsonanum, commonly known as the Chinese stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to China.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow or orange. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its low maintenance and drought tolerance make it an ideal plant for xeriscaping.
