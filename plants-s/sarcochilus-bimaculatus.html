Sarcochilus bimaculatus

Sarcochilus bimaculatus, commonly known as the two-spotted sarcochilus, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It features a rhizomatous growth habit with erect, linear leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green, with distinctive two-spotted petals. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique floral structure.
