Saussurea licentiana

Saussurea licentiana, commonly known as the Licentian thistle, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces yellow flowers that are surrounded by spiny bracts, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in rocky and arid environments, often growing at high altitudes.

Saussurea licentiana is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
