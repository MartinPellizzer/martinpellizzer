Solanum oliganthum

Solanum oliganthum, commonly known as the small-flower nightshade, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant produces small, white or pale green flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Solanum oliganthum is often found in humid, shaded environments and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.
