Saussurea yangii

Saussurea yangii, commonly known as the Yunnan saffron, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems and dark purple to reddish flowers. The plant produces small, yellowish to brownish seeds that are dispersed by wind. Its fibrous roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Due to its unique habitat and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
