Scutellaria heterochroa

Scutellaria heterochroa, commonly known as the heterochroous skullcap, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, including China and Korea.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. It is often found in rocky or forested habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Scutellaria heterochroa is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
