Sphaerolobium linophyllum

Sphaerolobium linophyllum, commonly known as the yellow globemallow, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its globular, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, bean-like pods that contain its seeds.

It is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
