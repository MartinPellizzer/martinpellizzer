Stizolobium tuberosum

Stizolobium tuberosum, commonly known as the tuberous legume, is a perennial shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink, followed by pod fruits containing several seeds.

It is often found in dry, sandy soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
