Schefflera sibayakensis

Schefflera sibayakensis, commonly known as the Sibayak Schefflera, is a tropical plant native to Indonesia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of compound leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Schefflera sibayakensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor conditions.
