Salacia elliptica var. oblongifolia

Salacia elliptica var. oblongifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf salacia, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elliptical, often slightly elongated leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, which bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a small, fleshy berry that turns red when mature.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
