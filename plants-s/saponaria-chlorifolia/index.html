Saponaria Chlorifolia

Saponaria chlorifolia is a rare and striking plant known for its unique foliage and delicate flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, it thrives in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are a striking shade of blue-green, which sets it apart from other members of the carnation family. The plant produces clusters of pink or white flowers that bloom in late spring. Due to its limited range and specific habitat requirements, Saponaria chlorifolia is considered a vulnerable species.
