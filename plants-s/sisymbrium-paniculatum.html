Sisymbrium paniculatum

Sisymbrium paniculatum, commonly known as yellow rocket, is an annual herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, branched stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers arranged in dense, branching clusters called panicles. Its leaves are alternate, ovate to lanceolate, and often have a slightly lobed or toothed appearance.

Sisymbrium paniculatum is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in disturbed soils, making it a common weed in agricultural settings.
