Spiraea fortunei var. semperflorens

Spiraea fortunei var. semperflorens, commonly known as the everblooming spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and profuse clusters of small, white to pink flowers that bloom repeatedly throughout the growing season. The plant produces oval, dark green leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its showy inflorescences. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is well-suited for garden borders or as a specimen plant.

Its adaptability and long blooming period make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
