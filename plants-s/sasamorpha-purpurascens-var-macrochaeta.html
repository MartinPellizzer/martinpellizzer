Sasamorpha purpurascens var. macrochaeta

Sasamorpha purpurascens var. macrochaeta, commonly known as the purple-flowered sasamorpha, is a species of flowering plant in the Acanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from violet to deep purple. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded, moist environments. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The variety macrochaeta is distinguished by its longer, more prominent bracts compared to the typical form.
