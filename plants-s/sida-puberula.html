Sida puberula

Sida puberula, commonly known as rough sida, is a flowering plant species native to the Americas.

It is a herbaceous annual or perennial plant that typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant has hairy, often glandular leaves and produces small, white to pale blue flowers. Its fruit is a capsule that contains numerous tiny seeds.

Sida puberula is often found in fields, roadsides, and other sunny, well-drained areas.
