Sideritis hyssopifolia var. longespica

Sideritis hyssopifolia var. longespica, commonly known as the long-stemmed sidewort, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and has small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This variety is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
