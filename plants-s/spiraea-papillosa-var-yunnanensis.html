Spiraea papillosa var. yunnanensis

Spiraea papillosa var. yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan spiraea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit and clusters of small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptic, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. This species is often found in moist, well-drained soils in mountainous regions.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
