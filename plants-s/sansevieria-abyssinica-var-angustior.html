Sansevieria abyssinica var. angustior

Sansevieria abyssinica var. angustior, commonly known as the narrow-leaf African spear, is a striking tropical plant native to Ethiopia.

It features long, narrow, upright leaves that are typically bright green with subtle variegation. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it an ideal choice for indoor environments. Its leaves are sword-shaped and can grow up to several feet in height, adding a dramatic visual impact to any space.

This variety is also noted for its air-purifying qualities, contributing to improved indoor air quality.
