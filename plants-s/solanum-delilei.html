Solanum delilei

Solanum delilei, commonly known as the Delile's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers. The plant typically grows as an annual or biennial herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Solanum delilei is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.
