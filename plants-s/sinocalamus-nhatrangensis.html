Sinocalamus nhatrangensis

Sinocalamus nhatrangensis, commonly known as the Nhatrang bamboo, is a species of grass belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in Vietnam and parts of southern China. This bamboo is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 10 meters. The leaves are long and narrow, with a smooth, grass-like texture, and the plant thrives in moist, fertile soils.

It plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing habitat for various wildlife species.
