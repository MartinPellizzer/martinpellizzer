Strelitzia reginae var. lemoinieri

Strelitzia reginae var. lemoinieri, commonly known as the yellow strelitzia, is a striking hybrid of the bird-of-paradise plant.

It is characterized by its elongated, banana-like leaves that are a deep green with a subtle yellow hue. The flowers are unique, featuring a vibrant yellow spathe that resembles a bird in flight. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.

It requires well-draining soil, partial shade, and regular watering to thrive.
