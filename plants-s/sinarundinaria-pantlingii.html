Sinarundinaria pantlingii

Sinarundinaria pantlingii, commonly known as the Chinese cliffbrake, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are typically dark green and have a leathery texture. Sinarundinaria pantlingii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
