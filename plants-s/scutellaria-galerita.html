Scutellaria galerita

Scutellaria galerita, commonly known as narrowleaf skullcap, is a small perennial herb native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. The plant features slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are violet to blue in color, arranged in whorls along the stem.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
