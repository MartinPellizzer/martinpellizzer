Senecio pissisi

Senecio pissisi, commonly known as the Pissisi groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It has silver-gray leaves covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid environments.

Senecio pissisi is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
