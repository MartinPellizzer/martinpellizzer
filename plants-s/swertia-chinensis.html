Swertia chinensis

Swertia chinensis, commonly known as Chinese swertia, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its slender stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Swertia chinensis is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
