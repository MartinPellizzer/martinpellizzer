Schismatoglottis calyptratoides

Schismatoglottis calyptratoides, commonly known as the helmet orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a short, creeping rhizome and produces striking, helmet-shaped flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with a red or purple lip. The plant has elliptical, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, and it thrives in shaded, moist environments.

Schismatoglottis calyptratoides is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouse settings due to its specific humidity and temperature requirements.
