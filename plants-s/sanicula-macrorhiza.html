Sanicula macrorhiza

Sanicula macrorhiza, commonly known as large-root sandwort, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It belongs to the family Ranunculaceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in late spring, which are clustered in a raceme. Its roots are thick and fleshy, adapted for storing nutrients and moisture.
