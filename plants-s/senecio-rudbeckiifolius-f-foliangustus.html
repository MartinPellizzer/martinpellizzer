Senecio rudbeckiifolius f. foliangustus

Senecio rudbeckiifolius f. foliangustus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf Senecio, is a variant of the Senecio rudbeckiifolius species, characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is native to South Africa. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun exposure. Its flowering period occurs during the summer months, producing small, yellowish flowers in clustered inflorescences.

Due to its unique foliage and ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it may be challenging to grow outside its native habitat.
