Sorghum zizanioides

Sorghum zizanioides, commonly known as jointed grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. This plant is often found in wetland areas and can thrive in a variety of soil types. It plays an important role in ecosystems as both a food source for wildlife and a potential invasive species in certain regions.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a significant component of wetland vegetation.
