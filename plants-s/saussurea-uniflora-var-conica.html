Saussurea uniflora var. conica

Saussurea uniflora var. conica, commonly known as the Himalayan tower gentian, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and a single, showy blue or purple flower at the top. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette at the base. It thrives in cold, arid environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
