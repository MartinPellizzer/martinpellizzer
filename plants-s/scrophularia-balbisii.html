Scrophularia balbisii

Scrophularia balbisii, commonly known as Balbis's figwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or dry soils and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces showy, two-lipped flowers that are often pink or purple in color. It belongs to the family Plantaginaceae and is known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.

Scrophularia balbisii is also valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
