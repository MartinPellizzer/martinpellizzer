Scleria laevis

Scleria laevis, commonly known as the smooth bulbous sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. Scleria laevis produces small, clustered flowers that are often green or brown, and it thrives in wet or marshy habitats.
