Seychellaria thomassetii

Seychellaria thomassetii, commonly known as the Seychelles starling tree, is a rare endemic species native to the Seychelles.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and showy, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, black, berry-like fruits that are an important food source for local wildlife. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered vulnerable to environmental changes and habitat loss.
