Senecio kiwuensis

Senecio kiwuensis, commonly known as the African Daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy yellow flowers with a central disk, surrounded by ray florets that resemble petals. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Senecio kiwuensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
