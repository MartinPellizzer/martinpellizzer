Sarcochilus robertsii

Sarcochilus robertsii, commonly known as the Roberts' sarcochilus, is a terrestrial orchid native to New Zealand.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome and produces fleshy, strap-like leaves. The plant blooms with yellow to orange flowers that have a distinctive, wrinkled appearance. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a conservation concern.
