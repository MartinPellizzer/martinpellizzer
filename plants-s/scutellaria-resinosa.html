Scutellaria resinosa

Scutellaria resinosa, commonly known as resinous skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular blue or violet flowers that bloom in late summer. Scutellaria resinosa is known for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported calming effects.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ecologically significant species in its native habitat.
