Schefflera cavaleriei

Schefflera cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features a dense, upright growth habit with thick, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red berries, though it is primarily grown for its ornamental foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering, making it a popular indoor plant.

Its adaptability to various environmental conditions contributes to its widespread cultivation.
