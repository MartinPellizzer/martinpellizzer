Solanum denticulatum

Solanum denticulatum, commonly known as the toothed nightshade, is a shrub native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like tomatoes and peppers. The plant features small, yellow flowers and produces berries that turn from green to red as they mature. Its leaves are characterized by their finely toothed margins, giving it its common name.

The plant contains toxic compounds, making it potentially harmful if ingested.
