Stelis graminea

Stelis graminea, commonly known as the grassy orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and compact growth habit. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. Stelis graminea thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
