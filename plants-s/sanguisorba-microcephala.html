Sanguisorba microcephala

Sanguisorba microcephala, commonly known as dwarf dropwort, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It features upright, branched stems that bear small, clustered leaves. The plant produces tiny, reddish-brown flowers that bloom in late summer. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their astringent properties.

Sanguisorba microcephala is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in moist, shady conditions.
