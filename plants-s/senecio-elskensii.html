Senecio elskensii

Senecio elskensii, commonly known as the Eland's milkweed, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow flowers and linear leaves. The plant is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid environments. It has a low-growing habit and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
