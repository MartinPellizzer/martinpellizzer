Stipularia elliptica

Stipularia elliptica, commonly known as the narrow-leaf stipularia, is a flowering plant species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its elliptical, narrow leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. The plant produces small, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators. Stipularia elliptica is often found in rocky or sandy soils, thriving in full sun to partial shade.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ornamental addition to native plant collections.
