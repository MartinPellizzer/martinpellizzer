Stelis flavida

Stelis flavida, commonly known as the yellow stelis orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features slender, often branched stems and produces showy, yellowish-orange flowers with a distinctive spurred lip. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air and rain.

Stelis flavida is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or terrariums.
