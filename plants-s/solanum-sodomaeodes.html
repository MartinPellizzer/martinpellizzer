Solanum sodomaeodes

Solanum sodomaeodes, commonly known as the Jerusalem cherry, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant produces small, bright orange berries that are often cultivated as ornamental plants. These berries are edible but can be toxic in large quantities.

It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.
