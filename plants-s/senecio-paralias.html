Senecio paralias

Senecio paralias, commonly known as the coastal groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically thrives in coastal and sandy environments. The plant features narrow, silver-green leaves and produces small, yellow flowers in clusters. It is adapted to withstand salt spray and harsh coastal conditions.

Its botanical characteristics make it a hardy and resilient species often found in dune ecosystems.
