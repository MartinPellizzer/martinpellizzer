Saussurea clarkeana

Saussurea clarkeana, commonly known as the Tibetan saffron.

It is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas, particularly in regions of Tibet, Nepal, and northern India. The plant is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems and dense clusters of yellow to orange flowers. Its medicinal properties have been recognized in traditional Tibetan medicine for treating various ailments.

The plant thrives in cold, arid environments and is adapted to withstand harsh climatic conditions.
