Stelis debilis

Stelis debilis, commonly known as the weak orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant has slender, wiry stems and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in warm, moist conditions with good air circulation.

Due to its delicate nature, it requires careful cultivation to ensure its survival.
