Specklinia neobradei

Specklinia neobradei, commonly known as the Neobrada's bryophyte, is a rare and enigmatic member of the moss family.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated stems and dense, cushion-like growth form. The plant exhibits a unique combination of green and reddish hues in its foliage, which becomes more pronounced under certain light conditions. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its limited distribution and specialized habitat requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species in conservation efforts.
