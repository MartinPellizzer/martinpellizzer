Specklinia glandulosa

Specklinia glandulosa, commonly known as the glandular specklinia, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often hairy foliage. The plant typically produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
