Scorzonera koeieana

Scorzonera koeieana, commonly known as the Koeie's scorzonera, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and basal leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers with ray florets, typically blooming in spring. Its root system is fibrous and often used in traditional medicine.

Scorzonera koeieana thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland and rocky habitats.
