Sitanion × anomalum

Sitanion × anomalum, commonly known as the hybrid sedge, is a member of the Cyperaceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its grass-like appearance with slender, triangular stems. The plant typically grows in wet or moist environments, such as marshes and wetlands. Its leaves are narrow and have a rough texture, while its flowers are small and clustered in spikelets.

Sitanion × anomalum is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
