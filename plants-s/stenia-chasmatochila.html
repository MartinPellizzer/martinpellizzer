Stenia chasmatochila

Stenia chasmatochila, commonly known as the narrow-leafed stenia, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is characterized by its slender, upright stems. The plant produces small, white to pale purple flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are narrow and often have a hairy texture, which helps reduce water loss in arid conditions.

Stenia chasmatochila is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
