Selaginella lakkidiana

Selaginella lakkidiana, commonly known as the tropical spikemoss, is a member of the Selaginellaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is a low-growing, evergreen plant that typically forms dense mats on the forest floor. The plant has small, scale-like leaves that are often clustered around the stem, giving it a unique appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Selaginella lakkidiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant in tropical gardens.
