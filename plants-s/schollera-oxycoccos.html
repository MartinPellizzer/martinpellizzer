Schollera oxycoccos

Schollera oxycoccos, commonly known as the rock fig, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It has leathery, oval-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by edible, pear-shaped fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Schollera oxycoccos is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
