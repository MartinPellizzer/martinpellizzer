Saxifraga × timbalii

Saxifraga × timbalii, commonly known as the hybrid saxifrage, is a low-growing, herbaceous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features dense clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a subtle purple or red tinge. The plant has succulent-like leaves that are typically fleshy and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water retention. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for alpine gardens.

Saxifraga × timbalii is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in cooler climates.
