Scorzonera hispanica subsp. asphodeloides

Scorzonera hispanica subsp.

asphodeloides, commonly known as Spanish scorzonera, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and North Africa. It features a robust root system with fleshy, tuberous roots that are often used in traditional medicine.

The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green in color, with a rough texture. Its flowers are yellow, with five petals, and are arranged in a loose cluster at the top of the stem.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.
