Squilla maritima

Squilla maritima, commonly known as the sea squill, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that grows in coastal areas and saline soils. The plant produces striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming a rosette at the base.

Squilla maritima is adapted to salt spray and can thrive in harsh maritime environments.
