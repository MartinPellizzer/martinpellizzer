Stelis agatha

Stelis agatha, commonly known as the Agatha's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant typically produces a single, slender stem that bears a few lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with a distinctive coloration that varies from white to pale yellow.

Stelis agatha thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
