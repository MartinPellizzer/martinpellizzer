Senegalia Garrettii

Senegalia garrettii is a hardy, drought-resistant shrub native to arid regions of Africa and the Middle East. It thrives in harsh environments with minimal water and is often found in rocky soils and savannah landscapes. This plant is valued for its ability to prevent soil erosion and its potential use in traditional medicine. Its small, pale flowers and thorny branches make it a distinctive feature in its native habitat. Despite its tough appearance, Senegalia garrettii plays an important ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
