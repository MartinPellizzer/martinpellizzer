Sedum adenocalyx

Sedum adenocalyx, commonly known as the silver rose, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white powder, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in arid climates.
