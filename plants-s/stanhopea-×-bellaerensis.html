Stanhopea × bellaerensis

Stanhopea × bellaerensis, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Stanhopea plicata and Stanhopea arnhemica.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to the rainforests of northern Australia and southern New Guinea. The plant features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow with red or orange markings, and it blooms in late spring to early summer. Stanhopea × bellaerensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.

It requires high humidity, bright indirect light, and well-draining soil to thrive.
