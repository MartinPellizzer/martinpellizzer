Sesleria italica

Sesleria italica, commonly known as the Italian brownsedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the family Poaceae and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bloom in late spring or early summer.

Sesleria italica is often used in rock gardens and as a ground cover due to its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
