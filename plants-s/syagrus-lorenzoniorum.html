Syagrus lorenzoniorum

Syagrus lorenzoniorum, commonly known as the Lorentz palm, is a species of palm native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils and salty air. It produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into clustered fruits.

Syagrus lorenzoniorum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in landscaping in tropical regions.
