Scutellaria ussuriensis f. humilis

Scutellaria ussuriensis f. humilis, commonly known as the Ussuri Scutellaria, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue to purple in color, arranged in whorled clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal potential, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
