Senna rizzinii

Senna rizzinii, commonly known as the golden senna, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its bipinnate leaves, which are divided into small, delicate leaflets. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to its habitat. Senna rizzinii thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for cultivation in warm climates.

It is also valued for its medicinal properties and use in traditional herbal remedies.
