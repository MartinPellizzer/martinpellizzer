Senecio ceratophyllus var. nanus

Senecio ceratophyllus var. nanus, commonly known as the dwarf sunset lily, is a low-growing succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its compact size and ornamental flowers, it is a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
