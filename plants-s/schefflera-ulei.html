Schefflera ulei

Schefflera ulei, commonly known as the African schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of leaflets, giving it a distinctive, palm-like appearance. The plant has compound, pinnately arranged leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. It produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by yellowish fruits.

Schefflera ulei is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
