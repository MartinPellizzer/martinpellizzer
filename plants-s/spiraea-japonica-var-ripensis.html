Spiraea japonica var. ripensis

Spiraea japonica var. ripensis, commonly known as Japanese spirea or river spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy on the underside. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found near streams or in woodland areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade.
