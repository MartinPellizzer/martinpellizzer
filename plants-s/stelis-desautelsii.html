Stelis desautelsii

Stelis desautelsii, commonly known as the Desautels's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often found in the understory of tropical forests. The plant features a slender, erect stem with several elongated, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually in a raceme, with white or pale pink petals.

Stelis desautelsii is valued for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
