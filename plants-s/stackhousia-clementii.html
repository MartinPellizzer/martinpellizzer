Stackhousia clementii

Stackhousia clementii, commonly known as the Mount Lofty wattle, is a small shrub native to South Australia.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring, adding a striking visual contrast to its grey-green foliage. The plant has a compact growth habit with slender, wiry stems and small, leathery leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Stackhousia clementii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and seasonal floral display.
