Scabiosa Columbaria Subsp. Triandra

Scabiosa columbaria subsp. triandra is a lesser-known yet striking member of the Caprifoliaceae family. Native to southern Europe, this plant thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas. Its unique, daisy-like flowers feature pinkish-white petals and a central cluster of stamens, giving it a delicate yet bold appearance. Known for its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty and ability to attract pollinators. Despite its charm, it remains underappreciated compared to more common garden flowers.
