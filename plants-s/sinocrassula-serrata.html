Sinocrassula serrata

Sinocrassula serrata, commonly known as the serrated stonecrop, is a low-growing succulent native to China.

It features fleshy, upright leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tint. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact size and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
