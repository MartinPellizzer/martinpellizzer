Solanum phlomidifolium

Solanum phlomidifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant features small, greenish-white flowers and produces berries that turn from green to red as they mature. Its leaves are typically narrow and have a slightly hairy texture.

It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its compact size and attractive foliage.
