Sinningia × youngeana

Sinningia × youngeana, commonly known as the pink fairy wand, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the Andes Mountains in South America, where it thrives in cool, humid environments. The plant is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple, often with a contrasting throat. Its leaves are typically velvety and dark green, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Sinningia × youngeana is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
