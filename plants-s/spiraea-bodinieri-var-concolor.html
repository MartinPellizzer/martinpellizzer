Spiraea bodinieri var. concolor

Spiraea bodinieri var. concolor, commonly known as the Chinese spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features slender, arching stems that produce clusters of delicate, white to pinkish flowers in early summer. The foliage is typically bright green, turning yellow in the fall. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation in gardens.
