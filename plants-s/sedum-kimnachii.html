Sedum kimnachii

Sedum kimnachii, commonly known as the Kimnach's stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit and fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy and drought-tolerant species.

Sedum kimnachii is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
