Senecio hederifolius

Senecio hederifolius, commonly known as the ivy-leaved groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that features dark green, heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Due to its attractive foliage and easy maintenance, it is often cultivated in gardens for ornamental purposes.
