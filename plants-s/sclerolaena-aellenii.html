Sclerolaena aellenii

Sclerolaena aellenii, commonly known as the Cape rose, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its woody stems and dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Sclerolaena aellenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience to harsh environmental conditions.
