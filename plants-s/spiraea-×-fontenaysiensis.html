Spiraea × fontenaysiensis

Spiraea × fontenaysiensis, commonly known as the Fontenay weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy, cone-shaped clusters of pink or red flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has narrow, dark green leaves that provide attractive foliage throughout the growing season. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Spiraea × fontenaysiensis is relatively low maintenance and thrives in full sun to partial shade.
