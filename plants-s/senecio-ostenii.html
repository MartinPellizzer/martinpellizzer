Senecio ostenii

Senecio ostenii, commonly known as the Osten's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Senecio ostenii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and unique appearance.
