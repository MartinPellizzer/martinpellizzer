Sida procumbens

Sida procumbens, commonly known as prostrate sida, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a low-growing, herbaceous species that typically forms dense mats on the ground. The plant features small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in loose inflorescences. Its leaves are ovate to elliptic in shape and covered with fine hairs.

Sida procumbens is often found in disturbed areas, such as roadsides and fields, and is adapted to a variety of soil types.
