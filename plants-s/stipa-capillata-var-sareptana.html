Stipa capillata var. sareptana

Stipa capillata var. sareptana, commonly known as the Sareptan feathergrass, is a variant of the slender wheatgrass species.

It is a perennial grass native to regions of Eastern Europe and Western Asia. The plant features slender, wiry stems with fine, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. Its inflorescence consists of a narrow, spike-like cluster of small, spikelets that give it a feathery appearance.

This grass is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.
