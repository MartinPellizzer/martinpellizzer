Sisymbrium daghestanicum

Sisymbrium daghestanicum, commonly known as the Daghestan mustard, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other mustard plants. The plant produces small, yellow flowers and has linear, hairy leaves. It is typically found in rocky or dry habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.

Sisymbrium daghestanicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some historical use in local traditional medicine.
