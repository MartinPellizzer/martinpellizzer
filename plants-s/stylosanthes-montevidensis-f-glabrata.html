Stylosanthes montevidensis f. glabrata

Stylosanthes montevidensis f. glabrata, commonly known as the glabrous stylosanthes, is a perennial legume native to South America.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is typically found in dry, sandy soils and is well adapted to arid conditions. It has a deep root system that helps it survive in nutrient-poor environments.

This variety is valued for its use in forage and soil stabilization in agricultural settings.
