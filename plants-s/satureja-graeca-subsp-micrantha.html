Satureja graeca subsp. micrantha

Satureja graeca subsp.

micrantha, commonly known as Greek thyme, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It features small, aromatic leaves and pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and has a compact, low-growing habit. Its essential oils contain thymol and carvacrol, contributing to its strong aromatic profile.

It is valued in both culinary and medicinal applications for its distinctive flavor and potential health benefits.
