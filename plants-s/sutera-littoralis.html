Sutera littoralis

Sutera littoralis, commonly known as the coastal mallow, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its showy, pink or white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has hairy, ribbed stems and heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in coastal areas and sandy soils, often growing near the sea.

Sutera littoralis is adapted to salty air and can tolerate moderate drought conditions.
