Statice bocconei

Statice bocconei, commonly known as the Boccone's sea lavender, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit. Statice bocconei is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for coastal and dry garden environments.
