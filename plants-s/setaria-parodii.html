Setaria parodii

Setaria parodii, commonly known as parodii foxtail, is a grass species native to Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, narrow, and often spiky inflorescence that resembles a foxtail. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are linear and covered with fine hairs, contributing to its drought tolerance.

Setaria parodii is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and can thrive in a variety of soil types.
