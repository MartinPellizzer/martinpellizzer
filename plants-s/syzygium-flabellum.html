Syzygium flabellum

Syzygium flabellum, commonly known as the fan-flower or white fan-flower, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its large, showy white or pale pink flowers that resemble a fan, hence its common name. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters.

Syzygium flabellum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
