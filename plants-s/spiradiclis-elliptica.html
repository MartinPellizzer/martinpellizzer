Spiradiclis elliptica

Spiradiclis elliptica, commonly known as the spiral leaf plant, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. Spiradiclis elliptica thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.
