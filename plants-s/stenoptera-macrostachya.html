Stenoptera macrostachya

Stenoptera macrostachya, commonly known as the giant lemur orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its large, lemur-like flowers that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its unique, pendulous inflorescences. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Stenoptera macrostachya thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high levels of moisture and nutrient-rich soil.
