Senecio mirus

Senecio mirus, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of light conditions, making it a popular houseplant. Its flowers typically range in color from white to purple, and it produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Senecio mirus is also valued for its ornamental beauty and ease of care.
