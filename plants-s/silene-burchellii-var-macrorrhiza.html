Silene burchellii var. macrorrhiza

Silene burchellii var. macrorrhiza, commonly known as the large-lobed campion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has palmate leaves with lobed margins, and its flowers are typically arranged in dense clusters at the top of the stems. It is often found in open grasslands and along riverbanks, thriving in well-drained soils.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
