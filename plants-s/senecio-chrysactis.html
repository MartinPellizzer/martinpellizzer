Senecio chrysactis

Senecio chrysactis, commonly known as the yellow star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features wiry, branched stems and produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its drought-resistant nature.

Senecio chrysactis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
