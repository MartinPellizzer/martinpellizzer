Sophora albicans

Sophora albicans, commonly known as the white sophora, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy white flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it beneficial for soil enrichment.
