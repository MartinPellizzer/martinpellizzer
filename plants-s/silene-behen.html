Silene behen

Silene behen, commonly known as Indian madder, is a flowering plant native to Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and clusters of pink or white flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. Its leaves are usually alternate and have a slightly hairy texture.

Silene behen is often found in grassy areas and along roadsides, thriving in temperate climates.
