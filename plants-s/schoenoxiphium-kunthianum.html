Schoenoxiphium kunthianum

Schoenoxiphium kunthianum, commonly known as the Kunth's false gentian, is a flowering plant native to the Andes of South America.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in high-altitude environments, often in rocky or alpine regions. The plant features showy blue to purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged oppositely along the stems.
