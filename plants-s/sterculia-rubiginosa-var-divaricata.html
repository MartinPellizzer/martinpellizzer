Sterculia rubiginosa var. divaricata

Sterculia rubiginosa var. divaricata, commonly known as the red-stemmed sterculia, is a shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its rough, reddish bark and compound leaves with elongated leaflets. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, followed by woody pods containing flat, elliptical seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, shaded areas.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive species within the Sterculiaceae family.
