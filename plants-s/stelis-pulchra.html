Stelis pulchra

Stelis pulchra, commonly known as the beautiful stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. The plant features slender, erect stems with lance-shaped leaves that are often a vibrant green. Its flowers are showy, with a white or pale pink coloration and a distinctive, fragrant scent.

Stelis pulchra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
