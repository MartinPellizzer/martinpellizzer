Senecio verdoorniae

Senecio verdoorniae, commonly known as the Verdoorn's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions. It produces small yellow flowers that bloom in spring, and its leaves are usually hairy and arranged in a rosette pattern.
