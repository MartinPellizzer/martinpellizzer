Scorzonera petiolaris

Scorzonera petiolaris, commonly known as the slender scorzonera, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and robust, hairy stems. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer, followed by spiky, bract-like fruits. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, often with a reddish or purple hue at the base.

Scorzonera petiolaris is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
