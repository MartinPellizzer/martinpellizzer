Salacia accedens

Salacia accedens, commonly known as the yellow salacia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Salicaceae, which includes willows and poplars. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, yellowish-green flowers. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a glandular surface.

Salacia accedens is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth.
