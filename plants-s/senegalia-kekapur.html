Senegalia kekapur

Senegalia kekapur, commonly known as the Kekapur acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant is adapted to survive in harsh environments with its deep root system and ability to fix nitrogen. Its flowers are typically yellow or pale green, attracting various pollinators.

The bark and leaves have been used traditionally for their medicinal properties.
