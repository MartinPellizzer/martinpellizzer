Scrophularia heldreichii

Scrophularia heldreichii, commonly known as the Heldreich's figwort, is a perennial herb native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It typically grows in rocky or limestone soils, often in mountainous regions. The plant features elongated, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of tubular, pink or purple flowers. It is part of the Scrophulariaceae family, which includes many species with medicinal and ornamental value.

Scrophularia heldreichii is noted for its adaptability to harsh environments and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental appeal.
