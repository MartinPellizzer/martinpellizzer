Sarothamnus vulgaris var. foliosus

Sarothamnus vulgaris var. foliosus, commonly known as the leafy sea-blite, is a flowering plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that thrives in coastal and saline environments. The plant features succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in arid conditions. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, and it is often found in sandy or rocky soils.

This variety is notable for its adaptability to harsh coastal climates and its role in stabilizing dunes.
