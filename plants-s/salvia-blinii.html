Salvia blinii

Salvia blinii, commonly known as the Chinese sage or blue sage, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a bluish-green hue and produces striking violet-blue flowers in whorled clusters. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and traditional medicinal uses.

Salvia blinii thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and herbal collections.
