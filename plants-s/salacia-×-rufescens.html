Salacia × rufescens

Salacia × rufescens, commonly known as the red salacia, is a hybrid shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of red and orange. The plant produces bright red berries that are edible and often used in traditional medicine. Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Salacia × rufescens is also valued for its role in supporting local biodiversity and ecosystem health.
