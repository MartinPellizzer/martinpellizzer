Stenostelma Corniculatum

Stenostelma corniculatum is a unique and lesser-known plant species that thrives in specific ecological niches. It is characterized by its distinctive, often spiny leaves and small, clustered flowers. Native to arid and semi-arid regions, this plant has adapted to harsh environmental conditions. Its ability to store water makes it resilient in dry climates. Despite its hardy nature, it remains a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
