Sideritis bolleana

Sideritis bolleana, commonly known as the Balkan ironweed, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants known for their medicinal properties. The plant is characterized by its slender stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of pink to purple. Sideritis bolleana is often found in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments.

It is valued for its potential medicinal uses, including as a remedy for respiratory and digestive ailments.
