Specklinia sulphurea

Specklinia sulphurea, commonly known as the golden moss orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, moss-like growth habit and its striking yellow-green inflorescence. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically arranged in a raceme, with sepals and petals that are often tinged with golden hues. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Due to its unique appearance and rarity, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
