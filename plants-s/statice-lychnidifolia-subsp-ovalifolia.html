Statice lychnidifolia subsp. ovalifolia

Statice lychnidifolia subsp.

ovalifolia, commonly known as the oval-leaf statice, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa. It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its erect, branching stems and clusters of small, showy flowers.

The leaves are narrow and oval-shaped, giving the plant its subspecific name. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in coastal and sandy habitats.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is commonly cultivated in gardens for its long-lasting blooms.
