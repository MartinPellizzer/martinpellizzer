Senecio australis

Senecio australis, commonly known as the Australian groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

Senecio australis is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
