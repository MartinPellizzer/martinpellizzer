Scutellaria orientalis var. porphrantha

Scutellaria orientalis var. porphrantha, commonly known as the oriental blue skullcap, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that bear opposite pairs of lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular blue or purple flowers during the summer months. Its flowers are typically arranged in whorls along the stem, giving it a distinctive appearance.

This species is valued in traditional herbal medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
