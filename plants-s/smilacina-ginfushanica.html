Smilacina ginfushanica

Smilacina ginfushanica, commonly known as the Yunnan smilacine, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It belongs to the family Smilacaceae and is characterized by its climbing habit and distinctive, spiral-shaped stems. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in racemes, and it is often found in shaded, moist forest environments.
