Sparganium emersum f. angustifolium

Sparganium emersum f. angustifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved burr rush, is a flowering plant in the Cyperaceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its tall, erect stems that bear spiky, cylindrical inflorescences. The leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slightly rough texture. This plant typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of water bodies.

It plays an important ecological role by providing habitat and food for various aquatic and terrestrial organisms.
