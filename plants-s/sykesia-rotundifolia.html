Sykesia rotundifolia

Sykesia rotundifolia, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are round or heart-shaped, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Sykesia rotundifolia is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
