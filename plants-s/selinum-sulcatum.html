Selinum sulcatum

Selinum sulcatum, commonly known as the zigzag goldenroot, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzagging stems and yellowish flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Selinum sulcatum is valued in herbal medicine for its potential health benefits.
