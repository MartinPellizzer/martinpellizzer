Solanum xanti

Solanum xanti, commonly known as the Xanthoxylopsis, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

The plant produces berries that are typically yellow or orange when ripe, containing seeds that can be dispersed by animals. Solanum xanti is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to withstand arid conditions.
