Stelis petropolitana

Stelis petropolitana, commonly known as the Polaris orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to the Caribbean.

It typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. The plant features long, slender stems and produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water retention.

Stelis petropolitana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
