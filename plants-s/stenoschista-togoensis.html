Stenoschista togoensis

Stenoschista togoensis, commonly known as the Togo grass.

It is a perennial grass species native to West Africa, particularly found in countries like Togo, Ghana, and Benin. The plant is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and compact growth habit. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in open woodlands or grasslands.

Stenoschista togoensis is valued for its potential in soil stabilization and as a forage plant for livestock.
