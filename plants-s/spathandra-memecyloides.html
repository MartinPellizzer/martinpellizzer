Spathandra memecyloides

Spathandra memecyloides, commonly known as the red-flowered spathandra, is a striking member of the Araceae family.

It is native to the southeastern United States, particularly Florida and Georgia, where it thrives in moist, woodland habitats. The plant is characterized by its large, showy inflorescence, which consists of a spadix surrounded by a vibrant red spathe. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and glossy, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Spathandra memecyloides is often cultivated for its unique floral display and ability to add color to shaded garden areas.
