Sisymbrium columnae var. orientale

Sisymbrium columnae var. orientale, commonly known as the oriental heartsease, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant produces elongated, slender pods that contain several seeds, typical of the mustard family. Native to regions of southern Europe and western Asia, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.
