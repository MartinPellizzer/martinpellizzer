Satyrium hallackii subsp. hallackii

Satyrium hallackii subsp.

hallackii, commonly known as the Hallack's satyrium, is a member of the Orchidaceae family. It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist habitats such as forests and woodlands.

The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip that is often marked with purple or brown spots. It is native to parts of Central and South America, particularly in countries like Brazil and Peru.
