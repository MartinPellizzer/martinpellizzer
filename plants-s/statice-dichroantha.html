Statice dichroantha

Statice dichroantha, commonly known as the two-colored glasswort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range from pink to white. The plant typically grows in saline or coastal environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its foliage is succulent, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Statice dichroantha is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant color and resilience.
