Saxifraga heuffelii

Saxifraga heuffelii, commonly known as the Alpine saxifrage, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It typically thrives in rocky and alpine environments, where it forms dense mats of foliage. The plant features small, usually green or reddish leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers, which are typically pink or white, bloom in early summer and are clustered in dense inflorescences.

Saxifraga heuffelii is adapted to harsh conditions, making it a resilient member of high-altitude flora.
