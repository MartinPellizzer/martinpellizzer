Solanum mantenangense

Solanum mantenangense, commonly known as the Mantanani nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and is characterized by its small, dark green leaves and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are often used in traditional medicine. Solanum mantenangense belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is known for its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties.

However, its berries contain toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
