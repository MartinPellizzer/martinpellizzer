Synaedrys balansae

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its finely divided, lacy fronds that resemble those of other ghost ferns. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and requires high humidity and well-drained soil to flourish. Due to habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable and is often sought after by botanists and conservationists.
