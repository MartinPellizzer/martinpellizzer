Sarcocaulon camdeboense

Sarcocaulon camdeboense, commonly known as the Camdeboo succulent, is a unique plant native to South Africa.

It is a dwarf shrub characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and fleshy, cylindrical stems. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in succulent botany.
