Synadenium volkensii

Synadenium volkensii, commonly known as the horned cucumber, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiky, green fruits that resemble small horns, which are covered in soft, velvety hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers with five petals, typically blooming in late summer. Its leaves are large, heart-shaped, and have a rough texture.

Synadenium volkensii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and adaptability to various growing conditions.
