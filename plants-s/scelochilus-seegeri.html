Scelochilus seegeri

Scelochilus seegeri, commonly known as the Seeger's rock orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes, often in shaded or semi-shaded environments. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single or few erect flowers that are usually pale green to yellowish in color.
