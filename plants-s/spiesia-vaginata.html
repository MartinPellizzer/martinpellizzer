Spiesia vaginata

Spiesia vaginata, commonly known as the Cape jasmine, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its attractive, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a waxy texture.

Spiesia vaginata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in various soil conditions.
