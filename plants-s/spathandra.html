Spathandra

Spathandra, commonly known as the Christmas star, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer to early fall. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas and has a rhizomatous root system that helps it spread. Its leaves are broad, heart-shaped, and often have a velvety texture.

Spathandra is valued in gardens for its ornamental beauty and ability to thrive in woodland environments.
