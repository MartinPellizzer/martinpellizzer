Spiraea japonica var. ruberrima

Spiraea japonica var. ruberrima, commonly known as the Japanese spirea or redleaf spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features dense clusters of small, showy flowers that range from white to pink and, in some varieties, a striking red hue. The plant has slender, arching branches and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a versatile choice for landscaping.

The leaves turn vibrant shades of red and orange in the fall, adding seasonal interest to the landscape.
