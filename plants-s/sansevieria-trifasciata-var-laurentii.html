Sansevieria trifasciata var. laurentii

Sansevieria trifasciata var. laurentii, commonly known as the Laurentii Snake Plant, is a striking member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to tropical Africa and is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are dark green with prominent yellow variegations. The plant is highly adaptable, thriving in low-light conditions and requiring minimal water, making it an excellent choice for indoor environments. Its air-purifying qualities have also contributed to its popularity among plant enthusiasts.

The variegation in its leaves is a result of genetic mutations that affect chlorophyll production, resulting in the unique color patterns.
