Scrophularia nodosa var. purpurascens

Scrophularia nodosa var. purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-flowered figwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features upright stems that are often hairy and bear opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces clusters of tubular, two-lipped flowers that are typically purple or reddish-purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges or rocky habitats.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and its role in supporting pollinators.
