Solidago ceylanica

Solidago ceylanica, commonly known as the Ceylon goldenrod, is a perennial herb native to Sri Lanka.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces bright yellow flower clusters in the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Its botanical structure supports its role as a nectar source for various pollinators.
