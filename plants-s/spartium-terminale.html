Spartium terminale

Spartium terminale, commonly known as needlepod bupleurum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Leguminosae (Fabaceae) family, characterized by its pod-like fruits and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and has small, compound leaves with a distinctive needle-like appearance. Its flowers are usually pale yellow and appear in clusters, followed by elongated, slender pods that contain the seeds.

Spartium terminale is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its potential health benefits.
