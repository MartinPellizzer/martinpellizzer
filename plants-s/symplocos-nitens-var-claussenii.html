Symplocos nitens var. claussenii

Symplocos nitens var. claussenii, commonly known as the Philippine persimmon, is a shrub or small tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its elliptical, leathery leaves that are often reddish underneath. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by orange-red, fleshy fruits that are edible. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in montane forests.
