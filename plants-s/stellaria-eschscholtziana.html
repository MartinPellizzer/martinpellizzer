Stellaria eschscholtziana

Stellaria eschscholtziana, commonly known as the California starwort, is a small annual herb native to the western United States.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant features slender, branching stems and small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with hairy surfaces that help reduce water loss.

Stellaria eschscholtziana is often found in coastal and inland regions, contributing to local biodiversity.
