Stenolobus

Stenolobus, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to Africa.

These plants are characterized by their compact rosettes of velvety, dark green leaves and showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. Stenolobus species are typically herbaceous, with some varieties growing as shrubs. They thrive in well-drained soil and require bright, indirect light to flourish.

The genus is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
