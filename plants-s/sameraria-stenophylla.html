Sameraria stenophylla

Sameraria stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf sameraria, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically narrow and elongated, with a smooth, waxy surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories or along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it an interesting subject for ecological and taxonomic studies.
