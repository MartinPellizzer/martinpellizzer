Schefflera stenopetala

Schefflera stenopetala, commonly known as the narrow-petaled umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clustered, umbrella-like leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil and indirect sunlight.

Schefflera stenopetala is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.
