Solanum × juzepczukii f. wila-ckaisalla

Solanum × juzepczukii f. wila-ckaisalla, commonly known as the Wila-ckaisalla nightshade, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black berries, which are typically small and clustered. The plant exhibits a shrub-like growth habit with hairy stems and lobed leaves. It is native to regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
