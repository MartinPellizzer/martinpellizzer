Sansevieria laevifolia

Sansevieria laevifolia, commonly known as the Variegated Snake Plant, is a succulent plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with distinctive white or yellow variegation. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low light conditions and requires minimal watering. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water, making it highly drought-tolerant.

Sansevieria laevifolia is also noted for its air-purifying properties, making it a popular houseplant.
