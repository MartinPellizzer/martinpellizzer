Senna acuruensis var. acuruensis

Senna acuruensis var. acuruensis, commonly known as the acuru tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant is notable for its bright yellow flowers that bloom during the dry season. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The acuru tree plays a vital role in the ecological balance of its native habitat.
