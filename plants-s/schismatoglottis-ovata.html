Schismatoglottis ovata

Schismatoglottis ovata, commonly known as the broad-leafed schismatoglottis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant features broad, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in a spike, which bloom during the wet season.

Schismatoglottis ovata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
