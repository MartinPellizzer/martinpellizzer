Soroseris Gillii

Gill's soroseris, scientifically known as Soroseris gillii, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the arid regions of western Australia. This species belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its small, clustered yellow flowers. Despite its modest appearance, Gill's soroseris plays a vital role in its ecosystem, providing nectar for local pollinators and supporting biodiversity. Due to habitat loss and climate change, it faces increasing threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are essential to protect this unique plant for future generations.
