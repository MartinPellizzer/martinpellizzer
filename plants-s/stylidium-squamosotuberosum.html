Stylidium squamosotuberosum

Stylidium squamosotuberosum, commonly known as the tuberous sun-orchid, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its unique tuberous root system. The plant produces striking, showy flowers with a distinctive spurred lip. It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils in arid or semi-arid regions.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in the study of orchid diversity and adaptation.
