Stipa splendens var. gracilis

Stipa splendens var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender feather grass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage and delicate, feathery seed heads that add a soft, ornamental quality to gardens. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. Its low-growing habit and drought tolerance make it suitable for xeriscaping and sustainable landscaping.

The botanical features of Stipa splendens var. gracilis contribute to its aesthetic appeal and ecological value in diverse habitats.
