Stelis capillipes

Stelis capillipes, commonly known as the feather-leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a lithophytic plant, meaning it grows on rocks, often in tropical or subtropical regions. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, with a variety of colors including white, yellow, and green.

Stelis capillipes requires specific growing conditions, including high humidity and indirect light, to thrive.
