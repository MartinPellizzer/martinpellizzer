Selenipedium × chlorops

Selenipedium × chlorops, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a rare and exotic plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments and requires specific conditions to thrive. Its flowers are often a vibrant mix of green and yellow, giving it its scientific name.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly sought after by botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
