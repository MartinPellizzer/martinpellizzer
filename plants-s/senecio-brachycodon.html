Senecio brachycodon

Senecio brachycodon, commonly known as the dwarf African daisy, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant yellow flowers. The plant features narrow, blue-green leaves and produces small, daisy-like blooms. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.

Senecio brachycodon prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.
