Schefflera papuana

Schefflera papuana, commonly known as the Papuan umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to New Guinea.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like crown of large, leathery, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland and montane forests.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular ornamental plant in tropical gardens.
