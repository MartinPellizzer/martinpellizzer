Solanum argyracanthum

Solanum argyracanthum, commonly known as silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant produces berries that start green and mature to a deep red or black color. It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats.

Due to its toxic properties, it is generally avoided for consumption by humans and livestock.
