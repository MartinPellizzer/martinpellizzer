Scolopia rhinanthera var. siamensis

Scolopia rhinanthera var. siamensis, commonly known as the Siam arrowroot, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces inflorescences consisting of a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
