Sapium Mannianum

Sapium mannianum is a unique plant known for its striking red berries and ornamental value. Native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia, it thrives in warm, humid climates. Its leaves are large, heart-shaped, and often have a velvety texture. The plant is commonly used in landscaping for its vibrant foliage and seasonal fruit display. Despite its beauty, it requires careful cultivation to ensure optimal growth and health.
