Saldanhaea argentea

Saldanhaea argentea, commonly known as silverleaf daphne, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and clusters of fragrant, white or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is a shrub that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters.

Saldanhaea argentea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive appearance.
