Stellaria emirnensis

Stellaria emirnensis, commonly known as the Emirnensis starwort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Iran.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pink. The plant has small, opposite leaves and thrives in well-drained, rocky soils. It is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats, where it contributes to the biodiversity of these ecosystems.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species in its native range.
