Senecio filaginoides var. lobulatus

Senecio filaginoides var. lobulatus, commonly known as the lobed groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid regions. The plant features narrow, lobed leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. It is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
