Spiraea altaiensis

Spiraea altaiensis, commonly known as Altai spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It typically grows in alpine and subalpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils and full sun. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly serrated edge.

Spiraea altaiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
