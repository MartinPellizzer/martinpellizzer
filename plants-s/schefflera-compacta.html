Schefflera compacta

Schefflera compacta, commonly known as the umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, bushy growth habit with multiple stems that branch out from a central point. The plant produces small, clustered leaves that are glossy and dark green, giving it a lush, full appearance. Schefflera compacta is known for its adaptability to indoor conditions, making it a popular choice for houseplants.

It also has a striking floral display with white, star-shaped flowers that appear in spring.
