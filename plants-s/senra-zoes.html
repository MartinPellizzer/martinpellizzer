Senra zoes

Senra zoes, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features vibrant yellow and orange flowers that resemble flames, giving it its common name. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
