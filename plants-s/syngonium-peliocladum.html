Syngonium peliocladum

Syngonium peliocladum, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by blue-black berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light.

Syngonium peliocladum is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and air-purifying qualities.
