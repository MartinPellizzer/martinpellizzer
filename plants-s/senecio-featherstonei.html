Senecio featherstonei

Senecio featherstonei, commonly known as the featherstone groundsel, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub native to the arid regions of southern Africa. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is often cultivated in rock gardens and arid landscapes.
