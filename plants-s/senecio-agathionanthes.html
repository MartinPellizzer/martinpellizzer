Senecio agathionanthes

Senecio agathionanthes, commonly known as the Agathionanthes groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its upright, branched stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a dense, spherical inflorescence, which is typical of the Senecio genus. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
