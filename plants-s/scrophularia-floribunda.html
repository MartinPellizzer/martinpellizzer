Scrophularia floribunda

Scrophularia floribunda, commonly known as the flowering scrophularia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features erect stems that bear opposite, lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges. Its showy flowers, which range from pink to purple, are clustered in spikes and bloom in late spring to early summer.

Scrophularia floribunda is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
