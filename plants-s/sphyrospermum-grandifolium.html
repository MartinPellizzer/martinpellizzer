Sphyrospermum grandifolium

Sphyrospermum grandifolium, commonly known as the large-leaved sphaerosperm, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
