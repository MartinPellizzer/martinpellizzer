Seddera media

Seddera media, commonly known as the common seddera, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it adaptable to various garden conditions.

Seddera media is often used in ornamental gardening for its delicate appearance and ease of cultivation.
