Stelis villifera

Stelis villifera, commonly known as the veined stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees in humid, shaded forest environments. The plant features a short, creeping stem with numerous clustered, small, greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are elongated and often have a waxy texture to reduce water loss.

Stelis villifera is valued in horticulture for its delicate appearance and adaptability to cultivated conditions.
