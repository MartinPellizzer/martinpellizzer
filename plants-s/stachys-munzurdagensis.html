Stachys munzurdagensis

Stachys munzurdagensis, commonly known as the Munzur sedge, is a flowering plant species native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly habitats, often in mountainous regions. It produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or purple in color.
