Stelis portillae

Stelis portillae, commonly known as the Portilla's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its sympodial growth habit and pseudobulbs. The plant typically produces a single or few upright, linear leaves. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with a distinctive coloration that varies by species.

Stelis portillae is often found in moist, shaded environments in tropical and subtropical regions.
