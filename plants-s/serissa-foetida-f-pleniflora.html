Serissa foetida f. pleniflora

Serissa foetida f. pleniflora, commonly known as the fragrant serissa, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage and clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom throughout the summer. The plant produces small, round, yellowish fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for bonsai and indoor gardening.

Its ornamental value comes from its delicate blossoms and compact growth habit.
