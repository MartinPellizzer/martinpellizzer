Sprengelia ponceletia

Sprengelia ponceletia, commonly known as the red spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a slender, upright stem with a basal rosette of lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, red and orange flowers with a distinctive spider-like appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Sprengelia ponceletia is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
