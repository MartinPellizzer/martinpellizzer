Syzygium idrisii

Syzygium idrisii, commonly known as the Idrisii apple.

It is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family, native to the mountainous regions of Borneo. The plant is characterized by its small, edible fruits that are typically red or yellow when ripe. Its leaves are elliptical and have a glossy surface, while its flowers are white and clustered in inflorescences.

Syzygium idrisii is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
