Spicanta nipponica var. reflexipinnula

Spicanta nipponica var. reflexipinnula, commonly known as the Japanese spiky fern, is a variant of the Japanese spiky fern characterized by its distinctive, reflexed pinnae.

It is native to Japan and thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in mountainous regions. The plant features a robust rhizome system that supports its upright growth habit. Its fronds are bi- to tri-pinnate, with a leathery texture and a dark green coloration.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with high humidity and partial shade.
