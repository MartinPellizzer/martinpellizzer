Senecio fussii

Senecio fussii, commonly known as the Cape mountain snowflake, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. It is known for its compact growth habit and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Senecio fussii is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
