Selenipedium × calurum

Selenipedium × calurum, commonly known as the hybrid giant spider-orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is a hybrid between Selenipedium albidum and Selenipedium suaveolens, characterized by its tall, spindly stems and large, showy white or pale green flowers. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific conditions to bloom, making it a challenging species to cultivate.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and role in the ecological dynamics of its native habitat.
