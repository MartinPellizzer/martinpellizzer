Scutellaria pallida

Scutellaria pallida, commonly known as pale skullcap, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, blue or violet flowers in whorls along the stem. Its fruit is a capsule that contains numerous tiny seeds.

Pale skullcap is valued in traditional medicine for its purported calming and antispasmodic properties.
