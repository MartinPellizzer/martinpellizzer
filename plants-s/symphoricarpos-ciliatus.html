Symphoricarpos ciliatus

Symphoricarpos ciliatus, commonly known as the hairy rosehip, is a deciduous shrub native to North America.

Symphoricarpos ciliatus thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.
