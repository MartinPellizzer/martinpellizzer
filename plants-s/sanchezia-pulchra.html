Sanchezia pulchra

Sanchezia pulchra, commonly known as the golden shower plant, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its striking, pendulous inflorescences that consist of bright yellow bracts. Its leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, with a smooth texture.

Sanchezia pulchra is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant appearance and ease of care.
