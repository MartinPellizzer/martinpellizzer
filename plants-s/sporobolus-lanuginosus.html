Sporobolus lanuginosus

Sporobolus lanuginosus, commonly known as silky sandreed, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and compact, tufted growth habit. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils, often in arid or semi-arid environments. Its seed heads are small and spike-like, and the plant is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Sporobolus lanuginosus plays a role in stabilizing soils and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
