Swartzia buntingii

Swartzia buntingii, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a tropical tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its dense, hard wood that is highly valued for its durability. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in racemes. Swartzia buntingii plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
