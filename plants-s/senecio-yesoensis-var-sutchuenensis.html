Senecio yesoensis var. sutchuenensis

Senecio yesoensis var. sutchuenensis, commonly known as the Sutchuen Senecio, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous regions, adapting to harsh environmental conditions. Its foliage is usually narrow and grass-like, with small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in the summer.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and resilience.
