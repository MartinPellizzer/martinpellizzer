Specklinia tribuloides

Specklinia tribuloides, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on tree trunks or rocks.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
