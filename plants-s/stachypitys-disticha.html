Stachypitys disticha

Stachypitys disticha, commonly known as the spiral grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves around the stem, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

Stachypitys disticha thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grassland or woodland habitats.
