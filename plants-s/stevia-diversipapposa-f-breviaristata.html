Stevia diversipapposa f. breviaristata

Stevia diversipapposa f. breviaristata, commonly known as the short-awned stevia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

The plant is notable for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and climates, making it adaptable to different environments.
