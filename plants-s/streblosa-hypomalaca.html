Streblosa hypomalaca

Streblosa hypomalaca, commonly known as the Malacca orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its intricate, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical rainforests, where it thrives in humid and shaded conditions. Its roots are often epiphytic, meaning they grow on the surface of other plants rather than in soil.
