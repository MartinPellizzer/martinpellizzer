Stephanotis taynguyenensis

Stephanotis taynguyenensis, commonly known as the Tay Nguyen stephanotis, is a climbing plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Oleaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
