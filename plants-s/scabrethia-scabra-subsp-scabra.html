Scabrethia scabra subsp. scabra

Scabrethia scabra subsp.

scabra, commonly known as the rough scabrethia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its hairy, rough-textured stems and leaves, which are often covered in fine, stiff hairs.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in dry, rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open grasslands.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
