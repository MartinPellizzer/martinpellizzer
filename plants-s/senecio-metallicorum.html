Senecio metallicorum

Senecio metallicorum, commonly known as the silverleaf senecio, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its silvery, fuzzy foliage. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It produces small yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Due to its drought tolerance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in arid landscapes.
