Solanum coelestispetalum

Solanum coelestispetalum, commonly known as the celestial petal nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that resemble petals, which are typically violet or blue in color. The plant produces small, spherical berries that contain the toxic seeds typical of the genus Solanum. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is native to certain regions of South America.

Due to its toxic properties, it is not recommended for cultivation or consumption.
