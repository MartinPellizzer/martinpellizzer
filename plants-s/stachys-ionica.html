Stachys ionica

Stachys ionica, commonly known as the Greek violet or Ionian violet, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches heights of up to 30 centimeters. The plant features oval, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture and produces clusters of small, violet-blue flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Stachys ionica is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
