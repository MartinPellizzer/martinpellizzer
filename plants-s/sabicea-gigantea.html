Sabicea gigantea

Sabicea gigantea, commonly known as the giant sabicea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmately compound leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
