Sisymbrium austriacum var. macropetalum

Sisymbrium austriacum var. macropetalum, commonly known as the large-petaled field pennycress, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers with large, showy petals that are typically arranged in racemes. The plant produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds. It is native to Europe and is often found in disturbed soils, such as roadsides and fields.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and easily identifiable species within its native habitat.
