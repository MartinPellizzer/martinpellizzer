Schizostachyum khoonmengii

Schizostachyum khoonmengii, commonly known as the Khoonmeng bamboo, is a species of grass belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Cambodia. This plant is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, well-drained soils.

Schizostachyum khoonmengii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its aesthetic qualities.
