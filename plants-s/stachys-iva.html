Stachys iva

Stachys iva, commonly known as blueweed or narrowleaf blueweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant produces small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
