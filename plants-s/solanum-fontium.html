Solanum fontium

Solanum fontium, commonly known as the Fontium nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, peppers, and potatoes. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale purple in color. Its fruit is a small, round berry that changes from green to yellow as it matures.

Solanum fontium is often found in moist, shaded habitats and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
