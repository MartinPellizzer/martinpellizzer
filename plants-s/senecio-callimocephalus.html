Senecio callimocephalus

Senecio callimocephalus, commonly known as the giant senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that are adapted to arid conditions, allowing it to store water efficiently. It grows as a shrub or small tree, often reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

Due to its striking appearance, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens.
