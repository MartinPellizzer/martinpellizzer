Sarcoyucca aloifolia

Sarcoyucca aloifolia, commonly known as the dragon tree, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are often used in traditional medicine and as a source of fiber. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically followed by red, fleshy fruits containing seeds. Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

However, it requires well-drained soil and protection from frost to thrive.
