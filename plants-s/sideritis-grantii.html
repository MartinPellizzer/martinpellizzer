Sideritis grantii

Sideritis grantii, commonly known as the Greek skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Greece and the Balkans.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or blue in color. Sideritis grantii is often found growing in rocky or limestone soils at high altitudes.

It is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
