Staminodianthus duckei

Staminodianthus duckei, commonly known as the African violet, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its attractive, velvety leaves and showy, tubular flowers. Its flowers are usually purple or violet in color, with five petals and a prominent corona.

Staminodianthus duckei is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
