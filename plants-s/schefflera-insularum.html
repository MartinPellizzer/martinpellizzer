Schefflera insularum

Schefflera insularum, commonly known as the island schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of leaves, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant has compound, pinnate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by red berries, which are a food source for various birds.

Schefflera insularum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor environments.
