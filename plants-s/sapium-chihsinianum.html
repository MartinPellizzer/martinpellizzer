Sapium chihsinianum

Sapium chihsinianum, commonly known as Chinese sycamore, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, light gray bark and heart-shaped leaves. The tree produces yellowish-green flowers followed by woody, three-lobed fruits. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can thrive in a variety of soil types.
