Simsia holwayi

Simsia holwayi, commonly known as the Holway's sunflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has hairy stems and leaves that are often glandular, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Simsia holwayi is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
