Syagrus menzeliana

Syagrus menzeliana, commonly known as the Menzel's palm, is a species of palm native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces yellowish-orange inflorescences, which bear small, fragrant flowers. Its fruit is a dark red drupe containing a single seed.

Syagrus menzeliana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to tropical and subtropical climates.
