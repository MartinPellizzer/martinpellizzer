Senecio rivularis var. glabriusculus

Senecio rivularis var. glabriusculus, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial with slender, often hairy stems and small, yellowish-green leaves. The plant produces daisy-like flowers with yellow disc florets surrounded by white to pale blue ray florets. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
