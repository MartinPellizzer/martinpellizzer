Stelis longihirta

Stelis longihirta, commonly known as the hairy stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, often branched stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in forested areas, often as an epiphyte on trees. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with a distinctive hairy texture on the stems and leaves.

Stelis longihirta is appreciated for its ornamental value and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance.
