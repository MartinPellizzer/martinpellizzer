Satureja makinoi

Satureja makinoi, commonly known as Japanese thyme, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features small, pink or purple flowers and has fragrant, narrow leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

Satureja makinoi is valued for its culinary and medicinal uses in traditional Asian practices.
