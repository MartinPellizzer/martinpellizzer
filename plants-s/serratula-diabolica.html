Serratula diabolica

Serratula diabolica, commonly known as the devil's lettuce, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers and dark green, serrated leaves. The plant is often found in rocky or disturbed soils, thriving in sunny environments. It produces small, oblong seeds that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its spread.

Due to its toxic properties, it is considered a noxious weed in some areas.
