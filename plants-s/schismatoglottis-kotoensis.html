Schismatoglottis kotoensis

Schismatoglottis kotoensis, commonly known as the Koto's schismatoglottis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family native to the Philippines.

It is a small, herbaceous plant characterized by its narrow, dark green leaves and delicate, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in mountainous regions. It has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and floral structures.
