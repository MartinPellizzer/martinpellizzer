Sericolea lamii

Sericolea lamii, commonly known as the silky leaf orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant is characterized by its elongated, strap-like leaves that are covered in a fine, silky hair, giving it its common name. Its flowers are small and clustered, often displaying shades of white and green.

Sericolea lamii is typically found growing in shaded, humid environments such as forests or undergrowth.
