Solanum reitzii

Solanum reitzii, commonly known as the Reitz's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by red, fleshy berries. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a rough texture with a distinct scent.

Solanum reitzii belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and contains toxic compounds, making it potentially harmful to humans and animals.
