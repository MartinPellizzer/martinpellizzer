Symplocos odoratissima var. aluminosa

Symplocos odoratissima var. aluminosa, commonly known as the aluminous beautyberry, is a deciduous shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its clusters of small, bright blue berries that ripen in the fall. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a glossy surface.
