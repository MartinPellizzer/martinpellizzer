Sideritis ibrahimii

Sideritis ibrahimii, commonly known as the Ibrahim's sainfoin, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in high-altitude environments.

Sideritis ibrahimii is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
