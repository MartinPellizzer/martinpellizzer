Sedum henriquesii

Sedum henriquesii, commonly known as the Henriques' stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical leaves that are often blue-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Due to its compact size and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping and low-maintenance gardens.
