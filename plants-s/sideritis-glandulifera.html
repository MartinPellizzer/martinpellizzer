Sideritis glandulifera

Sideritis glandulifera, commonly known as the glandular heath, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its small, glandular leaves and spikes of blue or purple flowers. It thrives in rocky or dry habitats and is often found in mountainous areas.

Sideritis glandulifera is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
