Saintpaulia ionantha subsp. occidentalis

Saintpaulia ionantha subsp.

occidentalis, commonly known as the Western African violet, is a species of African violet native to western Africa. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, velvety foliage.

The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with a contrasting throat. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care in domestic settings.
