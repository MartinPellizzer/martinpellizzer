Stellaria Crassifolia Var. Paludosa

Woolly starwort, scientifically known as Stellaria crassifolia var. paludosa, is a unique and resilient plant species native to wetland environments. Its distinctive appearance, characterized by dense woolly foliage and small star-shaped flowers, makes it easily identifiable in its natural habitat. This variant of the starwort genus thrives in moist, often boggy soils, showcasing its adaptability to challenging conditions. Despite its delicate look, Woolly starwort plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and supporting local ecosystems. Its presence highlights the importance of preserving wetland habitats for biodiversity.
