Spiraea Dahurica

Spiraea dahurica is a deciduous shrub known for its showy, cone-shaped flower clusters. It thrives in temperate climates and is commonly found in woodland and meadow habitats. This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators. Its leaves are typically oval and have a soft, velvety texture. Spiraea dahurica is a versatile plant that can be used in garden landscaping or natural restoration projects.
