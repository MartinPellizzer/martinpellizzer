Sauroglossum monophyllum

Sauroglossum monophyllum, commonly known as the Brazilian star vine, is a climbing plant native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces clusters of small, star-like white flowers. The plant has a woody stem with thorns and is often found growing along forest edges or in shaded areas. Its botanical name reflects its unique floral structure and its native habitat.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
