Scutellaria tienchuanensis

Scutellaria tienchuanensis, commonly known as the Tienchuan skullcap, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
