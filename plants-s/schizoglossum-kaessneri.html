Schizoglossum kaessneri

Schizoglossum kaessneri, commonly known as the desert star cactus, is a species of flowering plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its spiny, cylindrical stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in harsh, dry environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking visual contrast to its rugged appearance. Schizoglossum kaessneri is adapted to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a resilient choice for xeriscaping.
