Synaedrys scortechinii

Synaedrys scortechinii, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, velvety hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne on long, slender stalks. Synaedrys scortechinii is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and agricultural fields.
