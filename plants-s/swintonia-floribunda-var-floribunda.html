Swintonia floribunda var. floribunda

Swintonia floribunda var. floribunda, commonly known as the pink shower tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and clusters of small, fragrant, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in spring. The leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly serrated margin. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for tropical and subtropical climates.

Its ornamental value comes from its showy flowers and delicate foliage.
