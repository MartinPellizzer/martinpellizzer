Schefflera nabirensis

Schefflera nabirensis, commonly known as the Nabire Schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the island of New Guinea.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded canopy of large, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, white, clustered flowers that are followed by bright red, fleshy fruits. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
