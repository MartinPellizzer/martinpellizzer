Stenotus falcatus

Stenotus falcatus, commonly known as the narrowleaf sunflower, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and other composite flowers. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces bright yellow flowers with a prominent central disk. It is often found in dry, open habitats and is adapted to arid conditions.

Stenotus falcatus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
