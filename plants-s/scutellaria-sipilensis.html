Scutellaria sipilensis

Scutellaria sipilensis, commonly known as the narrowleaf skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy surface.
