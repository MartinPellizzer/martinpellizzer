Setaria hunzikeri

Setaria hunzikeri, commonly known as the slender dropseed, is a grass species native to the central and southern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are typically found in open, sunny habitats. It is often found in prairies, disturbed soils, and along roadsides.

Setaria hunzikeri is adapted to a variety of soil types and is relatively drought-tolerant.
