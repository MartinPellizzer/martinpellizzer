Senecio icoglossus var. araneosus

Senecio icoglossus var. araneosus, commonly known as the spiderwort senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The leaves are typically blue-green in color and have a velvety texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

This variant is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique foliage and delicate blooms.
