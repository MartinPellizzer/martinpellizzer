Solandra oppositifolia

Solandra oppositifolia, commonly known as the opposite-leaved solandra, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its opposite arrangement of leaves, which gives it its scientific name. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in warm, sunny environments and is often found in rocky or dry soils.

Solandra oppositifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
