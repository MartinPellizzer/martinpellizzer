Stellera lathraeoides

Stellera lathraeoides, commonly known as the starflower, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the northeastern United States.

It features showy, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from March to May. The plant has alternate, ovate leaves with hairy surfaces and a fibrous root system. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Stellera lathraeoides is often found in woodlands and along stream banks, where it contributes to early-season floral diversity.
