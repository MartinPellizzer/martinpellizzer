Solanum fragariifructum

Solanum fragariifructum, commonly known as the strawberry nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other notable plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant produces small, red berries that resemble strawberries, hence its common name. These berries contain toxic compounds, making the plant potentially harmful to humans and animals.

It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland areas or along streambanks.
