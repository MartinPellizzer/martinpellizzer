Statice pycnantha

Statice pycnantha, commonly known as sea lavender, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, showy blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is often found growing in coastal areas and saline soils. It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in harsh conditions.
