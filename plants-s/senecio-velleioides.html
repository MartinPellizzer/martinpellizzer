Senecio velleioides

Senecio velleioides, commonly known as the Cape York Senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its fleshy, gray-green leaves and yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in arid and rocky environments, showcasing adaptations to drought and poor soil conditions.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
