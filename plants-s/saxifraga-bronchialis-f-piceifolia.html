Saxifraga bronchialis f. piceifolia

Saxifraga bronchialis f. piceifolia, commonly known as the dark-leaved saxifrage, is a botanical variant characterized by its dark green, almost black foliage.

It is a low-growing perennial plant that typically thrives in rocky or alpine environments. The leaves are usually ovate to lanceolate in shape and are densely arranged along the stem. It produces small, white or pale pink flowers during the summer months.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is often found in moist, shaded habitats.
