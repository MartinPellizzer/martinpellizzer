Statice alliacea var. apollinaris

Statice alliacea var. apollinaris, commonly known as the Apollinaris statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces small, showy blue to purple flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves, and it is often used in floral arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms.

The variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in dry conditions.
