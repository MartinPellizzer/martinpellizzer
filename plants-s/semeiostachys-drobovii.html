Semeiostachys drobovii

Semeiostachys drobovii, commonly known as the Drobovii sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the wetlands of eastern Europe and western Asia.

The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in spikelets, which are characteristic of the Cyperaceae family. It has fibrous roots and is often found in marshy areas, contributing to soil stabilization.
