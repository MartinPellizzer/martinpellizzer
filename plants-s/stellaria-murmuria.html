Stellaria murmuria

Stellaria murmuria, commonly known as mouse-ear chickweed, is a low-growing annual herb native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It features small, white flowers with five petals that resemble stars, giving it its scientific name. The plant has hairy, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, along paths, and in gardens.

Stellaria murmuria is known for its delicate appearance and is sometimes used in herbal medicine.
