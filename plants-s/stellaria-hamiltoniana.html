Stellaria hamiltoniana

Stellaria hamiltoniana, commonly known as Hamilton's starwort, is a small, herbaceous plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Stellaria hamiltoniana is known for its low-growing habit and is often found in temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.
