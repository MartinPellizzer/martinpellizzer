Schefflera nicaraguensis

Schefflera nicaraguensis, commonly known as the Nicaraguan umbrella tree, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its large, umbrella-shaped crown composed of multiple leaflets radiating from a central stem. The plant features compound, palmate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Schefflera nicaraguensis is also valued for its ability to purify indoor air and adapt to various light conditions.
