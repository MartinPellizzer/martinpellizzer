Stachys sphaerodonta

Stachys sphaerodonta, commonly known as the globe thyme, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features small, round, aromatic leaves that are densely packed, giving it a compact, cushion-like appearance. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale blue to purple flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its medicinal properties have been historically recognized for their antiseptic and calming effects.
