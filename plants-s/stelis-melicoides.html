Stelis melicoides

Stelis melicoides, commonly known as the silver spider orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and its distinctive, spider-like inflorescence. The plant produces small, white to pale green flowers with intricate patterns that resemble spider webs. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in forest understories.

Stelis melicoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
