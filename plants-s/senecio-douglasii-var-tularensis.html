Senecio douglasii var. tularensis

Senecio douglasii var. tularensis, commonly known as the Tulare Senecio, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces yellow to orange ray flowers. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
