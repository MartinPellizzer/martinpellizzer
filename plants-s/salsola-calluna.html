Salsola calluna

Salsola calluna, commonly known as Russian thistle or goatweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its erect, spiny stems and small, greenish flowers. Salsola calluna is also known for its ability to spread rapidly through seeds, making it an invasive species in some regions.
