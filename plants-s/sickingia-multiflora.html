Sickingia multiflora

Sickingia multiflora, commonly known as the African ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, ribbed stems and leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red during the warmer months. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.

Sickingia multiflora is also valued for its ability to prevent soil erosion in dry environments.
