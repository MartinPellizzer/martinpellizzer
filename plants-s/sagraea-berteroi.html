Sagraea berteroi

Sagraea berteroi, commonly known as the yellow star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow, star-shaped flowers during the spring season. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are upright and branching.

Sagraea berteroi is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
