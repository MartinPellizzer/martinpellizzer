Senecio platensis

Senecio platensis, commonly known as the New Zealand Senecio, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Senecio platensis is often found in open habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
