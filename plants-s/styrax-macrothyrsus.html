Styrax macrothyrsus

Styrax macrothyrsus, commonly known as the large-thorned styrax, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and emit a fragrant aroma when crushed. The plant produces clusters of white, fragrant flowers in the spring, which are followed by small, orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for ornamental gardens.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
