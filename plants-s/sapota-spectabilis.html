Sapota spectabilis

Sapota spectabilis, commonly known as the beautiful sapote, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is a small evergreen tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall, characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces yellowish-white flowers that are fragrant and attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its fruit is a rounded, yellow or orange berry that ripens in late summer.

The fruit's pulp is sweet and creamy, often used in culinary applications.
