Stellaria delavayi

Stellaria delavayi, commonly known as Delavay's starwort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southwestern China.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, star-shaped white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its leaves are opposite and linear, often covered with fine hairs.

Stellaria delavayi is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
