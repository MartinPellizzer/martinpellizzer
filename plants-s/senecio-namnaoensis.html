Senecio namnaoensis

Senecio namnaoensis, commonly known as the Namnaoense ragwort, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in fine hairs, and it produces small yellow flowers.

Senecio namnaoensis is adapted to withstand drought conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
