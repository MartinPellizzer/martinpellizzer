Silvinula

Silvinula, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage and vibrant flowers that range from blue to purple. Native to tropical regions of South America, it thrives in humid, well-drained soils. The plant is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental value.

It requires bright, indirect light and moderate watering to flourish.
