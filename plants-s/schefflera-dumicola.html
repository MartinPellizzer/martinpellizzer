Schefflera dumicola

Schefflera dumicola, commonly known as the desert schefflera, is a rare and striking plant native to the arid regions of Namibia.

It is characterized by its unique, umbrella-like arrangement of leaves, which are typically a vibrant green and slightly leathery in texture. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for harsh desert environments.

Due to its limited distribution and ecological significance, it is considered a species of conservation concern.
