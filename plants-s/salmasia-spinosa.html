Salmasia spinosa

Salmasia spinosa, commonly known as the spiny salmasia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
