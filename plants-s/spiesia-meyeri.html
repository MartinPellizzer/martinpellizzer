Spiesia meyeri

Spiesia meyeri, commonly known as Meyer's spurge, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Spiesia meyeri is also known for its medicinal properties, with some traditional uses in herbal remedies.
