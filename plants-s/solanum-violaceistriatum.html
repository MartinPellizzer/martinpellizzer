Solanum violaceistriatum

Solanum violaceistriatum, commonly known as the violet-striated nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in humid, tropical environments. The plant produces showy, violet-colored flowers with distinctive white stripes, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are usually ovate or lanceolate, with a glossy green surface.

The fruit is a small, fleshy berry that turns from green to yellow or red as it matures.
