Senecio coronatus

Senecio coronatus, commonly known as the crown-of-thorns or crown-of-thorns plant, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa.

It features dense clusters of small, spiny leaves that form a spherical or bushy shape, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in late spring to early summer, which are typically arranged in clusters at the tips of the stems. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Its unique morphology and hardy nature make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and ornamental gardening.
