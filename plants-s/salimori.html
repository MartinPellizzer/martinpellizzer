Salimori

Salimori, commonly known as the desert hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that come in shades of red, yellow, and orange. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves and stems that help it retain water in dry environments. Salimori is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

Its ability to thrive in harsh conditions makes it an important species in desert ecosystems.
