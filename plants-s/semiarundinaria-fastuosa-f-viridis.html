Semiarundinaria fastuosa f. viridis

Semiarundinaria fastuosa f. viridis, commonly known as the Emerald Bamboo, is a variant of the silver bamboo species native to East Asia.

This plant is characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and striking emerald-green foliage. The leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, providing a lush, textured appearance. It is a clumping bamboo that forms tight, compact clusters, making it ideal for small garden spaces.

The plant is relatively low-maintenance, thriving in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
