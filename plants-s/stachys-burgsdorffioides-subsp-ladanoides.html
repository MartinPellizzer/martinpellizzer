Stachys burgsdorffioides subsp. ladanoides

Stachys burgsdorffioides subsp.

ladanoides, commonly known as the woolly betony, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It features upright stems covered in dense, soft hairs, giving it a woolly appearance.

The plant produces spikes of pale blue to purple flowers, which bloom in summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and also bear a fuzzy texture.

It is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
