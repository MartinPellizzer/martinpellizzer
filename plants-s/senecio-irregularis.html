Senecio irregularis

Senecio irregularis, commonly known as the irregular senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features narrow, fleshy leaves arranged in a spiral pattern along its stems. It produces small, yellowish flowers during the warmer months, which attract various pollinators.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
