Solanum tredecimgranum

Solanum tredecimgranum, commonly known as the thirteen-grained nightshade, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant produces small, purple berries that contain numerous tiny seeds, giving it its common name. It is characterized by its hairy stems and lobed leaves.

Due to its toxic properties, it is not typically cultivated for agricultural purposes.
