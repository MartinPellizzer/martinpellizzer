Solanum venosum

Solanum venosum, commonly known as the veiny nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a shrub characterized by its woody stems and oval, dark green leaves with prominent veins. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring, followed by berries that change from green to red as they mature. Solanum venosum contains toxic compounds, making it potentially harmful to humans and animals.

It is often found in dry, sandy soils and is part of the nightshade family, Solanaceae.
