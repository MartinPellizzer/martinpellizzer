Schefflera tamana

Schefflera tamana, commonly known as the Tamana umbrella tree, is a tropical plant native to the island of Madagascar.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded crown of large, glossy, dark green leaves that resemble an umbrella. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by red, fleshy fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Schefflera tamana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
