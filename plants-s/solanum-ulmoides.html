Solanum ulmoides

Solanum ulmoides, commonly known as the slender nightshade, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30-60 centimeters. The plant features small, green, ovate leaves and produces clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in the late afternoon. Its fruit is a small, round berry that turns from green to yellow or red as it matures.

Solanum ulmoides is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
