Salsola kali var. rubella

Salsola kali var. rubella, commonly known as Russian thistle or goatweed, is a flowering plant in the family Amaranthaceae.

It is a short-lived perennial that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features a sprawling growth habit with spiny stems and small, fleshy leaves. Its flowers are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences, which are often pink to reddish in color.

It is well adapted to harsh conditions, making it a common weed in disturbed soils.
