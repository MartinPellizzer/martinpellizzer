Sphaerolobium acanthos

Sphaerolobium acanthos, commonly known as the roughleaf burr legume, is a small shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features serrated, rough-textured leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its fruit is a hairy, woody pod that splits open to release seeds.

Sphaerolobium acanthos is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
