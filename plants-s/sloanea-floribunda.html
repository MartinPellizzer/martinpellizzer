Sloanea floribunda

Sloanea floribunda, commonly known as the showy sloanea, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant produces small, elliptical berries that are typically red when mature, containing several seeds. Sloanea floribunda is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive appearance and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for tropical gardens and greenhouses.
