Saxifraga paniculata f. tremolsii

Saxifraga paniculata f. tremolsii, commonly known as the tremolosii saxifrage, is a compact, low-growing perennial that typically reaches a height of about 15 cm.

It features dense, upright clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white pubescence. It is native to mountainous regions of Europe and thrives in well-drained, rocky or gravelly soils.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens or alpine displays.
