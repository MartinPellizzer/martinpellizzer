Sideritis cretica var. condensata

Sideritis cretica var. condensata, commonly known as the Greek ironwort, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale blue or violet in color. It thrives in rocky or limestone soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats.

Sideritis species are known for their medicinal properties, and this variety is no exception.
