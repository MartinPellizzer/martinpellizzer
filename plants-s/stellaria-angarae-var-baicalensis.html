Stellaria angarae var. baicalensis

Stellaria angarae var. baicalensis, commonly known as the Baical starwort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Eastern Siberia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, star-shaped white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, thriving in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs, while its stems are slender and branching.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
