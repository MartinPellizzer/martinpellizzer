Stemmacantha insignis

Stemmacantha insignis, commonly known as the African violets, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, branching stems and oval, waxy leaves that are typically dark green. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. It is a popular ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and blooms.

Stemmacantha insignis thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.
