Stachys recta var. nitens

Stachys recta var. nitens, commonly known as silver leaf betony, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It features upright, square stems covered in dense, silvery-white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The leaves are lance-shaped, also hairy, and have a slightly aromatic scent. It produces spikes of pale blue to violet flowers in late summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
