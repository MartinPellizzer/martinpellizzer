Strepsanthera macrophylla

Strepsanthera macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaved strepsanthera, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
