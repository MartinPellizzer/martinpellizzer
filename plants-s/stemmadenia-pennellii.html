Stemmadenia pennellii

Stemmadenia pennellii, commonly known as the African star gooseberry, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in opposite pairs along its slender, woody stems. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences. Its fruit is a small, yellow, fleshy berry that ripens in late summer.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
