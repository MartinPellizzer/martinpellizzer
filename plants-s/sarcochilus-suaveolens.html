Sarcochilus suaveolens

Sarcochilus suaveolens, commonly known as the sweet-scented saxifrage, is an epiphytic orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Sarcochilus suaveolens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions.
