Serruria congesta

Serruria congesta, commonly known as the red-hot poker, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, spiky flower spikes that bloom in vibrant red or orange hues. The plant belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is known for its upright growth habit. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

Serruria congesta thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
