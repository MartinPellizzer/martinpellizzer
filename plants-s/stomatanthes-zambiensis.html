Stomatanthes zambiensis

Stomatanthes zambiensis, commonly known as the Zambian flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Central and Southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects. The plant has palmate leaves composed of five to seven lobes, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often growing in savanna or woodland habitats.

Its botanical features make it a popular ornamental plant in tropical gardens.
