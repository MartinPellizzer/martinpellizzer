Stipa × subdrobovii var. pubescens

Stipa × subdrobovii var. pubescens, commonly known as the pubescent feathergrass, is a hybrid grass species characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage and dense, tufted growth habit.

It is native to regions of Central Asia and is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. The plant produces slender, spike-like inflorescences that are adorned with small, wind-dispersed seeds. Its leaves are covered in fine, short hairs, giving it a distinctive pubescent appearance.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
