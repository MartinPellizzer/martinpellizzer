Senecio acaulis

Senecio acaulis, commonly known as the cushion senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its dense, compact growth habit, which forms a low, cushion-like structure. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in the spring. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in arid environments.

Senecio acaulis is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is well-suited to dry, sunny conditions.
