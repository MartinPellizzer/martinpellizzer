Senecio millikenii

Senecio millikenii, commonly known as Milliken's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments. Its foliage is usually gray-green, with hairy leaves that help reduce water loss.

It produces small yellow flowers that bloom in the spring and summer months.
