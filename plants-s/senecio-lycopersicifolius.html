Senecio lycopersicifolius

Senecio lycopersicifolius, commonly known as the tomato leaf senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that resemble those of tomato plants, giving it its common name. It produces yellow flowers that develop into cylindrical, spiny seed heads.

Senecio lycopersicifolius is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and striking floral display.
