Staphysora albida

Staphysora albida, commonly known as the white-stemmed grassvetch, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant has trifoliate leaves, with each leaflet being lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops.

Staphysora albida is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, contributing to soil fertility.
