Schefflera monticola

Schefflera monticola, commonly known as the mountain schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It features a dense, rounded crown with thick, leathery, dark green leaves that are arranged in a distinctive, umbrella-like pattern. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in montane forests.

Schefflera monticola is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
