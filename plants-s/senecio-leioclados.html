Senecio leioclados

Senecio leioclados, commonly known as the blue ice plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-gray leaves that store water, adapted to arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique coloration and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
