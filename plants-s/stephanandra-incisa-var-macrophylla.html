Stephanandra incisa var. macrophylla

Stephanandra incisa var. macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leafed stephanandra, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its broad, elliptical leaves that are often leathery in texture. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in shaded to semi-shaded environments and prefers well-drained, moist soil.

This variety is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and moderate growth habit.
