Stachys dregeana var. tenuior

Stachys dregeana var. tenuior, commonly known as narrowleaf hedge nettle, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features upright stems covered in fine, soft hairs and produces small, purple to pinkish flowers in whorled clusters. The leaves are lance-shaped, with a hairy surface and a distinctive, slightly serrated edge. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine.
