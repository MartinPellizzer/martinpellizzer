Solanum harmandii

Solanum harmandii, commonly known as Harmand's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, peppers, and potatoes. The plant is characterized by its small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically purple or white in color. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and often have a rough texture.

Solanum harmandii is typically found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments.
