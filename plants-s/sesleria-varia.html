Sesleria varia

Sesleria varia, commonly known as the variable sesleria, is a species of grass native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Poaceae and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and fine, grass-like leaves. It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained environments.
