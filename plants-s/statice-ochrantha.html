Statice ochrantha

Statice ochrantha, commonly known as red statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, spike-like inflorescences. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to deep red, depending on the variety. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often bluish-green in appearance.

Statice ochrantha is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
