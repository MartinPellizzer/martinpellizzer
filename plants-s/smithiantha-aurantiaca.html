Smithiantha aurantiaca

Smithiantha aurantiaca, commonly known as the golden mistflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, orange-yellow inflorescences. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are clustered in dense, spherical heads, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its foliage is typically narrow and grass-like, complementing its vibrant floral display.
