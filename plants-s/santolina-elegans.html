Santolina elegans

Santolina elegans, commonly known as Spanish santolina, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features compact, aromatic foliage with silvery-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Santolina elegans is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract beneficial insects.
