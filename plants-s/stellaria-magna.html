Stellaria magna

Stellaria magna, commonly known as greater mouse-ear, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit, with hairy stems and small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs, giving the plant a distinctive appearance. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in meadows and disturbed soils.

This plant is often found in moist environments and is known for its ability to spread via seeds dispersed by wind.
