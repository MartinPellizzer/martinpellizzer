Solanum nigrum f. angulosum

Solanum nigrum f. angulosum, commonly known as the angular black nightshade, is a variant of the common black nightshade.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its angular, ridged leaves and small, dark purple berries. The plant belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades such as tomatoes and potatoes. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in gardens and waste areas.

Its botanical features make it distinct from the typical form of Solanum nigrum.
