Selaginella moszkowskii

Selaginella moszkowskii, commonly known as the Moszkowski's spikemoss, is a species of spikemoss belonging to the family Selaginellaceae.

It is a small, evergreen plant that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant has a distinctive appearance with slender, branching stems and small, scale-like leaves. It produces sporophylls that bear spores, which are essential for its reproductive cycle.

Selaginella moszkowskii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
