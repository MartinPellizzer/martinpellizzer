Saussuria

Saussuria, commonly known as swamp milkweed, is a genus of flowering plants in the sunflower family, Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its showy, usually yellow or white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plants typically have opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Saussuria species are native to various regions in the Americas and are often found in wet or moist habitats.

They play an important role in supporting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
