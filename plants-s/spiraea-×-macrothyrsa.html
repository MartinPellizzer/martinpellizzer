Spiraea × macrothyrsa

Spiraea × macrothyrsa, commonly known as the weeping spiraea, is a deciduous shrub native to the United States.

It is characterized by its cascading clusters of white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, arching branches that give it a graceful, weeping appearance. Its leaves are oval-shaped and turn yellow in the fall, adding seasonal interest.

Spiraea × macrothyrsa is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil conditions.
