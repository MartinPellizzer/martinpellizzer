Stenotaphrum clavigerum

Stenotaphrum clavigerum, commonly known as silver bluestem, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and dunes. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically covered in a fine, silvery pubescence, giving it its common name. It is drought-tolerant and can form dense mats, making it useful for erosion control.

Stenotaphrum clavigerum is also valued for its ornamental appeal in landscaping due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
