Stelis fissa

Stelis fissa, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on tree trunks or rocks. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with a pleasant, sweet scent.

Stelis fissa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique fragrance.
