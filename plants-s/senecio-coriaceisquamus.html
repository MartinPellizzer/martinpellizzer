Senecio coriaceisquamus

Senecio coriaceisquamus, commonly known as the thick-leaved groundsels, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in dense clusters, and bloom during the warmer months.
