Stereochilus brevirachis

Stereochilus brevirachis, commonly known as the short-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
