Scutellaria yunnanensis

Scutellaria yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants known for their medicinal properties. The plant features square stems, opposite leaves, and small, two-lipped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It is often found in moist, shaded habitats at higher elevations.

Scutellaria yunnanensis is valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
