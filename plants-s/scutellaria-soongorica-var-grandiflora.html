Scutellaria soongorica var. grandiflora

Scutellaria soongorica var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered skullcap, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia.

It features square, hairy stems and opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces showy blue to violet flowers in whorled clusters, typical of the Scutellaria genus. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
