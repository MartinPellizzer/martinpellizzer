Senecio flosfragrans

Senecio flosfragrans, commonly known as the fragrant senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces showy, daisy-like blooms that range in color from white to yellow, often with a subtle fragrance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of cultivation, it is widely grown in gardens across temperate regions.
