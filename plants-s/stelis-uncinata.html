Stelis uncinata

Stelis uncinata, commonly known as the hooked stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees in tropical rainforests, where it derives moisture and nutrients from the air and rain. The plant features a short, horizontal rhizome with clustered, small, green leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with a distinctive spurred shape.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
