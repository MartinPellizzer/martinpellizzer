Satyrium aculeatum

Satyrium aculeatum, commonly known as the spiny orchid, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, often spiny or hairy leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in shaded, moist environments.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
