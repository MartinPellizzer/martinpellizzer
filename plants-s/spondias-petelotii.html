Spondias petelotii

Spondias petelotii, commonly known as the red mombin, is a small to medium-sized tree native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Anacardiaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and elliptical leaves with a slightly serrated margin. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry forests and savannas.
