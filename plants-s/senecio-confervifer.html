Senecio confervifer

Senecio confervifer, commonly known as the silky blueweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, silvery-blue leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in spring.

Senecio confervifer is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
