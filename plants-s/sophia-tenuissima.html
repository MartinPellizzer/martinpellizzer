Sophia tenuissima

Sophia tenuissima, commonly known as the slender sophia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its small, narrow leaves and clusters of pale yellow flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its delicate appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
