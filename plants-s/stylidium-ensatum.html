Stylidium ensatum

Stylidium ensatum, commonly known as the spurred sun-orchid, is a small, herbaceous plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its distinctive spurred petals. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils in arid or semi-arid regions. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, with a unique spur that resembles a sunflower.

Stylidium ensatum is adapted to survive in harsh environments, often flowering after rainfall.
