Stromanthe guapilesensis

Stromanthe guapilesensis, commonly known as the Guapiles Spider Plant, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence composed of a spadix and spathe. The plant features elongated, waxy leaves with intricate vein patterns, often displaying variegation in shades of green, white, and pink. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Stromanthe guapilesensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
