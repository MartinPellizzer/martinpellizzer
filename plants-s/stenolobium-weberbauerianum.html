Stenolobium weberbauerianum

Stenolobium weberbauerianum, commonly known as the Weberbauer's flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, flame-like inflorescences that bloom in spring. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval leaflets and produces pods that contain several seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant, showy flowers.
