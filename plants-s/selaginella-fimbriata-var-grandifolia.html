Selaginella fimbriata var. grandifolia

Selaginella fimbriata var. grandifolia, commonly known as the giant thread moss, is a species of spore-bearing plant belonging to the family Selaginellaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, thread-like leaves that are typically arranged in whorls around the stem. The plant is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its reproductive structures, including strobila, are prominent and aid in spore dispersal.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique appearance.
