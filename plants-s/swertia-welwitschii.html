Swertia welwitschii

Swertia welwitschii, commonly known as the African gentian, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous areas, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, while its stems are often branched.

Swertia welwitschii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
