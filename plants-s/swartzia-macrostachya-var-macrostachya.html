Swartzia macrostachya var. macrostachya

Swartzia macrostachya var. macrostachya, commonly known as the Brazilian rosewood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its dark, dense wood with a rich, reddish-brown color and a distinctive grain pattern. The plant has simple, alternate, oblong leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and white or pale yellow, typically appearing in clusters.

The species is valued for its timber but is also cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and growth habit.
