Stelis micragrostis

Stelis micragrostis, commonly known as the small-bracted stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest undergrowth. The plant features a short, rhizomatous stem with few leaves and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pale green. Its flowers are characterized by a narrow, hooded labellum and are usually arranged in a spike.

Stelis micragrostis is native to Southeast Asia and is appreciated for its ornamental value in horticulture.
