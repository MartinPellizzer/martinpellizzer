Sageretia bodinieri

Sageretia bodinieri, commonly known as the Chinese sand thorn, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in dry, rocky soils and arid environments. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough, sandpaper-like texture.

Sageretia bodinieri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
