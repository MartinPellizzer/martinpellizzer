Senna artemisioides subsp. alicia

Senna artemisioides subsp.

alicia, commonly known as Alicia's senna, is a shrub native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a prominent midrib.

The plant produces bright yellow flowers in clusters, which are typical of the legume family. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

The subspecies is distinguished by its unique leaf morphology and floral structure compared to other senna species.
