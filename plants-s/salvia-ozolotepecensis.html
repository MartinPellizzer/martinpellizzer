Salvia ozolotepecensis

Salvia ozolotepecensis, commonly known as the Ozolotepec sage, is a species of flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. This perennial herb typically grows in rocky and well-drained soils, often in mountainous regions. Its flowers are typically violet or purple, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in local herbal medicine.
