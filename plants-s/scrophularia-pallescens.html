Scrophularia pallescens

Scrophularia pallescens, commonly known as the pale scrophularia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of tubular, pale pink to white flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer.

Scrophularia pallescens is a perennial herb that plays a role in supporting local pollinators.
