Scutellaria abbreviata

Scutellaria abbreviata, commonly known as the short-stemmed skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features square, hairy stems and small, tubular flowers that are typically blue to purple in color. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy surface.

Scutellaria abbreviata is known for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
