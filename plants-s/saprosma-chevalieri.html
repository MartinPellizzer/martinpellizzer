Saprosma chevalieri

Saprosma chevalieri, commonly known as the Madagascar flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall, characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and striking red or orange flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are edible for some animals but toxic to humans. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
