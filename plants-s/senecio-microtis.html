Senecio microtis

Senecio microtis, commonly known as the narrowleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. Its small, yellow flowers bloom in spring, and the plant is often found in open woodlands or grasslands.
