Spiraeopsis myriantha

Spiraeopsis myriantha, commonly known as the clustered spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It features dense, rounded clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, arching branches and dark green, ovate leaves that turn red in the fall. It typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and prefers well-drained soil in full sun.

Spiraeopsis myriantha is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of conditions.
