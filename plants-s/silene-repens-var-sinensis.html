Silene repens var. sinensis

Silene repens var. sinensis, commonly known as Chinese selfheal, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is native to East Asia, particularly China, and is characterized by its upright stems and clusters of pink to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as forest floors and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Silene repens var. sinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
