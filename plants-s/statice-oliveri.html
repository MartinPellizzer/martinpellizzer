Statice oliveri

Statice oliveri, commonly known as the Oliver's sea thrift, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and its ability to thrive in salty, well-drained soils. The plant produces small, spike-like clusters of blue to purple flowers, which bloom in late summer and early autumn. Its leaves are narrow and slightly succulent, adapted to retain moisture in arid conditions.

Statice oliveri is often used in coastal landscaping due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.
