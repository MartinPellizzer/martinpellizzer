Salix Myrtillifolia Var. Lingulata

Salix myrtillifolia var. lingulata is a unique variant of the willow family known for its distinctive leaf shape and ecological significance. This plant thrives in moist, temperate environments and is often found near streams and wetlands. Its leaves are narrow and elongated, giving it a distinct appearance compared to other willow species. The plant plays an important role in supporting local wildlife and maintaining soil stability. Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and valuable species in certain regions.
