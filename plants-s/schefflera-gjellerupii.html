Schefflera gjellerupii

Schefflera gjellerupii, commonly known as the Gjellerup schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded crown and compound leaves that are divided into numerous leaflets. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil.

Schefflera gjellerupii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor conditions.
