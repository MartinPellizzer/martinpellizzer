Schizostachyum fenixii

Schizostachyum fenixii, commonly known as the Phoenix palm, is a species of palm native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds that give it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by edible, sweet fruits. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in coastal areas.

Schizostachyum fenixii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine.
