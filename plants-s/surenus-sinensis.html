Surenus sinensis

Surenus sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its tall, arrow-shaped leaves and striking, spadix-like inflorescence. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense clumps.

Surenus sinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and air-purifying properties.
