Senecio clarenceanus

Senecio clarenceanus, commonly known as the Cape mountain snowflake, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking white, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. It has a compact, low-growing habit with hairy leaves and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
