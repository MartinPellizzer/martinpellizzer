Sorbus rehderiana var. rehderiana

Sorbus rehderiana var. rehderiana, commonly known as the Chinese whitebeam, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible for birds but not for humans. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in temperate regions.
