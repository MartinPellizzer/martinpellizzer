Solanum tegore

Solanum tegore, commonly known as the tropical nightshade, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by berries that change color as they mature. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with a slightly hairy texture.

Solanum tegore is often found in moist, shaded environments and can be cultivated as an ornamental plant.
