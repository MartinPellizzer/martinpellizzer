Senecio uvens

Senecio uvens, commonly known as the blue ice plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-gray leaves that are often covered in a powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
