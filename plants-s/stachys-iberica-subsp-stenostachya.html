Stachys iberica subsp. stenostachya

Stachys iberica subsp.

stenostachya, commonly known as narrow-stemmed betony, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe. It features upright, branched stems covered in fine hairs and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface.

The plant produces spikes of violet to purple flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional herbal medicine.
