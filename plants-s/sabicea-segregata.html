Sabicea segregata

Sabicea segregata, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. Its fruit is a pod that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Sabicea segregata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
