Sicyos motozintlensis

Sicyos motozintlensis, commonly known as the Mexican bottle gourd, is a climbing vine native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes other gourds and cucurbits. The plant produces large, bottle-shaped fruits that can grow up to 2 meters in length. Its leaves are large, heart-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

The plant is valued for its edible fruits and has cultural significance in traditional Mesoamerican communities.
