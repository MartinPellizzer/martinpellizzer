Senecio oederifolius

Senecio oederifolius, commonly known as the Oeder's senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in dry, sunny environments. It features narrow, linear leaves and produces small yellow flowers that bloom in summer.
