Setaria teysmannii

Setaria teysmannii, commonly known as slender dropseed, is a grass species native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in prairie and steppe habitats.

Setaria teysmannii is drought-tolerant and plays a role in stabilizing soil in native plant communities.
