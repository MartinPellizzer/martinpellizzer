Sobralia macrantha f. alba

Sobralia macrantha f. alba, commonly known as the white-flowered sobralia, is a hybrid orchid species native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its foliage consists of broad, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in shaded gardens or greenhouses.
