Stellaria borealis

Stellaria borealis, commonly known as the Arctic star, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It produces small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has a mat-forming habit, with slender, branching stems and small, ovate leaves. It thrives in nutrient-poor, well-drained soils and is often found in tundra and rocky habitats.

Stellaria borealis is adapted to cold climates and has a short growing season.
