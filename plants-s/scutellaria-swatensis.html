Scutellaria swatensis

Scutellaria swatensis, commonly known as Swat skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the Swat Valley in Pakistan.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features square, hairy stems and opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. Its small, tubular flowers are typically blue to purple in color, arranged in whorls along the stem.

Scutellaria swatensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
