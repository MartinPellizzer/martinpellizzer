Senecio triangularis var. trigonophyllus

Senecio triangularis var. trigonophyllus, commonly known as the triangular groundsel, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, triangular-shaped leaves that are often tinged with blue or purple hues. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique leaf shape and drought tolerance make it a distinctive addition to succulent collections.
