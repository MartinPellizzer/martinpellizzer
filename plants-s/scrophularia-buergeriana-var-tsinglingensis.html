Scrophularia buergeriana var. tsinglingensis

Scrophularia buergeriana var. tsinglingensis, commonly known as the Tsingling scrophularia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae, which includes many species with medicinal and ornamental value. The plant typically features erect stems with opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves. Its flowers are usually pale pink to white, arranged in whorls along the stem, and bloom during the summer months.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions of southern China.
