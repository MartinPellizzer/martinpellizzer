Senecio phellorrhizus

Senecio phellorrhizus, commonly known as the yellow star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers. The plant has a fibrous root system and is often found growing in rocky or sandy soils. It produces small, greenish to yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

Senecio phellorrhizus is typically drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun conditions.
