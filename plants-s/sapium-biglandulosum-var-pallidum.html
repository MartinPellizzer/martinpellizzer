Sapium biglandulosum var. pallidum

Sapium biglandulosum var. pallidum, commonly known as the pale-leaf Chinese lantern plant, is a variant of the Chinese lantern plant native to China.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are pale green with a waxy texture. The plant produces striking orange-red, lantern-shaped fruits that are often used in ornamental gardening. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This variant is valued for its unique foliage and vibrant fruiting structures, making it a popular choice for garden displays.
