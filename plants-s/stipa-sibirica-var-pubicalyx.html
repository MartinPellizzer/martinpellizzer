Stipa sibirica var. pubicalyx

Stipa sibirica var. pubicalyx, commonly known as Siberian feathergrass, is a perennial grass native to the cold climates of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and delicate, feathery plumes that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, dry habitats such as steppes and rocky slopes. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in landscaping for its elegant, wind-blown foliage.
