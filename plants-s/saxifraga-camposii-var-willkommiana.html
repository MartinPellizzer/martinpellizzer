Saxifraga camposii var. willkommiana

Saxifraga camposii var. willkommiana, commonly known as the Willkomm's saxifrage, is a perennial flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in mountainous or coastal regions. The plant features succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in arid environments. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, arranged in clusters at the ends of stems.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained conditions.
