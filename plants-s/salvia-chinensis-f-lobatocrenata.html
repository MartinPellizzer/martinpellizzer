Salvia chinensis f. lobatocrenata

Salvia chinensis f. lobatocrenata, commonly known as the broad-leaf Chinese sage, is a compact, herbaceous perennial with striking violet-blue flowers.

It features lance-shaped, hairy leaves that are often lobed, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant is native to China and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It is valued for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to attract pollinators.

Its foliage and flowers contribute to its use in traditional medicine and horticulture.
