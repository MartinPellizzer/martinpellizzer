Sinobambusa rubroligula

Sinobambusa rubroligula, commonly known as the red-fruited bamboo, is a species native to China.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, upright stems. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with reddish bracts, which give it its common name. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a smooth, glossy surface.

This bamboo is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
