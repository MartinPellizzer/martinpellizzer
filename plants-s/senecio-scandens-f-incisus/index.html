Senecio Scandens F. Incisus

Senecio scandens f. incisus is a unique and striking plant known for its distinctive foliage and adaptability. Native to southern Africa, this cultivar thrives in well-drained soil and full sun conditions. Its lance-shaped leaves are adorned with intricate, serrated edges that give it a bold, almost sculptural appearance. Often grown as a houseplant or in rock gardens, it adds a touch of exotic beauty to any setting. Despite its ornamental appeal, it requires careful watering to avoid root rot and maintain its vibrant health.
