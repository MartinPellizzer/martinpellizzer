Scutellaria cardiophylla

Scutellaria cardiophylla, commonly known as heart-leaf skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30-60 centimeters, with opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are blue to violet in color, arranged in whorls along the stem, and bloom during the summer months.

Scutellaria cardiophylla is noted for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
