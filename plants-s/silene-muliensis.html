Silene muliensis

Silene muliensis, commonly known as the Muliense campion, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its pink to red flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils. Silene muliensis is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological and pharmacological studies.
