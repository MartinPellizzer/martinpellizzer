Syngonium podophyllum var. peliocladum

Syngonium podophyllum var. peliocladum, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light. Its stems can grow quite long, making it suitable for hanging baskets or trained on trellises.

Botanically, it belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its unique leaf morphology and ability to produce aerial roots.
