Strychnos solerederi

Strychnos solerederi, commonly known as the golden star tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it its common name. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, dark seeds.

Strychnos solerederi is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm climates.
