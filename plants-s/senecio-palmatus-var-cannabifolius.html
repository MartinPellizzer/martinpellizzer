Senecio palmatus var. cannabifolius

Senecio palmatus var. cannabifolius, commonly known as the "cannabis leaf senecio," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, large, palmately lobed leaves that resemble cannabis leaves, hence its common name. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which are typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
