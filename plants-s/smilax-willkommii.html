Smilax willkommii

Smilax willkommii, commonly known as the willkomm's catbrier, is a climbing vine native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It features a woody stem with thorned nodes and produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in racemes. The plant bears bright yellow berries that are an important food source for various bird species. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped with a rough texture and are often used in traditional medicine.

Smilax willkommii thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forests and along stream banks.
