Scindapsus splendidus

Scindapsus splendidus, commonly known as the silver mitten plant, is a member of the Araceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a climbing or trailing plant characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves that often have a striking silver or variegated pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to maintain healthy growth.

Due to its ornamental foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and hanging baskets.
