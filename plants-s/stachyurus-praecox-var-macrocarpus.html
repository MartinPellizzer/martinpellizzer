Stachyurus praecox var. macrocarpus

Stachyurus praecox var. macrocarpus, commonly known as the large-fruited stachyurus, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its delicate, pendulous flowers that appear in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant produces showy, clustered white or pink flowers that are followed by distinctive, elongated, red berries. Its foliage consists of small, ovate leaves with a smooth texture.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
