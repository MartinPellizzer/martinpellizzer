Senecio lentior

Senecio lentior, commonly known as the wooly senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. Senecio lentior thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a favored ornamental plant.
