Schefflera leiophylla

Schefflera leiophylla, commonly known as the smooth-leaf umbrella plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound, umbrella-like leaves that are smooth and glossy, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers, followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant.

Its adaptability to various light conditions makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
