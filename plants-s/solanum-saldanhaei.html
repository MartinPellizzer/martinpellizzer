Solanum saldanhaei

Solanum saldanhaei, commonly known as the Saldanha's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and peppers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are ovate and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are usually white or pale purple.

The fruit is a small, yellowish berry that ripens in the autumn.
