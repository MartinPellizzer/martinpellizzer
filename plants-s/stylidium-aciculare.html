Stylidium aciculare

Stylidium aciculare, commonly known as the needle-stalked sun-orchid, is a small, herbaceous plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, needle-like leaves and a striking, spurred flower that resembles an orchid. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils in arid or semi-arid regions. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom during the warmer months.

Stylidium aciculare is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and along roadsides.
