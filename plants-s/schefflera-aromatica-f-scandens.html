Schefflera aromatica f. scandens

Schefflera aromatica f. scandens, commonly known as the Chinese Evergreen, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features glossy, dark green, palmately compound leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by dark blue berries. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance of low light and minimal water requirements.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental purposes in both residential and commercial settings.
