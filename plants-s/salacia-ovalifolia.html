Salacia ovalifolia

Salacia ovalifolia, commonly known as the oval-leaf salacia, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Salaciacae and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically yellow or orange when mature. It is often found growing in the understory of humid, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal potential, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and research settings.
