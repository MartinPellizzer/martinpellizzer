Saussurea brahuica

Saussurea brahuica, commonly known as the Himalayan yellow gentian, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, thriving in cold and arid conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

The plant is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Ayurvedic practices.
