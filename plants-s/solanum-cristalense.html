Solanum cristalense

Solanum cristalense, commonly known as the crystalline nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and peppers. The plant is characterized by its hairy stems and small, clustered white or purple flowers. Its fruit is a berry that turns red when mature, containing several seeds.

Due to its toxic properties, it is not typically cultivated for food or ornamental purposes.
