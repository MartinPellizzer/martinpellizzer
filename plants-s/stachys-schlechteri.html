Stachys schlechteri

Stachys schlechteri, commonly known as the Schlechter's hedge-nettle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, pale blue to purple flowers arranged in spikes, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
