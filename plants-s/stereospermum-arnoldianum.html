Stereospermum arnoldianum

Stereospermum arnoldianum, commonly known as the Arnold's starfruit tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall. The plant features glossy, elliptical leaves and produces yellow, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a unique, star-shaped berry that changes color from green to yellow as it ripens.

The botanical characteristics of this plant make it a notable species in both horticulture and ecological studies.
