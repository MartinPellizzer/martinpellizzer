Smilax microchina

Smilax microchina, commonly known as Chinese smilax, is a climbing shrub native to East Asia.

It features thorny stems and heart-shaped leaves with a rough, waxy texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into bright red berries. It is often found in moist woodlands and is valued for its ornamental qualities.

Botanically, it is characterized by its distinctive morphology and adaptability to various soil conditions.
