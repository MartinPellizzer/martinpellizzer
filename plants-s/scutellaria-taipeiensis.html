Scutellaria taipeiensis

Scutellaria taipeiensis, commonly known as Taipei skullcap, is a species of flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
