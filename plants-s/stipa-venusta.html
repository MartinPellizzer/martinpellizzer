Stipa venusta

Stipa venusta, commonly known as the showy feathergrass, is a species of grass native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and fluffy, silver-green plumes that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. Its flowers are wind-pollinated, and the seeds are dispersed by the wind, contributing to its ability to colonize new areas.

Stipa venusta is often used in ornamental gardening for its elegant, feathery look.
