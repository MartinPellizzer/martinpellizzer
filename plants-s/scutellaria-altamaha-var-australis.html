Scutellaria altamaha var. australis

Scutellaria altamaha var. australis, commonly known as the Florida skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square, hairy stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wetlands.
