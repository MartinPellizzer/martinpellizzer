Scilla flaccidula

Scilla flaccidula, commonly known as the flaccid squill, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and clusters of bell-shaped blue or violet flowers.
