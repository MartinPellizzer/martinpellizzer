Seruneum crucianum

Seruneum crucianum, commonly known as the crucian seruneum, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, cruciform flowers that typically bloom in early spring. The plant has narrow, elongated leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its seeds are dispersed by water, contributing to its spread in aquatic environments.
