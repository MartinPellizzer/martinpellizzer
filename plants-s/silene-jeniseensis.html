Silene jeniseensis

Silene jeniseensis, commonly known as the Siberian catchfly, is a flowering plant native to the cold regions of Siberia.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late summer.
