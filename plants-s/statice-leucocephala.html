Statice leucocephala

Statice leucocephala, commonly known as white statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, white, bell-shaped flowers. The plant is often used in floral arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms and delicate appearance. Botanically, it belongs to the family Plumbaginaceae and is known for its salt tolerance, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Its foliage is typically narrow and grass-like, with a gray-green hue that adds visual interest.
