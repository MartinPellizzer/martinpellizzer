Stelis magnipetala

Stelis magnipetala, commonly known as the large-petaled stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Stelis magnipetala is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
