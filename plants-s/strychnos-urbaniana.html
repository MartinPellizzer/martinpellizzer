Strychnos urbaniana

Strychnos urbaniana, commonly known as the African strychnos, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Loganiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces woody, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
