Salsola salsa

Salsola salsa, commonly known as desert sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its small, fleshy leaves and clusters of greenish-white flowers. The plant is highly drought-tolerant and thrives in sandy or gravelly soils. It plays a role in stabilizing soils in desert environments and is often found in coastal dunes.

Salsola salsa is also used in traditional medicine in some regions for its purported health benefits.
