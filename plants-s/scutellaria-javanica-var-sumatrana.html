Scutellaria javanica var. sumatrana

Scutellaria javanica var. sumatrana, commonly known as the Sumatran skullcap, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. The plant features square stems and opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, two-lipped flowers are typically blue or purple, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

It is often found in moist, shaded habitats like forests and streambanks.
