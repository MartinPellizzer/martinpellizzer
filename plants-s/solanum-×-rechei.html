Solanum × rechei

Solanum × rechei, commonly known as the hybrid nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or purple in color. Its fruit, a yellow or orange berry, is edible but contains toxic compounds.

Solanum × rechei is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and flowering habit.
