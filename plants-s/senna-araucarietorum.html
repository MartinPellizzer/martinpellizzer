Senna araucarietorum

Senna araucarietorum, commonly known as the Australian senna, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal regions and sandy soils. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are bipinnate, meaning they are divided into small leaflets, and the plant is known for its rapid growth rate.

Senna araucarietorum is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, which enriches the soil.
