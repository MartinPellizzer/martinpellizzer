Semecarpus angustifolius

Semecarpus angustifolius, commonly known as the Indian chestnut or broadleaf chestnut, is a deciduous tree native to the Indian subcontinent.

The plant is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional Ayurvedic medicine.
