Statice confusa var. intermedia

Statice confusa var. intermedia, commonly known as the common statice.

It is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region and belongs to the Amaranthaceae family. This species is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, spike-like inflorescences composed of small, colorful flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, and the plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens and floral arrangements.
