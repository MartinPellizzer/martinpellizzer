Sphenostigma polycephalum

Sphenostigma polycephalum, commonly known as the clustered sphenostigma, is a rare and unique plant species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, clustered growth habit, with multiple stems arising from a single base. The plant features small, delicate leaves that are often arranged in a spiral pattern along the stems. Its flowers are typically small and inconspicuous, appearing in clusters near the base of the plant.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, Sphenostigma polycephalum is considered a botanically significant but endangered species.
