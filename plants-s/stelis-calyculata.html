Stelis calyculata

Stelis calyculata, commonly known as the calyculata orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including regions such as Indonesia and the Philippines. This epiphytic plant typically grows on trees or rocks, anchoring itself with its roots without deriving nutrients from its host. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pink and yellow.

Stelis calyculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
