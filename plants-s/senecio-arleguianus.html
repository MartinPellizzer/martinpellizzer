Senecio arleguianus

Senecio arleguianus, commonly known as the Eland's milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Senecio arleguianus is adapted to arid environments and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
