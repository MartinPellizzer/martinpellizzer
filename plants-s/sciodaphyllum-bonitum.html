Sciodaphyllum bonitum

Sciodaphyllum bonitum, commonly known as the bonnet fern, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its unique, helmet-shaped fronds.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to the wet tropical forests of Southeast Asia. The plant features a short, creeping rhizome with dense, dark green, leathery fronds that are often adorned with a distinctive, rounded apical structure. Sciodaphyllum bonitum thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to retain moisture and its elegant, almost sculptural foliage.
