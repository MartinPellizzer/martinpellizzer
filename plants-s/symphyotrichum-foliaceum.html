Symphyotrichum foliaceum

Symphyotrichum foliaceum, commonly known as leafy aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late summer to early fall. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or along stream banks.

Symphyotrichum foliaceum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
