Specklinia pandurifera

Specklinia pandurifera, commonly known as the golden rosette, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. This plant is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit and striking golden-yellow leaves that are often adorned with dark green markings. The flowers are small and greenish-white, arranged in a spike, and bloom during the wet season.

Specklinia pandurifera is a popular houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
