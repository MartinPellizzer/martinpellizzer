Saintpaulia pusilla

Saintpaulia pusilla, commonly known as the miniature African violet, is a compact, low-growing plant native to Tanzania.

It features small, velvety leaves and produces clusters of delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers consistently moist, well-draining soil. It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental appeal and ease of care.

Saintpaulia pusilla is also valued for its ability to bloom year-round under optimal conditions.
