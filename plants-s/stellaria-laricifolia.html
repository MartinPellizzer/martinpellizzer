Stellaria laricifolia

Stellaria laricifolia, commonly known as the pine-leaf starwort, is a flowering plant native to the northern hemisphere.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically thrives in moist, acidic soils, often found in forested areas. The plant features small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are narrow and resemble pine needles, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Stellaria laricifolia is part of the Caryophyllaceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with similar characteristics.
