Specularia speculum-veneris var. libanotica

Specularia speculum-veneris var. libanotica, commonly known as the Lebanon bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of delicate, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue to violet. The leaves are lanceolate and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal. This species thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique floral structure and historical use in traditional medicine.
