Schilleria umbrosa

Schilleria umbrosa, commonly known as the shadow fern, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, intricately veined fronds that often appear in shades of green and silver. The plant thrives in shaded, humid environments, making it well-suited for woodland or shaded garden settings. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread slowly, forming dense mats over time.

Schilleria umbrosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its aesthetic foliage.
