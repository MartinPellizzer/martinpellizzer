Sisymbrium austriacum var. acutangulum

Sisymbrium austriacum var. acutangulum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved shepherd's purse, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright, branching stems and small, triangular seed pods. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as meadows and rocky soils. Its leaves are alternate, pinnately lobed, and have a distinctive, serrated edge.

It produces yellow flowers in early summer, which attract various pollinators.
