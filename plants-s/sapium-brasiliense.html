Sapium brasiliense

Sapium brasiliense, commonly known as Brazilian wild chenopod, is a flowering plant native to South America.

The plant produces small, greenish flowers in clusters, which are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
