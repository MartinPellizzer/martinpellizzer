Spondias negrosensis

Spondias negrosensis, commonly known as the Negros satinash, is a deciduous tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Anacardiaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, grey-brown bark and alternate, simple leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by edible, yellow-orange fruits that are similar in appearance to mangoes.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in lowland forests and secondary growth areas.
