Spicanta polypodioides

Spicanta polypodioides, commonly known as the narrow-leaf staghorn fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It features a distinctive, tree-like appearance with a central trunk covered in dense, overlapping fronds. The fronds are typically narrow and leathery, with a dark green color and a waxy texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on tree trunks or rocks.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in tropical and subtropical climates.
