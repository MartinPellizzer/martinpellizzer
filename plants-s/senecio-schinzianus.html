Senecio schinzianus

Senecio schinzianus, commonly known as the Schinz's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a cluster at the tips of its stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

The species is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
