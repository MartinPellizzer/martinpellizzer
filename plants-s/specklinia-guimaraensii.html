Specklinia guimaraensii

Specklinia guimaraensii, commonly known as the Guimaraensii orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to the rainforests of Brazil.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and clustered inflorescences. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with a distinctive speckled pattern on the petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, organic-rich substrates for optimal growth.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
