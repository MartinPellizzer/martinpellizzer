Saxifraga rivularis var. laurentiana

Saxifraga rivularis var. laurentiana, commonly known as the Laurentian saxifrage, is a perennial flowering plant native to the cooler regions of North America.

It typically grows in moist, rocky habitats such as stream banks and shaded woodlands. The plant features small, clustered leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. Its flowers are pink to reddish in color, with five petals and a central cluster of stamens.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in challenging environmental conditions.
