Scutellaria mesostegia

Scutellaria mesostegia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved skullcap, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, wooded areas and has small, tubular blue or violet flowers. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a hairy texture, and it produces small, nut-like seeds.

Scutellaria mesostegia is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
