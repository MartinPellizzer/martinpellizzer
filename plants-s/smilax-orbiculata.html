Smilax orbiculata

Smilax orbiculata, commonly known as the roundleaf arrowhead, is a perennial vine native to the southeastern United States.

It features a robust, woody stem with tendrils that allow it to climb and spread across vegetation. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by spiny, berry-like fruits. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often have a waxy texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Smilax orbiculata is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and light conditions.
