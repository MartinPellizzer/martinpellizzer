Salix Silesiaca Var. Fagifolia

Salix silesiaca var. fagifolia is a unique willow species native to the mountainous regions of central Europe. This variant is distinguished by its distinctive leaf shape and bark texture, setting it apart from other willow varieties. It thrives in cool, moist environments, often found in forested areas with well-drained soil. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is increasingly appreciated in horticulture and conservation efforts. Its presence highlights the biodiversity of temperate forest ecosystems.
