Sterculia impressinervis

Sterculia impressinervis, commonly known as the broad-veined sterculia, is a tropical tree native to regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves with prominent veins that give it its scientific name. The plant produces showy, yellowish-white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local communities.
