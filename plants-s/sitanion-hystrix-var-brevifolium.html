Sitanion hystrix var. brevifolium

Sitanion hystrix var. brevifolium, commonly known as the short-leaved porcupine plant, is a succulent species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and compact growth habit. The plant's leaves are short and thick, often covered in fine, bristly hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
