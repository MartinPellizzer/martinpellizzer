Steudnera gagei

Steudnera gagei, commonly known as the Gage's arrowroot, is a member of the Araceae family and is native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that are often marked with distinctive patterns and colors. The plant produces striking inflorescences with a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Steudnera gagei is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its historical use in traditional medicine.
