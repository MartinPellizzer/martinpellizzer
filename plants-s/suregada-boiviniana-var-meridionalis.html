Suregada boiviniana var. meridionalis

Suregada boiviniana var. meridionalis, commonly known as the southern suregada, is a species native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its small, elliptical leaves with a glossy green surface. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and sandy environments.

Its ornamental value and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for garden landscaping.
