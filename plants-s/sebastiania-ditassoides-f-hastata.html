Sebastiania ditassoides f. hastata

Sebastiania ditassoides f. hastata, commonly known as the broad-leaved sebastiania, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with a waxy surface. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that are followed by red, fleshy fruits. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage and unique fruit structure.
