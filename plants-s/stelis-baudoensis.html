Stelis baudoensis

Stelis baudoensis, commonly known as the Baudo orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of Ecuador.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

Stelis baudoensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
