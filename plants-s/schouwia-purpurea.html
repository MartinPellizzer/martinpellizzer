Schouwia purpurea

Schouwia purpurea, commonly known as the purple schouwia, is a striking member of the Gesneriaceae family native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple and red flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, aiding in moisture retention. Schouwia purpurea is a perennial shrub that thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements have made it a favored plant among horticultural enthusiasts.
