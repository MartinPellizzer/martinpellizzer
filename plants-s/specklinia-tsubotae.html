Specklinia tsubotae

Specklinia tsubotae, commonly known as the Tsubota's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often during the rainy season. Specklinia tsubotae thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance and rarity make it a sought-after specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
