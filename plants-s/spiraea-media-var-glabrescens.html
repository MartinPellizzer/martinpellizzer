Spiraea media var. glabrescens

Spiraea media var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth spiraea, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It features arching stems that produce clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers in the spring. The leaves are oval-shaped, with a smooth texture and a glossy appearance. It typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This plant is valued for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
