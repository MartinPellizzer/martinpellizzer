Sarcoglottis hemichrea

Sarcoglottis hemichrea, commonly known as the red spider orchid, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive red and green foliage and produces small, delicate flowers with a unique spider-like appearance. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is often found in tropical rainforests. It has fibrous roots and thrives in shaded, moist environments.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a conservation concern in some regions.
