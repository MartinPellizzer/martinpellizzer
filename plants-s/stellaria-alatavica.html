Stellaria alatavica

Stellaria alatavica, commonly known as the Armenian starwort, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to the Caucasus region.

It features small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to July. The plant has slender stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in mountainous areas.

Stellaria alatavica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
