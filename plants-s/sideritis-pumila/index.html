Sideritis Pumila

Sideritis pumila is a small, herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, known for its aromatic and medicinal properties. This species is characterized by its delicate pink or purple flowers and hairy, narrow leaves. It thrives in rocky and dry environments, often growing in alpine meadows. Due to its unique appearance and potential health benefits, Sideritis pumila has gained attention among botanists and herbalists alike.
