Schizoglossum aschersonianum

Schizoglossum aschersonianum, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact, often hairy stems and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are usually pale blue or purple and bloom in spring.

Schizoglossum aschersonianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
