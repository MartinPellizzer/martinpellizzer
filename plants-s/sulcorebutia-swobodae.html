Sulcorebutia swobodae

Sulcorebutia swobodae, commonly known as the Desert Rose, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and fleshy, cylindrical stems that store water. The plant produces showy, pink or purple flowers during the warmer months, adding ornamental value. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for xeriscaping.

Due to its slow growth and unique appearance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
