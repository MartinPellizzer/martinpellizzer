Sinocrassula indica var. serrata

Sinocrassula indica var. serrata, commonly known as the serrated sinocrassula, is a compact, succulent plant native to China.

It features dense, rosette-shaped foliage composed of fleshy, bluish-green leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink during the spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container plantings.
