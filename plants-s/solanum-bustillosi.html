Solanum bustillosi

Solanum bustillosi, commonly known as the Bustillos's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the Andean region of South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically purple or violet in color. Its leaves are ovate and have a slightly hairy texture.

Solanum bustillosi is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique appearance.
