Senecio turkestanicus

Senecio turkestanicus, commonly known as the Turkestan groundsel, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are usually gray-green with a fuzzy texture, and it produces small yellow flowers in the summer.

Senecio turkestanicus is often found in mountainous regions and is valued for its resilience and ornamental appeal.
