Stylosanthes diarthra

Stylosanthes diarthra, commonly known as the smallleaf stylo, is a perennial leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, elongated pods containing several seeds. It is well adapted to poor soils and is often used in forage systems due to its high nutritional value.

Stylosanthes diarthra is also valued for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, improving soil fertility.
