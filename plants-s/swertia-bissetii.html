Swertia bissetii

Swertia bissetii, commonly known as the Tibetan gentian, is a perennial herb native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, thriving in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged oppositely along the stem.

Swertia bissetii is valued for its ornamental beauty and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
