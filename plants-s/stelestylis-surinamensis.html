Stelestylis surinamensis

Stelestylis surinamensis, commonly known as the Surinam spider orchid, is a tropical orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, spider-like flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its flowers are usually pale green or white, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insect species.

Stelestylis surinamensis is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
