Salacia erecta var. kabweensis

Salacia erecta var. kabweensis, commonly known as the Kabwe salacia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the family Salicaceae, which includes willows and poplars. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered leaves. Its flowers are typically greenish-yellow and appear in spring.

It is often found in moist, wooded areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
