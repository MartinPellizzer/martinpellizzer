Swertia wellbyi

Swertia wellbyi, commonly known as the wellby's gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are usually opposite and lance-shaped, with a rough texture.

Swertia wellbyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
