Solanum zanzibarense var. abbreviatum

Solanum zanzibarense var. abbreviatum, commonly known as the Zanzibar nightshade, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family.

It is native to the coastal regions of East Africa, particularly Zanzibar and nearby islands. The plant is characterized by its small, green, egg-shaped leaves and clusters of white or pale purple flowers. It produces small, round berries that are typically green when unripe and turn red when mature.

This variety is often found in moist, shaded habitats and is adapted to tropical climates.
