Searsia aucheri

Searsia aucheri, commonly known as the silverleaf bush, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage that turns a striking silver-gray in the winter months. The plant produces small, white flowers that appear in clusters during the late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a waxy texture.

Searsia aucheri is well-suited for coastal gardens due to its tolerance of salty air and drought conditions.
