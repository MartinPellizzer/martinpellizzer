Satureja schusteri

Satureja schusteri, commonly known as Schuster's savory, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants known for their medicinal and culinary uses. The plant features small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, and its leaves are aromatic, often used in traditional medicine. Satureja schusteri thrives in rocky soils and is adapted to harsh, alpine environments.

It is valued for its essential oils, which contain compounds with potential antimicrobial properties.
