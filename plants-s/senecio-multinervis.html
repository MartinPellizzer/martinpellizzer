Senecio multinervis

Senecio multinervis, commonly known as the Narrow-veined Senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It features narrow, linear leaves and produces small yellow flowers arranged in clusters.

Senecio multinervis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought-resistant properties.
