Spartium gracile

Spartium gracile, commonly known as slender spartia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches heights of about 30 centimeters. The plant features slender, branched stems covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped. It produces small, pale blue or white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer.

Spartium gracile is often found in dry, open habitats such as rocky slopes and sandy soils.
