Stephanomeria exigua subsp. carotifera

Stephanomeria exigua subsp.

carotifera, commonly known as the slender stephanomeria, is a flowering plant native to California. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and sandy soils. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

This subspecies is often found in coastal and inland areas of southern California.
