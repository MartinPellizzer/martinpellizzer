Scutellaria tarokoensis

Scutellaria tarokoensis, commonly known as the Taroko blueflower, is a perennial herb native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
