Stromanthe papillosa

Stromanthe papillosa, commonly known as the velvet leaf plant, is a tropical ornamental plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage with intricate patterns of green, silver, and pink. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptical in shape and often have a waxy texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.

This plant is popular in indoor gardening for its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
