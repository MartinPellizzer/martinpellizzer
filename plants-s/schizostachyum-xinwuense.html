Schizostachyum xinwuense

Schizostachyum xinwuense, commonly known as the Xinwu bamboo, is a species of grass native to China.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 10 meters. The plant has narrow, elongated leaves that are typically green and have a smooth texture. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Schizostachyum xinwuense is valued for its ornamental appeal and has potential uses in landscaping and sustainable construction.
