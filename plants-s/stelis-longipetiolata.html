Stelis longipetiolata

Stelis longipetiolata, commonly known as the long-petioled stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features slender, erect pseudobulbs covered in smooth, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers during the warmer months. Its roots are adapted for aerial attachment to trees, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Stelis longipetiolata is valued in horticulture for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
