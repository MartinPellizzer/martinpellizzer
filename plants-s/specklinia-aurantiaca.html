Specklinia aurantiaca

Specklinia aurantiaca, commonly known as the golden speckled orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, hairy stems and bright yellow flowers with distinctive speckled markings. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine, soft hairs.

Specklinia aurantiaca is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
