Stelis lindleyana var. carnosior

Stelis lindleyana var. carnosior, commonly known as the waxy stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

This orchid is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit and its striking, often fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and pink. The leaves are typically thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in its natural habitat. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
