Stapelia venusta

Stapelia venusta, commonly known as the starry stapelia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed stems and distinctive star-shaped flowers. The flowers are typically yellow with a red or orange center, and they emit a strong, unpleasant odor to attract pollinators. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

This plant is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
