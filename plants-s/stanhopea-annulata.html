Stanhopea annulata

Stanhopea annulata, commonly known as the ringed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated pseudobulbs and striking, showy flowers that often feature intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. Its flowering period occurs during the dry season, and it is prized for its ornamental value in horticulture.

Stanhopea annulata requires specific care, including proper light exposure and careful watering to thrive.
