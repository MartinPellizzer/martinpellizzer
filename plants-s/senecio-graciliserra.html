Senecio graciliserra

Senecio graciliserra, commonly known as the slenderleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial with slender, branching stems and small, narrow leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom from late spring to early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Senecio graciliserra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
