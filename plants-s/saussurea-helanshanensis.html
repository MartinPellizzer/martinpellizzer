Saussurea helanshanensis

Saussurea helanshanensis, commonly known as the Helanshan Saussurea, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stem and dense, woolly foliage. The plant produces yellow to orange composite flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is adapted to survive in harsh, semi-desert environments with limited water availability.
