Saccus dadah

Saccus dadah, commonly known as the African horned vulture, is a large bird of prey native to sub-Saharan Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, wrinkled skin and prominent, curved beak. The bird has strong wings that allow it to soar for long distances in search of food. It primarily feeds on carrion and plays a crucial role in the ecosystem by helping to clean the environment.

Its habitat includes open savannas and grasslands where it can easily spot potential food sources.
