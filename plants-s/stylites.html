Stylites

Stylites, commonly known as the "columnar plant," are unique botanical structures that resemble pillars or spires.

They are typically found in arid environments, where they form dense clusters of small, needle-like leaves. These plants are adapted to extreme conditions, with deep root systems that help them access water from underground sources. Their compact growth form minimizes water loss through transpiration.

Stylites are often used in traditional medicine and are valued for their resilience and aesthetic appeal.
