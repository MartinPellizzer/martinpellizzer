Sinningia insularis

Sinningia insularis, commonly known as the Insular Gloxinia, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its tuberous root system, which allows it to store nutrients and water. The plant produces striking, velvety flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, often with contrasting patterns. Its foliage consists of heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green.

Sinningia insularis is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
