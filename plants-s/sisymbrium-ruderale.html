Sisymbrium ruderale

Sisymbrium ruderale, commonly known as field pennycress, is an annual herbaceous plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, which are often dispersed by wind. Native to Europe, it is now found in various parts of the world, including North America.

Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and slightly hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
