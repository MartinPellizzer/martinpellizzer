Senecio atratus var. milleflorus

Senecio atratus var. milleflorus, commonly known as the purple coneflower, is a perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, deep purple to almost black flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces fluffy, seed-filled spheres that are attractive to birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and drought tolerance.
