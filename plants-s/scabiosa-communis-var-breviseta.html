Scabiosa communis var. breviseta

Scabiosa communis var. breviseta, commonly known as the dwarf milkweed or small-leaved scabious, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe.

It features delicate, pink or white flowers that bloom in summer, typically arranged in flat-topped clusters. The plant has narrow, hairy leaves that are shorter and more compact compared to other varieties of Scabiosa. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its botanical characteristics make it both ornamental and beneficial to pollinators.
