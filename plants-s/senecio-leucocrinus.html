Senecio leucocrinus

Senecio leucocrinus, commonly known as the white crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Senecio leucocrinus is known for its ornamental value and is cultivated for its attractive, long-lasting blooms.
