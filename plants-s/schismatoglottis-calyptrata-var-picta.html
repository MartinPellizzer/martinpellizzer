Schismatoglottis calyptrata var. picta

Schismatoglottis calyptrata var. picta, commonly known as the painted schismatoglottis, is a striking member of the Araceae family native to tropical regions of Africa.

This plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with intricate, often variegated patterns that range from green to white and pink. The inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Due to its unique foliage and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
