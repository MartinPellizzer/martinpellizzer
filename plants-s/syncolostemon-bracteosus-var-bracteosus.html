Syncolostemon bracteosus var. bracteosus

Syncolostemon bracteosus var. bracteosus, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of showy, tubular flowers in shades of violet, pink, and white. The plant typically grows as a compact shrub, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Syncolostemon bracteosus var. bracteosus is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
