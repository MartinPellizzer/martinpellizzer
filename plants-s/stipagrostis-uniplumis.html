Stipagrostis uniplumis

Stipagrostis uniplumis, commonly known as the single-flowered grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically has narrow, grass-like leaves and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. It is native to parts of Africa and is often found in sandy or rocky soils.

Due to its unique morphology and ecological niche, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
