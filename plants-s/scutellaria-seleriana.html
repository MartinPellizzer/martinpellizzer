Scutellaria seleriana

Scutellaria seleriana, commonly known as the Seler's skullcap, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Scutellaria seleriana is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
