Scutellaria hypopolia

Scutellaria hypopolia, commonly known as the dwarf skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers sunny to partially shaded environments.

Scutellaria hypopolia is valued for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
