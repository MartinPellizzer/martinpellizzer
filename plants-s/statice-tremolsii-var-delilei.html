Statice tremolsii var. delilei

Statice tremolsii var. delilei, commonly known as the Delile's sea thrift, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Europe.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces dense clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and purple. The plant is well adapted to salty, windy environments and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often used in coastal landscaping due to its hardiness and ability to stabilize dunes.

The flowers are typically visited by various pollinators, including bees and butterflies.
