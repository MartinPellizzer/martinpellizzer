Senecio petasitoides

Senecio petasitoides, commonly known as the African daisy or African sunflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow to orange composite flowers. The plant has a rosette-forming habit with narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Senecio petasitoides is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
