Saussurea costus

Saussurea costus, commonly known as Indian costus or costus root, is a perennial herb native to the Himalayas and parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, narrow leaves and yellowish flowers. The plant is notable for its aromatic, rhizomatous root system, which is widely used in traditional medicine. It thrives in high-altitude regions with cool, moist climates and well-drained soils.

Saussurea costus is also valued for its ornamental qualities and has been studied for its potential pharmacological properties.
