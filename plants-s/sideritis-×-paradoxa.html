Sideritis × paradoxa

Sideritis × paradoxa, commonly known as the hybrid sainfoin, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. The plant features slender stems and small, pink or purple flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, and it is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Sideritis × paradoxa is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional herbal remedies.
