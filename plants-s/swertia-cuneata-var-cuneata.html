Swertia cuneata var. cuneata

Swertia cuneata var. cuneata, commonly known as the Chinese gentian, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and mountainous regions. The plant features striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

It is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits and is also cultivated for ornamental purposes.
