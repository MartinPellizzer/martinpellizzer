Spiranthes colorata

Spiranthes colorata, commonly known as the pink spire orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its delicate, spiral-shaped inflorescence. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wetlands. Its flowers are usually pink to lavender in color, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain seeds.

Spiranthes colorata is often found in the eastern United States and is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological significance.
