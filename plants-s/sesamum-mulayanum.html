Sesamum mulayanum

Sesamum mulayanum, commonly known as the African sesame or wild sesame, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It belongs to the Pedaliaceae family and is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, black, oily seeds. These seeds are rich in essential oils and are used in traditional medicine and as a source of oil.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and agricultural contexts.
