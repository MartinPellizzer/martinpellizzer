Strigilia chrysastera

Strigilia chrysastera, commonly known as the golden star lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features strap-like, narrow leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
