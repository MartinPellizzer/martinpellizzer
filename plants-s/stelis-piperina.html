Stelis piperina

Stelis piperina, commonly known as the Piperina orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in tropical rainforests. The plant has a short, creeping rhizome with numerous pseudobulbs, each bearing a single leaf. Its flowers are small and clustered, often in a raceme, with a distinctive yellowish-green coloration.

Stelis piperina prefers high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
