Sphaeralcea pedata

Sphaeralcea pedata, commonly known as the showy milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its branched stems and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are alternate, pinnately divided, and covered in fine hairs.
