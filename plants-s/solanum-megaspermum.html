Solanum megaspermum

Solanum megaspermum, commonly known as the giant nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial native to South America.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range from white to purple. The plant produces berries that are typically yellow or orange when ripe, containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its toxic properties, it is important to handle the plant with care.
