Serapias × intermedia subsp. hyblaea

Serapias × intermedia subsp.

hyblaea, commonly known as the Hyblaea orchis, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Orchidaceae. It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically display a mix of yellow and orange hues.

The plant is native to the Mediterranean region, particularly Sicily and southern Italy, where it thrives in rocky and calcareous soils. It produces a single, erect stem with several flowers arranged in a loose raceme.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
