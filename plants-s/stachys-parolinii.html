Stachys parolinii

Stachys parolinii, commonly known as the Parolin's hedge nettle, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its small, tubular flowers are usually pink or purple, and they bloom in late spring to early summer.

Stachys parolinii is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
