Scorodonia massiliensis

Scorodonia massiliensis, commonly known as the Massiliense scorodonia, is a rare and lesser-known plant species.

It is characterized by its unique morphology, featuring a basal rosette of narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It is native to specific regions in the Mediterranean and thrives in rocky, well-drained soils.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
