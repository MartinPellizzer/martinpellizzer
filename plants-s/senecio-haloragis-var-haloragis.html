Senecio haloragis var. haloragis

Senecio haloragis var. haloragis, commonly known as the silverleaf senecio, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features wiry, silver-gray stems and narrow, linear leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive silvery appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is often cultivated in arid regions.
