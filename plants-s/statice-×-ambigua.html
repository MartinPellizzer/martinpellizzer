Statice × ambigua

Statice × ambigua, commonly known as the common statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions. The plant produces dense clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, purple, and white, which bloom throughout the summer. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often used in floral arrangements due to their durability.

Statice × ambigua is also valued for its ability to withstand salt spray and harsh coastal environments.
