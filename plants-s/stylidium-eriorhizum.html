Stylidium eriorhizum

Stylidium eriorhizum, commonly known as the hairy sun-orchid, is a small, flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its bright yellow or orange flowers. The plant has hairy, grass-like leaves and produces a single, erect flowering stem. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.

Stylidium eriorhizum is adapted to withstand drought and heat, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
