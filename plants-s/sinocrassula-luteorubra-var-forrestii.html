Sinocrassula luteorubra var. forrestii

Sinocrassula luteorubra var. forrestii, commonly known as the Forrester's stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to China.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming growth habit. The leaves are typically fleshy, with a reddish or orange hue, especially when exposed to bright light. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or red during the spring or early summer.

This variety is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
