Schefflera calyptrata

Schefflera calyptrata, commonly known as the African shield fern, is a tropical plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by spherical, spore-bearing structures called sorus. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
