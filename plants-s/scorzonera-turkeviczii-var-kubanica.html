Scorzonera turkeviczii var. kubanica

Scorzonera turkeviczii var. kubanica, commonly known as the Kuban scorzonera, is a perennial herb native to the Caucasus region.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its robust, hairy stems and large, showy yellow flowers. The plant produces a thick, tuberous root system that is often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and is typically found in steppe and rocky habitats.

Its botanical features make it an important species for both ecological and medicinal studies.
