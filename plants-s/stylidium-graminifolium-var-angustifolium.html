Stylidium graminifolium var. angustifolium

Stylidium graminifolium var. angustifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sun-orchid, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and vibrant yellow flowers that resemble orchids. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils in arid or semi-arid regions. It is a perennial herb that thrives in full sun and has a short growing season.

Its unique morphology and striking appearance make it a notable species in its native habitat.
