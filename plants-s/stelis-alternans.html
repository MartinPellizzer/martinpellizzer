Stelis alternans

Stelis alternans, commonly known as the variable spider orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a short rhizome and a rosette of fleshy, dark green leaves. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences bearing small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding substrates.

Stelis alternans is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique floral display.
