Salvia verbenaca var. delicatula

Salvia verbenaca var. delicatula, commonly known as the slender sage, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems covered in soft, gray-green foliage. The plant produces spikes of violet to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its aromatic leaves and showy flowers make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and pollinator-friendly landscapes.
