Sedum kouyangense

Sedum kouyangense, commonly known as the Kouyang stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to China.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in summer. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
