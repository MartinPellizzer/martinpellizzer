Spiraea cantoniensis var. lanceata

Spiraea cantoniensis var. lanceata, commonly known as the lance-leaf weeping spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers in early summer, which are borne on long, arching stems. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its graceful appearance and seasonal blooms make it a valuable addition to gardens and landscapes.
