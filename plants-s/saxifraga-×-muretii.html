Saxifraga × muretii

Saxifraga × muretii, commonly known as the alpine saxifrage, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Saxifragaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Europe and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features rosettes of dark green, hairy leaves and produces small, white or pink flowers in early summer. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, alpine environments.

Saxifraga × muretii is often found in high-altitude meadows and is valued for its ornamental appeal in rock gardens.
