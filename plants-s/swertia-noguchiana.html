Swertia noguchiana

Swertia noguchiana, commonly known as the Japanese swertia, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its slender stems and small, blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Swertia noguchiana is valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
