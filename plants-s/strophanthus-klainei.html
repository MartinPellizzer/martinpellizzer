Strophanthus klainei

Strophanthus klainei, commonly known as the red-stemmed arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, pendulous flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a red or orange throat. The plant produces woody capsules that contain seeds, and its leaves are typically alternate and simple. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or savanna habitats.

Strophanthus klainei is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
