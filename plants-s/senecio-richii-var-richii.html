Senecio richii var. richii

Senecio richii var. richii, commonly known as the Orange Sword, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking orange-colored bracts that surround its small yellow flowers. The plant has a rosette-forming habit with fleshy, sword-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
