Stipa prilipkoana

Stipa prilipkoana, commonly known as the Prilipko's feathergrass, is a species of grass native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and dense, tufted growth habit, which are typical of many grass species in arid environments. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, rocky habitats.

Due to its hardy nature, it is adapted to withstand drought and cold temperatures.
