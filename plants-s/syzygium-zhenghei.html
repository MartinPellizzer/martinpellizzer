Syzygium zhenghei

Syzygium zhenghei, commonly known as the Guangdong tamarind.

It is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family, native to southern China. The plant is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. Its fruits are typically round, yellowish, and contain several seeds.

Syzygium zhenghei is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
