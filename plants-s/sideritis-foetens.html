Sideritis foetens

Sideritis foetens, commonly known as stinking rock rose, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its small, hairy leaves and spikes of pink or purple flowers. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats.

Sideritis foetens is noted for its strong, sometimes unpleasant odor, which is a distinctive botanical feature.
