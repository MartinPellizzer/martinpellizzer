Spiraea mairei

Spiraea mairei, commonly known as the Maire's spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to New Zealand.

It features slender, arching branches that bear clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers in early spring. The plant has elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter green underneath. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest margins or streamside habitats.

Spiraea mairei is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting native wildlife.
