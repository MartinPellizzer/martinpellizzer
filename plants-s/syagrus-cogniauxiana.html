Syagrus cogniauxiana

Syagrus cogniauxiana, commonly known as the royal palm, is a striking tropical plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that give it a majestic appearance. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is well adapted to saline and humid environments. Its flowers are small and yellow, appearing in clusters at the base of the leaves, followed by bright orange fruits.

Syagrus cogniauxiana is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic value and resilience.
