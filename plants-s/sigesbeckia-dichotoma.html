Sigesbeckia dichotoma

Sigesbeckia dichotoma, commonly known as the two-branched herb, is a flowering plant native to Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive branched stem and small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist environments and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

Sigesbeckia dichotoma is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
