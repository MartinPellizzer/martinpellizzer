Sobralia candida

Sobralia candida, commonly known as the white sobralia, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant produces showy, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Sobralia candida is often cultivated for its ornamental value in indoor gardens and greenhouses.
