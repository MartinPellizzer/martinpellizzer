Solenocentrum asplundii

Solenocentrum asplundii, commonly known as the Asplund's spider plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is a compact, evergreen shrub characterized by its dense clusters of narrow, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are typically borne in clusters at the base of the leaves. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, well-drained soils and partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
