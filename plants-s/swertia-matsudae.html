Swertia matsudae

Swertia matsudae, commonly known as Japanese Swertia, is a herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its slender stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Swertia matsudae is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
