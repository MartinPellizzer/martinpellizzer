Salacia micrantha var. lancifolia

Salacia micrantha var. lancifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved salacia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Salicaceae.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including India and Indonesia, and thrives in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that appear in clusters. It is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and along riverbanks.

The plant has been studied for its potential medicinal properties due to the presence of various bioactive compounds.
