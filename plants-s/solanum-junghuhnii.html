Solanum junghuhnii

Solanum junghuhnii, commonly known as the Junghuhn's nightshade, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by berries, which are often red when mature. Solanum junghuhnii is part of the nightshade family (Solanaceae), which includes other well-known plants such as tomatoes and potatoes.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and fruit.
