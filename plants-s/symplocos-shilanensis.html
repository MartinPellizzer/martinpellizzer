Symplocos shilanensis

Symplocos shilanensis, commonly known as the Shilan evergreen holly, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by bright red berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural use in shaded gardens.
