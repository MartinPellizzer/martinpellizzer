Stegosia cochinchinensis var. robusta

Stegosia cochinchinensis var. robusta, commonly known as the robust stegosia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or red in color. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections.
