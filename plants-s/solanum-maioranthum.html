Solanum maioranthum

Solanum maioranthum, commonly known as the large-flowered nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered in a fine layer of hair.

Solanum maioranthum is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking floral display.
