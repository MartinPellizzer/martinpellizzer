Senecio aconitifolius

Senecio aconitifolius, commonly known as the death's-head hawkmoth plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features striking, dark green, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a deep, almost black hue. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a distinctive addition to succulent collections.
