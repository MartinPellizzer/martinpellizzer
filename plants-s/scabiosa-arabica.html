Scabiosa arabica

Scabiosa arabica, commonly known as the Persian mullein, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright stems and clusters of small, pink or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has palmate leaves that are lobed and covered in fine hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Scabiosa arabica is also valued for its ornamental beauty and historical use in herbal medicine.
