Stenorrhynchos macranthum f. multiflorum

Stenorrhynchos macranthum f. multiflorum, commonly known as the multifloral stenorrhynchos, is a variant of the stenorrhynchos genus, which belongs to the orchid family.

It is characterized by its clustered growth habit and the production of multiple flowers on each inflorescence, distinguishing it from other forms of the species. The plant features elongated, strap-like leaves and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

This form is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique floral display.
