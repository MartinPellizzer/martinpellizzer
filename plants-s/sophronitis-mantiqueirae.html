Sophronitis mantiqueirae

Sophronitis mantiqueirae, commonly known as the Mantiqueira orchid, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments within the Atlantic Forest biome. Its flowers are usually pale pink or white, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insect species.

Sophronitis mantiqueirae is a rare and endangered species, highlighting the importance of conservation efforts in its native habitat.
