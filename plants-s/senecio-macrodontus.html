Senecio macrodontus

Senecio macrodontus, commonly known as the giant's beard or giant senecio, is a striking member of the Asteraceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its large, fleshy leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant is often found in rocky or sandy soils and can grow up to several meters in height. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its ornamental value, it is also cultivated in various parts of the world.
