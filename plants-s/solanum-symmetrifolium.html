Solanum symmetrifolium

Solanum symmetrifolium, commonly known as the symmetrical leaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, symmetrical leaf structure, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, violet-colored flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruits are typically berries that change color as they mature.

Solanum symmetrifolium is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and aesthetic appeal.
