Silene boryi var. sefidiana

Silene boryi var. sefidiana, commonly known as the Persian catchfly, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

Silene boryi var. sefidiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
