Stranvaesia calleryana

Stranvaesia calleryana, commonly known as the Chinese fringe tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its striking panicle inflorescences that display a gradient of colors from white to pink to red. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a serrated margin and a rough texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Stranvaesia calleryana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
