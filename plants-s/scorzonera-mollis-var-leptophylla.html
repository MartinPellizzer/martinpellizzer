Scorzonera mollis var. leptophylla

Scorzonera mollis var. leptophylla, commonly known as the slender-leaf scorzonera, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features a robust taproot that is often harvested for its edible tuberous roots. The plant has basal leaves that are narrow and elongated, with a hairy surface. Its flowers are yellow and daisy-like, typically appearing in late summer.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats.
