Sannantha angusta

Sannantha angusta, commonly known as the narrowleaf sannantha, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is found in regions with a Mediterranean climate. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, white or pale blue flowers. It is often used in rock gardens due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.

Sannantha angusta is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
