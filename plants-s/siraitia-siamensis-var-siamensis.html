Siraitia siamensis var. siamensis

Siraitia siamensis var. siamensis, commonly known as the giant giant panda fruit or giant panda fruit, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It produces large, yellow-orange berries that are edible and have a sweet, tangy flavor. The plant has thick, waxy leaves and can grow up to several meters in length. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.

The fruit is prized for its unique taste and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
