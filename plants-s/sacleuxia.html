Sacleuxia

Sacleuxia, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a striking aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom on long, erect stalks above the water's surface. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that provide a lush green backdrop to its vibrant blossoms. Sacleuxia is often associated with purity and spiritual significance in various cultures.

Its botanical features make it a popular subject in horticulture and symbolic art.
