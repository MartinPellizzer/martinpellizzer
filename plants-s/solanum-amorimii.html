Solanum amorimii

Solanum amorimii, commonly known as the Amorimii nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale purple. Its berries are toxic and should not be consumed by humans or animals.

It is often found in shaded, humid environments within its native habitat.
