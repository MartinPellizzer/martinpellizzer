Swertia javanica subsp. confusa

Swertia javanica subsp.

confusa, commonly known as the mountain swertia, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia. It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves.

The plant produces small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and rocky slopes.

Its medicinal properties have been traditionally used in local herbal remedies.
