Scolymocephalus tenax

Scolymocephalus tenax, commonly known as the rigid scolymus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branching stems that are typically covered in fine, hairy hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in dense, compact clusters known as capitula. Its leaves are narrow, linear, and often have a slightly rough texture.

Scolymocephalus tenax is adapted to dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils.
