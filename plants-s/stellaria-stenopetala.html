Stellaria stenopetala

Stellaria stenopetala, commonly known as the narrow-petaled starwort, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, star-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pink. The plant has slender, branching stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

Stellaria stenopetala is a perennial herb that blooms from late spring to early summer.
