Stellaria alsine subsp. alsine

Stellaria alsine subsp.

alsine, commonly known as mouse-ear chickweed, is a low-growing perennial herb native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia. It typically features prostrate or ascending stems covered in fine, soft hairs.

The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom from spring to early summer. Its leaves are usually oval or rounded, with a hairy surface and a slightly succulent texture. Stellaria alsine subsp.

alsine thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodland floors and garden beds.
