Schedonnardus paniculatus

Schedonnardus paniculatus, commonly known as the clustered panicled orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant features erect, hairy stems that bear clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid forests.
