Syzygium phengklaii

Syzygium phengklaii, commonly known as the Phengklai apple, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to Thailand and is characterized by its small, red, apple-like fruits that are edible. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are elliptical, glossy, and dark green, while its flowers are white and fragrant.

Syzygium phengklaii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
