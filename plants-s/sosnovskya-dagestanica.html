Sosnovskya dagestanica

Sosnovskya dagestanica, commonly known as the Dagestani bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Caucasus region.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to violet, typically blooming in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or mountainous habitats.

Sosnovskya dagestanica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
