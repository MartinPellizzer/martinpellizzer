Stelis minimiflora

Stelis minimiflora, commonly known as the miniature flower orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. The plant features slender, wiry stems and produces tiny, delicate flowers in shades of white and pale pink. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that help it conserve moisture.

Stelis minimiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
