Statice thymoides

Statice thymoides, commonly known as sea lavender, is a flowering plant native to coastal areas of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its dense, spikes of pink or purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, gray-green leaves that help reduce water loss in saline environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dunes and salt marshes.

Statice thymoides is valued for its ornamental use in gardens and its ability to stabilize coastal soils.
