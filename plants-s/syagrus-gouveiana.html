Syagrus gouveiana

Syagrus gouveiana, commonly known as the Gouveia palm, is a species of palm native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils and salt spray. It produces small, yellowish flowers followed by round, orange-colored fruits.

Syagrus gouveiana is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
