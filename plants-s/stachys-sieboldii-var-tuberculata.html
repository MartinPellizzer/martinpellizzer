Stachys sieboldii var. tuberculata

Stachys sieboldii var. tuberculata, commonly known as the tuberculate betony, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features square, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with a rough, textured surface. The plant produces spikes of purple or pink flowers in the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
