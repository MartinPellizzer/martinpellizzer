Smilax arisanensis

Smilax arisanensis, commonly known as the Japanese devil's backbone, is a climbing shrub native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forests and along stream banks.

Smilax arisanensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
