Syzygium splendens

Syzygium splendens, commonly known as the splendid apple or golden apple, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and showy, fragrant white flowers. The tree produces small, round, yellow-orange fruits that are edible and have a sweet, acidic flavor. Syzygium splendens thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fruit.

It is also valued in traditional medicine for its various health benefits.
