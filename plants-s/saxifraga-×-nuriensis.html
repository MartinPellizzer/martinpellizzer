Saxifraga × nuriensis

Saxifraga × nuriensis, commonly known as the Korean saxifrage, is a hybrid plant native to Korea.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant features rosettes of elliptical, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It produces small, white or pinkish flowers in late spring to early summer.

Saxifraga × nuriensis is often found in rocky or shaded environments, thriving in moist, well-drained soils.
