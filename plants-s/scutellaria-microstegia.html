Scutellaria microstegia

Scutellaria microstegia, commonly known as the small-stemmed skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, tubular flowers that are typically blue to purple in color. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Scutellaria microstegia is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
