Scutellaria grandiflora subsp. grandiflora

Scutellaria grandiflora subsp.

grandiflora, commonly known as the largeflower skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and aromatic leaves.

The plant produces showy blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It typically grows in moist, wooded areas and thrives in partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for native plant gardens.
