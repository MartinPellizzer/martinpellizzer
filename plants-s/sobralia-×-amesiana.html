Sobralia × amesiana

Sobralia × amesiana, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Sobralia grandiflora and Sobralia macrantha.

It is a compact evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, with a distinctive spurred lip. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil and bright, indirect light.

Sobralia × amesiana is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
