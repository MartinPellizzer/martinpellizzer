Sedum dasyphyllum var. dyris

Sedum dasyphyllum var. dyris, commonly known as the Dyris stonecrop, is a low-growing succulent native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for gardeners in arid climates.
