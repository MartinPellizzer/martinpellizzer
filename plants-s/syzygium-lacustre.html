Syzygium lacustre

Syzygium lacustre, commonly known as the lake syzygium, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically red or yellow when mature. Syzygium lacustre thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found near water bodies.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and has traditional uses in local medicine.
