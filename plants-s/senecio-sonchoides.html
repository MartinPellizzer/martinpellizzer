Senecio sonchoides

Senecio sonchoides, commonly known as the yellow oxeye daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features bright yellow flowers with a central disk surrounded by ray florets, resembling a daisy. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Senecio sonchoides is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in various herbal remedies.
