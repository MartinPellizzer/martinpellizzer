Selinum proliferum

Selinum proliferum, commonly known as the Mexican parsley or desert carrot, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Mexico and the southwestern United States.

Its fragrance and ornamental appeal make it a popular plant in both gardens and landscapes.
