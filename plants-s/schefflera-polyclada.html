Schefflera polyclada

Schefflera polyclada, commonly known as the umbrella tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of large, leathery leaves. The plant has a woody stem that can grow quite tall, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor cultivation. Its foliage is typically dark green, with a glossy texture that adds visual appeal.

Schefflera polyclada thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to maintain its health.
