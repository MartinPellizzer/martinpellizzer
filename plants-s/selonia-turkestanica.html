Selonia turkestanica

Selonia turkestanica, commonly known as the Turkistan selonia, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia.

It typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers during the summer months. It is often found in rocky or sandy habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments.

Selonia turkestanica is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
