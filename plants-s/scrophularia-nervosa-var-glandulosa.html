Scrophularia nervosa var. glandulosa

Scrophularia nervosa var. glandulosa, commonly known as the glandular scrophularia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with prominent veins and glandular dots on the underside. Its showy, tubular flowers are usually pink or purple, with five lobes and a distinctive hooded structure.

It is often found in moist soils and can thrive in partial to full shade.
