Senecio congestus subsp. tonsus

Senecio congestus subsp.

tonsus, commonly known as the dense senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. It features upright stems with dense clusters of small, yellowish to brownish flowers.

Its foliage is often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its drought-resistant adaptations.
