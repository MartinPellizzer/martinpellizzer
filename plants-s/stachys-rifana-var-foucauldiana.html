Stachys rifana var. foucauldiana

Stachys rifana var. foucauldiana, commonly known as the narrow-leaved betony, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces spikes of violet-blue flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
