Salicornia prostrata

Salicornia prostrata, commonly known as sea beans or glassworts, is a halophyte plant that thrives in coastal environments.

It is characterized by its prostrate growth habit and succulent, salt-tolerant stems and leaves. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. It plays an important role in stabilizing coastal soils and providing habitat for various marine and terrestrial organisms.

Salicornia prostrata is widely distributed along shores and salt marshes in temperate regions.
