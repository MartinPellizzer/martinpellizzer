Sisymbrium arundanum

Sisymbrium arundanum, commonly known as field winter cress, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is an annual herb that typically grows in disturbed soils, such as roadsides and fields. The plant features slender, branching stems and small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The plant produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
