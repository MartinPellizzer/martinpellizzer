Stapelia villetiae

Stapelia villetiae, commonly known as the Cape Town Stapelia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems that store water. The plant produces striking, lantern-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. These flowers are adapted for pollination by birds rather than insects.

Stapelia villetiae is a low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.
