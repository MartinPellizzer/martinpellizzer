Stelis silverstonei

Stelis silverstonei, commonly known as the Silverstone orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

Stelis silverstonei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
