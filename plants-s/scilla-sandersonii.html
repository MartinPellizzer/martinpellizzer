Scilla sandersonii

Scilla sandersonii, commonly known as the Sanderson's scilla, is a perennial bulbous plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and clusters of blue to violet flowers. Scilla sandersonii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native plant landscapes.
