Sedum pulchrum

Sedum pulchrum, commonly known as the beautiful stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features fleshy, upright stems and clusters of star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is adapted to rocky and arid environments, with thick leaves that store water. It blooms during the summer months, adding a touch of color to rocky gardens.

Sedum pulchrum is easy to care for and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
