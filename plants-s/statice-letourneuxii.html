Statice letourneuxii

Statice letourneuxii, commonly known as the pink statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and showy, spike-like clusters of small, pink or purple flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens. It is often used in floral arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms and delicate appearance.

Statice letourneuxii is also valued for its ability to tolerate salty air and sandy soils, which contributes to its ecological significance in maritime environments.
