Sideritis nusairiensis

Sideritis nusairiensis, commonly known as the Nusairi Sideritis, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Eastern Mediterranean.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in rocky and well-drained soils, often growing in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
