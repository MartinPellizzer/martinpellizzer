Schizaea intermedia

Schizaea intermedia, commonly known as the intermediate clubmoss, is a perennial evergreen plant belonging to the family Microsoridae.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems covered in small, scale-like leaves. The plant produces spore-bearing structures called sporangia, which are clustered in cone-like formations called strobili. Its unique morphology and reproductive structures make it an important component of certain ecosystems.
