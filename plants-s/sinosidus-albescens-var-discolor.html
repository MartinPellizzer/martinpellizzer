Sinosidus albescens var. discolor

Sinosidus albescens var. discolor, commonly known as the white-flowered sinosidus, is a rare and striking plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, pale white petals that contrast with its deep green foliage. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are small but numerous, arranged in clusters that add to its ornamental value.
