Sassea columnaris

Sassea columnaris, commonly known as the columnar sassea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a tall, slender stem that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height, often with a woody base. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters at the top of the stem. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a rough, hairy texture.

Sassea columnaris is adapted to dry, rocky environments and is often found in coastal areas.
