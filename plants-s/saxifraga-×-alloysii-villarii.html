Saxifraga × alloysii-villarii

Saxifraga × alloysii-villarii, commonly known as the hybrid saxifrage, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
