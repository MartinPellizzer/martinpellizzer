Spiranthes preslii

Spiranthes preslii, commonly known as the Preslia's spiral orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its delicate, spiraling arrangement of flowers, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and swampy areas. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and the flowers are usually pale green to white in color.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its unique floral structure and ecological significance.
