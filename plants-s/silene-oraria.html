Silene oraria

Silene oraria, commonly known as the scarlet pimpernel, is a flowering plant native to Europe, North Africa, and parts of Asia.

It is a herbaceous annual with slender stems and small, showy flowers that are typically pink or red. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

Silene oraria is known for its rapid growth and short life cycle, completing its development within a single growing season.
