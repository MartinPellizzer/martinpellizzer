Stelis atroviolacea

Stelis atroviolacea, commonly known as the dark violet stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and produces clusters of dark violet to purple flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are narrow and strap-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

It is valued in horticulture for its striking floral display and relatively easy care requirements.
