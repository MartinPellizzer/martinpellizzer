Solanum commersonii

Solanum commersonii, commonly known as the Commerson's nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades, tomatoes, and peppers. The plant features small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale purple, and it produces berries that change from green to red as they mature. It is often found in coastal areas and can thrive in sandy or rocky soils.

The plant is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique appearance and hardy nature.
