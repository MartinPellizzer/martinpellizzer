Saxifraga bronchialis var. yuparensis

Saxifraga bronchialis var. yuparensis, commonly known as the Yuparensis saxifrage, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often pink or white flowers. The plant typically thrives in rocky or alpine environments, where it forms dense mats or small clusters. Its leaves are usually succulent, adapted to retain moisture in arid conditions.

This variety is of interest to botanists due to its unique adaptations and limited distribution.
