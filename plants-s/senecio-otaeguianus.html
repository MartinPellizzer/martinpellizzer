Senecio otaeguianus

Senecio otaeguianus, commonly known as the Otago senecio, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. This species typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. The plant features yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting various pollinators.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
