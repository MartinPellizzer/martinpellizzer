Styrax japonicus var. kotoensis

Styrax japonicus var. kotoensis, commonly known as the Koto styrax, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has elliptical, dark green leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It produces small, hard, reddish-brown fruits that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional Japanese gardens.
