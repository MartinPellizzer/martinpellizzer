Solidago rigidiuscula

Solidago rigidiuscula, commonly known as stiffstem goldenrod, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes and stream banks. It features narrow, rigid stems and small, yellow to orange composite flowers that bloom in late summer.

Solidago rigidiuscula plays an important role in supporting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
