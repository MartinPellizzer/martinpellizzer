Sabicea urniformis

Sabicea urniformis, commonly known as the urn-shaped sabicea, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, urn-shaped pods. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments within lowland forests. Its leaves are compound, with oval-shaped leaflets, and it produces small, fragrant flowers.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
