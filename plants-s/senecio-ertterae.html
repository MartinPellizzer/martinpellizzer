Senecio ertterae

Senecio ertterae, commonly known as the Ertter's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It has small, narrow leaves and produces achenes as its seed type.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species.
