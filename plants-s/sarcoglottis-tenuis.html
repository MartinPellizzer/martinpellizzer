Sarcoglottis tenuis

Sarcoglottis tenuis, commonly known as the slender sarcoglottis, is a climbing vine belonging to the family Aristolochiaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, twining stems and heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with a distinctive, intricate pattern. Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and traditional herbal practices.
