Solanum ratum

Solanum ratum, commonly known as the small nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features small, green leaves with a hairy texture and produces clusters of small, white or yellowish flowers. Its fruit is a smooth, spherical berry that changes color from green to red as it matures.

Solanum ratum is known for its toxic properties, containing various alkaloids that can be harmful to humans and animals.
