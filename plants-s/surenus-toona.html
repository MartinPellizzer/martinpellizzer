Surenus toona

Surenus toona, commonly known as the Indian toona, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes species known for their aromatic wood and medicinal properties. The tree is valued for its hard, durable wood and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
