Schefflera rubriflora

Schefflera rubriflora, commonly known as the red-flowered schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of leaflets, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, with some varieties exhibiting a reddish tinge. Its foliage is typically dark green and glossy, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Schefflera rubriflora thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
