Stillingia gymnogyna

Stillingia gymnogyna, commonly known as the Chinese stellaria, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its leaves are alternate and have a distinctive, slightly serrated edge.
