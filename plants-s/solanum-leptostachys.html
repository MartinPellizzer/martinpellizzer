Solanum leptostachys

Solanum leptostachys, commonly known as the slender-stalked nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant features slender stems and small, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its flowers are typically white or pale purple, with five petals and a distinctive calyx.

Solanum leptostachys produces small, yellow berries that contain toxic seeds, making it important to handle with care.
