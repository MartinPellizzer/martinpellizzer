Scutellaria laeteviolacea var. kurokawae

Scutellaria laeteviolacea var. kurokawae, commonly known as the Kurokawa's blue skullcap, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its small, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
