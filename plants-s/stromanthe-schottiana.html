Stromanthe schottiana

Stromanthe schottiana, commonly known as the silver star plant, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that display shades of green, silver, and white, often with a star-like pattern. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil. It is a member of the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants such as philodendrons and peace lilies.

Stromanthe schottiana is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
