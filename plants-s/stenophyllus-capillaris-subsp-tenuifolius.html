Stenophyllus capillaris subsp. tenuifolius

Stenophyllus capillaris subsp.

tenuifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved stenophyllus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to specific regions of southern Africa. It is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences bearing tiny white or pale blue flowers. It typically grows in well-drained soils in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops.

Due to its limited distribution and specific ecological requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
