Senecio albirameus

Senecio albirameus, commonly known as the white-rayed senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its flowers consist of a central disk surrounded by white ray florets, giving it a delicate and airy appearance.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
