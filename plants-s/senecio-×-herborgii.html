Senecio × herborgii

Senecio × herborgii, commonly known as the Herborg's groundsel, is a hybrid plant species within the Asteraceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features narrow, gray-green leaves with a woolly texture and produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and drought tolerance.

Senecio × herborgii thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
