Saussurea saxatilis

Saussurea saxatilis, commonly known as the rock saussurea, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and bract-like leaves. Its yellowish to brownish flowers bloom in late summer, and the plant is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats.

Saussurea saxatilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
