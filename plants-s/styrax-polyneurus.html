Styrax polyneurus

Styrax polyneurus, commonly known as the Chinese frankincense tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces a resinous sap, which has been historically used in traditional medicine and incense. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a smooth texture.

The tree typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 meters and thrives in well-drained, acidic soils.
