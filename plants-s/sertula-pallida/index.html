Sertula Pallida

Pale sertula, scientifically known as Sertula pallida, is a rare and delicate species of sea anemone found in coastal waters. It is characterized by its translucent, pale coloration and slender, elongated body. This marine organism thrives in shallow, warm waters where it attaches itself to rocky substrates. Due to its fragile nature, it is highly sensitive to environmental changes and pollution. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this species from habitat degradation and overcollection.
