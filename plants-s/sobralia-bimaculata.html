Sobralia bimaculata

Sobralia bimaculata, commonly known as the spotted sobralia, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange and yellow flowers, which are adorned with distinctive dark spots. The plant has a compact, pseudobulbous structure with long, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Sobralia bimaculata is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique floral display.
