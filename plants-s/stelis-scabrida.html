Stelis scabrida

Stelis scabrida, commonly known as the rough stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. The plant features a short, branching stem with lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a rough, scaly texture. Its flowers are small and clustered, often in shades of white or pale yellow.

Stelis scabrida is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and ability to thrive in specific, well-drained growing conditions.
