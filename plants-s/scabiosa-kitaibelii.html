Scabiosa kitaibelii

Scabiosa kitaibelii, commonly known as the Italian sainfoin, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of pink or white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves and is often grown as an ornamental in gardens for its delicate appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Scabiosa kitaibelii is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
