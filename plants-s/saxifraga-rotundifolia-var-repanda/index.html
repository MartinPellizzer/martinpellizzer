Saxifraga Rotundifolia Var. Repanda

Alpine saxifrage, scientifically known as Saxifraga rotundifolia var. repanda, is a hardy flowering plant that thrives in the cold, rocky environments of high altitudes. This resilient species is commonly found in alpine tundra and mountainous regions across the Northern Hemisphere. Its compact growth habit and vibrant pink or white flowers make it a striking addition to its harsh habitat. The plant's ability to survive in extreme conditions highlights its adaptability and ecological significance. With its unique appearance and ecological role, the Alpine saxifrage is both a fascinating subject of study and a symbol of nature's resilience.
