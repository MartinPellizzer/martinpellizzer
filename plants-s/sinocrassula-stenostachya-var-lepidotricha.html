Sinocrassula stenostachya var. lepidotricha

Sinocrassula stenostachya var. lepidotricha, commonly known as the silver moss rose, is a succulent plant native to China.

It features dense, compact growth with fleshy, bluish-green leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ease of care in rock gardens or as a houseplant.
