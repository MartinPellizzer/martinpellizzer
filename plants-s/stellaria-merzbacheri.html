Stellaria merzbacheri

Stellaria merzbacheri, commonly known as the Merzbacher's starwort, is a low-growing annual herb native to arid regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its small, star-shaped white flowers and narrow, linear leaves. The plant typically thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions. Its flowering period occurs during the summer months, and it plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native habitat.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
