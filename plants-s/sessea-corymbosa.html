Sessea corymbosa

Sessea corymbosa, commonly known as the tropical milk tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds.

It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in lowland forests.
