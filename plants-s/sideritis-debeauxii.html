Sideritis debeauxii

Sideritis debeauxii, commonly known as the Balkan ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in fine hairs.

Sideritis debeauxii is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
