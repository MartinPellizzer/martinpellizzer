Scleria gonocarpa

Scleria gonocarpa, commonly known as the African umbrella plant, is a herbaceous perennial belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces distinctive, umbrella-shaped inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is native to southern Africa. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, with three stamens and a single pistil.

Scleria gonocarpa is often used in ornamental gardening for its unique foliage and ability to thrive in humid environments.
