Scrophularia laevis

Scrophularia laevis, commonly known as smooth scrophularia, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Scrophulariaceae family, which includes many species with showy flowers. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine, smooth texture, while its flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom in the summer.

Scrophularia laevis is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
