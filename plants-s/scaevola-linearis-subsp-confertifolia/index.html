Scaevola Linearis Subsp. Confertifolia

Scaevola linearis subsp. confertifolia is a unique and striking member of the Goodeniaceae family known for its vibrant blue and purple flowers. This subspecies is native to coastal regions of eastern Australia, where it thrives in sandy soils and salty air. Its compact growth habit and showy blooms make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking drought-tolerant ornamental plants. The plant's ability to withstand harsh coastal conditions highlights its adaptability and resilience. With its delicate foliage and striking coloration, it adds both beauty and ecological value to its environment.
