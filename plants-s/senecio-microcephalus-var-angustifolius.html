Senecio microcephalus var. angustifolius

Senecio microcephalus var. angustifolius, commonly known as narrow-leaf Senecio, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. It is adapted to arid environments and thrives in well-drained soils.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
