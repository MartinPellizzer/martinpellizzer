Swida catalinensis

Swida catalinensis, commonly known as the Catalinensis Swida, is a rare shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces white to pale pink flowers in clusters, which bloom in spring. Its fruits are small, dry capsules that contain several seeds.

Swida catalinensis is adapted to the arid and coastal conditions of its native habitat.
