Sideritis Hyssopifolia Var. Pyrenaica

Sideritis hyssopifolia var. pyrenaica is a rare and delicate plant native to the mountainous regions of the Iberian Peninsula. Known for its aromatic leaves and small, pale flowers, it thrives in rocky and limestone soils. This variety of Sideritis is often found in high altitude environments where it adapts to harsh climatic conditions. Its medicinal properties have been recognized in traditional herbal practices for centuries. Due to habitat loss and climate change, it is now considered a vulnerable species requiring conservation efforts.
