Stipa speciosa f. abscondita

Stipa speciosa f. abscondita, commonly known as the hidden beauty grass, is a rare and ornamental grass species belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-blue foliage and compact growth habit, making it visually striking in gardens. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a sought-after specimen for botanical enthusiasts.

Due to its rarity, it is often cultivated in specialized horticultural settings.
