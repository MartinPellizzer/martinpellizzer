Silene piliancyrana

Silene piliancyrana, commonly known as the Altai catchfly, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant produces small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, adapted to reduce water loss in arid conditions.
