Senecio exsertiflorus

Senecio exsertiflorus, commonly known as the erect-flowered senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the sunflower family, Asteraceae, and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow-orange composite flowers. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It typically thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is sometimes cultivated in horticulture.
