Scorzonera tingitana

Scorzonera tingitana, commonly known as the Moroccan scorzonera, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a robust, tuberous root system that is often used in traditional medicine. The plant has basal leaves that are typically lobed and hairy, with a height ranging from 30 to 100 centimeters. Its flowers are yellow and daisy-like, with a central disc and ray florets.

Scorzonera tingitana is adapted to dry and rocky soils, making it a resilient plant in arid environments.
