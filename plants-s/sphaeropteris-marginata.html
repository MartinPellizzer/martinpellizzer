Sphaeropteris marginata

Sphaeropteris marginata, commonly known as the marginated cycad, is a member of the cycad family, Cycadaceae.

It is a tropical plant characterized by its stout, unbranched trunk and large, pinnately compound leaves. The leaves are typically bright green and have a distinctive margin that is often wavy or undulating. This species is native to Southeast Asia and is adapted to humid, tropical climates.

It plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various organisms.
