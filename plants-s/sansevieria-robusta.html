Sansevieria robusta

Sansevieria robusta, commonly known as the Robusta Snake Plant, is a species of flowering plant in the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to tropical Africa and is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with pale yellow or white margins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It is highly resilient, capable of thriving in low light and requiring minimal water, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ability to purify the air and its low maintenance requirements contribute to its widespread cultivation.
