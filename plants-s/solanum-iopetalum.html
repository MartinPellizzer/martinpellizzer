Solanum iopetalum

Solanum iopetalum, commonly known as the Christmas bell, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades, and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, with hairy stems and oval, pointed leaves. Its flowers bloom in winter, making it a popular choice for gardens in regions with mild winters.

The fruit is a small, yellow berry that contains seeds, and the plant is known for its ornamental value.
