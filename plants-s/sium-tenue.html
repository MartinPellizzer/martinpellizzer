Sium tenue

Sium tenue, commonly known as small water parsley, is a herbaceous plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and small, white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as stream banks and wetlands. Its roots are sometimes used in traditional medicine for their purported diuretic properties.

Sium tenue plays a role in supporting local ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various insects and wildlife.
