Scrophularia clausii

Scrophularia clausii, commonly known as the narrow-leaved figwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid environments. The plant features slender, upright stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are tubular and usually violet or purple in color, blooming in late spring to early summer.

Scrophularia clausii is part of the Plantaginaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
