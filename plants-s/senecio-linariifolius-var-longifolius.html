Senecio linariifolius var. longifolius

Senecio linariifolius var. longifolius, commonly known as the long-leaved groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellow flowers in clusters. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats. It is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

This variant is distinguished by its longer leaves compared to other varieties of Senecio linariifolius.
