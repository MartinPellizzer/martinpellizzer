Solanecio tuberosus var. pubescens

Solanecio tuberosus var. pubescens, commonly known as the hairy tuberous solanecio, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it its varietal name "pubescens.". The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its striking appearance.

The species belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
