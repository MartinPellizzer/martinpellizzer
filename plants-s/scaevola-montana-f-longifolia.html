Scaevola montana f. longifolia

Scaevola montana f. longifolia, commonly known as the mountain scaevola, is a compact, mat-forming perennial shrub native to Australia.

It features small, bright blue or violet flowers that are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant has silver-gray foliage with hairy leaves that help reduce water loss in arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
