Streptocarpus ionanthus subsp. rupicola

Streptocarpus ionanthus subsp.

rupicola, commonly known as the rock streptocarpus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa. It features succulent, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss in its arid environment.

The plant produces showy, violet-blue flowers that are typically clustered in racemes, adding ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

This subspecies is valued for its resilience and aesthetic appeal in rock gardens and xeriscaping.
