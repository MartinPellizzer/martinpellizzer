Senecio gynuropsis

Senecio gynuropsis, commonly known as the African snowflake, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant features silver-gray, woolly foliage that helps it retain moisture in arid environments. It produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer.

Senecio gynuropsis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and hardy nature.
