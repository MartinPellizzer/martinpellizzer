Schlechteria capensis

Schlechteria capensis, commonly known as the Cape bellflower, is a strikingly beautiful plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to violet. Schlechteria capensis thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
