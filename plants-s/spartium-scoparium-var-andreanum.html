Spartium scoparium var. andreanum

Spartium scoparium var. andreanum, commonly known as the Andreean spartium, is a variant of the spartium plant native to certain regions of the Mediterranean.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its small, needle-like leaves and clusters of pink to purple flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or arid environments. Its botanical features include a woody stem and small, serrated leaves that contribute to its drought-resistant nature.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
