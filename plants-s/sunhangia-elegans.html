Sunhangia elegans

Sunhangia elegans, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage and delicate, fragrant blooms that typically appear in shades of white and pale pink. The plant thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and indoor cultivation.

Sunhangia elegans is also valued for its ability to purify the air and enhance the aesthetic appeal of any garden space.
