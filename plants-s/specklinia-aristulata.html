Specklinia aristulata

Specklinia aristulata, commonly known as the noble orchid, is a species native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is a small epiphytic orchid, typically growing on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features dark green, strap-like leaves and produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive, elongated spur. It thrives in high humidity and requires consistent moisture and indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in greenhouses or terrariums.
