Spielmannia jasminum var. nitida

Spielmannia jasminum var. nitida, commonly known as the silverleaf jasmine, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a hedge plant.
