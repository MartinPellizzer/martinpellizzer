Salacia kanukuensis

Salacia kanukuensis, commonly known as the Kanuku salacia, is a species of flowering plant native to the rainforests of Central Africa.

It belongs to the family Salicaceae, which includes willows and poplars, though it is more closely related to other members of the genus Salacia. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a velvety texture.

Salacia kanukuensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
