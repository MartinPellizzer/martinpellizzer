Sophia brevipes

Sophia brevipes, commonly known as the shortfoot sophia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and has narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
