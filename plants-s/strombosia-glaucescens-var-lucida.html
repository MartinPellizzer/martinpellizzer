Strombosia glaucescens var. lucida

Strombosia glaucescens var. lucida, commonly known as the shining strombosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year, attracting various pollinators. Its fruit is a capsule containing several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local communities.
