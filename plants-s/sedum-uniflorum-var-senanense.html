Sedum uniflorum var. senanense

Sedum uniflorum var. senanense, commonly known as the Senanese stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow to orange. The plant has thick, water-storing leaves that help it survive in arid conditions. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a hardy choice for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant blooms make it an attractive addition to gardens with low maintenance requirements.
