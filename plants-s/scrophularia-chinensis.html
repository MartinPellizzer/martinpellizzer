Scrophularia chinensis

Scrophularia chinensis, commonly known as Chinese figwort, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or rocky areas.

Scrophularia chinensis has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
