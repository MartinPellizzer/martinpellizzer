Sedum telephium var. verlotii

Sedum telephium var. verlotii, commonly known as the red sedum, is a succulent plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features fleshy, upright stems and leaves that are typically blue-green to red in color, depending on light exposure. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink to red, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

This variety is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
