Stromanthe lubbersiana

Stromanthe lubbersiana, commonly known as the variegated prayer plant, is a tropical foliage plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaf patterns that often feature shades of green, white, and silver. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette formation. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.

Stromanthe lubbersiana is prized for its ornamental value and is popular among houseplant enthusiasts.
