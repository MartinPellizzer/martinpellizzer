Schismatoglottis acuminatissima

Schismatoglottis acuminatissima, commonly known as the sharp-pointed schismatoglottis, is a species of flowering plant in the family Orchidaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
