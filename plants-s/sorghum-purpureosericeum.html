Sorghum purpureosericeum

Sorghum purpureosericeum, commonly known as the purple silky sorghum, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its striking purple foliage and silky, silver-haired inflorescences. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, with narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or grasslands.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
