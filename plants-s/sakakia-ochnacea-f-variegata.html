Sakakia ochnacea f. variegata

Sakakia ochnacea f. variegata, commonly known as the variegated ochna, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Ochnaceae and is characterized by its striking variegated foliage with white and green patterns. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in panicles, adding ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its unique leaf coloration makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
