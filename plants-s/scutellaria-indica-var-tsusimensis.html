Scutellaria indica var. tsusimensis

Scutellaria indica var. tsusimensis, commonly known as Japanese skullcap, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features opposite, lanceolate leaves that are often hairy and have a slightly serrated margin. The plant produces small, tubular blue or violet flowers arranged in whorled clusters along the stem. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its medicinal properties have been studied for their potential anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects.
