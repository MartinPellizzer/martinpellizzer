Selenipedium × sedenii

Selenipedium × sedenii, commonly known as the hybrid giant spider-orchid, is a rare and striking orchid species native to the tropical rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that can reach several feet in length, bearing numerous violet to purple flowers. The plant is a hybrid between Selenipedium albertii and Selenipedium petraeum, resulting in unique morphological features. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires specific soil conditions to flourish.

Due to its rarity and habitat specificity, it is highly sought after by botanists and conservationists.
