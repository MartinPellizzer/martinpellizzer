Spiraea chinensis var. angustifolia

Spiraea chinensis var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf weeping spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features graceful, arching branches that bear clusters of small, white flowers in early summer. The leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, providing an attractive foliage contrast throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
