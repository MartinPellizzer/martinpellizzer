Sparganium chlorocarpum var. acaule

Sparganium chlorocarpum var. acaule, commonly known as the slender bur-reed, is a variant of the bur-reed plant native to wetland habitats.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems that lack the short basal leaves typical of the species. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in spikelets, which are typically found in clusters along the stem. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often floating on the water surface.

This variant is adapted to aquatic environments and plays a role in wetland ecosystems by providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
