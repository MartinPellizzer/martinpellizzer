Saxifraga Bronchialis Var. Funstonii

Saxifraga bronchialis var. funstonii is a rare and striking plant native to the rocky slopes of Texas. It is known for its vibrant pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring. This variant is a subspecies of Saxifraga bronchialis, which is part of the Saxifragaceae family. Its unique coloration and habitat make it a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to survive.
