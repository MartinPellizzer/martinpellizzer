Sedum tschernokolevii

Sedum tschernokolevii, commonly known as the Tschernokolevii stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and clusters of small, star-shaped yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are thick and waxy, helping it retain moisture in dry climates.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in low-water settings.
