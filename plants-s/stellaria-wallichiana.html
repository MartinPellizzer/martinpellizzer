Stellaria wallichiana

Stellaria wallichiana, commonly known as the Himalayan starwort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, star-shaped white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving at high altitudes. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in a fine layer of hair.

Stellaria wallichiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
