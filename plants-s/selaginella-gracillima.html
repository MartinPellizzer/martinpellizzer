Selaginella gracillima

Selaginella gracillima, commonly known as the slender clubmoss, is a small, evergreen plant belonging to the family Selaginellaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, particularly India and Sri Lanka. The plant features delicate, thread-like leaves arranged in a spiral pattern around its branching stems. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Selaginella gracillima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
