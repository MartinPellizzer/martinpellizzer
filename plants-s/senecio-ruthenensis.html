Senecio ruthenensis

Senecio ruthenensis, commonly known as the Ruthenian groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is found in arid or semi-arid regions. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, and its leaves are usually narrow and hairy.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
