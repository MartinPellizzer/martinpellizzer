Senna robiniifolia

Senna robiniifolia, commonly known as the golden senna, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to regions of Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are typically bipinnate and adorned with small, bright yellow flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. Senna robiniifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.

It thrives in warm, sunny climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
