Scleria Woodii Var. Ornata

Scleria woodii var. ornata is a striking aquatic plant native to the wetlands of southern Africa. Known for its delicate, spike-like inflorescences and vibrant blue-green foliage, it adds a unique aesthetic to water gardens. This variety is prized by horticulturists for its ornamental value and ease of care in controlled environments. It thrives in shallow waters with ample sunlight and nutrient-rich substrates. Its beauty and adaptability make it a popular choice for both ornamental ponds and botanical collections.
