Spadonia polymorpha var. elaeagnifolia

Spadonia polymorpha var. elaeagnifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf spadonia, is a variant of the spadonia plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, dense hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a unique and visually striking addition to botanical gardens and natural habitats.
