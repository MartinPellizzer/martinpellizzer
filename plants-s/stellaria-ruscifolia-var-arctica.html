Stellaria ruscifolia var. arctica

Stellaria ruscifolia var. arctica, commonly known as Arctic starwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to arctic and alpine regions.

It features small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to August. The plant has a mat-forming growth habit, with slender, ascending stems covered in fine, soft hairs. Its leaves are opposite, ovate to elliptic, and often have a slightly wavy margin.

This species is adapted to cold climates and is often found in rocky or sandy soils in tundra and mountainous areas.
