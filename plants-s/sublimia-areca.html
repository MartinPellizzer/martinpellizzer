Sublimia areca

Sublimia areca, commonly known as the golden cane palm, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features tall, slender stems that are covered in a distinctive golden-green sheath, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that eventually develop into orange-red fruits. Its leaves are large, fan-shaped, and often used in traditional crafts and as decorative elements.

Sublimia areca is well-suited for indoor cultivation and thrives in warm, humid environments.
