Stelis guerrerensis

Stelis guerrerensis, commonly known as the Guerrerense orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its erect, often branched stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in the understory of tropical forests. Its flowers are usually fragrant and come in shades of white, yellow, or green.

Stelis guerrerensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
