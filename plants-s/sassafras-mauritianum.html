Sassafras mauritianum

Sassafras mauritianum, commonly known as the Mauritius sassafras, is a flowering plant native to the island of Mauritius.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments within tropical rainforests. Its bark and leaves contain essential oils that have been used in traditional medicine.

Sassafras mauritianum is also valued for its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
