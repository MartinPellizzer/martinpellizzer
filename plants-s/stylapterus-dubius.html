Stylapterus dubius

Stylapterus dubius, commonly known as the slender lichen moth, is a species of moth in the family Erebidae.

It is characterized by its small, delicate wings that are often adorned with intricate patterns of white, black, and orange. This species is typically found in forested areas where it feeds on lichens and other moist substrates. The larvae of Stylapterus dubius are cryptic, often blending with their environment to avoid predation.

Its life cycle is closely tied to the availability of its specific food sources, making it an indicator species for certain forest ecosystems.
