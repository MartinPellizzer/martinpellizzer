Stellaria platyphylla

Stellaria platyphylla, commonly known as the broad-leaved starwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features small, white flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has oval, broad leaves that are often hairy, and it thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodland floors. Stellaria platyphylla is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family, which includes other flowering plants with similar characteristics.

Its delicate appearance makes it a notable species in ecological studies and botanical gardens.
