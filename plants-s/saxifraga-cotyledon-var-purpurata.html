Saxifraga cotyledon var. purpurata

Saxifraga cotyledon var. purpurata, commonly known as the purple-leaf saxifrage, is a succulent plant native to rocky areas in Europe.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a striking purple or reddish hue on the underside. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Due to its compact size and ornamental leaves, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and containers.
