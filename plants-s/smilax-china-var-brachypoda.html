Smilax china var. brachypoda

Smilax china var. brachypoda, commonly known as Chinese smilax, is a climbing shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features a vigorous vine with sharp, spiny stems and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellowish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forests and along stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
