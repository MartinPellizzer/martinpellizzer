Scyphostigma bennetii

Scyphostigma bennetii, commonly known as the Bennett's ghost flower, is a striking member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. These bracts can range in color from deep red to orange and are used in traditional medicine by local communities.

Scyphostigma bennetii thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in rainforests.
