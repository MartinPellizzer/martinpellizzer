Snowdenia gracilis

Snowdenia gracilis, commonly known as the slender Snowden's bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Wales.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. The plant typically grows in rocky or grassy habitats at high altitudes. Snowdenia gracilis has slender stems and small, lance-shaped leaves.

It is a rare and protected species, often found in specific microhabitats within the Welsh uplands.
