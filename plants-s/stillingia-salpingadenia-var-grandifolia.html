Stillingia salpingadenia var. grandifolia

Stillingia salpingadenia var. grandifolia, commonly known as the largeflowered stellaria, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the family Stellariaceae and is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant has slender, branching stems and small, opposite leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is not widely distributed outside its native habitat.
