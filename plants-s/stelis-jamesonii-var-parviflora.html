Stelis jamesonii var. parviflora

Stelis jamesonii var. parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments within forest understories. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. Its flowers are small and usually pale green or white, with a delicate appearance.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to cultivation.
