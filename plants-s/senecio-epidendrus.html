Senecio epidendrus

Senecio epidendrus, commonly known as the Cape mountain sandthorn, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, and it is adapted to arid environments.
