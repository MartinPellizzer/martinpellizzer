Sedum annuum var. racemiferum

Sedum annuum var. racemiferum, commonly known as the Christmas Joy sedum, is a succulent plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant produces bright yellow to orange flowers, which are often followed by red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it suitable for xeriscaping and other water-efficient landscaping practices.
