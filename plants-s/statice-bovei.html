Statice bovei

Statice bovei, commonly known as the Bove's sea thrift, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its dense clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows in saline or coastal environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its narrow, grass-like leaves are often covered in a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss.

Statice bovei is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
