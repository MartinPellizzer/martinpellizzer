Sphaeradenia brachiolata

Sphaeradenia brachiolata, commonly known as the broad-leafed star sphaera, is a tropical climbing plant belonging to the Araceae family.

Sphaeradenia brachiolata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in tropical gardens.
