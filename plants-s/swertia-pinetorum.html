Swertia pinetorum

Swertia pinetorum, commonly known as the pine forest gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments within coniferous forests. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Swertia pinetorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
