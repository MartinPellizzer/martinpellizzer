Silene broussonetiana

Silene broussonetiana, commonly known as the Broussonet's campion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces clusters of pink to purple flowers, which bloom in summer. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

Silene broussonetiana is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
