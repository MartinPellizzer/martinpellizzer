Senecio orientalis

Senecio orientalis, commonly known as the Eastern Senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments. Senecio orientalis is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.
