Saxifraga lychnitis var. lychnitis

Saxifraga lychnitis var. lychnitis, commonly known as the alpine saxifrage, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure. The plant features upright stems with small, clustered leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. Its flowers are pink to reddish in color, with five petals and numerous stamens, and bloom in early summer.

Saxifraga lychnitis var. lychnitis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in alpine ecosystems.
