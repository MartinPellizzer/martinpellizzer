Sinocalamus maiensis

Sinocalamus maiensis, commonly known as the Yunnan bamboo, is a tall, slender grass-like plant native to China.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its hollow, jointed stems and narrow, linear leaves. This species is often found in mountainous regions with moist, well-drained soils. Its growth habit makes it a valuable resource for traditional uses and ecological restoration.

The plant plays an important role in maintaining soil stability and biodiversity in its native habitat.
