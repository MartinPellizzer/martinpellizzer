Spiranthes aestivalis

Spiranthes aestivalis, commonly known as the summer twayblade, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and its distinctive spiral-shaped flower clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wet meadows.
