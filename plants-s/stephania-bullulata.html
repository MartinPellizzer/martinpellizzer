Stephania bullulata

Stephania bullulata, commonly known as the bullate stephania, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Menispermaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by bright red berries. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
