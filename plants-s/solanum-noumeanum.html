Solanum noumeanum

Solanum noumeanum, commonly known as the New Caledonian nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the island of New Caledonia.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades, tomatoes, and peppers. The plant produces striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in the evening, attracting nocturnal pollinators. Its leaves are ovate and glossy, with a slightly hairy texture.

Solanum noumeanum is often found in moist, forested areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
