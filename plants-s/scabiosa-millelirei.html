Scabiosa millelirei

Scabiosa millelirei, commonly known as the Italian sownflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Europe.

It features showy, pink or white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, branched stems and lance-shaped leaves that are hairy to the touch. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its nectar-rich flowers attract bees and butterflies, contributing to pollinator biodiversity.
