Spodiopogon depauperatus var. purpurascens

Spodiopogon depauperatus var. purpurascens, commonly known as the purple flowering spikerush, is a grass-like plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and small, clustered inflorescences that often display a purplish hue. The plant typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes and along the edges of water bodies. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a slight sheen, and it produces small, wind-pollinated flowers.

This variant is notable for its ornamental value and ecological importance in maintaining wetland biodiversity.
