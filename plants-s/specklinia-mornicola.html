Specklinia mornicola

Specklinia mornicola, commonly known as the morning star orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, clustered growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink. The plant typically blooms during the early morning, giving it its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Specklinia mornicola is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in terrariums or as a houseplant.
