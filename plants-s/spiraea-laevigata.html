Spiraea laevigata

Spiraea laevigata, commonly known as the smooth spiraea, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It features arching clusters of fragrant, white to pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has slender, green stems with a smooth, almost waxy texture. Its leaves are oval-shaped, with a glossy upper surface and a lighter green underside.

Spiraea laevigata is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
