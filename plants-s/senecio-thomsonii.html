Senecio thomsonii

Senecio thomsonii, commonly known as Thomson's groundsels, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and northern Mexico.

Senecio thomsonii produces small yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
