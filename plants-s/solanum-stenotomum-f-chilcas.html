Solanum stenotomum f. chilcas

Solanum stenotomum f. chilcas, commonly known as the Chilca tomato, is a botanical variant of the species Solanum stenotomum.

It is a herbaceous plant native to South America, particularly found in regions of Peru and Bolivia. The plant produces small, yellow-orange fruits that are typically harvested when they are still immature. It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its hairy stems and lobed leaves.

This cultivar is valued for its unique flavor and is often used in local cuisines and traditional medicine.
