Scorzonera tau-saghyz subsp. usbekistanica

Scorzonera tau-saghyz subsp.

usbekistanica, commonly known as the Turkmenian scorzonera, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and lobed leaves.

The plant produces yellow flowers with ray florets, typical of the genus Scorzonera. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils.

This subspecies is of interest for its potential agricultural uses due to its edible tubers.
