Sida guichenotiana

Sida guichenotiana, commonly known as velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves covered in fine, soft hairs that give it a velvety texture. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes. It is an annual herb that thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed areas.

Sida guichenotiana is also known for its ability to spread rapidly, making it a common weed in agricultural fields.
