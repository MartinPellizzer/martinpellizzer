Spergularia Campestris Var. Longipes

Spergularia campestris var. longipes is a lesser-known flowering plant belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae. It is characterized by its slender stems and small, pinkish flowers that bloom in early summer. This variety is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats such as roadsides and fields. Despite its modest appearance, it plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and soil stability. Its unique morphology and ecological significance make it an intriguing subject for botanical study.
