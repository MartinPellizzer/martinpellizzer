Senecio pleistocephalus

Senecio pleistocephalus, commonly known as the giant senecio, is a striking member of the Asteraceae family native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, fleshy, blue-green leaves and showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant can grow up to 2 meters tall, making it one of the tallest succulents in its native habitat. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy environments.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping and botanical gardens.
