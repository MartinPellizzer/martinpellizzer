Statice dodartii var. humilis

Statice dodartii var. humilis, commonly known as the dwarf statice, is a compact, herbaceous annual plant native to southern Europe.

It features narrow, linear leaves and produces spikes of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, purple, and white. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. It is valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.

Its botanical structure supports its use in both ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
