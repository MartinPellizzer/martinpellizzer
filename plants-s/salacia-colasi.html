Salacia colasi

Salacia colasi, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Salicaceae and is characterized by its pendulous clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant is known for its rapid growth and is often cultivated as an ornamental tree due to its showy appearance. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a smooth texture, while its bark is smooth and light gray in color.

The species is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
