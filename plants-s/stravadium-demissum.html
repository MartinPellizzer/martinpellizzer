Stravadium demissum

Stravadium demissum, commonly known as the slender thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and silver-gray foliage covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Stravadium demissum is typically found in rocky soils and dry environments.

It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
