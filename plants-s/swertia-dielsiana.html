Swertia dielsiana

Swertia dielsiana, commonly known as the Himalayan gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine environments, thriving in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Swertia dielsiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
