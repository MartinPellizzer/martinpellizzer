Scindapsus pictus var. oblongifolius

Scindapsus pictus var. oblongifolius, commonly known as the Marble Queen philodendron, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with a mix of green, white, and cream patterns. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a waxy texture, enhancing its ornamental appeal. Native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia, it thrives in bright, indirect light and moderate humidity.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and ability to purify indoor air.
