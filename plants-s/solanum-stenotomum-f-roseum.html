Solanum stenotomum f. roseum

Solanum stenotomum f. roseum, commonly known as the pink-flowered nightshade, is a unique variant of the Solanum stenotomum species.

It is characterized by its striking pink to pale purple flowers, which contrast with its green foliage. This plant is a flowering shrub native to certain regions of South America, particularly in Argentina. It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae), which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers.

The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to thrive in arid environments.
