Spathulata spuria

Spathulata spuria, commonly known as the broad-leaved spathulate, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Amaranthaceae.

It is characterized by its broad, spatulate-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish or reddish in color. It is native to regions of South America and thrives in warm, sunny environments.
