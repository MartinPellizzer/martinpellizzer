Stelis Resupinata

Stelis resupinata is a unique orchid species known for its striking appearance and adaptability. Native to parts of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers often feature vibrant colors and intricate patterns, making it a favorite among orchid enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it requires specific care to flourish in cultivation. This article explores the characteristics, habitat, and cultivation tips for Stelis resupinata.
