Symplocos lutescens

Symplocos lutescens, commonly known as the yellow-leafed symplocos, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Ericaceae and is characterized by its simple, elliptical leaves that are usually yellowish-green in color. The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
