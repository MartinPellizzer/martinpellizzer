Senecio dernburgianus

Senecio dernburgianus, commonly known as the Dernburg's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals, blooming in spring and early summer. It has narrow, linear leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs.

Senecio dernburgianus is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and along coastal areas.
