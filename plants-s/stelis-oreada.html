Stelis oreada

Stelis oreada, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or mountainous habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant produces tall, slender stems with clustered flowers that are often pale green or yellowish in color. Its flowers are characterized by their intricate structure and are pollinated by specific insects.

Stelis oreada is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
