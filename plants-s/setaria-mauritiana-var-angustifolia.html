Setaria mauritiana var. angustifolia

Setaria mauritiana var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf foxtail, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, spike-like inflorescence and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions. It is often found in agricultural areas and can be invasive in some regions.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological and agricultural studies.
