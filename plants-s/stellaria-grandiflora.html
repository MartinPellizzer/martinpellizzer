Stellaria grandiflora

Stellaria grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered starwort, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like carnations and sweet William. The plant is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped white or pink flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Stellaria grandiflora typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.
