Spathandra Melastomoides

Redroot spathandra, scientifically known as Spathandra melastomoides, is a striking perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of eastern China. With its distinctive red roots and showy white flowers, it stands out in both botanical gardens and natural habitats. This species belongs to the family Melastomataceae, which includes many ornamental and ecologically significant plants. Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a subject of interest for botanists and gardeners alike. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in the broader horticultural world.
