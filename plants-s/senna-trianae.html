Senna trianae

Senna trianae, commonly known as the yellow senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant belongs to the Fabaceae family, also known as the legume or pea family. Senna trianae is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for warm climates.
