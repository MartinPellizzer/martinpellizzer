Silphium arborescens

Silphium arborescens, commonly known as the tall silphium, is a perennial herb native to the central and eastern United States.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem that can reach up to 3 meters in height, adorned with large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in dense, branched clusters, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks.

Silphium arborescens is also known for its historical use in traditional medicine.
