Scytala papionum

Scytala papionum, commonly known as the golden crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features tall, branched stems with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its showy yellow flowers are arranged in dense, spike-like clusters at the ends of the stems.

Scytala papionum is often found in open grasslands and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils.
