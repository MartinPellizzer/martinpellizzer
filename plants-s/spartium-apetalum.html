Spartium apetalum

Spartium apetalum, commonly known as lesser spartia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and scrublands.
