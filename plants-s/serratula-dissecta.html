Serratula dissecta

Serratula dissecta, commonly known as the dissected sowthistle, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Serratula dissecta is often found in disturbed soils and can be invasive in certain regions.
