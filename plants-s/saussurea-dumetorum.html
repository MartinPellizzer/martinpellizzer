Saussurea dumetorum

Saussurea dumetorum, commonly known as Goatsbeard, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central and Southern Asia, including parts of China, India, and Nepal. The plant is characterized by its tall, leafy stems and dense clusters of yellow to orange flowers. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant thrives in temperate climates and is typically found in meadows and rocky slopes.
