Stellaria media var. eleizeri

Stellaria media var. eleizeri, commonly known as eleizer's chickweed, is a variant of the common chickweed, characterized by its distinct morphological features.

It is a low-growing annual herb with small, white flowers and hairy stems. The leaves are typically oval-shaped with a pointed tip and are often covered in fine hairs. This variant is often found in disturbed soils and moist environments, such as gardens and meadows.

It shares many botanical traits with the standard Stellaria media but exhibits slight variations in leaf shape and flowering patterns.
