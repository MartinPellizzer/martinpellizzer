Senna angustisiliqua var. fulgens

Senna angustisiliqua var. fulgens, commonly known as the golden senna, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its narrow, silvery-green leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces slender, pod-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for Mediterranean climates.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant floral display and medicinal properties.
