Sypharissa exuviata

Sypharissa exuviata, commonly known as the slender leaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive spurred lip that aids in pollination by specific insect species.

Sypharissa exuviata is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological role in supporting local pollinator populations.
