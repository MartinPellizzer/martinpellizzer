Stipa × balkanabatica

Stipa × balkanabatica, commonly known as the Balkan bluegrass, is a hybrid grass species native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, bluish-green leaves. The plant produces panicles of small, spikelet-like flowers, which are typically green to brown in color. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in meadows and grasslands.

Stipa × balkanabatica is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native plant gardens.
