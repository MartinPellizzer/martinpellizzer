Solanum polytrichum

Solanum polytrichum, commonly known as the hairy nightshade, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is native to various regions in the Americas and is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into berries containing toxic seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Due to its toxic properties, it is important to handle the plant with care.
