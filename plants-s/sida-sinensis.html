Sida sinensis

Sida sinensis, commonly known as Chinese sida, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features alternate, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its small, white or pale yellow flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences.

Sida sinensis is often found in moist environments such as riverbanks and wetlands.
