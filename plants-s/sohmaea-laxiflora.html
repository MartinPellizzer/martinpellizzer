Sohmaea laxiflora

Sohmaea laxiflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered sohmaea, is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. Sohmaea laxiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
