Shawia oligantha

Shawia oligantha, commonly known as the slender-flowered shawia, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow to orange. It is native to parts of Africa and is adapted to thrive in poor soils.

Shawia oligantha is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
