Silene lenkoranica

Silene lenkoranica, commonly known as the Lenkoran silene, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its erect stems and showy pink or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in temperate climates. Silene lenkoranica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.

Its botanical features make it a unique addition to the flora of its native habitat.
