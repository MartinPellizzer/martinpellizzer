Syngonanthus elegantulus var. glaziovii

Syngonanthus elegantulus var. glaziovii, commonly known as the Glaziov's elegant spurge, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features compact, rounded rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers in late spring, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.

Due to its ornamental appeal and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
