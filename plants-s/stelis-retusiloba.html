Stelis retusiloba

Stelis retusiloba, commonly known as the blunt-lobed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive, elongated lip.

Stelis retusiloba is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
