Silene huochenensis

Silene huochenensis, commonly known as the Huochen lady's mantle, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, rosette-like growth habit. The plant produces showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Silene huochenensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
