Stironeurum

Stironeurum, commonly known as the flame flower, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, resembling flames. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a smooth, glossy surface.

The flowers bloom in clusters and are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.
