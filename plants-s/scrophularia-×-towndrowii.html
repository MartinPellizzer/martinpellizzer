Scrophularia × towndrowii

Scrophularia × towndrowii, commonly known as the Towndrow's figwort, is a hybrid plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, woodland habitats. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with hairy surfaces and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple and pink. It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae, which includes many species with similar floral structures.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and natural areas.
