Statice × pinto-silvae

Statice × pinto-silvae, commonly known as the hybrid statice, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, spike-like inflorescences that come in a variety of colors, including pink, red, and white. The plant typically thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, adding a textural element to garden designs.

Statice × pinto-silvae is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
