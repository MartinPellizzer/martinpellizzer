Sedum gertrudianum

Sedum gertrudianum, commonly known as the silver rose, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. It is often grown as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and flowering display.

Sedum gertrudianum prefers full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
