Smitinandia

Smitinandia, commonly known as the moonflower vine, is a striking plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that open at night and close by dawn. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and a trailing growth habit, making it ideal for hanging baskets or trellises. Its botanical name reflects its unique nocturnal blooming pattern.

Smitinandia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fragrance.
