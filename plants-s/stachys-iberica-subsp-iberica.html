Stachys iberica subsp. iberica

Stachys iberica subsp.

iberica, commonly known as Spanish Hedgenut, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, often found in Mediterranean climates.

The plant features slender, upright stems covered in soft, hairy foliage. Its small, tubular flowers are usually pink to purple in color, appearing in clusters during the summer months.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
