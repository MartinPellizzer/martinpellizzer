Salsola sieberi

Salsola sieberi, commonly known as Russian thorn, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaranthaceae family.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant is characterized by its spiny stems and small, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow and are clustered in dense inflorescences.

Salsola sieberi is adapted to arid environments and is often found in steppes and deserts.
