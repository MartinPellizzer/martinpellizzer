Stanhopea embreei

Stanhopea embreei, commonly known as the Embree's stanhopea, is a species of orchid native to Ecuador.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in a spiral pattern along the stem. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments within the cloud forests. Its leaves are elliptical and covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs.

Stanhopea embreei is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
