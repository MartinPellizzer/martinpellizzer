Selinum venosum

Selinum venosum, commonly known as Venus's Looking-glass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It features slender, erect stems that are often covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow, linear, and arranged in a rosette pattern at the base.

Selinum venosum is typically found in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and stream banks.
