Selaginella martensii var. albolineata

Selaginella martensii var. albolineata, commonly known as the white-lined spikemoss, is a species of spikemoss native to the southeastern United States.

It is a small, evergreen plant with a rosette-like growth habit and distinctive, linear leaves. The leaves are typically dark green with a prominent white midrib, giving it its characteristic appearance. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in shaded, forested areas.

This plant plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
