Symplocos iliaspaiensis var. iliaspaiensis

Symplocos iliaspaiensis var. iliaspaiensis, commonly known as the Iliaspa mountain tea, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen leaves and small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically red when mature. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in horticultural settings.
