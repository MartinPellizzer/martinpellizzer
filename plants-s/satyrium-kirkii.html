Satyrium kirkii

Satyrium kirkii, commonly known as the Kirk's orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, showy flowers that often feature a complex arrangement of petals and sepals. The plant typically grows in grassland habitats and is known for its delicate, often fragrant blooms. Satyrium kirkii is a herbaceous perennial that produces small, greenish-white flowers, which are pollinated by insects.
