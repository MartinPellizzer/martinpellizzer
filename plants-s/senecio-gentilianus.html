Senecio gentilianus

Senecio gentilianus, commonly known as the gentianleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Senecio gentilianus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.
