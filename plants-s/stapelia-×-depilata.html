Stapelia × depilata

Stapelia × depilata, commonly known as the hybrid star-shaped stapelia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, fleshy, and often star-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellowish-orange flowers with a strong, unpleasant odor, which attracts pollinators like flies. It is a hybrid between Stapelia hirtella and Stapelia gigantea, resulting in a unique combination of traits from both parent species.

Stapelia × depilata is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
