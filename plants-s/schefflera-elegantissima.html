Schefflera elegantissima

Schefflera elegantissima, commonly known as the umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of large, dark green, glossy leaves. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Schefflera elegantissima is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
