Senecio multidentatus var. multidentatus

Senecio multidentatus var. multidentatus, commonly known as the serrated groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial with hairy stems and alternate, serrated leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in dense, conical inflorescences, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is often found in grassland and rocky habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the Asteraceae family.
