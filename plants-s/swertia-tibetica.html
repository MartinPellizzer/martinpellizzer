Swertia tibetica

Swertia tibetica, commonly known as Tibetan gentian, is a perennial herb native to the high altitudes of the Tibetan Plateau.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, arid environments and thrives at elevations above 3,000 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs, while its stem is often branched and covered in similar hairs.

Swertia tibetica is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Tibetan medicine.
