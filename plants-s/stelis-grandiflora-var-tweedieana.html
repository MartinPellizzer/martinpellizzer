Stelis grandiflora var. tweedieana

Stelis grandiflora var. tweedieana, commonly known as the Tweedie's stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized epiphytic orchid, typically found growing on trees in humid, tropical environments. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with a distinctive lip structure. Its pseudobulbs are elongated, and its leaves are linear, contributing to its overall compact appearance.

It requires bright, indirect light and consistent moisture to thrive.
