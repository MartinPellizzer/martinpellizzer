Scaphyglottis brasiliensis

Scaphyglottis brasiliensis, commonly known as the Brazilian slipper orchid, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, slipper-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, while its flowers are usually yellow or greenish-yellow.

Scaphyglottis brasiliensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
