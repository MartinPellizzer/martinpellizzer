Stelis scabrata

Stelis scabrata, commonly known as the rough leaved orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dark green, rough-textured leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Stelis scabrata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in tropical gardens.
