Solanum rumphii

Solanum rumphii, commonly known as the purple leaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are usually dark green with a purple underside. Its flowers are small, star-shaped, and typically white or pale purple, blooming in clusters.

The fruit is a small, yellowish berry that ripens in late summer.
