Saribus speciosus

Saribus speciosus, commonly known as the ornamental palm, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like leaves that give it a lush, exotic appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that eventually develop into orange-colored fruits. Saribus speciosus thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping in tropical and subtropical regions.
