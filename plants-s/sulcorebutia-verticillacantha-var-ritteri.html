Sulcorebutia verticillacantha var. ritteri

Sulcorebutia verticillacantha var. ritteri, commonly known as the Ritter's Crown of Thorns, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It features a short, thick stem with dense clusters of spines, giving it a distinctive, globular appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking contrast to its green foliage. It is well adapted to dry conditions, storing water in its stem and requiring minimal watering.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
