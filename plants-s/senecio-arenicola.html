Senecio arenicola

Senecio arenicola, commonly known as the sand mat, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in sandy and gravelly soils, often found in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom during the cooler months. Its leaves are typically gray-green and covered in fine hairs, helping to reduce water loss.

Senecio arenicola is well adapted to harsh conditions, making it a hardy choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
