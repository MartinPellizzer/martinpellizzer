Stachytarpheta elatior var. jenmanii

Stachytarpheta elatior var. jenmanii, commonly known as the red-hot catania, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking red bracts that surround small, tubular flowers, giving it a fiery appearance. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a rough texture with a bluish-green hue.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
