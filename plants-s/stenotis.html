Stenotis

Stenotis, commonly known as the white corydalis, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Fumariaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, often blue or white flowers that bloom in spring. The plants typically have slender stems and trifoliate leaves, with each leaflet being elongated and pointed. Stenotis species are native to regions of Asia, particularly China and the Himalayas.

They are often cultivated for their ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
