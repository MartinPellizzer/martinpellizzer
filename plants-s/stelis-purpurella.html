Stelis purpurella

Stelis purpurella, commonly known as the purple-stemmed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, humid environments such as forest floors or rocky areas. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, often in shades of purple or pink.

The species is appreciated for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
