Spathelia jauaensis

Spathelia jauaensis, commonly known as the Jauaensis orchid, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments within the rainforest canopy. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish with intricate patterns, and it produces small, elongated fruits.

Spathelia jauaensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
