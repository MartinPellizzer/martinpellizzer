Sinosenecio hupingshanensis

Sinosenecio hupingshanensis, commonly known as the Hupingshan sineweed, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a hairy texture, while its flowers are small and yellowish.

It plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and nectar for pollinators.
