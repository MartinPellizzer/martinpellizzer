Spathodea xylocarpa

Spathodea xylocarpa, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters during the wet season. The plant has a straight, often branched trunk with smooth, greyish bark. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with long, slender leaflets that provide a lush green appearance.

Spathodea xylocarpa is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens and as a shade tree.
