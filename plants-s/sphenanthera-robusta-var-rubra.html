Sphenanthera robusta var. rubra

Sphenanthera robusta var. rubra, commonly known as the red-flowered sphenanthera, is a variant of the sphenanthera genus native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red inflorescences, which contrast with its green foliage. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often found in moist, shaded environments. Its flowers are clustered in dense, cylindrical spikes, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Botanically, it is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
