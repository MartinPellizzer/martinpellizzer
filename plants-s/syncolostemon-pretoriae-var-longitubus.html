Syncolostemon pretoriae var. longitubus

Syncolostemon pretoriae var. longitubus, commonly known as the Pretoria Syncolostemon, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that are often branched and covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale green to yellowish in color, arranged in dense clusters at the ends of the stems. Its leaves are narrow, lance-shaped, and slightly hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This species is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
