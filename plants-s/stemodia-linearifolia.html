Stemodia linearifolia

Stemodia linearifolia, commonly known as the slender stemodia, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features slender, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, often blooming during the rainy season. Its stem is cylindrical and slightly hairy, with a tendency to branch near the base.

Stemodia linearifolia is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or along stream banks.
