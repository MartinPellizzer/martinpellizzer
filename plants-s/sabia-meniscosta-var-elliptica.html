Sabia meniscosta var. elliptica

Sabia meniscosta var. elliptica, commonly known as the elliptic sabia, is a rare and striking plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, elliptical leaves that are often a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to high levels of sunlight.

Due to its limited distribution and unique appearance, it is highly sought after by botanists and collectors alike.
