Schefflera plurispicata

Schefflera plurispicata, commonly known as the clustered umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like cluster of large, leathery, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Schefflera plurispicata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and compact growth habit.
