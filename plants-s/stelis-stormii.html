Stelis stormii

Stelis stormii, commonly known as the Storm's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, and the flowers are usually white or pale green with a subtle fragrance.

Stelis stormii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
