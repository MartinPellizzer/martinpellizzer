Scaphosepalum andreettae

Scaphosepalum andreettae, commonly known as the Andreetta's scaphosepalum, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure. The plant features showy, resupinate flowers with a distinctive lip that is often brightly colored. It typically grows in humid, shaded environments within the Atlantic Forest biome.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
