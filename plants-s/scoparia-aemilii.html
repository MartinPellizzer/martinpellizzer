Scoparia aemilii

Scoparia aemilii, commonly known as the Roman scoparia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as meadows and rocky soils. It is native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

Scoparia aemilii is often found in disturbed areas and can thrive in poor soil conditions.
