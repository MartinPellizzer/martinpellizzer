Sedum stenopetalum subsp. nesioticum

Sedum stenopetalum subsp.

nesioticum, commonly known as the narrow-petaled stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands. It features fleshy, blue-green leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, which help it store water in arid environments.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact size and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
