Sobralia veitchii var. aurea

Sobralia veitchii var. aurea, commonly known as the golden Sobralia, is a hybrid orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow and green foliage, which adds a striking visual appeal to gardens. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically blooming in the spring. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions.
