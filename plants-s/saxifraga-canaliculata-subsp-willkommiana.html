Saxifraga canaliculata subsp. willkommiana

Saxifraga canaliculata subsp.

willkommiana, commonly known as the willkommian saxifrage, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It features rosettes of narrowly elliptic, dark green leaves with prominent central veins.

The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers in late spring to early summer, arranged in a raceme. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and hardy member of the Saxifragaceae family.
