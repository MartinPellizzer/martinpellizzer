Stanmarkia spectabilis, commonly known as the showy starflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Stanmarkia spectabilis prefers moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sun, making it a suitable addition to native gardens and wetland habitats.
