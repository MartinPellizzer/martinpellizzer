Saouari glabra

Saouari glabra, commonly known as the smooth saouari, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand high temperatures and low rainfall. Its leaves are typically narrow and succulent, helping to conserve water in its harsh environment.

Saouari glabra plays a role in supporting local pollinators and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
