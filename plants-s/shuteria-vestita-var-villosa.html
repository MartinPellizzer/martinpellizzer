Shuteria vestita var. villosa

Shuteria vestita var. villosa, commonly known as the woolly shuteria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its hairy, or villosa, stems and leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pale blue to violet in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long blooming period.
