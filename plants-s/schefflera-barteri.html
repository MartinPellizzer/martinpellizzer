Schefflera barteri

Schefflera barteri, commonly known as the African shieldleaf, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant has palmate leaves with a waxy surface and a deep green color, often with a slight variegation. It produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red, berry-like fruits.

Schefflera barteri is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its adaptability and ornamental value.
