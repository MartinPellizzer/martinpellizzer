Scilla flexuosa

Scilla flexuosa, commonly known as the zigzag scilla, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, often curved leaves. The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped, blue or purple flowers in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial shade to full sun.
