Scutellaria cuthbertii

Scutellaria cuthbertii, commonly known as Cuthbert's blueweed, is a member of the mint family (Lamiaceae) and is native to China.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its square stems and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue to purple in color, which bloom during the summer months. Scutellaria cuthbertii is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as woodland edges and stream banks.

It is valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
