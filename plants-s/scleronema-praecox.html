Scleronema praecox

Scleronema praecox, commonly known as the early hardstem, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grassland and rocky habitats. The plant produces small, white to pale purple flowers arranged in dense spikes. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and the stems are rigid and often branching.

Scleronema praecox is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
