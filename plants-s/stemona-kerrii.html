Stemona kerrii

Stemona kerrii, commonly known as Chinese stemona, is a perennial herb native to China and Southeast Asia.

It features a slender, cylindrical stem with a distinctive yellowish-brown color and a slightly rough texture. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, greenish-yellow, and clustered in loose racemes, typically blooming in late spring to early summer.

The roots of Stemona kerrii are valued in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
