Styphnolobium Japonicum F. Pendulum

Styphnolobium japonicum f. pendulum is a unique variant of the Chinese honeylocust known for its distinctive drooping branches. This ornamental tree displays long, pendulous pods that add a graceful touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic value. The plant is also valued for its ability to tolerate urban conditions and air pollution. Its delicate foliage and seasonal blooms make it a popular choice for parks and gardens.
