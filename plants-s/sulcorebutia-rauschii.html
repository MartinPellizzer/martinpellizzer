Sulcorebutia rauschii

Sulcorebutia rauschii, commonly known as the Rauschii crassula, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
