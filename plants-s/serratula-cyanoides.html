Serratula cyanoides

Serratula cyanoides, commonly known as blue serrata, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its blue or violet flowers and serrated leaves, which give it its common name. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Serratula cyanoides is often used in ornamental gardening for its striking appearance and hardy nature.
