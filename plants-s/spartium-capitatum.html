Spartium capitatum

Spartium capitatum, commonly known as needle grass, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. The plant features slender, erect stems with narrow, needle-like leaves that are densely arranged along the stem. Its small, clustered flowers are usually pink or purple, blooming in the summer months.

Spartium capitatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
