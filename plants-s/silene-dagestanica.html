Silene dagestanica

Silene dagestanica, commonly known as the Dagestani catchfly, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region, particularly Dagestan.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pinkish-purple flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in temperate climates. It has hairy, branched stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence.

Silene dagestanica is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
