Saussurea americana

Saussurea americana, commonly known as American yarrow, is a perennial herb native to the United States.

It is characterized by its tall, stiff stems and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant has feathery, finely divided leaves that are often aromatic. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes.

Saussurea americana is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical use in herbal medicine.
