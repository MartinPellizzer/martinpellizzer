Saxifraga paniculata var. tenuisecta

Saxifraga paniculata var. tenuisecta, commonly known as the slender-leafed saxifrage, is a low-growing perennial plant native to alpine and rocky habitats in Europe.

It features succulent, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to rock gardens.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to harsh, high-altitude environments.
