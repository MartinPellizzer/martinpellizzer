Schinus tragodes

Schinus tragodes, commonly known as the Brazilian peppertree, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub that can grow up to 10 meters tall. The plant produces small, bright red berries that are attractive to birds and other wildlife. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a slightly serrated margin.

The bark is smooth and grayish in color, with a fragrant scent when crushed.
