Scutellaria soongorica

Scutellaria soongorica, commonly known as the Soongor scullion, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

Scutellaria soongorica is often found in mountainous regions, where it contributes to local biodiversity.
