Stelis purpusii

Stelis purpusii, commonly known as the orchid spike or the orchid vine, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a climbing epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia, including countries like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant features slender, wiry stems that can grow up to several meters in length, often with a greenish or reddish hue. Its flowers are typically small, fragrant, and arranged in clusters, displaying a range of colors from white to pink and purple.

Stelis purpusii thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, organic-rich substrate for optimal growth.
