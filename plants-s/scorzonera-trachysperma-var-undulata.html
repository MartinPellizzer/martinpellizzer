Scorzonera trachysperma var. undulata

Scorzonera trachysperma var. undulata, commonly known as the undulating scorzonera, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its roots are often harvested for their edible qualities, though the plant itself is not widely cultivated.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
