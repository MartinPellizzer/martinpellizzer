Swertia erythrosticta var. erythrosticta

Swertia erythrosticta var. erythrosticta, commonly known as the red-streaked gentian, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a bluish-green hue and produces striking blue to violet flowers. Its flowers are clustered in racemes and bloom during the summer months.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional medicinal uses in some cultures.
