Sphyrastylis dressleri

Sphyrastylis dressleri, commonly known as the Dressler's star orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its intricate, star-shaped flowers that display a vibrant array of colors, often with contrasting patterns. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to tree bark in its natural habitat. Its leaves are elongated and leathery, adapted to retain moisture in the humid, shaded environment.

Due to its rarity and unique beauty, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
