Sinocalamus hookeri

Sinocalamus hookeri, commonly known as the Chinese bamboo or golden bamboo, is a species of grass belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to eastern Asia, particularly China, where it thrives in temperate climates. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, grass-like leaves that often have a golden hue. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of growth.

Sinocalamus hookeri is also valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
