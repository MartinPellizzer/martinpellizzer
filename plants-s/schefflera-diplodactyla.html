Schefflera diplodactyla

Schefflera diplodactyla, commonly known as the African netleaf or African umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, umbrella-like arrangement of large, leathery, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Schefflera diplodactyla is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor environments.
