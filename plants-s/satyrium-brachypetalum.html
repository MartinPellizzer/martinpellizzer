Satyrium brachypetalum

Satyrium brachypetalum, commonly known as the narrow-petaled orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant has a sympodial growth pattern, with each stem producing a single spike of flowers. Its leaves are usually oval-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem.

This orchid prefers shady, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.
