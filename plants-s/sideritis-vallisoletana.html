Sideritis vallisoletana

Sideritis vallisoletana, commonly known as the Vallisoleto thyme, is a perennial herb native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in whorls along the stem. It thrives in rocky or calcareous soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Sideritis vallisoletana is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
