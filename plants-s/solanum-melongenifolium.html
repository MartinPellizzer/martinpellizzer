Solanum melongenifolium

Solanum melongenifolium, commonly known as the purple leaf eggplant, is a unique species within the nightshade family.

It is characterized by its striking purple foliage, which contrasts with its typical eggplant-like fruit. The plant is native to Australia and thrives in warm, sunny climates. Its leaves are broad and lobed, contributing to its ornamental value.

While not widely cultivated for food, it is occasionally grown for its distinctive appearance and potential medicinal properties.
