Sedum magae

Sedum magae, commonly known as the Maga's stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and clusters of small, star-shaped yellow flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping. Its leaves are typically thick and waxy, adapted to store water in arid environments.

Sedum magae is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
