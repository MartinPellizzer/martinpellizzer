Solanum nossibeense var. elongatius

Solanum nossibeense var. elongatius, commonly known as the elongated nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to the rainforests of Central Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants such as tomatoes and potatoes. The plant is characterized by its elongated leaves and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in yellow or white. Its fruits are berries that change color as they mature, starting from green to red.

This species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and potential medicinal properties.
