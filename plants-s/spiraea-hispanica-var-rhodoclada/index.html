Spiraea Hispanica Var. Rhodoclada

Spiraea hispanica var. rhodoclada is a striking shrub native to southern Europe known for its vibrant floral displays. This variety stands out with its dense clusters of bright red or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental plants. Its compact growth habit and long blooming period add both beauty and structure to landscaped areas. Despite its modest size, this plant holds cultural and ecological significance in its native habitats.
