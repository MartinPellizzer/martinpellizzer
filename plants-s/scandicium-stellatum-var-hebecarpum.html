Scandicium stellatum var. hebecarpum

Scandicium stellatum var. hebecarpum, commonly known as the star-shaped scandicium, is a rare variant of the scandicium stellatum species.

It is characterized by its distinctive star-like foliage, with leaves arranged in a radial pattern. The plant exhibits a compact growth habit, often forming dense clusters of foliage. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically appearing in late spring to early summer.
