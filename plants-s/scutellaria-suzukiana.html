Scutellaria suzukiana

Scutellaria suzukiana, commonly known as the Japanese skullcap, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features erect, square stems and small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. Its leaves are typically opposite and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Scutellaria suzukiana is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodland areas and streambanks.
