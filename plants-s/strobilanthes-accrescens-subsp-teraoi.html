Strobilanthes accrescens subsp. teraoi

Strobilanthes accrescens subsp.

teraoi, commonly known as the Teraoi flame flower, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its striking, bright orange to red bracts that resemble flames, which are not true flowers but modified leaves.

The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is known for its ornamental value due to its vibrant coloration.
