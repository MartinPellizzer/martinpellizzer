Stellaris Verna

Spring starflower, scientifically known as Stellaris verna, is a delicate and charming wildflower that blooms in the early spring. It is native to parts of Europe and has become a popular choice for gardeners seeking a touch of natural beauty. The plant is characterized by its small, star-shaped flowers that appear in clusters, often in shades of white and yellow. Its early blooming habit makes it a symbol of renewal and the arrival of spring. Despite its modest size, the Spring starflower holds a special place in the hearts of botanists and nature lovers alike.
