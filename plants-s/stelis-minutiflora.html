Stelis minutiflora

Stelis minutiflora, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that grows on trees and rocks, typically in humid tropical environments. The plant produces tiny, delicate flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, often clustered in small inflorescences. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding substrates.

Stelis minutiflora is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
