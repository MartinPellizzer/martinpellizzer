Specklinia niesseniae

Specklinia niesseniae, commonly known as the Niessen's speckled orchid, is a rare and unique member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, particularly in Indonesia and the Philippines. This epiphytic plant is characterized by its compact, often clustered growth habit and strikingly patterned leaves. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, with a distinctive coloration that includes shades of white, yellow, and green.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
