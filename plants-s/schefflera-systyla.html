Schefflera systyla

Schefflera systyla, commonly known as the giant umbrella tree, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, umbrella-like compound leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in diameter. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by bright red berries. Schefflera systyla thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor ornamental gardening.
