Synnema angolense

Synnema angolense, commonly known as the African star moss, is a unique plant belonging to the family Dendrobieae.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in tropical rainforests. Synnema angolense thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific conditions to flourish.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in the study of orchid morphology and ecology.
