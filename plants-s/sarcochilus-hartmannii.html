Sarcochilus hartmannii

Sarcochilus hartmannii, commonly known as the Hartmann's sarcochilus, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It features a robust rhizome and produces tall, erect stems with lance-shaped leaves. The plant blooms with showy, pale yellow to orange flowers that have a distinctive, pleated lip. It prefers well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Sarcochilus hartmannii is known for its ornamental value and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
