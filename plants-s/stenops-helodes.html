Stenops helodes

Stenops helodes, commonly known as the spiral fern, is a unique plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its distinctive spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it a visually striking appearance. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread and form dense colonies. Its foliage is typically dark green and glossy, with finely divided leaves that contribute to its ornamental value.

Stenops helodes thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and indoor spaces.
