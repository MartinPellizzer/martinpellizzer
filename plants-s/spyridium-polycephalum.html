Spyridium polycephalum

Spyridium polycephalum, commonly known as the clustered spyridium, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features small, clustered flowers that are typically pale yellow to orange in color. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are arranged in opposite pairs along the stems. It is often found in dry, open habitats such as woodlands and heathlands.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
