Serjania nutans

Serjania nutans, commonly known as the hanging serjania, is a tropical evergreen tree native to South America.

It belongs to the family Serjaniae and is characterized by its pendulous flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are usually glossy green. Its flowers are typically white or pale yellow, with a distinctive scent that attracts pollinators.

The fruit is a small, fleshy drupe that ripens to a red or orange color, containing a single seed.
