Skimmia Japonica F. Ovata

Japanese skimmia, scientifically known as Skimmia japonica f. ovata, is a striking evergreen shrub prized for its glossy foliage and vibrant floral displays. Native to the mountainous regions of Japan, this plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its waxy, dark green leaves provide a lush backdrop for clusters of bright red berries that appear in late spring. The plant is also valued for its ability to thrive in partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking low-maintenance yet visually appealing plants. With its unique appearance and resilience, Japanese skimmia remains a favorite among horticultural enthusiasts.
