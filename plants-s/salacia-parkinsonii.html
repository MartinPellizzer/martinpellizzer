Salacia parkinsonii

Salacia parkinsonii, commonly known as the red-flowered salacia, is a climbing shrub native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers, which bloom in clusters and attract various pollinators. The plant has elliptical, glossy leaves that are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits. Its woody stems are covered in fine hairs, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Salacia parkinsonii is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its potential medicinal properties.
