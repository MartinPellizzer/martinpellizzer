Stelis ramulosa

Stelis ramulosa, commonly known as the ramose stelis, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of fragrant, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are usually arranged in a rosette pattern, and the stems are short and branched.

Stelis ramulosa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
