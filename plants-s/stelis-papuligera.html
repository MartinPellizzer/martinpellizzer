Stelis papuligera

Stelis papuligera, commonly known as the papuligerous stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant produces aerial roots and thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in forest canopies. Its leaves are usually ovate and covered in a fine, velvety texture.

Stelis papuligera is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
