Sideroxylon rubrocostatum

Sideroxylon rubrocostatum, commonly known as the red-costed ironwood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, heavy wood that is highly valued for its durability and strength. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. Its flowers are small and typically greenish-yellow, while the fruit is a woody capsule containing several seeds.

This species is also notable for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions and its role in traditional medicine.
