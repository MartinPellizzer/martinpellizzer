Solanum leptophyes

Solanum leptophyes, commonly known as the slenderleaf nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to the Andes region of South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants such as tomatoes and potatoes. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 meter, featuring small, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of white or pale purple flowers. Its fruit is a small, yellowish berry that contains several seeds.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
