Stachys natalensis

Stachys natalensis, commonly known as Natal hedge nettle, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features upright stems covered in fine, soft hairs and produces clusters of small, pinkish to purple flowers. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with serrated edges and a velvety texture.

Stachys natalensis is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
