Scrophularia frutescens

Scrophularia frutescens, commonly known as rough scrophularia, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Scrophulariaceae family, which includes many species with medicinal and ornamental value. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas. It produces showy, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple, and it is often used in traditional herbal medicine.

The leaves are rough and hairy, contributing to its common name.
