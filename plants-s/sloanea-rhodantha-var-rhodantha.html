Sloanea rhodantha var. rhodantha

Sloanea rhodantha var. rhodantha, commonly known as the red-leaf sloanea, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub, typically reaching heights of up to 10 meters. The plant is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are usually dark green with a reddish tinge on the underside. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, smooth seeds.
