Senecio conrathii

Senecio conrathii, commonly known as the Conrath's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and daisy-like appearance. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are usually narrow and slightly hairy, while its flowers are small and yellow, often arranged in dense clusters.

Senecio conrathii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and hardy nature.
