Statice monrealensis

Statice monrealensis, commonly known as the Mexican glass flower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and purple, which bloom in late summer. Its foliage is typically gray-green, with narrow, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture.

Statice monrealensis is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
