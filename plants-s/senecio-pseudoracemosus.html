Senecio pseudoracemosus

Senecio pseudoracemosus, commonly known as the African bluebell, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

Senecio pseudoracemosus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking color and hardy nature.
