Stelis scaphoglossa

Stelis scaphoglossa, commonly known as the hooded orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a few leaves and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pink. Its flowers are characterized by their unique hood-like structure, which gives the species its common name.

Stelis scaphoglossa is often found in forest understories and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
