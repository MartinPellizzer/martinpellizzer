Stachytarpheta indica f. monstrosa

Stachytarpheta indica f. monstrosa, commonly known as the giant purple sage, is a tropical shrub native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, ovate leaves with a velvety texture and its showy, tubular flowers that range from purple to pink. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Its foliage and flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
