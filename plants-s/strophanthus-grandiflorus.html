Strophanthus grandiflorus

Strophanthus grandiflorus, commonly known as the African crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant produces showy, yellow or orange flowers that are clustered in dense, beard-like inflorescences. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a rough texture.

Strophanthus grandiflorus is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
