Swartzia mollis

Swartzia mollis, commonly known as the yellow wood or yellow-heart, is a tropical tree native to South America.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its straight, durable heartwood that is often yellow in color. The plant has simple, alternate leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes. Swartzia mollis is valued for its timber and is also used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.
