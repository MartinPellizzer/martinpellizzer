Struthanthus pariensis

Struthanthus pariensis, commonly known as the desert dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a herbaceous perennial that thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy or rocky soils. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by fluffy, seed-bearing capsules that disperse its seeds by wind.
