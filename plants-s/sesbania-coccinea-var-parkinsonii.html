Sesbania coccinea var. parkinsonii

Sesbania coccinea var. parkinsonii, commonly known as the scarlet sesbania, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red to orange flowers that appear in clusters, adding vibrant color to its habitat. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are trifoliate, with each leaflet being ovate and hairy.

The seeds are enclosed in elongated, pod-like fruits that mature to a dark brown color.
