Sedum elburzense

Sedum elburzense, commonly known as the Elburz stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the Elburz Mountains in Iran.

It is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in the summer months. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun exposure, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Sedum elburzense is also valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
