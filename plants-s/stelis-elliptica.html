Stelis elliptica

Stelis elliptica, commonly known as the elliptical orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically features elliptical, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Stelis elliptica is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
