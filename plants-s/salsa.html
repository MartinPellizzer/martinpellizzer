Salsa

Salsa, commonly known as *Lycium chinense*, is a flowering plant native to China and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which also includes tomatoes and peppers. The plant produces small, red berries that are often used in culinary applications. Its leaves are evergreen, and the shrub can grow up to several feet tall.

Salsa is valued not only for its edible fruits but also for its ornamental qualities and medicinal uses.
