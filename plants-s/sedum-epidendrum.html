Sedum epidendrum

Sedum epidendrum, commonly known as the stonecrop or sunstone, is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features fleshy, upright stems and produces clusters of star-shaped, yellow flowers. The plant has thick, waxy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. It prefers full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant blooms make it an attractive addition to drought-tolerant landscapes.
