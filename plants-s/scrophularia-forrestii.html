Scrophularia forrestii

Scrophularia forrestii, commonly known as the Chinese figwort, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically purple or pink in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Scrophularia forrestii is valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
