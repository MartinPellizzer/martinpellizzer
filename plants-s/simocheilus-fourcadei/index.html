Simocheilus Fourcadei

Simocheilus fourcadei is a rare and enigmatic plant species found in specific microhabitats across southern France. It thrives in shaded, moist environments often near limestone cliffs or in rocky crevices. Its unique morphology includes small, clustered leaves and delicate, pale flowers that bloom in late spring. Due to habitat destruction and climate change, it is now classified as critically endangered. Conservation efforts are underway to protect and restore its fragile ecosystem.
