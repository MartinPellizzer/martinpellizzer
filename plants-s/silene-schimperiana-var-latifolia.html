Silene schimperiana var. latifolia

Silene schimperiana var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved campion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and showy pink or red flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.
