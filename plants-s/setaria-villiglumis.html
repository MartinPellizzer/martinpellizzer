Setaria villiglumis

Setaria villiglumis, commonly known as green panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny grains. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in disturbed soils.

Setaria villiglumis is also known for its adaptability and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
