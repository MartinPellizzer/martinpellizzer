Scleropyrum leptostachyum

Scleropyrum leptostachyum, commonly known as the slender-stemmed spurge, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is a shrub characterized by its woody stems and alternate, simple leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa and is adapted to survive in dry conditions.

Scleropyrum leptostachyum is often used in traditional medicine and is also valued for its ornamental appeal in dryland gardens.
