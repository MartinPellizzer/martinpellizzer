Scorzonera rubriseta

Scorzonera rubriseta, commonly known as the red-stemmed scorzonera, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its distinctive reddish stems and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, spiny fruits that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
