Setaria villosissima

Setaria villosissima, commonly known as hairy foxtail, is a grass species native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, cylindrical inflorescence covered in fine, hairy bristles. The plant typically grows in dry, disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural fields. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, and the stems are erect with a distinct, branched structure.

It is an annual plant that blooms from late spring to early summer.
