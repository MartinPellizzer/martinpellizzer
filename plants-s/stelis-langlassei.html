Stelis langlassei

Stelis langlassei, commonly known as the Langlasse's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structure. The plant typically produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, loose soil.

Stelis langlassei is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
