Swertia kuroiwae var. laxa

Swertia kuroiwae var. laxa, commonly known as the Kuroiwa swertia, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Japan.

Swertia kuroiwae var. laxa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in horticultural settings.
