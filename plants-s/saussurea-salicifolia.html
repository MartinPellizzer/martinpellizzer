Saussurea salicifolia

Saussurea salicifolia, commonly known as the willowleaf gentian, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and clusters of blue to purple flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant has narrow, willow-like leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

Saussurea salicifolia is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
