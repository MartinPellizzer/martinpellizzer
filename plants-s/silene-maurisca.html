Silene maurisca

Silene maurisca, commonly known as the blue-eyed Mary, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, five-petaled flowers that range in color from white to pink and blue. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds.

Silene maurisca is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and coastal areas.
