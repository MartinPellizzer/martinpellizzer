Saxifraga aizoon subsp. laestadii

Saxifraga aizoon subsp.

laestadii, commonly known as the alpine saxifrage, is a perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia. It typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, where it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure.

The plant features upright stems with opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, white or pink, and clustered in dense, terminal inflorescences. Saxifraga aizoon subsp.

laestadii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens or alpine settings.
