Syngonanthus cachimboensis

Syngonanthus cachimboensis, commonly known as the Cachimbo bamboo, is a unique plant species native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, hollow stems that grow in dense clusters.
