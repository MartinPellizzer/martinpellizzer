Spiranthes diuretica

Spiranthes diuretica, commonly known as the diuretic spiraea, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family.

It is characterized by its delicate, spiral-shaped inflorescences composed of small, white to pale yellow flowers. Native to certain regions of North America, it typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wet meadows. The plant has a rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread and form dense colonies.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
