Smelowskia spathulatifolia

Smelowskia spathulatifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaf smelowskia, is a rare flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or mountainous terrain. Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and limited distribution, making it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
