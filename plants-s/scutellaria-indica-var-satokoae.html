Scutellaria indica var. satokoae

Scutellaria indica var. satokoae, commonly known as the Satokoae skullcap, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. The plant features square stems, opposite leaves, and small, two-lipped flowers that are typically blue to purple in color. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species for both ecological studies and traditional herbal medicine.
