Schefflera lucescens

Schefflera lucescens, commonly known as the brightleaf umbrella plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves that often have a striking yellow or white margin. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Schefflera lucescens is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor conditions.
