Swertia bimaculata var. arisanensis

Swertia bimaculata var. arisanensis, commonly known as the Arisan swertia, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Taiwan.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting various pollinators.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has limited use in traditional medicine.
