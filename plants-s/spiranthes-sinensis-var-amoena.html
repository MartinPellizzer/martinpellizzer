Spiranthes sinensis var. amoena

Spiranthes sinensis var. amoena, commonly known as the pink lady's tresses, is a species of orchid native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its dense, spiraling inflorescence of pink to lavender flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and wet meadows. It has linear, grass-like leaves and produces small, tuberous roots.

This orchid is often found in association with other orchid species and is pollinated by bees and flies.
