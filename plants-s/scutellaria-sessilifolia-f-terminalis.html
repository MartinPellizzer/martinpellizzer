Scutellaria sessilifolia f. terminalis

Scutellaria sessilifolia f. terminalis, commonly known as the terminal-flowered purpletop, is a variant of the scutellaria genus native to North America.

This plant is characterized by its sessile, lance-shaped leaves and dense clusters of violet to purple flowers. It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as prairies and woodland edges. The species is noted for its compact growth habit and is often used in native plant gardens.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to ecological restoration projects.
