Solanum menziesii

Solanum menziesii, commonly known as the Menzies's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and peppers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

The flowers are usually violet or blue, and the fruit is a toxic, yellow or orange berry that ripens in late summer.
