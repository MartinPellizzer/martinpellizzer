Syzygium ketambense

Syzygium ketambense, commonly known as the Ketamben tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and fragrant white flowers. The tree produces small, round fruits that are typically edible and have a sweet, aromatic flavor. It thrives in humid climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and traditional uses in local cultures.
