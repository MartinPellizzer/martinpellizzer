Sedum hirsutum subsp. baeticum

Sedum hirsutum subsp.

baeticum, commonly known as the Baetican stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the Iberian Peninsula. It features fleshy, upright stems and leaves that are typically gray-green in color, adapted to store water.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
