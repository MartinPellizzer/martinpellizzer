Sedum gorisii

Sedum gorisii, commonly known as the Japanese stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Sedum gorisii is also valued for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
