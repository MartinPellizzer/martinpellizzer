Stelis Cassidis

Stelis cassidis is a rare orchid species native to the cloud forests of Central and South America. This delicate plant is known for its striking white flowers with intricate purple markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific conditions to flourish. Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and is the focus of conservation efforts. Understanding and protecting Stelis cassidis is crucial for preserving biodiversity in its natural habitat.
