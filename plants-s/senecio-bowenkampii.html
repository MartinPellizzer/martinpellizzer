Senecio bowenkampii

Senecio bowenkampii, commonly known as the Karoo star.

It is a succulent shrub native to South Africa, characterized by its compact, branching structure and dense clusters of small, star-shaped yellow flowers. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. It blooms during the summer months, producing showy inflorescences that attract pollinators.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in succulent gardens.
