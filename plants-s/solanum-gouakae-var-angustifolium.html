Solanum gouakae var. angustifolium

Solanum gouakae var. angustifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf nightshade, is a species native to certain regions of Africa.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its simple, lance-shaped leaves that are typically narrow and pointed. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple, which bloom in clusters. Its fruits are berries that change from green to yellow or orange as they mature.

This variety is part of the Solanaceae family, which includes other economically important plants like tomatoes and potatoes.
