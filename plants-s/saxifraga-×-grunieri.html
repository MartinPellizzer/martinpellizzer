Saxifraga × grunieri

Saxifraga × grunieri, commonly known as the hybrid saxifrage, is a flowering plant belonging to the Saxifragaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit and produces clusters of small, white or pinkish flowers. Native to the alpine regions of Europe, it thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and cool climates. The plant typically blooms in late spring to early summer, adding a delicate floral display to its natural habitat.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine plantings.
