Schmidelia rigida

Schmidelia rigida, commonly known as the rigid schmidelia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in early spring. Schmidelia rigida is adapted to dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun.
