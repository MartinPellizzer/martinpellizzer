Silene rouyana

Silene rouyana, commonly known as the Rouyan's campion, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its pink or white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Silene rouyana has elliptical leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem.

It is often found in mountainous regions and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
