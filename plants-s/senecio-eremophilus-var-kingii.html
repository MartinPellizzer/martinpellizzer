Senecio eremophilus var. kingii

Senecio eremophilus var. kingii, commonly known as King's ice plant, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a powdery white coating, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in clusters during the winter months. It is adapted to arid environments and thrives in full sun with well-draining soil.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal, it is popular in xeriscaping and rock gardens.
