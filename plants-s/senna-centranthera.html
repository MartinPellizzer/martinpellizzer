Senna centranthera

Senna centranthera, commonly known as the golden senna, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, pinnately compound leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in full sun.

Senna centranthera is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
