Sedum gombertii

Sedum gombertii, commonly known as the Gombert's stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe.

It is characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant has thick, water-storing leaves that help it survive in arid environments. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy choice for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Sedum gombertii is also valued for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions and its low maintenance requirements.
