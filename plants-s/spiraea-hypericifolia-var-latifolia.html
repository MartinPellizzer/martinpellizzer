Spiraea hypericifolia var. latifolia

Spiraea hypericifolia var. latifolia, commonly known as broad-leaved weeping spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It features arching stems that produce clusters of small, white flowers in early summer. The leaves are ovate to elliptic, with a smooth texture and a bluish-green hue. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
