Sophronitis milleri

Sophronitis milleri, commonly known as the Mexican violet, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are oval-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, while its flowers bloom in clusters.

Sophronitis milleri is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
