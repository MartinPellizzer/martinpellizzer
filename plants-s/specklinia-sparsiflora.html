Specklinia sparsiflora

Specklinia sparsiflora, commonly known as the sparse-flowered orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded forest environments. The plant features dark green, leathery leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Its flowers are arranged in a loose raceme, and the plant is known for its compact size and ornamental value.

Specklinia sparsiflora requires specific care, including high humidity and indirect light, to thrive in cultivation.
