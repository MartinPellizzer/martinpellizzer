Sideritis tragoriganum subsp. densispica

Sideritis tragoriganum subsp.

densispica, commonly known as the denseflowered ironweed, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

The plant is characterized by its upright stems and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in fine hairs.

Sideritis species, including this subspecies, are valued for their aromatic properties and traditional medicinal uses.
