Specklinia ornata

Specklinia ornata, commonly known as the velvet fern, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage that gives it its common name. The plant features small, clustered leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish or purple hue. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks.

Specklinia ornata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is popular in indoor gardening.
