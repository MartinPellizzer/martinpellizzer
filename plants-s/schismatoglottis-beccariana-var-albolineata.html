Schismatoglottis beccariana var. albolineata

Schismatoglottis beccariana var. albolineata, commonly known as the white-lined schismatoglottis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial herb with slender, upright stems and narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a dense spike, which are typically borne on the upper part of the stem. It prefers shaded, humid environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
