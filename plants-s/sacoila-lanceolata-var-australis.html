Sacoila lanceolata var. australis

Sacoila lanceolata var. australis, commonly known as the Australian lance-leaf sacoila, is a perennial herb native to southeastern Australia.

It features slender, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of greenish-white, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or grasslands.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
