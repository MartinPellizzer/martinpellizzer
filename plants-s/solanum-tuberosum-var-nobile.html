Solanum tuberosum var. nobile

Solanum tuberosum var. nobile, commonly known as the noble potato, is a botanical variant of the common potato.

It is characterized by its slightly smaller tubers and a more refined flavor profile compared to standard potato varieties. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, producing purple-tinged foliage and white or pale purple flowers. Its tubers are often used in gourmet cooking due to their delicate taste and texture.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value as well as its culinary versatility.
