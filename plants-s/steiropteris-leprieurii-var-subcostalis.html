Steiropteris leprieurii var. subcostalis

Steiropteris leprieurii var. subcostalis, commonly known as the narrow-ribbed tree fern, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds that can reach several meters in length. The fronds are typically dark green and have a distinctive, narrow rib pattern that gives the plant its scientific name. This variety thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical rainforests.

It is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant in botanical gardens.
