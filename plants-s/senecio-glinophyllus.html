Senecio glinophyllus

Senecio glinophyllus, commonly known as the silverleaf senecio, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
