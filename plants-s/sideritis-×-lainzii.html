Sideritis × lainzii

Sideritis × lainzii, commonly known as the hybrid catnip, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants known for their medicinal properties. The plant features square stems and opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically blue to purple in color and are clustered in whorls along the stems.

Sideritis × lainzii is valued for its aromatic foliage and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
