Sonchus fischeri

Sonchus fischeri, commonly known as Fischer's sowthistle, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and leafy stems. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and wastelands.
