Stoebe spiralis var. flavescens

Stoebe spiralis var. flavescens, commonly known as yellow sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to North America.

It features dense clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, wiry stems and hairy leaves that are often blue-green in color. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open, disturbed areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to harsh, arid environments.
