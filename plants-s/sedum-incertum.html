Sedum incertum

Sedum incertum, commonly known as the uncertain stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, clustered leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tint. The plant produces yellow flowers in late spring to early summer, which are borne on short, upright pedicels. Sedum incertum is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its adaptability and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking hardy, decorative plants.
