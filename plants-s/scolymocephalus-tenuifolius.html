Scolymocephalus tenuifolius

Scolymocephalus tenuifolius, commonly known as the slender-leaved scolymus, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, narrow leaves and small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun exposure. It is often found in coastal areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in some regions.
