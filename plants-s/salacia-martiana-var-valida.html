Salacia martiana var. valida

Salacia martiana var. valida, commonly known as the golden rain tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking golden-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it its common name. The plant has compound leaves with elongated leaflets and produces small, round fruits that turn from green to red as they mature. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
