Seriphidium santonicum subsp. patens

Seriphidium santonicum subsp.

patens, commonly known as the Patens Wormwood, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features slender, aromatic stems that are often branched and covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in compact clusters, typical of the Asteraceae family. Its leaves are lance-shaped, with a silvery-green hue due to dense pubescence.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.
