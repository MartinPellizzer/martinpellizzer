Sinobambusa seminuda

Sinobambusa seminuda, commonly known as the slender bamboo, is a species of grass family plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with pale green to yellowish flowers. Its leaves are narrow and long, with a smooth texture and a slightly pointed tip.

Sinobambusa seminuda is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its elegant appearance and adaptability to various soil conditions.
