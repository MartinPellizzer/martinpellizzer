Senecio penninervius

Senecio penninervius, commonly known as the narrow-veined senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces small yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which attract various pollinators.

Senecio penninervius is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun conditions.
