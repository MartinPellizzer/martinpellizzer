Septilia crenulata

Septilia crenulata, commonly known as the curlyleaf sedum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, dark green leaves with a distinctive wavy or curled edge. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Septilia crenulata is often grown as an ornamental plant in rock gardens or containers due to its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements.
