Spatholobus varians

Spatholobus varians, commonly known as the variable wisteria, is a climbing legume native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, pendulous flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces long, cylindrical pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
