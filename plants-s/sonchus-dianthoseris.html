Sonchus dianthoseris

Sonchus dianthoseris, commonly known as the purple leaf sowthistle, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of smooth, waxy, purple-tinged leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that are typically arranged in a composite head, typical of the dandelion family. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been introduced to other regions.

Sonchus dianthoseris is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats.
