Solanum persicifolium

Solanum persicifolium, commonly known as the peachleaf solanum, is a flowering plant in the nightshade family.

It is native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves that resemble peach leaves, hence its common name. It produces small, yellowish-white flowers that develop into berry-like fruits.

The plant is often grown as an ornamental due to its attractive foliage and unique appearance.
