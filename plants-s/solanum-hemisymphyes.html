Solanum hemisymphyes

Solanum hemisymphyes, commonly known as the hemisymphyes nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is native to parts of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale blue in color. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The fruit is a small, fleshy berry that turns red when mature, containing several seeds.
