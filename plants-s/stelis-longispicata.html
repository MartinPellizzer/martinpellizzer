Stelis longispicata

Stelis longispicata, commonly known as the long-spined stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with long, slender spurs, which are a distinctive botanical feature. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Stelis longispicata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique floral morphology.
