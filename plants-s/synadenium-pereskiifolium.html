Synadenium pereskiifolium

Synadenium pereskiifolium, commonly known as the Christmas star or Christmas cactus, is a succulent plant native to Brazil.

It features fleshy, ribbed stems that branch out into clusters of vibrant, star-shaped flowers. The plant is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that help it store water, adapting to its natural habitat. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Synadenium pereskiifolium is a popular ornamental plant, especially during the holiday season due to its seasonal blooming.
