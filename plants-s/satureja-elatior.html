Satureja elatior

Satureja elatior, commonly known as mountain savory, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or alpine environments. Its essential oils contain compounds like thymol and carvacrol, which contribute to its strong scent and medicinal properties.

Satureja elatior is valued in both culinary and traditional herbal applications.
