Sphaerosepalum alternifolius

Sphaerosepalum alternifolius, commonly known as the alternate-leaved globe orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its unique, spherical or globular sepals that give it its scientific name. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded, moist environments. Its leaves are alternate in arrangement, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

The flowers are usually small and clustered, adding to the plant's ornamental value.
