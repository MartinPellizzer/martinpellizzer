Schoberia hortensis

Schoberia hortensis, commonly known as the garden bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple, often with a white or yellow throat. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Schoberia hortensis is known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
