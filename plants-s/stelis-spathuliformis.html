Stelis spathuliformis

Stelis spathuliformis, commonly known as the broad-leafed stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its sympodial growth habit and pseudo-bulbs. The plant produces linear, broad leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of white to pale green hairs. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, with a distinctive spatulate-shaped lip.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia and is often found in shaded, moist environments.
