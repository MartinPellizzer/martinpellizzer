Senecio weinmannifolius f. quindiensis

Senecio weinmannifolius f. quindiensis, commonly known as the Theni's senecio, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming growth habit. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. It produces small yellow flowers arranged in a compact inflorescence, which bloom in spring.

This species is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-drained soil.
