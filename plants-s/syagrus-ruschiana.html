Syagrus ruschiana

Syagrus ruschiana, commonly known as the royal palm, is a tall, slender palm native to Brazil.

It features a slender trunk covered in smooth, fibrous bark and large, fan-shaped leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in diameter. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which develop into feather-like fruits. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Syagrus ruschiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in landscaping and as a symbol of resilience.
