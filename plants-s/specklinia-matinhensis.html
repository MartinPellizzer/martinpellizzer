Specklinia matinhensis

Specklinia matinhensis, commonly known as the Matinhos fern, is a member of the Polypodiaceae family and is native to Brazil.

It is a small, epiphytic fern that typically grows on trees in tropical rainforests. The plant has delicate, leathery fronds with a distinctive pattern of light and dark green. Its rhizomes are short and thick, helping it anchor to its host.

Specklinia matinhensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in humid, shaded environments.
