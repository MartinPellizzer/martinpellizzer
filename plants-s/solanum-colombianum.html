Solanum colombianum

Solanum colombianum, commonly known as the Colombian nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Colombia.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are ovate with a rough texture, and it produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters.

The fruit is a globose berry that changes color from green to red as it matures.
