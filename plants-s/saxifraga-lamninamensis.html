Saxifraga lamninamensis

Saxifraga lamninamensis, commonly known as the Laminamense saxifrage, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are usually white or pale pink, clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine layer of white hairs, which help to reduce water loss in harsh climates.

Saxifraga lamninamensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens and alpine displays.
