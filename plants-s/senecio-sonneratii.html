Senecio sonneratii

Senecio sonneratii, commonly known as the Sonnerat's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. The plant typically thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments. Its leaves are usually gray-green and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Senecio sonneratii is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
