Sophronitis tenebrosa

Sophronitis tenebrosa, commonly known as the dark sophronitis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers with a unique coloration. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are often pale green or white with a contrasting purple or reddish hue.

Sophronitis tenebrosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and rarity.
