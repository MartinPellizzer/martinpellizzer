Saxifraga helianthemifolia

Saxifraga helianthemifolia, commonly known as the rock rose saxifrage, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in full sun. The plant features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, often appearing in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are usually succulent, adapted to retain water in arid conditions.

Saxifraga helianthemifolia is often used in rock gardens due to its ornamental value and hardy nature.
