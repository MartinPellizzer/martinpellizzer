Stelis bialata

Stelis bialata, commonly known as the Bialata orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that thrives in humid tropical environments. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique inflorescence structure.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its beauty and rarity.
