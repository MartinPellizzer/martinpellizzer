Stichorkis pseudodisticha

Stichorkis pseudodisticha, commonly known as the slender-leaved stichorkis, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. The plant features linear, grass-like leaves that are often hairy, and it produces small, yellowish flowers in early spring. Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

It is often found in open habitats such as cliffs, slopes, and coastal areas.
