Stylidium brunonis

Stylidium brunonis, commonly known as the scarlet sun-orchid, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its striking, vividly colored flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and heaths. Its flowers are spurred and often have a distinctive shape that aids in pollination by specific insects.

Stylidium brunonis is known for its short-lived blooms and is often found in small colonies.
