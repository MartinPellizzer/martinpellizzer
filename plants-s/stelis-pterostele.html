Stelis pterostele

Stelis pterostele, commonly known as the winged orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated pseudobulbs and slender, wiry stems that give it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a raceme, which bloom during the rainy season. Stelis pterostele thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts seeking a distinctive and hardy specimen.
