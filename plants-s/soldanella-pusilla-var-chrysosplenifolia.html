Soldanella pusilla var. chrysosplenifolia

Soldanella pusilla var. chrysosplenifolia, commonly known as the golden spleenwort, is a small, herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

Due to its ornamental appeal and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in rock gardens or as a wildflower in suitable climates.
