Silene jeniseensis var. vegetior

Silene jeniseensis var. vegetior, commonly known as the Siberian campion, is a flowering plant native to the cold climates of eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces showy pink or purple flowers in late summer. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are typically lance-shaped. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or rocky habitats.

Silene jeniseensis var. vegetior is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native range.
