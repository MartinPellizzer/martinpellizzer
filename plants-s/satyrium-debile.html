Satyrium debile

Satyrium debile, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid characterized by its delicate, pale green to brownish leaves and slender, erect stems. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color, with a faintly fragrant scent. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Satyrium debile is known for its subtle beauty and is often overlooked due to its modest appearance.
