Sideritis cretica var. stricta

Sideritis cretica var. stricta, commonly known as Greek catnip, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants known for their medicinal properties. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, gray-green leaves with a distinctive minty aroma. Its flowers are typically pale blue to violet, arranged in whorls along the stems.

It is often used in traditional medicine for its calming and antispasmodic effects.
