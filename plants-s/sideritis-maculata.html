Sideritis maculata

Sideritis maculata, commonly known as spotted woundwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its hairy stems and small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are often covered in spots, giving it its common name.

Sideritis maculata is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
