Swertia centrostemma

Swertia centrostemma, commonly known as the blue gentian, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Swertia centrostemma is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
