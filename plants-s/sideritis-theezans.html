Sideritis theezans

Sideritis theezans, commonly known as Greek catnip, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, gray-green leaves with a distinctive minty scent. Its flowers are typically pale blue or purple, arranged in whorls along the stem.

Sideritis theezans is often grown for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
