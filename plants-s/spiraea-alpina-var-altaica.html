Spiraea alpina var. altaica

Spiraea alpina var. altaica, commonly known as the alpine spiraea, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of central Asia.

It typically grows in alpine and subalpine environments, where it thrives in well-drained, rocky soils. The plant is characterized by its clusters of small, white to pink flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are elliptical and dark green, providing a striking contrast to the delicate flowers.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand harsh climatic conditions.
