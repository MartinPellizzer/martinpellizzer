Smilax riparia var. ussuriensis

Smilax riparia var. ussuriensis, commonly known as the Ussuri arrowhead, is a species of climbing shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its arrowhead-shaped leaves and produces yellowish-green flowers that develop into red berries. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. It has a robust root system that allows it to anchor itself in soil and absorb nutrients efficiently.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the understory vegetation in its native habitats.
