Sphacele pinetorum f. flaviflora

Sphacele pinetorum f. flaviflora, commonly known as the yellow-flowered pine sphacele, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with narrow, blade-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that bear yellowish to orange flowers, which are typically arranged in spikelets. This fern-like plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
