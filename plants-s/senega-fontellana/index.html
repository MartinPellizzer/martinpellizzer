Senega Fontellana

The senega fontellana is a rare and enigmatic plant native to the mountainous regions of south-eastern europe. it belongs to the apiaceae family, known for its aromatic and often medicinal properties. its delicate white flowers bloom in late spring, adding a unique beauty to its natural habitat. despite its striking appearance, the plant remains poorly understood by botanists due to its elusive nature and limited distribution. conservation efforts are underway to protect this endangered species from habitat loss and climate change.
