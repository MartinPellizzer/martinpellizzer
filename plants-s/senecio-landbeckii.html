Senecio landbeckii

Senecio landbeckii, commonly known as the Landbeck's groundsels, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small yellow flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open, sunny environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
