Setaria onurus f. ramulosa

Setaria onurus f. ramulosa, commonly known as the slender false panicgrass, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences. Its morphology makes it distinguishable from other species within the Setaria genus.
