Silene hupehensis var. pubescens

Silene hupehensis var. pubescens, commonly known as the hairy Chinese catchfly, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or shady areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
