Spiranthes macrostachya

Spiranthes macrostachya, commonly known as the showy orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its distinctive spiral arrangement of flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wetlands. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a unique, twisted structure.
