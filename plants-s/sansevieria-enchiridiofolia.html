Sansevieria enchiridiofolia

Sansevieria enchiridiofolia, commonly known as the "Coral Necklace" plant, is a species of snake plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender leaves that are typically green with a pink or red tint along the edges. The plant has a robust root system and is highly drought-tolerant, making it suitable for indoor cultivation. It produces small, white flowers in clusters during the growing season.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for ornamental purposes.
