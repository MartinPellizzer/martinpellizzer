Sphaerostema pyrifolium

Sphaerostema pyrifolium, commonly known as the pineapple tree, is a unique plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, clustered inflorescence that resembles a pineapple, hence its common name. The plant features a short, woody stem with multiple leaves radiating from the base. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically white or pale yellow, and it produces a fleshy, edible fruit.

Sphaerostema pyrifolium is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of edible fruit in tropical regions.
