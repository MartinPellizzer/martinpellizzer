Steiroglossa rigidula

Steiroglossa rigidula, commonly known as the rigid lily.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. The plant features upright, branching stems covered in stiff, hairy leaves. Its flowers are typically pink or purple, with showy, spurred petals.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.
