Staurogyne siamensis

Staurogyne siamensis, commonly known as Siam's starwort, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in slow-moving freshwater environments such as ponds and streams. The plant has finely divided, ribbon-like leaves that are often submerged, with some floating on the water surface. It produces small, white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences.

Staurogyne siamensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting aquatic biodiversity.
