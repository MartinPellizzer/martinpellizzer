Sinningia defoliata

Sinningia defoliata, commonly known as the fairy lantern, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is prized for its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. Its flowers bloom in late summer to early autumn, attracting pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees.

Sinningia defoliata is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening and rock gardens.
