Saccolabium pechei

Saccolabium pechei, commonly known as the Mexican legume or pechei bean, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellowish-white flowers. Its seeds are enclosed in a pod and are often used in traditional medicine.

Saccolabium pechei is valued for its ecological role and potential medicinal properties.
