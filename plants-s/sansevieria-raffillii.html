Sansevieria raffillii

Sansevieria raffillii, commonly known as the "Raffill's Snake Plant," is a species native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with striking white or silver variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-yellow in color. It is highly drought-tolerant and thrives in low-light conditions, making it an excellent choice for indoor gardening.

Its air-purifying properties also make it a popular houseplant.
