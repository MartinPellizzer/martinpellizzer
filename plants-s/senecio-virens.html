Senecio virens

Senecio virens, commonly known as Greenleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sandy or rocky habitats and is often found in coastal areas. It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring and summer months.

Senecio virens is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
