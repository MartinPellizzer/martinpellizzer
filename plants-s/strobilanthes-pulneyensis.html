Strobilanthes pulneyensis

Strobilanthes pulneyensis, commonly known as the Pulney's flameflower, is a herbaceous plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is characterized by its striking inflorescence, which consists of clusters of bright red, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest understories and prefers well-drained, acidic soils.
