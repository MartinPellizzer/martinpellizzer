Spermacoce baurii

Spermacoce baurii, commonly known as the African catchfly, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters at the ends of its stems. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

Spermacoce baurii is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides, thriving in full sun to partial shade.
