Sisymbrium dubium

Sisymbrium dubium, commonly known as the yellow rocket, is an annual herbaceous plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and pinnately divided leaves. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas. It produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds.

Sisymbrium dubium is often considered a weed but has historical uses in traditional medicine.
