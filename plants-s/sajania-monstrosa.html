Sajania monstrosa

Sajania monstrosa, commonly known as the giant spider lily, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers with prominent red or orange stamens that resemble spider legs, giving it its common name. The plant produces broad, arrow-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, contributing to its dramatic appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in the undergrowth of tropical forests.

Sajania monstrosa is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with the right conditions.
