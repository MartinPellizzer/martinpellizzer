Solenangis cyclochila

Solenangis cyclochila, commonly known as the cyclochila orchid, is a species of terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically features a sympodial growth habit, with a rhizome that produces multiple shoots. Its leaves are usually linear and arranged in a rosette pattern, and the flowers are borne on tall, erect inflorescences.

Solenangis cyclochila is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
