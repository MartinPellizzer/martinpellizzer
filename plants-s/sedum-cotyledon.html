Sedum cotyledon

Sedum cotyledon, commonly known as the Jade Plant or Crassula ovata, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or red during the spring and summer months. Its compact growth habit makes it an ideal choice for indoor gardening and container planting.

Sedum cotyledon is also popular for its air-purifying properties and low maintenance requirements.
