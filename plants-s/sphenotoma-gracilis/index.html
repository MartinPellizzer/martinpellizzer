Sphenotoma Gracilis

Sphenotoma gracilis is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high altitudes of South America. It is known for its slender stems and striking, pale pink blooms that appear during the short growing season. This plant thrives in cool, moist environments and requires careful cultivation to survive. Due to its limited habitat and slow growth, it is considered endangered by conservationists. Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it a subject of interest for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike.
