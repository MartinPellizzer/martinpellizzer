Strelitzia ovata

Strelitzia ovata, commonly known as the White Bird of Paradise, is a striking tropical plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, white, bird-like flowers that are highly ornamental and often used in floral arrangements. The plant has broad, dark green, strap-shaped leaves that grow in a fan-like pattern. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil and bright, indirect light.

Strelitzia ovata is a member of the ginger family (Zingiberaceae) and is prized for its unique and exotic appearance.
