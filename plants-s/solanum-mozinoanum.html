Solanum mozinoanum

Solanum mozinoanum, commonly known as the Mozambique nightshade, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in woodland and savanna habitats. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by berries that range in color from green to red. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Solanum mozinoanum is part of the nightshade family, which includes many plants with medicinal and toxic properties.
