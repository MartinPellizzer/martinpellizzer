Stemodia hypericifolia

Stemodia hypericifolia, commonly known as the coastal stemodia, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils, often in areas with high salt exposure. The plant has small, narrow leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-yellow, and they appear in clusters at the ends of the branches.

It is adapted to withstand harsh coastal conditions, including strong winds and salt spray.
