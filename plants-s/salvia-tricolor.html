Salvia tricolor

Salvia tricolor, commonly known as the tri-color sage, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features striking, three-toned flowers that range from blue to violet to pink, creating a vibrant display. The plant has lance-shaped, gray-green leaves with a slightly fuzzy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Salvia tricolor is also valued for its aromatic properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
