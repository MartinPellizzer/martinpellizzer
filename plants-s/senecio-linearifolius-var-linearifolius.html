Senecio linearifolius var. linearifolius

Senecio linearifolius var. linearifolius, commonly known as the linearleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb with slender, upright stems and linear, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a cluster at the ends of the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its unique foliage and delicate blooms make it an attractive addition to native plant collections.
