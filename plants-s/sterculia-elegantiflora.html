Sterculia elegantiflora

Sterculia elegantiflora, commonly known as the elegant flame tree, is a tropical tree native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, flame-like inflorescences that display vivid red and orange hues. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous flat, winged seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental species.

Its rapid growth and showy flowers make it a valuable addition to gardens and urban landscapes.
