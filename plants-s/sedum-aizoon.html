Sedum aizoon

Sedum aizoon, commonly known as the stonecrop or blue stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or purple during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Sedum aizoon is drought-tolerant and requires minimal maintenance, making it ideal for arid environments.
