Sansevieria pinguicula f. disticha

Sansevieria pinguicula f. disticha, commonly known as the Distich Sansevieria, is a unique cultivar of the Sansevieria genus native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit and narrow, dark green leaves with a subtle blueish tinge. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers on slender stalks, which are typically followed by red berries. It is a low-maintenance plant that thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its compact size and striking foliage make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and rock gardens.
