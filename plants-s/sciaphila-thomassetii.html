Sciaphila thomassetii

Sciaphila thomassetii, commonly known as the ghost orchid, is a rare and enigmatic flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

The plant is characterized by its delicate, white, spider-like flowers that bloom in the shade of the forest canopy. Sciaphila thomassetii requires high humidity and indirect light to thrive, making it a challenging species to cultivate.
