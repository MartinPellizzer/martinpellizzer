Skutchia

Skutchia, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a popular ornamental plant native to South America.

It belongs to the genus Schlumbergera and is characterized by its segmented, leaf-like stems that form a bushy shape. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, pink, and white during the winter months, typically around Christmas time. Skutchia thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ease of care and festive blooms.
