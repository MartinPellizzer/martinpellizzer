Senecio gardneri

Senecio gardneri, commonly known as Gardner's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northwestern Mexico.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in arid or semi-arid environments.
