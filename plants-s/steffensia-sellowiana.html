Steffensia sellowiana

Steffensia sellowiana, commonly known as the Sellow's steppe grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. The plant has slender, erect stems that are often branched and bear small, narrow leaves. Its flowers are clustered in dense, spike-like inflorescences and are usually pink or purple in color.

Steffensia sellowiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
