Senecio schizopetalus

Senecio schizopetalus, commonly known as the African marguerite, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. Senecio schizopetalus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and resilience.
