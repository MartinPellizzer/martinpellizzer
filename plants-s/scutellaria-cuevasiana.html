Scutellaria cuevasiana

Scutellaria cuevasiana, commonly known as the Cueva's blue skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are typically opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

Scutellaria cuevasiana is often found in rocky or forested habitats at high elevations.
