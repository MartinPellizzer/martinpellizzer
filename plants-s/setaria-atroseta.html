Setaria atroseta

Setaria atroseta, commonly known as blackseta grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to various regions of Africa and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, pale green to brownish flowers. It is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

Setaria atroseta is a hardy annual grass that is sometimes used as a forage crop in arid regions.
