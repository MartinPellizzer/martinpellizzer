Swainsona grandiflora

Swainsona grandiflora, commonly known as the giant hyacinth bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is a perennial shrub that can grow up to 3 meters in height, featuring compound leaves and showy, violet to purple flowers. The plant produces pods that contain edible seeds, which are often used in traditional cuisines. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Swainsona grandiflora is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, which benefits soil fertility.
