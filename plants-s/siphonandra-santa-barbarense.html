Siphonandra santa-barbarense

Siphonandra santa-barbarense, commonly known as the Santa Barbara siphonandra, is a rare shrub native to the coastal regions of Southern California.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a bluish-gray hue, adapted to the arid and salty coastal environment. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and early summer. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and ability to thrive in well-drained, sandy soils.
