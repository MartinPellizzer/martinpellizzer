Swertia lihengiana

Swertia lihengiana, commonly known as the Liheng gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes. Its leaves are lanceolate and arranged alternately along the stem.

Swertia lihengiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional medicinal uses in Chinese herbal medicine.
