Stelis asperrima

Stelis asperrima, commonly known as the rough stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and hairy, often reddish stems. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Stelis asperrima is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
