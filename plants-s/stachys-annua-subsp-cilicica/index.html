Stachys Annua Subsp. Cilicica

Stachys annua subsp. cilicica is a lesser-known yet fascinating member of the mint family known for its unique botanical characteristics. Native to the mountainous regions of Turkey and Cyprus, this subspecies thrives in rocky and arid environments. Its slender stems and small, hairy leaves distinguish it from other members of the genus. Despite its modest appearance, it plays an important role in local ecosystems. This plant offers valuable insights into the adaptability of flora in challenging climates.
