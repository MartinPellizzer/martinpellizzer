Scillopsis rosea

Scillopsis rosea, commonly known as the pink scillopsis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features upright, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces clusters of showy, pinkish-white flowers in early to mid-summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Scillopsis rosea is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
