Satyrium yunnanense

Satyrium yunnanense, commonly known as the Yunnan satyrium, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in mountainous regions. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, while its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white.
