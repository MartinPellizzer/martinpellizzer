Sarracenia × exculta

Sarracenia × exculta, commonly known as the hybrid pitcher plant, is a fascinating carnivorous plant native to North America.

It is a hybrid between Sarracenia leucophylla and Sarracenia rubra, resulting in a unique combination of traits from both parent species. The plant produces striking, tubular pitcher-shaped leaves that secrete a digestive enzyme-laden liquid to trap and digest insects. Its vibrant red and green coloration, along with intricate patterns, makes it visually appealing to plant enthusiasts.

Sarracenia × exculta thrives in wet, acidic soils and requires a humid, sunny environment to flourish.
