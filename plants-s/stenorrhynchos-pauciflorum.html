Stenorrhynchos pauciflorum

Stenorrhynchos pauciflorum, commonly known as the rare orchid of the genus Stenorrhynchos, is a terrestrial orchid native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest undergrowth, where it relies on mycorrhizal fungi for nutrient uptake. Its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements make it a vulnerable species.
