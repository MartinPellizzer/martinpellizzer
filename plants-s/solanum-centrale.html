Solanum centrale

Solanum centrale, commonly known as the African nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, white or purple flowers that bloom in clusters. Its berries are initially green and turn red when mature, containing seeds within a fleshy pulp.

Solanum centrale belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like tomatoes and potatoes.
