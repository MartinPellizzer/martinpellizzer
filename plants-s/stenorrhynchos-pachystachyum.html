Stenorrhynchos pachystachyum

Stenorrhynchos pachystachyum, commonly known as the giant bamboo orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, showy flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces striking white or pale pink flowers with intricate patterns, often resembling bamboo in appearance. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This ornamental plant is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
