Sesbania exaltata

Sesbania exaltata, commonly known as the tall cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, erect growth habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces elongated, yellow flowers followed by pods containing edible seeds. It is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, improving soil fertility.

Sesbania exaltata is often used in agroforestry and as a forage crop due to its rapid growth and nutritional value.
