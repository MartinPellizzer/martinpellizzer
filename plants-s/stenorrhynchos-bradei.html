Stenorrhynchos bradei

Stenorrhynchos bradei, commonly known as the Brade's spider orchid, is a species of orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, spider-like appearance, with long, slender petals and a prominent, narrow column. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy habitats, often in areas with good drainage. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insect species.

Stenorrhynchos bradei is a rare and endangered species, making conservation efforts crucial for its survival.
