Solanum verecundum

Solanum verecundum, commonly known as the shy plant, is a member of the nightshade family (Solanaceae).

It is characterized by its small, white or pale green flowers and oval-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its fruit is a small, yellowish berry that ripens in late summer.

Solanum verecundum is noted for its low-growing habit and is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
