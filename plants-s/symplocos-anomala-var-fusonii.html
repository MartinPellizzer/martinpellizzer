Symplocos anomala var. fusonii

Symplocos anomala var. fusonii, commonly known as the broad-leafed evergreen, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features elliptical to ovate leaves with a smooth surface and a glossy appearance. Its flowers are small, white, and clustered in inflorescences, while the fruit is a drupe with a single seed.

It is often found in forest understories and is valued for its ornamental qualities and potential medicinal uses.
