Sarcochilus parviflorus

Sarcochilus parviflorus, commonly known as the small-flowered helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its compact, hairy pseudobulbs and narrow, strap-like leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive helmet-shaped labellum. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest floors.

Sarcochilus parviflorus is a rare and protected species, reflecting its ecological significance in New Zealand's flora.
