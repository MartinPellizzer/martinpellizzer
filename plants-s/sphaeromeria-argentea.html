Sphaeromeria argentea

Sphaeromeria argentea, commonly known as silverleaf sphaeromeria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It is a hardy, drought-tolerant species often grown as an ornamental plant.
