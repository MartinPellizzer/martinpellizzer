Stemodia glabra

Stemodia glabra, commonly known as the smooth stemodia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It is a member of the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, smooth stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale pink to white in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.
