Simarouba opaca

Simarouba opaca, commonly known as yellow mangrove, is a tropical tree native to the Caribbean and northern South America.

It is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by orange-red fruits that contain a single seed. It thrives in coastal areas and is often found in brackish water environments.

Its ecological importance includes providing habitat for various marine and terrestrial species.
