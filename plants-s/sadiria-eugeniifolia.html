Sadiria eugeniifolia

Sadiria eugeniifolia, commonly known as the Christmas bell, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and glossy, providing a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms.

Sadiria eugeniifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a potted plant.
