Scrophularia duriaei

Scrophularia duriaei, commonly known as the Sierra Nevada scrophularia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of California and Nevada.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils at high elevations. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple in color, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture, aiding in moisture retention in its arid habitat.

Scrophularia duriaei is part of the Scrophulariaceae family, known for its diverse and often ornamental members.
