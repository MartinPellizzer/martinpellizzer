Spananthe peruviana

Spananthe peruviana, commonly known as the Peruvian orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, fragrant blossoms. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in cloud forests. Its leaves are broad and leathery, while its roots are fibrous and adapted to nutrient-poor soils.

Spananthe peruviana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
