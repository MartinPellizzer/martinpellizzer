Solanum × ajanhuiri var. yari

Solanum × ajanhuiri var. yari, commonly known as the Yari tomato, is a hybrid species native to the Andean region of South America.

It is characterized by its small to medium-sized fruits, typically ranging from 50 to 100 grams, with a bright red to orange color when mature. The plant is an annual herb with hairy stems and alternate leaves that are ovate to lanceolate in shape. It produces yellow flowers that develop into berries containing numerous seeds.

This variety is valued for its flavor and is often used in local cuisines and traditional medicine.
