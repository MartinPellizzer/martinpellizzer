Streptocarpus ionanthus subsp. grandifolius

Streptocarpus ionanthus subsp.

grandifolius, commonly known as the large-leaved streptocarpus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its broad, velvety leaves that are typically dark green and glossy.

The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of blue, violet, or white, which bloom in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in a variety of indoor and outdoor settings.
