Sideritis pycnostachys

Sideritis pycnostachys, commonly known as the dense-stemmed ironwort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit, hairy stems, and small, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of violet or blue. It thrives in rocky or limestone soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used traditionally in herbal remedies for its calming and antispasmodic effects.
