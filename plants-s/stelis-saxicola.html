Stelis saxicola

Stelis saxicola, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic or lithophytic plant, typically growing on rocks or tree trunks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with a short rhizome and a cluster of fleshy, oval pseudobulbs. Its flowers are usually small, fragrant, and come in various colors, including white, yellow, and green.

Stelis saxicola is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in terrariums or as a houseplant.
