Senecio columbaria

Senecio columbaria, commonly known as the columnar senecio, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, unbranched stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are typically narrow and fleshy, adapted to store water in arid environments.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
