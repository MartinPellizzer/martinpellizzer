Spathiphyllum monachinoi

Spathiphyllum monachinoi, commonly known as the Monachino's peace lily, is a tropical evergreen plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its dark green, waxy, and leathery leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces striking white spathes that enclose a cluster of small, yellowish-green flowers. It thrives in low-light conditions and requires consistent moisture and moderate humidity.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
