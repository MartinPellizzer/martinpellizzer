Senecio candelariae

Senecio candelariae, commonly known as the Candelabra Senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its branched stems and dense clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant has fleshy, narrow leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps it retain water in arid environments. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
