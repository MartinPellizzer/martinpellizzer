Silene aucheriana f. stenophylla

Silene aucheriana f. stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leafed campion, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils in arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and often hairy, adapted to reduce water loss in harsh conditions.

This subspecies is native to regions of southern Europe and western Asia.
