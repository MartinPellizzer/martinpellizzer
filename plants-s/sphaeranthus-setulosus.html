Sphaeranthus setulosus

Sphaeranthus setulosus, commonly known as the roughhead tickseed, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States and northern Mexico.

Sphaeranthus setulosus is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in arid conditions.
