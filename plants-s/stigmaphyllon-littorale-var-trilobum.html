Stigmaphyllon littorale var. trilobum

Stigmaphyllon littorale var. trilobum, commonly known as the coastal stigmaphyllon, is a species native to coastal regions of South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils along the shoreline.
