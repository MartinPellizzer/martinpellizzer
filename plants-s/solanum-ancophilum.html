Solanum ancophilum

Solanum ancophilum, commonly known as the African nightshade, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 meter. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by orange-red berries. Its leaves are ovate with a hairy texture, and the plant is often found in grassy areas and along roadsides.

Solanum ancophilum is part of the nightshade family, which includes many economically important crops and ornamental plants.
