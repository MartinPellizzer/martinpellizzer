Searsia humpatensis f. subglabra

Searsia humpatensis f. subglabra, commonly known as the smooth-leaved humpatensis, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Rosaceae and is characterized by its smooth, glabrous leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and seasonal fruit.
