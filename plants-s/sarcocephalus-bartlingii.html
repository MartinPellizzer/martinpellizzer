Sarcocephalus bartlingii

Sarcocephalus bartlingii, commonly known as the African wild coffee, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by red, fleshy berries. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a slightly serrated margin.
