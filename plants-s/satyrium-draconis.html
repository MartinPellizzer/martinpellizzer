Satyrium draconis

Satyrium draconis, commonly known as the dragon's tail orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its unique, spiraling inflorescence that resembles a dragon's tail, hence its common name. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in sunny to partially shaded environments. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinct lip that is often darker.

Satyrium draconis is a short-lived perennial, often found in dry, calcareous habitats.
