Stobaea rigida

Stobaea rigida, commonly known as the rigid stobaea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Stobaea rigida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
