Stachys hantamensis

Stachys hantamensis, commonly known as the Hantam betony, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and spikes of small, tubular flowers. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid conditions.

Stachys hantamensis is often found in the Western Cape region of South Africa.
