Scutellaria macra

Scutellaria macra, commonly known as the tall skullcap, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants such as mint and sage. The plant features square stems, opposite leaves, and spikes of blue to violet flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and thrives in partial to full shade.

Scutellaria macra is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
