Senecio cymbalarioides subsp. moresbiensis

Senecio cymbalarioides subsp.

moresbiensis, commonly known as the Moresby's ice plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and produces small, yellow flowers in clusters.

The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a valuable addition to xeriscaped landscapes.
