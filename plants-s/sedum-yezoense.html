Sedum yezoense

Sedum yezoense, commonly known as the Yezo stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Japan and parts of Russia.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Sedum yezoense is drought-tolerant and requires minimal maintenance once established.
