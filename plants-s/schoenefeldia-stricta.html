Schoenefeldia stricta

Schoenefeldia stricta, commonly known as the narrowleaf star-of-Bethlehem, is a herbaceous perennial native to South Africa.

It features slender, upright stems and small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique addition to Mediterranean-style gardens.
