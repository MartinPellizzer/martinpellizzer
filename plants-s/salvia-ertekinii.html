Salvia ertekinii

Salvia ertekinii, commonly known as the Turkish sage, is a perennial herb native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid environments. Its flowers are usually violet or purple, arranged in whorled clusters, and bloom during the summer months.

Salvia ertekinii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and visual interest.
