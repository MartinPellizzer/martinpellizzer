Salvia pseudochia

Salvia pseudochia, commonly known as false chia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in fine hairs.

Salvia pseudochia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
