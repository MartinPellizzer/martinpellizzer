Streptocarpus prostratus var. prostratus

Streptocarpus prostratus var. prostratus, commonly known as the prostrate streptocarpus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing, trailing plant with fleshy, dark green leaves that form a dense mat. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple and blue, which bloom in clusters at the leaf axils. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

This variety is popular among succulent and tropical plant enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of care.
