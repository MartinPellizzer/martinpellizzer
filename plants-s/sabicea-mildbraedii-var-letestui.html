Sabicea mildbraedii var. letestui

Sabicea mildbraedii var. letestui, commonly known as the Letestu's sabicea, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Leguminosae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, typical of many legumes. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
