Strobilanthes zeylanica

Strobilanthes zeylanica, commonly known as the Ceylon starflower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along streams. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a smooth texture.
