Sorbus erzincanica

Sorbus erzincanica, commonly known as the Erzincan whitebeams, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to Turkey and is characterized by its small, white flowers that appear in clusters. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible for birds and other wildlife. It is a deciduous tree or shrub that thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
