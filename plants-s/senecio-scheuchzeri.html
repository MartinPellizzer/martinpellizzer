Senecio scheuchzeri

Senecio scheuchzeri, commonly known as the Alpine groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the high mountain regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves and yellow composite flowers. The plant thrives in alpine and subalpine environments, often growing at elevations above 2,000 meters. It has a compact growth habit and is adapted to cold, rocky soils.
