Senecio taitungensis

Senecio taitungensis, commonly known as Taitung groundsel, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or open habitats, showcasing its adaptability to diverse environments. Its leaves are usually gray-green with a fuzzy texture, and it produces small yellow flowers in clusters.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local pollinators.
