Stelis carnosula var. concolor

Stelis carnosula var. concolor, commonly known as the pink-stemmed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic orchid that typically grows on tree trunks or branches in humid tropical forests. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. Its flowers are small, clustered, and often have a pale pink or white coloration.

The varietal name 'concolor' refers to the uniform coloration of its stems and leaves.
