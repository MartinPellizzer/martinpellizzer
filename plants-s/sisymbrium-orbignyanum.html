Sisymbrium orbignyanum

Sisymbrium orbignyanum, commonly known as the orbignya mustard, is an annual herbaceous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other mustard plants. The plant features slender stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are alternate, pinnately divided, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Sisymbrium orbignyanum is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
