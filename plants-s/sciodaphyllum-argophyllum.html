Sciodaphyllum argophyllum

Sciodaphyllum argophyllum, commonly known as the silverleaf fern, is a species of fern native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its delicate, silver-gray foliage that gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest understories. Its fronds are usually compound, with a feathery appearance that contributes to its ornamental value.

Sciodaphyllum argophyllum plays a role in maintaining ecological balance by providing habitat and shelter for small organisms.
