Stipellaria villosa

Stipellaria villosa, commonly known as the hairy stipple vine, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and distinctive hairy stems. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy areas.

Stipellaria villosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
