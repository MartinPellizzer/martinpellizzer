Santolina rosmarinifolia

Santolina rosmarinifolia, commonly known as silver santolina, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features compact, silver-gray foliage that is aromatic and often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in late spring to early summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its hardy nature and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
