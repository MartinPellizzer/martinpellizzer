Senecio lanuriensis

Senecio lanuriensis, commonly known as the lanuriensis groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun exposure. It produces small yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months, and its leaves are usually hairy and lance-shaped.

Senecio lanuriensis is often used in rock gardens due to its hardy nature and ornamental appeal.
