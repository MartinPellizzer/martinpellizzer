Sibangea arborescens

Sibangea arborescens, commonly known as the flame tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has a shrub or small tree form, with smooth, greyish bark and opposite, simple leaves. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing along roadsides and in disturbed areas.

Sibangea arborescens is valued for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
