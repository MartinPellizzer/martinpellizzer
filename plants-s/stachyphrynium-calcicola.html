Stachyphrynium calcicola

Stachyphrynium calcicola, commonly known as the limestone lily, is a rare and specialized plant native to the limestone regions of southern Africa.

Its unique morphology and restricted distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists studying plant adaptation.
