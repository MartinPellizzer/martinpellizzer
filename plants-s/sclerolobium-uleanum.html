Sclerolobium uleanum

Sclerolobium uleanum, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a straight, cylindrical trunk with a smooth, greyish-brown bark. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

Sclerolobium uleanum is valued for its hard, durable wood and is often used in construction and furniture-making.
