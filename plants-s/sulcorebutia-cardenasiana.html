Sulcorebutia cardenasiana

Sulcorebutia cardenasiana, commonly known as the Cardenasian bulbous cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is a compact, low-growing plant with a short, thick stem and a cluster of fleshy, ribbed tubers at its base. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the spring and summer months. Its succulent leaves are adapted to store water, allowing it to thrive in dry conditions.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience.
