Scrophularia serrulata

Scrophularia serrulata, commonly known as the serrated scrophularia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Scrophulariaceae.

It is native to regions of Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in woodland and grassland habitats. The plant features upright stems with opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often serrated along the edges. Its flowers are typically pink or purple, arranged in clusters, and bloom in the summer months.

Scrophularia serrulata is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
