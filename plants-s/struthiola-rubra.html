Struthiola rubra

Struthiola rubra, commonly known as the red struthiola, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to deep red. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, adapting to its arid environment.

Struthiola rubra is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
