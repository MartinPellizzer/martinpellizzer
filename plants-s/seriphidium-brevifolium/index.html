Seriphidium Brevifolium

Seriphidium brevifolium is a hardy flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia. It thrives in harsh environments with limited water and extreme temperatures. Known for its compact growth and silvery foliage, it is well adapted to survive in challenging climates. This plant is often found in rocky soils and dry grasslands. Its resilience makes it an important species for ecological studies and desert landscaping.
