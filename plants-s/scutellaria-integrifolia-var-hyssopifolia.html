Scutellaria integrifolia var. hyssopifolia

Scutellaria integrifolia var. hyssopifolia, commonly known as blue skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and produces spikes of blue or purple flowers. Its genus, Scutellaria, is characterized by its distinctive hooded flowers and erect growth habit.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in herbal medicine.
