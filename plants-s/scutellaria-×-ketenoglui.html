Scutellaria × ketenoglui

Scutellaria × ketenoglui, commonly known as the hybrid skullcap, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the mint family, Lamiaceae.

It is characterized by its square, hairy stems and opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often hairy on both surfaces. The plant produces showy blue to violet flowers in whorled clusters at the leaf axils, which bloom from late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal potential, it is cultivated in gardens and studied for its bioactive compounds.
