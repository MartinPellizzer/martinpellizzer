Scilla barnardia

Scilla barnardia, commonly known as the Cape Lily, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in early spring, often preceding the leaves. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically evergreen, providing visual interest throughout the year. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Scilla barnardia is valued for its ornamental beauty and relatively low maintenance requirements.
