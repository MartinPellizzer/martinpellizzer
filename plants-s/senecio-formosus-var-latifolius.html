Senecio formosus var. latifolius

Senecio formosus var. latifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces yellow daisy-like flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
