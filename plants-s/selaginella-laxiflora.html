Selaginella laxiflora

Selaginella laxiflora, commonly known as the slender spike moss, is a species of spore-bearing plant belonging to the family Selaginellaceae.

It is a small, herbaceous plant that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant has a distinctive appearance with its delicate, thread-like leaves and small, clustered sporangia. It is characterized by its ability to survive in low-light conditions and its unique reproductive structures.

Selaginella laxiflora plays a role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for various microorganisms and invertebrates.
