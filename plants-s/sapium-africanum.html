Sapium africanum

Sapium africanum, commonly known as African soap tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and alternate, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish flowers followed by orange-red, fleshy fruits that contain seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in some regions for its soapy sap.
