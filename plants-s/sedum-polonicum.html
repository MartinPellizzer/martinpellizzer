Sedum polonicum

Sedum polonicum, commonly known as Polish stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features fleshy, upright stems and leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Sedum polonicum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in arid environments.
