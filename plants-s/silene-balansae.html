Silene balansae

Silene balansae, commonly known as the Balansae campion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous annual or biennial species that typically grows in rocky or arid environments. The plant features simple, linear leaves and produces small, pink or white flowers in a raceme. It is known for its ability to thrive in poor soils and its resilience to drought conditions.

Silene balansae is also valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens and its ecological role in supporting pollinators.
