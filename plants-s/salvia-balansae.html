Salvia balansae

Salvia balansae, commonly known as the Mexican bush sage, is a perennial shrub native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features narrow, gray-green leaves and produces spikes of violet to purple flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Salvia balansae is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
