Solanum sordidescens

Solanum sordidescens, commonly known as the dirty nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a fleshy berry that turns red when mature.

Like other members of the nightshade family, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
