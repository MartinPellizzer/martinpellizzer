Stipagrostis Plumosa Var. Intermedia

Intermediate feather grass, scientifically known as Stipagrostis plumosa var. intermedia, is a striking ornamental grass native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa and the Middle East. Its unique appearance features slender, feathery plumes that sway gracefully in the wind, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, requiring minimal water once established. Its resilience and low maintenance make it ideal for xeriscaping and sustainable gardening practices. With its elegant form and adaptability, Intermediate feather grass adds both beauty and functionality to any outdoor space.
