Stylurus caleyi

Stylurus caleyi, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, velvety flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often have a waxy texture, which helps in water retention.

Stylurus caleyi is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
