Statice plantaginea var. zaianica

Statice plantaginea var. zaianica, commonly known as the Zaianica statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, long-lasting flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant produces small, clustered blooms on slender stems, making it a popular choice for dried floral arrangements. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and suitable for xeriscaping in arid climates.
