Spodiopogon yuexiensis

Spodiopogon yuexiensis, commonly known as the Yuxi spikerush, is a perennial grass species native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical inflorescence that resembles a spike, with small, spikelet-like flowers. The plant typically grows in wetland habitats, such as marshes and along rivers, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a bluish-green hue.

Spodiopogon yuexiensis plays an important ecological role in maintaining wetland biodiversity and soil stability.
