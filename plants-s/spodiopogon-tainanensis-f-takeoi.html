Spodiopogon tainanensis f. takeoi

Spodiopogon tainanensis f. takeoi, commonly known as the Tainan sedge, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, linear leaves and small, clustered inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests or wetlands. Its botanical features include a rhizomatous root system and spikelets that bear three stamens and a single pistil.

It is native to southern China and Taiwan, where it thrives in humid climates.
