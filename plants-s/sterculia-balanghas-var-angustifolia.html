Sterculia balanghas var. angustifolia

Sterculia balanghas var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf sterculia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves that are often arranged alternately along the stems. The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local medicine and crafts.
