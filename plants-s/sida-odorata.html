Sida odorata

Sida odorata, commonly known as sweet sida, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its small, pale yellow flowers and hairy, ovate leaves. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

Sida odorata is known for its aromatic leaves and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
