Streptolirion cordifolium

Streptolirion cordifolium, commonly known as the heart-leaved streptolirion, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its unique foliage and delicate blooms make it a popular choice among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts.
