Spiniluma

Spiniluma, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a unique species native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which helps it reflect sunlight and reduce water loss in hot, dry environments. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale green, blooming during the cooler months. Spiniluma is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and hardy nature.

Its leaves are also sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies for their purported medicinal properties.
