Solanum hotteanum

Solanum hotteanum, commonly known as the nightshade or the hotteanum tomato, is a flowering plant belonging to the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is native to South America and is characterized by its hairy stems and violet or purple flowers. The plant produces small, yellowish-green berries that are edible when fully ripe. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its toxic properties when unripe, it is important to handle the plant with care.
