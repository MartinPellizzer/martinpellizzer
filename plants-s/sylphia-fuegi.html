Sylphia fuegi

Sylphia fuegi, commonly known as the Andean bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

Sylphia fuegi is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens that replicate its natural habitat.
