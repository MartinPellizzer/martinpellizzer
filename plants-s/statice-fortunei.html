Statice fortunei

Statice fortunei, commonly known as sea lavender, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its dense, spike-like inflorescences of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant thrives in salty, well-drained soils and is often found in dune environments. Its narrow, grass-like leaves are adapted to withstand harsh coastal conditions.

Statice fortunei is widely cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in dried floral arrangements.
