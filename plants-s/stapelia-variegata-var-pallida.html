Stapelia variegata var. pallida

Stapelia variegata var. pallida, commonly known as the pale variegated stapelia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems that are often ribbed and covered in a waxy coating. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and may exhibit a variegated pattern of green and white. It produces striking, lantern-like flowers that are usually yellow or orange in color.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
