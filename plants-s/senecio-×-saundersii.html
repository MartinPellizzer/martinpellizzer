Senecio × saundersii

Senecio × saundersii, commonly known as the Blueweed or Ice Plant, is a hybrid succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, blue-gray foliage with a waxy texture, adapted to store water in its thick, fleshy leaves. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its striking color and drought tolerance.
