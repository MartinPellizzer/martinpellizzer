Senecio retrorsus

Senecio retrorsus, commonly known as the backward senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Senecio retrorsus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
