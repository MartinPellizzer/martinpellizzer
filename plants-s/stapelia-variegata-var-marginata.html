Stapelia variegata var. marginata

Stapelia variegata var. marginata, commonly known as the variegated stapelia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems that are often ribbed and covered in a waxy coating. The leaves are typically narrow and pointed, with a distinctive variegation pattern of green and white. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom in the evening.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
