Scyphanthus grandiflorus

Scyphanthus grandiflorus, commonly known as the giant star orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 centimeters in diameter, featuring vibrant yellow petals with a contrasting purple lip. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb and long, strap-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a warm, humid environment to flourish.

Scyphanthus grandiflorus is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
