Stelis gonzaleziana

Stelis gonzaleziana, commonly known as the Gonzalez's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically found growing on trees in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration that varies by species. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

The botanical features of Stelis gonzaleziana make it a popular subject in both horticulture and botanical research.
