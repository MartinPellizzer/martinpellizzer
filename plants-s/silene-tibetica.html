Silene tibetica

Silene tibetica, commonly known as the Tibetan campion, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Tibetan Plateau.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its striking pink or red flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, adapting to the harsh conditions of its habitat. Silene tibetica has slender stems and small, oval-shaped leaves.
