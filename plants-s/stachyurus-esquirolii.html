Stachyurus esquirolii

Stachyurus esquirolii, commonly known as the Chinese weeping honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features delicate, pendulous flowers that bloom in early spring, often in shades of white or pale yellow. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that turn yellow in the fall, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Stachyurus esquirolii is valued for its graceful appearance and is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal.
