Sempervivum monanthes var. filicaulis

Sempervivum monanthes var. filicaulis, commonly known as the "Coral Bells" or "Hens and Chicks," is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe.

It features rosettes of fleshy, thick leaves that are often tinged with red or purple hues, especially in cooler weather. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the summer months. It is a low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Its compact growth habit makes it an ideal choice for rock gardens or as a container plant.
