Shorea wangtianshuea

Shorea wangtianshuea, commonly known as the Wangtianshuea ironwood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Dipterocarpaceae family and is characterized by its dense, heavy wood that is highly valued for its strength and durability. The plant typically grows in lowland rainforests and can reach heights of up to 40 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a leathery texture, while its flowers are small and clustered.

The tree plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
