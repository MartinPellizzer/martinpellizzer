Statice doerfleri

Statice doerfleri, commonly known as the desert statice, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, bract-like structures that surround its blooms. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Statice doerfleri is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
