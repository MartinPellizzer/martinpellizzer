Stylosanthes pohliana

Stylosanthes pohliana, commonly known as the pohliana stylo, is a perennial legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces cylindrical, woody pods containing small, kidney-shaped seeds. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a valuable forage crop.

Stylosanthes pohliana is also known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, improving soil fertility.
