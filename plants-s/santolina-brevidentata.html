Santolina brevidentata

Santolina brevidentata, commonly known as narrow-leaved santolina, is a low-growing, evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features silver-gray foliage with narrow, linear leaves that are densely packed along its stems. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flower heads that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Santolina brevidentata is valued for its ornamental appeal and aromatic properties.
