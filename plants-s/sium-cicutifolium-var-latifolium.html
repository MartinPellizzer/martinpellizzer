Sium cicutifolium var. latifolium

Sium cicutifolium var. latifolium, commonly known as broadleaf water parsley, is a herbaceous plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in umbels, which bloom in early summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as stream banks and wetlands.

Its root system is fibrous and can be used in traditional herbal medicine.
