Senecio willowensis

Senecio willowensis, commonly known as the Willowleaf Groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. It features narrow, willow-like leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens for its ornamental value.
