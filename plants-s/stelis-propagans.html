Stelis propagans

Stelis propagans, commonly known as the spreading stelis, is a member of the orchid family (Orchidaceae).

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its erect, hairy stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces delicate, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Stelis propagans is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
