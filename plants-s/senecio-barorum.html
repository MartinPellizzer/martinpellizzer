Senecio barorum

Senecio barorum, commonly known as the Barrow's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are usually gray-green with a fuzzy texture, and it produces small yellow flowers.
