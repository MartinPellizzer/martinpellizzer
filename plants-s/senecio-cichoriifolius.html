Senecio cichoriifolius

Senecio cichoriifolius, commonly known as the African Daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant features basal rosettes of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy yellow flowers with white centers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value is complemented by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
