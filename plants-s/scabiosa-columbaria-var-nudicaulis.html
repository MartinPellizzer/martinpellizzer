Scabiosa columbaria var. nudicaulis

Scabiosa columbaria var. nudicaulis, commonly known as the nakedstem scabious, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its slender, often hairless stems and clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and usually lack hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It is a perennial species that produces numerous seeds, aiding in its propagation and distribution.
