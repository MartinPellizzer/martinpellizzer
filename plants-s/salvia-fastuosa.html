Salvia fastuosa

Salvia fastuosa, commonly known as the showy sage, is a perennial flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant features narrow, gray-green leaves with a slightly fuzzy texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and aromatic foliage.
