Saxifraga × lhostei

Saxifraga × lhostei, commonly known as the hybrid saxifrage, is a perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Saxifraga oppositifolia and Saxifraga hirculus. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, where it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. It features dense clusters of small, white or pink flowers that bloom in late summer.

Saxifraga × lhostei is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in harsh climates.
