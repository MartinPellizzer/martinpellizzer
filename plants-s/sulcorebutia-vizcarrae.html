Sulcorebutia vizcarrae

Sulcorebutia vizcarrae, commonly known as the Vizcarras Sulcorebutia, is a species of flowering plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular shape and ribbed structure. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, which are typically borne on short, lateral stems. It is well adapted to dry environments, featuring succulent stems that store water and spiny areoles for protection.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts and collectors.
