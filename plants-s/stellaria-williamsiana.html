Stellaria williamsiana

Stellaria williamsiana, commonly known as Williams' starwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family, which includes other flowering plants such as carnations and sweet peas. The plant features slender stems that are often reddish in color and produce small, white or pale blue flowers with five petals arranged in a star-like pattern. Its leaves are typically opposite, oval-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Stellaria williamsiana is adapted to rocky and alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils and full sun exposure.
