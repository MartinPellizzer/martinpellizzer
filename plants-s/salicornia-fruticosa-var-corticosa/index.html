Salicornia Fruticosa Var. Corticosa

Salicornia fruticosa var. corticosa is a hardy halophyte plant commonly found in coastal regions. It thrives in saline environments and is known for its unique succulent stems and tiny, salt-tolerant leaves. This plant plays a vital role in stabilizing coastal soils and preventing erosion. It is also valued for its nutritional content and is used in various culinary and medicinal applications. Its resilience makes it an important species in ecological restoration projects.
