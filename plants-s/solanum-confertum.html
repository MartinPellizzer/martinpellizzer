Solanum confertum

Solanum confertum, commonly known as the clustered nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by berries that range in color from green to red. It is characterized by its dense clusters of foliage and branched stems.

Solanum confertum is often found in moist, shaded areas and can be cultivated as an ornamental plant.
