Symphyotrichum oolentangiense var. laevicaule

Symphyotrichum oolentangiense var. laevicaule, commonly known as the smooth blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and showy, blue to purple ray flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as woodland edges and wetlands. It has smooth, lance-shaped leaves and produces fluffy, white to blue seed heads that are attractive to pollinators.

This variety is distinguished by its relatively smooth stem and foliage compared to other varieties of the species.
