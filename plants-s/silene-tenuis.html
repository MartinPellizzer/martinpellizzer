Silene tenuis

Silene tenuis, commonly known as slender catchfly, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family and typically grows in dry, sandy soils. The plant produces small, pink or white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, and it is often found in open habitats such as prairies and roadsides.

Silene tenuis is a perennial herb that plays a role in supporting local pollinators.
