Scabiosa superba f. elatior

Scabiosa superba f. elatior, commonly known as the Italian skyflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caprifoliaceae family.

It is characterized by its clusters of pink to purple, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves, often with a distinctive white or grayish underside. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
