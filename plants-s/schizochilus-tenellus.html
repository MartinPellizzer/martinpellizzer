Schizochilus tenellus

Schizochilus tenellus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved schizochilus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, erect stem and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in a loose inflorescence. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
