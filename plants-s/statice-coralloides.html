Statice coralloides

Statice coralloides, commonly known as coral statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and its clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from pink to white. The plant produces numerous narrow, spike-like inflorescences that are often used in floral arrangements due to their long-lasting blooms. Its leaves are linear and slightly hairy, and it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Statice coralloides is a hardy perennial that is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
