Sedum morganianum

Sedum morganianum, commonly known as the "Donkey's Ear" or "Hairy Sedum," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, spoon-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it ideal for rock gardens or container planting.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
