Stenopetalum incisifolium

Stenopetalum incisifolium, commonly known as the serrated leaf hibiscus, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Australia.

It features lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges, giving it its common name. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers in shades of yellow and red, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and inland gardens.

Its ornamental value and adaptability make it a popular choice for landscaping in suitable climates.
