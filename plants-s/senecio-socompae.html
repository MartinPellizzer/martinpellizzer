Senecio socompae

Senecio socompae, commonly known as the Socompae groundsels, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are often hairy and have a serrated edge, while the flowers are small and yellow, arranged in clusters.

Senecio socompae is primarily found in the Western Cape region of South Africa.
