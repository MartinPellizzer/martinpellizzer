Stelis minuscula

Stelis minuscula, commonly known as the miniature stelis, is a small orchid native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on tree branches or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic growth habit. The plant features slender, wiry stems and small, clustered leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale green, with a delicate, intricate structure typical of orchids.

It is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
