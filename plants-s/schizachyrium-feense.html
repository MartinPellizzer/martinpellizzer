Schizachyrium feense

Schizachyrium feense, commonly known as slender false panicgrass, is a grass species native to eastern North America.

It typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as swamps, marshes, and along stream banks. The plant has slender, erect stems that are often branched and bear small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue when fresh.

Schizachyrium feense plays a role in stabilizing wetland soils and provides habitat for various invertebrates.
