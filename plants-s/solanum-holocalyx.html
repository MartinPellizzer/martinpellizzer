Solanum holocalyx

Solanum holocalyx, commonly known as the white eggplant or white nightshade, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive white, egg-shaped fruits. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with hairy stems and lobed leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale purple, and it produces small, fleshy berries that contain the seeds.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and traditional herbal practices.
