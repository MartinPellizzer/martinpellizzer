Senecio adustus

Senecio adustus, commonly known as the dusty senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its gray-green, fuzzy foliage and small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
