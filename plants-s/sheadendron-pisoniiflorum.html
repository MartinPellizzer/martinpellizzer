Sheadendron pisoniiflorum

Sheadendron pisoniiflorum, commonly known as the pink-flowered champa, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has compound leaves with small, ovate leaflets and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its flowers are also used in floral arrangements due to their delicate appearance and pleasant scent.
