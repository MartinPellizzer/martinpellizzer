Senecio fauriei

Senecio fauriei, commonly known as the Desert Senecio, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the rainy season. Its leaves are usually narrow and fleshy, adapted to store water.

Senecio fauriei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil.
