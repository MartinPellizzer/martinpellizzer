Scutellaria gracilis

Scutellaria gracilis, commonly known as slender skullcap, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and has small, tubular blue or purple flowers. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often have a rough texture.

Scutellaria gracilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
