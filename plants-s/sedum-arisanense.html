Sedum arisanense

Sedum arisanense, commonly known as the Japanese stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to East Asia.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink or white. The plant is adapted to rocky and arid environments, with thick leaves that store water. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a hardy choice for rock gardens.

Sedum arisanense is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
