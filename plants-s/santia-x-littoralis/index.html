Santia × Littoralis

Santia × littoralis is a unique hybrid plant known for its adaptability to coastal environments. It thrives in sandy soils and salt-laden air, making it a common sight along shorelines. This plant exhibits characteristics of both its parent species, combining resilience with aesthetic appeal. Its ability to withstand harsh conditions makes it valuable for ecological restoration projects. Despite its hardiness, it remains a subject of interest for botanists and gardeners alike.
