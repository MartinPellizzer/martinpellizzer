Scorzoneroides autumnalis var. cinerascens

Scorzoneroides autumnalis var. cinerascens, commonly known as the cinerascens autumnal scorzoneroides, is a variant of the autumnal scorzoneroides, a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, grayish-blue foliage that turns reddish in the fall, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers in late summer to early autumn, which are clustered in racemes. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
