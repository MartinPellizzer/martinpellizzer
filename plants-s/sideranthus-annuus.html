Sideranthus annuus

Sideranthus annuus, commonly known as the scarlet pimpernel, is an annual flowering plant native to the southern United States and parts of South America.

The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. Sideranthus annuus is often found in disturbed soils, such as along roadsides or in open fields, and is adapted to thrive in full sun.
