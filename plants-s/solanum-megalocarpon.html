Solanum megalocarpon

Solanum megalocarpon, commonly known as the giant nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants such as tomatoes and peppers. The plant produces large, showy flowers that are typically purple or blue in color. Its fruit is a fleshy berry that can grow up to 15 centimeters in length, containing several seeds.

Due to its toxic properties, it is not edible and should be handled with care.
