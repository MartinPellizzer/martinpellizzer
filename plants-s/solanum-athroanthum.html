Solanum athroanthum

Solanum athroanthum, commonly known as the purple heart vine, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades, and is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers. The plant produces small, egg-shaped berries that are typically yellow or orange when ripe. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a hairy texture.

Solanum athroanthum is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.
