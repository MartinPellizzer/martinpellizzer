Salacia erecta var. dewildemaniana

Salacia erecta var. dewildemaniana, commonly known as the dewildemaniana salacia, is a rare and unique variant of the Salacia genus.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in the understory of moist evergreen forests. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with elongated stems that support its dense foliage. Its leaves are typically elliptical, with a glossy surface and a slightly serrated edge.

While not widely cultivated, it is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
