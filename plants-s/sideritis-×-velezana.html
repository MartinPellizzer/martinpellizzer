Sideritis × velezana

Sideritis × velezana, commonly known as the hybrid sainfoin, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, arranged in dense spikes. Sideritis × velezana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.

It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.
