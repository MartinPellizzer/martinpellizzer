Schlegelia scandens

Schlegelia scandens, commonly known as the climbing schlegelia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its trailing or climbing growth habit. The plant features small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, giving them a soft texture.

Schlegelia scandens is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.
