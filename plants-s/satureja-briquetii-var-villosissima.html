Satureja briquetii var. villosissima

Satureja briquetii var. villosissima, commonly known as the woolly mountain savory, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant leaves and clusters of pink or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils, thriving in full sun. Its aromatic leaves contain essential oils that contribute to its distinctive scent.

It is often used in traditional herbal medicine and as a culinary herb.
