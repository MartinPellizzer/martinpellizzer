Sobralia recta

Sobralia recta, commonly known as the straight sobralia, is a species of orchid native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its pendulous, showy flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in a basal rosette.

Sobralia recta thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
