Statice × neumanii

Statice × neumanii, commonly known as the common statice.

It is a hybrid perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region. The plant is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and spikes of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, purple, and white. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Statice × neumanii is also valued for its long-lasting flowers, which are often used in floral arrangements.
