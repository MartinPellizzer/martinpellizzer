Senna Benitoensis

Senna benitoensis is a rare and striking flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America. Known for its vibrant yellow blooms and feathery leaves, it captivates botanists and gardeners alike with its unique beauty. This species belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes many legumes and is characterized by its nitrogen-fixing abilities. Despite its ornamental appeal, Senna benitoensis is also valued for its ecological role in supporting local wildlife. However, due to habitat loss and limited cultivation, it remains a threatened species in the wild.
