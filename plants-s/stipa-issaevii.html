Stipa issaevii

Stipa issaevii, commonly known as the Issaev's feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to Central Asia.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as steppes and rocky slopes. The plant features slender, wiry stems and produces narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its inflorescence consists of a dense, cylindrical cluster of spikelets, which are characteristic of the grass family.

Stipa issaevii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
