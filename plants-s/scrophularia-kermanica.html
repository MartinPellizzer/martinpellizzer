Scrophularia kermanica

Scrophularia kermanica, commonly known as Kerman scrophularia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or dry soils. The plant features erect stems with opposite leaves that are usually lance-shaped and hairy. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems.

It is part of the Scrophulariaceae family, known for its ornamental and medicinal value.
