Scutellaria playfairii

Scutellaria playfairii, commonly known as Playfair's skullcap, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color, arranged in whorls along the stem. Scutellaria playfairii is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value and medicinal potential have made it a subject of botanical interest.
