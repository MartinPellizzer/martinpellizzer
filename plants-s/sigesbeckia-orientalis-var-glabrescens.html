Sigesbeckia orientalis var. glabrescens

Sigesbeckia orientalis var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth sigesbeckia, is a flowering plant native to parts of East Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is often found in moist, disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats.

Due to its rapid growth and ability to spread, it is considered a potential invasive species in some regions.
