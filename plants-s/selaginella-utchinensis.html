Selaginella utchinensis

Selaginella utchinensis, commonly known as the Ustuzhinskii spikemoss, is a species of spikemoss belonging to the family Selaginellaceae.

It is a small, evergreen plant that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features a rhizomatous stem with small, scale-like leaves arranged in whorls. Its reproductive structures include microsporangia and megasporangia, which are borne on specialized stalks.

Selaginella utchinensis is notable for its ability to survive in harsh conditions, making it an important component of certain ecosystems.
