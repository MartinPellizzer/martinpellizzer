Senecio triangularis var. trichophyllus

Senecio triangularis var. trichophyllus, commonly known as the Trichophyllous Blue-Eyed Mary, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a velvety texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
