Stachys swainsonii var. scyronica

Stachys swainsonii var. scyronica, commonly known as Swainson's hedge nettle, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features square, hairy stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges. Its small, clustered flowers are usually pink or purple, blooming in late spring to early summer.

It is often found in open woodlands, grasslands, and along streambanks.
