Sterculia lanceolata

Sterculia lanceolata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sterculia, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. The plant's fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.

The species is valued for its hard, durable wood and is also used in traditional medicine.
