Synosma

Synosma, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to tropical Africa.

These plants are characterized by their rosettes of velvety, waxy leaves and their showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. Synosma species are typically evergreen and thrive in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. They are popular houseplants due to their ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

The flowers often bloom in spring and summer, adding a vibrant touch to indoor gardens.
