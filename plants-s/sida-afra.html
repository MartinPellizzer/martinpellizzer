Sida afra

Sida afra, commonly known as common sida, is an annual herb native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, pale green leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in loose inflorescences. Its seeds are tiny and numerous, often dispersed by wind.
