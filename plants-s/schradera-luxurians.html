Schradera luxurians

Schradera luxurians, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense, velvety texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, which are followed by winged fruits. Schradera luxurians thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the ecosystems it inhabits.
