Specklinia grisebachiana

Specklinia grisebachiana, commonly known as the gray speckled orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments within forest understories. The plant features a rhizomatous root system and produces small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green or white with a distinctive speckled pattern on the petals.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
