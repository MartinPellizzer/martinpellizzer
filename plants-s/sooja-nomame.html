Sooja nomame

Sooja nomame, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and showy, velvety flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Sooja nomame is known for its ability to thrive in humid environments and requires regular watering and moderate temperatures.
