Syngonium hastifolium

Syngonium hastifolium, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a tropical perennial native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, arrow-like leaves that are typically a vibrant green, often with a variegated pattern of white or cream. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers indirect sunlight. It is known for its rapid growth and ability to climb or trail, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Syngonium hastifolium is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and aesthetic appeal.
