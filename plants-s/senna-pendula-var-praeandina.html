Senna pendula var. praeandina

Senna pendula var. praeandina, commonly known as the yellow senna, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, flat pods that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine.
