Silene melzheimeri

Silene melzheimeri, commonly known as the Melzheimer's campion, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its pink or white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky areas. It has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves, and its flowers are often arranged in racemes.

Silene melzheimeri is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
