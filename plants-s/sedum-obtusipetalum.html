Sedum obtusipetalum

Sedum obtusipetalum, commonly known as the broad-petaled stonecrop, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine environments. It is a perennial species that is relatively easy to grow in gardens with full sun exposure.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
