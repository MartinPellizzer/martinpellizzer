Saxifraga damingshanensis

Saxifraga damingshanensis, commonly known as the Daming Mountain saxifrage, is a rare flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its succulent leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, mountainous regions, where it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.

Due to habitat destruction, it is considered endangered and requires protection to ensure its survival.
