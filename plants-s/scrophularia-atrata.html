Scrophularia atrata

Scrophularia atrata, commonly known as the black scrophularia, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in woodland edges and disturbed areas. The plant has slender, erect stems that bear opposite, lance-shaped leaves with hairy surfaces. Its flowers are tubular and usually purple or violet, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems.

Scrophularia atrata is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
