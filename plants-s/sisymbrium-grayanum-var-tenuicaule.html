Sisymbrium grayanum var. tenuicaule

Sisymbrium grayanum var. tenuicaule, commonly known as slender shepherd's purse, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, cruciform flowers and capsule fruits. The plant typically grows in rocky or disturbed soils and has a low, spreading habit. Its leaves are pinnately lobed, and it produces distinctive triangular seed pods.

It is often found in dry, sunny habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
