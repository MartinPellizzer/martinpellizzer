Soroseris rosularis

Soroseris rosularis, commonly known as the Rosular Sorrel, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its rosette-like arrangement of fleshy, succulent leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, adding a subtle ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is often cultivated in rock gardens and arid landscapes.
