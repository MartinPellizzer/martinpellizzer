Stellaria leptopetala

Stellaria leptopetala, commonly known as the slender starwort, is a low-growing annual herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It typically features slender stems and small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky habitats.

Stellaria leptopetala is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family, which includes other flowering plants with showy blooms.
