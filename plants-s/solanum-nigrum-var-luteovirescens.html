Solanum nigrum var. luteovirescens

Solanum nigrum var. luteovirescens, commonly known as the yellow-green nightshade, is a variant of the common black nightshade.

It is an annual herbaceous plant native to Europe, Asia, and Africa. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape, with a hairy surface. Its flowers are typically yellow-green, and it produces small, berry-like fruits that are toxic when ripe.

This variant is often found in disturbed soils and is part of the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades.
