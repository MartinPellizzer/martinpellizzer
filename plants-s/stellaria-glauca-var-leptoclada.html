Stellaria glauca var. leptoclada

Stellaria glauca var. leptoclada, commonly known as slender starwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its small, star-shaped white flowers and thin, wiry stems. The leaves are typically narrow and lanceolate, with a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.

This variety is often found in moist soils and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
