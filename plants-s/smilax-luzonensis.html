Smilax luzonensis

Smilax luzonensis, commonly known as the Luzon arrowhead, is a climbing vine native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its woody stems and distinctive arrowhead-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in spikes, followed by bright red berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Smilax luzonensis is valued for its ornamental appearance and has traditional uses in local medicine.
