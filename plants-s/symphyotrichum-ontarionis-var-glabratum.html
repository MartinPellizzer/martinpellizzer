Symphyotrichum ontarionis var. glabratum

Symphyotrichum ontarionis var. glabratum, commonly known as the smooth blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as woodlands and grasslands. It has smooth, hairless stems and leaves, which distinguish it from other similar species.

Its botanical features make it an important component of native plant communities and a subject of ecological study.
