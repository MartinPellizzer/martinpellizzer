Solanum isodynamum

Solanum isodynamum, commonly known as the dynamic nightshade, is a rare and lesser-known species within the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking violet-blue flowers and velvety, dark green foliage. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its fruits are small, berry-like structures that contain toxic alkaloids, similar to other members of the nightshade family.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, Solanum isodynamum is considered a rare and endangered species.
