Salacia distincta

Salacia distincta, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a fiery appearance. The plant belongs to the family Salicaceae, which includes willows and poplars, though it is not closely related to them. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a velvety texture.

Salacia distincta is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and parks due to its vibrant floral display.
