Schefflera todayensis

Schefflera todayensis, commonly known as the Today's Schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of leaflets, which give it a distinctive, palm-like appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by bright red berries, attracting birds and other wildlife. Its foliage is typically dark green and glossy, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor landscaping.

Schefflera todayensis thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil and indirect sunlight for optimal growth.
