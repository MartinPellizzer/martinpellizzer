Sphenomeris afra

Sphenomeris afra, commonly known as the African star grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color and arranged in a rosette formation. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Sphenomeris afra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
