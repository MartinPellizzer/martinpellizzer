Strombocactus schwarzii

Strombocactus schwarzii, commonly known as the Schwaiger's cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a columnar cactus with a distinctive ribbed structure and produces yellow flowers during the warmer months. The plant has a dense, spiny exterior that helps it retain moisture in its harsh environment. Its growth is slow, and it thrives in well-draining soil with minimal water.

Strombocactus schwarzii is often cultivated for its unique appearance and resilience.
