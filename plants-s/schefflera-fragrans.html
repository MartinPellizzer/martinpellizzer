Schefflera fragrans

Schefflera fragrans, commonly known as the fragrant schefflera, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its lush, glossy, dark green leaves that are arranged in a distinctive, umbrella-like pattern. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a subtle scent to the air. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.

Schefflera fragrans is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor cultivation.
