Senna dariensis

Senna dariensis, commonly known as the African senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry or semi-arid regions.

Senna dariensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some African cultures.
