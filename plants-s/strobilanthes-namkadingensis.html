Strobilanthes namkadingensis

Strobilanthes namkadingensis, commonly known as the Namkading flame flower, is a flowering plant native to the eastern Himalayas.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats at high altitudes. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with a smooth surface and a prominent midrib.

The species is noted for its seasonal blooming, producing vibrant red or orange flowers during the monsoon season.
