Saussurea katochaetoides

Saussurea katochaetoides, commonly known as the Himalayan arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its robust, hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the family. It thrives in cold, arid environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

Due to its unique adaptations, it plays a significant role in the ecological balance of its native habitat.
