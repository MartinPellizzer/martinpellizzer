Sideritis × vincenti

Sideritis × vincenti, commonly known as the hybrid geranium, is a flowering plant in the Lamiaceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Sideritis species, characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, two-lipped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Sideritis × vincenti is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
