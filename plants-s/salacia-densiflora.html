Salacia densiflora

Salacia densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered salacia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, woody stems and dense clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically yellow or orange when ripe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal potential, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and research settings.
