Sideritis ovata

Sideritis ovata, commonly known as the oval sainfoin, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, pale blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, and it produces small, oval-shaped seeds.

Sideritis ovata is often found in rocky or dry habitats and is valued for its medicinal properties.
