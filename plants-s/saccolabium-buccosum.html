Saccolabium buccosum

Saccolabium buccosum, commonly known as the yellow milkbush, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant is notable for its bright yellow flowers, which bloom in clusters during the warmer months. Its leaves are trifoliate, with each leaflet being oval and slightly hairy.

Saccolabium buccosum is often used in traditional medicine and is adapted to thrive in arid environments.
