Schizachyrium Salzmannii Subsp. Imberbe

Schizachyrium salzmannii subsp. imberbe is a rare grass species native to southern Africa. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is known for its distinctive silver-gray foliage. This subspecies is often found in open woodlands and rocky outcrops. Its unique appearance makes it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Due to habitat loss, it faces threats to its survival.
