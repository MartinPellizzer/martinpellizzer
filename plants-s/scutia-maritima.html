Scutia maritima

Scutia maritima, commonly known as sea scutia, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Americas.

It is a shrub that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils along the shoreline. The plant produces small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, providing a dense cover that helps stabilize dune ecosystems.

Scutia maritima is well adapted to salt spray and wind, making it an important species in coastal habitats.
