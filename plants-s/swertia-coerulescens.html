Swertia coerulescens

Swertia coerulescens, commonly known as blue swertia, is a perennial herb native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

Swertia coerulescens is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
