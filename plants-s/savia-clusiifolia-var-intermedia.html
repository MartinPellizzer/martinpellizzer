Savia clusiifolia var. intermedia

Savia clusiifolia var. intermedia, commonly known as the intermediate clusiifolia, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, often colorful leaves that can range from green to red or purple, depending on environmental conditions. The plant typically has a shrub-like growth habit with smooth, sometimes woody stems. Its flowers are small and clustered, often surrounded by modified leaves called bracts.

It is native to tropical regions and is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant foliage.
