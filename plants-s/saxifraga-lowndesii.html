Saxifraga lowndesii

Saxifraga lowndesii, commonly known as the Lowndes saxifrage, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in alpine or subalpine environments. The plant features small, clustered leaves that are usually succulent and adapted to arid conditions. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, appearing in clusters and blooming in late spring to early summer.

Saxifraga lowndesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens or as a container plant.
