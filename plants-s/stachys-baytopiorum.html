Stachys baytopiorum

Stachys baytopiorum, commonly known as the Baytop's hedge nettle, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Turkey.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic foliage. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or pink in color, arranged in dense spikes. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
