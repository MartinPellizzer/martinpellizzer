Solanum ovigerum var. violaceum

Solanum ovigerum var. violaceum, commonly known as the violet leaf nightshade, is a species native to South America.

It is characterized by its dark violet to purple leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces berries that are typically green when unripe and turn red when mature. It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae), which includes other plants like tomatoes and potatoes.

This variant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage.
