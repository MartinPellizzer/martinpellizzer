Stephanomeria ramosa

Stephanomeria ramosa, commonly known as the slender stem sunflower, is a flowering plant native to California.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and other composite flowers. The plant is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in coastal scrub habitats.
