Solanum swartzianum var. tomentosum

Solanum swartzianum var. tomentosum, commonly known as the tomentose nightshade, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and stems, which give it a distinctive, fuzzy appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or purple in color. Its fruit is a rounded berry that turns from green to yellow or red as it matures.

This species is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
