Sannantha brachypoda

Sannantha brachypoda, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sannantha, is a perennial herb native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, narrow leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Sannantha brachypoda is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
