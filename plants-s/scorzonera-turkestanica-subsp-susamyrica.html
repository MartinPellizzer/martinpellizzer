Scorzonera turkestanica subsp. susamyrica

Scorzonera turkestanica subsp.

susamyrica, commonly known as the Turkestan scorzonera, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its root system is fibrous and often used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soils in its native habitat.
