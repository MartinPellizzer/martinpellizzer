Saprosma distans

Saprosma distans, commonly known as the coral tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling coral in appearance. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and typically have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular ornamental species in warm climates.
