Stapelia × sanguinea

Stapelia × sanguinea, commonly known as the hybrid stapelia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its unique, fleshy, and often reddish stems. The plant produces large, showy flowers that are typically yellow or orange with a strong, unpleasant odor, which attracts pollinators like flies. It is a hybrid between Stapelia gigantea and Stapelia hirsuta, resulting in a plant that combines traits from both parents.

Stapelia × sanguinea is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
