Solanum ochroleucum

Solanum ochroleucum, commonly known as the yellow-leaved nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant is characterized by its yellowish leaves and small, white or pale flowers. It produces berries that are typically green when unripe and turn red when mature.

Due to its toxic properties, it is not suitable for human consumption.
