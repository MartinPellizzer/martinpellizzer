Sabicea gigantostipula

Sabicea gigantostipula, commonly known as the giant-stipule sabicea, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its large, leathery leaves with prominent stipules that are often as large as the leaf blades themselves. Its flowers are typically small and clustered in dense inflorescences, often with a distinctive yellow or greenish hue.

The plant is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique foliage and adaptability to various soil conditions.
