Sutherlandia frutescens

Sutherlandia frutescens, commonly known as cancer bush, is a small, shrub-like plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and woody stems, which are covered in fine, hairy structures. The plant thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in grassland and rocky habitats. Its leaves are opposite and elliptical, with a slightly serrated edge.

Sutherlandia frutescens is notable for its traditional use in herbal medicine, particularly for its purported anti-cancer properties.
