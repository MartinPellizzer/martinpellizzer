Semnos paniculata

Semnos paniculata, commonly known as the golden panicled orchid, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in panicles, giving it a showy appearance. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are elongated and glossy, providing a lush green backdrop to its vibrant blooms.

Semnos paniculata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in warm climates.
