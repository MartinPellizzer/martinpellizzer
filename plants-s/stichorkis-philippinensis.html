Stichorkis philippinensis

Stichorkis philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, with a distinctive lip that aids in pollination.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable and is often found in protected areas.
