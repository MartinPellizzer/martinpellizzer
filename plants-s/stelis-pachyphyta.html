Stelis pachyphyta

Stelis pachyphyta, commonly known as the thick-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting it to its natural habitat in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is often found in shaded, moist environments.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both ornamental and horticultural purposes.
