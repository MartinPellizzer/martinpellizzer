Statice × girardianoides

Statice × girardianoides, commonly known as the hybrid statice, is a flowering plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and vibrant, long-lasting blooms that come in a variety of colors, including pink, red, and white. The plant typically thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens due to its salt tolerance. Its flowers are composed of numerous small, scale-like bracts that give the appearance of a single, showy bloom.

Statice × girardianoides is often used in floral arrangements and as a border plant because of its ornamental value and hardiness.
