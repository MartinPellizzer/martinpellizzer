Scorzonera laciniata var. subulata

Scorzonera laciniata var. subulata, commonly known as the slender scorzonera, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features a robust taproot system, which is often harvested for its edible tubers. The plant has dark green, deeply lobed leaves that are typically hairy and arranged alternately along the stem. Its flowers are yellow and daisy-like, with a central disk surrounded by ray florets.

Scorzonera laciniata var. subulata thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, arid environments.
