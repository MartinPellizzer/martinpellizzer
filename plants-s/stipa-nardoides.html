Stipa nardoides

Stipa nardoides, commonly known as the narrowleaf needlegrass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, gray-green leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Stipa nardoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
