Senecio alleizettei

Senecio alleizettei, commonly known as the KwaZulu-Natal senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions. It features narrow, linear leaves and produces small yellow flowers arranged in a cluster.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
