Sedum yvesianum

Sedum yvesianum, commonly known as the Yves' stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has a compact growth habit and is well adapted to rocky and arid environments. Its leaves are thick and waxy, helping it store water efficiently.

It is often cultivated in rock gardens and as a drought-tolerant ornamental plant.
