Sisymbrium preauxianum

Sisymbrium preauxianum, commonly known as the narrowleaf tansy, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant features slender stems with alternate, linear leaves that are often hairy. Its small, yellow flowers are arranged in racemes and bloom in the summer months.

Sisymbrium preauxianum is characterized by its capsule fruits that release tiny seeds when mature.
