Saccharum obscurum

Saccharum obscurum, commonly known as the dark sugar cane.

It is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas. The plant is characterized by its tall, sturdy stalks and coarse, fibrous leaves. It produces a high amount of sucrose, making it valuable for sugar production.

However, it is less commonly cultivated than other sugarcane varieties due to its lower yield and higher maintenance requirements.
