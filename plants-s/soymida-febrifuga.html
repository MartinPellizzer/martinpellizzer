Soymida febrifuga

Soymida febrifuga, commonly known as the feverfew tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant is notable for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional medicine for treating fever and inflammation. It thrives in humid climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Its bark and leaves contain various bioactive compounds that contribute to its therapeutic uses.
