Senecio Atropurpureus Subsp. Frigidus

Senecio atropurpureus subsp. frigidus is a rare and hardy plant native to high altitude environments. It thrives in cold climates and is often found in alpine regions. This subspecies is known for its striking purple foliage and compact growth habit. It is a popular choice among gardeners looking to add unique texture to rock gardens. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a valuable and delicate species.
