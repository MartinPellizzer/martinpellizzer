Soliva anthemifolia var. anthemifolia

Soliva anthemifolia var. anthemifolia, commonly known as silverleaf flax, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, erect stems that can grow up to 1 meter in height, covered in fine, silvery hairs. The leaves are lance-shaped, with a distinctive silver or gray-green color due to the dense pubescence. It produces small, yellow flowers in racemes during the summer months.

This plant is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
