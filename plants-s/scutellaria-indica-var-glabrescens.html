Scutellaria indica var. glabrescens

Scutellaria indica var. glabrescens, commonly known as Indian skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or grassland habitats.

Scutellaria indica var. glabrescens is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
