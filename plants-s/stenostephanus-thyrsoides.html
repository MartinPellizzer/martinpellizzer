Stenostephanus thyrsoides

Stenostephanus thyrsoides, commonly known as the mountain cedar, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and small, leathery leaves that are typically gray-green in color.
