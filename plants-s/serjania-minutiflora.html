Serjania minutiflora

Serjania minutiflora, commonly known as the small-flowered serjania, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to South America.

It belongs to the family Serjaniae, which includes species with distinctive floral structures. The plant features opposite, simple leaves that are often elliptical or ovate in shape. Its small, fragrant flowers are typically white or pale yellow, and they are clustered in inflorescences.

Serjania minutiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
