Streptanthus validus

Streptanthus validus, commonly known as the Sierra madre gorse.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States, particularly California. The plant features showy yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Streptanthus validus is often found in rocky soils and open habitats, showcasing its adaptability to diverse environmental conditions.
