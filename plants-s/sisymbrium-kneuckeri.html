Sisymbrium kneuckeri

Sisymbrium kneuckeri, commonly known as the Kneucker's winter cress, is a small annual herbaceous plant native to Central Europe.

It typically grows in disturbed soils, such as roadsides and fields, and thrives in temperate climates. The plant features basal leaves that are lobed or toothed, and its flowers are small, yellow, and arranged in racemes. It produces slender, elongated pods that contain several seeds.
