Scaevola macrophylla

Scaevola macrophylla, commonly known as the coastal morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its showy, bilaterally symmetrical flowers that come in a range of colors, including pink, red, and white. The plant has fleshy, waxy leaves that help it retain moisture in arid environments. It thrives in coastal areas and well-drained soils, often growing in sandy or gravelly conditions.

Scaevola macrophylla is a popular ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and drought tolerance.
