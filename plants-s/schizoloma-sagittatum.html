Schizoloma sagittatum

Schizoloma sagittatum, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a member of the Araceae family and is native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves, which are typically bright green and have a smooth texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in an inflorescence called a spadix, surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. Schizoloma sagittatum thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.
