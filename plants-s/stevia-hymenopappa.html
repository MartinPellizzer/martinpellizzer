Stevia hymenopappa

Stevia hymenopappa, commonly known as Chinese stevia, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and small yellow flowers. The plant is known for its sweet-tasting leaves, which contain high levels of steviol glycosides, natural compounds used as a sugar substitute. It thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Stevia hymenopappa is often cultivated for both ornamental and medicinal purposes.
