Syzygium capoasense

Syzygium capoasense, commonly known as the Capoasense apple.

It is a small evergreen tree native to the rainforests of Brazil. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically red or yellow when mature. The leaves are elliptical, glossy, and have a slight waxy coating.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
