Spathoglottis lobbii

Spathoglottis lobbii, commonly known as the Lobb's spat orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the mountainous regions of Borneo.

It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has a complex root system that helps it thrive in humid, shaded forest environments. Its flowers are highly fragrant, attracting various pollinators such as bees and flies.

Spathoglottis lobbii is a rare and endangered species, making conservation efforts crucial for its survival.
