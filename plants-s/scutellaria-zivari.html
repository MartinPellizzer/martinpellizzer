Scutellaria zivari

Scutellaria zivari, commonly known as Zivari's bluehead, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Europe.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Scutellaria zivari is typically found in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.
