Spiraea × vanhouttei

Spiraea × vanhouttei, commonly known as the Vanhoutte spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to Europe.

It features dense, rounded growth with clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has finely serrated leaves that turn yellow in the fall, adding seasonal interest. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Spiraea × vanhouttei is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation in gardens.
