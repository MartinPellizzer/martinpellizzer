Sedum sinforosanum

Sedum sinforosanum, commonly known as the Sinforosa stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It is often found growing in rocky or sandy soils, making it well adapted to rocky landscapes.

Due to its hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
