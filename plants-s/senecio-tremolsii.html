Senecio tremolsii

Senecio tremolsii, commonly known as the Desert Senecio, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It is a succulent shrub that thrives in rocky soils and dry environments. The plant features small, narrow leaves and produces clusters of yellow flowers during the warmer months. It is adapted to withstand drought and high temperatures, making it a resilient species.

Its botanical characteristics make it well-suited for xeriscaping and ecological restoration projects.
