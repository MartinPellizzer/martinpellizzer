Spiranthes orchioides

Spiranthes orchioides, commonly known as the pink spiraea, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its delicate, spiral-shaped inflorescence. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wetlands. Its flowers are pink to purple in color and bloom in late summer.

It is a perennial herb that plays a role in supporting local pollinator populations.
