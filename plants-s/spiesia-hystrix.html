Spiesia hystrix

Spiesia hystrix, commonly known as the spiny hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive spiny leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to red. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its flowers bloom throughout the year, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds.

The plant is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and savannah habitats.
