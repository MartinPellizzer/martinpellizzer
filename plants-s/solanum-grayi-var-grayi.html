Solanum grayi var. grayi

Solanum grayi var. grayi, commonly known as the Gray's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants such as tomatoes and peppers. The plant produces small, violet-colored flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the rainy season. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, while the fruit is a small, yellowish berry.

This species is often found in rocky or disturbed habitats and is adapted to thrive in arid conditions.
