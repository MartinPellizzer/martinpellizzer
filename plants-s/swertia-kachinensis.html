Swertia kachinensis

Swertia kachinensis, commonly known as Kachin swertia, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its slender stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, blue or violet flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Swertia kachinensis is valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
