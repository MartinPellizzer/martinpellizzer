Sauropus calcareus

Sauropus calcareus, commonly known as the limestone senna, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal areas and on limestone soils. The plant features compound leaves with serrated edges and produces yellow flowers arranged in racemes. It is adapted to saline and arid conditions, making it a hardy species.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
