Senegalia yunnanensis

Senegalia yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to China, particularly Yunnan Province.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and ability to fix nitrogen in the soil. The plant features bipinnate leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy species suitable for reforestation and soil stabilization efforts.

The seeds of Senegalia yunnanensis are encased in pods and can be used for various traditional and ecological purposes.
