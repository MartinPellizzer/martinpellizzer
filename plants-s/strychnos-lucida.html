Strychnos lucida

Strychnos lucida, commonly known as the bright gloryberry, is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Loganiaceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers.
