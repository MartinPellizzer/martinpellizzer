Stapelianthus insignis

Stapelianthus insignis, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall, featuring a straight trunk and a rounded crown. The plant produces large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with small, oval-shaped leaflets.

Stapelianthus insignis is well adapted to tropical and subtropical climates, thriving in full sun and well-drained soil.
