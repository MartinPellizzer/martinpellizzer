Sedum wootonii

Sedum wootonii, commonly known as the Wooton's stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces showy, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or purple during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Sedum wootonii is also valued for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
