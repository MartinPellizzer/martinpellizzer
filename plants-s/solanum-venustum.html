Solanum venustum

Solanum venustum, commonly known as the beautiful nightshade, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its striking, often violet or purple flowers. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are usually ovate or lanceolate, with a rough texture and a slightly hairy surface.

Solanum venustum produces small, spherical berries that are toxic to humans and animals, making it a plant that requires caution when handling.
