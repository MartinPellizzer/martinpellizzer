Senecio scorzonerifolius

Senecio scorzonerifolius, commonly known as the African Daisy or Cape Daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its vibrant color and drought tolerance.

Senecio scorzonerifolius is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
