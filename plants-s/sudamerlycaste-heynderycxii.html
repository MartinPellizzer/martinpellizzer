Sudamerlycaste heynderycxii

Sudamerlycaste heynderycxii, commonly known as the Heynderyck's cycad, is a rare and ancient plant belonging to the Cycadaceae family.

Native to the humid lowland forests of southern Brazil, it thrives in warm, moist environments with well-drained soil.
