Senna polyantha

Senna polyantha, commonly known as the Indian senna, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, including India and China.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters tall, characterized by its pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes, which bloom during the summer months. Senna polyantha is known for its medicinal properties, with its leaves and flowers used in traditional medicine.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its foliage and floral display.
