Sulcorebutia xanthoantha

Sulcorebutia xanthoantha, commonly known as the yellow-topped crassula, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that are typically green with a yellowish tinge at the tips. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it an ideal choice for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Its unique coloration and drought tolerance make it a popular ornamental plant among succulent enthusiasts.
