Scrophularia nudicaulis

Scrophularia nudicaulis, commonly known as the slender lungwort, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. The plant features opposite, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are clustered in whorls and are usually pink or purple, with five lobes.

Scrophularia nudicaulis is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
