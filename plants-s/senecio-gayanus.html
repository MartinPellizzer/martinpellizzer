Senecio gayanus

Senecio gayanus, commonly known as the African Daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy yellow ray flowers surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers, creating a daisy-like appearance. Senecio gayanus typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
