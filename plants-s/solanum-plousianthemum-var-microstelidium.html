Solanum plousianthemum var. microstelidium

Solanum plousianthemum var. microstelidium, commonly known as the dwarf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial with small, hairy leaves and clusters of violet to purple flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically yellow when ripe. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
