Styrax martii var. rotundatus

Styrax martii var. rotundatus, commonly known as the rounded styrax, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its clusters of fragrant, white flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has simple, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. Its bark is smooth and reddish-brown, becoming more fissured with age.

It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
