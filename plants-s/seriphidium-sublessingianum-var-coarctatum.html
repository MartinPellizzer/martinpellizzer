Seriphidium sublessingianum var. coarctatum

Seriphidium sublessingianum var. coarctatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved tansy.

This plant is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and yellow flower heads. It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

It is adapted to withstand drought conditions and is sometimes used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements.
