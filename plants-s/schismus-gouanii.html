Schismus gouanii

Schismus gouanii, commonly known as the African sand mat, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that forms dense mats of fleshy, succulent leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers that appear in clusters. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy or rocky soils.

Schismus gouanii is adapted to withstand drought and has a unique ability to store water in its leaves.
