Statice acutifolia

Statice acutifolia, commonly known as sea lavender, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and spikes of blue to purple flowers. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and is well adapted to salty, windy environments. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Statice acutifolia is also valued for its use in floral arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms.
