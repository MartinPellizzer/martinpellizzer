Sida arsiniata

Sida arsiniata, commonly known as the Indian mallow or desert mallow, is a flowering plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands. Sida arsiniata is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
