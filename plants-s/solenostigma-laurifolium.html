Solenostigma laurifolium

Solenostigma laurifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf solenostigma, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped, leathery leaves that are dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by round, orange-red berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation in warm climates.
