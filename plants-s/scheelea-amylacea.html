Scheelea amylacea

Scheelea amylacea, commonly known as the white spider plant, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
