Senecio sinicus

Senecio sinicus, commonly known as the Chinese groundsel, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and ragworts. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small yellow flowers arranged in clusters. It is typically found in dry, rocky habitats and is often used in traditional medicine.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in arid ecosystems.
