Statice dubyi

Statice dubyi, commonly known as Duby's statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and thrives in full sunlight. Its flowers come in a range of colors, including pink, red, and white, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Duby's statice is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.
