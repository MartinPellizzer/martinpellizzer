Stromanthe sellowiana

Stromanthe sellowiana, commonly known as the pink prayer plant, is a tropical foliage plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that often display shades of pink, white, and green. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to maintain its vibrant appearance. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a waxy texture, which helps in retaining moisture.

Stromanthe sellowiana is popular in indoor gardening for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in humid environments.
