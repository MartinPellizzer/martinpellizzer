Scutellaria khasiana

Scutellaria khasiana, commonly known as the Khasi skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the northeastern region of India.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is typically found in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and mountainous regions.
