Swertia speciosa var. speciosa

Swertia speciosa var. speciosa, commonly known as the Himalayan gentian, is a perennial herb native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is valued for its ornamental beauty and traditional medicinal uses.

The species is also noted for its ability to thrive in cool, mountainous climates.
