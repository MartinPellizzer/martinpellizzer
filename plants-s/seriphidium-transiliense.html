Seriphidium transiliense

Seriphidium transiliense, commonly known as the Transilian wormwood, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, glandular leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils. It is often found in mountainous regions and can reach heights of up to 1 meter.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
