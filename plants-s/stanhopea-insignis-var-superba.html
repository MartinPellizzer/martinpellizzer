Stanhopea insignis var. superba

Stanhopea insignis var. superba, commonly known as the Superb Stanhopea, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that range in color from yellow to orange with prominent purple markings. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its pseudobulbs are elongated and covered in a thick, leathery epidermis, which helps store water.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and unique floral structure.
