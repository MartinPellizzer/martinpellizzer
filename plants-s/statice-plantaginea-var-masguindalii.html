Statice plantaginea var. masguindalii

Statice plantaginea var. masguindalii, commonly known as the Masguindal's statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit. The plant produces dense clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, purple, and white, which bloom in summer. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-gray hue that adds visual interest.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
