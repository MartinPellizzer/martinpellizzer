Scutellaria muramatsui

Scutellaria muramatsui, commonly known as the Japanese skullcap, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular blue or violet flowers that bloom in summer. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides.

Scutellaria muramatsui is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
