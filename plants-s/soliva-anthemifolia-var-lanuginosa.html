Soliva anthemifolia var. lanuginosa

Soliva anthemifolia var. lanuginosa, commonly known as silky bluegrass, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its fine, soft, bluish-green foliage that gives it a silky appearance. The plant produces small, pale blue to lavender flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, loamy soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for native plant gardens and erosion control.
