Spiraea japonica var. alba

Spiraea japonica var. alba, commonly known as white spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features slender, arching branches that bear clusters of small, white flowers in early summer. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green during the growing season. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This ornamental plant is often used in gardens for its delicate floral display and compact growth habit.
