Stelis lancifera

Stelis lancifera, commonly known as the lancifera orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees in humid tropical environments. The plant features linear, lance-shaped leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern. Its flowers are small and usually white or pale green, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

Stelis lancifera is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
