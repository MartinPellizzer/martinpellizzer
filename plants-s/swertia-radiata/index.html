Swertia Radiata

Swertia, scientifically known as Swertia radiata, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the Himalayan region. This perennial herb is celebrated for its vibrant blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas at high altitudes. The plant has been traditionally used in herbal medicine for its purported health benefits. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and conservation efforts.
