Scutellaria franchetiana

Scutellaria franchetiana, commonly known as the Chinese skullcap, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions.

Scutellaria franchetiana is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
