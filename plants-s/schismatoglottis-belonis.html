Schismatoglottis belonis

Schismatoglottis belonis, commonly known as the Belon's schismatoglottis, is a member of the Araceae family, native to Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its rosette of broad, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a dense, white pubescence. The plant produces striking inflorescences with a single, showy spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, rich soil.

Schismatoglottis belonis is popular among collectors for its ornamental value and unique foliage.
