Sedum lineare f. variegatum

Sedum lineare f. variegatum, commonly known as the variegated linear sedum, is a succulent plant characterized by its fleshy, linear leaves that exhibit a striking variegation pattern of green and yellow.

It is native to regions of Europe and Asia, where it thrives in rocky or gravelly soils. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It is a low-maintenance plant that prefers full sun and well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
