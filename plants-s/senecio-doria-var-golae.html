Senecio doria var. golae

Senecio doria var. golae, commonly known as the Doria's Senecio, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. It is often found in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in cool, moist conditions.
