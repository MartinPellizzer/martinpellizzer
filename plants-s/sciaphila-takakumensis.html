Sciaphila takakumensis

Sciaphila takakumensis, commonly known as the shade-loving orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, which bloom during the warmer months. Sciaphila takakumensis is an epiphytic orchid, meaning it grows on the surface of trees rather than in soil, using its roots to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air.
