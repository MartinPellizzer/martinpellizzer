Seris

Seris, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers and silver-gray foliage, which help it reflect sunlight in its coastal habitats. The plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soils. Seris typically grows in rocky or sandy areas, often near the shoreline.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
