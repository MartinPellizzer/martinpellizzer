Stelis microsynema

Stelis microsynema, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that grows on trees in tropical rainforests. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with intricate patterns. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Stelis microsynema is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and rarity.
