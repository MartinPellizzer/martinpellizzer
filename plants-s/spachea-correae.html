Spachea correae

Spachea correae, commonly known as the Correa's spurge, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive yellow-orange flowers. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture and produces small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Spachea correae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
