Salacia cauliflora

Salacia cauliflora, commonly known as the tropical snowball tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique cauliflorous growth habit, where flowers and fruits develop directly on the trunk and branches. The plant produces clusters of white, waxy flowers that bloom in dense, spherical inflorescences. Its fruit is a fleshy, red berry that contains numerous seeds.

Salacia cauliflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
