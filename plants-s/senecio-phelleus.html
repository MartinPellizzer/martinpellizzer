Senecio phelleus

Senecio phelleus, commonly known as the orange ragwort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers and fuzzy, lobed leaves. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. Senecio phelleus typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.

It is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance.
