Sulcorebutia formosa

Sulcorebutia formosa, commonly known as the Orange Ghost Cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of South America.

It is a compact, globular cactus with a distinctive orange to red coloration, especially when exposed to bright light. The plant features a thick, ribbed stem that stores water, allowing it to survive in dry conditions. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, blooming in late spring or early summer.

Sulcorebutia formosa is prized for its vibrant color and compact growth habit, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
