Senecio gilvus

Senecio gilvus, commonly known as the golden groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and fuzzy, silver-green leaves. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. Senecio gilvus typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
