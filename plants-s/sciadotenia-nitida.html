Sciadotenia nitida

Sciadotenia nitida, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of white flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant has compound leaves with small, ovate leaflets and produces small, berry-like fruits that are edible for some animals. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
