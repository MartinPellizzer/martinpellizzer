Stachys setifera subsp. lycia

Stachys setifera subsp.

lycia, commonly known as the Lybian hedge-nettle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features upright stems covered in fine, soft hairs, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a serrated margin.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink to purple in color, blooming in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments. Stachys setifera subsp.

lycia is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to attract pollinators.
