Sterculia verticillata

Sterculia verticillata, commonly known as the red-flowered sterculia, is a tropical tree native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves and showy, red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces woody capsules that contain numerous seeds, which are often dispersed by animals. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ecological and ornamental purposes.
