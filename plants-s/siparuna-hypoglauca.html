Siparuna hypoglauca

Siparuna hypoglauca, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking golden-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling flames. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and emit a subtle fragrance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Siparuna hypoglauca is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
