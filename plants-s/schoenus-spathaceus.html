Schoenus spathaceus

Schoenus spathaceus, commonly known as the flat-sheath sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges, rushes, and grasses. Schoenus spathaceus plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
