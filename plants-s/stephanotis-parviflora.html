Stephanotis parviflora

Stephanotis parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered starling vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features lush, dark green leaves and produces fragrant, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is characterized by its rapid growth habit and is often used in ornamental gardening. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, contributing to its ecological significance.
