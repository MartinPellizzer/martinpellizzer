Sicyos anunu

Sicyos anunu, commonly known as the giant burr cucumber, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of South America.

It produces large, spiny fruits that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and is characterized by its vigorous growth habit. Its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the same plant.

The fruit is used in traditional medicine and is also valued for its ornamental qualities.
