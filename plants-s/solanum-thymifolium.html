Solanum thymifolium

Solanum thymifolium, commonly known as the thyme-leaved nightshade, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, ovate leaves that are covered in fine hairs and have a distinct thyme-like fragrance. The plant produces clusters of small, white or yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its fruits are berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

Solanum thymifolium is native to southern Europe and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
