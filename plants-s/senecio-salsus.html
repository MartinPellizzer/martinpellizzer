Senecio salsus

Senecio salsus, commonly known as the saltbush senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant has small, narrow leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white wax, which helps reduce water loss. It produces yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months.

Senecio salsus is adapted to withstand drought and high salinity, making it a hardy species in challenging conditions.
