Stapelia × burtinii

Stapelia × burtinii, commonly known as the hybrid stapelia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed stems that store water. The plant produces striking, lantern-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. These flowers are adapted for pollination by specific insects, such as flies and beetles.

Stapelia × burtinii is often cultivated for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
