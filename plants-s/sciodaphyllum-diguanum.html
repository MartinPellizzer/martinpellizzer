Sciodaphyllum diguanum

Sciodaphyllum diguanum, commonly known as the moonlight fern, is a rare and enigmatic plant found in the dense, humid undergrowth of tropical rainforests.

The plant's leaves are covered in fine, translucent scales that help retain moisture in its moist, shaded habitat. Due to its elusive nature and limited distribution, Sciodaphyllum diguanum remains a subject of scientific fascination and conservation concern.
