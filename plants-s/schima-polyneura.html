Schima polyneura

Schima polyneura, commonly known as the Chinese teak or red flame tree, is a deciduous evergreen shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and distinctive, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has elliptical leaves with a leathery texture and a prominent midrib. Schima polyneura is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.
