Specklinia gracillima

Specklinia gracillima, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, grass-like foliage and small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the soil.

Specklinia gracillima is valued in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
