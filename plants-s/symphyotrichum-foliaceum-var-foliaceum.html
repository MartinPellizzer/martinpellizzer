Symphyotrichum foliaceum var. foliaceum

Symphyotrichum foliaceum var. foliaceum, commonly known as the foliage blueweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its herbaceous growth habit. The plant produces small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer to early fall. It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands.

Its foliage is often covered in fine, leaf-like hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.
