Stachys strictiflora var. strictiflora

Stachys strictiflora var. strictiflora, commonly known as narrow-leaved hedge-nettle, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It features upright, hairy stems that bear opposite, lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in dense clusters at the ends of the stems. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the Lamiaceae family.
