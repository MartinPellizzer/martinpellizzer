Satureja fruticosa

Satureja fruticosa, commonly known as Spanish thyme, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, aromatic leaves that are often used in culinary applications. The plant produces tiny white or pink flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves contain essential oils rich in thymol, contributing to its strong scent and medicinal properties.

Satureja fruticosa thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy and drought-tolerant species.
