Stelis fractiflexa

Stelis fractiflexa, commonly known as the flexible stelis, is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often found in the understory of tropical forests. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. Its inflorescence consists of several small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale green in color.

Stelis fractiflexa is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
