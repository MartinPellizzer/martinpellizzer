Stachys strictiflora

Stachys strictiflora, commonly known as narrowleaf betony, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which is known for its aromatic herbs and medicinal plants. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its small, tubular flowers are usually pink or purple, and they bloom from late spring to early autumn.

Stachys strictiflora is often used in traditional medicine for its purported calming and healing properties.
