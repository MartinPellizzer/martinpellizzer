Senecio roccatii

Senecio roccatii, commonly known as the rock senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid environments. It features narrow, fleshy leaves and produces small yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in dry gardens.
