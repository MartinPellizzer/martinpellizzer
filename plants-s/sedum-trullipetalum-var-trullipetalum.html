Sedum trullipetalum var. trullipetalum

Sedum trullipetalum var. trullipetalum, commonly known as the trullipetalum sedum, is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, typical of many sedum species. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for gardeners in arid climates.
