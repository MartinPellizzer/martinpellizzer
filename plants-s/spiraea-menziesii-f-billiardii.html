Spiraea menziesii f. billiardii

Spiraea menziesii f. billiardii, commonly known as the pink spiraea, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It features small, fragrant, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has narrow, dark green leaves that provide attractive foliage throughout the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value lies in its delicate blossoms and neat growth habit.
