Senecio moscopanus

Senecio moscopanus, commonly known as the Cape snowbush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features a bushy growth habit with narrow, silver-green leaves. It produces small, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in spring.

Senecio moscopanus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
