Stachyurus salicifolius subsp. lancifolius

Stachyurus salicifolius subsp.

lancifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved stachyurus, is a deciduous shrub native to China. It is characterized by its slender, willow-like leaves that are lanceolate in shape, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The plant produces pendulous clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers in early spring, which are followed by small, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sun, making it a suitable ornamental plant for gardens.

Its unique foliage and delicate flowers make it a valuable addition to the botanical diversity of temperate landscapes.
