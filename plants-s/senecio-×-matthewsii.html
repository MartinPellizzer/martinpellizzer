Senecio × matthewsii

Senecio × matthewsii, commonly known as the hybrid groundsels, is a perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, often hairy foliage and clusters of yellow or orange flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms and hardy nature.

Senecio × matthewsii is also noted for its ability to thrive in arid conditions, making it a suitable choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
