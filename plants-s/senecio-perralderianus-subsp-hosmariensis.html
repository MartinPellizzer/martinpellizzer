Senecio perralderianus subsp. hosmariensis

Senecio perralderianus subsp.

hosmariensis, commonly known as the Hosmariense Senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and yellowish flowers.

The subspecies is typically found in rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
