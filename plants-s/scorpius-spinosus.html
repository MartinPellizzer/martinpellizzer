Scorpius spinosus

Scorpius spinosus, commonly known as the spiny scorpion plant, is a unique succulent native to arid regions.

It features thick, spiky stems that store water, allowing it to survive in dry environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warm season. Its leaves are typically reduced to spines, which help deter herbivores and reduce water loss.

Scorpius spinosus is often cultivated for its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements.
