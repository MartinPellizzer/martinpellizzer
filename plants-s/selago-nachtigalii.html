Selago nachtigalii

Selago nachtigalii, commonly known as the nightshade starflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white and purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are upright and branching.

Selago nachtigalii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
