Saxifraga Bronchialis Subsp. Funstonii

Saxifraga bronchialis subsp. funstonii is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the rocky landscapes of Texas. This subspecies thrives in arid environments, often growing in crevices where moisture is scarce but essential. Its small, white flowers bloom in spring, adding a subtle beauty to its rugged habitat. Due to its limited range and fragile nature, it is considered endangered and requires careful conservation efforts. The plant's unique adaptations highlight the resilience of flora in extreme conditions.
