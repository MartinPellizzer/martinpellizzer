Stenoloma blumeanum

Stenoloma blumeanum, commonly known as the Blume's stenoloma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

The plant is characterized by its dark green, arrow-shaped leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers clustered in a spadix, typical of the Araceae family.
