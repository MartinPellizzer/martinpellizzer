Stellaria pallida

Stellaria pallida, commonly known as pale starwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family, which includes other flowering plants such as carnations and pinks. The plant features small, white or pale pink flowers that are arranged in clusters, giving it a star-like appearance. Its leaves are typically narrow and slightly hairy, and it often grows in moist, grassy areas.

Stellaria pallida is known for its delicate structure and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
