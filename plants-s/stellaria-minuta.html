Stellaria minuta

Stellaria minuta, commonly known as common mouse-ear, is a small flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit, with tiny, oval leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, star-shaped white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.

Stellaria minuta is often found in temperate regions of Europe, Asia, and North America.
