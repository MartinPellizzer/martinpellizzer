Solanum vespertilioneum

Solanum vespertilioneum, commonly known as the evening bat solanum, is a flowering plant belonging to the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and oval, dark green leaves with a slightly serrated edge. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically purple or violet in color, which bloom in the evening. Its fruit is a fleshy berry that turns from green to yellow as it matures.

This species is native to certain regions of South America and is often found in moist, shaded environments.
