Schizachne purpurascens f. albicans

Schizachne purpurascens f. albicans, commonly known as the white-flowered grass or white lemongrass, is a variant of the species Schizachne purpurascens.

It is a perennial grass native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves with a distinctive white or pale pink coloration on the inflorescence. It grows in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The white-flowered form is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
