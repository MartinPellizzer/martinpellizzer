Schefflera schultzei

Schefflera schultzei, commonly known as the Schultze's umbrella plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of large, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Schefflera schultzei is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
