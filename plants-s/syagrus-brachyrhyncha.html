Syagrus brachyrhyncha

Syagrus brachyrhyncha, commonly known as the royal palm, is a species of palm native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like leaves that form a crown at the top. The plant produces yellowish-white flowers that develop into small, round fruits, which are edible and often consumed by various bird species. Syagrus brachyrhyncha thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sunlight to grow optimally.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical and subtropical climates.
