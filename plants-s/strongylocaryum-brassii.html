Strongylocaryum brassii

Strongylocaryum brassii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant thrives in humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil. It is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.

Strongylocaryum brassii is known for its ability to bloom repeatedly throughout the year under optimal conditions.
