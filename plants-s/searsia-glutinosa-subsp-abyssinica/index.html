Searsia Glutinosa Subsp. Abyssinica

Searsia glutinosa subsp. abyssinica is a unique shrub native to the highlands of Ethiopia. It is known for its distinctive yellow flowers and its ability to thrive in arid conditions. This plant is often found in rocky soils and is well adapted to the region's climate. It plays an important role in local ecosystems and is valued for its ornamental qualities. Despite its resilience, it remains relatively unknown outside of its native habitat.
