Stelis llipiensis

Stelis llipiensis, commonly known as the Llipi orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of Peru.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a slender stem with a few leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Its flowers are relatively small but are noted for their intricate structure and ornamental appeal.

Stelis llipiensis is a rare and endangered species, making it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
