Solanum pisinnum

Solanum pisinnum, commonly known as the Pisoni nightshade, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant typically grows as an erect shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are ovate with a rough texture, and it produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters.

The fruit is a berry that turns from green to red when mature, containing several seeds.
