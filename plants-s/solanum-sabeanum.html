Solanum sabeanum

Solanum sabeanum, commonly known as the Sabean nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Arabian Peninsula.

It is a shrub that typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually white or pale purple, which bloom in the evening. Its fruit is a yellowish or orange berry that contains several seeds.

Solanum sabeanum is part of the nightshade family, which includes many economically important plants.
