Stapelia molonyae

Stapelia molonyae, commonly known as the "Cape Town Stapelia," is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a succulent species characterized by its fleshy, ribbed stems and distinctive, meaty flowers. The plant produces large, star-shaped blooms that are often yellow or orange in color, with a strong, unpleasant odor resembling decay. It thrives in arid environments and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for xerophytic gardens.

Stapelia molonyae is a popular choice for rock gardens and is valued for its unique appearance and resilience.
