Spermacoce involucrata

Spermacoce involucrata, commonly known as the African starbursts, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is an annual herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.

Spermacoce involucrata is often found in disturbed soils and is adaptable to various environmental conditions.
