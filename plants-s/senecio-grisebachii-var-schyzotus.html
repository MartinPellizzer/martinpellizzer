Senecio grisebachii var. schyzotus

Senecio grisebachii var. schyzotus, commonly known as the Schizotus Senecio, is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its distinctive floral structures.

It is native to South Africa, where it typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish composite flowers arranged in heads. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique appearance and hardy nature.

This variant is notable for its compact growth habit and ability to thrive in well-drained conditions.
