Sedum ovatisepalum

Sedum ovatisepalum, commonly known as the ovate-sepal sedum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Crassulaceae and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves that help it store water. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its resilience and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in arid and semi-arid regions.
