Spigelia polystachya

Spigelia polystachya, commonly known as the clustered flame flower, is a member of the Apocynaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its showy, red-orange flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.
