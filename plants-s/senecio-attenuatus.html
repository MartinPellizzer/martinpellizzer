Senecio attenuatus

Senecio attenuatus, commonly known as the slender groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. Its leaves are linear and pointed, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in clusters.

Senecio attenuatus is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
