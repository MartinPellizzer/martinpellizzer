Stenopetalum filifolium

Stenopetalum filifolium, commonly known as the thread-leaved hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, thread-like leaves that give it its scientific name, *filifolium* meaning "thread leaf.". The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Stenopetalum filifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
