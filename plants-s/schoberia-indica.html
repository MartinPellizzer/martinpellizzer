Schoberia indica

Schoberia indica, commonly known as the Indian thornapple, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, including India and China.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are typically lance-shaped with serrated edges. Its fruit is a capsule that contains numerous small, hard seeds.

Schoberia indica is often found in dry, disturbed habitats and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.
