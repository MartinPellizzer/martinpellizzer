Senecio polychotomus

Senecio polychotomus, commonly known as the many-branched groundsel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, blue-green leaves and produces daisy-like yellow flowers with dark centers. The plant has a fibrous root system and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often found in rocky or sandy habitats, preferring full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
