Selaginella sanguinolenta f. aitchisonii

Selaginella sanguinolenta f. aitchisonii, commonly known as the Aitchison's spikemoss, is a rare and unique fern ally belonging to the family Selaginellaceae.

The plant produces sporophylls that bear spores, and it is often found in moist, shaded environments such as rocky crevices or forest floors.
