Strychnos acutissima

Strychnos acutissima, commonly known as the African angel's trumpet, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Loganiaceae family and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and showy, tubular, yellowish-white flowers. Despite its ornamental appeal, it contains toxic alkaloids and should be handled with care.
