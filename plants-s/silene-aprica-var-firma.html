Silene aprica var. firma

Silene aprica var. firma, commonly known as the alpine sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer.
