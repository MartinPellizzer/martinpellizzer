Solanum subtusviolaceum

Solanum subtusviolaceum, commonly known as the purple underside nightshade, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is native to parts of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of purple and white, with a distinctive purple underside on the petals. Its leaves are ovate and covered in a fine layer of pubescence, contributing to its unique appearance.

Solanum subtusviolaceum is often found in shaded, moist environments such as forests and undergrowth.
