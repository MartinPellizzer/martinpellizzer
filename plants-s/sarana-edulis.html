Sarana edulis

Sarana edulis, commonly known as the edible leaf plant, is a herbaceous species native to tropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a smooth, waxy surface that aids in water retention. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its leaves are harvested for culinary use, offering a mild, slightly nutty flavor.

Botanically, it belongs to the family Araceae, which includes other economically important plants like taro and calla lilies.
