Solidago canadensis var. procera

Solidago canadensis var. procera, commonly known as tall goldenrod, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant produces abundant yellow inflorescences composed of small disk flowers surrounded by ray florets. Its leaves are lance-shaped, alternate, and covered in fine hairs.

Tall goldenrod thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a common sight in meadows and disturbed habitats.
