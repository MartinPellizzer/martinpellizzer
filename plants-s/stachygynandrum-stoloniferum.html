Stachygynandrum stoloniferum

Stachygynandrum stoloniferum, commonly known as the stoloniferous stachygynandrum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a perennial herb that produces dense clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit, which allows it to spread via stolons. Its leaves are typically elongated and arranged in a rosette pattern.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
