Sida aurescens

Sida aurescens, commonly known as the golden sida, is an annual herb native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, erect stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in loose inflorescences. Its leaves are ovate to elliptic in shape and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Sida aurescens is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
