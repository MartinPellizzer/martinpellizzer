Salvia weihaiensis

Salvia weihaiensis, commonly known as the Weihai sage, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces striking violet-blue flowers in whorled clusters, which bloom in late summer. Its foliage is aromatic and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Salvia weihaiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in gardens.
