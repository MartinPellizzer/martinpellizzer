Schlumbergera epiphylloides

Schlumbergera epiphylloides, commonly known as the rose-bud Christmas cactus, is a species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its segmented, woody stems that bear clusters of vibrant, funnel-shaped flowers. The plant typically blooms during the holiday season, producing shades of red, pink, and white. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native arid environment.

Schlumbergera epiphylloides is a popular ornamental plant due to its long blooming period and ease of care.
