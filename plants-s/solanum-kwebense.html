Solanum kwebense

Solanum kwebense, commonly known as the Kweben solanum, is a species native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by berries that range in color from green to red.
