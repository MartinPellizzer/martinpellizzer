Salicornia herbacea var. stricta

Salicornia herbacea var. stricta, commonly known as sea beans or glassworts, is a halophytic plant adapted to saline environments.

It is a succulent species that thrives in coastal areas, salt marshes, and other salty soils. The plant features erect, cylindrical stems covered in small, scale-like leaves. It produces tiny, greenish flowers that are clustered in spike-like inflorescences.

This variety is often used in culinary applications for its crisp, salty flavor and nutritional value.
