Senecio paulsenii subsp. khorasanicus

Senecio paulsenii subsp.

khorasanicus, commonly known as the Khorasan Senecio, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, typical of the daisy family. It is adapted to survive in dry environments with minimal water requirements.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
