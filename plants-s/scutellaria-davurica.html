Scutellaria davurica

Scutellaria davurica, commonly known as Siberian skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It features square, hairy stems and opposite leaves that are lance-shaped with serrated edges. The plant produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers arranged in whorls along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or forested habitats.

Scutellaria davurica is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
