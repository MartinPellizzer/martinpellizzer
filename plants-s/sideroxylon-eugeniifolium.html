Sideroxylon eugeniifolium

Sideroxylon eugeniifolium, commonly known as the wax tree or red wax tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, followed by woody, oval-shaped fruits. Its bark is thick and fibrous, often exuding a resinous substance.

The tree is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional medicine.
