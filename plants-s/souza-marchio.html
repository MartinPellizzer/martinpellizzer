Souza marchio

Souza marchio, commonly known as the Brazilian violet, is a flowering plant native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in a fine pubescence.

The flowers bloom in clusters and are pollinated primarily by bees and other insects.
