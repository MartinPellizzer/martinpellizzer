Scorzonera laciniata var. calcitrapifolia

Scorzonera laciniata var. calcitrapifolia, commonly known as the limestone scorzonera, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and produces yellow flowers with a distinctive, somewhat prickly appearance. The plant thrives in rocky, calcareous soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to harsh, limestone-rich habitats.
