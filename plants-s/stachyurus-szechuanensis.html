Stachyurus szechuanensis

Stachyurus szechuanensis, commonly known as the Chinese snowbell, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features delicate, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring, typically in shades of white or pale yellow. The plant has slender, arching branches and small, ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Stachyurus szechuanensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
