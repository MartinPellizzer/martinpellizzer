Senecio corazonensis

Senecio corazonensis, commonly known as the Heartleaf Senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its yellow-orange flowers bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
