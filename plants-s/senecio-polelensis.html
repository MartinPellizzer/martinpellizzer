Senecio polelensis

Senecio polelensis, commonly known as the Cape fynbos senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to the Mediterranean climate of the region. Senecio polelensis features narrow, linear leaves and produces small yellow flowers.

It is often found in fynbos vegetation, contributing to the biodiversity of its native habitat.
