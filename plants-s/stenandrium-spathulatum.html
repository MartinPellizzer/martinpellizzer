Stenandrium spathulatum

Stenandrium spathulatum, commonly known as the broad-leafed stenandrium, is a tropical plant native to regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are typically glossy and dark green in color. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
