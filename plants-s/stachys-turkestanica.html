Stachys turkestanica

Stachys turkestanica, commonly known as Turkestan hedge-nettle, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features square stems and opposite leaves with a rough texture. Its flowers are typically purple or pink, arranged in whorls along the stem.

It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
