Synaphea intricata

Synaphea intricata, commonly known as the intricate synaphea, is a unique species within the family Synapheaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, branched structure and small, scale-like leaves. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate rainforests.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in plant morphology and ecological adaptation.
