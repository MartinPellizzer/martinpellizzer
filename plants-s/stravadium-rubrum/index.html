Stravadium Rubrum

Red stravadium, scientifically known as Stravadium rubrum, is a rare and striking plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes. Its vibrant red petals, which resemble small flames, make it a subject of fascination for botanists and gardeners alike. This unique species thrives in cool, moist environments and requires specific conditions to flourish. Despite its beauty, Stravadium rubrum is endangered due to habitat loss and climate change. Conservation efforts are underway to protect this remarkable plant for future generations.
