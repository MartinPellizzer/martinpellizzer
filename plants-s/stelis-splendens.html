Stelis splendens

Stelis splendens, commonly known as the splendid stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Stelis splendens thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light.
