Sisymbrium runcinatum var. glabrum

Sisymbrium runcinatum var. glabrum, commonly known as the smooth shepherd's purse, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive triangular seed pods and lobed leaves. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in various regions across Europe and parts of Asia. It is an annual herb that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil.

The smooth surface of its stems and leaves distinguishes it from other varieties of the species.
