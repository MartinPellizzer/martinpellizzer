Symphyotrichum Ericoides F. Gramsii

Symphyotrichum ericoides f. gramsii is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific regions of the northeastern United States. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in late summer. This fern-like variant of Symphyotrichum ericoides is often found in moist, shaded woodlands where it thrives in the understory. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Due to habitat loss, this plant faces increasing threats to its survival.
