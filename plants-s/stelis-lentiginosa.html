Stelis lentiginosa

Stelis lentiginosa, commonly known as the spotted stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid and shaded environments. The plant features a short, fleshy stem with several leaves arranged in a rosette pattern. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and often display a mix of white and green hues.

Stelis lentiginosa is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or terrariums.
