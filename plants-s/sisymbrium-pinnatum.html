Sisymbrium pinnatum

Sisymbrium pinnatum, commonly known as narrowleaf tansy, is an annual herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many economically important crops and weeds. The plant features slender, branching stems and small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes. Its leaves are pinnately divided, giving it a feathery appearance.

Sisymbrium pinnatum is often found in disturbed soils and can be invasive in certain regions.
