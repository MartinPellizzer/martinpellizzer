Stelis stigmatosa

Stelis stigmatosa, commonly known as the showy stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees in humid tropical environments, relying on its aerial roots for nutrient absorption. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with distinctive markings. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Stelis stigmatosa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
