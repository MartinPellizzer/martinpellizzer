Spiraea Ovalis Var. Teretiuscula

Spiraea ovalis var. teretiuscula is a lesser-known but strikingly beautiful shrub native to the mountainous regions of western North America. This compact, deciduous plant is prized for its clusters of delicate, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full to partial sun, making it a versatile choice for gardeners seeking hardy, low-maintenance plants. Its unique foliage, which turns vibrant shades of red in the fall, adds year-round interest to the landscape. Despite its charm, it remains underappreciated in mainstream horticulture, offering a hidden gem for those who appreciate native flora.
