Sinningia × ornata

Sinningia × ornata, commonly known as the frost plant, is a hybrid species within the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, where it thrives in cool, humid environments. The plant is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves and showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

Sinningia × ornata typically grows as a low, compact shrub with a dense, bushy appearance.
