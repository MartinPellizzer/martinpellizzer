Sansevieria longistyla

Sansevieria longistyla, commonly known as the long-styled snake plant, is a species native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically variegated with green and yellow hues. It is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-draining soil and indirect light.
