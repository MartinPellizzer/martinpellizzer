Senecio ramosissimus

Senecio ramosissimus, commonly known as the mountain ragwort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its branched stems and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats. It has finely divided, grass-like leaves and is part of the Asteraceae family.

Due to its toxicity, it should be handled with care.
