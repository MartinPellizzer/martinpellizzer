Statice axillaris

Statice axillaris, commonly known as the coastal statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in coastal areas, thriving in sandy or gravelly soils and tolerating salt spray. Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves that are usually blue-green in color.

Statice axillaris is often used in garden landscapes for its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
