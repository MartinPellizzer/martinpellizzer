Spathodea candolleana var. lanceolaris

Spathodea candolleana var. lanceolaris, commonly known as the lanceolate flame tree, is a striking tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that are typically orange-red in color, arranged in panicle inflorescences. The leaves are lanceolate, hence the variety name, and are alternate, simple, and often have a waxy texture. This plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

It is also valued for its rapid growth and ability to provide shade in tropical landscapes.
