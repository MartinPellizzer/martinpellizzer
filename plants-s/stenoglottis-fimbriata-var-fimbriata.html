Stenoglottis fimbriata var. fimbriata

Stenoglottis fimbriata var. fimbriata, commonly known as the fimbriate rock orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its distinctive, fimbriated (fringed) leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in spikes, and it is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
