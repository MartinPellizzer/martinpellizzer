Syngonium macrophyllum

Syngonium macrophyllum, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a tropical perennial native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves that vary in color from solid green to variegated patterns of white, yellow, and pink. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil. Its rapid growth makes it an excellent choice for hanging baskets or as a trailing plant.

Syngonium macrophyllum is also popular for its air-purifying qualities and ability to thrive in humid environments.
