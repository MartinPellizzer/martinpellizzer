Stelis veraguasensis

Stelis veraguasensis, commonly known as the Veraguas orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and clustered inflorescences. The plant typically produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Stelis veraguasensis is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions.
