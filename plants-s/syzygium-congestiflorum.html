Syzygium congestiflorum

Syzygium congestiflorum, commonly known as the clustered clove.

It is a tropical evergreen tree native to the rainforests of Queensland, Australia. The plant is characterized by its dense clusters of fragrant white flowers, which bloom in spring. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and glossy, providing a lush green canopy.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its visual and aromatic qualities.
