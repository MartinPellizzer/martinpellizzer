Scutellaria bicolor

Scutellaria bicolor, commonly known as blue violet, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and the Americas.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and ornamental plants. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, two-lipped flowers in shades of blue, purple, or white. Scutellaria bicolor is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a ground cover.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it adaptable to a variety of growing conditions.
