Sphyrospermum linearifolium

Sphyrospermum linearifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sphyrospermum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, with ray florets surrounding tubular central florets.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
