Strychnos mostueoides

Strychnos mostueoides, commonly known as the African strychnos, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Loganiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and yellowish flowers. Due to its toxic properties, it is important to handle the plant with caution.
