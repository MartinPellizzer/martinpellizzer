Sarcochilus erectus

Sarcochilus erectus, commonly known as the upright helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It features a distinctive, upright growth habit with a thick, fleshy rhizome and a single, erect pseudobulb. The plant produces a single, showy flower with a greenish-yellow perianth and a prominent, helmet-like lip. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in rainforest areas.

The species is valued for its unique morphology and is a popular subject in botanical studies.
