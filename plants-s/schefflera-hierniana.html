Schefflera hierniana

Schefflera hierniana, commonly known as the Hiern's umbrella tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like crown of large, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Schefflera hierniana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor conditions.
