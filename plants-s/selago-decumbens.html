Selago decumbens

Selago decumbens, commonly known as the prostrate lily or groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in loose inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often covered in fine hairs.

Selago decumbens is adapted to thrive in arid and rocky environments, making it a resilient species in harsh conditions.
