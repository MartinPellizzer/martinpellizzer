Scutellaria katangensis

Scutellaria katangensis, commonly known as Katanga skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the Democratic Republic of the Congo.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
