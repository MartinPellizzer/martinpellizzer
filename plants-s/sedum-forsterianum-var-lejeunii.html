Sedum forsterianum var. lejeunii

Sedum forsterianum var. lejeunii, commonly known as the Blue Sedum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of blue-gray foliage that are fleshy and arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of blue and purple during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact size and striking color make it a popular choice for both ornamental and low-maintenance landscaping.
