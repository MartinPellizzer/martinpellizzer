Saussurea elongata subsp. recurvata

Saussurea elongata subsp.

recurvata, commonly known as the Himalayan arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas. It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and large, reflexed, blue to violet flower heads.

The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is adapted to cold, arid environments. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, and it produces numerous small, tubular florets.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often found in alpine meadows at elevations above 3,000 meters.
