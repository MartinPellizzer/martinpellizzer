Statice beaumieriana var. tripeaui

Statice beaumieriana var. tripeaui, commonly known as the coastal statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and produces showy, spike-like inflorescences of blue to violet flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained, sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal dunes and rocky habitats. It is drought-tolerant and prefers full sun, making it a hardy choice for coastal gardens.

The leaves are narrow and linear, contributing to its ornamental appeal in garden settings.
