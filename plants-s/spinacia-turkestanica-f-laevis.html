Spinacia turkestanica f. laevis

Spinacia turkestanica f. laevis, commonly known as the smooth leaf spinach, is a cultivated form of the spinach plant native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, hairless leaves, which are typically dark green and slightly elongated. This variety is valued for its mild flavor and nutritional content, similar to common spinach. It thrives in cool climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The plant is often grown for both culinary and ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage.
