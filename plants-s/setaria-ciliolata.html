Setaria ciliolata

Setaria ciliolata, commonly known as slender dropseed, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like clusters of flowers that are typically pale green to brown in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and along roadsides.

Setaria ciliolata is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in disturbed areas.
