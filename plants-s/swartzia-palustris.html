Swartzia palustris

Swartzia palustris, commonly known as swamp swartzia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub that typically grows in wetland or swampy habitats. The plant produces small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the rainy season. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a glossy appearance.
