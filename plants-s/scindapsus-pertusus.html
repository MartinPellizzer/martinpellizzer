Scindapsus pertusus

Scindapsus pertusus, commonly known as the silver mitten plant, is a tropical evergreen vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with striking silver veins that create a mottled appearance. The plant typically grows in a trailing or climbing habit, making it suitable for hanging baskets or shelves. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Its air-purifying qualities make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
