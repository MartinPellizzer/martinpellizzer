Swietenia mahagoni

Swietenia mahagoni, commonly known as the Cuban mahogany, is a tropical hardwood species native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is a large evergreen tree that can grow up to 40 meters tall, with a straight trunk and a dense canopy. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers and oval, leathery fruits containing winged seeds. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a pinnate venation pattern.

The wood is highly valued for its durability, fine grain, and rich reddish-brown color.
