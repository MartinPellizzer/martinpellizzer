Senecio cymbalariifolius

Senecio cymbalariifolius, commonly known as the Cape York daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, daisy-like flowers. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

Senecio cymbalariifolius is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive floral display.
