Shangwua denticulata var. denticulata

Shangwua denticulata var. denticulata, commonly known as the Chinese dandelion, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or stream banks. It has small, rough, lance-shaped leaves with fine teeth along the edges.

The flowers bloom in the spring and are followed by fluffy, wind-dispersed seeds.
