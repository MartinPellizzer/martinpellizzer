Stelis melanopus

Stelis melanopus, commonly known as the black-armed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on forest floors or along stream banks. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single or few flowers that are usually greenish or brownish in color. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Stelis melanopus is valued for its unique appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
