Scutellaria jabalambrensis

Scutellaria jabalambrensis, commonly known as the Jabalambren mountain skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its small, tubular blue or purple flowers and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
