Stromanthe angustifolia

Stromanthe angustifolia, commonly known as the Narrow-leaf Stromanthe, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with shades of green, white, and pink, which intensifies in indirect light. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns and a waxy texture. It thrives in humid environments with well-draining soil and prefers bright, indirect sunlight.

Stromanthe angustifolia is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
