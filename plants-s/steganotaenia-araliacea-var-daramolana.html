Steganotaenia araliacea var. daramolana

Steganotaenia araliacea var. daramolana, commonly known as the Daramola's spurge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Africa.

It features upright stems that are often branched and covered in fine, white hairs. The leaves are alternate, ovate to elliptic in shape, and have a slightly serrated margin. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by spherical, orange-red berries.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodland or grassland habitats.
