Senecio Hercynicus Var. Expansus

Senecio hercynicus var. expansus is a striking succulent native to southern Africa. It is known for its unique, fleshy leaves that form a rosette shape. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the growing season. It is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its ease of care and ornamental value. This variety is a fascinating example of nature's adaptability to arid environments.
