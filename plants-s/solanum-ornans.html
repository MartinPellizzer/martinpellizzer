Solanum ornans

Solanum ornans, commonly known as the ornate nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically purple or white in color. Its leaves are ovate and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Solanum ornans is typically found in tropical and subtropical regions, often growing in shaded, moist environments.
