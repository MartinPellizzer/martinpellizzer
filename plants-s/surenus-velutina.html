Surenus velutina

Surenus velutina, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are velvety to the touch, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Its fruit is a capsule that contains numerous small, dry seeds.

Surenus velutina is often found in disturbed habitats and can be invasive in certain areas.
