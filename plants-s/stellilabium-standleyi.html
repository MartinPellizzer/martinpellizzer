Stellilabium standleyi

Stellilabium standleyi, commonly known as the star-labium orchid, is a flowering plant native to the cloud forests of Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, star-shaped floral structure. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
