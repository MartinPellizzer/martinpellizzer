Spathoglottis plicata

Spathoglottis plicata, commonly known as the pleated orchid, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, pleated leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with a fragrant aroma. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing in forest understories.

Spathoglottis plicata is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental beauty and adaptability to cultivation.
