Scutellaria vigineixii

Scutellaria vigineixii, commonly known as the purple skullcap, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of East Asia.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or blue in color, which bloom in late summer. Scutellaria vigineixii is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its ornamental value and medicinal potential have made it of interest to botanists and herbalists alike.
