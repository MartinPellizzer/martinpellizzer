Senecio scandens var. crataegifolius

Senecio scandens var. crataegifolius, commonly known as the rosebay willowherb, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant features serrated, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in disturbed soils and can become invasive in some regions.

It is often found in meadows, roadsides, and along riverbanks.
