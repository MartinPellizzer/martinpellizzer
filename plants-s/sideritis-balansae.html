Sideritis balansae

Sideritis balansae, commonly known as Balansae mint, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants known for their medicinal properties. The plant features slender, hairy stems and small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. Its leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slight serration along the edges.

Sideritis balansae is valued for its aromatic foliage and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its potential health benefits.
