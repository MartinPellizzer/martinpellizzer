Silene pyrenaica

Silene pyrenaica, commonly known as the Pyrenean campion, is a flowering plant native to the Pyrenees mountain range.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or alpine environments. The plant produces striking pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

Silene pyrenaica is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in mountain ecosystems.
