Stachys lavandulifolia var. brachyodon

Stachys lavandulifolia var. brachyodon, commonly known as narrow-leaved betony, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright stems that bear opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a bluish-green hue and a fuzzy texture. The plant produces spikes of small, pale blue to violet flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its aromatic foliage and ornamental value make it a popular choice in horticulture.
