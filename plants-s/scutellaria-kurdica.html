Scutellaria kurdica

Scutellaria kurdica, commonly known as Kurdish skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Turkey and Iran.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. The plant features square stems, opposite leaves, and small, two-lipped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It is often found in rocky or grassy habitats at moderate to high altitudes.

Scutellaria kurdica is valued for its potential medicinal properties, though further research is needed to confirm its therapeutic uses.
