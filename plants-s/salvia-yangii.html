Salvia yangii

Salvia yangii, commonly known as the Yunnan sage, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces striking violet to blue flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Salvia yangii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
