Salsola murgabica

Salsola murgabica, commonly known as the Murgab saltwort, is a halophyte shrub native to Central Asia.

It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, particularly in saline soils. The plant has succulent leaves and small, greenish flowers that bloom in summer. It is adapted to withstand extreme temperatures and low water availability.

Salsola murgabica plays a role in stabilizing soils in desert regions.
