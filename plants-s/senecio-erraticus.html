Senecio erraticus

Senecio erraticus, commonly known as the wandering senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces showy yellow flowers in late summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Senecio erraticus is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
