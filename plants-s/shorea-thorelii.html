Shorea thorelii

Shorea thorelii, commonly known as the African teak, is a tropical tree native to West Africa.

It belongs to the Dipterocarpaceae family and is valued for its durable, heavy timber. The tree can grow up to 40 meters tall with a straight trunk and smooth, gray bark. Its leaves are alternate, elliptical, and have a leathery texture.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers and round, woody fruits that contain one or two seeds.
