Schoenorchis paniculata

Schoenorchis paniculata, commonly known as the panicled twayblade, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant has a slender, erect stem with a single, lance-shaped leaf. Its flowers are arranged in a paniculate inflorescence, featuring white to pale yellow petals and sepals.

The species is characterized by its lack of a visible above-ground stem, with most of its structure below the soil surface.
