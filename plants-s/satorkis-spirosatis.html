Satorkis spirosatis

Satorkis spirosatis, commonly known as the spiral-leaved satorkis, is a rare flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale blue in color. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky soils.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
