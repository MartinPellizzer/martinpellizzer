Senecio pisidicus

Senecio pisidicus, commonly known as the Crimean groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky, or sandy soils. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a composite head. It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and shrublands.

Senecio pisidicus is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
