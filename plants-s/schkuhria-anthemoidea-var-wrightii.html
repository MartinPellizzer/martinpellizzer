Schkuhria anthemoidea var. wrightii

Schkuhria anthemoidea var. wrightii, commonly known as Wright's goldenweed, is a perennial herb native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant produces yellow flowers in elongated clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in desert scrub and chaparral habitats.
