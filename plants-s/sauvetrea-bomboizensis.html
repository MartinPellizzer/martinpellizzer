Sauvetrea bomboizensis

It features striking violet-blue petals with intricate veining, and its leaves are broad and waxy, adapted to conserve moisture in its arid environment. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and moths.
