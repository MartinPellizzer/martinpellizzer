Sarcochilus usneoides

Sarcochilus usneoides, commonly known as the hanging orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to New Zealand.

It features a short, horizontal rhizome with numerous aerial roots that help it attach to trees and rocks. The plant produces delicate, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive, fragrant scent. Its leaves are strap-like and often covered in a fine, white coating.

Sarcochilus usneoides thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas.
