Schizostachyum dakrongense

Schizostachyum dakrongense, commonly known as the Dakrong bamboo, is a species of grass belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Vietnam. This plant is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can grow up to several meters in height. The leaves are long and narrow, with a smooth texture and a grassy aroma.

It is often used in traditional construction and as a source of sustainable materials due to its rapid growth rate.
