Sceptridium Ternatum Var. Dissectum

Sceptridium ternatum var. dissectum is a unique and delicate fern known for its intricate, lacy fronds. Native to the cool, shaded environments of eastern North America, this plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil. Its finely divided leaves give it a feathery appearance, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens. This variety is often mistaken for other ferns due to its delicate look, but it has distinct characteristics that set it apart. Growing slowly, it requires careful attention to maintain its lush, elegant form.
