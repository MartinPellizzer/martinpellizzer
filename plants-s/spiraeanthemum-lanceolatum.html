Spiraeanthemum lanceolatum

Spiraeanthemum lanceolatum, commonly known as the lanceleaf spirea, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, showy flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its vibrant floral display. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Spiraeanthemum lanceolatum is relatively low maintenance and drought tolerant once established.
