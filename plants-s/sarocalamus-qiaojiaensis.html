Sarocalamus qiaojiaensis

Sarocalamus qiaojiaensis, commonly known as the Qiaojia bamboo, is a species of grass family plant native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and dense clusters of leaves, typical of many bamboo species. This plant thrives in moist, temperate climates and is often found in mountainous regions. Its botanical structure includes hollow, jointed stems and long, narrow leaves.

Sarocalamus qiaojiaensis plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and contributing to soil stability.
