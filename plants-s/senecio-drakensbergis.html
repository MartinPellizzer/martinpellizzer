Senecio drakensbergis

Senecio drakensbergis, commonly known as the Drakensberg groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the Drakensberg mountain range in South Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils at high altitudes. The plant features a rosette of narrow, silver-green leaves and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a cluster. It is adapted to harsh, alpine conditions and is often found in open grasslands.

Senecio drakensbergis is an important component of the local flora and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
