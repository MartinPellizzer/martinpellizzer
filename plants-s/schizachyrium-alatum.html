Schizachyrium alatum

Schizachyrium alatum, commonly known as eastern grassland blazingstar, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the central and eastern United States.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and showy, panicle-like flower heads. The plant typically grows in open woodlands, prairies, and disturbed sites, thriving in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with the leaves often exhibiting a bluish-green hue.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to support local pollinators, making it a popular choice in native plant gardens.
