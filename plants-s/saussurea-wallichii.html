Saussurea wallichii

Saussurea wallichii, commonly known as the Tibetan yellow flower, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

The plant is adapted to cold and arid environments, with its leaves often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs to reduce water loss. Saussurea wallichii is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Tibetan and Chinese medicine.
