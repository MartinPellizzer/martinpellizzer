Stipa iskanderkulica

Stipa iskanderkulica, commonly known as the Iskanderkul grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to Central Asia, particularly found in regions of Kazakhstan and Uzbekistan. This perennial grass is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, blade-like leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that contain both male and female flowers.

Stipa iskanderkulica is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in harsh climatic conditions.
