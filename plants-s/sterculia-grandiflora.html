Sterculia grandiflora

Sterculia grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered sterculia, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. The plant has a rough, bark-like trunk and broad, palmately lobed leaves. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Sterculia grandiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in some cultures.
