Sphacophyllum tenerum

Sphacophyllum tenerum, commonly known as the slender clubmoss, is a small, evergreen plant belonging to the family Lycopodiaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit and its ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments. Sphacophyllum tenerum is often found in woodland areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal in shaded garden settings.
