Stackhousia spathulata f. obcordata

Stackhousia spathulata f. obcordata, commonly known as the obcordate stackhousia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves with a glossy, dark green surface. The plant produces clusters of white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. Its foliage is dense and makes it an attractive choice for rock gardens or as a container plant.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
