Stelis gutturosa

Stelis gutturosa, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including India, China, and Southeast Asia. The plant typically grows as an epiphytic or lithophytic species, attaching itself to trees or rocks. Its flowers are small, often white or pale yellow, and are characterized by their intricate, fragrant structure.

Stelis gutturosa thrives in humid environments with high light exposure and well-draining substrates.
