Solanum prinophyllum

Solanum prinophyllum, commonly known as the pink-flowered nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its small, pink or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded, moist environments. Its leaves are usually oval-shaped with a slightly hairy texture.

Solanum prinophyllum is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its attractive blooms.
