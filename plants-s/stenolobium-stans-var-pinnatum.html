Stenolobium stans var. pinnatum

Stenolobium stans var. pinnatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved burr legume, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with a pinnate structure, giving it its botanical name. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clustered inflorescences. Its fruits are legumes that develop into spiny pods, which contain seeds used for propagation.

This species is adapted to dry environments and is often found in savanna and rocky habitats.
