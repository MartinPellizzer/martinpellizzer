Stellaria pungens

Stellaria pungens, commonly known as the prickly starwort, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its star-shaped flowers and hairy, sometimes prickly stems and leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and streambanks. Its flowers are usually pink or white, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Stellaria pungens is often used in herbal medicine for its purported diuretic and anti-inflammatory properties.
