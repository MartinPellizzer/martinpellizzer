Sebastiania corniculata var. macrophylla

Sebastiania corniculata var. macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf sebastiania, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, elliptical leaves that are often dark green in color. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that are typically surrounded by red bracts, giving it a striking appearance. It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil.

This species is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and unique floral display.
