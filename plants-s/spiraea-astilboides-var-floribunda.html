Spiraea astilboides var. floribunda

Spiraea astilboides var. floribunda, commonly known as the flowering spiraea, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It features dense clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, arching stems and heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
