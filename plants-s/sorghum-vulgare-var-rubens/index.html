Sorghum Vulgare Var. Rubens

Sorghum vulgare var. rubens is a strikingly colorful variant of the common sorghum plant known for its vibrant red stalks. This variety is native to tropical and subtropical regions and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its tall, upright growth habit makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, requiring minimal water once established. Its unique appearance has made it a favorite among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts alike.
