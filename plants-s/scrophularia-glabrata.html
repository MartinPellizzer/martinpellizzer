Scrophularia glabrata

Scrophularia glabrata, commonly known as the smooth scrophularia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in woodland edges and grasslands. The plant features upright stems that are usually smooth and hairless, with opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves. Its flowers are tubular and typically pink or purple, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems.

Scrophularia glabrata is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
