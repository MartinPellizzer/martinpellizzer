Sansevieria masoniana

Sansevieria masoniana, commonly known as the Mason's Snake Plant, is a species of succulent native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a striking silver or white variegation. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it an excellent choice for indoor environments. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water, which is a common feature among succulents.

Sansevieria masoniana is also noted for its air-purifying properties, contributing to improved indoor air quality.
