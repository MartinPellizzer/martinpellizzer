Sarcocaulon lheritieri

Sarcocaulon lheritieri, commonly known as the silver grass, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and produces small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant has a fibrous root system and thrives in well-drained sandy soils. It is often found in coastal and inland grassland habitats.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
