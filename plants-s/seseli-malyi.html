Seseli malyi

Seseli malyi, commonly known as small seseli, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender stems and small, compound leaves that are often finely divided. The plant produces umbellate inflorescences, which are clusters of small white or greenish flowers. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is typically found in mountainous regions.

Seseli malyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
