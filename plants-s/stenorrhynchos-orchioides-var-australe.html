Stenorrhynchos orchioides var. australe

Stenorrhynchos orchioides var. australe, commonly known as the Australian small-lobed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stem and small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a single, fleshy rhizome and produces a single spike of flowers with narrow, linear petals. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassy areas or open forests.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and relatively rare orchid species within its native range.
