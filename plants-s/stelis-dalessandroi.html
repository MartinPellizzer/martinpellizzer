Stelis dalessandroi

Stelis dalessandroi, commonly known as the Dalessandro's orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its floral structure includes a single, showy bloom with intricate petal arrangements.

Stelis dalessandroi is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
