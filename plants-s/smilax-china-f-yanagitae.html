Smilax china f. yanagitae

Smilax china f. yanagitae, commonly known as the Japanese arrowroot or Chinese smilax, is a variant of the smilax genus native to East Asia.

It is a perennial climbing shrub characterized by its stout, thorned stems and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into red, fleshy berries. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in horticulture and traditional medicine.
