Spiranthes niklasii

Spiranthes niklasii, commonly known as the Niklas' ladies' tresses, is a species of orchid native to the United States.

It is characterized by its distinctive spiral arrangement of flowers, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as bogs and wetlands. It has linear, grass-like leaves and produces a delicate, fragrant bloom.

Spiranthes niklasii is a rare and endangered species, requiring specific environmental conditions for successful growth.
