Silene miqueliana f. plena

Silene miqueliana f. plena, commonly known as the plena form of Miquel's catchfly, is a flowering plant characterized by its dense, showy inflorescence.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is native to regions of southern Europe and western Asia. The plant features upright stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of pink to red flowers. Its full, double blooms make it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
