Stachyandra merana var. merana

Stachyandra merana var. merana, commonly known as the mountain stachyandra, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically blooming during the cooler months. Its leaves are narrow, grass-like, and covered in a fine layer of white hairs, which help reduce water loss in its arid habitat.

This species is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
