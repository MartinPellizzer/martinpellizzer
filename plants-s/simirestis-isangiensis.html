Simirestis isangiensis

Simirestis isangiensis, commonly known as the Congo flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Central and West Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has compound leaves with numerous small leaflets and produces pods that contain edible seeds.

It is often found in moist lowland forests and is valued for its ornamental beauty and potential medicinal properties.
