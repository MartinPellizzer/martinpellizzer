Smilax guiyangensis

Smilax guiyangensis, commonly known as the Guiyang smilax, is a climbing shrub native to China, particularly found in the Guangxi and Guizhou provinces.

It belongs to the family Aristolochiaceae and is characterized by its thorny stems and heart-shaped leaves. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
