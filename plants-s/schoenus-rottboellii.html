Schoenus rottboellii

Schoenus rottboellii, commonly known as the marsh grass or narrowleaf sedges, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to various regions in South America.

The plant has slender, cylindrical stems that are often slightly hairy, and its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green color. Schoenus rottboellii produces small, inconspicuous flowers in dense clusters, which are usually greenish or brownish in color.
