Stipa julietii

Stipa julietii, commonly known as Juliett's feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and fine, silver-green foliage that gives it a delicate, feathery appearance. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are often used in ornamental gardening for their elegant, airy look. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Stipa julietii is valued for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
