Stenotus Interior

Stenotus interior is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the arid regions of western North America. It is known for its small, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white, often during the summer months. This plant thrives in rocky soils and is often found growing in crevices along canyon walls. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to survive. Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it a subject of interest for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike.
