Styrax yapobodensis

Styrax yapobodensis, commonly known as the yapobod endemic styrax, is a rare flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Styracaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a small shrub or tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a smooth or slightly serrated edge.

The bark and flowers of Styrax yapobodensis have been studied for their potential medicinal properties.
