Sedum drymarioides

Sedum drymarioides, commonly known as the stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Sedum drymarioides is also valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
