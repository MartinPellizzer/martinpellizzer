Stenophyllum senecionis

Stenophyllum senecionis, commonly known as the narrowleaf groundsel, is a herbaceous perennial native to the western United States.

It typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellow composite flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It has a fibrous root system and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

Stenophyllum senecionis is adapted to arid conditions and is relatively drought-tolerant.
