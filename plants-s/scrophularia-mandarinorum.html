Scrophularia mandarinorum

Scrophularia mandarinorum, commonly known as the Chinese figwort, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often hairy and have a rough texture. Its flowers are usually pink to purple, arranged in clusters, and bloom in the summer months.

Scrophularia mandarinorum is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
