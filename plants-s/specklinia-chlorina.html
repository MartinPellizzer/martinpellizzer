Specklinia chlorina

Specklinia chlorina, commonly known as the green-stem orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a slender, erect stem with lance-shaped leaves that are usually a vibrant green. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, often arranged in a loose spike.

Specklinia chlorina is valued for its unique coloration and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
