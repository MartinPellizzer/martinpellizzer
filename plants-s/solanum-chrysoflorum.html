Solanum chrysoflorum

Solanum chrysoflorum, commonly known as the golden star solanum, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that resemble stars. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate with a hairy surface.

The fruit is a fleshy berry that turns from green to yellow when mature, containing numerous seeds.
