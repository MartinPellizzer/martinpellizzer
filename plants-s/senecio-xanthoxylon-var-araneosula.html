Senecio xanthoxylon var. araneosula

Senecio xanthoxylon var. araneosula, commonly known as the spiderweb senecio, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a branched, upright stem with dense, woolly foliage that resembles a spiderweb in appearance. The leaves are typically blue-gray in color and are arranged in a rosette pattern. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers during the summer months.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens and requires well-draining soil and full sun.
