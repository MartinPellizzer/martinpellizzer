Sanchezia aurea

Sanchezia aurea, commonly known as the golden sanchezia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with yellow and green patterns. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Sanchezia aurea is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
