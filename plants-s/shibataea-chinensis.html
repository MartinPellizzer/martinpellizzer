Shibataea chinensis

Shibataea chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese fir, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the family Pinaceae and is characterized by its needle-like leaves and cone-bearing structure. The tree typically grows in mountainous regions and thrives in temperate climates. It has a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 30 meters.
