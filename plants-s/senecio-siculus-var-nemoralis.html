Senecio siculus var. nemoralis

Senecio siculus var. nemoralis, commonly known as the woodland senecio, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It typically grows in woodland and rocky habitats, preferring well-drained soils. The plant features slender, upright stems covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a serrated margin. It produces small, yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months.

This variety is notable for its ability to thrive in shaded environments while maintaining a delicate, ornamental appearance.
