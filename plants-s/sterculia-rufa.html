Sterculia rufa

Sterculia rufa, commonly known as the red sterculia, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, greyish-brown bark and large, palmately lobed leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. Its roots are known to have medicinal properties used in traditional herbal remedies.

The tree is also valued for its hard, durable wood used in construction and furniture-making.
