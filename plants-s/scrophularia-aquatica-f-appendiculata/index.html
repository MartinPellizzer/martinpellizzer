Scrophularia Aquatica F. Appendiculata

Scrophularia aquatica f. appendiculata is a rare aquatic plant belonging to the Scrophulariaceae family. It is commonly found in wetlands and marshy areas where water levels remain consistently high. This botanical variant is distinguished by its unique appendicular leaves that extend from the main stem. Its presence indicates a healthy ecosystem with stable hydrological conditions. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a valuable indicator species for wetland conservation efforts.
