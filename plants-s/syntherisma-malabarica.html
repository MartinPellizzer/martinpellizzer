Syntherisma malabarica

Syntherisma malabarica, commonly known as the Malabar syntherism, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is characterized by its delicate, clustered inflorescences and small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments within tropical forests. Its leaves are ovate and have a smooth texture, while its stems are slender and somewhat woody.

Syntherisma malabarica is of interest to botanists due to its unique floral structure and limited distribution.
