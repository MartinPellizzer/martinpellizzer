Spartium heterophyllum

Spartium heterophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spartia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are alternate and pinnately compound, with narrow, linear leaflets.

Spartium heterophyllum is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in chaparral and coastal scrub habitats.
