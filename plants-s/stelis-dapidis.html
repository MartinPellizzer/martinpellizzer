Stelis dapidis

Stelis dapidis, commonly known as the Dapidis orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with narrow, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically arranged in a raceme. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Stelis dapidis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
