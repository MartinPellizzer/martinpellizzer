Sedum ichangensis

Sedum ichangensis, commonly known as the Ichang stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to China.

It is characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and small, clustered leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to rocky and arid environments, making it a hardy species suitable for rock gardens.

Sedum ichangensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
