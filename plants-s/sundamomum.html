Sundamomum

Sundamomum, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a tropical aquatic plant native to Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom on long, winding stems above the water surface. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that float on the water's surface, providing habitat for various aquatic organisms. Its flowers typically range in color from white to pink and yellow, depending on the species.

Sundamomum plays an important role in traditional medicine and is also revered in many cultural and religious practices.
