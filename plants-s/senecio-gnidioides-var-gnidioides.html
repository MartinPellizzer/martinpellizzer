Senecio gnidioides var. gnidioides

Senecio gnidioides var. gnidioides, commonly known as the Cape marigold, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It features hairy, branching stems and produces vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is adapted to arid environments and is drought-tolerant.

The flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, making it a valuable addition to gardens.
