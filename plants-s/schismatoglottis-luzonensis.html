Schismatoglottis luzonensis

Schismatoglottis luzonensis, commonly known as the Luzon slipper orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its unique, slipper-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are usually broad and grass-like, while its roots are fibrous and adapted for nutrient absorption.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its striking floral display.
