Sorghastrum secundum

Sorghastrum secundum, commonly known as blue grama, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green blades and tufted inflorescences that are often tinged with purple or red. This plant thrives in dry, sandy soils and is commonly found in grasslands and open woodlands. It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing forage for wildlife.

Its drought tolerance makes it a valuable component of native plant communities.
