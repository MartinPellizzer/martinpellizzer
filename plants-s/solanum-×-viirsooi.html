Solanum × viirsooi

Solanum × viirsooi, commonly known as the hybrid nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that develop into berries containing numerous seeds. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with a slightly hairy texture.

Solanum × viirsooi is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and fruiting habits.
