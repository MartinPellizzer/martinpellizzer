Sansevieria formosa

Sansevieria formosa, commonly known as the "Mother-in-Law's Tongue," is a striking evergreen plant native to Africa.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, yellow, and white, creating an eye-catching appearance. The plant is highly tolerant of low light and requires minimal watering, making it ideal for indoor environments. Its air-purifying qualities make it a popular choice for improving indoor air quality.

Sansevieria formosa is also known for its resilience, thriving in a variety of conditions with minimal care.
