Schoenoplectus juncoides var. hotarui

Schoenoplectus juncoides var. hotarui, commonly known as the Hotarui bulrush, is a perennial wetland plant native to Japan.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height, often forming dense stands in marshy or aquatic environments. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers, which are wind-pollinated. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, typically emerging from the base of the plant.

This variety is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing wetland soils and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
