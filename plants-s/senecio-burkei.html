Senecio burkei

Senecio burkei, commonly known as the Burke's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with slender, branched stems and small, daisy-like flowers. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Senecio burkei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
