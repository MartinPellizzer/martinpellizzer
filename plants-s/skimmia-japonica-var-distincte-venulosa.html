Skimmia japonica var. distincte-venulosa

Skimmia japonica var. distincte-venulosa, commonly known as the variegated skimmia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with striking white and yellow variegation along the veins, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in late winter or early spring, followed by red berries that persist into winter. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens or as a container plant.

Its ornamental value lies in its foliage and seasonal interest, making it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
