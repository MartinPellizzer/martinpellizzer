Sphaerostephanos arbuscula subsp. africana

Sphaerostephanos arbuscula subsp.

africana, commonly known as the African star orchid, is a small, herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It features a compact, branching stem with dense clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue.

The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
