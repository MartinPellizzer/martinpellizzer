Sarcanthus laosensis

Sarcanthus laosensis, commonly known as the Laos orchid, is a rare and endemic species found primarily in the mountainous regions of Laos.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant has narrow, strap-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in primary rainforests.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, Sarcanthus laosensis is considered a vulnerable species.
