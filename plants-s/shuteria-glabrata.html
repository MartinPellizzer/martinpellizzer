Shuteria glabrata

Shuteria glabrata, commonly known as the smooth shuteria, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial with narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically covered in a fine layer of hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink, blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is often cultivated in gardens across temperate climates.
