Stelin album

Stelin album, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Mediterranean region.

It features striking silvery-white leaves with a velvety texture, which help reflect sunlight and reduce water loss. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers in the spring, adding a subtle aesthetic to gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it adaptable to various garden settings.

Stelin album is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
