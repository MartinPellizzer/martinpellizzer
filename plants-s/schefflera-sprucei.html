Schefflera sprucei

Schefflera sprucei, commonly known as the Spruce's umbrella tree, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded foliage that resembles an umbrella, with thick, leathery leaves arranged in a compact, symmetrical pattern. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters in its natural habitat. Its leaves are dark green and glossy, providing a striking visual appeal.

Schefflera sprucei is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
