Schefflera enneaphylla

Schefflera enneaphylla, commonly known as the nine-leaf schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It features a distinctive, umbrella-like canopy composed of multiple leaflets radiating from a central stem. The plant's leaves are typically dark green and glossy, with a slightly waxy texture. It produces small, white, fragrant flowers followed by orange-red berries.

Schefflera enneaphylla is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to indoor environments.
