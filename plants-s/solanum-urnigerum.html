Solanum urnigerum

Solanum urnigerum, commonly known as the purple nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 meter, featuring hairy stems and broad, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to violet, which bloom in the spring. Its fruit is a fleshy berry that turns from green to purple as it matures, often containing several seeds.

Solanum urnigerum belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae), which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes.
