Scaphosepalum gibberosum

Scaphosepalum gibberosum, commonly known as the rough-stemmed orchid, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. The flowers are typically small and clustered, with a distinctive hooded labellum that gives the plant its scientific name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
