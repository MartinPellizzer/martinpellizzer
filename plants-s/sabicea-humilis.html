Sabicea humilis

Sabicea humilis, commonly known as the dwarf sabicea, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. The plant features simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its small, white or pale yellow flowers are clustered in racemes and bloom during the rainy season.

Sabicea humilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
