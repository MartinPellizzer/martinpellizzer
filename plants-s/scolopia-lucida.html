Scolopia lucida

Scolopia lucida, commonly known as the shining nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and tobacco. The plant features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue, with a distinctive yellowish throat. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.
