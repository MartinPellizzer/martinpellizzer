Silene quadridentata var. marchesettii

Silene quadridentata var. marchesettii, commonly known as the Marchesettii campion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features slender stems and small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
