Schefflera merrittii

Schefflera merrittii, commonly known as the Hawaiian schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Hawaii.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like clusters of leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant has compound leaves with multiple leaflets arranged in a radiating pattern, and its flowers are small, greenish-white, and clustered in inflorescences. Schefflera merrittii thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas and moist forests.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
