Statice boissieri

Statice boissieri, commonly known as the desert statice, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of the Mediterranean.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and small, showy flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant is adapted to survive in dry environments, with hairy leaves that reduce water loss. It blooms during the summer months and is often used in ornamental gardening due to its drought tolerance and vibrant appearance.

Statice boissieri is also valued for its ability to thrive in poor soils, making it a hardy choice for xeriscaping.
