Scutellaria ambigua

Scutellaria ambigua, commonly known as the ambiguous skullcap, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of North America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features square, hairy stems and opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. Its small, tubular flowers are typically blue to violet in color, arranged in whorls along the stem.

Scutellaria ambigua is often found in moist habitats such as meadows and streambanks.
