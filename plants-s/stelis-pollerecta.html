Stelis pollerecta

Stelis pollerecta, commonly known as the erect-stem orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, branching stems and small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem.

Stelis pollerecta is known for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
