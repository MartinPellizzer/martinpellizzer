Sobralia blanda

Sobralia blanda, commonly known as the soft sobralia, is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. This plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, pale yellow to orange flowers. The leaves are typically broad and ovate, with a smooth texture.

Sobralia blanda thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
