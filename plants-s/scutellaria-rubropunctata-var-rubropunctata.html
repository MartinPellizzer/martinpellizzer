Scutellaria rubropunctata var. rubropunctata

Scutellaria rubropunctata var. rubropunctata, commonly known as the red-spotted skullcap, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist to wet soils in woodlands and along stream banks. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are blue to violet, tubular, and borne in whorls along the stem.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
