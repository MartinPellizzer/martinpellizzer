Siebera oblonga

Siebera oblonga, commonly known as the narrowleaf siebera, is a member of the Ericaceae family.

It is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, particularly in countries like China, Vietnam, and Myanmar. The plant features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, and it produces small, berry-like fruits.

Siebera oblonga is often found in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in cool, moist environments.
