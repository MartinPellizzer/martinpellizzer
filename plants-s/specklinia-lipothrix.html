Specklinia lipothrix

Specklinia lipothrix, commonly known as the waxy leaf orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that help retain moisture in its natural habitat. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom during the rainy season. Specklinia lipothrix thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Its unique botanical features make it a popular choice for collectors and enthusiasts of rare orchids.
