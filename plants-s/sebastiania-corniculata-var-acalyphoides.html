Sebastiania corniculata var. acalyphoides

Sebastiania corniculata var. acalyphoides, commonly known as the red-leafed sebastiania, is a flowering plant native to parts of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange foliage, which contrasts with its small, inconspicuous green flowers. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, often reaching heights of up to 1 meter. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique leaf coloration and compact growth habit make it a distinctive addition to tropical and subtropical landscapes.
