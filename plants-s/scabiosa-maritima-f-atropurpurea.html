Scabiosa maritima f. atropurpurea

Scabiosa maritima f. atropurpurea, commonly known as the purple scabious, is a compact perennial plant native to coastal regions of Europe.

It features small, clustered flowers in shades of pink and purple, which bloom from late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, hairy stems and heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly rough texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and ability to attract pollinators.
