Salacia floribunda f. subintegra

Salacia floribunda f. subintegra, commonly known as the integrated flowering salacia, is a unique variant of the Salacia floribunda species.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and showy, clustered flowers that bloom in vibrant shades of yellow and orange. The plant is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are typically elliptical with a smooth texture and a glossy surface, enhancing its ornamental appeal.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and prolonged flowering period.
