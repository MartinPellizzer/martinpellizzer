Sicyos remyanus

Sicyos remyanus, commonly known as the giant rat-tail fern, is a tropical plant native to parts of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, feather-like fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant has a unique rhizome system that allows it to spread and form dense colonies. Its reproductive structures include both male and female spore-bearing cones, typical of ferns.

Sicyos remyanus thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found near water sources.
