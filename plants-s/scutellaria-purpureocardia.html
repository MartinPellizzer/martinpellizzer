Scutellaria purpureocardia

Scutellaria purpureocardia, commonly known as purpleheart skullcap, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its showy, violet-blue flowers are borne in whorled clusters at the tips of the stems.

Scutellaria purpureocardia is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
