Sedum cretense

Sedum cretense, commonly known as the Crete stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and small, clustered leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to dry, rocky environments and is drought-tolerant.

Sedum cretense is often used in rock gardens and as a decorative plant in arid landscapes.
