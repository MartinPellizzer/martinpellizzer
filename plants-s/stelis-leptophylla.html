Stelis leptophylla

Stelis leptophylla, commonly known as the slender-leaf stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and narrow, elongated leaves. The plant typically produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Stelis leptophylla is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
