Stachys subcrenata f. baldaccii

Stachys subcrenata f. baldaccii, commonly known as the Baldaccii hedge nettle, is a compact, low-growing perennial plant native to southern Europe.

It features hairy, oval-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale purple to pink flowers arranged in whorled clusters along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique foliage and delicate flowering habit.
