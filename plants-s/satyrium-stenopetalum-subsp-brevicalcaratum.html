Satyrium stenopetalum subsp. brevicalcaratum

Satyrium stenopetalum subsp.

brevicalcaratum, commonly known as the narrow-petaled satyrium, is a member of the Orchidaceae family. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive, narrow petals.

The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understoreys. Its flowers are usually small and have a unique structure with a spurred lip.

This subspecies is notable for its limited distribution and is often found in specific regions of Southeast Asia.
