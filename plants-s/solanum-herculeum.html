Solanum herculeum

Solanum herculeum, commonly known as the Hercules tomato, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other notable plants like potatoes and peppers. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into fleshy, orange-red berries. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with a rough texture and a strong, sometimes unpleasant odor.

Solanum herculeum is noted for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
