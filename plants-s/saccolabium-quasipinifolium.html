Saccolabium quasipinifolium

Saccolabium quasipinifolium, commonly known as the needleleaf saccolabium, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of South America.

Saccolabium quasipinifolium is often found in rocky or sandy habitats and is adapted to thrive in harsh, semi-desert conditions.
