Stellularia media var. bononiensis

Stellularia media var. bononiensis, commonly known as the Italian starwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive white midrib and produces small, star-shaped white flowers in early summer. Its botanical characteristics include a fibrous root system and a basal rosette of leaves.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is part of the Cyperaceae family, commonly known as the sedge family.
