Scutellaria hastata

Scutellaria hastata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved skullcap, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, tubular blue or violet flowers. It typically grows in moist, wooded areas and is often found in swampy or wetland habitats.

Scutellaria hastata is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
