Stipa × bloomeri

Stipa × bloomeri, commonly known as the Blue Grama grass, is a hybrid grass species native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green blades and compact growth habit, making it well-suited for dry, prairie-like environments. The plant produces slender, cylindrical seed heads that are often tinged with reddish hues, adding to its ornamental value. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for native plant gardens.

Its ecological importance includes providing habitat and forage for various wildlife species.
