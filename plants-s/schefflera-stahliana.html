Schefflera stahliana

Schefflera stahliana, commonly known as the Stahliana umbrella plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of leaves, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant features palmate leaves with a glossy, dark green surface and a slightly waxy texture. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits.

Schefflera stahliana is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in indoor environments.
