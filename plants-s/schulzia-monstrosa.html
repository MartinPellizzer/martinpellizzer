Schulzia monstrosa

Schulzia monstrosa, commonly known as the giant rock fig, is a striking shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, waxy, green leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by edible, fig-like fruits. Schulzia monstrosa is adapted to arid environments and thrives in well-drained soils.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
