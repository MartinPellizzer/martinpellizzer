Stapelia × trifida

Stapelia × trifida, commonly known as the hybrid star cactus, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a branched stem structure with thick, fleshy leaves that store water, enabling it to survive in arid conditions. The plant produces striking, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom in late spring or early summer. Its leaves are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, and the plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Stapelia × trifida is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
