Stachys Recta Var. Labiosa

Stachys recta var. labiosa is a unique variant of the common redstem plant known for its distinctive pinkish-purple flower spikes. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its compact growth habit makes it ideal for rock gardens and borders. The flowers bloom in late spring, adding a splash of color to the landscape. It is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
