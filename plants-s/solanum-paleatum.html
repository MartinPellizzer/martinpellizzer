Solanum paleatum

Solanum paleatum, commonly known as the pale nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant features small, pale green leaves and produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. Its fruit is a small, round berry that turns from green to red as it matures.

Solanum paleatum is often grown for its ornamental value in gardens due to its unique foliage and delicate flowers.
