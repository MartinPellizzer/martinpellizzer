Statice minuta var. calcarae

Statice minuta var. calcarae, commonly known as the rock statice, is a compact, flowering plant native to rocky and coastal environments.

It is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and clusters of tiny, showy flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. It is drought-tolerant and often used in xeriscaping due to its low maintenance requirements.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in harsh environments.
