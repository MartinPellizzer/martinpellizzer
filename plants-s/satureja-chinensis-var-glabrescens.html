Satureja chinensis var. glabrescens

Satureja chinensis var. glabrescens, commonly known as Chinese thyme, is a perennial herb native to China.

It is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its essential oils, rich in thymol and carvacrol, contribute to its medicinal and culinary uses.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a spice in traditional Chinese cuisine.
