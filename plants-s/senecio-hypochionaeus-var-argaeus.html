Senecio hypochionaeus var. argaeus

Senecio hypochionaeus var. argaeus, commonly known as the Argaeus groundsels, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters. The plant typically has narrow, linear leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It is native to southern Africa and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
