Stelis leptochila

Stelis leptochila, commonly known as the slender-leafed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees in tropical rainforests. The plant has small, narrow leaves and produces delicate, white or pale pink flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
