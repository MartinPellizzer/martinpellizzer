Selinum rigidulum

Selinum rigidulum, commonly known as rigid wormwood, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that are often covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in dense, spherical clusters called capitula. Its leaves are narrow, linear, and have a strong, aromatic scent.

Selinum rigidulum is often found in dry, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.
