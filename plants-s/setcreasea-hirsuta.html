Setcreasea hirsuta

Setcreasea hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy setcreasea, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and hairy foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice among gardeners in arid regions.
