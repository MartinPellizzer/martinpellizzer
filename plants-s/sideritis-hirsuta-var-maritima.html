Sideritis hirsuta var. maritima

Sideritis hirsuta var. maritima, commonly known as the coastal sideritis, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its hairy, gray-green foliage and small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. This plant thrives in coastal and sandy environments, often growing in well-drained soils. It is adapted to withstand salt spray and strong winds, making it a resilient species in harsh maritime conditions.

The plant is also valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
