Stelis caliensis

Stelis caliensis, commonly known as the Caliente stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America.

This epiphytic orchid typically grows on tree branches or rocks, relying on its roots to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale pink, often with intricate patterns. The plant prefers warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining substrates.

Its unique morphology and ornamental flowers make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
