Sabicea duparquetiana var. robbianella

Sabicea duparquetiana var. robbianella, commonly known as the Robbianella sabeltree, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features trifoliate leaves with serrated edges and produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers. Its fruit is a slender pod that splits open to release seeds, contributing to its ecological role in seed dispersal.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and floral display.
