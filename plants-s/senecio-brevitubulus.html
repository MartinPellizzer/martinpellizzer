Senecio brevitubulus

Senecio brevitubulus, commonly known as the short-tubed groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial with narrow, linear leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. It typically thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
