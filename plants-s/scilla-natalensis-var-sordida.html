Scilla natalensis var. sordida

Scilla natalensis var. sordida, commonly known as the Natal sapphire, is a bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features striking blue-violet flowers that bloom in early spring, typically in clusters of 3-6. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are evergreen and persist through the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate appearance and seasonal blooming habit.
