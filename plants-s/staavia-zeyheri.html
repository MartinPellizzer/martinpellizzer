Staavia zeyheri

Staavia zeyheri, commonly known as Zeyher's daisies, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils. It produces small, inconspicuous fruits that contain seeds dispersed by wind.
