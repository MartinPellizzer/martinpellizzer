Stachys kouyangensis

Stachys kouyangensis, commonly known as the Kouyang betony, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or blue in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
