Spigelia pauciflora

Spigelia pauciflora, commonly known as the few-flowered spigelia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae, which includes milkweeds and related plants. The plant features opposite, elliptic leaves with a slightly hairy surface and produces clusters of small, tubular, red to orange flowers.
