Swertia shintenensis

Swertia shintenensis, commonly known as the Shinten gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest understories. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Swertia shintenensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
