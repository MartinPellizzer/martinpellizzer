Streptocarpus × taylorii

Streptocarpus × taylorii, commonly known as the hybrid streptocarpus, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Streptocarpus section Streptocarpus and Streptocarpus section Pseudostreptocarpus. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including purple, pink, and white. It typically has a compact, bushy growth habit with broad, velvety leaves.

Streptocarpus × taylorii is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
