Solanum terminale subsp. welwitschii

Solanum terminale subsp.

welwitschii, commonly known as the Welwitschii nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa. It typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, adapting to harsh environmental conditions.

The plant features small, white or pale green flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by berries that range in color from yellow to red. Its leaves are usually narrow and fleshy, helping it conserve water.

The species is notable for its resilience and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
