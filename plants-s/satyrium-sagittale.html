Satyrium sagittale

Satyrium sagittale, commonly known as the arrowhead orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with purple or brown markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
