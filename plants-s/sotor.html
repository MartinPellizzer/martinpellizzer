Sotor

Sotor, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other similar plants. The plant features showy, daisy-like flowers that are typically white or yellow with a central disk. Sotor is often found in grasslands and open habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
