Sweetiopsis hassleri var. glabrescens

Sweetiopsis hassleri var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth sweetiopsis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white to pale pink petals. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun exposure. It has smooth, glabrous leaves and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its attractive blooms and hardy nature.
