Senecio imaii

Senecio imaii, commonly known as the Imai's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the island of Socotra.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant is notable for its unique, elongated leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs. Senecio imaii thrives in arid and rocky environments, showcasing its adaptation to the harsh conditions of its native habitat.

Due to its limited distribution and ecological significance, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
