Sphaeranthus amaranthoides

Sphaeranthus amaranthoides, commonly known as the globe amaranth, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its compact, globe-shaped inflorescence composed of numerous small, showy flowers. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with hairy, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually red, orange, or yellow, and they bloom in dense clusters.

Sphaeranthus amaranthoides is often cultivated as a ornamental plant due to its vibrant color and unique appearance.
