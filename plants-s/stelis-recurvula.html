Stelis recurvula

Stelis recurvula, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are small and clustered, often displaying vibrant colors such as pink and white.

Stelis recurvula is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its distinctive appearance.
