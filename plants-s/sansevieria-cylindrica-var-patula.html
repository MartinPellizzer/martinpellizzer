Sansevieria cylindrica var. patula

Sansevieria cylindrica var. patula, commonly known as the "snake plant" or "mother-in-law's tongue," is a popular indoor plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with light green variegation. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it ideal for beginners. Its leaves are stiff and slightly curved, giving it a distinctive appearance.

This variety is also noted for its air-purifying qualities, as it can help remove toxins from the air.
