Schefflera weibeliana

Schefflera weibeliana, commonly known as the Weibel's umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like crown of large, compound leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by red berries, which are an important food source for birds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.

Schefflera weibeliana is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
