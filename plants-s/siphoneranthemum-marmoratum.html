Siphoneranthemum marmoratum

Siphoneranthemum marmoratum, commonly known as the marble leaf plant, is a unique species native to South Africa.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its striking, mottled leaves that display a marbled pattern of green, white, and pink. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its distinctive appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
