Swartzia cubensis var. nicaraguensis

Swartzia cubensis var. nicaraguensis, commonly known as the Nicaraguan swartzia, is a tropical tree species native to Central America.

It belongs to the Leguminosae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces elongated pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland tropical forests.
