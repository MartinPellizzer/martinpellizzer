Styrax japonicus f. zigzag

Styrax japonicus f. zigzag, commonly known as zigzag styrax, is a unique cultivar of the styrax plant characterized by its distinctive, zigzagging growth pattern.

This ornamental plant is native to Japan and is prized for its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in late spring. The leaves are typically elliptical and glossy, providing an attractive backdrop to the blossoms. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, acidic soil.

Due to its unusual form and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and used in landscape design.
