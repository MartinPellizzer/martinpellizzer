Sideritis romana var. lazae

Sideritis romana var. lazae, commonly known as the Laza's ironwort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its upright stems, opposite leaves, and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. It thrives in rocky and dry environments, often found in alpine and subalpine habitats.

Sideritis romana var. lazae is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
