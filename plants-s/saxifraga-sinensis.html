Saxifraga sinensis

Saxifraga sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese stonecrop, is a perennial plant native to China.

It is characterized by its fleshy, succulent leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or red, which bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Saxifraga sinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature in temperate climates.
