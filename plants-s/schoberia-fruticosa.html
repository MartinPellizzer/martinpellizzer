Schoberia fruticosa

Schoberia fruticosa, commonly known as the evergreen shrub, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woody growth habit and produces small, fragrant yellow flowers. The plant has narrow, leathery leaves that are typically arranged in opposite pairs along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Schoberia fruticosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticulture for its hardy nature and visual interest.
