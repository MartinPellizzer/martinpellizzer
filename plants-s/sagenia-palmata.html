Sagenia palmata

Sagenia palmata, commonly known as the giant palm, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that can reach several meters in diameter. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-white flowers, which are followed by bright red, fleshy fruits. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to thrive in moist, well-drained soils.

Sagenia palmata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
