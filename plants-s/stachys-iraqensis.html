Stachys iraqensis

Stachys iraqensis, commonly known as the Iraqi hedge-nettle, is a flowering plant native to the Middle East, particularly Iraq.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and clusters of small, tubular flowers. It typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions.

Stachys iraqensis is noted for its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties.
