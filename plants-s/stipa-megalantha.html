Stipa megalantha

Stipa megalantha, commonly known as giant feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and feathery plumes that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Stipa megalantha is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in landscaping and restoration projects.
