Stenochlaena raciborskii

Stenochlaena raciborskii, commonly known as the water fern, is a species of aquatic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It thrives in freshwater environments, particularly in slow-moving rivers and ponds. The plant has delicate, floating leaves that are divided into small, finger-like segments. Its rhizomes form dense mats on the water surface, contributing to its ecological role in stabilizing water bodies.

Stenochlaena raciborskii is also valued for its ornamental appeal in aquatic gardens.
