Stipa paramilloensis

Stipa paramilloensis, commonly known as the Paramillo grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly found in Argentina and Chile. This perennial grass is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, blade-like leaves. It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

Stipa paramilloensis plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small fauna in its native environment.
