Stenia aurorae

Stenia aurorae, commonly known as the Dawn's Grace, is a rare flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

It features striking, velvety purple petals that bloom at dawn, giving it its poetic name. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with slender, hairy stems. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine, silvery down.

Stenia aurorae thrives in cool, moist environments and is highly sensitive to changes in climate.
