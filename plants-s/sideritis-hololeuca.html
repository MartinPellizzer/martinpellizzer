Sideritis hololeuca

Sideritis hololeuca, commonly known as the white-haired ironwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, pale blue or white flowers. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Sideritis hololeuca is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is valued for its medicinal properties.
