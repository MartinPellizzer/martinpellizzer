Salacia chesseana

Salacia chesseana, commonly known as the Chessean salacia, is a flowering plant species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Salicaceae, which includes willows and poplars, though it is more closely related to the genus Salacia. The plant is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and heart-shaped leaves. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments within forests.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
