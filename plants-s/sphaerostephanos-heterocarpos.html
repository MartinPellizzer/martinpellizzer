Sphaerostephanos heterocarpos

Sphaerostephanos heterocarpos, commonly known as the African starfruit, is a tropical shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, edible berries that are typically yellow or orange when ripe. The leaves are simple, alternate, and have a rough texture with a strong scent.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a warm, humid climate for optimal growth.
