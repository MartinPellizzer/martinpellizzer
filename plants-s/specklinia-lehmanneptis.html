Specklinia lehmanneptis

Specklinia lehmanneptis, commonly known as the Lemon Tree Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces fragrant, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling small lemons, hence its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil.

Specklinia lehmanneptis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
