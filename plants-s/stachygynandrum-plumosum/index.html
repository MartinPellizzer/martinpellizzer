Stachygynandrum Plumosum

Mexican feather grass, scientifically known as Stachygynandrum plumosum, is a striking ornamental plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America. With its delicate, feathery plumes that sway gracefully in the breeze, it adds a touch of elegance to any garden or landscape. This plant is prized for its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for both residential and commercial landscaping. Whether used as a border plant or in mass plantings, Mexican feather grass offers visual interest throughout the growing season.
