Senecio aloides

Senecio aloides, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers and upright growth habit. The plant features succulent, fleshy leaves that help it conserve water in arid environments. It produces small, daisy-like blooms that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Senecio aloides is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant appearance and low maintenance requirements.
