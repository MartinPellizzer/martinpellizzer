Streptocarpus polyanthus subsp. comptonii

Streptocarpus polyanthus subsp.

comptonii, commonly known as the Compton's streptocarpus, is a species native to South Africa. It is a flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family, characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, tubular flowers.
