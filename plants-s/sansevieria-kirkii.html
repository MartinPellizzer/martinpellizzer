Sansevieria kirkii

Sansevieria kirkii, commonly known as the African spear or Kirk's snake plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, upright leaves that are typically green with a pale yellow or white margin. The plant is characterized by its ability to store water in its fleshy, sword-shaped leaves, making it highly drought-tolerant. It produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that emerge from the base of the plant.

Sansevieria kirkii is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and air-purifying properties.
