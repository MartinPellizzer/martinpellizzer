Struthanthus pseudolepidotus

Struthanthus pseudolepidotus, commonly known as the desert star.

This flowering plant is native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. It is a low-growing shrub with small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant has adapted to harsh desert conditions with thick, waxy leaves that reduce water loss.

Its unique morphology and drought tolerance make it an important component of desert ecosystems.
