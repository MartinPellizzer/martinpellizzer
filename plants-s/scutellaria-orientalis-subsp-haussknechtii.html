Scutellaria orientalis subsp. haussknechtii

Scutellaria orientalis subsp.

haussknechtii, commonly known as the Eastern skullcap, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern Mediterranean region. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny to partially shaded environments.

The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by aromatic foliage and showy flowers. Scutellaria orientalis subsp.

haussknechtii is often found in dry habitats such as cliffs, slopes, and open woodlands.
