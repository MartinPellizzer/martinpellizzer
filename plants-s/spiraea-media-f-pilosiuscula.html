Spiraea media f. pilosiuscula

Spiraea media f. pilosiuscula, commonly known as the silver-leaf spiraea, is a compact, deciduous shrub characterized by its dense, upright growth habit.

It features small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. The foliage is distinctive, with a silvery-gray underside that gives it a unique, almost metallic appearance. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of maintenance in landscaping.
