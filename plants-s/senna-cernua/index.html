Senna Cernua

Senna cernua is a delicate flowering plant known for its unique weeping habit and vibrant yellow blooms. Native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia, it thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil. Its cascading branches and clusters of small, fragrant flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardens. The plant is also valued for its medicinal properties, with traditional uses in treating various ailments. Despite its beauty, Senna cernua requires careful attention to avoid overwatering and pests that can threaten its health.
