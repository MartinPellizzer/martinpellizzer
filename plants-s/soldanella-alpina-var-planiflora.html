Soldanella alpina var. planiflora

Soldanella alpina var. planiflora, commonly known as the Alpine soldanella, is a low-growing perennial plant native to alpine regions of Europe.

It features small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has basal leaves that are oval to lance-shaped with hairy surfaces. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in mountain meadows.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to cold, high-altitude environments.
