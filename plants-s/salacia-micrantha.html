Salacia micrantha

Salacia micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered salacia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Salaciacae and is characterized by its small, clustered white or greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture.

Salacia micrantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
