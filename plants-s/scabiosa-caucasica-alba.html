Scabiosa caucasica alba

Scabiosa caucasica alba, commonly known as the Caucasian white scabious, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its daisy-like flowers, which are typically white or pale pink in color. The plant features a central yellow disc surrounded by numerous slender, white petals. It grows in meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils and full sun.

Scabiosa caucasica alba is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
