Salix triandra var. androgyna

Salix triandra var. androgyna, commonly known as the Androgyna willow, is a variant of the European white willow.

It is characterized by its dioecious nature, with separate male and female plants. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a smooth, shiny surface. It thrives in moist, temperate climates and is often found near water bodies.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
