Solanum kaernbachii

Solanum kaernbachii, commonly known as the Kaernbach's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and peppers. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into berries containing toxic seeds. It is typically found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its toxic properties, it is not commonly cultivated for agricultural purposes.
