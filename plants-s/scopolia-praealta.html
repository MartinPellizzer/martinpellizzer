Scopolia praealta

Scopolia praealta, commonly known as the giant scopolia, is a striking perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters, making it a dramatic addition to gardens. The plant produces large, showy white or pale purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of heart-shaped, deeply lobed leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down.

Scopolia praealta is known for its strong fragrance and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
