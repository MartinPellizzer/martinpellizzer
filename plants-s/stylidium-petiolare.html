Stylidium petiolare

Stylidium petiolare, commonly known as the petioled sun-orchid, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, sun-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in coastal areas. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are bright yellow with a distinctive, spurred lip.

Stylidium petiolare is known for its short flowering period and is often found in coastal heathlands.
