Saintpaulia magungensis

Saintpaulia magungensis, commonly known as the Magungensis African Violet, is a species native to Tanzania.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant features velvety, dark green leaves with a waxy texture and produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink and white. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and moderate temperatures for optimal growth.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
