Sphaerocionium nitidulum

Sphaerocionium nitidulum, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed sphaerocion, is a small, herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open woodland or grassland habitats. The plant has smooth, cylindrical stems that are usually less than 30 centimeters in height. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species.
