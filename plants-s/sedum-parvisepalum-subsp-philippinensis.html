Sedum parvisepalum subsp. philippinensis

Sedum parvisepalum subsp.

philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine sedum, is a succulent plant native to the Philippines. It features fleshy, upright stems and small, clustered leaves that are typically green or slightly bluish in color.

The plant produces star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange, which bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

This subspecies is noted for its drought tolerance and ability to flourish in arid environments.
