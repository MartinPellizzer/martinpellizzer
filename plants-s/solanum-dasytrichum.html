Solanum dasytrichum

Solanum dasytrichum, commonly known as the hairy nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South America.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that develop into berry-like fruits, which are typically green when mature. It thrives in moist, temperate climates and is often found in shaded, woodland environments.

Due to its toxic properties, it is important to handle the plant with care.
