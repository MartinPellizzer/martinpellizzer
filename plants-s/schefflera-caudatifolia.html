Schefflera caudatifolia

Schefflera caudatifolia, commonly known as the cat-tail schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, umbrella-like inflorescence that resembles a cat's tail, giving it its common name. The plant features large, palmate leaves with a glossy, dark green surface and prominent veins. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant.

Schefflera caudatifolia is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for both outdoor gardens and indoor spaces.
