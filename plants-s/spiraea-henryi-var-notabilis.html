Spiraea henryi var. notabilis

Spiraea henryi var. notabilis, commonly known as the Chinese spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features dense, rounded growth with small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has alternate, simple leaves that are elliptic to ovate in shape. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value comes from its compact form and showy floral display.
