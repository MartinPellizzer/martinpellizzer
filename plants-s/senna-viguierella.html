Senna viguierella

Senna viguierella, commonly known as the golden senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with small, serrated leaflets and yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Senna viguierella is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
