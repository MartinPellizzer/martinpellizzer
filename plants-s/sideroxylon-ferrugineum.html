Sideroxylon ferrugineum

Sideroxylon ferrugineum, commonly known as the red ironwood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, hard wood that is highly valued for its durability and strength. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody capsules containing numerous seeds.

The tree is often found in lowland and hill forest areas, contributing to the biodiversity of its native habitat.
