Senecio fukienensis

Senecio fukienensis, commonly known as the Chinese groundsel, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid environments. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous fruits.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
