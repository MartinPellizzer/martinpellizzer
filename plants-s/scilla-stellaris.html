Scilla stellaris

Scilla stellaris, commonly known as the star-of-Bethlehem, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features slender, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of star-shaped, sky-blue flowers in early spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and is also used in traditional medicine.

Scilla stellaris is known for its ability to naturalize and spread readily in suitable conditions.
