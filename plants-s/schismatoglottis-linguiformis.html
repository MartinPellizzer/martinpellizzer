Schismatoglottis linguiformis

Schismatoglottis linguiformis, commonly known as the sword leaf orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its elongated, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Schismatoglottis linguiformis is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant due to its attractive foliage.
