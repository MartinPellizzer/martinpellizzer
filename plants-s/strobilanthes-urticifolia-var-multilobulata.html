Strobilanthes urticifolia var. multilobulata

Strobilanthes urticifolia var. multilobulata, commonly known as the multilobed flame flower, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its striking, tubular, red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are typically ovate to lanceolate, with serrated margins and a hairy surface. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

This plant is notable for its ornamental value and is cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
