Sempervivum boutignyanum var. adoxum

Sempervivum boutignyanum var. adoxum, commonly known as the "Snowy Peaks" or "Alpine Carpet," is a hardy succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features dense clusters of fleshy, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically blue-gray or silver in color, providing a striking visual contrast against its surroundings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It is well adapted to rocky, well-drained soils and can thrive in full sun to partial shade.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to form dense, low-maintenance ground covers in alpine gardens.
