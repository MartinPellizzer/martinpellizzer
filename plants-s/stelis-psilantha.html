Stelis psilantha

Stelis psilantha, commonly known as the slender stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features a short, erect stem with few leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Stelis psilantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
