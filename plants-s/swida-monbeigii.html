Swida monbeigii

Swida monbeigii, commonly known as the Chinese maple, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its ornamental value due to its vibrant autumn foliage. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that turn red or orange in the fall. It produces small, white flowers in the spring, followed by small, red fruits.

Swida monbeigii is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
