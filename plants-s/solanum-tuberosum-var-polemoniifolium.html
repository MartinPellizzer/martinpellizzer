Solanum tuberosum var. polemoniifolium

Solanum tuberosum var. polemoniifolium, commonly known as the polemoniifolium potato, is a variant of the common potato with distinctive foliage.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces clusters of white or pale purple flowers, typical of the Solanaceae family. Its tubers are generally smaller than standard potatoes but are noted for their unique flavor and texture.

This variety is less commonly cultivated but appreciated for its ornamental value and culinary potential.
