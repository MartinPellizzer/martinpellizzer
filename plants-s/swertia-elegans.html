Swertia elegans

Swertia elegans, commonly known as the elegant swertia, is a perennial herb native to the Himalayas and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and mountain slopes. The plant features slender, upright stems covered with small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are blue to violet in color, arranged in loose clusters at the top of the stems.

Swertia elegans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
