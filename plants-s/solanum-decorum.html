Solanum decorum

Solanum decorum, commonly known as the ornamental nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Solanum decorum is part of the nightshade family (Solanaceae), which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers.
