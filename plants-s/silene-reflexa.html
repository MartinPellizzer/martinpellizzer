Silene reflexa

Silene reflexa, commonly known as the reflexed catchfly, is a flowering plant native to northern regions of Europe, Asia, and North America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. Its leaves are alternate, linear, and often covered in fine hairs.

Silene reflexa is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in sandy or gravelly soils.
