Stelis multirostris

Stelis multirostris, commonly known as the multirostris orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically features multiple erect or spreading stems with lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive lip that has multiple lobes.

Stelis multirostris thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media for optimal growth.
