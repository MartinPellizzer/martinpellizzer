Sedum goldmanii

Sedum goldmanii, commonly known as the Gold Dust Sedum, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features small, rounded leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a subtle bluish or purplish hue. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it ideal for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
