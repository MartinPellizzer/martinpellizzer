Salsola aucheri

Salsola aucheri, commonly known as Armenian saltwort, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant thrives in arid and saline environments, making it well adapted to harsh, dry conditions. Its growth habit is typically prostrate or spreading, forming dense mats that help prevent soil erosion.

Salsola aucheri is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental value.
