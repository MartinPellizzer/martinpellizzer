Scrophularia lesbiaca

Scrophularia lesbiaca, commonly known as the Lesbia's figwort, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans and parts of Turkey.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and pairs of opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers sunny to partially shaded habitats.

Scrophularia lesbiaca is often found in rocky or alpine environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.
