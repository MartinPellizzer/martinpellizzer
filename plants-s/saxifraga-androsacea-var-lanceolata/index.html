Saxifraga Androsacea Var. Lanceolata

Saxifraga androsacea var. lanceolata is a striking perennial plant native to the cold climates of northern Europe and Siberia. It is known for its vibrant blue to purple flowers that bloom in early summer, adding a splash of color to rocky and alpine environments. This variety is particularly notable for its lance-shaped leaves, which are often covered in a fine, silvery down. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine displays. Due to its hardiness and ornamental value, it is increasingly appreciated by gardeners and botanists alike.
