Stellaria antillana

Stellaria antillana, commonly known as the Caribbean starwort, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean region.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, star-shaped white or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, sandy soils along coastal areas and riverbanks. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Stellaria antillana is often found in disturbed habitats and is adapted to thrive in tropical and subtropical climates.
