Stylophorum crassifolium

Stylophorum crassifolium, commonly known as broadleaf goldenstar, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features basal leaves that are broad and heart-shaped, with a smooth or slightly toothed edge. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers in early spring, which are surrounded by broad, reflexed bracts. It thrives in moist, woodland habitats and prefers partial to full shade.

Its foliage and flowers contribute to its ornamental value in native plant gardens.
