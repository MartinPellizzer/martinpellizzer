Scheelea zonensis

Scheelea zonensis, commonly known as the zonate palm, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and pinnate leaves. The plant typically grows in humid, lowland areas with well-drained soil. Its flowers are small and clustered, and it produces edible fruits that are often consumed by local communities.
