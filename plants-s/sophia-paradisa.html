Sophia paradisa

Sophia paradisa, commonly known as the celestial orchid, is a rare and exotic flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, large, and intricately patterned blossoms that come in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil to flourish. Its leaves are broad and waxy, adapted to retain moisture in its native habitat.

Due to its rarity and beauty, Sophia paradisa is highly sought after by botanists and horticulturists alike.
