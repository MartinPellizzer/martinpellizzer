Searsia africana

Searsia africana, commonly known as the silver tree, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Rosaceae and is characterized by its dense, silvery-white undersides on its leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by bright red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal and inland areas.

Searsia africana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
