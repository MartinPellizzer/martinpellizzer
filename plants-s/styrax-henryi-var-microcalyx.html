Styrax henryi var. microcalyx

Styrax henryi var. microcalyx, commonly known as the small-fruited Chinese frankincense, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant produces small, smooth, black fruits that are clustered in racemes. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its fragrance and aesthetic appeal.
