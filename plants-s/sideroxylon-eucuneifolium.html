Sideroxylon eucuneifolium

Sideroxylon eucuneifolium, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, hard, and heavy wood, which is highly valued for its durability and strength. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, woody fruits.

The tree is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
