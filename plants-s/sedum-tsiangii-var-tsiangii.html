Sedum tsiangii var. tsiangii

Sedum tsiangii var. tsiangii, commonly known as the Chinese stonecrop, is a low-growing succulent plant native to China.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, clustered leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for gardeners in arid climates.
