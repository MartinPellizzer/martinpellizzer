Sasa kurilensis var. nebulosa

Sasa kurilensis var. nebulosa, commonly known as the clouded sasa, is a shrub-like plant native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its dense, clumping growth habit. The plant features slender, arching culms covered with fine, soft leaves that have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest understories.

Its unique varietal name, *nebulosa*, refers to the cloudy or misty appearance of its foliage.
