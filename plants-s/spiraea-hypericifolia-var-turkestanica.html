Spiraea hypericifolia var. turkestanica

Spiraea hypericifolia var. turkestanica, commonly known as the Turkestan spiraea, is a deciduous shrub native to Central Asia.

It features slender, arching stems covered in small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath, providing a striking contrast. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact size and prolonged flowering period.
