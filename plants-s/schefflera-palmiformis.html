Schefflera palmiformis

Schefflera palmiformis, commonly known as the laceleaf schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, palm-like foliage composed of glossy, ovate leaves arranged in a compact, umbrella-shaped pattern. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers followed by black, berry-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance.

Schefflera palmiformis is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
