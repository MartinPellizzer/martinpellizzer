Sonchus neriifolius

Sonchus neriifolius, commonly known as blackleaf sowthistle, is a flowering plant in the dandelion family (Asteraceae).

It is native to Europe, North Africa, and parts of Asia, and it is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides. The plant has alternate, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a fuzzy texture. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family.

It is an annual herb that can grow up to 60 cm tall and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
