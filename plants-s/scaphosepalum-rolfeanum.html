Scaphosepalum rolfeanum

Scaphosepalum rolfeanum, commonly known as the Rolfe's hooded orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with a caudex and produces showy, hooded flowers in shades of yellow and orange. Its inflorescence is a raceme with resupinate flowers, and it has a distinctive, elongated sepal that forms a hood over the labellum.

Scaphosepalum rolfeanum is typically found in humid, shaded environments within the understory of tropical forests.
