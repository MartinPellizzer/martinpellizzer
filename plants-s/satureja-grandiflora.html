Satureja grandiflora

Satureja grandiflora, commonly known as sweet basil or wild basil, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, lance-shaped leaves with a strong, spicy scent. The plant produces small, white to pale purple flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy and adaptable species.

Satureja grandiflora is often used in culinary and medicinal applications due to its essential oils and antioxidant properties.
