Saxifraga pedemontana var. ayachica

Saxifraga pedemontana var. ayachica, commonly known as the Ayachica saxifrage, is a perennial flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Mexico.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its flowers, which range in color from white to pink, bloom in late spring to early summer.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in rock gardens due to its compact growth habit.
