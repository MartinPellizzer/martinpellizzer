Saussurea × karuizawensis

Saussurea × karuizawensis, commonly known as the Karuizawa saussurea, is a hybrid plant native to the alpine regions of Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and dense, hairy foliage. The plant produces yellow to orange composite flower heads, which are typical of the genus Saussurea. It thrives in cool, moist environments at high altitudes, often found in meadows and rocky slopes.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
