Stelis erectiflora

Stelis erectiflora, commonly known as the upright-flowered stelis, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically features strap-like leaves and produces white or pale yellow blooms. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Stelis erectiflora is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
