Solanum stenotomum f. huanuchi

Solanum stenotomum f. huanuchi, commonly known as the Huanuchi tomato, is a botanical variant of the nightshade family (Solanaceae).

It is characterized by its small, elongated fruits that typically range in color from yellow to orange. The plant exhibits a compact growth habit with hairy stems and leaves that are usually ovate in shape. It thrives in warm, temperate climates and is often cultivated for its unique flavor and ornamental value.

This variety is notable for its adaptability to various soil types and its relatively disease-resistant nature.
