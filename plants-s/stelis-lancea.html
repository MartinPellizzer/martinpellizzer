Stelis lancea

Stelis lancea, commonly known as the lance-shaped orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a raceme, which bloom during the rainy season. Stelis lancea typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and ecological studies.
