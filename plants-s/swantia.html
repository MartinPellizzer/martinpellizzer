Swantia

Swantia, commonly known as the golden trumpet or yellow trumpet, is a flowering plant belonging to the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its showy, tubular yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Swantia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
