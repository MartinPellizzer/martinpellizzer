Stellaria davidi

Stellaria davidi, commonly known as David's starwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family, characterized by its showy, star-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pink. The plant has slender, branched stems and small, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Stellaria davidi is adapted to rocky and alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils and cool climates.
