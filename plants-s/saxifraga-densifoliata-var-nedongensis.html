Saxifraga densifoliata var. nedongensis

Saxifraga densifoliata var. nedongensis, commonly known as the Nedong saxifrage, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers in late summer. The plant thrives in rocky, alpine environments and is adapted to cold, high-altitude conditions. It has a compact growth habit, with leaves arranged closely along the stem.

This variety is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological adaptations.
