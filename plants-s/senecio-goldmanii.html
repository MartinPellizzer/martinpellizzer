Senecio goldmanii

Senecio goldmanii, commonly known as the golden groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers and fuzzy, silver-green foliage. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a desirable addition to drought-tolerant landscapes.
