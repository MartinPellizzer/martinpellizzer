Syzygium cinerascens

Syzygium cinerascens, commonly known as the ash-wood apple, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Malaysia and Indonesia. Syzygium cinerascens is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic features.
