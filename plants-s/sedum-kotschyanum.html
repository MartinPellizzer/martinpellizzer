Sedum kotschyanum

Sedum kotschyanum, commonly known as the Kotschyan's stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the Caucasus region.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink to purple. The plant is adapted to rocky and arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a hardy choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long blooming period.
