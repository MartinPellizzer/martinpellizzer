Scutellaria pekinensis var. purpureicaulis

Scutellaria pekinensis var. purpureicaulis, commonly known as purple-stemmed skullcap, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family and is characterized by its square, hairy stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, two-lipped flowers that are typically blue to purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal potential, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
