Solanum oldfieldii var. oldfieldii

Solanum oldfieldii var. oldfieldii, commonly known as Oldfield's nightshade, is a species native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 feet. The plant produces small, white or pale green flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its fruit is a spherical berry that turns red when mature, containing several seeds.

It is often found in moist, wooded areas and is adapted to partial shade.
