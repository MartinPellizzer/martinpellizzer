Saintpaulia watkinsii

Saintpaulia watkinsii, commonly known as the Watkinsii African violet, is a species native to Tanzania.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green foliage and clusters of vibrant violet flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 10 to 15 centimeters, with upright stems and compact growth habit. Its flowers are usually borne on short pedicels, adding a decorative touch to its appearance.

Saintpaulia watkinsii prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
