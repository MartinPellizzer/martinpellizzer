Sagittaria sagittifolia var. rigida

Sagittaria sagittifolia var. rigida, commonly known as rigid arrowhead, is a perennial aquatic plant native to North America.

It features arrow-shaped leaves that are typically submerged or floating, with a distinctive rigid texture. The plant produces small white flowers that bloom in summer, followed by dark, spherical berries. It thrives in shallow water and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of ponds.

Its dense growth helps stabilize soil and provides habitat for aquatic life.
