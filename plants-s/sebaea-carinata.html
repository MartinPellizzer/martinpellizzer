Sebaea carinata

Sebaea carinata, commonly known as the zigzag vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, zigzagging stems and opposite, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Sebaea carinata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
