Sparrmannia abyssinica var. micrantha

Sparrmannia abyssinica var. micrantha, commonly known as the Abyssinian sparrmannia, is a perennial herb native to the high-altitude regions of Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces yellowish to brownish composite flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in fine hairs.

This variant is distinguished by its smaller flowers and shorter stature compared to the typical form of Sparrmannia abyssinica.
