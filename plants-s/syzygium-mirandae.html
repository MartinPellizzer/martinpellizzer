Syzygium mirandae

Syzygium mirandae, commonly known as the Miranda satinash, is a rare and ornamental plant native to the rainforests of Queensland, Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes eucalyptus and clove. The plant is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a small, round berry that turns from green to red as it matures.
