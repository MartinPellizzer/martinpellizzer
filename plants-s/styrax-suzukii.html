Styrax suzukii

Styrax suzukii, commonly known as the Japanese styrax, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Japan.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces small, dark purple berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans. Its leaves are glossy and oval-shaped, providing ornamental value in gardens.

Styrax suzukii is often cultivated for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
