Senna tenuifolia

Senna tenuifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaved senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a small shrub or tree that typically grows up to 3 meters tall, characterized by its pinnately compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry or semi-arid environments.

Senna tenuifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some cultures.
