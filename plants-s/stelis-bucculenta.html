Stelis bucculenta

Stelis bucculenta, commonly known as the orchid with a fleshy stem, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic. The plant features a short, thickened stem covered in a layer of spongy tissue that helps store water. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, with a variety of colors depending on the species.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
