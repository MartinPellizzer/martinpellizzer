Senecio bellidioides var. setosus

Senecio bellidioides var. setosus, commonly known as the woolly senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-gray foliage and yellow-orange composite flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it an attractive addition to dryland landscapes.
