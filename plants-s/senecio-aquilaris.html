Senecio aquilaris

Senecio aquilaris, commonly known as the African snowball, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, bushy growth habit with dense clusters of small, white, woolly flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has fleshy, gray-green leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
