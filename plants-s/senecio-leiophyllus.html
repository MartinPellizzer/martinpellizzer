Senecio leiophyllus

Senecio leiophyllus, commonly known as the softleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid regions. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are soft and fleshy, adapted to conserve water in dry environments.
