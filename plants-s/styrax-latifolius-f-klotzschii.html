Styrax latifolius f. klotzschii

Styrax latifolius f. klotzschii, commonly known as the broadleaf styrax, is a cultivar of the styrax plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its large, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces fragrant, white to pale pink flowers in early summer, which are clustered in panicles. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can thrive in USDA hardiness zones 6 to 8.

The cultivar is noted for its compact growth habit and ability to tolerate partial shade.
