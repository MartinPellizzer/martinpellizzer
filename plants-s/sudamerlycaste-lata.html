Sudamerlycaste lata

Sudamerlycaste lata, commonly known as the Brazilian orchid, is a species of orchid native to the rainforests of Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, large, and often fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, white, and purple. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees and drawing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain. Its roots are adapted to absorb water efficiently, allowing it to thrive in the humid, shaded environments of the rainforest canopy.
