Sersalisia chartacea

Sersalisia chartacea, commonly known as the paperleaf sersalisia, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, papery leaves that are often translucent and veined with a pale green color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically arranged in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its unique leaf structure, it is of interest to botanists studying plant adaptations to high-light conditions.
