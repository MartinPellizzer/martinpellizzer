Sauropus brunonis var. ovatus

Sauropus brunonis var. ovatus, commonly known as the broadleaf sauropus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland forests or along riverbanks. Its foliage is dense and can be used in ornamental gardening due to its aesthetic appeal.

The leaves are also sometimes used in traditional medicine in its native regions.
