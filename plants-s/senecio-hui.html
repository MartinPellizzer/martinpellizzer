Senecio hui

Senecio hui, commonly known as the Hui's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It produces small yellow flowers that are clustered in composite inflorescences.

Senecio hui is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact growth habit and attractive foliage.
