Stromanthe tonckat

Stromanthe tonckat, commonly known as the velvet leaf plant, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to the tropical rainforests of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage with deep green to purple hues. Its leaves are often variegated, featuring intricate patterns of silver and green.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.
