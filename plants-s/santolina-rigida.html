Santolina rigida

Santolina rigida, commonly known as rigid santolina, is a low-growing, evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, silver-gray foliage that is densely packed and finely textured. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flower clusters in late spring to early summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Santolina rigida is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract beneficial insects.
