Senecio amoenus

Senecio amoenus, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, daisy-like blooms with yellow centers and white to pink petals, often appearing in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Senecio amoenus is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
