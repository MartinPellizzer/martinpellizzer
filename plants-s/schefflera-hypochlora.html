Schefflera hypochlora

Schefflera hypochlora, commonly known as the silver schefflera, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with silver or white markings. The plant has a dense, bushy growth habit and produces small, inconspicuous yellowish-green flowers. Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental appeal.

Schefflera hypochlora prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil for optimal growth.
