Specklinia Aristata

Coral bells, scientifically known as Specklinia aristata, are a striking addition to any indoor or outdoor garden. This unique plant is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid and shaded environments. Its foliage features vibrant, velvety leaves that range from deep green to shades of red and purple, creating a visually appealing display. Specklinia aristata is prized for its low maintenance and ability to flourish in a variety of conditions, making it a favorite among plant enthusiasts. Whether grown in pots or in the ground, this plant adds a touch of elegance and color to any space.
