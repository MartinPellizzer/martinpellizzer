Stenorrhynchos latipetalum

Stenorrhynchos latipetalum, commonly known as the broad-petaled rock orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers with broad, spreading petals. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, and it blooms during the late summer to early autumn.

Stenorrhynchos latipetalum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with well-draining soil.
