Sideritis × laurentii

Sideritis × laurentii, commonly known as the hybrid sainfoin, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants such as mint and lavender. The plant features square, hairy stems and opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, clustered flowers are typically pink or purple, blooming in summer.

Sideritis × laurentii is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional herbal medicine.
