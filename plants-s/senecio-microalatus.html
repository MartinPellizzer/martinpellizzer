Senecio microalatus

Senecio microalatus, commonly known as the slender senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

Senecio microalatus produces small yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped cluster, making it a distinctive and attractive addition to rock gardens or dry landscapes.
