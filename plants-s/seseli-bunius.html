Seseli bunius

Seseli bunius, commonly known as the common seseli, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apiaceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in grassy areas and along roadsides. The plant features slender stems and small, greenish-white flowers arranged in umbels. Its leaves are finely divided and have a mild, aromatic scent.
