Stachys darcyana

Stachys darcyana, commonly known as Darcy's hedge-nettle, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the mint family (Lamiaceae) and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, pale purple flowers arranged in whorls along the stem. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

Stachys darcyana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
