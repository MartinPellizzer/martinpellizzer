Scutellaria dombeyi

Scutellaria dombeyi, commonly known as Dombey's skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square, hairy stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. Its small, tubular flowers are typically blue to purple in color, and they bloom during the spring and summer seasons.

Scutellaria dombeyi is often found in moist, shaded habitats within cloud forests.
