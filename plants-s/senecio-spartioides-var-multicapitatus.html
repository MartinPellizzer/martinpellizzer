Senecio spartioides var. multicapitatus

Senecio spartioides var. multicapitatus, commonly known as the Cape Hottentot, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

This variety is notable for its compact form and is often used in rock gardens or as a container plant.
