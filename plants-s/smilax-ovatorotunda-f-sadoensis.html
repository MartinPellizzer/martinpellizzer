Smilax ovatorotunda f. sadoensis

Smilax ovatorotunda f. sadoensis, commonly known as the Sado smilax, is a variant of the ovate-leaf smilax native to Japan.

It is a climbing shrub characterized by its woody stems and spiny leaves. The plant features small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in branched inflorescences. Its fruit is a red, berry-like structure that contains several seeds.

This plant is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky slopes.
