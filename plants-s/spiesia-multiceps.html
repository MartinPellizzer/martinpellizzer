Spiesia multiceps

Spiesia multiceps, commonly known as the white mistflower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, white, mist-like flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Spiesia multiceps is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
