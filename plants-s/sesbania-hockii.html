Sesbania hockii

Sesbania hockii, commonly known as the Hockii sesbania, is a leguminous plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil enrichment. It thrives in warm climates and is often used in agricultural systems for its forage value.

Sesbania hockii is also valued for its rapid growth and adaptability to various soil types.
