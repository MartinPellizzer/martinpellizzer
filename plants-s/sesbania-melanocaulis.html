Sesbania melanocaulis

Sesbania melanocaulis, commonly known as the black-stem sesbania, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, often dark-colored stems and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers followed by elongated pods containing edible seeds. It is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it valuable in soil enrichment.

Sesbania melanocaulis is also used in traditional medicine and as a forage crop for livestock.
