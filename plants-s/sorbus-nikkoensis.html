Sorbus nikkoensis

Sorbus nikkoensis, commonly known as the Nikko rowan, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae.

It is native to Japan and is characterized by its small, white flowers and red berries. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and thrives in cool, humid climates. Its leaves are alternate and oval-shaped, with serrated edges.

The fruit, which is edible, is a key feature in its ecological role and ornamental value.
