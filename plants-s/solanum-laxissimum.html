Solanum laxissimum

Solanum laxissimum, commonly known as the laxissimum nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are ovate to elliptical in shape. It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants such as tomatoes and potatoes.

Solanum laxissimum is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to thrive in arid conditions.
