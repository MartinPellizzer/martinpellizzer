Schefflera vanuatua

Schefflera vanuatua, commonly known as the Vanuatu umbrella tree, is a tropical plant native to the islands of Vanuatu.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that radiate from a central stem, giving it a distinctive umbrella-like appearance. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters in its natural habitat. Its leaves are glossy and dark green, often with a slightly waxy texture.

Schefflera vanuatua is well adapted to humid, tropical climates and thrives in well-drained soil with partial to full sunlight.
