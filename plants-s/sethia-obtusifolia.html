Sethia obtusifolia

Sethia obtusifolia, commonly known as the bluntleaf Sethia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its simple, ovate to elliptic leaves with rounded or blunt tips. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that are typically yellow or white in color. It is native to regions of South America, particularly in Argentina and Chile.

Sethia obtusifolia is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.
