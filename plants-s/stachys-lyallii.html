Stachys lyallii

Stachys lyallii, commonly known as Lyall's hedge nettle, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, pale blue or purple flowers arranged in spikes, which bloom in spring. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks.
