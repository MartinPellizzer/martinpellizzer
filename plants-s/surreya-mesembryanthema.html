Surreya mesembryanthema

Surreya mesembryanthema, commonly known as the ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, white or yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is often used in rock gardens and as a decorative plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.

Its ability to survive in harsh conditions makes it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
