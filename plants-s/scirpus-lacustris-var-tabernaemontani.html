Scirpus lacustris var. tabernaemontani

Scirpus lacustris var. tabernaemontani, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bulrush, is a perennial aquatic plant native to wetlands and marshy areas.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green to bluish-green in color. The plant produces cylindrical, spike-like inflorescences that contain small, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in shallow water and is often found in the edges of ponds and lakes.

This variety is valued for its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for aquatic life.
