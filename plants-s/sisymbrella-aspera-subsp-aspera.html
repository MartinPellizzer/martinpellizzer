Sisymbrella aspera subsp. aspera

Sisymbrella aspera subsp.

aspera, commonly known as the rough hawksbeard, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cress species.

The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and wastelands. Its leaves are pinnately divided and covered in fine hairs, while its yellow flowers are arranged in elongated racemes.
