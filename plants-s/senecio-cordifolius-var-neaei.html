Senecio cordifolius var. neaei

Senecio cordifolius var. neaei, commonly known as the Neaean heartleaf senecio, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically green with a bluish or purplish hue. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in summer, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
