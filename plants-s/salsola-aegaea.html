Salsola aegaea

Salsola aegaea, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its spiny leaves and blue or purple flowers. The plant thrives in coastal and saline environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its fleshy stems and leaves store water, aiding survival in arid climates.

Salsola aegaea plays a role in stabilizing coastal soils and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
