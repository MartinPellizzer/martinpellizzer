Solanum melongena var. racemosum

Solanum melongena var. racemosum, commonly known as the racemosum eggplant, is a botanical variant of the eggplant species.

It is characterized by its elongated, cylindrical fruit that often has a pale purple or white coloration. The plant belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshade plants such as tomatoes and peppers. It typically grows as an annual herb with hairy stems and large, lobed leaves.

The flowers are usually yellow with a distinctive racemose inflorescence, contributing to its botanical name.
