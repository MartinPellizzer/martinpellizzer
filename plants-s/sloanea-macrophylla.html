It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. Sloanea macrophylla is often found in humid, lowland forests and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
