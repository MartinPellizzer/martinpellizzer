Scorzonera dalmatica

Scorzonera dalmatica, commonly known as Dalmatian scorzonera, is a perennial herb native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and basal rosette of leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers with a distinctive central disk, typically blooming in summer. Its root system is tuberous and has been historically used in traditional medicine.

Scorzonera dalmatica thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in meadows and along roadsides.
