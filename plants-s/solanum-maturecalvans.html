Solanum maturecalvans

Solanum maturecalvans, commonly known as the maturecalvan nightshade, is a shrub native to certain regions of South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale purple in color. Its berries, which are toxic, are often mistaken for edible fruits due to their appearance.

The plant is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves with a distinct, often aromatic scent.
