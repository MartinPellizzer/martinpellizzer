Scrophularia amplexicaulis

Scrophularia amplexicaulis, commonly known as the clasping weed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Scrophulariaceae.

It is characterized by its basal leaves that clasp the stem, giving it its common name. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.

Scrophularia amplexicaulis is an annual herb that thrives in moist, temperate climates.
