Solanum edule var. multifidum

Solanum edule var. multifidum, commonly known as the multifidum tomato, is a variant of the common tomato plant.

It is characterized by its distinctive, deeply lobed leaves that give it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically red when ripe, though they can also be yellow or orange. It is a herbaceous annual that thrives in warm climates and well-drained soil.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value as well as its edible fruits.
