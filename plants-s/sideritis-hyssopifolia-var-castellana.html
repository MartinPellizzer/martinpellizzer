Sideritis hyssopifolia var. castellana

Sideritis hyssopifolia var. castellana, commonly known as the Castellana ironweed, is a perennial herb native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a slightly rough texture. The plant produces clusters of small, pale blue to violet flowers during the summer months. It thrives in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and open woodlands.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in Mediterranean flora.
