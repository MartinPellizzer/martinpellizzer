Sphaeropteris tripinnata

Sphaeropteris tripinnata, commonly known as the three-pinnate cycad, is a species of cycad native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its stout, unbranched trunk and large, pinnately compound leaves that can reach several meters in length. The leaves are typically dark green and have a leathery texture, with a prominent midrib and multiple pairs of leaflets. This plant thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Its reproductive structures include cones, with male and female cones occurring on separate plants.
