Stenospermation dictyoneurum

Stenospermation dictyoneurum, commonly known as the narrow-seed orchid, is a rare and specialized orchid species native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its unique floral structure, featuring a narrow, elongated ovary and a distinctive, intricate pattern of veins on its petals. The plant exhibits a complex relationship with mycorrhizal fungi, which is essential for its nutrient uptake and survival. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, with a coloration that ranges from pale green to yellowish-white.
