Solanum burchellii var. schaeferi

Solanum burchellii var. schaeferi, commonly known as the Schaefer's nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring simple, ovate leaves and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. Its fruit is a globose berry that changes from green to red as it matures, often containing several seeds.

This species is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showing resilience to drought conditions.
