Shirakiopsis

Shirakiopsis, commonly known as the Japanese star anise, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Schisandraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, star-shaped fruit. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into the distinctive, capsule-like fruits used in traditional medicine and as a spice.
