Stelis cinerea

Stelis cinerea, commonly known as the silver stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its pale green to silver-colored stems and leaves, which are covered in a fine, velvety coating. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Stelis cinerea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
