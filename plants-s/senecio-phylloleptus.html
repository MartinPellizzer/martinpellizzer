Senecio phylloleptus

Senecio phylloleptus, commonly known as the silverleaf senecio, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically covered in a fine layer of white or grayish hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves to survive dry conditions.

This species is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements.
