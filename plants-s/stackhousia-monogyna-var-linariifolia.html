Stackhousia monogyna var. linariifolia

Stackhousia monogyna var. linariifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved stackhousia, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, evergreen leaves that are typically linear to narrowly elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple, arranged in clusters along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to Mediterranean climates.
