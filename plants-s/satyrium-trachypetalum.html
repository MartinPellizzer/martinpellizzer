Satyrium trachypetalum

Satyrium trachypetalum, commonly known as the narrow-petalled satyrium, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant features small, intricate flowers with a distinctive structure, including a narrow, elongated lip. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a subject of interest for both horticultural and scientific study.
