Soehrensia bruchii var. atropurpureiflora

Soehrensia bruchii var. atropurpureiflora, commonly known as the purple-flowered soehrensia, is a species native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its woody stems and dense foliage. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically purple or reddish in color, which are striking against its green leaves. Its leaves are usually ovate or elliptical, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

This plant is often found in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
