Sabicea trinitensis

Sabicea trinitensis, commonly known as the Trinidad velvet tamarind, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to the Caribbean, particularly found in the island of Trinidad. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a pod containing several seeds, which are often dispersed by animals.

Sabicea trinitensis is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
