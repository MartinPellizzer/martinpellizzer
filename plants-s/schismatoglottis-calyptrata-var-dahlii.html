Schismatoglottis calyptrata var. dahlii

Schismatoglottis calyptrata var. dahlii, commonly known as the Dahlii Schismatoglottis, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces striking white or pink flowers that bloom in summer. It prefers warm, humid conditions and well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant.
