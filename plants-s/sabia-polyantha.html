Sabia polyantha

Sabia polyantha, commonly known as the pink-flowered lily, is a striking perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its elegant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pale pink to deep magenta. The plant features narrow, strap-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern at the base. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full to partial sunlight for optimal growth.

Sabia polyantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
