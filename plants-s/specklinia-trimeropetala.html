Specklinia trimeropetala

Specklinia trimeropetala, commonly known as the three-petaled orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit and its striking, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The plant features long, slender pseudobulbs and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. Its flowers are notable for their three petals, which are usually a combination of white and pink hues.

Specklinia trimeropetala is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
