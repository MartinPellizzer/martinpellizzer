Spiraea japonica var. ovatifolia

Spiraea japonica var. ovatifolia, commonly known as Japanese spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features narrow, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy on the underside. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pink flowers in early summer, which attract pollinators. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

This ornamental plant is valued for its compact growth and seasonal interest.
