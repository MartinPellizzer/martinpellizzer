Spiraea dasyantha

Spiraea dasyantha, commonly known as the clustered spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It features multiple stems that bear small, white to pinkish flowers in dense, flat-topped clusters. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and has finely divided, feathery leaves. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Spiraea dasyantha is valued for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
