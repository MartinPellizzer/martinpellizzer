Stemona lucida

Stemona lucida, commonly known as the bright stemona, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often glossy in appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. Its roots are valued in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Stemona lucida is typically grown in shaded, moist environments and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
