Sideritis catalaunica var. cinerascens

Sideritis catalaunica var. cinerascens, commonly known as the ash-gray ironweed, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, gray-green leaves that have a distinctive ash-colored hue. Its flowering period typically occurs in late spring to early summer, producing clusters of tiny, pale blue to violet flowers.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
