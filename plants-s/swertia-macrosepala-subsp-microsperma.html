Swertia macrosepala subsp. microsperma

Swertia macrosepala subsp.

microsperma, commonly known as the small-seeded swertia, is a member of the Gentianaceae family. It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its slender stems and lanceolate leaves.

The flowers are typically blue or purple, with five petals and prominent yellow glands. It is native to the Himalayas and thrives in moist, shaded environments.

The subspecies is distinguished by its smaller seeds compared to other varieties within the species.
