Stachys officinalis subsp. haussknechtii

Stachys officinalis subsp.

haussknechtii, commonly known as the Hausknecht's hedge nettle, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

The plant features upright stems with opposite, ovate leaves that have serrated edges. Its small, pinkish-white flowers are clustered in whorls along the stems, and it produces small, dry fruits known as nutlets.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental qualities and historical use in traditional herbal medicine.
