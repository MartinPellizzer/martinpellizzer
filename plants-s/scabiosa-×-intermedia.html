Scabiosa × intermedia

Scabiosa × intermedia, commonly known as the common globe thistle, is a hybrid perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its spherical, blue to purple flower heads that resemble small globes, surrounded by spiky, silver-green bracts. The plant typically grows to a height of 12 to 30 inches and produces small, white to yellow flowers in the center. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its foliage is gray-green and slightly fuzzy, adding texture to garden landscapes.
