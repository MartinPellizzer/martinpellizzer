Senecio ommannei

Senecio ommannei, commonly known as the Orange River senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its yellow flowers are arranged in dense, daisy-like clusters, and it produces small, oval-shaped seeds.
