Stevia enarthotricha

Stevia enarthotricha, commonly known as the silver leaf stevia, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant is notable for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions and its drought tolerance. It is often used in horticulture for ornamental purposes due to its attractive appearance.

Stevia enarthotricha is also valued for its potential medicinal properties, though it is not as widely studied as other stevia species.
