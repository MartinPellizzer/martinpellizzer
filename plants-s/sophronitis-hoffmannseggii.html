Sophronitis hoffmannseggii

Sophronitis hoffmannseggii, commonly known as the Hoffmannsegg's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded environments at elevations ranging from 1,500 to 3,000 meters. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

The species is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in association with other orchid species in montane forests.
