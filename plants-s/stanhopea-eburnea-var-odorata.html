Stanhopea eburnea var. odorata

Stanhopea eburnea var. odorata, commonly known as the fragrant stanhopea, is a species of orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, pendant-like flower spikes that bear numerous fragrant, white to pale yellow blooms. The plant produces showy, lip-like petals that are often adorned with intricate patterns and a subtle fragrance. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and unique scent.
