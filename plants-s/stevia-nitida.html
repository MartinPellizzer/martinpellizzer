Stevia nitida

Stevia nitida, commonly known as silver leaf stevia, is a perennial shrub native to Paraguay and Brazil.

It belongs to the sunflower family, Asteraceae, and is characterized by its silvery-green, velvety leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in heads, typical of the Asteraceae family. Stevia nitida is cultivated for its aromatic leaves, which are used in traditional medicine and as a natural sweetener.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
