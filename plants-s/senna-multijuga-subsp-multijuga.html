Senna multijuga subsp. multijuga

Senna multijuga subsp.

multijuga, commonly known as the yellow senna, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its pinnately compound leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year.

The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Botanically, it is notable for its medicinal properties and use in traditional medicine.
