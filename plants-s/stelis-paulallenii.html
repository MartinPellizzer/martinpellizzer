Stelis paulallenii

Stelis paulallenii, commonly known as the Allen's stelis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are usually dark green and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in moisture retention.

Stelis paulallenii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
