Saussurea hosoiana

Saussurea hosoiana, commonly known as the Himalayan arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive arrowhead-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellowish to brownish composite flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in rocky and alpine environments, often found at elevations between 3,000 and 5,000 meters.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
