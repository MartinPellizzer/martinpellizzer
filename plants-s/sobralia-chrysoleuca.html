Sobralia chrysoleuca

Sobralia chrysoleuca, commonly known as the golden sobralia, is a striking orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features showy, yellow-orange flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are broad and leathery, providing a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms.

Sobralia chrysoleuca thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.
