Sabicea ferruginea var. lasiocalyx

Sabicea ferruginea var. lasiocalyx, commonly known as the rusty senna, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant has a woody stem and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in open woodlands and savannas, where it thrives in full sun.

The leaves are typically pubescent, with a distinctive rusty-colored underside, contributing to its common name.
