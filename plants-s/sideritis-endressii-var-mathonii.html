Sideritis endressii var. mathonii

Sideritis endressii var. mathonii, commonly known as the Mathon's ironweed, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants known for their medicinal properties. The plant features slender stems and small, narrow leaves, typically growing in rocky or dry soils. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, arranged in whorls along the stem, and bloom during the summer months.

This variety is valued for its potential medicinal uses and is often found in regions with a temperate climate.
