Sutera rigida

Sutera rigida, commonly known as rigid sutera, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy blue to violet flowers. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.

Sutera rigida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
