Sisymbrium elegantulum

Sisymbrium elegantulum, commonly known as the elegant mustard, is an annual herbaceous plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant produces slender, elongated pods that contain several seeds, which are released upon maturity. Native to regions of Europe and western Asia, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats.

Its foliage is typically lobed and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
