Sideroxylon sphaerocarpum

Sideroxylon sphaerocarpum, commonly known as the globe wood or African ironwood, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, heavy wood that is highly valued for its strength and durability. The plant has simple, opposite, and often leathery leaves that are dark green in color. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically yellow or green, and it produces woody, spherical fruits that contain several seeds.

The tree is adapted to dry conditions and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.
