Sedum rubicundum

Sedum rubicundum, commonly known as the red sedum, is a succulent plant native to the United States.

It features fleshy, upright stems that are often tinged with red, especially under stress or in full sun. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red, blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant color make it an attractive addition to both wild and cultivated landscapes.
