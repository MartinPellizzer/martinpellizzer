Spartium odoratum

Spartium odoratum, commonly known as sweet clover, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant has a strong, sweet fragrance, which is particularly noticeable when the flowers are in bloom. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Spartium odoratum is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used historically in herbal remedies.
