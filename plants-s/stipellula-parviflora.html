Stipellula parviflora

Stipellula parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered stipellula, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small shrub characterized by its compact growth habit and numerous small, white or pale pink flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and arranged in opposite pairs along the stems. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
