Strychnos unguacha var. polyantha

Strychnos unguacha var. polyantha, commonly known as the golden star tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that resemble stars, which bloom in clusters along the branches. The plant produces small, round fruits that turn red when mature, containing seeds surrounded by a fleshy aril. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a waxy texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
