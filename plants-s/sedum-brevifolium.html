Sedum brevifolium

Sedum brevifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and fleshy, narrow leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Sedum brevifolium is also valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
