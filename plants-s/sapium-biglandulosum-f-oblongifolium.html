Sapium biglandulosum f. oblongifolium

Sapium biglandulosum f. oblongifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf Chinese lantern, is a variant of the Chinese lantern plant characterized by its elongated, oval-shaped leaves.

The leaves are typically a deep green with a slightly waxy texture and are arranged alternately along the stems. This plant produces vibrant red, lantern-shaped fruits that are a striking feature, often used in ornamental gardening. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

The botanical features make it a popular choice for both garden displays and ecological studies.
