Salsola mucronata

Salsola mucronata, commonly known as Russian thistle or goatweed, is a flowering plant in the family Amaranthaceae.

It is an annual shrub that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, such as deserts and disturbed soils. Salsola mucronata is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in harsh, dry conditions.
