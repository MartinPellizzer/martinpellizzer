Solenostemon scutellarioides

Solenostemon scutellarioides, commonly known as Indian violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and clusters of small, violet to purple flowers. It is often grown as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and modest blooms.

Solenostemon scutellarioides prefers moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.
