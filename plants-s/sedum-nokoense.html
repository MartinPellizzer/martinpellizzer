Sedum nokoense

Sedum nokoense, commonly known as the Nokoense stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant typically produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the summer months. It grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its hardiness and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
