Sansevieria carnea variegata

Sansevieria carnea variegata, commonly known as the Variegated Snake Plant, is a striking succulent native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are a mix of green, white, and yellow, creating a visually appealing variegation pattern. The plant is highly drought-tolerant, making it ideal for low-maintenance indoor gardening. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Its air-purifying qualities make it a popular choice for both decorative and functional purposes.
