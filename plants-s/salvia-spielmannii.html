Salvia spielmannii

Salvia spielmannii, commonly known as summer sage or Spanish sage, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces vibrant violet or blue-purple flowers in summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly fuzzy texture and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its nectar-rich flowers attract bees and butterflies, contributing to pollinator habitats.
