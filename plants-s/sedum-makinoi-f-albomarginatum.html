Sedum makinoi f. albomarginatum

Sedum makinoi f. albomarginatum, commonly known as the white-margin stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Japan.

It features fleshy, upright stems with opposite, oblong leaves that are typically green with white margins. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink to purple during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a desirable addition to both garden and container settings.
