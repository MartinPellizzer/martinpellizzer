Senecio limbardioides var. angustifolius

Senecio limbardioides var. angustifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense, spherical clusters called cymes. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and arid landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is often cultivated in xeriscaped environments.
