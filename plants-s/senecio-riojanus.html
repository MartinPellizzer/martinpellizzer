Senecio riojanus

Senecio riojanus, commonly known as the Rioja Senecio, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of northern Spain.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Senecio riojanus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
