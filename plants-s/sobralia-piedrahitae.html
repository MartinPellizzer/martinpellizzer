Sobralia piedrahitae

Sobralia piedrahitae, commonly known as the Piedrahitae orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to the mountainous regions of Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its foliage consists of broad, leathery leaves that help it conserve water in its arid environment.

Sobralia piedrahitae is prized by horticulturists for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
