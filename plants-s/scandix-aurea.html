Scandix aurea

Scandix aurea, commonly known as golden scurvygrass, is a flowering plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It is a low-growing herb with bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant has smooth, waxy leaves that help it retain moisture in harsh climates. It thrives in moist, rocky soils and is often found near coastal areas.

Scandix aurea plays an important role in the ecosystem by providing food for various insects and small animals.
