Symbegonia parvifolia

Symbegonia parvifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved begonia, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy leaves and clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in humid, shady environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value. Its leaves are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, and it produces fibrous roots that help in water retention.

Symbegonia parvifolia is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
