Scabiosa lucida var. alpestris

Scabiosa lucida var. alpestris, commonly known as alpine scabious, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its delicate, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in well-drained, rocky or sandy soils. It is often found in alpine meadows and is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators in its native habitat.
