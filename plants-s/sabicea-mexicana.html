Sabicea mexicana

Sabicea mexicana, commonly known as Mexican sabicea, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Leguminosae family, also known as the legume or bean family. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink. Its fruits are legumes, which contain seeds that are dispersed by wind or animals.

Sabicea mexicana is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is valued for its ornamental qualities.
