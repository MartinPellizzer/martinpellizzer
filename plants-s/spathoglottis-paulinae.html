Spathoglottis paulinae

Spathoglottis paulinae, commonly known as the Paulina's spider orchid, is a tropical orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its unique, spider-like floral structure with long, slender petals and a prominent, club-shaped lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually a combination of white and purple, with a subtle fragrance.

Spathoglottis paulinae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
