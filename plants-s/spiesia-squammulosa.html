Spiesia squammulosa

Spiesia squammulosa, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, daisy-like yellow flowers with a distinctive dark center. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and covered in a fine, scaly texture, giving it a unique appearance.

Spiesia squammulosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
