Sorghum × sudanense

Sorghum × sudanense, commonly known as Sudan grass, is a hybrid plant that combines traits from sorghum and sudangrass.

It is a warm-season grass characterized by its tall stature and rapid growth rate, making it suitable for forage and biomass production. The plant has a robust root system that helps prevent soil erosion and improves soil structure. Its leaves are broad and serrated, while its stems are hollow and fibrous.

Sorghum × sudanense is adapted to a variety of climates and is often used in agricultural systems for its high yield and drought tolerance.
