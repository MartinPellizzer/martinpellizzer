Sedum longistylum

Sedum longistylum, commonly known as the long-stemmed stonecrop, is a perennial succulent native to North America.

It features fleshy, cylindrical leaves that are typically green or reddish in color. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped, yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for landscaping in arid regions.
