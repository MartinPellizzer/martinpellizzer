Spiraea pubescens f. hypoleuca

Spiraea pubescens f. hypoleuca, commonly known as the white spiraea, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It features small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring, typically in clusters along the stems. The plant has finely textured, gray-green foliage that provides ornamental value throughout the growing season. It prefers partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

Its compact size and attractive flowers make it a popular choice for borders and small gardens.
