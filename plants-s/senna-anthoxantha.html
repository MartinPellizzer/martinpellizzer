Senna anthoxantha

Senna anthoxantha, commonly known as the yellow senna, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy areas.

Senna anthoxantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
