Silphium ovatum

Silphium ovatum, commonly known as the broadleaf silphium, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. Silphium ovatum is known for its medicinal properties, particularly for its use in treating digestive issues.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
