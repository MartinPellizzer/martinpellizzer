Stenocalyx impunctatus

Stenocalyx impunctatus, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, smooth-stemmed structure and large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, round seeds. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in regions with suitable climates.
