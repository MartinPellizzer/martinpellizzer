Salacia sinensis

Salacia sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese salacia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Salicaceae, which includes willows and poplars. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. It is often found in moist, forested areas and thrives in temperate climates.

The bark and leaves of Salacia sinensis have been used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
