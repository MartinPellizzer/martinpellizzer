Solanum martii

Solanum martii, commonly known as the Martii nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and peppers. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, featuring branched stems and hairy leaves. Its small, yellowish flowers give way to berries that start green and mature to a dark purple or black color.

Solanum martii is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its striking fruit.
