Saraca kunstleri

Saraca kunstleri, commonly known as the yellow flame tree, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer seasons. The plant belongs to the Fabaceae family, also known as the legume or pea family. Its leaves are bipinnate, meaning they are divided into small, feather-like segments.

Saraca kunstleri is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.
