Spiranthes hostmannii

Spiranthes hostmannii, commonly known as the hostmann's spirea, is a rare and delicate orchid native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and its distinctive, spiral-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer.
