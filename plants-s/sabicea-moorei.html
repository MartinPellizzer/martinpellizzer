Sabicea moorei

Sabicea moorei, commonly known as the Moore's sabicea, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. Due to habitat loss, Sabicea moorei is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
