Stanfieldiella oligantha

Stanfieldiella oligantha, commonly known as the slender-stemmed lily, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the Alstroemeriaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, erect stems and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
