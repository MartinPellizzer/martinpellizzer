Scutellaria stenosiphon

Scutellaria stenosiphon, commonly known as narrow-lip skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular blue or violet flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or wetland habitats.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been historically used in herbal remedies.
