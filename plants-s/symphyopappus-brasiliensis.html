Symphyopappus brasiliensis

Symphyopappus brasiliensis, commonly known as the Brazilian sunflower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

The plant produces yellow flowers with a central disk surrounded by ray florets, typical of the family. It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.
