Spondias soyauxii

Spondias soyauxii, commonly known as the African sour plum, is a small to medium-sized tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and simple, alternate leaves. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by oval, yellow or orange fruits with a sour taste.
