Stellaria longifolia var. legitima

Stellaria longifolia var. legitima, commonly known as the slender starwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are typically narrow and opposite, with a slightly hairy texture. This variety is native to parts of Europe and Asia, often found in moist, grassy habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
