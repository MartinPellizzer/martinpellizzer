Strobilanthes feddei

Strobilanthes feddei, commonly known as the "copperleaf" or "fireplant," is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking copper-colored leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in horticulture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires partial to full sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance and seasonal blooming, it is a popular choice for gardeners seeking vibrant, exotic foliage.
