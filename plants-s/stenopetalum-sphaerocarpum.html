Stenopetalum sphaerocarpum

Stenopetalum sphaerocarpum, commonly known as the globular lily, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, globular inflorescence, which consists of numerous small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its unique appearance.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
