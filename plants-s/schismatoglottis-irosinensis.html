Schismatoglottis irosinensis

Schismatoglottis irosinensis, commonly known as the Irosin swamp orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the family Orchidaceae.

It is native to the tropical rainforests of Papua New Guinea, where it thrives in shaded, humid environments. The plant is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It is often found growing in swampy or wetland habitats, which contribute to its unique ecological niche.

Due to its habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
