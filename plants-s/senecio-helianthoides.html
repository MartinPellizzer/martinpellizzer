Senecio helianthoides

Senecio helianthoides, commonly known as the yellow owl's eye, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features bright yellow ray flowers surrounding a central disk, giving it a striking appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its unique morphology and vibrant color make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
