Serjania sphenocarpa

Serjania sphenocarpa, commonly known as the Brazilian cherry or red serjania, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Brazil.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its distinctive red, fleshy fruits that resemble cherries. Serjania sphenocarpa is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local medicine.
