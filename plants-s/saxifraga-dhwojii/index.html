Saxifraga Dhwojii

Saxifraga dhwojii is a rare and striking alpine plant native to the Himalayas. It is known for its vibrant pink and white flowers that bloom in the summer months. This species thrives in rocky, high-altitude environments where few plants can survive. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Protecting Saxifraga dhwojii is crucial for preserving biodiversity in its fragile ecosystem.
