Stellaria infracta

Stellaria infracta, commonly known as the narrowleaf starwort, is a small, herbaceous plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, white or pale pink flowers that are arranged in clusters. The leaves are typically narrow and linear, with a slightly hairy surface. This species is often found in moist, calcareous soils in meadows and along stream banks.

It blooms from late spring to early summer, adding delicate floral interest to its habitat.
