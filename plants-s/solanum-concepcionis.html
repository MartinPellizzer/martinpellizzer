Solanum concepcionis

Solanum concepcionis, commonly known as the Concepcion nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by oval, yellowish berries. Its leaves are typically ovate with a slightly hairy texture, and it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates.

Solanum concepcionis is often found in forest understories and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
