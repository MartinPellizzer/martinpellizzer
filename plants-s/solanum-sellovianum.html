Solanum sellovianum

Solanum sellovianum, commonly known as the Sellovian nightshade, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly found in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are ovate to elliptic, with a rough texture, and it produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters.

The fruit is a globose berry that turns from green to red when mature, often containing several seeds.
