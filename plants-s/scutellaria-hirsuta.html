Scutellaria hirsuta

Scutellaria hirsuta, commonly known as hairy skullcap, is a flowering plant native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, and its small, tubular blue or purple flowers. It typically grows in moist woodland habitats and is often found in shaded areas.

Scutellaria hirsuta is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
