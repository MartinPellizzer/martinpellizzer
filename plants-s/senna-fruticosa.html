Senna fruticosa

Senna fruticosa, commonly known as the African senna, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are often trifoliate or pinnate, and its yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savannas and woodland areas.

Senna fruticosa is also valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
