Stipa orientalis

Stipa orientalis, commonly known as the oriental feather grass, is a perennial grass species native to the temperate regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and fluffy, feathery seed heads that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and rocky slopes. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Stipa orientalis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and garden design.
