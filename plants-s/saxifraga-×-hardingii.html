Saxifraga × hardingii

Saxifraga × hardingii, commonly known as the hardy saxifrage, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Saxifragaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Europe and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves and clusters of small, pink or white flowers. The plant is often found in rocky or alpine habitats, where it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. It is a perennial species that is relatively low maintenance and suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its hardiness and ornamental value make it a popular choice among gardeners in temperate climates.
