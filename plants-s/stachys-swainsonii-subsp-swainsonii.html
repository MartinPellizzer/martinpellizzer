Stachys swainsonii subsp. swainsonii

Stachys swainsonii subsp.

swainsonii, commonly known as Swainson's hedge nettle, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the eastern United States. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves.

The plant typically grows in moist, wooded areas and has opposite, ovate leaves with hairy surfaces. Its small, pale purple flowers are clustered in whorled inflorescences, and it produces small, dry fruits called nutlets.

This species is often found in shaded or partially shaded environments and is adapted to thrive in humid climates.
