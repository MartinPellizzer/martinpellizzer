Selaginella Plumosa

Selaginella plumosa, also known as the feather moss, is a unique and ancient plant that has thrived for millions of years. It belongs to the family Selaginellaceae and is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rock crevices. This plant is notable for its delicate, feathery appearance and its ability to survive in harsh conditions. Its resilience and beauty make it a popular choice for both horticulture and scientific study. Despite its small size, Selaginella plumosa plays a significant role in ecosystem health and biodiversity.
