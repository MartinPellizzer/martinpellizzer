Silene szechuensis

Silene szechuensis, commonly known as the Sichuan catchfly, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive pink to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous regions, preferring well-drained soils. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in summer.

Silene szechuensis is often found in alpine environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
