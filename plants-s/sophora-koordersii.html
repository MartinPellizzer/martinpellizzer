Sophora koordersii

Sophora koordersii, commonly known as the koord's sophora, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is notable for its ability to fix nitrogen through symbiotic relationships with root nodules.

It typically grows in rocky or mountainous areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
