Solanum ultraspinosum

Solanum ultraspinosum, commonly known as the ultra-spinous nightshade, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and leaves, which serve as a defense mechanism against herbivores. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or violet in color. Its fruit is a yellow, berry-like structure that contains several seeds.

Solanum ultraspinosum is part of the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants such as tomatoes and potatoes.
