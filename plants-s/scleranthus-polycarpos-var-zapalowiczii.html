Scleranthus polycarpos var. zapalowiczii

Scleranthus polycarpos var. zapalowiczii, commonly known as the Zapalowicz's thornweed, is a variant of the common thornweed, characterized by its distinctive morphological features.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae, known for its small, pinkish or white flowers.
