Sinoseris

Sinoseris, commonly known as the Chinese sunflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its bright yellow composite flowers and linear, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas and is adapted to temperate climates. Sinoseris produces small, oval-shaped seeds that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its propagation.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant blooms and resilience.
