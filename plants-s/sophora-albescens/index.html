Sophora Albescens

Sophora albescens is a rare and striking plant native to specific regions of Asia. It is known for its unique white flowers that bloom in spring, creating a stunning visual display. This species belongs to the legume family and is often found in forested areas with moist, well-drained soil. Its delicate appearance belies its resilience, thriving in challenging environmental conditions. Due to habitat loss, Sophora albescens is becoming increasingly rare, making conservation efforts crucial for its survival.
