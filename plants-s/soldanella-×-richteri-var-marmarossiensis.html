Soldanella × richteri var. marmarossiensis

Soldanella × richteri var. marmarossiensis, commonly known as the Marmarossian hybrid bellflower, is a hybrid plant species within the Primulaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that typically range in color from white to pale pink. The plant features hairy stems and leaves that are often lance-shaped with serrated edges. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
