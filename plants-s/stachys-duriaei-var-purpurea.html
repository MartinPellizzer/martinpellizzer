Stachys duriaei var. purpurea

Stachys duriaei var. purpurea, commonly known as purple betony, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of violet-blue tubular flowers that bloom in late summer. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a fuzzy texture. This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to thrive in well-drained soils.

It is also noted for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
