Swertia chumbica

Swertia chumbica, commonly known as Himalayan gentian, is a perennial herb native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, arid environments and thrives at elevations between 2,000 and 4,000 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem, while its flowers are clustered in racemes.

Swertia chumbica is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
