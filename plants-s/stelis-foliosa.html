Stelis foliosa

Stelis foliosa, commonly known as the leafy stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a short, branched stem and produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant has fleshy, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, loose soil.

Stelis foliosa is appreciated for its unique foliage and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
