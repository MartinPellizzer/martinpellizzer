Streptanthus tortuosus var. flavescens

Streptanthus tortuosus var. flavescens, commonly known as the yellow-stemmed fairy lantern, is a flowering plant native to California.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, showy flowers and capsule fruits. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its yellowish stems and pale yellow to white flowers make it distinctive.
