Sedum × pascalianum

Sedum × pascalianum, commonly known as the hybrid stonecrop, is a low-growing succulent plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features fleshy, upright stems and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is characterized by its ability to thrive in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Its leaves are typically thick and waxy, adapted to store water and reduce transpiration.

Sedum × pascalianum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
