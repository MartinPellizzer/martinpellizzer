Specklinia laciniata

Specklinia laciniata, commonly known as the lace fern, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that give it a lace-like appearance. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually light green with a slightly waxy texture.

Specklinia laciniata is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of care.
