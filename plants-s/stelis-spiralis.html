Stelis spiralis

Stelis spiralis, commonly known as the spiral stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, utilizing its aerial roots to absorb moisture and nutrients from the environment. Its leaves are narrow and arranged in a spiral pattern, giving the plant its distinctive appearance.

Stelis spiralis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
