Seriphidium herba-alba

Seriphidium herba-alba, commonly known as white herbaceous thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its gray-green, woolly leaves and yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. It has a taproot system that allows it to survive in harsh conditions.

Seriphidium herba-alba is often used in traditional medicine for its potential anti-inflammatory properties.
