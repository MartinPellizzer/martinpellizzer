Saxifraga sampaioi

Saxifraga sampaioi, commonly known as the Sampaio's saxifrage, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Portugal.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and attractive, often pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are usually basal, with a rosette structure that helps conserve moisture.

Saxifraga sampaioi is a relatively rare species, making it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
