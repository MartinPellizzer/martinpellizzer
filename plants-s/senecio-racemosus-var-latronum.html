Senecio racemosus var. latronum

Senecio racemosus var. latronum, commonly known as the African marguerite, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in mountainous regions and is well adapted to arid environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
