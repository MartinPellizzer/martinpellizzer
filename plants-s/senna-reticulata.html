Senna reticulata

Senna reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters tall, characterized by its pinnately compound leaves with a distinctive net-like venation pattern. The plant produces yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes, and it is often found in open woodlands and savannahs.

Senna reticulata is known for its medicinal properties, with various parts of the plant used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
