Stylosanthes nervosa

Stylosanthes nervosa, commonly known as the narrow-veined stylosanthes, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it valuable in agricultural systems. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used as a forage crop or in soil improvement practices.

Its rapid growth and drought tolerance make it a useful species in ecological restoration projects.
