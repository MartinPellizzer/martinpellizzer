Sobralia stevensonii

Sobralia stevensonii, commonly known as Stevenson's sobralia, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences that consist of numerous small, brightly colored flowers clustered together. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Sobralia stevensonii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
