Securinega melanthesoides

Securinega melanthesoides, commonly known as the African spurge, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous shrub that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
