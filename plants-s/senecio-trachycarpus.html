Senecio trachycarpus

Senecio trachycarpus, commonly known as the rough-leaved groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils. The plant features dark green, rough-textured leaves with a hairy surface. Its flowers are small, yellow, and arranged in clusters, blooming in late spring to early summer.

It is often used in rock gardens due to its hardy nature and ornamental appearance.
