Sykesia phyllostachya

Sykesia phyllostachya, commonly known as the African fern tree, is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its feathery, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant produces long, cylindrical pods that contain several seeds. It is often found in woodland and savanna habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.

Sykesia phyllostachya is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some African cultures.
