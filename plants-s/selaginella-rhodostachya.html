Selaginella rhodostachya

Selaginella rhodostachya, commonly known as the red-stemmed spikemoss, is a species of spikemoss belonging to the family Selaginellaceae.

It is a small, evergreen plant that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. The plant features slender, branching stems covered in small, scale-like leaves. Its most distinctive characteristic is the bright red coloration of its stems, which contrasts with its dark green foliage.

Selaginella rhodostachya is known for its ability to thrive in humid conditions and is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value.
