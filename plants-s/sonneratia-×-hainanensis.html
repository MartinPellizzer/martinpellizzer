Sonneratia × hainanensis

Sonneratia × hainanensis, commonly known as the Hainan mangrove.

It is a hybrid species native to the coastal regions of Hainan Island in China. This plant is a shrub or small tree that thrives in saline and brackish water environments. It has smooth, grayish bark and produces small, yellow flowers.

The plant plays an important ecological role in stabilizing coastal soils and providing habitat for various marine and terrestrial species.
