Scleranthus × hasskarlii

Scleranthus × hasskarlii, commonly known as the hybrid meadow thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its erect, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces pink to purple flowers in clusters, typically blooming from June to August. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, pastures, and along roadsides.
