Sideritis cretica

Sideritis cretica, commonly known as Greek woundwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features upright, branched stems and small, hairy leaves that are typically gray-green in color. Its flowers are pink to purple in hue and appear in whorls along the stems.

Sideritis cretica is often found in rocky or limestone soils and is valued for its medicinal properties.
