Senecio abadianus var. araneosus

Senecio abadianus var. araneosus, commonly known as the spider plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, silver-green leaves that are covered in fine, hair-like structures, giving it a textured appearance. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers in clusters during the growing season. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
