Swertia peloria

Swertia peloria, commonly known as the Himalayan gentian, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, thriving in cold and moist conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine, silvery-white down.

Swertia peloria is valued not only for its ornamental appeal but also for its traditional medicinal uses in local communities.
