Sarcochilus breviscapus

Sarcochilus breviscapus, commonly known as the short-stemmed helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It features a compact, low-growing habit with a dense rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces striking, helmet-shaped flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with a darker throat. It thrives in shady, humid environments and is often found in rainforest or woodland areas.

Sarcochilus breviscapus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable conditions.
