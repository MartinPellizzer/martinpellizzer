Shorea astrosticta

Shorea astrosticta, commonly known as the Malayan ironwood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Dipterocarpaceae family and is characterized by its dense, heavy wood that is highly valued for its strength and durability. The tree can grow up to 50 meters tall, with a straight trunk and a broad, umbrella-like crown. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptic in shape, often with a glossy upper surface.

The flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically appearing in clusters, and the tree produces heavy, woody fruits that contain one or two seeds.
