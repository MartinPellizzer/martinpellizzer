Strophocactus brasiliensis

Strophocactus brasiliensis, commonly known as the Brazilian apple cactus, is a species native to Brazil.

It is a columnar cactus characterized by its smooth, ribbed stems and sparse, woolly spines. The plant produces large, showy yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in arid climates.
