Senecio tenellus

Senecio tenellus, commonly known as the narrowleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 10-30 centimeters. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Senecio tenellus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
