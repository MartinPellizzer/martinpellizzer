Saxifraga cebennensis

Saxifraga cebennensis, commonly known as the Cebenna saxifrage, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe.

It typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or white, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are basal, rosette-shaped, and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Saxifraga cebennensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens or alpine settings.
