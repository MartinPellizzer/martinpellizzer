Sickingia pikia

Sickingia pikia, commonly known as the Ghost Plant, is a unique succulent native to South Africa.

It features striking, translucent, waxy leaves that resemble ghostly shapes, giving it its common name. The plant has a rosette growth pattern and thrives in well-draining soil with minimal water. It produces delicate, pale blue or purple flowers during the cooler months.

Sickingia pikia is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its low maintenance requirements.
