Sphenogyne crocea

Sphenogyne crocea, commonly known as the slender sphenogyne, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Sphenogyneae.

It is characterized by its delicate, grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or pale yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate regions.

Sphenogyne crocea is noted for its low-growing habit and is often used in horticultural settings for its ornamental value.
