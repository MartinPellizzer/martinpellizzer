Smilax rubra

Smilax rubra, commonly known as the red smilax, is a perennial vine native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its woody stems and compound leaves with spiny tips. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into red, fleshy berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

Smilax rubra is notable for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
