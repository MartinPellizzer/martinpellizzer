Sansevieria dawei

Sansevieria dawei, commonly known as the "Dawei Snake Plant," is a hardy succulent native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with prominent, light green variegation. The plant is characterized by its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it an ideal indoor plant. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water efficiently.

Sansevieria dawei is also noted for its air-purifying properties, contributing to improved indoor air quality.
