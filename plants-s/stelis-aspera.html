Stelis aspera

Stelis aspera, commonly known as the rough-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, often hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers with a subtle fragrance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Stelis aspera is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
