Stanhopea × lewisae

Stanhopea × lewisae, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species within the Stanhopea genus, which belongs to the orchid family Orchidaceae.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that often display a complex mix of colors, including shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of trees or other plants without being parasitic. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Stanhopea × lewisae is highly valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
