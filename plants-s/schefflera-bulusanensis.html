Schefflera bulusanensis

Schefflera bulusanensis, commonly known as the Bulusan schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of leaves, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by bright red berries, attracting various birds and animals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
