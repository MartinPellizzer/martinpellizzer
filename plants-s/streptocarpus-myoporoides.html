Streptocarpus myoporoides

Streptocarpus myoporoides, commonly known as the mountain streptocarpus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically produces violet to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

Streptocarpus myoporoides prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and shaded borders.
