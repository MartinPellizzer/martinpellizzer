Sutera polyantha

Sutera polyantha, commonly known as the Chinese rose or the common rose of China, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate with a smooth margin.

The plant thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.
