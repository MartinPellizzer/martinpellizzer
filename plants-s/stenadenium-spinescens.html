Stenadenium spinescens

Stenadenium spinescens, commonly known as the spiny pincushion, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features dense clusters of spiny, fleshy stems that form a compact, cushion-like structure. The plant has small, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
