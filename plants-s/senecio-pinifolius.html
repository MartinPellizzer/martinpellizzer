Senecio pinifolius

Senecio pinifolius, commonly known as the pine-leaf senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its evergreen, needle-like leaves that resemble pine needles. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
