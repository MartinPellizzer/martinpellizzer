Schoenobiblus daphnoides

Schoenobiblus daphnoides, commonly known as the silverleaf daphnoid, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage that gives it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer seasons. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.

Schoenobiblus daphnoides is also valued for its resilience to drought and partial shade.
