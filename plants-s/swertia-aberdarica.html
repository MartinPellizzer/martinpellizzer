Swertia aberdarica

Swertia aberdarica, commonly known as the Himalayan gentian, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, rocky habitats and thrives in cool, temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged oppositely along the stem.

Swertia aberdarica is valued for its ornamental beauty and has traditional medicinal uses in some regions.
