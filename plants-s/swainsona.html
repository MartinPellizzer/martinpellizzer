Swainsona

Swainsona, commonly known as the Crown of Thorns, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

These plants are native to Australia and are characterized by their spiny stems and showy, often yellow or orange, flowers. Many species of Swainsona are drought-tolerant and thrive in arid or semi-arid environments. They have small, palmate leaves and produce pod-like fruits that contain seeds.

The genus includes both ornamental and some species with medicinal properties.
