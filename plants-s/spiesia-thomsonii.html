Spiesia thomsonii

Spiesia thomsonii, commonly known as Thomson's spurge, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the spring and summer seasons. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

Spiesia thomsonii is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
