Stillingia macrantha

Stillingia macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered stillingia, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its striking, showy red or orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its unique floral structure and ecological role make it a notable species in botanical studies.
