Senna atomaria

Senna atomaria, commonly known as the yellow senna, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its feathery, bipinnate leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces small, flat pods that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and rocky habitats.

Senna atomaria is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
