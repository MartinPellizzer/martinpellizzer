Stellaria alsinoides

Stellaria alsinoides, commonly known as mouse-ear chickweed, is a low-growing annual herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, white flowers with five petals arranged in a star-like pattern, which is characteristic of the Caryophyllaceae family. The plant has hairy, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, soft hairs. It typically thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and gardens.

Stellaria alsinoides is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through seeds and runners.
