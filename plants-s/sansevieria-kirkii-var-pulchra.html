Sansevieria kirkii var. pulchra

Sansevieria kirkii var. pulchra, commonly known as the "Ruffled Sansevieria," is a striking succulent native to South Africa.

It features tall, upright leaves that are dark green with prominent, wavy, ruffled edges and a pale yellowish-green midrib. The plant is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its fleshy, sword-like leaves. It produces small, inconspicuous white flowers on slender stalks during the growing season.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
