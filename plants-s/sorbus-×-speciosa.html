Sorbus × speciosa

Sorbus × speciosa, commonly known as the Chinese whitebeam, is a hybrid species of the genus Sorbus.

It is characterized by its showy white to pale pink flowers that appear in clusters, typically in late spring. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible for birds but not for humans. It is native to China and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

Sorbus × speciosa is a deciduous shrub or small tree that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.
