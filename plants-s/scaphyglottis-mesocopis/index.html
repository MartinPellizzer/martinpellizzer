Scaphyglottis Mesocopis

Scaphyglottis mesocopis is a rare and fascinating orchid native to the cloud forests of Central America. This plant is known for its striking, hooded flowers that resemble small cups. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific conditions to flourish. Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and is the subject of conservation efforts. Its unique morphology and ecological importance make it a valuable species for botanical study.
