Senecio fueginus

Senecio fueginus, commonly known as the Andes groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the high Andes of South America.

Senecio fueginus is adapted to cold, high-altitude conditions and plays a role in the local ecosystem by providing habitat and nectar for pollinators.
