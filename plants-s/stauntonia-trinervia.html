Stauntonia trinervia

Stauntonia trinervia, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or the broadleaf arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by edible tubers similar to arrowroot. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its botanical features make it both aesthetically pleasing and useful in culinary applications.
