Saxifraga balongshanensis

Saxifraga balongshanensis, commonly known as the Balongshan saxifrage, is a rare flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves and clusters of small, white or pink flowers. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to high-altitude environments. It is often found in alpine meadows and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance.
