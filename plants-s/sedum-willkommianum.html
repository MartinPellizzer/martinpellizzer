Sedum willkommianum

Sedum willkommianum, commonly known as the Willkomm's stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, allowing it to store water efficiently. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
