Satureja lanata

Satureja lanata, commonly known as woolly savory, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-gray foliage and compact growth habit. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Satureja lanata is valued for its aromatic properties and is often used in herbal teas and culinary applications.
