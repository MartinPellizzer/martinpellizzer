Sapium dalzielii

Sapium dalzielii, commonly known as the Dalziel's Chinese gallnut tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its alternate, simple, and often pubescent leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by distinctive, spiny fruits containing a single seed.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
