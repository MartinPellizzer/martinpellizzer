Stachys annua

Stachys annua, commonly known as annual hedge-nettle, is an annual herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features erect stems that are often hairy and can grow up to 60 cm in height. The plant produces small, pink to purple flowers arranged in whorls along the stem, which bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate to lanceolate, and covered in fine hairs.

Stachys annua is often found in disturbed soils, along roadsides, and in waste areas.
