Sinningia tubiflora

Sinningia tubiflora, commonly known as the tubular-flowered gloxinia, is a species native to Brazil.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its tuberous roots and striking, tubular-shaped flowers. The flowers are typically pink or purple and bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its unique floral structure and vibrant colors make it a standout in the Gesneriaceae family.
