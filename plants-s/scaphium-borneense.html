Scaphium borneense

Scaphium borneense, commonly known as the Bornean arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces striking inflorescences with a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Scaphium borneense is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local medicine.
