Senecio aureus var. balsamitae

Senecio aureus var. balsamitae, commonly known as the Balsamita Senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has silver-gray foliage with a fuzzy texture, which helps it retain moisture in its arid habitat. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

This variety is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
