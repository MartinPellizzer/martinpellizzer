Schizachyrium thollonii

Schizachyrium thollonii, commonly known as Thollon's sideblades, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in wetland habitats such as marshes and swamps, where it thrives in moist to saturated soil conditions. The plant features slender, upright stems that bear narrow, grass-like leaves with a bluish-green hue. Its inflorescence consists of spikelets that are often reddish-brown, adding visual interest during the flowering season.

Schizachyrium thollonii plays a role in stabilizing wetland soils and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
