Stemodiopsis buchananii var. pubescens

Stemodiopsis buchananii var. pubescens, commonly known as the pubescent stemodiopsis, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a slender, erect stem that is densely covered with soft, white pubescence. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pale blue or violet in color. Its leaves are narrow and linear, with a slightly hairy surface.

This species is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun conditions.
