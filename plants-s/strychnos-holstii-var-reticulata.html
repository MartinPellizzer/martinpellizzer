Strychnos holstii var. reticulata

Strychnos holstii var. reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined strychnos, is a deciduous shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Loganiaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves with a distinctive reticulate venation pattern. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by red, fleshy berries. Due to its ornamental value and ecological role, it is sometimes cultivated in subtropical gardens.
