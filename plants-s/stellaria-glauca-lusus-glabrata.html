Stellaria glauca lusus glabrata

Stellaria glauca lusus glabrata, commonly known as the glabrous starwort, is a low-growing herbaceous plant characterized by its delicate, star-shaped flowers.

It typically features slender, branching stems covered in fine, soft hairs. The leaves are usually opposite, ovate to lanceolate in shape, and often have a bluish-green hue. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors or stream banks.

The plant is known for its small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a subtle beauty to its habitat.
