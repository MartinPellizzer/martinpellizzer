Sarcochilus maculatus

Sarcochilus maculatus, commonly known as the spotted hoodsia, is a terrestrial orchid native to New Zealand.

It features a distinctive appearance with dark green, strap-like leaves and a showy, hooded flower that is typically pink or purple. The plant produces a single, curved spike bearing several small, fragrant flowers. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Sarcochilus maculatus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is a popular choice for shady garden settings.
