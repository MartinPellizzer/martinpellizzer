Selliguea hastata subsp. montana

Selliguea hastata subsp.

montana, commonly known as the mountain moss, is a species of liverwort found in temperate regions. It is characterized by its dense, cushion-like growth form and dark green, glossy leaves.

The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices. Its leaves are usually ovate to elliptic in shape, with a smooth or slightly serrated margin. Selliguea hastata subsp.

montana plays a role in soil stabilization and provides microhabitats for various invertebrates.
