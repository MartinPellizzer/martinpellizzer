Stenocalyx brasiliensis var. silvestris

Stenocalyx brasiliensis var. silvestris, commonly known as the Brazilian flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to South America.

It features large, showy flowers that are typically red or orange in color, arranged in clusters at the ends of branches. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal or lowland areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes in tropical and subtropical regions.
