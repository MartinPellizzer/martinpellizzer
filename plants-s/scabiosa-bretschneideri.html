Scabiosa bretschneideri

Scabiosa bretschneideri, commonly known as the Chinese spring gentian, is a perennial flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in early spring. Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
