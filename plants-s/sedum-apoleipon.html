Sedum apoleipon

Sedum apoleipon, commonly known as the stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features fleshy, upright stems and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is adapted to grow in rocky or dry environments, storing water in its thick leaves. It blooms in late spring to early summer, often in full sun.

Sedum apoleipon is a hardy perennial that is popular in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance requirements.
