Stylidium planirosula

Stylidium planirosula, commonly known as the flat-leaf sun-orchid, is a small, flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its unique, sun-like floral structure. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange and bloom during the warmer months.

Stylidium planirosula is known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and insects with its nectar-rich blooms.
