Sansevieria thunbergii

Sansevieria thunbergii, commonly known as the snake plant, is a succulent perennial plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are usually dark green with light green variegation. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in low-light conditions, making it an ideal indoor plant. It produces small, white flowers on slender stalks during the summer months.

Sansevieria thunbergii is also noted for its ability to purify the air by removing toxins.
