Solanum rivicola

Solanum rivicola, commonly known as the waterleaf nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy areas, thriving in moist environments. It features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, violet-colored flowers.

Its berries are toxic and should not be consumed by humans or animals.
