Sedum uniflorum var. japonicum

Sedum uniflorum var. japonicum, commonly known as Japanese stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Japan.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink or white. The plant has thick, water-storing leaves that help it survive in dry conditions. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for landscaping.
