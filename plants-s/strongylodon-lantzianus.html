Strongylodon lantzianus

Strongylodon lantzianus, commonly known as the giant rock fig, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, woody structure and is often found growing on rocky outcrops. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Its fruit, which is edible, is a type of fig and contains a high concentration of sugars.

Due to its unique growth habits and ecological role, it is an important species in its native habitat.
