Stelis obscurata

Stelis obscurata, commonly known as the dark-stem orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant has a compact, pseudobulbous structure with narrow, dark green leaves. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and usually white or pale yellow, arranged in a spike.

Stelis obscurata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or terrariums.
