Spilanthes grisea

Spilanthes grisea, commonly known as the yellow toothbrush plant, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in dry, open habitats and is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils.

Spilanthes grisea is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
