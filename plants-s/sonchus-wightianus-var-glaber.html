Sonchus wightianus var. glaber

Sonchus wightianus var. glaber, commonly known as hairyleaf sowthistle, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, hairy leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Native to parts of Asia, it is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its growth habit and appearance make it distinguishable from other similar species within the genus.
