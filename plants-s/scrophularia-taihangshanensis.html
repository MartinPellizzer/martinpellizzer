Scrophularia taihangshanensis

Scrophularia taihangshanensis, commonly known as the Taihang Mountain scrophularia, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Scrophulariaceae family and is characterized by its erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
