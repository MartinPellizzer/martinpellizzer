Schizachyrium beckii

Schizachyrium beckii, commonly known as prairie dropseed, is a native grass species of the United States.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-green foliage and delicate, feathery seed heads that appear in late summer. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a valuable component of native prairie ecosystems. Schizachyrium beckii is drought-tolerant and requires minimal maintenance once established.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping and restoration projects.
