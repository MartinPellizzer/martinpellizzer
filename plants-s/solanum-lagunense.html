Solanum lagunense

Solanum lagunense, commonly known as the Lake Island nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale green, which bloom at night. Its fruits are berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

Solanum lagunense is part of the nightshade family, which includes many economically important plants.
