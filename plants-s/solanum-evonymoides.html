Solanum evonymoides

Solanum evonymoides, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its silver-white, velvety foliage. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically purple or white, followed by berries that turn from green to yellow or orange as they mature. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in coastal areas.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
