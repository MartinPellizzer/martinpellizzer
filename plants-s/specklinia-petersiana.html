Specklinia petersiana

Specklinia petersiana, commonly known as the Peters' speckled orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, speckled leaves that range in color from dark green to deep purple. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, often with a yellowish or greenish hue. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in its natural habitat.

Specklinia petersiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance.
