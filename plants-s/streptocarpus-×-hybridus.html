Streptocarpus × hybridus

Streptocarpus × hybridus, commonly known as the African violet hybrid, is a popular ornamental plant characterized by its velvety foliage and showy flowers.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa. The plant typically produces clusters of tubular or spurred flowers in shades of purple, pink, white, and red. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Streptocarpus × hybridus is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and is relatively easy to care for in a home environment.
