Stapelia penduliflora

Stapelia penduliflora, commonly known as the hanging stapelia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces striking, pendulous flowers that are typically yellowish-green with a distinctive, waxy texture. These flowers bloom in summer and attract pollinators such as flies and beetles.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for succulent collections.
