Senecio rigidifolius

Senecio rigidifolius, commonly known as the rigid-leaved groundsels, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa, and is often found in rocky and arid environments. Its flowers are typically yellow and appear in small clusters, contributing to its ornamental value.

Senecio rigidifolius is adapted to survive in harsh conditions, making it a resilient and drought-tolerant species.
