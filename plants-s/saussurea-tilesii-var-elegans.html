Saussurea tilesii var. elegans

Saussurea tilesii var. elegans, commonly known as the elegant saussurea, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, cushion-like growth habit. The plant features grayish-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, yellowish composite flowers in the summer months. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, arid environments.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species.
