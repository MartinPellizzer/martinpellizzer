Scutellaria boissieri

Scutellaria boissieri, commonly known as the Boissier's skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny to partially shaded environments. Its small, tubular flowers are usually blue to purple in color, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Scutellaria boissieri is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
