Scutellaria araxensis

Scutellaria araxensis, commonly known as the Arax Valley skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the highlands of Armenia.

It belongs to the mint family (Lamiaceae) and is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to violet. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are square and somewhat woody.

Scutellaria araxensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
