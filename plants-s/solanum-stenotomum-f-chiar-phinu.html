Solanum stenotomum f. chiar-phinu

Solanum stenotomum f. chiar-phinu, commonly known as the vivid leaf nightshade, is a unique botanical variant of the nightshade family.

It is characterized by its strikingly colored leaves, which often display vivid shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, thriving in warm, humid climates. Its leaves are often used in ornamental gardening due to their aesthetic appeal.

Despite its beauty, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
