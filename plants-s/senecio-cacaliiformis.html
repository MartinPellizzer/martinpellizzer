Senecio cacaliiformis

Senecio cacaliiformis, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other composite flowers. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow ray florets surrounding a central disk of smaller, tubular flowers. It typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in full sun and well-drained soils.

Senecio cacaliiformis is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive, daisy-like appearance.
