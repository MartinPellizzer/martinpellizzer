Senecillis robusta

Senecillis robusta, commonly known as the robust wild lettuce, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and the Middle East.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features serrated, lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Senecillis robusta is often found in rocky or arid environments, where it thrives in well-drained soils.

Its medicinal properties have been studied for their potential anti-inflammatory and sedative effects.
