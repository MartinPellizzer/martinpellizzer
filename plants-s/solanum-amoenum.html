Solanum amoenum

Solanum amoenum, commonly known as the velvetleaf nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, velvety leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that are typically yellow or orange when mature. Solanum amoenum belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes.

It is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and agricultural fields.
