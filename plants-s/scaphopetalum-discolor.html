Scaphopetalum discolor

Scaphopetalum discolor, commonly known as the yellow bellflower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Scaphopetalum discolor is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
