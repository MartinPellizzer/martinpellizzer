Sempervivum mettenianum var. pallidum

Sempervivum mettenianum var. pallidum, commonly known as the Silver Hens and Chicks, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features rosettes of fleshy, silver-gray leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white trichomes, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant is well adapted to rocky and alpine environments, thriving in full sun and well-draining soil. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the summer months.

This variety is prized for its hardiness and ornamental value in rock gardens and container plantings.
