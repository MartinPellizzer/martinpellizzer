Stelis acutissima

Stelis acutissima, commonly known as the sharp-leaved orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in tropical rainforests. The plant has narrow, pointed leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in humid environments with indirect sunlight and well-draining substrates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique appearance and fragrance.
