Solanum ugentii

Solanum ugentii, commonly known as the Ugenti nightshade, is a species of flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and tobacco. The plant is characterized by its small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple. Its leaves are typically ovate with a rough texture, and it produces small, yellow berries that are toxic to humans.
