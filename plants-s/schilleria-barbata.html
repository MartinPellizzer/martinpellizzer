Schilleria barbata

Schilleria barbata, commonly known as the featherleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and white. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Schilleria barbata is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
