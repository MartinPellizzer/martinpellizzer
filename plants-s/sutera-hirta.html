Sutera hirta

Sutera hirta, commonly known as woolly nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is native to parts of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands. The plant is characterized by its hairy, glandular stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. Its small, star-shaped flowers are typically white or pale blue, and it produces berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

Sutera hirta is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
