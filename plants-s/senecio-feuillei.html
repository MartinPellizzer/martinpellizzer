Senecio feuillei

Senecio feuillei, commonly known as the Cape snowflake, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its fluffy, white or pale blue flower heads that resemble snowflakes. The plant typically grows as a low, spreading shrub with slender, hairy stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Senecio feuillei is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique, delicate appearance.
