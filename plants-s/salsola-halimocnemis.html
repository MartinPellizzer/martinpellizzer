Salsola halimocnemis

Salsola halimocnemis, commonly known as sandmat or desert sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Asia and the Middle East.

It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant produces small, green to yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are usually succulent, adapted to store water in harsh environments.

Salsola halimocnemis is often found in sandy soils and can thrive in areas with minimal rainfall.
