Stelis recurvilabia

Stelis recurvilabia, commonly known as the curling orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, spiraling growth habit and compact, often twisted stems. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Stelis recurvilabia thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for collectors and enthusiasts of epiphytic orchids.
