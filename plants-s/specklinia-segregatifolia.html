Specklinia segregatifolia

Specklinia segregatifolia, commonly known as the segmented leaf orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, segmented leaves that give it its common name. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are small, white or pale green, and are often clustered in loose inflorescences.

Specklinia segregatifolia is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
