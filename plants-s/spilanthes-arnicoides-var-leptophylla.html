Spilanthes arnicoides var. leptophylla

Spilanthes arnicoides var. leptophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf false hawksbeard, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and bright yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally for its anti-inflammatory and analgesic effects.
