Senecio parodii

Senecio parodii, commonly known as the Blue Echeveria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, blue-gray leaves that store water, adapting to arid environments. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers in the spring. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, requiring minimal water.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
