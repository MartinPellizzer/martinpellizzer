Symplocos glaberrima

Symplocos glaberrima, commonly known as the smooth symplocos, is a deciduous shrub native to tropical regions of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous areas. The plant features small, oval-shaped leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. Its flowers are white or pale yellow, and it produces small, fleshy berries that are edible.

Symplocos glaberrima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
