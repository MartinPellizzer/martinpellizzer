Stelis cylindrata

Stelis cylindrata, commonly known as the cylindrical stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. The plant features linear, fleshy leaves that store water, aiding in its survival in tropical conditions. Its flowers are small, white or pale pink, and often clustered in inflorescences.

Stelis cylindrata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or terrariums.
