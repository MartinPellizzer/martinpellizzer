Sportella atalantioides

Sportella atalantioides, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, pendant clusters of yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for warm climates.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its fragrant blossoms and ability to provide shade in landscaped areas.
