Stanhopea tigrina var. grandiflora-superba

Stanhopea tigrina var. grandiflora-superba, commonly known as the Tiger Orchid, is a striking tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that often display a combination of red, orange, and yellow hues. The plant produces large, pendulous blooms with intricate patterns that resemble a tiger's stripes, hence its common name. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
