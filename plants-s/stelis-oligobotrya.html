Stelis oligobotrya

Stelis oligobotrya, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate white or pale yellow flowers with a distinct three-lobed lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
