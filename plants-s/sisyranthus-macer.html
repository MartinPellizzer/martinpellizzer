Sisyranthus macer

Sisyranthus macer, commonly known as the purple orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking purple flowers and compact growth habit. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are clustered in inflorescences and are known for their intricate, fragrant structure.

Sisyranthus macer is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and unique floral characteristics.
