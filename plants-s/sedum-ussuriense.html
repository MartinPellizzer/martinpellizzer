Sedum ussuriense

Sedum ussuriense, commonly known as the Ussuri stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and small, clustered leaves that are typically green or reddish in color. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Sedum ussuriense is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in arid environments.
