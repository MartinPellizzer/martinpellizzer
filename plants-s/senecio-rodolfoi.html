Senecio rodolfoi

Senecio rodolfoi, commonly known as the Rodolfo's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It has narrow, linear leaves and is often found in mountainous regions.

Senecio rodolfoi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
