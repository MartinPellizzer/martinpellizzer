Sinofranchetia chinensis

Sinofranchetia chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese sinofranchetia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Sinofranchetia chinensis is also noted for its ecological importance in supporting local biodiversity.
