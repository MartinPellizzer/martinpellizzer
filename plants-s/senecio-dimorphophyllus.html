Senecio dimorphophyllus

Senecio dimorphophyllus, commonly known as the African snowball, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, spherical clusters of white, woolly flowers that bloom in winter. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, adapted to store water. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its unique appearance and cold hardiness, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
