Selaginella mosenii

Selaginella mosenii, commonly known as the Mosen's spikemoss, is a species of vascular plant belonging to the family Selaginellaceae.

It is a small, evergreen herbaceous plant that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features a creeping stem with whorled leaves that are usually linear to lanceolate in shape. Selaginella mosenii is notable for its ability to survive in low-light conditions and its capacity for vegetative propagation through spores.
