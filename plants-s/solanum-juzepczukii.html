Solanum juzepczukii

Solanum juzepczukii, commonly known as the Juzepczuk's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Argentina.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and peppers. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with hairy stems and alternate leaves. Its flowers are small, bell-shaped, and usually white or greenish in color.

The fruit is a berry that turns from green to yellow or red as it matures.
