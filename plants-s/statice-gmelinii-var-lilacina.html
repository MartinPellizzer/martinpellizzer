Statice gmelinii var. lilacina

Statice gmelinii var. lilacina, commonly known as the lilac statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and produces dense clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink to lilac. The plant typically blooms from late summer to early autumn, adding color to gardens during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal and rock garden settings.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting flowers, which are often used in floral arrangements.
