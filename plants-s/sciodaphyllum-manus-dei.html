Sciodaphyllum manus-dei

Sciodaphyllum manus-dei, commonly known as the sacred hand tree, is a rare and enigmatic plant found in the dense rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, hand-like foliage that gives it its mystical name. The plant has thick, waxy leaves that help it retain moisture in its humid, shaded habitat. Its flowers are small and clustered, often blooming in shades of white and pale yellow.

Despite its striking appearance, little is known about its reproductive habits and ecological role.
