Symplocos ferruginea var. philippinensis

Symplocos ferruginea var. philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine rusty-leafed wax tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its rough, reddish-brown bark and elliptical, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, white, bell-shaped flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by bright red, fleshy berries.
