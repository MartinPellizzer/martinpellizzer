Sideritis pungens

Sideritis pungens, commonly known as the prickly sida, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its slender stems and small, pale purple flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine hairs, giving it a slightly rough texture.

Sideritis pungens is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to thrive in arid environments.
