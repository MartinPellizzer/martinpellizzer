Schefflera waterhousei

Schefflera waterhousei, commonly known as the Waterhouse schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the island of Madagascar.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like clusters of leaflets, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by bright red berries, attracting various birds and small mammals. Its leaves are typically glossy and dark green, with a leathery texture.

Schefflera waterhousei is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and adaptability to indoor conditions.
