Steviopsis thyrsiflora

Steviopsis thyrsiflora, commonly known as the silverleaf stevia, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and compact, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its aromatic leaves, it is sometimes used in herbal teas and as a natural sweetener.
