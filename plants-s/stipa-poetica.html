Stipa poetica

Stipa poetica, commonly known as the poetic grass or poet's grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia, where it thrives in rocky and well-drained soils. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of narrow, silver-green leaves. Its small, spike-like inflorescence produces fluffy, wind-dispersed seeds, aiding in its propagation.

Stipa poetica is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
