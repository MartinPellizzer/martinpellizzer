Silene veselskyi subsp. widderi

Silene veselskyi subsp.

widderi, commonly known as the Widder's campion, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It is characterized by its pink or purple flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and prefers well-drained environments. It has a compact growth habit with basal leaves and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.
