Straussia mariniana

Straussia mariniana, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flower heads surrounded by spiny, leaf-like bracts. The plant thrives in saline and well-drained soils, often found in dune environments. Its succulent leaves and stems help it retain moisture in arid conditions.

Straussia mariniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing coastal habitats.
