Stenorrhynchos cinnabarinum var. luteoalbus

Stenorrhynchos cinnabarinum var. luteoalbus, commonly known as the yellow-flowered spider orchid, is a unique orchid species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers, which contrast with its greenish-brown stems and leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid environments. Its flowers are small and clustered, with a distinctive spider-like appearance due to the shape of the petals and sepals.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
