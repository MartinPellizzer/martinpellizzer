Sigmatostalix dilatata

Sigmatostalix dilatata, commonly known as the broadleaf tree, is a species of flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in humid tropical or subtropical climates. Its botanical features make it an important component of local ecosystems, supporting various forms of wildlife.
