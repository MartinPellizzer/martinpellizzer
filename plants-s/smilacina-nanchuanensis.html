Smilacina nanchuanensis

Smilacina nanchuanensis, commonly known as the Nanchuan smilacina, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Smilacaceae and is characterized by its climbing habit and distinctive foliage. The plant features slender, twining stems and heart-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple. Its flowers are small, greenish-yellow, and clustered in umbels, typically appearing in late spring.

Smilacina nanchuanensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
