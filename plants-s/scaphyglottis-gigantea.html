Scaphyglottis gigantea

Scaphyglottis gigantea, commonly known as the giant scaphyglottis, is a striking member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 centimeters in diameter, featuring a distinctive yellow and purple coloration. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy texture that helps retain moisture.

Scaphyglottis gigantea is a perennial herb that grows from a rhizomatous stem, making it a popular choice for botanical gardens and orchid enthusiasts.
