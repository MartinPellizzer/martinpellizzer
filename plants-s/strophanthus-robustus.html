Strophanthus robustus

Strophanthus robustus, commonly known as the African arrowroot or red milkweed, is a climbing shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its woody stems and large, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Its leaves are typically alternate and have a rough texture with serrated edges.

Strophanthus robustus is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some African cultures.
