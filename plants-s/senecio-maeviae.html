Senecio maeviae

Senecio maeviae, commonly known as the Maevia's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like inflorescence. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It features narrow, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized species.
