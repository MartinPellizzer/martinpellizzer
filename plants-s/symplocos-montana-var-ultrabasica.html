Symplocos montana var. ultrabasica

Symplocos montana var. ultrabasica, commonly known as the ultra-basic symplocos, is a variant of the symplocos plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are usually berries that contain several seeds.

This variant is adapted to grow in acidic to ultra-basic soils, reflecting its unique ecological niche.
