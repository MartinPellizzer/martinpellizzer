Silene tunetana

Silene tunetana, commonly known as the Tunisian catchfly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region, particularly Tunisia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pinkish-purple flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and fleshy, helping it conserve water in dry conditions.

Silene tunetana is often found in open habitats such as coastal areas and limestone formations.
