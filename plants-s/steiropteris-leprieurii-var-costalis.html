Steiropteris leprieurii var. costalis

Steiropteris leprieurii var. costalis, commonly known as the Costate Shield Fern, is a tropical fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its pinnately compound leaves with a distinctive, wavy margin. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest understories. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a leathery texture.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in humid, shaded gardens.
