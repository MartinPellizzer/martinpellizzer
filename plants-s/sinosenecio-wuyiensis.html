Sinosenecio wuyiensis

Sinosenecio wuyiensis, commonly known as the Wuyi sinosenecio, is a flowering plant native to China, specifically found in the Wuyi Mountains.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are small and clustered in a composite head.

This species is of interest to botanists due to its limited distribution and potential ecological significance in its native habitat.
