Sabicea urceolata

Sabicea urceolata, commonly known as the urn-shaped sabicea, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive urn-shaped inflorescences, which are composed of clustered, small, white flowers. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its unique floral structure, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
