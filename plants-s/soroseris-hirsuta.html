Soroseris hirsuta

Soroseris hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy soroseris, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features hairy stems and leaves, giving it its common name. Its yellow flowers are typically arranged in a flat-topped cluster.

Soroseris hirsuta is often found in moist, open habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges.
