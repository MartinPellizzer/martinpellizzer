Stelis mononeura

Stelis mononeura, commonly known as the single-veined orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, slender stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Stelis mononeura is noted for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
