Stewartia obovata

Stewartia obovata, commonly known as the Chinese stewartia, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that turn yellow in the fall. The tree produces small, fragrant white flowers in the spring, followed by ornamental red or orange capsules. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

Stewartia obovata is valued in horticulture for its elegant form and seasonal interest.
