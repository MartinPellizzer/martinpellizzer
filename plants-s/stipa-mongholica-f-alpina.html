Stipa mongholica f. alpina

Stipa mongholica f. alpina, commonly known as the alpine feathergrass, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is a perennial species characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and delicate, silver-green foliage.
