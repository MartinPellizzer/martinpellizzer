Stylagrostis chrysantha

Stylagrostis chrysantha, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow and orange flowers that resemble flames, which bloom in clusters during the warm seasons. The plant has a slender, erect stem with lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the rich, moist soil of its natural habitat.

Stylagrostis chrysantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
