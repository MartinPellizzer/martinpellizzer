Senecio chrysanthemoides var. spectabilis

Senecio chrysanthemoides var. spectabilis, commonly known as the showy groundsella, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its upright, branched stems and dense clusters of small, yellow flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and linear, with a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
