Salacia elegans var. elegans

Salacia elegans var. elegans, commonly known as the elegant salacia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and striking, colorful flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a glossy surface and a strong, aromatic scent. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, with prominent red stamens that attract pollinators.

Salacia elegans var. elegans is valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
