Stachys siirtensis

Stachys siirtensis, commonly known as Siirt hedge-nettle, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and pairs of opposite leaves. Its small, tubular flowers are typically purple or pink, arranged in dense spikes.

Stachys siirtensis is often found in rocky or sandy soils in open habitats.
