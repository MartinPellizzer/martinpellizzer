Scabiosa triniifolia var. setigera

Scabiosa triniifolia var. setigera, commonly known as the fringed scabious, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It features slender, branched stems that grow up to 30 cm in height, often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, pink or purple flowers with distinctive fringed petals, which bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a soft, white down, giving them a fuzzy appearance.

This species is often found in dry, sunny grasslands and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
