Scabiosa japonica var. japonica

Scabiosa japonica var. japonica, commonly known as Japanese scabious, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It is characterized by its upright, branched stems and clusters of small, sky-blue to violet flowers. The plant produces fluffy, seed-like structures that are dispersed by the wind, aiding in its propagation. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its delicate appearance and extended blooming period make it a valuable addition to ornamental gardens.
