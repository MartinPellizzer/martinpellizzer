Salsola Dentata

Salsola dentata is a hardy shrub commonly found in arid and semi-arid regions. It is known for its ability to thrive in harsh environments with minimal water. The plant has small, spiny leaves that help reduce water loss. It is often used as a forage plant for livestock in dry climates. Salsola dentata plays an important role in stabilizing soil and preventing erosion.
