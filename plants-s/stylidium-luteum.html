Stylidium luteum

Stylidium luteum, commonly known as the yellow sun-orchid, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal regions. Its flowers are small and clustered, with a unique structure that includes a specialized column for pollination.

Stylidium luteum is also known for its ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
