Silene rhodopea

Silene rhodopea, commonly known as the Rhodope catchfly, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its erect stems and small, pinkish-red flowers. Silene rhodopea is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
