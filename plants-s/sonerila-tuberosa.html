Sonerila tuberosa

Sonerila tuberosa, commonly known as the tuberous sonerila, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant produces tuberous roots, which are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation in tropical gardens.
