Stenorrhynchos bonariense

Stenorrhynchos bonariense, commonly known as the Buenos Aires orchid, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are broad and leathery, adapted to retain moisture in its native habitat.
