Senecio kirkii

Senecio kirkii, commonly known as the Kirk's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid environments. It features silver-green leaves with a fuzzy texture and produces small yellow flowers.

Senecio kirkii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
