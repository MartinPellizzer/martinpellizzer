Senecio hockii

Senecio hockii, commonly known as the Hockii groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun exposure. It features narrow, linear leaves and produces small yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

Senecio hockii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
