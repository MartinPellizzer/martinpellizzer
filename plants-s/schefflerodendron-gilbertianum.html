Schefflerodendron gilbertianum

Schefflerodendron gilbertianum, commonly known as the Gilbert's schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of compound leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers that are followed by bright red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a ornamental plant.
