Styrax diplotrichus

Styrax diplotrichus, commonly known as the Chinese frankincense tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Styracaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces a resinous sap that has been historically used in traditional medicine and as a perfume. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a smooth texture, while its bark is rough and grayish in color.

Styrax diplotrichus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and its historical significance in herbal remedies.
