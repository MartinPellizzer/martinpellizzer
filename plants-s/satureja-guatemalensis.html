Satureja guatemalensis

Satureja guatemalensis, commonly known as Guatemalan thyme, is a perennial herb native to Central America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants known for their essential oils. The plant features small, pink to purple flowers and aromatic leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for Mediterranean climates.

Its chemical composition includes compounds like thymol and carvacrol, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
