Sedum jaeschkei

Sedum jaeschkei, commonly known as the Jaeschke's stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, clustered leaves that are typically green to blue-green in color. The plant produces star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange, which bloom in late summer. It is well adapted to arid and rocky environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

Sedum jaeschkei is often cultivated in rock gardens and as a decorative succulent due to its hardiness and attractive appearance.
