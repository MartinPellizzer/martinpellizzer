Stelis tenuilabris

Stelis tenuilabris, commonly known as the narrow-lipped stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a slender, wiry stem with several nodes and small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with a narrow lip that distinguishes it from other species.

It requires high humidity and indirect light to thrive, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
