Saxifraga sinensis var. discolor

Saxifraga sinensis var. discolor, commonly known as the Chinese snowbell, is a perennial herb native to China.

It features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with a bluish-white underside. The plant produces bell-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and shady borders.
