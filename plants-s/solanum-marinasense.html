Solanum marinasense

Solanum marinasense, commonly known as the Marinasense nightshade, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant typically features small, yellow flowers and produces berries that are toxic to humans and animals. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with well-drained soil.

Due to its toxicity, it is often found in wild or undisturbed habitats.
