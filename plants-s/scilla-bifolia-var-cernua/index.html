Scilla Bifolia Var. Cernua

Scilla bifolia var. cernua is a strikingly beautiful plant known for its delicate, nodding blue flowers. Native to parts of Europe and the Middle East, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation in gardens. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that add texture to the landscape. With its unique appearance and hardy nature, it remains a favorite among gardeners and botanists alike.
