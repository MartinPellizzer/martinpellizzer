Senecio katangensis

Senecio katangensis, commonly known as the Katanga groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the Democratic Republic of the Congo.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and usually have a rough texture.

Senecio katangensis produces small yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, hard fruits.
