Schizostachyum pilosum

Schizostachyum pilosum, commonly known as the hairy palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and pinnate leaves. Its adaptability to various soil types makes it a resilient species in tropical environments.
