Statice ambigua var. ponzoi

Statice ambigua var. ponzoi, commonly known as the Ponzian statice, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces dense clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with the leaves often covered in a fine, silvery-white coating.

The species is known for its long-lasting blooms, which make it a favorite among gardeners and florists alike.
