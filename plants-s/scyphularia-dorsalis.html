Scyphularia dorsalis

Scyphularia dorsalis, commonly known as the broad-leaved scyphularia, is a flowering plant belonging to the sunflower family (Asteraceae).

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and is characterized by its basal rosette of broad, hairy leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a flat, disc-like head, which is typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in alpine environments.

Scyphularia dorsalis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
