Setaria kersteniana

Setaria kersteniana, commonly known as kersten's foxtail, is a species of grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny grains. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in open habitats.

Setaria kersteniana is adapted to arid conditions and is drought-tolerant.
