Stenorrhynchos lanceolatum var. luteoalbum

Stenorrhynchos lanceolatum var. luteoalbum, commonly known as the yellow-white orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces strikingly colorful flowers with a distinctive yellow and white coloration. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
