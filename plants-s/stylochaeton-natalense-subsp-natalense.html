Stylochaeton natalense subsp. natalense

Stylochaeton natalense subsp.

natalense, commonly known as Natal grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa. It typically grows in open woodland and grassland habitats, preferring well-drained soils.

The plant features slender, erect culms with narrow, linear leaves that are often hairy along the edges. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous tiny flowers.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
