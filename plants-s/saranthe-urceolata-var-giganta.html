Saranthe urceolata var. giganta

Saranthe urceolata var. giganta, commonly known as the giant urn plant, is a striking member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, urn-shaped flowers that are typically red or purple in color. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
