Saussurea pseudogracilis

Saussurea pseudogracilis, commonly known as the slender yarrow, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, including parts of China, Mongolia, and Kazakhstan. Saussurea pseudogracilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its resilience and aesthetic features.
