Statice bellidifolia subsp. dubyi

Statice bellidifolia subsp.

dubyi, commonly known as the Duby's statice, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe. It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, showy flowers.

The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions. Its flowers are usually pink or red, and it blooms from summer to autumn.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in garden settings.
