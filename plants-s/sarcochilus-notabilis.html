Sarcochilus notabilis

Sarcochilus notabilis, commonly known as the showy sarcochilus, is an epiphytic orchid native to eastern Australia.

It features a thick, fleshy rhizome and produces striking, showy flowers with dark red petals and a yellow lip. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers bloom in spring and are highly fragrant, attracting various pollinators.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and protected species in its native range.
