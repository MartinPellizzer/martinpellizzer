Stelis floresii

Stelis floresii, commonly known as the Flores stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to the island of Flores in Indonesia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and clustered inflorescences. The plant typically features long, slender pseudobulbs and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrate for optimal growth.

Stelis floresii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
