Spathoglottis alpina

Spathoglottis alpina, commonly known as the Alpine spathoglottis, is a species of orchid native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas and Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial herb with erect, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, showy flowers with a greenish-yellow spathe and a yellowish-white lip. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky areas.

Spathoglottis alpina is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
