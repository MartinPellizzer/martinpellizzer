Sansevieria craigii

Sansevieria craigii, commonly known as Craig's Snake Plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are dark green with prominent yellow variegation. The plant is highly resilient, capable of thriving in low-light conditions and requiring minimal water. Its leaves are stiff and fibrous, which helps it store water efficiently.

Sansevieria craigii is also known for its air-purifying properties, making it a popular houseplant.
