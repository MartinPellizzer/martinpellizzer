Sphenanthera robusta var. viridis

Sphenanthera robusta var. viridis, commonly known as the green sphenanthera, is a variant of the sphenanthera species native to specific regions in Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its robust growth habit and distinctive foliage, featuring broad, lance-shaped leaves with a vibrant green coloration. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, contributing to its ornamental value. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade, making it suitable for shaded garden environments.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated for horticultural purposes.
