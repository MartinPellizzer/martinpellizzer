Sorbus pseudovilmorinii

Sorbus pseudovilmorinii, commonly known as the Himalayan whitebeams, is a species of shrub or small tree native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its small, white to yellowish flowers that appear in clusters. The plant produces small, red or orange berries that are edible but not widely consumed by humans. It thrives in high-altitude environments and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

Its foliage is typically dense and provides habitat for various wildlife species.
