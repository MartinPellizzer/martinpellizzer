Seriphidium lercheanum var. astrachanicum

Seriphidium lercheanum var. astrachanicum, commonly known as the Astrachan wormwood, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in steppes and rocky areas. The plant features gray-green, woolly leaves and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters. It is characterized by its aromatic properties and is often used in traditional medicine.

Its resilience to harsh climates makes it an important species in its ecological niche.
