Scorzonera brevicaulis

Scorzonera brevicaulis, commonly known as the short-stemmed scorzonera, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy, cylindrical stems and broad, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers with five petals, typically blooming in late summer. Its root system is fibrous and often used in traditional medicine.

Scorzonera brevicaulis thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, arid environments.
