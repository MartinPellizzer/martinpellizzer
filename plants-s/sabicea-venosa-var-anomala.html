Sabicea venosa var. anomala

Sabicea venosa var. anomala, commonly known as the veined sabicea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive veined leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides.
