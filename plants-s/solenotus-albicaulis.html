Solenotus albicaulis

Solenotus albicaulis, commonly known as the white-stemmed solenotus, is a small herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and shrublands. The plant features slender, hairy stems and small, opposite leaves that are often rounded or ovate in shape. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, clustered in dense inflorescences, and are borne on short pedicels.

Solenotus albicaulis is known for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
