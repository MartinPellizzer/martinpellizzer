Scorzoneroides montaniformis

Scorzoneroides montaniformis, commonly known as the mountain scorzonera, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically thrives in rocky, well-drained soils at high altitudes. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, oval-shaped fruits that contain seeds.

Scorzoneroides montaniformis is often found in meadows and open slopes where it contributes to the biodiversity of mountain ecosystems.
