Sonchus × rupicola

Sonchus × rupicola, commonly known as the rock lettuce, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its robust growth habit and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils. The plant features basal rosettes of spoon-shaped leaves with serrated edges, and it produces yellow flowers in composite inflorescences. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it adaptable to arid environments.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
