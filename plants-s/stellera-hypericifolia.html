Stellera hypericifolia

Stellera hypericifolia, commonly known as the starflower or golden star, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, gray-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy yellow flowers in late spring to early summer, arranged in a star-like pattern. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in woodland or wetland habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
