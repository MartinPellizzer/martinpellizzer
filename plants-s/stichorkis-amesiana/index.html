Stichorkis Amesiana

Stichorkis amesiana is a rare and elusive plant native to the wetlands of southern Florida. It is known for its delicate, pale green leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers that bloom in late spring. This species is classified as endangered due to habitat loss and environmental changes. Its unique ecological role supports a variety of pollinators and aquatic life. Conservation efforts are ongoing to protect and restore its natural habitat.
