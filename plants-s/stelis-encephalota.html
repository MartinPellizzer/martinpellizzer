Stelis encephalota

Stelis encephalota, commonly known as the helmet orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, helmet-like floral structure, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually pale green or white, with intricate patterns that enhance its ornamental value.

Stelis encephalota is valued in horticulture for its distinctive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
