Sideritis calycantha

Sideritis calycantha, commonly known as the purple calycanthus, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its distinctive purple flowers and hairy, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or dry habitats.

Sideritis calycantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its potential health benefits.
