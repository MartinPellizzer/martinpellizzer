Silene Arvensis

Silene arvensis is a common flowering plant found in various habitats across Europe and parts of Asia. It typically grows in disturbed soils and is often seen in fields and along roadsides. The plant features pink or red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture. Silene arvensis plays a role in supporting local pollinators and is part of the Caryophyllaceae family.
