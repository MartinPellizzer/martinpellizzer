Scutellaria honanensis

Scutellaria honanensis, commonly known as Honan skullcap, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the mint family (Lamiaceae) and is characterized by its square, hairy stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or violet in color, arranged in whorls along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or shaded areas.

Scutellaria honanensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.
