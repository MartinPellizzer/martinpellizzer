Senecio pampeanus f. pampeanus

Senecio pampeanus f. pampeanus, commonly known as the Pampas senecio, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Argentina.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
