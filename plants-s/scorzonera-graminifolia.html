Scorzonera graminifolia

Scorzonera graminifolia, commonly known as the slender scorzonera, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright, branched stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is often found in rocky or alpine environments where it thrives in well-drained soils.
