Senecio procerus var. auratus

Senecio procerus var. auratus, commonly known as the golden beard plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and yellow-orange flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in arid environments. Its leaves are typically blue-gray in color and have a distinctive, woolly texture.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and drought tolerance.
