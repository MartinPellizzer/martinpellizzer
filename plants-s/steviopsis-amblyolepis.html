Steviopsis amblyolepis

Steviopsis amblyolepis, commonly known as the mountain stevia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and serrated leaves. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is often found in rocky or mountainous regions, thriving in well-drained soils.

Steviopsis amblyolepis is valued for its aromatic foliage and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
