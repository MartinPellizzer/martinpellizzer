Smilax kwangsiensis var. kwangsiensis

Smilax kwangsiensis var. kwangsiensis, commonly known as the Guangxi smilax, is a perennial climbing shrub native to Southeast China.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
