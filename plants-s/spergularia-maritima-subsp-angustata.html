Spergularia maritima subsp. angustata

Spergularia maritima subsp.

angustata, commonly known as narrowleaf sandspurry, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family. It is a low-growing perennial herb that thrives in coastal and sandy environments.

The plant features small, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer, and its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped. It is often found in dune habitats and is adapted to withstand salty and windy conditions.

Its ecological role includes stabilizing sand dunes and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
