Syngramma hookeri

Syngramma hookeri, commonly known as the Hooker's syngramma, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and white. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Syngramma hookeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
