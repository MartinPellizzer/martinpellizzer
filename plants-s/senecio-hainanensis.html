Senecio hainanensis

Senecio hainanensis, commonly known as Hainan groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the island of Hainan in China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests and rocky areas. It features slender, branched stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

Its yellow flowers bloom in the summer months, attracting various pollinators like bees and butterflies.
