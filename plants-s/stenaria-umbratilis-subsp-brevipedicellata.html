Stenaria umbratilis subsp. brevipedicellata

Stenaria umbratilis subsp.

brevipedicellata, commonly known as the short-stemmed stenaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, daisy-like flowers that typically bloom in the summer months.

The subspecies is distinguished by its shorter peduncles compared to other members of the species. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a valuable plant for ecological studies and native garden landscapes.
