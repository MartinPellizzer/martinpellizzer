Solanum rufopilosum

Solanum rufopilosum, commonly known as the red-hair nightshade, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by berries that range in color from red to purple. Its leaves are often covered in fine, reddish hairs, giving it its common name.

Solanum rufopilosum belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is known for its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties.
