Swertia oxyphylla var. oxyphylla

Swertia oxyphylla var. oxyphylla, commonly known as the Himalayan gentian, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of the Himalayas.

It is a herbaceous perennial with lance-shaped leaves and produces striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows at high altitudes, often in rocky or gravelly soils. It is part of the Gentianaceae family, known for its showy, often blue or purple flowers.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens in cooler climates.
