Sutera foliolosa

Sutera foliolosa, commonly known as the leafy sutera, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Campanulaceae.

It is native to the Canary Islands and is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. Sutera foliolosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a garden setting.
