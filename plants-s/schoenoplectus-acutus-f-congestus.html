Schoenoplectus acutus f. congestus

Schoenoplectus acutus f. congestus, commonly known as the dense bulrush, is a flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, cylindrical inflorescence and narrow, grass-like leaves. This plant typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes and shallow waters. It has fibrous roots that help stabilize soil and improve water filtration.
