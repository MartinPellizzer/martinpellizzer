Solanum nemophilum var. typicum

Solanum nemophilum var. typicum, commonly known as the woodland nightshade, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and forest understories. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. Its flowers are bell-shaped, often purple or violet, and appear in clusters.

The fruit is a fleshy berry that contains several seeds, and the plant is part of the nightshade family, Solanaceae.
