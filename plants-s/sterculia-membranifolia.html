Sterculia membranifolia

Sterculia membranifolia, commonly known as the paper-leaf sterculia, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, thin, and papery leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody pods containing flat, winged seeds. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local cultures.
