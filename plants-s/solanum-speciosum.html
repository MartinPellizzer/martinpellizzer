Solanum speciosum

Solanum speciosum, commonly known as the showy nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its fruits are berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

Solanum speciosum is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and flowers.
