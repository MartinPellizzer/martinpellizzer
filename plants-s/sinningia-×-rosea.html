Sinningia × rosea

Sinningia × rosea, commonly known as the gloxinia, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to South America and is characterized by its velvety, heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant typically produces vibrant blooms in shades of red, pink, and white, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil.

Sinningia × rosea is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
