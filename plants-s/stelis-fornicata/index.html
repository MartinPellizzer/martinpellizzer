Stelis Fornicata

Stelis fornicata is a rare and delicate orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. It is known for its striking white flowers and unique growth habits. This species thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-drained soil. Due to habitat loss, it faces significant threats in the wild. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this beautiful and endangered plant.
