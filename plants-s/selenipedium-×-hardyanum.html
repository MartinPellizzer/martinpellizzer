Selenipedium × hardyanum

Selenipedium × hardyanum, commonly known as the hybrid giant spider-orchid, is a rare and fascinating orchid native to the tropical rainforests of Central and South America.

It is a hybrid between Selenipedium grandiflorum and Selenipedium wittigianum, characterized by its large, showy, spider-like flowers. The plant is known for its unique morphology, with long, arching inflorescences and striking, violet-colored blooms. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires specific conditions to flourish.

Due to its rarity and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
