Simirestis polyantha

Simirestis polyantha, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly serrated edge.

Simirestis polyantha thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for garden borders and container planting.
