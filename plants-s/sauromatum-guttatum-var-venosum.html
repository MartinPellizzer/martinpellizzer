Sauromatum guttatum var. venosum

Sauromatum guttatum var. venosum, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a striking tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It features large, showy flowers with deep red petals and prominent yellow veins, which give it its common name. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are often spotted with white or yellow markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found near water sources.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and exotic plant collections.
