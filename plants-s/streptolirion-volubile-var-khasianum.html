Streptolirion volubile var. khasianum

Streptolirion volubile var. khasianum, commonly known as the Khasi climbing bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the northeastern region of India.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and showy, bell-shaped flowers.
