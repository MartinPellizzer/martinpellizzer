Sesleria

Sesleria, commonly known as the pink grass or bog grass, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Poaceae.

It consists of approximately 100 species, predominantly found in temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere. Sesleria species are valued for their ornamental appeal and ecological importance in wetland habitats.
