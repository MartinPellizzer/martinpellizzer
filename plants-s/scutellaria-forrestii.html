Scutellaria forrestii

Scutellaria forrestii, commonly known as Forest's blueweed, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Scutellaria forrestii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
