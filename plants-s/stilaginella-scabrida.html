Stilaginella scabrida

Stilaginella scabrida, commonly known as the rough-stemmed stiltgrass, is a member of the Poaceae family.

It is a perennial grass that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant has slender, upright stems with a rough, textured surface. Its narrow leaves are usually a dull green and are arranged alternately along the stem.

Stilaginella scabrida produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in spikelets, typical of grasses.
