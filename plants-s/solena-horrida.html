Solena horrida

Solena horrida, commonly known as the horrid solena, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Solena horrida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its distinctive appearance.
