Strychnos hippocrateoides

Strychnos hippocrateoides, commonly known as the yellow strychnos, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by orange or red berries containing seeds. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly serrated margin.

The plant is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its attractive foliage and fruit.
