Satureja anagae

Satureja anagae, commonly known as the Anaga thyme, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky and coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are fragrant and often used in local cuisine and traditional medicine.

Satureja anagae is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
