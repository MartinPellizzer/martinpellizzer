Sickingia hadrantha

Sickingia hadrantha, commonly known as the blue ice plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that form a dense, mat-like growth. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink to purple during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.

Sickingia hadrantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in arid environments.
