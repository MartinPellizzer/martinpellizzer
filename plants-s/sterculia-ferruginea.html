Sterculia ferruginea

Sterculia ferruginea, commonly known as the rusty sterculia, is a tropical tree native to northeastern Australia.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its rough, fissured bark and large, heart-shaped leaves. The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses by Indigenous communities.
