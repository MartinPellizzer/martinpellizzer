Stellaria verna

Stellaria verna, commonly known as common chickweed, is a low-growing annual herb native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It features small, white star-shaped flowers and is characterized by its prostrate or ascending growth habit. The plant has thin, hairy stems and alternate, spoon-shaped leaves with a hairy texture. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in lawns, gardens, and along pathways.

Stellaria verna is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through seeds and rhizomes.
