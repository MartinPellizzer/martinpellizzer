Spiranthes hyemalis

Spiranthes hyemalis, commonly known as the winter spire orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its delicate, spiral-shaped inflorescence. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wetlands. Its flowers are small and white or pale green, often appearing in late winter or early spring.

Spiranthes hyemalis has a rhizomatous root system and produces seeds that are dispersed by wind.
