Senecio pachyphyllos

Senecio pachyphyllos, commonly known as the thick-leaved senecio, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It prefers well-draining soil and full sun exposure, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its slow growth and unique appearance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
