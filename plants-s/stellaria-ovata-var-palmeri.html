Stellaria ovata var. palmeri

Stellaria ovata var. palmeri, commonly known as Palmer's starwort, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, star-shaped white flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and has hairy, slender stems. Its leaves are opposite and linear, with a slightly rough texture.
