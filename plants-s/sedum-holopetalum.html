Sedum holopetalum

Sedum holopetalum, commonly known as the stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to arid and rocky environments.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in dry conditions. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red, typically blooming in summer. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Sedum holopetalum is also valued for its ability to prevent soil erosion and its low maintenance requirements.
