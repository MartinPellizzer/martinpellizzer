Stenocarpus heterophyllus

Stenocarpus heterophyllus, commonly known as the New Zealand bottlebrush, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its unique, bottle-shaped fruits that contain numerous seeds. The plant has compound leaves with alternating leaflets and produces striking red or orange flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in coastal and forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections worldwide.
