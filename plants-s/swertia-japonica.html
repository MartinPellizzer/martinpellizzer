Swertia japonica

Swertia japonica, commonly known as Japanese swertia, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Swertia japonica is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
