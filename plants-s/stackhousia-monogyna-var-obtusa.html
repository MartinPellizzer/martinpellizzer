Stackhousia monogyna var. obtusa

Stackhousia monogyna var. obtusa, commonly known as the smooth stackhousia, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in winter, attracting pollinators like bees and birds. Its leaves are narrow and leathery, with a smooth texture that distinguishes it from other varieties.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive appearance and adaptability to well-drained soils.
