Semeiostachys ugamica

Semeiostachys ugamica, commonly known as the golden spike grass, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green inflorescences that resemble spikes, which are typical of the genus Semeiostachys. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland forests and wetlands.
