Schaefferia cuneifolia var. pedicellata

Schaefferia cuneifolia var. pedicellata, commonly known as the pedicellate schaefferia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a distinctive blue bloom. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually blue or purple, arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact size and long-lasting blooms.
