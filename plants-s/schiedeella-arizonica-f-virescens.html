Schiedeella arizonica f. virescens

Schiedeella arizonica f. virescens, commonly known as the Arizona greenleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, yellow composite flowers that bloom in early spring. It is native to the southwestern United States, particularly Arizona.

This variety is distinguished by its darker foliage compared to other forms of Schiedeella arizonica.
