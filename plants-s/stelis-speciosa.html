Stelis speciosa

Stelis speciosa, commonly known as the showy stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a short, branching stem with clustered, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are showy, with bright colors and intricate patterns, often appearing in spring or early summer.

Stelis speciosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in tropical gardens.
