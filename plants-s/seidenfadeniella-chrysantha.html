Seidenfadeniella chrysantha

Seidenfadeniella chrysantha, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its vibrant yellow and orange flowers. The plant features slender, erect stems with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. Its flowers are borne in clusters and are known for their intricate, showy structure.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in greenhouses and tropical gardens.
