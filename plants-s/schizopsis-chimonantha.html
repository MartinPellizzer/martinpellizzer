Schizopsis chimonantha

Schizopsis chimonantha, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers with a distinctive star-shaped pattern on the petals. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Schizopsis chimonantha is valued in horticulture for its unique floral display and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
