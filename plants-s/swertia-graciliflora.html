Swertia graciliflora

Swertia graciliflora, commonly known as slender-flowered swertia, is a herbaceous plant native to the Himalayas.

It typically grows in alpine and subalpine regions, preferring cool, moist environments. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, blue to purple flowers clustered in racemes.
