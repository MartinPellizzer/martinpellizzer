Senecio gunckelii

Senecio gunckelii, commonly known as the Gunckel's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid environments. It features narrow, linear leaves and produces small yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
