Sempervivum × Funckii Var. Stenopetalum

Sempervivum × funckii var. stenopetalum is a striking plant known for its vibrant, rosette-shaped foliage. It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is often referred to as the "Alpine houseleek" due to its hardy nature. This variety is prized for its compact growth and colorful leaves that range from green to red and purple. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers. Its resilience and aesthetic appeal make it a favorite among succulent enthusiasts.
