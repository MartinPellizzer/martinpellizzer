Sisymbrium columnae subsp. gaussenii

Sisymbrium columnae subsp.

gaussenii, commonly known as the Gaussen's tansy, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants.

The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small yellow flowers arranged in racemes. Its leaves are pinnately divided, giving it a delicate, fern-like appearance.
