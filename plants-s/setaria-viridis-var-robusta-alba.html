Setaria viridis var. robusta-alba

Setaria viridis var. robusta-alba, commonly known as white foxtail, is a variant of the green foxtail grass.

It is characterized by its tall, robust stalks and dense, fluffy white inflorescences that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant is native to Africa and is often found in disturbed soils and agricultural fields. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, wind-dispersed seeds.

This variant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in ecological restoration projects.
