Schubertia sempervirens

Schubertia sempervirens, commonly known as the evergreen schubertia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink or white. Schubertia sempervirens thrives in well-drained soils and requires a cool, temperate climate.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
