Silene bocquetiana

Silene bocquetiana, commonly known as the Bocquet's campion, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a perennial herb with slender, upright stems and produces showy pink to purple flowers. The plant has alternate, ovate leaves that are often hairy, and it thrives in rocky or sandy soils. It is part of the Caryophyllaceae family, which includes many flowering plants with distinctive flower structures.
