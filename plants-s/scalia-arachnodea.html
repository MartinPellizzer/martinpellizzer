Scalia arachnodea

Scalia arachnodea, commonly known as the spider scale plant, is a unique succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, scale-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to its otherwise spiky appearance. Its leaves are adapted to store water, making it highly drought-tolerant.

The plant's distinctive appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
