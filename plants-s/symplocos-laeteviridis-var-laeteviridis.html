Symplocos laeteviridis var. laeteviridis

Symplocos laeteviridis var. laeteviridis, commonly known as the green-leaved evergreen shrub, is a member of the Ericaceae family.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that remain throughout the year. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers in clusters, which are typically followed by bright red berries. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
