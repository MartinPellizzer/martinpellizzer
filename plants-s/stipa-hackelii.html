Stipa hackelii

Stipa hackelii, commonly known as the Hackel's needlegrass, is a species of grass native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial plant characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and slopes.

Stipa hackelii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in arid environments.
