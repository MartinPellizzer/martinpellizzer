Specklinia barbacenensis

Specklinia barbacenensis, commonly known as the Brazilian tree fern, is a member of the Cyatheaceae family and is native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feather-like fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests. Its rhizome is thick and fibrous, supporting its growth and enabling it to store moisture.

Specklinia barbacenensis is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
