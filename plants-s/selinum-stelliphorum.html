Selinum stelliphorum

Selinum stelliphorum, commonly known as starflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It features a basal rosette of silver-gray, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces striking, showy yellow flowers in clusters that resemble stars, hence its common name. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

Selinum stelliphorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
