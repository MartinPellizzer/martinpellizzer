Stizolobium junghuhniana

Stizolobium junghuhniana, commonly known as the yellow flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its striking yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has bipinnate leaves, which are divided into small, feather-like segments. Its fruit is a legume pod that contains several seeds, and the tree is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Stizolobium junghuhniana is well adapted to warm, humid climates and can thrive in a variety of soil types.
