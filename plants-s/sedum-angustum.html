Sedum angustum

Sedum angustum, commonly known as narrowleaf stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features upright, branching stems covered in small, fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a tight rosette. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped, yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Sedum angustum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
