Stachys × medebachensis

Stachys × medebachensis, commonly known as the hybrid mint or velvetleaf hedge.

This plant is a perennial herbaceous species native to the Mediterranean region, characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. It produces clusters of purple or pink flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and its ability to thrive in well-drained soil.

It is also known for its aromatic leaves, which can be used in herbal teas.
