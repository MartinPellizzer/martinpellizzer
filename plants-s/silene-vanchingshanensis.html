Silene vanchingshanensis

Silene vanchingshanensis, commonly known as the Vanchingshan catchfly, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its delicate pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous habitats, thriving in temperate climates. It has slender stems and small, lance-shaped leaves.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
