Scabiosa comosa f. albiflora

Scabiosa comosa f. albiflora, commonly known as the white scabious, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caprifoliaceae family.

It is characterized by its fluffy, white blooms that resemble small clouds, giving it a delicate and ethereal appearance. The foliage is usually gray-green with hairy surfaces, adding to its distinctive botanical features.
