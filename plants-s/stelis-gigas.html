Stelis gigas

Stelis gigas, commonly known as the giant stelis orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, showy flowers that can reach up to 15 centimeters in diameter. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, utilizing its aerial roots to absorb moisture and nutrients from the environment. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive three-lobed lip that enhances its ornamental appeal.

Stelis gigas is valued in horticulture for its dramatic appearance and is often cultivated in warm, humid climates.
