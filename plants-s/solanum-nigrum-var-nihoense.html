Solanum nigrum var. nihoense

Solanum nigrum var. nihoense, commonly known as the Japanese black nightshade, is a variant of the common nightshade plant.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces small, dark purple to black berries that are edible but contain toxic compounds. Its leaves are ovate with serrated edges and have a slightly prickly texture.

It is native to parts of East Asia, including Japan, and is often found in woodland and garden settings.
