Scutellaria hebeclada

Scutellaria hebeclada, commonly known as the narrow-leaved skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.

Scutellaria hebeclada is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
