Stachys duriaei

Stachys duriaei, commonly known as the Duriaei hedge nettle, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, arranged in dense spikes. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Stachys duriaei is typically found in rocky or disturbed soils and is adapted to arid environments.
