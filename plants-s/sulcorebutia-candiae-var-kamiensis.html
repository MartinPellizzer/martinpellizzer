Sulcorebutia candiae var. kamiensis

Sulcorebutia candiae var. kamiensis, commonly known as the Kamiensis Pincushion Cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It features a dense, spiny exterior with a rounded shape, and its stems are often covered in a layer of white, powdery bloom. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It is well-suited for rock gardens and containers due to its slow growth and drought tolerance.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
