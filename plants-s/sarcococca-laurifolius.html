Sarcococca laurifolius

Sarcococca laurifolius, commonly known as the fragrant wintergreen, is a dense evergreen shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features glossy, dark green, leathery leaves that remain throughout the year. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers in late winter, followed by bright red berries that persist into spring. It thrives in partial to full shade and moist, well-drained soil.

Sarcococca laurifolius is valued for its ornamental foliage and winter interest in gardens.
