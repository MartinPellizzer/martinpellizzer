Scorzonera tau-saghyz subsp. mariae

Scorzonera tau-saghyz subsp.

mariae, commonly known as the Caucasian scorzonera, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Caucasus region. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and basal rosettes of leaves.

The plant produces yellow flowers with ray florets, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It is adapted to grow in rocky or dry soils and is often found in mountainous areas.
