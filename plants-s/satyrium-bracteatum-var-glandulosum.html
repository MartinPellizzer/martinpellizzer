Satyrium bracteatum var. glandulosum

Satyrium bracteatum var. glandulosum, commonly known as the glandular leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves that are covered in glandular hairs. The flowers are small and greenish-white, often clustered in loose inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically in forest understories.

This variety is notable for its unique morphology and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
