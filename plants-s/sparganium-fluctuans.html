Sparganium fluctuans

Sparganium fluctuans, commonly known as floating burr rush, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is characterized by its floating stems and specialized structures that allow it to remain buoyant on the water's surface. The plant produces small, spiky flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color. It thrives in shallow, still or slow-moving freshwater environments.
