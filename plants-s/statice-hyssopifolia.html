Statice hyssopifolia

Statice hyssopifolia, commonly known as sea lavender, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant thrives in well-drained, sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal areas. Its foliage is narrow and gray-green, with a slightly woolly texture.

Statice hyssopifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in garden landscapes and floral arrangements.
