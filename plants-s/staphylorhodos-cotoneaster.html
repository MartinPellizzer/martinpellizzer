Staphylorhodos cotoneaster

Staphylorhodos cotoneaster, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter on the underside. The plant produces clusters of white or pink flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for landscaping and hedges.
