Stellera passerina var. tenuior

Stellera passerina var. tenuior, commonly known as slender starflower, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the northeastern United States.

It features small, star-shaped white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has thin, wiry stems and alternate, narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in woodlands and shaded areas.

Its botanical structure supports its role as a nectar source for early pollinators.
