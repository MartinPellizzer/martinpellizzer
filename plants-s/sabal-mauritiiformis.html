Sabal mauritiiformis

Sabal mauritiiformis, commonly known as the Florida fan palm, is a species native to the southeastern United States, particularly Florida.

It is a member of the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its fan-shaped leaves that radiate from a central stem. The plant typically grows in wetlands and coastal areas, where it thrives in moist, sandy soils. Its trunk is slender and often has a smooth, grayish-brown appearance.
