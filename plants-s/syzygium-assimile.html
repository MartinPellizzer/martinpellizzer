Syzygium assimile

Syzygium assimile, commonly known as the white apple or white lily.

It is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically yellow or red when mature. Syzygium assimile is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both horticultural and ecological applications.
