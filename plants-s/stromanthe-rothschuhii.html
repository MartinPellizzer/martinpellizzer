Stromanthe rothschuhii

Stromanthe rothschuhii, commonly known as the silver stripe stromanthe, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that feature silver and green stripes, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil. It is a member of the Marantaceae family, known for its ornamental foliage.

Stromanthe rothschuhii is relatively easy to care for, making it suitable for both novice and experienced plant enthusiasts.
