Scrophularia libanotica var. nevshehirensis

Scrophularia libanotica var. nevshehirensis, commonly known as the Nevshehir scrophularia, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae and is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces clusters of tubular, two-lipped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

This variety is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and limited distribution.
