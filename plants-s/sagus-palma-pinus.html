Sagus palma-pinus

Sagus palma-pinus, commonly known as the pine palm, is a unique hybrid plant that combines characteristics of both palms and pines.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features a tall, slender trunk with a crown of feather-like leaves resembling those of a palm. Its needles are actually modified leaves, and it produces cones similar to those of coniferous trees.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to warm, humid climates.
