Spermacoce linearifolia

Spermacoce linearifolia, commonly known as the slender starling bean, is a tropical plant native to regions of Africa, Southeast Asia, and the Pacific Islands.

It is a herbaceous annual plant that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, lanceolate leaves that are often hairy, and it produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release numerous tiny, smooth seeds.

The plant is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its foliage and floral display.
