Scorzonera turkeviczii

Scorzonera turkeviczii, commonly known as Turk's root, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and basal leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers with ray florets, followed by a tuberous root system that is often harvested for its edible qualities. It thrives in rocky and arid environments, showcasing adaptability to harsh conditions.

Scorzonera turkeviczii is valued both for its culinary use and its ornamental appeal in gardens.
