Spartium glabrum

Spartium glabrum, commonly known as slender spartium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its upright, herbaceous growth habit. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are simple and alternate, with a hairy texture that becomes smoother as the plant matures.

Spartium glabrum is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.
