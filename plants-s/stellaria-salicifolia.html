Stellaria salicifolia

Stellaria salicifolia, commonly known as willowleaf starwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

It features slender, willow-like leaves that are lanceolate and often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its flowers have five petals and are often accompanied by five stamens.

Stellaria salicifolia is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as woodland floors and stream banks.
