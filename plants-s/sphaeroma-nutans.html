Sphaeroma nutans

Sphaeroma nutans, commonly known as the nodding sea holly, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its compact, spherical shape and dense clusters of small, blue or violet flowers. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in dune environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, wind-dispersed seeds.

Sphaeroma nutans is adapted to salt spray and can tolerate moderate drought conditions.
