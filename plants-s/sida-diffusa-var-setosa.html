Sida diffusa var. setosa

Sida diffusa var. setosa, commonly known as the woolly sida, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb, often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides. Its fibrous stems and serrated leaf margins are notable botanical features.
