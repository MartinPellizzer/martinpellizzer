Scutellaria somalensis

Scutellaria somalensis, commonly known as Somali skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the Horn of Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky soils.

Scutellaria somalensis is valued for its medicinal properties, with traditional uses in treating various ailments.
