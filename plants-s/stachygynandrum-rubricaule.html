Stachygynandrum rubricaule

Stachygynandrum rubricaule, commonly known as the red-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems that often display a distinctive reddish hue. The plant produces small, clustered flowers with a pale yellow to white coloration, often with a red or pinkish tinge. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is typically found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
