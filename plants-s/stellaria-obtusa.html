Stellaria obtusa

Stellaria obtusa, commonly known as blunt starwort, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically features small, star-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale pink, with five petals. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. Stellaria obtusa thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.

It is often found in temperate regions and is known for its delicate, ephemeral blooms.
