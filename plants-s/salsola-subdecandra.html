Salsola subdecandra

Salsola subdecandra, commonly known as desert sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of the Americas.

The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne on slender pedicels. Salsola subdecandra is often found in sandy soils and is considered an important component of desert ecosystems.
