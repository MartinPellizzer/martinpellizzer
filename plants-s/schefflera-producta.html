Schefflera producta

Schefflera producta, commonly known as the umbrella tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, umbrella-like arrangement of large, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Schefflera producta is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various light conditions.
