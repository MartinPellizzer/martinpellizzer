Solanum formosanum

Solanum formosanum, commonly known as the Taiwan nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its woody stems and oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into berries, which are typically red when mature. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.

Solanum formosanum is noted for its ornamental value and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
