Sulcorebutia vasqueziana subsp.

losenickyana, commonly known as the Losenicky's crested cactus, is a species native to the Andes Mountains of South America. It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and a crown of spines that give it a distinctive appearance.

The plant typically produces yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.
