Stewartia sinensis var. brevicalyx

Stewartia sinensis var. brevicalyx, commonly known as the Chinese stewartia, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It features broad, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and have a smooth, waxy texture. The tree produces small, white, fragrant flowers in the spring, followed by ornamental red or orange capsules that persist into winter. Its bark is thick, flaky, and develops a distinctive mottled appearance with age.

This plant is valued in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
