Stelis cyathiflora

Stelis cyathiflora, commonly known as the cup-shaped orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its flowers are arranged in a dense, cup-like inflorescence, which is a distinctive botanical feature.

Stelis cyathiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
