Syringa × chinensis f. saugeana

Syringa × chinensis f. saugeana, commonly known as the saugeana weigela, is a hybrid shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant produces clusters of pink to lilac-colored blossoms that are highly attractive to pollinators. Its foliage is typically dark green and provides a striking contrast to the floral display.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to various garden conditions.
