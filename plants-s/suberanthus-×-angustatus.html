Suberanthus × angustatus

Suberanthus × angustatus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved suberanthus, is a hybrid plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in coastal regions. It has a deep root system that allows it to survive in arid conditions.

The species is of interest to botanists due to its unique hybrid characteristics and ecological adaptations.
