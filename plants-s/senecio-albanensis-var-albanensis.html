Senecio albanensis var. albanensis

Senecio albanensis var. albanensis, commonly known as the Albanian senecio, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing adaptations to arid environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers are yellow with a central disc.

This variant is distinguished by its specific geographic distribution and morphological features unique to its native range.
