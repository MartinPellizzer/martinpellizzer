Saxifraga × ramondii

Saxifraga × ramondii, commonly known as the Chinese stonecrop, is a hybrid plant native to East Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant features succulent, fleshy leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or purple during the summer months.

Saxifraga × ramondii is well adapted to rocky and well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.
