Stachys bigelovii

Stachys bigelovii, commonly known as blueweed or purple deadnettle, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant features square stems and opposite leaves with a distinctive blue or purple hue. Its flowers are small and clustered in whorls, with purple petals and yellow stamens.

Stachys bigelovii is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
