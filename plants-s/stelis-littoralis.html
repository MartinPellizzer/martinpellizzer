Stelis littoralis

Stelis littoralis, commonly known as the coastal stelis, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and often features attractive, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as along forest edges or near streams. Its leaves are usually broad and lance-shaped, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Stelis littoralis is valued in horticulture for its beauty and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
