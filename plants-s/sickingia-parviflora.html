Sickingia parviflora

Sickingia parviflora, commonly known as the dwarf ice plant, is a succulent native to southern Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, adapted for water storage. The plant produces tiny, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white, which bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Due to its drought tolerance, it is an easy-to-care-for plant suitable for arid climates.
