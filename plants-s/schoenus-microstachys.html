Schoenus microstachys

Schoenus microstachys, commonly known as the dwarf bulrush, is a perennial grass-like plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in wetland habitats such as marshes, swamps, and along the edges of water bodies. The plant has slender, erect stems that are often covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are narrow and grass-like in appearance. Its inflorescence consists of small, clustered spikelets that produce tiny seeds.

Schoenus microstachys plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic and semi-aquatic organisms.
