Silene orchidea

Silene orchidea, commonly known as the pink lady's slipper, is a striking member of the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy pink or purple flowers that resemble orchids in appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slight hairy texture.

Silene orchidea is known for its delicate beauty and is often found in temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.
