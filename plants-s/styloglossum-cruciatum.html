Styloglossum cruciatum

Styloglossum cruciatum, commonly known as the cross-stemmed orchid, is a unique flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive cross-shaped stem, which gives it its scientific name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. Styloglossum cruciatum thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to flourish.

Its botanical features make it a rare and visually striking addition to botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
