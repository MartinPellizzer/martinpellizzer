Sarracenia × sanderae

Sarracenia × sanderae, commonly known as the hybrid pitcher plant, is a fascinating carnivorous plant resulting from the cross between Sarracenia leucophylla and Sarracenia rubra.

It is characterized by its tall, sturdy stems and large, colorful pitcher-shaped leaves that trap and digest insects. The plant's pitchers are often red or orange, with intricate patterns that enhance their attractiveness to prey. It thrives in acidic, nutrient-poor soils and requires ample sunlight for optimal growth.

Sarracenia × sanderae is a popular choice among carnivorous plant enthusiasts due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
