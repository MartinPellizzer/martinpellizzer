Sabazia triangularis

Sabazia triangularis, commonly known as the triangular leaf orchid, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, triangular-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle, which helps reduce water loss in its humid habitat. The plant produces delicate, orchid-like flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
