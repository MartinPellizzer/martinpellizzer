Semenovia alaica

Semenovia alaica, commonly known as the Alay saxifrage, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Saxifragaceae and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to high-altitude environments.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species.
