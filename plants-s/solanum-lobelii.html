Solanum lobelii

Solanum lobelii, commonly known as the Lobel's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, peppers, and potatoes. The plant features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically violet or blue in color. It produces small, berry-like fruits that are toxic to humans but serve as a food source for certain animals.

Solanum lobelii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its striking appearance.
