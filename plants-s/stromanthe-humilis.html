Stromanthe humilis

Stromanthe humilis, commonly known as the dwarf stromanthe, is a compact, herbaceous plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It features striking, variegated leaves with a combination of green, white, and pink hues, often displaying a waxy texture. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil. Its foliage remains evergreen throughout the year, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Stromanthe humilis is known for its ability to thrive in humid environments, making it ideal for tropical or subtropical climates.
