Sanguisorba dictyocarpa

Sanguisorba dictyocarpa, commonly known as the clustered rosebay, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of East Asia.

It belongs to the rose family (Rosaceae) and is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, rose-like flowers. Sanguisorba dictyocarpa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in moist, well-drained soils.
