Salvia Fulgens F. Boucheana

Salvia fulgens f. boucheana is a striking ornamental plant known for its vibrant red bracts and compact growth habit. Native to Mexico, this cultivar thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. Its long-lasting flowers attract pollinators and add a dramatic visual impact to any landscape. The plant's unique coloration and resilience make it a favorite among gardeners seeking both beauty and durability. With proper care, it can bloom repeatedly throughout the growing season.
