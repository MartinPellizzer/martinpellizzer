Schefflerodendron usambarense var. macrophyllum

Schefflerodendron usambarense var. macrophyllum, commonly known as the large-leafed African schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like inflorescence composed of numerous small white flowers. The leaves are large, palmate, and often have a waxy texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The plant is valued for its foliage and is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant in warm climates.
