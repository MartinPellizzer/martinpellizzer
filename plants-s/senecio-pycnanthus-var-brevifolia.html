Senecio pycnanthus var. brevifolia

Senecio pycnanthus var. brevifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved groundsels, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and narrow, fleshy leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It produces small yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
