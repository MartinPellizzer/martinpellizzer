Schefflera tremuloidea var. marahuacensis

Schefflera tremuloidea var. marahuacensis, commonly known as the Marahuacan schefflera, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of leaves, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its foliage is glossy and dark green, with leaves that are oval to lance-shaped.

It thrives in humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant in warm climates.
