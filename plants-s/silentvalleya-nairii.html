Silentvalleya nairii

Silentvalleya nairii, commonly known as the Silent Valley orchid, is a rare and endemic species found primarily in the Western Ghats of India.

It is a terrestrial orchid with slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, pale green to white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded forest environments and is adapted to the specific microclimate of its habitat.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
