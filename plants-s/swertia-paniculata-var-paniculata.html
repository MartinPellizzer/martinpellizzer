Swertia paniculata var. paniculata

Swertia paniculata var. paniculata, commonly known as blue gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests and rocky slopes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in whorls around the stem.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
