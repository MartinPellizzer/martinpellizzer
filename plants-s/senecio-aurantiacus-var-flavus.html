Senecio aurantiacus var. flavus

Senecio aurantiacus var. flavus, commonly known as the Orange Senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to its habitat. Its leaves are typically narrow and slightly hairy, adapted to arid environments.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and drought tolerance.
