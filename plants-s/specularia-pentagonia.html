Specularia pentagonia

Specularia pentagonia, commonly known as the pentagon leaf sunflower, is a herbaceous plant native to the arid regions of South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, pentagonal-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with a distinctive central disk, typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun exposure to flourish.

Due to its unique morphology and adaptability, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping and drought-tolerant gardens.
