Schefflera angkae

Schefflera angkae, commonly known as the Angkara schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-shaped clusters of leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers that are followed by bright red berries, attracting various birds and insects. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Schefflera angkae is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor environments.
