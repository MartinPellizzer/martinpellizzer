Silene ucrainica

Silene ucrainica, commonly known as the Ukraine catchfly, is a flowering plant native to Eastern Europe and parts of Western Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. Silene ucrainica is often found in disturbed areas and can thrive in poor soil conditions.
