Stelis conochila

Stelis conochila, commonly known as the hooded orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in tropical rainforests. The plant has a compact growth habit with a short, horizontal stem and a single, showy flower. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow with a distinctive hood-like structure over the reproductive organs.

Stelis conochila is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
