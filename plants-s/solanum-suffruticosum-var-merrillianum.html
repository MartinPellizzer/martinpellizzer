Solanum suffruticosum var. merrillianum

Solanum suffruticosum var. merrillianum, commonly known as the Merril's nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant features small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by berries that range in color from green to red. Its leaves are typically ovate with a rough texture, and the plant often grows in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its toxic properties, it is not recommended for cultivation in home gardens.
