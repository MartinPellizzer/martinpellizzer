Satureja isophylla

Satureja isophylla, commonly known as narrow-leaved savory, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are small, narrow, and have a strong, pungent aroma similar to thyme and oregano.

Satureja isophylla is often used in traditional herbal medicine and as a culinary herb.
