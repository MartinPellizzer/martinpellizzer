Salacia miqueliana

Salacia miqueliana, commonly known as the Miquel's salacia, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Salaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and compound leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
