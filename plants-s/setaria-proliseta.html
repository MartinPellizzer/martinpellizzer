Setaria proliseta

Setaria proliseta, commonly known as slender barnyard grass, is a flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to Africa and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields. The plant has narrow, erect leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its seeds are dispersed by wind, allowing it to spread easily in agricultural environments.

It is considered a weed in many regions due to its rapid growth and competitive nature.
