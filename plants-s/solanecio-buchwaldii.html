Solanecio buchwaldii

Solanecio buchwaldii, commonly known as the Buchwald's sunflower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are usually alternate and lance-shaped, while its stems are erect and hairy.

Solanecio buchwaldii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its bright, showy blooms.
