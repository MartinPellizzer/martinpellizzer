Sinocrassula indica

Sinocrassula indica, commonly known as the Indian stonecress, is a low-growing, succulent plant native to the arid regions of India.

It features small, fleshy, ovate leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white or pale yellow flowers during the warmer months. It is adapted to thrive in poor, well-drained soils and requires minimal water.

Sinocrassula indica is often used in rock gardens or as a ground cover due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.
