Senecio lejolyanus

Senecio lejolyanus, commonly known as the Lejoly's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is often cultivated in rock gardens and as an ornamental plant.
