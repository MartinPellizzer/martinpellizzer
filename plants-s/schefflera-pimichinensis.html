Schefflera pimichinensis

Schefflera pimichinensis, commonly known as the Pimichinensis umbrella tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of large, leathery leaves. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters in its natural habitat. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, with a prominent central stem from which the leaves radiate.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to indoor environments.
