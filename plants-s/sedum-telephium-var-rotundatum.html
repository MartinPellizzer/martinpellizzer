Sedum telephium var. rotundatum

Sedum telephium var. rotundatum, commonly known as "Roundleaf Stonecrop," is a succulent perennial native to Europe.

It features fleshy, rounded leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it well-suited for arid environments.
