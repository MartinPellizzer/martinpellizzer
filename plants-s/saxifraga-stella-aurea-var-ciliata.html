Saxifraga stella-aurea var. ciliata

Saxifraga stella-aurea var. ciliata, commonly known as the star-like yellow saxifrage, is a low-growing perennial plant native to alpine and rocky habitats in Europe.

It features dense, mat-forming growth with small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The leaves are typically oval to lance-shaped, with finely toothed margins and a hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained, nutrient-poor soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
