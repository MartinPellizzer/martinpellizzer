Serruria elevata

Serruria elevata, commonly known as the mountain serruria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils at high altitudes. The plant produces striking blue or purple flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are narrow and serrated, giving it its scientific name.

It is often found in mountainous regions and is adapted to withstand harsh, arid conditions.
