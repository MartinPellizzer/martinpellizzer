Sphaeranthus ukambensis var. ukambensis

Sphaeranthus ukambensis var. ukambensis, commonly known as the East African thimbleweed, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is a small annual herb with narrow, linear leaves and small yellow flowers arranged in dense terminal clusters. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has a fibrous root system and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds.

The species is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive floral morphology.
