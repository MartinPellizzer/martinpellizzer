Sacciolepis cymbiandra

Sacciolepis cymbiandra, commonly known as the Madagascar star orchid, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, while its flowers are fragrant and attract various pollinators.

Sacciolepis cymbiandra is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
