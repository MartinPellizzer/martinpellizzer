Stolzia diffusa

Stolzia diffusa, commonly known as the spreading stolzia, is a South African plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its sprawling growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant has trifoliate leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in some regions.
