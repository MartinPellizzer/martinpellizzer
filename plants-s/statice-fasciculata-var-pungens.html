Statice fasciculata var. pungens

Statice fasciculata var. pungens, commonly known as the spiked statice, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and its clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant produces narrow, grass-like leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy choice for coastal or dry garden settings.

The variety is noted for its ability to bloom throughout the summer months.
