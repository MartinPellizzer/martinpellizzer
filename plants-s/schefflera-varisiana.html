Schefflera varisiana

Schefflera varisiana, commonly known as the Variegated Umbrella Plant, is a member of the Araliaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that are typically variegated with white and green patterns. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Schefflera varisiana is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
