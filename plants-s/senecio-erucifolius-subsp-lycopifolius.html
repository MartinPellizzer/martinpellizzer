Senecio erucifolius subsp. lycopifolius

Senecio erucifolius subsp.

lycopifolius, commonly known as the yellow starbursts, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that appear in dense, star-like clusters.

The plant has serrated, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in various regions with a Mediterranean climate.
