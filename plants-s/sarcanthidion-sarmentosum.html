Sarcanthidion sarmentosum

Sarcanthidion sarmentosum, commonly known as the creeping orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and purple. The plant is perennial and forms dense mats of foliage, with thin, wiry stems that root at the nodes. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical structure includes rhizomes and stolons, which help it spread and establish itself in suitable habitats.
