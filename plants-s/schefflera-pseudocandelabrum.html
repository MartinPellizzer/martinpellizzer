Schefflera pseudocandelabrum

Schefflera pseudocandelabrum, commonly known as the umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of large, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor decoration due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
