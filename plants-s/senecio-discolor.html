Senecio discolor

Senecio discolor, commonly known as the white ragwort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and silver-gray foliage, which are covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant is part of the Asteraceae family, commonly known as the daisy or sunflower family. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open areas such as grasslands and roadsides.

Senecio discolor is an annual or biennial herb that can reach heights of up to 60 cm.
