Sophora platycarpa

Sophora platycarpa, commonly known as the Chinese wisteria, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are pinnately arranged and have a distinctive blue-green color. The plant produces long, pendulous clusters of small, pale yellow flowers in the spring. Its fruit is a woody pod that splits open to release flat, winged seeds.

Sophora platycarpa is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its showy floral display.
