Streptocarpus hilsenbergii var. angustifolius

Streptocarpus hilsenbergii var. angustifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaf streptocarpus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of tubular, violet-blue flowers. The leaves are lance-shaped and have a waxy texture, which helps reduce water loss in its native arid environment. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This species is popular among succulent and bromeliad enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
