Setaria composita

Setaria composita, commonly known as yellow foxtail, is a flowering grass species native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and dense, spikelike inflorescences that produce small, golden-yellow seeds. The plant thrives in a variety of soil types and is often found in disturbed areas such as fields and roadsides. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue when young.

Setaria composita is an annual plant that plays a role in both ecological and agricultural systems.
