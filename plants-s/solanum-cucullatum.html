Solanum cucullatum

Solanum cucullatum, commonly known as the bellflower nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant is characterized by its distinctive bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple. Its leaves are typically oval and have a slightly hairy texture.

Solanum cucullatum is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
