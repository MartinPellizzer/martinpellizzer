Spiesia floribunda

Spiesia floribunda, commonly known as the yellow bellflower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Spiesia floribunda is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
