Scrophularia nectarifera

Scrophularia nectarifera, commonly known as the nectariferous scrophularia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae, which includes many species with showy flowers and medicinal properties. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of tubular, pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and slightly hairy, often with a reddish tinge.

Scrophularia nectarifera is known for its nectar-rich flowers, which attract various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
