Sobralia panamensis var. albiflos

Sobralia panamensis var. albiflos, commonly known as the Whiteflower Sobralia, is a bromeliad native to Panama.

It is characterized by its striking white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features broad, strap-like leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, forming a central cup that collects rainwater. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in tropical gardens and indoor plant collections.
