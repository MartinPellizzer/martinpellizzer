Sclarea verbenaca

Sclarea verbenaca, commonly known as sweet fennel, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features feathery, finely divided leaves that resemble those of dill, and it produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in umbels. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, licorice-scented seeds, which are often used in culinary and medicinal applications. Sclarea verbenaca thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and herbal cultivation.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and useful in traditional herbal medicine.
