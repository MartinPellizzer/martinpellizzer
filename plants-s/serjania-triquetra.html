Serjania triquetra

Serjania triquetra, commonly known as the three-cornered serjania, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Serjaniae and is characterized by its woody, shrub-like structure. Serjania triquetra is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in moist, well-drained soils.
