Stelis hoeijeri

Stelis hoeijeri, commonly known as the Orchid of the Clouds, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Stelis hoeijeri is prized in horticulture for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
