Stelis glanduligera

Stelis glanduligera, commonly known as the glandular stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is an epiphytic orchid, meaning it grows on the surface of other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, greenish-white flowers. It typically thrives in humid, tropical environments with high light exposure.

Its unique botanical features make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
