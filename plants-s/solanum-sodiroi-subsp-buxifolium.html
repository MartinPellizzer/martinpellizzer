Solanum sodiroi subsp. buxifolium

Solanum sodiroi subsp.

buxifolium, commonly known as the waxleaf nightshade, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America. It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by berries that mature to a deep purple color. It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
