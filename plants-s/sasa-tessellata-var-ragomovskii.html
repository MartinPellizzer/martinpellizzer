Sasa tessellata var. ragomovskii

Sasa tessellata var. ragomovskii, commonly known as the ragomovskii bamboo, is a clumping bamboo native to East Asia.

It features slender, culms that are typically green and covered with a whitish bloom. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with silver on the underside. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its elegant, upright growth habit.
