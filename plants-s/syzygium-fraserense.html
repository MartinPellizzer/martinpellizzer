Syzygium fraserense

Syzygium fraserense, commonly known as the Fraser's apple.

It is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Philippines, belonging to the Myrtaceae family. The plant is characterized by its glossy, oval-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant white flowers that bloom during the rainy season. Its fruit, resembling a small apple, is edible and has a sweet, aromatic flavor.

Syzygium fraserense is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
