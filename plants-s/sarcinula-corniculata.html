Sarcinula corniculata

Sarcinula corniculata, commonly known as the horned sea lettuce, is a marine green alga found in coastal areas.

It has a distinctive appearance with a broad, flat thallus that is often ribbed or ridged. The plant thrives in intertidal zones and is commonly found attached to rocks or other substrates. It plays a role in coastal ecosystems by providing habitat for small marine organisms.

Sarcinula corniculata is typically green in color and can vary in size depending on environmental conditions.
