Sapium poeppigii

Sapium poeppigii, commonly known as the Chinese soapberry tree, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and palmately lobed leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by orange-red, fleshy fruits that contain seeds.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
