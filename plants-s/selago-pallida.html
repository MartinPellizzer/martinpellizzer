Selago pallida

Selago pallida, commonly known as the pale selago, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with small, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of pale yellow to greenish-white flowers, which bloom in the spring. Selago pallida thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to dry, sunny environments.
