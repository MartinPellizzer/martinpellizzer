Schefflera lilacina

Schefflera lilacina, commonly known as the pink schefflera, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are arranged in a distinctive, umbrella-like pattern. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.

Schefflera lilacina is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
