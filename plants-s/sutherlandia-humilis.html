Sutherlandia humilis

Sutherlandia humilis, commonly known as the African smoke bush, is a low-growing shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its hairy, woody stems and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces small, round, brown seed pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in semi-arid regions.

Sutherlandia humilis is known for its medicinal uses in traditional African medicine.
