Sisymbrium patagonicum

Sisymbrium patagonicum, commonly known as Patagonian babbleweed, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It produces small, yellow flowers and has pinnately divided leaves.

The seeds are enclosed in pods that split open when mature, dispersing the seeds by wind.
