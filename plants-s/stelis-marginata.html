Stelis marginata

Stelis marginata, commonly known as the marginata orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically features dark green, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of pale yellow to white blooms. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Stelis marginata is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
