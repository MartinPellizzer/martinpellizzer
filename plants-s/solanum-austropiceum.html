Solanum austropiceum

Solanum austropiceum, commonly known as the Australian nightshade, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its small, white or pale blue flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically yellow or orange when ripe. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky soils.

Due to its toxic properties, it is important to handle the plant with care.
