Stachys mediterranea

Stachys mediterranea, commonly known as purple deadnettle, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features square stems covered in fine hairs and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a common sight in gardens and natural habitats.

Stachys mediterranea is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
