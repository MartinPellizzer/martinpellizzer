Stranvaesia bodinieri

Stranvaesia bodinieri, commonly known as the Bodinier's stranvaesia, is a rare and striking plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

The plant produces striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects.
