Stelis uberis

Stelis uberis, commonly known as the hairy stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically growing on trees or rocks. The plant features a short, thick rhizome and produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. Its leaves are often hairy, giving it its common name, and it is known for its compact, attractive appearance.

Stelis uberis is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
