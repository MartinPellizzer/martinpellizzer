Saxifraga aizoon sempervivum

Saxifraga aizoon sempervivum, commonly known as the stonecrop or houseleek, is a succulent plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features fleshy, upright stems and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is characterized by its ability to store water in its thick leaves, which helps it survive in arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.

Its hardy nature and low maintenance requirements make it a favored ornamental plant in gardens across temperate climates.
