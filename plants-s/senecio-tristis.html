Senecio tristis

Senecio tristis, commonly known as the weeping senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its distinctive, drooping inflorescences. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.

Senecio tristis is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
