Sitobolium obtusifolium

Sitobolium obtusifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf sitobolium, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are typically covered in a fine pubescence. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Its seeds are encased in a hard pod, which splits open upon maturity to release the seeds.
