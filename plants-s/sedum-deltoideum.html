Sedum deltoideum

Sedum deltoideum, commonly known as the diamond sedum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically blue-green in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact size and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
