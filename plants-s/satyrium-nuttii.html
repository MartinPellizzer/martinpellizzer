Satyrium nuttii

Satyrium nuttii, commonly known as the Nuttall's satyrium, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette.

The flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer, are often violet or blue in color and have a distinctive lip structure typical of orchids.
