Spathoglottis altigena

Spathoglottis altigena, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Spathoglottis altigena is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in many botanical gardens and private collections.
