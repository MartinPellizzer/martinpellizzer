Saxifraga umbrosa var. cuneata

Saxifraga umbrosa var. cuneata, commonly known as the shadow saxifrage, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in rocky or forested areas. The plant features small, dark green leaves that are often heart-shaped or lanceolate in shape. Its flowers are typically pink or white, appearing in clusters during the early summer months.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging growing conditions.
