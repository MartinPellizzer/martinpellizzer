Sisymbrium parra

Sisymbrium parra, commonly known as the common tansy, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a biennial or perennial herb that typically grows in disturbed soils and grasslands. The plant features pinnately compound leaves and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes. Its seeds are enclosed in long, slender pods that split open when mature.
