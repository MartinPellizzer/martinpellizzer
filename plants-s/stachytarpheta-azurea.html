Stachytarpheta azurea

Stachytarpheta azurea, commonly known as blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

It is characterized by its striking blue-violet bracts that surround small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.
