Sinosenecio handelianus

Sinosenecio handelianus, commonly known as the Hainan sineweed, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow to white. It is typically found in moist, forested areas and thrives in temperate climates.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
