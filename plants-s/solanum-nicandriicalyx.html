Solanum nicandriicalyx

Solanum nicandriicalyx, commonly known as the Nicandriicalyx nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and oval-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its fruits are berries that change color from green to red as they mature, and the plant is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it may have limited commercial use.
