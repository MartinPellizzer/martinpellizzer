Senecio Pinnatifolius Var. Lanceolatus

Senecio pinnatifolius var. lanceolatus is a striking succulent native to southern Africa. It features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that give it a unique and elegant appearance. This plant is known for its ability to store water, making it highly resilient in arid conditions. Its vibrant green foliage often turns reddish in full sun, adding to its visual appeal. It is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts for its low maintenance and ornamental value.
