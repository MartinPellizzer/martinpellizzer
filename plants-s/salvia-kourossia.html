Salvia kourossia

Salvia kourossia, commonly known as the Greek sage, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces spikes of violet to purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a strong, minty fragrance.

It is often used in traditional medicine and as an ornamental plant in gardens.
