Saussurea ceratocarpa

Saussurea ceratocarpa, commonly known as the horned saussurea, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas and parts of Central Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and fibrous roots. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky slopes at high altitudes. Its distinctive feature is the presence of prominent, horn-like structures on its stems.

It produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the summer months.
