Sideritis gouyouniana

Sideritis gouyouniana, commonly known as the Greek ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Greece.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of purple and blue. It thrives in rocky and dry habitats, often found in alpine and subalpine zones.

Sideritis gouyouniana is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
