Simaba floribunda

Simaba floribunda, commonly known as the Caribbean cherry or yellow mangosteen, is a small evergreen tree native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It produces small, yellow to orange fruits that are rich in vitamin C and have a tart flavor. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape with a smooth margin. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

The tree is valued for its edible fruits and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
