Sanguisorba sanguisorba var. microphylla

Sanguisorba sanguisorba var. microphylla, commonly known as the dwarf bloodwort, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe.

It features upright, branched stems that bear small, clustered inflorescences of pink to red flowers. The leaves are lance-shaped, with a serrated edge, and are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This plant is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
