Stelis pilosa

Stelis pilosa, commonly known as the hairy stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and hairy stems, which give it its common name. Native to Southeast Asia, this epiphytic orchid typically grows on trees or rocks in humid environments. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for collectors and enthusiasts of miniature orchids.
