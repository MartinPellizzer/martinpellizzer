Setaria stolzii

Setaria stolzii, commonly known as silvergrass, is a perennial grass native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It features narrow, silver-gray blades that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear minute, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it adaptable to various environments.

Setaria stolzii is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
