Stelis enormis

Stelis enormis, commonly known as the giant stelis orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 centimeters in length. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive lip that is often marked with purple or red.

Stelis enormis is prized in horticulture for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
