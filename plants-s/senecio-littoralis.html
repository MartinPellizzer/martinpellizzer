Senecio littoralis

Senecio littoralis, commonly known as sea lettuce, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant thrives in saline environments and is often found growing in rocky coastal areas or on tidal flats. Senecio littoralis is adapted to withstand harsh coastal conditions, including salt spray and strong winds.
