Smilax poilanei

Smilax poilanei, commonly known as the Madagascar arrowroot.

This climbing vine is native to Madagascar and features a woody stem with thorned nodes. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a waxy coating. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by bright red berries.

It is valued for its edible tubers, which are similar in texture to arrowroot.
