Spartium villosum

Spartium villosum, commonly known as hairy spartium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and nitrogen-fixing capabilities. The plant features small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its hairy stems and leaves are distinctive features that help it adapt to dry and rocky environments.

Spartium villosum is often found in open woodlands and along coastal areas where it thrives in well-drained soils.
