Senecio ludens

Senecio ludens, commonly known as the "Little Lion's Tail," is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features wiry, sparsely leafed stems and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Senecio ludens is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.

It is also used in traditional medicine in some regions for its purported health benefits.
