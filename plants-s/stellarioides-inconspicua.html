Stellarioides inconspicua

Stellarioides inconspicua, commonly known as the inconspicuous starwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It features a mat-forming growth habit with small, narrow leaves arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces tiny, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale green, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It is adapted to survive in harsh environments with limited water availability.

Its foliage and flowers are often overlooked due to its subtle appearance, making it a less conspicuous member of its ecological community.
