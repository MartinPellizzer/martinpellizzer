Stachys aspera f. glabrata

Stachys aspera f. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth hedge nettle, is a variant of the common hedge nettle.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are generally smooth to the touch, distinguishing it from the typical form. It produces small, pale purple flowers in whorled clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer.

This plant is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides, thriving in temperate climates.
