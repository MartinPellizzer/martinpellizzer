Senecio nandensis

Senecio nandensis, commonly known as the Nandens Mountain Groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, thriving in arid and semi-arid environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are small and yellowish.

Senecio nandensis is often found in mountainous areas, where it adapts to the harsh climatic conditions.
