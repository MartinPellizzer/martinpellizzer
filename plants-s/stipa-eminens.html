Stipa eminens

Stipa eminens, commonly known as the tall feathergrass, is a perennial grass native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and feathery, silver-green leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are often adorned with silky, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid climates.

Stipa eminens is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
