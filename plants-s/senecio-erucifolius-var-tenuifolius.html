Senecio erucifolius var. tenuifolius

Senecio erucifolius var. tenuifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant features slender, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces small yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

This species is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
