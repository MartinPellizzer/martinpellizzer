Sigesbeckia glabrescens

Sigesbeckia glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth sigesbeckia, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, branched stems and heart-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in the summer months. It is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.

Sigesbeckia glabrescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
