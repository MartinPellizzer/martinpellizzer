Silene krymensis

Silene krymensis, commonly known as the Krymskaya silene, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or alpine environments. The plant produces striking pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, adapted to withstand harsh mountain conditions.

Silene krymensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil.
