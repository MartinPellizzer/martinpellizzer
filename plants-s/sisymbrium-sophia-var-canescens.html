Sisymbrium sophia var. canescens

Sisymbrium sophia var. canescens, commonly known as hoary sissyphe, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbages, and is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and pinnate leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in grasslands and along roadsides.
