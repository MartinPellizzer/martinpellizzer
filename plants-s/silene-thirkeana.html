Silene thirkeana

Silene thirkeana, commonly known as the Thirkean campion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to red flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a hairy texture.

Silene thirkeana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
