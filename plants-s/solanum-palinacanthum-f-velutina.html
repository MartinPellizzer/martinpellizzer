Solanum palinacanthum f. velutina

Solanum palinacanthum f. velutina, commonly known as the velvet nightshade, is a variant of the nightshade family (Solanaceae) characterized by its dense, velvety pubescence.

It is a shrub-like plant with elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and covered in fine hairs. The flowers are usually white or pale purple, with five petals and a distinctive, bell-shaped corolla. The fruit is a globose berry that ripens to a deep red or black color, containing several small seeds.

This plant is native to tropical regions and is often found in humid, shaded environments.
