Stylidium scintillans

Stylidium scintillans, commonly known as the glittering sun-orchid, is a striking member of the orchid family native to Australia.

This small, herbaceous plant features vibrant, showy flowers with bright yellow and orange hues, often adorned with intricate patterns. Its unique structure includes a specialized lip that aids in pollination by attracting specific insect pollinators. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, typically found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its beauty and ecological significance, it is a subject of interest for both botanists and conservationists.
