Senecio doronicum

Senecio doronicum, commonly known as the Doronicum groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a raceme. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

The species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its attractive foliage and blooms.
