Senecio odontophyllus

Senecio odontophyllus, commonly known as the toothed leaf senecio, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its distinctive, toothed leaves. The plant typically grows as a succulent shrub, with hairy stems and small, yellowish flowers. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is often cultivated in rock gardens and xeriscapes.
