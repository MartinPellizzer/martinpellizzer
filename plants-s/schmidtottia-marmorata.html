Schmidtottia marmorata

Schmidtottia marmorata, commonly known as the marbled orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, marbled-patterned leaves and clusters of delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with intricate markings that enhance its ornamental value.

Schmidtottia marmorata is prized by horticulturists for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
