Schoenus subaxillaris

Schoenus subaxillaris, commonly known as the narrowleaf bulbous sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges, and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flower heads that are typically green or reddish in color, and it thrives in wet or marshy environments.
