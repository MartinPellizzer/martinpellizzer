Salacia lehmbachii var. lehmbachii

Salacia lehmbachii var. lehmbachii, commonly known as the lehmbachii variant of the salacia plant, is a species native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its climbing habit, with long, trailing stems that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a waxy texture.

This variant is valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
