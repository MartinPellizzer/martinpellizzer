Stephanotis floribunda

Stephanotis floribunda, commonly known as the sweet screwbean, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white, waxy flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in floral arrangements.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release its seeds.
