Solanum emulans

Solanum emulans, commonly known as the emerald morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other notable plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. It thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Solanum emulans is also known for its rapid growth and ability to climb using its tendrils.
