Sium cynapium

Sium cynapium, commonly known as bull's horn pepper, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in moist, calcareous soils. The plant features a tall, branching stem with alternate, heart-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, white or pale yellow, and arranged in racemes.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are used in traditional herbal medicine.
