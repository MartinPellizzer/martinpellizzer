Stachys artemisia

Stachys artemisia, commonly known as silverleaf hedge nettle, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-gray leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale purple flowers arranged in whorls along the stems. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in grasslands and woodland edges.

Stachys artemisia is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
