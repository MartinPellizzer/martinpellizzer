Senna sulfurea

Senna sulfurea, commonly known as golden senna, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and produces elongated pods that contain seeds. It is often found in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sunlight.

Senna sulfurea is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
