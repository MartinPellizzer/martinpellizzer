Stellaria diversiflora f. angustifolia

Stellaria diversiflora f. angustifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaved starwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and small, star-like white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It has a low-growing habit with stems that often lie close to the ground.

Its foliage and floral structure make it a distinctive member of the genus Stellaria.
