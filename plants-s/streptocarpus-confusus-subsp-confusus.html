Streptocarpus confusus subsp. confusus

Streptocarpus confusus subsp.

confusus, commonly known as the African violet, is a species native to eastern Africa. It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its rosette-forming leaves and showy, violet-colored flowers.

The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, black seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate light conditions. Streptocarpus confusus subsp.

confusus is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
