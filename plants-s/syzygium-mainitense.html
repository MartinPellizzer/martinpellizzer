Syzygium mainitense

Syzygium mainitense, commonly known as the Mindanao apple or white apple, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the Philippines, particularly found in the mountainous regions of Mindanao. The plant is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree, typically reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are lanceolate, glossy, and dark green, while its flowers are white and fragrant.

The fruit is a small, round, yellowish berry that is edible and has a sweet, acidic flavor.
