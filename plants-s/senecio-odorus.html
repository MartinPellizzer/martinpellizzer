Senecio odorus

Senecio odorus, commonly known as the fragrant senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and distinctive aromatic properties.

Senecio odorus thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and borders.
