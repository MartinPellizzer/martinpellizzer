Sericandra

Sericandra, commonly known as the moonseed or nightshade, is a genus of flowering plants in the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It consists of several species, most of which are native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and Africa. Sericandra species are often grown for their ornamental value due to their unique appearance and fragrance.
