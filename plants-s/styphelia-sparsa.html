Styphelia sparsa

Styphelia sparsa, commonly known as the sparse heath, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the coastal regions of western Australia.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its compact, moss-like foliage and small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink.
