Sideritis castellana

Sideritis castellana, commonly known as Castilian ironwort, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. It produces clusters of pale blue to purple flowers, which bloom in summer.

Sideritis castellana is often found in rocky or dry habitats and is valued for its medicinal properties.
