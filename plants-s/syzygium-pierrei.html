Syzygium pierrei

Syzygium pierrei, commonly known as the Kauai apple or Hawaiian apple, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the island of Kauai in Hawaii and is characterized by its small, red, apple-like fruits. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are elliptical, glossy, and dark green, while its flowers are white and fragrant.

Syzygium pierrei is considered a rare and endemic species, often found in moist, shaded habitats within the Hawaiian rainforests.
