Senecio cannabinifolius

Senecio cannabinifolius, commonly known as the Cannabis leaf senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, cannabis-like foliage. The plant features narrow, serrated leaves that resemble those of the Cannabis sativa plant, hence its common name. It produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, typical of the daisy family.

Senecio cannabinifolius is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
