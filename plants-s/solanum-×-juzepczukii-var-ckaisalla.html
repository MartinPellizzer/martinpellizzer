Solanum × juzepczukii var. ckaisalla

Solanum × juzepczukii var. ckaisalla, commonly known as the hybrid nightshade, is a botanical variant of the nightshade family (Solanaceae).

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its oval, hairy leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range from white to violet in color. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically yellow or orange when mature. It is native to South America and thrives in warm, humid climates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens, though it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
