Stenophalium gardneri

Stenophalium gardneri, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in a variety of colors, including purple, pink, and white. Its leaves are velvety and typically have a dark green color with a waxy texture.

Stenophalium gardneri is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
