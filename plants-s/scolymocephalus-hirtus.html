Scolymocephalus hirtus

Scolymocephalus hirtus, commonly known as the hairy scolymus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and is native to the Mediterranean region. The plant features narrow, hairy leaves and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in composite heads. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in horticulture.
