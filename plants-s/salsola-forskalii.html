Salsola forskalii

Salsola forskalii, commonly known as Russian thorn, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Amaranthaceae.

It is a halophyte, meaning it thrives in saline environments, and is native to Central Asia. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves and produces small, greenish flowers. It is often found in arid and semi-arid regions, growing on sandy or saline soils.

Salsola forskalii is adapted to withstand drought and high salt concentrations, making it a resilient species in harsh environments.
