Schefflera aruensis

Schefflera aruensis, commonly known as the Aru umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Maluku Islands of Indonesia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of large, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by round, red berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Schefflera aruensis is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor conditions.
