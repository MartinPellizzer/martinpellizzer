Stachys aspera var. chinensis

Stachys aspera var. chinensis, commonly known as Chinese betony, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and produces spikes of violet or blue flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
