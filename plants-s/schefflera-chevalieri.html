Schefflera chevalieri

Schefflera chevalieri, commonly known as the Madagascar schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of leaflets, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant has compound, pinnately arranged leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by bright red berries.

Schefflera chevalieri is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its adaptability and attractive foliage.
