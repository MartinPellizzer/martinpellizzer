Senecio sieheanus

Senecio sieheanus, commonly known as the Cape Mountain daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in mountainous regions and is valued for its ornamental beauty.

Senecio sieheanus is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
