Scaphochlamys oculata

Scaphochlamys oculata, commonly known as the eye-leaf orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, brightly colored leaves that are often marked with distinctive eye-like patterns, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically in shades of yellow and orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Its unique foliage and ornamental value make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
