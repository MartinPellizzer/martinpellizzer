Stellaria vestita

Stellaria vestita, commonly known as the white starwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, white star-shaped flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to July. The plant has slender, branching stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.

Stellaria vestita is often found in calcareous soils and is a common species in damp meadows and grasslands.
