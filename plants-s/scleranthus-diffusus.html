Scleranthus diffusus

Scleranthus diffusus, commonly known as the common sowthistle, is an annual herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in disturbed soils, such as roadsides and fields, and has a sprawling growth habit. The plant features alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small yellow flowers are clustered in dense heads, and it produces numerous seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Scleranthus diffusus is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
