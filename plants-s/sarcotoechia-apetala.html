Sarcotoechia apetala

Sarcotoechia apetala, commonly known as the leafy bladder nut, is a rare and unique plant species native to specific regions of South America.

The plant produces small, bladder-like fruits that contain seeds, a feature that sets it apart from many other members of the plant kingdom.
