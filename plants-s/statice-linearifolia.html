Statice linearifolia

Statice linearifolia, commonly known as sea lavender, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean and the eastern Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and small, showy purple or pink flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in saline and well-drained soils, often growing in dunes or along rocky shores. Its ability to tolerate salt spray and harsh coastal conditions makes it a hardy species.

Statice linearifolia is often used in floral arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms and ornamental value.
