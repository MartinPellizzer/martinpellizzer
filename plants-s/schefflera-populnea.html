Schefflera populnea

Schefflera populnea, commonly known as the Poplar Schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It features a dense, rounded crown with large, palmate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red, berry-like fruits that are edible for birds. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its adaptability makes it suitable for both indoor and outdoor gardening in suitable climates.
