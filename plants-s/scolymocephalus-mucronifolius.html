Scolymocephalus mucronifolius

Scolymocephalus mucronifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved scolymus, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of pale blue to violet flowers, which bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its botanical features make it an attractive addition to native plant collections.
