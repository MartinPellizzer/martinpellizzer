Sideritis × laderoi

Sideritis × laderoi, commonly known as the hybrid sainfoin, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. The plant features square, hairy stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its small, pale blue or purple flowers are clustered in whorls along the stems, typical of the genus Sideritis.

It is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
