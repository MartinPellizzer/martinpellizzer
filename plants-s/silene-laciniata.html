Silene laciniata

Silene laciniata, commonly known as the fringed pink, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, pinkish-purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has slender, branched stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly toothed edge. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and streambanks.

Silene laciniata is a perennial herb that plays a role in supporting local pollinators.
