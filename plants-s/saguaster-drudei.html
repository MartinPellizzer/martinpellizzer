Saguaster drudei

Saguaster drudei, commonly known as the desert morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant has slender, trailing stems and heart-shaped leaves that are adapted to conserve water in its harsh environment.
