Stellaria neglecta subsp. postii

Stellaria neglecta subsp.

postii, commonly known as the lesser starwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to parts of Europe and Asia. It typically features small, star-shaped white or pale blue flowers that bloom in early summer.

The plant has slender, branching stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a common sight in disturbed or semi-natural habitats.
