Stellaria diversiflora var. yakumontana

Stellaria diversiflora var. yakumontana, commonly known as the Yakumontana starwort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a low-growing herbaceous perennial with slender, branched stems that often lie close to the ground. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers arranged in clusters, giving it a star-like appearance. Its leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy surface.

It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, alpine environments.
