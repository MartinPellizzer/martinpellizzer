Solanum nigrum var. alatum

Solanum nigrum var. alatum, commonly known as the winged nightshade, is a variant of the common nightshade plant.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features distinctive winged leaves that give it its common name, and its flowers are usually yellow with five petals. The fruit is a small, fleshy berry that turns from green to black as it matures.

It is native to Europe, western Asia, and North Africa, and is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.
