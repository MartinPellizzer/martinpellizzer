Statice nebrodensis var. duriaei

Statice nebrodensis var. duriaei, commonly known as the Sicilian statice, is a flowering plant native to the island of Sicily.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, pink to purple flowers. The plant typically thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas. Its foliage is covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid conditions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in Mediterranean horticulture.
