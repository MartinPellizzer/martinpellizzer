Schlechterianthus Merrillii

Schlechterianthus merrillii is a rare and fascinating flowering plant native to the highlands of Tanzania. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, known for its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. This species is characterized by its striking yellow petals and compact growth habit. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts. Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
