Stemona cochinchinensis

Stemona cochinchinensis, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features a rhizomatous root system and lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale blue, arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional Chinese medicine.
