Sauropus asteranthos

Sauropus asteranthos, commonly known as the starflower or velvetleaf, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features palmately compound leaves with a velvety texture and a distinctive star-shaped arrangement of leaflets. The plant produces small, clustered white or pink flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for both its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
