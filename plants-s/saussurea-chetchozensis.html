Saussurea chetchozensis

Saussurea chetchozensis, commonly known as the Chetchozensis yarrow, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, branched stems and fluffy, white to pale yellow flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, thriving in cool and moist conditions. Due to its habitat and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species in its native range.
