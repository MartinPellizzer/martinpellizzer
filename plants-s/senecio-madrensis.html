Senecio madrensis

Senecio madrensis, commonly known as the Madrean skyflower, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and leafy stems. The plant typically grows in rocky, arid environments, showcasing its adaptation to dry conditions. Its yellow flowers bloom in the spring, adding a striking visual element to its habitat.

Senecio madrensis is also noted for its medicinal properties, traditionally used by Indigenous peoples for various health purposes.
