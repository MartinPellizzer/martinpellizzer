Sporobolus nebulosus

Sporobolus nebulosus, commonly known as the cloudgrass, is a perennial grass species native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and fine, bluish-green foliage. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in desert and arid environments.

Sporobolus nebulosus is adapted to withstand drought and high temperatures, making it a resilient component of its ecosystem.
