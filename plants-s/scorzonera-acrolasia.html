Scorzonera acrolasia

Scorzonera acrolasia, commonly known as the alpine scorzonera, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and basal rosette of leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers with ray florets, typically blooming in the summer months. Scorzonera acrolasia is adapted to cold and arid environments, often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes.

Its root system is robust, allowing it to survive in nutrient-poor soils.
