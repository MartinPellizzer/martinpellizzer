Sabina chinensis f. variegata

Sabina chinensis f. variegata, commonly known as the variegated Chinese juniper, is a compact evergreen shrub native to China.

It features small, scale-like leaves that are typically green with distinctive white or yellow variegation. The plant produces small, blue-gray berries that are ornamental and attract wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Its slow growth and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
