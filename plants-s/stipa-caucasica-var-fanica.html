Stipa caucasica var. fanica

Stipa caucasica var. fanica, commonly known as the Caucasian feather grass, is a perennial grass species native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, feathery plumes of flowers during the summer months. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening due to its graceful appearance.

The variety 'fanica' is distinguished by its more refined and compact growth habit compared to the typical form.
