Silene supina

Silene supina, commonly known as the bent silene, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of up to 20 centimeters. The plant features slender, ascending stems with small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette at the base.

Silene supina is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to full sun conditions.
