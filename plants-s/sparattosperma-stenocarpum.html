Sparattosperma stenocarpum

Sparattosperma stenocarpum, commonly known as the narrow-fruit spathiphyllum, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its broad, arrow-shaped leaves and a striking inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered around the spadix. It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia and thrives in humid, shaded environments.

The fruit is a small, capsule-like structure that contains several seeds.
