Salicornia helmutii

Salicornia helmutii, commonly known as sea beans, is a halophytic plant adapted to saline environments.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy stems that store water. The plant produces small, green flowers that are typically clustered in spikes. It is native to coastal regions and can thrive in salt marshes and sandy soils.

Salicornia helmutii plays a role in stabilizing coastal dunes and providing habitat for various small organisms.
