Stelis gemmulosa

Stelis gemmulosa, commonly known as the gemmulose orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in a rosette pattern.

The orchid's flowers are often pale green or yellowish with a distinctive, spurred lip.
