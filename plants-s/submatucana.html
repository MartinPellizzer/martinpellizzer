Submatucana

Submatucana, commonly known as the Brazilian nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically purple or violet in color. Its leaves are broad and lobed, and it produces small, berry-like fruits that are toxic to humans.
