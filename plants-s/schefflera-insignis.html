Schefflera insignis

Schefflera insignis, commonly known as the Hawaiian umbrella tree, is a tropical plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like arrangement of large, leathery leaves. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters in its natural habitat. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, with a distinctive veining pattern.

Schefflera insignis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
