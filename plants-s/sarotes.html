Sarotes

Sarotes, commonly known as the common thistle, is a perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple or pink flower heads that resemble thistles. The plant typically grows in dry, disturbed soils and is found in various regions across Europe and parts of Asia. Sarotes has a robust root system and can thrive in a range of environmental conditions.

It plays a role in supporting pollinators and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
