Solanum vanheurckii

Solanum vanheurckii, commonly known as the Vanheurck's nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant typically grows as an erect shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, with simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate or elliptic in shape. Its flowers are usually small, clustered in umbels, and can be white, pink, or purple in color.

The fruit is a globose berry that changes color from green to yellow or red as it matures.
