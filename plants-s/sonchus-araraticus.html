Sonchus araraticus

Sonchus araraticus, commonly known as Armenian sowthistle, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is a herbaceous annual or biennial plant native to the temperate regions of Europe, Asia, and North Africa. The plant features alternate, rough-hairy leaves that are typically lobed or deeply toothed. It produces yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, which are surrounded by bracts.

The species is often found in disturbed soils and can be invasive in certain regions.
