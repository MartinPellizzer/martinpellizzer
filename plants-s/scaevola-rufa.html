Scaevola rufa

Scaevola rufa, commonly known as the red-flowered fanflower, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its vibrant pink to red flowers that bloom in dense, fan-shaped clusters. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. Scaevola rufa thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens.
