Sideritis cabrerae

Sideritis cabrerae, commonly known as Cape Verde ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Cape Verde Islands.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically pale blue to violet, clustered in whorls along the stem.

Sideritis cabrerae is adapted to arid environments and is often found growing in rocky or sandy soils.
