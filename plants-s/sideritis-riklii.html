Sideritis riklii

Sideritis riklii, commonly known as the Balkan ironwort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its slender stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. It thrives in rocky and well-drained soils, often growing in alpine and subalpine habitats.

Sideritis riklii is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
