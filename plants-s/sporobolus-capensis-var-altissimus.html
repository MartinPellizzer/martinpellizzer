Sporobolus capensis var. altissimus

Sporobolus capensis var. altissimus, commonly known as the tall speargrass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers, typical of the Poaceae family. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and coastal dunes.

This variant is notable for its height and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
