Sansevieria raffillii var. glauca

Sansevieria raffillii var. glauca, commonly known as the Blue Glow Snake Plant, is a strikingly decorative foliage plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, upright leaves that are typically a vibrant blue-green color, with a waxy texture and a subtle sheen. The plant is known for its low maintenance requirements, thriving in low light conditions and requiring minimal watering. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and as a statement plant in modern interiors.

The leaves are also capable of producing small, inconspicuous white flowers under optimal growing conditions.
