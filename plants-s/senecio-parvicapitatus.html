Senecio parvicapitatus

Senecio parvicapitatus, commonly known as the small-headed groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial with small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Senecio parvicapitatus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
