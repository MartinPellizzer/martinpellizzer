Splitgerbera macrostachya

Splitgerbera macrostachya, commonly known as the giant gerbera, is a striking member of the Asteraceae family native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers with a distinctive red center and broad, dark green foliage. The plant produces numerous stems that can grow up to several meters in height, making it a dramatic addition to gardens. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, requiring regular watering during its active growing season.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in tropical and subtropical regions.
