Solidago anticostensis

Solidago anticostensis, commonly known as Anticosti goldenrod, is a species of flowering plant in the sunflower family.

It is native to the island of Anticosti in the Gulf of Saint Lawrence, Canada. This perennial herb features erect, branched stems covered in fine hairs and produces numerous small, yellow flowers in late summer. The plant thrives in moist, sandy soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Its ecological importance includes providing nectar for pollinators and contributing to the biodiversity of its native region.
