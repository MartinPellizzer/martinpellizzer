Schnella platycalyx

Schnella platycalyx, commonly known as the broad-calyx morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in the early morning. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its rapid growth rate and ornamental value make it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
