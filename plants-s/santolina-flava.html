Santolina flava

Santolina flava, commonly known as golden santolina, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features silvery-gray foliage with bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has a compact, bushy habit and is often used in rock gardens or as a border plant due to its ornamental value. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, requiring minimal water once established.

Santolina flava is also valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in herbal remedies.
