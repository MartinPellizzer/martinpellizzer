Senecio peteroanus

Senecio peteroanus, commonly known as the Peter's groundsels, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Senecio peteroanus is also noted for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
