Securinega melanthesoides var. aridicola

Securinega melanthesoides var. aridicola, commonly known as the desert securinega, is a succulent shrub native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It features small, spiny leaves that help reduce water loss in its harsh environment. The plant produces yellow flowers during the rainy season, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its thick, fleshy stems store water, enabling it to survive prolonged droughts.

It is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to high temperatures and low rainfall.
