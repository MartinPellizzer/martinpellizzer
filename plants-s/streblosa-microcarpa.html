Streblosa microcarpa

Streblosa microcarpa, commonly known as the small-fruited streblosa, is a member of the Rhamnaceae family.

It is a shrub native to southern Africa, particularly found in regions with sandy soils. The plant features small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the same plant.

The fruit is a small, fleshy drupe that ripens to a red color, attracting various bird species.
