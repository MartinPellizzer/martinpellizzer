Senecio clivicola var. pampae

Senecio clivicola var. pampae, commonly known as the Pampas senecio, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly found in the grasslands of Argentina and Uruguay.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. This variant is distinguished by its slender, upright stems and small, yellowish to greenish flowers that bloom in clusters.
