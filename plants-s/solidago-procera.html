Solidago procera

Solidago procera, commonly known as the tall goldenrod, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant produces bright yellow inflorescences composed of numerous small, clustered flowers. Solidago procera thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in open fields and along roadsides.

It plays an important role in supporting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
