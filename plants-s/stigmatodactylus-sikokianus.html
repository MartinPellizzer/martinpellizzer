Stigmatodactylus sikokianus

Stigmatodactylus sikokianus, commonly known as the KwaZulu-Natal starfish, is a unique plant species native to the coastal regions of South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped leaves and fleshy, succulent stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, adding to its ornamental appeal. Its leaves are covered in a waxy cuticle, which helps reduce water loss and protect against intense sunlight.

Stigmatodactylus sikokianus is often cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements.
