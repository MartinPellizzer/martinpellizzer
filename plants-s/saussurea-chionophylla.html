Saussurea chionophylla

Saussurea chionophylla, commonly known as the snow-leaf saussurea, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

Saussurea chionophylla is often found in rocky soils and is an important component of alpine ecosystems.
