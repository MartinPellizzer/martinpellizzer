Senecio ramsbottomii

Senecio ramsbottomii, commonly known as the ram's bottom groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant typically grows in arid and rocky habitats, showcasing adaptations to drought and poor soil conditions. It features narrow, blue-green leaves and produces small yellow flowers that bloom in the spring.
