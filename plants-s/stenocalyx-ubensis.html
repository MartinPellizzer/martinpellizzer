Stenocalyx ubensis

Stenocalyx ubensis, commonly known as the African snowberry, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid regions. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by bright red or orange berries. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a slightly serrated edge.

Stenocalyx ubensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and fruit.
