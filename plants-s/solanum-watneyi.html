Solanum watneyi

Solanum watneyi, commonly known as Watney's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually broad and ovate, with a slightly hairy texture.

Solanum watneyi is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its attractive blooms.
