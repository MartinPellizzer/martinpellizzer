Stanhopea eburnea var. spectabilis

Stanhopea eburnea var. spectabilis, commonly known as the Spectabilis Stanhopea, is a striking orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, branched inflorescence and large, showy flowers with a pale pink to white coloration. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth. Its foliage consists of broad, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
