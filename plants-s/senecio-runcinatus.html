Senecio runcinatus

Senecio runcinatus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.
