Selago setulosa

Selago setulosa, commonly known as the hairy selago, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and small, hairy leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy habitats.
