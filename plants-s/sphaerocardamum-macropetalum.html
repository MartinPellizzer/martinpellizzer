Sphaerocardamum macropetalum

Sphaerocardamum macropetalum, commonly known as the giant petal orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy, fragrant flowers that can reach up to 15 centimeters in diameter. The plant features a short, stocky stem with a single, fleshy pseudobulb that supports its floral display. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy texture, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is highly sought after by horticulturists and conservationists alike.
