Syzygium vrieseanum

Syzygium vrieseanum, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery red flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has large, glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. Its fruit is a woody capsule that contains several seeds.

Syzygium vrieseanum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in landscaping in warm climates.
