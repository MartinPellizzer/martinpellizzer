Scrophularia sinensis

Scrophularia sinensis, commonly known as Chinese toadflax, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in clusters at the ends of the branches. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or rocky habitats.

Scrophularia sinensis is valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
