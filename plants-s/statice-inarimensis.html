Statice inarimensis

Statice inarimensis, commonly known as the Inarim statice, is a flowering plant native to the island of Inarim in the Pacific.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, which bloom throughout the summer months. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue that adds visual contrast to its floral display.

Statice inarimensis is adapted to coastal environments and thrives in well-drained, sandy soils.
