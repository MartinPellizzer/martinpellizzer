Solanum demissum

Solanum demissum, commonly known as the velvetleaf nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its broad, velvety leaves that are typically dark green with a pubescent surface. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into rounded, fleshy berries containing numerous seeds. Solanum demissum belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae), which includes other economically important plants such as tomatoes and potatoes.

It is often found in disturbed habitats and can be invasive in certain regions.
