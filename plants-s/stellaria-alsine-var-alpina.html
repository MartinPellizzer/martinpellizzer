Stellaria alsine var. alpina

Stellaria alsine var. alpina, commonly known as alpine starwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in the spring and summer. The plant has slender, branching stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained, rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes.

Stellaria alsine var. alpina is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in harsh environments.
