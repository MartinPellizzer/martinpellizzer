Scabiosa odontostemma

Scabiosa odontostemma, commonly known as the toothed scabious, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, pink or purple flowers that bloom in clusters, typically from late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Scabiosa odontostemma is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
