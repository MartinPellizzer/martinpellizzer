Specklinia peduncularis

Specklinia peduncularis, commonly known as the slender-stemmed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a short, creeping rhizome and produces delicate, greenish-white flowers arranged in a raceme. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are elongated and often have a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

Specklinia peduncularis is valued in horticulture for its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
