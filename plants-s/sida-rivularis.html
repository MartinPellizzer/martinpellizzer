Sida rivularis

Sida rivularis, commonly known as river sida, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a herbaceous annual or perennial plant that typically grows in wet or moist environments. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate or lanceolate in shape. Its small, white or pale pink flowers are clustered in loose inflorescences.

Sida rivularis is often found near water sources such as rivers, streams, and wetlands.
