Sphaerocionium productum

Sphaerocionium productum, commonly known as the yellow-flowered sphaerocionium, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous shrub that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in fine hairs.

Sphaerocionium productum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
