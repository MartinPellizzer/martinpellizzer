Setaria tenuiseta

Setaria tenuiseta, commonly known as the narrowset panicgrass, is a species of grass native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous tiny florets. It is adapted to survive in drought conditions, with deep roots and a low-growing habit.

Setaria tenuiseta is often found in disturbed soils and is used in some regions for forage or soil stabilization.
