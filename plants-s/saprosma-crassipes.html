Saprosma crassipes

Saprosma crassipes, commonly known as the broad-leaved saprosma, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub that typically grows in humid, lowland areas. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and a glossy surface. Its flowers are small, clustered in inflorescences, and usually have a yellowish or greenish hue.

The fruit is a fleshy, berry-like structure that contains several seeds.
