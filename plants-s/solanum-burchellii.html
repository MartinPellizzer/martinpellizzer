Solanum burchellii

Solanum burchellii, commonly known as the African nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its spiny stems and clusters of small, white or purple flowers. The plant produces berries that change color from green to yellow and then to red as they mature. It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions.

Solanum burchellii is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
