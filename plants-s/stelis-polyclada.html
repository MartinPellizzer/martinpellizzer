Stelis polyclada

Stelis polyclada, commonly known as the clustered stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its compact growth habit and clustered pseudobulbs. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is native to Southeast Asia.

Stelis polyclada is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
