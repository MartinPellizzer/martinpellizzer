Somalia bottae

Somalia bottae, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette of velvety, waxy leaves and clusters of small, showy flowers. The plant prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.

Somalia bottae is known for its ability to bloom throughout the year under proper conditions.
