Stella

Stella, commonly known as the starflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the genus *Leucanthemum* in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with white petals surrounding a yellow center, resembling a star. The plant typically grows in temperate regions and thrives in well-drained soil. Stella is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Its blooming period occurs in late spring to early summer, adding a delicate beauty to the environment.
