Salacia germainii var. cordata

Salacia germainii var. cordata, commonly known as the cordate salacia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Salicaceae.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in catkin-like inflorescences. It is native to certain regions of Southeast Asia, particularly in countries like Thailand and Vietnam.

This species is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forest understories.
