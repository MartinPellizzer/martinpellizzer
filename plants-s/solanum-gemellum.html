Solanum gemellum

Solanum gemellum, commonly known as the gemellum nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and peppers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are ovate with a hairy texture, and it produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into berries.

The fruit is edible but contains toxic compounds, making it important to handle with care.
