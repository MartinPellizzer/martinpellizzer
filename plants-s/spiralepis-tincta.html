Spiralepis tincta

Spiralepis tincta, commonly known as the pink spiral orchid, is a strikingly beautiful orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its intricate, spiral-shaped inflorescence that gives it its common name. The plant features vibrant pink and white flowers that bloom in a unique, curling pattern. Spiralepis tincta thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and specialized collections.
