Senecio dichotomus

Senecio dichotomus, commonly known as the two-branched groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant has a sprawling growth habit with hairy stems and small, alternate leaves. Its yellow flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom during the cooler months.

It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
