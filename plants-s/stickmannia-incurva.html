Stickmannia incurva

Stickmannia incurva, commonly known as the curling stickman plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that curl and twist as they grow, creating a unique, whimsical appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its distinctive morphology has made it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and succulent collections.
