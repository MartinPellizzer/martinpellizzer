Satyrium breve

Satyrium breve, commonly known as the short-tailed orchid, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and pine flatwoods. The plant has a slender, erect stem with a single, narrow leaf and produces a spike of small, greenish-white flowers. Its flowers are usually less than 1 inch in length and have a distinctive, short spur.

Satyrium breve is a rare and endangered species, requiring specific habitat conditions for successful growth and reproduction.
