Spiraea koreana

Spiraea koreana, commonly known as Korean weeping spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to Korea.

It features graceful, arching branches that bear clusters of pinkish-white flowers in early summer. The plant has small, ovate leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun.

Spiraea koreana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its elegant appearance.
