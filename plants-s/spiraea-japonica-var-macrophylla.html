Spiraea japonica var. macrophylla

Spiraea japonica var. macrophylla, commonly known as Japanese spirea with large leaves, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features showy, flat-topped clusters of white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has elongated, oval leaves that are typically larger than those of the standard Spiraea japonica. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
