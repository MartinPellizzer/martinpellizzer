Soda foliosa

Soda foliosa, commonly known as the leafy soda plant, is a succulent species native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, enabling it to thrive in dry conditions. The plant produces small, white flowers during the growing season, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. Its root system is deep and robust, allowing it to access moisture from lower soil layers.

Soda foliosa is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ability to withstand extreme temperatures.
