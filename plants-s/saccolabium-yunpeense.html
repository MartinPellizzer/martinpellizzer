Saccolabium yunpeense

Saccolabium yunpeense, commonly known as the Yunpeen thornveld.

It is a shrub native to southern Africa, characterized by its woody stems and small, elliptical leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in harsh climates.

Its root system helps prevent soil erosion, contributing to its ecological importance in its native habitat.
