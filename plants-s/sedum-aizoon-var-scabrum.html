Sedum aizoon var. scabrum

Sedum aizoon var. scabrum, commonly known as stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to arid and rocky environments.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, clustered leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange, which bloom in the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ability to store water in its leaves make it a hardy and low-maintenance choice for gardeners.
