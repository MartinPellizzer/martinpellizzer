Shuttleworthia tenera

Shuttleworthia tenera, commonly known as the slender shuttleworthia, is a small, herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring or early summer. It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and along rocky outcrops.

Shuttleworthia tenera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
