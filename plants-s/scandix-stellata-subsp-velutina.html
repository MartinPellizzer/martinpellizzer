Scandix stellata subsp. velutina

Scandix stellata subsp.

velutina, commonly known as the velvet star, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It features a distinctive star-shaped inflorescence with violet-blue petals and yellow stamens.

The plant has hairy, velvety foliage that gives it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or dry habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
