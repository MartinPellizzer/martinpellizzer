Sedum jiuhuashanense

Sedum jiuhuashanense, commonly known as the Jiuhuashan stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to China.

It is characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow to orange. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are typically thick and waxy, adapted to store water and withstand arid conditions.

Sedum jiuhuashanense is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and aesthetic appeal.
