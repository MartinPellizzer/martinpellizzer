Senecio taliensis

Senecio taliensis, commonly known as the Taliensis groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Senecio taliensis is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and along riverbanks.
