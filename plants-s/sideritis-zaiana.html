Sideritis zaiana

Sideritis zaiana, commonly known as Greek heather, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Greece.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in high-altitude environments.

Sideritis zaiana is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Greek herbal remedies.
