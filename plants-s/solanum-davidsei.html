Solanum davidsei

Solanum davidsei, commonly known as David's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and peppers. The plant typically grows as a shrub with oval, hairy leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers. Its fruits are berries that start green and turn red when mature.

The species is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
