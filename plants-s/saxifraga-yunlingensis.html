Saxifraga yunlingensis

Saxifraga yunlingensis, commonly known as the Yunling saxifrage, is a flowering plant species native to China.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and clusters of small, white or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine or subalpine regions, often in rocky or well-drained soils. It is known for its ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions, making it a hardy species.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic plant.
