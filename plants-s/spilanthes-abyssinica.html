Spilanthes abyssinica

Spilanthes abyssinica, commonly known as Abyssinian toothache plant, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the genus Spilanthes within the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers. The plant is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and hairy leaves. It grows in arid and semi-arid regions, often in rocky soils.

The plant has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, particularly for treating toothaches and other oral ailments.
