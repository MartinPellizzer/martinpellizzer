Scaligeria rotundifolia

Scaligeria rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leaf scaligeria, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, round leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or calcareous habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
