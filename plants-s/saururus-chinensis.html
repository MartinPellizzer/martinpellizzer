Saururus chinensis

Saururus chinensis, commonly known as Chinese cress or fish腥草, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, fern-like leaves and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wetlands.

Saururus chinensis is known for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
