Stipa przewalskyi

Stipa przewalskyi, commonly known as the Przewalski's feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to the steppes of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and dense, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous narrow, awned florets. It thrives in dry, open habitats and is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.

Stipa przewalskyi plays an important role in stabilizing soils and supporting local wildlife in its native range.
