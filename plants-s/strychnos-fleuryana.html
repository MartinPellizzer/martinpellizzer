Strychnos fleuryana

Strychnos fleuryana, commonly known as the Fleury's bitter apple, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Loganiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers.
