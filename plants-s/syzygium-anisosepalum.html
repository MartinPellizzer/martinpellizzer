Syzygium anisosepalum

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white or pink flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a smooth texture with a glossy surface.
