Scutellaria deltoidea

Scutellaria deltoidea, commonly known as delta skullcap, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Scutellaria deltoidea typically grows in moist, wooded areas and has a compact growth habit.

It is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine, though its medicinal properties are not well-documented.
