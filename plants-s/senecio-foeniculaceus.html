Senecio foeniculaceus

Senecio foeniculaceus, commonly known as the African Daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other composite flowers. The plant features showy yellow flowers with white ray florets, arranged in a composite head that resembles a daisy. It is typically found in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in full sun and well-drained soil.

Senecio foeniculaceus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
