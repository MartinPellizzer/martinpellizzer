Sphaerolobium macranthum var. pulchellum

Sphaerolobium macranthum var. pulchellum, commonly known as the pink burr sweetpea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its leguminous properties and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant features compound leaves and produces distinctive, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well-suited to dry, well-drained soils.

This variety is known for its resilience and ability to thrive in arid environments.
