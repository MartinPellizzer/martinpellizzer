Schilleria populifolia

Schilleria populifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf schilleria, is a unique plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its broad, flat leaves that resemble those of a poplar tree, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. Schilleria populifolia thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical or subtropical regions.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
