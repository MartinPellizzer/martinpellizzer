Salicornia pacifica var. utahensis

Salicornia pacifica var. utahensis, commonly known as Utah sea blush, is a halophytic plant adapted to saline environments.

It is native to the western United States, particularly in Utah and surrounding regions. The plant features succulent, cylindrical stems that are often pink or red in color. It produces small, green flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months.

Utah sea blush is often found in coastal or inland salt marshes and is valued for its ornamental and ecological benefits.
