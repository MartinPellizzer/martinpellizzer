Stomatium integrum

Stomatium integrum, commonly known as the complete-stoma plant, is a rare species characterized by its unique stomatal structure.

Its leaves feature fully developed stomata that are evenly distributed across the surface, allowing for efficient gas exchange. The plant's botanical classification places it within the family Stomatiaceae, reflecting its specialized features. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, Stomatium integrum is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
