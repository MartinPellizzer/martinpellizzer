Spilanthes palustris

Spilanthes palustris, commonly known as swamp bladderpod, is a flowering plant native to wetland environments in North America.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cress plants. The plant typically grows in marshy areas and is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and pinnately divided leaves. Its seeds are enclosed in inflated, bladder-like pods, giving it its common name.
