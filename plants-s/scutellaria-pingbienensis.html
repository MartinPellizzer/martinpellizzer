Scutellaria pingbienensis

Scutellaria pingbienensis, commonly known as the Pingbian skullcap, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is known for its aromatic plants. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small, tubular, and usually blue or purple in color, attracting various pollinators.

Scutellaria pingbienensis is valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
