Sponia integerrima

Sponia integerrima, commonly known as the rough sponia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun exposure, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Sponia integerrima is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
