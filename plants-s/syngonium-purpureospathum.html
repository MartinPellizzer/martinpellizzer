Syngonium purpureospathum

Syngonium purpureospathum, commonly known as the purple-leaf arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that often display vibrant shades of purple, pink, and red, especially when exposed to bright light. The plant has a trailing growth habit, making it popular as a houseplant or for hanging baskets. Its stems are slender and can produce aerial roots, which help it climb or trail.

Syngonium purpureospathum thrives in humid environments with indirect sunlight and well-draining soil.
