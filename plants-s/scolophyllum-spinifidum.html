Scolophyllum spinifidum

Scolophyllum spinifidum, commonly known as the spiny sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in coastal areas and saline soils. The plant features a rosette of spiny, fleshy leaves and produces yellow flowers in late summer. Its unique morphology allows it to thrive in harsh, salty environments.

Scolophyllum spinifidum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in coastal ecosystems.
