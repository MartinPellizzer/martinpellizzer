Symplocos prunifolia f. uiae

Symplocos prunifolia f. uiae, commonly known as the dwarf evergreen holly, is a compact shrub native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It features small, glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers in the spring, which are followed by bright red berries in the fall. Its dense growth habit makes it suitable for use as a hedge or ornamental plant in gardens.

The species is valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
