Stelis perpusilliflora

Stelis perpusilliflora, commonly known as the minute-flowered stelis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in the Philippines and Borneo. The plant has slender, wiry stems and produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its leaves are narrow and arranged in a rosette pattern, often covered in a fine, velvety coating.

Stelis perpusilliflora thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.
