Silene Syngei

Silene syngei is a rare and striking plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Tibetan Plateau. Known for its vivid pink to purple flowers, it blooms during the short summer season in its harsh environment. This species is highly adapted to extreme conditions, including low oxygen levels and intense sunlight. Due to its limited distribution and habitat threats, Silene syngei is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique and beautiful flora.
