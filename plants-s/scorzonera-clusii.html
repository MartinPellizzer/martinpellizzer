Scorzonera clusii

Scorzonera clusii, commonly known as the Clusii scorzonera, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of dark green, hairy leaves. The plant produces striking yellow-orange flowers with a distinctive central disk, blooming from late spring to early summer. It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a garden plant.
