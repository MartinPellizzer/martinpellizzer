Solanum florulentum

Solanum florulentum, commonly known as the showy nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of purple and blue. The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that are typically yellow or orange when ripe. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in shaded or semi-shaded areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses for its vibrant floral display.
