Senecio limumae

Senecio limumae, commonly known as the Limumae groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, attracting various pollinators.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
