Senecio aetfatensis

Senecio aetfatensis, commonly known as the Cape St. Francis daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and has adapted to sandy soils and high salinity. It produces small, oval-shaped leaves and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Senecio aetfatensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in challenging environments.
