Salsola acocksii

Salsola acocksii, commonly known as the Namib Desert goosefoot, is a flowering plant native to the Namib Desert in Namibia.

It is a low-growing shrub that thrives in arid and saline environments, showcasing adaptations to extreme desert conditions. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are usually small, fleshy, and succulent, helping to store water and reduce transpiration.
