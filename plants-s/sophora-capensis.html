Sophora capensis

Sophora capensis, commonly known as the flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking orange-red flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has compound leaves that are pinnately arranged and often have a velvety texture. It produces small, woody pods that contain several seeds.

Sophora capensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often planted in gardens and public spaces.
