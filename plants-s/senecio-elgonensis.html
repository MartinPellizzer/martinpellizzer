Senecio elgonensis

Senecio elgonensis, commonly known as Elgon groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of East Africa, particularly around Mount Elgon.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant typically grows in rocky or open habitats, thriving in cool, moist environments. It features narrow, linear leaves and produces small yellow flowers arranged in clusters.

Due to its habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endemic species with limited distribution.
