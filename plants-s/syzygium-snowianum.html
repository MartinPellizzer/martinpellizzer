Syzygium snowianum

Syzygium snowianum, commonly known as the Snow's Syzygium, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically red or yellow when mature. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value and limited natural range, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
