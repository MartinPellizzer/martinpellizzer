Sophora pachycarpa

Sophora pachycarpa, commonly known as the broad-leaved Sophora, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces large, woody pods that contain edible seeds, which are often used in traditional Chinese cuisine.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas or on rocky slopes.
