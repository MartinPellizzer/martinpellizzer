Struthanthus maricensis

Struthanthus maricensis, commonly known as the Mariana starflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mariana Islands.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in coastal and limestone habitats. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, blooming during the dry season. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, often covered in fine hairs.

Struthanthus maricensis is adapted to the unique environmental conditions of its island habitat, making it an important part of the local flora.
