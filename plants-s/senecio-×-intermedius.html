Senecio × intermedius

Senecio × intermedius, commonly known as the hybrid groundsels, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaves. The plant typically grows as a shrub or subshrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are usually gray-green with a fuzzy texture, and it produces yellow flowers in clusters.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its attractive foliage and flowers.
