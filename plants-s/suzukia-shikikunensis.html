Suzukia shikikunensis

Suzukia shikikunensis, commonly known as the Japanese water lily, is a aquatic plant native to Japan.

It is characterized by its broad, floating leaves and showy white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments. Its leaves are often covered in a waxy cuticle to prevent water loss.

Suzukia shikikunensis plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
