Spartium molle

Spartium molle, commonly known as soft spartia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a soft, velvety texture.

Spartium molle is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
