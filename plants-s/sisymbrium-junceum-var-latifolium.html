Sisymbrium junceum var. latifolium

Sisymbrium junceum var. latifolium, commonly known as broadleaf hoary vetch, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with small, cross-shaped flowers. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. Its leaves are alternate and pinnately divided, often with a hairy texture.
