Schefflera williamsii

Schefflera williamsii, commonly known as the silver schefflera, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palm-like leaves that are glossy and typically have a silvery or white midrib. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.

Its adaptability and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor landscaping.
