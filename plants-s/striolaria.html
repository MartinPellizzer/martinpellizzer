Striolaria

Striolaria, commonly known as the star sapphire, is a striking flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant blue or purple flowers that resemble stars. The plant features strap-like, narrow leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. Striolaria thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
