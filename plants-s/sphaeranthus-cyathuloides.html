Sphaeranthus cyathuloides

Sphaeranthus cyathuloides, commonly known as the globe thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spherical, spiny seed heads that resemble small globes, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are small and usually pink or purple, blooming in the summer months.

Sphaeranthus cyathuloides is often used in ornamental gardening due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
