Sobralia rupicola

Sobralia rupicola, commonly known as the rock sobralia, is a striking bromeliad native to the cloud forests of Costa Rica and Panama.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange to red bracts that surround the inflorescence, making it a visually captivating plant. The species thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found growing on rocky outcrops or in disturbed areas. Its foliage consists of long, narrow leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, aiding in water retention.

Sobralia rupicola is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
