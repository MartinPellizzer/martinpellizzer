Solena humboldtiana

Solena humboldtiana, commonly known as the Humboldt's solena, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils. Its flowers are yellow and bloom during the warmer months.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive appearance.
