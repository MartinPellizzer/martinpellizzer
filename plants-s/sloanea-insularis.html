Sloanea insularis

Sloanea insularis, commonly known as the island sloanea, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Caribbean islands.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Sloanea insularis is typically found in moist, shaded habitats such as forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the island's endemic flora.
