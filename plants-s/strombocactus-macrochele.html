Strombocactus macrochele

Strombocactus macrochele, commonly known as the giant horn cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its large, ribbed stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces striking yellow flowers that bloom during the summer months. Its thick, fleshy stems are adapted to store water, allowing it to survive in harsh, dry environments.

Strombocactus macrochele is a slow-growing cactus with a unique, horn-like appearance that makes it a striking addition to arid landscapes.
