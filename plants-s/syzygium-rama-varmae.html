Syzygium rama-varmae

Syzygium rama-varmae, commonly known as the Rama Varma's clove.

This species is a member of the Myrtaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of India. It is characterized by its aromatic, reddish-brown flower buds that are used in traditional medicine and as a spice. The plant typically grows as a small tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a smooth, glossy surface, while its flowers are clustered in inflorescences.
