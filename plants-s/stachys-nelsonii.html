Stachys nelsonii

Stachys nelsonii, commonly known as Nelson's hedge nettle, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the mint family (Lamiaceae) and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in rocky or dry soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Stachys nelsonii is generally herbaceous, with a low-growing habit and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
