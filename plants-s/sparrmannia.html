Sparrmannia

Sparrmannia, commonly known as the silverweed, is a perennial plant belonging to the rose family (Rosaceae).

It is characterized by its silvery, fuzzy leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Sparrmannia is typically found in moist, sandy soils in temperate regions.

It is often used in herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
