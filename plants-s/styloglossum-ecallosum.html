Styloglossum ecallosum

Styloglossum ecallosum, commonly known as the slender tailed orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its slender, trailing stems and small, greenish-white flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Styloglossum ecallosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
