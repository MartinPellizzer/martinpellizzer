Swertia asarifolia

Swertia asarifolia, commonly known as the Himalayan gentian, is a perennial herb native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, thriving in cool and moist conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered with fine hairs, while its stems are slender and upright.

Swertia asarifolia is valued for its ornamental beauty and has traditional medicinal uses in some regions.
