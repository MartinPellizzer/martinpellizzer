Sorghum arduinoi

Sorghum arduinoi, commonly known as the dwarf sorghum, is a species of grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows to a height of less than one meter, making it suitable for ornamental use in gardens. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Sorghum arduinoi is adapted to arid and semi-arid climates, thriving in well-drained soils.
