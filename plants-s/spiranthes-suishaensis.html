Spiranthes suishaensis

Spiranthes suishaensis, commonly known as the Suisha ladies' tresses, is a rare orchid species native to China.

It is characterized by its delicate, spiraling arrangement of small, white flowers along a slender stem. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as forest floors or wet meadows. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its unique floral structure that resembles a twisted ribbon.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
