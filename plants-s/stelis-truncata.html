Stelis truncata

Stelis truncata, commonly known as the truncated stelis, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant has a short, thick stem with dense clusters of small, white to pale green flowers. Its leaves are elongated and often covered in a fine, waxy coating.

Stelis truncata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in terrariums and greenhouses.
