Sabicea acutissima

Sabicea acutissima, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sarsaparilla, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, shaded environments. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a serrated margin and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, and the plant is known for its aromatic properties.

Sabicea acutissima is valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
