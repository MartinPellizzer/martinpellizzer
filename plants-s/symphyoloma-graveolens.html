Symphyoloma graveolens

Symphyoloma graveolens, commonly known as the Cape fennel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, feathery leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.

Its distinctive scent and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for gardens.
