Senecio latifolius var. sinuatifolius

Senecio latifolius var. sinuatifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, broad, and often lobed leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a dense, spherical inflorescence called a capitulum. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

This variant is noted for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
