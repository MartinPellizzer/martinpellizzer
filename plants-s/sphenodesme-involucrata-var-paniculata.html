Sphenodesme involucrata var. paniculata

Sphenodesme involucrata var. paniculata, commonly known as the paniculate sphenodesme, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, clustered flowers that bloom in summer. The leaves are typically narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in paniculate inflorescences, which are highly branched and resemble a feather-like structure.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland or rocky habitats.
