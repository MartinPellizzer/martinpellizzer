Senecio magistri

Senecio magistri, commonly known as the African Daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and features narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Senecio magistri is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
