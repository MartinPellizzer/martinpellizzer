Solanum mauense

Solanum mauense, commonly known as the Mau nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are ovate and hairy, while its flowers are usually white or pale purple, with five petals.

The fruit is a fleshy berry that turns red when mature, containing several seeds.
