Stelis memorialis

Stelis memorialis, commonly known as the memory orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with slender, erect stems and linear, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Stelis memorialis is prized for its elegant appearance and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
