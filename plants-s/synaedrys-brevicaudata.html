Synaedrys brevicaudata

Synaedrys brevicaudata, commonly known as the short-tailed featherflower, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate, feathery flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal regions. It has small, narrow leaves and produces small, woody fruits that contain seeds.
