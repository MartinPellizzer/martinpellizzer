Stellaria ciliatosepala

Stellaria ciliatosepala, commonly known as the hairy starwort, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It typically features small, white or pinkish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the summer months. The leaves are usually narrow and covered in fine hairs, giving the plant its "hairy" appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows or along stream banks.

The species is characterized by its delicate structure and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
