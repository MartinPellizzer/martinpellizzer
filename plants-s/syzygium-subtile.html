Syzygium subtile

Syzygium subtile, commonly known as the delicate apple or sweet apple, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia and the Philippines. Its fruits are small, round, and have a sweet, fragrant aroma, making it a valued ornamental and edible plant.
