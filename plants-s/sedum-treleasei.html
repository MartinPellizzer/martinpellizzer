Sedum treleasei

Sedum treleasei, commonly known as the Blue Sedum, is a succulent plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps it store water efficiently. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale purple, typically blooming in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy choice for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal, Sedum treleasei is a popular plant among gardeners seeking low-maintenance, water-efficient landscaping options.
