Stelis barrensis

Stelis barrensis, commonly known as the barren mountain orchid, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils at high altitudes. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are usually white or pale pink in color. It has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

Stelis barrenensis is adapted to harsh, arid environments and is often found in cloud forests or alpine meadows.
