Stauntonia racemosa

Stauntonia racemosa, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often appearing in clusters. The plant has simple, opposite, and ovate leaves with a smooth texture. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, moist soil.

Stauntonia racemosa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and adaptability to various garden conditions.
