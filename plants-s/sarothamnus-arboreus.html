Sarothamnus arboreus

Sarothamnus arboreus, commonly known as the tree spurge or desert tree spurge, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its woody stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has small, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it well adapted to arid environments.

Sarothamnus arboreus is often found in rocky habitats and is considered a hardy, resilient plant.
