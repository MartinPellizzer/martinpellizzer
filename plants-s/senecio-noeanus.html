Senecio noeanus

Senecio noeanus, commonly known as the Cape snowbush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy choice for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Senecio noeanus is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
