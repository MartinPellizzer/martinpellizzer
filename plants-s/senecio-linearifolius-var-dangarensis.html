Senecio linearifolius var. dangarensis

Senecio linearifolius var. dangarensis, commonly known as the Dangarensis Senecio, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, erect stems with linear, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in clusters during the warmer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil to thrive.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
