Stelis rotunda

Stelis rotunda, commonly known as the round-stemmed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its compact growth habit and pseudobulbs. The plant typically features elliptical or ovate leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with a distinctive coloration that varies by species.

Stelis rotunda thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
