Sempervivum cebennense

Sempervivum cebennense, commonly known as the Cebenna houseleek, is a succulent plant native to the Pyrenees region.

It features fleshy, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically green with red or purple hues, especially in cooler temperatures. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Sempervivum cebennense is also known for its ability to survive in harsh alpine conditions.
