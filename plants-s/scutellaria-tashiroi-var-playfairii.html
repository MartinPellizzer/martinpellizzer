Scutellaria tashiroi var. playfairii

Scutellaria tashiroi var. playfairii, commonly known as the Playfair's skullcap, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically blue or violet, arranged in whorls along the stem, and bloom during the summer months.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
