Syagrus × teixeirana

Syagrus × teixeirana, commonly known as the golden palm or hybrid royal palm, is a natural hybrid between Syagrus romanzoffiana and Syagrus oleracea.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, fan-shaped leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant is native to Brazil and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in tropical landscapes. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Syagrus × teixeirana is also valued for its ability to provide shade and its use in landscaping and horticultural settings.
