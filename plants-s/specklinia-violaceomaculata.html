Specklinia violaceomaculata

Specklinia violaceomaculata, commonly known as the violet-streaked orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small epiphytic orchid, meaning it grows on the surface of other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. Its flowers are delicate, with a violet hue and distinctive streaks, often appearing in clusters.

Specklinia violaceomaculata thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires careful cultivation to mimic its natural habitat.
