Sutherlandia vesicaria

Sutherlandia vesicaria, commonly known as the "cancer plant" or "sutherlandia," is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and woody, creeping stems. The plant produces small, green, inflated pods that contain seeds, and it thrives in dry, sandy soils.

It is often found in grasslands and along riverbanks, and it has been traditionally used in herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
