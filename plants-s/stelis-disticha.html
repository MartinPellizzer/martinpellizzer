Stelis disticha

Stelis disticha, commonly known as the twin-leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a slender, upright stem with two leaves that are often arranged symmetrically. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and clustered in a raceme, often with a subtle fragrance.

Stelis disticha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
