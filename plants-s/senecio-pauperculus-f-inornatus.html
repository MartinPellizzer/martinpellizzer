Senecio pauperculus f. inornatus

Senecio pauperculus f. inornatus, commonly known as the bare-stemmed groundsel, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a low-growing, mat-forming structure with pale green, fleshy stems and small, yellow flowers. The leaves are typically reduced to scales, giving the plant a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or arid landscapes.

This variety is noted for its drought tolerance and minimal care requirements.
