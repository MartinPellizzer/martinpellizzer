Stadtmannia integrifolia

Stadtmannia integrifolia, commonly known as the broad-leafed stromboid, is a tropical plant native to parts of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically smooth and glossy. The plant produces showy, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a popular ornamental plant in tropical gardens.
