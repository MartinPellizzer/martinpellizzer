Stelis maloi

Stelis maloi, commonly known as the Maloi orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments within forest undergrowth. The plant features a slender, upright stem with few leaves and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Stelis maloi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
