Stephegyne birmanica

Stephegyne birmanica, commonly known as the Burmese morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in the early morning. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces spiral-shaped seed pods. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Stephegyne birmanica is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
