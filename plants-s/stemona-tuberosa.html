Stemona tuberosa

Stemona tuberosa, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features a rhizomatous root system and erect, branched stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue in color. Its leaves are lanceolate, with a slightly rough texture, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

The plant is valued in traditional medicine for its purported therapeutic properties.
