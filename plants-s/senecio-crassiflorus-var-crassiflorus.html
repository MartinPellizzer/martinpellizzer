Senecio crassiflorus var. crassiflorus

Senecio crassiflorus var. crassiflorus, commonly known as the thickflower senecio, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its robust, upright stems and large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features broad, fleshy leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for low-maintenance gardens.
