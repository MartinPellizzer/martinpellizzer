Syzygium cyclophyllum

Syzygium cyclophyllum, commonly known as the cyclophyll pineapple.

It is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its dense, glossy foliage and fragrant flowers. The plant produces a unique fruit with a spiky exterior and a sweet, aromatic pulp. It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is valued for both its ornamental and culinary uses.

Syzygium cyclophyllum thrives in humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
