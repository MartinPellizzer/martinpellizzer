Silene bastardii

Silene bastardii, commonly known as the bastard catchfly, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in rocky, arid environments. The plant produces small, pink or white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.
