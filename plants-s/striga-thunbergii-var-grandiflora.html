Striga thunbergii var. grandiflora

Striga thunbergii var. grandiflora, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant is a herbaceous perennial, often grown as a houseplant due to its compact size and ornamental value. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
