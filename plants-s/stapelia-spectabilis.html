Stapelia spectabilis

Stapelia spectabilis, commonly known as the giant star stapelia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems that can grow up to 1.5 meters in length. The plant produces large, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, with a strong, unpleasant odor. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Stapelia spectabilis is often cultivated for its unique appearance and resilience in arid environments.
