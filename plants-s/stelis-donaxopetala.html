Stelis donaxopetala

Stelis donaxopetala, commonly known as the orchid with feather-like leaves, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and elongated, lance-shaped leaves that resemble feathers. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often arranged in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Stelis donaxopetala is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique foliage structure.
