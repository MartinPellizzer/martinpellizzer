Stephanotis crinita

Stephanotis crinita, commonly known as the sweet scent vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and the Americas.

It is characterized by its long, trailing stems and clusters of fragrant, white, star-shaped flowers. The plant produces small, green, fleshy fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Stephanotis crinita is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant aroma.
