Schizanthus litoralis var. litoralis

Schizanthus litoralis var. litoralis, commonly known as the coastal scurfpea, is a herbaceous annual plant native to coastal regions of South America.

It features delicate, pea-like flowers that are typically pale purple or white, with a distinctive, hairy appearance. The plant has slender, branching stems and small, palmately lobed leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to coastal environments with high salinity and wind exposure.

Schizanthus litoralis var. litoralis is often found in dunes and along the edges of tidal zones.
