Stylosanthes purpurata

Stylosanthes purpurata, commonly known as purple stylo, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and trifoliate leaves with a distinctive purple hue. The plant produces small, yellow flowers followed by pod-like fruits containing elliptical seeds. It is well adapted to dry conditions and is often used in pasture systems due to its high nutritional value.

Stylosanthes purpurata also has the ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, improving soil fertility.
