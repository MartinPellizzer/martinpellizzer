Sinningia sceptrum

Sinningia sceptrum, commonly known as the royal purple gloxinia, is a striking hybrid plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is prized for its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range from deep purple to red. The plant features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Sinningia sceptrum is often cultivated as a decorative plant for indoor gardens and greenhouse displays.
