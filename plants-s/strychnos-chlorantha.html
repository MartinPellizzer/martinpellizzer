Strychnos chlorantha

Strychnos chlorantha, commonly known as the green star apple, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, star-shaped flowers and smooth, greenish-yellow fruits that resemble apples. The plant belongs to the Loganiaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in gardens. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a smooth margin.
