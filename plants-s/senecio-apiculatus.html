Senecio apiculatus

Senecio apiculatus, commonly known as the Cape snowflake, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves and produces showy, star-shaped yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it an attractive addition to dry landscapes.
