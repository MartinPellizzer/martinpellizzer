Sobralia callosa

Sobralia callosa, commonly known as the hairy sobralia, is a tropical orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually broad and leathery, adapted to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

Sobralia callosa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
