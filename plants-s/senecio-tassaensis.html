Senecio tassaensis

Senecio tassaensis, commonly known as the Tassaensis groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It features narrow, gray-green leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

Senecio tassaensis is often used in rock gardens due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
