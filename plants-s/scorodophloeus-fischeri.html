Scorodophloeus fischeri

Scorodophloeus fischeri, commonly known as the Fischer's sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in moist, sandy soils and is often found in open grasslands and along riverbanks. The plant has slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets.
