Saussurea stoliczkae

Saussurea stoliczkae, commonly known as the Himalayan spider flower, is a perennial herb native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, spindly stem and clusters of small, yellowish to white flowers. The plant has fibrous roots and is adapted to cold, arid environments. It is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes at elevations above 3,000 meters.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
