Solanum stelligerum

Solanum stelligerum, commonly known as the stelligerum nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and often have a rough texture, while its flowers are usually white or pale blue.

The fruit is a globose berry that turns from green to yellow when mature, containing several seeds.
