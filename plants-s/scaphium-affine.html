Scaphium affine

Scaphium affine, commonly known as the African buttercup, is a striking member of the Araceae family.

It is native to tropical Africa and features large, showy yellow flowers with a distinctive cup-shaped spathe. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Scaphium affine is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant appearance.
