Stephanomeria virgata subsp. pleurocarpa

Stephanomeria virgata subsp.

pleurocarpa, commonly known as the silverleaf stephanomeria, is a perennial herb native to California. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and showy yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and coastal dunes. It has a compact growth form with upright stems and is often found in disturbed soils.

The subspecies pleurocarpa is distinguished by its more robust stems and larger flower heads compared to other subspecies of Stephanomeria virgata.
