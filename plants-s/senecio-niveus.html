Senecio niveus

Senecio niveus, commonly known as the snow senecio, is a perennial flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-white, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
