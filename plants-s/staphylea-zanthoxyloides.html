Staphylea zanthoxyloides

Staphylea zanthoxyloides, commonly known as the yellow-flowered staphylea, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Staphyleaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of yellowish-white flowers. The plant produces small, elongated fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both ecological studies and horticultural cultivation.
