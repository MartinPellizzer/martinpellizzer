Sedum heckneri

Sedum heckneri, commonly known as the Mexican stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, clustered leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Sedum heckneri is drought-tolerant and requires minimal maintenance once established.
