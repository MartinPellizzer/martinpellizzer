Smodingium argutum

Smodingium argutum, commonly known as the sharpleaf smudging plant, is a member of the family Smodingiaceae.

It is a small, herbaceous plant characterized by its narrow, pointed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish or white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
