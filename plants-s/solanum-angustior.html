Solanum angustior

Solanum angustior, commonly known as the narrowleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into berries containing toxic seeds. Its leaves are narrow and have a slightly hairy texture.

Solanum angustior belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other toxic and economically important plants.
