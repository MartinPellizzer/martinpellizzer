Salacia multiflora

Salacia multiflora, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom profusely during the rainy season. The plant produces small, round fruits that are edible and have a sweet, citrus-like flavor. Salacia multiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
