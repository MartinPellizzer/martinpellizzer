Seringia macrantha

Seringia macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered seringia, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features slender, branched stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces showy, pink to purple flowers in clusters, which bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Seringia macrantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
