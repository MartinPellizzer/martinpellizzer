Stipagrostis hermannii

Stipagrostis hermannii, commonly known as Hermann's grass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy or rocky soils.

Stipagrostis hermannii is adapted to withstand drought and high temperatures, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
