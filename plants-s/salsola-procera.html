Salsola procera

Salsola procera, commonly known as desert sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of the world.

It is a shrub or perennial herb that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in summer, and its leaves are usually small, fleshy, and adapted to reduce water loss.
