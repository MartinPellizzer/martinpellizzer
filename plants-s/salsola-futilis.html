Salsola futilis

Salsola futilis, commonly known as sandmat or desert sandmat, is a flowering plant in the Amaranthaceae family.

It is a low-growing, perennial shrub that thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are typically small, fleshy, and succulent, adapted to conserve water.

Salsola futilis is often found in sandy soils and is well-suited to harsh desert conditions.
