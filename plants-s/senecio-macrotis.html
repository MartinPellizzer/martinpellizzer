Senecio macrotis

Senecio macrotis, commonly known as the giant blueweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. Senecio macrotis typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.
