Schiedeella

Schiedeella, commonly known as the ghost fern, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Schiedeellaceae.

It consists of a single species, Schiedeella hypoleuca, which is native to the tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its unique, delicate foliage and small, inconspicuous flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Schiedeella is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
