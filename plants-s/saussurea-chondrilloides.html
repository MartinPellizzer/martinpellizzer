Saussurea chondrilloides

Saussurea chondrilloides, commonly known as the Himalayan arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of the Himalayas.

It is a perennial herb with a robust, upright stem and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in a dense, spike-like inflorescence. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and well-drained soil.

Its botanical features make it an important species in the study of alpine flora and ecological adaptation.
