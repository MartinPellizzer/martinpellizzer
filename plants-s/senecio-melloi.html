Senecio melloi

Senecio melloi, commonly known as the Mello's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It features small, daisy-like yellow flowers and has a low, spreading growth habit.

Senecio melloi is often used in rock gardens due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
