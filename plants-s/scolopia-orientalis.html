Scolopia orientalis

Scolopia orientalis, commonly known as the oriental scolopia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Scolopia orientalis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate beauty.
