Shangwua denticulata

Shangwua denticulata, commonly known as the Chinese denticulate orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender stems and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and requires specific conditions to thrive. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a waxy coating, while its flowers display a range of colors, including white, pink, and purple.
