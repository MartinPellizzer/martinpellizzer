Stelis oscargrouchii

Stelis oscargrouchii, commonly known as the Oscar Grouch orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structure. The plant typically produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with a distinctive throat coloration. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
