Scilla koelzii

Scilla koelzii, commonly known as the Koelz's squill, is a perennial bulbous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It produces striking blue or violet flowers in early spring, typically blooming before the leaves emerge. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are evergreen and persist through the winter. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Scilla koelzii is valued for its ornamental beauty and ease of cultivation.
