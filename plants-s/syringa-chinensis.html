Syringa chinensis

Syringa chinensis, commonly known as Chinese lilac, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of white, pink, and lilac in the spring. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil.

Syringa chinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly used in landscaping.
