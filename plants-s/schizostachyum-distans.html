Schizostachyum distans

Schizostachyum distans, commonly known as the bamboo palm, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and feathery leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Schizostachyum distans is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.

Its growth habit makes it suitable for both ornamental gardening and reforestation projects.
