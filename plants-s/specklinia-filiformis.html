Specklinia filiformis

Specklinia filiformis, commonly known as the thread-leaf orchid, is a small epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, thread-like leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters during the wet season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Specklinia filiformis is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
