Saxifraga maireana

Saxifraga maireana, commonly known as Maire's saxifrage, is a low-growing perennial plant native to New Zealand.

It typically thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky areas. The plant features small, clustered leaves that are usually dark green and slightly hairy. Its flowers are white or pale pink, with five petals and numerous stamens, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Saxifraga maireana is adapted to cool climates and is often found in areas with high humidity and well-drained soil.
