Solanum cufodontii

Solanum cufodontii, commonly known as the Cufodontii nightshade, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by berries that turn from green to red as they mature. It is adapted to thrive in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and traditional herbal practices.
