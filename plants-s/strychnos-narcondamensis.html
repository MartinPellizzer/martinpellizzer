Strychnos narcondamensis

Strychnos narcondamensis, commonly known as the Himalayan nux vomica, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the family Loganiaceae and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. Due to its medicinal and toxic properties, it is of interest in both traditional and modern pharmacological research.
