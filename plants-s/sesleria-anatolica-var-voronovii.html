Sesleria anatolica var. voronovii

Sesleria anatolica var. voronovii, commonly known as the Anatolian sesleria, is a perennial grass-like plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the family Poaceae and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing white to pale blue flowers. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.
