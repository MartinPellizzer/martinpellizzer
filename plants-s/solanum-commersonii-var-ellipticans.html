Solanum commersonii var. ellipticans

Solanum commersonii var. ellipticans, commonly known as the Commerson's nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial native to South America.

It features elliptical, waxy leaves that are often hairy and have a distinctive dark green color. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. Its fruit is a yellowish, fleshy berry that contains several seeds.

This species is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
