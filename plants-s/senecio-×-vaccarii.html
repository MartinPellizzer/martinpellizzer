Senecio × vaccarii

Senecio × vaccarii, commonly known as the African daisy, is a hybrid plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows as a low-growing shrub with narrow, gray-green leaves and bright yellow flower heads. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Senecio × vaccarii is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
