Sideroxylon rubiginosum

Sideroxylon rubiginosum, commonly known as the red ironwood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, hard, and dark-colored wood, which is highly valued for its durability and strength. The plant has simple, opposite, and often leathery leaves that are typically dark green in color. It produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in spikes, followed by woody capsules containing numerous seeds.

The tree is well adapted to humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.
