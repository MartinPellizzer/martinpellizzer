Silene stylosa

Silene stylosa, commonly known as the slender catchfly, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pinkish to white flowers. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as fields and roadsides. Its leaves are linear and often covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and branching.

Silene stylosa is an annual plant that blooms from late spring to early summer.
