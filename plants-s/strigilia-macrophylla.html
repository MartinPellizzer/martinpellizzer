Strigilia macrophylla

Strigilia macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf strigilia, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, lance-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands or rocky areas.

Strigilia macrophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
