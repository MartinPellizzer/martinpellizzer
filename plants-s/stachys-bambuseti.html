Stachys bambuseti

Stachys bambuseti, commonly known as the bamboo leaved stachys, is a flowering plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is native to southern China and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, arranged in spikes at the top of the stems. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil.

Stachys bambuseti is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun to partial shade.
