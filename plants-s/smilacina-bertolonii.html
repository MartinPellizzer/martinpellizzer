Smilacina bertolonii

Smilacina bertolonii, commonly known as the Bertoloni's greenbriar, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the Smilacaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and woody stems. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically borne in clusters. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly serrated edge.
