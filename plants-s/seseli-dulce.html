Seseli dulce

It is characterized by its finely divided, feathery leaves and small, white or yellowish flowers that bloom in umbrella-like clusters. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in dry, sunny environments. The plant is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to withstand arid conditions.
