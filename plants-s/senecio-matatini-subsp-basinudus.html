Senecio matatini subsp. basinudus

Senecio matatini subsp.

basinudus, commonly known as the Basinudus Groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. It features narrow, linear leaves and produces small yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters.
