Sympetaleia rupestris

Sympetaleia rupestris, commonly known as the rock petal orchid, is a rare and delicate orchid species native to specific rocky habitats in southern Africa.

The plant typically grows in well-drained, calcareous soils and thrives in sunny, open environments.
