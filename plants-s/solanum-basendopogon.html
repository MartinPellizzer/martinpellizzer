Solanum basendopogon

Solanum basendopogon, commonly known as the woolly nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in umbels. Its fruit is a spherical berry that turns from green to red as it matures.

Solanum basendopogon belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is often found in dry, open habitats.
