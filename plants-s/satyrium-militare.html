Satyrium militare

Satyrium militare, commonly known as the military orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, erect spikes of flowers.

Satyrium militare is known for its relatively short flowering period and is often found in fragmented populations across its native range.
