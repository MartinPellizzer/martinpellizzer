Solanum pseudogracile

Solanum pseudogracile, commonly known as the small-leaved nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 cm. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are ovate and have a slightly hairy texture.

Solanum pseudogracile is often found in moist, shaded areas and is part of the nightshade family, Solanaceae.
