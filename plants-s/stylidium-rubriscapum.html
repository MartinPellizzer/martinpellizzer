Stylidium rubriscapum

Stylidium rubriscapum, commonly known as the red-capped triggerplant, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are often hidden beneath the red cap, which is a unique adaptation for attracting specific pollinators.
