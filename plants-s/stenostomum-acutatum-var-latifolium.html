Stenostomum acutatum var. latifolium

Stenostomum acutatum var. latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf stenostomum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or grassland habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
