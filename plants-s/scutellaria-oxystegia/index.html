Scutellaria Oxystegia

Scutellaria oxystegia is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of East Asia. Known for its vibrant blue and purple flowers, it thrives in cool, moist environments. This member of the mint family is often found in rocky soils and shaded forest floors. Its unique leaf shape and delicate blossoms make it a favorite among botanists and gardeners. Due to habitat loss, it is now considered a threatened species requiring conservation efforts.
