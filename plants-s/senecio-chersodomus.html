Senecio chersodomus

Senecio chersodomus, commonly known as the yellow starbursh, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features wiry, branched stems and narrow, linear leaves. Its bright yellow flowers are arranged in dense clusters, creating a striking visual display.

Senecio chersodomus is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
