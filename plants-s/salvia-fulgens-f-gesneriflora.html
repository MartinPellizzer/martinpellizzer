Salvia fulgens f. gesneriflora

Salvia fulgens f. gesneriflora, commonly known as the scarlet salvia or Mexican flamebush, is a hybrid cultivar of the common salvia.

It is native to the southern United States and Mexico, where it thrives in warm, sunny climates. The plant features striking, tubular red or orange-red flowers that bloom in dense clusters during the summer months. Its foliage is glossy and dark green, with lance-shaped leaves that add to its ornamental appeal.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and drought tolerance.
