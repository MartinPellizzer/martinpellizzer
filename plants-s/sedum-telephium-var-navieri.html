Sedum telephium var. navieri

Sedum telephium var. navieri, commonly known as the Navier's stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features fleshy, upright stems and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red. The leaves are typically blue-green, with a waxy coating that helps reduce water loss. This variety is well adapted to rocky and arid environments, making it a hardy choice for rock gardens.

It blooms in summer, adding a vibrant touch to gardens with its colorful flowers.
