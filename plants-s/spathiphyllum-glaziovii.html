Spathiphyllum glaziovii

Spathiphyllum glaziovii, commonly known as the Glaziov's peace lily, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Central and South America.

It features broad, dark green, waxy leaves that are elliptical in shape and can grow up to 30 inches in length. The plant produces striking white spathes that enclose small, inconspicuous flowers, which are typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in low-light conditions and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its air-purifying qualities and ease of care.
