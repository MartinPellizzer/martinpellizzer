Stenocarpus balansae

Stenocarpus balansae, commonly known as the Balansae hakea, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage and striking inflorescences. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers that are clustered in cylindrical or spike-like formations. Its seeds are encased in woody, fibrous capsules that remain on the plant for several years.

Stenocarpus balansae is adapted to nutrient-poor soils and is often found in heathlands and coastal areas.
