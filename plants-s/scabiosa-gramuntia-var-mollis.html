Scabiosa gramuntia var. mollis

Scabiosa gramuntia var. mollis, commonly known as the soft scabious, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of pink or purple flowers with a distinctive, slightly fuzzy appearance. The leaves are typically rounded and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its soft texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is known for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators such as butterflies and bees.
