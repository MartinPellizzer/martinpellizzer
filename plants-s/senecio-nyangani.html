Senecio nyangani

Senecio nyangani, commonly known as the Natal Senecio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in grassland or forest margins. It produces small yellow flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Its foliage is usually gray-green with a fuzzy texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
