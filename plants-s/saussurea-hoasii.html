Saussurea hoasii

Saussurea hoasii, commonly known as the Himalayan saffron.

This perennial herb is native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas, where it thrives in cool, moist environments. It features a tall, slender stem with narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers in the summer. The plant is notable for its medicinal properties, often used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and analgesic effects.

Its botanical significance lies in its role as a valuable species in alpine ecosystems and its potential for pharmaceutical applications.
