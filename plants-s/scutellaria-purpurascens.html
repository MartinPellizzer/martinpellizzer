Scutellaria purpurascens

Scutellaria purpurascens, commonly known as purple scutellaria, is a perennial herb native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple, often with a distinctive purple hue. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or swampy areas.

Scutellaria purpurascens is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
