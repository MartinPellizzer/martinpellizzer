Senecio provincialis var. corbariensis

Senecio provincialis var. corbariensis, commonly known as the Corbariense Senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid environments. It features narrow, linear leaves and produces small yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and resilience in harsh conditions.
