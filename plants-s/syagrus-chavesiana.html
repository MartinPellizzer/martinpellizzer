Syagrus chavesiana

Syagrus chavesiana, commonly known as the Chaves palm, is a species of palm native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant is typically found in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils. It produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into clustered fruits.

Syagrus chavesiana is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
