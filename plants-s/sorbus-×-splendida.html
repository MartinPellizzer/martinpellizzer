Sorbus × splendida

Sorbus × splendida, commonly known as the Chinese whitebeam, is a hybrid deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its white or pale pink flowers that appear in clusters during the spring. The tree produces small, red berries that are typically consumed by birds. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a serrated edge.

Sorbus × splendida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often planted in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
