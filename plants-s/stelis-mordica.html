Stelis mordica

Stelis mordica, commonly known as the Mordica orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its epiphytic growth habit, often found growing on trees in tropical rainforests. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. Stelis mordica thrives in humid environments with high light exposure and well-draining substrates.

Its unique botanical features make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
