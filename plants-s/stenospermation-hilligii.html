Stenospermation hilligii

Stenospermation hilligii, commonly known as the narrow-seed orchid, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant produces a unique type of seed capsule that is elongated and narrow, giving it its scientific name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.
