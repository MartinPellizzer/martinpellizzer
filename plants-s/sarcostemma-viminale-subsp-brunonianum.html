Sarcostemma viminale subsp.

brunonianum, commonly known as the climbing milkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, particularly India and Southeast Asia. It is a perennial vine characterized by its slender, climbing stems and opposite, heart-shaped leaves.
