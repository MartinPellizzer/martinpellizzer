Sidalcea spicata var. tonsa

Sidalcea spicata var. tonsa, commonly known as the Sierra foothill checkerberry, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, also known as the legume or pea family, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, pea-like flowers.
