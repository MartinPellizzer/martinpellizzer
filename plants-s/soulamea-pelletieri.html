Soulamea pelletieri

Soulamea pelletieri, commonly known as the golden star fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that emit a subtle, sweet fragrance. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.

Soulamea pelletieri is also valued for its ability to purify the air and enhance the aesthetic appeal of any space.
