Simaba cuspidata var. typica

Simaba cuspidata var. typica, commonly known as the Caribbean mahogany, is a tropical tree native to the Caribbean region.

It belongs to the family Sterculiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, reddish-brown bark and simple, alternate leaves. However, due to overharvesting, it is now considered a vulnerable species in its native habitat.
