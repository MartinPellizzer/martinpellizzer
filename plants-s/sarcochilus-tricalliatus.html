Sarcochilus tricalliatus

Sarcochilus tricalliatus, commonly known as the Three-lobed Sword Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It features a rhizomatous growth habit with strap-like, dark green leaves that are often adorned with white or pale markings. The plant produces striking, sword-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange with a contrasting lip. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forested areas.

Sarcochilus tricalliatus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae.
