Sicydium

Sicydium, commonly known as the sicydium tree, is a member of the Malvaceae family, native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and lance-shaped leaves. Sicydium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
