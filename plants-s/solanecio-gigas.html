Solanecio gigas

Solanecio gigas, commonly known as the giant evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that open in the evening. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Solanecio gigas thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in sandy or rocky soils.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and potential medicinal research.
