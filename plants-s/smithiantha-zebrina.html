Smithiantha zebrina

Smithiantha zebrina, commonly known as the zebra flower, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a distinctive zebra-like pattern on the petals. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy inflorescences that attract pollinators such as birds and insects. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Smithiantha zebrina is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
