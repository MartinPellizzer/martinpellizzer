Senecio wollastonii

Senecio wollastonii, commonly known as the Wollaston's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky, arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. It features narrow, silvery-green leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring.
