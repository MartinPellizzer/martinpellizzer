Specklinia murexoidea

Specklinia murexoidea, commonly known as the mossy fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing form and dark green, leathery fronds that are covered in a dense layer of fine, moss-like structures. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and rock gardens.

The fronds are usually evergreen, providing a consistent aesthetic throughout the year.
