Spiranthes lechleri

Spiranthes lechleri, commonly known as the lechleri spirea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its delicate, spiral-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as cloud forests and mountainous regions. Its rhizomatous root system allows it to spread and form dense colonies.

The flowers are usually white or pale pink, and the plant is known for its ornamental value in horticulture.
