Stelis bruchmuelleri

Stelis bruchmuelleri, commonly known as the Bruchmueller's spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to tropical regions of South America.

It typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on forest floors or in rocky areas. The plant features a slender, upright stem with few leaves and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and green. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture from the surrounding soil.

This orchid is valued for its unique appearance and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its ornamental qualities.
