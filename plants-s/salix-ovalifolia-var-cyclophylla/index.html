Salix Ovalifolia Var. Cyclophylla

Salix ovalifolia var. cyclophylla is a rare and lesser-known member of the willow family. Native to specific regions in the western United States, this variant thrives in moist, forested environments. Its unique characteristics include oval-shaped leaves and a distinctive growth pattern that sets it apart from other willow species. Despite its ecological importance, it remains under-studied and often overlooked by botanists. This article explores its biology, habitat, and conservation status.
