Senecio lastarrianus

Senecio lastarrianus, commonly known as the Lastarrian's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant typically grows in rocky, arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are usually gray-green with a woolly texture, and it produces small yellow flowers in clusters.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses in herbal medicine.
