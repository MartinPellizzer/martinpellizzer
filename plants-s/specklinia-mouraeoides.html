Specklinia mouraeoides

Specklinia mouraeoides, commonly known as the Moura's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical rainforests of Brazil.

It is a small epiphytic plant, typically growing on tree trunks or branches in the canopy layer of the forest. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green with a waxy texture. Its flowers are delicate, often displaying a mix of white and pink hues, and are borne in clusters.

Specklinia mouraeoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded greenhouses or terrariums.
