Senecio brachypodus var. pleistocephalus

Senecio brachypodus var. pleistocephalus, commonly known as the broad-leaf senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. It produces showy yellow flowers arranged in dense, spherical clusters.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
