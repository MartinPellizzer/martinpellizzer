Secale strictum var. ciliatoglume

Secale strictum var. ciliatoglume, commonly known as the slender ryegrass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves with finely serrated margins. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous tiny florets. It thrives in moist, temperate climates and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and agricultural research.
