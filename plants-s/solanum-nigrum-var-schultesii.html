Solanum nigrum var. schultesii

Solanum nigrum var. schultesii, commonly known as the black nightshade variant, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, dark purple to black berries that mature in the fall. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in various regions across Europe and Asia. It has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges.

The berries contain toxic compounds, making it potentially harmful if ingested.
