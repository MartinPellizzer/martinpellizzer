Scutellaria stevenii

Scutellaria stevenii, commonly known as Steven's blue skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular blue or violet flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

Scutellaria stevenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
