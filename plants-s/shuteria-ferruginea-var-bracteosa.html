Shuteria ferruginea var. bracteosa

Shuteria ferruginea var. bracteosa, commonly known as the rusty shuteria, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a rusty-colored pubescence, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in the spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in temperate climates.
