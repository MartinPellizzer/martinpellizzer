Symplocos schaefferae

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves with a glossy upper surface. The plant produces clusters of small, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, smooth seeds.
