Stipa capillata var. coronata

Stipa capillata var. coronata, commonly known as the crowned feather grass, is a perennial grass native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, hair-like leaves and dense, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that give it a feathery appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in landscaping and restoration projects.
