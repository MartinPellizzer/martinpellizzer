Senna herzogii

Senna herzogii, commonly known as the yellow senna, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces slender, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Senna herzogii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
