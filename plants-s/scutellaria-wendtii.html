Scutellaria wendtii

Scutellaria wendtii, commonly known as Wendt's skullcap, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Scutellaria wendtii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.
