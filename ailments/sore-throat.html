sore throat

intro

A sore throat is a common condition characterized by inflammation and pain in the throat, often caused by viral or bacterial infections, allergies, or overuse of the voice. Symptoms may include a scratchy or burning sensation, difficulty swallowing, and hoarseness. Treatment typically involves rest, staying hydrated, and using over-the-counter pain relievers or throat lozenges to alleviate discomfort and promote healing.
