anxiety

intro

Anxiety is a common mental health condition characterized by feelings of worry, nervousness, and fear that are persistent and overwhelming. These emotions can manifest in physical symptoms such as rapid heartbeat, trembling, and shortness of breath, making everyday tasks and activities a daunting challenge. If left unmanaged, anxiety can interfere with a person's quality of life, relationships, and overall well-being, highlighting the importance of seeking professional help and support.
