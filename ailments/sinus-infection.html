sinus infection

intro

A sinus infection, also known as sinusitis, occurs when the sinuses become inflamed and infected, often due to a viral or bacterial infection. This can cause symptoms such as headaches, facial pain, and pressure, as well as congestion, runny nose, and coughing. If left untreated, a sinus infection can lead to serious complications, such as a prolonged recovery or even respiratory problems.
