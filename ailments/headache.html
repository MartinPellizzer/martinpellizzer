headache

intro

A headache is a common and often debilitating condition characterized by pain or discomfort in the head, neck, or scalp. This condition can be caused by a variety of factors, including stress, lack of sleep, dehydration, and underlying medical conditions such as migraines or sinusitis. Treatment options for headaches range from over-the-counter pain relief medications to lifestyle changes, such as maintaining a consistent sleep schedule and staying hydrated.
