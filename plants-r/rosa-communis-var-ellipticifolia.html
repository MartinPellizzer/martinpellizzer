Rosa communis var. ellipticifolia

Rosa communis var. ellipticifolia, commonly known as the ellipticifolia rose, is a botanical variant of the common rose species.

It is characterized by its elliptical-shaped leaves and fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is a deciduous shrub, typically growing to a height of 1 to 2 meters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant blooms and sturdy growth habit.
