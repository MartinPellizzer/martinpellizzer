Ranunculus eryuanensis

Ranunculus eryuanensis, commonly known as the Eryuan buttercup, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers with numerous petals. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountainous regions. It has palmate leaves and produces small, greenish-yellow sepals.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
