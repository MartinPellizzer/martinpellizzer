Rubus setigerus var. sinutidentatus

Rubus setigerus var. sinutidentatus, commonly known as the Chinese blackberry, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features prickly stems and compound leaves with serrated margins. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or black when mature. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in both horticulture and ecological studies.
