Rhododendron caucasicum var. roseum-delicatissimum

Rhododendron caucasicum var. roseum-delicatissimum, commonly known as the Caucasian roseum-delicatissimum rhododendron, is a flowering shrub native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pale pink to deep rose. The plant features broad, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and arranged in whorls around the stems. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and prefers cool, moist climates with partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
