Rosa × schwertschlageri

Rosa × schwertschlageri, commonly known as the Schwertschlägeri rose, is a hybrid shrub native to Europe.

It features dark green, serrated leaves and produces fragrant, semi-double pink flowers with a strong, sweet scent. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and thorny stems, typical of many rose species. It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and is relatively disease-resistant.

This rose thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a versatile choice for gardeners.
