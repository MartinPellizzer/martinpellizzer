Rapuntium chinense

Rapuntium chinense, commonly known as Chinese cress, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbage and mustard. The plant produces small, yellow flowers and has edible leaves and seeds that are used in traditional Chinese cuisine. It thrives in moist, temperate climates and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a common weed in agricultural areas.
