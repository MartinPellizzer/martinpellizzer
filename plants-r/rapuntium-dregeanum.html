Rapuntium dregeanum

Rapuntium dregeanum, commonly known as the Cape dandelion, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, spoon-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers that develop into fluffy, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Rapuntium dregeanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
