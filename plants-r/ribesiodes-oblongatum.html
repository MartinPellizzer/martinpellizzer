Ribesiodes oblongatum

Ribesiodes oblongatum, commonly known as the broadleaf ribes, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, elliptical leaves that are often hairy on the underside. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early summer, followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
