Romulea reflexa

Romulea reflexa, commonly known as the reflexed romulea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa, where it thrives in rocky and sandy soils. Romulea reflexa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
