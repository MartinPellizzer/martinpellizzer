Rubeola latifolia

Rubeola latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf rubeola, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces oval-shaped, orange-red berries that contain several seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a landscape plant.
