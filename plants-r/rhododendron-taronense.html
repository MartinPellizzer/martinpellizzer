Rhododendron taronense

Rhododendron taronense, commonly known as the Taronese rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in shaded to partially sunny conditions. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant floral display.

Rhododendron taronense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and natural landscapes.
