Romulea tubulosa

Romulea tubulosa, commonly known as the tubular romulea, is a perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and striking, tubular-shaped flowers. The flowers are typically yellow or orange, with a distinctive three-lobed structure at the tip. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or sandy habitats.

The plant is prized for its ornamental value and is cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
