Rhodiola handelii

Rhodiola handelii, commonly known as the Tibetan rhodiola, is a perennial flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas and Central Asia.

The plant is adapted to cold, arid environments and thrives in rocky, mountainous regions. Its botanical features make it well-suited for survival in extreme climates and have led to its use in traditional medicine.
