Rhodolirium Andicola

Rhodolirium andicola is a rare and unique flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes. This species is known for its striking violet-colored flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in cloud forests. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered endangered and is protected in several conservation areas. Its beauty and ecological importance make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
