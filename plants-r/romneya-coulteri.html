Romneya coulteri

Romneya coulteri, commonly known as the Coulter's lily, is a striking perennial plant native to the southern California coastal ranges.

It features large, showy white or pale pink flowers with a distinctive red or orange interior, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has strap-like, blue-green leaves that are evergreen and can grow up to 3 feet tall. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Romneya coulteri is also valued for its ornamental appeal and historical significance in California's native flora.
