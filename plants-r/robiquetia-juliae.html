Robiquetia juliae

Robiquetia juliae, commonly known as the Cape rose.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, characterized by its striking pink or red flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has woody stems and lance-shaped leaves, which are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and compact growth habit.
