Rubentia

Rubentia, commonly known as the redroot or redroot dogbane, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to reddish flowers that bloom in summer. Its roots are reddish in color, which gives it its common name.

Rubentia is often found in moist soils along stream banks and in woodland areas.
