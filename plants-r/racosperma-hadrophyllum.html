Racosperma hadrophyllum

Racosperma hadrophyllum, commonly known as the large-leafed acacia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellow flowers. The plant has a woody stem with a rough, fissured bark, and its leaves are typically long and narrow.
