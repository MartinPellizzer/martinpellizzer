Racosperma perangustum

Racosperma perangustum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf acacia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features slender, often hairy leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. Its flowers are typically yellow or pale yellow, clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences.

Racosperma perangustum is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions of southern Australia.
