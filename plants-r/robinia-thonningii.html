Robinia thonningii

Robinia thonningii, commonly known as the African honeythorn, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant produces yellow, tubular flowers that attract bees and other pollinators. Its leaves are bipinnately compound, with numerous small leaflets.

The fruit is a pod that splits open to release seeds, contributing to its spread in its native habitat.
