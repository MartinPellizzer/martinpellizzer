Rosa × dumetorum f. gradskoensis

Rosa × dumetorum f. gradskoensis, commonly known as the Gradskoensis rose, is a botanical hybrid native to the Balkan region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant pink flowers. The plant typically has dark green, serrated leaves and thorny stems, typical of many rose species. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

This form is valued for its ornamental qualities and historical significance in horticultural collections.
