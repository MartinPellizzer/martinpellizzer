Rosa pendulina f. lagenaria

Rosa pendulina f. lagenaria, commonly known as the hanging rose or the bell-shaped rose, is a unique cultivar of the Rosa pendulina species.

It is characterized by its long, drooping stems that produce clusters of bell-shaped flowers. The flowers are typically pink or white, with a distinctive, elongated form that sets it apart from other rose varieties. This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its visual interest.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
