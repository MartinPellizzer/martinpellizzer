Rosa dicksonii

Rosa dicksonii, commonly known as the Dickson's rose, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, pink to white flowers. The plant has simple, alternating leaves that are typically oval-shaped with a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Rosa dicksonii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its attractive blooms and hardy nature.
