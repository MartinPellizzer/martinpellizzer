Rosa Arvensis F. Repens

Rosa arvensis f. repens is a low-growing, sprawling form of the field rose known for its adaptability to various soil types. It thrives in disturbed areas and is often found along roadsides and in open fields. The plant produces fragrant pink flowers that bloom in summer, adding beauty to its natural habitat. Its stems spread horizontally, forming dense mats that can suppress weeds. This resilient plant is both a valuable ornamental and an important component of ecological landscapes.
