Rubus foliosus var. abstrusus

Rubus foliosus var. abstrusus, commonly known as the obscure leaf raspberry, is a shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are typically arranged in a pinnate pattern with serrated margins. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are usually red or black when mature. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland edges or moist soils.

Due to its limited distribution and unique morphology, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
