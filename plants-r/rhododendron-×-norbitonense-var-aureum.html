Rhododendron × norbitonense var. aureum

Rhododendron × norbitonense var. aureum, commonly known as the Golden Norbiton rhododendron, is a hybrid evergreen shrub native to China.

It features dense, leathery leaves that are typically dark green with a slight gloss, and its most striking characteristic is its clusters of showy, golden-yellow flowers. The plant thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for shaded garden settings. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in horticulture for its vibrant blooms.

The variety is a result of crosses between different rhododendron species, showcasing the diversity within the genus.
