Rhododendron zeylanicum

Rhododendron zeylanicum, commonly known as the Ceylon rhododendron, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Sri Lanka.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters in its natural habitat. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous areas with rich, acidic soils.

The leaves are leathery and elliptical, providing a lush green backdrop to its vibrant blooms.
