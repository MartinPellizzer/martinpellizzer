Rubus × pseudogracilior

Rubus × pseudogracilior, commonly known as the hybrid blackberry or loganberry, is a deciduous shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between *Rubus ursinus* (blackberry) and *Rubus pectoralis* (loganberry), resulting in a plant with unique botanical characteristics. The plant typically features thorny stems, trifoliate leaves, and aggregate fruits that are dark purple to black in color. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its edible berries.

The hybrid exhibits traits from both parent species, making it a popular choice for horticultural purposes.
