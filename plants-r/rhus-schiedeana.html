Rhus schiedeana

Rhus schiedeana, commonly known as the Schiede's sumac, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Anacardiaceae family, which includes other sumac species. The plant features compound leaves with serrated edges and produces small, clustered yellow flowers in the spring. Its fruit is a red, fuzzy drupe that matures in the fall.

Rhus schiedeana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in dry, rocky soils.
