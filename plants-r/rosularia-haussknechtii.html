Rosularia haussknechtii

Rosularia haussknechtii, commonly known as the Hausknecht's roseleaf, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Rosaceae and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring. Its leaves are arranged in a rosette pattern, and the stems are covered in fine, white hairs.

Rosularia haussknechtii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to arid environments.
