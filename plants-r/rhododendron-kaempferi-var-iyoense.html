Rhododendron kaempferi var. iyoense

Rhododendron kaempferi var. iyoense, commonly known as the Iyo-en rhododendron, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It features dense, dark green foliage and produces clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters and thrives in acidic, well-drained soils. It prefers partial shade to full sun and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for rock gardens and as a specimen plant in shaded areas.
