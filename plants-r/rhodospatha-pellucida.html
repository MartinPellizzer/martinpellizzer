Rhodospatha pellucida

Rhodospatha pellucida, commonly known as the glassy orchid, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, translucent leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Rhodospatha pellucida thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
