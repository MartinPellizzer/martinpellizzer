Riedelia orchioides

Riedelia orchioides, commonly known as the orchid-like riedelia, is a flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often showy flowers. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are typically small and arranged in a dense inflorescence, often displaying a range of colors from white to pink.

Riedelia orchioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated by enthusiasts of rare and exotic plants.
