Rosa lucieae var. fujisanensis

Rosa lucieae var. fujisanensis, commonly known as the Fujisan rose, is a compact shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its dense, thornless stems and dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and hardiness in cooler climates.
