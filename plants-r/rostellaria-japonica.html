Rostellaria japonica

Rostellaria japonica, commonly known as the Japanese rostellaria, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae, which includes many orchid species. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale pink. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Rostellaria japonica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
