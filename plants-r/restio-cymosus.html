Restio cymosus

Restio cymosus, commonly known as the mountain restio, is a perennial grass-like plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Restionaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and unbranched inflorescences. Restio cymosus is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in coastal conditions.
