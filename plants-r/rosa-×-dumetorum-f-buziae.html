Rosa × dumetorum f. buziae

Rosa × dumetorum f. buziae, commonly known as the Buzia rose, is a naturally occurring hybrid species within the rose family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in rocky or alpine environments. The plant typically produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink or white. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and covered in fine, soft hairs.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to harsh growing conditions.
