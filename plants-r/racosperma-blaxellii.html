Racosperma blaxellii

Racosperma blaxellii, commonly known as the Blaxell's acacia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features small, yellow flowers arranged in dense, spherical clusters. Its leaves are typically bipinnate, with numerous small leaflets.

Racosperma blaxellii is adapted to arid environments and is often found in sandy soils.
