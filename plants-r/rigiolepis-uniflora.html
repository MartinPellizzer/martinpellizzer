Rigiolepis uniflora

Rigiolepis uniflora, commonly known as the single-flower rigiolepis, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale purple. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid forest understories.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
