Rosa arvensis splendens

Rosa arvensis splendens, commonly known as the shining field rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and along roadsides. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in summer. Its foliage is typically dark green with a slightly hairy texture.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and can thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
