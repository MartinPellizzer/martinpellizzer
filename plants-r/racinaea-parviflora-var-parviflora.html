Racinaea parviflora var. parviflora

Racinaea parviflora var. parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered racinaea, is a tropical flowering plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. Due to its ornamental value and rarity, it is often cultivated in greenhouses and botanical gardens.
