Rhynchoglossum rheedei

Rhynchoglossum rheedei, commonly known as the yellow star orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow petals and a distinctive star-shaped lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments within tropical forests. Its flowers are highly fragrant, attracting pollinators such as bees and wasps.

Rhynchoglossum rheedei is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its ecological role in its native habitat.
