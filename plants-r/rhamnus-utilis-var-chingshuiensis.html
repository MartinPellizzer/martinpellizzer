Rhamnus utilis var. chingshuiensis

Rhamnus utilis var. chingshuiensis, commonly known as the Chingshui boxthorn, is a shrub native to eastern Asia, particularly found in China.

It belongs to the Rhamnaceae family and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which are followed by small, fleshy berries that are edible for birds and some mammals.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions with acidic soils.
