Romulea campanuloides var. camerooniana

Romulea campanuloides var. camerooniana, commonly known as the Cameroon bell orchid, is a perennial bulbous plant native to Cameroon.

It features striking bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a yellow or green throat. The plant produces a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and has a short, erect flowering stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
