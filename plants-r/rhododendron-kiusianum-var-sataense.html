Rhododendron kiusianum var. sataense

Rhododendron kiusianum var. sataense, commonly known as the Sataenese rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Japan.

It features broad, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slight pubescence on the underside. The plant produces clusters of tubular, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink to red, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and natural landscapes.
