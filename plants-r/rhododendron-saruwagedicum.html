Rhododendron saruwagedicum

Rhododendron saruwagedicum, commonly known as the Saruwagedi rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of eastern India and Nepal.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows at high altitudes, often in humid and cool climates. The plant features dense, leathery leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, appearing in clusters and blooming in late spring to early summer.

Rhododendron saruwagedicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its beauty and ecological significance.
