Rubus × polyanchus

Rubus × polyanchus, commonly known as the evergreen blackberry.

It is a hybrid shrub that exhibits characteristics of both brambles and evergreen raspberries. The plant produces dark, edible berries that are often used in culinary applications. It thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Its thorny stems and dense foliage make it a hardy, adaptable species suitable for various garden settings.
