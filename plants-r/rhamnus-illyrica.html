Rhamnus illyrica

Rhamnus illyrica, commonly known as the Illyrian buckthorn, is a shrub native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It is characterized by its small, oval leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which are followed by small, reddish berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland and scrub habitats.

Rhamnus illyrica is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
