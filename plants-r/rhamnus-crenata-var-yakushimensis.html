Rhamnus crenata var. yakushimensis

Rhamnus crenata var. yakushimensis, commonly known as the Yakushimanese buckthorn, is a shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, followed by dark blue berries that are edible. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in the Rhamnaceae family.
