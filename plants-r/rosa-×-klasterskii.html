Rosa × klasterskii

Rosa × klasterskii, commonly known as the Klasterskii rose, is a hybrid shrub native to the Caucasus region.

It features dense, thorny stems and is characterized by its upright growth habit. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of pink to red, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of dark green, serrated leaves that provide a lush backdrop to the blooms.

Rosa × klasterskii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and hardiness.
