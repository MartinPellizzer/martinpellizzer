Rosa cinnamomea var. korolkowii

Rosa cinnamomea var. korolkowii, commonly known as the Korolkowii shrub rose, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to the Caucasus region.

It features fragrant, semi-double to double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has arching, thorny stems and dark green, ovate leaves with serrated edges. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a border plant due to its compact size and ornamental value.

The rose is also valued for its cold hardiness and ability to thrive in well-drained soil.
