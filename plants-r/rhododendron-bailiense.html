Rhododendron bailiense

Rhododendron bailiense, commonly known as the Yunnan rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments at high altitudes. Its botanical features include thick, leathery leaves and a compact growth habit.

Rhododendron bailiense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
