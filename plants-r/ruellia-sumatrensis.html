Ruellia sumatrensis

Ruellia sumatrensis, commonly known as the Sumatran ruellia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its erect, herbaceous growth habit. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs.
