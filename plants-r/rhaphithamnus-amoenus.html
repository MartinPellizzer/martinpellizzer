Rhaphithamnus amoenus

Rhaphithamnus amoenus, commonly known as the sweet thorn, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, edible pods that contain seeds, which are dispersed by animals. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky soils.
