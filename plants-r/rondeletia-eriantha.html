Rondeletia eriantha

Rondeletia eriantha, commonly known as the velvetleaf rondeletia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in a fine, fuzzy texture.

Rondeletia eriantha is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
