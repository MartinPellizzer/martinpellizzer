Ratzeburgia schimperi

Ratzeburgia schimperi, commonly known as the Ethiopian orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Ethiopia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its delicate, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive white lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. It has a short, fibrous root system and produces small, elliptical leaves.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
