Rosa granulifera

Rosa granulifera, commonly known as the granulate rose, is a species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub-like plant characterized by its thorny stems and dense foliage. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are compound, consisting of multiple oval-shaped leaflets.

Rosa granulifera is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
