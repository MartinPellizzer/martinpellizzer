Ruizia parviflora

Ruizia parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered ruizia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy, arranged in whorls around the stems. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Ruizia parviflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
