Rhodanthe diffusa subsp. diffusa

Rhodanthe diffusa subsp.

diffusa, commonly known as the variable rhodanthe, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia. It is a perennial shrub characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant features narrow, linear leaves and is often found in coastal and sandy soils. It is well adapted to dry conditions and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.

The subspecies is known for its variability in flower color and form, making it a popular choice for horticultural use.
