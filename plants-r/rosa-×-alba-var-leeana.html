Rosa × alba var. leeana

Rosa × alba var. leeana, commonly known as the Leeana white rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from the United Kingdom.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant, white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has a shrub-like growth habit with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and floral arrangements.
