Ruschia patulifolia

Ruschia patulifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
