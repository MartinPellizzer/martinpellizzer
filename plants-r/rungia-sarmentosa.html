Rungia sarmentosa

Rungia sarmentosa, commonly known as the creeping rungea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes coffee and gardenias, and is characterized by its trailing growth habit. Rungia sarmentosa is often grown as a ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
