Rosa × oldhamii

Rosa × oldhamii, commonly known as the Oldham rose, is a hybrid rose species native to China.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant flowers that typically range in color from pale pink to deep red. The plant has thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Rosa × oldhamii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its showy blooms.
