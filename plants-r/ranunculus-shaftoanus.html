Ranunculus shaftoanus

Ranunculus shaftoanus, commonly known as the Shafto's buttercup, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family, characterized by its showy, often yellow or white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or grassy areas and has palmately divided leaves. Its flowers consist of numerous petal-like sepals and numerous stamens, typical of the genus Ranunculus.

It is often found in coastal regions and is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
