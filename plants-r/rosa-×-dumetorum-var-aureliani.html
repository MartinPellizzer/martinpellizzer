Rosa × dumetorum var. aureliani

Rosa × dumetorum var. aureliani, commonly known as the Aureliani rose, is a hybrid species within the rose family, Rosaceae.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, semi-double to fully double flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically produces small, oval leaves with serrated edges, and its thorns are short and curved. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and hardiness in temperate climates.
