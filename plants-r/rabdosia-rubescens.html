Rabdosia rubescens

Rabdosia rubescens, commonly known as red-stemmed rabdosia, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its reddish stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in spikes.

Rabdosia rubescens is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides.
