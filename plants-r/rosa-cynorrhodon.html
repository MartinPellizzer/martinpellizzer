Rosa cynorrhodon

Rosa cynorrhodon, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. Its flowers are often used in traditional Chinese medicine and as ornamental plants in gardens.

Rosa cynorrhodon is also valued for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
