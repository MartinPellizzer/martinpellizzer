Ruschia pinguis

Ruschia pinguis, commonly known as the fatty ruschia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Ruschia pinguis is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
