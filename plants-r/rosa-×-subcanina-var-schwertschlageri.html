Rosa × subcanina var. schwertschlageri

Rosa × subcanina var. schwertschlageri, commonly known as the Schwertschlaegeri rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from Germany.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically has dark green, glossy foliage and thorny stems typical of many rose species. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.

This variety is relatively hardy and can thrive in a range of soil conditions, making it a popular choice for gardeners.
