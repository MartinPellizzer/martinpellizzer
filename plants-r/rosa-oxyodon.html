Rosa oxyodon

Rosa oxyodon, commonly known as the sharp-toothed rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers. The plant produces rose hips, which are the fruit containing the seeds. Rosa oxyodon is native to parts of Asia and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sunlight for optimal growth.
