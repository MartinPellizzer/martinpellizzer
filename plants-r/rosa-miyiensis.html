Rosa miyiensis

Rosa miyiensis, commonly known as the Mihai rose, is a species native to China.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are compound, consisting of 5 to 7 elliptical leaflets.

Rosa miyiensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
