Rosa Tomentella Var. Abietina

Rosa tomentella var. abietina is a rare and delicate shrub native to the mountainous regions of southwestern China. It is characterized by its fuzzy, grayish foliage and small, pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring. This plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in high-altitude forests. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered endangered by conservationists. Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it a subject of interest for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike.
