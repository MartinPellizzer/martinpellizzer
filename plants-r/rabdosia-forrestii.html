Rabdosia forrestii

Rabdosia forrestii, commonly known as Forest's Rabdosia, is a perennial shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its opposite, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Rabdosia forrestii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
