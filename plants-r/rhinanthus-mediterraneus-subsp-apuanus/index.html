Rhinanthus Mediterraneus Subsp. Apuanus

The rhinanthus mediterraneus subsp. apuanus is a rare and striking flowering plant native to southern europe. it belongs to the orchid family and is known for its vibrant orange-red flowers that bloom in late spring. this subspecies is particularly found in calcareous grasslands and rocky areas, often in the apuan alps region of tuscany. its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. due to habitat loss and environmental changes, it faces threats that highlight the need for its protection.
