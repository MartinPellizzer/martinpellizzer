Rosa multiflora var. multiflora

Rosa multiflora var. multiflora, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features compound leaves with five to seven elliptical leaflets, and its flowers are typically pink or white, with a sweet fragrance. The plant produces rose hips, which are the fruit containing numerous seeds. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticulture for its hardiness and adaptability.

However, it can become invasive in some regions if not properly managed.
