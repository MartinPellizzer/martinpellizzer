Rourea luizalbertoi

Rourea luizalbertoi, commonly known as the Luiz Alberti's rourea, is a flowering plant native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its inflorescence consists of clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers, which are often arranged in panicles.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
