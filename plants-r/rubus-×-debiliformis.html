Rubus × debiliformis

Rubus × debiliformis, commonly known as the fragrant raspberry.

It is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family, characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow when ripe. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland edges or disturbed soils.

Due to its ornamental value and edible fruits, it is cultivated in gardens and agricultural settings.
