Rubus × onustus

Rubus × onustus, commonly known as the blackberry bramble, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves, which are typical of the Rubus genus. The plant produces aggregate fruits, known as berries, which are typically dark purple to black in color when mature. These berries are not only edible but also contain high levels of antioxidants and are used in various culinary applications.

Its growth habit makes it both a valuable agricultural crop and a common weed in many regions.
