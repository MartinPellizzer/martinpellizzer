Rubus praealtus

Rubus praealtus, commonly known as the tall blackberry.

It is a species of brambles native to the mountainous regions of South America. The plant is characterized by its thorny stems and trifoliate leaves. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, and it produces edible berries that are similar to blackberries.

The plant is valued for both its ornamental qualities and its fruit, which is often used in jams and desserts.
