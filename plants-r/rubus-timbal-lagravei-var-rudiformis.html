Rubus timbal-lagravei var. rudiformis

Rubus timbal-lagravei var. rudiformis, commonly known as the Timbal-Lagrave's raspberry.

This variant is a shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. It produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red when mature. The plant is native to specific regions in Southeast Asia, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its limited distribution and unique morphological features, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
