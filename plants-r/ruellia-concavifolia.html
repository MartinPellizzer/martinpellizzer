Ruellia concavifolia

Ruellia concavifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf ruellia, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States and parts of Mexico.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland areas and along stream banks.
