Rhapis flabelliformis var. foliis-variegatis

Rhapis flabelliformis var. foliis-variegatis, commonly known as the variegated palm, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features elegant, fan-shaped leaves with a distinctive variegated pattern of green, cream, and gold. The leaves are narrow and arching, creating a lush, tropical appearance. This variety is highly valued for its ornamental beauty and adaptability to indoor environments.

It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
