Rubus palmatifolius

Rubus palmatifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf raspberry, is a species of shrub in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are palmately divided into five to seven lobes. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow when mature. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value and edible fruits, it is cultivated in some regions for both aesthetic and culinary purposes.
