Rosa montana var. schmidelyana

Rosa montana var. schmidelyana, commonly known as the Schmidelyana rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features deep green, glossy leaves that are typically ovate to elliptical in shape. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to red, often appearing in late spring to early summer. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact growth habit.

This variety is noted for its hardiness and ability to thrive in well-drained soil.
