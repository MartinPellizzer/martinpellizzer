Ribes houghtonianum

Ribes houghtonianum, commonly known as Houghton's gooseberry, is a deciduous shrub native to the western United States.

It belongs to the genus Ribes, which includes various species of gooseberries and currants. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and features compound leaves with three to five leaflets. Its flowers are usually yellow or greenish-yellow, and it produces small, edible berries that are often used in culinary applications.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and its role in supporting local wildlife.
