Rhododendron scabrum var. kaempferi

Rhododendron scabrum var. kaempferi, commonly known as the Japanese rhododendron, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It features dense, dark green foliage that turns a striking bronze hue in the winter months. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink to red, typically blooming in late spring. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is a popular choice for garden landscapes in temperate climates.
