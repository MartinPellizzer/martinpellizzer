Rosa wichurana f. plena

Rosa wichurana f. plena, commonly known as the double-flowered Rosa wichurana, is a hybrid rose species characterized by its abundant, full blooms.

It features large, showy flowers with multiple layers of petals, giving it a lush, ornamental appearance. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, glossy foliage. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil and full sun for optimal growth.

This cultivar is prized for its vibrant colors and is often used in gardens and floral arrangements.
