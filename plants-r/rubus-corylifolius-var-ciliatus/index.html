Rubus Corylifolius Var. Ciliatus

Corylifolius raspberry, scientifically known as Rubus corylifolius var. ciliatus, is a unique species of raspberry native to parts of Asia. This plant is characterized by its distinctive foliage and clusters of small, edible berries. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in shaded woodland areas. The berries are prized for their tart flavor and nutritional value. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable plant in botanical studies.
