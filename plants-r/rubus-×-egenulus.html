Rubus × egenulus

Rubus × egenulus, commonly known as the blackberry dewberry.

It is a hybrid plant belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits. The plant typically grows in temperate regions and thrives in well-drained soils. Its leaves are alternate, palmately compound, and have serrated edges.

The fruit, which is a dark purple to black berry, is edible and often used in culinary applications.
