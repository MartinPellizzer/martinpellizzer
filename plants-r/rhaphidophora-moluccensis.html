Rhaphidophora moluccensis

Rhaphidophora moluccensis, commonly known as the Moluccan arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and striking inflorescences. The plant produces a starchy rhizome that is used as a food source in some regions. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and greenhouse cultivation.
