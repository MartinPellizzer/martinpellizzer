Rosa cognata

Rosa cognata, commonly known as the French rose, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae.

It is native to southern Europe and western Asia, and it is characterized by its fragrant, pink to white flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves. Rosa cognata is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens.

Its flowers are used in the production of perfumes and culinary applications.
