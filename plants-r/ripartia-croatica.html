Ripartia croatica

Ripartia croatica, commonly known as the Croatian split-leaf plant, is a member of the Rutaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, deeply lobed leaves that give it a unique appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests or along stream banks. Its flowers are small and clustered, often white or yellow in color.
