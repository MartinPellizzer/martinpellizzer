Rutaea heterophylla

Rutaea heterophylla, commonly known as the variable leaf rutaea, is a flowering plant species native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Rutaceae, which includes citrus and other aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its variable leaf shapes, ranging from ovate to elliptical, and its small, white to yellowish flowers. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in shrubland or woodland habitats.
