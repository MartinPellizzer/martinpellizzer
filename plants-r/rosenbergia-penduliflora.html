Rosenbergia penduliflora

Rosenbergia penduliflora, commonly known as the hanging flower, is a unique member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. Its foliage consists of glossy, ovate leaves that are often variegated with contrasting colors.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
