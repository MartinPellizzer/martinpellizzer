Rosa × dumetorum f. fertilis

Rosa × dumetorum f. fertilis, commonly known as the fertile form of the shrub rose, is a hybrid plant characterized by its robust growth and dense foliage.

It typically features compound leaves with serrated edges and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to red. The plant is known for its strong thorny stems, which provide structural support and deter herbivores. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy addition to gardens and landscapes.

This form is particularly valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various climates.
