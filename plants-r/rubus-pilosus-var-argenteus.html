Rubus pilosus var. argenteus

Rubus pilosus var. argenteus, commonly known as the silver-haired raspberry.

This variant is characterized by its silvery-white pubescent leaves and stems, giving it a distinctive appearance. It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is native to certain regions of North America. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red when ripe.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or shrubland habitats.
