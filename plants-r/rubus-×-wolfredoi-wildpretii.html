Rubus × wolfredoi-wildpretii

Rubus × wolfredoi-wildpretii, commonly known as the wild raspberry.

This hybrid plant is a member of the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits. It typically grows in temperate regions and thrives in well-drained soil. The plant produces small, edible berries that are often used in culinary applications.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ecological and agricultural purposes.
