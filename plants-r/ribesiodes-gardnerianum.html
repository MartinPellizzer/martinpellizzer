Ribesiodes gardnerianum

Ribesiodes gardnerianum, commonly known as the Gardner's ribes, is a deciduous shrub native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, white to pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has elliptical leaves with serrated edges and produces red, fleshy berries in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soils and full to partial sunlight.

Ribesiodes gardnerianum is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
