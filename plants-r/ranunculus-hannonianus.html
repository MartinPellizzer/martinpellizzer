Ranunculus hannonianus

Ranunculus hannonianus, commonly known as Hannon's buttercup, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, cup-shaped flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family, which includes many other buttercup species.

Its botanical features make it a notable wildflower in its native habitat.
