Rhamnidium pruinosum

Rhamnidium pruinosum, commonly known as the silky rhamnus, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its smooth, waxy leaves that give it a distinctive silvery appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are small, round berries that turn red when mature.

Rhamnidium pruinosum is often found in moist, shaded areas and is valued for its ornamental qualities.
