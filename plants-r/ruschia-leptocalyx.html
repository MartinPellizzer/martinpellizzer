Ruschia leptocalyx

Ruschia leptocalyx, commonly known as the slender-leaved Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
