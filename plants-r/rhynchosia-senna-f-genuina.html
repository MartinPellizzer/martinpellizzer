Rhynchosia senna f. genuina

Rhynchosia senna f. genuina, commonly known as the African senna, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces elongated pods that contain small, hard seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is cultivated in some regions for both horticultural and traditional uses.
