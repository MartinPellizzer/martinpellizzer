Ranunculus changpingensis

Ranunculus changpingensis, commonly known as the Changping buttercup, is a rare flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family, which includes many showy, often toxic species. The plant features delicate, palmate leaves and vibrant yellow or white flowers with numerous stamens. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountainous regions.
