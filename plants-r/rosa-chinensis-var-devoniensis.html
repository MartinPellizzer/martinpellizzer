Rosa chinensis var. devoniensis

Rosa chinensis var. devoniensis, commonly known as the Devonshire rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to China.

It features vibrant, semi-double to fully double flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom profusely throughout the growing season. The plant has glossy, dark green foliage that provides a striking contrast to its showy blooms. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained, fertile soil for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and as a potted plant.
