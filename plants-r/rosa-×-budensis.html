Rosa × budensis

Rosa × budensis, commonly known as the Hungarian rose, is a hybrid species of the Rosa genus.

It is characterized by its fragrant, semi-double to double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. Its foliage consists of dark green, serrated leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its showy blooms.

Rosa × budensis is native to Hungary and is valued for its ornamental qualities in gardens and floral arrangements.
