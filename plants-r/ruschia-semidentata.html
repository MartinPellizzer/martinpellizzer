Ruschia semidentata

Ruschia semidentata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, narrow leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a fuzzy texture. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
