Rosa afzeliana var. noerholmensis

Rosa afzeliana var. noerholmensis, commonly known as the Noerholm rose, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and dark green, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It is well adapted to cool, moist climates and thrives in partial to full sunlight.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and resilience.
