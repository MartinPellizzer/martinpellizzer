Rosa hybrida f. laeta

Rosa hybrida f. laeta, commonly known as the Double-flowered Hybrid Rose, is a cultivar characterized by its abundant, full blooms.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is known for its showy, layered petals that create a lush, almost spherical appearance. The plant typically has thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

This rose is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in floral arrangements and decorative gardening.
