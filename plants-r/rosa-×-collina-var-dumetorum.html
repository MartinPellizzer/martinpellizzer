Rosa × collina var. dumetorum

Rosa × collina var. dumetorum, commonly known as the alpine rose, is a low-growing shrub native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features dense, mat-forming growth with small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink to white. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hair, aiding in water retention. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to cold, alpine climates.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
