Rubus × amblystachys

Rubus × amblystachys, commonly known as blackberry bramble, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces aggregate fruits, which are typically dark purple to black in color when mature. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland areas or along hedgerows.

Its botanical features make it a common subject in both horticulture and ecological studies.
