Ricinocarpus ruizianus

Ricinocarpus ruizianus, commonly known as the red mangrove, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rhizophoraceae.

It is characterized by its prop roots that extend into the water, providing structural support in tidal environments. Ricinocarpus ruizianus is adapted to saline and brackish water conditions, making it a key component of mangrove forests.
