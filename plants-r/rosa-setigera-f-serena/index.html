Rosa Setigera F. Serena

Serena rose, scientifically known as Rosa setigera f. serena, is a rare and elegant hybrid rose prized for its delicate beauty and unique fragrance. Native to the mountainous regions of western North America, this plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded woodland areas. With its pale pink blooms and soft, velvety petals, the Serena rose stands out as a subtle yet striking addition to any garden. Its compact growth habit makes it ideal for small spaces, and its ability to bloom throughout the growing season adds lasting charm. Due to its limited distribution and specific growing requirements, cultivating the Serena rose requires care and attention to ensure its survival.
