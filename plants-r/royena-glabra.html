Royena glabra

Royena glabra, commonly known as the smooth royal fern, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the family Araceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its large, pinnately compound leaves and a distinctive spadix inflorescence. Its smooth, glabrous stems and leaves contribute to its common name.
