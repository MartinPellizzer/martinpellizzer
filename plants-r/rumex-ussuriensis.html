Rumex ussuriensis

Rumex ussuriensis, commonly known as Ussuri sorrel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive reddish coloration on the underside. Its flowers are small and clustered in spikes, and it produces capsules that contain numerous seeds.

Rumex ussuriensis is valued for its edible leaves, which have a tangy, slightly sour flavor.
