Rosa hemitricha var. beugesiaca

Rosa hemitricha var. beugesiaca, commonly known as the Beugesiaca rose, is a compact, shrub-like species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It features small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink to reddish in color, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and its stems are covered in thorns. It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
