Rubus × domenjouanus

Rubus × domenjouanus, commonly known as the Domenjouan blackberry, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Rubus genus in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves, typical of many brambles. The plant produces aggregate fruits, known as blackberries, which are typically dark purple to black in color. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland edges or disturbed soils.

Due to its ornamental and edible qualities, it is cultivated in some regions for both aesthetic and culinary purposes.
