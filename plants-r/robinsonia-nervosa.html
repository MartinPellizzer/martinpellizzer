Robinsonia nervosa

Robinsonia nervosa, commonly known as the slender leaf fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, hair-like structures. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides. Its reproductive structures, known as sori, are found on the underside of the fronds.

Robinsonia nervosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
