Rosa Eglanteria Var. Rotundifolia

Rosa eglanteria var. rotundifolia is a unique and lesser-known member of the rose family known for its striking appearance and hardy nature. This variant features round, leathery leaves and thorny stems that make it both distinctive and resilient. Native to mountainous regions of Central Asia, it thrives in harsh climates and poor soils. Its flowers, though small, are often fragrant and bloom in shades of pink and white. Despite its beauty, it remains a rare and underappreciated plant in horticulture.
