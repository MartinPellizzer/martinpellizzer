Racosperma pycnocephalum

Racosperma pycnocephalum, commonly known as the dense-headed racosperma, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces pods that contain seeds, which are adapted for dispersal by wind or animals.

It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is valued for its hardy nature and ornamental appeal.
