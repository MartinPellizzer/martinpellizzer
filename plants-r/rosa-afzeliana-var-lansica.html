Rosa afzeliana var. lansica

Rosa afzeliana var. lansica, commonly known as the Chinese rose or evergreen rose, is a shrub native to China and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, pink or white flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. It is well adapted to humid climates and thrives in partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
