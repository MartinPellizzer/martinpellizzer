Russelia sarmentosa

Russelia sarmentosa, commonly known as the trailing russelia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a perennial shrub that produces vibrant, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has trailing stems that often root at the nodes, allowing it to spread along the ground. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

Russelia sarmentosa thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often used in ornamental gardening for its showy blooms.
