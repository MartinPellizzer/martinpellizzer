Ruyschiana discolor

Ruyschiana discolor, commonly known as the variegated ruyschiana, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features lance-shaped leaves with striking white and green variegation, which enhance its ornamental value. The plant produces small, fragrant yellow flowers in early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its foliage remains attractive throughout the growing season, offering year-round visual interest.
