Rabdosia × togashii

Rabdosia × togashii, commonly known as the hybrid blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its foliage is often covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Rabdosia × togashii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of garden conditions.
