Rosa exporrigens

Rosa exporrigens, commonly known as the alpine rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Europe and is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically narrow and serrated, forming a dense, mat-like structure.

Rosa exporrigens is well adapted to cold climates and is often found growing in rocky or alpine environments.
