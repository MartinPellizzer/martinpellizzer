Rosa communis var. systyla

Rosa communis var. systyla, commonly known as the upright pink rose, is a cultivar characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and vibrant pink flowers.

It produces semi-double to fully double blooms that are typically fragrant and showy. The plant features thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its blossoms. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden borders or container planting.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and hardiness in temperate climates.
