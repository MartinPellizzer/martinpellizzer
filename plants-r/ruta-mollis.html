Ruta mollis

Ruta mollis, commonly known as soft rue, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features aromatic, dark green leaves that are soft to the touch, distinguishing it from other members of the Ruta genus. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early summer, followed by small, round berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used historically in herbal remedies.
