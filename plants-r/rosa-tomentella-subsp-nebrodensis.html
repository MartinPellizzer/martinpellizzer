Rosa tomentella subsp. nebrodensis

Rosa tomentella subsp.

nebrodensis, commonly known as the Nebrodensis rose, is a shrub-like plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features small, clustered flowers that are typically pink to white in color.

The plant has a dense, woolly foliage that helps it retain moisture in its arid habitat. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature.
