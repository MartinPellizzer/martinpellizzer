Rosa reynieri

Rosa reynieri, commonly known as the Reynier's rose, is a species of flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually covered in a fine layer of down. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Rosa reynieri is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in perfumery.
