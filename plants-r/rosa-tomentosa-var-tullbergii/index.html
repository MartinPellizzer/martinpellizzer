Rosa Tomentosa Var. Tullbergii

Rosa tomentosa var. tullbergii is a unique and rare cultivar of the rose family known for its distinctive appearance and hardy nature. This variety is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and clusters of fragrant, pink flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Native to specific regions in Europe, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight. Its compact growth habit makes it ideal for small gardens or container planting. Despite its modest size, this rose holds a special place in horticultural circles for its beauty and resilience.
