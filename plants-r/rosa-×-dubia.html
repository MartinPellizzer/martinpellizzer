Rosa × dubia

Rosa × dubia, commonly known as the hybrid tea rose, is a popular ornamental plant characterized by its large, showy flowers.

It is a result of crossbreeding between different rose species, leading to a wide variety of colors and forms. The plant typically has thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun for optimal growth and flowering.

Rosa × dubia is widely cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
