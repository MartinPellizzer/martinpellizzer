Rhopalephora blumei

Rhopalephora blumei, commonly known as the Blume's climbing fern, is a member of the Araliaceae family.

It is a climbing plant characterized by its slender, aerial roots and pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers arranged in umbels, followed by red, berry-like fruits. Native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and greenhouse cultivation.
