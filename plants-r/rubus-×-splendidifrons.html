Rubus × splendidifrons

Rubus × splendidifrons, commonly known as the splendidifrons blackberry, is a hybrid species within the Rubus genus.

It is characterized by its large, dark purple to black berries that are typically sweet and aromatic. The plant is a deciduous shrub with thorny stems and compound leaves that have serrated edges. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland areas or along hedgerows.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both ornamental and culinary purposes.
