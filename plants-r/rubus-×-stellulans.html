Rubus × stellulans

Rubus × stellulans, commonly known as the evergreen blackberry shrub, is a hybrid species within the Rubus genus.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny growth habit and dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically dark purple to black in color. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland edges or disturbed soils.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and ecologically significant in native habitats.
