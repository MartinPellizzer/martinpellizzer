Rudgea burchelliana

Rudgea burchelliana, commonly known as the Burchell's rudgea, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and showy, tubular flowers. Rudgea burchelliana is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
