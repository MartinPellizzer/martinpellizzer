Ruschia umbellata

Ruschia umbellata, commonly known as the starburst plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a slightly fuzzy texture. The plant produces clusters of bright orange or yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid or semi-arid climates.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
