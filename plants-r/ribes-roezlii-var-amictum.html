Ribes roezlii var. amictum

Ribes roezlii var. amictum, commonly known as the smooth currant, is a compact shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features narrow, elliptical leaves that are typically smooth and dark green on top, with a lighter underside. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early summer, followed by red, edible berries that are somewhat tart in flavor. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential use in local cuisines.
