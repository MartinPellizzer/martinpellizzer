Rubia danaensis

Rubia danaensis, commonly known as Dana's rue, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes many species of shrubs and herbs. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly serrated edge.

Rubia danaensis is known for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
