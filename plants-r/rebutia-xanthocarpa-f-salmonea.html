Rebutia xanthocarpa f. salmonea

Rebutia xanthocarpa f. salmonea, commonly known as the salmon-pink rebutia, is a hybrid cactus species native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact, rounded stems that are typically covered in dense, woolly spines. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of pink and salmon, which bloom during the warmer months. Its foliage is usually green, with a slightly ribbed texture, and it thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

This succulent is popular among cactus enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
