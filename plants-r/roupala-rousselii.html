Roupala rousselii

Roupala rousselii, commonly known as the African snowball, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Africa.

It features rounded, clustered inflorescences that produce small, white, woolly flowers, which bloom in spring. The plant has smooth, gray-green leaves that are alternate and ovate in shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Roupala rousselii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
