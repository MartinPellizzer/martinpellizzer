Rotheca violacea subsp. kigeziensis

Rotheca violacea subsp.

kigeziensis, commonly known as the Kigezi violet, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of East Africa. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of violet to purple flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its leaves are ovate to elliptical in shape, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
