Rosa × centifolia nivea

Rosa × centifolia nivea, commonly known as the white centifolia rose, is a hybrid species of rose native to France.

It is characterized by its numerous, fragrant, white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant is a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun for optimal growth.

This variety is highly valued in perfumery and horticulture for its delicate appearance and strong aromatic qualities.
