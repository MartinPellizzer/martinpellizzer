Rosa californica florepleno

Rosa californica florepleno, commonly known as the California floribunda rose, is a hybrid rose species native to California.

It is characterized by its abundant, clustered blooms of bright pink to red flowers that appear throughout the growing season. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with thorny stems and dark green, aromatic foliage. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its hardiness and showy flowers make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and pollinator-friendly gardens.
