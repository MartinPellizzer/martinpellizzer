Rhododendron xanthoneuron

Rhododendron xanthoneuron, commonly known as the yellow rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of showy, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It prefers well-drained, acidic soils and partial to full shade.

Rhododendron xanthoneuron is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
