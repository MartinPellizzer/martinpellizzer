Rumex amurensis

Rumex amurensis, commonly known as Amur dock, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish flowers that are typically arranged in a dense inflorescence. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.

The leaves and stems are sometimes used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
