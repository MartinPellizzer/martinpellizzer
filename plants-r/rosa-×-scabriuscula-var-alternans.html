Rosa × scabriuscula var. alternans

Rosa × scabriuscula var. alternans, commonly known as the alternate-stem Rosa.

It is a hybrid rose species characterized by its distinctive foliage, where the upper and lower surfaces of the leaves exhibit different textures and colors. The plant typically features thorny stems and produces fragrant, pink to red flowers in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its unique leaf morphology and ornamental flowers make it a valuable addition to horticultural collections.
