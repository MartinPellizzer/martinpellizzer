Rubus melanolasius var. discolor

Rubus melanolasius var. discolor, commonly known as the variable blackberry.

It is a shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits. The plant typically grows in temperate regions and thrives in well-drained soils. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have serrated margins.

The berries, which are usually dark purple to black, are edible and often used in culinary applications.
