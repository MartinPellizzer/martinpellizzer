Rubus × callitrichus

Rubus × callitrichus, commonly known as the evergreen blackberry.

It is a hybrid shrub native to the coastal regions of western North America. The plant features thorny stems and dark green, leathery leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. Its flowers are usually pink or white, and it produces small, edible berries that are often used in culinary applications.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
