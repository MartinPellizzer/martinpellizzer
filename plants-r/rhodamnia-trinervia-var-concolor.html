Rhodamnia trinervia var. concolor

Rhodamnia trinervia var. concolor, commonly known as the smooth-leafed rhodamnia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, elliptical leaves that are typically smooth and glossy on both sides. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to white, which bloom in spring. It is often found in heathlands and open forests, thriving in well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
