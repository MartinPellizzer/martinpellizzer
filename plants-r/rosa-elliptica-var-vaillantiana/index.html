Rosa Elliptica Var. Vaillantiana

Rosa elliptica var. vaillantiana is a rare and striking species of rose native to the mountainous regions of China. This variety is known for its vibrant pink to red flowers and its unique elliptical-shaped leaves. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas at high elevations. Due to its limited natural range, it is considered a delicate and endangered plant. Cultivating this rose requires careful attention to soil, climate, and seasonal conditions.
