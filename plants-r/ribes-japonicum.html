Ribes japonicum

Ribes japonicum, commonly known as Japanese gooseberry, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It features small, yellow, edible berries that are often used in culinary applications. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant spring foliage and attractive winter structure.
