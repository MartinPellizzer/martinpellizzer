Rhynchospora flexuosa subsp. tepuiana

Rhynchospora flexuosa subsp.

The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pale green to brown in color. It is characterized by its flexible stems and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Rhynchospora flexuosa subsp.
