Rhynchospora racemosa f. setifera

Rhynchospora racemosa f. setifera, commonly known as the slender spike-rush, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that bear dense, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wetlands and moist soils, thriving in environments with consistent water availability. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a distinct midrib.

The inflorescence consists of small, three-lobed flowers that are usually greenish or brownish in color.
