Ruschia valida

Ruschia valida, commonly known as the silver leaf Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, silver-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps it store water and reduce transpiration. The plant produces vibrant red or orange flowers during the summer months, adding visual interest to its appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact size and low maintenance requirements make it an ideal plant for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
