Ruellia nobilis

Ruellia nobilis, commonly known as the purple heart plant, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking tubular purple flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a vibrant splash of color to gardens. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and often have a slight waxy texture. Ruellia nobilis is a perennial shrub that thrives in full sun to partial shade and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.

It is popular in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators like hummingbirds and butterflies.
