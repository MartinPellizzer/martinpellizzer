Rebutia neocumingii

Rebutia neocumingii, commonly known as the Neocuming's Rebutia, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming plant with short, cylindrical stems that are typically green or blue-green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, funnel-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Rebutia neocumingii is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
