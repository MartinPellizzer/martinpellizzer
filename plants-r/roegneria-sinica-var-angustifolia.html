Roegneria sinica var. angustifolia

Roegneria sinica var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf bluegrass, is a perennial grass species native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
