Rubus × semibanaticus

Rubus × semibanaticus, commonly known as the blackberry-loganberry hybrid, is a deciduous shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Rubus ursinus (blackberry) and Rubus loganobaccus (loganberry), resulting in a plant with unique botanical characteristics. The plant typically has thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are white or pale pink, and it produces edible berries that are a cross between blackberries and loganberries.

This hybrid is valued for its hardy nature and adaptability to various growing conditions.
