Rhipsalis cassythoides

Rhipsalis cassythoides, commonly known as the silver leaf cactus, is a climbing cactus native to Brazil.

It features soft, flexible stems that are covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks.

Its unique morphology and ability to climb make it an interesting addition to tropical gardens.
