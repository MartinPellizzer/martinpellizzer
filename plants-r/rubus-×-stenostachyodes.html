Rubus × stenostachyodes

Rubus × stenostachyodes, commonly known as the evergreen blackberry, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and trifoliate leaves, which are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are usually dark purple to black in color. It is native to eastern Asia and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and edible berries.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits a range of morphological variations in its botanical features.
