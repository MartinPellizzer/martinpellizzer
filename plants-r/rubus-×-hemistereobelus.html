Rubus × hemistereobelus

Rubus × hemistereobelus, commonly known as the evergreen blackberry.

It is a hybrid plant belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, dark berries that are typically harvested in late summer. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in shaded, moist environments.

Due to its ornamental value and edible fruit, it is cultivated in gardens and small-scale farms.
