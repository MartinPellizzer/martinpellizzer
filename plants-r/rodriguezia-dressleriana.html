Rodriguezia dressleriana

Rodriguezia dressleriana, commonly known as the Dressler's rodriguezia, is a striking tropical plant native to the mountainous regions of Colombia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant features broad, dark green leaves with a velvety texture, and it produces attractive, clustered inflorescences.
