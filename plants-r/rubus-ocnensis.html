Rubus ocnensis

Rubus ocnensis, commonly known as the Chinese blackberry, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically dark red to black in color. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and shrublands.

Due to its ornamental value and edible fruits, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and for local consumption.
