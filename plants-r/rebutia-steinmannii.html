Rebutia steinmannii

Rebutia steinmannii, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a species of flowering cactus native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its compact, branching stems that are covered in short, woolly hairs. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, pink, and white during the winter months. It prefers well-draining soil and bright, indirect light.

Rebutia steinmannii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
