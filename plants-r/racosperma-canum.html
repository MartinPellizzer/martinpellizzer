Racosperma canum

Racosperma canum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf wattle, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, yellow flowers and bipinnate leaves. The plant is often found in sandy or rocky soils in arid and semi-arid regions. It has a woody stem and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.
