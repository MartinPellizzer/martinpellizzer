Rhododendron hirsutum var. variegatum

Rhododendron hirsutum var. variegatum, commonly known as the variegated hirsute rhododendron, is a compact evergreen shrub native to the Himalayas.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with distinctive white or yellow variegation patterns. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, and white during late spring to early summer. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and shaded borders.
