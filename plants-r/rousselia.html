Rousselia

Rousselia, commonly known as the silverleaf rosselia, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the family Acanthaceae.

It is characterized by its striking silver-white leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. Native to tropical regions of South America, it thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique foliage and delicate blooms.

It prefers partial shade and regular watering to maintain its vibrant appearance.
