Ranunculus meilixueshanicus

Ranunculus meilixueshanicus, commonly known as the Meilixueshan buttercup, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family, which includes many showy and often toxic species. The plant is characterized by its delicate, palmately lobed leaves and clusters of bright yellow, cup-shaped flowers. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is typically found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
