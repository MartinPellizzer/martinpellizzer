Rubus vestitus var. macrothyrsus

Rubus vestitus var. macrothyrsus, commonly known as the hairy raspberry.

It is a variant of the raspberry plant characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and stems. The plant produces aggregate fruits composed of small drupes, which are typically red when ripe. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland edges or disturbed soils.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive species within the Rubus genus.
