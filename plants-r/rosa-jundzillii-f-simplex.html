Rosa jundzillii f. simplex

Rosa jundzillii f. simplex, commonly known as the simple form of the Jundzillii rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from pale pink to deep red, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, thornless stems and dark green, glossy leaves that provide a striking contrast to its colorful blooms. It is well adapted to rocky and well-drained soils, making it a hardy choice for garden cultivation.

Its simple growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular selection for rock gardens and borders.
