Roemeria hybrida f. adpressa

Roemeria hybrida f. adpressa, commonly known as the compact form of the hybrid roemeria, is a low-growing, mat-forming plant characterized by its dense, prostrate growth habit.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale pink, often arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to form a dense ground cover.
