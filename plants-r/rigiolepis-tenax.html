Rigiolepis tenax

Rigiolepis tenax, commonly known as the hardy ground orchid, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in alpine or subalpine regions. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive spurred petal. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it has a short, fibrous root system.

Rigiolepis tenax is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil.
