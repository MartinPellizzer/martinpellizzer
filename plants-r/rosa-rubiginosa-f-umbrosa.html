Rosa rubiginosa f. umbrosa

Rosa rubiginosa f. umbrosa, commonly known as the dark rose or shadow rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dark red to almost black flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dense, thorny stems and small, dark green leaves that are covered in a fine, fuzzy layer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it adaptable to various garden conditions.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and its ability to attract pollinators.
