Rosa agrestis var. vallombrosae

Rosa agrestis var. vallombrosae, commonly known as the Vallombrosa rose, is a compact shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, thorny stems and dark green, trifoliate leaves. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its historical significance includes being cultivated in monastic gardens, particularly in Italy.
