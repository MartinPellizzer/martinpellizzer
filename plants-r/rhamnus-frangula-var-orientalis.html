Rhamnus frangula var. orientalis

Rhamnus frangula var. orientalis, commonly known as the oriental buckthorn, is a shrub or small tree native to parts of Asia.

It features dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, followed by dark blue, fleshy berries. It is adapted to temperate climates and can thrive in a variety of soil types.

The species is notable for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
