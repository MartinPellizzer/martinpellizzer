Rosa complens

Rosa complens, commonly known as the common rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its thorny stems, compound leaves, and showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant produces hips, which are the fruiting bodies containing seeds. Rosa complens is native to Europe and western Asia and is widely cultivated for ornamental purposes.

It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil and full sunlight.
