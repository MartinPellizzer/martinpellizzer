Rosa geracantha

Rosa geracantha, commonly known as the Greek rose, is a species of flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with serrated leaflets that provide a lush green appearance.

Rosa geracantha is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
