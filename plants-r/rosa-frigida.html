Rosa frigida

Rosa frigida, commonly known as the alpine rose, is a hardy shrub native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has dark green, serrated leaves and produces rose hips that are rich in vitamin C. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky soils.

Rosa frigida is valued for its ornamental beauty and its ability to withstand harsh climatic conditions.
