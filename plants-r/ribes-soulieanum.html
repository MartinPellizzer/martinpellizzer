Ribes soulieanum

Ribes soulieanum, commonly known as the Chinese gooseberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Grossulariaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The species is also valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
