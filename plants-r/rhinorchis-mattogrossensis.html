Rhinorchis mattogrossensis

Rhinorchis mattogrossensis, commonly known as the Mattogrossense orchid, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in the understory of tropical forests. Its flowers are usually small and have a distinctive coloration, often with a yellow or greenish hue.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
