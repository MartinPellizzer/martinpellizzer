Rubus assaortinus

Rubus assaortinus, commonly known as the Assorted Raspberry.

This species is a deciduous shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits. It typically grows in temperate regions, preferring well-drained soils and partial shade. The plant produces small, edible berries that are often used in culinary applications.

Its botanical features include compound leaves with serrated edges and a flowering period in late spring.
