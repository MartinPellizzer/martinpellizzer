Rosa gallica burboniana

Rosa gallica burboniana, commonly known as the Bourbon rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and has been cultivated for its ornamental value. The plant features fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are compound, consisting of five to seven oval-shaped leaflets.

Rosa gallica burboniana is valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
