Reldia alternifolia

Reldia alternifolia, commonly known as the zigzag tree, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, alternating leaf arrangement and slender, branched stems. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, which bloom in clusters. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and often have a slightly waxy texture.

Reldia alternifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
