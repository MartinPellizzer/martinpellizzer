Rhipsalis herminiae

Rhipsalis herminiae, commonly known as the silver leaf cactus, is a species of epiphytic cactus native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its soft, leaf-like stems that are covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom during the winter months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
