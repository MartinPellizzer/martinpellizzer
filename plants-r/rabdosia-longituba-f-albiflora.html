Rabdosia longituba f. albiflora

Rabdosia longituba f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered rabdosia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, arranged in clusters at the leaf axils.

This variant is distinguished by its notably pale floral coloration compared to the typical form of Rabdosia longituba.
