Rhododendron × bungonishikii

Rhododendron × bungonishikii, commonly known as the Bungonishikii rhododendron, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Rhododendron brachycarpum and Rhododendron mucronulatum.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant is evergreen and typically grows in a compact, shrub-like form. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
