Ranunculus politus

Ranunculus politus, commonly known as the smooth buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features bright yellow flowers with 5 to 15 petals, often surrounded by a ring of sepals that resemble additional petals. The plant has smooth, glossy leaves that are typically trifoliate or palmately divided. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

The species is known for its ornamental value and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant, cheerful blooms.
