Rubus × petrogenes

Rubus × petrogenes, commonly known as the blackberry raspberry hybrid, is a deciduous shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Rubus ursinus (blackberry) and Rubus chamaemorus (cloudberry), resulting in a plant with unique botanical characteristics. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red to dark purple in color. Its leaves are compound, with three to five leaflets, and it has thorny stems typical of the Rubus genus.

It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in moist, acidic soils.
