Rosa rigida

Rosa rigida, commonly known as the rigid rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family, Rosaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, including parts of Kazakhstan and the Tian Shan. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit, with dark green, serrated leaves and fragrant pink to red flowers that bloom in summer. Its thorny stems and showy blooms make it a distinctive ornamental plant.

Rosa rigida is often cultivated for its aesthetic value and is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
