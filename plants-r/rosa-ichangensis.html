Rosa ichangensis

Rosa ichangensis, commonly known as the Ichang rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has alternate, pinnately compound leaves with serrated edges. Its stems are armed with thorns, and it produces oval, red-brown fruits known as hips.

Rosa ichangensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
